Preamble: The associations of Government servants (non-industrial) are recognized by the Government subject to the provisions of Rule 29, Rule 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 and the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure, Schedule I and Schedule II of the said Rules and the Constitution and Rules of the" New Model Rules."
The procedure to be followed for the same has been prescribed vide Government Circular dated 18.3.2015 at Reference 3.
The Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai vide his letter dated 28.07.2017 has submitted to the Government a proposal for grant of Government recognition to the" Maharashtra State Nurses Association, HQ Latur" (hereinafter referred to as" the Association") under the prescribed format of application. The matter of granting recognition to this organization under the provisions of Rule 29 and 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 was under the consideration of the Government. As a result, the following decisions are made:
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Nurses Association, HQ Latur is being recognized by the Government subject to the terms and conditions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, Rules No. 29 and 30 and the New Model Rules, Schedule II, Rules No. 1 to 29 and subject to the other terms and conditions prescribed below:
The association will be affiliated under the administrative control of the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals.
(2) The Secretary of the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals in the Ministry shall be the competent authority in case of disputes regarding inspection of the office of the Association, elections to the Association, office bearers. He may also nominate an officer subordinate to him (who is not inferior to an officer of the rank of Deputy Secretary) for this function.
