The Proposal: -
The rate of remuneration / honorarium, overtime allowance / election allowance payable to the officers / staff to be appointed for the purpose of General / Bye-elections to the House of the People / Legislative Assembly under the jurisdiction of the Election Commission of India vide Government Reference No. The governments from 1 to 4 are determined by decisions. The Election Commission of India referred him to No. D at 5b6. Instructions have been given to revise the rates of remuneration / honorarium, overtime allowance / election allowance etc. payable to the officers / staff to be appointed for the Vala election peat on 08.08.2025. As per the instructions of the said letter of the Election Commission of India, the Government was under consideration to revise the emoluments / honorarium, overtime allowances / election allowances etc. payable to the returning officers / staff to be appointed for the duties of the forthcoming Lok Sabha / Assembly elections in the State under the jurisdiction of the Election Commission of India. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken:
