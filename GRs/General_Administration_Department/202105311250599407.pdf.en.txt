Paragraph 2. The benefit of reservation for economically weaker sections shall be admissible subject to the following conditions:-
(a) 10% reservation in the total number of seats in Government Educational Institutions/ Aided Schools, Colleges, Educational Institutions, Unaided Schools, Colleges, Educational Institutions, Autonomous Universities for the economically weaker sections of the people in the State, whose castes are not included in the Maharashtra State Public Service (Reservation for Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Tribes, Nomadic Tribes, Special Backward Classes and Other Backward Classes) Act, 2001 (Maharashtra Act No. 8 of 2004). This reservation shall not apply in the minority institutions referred to in clause (1) of Article 30 of the Constitution of the State.
Also, 10% of the total number of vacancies in any cadre of direct service posts in Government establishments, semi-Government establishments, Boards/ Corporations/ Urban Local Bodies/ Rural Local Bodies Authorities shall be reserved for economically weaker sections.
(b) This 10% reservation shall be in addition to the existing reservation for Backward Classes in the State as prescribed under the State Public Service (Reservation for Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Tribes, Nomadic Tribes, Special Backward Classes and Other Backward Classes) Act, 2001 (Maharashtra Act 8 of 2004).
