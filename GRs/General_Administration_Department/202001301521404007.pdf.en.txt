As per the decision of the Government at Roll No. 4, the duration of the training phases has been revised in line with the implementation of the Post Graduate Degree Courses under the Combined Peripheral Training Programme (CPTP).
Provisions have been made to provide bulk pay to probationary officers for the period of their initial training in training institutions to ensure that probationary officers do not have to remain unpaid during the initial period of training, taking into account the time taken by the administrative departments concerned to meet the technicalities, such as making in-service records regarding the salary to be paid to probationary officers, obtaining staff specifications under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme, etc.
The 7th Pay Commission has been approved for the officers/ employees of the State Government with effect from 1st January, 2019 and accordingly, salaries are being provided under the revised pay schedule. Considering these aspects, the Government was considering to increase the total salary amount to be provided to the probationary officers during the training period who are undergoing training under the Integrated Probationary Training Programme. The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has decided to increase the salary of Group A and Group B probationary officers selected through the state service examination and trained under the Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP).
