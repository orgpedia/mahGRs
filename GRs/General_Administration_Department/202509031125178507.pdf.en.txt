Preamble: - Regarding the social, educational and economic condition of the Maratha community and the action to be taken on the recommendations on other allied matters. The Minister for Water Resources (Godavari B. Krishna Basin Development Corporation) chaired the Read No. 1 here. A Cabinet Sub-Committee has been constituted by a Government Decision dated 22.08.2025.
Considering the demands received by the government of the Maratha community, it was decided to discuss and take a decision on these demands. The Minister for Water Resources (Godavari B. Krishna Basin Development Corporation) chaired the meeting. The meeting was held on 02.09.2025. The Government is taking the following decisions in accordance with the directions given in this meeting.
The Government's Decisions:
On the demands of the Maratha community, Ma. The Government is taking the following decisions as per the directions given by the Cabinet Sub-Committee.
1) The pending proceedings regarding the grant of financial assistance and employment to the next of kin of those who died in the Maratha reservation agitation should be taken up immediately.
2) Department of Home Affairs, Government of India. The rest of the process of withdrawing the cases against Maratha protesters should be completed by the end of September, 2025, as per the 20.09.2022.
3) The 58 lakh records / records discovered so far should be published on the notice boards of all Gram Panchayats. All the District Collectors should complete the process in their respective districts.
4) The process of verification of caste certificate shall be completed within 90 days from the date of receipt of the application under the Regulation of Certification of Caste Act, 2000 and rules made thereunder.
