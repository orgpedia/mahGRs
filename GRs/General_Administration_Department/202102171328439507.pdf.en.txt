Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification No. 04.01.2021 has been published under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the website of the Central Government at www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government Resolution, the Government was contemplating to ensure posts for the disabled in the Group-A to Group-D cadre in the office of the Additional Chief Secretary and Resident Commissioner, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi under the General Administration Department.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification to ensure reservation of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, the list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published.
As mentioned in it, the Central Government has ensured posts for the disabled from Group' A'.
