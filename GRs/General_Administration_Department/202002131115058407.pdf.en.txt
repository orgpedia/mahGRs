Read:- 1. Government Decision No. LPL-1019/ P.S. No. 09/ Works. July 8, 2019.
The Cabinet also approved the proposal to amend the Maharashtra Lok Ayukta and Deputy Lok Ayukta Act, 1971 and to grant daily allowances, sitting allowances and travel allowances to the non-official members and special invitees of the Joint Drafting Committee constituted for the purpose of drafting the amended Act.
(2) the decision of the relevant Government. As on 8.7.2019, Non-official members of Joint Drafting Committee and Special Invitees are entitled to a sitting allowance of Rs. 300/- has been approved. A proposal to revise the rate of allowance was under the consideration of the government.
3. The non-official members and special invitees of the Joint Drafting Committee shall be paid a sitting allowance of Rs. 500/- respectively.
