Preamble: As per the provisions of Section 13 of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, As per the notification dated 30th July, 2016, the Maharashtra State Commission for Right to Service has been reduced.
As per the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I, Roll No. 1, Rule No. 2 of the Bombay Fiscal Rules, 1959, as per the financial powers conferred on the administrative department under the rules, the As per the Government Order dated 20th March, 2017, the Deputy Secretary in the office of the State Chief Commissioner of Right to Service has been designated as the Head of Department and Referral Officer of the Maharashtra State Commission for Right to Service. As per the Government decision dated 17.12.2021, the State Right to Service Commissioner, Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary (if the post of Deputy Secretary is upgraded to the rank of Joint Secretary in future) in the office of Konkan Division has been declared as" Head of Department" in the office of State Right to Service Commission, Konkan Revenue Division.
Section 13 of the Maharashtra Right to Service Act, 2015 provides that all Commissioners, Maharashtra State Right to Service Commission shall have the power to exercise all the powers which are exercisable by the State Chief Rights Commission. Therefore, although the general superintendence, direction and management of the affairs of the Commission are vested in the Chief Commissioner, each Revenue Department being a separate office, it is necessary to appoint a separate Head of Department for the said office, so that the financial powers of the Head of Department thereof do not require the approval of the Office of the State Chief Service Rights Commissioner, Maharashtra State Service Rights Commission in respect of limited financial matters. The State Government has issued notification for the post of State Service Commissioner, Nashik/ Pune/ Nagpur/ Amravati/ Aurangabad. 23/11/2021

Government's decision:
The decision was taken by the Finance Ministry. In accordance with the Financial Powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959, in accordance with Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part-I, Sub-Division-I in the Annexure appended as Annexure-I dated 17th April, 2015, the Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary (if the post of Deputy Secretary is upgraded to the rank of Joint Secretary in future) in the Revenue Department Office of the Maharashtra State Service Commission, Nashik/ Pune/ Nagpur/ Amravati/ Aurangabad is being declared as the" Head of Department" of the respective Revenue Department Office of the Maharashtra State Service Commission.
(2) However, if the post of Deputy Secretary in the 6 Revenue Divisional Offices of the Maharashtra State Service Commission, Konkan/ Nashik/ Pune/ Nagpur/ Amravati/ Aurangabad, is vacant, the said vacancy shall be filled by an officer holding the post of Deputy Secretary in any department of the Ministry or holding the post equivalent to the post of Deputy Secretary in any office of the State Government, until he is appointed on deputation and on the date he assumes charge of his office, he shall hold the charge of Deputy Secretary in the Maharashtra State Service Commission, Konkan/ Nashik/ Pune/ Nagpur/ Amravati/ Aurangabad. As per the Government Order dated 20.03.2017, the post of Deputy Secretary and Deputy Secretary in the office of the State Chief Commissioner of Civil Services shall continue to be Head of the Department in case the post is upgraded to the rank of Joint Secretary in future.
These rights shall be limited to financial matters.
