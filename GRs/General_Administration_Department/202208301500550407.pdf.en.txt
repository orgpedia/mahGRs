5. Thematic work as mentioned above Rs. The Government hereby grants administrative approval to the Budget Estimates amounting to Rs. 51,67,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Fifty Crores only) subject to the following conditions:
The Collector will be responsible for ensuring that the construction of these warehouses for permanent storage of EVMs and VVPATs is completed at a minimum cost and quality as per the guidelines of the Election Commission of India.
b) The Election Commission of India (ECI) has issued a notification for the storage of EVMs and VVPAT machines. 08.08.2017, Dy. The construction of these godowns should be done keeping in view the guidelines issued on 12.03.2019 as well as by the Election Commission of India from time to time.
(c) Use of e-tendering system for procurement related matters in the proposal or e-auction of e-auction from Department of Industries, Power and Labour. The GeM portal developed by the Central Government for procurement of goods and services under the Government decision dated 24.08.2017 should be used.
d. The work should be completed as soon as possible with care that no revised administrative approval is given for the thematic work.
Before starting the work, the design map, layout map and detailed map should be started only after getting approval from the architect.
f. While dealing with the provision made in a loose form, the work should be undertaken only after making a detailed budget.
g. While doing actual work, the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1, D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Public Works Department Government Decision No. BDG 2017/ Q. No. 60/ Building-2, D. Action should be taken as per 07/07/2017.
As per the guidelines of the Election Commission of India, the facility of FLC Hall, Guard Room, Counting Hall, separate washroom for men and women, separate tap connection for water supply, CCTV system, fire fighting equipment, electrification, parking arrangements etc. should be provided in the said EVM godown.
(j) the accommodation to be constructed at the said place shall be provided by the officers as may be determined by the Collector along with the staff working in the office of the Chief Electoral Officer and those who have been transferred or retired from the office of the Chief Electoral Officer.
