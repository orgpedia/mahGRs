The Proposal -
As per the notification dated 06.12.2023 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, the strength of the cadre posts in the Indian Administrative Service in the state has been fixed.
Accordingly, 8 cadre posts are sanctioned in Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix. Also, as per the provision in Rule 12 (7) of the Pay Scale Rules, 2016, 8 more Non-Cadre Posts (SDRs) can be filled. The creation of temporary posts in the cadre was under the consideration of the government due to administrative exigencies.
The Government's Decisions:
1) Under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, 03 posts out of 05 temporarily created under Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix are being extended from the date 01.01.2026 to the date 30.04.2026 as per the Government decision dated 26.12.2024.
2) Under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, the Government is approving the creation of 04 new posts in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix tentatively from the date 01.01.2026 to the date 31.12.2026.
