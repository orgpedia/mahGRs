the order
Shri S. A. Kamble, Cell Officer, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department is being appointed on deputation for the first time as" Cell Officer" in the office of the State Commission for the Right to Service, Nagpur Revenue Division under the General Administration Department for a period of one year subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mr. S.A. Kamble, Cell Officer, will commence his deputation on the date on which he enters upon the office of the Deputation, and shall, after one year, assume charge of the office of the Deputation and join his parent Department on the date on which he ceases to hold the office of the Deputation.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of their deputation, if the Government considers their services necessary in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance and the Department of General Administration shall determine the terms and conditions for the deputation of Mr. S.A. Kamble after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II and III) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mr. S.A. Kamble, Cell Officer will be handled by the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department.
4. Mr. S.A. Kamble, Cell Officer to be relieved immediately by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance to accept the appointment on deputation.
