The Government of Maharashtra Secondary Ministerial Services (Lower Level Clerks) Post-Entrance Training Examination Rules, 1977. June 6, 1977.
As per the Government of Maharashtra Secondary Ministerial Services (Lower Level Clerks) Post Entry Training Examination Rules, 1977, a post-service training and examination is conducted every year for the recruitment of clerks and typists in the ministerial cadre. The syllabus for the examination is set out in Annexure A to Rule 10 of the said Rules. However, since 1977, there have been many improvements/ changes in the way the office functions over time.
The use of information technology in government is also increasing. Considering e-office, online Right to Information, our government, etc., it was under the consideration of the government to revise the curriculum of the Clerk-Typist Service Post-Entrance Training Examination. So the government is taking steps in this regard.
The syllabus for the Government Decision Clerk-Typist Services Post-Entrance Training Examination is being decided as follows:

This course will be applicable for the Clerk-Typist Services Post-Entrance Training Examination 2018-19 and subsequent examinations till further orders.
The 100 marks for each of the above papers will be divided as follows.
