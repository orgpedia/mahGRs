Introduction: Directorate General of Information and Public Relations is broadcasting the program Jai Maharashtra on Doordarshan and Dilkhulas on All India Radio for the publicity of government schemes. The Deputy Director, Programmes, Prasar Bharati, Mumbai has informed that the broadcasting tariff of Prasar Bharati, All India Radio and Doordarshan under the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Government of India, has been revised. It was published on March 28, 2019.
Due to revision in tariff, the cost of production and transmission of programs has increased. Therefore, the matter of approving the production and broadcasting expenses of these programs as per the revised rate was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
