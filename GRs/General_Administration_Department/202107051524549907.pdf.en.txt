The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts notified for Persons with Disabilities under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. The Social Justice and Special Assistance Department of the state government has issued a notification in this regard. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions in the said Government Decision dated 3.6.2021, the Additional Deputy Commissioner under the General Administration Department

The posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre in the office of the Chief Secretary and Resident Commissioner, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi have been ensured for the disabled category. Further, in the meeting dated 21.6.2021, the Expert Committee has suggested that the post of" Driver" in Group-C cadre be excluded from the list of persons with disabilities.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, the list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published. Out of this, the decision to exclude the post of" Driver" from the list of persons with disabilities has been taken in the meeting of the Expert Committee dated 21.6.2021.
Accordingly, the post of" Driver" in Group-C cadre in the office of the Additional Chief Secretary and Resident Commissioner, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi under the General Administration Department is being excluded from the reservation of posts for the disabled.
