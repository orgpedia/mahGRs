The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 21.10.2022, a revised figure of 135 posts in the office of Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal has been approved. The government's policy is to do these works through external agencies without creating new posts wherever possible in order to ensure adequate funds for development works while keeping government expenditure in check. The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal. To work according to the orders of the officers and officials. As there is an urgent need for constables at the residence of the office bearers, a proposal was under consideration of the government to provide the services of 4 unskilled posts of constables to carry out the work of the post of constable of Group-D in the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal through an external agency.
Government's decision:
In view of the urgency of the office of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai, it is being approved to provide the services of 4 unskilled posts of constables to carry out the work of the post of constable of Group-D in the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal through an external agency subject to the terms and conditions in paragraph 2 below.
(2) Terms and conditions:
(1) While carrying out the work of the post of Group-D peon in the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal through an external agency, care should be taken to strictly follow the guidelines in the Government Decision dated April 27, 2022 of the Finance Department.
(2) The Head of Department/ Head of Office shall be responsible for ensuring that the expenditure incurred is minimal and proportionate.
(3) The rules and procedures laid down by the Government for carrying out works through external agencies (contractor/ contractor/ company/ institution) must be complied with.
