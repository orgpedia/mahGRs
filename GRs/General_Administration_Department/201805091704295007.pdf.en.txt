Introduction: Passenger traffic at the existing airport in Pune is steadily increasing.
Therefore, in addition to the existing airport at Lohegaon, an international airport is planned to be built in Pune district.
Since 2005, efforts are being made to build a new international airport in Pune. Earlier, the government and the Airport Authority of India had inspected Chakan, Rajgurunagar, Khed and Koyegaon in Pune district to select a suitable site. Subsequently, an international airport is proposed to be constructed on public-private partnership basis through Maharashtra Airport Development Company at Rajgurunagar (New Chakan) near the industrial estate of Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation on the north side of Bhama river near Pune-Nashik highway, about 35 km north of Pune city. The administrative approval was given by the government decision of 27 February 2009. Also, after that.
