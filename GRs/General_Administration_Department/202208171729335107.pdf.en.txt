Circular Referred to in Article No. 1 of the Government Circular dated 31st December, 2021, taking the Sadbhavna Diwas pledge on 20th August, 2022 and paying homage to Late Shri. A wreath-laying ceremony was organised to pay tributes to Rajiv Gandhi on his birth anniversary.
However, the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India vide letter No.2 dated 12.08.2022 has decided to take Sadbhavana Diwas pledge on 18 th August, 2022 instead of 20 th August, 2022 and to declare the day as Sadbhavana Diwas. A wreath-laying ceremony will be held to mark the birth anniversary of Rajiv Gandhi. (Attached is the link. )
3. Therefore, the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India may take necessary action in respect of the suggestions given under the letter dated 12.08.2022 and take the Sadbhavana Diwas pledge and extend the same to all the youth of the country. It has been decided to pay tributes to Rajiv Gandhi on his birth anniversary.
In order to take necessary precautions to prevent the spread of coronavirus in the state, the program has been organized in the presence of minimum officers and staff by maintaining proper social distance, wearing a face mask and following the instructions issued from time to time.
