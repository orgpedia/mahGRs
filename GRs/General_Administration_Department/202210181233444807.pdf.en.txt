A copy of the Office Memorandum dated 08.10.2022 No. 42/07/2022-P& PW (D), Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Department of Pension& Pensioners' Welfare, Government of India, New Delhi, forwarded for information and appropriate action in respect of pensioners/ family pensioners of All India Services of Maharashtra State Cadre.
As per the aforesaid Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Government of India, with effect from 01.07.2022, the 4% increase in Dearness Relief rate and other provisions mentioned in the Memorandum will be applicable to the All India Service Pensioners/ Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre.
Accordingly, now All India Service Pensioners/ Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre will be eligible to get the benefit of DA. Dearness Relief@ 38% will be admissible with effect from 01.07.2022.
