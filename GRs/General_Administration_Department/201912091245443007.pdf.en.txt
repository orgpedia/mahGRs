Date: December 9, 2019
Government's decision
General elections to the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly will be held on October 21, 2019. From the date of notification of General Election to the Legislative Assembly on 21st September 2019 till the completion of the election process on 27th October 2019, the Gazetted Officers at the State, District and Tehsil level, who were directly involved in the election work, had to carry out the sensitive, masonry work with utmost diligence and responsibility in this time frame. Keeping in view the workload, scope of work and working days etc. of the officers concerned in these elections, the Government is approving the payment of remuneration/ honorarium to the following State, District and Tehsil level and Gazetted Officers in connection with the election work in the month of October 2019. Provided that such amount of any officer shall not exceed one month's salary and the said remuneration shall be payable to an officer only once.
State Level: 1. Chief Secretary 2. Additional Chief Secretary (Home) 3. Principal Secretary and Chief Electoral Officer 4. Principal Secretary (Special), Home Department 5. Director General, Directorate General of Information and Public Relations 6. Additional Chief Electoral Officer 7. Joint Secretary, Office of the Chief Secretary 8. State Informatics Officer, Maharashtra State 9. Gazetted Officer in the Office of the Chief Electoral Officer 10. Gazetted Officer in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations 11. Gazetted Officer in the National Informatics Centre (NIC) 12. Gazetted Officer in the Office of the Chief Electoral Officer and in the Office of the Chief Electoral Officer.
