The Proposal:
As per the 103rd Constitutional Amendment Bill, the Parliament has provided 10 per cent reservation to economically weaker sections in educational institutions and government jobs. Accordingly, the State Government vide reference No. Under the scheme, 10 per cent seats are reserved for economically weaker sections in government jobs and educational institutions. For the benefit of this reservation, economically weaker sections of the open category have been included, who are not given the benefit of reservation in any backward class.
The 10 per cent reservation allowed for economically weaker sections is not applicable to the Socially and Educationally Backward Class (SEBC) category in the state as the state has provided reservation in admission to educational institutions and appointments in government services.
