The Proposal: -
Read here No. Shri Ravindra Hanumantrao Thackeray has been appointed as the State Information Commissioner vide Government of Maharashtra Notification No. 3 above, dated 4th April, 2025. Accordingly, Shri Ravindra Hanumantrao Thackeray assumed charge as State Information Commissioner, State Information Commission, Amaravati Bench on 08.05.2025 (Retd. have been established). Shri Ravindra Hanumantrao Thackeray, State Information Commissioner is a retired officer. This results in the receipt of the state government's retirement pay. In view of this, the Government of India vide Central Government Notification, dated 24th October, 2019 vide Rule 14 (3) is now taking a decision regarding fixation of pay of Shri Ravindra Hanumantrao Thackeray as State Information Commissioner as under: -
The government's decision:
The following is being done in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019, dated 30th January, 2019 at No.9 here and the Government of India Notification No.2 dated 24th October, 2019: -
Date of fixation of pay: - 08th May, 2025 (ex. P),
I. Monthly Salary: Rs. 2,25,000 - (Definite)
The pension payable to Mr. Ravidra Hanumantrao Thackeray for the post of State Information Commissioner shall be paid to him from the total salary payable to him every month, minus the amount due to him along with the fractional pension.
[V. Dearness Allowance, Leave, King Cashing, Medical Facilities, Accommodation, Leave WATT Exemption, Travel Allowance and Daycare Allowance shall be admissible as per the rules and regulations issued for the post of State Information Commissioner vide notification dated 24th October, 2019 of the Central Government as amended from time to time.
