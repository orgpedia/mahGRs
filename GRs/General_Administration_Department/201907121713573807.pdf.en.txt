The Proposal:
The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the Government Decision, General Administration Department, dated 20.01.2014. Accordingly, the training under the Integrated Scrutiny of Training Programme-4 was conducted from 17.06.2019 to 30.06.2019 at Vasantrao Naik Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur and the Government was considering to disburse funds to Vasantrao Naik Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur during the financial year 2019-20 to meet the expenditure incurred on training and salary for this phase.
Government Decision: 2. Accordingly, under the said Government Decision, for the training programme under Combined Schedules, Vasantrao Naik Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur, has been provided with Accounts Title-A-4, 2070 Other Administrative Services 00-003 Training (00) (11) Assistant Grant (Compulsory) (20700862) for the training programme under Combined Schedules to the training institute, Vanamati, Nagpur, under the head of Accounts Title-A-4, 2070. 40,17,000/- (Rupees Forty Lakhs Seventeen Thousand only) is being approved by the Government for disbursement under the objectives of 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) and 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Salary) subject to the following terms and conditions.
