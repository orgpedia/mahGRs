the order
Mrs. A.K. Khanwilkar, Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department is being appointed on deputation for a period of two years initially to the post of Deputy Secretary in the office of the State Service Rights Commissioner, Pune under the General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mrs. A.K. Khanwilkar, Deputy Secretary shall commence her service as a Deputy Secretary on the date on which she enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall, on the date on which she enters upon her parent department, cease to hold the office of Deputy Secretary two years thereafter.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(a) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months' notice to the Government/ competent authority; and (d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. The terms and conditions of the deputation shall be decided by the General Administration Department in consultation with the General Administration Department, Democracy Day Cell, after taking into consideration the terms and conditions mentioned in Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters relating to the deputation of Mrs. A.K. Khanwilkar, Deputy Secretary, will be handled by the General Administration Department, Democracy Day Cell.
