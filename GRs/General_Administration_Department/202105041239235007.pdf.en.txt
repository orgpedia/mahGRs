Government's decision:
The officers/ staff of the Home Guard Department working in the Government Building Old Secretariat, 2nd Floor, Fort, Mumbai have been shifted to the ground floor, first floor and third floor of the Home Guard Department and shifted to the second floor (west side) of the Old Secretariat Building at 4174.88 sq. The site should be taken over immediately. 1000 square feet of land was allotted to the high-level inquiry committee. A modern court room and office work room should be constructed for the office of the committee in the foot space and a report should be submitted to the government.
