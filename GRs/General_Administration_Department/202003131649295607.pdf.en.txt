Government circular:
Both the Houses of the Maharashtra Legislature, the Legislative Assembly and the Legislative Council, will continue to function on Saturday, March 14, 2020, a public holiday.
All the Ministries/ Departments are hereby informed that in view of the ensuing business of both Houses of the Legislature on Saturday, the 14th March, 2020, the officers/ staff of the concerned Ministries/ Departments are requested to be present. Also, the concerned officers/ staff of the offices under their administrative control shall remain present in the office, for which necessary measures should be taken.
