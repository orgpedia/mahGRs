Preamble: The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the Government Decision General Administration Department, dated 20/01/2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2021-22, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute under the head 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) for the purpose of Assistant Grants (20700862) to the Institute of Accounts Title A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for training programme under Integrated Schedules. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:

A total of Rs 6,79,21, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,55,12, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Twelve Thousand only) has been approved.
For the financial year 2021-22, under the head 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the Institute of A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for Combined Undergraduate Training Programme (20700862), so far Rs. 49,17,403/- (Rupees Nineteen Fifty Lakhs Seventeen Thousand Four Hundred and Three only) has been disbursed to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute.
As per the request of the Vanamati Training Institute at Roll No. 12 herein read above, under the said Government Decision, for the financial year 2021-22, an amount of Rs. 20 lakh has been released to the Institute of Accounts A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for the salary of the month of January, 2022 and February, 2022 under the head 36-Assistant Grant (Salary) for the training programme under Combined Schedules (20700862). 7,59,100/- (Rupees Seven Lakhs Sixty-Nine Thousand Hundred only) is being distributed by the Bureau of Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BIMS) to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute subject to the following terms and conditions.
The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Manual and all the terms and conditions of the State Government before drawing the funds available on the Budget Distribution System from the Treasury and while spending.
b) The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the disbursed subsidy and ensure that the amount is drawn from the treasury by depositing in the treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
(e) submit to the Treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred and after collecting the amount, it should be ascertained whether the amount collected has been recorded under the same head in the account in the Accountant General's Office and the certificate regarding the same should be submitted to the Government within 15 days.
e) The entire responsibility of meeting the expenditure incurred during the financial year 2021-22 will be of the concerned Controlling Officer as well as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
