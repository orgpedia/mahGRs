Government's decision
The Hon'ble Minister of the State of Maharashtra, Shri Dhananjay Panditrao Munde was allotted the Government Bungalow/ Residence at C-6 (Prachitgarh) vide Government Resolution dated 11.07.2023. Instead, the government has decided to allot them government bungalows in Satpura, Malabar Hill and Mumbai.
(2) It shall be mandatory for the Hon'ble Minister to vacate the accommodation allotted to him within a period of fifteen days after demitting office.
