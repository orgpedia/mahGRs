The Proposal:
The vacant room for the office of the Rashtriya Samaj Paksha (Rashtriya Samaj Paksha) in the barracks behind the Yashodhan building in Churchgate, No. 5-451.00 sq. A proposal to allot the land on a temporary basis was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The vacant room in the barracks behind the Yashodhan building located at Churchgate No. 5- A site with an area of 451.00 square feet is being temporarily allocated for the office of the Rashtriya Samaj Party.
The Executive Engineer, Ilakha City (West) Division, Nirman Bhawan Building, 25 Marzban Road, Fort, Mumbai, shall hand over the possession of the said site to the Rashtriya Samaj Party and submit a report to the Government.
