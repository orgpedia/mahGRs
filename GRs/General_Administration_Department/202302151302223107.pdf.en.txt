Preamble: The requisition for 22 posts of Zila Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A was sent to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission under the Government Letter General Administration Department No. Saikvi-2021/ PR No. 87/28, dated 29/09/2021.
Accordingly, the Commission has issued Advertisement No. Out of the 22 vacancies advertised under 259/2021, candidates for 11 vacancies have been recommended on the basis of merit in the letter under reference.
Out of the 11 recommended candidates, Jyoti Motiram Gulve did not appear for document verification. Similarly, it will not be appropriate to appoint them considering that they have not received their medical examination certificate and character verification certificate and will not join. Accordingly, the remaining 10 candidates were under the consideration of the Government to be appointed to the post of District Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A by obtaining the option of Revenue Department as per the notification dated 14/06/2021 of the General Administration Department regarding the Revenue Department Allotment Rules, 2021 for appointment to Group A and Group B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) posts of the Government of Maharashtra by direct service and promotion.
Government Order: Out of the total 11 candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, 10 candidates have been selected for the post of District Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A as per the Seventh Commission Pay Level S-18: Rs. 49,100-1, 55,800 under the definition of the term' Subject to the Schedules prescribed in Rule 9 (43) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (General Terms and Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981.

The said 10 officers are being given temporary appointment from the date they assume charge of the post subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The appointed candidates shall join within a period of 30 days from the date of such order. Candidates who do not join within this period will lose the seniority given by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and their appointment orders will be cancelled without any prior notice.
(2) If it is not possible to join in exceptional circumstances within the prescribed period of 30 days for the appointment, the concerned candidates should submit the application to the government with supporting reasons immediately. After examining the matter and ensuring that the time for joining is extended, a decision will be taken at the government level to extend the time for the concerned candidate to appear in the appointment post. However, after such extension, the candidate will lose the seniority as admissible to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in order of merit.
3) The candidates are required to bear the travel expenses to accept the appointment.
4) The above candidates are appointed by the General Administration Department, Govt. The new policy will come into effect from June 22, 2021. Their probation period will be two years.
If their work and conduct during the probation period is not satisfactory, their government service will be terminated without any prior notice.
5) The appointment of recommendatory candidates will be subject to verification of the military service record. This service record verification should be done by the Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune. The Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune should ensure that the verification report is satisfactory/ unsatisfactory and submit the same to the Government at the earliest.
6) Before appointment, the authenticity of all the certificates of the above candidates should be checked by the Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune. The candidates recommended by the Commission for the post of Zila Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A, in A.No. 1 to 4 and 10 are selected from the" Open" category and their caste claim is not required to be verified.
7) and No. 5 to No. 9 Candidates are required to verify their caste claim. They should be submitted at the time of appointment. Also, since the candidate at No. 10 has been selected for the post reserved for women in the open category, before their appointment, their certificate of not falling in the advanced and advanced category must be checked by the Director, Women and Child Welfare, Maharashtra State, 3, Church Road, Pune. The Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune should take action in this regard.
8) Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune shall ensure that the aforesaid requirements are met before issuing detailed appointment orders to the said candidates and report the same to the Government.
9) The report of the previous character and conduct verification of the above candidates has been received and it is satisfactory.

12) The above candidates will be required to submit a government approved certificate regarding knowledge of computer handling/ usage as per the provisions of Government Decision General Administration Department, No. 2000/ PR No. 61/2001/39, dated 19th March, 2003 within the prescribed time.
13) If the performance of the above appointed officers is found to be unsatisfactory or their conduct is found to be unbecoming of a government servant, their services shall be terminated without any prior notice.
14) If the above-mentioned candidates want to resign from their service, they will be required to give at least one month's advance notice to the Government as per the provision in Circular General Administration Department No. SRV- 1092/1033/ PR No. 33/92/8 D. 02.12.1997. Similarly, one month's advance notice will be given to the government when the services of these candidates are to be terminated. However, the government will not be obliged to give notice to the above candidates at the time of termination of service for reasons such as ill-discipline, violation of service rules, etc. Any such notice shall be subject to change from time to time.
15) Government Decision Finance Department No. Anio- 1005/126/ Service-4, Dy. 17) The service conditions prescribed for the District Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A Cadre shall be applicable to the said candidates.
18) In addition to the above specified terms and conditions, all civil service rules applicable to Group-A officers of the Government of Maharashtra shall continue to apply to these officers.
