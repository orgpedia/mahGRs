Introduction: In order to enable the final recommendation of all the candidates selected for interview through the State Service Examination on the basis of the medical report obtained from the medical examination and the preference of the posts given by the candidates, the prescribed procedure regarding the medical examination of the candidates shall be mentioned in the Reference No. 4 here. The change has been made as per the Government decision dated 27.06.2023. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has decided to conduct the medical examination of the candidates who have qualified for the interview after the main examination from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission's State Service Main Examination 2022.
Since the Maharashtra Public Service Commission needs some time to set up divisional medical boards at designated places for medical examination of all candidates who have qualified for the interview, and to develop a separate computer system for conducting medical examination of candidates, the procedure of conducting medical examination of candidates selected for interview through the State Service Examination at the interview stage was under consideration of the Government.
