Read the letter of the Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune No. 11025/ MESCO STDC/ DSW- 1/541 dated 12.02.2016.
The Department of Sainik Welfare implements various welfare schemes for ex-servicemen, widows and dependents of ex-servicemen in the state. For this, the documents of ex-servicemen and their families, evidence and other documents are required. These documents have to be submitted to the District Sainik Welfare Office and the Sainik Welfare Department. As per the policies of the e-office system of the state and central government, the Sainik Welfare Department, Pune will computerize the Sainik Welfare Department and all the Zila Sainik Welfare Offices of the state under the e-system. For this, the government was considering computerization of 5 offices in the first phase under the Sainik Welfare Department, Pune.
Government's decision:
Under e-Governance, Digitization and Online Services System of Sainik Welfare Department, Pune and Zila Sainik Welfare Offices,/ Cell under" Mahasanik Mitra," in the first phase, 5 offices will be computerized and for this, the proposed Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded for expenditure of Rs. 88,58,000/- (Rupees Eighty Eight Lakhs Fifty Eight Thousand only).
The expenditure on these items should be met from the sanctioned provision available under the head Demand No. A-5" 2235" Social Security and Welfare 200 Other Programmes, (00) (02) Zila Sainik Welfare Offices (2235-0042) 17 Computer Expenditure Scheme. Budgetary provisions will be made available in a phased manner to operationalize the computer system for the remaining offices.
The Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune is being authorized to draw and disburse the said amount. Also, the information regarding the above headings/ post-plan expenditure, matching of expenditure, and Utilization and Completion Certificate in the prescribed format will be communicated to this Department from time to time as well as to the Finance Department.
