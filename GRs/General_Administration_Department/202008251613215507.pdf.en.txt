No. 308/16-B, D. 21.12.2009 2) Government Resolution, General Administration, No.-BCC-2018/ Q.No. 187/16-B, d. 30.6.2020 3) Government Decision, General Administration, No.-BCC-2019/ Q.No. 210/34-A, d. 4.2.2020 Preamble: Under the Government decision dated 21.12.2019 in the context of the General Administration Department, instructions have been given to create a maximum number of posts for the officers/ employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribe category, but have not been able to submit the caste certificate or other caste validity certificate, and to send a requisition to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to fill the vacancies of the officers/ employees appointed to the post of the said Scheduled Tribe.
Accordingly, the creation of 10 posts of officers/ employees in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations under the General Administration Department has been approved. The approval has been given as per the Government Resolution dated 4.2.2020. Now, the government was considering the creation of a total of 4 supernumerary posts of Deputy Director-1, Supervisor-1, Rapporteur-1 and Teleprinter-1. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government Decision: If the Scheduled Tribe certificate is invalid, if the caste validity certificate of the Special Backward Class or any other backward class is submitted after the invalidation, the caste verified, against the decision of the caste verification committee. However, a petition was filed in the court. Under this order, 4 supernumerary posts are being created on the establishment of the concerned office for re-employment of the officers/ employees whose service has been terminated due to non-interim stay by the court and invalidation of caste validity certificate.
As mentioned above, a total of 4 supernumerary posts are being created at the establishment of the concerned office.
