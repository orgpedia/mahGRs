In pursuance of the decision taken in the meeting of the Cabinet held on 17th November, 2022, the Supplementary Demands for Grants in the Winter Session (December, 2022) amounting to Rs. An additional provision of Rs. 31,10,00, 000/- has been approved. So far, 96% of this provision has been received. Out of this total budgetary provision, Reference No. Under 2 to 7, Freedom Fighters/ their spouses will be entitled to regular monthly pension (Monthly April to December, 2022) and Reference No. Under Section 8, the arrears of pension increment (Monthly November to December, 2022) as well as reference No. Under Section 9, funds to the extent of Monthly Regular Pension (Mah Jan, 2023) to the Freedom Fighters/ their spouses have been disbursed to the respective Collectorates.
Now, the proposal to transfer the required amount of funds for Mahe (February to March, 2023) out of the sanctioned budgetary provision is under consideration of the Government.

NO. 73/2022/ SWASAIC. 1 GOVERNMENT DECISION: For the financial year 2022-23, in the Head of the Head of Accounts 2235, the Pension (Compulsory) to freedom fighters and their dependents, etc., (2235 0015), the following Rs. The budgetary provision of Rs. 110,90,44, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores Ninety Lakhs Forty Four Thousand only) has been approved. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 24,63,80, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Thirty Three Lakh Eighty Thousand only) is being released to the concerned District Collectors to meet the expenditure of Freedom Fighters Pension for the month (February to March, 2023) as shown against the names of their districts in the Statement A attached with the Government Decision.
The amount of subsidy spent from the fund distributed to the District Collector should be reconciled with the Accountant General-1, Mumbai/ Accountant General-2, Nagpur within the prescribed time and submit the monthly report of the expenditure meeting to the government regularly. In this regard, action should be taken as per Government Circular No. Expenditure-Mel 2011/ PR No. 139/ 2011/ SWASAK-1, dated 16/08/2011.
