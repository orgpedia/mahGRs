Preamble- General Administration Department, Government of India. As on 27th October, 2009, 10% Parallel Reservation has been made applicable to the part-time candidates holding Graduation/ Diploma in Government/ Paramilitary Services for direct recruitment to Group-C posts outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission. Also the. The maximum age limit prescribed for direct service recruitment of graduate/ diploma holder part-time candidates in the state, as per the government decision of January 2, 2019
From 46 to 55 years. As per the norms, the candidates should be a graduate/ post graduate.
Government Circular- Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has invited applications for recruitment to the post of Graduate/ Diploma holders.
The Government decision regarding 10% parallel reservation for direct recruitments to Group-C posts outside the ambit and raising the age limit from 46 to 55 years for them should be strictly implemented by all the appointing authorities while making recruitments.
This circular contains instructions to the Government/ Paramilitary Services, Government undertakings, Corporations, Boards,
All government-aided institutions and services to which the government has the power to issue guidelines will be applicable for appointments in these institutions. The administrative departments should bring these suggestions to the notice of the heads of departments under them.
