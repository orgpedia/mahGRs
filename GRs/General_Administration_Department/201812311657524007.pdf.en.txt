Government circular:
Guidelines for grant of Permanent Identity Cards to Government officers/ employees after their retirement have been issued vide Circular No. However, many departments ask the retired officer/ employee about the exact department/ office from which he/ she is supposed to get the identity card. Accordingly, the following steps should be taken.
(a) In general, the department/ office from which the concerned officer/ employee has retired should take action to issue the identity card.
b) If the concerned officer/ employee has retired from the post of deputation, then his parent department/ office should issue an identity card.
e) The officers/ staff working in other departments/ offices like Accounts Officer/ Statistical Officer should be issued identity cards by their parent departments (in this case, Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries/ Directorate of Finance and Statistics).
