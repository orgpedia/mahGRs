The following instructions shall be scrupulously followed by all the concerned departments with a view to effecting the transfers of the above mentioned officers with immediate effect.
(1) The concerned department of the transferred officers shall immediately relieve the officers of that department without waiting for the appearance of the substitute officer.
(2) The officers shall be made to appear before the Department without delay on their arrival to join from other departments as per the order of transfer. The department concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of grant of compulsory waiting period due to non-recruitment of an officer.
(3) If any officer directly or indirectly pressurises any officer to modify the transfer order, such officer shall be liable to disciplinary action.
(4) In view of the need for effective implementation of these transfers, the salary of the transferred officers from the month of July 2023 should not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer department under any circumstances, the salary should be deducted by the post-transfer department after the concerned officer appears in the transfer department and such report should be submitted to the General Administration Department/ Work. 14 to be submitted.
