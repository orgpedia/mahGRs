In view of the need for implementation of the Maharashtra Right to Services Act, 2015, the General Administration Department, vide Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No.137/ Q.12, dated 17.12.2016, has decided the policy of deputation and the procedure to be followed for deputation of officers/ employees of the ministerial cadre. No. 120/14, dated 31.8.2017 has been prescribed by the Government. As per the provisions of this Government Resolution, the period of deputation of Smt. Sawant is being extended from 05-11-2017 for a further period of two years (upto 04-11-2019) subject to the following conditions:
(a) the extension of service of the deputation shall commence from the date on which the period of deputation of the first six months has expired and shall end on the date on which the employee concerned resumes his official charge.
(b) If his services are required by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ Cadre Controller/ Competent Authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
