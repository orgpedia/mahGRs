The government was considering to prepare a list of institutions which have the monopoly of the government/ semi-government/ corporation and those institutions which are not on the list of DAVP of the central government to inform and publicize the policies of the government, various public welfare schemes/ programs, to advertise and publicize through external media to reach the information of various schemes of the government to the public.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
Information about the government's policies and programmes
To disseminate information about various government schemes to the public.
However, the agencies which have the monopoly of government/ semi-government and corporations for advertising and publishing through outdoor media through the Directorate General of Public Relations.
These institutions are not on the DAVP list of the central government. E-tenders were invited from such organizations through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. The tenders were scrutinized at the level of Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. The following institutions and rates are being approved by the government.

The functions of these institutions are as follows:
The media-wise rates have been fixed keeping in mind the 0-15 days duration of the publicity campaign by the external media of the schemes implemented by various departments of the government. If the duration of the campaign is 15-30 days, 5% of the total amount will be deducted from the applied rate. If the duration of the campaign is up to 30-90 days, 10% of the total amount will be deducted from the applied rate. Also, if the duration of the campaign is more than 90 days, 15% of the total amount will be deducted from the applicable rate. (In addition to the applicable rate, GST will be independent.) (2) It shall be obligatory for the institution concerned to submit the certificate of the authority having monopoly of the medium concerned as proof of the transmission of the message.
The organization will be obliged to submit the photo/ log report of the medium through which the advertising message was transmitted along with the payment.
4. the size of the daily frequency delay in the event of a delay in the transmission of advertising messages
The amount will be deducted from the organisation's dues.
The organization shall be fully responsible for ensuring proper dissemination of advertising messages through the media during the campaign period.
6. If the code of conduct is applicable in an area for any reason, the organization will be fully responsible for ensuring that the message is not broadcast in that area.
7. In case of change in the advertisement message to be broadcasted due to any unforeseen reason, it shall be obligatory for the organization to comply with the directions given by the Directorate General in time.
The said list and rates shall remain in force for a period of two years from the date of issue of the said Government Order or until a new list is prepared by the Government, whichever is earlier.
