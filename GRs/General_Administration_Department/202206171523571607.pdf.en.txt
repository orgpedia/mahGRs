As on 20/01/2014, a combined probationary training programme is being implemented for Group A and Group B cadre officers who are directly recruited in Government service. For the training programme under the Integrated Schedules, the training institute of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune has been provided with Reference No. A total of 12 posts have been approved under the government decision. Out of which, only one post of Accounts Officer, Class-I has been filled up from 26/08/2020. Now, in the financial year 2022-23, a total amount of Rs 6,50,000/- was under the consideration of the Government for their salaries from March to June 2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the said Government decision, Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune, has been sanctioned a sum of Rs. 6,50,000/- (Rupees Six Lakh Fifty Thousand only) is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the grants distributed and shall ensure that the payments are drawn from the treasury at par with the actual expenditure.
(b) after depositing the amount in the Treasury equal to the actual expenditure incurred, it shall be ascertained whether the amount so withdrawn has been recorded under the same head in the records of the Accountant General's Office and

The certificate should be submitted to the government within 15 days.
e) The entire responsibility of reconciling the expenditure incurred in the financial year 2022-23 will be of the concerned Controlling Officer as well as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
