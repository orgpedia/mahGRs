General Administration Department/ s. In accordance with the provisions of Government Decision dated 20.4.2021 No. 16-B. A Special Leave Petition No. Subject to the Judgment No. 28306/2017 as well as the following conditions, the concerned Clerk-Typist is being promoted to the open category vacancy in the Short-Typist Cadre on purely temporary basis:
(1) The promotion shall be of temporary nature as an administrative exigency.
2) These promotions are being given only on temporary basis considering the fact that at present, there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of short story writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
3) The promotion is temporary in nature and there will be no permanent retention of the concerned in this post.
The selection of eligible Clerks/ Typists for the year 2022-2023 is provisional and promotion to the post of Short Story Writer is provisional. As a result, the selected clerical typists will not get any rights to the post of regular short story writer and seniority in the service.
In case of non-acceptance of temporary promotion to the post of Short Writer by the concerned Clerk-Typist, his name will be removed from the selection list for the year 2022-2023 as per General Administration Department, Government Decision, No. SRV-2015/ P.S. 303/ K. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and his eligibility will be checked for temporary promotion to the post of Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his name in the next two years selection lists.

(6) The temporary promotion of the said Clerk-Typist to the post of Short Writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental enquiry has been initiated or proposed against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.

8) Mr. N. B. Kamble, Clerk-typist has requested for promotion to the post of Short Writer (Short Writer Cadre). As per the request of Mr. Kamble, he is being promoted to the post of Short Writer under this order. As Mr. Kamble has accepted the cadre of Typist, he will no longer be entitled to promotion from the cadre of Clerk- Typist to the cadre of Assistant Cell Officer.
9) The order will come into force with immediate effect. As per Para (d) (3) of the Government Notification dated 21.6.2021, the post Clerk-Typist should join the department in which he is posted within thirty days from the date of issue of this order. The concerned departments should send the copies of the orders issued by the concerned departments.
