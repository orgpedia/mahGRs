Preamble: The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the Government Decision General Administration Department, dated 20/01/2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2021-22, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute under the head 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Payable) under the head-Assistant Grants (20700862) for the training programme under the consolidated purview of Accounts Title A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2021-22, the Institute of Accounts Heading A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur has been provided with an amount of Rs. 75,91,000/- and 31-Additional Grants (Non-Payment) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,79,21, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,55,12, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Twelve Thousand only) has been approved.
2. Under Reference No. 4, the Vanamati Training Institute has sanctioned a sum of Rs.31-Assistant Grants (Non-salary) to the Institute of Accounts, Titles A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, under the head of Accounting, Fundamental Training under Combined Probationary Training-7 and Assistant Grants (20700862) for the eight-week training programme for the semester I examination of the University of Mumbai. Out of the budgetary provision of Rs. 6,79,21, 000/-, a grant of Rs. 275, 31,000/- (Rupees Two Crore Seventy Five Lakh Thirty One Thousand only) has been sought.

The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Manual and all the terms and conditions of the State Government before drawing the funds available on the Budget Distribution System from the Treasury and while spending.
b) The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the disbursed subsidy and ensure that the amount is drawn from the treasury by depositing in the treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
(e) submit to the Treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred and after collecting the amount, it should be ascertained whether the amount collected has been recorded under the same head in the account in the Accountant General's Office and the certificate regarding the same should be submitted to the Government within 15 days.
