Circular:
On Tuesday, March 20, 2018, the rail traffic on the Central line was disrupted due to a rail roko agitation by students over train recruitment between Matunga-Dadar railway stations on the Central Railway line. Therefore, it was under consideration of the Government to grant special casual leave on Tuesday, 20th March, 2018 to the officers/ staff who were absent due to their residence in Central Railway where majority of the State Government officers/ officials could not attend office.
In this regard, the Government has decided to grant special casual leave on Tuesday, the 20th March, 2018 to the State Government officers/ employees who are residing on the railway tracks and could not attend their offices due to the above mentioned reasons.
