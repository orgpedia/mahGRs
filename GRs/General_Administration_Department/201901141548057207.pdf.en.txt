Preamble As per the notification dated 15.06.2018 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, the revised strength of cadre posts in the Indian Administrative Service in the State has been decided.
Earlier, as per the notification dated 16.02.2015 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, 7 cadre posts were sanctioned in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix. Also, 7 more Non-Cadre posts (SDR) can be filled as per the provision of Rule 12 (7) of the Personnel (Pay) Rules, 2016. The question of creating two more posts in this cadre was under the consideration of the government.
