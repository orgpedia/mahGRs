As per the General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 20.01.2014, Maharashtra Public Service Commission is conducting a combined probationary training program through Vanamati, Nagpur for the posts in Group-B recruited directly every year through the State Service Examination. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has recommended 18 candidates for the post of Cell Officer on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination-2018. Under the CPTP-6 training started with effect from 04.10.2019 vide order No. 4 in the order No. The term of office of the Chairperson shall be for a period of two years from the date of her appointment.
2. Combined Undergraduate Training Programme under reference No. It has been decided to implement the Government Decision 1 to 3 within a period of two years. The training in the Account-wise District Affiliation Training Phase of the Integrated Peripheral Training Programme is to be imparted by the concerned Administrative Department. Accordingly, Account-wise District Engagement Training of 18 Sub-Divisional Cell Officers appointed in CPTP 6 has started from 16.03.2020.
3. Under the Combined Subordinate Training Programme-6, a training programme on Account-wise District Engagement Training to be imparted to Subordinate Cell Officers has been prescribed.
