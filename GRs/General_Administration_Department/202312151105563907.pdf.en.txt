The Proposal:
Shri Sunil Porwal, State Information Commissioner, Brihanmumbai Bench is completing 65 years of age on 15th December, 2023. As per Section 16 (2) of the Right to Information Act, 2005, the post is falling vacant due to the completion of his tenure. Also, Shri.
Sunil Porwal has been given the additional charge of the post of State Chief Information Commissioner and the State Information Commissioner, Brihanmumbai Bench. The government has already taken the decision.
Government's decision:
Shri Sunil Porwal, State Information Commissioner, Brihanmumbai on completion of 65 years of age as on 15th December, 2023. The additional charge of the post of State Information Commissioner, Brihanmumbai, and the vacant post of State Chief Information Commissioner, falling vacant on the expiry of 16.12.2023, shall be vested in Mr. Sameer Sahai, State Information Commissioner, Pune Bench. from p.).
