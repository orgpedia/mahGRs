Date: December 4, 2023.
Read: Circular dated November 17, 2022
Government Circular Notice has been issued vide Government Circular dated 17th November, 2022 that the entire process of writing performance appraisal report of Government officers/ employees should be completed by 31st December. However, the government has pointed out that the performance appraisal reports are not being written on time.
In view of the above, all the Establishment/ Processing Officers may take note of the instructions given in the Notice dated 17.11.2022 and ensure completion of the performance appraisal report for the Report Year 2022-23 within the stipulated time i.e. by 31.12.2023. Also, it will be the responsibility of the concerned establishment/ processing officer to ensure that the entire action to be taken in accordance with the performance appraisal report is done within the prescribed time.
