Preamble: The Union Cabinet in its meeting held on 22nd December, 2023 has approved the establishment of a Pre-Service Institute for Girls at Nashik on the pattern of Sainik Pre-Service Institute, Aurangabad. The above reference No. Under the Government Decision dated 10/01/2023, Military Pre-Service Education Institute for Girls has been established at Nashik. The institute will be a branch of the Aurangabad-based Boys Pre-Service Institute of Military Service and will be controlled by the director of the Aurangabad-based Boys Pre-Service Institute. As per the decision of the Government, the academic session of the Military Pre-Service Institute for Girls will start from June 2023. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to charge fees for security, cushion money, food, etc. from the students on the lines of Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad, and to determine the amount of scholarship stipend to be given by the government.
