The functions of the said Committee shall be as follows: 1. The Committee shall plan the entire site of the Shahu Mill at Kolhapur and prepare various plans for the city and the Shahu Maharaj and take a final decision in connection with the construction of the memorial.
Steps should be taken to build this memorial through the Kolhapur Municipal Corporation.
(3) The Committee shall make recommendations to the Government with regard to the nature, concept and design of the memorial and the budgets and construction of the memorial and matters connected therewith and submit a report therewith within six months from the date of the decision of the Government.
4. To decide on the matters arising in connection with the transfer of land of Shahu Mill under the Textile Corporation under the administrative control of the Cooperation and Textiles Department to the Urban Development Department.
The Urban Development Department should take action to build the memorial and raise funds for it as per the Government Decision S.P.V., dated August 21, 2013.
The term of the Committee shall be one year from the date of its constitution.
The Committee may appoint history experts if it deems necessary.
