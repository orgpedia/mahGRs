Introduction: Directorate General of Information and Public Relations is broadcasting Jai Maharashtra program on Doordarshan and Dilkhulas program on All India Radio.
Prasar Bharati of the Central Government. Due to the increase in the cost of production and transmission of programs due to the revision of rates under the letter of February 15, 2017, the matter of approving the revision of production and transmission costs of these programs was under the consideration of the government. In this regard, the government has taken the following decisions: Government Decision: Jai Maharashtra, a program to publicize government schemes through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, is being started on Doordarshan and Dilkhulas, a program on All India Radio. Prasar Bharati of the Central Government. As per the letter dated 15th February, 2017, the Government has approved the following rates for the production and transmission of programmes.
