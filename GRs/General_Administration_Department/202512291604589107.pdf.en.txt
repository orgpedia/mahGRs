In response to: -
National Men's / Great Men's Birthdays in 2026 and National Day events linked
Celebrations should be held in the ministry and in all government / semi-government offices as per the schedule.
Programmes shown in the Annexure include public holidays, weekly holidays (Saturdays and Sundays) and local holidays.
The days may be approaching and the celebrations may be held as and when the Central Government suggests changes in the programme in this regard.
Otherwise, those events should be celebrated on the same day.
3. Divisional Commissioner / Collector to all Government / Semi-Government in his / her Division / District.
Issue necessary instructions regarding the conduct of the event in the office and their implementation.
Appropriate action should be taken.
Also, the said Government Circular shall apply to all Government / Para-Government offices as well as all colleges in the State.
This will apply to all schools as well.
4. To make the said Government Circular available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharashtra.gov.in.
