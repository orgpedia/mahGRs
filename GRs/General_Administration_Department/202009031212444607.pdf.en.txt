In exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments by the Finance Department, 315 temporary posts of various cadres on the establishment of the General Administration Department (itself) and five other offices under this Department have been created vide order No. Decision dated 3rd March 2020
The deadline has been extended from March 2020 to August 31, 2020.
Now the government is approving the extension of 315 temporary posts on the establishment of the General Administration Department (itself) and five other offices under this department from September 1, 2020 to February 28, 2021.
