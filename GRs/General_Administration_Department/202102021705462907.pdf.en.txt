Preamble Under the Finance Department, Government Resolution, dated 10.09.2001, a policy has been prescribed to review the posts in all government offices under the State Government and fix the revised figure. Accordingly, a proposal to prescribe a revised pattern of posts in Sainik Welfare Department, Maharashtra State, Pune and Subordinate Offices was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
A total of 408 posts, including 283 regular posts in the cadre 1 to 16 and 125 supernumerary posts in the cadre 17 to 21, have been revised. The High Level Committee of Secretaries chaired by the Chief Secretary has approved the same in its meeting dated 03.08.2019. Accordingly, the revised pattern of posts in the Sainik Welfare Department, State of Maharashtra, Pune and the District Sainik Welfare Offices under this office, Boys/ Girls Hostels, Sainik Rest Homes, Pre-Student Training Centre, Nashik, as mentioned in Annexure A, is being approved by this Government.
Further, as per the directions of the High Level Committee of Secretaries, 12 posts (25%) in the peon cadre in the Sainik Welfare Department and Subordinate Offices as indicated in Annexure B are being abolished.
