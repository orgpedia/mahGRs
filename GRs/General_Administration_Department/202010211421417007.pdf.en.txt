Government circular
It has been decided to organize Vigilance Awareness Week in the State every year by the Government Order dated 22nd October, 2001.
The Central Vigilance Commission, New Delhi vide its circular dated 8th September, 2020 has informed that this year Vigilance Awareness Week will be observed from 27th October, 2020 to 2nd November, 2020 with the theme of" Satark Bharat, Samriddh Bharat (Vigilant India, Prosperous India)." It is attached.
It is informed that this year Vigilance Awareness Week will be organized in the state from 27th October, 2020 to 2nd November, 2020.
While organizing the week, Central Vigilance Commission vide No. 08/09/2019 dated The following instructions in the circular 020/ VGL/ 036/ 459673 should be followed.
a) All Organisations are advised to strictly adhere to the extant Covid-19 prevention guidelines at all locations and events issued from time to time by the competent authority.
(b) The week should be organized by all the departments of the State Government and all the heads of departments, head offices under their control, approved enterprises of the State Government, cooperative societies, autonomous institutions.
(c) The programme will begin with the pledge to eradicate corruption at 11.00 am on the working day i.e. 27th October, 2020.
