Mantralaya, Mumbai-32.

Reference: - 1) General Administration Department, Government Decision No. Majam 1001/169 Q. No. 14/01/34 - A, D. 24.07.2001.
2) General Administration Department, Government Decision No. Mausam 2016 / Q. No.142 / Ka.34-A, dated 01.10.2016.
3) General Administration Department, Government Decision No. Mouse 2023/111 Q. No. 16/34 -A, Date 14.02.2023.
The Government Court: -
The post of Director (Information) (Administration) is working in the headquarters of Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, Mumbai. According to the statement "A" accompanying the Government Decision in the said Reference No. 2, the post is assigned the subject "Preparation of Media Framework." However, the matter is now being handed over to the Director (Information), Marathwada Division, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, for further consideration for administrative convenience.
The Director (Information), Marathwada Division, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar shall deal with the aforesaid matters including the functions assigned to his office by order in the said Reference No. 1.
These orders shall come into force on the date of issue.
