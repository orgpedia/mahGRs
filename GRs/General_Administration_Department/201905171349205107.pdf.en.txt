Circular:
In pursuance of the Government Circular dated 26/12/2018 referred to in Article 1, it has been decided to hold a pledge taking event against terrorism and violence on 21st May, 2019.
Further, Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India vide letter dated 01.05.2019 in the Reference Article 2, herewith, has notified the observance of the Anti-Terrorism and Anti-Violence Day on 21.05.2019 by taking the pledge against terrorism and violence (copy enclosed)
However, the Ministry of Home Affairs is requested to take necessary action in the light of the instructions issued by the Government of India vide letter dated 01.05.2019 and to observe the Anti-Terrorism and Anti-Violence Day on Tuesday, the 21st May, 2019 and to hold the' Anti-Terrorism and Anti-Violence Pledge' programme in the forenoon.
