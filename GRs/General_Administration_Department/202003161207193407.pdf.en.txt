Government's decision:
Medical Examination to be conducted by Indian Administrative Service Officers for the year 2019-20. 11.12.2019, Dy. 13.12.2019 and D. Orders have been issued under the Government Decision dated 10.01.2020. These medical examinations must be conducted by 31st March, 2020
In view of the increasing prevalence of Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19), the Office Memorandum of Reference No. 2 specifies the time limit for conducting medical examination for the year 2019-20 by Group A officers of Central Civil Services. It is proposed to extend it up to 30.06.2020.
In view of the increasing outbreak of Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) in Maharashtra, the deadline for conducting medical examinations for IAS officers of Maharashtra cadre aged 40 years and above for the year 2019-20 has been extended. It is being extended up to 30.06.2020.
