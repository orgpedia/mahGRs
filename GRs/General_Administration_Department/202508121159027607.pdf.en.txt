The Proposal: -
Government Decision, General Administration Department, No.1016 / No.140 / 2016 / 10, dated 13th January, 2023 in respect of All India Services (IAS) working in Tribal / Naxal affected areas. P.S., B. P.S. and B. It has been decided to allow the benefit of fixation of one-time promotion pay or one-and-a-half times pay to the officers as per the structure of the Seventh Pay Commission as well as the rules to be amended under the next Pay Commission.
The above benefits are admissible to officers working in Naxal affected areas as per their pay as declared by the Home Department from time to time. The entire Gadchiroli district of the state and Gondia, Saleksa, Deori and Ajuni Morgaon taluks of Gondia district have been declared as revised Naxalite affected areas vide Government Decision, Home Department, No. NAX 1220 / Q. No. 253 / VS1B dated 27th June, 2025.
Circular: -
Government Decision, General Administration Department, No. 1016 / Q. 140 / 2016 / 10, dated 13th January, 2023, the benefit of fixation of one-time promotion pay or one-and-a-half times the salary allowed for the Naxal affected area will now be admissible only for the revised Naxal affected area declared by the Home Department beyond 27th June, 2025.
