Degaokar, Joint Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department, is being appointed on deputation for a period of three years initially to the post of" Managing Director" in Maharashtra Petrochemicals Corporation Limited (MPCL), Navi Mumbai under Industries, Power and Labour Department subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Mr. Sanjay Shah. The service of the Deputy Commissioner shall commence on the date he assumes charge of his office as Joint Secretary and shall end on the date he resumes charge of his office.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(a) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and (d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
