The Proposal:
The coronavirus disease (Covid-19) has been wreaking havoc across the country. In this regard, it is imperative that the World Health Organization (WHO) and the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India take urgent measures to implement the various measures disseminated in the context of the control of the infectious disease (COVID-19) and the outbreak of the corona virus in the state. Accordingly, the Public Health Department has issued a notification to implement Clauses 2, 3 and 4 of the Epidemic Diseases Act in the state of Maharashtra as per Clause 2 of the Epidemic Diseases Act, 1897. It was launched on March 13, 2020. A state-level control room is to be set up in the Mantralaya building to handle health emergencies due to the infectious disease caused by the corona virus (COVID-19) in the state. For this, the matter of providing space to this control room as a temporary arrangement was under the consideration of the government.
