Collector Pune Letter No. Account/ Emergency/ 31/2019/ Pune-1D 28.02.2019 Intro: Emergency was declared in India from 25.6.1975 to 31.3.1977. A policy has been formulated to honour/ suitably honour the persons who have undergone imprisonment during this period by providing financial assistance to them.
The matter of sanctioning honorarium to eligible persons under this policy is under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:-Distribution of funds required to implement the policy of honouring/ due respect to persons who had to undergo imprisonment for fighting for democracy during the Emergency period from 1975 to 1977 is indicated in the statement attached to the Government Decision. This amount is Rs. 42,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Two Crores only) and out of this amount, it is required to be disbursed for Pune district of the State. The Pune district collector has submitted a report to the state government.
4,23,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crore Twenty Three Lakh only) is being disbursed as per the second list of eligible 392 applicants.
