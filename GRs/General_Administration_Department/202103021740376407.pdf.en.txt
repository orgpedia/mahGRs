Introduction: The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations uses various media to inform and publicize various public welfare schemes of the government. 1) Corporate Pullup Standees 2) Stage Backdrop 3) Advertisement Messages Circulating on Sunboard with Vinyl Printing, Mounting 4) Advertisement Messages Circulating on LD Foamsheet with Printing 5) Advertisement Messages Digital Wall Printing 6) Advertisement at their level by DG I& PR for long lasting Asepi Display Boards (with UV Printing) etc.
Government Decision: The Government is giving approval to these institutions and their rates for working through various media for information and publicity of various welfare schemes of the Government through Corporate Pull-up Standees subject to the following conditions. :
In order to make the tender process inclusive and healthy for the information and publicity of the government's public welfare schemes, the tender process should be completed within the prescribed time frame every year in a pre-planned manner so that government expenditure can also be saved.
The Directorate General is of the view that the rates quoted by the Institute in the said tender in June/ July, 2019 are the lowest as on date.
