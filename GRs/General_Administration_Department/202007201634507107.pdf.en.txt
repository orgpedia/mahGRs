Introduction: In the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, social media is increasingly being used along with newspapers and television channels. It has been brought to the notice of the Government that the technical and professional expertise required for this is currently lacking in the Directorate General. The Directorate General has informed the government that the only way out is to work with external agencies. As a result, to work more effectively within the limits of available financial provision, to disseminate information about the people-oriented decisions, initiatives, schemes, government policies, etc. of the state government to the public, and to provide direct contact with the Chief Minister's Secretariat, the government was considering appointing an external organization at the level of the Directorate General for effective media work, such as pictures, videos, animations, etc., using social media platforms such as Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, blog, website, etc.
Government Decisions: To disseminate information about the people-oriented decisions, initiatives, schemes, government policies etc. of the State Government to the public. The people of the state can directly contact the Chief Minister's Secretariat. The announcements made by the Chief Minister as well as the instant messages that will be useful to the people are available on Twitter, Facebook, Blogger, YouTube, Instagram, SoundCloud, Mahasamvad: WhatsApp Bulletin,

The Directorate General for Social Media works at the level of Chief Minister's Secretariat and Directorate General of Information Public Relations to reach out to the public through the media
Government approval for appointment of external agencies is being given subject to the following conditions:
(1) Government Decision No. 2020/ No. 33/ 34 of this Department, d. A panel has been formed to take up the works related to advertisement from private agencies by March 16, 2020. The Directorate General shall select the institution for the above purpose by adopting the tender method from among the institutions in this panel.
2) The Directorate General will be responsible for maintaining the necessary control over the activities of the relevant organization so that there are no defects and errors in the work of publicity through social media.
The agency appointed through the tender process will work according to the procedures set by the Directorate General through the field and office level teams.
For the said purpose, the Directorate General will be responsible to plan the expenditure within the limit of 33% by reviewing the provisions under the program made in the budget for 2020-21, as notified in the Finance Department Government Decision No.Finance-2020/ PR.No.65/ Finance-3 dated 4 May 2020.
