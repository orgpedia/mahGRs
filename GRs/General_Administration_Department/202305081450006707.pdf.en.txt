GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: RASAPE-1423/ PR No.114/ SWASAIC-2 The name of Vithalrao Ramchandra Vore has been found in the register of political prisoners in the Central Prison, Kalaburagi (Gulbarga). Therefore, the divisional level inquiry committee has recommended to the state government to give them freedom fighter benefits.
The name of Shri Vitthalrao Ramchandra Vore has been found in the register of political prisoners in Central Jail, Kalaburagi (Gulbarga). Hence, the government took this decision. Shri Vithalrao Ramchandra Vore is eligible for the freedom fighter pension of the State Government as he meets the basic criteria as on 04.07.1995. Therefore, the matter of sanctioning freedom fighter pension by the state government was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
A departmental inquiry committee. On 10.08.2021, a meeting was held under the chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar and Committee Chairman. According to the minutes of the meeting, the committee has recommended to the government to provide freedom fighter benefits to Vitthalrao Ramchandra Bhore.
2. Accordingly, Shri Vithalrao Ramchandra Vore, Rt. Ambalaga, Ta. Nilanga, G. Latur, Govt. Subject to the provisions of 30.07.2016, the Swatantrata Sainik Pension of the State Government is being sanctioned with effect from the date of this Government decision.
3. Expenditure for this purpose vide Demand No. A-5, Principal Account Head- 2235, Social Security and Welfare (60) Other Social Security and Welfare Programmes (102) Pension under Social Security Scheme (00) (01) Pension to freedom fighters and their dependents etc.
4.According to the above order, the Collector, Latur should take immediate action and submit a report to the government.
