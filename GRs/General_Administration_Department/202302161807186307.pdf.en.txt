the order
Shri Sanjay Dattatraya Kamlakar, Joint Secretary, Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department is being appointed as Member Secretary, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Maharashtra State Commission for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes, Mumbai on deputation for a period of two years initially subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Mr. Sanjay Dattatraya Kamlakar, Joint Secretary shall commence the service of the Deputy Secretary on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his original post, cease to hold the office of Deputy Secretary after a period of two years from the date on which he enters upon his present office.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(a) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and (d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. The terms and conditions of the deputation shall be decided by the Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textiles and the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance in consultation with each other after taking into consideration the terms and conditions mentioned in Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters relating to the deputation of Shri Sanjay Dattatraya Kamalakar, Joint Secretary will be handled by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
4. Sanjay Dattatraya Kamlakar, Joint Secretary, Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles Department to be relieved of his duties immediately to join as Member Secretary, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Maharashtra State Commission for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes, Mumbai. The Maharashtra State Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission, Mumbai should immediately appoint Mr. Sanjay Dattatraya Kamalakar, Joint Secretary.
