Aurangabad Airport Expansion Project at 14.14 am at Murtizapur in the notified area of New Aurangabad Project. The total area of R and Mauje Mukundwadi is 45.03 ha comprising 30.89 ha. R. Includes the acquired land. Of this, 30.89 ha of land in Mauje Mukundwadi has been acquired by CIDCO. 40,00,000/- has been paid by CIDCO. However, the landholders in these areas have been demanding an increase in the compensation amount under Section 18 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894. A total of 22 cases were filed in the district court. The District Court has ordered that in 8 cases, Cidco should pay the fine amount and in 14 cases, the fine amount should be paid by the Airport Authority of India. Though the district court had ruled that the Airport Authority should pay the enhanced amount in the remaining 14 cases out of the total 22 cases, the government was responsible for these cases on behalf of the Airport Authority. District Collector, Aurangabad vide letter dated 13/04/2011 had requested that 75% of the total amount of Rs. 7,75,65, 984/- be paid by CIDCO. In the above case, in the meeting held on 20th January, 2011 under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra, it has been directed that Cidco should take action to pay compensation for the land acquired by Cidco and the enhanced compensation for land acquisition.
Cidco As per the High Court order, Rs. 7,75,65, 984/- amounting to 75% of Rs. 5,81,74, 489/- has obtained stay orders of the District Court. The Board of Directors of Cidco has decided to pay Rs. 40,00,000/- and in respect of enhanced remuneration in Court.
