The Department of General Administration, Government Decision No: SRV 2011/ PR No.137/ Q. 12, dated 17.12.2016 has laid down the policy for deputation of officers/ staff of the ministerial cadre. Out of the above mentioned employees, Mr. Ltd. Mr. Salunkhe, Assistant Cell Officer is being appointed on deputation for one year in the post of Assistant Cell Officer in the headquarters of the State Information Commission and all other employees are being appointed on deputation to the office and designation of the State Information Commission indicated against their name for a period of two years subject to the following conditions.
(a) the service of the deputation shall commence on the date on which the employee hands over charge of the post in the Government service and shall end on the date on which the employee resumes charge of his Government post.
(b) If his services are required by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ Cadre Controller/ Competent Authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
(a) If his services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send him back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
