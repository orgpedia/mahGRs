the order
The officers who are directly related to the election work in the upcoming assembly elections, or who are working in their own district. The Election Commission of India vide letter dated 16.1.2019 has directed that the officers who have completed 3 years in the same district before 31.10.2019 or who have been in the same district for the last 4 years shall be transferred from the district in which they are working. Therefore, in accordance with Rule 5 of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharging Government Duties Act, 2005, the Government has decided to transfer Group A and Group B officers of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for the period of the Assembly elections.

2. The above officers are being relieved with immediate effect to join the transferred post.
These officers should immediately join the post mentioned in his name without waiting for any other order and submit the necessary report to all the concerned offices and the government without any delay.
