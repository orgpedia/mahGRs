Government's decision:
Medical Examination to be conducted by Indian Administrative Service Officers for the year 2019-20. The orders have been issued in pursuance of the Government Decision dated 11.12.2019. These medical examinations are required to be conducted by 31st March, 2020. In view of the increasing prevalence of Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19), the deadline for conducting these medical examinations has been extended as per the Government decisions in Reference No. 2, in accordance with the instructions given under the Office Memorandum of the Department of Personnel and Training. Instructions have been issued for extension up to 30.06.2020 and thereafter up to 31.12.2020.
However, reference no. In view of the increasing cases of Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19), the All India Services have relaxed the condition of attaching the Medical Test Report to the Performance Appraisal Report (PAR) for the year 2019-20.
Accordingly, for the year 2019-2020, the condition of attaching the Medical Test Report to the Performance Appraisal Report (PAR) by the IAS officers of Maharashtra cadre who are 40 years of age or above is being relaxed.
