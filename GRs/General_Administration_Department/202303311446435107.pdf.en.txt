The Proposal:
Government Decision The Government has decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B who are directly recruited in the Government service as per the General Administration Department, dated 20/01/2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2022-2023, the total amount of Rs. 1,35,84, 200/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Five Lakhs Eighty Four Thousand Two Hundred Only) has been provided to the Institute of Accounts Head A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for the Combined Under-Survey Training Programme under the head Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) for the year 2022-2013.

Government's decision:
For the financial year 2022-23, under the head of Accounts, A-4, 2070-Assistant Grants (Salary) to the Institute of Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for Combined Undergraduate Training Programme (20700862), a sum of Rs. 82,74,000/- and 31 Supplementary Grants (Non-salaried) amounting to Rs. 6,79,21,000/- aggregating to Rs. A budget provision of Rs.7,61,95,000/- (Rupees Seven Crore Sixty Six Lakh Ninety Five Thousand only) has been approved. The Revised Estimates for the year 2022-23 under the head 2070 0862 for 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaried) amounting to Rs. 5,43,37000/- (Rupees Five Crore Thirty Three Lakh Thirty Seven Thousand only) have been approved. Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 5,43,37, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore Thirty Three Lakh Thirty Seven Thousand) has been disbursed to Vanamati, Nagpur. For the remaining expenditure, a sum of Rs. 1,35,84, 200/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Five Lakhs Eighty Four Thousand Two Hundred only) is required by Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute. In view of the above, it is hereby approved to provide a sum of Rs. 1,35,84, 200/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Five Lakh Four Thousand Eighty Four Thousand Two Hundred only) to Vanamati, Nagpur, Training Institute under the head Combined Undergraduate Training Programme 8 (2070 0862) as Auxiliary Grants-in-Aid (Non-Salary) for the year 2022-23, subject to the following terms and conditions:
a) Follow the prescribed procedure for reallocation of the said funds.
(b) The Director shall be responsible for ensuring that the funds are spent and appropriated before 31/03/2023.
The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur will be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Rules of Business, Financial Rules, Budget Rule Book of the State Government and all the terms and conditions before drawing the funds available on the Budget Distribution System from the treasury and while spending.
e) The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the disbursed subsidy and ensure that the amount is drawn from the treasury by depositing in the treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
After depositing the amount in the treasury, the amount collected should be verified whether the amount is recorded under the same head in the accountant general's office and the certificate should be submitted to the government within 15 days.
The concerned Controlling Officer and Drawing and Disbursing Officer will be fully responsible for meeting the expenditure incurred during the financial year 2022-2023.
