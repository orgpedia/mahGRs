Due to heavy rains in Mumbai and Mumbai suburbs on Tuesday, August 4, 2020, a special casual leave has been approved for this day.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Circular No: WINASU-2020/ P.No.80/ K. 29
Madame Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032.
Date is September 7, 2020
Circular:
On Tuesday, August 4, 2020, due to heavy rains in Mumbai and Mumbai suburbs, flood-like situation, waterlogging on roads in Mumbai and suburbs, closure of rail traffic on all routes, the government was considering granting special casual leave to the officers/ employees of government/ semi-government offices in Mumbai and Mumbai suburbs as they were unable to attend office.
In this regard, the Government has decided that on Tuesday, the 4th of August, 2020, all the officers/ employees of the Government/ Paramilitary offices in Mumbai and Mumbai Suburbs should be granted special casual leave for that day for the above-mentioned reasons.
