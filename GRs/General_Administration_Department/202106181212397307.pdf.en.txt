Preamble: For the financial year 2021-22, in the Head of Accounts, Rs.2235 crore has been allocated under Pension (Compulsory) to Freedom Fighters and their dependents, etc. A budget provision of Rs. 110,25,81, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores Twenty Five Lakhs Eighty One Thousand only) has been approved. Out of this budgetary allocation, so far, in three phases, a total of 25% has been allocated at the rate of Rs. 27,56,45, 250/- has been received. Out of this budget provision, the proposal to hand over the required amount of funds to the concerned District Collector for the month of June, 2021 is under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2021-22, in the Head of the Head of Accounts 2235, Pension (Compulsory) to Freedom Fighters and their dependents, etc., (2235 0015), the following Rs. A budget provision of Rs. 110,25,81, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores Twenty Five Lakhs Eighty One Thousand only) has been approved. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 11,90,40,000/ (Rupees Eleven Crore Ninety Lakh Forty Thousand only) is being released to the respective District Collectors to meet the expenditure for the month of June, 2021 as shown against the names of their districts in the statement A attached with the Government Resolution.
The amount of subsidy spent from the funds distributed to the District Collector should be reconciled with the Accountant General-1, Mumbai/ Accountant General-2, Nagpur within the prescribed time and submit the monthly report of the expenditure to the government on a regular basis. In this case, action should be taken as per Government Circular No. Expenditure-Mel 2011/ PR No. 139/2011/ SWASAC-1, dated 16/08/2011.
