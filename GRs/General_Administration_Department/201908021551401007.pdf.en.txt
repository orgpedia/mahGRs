The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 has been notified by the Government in this regard. Under Section 9 (1) and (2) of the said Act, every Head of Department/ Head of Office/ Head of Department shall delegate the powers to the Subordinate Officer to take the final decision. Also, it has been directed to earmark three levels of officers who will present cases for taking final decision on all matters related to the department/ office. Accordingly, the levels are being determined as shown in the accompanying table regarding the subject matter related to this assignment. However, if there is a direction from higher authorities or if there is a need in certain exceptional cases, it will be suitably modified on a case to case basis.
