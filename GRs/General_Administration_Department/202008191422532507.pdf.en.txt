the order
The services of Mr. Ch. S. Katkade, Cell Officer, Planning Department are being handed over to the office of the Cell Officer, Nashik Bench Office of the Commission under the Maharashtra State Information Commission on deputation for a period of one year subject to the following conditions.
(a) Mr. Katkade shall commence his service as a Deputy on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his parent department, cease to hold the office of Deputy Commissioner one year thereafter.
The Government/ Competent Authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of his deputation, if his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If his services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall be entitled to send him back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and d) he shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that he intends to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. (e) In accordance with the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981, the General Administration Department, Office No. 6 Further, the Planning Department should decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with each other.
3. All other administrative matters of Mr. Katkade shall be dealt with by the General Administration Department, Office No. 6 will also be handled by the Planning Department.
4. Mr. Katkade should be relieved immediately by the Planning Department.
