Preamble: The call given by the Brihanmumbai State Government Employees Association and the Central Association of State Government Employees, Maharashtra. Absence of Government employees/ officers who participated in the strike from 14th March to 20th March, 2023, from the office is mentioned in Reference No. 2 here. It was regularized as" Extraordinary Leave" vide Government Resolution dated 28th March, 2023. The government was considering amending the order to treat the period of absence as" earned leave" instead of" extraordinary leave."
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has decided to grant" earned leave" in lieu of" extraordinary leave" to the Government employees/ officers who participated in the strike called by the Brihanmumbai State Government Employees Association and the State Government Employees Central Association, Maharashtra from March 14, 2023 to March 20, 2023 in connection with various pending demands of the State Government employees.
