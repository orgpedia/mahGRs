Government's decision:
The Maharashtra government has decided to fill all the posts of Group-C clerical cadre in the state government offices through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. The procedure prescribed under the present Government decision shall be applicable for recruitment to all the posts of clerks. (4) The standard procedure for sending the requisition for the posts of direct service under the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the Government Service to the Commission has been published by the General Administration Department, Government Decision, No: Maloa 1120/ PR No. 153/ K-8, dated December 2, 2021. (4) In sending the requisition as mentioned in point (a) (4) of the Government Order, dated 2nd December, 2021, for posts outside Greater Mumbai, the requisition should be sent in accordance with the statement attached with the Government Order, No. 3 of the requisition. Action taken at the level of Maharashtra Public Service Commission and Ministerial Administrative Department:
Competitive Examination for the post of Clerk-Typist will be conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission as per the requisition received from all the Ministerial Administrative Department.
(2) The candidates will be taken into account in the appointment authority wise preference while submitting the application as per the advertisement.
(3) Based on the final result of the examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the recommended list of candidates will be published according to the preference of the candidates.
(4) The recommendation list of candidates from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission will be sent to the concerned Ministerial Administrative Department according to the appointing authority.
(5) The Maharashtra Public Service Commission should send the recommended list of candidates of the Ministerial Administrative Department (itself) to the General Administration Department/ Office 14-A. On the basis of this list, the General Administration Department/ Office-14-A should make the due allocation of candidates to various administrative departments in the ministry.
(6) In the case where the Maharashtra Public Service Commission is the appointing authority, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission shall make the prescribed allotment of candidates on the establishment of the Commission in accordance with the prescribed list of candidates.
(7) The recommendation list of the candidates received as per the appointment authority as per (b) (4) above to the administrative department of the Ministry shall be forwarded by the concerned departments as per the appointment authority. On the basis of this list, the concerned appointing authority should make the due allocation of candidates as per the prevailing policy.
(8) After the due allotment of candidates, the appointing authority in the concerned office shall proceed to admit the candidates after verifying the documents of the recommended candidates (documents attached with the application and certificates required for verification of the claim in the application).
(2) Even though the posts of clerks under the purview of the Secondary Service Selection Board have been brought under the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the reservation and ancillary facilities for these posts will continue to be applicable as per the reservation applicable earlier.
(3) While sending the requisition to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for recruitment, the vacancies should be calculated as per the orders issued by the Government from time to time regarding compassionate recruitment. The recruitment on compassionate grounds will be out of the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
