Government circular
A provisional Common Seniority List for Senior Personal Assistants in the Ministry Department dated 01.01.2023 has been prepared and annexed to this Circular. The said seniority has been created as per the instructions of General Administration Department, Government Notification dated 21.06.2021.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
3. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring to the notice of all the employees (including those on leave/ deputation) in the Senior Personal Assistant cadre of your Department, the provisional shared seniority of Senior Personal Assistant as on 01.01.2023. In this regard, the objections/ errors received from the concerned senior personal assistants should be examined as per the records of the department and the statements with the opinion of the department should be sent to this department by 06.02.2023.
In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct. This senior A Special Leave Petition No. It is being published under the decision of 28306/2017.
