Read- Government Resolution No. 276/ 2021/ Q. The 28th. December 01, 2021 Government Supplementary Letter: The Central Government has issued a reference letter to submit a report to the Government for the opening of a pre-service military educational institution for girls in Nashik on the lines of the Sainik Pre-Service Education Institute for Boys in Aurangabad. As per the Government decision dated 01.12.2021, a Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Nashik. Military Pre-Service School students have to complete their Class XI and XII from a local college along with the studies they have to do as part of the National Defence Academy entrance examination. Also, sports and games are required. For this, the following three officers are being included in the committee- 1) District Education Officer, Nashik 2) District Sports Officer, Nashik 3) Sports Officer, Municipal Corporation, Nashik
