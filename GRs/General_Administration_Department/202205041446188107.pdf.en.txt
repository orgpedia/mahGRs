The proposal-
The contours of the posts in the Ministry of Food and Civil Supplies were earlier finalised as per the Government decision on Reference No. 1. Now, instructions have been issued by the Government Circular in Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department to review the sanctioned posts in the administrative departments and offices under them and fix the revised figure. Accordingly, the proposal to fix the revised figure after reviewing the sanctioned posts on the establishment of Mantralaya Restaurant, Square Restaurant, Vidhan Bhavan Restaurant and Konkan Bhavan Restaurant in Navi Mumbai Belapur under the General Administration Department. The committee was headed by the Chief Secretary. The High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 7/01/2022 has approved the proposal for the revised figure of taking 229 regular posts and 70 posts of manpower from external agencies by reducing 110 posts out of 339 posts in the Ministry of Food/ Restaurant Establishment and the matter of approving the revised figure approved by the Committee was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the General Administration Department, 229 regular posts as per attached statement A and 70 manpower as per statement B of the establishment of Mantralaya Restaurant, Square Restaurant, Vidhan Bhavan Restaurant and Konkan Bhavan Restaurant in Navi Mumbai Belapur are being approved to be taken by external agencies.

Ministry Out of the total 33 cadres on the establishment of food court/ restaurant, 12 cadres have been consolidated to 3 cadres and 9 cadres have been killed. A total of 24 cadres have been created in the revised figure.
The post of Manager, Mantralaya Restaurant (S 15-41800-132300) has been downgraded to its pay scale (S 14-38600-122800). Therefore, the pay scale of Manager, Mantralaya Restaurant, Manager, Konkan Bhawan Restaurant and Manager, Vidhan Bhawan Restaurant has been made the same (S 14-38600-122800) and the Manager has been made the same cadre.
Clerical and Clerical Typist have been merged into a single cadre of Clerical Typist (S-6 19900-63200) with 4 posts of designation under the same cadre.
The 8 categories of Group-D tea maker, assistant cook, supervisor, waiter, carpenter, porter, sweeper, and boy waiter have been combined into a single multitasking cadre of waiter and assistant cook (S-2, 15300-48700) and 161 posts have been sanctioned for this cadre.
6. Multitasking services of the designation Loader-cum-Cleaner in Group-D cadre within the limit of 70 number of manpower mentioned in Form B are being permitted to be taken up by external agencies as per requirement.
The guidelines of the Government Circulars dated 27/9/2010, 2/2/2013, 2/12/2013, 2/8/2016 of the Finance Department should be followed while taking the services of the posts through external agencies.
8. The details of sanctioned posts in accordance with each restaurant-wise diagram of Mantralaya Restaurant, Square Restaurant, Vidhan Bhavan Restaurant and Konkan Bhavan Restaurant are shown in the return" C."
9. Expenditure on establishment of Mantralaya Restaurant, Square Restaurant, Vidhan Bhavan Restaurant and Konkan Bhavan Restaurant for regular posts and for posts to be paid by external agencies shall be covered under the head of accounts for that restaurant and shall be met from the sanctioned grant for that year.
This Government decision is being issued pursuant to the approval in the minutes of the 1st meeting of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries held on 7/01/2022 and the concurrence received under Informal Reference No. 143/ APUC, dated 22/3/2022 of the Department of Finance.
