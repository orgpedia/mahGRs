Government's decision:
A total of 24 temporary posts have been created in the office of the Regional Divisional Inquiry Officer under the Government decision dated 28th October, 2009 for conducting departmental inquiry of Group A and Group B gazetted officers in the State Service, namely, 6 posts of Regional Divisional Inquiry Officer, 6 posts of Short Writer (Lower Grade), 6 posts of Typist and 6 posts of Constable. These temporary posts are mentioned in Reference No. The extension granted under the order will expire on February 29, 2020. Therefore, the proposal to extend the tenure of these 24 temporary posts was under the consideration of the government.
