Preamble: The Government was contemplating to recruit two computer engineers (in one or two sessions each) through external agencies to provide computer related technical support to the computers in the office and rooms of the Sahyadri State Guest House, as well as to make presentations in various meetings held in the guest house, video conferences, etc.
Accordingly, a proposal to create two notional posts of Computer Engineer and appoint them through an external agency was placed before the Gathit Sub-Committee under the Government Resolution dated 9th June, 2017 of the Finance Department. The proposal has been approved in the meeting of the Sub-Committee held on 30th May, 2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the approval given by the Committee at the meeting of the Gathit Sub-Committee held on 30th May, 2019 to approve the creation of new notional posts, two notional posts of Computer Engineers for Sahyadri State Guest House are being created and filled up through external agencies.
These posts will be filled by the contractor appointed for the facility management and manpower supply for the Sahyadri and Nandagiri State Guest House Management through tender process.
The expenditure on this should be shared from the grant sanctioned under the head of the demand No.A-4, 2052 Secretariat General Service (090) Secretariat (00) (02), General Administration Department, Rajshastra, 10 Contractual Service, (2052 0321).
