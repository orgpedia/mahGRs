Preamble: - The revised structure of the posts at the establishment of the General Administration Department (itself) has been approved by the Government vide Government Decision dated 17.01.2023 at Reference 1. There are a total of 11 posts of Telephone Driver in the details of total 803 posts in Annexure "A" of this Government Decision. Out of these, 9 posts of Telephone Driver have been abolished as per Table "D" (posts to be abolished) and the remaining 2 permanent posts of Telephone Driver are sanctioned for posting as per "Annexure B" Drawing and Distributing Officer-wise allocation.
A proposal was submitted to the Department of Finance through the ASA Task Force to take over the 2 posts of Telephone Operators in the General Administration (itself) establishment through external agencies. The Finance Department had allowed the proposal to be submitted to the sub-committee subject to the abolition of 2 sanctioned posts of telephone drivers. In the 7th meeting of the Sub-Committee dated 27.10.2023, it was approved to take the services of 2 telephone drivers from the establishment of the General Administration Department (itself) through external agencies. The Department was considering the abolition of 2 permanent posts of telephone driver at the establishment of the General Administration Department (itself).
The Government's Decisions:
Approval for the establishment of the General Administration Department (itself).
Group C cadre telephone drivers (pay scale S-7 Rs. 21700-69100/-)
2 permanent posts are being abolished.
