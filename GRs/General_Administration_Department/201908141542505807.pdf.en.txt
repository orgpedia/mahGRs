Government circular
The Provisional Additional Provisional Seniority as on 01.01.2019 for Senior Grade Short Writers in the Ministry was issued vide Government Circular dated 25.03.2019. All the Ministries/ Departments were informed to forward the objections/ objections, if any, to the General Administration Department by 30.04.2019.
However, no errors/ objections have been received from the Ministries/ Departments. Therefore, the aforesaid seniority is being made interim and is annexed herewith as Annexure-A to this Circular. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
