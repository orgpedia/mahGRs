Preamble- Provisions/ instructions have been issued by various Government Decisions/ Circulars regarding the allotment of Government Accommodation in Brihanmumbai under the General Administration Department and related matters. It is proposed to formulate a new policy by incorporating these provisions/ suggestions and making some amendments in it and it was under consideration of the government to form a committee to recommend the policy to the government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SHANIVA 1518/ PR No. 461/23-A
GOVERNMENT DECISION:- A Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department to recommend to the Government regarding the policy proposed in respect of allotment of Government Accommodation and ancillary matters in Brihanmumbai under the General Administration Department as follows:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1 | Additional Chief Secretary (Services)                       | Chairman             |
| 2 | Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue)                        | the members          |
| 3 | Secretary (Buildings), Public Works Department              | the members          |
| 4 | Joint Secretary (KA-23-A) General Administration Department | The Member Secretary |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The committee will have to submit the draft policy along with its recommendation to the government for approval within 15 days.
