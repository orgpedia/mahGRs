Reference No. As per the Government Order dated 28-08-2017, the services of Shri S.S. Shinde, Assistant Cell Officer, Public Works Department, were made available to the State Information Commission, Konkan Bench, Navi Mumbai on deputation for a period of 2 years.
Accordingly, Shri Shinde is on deputation to the State Information Commission, Konkan Bench, since 26-09-2017. After expiry of 2 years period of his deputation dated 25-09-2019, he has been posted as Superintendent, No. 2 By order dated 17-01-2020, hereby revoking the appointment of Mr. Shinde as Deputy Commissioner for a period of 3rd year and in respect of No. 3 vide order dated 28-10-2020, the period was extended for the 4th year up to 25-09-2021.
2. Reference No. 4 vide letter dated 15-09-2021, the State Information Commission, Konkan Bench, has recommended extension of deputation of Shri Shinde for one year beyond 26-09-2021, citing administrative reasons.
There are a large number of vacancies in the Ministry in the cadre of Assistant Cell Officers. The vacancies in the direct services and limited departmental competition quota in the ministry are unlikely to be filled for some time due to administrative reasons. However, in view of the repeated requests from the Ministry Department to fill the vacancies in that department, it has been decided to withdraw the services of the employees on deputation.
The services of Mr. S.S. Shinde, Assistant Cell Officer are being repatriated from the State Information Commission, Konkan Bench and their services are being made available to their parent department.
Shri S.S. Shinde, Assistant Cell Officer, was appointed as the Chairman of the State Information Commission, Konkan Bench on 15-12-2021 (M.S. being unilaterally released from No. Mr. Shinde should join his parent department immediately. After 15-12-2021, Mr. Shinde should not be paid salary and allowances from the State Information Commission, Konkan Bench.
