Preamble: Reference No. 1 The Government have, by a Government Resolution dated 29.10.2001, announced an e-Governance Policy for complete computerization of Government activities. In Section No. In section 7, the concerned administrative departments have given guidelines regarding the formation of the project implementation committee. Also, reference No. 2 The composition and functions of the Project Implementation Committee have been laid down under the Government Decision dated 21.09.2012. Under this provision, the matter of forming a project implementation committee for the State Chief Service Commissioner's Office/ Service Right Commissioner's Office was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Considering the guidelines/ directions given by the General Administration Department under the Government Decisions dated 29.10.2001 and 21.09.2012, it is being approved to constitute the following Project Implementation Committees for the State Chief Commissioner's Office, Mumbai and all the Commissioner's Offices.
