As per the provisions of Article 320 of the Constitution, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) makes recommendations for the appointment of suitable and qualified candidates to various posts in the state services. The Commission has issued instructions to all the Divisional Commissioners/ Collectors to conduct frisking of the candidates by the police in the security system at the examination centres as a safety measure for the planned conduct and effective control of the examination, considering the possibility of occurrence of cases of malpractices with the help of sophisticated telecommunication devices such as Blue Tooth, Head Phone, Small Camera fitted on watches, Shirt Button, Pens, Rings Spy Camera, Smart Watches, Lenses etc. The circular No. As per the rules 1, 3 and 17, the government was considering appointing police for frisking the candidates for the examinations to be conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the lines of the Union Public Service Commission and giving them honorarium.
Government's decision:
In order to prevent malpractices in the examinations conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC), 3 male and 2 female police personnel will be deployed at each sub-centre on the day of the examination to conduct frisking of the candidates by the police in the security system. The fee is Rs. 200/-.
