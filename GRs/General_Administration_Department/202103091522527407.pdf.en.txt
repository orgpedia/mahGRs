Preamble: As per the Government Resolution, General Administration Department, dated 20.01.2014, a combined probationary training programme is being implemented for various cadres of Group-A and Group-B officers who are directly recruited in Government service. Accordingly, for the financial year 2020-21, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute under the objective of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) under the article Heading A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Subsidiary Grants for Training Programme under Integrated Schedules (20700862) to Vanamati, Nagpur. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2020-21, the Institute of Accounts Heading A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur has been provided with an amount of Rs. 70,94,000/- and 31-Additional Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,86,07, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,57,01, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Seven Lakhs One Thousand only) has been approved.
For the financial year 2020-21, under the head' Subsidiary Grants (Salary) to other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for the period April, 2020 to January, 2021, a total grant of Rs. 60,29,900/- (Rupees Sixty Lakhs Thirty-nine Thousand Nine Hundred only) has been disbursed to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur.
Now, for the financial year 2020-21, under the head' Subsidiary Grants for Training Programme under Consolidated Schedules (20700862)' to the Institute of Accountants A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, Rs. 8,51,280/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Fifty One Thousand Two Hundred Eight only) is being distributed by the Bureau of Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BIMS) to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The Director, Vanamati, Nagpur shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the grant disbursed and shall ensure that the amount is drawn from the Treasury by depositing in the Treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
(b) submit to the Government within 15 days the amount collected after depositing in the Treasury the amount equal to the actual expenditure and ensure that the amount collected is recorded under the same head in the records of the Accountant General's Office.
