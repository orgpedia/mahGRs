The Proposal:
" On the various demands of freedom fighters and their families. The Chief Minister presided over the function. The meeting was held on 29th September, 2022. As per the decision taken in the meeting, the issue of issuing an identity card to a person nominated by the freedom fighters of the state was under the consideration of the government.
Government circular:
The Chief Minister presided over the function. As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 29.9.2022, only one heir (son or daughter) nominated by the freedom fighter or if there is no heir, only one child nominated by the freedom fighter is being approved to issue identity cards from the respective Collectorates subject to the following provisions:
Only one heir (son or daughter) nominated by the freedom fighter/ his spouse for the identity card or if there is no heir, only one child nominated by the freedom fighter for the identity card should be issued the identity card by the concerned Collectorate after getting their police verification done.
The use of this identity card by the holder of the identity card will be allowed only for entry into government offices and ministries. This card cannot be used elsewhere.
This ID card will be considered for entry into the Mantralaya for security purposes and one-day Mantralaya Admit Card will be issued to such ID card holders for entry after 02.00 PM.
While issuing such identity cards through the Collector's offices, the photo of the concerned identity card holder should have the seal of the concerned Collector's office and the signature of the Collector on the identity card and the signature of the identity card holder.
If the misuse of this ID is found, action should be taken against the concerned ID card holder as per the prevailing procedure. Also, the identity cards issued should not be confiscated and distributed to the concerned in future.
This ID will be non-transferable.
The heirs/ children nominated by the freedom fighters/ spouse for the identity card should provide their recent photograph for the identity card to the concerned Collectorate.
