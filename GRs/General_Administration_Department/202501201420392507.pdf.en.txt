The Government's Decision
The decision to allot the Government accommodation "Avanti-5," Malabar Hill, Mumbai to Shri Pratap Indirabai Baburao Sarnaik, Hon'ble Minister was taken as per the relevant Government decision.
It is now being cancelled.
Shri Pratap Indirabai Baburao Sarnaik, Hon'ble Minister has been allotted Government Residence Suruchi-18 at Churchgate instead of Government Residence Avanti-5, Malabar Hill, Mumbai.
The government has taken a decision.
Fifteen Residences allotted to the Hon'ble Minister after he demits office.
It will be mandatory to vacate within a period of days.
The Government decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
