Mrs. K. A. Joshi, Assistant Cell Officer, working in the office of the State Information Commission, Bench Nagpur on deputation for a period of 4 years, has been posted as Officer on Reference No. The last extension of one year was granted for the 5th year (from 27-08-2019 to 26-08-2020) vide order dated 19-09-2019. The order Clause 6 states that the extension of Ms. Joshi's deputation is final and she shall not be granted any further extension. Also, the proposal to extend them should not be submitted again by the State Information Commission, Nagpur Bench to this department. Smt. Joshi's term of deputation for the 5th year has ended on 26-08, 2020. However, Smt. Joshi has not joined her parent department (General Administration Department) on 27-08-2020 or till date.
2. General Administration Department, Government Decision No: SRV 2011/ PR No.137/ Q. 12, dated 17.12.2016, the policy of deputation has been fixed and this Government Decision has been approved by Sh. N.S.R.V. 2016/ p. No.510/ Work-12, dated 16-02-2018. In the Government Resolution of 17-12-2016. 5 has been amended as follows: (a) Par. 5 (a) (3) is amended as follows: 5 (a) (3):- The deputation should be given for 3 years in the first instance and the period should be extended for the fourth year with the willingness of the liaison officer and with the consent of the cadre control department. But after the completion of the four-year deputation period, if the extension is to be given for another year i.e. for the 5th year.
The Chief Minister's approval should be sought. No extension will be allowed after the expiry of 5 years.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order in para 2 above, the services of Mrs. K. A. Joshi, Assistant Cell Officer on deputation to the State Information Commission, Nagpur Bench, are being terminated and her services are being restored to her parent department. Smt. Joshi is being relieved unilaterally with effect from 15-10-2020 (M. No. Mrs. Joshi should join her parent department (General Administration Department) immediately after repatriation.
