Government Memorandum: Funds are provided under the programme head 3053-0164, 31-Support Grants for the development of airports in the State. For the year 2022-23, Rs. A sum of Rs. 251.07 crore has been allocated for the project. So far, in the financial year 2022-23, under this head, Maharashtra Airport Development Company has received Rs. 150,64,50, 000 has been provided.
Development of Kolhapur Airport. As per the Government Decision dated 5 th May, 2022, Rs. Revised Administrative and Financial Sanction of Rs. 212,25,25, 660/- has been accorded. Reference No. 4 for the acquisition of land for the airport at Kolhapur in the financial year 2022-23. 52,72,57, 500/- has been disbursed. Also Reference No. 7 crore for the acquisition of land for the airport at Kolhapur in the financial year 2022-23. 36,91,92, 500/- has been disbursed.
Development of Ratnagiri Airport. As per Government Decision dated 04.10.2022, Rs. Revised administrative and financial sanction of Rs. 97.44 crore has been accorded. Reference No. 6 for the acquisition of land for the airport at Ratnagiri in the financial year 2022-23. 35,15,05, 000/- has been disbursed. Also Reference No. 7 for the acquisition of land for the airport at Ratnagiri in the financial year 2022-23. 25,84,95, 000/- has been disbursed.
In the financial year 2022-23, the establishment cost of Kolhapur Airport-Maharashtra Airport Development Company within the revised estimate limit under head 3053 0164 is Rs.8, 16,35,602/-, Kolhapur Airport Land Acquisition Cost is Rs. 8, 20,39,398/-, Ratnagiri Airport- Establishment cost of Maharashtra Airport Development Company- Rs. 3,74,00, 000/-, Amravati Airport-Terminal building construction cost Rs. A total of Rs 5,00,00, 000. The Planning Department and the Finance Department have approved the disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 25,10,75, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crore Ten Lakh Seventy Five Thousand Only). Accordingly, Rs. 25,10,75, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores Ten Lakh Seventy Five Thousand only) is being disbursed on Budget Distribution System for making available funds to Maharashtra Airport Development Company.
