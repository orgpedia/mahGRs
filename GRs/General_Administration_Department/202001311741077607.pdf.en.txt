the order
The services of Mr. D. S. Bhagure, Cell Officer, Public Works Department are being handed over to the office of the State Service Commission, Konkan Division under the" Maharashtra State Service Commission" as Cell Officer on deputation for a period of one year subject to the following conditions.
(a) Mr. Bhagure shall commence his service as a Deputy on the date on which he enters upon his office of Deputy Commissioner, and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his original office, cease to hold the office of Deputy Commissioner after one year.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. (e) In accordance with the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981, the General Administration Department, Office No. 18 Further, the Public Works Department (Establishment) shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with each other.
3. All other administrative matters of Mr. Bhagure shall be dealt with by the General Administration Department, Office No. 18 will also be handled by the Public Works Department (Establishment).
4. The Public Works Department should immediately relieve Mr. Bhagure to join the place of deputation.
