The Proposal:
A memorial for Shahu Maharaj will be built at Shahu Mill in Kolhapur. On 11/12/2012 Ma. The decision was taken at a meeting chaired by the Chief Minister. Subject to the decision of the court, in-principle approval has been given for the construction of a grand memorial of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj on the land of Shahu Mill in Kolhapur. Accordingly, a committee was constituted under the chairmanship of the District Guardian Minister of Kolhapur under the decision of the Government dated March 12, 2013, to build a memorial of international standards of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj on the site of Shahu Mill in Kolhapur. The term of the committee has expired and the matter of reconstitution of the committee was under the consideration of the government as the chairman and some members of the committee were no longer in that position.
Government's decision:
The Government is reconstituting the following committee under the chairmanship of Guardian Minister District-Kolhapur for the construction of a grand memorial of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj on the land of Shahu Mill in Kolhapur:

The Committee shall consist of the following members:
(a) The Committee shall plan the entire site of the Shahu Mill at Kolhapur and prepare various plans for the city and the Shahu Maharaj and take a final decision in connection with the construction of the memorial.
(b) The said memorial shall be constructed by the Kolhapur Municipal Corporation.
The Committee shall make recommendations to the Government on the design, design, budget and construction of the memorial and the matters connected therewith and submit a report to the Urban Development Department within six months from the date of the decision of the Government.
a) The land of Shahu Mill is currently under the control of the Textile Corporation and the proposal to pay the entire land to the Corporation should be examined and submitted to the Urban Development Department with recommendations. Further, as per the Government Decision S.P.V., dated 21st August, 2013, the Urban Development Department is required to take action for the construction of the memorial and raising funds for the same.
(d) The term of office of the Committee shall be one year from the date of its constitution.
(e) The Committee may appoint history experts, if it deems necessary.
