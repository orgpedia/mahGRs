Suspension of the use of biometric attendance system for a limited period as a preventive measure against the spread of coronavirus.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Circular No: Time-2020/ PR No. 31/18 (Rs. And why )
Mantralaya, Madame Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: March 16, 2020.
Government circular:
Coronavirus has spread all across the world. This has led to serious health problems and loss of life. Corona virus infection is on the rise across the country.
In view of the above, the use of biometric attendance system in all government offices in the state is being suspended until further orders as a preventive measure to prevent the spread of coronavirus in the offices of the state and to prevent the infection of the officers/ employees of the state. During this period, the attendance of the officers/ employees in the office should be recorded on the attendance card as before. In this regard, the establishment branches of the departments/ offices should take necessary measures.
