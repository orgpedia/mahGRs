Circular dated November 17, 2022 and dated 4.12.2023
Government circular
The circular dated 17 th November, 2022 has instructed to complete the entire process of writing the annual performance appraisal report of the government officers/ employees by 31 st December.
In view of the technical difficulties faced in filing the performance appraisal report in the Mahapar (especially the difficulties faced by the newly inducted officers in the Mahapar) as well as the winter session of the legislature, the due date for completion of the entire process of writing the performance appraisal report is being extended till 15.1.2024. The extension will be final.
Confidential reports for the year 2022-23 in respect of officers involved in the Mahapara will be closed in the Mahapara system on 16th January, 2024. The performance appraisal reports for the year 2022-23 in respect of officers/ staff not included in the Mahapara should be finalized by 15th January, 2024.
It shall be the responsibility of the concerned Establishment Officer/ Processing Officer to ensure that all these actions are taken within the time mentioned above.
