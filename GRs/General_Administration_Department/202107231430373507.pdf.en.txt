The President, Mr. Complaints received online from the Prime Minister, Central Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances Department, New Delhi are forwarded by this office to the concerned department for necessary action. The following instructions have been received from the Central Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances Department regarding the complaints received.
The grievance should be redressed within a maximum period of 45 days from the date of receipt of the complaint on the CPGRAM portal.
In cases where it is not possible to dispose of the court cases/ policy matters/ grievances within the stipulated time (45 days), it will be mandatory for the complainant to give an interim reply with humility.
The complaint under COVID-19 should be disposed of within three days on priority.
The concerned nodal officers of all the departments will be personally responsible to ensure that the above instructions are strictly followed.
