Govt. Decision No.No.1015/ Q.No. 261/2015 (2018)/ SWASAC-1
Government Decision: Emergency was declared in India from 25.6.1975 to 31.3.1977. The policy for honour/ due respect to persons who have undergone imprisonment during this period shall be as follows: (i) Those who have undergone imprisonment for more than one month during the period of imprisonment shall be given a remuneration of Rs.10,000/- per month and Rs.5,000/- per month to the wife/ husband. Whereas, those who have undergone imprisonment for less than one month will be given a monthly remuneration of Rs 5,000, while their wives/ husbands will be given Rs 2,500.
2) The persons who have been imprisoned during the Emergency should submit an affidavit to the Collector's office along with the application. A copy of the application form is attached to Annexure A.
(3) The District Collector concerned shall have the power to sanction honorarium to persons arrested under MISA. The list of persons who have been sanctioned honorarium by the concerned Collector will be submitted to the government. Based on this list, the government will provide funds to the concerned district collector's office.
The applicant must be a resident of Maharashtra.
5) The policy. It will come into effect from January 2, 2018.
