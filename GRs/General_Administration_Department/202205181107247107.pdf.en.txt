Government Circular: A comprehensive policy on preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in the Government service every year. 1 has been prescribed by the government here. Accordingly, the temporary seniority of the cadre of Senior Assistant Director (Information)/ District Information Officer/ Senior Deputy Editor/ Public Relations Officer, Group-A (Junior) in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for the period from 1.1.2018 to 1.1.2020 is referred to as Reference No. 2 was published here. Prashant Satpute, Senior Assistant Director (Information), Shri. The objection in the letter dated 28.9.2020 mentions temporary promotion instead of ad-hoc promotion. The Senior Assistant Director (Information)/ District Information Officer/ Senior Deputy Editor/ Public Relation Officer, Group-A (Junior) Cadre Officers for the period from 1.1.2018 to 1.1.2020 are now being finalized/ provisional and are annexed to this circular.
The aforesaid seniority list is being published subject to the final decision of the Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. 28306/ 2017 filed by the State Government in the Supreme Court, New Delhi.
The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations shall inform all the offices under its control and all the officers of the cadre of Senior Assistant Director (Information)/ District Information Officer/ Senior Deputy Editor/ Public Relations Officer, Group-A (Junior) to make the copies of the said seniority list available on the website of the Government.
