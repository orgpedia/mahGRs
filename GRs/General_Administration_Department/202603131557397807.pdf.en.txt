The government's decision:
The government is approving the empanelment of non-IT, research, management consultants for the government of Maharashtra in the category of companies and firms, NGOs, and academic research institutes. The appointment will be valid for three years from the date of this Government decision.
Any Department of the Government of Maharashtra, its Subordinate Offices, Government / Para-Governmental Organizations / Corporations / Authorities, etc. (hereinafter referred to as the Departments) may, by following the procedure mentioned in these Government Decisions, seek the services of advisors from the Institutions in the Panel.
