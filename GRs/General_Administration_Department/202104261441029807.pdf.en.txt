Government's decision:
The allotment of Government accommodation in Brihanmumbai under the General Administration Department to the officers/ employees working in the Government offices in Brihanmumbai is done through the General Administration Department. Also, the officers of the Indian Police Service working in the establishment of the office of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, are allotted government residences in the building called Nilima, which is under the jurisdiction of the office of the Director General of Police. In addition, officers of the Indian Police Service working in the establishment of the office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, are allotted government residences in Brihanmumbai under the jurisdiction of the Office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai. The Indian Police Service officers working in these establishments submit applications to the respective establishments as well as to the General Administration Department for obtaining government accommodation. There is no coordination in the allotment of government accommodation.
In order to ensure coordination in the allotment of Government accommodation in view of the above, the Government have decided that the Indian Police Service officers working on the establishment of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, will be required to attach a certificate regarding the non-availability of Government accommodation permissible to the said officer under the jurisdiction of the office of the Police Department in Brihanmumbai (Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai) along with the application to the General Administration Department for allotment of Government accommodation.
(3) In every case where the officers of the Indian Police Service working in the establishment of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, are already or will be further submitted applications for obtaining government accommodation under the General Administration Department, the office concerned shall submit to the General Administration Department along with the certificate of non-availability of government accommodation permissible to the officer concerned under the jurisdiction of the Office of the Director General of Police and the Office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, and the list of the officers of the Indian Police Service allotted government accommodation under their jurisdiction.
