The Proposal:
The list of advertising agencies for carrying out publicity and advertising related works for the schemes of various departments of the government is given in the above reference number as per their' A',' B' and' C' categories. It was created by a government decision dated March 16, 2020. According to the said government decision, the list of advertising agencies mentioned in it was given a period of 2 years. The deadline is March 15, 2022.
As a result, the publicity and advertising activities of various government departments are being hampered. Therefore, in order to carry out the functions of publicity and advertising of the schemes of various departments of the government smoothly, it was under the consideration of the government to extend the list of advertising agencies listed under the government decision dated March 16, 2020.
