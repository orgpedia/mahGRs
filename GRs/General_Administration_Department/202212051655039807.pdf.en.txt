Constitution of a Special Advisory Committee to assist the Cabinet Sub-Committee constituted for taking action on the recommendations on the social, educational and economic condition of the Maratha community and other allied matters.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Resolution No: BCC 1112/ Q.No.628/ Q. 16-B (A)
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: December 5, 2022
Read: General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. BCC 1112/ Q.No. 592/16-B (A), Dy. 4th of October 2022
Preamble: The State Backward Classes Commission is to take legal action on the recommendations made in the report submitted by it on the social, educational and economic condition of the Maratha community and other related matters. The cabinet sub-committee has been constituted as per the government decision dated October 4, 2022. The committee 11.10.2022 decided to constitute a Special Advisory Committee to assist the Cabinet Sub-Committee.
Government Decision: A special advisory committee is being formed as follows to assist the cabinet sub-committee formed for the Maratha community reservation and facilities.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1  | Praveen Darekar, Shri.                                                              | a member of the Advisory |
| 2  | Bharat Gogavale, Shri.                                                              | a member of the Advisory |
| 3  | Yogesh Kadam, Shri.                                                                 | a member of the Advisory |
| OC | Narendra Patil, Chairman, Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation | a member of the Advisory |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The committee will have the power to assist the cabinet sub-committee in providing solutions/ facilities in accordance with the demands of the Maratha community.
