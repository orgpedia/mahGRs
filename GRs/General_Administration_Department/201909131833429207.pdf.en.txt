the prelude
A total of 74 category-wise posts were sent to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission with the approval of the General Administration Department/ Backward Classes Cell for the Assistant Cell Officer Limited Departmental Examination 2017. The Maharashtra Public Service Commission vide its letter dated 25.10.2017 had recommended a total of 50 candidates who were available category wise for the ministerial departments from the Assistant Cell Officer Limited Departmental Examination, 2017. There were no candidates in the fray for 24 seats.
In the meantime, Ma. The High Court, Bombay in Writ Petition No. 2797/2015 passed an order dated 04.08.2017 quashing the Government Order dated 25.05.2004 regarding the implementation of reservation at all stages of promotion in Government service. In this regard, Ma. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed on behalf of the Government in the Supreme Court.
Accordingly, out of the 50 candidates who qualified in the Assistant Cell Officer Limited Departmental Examination 2017, 32 candidates were allotted to the open category as per the merit as per the order dated 30.12.2017.
Meanwhile, some of the candidates who appeared for the exam. Considering the decision of the High Court to cancel the reservation in promotion, the limited departmental examination 2017 had sought appointment on merit for 74 posts mentioned in the demand letter. In this regard, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission was informed to send a revised recommendation list for 74 posts according to merit after taking the feedback of the Backward Classes Cell. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission vide its letter dated 03.11.2018 has provided the recommended list of 74 candidates as per merit from the Limited Departmental Examination, 2017. The revised recommendation list will be based on the merit list sent by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.

A total of 42 candidates have been allotted to the post of Assistant Cell Officer as per the order dated 06.12.2018.
Of the 18 candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) in the merit list, 10 have been appointed on the basis of merit. Only eight candidates were not included in the first list. In a similar case, in the case of Sub-Inspector of Police Limited Departmental Examination, 2016, as per the action taken by the Home Department, the number of posts in the post of Assistant Cell Officer Limited Departmental Examination, 2017 was increased to 8 backward class candidates from the original recommended list
A proposal for the appointment had been submitted. On this proposal, the Backward Classes Cell said," It will be justified to appoint 8 backward class candidates by creating more number of posts in the post of Assistant Cell Officer. " He opined. Accordingly, the proposal for the creation of 8 supernumerary posts was submitted to the High Level Committee of Secretaries as per the opinion of the Finance Department. On this proposal, the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the creation of 8 supernumerary posts in the cadre of Assistant Cell Officer in the open category on the condition that 8 posts in the cadre of Assistant Cell Officer are kept vacant to provide posting to 8 candidates who could not be given the post. In pursuance of this approval, the matter of creation of 8 supernumerary posts in the cadre of Assistant Cell Officers was under consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government: A total of 8 supernumerary posts in the pay scale S-14 38600-122800 are being approved to be created in the open category for the temporary appointment of 8 candidates recommended for Backward Classes from the original results of the Limited Departmental Examination-2017 in the Group-B (Non-Gazetted) Cadre of the Ministry.
(2) The number of candidates for whom the said posts have been created shall not exceed the number of posts for which the candidate is working in the post of Assistant Cell Officer. These will continue to function till the final adjudication of the Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court, whichever is earlier.
As per the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries, at the time of creation of these additional posts, 8 posts in the category of Assistant Cell Officers are kept vacant in the open category.
