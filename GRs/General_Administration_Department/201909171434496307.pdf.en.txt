the order
Mrs. Madhuri. The services of the potter, chamber officer, water supply and sanitation department are being handed over to the Deputy Executive Officer in the office of Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, Satara under the Industries, Energy and Labour Department by deputation for a period of one year subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mrs. Kumbhar shall commence her service as a Deputy on the date on which she enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which she enters upon her parent department, complete her term of office by handing over the charge of the Deputy Commissioner one year thereafter.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. The Department of Industries, Energy and Labour (Labour 7) and the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation (PAPU 01) shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with each other, taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mrs. Kumbhar will be handled by the Industries, Energy and Labour Department (Labour 7) and Water Supply and Sanitation Department (Papu-01).
4. The Water Supply and Sanitation Department should relieve Mrs. Kumbhar to join the place of deputation.
