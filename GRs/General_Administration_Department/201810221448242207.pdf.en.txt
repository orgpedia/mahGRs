Read: Letter from the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations No. Documentary 18/ Special Publication 18-19 Proposal K- 7/476 d. 10.09.2018 Press Release: The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations publishes various schemes to make the general public aware of the various schemes and ambitious projects of the Government.
In the financial year 2018-19, the government was considering broadcasting government messages through private television channels and FM radio etc. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government decisions: Government messages are broadcast through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations to make the general public aware of the various schemes and ambitious projects of the government.
In the financial year 2018-19, the government is giving approval to broadcast government messages through TV channels and FM radio etc. Its details are as follows.
