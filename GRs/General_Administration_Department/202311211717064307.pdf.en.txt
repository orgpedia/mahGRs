Preamble: The Government of Maharashtra has approved the establishment of Maharashtra Policy Research Institute, Pune as an apex institution to conduct regular evaluation of policies, plans and programmes in the State, conduct policy research for policy evaluation, evaluate policy options and select the most suitable ones and promote administrative reforms through training.
The Maharashtra Institute of Policy Research will be implemented as a scheme under the scheme.
Similarly, in the initial phase, the work of the Institute will be carried out by the Centre for Policy Research at YASHADA, Pune with an annual outlay of Rs. The sanction for providing funds upto Rs. 5.00 crore to YASHADA-Pune has been accorded vide Government Resolution No. (1) above. Accordingly, as per the Government Circular No. (2), for the financial year 2023-24, vide Demand No: A-4 Main Accounts Head 2070-Other Administrative Services-003 Training- (00) (13) Yash Das Sahayak Grants (Programme) for Maharashtra Policy Research Institute 31, Sahayak Grants (Non-Munerative) 2070 A011 under the head Rs. 50.00 lakh has been provided in the Budget. No expenditure has been incurred under this head so far.
Yashada, Pune vide letter No. 3, under reference, has sanctioned Rs. 35.32, 830/- (Rupees Thirty Five Lakh Thirty Two Thousand Eight Hundred Thirty only) has been submitted for administrative approval and disbursement of funds. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
