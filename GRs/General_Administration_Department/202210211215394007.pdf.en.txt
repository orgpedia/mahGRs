The Proposal:
The revised figure of 126 posts in the office of Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal has been fixed as per the Government decision dated 27.10.2021. The number of posts in the cadre of Drivers, Constables and Personal Assistants in this figure has been revised as per the Government decision dated 21.06.2022. Now, the proposal to revive the 8 posts which were reduced in the revised figure dated 27.10.2021 of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal Office was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The establishment of the office of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai is provided in Table (A) below. The revised figure of total 126 posts as shown in Figure 4 was approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting dated 03.02.2020. The Government decision in this regard has been issued on 27.10.2021. Table No. The High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 29.04.2022 has approved the change in the number of posts in 3 cadres as indicated in 5. The Government decision in this regard was issued on 21.06.2022.
Now, in pursuance of the proposal received from the Office of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal to revive the 8 posts which have been reduced in the revised figure fixed under the Government Decision dated 27.10.2021 of the said Office, the High-Level Committee has decided to re-designate the posts in Table (A) as No. The High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the change in the number of posts indicated in Section 6. In view of the decisions taken earlier by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting dated 03.02.2020 and 29.04.2022 and the changes approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in the same, the office of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai is being sanctioned a total of 121 regular posts and 14 external services as shown in Table (a).
