the order
Mr. G. P. Magdum, Cell Officer, Minority Development Department has been appointed as Cell Officer in the office of Maharashtra State Haj Committee on deputation for a period of one year, as per the order issued in this regard. Mr. Magdum, Cell Officer is working in the office of Maharashtra State Haj Committee on deputation since 15.10.2020.
The services of Mr. Magdum, Cell Officer on deputation to the office of the Maharashtra State Haj Committee are being recalled and Mr. Magdum, Cell Officer is being appointed on deputation to the office of Maulana Azad Minorities Economic Development Corporation, Mumbai as" Manager" for a period of one year subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mr. Magdum shall commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner on the date on which he enters upon his office of Deputy Commissioner, and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his original office, cease to hold the office of Deputy Commissioner one year after the date on which he enters upon his office of Deputy Commissioner.
(b) If his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of his deputation.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. (e) Considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981, the Department of Minority Affairs shall determine the terms and conditions of the deputation.
All other administrative matters of Mr. Magdum will be handled by the Minority Development Department.
