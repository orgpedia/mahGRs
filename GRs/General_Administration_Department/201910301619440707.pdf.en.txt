3) Government Notification, General Administration Department, No: RTS 2016/ PR No. 124 (1)/ 18 (Rs. And why dated July 30, 2016.
Government Resolution: In pursuance of the provisions of clause 1 of section-13 of the Maharashtra Right to Service Act, 2015, the Government vide Notification dated 30th July, 2016 has constituted the Maharashtra State Right to Service Commission for the purposes of this Act. Further, as per Section 13 (2) (a) of the said Act, the offices of the State Chief Commissioner of Right to Services for Mumbai City District and Mumbai Suburban District and as per Section 13 (2) (b), one Commissioner of State Right to Services for each Revenue Division excluding the area of Mumbai City District and Mumbai Suburban District have been constituted. A total of 110 posts have been approved for these offices as shown in the table in Para 1 of the Government Resolution dated 17th June, 2016 and the posts of Drivers (8 posts) and Constables (24 posts) will be filled through external agencies. The issue of office-wise allocation of these posts was under consideration of the government.
The sanctioned posts for a total of six offices of State Chief Commissioner of Right to Service and one for each of the Revenue Divisions are being allocated as under:
