Preamble: General Administration Department, Government Decisions, D. As on 20/01/2014, a combined probationary training programme is being implemented for the majority of the Group A and Group B cadres who are directly recruited in the Government service. For the training programme under the Integrated Schedules, the training institute of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune has been provided with Reference No. A total of 12 posts have been approved under the government decision. At present, only one post of Accounts Officer, Class 1 has been filled up with effect from 26/08/2020. In the financial year 2021-22, for their salary from March to December, 2021, Rs. 13,53,302/- (Rupees Thirteen Lakhs Thirty Five Thousand Three Hundred Two only) has been distributed to Yashoda, Pune. Now for the salary from January, 2022 to March, 2022, Rs. 2,69,002/- is under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the said Government Decision, a sum of Rs. 2,69,002/- (Rupees Two Lakh Seventy Nine Thousand Two only) is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions: I. The Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (Yashada) Pune should keep the expenditure within the limits of the disbursed grant and ensure that the payments are drawn from the treasury at par with the actual expenditure.
ii. the amount so withdrawn after depositing the amount in the treasury equal to the actual expenditure incurred, was recorded in the records of the Accountant General under the same head.
