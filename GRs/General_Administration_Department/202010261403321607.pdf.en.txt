Reference No. The services of Mr. Renge were provided on deputation in the office of Assistant Cell Officer at Belapur, Navi Mumbai. In order to join the post of Deputy Commissioner, No. He was relieved from his duties vide office order dated 04-09-2018.
Accordingly, Shri Renge has been working under Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission, State Mission Management Cell, Navi Mumbai since 05-09-2018 and his 2 years deputation period has ended on 04-09-2020.
2. Reference No. 3 here. vide letter dated 19-08-2020, the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission, State Mission Management Cell, Navi Mumbai has recommended extension of one year of deputation of Mr. Renge to the concerned office stating that his services are required.
3. There are large number of vacancies in the cadre of Assistant Cell Officers in the Ministries. Therefore, without granting any extension to Mr. Renge, his period of deputation has been terminated.
The services of Mr. S. B. Renge, Assistant Cell Officer, Planning Department on deputation are being repatriated to their parent departments from the date of order.
4. Mr. S. B. Renge should immediately join his parent department (Planning Department) and submit a report to this department.
The Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission, State Mission Management Cell, Navi Mumbai should relieve Mr. Renge immediately and no salary and allowances should be paid after 31-10-2020.
6. The administrative departments of the respective Ministries shall inform the Department of the fact that Mr. S. B. Renge, Assistant Cell Officer, has joined the administrative department of their Ministry.
