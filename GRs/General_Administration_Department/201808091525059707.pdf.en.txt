Conversion of the post of Director (Information) (Media Coordination) in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations to the original post of Deputy Director (Information).

Reference (1) Government Resolution, General Administration Department, No. Mavaj-2016/ No. 125/ 34-A, D. 1.10.2016 (2) Government Resolution, General Administration Department, No.Mavaj-2018/ PR No. 124/ KA-34-A, Dy. 30.7.2018
Government Decision: Considering the increasing use of technology and the increasing number of news channels, to establish a control room at the headquarters of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations to properly control the media, and to establish a control room at the headquarters of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. The post of Deputy Director (Information) (Administration) in the Directorate General of Information has been upgraded to Director (Information) (Media Coordination) for a period of two years, subject to the approval of the Government.
In the last two years, the construction of a state-of-the-art studio and a Media Monitoring Centre have been completed in the Ministry. Further, vide Government Order dated 30.07.2018, the Director (Information) (Media Coordination) has been transferred and posted as Director (Information) (Circles and Public Relations). Considering these factors, the vacant post of Director (Information) (Media Coordination) at Headquarters (Pay Scale Rs. 15600-39100 Grade Pay Rs.7600) from the date of this order by downgrading the same to Deputy Director (Information) (Administration) (Pay Scale Rs. 15600
39100 Grade Pay 6600) is being converted to the original post.
This Government Resolution is being issued with the concurrence received under the Informal Reference No. 163/2018/ Service-9, dated 3.8.2018 of the Department of Finance.
