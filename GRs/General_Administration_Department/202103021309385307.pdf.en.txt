Read:- 1. Department of Finance Government Resolution, No. 2018/ No. 113/ A.P.C.D. 11.12.2018 2. Finance Department Government Circular, No. Sankiran-2020/ No.24/ Apuk, Dy. 30.9.2020 Proposal:
The matter of approval to fill up 6 posts on contract basis through external mechanism was under consideration to avoid financial loss to the government due to the closure of vehicles in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations due to lack of drivers. Finance Department No. Under the Government decision dated 11.12.2018, all the administrative departments were empowered to fill up the posts through external agencies. However, since the said Government decision has expired, the Finance Department was requested to approve the filling up of 6 posts through external mechanism on contract basis. The Sub-Committee of the Finance Department has approved the filling up of 6 posts of Drivers through external agencies for a period of 1 year or till the revised pattern is finalised, whichever is less, keeping in view the nature of work in the Directorate General of Transport. Based on this, the following decisions are taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Approval is being given to fill up 6 posts of Drivers Group-C cadre in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations through external agencies for a period of 1 year or till the revised pattern is finalised, whichever is less.
2. In the recruitment of these posts, the Department of Finance. The instructions in the circular dated 30.9.2020 should be followed.
The said Government Resolution is being issued subject to the approval of the Finance Department vide its Informal Circular No. 524 dated 4.2.2021.
