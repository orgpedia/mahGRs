Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015.
Government circular:
As per Section 13 (2) of the Maharashtra Right to Service Act, 2015, the Maharashtra State Right to Service Commission has been constituted with one State Chief Right to Service Commissioner and six State Right to Service Commissioners, one for each revenue division, with provisions regarding their qualifications prescribed in Section 13 (5) and 13 (6) of the Act. Similarly, the provisions regarding their tenure and conditions of service have been made in Article 14 (1).
The procedure for selection of State Chief Service Commissioner and State Service Commissioner is prescribed as follows:
a) Advertisement and Application Details for Selection: Advertisement for the vacant posts of State Chief Service Commissioner and State Service Commissioner should be given by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations in the major newspapers of the state as well as on the government website. In this advertisement, the personal information of the candidate, the period of performance/ work experience and the details of the same will be submitted by the concerned candidates as per Form" A,"" B" and" C" respectively. A writ petition (Civil) No. In line with the Search Committee constituted pursuant to Interim Order No. 436/ 2018, a Scrutiny Committee is being constituted for appointment to the vacant posts of State Chief Service Commissioner and State Service Commissioner.
