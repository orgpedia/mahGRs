The Maharashtra Civil Services (Requirement of Knowledge of Computer Handling/ Usage) Rules, 1999, which prescribe the requirement of knowledge of computer handling/ usage for appointment to Group' A', Group' B' and Group' C' cadre posts in the Government Service, has been amended vide Reference No. This was notified vide Notification dated 25th January 1999. In view of the Government Orders issued from time to time in this regard, the said Rules, vide Reference No. Amended vide Notification dated 28th May, 2018.
