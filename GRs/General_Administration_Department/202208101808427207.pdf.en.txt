Government circular:
The offices of all the administrative departments in the ministry and the new administrative building will be kept clean and tidy. A circular has been issued to implement the summer campaign and cleanliness action programme from April 1 to May 31, 2022. Instructions were given to carry out cleanliness drive in all the Ministries/ Departments during the said action programme.
It was further directed that Joint/ Deputy Secretary of Establishment Branch of each department should be appointed as Nodal Officer for the implementation of the cleanliness drive in all the administrative departments of the Ministry and weekly report of action taken during the said cleanliness drive should be submitted to the General Administration Department in Annexure A along with the office-wise contextual circular.
Now, in order to ensure regular cleanliness drive and proper maintenance of records in all the administrative departments/ offices of the Ministry, the time limit for implementation of the said action plan is being extended till 31st October, 2022. All the administrative departments are being instructed to ensure that all the administrative departments of the Ministry carry out the cleanliness drive, including cleaning of offices and desilting of archives as mentioned in the circular in this regard, and strictly implement the instructions given in this regard from time to time. Also, the report of the action taken in accordance with the cleanliness campaign should be submitted to the General Administration Department. All the administrative departments of the ministry should ensure that the above instructions are followed.
