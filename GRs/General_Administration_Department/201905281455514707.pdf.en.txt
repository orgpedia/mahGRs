3. The above officers are being promoted on temporary basis subject to the following conditions: (a) The said promotion is subject to the approval of the Hon. The Supreme Court of India in its Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017. The final decision will be taken by the Supreme Court.
(b) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the aforesaid officers shall not be entitled to any permanent regularisation and extension of service consequent upon such temporary promotion.
4.Sarvashree Alurkar and Ashtaputre should join their new place of posting immediately without waiting for any other orders. He should immediately take charge of the new post and submit a report to the government without delay. Necessary alternative arrangements should be made by the concerned Controlling Authorities in respect of the posts falling vacant due to the promotion of these officers.
5.Sarvashree Alurkar and Ashtaputre should give the option of salary fixation within 1 month from the date of assuming the post of promotion. In case such option is not given within the prescribed time, their salary will be determined as per Rule No. 1 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981. 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion shall be taken into account.
