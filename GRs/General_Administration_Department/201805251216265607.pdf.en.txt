(1) On receipt of transfer orders, all the departments shall relieve the transferred cell officers by 31st May, 2018 (M.No.) without waiting for the appearance of the alternate cell officers.
(2) The transferred cell officers shall be reinstated without delay after they have been relieved from their respective departments and come to join. If there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of a cell officer, the establishment officer of the concerned department will be held responsible.
(3) If any officer of the cell directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the officer to modify the order of transfer, such officer shall be liable to disciplinary action.
(4) In view of the need for effective implementation of these transfers, the salary of all the transferred Cell Officers as on 31st May, 2018 shall be paid by the Department in which they are presently working.
Salaries due from 01.06.2018 should not be withdrawn from the department before transfer under any circumstances. After the concerned cell officer appears in the department of transfer, the post-transfer department should draw the salary and the report should be submitted to the General Administration Department.
