circular
The State Government Group D (Class IV) Employees Central Federation, Maharashtra has given a notice to the Government that they will be holding a march on November 25, 2021 at Mantralaya, Mumbai regarding various pending demands of the State Government employees.
As per Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979, a government employee is not supposed to participate in any strike/ direction. The State Government Group D (Class IV) Employees Central Federation, Maharashtra has been recognized under Rule No. 29 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Many local Class IV employees' unions across the state are affiliated to the federation. The Maharashtra State Government Employees Union has also called for a strike. Therefore, the possibility of Group B, Group C and Group D employees of all state government offices in the state joining the statewide march cannot be ruled out.
In order to ensure smooth conduct of Government/ Paramilitary activities on the day of March 25, 2021 by the State Government Group D (Class IV) Employees Central Federation, Maharashtra, all the Ministries/ Departments and their subordinate offices are hereby directed to take the following measures: (a) Participation of Government/ Paramilitary officers/ employees in the office march on time shall be treated as misconduct and disciplinary action shall be taken against such employees. A copy of the order should also be pasted on the notice board of every government department/ office.
(b) The officers/ employees should try to convince them not to participate in the march during office hours. Processions, demonstrations and agitations will also be prohibited during office hours.
