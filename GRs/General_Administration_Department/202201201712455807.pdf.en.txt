Introduction: Department of General Administration. As per the Government decision dated 10th June, 2019, the consolidated procedure for premature retirement of Government officers/ employees beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after 30 years of qualifying service has been prescribed. The Government decision provides that the committee to be constituted for reviewing the service of the officers/ employees shall consist of a senior officer of Group-A in addition to the head of the department as the chairman and another senior officer of Group-A in the 1 member department. It also provides that in the Grievance Committee, the Chairman shall be the Head of the Department/ Head of the Office, one member shall be a Group-A officer under the purview of the other Head of Department under the control of the Department of Ministerial Administration and one member shall be the concerned appointing authority and the Member Secretary shall be a Group-A officer in the Office of the Head of the Department. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the government to form a review committee and a representation committee for the office of Government Transport Service, Worli, Mumbai.
Government's decision:
The Committee for Review of Service beyond 50/55 years of age/ 30 years of qualifying service of the officers/ staff of the Government Transport Service, Worli, Mumbai under this Department and the Committee for Consideration of the grievances against the decision of premature retirement after review of service are being constituted as follows:
