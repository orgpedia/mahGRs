the prelude
As per the notification dated 15.06.2018 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, the revised strength of cadre posts in the Indian Administrative Service in the State has been fixed. Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix is approved for 8 cadre posts. Also, 8 more Non-Cadre posts (SDR) can be filled as per the provision of Rule 12 (7) of the Personnel (Pay) Rules, 2016. As per the administrative requirement, the government was considering the creation of 1 more post in the cadre on a temporary basis.
Government Decision Under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, one post in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix is being provisionally sanctioned from 01.05.2020 to 30.06.2020.
