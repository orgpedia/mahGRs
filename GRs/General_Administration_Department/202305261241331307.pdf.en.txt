Mumbai Metro Phase-III (Colaba-Bandra-Seepz) regarding temporary shifting of offices of affected State Government Employees Association and allotment of space to them.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Resolution No.Shakaja-1115/ PR No. 109/2015/22
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date of release: May 26, 2023
In the Mumbai Metro Phase-III (Colaba-Bandra-Seepz) project plan, the office of the State Government Employees Association in the cottages on the affected Free Press Journal line was temporarily shifted to the 3rd floor, Thackersey House, Ballard Estate, Mumbai. As the offices on the third floor have been shifted to other locations, the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation (MMRC) has to pay rent for the vacant space in addition to this office. Therefore, in order to avoid financial loss to the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation (MMRC), the State Government Employees Association's office was proposed to be shifted to the 3rd floor, Shreyas Chamber, near Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus, Mumbai, in an approximate area of 911 square feet.
Government's decision:
The office of the State Government Employees Association is being shifted from 3rd Floor, Thackersey House, Ballard Estate, Mumbai to 3rd Floor, Shreyas Chamber, Near Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus, Mumbai in an approximate area of 911 sq.ft.
The concerned departments/ offices should prepare a plan on how the office structure should be at the new shifted location and make the plan available to the MPV authority and the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation (MMRC).
The authority and MMRC should take necessary action. The authority and MMRC should take necessary action.
