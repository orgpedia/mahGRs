Preamble: The associations of Government servants (non-industrial) are recognized by the Government subject to the provisions of Rule 29, Rule 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 and the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure, Schedule I and Schedule II of the said Rules and the Constitution and Rules of the" New Model Rules." The procedure to be followed for the same has been prescribed vide Circular No.3 dated 18.3.2015.
The Deputy Inspector General of Registration (HQ), Maharashtra State, Pune vide his letter dated 1.02.2017 has submitted to the Government a proposal for grant of Government recognition to the Association of Registration and Stamps Department, Non-Gazetted Officers and Employees Association (Maharashtra State) (hereinafter referred to as" the Association") under the prescribed format. The matter of granting recognition to this organization under the provisions of Rule 29 and 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 was under the consideration of the Government. As a result, the following decisions are made:
Government's decision:
The Association of Government Employees (Non-Industrial), Department of Registration and Stamps, Non-Gazetted Officers and Employees Association (Maharashtra State) is being recognized by the Government subject to the terms and conditions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, Rules No. 29 and 30 and the New Model Rules, Schedule II, Rules No. 1 to 29 and subject to the other terms and conditions prescribed below:
The Association shall be affiliated under the administrative control of the Department of Revenue and Forest, Ministry, Mumbai.
