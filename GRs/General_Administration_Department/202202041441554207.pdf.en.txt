Preamble: Since the State Election Commission, Maharashtra is a constitutional body independent of the State Government, it is necessary that the planning of media plans/ publicity campaigns, preparation and distribution of advertisements etc. should be done at the level of the Commission. For this, it is necessary to exclude the State Election Commission, Maharashtra from the government advertisement distribution system.
The office of the Chief Electoral Officer, State of Maharashtra was excluded from the government advertisement distribution process under the Government No. 2 decision here. Similarly, the State Election Commission, Maharashtra should also be excluded. State Election Commissioner, Maharashtra. Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra. The meeting was held on October 29, 2021. Accordingly, the government was considering the matter of excluding the State Election Commission, Maharashtra from the government advertisement distribution process.
Government Decision: Since the State Election Commission, Maharashtra is an independent constitutional body, the planning and implementation of media plans/ publicity campaigns, as well as the distribution of all types of advertisements, etc. are being excluded from the advertising distribution system of the State Government. All these things can be done at the level of State Election Commission, Maharashtra, but if the State Election Commission, Maharashtra, informs, then all the work related to publicity and publicity (including appointment of advertising agencies, preparation of publicity material, etc.) should be done by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations.
The rates of the State Government will be applicable to the advertisements distributed by the State Election Commission, Maharashtra.
