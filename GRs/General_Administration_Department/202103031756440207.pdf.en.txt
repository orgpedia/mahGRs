Preamble to the Letters dated 10.12.2020: The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations uses various means to disseminate information and publicize various public welfare schemes of the Government.
1) Corporate Pullup Standees 2) Vinyl Printing on Sun Board, Mounting 3) Digital Wall Painting 4) OCP Display Board (with UV Printing) 5) LD Foamsheet Banner 6) Stage Backdrop etc. The matter was under consideration of the government. In this regard, the Government Decision dated 2 nd March, 2021 was issued.
Government Decision: The Government is giving approval to the organization Advay Design, Kolhapur and their rates under the following conditions to work through various media to inform and publicize the various welfare schemes of the government through vinyl printing and mounting on sunboard.
In order to make the tender process inclusive and healthy for the information and publicity of the government's public welfare schemes, the tender process should be completed within the prescribed time frame every year in a pre-planned manner so that government expenditure can also be saved.
The Directorate General is of the view that the rates quoted by the Institute in the said tender in June/ July, 2019 are the lowest as on date.
