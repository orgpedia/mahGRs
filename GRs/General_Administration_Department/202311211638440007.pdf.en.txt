The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 has been passed with effect from 28th April, 2015 to provide all services provided by the State Government to the citizens in a transparent, dynamic and time bound manner. Necessary Rules for implementation of the Act have been issued on 16th November, 2016.
As per Section 3 (1) of the Maharashtra Right to Public Services Act, 2015, it is provided that public authorities shall display the list of public services to be provided by them, the prescribed time limit, the names of the designated officers, the first appellate authority and the second appellate authority, the fee, if any, on the notice board of their office or the portal of the government or department or office, if any. In addition to this information, the public authorities should also display the e-mail address, telephone number and provision of third appeal of the concerned State Right to Service Commissioner on the notice board of the office or portal of the government or department or office, if any.
Similarly, the Commission shall recommend to the Government, in consultation with the Administrative Secretary in charge of the Department, to notify the services provided by the Public Authority which are not yet notified under the Maharashtra Right to Service Act, 2015.
