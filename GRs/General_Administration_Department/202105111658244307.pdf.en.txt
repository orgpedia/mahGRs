Preamble: General Administration Department, Government Decisions, D. As on 20/01/2014, a combined probationary training programme is being conducted for the officers of Group A and Group B cadre who are directly recruited in Government service. For the training programme under the Integrated Schedules, the training institute of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune has been provided with Reference No. A total of 12 posts have been approved under the government decision. Out of which, only one post of Accounts Officer, Class-I has been filled up from 26/08/2020. Now, in the financial year 2021-22, the government was considering to disburse a total of Rs 6,13,208/- for their salary from March to June 2021 and dearness allowance difference and others.
Government Decision Under the said Government Decision, Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune has been sanctioned a sum of Rs. 6,13,208/- (Rupees Six Lakhs Thirteen Thousand Two Hundred and Eight only) is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the grants distributed and shall ensure that the payments are drawn from the treasury at par with the actual expenditure.
(b) submit to the Treasury the amount of the payment equal to the actual expenditure incurred and ensure that the amount so withdrawn is recorded under the same head in the records of the Accountant General's Office and submit the certificate to the Government within 15 days.
e) The entire responsibility of meeting the expenditure incurred during the financial year 2020-21 shall be of the respective Controlling Officer as well as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
e) Distributed by Yashwantrao Chavan Development Administration Prabodhini (YASHADA), Pune
