Read:- Letter from the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations No. About Creating a List/ Why- 7/989 D. 24/12/2018 Preamble: Livestreaming is being used to disseminate information about various schemes of the government to the public as well as to connect with rural, urban, out of state and international public and for training. Social media is changing day by day and more and more people are using it. The matter of preparing a list of empanelment of live streaming agencies was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations had invited e-tenders from advertising agencies having specific experience and professional qualification in the field of advertising through e-tendering to prepare a list of empanelment of live streaming agencies for training and to communicate to the rural, urban, out-of-state and international public about various schemes of the government. The tenders were scrutinized at the level of Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. The following institutions and rates are being approved by the government.
