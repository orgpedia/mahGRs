Date is August 13, 2020.
Selection procedure: The selection of candidates for various posts in the government service is done through a competitive examination. Earlier, the process of filling these posts was being done by reserving the promotion. However, Ma. The High Court. The reservation in promotion has been cancelled vide order dated 4.08.2017.
Therefore, the government was considering the formation of a committee of secretaries to study and recommend to the government on determining the seniority of employees appointed to higher posts through limited departmental competitive examination. Accordingly, the following Committee of Secretaries is being constituted under the Chairmanship of the Additional Chief Secretary (Services).

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The committee will submit its recommendations to the government within two months.
