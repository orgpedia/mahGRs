Preamble: Subject to the provisions of Rule 29, Rule 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979 and the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure, Schedule I and Schedule II of the said Rules and the Constitution and Rules of the" New Model Rules," the provisions of the said Rules shall apply to the Government Employees (Non-Industrial) Associations. Maharashtra Sales Tax Employees' Association (Non-Gazetted) Mumbai was recognized by the Government under the Government Decision No. 1. The Goods and Services Tax (GST) has replaced the sales tax. On this concept, the sales tax department of Maharashtra was named. The name of the Department has been changed to" Department of Goods and Services Tax" with effect from 1.7.2017. Reference No. Under section 2, as per the proposal submitted by the Finance Department, the Government was considering a proposal to change the name of Maharashtra Sales Tax Employees Association (Non-Gazetted) Mumbai Maharashtra Sales Tax Staff Association (Class III) Bombay to Maharashtra Goods and Services Tax Employees Association (Non-Gazetted) Mumbai Maharashtra Goods and Service Tax Staff Association (Nongazetted) Mumbai.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government, the Maharashtra Sales Tax Employees Association (Non-Gazetted), Mumbai, has been renamed as Maharashtra Sales Tax Staff Association (Class III), Bombay. 3 The Government is approving the change to" Maharashtra Goods and Service Tax Employees Association (Non-Gazetted) Mumbai Maharashtra Goods and Service Tax Staff Association (Nongazetted) Mumbai" as per the procedure mentioned in the Government circular here.
