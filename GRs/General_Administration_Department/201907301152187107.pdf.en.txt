The scope of work of the Study Committee is as follows:
a) To know the problems of the ex-servicemen of the state and take measures for them.
(b) To inquire into the applications of ex-servicemen made by the Government with the help of Government machinery.
(c) to examine the applications received from the ex-servicemen and dispose of the same on a case to case basis at the Government level.
The Committee shall meet once in three months.
