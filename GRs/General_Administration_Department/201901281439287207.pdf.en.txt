Preamble: Emergency was declared in India from 25.6.1975 to 31.3.1977. A policy has been formulated to honour/ suitably honour the persons who have undergone imprisonment during this period by providing financial assistance to them.
The matter of sanctioning honorarium to eligible persons under this policy is under consideration.
Government Decision: To disburse the funds required to implement the policy regarding the honour/ due respect to the persons who had to undergo imprisonment for fighting for democracy during the Emergency period from 1975 to 1977.
As indicated in the attached statement, the provision of the scheme entitled 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 102 Pension under Social Security, (00) (05) Financial assistance to persons fighting for democracy during the period of emergency (mandatory) 50 Other expenses (2235 C205) has been approved by the Supplementary Demand. This amount is Rs. 42,00,0, 000/- (Rupees Forty Two Crores only) which is required to be disbursed to 12 districts of the State (Wardha, Sindhudurg, Nashik, Gondia, Nandurbar, Bhandara, Raigad, Latur, Hingoli, Solapur, Jalgaon, Pune).
As per the list of eligible applicants submitted to the Government by the concerned District Collector, Rs. 5,87,40, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores Eighty Seven Lakhs Forty Thousand only).
The amount of subsidy is being distributed as per the amount shown against the names of the districts.
