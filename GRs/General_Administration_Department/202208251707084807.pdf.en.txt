Government circular:
The Right to Information Act, 2005 provides for the protection of information until a final decision is taken. The State Information Commissioner, Brihanmumbai has issued the order under Section 19 (8) (a) (4) of the Right to Information Act. Accordingly, all public authorities are being informed as follows.
(2) Any information sought by an applicant under the Right to Information Act, 2005, shall be preserved securely until the final adjudication thereon.
The provisions of the above circular shall be brought to the notice of the Heads of Departments, Public Authorities and all concerned under them by the Heads of Departments of all Ministries and shall be intimated to take necessary action accordingly.
