Government's decision
As per the provisions of Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 of the Central Government, in accordance with Section 20 of the Act, it is necessary to appoint a Grievance Redressal Officer in every establishment of the Government to resolve the grievances arising out of the disability of the officers and employees working in the government service.
Accordingly, the Administration Officer under the Office of the Ministry Restaurants, Ministry Restaurants is being declared as the Grievance Redressal Officer.
