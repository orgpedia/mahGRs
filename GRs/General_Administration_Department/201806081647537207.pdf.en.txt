Government's decision:
The General Administration Department has decided to develop an e-project Human Resource Management System (HRMS) under which the functioning of 12 major service matters in the government service from the appointment of a government employee to retirement will be done through computerized process. E& Y has been appointed as the consultant for this work and the Detailed Project Report (DPR) and the Tender Report (RFP) submitted by the company have been approved by the High Level Secretary Committee constituted by the Directorate of Information and Technology.
Accordingly, under this project, the tender has been published on the government website by the end of March 2018, according to which the tenderer (System Integrator (SI)) will be selected in the current financial year and the actual computerization work will start. For this, the tenderer is required to computerize 12 basic service components in a phased manner over a period of 2 years and each component is to be computerized in 3 stages. While doing this work, the tenderer has to pay the work fee according to the level-wise work of each component. Therefore, the tenderer is required to report the progress of the work every month and release funds immediately. If these funds are not disbursed in time, the computerization of the next unit will not be carried out on schedule. However, it will not be possible to disburse funds every month by adopting the prevailing system of administration funds distribution.
Considering this, the Project Implementation Committee has decided to appoint Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Training Prabodhini, Pune as the nodal agency to facilitate the process of disbursement of funds under this project. It has been approved in the meeting dated 24/05/2018.
As per the decision of the Government, Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Training Prabodhini, Pune is being appointed as the Nodal Agency under the Human Resource Management System and the Director, Center for Information Technology, Yashada will look after the work on behalf of this organization.

Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Training Prabodhini, Pune will have to perform the following responsibilities as the nodal agency.
The cost of the amount payable to the tenderer (SI) under the project Human Resource Management System (HRMS) will be borne by YASHADA from the head of account of its e-Governance project and the amount will be transferred to YASHADA from the following budgetary provision under the head Demand No. A-4, 2052 Secretariat, General Services (00) (17) Provision for implementation of e-Governance project (2052 5081), 17 Computer Expenditure. This will continue till the completion of the project.
While disbursing funds to YASHADA through the General Administration Department, it will be disbursed as per the prevailing policy of the government as well as the directions given by the Finance Department from time to time.
This decision was taken by the Finance Ministry vide its notification No. 355/2017/ Expenditure-4, Dy. Issued pursuant to approval received on 13/07/2017.
