Government circular
During the period from 1.1.2019 to 31.12.2019, no employee has been promoted to the post of Short Story Writer and also not recruited to the post directly. Hence, the said seniority is being made interim and is annexed herewith as Annexure-A to this Circular. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
