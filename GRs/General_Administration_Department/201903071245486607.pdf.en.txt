Circular: The rules regulating the recruitment of Group-D cadre posts in the administrative departments under the control of the Government of Maharashtra have been prescribed by a notification dated 30th October 1995. Accordingly, the Schedule to the Service Admission Rules for the posts in Group-D cadre prescribed meet the conditions prescribed for promotion to the post of Jamadar/ Chopdar in Group-A. The consolidated provisional seniority of all such Class IV employees up to 1/1/2019 has been notified vide letter dated 16th January, 2019.
All the Ministries/ Departments have been advised to bring the present temporary seniority to the notice of all the concerned Group-D cadre employees of their department and take action to address their objections, if any, and incomplete information.
On the basis of the information received from the Department of the Ministry as above, the last date of January 1, 2019 for promotion to the fixed post of Chopdar/ Jamadar (Part-A) in Group-D Cadre (Part-B and Part-C) of the Ministerial Establishment is being published as per Annexure-A.
4. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring this circular to the notice of all the concerned who are included in the notice of seniority in the Annexure and who are retiring from the department in which they are working and who are on deputation from the establishment of the department.
