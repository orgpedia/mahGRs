Mrs. Pu. Yo. Sable, Senior Short Writer, Revenue and Forest Department is being appointed on deputation to the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Pune for a period of two years with the following terms and conditions.
(a) the service of the deputation shall commence on the date on which the employee hands over charge of the post in the Government service and shall end on the date on which the employee resumes charge of his Government post.
(b) The Government/ Cadre Controller/ Competent Authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(a) If his services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send him back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and (d) he shall be entitled to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that he intends to return to the parent department.
