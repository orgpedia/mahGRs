GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. PUP-2007/ PR No.58/ KA.34 dated 9th June, 2010
The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations publishes Lokrajya Marathi, Lokrajya Urdu, Lokrajya Hindi, Lokrajya Gujarati, Maharashtra Ahead (English) and Apna Mantralaya monthly. This magazine is an effective medium to convey the government's goals and schemes to the people. Since the Government Printing Press is not equipped with the latest machinery and is unable to print large number of magazines in four colours and within the stipulated time frame, the magazines namely Lokrajya Urdu, Lokrajya Hindi, Lokrajya Gujarati, Maharashtra Ahead (English), Apna Mantralaya are being printed by the private printing presses through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations with the consent of the Industries, Energy and Labour Department. The Department of Industrial Energy and Labour, while agreeing to the proposal earlier, had given its opinion to continue the process till the modernization of the Government Press. Accordingly, the Government was considering extending the periodicity of printing of Lokrajya Marathi, Lokrajya Urdu, Lokrajya Hindi, Lokrajya Gujarati, Maharashtra Ahead (English) and Apna Mantralaya magazines by private printing presses through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations from April, 2018 to March, 2019.
Decision of the Government: Since there is no four-color printing system available in the government printing press, the government is approving the printing of Marathi Lokrajya, Urdu Lokrajya, Hindi Lokrajya, Gujarati Lokrajya, Maharashtra Ahead (English), Apna Mantralaya magazines by private printing presses by inviting e-tenders and following the prescribed government procedures for a period of one year from April, 2018 to March, 2019 until the system is updated.
