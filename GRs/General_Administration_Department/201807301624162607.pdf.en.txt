Government Order: As per the decision of the Government, the post of Deputy Director (Information) in the headquarters of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations was upgraded to Director (Information) (Media Coordination) and Mr. Shivaji Mankar was appointed to that post.
The post of Media Coordinator was upgraded for certain posts and periods. Since the work has been completed, a proposal has been submitted to the Finance Department to downgrade the post to Deputy Director (Information).
The post of Director (Information) (News and Public Relations) at Headquarters is lying vacant since June, 2017 and the additional charge of the post has been given to Shri Shivaji Mankar.
Mr. Mankar is presently working in the post which is being downgraded.
Shivaji Mankar has been posted as Director (Information) (News and Public Relations).
