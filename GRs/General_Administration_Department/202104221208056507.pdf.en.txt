Government circular:
1) Refer No. 1 As per rule 2 (d) of the Government Notification here, for promotion to the post of Cell Officer, it is necessary to prepare a common seniority of eligible Assistant Cell Officers.
According to reference no. 3 The Government Circular dated 01.01.2019 has published the Interim Common Seniority of Assistant Cell Officers eligible for promotion to the post of Cell Officer.
2) Refer No. 2 As per the provisions of the Government Order dated 01.01.2020, the Assistant Cell Officers who have been working continuously in the rank of Assistant Cell Officer in the ratio (from the revised date of seniority in respect of the Assistant Cell Officers who have lost their original seniority) for more than 5 years with regular appointment/ promotion and who have passed the departmental examination prescribed for promotion to the post of Cell Officer.
The provisional Additional Seniority List dated 01.01.2020 of eligible Assistant Cell Officers who have done or are exempted from passing the said examination has been prepared and annexed to this Circular.
3) As per the rules of the Assistant Cell Officer Cadre, eligible clerks-typists are promoted to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the promotion quota of 30% of the seats. Accordingly, the process of regular promotion of clerical typists to the post of Assistant Cell Officer is underway.
Procedure for regular promotion of Clerk-Typist to the post of Assistant Cell Officer
As completed, the Assistant Cell Officers in the promoted quota will be included in the seniority list at the appropriate place.

The Assistant Cell Officers from 1st to 28th will be included in their respective places as per the seniority number in the Interim Common Seniority List dated 01.01.2019 and the Assistant Cell Officers from A.No. 29 to 87 will be included after the last seniority number in the seniority list dated 1.1.2019 (viz. 760 onwards). The names of the Auxiliary Cell Officers who have lost their seniority and whose seniority cannot be ascertained at present are shown in a separate table below the seniority list. Once the seniority of the Assistant Cell Officers is ascertained, their names will be included in the seniority list of that year as per their seniority.
5) All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned and keep a signed copy of the same in the archives. If there are any genuine errors or omissions in the said seniority list, the concerned should submit a representation to your department by May 7, 2021, after which the objections received will be considered
They should not be given any notice. Accordingly, the facts of the representation received till May 7, 2021, should be checked by the concerned departments from the service book and other available records and along with the feedback of the department, the General Administration Department should be informed by May 12, 2021, so that it can be verified and necessary changes can be made in the seniority list.
Please note that the seniority list will be finalized assuming that there is no objection from the concerned in the department if no information is received till May 12, 2021.
