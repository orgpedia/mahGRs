The proposal: The Governor, Ma. Photography and videography is done during the official visits of the Chief Minister, Ministers and senior officials to Delhi. Therefore, the office of Maharashtra Parichay Kendra, New Delhi must have its own updated camera. However, due to the non-availability of sophisticated cameras with the Delhi office, it is not possible to provide photographs to the Chief Minister's Office as well as journalists on demand. The Government was considering the purchase of new upgraded cameras and accessories for the office of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations at Maharashtra Parichay Kendra, New Delhi.
Government's decision:
For the purchase of new updated camera and accompanying literature for photography/ videography for Maharashtra Parichay Kendra, New Delhi under the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, an estimated amount of Rs. An amount of Rs. 6.50 lakh is being provided as administrative and financial sanction.
Government e-Marketplace (GeM) is a portal developed by the Government of India for procurement of goods and services by Government departments/ agencies.
