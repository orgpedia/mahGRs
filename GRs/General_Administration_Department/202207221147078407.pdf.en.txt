Subject: Filling up of the post of" Member Secretary" on the establishment of Maharashtra State Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission by deputation.
request from the Joint Secretary of the Ministry.
Sir/ Madam, the post of" Member Secretary" on the establishment of the Maharashtra State Commission for Scheduled Castes under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance is to be filled on deputation from the officers of the Joint Secretary cadre in the Department of Ministerial Affairs.
Accordingly, all the administrative departments of the Ministry are requested to provide the name of the Joint Secretary of their department who is willing to go on deputation to the post of Member Secretary on the establishment of Maharashtra State Scheduled Castes and Tribes Commission, along with certified copies of his confidential reports for the last five years, personal information (bio-data), complete service details (duration, department, post), no-departmental inquiry certificate and no-objection certificate of the department, etc. It should be sent in a manner that it is received by 05.08.2022. Also, if any officer is not interested, he should inform this department.
