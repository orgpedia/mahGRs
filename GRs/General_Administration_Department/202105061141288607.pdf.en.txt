Preamble: GOVERNMENT DECISION The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the General Administration Department, dated 20.01.2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2021-22, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute under the objective of 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) under the article Title A-4, 2070 Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Subsidiary Grants to Vanamati, Nagpur for training programme under Integrated Scrutiny (20700862). Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2021-22, the Institute of Accounts Heading A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur has been provided with an amount of Rs. 75,91,000/- and 31-Additional Grants (Non-Payment) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,79,21, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,55,12, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Twelve Thousand only) has been approved.
As per the circular dated 06.04.2021 of the Department of Finance, a grant of Rs. 7,59,100/- has been made available on Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEAMS), for the financial year 2021-22, for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) for the month of April, 2021, subject to a ceiling of 10% of the total annual allocation.
Thereafter, the Department of Finance has provided a subsidy of Rs. 3,79,550/- as on 28.04.2021, through Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEAMS), subject to a ceiling of 5% of the total annual allocation. Accordingly, at present, a total subsidy of Rs. 11,38,650/- has been provided by the Finance Department under the 36-Assistant Grants (Salary) Scheme.
As per the request of the Vanamati Training Institute, Vanamati, Nagpur, for the financial year 2021-22, a grant-in-aid of Rs.36-crore has been sanctioned to the Institute of Accounts A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, under the head' Subsidiary Grant for Training Programme under Integrated Schedules (20700862)'.
