Various welfare schemes are being implemented by the state government. It is necessary to publicize these schemes through the media so that the information of these schemes reaches the general public. For this, various schemes/ campaigns are being implemented by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. Among these schemes,' Special Publicity Campaign' is a scheme under the scheme and the government was considering to broadcast the government message through public billboards from the provision made available in the 2019-20 budget.
The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
For the year 2019-20, Rs. Administrative and financial sanction is being accorded under this Government Resolution for expenditure on the following items amounting to Rs. 5,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore Fifty Lakh only).
