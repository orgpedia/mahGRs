Government's decision
It has been prescribed under the above order that the officers/ employees of the State Government shall submit their statements of remuneration and liabilities every year. Accordingly, the employees are required to submit their statement of remuneration and liabilities as on 31st March of every year to the prescribed authority by 31st May of that year. The above provision has also been made applicable to all the semi-government institutions, Panchayat Raj Institutions, Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats, Statutory Bodies, Public Undertakings, Corporations, Boards under the various administrative departments of the State Government.
In view of the situation arising out of Corona (COVID 19) virus infection in the State, the time limit prescribed under the above orders for furnishing of periodic statements of remuneration and liabilities for each year up to 31st May and for furnishing of statement of remuneration and liabilities in respect of officers/ employees first appointed as per the position at the time of their first appointment has been relaxed as a special case for this year and further extended up to 31st August, 2020, as mentioned in the above order. A copy of the order is attached.
Now after the lockdown, the proportion of office attendance of employees under the mission has been increased again, but it has still been limited. In view of the above and the overall facts, the time limit extended vide order (3) above upto 31st August, 2020 is being further extended upto 30th September, 2020.
