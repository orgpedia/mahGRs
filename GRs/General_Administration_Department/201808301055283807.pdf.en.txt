As per the Government decision dated 15.04.2017, the penal rate of licence fee to be collected from Government officers/ employees/ their families for stay beyond permissible period in Government accommodation under General Administration Department, Brihanmumbai is Rs. 100/- per sq.ft. per month. Also referred to as No. 6 here. As per the Government decision dated 01.06.2018, the Public Works Department has fixed the penal rate of licence fee at Rs.150/- per sq.ft. per month for the period beyond the permissible period of stay in the Government accommodation in the area of Brihanmumbai under its jurisdiction.
However, despite the increase in the penal rate, there is a tendency among the government officials and employees to occupy the government residences unofficially even after retirement and transfer. As a result, the needy officers/ employees of the waiting list working in the government offices in Brihanmumbai have been deprived of government accommodation for many years. Hence, such behaviour should be avoided. Public Works Department also. In view of the increase in the penal rate under the Government decision dated 01.06.2018, the matter of increasing the penal rate in respect of Government residences under the General Administration Department in Mumbai was under consideration of the Government in order to have uniformity in the penal rate of license fee levied in respect of Government residences in Mumbai.
Government Decision: 5 here. The penal rate (licence fee) prescribed for stay beyond permissible period in Government accommodation under Government decision dated 15.04.2017 is Rs. 100/- per month to Rs. 150/- per month.
These orders will come into effect from September 1, 2018.
(3) It is hereby clarified that in the cases where before the date of this Order, for the period after 31.08.2018, the previous rate (Rs. 100/- per sq.ft. per month) has also been extended/ permitted. 01.09.2018 to Rs. 150/- per sq.ft. per month.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the concurrence given under the Informal Reference No. 79/18/ Service-5, dated 13.06.2018 of the Department of Finance.
