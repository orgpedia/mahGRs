The Proposal: -
Evaluating the service progress of officers / staff to achieve the goals of transparent, dynamic good governance. The Chief Minister's 150-day service programme is proposed to be implemented in respect of officers / staff of the subordinate field offices, local bodies, boards, corporations, authorities, agencies, autonomous bodies, PSUs, commissions of the Ministries / Administrative Departments.
The progress made by the department under the 150-day service personnel programme will be multiplied and 20 out of the total Yoo marks will be provided for scanning and uploading on the online system the offline submitted performance appraisal reports of Group-A, Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers working under the concerned department and on deputation. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to issue guidelines to all the "Cadre Controlling Authorities."

Method of Scoring if the information is not submitted within the prescribed time: - If the department does not provide information about the officers serving and officers deputed during the prescribed time, the Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers of the department will be scored taking into account the sanctioned number of posts.
B. Familiarity with scanning the work assessment report: -
Preparation for scanning: - Work assessment reports recorded in offline mode should be submitted under the supervision of the Editing Officer. Make sure that all work assessment report entries (signatures, stamps, notes, scores, etc.) are complete. Keep the offline work assessment report ready for scanning if it is cracked, folded, blurred, cluttered, etc.
Method of scanning: - Scan the performance appraisal report Black & White Ha. The performance appraisal report for each reporting year should be scanned separately. If there is more than one performance appraisal report in a reporting year, they should be scanned chronologically and a single PDF file of all performance appraisal reports in a year should be created.
The procedure after the scan:
The size of the PDF file generated after scanning the performance appraisal report for each reporting year should be a maximum of 3 MB.
Compress the file if the size of the PDF file is more than 3 MB.
The quality of the PDF file should be checked before uploading after scanning the performance appraisal report as follows: -
> Is the PDF file fully readable?
> Are the signatures, stamps, or important information in the PDF file clear? > Do all the documents in the PDF file look like the original documents?
> Does the number and order of pages in the PDF file match?
