Preamble: As per the Government Resolution dated 29th October 2001 of the General Administration Department, the modalities have been prescribed for complete computerization of Government activities. Every Department shall constitute a Project Implementation Committee to implement the computerization projects under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary/ Secretary or an officer of the rank of Secretary as may be directed by the Secretary. The committee will have secretary (mother), planning department and finance department as members. Accordingly, the matter of forming a Project Implementation Committee for the procurement of computer related projects/ materials for the General Administration Department (GAD) was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is approving the constitution of Project Implementation Committee as follows for approving the proposals for procurement of projects/ materials for computerization of General Administration Department (GAD).
