Finance Department Reference No. As per the revised figure of posts in the office of Assistant Commissioner (Backward Classes Cell) under the Divisional Commissioner, as per the provision of the Government decision here, a proposal is under consideration of the Government to convert 80% of the total 42 (36 regular sanctioned+ 6 supernumerary posts) posts into permanent and 20% posts into temporary posts.
Government's decision:
A total of 42 (36 regular sanctioned+ 6 supernumerary posts) are sanctioned as per the revised figure of posts in the office of Assistant Commissioner (Backward Classes Cell) under Divisional Commissioner. Out of the sanctioned posts, 34 posts will be made permanent with a ceiling of 80%, 2 posts with a ceiling of 20% and a maximum of 6 posts with a ceiling of 8 posts. 1 here. The approval is being granted subject to the conditions contained in the Government Resolution dated 21.06.2006. The classification of these posts as permanent/ temporary is being done as indicated in the attached Form-A.
