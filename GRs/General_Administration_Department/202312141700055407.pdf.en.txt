To set up a committee to completely protect the functioning of examinations conducted for various posts in the state government.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Resolution No.Maloa-1123/ P.No.202/ K.Maloa
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: December 14, 2023
Preamble: The matter of fully protecting the procedures of examinations conducted for recruitment to various posts in the State Government was raised in the 2nd (Monsoon Session, 2023). An expert committee has been formed by the government to look into the matter. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: In view of the above facts, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission is constituting an expert committee as follows to make recommendations to the Government to completely protect the examination system and to suggest measures in accordance with paper leak and other malpractices.

The functions of the said Committee shall be as follows: (1) To suggest administrative and legal measures to completely protect the functioning of the examination of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and to prevent paper leakage and other malpractices.
(2) To suggest administrative and legal measures to prevent paper leakage and other malpractices in relation to the examinations of various cadres under the department other than the examinations in the office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within three months from the date of issue of the said Government Resolution.
