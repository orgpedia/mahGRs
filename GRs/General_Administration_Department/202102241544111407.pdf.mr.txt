ORDER
Shri Sanjay S. Aghav, Desk Officer, Planning Department, Government of Maharashtra
(presently working as, Under Secretary in the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India
on deputation basis) has been selected as Additional PS in the personal staff of Hon'ble Minister
of State for Education Ministry (Shri Dhotre Sanjay Shamrao), Government of India on
deputation basis as conveyed by GOI vide letter under reference No.1. Government of
Maharashtra, General Administration Department, vide letter under reference No.2 has given
cadre clearance regarding appointment of Shri Sanjay S. Aghav on deputation basis for this post.
2. Henceforth, the services of Shri Sanjay S. Aghav, presently working as Under Secretary
in the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India on deputation basis has been transferred
to the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India as Additional PS in the
personal staff of Hon'ble Minister of State for Education Ministry (Shri Dhotre Sanjay Shamrao)
on deputation basis initially for the period of 1 years with the following terms and conditions:
a) The period of deputation will start from the date of joining of Shri Aghav in the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India and it will end when Shri Aghav will return to his parent department after handing over the charge in the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.
b) The Government of Maharashtra reserves the right of premature reversion if the services of the employee needed in larger public interest;

conditions of deputation with reference to MCSR(Joining Time, Foreign Services......etc) 1981,
Rule 40 (Annexure-2) in consultation with Ministry of Human Resource Development,
Government of India.
4. All administrative matters of Shri Aghav, shall be handled by the Planning Department,
Government of Maharashtra as well as by the Ministry of Human Resource Development,
Government of India.
