Introduction: The Marathwada region of the state was liberated from the Nizam rule on 17 September 1948 by the action of the Hyderabad police. Every year, September 17 is celebrated as Marathwada Mukti Sangram Diwas in all the districts of the Marathwada region. On this day, the national flag is hoisted on the buildings of all government, semi-government and local bodies in Aurangabad, the divisional headquarters of Marathwada region. On this occasion, the flag was hoisted at all the district headquarters by the Chief Minister, Deputy Chief Minister, Deputy Chief Minister, Chief Secretary and all the district magistrates. In this case, the government was considering to appoint a cabinet sub-committee to organize various programs in Marathwada and prepare the framework for the program on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of the Marathwada Liberation Struggle.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Liberation Struggle is commencing on 17 th September, 2022. On this occasion, the government is approving the formation of a" Cabinet Sub-Committee" as follows to organize the program and determine its framework.

Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad is being appointed as Member Secretary of the Cabinet Sub-Committee.
The mandate of the Cabinet Sub-Committee is proposed to be as follows: To work out the modalities for celebrating the Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Liberation Day in each district of the Marathwada region.
Setting guidelines for organizing events in the memory of martyrs who sacrificed their lives to free Marathwada from the Nizam's rule.
Planning for organizing government programs, blood donation camps etc. on the occasion of Amrit Mahotsav in all government/ semi-government offices and important places in Marathwada region and to involve the people of Marathwada region in various programs to be organized on the occasion of Amrit Mahotsav.
A report on the events organized on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of the Marathwada Liberation Day. to present to the Prime Minister.
