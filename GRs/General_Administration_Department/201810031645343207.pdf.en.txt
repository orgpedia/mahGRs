The services of the above mentioned employee are being made available on deputation to the office and designation of the State Information Commission mentioned against his name for a period of two years at first instance, subject to the following conditions:
(a) the service of the deputation shall commence on the date on which the employee hands over charge of the post in the Government service and shall end on the date on which the employee resumes charge of his Government post.
If his services are required by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ Cadre Controller/ Competent Authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and (d) he shall be entitled to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that he intends to return to the parent department.
(3) No deputation allowance shall be payable to the employees concerned during the period of deputation. (e) Considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Rules, 1981 and the General Administration Department, Government Resolution, dated 17-12-2016, the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with the General Administration Department/ Office-6.
