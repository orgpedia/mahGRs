The Proposal:
General Administration Department, Government Decisions, Dy. As on 20/01/2014, Combined Peripheral Training Programme (CPoP) is being implemented for Group-A and Group-B cadre officers who are directly recruited in Government service and the funds to be made available per trainee per day for training has been fixed under General Administration Department, Government Resolution, dated 08/08/2014. Accordingly, in the Budget 2022-23, for the ongoing foundation training at Vanamati, Nagpur of Batch-8 of Combined Peripheral Training Programme, Vanamati, Nagpur has been allocated Rs. 39,20,000/- to be distributed through Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHADA).
Government's decision
The training of the candidates recommended under the State Service Examination-2020 has been started at Vanamati, Nagpur from 23/08/2022 under the Combined Peripheral Training Programme-8 (CPTP-8). Due to less number of candidates recommended for Group-A cadre in CPTP-8 batch, the training of these candidates has been started at Vanamati, Nagpur along with the trainee candidates of Group-B cadre instead of YASHADA, Pune.
