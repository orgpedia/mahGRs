Government Circular- Reference No. As per Circular No. 1, the Assistant Cell Officers who have the first opportunity to appear in the Departmental Examination for promotion to the post of Cell Officer for the year 2023 are being trained for the Departmental Examination, the applications for the examination of only such candidates were first invited. However, the applications of the candidates who have had the first opportunity to appear in the departmental examination will be called separately. 1 was reported.
Accordingly, the circular is now inviting applications for the promotion to the post of Cell Officer in the year 2023 for all those candidates whose departmental examination is not the first opportunity and who have not yet passed the previous examination (have failed in one or more subjects in the previous examination or have been absent in the previous examination or have not submitted an application for the previous examination) in the form attached to the examination. The concerned candidates are required to submit the said applications to this Department through their establishments latest by 21st March, 2023.
The new syllabus for the examination. It has been implemented from the year 2022 examination, determined by the government decision of 2. In the said decision, it was stated that two examinations will be conducted as per the old syllabus as a special case for the Assistant Cell Officers who have failed in some subjects in the examination conducted as per the old syllabus.
According to the old syllabus, one exam was held in the year 2022 and now the second exam will be held in the year 2023. Therefore, all the candidates who apply should note that all the subsequent examinations will be held according to the new syllabus only.
All the administrative departments of the Ministry should bring this circular to the notice of the concerned Assistant Cell Officers (including the Assistant Cell Officers on deputation/ leave) in writing.
