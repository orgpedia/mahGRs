ORDER
With reference to letter dated 31st July, 2019, Hon'ble Minister of State,
Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution, Government of India has
decided that Shri J.S. Gadhe (Gadhave), who is presently working as, Assistant
Section Officer, with Government of Maharashtra in the Department of Revenue
& Forest Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai may be posted as his Assistant
Private Secretary in personal staff on co-terminus basis.
2. Revenue and Forest Department, Government of Maharashtra has
submitted Vigilance Clearance, Cadre Clearance and other required information
to Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution vide letter dated
18-10-2019
3. Hence, Shri J.S. Gadhe (Gadhave), Assistant Section Officer, with
Government of Maharashtra, Revenue and Forest Department, Mantralaya,
Mumbai has been appointed as Assistant Private Secretary to the Minister of
State, Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution in the Department of
Consumer Affairs, New Delhi on deputation basis for a period of 2 years with
the following terms and conditions:
a) The period of deputation will start from the date of joining of Shri J.S.
Gadhe (Gadhave) as Assistant Private Secretary to the Minister of State,

Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution in the Department of Consumer
Affairs, New Delhi and it will end when Shri Gadhe will return to his parent
department after handing over the charge as Assistant Private Secretary to
the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution, Department of
Consumer Affairs.
b) The Government of Maharashtra reserves the right of premature
reversion if the services of the employee needed in larger public interest;
c) In case the borrowing department does not need the services of the
employee, the services of the same may be returned to the lending department
by giving three months' notice to the competent authority/lending department;
and
d) If Shri Gadhe (Gadhave) wants to return to his parent cadre, he will be
allowed to return when the same is conveyed by him in writing with at least
three months prior notice to the competent authority/borrowing department.
5. The Revenue & Forest Department, Government of Maharashtra shall
decide other terms and conditions of deputation with reference to Maharashtra
Civil Services Rule (Joining Time, Foreign Services.....etc.) 1981, Rule 40
(Annexure-2), and Government of Maharashtra, General Administration
Department, Government Decision, dated 17-12-2016. In consultation with the
Department of Consumer Affairs, Government of India.
6. All administrative matters of Shri Gadhe (Gadhave) shall be handled by
Revenue & Forest Department, Government of Maharashtra as well as by the
Department of Personnel & Trainings, Government of India.
