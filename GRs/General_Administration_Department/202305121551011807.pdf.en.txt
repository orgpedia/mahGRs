Applications are invited from the candidates whose first chance will be the Assistant Cell Officer Post-Interview Training Examination of the Government Circular Year 2023.
The first chance candidates should submit their applications to this Department through the Establishment Branch of their Department by May 31, 2023 in the form attached with this circular. Separate applications will be invited from all candidates whose Post-Service Training Examination, 2023 is not a first-chance examination.
Since the training for the year 2023 is scheduled to be held in June, 2023, the matter of applying within the stipulated time should be brought to the notice of all the eligible Auxiliary Cell Officers (including Auxiliary Cell Officers on deputation/ leave) immediately by the Establishment Branch Officers of all the Departments and their signatures should be taken and preserved in their records.
