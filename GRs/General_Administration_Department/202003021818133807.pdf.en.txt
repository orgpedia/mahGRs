the Government Order
As per the orders of the Government in this regard, the postings of Assistant Cell Officers and Clerks-Typists for the conduct of Assembly Elections 2019 have been made in the General Administration Department/ Election Branch (Office Post-33). The services of these officers/ staff were made available on a temporary basis till the completion of the election process.
The Election Commission has requested the officers/ staff working in the Election Branch to be appointed permanently to the newly created 128 sanctioned posts in the Chief Electoral Officer's Office as the officers/ staff working in the election branch have become aware of the work in the office of the Chief Electoral Officer.
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharging of Government Duties Act, 2005, the services of the following officers/ employees are being made available to the General Administration Department/ Office Section-33 (Election Branch) on regular basis by partially modifying the order issued in the context of the posting and temporary services provided by the replacement of the Assistant Cell Officer/ Clerk-Typist in the following Ministries:
