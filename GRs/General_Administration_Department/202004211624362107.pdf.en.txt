Government's decision:
In order to prevent the spread of Corona Virus (COVID-19) in the offices of the State, and to take various preventive measures to prevent the officers/ employees of the State from getting infected, the said Reference No. Under the government orders mentioned at 2, 3, 4 and 7, instructions have been given to control the total attendance of employees in government offices.
2. Reference No. 8 In pursuance of the order dated 27.03.2020, instructions have been issued to exempt the differently abled employees from attending office for rendering essential services. Accordingly, the Maharashtra State Handicapped Employees Association has demanded exemption of all disabled employees from attending office during the lockdown period as the immunity of the disabled persons is not as good as compared to the general public and it is difficult and inconvenient for the disabled to face the problems arising at the time of commuting. 9 is written here.
Under the above circumstances, all the disabled officers/ employees are exempted from attending government offices during the period of lockdown in the state.
4) This order shall remain in force until further orders.
