the prelude
As per the directions of the Election Commission of India, SVEEP is expected to seek the cooperation of various departments of the Central and State Governments, Government and Non-Government Organizations, Educational Institutions, Information and Public Relations Department, etc. Accordingly, it was under consideration to form a State Election Literacy Committee at the state level and a District Election Literacy Committee at the district level to create awareness among the citizens about the elections, plan various campaigns, implement and evaluate them.
Government's decision
State level and district level Election Literacy Committees are being constituted to create awareness about elections among the citizens at the state level, to plan various campaigns, to implement and evaluate them.
