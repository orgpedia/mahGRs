Preamble: For the financial year 2023-2024, the Institute of Accounts, Title A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur has been provided with an amount of Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 1,23,73, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Three Lakh Seventy Three Thousand only) has been approved. Out of which, so far, Vanamati Training Institute has been provided with 36 Auxiliary Grants-in-aid (Salary) amounting to Rs. 58,68,314/- has been disbursed. Now, under the said objective, the Government was considering to disburse a total grant of Rs. 20,50,406/- (Rupees Twenty Lakh Fifty Thousand Four Hundred and Six only) for the payment of arrears of salary for the month of September, 2023 to November, 2023 and for the Diwali festival in advance, as per the following decision:-
b) The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the disbursed subsidy and ensure that the amount is drawn from the treasury by depositing in the treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
(e) submit to the Treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred and after collecting the amount, it should be ascertained whether the amount collected has been recorded under the same head in the account in the Accountant General's Office and the certificate regarding the same should be submitted to the Government within 15 days.
e) The entire responsibility of meeting the expenditure incurred in the financial year 2023-24 shall be of the concerned Controlling Officer as well as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The UTILIZATION CERTIFICATE of the subsidy being distributed by the Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur, should be submitted to the Accountant General, Audit, Maharashtra 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur and the Government without fail within the stipulated time.
