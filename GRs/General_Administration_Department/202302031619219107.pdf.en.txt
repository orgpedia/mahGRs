3. Provisional promotion of the said short story writers to the post of lower grade short story writers is being made subject to the following conditions.
(1) The promotion shall be of temporary nature as an administrative exigency.
2) These promotions are being given only on temporary basis considering the fact that at present, there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of short story writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
3) The promotion is temporary in nature and there will be no permanent retention of the concerned in this post.
The selection of eligible short story writers for the year 2022-2023 is provisional in nature and promotions to the post of lower grade short story writer is provisional in nature.
As a result, shortlisted short writers will not be entitled to the regular post of lower grade short writer and seniority in service.
In case of non-acceptance of promotion to the post of Lower Grade Short Writer by the concerned Short Writer, his/ her name will be removed from the selection list for the year 2022-2023 under General Administration Department, Government Decision, No. SRV-2015/ Q. No. 303/ Q. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and his/ her eligibility will be checked for temporary promotion to the post of Lower Grade Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his/ her name in the subsequent two years selection lists.
(6) The temporary promotion of the said short story writer to the post of lower grade short story writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
7) The promotion. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 is being made subject to adjudication.
8) Short writers who have been given only temporary promotion to the post of lower grade short writers must join the post of lower grade short writers within 30 days from the date of issue of this order.
