Under the Transfer Act, 2005, the officers of the cadre of Under-Secretary in the Ministry Department have been transferred and posted as per the order at Reference No.1. Some of the orders
Secretaries have not been relieved of their duties. The Department has also received requests for extension/ postponement/ cancellation of transfer of certain Under Secretaries. These requests have been denied. Accordingly, the Under Secretaries mentioned in the following table are being relieved unilaterally with effect from 31.07.2018 (M.No.) to join the Department of Transfer at Rakana No. 4 from the Department of Rakana No. 3 in front of their names in accordance with the Government Circular/ Government Decision at Reference No. 2& 3.

The concerned Under-Secretary shall join the department indicated in Rakkana No. 4 before his name on 01.08.2018 (BC) without waiting for any other order. These orders are orders of exemption and there is no need for separate orders to be issued by the concerned departments.
As the period of superannuation of the undersecretaries mentioned in the above table is not admissible under the provisions of Rule 12 of the M.N.S. (Period of Incorporation, Post-retirement Service and Provisions during Suspension, Removal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, the period of absence of the undersecretaries shall be deemed to be a period of service if they do not join immediately in the department indicated in the table before their name.
