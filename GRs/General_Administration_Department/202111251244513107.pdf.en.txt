Sir/ Madam, The procedure for deputation of officers/ staff of the ministerial cadre has been laid down under the relevant Government decision. As per the said procedure, the posts of 2 Cell Officers in the office of the State Information Commission, Bench Amravati, are to be filled by deputation from the cadre of Ministerial Cell Officers.
Accordingly, applications are being invited from the officers of the cadre of Cell Officers in the Ministry Department for filling up the posts of Cell Officers on deputation as above.
2. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring this letter to the immediate notice of the Officer on Establishment Cell of their Department. Accordingly, the information in the accompanying prospectus of the Cell Officers who have expressed their willingness for deputation shall be furnished to this Department with the approval of the Secretary of the Department. be made available by 10.12.2021. The willingness shown by the interested cell officers cannot be withdrawn. Also, if any of the room officers are not willing, they should also be informed.
