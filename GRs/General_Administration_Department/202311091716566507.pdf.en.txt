Introduction: An independent State Election Commission has been constituted in each State to conduct elections to local bodies in a free, fair and transparent manner on the lines of Lok Sabha and Vidhan Sabha elections in the State (Articles 243K and 243ZA).
The Maharashtra State Election Commission, established in 1994 under the above amendment to the Constitution, is appointed by the Secretary as the head of the administrative department to carry out its functions properly and efficiently.
At present, the post of Secretary in the State Election Commission. The post is lying vacant since 29.11.2022 and the additional charge of the post has been given to other officers. As a result, the day-to-day functioning of the State Election Commission is adversely affected. As per the directions of the Chief Minister, the selection of one officer from the panel of 3 officers from the Indian Administrative Service for the post of Secretary, State Election Commission was made to the State Election Commission under the said Reference No. (2) of the General Administration Department Office-10. Accordingly, the Commissioner, State Election Commission has proposed under the said letter at Reference No. 3 that Shri S.M. Kakani, IAS (Retd.) be appointed to the said post.
Government's decision:
1) Shri S.M. Kakani, (Retd. Secretary, State Election Commission, is being appointed on contract basis for a period of one year from the date he assumes charge of his office.
2) Shri S.M. Kakani, (Retd. The State Election Commission shall conduct all the functions of the State Election Commission under the Chairmanship of the State Election Commissioner.
Mr. S.M. Kakani, is being appointed subject to the following terms and conditions:
