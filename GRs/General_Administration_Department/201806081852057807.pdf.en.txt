2. The following instructions shall be strictly followed by all the concerned departments so that the transfer orders are implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The concerned department of the transferred officers shall immediately relieve the officers of that department without waiting for the appearance of the substitute officer.
(2) The transferred officers shall be reinstated without delay on their arrival in the department to join after being relieved from other departments as per the order of transfer. In case of non-recruitment of an officer, the establishment officer of the concerned department will be held responsible for sanctioning the compulsory waiting period.
(3) If any officer directly or indirectly pressurises any officer to modify the transfer order, such officer shall be liable to disciplinary action.
