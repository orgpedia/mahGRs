GOVERNMENT DECISION: The terms and conditions of the appointment of Mr. Rahul Tidke (IS: 2012) as Deputy Director (Information) are as follows.
Period of Deputation: Shri Rahul Tidke (IS: 2012) as Deputy Director (Information) w.e.f. 01/12/2022 (w. will be on deputation for the next two years. Provided that, (a) if the Government considers it necessary in the interest of public service, the Government shall have the right to extend the period of service of such person on or before the expiry of his service as may be prescribed by rules made in that behalf by the Central and State Governments.
(b) If their services are not required by the Government, the State Government shall be competent to return them to their original service. However, this must be done at least three months in advance.

Salary: Mr. Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012) will hold the post of Deputy Director (Information) during the period of this deputation. During this period, as per their Final Pay Certificate (LPC), they will draw salary in the Level-11 Basic Pay Scale (67,700-2, 08,700) as per the 7th Central Pay Commission. The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations will take appropriate action as per the 7th Pay Commission regarding the sanction of their annual increment.
(3) Deputation Allowance:- During the period of deputation to Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012), the Central Government has issued Office Order No. 01/11/2017, dated 24/11/2017 at the rate of 5% of the basic pay or a maximum of Rs. 4500/- per month is admissible.
4) Transport Allowance: Ministry of Finance, Department of Expenditure, Government of India, Office Order No. Transport allowance as on 21/05/2017, dated 07/07/2017 will continue to be admissible. However, the transport allowance will not be admissible if the vehicle is provided by the office of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations.
Travel and Daily Allowance: Travel and Daily Allowance will be admissible as per the Office Order No. 19030/01/2017-E.IV dated 13th July, 2017 of the Ministry of Finance, Department of Expenditure, Government of India.
The LTC facility was established by the Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India vide O.O. Order No. LTC facility shall be admissible as per revised order dated 31011/04/2008, dated 23/09/2008 and 310/11/07/ 2013 dated 26/09/2014 of the same department.
Educational Allowance for Children: The Office of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Government of India vide Order No. Educational allowance for children as on 27012/02/2017, dated 16/08/2017 will continue to be admissible.
Dearness Allowance: Dearness Allowance will be admissible to the Indian Information Service (IIS) officers at the rate at which Dearness Allowance is admissible to them during the period of their deputation.
Local Supplementary Allowance: Local Supplementary Allowance shall be admissible to them at such rate as may be admissible to them from time to time in accordance with the rules/ orders of the State Government.
10. House Rent Allowance in lieu of Residence: If the Government is not able to provide them accommodation commensurate with their status during the period of their deputation, they will be entitled to House Rent Allowance as per the rules of the Central Government.
11. Period of Incorporation Salary and Transferable Travel Allowance: Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012) will be paid his salary and travel allowance during the period of assumption of office by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, as per the rules of the Government in which he had gone to the post-retirement service or as per the rules of the Government in which he will go to the service after returning from the post-retirement service.
Travel Allowance for travel for duty during the period of deputation: Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012), on joining the post-retirement service, is required to travel for duty as per the rules of the Central Government or the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, Government of Maharashtra.

Under the rules, they will be able to give the option of taking travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of which rules will be more beneficial.
Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012) will be governed by the Central Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1972 during the period of his deputation. The Head of the Department will be competent to grant leave other than casual leave to them during the period of deputation. The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations shall bear the entire expenditure on leave salary and local supplementary allowance payable during the period of post-retirement service or for the period of leave taken at the end.
Leave Pay in respect of Disability Leave: In respect of leave to be granted in respect of disability arising out of non-service or in respect of disability arising out of such service, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations shall be responsible for payment of leave pay even if such disability comes to light after the end of non-service.
Leave Salary: In the case of Shri Rahul Tidke (IS: 2012), the leave salary shall be calculated at the prevailing rate and credited under the respective head of accounts by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. Further, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations shall be liable to pay penal interest admissible during the period of delay in payment of these amounts during the period of deputation.
16) Casual Leave: Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012) will enjoy casual leave as per the rules of the State Government during the period of his deputation. The Head of the Department will be competent to grant casual leave during the period of deputation.
Leave Travel Concession: Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012) will be entitled to get Leave Travel Concession as per the provisions of the Central Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1972, the cost of which will be borne by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. The Head of the Department will be competent to grant leave relaxation during the period of earned leave as the Head of the Department is competent to grant leave other than casual leave during the period of deputation. The Head of the Department will be competent to grant casual leave to them during the period of deputation as well as grant leave travel concession during the period of casual leave.
Medical Exemption: Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012) will be entitled to medical facilities as per the provisions of the Maharashtra State (Medical Care) Rules, 1961. The expenditure will be borne by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. However, there will be separate rules for grant of medical exemption to Central Government employees and if Shri Rahul Tidke (Indian Information Service) wants to take advantage of it, he will be free to give option to avail the benefit of that rule during the period of deputation.
19) National Pension Scheme: During the period of deputation of Shri Rahul Tidke (IS: 2012), the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations will take action regarding deduction of his National Pension Scheme contribution from his salary every month and crediting the same in his NPS account as per rules and accordingly the Government share will be paid.

Departmental Inquiry: If a departmental inquiry is to be conducted against Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012), the Central Civil Services (CCA) Rules, 1965 shall apply. When the time comes to conduct a departmental inquiry against them while on deputation, they will be repatriated to central government service.
21) Group Insurance Scheme: Central Government employees will be paid the prescribed amount from their salary every month as a contribution towards the Group Insurance Scheme.
Any delay in payment will be subject to a late payment penalty. Information and Public Relations Department will be responsible for this.
Loans and advances: During the period of deputation of Shri Rahul Tidke (IS: 2012), his parent department will be able to sanction house building advances, motor car advances and other advances. The applications should be sent to their parent department through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations.
Further, if Mr. Tidke has already been sanctioned house construction advance, motor car advance, personal computer advance by the Central Government and the principal advance or interest in respect thereof is pending, the same shall be recovered from the monthly salary and credited to Mr. Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012) through Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, Mumbai under the head of accounts mentioned in the order of advance sanction.
Appointment on return from leave or training while on deputation: If Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012) goes on leave during the period of deputation or goes for training and other officers are appointed in the post held by him, all expenses in respect of salary, salary, period of post-taking, compulsory leave etc. shall be borne by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations from the date of expiry of leave or training till the date on which he assumes charge of the other post.
To enforce the rules prescribed by the Government from time to time: During the period of deputation of Shri Rahul Tidke (IIS: 2012), the rules/ executive orders made or issued by the Government from time to time shall be applicable.
Travel within the country/ abroad during the period of deputation: While on deputation in the office of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations under the General Administration Department, prior permission of the Government will not be required to travel anywhere in the country with the consent of the Head of the General Administration Department in connection with the work of the Department. However, if it is necessary to go abroad in connection with the work of the General Administration Department or for private work, then prior permission of the government will be required.
26) Regarding terms/ conditions not included in the prescribed terms and conditions: The rules, regulations and executive orders applicable to the officers of the Central Civil Services Cadre in respect of specific terms/ conditions not included in the above mentioned terms/ conditions shall be applicable.
