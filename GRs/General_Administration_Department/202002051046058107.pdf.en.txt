Preamble: The responsibility of air travel of high-profile persons in the state is carried out by the Directorate General of Aviation under the state government. Many technical matters arising under the office of the Director, Aviation cannot be dealt with by this office without the assistance of technical experts. Also, the e-tendering process conducted by this office has also had to be cancelled due to errors in the tender documents and the tender process. Therefore, the matter of providing technical assistance to the Director, Aviation was under consideration of the Government.
In this regard, the proposal to appoint Air India Engg Services Ltd as a technical advisor to this organization was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: To take decisions regarding matters related to the functioning of the Directorate General of Aviation. The Empowered Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 15/2/2018 of the General Administration Department under the Chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. It has been decided to accept the above proposal in the meeting of the Empowered Committee dated 13/8/2019. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate General of Civil Aviation, Mumbai was authorised to enter into an agreement with Air India Engg Services Ltd. Accordingly, the agreement has been signed with the approval of the Government on 14/11/2019. Costs are covered by the agreement.
