Government circular:
The Government has decided to engage the services of Government/ semi-Government retired officers for certain work on contract basis. Issued on 17.12.2016. In the said Government decision, instructions were given to meet the expenditure on remuneration and allowances of persons appointed on contract basis from the provision available under the head" Office Expenditure" of the concerned office. Department of Finance The circular dated 29.01.2020 has directed to increase the remuneration/ honorarium etc. of temporary/ contractual/ seasonal employees from the target head" 10 Contractual Services."
2. Since the services of retired officers are also contractual in nature, in order to have a strict classification of expenditure, the cost of the services of retired officers for certain work was also being considered to be covered under the" Objective Head" of" 10 Contractual Services." Following instructions have been issued:
3. from the date of this Government Circular. """ Expenditure on account of remuneration and allowances of retired officers engaged on contract basis for certain work shall be covered under the\"" Objective Head\""\"" 10 Contract Services\"" in accordance with the procedure laid down in the Government Resolution dated 17.12.2016."""
All the departments should ensure that in the budget for FY 2022-23, the necessary provision of funds for the provision of expenditure on service contract of retired officers is budgeted under the target head" 10 Contractual Services."

As per the Government decision dated 17.12.2016, if the funds for contractual services of retired officers are allocated below the target head of 13 office expenses and 10 contract services are available under the scheme indicators, the subsidy should be reallocated from 13 office expenses to 10 contract services. However, in case the" Target Head" of" 10 Contractual Services" is not available under the scheme notation, the expenditure for the current financial year will be covered under the" Target Head" of" 13 Office Expenditure." The departments should take necessary action to provide 10 contractual services in the next financial year.
