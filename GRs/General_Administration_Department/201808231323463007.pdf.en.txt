The Proposal:
In order to facilitate the functioning of the Public Works Department, a proposal was under the consideration of the government to allot the main building, second floor hall used for the Principal Secretary (Transport and Ports) to the Principal Secretary, Public Works Department. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The main building used for the Principal Secretary (Transport& Ports), the second floor hall used for the Principal Secretary, Public Works Department and the Principal Secretary (Public Works Department) No. 618 (Extension), 6th Floor, is being allotted to Principal Secretary (Transport& Ports), Home Department.
