Relocation of the office of the National Informatics Centre (NIC) on the third floor of the main building of the Ministry. ..
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department,
GOVERNMENT DECISION, NO. SHAKAJA-1122/ PR. NO. 55/2022/22,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date of release: September 7, 2022
Preamble: With a view to consolidate the Department of Ministerial Administration on a single floor, the office of the National Informatics Centre (NIC) has been shifted from the third floor of the Ministry Main Building to the 11th floor of the New Administration Building, 310 Sq. The proposal to allot the land was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Government has taken the following decisions:- The Government has decided to shift the office of the National Informatics Centre (NIC) from the third floor of the Mantralaya Main Building to the 11th floor of the New Administration Building. Space is being allocated.
The Executive Engineer, City (East) Division, Nirman Bhawan, Mumbai shall hand over the possession of the said land to the concerned and submit a report to the Government.
