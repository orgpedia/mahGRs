The Proposal: -
To provide network infrastructure to Gram Panchayats in the state of Maharashtra.
In 2019, the state government has approved the BharatNet Phase-II project. Project Maharashtra
Financial assistance from the Central Government and the State Government through the Information Technology Corporation (MahaIT).
is being implemented. As of now, 10,053 Gram Panchayat Passenger Routers have been completed under this project.
Out of which, 9,519 (76%) Gram Panchayats are ready with services and 56,067 km of fibre.
Optical cables are targeted, of which 50,704 km (90 per cent) are fibre optical cables.
The demolition work has been completed.

A time-bound programme for completion of BharatNet Phase-II project by setting up a committee.
