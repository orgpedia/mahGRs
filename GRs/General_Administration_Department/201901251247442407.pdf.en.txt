Officers who have been appointed during the period 31.12.2016 by way of regular promotion, direct service or otherwise. A provisional seniority list for the year 1.1.2017 has been prepared and is annexed to this circular.
The said seniority list of Assistant Director (Administration), Group-B Cadre Officers is provisional as on 1.1.2017. The names of officers who retired and were promoted to the senior cadre as on 31.12.2016 have been excluded from the list of seniority.

Please note that if no objections are received by the Government by 20.02.2019, the Seniority List will be finalised assuming that there are no objections from the concerned.
