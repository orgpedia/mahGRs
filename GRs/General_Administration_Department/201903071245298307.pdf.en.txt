OFFICE ORDER: To prevent sexual harassment at workplace. The writ petition was filed in the Supreme Court vide Writ Petition No. 666-70/1992. In it. The Supreme Court has stayed the implementation of the Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention and Redressal) Act, 2013. It was released on December 9, 2013. The Department of Women and Child Development has issued instructions to form a committee to investigate complaints of sexual harassment under the provisions of the Act and recommend action accordingly. 2 decisions have been made by the government. According to the Minister, Chief Minister's Secretariat, The Minister and The following Committee is being constituted to inquire into complaints of sexual harassment of women officers/ staff working on deputation in the office of the Minister of State and recommend action accordingly.
