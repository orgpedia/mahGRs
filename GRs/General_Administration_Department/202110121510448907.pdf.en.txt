Circular:
The training policy of the state has been decided as per the decision of the government.
It has detailed provisions for budgetary allocation of funds for all types of training to be conducted under the State Training Policy. Further, as per the State Training Policy, Yashada Pune has been directed to submit the technical training plan to the concerned department and accordingly, the concerned department should budget the funds for technical training.
However, in the Service Review Meetings held from time to time by the Administrative Departments, it has been observed that the budgetary provisions made by various departments for technical training remain largely unutilized. As per Para 2.2 of the State Training Policy, the responsibility of planning, development, planning, coordination, implementation, provision of financial outlay, etc. of technical training lies with the concerned administrative department. Accordingly, for effective implementation of the State Training Policy, all the Ministries/ Departments are being advised as under:
a) All the concerned administrative departments should ensure that the funds budgeted by each department for this technical training are not wasted and properly utilized as per the annual plan.
(b) To review the funds spent on a regular basis during the period of the financial year by holding coordination meetings with the Regional Offices and Technical Training Institutes under the Department from time to time.
3. All the Ministries/ Departments shall take note of the above instructions and take necessary action within the prescribed time.
