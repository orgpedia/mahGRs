Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC): The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has invited applications for recruitment to various posts. A two-year integrated training programme is carried out in accordance with the provisions prescribed by the Government. Read above. The existing Combined Probationary Training Programme (CBT) has been revamped and implemented from the fourth batch of the said programme (CPTP-4). Also read the above. In accordance with the provisions of Section 3, a four-session Post-Graduate Degree course affiliated to the University of Mumbai is being conducted for candidates who are being trained in CPTP from batch CPTP-4.
2) Read the above. The duration of this phase of basic training in Combined Probationary Training Programme has been fixed at 6 weeks. The syllabus and examinations of the first semester of the newly introduced postgraduate degree course under the said programme are required to be completed within the basic training period. Since the 6-week period of basic training is not sufficient to complete the first semester course and conduct the examinations accordingly, the duration of the training is not sufficient.
