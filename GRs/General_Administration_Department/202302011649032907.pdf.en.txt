Mr. D.H. Daberao, Cell Officer, Planning Department is being appointed on deputation for the first time as" Cell Officer" in the office of the State Commission for the Right to Service, Amravati Revenue Division, Amravati under the General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions for one year.
(a) Mr. D.H. Daberao, the Cell Officer shall commence his deputation from the date on which he enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner, and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his parent Department, complete his deputation by handing over the charge of the office of the Deputy Commissioner, one year thereafter.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
After repatriation of the deputation, Mr. D.H. Daberao, Cell Officer will be required to join his parent department i.e. Planning Department.
The Planning Department and the General Administration Department shall determine the terms and conditions for the deputation of Mr. D.H. Daberao after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II and III) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mr. D.H. Daberao, Cell Officer will be handled by the Planning Department.
5. Mr. D.H. Dabarao, Cell Officer to be relieved immediately by the Planning Department for accepting appointment on deputation.
