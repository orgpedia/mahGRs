14) General Administration Department, Government Decision No. Dated 15.01.2021 Preamble: Government has decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for various cadres of Group-A and Group-B officers to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per Government Decision General Administration Department, dated 20.01.2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2020-21, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute under the objective of 36-Assistant Grant (Salary) under the article Heading A-4, 2070 Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Subsidiary Grant for Training Programme under Integrated Schedules to Vanamati, Nagpur. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:

For the financial year 2020-21, under the objective of 36-Assistant Grant (Salary) to the Institute of Administrative Services, Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur under the heading of Subsidiary Grant (20700862) for Training Programme under Integrated Schedules, a total amount of Rs. 56,75,200/- (Rupees Sixty-six Lakhs Seventy-five Thousand Two Hundred only) has been disbursed to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Management Extension Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute during the month of April, 2020 to January, 2021 as per the Government Decision No.
Now, for the financial year 2020-21, under the target of 36-Assistant Grant (Salary) under the head of A4, 2070-Assistant Grant (20700862) to the Institute of Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for training programme under Integrated Schedules, an amount of Rs. 3,54,700/ (Rupees Three Lakhs Fifty Four Thousand Seven Hundred only) is being distributed by the Bureau of Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control (BIMS) to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The Director, Vanamati, Nagpur shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the grant disbursed and shall ensure that the amount is drawn from the Treasury by depositing in the Treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
(b) submit to the Government within 15 days the amount collected after depositing in the Treasury the amount equal to the actual expenditure and ensure that the amount collected is recorded under the same head in the records of the Accountant General's Office.
e) The entire responsibility of meeting the expenditure incurred during the financial year 2020-21 shall be of the respective Controlling Officer as well as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
UTILIZATION CERTIFICATE of the grant being distributed by the Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur, to the Accountant General, Audit, Maharashtra 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur and to the Government.
The Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur should maintain separate accounts and books of expenditure incurred from the above grant. The powers to examine the books and accounts shall be vested in the Accountant General, Maharashtra 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur, for which Vanamati will provide full facilities.
