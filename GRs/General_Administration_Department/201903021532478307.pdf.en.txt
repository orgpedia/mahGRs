Government's decision:
The 28 temporary posts indicated in the accompanying Annexure on the Establishment of the Office of the Secretary to the Governor, subject to the condition that the revised figure is immediately approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries, are being allowed to continue from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
The expenditure on these posts shall be met from the grant sanctioned during the financial year 2019-20 under the head of accounts shown in column 6 in front of the post mentioned in column 2 of the Annexure.
8/16/ A.P.C., Dy. being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under 21.02.2019.
