The Proposal:
Before independence, Marathwada was a part of the princely state of Hyderabad. The people of Marathwada had to fight a long battle against the Nizam of Hyderabad to get freedom.
Marathwada was liberated on 17 September 1948. On the occasion of the Amrit Varsh of Marathwada Mukti Sangram, various programs and activities are being implemented in Marathwada by the state government from 17 th September 2022 onwards.
The Collector, Latur vide letter dated 12.9.2023, has announced to celebrate the Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Muktisangram with the participation of Shri. Ramgarh (Raigad). In Udgir, Latur district, the construction of a gate in memory of the freedom fighters who were martyred in the Marathwada Liberation War was estimated at Rs. A budget of Rs 97.78 lakh has been presented to the government.
The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government's decision:
On the occasion of Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Mukti Sangram, Shri. Ramgarh (Raigad). In Udgir, Latur district, the construction of a gate in memory of the freedom fighters who were martyred in the Marathwada Liberation War was estimated at Rs. 97.78 lakhs (Rupees Ninety Seven Lakhs Seventy Eight Thousand only) is being accorded administrative approval.
