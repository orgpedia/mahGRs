Government Decisions for the disabled soldiers/ heirs of the soldiers who have been martyred in war, war-like situation and security related operations in the country/ abroad.
The widows/ families of the officers/ jawans of the Border Security Force and other paramilitary forces who have been martyred in an encounter in a security related operation in the country are given financial assistance as per the decision of the Government dated 27/03/2018. If the martyr is married and his parents are also alive, then a) his brave wife- 60%, b) the brave mother- 20%, the brave father- 20%
(a) 40% of the share of the deceased soldier's parents is divided equally between the parents of the deceased soldier, if his parents are alive, and (b) if the deceased soldier is married and his parents are not alive, then the share of the deceased soldier's wife is 100%. Further, Rs. 8.50, 000/-has been paid vide Government Resolution No. 2018/ PR No. 110/28 dated 7/03/2018. The remaining amount of Rs. 25,00,000/- Rs. 8,50,000/-= Rs. 16,50,000/- is proposed to be provided as the balance assistance.
