Introduction The Government of Maharashtra has issued new rules from time to time in accordance with the changes that have taken place in the field of advertising in the last 15 years by formulating rules for the distribution policy of classified and front-page advertisements to newspapers/ magazines. In view of the changes in the field of advertising and the new media including social media, the Government has issued the Circulation of Messages Rules-2018. After the issuance of this rule, various demands, objections, amendments and suggestions have been submitted by the Editor and Journalist Service Union of Small Newspapers in Maharashtra, Maharashtra, Mumbai and other organizations. The matter of making some amendments after considering these objections and suggestions was under the consideration of the government.
The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The provisions and amendments in the Government Communication Rules-2018 are as follows:
