Government's decision:
The 19 non-permanent posts (excluding 9 posts for Statutory Development Board) indicated in the accompanying Annexure on Establishment of the Office of the Secretary to the Governor, are being allowed to continue from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 subject to the condition that the revised figure is immediately approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries.
The expenditure on these posts shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21 under the head of accounts shown in column 6 in front of the post mentioned in column 2 of the Annexure.
