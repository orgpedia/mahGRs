Mr. and Mrs.,
The General Administration Department, vide Government Resolution dated 23.09.2011, has laid down the training policy for all Government officers/ employees. Further, the General Administration Department, vide Government Notification dated 02.02.2022, has notified the Maharashtra Civil Services (Time Tabled Training) Rules, 2022. As per the rules, basic, revision and post-promotion training has been made mandatory for all government officials/ employees. As per the General Administration Department, Government Resolution, dated 23.09.2011, the duration of post-promotional training for Group-A has been prescribed as two weeks under the State Training Policy.
2.According to the letter No. The officers who have been promoted to the post of Under Secretary in the Ministry Department have been nominated for 12 days post-promotion training at Maharashtra Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology, Model Colony, Pune from 03.10.2023 to 14.10.2023. Now, the following Under Secretaries are being nominated for this training.
