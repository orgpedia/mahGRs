Preamble: The services and conditions of the State Chief Information Commissioner are prescribed under the Government Order, General Administration Department, dated 7.9.2007. As per the provisions, residential telephone is admissible to them under the post-retirement facility and the amount of telephone refund is admissible up to Rs 1500 per month.
Instead, the government was considering paying a lump sum monthly telephone allowance to the state chief information commissioner after his retirement.
Government Decision: State Chief Information Commissioner will be entitled to a monthly telephone allowance of Rs. 1,610/- (Rupees One Thousand Six Hundred and Ten only).
