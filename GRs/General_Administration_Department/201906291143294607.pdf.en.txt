The widows/ families of the Border Security Force (BSF) and other paramilitary forces personnel who have been martyred in an encounter in a security related operation in the country are provided financial assistance as per the decision of the Government dated 30.11.2016
The aid is divided into the following categories: (i) If the martyr is married and his parents are also alive, then a) his brave wife- 60%, b) the brave mother- 20%, the brave father- 20%, c) if the mother or father of his parents is alive, then 40%, 2) the martyr soldier is married and his parents are not alive, then his brave wife is 100%, 3) the martyr soldier is unmarried and his parents are also alive, then by dividing equally between his parents, 4) the martyr soldier is unmarried and if his parents are not alive, then his brother/ sister is divided equally.
