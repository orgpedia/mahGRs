Government's decision:
The Collector and District Election Officer, Hingoli Office has issued a notification for the repair of the godowns taken on long-term lease from the Maharashtra State Warehousing Corporation required for the storage of EVM-VVPAT machines, after scrutinizing the budget through the Public Works Department with technical consent and with the recommendation of the Chief Engineer. 2 received Rs. 19,71,791/- in respect of proposal No. Administrative approval has been granted under Section 3. However, the said administrative sanction did not include the cost of separate toilets for men and women, compound wall, CCTV and borewell etc.
According to the guidelines of the Election Commission of India, the godowns for storage of EVMs should have CCTVs, separate toilets for men and women and clean drinking water. Further, since there are 2 other godowns in the premises of EVM godown, it is necessary to have a compound wall for security purposes during the election period and for permanent isolation, in this godown, separate toilets for men and women, compound wall, CCTV and borewell etc. 57,80,000/-with the signature of the Chief Engineer, the Budget was presented by the Collector and District Election Officer, Hingoli in his Reference No. The letter is presented in 1.
