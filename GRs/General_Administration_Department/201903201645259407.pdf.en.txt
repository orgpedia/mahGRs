In view of the amendments made by the Parliament under the 103rd Constitutional Amendment, the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, vide its Order No. 1, inter alia, provides for direct admission to the civil services and posts in the Central Government and educational institutions for the economically weaker sections of the society.
10% reservation is provided for admission. Accordingly, the Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India, vide order No. 2, has prescribed 10% reservation for economically weaker sections of the society for direct entry into the Central Government Civil Services and posts.
Further, the Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India, vide order No.3, has prescribed the pattern of income and dearness allowance certificate for the benefit of reservation for economically weaker sections for direct entry into the civil services and posts in the Central Government. (Attached)
(3) Therefore, the competent authority as declared by the Central Government should immediately issue the necessary certificate in the prescribed format to the economically weaker section to avail the benefit of reservation, so that it is possible for all eligible citizens to take advantage of the said reservation.
