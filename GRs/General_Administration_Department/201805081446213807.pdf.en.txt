The Proposal:
The associations of Government servants (non-industrial) are recognized by the Government subject to the provisions of Rule 29, Rule 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 and the terms and conditions prescribed in the Annexure, Schedule I and Schedule II of the said Rules and the Constitution and Rules of the" New Model Rules." The procedure to be followed for the same has been prescribed vide Government Circular dated 18.3.2015 at Reference 3.
The District Collector, Nanded vide his letter dated 5.1.2017 has submitted to the Government a proposal for grant of Government recognition to the Association" Nanded District Revenue Employees Association, Nanded" (hereinafter referred to as" the Association") under the prescribed format of application. The matter of granting recognition to this organization under the provisions of Rule 29 and 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 was under the consideration of the Government. As a result, the following decisions are made:
Government's decision:
The Nanded District Revenue Employees Association, Nanded, a Government Employees (Non-Industrial) Association, is being recognized by the Government subject to the terms and conditions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, Rules No. 29 and 30 and the New Model Rules, Schedule II, Rules No. 1 to 29 and subject to other terms and conditions prescribed below
The Association shall be affiliated under the administrative control of the Department of Revenue and Forest, Ministry, Mumbai 32.
(2) The Secretary in charge of the Revenue and Forest Department shall be the competent authority in case of disputes in connection with the office of the Association, elections to the Association and office bearers. They may also nominate an officer subordinate to them (who is not inferior to the Deputy Secretary of the HUA) for this function.
The Nanded District Revenue Employees Association represents the Group-C category. Not less than 25 per cent of the total number of government employees in that category should be permanent members of the association.

(4) The Association shall not be affiliated to any political party or political organization or take part in any political movement. The Association shall not publish any promotional or inflammatory material, newspaper or otherwise, on behalf of the members or permit a member of the Association to publish.
The minutes book, register of members, books of accounts and certificates and all other documents of the Association shall be kept open for inspection by any officer authorised by the Government in this behalf.
(6) The Association shall submit to the Secretary of the Department concerned, by such date as may be prescribed by such officer as the Government may authorise in this behalf, a copy of the annual returns and audited accounts of its members. It will also be mandatory to hold elections of office bearers of the organization within the stipulated time.
7. If the" Nanded District Revenue Employees' Association" violates the prescribed terms and conditions in Rule No. 29 and 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 as well as the above mentioned Conditions No. 3 to 6, the recognition of the organization will be cancelled.
The Revenue and Forest Departments shall ensure from time to time that the aforesaid conditions are duly complied with by the said organization. 2 Necessary action should be taken as per the instructions mentioned in the government circular here.
