Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for a person with disability to be appointed to a post in the government service. Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi. The list of posts ensured for the disabled has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. According to the notification, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has been directed to ensure posts for the disabled in state government establishments (on the lines of the Central Government). The instructions have been issued under the Government Decision dated 02.02.2021. As per the directions of the said decision, the matter of ensuring the posts for the disabled in the establishment of the Directorate General of Civil Aviation, Mumbai, under the control of the General Administration Department, was under the consideration of the Government.
