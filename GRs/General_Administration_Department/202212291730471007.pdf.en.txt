Memorandum: As per the provisions of Rule 16 (2) of the A.B.S. (Conduct) Rules, 1968, From p.
It is mandatory for the officers to submit their annual immovable property return to the government in the prescribed format by January 31 every year. As per instructions issued vide letter No. 3 of the Department of Personnel and Training, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Government of India, the said returns are required to be filed online. Accordingly, this year also, instructions have been issued under Reference No. 5 for online submission of Annual Real Estate Returns showing the status as on 1st January, 2023 for the year 2022.
Accordingly, all the officers working in the BPS in the cadre of Maharashtra State should submit the annual immovable property returns for the year 2022 in the online mode. As per the decisions taken by the Central Government from time to time, all the IPS officers of the cadre of this state should follow the procedure prescribed as follows for submitting their immovable property returns.
I. For online submission of Immovable Property Returns (IPR), the Home Page of SPARROW Computer System of Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India (http:// sparrow.org/) is available. A navigation tab for IPR Module has been made available on eoffice. gov. in.
for the Sparrow computing system. The immovable property returns should be submitted using the User ID and Password given to the SEO Officers and authenticated using the Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) or E Sign which has already been made available to the SPARROW Computer System.
If for any reason the concerned officer is not able to use the Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) or has not received the DSC/ E Sign, he/ she may file his/ her immovable property returns.
