GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: On the establishment of the Sainik Welfare Department, Pune Office, 3 posts have been created viz., 1 post of Deputy Director vide GOVERNMENT DECISION No. 19/7/1984 dated 19/7/1984 and 2 posts of Deputy Director vide GOVERNMENT DECISION No. 2 dated 15/11/1990. There are 3 posts of Deputy Director General (Administration), Deputy Director General (Rehabilitation) and Deputy Director General (Militarization). However, these posts have not been declared as the head of the office.
2. Now the Deputy Director (Administration) on establishment of Sainik Welfare Department, Pune Office is being declared as" Head of Office" under this Order.
(3) This Government Order is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under Rule No. 2 of Schedule III to the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part I, Sub-Division I.
