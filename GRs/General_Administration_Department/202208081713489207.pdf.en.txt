The Proposal:
The Government has taken a policy decision to start the Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP) of probationary officers of various cadres who are directly recruited to Group A and Group B through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission through the State Service Examination through the training institutes of Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur respectively. The training of the candidates who have been recommended on the basis of the final result of the State Service Examination-2020 conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission will start from 23.08.2022 under the batch of Combined Probationary Training Programme-8 (CPTP-8). In this regard, the matter of issuing guidelines for the candidates joining this training program, the training institutions and the concerned administrative departments was under consideration of the government.
Following instructions have been issued:
Government Circular: The following instructions are being issued regarding various issues regarding the Combined Probationary Training Programme-8 (CPTP-8) to be conducted for the candidates recommended under the final result of the State Service Examination-2020.
(i) Guidelines for commencement of training: 1. Out of the candidates joining for training of CPTP-8 batch, the verification of original certificates of candidates belonging to Group-A cadre has been done on 23.06.2022 at Yashoda Pune. The scrutiny of original certificates of candidates belonging to Group-B cadre has been done at Vanamati, Nagpur from 27.06.2022 to 1.07.2022. These candidates have been appointed as Probationary Officers under CPTP-8 with effect from 23.08.2022, subject to verification of their character, medical examination and educational and other necessary documents by the concerned agencies, reports of the said checks and verifications.
All Probationary Officers of CPTP-8 batch are required to attend training at Vanamati, Nagpur on 23.08.2022. The candidates who are going to join the training of CPTP-8 in the service of the State Government, they should be relieved from the offices where they are currently working to join the training institute on 23.08.2022 (before midday).
The Government will take a decision in accordance with the candidates who are going to join the training of CPTP-8 batch, who are already in the service of the State Government. The 1 month notice period prescribed vide No. 33/92/8, dated 2.12.1997 is being relaxed. as well as in government service
The period from the date of discharge of the candidates till the commencement of training shall be regularized as the period of duty in the previous post.
(ii) In respect of salary of probationary officers: 1. In order to ensure that probationary officers of CPTP-8 batch receive salary and other incidental allowances, the concerned administrative departments shall register these officers in the service system, obtain their number for DCPS and appoint Drawing and Disbursing Officers for their salary, etc.
