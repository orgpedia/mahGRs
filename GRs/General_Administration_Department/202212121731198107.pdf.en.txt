Government Circular: As per Government Circular, General Administration Department No. Sarvasu-1196/ PR No. 5/96/29, dated 18th September, 1996, Government/ Semi-Government Offices in Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban District are declared local holiday on the day of' Anant Chaturdashi' every year and on the occasion of' Gopalkala' (Dahi Handi) from the year 2007. Accordingly, a local holiday is being declared for all government/ semi-government offices in Mumbai and Mumbai Suburban District in the year 2023.
