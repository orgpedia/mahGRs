Preamble: As per the notification dated 15.12.2022, a separate Department of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities has been created out of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. The Department of Disabled Welfare has constructed a building in a private building at Mittal Tower, 3rd Floor, Nariman Point, 3080 sq. The place has been rented out temporarily. The place is 400-500 meters away from Mantralaya. People with disabilities are required to come to the Ministry for work related to all departments of the Ministry. If the office of the Department of Disabled Welfare is outside the Ministry, it will be very difficult and cumbersome for the disabled to contact the Ministry and the Department of Disabled Welfare. Therefore, considering the sensitivity of the matter, it is necessary to provide space to the Department of Disabled Welfare in the Ministry on the ground or first floor.
Accordingly, the proposal to allot space to the Department of Disabled Welfare in the Ministry building on the ground or first floor was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The main building of the Ministry of Tourism and Cultural Affairs, the ground floor and the Ministry Extension Building on the 1st, 4th and 5th floors of 2342 sq. ft. of space and 700 sq. ft. on the first floor of the Ministry of Social Justice and Special Assistance Extension Building. The total area is 3042 square meters. ft. of space is being allocated to the newly created Department of Disabled Welfare.
10 Rule No. 115 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I, vests the administrative department with full powers in the matter of obtaining approval for renting and providing rent for office premises. In exercise of this power, the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs shall complete the process of renting out the required space for the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs within one month and hand over the possession of the space in the Ministry to the Department of Disabled Welfare.
The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has constructed a new building on the first floor of the Ministry Extension Building at 4567 sq. 700 square feet. The foot space should be vacated and its possession should be given to the Disabled Welfare Department.
4. the decision of the government. The decision should be implemented immediately as it is being issued with the approval of the Chief Minister.
The Executive Engineer, City (East) Division, Nirman Bhawan, Mumbai shall hand over the possession of the said land to the concerned and submit a report to the Government.
