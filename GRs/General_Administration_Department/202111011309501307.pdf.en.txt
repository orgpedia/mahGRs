August 2, 2021 Preamble The revised pattern of posts in the office of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad was approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries as per the minutes of its meeting dated August 3, 2019. Accordingly, the Government Resolution at Reference No.5 was issued. However, there was an error in the design. Mali has been declared dead. However, the Finance Department has amended the minutes of the high-level committee of secretaries as one employee is working in this cadre. Accordingly, the proposal to approve the revised figure was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: On the revised pattern of posts in the office of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad. The Government's decision of February 2, 2021 is hereby rescinded.
Now, with this decision of the Government, the revised pattern of posts in the establishment of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad is being approved. Accordingly, 8 regular posts of A.No. 1 to 8 cadre have been approved along with the government decision. Also, the cadres of No. 9 to 18 have been declared dead. The number of posts should be considered as the number of employees working in this dead cadre.
