Government circular
The provisional additional provisional seniority list of lower grade scribes in the Ministry Department as on 01.01.2019 is annexed to this circular at Annexure A. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
3. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring to the notice of all the employees (including those on leave/ deputation) in the cadre of Lower Grade Short Writer, in their Department, the present date of 01.01.2019, of additional provisional seniority. In this regard, the objections/ errors received from the concerned lower grade scribes should be examined as per the records of the department and sent to this department along with the comments of the department by 30.04.2019. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
