The services of Smt. Urmila Joshi, Cell Officer, Home Department (presently working on deputation in the office of the State Chief Commissioner of Right to Service, Mumbai) are being handed over by deputation for a period of one year to the post of Cell Officer in the office of the State Commission for Right to Service, Konkan Division under the" Maharashtra State Commission for Right to Service."
(a) Mrs. Joshi shall commence her term of service on the date on which she enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which she enters upon her parent department, demit the office of the Deputy Commissioner after one year from the date on which she enters upon her office.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. (e) In accordance with the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981, the General Administration Department, Office No. 18 and the Home Department (Establishment) shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with each other.
3. All other administrative matters of Smt. Joshi, Cell Officer, General Administration Department, Office No. 18 and will be handled by the Home Department (Establishment).
