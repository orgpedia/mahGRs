The Proposal:
The associations of Government servants (non-industrial) are recognized by the Government subject to the provisions of Rule 29, Rule 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 and the terms and conditions prescribed in the Annexure, Schedule I and Schedule II of the said Rules and the Constitution and Rules of the" New Model Rules." The procedure to be followed for the same has been prescribed vide Circular No.3 dated 18.3.2015.
The Chief Inspector of Electricity, Industries, Power and Labour Department vide his letter dated 31.05.2018 has submitted to the Government a proposal for grant of Government recognition to the Maharashtra State Electricity Inspectorate Employees Association (hereinafter referred to as" the Association") under the prescribed format. The matter of granting recognition to this organization under the provisions of Rule 29 and 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 was under the consideration of the Government. As a result, the following decisions are made:
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Electricity Inspectorate Employees Association, a Government Employees (Non-Industrial) Association, is being recognized by the Government subject to the terms and conditions contained in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979, Rules No. 29 and 30 and the New Model Rules, Schedule II, Rules No. 1 to 29 and subject to the other terms and conditions prescribed below:
The Association shall be affiliated under the administrative control of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour.
(2) The Secretary in charge of Industry, Energy and Labour Department is the competent authority in case of dispute regarding inspection of the office of the said organization, election of the organization, office bearers.
