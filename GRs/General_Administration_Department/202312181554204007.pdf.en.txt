In view of the urgency of effective external media to inform the public about the goals/ policies/ decisions/ schemes of the government, the single source agencies, which have the monopoly of government/ semi-government/ corporation, however, are not on the DAVP list of the central government, the matter of approving the old/ existing DAVP of the same media and the agency for advertising through local cable and the old DAVP rate was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decisions: In view of the urgency of the effective use of external media to inform the public about the objectives/ policies/ decisions/ schemes of the Government, the single source agencies for external media (which are monopolized by the Government/ semi-government/ corporation) and DAVP rates of old/ existing common media and advertising agencies through local cable and their old DAVP rates are being approved as a special case for one year as follows:
