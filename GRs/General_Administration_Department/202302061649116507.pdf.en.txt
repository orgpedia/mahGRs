The Proposal:
The office of the Chief Electoral Officer, State of Maharashtra had three servers for maintaining and updating the website and database of voters in the electoral roll. These servers have been in use for more than 10 years. The servers are currently down. Therefore, the Director, Department of Information and Technology has provided a server to keep a database of voters in the electoral roll on a temporary basis and to keep the website of the Chief Electoral Officer's office running. Since the provision is temporary in nature, the government was considering granting administrative approval to the proposal to immediately purchase two new servers to keep the database of voters in the electoral roll and to keep the website of the Chief Electoral Officer's office running.
Government's decision
A total of Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded for procurement of total two new servers at a ceiling price of Rs. 40,00,000/- through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal by following the procedure laid down in the Government Order under the Department of Industries, Power and Labour.
The specifications of the presented servers have been certified by the Information and Technology Corporation.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2022-23 under the head" Demand No. A-2, 2015-Elections, 103- Preparation and printing of electoral rolls, (00) (01) Preparation and printing of electoral rolls (20150032), 13 Office expenses."
