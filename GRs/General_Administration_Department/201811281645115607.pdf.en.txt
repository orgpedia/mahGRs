Introduction: As per Section 16 (5) (a) of the Right to Information Act, 2005, the salary and allowances of the State Chief Information Commissioner shall be the same as that of the Central Election Commissioner and as per Section 16 (5) (b), the salary and allowances of the State Information Commissioner shall be the same as that of the Chief Secretary of the State. The conditions of service of the State Chief Information Commissioner and the State Information Commissioners have been issued vide two separate Government Orders dated 7.9.2007. The Seventh Pay Commission. State Chief Information Commissioner
The matter of revising the salary of the State Information Commissioner was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As per the Seventh Pay Commission, with effect from 1st January, 2016, the post of" State Chief Information Commissioner" will be paid an emolument of Rs. 2,50,000/- (Fixed) (Level 18 in Pay Matrix) and Rs. Level 17 in the Pay Matrix (Rs. 2,25,000/-).
