ORDER
Smt. Anita Dipak Ripote, Section Officer, Law and Judiciary Department, Government of Maharashtra has been selected to the post of Section Officer/Court Officer in the Central Administrative Tribunal, Hyderabad Bench, Hyderabad on deputation basis initially for a period of one year from the date of taking over the charge of said post, as per conveyed by Central Administrative Tribunal, Principal Bench vide it's letter under reference No.1.
2. Therefore, the services of Smt. Anita Dipak Ripote, Section Officer, Law and Judiciary Department, Government of Maharashtra has been transferred to the Central Administrative Tribunal, Hyderabad Bench, Hyderabad as section officer/court officer, on deputation basis for the period of one year with the usual terms and conditions of deputation as stipulated in the Department of Personnel & Training's OM No.6/8/2009 Estt. (Pay II) dated 17.06.2010 as amended from time to time as conveyed vide letter under reference No.1 and with the following terms and conditions :
a) The period of deputation will start from the date of joining of Smt. Anita Dipak Ripote in the Central Administrative Tribunal, Hyderabad Bench, Hyderabad and it will end when Smt Ripote will return to her parent Department after hand over the charge in the Central Administrative Tribunal, Hyderabad Bench, Hyderabad.
b) The Government of Maharashtra reserves the right of premature reversion if the services of the employee needed in larger public interest; c) In case the borrowing Department does not need the services of the employee, the services of the same may be returned to the lending Department by giving three months notice to the competent authority/lending Department; and d) If Smt. Ripote wants to return to her parent cadre, she will be allowed to return when the same is conveyed by her in written with at least three months notice to the Competent authority/borrowing Department.
