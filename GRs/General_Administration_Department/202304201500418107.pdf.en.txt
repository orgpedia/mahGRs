Government's decision:
As per the Government decision dated 6th February, 2023, a total amount of Rs. Administrative approval was accorded for procurement of total two new servers at a ceiling price of Rs. 40,00,000/- through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal by following the procedure laid down in the Government Resolution dated 24.08.2017 of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour. As per administrative approval, the expenditure for procurement of these servers was approved to be met from the approved provision available in the financial year 2022-2023 under the head Demand No.A-2, 2015-Elections, Preparation and Printing of 103 Electoral Rolls (00) (01) Preparation and Printing of Electoral Rolls, 13 Office Expenditure (20150032). However, due to the non-completion of procurement of servers in the financial year 2022-23, it has not been possible to procure servers in this financial year.
Since the process of procurement of new servers has been completed in 2023-24, it is being approved to meet the cost of procurement of these two new services from the approved provision available in 2023-24 under the head" 13-Office Expenditure (20150032)."
This Government Order is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Departments by the Finance Department, Government Order No: 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17th April, 2015 and the Financial Powers Order, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-II, serial No. 27-A, Rule No. 76.
