Government Resolution, General Administration Department No. No. 31/2013/ K. 9, dated 28.08.2019 Preamble: Provision has been made under the Government Order dated 8th May, 2013 of the General Administration Department to sanction lump sum compensation to the Secretaries, Principal Secretaries and Additional Chief Secretaries of the Ministries/ Departments at a fixed rate of pay in accordance with the grade. In the same manner. The decision regarding sanction of bulk compensation to the Secretary to the Governor has been taken in pursuance of the Government Decision dated 21.12.2018. Now, the General Administration Department has revised the amount of bulk compensation to be sanctioned to the officers working in the category of Secretary and above in the Ministry as per the Seventh Pay Commission Government Decision dated 28.08.2019.
According to The matter of revising the wholesale compensation amount sanctioned to the Secretary to the Governor was under the consideration of the Government.
Government decision: It has been decided under the Government Resolution dated 21.12.2018 to pay the consolidated lump sum compensation amount per month to the Secretary to the Governor as per the provisions of the Government Resolution, General Administration Department, dated 8.5.2013. The total amount of compensation sanctioned under the said Government Decision is being revised as per the provisions of the Government Decision, General Administration Department, dated 28.8.2019.
2. Also, the revision of the wholesale rates of the Secretaries and above officers in the Ministries Department from time to time shall be continued. It will also apply to the Secretary to the Governor.
This Government Resolution is being issued pursuant to the concurrence received under the Informal Reference No. NO. 492/19/ SERVICE-3, dated 22.11.2019 of the Department of Finance.
