Preamble: For computerization of work in Government Departments/ Offices, Reference No. As per the decision of the government, a project implementation committee has been directed to be formed in every administrative department to implement the computerization project under the administrative department and the offices under the department. Accordingly, a large project implementation committee under the General Administration Department has been formed to launch a separate website of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. The decision was taken by the government. In the context of Government Resolution No. As per Section 3, the e-governance policy of the state has been decided and a comprehensive strategy has been decided to make the governance of the state more dynamic and people-oriented. In view of the e-governance policy of the State and the financial constraints in the implementation of the project in the Information and Technology Policy, which have changed from time to time, this Department needs to make changes in the composition of the Major Project Implementation Committee constituted under the Government Decision No. 2 for the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. Hence, the government has decided to reconstitute the project implementation committee.
Government's decision:
Regarding the establishment of a large project implementation under the General Administration Department regarding the launch of a separate website of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission Office, Reference No. In the 2. Apart from the above members, the Committee may invite experts in specific fields, officers from other departments as per requirement for the meetings of the Committee.
The functions of the Project Implementation Committee shall be as follows: (a) To advise, co-ordinate and control the computerization and information technology related projects of the office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
(b) Rs. Approval for computerization projects upto Rs.5 crore. To approve proposals for computerization/ IT projects/ procurement of computer equipment above Rs. To recommend to the High Powered Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Chief Secretary.
The General Administration Department shall have the power to grant administrative approval to the project approved by the Project Implementation Committee in accordance with the provisions of Part I, Paragraph 4, Paragraph 27 (2) of Sub-Division III of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 in the Annexure to the Finance Department, Government Decision, Finance Department, No.
