GOVERNMENT DECISION, GENERAL ADMINISTRATION DEPARTMENT OF THE HOUSE- 2018/125/ Q. No. 43/20-A, dated 17th November, 2018.
The Proposal:
As per the Government Order dated 17.11.2018 issued in respect of reservation of rooms in New Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi, rooms and other facilities are provided as per the revised rates, classification, terms and conditions. The government was considering a proposal to amend some of the provisions of the Bill.
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to amend the Government Resolution dated 17th November, 2018 regarding reservation and rates of rooms at New Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi as follows.
