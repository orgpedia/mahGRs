circular
The Central Association of State Government Employees, Maharashtra and the Central Association of State Government Employees, Maharashtra have given a memorandum to various pending demands of the employees. The strike has been called for three days on August 7, 8 and 9, 2018. On the withdrawal of the strike. Under the chairmanship of Chief Minister. On 4.8.2018, a meeting was held with the representatives of all the organizations in which various demands of the organizations were discussed. At the end of the meeting. Since the Chief Minister has given satisfactory options to the unions, the unions should consider all the factors and withdraw the decision to go on strike.
However, the possibility of state officials/ employees joining the strike cannot be ruled out as no official announcement has been made by the unions yet.
The State Government 4th Class Employees Central Association, Maharashtra and the State Government Employees Central Association, Maharashtra are not recognized by the Government under Rule 29 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
3. d. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the Government during the three day strike period on 7th, 8th and 9th August, 2018, all the Ministries/ Departments and their subordinate offices are being advised to take the following measures:
a) Participation in the strike by government and semi-government employees will be considered as misconduct and disciplinary action will be taken against such employees, such orders should be issued without delay and they should be brought to the notice of all employees personally. A copy of the order should also be pasted on the notice board of every government department/ office.

b) Employees should try to persuade them to attend office as usual, without participating in the strike.
c) Proper arrangements should be made for opening and closing of the office at regular time during the strike period and the arrangements should be made by the responsible officer. If necessary, seek the help of the police.
(d) Heads of Departments/ Offices should not leave their headquarters during the strike period.
(e) The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office shall not grant any leave to any Government employee from the date of this order till the end of the strike and shall, after considering every case where the employee is on leave, cancel his leave and call him to work immediately or decide how.
e) The employees should be made aware that the Central Government's" No Work, No Pay" policy regarding the strike of government employees is also being followed by the state government.
(f) To inform the employees about the government services which have been declared under the Maharashtra Essential Services Act. Action should be taken against the employees who are on strike.
4. All the Ministries/ Departments shall take measures to ensure that essential and other services continue smoothly in the offices under their control during the strike period.
The zonal offices should inform the concerned Ministries/ Departments about the attendance of the employees in the office on each day of the strike period and the Ministries/ Departments should submit the information of their departments to the Office of the Commissioner in the prescribed format attached to this circular by 1 2.00 pm and the information collected from the subordinate offices by 2.00 pm to Office of the Commissioner 16A, General Administration Department, New Administration Building, 6th Floor, Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032. Email: dilip. deshmukh@nic. in. Earlier it has been noticed that the office wise details of attendance of employees are not being provided at the prescribed time. Be sure to provide this information in a timely manner.
All the Divisional Commissioners should send information about the attendance during the strike in their department and all the government and semi-government offices under them to this department by 12.00 noon. Also, the percentage of attendance during the strike period should be sent to this department by 12.00 noon in such a way that it receives accurate information.
