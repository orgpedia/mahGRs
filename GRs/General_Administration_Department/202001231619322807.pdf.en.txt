Memorandum of Government: Funds are released under the head of accounts (3053 0146) for the development of airports/ runways in the State. For the financial year 2019-20, the basic budgetary provision under head 3053-0146 is Rs. 2.00 crore and Rs. 10.00 crore. 12.00 crore has been provided. As per the circular issued by the Finance Department, funds have been sanctioned within the limit of 60 per cent of the original budgetary allocation.
Accordingly, out of the original budget provision of Rs. 1,19,99, 400/- has already been made available to Maharashtra Airport Development Company.
Now the Account Head (3053 0146) is sanctioned at Rs. 10.00 crore Supplementary Demand Fund to the extent of 60% of the amount to be released on Budget Distribution System for making available to Maharashtra Airport Airport Development Company for safety, cleaning, repair and maintenance of airports.
