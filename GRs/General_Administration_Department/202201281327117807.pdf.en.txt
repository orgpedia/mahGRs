Subject: Filling up of the post of Member, Maharashtra State Waqf Tribunal, Aurangabad by deputation.
request from the Deputy Secretary of the Ministry.
Sir/ Madam, the post of Member, Maharashtra State Wakf Tribunal, Aurangabad under the Minority Development Department is to be filled up by deputation from the officers of the cadre of Deputy Secretary in the Ministry. The person to be appointed for the said post shall be appointed in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra State Waqf Tribunals (Appointment and Conditions of Service of Chairperson and Members) Rules, 2015. 4 (3) (i): The candidate must hold a degree in Law and must have knowledge of Muslim Law.
Accordingly, all the administrative departments of the Ministry are requested to provide the names of the members under the Minority Development Department, Deputy Secretaries of their department who are willing to go on deputation to the Maharashtra State Waqf Tribunal, Aurangabad, along with the certified copies of their confidential reports for the last five years, personal information (bio-data), complete service details (duration, department, post), no-departmental enquiry certificate and no-objection certificate of the department, etc. It should be sent in a manner that it is received by 11.02.2022.
Also, if any officer is not interested, he should inform this department.
