the order
Smt.Priyanka Ranjan Chhapawale, Under Secretary, Planning Department is being appointed on deputation for a period of two years initially to the post of Executive Officer in Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai under the Department of Law& Justice subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Mrs. Priyanka Ranjan Chhapwale, Under Secretary, will commence her service as a Deputy Secretary on the date she assumes charge of her office and shall cease to hold office on the date she assumes charge of her office.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The terms and conditions for the deputation of Smt. Priyanka Ranjan Chhapwale are to be mutually decided by the Planning Department and the Department of Law& Justice after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Tenure of Office, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Smt. Priyanka Ranjan Chhapawale, Under Secretary, will be handled by the Planning Department.
