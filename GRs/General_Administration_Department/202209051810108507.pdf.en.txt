GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: ASC 1522/ PR No. 68/14-A dated 05-09-2022
General Administration Department/ s. In accordance with the provisions of Government Decision dated 20.4.2021 No. 16-B. A Special Leave Petition No. Subject to the Judgment No. 28306/2017 and the following conditions, the concerned Senior Grade Short Writers are being promoted to the open category vacancies in the Select Grade Short Writers Cadre on purely temporary basis:
(1) The promotion shall be of temporary nature as an administrative exigency.
2) These promotions are being given only on temporary basis considering the fact that at present, there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Senior Short Writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
3) The promotion is temporary in nature and there will be no permanent retention of the concerned in this post.
4) The selection of eligible upper category shortlists for the year 2021-2022 is provisional and promotions to the post of category shortlists are provisional.
As a result, the selected upper category short-writers will not get any rights of regular post and seniority of service.
In case of non-acceptance of provisional promotion to the post of Selection Grade Short Writer by the concerned Senior Grade Short Writer, his/ her name will be deleted from the list for the year 2020-2021 under General Administration Department, Government Resolution, No. SRV-2015/ PR No. 303/ K. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and his/ her eligibility will be checked for provisional promotion to the post of Selection Grade Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his/ her name in the lists to be held for the next two years.
(6) The temporary promotion of the said Senior Short Writer to the post of Senior Short Writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed to be initiated against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
7) The promotion of upper class book writers of backward classes is being done subject to the condition that they have not been elevated in the service merit list with the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2004 and the caste validity certificate of the upper class book writers has been issued by the concerned caste verification committee. The concerned department should look into it.
8) These orders shall come into force with immediate effect. The higher grade short writers who have been promoted to the post of selected category short writers should be relieved immediately by the concerned department. the date of the government notification
