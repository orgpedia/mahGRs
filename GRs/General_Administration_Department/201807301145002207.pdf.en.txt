With regard to the abolition of the supernumerary posts created for the period of probationary training (CPTP-4) of ministerial cell officers, Group B cadre candidates selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final results of the State Service (Main) Examination-2015 and 2016.

the General Administration Department

Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 30.07.2018
GOVERNOR'S DECISIONS, SYNOPSIS DATED 29.07.2017 AND 26.09.2017
The Proposal:
General Administration in Group B cadre of Ministerial Cell Officers as per the decision of the Government in respect of 32 Subordinate Officers recommended for the post of Ministerial Cell Officers through State Service Examination 2015 and 2016 and undergoing training under Combined Subordinate Training Programme (CPTP-4).
Creation of 32 supernumerary posts at the establishment of the Department for the period from 29.07.2017 to 31.07.2019
had been made. However, out of 13 candidates recommended through the State Service Examination 2015, 5 candidates have not joined the training. Also, out of the 19 candidates recommended by the State Service Examination 2016
9 candidates have joined the training and 7 candidates have been given an extension of one year to join the training by the government, while the remaining candidates have not joined the training program. Accordingly, as per the decisions of the Government in this regard, a proposal to abolish 15 supernumerary posts out of 32 posts created for the period from 29.07.2017 to 31.07.2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Integrated Peripheral Training Programme under CPTP-4 as per the decisions of the Government in this regard.
As per the decision of the Government, 15 posts out of 32 posts created for the period from 29.07.2017 to 31.07.2019 in the General Administration Department in the Group B cadre of Ministerial Cell Officers are being abolished.
