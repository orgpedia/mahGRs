Government Decision: Govt. As per the Government decision dated 10th June, 2019, the consolidated procedure for premature retirement of Government officers/ employees beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after 30 years of qualifying service has been prescribed. Accordingly, Sahyadri and Nandgiri guest houses were established. As per the sanctioned pattern as on 09.03.2021, the Manager is a Group-B (Non-Gazetted) post, with a total sanctioned strength of 80 posts comprising of 22 posts of Group-C and 57 posts of Group-D. There are 47 employees.
A proposal to form a committee under the above provision was under the consideration of the Government.
The Review Committee to review the service of the employees of Sahyadri and Nandagiri State Guest House under this Department beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after 30 years of qualifying service and the Committee to consider the grievances against the decision of premature retirement after review of service are being constituted as follows.
