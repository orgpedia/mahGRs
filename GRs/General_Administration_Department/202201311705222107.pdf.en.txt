The use of biometric attendance system has been suspended till 31 st January, 2022 under the aforesaid guidelines as a preventive measure in view of the surge in cases due to" Omicron" variant of Corona Virus.
In view of the prevailing situation of Corona Virus (COVID-19) and Omicron Virus infection, the use of Biometric Attendance System in the Ministry and New Administrative Building as well as in all Government offices in the State is being suspended till February 28, 2022.
During this period, as per the instructions in the Government Circular dated 05.01.2022, the attendance of the officers/ employees in the office should be recorded on the attendance card. The administrative departments and the establishment branches of the regional offices should take necessary measures in this regard.
