Corrigendum: Circular dated 31st December, 2021 has been issued regarding celebration of Birth Anniversary and National Day of the Nation Man/ Great Man in the year 2022.
Annexure No.9 annexed to the Circular dated 31st December, 2021 reads as under:
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | The National Day or Birthday | English Month and Day. | war         | Indian month and day. | The nature of the program              |
| 9.   | Sant Ravidas Maharaj Jayanti | February 23, 2022      | Wednesday's | 04 Falgun, Sakae 1943 | Offering floral tributes to the statue |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instead, read as follows:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | The National Day or Birthday | English Month and Day. | war         | Indian month and day.                 | The nature of the program              |
| 9    | Sant Ravidas Maharaj Jayanti | February 16, 2022      | Wednesday's | According to the date (Magha Purnima) | Offering floral tributes to the statue |
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Divisional Commissioner/ Collector shall issue necessary instructions regarding the celebration of Sant Ravidas Maharaj Jayanti (Magh Purnima as per the date) on February 16, 2022 in all government/ semi-government offices in his department/ district and take appropriate action regarding its implementation.
