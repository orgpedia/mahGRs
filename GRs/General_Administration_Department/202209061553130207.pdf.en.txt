Government circular:
Circular regarding celebration of birth anniversaries of national leaders and national days in the year 2022 in Ministries and all Government/ semi-Government offices issued. It was released on December 31, 2021.
The events mentioned in this circular shall be celebrated on public holidays, even if they fall on Saturday and Sunday.
On the occasion of the birth anniversary of the national person/ great person, the information of the concerned national person/ great person should be displayed on all display boards under the Department of Information and Public Relations in the Ministry throughout the day.
On the occasion of the birth anniversary of the national person/ great person, various programs such as lectures, essay competitions should be organized in schools and colleges so that all school and college students know about the invaluable work done by the respective national person/ great person for the country. So that the future generation will know the great work done by the great men of the nation for the country. Also, if the birth anniversary falls on a holiday, it would be appropriate for the schools/ colleges to organise various programmes like lectures, essay competitions etc. on the eve of the holiday.
The Divisional Commissioner/ Collector/ Chief Executive Officer/ Commissioner, Municipal Corporation shall issue necessary instructions regarding the conduct of the said programme in all the schools/ colleges of their division/ district/ Municipal Corporation and take appropriate action regarding their implementation.
