Preamble: For the financial year 2023-24, Demand No: A-4, 2059, Conservation and Maintenance of Martyr's Memorials (Compulsory), 27 Minor Constructions (2059 0015) under the head Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 20,60,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) has been approved. Out of this budget, 10% i.e. Rs. 2,06,000/- (Rupees Two Lakh Six Thousand only) has been received directly from the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEAMS). A proposal is under the consideration of the government to make this fund available to the concerned district collectors to meet the expenses related to the preservation and maintenance of martyr memorials.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2023-24, under the head" Demand No: A-4, 2059, Preservation and Maintenance of Martyr's Memorials (Compulsory), 27 Minor Constructions 2059 0015," Rs. A sum of Rs. 2,06,000/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Six Thousand only) is being distributed to the concerned District Collectors for the preservation and maintenance of Martyr Memorials in proportion to the number of Martyr Memorials shown against their district in the Statement A attached with the Government Decision.
(2) The said fund shall be spent for the maintenance of the memorial and for the maintenance of the buildings of the Martyrs' Memorial as well as for the lighting and gardening facilities in their vicinity, in accordance with the provisions of the decision of the Government in question. Accordingly, all the district collectors should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the subsidy disbursed to them, as well as to spend only for the matter approved by the government and submit the utilization certificate of the amount spent to the government from time to time.
The amount of grants spent from the funds distributed to all the District Collectors should be reconciled with the Accountant General-1, Mumbai/ Accountant General-2, Nagpur within the prescribed time and submit a monthly report of the expenditure to the government on a regular basis. In this case, action should be taken according to the Government Circular No: Expenditure-201/ PR No. 139/ 2011/ SWASAK-1, dated 16/08/2011.
