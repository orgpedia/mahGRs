Government's decision:
With this decision, the government has decided to honour Padma Bhushan Krantiveer Dr. Naganathanna Nayakwadi. For the construction of Phase-II of the memorial at Nagnathnagar, Walwa, G. 52,59,500/- (Rs. Fifty-two lakh sixty-nine thousand five hundred only) funds are being approved to be distributed.
The expenditure shall be met from the provision approved in the financial year 2022-23 and shall be covered under the following heads:
Demand No.A-4, Head of Accounts, 2059 Public Works 80 General 800 Other Expenditure (00) (00) (02) Construction of Monuments and Statues of National Heroes (Programme) (2059 3177), 53 Major Constructions
The provisions of the Finance Department, Government Circular No. 2022/ P. No. 43/ Finance-3, dated April 04, 2022 should be complied with. Also, the Collector, Sangli should take care to spend the money for the memorial work in the current financial year without transferring the funds to the Public Works Department in the form of deposit construction.
District Planning Officer, District Planning Committee, Collector Office, Sangli (Statutory Number 2401001039) is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending from the aforesaid fund. The Collector, Sangli will be responsible for the expenditure of this fund, submitting the utilization certificate of the fund to the Accountant General, Mumbai and matching the expenditure with the accounts of the Accountant General.
