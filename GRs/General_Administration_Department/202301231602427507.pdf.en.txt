Government circular
A provisional Common Seniority List for upper grade scribes in the Ministry as on 01.01.2023 has been prepared and annexed to this Circular.
The said seniority has been created as per the instructions of General Administration Department, Government Notification dated 21.06.2021.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
3. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring to the notice of all the employees (including those on leave/ deputation) in the cadre of Senior Grade Short Writer, of the present date 01.01.2023, the provisional shared seniority of Senior Grade Short Writer.
In this regard, the objections/ errors received from the concerned senior level scribes should be examined as per the records of the department and sent to this department by 06.02.2023 along with the comments of the department. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
