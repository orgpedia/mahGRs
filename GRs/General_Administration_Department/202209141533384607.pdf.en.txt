Accordingly, the government had given permission to make the film under the government decision of Reference Wall No. 1. To complete the film, e-tendering will be done through open market. Ilociones Pvt. Ltd. was appointed. The work of writing the code of the film was completed by this organization. The Expert Committee constituted under the Government Resolution No. 3 has agreed that the writing of the Code is satisfactory. To speed up the production of the film. A meeting was held at the Chief Secretary's level and the recommendations were made after a detailed review of the film code prepared, the preparations to be made for the production of the film, the agreement made with the film production organization, the amendments to be made in it and the supplementary agreement to be made, the extension of the deadline given earlier for the production of the film, the regularization of the extension, the availability of funds for the production of the film and the extension of the deadline for the production of the film. Accordingly, the matter of issuing guidelines for the action to be taken regarding the film production was under the consideration of the government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations should take the following action regarding the production of the film Mahatma Jyotiba Phule. The members of the expert committee have agreed to start the process of film production as the code is satisfactory in film production and film production should be started accordingly.

Accordingly, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations shall, while entering into a supplementary agreement with the film production house, include every element of the film production (deliverable), its time frame and the required funds.
