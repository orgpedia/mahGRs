The Proposal:
Government Decision The Government has decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B who are directly recruited in the Government service as per the General Administration Department, dated 20/01/2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2021-22, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the Institute under the head 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) for the purpose of Assistant Grants (20700862) to the Institute of Accounts Title A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for training programme under Integrated Schedules. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2021-22, the Institute of Accounts, Title A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur has been provided with an amount of Rs. 75,91,000/- and 31-Additional Grants (Non-Payment) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,79,21, 000. A budgetary provision of Rs. 7,55,12, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Twelve Thousand only) has been approved.
For the financial year 2021-22, under the head 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the Institute of A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for the training programme under the consolidated scope (20700862), an amount of Rs. 22,77,300/- (Rupees Twenty Two Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Three Hundred only) has been disbursed to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute.
As per the request of Vanamati Training Institute at serial number 6, for the financial year 2021-22, as per the decision of the said Government, Head of Accounts A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11),
