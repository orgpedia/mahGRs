The proposal: The High Court of Bombay, in Writ Petition No. 2797/2015, State of Maharashtra Vs.
Vijay Ghogre and others. On 4th August, 2017, the reservation in promotion was declared invalid. Decision of 25th May, 2004 is hereby rescinded. The state government objected to it. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed and is presently pending. Accordingly, the matter of forming a Group of Ministers to take a decision on reservation in promotion was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the Government has decided to constitute the following Group of Ministers.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A Group of Ministers is being constituted to decide on the reservation in promotion of officers and employees belonging to Backward Classes
