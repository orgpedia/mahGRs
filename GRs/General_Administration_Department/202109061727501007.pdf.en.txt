GOVERNMENT ORDER: In view of the COVID-19 pandemic, a revised order has been issued under the General Administration Department, Government Resolution, dated 29th July, 2021 regarding the changes for the current financial year 2021-22. Considering the possibility of a third wave,
Further, in view of the COVID-19 pandemic and the consequent restrictions, the expenditure on transfer allowance for the year 2021-22 has been limited.
This Government has decided to limit transfers on special grounds to 10% of the total number of working posts in the current financial year.
In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the following officers of the cadre of Senior Assistant Director (Information)/ Senior Deputy Editor/ District Information Officer/ Public Relation Officer, Group-A (Pay Scale S18: 49100 155800) in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations are being transferred and posted to the place indicated in column 4 before their names for ease of functioning and administrative convenience.
