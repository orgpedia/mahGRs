General Administration Department, Government Circular No. 250/16-A, dated 18.3.2015 Preamble: The Government recognition to the associations of Government servants (non-industrial) is subject to the provisions of Rule 29, Rule 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 and the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure, Schedule I and Schedule II of the said Rules and the Constitution and Rules of the" New Model Rules."
The procedure to be followed for the same has been prescribed vide Circular No.3 dated 18.3.2015.
The Joint Director Health Services vide his letter dated 3.04.2018 has submitted to the Government a proposal for grant of Government recognition to the" Maharashtra State Laboratory Scientific Officers Central Association (Malaria& Horticulture Department)" (hereinafter referred to as" the said Association") under the prescribed format of application. The matter of granting recognition to this organization under the provisions of Rule 29 and 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 was under the consideration of the Government. As a result, the following decisions are made:
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Laboratory Scientific Officers Central Association (Malaria& Horticulture Department), an Association of Government Employees (Non-Industrial), is being recognized by the Government subject to the terms and conditions contained in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, Rules 29 and 30 and the New Model Rules, Schedule II, Rules 1 to 29 and subject to the other terms and conditions prescribed below:
The Association shall be affiliated under the administrative control of the Department of Public Health, Ministry of Health, Mumbai.
(2) The Secretary of the Department of Public Health in the Ministry shall be the competent authority in case of disputes regarding inspection of the office of the organization, election of the organization, office bearers. He may also nominate an officer subordinate to him (who is not inferior to an officer of the rank of Deputy Secretary) for this function.
