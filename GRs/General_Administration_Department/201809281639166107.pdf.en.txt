Subject: Filling up of the post of Additional Commissioner (Administration) in the office of Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad on deputation.
Request for Intent from the Joint Secretary of the Ministry.
Mr. and Mrs.,
The post of Additional Commissioner (Administration) in the office of Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad is to be filled by deputation from the Joint Secretary Cadre in the Ministry.
Accordingly, all the administrative departments of the Ministry are requested to send the names of the Joint Secretaries of their department who are willing to be appointed as Additional Commissioner (Administration) in the office of Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad, along with the certified copies of their confidential reports for the last five years, along with personal information (bio-data), complete service details (duration, department, post), no-departmental enquiry certificate and no-objection certificate of the department, etc. As on 05.10.2018
It should be sent in a manner that it will be received. Also, if any officer is not interested, he should inform this department.
