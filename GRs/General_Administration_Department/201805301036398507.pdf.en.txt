Preamble: As per the decision of the Government in Reference-1, it has been approved to adopt the policy of Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity (DAVP) in respect of Government advertisements to be given to All India Radio, Doordarshan and other TV channels. Accordingly, the rates agreed by DAVP are implemented in respect of advertisements given to All India Radio, Doordarshan and other television channels.
Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity (DAVP) As on 23.4.2018, the rates of new TV channels have been fixed and the names of some Marathi TV channels have not been included in the list. Therefore, a proposal was under consideration of the government on the rate at which these channels should be given advertisements. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: The advertisements issued by the government are expected to be in Marathi. However, the Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity (DAVP) has The list of TV channels published under the order dated 23.4.2018 does not include the names of some Marathi TV channels. Therefore, the Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity is granting Government approval for a period of one year from 2018-19 till the new list/ rates are in place at the earlier rates approved by the Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity (DAVP) while giving advertisements to the channels which are not included in the new rates of DAVP. Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity (DAVP)
However, the advertisements to the TV channels covered in the order dated 23.04.2018 shall be provided at the rates as per the earlier agreed rates of Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity (DAVP).
