Conditions and terms are as follows:
The appointment to this post will be purely on contractual basis.
Employees of this post will not be considered as government employees.
The driver should be an Indian citizen and should be able to read/ write Marathi.
The age of the driver should be 18 years and not more than 58 years.
5.The driver should be accompanied by his/ her identity card (one of Aadhaar Card/ Voter ID/ PAN Card) besides his/ her driving license.
The driver provided by the organization should not be of criminal background/ tendency.
