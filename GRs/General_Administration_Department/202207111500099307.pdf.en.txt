Government order:
Government notification As on 13th March, 2018, the appointment to the post of Deputy Secretary in the Commission's office is made by the Service Entry Rules to be filled up by deputation from officers holding the post of Deputy Secretary in any department of the Ministry or holding the post equivalent to the post of Deputy Secretary in any office of the State Government. Also referenced. As per the Government decision dated 30.10.2019, 1 post of Deputy Secretary has been sanctioned for the Divisional Office of Maharashtra State Right to Service Commission, Nashik/ Amravati/ Nagpur respectively, which is currently vacant.
However, with effect from the date of this order, the additional charge of the vacant post of Deputy Secretary, State Service Commission, Nashik/ Amravati/ Nagpur is being assigned to the" Deputy Commissioner (General Administration)" in the office of Divisional Commissioner (Revenue), Nashik/ Amravati/ Nagpur respectively, until further orders.
