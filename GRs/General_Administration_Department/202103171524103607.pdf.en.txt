The Proposal:
The Legislature Restaurant and Square Food House, under the General Administration Department, has to buy food, raw materials and other materials during the Maharashtra Legislature session.
As per the provisions of Part I, Sub-Division 2, Sequence No. 11-A, Rule No. 34 of the Financial Powers Rule Book, the Administrative Department is authorized to draw funds up to Rs. 4.00 lakh on short payment. As per the said financial limit, the statement in Annexure-I of the Maharashtra Contingent Expenditure Rules, 1965
The amount can be withdrawn on short payment only for the matters mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 28/6/2005 of the Department of Finance. However, the matters for which funds are drawn by the Ministry of Food/ Restaurants are not covered in the Contingency Expenditure Rules and the Government Decision dated 28/6/2005, the funds are drawn by way of summary payment with the approval of the Finance Department at the time of each session.
The expenditure incurred for the session of the legislature is mandatory on a regular basis
Instead of seeking approval from the Finance Department, the government was considering giving financial powers to the Administrative Department to allow the Vidhan Bhavan Restaurant and Square Dining Room to bear the expenses incurred on the purchase of food, raw materials and other materials during the session of the Legislature and to recover the amount on a lump sum payment.
Government's decision:
It is hereby approved to authorize the Administrative Department to grant financial sanction to Vidhan Bhavan Restaurant and Square Dining Room under General Administration Department for meeting the expenditure incurred on purchase of food, raw material and other materials during the session of the Legislature and to recover the amount on short payment as per the accompanying Annexure under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-II.
