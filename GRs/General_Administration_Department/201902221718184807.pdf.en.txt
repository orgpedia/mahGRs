Introduction: Regarding the appointment of Aviation Management Service for the government aircraft Cessna Citation 560 XLS VT-VDD. Aero Aircraft Sales& Chartered Pvt Ltd was appointed on 1/1/2016 till 31/12/2018. After the contract was terminated. The government was considering a proposal to give an extension to Aircraft Sales and Chartered Pvt Ltd for three months or till the new tender process is completed. Aviation Ministry's proposal The Empowered Committee constituted under the Government Resolution dated 15/2/2018 of the General Administration Department to decide on matters related to the functioning of the Directorate General of Aviation under the State Government, headed by the Chief Secretary, has been approved in its meeting dated 1/1/2019.
In pursuance of the decision taken in the meeting of the Empowered Committee, the following decisions are being issued:
Government Decision: Government has decided to provide Aviation Management Service for the state-owned aircraft Cessna Citation 560 XLS VT-VDD. Aero Aircraft Sales and Chartered Pvt Ltd, New Delhi was appointed as the new director of the company. Aero Aircraft Sales and Chartered Pvt. Ltd., New Delhi for providing Aviation Management Services as per the tariff determined in the Government Resolution dated 23/3/2016, the existing contract is being extended for a period of three months or till the new tender process is completed.
