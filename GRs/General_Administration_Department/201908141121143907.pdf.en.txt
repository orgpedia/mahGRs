Introduction: Mumbai Transformation Support Unit (MTSU) was a Central Government organization working under Local Self Government since 2005. This organization was mainly working for the development of Mumbai. After the completion of the Mumbai development phase, the Mumbai Transformation Support Unit was closed by the Local Self-Government. However, as per the Government decision dated 31.08.2015, the Mumbai Transformation Support Unit was functioning as a separate entity after 30.06.2015 i.e. The Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) was providing short-term funding for the Mumbai Transformation Support Unit. Mr. B.C. (Retd.) was appointed as the Director of MTSU for the period from 01.07.2015 to 31.07.2017. However, all the projects being reviewed by MTSU are being reviewed by Ma. Since it is being conducted through the war room in the Chief Minister's office, it is no longer useful to continue MTSU. Accordingly, the matter of taking a decision on MTSU was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the Government has taken the following decisions in respect of Mumbai Transformation Support Unit (MTSU).
As various ambitious projects related to Mumbai city are being reviewed directly through the war room in the Chief Minister's Office, the MTSU is being terminated with effect from August 31, 2019.
The term of office of Shri B.C. Khatua as Director, MTSU has expired on 31st July, 2017. Therefore, the appointment of Shri B.C. Khatua as Director, MTSU stands terminated with effect from 31st July, 2017.
3) The MTSU should send the detailed utilization certificate of the funds given by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) to the MTSU for the purpose for which the MTSU has been utilized directly to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) and submit the report to the government.
