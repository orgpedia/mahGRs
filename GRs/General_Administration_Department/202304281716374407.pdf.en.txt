Complete control, management and administration of the Chief Minister's Charitable Endowment Fund. The Chief Minister is in the chair. The Chief Minister's Charitable Endowment Fund will benefit 865 villages in the Maharashtra-Karnataka border areas, as well as social, educational, cultural, charitable or similar public or semi-public institutions or organizations in the state of Maharashtra. The financial assistance will be provided as per the Chief Minister's discretion.
