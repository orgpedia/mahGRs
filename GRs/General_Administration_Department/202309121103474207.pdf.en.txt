Government's decision:
753 posts in the General Administration Department (itself) and the department's ministerial level offices
The revised figure has been issued vide Government Resolution No. 1 dated 17th January, 2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments by the Finance Department, 281 temporary posts of various cadres on the establishment of General Administration Department (itself) and four other offices under this Department have been extended from 1st March, 2023 to 31st August, 2023 under the Government Decision No. 2 dated 17th February, 2023.
Now the government is approving the extension of 281 temporary posts on the establishment of the General Administration Department (itself) and four other offices under this department from September 1, 2023 to February 29, 2024. The formation orders of 281 temporary posts, including 106 on the establishment of the General Administration Department (itself), 1 on the office of the Civil Defense Committee, 11 on the ministerial establishment, 12 on the establishment of the Controller of Accommodation, 151 on the office of the Chief Electoral Officer, have been shown in the" Returns A" and" Returns B" along with the details of the headings. The expenditure on these posts shall be covered under the head of accounts shown in column-5 of the relevant return A and shall be met from the approved grants for the year 2023-24.
