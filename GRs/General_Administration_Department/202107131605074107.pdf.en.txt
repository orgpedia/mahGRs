As many as 28 applicants from Osmanabad and Latur districts, who had applied for the state government's freedom fighter pension for their participation in the Hyderabad liberation movement, have submitted jail certificates and verification reports of the Gulbarga Central Prison as evidence. The government letter As on 09.11.2017, based on the final report submitted by the State Crime Investigation Department regarding the said applicants, it was directed to conduct an in-depth inquiry through a divisional level committee and submit a report with a recommendation. Accordingly, as per the report received from the State Crime Investigation Department, Maharashtra State, Pune, the cases of some of the applicants are not traceable to Kalaburagi (Gulbarga), Karnataka Jail. As per the directions of the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad, and the committee chairman, a team was formed to verify the documents submitted by the applicants from the available records in Kalaburagi jail. Accordingly, a team appointed by the District Collector, Osmanabad. On 14.11.2019, the Central Prison, Kalaburagi (Gulbarga), Karnataka has examined the records and submitted a report on 11 cases.
