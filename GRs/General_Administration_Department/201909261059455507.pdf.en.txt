the prelude
Under the Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP) to be implemented for the candidates selected through the State Service Examination, the Government has decided to provide a Post Graduate Degree Course (M.A.in Dev. Administration) has been started in coordination with the University of Mumbai. In the annexure to this Government Decision, it has been explained how to implement postgraduate degree courses for those candidates who have undergone training under CPTP, but due to cadre upgradation after re-selection through the State Service Examination, they are going to re-join training in the new post. However, it has been observed that in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 19 and Annexure to the said Government Decision, certain technical difficulties may arise in respect of semester-wise examination, training assessment, award of Master's degree, etc. of candidates who have completed their Master's degree course under the Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP) and are due to rejoin the CPTP training batch due to cadre upgradation. Therefore, a proposal to amend the said Annexure was under consideration.

Instead, paragraph 12 will remain as follows.
The officers of the cadre under the Home Department will be trained as per the PG course under CPTP as follows.
(a) The course of Deputy Superintendent of Police/ Assistant Commissioner of Police, Group-A, under the Home Department, will be completed through Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik. The Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik has been assigned the responsibility of conducting training and examination of the above mentioned cadres in semester 2 and 3. This includes conducting course sessions for probation officers, conducting final examinations and viva voce as well as compiling and evaluating project reports submitted by trainees, etc. In this regard, the Maharashtra Police Academy should seek guidance from the curriculum board of Mumbai University through Yashda, the institute.
(b) Provision is being made for training of Superintendents (State Excise), Group-A, Deputy Superintendents (State Excise), Group-B, Assistant Commissioners (State Excise), Group-B and Assistant Regional Transport Officers, Group-B under the cadre of Sub-Inspectors under the Home Department.
The training of the probationary officers of the cadre of Superintendent (State Excise), Group-A, Deputy Superintendent (State Excise), Group-B, Assistant Commissioner (State Excise), Group-B, and Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Group-B will be completed through Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik. The curriculum of this session will also include field training, weapon handling, training in forensic laboratory, etc. Further, the final written examination will also have to cover the criminal investigation procedure, criminal laws, rules and procedures and matters relating to the functioning of this cadre such as Indian Penal Code, Code of Criminal Procedure, Indian Evidence Act etc. as may be determined by the Home Department from time to time. The Maharashtra Police Academy is being assigned the responsibility of conducting training and examinations for the said cadres in the course session 2.
