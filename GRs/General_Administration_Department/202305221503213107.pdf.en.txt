The Proposal:
The General Administration Department has prepared the" Office Procedure Manual" containing consolidated instructions on the manner in which the administrative departments of the Ministry should conduct their official business. Section 79 of the Rules of Procedure of this Office provides for compilation and updating of important orders issued in every financial year.
The memorandum will contain government orders, government circulars and government memorandums. Also, in line with the computerization policy of the Government of Maharashtra, the Information and Technology Department of the General Administration Department aims to bring dynamism and transparency in the administration and provide easy and fast services to the citizens through computers.
