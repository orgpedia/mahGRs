Circular:
Guidelines for installation of Statues of National Heroes/ Great personalities have been prescribed in the Government Decision, S.P.V. dated 2nd May, 2017 and the Government Preamble dated 6th May, 2017. As per the representations received from various organizations/ institutions, the Government/ District Collector has accorded approval from time to time for erecting the statue. The maintenance of the approved statues by the statue-building agencies is required in accordance with this guideline.
However, the Collector should contact all the concerned agencies and ensure that all the approved statues and the parks surrounding them are in good condition.
