GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In order to reduce the congestion in public transport system as a preventive measure to prevent the spread of Corona Virus (COVID-19) in the offices of the State and to prevent the infection of the officers/ employees of the State, the Department was instructed to take measures in accordance with paragraph No. 2-A of the Government Decision dated March 18, 2020 to ensure 50 percent attendance of the total officers/ employees in the office.
However, as a more urgent measure to prevent the spread of coronavirus through public transport, the Government has taken the following decisions:
All government, semi-government and municipal offices in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region, Pune, Pimpri-Chinchwad and Nagpur city will have 25 per cent attendance. For this, the secretary of the administrative department of the ministry/ municipal commissioner/ head of the department concerned should take necessary measures immediately.
(b) However, the presence of all officers/ employees of Government/ semi-Government/ Municipal offices in the areas mentioned in paragraph 2A, providing essential services to the citizens, shall be mandatory.

The Epidemic Diseases Prevention Act, 1897 has come into force with effect from 13th March, 2020 vide Government Notification dated 14th March, 2020 of the Department of Public Health. As per the provisions of the said Act, all private establishments in Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Area, Pune, Pimpri-Chinchwad and Nagpur City shall remain closed from 21st March, 2020 to 31st March, 2020. Grocery stores, vegetables, fruits, medicines and medical services will remain open. In case of any ambiguity regarding essential services, the concerned District Collector will take appropriate decision/ clarification.
