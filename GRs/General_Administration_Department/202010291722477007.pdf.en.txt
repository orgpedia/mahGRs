Clerk-typists in the Ministry Department have been given ad-hoc promotion to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the ad-hoc selection list for the year 2019-2020. The above Mr. S. C. Mane, Clerk- Typist has declined the promotion given to him.
Since Mr. Mane has refused the promotion given to him, the General Administration Department has issued Government Resolution No. SRV-2015/ PR. 303/ K-12, dated 12-09-2016, their names are being removed from the Adhoc Selection List for the year 2019-2020. Similarly, Mr. Mane's name will not be considered in the selection lists for the next two years i.e. 2020-2021 and 2021-2022, but his eligibility will be checked for promotion to the post of Assistant Cell Officer in the third year i.e. 2022-2023 selection list.
303/ K-12, dated 12-09, No.8 of 2016 regarding withdrawal of benefits under Assured Progress Scheme due to denial of promotion by Mr. S. C. Mane, Clerk-Typist, should be dealt with by the concerned Ministerial Department as per the orders issued by the Finance Department from time to time.
