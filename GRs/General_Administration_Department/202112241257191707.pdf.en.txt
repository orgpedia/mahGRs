the order
Shri Prahlad Shankar Rode, Additional Secretary, Water Resources is being appointed on deputation for a period of one year initially to the post of" Chief Executive Officer" on establishment of Smart City Project, Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation, G.
(a) Mr. Prahlad Shankar Rode, Under-Secretary shall commence the service of the Deputy Secretary on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall cease to hold office on the date on which he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Department of Water Resources and the Department of Urban Development may, after considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II and III) of the Rules, 1981, mutually decide the terms and conditions for the deputation of Shri Prahlad Shankar Rode.
3. All other administrative matters of Shri Prahlad Shankar Rode, Under Secretary will be handled by Water Resources Department.
