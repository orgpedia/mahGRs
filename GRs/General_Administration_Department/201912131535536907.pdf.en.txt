Circular:
The birth anniversaries and National Days of the great men and women of the country in the year 2020 shall be celebrated in the Ministries and all Government/ Paramilitary offices as per the schedule attached.
The programmes mentioned in the Annexure shall fall on public holidays, Sundays or second and fourth Saturdays of the month and shall be celebrated as per the change of programme suggested by the Central Government in this regard, otherwise they shall be celebrated on the same day.
The Divisional Commissioner/ Collector shall issue necessary instructions regarding the conduct of the said programme in all the government/ semi-government offices of his department/ district and take appropriate action regarding their implementation.
