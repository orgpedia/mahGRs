Preamble: - The cost of construction of the planned building of the office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission at Belapur, CBD has been approved by the Administration vide Government Decision dated 9.11.2022 at No.1, Bacha, for an amount of Rs. 282,25,00, 000 / - (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Two Crore Twenty Five Lakhs only). It provided for a sum of Rs. 10,01,75, 250 / - (Rupees Ten Crore One Lakh Seventy Five Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty Only) for furniture work.
However, keeping in view the sensitivity of the Commission's office, confidentiality as well as the specific nature of the work, all the aspects of the present and future requirements of the Commission have been taken into consideration and revised meeting arrangements have been made in the building. As a result, the previous budget has been revised to Rs. 17,15,58, 770 / -. In this regard, a letter dated 6th January, 2026 from the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Panvel, No. 2 has submitted to the Commission a revised detailed budget of Rs. 27,17,34, 020 / - for the furniture as per the building seating arrangement plan approved by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
Accordingly, the Government was considering approving the revised budget of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for the construction of a new building at CBD Belapur at a cost of Rs. 27,17,34, 020 / - (Rupees Twenty Seven Crore Seventy Seven Lakh Thirty Four Thousand Twenty Only).
The Government's Decisions:
Based on the modus operandi, confidentiality, requirements and budget approved by the Public Works Department of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the original administrative sanction of Rs. 10,01,75, 250 / - (Rupees Ten Crore One Lakh Seventy Five Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty Only) for PUA work in the office of the Commission is being revised.
