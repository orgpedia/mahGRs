Preamble: Under the revised pattern of 271 posts for a total of 26 cadres in the office of Maharashtra Public Service Commission. 1 Approval has been granted vide Government Resolution dated 25th January, 2022. Under this framework, the posts of System Analyst-1, Data Entry Operator-3 and Programmer-2 have been sanctioned in line with the Information Technology activities of the Commission. Out of these, the posts of System Analyst and Data Entry Operator are still vacant due to service access rules and other reasons. Out of these sanctioned posts, 1 post of System Analyst and 3 posts of Data Entry Operator are equivalent in nature to the above mentioned No. 5 In the Government decision dated September 27, 2022 of the Department of Information and Technology here, in Annexure B, it was under the consideration of the Government to allow the services of a total of 4 technical manpower, namely 1 post of System Support Engineer and 3 posts of IT Office Assistant, to be filled through external agencies for a period of 11 months. Accordingly, the following decisions were taken:
Government's decision
As per the approval given by the Sub-Committee of the Finance Department in its meeting dated 5 th August, 2022, by this Government Decision, in place of 1 post of System Analyst and 3 posts of Data Entry Operator in the approved format of Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the nature of work equivalent to these posts will be changed.
