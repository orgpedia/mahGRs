Government's decision
It has been prescribed under the above order that the officers/ employees of the State Government shall submit their statements of remuneration and liabilities every year. Accordingly, the employees are required to submit their statement of remuneration and liabilities as on 31st March of every year to the prescribed authority by 31st May of that year. The above provision has also been made applicable to all the semi-government institutions, Panchayat Raj Institutions, Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats, Statutory Bodies, Public Undertakings, Corporations, Boards under the various administrative departments of the State Government.
At present, the corona virus (COVID 19) is spreading in the state. A lockdown has been declared by the government to prevent the further spread of the virus. The administrative machinery is engaged in various tasks such as taking measures to prevent the spread of coronavirus infection, providing medical assistance to the citizens, dealing with the law and order situation, ensuring the availability of essential commodities to the citizens. Restrictions have also been imposed on the office attendance of the employees to prevent the spread of the virus in government offices and to prevent the officers/ employees from getting infected. Therefore, it will not be possible to submit the Statement of Obligations and Liabilities within the time limit prescribed under the aforesaid orders.
In view of the above situation, the time limit prescribed under the aforesaid orders for furnishing of periodic statements of accounts of interest and liabilities for each year up to 31st May, 2020 is being relaxed as a special case for this year. It is hereby notified that the officers/ employees of the State Government and all other institutions where the above orders are in force shall submit their statement of remuneration and liabilities as on March, 2020 to the prescribed authorities by 31st August, 2020.
