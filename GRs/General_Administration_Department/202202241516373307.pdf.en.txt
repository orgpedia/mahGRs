Government order:
Shri Swadheen Kshatriya, who is working as State Chief Commissioner of Right to Service, is 65 years old. His tenure in the said post as per the provision of Section 14 (1) of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, expires on January 25, 2022. It will expire on January 25, 2022. As a result, the post of State Chief Service Rights Commissioner has become vacant. Shri Dilip Mohanrao Shinde, State Right to Service Commissioner, Pune, will hold the additional charge of the post of State Chief Right to Service Commissioner, till the appointment of a regular incumbent to the said post. The government is giving approval to handover from February 21, 2022, till further orders.
Abhay Budhadev Yavalkar, State Right to Service Commissioner, Revenue Department, Nagpur, has been given the additional charge of the post. The Government is granting approval for handing over from 21st February, 2022 till further orders.
