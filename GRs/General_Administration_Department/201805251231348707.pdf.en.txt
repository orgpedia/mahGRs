2. All the Ministries/ Departments to ensure that the transfer orders are implemented promptly and efficiently.
The following instructions shall be strictly followed: (i) On receipt of transfer orders, all the Departments shall relieve the transferred employees as on 31.05.2018 (M.No.) without waiting for the arrival of the replacement staff. The establishment officer of the department concerned shall be held liable in case of non-payment of work and payment of allowances due to non-recruitment of an employee. (3) If any employee directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the employee to change the transfer order, such employee shall be liable for disciplinary action.
(4) In order to ensure effective implementation of these transfers, the salaries of all the transferred employees as on 31.05.2018 shall be deducted by the department in which they are presently working.
The salary from 01.06.2018 should not be withdrawn from the department before the transfer under any circumstances. After the concerned employee appears in the transfer department, the post-transfer department should draw the salary and submit the report to the General Administration Department. dated 01.06.2018
Instructions are being given to all the administrative departments that if the drawing and disbursing officer of the department working before the transfer is paid, he will be held personally responsible. The remuneration of all the transferred employees as on 31.05.2018 is to be paid by the existing department and the remuneration from 01.06.2018 is to be paid by the transferred department.
Employees transferred by the Department w.e.f. 31.05.2018. All the concerned should note that if the work is not done till No.), action will be taken by the General Administration Department in accordance with the provisions of Government Decision No. 186/16/ Q. 14, dated 23.12.2016.
4. Government Circular No. regarding conduct of post-transfer training to all cadre officers/ employees of Ministries/ Departments as per State Training Policy:
Notices have been issued vide No. 1116/ Q. No. 32/14-B dated 23.05.2016.
Accordingly, the post-transfer training of the transferred employees in the department should be organized by the concerned ministerial departments.
