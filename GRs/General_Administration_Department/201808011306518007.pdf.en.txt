The Proposal:
The government has referred. As per the Government Order dated 20.11.2015, the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, in Part II, provide financial powers to the General Administration (Self) and Head of Department/ Head of Office under the administrative control of the Department. The Government have decided to sanction a sum of Rs. 3, 4 and 5 each to the Principal Secretary and the Public Prosecution Officer for sanction of expenditure for the celebration of Republic Day (26th January), Maharashtra Day (1st May) and Independence Day (15th August) respectively. 25 lakh has been provided. The proposal to increase the financial limit was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Principal Secretary and Chief Administrative Officer are required to pay Rs. 25 lakh has been provided under the Government Resolution dated 20th November, 2015.
