Preamble: The requisition for 22 posts of Zila Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A was sent to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission under the General Administration Department, Letter No. 87/28, dated 29/09/2021. Accordingly, the Commission has issued Advertisement No. Candidates were recommended for 11 of the 22 seats advertised under 259/2021. Out of the 11 recommended candidates, Smt Jyoti Motiram Gulve (Open Woman 1) did not appear for document verification. Also, their medical examination certificates and character verification certificates were not received. Accordingly, assuming that they will not join, the remaining 10 candidates for the post of District Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A have been appointed under the Government Order of Reference No. 2 in accordance with the prescribed rules regarding appointment.
Maharashtra Public Service Commission vide letter No. 3 Mr. President. Ananda Sharad Patharkar (Arakhiv) has been recommended on merit as there is no Arakhiv woman in the reserved post of Arakhiv from the waiting list of Sarad Seva Zila Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A instead of Gullave (Khula Mahila 1). Accordingly, the Government was considering to appoint the said candidate to the post of District Sainik Welfare Officer by obtaining the option of Revenue Department from the said candidate as per the notification dated 14/6/2021 of the General Administration Department's Revenue Department Allotment-Rules, 2021 regarding the appointment to the Maharashtra Government Group' A' and Group' B' (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) posts by direct service and promotion.
Government Order: The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has recommended Mr. Ananda Sharad Patharkar (Arakheev) for the post of Zila Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A, as per the Seventh Commission Pay Level S-18: Rs. 49,100-1, 55,800 as per the definition of the term under the purview prescribed in Rule 9 (43) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (General Terms and Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981.

2.The temporary appointment is being made subject to the following terms and conditions from the date on which Shri Anand Sharad Patharkar assumes charge of this post.
1) Mr. Patharkar is required to join the said post within a period of 30 days from the date of the order of appointment. If they do not join within this period, they will lose the seniority given to them by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and their appointment orders will be cancelled without any prior notice.
If Mr. Patharkar is not able to join in exceptional circumstances within the prescribed period of 30 days for appointment, he should immediately submit an application to the Government with justifiable reasons. After examining the matter, it is necessary to extend the time for joining, once the situation is confirmed, a decision will be taken at the government level to extend the time for them to appear in the appointed post. However, after such extension, the candidate will lose the seniority as admissible to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in order of merit.
3) Mr. Patharkar is required to bear the travel expenses for accepting the appointment.
4) General Administration Department, giving the decision of the government. The new probationary policy of June 22, 2021 will be applicable to Mr. Patharkar. Their probation period will be two years. If their work and conduct during the probation period is not satisfactory, their government service will be terminated without any prior notice.
5) Mr. Patharkar's appointment will be subject to verification of Military Service Records. This service record verification should be done by the Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune. The Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune should ensure that the verification report is satisfactory/ unsatisfactory and submit the same to the Government at the earliest.
Before appointment, all the certificates of Mr. Patharkar should be verified by the Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune. Shri Patharkar, who has been recommended by the Commission for the post of District Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A, has been selected from the category of" Arakhiv" and hence his caste claim verification is not required.
7) The Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune shall ensure that the aforesaid requirements are met before issuing the detailed appointment order of Shri Patharkar and report the same to the Government.
8) The report on verification of antecedents and conduct of Mr. Patharkar has been received and is satisfactory.
Mr. Patharkar, who has been appointed as Zila Sainik Welfare Officer, Group-A, has been medically examined and his medical report has been received and he is medically fit for government service.
As per the rules regarding the Hindi and Marathi language examination, if Mr. Patharkar has not passed the said examination earlier or he has not been exempted from passing the said examination, then he will be required to pass the said examination.
