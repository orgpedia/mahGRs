Date of release: September 14, 2021
GOVERNMENT Circular: It is hereby informed to all the administrative departments of the Ministry that while transferring and posting officers/ employees in all government offices, it should be ensured that the spouse or close relatives of the concerned government official are not appointed directly under the authority of the concerned government official.
However, if it is unavoidable to make such an appointment, the General Administration Department, Government Circular No. CFR 1221/ Q.No.61/ K.A.13D. Suggestions submitted as of July 12, 2021 should be considered.
