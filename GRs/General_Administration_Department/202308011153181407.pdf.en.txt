Preamble: The Good Governance Committee was constituted under the Government Resolution dated 27 th May, 2022 to prepare a draft of the Manual of Good Governance for making governance accountable, accessible, clean, transparent and dynamic and to submit the report of the Committee to the Government.
The Committee has submitted its report to the Chief Secretary on 28.02.2023. The report was approved by the government on May 19, 2023, and the government was considering publishing the report on the government website in order to take action on the report of the committee.
