Preamble: Subject to the provisions of Rule 29, Rule 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979 and the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure, Schedule I and Schedule II of the said Rules and the Constitution and Rules of the" New Model Rules," the provisions of the said Rules shall apply to the Government Employees (Non-Industrial) Associations. The Maharashtra Sales Tax Group D Employees Association, Mumbai was recognized by the Government under the Government Decision No. 1. The Goods and Services Tax (GST) has replaced the sales tax. On this concept, the sales tax department of Maharashtra was named. The name of the Department has been changed to" Department of Goods and Services Tax" with effect from 1.7.2017. Reference No. """ Under Section 2 of the Act, pursuant to the proposal submitted by the Finance Department, a proposal to change the name of Maharashtra Sales Tax Group D Employees Association, Mumbai to\"" Maharashtra Goods and Services Tax Group D Employees Association Mumbai\"" was under consideration of the Government."""
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Sales Tax Group D Employees Association, Mumbai, a Government Employees (Non-Industrial) Association, is registered at No. 3 This Government has decided to change the name of Maharashtra Goods and Services Tax Group D Employees Association to" Maharashtra Goods and Services Tax Group D Employees Association, Mumbai" in accordance with the procedure laid down in the Government Circular here.
As per the above approval, the organization is only changing its name and the original government approval remains intact. 1 It is necessary to mention the number of the government approval here and the number of this government decision under it.
