Preamble: Proposal for appointment of a new agency for Aviation Management Services for aircraft Cessna Citation 560 XLS VT-VDD and Government Helicopter Airbus H145 VT GOV This was approved by the Empowered Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary in its meeting dated 13.08.2019. It was also approved to entrust the responsibility of the new tender process to Maharashtra Aircraft Development Company. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Airport Development Company has completed the tender process in March-2023 and is expected to be completed by May 2023. Aero Aircraft Sales and Charters Pvt Ltd, New Delhi accepted the tender as it was the lowest bidder and they expressed their willingness to work as per the terms and conditions of the tender.
Thereafter, the Director, Aviation, took over his post. The agreement was signed with Aero Aircraft Sales and Charters Pvt Ltd, New Delhi for a period of three years with effect from 1/4/2023.
The Director, Directorate General of Civil Aviation, Mumbai, vide this Memorandum of Agreement, Reference No. 2 was submitted to the government. However, the proposal was not approved by the government. This was submitted in the meeting dated 03/10/2023 of the Empowered Committee headed by the Chief Secretary. The Empowered Committee in its meeting has decided to give ex-post facto approval to the agreement entered into by the Director, Directorate General of Civil Aviation, Mumbai with Aero Aircraft Sales and Chartered Pvt Ltd, New Delhi for providing management services for the aircraft Cessna Citation 560 XLS VT-VDD and Government Helicopter Airbus H145 VT GOV.
