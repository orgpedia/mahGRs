Preamble: In accordance with the provisions of Section 13 of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015. As per the notification dated 30th July, 2016, the Maharashtra State Commission for Right to Service has been reduced. General Administration Department. As per the Government decision dated 20th March, 2017, pursuant to the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I, Roll No. 1, Rule No. 2, the Deputy Secretary in the office of the State Chief Commissioner of Service Rights has been declared as the" Head of Department" of the Maharashtra State Commission for Service Rights in accordance with the financial powers conferred on the administrative department under the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959.
In order to ensure the regular functioning of the office of the State Service Rights Commissioner, Konkan Revenue Department, the Joint Secretary, State Service Rights Commission, Konkan Revenue Department has been declared as the head of the department. The request was made on October 26, 2021. It was referred to the office of the State Chief Service Commissioner. The agreement was signed on November 6, 2021. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The decision was taken by the Finance Ministry. In accordance with the Financial Powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959, as per Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part I, Sub-Division-I in the Annexure annexed as of 17th April, 2015, the Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary in the office of the State Service Rights Commissioner, Konkan Division (if the post of Deputy Secretary is upgraded to the rank of Joint Secretary in future) is being declared as the" Head of Department" in the office of the State Service Rights Commission, Konkan Revenue Division.
