The Proposal: -
The goal of the state government is to create a system based on policies and public welfare schemes. "Jai Maharashtra" is based on interviews with ministers, senior government officials, and experts on various subjects related to the government.
The programme is produced by the Directorate General. The show was aired on Doordarshan's Sayadri channel.
It was broadcast on Tuesdays and Fridays. However, the show was taken off the air during the COVID-19 period. At present, the programme is being telecast by the Directorate General through social media.
The show has been re-telecast on Doordarshan to reach out to more and more people.
The issue of starting broadcasting from the channel was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Considering the proposal made under the letter dated 3.11.2023 under the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, the programme "Jai Maharashtra" should be re-telecast on Doordarshan's Syadri channel.
