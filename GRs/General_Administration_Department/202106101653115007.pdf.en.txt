The Maharashtra State Information Commission is headquartered in Mumbai and has eight offices, one each in Brihanmumbai, Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur.
The vacancies in various cadres indicated in the following Return-1 in the office of the State Information Commission are to be filled by deputation. All the posts mentioned below in the office of the Commission are considered to be vacant.
Officers/ staff whose period of deputation has expired, officers/ staff whose period of deputation is 5 years or more, posts in the cadre which have been filled on contract basis, officers/ staff whose services have been repatriated/ relieved unilaterally.

The Service Admission Rules provide that appointments to the posts of Secretary, Deputy Secretary, Cell Officer and Assistant Cell Officer in the office of the State Information Commission, Upper Grade Short Writer, Lower Grade Short Writer, Clerk-Typist, etc. shall be made by the appointment of an eligible person holding the post concerned in any department of the Ministry or holding the post equivalent to the post concerned in any office of the State Government.
6. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring this circular to the notice of all the field offices under their administrative control. The officers/ staff desirous of going on deputation to the above mentioned vacant posts should submit their applications to the concerned Administrative Department of the Ministry through their Head of Office and in no case should apply directly to this Department. No action will be taken on the application received directly.
After receiving the applications of the interested officers/ employees, the applications should be scrutinized in accordance with the provisions of the relevant government decision and the proposal for full deputation of the concerned officers/ employees with the approval of the Secretary of the concerned department along with the information in the attached Form-1 should be sent to this office in such a way (not to be sent to the registration branch) that it reaches by July 31, 2021.
The name of the selected officer/ employee cannot be withdrawn. Also, if any officer/ employee from your department is not willing to go for deputation to the said post, then also please be informed by 31st July, 2021.
