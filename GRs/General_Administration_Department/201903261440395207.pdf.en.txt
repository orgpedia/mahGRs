Government circular
The provisional additional provisional seniority of Senior Personal Assistants in the Ministry Department as on 01.01.2019 is annexed herewith as Annexure A. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
3. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring to the notice of all the employees (including those on leave/ deputation) in the Senior Personal Assistant cadre of your Department the present date of 01.01.2019 of the date of submission of additional provisional seniority. In this regard, the objections/ errors received from the concerned senior personal assistants should be examined as per the records of the department and the statements with the opinion of the department should be sent to this department by 30.04.2019. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
