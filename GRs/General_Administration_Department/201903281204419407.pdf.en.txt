Now, under the Governing Decision dated 21/02/2019 of the Department of Finance, the Administrative Departments have been permitted to continue the temporary posts from 01/03/2019 to 30/09/2019.
As per the decision taken by the Director, Institute of Military Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad vide their letter dated 22/02/2019, it is proposed that a total of 5 temporary posts on the establishment of the Institute of Military Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad are not vacant for more than six months and it is necessary to continue those posts, the Government is approving the continuation of the following total 5 temporary posts on the establishment of the Institute of Military Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad for a period of 6 months from 01/03/2019 to 30/09/2019.
