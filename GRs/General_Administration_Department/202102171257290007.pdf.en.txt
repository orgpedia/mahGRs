Reference 1) Government Decision General Administration Department (Restaurants) No.Aastha 2017/ PR No. 495/17/ MANUUKA, dated 8/02/2018 2) Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 3) Notification dated 4/01/2021 by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi 4) Government Decision, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance to Persons with Disabilities No. 2019/ PR. 251/ D.C. 2, dated 02/02/2021 Preamble The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 has come into force with effect from 28th December, 2016. As per Rule 33 of the said Act, the posts in the government service are to be ensured for the disabled. The Central Government has revised the list of posts for persons with disabilities notified vide notification dated 4/01/2021 and published the same on the website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in. Accordingly, instructions have been issued under the Government Order dated 2/02/2021 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities.
Accordingly, as per the Central Government notification dated 4/01/2021, the matter of reviewing the posts reserved for persons with disabilities on the establishment of Mantralaya Restaurants/ Eateries was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
The revised order is being issued by superseding the Government decision in Reference No. 1 regarding ensuring suitable posts for persons with disabilities in the posts in the establishment of Mantralaya Restaurant, Vidhan Bhavan Restaurant, Konkan Bhavan Restaurant and Square Restaurant under the General Administration Department.
As per the provisions of the Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Ministry shall fill up the posts in Group-D cadre of Restaurants/ Restaurants in direct service on par with the posts in the list prescribed for reservation for persons with disabilities as per the notification dated 4/01/2021 issued by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India.

The posts are being ensured for persons with disabilities as indicated in Annexure-' A' accompanying this Government decision.
In accordance with Section 34 of the Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Ministry under the General Administration Department is recruiting direct service in Group D posts in the establishment of restaurants/ eating houses with at least 40% disability (a) Blindness/ short-sightedness, (b) Hearing or hearing impairment, (c) Cerebral Palsy/ Leprosy cured/ Dwarfism/ Acid attack victims/ Muscular Dystrophy, (d) Autism/ Also, the post which is ensured for Divyang at the lower (feeder cadre) level should automatically be considered as ensured for the said category of Divyang for the post of promotion.
This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the instructions given under the Government Decision dated 02/02/2021 dated Social Justice and Special Assistance to Divyangjan No. 2019/ PR No. 251/ Dec.2.
