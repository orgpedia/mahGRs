Circular
In view of the decision to organize the Vigilance Awareness Week every year in the state, it has been decided to organize the Vigilance Awareness Week every year. It has been taken up by Government Order dated 22nd October, 2001. In November of this year,
The Central Vigilance Commission has announced that Vigilance Awareness Week will be observed from 27th October, 2025 to 2nd 2025. The theme for this week this year is "Vigilance: Our Shared Responsibility."
Accordingly, this year in the state also. Vigilance Awareness Week is scheduled to be held from 27th October, 2025 to 2nd November, 2025. Suggestions in this regard are as follows:
a) Vigilance Awareness Week should be organized during the above period by all the Departments of the State Government and all the Heads of Departments / Offices under their control, activities adopted by the State Government, Cooperative Societies, Autonomous Bodies.
