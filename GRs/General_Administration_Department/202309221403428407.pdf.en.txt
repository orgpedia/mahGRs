The Proposal:
The Government has taken a policy decision to start the Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP) of probationary officers of various cadres who are directly recruited in Group A and Group B through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission through the State Service Examination through training institutes at Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur respectively. Also Reference No. 6 It has been decided to conduct the postgraduate degree course for probationary officers undergoing training under the Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP) in collaboration with Yashoda, Pune and Gokhale Institute of Politics and Economics from the batch" CPTP-9."
Training of candidates who have been recommended on the basis of the final result of the State Service Examination-2021 conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission through the Combined Probationary Training Programme-9 (CPTP-9).
The batch will commence from 03.10.2023. In this regard, the matter of issuing guidelines for the candidates joining this training program, the training institutions and the concerned administrative departments was under the consideration of the government.
Accordingly, the following recommendations are made:

Government Circular: The following instructions are being given regarding various issues regarding the Combined Probationary Training Programme-9 (CPTP-9) to be conducted for the candidates recommended under the final result of the State Service Examination-2021.
(i) Guidelines for commencement of training: 1. Out of the candidates joining for training of CPTP-9 batch, 137 candidates of Group-A cadre and 263 candidates of Group-B cadre have been screened for their original certificates from 03.07.2023 to 07.07.2023 at Yashada Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur respectively. Out of these, 136 candidates belonging to Group-A and 250 candidates belonging to Group-B have been appointed as Probationary Officers under CPTP-9 with effect from 03.10.2023 subject to verification of their character, medical examination and educational and other necessary documents by the concerned agencies and subject to the said inspection/ verification reports.
All probationary officers of Group-A cadre of CPTP-9. On 03.10.2023, all Probationary Officers of Group-B cadre should attend training at Yashda, Pune and at Vanamati, Nagpur. The candidates who are going to join the training of CPTP-9 in the service of the State Government, they should be relieved from the offices where they are currently working to join the training institute on 03.10.2023 (before midday).
The Government will take a decision in accordance with the candidates who are going to join the training of CPTP-9 batch in the service of the State Government, General Administration Department Number SRV-1092/ PR No. 1033/ PR. The 1 month notice period prescribed vide No. 33/92/8, dated 2.12.1997 is being relaxed. Also, the period from the date of retirement of the candidates in the government service till the commencement of the training will be regularized as the period of duty in the previous post.
(ii) Regarding the salary of Probationary Officers: 1. In order to ensure that the probationary officers of the CPTP-9 batch receive their salary and other incidental allowances, the administrative departments concerned shall take steps to register these officers in the service system, obtain their number for DCPS and appoint Drawing and Disbursing Officers for their salary, etc. within a period of four weeks from the beginning of the training. The probationary officers should be provided regular pay of the concerned post from the first month of training.
The administrative departments should take action to create more number of posts for the candidates joining the CPTP-9 batch from the date of commencement of training, taking into account the number of candidates joining the training.
(iii) Regarding Bonds to be taken from Probationary Officers: The Probationary Officers joining for training of CPTP-9 batch will be required to furnish Bonds as per the provisions of Government Resolution, General Administration Department No: TRN 0514/ PR No. 7/14/ 18-B, dated 28th February, 2017 before joining the training.

(iv) Exemption for candidates who have completed their Master's Degree course under Combined Probationary Training Programme: If the candidates who have completed their Master's Degree course under Combined Probationary Training Programme have been recommended for other cadres through the State Service Examination-2021 and the concerned candidates are willing to take up the new recommended post, the necessary action regarding exemption of such candidates from the stage of training as mentioned in Roll No. 3 and Roll No. 4 above shall be taken by the Training Institute at Yashda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur.
(v) Service Excellence of Probationary Officers: The service excellence of Probationary Officers of other cadres, excluding the Probationary Officers of the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Police/ Assistant Commissioner of Police, Group-A, shall be determined as specified in the Maharashtra Civil Services [Determination of the service excellence of the candidates who are selected for the post of Group' A" and Group' B" (Gazetted) through the State Civil Services Examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, and are appointed directly and trained under the Combined Probationary Training Programme] Rules, 2015, dated 7.11.2015 and dated 31.12.2018.
The Service Excellence of Probationary Officers in the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Police/ Assistant Commissioner of Police, Group-A [to determine the service excellence of the candidates who are selected for the post of Group' A" and Group' B" (Gazetted) through the State Civil Services Examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and who are directly recruited and trained under the Combined Probationary Training Programme] Rules, 2015, dated 7.09.2018.
(vi) Service Book of Probationary Officers: Document Verification of Probationary Officers undergoing training under CPTP-9 at Training Institutes at Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur. 6 and No. 7 has been done in the presence of nodal officers as per the circular mentioned here. The process of preparing the service books of probationary officers joining the said batch should be done by the concerned administrative departments within a period of four weeks from the beginning of the training. Necessary coordination will be done through Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur.
(viii) Implementation of Post-Graduate Course Programme Provision/ guidelines have been issued by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission under the Government Decision at Annexure 3 and Annexure 4 above regarding the implementation of Post-Graduate Course under Combined Probationary Training Programme for the officers of Group-A and Group-B selected through State Service Examination. The provisions and guidelines of this Government Order shall be applicable to probationary officers undergoing training in CPTP-9 batch. Further, as per the Government Circular No.6, the Departmental Examinations Rules, 2018, dated 13.04.2018, will be applicable to the Probationary Officers of CPTP-9 batch for the Post Graduate Degree Courses for Group A and Group B (Gazetted) posts covered under the Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP).
