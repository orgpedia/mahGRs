GOVERNMENT PURPOSE: The Government, in consultation with the State Election Commission, have issued a Government Resolution dated 17.12.2018 appointing Shri Kiran Kurundkar, Retd. The government decision (3) (a) is omitted.
2) The decision of the Government. 3 (ii) (a) reads as follows:
(a) During the period of appointment, a monthly remuneration equivalent to the amount remaining after deducting his pension from the salary payable to Mr. Kurundkar at the time of his retirement, including dearness allowance, shall be admissible. The travelling allowance and daily allowance admissible to the post of Secretary shall be applicable for the travel required to be undertaken by Mr. Kurundkar for election work. They will also be entitled to receive the allowances admissible to the post such as telephone allowance, newspaper allowance, residential peonage allowance, etc.
