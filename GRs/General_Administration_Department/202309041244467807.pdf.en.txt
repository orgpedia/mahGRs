Government Circular: A comprehensive policy on preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in the Government service every year. 1 has been prescribed by the government here.
Accordingly, the temporary seniority of Senior Assistant Director (Information)/ District Information Officer/ Senior Deputy Editor/ Public Relations Officer, Group-A (Junior) cadre officers in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations up to 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022, and 01.01.2023 is referred to as Reference No. 2 was published here. The names of the officers who were promoted during the period mentioned above, including the names of officers who were appointed through regular promotion, direct service or other prescribed route and excluding the names of officers who retired voluntarily and those who retired on regular basis, were included in Annexure-A and the names of the officers who were promoted ad-hoc as they fulfilled the terms and conditions for ad-hoc promotion were included in Annexure-B and the provisional seniority as on 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022 and 01.01.2023 was published on the website.

According to clause 3 (3) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, there is no need to publish an ad-hoc seniority list. Therefore, the ad-hoc seniority list published earlier need not be considered at present. Further, as no objections, objections and errors have been received by the Government on the provisional seniority list, the seniority list of Senior Assistant Director (Information)/ District Information Officer/ Senior Deputy Editor/ Public Relation Officer, Group-A (Junior) cadre officers showing their status as on 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022, and 01.01.2023 along with the seniority list for ad-hoc promotion is being finalised and annexed to this circular.
The aforesaid seniority list is being published subject to the final decision of the Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed by the State Government in the Supreme Court, New Delhi. The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations shall inform all the offices under its command and all the officers of the cadre of Senior Assistant Director (Information)/ District Information Officer/ Senior Deputy Editor/ Public Relations Officer, Group-A (Junior) to make the copies of the said seniority list available on the website of the Government.
