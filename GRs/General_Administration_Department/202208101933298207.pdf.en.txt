Government circular
India will be celebrating 75 years of Independence on August 15, 2022. As per the year 2019, the following directions are being issued for organizing the Independence Day programme in the State this year:
1) The Independence Day should be celebrated by hoisting the flag at all divisional/ district/ sub-divisional/ taluka headquarters as well as gram panchayat headquarters in the state.
Divisional Commissioners of Pune, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Nashik, Amravati and Konkan should make appropriate arrangements for holding the flag hoisting ceremony in their respective divisions and district headquarters.
3) The main government program of the state. The Chief Minister will unfurl the National Flag at Mantralaya, Mumbai at 9.05 am. In Pune. The flag will be hoisted by the Governor.
4) The main official ceremony of the flag hoisting should be held simultaneously across the state at 9.05 am. On this day from 8.35 am to 9.35 am. No flag hoisting or any other official or semi-official function should be held during this period. If an office or institution wants to have its own flag hoisting ceremony, they should do it at 8.35 am. before 9.35 a.m. to be organized later.
5) The national anthem should be played while saluting the national flag. The band prepared at the time of opening should be played before and after opening.
6) The theme of the speech should be one that highlights the significance of Independence Day and inspires the audience to work towards preserving the unity and integrity of the country.
7) The concerned sub-divisional officer and tehsildar will hoist the flag at the sub-divisional headquarters and taluk headquarters respectively and the sarpanch or village head will hoist the flag at the gram panchayat headquarters.
(8) All persons attending the ceremony shall wear the national dress.
During the day, various programs such as:- Tree plantation, debate competition through online mode at the inter-school/ inter-college level, quiz/ patriotic essay and poetry competition should be organized. Organize patriotic songs/ speeches of selected students through social media. Government buildings should be illuminated, webinars should be organized on a particular topic, patriotic online campaigns should be run by NSS and NYKS, and songs related to patriotism or national integration should be promoted/ given a message through social media and digital media.

The Divisional Commissioner and the District Collector should personally ensure that the directions given in this circular are followed properly.
