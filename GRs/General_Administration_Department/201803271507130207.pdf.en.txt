Since no employee joined the post of Short Story Writer from 1.1.2017 to 31.12.2017 by direct service or promotion, the provisional additional provisional seniority as on 1.1.2018 was not published. The final seniority of the Ministries/ Departments was notified vide Circular dated 18.03.2017 with effect from 01.01.2017. The seniority of the date 1.1.2018 is being made interim by filling up the errors/ objections in the seniority list of short story writers published on 1.1.2017 and is annexed to this Circular at Annexure-A. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
