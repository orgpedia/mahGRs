The Proposal:
Payment of rent for long-term warehouses taken on rent for storage of Electronic Voting Machines in the State and for other expenses incurred thereon. The new subtitle has been opened under the Government decision dated 08.12.2021. The Deputy Secretary and Joint Chief Electoral Officer, State of Maharashtra have been declared as the State Level Controlling Authorities for spending the provisions under this head. However, since it is necessary to declare a Drawing and Disbursing Officer under the control of the Controlling Officer at the State level to spend the provision at the State level under the said head of accounts, the matter of declaring a Drawing and Disbursing Officer to spend the provision at the State level was under consideration.
Government's decision:
For the purpose of payment of rent for long-term hired godowns for storage of Electronic Voting Machines in the State and other expenditure incurred thereon, the State Level Controlling Officer, Deputy Secretary and Joint Chief Electoral Officer, State of Maharashtra, under the charge of Under Secretary/ Cell Officer, General Administration Department (Works. 26/ Cash Branch), Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032 are being approved to be declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officers.
