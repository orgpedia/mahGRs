Government Circular- In-service training programme schedule for the year 2021-22 has been issued by the Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India. The letter dated 22.09.2021 has been sent to the State Government and the details of the training institute and duration have been communicated. The Central Government has instructed the IPS officers to submit their willingness for this training program in online form through https:// mctp-inservice. nic. in. The schedule of in-service training and the instructions of the Central Government in this regard have been communicated to all the State Government Railway Officers under the page dated 30.09.2021.
The nominations of IAS officers are finalised by the Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT) after the officers of the Indian Administrative Service (IAS) report their willingness for in-service training in online format to the Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT).
Thereafter, letters are sent from them to the concerned authorities and the training institute.
After the nomination of the in-service officer for one week's in-service training is finalised by the Department of Personnel and Training of the Central Government, it takes some time for the department to receive a copy of his letter in this regard. However, a copy of the letter for finalization of the nomination of the concerned officer is sent to the concerned officer immediately by mutual e-mail/ fax from the Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India. To attend this training, the concerned officials wait for the receipt of the letter from the General Administration Department permitting them to attend the training programme. But the letter of the Department of Personnel and Training regarding the finalization of the nomination of officers to this department
Due to the delay in receiving, there is a delay in informing the concerned officials by this department.

Accordingly, the following instructions are being issued regarding the administrative arrangements to be made for the officers of the Indian Administrative Service to attend the training during the one-week in-service training program, 3.1) After the letter of nomination for the one-week in-service training is received by the concerned officers, they should make the administrative arrangements for the period of absence of training at the local level in consultation with the senior officers. After that, you can go back to work and attend in-service training. For this, it is not necessary to wait for the letter of the General Administration Department. However, after completing the training, the information should be reported to the General Administration Department (Office 9/9 A/ 10) along with the certificate of completion of the training.
3.2) After finalizing the nomination for one week's in-service training of the Deputy Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary only in the administrative department of the Ministry, the information should be immediately communicated to the General Administration Department/ Office 10. The process of assigning additional charge of the post in which the officer is working during the training period will be done by the General Administration Department (Office 10).
Only then the concerned officers should be relieved from work and attend the training. Also, after completing the training, the information about it should be sent to the General Administration Department (Office 9/9 A/ 10) along with the certificate of completion of the training.
