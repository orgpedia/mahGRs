the order
Shri Vithal Dattatraya Bhaskar, Under Secretary, General Administration Department is being appointed on deputation for the post of" Establishment Officer (Vigilance)" in Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under Water Supply and Sanitation Department for a period of three years initially subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mr. Vittal Dattatraya Bhaskar, Under-Secretary shall commence the service of the Deputy Secretary on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall cease to hold office on the date on which he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(a) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and (d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Residential Service. The terms and conditions of the deputation of Shri Vittal Dattatraya Bhaskar, Under Secretary, Department of General Administration and Department of Water Supply and Sanitation shall be decided in consultation with each other, after taking into consideration the terms and conditions mentioned in Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters regarding the deputation of Shri Vitthal Dattatraya Bhaskar, Under Secretary will be handled by the Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
4. The General Administration Department should relieve Shri Vitthal Dattatraya Bhaskar, Under Secretary, to join the post of" Establishment Officer (Vigilance)" in Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department with immediate effect. Also, the Maharashtra Life Authority under the Water Supply and Sanitation Department should immediately appoint Mr. Vitthal Dattatraya Bhaskar, Under Secretary.
