The proposal: self. Mumbai: The Mayor's bungalow in Shivaji Park has been allocated for Shiv Sena president Uddhav Thackeray's memorial. A public trust has been set up for the purpose. According to the The public trust and institution was formed under the chairmanship of Shri Uddhav Thackeray as per the decision of the Government on September 27, 2016.
The post of president of the Balasaheb Thackeray National Memorial Public Trust fell vacant after Uddhav Thackeray resigned from the post.
Further, as per the provisions of Rule 14 of the Memorandum of Association and Rules and Regulations of the Trust, the proposal to fill up the posts of Chairman and Members, except ex-officio members, was under consideration as the three-year period of first appointment of Chairman and other members on the Trust had expired.
Government decision: A memorial to Balasaheb Thackeray. Shri Uddhav Thackeray, Chairman of the first Board of Governors/ Trustees constituted on 27th September, 2016. Approval is hereby accorded for the appointment of the following new members to the post of Chairperson and Members of the Board, which have become vacant due to resignation under letter dated 25.11.2019:
