Government circular
The Departmental Examination is to be conducted for the promotion of Assistant Cell Officers to the post of Cell Officer in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. It was held on August 19, 2019.
In addition to the candidates mentioned above, the Assistant Section Officers of the Administrative Department who have previously appeared in the departmental examination and have failed in the said examination or have been absent in the examination, if such candidates are to appear in the Cell Officer Departmental Examination 2019, their applications in the prescribed format (mentioning the full name of the candidate in the application form) should be sent to the Administrative Department by the Establishment Officers of the Administrative Departments after verifying that all the details in the application form are correct and in such a manner that it will reach the Department by 01 October 2019. Applications received after this time will not be considered. The prescribed format of application is available on the Ministry Intranet under the heading" Application Form."
3. Qualified candidates who have given synchronized marks for the training and examination presented. As per the circular dated 12th July, 2019, the candidates who have not applied within the prescribed time limit, but who are appearing for the 2019 examination for the first time, should send their applications in the prescribed format along with the applications of the re-appearing candidates to this office by 1st October, 2019.
All the administrative departments of the Ministry may kindly bring this circular in writing to the notice of the concerned Assistant Section Officer (including the Assistant Section Officer on deputation/ leave) immediately and take their signatures and preserve the documents in their records.
