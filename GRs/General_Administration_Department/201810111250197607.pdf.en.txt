INTRODUCTION Subject to the decision of the Government, General Administration Department, No. Under the Composite 2002/1973/ PR No. 211/2002/28, dated 16/10/2002, it has been approved to create 4 posts in Group-C and Group-D on daily wage basis and to fill these posts in the prescribed manner. In pursuance of the decision taken to extend the temporary posts under the Government Decision dated 04/10/2004 under reference, the Government has extended the period from time to time. Under 23/03/2018, these posts have been extended from 01/03/2018 to 30/09/2018.
GOVERNMENT DECISION The Government have approved the continuation of the following 4 daily wage posts in Group-C and Group-D created on daily wage basis on the establishment of Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad from 01/10/2018 to 28/02/2019.

The Director, Institute of Military Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad shall appoint such persons to the post of daily wagers for a maximum period of 28 days in a month.
After this, the person will not mind being given a technical break of at least three days and re-appointed. However, it should be ensured that the total period of appointment of such persons to the post of daily wagers is not more than three months.
