Letter from Director (Information) (News and Public Relations) No. News-2019/ Journalist Honour Scheme/ KA-2/ 746D. 05.02.2019 Preamble: With a view to provide assistance to the journalists in case of serious illness, accident or accidental death to them or their family members, the Government has decided to establish" Shankarrao Chavan Golden Jubilee Journalist Welfare Fund (Trust)" vide Resolution No. 1 has been established. Out of this, a total of Rs. An amount of Rs. 10.00 crore has been provided. These funds are invested in the form of fixed deposits in nationalized banks and the interest earned on them is used to provide financial assistance to journalists.
Under the scheme of" Acharya Balashastri Jambhekar Patrakar Samman," financial assistance will be provided to senior journalists of the state to express their respect and gratitude. Approval has been granted by the Government Decision dated 02/02/2019. To meet the expenditure on this scheme, a deposit amount of Rs. 10.00 crore to Rs. 25.00 crore was under consideration of the Government. The Government has taken the following decisions:- The Government has approved to increase the deposit amount of Shankarrao Chavan Golden Jubilee Journalist Welfare Fund from Rs. 10.00 crore (Rs.10 crore only) to Rs. 25.00 crore (Rs.
