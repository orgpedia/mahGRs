The Proposal -
The General Administration Department gave the government decision. 22.07.2024 and d. Under 05.09.2024, candidates belonging to SEBC and OBC categories have to submit a caste validity certificate, apply for admission.
An extension of 6 months was granted from the date. However, some of the candidates admitted during the said period could not produce the caste validity certificate within the prescribed period. Sadar
In view of the educational interest of the candidates who have not been able to produce the caste validity certificate, the government has decided as follows.
The Government's Decision-
For admission to professional courses (including engineering, medical, and other professional courses) in various educational institutions in the state in the current year 2024-25, candidates belonging to the SEBC and OBC categories who have been admitted but within the prescribed time period.
Candidates who have not been able to produce the caste validity certificate are being given a period of three months beyond the date of this order to submit the caste validity certificate.
The maximum period of three months is for candidates belonging to SEBC and OBC categories whose admission has been fixed but could produce the caste validity certificate within the prescribed time.
Otherwise, it will remain only for such candidates. Also, the three-month period for submission of caste validity certificate will be final and no extension will be granted thereafter.
3. Candidates who have not been able to produce the caste validity certificate during this period will not be admitted.
If possible, the candidates concerned will be responsible for it.
