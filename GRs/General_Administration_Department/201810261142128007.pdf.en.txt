A copy of the Office Memorandum dated 18/09/2018, No. 42/06/2018-P& PW (G), Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Department of Pension& Pensioners' Welfare, Government of India, New Delhi, forwarded for information and appropriate action in respect of All India Service Pensioners/ Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre.
As per the aforesaid Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Government of India, with effect from 01.07.2018, the 2% increase in Dearness Relief rate and other provisions mentioned in the Memorandum will be applicable to All India Service Pensioners/ Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre. Dearness Relief at the rate of 9% will now be admissible to All India Service Pensioners/ Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre with effect from 01.07.2018.
