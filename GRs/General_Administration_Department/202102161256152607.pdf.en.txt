The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of this Government decision, the matter of ensuring the disabled category in the Group-A to Group-D cadre in the office of the Lok Ayukta and Deputy Lok Ayukta under the General Administration Department was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for PwDs as per the notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions that the posts in Group A to Group D, which have been ensured for the disabled by the Central Government, will be ensured for the disabled in the State Government service, even if the designation of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government Service, are different. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021.
