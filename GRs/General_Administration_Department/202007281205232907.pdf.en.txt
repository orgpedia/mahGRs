Preamble: Under the General Administration Department. Instructions have been given to create more number of posts for the officers/ employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribe category under the government decision dated 21.12.2019, but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or have submitted the other caste validity certificate, and to send a requisition to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to fill the vacancies of the officers/ employees appointed to the post of the said Scheduled Tribe. Accordingly, Shri Subodh Vasudev Kolabkar, Assistant, Group-C in the Office of the Secretary to the Governor of Maharashtra, has proposed to create a supernumerary post for the transfer of his services to a higher post due to non-submission of caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes, and Smt. Asha Raut, Gardener, Group-D in the Office of the Governor Family Manager, who has not submitted a proposal to the Caste Verification Committee within the stipulated time for verification of the caste certificate after appointment. 00.05.2020 has been submitted. Following that, Ma. The government is considering the creation of one additional post in the office of the secretary to the governor and one in the office of the family manager to the governor.
Government's decision:
The services of Mr. Subodh Vasudev Kolabkar, Assistant in the Office of the Secretary to the Governor were rendered on the ground that he had submitted the caste validity certificate of the Special Backward Class after his Scheduled Tribe caste validity certificate was invalid. Office of the Secretary to the Governor for transfer of his services to the rank of Superintendent with effect from 05.03.2020
