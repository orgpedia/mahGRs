Read: 1) Circular of the Government, General Administration Department, No: 2015/ 11/18 (R. And why dated March 11, 2016.
The Proposal:
A comprehensive revised circular dated 11th March, 2016 has been issued by authorizing all the circulars regarding how the government officials should attend the meetings held by the persons given the status of ministers by the government/ non-government members of parliament/ members of the legislature etc. for the disposal of pending works so that the issues of public interest and public works are not delayed. However, it was under the consideration of the government to issue instructions regarding the attendance of government officials in the meetings convened by government committees whose chairpersons are non-government persons or members of parliament or legislature.
Circular:
It is hereby clarified that the meetings convened by the Chairpersons of the Committees of Government of which the Chairperson is a non-official person or a Member of Parliament or a Member of the Legislature shall be attended by the officers concerned in the jurisdiction of the Committee concerned with the subject matter of the meeting. However, if other Members of Parliament/ Members of Legislative Assemblies wish to hold a meeting in connection with the work of their constituencies, they are required to hold the meeting at the office of the Collector in accordance with the circular dated 11th March, 2016. However, all departments/ offices shall strictly follow these instructions.
