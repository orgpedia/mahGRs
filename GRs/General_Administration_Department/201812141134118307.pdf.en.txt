However, as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 4, the candidates selected to the post of Municipalities/ Municipal Councils, Headmaster, Group-A and Group-B in the State Service Examination by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission have been excluded from the training of the Combined Probationary Training Programme.
Therefore, it is being annexed with the revised" Statement A" showing details of 26 cadres included in the Combined Probationary Training Programme, excluding 2 cadres.
