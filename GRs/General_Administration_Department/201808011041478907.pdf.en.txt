Government circular:
Read the revised sample of the performance appraisal report of Group-A government officers here. As per the Government decision dated 2.2.2017, it has been decided to register the performance appraisal reports of officers of selected 12 services for the year 2016-17 through online mode. For this, a computer system called MahaPAR has been developed by NIC.
As per the Government Decision dated 2.2.2018, the time limit for completion of the work on the performance appraisal reports for the year 2016-17 in the Mahapar system was extended till 16/2/2018. Accordingly, the performance appraisal reports for the year 2016-17 are now being closed on 8.8.2018 at the stage when the system is in place. After 8.8.2018, no action can be taken in respect of the performance appraisal report for the year 2016-17 in the said system. All the concerned officers, Reporting and Review Officers and Editing Officers should note that the status of the evaluation reports is final.
The circular has been made available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www. mahararshtra. gov. in and its code is 201808011041478907. This circular is being digitally signed.
