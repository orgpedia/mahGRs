Government Decision: An annual contract was to be made for the purchase of stationery/ stationery, computer materials and other items for the offices in the General Administration Department (GAD). For this, e-tenders were invited from the government website.
May 2. The prices of the items offered by R.K. Enterprises, Mumbai are lower than other bidders.
Also, with the letter dated 11 th April 2022, they have reduced the prices of some items wherever possible. May be as a supplier for supply of stationery/ stationery, computer material and other items to the Department. A.R.K. Enterprises, Mumbai is being appointed with effect from 18th April 2022.
3. General Administration Department (GAD) has been appointed as Supplier for supply of stationery/ stationery, computer material and other items for a period of one year from 18th April, 2022 to 17th April, 2023. After the expiry of this period, it has been made mandatory for the supplier to supply the material according to the tender price until a new e-tendering process is implemented and the supplier is determined.
The General Administration Department is the largest department in the Ministry. Office of the Chief Secretary. Since the offices and offices of the Secretary have to supply the materials immediately, it will be the responsibility of the concerned supplier to supply the materials within 1 hour after the department has requested for the supply.
May 5. The reserve amount paid by R.K. Enterprises, Mumbai along with the e-tender is Rs. 10,000 will be deposited with the government as a reserve amount during the service period.
May 6. The Government reserves the right to cancel the appointment of R. K. Enterprises, Mumbai as the supplier of the material at any time if it is found that there is a delay in the supply of the material or if there is a complaint about the quality of the material.
May 7. A fine of Rs.500/- per day will be imposed on R.K. Enterprises, Mumbai for non-supply of material within 24 hours from the date of supply order. R. K. Enterprises, Mumbai will be required to supply to the department the essential commodities not mentioned in the list dated 11 th April 2022, if required by the department, by the concerned suppliers as per the market price.
