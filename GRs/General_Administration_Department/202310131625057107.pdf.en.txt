Before the interim stay of the Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) Reservation Act, 2018 by the Hon'ble Supreme Court on 09.09.2020, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the Selection Boards and other recruiting agencies had recommended for appointment in the Government service but the Government had not approved the same. The Maharashtra Appointment of Selected Candidates and Creation of Superintendents of Posts Act, 2022 has been passed to provide for appointment of candidates who have not been appointed before 09.09.2020. The same has been published in the Gazette on 08.09.2022. Thereafter, by amending the aforesaid principal Act, the Government passed the Maharashtra Appointment of Superintendents of Posts and Selected Candidates (Amendment) Act, 2022.
