Allotment of additional space for substation to Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport (BEST) Company.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department,
Government Resolution, No. Shakaja-1121/ P.S. No. 77/2021/22,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: February 1, 2022
Introduction: The Ministry of Education. As the demand for power supply has increased due to the renovation of the ministers' chambers and the offices of various administrative departments, a new substation has been constructed at the rear side of the Mantralaya Extension building to provide additional power. 89.64 sq.m. additional space near the site of the substation of Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport (BEST) Company and 8.08 sq.m. area under Mantralaya Extension Building for electricity meter room. In the place of m. The proposal to allocate to the Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport (BEST) company was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Government has taken the following decisions: The Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport (BEST) company has been asked to set up an upgraded substation to provide additional power to the Ministry. The 89.64-square-metre building is located near the site of the existing substation of the Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport (BEST) company.
For additional space and electric meter room, 8.08 sq.m. of space under Ministry Extension building is being allotted subject to the following conditions: 1) It should be ensured that the space to be used for the sub-station does not pose any threat to the basic structure of the building.
2) Proper precautions should be taken as the restaurant and other underground drainage lines pass through the space used for the sub-station.
3) There should be a space around the building for fire engines to move.
The Executive Engineer, City (East) Division, Nirman Bhawan, Mumbai shall hand over the possession of the said land to the concerned and submit a report to the Government.
