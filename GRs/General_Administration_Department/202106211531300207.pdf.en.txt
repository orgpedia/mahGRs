Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for a person with disability to be appointed to a post in the government service. In this regard, the Central Government vide No. The list of posts earmarked for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 4/1/2021. As per this list, the posts in the subordinate offices of this department should be ensured for the disabled and orders should be issued accordingly. 4 The days of social justice and special assistance mentioned here were given under the Government Decision of 2/2/2021. Accordingly, suitable posts for Divyangjan have been identified in Group' A,"' C" and' D" cadre posts on the establishment of Directorate General of Aviation. However, the proposal to exempt the posts of Chief Pilot (Helicopter), Chief Pilot (Aircraft), Pilot and Police Constable Driver in Cadre' A" and' C" of the said office while determining the post for the disabled was submitted for the approval of the Expert Committee. The Expert Committee in its meeting held on 22nd and 23rd February, 2021 has recommended the removal of these posts. Accordingly, a proposal was under the consideration of the government to exclude 11 posts of Chief Pilot (Helicopter), Chief Pilot (Aircraft), Pilot and Police Constable Driver in Group' A' and' Category' on the establishment of the Directorate of Aviation under the General Administration Department.
