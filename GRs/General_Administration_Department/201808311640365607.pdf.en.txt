The Ministry under the General Administration Department has decided to promote the officers/ employees appointed through this route in the cadre of Group-B Manager and Group-C Deputy Manager and Assistant Manager from the managerial side in the establishment of food/ restaurants. 01.01.2016 to D. 31.12.2016 and D. The provisional Common Seniority List for the period 01.01.2017 to 31.12.2017 has been prepared and annexed to this Circular as Annexure-A and Annexure-B.
The said provisional common seniority list is being brought to the notice of all officers/ staff of Group-B and Group-C cadre. In case of any errors and factual errors in the said provisional seniority list, the concerned should bring it to the notice of the Ministry of Tourism within one month from the date of this circular. Please note that in case of non-receipt of statement of errors and factual inaccuracies during the said period, the seniority list will be finalised assuming that there are no objections in the said seniority list.
