The Proposal:
Reference No. 1 to the procedure for filling up the posts of reservation of Backward Classes in the Minor Cadre. The guidelines have been issued vide Government Circular dated 29th May, 2017.
Reference No. 3 is here. The Government Circular dated 21st August, 2019 has prescribed the procedure for filling up of the posts reserved for Backward Classes in Minor Cadre including Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) and Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) reservation.
In pursuance of the Hon'ble Supreme Court's judgment dated 5th May, 2021 in Civil Petition No. 3132/ 2020, the revised Point Names have been prescribed for direct recruitment in Government/ Paramilitary Services in the State Public Service by cancelling the Point Names prescribed vide Government Order dated 4th July, 2019 and vide Government Order dated 6th July, 2021. Reference No. 6 here. As per the Government decision dated 23 rd September, 2021, revised reservation has been prescribed for the posts of Group C and Group D cadres at the district level in the districts of Nashik, Dhule, Yavatmal, Chandrapur, Raigad, Gadchiroli, Nandurbar and Palghar having more population of Scheduled Tribes.
In the above background, regarding filling up of reserved posts of Backward Classes in Minor Cadre, Reference No. 3 here. The matter of issuing revised instructions by superseding the instructions issued under the Government Circular dated August 21, 2019 was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following procedure is being prescribed for filling up the reserved posts of Backward Classes in the Minor Cadre.

Government's decision:
The procedure for filling up the reserved posts of Backward Classes in the Minorities in the direct service in the non-tribal districts of the State is prescribed in Schedule A attached to the Schedule and in Schedule B, A, D, E and E attached to the procedure for filling up the reserved posts of Backward Classes in the Minorities in the direct service in the tribal dominated districts of Palghar, Nashik, Dhule, Nandurbar, Yavatmal, Raigad, Chandrapur and Gadchiroli having enhanced reservation for Scheduled Tribes.
(2) In respect of direct service in the matter of filling up of the reserved posts of Backward Classes in the Minor Cadre in the non-tribal districts of the State and in the tribal dominated districts of Palghar, Nashik, Dhule, Nandurbar, Yavatmal, Raigad, Chandrapur and Gadchiroli, where there is increased reservation for Scheduled Tribes. 1 here. In view of the instructions given in the Government Circular dated 29th May, 2017, action should be taken as per Schedule A, B, C, D, E and E attached.
3. All administrative departments shall bring this decision to the notice of all heads of departments/ appointing authorities under them.
