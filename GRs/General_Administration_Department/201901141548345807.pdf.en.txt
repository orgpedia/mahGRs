Preamble As per the notification dated 15.06.2018 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, the revised strength of cadre posts in the Indian Administrative Service in the State has been fixed. Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix. Also, 8 more Non-Cadre posts (SDR) can be filled as per the provision of Rule 12 (7) of the Personnel (Pay) Rules, 2016. The question of creating two temporary posts in the cadre was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision Under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, two posts in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix are being provisionally sanctioned from 15.01.2019 to 31.08.2019.
