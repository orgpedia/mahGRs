ORDER
Shri Keshav S.Jadhav, Desk Officer, Industries, Energy and Labour Department, Government of
Maharashtra (presently working as, Assistant Director, in the office of Directorate of Archives, Kolhapur
on deputation basis) has been selected as Assistant Development Commissioner in the Office of the
Development Commissioner, SEEPZ-SEZ, Mumbai on deputation basis as conveyed by GOI vide letter
under reference No.1 and 2.
2. Henceforth, the services of Shri Keshav S.Jadhav, presently working as Assistant Director, in the
office of Directorate of Archives, Kolhapur on deputation basis has been transferred to the Office of the
Development Commissioner, SEEPZ-SEZ, Mumbai as Assistant Development Commissioner, on
deputation basis for the period of 1 years with the following terms and conditions:
a) The period of deputation will start from the date of joining of Shri Jadhav in the Office of the Development Commissioner, SEEPZ-SEZ, Mumbai and it will end when Shri Jadhav will return to his parent department after handing over the charge in the Office of the Development Commissioner, SEEPZ-SEZ, Mumbai.
b) The Government of Maharashtra reserves the right of premature reversion if the services of the employee needed in larger public interest;
c) In case the borrowing department does not need the services of the employee, the services of the same may be returned to the lending department by giving three months notice to the competent authority/lending department; and
d) If Shri Jadhav wants to return to his parent cadre, he will be is allowed to return when the same is conveyed by him in written with at least three months notice to the Competent authority/borrowing department.
