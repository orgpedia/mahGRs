So far, 25% of the budgeted allocation of Rs. 19,95,11, 000/- (Rupees Nineteen Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Eleven Thousand only) has been received. Out of this budgetary provision, a proposal is under consideration of the Government to transfer the required amount of funds to the concerned District Collector for the month of May to June 2022.
Government Decision: For the financial year 2022-23, in the head of head account 2235, pension to freedom fighters and their dependents, etc. (mandatory), (2235 0015), the following Rs. 79,80,44, 000/- (Rupees Ninety-nine crore eighty lakh forty four thousand only). Out of the sanctioned budgetary provision, a sum of Rs. 12,18,00, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crore Eighteen Lakhs Only) is being released to the respective District Collectors to meet the expenditure for the month (May to June 2022) as shown against the names of their districts in the Statement A attached with the Government Resolution.
The amount of subsidy spent from the fund distributed to the District Collector should be reconciled with the Accountant General-1, Mumbai/ Accountant General-2, Nagpur within the prescribed time and submit the monthly report of the expenditure meeting to the government regularly. In this case, action should be taken as per Government Circular No. Expenditure-Mel 2011/ PR No. 139/2011/ SWASAC-1, dated 16/08/2011.
