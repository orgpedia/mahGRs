the order
Smt. Ranjana Kumre, Cell Officer, General Administration Department is being appointed on deputation for a period of one year initially to the post of" Cell Officer" in the office of the State Commission for the Right to Service, Nagpur Division, Nagpur under the General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Smt. Ranjana Kumre, Officer on deputation shall commence her service on the date on which she enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner, and shall, on the date on which she enters upon her parent department, complete her term of deputation by handing over the charge of the office of the Deputy Commissioner, one year thereafter.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
After the repatriation of the deputation, Smt. Ranjana Kumre, the Cell Officer will be required to join her parent department i.e. General Administration Department.
The General Administration Department shall decide the terms and conditions for the deputation of Smt. Ranjana Kumre after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II& III) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mrs. Kumre, Cell Officer will be handled by the General Administration Department.
5. Mrs. Kumre, the Cell Officer should be relieved immediately by the General Administration Department to accept the appointment on deputation.
