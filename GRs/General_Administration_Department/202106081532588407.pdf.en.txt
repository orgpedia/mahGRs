The Combined Competitive Training Programme-I (CPTP-I) of the candidates recommended under State Service (Main) Examination-2013 and appointed to the post of Sub Divisional Deputy Executive Officer/ Block Development Officer, (Group-A) under probation under the Office Order dated 27/06/2014 has been completed during the period from 09/06/2014 to 30/06/2016 and the training has been satisfactorily completed by the candidates in the following statement.
The training period of CPTP-1 batch is from 09/06/2014 to 30/06/2016. During this period, the departmental examination for the post of Deputy Executive Officer/ Block Development Officer, Group-A has been conducted once. As per Rule 7 (b) of the Maharashtra Development Services, Class-I and Class-II (Zonal Examination) Rules, 1991, if for any reason the zonal examination has not been conducted in any particular year or half-year, the said year or half-year shall be excluded from the period considered in Rule-3. Smt. Meena Janya Rawatale has passed the 4th attempt i.e. after 30/06/2016, the last date of training. Therefore, the probation period of Smt. Rawatale is being extended upto the next date of completion of the fourth chance examination i.e. after 30/06/2016 and ending on the date mentioned in column 7 of the following statement.
(3) In accordance with the provisions of clause 1 (3) (b) of the General Administration Department, Government Decision dated February 29, 2016, the probation period of the candidates who enjoy any form of leave other than temporary leave during the probation period is extended to the same period as the leave period. The probation period of Smt. Sanghamitra Madhavrao Kolhe is in Pillar No. The leave period mentioned in column 5 is being extended. The probationary period is expiring on the date mentioned in Section 7. Also, the leave of Mr. Lokhande during his training period is irrelevant. Hence, the date of expiry of their probation period is proposed to be 30/06/2016.
