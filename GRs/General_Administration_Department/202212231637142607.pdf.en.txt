Preamble: The Government Decision dated 9th November, 2020 specifies the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) required for effective implementation of e-Office system in Government offices in the Ministries/ Departments for faster and faster delivery of documents and information and for facilitating decision-making process. Following that, Ma. The Chief Secretary in a meeting held on 08/12/2022 discussed and reviewed the use of e-office system in the Ministry. As per the directions of the meeting, the government was considering the formation of a committee to operate at the ministry level in order to effectively use the e-office system in the functioning of the ministry and field offices.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In order to make the use of computer in the government affairs of the state government faster and smoother, to make the decision process easier by securing documents and information in a safe, speedy and fast manner, a committee is being formed to implement this system at the ministry level by effectively using the e-office system in the government affairs in the ministerial departments and field offices.

The functions and functions of the said Committee shall be as follows:
The Committee shall, from time to time, prescribe guidelines and instructions for effective use of e-office system in the functioning of the Ministry and the field offices.
(ii) To review and resolve the issues arising out of the effective use of e-Office system from time to time and monitor the use and implementation of e-Office system.
The committee will meet once a week.
The Committee will review every month the effective use of e-Office system in the functioning of the Ministries and Regional Offices.
