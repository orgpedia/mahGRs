The distribution of financial assistance payable in respect of the deceased soldier is divided as follows. (i) If the martyr is married and his parents are also alive, then a) his brave wife- 60%, b) the brave mother- 20%, the brave father- 20%, c) if either of his parents is alive, then 40%, 2) the martyr soldier is married and his parents are not alive, then his brave wife should be given 100%, 3) the martyred soldier is unmarried and his parents are also alive, then it should be divided equally between his parents.
