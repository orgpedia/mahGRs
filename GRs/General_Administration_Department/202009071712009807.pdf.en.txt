Introduction: To resolve the Maharashtra-Karnataka border issue. Under the chairmanship of Chief Minister. A High Powered Committee has been constituted vide Government Circular dated 02/05/2000. As per the requirement of the said High Powered Committee. 26/05/2014, Dy. 19/06/2014 and D. It was reconstituted vide Government Circular dated 05/03/2015. The new government was formed after the Maharashtra Assembly and General Elections 2019. Under the given circumstances, the matter of reconstitution of the High Power Committee was under the consideration of the Government.
Karnataka-Maharashtra border issue Under the chairmanship of Chief Minister. The Government hereby approve the reconstitution of the High Powered Committee as follows by superseding the Government Circular dated 05/03/2015.
1) Ma. Deputy Chief Minister Ajit Pawar (2) Mr. Sharad Pawar, Member of Parliament 4. Chhagan Bhujbal, Shri. Minister, Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection and Coordinating Minister (Border Issues) 5. Minister of Urban Development, Public Works (Public Undertakings) and Coordinating Minister (Boundary Question) 6. Satej alias Bunty D. Patil, Ma. Minister of State, Housing 8) Prof. N.D. Leader of Opposition, Legislative Assembly (10) Leader of the Opposition, Legislative Council
The committee will have the power to decide on the future course of action to resolve the Maharashtra-Karnataka border issue.
