Supplementary Letter: Government Circular on Review of Service Matters of Government Officers/ Employees. Issued on 20.10.2020. The circular prescribes the details of the issues to be taken up for consideration/ discussion in the monthly review meetings with Joint/ Deputy Secretaries handling field establishments in the Ministries/ Departments.
The updated information regarding the recruitment process in the office of the State Government's Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board must be obtained from the administrative administrative departments of the Ministry. For this, the review of the service matters of government officers/ employees will be reviewed. In the Government Circular dated 20.10.2020, vide return No. 16A and 16B are included.
