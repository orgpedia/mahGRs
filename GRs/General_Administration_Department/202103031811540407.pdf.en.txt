Introduction: The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations uses various means to inform and publicize the various public welfare schemes of the government. For example, 1) Corporate Pullup Standees 2) Vinyl Printing on Sun Board, Mounting 3) Digital Wall Painting 4) Asepi Display Board (with UV Printing) 5) LD Foamsheet Banner 6) Stage Backdrop etc. The matter was under consideration of the government. In this regard, the Government Decision dated 2 nd March, 2021 was issued.
Government Decision: The Government is giving approval to the organization Advay Design, Kolhapur and their rates under the following terms and conditions to work through various media to inform and publicize the various welfare schemes of the Government through OCP Display Board (including UV printing).
The tender process for the information and publication of the government's public welfare schemes should be completed within the prescribed time frame every year in a pre-planned manner so that the government can save on government expenditure.
The Directorate General is of the view that the rates quoted by the Institute in the said tender in June/ July, 2019 are the lowest as on date.
3. Subject-wise tendering process is carried out by the Directorate General as per the general CVC guidelines of the Central Government regarding the tendering process.
4. The government should be informed every month about the implementation of various public welfare schemes of the government in a quantitative manner or how the results will be known after the implementation of the publication.

3) While giving the work order to the above-mentioned organization, the work order should be given only after ensuring that all the documents required for the said work are validly with the concerned organization and a copy of it should be sent to the department.
