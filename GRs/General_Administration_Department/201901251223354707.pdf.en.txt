Officers who have been appointed during the period 31.12.2016 by way of regular promotion, direct service or otherwise. A provisional seniority list for the year 1.1.2017 has been prepared and is annexed to this circular.
(3) The said seniority list is of the temporary seniority of Assistant Director (Information)/ Information Officer/ Superintendents, Books and Publications, Group-B Cadre Officers. The names of officers who retired and were promoted to the senior cadre as on 31.12.2016 have been excluded from the list of seniority.
The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned and keep a copy of their signature in the archive in case of such intimation.
If there are any genuine errors or omissions in this seniority list, the concerned should object to it. The same has been examined at the Directorate General level and submitted along with the comments of the Director General, after seeking the same by 10.02.2019. be submitted to the Government by 20.02.2019. The objections received by the Government after the said date shall not be considered. Please note that if no objections are received by the Government till 20.2.2019, the Seniority List shall be finalised assuming that there are no objections from the concerned.
