The proposal: The Supreme Court in its order dated 13.05.2015 has directed the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting to constitute a three-member committee for timely redressal of grievances arising out of publication of Government of India advertisements. Accordingly, the Press Information Bureau (PIB) has constituted a three-member committee at its level.
Supreme Court of. Order dated 13.05.2015 vide Rule No. As per the provisions of section 6, the Government was considering the formation of a three-member committee of experts to resolve the complaints arising from time to time regarding the publication of government-approved advertisements by the Government of Maharashtra. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: A three-member panel of experts will be formed to resolve the complaints arising from time to time regarding the publication of government-approved advertisements by the Government of Maharashtra.

The powers and functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
(a) All the Ministries/ Departments of the Government of Maharashtra (b) Public Enterprises/ Undertakings recognized by the Government of Maharashtra (c) Government of Maharashtra/ Local Self Governments and other Autonomous Bodies/ Organizations.
The various advertisements issued by all of the above will come under the purview of this committee.
The term of office of the members of the Committee shall be initially for a period of two years and may be extended by one year.
The term of the committee cannot be extended more than twice.
The members attending the meetings of the aforesaid three-member committee are advised to refer to the Government Resolution No. 2012/ No. 1/ Service-5, D. Meeting Allowance as on 24th May, 2012 and Government Resolution No. Travel expenses and travel allowance will be paid as of March 3, 2010.
