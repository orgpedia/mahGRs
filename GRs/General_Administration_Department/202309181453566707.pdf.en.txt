Preamble:" Aapka Sarkar 2.0" is an important project of the Government to create a bridge for citizens and administration to interact online. Also, the PG portal is a system run by the central government. The aim of this integration is to enable the citizens of the state to get their grievances redressed online at one place. The" Aapka Sarkar" grievance redressal system has been implemented in all 36 districts of the state along with the ministry departments so that the citizens of the state can file complaints online and the administration can resolve such complaints online. The grievance redressal system is available on the website www. aaplesarkar. maharashtra. gov. in.
For effective implementation of the system, it was necessary to upgrade the system from administrative and technical point of view. For this, the process of filing complaints online, online redressal of complaints by the administration and control of this process by senior offices, etc.
In this regard, the matter of prescribing the procedure for the implementation of this system was under the consideration of the government.
Government circular:
1) After considering the above background, the Government is now prescribing the following new features for the implementation of the computerized grievance redressal system" Aapka Sarkar 2.0."
