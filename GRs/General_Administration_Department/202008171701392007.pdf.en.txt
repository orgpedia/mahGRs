Preamble: District Selection Committees under the Chairmanship of District Collectors, Regional Selection Committees under the Chairmanship of Regional Heads of Departments and State Level Selection Committees under the Chairmanship of Heads of Departments have been constituted to fill up the vacancies in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the functioning of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Boards (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) on direct service basis.
The comprehensive procedure for recruitment to these posts has been detailed in the Government Resolution dated 13th June, 2018.
As per the decision of the Government dated 19th September, 2017 in the context of General Administration (IT) Department, it was made mandatory to use the Maha Pareeksha Portal while implementing the recruitment process.
Accordingly, detailed instructions regarding the recruitment process for Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the jurisdiction of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Boards (outside the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission) through the MahaPariksha Portal have been issued vide Government Circular dated 14th March, 2018.
Now, the General Administration (Information Technology) Department has issued instructions to the OMR Vendor (Service Provider) empanelled by MahaIT to carry out the examination process in respect of recruitment to Group-C and Group-D posts of various departments of the Government by superseding the Government Decision dated September 19, 2017, dated February 20, 2020 and the Government Corrigendum dated February 24, 2020.

In pursuance of these instructions, the circular dated 14th March, 2018 regarding the use of MahaPariksha Portal for recruitment to the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadre in the jurisdiction of the erstwhile Secondary Service Selection Boards (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) is hereby superseded and revised instructions regarding the conduct of the examination are issued as under:
Regarding the recruitment process for Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the jurisdiction of the erstwhile Secondary Service Selection Boards (outside the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission), it is being ordered that the concerned District Selection Committees, Regional Level Selection Committees and State Level Selection Committees should conduct the examination process for the competitive examinations regarding the recruitment process by selecting an OMR Vendor (Service Provider) from the list of empanelled Vendors as mentioned in the Government Decision dated February 20, 2020 of the General Administration (Information Technology) Department. For this, the concerned selection committees are being authorized as nodal agencies and the chairman of the selection committee as nodal officer. These powers may be delegated to any officer of the selection committee as required by the chairman of the selection committee.
The responsibility of conducting the examination process, from the advertisement of posts, selection process to the publication of the final selection list, will be of the concerned selection committee. The selection process for the post should be conducted by the respective selection committees from the OMR Vendor (Service Provider) selected from the empanelment.
The selection committees will have to enter into an appropriate Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the OMR Vendor for the purposes of accepting applications for competitive examinations from the candidates, accepting the examination fee, checking it, preparing the admit card of the examination by following the due process, publishing the results of the examination as well as the list of recommended/ non-recommended candidates. Detailed instructions regarding the MoU will be issued in consultation with the Department/ Directorate of Information Technology, upon empanelment.
The responsibility of preparing question papers by expert persons in accordance with the syllabus of the examinations for recruitment to the posts concerned and preparing question sets/ answer sheets in proportion to the number of examinees will be the responsibility of the chairpersons of the respective district/ regional/ state selection committees.
Before the issuance of this order, in all cases where the selection process has been started after the publication of the advertisement or the examinations are yet to be conducted, further proceedings should be completed by the selected OMR Vendor. For this, all the necessary information will be transferred from MahaIT to the respective selection committees.

A" Grievance Redressal Cell" should be set up to address grievances related to examinations during the period of recruitment.
Some of the Ministries/ Administrative Departments have prescribed different procedures for recruitment of direct services in the purview of Ex-Secondary Services Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) than the procedure prescribed by the General Administration Department (e.g., direct service recruitment to non-gazetted posts in Shikshan Sevaks (School Education and Sports Department), Police Constables (Home Department), Krishi Sevaks (Agriculture and Padum Department), Contractual Gram Sevaks (Rural Development and Water Conservation Department), Anganwadi Sevikas (Women and Child Development Department), Arogya Sevaks (Public Health Department), Municipalities/ Municipalities (Urban Development Department) etc.). All public sector undertakings, boards, corporations and directorates under the state government and all local bodies in the state will be able to opt for the revised examination system for recruitment to their respective posts.
