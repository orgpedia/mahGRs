Subject: Filling up of the post of Deputy Secretary in the office of the State Information Commission, Bench Amravati on deputation.
Request for Interpretation from the Deputy Secretary of the Ministry.
Mr. and Mrs.,
The post of Deputy Secretary in the Office of the State Information Commission, Bench Amravati
The Deputy Secretary of the Department is to be filled on deputation from the officers of the cadre.
Accordingly, all the Ministries/ Administrative Departments are requested to inform the State Information Commission,
Those desirous of going on deputation to the office of Deputy Secretary, Bench Amravati
The names of the deputy secretaries in your department, along with certified copies of their confidential reports for the past five years,
Personal information (bio-data), complete service details (duration, department, post), non-departmental enquiry
Provide information such as certificates and no-objection certificates of the department to this department. Definitely by 30.11.2021
It should be sent in the manner in which it is received. Also, if any officer is not willing, he should be asked to leave.
Ask the department to let you know.
