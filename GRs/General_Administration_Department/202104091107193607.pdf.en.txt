Government's decision:
Complete control, management and administration of the Chief Minister's Charitable Endowment Fund. The Chief Minister is in the chair. The Chief Minister's Charitable Endowment Fund provides financial assistance to social, educational, cultural, charitable or similar public or semi-public institutions or organizations in Maharashtra or outside the state. Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum, Pune On the recommendation of the committee headed by the Chief Secretary, the then Hon. The Chief Minister sanctioned Rs. 1 crore has been sanctioned. The first Rs. 50 lakh and the remaining Rs. 50 lakh has been sanctioned subject to the condition that the amount released in the first instalment should be released after receipt of utilization certificate from the Institute. Follow that. As per the Government Decision dated 30.5.2019, Rs. Rs 50 lakh was paid. The Institute has submitted utilization certificate of the said amount and the remaining Rs. 50 lakh to be utilised for the purpose. The Chief Minister has approved it.
Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum, Pune. Out of the financial assistance of Rs.1 crore sanctioned by the Chief Minister, the remaining amount of Rs.50 lakh (Rs.50 lakh only) is being sanctioned through this.
The Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum, Pune has been sanctioned funds to meet the pre-construction cost of the new building of the museum. The organization should spend the said fund for the same purpose and the utilization certificate certified by the" Chartered Accountant" in accordance with the expenditure should be submitted within six months of receipt of the fund. Also, the registered organization should submit the audit report and detailed statement of expenses to the government after conducting the audit as per the rules.
