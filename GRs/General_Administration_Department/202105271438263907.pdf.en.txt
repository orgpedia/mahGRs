Preamble: To advise/ make recommendations regarding the deposit of money collected under the Special Fund for Rehabilitation and Resettlement of Ex-Servicemen vide Notification dated 29/07/1968 in Government recognized Financial Institutions and Nationalized Banks. The investment sub-committee was constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Massac), General Administration Department.
The said Investment Sub-Committee was reconstituted vide Government Resolution, General Administration Division, No: 2006/1481/2006/ 28, dated 11/10/2006. As per the Government Resolution, General Administration Department, No: 2013/ (P.No. 370/13)/ 28, dated 19/10/2013, the said Investment Sub-Committee was headed by Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (MoS). General Administration Department has also been appointed as a member.
With the resignation of Air Vice Marshal (retd) M W Desai, a member of the investment sub-committee and investment adviser, as its member and investment adviser, the government was considering a fresh appointment to the post. Following this, the government took decision:

The government has taken a decision. Shri Shilanath Jadhav, Retired Director, Accounts and Treasury, State of Maharashtra is being appointed to the post of Investment Sub-Committee Member and Investment Adviser, which is vacant due to the resignation of Shri Desai (Retd.
