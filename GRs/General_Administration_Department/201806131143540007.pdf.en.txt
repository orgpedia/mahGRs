Tapase, Assistant Cell Officer, Revenue and Forest Department and Mr. A.S. Ghodke, Clerk Typist, Parliamentary Affairs Department are being appointed on deputation for a period of two years initially in the subordinate office of Maulana Azad Minorities Economic Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai subject to the following conditions:
(a) the service of the deputation shall commence on the date on which the employee hands over charge of the post in the Government service and shall end on the date on which the employee resumes charge of his Government post.
(b) If his services are required by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ Cadre Controller/ Competent Authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation. However, before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority and he shall be entitled to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that he intends to return to the parent department.
