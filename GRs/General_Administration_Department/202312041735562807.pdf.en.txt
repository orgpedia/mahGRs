the prelude
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) conducts the examination for recruitment to the posts of Group A and Group B. of this department. As per the Government decision dated 21.07.2017, a two-year post-graduate degree course has been prescribed for the officers under the said Schedules.
(2) In pursuance of the Government decision at Annexure No. 6, the Department of Home has prescribed the procedure for training of probationary officers of the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Police/ Assistant Commissioner of Police, Group-A, Superintendent (State Excise), Group-A, Deputy Superintendent (State Excise), Group-B, Assistant Commissioner (State Excise), Group-B, and Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Group-B in the post-graduate degree course.
Accordingly, the responsibility of training probationary officers of the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Police/ Assistant Commissioner of Police, Group-A, in session-2 and session-3 of the post-graduate degree course under CPTP has been fixed on the Maharashtra Police Academy. Reference No. The mandatory zonal affiliation as mentioned in the Government Resolution is included in Session-3 and Maharashtra Darshan and Parliament affiliation in Session-4.
The training provided through the Maharashtra Police Academy includes training within the department, and it has been observed that the Army, NDRF, MRA, rural and tribal villages, legal boards and judicial affiliations are difficult to be taken up through the Maharashtra Police Academy within the stipulated time. Accordingly, considering the difficulties faced while implementing Post Graduate Degree Courses under CPTP for the cadres of Deputy Superintendent of Police/ Assistant Commissioner of Police, Group-A,

Government's decision:
The number 6 is listed above. In the Government Resolution dated 25.09.2019, vide Para No. 12A (i) is being incorporated as follows:
The course of probationary officers of the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Police/ Assistant Commissioner of Police, Group-A, under the Home Department will be completed through the District Affiliation and Divisional Examination, Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik in Semester 3. Mandatory sectoral affiliation in semester 3 and Maharashtra Darshan and Parliament affiliation in semester 4 will be completed by YASHADA, Pune.
The above amendment will be applicable from the CPTP-9 batch.
