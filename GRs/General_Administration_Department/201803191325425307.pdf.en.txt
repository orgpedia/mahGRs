Appointment of Managing Director of Maharashtra Ex-Servicemen Corporation (MESCO) to implement rehabilitation and other welfare programmes for ex-servicemen in the State of Maharashtra.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MASSAM-2016/ PR No. 194/16/28
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: March 19, 2018.
Read: Government Resolution No. Masaim 1099/2390/ Q. No. 244/99/28, dated 18th January, 2002.
The Maharashtra Ex-Servicemen Corporation (MESCO) has been constituted under the Government Resolution, General Administration Department, No.: MASSAM- 1099/2390/ PR No. 244/99/28, dated 18th January, 2002, to implement the rehabilitation and other welfare programmes for ex-servicemen in the State of Maharashtra. As per decision No. 3 of the said Government Decision, it was also decided that the work of the Director of the said Corporation, Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune would be looked after as an additional charge. In view of the increased scope of the activities of the MESCO Corporation, the matter of appointing an independent officer to the post of Managing Director of the corporation was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 2. The additional charge of the post of Managing Director, Maharashtra Ex-Servicemen (MESCO) Corporation, which was entrusted to the Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune, vide Government Order dated 18th January, 2002, stands terminated with effect from the date of this Government Order.
Orders regarding appointment, service conditions, suspension, etc. of regular Managing Director to the post of Director of Maharashtra Ex-Servicemen Corporation shall be issued separately.
4. Under this Government decision, it is being approved to give temporary additional charge of the post of Managing Director of Maharashtra Ex-Servicemen Corporation to Deputy Secretary (Office Post-28 Ex-Servicemen Welfare), General Administration Department till the appointment of a regular Managing Director on the Maharashtra Ex-Servicemen Corporation. However, Deputy Secretary (Ex-Servicemen Welfare) (Shri S. M. Khade) is required to attend the Ministry four days a week.
