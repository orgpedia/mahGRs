2. m. The High Court, Bombay vide its judgment dated 04.08.2017 in Petition No. 2797/2015 has declared the reservation in promotion as invalid. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed in the Supreme Court. The petition is still pending. The Department of Law and Justice in its opinion dated 28.12.2017 has opined that there is no statutory impediment in granting promotion to open category employees. Accordingly, at present, the vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota are only temporary. It has to be done as per the Supreme Court's verdict. While taking such action, every Administrative Department shall ensure that the Backward Class officers/ employees in the Service Merit List do not come out on top of the Service Merit List by availing the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2004, before being promoted, the SPV/ Office Rule 16B vide letter dated 29.12.2017 has directed.
Accordingly, the Assistant Commissioner has been promoted to the rank of Assistant Commissioner. Subject to Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court, the vacancy in the open category in the quota of promotion is being made only on a temporary basis.
