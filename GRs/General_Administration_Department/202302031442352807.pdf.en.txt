The Rajiv Gandhi Science and Technology Commission has been constituted under Maharashtra Act No. 15 of 2004 with the objective of adopting, disseminating and promoting science and technology for the benefit of society in various social issues of the state. As per Rule 14 of the said Act, the Rajiv Gandhi Commission for Science and Technology (RGSCST) is funded by the Consolidated Fund of the Government in the form of grants-in-aid in each financial year.

Meanwhile, as per the Governing Decision dated 4 th April, 2022 of the Department of Finance, the Department is provided with only 60% of the total funds sanctioned for schemes under the programme, through Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEAMS).
