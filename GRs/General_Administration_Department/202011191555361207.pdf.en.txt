The date: November 19, 2020
General Administration Department, Government Resolution No.225/ 2018/28 dated 8.7.2019
Preamble: As per the decision taken in the meeting of Zila Sainik Welfare Officers held on 14th and 15th January, 2013 under the Chairmanship of Director, Sainik Welfare Department, Pune, a Patience Cell was established at Sainik Welfare Department, Pune with effect from 16th January, 2013 to enable the pensioners ex-servicemen/ widows of Maharashtra who are not getting pension as per the Sixth Pay Commission and its amendments. However, in 2018, the number of such cases has decreased and it has been observed that soldiers from all districts are finding it difficult to come to Pune for the same. At present, the scope of work in the Pension Cell of Sainik Welfare Department, Pune is very limited. Therefore, the matter of closing the pension cell was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Pension Cell set up at Sainik Welfare Department, Pune since 2013 to follow up on the pension due to pensioners ex-servicemen/ widows in Maharashtra as per the Sixth Pay Commission and its amendments is being closed down.
