Preamble As per the revised Indian Administrative Service (Fixation of Cadre), Amendment, 2015 notification dated 16.02.2015 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, the number of cadre posts in the Indian Administrative Service in the State has been fixed. Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix. Also, 7 more Non-Cadre posts (SDR) can be filled as per the provision of Rule 12 (7) of the Personnel (Pay) Rules, 2016. The question of creation of temporary posts in the cadre under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, the creation of 1 post in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix from 01.03.2018 to 30.04.2018 and 1 post from 01.05.2018 to 31.07.2018 is being approved subject to the proposal for revision of the cadre strength of the Central Government for the State of Maharashtra pending before it.
