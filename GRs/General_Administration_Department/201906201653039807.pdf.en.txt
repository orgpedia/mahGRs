The State Government implements various schemes, policies, schemes and programmes for the welfare of the people. Various publicity schemes/ campaigns are carried out by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations to make the general public aware of these schemes. 'Special Publicity Campaign' is one of these schemes. The government was considering to broadcast government messages on All India Radio, Cable TV, cinema halls and television channels from the provision made in the 2019-20 budget for this scheme. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
For the year 2019-20, Rs. Administrative and financial sanction is being accorded under this Government Resolution for expenditure on the following items amounting to Rs. 11,99,11, 050/- (Rupees Eleven Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Eleven Thousand Fifty only).
