As per the Government Decision dated 29th October, 2001, the modalities have been prescribed for complete computerization of Government activities. Every department shall constitute a Project Implementation Committee to implement the computerization projects under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary/ Secretary or Secretary level officers as may be directed by the Secretary. The committee will have secretary (mother), planning department and finance department as members. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to constitute a Project Implementation Committee for the purchase of computer related projects/ materials in the office of the Lok Ayukta and Upa Lokayukta, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, under the General Administration Department.
Government Decision: Approval is being given to constitute the following Project Implementation Committees for approving the proposals for procurement of projects/ materials for computerization of the office of the Lok Ayukta and Deputy Lok Ayukta, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.

The functions of the Project Implementation Committee are as follows:
To advise, co-ordinate and control the office of the Lokayukta and Upa Lokayukta, State of Maharashtra, on projects related to computerization and information technology.
To approve the proposal for the purchase of computerization projects/ computer materials up to Rs.5 crore related to the office of the Lok Ayukta and Upa Lokayukta, Maharashtra State.
To approve the proposal for procurement of computerization projects/ computer components for an amount not exceeding Rs. 5.00 crore. To recommend to the High Powered Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Chief Secretary.
