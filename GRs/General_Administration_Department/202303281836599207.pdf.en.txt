In the Preamble, the Brihanmumbai State Government Employees Association and the State Government Employees Central Association, Maharashtra had given a notice to the Government regarding the State-wide" indefinite strike" agitation from March 14, 2023, regarding various pending demands of the State Government employees. The government has appealed to employees not to participate in the strike. However, some employees participated in the protest. The indefinite strike called on 20.3.2023 was withdrawn after the Deputy Chief Minister assured to take necessary positive decision after the report of the three-member committee on the implementation of the old pension scheme. It was under the consideration of the government to regularize the absence of government employees who were absent in the office.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Brihanmumbai State Government Employees Association and the State Government Employees Central Association, Maharashtra have decided to regularize the absence of government employees who participated in the strike from March 14, 2023 to March 20, 2023 in connection with various pending demands of the state government employees as" extraordinary leave" without considering the break in service. However, the period of such extraordinary leave shall be deemed to be service qualifying for pension without any limitation of service, with the exception of Government Decision, Finance Division, No. Seniway 1001/29/ Service-4, dated 14.01.2001.
