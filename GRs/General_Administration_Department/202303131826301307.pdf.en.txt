circular
The Brihanmumbai State Government Employees Association and the State Government Employees Central Association, Maharashtra have served a notice to the Government regarding the State-wide" Unscheduled Strike" agitation from 14th March, 2023 in connection with various pending demands of the State Government employees.
As per Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979, a government employee is not supposed to participate in any strike/ direction. The Brihanmumbai State Government Employees Association is recognized under Rule No. 29 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. The Maharashtra State Government Employees Central Association is not a government recognized organization. However, the possibility of employees of all state government offices in the state joining the state-wide strike cannot be ruled out.
In order to ensure smooth functioning of Government/ Paramilitary activities on the day of State-wide indefinite strike called by Brihanmumbai State Government Employees Association and State Government Employees Central Association, Maharashtra from 14th March, 2023, all the Ministries/ Departments and Regional Offices under them are hereby directed to take the following measures: (a) Participation in the strike by Government/ Paramilitary employees will be treated as misconduct and disciplinary action will be taken against such employees.
A copy of the order should also be pasted on the notice board of every government department/ office.
b) Employees should try to persuade them to attend office as usual, without participating in the strike.
c) Proper arrangements should be made for opening and closing of the office at regular time during the strike period and the arrangements should be made by the responsible officer. If necessary, seek the help of the police.
(d) Heads of Departments/ Offices should not leave their headquarters during the strike period.
(e) The Head of Department/ Head of Office shall not grant leave of any kind to any Government employee from the date of this order till the end of the strike and the employee who is on leave.
