circular
The Ministry under the General Administration Department is responsible for the administrative side of the establishment of food/ restaurants, the Chief Clerk of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and the Senior Clerk/ Deputy Accountant/ Senior Storekeeper of Group-C and the Paying Accountant/ Accounts Clerk/ Storekeeper/ Clerk/ Clerk-Typist in the cadre of officers/ employees appointed through direct service/ compassionate/ promotion. 01.01.2016 to D. 31.12.2016 and D. 01.01.2017 to D. A provisional Common Seniority List for the period 31.12.2017 has been prepared and annexed to this Circular as per Annexure-" A."
The provisional common seniority list is being brought to the notice of all officers/ staff of Group-B and Group-C cadre. In case there are any errors and factual errors in the said provisional seniority list, the concerned should bring it to the notice of the Ministry Hotel Office through a written representation within one month from the date of this circular. Please note that in case of non-receipt of statement of errors and factual inaccuracies during the said period, the seniority list will be finalised assuming that there are no objections in the said seniority list.
