Ministry Group on Establishment of Food/ Restaurant D. Final Seniority List as on 01.01.2016 (Duration Dt. 01.01.2016 to D. 31.12.2016)
The Government of Maharashtra
General Administration Department (Restaurants)
Government Circular No: Aastha-2017/ PR No.275/ MANUUKA
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 13th of June 2018
See also: Government. Circular dated 11.10.2017
circular
As per the circular, the Ministry under the General Administration Department has decided to appoint Group-D cooks/ tea experts, coupon sellers, etc. in the establishment of food/ restaurants. The staff members who have been appointed through direct service/ compassionate/ additional cadre cells in the cadre of cook/ tea maker, supervisor, sweeper, waiter, carpenter, boy waiter, peon and porter. 01.01.2016 to D. Provisional Common Seniority List was prepared on 31.12.2016. No objections have been received from the employees. However, the common seniority list has been finalised after taking into account the errors/ factual errors. The said seniority is annexed to this circular as Annexure A, B, C and D.
The Ministry shall inform all the managers of the food/ restaurant, the final seniority of which shall be brought to the notice of all Group-D employees under its command.
