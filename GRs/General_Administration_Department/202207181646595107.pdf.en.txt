The Proposal:
The pattern of posts in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations was decided by the Government decision dated 28.02.2006 in Reference-1. The Finance Department had directed the administrative departments to review the sanctioned posts in the offices under their control and get the approval of the high-level committee of secretaries for the revised pattern. Accordingly, after reviewing the posts in the office of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations under the control of the General Administration Department, the proposal was presented in the meeting of the Sub-Committee and Scrutiny Committee constituted under the Government Decision of the Finance Department in Reference No. 6 dated November 21, 2019. The screening committee approved a total of 1003 posts against the 1117 revised posts submitted for the office of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations under the General Administration Department and informed that the proposal would be submitted to the high-level committee of secretaries for final approval. According to A proposal to finalize the contours for the Directorate of Information and Public Relations and subordinate offices was submitted in the meeting of the High Level Committee of Secretaries chaired by the Chief Secretary on June 14, 2021.
(2) The High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the revised pattern of 1003 posts in 52 cadres in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, Mumbai and its subordinate field offices. The High Level Committee of Secretaries has recommended a total of 56 posts in Group-C cadre (receptionist-4, senior clerk-8, short story writer-2, cinematographer-2, clerk-typist-17, driver-23 and 58 posts in Group-D cadre (receptionist/ roneo operator-9, peon (porter, cleaner, cleaner, attendant etc.)

As many as 114 posts have been cancelled. Accordingly, the matter of approving the revised pattern of 1003 posts was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The revised pattern of posts of Director General, Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and its Subordinate Regional Offices has been notified on 14th June, 2021. The approval is being given as per the approval given in the meeting of the High Level Committee of Secretaries chaired by the Chief Secretary.
(1) As per the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries, the Government is approving the delineation of a total of 1003 posts in 52 cadres in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations and Subordinate Offices as shown in the attached statement-1. The details of the total 37 posts of Group-D cadre which have been abolished are shown in the Statement-2. 56 posts in Group-C cadre and 58 posts in Group-D cadre have lapsed and their details have been shown in Returns-3 and 4 respectively.
The department-wise and district-wise number of posts of Group-A to Group-D is shown in Form-1 to 4.
(2) The posts of (i) Deputy Editor, Information Assistant, Receptionist-cum-Information Assistant in Group-C cadre of Directorate General of Information and Public Relations are hereby abolished and replaced by the posts of (ii) Librarian-cum-Reference List Assistant,) Librarian (Goa) and replaced by the posts of (iii) Librarian-cum-Reference List Assistant, (iv) Assistant Superintendent/ Accountant, Film Publicity Organizer, Chief Clerk-cum-Recovery Officer, Assistant (Delhi) and replaced by the posts of (iv) Receptionist, Translator, Reporter, Urdu Translator and replaced by the posts of (v) Reporter, Receptionist, Urdu Translator (v) Supervisor (Publications Cell).
(3) The Director, Information and Public Relations Office, Mumbai shall take necessary action for making necessary budgetary provision for the salary and allowances for the sanctioned posts in the revised pattern.
