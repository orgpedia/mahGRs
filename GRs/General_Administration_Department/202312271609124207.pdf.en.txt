Circular:
The birth anniversaries and National Days in 2024 should be celebrated in Ministries and all Government/ Paramilitary offices as per the schedule attached.
The programmes mentioned in the Annexure shall fall on public holidays, weekly holidays (Saturdays and Sundays) and local holidays and shall be celebrated in accordance with the changes suggested by the Central Government in this regard, otherwise they shall be celebrated on the same day.
The Divisional Commissioner/ Collector shall issue necessary instructions regarding the conduct of the said programme in all the government/ semi-government offices of his department/ district and take appropriate action regarding their implementation.
A separate government circular will be issued regarding the inclusion of Lord Sarvagya Sri Chakradhar Swami Avatar Day in the birth anniversary program.
