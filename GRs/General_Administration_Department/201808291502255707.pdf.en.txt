the Government Order
The Department of General Administration, Government Decision No: SRV 2011/ PR No.137/ Q. 12, dated 17.12.2016 has laid down the policy for deputation of officers/ staff of the ministerial cadre. No. 120/14, dated 31.8.2017 has been prescribed by the Government. The services of Renge, Assistant Cell Officer, Planning Department are being made available on deputation in the post of" Assistant Cell Officer" in the office of Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission, State Mission Management Cell, Cidco Bhawan, 5th Floor, (South Cell) CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai under the aegis of Rural Development Department.
Relaxation of the service condition of 7 years in the Deputation Policy of Renge, Assistant Cell Officer, Planning Department, they are being deputed on deputation for a period of 2 years initially to the State Mission Management Cell, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai under Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission subject to the following conditions:
(a) the service of the deputation shall commence on the date on which the employee hands over charge of the post in the Government service and shall end on the date on which the employee resumes charge of his Government post.
If his services are required by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ Cadre Controller/ Competent Authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and (d) he shall be entitled to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that he intends to return to the parent department.
