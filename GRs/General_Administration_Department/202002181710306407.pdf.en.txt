As per the Maharashtra Right to Service Act, 2015, the post of State Right to Service Commissioner, Konkan Revenue Department is held by Smt. Medha Gadgil, Retd. 1 here. The appointment has been made vide notification dated 13.09.2019. The matter of providing them space for the office of the State Right to Service Commissioner, Konkan Revenue Department was under the consideration of the government. In this regard, the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division has informed that the 7th floor space in Belapur Bhavan building and the space occupied by Cidco Corporation in Vashi Railway Complex are vacant for the office. In such a situation, it is necessary to provide space to the State Right to Service Commissioner, Konkan Revenue Department to make it possible to run the affairs in the Konkan Division area as per the provisions of the said Act. Therefore, as a temporary arrangement, he has to be given room number 725, a government place under the control of the office of the President, District Caste Verification Committee, Thane, Konkan Bhavan, Navi Mumbai, to set up the office.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division has issued the following order in respect of the office of the State Right to Service Commissioner, Konkan Division, to be started immediately in the jurisdiction of Konkan Revenue Division. 2 As informed in the letter, room no. 725 under the control of the office of the Chairman, District Caste Verification Committee, Thane, Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai is being made available temporarily for the office of the State Right to Service Commissioner, Konkan Division.
The State Right to Service Commissioner, Konkan Division shall take possession of the said place immediately from the Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division and establish his office there.
