The services of Koyande, Cell Officer, Finance Department are being handed over on deputation for a period of two years to the office of the State Chief Commissioner of Service Rights, Nirmal Bhawan, 2nd Floor, Nariman Point, Mumbai under the Maharashtra State Commission for the Right to Service subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mr. Koyande shall commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner from the date on which he enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his original office after demitting the office of the Deputy Commissioner, complete two years of his service.
(b) If his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of his deputation.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
Mr. Koyande will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ Competent Authority that he intends to return to the parent department.
