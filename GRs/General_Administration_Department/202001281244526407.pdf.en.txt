General Elections 2019: State, District and Tehsil level gazetted officers to be paid remuneration
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Corrigendum No.CEL-2019/ PR No. 1396/19/33
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: January 28th, 2020
Reference- Government Resolution No: CEL-2019/ PR No. 1396/19/33 (1) dated 09.12.2019
Government Papers-
As per the decision of the Government in this regard, the State/ District/ Tehsil level Gazetted Officers, who are directly involved in election work, have been allowed to draw remuneration/ honorarium equivalent to their basic salary for the month of October, 2019, from the date of notification of General Election to the Legislative Assembly, till the completion of the election process on October 27, 2019.
In this Government decision, paragraph 1 (a) of State No. 12 shall be read as follows:
12. Gazetted officers whose services have been acquired by the office of the Chief Electoral Officer and whose services have been made available by the General Administration Department. "
This PDF is being issued with concurrence of Department of Finance under Non-Formal Reference No.: 315/19/ Service-6, dated 13.01.2020 and Non-Formal Reference No.: 34/20/ Expenditure-4, dated 14.01.2020.
