Preamble: - To promote research, development, skill development, technology innovation and policy making in Maharashtra, to accelerate R & D of AI, to find new ideas, technologies and solutions for the progress of the state, to create a Centre of Excellence and Innovation globally in coordination with the Government of India, through the Department of Task Force under the above reference. The Task Force has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, IT vide Government Decision dated 02.02.2025. The task force was required to submit recommendations to the government within 3 months.
However, the government was considering extending the task force as it would take some time for the committee to submit its recommendations.
The Government's Decisions:
Centre of Excellence and Innovation. A Task Force under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, IT has been constituted under the Government Decision dated 02.02.2025. The task force was required to submit recommendations to the government within 3 months. However, since the committee will need some more time to submit its recommendations, the task force has sought reference no. 2 The extension was granted by a government decision here. The task force could not submit its report despite being given an extension of one month.
The deadline is being extended.
