Government circular:
Reference No. (2) and (3) respectively, the Interim Common Seniority as on 01.01.2017 and the Provisional Additional Common Seniority as on 01.01.2018 of the eligible Assistant Cell Officers for promotion to the post of Cell Officers were published.
In view of the objections, objections and errors received on the said seniority list, the Interim Common Seniority Notice dated 01.01.2018 of the Assistant Cell Officer eligible for promotion to the post of Cell Officer is now being published and is annexed to this circular.
Clerk Typists are promoted to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the 30% promotion quota as per the rules of entry into service of the Assistant Cell Officer cadre. As the process of regular promotion of Clerk-Typist to the post of Assistant Cell Officer is underway for various administrative reasons, such Assistant Cell Officers have not been included in the said seniority list. As soon as the process of regular promotion of clerical typists to the post of assistant cell officer is completed, the assistant cell officer of the promoted quota will be included in the seniority list.
The names of the Assistant Cell Officers who have not yet received orders from the concerned department regarding exemption from passing the post-service entrance examination and departmental examination for promotion to the post of Cell Officer, have not been included in the said interim common seniority list.
