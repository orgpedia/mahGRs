GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the determination of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.
As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities has been published.
As mentioned in the notification, the posts ensured for the disabled by the Central Government are in the state government establishments, in respect of such posts, the posts in the list published by the Central Government whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, even if the designations of such posts are different, the posts in the state government service will be ensured for the disabled. The proposal for the remaining 6 posts was approved by the Expert Committee headed by the Principal Secretary, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department. was presented in the meeting dated 22.02.2021. The recommendations of the expert committee were considered. The Expert Committee recommends that the Establishment Department should decide on the posts of deputation of 1) Law Officer, 2) Accounts Officer, 3) Statistical Officer and 4) Legal Assistant in the Commission.
Since these posts are stand-alone and on deputation and the posts to be filled by the deputation are available from the parent establishment/ administrative department, the terms and conditions for the same are mentioned in the service entry rules of the respective offices. Also, as per the recommendation of the expert committee, the post of driver is being excluded from ensuring for the disabled.
Apart from this, the post of examination research officer is being ensured for the disabled as per the accompanying prospectus as recommended by the expert committee.
