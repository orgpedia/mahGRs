Government Circular: Applications are hereby invited in the prescribed format from eligible candidates for the training and examination to be conducted in accordance with the examination to be held in 2019 for promotion to the post of Assistant Cell Officer in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission: As per the provisions of Rule 3 of the Maharashtra Government Secondary Ministerial Services Departmental Examination Rules, 1977, the regular Assistant Cell Officers working in the Administrative Department of the Ministry as well as in the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission who are completing five years of uninterrupted service as on November 1, 2019 in the post of Assistant Cell Officer and who have passed the post-service training examination, shall submit the applications in the prescribed format as per Schedule B of Rule 16 of the Cell Officers Departmental Examination Rules, 1977 to their administrative departments. The prescribed format of applications has been made available under the heading" Application Form" on the Ministry Intranet.
The eligible Assistant Section Officers as above who have not availed the training of this Departmental Examination earlier are applying for this examination for the first time. Submissions must be received by August 9, 2019.
The applications of the Assistant Cell Officers who have failed in the departmental examination held earlier or have remained absent in the examination to be held in the year 2019 should be sent as and when called for by a separate circular.
4. Establishment Officers of all Administrative Departments may kindly forward this circular to all concerned Assistant Cell Officers (including Assistant Cell Officers on deputation/ leave).
