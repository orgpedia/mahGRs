GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Department of General Administration (itself) has received a proposal from Lista& Co., Ballard Estate, Mumbai for supply of Recycling Toner for the period 2019-2020. 01.12.2019 to dt. Government Decision for the period 30.11.2020, d. The appointment was made on 30.11.2019.
