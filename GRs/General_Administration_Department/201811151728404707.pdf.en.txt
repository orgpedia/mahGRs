Policy on Honouring/ Reasonably Honouring Persons Who Fought for Democracy During Emergency Period from 1975 to 1977
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Corrigendum No.Nivruve-1015/ P.S. 261/2015 (2018)/ SWASAIC-1, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 32
The date is November 15, 2018.
Reference:- Government Decision No.Nivruve-1015/ P.S. 261/2015 (2018)/ SWASAC-1 dated 3rd July, 2018
Government Corrigendum: In pursuance of the decision of the Government in question, it is informed that a policy has been formulated to honour/ suitably honour the persons who had to undergo imprisonment for fighting for democracy during the Emergency period from 1975 to 1977.
In the aforesaid Government Decision No. 03 is being amended as follows:
03:-" The concerned Collector shall have the power to sanction honorarium to persons arrested for political or social reasons under MISA and DIR (Emergency). The list of persons who have been sanctioned honorarium by the concerned Collector will be submitted to the government. Based on this list, the government will provide funds to the concerned district collector's office. "
