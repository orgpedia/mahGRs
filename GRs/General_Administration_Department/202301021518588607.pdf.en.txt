Floral tribute to Savitribai Phule on her birth anniversary.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Circular No: Japuti-2022/ PR No. 243/ Works.29,
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: January 2, 2023.
Birth anniversaries and National Days of the great men and women of the nation are proposed to be celebrated in 2023. Accordingly, on the occasion of the birth anniversary of Savitribai Phule, a function to offer floral tributes to her statue should be celebrated on Tuesday, January 03, 2023 in the Ministry and all Government/ Paramilitary offices.
The Divisional Commissioner/ Collector shall issue necessary instructions regarding the conduct of the said programme in all the government/ semi-government offices of his department/ district and take appropriate action regarding their implementation.
