Preamble The revised figure of 20 posts, including 8 regular posts and 12 supernumerary posts, on the establishment of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad office, has been approved under the Government decision dated 02.02.2021 of this Department. Accordingly, the vacancies in the above-mentioned posts will lapse as and when they become vacant on account of retirement, resignation, death, etc. The Finance Department has given instructions to hire multi-purpose services for these posts. In this regard, it was under the consideration of the government to issue comprehensive instructions as per the feedback given by the Finance Department regarding multi-purpose services i.e. taking the services of employees on contract basis through external agencies.
Government's decision:
The following instructions are being given for the work of taking multi-purpose services in the majority of posts on the establishment of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad: a) Taking multi-purpose services i.e. without appointing separate staff for different work.
Many different types of work by the same person. The workers have to do the cleaning and mopping work.
(b) The work to be carried out by external agencies means that the work required to be carried out by the contractor/ company/ institution by inviting tenders in the prescribed manner. Contract with the company/ organization you want to work for. It should be ensured that the responsibility of the officers/ employees working on behalf of the company/ institution in this agreement will not be on the government.
(c) the payment of wages for work done by an external agency, not to be shown under the heading' Salaries"
It is necessary to indicate under the detailed heading" Office expenses."
d) In the case of taking services of multi-purpose nature through external agencies, the Finance Department, Government Circular No.
The instructions issued under 27.09.2010, 2.2.2013, 2.12.2013, 2.8.2016 and 30.09.2020 as well as from time to time shall be followed.
