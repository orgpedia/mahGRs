A copy of the official memorandum dated 42/02/2024 -P & PW (D) 08/10/2025 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Government of India, Department of Pension & Pensioners "Welfare, New Delhi forwarded to the All India Service Pensioner / Family Pensioner Officer of Maharashtra State Cadre for information and appropriate action.
As per the above mentioned Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Government of India, the rate hike in Dearness Relief @ 3% with effect from the date 01/07/2025 and other provisions mentioned in the Memorandum shall also be applicable to All India Service Pensioners / Family Pensioners of Maharashtra Cadre. Accordingly, Dearness Allowance at the rate of 58 per cent will now be admissible to All India Service Pensioners / Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre with effect from the date 01/07/2025.
The above page listing is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
