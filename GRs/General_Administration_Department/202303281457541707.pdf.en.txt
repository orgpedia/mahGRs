As on 20/01/2014, a Combined Competitive Training Programme (CPTP) is being implemented for Group-A and Group-B officers who are directly recruited in the Government service and the funds to be made available per trainee per day for training has been fixed by the General Administration Department, Government Resolution, dated 08/08/2014.
Accordingly, in the Budget Year 2022-23, for the mandatory attachment of CPTP-7 and 8 and for the training phase of Maharashtra Study Tour and Delhi Study Tour, Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (Yashada) has sanctioned Rs. 1,01,15, 000/- was sought under Reference No. 3. Of these, Rs. 66,99,840/- has been made available to YASHADA, Pune vide Government Resolution in Reference No.4. The remaining Rs. 34,15,160/- was under the consideration of the Government to make available to Yashada, Pune.
Government Decision In the financial year 2022-2023, Rs.31 crore has been allocated for training of CPTP-7 and CPTP-8 under Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary). 66,99,840/- has been made available and the remaining Rs. 34,15,160/- has been approved by the Department of Finance to be made available on the BEAMS system.
