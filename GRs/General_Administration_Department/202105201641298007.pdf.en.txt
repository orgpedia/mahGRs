The Proposal:
Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has invited applications for recruitment to various posts through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC).
In order to inculcate team spirit among the officers, to develop friendly and cordial relations among them and to ensure their all-round development, the Government has taken a policy decision to start a two-year integrated probationary training programme for the officers of the State Services on the lines of the All India Services. The government has taken a decision. As per the provisions of the said Government Decision, the candidates recommended by the State Service Examination of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission are provided training under the Integrated Sub-Control Training Programme. Currently, there are 26 programs in the program.
The new cadres are included in this training program with the aim of ensuring that the comprehensive training provided in the combined probationary training program and the guidance of expert lecturers and officers are available to the candidates of other cadres of the state government. Now Assistant Labour Commissioner, Group-A and Government Labour Officer, Group-B under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour will be included in the State Service Examination.
