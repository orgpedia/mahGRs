The Proposal:
The Government decision dated 17.2.2021 at Reference No.7 is hereby cancelled.
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in government service for the disabled. The central government has given. The list of posts notified for Persons with Disabilities under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of Post identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. The Social Justice and Special Assistance Department of the state government has issued a notification in this regard. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, New Delhi. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. These include posts in Group' A" to Group' D," as defined by the Central Government for persons with disabilities, in State Government establishments, and in the list published by the Central Government for posts having duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale similar to those in the State Government service.

However, those posts in the state government service will be ensured for the disabled, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance said. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021. As per the directions of this notification and the Government decision, the matter of ensuring posts for Divyang category in Group-A to Group-D cadre in the office of Additional Chief Secretary and Resident Commissioner, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi under the General Administration Department was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Issued by the Central Government. In view of the posts ensured for Divyangjan in the notification dated 04.01.2021, the Government have decided to ensure that the posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre in the office of the Additional Chief Secretary and Resident Commissioner, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi under the General Administration Department, as shown in the accompanying return-A for Divyangjan respectively.
