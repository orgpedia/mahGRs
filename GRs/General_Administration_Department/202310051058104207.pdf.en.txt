the General Administration Department

Date: October 5, 2023
Preamble:- The Government was considering providing an effective platform to promote innovation, incubation and entrepreneurship in the State of Maharashtra. Following this, the government took decision on this issue.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to set up a Maha-Hub, Incubator and Innovation Center in Thane district of Maharashtra.
A not-for-profit company should be set up as per Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 to run mega-hubs, incubators and innovation centres.
Board of Directors:
The Board of Directors of the Maha-Hub, Incubator and Innovation Centre Company will consist of nine Directors from the following areas:
Four directors of companies in the industry/ venture capital/ private equity fund sector with a contribution of Rs.10 crore each, three directors, one each in the rank of senior level dean from three premium educational institutions namely IIT-B, NLU-Mumbai, IIM-Mumbai, Principal Secretary, Director of Information Technology, 4. A representative of SIDBI- Director
The primary responsibilities of the Board of Directors:
Maha-Hub, Incubator and Innovation Center will be able to recruit full-time managers of the level of CEO, Chief Innovation Officer, Chief Operating Officer, etc. of the company to handle the administrative and technical aspects of the company on a contractual basis and all these appointments will be made with the approval of the Board of Directors and on a contractual basis.

The primary responsibilities of the Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation are:
The appointment of the initial stage CEO in order to establish the company and take further action should be completed by Maha-IT, Mumbai within 15 days from the date of this Government decision. Thereafter, the CEO of the Company shall be competent to deal with all further matters in relation to the Company with the approval of the Board of Directors.
The transfer of land:
For the company Maha-Hub, Incubator and Innovation Center, 25 hectares of encroachment-free Gayran land owned by the state government has been selected in Mauje Antarli, Khoni, G. Thane.
The planning of the construction:
The CEO of the company has selected a suitable place for setting up the company in the proposed 25 hectares of land and in the first phase, 6 lakh sq. The next step should be to start planning the construction.
The state government will sanction Rs 500 crore for the first phase of setting up the mega-hub, incubator and innovation centre company. Till the time the standalone capital account head of the company becomes operational, the fund will be kept under the head of accounting under the Department of Information Technology. The Department of Information Technology should submit a proposal seeking funds to the Finance Department. The Department of Information Technology will act as the nodal department for the construction of the mega-hub, incubator and innovation centre, while the Industries Department and Maharashtra State Industrial Development Corporation will provide necessary support.
Responsibilities of Director (IT):
5 crore will be given as seed capital from the Innovation Fund planned by the District Planning and Development Board Thane (DPDC) to the Zilla Parishad, Thane for the construction of the Maha-Hub. The fund will be used for preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR), appointment of early stage staff, consultants, technical assistants/ partners, project management consultants, architects, etc. and for meeting other complex expenditure of the company.
High-Level Committee: A high-level committee should be constituted to provide strategic guidance and vision to the under-construction Maha-Hub, Incubator and Innovation Centre.

The committee will have the power to appoint world-class architects for the construction and construction of the Maha-Hub Incubator and Innovation Center building and the planning of world-class facilities there. All the decisions of the committee will be binding on the district council. All the reports/ inspections/ recommendations made by the DPR staff, consultants, technical assistants, Vastu Visharad should be handed over to the committee by these Zilla Parishad Thana from time to time.
