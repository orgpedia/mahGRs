Preamble: The Government has taken a policy decision to provide two years of training under the Integrated Probationary Training Programme to the officers to be appointed in the Government service through the State Service Examination by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. In accordance with the provisions of this Government Decision, the training program is provided to the recommended candidates based on the final results of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission's State Service Examination in the cadre of Municipal Corporation/ Municipal Council, Headmaster Group-A and Group-B under the Urban Development Department. The various stages and their duration of the two-year training to be conducted under the Combined Probationary Training Programme have been prescribed under the Government Decision referred to in Annexure II hereunder.
2. A proposal was received from the Urban Development Department to provide only six weeks of basic training to the selected candidates under the combined probationary training program of the Municipal Corporation/ Municipal Council, Headmaster Group-A and Group-B cadres under the results of the State Service Examination-2017 through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and to exclude these candidates from the said training program and appoint them in the form of" On Feild Training" through the Urban Development Department.
At present, a total of 24 cadres are trained under the Combined Probationary Training Programme. Probationary Officers of all these cadres are required to complete training under the Combined Probationary Training Programme and complete the Post Graduate Degree Course as per the provisions of the Government Decision referred to in Annexure 3 above. As proposed by the Urban Development Department, the candidates belonging to the Municipal Corporation/ Municipal Council, Headmaster Group-A and Group-B are appointed in the form of" On Feild Training," the training/ post-graduate course for these cadres will not be completed as per the prescribed rules and schedule of the training programme. Similarly, Probationary Officers of all the cadres covered under this training programme have completed 2 years of training programme

After that, the appointment is made. Therefore, considering the possibility of discrepancies in the appointment of candidates from the Municipal Corporation/ Municipal Council, Headmaster Group-A and Group-B cadres in the form of" On Feild Training," the Government is taking the following decisions in accordance with the above proposal.
Government Decision As the Government policy is to provide uniform training to all the cadres under the Combined Probationary Training Programme, the Government is taking the following decisions regarding the training of the cadres of Municipalities/ Municipal Councils, Headmasters Group-A and Group-B under the Urban Development Department.
The candidates selected to the posts of Municipal Corporation/ Municipal Council, Headmaster Group-A and Group-B in the cadre of the State Service Examination by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission are henceforth excluded from the training of the Combined Probationary Training Program.
Therefore, the provisions set by various government decisions, circulars, notifications, etc. will no longer be applicable to the candidates selected to the posts of Nagar Palika/ Nagar Parishad, Headmaster Group-A and Group-B cadres through the State Service Examination by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
From the time of sending the requisition letters of these cadres to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for appointment in the cadres of Municipal Corporation/ Municipal Council, Headmaster Group-A and Group-B through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, their appointment, conducting their training and other related matters will be done by the Urban Development Department as the administrative department concerned with this category.
