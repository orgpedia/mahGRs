The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has invited applications from eligible candidates for recruitment to the post of Group B Officer on the basis of State Service (Main) Examination, 2015 and 2016. Under the Government Decision 2& 3, the establishment of General Administration Department was sanctioned an amount of Rs. 9300-34800, Grade Pay Rs. More number of posts have been created in the pay scale of Rs.4800/-.
Training programme for 17 probationary officers undergoing training under CPTP-4
During the period, for the independent charge of the post of Cell Officer, the reference No. The Ministerial Order 4
departments have been appointed. Since a large number of posts created on the establishment of the General Administration Department for these probationary cell officers are expiring on 31.07.2019, they are required to be given regular posting in the vacant posts of cell officer cadre.
Government Decision: The following are the present cadre of Cell Officers- Group B undergoing training under CPTP-4 as recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of State Service (Main) Examination-2015 and 2016
17 Subordinate Cell Officers are being posted in the Department indicated against their names with effect from 01.08.2019.
