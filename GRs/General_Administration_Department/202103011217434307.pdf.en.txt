General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. 2020/ PR No. 126/ Work 28, dated 15.02.2021.
Government Decision: As per Section-33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in government service for the disabled. Instructions regarding ensuring the posts for the disabled in the state government establishments as per the notification of the Central Government dated 04.01.2021 have been issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. As per Government Decision dated 02.02.2021. The posts in Group A to D cadre in the office of Sainik Welfare Department, Pune, under the control of this Department, as per the posts ensured under the Central Government notification dated 04.01.2021, have been ensured for Divyang by the Government decision dated 15.02.2021.
The duties and responsibilities of the posts of Exercise Instructor and Director of Physical Training in the Office of Sainik Welfare Department, Pune are different in nature and are not included in the list of vacancies for Divyangjan as per the Central Government notification dated 04.01.2021. Therefore, the proposal for ensuring the posts for the disabled in these posts was submitted to the expert committee constituted by the Government vide letter dated 12.11.2020 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. The committee As per the recommendation of the Expert Committee in its meeting held on 22.2.2021, the Department of Sainik Welfare, Pune has appointed a Training Instructor in the office of the Department of Military Affairs, Pune.
