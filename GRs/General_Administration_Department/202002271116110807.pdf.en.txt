The Proposal:
5 temporary posts in the office of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad, have been filled up vide Government Resolution No. SPI-2018/ PR No. 409/ K. 28 dated 19/10/2019 was approved to be continued from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020. Now under the Government decision dated 12/02/2020 of the Finance Department, the Administrative Departments have been permitted to continue the temporary posts from 01/03/2020 to 31/08/2020.
Government's decision
The Director, Institute of Military Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad vide his letter dated 11/02/2020 has proposed that a total of 5 temporary posts on the establishment of the Institute of Military Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad are not vacant for more than six months and they are required to be continued.
