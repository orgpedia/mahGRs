The Proposal:
A Government Order dated 17.11.2018 has been issued in respect of room booking and other facilities at New Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi. Accordingly, a Government Order dated 19.10.2020 has been issued in respect of the reservation of rooms and other facilities at Old Maharashtra Sadan. As per the decision of the government, rooms and other facilities are provided in the Maharashtra Sadan. The government has amended the rules from time to time. 2.1 (xvi) dated 17.11.2018 for New Maharashtra Sadan and 2.1 (16) dated 19.10.2020 for Old Maharashtra Sadan, 10% of the monthly basic pay shall be charged for allotment of a temporary accommodation to an officer transferred or deputed from Maharashtra to the Central Government till he gets permanent/ regular accommodation from the Central Government. However, the period of stay cannot be extended beyond 3 months. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to revise the rates to be charged for long-term use of the rooms at Maharashtra Sadan by the officers/ employees.
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to impose the following rates and provisions for long-term use of the premises at New Maharashtra Sadan and Junee Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi by the officers/ employees.

Further, the following conditions shall apply to the officers/ employees who are staying.
1) If the employees working in the new Maharashtra Sadan are allotted a room, then the transport allowance will not be permissible for them.
(2) The office of the Additional Chief Secretary and the Resident Commissioner shall have the authority to collect the fee of the room on the 7th of every month if the room is used for accommodation.
(3) If the officers/ employees mentioned in section 1 (a) (b) and 2 (a) of the head are not receiving house rent allowance, they will have to pay the fee as per the rates per room per day while on duty as per the Government decision in force at that time.
