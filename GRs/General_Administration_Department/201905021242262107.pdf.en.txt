Achyut Pandharinath Ipper, a Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) awardee, is currently working as an Assistant (presently designated as Assistant Cell Officer) with merit number 2/2009 (Open Category). General Administration Department/ Office 14-A vide letter dated 25.04.2011, Mr. Ipper was appointed as Assistant in Revenue and Forest Department and he was informed to accept the appointment within 30 days from the date of issue of the letter. The order also stated that if the appointment is not accepted within this period, seniority will not be available as per the merit order given by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. This 30-day period was ending on 24.05.2011.
Mr. Ipper was working as Sub-Inspector of Police since 01.09.2009. Mr. Ipper had applied to the Commissioner of Police, Mumbai, requesting to relieve him to join the new post of Assistant. As per this request, the proposal for the release of Mr. Ipper was being processed by the concerned agencies. Accordingly, Shri Ipper vide letter dated 23.05.2011 had requested the Department to grant extension of 07 days for accepting appointment to the post of Ministerial Assistant. Pursuant to the said request, vide letter dated 03.06.2011 of General Administration Department/ Office 14-A, Shri Ipper was allowed to join the office of his appointment on or before 05.06.2011.
In the meantime, under the orders of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, dated 31.05.2011 and the orders of the Senior Inspector of Police, Airport Police Station, Mumbai, dated 31.05.2011, Mr. Ipper has been directed to report to the place of his new posting from the post of Sub-Inspector of Police under probation, on 31.05.2011. In view of the aforesaid request made by Mr. Ipper and the delay caused to him in joining the post of Assistant Cell Officer on administrative grounds, the Government hereby approve the grant of appointment of Mr. Ipper to the post of Assistant Cell Officer as per Rule 5 (2) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, with effect from 28.04.2011.
Since Mr. Ipper was given the date of appointment as Assistant Cell Officer on 28.04.2011, the date of promotion of eligible Assistant Cell Officer to the post of Cell Officer is 01.01.2017 and
