3. Thematic work as mentioned in the above table Rs. 10,78,49, 075/- (Rupees Ten Crores Eighty Eight Lakhs Nineteen Thousand Seventy Five only) is hereby accorded administrative approval by this Order subject to the following conditions.
These warehouses, which will be repaired for permanent storage of EVMs and VVPATs, will be constructed at a minimal cost and as per the guidelines of the Election Commission of India.
The Collector will be responsible for ensuring quality, for which the e-tendering system should be used if necessary.
b) The Election Commission of India (ECI) has issued a notification for the storage of EVMs and VVPAT machines. 08.08.2017, Dy. 12.03.2019 as well as issued by the Election Commission of India from time to time.
Considering the guidelines, it should be ensured that the construction work of this warehouse will be quality.
(c) Use of e-tendering system for procurement related matters in the proposal or in the case of industries, energy and
Department of Labour. The GeM portal developed by the Central Government for procurement of goods and services under the Government decision dated 24.08.2017 should be used.
d. The work should be completed as soon as possible with care that no revised administrative approval is given for the thematic work.
Before starting the work, the design map, layout map and detailed map should be started only after getting approval from the architect.
