General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. 295/16-B, dated 11.05.2021
Government Decision: Supreme Court, Civil Appeal No. 3123/2020, Jayashree Lakshmanrao Patil Vs. Chief Minister, Maharashtra and others. A Committee under the Chairmanship of Mr. Dilip Bhosale, Former Judge, Allahabad High Court and Former Judge, Bombay High Court has been constituted to review the judgment dated 05.05.2021 passed by the Court. The committee has been asked to submit its report to the government. The deadline has been extended to June 7, 2021.
Shri Bhupender Gurav, Secretary, Department of Law& Justice, Ministry of Law& Justice is retiring on 31st May, 2021. They are allowed to continue as members of the committee till the report of the committee is submitted to the government.
