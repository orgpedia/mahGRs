Due to heavy rains in Mumbai and Mumbai Suburbs and Thane on Tuesday, July 2, 2019, a special casual leave has been approved for this day.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Circular No: VINESU-1519/ PR No. 96/29
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: July 31, 2019
Circular: Due to heavy rains in Mumbai and Mumbai Suburbs and Thane on Tuesday, July 2, 2019, the flood-like situation, waterlogging on the roads, closure of rail traffic on all routes, the officers/ employees of government/ semi-government offices in Mumbai and Mumbai Suburbs and Thane were unable to attend office, the matter of granting special casual leave was under consideration of the government.
In this regard, the government has decided that on Tuesday, July 2, 2019, all the officers/ employees of government/ semi-government offices in Mumbai and Mumbai Suburban and Thane should be granted special casual leave for that day for the above-mentioned reasons.
