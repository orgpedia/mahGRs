Government Resolution No: Law-1123/ No. 09/14-B
The request for deletion of the names of Mr. Deshpande and Mr. Salunkhe had been received from the Department earlier also and accordingly their names were deleted from the previous training session. It is hereby clarified that since the post-promotional training is being provided as per the State Training Policy and Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022, and is mandatory, the non-attendance of the above mentioned officer in the next training session shall be liable for action as per the provisions prescribed in Section 5 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022. Also, no request for exclusion from the next training session will be entertained. The matter should be brought to the notice of the authorities concerned. Also, please note that if some administrative matters arise due to the non-completion of the training of the said mentioned cell officers within the prescribed time, the entire responsibility will be of the concerned administrative department and the concerned officer.
