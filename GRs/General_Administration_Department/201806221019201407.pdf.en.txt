Preamble: Government Resolution No. 1 refers to the Government approved list of institutions which are engaged in the work of printing and removing hoardings and banners digitally through flex so that the general public can be informed about the policies, various public welfare schemes and programs of the Government. 1 has been approved. The validity of the same has been extended till May 31, 2018. The government was considering extending the deadline for the list and rates of the agencies appointed for the digital installation and removal of hoardings and banners.
Government Decision: Under this, the list and rate of institutions appointed for the digital installation and removal of hoardings and banners is being extended for a period of three months from June 1, 2018 to August 31, 2018.
