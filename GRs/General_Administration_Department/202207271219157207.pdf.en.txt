Government Order: Many infrastructure projects of the Government of Maharashtra are in progress and many new major projects are being taken up. The" War Room" in the Chief Minister's Secretariat will be revived for the planned and speedy implementation of all these projects. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Director General (War Room Infrastructure Projects) in the Chief Minister's Secretariat was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Shri Radheshyam Mopalwar, BAPS 1995 has retired as Secretary on 28.02.2018. Considering his long experience in various important positions in the administration, he is being appointed as the Director General (War Room-Infrastructure Projects) in the Chief Minister's Secretariat.
The terms and conditions of the appointment will be as follows: 1. The amount received by Shri Radheshyam Mopalwar after deducting his pension (including Dearness Allowance and Dearness Allowance) from the total salary (including basic pay and Dearness Allowance) received before retirement is being fixed as monthly wages.
If Mr. Radheshyam Mopalwar does not take any other accommodation facility from the government, then in return, he will be allowed to rent a house equivalent to the accommodation allowed to the officers of Super Time Scale in Bhopal.
(3) If Mr. Radheshyam Mopalwar does not take the facility of a vehicle from the Government, then in return he will be entitled to the allowance of the vehicle as per the rules of the Government from the day he joins the office.
4) The appointment will be effective from the date of joining of Shri Radheshyam Mopalwar. This will be for a period till further orders of the Chief Minister.
