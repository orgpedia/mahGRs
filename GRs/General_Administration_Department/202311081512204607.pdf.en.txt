Preamble: As per the Government Order dated 18.09.2023 of the Department of Law and Justice, Shri Prashant Gyandev Sadansiv, Deputy Secretary (Law) has been transferred from the Department of Agriculture and Padum to the Department of General Administration on administrative grounds. Accordingly, the Department of Agriculture and Padum, under the Office Order of Reference-2, has issued an order to Mr. Sadansiv dated September 20, 2023 (M.A. No) has been removed. Mr. Sadansiv has joined this department from this day.
The revised figure of 753 posts in various cadres on the establishment of General Administration Department (itself) has been approved by the Government Decision, General Administration Department No.- Review- 1616/ PR No. 213/19 A, dated 17 January 2023. There are 2 posts of Under Secretary (Law) in this department. Out of which 1 post is sanctioned for the office of Chief Secretary and the other vacant post is to be posted as Mr. Sadansiv, Deputy Secretary (Law). At present, for the posting of Mr. Sadansiv to the post of Deputy Secretary (Law), one post in the cadre of Under Secretary (Law) in the department, the rank of Deputy Secretary (Law) needs to be upgraded. Therefore, the matter of upgrading one post of Under Secretary (Law) cadre in the department to Deputy Secretary (Law) cadre was under consideration of the government.
Government Resolution: Under Secretary (Law) Cadre on establishment of General Administration Department (itself) (Pay Level S-23: Rs. 67,700-2, 08,700) 1 post Deputy Secretary (Law) in the cadre (Pay Level S-25: Rs. 78,800-2, 09,000) dated 20th September, 2023 (M. No.
2. In this augmented post, Shri Prashant Gyandev Sadansiv, Deputy Secretary (Law) has been transferred and appointed as Additional Secretary (Law) with effect from 20th September, 2023. The post of Deputy Secretary (Law) is being given from No. The post will continue to be held by Mr. Prashant Gyandev Sadansiv, Deputy Secretary (Law) till the time he holds the post, after which the promoted post will automatically get converted into the original post.
