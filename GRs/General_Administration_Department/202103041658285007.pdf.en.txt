As per the Government decision dated 02.03.2019, during the tenure of the 7th Pay Commission, assured progress under the revised service of three benefits after 10/20/30 years of regular service has been provided. The provisions have been made with effect from 01/01/2016. For this scheme and for regular promotion, a Divisional Promotion Committee of the following members is being constituted under the chairmanship of Joint Secretary (Rajashastra) to try out the qualifications of the employees of Sahyadri and Nandagiri State Guest House under the General Administration Department and recommend to the government.
