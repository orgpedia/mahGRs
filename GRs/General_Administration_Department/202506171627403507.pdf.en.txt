Further regular maintenance and repairs of the project are being carried out by the Maharashtra Ban Development Corporation. For this purpose, the Government decision at Reference 2 provides for regular maintenance and repair of the Biodiversity Park at a cost of Rs. Accordingly, the issue of approving increased recurring expenditure was under consideration.
Government Decision: Annual maintenance and repair of Biodiversity Park at Raj Bhavan Nagpur at a cost of Rs. Recurring expenditure from 27.194 focus to Rs. It is being approved to increase it to 40 lakhs.
