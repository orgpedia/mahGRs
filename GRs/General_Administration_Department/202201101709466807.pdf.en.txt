Preamble: The Government has taken a policy decision to start Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP) of probationary officers of various cadres who are directly recruited to Group A and Group B through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission through the State Service Examination through training institutes at Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur respectively. Further, it has been decided to start a Post Graduate Degree course affiliated to the University of Mumbai for probationary officers to be trained in CPTP.
The training of candidates who have been recommended on the basis of the revised/ final result of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission State Service Examination-2019 will start from 17.01.2022 under the batch of Combined Probationary Training Programme-7 (CPTP-7). In this regard, the matter of issuing guidelines for the candidates joining this training program, the training institutions and the concerned administrative departments was under consideration of the government. The government has issued a notification in this regard.

Government Circular: The following instructions are being issued regarding various issues regarding the Combined Probationary Training Programme-7 (CPTP-7) to be conducted for the candidates who have been recommended on the basis of the revised/ final result of the State Service Examination-2019.
(i) Guidelines for commencement of training: 1. The original certificates of the candidates joining for training of CPTP-7 batch have been verified from 25.10.2021 to 29.10.2021 at the Training Institutes at Yashda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur. These candidates have been appointed as Probationary Officers under CPTP-7 with effect from 17.01.2022, subject to verification of their character, medical examination and educational and other necessary documents by the concerned agencies.
The Probationary Officers of CPTP-7 batch should join the District Attach√© stage from 17.01.2022 in the office notified by the respective Administrative Departments. The training for this phase of District Engagement will be conducted from 17.01.2022 to 11.02.2022. Thereafter, the training of probationary officers will commence from 14.02.2022 in the training institutions.
The candidates who are going to join the training of CPTP-7 in the service of the State Government should be relieved from the offices where they are currently working to join the training on 17.01.2022 (before midday) at the place of district affiliation.
The Government will take a decision in accordance with the candidates who are going to join the training of CPTP-7 batch, who are already in the service of the State Government. 33/ 92/8 dated 2.12.1997, the notice period of 1 month prescribed is being relaxed. Also, the period from the date of retirement till the commencement of training will be regularised as the period of duty for candidates in government service.
(ii) Regarding the salary of Probationary Officers: 1. In order to ensure that the probationary officers of the CPTP-7 batch receive salary and other incidental allowances, the concerned administrative departments should register these officers in the service system, obtain their number for the DCPS and appoint Drawing and Disbursing Officers for their salary, etc. within a period of four weeks (from 17.01.2022 to 11.02.2022) of the district affiliation at the beginning of the training. The regular salary of the concerned post should be provided to the officer from the first month of training.
The administrative departments will take action to create more number of posts for the candidates joining the CPTP-7 batch from the date of commencement of training, taking into account the number of candidates joining the training.

(iii) Regarding Bonds to be taken from Probationary Officers: It shall be mandatory for Probationary Officers joining for training of CPTP-7 batches to furnish Bonds as per the provisions of the Government Resolution, General Administration Division No: TRN 0514/ PR No. 7/14/18-B, dated 28th February, 2017 before engaging them for training.
For candidates who have completed training in CPTP-4, CPTP-4/ A, CPTP-5 and CPTP-6.
Probationary Officers who have completed training in CPTP-4, CPTP-4/ A, CPTP-5 and CPTP-6 have been recommended for other cadres through State Service Examination-2019 and if the candidates are willing to take up the new recommended post, necessary action regarding exemption of such candidates from the stage of training as mentioned in Roll No. 3 and Roll No. 4 above should be taken by the Training Institutes at Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur.
(v) Service Excellence of Probationary Officers: The service excellence of Probationary Officers of other cadres, excluding the Probationary Officers of the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Police/ Assistant Commissioner of Police, Group-A, shall be determined as specified in the Maharashtra Civil Service (Determination of Service Excellence of candidates who are selected for Group' A' and Group' B' (Gazetted) posts through the State Civil Service Examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and are appointed in direct service and undergo training under the Combined Probationary Training Programme) Rules, 2015, dated 7.11.2015 and dated 31.12.2018.
The merit of service of Probationary Officers in the cadre of Deputy Superintendent of Police/ Assistant Commissioner of Police, Group-A shall be determined as prescribed in the Rules, 2015, dated 7.09.2018 [to determine the merit of service of candidates who are selected for the post of Group' A" and Group' B" (Gazetted) through the State Civil Services Examination conducted by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and are recruited directly and undergo training under the Combined Probationary Training Programme].
(vi) Service Book of Probationary Officers: Document Verification of Probationary Officers undergoing training under CPTP-7 at Training Institutes at Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur. 6 has been done in the presence of the Nodal Officers mentioned above. The process of preparation of service books of probationary officers joining the said batch should be done by the respective administrative departments within a period of four weeks (from 17.01.2022 to 11.02.2022) of the district affiliation at the beginning of the training.
(vii) Implementation of Post Graduate Course Programme Post Graduate Course under Combined Probationary Training Programme for Group-A and Group-B Officers selected through State Service Examination conducted by Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
