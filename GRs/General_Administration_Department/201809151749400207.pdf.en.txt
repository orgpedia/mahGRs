Government memorandum
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has released the final result of the Clerk-Typist Exam 2015 for the post of Clerk-Typist (Marathi/ English). Accordingly, candidate Shri Sahebrao Bhiku Chavan was allotted the post of Clerk-Typist (Marathi/ English) in the Department of Parliamentary Affairs vide Government Memorandum dated 24.07.2015.
In view of the fact that Mr. Chavan is eligible for action under the said instructions No. 1.3.1& 5.12.2, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has recommended permanent debarment of Mr. Chavan from all examinations and selections conducted by the Commission with effect from the date of the said examination i.e. 27th July, 2014 and his post of Clerk Typist in the Clerk-Typist Examination 2014 submitted by the Commission on 31/5/2018
