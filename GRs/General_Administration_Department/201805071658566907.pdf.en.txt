In order to ensure that the transfer orders are implemented promptly and effectively, all the Ministries/ Departments shall strictly follow the following instructions:
(1) On receipt of transfer orders, all the departments shall relieve the transferred employees immediately without waiting for the appearance of the replacement staff.
(2) The transferred employees shall be reinstated without delay after they have been relieved from their respective departments and come to join. The establishment officer of the concerned department will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of an employee by fixing a mandatory waiting period.
(3) If any employee directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, such employee shall be liable to disciplinary action.
(4) In order to ensure effective implementation of these transfers, the salaries of all the transferred employees as on 31.05.2018 shall be deducted by the department in which they are presently working.
The salary from 01.06.2018 should not be withdrawn from the department before the transfer under any circumstances. After the concerned employee appears in the transfer department, the post-transfer department should draw the salary and submit the report to the General Administration Department. Instructions are being issued to all the Administrative Departments that if the salary due from 01.06.2018 is paid by the Assessing and Disbursing Officer of the department who was working before the transfer, he will be held personally liable. The remuneration of all the transferred employees as on 31.05.2018 is to be paid by the existing department and the remuneration from 01.06.2018 is to be paid by the transferred department.
