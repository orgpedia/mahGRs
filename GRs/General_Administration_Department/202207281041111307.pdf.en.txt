Preamble: General Administration Department, Government Decisions, D. As on 20/01/2014, a combined probationary training programme is being implemented for the higher ranks of Group-A and Group-B cadres who are directly recruited in Government service. For the training programme under the Integrated Schedules, the training institute of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune has been provided with Reference No. A total of 12 posts have been approved under the government decision.
Out of which, only one post of Accounts Officer, Class-I has been filled up from 26/08/2020. Now in the financial year 2022-23, for their salary from March to June 2022, Rs. 6,50,000/- has been disbursed as per the Government decision dated 17/06/2022. Now for the four months salary from July, 2022 to October, 2022, Rs. 7,50,000/- was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the said Government decision, Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune, has been sanctioned a sum of Rs. The Government is granting approval for disbursement of funds to the extent of Rs. 7,50,000/- (Rupees Seven Lakh Fifty Thousand only) subject to the following terms and conditions:
Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune, shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the grants distributed and shall take care to draw from the treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
