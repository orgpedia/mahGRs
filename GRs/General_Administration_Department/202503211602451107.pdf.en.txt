Government Circular - Reference No. By circular dated 27th December, 2024, the Assistant Cell Officer Post-Intentional Training Examination 2025 and D.Sc. The circular dated 06.01.2025 has invited applications from eligible candidates for the training of Cell Officer Divisional Examination 2025. A combined training class for the two exams will be held on Monday. April 7, 2025 to D. It will be held at the Conference Hall, 6th Floor, Mantralaya Extension Building, Mumbai during 23rd May, 2025.

Paper-wise syllabus of the subjects covered for the Cell Officer Divisional Examination is as per Government Decision No. Vyapani - 2722 / P.No.8 / Ka.17, D. It will be as of April 5, 2022.
The above combined training is open to all officers / staff of the Ministry cadre, however, permission to attend the training is required to be obtained from the Establishment Branch of the concerned department. All the Ministries / Departments and the LOA Office are requested to bring such circular to the written notice of all the concerned Assistant Cell Officers (including candidates on deputation / leave) who have submitted the application and allow them to attend the training.
