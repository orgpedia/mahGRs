Now, the General Administration (IT) Department vide Government Resolution dated 21st January, 2021 and Government Supplementary Letter dated 04th March, 2021, in order to implement the revised examination system by changing the mode of competitive examinations in the recruitment process by the ministerial departments and government offices under them, MahaIT has published RFP (Request for Proposal) and selected five eligible OMR Vendors through tender process on the panel and made it mandatory for the recruitment process to be carried out by the company on this panel.
Accordingly, the following guidelines are being issued regarding the revised competitive examination procedure for recruitment to the posts of direct recruitment to the posts in the purview of Ex-Secondary Service Selection Boards (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission).
Government circular:
The process of direct recruitment to Group-B (non-gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the purview of Ex-Secondary Service Selection Boards (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) should be carried out by the respective District Selection Committees, Regional Selection Committees and State Level Selection Committees through a company empanelled by the General Administration (IT) Department (OMR Vendor).
