Preamble: To ensure that every office in the state is clean, beautiful and tidy, and the environment there is accessible to the citizens and workable, motivating and encouraging for the employees, the" Beautiful My Office" campaign will be launched every year from the next financial year at the state level to the taluka level. The scheme will be implemented in three phases from January 1 to March 31, May 1 to July 31 and September 1 to November 30.
In paragraph 4 of the above Government Decision, a Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri Sunil Kendrekar, Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad to formulate the guidelines for the preparation of this Mission at Taluka, District, Block and State level, the broad contours of the Mission, the modalities to be adopted, the award selection committees at various levels while selecting the award under the Mission, the factors to be considered at various levels for the Award and the evaluation method of the Mission. The committee has been asked to submit its report to the state government in two months" time.
Government Circular: In view of the above, a committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of Mr. Sunil Kendrekar, Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad, to prepare the preparation of the" Sundar Majhe Karyalaya" campaign at the Taluka, District, Division and State level, the broad contours of the campaign, the procedure to be adopted, as well as the guidelines to be followed while selecting the award under the campaign, the award selection committees at different levels, the issues to be considered at different levels for the award and the evaluation method of the campaign.
