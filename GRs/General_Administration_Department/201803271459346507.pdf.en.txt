Government circular
The Provisional Additional Provisional Seniority as on 01.01.2018 for Lower Grade Scribes in the Ministry was issued vide Circular dated 28.2.2018. All the Ministries/ Departments were informed to forward the objections/ objections, if any, to the General Administration Department by 15.3.2018. However, no errors/ objections have been received from the Ministries/ Departments. Provided that the said seniority is being made interim by making good the errors/ objections as well as the factual errors in the final seniority list of lower category scribes published as on 01.01.2017, which is annexed to this Circular at Annexure-A. The General Administration Department of the said Seniority Board has been created in accordance with the instructions in the Government Resolution No: SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
