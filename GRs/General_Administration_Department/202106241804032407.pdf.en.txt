Preamble: As per the provisions of Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the posts in Group A to C cadre in the Sainik Welfare Department, Pune and Subordinate Offices under the control of this Department, as per the notification dated 04.01.2021, have been ensured for Divyangjan as per the Government decision dated 15.02.2021. Further, since the duties and responsibilities of the posts of Group-C 2 cadre in these offices are different in nature, these posts have been reserved for the disabled persons as per the recommendation of the Expert Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 12.11.2020 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. This has been done in accordance with the Government Decision dated 26.2.2021.
2. As per the revised figure provided under the Government decision dated 02.02.2021 for the posts in Sainik Welfare Department, Pune and all the offices under it, a total number of 106 posts in 5 cadres namely Drivers in Group-C and Naiks, Sepoys, Chowkidars and Safaigars in Group-D have been fixed on the establishment of these offices. As the number of employees working in these superannuated posts become vacant due to retirement/ resignation/ death, the posts will lapse and they will become deceased cadre. These positions will not be filled in the future.

3. For the reasons mentioned in paragraph 2 above, the proposal to exclude the prescribed number of posts in the establishment of Sainik Welfare Department, Pune and Subordinate Offices from the list of posts for persons with disabilities was submitted in the meeting of the Expert Committee of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance dated 21.06.2021 and the Expert Committee has recommended to exclude the prescribed number of posts in the establishment of Sainik Welfare Department, Pune and Subordinate Offices from the list of posts for persons with disabilities. Accordingly, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance vide Reference No. As per the recommendation of the Expert Committee notified vide letter No. 8, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to exclude the posts of Drivers in Group-C cadre and Naiks, Sepoys, Chowkidars and Safaigars in Group-D cadre in the establishment of Sainik Welfare Department, Pune and Subordinate Offices from the list of disabled posts.
Government's decision:
In the General Administration Department, a total of 5 posts of Group-C cadre such as Drivers and Naiks, Sepoys, Chowkidars and Safaigars of Group-D cadre in the Sainik Welfare Department, Pune and Subordinate Offices Establishment have been decided as superannuation (deceased cadre) as per the Government Decision dated 02.02.2021 in the revised figure mentioned in Reference No. 5.
