Government Circular: The Report Year 2021-22 has ended with effect from 31st March, 2022 and the process of writing the Performance Appraisal Report for the Report Year is required to be completed within the stipulated time. The time schedule for writing the Performance Appraisal Report has been fixed as per the Government Decision dated 1.11.2011. Accordingly, action is expected to be taken for writing performance appraisal reports of all government officers/ employees. However, if the action is not yet taken accordingly, the processing officer/ establishment officer shall complete the work assessment report by filling up part 1 of the work assessment report and giving a sample of the work assessment report to the officer/ employee (in case of officers involved in the flood, create workflow and generate PAR and send it to the concerned officer), if the action has not been taken yet, in any case by 20.5.2022.
If the self-appraisal report has not yet been filled by the officer/ employee, it should be filled by 30.5.2022.
3. If not paid within this period, their performance appraisal reports should be filled by the Reporting Officer and Reviewing Officer without self-assessment. (Force should be forwarded in case of officers involved in the flood).
If the Reporting Officer has not yet made a report, it should be completed by 15.6.2022 and the performance appraisal report should be sent to the Reviewing Officer.
If the report is not submitted within this period, the versioning officer should send a copy of the self-assessment report directly to the reviewing officer. (Force should be forwarded in case of officers involved in the flood).
