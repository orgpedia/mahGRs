The Proposal: -
The administrative affairs of all four restaurants are handled by the Ministry Restaurant Office. It prepares all establishment matters relating to staff appointment, promotion, transfer and ancillary matters, salary of officers / staff, travel allowance, various advance payments, purchase payments for daily necessities of the restaurant. Office copies of these payments, employee service books, various registers, and office bags require shelves to be kept. Restaurants need closets to hold their bill of lading VICRO payments. The cabinets currently in use in the restaurant office are damaged and unfit for use. Many cupboards have rusty legs, their locks are broken, and some cupboard doors are not fastened. This closet is required to maintain and maintain records of service books and other important service matters of current officers / staff as well as retired officers / staff. For this, the government was considering the purchase of a new wrought iron case.
The Government's Decisions:
Administrative and financial approval is being accorded for the purchase of 21 new Iron Coats (Rupees Five Lakh Forty Five Thousand One Hundred and Sixty Only) for the record keeping of all the four restaurants namely, Mantralaya Restaurant, Chorus Restaurant, Vidhan Bhawan Restaurant and Konkan Bhawan Restaurant.
