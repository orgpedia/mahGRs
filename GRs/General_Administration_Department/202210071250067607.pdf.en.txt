Kannav, Cell Officer, Home Department are being appointed on deputation to the post of" Cell Officer" in the office of the State Service Commission, Nashik Revenue Division, Nashik under the General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions for a period of one year initially.
(a) Mr. Uday B. The service of the deputation will commence from the date on which the officer of the cell joins the post of deputation, after which the period of deputation will end on the date on which he joins his parent department by handing over the charge of the post of deputation after one year.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. After repatriation of deputation, Mr. Uday B. Kanav, Cell Officers will be required to join their parent department i. e. Home Department.
Considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II and III) of the Rules, 1981, the Home Department and the General Administration Department have appointed Shri. The terms and conditions of the appointment should be defined.
Mr. Uday B. All other administrative matters of the Kanavas, Cell Officers will be handled by the Home Department.
