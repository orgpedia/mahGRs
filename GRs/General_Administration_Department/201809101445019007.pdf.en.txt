Government Circular dated 4th July, 2018- For the Assistant Cell Officers in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the examination is conducted in accordance with the Assistant Services Post-Entrance Training Examination Rules, 1977 and the amendments made therein from time to time. In this regard, the Post-Interview Training Examination for the year 2018 for the Assistant Cell Officers in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission will be held from September 25, 2018 and September 26, 2018 (Tuesday-Wednesday) at the Goods and Services Tax Bhavan, 9th Floor, Maharashtra Public Service Commission Examination Room, Mazgaon, Mumbai 400 010.
The details of the candidates who have been allowed to appear in the said examination on the basis of the information received from the Ministries/ Departments and the offices of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, including the names, divisions and their seat numbers, etc. are indicated in Annexure' A'. The names of the candidates who have been exempted in the respective subjects as per the previous year's marks are indicated in Annexure' B' and the venue and schedule of the examination etc. are indicated in Annexure' C'. The important instructions given to the candidates for the examination are mentioned in Annexure D and are attached with the Annexure.
The Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission are requested to bring to the notice of all the candidates admitted to the said circular examination and preserve the records in the archives of their department.
The Xerox copies of the official government original books of the subjects marked as (*) in Annexure A or the respective official government books certified by the Establishment Officer of the Department shall be allowed to be used at the time of examination. However, it has been suggested that
