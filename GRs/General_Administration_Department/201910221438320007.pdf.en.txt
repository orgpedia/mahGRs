Government circular
It has been decided to organize Vigilance Awareness Week in the State every year by the Government Order dated 22nd October, 2001.
The Central Vigilance Commission, New Delhi vide its circular dated 2nd August, 2019 has informed that this year Vigilance Awareness Week will be observed from 28th October, 2019 to 2nd November, 2019 with the theme" Integrity- A way of life." It is attached.
This year, the Vigilance Awareness Week will be observed from October 28, 2019 to November 2, 2019.
The following tips should be followed while planning the week.
(a) The week should be organized by all the departments of the State Government and all the heads of departments, head of offices under their control, approved enterprises of the State Government, cooperative societies, autonomous institutions.
(b) The programme will begin with the pledge to eradicate corruption at 11.00 am on the working day i.e. 30th October, 2019. The Head of the Department, the Head of the Office or the senior most officer present will administer the oath to all. (A sample is attached.) (a) After taking oath. The Governor's message for this week will be read out to the audience.
d) This week. The Governor's message should be disseminated to the people of the state.
(c) The following activities will be held this week:
1.Placing of posters, cloth boards in the front and strategic areas of the office.
Organizing seminars/ debate competitions/ lectures/ essay competitions/ workshops to highlight the ill-effects of corruption in offices/ schools/ colleges.
3. Publication of Special Journal 4. Call upon the non-governmental organizations, NGOs and general public of the state to participate in vigilance awareness campaign.
Use of the material available on the Central Vigilance Commission's website (http:// www.cvc.nic.in) for public distribution.
