The Proposal:
The Department of Women and Child Development and the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, in association with the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, have constructed a new administrative building, 3rd Floor (East Side) of Water Supply Department at 1873 S.O. ft. of space on the 7th floor of the hospital. 2000 for the office of Rajmata Jijau Mata Bal Swasthya and Poshan Abhiyaan under the Department of Women and Child Development functioning in the ground floor and for the office of Rajmata Jijau Mata Bal Swasthya and Poshan Abhiyaan under the Department of Women and Child Development functioning in the 7th floor of GoT Hospital.
A proposal to allot 4 feet of land to the water supply department through interchange was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Government have taken the following decisions:- GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to construct a new Administrative Building, 3rd Floor (East side) of Water Supply Department at 1873 S.O. ft. of space on the 7th floor of Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital. The land is being allotted for the office of Rajmata Jijau Mata Bal Swasthya and Poshan Abhiyan under the Women and Child Development Department.
2000 Office of Rajmata Jijau Mata Child Health and Nutrition Mission under the Department of Women and Child Development on the 7th floor of Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital
Four feet of space is being allotted to the Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
Further, the officers/ staff of the administration working in the new Administration Building, 9th Floor, East Side, which has been temporarily allotted by the Department of Water Supply and Sanitation, should be shifted to the newly allotted space in the Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital Complex Building and vacate the 9th Floor, East Side.
The Executive Engineer, City (East) Division, Nirman Bhawan, Mumbai shall hand over the possession of the said land to the concerned. Also, the Water Supply and Sanitation Department should take possession of the land at the new administration building, 9th floor, east side, and submit a report to the government.
