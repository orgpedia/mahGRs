Under the decision of the Government in the context, a policy has been decided to honour the persons who had to suffer imprisonment for fighting for democracy during the period of Emergency from 1975 to 1977. The arrested persons will have to submit a self-sworn affidavit on a Rs 100 stamp paper as proof. The government is not asking for any more evidence.
