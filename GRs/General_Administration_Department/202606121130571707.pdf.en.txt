Limitation of Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation by Government Decision and Letter in Reference. The UAHA party B government offices operating in the government cottages (barracks) on the Fro Press Journal line have been temporarily shifted to alternate locations in line with the Mumbai Metro Phase-3 (Colaba-Bandra-Seepz) project work being carried out by the (MMRCL). Also, the rent of these offices was being paid by the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Limited till the completion of the project. With the completion of the project, the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Ltd. Renting has been stopped since 1.04.2026. A proposal was under the consideration of the government to provide an alternate site or grant administrative and financial approval to keep the office functional till the rehabilitation of the office was completed. Accordingly, the Government has decided to: -
Government Decision: The Department will take action to provide alternate space and pay rent to the offices mentioned in the Return-1 till their rehabilitation.
