The Department of General Administration, Government Decision No: SRV 2011/ PR No.137/ Q. 12, dated 17.12.2016 has laid down the policy for deputation of officers/ staff of the ministerial cadre. No. 120/14, dated 31.8.2017 has been prescribed by the Government.
