Government Decision: - Department of Finance The budgeted funds for mandatory and programmatic expenditure required for the first nine months of the year (end of December, 2024) for the objectives mentioned in Annexure 3 of Government Circular No. E.F. 2024 / Q. No. 82 / E.F. III, dated 25.07.2024 have been disbursed on BEAMS system within a limit of 60 of the total annual provision.
To meet the expenditure on remuneration for formulation of e-governance drone policy for the Directorate of Information Technology during the financial year 2024-25 (20525072) under this head 17 - Computer Expenditure (Programme) within the limits of the provision allocated from the approved budgetary provision as proposed by the Directorate of Information Technology.
