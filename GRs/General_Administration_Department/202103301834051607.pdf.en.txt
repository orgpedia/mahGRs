Appointment of a one-man high level inquiry committee of retired High Court Justice to look into the allegations made by Shri Param Bir Singh in his letter dated 20.3.2021.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Resolution No: Uchaus (Inquiry)-2021/ P.S. 49/21
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: March 30, 2021.
Preamble: The Government was contemplating to constitute a one-man high level inquiry committee comprising of retired Judges of the High Court to look into the allegations made by Shri Param Bir Singh in his letter dated 20.3.2021.
The government has taken the following decisions:
A one-man high level inquiry committee is being appointed by Shri Justice Kailash Uttamchand Chandiwal, retired Judge of the High Court, to look into the allegations made by Shri Param Bir Singh in his letter dated 20.3.2021.
2. The scope of the enquiry shall be as follows:
(c) Mr. Param Bir Singh. As alleged in the letter dated 20.03.2021 Ma. Has Mr. Param Bir Singh submitted any evidence in his letter that any misconduct/ crime has been committed by the Home Minister or any officer in his office or how?
Param Bir Singh was transferred from the post of Mumbai Police Commissioner on the basis of a tip-off received from Sachin Vaze, Assistant Police Inspector. """ From the allegations made in the letter dated 20.03.2021, does it emerge that the said Minister or his staff in office has committed any offence which requires investigation by the Anti-Corruption Bureau or any other investigating agency?"
c) Other useful recommendations related to the presented topic.
The high-level inquiry committee should submit its report to the government within six months.
Separate orders will be issued regarding the honorarium and other concessions to Justice Kailash Uttam Chandiwal.
