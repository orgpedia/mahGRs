the order
The services of Mr. P. B. Desai, Cell Officer, Public Works Department, as Deputy Executive Officer on deputation to the Sri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai, under the Department of Law and Justice, for a period of two years, subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mr. Desai shall commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner from the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Commissioner, cease to hold the office of Deputy Commissioner for a period of two years.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. (e) The terms and conditions of the deputation shall be decided by the Public Works Department (Establishment) and the Department of Law and Justice in consultation with each other after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Shri Desai shall be dealt with by the Public Works Department (Establishment) and the Department of Law and Justice.
4. The Public Works Department should immediately relieve Mr. Desai to join the place of deputation.
