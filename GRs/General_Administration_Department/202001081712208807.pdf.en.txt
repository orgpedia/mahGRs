the order
The services of Shri P. V. Kadam, Cell Officer, School Education and Sports Department are being handed over to Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai under the Department of Law and Justice as Deputy Executive Officer on deputation for a period of one year subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mr. Kadam shall commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner from the date on which he enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his original office after demitting the office of the Deputy Commissioner, complete one year of his service.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. The Department of School Education and Sports (PRSA-1) and the Department of Law and Justice shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with each other after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Shri Kadam will be handled by the Department of School Education and Sports (PRSA-1) and the Department of Law and Justice.
4. The School Education and Sports Department should immediately relieve Mr. Kadam to join the place of deputation.
