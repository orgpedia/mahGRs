Government's decision:
State Cabinet The amount of rent and convenience fee to be charged for accommodation in Government accommodation after the members resign from the membership or demit office has been prescribed under the decision of the Government in question. A proposal to this effect was under the consideration of the Government. In this regard, the government has now decided as follows: 2. It will be necessary to hand over the possession of the government residence to the government within 15 days from the date of resignation from the post of minister. From the date of resignation of the Minister, the following rent and convenience fee will be charged:
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1 | The first 15 days after leaving office        | Free of charge, including furniture, gas, electricity, water and telephone. |
| 2 | If the residence is not vacated after 15 days | Rs. 200/- per month with rent and internal facilities.                      |
