Preamble: The revised figure of 126 posts in the office of Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal has been fixed as per the Government decision dated 27.10.2021. A proposal to revise the number of posts in the cadre of driver, peon and personal assistant in this figure was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The revised figure of 126 posts in the office of Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal has been fixed as per the Government Decision dated 27.10.2021. To improve this figure. The approval was given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries chaired by the Chief Secretary in its meeting held on 29.04.2022. Accordingly, this Government resolution approves the following changes in the number of posts in the cadre of Drivers, Constables and Personal Assistants in the revised figure of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal:
