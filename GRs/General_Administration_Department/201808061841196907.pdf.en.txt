Subject: Filling up of the post of Secretary in the office of Maharashtra Public Service Commission by deputation.
Request for Intent from the Joint Secretary of the Ministry.
Reference- S.P.V. No. Maloya 1116/ p. No.143/ 8, Dy. dated 6.8.2018.
Mr. and Mrs.,
As per the letter, the ministry has requested to invite applications from officers of Joint Secretary cadre for filling up the vacant post of Secretary in the office of Maharashtra Public Service Commission on deputation. (Attached)
Accordingly, all the administrative departments of the Ministry are requested to provide the names of the Joint Secretaries of their department who are willing to join the post of Secretary, Maharashtra Public Service Commission, along with the certified copies of their confidential reports of the last five years, personal information (bio-data), complete service details (duration, department, post), no-departmental inquiry certificate and no-objection certificate of the department, etc. It should be sent in a manner that it is received by 10.08.2018. Also, if any officer is not interested, he should inform this department.
