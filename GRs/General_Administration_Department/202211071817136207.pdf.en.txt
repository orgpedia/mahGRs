The Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022 have been notified w.e.f. 02.02.2022. According to this rule, all government officials/ employees after the foundation, revision and promotion
Training is mandatory. The government has taken the decision. As per the State Training Policy dated 23.09.2011, the duration of post-promotional training for Group-B has been fixed at two weeks.
Accordingly, 12 days post-promotion training has been organized by Regional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (RAMETI), Pune from 14.11.2022 to 25.11.2022 for the officers promoted to the rank of Cell Officers. A list of the officers nominated for training is attached.

The training is being imparted as per the State Training Policy and Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022, and attendance is mandatory. Regarding the training, the government has issued a circular. The suggestions dated 13th May, 2015 should be brought to the notice of the concerned.
The Maharashtra Civil Services (Time-bound Training) Rules, 2022, provide for withholding of increments for officers who have not completed their post-promotion training. This should be brought to the notice of all the concerned officials.
The officer nominated for training shall be appointed on the day before the commencement of the training. They should be relieved on the condition that they should reach Regional Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (RAMETI), Pune by 6.00 pm on 13.11.2022 or by 9.00 am on the first day of training on 14.11.2022. The concerned department should immediately inform the trainees about the training period keeping in mind the need for reservation for travel. relating to the designated officers
