Government circular:
There was a dispute between the officers of the direct service cell and the promoted cell officers regarding the mutual seniority of the ministerial cell officers from 1986 to 2000 as on January 1. Writ Petition No. 1 filed by the Direct Service Cell Officer and the Promoted Cell Officer regarding the seniority of mutual service. 2982/2000, 2986/2000, 4958/2003 and 3374/2011 No. The High Court vide order dated 06.08.2018 has disposed of the matter. Against the order dated 06.08.2018, the officers of the Promotion Cell, Shri. Special Leave Petition (SLP) No. 25181/2018 (25639/2018, 26715-26713/2018) have been filed. The special leave petition is still pending.
2. m. Pursuant to the order dated 06.08.2018 of the High Court, the revised final seniority of the cadre of Cell Officers w.e.f. 01.01.1986 to 01.01.2016 is being revised. The Government Circular dated 03.02.2023 has been issued.
Provisional Common Seniority dated 01.01.2017 was issued vide Circular dated 31.07.2023 for inviting suggestions and objections, including those officers who have received date of regular promotion to the post of Cell Officer during the period 01.01.2016 to 31.12.2016 and who have been appointed to the post of Cell Officer during the said period. Considering the objections and suggestions received on the above seniority and excluding the retired/ voluntary retired/ regular promoted/ deceased Cell Officers in the last Common Seniority List dated 01.01.2016, the final Common Seniority List of Cell Officers cadre dated 01.01.2017 has been prepared and is annexed to this Circular.
4. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring this circular to the notice of all the concerned who are included in the accompanying seniority list and who have retired from the department in which they are working and are on deputation from the establishment of the department.
