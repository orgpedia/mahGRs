The Proposal:
The posts of Assistant Resident Commissioner (Revenue), Assistant Resident Commissioner (Liaison), Assistant Resident Commissioner (Security), Assistant Resident Commissioner (Information Technology) in Group-A (Gazetted) Sangarwa of Maharashtra Sadan Office were created vide Government Resolution dated 24.4.2012. The government has decided to fill up all the four posts. Thereafter, the revised figure of posts in Maharashtra Sadan office has been issued vide Government Resolution dated 24.8.2020. According to this diagram, some of the cadres have been merged and the designation of some of the cadres has been changed. In the revised pattern, the post of Assistant Resident Commissioner (Information and Technology) was replaced by the post of Assistant Resident Commissioner (Investment).
The matter of determining the procedure for appointment to the post of Assistant Resident Commissioner (Investment) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Resolution: Assistant Resident Commissioner (Investment): 7th Pay Commission Pay Scale S-23 67700-208700 (Rs. 156000-39100+ Grade Pay Rs.6,600 equivalent to the post of Under Secretary in the Ministry). The government has decided that the post should be filled by deputation of an officer working in the equivalent post in the state or central service.
