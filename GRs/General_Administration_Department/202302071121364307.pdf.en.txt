Preamble: Smt.Prachi Jambhekar was appointed as a Deputy Commissioner by the Government Order dated 20.9.2019 by temporarily upgrading the pay scale of the post of Anthropologist in the Tribal Cell at the establishment of the Office of the Secretary to the Governor. She joined the post on 27.9.2019. Subsequently, the Raj Bhavan, vide its order dated 31.12.2019, assigned the additional charge of the post of Deputy Secretary (Education) to Smt. Subsequently, the Raj Bhavan, vide order dated 30.3.2020, relieved Smt. Prachi Jambhekar as Anthropologist in the office of Secretary to Governor on 30.3.2020 and appointed her on deputation to the post of Deputy Secretary (Education) to the Governor with effect from the same date i.e. 30.3.2020. He was also given the additional charge of Deputy Secretary (Administration) to the Governor. The period of his deputation was also extended till 26.9.2022. Later, the Raj Bhavan gave him the post. The period of deputation of Deputy Secretary (Education) was further extended by one year from 26.9.2022 to 25.9.2023. Date of decision of the Government of Rural Development Department to Mrs. Jambhekar
As per the Government Order dated 28.12.2022, the Additional Chief Executive Officer (Selection Category) (S-27) has been promoted to the cadre of Deputy Secretary (Education), M. The form has been promoted to the office of the Secretary to the Governor. The duration of the application is 6 months.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: RAJB- 2023/16/ PR No. 6/20-A
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to upgrade the post of Deputy Secretary (Education) in the office of the Secretary to the Governor from the pay scale S-25: 78800-209200/ to the cadre of Joint Secretary pay scale S-27: 123100-215900 with effect from 28.12.2022 subject to the following conditions:
The post of Deputy Secretary (Education) in the office of the Secretary to the Governor shall continue to be held by Smt. Prachi Jambhekar, in that capacity, till the date she assumes charge of her office or till 25.9.2023, whichever is earlier. After that, the position will automatically be converted into the basic salary structure.
