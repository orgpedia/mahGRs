Preamble: Subject to the provisions of Rule 29, Rule 30 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979 and the terms and conditions prescribed in Annexure, Schedule I and Schedule II of the said Rules and the Constitution and Rules of the" New Model Rules," the provisions of the said Rules shall apply to the Government Employees (Non-Industrial) Associations. The Maharashtra State Food Inspectors Association, Food and Drug Administration, Aurangabad was approved by the Government under the Government Decision No. 1. The Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954 was enacted by the Central Government under the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006. Since it was abolished with effect from 5.8.2011, the proposal to change the name of the organization was under consideration of the Government.
Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken: Government Decision: In the name of Maharashtra State Food Inspectors Association, Food and Drug Administration, Aurangabad, Government Employees (Non-Industrial) Association, vide reference No. 3 The Government is approving the change to" Maharashtra State Food Officers Welfare Association" in accordance with the procedure laid down in the Government Circular here.
Maharashtra State Food Officers Welfare Association should submit 5 copies each of the amended new rules/ constitution to the respective office heads and the concerned ministerial administrative departments of the government, 5 copies of the old constitution/ rules of the organization and 5 copies of the new constitution with the amended/ new rules/ constitution.
