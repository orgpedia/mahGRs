Circular Referred to in Article No. 1 of the Government Circular dated 18th January, 2023, taking the Sadbhavana Day pledge on 20th August, 2023 and paying homage to Late Shri. A wreath-laying ceremony was organised to mark the birth anniversary of Rajiv Gandhi.
However, the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India vide letter No.2 dated 10.08.2023 has decided to take Sadbhavana Diwas pledge on Friday, 18th August, 2023 instead of Sunday, 20th August, 2023. A wreath-laying ceremony will be held to mark the birth anniversary of Rajiv Gandhi.
Accordingly, as per the instructions issued vide letter No. 2 of the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, Sadbhavana Diwas Pledge will be taken on Friday, the 18th August, 2023. A wreath-laying ceremony will be held to mark the birth anniversary of Rajiv Gandhi. (The Day of Goodwill is associated with the pledge.) )
