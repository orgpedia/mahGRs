Government circular
A provisional Common Seniority List for lower grade scribes in the Ministries/ Departments as on 01.01.2023 has been prepared and annexed to this Circular. The General Administration Department of the said seniority has been created as per the instructions in the Government Notification dated 21.6.2021.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been mainly adopted while preparing this seniority list.
3. All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring to the notice of all the employees (including those on leave/ deputation) belonging to the cadre of Lower Grade Short Writers, in their Department, the tentative common seniority as on 01.01.2023. In this regard, the information regarding the issues in the representations received from the concerned lower grade scribes regarding objections/ errors should be checked as per the records of the department and the representations along with the feedback of the department should be sent to this department by 06.02.2023. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct. This senior A Special Leave Petition No. It is being published under the decision of 28306/2017.
