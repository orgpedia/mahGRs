The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. In this regard, the Central Govt. 3 is listed here. The list of posts ensured for the disabled has been published as per the notification dated 4.1.2021. According to this list, the posts which are equal in the office under this department should be ensured for the disabled and such orders should be issued. 4 here Namud Government Decision, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Dy. It was released on February 2, 2021. Accordingly, the posts in Group A, B, C and D cadres in the office of the Lok Ayukta and Deputy Lok Ayukta under the General Administration Department are mentioned in Reference No. 1 The disabled have been ensured by the government decision here. However, the proposal to exempt the posts of driver cadre in Group-C of the said office while determining the posts for the disabled was submitted for the approval of the expert committee. Expert committee In the meeting dated 21.06.2021, it was decided to exclude the cadre of driver from this post.
A proposal to do away with the posts of Lok Ayukta and Deputy Lok Ayukta, Maharashtra State, Mumbai office under this department was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The posts of Lok Ayukta under the General Administration Department and Deputy Lok Ayukta, Maharashtra State, Mumbai office in Group-C cadre of drivers are being excluded from the disabled list.
