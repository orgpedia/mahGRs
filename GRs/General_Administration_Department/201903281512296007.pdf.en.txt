the order
Smt. Sheetal S. Nikam, Cell Officer, Industries, Power and Labour Department is being transferred and posted as Deputy Executive Officer in the office of Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, Pune under Industries, Power and Labour Department on deputation for a period of one year subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mrs. Nikam shall commence her service as a Deputy on the date on which she enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which she enters upon her parent department, relinquish charge of her office one year thereafter.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. The Department of Industry, Energy and Labour (Labour 7) and the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour (Administration 3) shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with each other, taking into account the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Smt. Nikam will be handled by the Department of Industries, Power and Labour (Labour 7) and Department of Industries, Power and Labour (Administration 3).
4. Industries, Power and Labour Department to relieve Smt. Nikam to join the place of deputation. If the services of Ms. Nikam are requisitioned for the Lok Sabha Elections 2019, she will be required to perform the duties assigned to her for election duty in the first instance, only after which she will be relieved for deputation.
