Meeting of the Nagpur Accommodation Committee: The meeting of the Committee shall be held one month before the proposed session at Nagpur. Before that, the Public Works Department should prepare a plan regarding the accommodation/ office arrangements. Since most of the work before the committee is related to the Public Works Department, the meeting will be organised by the Public Works Department. During the meeting, the details of the preparations for the session to be held during the session by the departments related to the Nagpur session arrangement will be presented to the approval of the committee. Further action on the same should be taken by the concerned departments/ offices of the General Administration Department as approved by the Committee in the meeting. After the approval of the committee, if any changes are required to be made as per the demand received from the ministerial departments regarding an issue, the concerned department should take further action with the approval of the committee.
The minutes of the meeting shall be issued by the Public Works Department in accordance with the decisions taken by the Committee at the meeting.
The decision taken by the said Committee on the arrangements for the Nagpur Session shall be final.
