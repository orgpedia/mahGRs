Government circular
As per Rule 19 Potnium (1) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979 and Note 3 thereunder, every Government officer/ employee (other than Group' D' employees) is required to submit a statement of his opinion and liability in the prescribed form at the time of his first appointment to any service/ post and thereafter at the time when the Government appoints him.
Every State Government officer/ employee (other than Group' D' employees) shall, within a period of two months from the date of his first joining the service and thereafter as on the 31st day of March of every year, submit to the prescribed authority his statement of remuneration and liabilities as on the 31st day of May of that year. Submission of Annual Property Returns for the year has been made a pre-condition for all stages of promotions, promotions under the Assured Scheme, deputation, foreign tours in the State Government. In this regard, instructions have been given from time to time to submit the opinion and liability statements. However, it has come to the notice of the government that the promotion is being delayed as such reports have not been submitted in time.
However, all the administrative departments should ensure that all the government officers/ employees (except Group' D' employees) submit their annual statements of remuneration and liabilities within the stipulated time.
The administrative departments should take action to bring this information to the notice of all the officers/ employees.
