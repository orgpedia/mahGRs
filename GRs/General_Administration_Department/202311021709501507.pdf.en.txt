Subject: Filling up of the post of Controller of Examinations and Joint Secretary in the office of Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Belapur on deputation.
Sir/ Madam, in the light of the above subject, it is informed that the post of Joint Secretary in the office of Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Belapur is to be filled by deputation from the Joint Secretary Cadre in the Ministry.
2.Thus, all the officers of the Joint Secretary cadre in your department should be informed about this deputation. The interested Joint Secretaries will be required to furnish personal details, complete service details, no-departmental enquiry certificate, no-objection certificate of the department, etc. along with attested copies of confidential reports of the last 5 years, as mentioned in the prospectus attached with the application. It should be sent in a manner that it is received by 17.11.2023. It is also requested that if any officer is not interested in this deputation, this department should also be informed.
Further, Joint Secretaries in the ministerial cadre desirous of going on deputation for the above post should not apply to this Department mutually. They have to submit the application to their respective departments. Applications will not be considered and no response will be provided.
