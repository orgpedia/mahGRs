Government circular:
On the occasion of World Women's Day, the Brihanmumbai State Government Employees Association, Mumbai is organizing a gathering for women employees of Government offices in Mumbai on Wednesday, March 08, 2023 at" Arogya Bhavan Auditorium, St. George Hospital premises, Mumbai" at 2.00 pm.
Accordingly, women employees of Government offices in Mumbai are being allowed to leave the office at 1.30 pm on Wednesday, March 08, 2023 to attend the above function.
