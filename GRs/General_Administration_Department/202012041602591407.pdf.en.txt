Date is December 4, 2020
Government Circular: As per Government Circular, General Administration Department No. Sarvasu-1196/ PR No. 5/96/29, dated 18th September, 1996, Government/ Semi-Government offices in Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban District are declared local holiday on the day of' Anant Chaturdashi' every year and from the year 2007 onwards on the day of' Dahi Handi'. Accordingly, in the year 2021, all government/ semi-government offices in Mumbai and Mumbai Suburban District are being declared a local holiday.

These orders will be applicable to the State Government/ Paramilitary offices in Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban District.
The above local holiday is being declared in accordance with the provisions of Government Decision No. P& S. No. P-13/ I/ B, dated November 5, 1958.
