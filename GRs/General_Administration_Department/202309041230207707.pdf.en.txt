Government Circular: A comprehensive policy on preparation and publication of seniority list of officers/ employees in the Government service every year. 1 has been prescribed by the government here.
Accordingly, the provisional seniority of the officers of the cadre of Director (Information), Group-A (Senior) in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations up to 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022, and 01.01.2023 has been increased from the existing period of reference no. 2 was published here. In the said cadre, the names of the officers who were appointed during the above period by regular promotion, direct service or other prescribed means, excluding the names of the officers who retired on regular basis, voluntarily retired, and the officers who were promoted on regular basis, were included in the Appendix-" A" and the names of the officers who were promoted on ad-hoc basis, as they fulfill the conditions and conditions for ad-hoc promotion, were included in the Appendix-" B" and the provisional seniority list up to 01.01.2021, 01.01.2022 and 01.01.2023 was published on the website.
According to clause 3 (3) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, there is no need to publish an ad-hoc seniority list. Therefore, the ad-hoc seniority list published earlier need not be considered at present.
