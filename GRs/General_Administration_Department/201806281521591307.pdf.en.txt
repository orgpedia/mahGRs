The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department,
Government Circular, No. Shakaja- 1118/ PR No. 62/2018/22,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 28th of June 2018
Government circular:
The shelves/ tables/ chairs/ stationery/ old documents are often kept in the premises of the Ministry and New Administrative Building building and in the corridor. As a result, the roads and surrounding areas look dirty. It also poses a risk of safety hazards and hinders the work of fire prevention measures. In this regard, instructions are being issued to all the departments of the Ministry through circulars that no kind of storage should be done in the premises of the Ministry building and in the corridor and staircase.
(2) No storage shall be allowed in the open space for the safety of the Ministry and the new administrative building. Also, do not use the parts that are attached to the genitals.
3. All the departments should desecrate the documents of the administration and destroy the unnecessary documents by taking proper notes and send the important documents required for a long time to the record room and keep them in a safe place.
4. The Public Works Department shall ensure that no hoarding is created in the premises of the Ministry and the new Administrative Building building and in the chhann path.
