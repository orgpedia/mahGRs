Government circular:
Reference No. 1 In accordance with the lockdown imposed as a preventive measure against the spread of the corona virus in the state, instructions have been given to use e-mail and WhatsApp in government work. However, the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Govt. 2 As per the" Email Policy" mentioned here, it is mandatory to use the government email service provided by the National Informatics Centre (NIC) under the central government in the affairs of the state government.
2. In view of the facts stated in the said paragraph, Reference No. 1 here. Circular dated June 5, 2020 is hereby cancelled. Similarly, the revised instructions regarding the use of Government e-mail service while doing Government business from home are as follows: (1) Government officers/ employees should use only NIC e-mail provided by NIC (National Informatics Centre) or Government e-mail provided by Information and Technology Department, Government of Maharashtra as per the guidelines of" E-mail Policy, 2014" of Central Government while doing work from home for the disposal of Government business. Don't use other email service providers.

(ii) Every officer/ employee shall provide his NIC e-mail/ Government e-mail ID as well as mobile number with SMS facility to his/ her Head of Office.
(iii) For government work, maximum disposal of government work should be done using NIC e-mail/ government e-mail ID.
(iv) After forwarding the proposal through e-mail, it should be ensured that the notification is immediately given to the concerned through SMS/ WhatsApp.
(v) A proposal submitted for final approval by email in accordance with the instructions received from superiors in the said manner shall be deemed to have been viewed, examined and approved by the officers at both the level of the submitter and the final approver.
The officers/ staff preparing the proposal should forward the proposal to the notice of all the concerned officers before finally forwarding it through e-mail and mark its copy (in C.C.) to all the concerned officers.
However, all Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries of the Ministries and all Heads of Departments and Heads of Offices under the Ministry are requested to bring these instructions to the notice of the officers/ staff under their control.
This circular shall be applicable only for the period of lockdown imposed as a preventive measure against the spread of Corona virus and as an urgent matter till the total attendance in all government offices in the entire state is 100%.
Since the" e-mail policy" in the state of Maharashtra is being implemented by the Information Technology Sub-Division, if there are any doubts/ problems regarding the NIC e-mail ID/ government e-mail ID of government officers/ employees, the IT Sub-Division should take necessary action in this regard.
