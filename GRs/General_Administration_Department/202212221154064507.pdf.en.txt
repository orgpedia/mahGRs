How to apply for the 2023 Departmental Examination for the promotion of Assistant Cell Officer to the post of Cell Officer?
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Circular No: Exam 2022/ PR No.46/ K. 17
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
The date is December 22, 2022.
The Departmental Examination for the promotion to the post of Cell Officer of the Government Circular Year 2023 is the first opportunity for candidates who are invited to apply for this examination.
The candidates having first chance and possessing the following qualifications are required to submit the application form to the Establishment Branch of their Department by 20th January, 2023 in the attached format:
As per the provisions of Rule 3 of the Government of Maharashtra Secondary and Higher Secondary Services Departmental Examination Rules, 1977, the Assistant Cell Officers (Law) in the Ministerial Cadre/ Maharashtra Public Service Commission and Assistant Cell Officers (Law) in the Department of Law and Justice have passed the post-service training examination for the post of Assistant Cell Officer and have been appointed to the said post. Five years of uninterrupted service is being completed by October 31, 2022. "
Separate applications for the examination will be invited from all candidates whose departmental examination in 2023 is not a first-chance examination.
Since the training for the year 2023 will be held in February, 2023, the matter of applying within the stipulated time should be immediately brought to the notice of all the eligible Auxiliary Cell Officers (including Auxiliary Cell Officers on deputation/ leave) by the Establishment Branch Officers of all the Departments and their signatures should be taken and preserved in their records.
