The policy of deputation has been framed vide order dated 17.12.2016 and the procedure for deputation of officers/ staff of the ministerial cadre has been prescribed by the General Administration Department vide Government Resolution No: 2017/ P.S. 120/14 dated 31.8.2017. No) Was relieved from the Housing Department by an office order dated 26-10-2018. Accordingly, Mr. V. P.
