1) Refer No. Interim Common Seniority as on 01.01.2017 of Assistant Cell Officers eligible for promotion to the post of Cell Officers vide Circular No.2 of 2017 has been published.
2) Refer No. In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order No. 1 dated 1.1.2018, a provisional additional common seniority has been created for those Assistant Cell Officers who have been continuously serving in the rank of Assistant Cell Officer for more than 5 years in the ratio (from the revised date of seniority in respect of Assistant Cell Officers who have lost their original seniority) and who have passed the departmental examination prescribed for promotion to the rank of Cell Officer or who are exempted from passing the said examination.
3) As per the rules of the Assistant Cell Officer Cadre, eligible clerks-typists are promoted to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the promotion quota of 30% of the seats. Accordingly, the process of regular promotion of Clerk Typist to the post of Assistant Cell Officer is underway, therefore, the said Assistant Cell Officer has not been included in the accompanying seniority list.
As soon as the process of regular promotion of Clerk-Typist to the post of Assistant Cell Officer is completed, the Assistant Cell Officer in the promoted quota will be included in the seniority list at the appropriate place.
The Assistant Cell Officers from 1st to 66th will be included in their respective places as per the seniority number in the final seniority list dated 1.1.2017 and the Assistant Cell Officers from No. 67 to 136th will be included after the last seniority number in the seniority list dated 1.1.2017 (viz. No. 759).

5) All the Ministries/ Departments are requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned and keep a signed copy of the same in the archives. Please note that the seniority list will be finalized assuming that there is no objection from the concerned in the department if no information is received till 20.06.2018.
