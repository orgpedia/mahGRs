Introduction: The Ministry of Finance, Government of India, provides the option to select Unified Pension Scheme (UPS) to Central Government employees and All India Services officers applying to the National Pension System (NPS).
With regard to giving. The notification has been issued on 24.1.2025. Also, the Central Government's Penhan Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA), New Delhi vide No. 6 here. The Pennan Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (Operation of UPS Scheme under NPS) Rules, 2025 have been issued through a gazette notification of 19.3.2025. Accordingly, the government was considering opening new headings for the implementation of UPS for officers in the All India Services of the Maharashtra cadre.
Government Decision: - For officers of All India Service (IAS / IPS / IFoS) of Maharashtra cadre to implement Unified Pension Scheme (UPS) in the State, the following heads of accounts are being approved to be opened: - (@) Jamechi oars (9) For officers of Indian Administrative Service (IAS) / Indian Police Service (IPS) / Indian Forest Service (IFoS) under Unified Pension Scheme (UPS) to collect State Government contribution.
