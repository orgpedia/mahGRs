the order
Mr. Devender. Chavan, Cell Officer, Home Department, is being appointed on deputation for the first time for one year in the post of" Cell Officer" in the office of the State Right to Service Commission, Amravati Revenue Division, Amravati under the General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mr. Devender. The service of the deputation will commence from the date on which the Cell Officer joins the post of the deputation, after which the period of deputation will end on the date on which he joins his parent department by handing over the charge of the post of the deputation after one year.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. After the repatriation of the deputation, Shri Devendra O. Chavan, the cell officer will be required to join his parent department i. e. Home Department.
In pursuance of the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II and III) of the Rules, 1981, the Department of Home Affairs and the General Administration Department have appointed Shri. The terms and conditions regarding Chavan's deputation should be decided.
Mr. Devendra. All other administrative matters of Chavan, the cell officer, will be handled by the home department.
5. Shri. The Home Department should immediately relieve Chavan, the cell officer, to accept the appointment on deputation.
