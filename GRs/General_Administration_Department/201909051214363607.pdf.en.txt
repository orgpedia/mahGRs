Government Circular dated 23rd July, 2019- For the Assistant Cell Officers in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the examination is conducted in accordance with the Assistant Services Post-Entrance Training Examination Rules, 1977 and the amendments made therein from time to time. Accordingly, the Post-Interview Training Examination, 2019 for Assistant Section Officers in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission will be held at Sydenham College of Commerce and Economics, B Road, Churchgate, Mumbai 400 020 on September 20, 2019 and September 21, 2019 (Friday-Saturday).
The details of the candidates who have been allowed to appear in the said examination on the basis of the information received from the Ministries/ Departments and the offices of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, including the names, divisions and their seat numbers, etc. are indicated in Annexure' A'. The names of the candidates who have been exempted in the respective subjects as per the previous year's marks are indicated in Annexure' B' and the venue and schedule of the examination etc. are indicated in Annexure' C'. The important instructions given to the candidates for the examination are mentioned in Annexure D and are attached with the Annexure.
The Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission are requested to bring to the notice of all the candidates admitted to the said circular examination and preserve the records in the archives of their department.
The Xerox copies of the official government original books of the subjects marked as (*) in Annexure A or the respective official government books certified by the Establishment Officer of the Department shall be allowed to be used at the time of examination. However, no other books should be written in such a prescribed manner. No other material/ comments etc. can be used during the examination.
