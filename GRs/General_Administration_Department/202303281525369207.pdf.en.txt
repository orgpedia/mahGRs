Government Circular: In order to make the government work faster, streamline the work, keep documents and information safe, and make the decision-making process faster and easier by using the e-office system in the government work in the state government, the necessary instructions have been given according to the above referential circulars. All the administrative departments of the Ministry and the regional offices. Reference Circular dated 01.04.2023 to make use of e-Office system effective from 01.04.2023. The standard operating procedure has been issued as on February 23, 2023.
e-Office is currently being implemented in 6.2 in the administrative departments and field offices of the Ministry. At this point, you need to upgrade to version 7.0. For this, e-Office 6.2, which is currently running, will not be available during this period from 29th March, 2023 to 17th April, 2023. However, instructions are being issued to take the following actions in respect of the existing e-office operating system: a) Upgradation of the existing e-office operating system from 6.2 to 7.0 in the Ministries/ Departments will not be available from 8.00 pm on 29th March, 2023 to 9.00 am on 3rd April, 2023. However, during this period, the Ministries/ Departments shall take action on the e-office system in physical mode on a temporary basis. Once the e-Office functionality is upgraded from 6.2 to 7.0 and available, the responsibility of scanning and uploading the mail/ documents in the new functionality will be with the respective departments. However, all the departments concerned should take note of this and take necessary action.
(b) Upgradation of the existing e-Office system from 6.2 to 7.0 in the field offices will not be available from 8.00 pm on 13th April, 2023 to 9.00 am on 17th April, 2023. However, during this period, the Regional Offices have taken action against the deficiencies in the functioning of e-Office system.
