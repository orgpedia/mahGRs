Preamble: The decision to make a film based on the life of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule was taken by the Government in the Cabinet meeting held on 14th February, 2018 and has been approved as per the decision of the Government in context. The General Administration Department is responsible for certifying the code/ script of the film, ensuring that the film is produced in accordance with historical facts, and supervising the production process. A Committee of Experts has been constituted under Mavz-2013/ No. 143/34 dated 27th April, 2018. The government was contemplating to form the committee. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approves the constitution of the following expert committee to oversee the process of making a film based on the life of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule.
