" Chief Minister Sukarmi Award Scheme" As per the Government decision dated 20th March, 2018, the scheme is being implemented for the year 2018-19 for the Ministerial Cadre. To monitor the implementation of the scheme. 9
The Control Committee has been constituted as per the Government Resolution dated April 9, 2018.
As per the provisions of the government decision, the control committee has the power to inspect the offices of the ministry department as per the requirement for the implementation of the scheme. A committee has been formed for this purpose.
Accordingly, the following teams are being formed for the office inspection and the departments assigned to them are as follows. :

The teams will be as follows: :
1.The team was sent to the concerned department for inspection. It will be held from May 7 to May 25, 2018.
2. All the officers of the establishment shall extend their best cooperation to the team.
3. The Office Inspection Team will review the action being taken to implement the Mukhya Mantri Sukarmi Yojana in the department.
The Office Inspection Team shall submit its report to the Chairman, Control Committee.
5. All the departments will be required to take action on the suggestions made in the report of the Office Inspection Team.
The Chairman, Controlling Committee shall decide on the re-examination of each department as required.
