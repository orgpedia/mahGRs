The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government of India dated 09.08.2016, the Government has established Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation. In this government decision, the organizational structure of the corporation has been fixed. Accordingly, on the basis of the institutional structure of the corporation, an expert person in the field of information technology and an expert person related to the education sector are appointed as a nominated director. Hemant Dabke and R.K. Mathai were appointed as directors. Shri Hemant Dabke and Shri R. K. Mathai have tendered their resignations due to personal reasons and the same has been accepted by the Government. Therefore, the matter of appointing other experts on the organizational structure of the corporation as nominated directors was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The following are being appointed as Nominated Directors on Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation:
Mr. Dhananjay Tambe, Nominated Director.
Anurag Singhvi, Advisor.
