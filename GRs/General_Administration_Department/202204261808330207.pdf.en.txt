the prelude
All India Service Officers in Maharashtra cadre are entitled to travel allowance as per the A.B.S. (Travel Allowance) Rules, 1954. Rule No. (3) (1) It is hereby prescribed that the travelling allowances of the officers of the All India Services shall be regulated in accordance with the rules made by the State Government. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide reference No. 5 is mentioned here. As per the Government decision dated 26.11.2012, the officers of All India Services (Bhaprasay/ Bhaposay/ Bhavasay) working in Maharashtra Cadre are required to submit the aforesaid Reference No. Travel allowance is sanctioned and paid as per the Government decisions mentioned herein 1 to 4. Concessions in respect of travel allowance, transfer travel allowance and air travel as per the Sixth Pay Commission to the All India Service Officers of Maharashtra Cadre vide the aforesaid Reference No. 9 are permitted.
Officers/ Employees of Central Government as per 7th Pay Commission Reference No. 8 mentioned here. As per the provisions of the notification dated 13.07.2017, travelling allowance and transfer travelling allowance have been made admissible.
All India Service officers of Maharashtra cadre will be able to get immediate reservation if they want to travel by any other means other than air travel for government/ court work.

A proposal was under consideration of the government to allow travel allowance and concessions in air travel on par with non-IAS officers in the central government to avoid delays and wastage of time while attending to urgent work.
Travel Allowance and Air Travel Concessions to All India Service Officers of Maharashtra Cadre will continue to be applicable as follows:
Pay Matrix Level-10 in All India Services (Rs. 56100-177500) will continue to be entitled to travel allowance and daily allowance at the rate of 7th Pay Commission for the period under review, at par with the officers of the said pay matrix level in the All India Services in the Central Government.
Level 11 (Pay Matrix)- Rs. 67700-208700, Pay Matrix Level (Level 12) Rs. 78800-209200 and Pay Matrix Level (Level-13) Rs. 123100-215900 in the pay matrix level or in the rank of Head of Department in the All India Services (Refer No. 6 As per the list of officers to be considered as Head of the Department as per the notification mentioned herein) Where the travel time by road/ rail is more than 300 km, air travel by economy class shall be permissible without prior permission of the Secretary of the Department or Divisional Commissioner. (a) Transfer Grant& Packaging Allowance payable after transfer to the officers of All India Services who join the service of the State Government on return/ repatriation from Central Deputation and to the officers of All India Services who reside permanently at a new residential address in another State after retirement from Maharashtra Cadre shall be admissible at the rate of 7th Pay Commission as per the Central Government.
