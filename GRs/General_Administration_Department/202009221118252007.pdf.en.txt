The Proposal:
5 temporary posts in the office of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad have been approved by the Government Decision No. SPI-2018/ Q. No. 409/ K. 28 dated 24/02/2020 was approved to continue from 01/03/2020 to 31/08/2020. Now, under the Government decision dated 31/08/2020 of the Finance Department, the Administrative Departments have been permitted to continue the temporary posts from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021.
Government's decision
The Director, Institute of Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad vide his letter dated 09/09/2020 has proposed that a total of 5 temporary posts on the establishment of the Institute of Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad are not vacant for more than six months and it is necessary to continue those posts, the Government is approving the continuation of the following total 5 temporary posts on the establishment of the Institute of Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad for a period of 6 months from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021.
