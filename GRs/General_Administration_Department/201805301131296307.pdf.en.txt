Circular:
Guidelines have been prescribed vide Circular dated 27th July, 2015 regarding the treatment of Members of the Legislature/ Parliament with respect to their dignity and courtesy and further instructions have been issued vide Circular dated 27th October, 2017 regarding strict compliance of the instructions contained in the said Circular.
However, it has been observed that many departments are not strictly following the instructions and are not giving access to the letters/ breach of privilege notices of the members of the Legislature/ Parliament and are not taking action on their letters and informing them. Many times, the government has been receiving complaints that the concerned legislators/ MPs are not being invited while organizing government functions.
(3) Therefore, all the administrative departments of the Ministry are again advised to strictly follow the instructions in the said circular. Similarly, all the departments/ offices under your control should take care of maintaining a separate register for processing of letters of the Legislature/ Members of Parliament and conduct quarterly reviews to ensure that the same are processed within the prescribed time frame.
