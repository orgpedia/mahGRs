The Proposal -
The revised structure of 271 posts for a total of 26 cadres in the Maharashtra Public Service Commission office was approved by the Government Decision No. 1 in the context. Further, 2 other Government Decisions have included 3 (2 - 1) posts in the composition of the Commission and at present, a total of 274 posts are sanctioned in the Commission. The Government Decision No. 4 in the context allowed the creation and filling up of 45 posts (Nomination 40, Promotion 5) in the Clerk-Typographer cadre at the establishment of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission by creating fictitious posts for a period of one year. With the expiry of this period, the letter at No. 5 in reference to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has again requested to create 45 vacant posts and allow them to be filled by external agencies.
Since the office requires manpower for the smooth functioning of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, it has created 45 hypothetical posts in the clerk-typician cadre after thorough deliberations.
