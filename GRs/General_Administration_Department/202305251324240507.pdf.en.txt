The Proposal:
The Government of India, vide its resolution dated 03.07.2018, has laid down the policy for giving due respect to the persons who were detained/ imprisoned during the period of Emergency declared in India from 25.06.1975 to 21.03.1977. Considering the revenue deficit due to the situation arising out of the outbreak of COVID virus, it was decided to discontinue the implementation of the scheme vide Government Resolution dated 31st July, 2020. After the formation of the new government. As per the decision taken in the Cabinet meeting, the scheme was re-launched with effect from 1 st August, 2022 as per the Government decision dated 28.07.2022.
The date from which the payment of honorarium to the eligible applicants whose applications remained pending from 3rd July, 2018 to 31st July, 2020 (till the closure of the scheme) was under consideration of the Government. Also, the matter of amending the existing policy in line with the issues raised by the Collector was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The policy for grant of honorarium to honour/ suitably honour the persons who had to undergo imprisonment/ imprisonment for social and political reasons under MISA and DIR during the Emergency period from 1975 to 1977 is being amended as follows. Apart from this, other terms and conditions of the government order will also remain the same.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: BUD-1422/ PR No. 197/ SWASAC-1 1) Only those persons who have undergone imprisonment/ imprisonment for social and political reasons under MISA and DIR while fighting for democracy during the Emergency period will be eligible for remuneration.
(2) If the applicant has been convicted twice for social and political reasons under MISA and DIR during the period of emergency, the honorarium shall be admissible to such applicants only once.
As per the Government decision dated 28th July, 2022, the last date for submission of fresh applications has been fixed as 31.10.2022. Applications received after this date will not be considered under any circumstances.
The applicant applying for honorarium under this scheme must be at least 18 years of age at the time of arrest.
5) In case of applicants who are eligible under the scheme, if they are below the age of 18 years, the concerned District Collector should decide on the approval of the application only after verifying the documents related to the arrest and verifying with the concerned jail administration.
The applicants whose applications remained pending from 3rd July, 2018 to 31st July, 2020 (till the closure of the Scheme), should be sanctioned the honorarium from the date of their application.
2.A. No. 6 The list of eligible applicants mentioned here should be submitted to the government by the concerned Collector with consideration of the dues.
