Government Circular: Applications are hereby invited in the prescribed format from eligible candidates for the following training and examination to be conducted in 2018 in connection with the examination for promotion to the post of Assistant Cell Officer in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission: As per the provisions of Rule 3 of the Maharashtra Government Secondary Ministerial Services Departmental Examination Rules, 1977, the regular Assistant Cell Officers working in the Administrative Department of the Ministry as well as in the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission who are completing five years of continuous service as Assistant Cell Officer as on 1st November, 2018 and who have passed the post-service training examination, shall submit the applications in the prescribed format as per Schedule B of Rule-16 of the Cell Officers Departmental Examination Rules, 1977 to their Administrative Department. The prescribed format of applications has been made available under the heading" Application Form" on the Ministry Intranet.
The eligible Assistant Section Officers as above who have not availed the training of this Departmental Examination earlier are applying for this examination for the first time. Submissions must be received by August 4, 2018.
The applications of the Assistant Cell Officers who have failed in the departmental examination held earlier or have been absent in the examination to be held in the year 2018 should be sent as soon as possible by a separate circular.
The Establishment Officers of all the Administrative Departments may kindly bring this circular to the notice of the concerned Assistant Cell Officers (including the Assistant Cell Officers on deputation/ leave) immediately and take their signatures and save the same in their records.
