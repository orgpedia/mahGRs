Government Decision: Procurement of 71 ALL IN ONE PC Computers for Officers/ Staff of the Department
A meeting of the Project Implementation Committee was held to approve the project. The meeting was held under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Services) in the 6th floor chamber of his Ministry on 5th October 2020 at 11.30 am. In the meeting, it has been approved to purchase 5 computers first and then every month in a phased manner. Accordingly, the purchase of 5 computers has been approved under the Government Decision dated 25th November 2020. After this, approval has been given to purchase 25 computers under the government decision dated December 8, 2020.
2. Tender for purchase of ALL IN ONE 25 NUG COMPUTER for officers/ staff of General Administration Department was floated on 23/12/2020 on GeM Portal
The tender was opened on 2/1/2021 and the Government is approving the purchase of GEM Portal 25 All In One Computer from KJM GROUP SYSTEM SOLUTIONS, MUMBAI on account of lower price. The total expenditure for the purchase of computers will be Rs. 10,62,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh Sixty Two Thousand only).
