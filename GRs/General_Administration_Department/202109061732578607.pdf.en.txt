Government order:
A revised order has been issued under the General Administration Department, Government Resolution, dated 29th July, 2021 regarding the changes in the current financial year 2021-22 in the wake of COVID-19 pandemic. Considering the possibility of a third wave in the current situation, as well as considering the COVID-19 pandemic and the restrictions imposed in view of it, in order to limit the expenditure on transfer allowance, this Government has decided to limit transfers on special grounds to 10% of the total working posts in the current financial year 2021-22.
In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the following officers of the cadre of Senior Assistant Director (Information)/ Senior Deputy Editor/ District Information Officer/ Public Relations Officer, Group-A, (Pay Scale S-15: 41800-132300) in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations are being transferred and posted to the place indicated in column 4 before their names in the interest of working and administrative convenience.
