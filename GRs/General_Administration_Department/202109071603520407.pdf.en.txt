Read 1) Government Circular No: SEPRP-2021/ Q. No.41/ Q. 17, D. August 6, 2021.
Applications are invited from all eligible candidates for the post of Assistant Section Officer in the Government Circular Post-Entrance Training Examination 2021. The application was filed on August 6, 2021. The post-service training class for the year 2021 of Assistant Cell Officers in the Ministry Administrative Department and Maharashtra Public Service Commission Office will be held on September 20, 2021.
The Conference is being held from 12th November, 2021 at Auditorium, 6th Floor, Mantralaya, Mumbai.
The names of the trainees admitted to the said training classes on the basis of information received from the administrative departments are indicated in Annexure' A'.
The programme of training is given in Annexure B and the subjects of training are given in Annexure C.
All the Ministries/ Departments and the Maharashtra Public Service Commission Office are requested to immediately bring this circular to the notice of all the concerned trainees (including those on deputation/ leave/ transferred from other departments) and take their signatures and preserve the same in their records. As per Rule 14 of the Maharashtra Secondary and Higher Secondary Services (Assistant) Services Post-Entrance Training Examination Rules, 1977, the concerned candidates should be given necessary permission to attend the said training classes commencing from September 20, 2021.
4. The prescribed books required for the study of the candidates admitted to the training should be provided by the concerned departments.
