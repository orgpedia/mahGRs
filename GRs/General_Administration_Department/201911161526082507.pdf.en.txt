Preamble: GOVERNMENT DECISION The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the General Administration Department, dated 20.01.2014. Accordingly, during the financial year 2019-20, the Government was considering to disburse funds to Vasantrao Naik Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur for training and salary under the combined probationary training programme-5 and 6 for the period from August, 2019 to December, 2019.
Government Decision: For the financial year 2019-2020, a total budgetary provision of Rs. 7,57,49, 000/- has been approved under the head Accounts 2070-Other Administrative Services-36-Assistant Grants (Salary) to the tune of Rs. 64,49,000/- and Rs. 6,93,00, 000/- under the 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Salary). Now as per the Government Circular at Roll No. 2 in respect of Finance Department and as per the Government Circular at Roll No. 3 in respect of Office 25, 60% of the total budgetary provision of Rs. 7,57,49, 000/- i.e. 4,54,49, 400/- for the first nine months (ending December 2019) to be disbursed through Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BIMS).
For the financial year 2019-2020, for the period April to July, 2019, the target of Rs. 24,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Four Lakhs only) and 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 16,17,000/- (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Seventeen Thousand only) aggregating to Rs. 40,17,000/- (Rupees Forty Lakh Seventeen Thousand only) to Vasantrao Naik Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur Training Institute.
