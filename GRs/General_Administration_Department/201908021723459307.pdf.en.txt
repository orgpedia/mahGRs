The process of calling the names of the candidates in the waiting list to the Commission must be completed before the expiry of the validity period of the waiting list fixed by the Commission. If a candidate from the original recommendation list is not able to join due to a court case, the administrative departments cannot make a demand for a candidate from the waiting list instead of that candidate.
The Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary of the Administrative Department of the Ministry administering the respective cadre shall be the controlling officer of the above procedure.
