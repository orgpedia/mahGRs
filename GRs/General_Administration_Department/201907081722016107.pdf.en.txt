Preamble: It was under the consideration of the Government to propose amendments to the Maharashtra Lok Ayukta and Deputy Lok Ayukta Act, 1971 and to grant daily allowance, sitting allowance and travelling allowance to the non-official members and special invitees of the Joint Drafting Committee constituted to deliberate and draft the amended Act for enacting the Saksham Lok Ayukta Act. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: Keeping in view the fact that the Joint Drafting Committee is going to do the important work of drafting the Act, the rates of daily allowance, sitting allowance and travel allowance are being fixed for the non-official members and special invitees of the Joint Drafting Committee as a special case. These rates will be applicable with retrospective effect to the earlier meetings held in this regard.
