The government has decided to provide government grants to 16 categories of gallantry medal/ service medal holders in the Army under the Maharashtra Gaurav Puraskar Yojana. Air Marshal Adi Rustamji Gandhi was awarded the Param Vishisht Seva Medal. 1,15,000/- is admissible. However, as per the Government Decision, S.P.V.S. Shoupu- 2001/2898/ P.S. No. 16/2002)/ 28, dated 16.08.2002, the recipients of the Shrestha Medal are to be paid the difference between the amount of the medal received earlier under the Gaurav Puraskar Scheme and the amount admissible for the Shrestha Medal received thereafter. Government Resolution, S.P.V. No. Shoupu 2003/ C.P. 113/2003/28, dated 03.09.2003, awarded to Shri Adi Rustamji Gandhi, Param Vishisht Seva Medal, a sum of Rs. Rs 58,000. Meanwhile, the Government Decision, S.P.V. No. Magopu 2016/ P. 247/28, dated 02.03.2019, the Government has decided to provide separate grant for each gallantry/ service medal under the Maharashtra Gaurav Puraskar Scheme to the Grandmothers/ Ex-Servicemen of the State of Maharashtra who have received more than one medal in the category of Gallantry/ Service Medal.
