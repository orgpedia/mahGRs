Introduction: Refer No. The decision to procure the new multi-role helicopter H145 from Airbus Helicopters, a French manufacturer, was taken for the Maharashtra government to be based in Nagpur for anti-Naxal operations. The airline will provide DGCA/ EASA approved training to the government pilots as per the terms and conditions of the tender process with Airbus and as per the provisions of the contract with them. Accordingly, the matter of sending the pilots of the Directorate General of Aviation abroad for revision training was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The following selected pilots of Directorate General of Aviation will be allowed to travel abroad for training in H145 helicopter during the period specified in their name. Government approval is being given to leave the headquarters on 3/6/2019.
