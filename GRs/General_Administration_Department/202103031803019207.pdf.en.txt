Introduction: The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations uses various means to inform and publicize the various public welfare schemes of the government. For example, 1) Corporate Pullup Standees 2) Vinyl Printing on Sun Board, Mounting 3) Digital Wall Painting 4) Asepi Display Board (with UV Printing) 5) LD Foamsheet Banner 6) Stage Backdrop etc. The matter was under consideration of the government. Further, the Government decision dated 2 nd March, 2021 was issued in this regard and the revised directions are being issued by superseding the said Government decision.
Government Decision: To work through various media for information and publicity of various welfare schemes of the Government through Corporate Pullup Standees, two organizations namely 1. Central Art, Mumbai 2. Krish Intratrade Pvt. Ltd. Aurangabad and their rates are being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions. :
In order to make the tender process inclusive and healthy for the information and publicity of the government's public welfare schemes, the tender process should be completed within the prescribed time frame every year in a pre-planned manner so that government expenditure can also be saved.
The Directorate General is of the view that the rates quoted by the Institute in the said tender in June/ July, 2019 are the lowest as on date.
3. Subject-wise tendering process is carried out by the Directorate General as per the general CVC guidelines of the Central Government regarding the tendering process.
4. The government should be informed every month about the implementation of various public welfare schemes of the government in a quantitative manner or how the results will be known after the implementation of the publication.
