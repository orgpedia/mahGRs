Government circular
The Provisional Common Seniority of Selected Category Short Writers in the Ministry Department was notified vide Government Circular dated 23.01.2023. All the Ministries/ Departments were informed to forward the objections/ objections, if any, to the General Administration Department by 06.02.2023. However, no errors/ objections have been received from the Ministries/ Departments. Therefore, the present seniority is being made interim and has been added. The General Administration Department, Government Notification 21.06.2021 has adopted the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 while preparing the said seniority list.
