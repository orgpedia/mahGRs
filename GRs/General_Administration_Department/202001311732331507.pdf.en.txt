the order
The services of Smt. Sandhya Gawas, Cell Officer, Women and Child Development Department are being handed over on deputation for a period of one year to the office of the State Chief Commissioner of Right to Service, Mumbai, under the Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights, subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mrs. Gawas shall commence her term of service as a Deputy on the date on which she enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall, on the date on which she enters upon her original office, cease to hold the office of Deputy Commissioner after one year.
(b) If his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of his deputation.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
Mrs. Gawas will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that she intends to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. (e) In accordance with the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981, the General Administration Department, Office No. 18 Further, the Department of Women and Child Development (DWCD) shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation in consultation with each other.
3. All other administrative matters of Mrs. Gawas, Cell Officer, General Administration Department, Office No. 18 and will be handled by the Department of Women and Child Development (Establishment).
