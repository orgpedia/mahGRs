Introduction: The Government Transport Service, Mumbai, under the General Administration Department (Rajshastra), maintains a fleet of D.V. vehicles under the Government Transport Service, Worli, Mumbai, for the transportation of dignitaries, state guests, etc. visiting Mumbai.
A review of 63 vehicles for the transportation of very important persons, state guests and other dignitaries for the office of Government Transport Service, Mumbai is given in the above Reference No. 1 has been approved by the government. At present, 7 new e-vehicles were under consideration to be purchased from the available financial provisions as the purchase of 8 vehicles was required in addition to the sanctioned vehicles.
As per the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 2, the Department of Environment and Climate Change has announced the Electric Vehicle Policy, 2021.
Since e-vehicles are not available on GeM portal, these vehicles need to be procured from the manufacturer/ distributor considering the urgency. As per the policy, it is mandatory to buy a total of 7 e-vehicles- 3 vehicles of MG ZX EV and 4 vehicles of Tata Motors' Tata Nexon EV.
Accordingly, Ma. As approved by the Deputy Chief Minister (Finance Minister), the Government has decided to purchase 7E-vehicles for the office of Government Transport Service, Mumbai as follows.
Government's decision:
A review of 63 vehicles has been approved for the transportation of very important persons, state guests and other dignitaries for the office of Government Transport Service, Mumbai. Since the 8 vehicles approved in the review need to be purchased and these vehicles are not available on the Government e-Market (GeM) portal, currently 7 new e-vehicles are being sold by Fortune Cars Pvt. Ltd. (Total 3 of them) are approved to be purchased by these companies from the authorized dealers of MG Motors.
