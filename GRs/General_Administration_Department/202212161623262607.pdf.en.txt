The order:
As per the Government Order dated 13.10.2022, a total of 30 Deputy Editors/ Information Assistants (Group-C) of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations in the pay scale S. 14:38,600- 1,22,800 in the selection list for the year 2021-2022 have been promoted to the post of Assistant Director/ Information Officer/ Superintendant Books and Publications Group-B (Junior) Pay band S. 15:41,800- 1,32,300 in net casual basis subject to the conditions prescribed by this order. However, 2 Information Assistants who have failed only the Hindi Language Test have qualified for promotion to the post of Assistant Director/ Information Officer/ Superintending Books and Publications Group-B (Junior) in the Divisional Promotion Committee meeting dated August 30, 2022, by passing the Hindi Language Test on August 26, 2022. These 2 Information Officers/ Assistant Directors are being promoted to the post indicated in column 6 against their name.
