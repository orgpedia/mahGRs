The Central Government has decided to open a new head of expenditure for availing the Incentive of Digitization Non-interest loan under Part IV to the Directorate General of Information Technology in the first phase under Part-I of the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Expenditure of the States.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department

Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is December 16, 2022.
Preamble: The Central Government has decided to provide 50 years of interest free loan to the States under the Special Assistance Scheme for capital investment in 2022-23. Under this, the Directorate of Information Technology has included the project of Incentive for Digitization under Part-4.
Under the above scheme, the Directorate of Information Technology has been given an incentive of Rs. Rs 91.73 crore. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to open a new head of expenditure for expenditure on works to be taken up from the borrowings received from the Central Government under the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Investment to the States.
Government Decision: Under the Central Government's Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment Scheme, the Directorate of Information Technology will receive Rs. Rs 91.73 crore. A new head of account is being opened for expenditure on works to be done from that fund as follows.
