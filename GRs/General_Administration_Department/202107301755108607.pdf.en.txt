Since these transfers are required to be effected effectively, the salary of all the transferred lower grade scribes up to 31.7.2021 should be deducted by the department in which they are presently working.

Instructions are being issued to all the administrative departments that if the salary from 1.8.2021 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the department who was working before the transfer, he will be held personally responsible.
