circular
The Departmental Examination-2018 for promotion to the post of Assistant Cell Officers in the Ministries Department and Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and for the directly recruited Cell Officers was held from Thursday, December 13, 2018 to Saturday, December 15, 2018 at the Examination Room of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Goods and Services Tax Bhawan, 9th Floor, Mazgaon, Mumbai 400 010. The result of the examination will be declared as mentioned in the annexure.
All the concerned Ministries/ Departments as well as the Maharashtra Public Service Commission office are requested to bring this result to the notice of the concerned candidates. Similarly, as per Rule 14 of the Maharashtra Secondary Ministerial Services (Cell Officers) Divisional Examination Rules, 1977 and Rule 9 of the Cell Officers in the Ministerial Department (Divisional Examination and Training) Rules, 1991, a candidate who passes the examination in the first attempt and obtains 70 per cent or more marks in any of the question papers, a special merit shall be recorded in his confidential report.
There is no provision for re-examination of answer sheets in the Maharashtra Government Secondary Ministerial Service (Cell Officers) Divisional Examination Rules. Government Circular, General Administration Department, No.Examination-1088/ P.No.
35/17 dated 25th April, 1988, the candidates who want to get their marks recalculated are required to send their applications to this office through their Administrative Department within one month from the date of declaration of the result of the examination. Applications will not be considered at this time.
4.The administrative departments should take appropriate action by taking a written explanation from the candidates who have been absent from the examination even after filling the admission form.
5.The result of the said examination should be recorded in the service book of the concerned candidates without fail by the administrative department.
