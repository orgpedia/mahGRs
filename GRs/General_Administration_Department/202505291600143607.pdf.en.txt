Read here No. He has been appointed as State Information Commissioner vide Government of Maharashtra Notification, dated 4th April, 2025. The government's decision:
The following is being done in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019, dated 30th January, 2019 at No.9 here and the Government of India Notification No.2 dated 24th October, 2019: -
I. Date of fixation of pay: - 05th May, 2025 (ex. P), Il. Monthly Salary: Rs. 2,25,000 - (fixed) III. Dearness Allowance, Leave, King Cashing, Medical Facilities, Accommodation, Holiday Travel AAA, Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance will be admissible as per the rules published for the post of State Information Commissioner vide Central Government Notification dated 24.10.2019 and as amended from time to time.
This Government decision is being issued subject to the provisions of Rule 8 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 and Rule 14, 15, 16, 17, 18, 19B of the Right to Information (Term of Office, Salary, Allowances and other Terms and Conditions of Service of the Chief Information Commissioner, Information Commissioner in the Central Information Commission and State Chief Information Commissioner and State Information Commissioner in the State Information Commission) Rules, 2019 of the Government of India.
