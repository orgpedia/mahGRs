Preamble: State Government 4th Class Employees Central Association, Maharashtra and State Government Employees Central Association, Maharashtra have submitted their various demands. The three-day strike was announced on August 7, 8 and 9, 2018. The employees have been asked not to participate in the strike. It was under the consideration of the government to regularize the absence of government employees who were absent in the office.

Absence of Government officers/ employees who participated in the three day strike on 7th, 8th and 9th August, 2018 should be regularized as" Extraordinary Leave" without any break in service. However, the period of such extraordinary leave shall be subject to the decision of the Government in the Finance Department, No. Seniway 1001/29/ SEWA 4, Dy. With the exception of the order dated 14.01.2001, the service should be treated as qualifying service for pension, irrespective of the length of service.
