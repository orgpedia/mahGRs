The second phase of the memorial to be built at Naganathanna Naikavadi in Walwa, G. Sangli, will cost Rs. 1665.00 lakh has been submitted for administrative approval and the proposal was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: 1. Padma Bhushan Krantiveer Dr. Nagnath Anna Naikavadi's Maje Nagnathnagar, Ta. The proposal of Rs. 1665.00 lakh for the second phase of construction of the memorial at Walwa, District Sangli submitted by the Collectorate, Sangli has been scrutinized by the Public Works Department and approved at an estimated cost of Rs. 1031.00 lakh. The Public Works Department and the Planning Department have agreed to the plan and budget of the said work subject to the following conditions.
a) While erecting a monument, it is necessary to fix responsibility for its maintenance and if the maintenance organization is non-governmental, then to sign a contract with the organization and such organization will take care of its maintenance in the future.
If unable to do so, the Collector, Sangli should take care of planning to first identify other institutions for maintenance and only then release the previous institution from responsibility.
(b) The District Collector, Sangli should take care that the construction of the memorial is completed on time and the current status of the construction of the memorial should be informed to the government in a timely manner. It should also be ensured that the monument does not deteriorate in the future.
(c) The Collector, Sangli shall ensure that the work is completed within the limit of administrative sanction amount granted to the memorial.

d) The Collector shall ensure that the expenditure of the administrative sanction amount shall be spent on the items approved by the Public Works Department and attached with the return.
(e) The work should be taken up on the provision made in a staggered manner and the work should be taken up only after making a detailed budget.
(e) When carrying out the actual work, the Department of the Environment shall act in accordance with the guidelines referred to in Government Decision No. ENV/ 2013/ Q. No. 177/ T.C. 1, dated 10/1/2014.
e) The Public Works Department should take action under the Government Decision No. BDG2017/ PR No. 60/ Buildings-2, dated 7/7/2017.
f) The competent officers of the Chief Engineer, S.B. Regional Department, Pune and the Collector should be testified on the budget of this work.
The expenditure on this work should be spent under the head Demand No. A-4, Head of Accounts, 2059 Public Works 80 General 800- State Plan (00) (02) Construction of Monuments and Statues of the Nation/ Great Persons (2059 3177), 53 Major Constructions.
