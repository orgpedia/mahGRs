Assistant Section Officer Post-Interview Training Examination, 2019. The request was made on May 17, 2019. Now, applications in the prescribed format are being invited from the candidates who have failed in the previous post-service training examination or have been absent in the examination, and the prescribed format of application has been made available on the intranet of the Ministry under the heading" Application Form."
However, all the administrative departments of the ministry and the establishment officers of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission shall ensure that all the details of the eligible candidate's application are correct and send the certified application to this department in a manner that it reaches the date of August 3, 2019. Please make sure that the candidate's full name is mentioned in the application.
Candidates who are eligible to appear for the first time in the examination of Assistant Cell Officer Post-Interview Training Examination-2019. Training classes for candidates whose applications have been sent within the prescribed time as per the circular dated 17th May, 2019 will be conducted at the time of registration. It was held on June 21, 2019. Apart from the candidates mentioned in Annexure A of the said circular, those who are eligible to appear for the first time in the Assistant Cell Officer Post-Interview Training Examination-2019, but have not applied for the examination. Candidates who have not sent their applications within the stipulated time as per the circular dated May 17, 2019, can also submit their applications in the prescribed format.
The establishment officers of the administrative department will certify the applications of such candidates in the prescribed format and hand them over to this department along with the application of the candidates appearing for the re-examination.
Submitted by August 3, 2019.
