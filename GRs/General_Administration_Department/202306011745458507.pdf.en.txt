Preamble Financial assistance is provided to the next of kin of the soldiers/ disabled soldiers of the State who have been martyred in war, combat situations and security related operations in the country and to the members of the Border Security Force and other similar paramilitary forces/ their families who have been martyred in security related operations in the country, as per the Government decision dated 04/10/2000. Accordingly, the Department of Sainik Welfare, Pune has issued a proposal to provide financial assistance to the dependents of the martyred soldiers Major Harsh Deepak Johri, G. 2 submitted to the government.
Government's decision
Pursuant to the proposal submitted by the Department of Sainik Welfare to the Government vide letter dated 19/05/2023, the Government is approving the grant of financial assistance to the dependents of Major Harsh Deepak Johri, a decorated jawan from the State of Maharashtra, as mentioned in the table below.
