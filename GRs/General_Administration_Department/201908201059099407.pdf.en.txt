Read 1) Government Circular No.: Examination 2119/ Q.No. July 12, 2019.
Government Circular Syllabus in the Government of Maharashtra Secondary Ministerial Services (Cell Officers) Divisional Examination Rules, 1977. This has been amended by notification dated 31st January, 2015. According to this course, the training class of the departmental examination to be held for the promotion of assistant cell officers in the administrative department of the ministry and the office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission will be held on Monday. Tuesday 26th August 2019. Conference Hall, 6th Floor on 1st October, 2019. It will be held at the Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai. The names of the candidates admitted to the said training class on the basis of the information received from the Ministries/ Departments as well as from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission are given in Annexure-A, the programme of training in Annexure-B and the details of the subjects of training, number of lecturers and lectures concerned in Annexure-C.
All the Ministries/ Departments and the office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission are requested to bring this circular to the notice of all concerned Assistant Section Officers (including candidates on deputation/ leave) in writing and allow them to attend the above training classes.
