A copy of the Office Memorandum dated 22/03/2018, No. 42/06/2018-P& PW (G), Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Department of Pension& Pensioners' Welfare, Government of India, New Delhi, forwarded for information and appropriate action in respect of All India Service Pensioners/ Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre.
As per the aforesaid Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Government of India, with effect from 01.01.2018, the increase in the rate of Dearness Relief by 2% and other provisions mentioned in the Memorandum shall also be applicable to All India Service Pensioners/ Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre. Dearness Relief at the rate of 7% will now be admissible to All India Service Pensioners/ Family Pensioners of Maharashtra State Cadre with effect from 01.01.2018.
