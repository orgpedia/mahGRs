Suggestions to the candidates:
1) It is mandatory for every candidate to carry his / her official identity card during the examination period for proof of identity for each session during the examination.
2) The seating arrangement will be as per Hall No. B Seat No. on the notice board at the examination centre.
3) The candidate should reach the examination centre 45 minutes before the commencement of the examination.
4) No candidate will be allowed to enter the examination hall after the commencement of the examination.
5) As per the schedule of the examination, a warning bell will be given ten minutes before the start of each paper as well as before the delivery of the paper.
6) Candidates must fill in all the required details on the cover of the answer sheet accurately before starting to write the answer sheet. The candidate should not write anything on the back of the homepage. The candidate should not write any text like his / her name, signature, seat number, etc. on the inside pages of the answer sheet as well as the supplementary number. No part of the answer sheet / question paper should be signed by the candidate. Also, signing or directing certain marks / marks for identification will be considered objectionable and action will be taken in the case as mentioned in A.No.9 below.
7) The candidate should write the answers on both sides of all other pages except the front page and the back side of the answer sheet.
8) Official Government books / Acts / Rules / Government Decisions prescribed for the examination shall be allowed to be used by the candidates in the examination hall at the time of the paper. In case the prescribed original books are not available, Xerox copies of the prescribed books certified by the establishment authorities of the concerned department will be allowed to be used during the examination. However, the books prescribed for the examination or Xerox copies of the books or copies of Acts / Rules / Government Decisions shall not contain any other kind of writing.
9) Candidates should not indulge in any kind of objectionable behaviour in the examination hall. A candidate who exhibits objectionable behaviour during the examination such as copying / viewing / writing answers from or in conversation with other candidates, carrying a mobile phone, pager or other electronic devices in the examination hall, etc., shall be declared unsuccessful in the relevant question paper and shall be disqualified from appearing in the question papers for the remainder of the year.
10) Minutes before the end of the exam, candidates should wrap their answer sheets and supplements together. Do not leave the venue while the exam is in progress and after the exam is over, unless permitted by the examiner.
11) The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued a notification regarding the provision of writing to persons with disabilities who are not able to write the answer sheets. As per the Government Circular dated 5th October, 2021, "Guidelines on Conduct of Written Examination in respect of Persons with Disabilities, 2021" has been prescribed. Candidates with disabilities who require scribes for Clerk-Typewriter Service Entrance Examination 2026 should submit an application to the General Administration Department / V.B.A. through their department along with the required documents as per the provisions of the above Government Circular.
12) A time limit of 2 hours has been fixed for each question paper of this examination.
