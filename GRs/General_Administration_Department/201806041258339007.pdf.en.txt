Smt. Reshma Katare, Auditor, Co-operative Societies, Grade-I, Palghar is being appointed on deputation for a period of one year initially to the post of Assistant Cell Officer in the office of the State Information Commission, Headquarters subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mrs. Reshma Katare, Auditor, Co-operative Societies, Grade-I shall commence her service as a Deputy on the date she assumes charge of her office and shall cease to hold office on the date she resumes her official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ Competent Authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
Rule 40 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981 (para. (ii) The Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles and the Department of General Administration shall mutually decide the terms and conditions of the deputation taking into account the provisions contained in clause (b).
All other administrative matters of Mrs. Reshma Katare, Auditor, Co-operative Societies, Category-1 will be handled by the Department of Co-operation, Marketing and Textiles.
