The Proposal:
The sealed tenders were invited for the period from 1/7/2018 to 30/6/2019 for the maintenance and repair of gas, stove burners for the operation of Mantralaya Restaurant, Square Restaurant, Vidhan Bhavan Restaurant, Mumbai and Konkan Bhavan Restaurant, Konkan Bhavan, Navi Mumbai for the sale of staff uniforms, empty cans, gunny bags etc. Refer to No. 1. The matter of approval of tenders in Annexure-A was under consideration as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 01.12.2016.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of General Administration is responsible for the maintenance of restaurants, square restaurants, Vidhan Bhavan restaurants, Mumbai and Konkan Bhavan restaurants, washing of uniforms of employees of restaurants/ restaurants in Konkan Bhavan, Navi Mumbai, sale of empty cans and gunny bags in restaurants, repair and maintenance of gas stoves in restaurants. Tenders were invited from local suppliers as per the provisions prescribed in the Government Resolution dated 01.12.2016. The bids received have been scrutinised. 01.07.2018 to D. For the period up to 30.06.2019, as per Schedule No. 1, 2 and 3 of Annexure A, tenders are being accepted for sale of empty bins and gunny bags at minimum rates for washing of uniforms and gas/ stove maintenance and repair of staff of the said food/ restaurant.
