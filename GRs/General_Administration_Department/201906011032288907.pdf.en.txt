Lok Sabha Elections 2019: Salary, allowances of gazetted officers at state, district and tehsil levels
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. CEL-2019/ PR No. 579/19/33 (1)
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: May 31, 2019
The Lok Sabha elections in Maharashtra will be held in four phases on April 11, 18, 23 and 29. From the announcement of the Lok Sabha General Election Schedule on 10th March 2019 to the completion of the election process on 27th May 2019, the Gazetted Officers at the State, District and Tehsil level, who were directly involved in the election work, had to carry out the sensitive, meticulous and responsible work in this time frame. Keeping in view the workload, scope of work and working days etc. of the officers concerned in these elections, the Government is approving the payment of remuneration/ honorarium to the below mentioned State, District and Tehsil level and Gazetted Officers related to election work as per the 7th Pay Commission in their month of April, 2019. Provided that such amount of any officer shall not exceed one month's salary and the said remuneration shall be payable to an officer only once.
a) State Level: 1. Chief Secretary 2. Additional Chief Secretary (Home) 3. Chief Electoral Officer 4. Director General, Directorate of Information and Public Relations 5. Additional Chief Electoral Officer 6. Joint Secretary, Office of the Chief Secretary 7. State Informatics Officer, Maharashtra State 8. Gazetted Officer in the Office of the Chief Electoral Officer 9. Gazetted Officer in the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations whose services have been made available for physical election work in the Office of the Chief Electoral Officer
Gazetted Officers in the National Informatics Centre (NIC) whose services have been made available for physical election work in the office of the Chief Electoral Officer.
