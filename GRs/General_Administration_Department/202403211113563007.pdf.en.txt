The Proposal:
Paraplegic Rehabilitation Centre, Khadki, Pune for Exes with 100% Disability
For rehabilitation of soldiers, accommodation and treatment expenses, etc., per annum, as per the Government Decision in the Reference No. (1), Rs. 10,00,000 - (Rupees Ten Lakhs only)
is approved.
Considering the fact that the institute provides all the maintenance to the Paraplegic Ex-Servicemen who are residents of the state of Maharashtra and the responsibility of the government as well as the rising inflation index.
In view of the background as well as the fact that there has been no increase in the said grant-in-aid for the last more than 11 years, the Government was contemplating to increase the grant-in-aid given annually for rehabilitation of Paraplegic Ex-Servicemen in the Institute in line with the annual expenditure on 13 Paraplegic Ex-Servicemen. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
The government's decision:
In the above context, the Institute of Paraplegic Rehabilitation Centre Khadki, Pune, for the rehabilitation of Paraplegic Ex-Servicemen domiciled in the State of Maharashtra, is under reference on 15th February, 2012.
The subsidy under the Government Decision of Rs. 10,00,000 - (Rupees Ten Lakhs only) per annum has been increased to Rs. 19,50,000 - (Rupees Nineteen Lakh Fifty Thousand only) is being approved by the Government.
Expenditure on this matter shall be incurred under the head 'Demand No. A-5, 2235 - Social Security and Welfare, 60 - Other Social Security and Welfare Programmes, 199 - Assistance to Other Non-Governmental Organisations, (00) (06) - Auxiliary Grants to Paraplegic Rehabilitation Centre for the Disabled, Khadki, Pune, 31 Auxiliary Grants (Non-Payment) (22358058)' and shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year.
