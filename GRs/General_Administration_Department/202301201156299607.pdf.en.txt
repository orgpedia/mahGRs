The Rajiv Gandhi Commission for Science and Technology (RGSCST) has been established under the Maharashtra Act No. 15 of 2004 with the objective of promoting, promoting and promoting the adoption of science and technology for the benefit of society in various social issues of the State. As per Rule 14 of the said Act, the Rajiv Gandhi Commission for Science and Technology (RGSCST) is funded by the Consolidated Fund of the Government in the form of grants-in-aid in each financial year.
Regular officers/ staff have been appointed to the sanctioned posts of the office of the Rajiv Gandhi Commission for Science and Technology with effect from May, 2018 and are being paid emoluments and allowances.
