Subject: Filling up of the posts of Under Secretary, Cell Officer and Assistant Cell Officer in the Mumbai office of the State Election Commission, Maharashtra on deputation.
Reference: Govt. Letter dated 03.11.2021.
Sir/ Madam, as per the aforesaid reference letter, the State Election Commission, Maharashtra, had invited applications for filling up the posts of Under Secretary (1 post), Cell Officer (1 post) and Assistant Cell Officer (2 posts) in its Mumbai office on deputation from the ministerial cadre. However, no information has been received from the department about any aspirant or any officer/ employee not willing for the post.
