Government's statement:
Funds are made available for the development of airports/ runways in the State under heading 3053 0146. Under this head, for the financial year 2022-23, Rs. 15.00 crore has been budgeted. So far, the Maharashtra Airport Development Company has not been provided funds from the original budget provision.
Under the head 3053-0146, within the limit of budgetary provision for development of airports/ runways in the State, Rs. 3.15 crore (Rs. 3.15 crore) has been released by the Finance Department as per Table 1 on Budget Distribution System for making available funds to Maharashtra Airport Development Company. The funds are being provided to the Maharashtra Airport Development Company (MADC).
