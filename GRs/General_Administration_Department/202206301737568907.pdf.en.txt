the order
Shri Anup Kudav, Cell Officer, Public Works Department is being appointed on deputation for a period of one year initially for the post of" Cell Officer" in the office of State Commission for Right to Service, Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai under General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Mr. Anup Kudav, Cell Officer shall commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner from the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Commissioner, and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his parent Department, complete the period of his deputation by handing over the charge of the Deputy Commissioner, one year thereafter.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of their deputation, if the Government considers their services necessary in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Public Works Department and the General Administration Department shall determine the terms and conditions of the deputation of Mr. Anup Kudav after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II and III) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mr. Anup Kudav, Cell Officer will be handled by the Public Works Department.
4. Mr. Anup Kudav, Cell Officer to be relieved immediately by the Public Works Department for accepting the appointment on deputation.
