Government circular
The Provisional Common Seniority of Short Story Writers in the Ministry Department dated 01.01.2023 was issued vide Government Circular dated 23.01.2023. All the Ministries/ Departments were informed to forward the objections/ objections, if any, to the General Administration Department by 06.02.2023. However, no errors/ objections have been received from the Ministries/ Departments regarding the said seniority list. Therefore, the said seniority is being made interim and is annexed to this circular. The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 dated 21.06.2021 have been adopted while preparing the said seniority list.
