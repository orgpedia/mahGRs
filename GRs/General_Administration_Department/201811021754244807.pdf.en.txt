The Proposal:
The renovation work of the restaurant in the main building of the Ministry is being carried out by the Public Works Department. In the Mantralaya restaurant, the tables and chairs used to be made of plywood on an iron frame. The tables and chairs were old and had cockroaches and other insects on them, so they had to be sprayed with insecticides frequently. Therefore, it was decided to remove all the tables and chairs in the restaurant and instead use stainless steel tables and chairs, as well as teakwood tables and chairs for the officers' room. Accordingly, the budget was sought from the Executive Engineer, Department of Town and Country Planning. The budget for the new table and chairs has been received from the Executive Engineer, Department of Town and Country Planning. The proposal was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Administrative approval is being accorded for the purchase of stainless steel table and chairs for staff dining room and teakwood table and chairs and other furniture for officers room in the restaurant in the main building of the Ministry under the General Administration Department for an amount of Rs. 51,48,235/- (Rupees One Hundred and Nine Lakh Forty Eight Thousand Two Hundred and Thirty Five only).
(2) The Executive Engineer, City Division, Mumbai shall take action to obtain the approval of the concerned Superintending Engineer on the rates beyond the prescribed rates and shall ensure completion of the work at the approved rates. The procurement process should be done as per the procurement manual along with the Government Decision dated 1/12/2016 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour.
The cost of the said work shall be met from the provision sanctioned under the head of Accounts 2075 Composite General Service, 108-Hotel Storage Division, (00) (01) Mantralaya Restaurant (2075 0131), 13 Office Expenditure for the year 2018-19 for Mantralaya Restaurant.
