Government Resolution No: Rep.-2022/ P. No. 146/12-A
It is hereby approved to upgrade the pay scale of the post of Assistant Professor Class-I (Pay Scale S 20-56100177500) in the rank of Mrs. Veena Vinayak Supekar, Assistant Commissioner (Development), Divisional Commissioner Office, Aurangabad or Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini Prabodhini, Pune. After this, the post of Assistant Professor Class-1 will automatically be demoted to the basic pay scale.
The appointment shall be subject to the following terms and conditions as provided in the Annexure-II of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Residential Service...) Rules, 1981. :
(1) The officer shall be recalled if the department concerned considers the need for service in the interest of public service before the expiry of the period of the said deputation. If the Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini, Pune does not require the services of the above employee, then the concerned will have to give a notice of 3 months. The government should be informed about this.
(2) The expenditure of salary/ leave salary during the period of deputation shall be borne by Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini, Pune.
3) The leave salary and pension contribution should be paid by the Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini Prabodhini, Pune as per the rates prescribed in Annexure-4 of the Maharashtra Civil Service (Period of Incumbency, Non-Residential Service...) Rules, 1981. ANNIO 2012/ Q.No.96/ SEWA-4, Dy. Action should be taken regarding National Pension as prescribed under 21.08.2014.
5) If a departmental inquiry is to be conducted against the concerned officer, his services should be returned to the parent department.
6) The decision was taken by the Finance Ministry. Reference No. 363/2022/ SERVICE-9 is being issued vide Consent received vide Dt. 24.11.2022.
