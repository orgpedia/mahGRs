Circular:
The State Cabinet in its meeting dated 3 rd October, 2018 had decided to undertake a special cleanliness drive in the Ministry as well as in several departments/ offices of the State Government in view of the large scale unhygienic conditions prevailing in the premises.
He mentioned the above. Necessary instructions were issued pursuant to the circular dated 19th October, 2018.
Section 11 of the Office Conduct Rules prescribes provisions relating to archiving, classification of records, their destruction and destruction. All the departments/ offices of the Ministry and State Government have been informed from time to time to take action accordingly. However, due to non-compliance of the said provisions, it has been observed that the closed files are lying in large numbers in many departments/ offices and many offices are found to be unhygienic. The headquarters of the state government is a ministry and in such an important building, useless items/ materials, unused records, etc. are lying in places. Therefore, it is necessary to dispose of such material in the prescribed manner and use this space.
Therefore, all the administrative departments of the Ministry as well as all the departments/ offices under their control are again advised to undertake a special drive in the month of July, 2021 for declassification, declassification and updation of records with a view to keep their departments/ offices clean. For this, action should be taken as per the instructions in Annexure A of the Government Resolution dated 15th February, 2018.
