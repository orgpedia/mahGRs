The Proposal: -
The offices of six Regional Zonal Investigating Officers at the level of Revenue Department have been established at Reference No. 1 of this Department to complete inquiries into disciplinary proceedings against State Government officials and submit reports to disciplinary authorities. 1 here. It has been created by the government decision of 28.10.2009. Since no gazetted post was included in the existing cadre of Regional Divisional Vigilance Officers, it was under consideration of the Government to provide one cell officer or equivalent rank of gazetted officer to each of these offices for discharging administrative and other important functions in these offices. The High-Level Committee of Secretaries has decided to create 6 posts of Cell Officers, one each for Regional Divisional Circle Officer Offices. Approval has been given in the meeting held on 14.07.2025. Accordingly, the Government has decided to: -
Government Decision: - A total of 6 posts, one Cell Officer each in the pay scale shown below, are being created at the establishment of Regional Divisional Vigilance Officers Offices in 6 Revenue Divisions namely Konkan, Pune, Nashik, Nagpur, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar and Amravati.
