The Proposal:
The Department of Finance, Government Resolution, dated 10.09.2001, has prescribed a policy for determining the revised figure after reviewing the posts in all the government offices under the State Government. Accordingly, a proposal to prescribe a revised pattern of posts in the office of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
In accordance with the guidelines issued by the Finance Department, Government Order, dated 10.09.2001, 81 regular posts in the cadre of S. No. 1 to 17 as shown in Annexure-' A' in the office of the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal and S.O. A total of 126 posts, including 45 supernumerary posts in the cadre of No. 18 to 22, have been revised.
The decision was taken by the High Level Committee of Secretaries under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary in its meeting held on 03.02.2020. Accordingly, the revised pattern of posts in the office of Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal as indicated in the said Annexure-A is being approved by this Government.
