Preamble- General Administration Department, Government of India. As of August 27, 2009, 2% parallel reservation has been implemented separately in the posts of Group-C and Group-D in the government service for the earthquake victims of the state.
This reservation is not applicable to the posts under the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. The government has received complaints that various institutions in the state are not strictly implementing the 2% reservation of earthquake victims while conducting the selection process for Group-C and Group-D posts.
Apart from earthquake victims, the state has prescribed reservation for project victims at 5%, ex-servicemen at 1 5%, women at 30%, disabled at 3%, sportspersons at 5%, part-time graduates/ diploma holders at 10% and orphans at 1%. All these elements also need to be filled up as per the prescribed reservation.
Government Circular- While filling up the posts of Group-C and Group-D in the State Government Service, all the appointing authorities should strictly implement the 2% reservation for earthquake victims.
Apart from this, parallel reservation has been given to project victims 5%, ex-servicemen 1 5%, women 30%, disabled 3%, sportspersons 5%, part-time graduates/ diploma holders 10%, and orphans 1% for admission to government services. Appointment authorities should calculate the posts according to the prescribed ratio and fill the posts accordingly. The said parallel reservation should be strictly implemented by the appointing authorities.
The circular will be applicable for appointments in government/ semi-government services, government undertakings, corporations, boards, government-aided institutions and all other institutions and services for which the government has the power to issue guidelines. The administrative departments should bring these suggestions to the notice of the heads of departments under them.
