Allotment of space for government offices in the administrative building at Konkan Bhavan.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department,
GOVERNMENT DECISION, NO. SHAKAJA-1119/ PR. 131/2019/22,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: February 01, 2020
The Proposal:
The proposal to allot the vacant space in the Konkan Bhavan building to the office of the Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism, Konkan Bhavan, Belapur, Navi Mumbai and the office of the Sub-Registrar, Co-operative Societies M Department, Mumbai Konkan Bhavan, Navi Mumbai, which had demanded space from the Divisional Commissioner Konkan Division, was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Government have now decided as follows: GOVERNMENT DECISION: The office of the Deputy Director, Directorate of Tourism, Konkan Bhawan, Belapur, Navi Mumbai, will be shifted to the office of the Directorate of Local Fund Audit, 7th Floor (Extension), Konkan Bhawan Building, 1300 sq. 500 square feet. Space is being allocated.
The office of the Co-operative Society M Department, Mumbai Konkan Bhavan, Navi Mumbai is located at Room No. 726, 308 S.O., 7th Floor (Extension), Konkan Bhavan Building. Space is being allocated.
2. The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division shall hand over the possession of the said land to the concerned and submit a report to the Government.
