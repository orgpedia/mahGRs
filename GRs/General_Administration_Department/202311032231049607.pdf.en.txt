The Proposal:
In the matter of issuing caste certificates of Maratha-Kunbi, Kunbi-Maratha caste to the eligible persons of the Maratha community in the Marathwada region of the state, the concerned persons should conduct a legal and administrative examination of the Nizam-era evidence submitted to the competent authority regarding the Kunbi caste, genealogy, educational evidence, revenue evidence, agreements made during the Nizam period, charters issued by the Nizam-era princely states, national documents, etc. Justice Sandeep Shinde (Retd. The Committee has been constituted as per the relevant Government decision dated 7.9.2023.
2. In pursuance of the decision taken to issue Kunabi caste certificate to the eligible persons of the Maratha community in the entire state of Maharashtra, the Hon. The issue of expanding the scope of Justice Shinde Committee was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: The procedure of the committee regarding the issuance of Kunbi caste certificate to the eligible persons of the Maratha community in the Marathwada region has already been given. This has been decided as per the Government decision dated 07.09.2023. Now, under this government decision, the concerned persons of the Maratha community have to approach the competent authority regarding the issue of Kunabi caste certificate to the eligible persons of the Maratha community in the entire state of Maharashtra, including Marathwada.
