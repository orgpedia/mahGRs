Opening of new heads of expenditure for availing interest free loans under the Scheme of Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment of Central Government for Airport Project works under Maharashtra Airport Development Company under Pradhan Mantri Gatishakti Yojana. (5053 0097)
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Resolution No.-MVV-1122/ P.No. 353/28-A
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg, Mantralaya,
Mumbai- 400032.
Date is December 19, 2022.
Preamble: The Central Government has decided to provide interest free loans to the States for a period of 50 years under the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Investment. The project involves setting up an Integrated Air Cargo Facility at Shirdi International Airport, Shirdi.
Under the above scheme, the project of Maharashtra Airport Development Company under the General Administration Department for Setting up an Integrated Air Cargo Facility at Shirdi International Airport, Shirdi has been sanctioned at a cost of Rs. Rs 55 crore. Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to open a new head of expenditure for expenditure on works to be done from the loans received from the Central Government under the Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment Scheme.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment Scheme of the Central Government, a new head of account is being opened for expenditure on works to be done from the loan received from the Central Government.
