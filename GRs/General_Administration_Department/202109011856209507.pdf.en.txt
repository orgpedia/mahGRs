the order
Shri S.H.Umaranikar, Deputy Secretary, Revenue and Forest Department is being appointed on deputation for a period of two years initially to the post of Controller of Examinations and Deputy Secretary in the office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mr. S.H. Umranikar, Deputy Secretary, shall commence the service of the Deputy Secretary on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall terminate on the date on which he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(a) If their services are not required by the employer, they are returned to the parent department.
The employer will have the right to send. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and (d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. The terms and conditions of the deputation shall be determined in consultation with the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mumbai through the Revenue and Forest Department and General Administration Department, Office 8, after taking into consideration the terms and conditions mentioned in Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) Rules 1981.
All other administrative matters relating to the deputation of Mr. S.H. Umranikar, Deputy Secretary, will be handled by the Revenue and Forest Department.
