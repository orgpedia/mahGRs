Setting up a committee to consider the rights and privileges of the minority communities in the Maharashtra-Karnataka border areas.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Sanitation Number: Maksi- 1012/347/ Q.No. 51/2020/36
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: December 9th, 2020
Read:- 1) Government Resolution No. Maksi- 1012/347/ P. No. 126/2012/36 D. The March 7, 2013
Subject to Government Resolution No. Under Article 1 of the Constitution of India, it is the responsibility of the state to ensure that the social, educational and cultural rights of the minority communities in the 865 villages claimed by the Maharashtra government in the Maharashtra-Karnataka border areas are not violated and the rights of the minority communities are protected and promoted in accordance with the provisions of the Constitution of India. In that sense, a short language
A committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Minority Development Department to review the facilities/ concessions to be provided to the citizens as per the Constitution and propose new concessions as per the need.
Corrigendum: In the Table 11 of the above Government Resolution, the serial number should read" Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department" instead of" Joint Secretary, Department of Minority Development."
