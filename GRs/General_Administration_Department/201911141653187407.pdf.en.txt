The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) is conducting a two-year integrated probationary training programme for Group A posts through Yashoda, Pune and Group B posts through Vanamati, Nagpur. The decision is being implemented as per the government's 1. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts for the candidates selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination-2017 and undergoing training in the Combined Subject Training Programme-5.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is approving the creation of a total of 12 supernumerary posts in the pay scale S-17 (47600- 151100) in the revised pay matrix for the 12 candidates selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final results of the State Service (Main) Examination-2017 and who have joined the training directly for the two years period from 30.09.2019 to 03.10.2021.
