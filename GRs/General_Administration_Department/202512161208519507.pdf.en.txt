GOVERNMENT RESOLUTION :-
In pursuance of the letters No.20011/4/92-AIS(D, dated 28th March, 2000 from the Government of India, Department of Personnel & Training, New Delhi, the State Government is pleased to constitute
following Screening Committee to consider the eligibility of officers for promotion in the various grades
{Selection Grade/Super Time Scale (Secretary)/Principal Secretary} of Indian Administrative Service.
1. Chief Secretary to the Government of Maharashtra - Chairman
2. One Senior-most Additional Chief Secretary working in the grade - Member
3. Additional Chief Secretary/Principal Secretary/Secretary (Personnel) - Member
2. Furthermore, the IAS officer of the Additional Chief Secretary/Principal Secretary Grade belonging
to backward class, who is working in the state government, will be included as an "Invitee Member" in
the Screening Committee to safeguard the interests of backward classes.
