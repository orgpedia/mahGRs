Preamble As per the notification dated 15.06.2018 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, the revised strength of cadre posts in the Indian Administrative Service in the State has been decided. Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix is approved for 8 cadre posts. Also, 8 more Non-Cadre posts (SDR) can be filled as per the provision of Rule 12 (7) of the Personnel (Pay) Rules, 2016. The question of creating 1 more temporary post in the said cadre was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision Under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, 1 post in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix is being sanctioned provisionally from 23.05.2019 to 31.08.2019.
