Government circular:
It has come to the notice of this Department that if the Secretaries of the other administrative departments of the Ministry are to be instructed by the various departments of the Ministry regarding the matters related to their department, such matters are requested to the Office of the General Administration Department (Office of Procedure-18). As per Rule-14 of the Maharashtra Government Rules of Business, the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary of the department is responsible and competent to follow the Government Rules of Business in every matter related to the subject matter of his department. Further, the subjects assigned to the General Administration Department under the First Schedule to the Government Rules do not include any subject for giving instructions to all the ministerial departments. However, in some exceptional and urgent cases, instructions have been issued by this department to all departments. As a result, it is not appropriate for the departments to demand that instructions be issued in every case by the General Administration Department.
In view of the above, it is brought to the notice of all the Ministerial Administrative Divisions that as per the provisions of Rule 14 of the Government Rules, the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary of the concerned department is competent to issue instructions to all the departments in respect of the subject matter related to each department (e.g., the appointment of a new Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Deputy Director/ Head of the Department).
Department of Parliamentary Affairs, Department of Public Accounts/ Department of Public Undertakings, Department of Finance, Department of Senior Citizen Policy, Department of Social Justice, Mission on Tree Plantation, Department of Forests, etc. For this, the structure and procedures of the general administration department should not be referred to the department.
