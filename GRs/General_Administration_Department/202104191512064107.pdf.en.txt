General Administration Department, Government Decisions, D. 29/02/2016 provides that" the first increment shall be given to the candidates under probation after they join the government service and the subsequent increment shall be given after they complete the probation period satisfactorily." Accordingly, the first increment has been sanctioned to the above candidates vide Office Order dated 04/12/2017 and now that they have satisfactorily completed the probation period, their second and subsequent increments are being approved.
The details of the leave taken during the training period of the Deputy Executive Officer/ Block Development Officer, Group-A under the trainee probation in the table in paragraph 2 above and the leave sanction orders have been received from the Administrative Department, Regional Offices and the Training Institute of YASHADA, Pune, based on which these orders are being issued.
