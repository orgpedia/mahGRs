the order
Rohini Jadhav, Under Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department, is being appointed on deputation to the office of Deputy Secretary, State Information Commission, Nagpur Bench, for a period of one year initially subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) The service of the Deputy Secretary shall commence on the date on which the Under Secretary joins the office of the Deputy Secretary and shall end on the date on which he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Department of Industries, Energy and Labour and the General Administration Department should decide the terms and conditions of the deputation mutually, taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mrs. Rohini Jadhav, Under Secretary will be handled by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour.
