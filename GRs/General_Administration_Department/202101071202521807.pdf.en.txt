The Proposal:
The proposal to shift the offices of the Sub-Treasury Office, Konkan Bhavan, Navi Mumbai and the Divisional Assistant Director of Languages, Directorate of Languages, Mumbai, which are currently functioning in the Konkan Bhavan building, was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Government have now decided as follows: GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Sub-Treasury Office, Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai will be shifted to 3rd Floor (Main) of Konkan Bhawan Building, Room No. 315 of 1276 sq. Ground Floor (Main), Room No. 400 in the G-16. A total of 1676 sq. Space is being allocated.
2. The Divisional Assistant Director of Languages, Directorate of Languages, Mumbai is to be posted at the ground floor (Main) of Konkan Bhawan Building, Room No. The rest of the G-16 is 1200 sq. Space is being allocated.
3. The Divisional Commissioner, Konkan Division shall hand over the possession of the said land to the concerned and submit a report to the Government.
