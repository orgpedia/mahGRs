Preamble: For the financial year 2023-24, the Institute of Accounts, Title A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, has been provided with a grant-in-aid of Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 1,23,73, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Three Lakhs Seventy Three Thousand only) has been approved. So far, a total grant of Rs. 42,06,820/- (Rupees Forty-four Lakhs Six Thousand Eight Hundred and Twenty only) has been provided to Vanamati, Nagpur, Training Institute under the 36-Assistant Grant (Salary) Scheme. As per the request of the Vanamati Training Institute at Roll No. 2 here, the matter of disbursement of funds under the target of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) under the head of Accounts A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11) (20700862) for the financial year 2023-24 was under the consideration of the Government, the Government is taking the following decisions.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- On the request of Vanamati Training Institute, at Roll No. 2 above, for the financial year 2023-24, the grant available under the objective of 36-Assistant Grant (Salary) to the Institute of Accounting A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur, under the heading of Auxiliary Grant (20700862) for Combined Undertaking Training Programme (20700862), through Budget Estimates, Distribution, Management and Control System (BEAMS), is Rs. 7,42,380/- (Rupees Seven Lakhs Forty Two Thousand Three Hundred Eight only) is being disbursed to Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Nagpur Training Institute subject to the following terms and conditions.
The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur will be responsible for complying with the provisions of the Working Manual, Financial Manual, Budget Rule Book of the State Government and all the terms and conditions before drawing the funds available on the Budget Distribution System from the treasury and while spending.
(a) The Director, Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI), Nagpur shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the grant disbursed and shall ensure that the amount is drawn from the treasury by depositing in the treasury the payments equivalent to the actual expenditure incurred.
(b) submit to the Government within 15 days the amount collected after depositing in the Treasury the amount equal to the actual expenditure and ensure that the amount collected is recorded under the same head in the records of the Accountant General's Office.
