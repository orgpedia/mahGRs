Government Circular As per the provisions of the Government of Maharashtra Secondary Ministry Service (Lower Level Clerks) Post Entry Training Examination Rules, dated 6th June, 1977, applications are invited in the prescribed format from eligible candidates who are appearing for the first time and who wish to appear again for the post entry training examination to be conducted in the year 2020 for Clerks and Clerical Typists in the Ministry Department.
2. Post-Interview Training Examination for Clerks/ Clerks-Typist in the Ministries/ Departments. Therefore, like last year, the training of post-service training of clerks/ clerks-typists in the ministerial department will be conducted during the basic training to be conducted under the State Training Policy. The training will be conducted at the Regional Institute of Agricultural Extension Management (RAMETI), Pune and Regional Institute of Administrative Training, Pune. The training will be held at the Regional Training Institute, Sangli.
3. The Examination Rules provide for withholding of increment in the salary of the concerned Clerk-Typist in case he fails to pass the post-entry training examination on time and within the stipulated time.
Therefore, all the departments are being informed that instructions should be given to all the concerned clerks and typists to take this training seriously.
4. Application in the prescribed format to be submitted for post-entry training and examination of Clerk/ Clerk-Typist in the Ministry Department is available under the heading" Application Form" on the Ministry Intranet. However, the applications in the prescribed format of Clerks/ Clerk-Typists of the Ministry Department who are eligible to appear for the first time in the examination of 2020 and want to appear again should be sent to this Department by March 20, 2020, after being certified by the Establishment Officer. Establishment officers should ensure that the application form contains correct information such as date of appointment of the candidate, whether the candidate has completed the basic training or how, and the nature of his appointment (direct service/ promotion/ compassionate leave). In the application form, the candidate should mention his/ her full name, date of appointment, name of the department, etc.
