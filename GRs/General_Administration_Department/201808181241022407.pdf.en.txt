Government Circular As per the provisions of the Government of Maharashtra Secondary Ministry Service (Lower Level Clerks) Post Entry Training Examination Rules, dated 6th June, 1977, applications in the prescribed format are invited from eligible candidates for appearing for the first time in the post entry training and examination to be conducted for the year 2018-19 for Clerks and Clerical Typists in the Ministry Department.
(2) As per the provisions of the said Rules, a qualified clerk/ clerk-typist can avail the benefit of training classes only once. Therefore, the clerks/ clerk-typists who have availed training earlier cannot avail the benefit of retraining. The applications of Clerks/ Clerk Typists who have failed in the previous Clerks/ Clerks-Typist Services Post-Entrance Training Examination or have remained absent in the said examination for the year 2018-19, will be called separately after completion of the above mentioned training.
3. The prescribed format of application for the said training and examination is available on the Ministry Intranet under the heading" Application Form." However, the applications in the prescribed format from the Clerks/ Clerk-Typists of the Ministry Department who are eligible to appear for the examination for the year 2018-19 for the first time and who have not yet undergone training for the post-service examination, should be sent to this Department by 7th September, 2018 after being certified by the Establishment Officer. Establishment officers should ensure that the application form contains correct information such as date of appointment of the candidate, whether the candidate has completed the basic training or how, and the nature of his appointment (direct service/ promotion/ compassionate leave). In the application form, the candidate should mention his/ her full name, date of appointment, name of the department, etc.
4. This circular may kindly be brought to the notice of all the concerned Clerks/ Clerk-Typists (including Clerks/ Clerk-Typists on deputation/ leave) in writing immediately.
