Government Circular: The following applications in the prescribed format are being invited from eligible candidates for the training and examination to be conducted in accordance with the examination to be conducted in the year 2021 for promotion to the post of Assistant Cell Officer in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission: As per the provisions of Rule 3 of the Maharashtra Government Secondary Ministerial Services Departmental Examination Rules, 1977, the regular Assistant Cell Officer who is working in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and who is completing five years of continuous service in the post of Assistant Cell Officer by October 31, 2021, and who has passed the post-service training examination, should submit the application in the prescribed format as per Rule-16, Schedule-B.
The eligible Assistant Section Officers as above who have not availed the training of this Departmental Examination earlier are applying for this examination for the first time. To be delivered by January 15, 2022. The prescribed format of applications has been made available under the heading" Application Form" on the Ministry Intranet.
The applications of the Assistant Cell Officers who have failed in the departmental examination held earlier or have remained absent in the examination to be held in the year 2021 should be sent as soon as possible by a separate circular.
