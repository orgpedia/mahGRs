the order
Mr. S. L. Ambilpure, Under Secretary, Planning Department is being appointed on deputation for the post of" General Manager" on establishment of Mantralaya Aahar/ Restaurants under General Administration Department for a period of one year initially subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mr. S.L. Ambilpure, Under-Secretary shall commence the service of the Deputy Secretary on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall cease to hold office on the date on which he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Planning Department and the General Administration Department shall mutually decide the terms and conditions of the deputation of Mr. S.L. Ambilpure taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mr. S.L. Ambilpure, Under Secretary will be handled by the Planning Department.
