the order
Shri Arjun Waghmode, Cell Officer, Home Department is being transferred and posted as Administrative Officer, Project Management Cell, Pune under the Maharashtra State Agribusiness and Rural Transformation (SMART) Project under the Department of Agriculture and Padum, for a period of one year on deputation subject to the following conditions:
(a) Mr. Waghmode shall commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner on the date on which he enters upon his office of Deputy Commissioner, and shall, on the date on which he enters upon his original office, cease to hold the office of Deputy Commissioner after one year.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. The terms and conditions of the deputation shall be decided by the Department of Agriculture& Farmers Welfare (KA-8O) and the Department of Home Affairs (Establishment) in consultation with each other after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mr. Waghmode will be handled by the Department of Agriculture and Padum (K-8O) as well as the Home Department (Establishment).
4. The Home Department should immediately relieve Mr. Waghmode to join the place of deputation.
