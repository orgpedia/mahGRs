The Government shall have the power to recall the Regional Divisional Inquiry Officer, Nagpur for administrative reasons at any time before the expiry of his service period of 3 years.
If the concerned officer wants to go back to the parent department before the completion of the period of 3 years, he will be required to give a written notice of at least 3 months.
