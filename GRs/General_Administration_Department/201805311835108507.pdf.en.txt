Disciplinary action is taken against government employees under the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979. Rule 13 (4) of the above Rules prescribes the following provision in case of death of a Government servant while disciplinary proceedings are in progress:
Disciplinary action ends immediately after the death of a government employee. Therefore, any disciplinary action under these rules cannot be continued after the death of the concerned errant government employee. " 2. In case of death of an employee during the period of disciplinary proceedings under the above provisions, the proceedings against him are required to be terminated forthwith. In cases where the government is the disciplinary authority, proposals to terminate such proceedings are first submitted to the General Administration Department for concurrence.
