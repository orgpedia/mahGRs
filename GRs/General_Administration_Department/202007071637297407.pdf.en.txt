Preamble: As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, general transfers of state government officers and employees are carried out in the month of April and May every year. Considering the expected revenue shortfall in the State's tax and non-tax revenue during the financial year 2020-21 due to the outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic and its impact on the State's economy, the Department of Finance has decided to revise the rates of interest under the aforesaid Regulation No. Various measures have been prescribed by the government here. With regard to the transfers of State Government officers/ employees through this Government Decision, Issue No. 15 It has been prescribed that" There is a need to maintain continuity in the various departmental measures taken in connection with the Corona epidemic. Keeping this in view, no cadre officer/ employee should be transferred during the current financial year. " As per the instructions received from the government in this regard, the matter of giving instructions regarding the transfers of state government officers/ employees in the current financial year was under consideration.
