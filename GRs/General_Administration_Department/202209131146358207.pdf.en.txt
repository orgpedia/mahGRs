the prelude
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has decided to provide two years of training under the Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP) to the candidates who are directly recruited through the State Service Examination. Under this Combined Probationary Training Programme, a Post-Graduate Degree Course in Administrative Affairs in coordination with the University of Mumbai, as per the Government Decision referred to in Annexure 3, read above
Master of Arts in Development Administration (M.A. in Dev. administration has been started. The PG course has been started from the batch of CPTP 4 recommended under the State Services Examination, 2016 and is presently being offered for CPTP 4 to CPTP 8.
2. Read the above: Yashoda, Pune has been approved to implement the Post Graduate Degree course as per the Government Decision mentioned at Annexure 1. There is a need to revamp the PG courses keeping in view the changes taking place in the government policies and administrative procedures from time to time. If this course is affiliated to a Pune-based university for making necessary improvements in this course to make it complementary to administrative work, then it will be convenient for Yashoda, Pune, which is implementing the postgraduate degree course, in terms of coordination. Therefore, the government was considering to conduct this course (from CPTP-9 batch) in collaboration with Gokhale Institute of Political Science and Economics, Pune. Government's decision:
The Post Graduate Degree Course for Probationary Officers undergoing training under the Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP) will be conducted in collaboration with Yashoda, Pune and Gokhale Institute of Political Science and Economics, Pune from the batch of CPTP-9 onwards.
The course will be implemented in accordance with the provisions prescribed under the Government Decision No. 2 and No. 3 above.
Under the Combined Probationary Training Programme, the postgraduate degree courses for CPTP-4 to CPTP-8 batches will be affiliated to the University of Mumbai.
Necessary amendments in this regard will be made in the Government Notification dated 13th April, 2018 issued regarding the Departmental Examination included in the Post Graduate Degree Courses.
