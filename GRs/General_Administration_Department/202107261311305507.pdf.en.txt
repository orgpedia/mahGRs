circular
The Ministry has notified that the Post-Service Training Examination, 2021 for Clerks/ Clerks-Typists in Administrative Department will be held on Wednesday, the 28th July, 2021 at Smt.Nathibai Damodar Thackersey College of Arts and Smt. All departments have been notified by letter. The examination will be conducted as per schedule.
2. The seat numbers, names and divisions etc. of the candidates allowed to appear in the said examination are indicated in Annexure' B'. The details of the subjects in which the candidates who have failed in the previous examination have been exempted are indicated in Annexure' C' and the important instructions for the candidates are mentioned in Annexure' D'.
The Administrative Departments of the Ministry are requested to immediately bring to the notice in writing of all the candidates (including candidates on leave/ deputation) admitted to the said Circular Examination and preserve the records of the same in the archives of their respective departments. The concerned department should ensure that there is no delay in this regard. In case any error/ discrepancy is found in the details indicated in Annexure' B" and' C," the concerned should immediately inform this section before the examination.
