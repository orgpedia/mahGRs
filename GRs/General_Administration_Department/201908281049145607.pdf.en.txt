Government's decision
Complete control, management and administration of the Chief Minister's Charitable Endowment Fund. The Chief Minister is in the chair. The Chief Minister's Charitable Endowment Fund will be used to provide financial assistance to social, educational, cultural, charitable or other public or semi-public institutions or organizations in Maharashtra or outside the state. The financial assistance will be provided as per the Chief Minister's discretion.
