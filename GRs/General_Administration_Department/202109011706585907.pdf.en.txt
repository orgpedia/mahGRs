Instructions have been issued under Government Circular No. 1116/ PR No. 32/14-B, dated 23.05.2016 regarding the post-transfer training to all the officers/ employees of the Ministries/ Departments as per the State Training Policy. Accordingly, the post-transfer training of the transferred employees in the department should be organized by the concerned ministerial departments.
The concerned departments should submit the information about the joining of the officer in their department to the General Administration Department (Office Rule 14-B).
