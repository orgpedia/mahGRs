The Proposal:
After the renovation of the building. It was decided to set up a Television Conference (VC) Cell under the control of the Department of Information Technology for the Chief Minister/ Minister/ Minister of State and senior administrative officials. For this television room, the Ministry has constructed a 7th floor of the main building, Hall No. 486 out of 702 sq. The proposal to allot the foot space to the Information and Technology Department was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has now decided as follows: Government Decision: 486 sq.ft. on the 7th floor of the main building of the Ministry for Television Conference (V.C.) room. ft area of No. 702 seats are being allotted to Directorate of Information Technology (DIT). The National Informatics Centre (NIC) can also use the hall for television conferences, as per requirement.
The Executive Engineer, City (East) Division, Nirman Bhawan, Mumbai shall hand over the possession of the said land to the concerned and submit a report to the Government.
