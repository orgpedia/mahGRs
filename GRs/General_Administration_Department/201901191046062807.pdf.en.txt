Nashik and Government of Maharashtra have entered into a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for the lease of Airport Terminal Building for 30 years at a rent of Rs.1000/- per annum. The MoU was signed on 1/8/2015. As per the MoU, the Government was considering to modify the decision of the Government in respect of fixing the lease rent of Rs.1000/-per annum for 30 years for the terminal building.
Government Decision The Government of Maharashtra has decided to extend the lease period of the Airport Terminal Building for 30 years at a rent of Rs.1000/- per annum between Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, Ozar, G. The MoU was signed on 1/8/2015. As per the MoU, the Government has decided to levy a lease rent of Rs.1000/- per annum for 30 years for the terminal building subject to modification in the relevant Government Decision.
