Read- 1) Government Circular No: SEPR-2018/ No.4/ K. 17, D. September 10, 2018.
The Government Circular has been published in the prospectus along with the result of the post-service training examination held for the post of Assistant Section Officer in the Ministry Department and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission from 25th September to 26th September, 2018 at the Examination Room of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Goods and Services Tax Bhavan, Mazgaon, Mumbai 400 010.
2.The office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission is requested to bring this result to the notice of all the concerned candidates. In respect of the Assistant Cell Officers who have not passed the examination within the prescribed time and opportunity, the Maharashtra Secondary Ministerial Services (Assistant) Service Post-Entrance Training Examination Rules, 1977 have been amended vide Rule No. 5 and Rule No. The relevant departments should take appropriate action in accordance with Article 13.
(3) There is no provision in the Rules relating to the examination for re-checking of the answer sheets of the candidates who have failed in the said examination or have obtained less marks than expected. However, Government Circular General Administration Department, No.Examination-1088/ Q.No. 35/17, No. If such candidates want to recheck the marks obtained in the answer sheets as on 26th May, 1994, they should submit the same to the General Administration Department/ Office 17 through the Administrative Department within one month from the date of the result of the examination.
4.The candidates who have missed the examination even after filling the application form, they will be required to get proper explanation from the concerned administrative departments. The result of this examination should be recorded in the service book of the concerned candidates without fail by the administrative department.
