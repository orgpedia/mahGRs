No. 34/20/ APUK, d. 23.06.2021 Preamble: 9 temporary posts created for the work of the Statutory Development Board on the establishment of the Office of the Secretary to the Governor will be filled by the General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 03.05.2021 from 01.05.2021. This has been extended till 31.07.2021.
As per the Cabinet Decision dated 27.11.2014, the deadline given to the Vidarbha, Marathwada and the rest of the Maharashtra Development Boards in the state has been extended. It ended on April 30, 2020.
The posts have been extended several times due to administrative reasons. These posts are meant for drawing the attention of the Central Government regarding the availability of funds for various development works in the area, pending matters related to departmental imbalances, matters related to the Tribal Development Cell, as well as appeals/ review applications under the Maharashtra Civil Services Rules. The Raj Bhavan Secretariat has informed that the Governor is being assisted. Therefore, it is proposed to make these temporary posts permanent in order to carry out all the responsibilities including the work of the statutory development board, service appeal application, tribal cell work in the future. It was submitted to a high-level committee headed by the Chief Secretary. The High-Level Committee of Secretaries has decided that the 9 posts created for the work of the Statutory Development Board on the establishment of the Office of the Secretary to the Governor should be continued on the establishment of Raj Bhavan on a temporary basis. The meeting was held on June 14, 2021.
Department of Finance In accordance with the Government decision dated 15.02.2021, temporary posts will be given. The administrative department is empowered to grant extension till 31.08.2021.
