According to the order, 24 temporary posts of various cadres for the office of the Chief Electoral Officer are being extended from time to time. The Cash Branch is facing difficulty in drawing salary from the services of these 2 Clerk-Typists as 2 Clerk-Typists have been appointed to the sanctioned posts of the said category under the Office Order No. 4, dated 20th December 2018 in respect of Establishment Branch against the posts of 2 Typists (Marathi/ English) in A.No. 11. As a result, 2 temporary" Typists" in the office of the Chief Electoral Officer (Office- 33 Election Branch) were under consideration to be converted into" Clerk- Typist" posts.
Government Decision: The posts of Typist (Marathi/ English) and Clerk- Typist in the Office of the Chief Electoral Officer (Office- 33 Election Branch) under the General Administration Department, both in Group-C cadre, will have a pay scale of PB-1, Rs 20200-5200 plus grade pay of Rs 1900 plus duties and responsibilities. This has led to the creation of Finance Department vide No. Complex-2015/ PR No. 41/ Finance-1 dated 2nd June, 2015 and General Administration Department.
