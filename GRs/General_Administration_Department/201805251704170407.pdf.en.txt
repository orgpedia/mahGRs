the order
Shri M.P. Deshpande, Under Secretary, Home Department (presently Deputy Secretary, State Information Commission, Nagpur Bench) is being appointed on deputation for the post of Registrar and Assistant Professor in the office of Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute, Dharampeth, Nagpur for a period of one year initially subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Mr. M.P. Deshpande, Under-Secretary shall commence the service of the Deputy Secretary on the date on which he enters upon the office of Deputy Secretary and shall terminate on the date on which he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority; and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The Home Department and the General Administration Department shall decide the terms and conditions of the deputation mutually, taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981.
3. All other administrative matters of Shri M.P. Deshpande, Under-Secretary shall be dealt with by the Home Department.
