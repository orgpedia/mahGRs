The candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for the post of Sub-Collector, (Group-A) under the State Service (Main) Examination-2015 were appointed as probationary officers under the office order dated 04/08/2016. The Combined Sub-Inspector Training Programme (CPTP 3 (2016)) was conducted from 02/05/2016 to 30/04/2018. Of these, Mr. Chormare, Sub-Inspector has satisfactorily completed the probation period and training.
In accordance with the General Administration Department, Government Decision No. 1 (3) (b) dated February 29, 2016, the probation period of the candidates who are enjoying any form of leave other than temporary leave during the probation period is extended to the same period as the leave period. Accordingly, Mr. Chormare's column No. The period of probation of the post of Deputy Collector, Group-A is being increased to the period of leave mentioned in column No. 5. The expiry date is 7.
