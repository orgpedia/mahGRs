All the vacancies in the promotion quota as per the provisions of the Act are purely temporary in nature as on 25.05.2004. The decision is being taken based on a High Court order.
3. Provisional promotion to the post of Upper Grade Short Writer of the said Lower Grade Short Writer is being made subject to the following conditions.
1) The promotion shall be ad-hoc in the nature of temporary promotion as an administrative exigency.
2) These promotions are being given only on temporary/ ad-hoc basis considering the fact that at present there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Lower Grade Short Writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
3) The promotion is temporary in nature and there will be no permanent retention of the concerned in this post.
4) The selection of eligible lower grade short writers for the selection year 2022-2023 is provisional and promotions to the post of upper grade short writers is provisional. As a result, the lower category short-writers in the selection list will not get any rights to the regular post of upper category short-writer and seniority in service.
In case of non-acceptance of promotion to the post of Upper Grade Short Writer by the concerned Lower Grade Short Writer, his/ her name will be deleted from the selection list for the year 2022-2023 as per General Administration Department, Government Decision, No. SRV-2015/ Q.S. 303/ Q. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and his/ her eligibility will be checked for promotion to the post of Upper Grade Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his/ her name in the subsequent two years selection lists.
(6) The temporary promotion of the said lower grade short writer to the post of upper grade short writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
7) The promotion. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 is being made subject to adjudication.
8) The lower grade short writers who have been given only temporary promotion to the post of upper grade short writers are required to join the post of upper grade short writers within 30 days from the date of issue of this order.
