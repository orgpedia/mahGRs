the General Administration Department

Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
The date: March 27, 2018
Introduction: There is a constant discussion about making elections in the state and the country more transparent. Accordingly, the question of forming a committee to study how to prevent the misuse of resources in various elections in the state, as well as to advise on whether elections in the state can also be held together, in line with the ongoing discussions on holding Lok Sabha and Assembly elections together, was under the consideration of the government.
The Government is directing that in order to make the elections in the State more transparent, the following committees are being constituted to study the issue of preventing misuse of resources in various elections and to advise the State Government on whether or not to hold elections to the Lok Sabha, Rajya Sabha and local bodies together.
Sudhir Mungantiwar, Minister of State (Finance and Planning)
2 Shri D.K. Sankaran, Member Former Chief Secretary
The committee will consider the following issues:
1. To study and advise on the prevention of misuse of resources in elections.
2.To study and advise on how elections to Lok Sabha, Vidhan Sabha and local bodies can also be held together.
