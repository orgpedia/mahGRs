Clarification on processing of applications for personal injury.
The Government of Maharashtra
the General Administration Department
Government Circular No: SRIKKEE- 2019/ PR No. 71/18 (R.& K. )
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date is October 14, 2019.
Circular:
It has been observed that some third parties are involved in correspondence and follow up of personal details of other persons. Many departments ask this department to take cognizance of such letters or how. The government was contemplating on the steps to be taken in this regard.
The rules prescribed under the Notification dated 3rd December, 1958 regarding preparation, submission and disposal of applications to the Government, in accordance with the Rules at Annexure 1, provide that applications for personal injury shall be accepted from the person against whom the wrong has been done. It has been provided that applications made to the government by or through agents should generally not be entertained.
(3) All Ministries/ Departments/ Regional Offices are hereby informed that any correspondence or follow-up by a third person/ institution with respect to the personal affairs of other persons shall be dealt with in accordance with the said Rules.
