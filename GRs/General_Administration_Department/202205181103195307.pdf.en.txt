Government Circular: A comprehensive policy on preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in the Government service every year. 1 has been prescribed by the government here. Accordingly, the Assistant Director (Information)/ Information Officer/ Superintendant, Books and Publications, Group-B, of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. Provisional Seniority of 3 years from 1.1.2018 to 1.1.2020 Reference No. 2 was published here. According to the objections, objections and errors received by the government on this seniority list, Ravindra Devram Thakur, Assistant Director (Information), has been appointed as the new Director. 1 It has been noted that the probation period has ended and Mr. Gajanan Koturwar, Director (Information) (Admn. 23 in the Service Merit List of 1.1.2020 is marked as" EMAV ELIGIBILITY." The Seniority of Assistant Director (Information)/ Information Officer/ Superintendents, Books and Publications, Group-B Officers of the cadre from 1.1.2018 to 1.1.2020 is now being finalized/ interimized and is annexed to this circular.
The above Seniority List is being published subject to the final decision of the Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed by the State Government in the Supreme Court, New Delhi. The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations shall inform all the offices under its command and all the officers of the cadre of Director (Information) Group-A (Senior) about the availability of copies of this seniority list from the government website.
