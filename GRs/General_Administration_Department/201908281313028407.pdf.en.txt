2. m. The High Court of Bombay, in Writ Petition No. 2797/2015. As per the decision dated 4.8.2017, reservation in promotion has been declared invalid. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed. The petition is still pending. The Department of Law and Justice has said that there is no legal problem in giving promotion to open category employees. dated 28.12.2017. Accordingly, at present, the vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota are only temporary. It has to be done as per the Supreme Court's verdict. While taking such action, the officers/ employees belonging to the Backward Classes in the Service Excellence List are given a promotion. Every administrative department shall ensure that the benefits of reservation are not extended to the higher ranks in the State merit list as per the provisions of the Government Order dated 25.05.2004. dated 29.12.2017. According to A Special Leave Petition No. Subject to the Judgment No. 28306/2017 and subject to the following conditions, the concerned lower grade Short Writer is being given ad-hoc promotion in the open category vacancy in the upper grade Short Writer cadre on purely temporary basis:

1) The promotion shall be ad-hoc in the nature of temporary promotion as an administrative exigency.
2) These promotions are being given only on temporary/ ad-hoc basis considering the fact that at present there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Lower Grade Short Writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
(3) The said ad-hoc promotion is temporary in nature and the concerned shall not hold the post permanently.
4) The selection of eligible lower grade short writers for the year 2018-2019 is ad-hoc in nature and promotions to the post of upper grade short writers are ad-hoc in nature.
As a result, the lower category short-writers in the selection list will not get any rights of the regular post of upper category short-writer and seniority in the service.
In case of non-acceptance of ad-hoc promotion to the post of Upper Grade Short Writer by the concerned Lower Grade Short Writer, his/ her name will be deleted from the selection list for the year 2018-2019 as per General Administration Department, Government Resolution, No. SRV-2015/ Q.S. 303/ Q. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and his/ her eligibility will be checked for ad-hoc promotion to the post of Upper Grade Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his/ her name in the subsequent two years selection lists.
(6) The ad-hoc promotion of the said lower grade short writer to the post of upper grade short writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
7) The promotion of lower category short writers in the backward class is being done subject to the condition that they have not been elevated in the service merit list with the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2004 and the caste validity certificate of the lower category short writers has been issued by the concerned caste verification committee. The concerned department should look into it.
8) These orders shall come into force with immediate effect. Copies of the orders issued by the concerned departments should be sent to this department.
