Sir/ Madam, the State Govt. As per the Government decision dated 12.02.2019, 10% seats have been reserved for the economically weaker sections in the open category for admission to Government services and educational institutions. This reservation is for direct entry into the central and state government civil services and posts and for admission to educational institutions. The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has invited online applications from eligible candidates for filling up the post of Assistant Professor in Civil Services Examination, 2017-2018. Therefore, it is demanded that all the eligible candidates from the economically weaker sections of Maharashtra who are giving this interview should be examined in the form prescribed by the Central Government regarding the income and income of these candidates for the year 2017-2018 and accordingly the process of giving the EWS certificate offline should be done immediately so that all the eligible candidates can take advantage of the said reservation.
