Preamble: The Government have decided to conduct a combined probationary training programme for the officers of various cadres of Group-A and Group-B to be appointed in direct service in the Government service as per the Government Decision General Administration Department, dated 20/01/2014. Accordingly, for the financial year 2023-24, the Government was contemplating to disburse a total amount of Rs. 24,74,600/- (Rupees Twenty Four Lakh Seventy Four Thousand Six Hundred Only) to the Institute of Accounts Head A-4, 2070 Other Administrative Services, (11), Vanamati, Nagpur under the head' Subsidiary Grants for Training Programme under Integrated Schedules (20700862)' for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the Institute for the period January, 2023 to February, 2023 and for the Quarterly Salaries and Dearness Allowance from March, 2023 to May, 2023. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2023-24, for the purpose of 36 Assistant Grants (Salaries) under the head Assistant Grants (20700862) to the Institute of Accountants A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (11), Vanamati, Nagpur for training programme under Integrated Scrutiny, an amount of Rs. 1,23,73, 000/- and 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. A total of Rs 6,79,21, 000. A budget provision of Rs.8,02,94,000/- (Rupees eight crore two lakh four thousand nine thousand only) has been approved.
On the request of Vanamati Training Institute, at Sl. No.2 above, for the financial year 2023-24, a sum of Rs. 24,74,600/- (Rupees Twenty Four Lakh Seventy Four Thousand Six Hundred Only) has been provided as Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control to the Institute of Accounts A-4, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (11), Vanamati, Nagpur under the head Assistant Grants for Training Programme under Consolidated Schedules (20700862) for the purpose of 36-Assistant Grants (Salaries) for the period January, 2023 to February, 2023 for the pending salary and for the quarter March, 2023 to May, 2023 for the salary and Dearness Allowance.
