Date: 26th of December 2018
Circular:
On Tuesday, July 3, 2018, the foot overbridge between Andheri railway station collapsed, disrupting rail traffic on the western line. As majority of the State Government officers/ employees could not attend office, the matter of grant of special casual leave to the officers/ employees of Western Railway on Tuesday, the 3rd July, 2018 was under consideration.
In this regard, the Government has decided that the State Government officers/ employees residing on Western Railway who could not attend their offices on Tuesday, July 3, 2018 due to the above mentioned reasons, should be granted special casual leave for that day.
