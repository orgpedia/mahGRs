The Proposal:
The proposal to allot space to the office of the Joint Director, Industrial Safety and Health, Raigad, and the office of the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Rural Livelihood Mission, State Mission Management Cell, Belapur, in the Konkan Bhavan building in view of the vacancies at present, as well as to other government offices that have demanded space in the office of the Divisional Commissioner Konkan Division, was under consideration of the government.
