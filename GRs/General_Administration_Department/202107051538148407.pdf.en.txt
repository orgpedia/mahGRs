The Proposal:
The Government decision dated 26.2.2021 at Reference No.7 is hereby cancelled.
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in government service for the disabled. The central government has given. The list of posts for Persons with Disabilities as notified vide Notification No. 04.01.2021 has been published under the heading List of Posts identification suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the website of the Central Government at www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. The Social Justice and Special Assistance Department of the state government has issued a notification in this regard. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, New Delhi. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions that the posts in Group A to Group D, which have been ensured by the Central Government for the disabled, are in the state government establishment, and the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, even if their designations are different, will be ensured for the disabled in the state government service. of 02.02.2021

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: RAJB-2021/ PR No. 41/20-A
The government has taken the decision. In accordance with the instructions in the notification and the government decision, the government was considering ensuring posts for the disabled category in the Group-A to Group-D category in the office of the Secretary to the Governor and the Family Manager in the Raj Bhavan Secretariat under the General Administration Department.
Government Decision: Considering the posts ensured for Divyangjan in the notification dated 04.01.2021, it is approved to ensure the posts in the Group-A to Group-D cadre in the Office of the Secretary to the Governor and the Office of the Family Manager in the Raj Bhavan Secretariat under the General Administration Department for Divyangjan as shown in the return A and B respectively.
The said Government Decision was issued by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi. Notification dated 04.01.2021 and issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. being issued as per the instructions issued vide Government Decision dated 02.02.2021.
