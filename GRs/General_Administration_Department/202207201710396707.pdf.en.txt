Intro: Demand No: A-5, Head of Accounts 2235, Pension to Freedom Fighters and their Dependents, etc. (Mandatory), 04-Pensionary Expenditure (22350015) below Rs. 79,80,44, 000/- (Rupees Ninety-nine crore eighty lakh forty four thousand only). So far, 50% of the sanctioned budget of Rs. 39,90,22, 000/- (Rupees Nineteen Crore Ninety Lakh Twenty Two Thousand only) has been received. Out of this budgetary provision, a proposal is under consideration of the Government to transfer the required amount of funds to the concerned District Collector for the month of July to September, 2022.
Government Decision: For the financial year 2022-23, in the head of head account 2235, pension to freedom fighters and their dependents, etc. (mandatory), (2235 0015), the following Rs. 79,80,44, 000/- (Rupees Ninety-nine crore eighty lakh forty four thousand only). Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 18,27,00, 000/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores Twenty Seven Lakhs only) is being disbursed to the respective District Collectors to meet the expenditure for the month of May (July to September, 2022) as shown against the names of their districts in the Statement A attached with the Government Decision.
The amount of subsidy spent from the fund distributed to the District Collector should be reconciled with the Accountant General-1, Mumbai/ Accountant General-2, Nagpur within the prescribed time and submit the monthly report of the expenditure meeting to the government regularly. In this regard, action should be taken as per Government Circular No. Expenditure-Mel 2011/ PR No. 139/ 2011/ SWASAK-1, dated 16/08/2011.
