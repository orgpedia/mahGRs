The Proposal:
As per General Administration Department, Government Decision, dated 20/01/2014, a combined probationary training programme is being conducted for Group-A and Group-B cadre officers who are directly recruited in the government service and the funds to be provided for training per trainee per day have been fixed under General Administration Department, Government Decision, dated 08/08/2014. Accordingly, for the training of the batch of Integrated Peripheral Training Programme-7, the funds required for the foundation training of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHADA) in the budget year 2021-22 is Rs. 2,06,08, 000/- (Rupees Two Crore Six Lakh Eight Thousand only) is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
The training of the candidates recommended under the State Service Examination-2019 has been started from 17/01/2022 and they were engaged in the respective administrative departments at the district affiliation stage. After completion of this four-week phase, the trainees have now been engaged at YASHADA, Pune for basic training from 14/02/2022. Accordingly, for 46 days from 14/02/2022 to 31/03/2022, Rs. 3500/- per day/ per trainee (46 days* 128 trainees Rs. 3500/- (Total Rs. 2,06,08, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Six Lakh Eight Thousand only) as reimbursement by Yashada, Pune vide Reference No. 3 has been requested.

Accordingly, under the said Government Decision, the Government is approving the disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 2,06,08, 000/- (Rupees Two Crore Six Lakh Eight Thousand only) to Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHADA), Pune for the financial year 2021-2022 under the head" Accounting-A-4, 2070 Other Administrative Services 00-003 Training (00) (12) Subsidiary Grants (20700871) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary) to YASHADA, Pune for Combined Undergraduate Training Programme," subject to the following terms and conditions.
I. The Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradarshini Prabodhini (Yashada), Pune, shall keep the expenditure within the limits of the grants distributed and shall take care to draw from the treasury payments equal to the actual expenditure incurred.
After depositing the amount in the treasury equal to the actual expenditure, it should be ensured that the amount withdrawn is recorded under the same head in the accountant general's office and the certificate should be submitted to the government within 15 days.
III. The Controller and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be solely responsible for meeting the expenditure incurred during the financial year 2021-22.
The UTILIZATION CERTIFICATE of the grant distributed by Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHADA), Pune should be submitted to the Accountant General, Maharashtra 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur and to the Government.
V. Separate accounts and books of expenditure made out of the above grant by Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (Yashada), Pune should be maintained. The powers to examine the books and accounts shall be vested in the Accountant General, Maharashtra 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur and Yashoda, Pune shall provide full facilities to him.
The fund should be spent in compliance with the terms/ conditions mentioned in the Finance Department, Government Circular, dated 06/04/2021 and Government Decision, dated 24/06/2021, dated 29/07/2021, dated 30/07/2021, dated 14/10/2021, dated 28/01/2022.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHADA), Pune shall draw the amount of grant-in-aid being disbursed to the institution as Rs. 2,06,08, 000/- (Rupees Two Crore Six Lakh Eight Thousand only).
