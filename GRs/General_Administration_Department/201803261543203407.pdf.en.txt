Preamble: 5 temporary posts in the office of the Military Pre-Service Education Institute, Aurangabad have been created vide Government Resolution No. SPI-2018/ Q. No. 40/18/ Q. 28, dated 29/01/2018 was approved to be continued from 01/01/2018 to 28/02/2018. Now, under the Government decision dated 05/03/2018 of the Department of Finance, the Administrative Departments have been permitted to continue the temporary posts from 01/03/2018 to 30/09/2018.
As per the decision taken by the Director, Institute of Military Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad vide their letter dated 05/03/2018, it is proposed that a total of 5 temporary posts on the establishment of the Institute of Military Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad are not vacant for more than six months and it is necessary to continue those posts, the Government is approving the continuation of the following total 5 temporary posts on the establishment of the Institute of Military Pre-Service Education, Aurangabad for a period of 7 months from 01/03/2018 to 30/09/2018.
