Preamble: Emergency was declared in India from 25.6.1975 to 31.3.1977. A policy has been formulated to honour/ suitably honour the persons who have undergone imprisonment during this period by providing financial assistance to them.
The matter of sanctioning honorarium to eligible persons under this policy is under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- Disbursement of funds required for implementing the policy of honouring/ due respect to persons who had to undergo imprisonment for fighting for democracy during the Emergency period from 1975 to 1977.
As indicated in the attached prospectus, Demand No. A-05 Principal Accounts head 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 102- Pension under Social Security, (00) (05) Financial assistance to persons fighting for democracy during the period of emergency (mandatory) 50 Other expenses (2235 C205) The provision of this scheme has been approved by the Supplementary Demand. This amount is Rs. 42,00,0, 000/- (Rupees Forty Two Crores only) and out of this amount, it is required to be distributed to Nagpur district of the State. The Nagpur district collector has submitted a report to the government.
An amount of Rs. 1,31,40, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty One Lakh Forty Thousand only) is being disbursed as per the list of eligible applicants.
