Government circular
The departmental examination for promotion to the post of Cell Officer has been conducted for only those Assistant Cell Officers who have failed earlier in the Administrative Department of the Ministry and the Office of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. from June 26, 2021. It will be held on June 28, 2021.
The Assistant Cell Officers who have previously appeared in the Departmental Examination and have failed in the said examination or have been absent in the examination, if such candidates are to appear in the Cell Officer Departmental Examination 2021, then only their applications in the prescribed format (mentioning the full name of the candidate in the application) should be sent to the Administrative Department by the Establishment Officers of the Administrative Departments after verifying that all the details in the application form are correct. Applications received after this time will not be considered. The prescribed format of application is available on the Ministry Intranet under the heading" Application Form."
All the administrative departments of the Ministry may kindly bring this circular in writing to the notice of the concerned Assistant Section Officer (including the Assistant Section Officer on deputation/ leave) immediately and take their signatures and preserve the documents in their records.
Please note that if the application of a qualified candidate is not received by the department within the prescribed time, the entire responsibility of the candidate and the department concerned will be taken care of.
