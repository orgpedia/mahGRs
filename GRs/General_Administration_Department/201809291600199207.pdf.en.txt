This basic training includes the syllabus of the post-entry training examination of the Government of Maharashtra Secondary Ministerial Services (Lower Level Clerks) Service. The decision of the Government regarding this course is annexed as Annexure-I. The decision of the government should be brought to the notice of the concerned clerk-typist who attends the basic training and fills the application form for the post-entry training examination of the Secondary Ministerial Service (Lower Level Clerk) Service of the Government of Maharashtra. Thereafter, the concerned Clerks-Typists will be informed through the General Administration Department, Office 17, Mantralaya, Mumbai, regarding the conduct of their Secondary Ministerial Service (Lower Level Clerks) Service Post-Entrance Training Examination. Instructions in this regard should be given to the clerks and typists by the concerned ministry department.
Please refer to the list of Clerks/ Typists of your Department selected for the training programme along with Form-1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6.
4. Attendance is mandatory as the training is being imparted as per the State Training Policy. The government circular said. The suggestions given as on 13th May, 2015 should be brought to the notice of the concerned. Since the basic training will be conducted during this period, it is necessary that the training of all the concerned clerical typists should be completed within this period. Applications received for exemption from training/ cancellation of training will not be entertained and the applications received in this regard will not be entertained by the Ministries/ Departments.
If in exceptional circumstances it is not possible to attend/ send the nominated clerk-typist to the training, then the process of transferring their training batch and releasing them for the basic training should be done through the respective department and their copy should be sent to the training institute and this department.
6. Instructions to be issued to the concerned office from the level of the department to release the work for training in respect of the clerks and typists who are working on deputation in the department.

7. The concerned department should immediately inform the trainees about the duration of the training keeping in view the need for reservation for travel. Trainers should take care to carry necessary clothing, track suits and sports shoes, considering the training locations and weather. The trainees who will be present in the training institute before the training day (1 day) should contact the concerned and give prior idea to the training institute as to whether they will be present for the lunch or how. On the last day of the training period, the subjects taught during the training will be evaluated by conducting a written examination, the instructions regarding this should be mentioned in the order to relieve the trainees.
(8) The trainees shall be discharged on the date of release of work for training as indicated in the table in paragraph 1. It is mandatory for the nominated person to be relieved by the concerned department head.
