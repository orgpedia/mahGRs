The Government's Decisions:
The government's policy proposes to provide space to various offices of the Maharashtra government on rent and no government office should be on rent. Accordingly, in addition to the offices of the Goods and Services Tax Department in the Goods and Services Tax Bhawan, Wadala, Mumbai building, it is proposed to provide space for various offices of the Government of Maharashtra in the remaining space. Accordingly, the following Committee is being constituted for allotment of space in the Government Building at GST Bhawan, Wadala: - 1) Secretary and V.O.A.
