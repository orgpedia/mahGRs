Circular:
Circular dated 31st December, 2021 has been issued regarding Celebration of Birth Anniversary and National Day of Rashtra Purush/ Utkrisht in 2022.
The circular dated December 31, 2021, did not include the birth anniversary of Balashastri Jambhekar. According to this government circular, the birth anniversary of Balashastri Jambhekar is mentioned as follows.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| The National Day or Birthday  | English Month and Day. | war                            | The nature of the program              |
| Birth anniversary of Jambekar | February 20, 2022      | Sunday (according to the date) | Offering floral tributes to the statue |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Divisional Commissioner/ Collector shall issue necessary instructions regarding the celebration of the birth anniversary of Balashastri Jambhekar on February 20, 2022, in all government/ semi-government offices in his department/ district and take appropriate action regarding its implementation.
