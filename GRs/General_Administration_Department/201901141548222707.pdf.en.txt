No. 685/2016/ X, D. 1.11.2017 Preamble As per the notification dated 15.06.2018 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, the revised strength of cadre posts in the Indian Administrative Service in the State has been fixed.
Earlier, as per the notification dated 16.02.2015 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, 7 cadre posts were sanctioned in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix. Also, 7 more Non-Cadre posts (SDR) can be filled as per the provision of Rule 12 (7) of the Personnel (Pay) Rules, 2016. The question of extending the tenure of two posts created temporarily in the said cadre by the government was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION It is hereby given ex-post facto approval for the extension of the period from 31.10.2018 to 31.12.2018 of the two posts created temporarily by the Government under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954 in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix.
