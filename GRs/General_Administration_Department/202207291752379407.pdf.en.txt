GOVERNMENT DECISION:" This Government has approved the re-launch of the scheme for honouring/ suitably honouring those who fought during the period of Emergency from 1975 to 1977, which was discontinued on 31st July, 2020.
The scheme will be implemented from August 1, 2022.
Accordingly, the persons who were earlier sanctioned the honorarium will be paid from August, 2022. Also, the beneficiaries who have paid honorarium under the scheme implemented under the Government decision dated July 3, 2018, will be eligible for the benefit of the scheme.
The arrears from the period will be admissible. From 31.07.2020 to till date, the number of honorarium holders, who have to pay the arrears, should be provided by the Collector in the statement-" A" attached with this Government decision.
Persons who have not applied earlier should submit their applications to the Collector's Office along with the affidavit in the Annexure prescribed under the Government Decision dated July 3, 2018. The last date for submission of fresh applications to the Collectorates for availing the benefit of this scheme will be 31/10/2022. Therefore, the district administration should point out that applicants should take care of submitting new applications by this date. The concerned District Collector will have the authority to sanction honorarium under these schemes. The list of persons who have been sanctioned new honorarium from the concerned Collector should be submitted to the government. According to this list, the government will provide funds to the concerned collector's office.
Under this scheme, persons who have undergone imprisonment for more than one month during the emergency period will be given a monthly pension of Rs. 10,000/- to his wife and Rs. Rs. 5,000/-. Whereas, for those who have undergone imprisonment for less than one month, a monthly penalty of Rs. 5,000/- to his wife and Rs. Rs 2,500/- will be admissible.
The beneficiary/ spouse will continue to be eligible for the remuneration under this policy as long as he/ she is alive. After that, the beneficiary will not be eligible for the benefit.
