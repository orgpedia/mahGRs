(3) The said ad-hoc promotion is temporary in nature and the concerned shall not hold the post permanently.
4) The selection of eligible upper category shortlists for the year 2020-21 is ad-hoc in nature and promotions to the post of category shortlists are provisional in nature. As a result, the selected upper category short-writers will not get any rights of regular post and seniority of service.
In case of non-acceptance of ad-hoc promotion to the post of Selection Grade Short Writer by the concerned Senior Grade Short Writer, his/ her name will be deleted from the selection list for the year 2019-2020 as per General Administration Department, Government Resolution, No. SRV-2015/ Q.S. 303/ Q. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and his/ her eligibility will be checked for ad-hoc promotion to the post of Selection Grade Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his/ her name in the subsequent two years selection lists.
(6) The ad-hoc promotion of the said Senior Short Writer to the post of Senior Short Writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental enquiry has been initiated or proposed to be initiated against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
7) The promotion of upper class book writers of backward classes is being done subject to the condition that they have not been elevated in the service merit list with the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2004 and the caste validity certificate of the upper class book writers has been issued by the concerned caste verification committee. The concerned department should look into it.
