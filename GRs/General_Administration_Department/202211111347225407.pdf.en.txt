Government Resolution, General Administration Department, No. 1922/ No. 61/ SWASAC-1, D. 04.05.2022
Preamble: A" Cabinet Sub-Committee" was constituted under the Government Resolution, dated 4 th May, 2022 to plan and chalk out programmes to celebrate the Amrit Mahotsav year of Marathwada Liberation Struggle. With the formation of the new cabinet in the state, the matter of reconstituting this committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the formation of" Cabinet Sub-Committee" as follows to plan and chalk out the programme for celebrating Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Liberation Struggle from 17 th September, 2022 to 17 th September, 2023.
The composition of the committee will be as follows:

Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad is being appointed as Member Secretary of the Cabinet Sub-Committee.
The mandate of the Cabinet Sub-Committee is as follows: To work out the modalities for celebrating the Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Liberation Day in each district of the Marathwada region.
Setting guidelines for organizing events in the memory of martyrs who sacrificed their lives to free Marathwada from the Nizam's rule.
Planning for organizing government programs, blood donation camps etc. on the occasion of Amrit Mahotsav in all government/ semi-government offices and important places in Marathwada region and to involve the people of Marathwada region in various programs to be organized on the occasion of Amrit Mahotsav.
