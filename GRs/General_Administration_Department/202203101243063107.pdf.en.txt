the Government Order
Mrs. Lily Denis Machado, Senior Grade Short Writer, Planning Department is being appointed as Senior Grade Short Writer on deputation in the office of the Commissioner, Vasai-Virar City Municipal Corporation for a period of 2 years subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) Mrs. Lily Denis Machado, Senior Short Writer, Planning Department, will commence her deputation service on the date on which she assumes the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall conclude her service on the date on which she resumes her official charge.
(b) If their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government/ competent authority shall be empowered to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, the non-recipient employer should give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before sending back the same
d) Ms. Machado will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least 3 months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that she intends to return to the parent department.
Mrs. L.D. Machado, Senior Short Writer, Planning Department, being appointed on deputation to Maharashtra Civil Service (Period of Incorporation, Non-Residential Service. (e) Considering the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Rules, 1981, the Planning Department and the Urban Development Department shall decide the terms and conditions of their deputation among themselves.
3. All other administrative matters of Mrs. L.D. Machado, Senior Short Writer, Planning Department will be handled by the Planning Department. The planning department should relieve Mrs. L.D. Machado, a high-ranking bookkeeper, to join the post of a deputy.
