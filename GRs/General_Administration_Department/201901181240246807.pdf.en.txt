Under the decision of the Government in the context, a policy has been decided to honour the persons who had to suffer imprisonment for fighting for democracy during the period of Emergency from 1975 to 1977. For this purpose, the Government in terms of Government Resolution No.Finance-2018/ P.No. 115/2018/ SWASAC-1, D. The new head of account has been opened vide Government Resolution dated 13/11/2018. Accordingly, the Deputy Secretary, Swatantrata Sainik Cell, General Administration Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for this title.
