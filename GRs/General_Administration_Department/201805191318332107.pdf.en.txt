GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: TRN-0418/ PR No.21/ CPTP-A
(a) The Director, YASHADA, Pune shall keep the expenditure up to the month of June, 2018 within the limits of the subsidy disbursed and shall ensure that the same amount is drawn from the treasury as the actual expenditure incurred.
b) Yashda, Pune vide circular dated 02/04/2018 of the Finance Department in Annexure Issue No. 8 (10) states that the funds are to be spent without depositing the money in a private account or bank account. Subject to these conditions, the said fund is being disbursed under the target of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries).
e) The Controller and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall be solely responsible for meeting the expenditure incurred during the financial year 2018-19.
e) Utilization Certificate of the grant being distributed by YASHADA, Pune should be submitted to the Accountant General, Audit, Maharashtra 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur and the Government.
3. Separate accounts and books of expenditure made from the above grant by Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (Yashada), Pune shall be maintained and the powers to examine the said accounts and books shall be vested in the Accountant General, Maharashtra 1/2, Mumbai/ Nagpur.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHADA), Pune has sanctioned a grant of Rs. 2,70,000/- (Rupees Two Lakh Seventy Thousand only).
