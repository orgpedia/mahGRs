The Combined Probationary Training Programme (CPTP) for the candidates selected under Circular State Service Examination-2017 and State Service Examination-2018 has been started from 4.10.2019 in two batches i.e. CPTP-5 and CPTP-6 at the Training Institutes at Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur respectively. Guidelines in this regard have been issued through the government circular at the above mentioned serial number. In paragraph (2) of these guidelines, instructions have been given regarding the salary of Probationary Officers and creation of supernumerary posts. In line with these instructions, the concerned departments have asked for the provision of bulk salary to these candidates and the creation of more number of posts.
Accordingly, the points in paragraph (two) of the circular at serial number 8, read above, are explained as follows.

1) For Group A officers, Rs. 30,000/- per month and Rs. 20,000/- should be made available to the training institutions by the Administrative Departments concerned of the cadre in advance for providing bulk stipend. The Document Verification of candidates joining the training batch CPTP-5 was conducted from 30.09.2019 to 1.02.2019 and for candidates joining the training batch CPTP-6 was conducted from 2.10.2019 to 3.10.2019. In accordance with the guidelines (I) 2 of the Government Circular No.8 herein, the advance funds for the salary for the period from the date on which the candidates of the said batches have appeared for document verification to the date 22.03.2020 should be made available immediately by the concerned departments to Yashda, Pune for the candidates of Group A cadre and Vanamati, Nagpur for the candidates of Group B cadre. "
The training under CPTP-5 and CPTP-6 has started from 4.10.2019. Accordingly, orders regarding creation of more number of posts for the candidates joining CPTP-5 and CPTP-6 batches from the date fixed for document verification should be issued immediately by the concerned departments taking into account the number and date of actual joining.
