The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations publishes Lokrajya Marathi, Lokrajya Urdu, Lokrajya Hindi, Lokrajya Gujarati, Maharashtra Ahead (English) and Apna Mantralaya monthly. This magazine is an effective medium to convey the goals and schemes of the government to the people. Since the government printing press does not have modern machinery, the printing of new attractive forms in four colors and large number of magazines in the prescribed time frame cannot be done in the government printing press, with the consent of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, Marathi Lokrajya, Urdu Lokrajya, Hindi Lokrajya, Gujarati Lokrajya, Maharashtra Ahead (English), Apna Mantralaya, these magazines are being printed by the private printing press through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, and are being distributed privately by inserting and pasting cards on them. The Department of Industrial Energy and Labour, while agreeing to the proposal earlier, had given its opinion to continue the process till the modernization of the Government Press. Therefore, it was under consideration of the Government to extend the period of printing of these magazines by private printing presses through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for one year from April, 2019 to March, 2020.
Decision of the Government: Since there is no four-color printing system available in the government printing press, the government is approving the printing of Marathi Lokrajya, Urdu Lokrajya, Hindi Lokrajya, Gujarati Lokrajya, Maharashtra Ahead (English) and Apna Mantralaya magazines by private printing presses by inviting e-tenders and following the prescribed government procedures for a period of one year from April, 2019 to March, 2020 until the system is updated.
