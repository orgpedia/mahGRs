2. Department of Industrial Energy and Labour As per the Minimum Wages Act, 1948, a weekly off is admissible to the employees as it is included in the Minimum Wages. However, it has been observed that 4 contractual drivers hired by Krishna Motors in the division are demanding salary and overtime allowance for attending office on all government holidays. A weekly holiday is admissible to the contractual drivers as per the Department of Industrial Energy and Labour Minimum Wages Act, 1948. In the week in which they have to attend work on all days due to government work, only one day of weekly off will be allowed. Therefore, all Saturdays should be considered as regular attendance days for contractual drivers. If these drivers are called to work on Sunday, they will be entitled to an overtime allowance for that day. Accordingly, the concerned institution will have to demand payment from the government.
