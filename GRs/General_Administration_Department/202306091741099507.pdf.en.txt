Government circular
The Provisional Common Seniority of Senior Grade Scribes in the Ministry Department dated 01.01.2023 was notified vide Government Circular dated 23.01.2023. All the Ministries/ Departments were informed to forward the objections/ objections, if any, to the General Administration Department by 06.02.2023. Accordingly, the objections/ errors of some of the senior short-listed authors have been corrected. Therefore, the present seniority is being made interim and has been added. The General Administration Department, Government Notification dated 21.06.2021 has adopted the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 while preparing the said seniority list.
The date of regular appointment has not yet been given to the lower grade bookkeepers who were promoted to the post of next higher grade bookkeeper from 1.1.2018 as they were promoted in the net temporary form from 2017-18 as per the instructions of General Administration Department/ 16-B dated 29.12.2017 and dated 7.5.2021.
