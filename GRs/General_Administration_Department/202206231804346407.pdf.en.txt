Introduction: Maharashtra in the first budget session of the Maharashtra Legislature in 2022
Budget 2022-2023 Deputy Chief Minister (Finance) It will be introduced in the Legislative Assembly on March 11, 2022. While presenting the budget. In the budget speech made by the Deputy Chief Minister," The Amrit Mahotsav year of Marathwada Mukti Sangram is starting on 17 th September, 2022. To mark this year, various events related to the Liberation War will be organized in all the eight districts of Marathwada. A cabinet sub-committee comprising all ministers and guardian ministers of Marathwada will be formed to finalise the modalities of these programmes. Funds had been released for this. "
Following this, the government took decision. A Cabinet Sub-Committee was constituted on 4.5.2022 and the matter of opening a separate head of account for budgeting the fund for the implementation of the programme was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
To commemorate the Amrit Mahotsav of Marathwada Liberation Struggle, various events to spread the message of Marathwada Liberation Struggle will be organized in all the eight districts of Marathwada region from 17 th September, 2022 to 17 th September, 2023. Opening of new head of accounts to budget the funds to meet the expenditure on the events to be held during this period is being approved as under:
