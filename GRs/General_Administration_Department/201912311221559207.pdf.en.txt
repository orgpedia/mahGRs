Government's decision
Considering the comprehensive and progressive changes in the people-oriented schemes, development projects and policies implemented by various departments of the government, the list of advertising agencies was approved for two years by the government decision of serial number-1 and serial number 2. In accordance with the Government Decision dated 01.11.2019. The date has been extended till 11.11.2019.
2.It will take some time to start the process of e-tendering and prepare the final list for inviting applications in the prescribed format and in the prescribed manner from the qualified institutions for carrying out the promotional and publicity and advertising activities of various departments of the government. Therefore, the Government is pleased to extend the said period up to 31.01.2020.
