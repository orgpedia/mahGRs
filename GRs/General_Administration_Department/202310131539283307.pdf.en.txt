Preamble: Instructions have been issued under Circular No. 1 of the Circular regarding the use of Government vehicle by the office bearers/ officers/ employees during the winter session of Nagpur.
The rates for the use of government vehicles for private purposes have been mentioned as mentioned in the government decision of the finance department of the reference serial number 2. Accordingly, this Department has issued directions vide Government Circular dated 12.10.2022 in serial number 5.
Circular: During the winter session of the legislature in Nagpur, the vehicles (motor and jeeps) required for the ministers, ministers of state as well as the officials of the government and the Maharashtra Legislative Secretariat and the ministerial camp office etc. are acquired by the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur Division for the conduct of the session. In order to cut down the expenditure on fuel of these government vehicles, the following directions are being issued: a) Government vehicles should be used only for government purposes.
(b) Government vehicles should not be taken outside the limits of Nagpur city, even for official purposes.
(e) However, if the Government vehicles are to be used outside the limits of Nagpur city due to some unavoidable reasons, the permission of the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur should be duly obtained for using these vehicles by submitting valid reasons for taking these vehicles out of the limits of Nagpur city.
e) The use of the said government vehicles shall not exceed 80 km per day.
(3) Notwithstanding the aforesaid standing instructions of the Government regarding the use of Government vehicles for Government business, certain officers/ employees misused the Government vehicles for private business during the session of the Legislature at Nagpur.
