the order
Shri Devisingh Harisingh Daberao, Under Secretary, Rural Development Department is being appointed on deputation for a period of three years initially to the post of" Deputy Secretary" on establishment of State Information Commission, Amaravati Bench, Amaravati under General Administration Department subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The service of the Deputy Commissioner shall commence on the date on which Mr. Devisingh Harisingh Daberao, Under-Secretary, enters upon the office of Deputy Commissioner and shall end on the date on which he resumes his official charge.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority shall have the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(a) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and (d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Residential Service. The terms and conditions of the deputation of Shri Devisingh Harisingh Daberao, Under Secretary, Rural Development Department and General Administration Department (Office-VI) shall be decided in consultation with each other, after taking into consideration the terms and conditions mentioned in Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters relating to the deputation of Shri Devisingh Harisingh Daberao, Under Secretary, will be dealt with by the General Administration Department (Office-Secretary-6).
4. Rural Development Department to relieve Shri Devisingh Harisingh Daberao, Under Secretary, to join as" Deputy Secretary" on the establishment of State Information Commission, Amaravati Bench, Amaravati with immediate effect. Also, the State Information Commission, Amravati Bench, Amravati under the General Administration Department should immediately appoint Mr. Devisingh Harisingh Daberao, Under Secretary.
