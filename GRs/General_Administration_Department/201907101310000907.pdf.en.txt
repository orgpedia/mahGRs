The Proposal:
In India. The Emergency was declared from 25.06.1975 to 31.03.1977. A policy has been formulated to honour/ suitably honour the persons who have undergone imprisonment during this period by providing financial assistance to them.
The matter of sanctioning honorarium to eligible persons under this policy is under consideration.
