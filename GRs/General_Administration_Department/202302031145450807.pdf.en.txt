As per the notification dated 15.06.2018 of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions (Department of Personnel and Training), Government of India, the number of cadre posts in the Indian Administrative Service in the State has been fixed. Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix is approved for 8 cadre posts. Further, 8 more Non-Cadre posts (SDR) may be filled up as per the provisions of Rule 12 (7) of the Employees (Pay) Rules, 2016. The government was considering the creation of temporary posts in this cadre as an administrative necessity.
GOVERNMENT DECISION 1) One post created provisionally under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954 in the category of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix vide Government Decision dated 07.03.2022 is being extended from 01.03.2023 to 28.02.2024.
(ii) In terms of the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, the Government have approved the creation of two temporary posts in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix from 01.03.2023 to 28.02.2024.
The Government is approving creation of seven posts in the cadre of Apex Scale: Level 17 in the Pay Matrix under the second proviso to Rule 4 (2) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, temporarily with effect from 03.02.2023 to 02.02.2024.
