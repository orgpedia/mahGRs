Government Circular: General Administration Department, Government Circular No. Sarvasu-1196/ No. 5/96/29, dated 18th September, 1996 has declared a local holiday for Government/ Semi-Government offices in Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban District on the occasion of" Anant Chaturdashi" every year and on the occasion of Gopalkala (Dahi Handi) from the year 2007. Now, the third local holiday is being declared by all government/ semi-government offices in Mumbai and Mumbai Suburban District in the year 2023.
