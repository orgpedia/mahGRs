Government Circular: -
Rule 9 (3) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979 provides that the disciplinary authority is required to refer the record of vigilance to the Commission for feedback in all cases where disciplinary action / departmental vigilance against government employees is required to be consulted by the Commission.
2. The list of documents while forwarding proposals for feedback to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has been notified under the circulars referred to above.
3. However, it has been observed that there are difficulties in forwarding the documents mentioned in the said Circulars to the Commission as well as that some of the issues are unnecessary in due course of time. Therefore, the above dates 14.12.1987 and d. The following revised instructions are being issued regarding the cancellation of the 06.02.2004 circular and sending the documents to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
1) While sending the proposal to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the details of the documents to be attached with the letter should be prepared according to the checklist attached. Original documents or attested copies should be attached.
2) The letter as well as the checklist should be signed by the Joint / Deputy Secretary concerned only to ensure that the proposal is complete.
The Government Circular was made available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
