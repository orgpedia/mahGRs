Preamble: A proposal to purchase a new helicopter for a high profile person of the State was under consideration. Accordingly, the tender was published on the website of Government of Maharashtra on 7th November, 2017 for submission of global tenders by helicopter manufacturing companies. DA/ ADM/ 697/2017, dated 6/6/2017 was opened before the Technical Committee constituted and thereafter the Financial bids were opened on 19/12/2017. The Technical Committee submitted its report and financial proposals. The decision was taken at a meeting of the Empowered Committee headed by the Chief Secretary on April 6, 2018.
The Empowered Committee, after studying the technical and financial bids, decided to procure the S76-D helicopter from Circosky International Operations, Inc. (a Lockheed Martin Company), a US-based manufacturing company, for the air travel of high-profile persons of the State Government. A government resolution in this regard was issued.
