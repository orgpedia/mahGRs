Introduction: Since the vehicles of the Government Transport Service Office are meant for the uniforms of the most famous dignitaries, they have to be kept in good condition. Therefore, it is very important to recruit skilled employees for the repair and maintenance of vehicles in the Government Transport Service Office.
Two posts have been sanctioned in the Power Minister Cadre (Group-C) in the office of Government Transport Service, Worli, Mumbai and both the posts are vacant. Further, considering the fact that 1 post in the cadre of Tushar Chithari (Group-C) has been sanctioned and the said post is also vacant, it is under consideration to approve the proposal for creation of non-fictional posts to fill up two posts of Minister of Power (Group-C)-1 and Tushar Chithari (Group-C)-1 through external agencies.
Government Decisions: Department of Finance, Government Decisions, D. As on 11.12.2018, the Administrative Departments have been empowered to create and fill up the notional posts by external mechanism within the limit of 70% of the sanctioned vacancies in the direct services as per the old format. Accordingly, the Government hereby approve the creation of non-fictional posts for filling up two posts of Minister of State for Power (Group-C)-1 and Tushar Chithari (Group-C)-1 in the office of Government Transport Service, Worli, Mumbai through external agencies and filling up of these posts through external agencies by implementing the prescribed tendering process. These positions are filled through external agencies, government decisions, finance department, etc. 02.02.2013 and D. Care should be taken to strictly comply with the provisions of 02.12.2013 as well as the conditions prescribed under sub-section-IV, No. 11 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978.
