Section 8 of the Act, dated 29/02/2016 provides that the first increment shall be given to the probation officers on completion of their probation period of one year, and the subsequent increment shall be given on satisfactory completion of probation period. Accordingly, the first increment to the above 02 probationary officers has been sanctioned vide Office Order dated 04/12/2017. Now that the probationary officers concerned have satisfactorily completed the probation period, the subsequent increments should be made admissible to them.
The details of leave taken during the training period by the Assistant Director, Maharashtra Finance and Accounts Service, Group-A, under the respective purview of the table in paragraph 2 above and the leave sanction order have been received from the Regional Office and the orders are being issued accordingly.
