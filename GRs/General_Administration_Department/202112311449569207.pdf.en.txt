Circular:
The birth anniversaries and National Days in 2022 should be celebrated in the Ministries and all Government/ Paramilitary offices as per the schedule attached.
The programmes mentioned in the Annexure shall fall on all Saturdays, Sundays and Public Holidays/ Local Holidays and shall be celebrated as per the changes suggested by the Central Government in the programme in this regard, otherwise, the programmes shall be celebrated on the same day.
The Ministerial Department/ Divisional Commissioner/ Collector shall issue necessary instructions regarding the conduct of the said programme in all the government/ semi-government offices of their department/ district and take appropriate action regarding their implementation.
