Preamble: In pursuance of the directions of the Election Commission of India in Reference No. 1, the Government have established 450 Voter Help Centres at 91 places in 359 taluks and towns in the State for the period from 1st March, 2013 to 31st March, 2014. Voter Help Centres have been extended from time to time and as per Reference No.3, these 450 Voter Help Centres have been extended till 31st March, 2022.
