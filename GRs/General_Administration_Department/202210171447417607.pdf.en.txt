circular
The Post-Service Training Examination for the year 2022 for Clerks-Typists in the Administrative Department of the Ministry will be held on Friday, December 22. It will be held on 4th November, 2022 at Sydenham College of Commerce and Economics, Churchgate, Mumbai. The examination will be conducted as per schedule.
2. The names of the candidates allowed to appear in the said examination and the department are indicated in Annexure' B'. The information regarding the subjects in which the candidates who have failed in the previous examination have been exempted due to passing is shown in Annexure' C' and important instructions for the candidates are mentioned in Annexure' D'. The seat number of the candidates will be mentioned at the examination centre on the day of the examination.
The Administrative Departments of the Ministry are requested to immediately bring to the notice in writing of all the candidates (including candidates on leave/ deputation) admitted to the said Circular Examination and preserve the records of the same in the archives of their respective departments. The concerned department should ensure that there is no delay in this regard. In case any error/ discrepancy is found in the details indicated in Annexure' B" and' C," the concerned should immediately inform this section before the examination.
