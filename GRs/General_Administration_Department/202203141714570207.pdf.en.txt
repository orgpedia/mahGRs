Government circular:
The reference No. 1 As per Rule 2 (d) of the Government Notification at Reference No. 3 here. Interim Common Seniority as on 01.01.2020 of eligible Assistant Cell Officers for promotion to the post of Cell Officers vide Government Circular dated 09.07.2021 has been published.
2. Refer No. 2 As per the provisions of the Government Order dated 01.01.2021, the eligible Assistant Cell Officers who have been continuously working in the rank of Assistant Cell Officer for more than 5 years in the ratio (from the revised date of seniority in respect of the Assistant Cell Officers who have lost their original seniority) and who have passed the departmental examination prescribed for promotion to the post of Cell Officer or who are exempted from passing the said examination, the provisional additional seniority as on 01.01.2021 has been prepared and attached to this circular.
3. As per the rules of admission to the service of the Assistant Cell Officer cadre, eligible clerks and typists are promoted to the post of Assistant Cell Officer from the promotion quota of 30% of the seats. Accordingly, the process of regular promotion of clerical typists to the post of Assistant Cell Officer is underway. As soon as the process of regular promotion of clerical typists to the post of Assistant Cell Officer is completed, the Assistant Cell Officer of the promoted quota will be included in the seniority list in the appropriate place.
