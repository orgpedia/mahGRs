the order
The appointment of Mr. B.S. Wankhede, Deputy Secretary, General Administration Department, as Regional Divisional Inquiry Officer, Pune, on deputation for a period of one year is being made subject to the following terms and conditions.
The service of deputation shall commence on the date on which the Deputy Secretary hands over the charge of his office in the Government service and shall end on the date on which the Deputy Secretary shall resume his charge of his office in the Government.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(a) If his services are no longer required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send him back to the parent department. However, the non-recipient employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority before such repatriation; and
(d) They shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least 3 months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
2. M.N.S. (Period of holding office, Non-Ministerial Service. The terms and conditions of the deputation shall be decided by the General Administration Department Office 19 and Office 11A in consultation with the President, after taking into consideration the terms and conditions mentioned in Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters regarding the deputation of Mr. Wankhede, Deputy Secretary will be handled by the General Administration Department.
