Introduction: Regarding the purchase of a new helicopter for the Maharashtra government for anti-Naxal operations. The decision was taken on September 7, 2017. Accordingly, the tender was published on the website of the Government of Maharashtra on 24th January, 2018 for submission of global tenders by helicopter manufacturing companies. The Technical Tender dated 17th February, 2018 was issued vide Government Memorandum No. DA/ ADM/ 697/2017 was opened before the Technical Committee constituted vide Dated 6th June, 2017 and thereafter the financial bids were opened on 20th February, 2018. The Technical Committee submitted its report and financial proposals. The decision was taken at a meeting of the Empowered Committee headed by the Chief Secretary on April 6, 2018.
The Empowered Committee, after studying the technical and financial bids, decided to procure H145 helicopters from Airbus Helicopters, a German manufacturer, for the anti-Naxal operations in Gadchiroli and for the transportation of police personnel for this purpose, as well as for the transportation of injured personnel in emergency situations and for the timely airlift of important persons of the state to Nagpur.
In pursuance of the decision of the Empowered Committee in its meeting dated 6th April, 2018, the minutes of the aforesaid Reference No.1 have been issued. In line with this trend, the following decisions are being taken.
Government's decision:
As per the terms and conditions of the tender process for procurement of new helicopters for anti-Naxal operations for the Government of Maharashtra to be located at Nagpur, the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) has placed an order for procurement of H145 helicopters with state-of-the-art equipment from Airbus Helicopters, Deutschland GmbH, IndustriestraBe 4, Donauw√∂rth, Germany.
