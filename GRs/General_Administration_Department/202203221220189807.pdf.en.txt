Government order In pursuance of the directions issued by the High Court of Delhi in Writ Petition No. 3396/2019, Government of India and others, dated 04.12.2020, regarding the constitution of an independent Committee at the level of Secretary under the Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013, to regulate the complaints of sexual harassment against officers of All India Services, the Government of India has constituted a Special Committee at the State level for the purpose of investigation. A committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary had been formed. The Government Resolution dated 23.09.2021 has been issued. According to The appointment of members to the Control Committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary was under the consideration of the Government.
