1) The promotion shall be ad-hoc in the nature of temporary promotion as an administrative exigency.
2) These promotions are being given only on temporary/ ad-hoc basis considering the fact that at present there is no pending court case regarding the seniority list of the post of Lower Grade Short Writer and this seniority list has not been challenged.
(3) The said ad-hoc promotion is temporary in nature and the concerned shall not hold the post permanently.
4) The selection of eligible lower grade short writers for the year 2020-2021 is ad-hoc in nature and promotions to the post of upper grade short writers are ad-hoc in nature.
As a result, the lower category short-writers in the selection list will not get any rights to the regular post of upper category short-writer and seniority in service.
In case of non-acceptance of ad-hoc promotion to the post of Upper Grade Short Writer by the concerned Lower Grade Short Writer, his/ her name will be deleted from the selection list for the year 2020-2021 as per General Administration Department, Government Decision, No. SRV-2015/ Q.S. 303/ Q. 12, dated 12.09.2016 and his/ her eligibility will be checked for ad-hoc promotion to the post of Upper Grade Short Writer in the third year selection list without considering his/ her name in the subsequent two years selection lists.
(6) The ad-hoc promotion of the said lower grade short writer to the post of upper grade short writer is being made subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed against him till the date of this order. The concerned department should look into it.
7) The promotion. A Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 is being made subject to adjudication.
8) The promotion. The High Court of Bombay, pending Writ Petition (Stamp) No. 10876/2021, Writ Petition (Stamp) No. 10878/2021. A (Stamp) No. 10911/2021 and 10910/2021 and Writ Petition (Stamp) No. 10909/2021 subject to adjudication.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The lower category short writers who have been promoted to the post of upper category short writers should be relieved immediately by the concerned department. Also, promoted lower grade scribes should join the posted department immediately. The concerned departments should send the copies of the orders issued by the concerned departments and the orders issued by the concerned departments.
