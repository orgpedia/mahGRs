The Proposal:
DV vehicles have been placed under all the District Collector offices for the transportation of state guests/ dignitaries coming to the state. However, if several dignitaries come to visit the district at the same time and the D.V. vehicles are not enough, an agreement is made to provide vehicles on rent from private vehicle suppliers. As the previous tender agreement with the Collector, Nashik office had ended, the e-tendering process was carried out through the Collector, Nashik office for hiring vehicles on lease. After this tender process, the matter of approving the tenderer and the tender submitted by him in the e-tender process for Nashik district was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Shri Ganesh Tours and Travels, Nashik, which has qualified in the e-tendering process for providing vehicles on hire from private vehicle suppliers in the absence of government D.V. vehicles for the transportation of guests and dignitaries visiting Nashik district for the office of the Collector, Nashik, and the tender submitted by them after negotiation (statement-a) is being approved by the Government under this Government decision subject to the terms and conditions of the tender.
District Collector, Nashik shall take action as follows in respect of hiring vehicles from private suppliers:
1) Subject to the terms and conditions in the tender notice, the supplier may, in accordance with the rates in the accompanying prospectus. Request you to immediately negotiate with Shri Ganesh Tours and Travels, Nashik. Also, ensure that the terms and conditions of the contract are strictly adhered to.
This Agreement shall remain in force for a period of two years from the date of this Government Decision, i.e. from 05/05/2022 to 04/05/2024. during the period from 01/09/2021 to 04/05/2022. The rates shall be as per the Rate Agreement dated 06.10.2017.
