Government circular
Assistant Section Officer Post-Interview Training Examination-2021, the first time eligible candidates for this examination can apply in the prescribed format. The circular dated August 6, 2021 was called for. Now, applications in the prescribed format are being invited from the candidates who have failed in the previous post-service training examination or have been absent in the examination, and the prescribed format of application has been made available on the intranet of the Ministry under the heading" Application Form."
However, all the administrative departments of the ministry and the establishment officers of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission should ensure that all the details of the eligible candidate's application are correct and send the certified applications to this department in a manner that they reach the date of November 15, 2021. Please make sure that the candidate's full name is mentioned in the application.
Candidates who are eligible to appear for the first time in the examination of Assistant Cell Officer Post-Interview Training Examination-2021. The training classes for the candidates whose applications have been sent within the prescribed time as per the circular dated August 06, 2021, will be held at the time of registration. It is scheduled to be held on September 7, 2021. Apart from the candidates mentioned in the" Annexure A" of this circular, those who are eligible to appear for the first time in the Assistant Cell Officer Post-Interview Training Examination-2021 but have not applied for the examination. Candidates who have not sent their applications within the prescribed time as per the circular dated August 6, 2021, can also submit their applications in the prescribed format.
