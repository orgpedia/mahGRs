Conditions: 1. The said administrative approval is being provided by the District Planning Committee, Collectorate, Nagpur subject to availability of funds under the District Annual Plans and Redeployment. In case of non-receipt of funds by 31.03.2019, the administrative approval will be automatically cancelled.
The Directorate and the concerned Dean shall ensure that no fresh appointment is required to handle the said machinery.
(3) The equipment shall not be left unused under any circumstances and no new designations shall be made for the equipment. The authorities concerned should take care of this.
In this regard, the procedures mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedures for the Purchase of Office by the Government Department" should be complied with.
5. Procedure for Procurement of the said equipment" Procurement of drugs, related consumables and medical devices Haffkine Bio-Pharmaceuticals Corporation Ltd. " Issued Government Decision, Department of Public Health, Purchase No. 2016/ PR No. 240/ Health-8, dated 26.07.2017.
The Director (VC) and the concerned Dean shall ensure that the requirement of the equipment is met and its price is not more than the market price.
The Director (VC) shall certify that the specifications of the said machinery are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the machinery accordingly.
The Director shall ensure that the proposed machinery is not included in any other proposal and that the machinery is required by the institution concerned.
The prices of the main machinery and its accessories must be shown differently.
(10) While taking a decision in this regard, it shall be ensured that no expenditure shall be incurred in excess of the provision of sanctioned grants available through re-allocation under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
11.The State Level Procurement Committees shall comply with the provisions of paragraph No. 3.1.2.1 under the heading" Price Estimation and Price Reasonableness" in the aforesaid Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labour& Labour Department, dated 01.12.2016.
The price of the machine complying with this provision shall be subject to revised administrative approval as per requirement.
