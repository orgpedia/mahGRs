Subject: Suspension of Continuing Nursing Education.
The Maharashtra Nursing Council, Mumbai has been receiving written and verbal complaints from the nurses/ staff attending the" CNE (Continuous Nursing Workshop)" training programme for renewal of license of nurses working in private and government hospitals. Some newspapers have reported about it.
In view of the above facts and the fact that there is no provision in the Maharashtra Nursing Act, 1966 regarding CNE training, the said Continuous Nursing Education is hereby stayed till further orders of the Government.
3) The suspension order shall come into force with immediate effect.
