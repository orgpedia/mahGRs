The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1, 31 temporary posts in Nursing Training Institute, Mumbai were extended from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018. The said extension has expired on 28.02.2018.
As per the norms of Nursing Council of India for teaching students admitted to B.Sc./ M.Sc. (Nursing) and M.Sc. (Nursing) courses and as per the requirement for renewal/ extension/ recognition of Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, 8 posts of Nursing Cadre have been created. These 8 posts are included in the proposal for extension of 23 temporary posts of Principal, Institute of Nursing Education, Mumbai and a total of 31 temporary posts of 23+8 are proposed to be continued with effect from 01.03.2018. 2 is presented here.

Government's decision:
(1) In the interest of the functioning of the Nursing Training Institute, Mumbai and patient care in the hospital, it is very necessary to continue these temporary posts. During this period, in any case, the administrative department will take the above mentioned government decisions taking into account the increasing use of technology in the functioning of the government, changes in the government's manpower over time and the need for effective use of manpower. As per the provision dated 05.03.2018, the revised contours of the review (including the newly created post) are required to be finalised with the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries.
In the absence of extension, if the question of salary of the said temporary post arises, the entire responsibility has been fixed on the concerned administrative department, therefore, the Director (VC) is advised to immediately submit any situation-appropriate proposal to the Government for approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries to the said figure after reviewing the figure of permanent, temporary and newly created posts, so that the question of salary does not arise in the absence of extension to the officers/ employees working in the said post. If this happens, please note that the entire responsibility will be on the directorate as the head of the department.
