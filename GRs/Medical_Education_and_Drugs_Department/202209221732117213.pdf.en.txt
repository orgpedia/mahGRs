Preamble: The funds budgeted under the target of (10) Contractual Services for the financial year 2022-23 to the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research are disbursed to the respective institutions at the rate of 7% per month through the Finance Department. However, as the said funds are insufficient, while submitting the proposal for disbursement of additional funds to the Finance Department, the Finance Department has approved the disbursement of additional funds to 8 Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals (10) under the Directorate for the purpose of contractual services. Accordingly, the following decisions have been taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have accorded approval to the 8 Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research (10) to disburse and spend the amount of Rs. 7,25,07, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Twenty Five Lakhs Seven Thousand only) as mentioned in the Statement-A excluding the funds already disbursed out of the funds budgeted for the financial year 2022-23 under the Objective of Contractual Services.
The Head of Department/ Head of Office/ Controller should strictly follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Rights Rulebook while spending the above-mentioned funds.
The Dean of the institution will be responsible for paying the honorarium/ salary of doctors, associate professors, assistant professors, security guards, cleaners and other staff working through external sources in Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research within the stipulated time.
