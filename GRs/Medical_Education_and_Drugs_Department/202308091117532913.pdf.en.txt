Preamble: Government Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 23.07.2015, approval has been given to start a Government Medical College with an intake capacity of 100 students and a 500-bed hospital at Chandrapur. The government was considering allowing the college to open its own private account for depositing inpatient fees, outpatient fees, laboratory fees and other amounts collected in the hospital affiliated to the college.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Decision of the Government: The Government Medical College, Chandrapur shall deposit the inter-patient fees, out-patient fees, laboratory fees and other amounts collected in the Government Hospital attached to the Government Medical College, Chandrapur, for making allowable expenditure therefrom and in terms of reference no. 2 The Government hereby approve the opening of a Personal Loan Account (PLA) in the name of the Dean, Government Medical College and Hospital, Chandrapur, as per the provisions of Rule 494 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, Clause-1, for expenditure for the matters prescribed under the above Government Decision dated 04.03.2020.
2.It will be mandatory to follow the guidelines and financial rules set by the government from time to time while using the funds deposited in this account. It will also be necessary to comply with the provisions of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules.
