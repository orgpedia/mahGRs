Government's decision:
As per the above mentioned letter dated 10/08/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for procurement of necessary furniture material for the newly established Government Medical College, Osmanabad under the control of the Department for the students who are to be admitted to 1st year MBBS to meet the standards of National Medical Commission, New Delhi. In pursuance of the said proposal, administrative approval is being accorded for the purchase of furniture materials mentioned in the table below at a cost of Rs. 1,23,04, 868/- (Rupees One Crore, Twenty Three Lakhs, Four Thousand, Eight Hundred and Sixty Six only) during the financial year 2022-23 from the grant sanctioned under the State Plan subject to the following conditions.
