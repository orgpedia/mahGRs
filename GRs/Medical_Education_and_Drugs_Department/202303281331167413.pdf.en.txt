Preamble: Government Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 31.01.2012, Government Medical College with an intake capacity of 100 students and a 500-bed hospital at Nandurbar have been approved. The government was considering allowing the patients to open a private account to deposit the patient fees, X-ray, CT scan, MRI, examination fees and other charges, as well as various donations received by the hospital.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The patient fees, X-ray, CT scan, MRI, examination fees and other fees paid by the patients admitted for treatment at Government Medical College and Hospital, Nandurbar, as well as the funds received through various donations etc. 2 The Government hereby approves the opening of a Personal Loan Account (PLA) in the name of the Dean, Government Medical College and Hospital, Nandurbar, as per the provisions of Rule 494 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, Clause-1, to meet the expenditure for the matters prescribed under the Government Decision dated 04.03.2020.
2.It will be mandatory to follow the guidelines and financial rules set by the government from time to time while using the funds deposited in this account. It will also be necessary to comply with the provisions of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules.
