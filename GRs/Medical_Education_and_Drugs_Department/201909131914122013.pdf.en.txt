Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid Government Decision, dated 14.08.2018, the Government Medical College and Hospital, Aurangabad has been granted administrative approval to purchase 3 Tesla MRI machines from the grant received from Shri Saibaba Sansthan Vishwastha Nisthan, Shirdi.
