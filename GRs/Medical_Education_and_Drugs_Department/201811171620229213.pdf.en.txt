Introduction: The existing 7 wards at the Government Ayurveda College Affiliated Hospital, Nanded are inadequate for the patients, due to lack of space for the patients admitted in the inpatient department of the hospital. Also, there is a problem in indicating the number of beds as per the sanctioned number of undergraduate and postgraduate students as per the standards of Indian Medical Council, New Delhi. Further, as per the proposal of the Dean, Government Ayurveda College, Nanded regarding the need to construct the building in the interest of the patients due to the increased number of patients, the matter of granting administrative approval to the building was under consideration of the Government in accordance with the proposal submitted by the Public Works Department, Nanded to the Director, Ayush regarding the administrative approval of the budget and plan of Rs. 4,97,77, 302/- for the construction of a ward for patients on the first floor of the Government Ayurveda College Affiliated Hospital, Nanded.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The said work, as explained in the Preamble, is being accorded administrative sanction by the Government subject to the fulfillment of the following conditions in the budget of Rs. 5,48,40, 112/- (Rupees Five Crore Forty Eight Lakh Forty Four Thousand One Hundred Twelve only) at the end of scrutiny by the Public Works Department.
a) Before starting the work, the design, layout and detailed map should be started with the approval of the architect.
(b) The ownership of the land should be ascertained before the commencement of the work.
(c) When dealing with a provision made in a lump-sum manner, the work should be taken up only after making a detailed budget.
d) In the course of the actual work, the Department of the Environment issued Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in A.I., dated 10th January, 2014.
e) Action should be taken under the Government Decision No. BDG 2017/ PR No. 60/ Buildings-2 of the Public Works Department, dated 07/07/2017.
