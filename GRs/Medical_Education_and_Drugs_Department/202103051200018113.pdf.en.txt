Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid Government letter dated 15.04.2020, approval was accorded to 6 Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department to set up Virus Research and Diagnostic Laboratories for COVID-19 testing under COVID-19 measures. In this approval, instructions were also given to make the purchase in accordance with the provisions of the above Government letter, dated 24.03.2020.
In this regard, approval is being granted subject to payment of the total amount of Rs. 2,06,09, 608/- (Rupees Two Crores, Six Lakhs, Nine Thousand, Six Hundred and Eight only) as per institution wise in Annexure-A, purchased from Haffkine Corporation for setting up of Virus Research and Diagnostic Laboratory in Government Medical College, Baramati, Jalgaon and Aurangabad under the control of the Department.

Conditions:
Administrative approval is being granted to Baramati and Jalgaon to pay for the equipment purchased for the functioning of the virus research and diagnostic laboratory from the funds received from the Central Government from the grant sanctioned under the State Plan to the Aurangabad Institute.
The Directorate and the concerned Dean have confirmed that no new design, construction and electrification is required to handle the said equipment.
In this regard, the prescribed procedure mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedure for the procurement of offices by the Government Department" has been complied with.
4. The said machinery has been provided to the Institute by Haffkine Corporation.
5. The said equipment has not been duplicated and no unnecessary purchase of the said equipment has been made.
6. It should be ascertained that the said payments have not been made to Haffkine Corporation earlier.
