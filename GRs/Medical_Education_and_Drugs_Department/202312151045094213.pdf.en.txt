Reference No. The proposal has been submitted to the government by the Commissioner (VC) and General Manager, Haffkine Corporation, Purchase Cell under Section 7 and 8 respectively. Further, as the Technical Committee constituted at the Directorate level has reported that the equipment is useful for critical pediatric patients in other diseases other than COVID, the newly revised administrative approval for the purchase of the equipment and equipment is being granted subject to the following conditions, for a total amount of approximately Rs. 52.24 crore in 9 items mentioned in the Annexure attached with the Government decision.
Conditions:
(1) In accordance with the said approval, the details of institution-wise machines and required numbers are indicated in Annexure A. According to this Appendix' A', now the number of nails required has been reduced. The Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education will be responsible to ensure that the price is calculated as per the requirement. Also, it should be ensured that there is no increase in the cost of original administrative approval of the proposed machinery or the revised administrative approval granted earlier.
2) The revised approval is being given on the condition that the Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education, take an explanation regarding the increase in the price per unit (Paediatric& Neonatal Ventilator) in Annexure' A', No. 07 (Original: Rs. 18 lakhs revised to Rs. 22.91 lakhs) as compared to the original administrative approval price.
3) The proposed procurement price is estimated at the time of administrative approval, so the price should be determined at the end of the procurement process. The Commissioner (VC) and Managing Director, Haffkine Bio Pharmaceuticals Corporation Limited, Mumbai shall ensure that the price is less than the market price and not more than the price at which the supplier has supplied the same elsewhere.
4) All other terms and conditions of the Government's decision dated 20.09.2021, dated 03.12.2021 and dated 14.06.2022 in this regard will remain as the medium of accounting.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the authority of the Finance Department, Government Resolution No.: 2013/ Q. No. 30/13/ Regulations, dated 17.04.2015 as well as the Administrative Department under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-II, Roll No. 3, Rule No. 7.
