Introduction: As Nanded city is developing as a major center in Marathwada from the educational and commercial point of view, many people from the district and surrounding areas visit the city. As a result, the health care system is strained. Therefore, the establishment of a nursing college attached to a government medical college will help reduce the stress on the health system.
Keeping this in mind, and keeping in mind the need of nurses in government medical colleges and health services in the state, Dr. Shankarrao Chavan Government Medical College and Hospital, Nanded has been given the capacity of 50 students.
