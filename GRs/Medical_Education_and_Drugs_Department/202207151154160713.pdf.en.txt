The Proposal:
The process of starting the newly established Government Medical College at Osmanabad with an intake capacity of 100 students and a 430-bed hospital attached to it is going on at a fast pace under the control of the department and the concerned deans have to attend frequent meetings at various office locations within the district as well as for other important work related to the college and hospital. Therefore, as the vehicle is essential for the day-to-day official work of the Dean, the General Administration Department was under consideration of the Government to allow the Government to hire 01 private vehicle from a Government approved supplier in the prescribed procedure as per the provision of Government Decision dated 10/08/2020. Following this, the government took decision:
Government's decision:
Directorate Reference No. 6 As per the letter mentioned herein, the Dean, Government Medical College and Hospital, Osmanabad, for day-to-day government business, vide Reference No. 3& 4 of Hyundai Xcent/ Maruti Suzuki Dzire/ Honda Amaze in accordance with the provisions of the Government Order and Government Letter, respectively.
