The government's decision:
Construction of new 100-student college and hospital building attached with yoo bed and other boys / girls hostel and officers / staff quarters and other buildings in Government Medical College and Hospital, Nandurbar under the control of the Department is in progress. In order to provide water supply from Virachak Project Dam to the medical officers / doctors, patients, relatives of patients, undergraduate and postgraduate students, nursing degree students as well as officers and staff and others for daily drinking water supply at the said site, the water supply budget prepared by the Maharashtra Life Authority, Nashik amounting to Rs. 8,53,75, 063 / - (literal Rs. Eight Crore Fifty Three Lakh Seventy Five Thousand Sixty Six Only) is being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
