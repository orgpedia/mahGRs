Introduction: Mohammadia Tibbia College and Hospital, Malegaon is run by Al Jamiatul Mohammadia Al Khairia, Mumbai. The college offers BUMS (Unani) degree course of five and a half years duration as prescribed by the Central Council of Indian Medicine, New Delhi.
During the financial year 2003-04, the Government has decided to sanction 100% wage subsidy to the private Government aided Ayurveda and Unani Institutions in the State with effect from January, 2004. Accordingly, the audited accounts of Mohammadiya Tibbia College and Hospital, Malegaon for the financial year 2007-08 to 2016-17 were valued at Rs. 1,65,485/- is being paid as recurring subsidy. Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning grant to the institution was under consideration of the Government.
