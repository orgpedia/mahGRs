The state government has decided to implement 10 per cent reservation for economically weaker sections in the open category for admission to health science courses.

Date of release: March 7, 2019
GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF GENERAL ADMINISTRATION, NO. RAADHO-4019/ Q.No. 31/16-A, dated 12th February, 2019.
Preamble: In addition to the categories provided under Article 15 (4) and (5) and Article 16 (4) of the Constitution of India, 10% reservation in the total number of seats in Government Educational Institutions/ Aided Schools, Colleges, All Higher Educational Institutions, Unaided Schools, Colleges and Educational Institutions, excluding educational institutions having minority status, has been provided for the economically weaker sections of the open category in the State (Backward Classes) by the General Administration Department vide its order dated 12.02.2019. Besides, 10 per cent reservation has been provided for the economically weaker sections in the state in the posts of direct services for appointment in government establishments, semi-government establishments, boards/ corporations/ urban local bodies/ rural local bodies, authorities. The terms and conditions for availing this reservation have been mentioned in the said Government Order. The Government Decision dated 12.02.2019 issued by the General Administration Department states that the necessary orders regarding admission to educational institutions should be issued by the concerned department immediately.

Government Resolution No: MED-1019/ PR No. 37/19/ Education-2
Accordingly, in pursuance of the said Government Order dated 12.02.2019, the matter of implementing reservation for economically weaker sections in the open category for admission to Health Science courses was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Bill provides for reservation of 10 per cent seats in government/ municipal/ government-aided/ private unaided medical colleges for economically weaker sections (EWS).
The procedure and other terms/ conditions regarding the eligibility certificate in connection with the receipt of the benefit of this reservation shall be as prescribed in the Government Order dated 12.02.2019 of the General Administration Department.
