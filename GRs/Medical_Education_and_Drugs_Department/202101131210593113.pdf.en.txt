The Proposal:
Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme is being implemented for eligible students belonging to economically weaker sections and eligible students who take admission in professional courses of health sciences through centralized admission process are given scholarship of 50 percent tuition fee under this scheme.
Also, under the Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme, students belonging to economically weaker sections of the open cadre are paid a per-salary maintenance allowance at a discounted rate.
Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme has been implemented for the students belonging to Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) by the Department of Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes from the academic year 2019-20.
The Hon'ble Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. 15737/2019 and other petitions dated 09/09/2020 has granted interim stay on reservation for Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBCs).
