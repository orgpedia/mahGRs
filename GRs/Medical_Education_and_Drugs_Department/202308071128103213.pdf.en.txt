Government's decision:
In pursuance of the aforesaid letter dated 14.07.2023, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of revised administrative approval for the newly established Government Medical College, Parbhani, under the control of the Department, to procure furniture material required for the academic requirements of the students admitted to first year MBBS to meet the standards of National Medical Commission, New Delhi.
Proposed furniture purchase reference no. 05 Administrative approval was granted earlier under the Government decision mentioned here. However, the institution has not been able to execute the procurement process of furniture material for the financial year 2022-23 due to administrative reasons. Accordingly, vide reference No. In supersession of the earlier Administrative Sanction Orders issued under Section 05, the Government Medical College, Parbhani is being granted administrative approval for the purchase of furniture materials mentioned in the table below at a cost of Rs. 1,86,55, 868/- (Rupees One Crore, Sixty Eight Lakhs, Fifty Five Thousand, Eight Hundred and Sixty Eight only) from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme during the financial year 2023-24, subject to the following conditions.

Conditions:
(i) The said administrative approval is being provided subject to availability of funds to the institution concerned from the grants sanctioned under the State Plan.
(2) In this regard, the prescribed procedure mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedures for the Purchase of Office to be made by the Government Department" should be complied with.
The procurement of these furniture items should be done through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal as per the provisions of the procurement policy dated 01.12.2016 of Government Decision, Department of Industry, Energy and Labour or as per the Government Decision, Department of Industry, Energy and Labour No.
4) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the concerned Dean shall ensure that the requirement of such furniture material is met and its price is not more than the market price.
5) It has been ensured that no new construction, electrification or new design is required for this furniture material.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the concerned Dean shall certify that the specifications of the furniture material/ equipment are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the equipment accordingly.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the Dean concerned shall ensure that the proposed furniture material is not included in any other proposal and that such material is required by the institution concerned.
8) It will be necessary to show the different prices of the main machine and its accessories.
9) While deciding on the purchase of furniture materials in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of subsidy sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
