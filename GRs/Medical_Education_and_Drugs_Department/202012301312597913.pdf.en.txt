Preamble: The concerned company has agreed to construct the lecture theatre complex G+ 3 required by the Government Medical College and Hospital, Aurangabad under the control of the department through the Jankidevi Bajaj Gram Vikas Sanstha, Pune under Bajaj Auto with the help of CSR funds. In this regard, in pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Directorate to the Government vide letter dated 05.11.2020, it was under consideration of the Government to accept the donations received through CSR for construction related matters and to grant administrative approval for construction through the respective donor agencies.
Government's decision:
The Government Medical College and Hospital, Aurangabad, under the control of the Department, is being provided administrative approval by the Janki Devi Bajaj Gram Vikas Sanstha, Pune, under Bajaj Auto, for the construction of Lecture Room G+ 3 Complex required by the Institute from the CSR Fund at a budget of Rs. 6,87,52, 811/- (Rupees Six Crores, Eighty Eight Lakhs, Fifty Two Thousand, Eight Hundred and Eleven only) subject to the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions: 1. If the said work comes under the jurisdiction of the Archaeology Department, the prior permission of the concerned department as well as the Municipal Corporation and similar authorities should be obtained.
