Preamble: The Department of Public Health has signed Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Regional Psychiatry Hospital, Nagpur and Pune under Centre of Excellence (CoE) scheme for starting Post Graduate courses in Psychiatry at IGG Government Medical College, Pune and Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur under the Directorate of Public Health Department and Post Graduate courses in Pediatrics and Gynaecology at Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur and Government Medical College and Hospital, Akola using the beds available at Nagpur and Akola under the Department of Public Health on 8th February, 2019. Further, as per the decision dated 16.09.2019 of the Department of Public Health, the Centre of Excellence, Government of India, has approved the opening of Post Graduate Programme in Psychiatry under the scheme of Directorate General of Health Services in collaboration with Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur and B.G. Government Medical College and Sassoon General Hospital, Pune, Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Nagpur and Pune.
