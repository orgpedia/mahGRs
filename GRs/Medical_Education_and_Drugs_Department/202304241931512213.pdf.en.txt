In accordance with the decision taken by the High Level Committee of Secretaries, the revised construction budget of Rs. 94,36,07, 937/- (Rupees Four-ninety Nine Crores, Thirty-six Lakhs, Seven Thousand, Nine Hundred and Thirty Seven only) is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
