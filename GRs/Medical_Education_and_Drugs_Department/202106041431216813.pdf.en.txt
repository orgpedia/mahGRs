Government's decision:
The Government Medical College and Hospital at Alibaug, which is under the control of the department, will be started in the near future and the District Hospital of the Public Health Department has been transferred to this department for this Government Medical College. To start this medical college, it is necessary to build a library and lecture room in the district hospital to meet the standards of the National Council of Medical Sciences, as well as to renovate the nurse's residence in the district hospital. Administrative approval is being accorded for these works for an amount of Rs. 15,46,33, 181/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores, Forty Six Lakhs, Thirty Three Thousand, One Hundred and Eighty One only).
