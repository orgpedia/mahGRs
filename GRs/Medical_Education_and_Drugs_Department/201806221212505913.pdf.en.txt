The Proposal:
A new government medical college with an intake capacity of 100 students and an attached hospital with 500 beds have been approved in Satara.
First Collector to take charge of Government Medical College
The place of Gayran at Mahuli was fixed. But it is only about 10 km from the city. As I am far away, it is inconvenient for college and hospital. Therefore, the transfer of land in Krishnanagar, Satara to Maharashtra Krishna Valley Corporation, Pune was under the consideration of the government.
The proposal was then sent to the Cabinet for approval. It was approved in the Cabinet meeting held on 29th May, 2018. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approves the transfer of 25 acres of land belonging to Maharashtra Krishna Valley Irrigation Development Corporation, Pune at Krishnanagar, Satara to the Department of Medical Education without any conditions and free of cost for the sanctioned Government Medical College at Satara.
This decision was taken by the government in reference to the Finance Department's Unofficial Reference No. 159/2018/ Expenditure-13, dated 28.03.2018 is being issued as per the approval given.
