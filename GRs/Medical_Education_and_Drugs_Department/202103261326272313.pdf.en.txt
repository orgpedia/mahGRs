Government's decision:
Government Medical College, Baramati, Pune vide Government Order dated 24.06.2019. Administrative approval for procurement of various equipment was accorded on 13.08.2019. However, in view of the situation arising out of Corona Virus infection in the last financial year, in order to regulate the treasury operations, the acceptance of subsidy related payments for the current financial year in all treasuries/ sub-treasuries and accounts offices should be continued only till 27th March, 2020. Thereafter, no other payments shall be accepted except for Corona related matters.
Further, vide the aforesaid G.O., Finance Department, dated 27.03.2020, the due date for receipt of Health Service related payments was extended up to 31.03.2020. In this regard, having Sub-Treasury Office at Baramati, the Institute is required to make the payments mentioned in the following table:
