Government's decision:
The following modalities are being prescribed for providing cleaning, hospital food, laundry and other works to the Government Medical/ Ayurveda/ Dental Colleges and affiliated hospitals under the Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department, as well as Group C and Group D posts on contract basis, security guards and other posts through external sources.
The instructions issued by the Finance Department under the Government Decision dated 27.04.2022 should be followed.
2) The financial approval of the administrative department should be obtained for the expenditure of work done by external agencies as per the Finance Department, Government Decision dated 17.04.2015, Financial Powers Rules Part-I, Sub-Division-4, No. 11.

(5) To avoid delays in the tender process and to remove the gap in the rates, each organization should enter into an agreement with the service provider at the minimum rate of the service provider by conducting the tender process through the Commissioner, Medical Education and Research, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 01.12.2016 of the Department of Industrial Energy and Labor, to fill the sanctioned notional/ contractual posts of Group C and Group D through external sources.
Providing posts through a service provider appointed through a tender process or a government approved organization, should be done in such a way that at least 20 to 30 percent savings are made as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 27.04.2022 of the Finance Department.
The savings should be recorded in the accompanying Form-A.
7) The selection of the external agency should be done in a very transparent manner by conducting the tender process in the prescribed manner for taking the service from the external source. Accordingly, the agreement with the service provider should be made at the level of the Commissioner, Medical Education, according to the necessary terms and conditions.
(8) Care should be taken to ensure that the Government is not liable for the services of the employees employed by the service provider.
9) It is a financial irregularity if the work is done without the approval of the competent authority or the payments are made without the approval of the administrative department.
So that there is no financial irregularity, it should be taken care of by the head of the department/ head of the office and the accounting officer of the organization.
10) While submitting the proposal to the government through the Commissioner, Medical Education, it should mention the duration of the contract, the amount of the proposal cost, the main terms and conditions of the contract, etc.
