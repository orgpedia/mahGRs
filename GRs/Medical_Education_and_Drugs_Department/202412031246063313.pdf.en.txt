completion of one year from the date of drawal of the funds/date of credit of amount to the VPDA
or the timeline mentioned in the sanction order whichever 15 earlier.
The Administrator with the District Treasury Officer shall reconcile the balances under the VPDA
at the end of every calendar month.
The balances shall be reconciled with the AG office and the Treasury officer shall submit Balance
Certificate to A.G. office for confirmation of their balances in the books of A.G. at the end of the
