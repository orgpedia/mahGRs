In order to avoid delay in submission of post-mortem viscera test reports, it has been decided to divide the police stations on the lines of district-wise medical colleges and to dispose of 6500 viscera samples pending at Sir J.G. Group of Hospitals, Mumbai in the next 3 to 6 months. 1 The above was done in pursuance of the Government Decision dated 22.12.2020. In this government decision, police stations were divided like district-wise medical college to avoid delay in submitting the report of viscera examination. However, it does not include the 17 police stations under the jurisdiction of the Commissionerate of Railway Police. Therefore, the Commissioner of Police, Railway Police requested to include the above police stations in Reference No. 3 dated 01.02.2021. Accordingly, the inclusion of the above 17 police stations was under the consideration of the government.
