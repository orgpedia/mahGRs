Sanvayshivam/ Vehicle Rental/ Shavaim, Alibag/ 2021-22/2254, Dy. 15/03/2022 Proposal:
The process of starting the newly established Government Medical College and its attached 500-bed hospital at Alibaug under the control of the department is going on at a fast pace and the concerned dean has to attend frequent meetings within the district as well as in Mumbai for important work related to the college and hospital. Therefore, as the vehicle is essential for the day-to-day official work of the Dean, the General Administration Department was under consideration of the Government to allow the Government to hire 01 private vehicle from a Government approved supplier in the prescribed procedure as per the provision of Government Decision dated 10/08/2020. Following this, the government took decision:
Government's decision:
Directorate Reference No. 6 As per the letter mentioned herein, the Dean, Government Medical College and Hospital, Alibaug, has been transferred for day-to-day administration in terms of Reference No. Approval is being given to hire any one of the vehicles Hyundai Accent/ Maruti Suzuki Dzire/ Honda Amaze on lease basis in the prescribed manner as per the provisions of Government Order and Government Letter respectively mentioned herein, subject to the condition that the proposal for purchase of new vehicle for the Institute shall be submitted to the Government immediately.
