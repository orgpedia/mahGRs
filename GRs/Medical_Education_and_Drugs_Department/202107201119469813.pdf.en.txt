Government Medical College and Hospital
The renaming of Sarvacharan Hospital, Latur as" Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Institute of Medical Sciences, Latur" has been approved vide the relevant Government Resolution dated 20.02.2020. Due to the above nomenclature, the general public does not understand that this institution is a government institution related to medical education. Also, as per the term of National Medical Commission, it is mandatory to address the medical college. Therefore, in order to bring uniformity in the names of all the institutions under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research in the future, the government was considering changing the name of the institution" Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Institute of Medical Sciences, Latur." The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Institute of Medical Sciences, Latur under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research is being renamed as follows.
Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Medical College, Latur.
Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Medical College and Hospital, Latur.
Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Medical College and Hospital, Latur.
