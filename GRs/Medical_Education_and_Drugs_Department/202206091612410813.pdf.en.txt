The annual expenditure of Rs. 13,12,87, 000/- (Rupees Thirteen Crores Twelve Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand only) as per the rates agreed with Sunil Traders, Dhule is being approved subject to the following conditions.
(i) The Directorate shall take into account the number of in-patients in the last three years institution-wise and the expenditure incurred on foodgrains, vegetables, fruits for providing food to the patients.
2) If the expenditure is more than the budgeted amount after considering the institution-wise expenditure, the government should approve the increased expenditure.
(3) The Dean of the institution concerned shall be fully responsible for ensuring regular availability of food to the in-patients in the hospitals under the Directorate.
4) According to the agreement made in the case. The Director, Medical Education and Research should ensure that Sunil Traders, Dhule is regularly supplying foodgrains, vegetables and fruits to the hospitals under the Directorate.
