Government's decision:
The Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government under the above mentioned letter dated 08.03.2021 for the purchase of common type of machinery and equipment required by the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department, institution wise, from the grants sanctioned under the District Annual and State Plan.
As per the proposal of the Directorate, the administrative approval is being granted to the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department to purchase machinery and equipment from the funds available under the District Annual Plan and the State Plan from the total amount of Rs.9, 35, 42, 360/- (Rupees Nine Crores, Fifty Five Lakhs, Forty Two Thousand, Three Hundred and Sixty only) mentioned in Annexure A from the institutionally sanctioned funds, subject to the following conditions.
Conditions: 1. The said administrative approval is being provided to the concerned institution under the District Annual Plan as well as from the grants made available under the State Plan.
(2) Care should be taken to ensure that the procurement of machinery is prioritized in proportion to the actual disbursement of funds out of the amount budgeted in the respective head of accounts in this regard.
The Directorate and the concerned Dean shall ensure that no new design, construction and electrification is required to handle the said machinery.
In this regard, the procedures mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedures for the Procurement of Offices by the Government Department" should be followed.
5. Procedure for Procurement of the said equipment" Procurement of drugs, related consumables and medical devices Haffkine Bio-Pharmaceuticals Corporation Ltd. " Issued Government Decision, Department of Public Health, Purchase No. 2016/ PR No. 240/ Health-8, dated 26.07.2017.
