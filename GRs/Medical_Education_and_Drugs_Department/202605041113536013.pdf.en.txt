The Government's Decisions:
To further strengthen the medical education system, nursing services and healthcare delivery system in the state, it has been decided to implement the project "Strengthening Tertiary Healthcare Delivery, Medical Education System & Nursing System in Maharashtra (I)" by providing loans through Japan International Co-operative Agency (ICA) as follows:
1) A loan of ₹3,708 crore is being approved from Japan International Co-operative Agency (JICA) to implement the project "Strengthening Tertiary Healthcare Delivery, Medical Education System & Nursing System in Maharashtra (I)" in the state.
2) The project cost in this case is ₹4635 crore, of which JICA will have a share of ₹3708 crore (80 per cent) and the state share will be ₹927 crore (20 per cent). The interest and other charges for this loan, as well as the duration of the loan, will be as follows:
1. Interest on loan amount directly used for project components - 2.70 XO Yo 2. Interest on loan amount used for project advisory services - 0.820 Yo 3. Front-end charge on loan amount - 0.2% (one time payment) 4. If the project is completed within the stipulated period, 0.9% of this front-end charge will be refunded immediately.
