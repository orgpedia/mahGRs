GOVERNMENT DECISION: Administrative approval is being accorded to the construction budget of Rs. 97, 88, 127/- (Rupees Ninety Seven Lakhs, Eighty Eight Thousand, One Hundred and Twenty Seven only) for the renovation of the auditorium and committee room of the Government Dental College and Hospital, Mumbai under the control of the Department subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The ownership of the land should be fulfilled before the work is done.
2) If the work falls under the jurisdiction of the Archaeological Department, prior permission of the concerned department as well as the Municipal Corporation and similar authorities should be taken.
3) While giving technical approval, the Government Circular No. 2017/ PR No. 11/ Planning-3, dated 11.04.2017 should be taken for the settlement of non-state tariff matters.
The suggestions issued under the Government Decision dated 24.08.2017 of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour should be considered by adopting the e-tendering system for procurement related matters in the proposal.
5) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No.177/ T. Action should be taken as per the guidelines mentioned in No. 1, dated January 10, 2014.
6) The electrification and other ancillary works should be properly planned and completed during the construction period itself.
