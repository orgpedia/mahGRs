Proposal for symbolic supplementary demand with grant for implementing the objectives required for the Government Medical College and Hospital, Satara, Directorate of Medical Education and Research vide Reference No. The above was submitted to the Government vide letter dated 16.06.2021. The proposal to approve the supplementary demand for this institution was presented in the Monsoon Session 2021. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 9,15,61, 000/- has been sanctioned through Supplementary Demand under the required target for Government Medical College and Hospital, Satara. The Finance Department was requested to disburse the funds sanctioned through the above Supplementary Demands. Accordingly, the Finance Department has sanctioned Rs. 9,15,61, 000/- out of which Rs. 1,53,45, 500 has been made available for distribution. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of this fund to the concerned institution was under the consideration of the government.
