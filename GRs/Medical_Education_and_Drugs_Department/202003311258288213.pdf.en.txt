The Government Medical/ Dental/ Ayurveda Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department are taken up on a turn key basis through Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) or similar companies at a budgetary cost of Rs.25 crore and above. As per the Government Decision dated 19.05.2018, a new head of accounts has been created with the targets of Demand No. S-04, 4210 2625, 53-Big Construction. In the year 2019-2020, a total amount of Rs. 150.00 crore was budgeted.
At present, the construction of Government Medical College and Hospital, Chandrapur is underway under this title. Under this head, Rs 315 crore was allocated for the construction of Government Medical College and Hospital, Chandrapur in the June-2019 session.
In this regard, out of the balance amount of Rs. 93,31,84, 666/- (Rupees Ninety Three Crores, Thirty One Lakhs, Forty Four Thousand, Six Hundred and Sixty Six only), the funds under the head Demand No. S-04, 4210 162952-Machinery and Equipment under the control of the Department were under consideration to be made available by way of redeployment for purchase of necessary machinery and equipment to the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department.
