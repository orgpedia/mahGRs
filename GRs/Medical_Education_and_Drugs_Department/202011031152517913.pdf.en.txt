Conditions:
(1) The requirement/ requisition of such medicinal and surgical materials, chemicals and equipments etc. shall be examined/ verified by the Directorate.
(2) It shall be ensured that the specifications of the said medicinal and surgical materials, chemicals and equipment etc. are comprehensive and comprehensive in such a manner that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the said items.
3) In this regard, the Government Decision No. Bhankhas-2014/ Q. S82/ Part/ Industry-4, issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, titled" Manual of Procedures for Office Purchase to be made by the Government Department," has been published. The prescribed procedure as mentioned in 01.12.2016 should be followed.
4) It should be ensured that the items proposed for purchase are not left unused and are used within the prescribed time.
5) Ensure that there is no duplication of purchases.
(6) Further necessary action should be taken immediately to obtain the said medicine and related matters from Haffkine Jeeva Pharmaceuticals Corporation as per Government Decision, Public Health Department, dated 26.07.2017.
(7) The Director of Medical Education, Mumbai has issued a notification to this effect in respect of the procurement matters mentioned in the prospectus/ table of the said Government Decision. The detailed information attached with the letter dated 01.10.2020 should be made available to Haffkine Corporation immediately.
8) Under no circumstances should be spent more than the limit of available funds.
The concerned agencies should immediately transfer the entire sanctioned funds to Haffkine Corporation for the purchase of drugs and related items under the Material and Supply Objectives for the financial year 2020-21 (21).
A fresh tender process for procurement of drugs and allied items under the Material and Supply Objective (21) for the financial year 2020-21 should be undertaken.
