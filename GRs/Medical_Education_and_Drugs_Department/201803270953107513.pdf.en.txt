Government's decision:
State share is provided to Kasturba Health Society, Sevagram, Wardha as ancillary grant amounting to 25% of the total cost. A sum of Rs. 28,40,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Eight Crores, Forty Lakhs only) has been budgeted for the financial year 2017-18 and the amount of Rs. 1 has been distributed here as per the government decision.
Further, for the financial year 2016-17, the Government of Maharashtra had budgeted and disbursed an amount of Rs. 32,42,06, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores, Forty Two Lakhs Six Thousand only) to the Institute.
The report submitted by the Director of Medical Education states that the state government owes a total of Rs 4.77 crore, which was Rs 0.74 crore in 2016-17 and Rs 4.03 crore in 2017-18. The said amount of Rs. 4,77,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs only) payable to the Institute has been provided by the Finance Department in the Revised Budget. The government has sanctioned the amount. This amount is within the proposed cash flow limit for 2017-18.
The grant of the said grant-in-aid is provisional and is being sanctioned subject to recovery from the institution of excess amount of grant-in-aid over and above the cost of final adjustment on the basis of actual expenditure incurred during the financial year 2017-18 and the terms and conditions of the statement A attached herewith. The utility certificate of the institution should be submitted to the government within 30 days.
