circular
The Election Commission of India undertakes a special summary revision programme every year to verify and rectify the errors in the existing electoral rolls. The programme of updating the electoral roll by registering new voters as per the eligibility date of January 1 is being implemented. Special Summary Revision Programme to be launched in 2023. The programme is being organized to revise the electoral roll by registering new voters who will complete 18 years of age on January 1, 2023. From 2023, citizens who complete 18 years of age on or before the four qualifying dates of January 1, April 1, July 1 and October 1 will be able to register in advance in the electoral roll. Under this special brief review programme, The draft electoral rolls for all the Assembly Constituencies of the State will be published on 09.11.2022. 09.11.2022 to D. Claims and objections will be accepted from 08.12.2022. Applications can be submitted after 09.11.2022.
The Additional Chief Secretary and Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra State have been requested to take up the initiative of enrolling the students of all the health science courses of Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Deemed Universities, AIIMS as voters in the electoral roll under the Special Summary Revision Programme, 2023.
In pursuance of the Special Summary Revision Programme of the Electoral Roll, the following activities should be undertaken by the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Deemed Universities, Colleges of all Health Science Courses at AIIMS: 1. The University and the college students under it should be informed about the Special Summary Revision Programme, 2023 by the Electoral Literacy Board.
2. Election through Literacy Board. 09.11.2022 to D. Applications for enrolment as a new voter for a student who is completing 18 years of age as on 01.01.2023 during the period 08.12.2022 should be filled.
The students who are going to complete 18 years of age by 01.10.2023 should fill the application form for the new voter registration in advance.
