Terms and Conditions:
1) As per the Government decision dated 26.07.2017 of the Public Health Department, it has been made mandatory to purchase the medicinal and surgical materials, chemicals and equipment etc. required by the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals and Institutions under the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals from the Haffkine Jeeva Drug Manufacturing Corporation. It should be purchased.
(ii) The requirement/ demand for the said medicinal and surgical materials, chemicals and equipments etc. has been ascertained by the Directorate.
It has been ensured that the specifications of the said medicinal and surgical materials, chemicals and equipment etc. are comprehensive and comprehensive, so that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the same.
4) In this regard, the Government Decision No. 2014/ Q. 82/ Part/ Industry-4, issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, titled" Manual of Procedures for Office Procurement to be made by the Government Department." The prescribed procedure as mentioned in the Government Order dated 01.12.2016 and the corresponding Government Decision should be followed.
5) Procurement of these items should be done through GeM portal/ Mahatender.
6) It should be ensured that the items proposed for purchase are not left unused and are used within the prescribed time.
The technical specifications of the said items are required to be certified by the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai from the National Institute of Virology, Pune (NIV)/ National Institute of Reproductive Health Research, Mumbai (NIRRH).
8) It should be ensured that there is no duplication of purchase matters.
9) The Director of Medical Education should immediately report the demand for urgent matters to the Haffkine Corporation. Also, the demand for items required every month should be registered with Haffkine Corporation at least 15 days in advance.

10) Haffkine Corporation shall issue supply orders on a monthly basis as per the demand received per month from the Director, Medical Education and Research, in respect of the above items, without placing supply orders at the same time to the supplier identified by GeM/ Mahatender for the purchase of the same.
11) Necessary further action should be taken immediately to obtain the said medicine and related matters from Haffkine Biopharmaceutical Corporation.
Under no circumstances should the expenditure be more than the limit of available funds.
13) The Director, Medical Education, Mumbai, in its revised estimates for the year 2020-21, under the head 2210-3314, has sanctioned Rs. 100.00 crore should be immediately transferred to Haffkine Corporation.
14) Haffkine Corporation should publish the tender every time considering the stock of test kits available and the expiry date.
The Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai and the Joint Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research (Procurement Cell), Mumbai shall be solely responsible for ensuring that the conditions mentioned in the above Annexure 1 to 14 are fulfilled and the procurement is as per the rules and requirements.
The expenditure for the purchase of the above items should be met from the next head of account of the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai.
