The Proposal:
A total area of 21.10 ha has been transferred at Mauje Usar for the construction of the newly started Government Medical College and Hospital under the control of the department, 500-bed hospital attached to Alibaug and ancillary buildings. The matter of granting administrative approval to the budget of the planned government medical college and hospital as well as the ancillary building was under the consideration of the government.
The government has decided to build a 100-student government medical college and a 500-bed attached hospital in Alibaug under the control of the department. In accordance with the decision taken by the High Level Committee, administrative approval is being accorded to the budget of Rs. 406,96,68, 336/- (Rupees Four Hundred and Six Crores, Ninety Five Lakhs, Sixty Eight Thousand, Three Hundred and Thirty Six only) subject to the following conditions.
