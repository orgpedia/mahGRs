A special task force will be set up to review the progress of ambitious projects in government medical colleges every month.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Medical Education and Drugs,
Government Circular No: VIP-2018/ PR No.40/ PRISA-1
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 7th of April 2018
Government Circular: A Special Task Force is being constituted under the control of the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai to review the construction and other administrative approvals of the ambitious projects in the Government Medical Colleges under the control of the Department every month and to check their progress from the government level.
