About the College: The college is affiliated to Maharashtra Medical Education and Research Centre, Pune and offers Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) courses. The college is affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik and the sanctioned student strength of the college is 60 and the sanctioned hospital bed strength is 150.
In the financial year 2003-04, the Government has decided to sanction 100% subsidy for the salaries of private Government aided Ayurveda and Unani institutions in the State from the month of January, 2004.
Accordingly, the Private Government Funded Zulekhabai Wali Mohammad Unani College, Pune has received Rs. 2,94,374/- (Rupees Two Lakh Ninety Four Thousand Three Hundred and Seventy Four only).
The state government has decided to provide grants to Ayurveda and Unani colleges and affiliated hospitals run by private institutions. The Government has made 100% of the salary amount subsidized for the salary expenditure of teachers and non-teaching staff from January 2004.
Accordingly, the said details and Assistant Director AYUSH, on physical verification, informed the Directorate of AYUSH that as per the assessment of the financial year 2017-2018, there is no grant-in-aid or recovery of grant-in-aid to Zulekhabai Wali Mohammad Unani College, Pune.
