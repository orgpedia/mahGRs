Preamble: Administrative approval has been accorded for procurement of testing kits for COVID-19 from Haffkine Corporation for an amount of Rs. 101.76 crore vide Government Decision dated 05.03.2021. The Government decision for this administrative approval has mentioned an approximate rate of Rs. 13.47/- for 60,00,000 pieces of VTM Kits, for which an amount of Rs. 8,08,20, 000/- has been approved. Haffkine Biopharmaceuticals Limited, Mumbai had floated a tender E-3432 for the supply of COVID-19 drug. As there was an increase in the rate for VTM Kits in the tender, the matter of giving revised approval was under the consideration of the government as per the proposal submitted by the Director, Medical Education and Research.
