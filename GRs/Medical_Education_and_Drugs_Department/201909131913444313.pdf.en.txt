Conditions:
1) The administrative approval is being provided from the grants available with the respective institutions for the purchase of the said equipment.
(2) It is confirmed by the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai and the competent authority of the concerned institution that the said equipment is of single source nature.
If anything happens in the future, it will be the responsibility of all concerned.
(3) The concerned have certified that the machinery is proprietary in nature, so the Haffkine Corporation should take action to purchase the machinery by checking whether the machinery is proprietary in nature or how and by checking the updated and prescribed certificate in this regard.
4) It will be necessary to show the different prices of the main machine and its accessories.
5. Procedure for procurement of the said equipment" Procurement of drugs, related consumables and medical devices Haffkine Bio-Pharmaceuticals Corporation Ltd. The Government Decision issued in this regard should be made in accordance with the Public Health Department, Purchase No. 2016/ PR No. 240/ Health-8, dated 26.07.2017.
(6) The Director shall ensure that the proposed machinery is not included in any other proposal and that the machinery is required by the institution concerned.
7) No new construction or design is required for this equipment.
8) While taking a decision on the purchase of machinery in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of subsidy sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
9) The State Level Procurement Committee shall comply with the provisions of paragraph No. 3.1.2.1 titled" Price Estimation and Price Reasonableness" in the aforesaid Government Decision, Department of Industry, Energy and Labour& Labour, dated 01.12.2016. The price of the machine complying with this provision shall be subject to revised administrative approval as per requirement.
