Government's decision:
Construction, machinery and electrical works required for setting up of Liver Transplant Cell at St. George's Hospital, Mumbai under the control of the Department The proposal submitted by the Directorate with a total amount of Rs. 4,30,24, 084/- (Rupees Four Crores, Thirty Lakhs, Twenty Four Thousand, Eighty Four only) mentioned in the following statement is being accorded administrative approval on turn key basis subject to the following terms and conditions. It is also being approved to allocate funds from the funds sanctioned annually to the institute as per the requirement for purchase of medicines and consumables.

Conditions: 1) The said administrative approval is being provided to the institution concerned in the annual year 2023-24 from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme.
2) If the construction is under the jurisdiction of the Archaeological Department, the prior permission of the concerned department as well as the Municipal Corporation and similar authorities should be obtained.
3) While giving technical approval, the Government Circular No. 2017/ PR No. 11/ Planning-3, dated 11.04.2017 should be taken for the settlement of non-state tariff matters.
4) The suggestions issued by the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour under the Government Decision dated 24.08.2017, adopting the e-tendering system for the procurement related matters in the proposal should be taken into consideration.
