The Proposal: -
The Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance, Government of India has decided to propose a fund flow mechanism "SNA-SPARSH" (Timely System Integrated Quick Transfer -] 208] 1 Time System of Integrated Quick Transfer) to disburse funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes through an integrated architecture of Public Financial Management System (PFMS), State Integrated Financial Management Information System (State FMIS) and Reserve Bank of India's e-Kuber system to achieve greater efficiency in cash management at both Central and State levels. 1 is taken from the memorandum here. Also, the Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance, Government of India, vide Reference No. Under the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU), it has been decided to include 37 more Centrally Sponsored Schemes under SNA SPARSH. These include the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of "Strengthening of States Drug Regulatory System" being implemented by the Department of Food and Drug Administration. As per the direction of the Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance, Government of India, the issue of approval to open a State Linked Schemes (SLS) -wise separate Drawing Account with the Reserve Bank of India in pursuance of implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes under SNA SPARSH was under consideration of the Government.
