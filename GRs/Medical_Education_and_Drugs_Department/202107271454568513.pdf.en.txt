Government's decision:
The administrative sanction for the construction of the administrative building in the premises of Sir J.G. Group of Hospitals, Mumbai under the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals was accorded vide Government Resolution, dated 31.10.2008 for an amount of Rs. 18,48,11, 500/-. The change in the foundation of the ground+ 7 floor building of the hospital, the increase in the price as per the recommendation of the Heritage Committee, the increase in the price due to the change in concept, the tender received at a higher rate, the increase in price and the lack of provision for centage charges have increased the budget price. As per the proposal received by the Department, revised administrative approval is being accorded for the construction of administrative building in Sir J.G. Group of Hospitals, Mumbai premises at an estimated cost of Rs.27, 18,70,389/- (Rupees Twenty Seven Crores, Eighteen Lakhs, Seventy Thousand, Three Hundred and Ninety Nine only).
