Government's decision:
Students are required to stay in the premises of Dr. Shankarrao Chavan Government Medical College, Nanded Vishnupuri, which is under the control of the department, for academic work, service in the Inter-Patient Department and Accident Department. As there is no children's hostel in the Vishnupuri area, the construction of the hostel is going on. In accordance with the decision taken by the High Powered Committee, administrative approval is being accorded to the budget of Rs. 18.86 crore subject to the following conditions.
