The Proposal:
The health services are provided in the Savner area through the Health Team, Savner, affiliated to the Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur Institute, under the control of the Department of Medical Education and Medicines. Two national highways pass close to the hospital. Accident victims are referred to the Government Medical College and Hospital in Nagpur for advanced treatment. A proposal to set up a 10-bed trauma care centre at Health Team, Saoner, was under consideration to provide immediate medical facilities to the accident victims.
Government's decision:
Administrative approval is being granted to start a 10-bed Trauma Care Centre (Level-III) at Health Team, Saoner attached to Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur Institute under the control of Medical Education and Drugs Department.
The non-recurring and recurring expenditure for the said Trauma Care Centre will be met from the respective head of accounts of the State Scheme.
This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the feedback given by the Planning Department under informal reference No. 43/ 1475 dated 31.01.2020 and by the Finance Department under informal reference No. 93/ 2020/ Expenditure-13 dated 18.02.2020.
