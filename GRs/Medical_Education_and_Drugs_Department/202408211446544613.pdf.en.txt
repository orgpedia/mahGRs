The Proposal:
The work on the underground sewerage scheme at Government Medical College and Hospital, Miraj of the department was accorded administrative sanction with a budget of Rs. 11.20 crores as per the Government Decision dated 13.02.2023. The change in GST of the underground sewerage scheme, ETP charges and saving in tariff led to an increase in the proposal from 40 to 50 per cent over the administrative sanction price, according to Reference No. The government was considering scrapping the administrative sanction here and approving a new construction budget.
The government's decision:
According to the Public Works Department (PWD), the change in GST, ETP charges and tariff savings on the underground sewerage scheme at Government Medical College and Hospital, Miraj under the control of the department have resulted in an increase of 40 to 50 per cent over the administrative sanction price and the work is yet to start. The new construction budget of Rs. 14,51,78, 200 / - (Rupees Fourteen Crores, One Hundred and Fifty Lakh, Seventy-eight Thousand, Two Hundred Only) is being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
