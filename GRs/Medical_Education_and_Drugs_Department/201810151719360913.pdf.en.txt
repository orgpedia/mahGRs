Government's decision
As per the policy decided by the Central Government, the Government is approving the establishment of Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratory (VRDL) at the state level and medical college level laboratories in the state of Maharashtra.
As per the existing policy of the Department of Health Research, Department of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India and the Indian Council of Medical Research, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India, under the 12th Five Year Plan Scheme on" Establishment of Network of Laboratories for Managing Epidemic and Natural Calamities for Implementation," approval is being accorded for setting up of a state level laboratory of Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratory (VRDL) at Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur and for signing of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) accordingly.
