The government's decision:
According to the Indian Council of Medical Sciences standard, the Government Medical College and Hospital, Nandurbar does not have an adequate staff of nurses and the hospital is required to have highly trained nursing staff. The creation of this nursing college is going to help in the availability of educated nursing staff. This requires the construction of a new nursing college as well as other ancillary buildings. The construction budget of Rs. 9,99,94, 995 / - (Rupees Nine Crore Ninety Nine Lakh AMOI One Thousand Nine Hundred and Ninety Five Only) is being accorded administrative sanction for the construction subject to the following conditions.
