GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the aforesaid Government Decision dated 10.11.2017, Government Medical College and Cancer Hospital, Aurangabad was granted approval for procurement of equipment for Cardiovascular Ultrasound Systems (2D Echo) from Central/ State share funds. Request for grant of administrative approval for the revised price of the said machinery has been made by the Directorate vide letter dated 09.03.2021.
In accordance with the proposal of the Directorate, the Government Medical College and Cancer Hospital, Aurangabad has been allotted the equipment for the purchase of" Cardiovascular Ultrasound Systems (2D Echo)" instead of the price mentioned in the above Government Decision dated 10.11.2017.
