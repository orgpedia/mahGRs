Government's decision:
As per the Government decision dated 26.07.2017 of the Public Health Department, it has been made mandatory to purchase the purchase of medicinal and surgical materials, chemicals and equipment etc. for the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals and affiliated institutions under the Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department from the Haffkine Jeeva Drug Manufacturing Corporation.
As per the provisions of the said Government Resolution, the funds received by the Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department for medicines, equipment and tools etc. have been transferred to Haffkine Corporation on payment of a lump sum. The details of these funds have not yet been received by the concerned agencies. The Government Medical/ Dental Colleges and Hospitals and affiliated institutions under the control of the Sub-Division are being granted approval to transfer the funds to Haffkine Jeeva Drug Manufacturing Corporation, Mumbai Purchase Cell on short payment of the amount of administrative sanction out of the funds received/ to be received for the purchase of drugs and materials and machinery and equipment through various sources in the year 2020-21 till March, 2021.
