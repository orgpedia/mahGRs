Introduction: Government Ayurveda Hospital, Nagpur is established in the border area of Nagpur city. There are many tourist places here. Due to this, the number of tourists in Nagpur is increasing. In view of this, to provide special facilities to tourists and to increase the revenue of the government, a new building is required to start paid Panchakarma in the hospital premises. 10,00,000/- was under consideration of the Government for grant of administrative approval.
Government Decision: Government Ayurveda Hospital, Nagpur has been sanctioned Rs. 25.00 lakh has been sanctioned and Rs. 10.00 lakhs is reserved. Therefore, administrative approval is being given for an amount of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh only) for the construction of temporary budget and plan at Ward No. 3 for starting paid special Panchakarma at Government Ayurveda Hospital, Nagpur, which is important from the point of view of academic and patient welfare.
