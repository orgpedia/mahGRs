The Proposal:
Government Medical College and Hospital, Alibag with a capacity of 100 students has started functioning from the year 2021-22. At present, the college is in the process of admitting 100 students in the third year of 2023-24 and the District Hospital, Alibag has been affiliated to the college. Accordingly, the building of the nurse's residence in the district government hospital has been given to the medical college for use. Since the institution is newly established and does not have its own buildings, the students of the college as well as intern doctors, junior and senior residential services and nurses will be able to stay on the hospital premises and provide immediate patient care. Also, as the building is completely dilapidated and lying in a state of disuse, the structural audit report of the building requires demolition and construction of a new building. According to reference no. 01 The Government has decided to provide Rs. 3,62,57, 626/- was under consideration of the Government for grant of administrative approval to the fresh construction proposal as requested by the Directorate of Medical Education and Research.
