The Proposal:
The Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur has been provided administrative approval for the installation of High Energy Linear Accelerator, IMRT, IGIT, SRS, SRT, SBRT with Accessories and Turn Key by the Government vide Government Order dated 28.03.2018 from the grants sanctioned under the Special Component Plan and Supplementary Grants from Tribal Utilities for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes respectively under the control of Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance and Tribal Development Department.
Accordingly, the funds received for the purchase of the proposed machinery amounting to Rs. 23.20 crore were transferred to Haffkine Corporation. Since there was no response from Haffkine Corporation to the tender process for the purchase of this machine and since the purchase had not been made even after more than one year of the said administrative approval, the matter of approval of the proposal submitted by the Directorate under the above mentioned letter dated 13.06.2022 for the revised administrative approval was under the consideration of the Government.
