In case of sending the sample 18 by post, the proof/ documents indicating the educational qualification concerned must be attested by the Designated Officer/ Assistant Designated Officer/ Gazetted Officer of the concerned district/ Notary Public.
(a) The Teachers' Association
As the electoral rolls are being prepared for the Teachers Legislative Council Constituency in the Mumbai and Nashik Division, the State Government, under the provisions of Section 27 (3) (b) of the Representation of the People Act, 1950, may, with the approval of the Election Commission, allot seats in educational institutions not lower than secondary school to the eligible candidates. Teachers who have been working as teachers for at least 3 years out of the six years immediately preceding the qualifying date of 01.11.2023 should be encouraged to enrol themselves in the electoral rolls of the respective Teachers Constituencies.
