Preamble: Reference No. 2 As per the Government decision dated 31.01.2012, approval has been given to start a Government Medical College with a capacity of 100 students and an attached 500-bed hospital at Satara. Also Reference No. 4 As per the Government decision dated 03.01.2022, approval has been accorded for admission of 100 students for the academic year 2021-22. The government was considering providing security guards through external sources for the safety of students, faculty and other government employees on the premises of the government medical college.
Government's decision:
For the safety of students, faculty members and other government employees admitted to the Government Medical College, Satara, 42 security guards (41 security guards and 1 lady security supervisor) will be provided by the Maharashtra Ex-Servicemen Corporation (MESCO), Pune. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 1,38,43, 950/- (Rupees One Crore Thirty Eight Lakh Forty Three Thousand Nine Hundred and Fifty only).
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the financial powers vested in the Administrative Department under the Finance Department, Government Decision dated 17.04.2015, Fiscal Powers Rules Part I, Sub-Division-4, No. 11.
