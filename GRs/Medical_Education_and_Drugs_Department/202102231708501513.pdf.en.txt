Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid Government Decisions dated 03.01.2019 and 18.03.2020, the Government Medical College, Miraj and Dr. Vaishyapayan Memorial Government Medical College, Solapur, under the control of the Department, respectively, were granted administrative approval to procure equipment for the Virus Research and Diagnostic Laboratory from the funds received from the Central Government. In pursuance of the said administrative approval, the revised prices of the below-mentioned machinery purchased from Haffkine Corporation are being approved by the said Government Resolution subject to the following conditions.
