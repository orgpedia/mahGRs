Government Resolution dated 07.03.2019 and dated 14.06.2019
Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 07.03.2019 of the above schedule of the Department, approval has been accorded for procurement of turn key machinery required for Government Medical/ Dental Colleges and Hospitals under the control of Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals through Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals.
Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai was appointed as ex-officio member secretary of the committee.
However, since then, vide Government Order dated 06/10/2021 of the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals, the post of Commissioner, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai has been created at the level of Directorate of Medical Education and Research and he has been declared as Regional Head at the zonal level and has been entrusted with the administrative and financial powers.
Accordingly, it was considered at the Government level to include Commissioner, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai as ex-officio Member Secretary in the said Committee in place of Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai.
Government's stand:
