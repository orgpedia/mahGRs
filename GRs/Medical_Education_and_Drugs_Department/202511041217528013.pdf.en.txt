Preamble: The Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, vide letter dated 07.10.2025 had requested the Government for administrative approval for installation of Kadiak Hass Unit, Anesthesia Workstation, Eco Machine (with all probes), TACT Machine and other allied equipment in 11 Government Medical Colleges and Allied Hospitals in the State on Public-Private Partnership (PPP) basis. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the establishment of Kadiyaq Cathlamb on PPP basis in 11 Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the Directorate.
Government Decision- Administrative approval is being accorded for the implementation of the tender process for installation of Kadiak Cathlab units, Anesthesia Workstations, Eco Machines and ACT Machines and Ancillary Equipment on Public-Private Partnership (PPP) basis in 11 Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals in the State subject to the following conditions and conditions.
