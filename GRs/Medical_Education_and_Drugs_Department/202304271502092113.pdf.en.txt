Preamble: Government Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 23.07.2015, Government Medical College with intake capacity of 100 students has been approved at Chandrapur. The government was considering allowing the students admitted to the college to deposit their educational fees and other fees, security reserve amount, gymkhana fee, development fund, hostel fee, etc., and to open their own provident fund account to make allowable expenditure from it.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The funds received from the residential/ non-residential students of Government Medical College, Chandrapur through educational fees, reserve amount, cushion money, library fees, hostel fees, scholarships, grants for university examinations and other student fees will be used for the purpose of utilization and reference no. 2 The Government hereby approve the opening of a Personal Loan Account (PLA) in the name of the Dean, Government Medical College, Chandrapur, as per the provisions of Rule 494 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, Clause-1, for expenditure for the matters prescribed under the Government Decision dated 04.03.2020.
2.It will be mandatory to follow the guidelines and financial rules set by the government from time to time while using the funds deposited in this account. It will also be necessary to comply with the provisions of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules.
