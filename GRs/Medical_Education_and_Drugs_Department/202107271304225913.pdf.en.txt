Introduction: The protective wall of the premises of Government Medical College and Hospital, Aurangabad is very old and dilapidated. The hospital caters to children, adults and senior citizens. The patients are also facing inconvenience due to the frequent traffic jams. Therefore, it was necessary to construct a new retaining wall in the RCC and the matter of giving administrative approval to the construction budget was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The compound wall of the Government Medical College and Hospital, Aurangabad, is very old and dilapidated. The hospital caters to children, adults and senior citizens. The patients are also facing inconvenience due to the frequent traffic jams. Therefore, the construction of the new proposed retaining wall in the RCC is very necessary. Administrative sanction for the construction of the retaining wall for an amount of Rs. 3,86,85, 557/- (Rupees Three Crores, Sixty Eight Lakhs, Eighty Five Thousand, Five Hundred and Fifty Seven only) is being accorded subject to the following terms and conditions.
