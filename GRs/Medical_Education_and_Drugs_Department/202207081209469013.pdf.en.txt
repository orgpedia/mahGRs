Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid letter dated 03/06/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for procurement of machinery and equipment to various Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department. In pursuance of the said proposal, administrative approval is being accorded to purchase of machinery and equipment in Annexure-A and B attached with the said Government Decision for the financial year 2022-23 from the grants sanctioned under the State Plan for various institutions under the Department at a cost of Rs. 63,38,26, 643/- (Rupees Thirty-three crore, Thirty-eight lakh, Twenty-six thousand, Six hundred and forty three only) subject to the following conditions.
Conditions:
(i) The said administrative approval is being provided subject to availability of funds to the institution concerned from the grants sanctioned under the State Plan.
2) Government Decisions issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedures for the Purchase of Office by the Government Department" in this regard

The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall certify that the specifications of the said equipment are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the equipment accordingly.

8) It will be necessary to show the different prices of the main machine and its accessories.
9) While taking a decision on the purchase of machinery and equipment in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of subsidy sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
10) The State Level Procurement Committee shall comply with the provisions of the above Government Decision, Paragraph No. 3.1.2 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, dated 01.12.2016 under the heading" Price Estimation and Price Reasonableness."
The cost of machinery and equipment complying with this provision shall be subject to revised administrative approval as required.
