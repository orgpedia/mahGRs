Government's decision:
In pursuance of the aforesaid letter dated 14.07.2023, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of revised administrative approval for the purchase of furniture material required for the hostel accommodation of first year MBBS admitted students to meet the standards of National Medical Commission, New Delhi, to the newly established Government Medical College, Parbhani under the control of the Department.
Proposed furniture purchase reference no. 05 Administrative approval was granted earlier under the Government decision mentioned here. However, the institution has not been able to execute the procurement process of furniture material for the financial year 2022-23 due to administrative reasons. Accordingly, vide reference No. Revoking the administrative sanction orders issued earlier under section 05, the Government Medical College, Parbhani is being granted administrative sanction for the purchase of furniture material mentioned in the table below at a cost of Rs. 63,34,735/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakh, Thirty Four Thousand, Seven Hundred and Thirty Five only) during the financial year 2023-24 from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme, subject to the following conditions.
