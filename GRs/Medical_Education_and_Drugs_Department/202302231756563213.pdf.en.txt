The Proposal:
The Maharashtra government is committed to providing quality healthcare to the people of the state. Under the" Strengthening of Tertiary Health Care and Medical Education in Maharashtra" project, the state government is in the process of seeking$ 500 million in funding and other technical assistance from the Asian Development Bank (ADB) for the construction, operation and upgradation of medical colleges and affiliated hospitals in selected districts.
In pursuance of the said project, a meeting was held between the Secretary, Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals and the representative of Asian Development Bank. It has been decided to take a decision on the policy matters related to this project in the meeting held on 30.01.2023. Accordingly, decisions were taken regarding the action to be taken on various policy issues such as drug testing, HMIS system, opening of new accounting titles, setting up of a separate cell under the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for recruitment of candidates for the Medical Education Department, separate cell for infrastructure control, incentive allowance under the Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and the necessary control mechanism for the project.
3. To ensure proper utilization of funds available from Asian Development Bank for the project" Strengthening of Tertiary Health Care and Medical Education in Maharashtra." The detailed project outline is to be decided under the guidance of the Steering Committee headed by the Chief Secretary. The government was also considering setting up a Project Management Unit (PMU) under the chairmanship of Secretary, Medical Education and Drugs Department as an implementation mechanism to ensure completion of the project within the stipulated time frame.
Government's decision:
In line with the financial support being provided by the Asian Development Bank for the project Strengthening of Tertiary Health Care and Medical Education in Maharashtra for the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals, a Project Management Unit (PMU) is being set up as an implementation mechanism at the Government level as follows:
