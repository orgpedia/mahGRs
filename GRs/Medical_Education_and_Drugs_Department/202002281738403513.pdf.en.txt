GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the aforesaid letter dated 10.12.2019, the Directorate had received a budget of Rs. 12,63,25, 673/- (Rupees Twelve Crore Sixty Five Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand Six Hundred and Seventy Three only) for the construction of new multi-storied building (basement+ ground floor+ 11 storey) at Sassoon Sarvacharan Hospital, Pune (substation, exterior improvement, retaining wall, walkway, tensile fabric roof, medical gas pipeline system room, covered walkway etc.). This budget is being given administrative approval subject to the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions 1. Before starting the work, the ownership rights of the land should be fulfilled.
The proposed work should be carried out in accordance with the prevailing and prescribed procedures of the Public Works Department.
Special care should be taken to ensure that no revised administrative approval is required for the subject wall work.
The consent and approval of the Department of Medical Education and Drugs should be obtained at various stages of the work.
