Terms and Conditions: - No. This administrative approval shall be limited to making a contract only. The actual procurement, supply orders and disbursement of funds will be subject to budgetary provisions and prescribed procedures for the respective year.

The Maharashtra Medical Commodities Procurement Authority, Mumbai will be responsible for strictly following the guidelines issued by the Central / State Finance Department on procurement of goods (Manual for Procurement of Goods, Second Edition, 2024) and the guidelines issued by the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour, Government of India dated 09.92.2098 while executing the procurement process of the items to be contracted and purchased accordingly.
The guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) should be strictly followed while executing the tender process for the entire tender.
The Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Medical Commodities Procurement Authority, Mumbai, should carry out the tariff agreement process only after ensuring that the drugs to be contracted are of high quality and WHO-GMP / CDSCO certified.
Once the contract has been finalised, the Directorate of Medical Education and Research shall, within the limits of funds available under the contract, compile and submit to the Government for administrative approval, a complete proposal from its subordinate institutions seeking information on the required drugs, pharmaceuticals and related matters.
The technical specifications of the items to be contracted and purchased accordingly should be as per the specifications agreed upon by the respective experts (Technical Committee / Officials). Also, it should be ensured that the technical specification is not designed to benefit any particular company.
Pre / Post Dispatch Random Sample Inspection of the items to be contracted and purchased accordingly shall be carried out by the National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) rated laboratory in the prescribed manner to ensure that the drugs with the appropriate specifications are procured by the Maharashtra Medical Goods Procurement Authority, Mumbai and other stakeholders.
