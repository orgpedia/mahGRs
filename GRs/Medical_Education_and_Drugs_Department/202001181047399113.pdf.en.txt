Conditions:
(1) The said administrative approval is being granted to the concerned institution from the funds received from the Securities and Exchange Board of India Limited.
(2) It is confirmed by the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai and the competent authority of the concerned institution that the said equipment is of single source nature. If any matter arises in the future, the entire responsibility will be of the concerned agency and directorate.
3) The concerned has certified that the said equipment is proprietary in nature.
However, Haffkine Corporation shall ensure that there are no other products which meet the specifications specified for the proposed machinery.
(4) Upon confirmation as per the aforesaid Article No. 3, the Haffkine Corporation shall proceed with the procurement after verifying the updated and signed certificate of the proposed machinery being proprietary in nature.
5) It will be necessary to show the different prices of the main machine and its accessories.
6. Procedure for procurement of the said equipment" Procurement of drugs, related consumables and medical devices Haffkine Bio-Pharmaceuticals Corporation Ltd. The Government Decision issued in this regard should be made in accordance with the Public Health Department, Purchase No. 2016/ PR No. 240/ Health-8, dated 26.07.2017.
(7) The Director shall ensure that the proposed machinery is not included in any other proposal and that the machinery is required by the institution concerned.
8) No new construction or design is required for this equipment.
9) While taking a decision on the purchase of machinery in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of subsidy sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
10) The State Level Procurement Committee shall comply with the provisions of the above Government Decision, Paragraph No. 3.1.2 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour& Labour, dated 01.12.2016 under the heading" Price Estimation and Price Reasonableness."
The price of the machine complying with this provision shall be subject to revised administrative approval as per requirement.
