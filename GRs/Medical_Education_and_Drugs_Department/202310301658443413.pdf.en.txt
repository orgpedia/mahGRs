The Proposal:
Purchase of Cobalt-60 HDR Brachytherapy Machine required for Government Medical College and Cancer Hospital, Aurangabad was approved vide Government Decision dated 31.03.2018. The statutory validity of the administrative approval granted for the purchase of the said equipment has been more than one year and fresh revised orders are required to be issued.
The matter was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: A fresh revised administrative approval is being granted to the Government Medical College and Cancer Hospital, Aurangabad for the purchase of Cobalt 60 HDR Brachytherapy Machine of the following table value of Rs. 3,71,77, 800/- (Rupees Three Crores, Seventy One Lakhs, Seventy Seven Thousand, Eight Hundred only) subject to the following conditions.

Conditions: 1) All the terms and conditions for the purchase of the said equipment shall be as on 31st March, 2018 as per the schedule of Government.
The Government is approving to spend the additional amount of Rs. 71,77,800/- (Rupees One Hundred and Seventy Seven Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Eight Hundred only) out of the balance amount of Rs. 2,96,75, 145/- from the sanction fund for the Linear Accelerator purchased for the said Institute. Since the procurement process of this machine is going on with Haffkine Corporation, the additional funds required should be transferred to Haffkine Corporation immediately and the procurement process should be completed immediately.
