The Proposal:
The area of the Government Medical College and Hospital is very large and it is very important to improve the drainage. Due to poor drainage system, the garbage is hazardous to health. As a result, patients are suffering. It is very important to keep the area clean and the construction of drainage is very important. Since the drainage work in the area is very important, the matter of giving administrative approval to the budget for the construction of the drainage line was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The area of the Government Medical College and Hospital is very large and it is very important to improve the drainage here. Due to poor drainage system, the garbage is hazardous to health. As a result, patients are suffering. It is very important to keep the area clean and to build drainage quickly. Administrative approval is being granted for the construction of this drainage at a budget of Rs. 4,21,13, 372/- (Rupees Four Crores, Twenty One Lakhs, Thirteen Thousand, Three Hundred and Seventy Two only) subject to the following conditions.
