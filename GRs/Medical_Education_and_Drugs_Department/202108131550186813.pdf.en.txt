Preamble As per the Government scheme of providing 25% subsidy to the Ayurveda clinics run by private charitable institutions to meet the salary, purchase of medicines and other expenses, the Kishanlal Jalan Charitable Ayurvedic Dispensary, Malad, Mumbai has been given the status of General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. 2804/33, dated 21.08.1939, the subsidy is admissible to the extent of 25% of the approved expenditure or less of the deficit. Accordingly, Kishanlal Jalan Charitable Ayurvedic Dispensary, Malad, Mumbai has received a sum of Rs. 2,05,094/- (Rupees Two Lakh Five Thousand Ninety Four only).
Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning grant to the institution was under consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government On the financial audited report of the Kishanlal Jalan Charitable Ayurvedic Dispensary, Malad, Mumbai for the year 2014-15, Rs. 2,05,094/- (Rupees Two Lakh Five Thousand Ninety Four only) is being approved by the Government.
