The Proposal:
The process of starting the newly established Government Medical College and its attached 500-bed hospital in Sindhudurg under the control of the department is going on at a fast pace and the concerned dean has to attend meetings frequently for the Collector's office and other important work related to the college and hospital. Therefore, as the vehicle is essential for the day-to-day official work of the Dean, the General Administration Department was under consideration of the Government to allow the Government to hire 01 private vehicle from a Government approved supplier in the prescribed procedure as per the provision of Government Decision dated 10/08/2020. Following this, the government took decision:
Government's decision:
