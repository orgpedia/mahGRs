The proposal-
There is a need to improve the quality of sanitation services in Government/ Medical/ Ayurveda/ Dental/ Homoeopathy Colleges and its affiliated hospitals in the State. Similarly, the Government Circular dated 14.07.2022 has mandated that an institution needs to be more efficient in terms of sanitation services to be accredited under the National Accreditation Board for Hospital Providers (NABH). For this, the guidelines issued by the National Accreditation Board for Hospital Providers (NABH) from time to time have important criteria for hygiene. Since the outbreak of COVID 19, there has been a lot of emphasis on cleanliness in hospitals and it is important to keep the hospital premises clean for the healthy health of the patients. 76 Government Medical/ Ayurveda/ Dental/ Homoeopathy Colleges and Affiliated Hospitals under Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department
