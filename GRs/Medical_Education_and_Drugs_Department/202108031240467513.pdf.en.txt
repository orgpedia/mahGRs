Preamble: In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has decided to introduce a scheme viz. The office memorandum dated 23/03/2021 has directed to adopt the revised procedure. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued revised instructions regarding Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for disbursement and utilization of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Government Circular No. 2. Accordingly, it is necessary to appoint a single nodal agency (SNA) for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and open a separate single bank account in the authorized bank for that scheme.
It has also been directed to appoint at least one officer of the rank of Deputy Secretary as the nodal officer for coordination of the said Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the instructions given by the Government Circular in Reference No.2 of the Finance Department, the following Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officers are being appointed for the implementation of ongoing/ future Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals.
