GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai, vide letter dated 08.03.2021, has submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining administrative approval for the purchase of machinery and equipment from the grant sanctioned under the Compulsory (Non-Plan) Scheme 52 Machinery and Equipment Scheme for the financial year 2020-21. In pursuance of this proposal, the Government Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon is being granted administrative approval for the purchase of machinery and equipment worth Rs. 49,42,984/- (Rupees Ninety Five Lakhs Forty Two Thousand Nine Hundred Eighty Four only) from the grant sanctioned by the State Government during the financial year 2020-21.
