The Proposal:
The Government has decided to start a new Government Medical College with an intake capacity of 100 students and a 500-bed hospital attached to it at Government Medical College, Nandurbar. 1 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 31.01.2012. Also, 510 posts of Class-1 to Class-4 have been created for this college. 2 dated 20.11.2020 and Reference No. 3 The above has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 27.05.2021. Government Medical College, Nandurbar completed the admission process of 100 students in the academic year 2020-21. The college has been operational since February 2021.

The District General Hospital, Nandurbar has been transferred to the Sadar College as an attached hospital. Government Reference No. 5 As per the Government decision dated 15.07.2021, administrative approval has been accorded for construction of Government Medical College with intake capacity of 100 students and 500-bed attached hospital building and ancillary buildings at Nandurbar at an estimated cost of Rs. 532.41 crore. The government has decided to construct the college and hostel buildings on a turn-key basis through a central public sector enterprise.
It will take a long time to complete the instructions. Therefore, in order to facilitate the accommodation of the students admitted in the said college immediately and to meet the standards of the National Medical Commission, the government was considering to approve the lease of the ready-to-use private building for the students at the government rate. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Government Medical College, Nandurbar will be allowed to take on lease a ready-to-use private building at Government rate for immediate accommodation of girl students admitted in the academic year 2021-22 and 2022-23 and for meeting the standards of the National Medical Commission and will be paid a monthly stipend of Rs. 6,62,772/- is being approved for payment of annual rent.
The lease agreement shall come into force from the date of making available the building to the college and shall be extended for a period of one year and thereafter in a phased manner as per the requirement of the college till the availability of the Government building.
This Government Order is being issued under the powers vested in the Administrative Department under the Government Order, Finance Division, No: 2013/ P.S. No. 30/2013/ Regulations Part-II, dated 17.04.2015 and under the Financial Powers Rules Manual Part-I, Sub-Division-I, Roll No.10.
