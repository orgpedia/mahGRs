GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the aforesaid letter dated 28/09/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for procurement of necessary furniture material for the newly established Government Medical College, Parbhani under the control of the Department for the hostel accommodation of first year MBBS students to meet the standards of National Medical Commission, New Delhi.
