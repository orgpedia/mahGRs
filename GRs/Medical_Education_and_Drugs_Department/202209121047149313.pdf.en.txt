The Proposal:
Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital, Mumbai has been sanctioned grant-in-aid under District Annual Plan for the financial year 2020-21. 5 Under the Government decision mentioned herein, administrative approval was granted for the purchase of 1 unit of" Trinocular Microscope with Camera and Laptop" for a total amount of Rs. 10,14,009/-. However, pursuant to the said administrative approval, in pursuance of the said purchase of machinery, the tender issued by Haffkine Corporation vide No. E-3712 has been cancelled for administrative reasons, as a result of which the purchase of this device has not been made. Since the institution requires the purchase of the presented machine for patient-friendly and educational purposes, it is expedient to conduct a re-tendering process through Haffkine for the purchase of this machine. Since the validity of the administrative approval obtained in the financial year 2020-21 for the re-purchase of this machine has expired, it has become mandatory for the government departments to purchase office equipment.
