The Proposal:
LEHAT Administrative Sanction amounting to Rs. 46.55 crores has been granted vide Government Decision dated 29.06.2019 for construction of Post Graduate and Inter Residential Hostel building at Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Medical College, Latur under the Department. However, the increase in the construction budget is due to the increase in the area of the building, the large price difference and price increase, the increase in the dimensions of internal roads, concrete drains, protective walls, etc., the increase in GST, furniture not included in the original budget, etc. A proposal was placed before the Expenditure Priority Committee for approval of a revised budget of over Rs. 28.15 crore to include and complete these works. As the revised proposal was agreed to by the Expenditure Priorities Committee, the issue of granting administrative approval to the revised proposal was under consideration of Jhasan.
The government's decision:
The Expenditure Priority Committee in its meeting dated 23.06.2025 has approved a budget of more than Rs. 28,15,41, 057 / - (Rupees Twenty-eight Crores, Fifteen Lakhs, Forty-Eight Thousand, Fifty-Four ATA) on account of increase in construction budget due to increase in area of Post Graduate and Inter Residential Hostel building at Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Medical College, Latur, large difference in rates and price hike, increase in dimensions of internal roads, concrete drains, protective walls, etc., increase in GST, furniture not included in the original budget, etc. In pursuance of this approval, this Government decision provides for revised administrative approval for a total budget of Rs. 74,70,88, 787 / - subject to the following conditions.
