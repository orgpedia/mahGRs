The Proposal:
As per the above decision, dated 26.06.2018, approval has been accorded for setting up of 100 bedded Cancer Hospital in the premises of Government Medical College and Hospital, Chandrapur Institute through State Government (Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals), Tata Trusts and District Mineral Foundation, Chandrapur. As per the provisions of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed between the concerned for the establishment of this cancer hospital, the government was considering the matter of appointing a member on the" Executive Committee" to be formed for this hospital.
Government's decision:
The MoU was signed between the State Government (Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Maharashtra), District Mineral Foundation, Chandrapur and Tata Trusts for setting up a 100-bed cancer hospital in the premises of the Government Medical College and Hospital, Chandrapur.
