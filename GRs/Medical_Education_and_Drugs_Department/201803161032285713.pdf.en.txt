(a) On finalisation by the General Administration Department of the divisional cadre allocation for promotion/ direct recruitment, the proposal for posting of officers in that list shall be placed before the Civil Services Board for recommendation.
(b) While placing the proposal for recommendation of transfers before the Civil Services Board (1), it shall be necessary to point out the facts regarding the proceedings as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees Act, 2005 as well as the background/ facts regarding the proposed transfers.
(c) On the recommendation of the Civil Services Board on the proposal for posting, the proposal shall be submitted to the competent authority for approval.
(d) On the recommendation of the Civil Services Board on the proposal for transfer, the transfer of Maharashtra Government employees shall be submitted to the competent authority for approval as per the provisions of the Regulation of Transfer Act.
(e) The proposal for transfer shall be dealt with in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees Act, 2005.
