Government's decision:
In pursuance of the aforesaid letter dated 29/11/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for the purchase of furniture material for the newly established Government Medical College, Parbhani under the control of the Department, for the purpose of meeting the standards of National Medical Commission, New Delhi for the academic requirements of the first year MBBS admitted students.
In pursuance of this proposal, administrative approval is being granted for the purchase of furniture materials mentioned in the table below at a cost of Rs. 1,86,55, 868/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Six Lakhs Fifty Five Thousand Eight Hundred and Sixty Six only) from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme during the financial year 2022-23, subject to the following conditions.
