As per the Government Decision dated 25th July, 2018, with a new head for" Tree Plantation and Maintenance," the Government has approved the provision of financial assistance to the concerned administrative departments from their respective budgetary allocations within the limit of 0.5% of" Programme Expenditure." The government was considering a new bill.
