The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government dated 31.01.2012, approval was granted to start a Government Medical College with a capacity of 100 students and an attached 500-bed hospital at Satara. In this regard, as per the decision of the Government dated 15.07.2019, the District Hospital of the Public Health Department has been temporarily transferred to the Medical Education Department to start the Government Medical College in Satara.
The matter of giving administrative approval to the budget of the newly started Government Medical College and Hospital as well as the ancillary building was under the consideration of the government.

Government's decision:
A 100-bed government medical college and a 500-bed attached hospital will be built in Satara under the control of the department. In accordance with the decision taken by the High Level Committee, administrative approval is being accorded to the budget of Rs. 495,46,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Hundred and Ninety Five Crores, Forty Six Lakhs only) subject to the following conditions.
