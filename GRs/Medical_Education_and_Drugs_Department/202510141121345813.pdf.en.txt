Introduction: - Dr. G. D. Paul Foundation's YMT College of Physiotherapy, Kharghar, Raigad-410210 has been established with a new Master of Physical Therapy degree course in Cardiovascular and Respiratory Physiotherapy with 03 student intake capacity at Reference No. The recommendation to the government was received from the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik in a letter dated 1. Accordingly, a competency test of the proposed institute was conducted at Reference No. Since the proposal was submitted to the government with a recommendation from the Directorate of Medical Education and Research under letter 2, the government was considering approving the introduction of the aforesaid new post-graduate course in physical therapy in the proposed college.
Government Decision: - The YMT College of Physiotherapy, Kharghar, Raigad-410210 of Dr. G.D. Paul Foundation is being granted Government approval to start a new post-graduate course in Physiotherapy mentioned in the following prospectus from the academic year 2025-26 with the student intake capacity indicated below: -
