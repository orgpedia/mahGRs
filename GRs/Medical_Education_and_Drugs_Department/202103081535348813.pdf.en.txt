the prelude
As per the above decision of the Government, dated 31.01.2012, approval was granted to start a Government Medical College with a capacity of 100 students and a hospital with 500 patients at Satara. The Executive Director of the Government Medical College, Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune has decided to transfer 36 acres and 20 guntas of land belonging to the Corporation to the Collector of Satara for the Medical College in addition to the land mentioned in the Government Decision dated 23.06.2018 and in lieu of this, the Water Resources Department has been given alternative government land.
Resolution regarding payment, providing funds to Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation by the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals for the restoration of the building on the site, etc., in the 104th meeting of the Regulatory Board held on 03.07.2020
was taken. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation, Pune has acquired 36.20 acres of land from Government Medical College, Satara Institute on 22.12.2020
