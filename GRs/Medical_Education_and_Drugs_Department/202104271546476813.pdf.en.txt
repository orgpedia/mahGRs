No work order was issued in pursuance of the Government decision dated 14.10.2016. Meanwhile, the intake capacity of Government Medical College and Hospital, Gondia has been increased from 100 students to 150 students. The number of patients has also swelled. In this regard, in view of the increase in the number of students of Gondia Government Medical College, the revised budget for the construction of the institution along with the revised scope of work was received by the department for administrative approval. The matter of granting administrative approval to the revised budget was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
As per the decision taken in the meeting of the High Level Committee held on 23.11.2020 and in accordance with the budgetary cost considered by the Public Works Department, the administrative approval for the construction of the Government Medical College and Hospital, Gondia, under the control of the Department, is being granted by cancelling the administrative approval order issued under the Government Decision dated 06.04.2016 for the construction of the Medical College with an intake capacity of 150 students, 650-bed attached hospital and ancillary buildings of the said Institute at a revised budget of Rs. 689,46,81, 715/- (Rupees Six Hundred and Ninety-Nine Crores, Forty-Six Lakhs, Eighty One Thousand and Seven Hundred and Fifteen only) subject to the following conditions:
