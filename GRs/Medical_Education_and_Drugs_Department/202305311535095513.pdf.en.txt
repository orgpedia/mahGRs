Preamble: Reference No. As per the decision of the Government dated 03.01.2013, Government Medical College with a capacity of 100 students and an attached 500 bedded hospital at Baramati has been approved. Also Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 05.07.2019, admission of 100 students has been allowed for the first year. The matter of nominating the institution was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
The government has decided to rename the Government Medical College, Baramati as' Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Government Medical College, Baramati." Necessary action should be taken by the Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai.
