Government's decision:
Pursuant to the proposal received from the Directorate vide its letter dated 10.12.2019, for laying of Medical Gas Pipeline for the new multi-storied building (Basement+ Ground Floor+ 11 Floor) at Sassoon General Hospital, Pune, administrative approval is being accorded for the Medical Gas Pipeline for the said building at an estimated cost of Rs. 14,81,52, 616/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores Eighty Lakhs Fifty Two Thousand Six Hundred Sixteen Only) subject to the following conditions.
