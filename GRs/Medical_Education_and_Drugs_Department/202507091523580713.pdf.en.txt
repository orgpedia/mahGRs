Government Decision - The government of this department decides. 18.7.2024 wbd. Under the 12.02.2025, the powers of temporary appointment to the post of Assistant Professor, Group-B in Government Medical College B Hospital and Government Dental College B Hospital for 364 days have been delegated to the respective Dean at the institute level. On the same lines, the Dean is authorised to make the first 364 days of provisional appointment to the posts of Dentist Cadre in the Department of Dentistry at Government Dental College B Hospital and Government Medical College and Hospital at the Institute level by following the prescribed procedure at the Institute level. In the event of violation of the procedure prescribed by the Government in making such temporary appointments, the respective Dean shall be liable for action.
Thereafter, after the completion of 364 days of provisional service of such candidates, the Commissioner, Medical Education, Research and AYUSH is being empowered to conduct GAM for 364 days twice after assessing the performance of the candidates concerned by giving a break of 01 day if they wish to continue their service.
3. Thereafter, such candidates, on completion of two 364 days of provisional service under the orders of the Commissioner, Medical Education, Research and AYUSH, should be proposed by the Directorate for issuance of orders from the Government if they wish to continue their service.
