Government's decision:
In pursuance of the proposal made by the Directorate under reference letter, the administrative approval is being granted to the Government Dental College and Hospital and Cama and Albless Hospital, Mumbai, under the control of the Department, for the purchase of machinery from the budgeted funds under the State Schemes, with the Government Decision, for a total amount of Rs. 2,71,53, 962 (Rupees Two Crores, Seventy One Lakhs, Fifty Three Thousand, Nine Hundred and Sixty Two only).
Conditions: 1. The said administrative approval is being provided to the concerned organization from the budgetary grant in this financial year under the head of accounts for the purchase of machinery and equipment under the State Plan.

(2) Care should be taken to ensure that the procurement of machinery is prioritized in proportion to the actual disbursement of funds out of the amount budgeted in the respective head of accounts in this regard.
The Directorate and the concerned Dean shall ensure that there is no need for construction of new posts and electrification for handling the said machinery.
In this regard, the procedures mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedures for the Purchase of Office by the Government Department" should be complied with.
5. Procedure for Procurement of the said equipment" Procurement of drugs, related consumables and medical devices by Haffkine Bio-Pharmaceuticals Corporation Ltd. " Issued Government Decision, Department of Public Health, Purchase No. 2016/ PR No. 240/ Health-8, dated 26.07.2017.
The Director (VC) and the concerned Dean shall ensure that the requirement of the equipment is met and its price is not more than the market price.
The Director (VC) shall certify that the specifications of the said machinery are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the machinery accordingly.
The Director shall ensure that the proposed machinery is not included in any other proposal and that the machinery is required by the institution concerned.
The prices of the main machinery and its accessories must be shown differently.
10. While deciding on the purchase of machinery in this regard, it should be ensured that no expenditure is incurred beyond the provision of subsidy sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
11.The Procurement Committee shall comply with the provisions of paragraph No. 3.1.2.1 under the heading" Price Estimation and Price Reasonableness" in the above Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labour and Labour Department, dated 01.12.2016. The price of the machine complying with this provision shall be subject to revised administrative approval as per requirement.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be met by the Government Dental College and Hospital and Cama and Albless Hospital, Mumbai from the funds sanctioned and actually disbursed respectively under the head of accounts mentioned below.
