The Proposal:
The academic allowance of Post Graduate students (Junior Resident and Senior Resident) in Government Medical Colleges, Government Dental Colleges and Government Ayurvedic Colleges in the State was increased by Rs.5,000/-per month with retrospective effect from August, 2015 as per the decision of the Government vide Reference No. 2. It was also decided to review every three years the increase in the salary of resident doctors. Accordingly, the Resident Doctors' Association MARD had requested the government to increase the amount of stipend for resident doctors. Accordingly, No. In pursuance of the above decision of the Government, it has been approved to effect a wholesale increase of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand only) per month with effect from 01.05.2020 for the students of Post Graduate Courses (Junior Resident- 1/2/3 and Senior Resident- 1/2/3) in Government Medical and Dental Colleges in the State. On the same lines, the government was considering to increase the tuition fee of the students of post-graduate courses (Junior Resident- 1/2/3) in Government Ayurvedic Colleges.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving a wholesale increase of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand only) per month with effect from 01.05.2020 in the academic allowance of Post Graduate students (Junior Resident 1/2/3) in Government Ayurveda Colleges in the State.
2. Dearness Allowance will be admissible to the students of the said Post-Graduate Course at the rate at which Dearness Allowance is admissible to the State Government employees from time to time.
(3) The cost of such expenditure shall be met out of the provisions available under the head of accounts under which the academic allowance of resident doctors is spent.
