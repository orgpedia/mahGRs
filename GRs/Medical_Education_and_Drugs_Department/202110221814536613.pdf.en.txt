The Proposal:
The Directorate has submitted a proposal vide letter dated 06.08.2021 to conduct tender process at Directorate level for supply of foodgrains, vegetables and fruits required for Government Medical/ Ayurveda Colleges and affiliated hospitals. The proposal
Accordingly, it was under consideration of the government to approve the terms and conditions of the tender submitted and to approve the tender process at the directorate level.
Government's decision:
For making available foodgrains, vegetables and fruits through e-tendering in Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals and affiliated institutions under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research, the tender is being approved from the Directorate level subject to the following terms and conditions.

1) Government approved cooperative societies and government approved institutions will be eligible to participate in the tender process.

8) CA Certified Turnover Certificate for the year 2018-2019, 2019-2020 and 2020-2021 should be submitted.
9) The tender holders, which will be commercial entities, will have to submit the authorization letter of the respective manufacturers.
10) The rates of the items should be mentioned per kg/ per kg/ per litre/ per packet/ per bottle. The rates of the items will be inclusive of all expenses including packing cost, porter transportation, duty/ toll/ VAT/ GST and other incidental charges and taxes and will be accessible to all medical colleges and hospitals, affiliated health teams, Ayurveda colleges and hospitals under the Directorate.
11) The tenderer shall not stop the supply for any reason and the rates once approved shall not be increased for any reason.
12) Delay in supply of goods shall be punishable with a penalty of half of the cost of the goods per week, to be recovered from the payment/ security of the supplier concerned.
