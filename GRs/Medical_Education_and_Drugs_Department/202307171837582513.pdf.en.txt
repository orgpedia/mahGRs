Introduction: The tender process has been implemented at the institution level for the daily cleaning of the building and premises of the Government Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research for three years through external inspection. The proposal to enter into an agreement with the service provider who submits the minimum rate in the tender under this tender process and to give administrative approval to the annual expenditure incurred for the same is referred to in Reference No. The above has been submitted to the Government by the Directorate vide letter dated 04.07.2023. The proposal had been approved by the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Government Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon, has invited applications from eligible candidates for filling up of vacancies to the post of Junior Research Fellow (JE) through direct recruitment. Sai Man Power Services Ltd. Jalgaon, the service provider has introduced minimum rates. The details of minimum charges received for sanitation services in the tender are as follows.
