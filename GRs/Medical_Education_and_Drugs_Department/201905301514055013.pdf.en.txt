Preamble: The Cabinet in its meeting held on 25.04.2017 has approved the construction, equipment and creation of posts for the construction of a new" Government Integrated Medical Hub" at Jalgaon as the number of medical graduates in the State is less as per WHO standards and considering the medical facilities available in the State, Jalgaon district is comparatively backward in providing medical services. Accordingly, as per the Government decision dated 11th May, 2017, approval has been accorded for opening of 500 bedded hospital attached to Government Medical College, Jalgaon. Therefore, as the required designation-wise post creation is required for the 500-bed hospital year-wise, the proposal to approve the designation-wise post creation as per the relevant government decision was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Government of India has approved the establishment of a Medical College with an intake capacity of 100 students at Government Medical College, Jalgaon under the affiliation of the Indian Council of Medical Sciences. As per the Statement of Requirements for 500 bedded Hospital at Government Medical College, Jalgaon, 581 posts are being sanctioned in 4 phases.
