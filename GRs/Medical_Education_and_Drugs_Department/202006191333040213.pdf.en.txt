Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid Government Decision, dated 03.07.2019, the Government Rural Medical College and Hospital, Ambajogai, an institution under the control of the Department, has been transferred to the Centre.
Administrative approval was granted for the purchase of 1.5 Tesla MRI Machine with Turn Key worth Rs. 9,52,00, 000/- (Rupees Nine Crores, Fifty Two Lakh only) from the grant sanctioned for the upgradation of Post Graduate Course. The additional funds required to purchase three Tesla MRI machines as per the aforesaid letter dated 11.06.2020 from the office of the Collector, Beed will be available under the District Annual Plan.
