Section 3 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra State Public Service (Reservation for Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Denotified Tribes, Nomadic Tribes, Special Backward Classes and Other Backward Classes) Act, 2001.
Preamble: Section 3 (1) (a) of the said Act provides that the said Act shall not apply to super-specialized posts in the medical, technical and educational fields. Therefore, social reservation cannot be applied for appointments to these posts. A proposal to declare certain teaching posts in government medical colleges in the state as highly specialised posts for this purpose was under the consideration of the government.
Following this, the state government took decision on this issue.
For the purposes of Section 3 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra State Public Service (Reservation for Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Denotified Tribes, Nomadic Tribes, Special Backward Classes and Other Backward Classes) Act, 2001, the Government is pleased to approve the declaration of the following teaching posts of Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor in Government Medical Colleges and Affiliated Hospitals in the State as Super Specialized Posts.
