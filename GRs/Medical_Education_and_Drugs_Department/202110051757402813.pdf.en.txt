Conditions: 1. The administrative sanction for the purchase of the said machinery and equipment is being provided to the Institute from the grant sanctioned under the MLA Local Area Development Programme.
2. Purchase Order Department of Public Health, No. Corona 2020/ PR No.58/ Health 5, dated 24th March, 2020 and the Government decision of the schedule, dated 16th April, 2020, as approved by the various Governments/ Government undertakings/ Haffkine's various rates/ other State Medical Corporation rates, as per the list, should be done by the committee headed by the Collector.
3. The Director (Medical Education) shall ensure that the requirement of the said equipment is met and its price is not more than the market price. A committee headed by the district collector will have to certify that the ventilators are required for treatment of the disease caused by the Covid-19 outbreak.
4. It has been ensured that no new construction, electrification or new designations are required for the said equipment.
The Director shall ensure that the proposed machinery is not included in any other proposal and that the machinery is required by the institution concerned.
(6) While deciding on the purchase of machinery in this regard, it shall be ensured that no expenditure shall be incurred in excess of the provision of subsidy sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
Avoid unnecessary purchases/ exemptions while making purchases and do so within the limit of subsidy available. It will be the responsibility of the procuring authority to ensure that these ventilators do not lie idle.
The ventilators will be used elsewhere as per the requirement in case of emergency and as per the directions of the state government.
2.The cost of the above-mentioned equipment should be deducted from the following head of account.
