Government's decision:
Considering the increased student intake capacity and patient load in the hospitals under the control of the department, Dr Shankarrao Chavan Government Medical College and Hospital, Vishnupuri, Nanded, the existing number of rooms needs to be increased as per the standards of the National Council of Medical Sciences. Construction of an additional ward in the Inpatient Department at Vishnupuri, Nanded. In accordance with the decision taken by the High Powered Committee, administrative approval is being accorded to the budget of Rs. 24.56 crore subject to the following conditions.
