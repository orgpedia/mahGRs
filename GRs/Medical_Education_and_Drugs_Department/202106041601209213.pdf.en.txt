The Proposal:
Bharat Biotech International Ltd. The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) in its meeting held on 28/04/2021 has approved the production of Covaxin COVID-19 vaccine by Haffkine Biopharmaceuticals Limited (HBDCL), Mumbai using its production technology.
It's arrived. The project is being implemented jointly by the Centre and the State government.
A high-level monitoring committee and a performance review committee were being set up by the government to speed up the project.
Government's decision:
Haffkine Biopharma Limited. The High Level Review Committee for setting up of the proposed COVID-19 Covaxin vaccine manufacturing plant at Mumbai in a time-bound manner and the Work Review Committee to review the day-to-day activities of the project are as follows:
This is approved. The composition and functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
