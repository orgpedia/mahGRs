Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid letter dated 21.06.2023, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for procurement of digital mammography equipment required for patient care for the X-ray department of Government Medical College and Cancer Hospital, Aurangabad under the control of the Department. In pursuance of this proposal, the Government of India has accorded administrative approval for the purchase of the following equipment under the Strengthening of Tertiary Care for Cancer Scheme of National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke (NPCDCS) at a cost of Rs. 2,30,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores, Thirty Lakh only) from the grant-in-aid in SNA account sanctioned by the Central Government under 60% share.
