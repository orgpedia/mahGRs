GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the aforesaid letter dated 10.12.2019, the Directorate has accorded administrative approval for the construction of 08 large operation theatres and 02 transplant operation theatres in the 11th floor of the new multi-storied building (basement+ ground floor+ 11 storey) at Sassoon Sarvachachar Hospital, Pune at an estimated cost of Rs. 14,74,31, 982/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores Four- Seventy Four Lakhs Thirty One Thousand Nine Hundred and Ninety Two only) subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. Before starting the work, the right of ownership of the place should be fulfilled.
The proposed work should be carried out in accordance with the prevailing and prescribed procedures of the Public Works Department.
Special care should be taken to ensure that no revised administrative approval is required for the subject wall work.
The consent and approval of the Department of Medical Education and Drugs should be obtained at various stages of the work.
