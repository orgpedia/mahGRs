Government Medical College and Hospital, Satara has been renamed as Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj Government Medical College and Hospital, Satara.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Medical Education and Drugs
Government Resolution No: 2023/ P.S. No.11/ 9 Administration-2
Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital premises, Lokmanya Tilak Marg
New Ministry, Mumbai- 400001
Date: 9th of August 2023.
Government Resolution Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals, No. MED 2010/ Q. No. 76/2010/ Education-1, dated 31.01.2012.
Preamble: Reference No. As per the decision of the Government dated 31.01.2012, Government Medical College with a capacity of 100 students and an attached 500-bed hospital have been approved to be set up in Satara. The matter of registering the institution was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
The government has decided to rename the Government Medical College and Hospital, Satara, as Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj Government Medical College and Hospital, Satara.
Necessary action should be taken by the Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai.
