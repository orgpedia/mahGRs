Administrative approval is being accorded for the structural repair and renovation of PGL building at Grant Government Medical College, Mumbai premises at an estimated cost of Rs. 1,64,23, 343/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Four Lakhs Twenty Three Thousand Three Hundred and Forty Three only).
