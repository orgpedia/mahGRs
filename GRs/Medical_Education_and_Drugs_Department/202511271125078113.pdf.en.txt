Introduction: There is an urgent need to provide state-of-the-art equipment required for cancer diagnosis and treatment in government medical colleges and hospitals in the state. The increasing number of cancer and other disease cases necessitates the availability of high-quality equipment for continuous improvement in diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up services.
Accordingly, the government continues to receive proposals from institutions for the purchase of such equipment. There is a considerable discrepancy between these received equipment purchase offers. It was under the consideration of the government to constitute a state-level committee to make recommendations to the government after considering all aspects together and fairly while procuring these equipment.
Government Decision: In accordance with the provisions of paragraph No. 2.9.2 of the Revised Procurement Manual published under the Government Decision dated 01.12.2016 of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, the following Central Procurement Committee is being constituted for the procurement of various types of machinery and equipment for the diagnosis and treatment of cancer for all the institutions under the jurisdiction of the Department.
The composition of the committee for cancer machinery is as follows: -
