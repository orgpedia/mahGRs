Government Decision: Due to the increase in the number of postgraduate students in the Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur under the department, to meet the standards of the National Medical Commission as well as to correct the shortcomings of the interresidential hostel, the Resident Medical Officer under the undergraduate grade will be responsible for the construction of the second, third and fourth floors of the hostel. In accordance with the decision taken by the High Level Committee of Secretaries, the construction budget of Rs. 22,68,96, 376/- (Rupees Twenty Two Crores, Sixty Six Lakhs, Ninety Five Thousand, Three Hundred and Seventy Six only) is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
