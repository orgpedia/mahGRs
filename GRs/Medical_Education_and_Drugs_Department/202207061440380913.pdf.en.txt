GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the above mentioned letter dated 14/03/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for purchase of necessary furniture in Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Government Medical College, Kolhapur, an institution under the control of the Department. The proposed purchase of furniture is referred to as reference No. 5 The administrative approval was earlier granted under the Government decision mentioned here. However, the procurement process could not be completed as the grant of administrative approval was not made available to the institute. Therefore, in pursuance of the said purchase, revoking the administrative sanction orders issued earlier vide Government Decision dated 30/03/2021, the following revised administrative sanction is being granted to Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Government Medical College, Kolhapur for the purchase of furniture materials mentioned in the table below at a cost of Rs. 85,06,043/- (Rupees Eighty Five Lakh, Six Thousand, Forty Three only) from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme during the financial year 2022-23, subject to the following conditions.
