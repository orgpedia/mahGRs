Government's decision:
Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur, under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research, has decided to make available 165 vacancies out of the regular sanctioned posts of Class IV through external sources and for this purpose, a sum of Rs. 3,49,69, 460/- in respect of expenditure No. The above administrative approval was granted vide Government Resolution dated 18.04.2022. Accordingly, the contract with the service provider at the institutional level has expired on 04.05.2023. Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur has invited online applications from eligible candidates for filling vacancies to the post of Junior Resident (Class IV) through direct recruitment.
The company is in the process of filing a complaint. However, it will take some time for these posts to become available on a regular basis. Accordingly, 165 posts of class 4 in Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur have been filled up through external sources. 4 In the above Government decision, the contract which was earlier signed at the institutional level for making available at nominal rates is being extended for the next 6 months or till the availability of regular posts through the Competition Commission through TCS, whichever is earlier.
