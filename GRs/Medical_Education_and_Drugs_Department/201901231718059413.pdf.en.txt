Introduction: Smt. K. G. Mittal Punarvasu Ayurveda Mahavidyalaya, Charni Road, Mumbai is a private government aided Ayurveda institution run by the parent body, Ayurveda Prachar Sanstha, Mumbai. The college offers BAMS (Ayurveda) degree course with intake capacity of 50 and has a sanctioned bed capacity of 100 in Sheth Tarachand Ramnath Dedicated Ayurveda Hospital, Pune affiliated hospital.
During the financial year 2003-04, the Government has decided to sanction 100% wage subsidy to the private Government aided Ayurveda and Unani Institutions in the State with effect from January, 2004. Accordingly, Smt. K. G. Mittal Punarvasu Ayurveda Mahavidyalaya, Charni Road, Mumbai has been awarded a sum of Rs. 5,11,206/- is being paid as recurring subsidy. Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning grant to the institution was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Aided Ayurveda and Unani Colleges run by private institutions in the state and which are given subsidy by the government for the salary expenditure of teachers and non-teaching staff.
