Government's decision:
The existing outpatient building at Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Institute of Medical Sciences, Latur, has become very old and there have been incidents of water leakage in the building, electric current landing in the wall, leaking ceiling plaster, etc. As the Structural Audit Report has found the Out Patient Department building unfit for use, administrative approval is being accorded for construction of new Out Patient Building in the said Institute at an estimated cost of Rs. 73,29,00, 000/- (Rupees Seventy Three Crores, Thirty Nineteen Lakhs, Only).
