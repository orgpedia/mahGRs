GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the aforesaid letter dated 10.12.2019, the Directorate is granting administrative approval for the construction of facilities like Central Sterile Supply System, Pneumatic Tube Supply System, Biomedical Chutes etc. at the new multi-storied building of Sassoon Sarvachachar Hospital, Pune at an estimated cost of Rs. 13,75,98, 226/- (Rupees Thirteen Crores Seventy Five Lakhs Ninety Eight Thousand Two Hundred and Twenty-Six only) subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. Before starting the work, the right of ownership of the place should be fulfilled.
The proposed work should be carried out in accordance with the prevailing and prescribed procedures of the Public Works Department.
Special care should be taken to ensure that no revised administrative approval is required for the subject wall work.
The consent and approval of the Department of Medical Education and Drugs should be obtained at various stages of the work.
