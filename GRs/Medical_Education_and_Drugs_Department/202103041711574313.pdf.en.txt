Government's decision:
The structural audit carried out by the College of Engineering, Pune and the Public Works Department, Pune, has found that the existing residential quarters for Class-4 employees of the Sassoon Sarvacharan Hospital, Pune, under the control of the department, are dangerous. In this regard, administrative approval is being granted for the construction of Type 1 No.A, B and C building of the said employees' residence at the following amount of Rs. 28,22,93, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Eight Crores, Twenty Two Lakhs, Ninety Three Thousand only).
