Government Resolution No: Committee 0717/ P.S. No. 194/ Act
Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital, 9th Floor
Lokmanya Tilak Marg, Mumbai- 400 001.
Date is September 17, 2019.
Preamble-" Proposal to enact an Act to prohibit the grant or acceptance of undue benefit in the Maharashtra Health Service. This was presented in the Cabinet meeting held on 19.07.2018. Following decisions were taken by the Cabinet at the meeting: ":
A committee of three ministers should be appointed under the chairmanship of the Minister of Medical Education. The committee shall discuss the proposed Act within fifteen days and make amendments and suggestions, if necessary, and the revised proposal shall be presented in the next meeting of the Cabinet.
In pursuance of the above decision of the Cabinet. The proposal to appoint a committee of ministers under the chairmanship of the Minister of Medical Education was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
To discuss and make necessary amendments and suggestions on the proposed Act regarding the prevention of giving or receiving undue benefits in the Maharashtra Health Service. The following committees are being constituted under the chairmanship of the Minister of Medical Education.
