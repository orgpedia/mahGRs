Introduction: The main building of the Government Ayurveda College, Nanded is very old and some parts of the building are completely leaking in the rainy season and a lot of the building has fallen. Also, since the structure of the department needs to be changed according to the standards of the Indian Medical Council, New Delhi, it is very necessary to make changes within the department, to paint the building, so the matter of administrative approval for the repair and renovation work of the said building was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The following administrative approval is being given for the expenses incurred for the repair and renovation of the college building at Government Ayurveda College, Nanded.
