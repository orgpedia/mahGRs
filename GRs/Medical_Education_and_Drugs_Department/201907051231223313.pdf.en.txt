Preamble: In pursuance of the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1, permission has been granted to establish a Medical College with an intake capacity of 100 students and a Hospital with 500 in-patients at Baramati. The Government Medical College, Baramati has been granted permission by the Central Government under Reference Letter No. (2) for the academic year 2019-20 subject to fulfilment of conditions for admission capacity of 100 students. Therefore, the matter of granting permission to the said college for the academic year 2019-20 for an intake capacity of 100 students was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government Medical College, Baramati has been approved by the Central Government for admission of 100 students for the academic year 2019-20 subject to the terms and conditions specified under Reference Letter No. Accordingly, permission is being granted to the said Medical College to admit 100 students for the academic year 2019-20 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The intake capacity of the Government Medical College, Baramati will be 100 as per the norms of the Central Government.
2) The course should be affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik and admissions should be made thereafter.
(3) The State/ Central Government. Supreme Court or High Court. The prescribed procedure for admission to the said course should be followed as per the orders passed by the High Court from time to time.
