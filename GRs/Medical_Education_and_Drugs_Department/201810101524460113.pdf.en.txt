Rules 80 to 83 of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 provide for grant of study leave to Government servants in view of the urgency of public service to complete a special course involving higher education or specialised training in a professional or technical subject directly or indirectly related to their field of duty, within or outside India.
Accordingly, since the Government is empowered to grant study leave to the officers/ employees of the Regional Office and the offices under them to complete the courses related to the above mentioned areas, it is necessary to submit the appropriate proposal for the approval of the Government in advance before the commencement of the course and issue appropriate Government Order accordingly.
Accordingly, vide Director's letter dated 07.09.2018, Ms. Vaishali Anand Chalke, Ms. Vidya Prakash Rane and Ms. Birjis Azmi Muhammad Azam Khan, Principals, Sir. The Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS), Mumbai has granted permission for admission to PG Group of Hospitals (GGGHs) in the subject areas of the College of Nursing, 13th Floor, Homi Bhabha Block, Tata Memorial Hospital, Parel, Mumbai for the academic year 2018-20 for a period of 24 months.
