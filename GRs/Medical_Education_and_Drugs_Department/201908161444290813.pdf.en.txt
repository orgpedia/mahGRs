Introduction: As the number of medical graduates in the state is low as per the standards of the World Health Organization and considering the medical facilities available in the state, Jalgaon district is relatively backward in providing medical services, necessary construction, equipment and posts have been provided for the construction of a new" Government Integrated Medical Hub" in Jalgaon. This has been approved in the Cabinet Meeting held on 25.04.2017.
Accordingly, the No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 11.05.2017, approval has been accorded for establishment of Government Ayurveda College with intake capacity of 100 students and an attached 100 bedded hospital in the said campus. Therefore, a new Government Ayurveda College at Jalgaon with an intake capacity of 100 students and a Government Ayurveda Hospital with an attached hospital of 100 patients are required to be created year-wise. 1 The proposal to approve the designation-wise post creation as per the government decision here was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the approval of the Central Government, under the affiliation of the Indian Council of Medical Sciences, a total of 136 posts as per Statement A for Government Ayurveda College with an intake capacity of 100 students at Jalgaon and 196 posts as per Statement B for Government Ayurveda Hospital with 100 patient beds attached to Government Ayurveda College, a total of 332 posts are being approved to be created in 4 phases. Out of these, a total of 140 posts (contractual posts) in Group-D are being approved to be filled up through outsourcing i.e. through a designated supplier company in the prescribed procedure.
