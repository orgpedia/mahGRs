1.The ownership of the land should be verified before giving administrative approval to the work.
2.The budget and maps submitted to the department in this regard should be signed by the concerned dean.
If the work falls under the jurisdiction of the Archaeological Department, prior permission of the concerned department as well as the Municipal Corporation and similar authorities should be taken.
4. While giving technical approval, the State Government should take action as per the Government Circular No. 2017/ PR No. 11/ Planning-3, dated 11.04.2017 for the rate of non-Red list items.
5. Department of Industries, Power and Labour to adopt e-tendering system for procurement related matters in the proposal. The suggestions issued under the Government decision dated 24.08.2017 should be taken into consideration.
6. While doing actual work, the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No.177/ T. No. 1, d. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
7. The electrification and other ancillary works should be properly planned and completed during the construction period itself.
8. Action should be taken in accordance with the instructions given by the Government from time to time regarding the various facilities to be provided for the disabled in the building.
9. The report submitted by the National Safety Council on fire and electrical safety measures taken by the Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur, in connection with the works to be accorded the said administrative approval, shall be brought to the notice of the Public Works Department immediately by the Dean concerned for further appropriate action.
10. On the subject M. The tender process should be carried out as per the procedure laid down immediately pursuant to the direction dated 30.06.2021 passed by the High Court, Bombay, Bench Nagpur in the case of Petition No. 3427/2000, C.H. Sharma and others Vs. Government of Maharashtra and others.
11.It shall be the responsibility of the agency concerned to submit a supplementary demand proposal in the relevant head of accounts through the Directorate for budgeting the funds for the said works in the upcoming legislative session for the demand of funds to be spent in the financial year 2021-22.
