The Following conditions shall be applicable to the Virtual Personal Deposit Account (VPDA)
1. The said VPDA shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms and conditions of the relevant GR
have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds
VPDA 15 applicable only for state schemes and not for Central sector and Centrally sponsored
schemes.
The Receipt to the said VPDA shall be only such grants drawn under MTR 44 for 31 Grants in Aid
(Non-Salary) under the MH 2210 Medical & Public Health with CRC 2210 1572 and 2210 1536.
No amount under Capital Heads & Loan heads of expenditure shall be transferred to VPDA. The
Administrator shall timely submit the Utilization Certificate after incurring expenditure from the
VPDA.
The Expenditure from the VPDA shall be as per the sanction order of the respective Grant.
The unspent balances under the VPDA shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund after
completion of one year from the date of drawal of the funds/date of credit of amount to the VPDA
or the timeline mentioned in the sanction order whichever 15 earlier.
The Administrator with the District Treasury Officer shall reconcile the balances under the VPDA
at the end of every calendar month.
The balances shall be reconciled with the AG office and the Treasury officer shall submit Balance
Certificate to A.G. office for confirmation of their balances in the books of A.G. at the end of the
Financial Year.
