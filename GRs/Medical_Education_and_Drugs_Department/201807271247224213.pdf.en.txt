The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 07.10.2016, guidelines were issued regarding the purchase of" Machinery and Equipment" from the funds received from the" Procurement of Hospital Machinery and Equipment" scheme of the District Annual Plan under various headings of accounts received by the Department for patient care and study of students in all Government Medical/ Dental Colleges of the State under the administrative control of the Department of Medical Education and Drugs and the hospitals attached thereto and the list of Government Medical/ Dental Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department. In this regard, the names of the Directorate of AYUSH under the control of the Department, Government Ayurveda Colleges and Hospitals under the control of Mumbai and Government Homeopathy Hospital were included in the government decision and the matter was under consideration to provide funds to these hospitals under the District Annual Plan.
