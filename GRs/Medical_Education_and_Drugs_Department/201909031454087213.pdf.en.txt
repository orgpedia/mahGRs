Date: 3rd of September 2019
Government Resolution, Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals No: PGM 1011/ PR No. 176/ 11/ Education-2, dated 11th May, 2012.
The Proposal:
Inter-resident trainees of Government Medical, Dental and Ayurveda and Government Aided Ayurveda and Unani Colleges in the State are being given a stipend of Rs.6000/- per month as per the Government decision dated 11/05/2012. Considering the rate of tuition fee paid to inter-resident trainees from other States, the Government was considering to increase the tuition fee to Rs.11000/- per month.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving to increase the stipend of Inter Resident Trainees from Rs.6000/- per month to Rs.11000/- per month in Government Medical, Dental and Ayurveda Colleges and Government Aided Ayurveda and Unani Colleges in the State.
The extension will be effective from 01/08/2019.
