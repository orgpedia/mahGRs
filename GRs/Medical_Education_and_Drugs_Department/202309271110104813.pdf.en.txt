Government's decision:
Since Government Medical College, Alibag requires Skill Lab Unit to meet the standards of National Medical Commission, New Delhi and in the academic interest of the students, the purchase of machinery and equipment worth Rs. 1,07,15, 759/- (Rupees One Crore, Seven Lakhs, Fifteen Thousand, Seven Hundred and Nineteen only) from the sanctioned grant under the State Scheme for the financial year 2023-24 is being given administrative approval under this Government decision subject to the following conditions.

Conditions:
1) The said administrative approval is being provided to the concerned institution from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme.
2) Since the machinery is to be purchased on turn key basis, the machinery should be purchased by following the procedure of the Government Decision No. IHN-2018/ PR No. 293/ PRISA-1, dated 7th March, 2019 of this Department.
