Preamble: Reference No. The Government has approved the establishment of a Medical College with an intake capacity of 100 students and a Hospital with 500 patients at Baramati above 4th floor as per the Government decision dated 03.01.2013. Further, vide Government Order dated 05.07.2019, admission of 100 students for the first year has been allowed in Government Medical College, Baramati. For the safety of students, teachers and other government employees in the Government Medical College campus, the government was considering providing security guards through external sources.
Government's decision:
Pune District Security Guard Board has decided to provide 80 security guards (various officers/ staff) to the students and faculty members of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Government Medical College, Baramati. The annual expenditure of Rs. 2,65,43, 640/- (Rupees Two Crores Sixty Five Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Six Hundred and Forty only) is being accorded administrative approval.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the financial powers vested in the Administrative Department under the Finance Department, Government Decision dated 17.04.2015, Fiscal Powers Rules Part I, Sub-Division-4, No. 11.
