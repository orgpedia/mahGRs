The Proposal:
The Government Medical College at Nandurbar with an intake capacity of 100 students and an attached hospital for 500 patients has been sanctioned vide order No. Issued under 1
have been made. The Director, Medical Education and Research, has decided to transfer the District General Hospital, Nandurbar, under the Directorate of Health Services, Nandurbar, to the Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Nandurbar, in order to meet the norms of the Indian Council of Medical Sciences before the actual opening of the proposed Government Medical College. 2 is proposed. Accordingly, the matter of transfer of the District General Hospital, Nandurbar to the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
The student intake capacity of Government Medical College, Nandurbar has been fixed at 100 and as per the procedure and conditions indicated in the Annexure accompanying this Government Resolution, the District General Hospital, Nandurbar under the Directorate General of Health Services is being transferred to the planned Government Medical College, Nandurbar to meet the norms of the Indian Council of Medical Sciences in this regard.
