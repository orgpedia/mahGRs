Proposal: Government Medical College under the Department of Medical Education and Drugs
The state government had earlier decided to rename Latur Sarvodaya Hospital after former Maharashtra chief minister Vilasrao Deshmukh. The government has taken the following decisions:
The Government Medical College and Hospital, Latur has been renamed as Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Institute of Medical Sciences, Latur. Necessary action in this regard should be taken by the Director, Medical Education and Research.
