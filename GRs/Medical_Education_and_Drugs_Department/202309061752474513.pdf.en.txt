Conditions:
1) The said administrative approval is being provided to the concerned institution from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme.
(2) In this regard, the prescribed procedure mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-II/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 and the corresponding Government Decisions issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedure for the Purchase of Offices by the Government Department" should be complied with.
The process of procurement of such furniture should be done as per the provisions of the Procurement Policy of Government Decision, Industries, Energy and Labour Department, dated 01.12.2016 or through the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal as prescribed under Government Decision, Industries, Energy and Labour Department No.
4) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the concerned Dean shall ensure that the requirement of such furniture is met and its price is not more than the market price.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the concerned Dean shall certify that the specifications of the said furniture/ equipment are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the equipment accordingly.
(6) The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall ensure that the proposed furniture is not included in any other proposal and that such equipment is required by the institution concerned.
7) It will be necessary to show the different prices of the main machine and its accessories.
8) While taking a decision on the purchase of furniture in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of subsidy sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
