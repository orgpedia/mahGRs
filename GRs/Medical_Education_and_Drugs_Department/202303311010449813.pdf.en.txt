Preamble: The Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur was earlier granted administrative approval to purchase machinery worth Rs. 8,30,00, 000/- from the grant available under District Annual Plan vide Government Decision, dated 02.08.2019.
As per this administrative approval, the procurement process of e-tendering of machinery was carried out at the Haffkine Corporation level, but the tenderer could not procure the machine as the tender was cancelled due to incomplete documents. Also, since the said Administrative Sanction Order has been in force for more than one year, it is required to be accorded revised Administrative Sanction. Accordingly, vide reference No. 08

The matter of granting revised administrative approval to the proposal submitted under the letter dated 09.02.2023 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur. Out of Rs. 8,30,00, 000/-, Rs. 4,81,89, 360/- has been disbursed by the District Planning Committee for the purchase of Nuclear Medicine (Setting up State of Art Nuclear Medicine Diagnostic Imaging Centre on Turn Key Basis) equipment as per the Government decision dated 05.04.2016. The remaining amount of Rs. 3,48,10, 640/- is yet to be disbursed. The revised administrative sanction is being provided under this Government decision subject to the condition that the entire amount required for this revised administrative sanction is Rs. 8,30,00, 000/- from the District Annual Plan.
The revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) All terms and conditions of this Agreement, except section 4, shall remain in effect.
2) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the concerned Dean have confirmed that the institution requires the equipment to be purchased in this regard and its price is not more than the market price.
(3) The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall ensure that the proposed equipment is not included in any proposal elsewhere and that the said equipment is required by the institution concerned.
4) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the concerned Dean have ensured that there will be no delay in the purchase of the equipment to be done in this regard.
(5) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the concerned Dean shall be fully responsible for any financial irregularities in the purchase of equipment to be made in this regard.
6) Since the funds received from the District Planning Committee, Reference No. Action should be taken in accordance with 7.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the authority of the Finance Department, Government Resolution No: 2013/ Q. No. 30/ 13/ Regulations, dated 17.04.2015 and under the authority of the Administrative Department as per the Financial Powers Rule Book Part-1, Sub-Division-2, Roll No. 3, Rule No. 7.
