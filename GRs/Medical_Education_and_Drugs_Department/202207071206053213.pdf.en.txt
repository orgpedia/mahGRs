The government decision: Patients coming for treatment are admitted to various rooms in the surgical building of the Government Medical College and Hospital, Aurangabad.
As the existing surgical building and electrical wiring are old, electrical repairs have to be carried out frequently. A new roof and replacement of the old one is required. Administrative Sanction for this work amounting to Rs. 6,17,55, 957/- (Rupees Six Crores, Seventeen Lakhs, Fifty Five Thousand, Nine Hundred and Fifty Seven only) is being accorded subject to the following terms and conditions.
