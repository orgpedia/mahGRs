GOVERNMENT DECISION: The review of sanctioned posts for the Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune has been completed and approved by the Government Resolution at Reference No. After the said review, the Government have approved the continuation of 17 temporary posts in the approved pattern of Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune till 28/02/2009 vide Government Resolution No. Out of these 17 temporary posts, the number of employees working in the notified posts of Driver (Class-3) and Safaigar (Class-4) has been included in the vacant posts of Junior Scribe and Safaigar in the organization. The remaining 15 temporary posts have been extended as per the government decision issued from time to time. The said posts were extended upto 31.08.2020 vide Government Order dated 16.03.2020.
The Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health had submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 01/08/2020 for grant of approval for continuation of 15 temporary posts in the Institute beyond 31/08/2020. In pursuance of the said proposal, in accordance with the above Government decision, the Finance Department, vide its order dated 31.08.2020, has been authorized to continue the said posts from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 subject to the following conditions, as the 15 temporary posts indicated in the prospectus A and B accompanying this Government decision are required for the continuation of the functioning of the concerned institution.
