" Supplement"
The Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals has issued instructions to the State Government for the purchase of medicines, surgicals, consumables and related items and machinery and equipment from the funds received under various heads of accounts and from the District Annual Plan for the study of patients and students in various Government Medical/ Dental/ Ayurveda/ Homoeopathy Colleges under the control of the Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department and the hospitals and training health teams attached to it. Guidelines have been issued as per the Government decisions mentioned herein 1 to 4 and the list of Medical/ Dental/ Ayurveda/ Homoeopathy Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department has been issued.
Now, in Annexure-A of the said Government Decisions, the names of all Government Medical/ Dental/ Ayurveda/ Homoeopathy Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department, newly started and to be started in future, are being included under this Supplementary Letter.
