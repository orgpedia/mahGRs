The government's decision:
Since the mortuary at Chhatrapati Pramilaraje Sarva Cure Hospital, Kolhapur under the control of the Department is unsafe and unfit for use, the demolition of the building and construction of a store and department building for the medical and medical department at the site is being accorded administrative sanction subject to the following conditions, with a budget of Rs. 14,89,15, 431 / - (Rupees Fourteen Crores, Nineteen Lakhs, Fifteen Thousand, Four Hundred and Thirty-One Only) (inclusive of all taxes).

1 The ownership of the land should be settled before this work is done.
