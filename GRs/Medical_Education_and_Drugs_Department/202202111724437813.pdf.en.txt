Preamble: As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1, permission has been granted to start a new Government Medical College at Sindhudurg with a capacity of 100 students and a 500-bed hospital by upgrading the District General Hospital attached to it. The Government Medical College, Sindhudurg has been granted permission by the Central Government for admission of 100 students for the academic year 2021-22, subject to fulfilment of conditions. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to allow the college to admit students for the academic year 2021-22 with an intake capacity of 100 students.
Government Decision: The Government Medical College, Sindhudurg has been approved by the Central Government for the academic year 2021-22 subject to the terms and conditions specified for admission capacity of 100 students vide letter No. Accordingly, permission is being granted to the said Medical College to admit 100 students for the academic year 2021-22 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The intake capacity of the Government Medical College, Sindhudurg will be 100 as per the norms of the Central Government.
2) The course should be affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik and admissions should be made thereafter.
(3) The State/ Central Government. Supreme Court or High Court. The procedure prescribed for admission to the said course should be followed as per the orders passed by the High Court from time to time.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MED 2016/ PR No. 231/16/ Education 1 4) It shall be mandatory to follow the rules and standards prescribed by the Central Government, National Medical Commission, New Delhi and State Governments from time to time in respect of medical courses.
5) Under no circumstances should students be admitted more than the student admission capacity approved by the government.
The permission granted by the Central Government is for the year 2021-22 and thereafter it is liable to be renewed every year. According to this, it will be mandatory for the students to renew the permission of the central government as well as the state government based on it before admission.
This Government Order is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Government by the National Medical Commission, New Delhi vide letter No. (2) and in exercise of the powers conferred on the Government under Section 64 of the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences Act, 1998.
