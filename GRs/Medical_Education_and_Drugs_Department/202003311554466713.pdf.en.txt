For the purchase of Operative Laproscope Set for Dr.Vaishampayan Memorial Government Medical College, Solapur, vide Government Resolution dated 30.01.2020, the Directorate is granting administrative approval for the purchase of the said equipment at the revised price of Rs. 46,00,000/- (Rupees Forty Seven Lakh only) instead of Rs. 25,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakh only) as proposed vide letter dated 05.03.2020.
The terms and conditions mentioned in the aforesaid Government Decision, dated 30.01.2020 and the relevant headings in respect of expenditure matters shall continue to remain in force.
