Preamble: As per the Government Decision dated 27.01.2021, a new Government Medical College with an intake capacity of 100 students and an attached 430-bed hospital at Dharashiv under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research has been approved. To start this college, it is necessary to meet the standards of the National Medical Commission.
According to the National Medical Commission (NMC) norms, a government medical college needs to have at least 20 acres of land at one place. For this purpose, the Department of Water Resources has been requested to provide 3.00 hectare R land under the control of Water Resources Department and 9.64 hectare R land under the control of Skill and Entrepreneurship Department. The space can be made available with the approval of the Cabinet. Accordingly, the proposal. It has been approved in the meeting of the Cabinet dated 16.09.2023.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
In order to meet the standards of National Medical Commission, Government Medical College, Dharashiv has been designated as Group No. Out of the total 11.99 ha R site in 366/1, 366/2, 366/3, 366/4, 366/5 and 366/6, 9.64 ha is open.
