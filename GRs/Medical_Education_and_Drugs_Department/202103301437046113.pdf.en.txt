Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid Government Decision, dated 02.11.2017, administrative approval was granted for the purchase of equipment to the Government Medical College and Super Specialty Hospital, Nagpur, under the control of the Department. Out of the machinery mentioned in this Administrative Sanction Order, the machinery mentioned in the following table has been procured by Haffkine Corporation and supplied to the Institute. No payment has been made for this equipment. Accordingly, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 26.03.2021 to approve the payment of the said amount to the concerned institution during the financial year 2021-21 from the funds released under the State Plan. In pursuance of the said proposal, approval is being accorded for payment of machinery and equipment amounting to Rs. 31,50,690 (Rupees Thirty One Lakh, Fifty Thousand, Six Hundred and Ninety only) as mentioned in the table below, subject to the following terms and conditions:
