Government's decision:
The Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur was granted administrative approval for the Robotic Surgery System with All Accessories under the District Mineral Foundation Trust, Nagpur vide Government Resolution dated 19.12.2018. The bidder has submitted a higher minimum bid of Rs. 20,62,42, 862/- as against the administrative sanction of Rs. 16,80,00, 000/-. Since the minimum rate of the proposed items is more than 10% of the administrative approval rate, the Directorate had requested for grant of revised administrative approval subject to the balance amount of Rs. 3,82,42, 862/- being sanctioned under District Mineral Foundation Trust, Nagpur vide letter dated 25.03.2022. In pursuance of this proposal, revised administrative approval is being granted as mentioned in the table below.
