Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid letter dated 12/10/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for procurement of machinery and equipment to various Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department. In pursuance of the said proposal, out of the grant sanctioned in the heads of accounts of the respective institutions for the financial year 2022-2023 for various institutions under the Department, an amount of Rs. Administrative approval is being granted under this Government Decision for the purchase of machinery and equipment in Annexure-A and B attached with this Government Decision for an amount of Rs. 5,96,76, 859/- (Rupees Five Crores, Ninety Five Lakhs, Seventy Six Thousand, Eight Hundred and Sixty Nine only) subject to the following conditions.
Conditions:
(i) The said administrative sanction is being provided subject to availability of funds to the concerned institution from the grants sanctioned in the heads of accounts of the respective institutions under the State Plan (Compulsory Expenditure).
