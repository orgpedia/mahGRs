Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 04.03.2016, administrative approval was accorded for the construction of 160 Girls Hostel (two buildings) and 176 Boys Hostel at Shri Vasantrao Naik Government Medical College, Yavatmal at an estimated cost of Rs. 1377.19 lakh and Rs. 752.62 lakh respectively. In pursuance of the said administrative approval, a proposal for payment of 12% Goods and Services Tax to the concerned tenderer in respect of tenders published by the Public Works Department was received by the Government vide letter dated 13.09.2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati, in connection with the construction of Shri Vasantrao Naik Government Medical College, Yavatmal, in accordance with his letter dated 09.01.2018 as mentioned in the approval given in the pre-tender meeting, has to pay 12% Goods and Services Tax to the contractor as the prevailing rate of Goods and Services Tax is payable on the tender amount.
