The Proposal:
Padmabhushan Vasantdada Patil Government Hospital, Sangli is the main medical facility of the district and in the structural audit of this building, it was considered by the government to give administrative approval to the budget of the building as the third floor and both toilet blocks are dangerous.
Government's decision:
Padmabhushan Vasantdada Patil Government Hospital, Sangli, is the main medical facility of the district and the structural audit of the building has found the third floor and both toilet blocks to be dangerous. Administrative approval is being accorded for the construction of the building at a cost of Rs.190.63.247/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety Lakhs Sixty Three Thousand Two Hundred and Forty Seven only) subject to the following conditions.
