The construction of a new children's hostel at the Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur, under the control of the department, has been completed with a capacity of 150 undergraduate students. Since the number of undergraduate students is now 200, the construction of the sixth and seventh floors of the new children's hostel is very important as it is necessary to meet the standards of the National Medical Commission. The revised budget of Rs. 11,58,91, 911/- (Rupees Eleven Crores, Fifty Eight Lakhs, Ninety One Thousand, Nine Hundred and Eleven only) considered by the Public Works Department is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
