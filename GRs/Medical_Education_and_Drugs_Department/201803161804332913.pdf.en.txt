Introduction: The residence for the Dean in the premises of Government Ayurveda College and Hospital, Nanded is very old and has been demolished. A part of the building has collapsed. At present, the building is lying unutilized for the last 15-20 years and no one is living in it and therefore, in accordance with the proposal of the Dean for the construction of the proposed new building on the site of the old building, the Director, AYUSH, has been given the budget and plans for the construction of the new building submitted by the Public Works Department, Nanded. In pursuance of the proposal submitted for grant of administrative approval for 1,24,19, 797/-, the matter of grant of administrative approval for the said construction was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Provisions have been proposed for this work as explained in the introduction, as per the recapitulation sheet attached with it, and an amount of Rs. 126.13 lakh has been considered. Administrative approval of the Government is also being granted subject to the conditions of fulfilling the following:
a) Before starting the work, the design, layout and detailed map should be started with the approval of the architect.
(b) When dealing with a provision made in a lump-sum manner, the work should be taken up only after making a detailed budget.
(a) In the course of its actual work, the Department of the Environment is governed by Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in A.I., dated 10th January, 2014.
d) If the proposed building comes under the jurisdiction of the Archaeology Department, prior permission of the concerned department must be obtained before demolishing the existing building.
