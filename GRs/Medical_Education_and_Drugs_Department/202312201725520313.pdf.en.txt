Preamble: The Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune was granted approval for procurement of 32 seater buses under the scheme Centre of Excellence by the Government of India vide Government Decision dated 19.03.2021. After this approval, the organization has not been able to purchase the vehicle due to technical difficulties in the purchase. Also, the legal validity of the approval given for the purchase of this vehicle has been more than one year and the issue of revised administrative approval was under consideration of the government as new orders of administrative approval were required to be issued.
Government's decision:
Revised administrative approval is being granted to Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune for procurement of 32 Seater Bus for the following amount of Rs. 19,68,000/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs, Sixty-Six Thousand only) from the funds available under Centre of Excellence Scheme of Government of India, subject to the following conditions.

Conditions: 1) The revised administrative approval should be provided to the Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune from the funds under the scheme of Centre of Excellence.
(2) In this regard, the prescribed procedure prescribed in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 and the ancillary Government Decisions issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedure for the procurement of offices by the Government Department" should be complied with.
3) The concerned director-professor should ensure that the vehicle is required and its price is not higher than the market price.
4) It has been confirmed that the vehicle does not require any new construction, electrification or new design.
The Director-Professor concerned shall certify that the specifications of the vehicle are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the equipment accordingly.
(6) The Director-Professor concerned shall ensure that the proposed vehicle is not included in any other proposal and that such equipment is required by the institution concerned.
7) While taking a decision on the purchase of a vehicle in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of subsidy sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
8) The State Level Procurement Committee shall comply with the provisions of the above Government Decision, Paragraph No. 3.1.2 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, dated 01.12.2016, under the heading" Price Estimation and Price Reasonableness."
The cost of equipment complying with this provision shall be subject to revised administrative approval as required.
