The Proposal:
The Government of Maharashtra has budgeted an amount of Rs.36, 75,69,000/- (Rupees Thirty Six Crores Fifty Seven Lakh Seventy Nine Thousand only) for the financial year 2019-20 for Kasturba Health Society, Sevagram, Wardha. This provision has been made available under the head 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries).
Government's decision:
As per the directions of the Department of Finance, the Government hereby approve the release of first instalment of Rs. 8,83,75, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Eighty Three Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand only) to the Institute for the financial year 2019-20. This amount is within the proposed cash flow limit for 2019-20.
The grant of the said grant-in-aid is provisional and is being sanctioned subject to recovery from the institution of excess amount of grant-in-aid over and above the cost of final adjustment on the basis of actual expenditure incurred during the financial year 2019-2020 and the terms and conditions contained in the Annexure A. The utilization certificate of the institution should be submitted to the government within 30 days from the date of issuance of the government decision.
