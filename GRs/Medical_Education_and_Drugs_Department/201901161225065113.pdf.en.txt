Preamble: As per the Government scheme of providing 25% subsidy of the actual cost to meet the salary, purchase of medicines and other expenses of Ayurveda clinics run by private charitable institutions, the Kishanlal Jalan Charitable Ayurvedic Dispensary, Malad, Mumbai in the State has been approved by the General Administration Department, Government Decision No. 2804/33, dt. Under 31/08/1939, subsidy is admissible to the extent of 25% of the approved expenditure or less of the deficit. Accordingly, Kishanlal Jalan Charitable Ayurvedic Dispensary, Malad, Mumbai has received a sum of Rs. 1,83,684/- is being paid as subsidy. Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning grant to the institution was under consideration of the Government.
