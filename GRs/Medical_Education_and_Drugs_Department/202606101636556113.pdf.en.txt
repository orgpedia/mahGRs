Proposal: - Ma. The Cabinet of Ministers. According to the approval given on 03.01.2017, read section no. 1 D. Under the Government Decision on 16.06.2017, approval was granted to the Central Government to enter into an agreement to strengthen the drug control system. Accordingly, for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of "Strengthening Drug Control Regimes" implemented by the Food and Drug Administration, read Department No. 2 d. As per the Government decision dated 15th October 2018, 08 new titles of accounts were approved.
For this scheme, all the headings were prepared for the General Category. However, it did not create a separate head of accounts for the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes component. At present, the funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes in the SNA-SPARSH fund flow mechanism will be classified into General, SC and ST categories. Accordingly, the issue of creation of head of accounts under SC / ST component is under consideration of the Government of India for provision of funds received under SC / ST component by the Central Government under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of "Strengthening of Drug Control Regimes": -
