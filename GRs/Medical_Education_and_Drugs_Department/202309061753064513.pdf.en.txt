Government's decision
Government Medical College and Hospital, Alibag with a capacity of 100 students has started functioning from the year 2021-22. At present, the college is in the process of admitting 100 students in the third year of 2023-24 and the District Hospital, Alibag has been affiliated to the college. Administrative approval is being accorded for the construction and electrification of additional two floors of the building for the office of Surgeon and Warehouse at District Hospital, Alibaug, at an estimated cost of Rs. 6,36,25, 223/- (Rupees Six Crores Thirty Six Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand Two Hundred and Twenty Three only) for academic purposes and meeting the standards of National Council of Medical Sciences, New Delhi, subject to the following conditions:
