Administrative approval has been given for the construction of hostel buildings for students/ students at a cost of Rs. 532.41 crore. As the government has directed to complete the construction of the college and hostel buildings on a turn-key basis through a central government PSU, it will take more time to complete it.
Therefore, in order to provide immediate accommodation to the students admitted in the said college and to meet the standards of the National Medical Commission, the government was considering to approve the leasing of the ready-to-use private building for the children's hostel at the government rate. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision
1 Government Medical College, Nandurbar is being approved to lease out the ready-to-use private building for immediate accommodation of students admitted in the academic year 2020-21 and to meet the standards of National Medical Commission at the rate certified by the Public Works Department and to pay a monthly rent of Rs. 1,25,386/-to the said institution from the funds available under the objectives of lease and taxes and to enter into a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for the same.
(2) The lease agreement shall come into force on the date on which the building is made available to the college. The tenure of the said agreement will be for a period of one year and thereafter it can be extended with the approval of the Government in a phased manner till the Government building is made available as per the requirement of the college.
This Government Order is being issued under the powers vested in the Administrative Department under the Government Order, Finance Division, No: 2013/ P.S. 30/2013/ Regulations Part-II, dated 17.04.2015 and as per the Financial Powers Rules Manual Part-I, Sub-Division-I, Roll No.10.
