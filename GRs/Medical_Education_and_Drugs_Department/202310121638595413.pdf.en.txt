The Proposal:
The government has decided to start a new government medical college with a capacity of 100 students and a hospital with 500 patients attached to it at Government Medical College, Sindhudurg. 1 approved by the government. Government Medical College, Sindhudurg has completed the admission process of 100 students in the academic year 2021-22. Government Medical College, Sindhudurg is a newly constructed college and the institute does not have its own hostel to accommodate the students admitted for medical education. Therefore, in order to facilitate the stay of the students admitted in the said college immediately and to meet the standards of the National Medical Commission, Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd. The matter of approving the building occupied by Sindhudurg to be taken on rent at government rates for students was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has taken a decision to provide immediate accommodation to the students admitted to Government Medical College, Sindhudurg and to meet the standards of National Medical Commission. Building in possession of Sindhudurg on rent at government rate
