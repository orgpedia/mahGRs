Government's decision:
As per the Government Decision dated 15.05.2018, administrative approval was accorded for construction of basement+ 2nd and 3rd floor under vertical extension of Radiotherapy Department at Government Cancer Hospital, Aurangabad at an estimated cost of Rs. 31,07,46, 000 (Rupees Thirty One Crore Seven Lakh Forty Six only).
The construction was carried out by HSCC (India) Limited.
