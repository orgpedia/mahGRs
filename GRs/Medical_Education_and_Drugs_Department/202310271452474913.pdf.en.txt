Preamble: Proposal for grant of approval to the Dean, Government Medical College, Sindhudurg to provide four wheeler vehicles on hire basis for their office work. 5 has been submitted to the government. The Dean of the newly established Government Medical College in Sindhudurg has not yet been provided with a vehicle. Therefore, the matter of granting permission to the Dean of the institution to provide four-wheeler vehicles on rent for their office work was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: For the day-to-day administration of the Dean, Government Medical College, Sindhudurg, Reference No. 4 The General Administration Department, Govt. of India vide its decision dated 01.06.2023, has issued a notification regarding the issue of registration of a Supplier Organization in the panel of the specified Supplier Organizations in Form No. Approval is being given to make available any one of the vehicles mentioned in Sections 1 and 2 on lease as per the fixed rates and to bear the annual expenditure of Rs. 6,36,000/- (excluding taxes) and allowable tax thereon.
