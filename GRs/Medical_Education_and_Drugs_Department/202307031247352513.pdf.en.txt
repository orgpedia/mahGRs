National Commission for Allied and Healthcare Professions (NCAHP)
The Act-2021 has come into effect from 25.05.2021. As per the provisions of the said Act, there is a provision to regulate and regulate the services and education of similar professions belonging to 10 recognized categories related to health care.
A committee headed by the Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai, was set up to implement the Act in the state.
Government's decision:
The government is approving the formation of the following committees in accordance with the implementation of the National Commission for Allied and Healthcare Professions (NCAHP) Act-2021
