Conditions: -
1) The ownership of the land should be settled before the work is done.
2) If the work is under the jurisdiction of the Archaeological Department, prior permission of the concerned department as well as the Municipal Corporation and similar authorities should be taken.
3) In granting technical approval, action should be taken in accordance with Government Circular No. 2017 / Q. 11 / Planning-3, dated 11.04.2017 for processing of matters outside the State Rates.
4) Department of Environment, Government Decisions, ENV-2013 / P.S. No. 177 / Hr. Action should be taken as per the guidelines mentioned in No. 1, dated 10th January, 2014.
5) These works should be completed during the construction period itself by proper planning of electrification as well as other ancillary works.
6) The thematic work should be done with special care so that revised administrative approvals are not required.
(2) The cost of the present construction shall be apportioned from the funds to be sanctioned in the following headings.
