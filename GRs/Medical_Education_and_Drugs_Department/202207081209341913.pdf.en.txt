Government's decision:
As per the above mentioned letter dated 02/06/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for procurement of machinery and equipment for Government Dental Colleges and Hospitals, Mumbai/ Aurangabad/ Nagpur under the control of the Department. In pursuance of this proposal, Government Dental Colleges and Hospitals under the Department, Mumbai/ Aurangabad/ Nagpur will be provided Rs.
