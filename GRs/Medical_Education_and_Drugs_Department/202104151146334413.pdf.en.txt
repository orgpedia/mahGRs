GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: VISHVI-2021/ P.S. NO. 132/ QUESTION-2 DATE: 14th April, 2021
Government's decision:
In view of the approval of the National Medical Commission for the admission of 100 students in the academic year 2021-22, the private building ready for use by the Government Medical College, Satara, is being approved to rent at the government rate of Rs. 7,68,511/- per month for 3 years and for the next 01 year as per the need, from the funds available to the institution under the 14-Lease and Tax Objectives and accordingly the MoU submitted by the Dean, Government Medical College, Satara.
The lease agreement will come into effect from the month in which the National Medical Commission first inspects the Government Medical College for approval.
The lease agreement will be effective from the academic year 2021-22 and will be valid for a period of three years and for a further period of one year as per requirement.
In case the college is not recognized for the academic year 2021-22, a written intimation in this regard shall be required to be submitted to the concerned institution through the Dean and the lease agreement shall continue to exist till the date of selection and shall be deemed to be null and void from the next month.
This Government Order is being issued under the powers vested in the Administrative Department vide Government Order, Finance Division, No: 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations Part-II, dated 17.04.2015 and as per the Financial Powers Rules Manual Part-I, Sub-Division-1, Roll No.10
