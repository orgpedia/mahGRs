Preamble: The Government have approved the establishment of a Government Medical College with a capacity of 100 students and a hospital with 500 patients in Satara. The Government Medical College, Satara has been granted permission by the Central Government for admission of 100 students for the academic year 2021-22 subject to fulfilment of conditions. Therefore, the matter of granting permission to the said college for the academic year 2021-22 for an intake capacity of 100 students was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Government Medical College, Satara has been approved by the Central Government for the academic year 2021-22 subject to the terms and conditions specified for admission capacity of 100 students under reference number (2). Accordingly, permission is being granted to the said Medical College to admit 100 students for the academic year 2021-22 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The student intake capacity in the Government Medical College, Satara will be 100 as determined by the Central Government.
2) The course should be affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik and admissions should be made thereafter.
(3) The State/ Central Government. Supreme Court or High Court. The prescribed procedure for admission to the said course should be followed as per the orders passed by the High Court from time to time.
