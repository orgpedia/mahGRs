The Proposal:
Construction of Administrative Building (Ground Floor-6 Floor) at Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur Reference No. Administrative approval has been granted by a Government decision herein. The government was considering sanctioning the additional cost of the institute's administrative building (floor-6) considering the additional aspects of the construction and the additional impact of the construction, as the construction had exceeded the original administrative sanction of 9.92%.
The government's decision:
Considering the additional aspects and the additional impact of the construction of the Administrative Building (Floor-6) at Indira Gandhi Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur, which has been extended beyond the original administrative sanction of the construction, the following immediate additional expenditure is being granted administrative sanction by this Government Decision subject to the following conditions.
