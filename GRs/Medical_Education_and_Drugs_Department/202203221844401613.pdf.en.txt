Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid letters dated 31/01/2022 and 10/03/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for purchase of necessary furniture in the newly constructed Government Medical College, Sindhudurg under the control of the Department. In pursuance of the said proposal, the Government Medical College, Sindhudurg is being granted administrative approval for the purchase of furniture materials mentioned in the table below at a cost of Rs. 71,54,166/- (Rupees Seventy One Lakh, Fifty Four Thousand, One Hundred and Sixty Six only) from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme during the financial year 2021-22 subject to the following conditions.
