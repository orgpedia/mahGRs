As per the Government decision dated 27.04.2021, the revised budget of Rs. 689,46,81, 715 (Rupees Six Hundred and Ninety-Nine Crores, Forty-Six Lakhs, Eighty-One Thousand, Seven Hundred and Fifteen only) for the construction of 150 student capacity, 650 bed hospital and ancillary buildings for the Government Medical College and Hospital, Gondia Institute under the control of the Department has been approved.
2. The Government directs that the said construction shall be carried out on turn key basis by a Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) as per the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 3, dated 08.02.2018 and dated 24.12.2018 of the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals.
