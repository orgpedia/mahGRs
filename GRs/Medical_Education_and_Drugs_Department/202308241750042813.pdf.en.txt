Government's decision:
In pursuance of the aforesaid letter dated 01.08.2023, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval to Dr. Shankarrao Chavan Government Medical College, Vishnupuri, Nanded under the control of the Department for procurement of machinery and equipment from Central Government funds for the Skill Centre (NELS). In pursuance of the said proposal, administrative approval is being accorded for the purchase of machinery and equipment mentioned in the table below for Dr. Shankarrao Chavan Government Medical College, Vishnupuri, Nanded under the Department at a cost of Rs. 1,42,51, 314/- (Rupees One Crore Forty Two Lakhs, Fifty One Thousand, Three Hundred and Four Hundred only) from the Central Government sanctioned fund for the financial year 2023-24 subject to the following conditions.

The Government Resolution No. 1 issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor. The prescribed procedure as laid down in the Government Order No. 82/ Part-II/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 and related Government Orders should be followed.
The procurement of the said machinery should be done through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal as per the provisions of the procurement policy of Government Decision, Industries, Power and Labour Department, dated 01.12.2016 or Government Decision, Industries, Power and Labour Department No. 2014/ PR No. 215/ Industries-4, dated 24.08.2017.
4) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the concerned Dean shall ensure that the requirement of such equipment is met and its price is not more than the market price.
5) It has been ensured that no new construction, electrification or new designations are required for the said equipment.
