The Proposal:
Purchase of 128 Slice City Scan Machines required for Government Medical College and Cancer Hospital, Aurangabad was approved vide Government Resolution dated 31.03.2018. The legal validity of the administrative approval given for the purchase of this machine has been more than one year and since a new order needs to be issued, the matter of giving revised administrative approval was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Revised administrative approval is being granted to Government Medical College and Cancer Hospital, Aurangabad for the purchase of Slice City Scan 128 machine for the following table: Rs. 8,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores only) subject to the following conditions.

Conditions: (1) The Director and the concerned Dean shall ensure that no fresh appointment is required to handle the said machinery.
(2) In this regard, the prescribed procedure mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedures for the Purchase of Office to be made by the Government Department" should be complied with.
3) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the Dean shall ensure that the requirement of the said equipment is met and its price is not more than the market price.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall certify that the specifications of the equipment are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the equipment accordingly.
(5) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the Dean shall ensure that the proposed machinery material is not included in any other proposal and that the said equipment is required by the institution concerned.
6) It will be necessary to show the different prices of the main machine and its accessories.
7) The head of the institution has confirmed that the equipment is listed in the list approved by the Central Government under the relevant scheme.
The Dean of the institution concerned shall be responsible for submitting the utilization certificate of the funds made available by the Central and State Governments in this regard to the Central Government from time to time in the prescribed manner. The agency concerned will submit a compliance report to the government through the directorate.
9) While deciding on the purchase of equipment in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of grant sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
10) The State Level Procurement Committee shall comply with the provisions of the above Government Decision, Paragraph No. 3.1.2 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, dated 01.12.2016 under the heading" Price Estimation and Price Reasonableness."
The cost of machinery/ equipment complied with the said provision shall be revised and administrative approval shall be granted as per requirement.
