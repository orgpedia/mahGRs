Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid letter dated 11/07/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for providing smart/ hybrid classroom facilities/ equipment for teaching and learning in 24 Government Medical Colleges, 3 Government Dental Colleges and 5 Government Ayurveda Colleges under the control of the Department. Pursuant to the said proposal, administrative approval is being accorded for the purchase of the following table machines at a cost of Rs. 3,76,08, 960/- (Rupees Three Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs Eight Thousand Nine Hundred Sixty Five only) from the grant sanctioned under the respective head of accounts under the State Plan for the financial year 2022-23, subject to the following conditions.

Conditions: 1) The said administrative approval is being provided subject to availability of funds to the institution concerned from the grants sanctioned under the State Plan during the financial year 2022-23.
(2) In this regard, the prescribed procedure mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-I/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 and the ancillary Government Decisions issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedure for the procurement of offices by the Government Department" should be complied with.
The procurement of the said machinery should be done through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal as per the provisions of the procurement policy of Government Decision, Industries, Power and Labour Department, dated 01.12.2016 or Government Decision, Industries, Power and Labour Department No. 2014/ PR No. 215/ Industries-4, dated 24.08.2017.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) as well as the concerned Dean shall ensure that the requirement of the said equipment is met and its price is not more than the market price.
5) It has been ensured that no new construction, electrification or new designations are required for the said equipment.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall certify that the specifications of the said equipment are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the equipment accordingly.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall ensure that the proposed equipment is not included in any other proposal and that the said equipment is required by the institution concerned.
8) It will be necessary to show the different prices of the main machine and its accessories.

9) While deciding on the purchase of machinery in this regard, it should be ensured that no expenditure is incurred beyond the provision of subsidy sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
10) The State Level Procurement Committee shall comply with the provisions of the above Government Decision, Paragraph No. 3.1.2 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, dated 01.12.2016 under the heading" Price Estimation and Price Reasonableness."
The cost of equipment complying with this provision shall be subject to revised administrative approval as required.
11) Haffkine Corporation shall take action for publishing a single tender for machinery and equipment of similar nature and specifications. In this way, it will be possible for Haffkine Corporation to publish the tender, in accordance with which the Commissioner, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai, should place a demand for the purchase of machinery.
12) Haffkine Corporation will procure such items by aggregating the demand for machinery required by more than one organization, which is less than Rs 10 lakh.
