Introduction: The existing girls' hostel in the college campus at Government Ayurveda College, Nanded has a capacity of 50 girl students, the college's girl student capacity has reached 100 and the existing hostel is inadequate for the girls' accommodation, causing a lot of inconvenience to the girl students and many students have to stay outside the college. Accordingly, in accordance with the proposal of the Dean, Government Ayurveda College, Nanded, the Director, AYUSH, has received the budget and plans for the construction of the second and third floors of the girls' hostel in the college campus at Government Ayurveda College, Nanded, submitted by the Public Works Department, Nanded, for Rs. In pursuance of the proposal submitted for grant of administrative approval for Rs. 3,37,02, 692/-, the matter regarding grant of administrative approval for the said construction was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Provisions have been proposed for this work as explained in the introduction, as per the recapitulation sheet attached with it, and an amount of Rs. 368.74/-lakh has been considered. Administrative approval of the Government is also being granted subject to the conditions of fulfilling the following:
a) Before starting the work, the design, layout and detailed map should be started with the approval of the architect.
(b) When dealing with a provision made in a lump-sum manner, the work should be taken up only after making a detailed budget.
(a) In the course of its actual work, the Department of the Environment is governed by Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in A.I., dated 10th January, 2014.
