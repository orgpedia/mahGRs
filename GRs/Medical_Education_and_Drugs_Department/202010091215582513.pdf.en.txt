In the academic year 2019-20, due to the implementation of the said SEBC and EWS reservation, the admission of some meritorious students of the open category to medical and dental courses was affected and they had to take admission in private unaided colleges instead of government colleges. In-principle approval was granted under the relevant Government Order No. 1 as" a special case" and" a one-time case" to the students of open category of medical and dental courses, who were affected during the academic year 2019-20, to remit the academic fees of their respective courses through the Government. This Government decision was given ex-post facto approval in the Cabinet meeting held on 12/08/2020
Accordingly, the matter of issuing separate instructions regarding the criteria and procedure for eligibility in respect of reimbursement of educational fees to the above affected students was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In principle approval has been given under Government Decision dated 20/09/2019 for reimbursement of tuition fees charged by the competent authority for their respective courses to the meritorious students of open category of medical and dental courses affected in the academic year 2019-20 due to implementation of SEBC and EWS reservation. The final decision will be taken after the approval of the cabinet.
The criteria for eligibility for reimbursement of academic tuition fees and the detailed procedure for reimbursement of fees as per the above Government Decision shall be as follows:
(1) The course-wise list of students who are eligible for reimbursement of academic fees as per the aforesaid Government Order shall be prepared by the Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai after taking into account the admission records.
2) In case the SEBC/ EWS reservation is not applicable considering the preference received by the students at the time of admission, the students who would have got admission in government colleges but had to take admission in private colleges due to the implementation of this reservation will be included in this list.
