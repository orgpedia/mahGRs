Conditions: - 1) The said administrative approval is being granted out of the available funds sanctioned under the State Schemes of the institution concerned during the financial year - 2025-26.
2) In this regard, the Government Decision No.Bhankhas-2014 / No.82 / Part-I issued by the Department of Industries, Power and Labour regarding the "Manual of Procedure for Office Purchase to be carried out by the Government Department." / Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 and the prescribed procedure mentioned in the relevant Government Decisions should be complied with.
3) The procurement of these furniture items / equipment should be done as per the provisions of the Government Decision, Procurement Policy of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, dated 01.12.2016 or as prescribed by the Government Decision, Department of Industries, Power and Labour, No: Nos.214 / Q.215 / Udyog-4, dated 24.08.2017 on the Government e-Marketplace (98 / Maha-Etander Portal) with lower rates.
4) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the Dean concerned shall ensure that the furniture material / equipment is required and the price is not more than the market price.
5) It has been ensured that no new construction, electrification as well as new postings are required for this furniture material / equipment.
6) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the respective Dean shall certify that the specifications of the furniture material / equipment are comprehensive, comprehensive and of such a nature that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers / suppliers who can supply the equipment accordingly.
7 The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall ensure that the proposed furniture material / equipment is not included in any proposal elsewhere and that the equipment is required by the institution concerned.
8) While deciding on the purchase of furniture materials / equipment in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of grant-in-aid sanctioned under the head of accounts provided for administrative approval.
9) The State Level Procurement Committee shall comply with the provision in the aforesaid judgment, paragraph no. 3.1.2 of the Department of Industry, Power and Labour, dated 01.12.2016, titled "Price Estimation and Price Reasonability" in paragraph no. 3.1.2.1.
