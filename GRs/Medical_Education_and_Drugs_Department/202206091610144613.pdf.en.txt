Preamble: Reference No. 2 As per the aforesaid letter dated 09.05.2022, a proposal has been submitted to the Government to disburse the funds budgeted under the target of (10) Contractual Services for the financial year 2022-2023 for the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the Directorate. Accordingly, while submitting the proposal to the Finance Department, the Finance Department has approved the disbursement of 30% of the budgeted funds to 30 Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals (10) under the Directorate for contractual services. The funds were to be distributed to the institutions concerned. Accordingly, the following decisions have been taken:
Government Decision Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under Directorate of Medical Education and Research (10) have been allocated 21% of the budgeted funds for the financial year 2022-23 under the target of Contractual Services. Now, under this Government decision, 30 institutions of Statement A are being approved to disburse and spend funds to the extent of Rs.109884,000 as per the approval given by the Finance Department.
2.A total of Rs. 109884 thousand (Rupees Ten Crores Ninety Eight Lakhs Eighty Four Thousand only) has been disbursed on the Budget Fund Distribution System and the Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer should strictly follow the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book, Financial Rights Rule Book while spending this provision.
(3) This Government Decision is an informal reference to the Finance Department No. 272/2022/ Expenditure-13, dated 30.05.2022 is being issued as agreed.
