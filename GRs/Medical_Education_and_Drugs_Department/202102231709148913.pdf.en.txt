Government's decision:
Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Institute of Medical Sciences, Latur, as per the National Council of Medical Sciences (NCMC) norms, must have a separate department for sanctioning new PG seats in the psychiatry department, a 30-bed hospital room, an outpatient room and other academic programs. For the construction of this facility, administrative approval is being accorded for the construction of first floor on the building of the Institute for the Department of Psychiatry for an amount of Rs. 5,55,54, 517/- (Rupees Five Crores, Fifty Five Lakhs, Fifty Four Thousand, Five Hundred and Seventeen only) subject to the following terms and conditions.
