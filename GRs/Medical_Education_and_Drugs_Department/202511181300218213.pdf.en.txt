The Proposal -
The Government Medical College and Sir J.G. Group of Hospitals, Bombay was established in 1845 and is celebrating its 180th anniversary in 2025. The institute's work in the areas of medical education, research, and patient care is extensive and notable. A proposal was submitted to the government in a letter dated 08.04.2025 of the directorate to produce a documentary, coffee table book and postal stamp to disseminate this history and contribution to the wider public. Production and publication of documentaries, coffee table books and postal stamps for the 180th anniversary year of HS Government Medical College and Sir J.G. Group Hospital were under the consideration of the Government.
The Government's Decision-
On the occasion of the 180th anniversary of Government Medical College and Sir J.G. Group Hospitals, Mumbai, the Media Framework for the production and circulation of documentaries, coffee table books and postal stamps has been approved by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations with administrative sanction for the cost of Rs. 1,87,97, 000 / - (Rupees One Crore Eighty Seven SRI Ninety Seven Thousand only) as mentioned in the table below for the publication of documentaries and printing of coffee table books and postal stamps by the Department.
