Proposal for starting Diploma in Psychiatric Nursing (DPN) course under Centre of Excellence (B) at Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Thane and Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Pune through Public Health Department respectively. (1) and (2) were received by the Government from the Maharashtra State Board of Nursing and Paramedical Education. The Maharashtra State Board of Nursing and Paramedical Education (MSBSHSE) had recommended the proposal. In view of the above recommendation, the Government was considering to grant permission to start new Diploma in Psychiatric Nursing (DPN) courses at Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Thane and Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Pune for the academic year 2022-23 with an intake capacity of 20 (twenty) students each.
Government Decision: Maharashtra State Board of Nursing and Paramedical Education has issued Reference No. Considering the recommendation under clauses (1) and (2), a new Diploma in Psychiatric Nursing (DPN) with intake capacity of 20 (twenty) students each for the academic year 2022-23 at Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Thane and Regional Psychiatric Hospital, Pune under Centre of Excellence (B), Govt.

4) The institution must obtain the approval of the Council/ Board every year by duly meeting the criteria prescribed for the Diploma in Psychiatric Nursing (DPN) course by the Nursing Council of India, New Delhi, Maharashtra State Board of Nursing and Paramedical Education.
