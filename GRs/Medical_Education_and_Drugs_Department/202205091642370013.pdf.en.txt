Introduction: As the number of medical graduates in the state is low as per the standards of the World Health Organization and considering the medical facilities available in the state, Jalgaon district is comparatively backward in providing medical services, necessary construction, equipment and posts have been provided for the construction of a new" Government Integrated Medical Hub" at Jalgaon. This has been approved in the Cabinet Meeting held on 25.04.2017. Accordingly, the No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 11.05.2017, Government Homoeopathic College with an intake capacity of 50 students and an attached 25 bedded hospital have been approved to be set up in the said campus.
Therefore, a new Government Homoeopathic College at Jalgaon with an intake capacity of 50 students and an attached Government Homoeopathic Hospital with 25 in-patients are required to be created year-wise as per the required designation. 1 The proposal to approve the designation-wise post creation as per the government decision here was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: For the Government Homeopathic College at Jalgaon with an intake capacity of 50 students, a total of 67 regular posts as indicated in the prospectus A and services in the limit of total 43 manpower as indicated in the prospectus B are being approved to be taken through external agencies. Further, for the Government Homeopathic Hospital with 25 beds at Government Homeopathic College Affiliated Hospital, Jalgaon, a total of 44 regular posts are to be created as shown in FORM C and a total of 30 as shown in FORM D.
