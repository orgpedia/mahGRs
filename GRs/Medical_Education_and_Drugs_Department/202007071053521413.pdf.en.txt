Various measures are being taken to prevent the spread of Covid-19. Accordingly, various guidelines/ instructions in this regard are issued from time to time by the Ministry of Health& Family Welfare, Government of India, New Delhi. In this regard, instructions have been issued vide Government Circular dated 23.06.2020 regarding strengthening of COVID-19 testing in the State under COVID-19 measures. In view of the above, as per the directions of the Government of India vide letter dated 01.07.2020, measures are to be taken to ensure that maximum number of COVID-19 tests are conducted as early detection and containment of COVID-19 is possible through Track-Test-Trace-Isolate mechanism. In this regard, there have been frequent requests from the concerned private or similar establishments regarding permission to conduct combined Covid-19 tests of the manpower working in private and other establishments in the state. Accordingly, private or similar establishments, which are required to conduct COVID tests of the manpower working under their offices, are being permitted to conduct COVID-19 tests subject to the following provisions:
At least 50 employees of the concerned establishment will be required to be tested.

(2) The head of the establishment concerned or the authority appointed by him in this behalf shall submit an application in this behalf to the District Collector concerned in respect of rural and urban areas and to the Municipal Commissioner concerned in respect of metropolitan areas.
3. The application shall clearly state the system by which the COVID-19 test of the proposed employees (RTPCR, TUNAAT, CBNAAT, Antibody, Antigen, etc.) will be conducted and by whom.
What is the testing capacity of the testing laboratory mentioned by the concerned establishment in their application. The concerned should know how many samples are sent to the laboratory by the government. Thereafter, within the limit of the remaining testing capacity in addition to the number of government samples, private establishments should grant approval for testing of employees proposed by the private establishment at the relevant laboratory mentioned in the application.
Positive (+ ve) and Negative (-ve) reports of the concerned staff on COVID-19 test should be reported to ICMR as per the standards/ guidelines of Indian Council of Medical Research. Also, information about the test reports should be made available to the concerned as per the orders issued by the District Collector, Municipal Commissioner, Public Health Department from time to time.
The General Manager of the concerned private or similar establishment or the person authorized in this regard shall be responsible for taking necessary action in coordination with the concerned health authorities for sending the COVID-19 positive (+ ve) patients to the isolation ward for further treatment as per the prescribed norms and procedures and for sending their contacts to the institutional quarantine.
If action is not taken in this regard as per the prescribed procedure, the concerned Municipal Commissioner, Collector, competent authority should take action as per the provisions of the Epidemic Diseases Act 1897 implemented in the state under the notification of the Public Health Department dated 14.03.2020.
By order and in the name of the Governor of Maharashtra.
