Reference:- 1) Government Resolution, Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department No.MED1015/ PR No. 262/15/ Education-2, dated 16/01/2016.
The Proposal:
The stipend of Post Graduate students (Junior Resident and Senior Resident) in Government Medical Colleges, Government Dental Colleges and Government Ayurvedic Colleges in the State has been increased by Rs.5,000/-per month with retrospective effect from August, 2015 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. It was also decided to review every three years the increase in the salary of resident doctors. Accordingly, the Resident Doctors' Association' MARD' had requested the government to increase the amount of education allowance for resident doctors. A decision in this regard was pending with the government.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving a wholesale increase of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand only) per month with effect from 01.05.2020 for Post Graduate students (Junior Resident- 1/2/3 and Senior Resident- 1/2/3) in Government Medical and Dental Colleges in the State.
2. Dearness Allowance will be admissible to the students of the said Post-Graduate Course at the rate at which Dearness Allowance is admissible to the State Government employees from time to time.
3. The cost of such expenditure shall be met from the grants available under the head" 34- Scholarships and Scholarships" in the accounts of the institution concerned.
