Conditions:
The said administrative approval is being provided subject to availability of funds to the institution concerned from the grants available under the State Scheme during the financial year 2022-23.
(2) In this regard, the prescribed procedure prescribed in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-II/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 and the ancillary Government Decisions issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedure for the procurement of offices by the Government Department" should be complied with.
The procurement of these machinery should be done through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal as per the provisions of the procurement policy of Government Decision, Industries, Power and Labour Department, dated 01.12.2016 or as per the Government Decision, Industries, Power and Labour Department No: 2014/ PR No. 215/ Industries-4, dated 24.08.2017.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) as well as the concerned Dean shall ensure that the requirement of the said equipment is met and its price is not more than the market price.
