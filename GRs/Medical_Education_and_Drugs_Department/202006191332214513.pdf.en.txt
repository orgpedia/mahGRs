GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the aforesaid Government Decision, dated 15.10.2016, Government Medical College and Cancer Hospital, Aurangabad has been accorded the status of" State Cancer Institute" under Central State Share Scheme. Approval is being accorded for procurement of DOSIMENTRY EQUIPMENT AND ACCESSORIES along with Linear Accelerator from the Central State Share Fund at a cost of Rs. 1,99,51, 680/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety Nine Lakh One Thousand Sixty Eight only), subject to the following terms and conditions:
1.The funds received from the Centre and the State are available to the Institute for procurement of equipment.
The Directorate and the concerned Dean have ensured that no new construction or electrification is required for the said equipment.
The Director shall certify that the specifications of the said machinery are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that at least three tenders can be received from at least three suppliers who can supply the machinery accordingly.
4. Purchase of said equipment is being approved subject to designations.

The procurement process of the said machinery should be carried out in accordance with the above Government Decision, Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, dated 01.12.2016.
The procurement of the said equipment should be done through Haffkine Jeeva Drug Manufacturing Corporation as per the provisions of the above Government Decision, Public Health Department, dated 26.07.2017.
The Director, Medical Education and the concerned Dean have ensured that the requirement of the equipment is met and its price is not more than the market price.
The Director shall ensure that the said machinery is not included in any proposal elsewhere and that the said machinery is required by the institution.
The prices of main machinery and its spare parts must be shown separately.
(10) While taking decisions in this regard, it shall be ensured that no expenditure shall be incurred in excess of the provision of grant sanctioned under the head of accounts for which administrative approval has been granted.
11. Purchase Committee to comply with the provisions of the aforesaid Government Decision, Paragraph No. 3.1.2 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour, dated 01.12.2016 under the heading" Price Estimation and Price Reasonableness" in Circular No. 3.1.2.1. Accordingly, the price of the machine will be revised as per the requirement.
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the funds available under the head of the following accounts.
