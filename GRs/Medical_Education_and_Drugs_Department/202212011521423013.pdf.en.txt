Preamble:- The rates of educational fees in the colleges of various health science courses in the government and government-aided colleges in the state have been fixed by the government decision dated 01/03/2011. However, while fixing the rates of the said educational fee, the rates of the educational fee for the Homeopathy course have not been fixed as there is no government or government-aided Homeopathy college in the state.
Now, Government Homoeopathy College, Jalgaon has been approved to start a new homoeopathy college in the state from 2022-23 and admit students. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to fix the rates of educational fees for Government/ Government aided Homeopathy courses.
Government Decision:- The Government is approving to charge the rates of academic fees for the academic year 2022-23 for the courses of Ayurveda and Unani as per the provisions of Government Decision dated 01/03/2011 for the academic year 2022-23.
This fee will be applicable from the academic year 2022-23 and from the academic year 2023-24 onwards, the fee will be increased by 10% for each subsequent academic year, as per the Government decision dated 01/03/2011, till the next academic year.
In addition to the tuition fee, the rates of fees such as Development Fund, Admission Fee, Gymkhana Fee, Hostel Fee, Library Reservation and Library Fee etc. shall also be payable at the prevailing rates applicable to Ayurveda/ Unani courses.
