GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the Government Decision dated 26.07.2017 of the Public Health Department, it has been made mandatory to purchase the purchase of medicinal and surgical materials, chemicals and equipment etc. for the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals and Institutions under the Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department from the Haffkine Jeeva Drug Manufacturing Corporation. In the Monsoon Session of September, 2020, Rs. A proposal for a supplementary demand of Rs. 300.00 crore has been approved. Accordingly, as proposed by the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai, the purchase of Emergency Medicine required for Covid, estimated at Rs. 53,73,72, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Three Crore Seventy Three Lakh Seventy Two Thousand only) for non-ICU drugs for COVID-19 hospitals, is being accorded administrative approval by Haffkine Corporation subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) As per the Government decision dated 26.07.2017 of the Department of Public Health, the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals and Institutions under the Department of Medical Education and Medicines,

(2) The Directorate shall verify the requirement/ demand of the said medicinal and surgical materials, chemicals and equipment etc.
It has been ensured that the specifications of the said medicinal and surgical materials, chemicals and equipment etc. are comprehensive and comprehensive, so that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the same.
4) In this regard, the Government Decision No. 2014/ Q. 82/ Part/ Industry-4, issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, titled" Manual of Procedures for Office Procurement to be made by the Government Department." The prescribed procedure as mentioned in 01.12.2016 should be followed.
5) It should be ensured that the items proposed for purchase are not left unused and are used within the stipulated time.
6) It should be ensured that there is no duplication of purchase matters.
7) The Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai shall take immediate further necessary action to obtain the said medicine and related matters from Haffkine Pharmaceuticals Corporation.
8) Under no circumstances should be spent more than the limit of available funds.
The Director, Medical Education, Mumbai vide his letter dated 2210-3314 vide Supplementary Demands for Grants to contain the spread of COVID-19, may transfer the amount of Rs. 53,73,72, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Three Crore Seventy Three Lakh Seventy Two Thousand only) to Haffkine Corporation immediately.
The Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai and the Joint Director, Directorate of Medical Education and Research (Procurement Cell), Mumbai shall be solely responsible for fulfilling the conditions mentioned in the above Annexure 1 to 9 and to ensure that the procurement is as per rules and requirements.
