Government Decision: The building at Grant Government Medical College in the premises of Sir J. G. Group of Hospitals under the department is old and it is very necessary to install broken couches on the second floor of the building and install broken wooden planks and other ancillary construction works etc. As per the proposal received by the Department, administrative approval is being given for the repair and renovation of the second floor of the Grant Government Medical College building in the premises of Sir J.G. Group of Hospitals, Mumbai at a budget of Rs 1,33,21, 378/- (Rupees One Crore, Thirty Three Lakhs, Twenty One Thousand, Three Hundred and Seventy Eight Only).
