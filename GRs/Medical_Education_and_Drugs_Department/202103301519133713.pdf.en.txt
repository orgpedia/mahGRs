GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the above Government Decision dated 01.12.2018, Grant Government Medical College, Mumbai was granted approval to purchase Automated Cover Sleeper from the funds under District Annual Plan. Request for approval of revised price of the said machinery has been made by the Directorate vide letter dated 31.03.2021.
2. Grant Government Medical College, Mumbai has not been able to procure the machine as the tender floated by Haffkine Corporation for the purchase of" Automated Cover Sleeper" has fetched a minimum price of Rs. 31,24,635/- and funds are not sufficient for the purchase of Automated Cover Sleeper. The District Planning Committee, Mumbai has sanctioned funds to the funded institution under the District Annual Plans for the financial year 2020-21. Out of the sanctioned grant, Rs. 31,24,635/- (Rupees Thirty One Lakhs, Twenty Four Thousand, Six Hundred and Thirty Five) has been spent for procurement.
