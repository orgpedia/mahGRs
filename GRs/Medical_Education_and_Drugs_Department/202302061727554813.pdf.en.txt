Government's decision:
In pursuance of the aforesaid letter dated 02.01.2023, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for procurement of new equipment (furniture material) for the newly established Government Medical College, Parbhani, under the control of the Department for the first year MBBS admitted students. In pursuance of the said proposal, an amount of Rs. 1,22,39, 024/- (Rupees One Crore, Twenty Two Lakhs, Nineteen Thousand, Twenty Four Only) is being administratively sanctioned from the grants sanctioned under the State Schemes during the financial year 2022-23, subject to the following conditions as decided by the Government.

(i) The said administrative approval is being provided subject to availability of funds to the institution concerned from the grants sanctioned under the State Plan.
(2) In this regard, the prescribed procedure mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedures for the Purchase of Office to be made by the Government Department" should be complied with.
The procurement of these furniture items should be done through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal as per the provisions of the procurement policy dated 01.12.2016 of Government Decision, Department of Industry, Energy and Labour or as per the Government Decision, Department of Industry, Energy and Labour No.
4) The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall ensure that the requirement of such furniture material is met and its price is not more than the market price.

The cost of machinery/ equipment complied with the said provision shall be revised and administrative approval shall be granted as per requirement.
