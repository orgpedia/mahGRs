Preamble: In the emergency situation arising out of the outbreak of COVID-19, the Government was considering the issue of ex-post facto approval for providing services through external sources to a total of 226 posts, including 11 posts of attendants, 104 posts of headmistresses and 111 posts of class-4 cadre in Sassoon Sarvachachar Hospital, Pune. Also, the issue of approval to spend the required amount of Rs. 5,96,00, 000/- from the personal provident fund was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Post facto approval is being accorded for outsourcing the services of the posts of Servitors, Superintendents and Class-IV in the Sassoon Sarvachachar Hospital, Pune for a period of 8 months (from 01.05.2020 to 31.12.2020) during the emergency situation arising out of the outbreak of COVID-19 virus, subject to the following conditions:
The 11 vacant posts of the service personnel should be filled through external sources.
2. 43 vacant posts of Superintendents to be filled up through external sources.
61 posts of Superintendents (104-43= 61) and 111 posts of Class-IV cadre are being sanctioned for a period of 8 months and these posts are to be filled up through external agencies.
4. The cost of filling up these posts through external agencies is Rs. 5,96,00, 000/- to be made from the self-financing account of Sassoon Sarvachai Hospital, Pune Page 1 of 2
