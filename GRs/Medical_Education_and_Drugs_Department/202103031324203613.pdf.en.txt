Introduction: Considering the number of government buildings in the vicinity of the hospital at M.A. Podar Hospital, Worli, Mumbai, a total of 13 security guards are required, 12 security guards and 1 Chief Security Officer, as informed by the Deputy Engineer, Public Works Department, Worli, Mumbai-18. The government was considering giving administrative approval to the expenditure incurred on the monthly salary of the 13 security guards appointed by the external agency.
Government Decision: As a demand is to be made to the District Security Guard Board to provide the services of a total of 13 security guards, 12 security guards and 1 chief security officer, for the security of the hospital premises in M. A. Podar Hospital, Worli, Mumbai, through an external system, the salary of these 13 security guards is estimated to be Rs. 3,25,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand only) and an annual estimate of Rs. 39,00,000/- (Rs. Forty-nine lakh only) is being administratively approved through this.
