Preamble: In pursuance of the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1, approval has been accorded for establishment of a new Government Medical College at Osmanabad with an intake capacity of 100 students and a 430-bedded hospital by upgrading the District General Hospital attached thereto.
The Government Medical College, Osmanabad has been approved by the Medical Assessment and Rating Board (MARB), National Medical Commission, New Delhi for the academic year 2022-23 subject to fulfilment of conditions for admission of 100 students as per letter of reference (2). Therefore, it was under consideration of the government to allow the college to admit students for the academic year 2022-23 with an intake capacity of 100 students.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Government Medical College, Osmanabad has been approved by the Medical Assessment and Rating Board (MARB) of the National Medical Commission, New Delhi for the academic year 2022-23 subject to the terms and conditions for admission of 100 students as per letter of reference (2). Accordingly, permission is being granted to the said Medical College to admit 100 students for the academic year 2022-23 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The intake capacity of Government Medical College, Osmanabad Medical College will be 100 as per the norms of the Central Government.
2) The course should be affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik and then the students should be admitted.
