GOVERNMENT DECISION: The review of sanctioned posts for the Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune has been completed and approved by the Government Resolution at Reference No. (1).
After the said review, the Government have approved the continuation of 17 temporary posts in the approved pattern of Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune till 28/02/2009 vide Government Resolution No. Out of these 17 temporary posts, the number of employees working in the notified posts of Driver (Class-3) and Safaigar (Class-4) has been included in the vacant posts of Junior Scribe and Safaigar in the organization. The remaining 15 temporary posts have been approved to be continued till 28.2.2018 as per the Government decision issued from time to time and also as per the decision of the Government vide Reference No.3. Since the 15 temporary posts indicated in the prospectus A and B accompanying this Government decision are necessary for the continuation of the functioning of the Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, the Government hereby grants approval for the continuation of the same from 1/3/2018 to 30/09/2018 subject to the following conditions, as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments by the Finance Department under Reference No.
