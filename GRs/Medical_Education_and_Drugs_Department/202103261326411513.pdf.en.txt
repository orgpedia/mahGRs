Government's decision:
The Rural Health Training Team, Paithan was granted administrative approval for procurement of various equipment as per the aforesaid Government Decision dated 27th March, 2020.
However, in view of the situation arising out of Corona Virus infection in the last financial year, in order to regulate the treasury operations, the acceptance of subsidy related payments for the current financial year in all treasuries/ sub-treasuries and accounts offices should be continued only till 27th March, 2020. Thereafter, no other payments shall be accepted except for Corona related matters.
Due to this situation, the institution concerned has not been able to pay the payments for the purchase of machinery mentioned in the attached Annexure. as well
