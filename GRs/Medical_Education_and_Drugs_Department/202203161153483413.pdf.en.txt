The Pune Municipal Corporation Medical Education Trust (PMC-MET) operated Bharat Ratna Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical College and Teaching Hospital, Pune was granted the Certificate of Necessity to start a medical college with an intake capacity of 100 students vide letter No.
Now, Bharat Ratna Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical College and Teaching Hospital, Pune, a Medical College run by the Pune Municipal Corporation Medical Education Trust (PMC-MET), has been approved by the Central Government for the academic year 2021-22 subject to fulfilment of conditions for admission capacity of 100 students under letter no. Therefore, the government was considering allowing the college to admit 100 students for the academic year 2021-22.
Government Decision: Bharat Ratna Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical College and Teaching Hospital, Pune, a Medical College run by Pune Municipal Corporation Medical Education Trust (PMC-MET), has been approved by the Central Government for the academic year 2021-22 subject to fulfilment of conditions for admission capacity of 100 students under letter of reference (2). Accordingly, permission is being granted to the said Medical College to admit 100 students for the academic year 2021-22 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
The admission capacity of Bharat Ratna Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical College and Teaching Hospital, Pune, run by the Pune Municipal Corporation Medical Education Trust (PMC-MET), will be 100 as per the central government.
