The Proposal:
As per the above decision, dated 26.06.2018, approval has been accorded for setting up of 100 bedded Cancer Hospital in the premises of Government Medical College and Hospital, Chandrapur Institute through State Government (Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals), Tata Trusts and District Mineral Foundation, Chandrapur. Cancer Hospital
As per the provisions of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed between the concerned, the government was considering the appointment of a representative from the civil society organization in the" Special Purpose Vehicle" board formed for the operation of these hospitals.
Government's decision:
The Government Medical College and Hospital, Chandrapur will be constructed under the control of the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals.

The 100-bed cancer hospital will be set up by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Mumbai, District Mineral Foundation, Chandrapur and Tata Trusts.
The Government has approved the appointment of Dr. Pravin Narendra Pant, Chandrapur as Director on the Board of Directors of the Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV). The appointments are being made in accordance with the provisions of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed between the two sides.
