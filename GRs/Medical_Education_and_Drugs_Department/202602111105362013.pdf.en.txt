Terms and Conditions: -
1) The requirement / demand for medicines, surgicals, chemicals, consumables and related items to be procured from the Maharashtra Medical Supplies Procurement Authority should be verified by the Directorate.
2) The technical specifications of the items to be purchased should be as per the specifications agreed by the respective experts (Technical Committee / Officials). Also, it should be ensured that the technical specification is not designed to benefit any particular company.
3) In this regard, the "Manual of Procedure for Office Procurement to be carried out by the Government Department" issued by the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, Government Decision No. \\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\ / Industry-4, date 01.12.2016 to comply with the prescribed procedure.
4) Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) guidelines should be strictly followed while executing the entire procurement process.
5) The proposed purchase rates are approximate when given administrative approval. Therefore, the minimum rates available on the GeM portal, other state contracts, National Medical Devices Authority should be taken into account. Also, the bid should be recorded as the estimated price at the time of placing the bid and the price should be fixed after the purchase process. This is the price below the market price and the rate at which the supplier concerned has supplied the item elsewhere. It should be ensured that the rate is not higher than that.
6) Under no circumstances should it be spent beyond the limit of available funds.
