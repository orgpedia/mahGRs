Conditions: - 1) This administrative sanction 2025-26 is being granted subject to the availability of funds under the State Plan in this financial year.
2) In this regard, the Government Decision No.
Bhankhas-2014 / P.No.82 / Part- / Industry-4, dated 09.92.2098 and the prescribed procedure mentioned in the relevant Government Decisions should be complied with.
3) The proposed purchase rates are approximate when given administrative approval. Hence, the prices should be fixed at the end of the procurement process. The Commissioner (Medical Education) as well as the Administrator concerned shall ensure that the rate is below the market price and does not exceed the rate at which the supplier concerned has supplied these items elsewhere.
4) The Commissioner (Medical Education) as well as the Principal concerned shall ensure that the equipment is required and the price is not more than the market price.
5) The equipment is single-source certified by the Commissioner, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai and the competent authority concerned. In case of any future issue, the entire responsibility will rest with the concerned agency and directorate.
6) The concerned have certified that the equipment is proprietary in nature.
However, the Commissioner, Medical Education should ensure that there are no other products that meet the specifications mentioned for the proposed equipment.
7) While deciding on the purchase of machinery in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of grant-in-aid sanctioned under the head of accounting provided for administrative approval.
8) The Commissioner, Medical Education shall take action on the purchase by verifying the updated and prescribed certificate that the proposed equipment is of a proprietary nature.
9) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the Dean concerned shall ensure that the BAA of the proposed equipment is not included in any other proposal / purchase and that the equipment is required by the institution concerned.
10) The aforesaid Judgment, Department of Industries, Power and Labour, Govt. Paragraph No in 1.12.2016.
3.1.2 under the heading "Price Forecasting and Price Reasonability" in Paragraph No. Comply with the provision in 3.1.2.1.
11) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the Principal concerned should ensure that the machine / device is single-source.
12) The Commissioner (Medical) and the Superintendent concerned shall ensure that the machine / device is useful and that the price offered is reasonable as per the market price.
