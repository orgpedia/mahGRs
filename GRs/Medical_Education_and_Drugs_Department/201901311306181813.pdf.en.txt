Introduction: Certification in Yoga and Naturopathy, Yoga and Naturopathy by Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik
Proposals were invited under the provisions of Section 64 (3) of the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences Act, 1998, to start four courses- Diploma of Naturopathy, Bachelor of Yoga and Naturopathy and Master of Yoga and Naturopathy. The first year curriculum of Bachelor of Naturopathy and Yoga Sciences course as well as the minimum standards of academic facilities, faculty, etc. have been prepared by the University. The proposal to grant approval to start the course was submitted by the university through a letter in this regard. Accordingly, the matter of granting recognition to the Bachelor of Naturopathy and Yoga Sciences (B.N.Y.S.) course was under the consideration of the Government.
New Yoga and Naturopathy Degree by Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik
The Bachelor of Naturopathy and Yoga Sciences (B.N.Y.S.) course is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions.
The syllabus and criteria for the course" Bachelor of Naturopathy and Yoga Sciences (B.N.Y.S.)" should be determined by the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences.
After the course and criteria of the course are determined, they should be submitted for approval by the government through the university.
