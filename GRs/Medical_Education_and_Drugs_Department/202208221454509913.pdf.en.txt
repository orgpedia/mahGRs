Preamble: A sum of Rs. 12,22,63, 000/- has been sanctioned to Sir J.G. Group of Hospitals, Mumbai for the financial year 2021-22 for the purchase of Cardiac Cathlab System from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme. 5 Administrative approval was granted in accordance with the government decision mentioned here. As the funds sanctioned for the purchase of the machine in accordance with the said administrative approval have not been transferred to Haffkine Corporation on technical grounds, the funds made available to the Institute for the financial year 2021-22 for the purchase of the said machine have lapsed and the Institute has not been able to procure the said machine. Since it is essential for the institution to procure the said machine in the interest of the patient and since the committee constituted at the Directorate level to determine the technical specifications of the machines and equipment to be procured for Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals through the e-device system has partially changed the name of the said machine on the e-device system, the Directorate in pursuance of the purchase of the said machine has issued Reference No. 6 The matter of granting fresh administrative approval along with availability of funds to the proposal submitted under the letter mentioned herein was under consideration of the Government. Following this, the government took decision:

i.The said administrative approval is being granted for the purchase of the proposed machinery subject to availability of funds in the respective head of accounts under the State Schemes during the financial year 2022-23.
ii.The cost of the equipment is verified by the concerned agency and directorate.
In this regard, the prescribed procedure mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 and the ancillary Government Decisions issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedure for the procurement of offices to be carried out by the Government Department" should be followed.
