Preamble: As per the aforesaid Government letter dated 24.06.2020, approval was accorded for procurement of machinery worth Rs. 6.00 crore to Sassoon Sarvacharan Sanstha, Pune under COVID-19 preventive measures. In this regard, as the Sassoon Pune Institute does not require ventilators at present, a proposal to purchase other equipment in lieu of the said equipment was submitted to the Government vide letter dated 08.12.2020 of the said Directorate.
Government's decision:
In view of the proposal received from the Directorate, the approval given to the B.J. Government Medical College and Sassoon Sarvacharan Hospital, Pune for the purchase of the above mentioned equipment in the Government letter dated 24.06.2020 is being cancelled. In lieu of the said machinery, the Institute is being granted approval to procure the following equipment required under COVID-19 measures for an amount of Rs. 5,92,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores, Ninety Two Lakh only) subject to the terms and conditions as specified in the Government Order.
