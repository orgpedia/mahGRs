Government's decision:
The Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government under the above mentioned letter dated 09.03.2021 for the purchase of machinery and equipment from the sanctioned grant for the purchase of machinery and equipment under the District Annual Plan for the financial year 2020-21 to the Government Medical College and Hospital and Nagpur under the control of the Department. In pursuance of the said proposal, Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur has been allotted Rs. 11,55,22, 064/- (Rupees Eleven Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand Sixty Four only).
