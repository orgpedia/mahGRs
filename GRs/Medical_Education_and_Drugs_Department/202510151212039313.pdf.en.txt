The Proposal:
Administrative sanction for construction of 160 Girls Hostel (2 buildings) building at Shri Vasantrao Naik Zaskiya Medical College, Yavatmal under the aegis of the Department has been granted vide Government Decision dated 04.03.2016 amounting to Rs. However, the increase in the Goods and Services Tax, the additional cost of price hike, the new provision of attached toilets-bathrooms, common rooms, TV rooms, gymnasiums, visitor rooms, etc., to each room in the hostel building has led to an increase in the construction budget. A proposal was placed before the Expenditure Priority Committee for approval of a revised budget of Rs. 16.96 crore to include and complete these works. Since the revised proposal was agreed to by the Expenditure Priorities Committee, the issue of granting administrative approval to the revised proposal was under consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
