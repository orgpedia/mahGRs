Introduction: Government Medical/ Dental/ Ayurveda Colleges and Hospitals are functioning under the control of Department of Medical Education and Drugs. As per the approval given by the government under the control of the department, government medical colleges will be operational in Alibaug, Satara, Sindhudurg, Osmanabad etc. in the near future. The department will have to carry out the work of these government medical colleges and hospitals as well as their ancillary buildings.
The department gets the works of Government Medical/ Dental/ Ayurveda Colleges and Hospitals functioning under the control of the department at a huge budgetary cost.
Under the Government Decisions dated 08.02.2018 and 24.12.2018, the Department of Medical Education has been empowered to take up works of Rs.25 crore and above in the above mentioned institutions under the control of the Department through Central Public Sector Undertaking Company or Public Works Department. As per the provisions of the said Government decision, if it is decided to carry out the construction of the above budgeted cost through the Public Works Department as per the Government Direction, then the matter of issuing a revised procedure for carrying out the construction through the Public Works Department was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As per the approval of the government, the government medical/ dental/ ayurveda colleges and hospitals under the control of the department have to be constructed at a cost of Rs 25 crore and above.

The construction department should take action on the implementation through the following procedures.
(1) The Director of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai shall prepare a consolidated budget from the Public Works Department concerned, after obtaining approval for the establishment of Government Medical Colleges under the control of the Department or in the future.
The Medical Education Department will take approval of the High Powered Committee headed by the Chief Secretary in accordance with the prevailing procedure and issue administrative approval with the approval of the Government.
3. The Government shall give directions as to the agency through which the construction of administrative approval should be carried out. According to the directive, the tender process for appointing project management consultants for the construction work to be carried out by the Public Works Department will be carried out by a committee headed by the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai. Joint Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai will be the Member Secretary of the Committee. The members of the committee will include Chief Engineer (Power), Public Works Department, Superintending Engineer (Construction), Public Works Department, Superintending Engineer (Conceptual), Public Works Department, Chief/ Deputy Chief Architect, Principal, JJ School of Architecture, Mumbai, Executive Engineer concerned, Public Works, concerned Dean, Government Medical College and Hospital. The tender for the appointment of Project Management Consultant for the proposed construction project will be published by the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai.
4. The above Committee shall recommend a qualified Project Management Consultant to the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals after evaluating the tender process in accordance with the prescribed procedures.
The recommendation received for selection/ appointment of Project Management Consultant by the said Committee shall be submitted by the Department of Medical Education and Drugs to the Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Finance) vide Government Resolution, Medical Education and Drugs Department, dated 08.02.2018.
After the approval of the said Committee, the Government decision regarding the appointment of the Project Management Consultant will be issued by the Department of Medical Education, in accordance with this decision, the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai will give the commissioning order to the Project Management Consultant.

After the commissioning order, the project management consultant will prepare the detailed budget, construction schedule, RFP, etc., as per the DPR TEFR, etc. as well as the construction plan of the Government Medical College and Hospital as per the standards of the National Council of Medical Sciences.
The Public Works Department will vet the budget prepared for the construction and take action with regard to providing technical approval to it.
The Public Works Department will take all permissions required for the construction project.
The Public Works Department will issue the combined tender of architectural and electrical works required for the proposed construction on turn key basis. A project manager will help you with this.
The technical scrutiny of the tenders and financial obligations received at the end of the construction tender will be done by the Project Management Consultant, Public Works Department and the proposal of the qualified tenderer will be submitted to the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals through the Office of the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai.
The proposal for acceptance of the tender received by the Medical Education Department will be submitted by the Medical Education Department before the" Tender Acceptance Committee" headed by the Additional Chief Secretary (Finance).
13.Now, after the decision of the Acceptance Committee, the Medical Education Department shall inform the Public Works Department about the issuance of the work order to the tenderer.
The proposed construction will be executed by the Public Works Department along with the Project Management Consultant after issuing the commissioning order to the tenderer concerned.
15.Sadar The funds required for the construction of the project will be made available to the Construction Department from the head of accounts under the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals.
The Public Works Department will supervise the quality and standard of construction.
17.The monthly progress report of the project will be certified by the Project Management Consultant, Public Works Department and submitted to the Medical Education Department.
The Project Management Consultant shall submit all information required by the Medical Education Department regarding the construction to the Department.
