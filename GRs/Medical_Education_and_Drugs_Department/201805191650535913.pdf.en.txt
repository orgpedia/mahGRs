The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 30.01.2018 has approved the construction of Government Medical/ Dental/ Ayurveda Colleges and Hospitals under the control of Medical Education Department at an estimated cost of Rs. 25.00 crore and above.
A policy decision has been taken to take the public sector companies NBCC, HLL and HSCC, which are mainly associated with the construction of hospitals, on the government panel, to build these structures on a turn-key basis by asking for rates from the company with the least debt or from other companies. The Government have taken steps in this regard under sub-sections (1) and (2) of the Act. In pursuance of this decision, the Government was considering the issue of providing a new head of account for providing funds for such construction related works.

Government's decision:
Government Medical/ Dental/ Ayurveda Colleges and Hospitals under the control of the Department of Medical Education with an outlay of Rs. 25.00 crore and above.
The construction works are being carried out by the Central Government through the Central Public Sector Undertakings (CPSEs) namely NBCC, HLL and HSCC, which are mainly engaged in the construction of hospitals.
The companies selected to carry out the said construction on turn key basis by following the government procedure by seeking rates from the company having minimum debt or other companies to budget the funds for the construction and the funds received will be utilized.
The government is approving the creation of an accounting head with the following objectives:
