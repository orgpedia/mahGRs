Preamble: Government Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 11.05.2017, approval has been given to start a Government Medical College with an intake capacity of 100 students and a 500-bed hospital at Jalgaon. The government was considering allowing the students to open their own provident fund account for depositing educational fees, security deposit, gymkhana fees, development fund, hostel fees and other fees, laboratory fees and other allowable expenses from it. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The students of Government Medical College, Jalgaon will have to pay the educational fees, security deposit, gymkhana fees, development fund, hostel fees and other fees, laboratory fees and other amounts admissible from it as well as reference no. 2 The Government hereby approve the opening of a Personal Loan Account (PLA) in the name of the Dean, Government Medical College, Jalgaon, as per the provisions of Rule 494 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, Clause-I, to meet the expenditure for the matters prescribed under the Government Decision dated 04.03.2020.

2.It will be mandatory to follow the guidelines and financial rules set by the government from time to time while using the funds deposited in this account. It will also be necessary to comply with the provisions of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules.
The amount in the said account shall be spent in accordance with the financial powers conferred on it for various purposes in accordance with the Government Decision, Finance Division No. VYPR-2013/ P.S. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17.04.2015 and amendments thereto from time to time.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the concerned institution will be responsible for monthly/ quarterly reconciliation of the amount deposited and spent on this account with the Accountant General's Office.
A detailed monthly statement will be submitted to the Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education and Research through the institute. A quarterly report will be submitted to the government by the directorate after examining it.
The Dean, Government Medical College, Jalgaon will be responsible for ensuring that the transactions on this account are carried out in accordance with the government/ financial rules and to maintain proper control over this account.
6. It is necessary to carry out regular transactions on the self-financing account. Also, it will be the responsibility of the Dean, Government Medical College, Jalgaon, to ensure that the account is not closed due to non-transaction.
7. The financial transactions on this account need to be regularly audited.
