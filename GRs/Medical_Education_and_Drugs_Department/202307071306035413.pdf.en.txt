Introduction: Considering the increasing number of students in government medical colleges and colleges in the state, it is necessary to provide state-of-the-art and innovative teaching methods to the students studying in the respective institutions and colleges. Considering the increasing cost of many medical textbooks, journals, magazines, research papers, medical related materials and related matters, there are difficulties in making these materials available in the respective colleges. Also, since the number of printed books available in the institutions is less, these books cannot be used by all the students at the same time. These books are also more likely to get damaged quickly due to frequent use by students. The e-Digital Library will provide world-class up-to-date medical materials to the faculty and students of Government Medical Colleges and Dental Colleges easily on mobile and tab. This will improve their quality and they will automatically receive the updated version of these books and educational materials. The e-library is also required for organizations like NAAC and regulatory bodies like NMC/ MCI/ DCI/ INC.
In view of the above fact, the Directorate of Medical Education and Research vide the aforesaid Reference No. As per the proposal submitted through the letter here, the matter of approving the proposal to start e-digital library in all government medical colleges and dental colleges under the department was under the consideration of the government.
