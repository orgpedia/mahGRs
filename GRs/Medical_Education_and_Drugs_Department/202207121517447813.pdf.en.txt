Government's decision:
As per the aforesaid letter dated 24/06/2022, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for procurement of necessary furniture material for the newly established Government Medical College, Sindhudurg under the control of the Department. In pursuance of the said proposal, administrative approval is being accorded for the purchase of furniture materials mentioned in the table below at a cost of Rs. 61,25,999/- (Rupees Sixty-one Lakh, Twenty-five Thousand, Nine Hundred and Ninety Nine only) from the grant sanctioned under the State Scheme during the financial year 2022-23, subject to the following conditions.

Conditions:
(i) The said administrative approval is being provided subject to availability of funds to the institution concerned from the grants sanctioned under the State Plan.
(2) In this regard, the prescribed procedure mentioned in the Government Decision No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part-II/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016 and the corresponding Government Decisions issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor regarding the" Manual of Procedure for the Purchase of Offices by the Government Department" should be complied with.
3. Procedure for procurement of such furniture material" Procurement of drugs, related consumables and medical devices Haffkine Bio-Pharmaceuticals Corporation Ltd.
The decision of the Government issued regarding the transfer should be made in accordance with the Public Health Department, Purchase No. 2016/ PR No. 240/ Health-8, dated 26.07.2017.
4) The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall ensure that the requirement of such furniture material is met and its price is not more than the market price.
5) It has been ensured that no new construction, electrification or new design is required for this furniture material.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall certify that the specifications of the said furniture material/ equipment are comprehensive, comprehensive and such that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers/ suppliers who can supply the equipment accordingly.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) shall ensure that the proposed furniture material is not included in any other proposal and that such material is required by the institution concerned.
