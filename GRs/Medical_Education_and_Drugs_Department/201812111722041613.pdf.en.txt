About the Institute: The Institute is run by Rani Jankibai Saheb Medical College, Sawantwadi. The college is affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (MUHS), Nashik and offers BAMS (Ayurveda) degree course with an intake capacity of 40.
During the financial year 2003-04, the Government has decided to sanction 100% wage subsidy to the private Government aided Ayurveda and Unani Institutions in the State with effect from January, 2004. Accordingly, Bhaisaheb Sawant Ayurveda Mahavidyalaya, Sawantwadi has been awarded Rs. 1,18,269/- is being paid as recurring subsidy. Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning grant to the institution was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government Decision, Medical Education and Medicines Department No. ADR-MUMS/ 2002/ PR No. 116/02/ AYU-2, dated 05/12/2003, has made 100% salary subsidy available to the aided Ayurveda and Unani Colleges and affiliated hospitals run by private institutions in the State and the Government provides subsidy for the salary expenditure of teachers and non-teaching staff.
Accordingly, the final instalment of the grant-in-aid for the years 2007-08 to 2016-2017 of the private government-aided Bhaisaheb Sawant Ayurveda College, Sawantwadi, has been completed and the final instalment of the grant-in-aid for the year 2016-2017 has been increased to Rs. 1,18,269/- (Rupees One Lakh, Eighteen Thousand, Two Hundred and Seventy-Nine only) is being sanctioned under this Government Resolution.
