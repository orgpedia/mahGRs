The Proposal:
Gangadhar Shastri Gune Ayurveda Mahavidyalaya, Ahmednagar is a private government-aided Ayurveda institution run by Ayurveda Prasarak Mandali, Pune. The college offers BAMS (Ayurveda) degree course with intake capacity of 50 and has a sanctioned bed capacity of 100 in Sheth Tarachand Ramnath Dedicated Ayurveda Hospital, Pune affiliated hospital.
During the financial year 2003-04, the Government has decided to sanction 100% wage subsidy to the private Government aided Ayurveda and Unani Institutions in the State with effect from January, 2004. Accordingly, Gangadhar Shastri Gune Ayurveda Mahavidyalaya, Ahmednagar has been awarded Rs. 2,63,833/- is being paid as recurring subsidy. Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning grant to the institution was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government have decided to provide 100% grant-in-aid to the aided Ayurveda and Unani colleges and affiliated hospitals run by private institutions in the State and to provide subsidy for the salary expenditure of teachers and non-teaching staff as per the Government Decision, Medical Education and Medicines Department No.ADR-MUMS/ 2002/ PR.No. 116/02/ AYUSH-2, dated 05/12/2003 with effect from January, 2004. Accordingly, the final instalment of the grant-in-aid for the years 2007-08 to 2016-2017 of the private government-aided Gangadhar Shastri Gune Ayurveda College, Ahmednagar, has been completed and the final instalment of the grant-in-aid for the year 2016-2017 is Rs. An amount of Rs. 2,63,833/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Eight Hundred Thirty Three only) is being sanctioned under this Government decision.
The cost of the project is Rs. 2,63,833/- (Rupees Two Lakh Thirty Three Thousand Eight Hundred Thirty Three only) to be met out of the approved provision for the financial year 2018-2019 and the said expenditure to be covered under the following head.
