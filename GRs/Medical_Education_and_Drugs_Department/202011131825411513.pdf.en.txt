Preamble: The Government have approved the establishment of Government Medical College at Nandurbar with an intake capacity of 100 students and a 500-bed hospital as per the Government Resolution No. 1. The Government Medical College, Nandurbar has been granted permission by the National Medical Commission, New Delhi for the academic year 2020-21 subject to fulfilment of conditions for admission of 100 students under Reference Letter No. Therefore, the matter of granting permission to the said college for the academic year 2020-21 for an intake capacity of 100 students was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Government Medical College, Nandurbar has been approved by the National Medical Commission, New Delhi for the academic year 2020-21 subject to the terms and conditions specified for admission of 100 students under Reference Letter No.
Accordingly, permission is being granted to the said Medical College to admit 100 students for the first year 2020-21 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The intake capacity of the Government Medical College, Nandurbar will be 100 as per the norms of the Central Government.
2) The course should be affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik and admissions should be made thereafter.
(3) The State/ Central Government. Supreme Court or High Court. The prescribed procedure for admission to the said course should be followed as per the orders passed by the High Court from time to time.
