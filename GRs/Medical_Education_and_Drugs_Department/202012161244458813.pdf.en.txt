The Proposal:
A budget of Rs. 39.83 crore was presented for the main administrative building, lecture hall, library etc. in Dr. Shankarrao Chavan Government Medical College, Vishnupuri, Nanded. On this occasion Ma. As per the decision of the High Powered Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary in its meeting held on 23.11.2020, an amount of Rs. 17,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Seventeen Crores only) has been agreed for the furniture work for the new administrative building and library building to be used in the institute. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of the budget in this regard was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Administrative Sanction is being accorded to Dr. Shankarrao Chavan Government Medical College, Vishnupuri, Nanded for construction of new administrative building and furniture for library building at a cost of Rs. 17,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Seventeen Crores only) subject to following conditions.
