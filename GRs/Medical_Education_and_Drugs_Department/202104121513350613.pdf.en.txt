15.01.2021 Preamble: As per the above decision dated 15.01.2021, administrative approval was accorded for the construction of Government Medical College at Satara with a capacity of 100 students and an attached 500-bed hospital and ancillary buildings at a cost of Rs. 495,46,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Hundred and Ninety Five Crores, Forty Six Lakhs only). As per the provisions and procedures of the above mentioned Government Decision, Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals, dated 08.02.2018 and dated 24.12.2018, instructions were also issued in the Government Decision Serial No. 15.01.2021 to appoint the Project Management Consultant on turn key basis through the Central Government PSU Company. In this regard, the construction of the Satara Institute through the Public Works Department instead of the Central Government Public Enterprise Company was under the consideration of the government.
