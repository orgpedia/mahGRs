GOVERNMENT DECISION: The review of sanctioned posts for the Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune has been completed and approved by the Government Resolution at Reference No. After the said review, the Government have approved the continuation of 17 temporary posts in the approved pattern of Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune till 28/02/2009 vide Government Resolution No. Out of these 17 temporary posts, the number of employees working in the notified posts of Driver (Class-3) and Safaigar (Class-4) has been included in the vacant posts of Junior Scribe and Safaigar in the organization. The remaining 15 temporary posts have been extended as per the government decision issued from time to time. The said posts were extended till 28.02.2021 vide the aforesaid Government Decision, dated 18th September, 2020.
The Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health has submitted a proposal to the Government under the aforesaid letter dated 03.03.2021 regarding grant of approval for continuation of 15 temporary posts in the Institute beyond 28.02.2021. In pursuance of the said proposal, in accordance with the above Government decision, the Finance Department, vide order dated 15.02.2021, as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department, 15 temporary posts as indicated in the prospectus A& B accompanying this Government decision are required for the continuation of the functioning of the concerned institution, the Government hereby grants approval for the continuation of the said posts from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 subject to the following conditions:
