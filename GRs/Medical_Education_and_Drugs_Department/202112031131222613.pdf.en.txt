Government's decision:
The administrative approval was granted for the Government Medical Colleges and Hospitals under the Department for the equipment required for the treatment of COVID-19 third wave and Mucormycosis as per the Government decision mentioned in Reference No. 4.
The Directorate has requested for revised administrative approval as per the changes suggested by the Expert Committee for the purchase of the following equipment required under the Covid third wave and mucormycosis measures in the said administrative approval vide letter no.
In pursuance of the said proposal, revised administrative approval for cash and price as mentioned in the table below is being accorded.
