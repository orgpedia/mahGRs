Conditions: 1) The administrative approval is being provided to the concerned institution from the grant sanctioned under the District Annual Plan.
(2) It is confirmed by the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai and the competent authority of the concerned institution that the said equipment is of single source nature.
If any matter arises in the future, the entire responsibility will be of the concerned agency and directorate.
3) The concerned has certified that the said equipment is proprietary in nature. However, Haffkine Corporation shall ensure that there are no other products which meet the specifications specified for the proposed machinery.
(4) Upon confirmation as per the aforesaid Article No. 3, the Haffkine Corporation shall proceed with the procurement after verifying the updated and signed certificate of the proposed machinery being proprietary in nature.
5) It will be necessary to show the different prices of the main machine and its accessories.
6. Procedure for procurement of the said equipment" Procurement of drugs, related consumables and medical devices Haffkine Bio-Pharmaceuticals Corporation Ltd. 8) No new construction or design is required for this equipment.
