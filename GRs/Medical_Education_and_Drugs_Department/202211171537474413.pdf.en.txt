Government Decision: The building at Grant Government Medical College in the premises of Sir J. G. R. Group of Hospitals under the department is old and the wooden slabs and cauldrons on the roof of the Department of Physiology and Department of Physiology building are damaged and it is very necessary to do these works and other ancillary works. As per the proposal received in this regard, administrative approval is being given for the repair and renovation of the roof of the Department of Physiology and Department of Physiology at Sir J. G. Group of Hospitals, Grant Government Medical College, Mumbai premises at a budget of Rs. 2,33,90, 886/- (Rupees Two Crores, Thirty Three Lakhs, Ninety Thousand, Eight Hundred and Eighty Six only).
