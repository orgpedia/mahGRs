Government's decision:
The health team attached to the Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur, which is under the control of the department, is currently in a dilapidated condition.
A 10-bed trauma care centre and other patient facilities will be set up there. In this building, all the patient departments like Maternity and Child Health Care Department, Rural Health Team, Emergency Department, Rehabilitation Department, Intensive Care Unit, Operation Theatre etc. will be functional/ constructed.
Administrative approval is granted subject to conditions.
