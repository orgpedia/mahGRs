Government's decision:
The number of students in the Government Medical College, Nagpur under the Department has increased and in accordance with the increased intake capacity, a new hostel building needs to be constructed to accommodate the students. In connection with the Amrit Mahotsav year of this institution, a new hostel building for girls with a capacity of 400 rooms will be constructed. In accordance with the decision taken by the High Level Committee of Secretaries, the construction budget of Rs. 44,90,76, 418/- (Rupees Forty Four Crores, Ninety Lakhs, Seventy Thousand, Four Hundred Eighteen only) is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
