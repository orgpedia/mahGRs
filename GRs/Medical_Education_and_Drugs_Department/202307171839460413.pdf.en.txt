Introduction: The tender process was carried out in the Government Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon for providing daily patient food services (morning tea+ breakfast, lunch, evening tea+ breakfast, dinner) through external sources. Proposal to enter into an agreement with the service provider who qualifies as per the minimum threshold from the tender process and approve the annual expenditure for the same. The above has been submitted to the Government by the Directorate vide letter dated 06.07.2023. The proposal had been approved by the government. The government has taken the following decisions:

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: VISHVI-2023/ PR No. 189/ PROSH-2 dated 17th July, 2023
Government's decision:
In the tender process for providing daily patient food service (morning tea+ breakfast, lunch, evening tea+ breakfast, dinner) in the Government Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon, the minimum rate was fixed at Rs. Approval is being accorded for signing of contract with Sai Investigation and Security Services for a period of one year with an annual outlay of Rs. 54,90,000/- (Rupees Fifty Four Lakh Ninety Thousand only).
The Dean of the institution will be fully responsible for ensuring that the food is regularly available to the in-patients of the hospital and the quality of the food is proper.
The quality of food provided to patients should be regularly monitored.
The expenditure for this work shall be met from the fund sanctioned for the financial year under the head (19) Aahar Seva of the Government Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon (2210 G 342).
This Government Decision The Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals vide Government Decision No. 45 dated 06.02.2015 vide Rule No. 169 is issued under the authority of the administrative department.
