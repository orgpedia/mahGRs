Preamble: - As per Section 3 (1) of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, every public authority is required to notify within three months of the enactment of the Act, and from time to time thereafter, the service rendered by its subordinate bodies, the time limit fixed for that service, the Designated Officer, the First Appellate Authority and the Second Appellate Authority. Accordingly, 3 services namely, Certificate of Medical Leave, Certificate of Medical Fitness and Age Certificate issued by Government Medical Colleges under the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals as notified by the Government dated 16.09.2025 have been included in the Right to Public Service Act, 2015. The government was considering setting up a standard operating procedure (SOP) prepared by the Directorate for online implementation of these services provided by government medical colleges.
# Page 2
Government Decision No: Narrow - 0925 / P.No.325 / Pr. 2 dated 04.11.2025
Government Decision: - Government approval is being granted as mentioned in Annexure A to the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) with respect to implementation of 3 services - Medical Fitness Certificate, Certificate for Medical Leave and Age Certificate to be made available to eligible persons by Government Medical Colleges under the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015.
The fees charged for these services have been fixed in accordance with the Government Decision dated 30.09.2025 mentioned in Read No. 6 of the Government Decision, subject to any amendments, corrections or fee hikes made by the Government from time to time, as the case may be.
The Dean / Medical Superintendent of the institution concerned shall take necessary action to ensure delivery of these services within the stipulated time frame and as per the procedure mentioned in the SOP.
This Government decision is available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharashtra.gov.in.
