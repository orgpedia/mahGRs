The Proposal:
The Director of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai, under the control of the department, has received Rs.240001 thousand in the revised estimate in the account head number S. 01, 2210 3314, 27 small constructions. In this regard, the Director of Medical Education and Research, Directorate of Medical Education, Mumbai, was contemplating to reallocate the funds received in the revised estimates under the aforesaid scheme to the institutions under the control of the Department, namely, Grant Government Medical College, Mumbai, Sir J.G. Group of Hospitals, Mumbai, St. George Hospital, Mumbai, Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital, Mumbai (Statement A), for expenditure under 27 schemes.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS For the financial year 2020-2021, approval is being accorded for reallocation of funds from the funds made available in the Revised Estimates under the scheme of 27-Small Structures under the head Demand No. S-01 2210 3314 under the control of the Department in the heads and accounts of the institutions mentioned in the accompanying statement No.
Approval has been taken for the expenditure of funds to be made available to the concerned institutions in this regard. If the payments for these items have not been made earlier, the concerned Dean/ Superintendent should verify this.
This Government Circular is being issued pursuant to the concurrence received under the Informal Reference No. 220/ 2021/ Expenditure 13 dated 31.03.2021 of the Department of Finance.
