Preamble: The review of sanctioned posts for Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune has been completed and approved by the Government Resolution at Reference No. 1. As a result of this review, the continuation of 17 temporary posts in the approved pattern of Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health, Pune has been approved by the Government Resolution in Reference No. 2. Out of these 17 temporary posts, the number of employees working in the notified posts of Driver (Class-3) and Sweeper (Class-4) has been included in the vacant posts of Junior Scribe and Sweeper in the organization. The remaining 15 temporary posts have been extended as per the Government decision issued from time to time and till 31.08.2021 as per the Government decision in Reference No.3 above. Since these temporary posts are required to be continued, the matter of extension of these temporary posts from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Maharashtra Institute of Mental Health has submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 27.08.2021 for grant of approval for continuation of 15 temporary posts in the Institute beyond 31.08.2021. In pursuance of the said proposal, the aforesaid Government Decision, as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Finance Department, dated 10.08.2021, requires 15 temporary posts as indicated in the prospectus A and B accompanying this Government Decision, to continue the functioning of the concerned organization.
