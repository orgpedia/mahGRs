The Proposal:
As per the minutes of the meeting held on 27.11.2017 under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary to the Hon'ble Chief Minister on" Creation of Cancer Treatment Facilities in Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur in SC/ ST Departments," funds are being provided for the creation of state-of-the-art facilities for the treatment of cancer patients through the Departments of Social Justice and Special Assistance. In this regard, the government was considering providing a new head of account to the department for utilising the funds received from the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department.
Government's decision:
The Government have approved the creation of an accounting fund with the following objectives for utilization of funds received from the Scheduled Castes Special Component Scheme of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance for creation of cancer treatment facilities at Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur.
