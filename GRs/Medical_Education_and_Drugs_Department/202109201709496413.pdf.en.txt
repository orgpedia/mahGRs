Government's decision:
In pursuance of the above mentioned letter dated 05.07.2021, the Directorate has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of administrative approval for procurement of necessary machinery and equipment for making additional arrangements of 100 beds by upgrading the existing facilities under COVID 19 measures in the Department controlled Kamla and Albless Hospital, Mumbai.
