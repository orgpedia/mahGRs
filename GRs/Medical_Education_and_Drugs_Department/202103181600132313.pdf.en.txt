Preamble: The Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals has issued guidelines dated 27.07.2018 and 09.12.2020 regarding the purchase of" Machinery and Equipment" for the purpose of patient care and study of students in Government Medical/ Dental/ Ayurveda Colleges and Hospitals attached to them in the State under the head of various accounts received by the Department and from the funds received from the scheme" Purchase of Hospital Machinery and Equipment" of the District Annual Plan. In the Annexure' A' of the said Government decision, the matter of inclusion of the name of Haffkine Training, Research and Testing Institute, Mumbai, under the control of the Department, for receipt of funds under the District Annual Plan was under consideration.
Government's decision:
The Government have decided to include Haffkine Institute of Training, Research and Testing, Mumbai in the list of Institutions under the control of Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals in Annexure A attached with the Government Decision, dated 27.07.2018, in accordance with the availability of funds from the Scheme for Procurement of Machinery and Equipment of District Annual Plan Hospitals.
