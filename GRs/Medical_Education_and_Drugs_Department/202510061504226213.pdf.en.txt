Preamble: Reference No. By a Government Decision at 2024-25 this financial year, administrative approval was granted for the procurement of "3D Electro-Anatomical Mapping System (EP Lab) On Turnkey Basis" equipment for 8 institutions under the State Plan. However, out of those institutions, Indira Gandhi Government Medical College, Nagpur and Government Medical College, Miraj have informed that the equipment is not required.
The government was considering granting administrative approval for the inclusion of these two GRAIG Government Medical College and Hospital, Baramati and Government Medical College and Hospital, Yavatmal.
Jhasan's decision:
The Government decision cancels the administrative recognition granted to Indira Gandhi Government Medical College, Nagpur and Government Medical College, Miraj, among the institutions granted administrative recognition by the Government Decision at Reference H.8 for the purchase of equipment for "3D Electro-Anatomical Mapping System (EP Lab) on Turnkey Basis." Instead, new administrative approval is being given to set up the corresponding machines at Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Jhaskia Medical College and Hospital, Baramati and Government Medical College and Hospital, Yavatmal.
