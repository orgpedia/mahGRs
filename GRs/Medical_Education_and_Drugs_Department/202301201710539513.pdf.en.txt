Terms and Conditions
1) To make these posts available through external sources, Government Reference No. 4 The guidelines issued under the Government Decision dated 06.12.2022 on providing services through external sources should be followed. Further action should be taken by considering the government decisions, orders and suggestions issued from time to time.
2) As per the prevailing guidelines of the Finance Department, at least 20 to 30 per cent of the expenditure incurred by the government should be saved by filling up the posts regularly while providing services from outside. The Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education and Research shall ensure compliance of this instruction while entering into agreements with the service providers.
(3) The expenditure on the said item shall be met from the grants sanctioned during the budget year under the target of" 10 Contractual Services" without deducting the expenditure under the target of" 01 Salary." Failure to do so will be considered an administrative violation.
(4) The service provider providing services through external sources shall be required to pay wages and allowances as per the rates fixed by the Government and taxes as applicable from time to time. Statutory liabilities payable to the manpower provided by the service provider e.g. Salary includes Provident Fund, ESIC, Bonus, Labour Welfare Fund, Leave encashment etc. It shall be the responsibility of the concerned service provider to deposit such legal liability with the concerned authority. It shall be the responsibility of the empanelled service provider to submit the relevant documents to the Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education and Research from time to time.
This Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the powers vested in the Administrative Department under the Finance Division, Government Resolution dated 17.04.2015, Fiscal Powers Rules Part I, Sub-Division-4, No. 11.
