Government's decision:
The admission capacity of Dr Shankarrao Chavan Government Medical College, Vishnupuri, Nanded, under the control of the department, has increased from 100 to 150 students. A girls hostel is being constructed in the campus. In accordance with the decision taken by the High Powered Committee, administrative approval is being accorded to the budget of Rs. 27.67 crore subject to the following conditions.
