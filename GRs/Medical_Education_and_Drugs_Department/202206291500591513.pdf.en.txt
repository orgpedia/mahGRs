The government has decided to build a 100-student capacity and 500-bed hospital and ancillary buildings for the newly started Government Medical College and Hospital, Sindhudurg. In pursuance of the decision taken by the High Level Committee of Secretaries, the construction budget of Rs. 427.5699 crore (Rupees Four Hundred and Twenty Seven Crores, Printed Target, Ninety Nine Thousand only) is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
1) Before the work is done, the ownership of the place should be fulfilled.
2) If the work falls under the jurisdiction of the Archaeological Department, prior permission of the concerned department as well as the Municipal Corporation and similar authorities should be taken.

3) While giving technical approval, the Government Circular No. 2017/ PR No. 11/ Planning-3, dated 11.04.2017 should be taken for the settlement of non-state tariff matters.
4) The suggestions issued by the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour under the Government Decision dated 24.08.2017, adopting the e-tendering system for the procurement related matters in the proposal should be taken into consideration.
5) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No.177/ T. Action should be taken as per the guidelines mentioned in No. 1, dated January 10, 2014.
6) The electrification and other ancillary works should be properly planned and completed during the construction period itself.
7) Action should be taken in accordance with the instructions given by the government from time to time regarding the various facilities to be provided for the disabled in the building.
8) As the proposed construction is based on a map prepared by a private architect, the map should be obtained with the consent/ signature of the Chief Architect, CBD, Mumbai. It should be verified by the chief architect that the area of the various rooms in the budget is as per the prescribed norms or how.
9) In the present case. The directions given in the meeting of the High Powered Committee held on 06/05/2022 under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary should be fulfilled.
10) Thematic work should be done with special care so that it does not require revised administrative approval.
A detailed proposal should be submitted to the committee on how the construction is going to be done on the land and then the construction of Phase-1 should be done as per the permissible FSI on that date. Further construction of remaining Phase-II should be completed after approval of the proposal for change of reservation in respect of providing 10.00.00 Ha.R. plot in Sindhudurg City approved development plan plot reserved for social forestry for Government Medical College.
As per the directions of the Government, the construction is being approved by the Public Works Department as per the revised procedures in the above Government Decision, Department of Medical Education and Medicines, dated 20/05/2021.
