Preamble: The" Maharashtra Medical Supplies Procurement Authority" Act, 2023" (13 of 2023) has been published on 17.03.2023 for the purchase of medicines, consumables, equipment and machinery and other ancillary items under the Public Health Department. This Act has come into force with effect from 10.05.2023. Under the Act, the state government will procure medical equipment, medicines, surgicals and other related items for the institutions under the Public Health Department, government medical colleges and hospitals under the Medical Education Department and other departments of the Maharashtra government. Accordingly, for the financial year 2023-2024, 66 Government Medical/ Dental Colleges and Hospitals under the Directorate of Medical Education and Research will be required to supply 213 types of medicines, surgicals and other materials in 13 schedules.

Administrative approval is subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The requirement/ demand for the said drugs, surgicals materials, chemicals, consumables and related matters shall be verified by the Directorate.
2) In this regard, the prescribed procedure mentioned in the" Manual of Procedures for Office Procurement to be carried out by the Government Department" issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, Government Decision No. 2014/ No. 82/ Part-III/ Industry-4, dated 01.12.2016, should be followed.
3) The Directorate shall ensure that the requisition for medicines, surgicals materials and related matters costing less than Rs. 10.00 lakh as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 07.05.2021 of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour shall not be sent to the Maharashtra Medical Commodities Procurement Authority.
(4) In the Table and Statement-B of the said Government Decision, the reference to the Commissioner, Directorate of Medical Education and Research regarding the matters related to the purchase of medicines No. 7 The details attached with the above letter dated 03.06.2022 should be immediately made available to the Maharashtra Medical Supplies Procurement Authority.
(5) The institution-wise funds budgeted under (21) Materials and Supplies for the financial year 2023-2024 as mentioned in the Statement-A for procuring the said medicines, surgicals materials and related items from the Maharashtra Medical Commodities Procurement Authority should be transferred to the Maharashtra Medical Commodities Procurement Authority immediately.
6) It should be ensured that the items proposed for purchase are not left unused and are used within the prescribed time.
7) It should be ensured that there is no duplication of purchase matters.
8) Under no circumstances should be spent more than the limit of available funds.
9) Drugs and other items as per the requisition for drugs, surgicals and related matters submitted to the Maharashtra Medical Commodities Procurement Authority in accordance with the said administrative approval.
