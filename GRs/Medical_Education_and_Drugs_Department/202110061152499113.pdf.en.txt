Government's decision:
The capacity of undergraduate medical admission in Vilasrao Deshmukh Government Institute of Medical Sciences, Latur has been increased from 100 to 150 in 2013. The institute started offering postgraduate courses in a total of 16 departments in 2010. As per the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) norms, a hospital should have a capacity of 700 beds.
Therefore, as an additional single storey is required to be constructed over the existing new 200 bedded hospital building to cater to the remaining 200 beds, an amount of Rs. 12,28,23, 373/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Twenty Eight Lakhs Twenty Three Thousand Thirty Three Seventy Seven only) is being accorded administrative approval for the construction subject to the following terms and conditions.
