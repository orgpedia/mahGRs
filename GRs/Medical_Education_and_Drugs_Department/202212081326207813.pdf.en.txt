Preamble: The CSSD Equipment required by Government Medical College, Baramati was accorded administrative approval under the State Scheme vide Government Order dated 24.06.2020. However, in the absence of some of the items required for the said machinery, revised administrative approval has been accorded for procurement of CSSD Equipment (including turn key) for an amount of Rs. 15,09,62, 550/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores, Nine Lakhs, Sixty Two Thousand, Five Hundred and Fifty only) under the Government Decision dated 24.02.2021.
Department of Industries, Energy and Labour, vide Government Resolution dated 1.12.2016, vide Issue No. As per 2:13, the administrative approval order of the Authority shall be valid for one year from the date of approval, the period of 1 year of administrative approval granted for CSSD Equipment (with turn key) has expired. Therefore, it is necessary to approve the re-tendering process for the purchase of the said machine and the matter of approval of the proposal submitted by the Directorate under the above letter dated 27.11.2022 regarding the revised administrative approval was under consideration of the Government.
