Government Resolution, Revenue and Forest Department, No. 2016/ P.No.80 (Part-II)/ F-11, dated 25th July, 2018.
Introduction: An ambitious vision of planting 50 crore trees has been conceived at the government level to reduce the intensity of the problem of global warming, climate and weather change. As per the above decision of the Department of Revenue and Forestry, funds are to be made available for the implementation of the scheme of" Tree Plantation and Maintenance" in each department within the limit of 0.5 percent of the" Programme Expenditure" in addition to the overall budgetary provision available to the department every year. The scheme was proposed to be implemented under the Skill and Entrepreneurship Department. Accordingly, a new head of accounts for the provision in the Budget for 2019-20 was under consideration.
Government Decision: 1. As per the Government Decision dated 25th July, 2018 in the context of the Department of Revenue and Forest, the Scheme of Tree Plantation and Maintenance will be implemented in the" Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department" and accordingly, it is being approved to open a new head of account to provide funds within the limit of 0.5 percent of the program cost available to the department every year.
