The Proposal:
In order to promote the industrial and economic development of the state and to promote enterprises based on innovative resolutions to address the problem of unemployment in the country, the Department has approved the" Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018" under the Government Decision dated 05/ 02/ 2018.
In line with this policy, the government was considering issuing guidelines to provide financial assistance to startups in the state to reimburse a part of the cost incurred for quality testing and certification.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018 has been approved to promote industries based on innovative resolutions to boost the industrial and economic development of the state and to address the problem of unemployment in the country. As per the policy, startups in the state will require financial assistance to reimburse part of the cost incurred for quality testing and certification. Accordingly, guidelines in Annexure A are being approved to provide financial assistance to startups for reimbursement of part of the cost incurred on Quality Testing& Certification.
Further necessary action to finance the Quality Testing& Certification in accordance with the said guidelines should be taken immediately by the Maharashtra State Innovation Society.
