The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship
Government Circular No: Schedule- 2019/ PR No.59/ PRS-2
Ministry (Main Building), Hall No. 204, 2nd Floor, Madame Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mumbai-400032
Date is June 24, 2019.
Letter from the Commissioner, Directorate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship No. Kauvirowausam-2/ Rajab/ Astha/ (17/18)/ 5331, dated 22/05/2019
Government circular:
The provisional seniority list of the officers of the cadre of" Assistant Director" Group-A in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System as on 01/01/2019 is being published in Form-A vide this circular.
The Commissioner, Directorate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship is hereby directed to bring the said provisional seniority list to the notice of all concerned authorities and if any genuine errors and omissions are found in the same, they should be reported to the Government within 15 days from the date of issue of this Government Circular so that necessary corrections in the seniority list can be made and final seniority list can be published.
