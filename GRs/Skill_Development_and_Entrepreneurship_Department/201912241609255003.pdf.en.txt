Read above. 1 The Commissioner, Employment and Self-Employment has been declared as the Head of Department of the Directorate of Employment and Self-Employment vide Government Resolution, dated 22.01.2001. Since then, senior Indian Administrative Service officers, including the Commissioner, have been appointed as heads of departments in the Directorate.
Read above. 4 The General Administration Department, dated 15.01.2015, has been renamed as Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
Accordingly, the Directorate of Employment and Self-Employment, Directorate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship and their subordinate offices and designations of various cadres have also been renamed. 4 The decision has been taken vide Government Order dated 01.07.2015.
As the head of the department of the Directorate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship is the commissioner, the matter of nominating the directorate as" commissionerate" for administrative ease and the designation of subordinate offices and posts in the structure of the directorate was under consideration of the government.

Government Resolution No: KOUVIU-2019/ PR No.51/ PRISA-2
Government Decision: The Government is approving to rename the Directorate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship as Commissionerate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship for administrative ease as the Chief Commissioner of the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship is the Chief Commissioner. Further, the Government have approved the following to be the names of the Divisional Headquarters and the posts under this
(a) Name of the Office:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | Current Name                                                               | New name                                                              |
| 1    | Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department Headquarters | Commissionerate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship |
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(a) Name of the post:
--------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | Current designation    | New designation            |
| 1    | Deputy Director        | the Deputy Commissioner    |
| 2    | the Assistant Director | the Assistant Commissioner |
--------------------------------------------------------------
The above changes are being made subject to the following conditions: 1. The Service Entry Rules of the said posts will also be changed in view of the change in the names of the posts.
(2) There shall be no demand for increase in the pay scale of any cadre in future on account of nomination of posts.
This Government decision has been taken with the approval of General Administration Department and Finance Department in their Informal Reference No. 998/ Q. 12, dated 02.11.2019 and Informal Reference No.448/ 2019/ Service-9, dated 09.12.2019.
