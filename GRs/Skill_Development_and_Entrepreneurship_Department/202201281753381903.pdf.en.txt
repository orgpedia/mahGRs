In pursuance of the aforesaid letter, you are hereby informed that Mr. Jaywant Keshav Bagul, Director of Crafts, Industrial Training Institute (Girls), Nashik, working under the aegis of Joint Director, Vocational Education and Training, Regional Office, Nashik, has been transferred and posted as Director, Industrial Training Institute, Nashik. Passed away on 09.12.2020 while in Government service.
He was posted at the Regional Office, Nashik. As per Rule No.134 (3) of Part I Sub-section-1 of the Financial Rules, 1959 dated 09.10.2009, during the financial year 2009-10, Rs. 2,20,800/- (Rs. Two lakh twenty thousand eight hundred only) house construction has been provided in advance. After the recovery of the principal amount of the house construction advance was completed, the office of the Regional Office Nashik issued an order. As on 09.12.2010 Rs. 1,49,362/- (Rupees One Lakh Nineteen Thousand Three Hundred and Sixty Two only) has been levied as interest. Accordingly, Rs. 1,49,362/- (Rupees One Lakh Nineteen Thousand Three Hundred and Fifty Two only) to Mr. Jaywant Keshav Bagul, Director of Crafts, before the recovery of the amount of interest. He passed away on 09.12.2020. Hence, interest on house building advance amounting to Rs. 1,49,362/- (Rupees One Lakh Nineteen Thousand Three Hundred and Fifty Two only) could not be recovered from them. A proposal had been sent to the government for sanction of the amount.
Department of Finance, Government Resolution No. 2012/ No. 30/2012/ Regulation D. As per the provision of Para 10 dated 20.02.2015, Shri Jaywant Keshav Bagul, Director of Sculpture, being deceased, his house construction advance of Rs. Under this decision of the Government, it is being approved to waive off the interest on the house building advance of Rs. 1,49,362/- (Rupees One Lakh Nineteen Thousand Three Hundred and Fifty Two only).
The Government Order is being issued with the approval of the Principal Secretary, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department.
