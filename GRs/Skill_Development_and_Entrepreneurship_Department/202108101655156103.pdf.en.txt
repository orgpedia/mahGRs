The Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training shall relieve the transferred officers of the above table immediately. The concerned officers, after appearing in the transferred post, should submit their joining report along with the transfer certificate to the government without any delay through the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai in the prescribed manner.
No leave of absence shall be granted to the officers posted as above without the prior approval of the Government or any leave applications of the officers posted as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave of Office) Rules, 1981 shall be accepted by the parent office.
(4) When the officers who are being appointed to such posts are appointed to the post assigned to them, the office concerned shall verify whether they were on leave/ absent or not and produce them on posting. The officer was absent on medical grounds.
If so, they should be sent for medical examination before being produced at the post office. If found on other leave, the leave application should be filled without fail from the concerned officer and then submitted. Also, the instructions in the government decisions/ circulars submitted by the government from time to time regarding the leave should be followed.
(5) The transferred officer shall ensure that he joins the place of transfer within the period of assumption of office as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Acceptance Service and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. Otherwise, if he does not join the place of transfer during this period, the period of absence shall be deemed to be non-dies.
If any officer directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order to be changed, and submits any kind of application for cancellation of the transfer, then disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned officer as this action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
