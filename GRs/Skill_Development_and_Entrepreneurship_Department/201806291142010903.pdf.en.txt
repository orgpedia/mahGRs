Government's stand:
The Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) has issued guidelines for the approval of standard procedures for applying online for grant of permission to start pre-vocational courses,+ 2 level vocational courses, new institutes and more batches of higher secondary vocational courses on a non-subsidized basis. In Annexure A of the Government Resolution dated 10/5/2018,
Issue No. 4. Application Process and Registration Fee. 4.2 The fee prescribed for the application process and registration fee shall be deposited in the back account of the" Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training" Society. Instead, it should read," The fee prescribed for application processing and registration fee should be deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State."
Issue No. 11. Sub-item mentioned in Deposit/ Expenditure No. 11.1 The fees to be charged for starting pre-vocational courses, new institutions of+ 2 level and higher secondary vocational courses and for starting vocational courses on a permanent non-subsidized basis will be collected as per the existing procedure. Instead, the fee to be charged for starting pre-vocational courses, new institutions of+ 2 level and higher secondary vocational courses and for starting vocational courses on a permanent basis on a non-subsidized basis should be deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State.
