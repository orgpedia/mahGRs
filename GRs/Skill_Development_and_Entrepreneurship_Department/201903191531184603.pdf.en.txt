circular
The Provisional Seniority List dated 01/01/2018 of the officers of the cadre of" Assistant Director" Group-A in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System was published vide Circular dated 16.11.2018 in the context of Form A respectively.
Taking note of the objections raised by Mr. V. R. Wise, suitable amendments have been made in the seniority list as per the merit list. Also, Mr. N.N. Suryavanshi has objected to the number in the seniority list of Mrs. Bajaj as she has been taken from the waiting list. Mr. Suryavanshi's objection is being rejected as Suryavanshi's merit number is 15.
Accordingly, the final seniority list of officers of the cadre of" Assistant Director" Group-A in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System as on 01/01/2018 is being published in Form A vide this circular.
