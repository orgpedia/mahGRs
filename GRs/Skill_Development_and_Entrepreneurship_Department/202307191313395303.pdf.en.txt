The proposal-
Under the Combined Peripheral Training Programme-2020 (CPTP-8), Assistant Commissioner in Skill Development, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation System, 1 in Group-A cadre and 4 in Group-B cadre have been added. Out of these, Group-A Cadre Officers will be trained by YASHADA, Pune and Group-B Officers will be trained by Vanamati, Nagpur for a period of two years under a combined probationary training programme. The decision is being implemented as per the government's 1. Accordingly, the matter of reimbursement of the technical training expenses for the candidates selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination-2020 in the cadre of Assistant Commissioner, Group A and Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Officer, Group-B and who are undergoing training in the Combined Subject Training Programme-8 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Assistant Director in Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System (presently designated as Assistant Commissioner) in Group-A cadre and Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Officer in Group-B cadre of Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the final of State Service (Main) Examination-2020
