Preamble: Review of posts in the establishment of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the Department of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation. With the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries headed by the Chief Secretary, this Department vide Reference No. (2) Here. The revised figure has been fixed as per the Government Decision dated 07/04/2022. Also, the number of posts in the field offices under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training has been fixed under this government decision.
Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department. (1) Here. As per the provision of the Government Decision dated 21/06/2006, as per the revised figure of posts in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the Department of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to convert 80 per cent of the total 15489 sanctioned posts into permanent posts.
Government Decision: According to the revised figure of posts on the establishment of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, a total of 15489 posts have been approved. Out of these sanctioned posts, 8523 posts are to be made permanent. (2) Here. The approval is being granted subject to the conditions contained in the Government Resolution dated 21/06/2006. The permanent/ temporary classification of these posts shall be as indicated in Form-A (1) to (7) attached.
