Government's stand:
In the online mode of online process of periodic/ special requests/ departmental transfers of employees of Group-C and Group-D cadres in Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and subordinate offices, as per the Government decision, in Phase-II, it has been decided to transfer 2.5 Group-" C" to Group-" D" employees. Employees who complete 3 years or more of service on June 30 will be considered transferable. "
instead of
" 2.5 The employees of Group-"" C"" cadre who will complete the period of service in accordance with the provisions of Section 3 (1) of the Maharashtra State Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act-2005 as on 31st May, 2020 in the post held by them will be considered transferable."
Generally, there will be no fixed tenure for Group D employees. They shall not be transferred outside the place where they are working, unless there is an unfilled vacancy in the place where they have requested for transfer, or the transfer has been sought by mutual consent, or there is a general grievance of serious nature against them.
