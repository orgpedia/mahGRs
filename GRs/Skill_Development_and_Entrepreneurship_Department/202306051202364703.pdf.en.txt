The Proposal:
Pursuant to the guidelines received through the Office Order at Reference 1 of the Central Government, the Department of Finance vide Reference No. 2 All the Administrative Departments have been advised to take action for opening a separate head for interest on the unspent amount of Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the head 801-Interest or interest on the unspent amount of subsidy received under the head/ sub-head of functional head relating to the department.
2. The Government was considering to approve the accounting of interest and other deposits on the unclaimed amount of State Account on the account of single nodal agency for all Centrally Sponsored Schemes being implemented by the Department of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation. A government resolution in this regard was issued.
Government's decision:
The following heads of accounts are being approved for crediting the interest and other deposits on the unclaimed amount of State Accounts on the account of Single Nodal Agency for all Centrally Sponsored Schemes implemented by the Department of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation:
