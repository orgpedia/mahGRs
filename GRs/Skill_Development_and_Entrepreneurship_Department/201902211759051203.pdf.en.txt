Introduction: The" Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018" has been approved under the Government Decision dated 05/02/2018 of the Department to promote industries based on innovative resolutions to promote industrial and economic development of the State and to address the problem of unemployment in the country. In line with this policy, the matter of approving the conduct of" Business Plan Competition" to develop the start-up culture in the state was under consideration of the government.
The Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018 has been approved to promote enterprises based on innovative concepts to promote industrial and economic development of the state and to address the problem of unemployment in the country.

The competition procedure is as follows:
The competition will be held once a year in each district. However, if a district deems it necessary, the competition can be organized at the division level instead of the district level in their district.
The competition will be organized by the District Innovation Council established by the Government Decision dated 14/08/2014 of the Planning Department.
3) The competition will be widely publicized to ensure maximum participation of the general public including school/ college students.
Any individual/ group/ establishment/ institution with innovative ideas that can be useful for solving problems of the respective district/ division as well as can be converted into a real industry can participate in the competition.
The participant will be required to participate in the competition with the required product/ service/ process, proof of concept and complete business plan to implement the innovative concept.
Applications will be invited from individuals/ groups/ institutions/ institutions who wish to participate in the competition and the selected participants (within a limit of 100) will be provided with a platform to present their innovative ideas.
Successful entrepreneurs, businessmen, investors, banks, etc. of the district (at least five) will be invited for the competition.
The selected themes will be evaluated by a committee constituted by the District Innovation Council and the final winners (within the limit of 05 in case of district level competition and 15 in case of block level competition, however, a maximum of 08 from the respective block level district and the remaining from other districts) will be declared.
Final winning projects will be awarded work orders to prove their Proof of Concept (POC) within a limit of Rs. 5.00 lakhs. They will also be mentored for their projects by the District Innovation Council and invited to participate in the" Maharashtra Startup Week" to be held at the state level. The work orders will be issued by the District Innovation Council under the appropriate department of the state government to implement these concepts and the payment will be made by the District Innovation Council.

The entire cost of the competition will be borne by the District Innovation Council. However, if the competition is held at the divisional level, 50% of the total cost of the competition and work order to the selected contestants from other districts will be given by the Maharashtra State Innovation Society.
11) The expenditure of this scheme will have to be approved by the District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee.
