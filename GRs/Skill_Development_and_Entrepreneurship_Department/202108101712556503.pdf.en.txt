(5) The transferred officer shall ensure that he joins the place of transfer within the period of assumption of office as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Post-Acceptance Service and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. Otherwise, if he does not join the place of transfer during this period, the period of absence shall be deemed to be non-dies.
If any officer directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order to be changed, and submits any kind of application for cancellation of the transfer, then disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned officer as this action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
