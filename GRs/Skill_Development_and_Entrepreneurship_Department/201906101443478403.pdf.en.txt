Annasaheb Patil Appointed Chairman of Economic Backward Development Corporation
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship
Government Resolution No. APAM 2017/ Q.No.90/ ROSWARO-1
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
The date: June 10, 2019.
Read:- Department of Employment and Self-Employment No. 1998/ PR. No.363/ ROSWARO-1, Dy. 27.11.1998
Preamble To provide employment and self-employment opportunities to the educated unemployed youth of the economically backward sections of the state, the Companies Act, 1956 has been amended.
Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation has been established on 27.11.1998. The appointment of the Chairperson of the Corporation was under the consideration of the Government as per the provisions of Rule 82 (1) of the Articles of Incorporation and the Establishment Rules.
Narendra Annasaheb Patil, Navi Mumbai has been appointed as the Chairman of Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation.
