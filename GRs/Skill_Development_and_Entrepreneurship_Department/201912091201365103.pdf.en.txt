Preamble: 576 temporary posts have been approved under the Government decision of the Context (2) for starting new Industrial Training Institutes in the newly created 32 taluks under the Craftsmen Training Scheme in the State under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and 414 temporary posts have been approved under the Government decision of the Context (3) under the scheme of Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education. (1) The powers to extend the tenure of temporary posts in all the administrative departments have been conferred under the Government Decision. issued by the Finance Department from time to time
As per the decision of the Government, these temporary posts have been extended till 30.09.2019. Under the Government Decision (4) issued in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020. As per the proposal submitted to the Government vide Government Resolution No: ITI-2019/ PR No.37/ Person-3 of Directorate of Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter dated 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020, extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the newly created 32 Talukas under the Shilpakari Training Scheme in the State under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 576 temporary posts under the sanctioned schemes and 414 temporary posts under the scheme of Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education (Form A and B attached) for the period from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 are being approved under this Government Decision subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
(4) In case of any question of remuneration of such temporary posts, the responsibility shall be of the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the approved grants under the following heads during the financial year 2019-20.
