Government Decision- To provide employment and self-employment opportunities to the educated unemployed youth of the economically backward class in the state, the Companies Act, 1956 has been amended. Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation was established on 27th November 1998. As per the provisions of Rule 82 (1) and (3) of the Articles of Incorporation and the Rules of Incorporation of the Corporation, the appointments of Chairman, Vice-Chairman, Non-Official Members and Special Ministers have been made in accordance with the Government Decision No. 2-4 above.
The appointments of Chairman, Vice-Chairman, all non-official members and special invitees as per the provisions of Rule 82 (4) of the Establishment Timetable and Establishment Rules of Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation are being cancelled under this Government decision.
