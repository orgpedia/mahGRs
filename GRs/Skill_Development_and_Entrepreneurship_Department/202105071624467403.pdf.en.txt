Preamble: Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training As per the proposal, the Bombay Engineers Group under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India, and the Centre's Industrial Training Institute, Kirkee, Pune, have been requested for approval to start business courses. The main objective of the institute is to develop the skills of the army personnel and provide them with skill certificates so that they can get employment after retirement. This institution has been recognized by the Central Government through DGT, New Delhi vide Reference No. 4 Under this letter, affiliation has been given to 11 business courses.
As per the demand of the institute and the proposal of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, the matter of granting a letter of intent to approve the business courses provided by the Central Government.
Government's decision:
The following professional courses which have been granted affiliation by the Central Government to the Bombay Engineers Group and the Centre's Industrial Training Institute, Kirkee, Pune, under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India, from the academic year 2021-22, are being approved by the State Government subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in Government Resolution 4.
