Praveen Kumar Ukhalikar, Vice-Principal, Industrial Training Institute, Jalna. (1) In accordance with Rule No. In compliance with the provisions of section 17, they were sent to Sydney, Australia. The Government is granting approval for his presentation of the paper titled" A Study of Auto Industry Accidents in India and HR Interventions for its Prevention" at the 23rd World Congress on Safety and Health at Work Sydney International Conference to be held from 27th to 30th November, 2023, subject to the following conditions:
1) Mr. Praveen Kumar Ukhalikar shall not violate any provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 during the period of the said tour.
2) Mr. Praveen Kumar Ukhalikar will not accept any travel expenses, foreign stay expenses or any other kind of hospitality from any organization in connection with this tour.
3) Daily allowance and travel allowance will not be admissible to Mr. Praveen Kumar Ukhalikar for the duration of the said tour.
(4) This order is being issued subject to grant of leave by the competent authority for the period of the said visit to Mr. Praveen Kumar Ukhalikar.
In case of violation of the above conditions, Mr. Praveen Kumar Ukhalikar shall be liable for disciplinary action as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979.
