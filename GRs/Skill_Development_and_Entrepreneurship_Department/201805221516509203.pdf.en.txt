Preamble: As per the decision of the Central Government regarding upgradation of 1,396 Industrial Training Institutes in the country through Public Private Partnership, the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government of India, vide its decision dated 31/12/2007, has approved the implementation of this scheme in the State.
The State Government has constituted a State Steering Committee under the Chairmanship of Industry Partner for upgradation of Government Industrial Training Institutes in the State through private participation (through Public Private Partnership) under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme. 2 Approved by the Government Decision dated 31 March 2008.
As per the guidelines of the Central Government, the Institute Management Committee is required to hold two meetings every month. On an average, there are 42 institutions under each joint director and the joint directors are not allowed to meet 84 times in a month. Accordingly, the State Steering Committee in its meeting held on 04.03.2017 has decided as under:

Government Resolution No: PPP-2018/ P.S. No. 90/18/ Vyashi-5,
" The government should issue a revised decision regarding the appointment of the concerned district vocational education and training officer instead of the joint director, regional office, as a representative of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training in the institute management committee. "
Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to amend the composition of the Institute Management Committee in the Government Resolution dated 31st March 2008 by approving the appointment of" Representative of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training- District Vocational Education and Training Officer- Member" instead of" Representative of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training- Divisional Joint Director Member" out of the five representatives nominated by the State Government in the Institute Management Committee.
Government's decision:
In the above background, it is now being approved under this Government decision to amend the structure of the Institute Management Committee constituted to upgrade the Government Industrial Training Institutes in the State through private participation (through Public Private Partnership) under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme as follows:
