Government order:
Shri P.T. Devtale, Joint Director, Vocational Education and Training, Regional Office, Nagpur, vide Reference No. (2) As per the order issued herein, the Joint Director, Vocational Education and Training, Regional Office, Aurangabad has been transferred.
Considering the administrative convenience and urgency, the additional charge of the post of Joint Director, Vocational Education and Training, Regional Office, Nagpur, which is going to be vacant due to the transfer of Devtale, is being handed over to Mr. K. M. Motghare, Deputy Director, Vocational Education and Training Regional Office, Nagpur, until further orders. Mr. Motghare will hold the additional charge of the said post while discharging the functions of his office.
The said order shall remain in force till regular appointment to the post of Joint Director, Vocational Education and Training, Regional Office, Nagpur or until further orders of the Government.
The Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai should immediately implement this order and submit a compliance report to the government.
