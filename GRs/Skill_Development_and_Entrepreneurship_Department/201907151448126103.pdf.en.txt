Regarding the formation of a committee for the control of the construction of industrial training institutes and skill development centers under the LWE.

Date: July 15, 2019.
The proposal-
Under the initiative to impart skill training to the youth in Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas in the state, the central government plans to provide 100% subsidy and 75:25 subsidy for capital expenditure. Gadchiroli and Gondia are the two districts in the state. The Central Government has proposed to set up a Monitoring Committee to regulate the quality of construction of ITIs/ Skill Development Centres (SDCs) in these districts. A state-level committee will be formed for this purpose.
Government's decision:
Under the initiative to impart skill training to the youth in Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas in the state, the central government plans to provide 100% subsidy and 75:25 subsidy for capital expenditure. Gadchiroli and Gondia are the two districts in the state. To regulate the quality of construction of ITIs/ Skill Development Centres (SDCs) in these districts, the Monitoring Committee is being nominated by the representatives to be constituted as follows:
the State Controlling Committee for Quality Control of Construction
Principal Secretary, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department- Chairman.
Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur- To check the quality of constructed buildings.
Chief Engineer (Electrical)- To check the quality of electrified work.
Director, Vocational Education and Training- Secretary and Coordinator of the Committee.
The quality of construction under these schemes will be reviewed every month by the State Controlling Committee.
