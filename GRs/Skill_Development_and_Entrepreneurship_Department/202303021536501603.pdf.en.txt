GOVERNMENT Circular: Provisional Seniority List dated 01/01/2022 of the officers of the cadre of" Assistant Commissioner" Group-A in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System is being published in Form-A through this circular.
The Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship is hereby informed that he shall bring this provisional seniority list to the notice of all concerned officers and if any genuine errors and omissions are found in it, they shall be reported to the Government within 15 days from the date of issue of this Government Circular so that necessary corrections in the seniority list can be made and the final seniority list can be published. Any suggestions/ comments received will not be considered.
