The Proposal:
The draft of Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy 2018 has been approved. Skill and Entrepreneurship Development and Skill and Entrepreneurship Training to candidates in rural and urban areas through various departments/ corporations of the State Government during the approval of the Cabinet.
Various programmes are being organized to mark the occasion. The Finance Department had suggested the formation of a high-level committee of representatives of the concerned departments under the chairmanship of the Additional Chief Secretary, Industries, to review these schemes and centralize and converge the schemes to avoid duplication. The department The Finance Department's views were agreed upon while presenting the draft Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy 2018 to the Cabinet for approval.
In this regard, the government was considering the formation of a high-level committee under the chairmanship of the Additional Chief Secretary, Industries, to centralize and converge the schemes of skill and entrepreneurship development being implemented by various departments.
Government's decision:
Under this decision, a high-level committee consisting of the following members, headed by the Additional Chief Secretary, Industries Department, is being constituted to centralize and converge the schemes implemented for skill and entrepreneurship development and capacity building in this regard by various departments/ corporations of the state government.
