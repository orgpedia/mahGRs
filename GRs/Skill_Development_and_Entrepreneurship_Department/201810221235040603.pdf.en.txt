The Proposal:
He said that a committee should be formed to make recommendations to the government in line with the policy decision to convert the HSC vocational course. The decision was taken in the meeting held on 24/09/2018 under the chairmanship of the Minister of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. The government had constituted a committee for the purpose.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, a committee of the following members will be formed to recommend to the Government in line with the policy decision to convert the H.S.C. Vocational Course.
