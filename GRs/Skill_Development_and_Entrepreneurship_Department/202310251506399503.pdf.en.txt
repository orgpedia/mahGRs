The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Skill University Act, 2021 (Maharashtra Act No. 7 of 2021) dated 23rd March, 2021 has been enacted to establish a public skill university on a funded basis to provide skill-based employable education to the youth of the state. The said Act has come into force with effect from 14th April, 2021 vide notification dated 6th April, 2021.
The Maharashtra State Skill Development Corporation (MSDSDC) is planning to start a state-of-the-art skill training institute in the state. Funds are required for the construction of the university for the purpose of starting the functioning of the Skill University. For the financial year 2022-23, Rs. A sum of Rs. 7.35 crore had been released. The Government has taken the following decisions with regard to the disbursement of funds required for the year 2023-24.
