The Proposal:
The committee was headed by Vikram Kale, VPS. The committee has been asked to submit its report within two months" time. The government was considering extending the deadline for the committee to submit its report. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The committee headed by Vikram Kale, V.P.S. The government has decided to extend the deadline till October 30, 2020.
The Government of India has stopped providing grants to the Higher Secondary Vocational Courses (HSC Vocational) [earlier known as Minimum Skill Vocational Courses (MCVC)] Scheme from 1997-98. The Central Government has mentioned in the notification in that regard that no financial assistance will be given to the courses which are not affiliated to NSQF. In this background, the committee should suggest options for conversion of this course to another and adjustment of teachers/ non-teaching staff accordingly.
Travel allowance and daily allowance for the committee members to attend the meeting shall be admissible as per rules. The expenditure on this purpose should be met by the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training from the grants received by them under the objective of" Office Expenditure."
