The Proposal:
In case of death of an employee in service of the State Government and who is a member of the defined contribution pension scheme/ national pension scheme, before completion of 10 years of service, the Government was considering opening of a new head of account for sanction of ex-gratia to his nominee, if not nominated, to his legal heir.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to open new accounts for grant of ex-gratia grant under Defined Contribution National Pension Scheme for teachers/ non-teaching staff of Non-Government Aided Technical Secondary Schools/ Central and Junior Colleges pursuing Pre-Vocational Courses and+ 2 level Vocational Courses as under:
