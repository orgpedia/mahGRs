Government order:
Provision has been made to sanction the first/ second benefit of pay structure of the promoted post under the Assured Progress Scheme under the revised service as per the Government Decision dated 20th July, 2001 and 1st April, 2010 of the Department of Finance. Accordingly, the first/ second benefit of the Assured Progress Scheme is being sanctioned to the officers who have completed 12 consecutive years of regular service in the post of Assistant Director, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System, Group-A (Pay Scale Rs. 9,300-34, 800 Grade Pay Rs. 5000) and Skill Development and Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Officer, Group-B (Pay Scale Rs. 9,300-34, 800 Grade Pay Rs. 4400).
Accordingly, the following Assistant Directors, Group-A (Pay Scale Rs. 9300-34,800 Grade Pay Rs.5000) are being sanctioned the pay scale of the post of Deputy Director, Group-A (Pay Scale Rs. 15600-39,100 Grade Pay Rs.6600):
