The Proposal:
Centre/ State for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship under the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship
Various government schemes are being implemented. The schemes are primarily aimed at generating employment and skilled manpower through skilling and necessary synergies between the systems related to employment/ self-employment. While implementing this scheme, it is necessary to avoid duplication of schemes, lack of coordination with entrepreneurs and non-achievement of the set targets. Supervision and control are the foundations of good governance. With a view to streamline the financial affairs of various institutions and field offices under the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department, the Government was considering to set up a" Project Approval Board (PAB)" at the State level for financial approval, supervision and control of all projects and schemes implemented under the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to set up a Project Approval Board (PAB) at the State level for financial approval, supervision and control of all projects and schemes under the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship.
The composition of the committee will be as follows:

Invited members to Secretaries of departments other than Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department
The Project Approval Board (PAB) will be the inviting authority.
The Project Approval Board (PAB) will supervise and control centrally sponsored, centrally sponsored and state government sponsored schemes/ projects/ programmes implemented by the offices of the Commissionerate of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Maharashtra Directorate of Vocational Training, Maharashtra State Council of Vocational Education, Maharashtra State Skill Development Examination Board, Annasaheb Patil Economic Development Board, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, Maharashtra State Innovation Society. Also, the concerned Regional Office/ Institute shall take approval of the Project Approval Board (PAB) while granting financial approval to the above Central and State Schemes/ Projects/ Programmes.
The functions of the Project Approval Board (PAB) shall be as follows:
(i) Centre: To approve the proposals/ schemes of State sponsored and State sponsored schemes/ programmes/ special projects before they are submitted to the Government.
(ii) To determine and approve the plan of implementation of schemes/ programmes/ special projects as well as schemes/ programmes/ projects of the State Government after approval by the Central Government.
Supervision and control over implementation of schemes/ programmes/ projects sanctioned by the Centre and State Governments.
Supervision and control of the allocation of funds sanctioned for Centrally Sponsored, Centrally Sponsored and State Government Schemes/ Programmes/ Projects.
