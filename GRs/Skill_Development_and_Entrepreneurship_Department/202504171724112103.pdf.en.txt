Introduction: - To enhance the employability of the youth of the state by providing them hands-on training after their education. The "Mukhyamantri Yuva Karyakarta Shiksha Yojana" has been implemented under the government decision of 09.07.2024. The financial coverage of the Mukhyamantri Yuva Karyakarta Yojana is extensive. In order to manage the expenditure sector wise, separate headings for general / SC / ST under the scheme should be proposed, as per the suggestions received from the Department of Finance, the proposal submitted for getting separate headings for general component under the Chief Minister's Youth Affairs Training Scheme has been forwarded to the Department of Finance, Govt. The Accountant General (Accounts and Licensure) has agreed. Accordingly, under the Chief Minister's Youth Work Training Scheme, the government was considering approving a separate head of accounts for the general component.
Government Decision: Under the Chief Minister's Youth Work Training Scheme, the following separate headings are being approved for the general component: -
