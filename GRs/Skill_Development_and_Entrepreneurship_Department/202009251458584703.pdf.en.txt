The Department of Vocational Education& Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has extended the period of second and third term of the existing Industrial Training Institutes in 43 Minority Concentration Areas of the State and a total of 417 temporary posts in Mandvi and Nehrunagar (Kurla) in Brihanmumbai from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021.

Letter No.4/ Aastha- 3/2019/ Extension/ 420 dated 11.08.2020 from the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai.
Preamble: The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has sanctioned 417 temporary posts under the scheme for opening of 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi and Nehrunagar (Kurla) in the second and third shifts and 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi and Kurla in 43 Minority Concentration Areas of the State.
As per the Government decision issued from time to time by the Finance Department, the said temporary posts have been extended up to 31.08.2020. Under the Government Resolution (2) issued in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter at (3), the matter of extension of temporary posts from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021 was under consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: ITI-2019/ PR. No. 36/ Special-3 GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, hereby approves the continuation of 417 temporary posts (attached Form A) under the schemes sanctioned for opening of 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi and Nehrunagar (Kurla) in 43 Minority Concentration Areas of the State in the second and third shifts and 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mumbai, subject to the following conditions and conditions:-
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
(4) In case of any question of remuneration of such temporary posts, the responsibility shall be of the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai.
Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the approved grants under the following heads during the financial year 2020-21.
The Director (Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall take care to submit the Budget Estimates, Revised Estimates and Final Revised Grants proposals under the head" Demand No. ZE-1, 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 02 Social Welfare, 200-Other Programmes, Schemes under State Schemes, (00) (16) Subsidiary Grants for starting second and third term in existing Industrial Training Institutes for Minority Students, (2235 A 534) within the prescribed time.
This Government Decision was issued by the Department of Minority Development vide Informal Reference No. 8/2020/ Q.8 and Finance Department vide Government Decision No. 8/16/ P.C., dated 31.08.2020 is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
