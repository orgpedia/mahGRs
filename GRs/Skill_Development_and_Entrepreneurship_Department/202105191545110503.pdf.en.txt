The government was considering to implement" Mukhyamantri Maha Arogya Skill Development Training Programme" in the state to create skilled manpower in this sector by assigning batch numbers.
Government's decision:
In order to ensure availability of skilled manpower in the health and medical sector in the emerging situation of the pandemic and to overcome the shortage of manpower in the resources of this sector, it is being approved to implement the" Mukhyamantri Maha Arogya Skill Development Training Program" under the Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission (PMKUVA) in the state to provide employment opportunities to the youth of the state who are willing to work in the health sector by providing skill development training in the fields of healthcare, medical and nursing and domestic workers.
2. The Guidelines and Criteria of the" Mukhyamantri Maha Arogya Kaushal Vikas Shikshan" programme are similar to the" Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission (PMKUVA)" as per the Government Decision No. 1 and currently the following guidelines and criteria have been relaxed to provide immediate manpower in the COVID-19 situation in the State.
2.1 All government hospitals and medical education institutions in the state will be selected by registering them as Vocational Training Institutes (VTIs) through the green channel.
2.2 The Head of the Department concerned shall have the power to take necessary decisions in relation to health skills training in the hospital.
2.3 The number of candidates in the training batch should be a minimum of 20 and a maximum of 30, but as a special case, the total number of trainees in the training batch for this scheme will be allowed to be a minimum of 5.
2.4 The course will also cover driving as well as ambulance driver for transportation of medical facilities required for treatment of COVID-19 patients.
2.5 All trainees who complete the training should be registered through the competent system. The concerned departments should take necessary steps in this regard.
2.6 All trainees who complete the training will be required to serve in government or private hospitals for at least 6 months.

3." Mukhyamantri Maha Arogya Kaushal Vikas Prashikshan" Programme No. 4 is being approved to be implemented from the funds received from other departments as per the decision of the Government.

Similarly, all the private and government medical institutions/ hospitals in the state which are implementing the said course are being declared as vocational training institutions and the process of issuing training batch numbers to such institutions is being approved by the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, Mumbai.
For effective and speedy implementation of this programme, Commissioner, Skill Development, Commissionerate of Employment and Entrepreneurship, Navi Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer," Assistant Director (Accounts), Konkan Bhawan as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and CEO, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, Mumbai as the" Nodal Agency."
The Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Navi Mumbai shall transfer the funds made available by the Government from time to time under this programme to the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, Mumbai for expenditure. The accounts of the funds available under the Pramod Mahajan Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Mission and other departments should be kept independently. Also, the legislative matters arising in connection with this programme should be handled by the concerned department for which funds have been provided.
