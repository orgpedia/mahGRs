(2) This order comes into force with immediate effect and the Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship should relieve the above officers immediately to join the post indicated by the transfer.
(3) Since the transfer is on request, the travel allowance, daily allowance and the period of holding office as admissible to them shall not be admissible.
4.The concerned officer should immediately submit a report to the government about the date on which he appeared at the place of posting.
If the transferred officer cancels his transfer or directly or indirectly presses for it, disciplinary action will be taken against him as the act violates Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
No leave shall be granted to the officers transferred as above without prior approval and no leave applications of the officers transferred as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981 shall be accepted by the parent office. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities by registered post to the original address.
