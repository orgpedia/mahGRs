Preamble:- Under the State Craftsmen Training Scheme under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 90 temporary posts under Tribal Appointments out of 576 sanctioned under the sanctioned scheme for setting up new Industrial Training Institutes in the newly created 32 Taluks have been approved under the Government decision under reference (1). Under the scheme of Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education, 414 temporary posts have been approved as per the decision of the Government under reference (2). The said temporary posts have been extended upto 31.08.2022 as per the Government decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time. Under the Government Resolution (3) issued in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023. Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra

Government Resolution No: ITI-2019/ PR No. 37/ Special-3 State, Mumbai As per the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter at (4), the Government was considering the extension of temporary posts.
Government Decision:- The 90 temporary posts under the sanctioned schemes for the establishment of new Industrial Training Institutes in the newly created 32 taluks under the Craftsmen Training Scheme in the state under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and 414 temporary posts under the scheme of Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education (Form A and B attached) are being approved to continue from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 under this Government Decision subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
(4) In case of any question of remuneration of such temporary posts, the responsibility shall be of the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal should be met from the approved grant under the following head for the financial year 2022-2023.
" Demand No. T-9, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, 2 Welfare of Scheduled Tribes, 796, Janjati Area Sub-Plan, 31 Schemes under Janjati Area Appropriation, Tribal Component Programme.
The Director (Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall ensure that the Budget Estimates, Revised Estimates and Final Revised Grant Proposals under the above headings are submitted within the prescribed time.
