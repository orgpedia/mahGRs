Guidelines for implementing the Grand Challenge in line with the Maharashtra State Innovation Startup Policy, 2018
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship,
Government Resolution No: KOUVIU-2019/ PR No.75/ Mission-1
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400032
Date is September 18, 2019.
The Proposal:
The" Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018" has been approved under the Government Decision dated 05/02/2018 of the Department to promote industries based on innovative resolutions to promote industrial and economic development of the State and to address the problem of unemployment in the country.
In pursuance of the said policy, the Government was contemplating to organize various events and competitions in the State to showcase and promote the Startup ecosystem nationally and internationally and to issue guidelines for organizing Grand Challenges to understand and solve the problems/ problems of various departments in the State.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018 has been approved to promote enterprises based on innovative resolutions to promote industrial and economic development of the state and to address the problem of unemployment in the country.
The Maharashtra State Innovation Society should immediately take the necessary action regarding the organization of the Grand Challenge in accordance with these guidelines.
Issues/ difficulties in this regard should be given with the approval of the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary of the concerned department. If two different departments give the same problem/ difficulty, a combined Grand Challenge will be held on it.
