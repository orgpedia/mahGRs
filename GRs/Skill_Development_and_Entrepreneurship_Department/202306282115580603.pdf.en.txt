If the transferred officer cancels his transfer or directly or indirectly presses for it, disciplinary action will be taken against him as the act violates Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
(6) During the period prescribed under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Cessation of Service), Non-Ministerial Service and Suspension, Promotion and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, the concerned officer shall take care to join immediately at the place of posting of the transferor. The transferred officials should take note of this.
No leave shall be granted without prior approval of the officers transferred as above and no leave applications of the officers transferred as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981 shall be accepted by the parent office. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities by registered post at the original address.
