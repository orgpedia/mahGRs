The Proposal:
Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation was established by the Department of Employment and Self-Employment vide No. 1, the decision of the government. It was established on November 27, 1998.
The Company is registered under the Companies Act, 1956. 50 crores. The head office of the corporation is in Mumbai and the maximum number of directors and members of the corporation is 15. The corporation has been formed to provide employment and self-employment to the unemployed youth and to provide social development to the economically backward sections of the state. As per the Government decision dated 31.03.2017, the shareholding in the Corporation has been increased to Rs.400 crore.
From 02.02.2018 onwards, three schemes viz., 1) Personal Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, 2) Group Loan Interest Repayment Scheme, 3) Group Project Loan Scheme are being implemented by the Corporation.
Annasaheb Patil has decided to hand over the responsibility of controlling the financial and administrative affairs of the Economic Backward Development Corporation to the Planning Department instead of the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department. This is in pursuance of the discussions held in the review meeting of Sarathi Sanstha held on 09.07.2020 under the chairmanship of Deputy Chief Minister. The matter of transferring the corporation from the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The entire work related to Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation is being transferred from the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department to the Planning Department along with all the schemes there.
