Preamble: As per the Government decision under reference (1), 576 temporary posts have been sanctioned under the sanctioned scheme for setting up new Industrial Training Institutes in the newly created 32 Taluks under the Craftsmen Training Scheme in the State under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. Under the scheme of Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education, 414 vacant posts have been approved under the Government decision of Reference (2). The said temporary posts have been extended upto 29.02.2020 as per the Government decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time. Under the Government Resolution (3) issued in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/03/2020 to 31/08/2020. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter at (4), the matter of extension of temporary posts from 01/03/2020 to 31/08/2020 was under consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: ITI-2019/ PR. No.37/ VISHI-3 GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, under the newly created 32 Taluks under the Craftsman Training Scheme in the State, has approved the continuation of 576 temporary posts under the sanctioned schemes and 414 temporary posts under the scheme of Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education (Form A& B attached) from 01/03/2020 to 31/08/2020, subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
(4) In case of any question of remuneration of such temporary posts, the responsibility shall be of the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the approved grants under the following heads during the financial year 2020-21.
