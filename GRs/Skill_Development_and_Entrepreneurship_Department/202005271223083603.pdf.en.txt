Preamble: The Government of India has created a separate Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship with a view to promote skill development and entrepreneurship and to ensure that the programme is addressed more effectively. On the lines of the Central Government, an independent Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department has also been established in the State under the said Government Decision dated 15th January, 2015 of the General Administration Department. Under the Skill Development Programme of the Government of India, a target has been set to create 50 crore skilled manpower by 2022.
In order to achieve the set targets in respect of the Skill Development Programme, the Government was contemplating to reconstitute the Committees constituted under the said Government Decision dated 12.08.2015 of the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department for the purpose of monitoring, coordinating and supervising the programme at the State level and to implement the programme at the district level throughout the State.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In order to effectively implement the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Programme in the State, the following Committees constituted earlier are being reconstituted and accordingly the Government Decision dated 12th August, 2015 regarding the earlier Committees is being superseded. The revised composition and functions of the State, Division and District level Committees will be as follows:
