Preamble: It has been a long time since xerox machines were installed in the offices of the Commissionerate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Divisional Commissionerate under Navi Mumbai, Nashik and its subordinate District Offices and Divisional Commissionerate, Aurangabad. These parts are not available in the market. The Commissionerate informed the Commissionerate that the concerned offices have informed the Commissionerate with the necessary technical report about the machines being non-readable. dated 02.08.2023. Accordingly, administrative approval for the purchase of new xerox machines for a total of 7 offices, including 2 Divisional Commissionerates and 5 District Offices, under the Commissionerate and financial sanction for an estimated amount of Rs. 8.75 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Administrative approval is hereby accorded for the purchase of new Xerox machines for the following 7 offices under the Commissionerate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship at a budget of Rs. 8.75 lakhs:
