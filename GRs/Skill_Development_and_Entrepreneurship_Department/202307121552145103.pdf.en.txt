The Proposal:
The Scheme for Skill Development of Youth in the districts affected by militancy has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 30th June, 2011. The scheme is centrally sponsored and the Centre and states share is 75 per cent and 25 per cent respectively. Under the scheme, approval has been given to start government industrial training institutes in the Naxal-affected districts of Jimlaghatta and Gondia in Gadchiroli district. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Accountant, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai-1 as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for drawing payments from the treasury.
Punjab National Bank (PNB) has opened an SNA account on the directions of the Central Government for the Skill Development Programme for the youth of the districts affected by militancy. The scheme has been mapped to PFMS and the Joint Secretary has been registered with PFMS as State Scheme Manager. The Scheme is being implemented by the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, MH-526, SNA Scheme Code MAP, for Skill Development of Youth in Districts Affected by Left Wing Extremism, DDO Code 7101002026 Accordingly, the Accountant, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai-1 is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for drawing payments from the treasury.
