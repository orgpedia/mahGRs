The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Skill Development Board, Maharashtra State, Mumbai (formerly Maharashtra State Board of Vocational Education Examination) offers part-time and full-time vocational certificate courses of 6 months, 1 year and 2 years duration on a self-financed basis from private institutions recognized by the Board. About 70 per cent of the private institutions offering these courses are in rural areas and 30 per cent in urban areas.
Since the tuition fees for these courses have not been fixed since the inception of the Maharashtra State Skill Development Board, private institutions affiliated to this board are charging tuition fees at different rates for different courses. The tuition fee for the short-term certificate course to be implemented by the Maharashtra State Board of Vocational Education (Maharashtra State Skill Development Board) under the government decision of March 19, 2013, is provisional as well as the amount of tuition fee is final in this government decision.

Government's decision:
The decision taken under the Government decision dated 19th March, 2013 regarding fixation of tuition fee for short term certificate course to private institutions recognized on self-financing basis by Maharashtra State Skill Development Board is being superseded by this Government decision.
Accordingly, the students belonging to Backward Classes category who have taken admission for certificate courses of part-time and full-time nature of six months, one year or less than two years duration to be run on self-financing basis through private institutions recognized by the Maharashtra State Skill Development Board, Government of India.
The benefit of post-matric scholarship, as well as tuition fee/ examination fee, is not admissible as per the guidelines of the Central Government.
