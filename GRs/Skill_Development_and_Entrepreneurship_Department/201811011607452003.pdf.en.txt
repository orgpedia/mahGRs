As per the proposal submitted to the Government vide Government Resolution No: ITI-2018/ PR No.81/ Person-3& Training), Directorate of Vocational Education& Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter dated 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019, the extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Craftsman Training Scheme under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, new posts will be created in 32 newly created taluks.
Under the Scheme for opening of Institutions, 576 temporary posts (shown in the accompanying Form) have been approved to be continued from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions
1) The above posts should be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed time.
4) The revised pattern after reviewing the posts needs to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. In case of non-payment of salary, the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai will be responsible for the same. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the following heads during the financial year 2018-19.
