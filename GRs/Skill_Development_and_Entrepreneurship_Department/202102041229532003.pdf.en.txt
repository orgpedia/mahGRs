The Proposal:
The Skill Acquisition and Knowledge Awareness for Livelihood Project (SANKALP) will be implemented by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India, under the National Skill Development Mission (NSDM), with the objectives of strengthening the institutional mechanism at the country and state level, creating a pool of quality trainers, centralizing all skill training programs implemented at the state level, creating an effective mechanism for monitoring and evaluating skill development training programs, providing opportunities for skill training for socially disadvantaged groups, creating skilled manpower required for the manufacturing sector and supplementing the Make in India initiative. The duration of the project is 6 years and the Central and State Governments share in the project cost is 60: 40. Administrative approval has been given to implement this project in the state under the decision of the Government No. 2. The Centre has allocated Rs. 22.40 crore.
