(2) The transferred officer shall, on receipt of the transfer order, report at the place of posting and shall be relieved forthwith by the Head of the office concerned. The concerned officers should submit their joining report along with the transfer certificate to the government without delay through the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
(3) No leave of absence shall be granted to any officer posted as above without the previous sanction of the Government or any leave application of the officer posted as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave Rules, 1981) shall be accepted by the parent office.
(4) When the officers who are being appointed to such posts are appointed to the post assigned to them, the office concerned shall verify whether they were on leave/ absent or how they were appointed and present them at the time of appointment. The officer should be sent to the medical board before presenting him at the posting absent on medical grounds. If found on another leave, fill the leave application form without fail from them

(5) The transferred officer shall ensure that he joins the place of transfer within the period of assumption of office as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Self-Election and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. Otherwise, if he does not join the place of transfer during this period, the period of absence shall be deemed to be non-dies.
If any officer tries to directly or indirectly pressurize the transfer order and submits any kind of application for cancellation of the transfer, then disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned officer as the action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
