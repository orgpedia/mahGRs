Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MAHALT) has been appointed to implement customized HTE service delivery system in all non-agricultural universities, all aided colleges and other aided institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education. Through this system, the salary and allowances of the employees of the vocational courses (primary and higher secondary vocational) in the non-government funded technical secondary schools and higher secondary schools under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under this department are paid online.
Since the contract for development and maintenance of the" HTE Sevarth" system has expired at the end of July 2018, the entire responsibility has been entrusted to Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MahalT). The Department of Finance vide letter dated 3rd November, 2018 has informed about the development and maintenance of customized service delivery system by engaging independent technical manpower in separate agreement with MahalT for regularization of the system.
As per the agreement with Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MAHALT), an agreement has been signed with MAHALT through the Higher and Technical Education Department for the development and maintenance of customized service delivery system and providing technical manpower. The system covers 69000 officers and staff of Higher and Technical Education Department and 9535 officers and staff of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department. The number of officers and staff is 87 per cent of the total strength of the Higher and Technical Education Department and 13 per cent of the total strength of the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
