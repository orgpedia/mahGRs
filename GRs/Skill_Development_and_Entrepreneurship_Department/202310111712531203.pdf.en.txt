While starting the MAGIC (Marathwada Accelerator for Growth and Incubation Council) Center, there will be no break in the ongoing academic course in Jalna ITI, ensuring that the place is provided to MAGIC Institute only after arranging for the transfer of trainees from the ITI to the same office premises.
