Government's decision:
The extension of tenure of temporary posts (attached Forms A, B, C& D) in various offices/ schools/ centres under Directorate of Vocational Education& Training from 01/03/2021 to 31/08/2021 is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
(1) It shall be used for the purpose for which the above posts are created.
(2) None of the extended posts shall remain vacant for a period of 6 months or more. This should be verified.
(3) Care should be taken to obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the review of the sanctioned posts as well as the revised review (including the newly created posts in the figure) during this period.
