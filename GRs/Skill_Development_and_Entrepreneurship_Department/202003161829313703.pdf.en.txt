Introduction: Higher Secondary Business Courses at+ 2 level (HSC Voc. ) On the various questions of the teacher directors. The meeting was chaired by the Minister of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship on 21st January 2020. In the meeting, it was decided to form a new committee by abolishing the two committees constituted under the Government Decision dated 28th October, 2013 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education and the Government Decision dated 22nd October, 2018 of the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship regarding the Higher Secondary Vocational Courses at the+ 2 level. The government was contemplating setting up a new committee.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approve the setting up of the following Committees to recommend to the Government in pursuance of the policy decision to abolish the two Committees constituted under the Government Decision dated 28th October, 2013 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education and the Government Decision dated 22nd October, 2018 of the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship regarding the Higher Secondary Business Courses at+ 2 level and to convert the Higher Secondary Business Courses at+ 2 level into other courses.
