Preamble: The Committee has been constituted by the decision dated 01.10.2015 of the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, regarding the sale and disposal of old and defunct machinery in the Industrial Training Institute. Instructions have also been given for the implementation of this scheme. Accordingly, a decision was taken to de-list the machines by holding a meeting of the Committee on 04.06.2019 at Industrial Training Institute, Beed, Aurangabad Division, Regional Office under the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide their Reference No. The proposal has been submitted to the government through a letter. A proposal was under consideration of the government to approve the pending de-recognition of machinery at the Industrial Training Institute, Nanded, which was recommended by the state-level de-recognition committee but the de-recognition order was not issued.
