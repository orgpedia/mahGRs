The order comes into force with immediate effect and the Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship should relieve the above officers to join the post indicated in the replacement immediately.
3. Since the transfers are periodic transfers, travel allowance, daily allowance will be admissible to them.
4.The concerned officer should immediately submit a report to the government about the date on which the transfer officer appeared at the place of posting.
If the transferred officer cancels his transfer or directly or indirectly presses for it, disciplinary action will be taken against him as the act violates Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
During the period of office prescribed under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Cessation of Office), Non-Ministerial Service and Suspension, Promotion and Removal from Service Rules, 1981, the concerned officer shall take care to join immediately at the place of posting of the transferor, failing which the period of absence shall be deemed to be a non-working day. The transferred officials should take note of this.
No leave shall be granted to the officers transferred as above without prior permission and no leave applications of the officers transferred as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981 shall be accepted by the parent office. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities by registered post to the original address.
