The proposal-
Instructions have been given to create more number of posts for the officers/ employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category under the Government decision dated 21.12.2019 under the General Administration Department, but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or have submitted the other caste validity certificate, and to send a requisition to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to fill the vacancies of the officers/ employees appointed to the post of the said Scheduled Tribes. Accordingly, a total of 3 supernumerary posts in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System- 1 post in Group-A (C) (TAN) cadre and 2 posts in Group-B (TAN) cadre- were under the consideration of the Government at the establishment of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
Government's decision
Those who were appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but could not submit the caste validity certificate, those who submitted the Other Backward Classes caste validity certificate, those who had pending court cases regarding the caste validity certificate and those whose caste validity certificate was invalidated, the service ended.

(a) A total of 3 supernumerary posts are being created, namely 1 post in (TAAN) cadre and 2 posts in Group-B (TAAN) cadre:
