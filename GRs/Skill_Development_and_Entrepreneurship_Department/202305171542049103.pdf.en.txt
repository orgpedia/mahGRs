Introduction: Maharashtra is an industrially advanced state where new industrial establishments have expanded their industrial production. The Industrial Development Corporation of India (MIDC) is located at Yerawada in Pune district. It is home to some of Asia's most prominent industries, including Fiat, Bajaj Electricals, LG Electronics, Haier India Ltd. Yezhaki, Baker Gages, etc. Industry is engaged in automobile, manufacturing, electrical, electronics.
In view of the increasing population of Pune city and the available sanctioned intake capacity in the Industrial Training Institute in Pune city, the admission of the aspiring candidates is not possible.
Accordingly, administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 16.12.2008 to start a new Industrial Training Institute at Yerawada based on modern technology and keeping in view the local needs and demands.
Department of Higher and Technical Education Government Decision dated 03.07.2014 has approved conversion of affiliated units of Centre of Excellence Basic Module into Vocational Courses under Craftsmen Training Scheme from the academic year 2014-15. Yerawada. A total of 40 posts were under consideration of the government for setting up a new Industrial Training Institute at Haveli, Pune with 18 vocational units and the manpower required for the same was 23 teaching, 8 non-teaching and 9 fourth grade/ watchmen/ sweepers to be provided by external agencies.
