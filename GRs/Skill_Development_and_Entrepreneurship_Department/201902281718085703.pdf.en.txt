As per the decision of the Government dated 10/09/2001 of the Department of Finance, the posts in the head office under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State Vocational Education Examination Board, all regional offices, office of the District Vocational Education and Training Officer, Government Technical School and Government Certificate Courses have been reviewed. After the review, the revised figure of posts in the above office has been approved for 3374 posts under the Government Decision dated 25/07/2007. After that, the Government Technical School/ Centre Sadoba Saoli Ta. and G. Yavatmal and Government Technical School/ Centre, Ghodegaon, Tt. As per the Government Decision dated 01/02/2008, a total of 26 teaching and non-teaching posts have been approved for Ambegaon, G. Pune to start these institutions under Tribal Appointment. The extension of these temporary posts has been granted from 01/03/2008 to 28/02/2019 subject to the terms of the Government Resolution issued from time to time. These 26 temporary posts have been sanctioned by the Finance Department vide Government Order dated 21/02/2019. 01/03/2019 to D. The matter of extending the deadline till 30/09/2019 was under consideration of the Government.
