Proposal for the General Administration Department. As per the provisions of 21/12/2019, the number of employees to be transferred to the superannuation post on various grounds of non-submission of caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes, the number of employees to be transferred to the superannuation post in respect of the post in which they are presently working, and the number of employees who have been relieved from service due to the invalidation of the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes before the decision of the Government of the General Administration Department mentioned above, shall be temporarily assigned to all the administrative departments for a period of 11 months or till the date on which they would have retired, whichever is earlier
2. General Administration Department The authority to create such number of additional posts has been delegated to the Administrative Department under Government Resolution 30.6.2020. According to reference no. Under Section 4, 74 officers have been created and transferred to the rank of Superintendent for 11 months. Since the period of transfer of service of these employees to the higher post ended on 15.06.2021, the matter of extension of their service beyond 16.06.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
As a result of this Government decision, out of the above 74 supernumerary posts, a total of 13 ST employees in Form A and 1 in Form B have retired, excluding these posts, the remaining 39 in Form A, 13 in Form B and 9 in Form C.
