As per the Government decision dated 21.09.2006, 576 temporary posts have been sanctioned to start new Government Industrial Training Institutes in the newly created 32 Taluks under the Craftsmen Training Scheme. Out of which, 27 Non-Tribal Industrial Training Institute sanctioned 486 temporary posts, all such temporary posts have been notified vide Reference No. The date has been extended up to 31.08.2022 as per the Government decision dated 09.09.2022. Accordingly, the matter of granting extension of all the above temporary posts from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The extension of the temporary posts (attached form A, B, C, D, E, F and G) in various Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and 06 Regional Offices under the State Government, Government Industrial Training Institutes, Advanced Vocational Training Systems, Machinery Inspection and Repair and Squad, Basic Training and Allied Instruction Center, Government Industrial Workshops and Similar Centers, Evening Classes for Industrial Workers, Hi-Tech Training Scheme from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 is being approved subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
(1) The said posts shall be utilised for the purpose for which they have been created.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
(3) Care should be taken to obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the review of the sanctioned posts as well as the revised review (including the newly created posts in the figure) during this period.
The expenditure for the said post shall be covered under the following heads of accounts and the expenditure shall be met from the provision approved in the financial year 2022-2023.
