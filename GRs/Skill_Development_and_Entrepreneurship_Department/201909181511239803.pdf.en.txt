Date is September 18, 2019.
The Proposal:
In order to promote the industrial and economic development of the state and to promote enterprises based on innovative resolutions to address the problem of unemployment in the country, the Department has approved the" Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018" under the Government Decision dated 05/ 02/ 2018.
In line with this policy, the government was considering issuing guidelines for financial assistance to start-ups for registering their intellectual property rights.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018 has been approved to promote industries based on innovative resolutions to boost the industrial and economic development of the state and to address the problem of unemployment in the country. As per the policy, startups need adequate knowledge of Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) and financial support for the same. Also it is important to protect the intellectual property rights of the innovative concept they have made. In this regard, the guidelines in Annexure A are being approved to provide support in the form of Intellectual Property Rights to startups.
In accordance with the said guidelines, the Maharashtra State Innovation Society should take the necessary action to finance the Intellectual Property Rights immediately.
