The Proposal:
The Department of Industries, Energy and Labour has issued a rule book on procedures for office procurement to be carried out by government departments under the Government Decision dated 30/12/2015. The composition and powers of the various Committees are set out in item No. 2.9 of Chapter II of the said Rules.
Provision for constitution of Procurement Committee under each Administrative Department/ Office under the Chairmanship of Head of Department (DPC/ PC) to undertake procurement process as per the procedure laid down in the said rule book (In the said rule book, item No. in 2.9.2). As per the said provision, the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (DSDE) has not constituted the DPC/ PC.
Purchase Committees have been constituted under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India, at State, Divisional and District levels vide order dated 13.11.2019. The Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) has also formed a committee to look into the matter.
