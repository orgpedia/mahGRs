The Proposal:
The Government of India has approved the implementation of Phase-III of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana 3.0 (100% Centrally Sponsored, State Managed PMKVY 3.0-CSSM) vide letter No. 13 dated 01.04.2013. A sum of Rs. 6,01,50, 000/- has been disbursed to the State Government category wise. Out of this amount, Rs. Nasti was in the process of approving the disbursement and expenditure of Rs. 51.73 lakh to the Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Navi Mumbai through Public Financial Management System (PFMS) and Budget Estimates, Distribution and Monitoring System.
Government Decision: Under" Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana 3.0" (100% Centrally Sponsored, State Managed- PMKVY 3.0-CSSM), Rs. 51.73 lakh has been disbursed by the Tribal Development Department under No. 16 to the Department of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation and the Commissionerate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Navi Mumbai is being approved to disburse and spend the same through Public Financial Management System (PFMS) and Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System.
The Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship shall draw the funds allocated for this scheme by the State Government from the treasury and make it available for expenditure to the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, Mumbai. For this, Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Navi Mumbai is being declared as" Controlling Officer" and Assistant Director (Accounts) Konkan Bhawan is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
3. Common Guidelines for implementing skill development programmes at the country level have been issued by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India through a contextual notification.
It shall be mandatory for the implementing agency to spend the amount as per the provisions of the Common Guidelines or as amended by the Central Government from time to time for the activities/ projects for which funds have been received from the Central Government. Separate articles on page 2.
