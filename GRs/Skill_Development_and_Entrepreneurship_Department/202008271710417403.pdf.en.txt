Maharashtra State Skill Development Examination Board was established in the year 1986. The basic purpose of the formation of the board is to provide employment or self-employment to the unemployed. For this, the Board runs the tender professional courses of 6 months, 1 year and 2 years duration on self-financing basis through various government and private institutions.
A certificate or diploma is awarded to the candidates who pass the examination conducted by the Board. For this, various institutions are affiliated to the board, permission to run courses, etc., are carried out by the board, however, it has been directed by the government that some institutions running business courses provide self-certification to mutual candidates by training them in professional courses without obtaining affiliation from the board. It was under the consideration of the government to establish a board through an Act to bring all such private institutions under the purview of the law by registering them with the Maharashtra Skill Development Examination Board and getting the candidates prepared by the board and giving them certificates. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has approved the constitution of a Committee for the preparation of the State Skill Examination Board Act and the Committee is being constituted as follows:

4) To conduct Diploma, Advance Diploma level courses and award certificates by the State Skill Development Examination Board.

The Maharashtra State Skill Development Examination Act was passed by the Legislative Assembly. As it is to be issued with the approval of the Governor, the above committee issues an order to the government within 1 month to prepare its report for enactment.
