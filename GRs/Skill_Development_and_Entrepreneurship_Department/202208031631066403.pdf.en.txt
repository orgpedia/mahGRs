Preamble: The Committee has been constituted by a decision dated 01.10.2015 of the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai regarding the de-registration and sale of old and defunct machinery in Industrial Training Institutes. Also, instructions have been given to prepare for the process of delineation. Accordingly, a meeting of the Committee was held on 31.08.2021 in the Regional Office under the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, Industrial Training Institute, Nagpur and Industrial Training Institute, Nagpur (Rural) in Nagpur Division. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide their Reference No. The proposal has been submitted to the government through letters 4 and 5. A proposal was under consideration of the government to approve the pending de-recognition of machinery in the Industrial Training Institute, Nagpur and Industrial Training Institute, Nagpur (Rural), which was recommended by the State Level De-recognition Committee but the de-recognition order was not issued.
