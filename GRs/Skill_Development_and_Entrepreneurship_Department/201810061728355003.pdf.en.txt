This Order shall be applicable to the admission of all existing trades/ batches in the Government Industrial Training Institute in the State approved for upgradation to the status of Public Private Partnership by the Central Government and to be started from August, 2018 onwards and the Institute Management Committee shall award 20% marks in the order of merit (out of supernumerary 30%) in excess of the intake capacity in all trades/ batches in the respective OPI from August, 2018 onwards. The admissions shall be made through online mode as per the Admission Manual issued by the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. of India vide Decision No. 3315/ No. 17/ 3 dated 14.05.2015.
The occupations/ units in the institution should be filled up to 20% of their intake capacity i.e. 12, 16, 20 respectively in the seats of Institution Management Committee of the concerned Government Industrial Institute for 2, 3 and 4 seats respectively and the remaining 10% (out of supernumerary 30%) seats i.e. 1, 2 and 2 seats respectively should be filled up by the concerned institution in the prevailing manner.
