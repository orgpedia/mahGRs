Preamble: Reference No. 1 for recommendation for promotion to the post of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) (Technical/ Intellectual) cadre in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship. (1) The Departmental Promotion Committee has been constituted under the Government decision dated 20th April, 2016. However, in reference to General Administration Department No. (2) Consolidated guidelines on the procedure to be followed for promotion of State Government officers/ employees have been issued vide Government Order dated 01.08.2019. As per the Government Order No. (1) Here. The matter of reconstitution of the Gathit Divisional Promotion Committee for making recommendations for promotion to the post of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) (Technical/ Technical) cadre of Maharashtra Education Service in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training by superseding the Government decision dated 16th April, 2016 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Departmental Promotion Committee is being reconstituted to recommend for promotion to the post of Group-A (Gazetted) (Technical/ Intellectual) cadre in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, under the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship as follows:
