Reference:- Government Circular, Finance Department No. Salary 2023/ S.No.13/ Service-3D. 28.06.2023
Preamble: The aforesaid Reference Government has issued guidelines for fixation of retirement pay to the employees who have retired/ are due to retire on 30th June, taking into account the hypothetical increment on 1st July. The matter was under the consideration of the government to bring the matter to the notice of the field offices for the teachers and non-teaching staff in the non-government aided institutions implementing professional courses under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training.
Government circular:
The instructions issued under the letter in the context of the Finance Department are being implemented in the case of teachers/ non-teaching staff in non-government aided institutions running higher secondary vocational courses, bilingual courses and pre-vocational courses under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training in the state. Be sure to follow these instructions exactly.
