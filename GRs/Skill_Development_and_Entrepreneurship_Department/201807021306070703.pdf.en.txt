Preamble:- Provisional posts under 414 schemes of" Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education" under Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved under the Government decision at (2). (1) All administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts under the Government decision. As per the Government Decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time, the above Reference No. The said 414 temporary posts have been extended till 31.05.2018. Under the Government Decision (3) in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 30.09.2018. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted to the Government by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter dated 01.06.2018 to 30.09.2018, the extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: ITI-2018/ PR No.78/ Person-3
Government Decision:- Under the scheme of" Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education" under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 414 temporary posts under the scheme in the accompanying Form A have been approved to be continued from 01.06.2018 to 30.09.2018, subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
(iv) The revised pattern of review of posts is required to be approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in any case by 30.09.2018. If the approval of the High-Level Secretary Committee is not taken within this period, then the extension of these posts will not be agreed by the Tribal Development Department.
(5) In case of any question of remuneration of these temporary posts, the responsibility shall be of the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the following heads:
" Demand No. The Director (Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall take care of submission of Budget Estimates, Revised Estimates and Final Revised Grants proposals under the head' T-5, Accounts Head 2230 Labour and Service Schemes, Schemes under Five Year Plans, Tribal Area Sub-Plan, 03-Training, 796-Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) Schemes under Tribal Area Deployment, (01) Schemes under State Schemes, (01) Industrial Training Institutes, Extension of Industrial Training Institutes to Tribal Areas (22300764)' within the prescribed time.
