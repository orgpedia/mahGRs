Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and its subordinate institutions and offices.
The revised pattern of posts is given in Reference No. (1) It is hereby approved by the Government. In this figure, the ranks of some institutions and offices have been upgraded and some functional posts have been eliminated. Since these posts have been lying vacant,
As the ranks of the posts in some offices and institutions have been upgraded, it has become the order of the day to adjust the officers working in those posts to the vacancies in the same cadre. Accordingly, options were sought from the additional designated officers through the Directorate. The options received from the officers and the proposal for adjustment accordingly are mentioned in Reference No. (2) received from the Director.
Proposal for adjustment of postings after considering the proposal of adjustment received from the Directorate and the options of the said officers. The Principal Secretary (R& AW), The Civil Service Board, headed by Shri. Presented in the meeting held on 07/09/2022. In pursuance of the recommendation made by the Civil Services Board, the proposal for grant of postings by adjustment has been approved by the competent authority. Accordingly, the following adjustments are being made to the additional appointed officers in the cadre of Assistant Director (Senior) (Atantrik), Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A (Junior) (Atantrik) in the same cadre:
