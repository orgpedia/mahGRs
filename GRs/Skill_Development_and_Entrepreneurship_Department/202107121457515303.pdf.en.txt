Preamble: In order to encourage and increase the recruitment of apprentices under the Apprenticeship Training Scheme in Government, Semi-Government and Private establishments, the" Maharashtra Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme" has been approved vide Government Decision dated 03.06.2021. Under this scheme, the government allows apprentices to receive 75 per cent of the salary or a maximum of Rs 5,000.
The proposal of Maharashtra State Information Technology Corporation (Maha IT) to create and operate the website for the scheme was under consideration of the Government to approve the expenditure from the financial provision for the financial year 2021-22.
