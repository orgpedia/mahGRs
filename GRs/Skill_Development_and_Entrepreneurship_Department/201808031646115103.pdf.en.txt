Introduction: For the year 2018-2019, 10 Fruitful Areas (K.R.A.) have been identified for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department. Therefore, the progress report of the department regarding the K.R.A. has been prepared by the Chief Minister's Office. It can be accessed at www. maharashtra. gov. in. Accordingly, for the year 2018-2019, the Government was considering to fix the responsibilities regarding the K.R.A. to be fixed for the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department and the action to be taken by the officials. In this regard, the Government had earlier vide letter dated 11.05.2018, issued K.R.A
was reported. It was now under consideration to inform the government in the form of a decision.
Government Decision: In order to enable the concerned to achieve the 10 K.R.A. assigned to the department for the year 2018-2019 within the stipulated time, the time period mentioned in the following statement has been fixed. All concerned should note that the annual performance evaluation of the officers will be done taking into account the performance towards this end.
