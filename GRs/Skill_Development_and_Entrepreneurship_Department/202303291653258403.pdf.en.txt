The Proposal:
There is a need to train the staff of the state to become a dynamic administration by increasing efficiency at all levels in the state government service. Training at all levels is necessary to meet the challenges arising out of economic, social, political and technological changes and to achieve the necessary flexibility for change in governance. The training of teachers (practical), employees of group C technical cadre was under the consideration of the government to be conducted at Industrial Training Institute, Pune, Satara and Kolhapur and to approve the expenses incurred for it.
Government's decision:
In view of the above, the Regional Office, Pune under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training is being given approval to conduct training of full time teachers and full time teachers (practical), Group C technical cadre staff of non-government aided institutions under the heading 2203-Technical Education (22033471) in their respective divisions at Industrial Training Institutes, Pune, Satara and Kolhapur subject to the following terms and conditions.
Priority should be given to teachers and staff of non-government aided institutions under this scheme.
The training program should be conducted in the district, department or place where all the facilities of training are available.
The training programme should include up-to-date knowledge of the subject concerned, use of modern audio-visual aids, instructional materials, new teaching methods/ technologies, student development and student handling methods, various programmes related to human resources, employment and self-employment, etc.
The training to be imparted to the officers should cover aspects such as leadership development, communication, administrative and financial matters, student welfare, good governance and managerial skills.
The training of non-teaching staff should include training, financial, store management, handling administrative matters related to the establishment, rules laid down by the government and corrections made in them from time to time, computer literacy, introduction of modern technology etc.
For pre-professional courses and+ 2 level business courses, books and other learning/ instructional materials, models, documentaries, etc. should be prepared.
9. (a) The ceiling of cost of training programme per trainee per day shall be Rs. 500/-, which will include the cost of teaching materials, expert remuneration, travel and accommodation expenses, as well as rent of space, stationery, printing, establishment visits and other facilities.
