Government's decision:
As per the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries headed by the Hon'ble Chief Secretary, a total of 945 posts in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System have been notified under the aforesaid reference No. 1 Approval has been granted pursuant to the Government Resolution dated April 9, 2021. Out of the total 945 posts, 189 are temporary. These 189 temporary posts are being filled up under reference No. 2 Under the Government decision herein, it has been approved to continue from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021. Now, as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Resolution dated 10.08.2021 of the Finance Department, in Reference No.3, a total of 189 temporary posts as indicated in Annexure 1 to 4 of the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Mechanism have been created with effect from 01.09.2021. Government approval is being given for continuation of the period up to 28.02.2022 (both days inclusive).
The expenditure for the continuation of the above posts shall be met from the budgetary provision of that financial year under the head of the following relevant accounts in Demand No.ZA-1: 1) 2230, Labour and Employment 02, Employment
