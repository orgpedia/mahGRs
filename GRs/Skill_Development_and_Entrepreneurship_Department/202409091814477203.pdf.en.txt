Introduction: - To enhance the employability of the youth of the state by providing them hands-on training after their education. The "Mukhyamantri Yuva Karyakarta Shiksha Yojana" has been implemented under the government decision of 09.07.2024. The implementation of the scheme is being reviewed from time to time at the senior level. The government was considering issuing additional instructions based on the discussions received from the field offices to overcome the difficulties faced by various field offices regarding the implementation of the scheme as well as to give wider publicity to the scheme so that more and more candidates could get opportunities for hands-on training.
