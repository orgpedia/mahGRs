Introduction: The" Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018" has been approved under the Government Decision dated 05/02/2018 of the Department to promote industries based on innovative resolutions to promote industrial and economic development of the State and to address the problem of unemployment in the country. In line with this policy, the matter of issuing guidelines regarding the establishment and expansion of incubators was under the consideration of the government.
Government decisions to promote industrial and economic development of the state and the country
The Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018 has been approved to promote enterprises based on innovative resolutions to address the problem of unemployment. In pursuance of this policy, the Government is approving the guidelines in Annexure-A for setting up and expansion of incubators in the State in collaboration with industry and academic institutions to create an enabling environment for the development of start-ups and to create successful entrepreneurs.
Further necessary action regarding the establishment and expansion of incubators in accordance with the said guidelines should be taken immediately by the Maharashtra State Innovation Society.
