GOVERNMENT ORDER: The revised figure of posts in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and the institutions and offices under it. (1) It is hereby approved by the Government. In this figure, the ranks of some institutions and offices have been upgraded and some functional posts have been eliminated. Since these functional posts have been abolished and the ranks of the posts in some offices and institutions have been upgraded, it has become expedient to adjust the officers working in those posts to the vacancies in the same organization. Accordingly, options were sought from the additional designated officers through the Directorate. The options received from the officers and the proposal for adjustment accordingly are mentioned in Reference No. (2) received from the Director. Proposal for adjustment of postings after considering the proposal of adjustment received from the Directorate and the options of the said officers. The Civil Services Board, headed by the Principal Secretary (Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation), Presented in the meeting held on 07/09/2022. In pursuance of the recommendation made by the Civil Services Board, the proposal for grant of postings by adjustment has been approved by the competent authority.
Accordingly, the officers who have been appointed as Manager, Industrial Training Institute/ Assistant Director (Intellectual) (Gazetted), Maharashtra Education Service, Group-B (Intellectual) (Gazetted) in the cadre post having been superseded/ upgraded, are being appointed to the vacant post in the same cadre post in the order of precedence as mentioned in column 4 of the following table:

The period of office, travel allowance, daily allowance and incidental benefits to the above officers shall continue to be admissible.
The leave of the concerned officers should not be sanctioned without prior permission of the government and the parent office should not accept any kind of leave applications of the officers who have been posted by adjustment as per rule 30C of the Maharashtra Civil Service (Leave) Rules, 1981. If the officer who has been posted with adjustment to the parent office sends the application for leave, it should be notified that the application will not be taken into account. The service book and service letter of the officers who have been posted by adjustment should be sent to the office of the post office without delay.
