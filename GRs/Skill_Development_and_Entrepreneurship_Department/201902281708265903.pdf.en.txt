In order to remove the regional imbalance in the seats of students of pre-professional courses, the government has decided to create a regular pay scale of 132 posts for the increased batch in the newly created and existing Government Technical Secondary School/ Centre in the state and to increase the rank of Headmaster (Group-B) in Government Technical School, Mulund. Approved on 04/5/2011. The temporary posts under these 133 schemes have been extended from 01/03/2012 to 28/02/2019.
With a view to continue the said batches in Government Technical Secondary Schools/ Centres, a total of 133 temporary posts under the schemes have been sanctioned by the Finance Department vide Government Resolution No. Post No. 2016/ Q. No. 8/16/ A.P.C., Dy. As per the Government decision dated 21/02/2019, the matter of extension from 01/03/2019 to 30/09/2019 was under consideration.

(2) Terms and conditions:
(1) It shall be used for the purpose for which the above posts are created.
(ii) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted is vacant for a period of 6 months or more.
(3) Care should be taken to obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the review of the sanctioned posts as well as the revised review (including the newly created posts in the figure) during this period.
