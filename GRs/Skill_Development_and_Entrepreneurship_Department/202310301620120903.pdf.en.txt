Preamble: As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, it is the policy of the Government to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them from Government service prematurely. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 here. The Government Decision dated 10/06/2019 prescribes the procedure and general instructions regarding service review. Accordingly, for the review of services of officers of the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service, Group A and B (Technical/ Intellectual) (Gazetted) under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Reference No. (2) A Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship or Innovation.
Manager under Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Industrial Training Institute/ Assistant Director (At. The Departmental Review Committee headed by Additional Chief Secretary, Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship or Innovation, has recommended a review of the services of Group-B (A) (Gazetted) cadre officers of Maharashtra Education Service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. Presented in the meeting dated 27/07/2023. As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee after evaluating the service of the said officers, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the officers who have been found eligible to continue in service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
