The Proposal:
The Future Technology Skill Foundation, Mumbai, a sponsoring body for establishing private skill universities in the state on a permanent self-financing basis, has approved the setting up of Atlas Skill Tech University, Mumbai. On 14.06.2021, the Govt. Compliance Report has been submitted pursuant to the Letter of Intent dated 20.05.2021. The sponsor board has requested the government to send an inspection committee to examine the compliance report. As per the request of Future Technology Skills Foundation, Mumbai, the Government was contemplating to constitute an Oversight Committee to examine the compliance report received in pursuance of the Letter of Intent for the proposed self-financing private skill university" Atlas Skill Tech University, Mumbai."
Government's decision:
As per the above proposal, the Monitoring Committee is being constituted as follows:

Committee's mandate:
The Committee will be responsible for verifying the compliance report and monitoring the fulfillment of all the criteria required for setting up a Skill University on a self-financing basis.
The committee will have to submit its report to the government within 15 days from the date of its constitution.
