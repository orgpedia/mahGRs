Preamble: Under the scheme approved for opening of 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi and Nehrunagar (Kurla) and 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi in Brihanmumbai and 2 existing Industrial Training Institutes at 43 Minority Concentration Areas in the State under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 417 temporary posts have been authorized to be extended to temporary posts in all administrative departments as per the decision of the Government in the Reference (1) of the Finance Department. The said temporary posts have been extended upto 30.09.2019 as per the Government decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time. Under the Government Decision (3) issued in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter at (4), the matter of extension of temporary posts from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: ITI-2019/ PR No.36/ Person-3
Government Decision: Under the scheme approved for opening of 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi and Nehrunagar (Kurla) and 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi in Brihanmumbai in the existing Industrial Training Institutes in 43 Minority Concentration Areas of the State under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 417 temporary posts (in Form A attached) are to be continued from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, in cases where the post creation has been approved thereafter, the process of approval of the contours of the posts along with the scrutiny of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
(4) In case of any question of remuneration of the said temporary posts, the entire responsibility shall lie with the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the approved grants under the following heads during the financial year 2019-20.
The Director (Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall take care to submit the Budget Estimates, Revised Estimates and Final Revised Grants proposals under the head" Demand No. ZE-1, 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 02 Social Welfare, 200-Other Programmes, Schemes under State Schemes, (00) (16) Subsidiary Grants for starting second and third term in existing Industrial Training Institutes for Minority Students, (2235 A 534) within the prescribed time.
3. The said Government Decision was taken by the Minority Development Department vide Informal Reference No. 1019/ Avv 36/ K. The 8. 8/16/ EC dated 11th September, 2019 is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
