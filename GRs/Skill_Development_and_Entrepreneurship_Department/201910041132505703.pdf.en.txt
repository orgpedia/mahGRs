GOVERNMENT DECISION: Extension of the period of service of temporary posts (attached Forms A, B, C, D& E) in various offices/ schools/ centres under Directorate of Vocational Education and Training from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020 is being granted subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
(1) The said posts shall be utilised for the purpose for which they have been created.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
(3) Care should be taken to obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the review of the sanctioned posts as well as the revised review (including the newly created posts in the figure) during this period.
(2) The expenditure for the said post shall be covered under the following heads of accounts and the expenditure shall be met from the provision approved in the financial year 2019-2020.
