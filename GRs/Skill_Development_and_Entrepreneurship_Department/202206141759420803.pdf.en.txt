Proposal for the General Administration Department. As per the provisions of 21/12/2019, the number of employees to be transferred to the superannuation post on various grounds of non-submission of caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes, the number of employees to be transferred to the superannuation post in respect of the post in which they are presently working and the number of employees to be transferred to the superannuation post in respect of the post in which they were working before the termination of their service due to invalidation of caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes before the decision of the Government of the General Administration Department mentioned above, in order to relieve them from the difficulties of administration and from the humanitarian point of view, all the administrative departments shall be temporarily appointed to the superannuation post for a period of 11 months or till the date on which they would have retired, whichever is earlier.
2. General Administration Department The authority to create such number of additional posts has been delegated to the Administrative Department under Government Resolution 30.6.2020. Accordingly, vide reference No. Under Section 4, 74 supernumerary posts have been created and their services have been transferred to the supernumerary post for 11 months. Thereafter, reference No. Under Section 7, 61 supernumerary posts have been created and their services have been transferred to the supernumerary post for 11 months. Reference No. Since the period of transfer of service of these employees from 7th to the higher post ended on 15.05.2022, the matter of extension of their service from 16.05.2022 onwards was under consideration of the Government.
