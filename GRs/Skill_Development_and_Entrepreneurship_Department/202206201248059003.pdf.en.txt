As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in the public interest at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them from Government service prematurely. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 here. The procedures and general instructions regarding service review have been prescribed under the Government Decision dated 10/06/2019. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A and Group-B (Technical/ Intellectual) (Gazetted) cadre officers under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training are being considered for re-employment. 2 here.
