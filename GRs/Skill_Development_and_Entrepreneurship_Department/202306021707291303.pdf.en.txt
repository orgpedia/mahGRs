GOVERNMENT ORDER: The revised figure of posts in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and the institutions and offices under it. (1) It is hereby approved by the Government. In this figure, the ranks of some institutions and offices have been upgraded and some functional posts have been eliminated. Since these functional posts have been abolished and the ranks of the posts in some offices and institutions have been upgraded, it has become expedient to adjust the officers working in those posts to the vacancies in the same organization. Accordingly, options were sought from the additional designated officers through the Directorate. The options received from the officers and the proposal for adjustment accordingly are mentioned in Reference No. (2) and (3) received from the Director. Proposal for adjustment of postings after considering the proposal of adjustment received from the Directorate and the options of the said officers. The Principal Secretary (R.O.
