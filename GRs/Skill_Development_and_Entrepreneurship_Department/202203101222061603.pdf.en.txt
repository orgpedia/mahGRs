The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MAHALT) has been assigned to implement the customized HTE servicing system in all non-agricultural universities, all aided colleges and other aided institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, which also includes the Department of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation. Through this system, the salaries and allowances of the employees of the vocational courses (primary and higher secondary vocational) in the non-government funded technical secondary schools and higher secondary schools under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under this department are paid online.
The system covers 69000 (87%) officers and staff of Higher and Technical Education Department and 9535 (13%) officers and staff of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation Department.
