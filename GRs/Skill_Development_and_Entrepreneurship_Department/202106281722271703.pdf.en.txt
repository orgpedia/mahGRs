GOVERNMENT ORDER: As per the Government decision dated 02.03.2019 of the Department of Finance, provision has been made to sanction 1st/ 2nd benefit/ 3rd benefit of pay structure of the promoted post under the Assured Progress Scheme under the revised service. Officers who have completed 30 years of regular service in the rank of I/ II/ III are being sanctioned the first/ second/ third benefit of Assured Progress Scheme (S23: 67700-208700).
