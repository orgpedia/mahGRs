Preamble: Department of Higher and Technical Education vide Government Notification dated 14.12.2012 has issued the Service Entry Rules for the post of Director of Pre-Vocational Courses, Director of Certificate Courses, Group-C in Government Technical Secondary Schools, Central Government Schools.
The ratio of promotion and nomination has been fixed at 10:90.
As the post of Workshop Attendant (Group-D) has been abolished by the Government, the staff in the post of Workshop Attendant has been converted into Class IV employees. The ratio of 10:90 has been fixed for the posting of workshop attendants to the post of Director in the Pre-Vocational Course. Since the 10% rate is too low, the workshop attendant may be promoted.

It will take many years. These workshop attendants have given in writing the option of going anywhere if they get a posting. The number of workshop attendants in the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department is low across the state and the government has been requested to make the promotion ratio 50:50 as a special case. In this regard, the Government was considering to make changes in the promotion and nomination criteria in the Service Entry Rules dated 14.12.2012 of the Government Notification for the post of Director of Pre-Vocational Courses, Director of Certificate Courses, Group-C in Government Technical Secondary School by promoting the workshop attendants included in Group-D and who are eligible for promotion.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby appoints the Director of Pre-Vocational Courses, Centre, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Director of Certificate Courses, Group-C posts, in accordance with the provisions of Rule No. 10 of the Service Recruitment Rules made under the notification dated 14.12.2012 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education, in the Service Admission Rules dated 14.12.2012 for promotion to the post of Director of Pre-Vocational Courses, Centre, Government Technical Secondary School, Centre, Director of Certificate Courses, Group-C, in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, in addition to the existing technical qualification holders and eligible Group-D (Class IV employees) for promotion.
The ratio of promotion and nomination to the posts of Director of Pre-Vocational Courses, Director of Certificate Courses, Group-C in Government Technical Secondary School, Centre, which fall vacant after the retirement of the concerned employees of the department, shall be 10:90 respectively as per the provisions of the Entry into Service Rules dated 14.12.2012.
(2) The Director of Pre-Vocational Courses, Director of Certificate Courses, Group-C staff who are eligible for promotion to Group-C posts in Government Technical Secondary School, Centre should be made eligible for promotion.
(3) A period of one year shall be granted to the eligible employees for passing the Qualifications and Aptitude Test to be conducted twice a year by the Director, Vocational Education and Training, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
4. Eligible candidates who pass the merit and eligibility test should be promoted in accordance with the cadre-wise reservation.
In case of failure to pass the merit and eligibility test in two occasions, the ratio of promotion and nomination as per the provisions of the Entry into Service Rules dated 14.12.2012 shall be 10:90 respectively.
