Government Order: Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and the posts in the institutions and offices under it
The revised diagram is given in Reference No. of this section. (1) It is hereby approved by the Government. In this figure, the ranks of some institutions and offices have been upgraded and some functional posts have been eliminated. Some of these offices have been closed down.
With the upgradation of posts in the institutions, it has become the order of the day to adjust the officers working in that post to the vacancies in the same cadre. Accordingly, options were sought from the additional designated officers through the Directorate. The options received from the officers and the proposal for adjustment accordingly are mentioned in Reference No. (2) received from the Director. Proposal for adjustment of postings after considering the proposal of adjustment received from the Directorate and the options of the said officers. The Principal Secretary (R& AW), The Civil Service Board, headed by Shri. Presented in the meeting held on 07/09/2022.
In pursuance of the recommendation made by the Civil Services Board, the proposal for grant of postings by adjustment has been approved by the competent authority. Accordingly, the Principal, Vice-Principal, Industrial Training Institute/ Inspector/ Controller of Examinations, Maharashtra State Board of Skill, Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A (Junior) (Technical) (Gazetted) cadre officers who have been appointed additionally are being adjusted in the vacant posts in the same cadre as follows:
