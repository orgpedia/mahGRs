The Proposal:
The government has decided to create a total of 52 posts in the first phase for 13 government technical secondary schools/ centres started under the Nashik Development Programme, 2009. Approved on 30/4/2011. These 52 temporary posts have been extended from 01/03/2012 to 30/09/2018 as per the Government decision issued from time to time. Out of these 52 posts, 28 temporary posts are in 7 Government Technical Secondary Schools/ Centres under Tribal Appointments in Tribal Areas. Since these temporary posts are required to be continued, these temporary posts are to be given. from 01/10/2018.
The matter regarding grant of extension up to 28/02/2019 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The following total 28 temporary posts sanctioned in the first phase for 7 Government Technical Secondary Schools/ Kendras under Tribal Appointments in Tribal Areas launched under Nashik Development Programme, 2009 are being sanctioned with effect from 01/10/2018 to 28/02/2019 subject to the following conditions.

Terms and Conditions:
(1) The said posts shall be utilised for the purpose for which they have been created.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
(3) Care should be taken to obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the review of the sanctioned posts as well as the revised review (including the newly created posts in the figure) during this period.
The expenditure on this post should be met from the grants available under the head of the post of 7 Government Technical Secondary Schools under Tribal Appropriation in the tribal areas, the salary and other incidental allowances sanctioned under this Government Decision for the Central Government Demand Number ZA-1, 2203, Technical Education, 003 Training, (01) Government Technical Secondary School, (01) (05) Creation of posts for various schemes under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, (2203 3513) 01, Salary.
