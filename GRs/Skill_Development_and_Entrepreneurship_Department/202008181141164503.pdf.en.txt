Introduction: The candidates who were to be appointed on compassionate grounds in Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited were given training in telecommunication business in Industrial Training Institutes during the period from 2013 to 2014. On the same lines, the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited had requested to provide training in telecommunication business to the unqualified dependents of the deceased employees who did not receive this training due to various reasons. Against this backdrop, the government was considering to provide training in telecommunication business to 569 unqualified dependents of deceased or permanently disabled employees of Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company from Industrial Training Institutes in 2020-21.
Government Decision: In the above background, it is being approved to impart training under SCVT to the unqualified dependents of deceased employees of Maharashtra State Power Distribution Company Limited in Industrial Training Institutes for the training of the 1 year duration of the vocational course of the Tantri in the vicinity of the electricity circle, where it is possible to provide this training, from September, 2020 as a one-time special case from a total of 27 batches of the Tantri profession, 1 batch per 21 intake capacity (including supernumerary) in 27 such Industrial Training Institutes, subject to the following terms and conditions: 1.
