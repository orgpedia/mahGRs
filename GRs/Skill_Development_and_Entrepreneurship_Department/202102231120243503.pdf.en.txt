Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts notified vide Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityotyaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of posts identification suitable for person with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government Decision dated 7th October, 2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities.
In pursuance of the directions in the said Government Resolution, the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai, was considering the matter of reviewing the list of vacancies in the Government service for the posts in the category of Group-A to Group-D on the establishment and ensuring these posts for the newly disabled category.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, vide notification dated 04.01.2021, has notified the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities for ensuring their status in the State Governments. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Order dated February 2, 2021, regarding the posts in Group A to Group D, which are in the state government establishment, the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, even if the designations of the posts are different.

In pursuance thereof, the Government of India vide its notification dated 04.01.2021 has decided to ensure that the posts in the cadre of" Group-A to Group-D" in the establishment of the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai, for the Group A to Group D cadre, as specified in the Statement-A in the Government Service for Persons with Disabilities, are being ensured.
The said Government Decision, vide notification dated 04.01.2021 of the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi and issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. It is being issued in accordance with the instructions issued under the Government Order dated 02.02.2021.
Pursuant to the notification dated 04.01.2021 issued by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, New Delhi, Government of India, and the Government Order dated 02.02.2021 issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government of Maharashtra, in terms of Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the following Departments/ Offices under the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship are hereby directed to take action at their level to ensure new category of posts for Divyangjan for Group-A to Group-D in their establishments and all types of institutions under their jurisdiction
