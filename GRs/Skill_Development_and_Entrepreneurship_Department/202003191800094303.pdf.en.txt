Government Decision: The temporary posts (attached forms A, B, C, D, E, F and J) working in various Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and 06 Regional Offices under the State Government, Government Industrial Training Institutes, Advanced Vocational Training Methods, Machinery Inspection and Repair and Squad, Basic Training and Allied Instruction Center, Government Industrial Workshops and Similar Centers, Evening Classes for Industrial Workers, Hi-Tech Training Scheme are being approved from 01/03/ 2020 to 31/08/2020 subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
(1) The said posts shall be utilised for the purpose for which they have been created.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
(3) Care should be taken to obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the review of the sanctioned posts as well as the revised review (including the newly created posts in the figure) during this period.
(2) The expenditure for the said post shall be covered under the following heads of accounts and the expenditure in respect of the said post shall be met from the provision approved in the financial year 2020-21.
