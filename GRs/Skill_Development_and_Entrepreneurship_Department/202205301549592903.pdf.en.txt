As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in the public interest at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them prematurely from Government service. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. (1) The procedures and general instructions regarding service review are prescribed under the Government Decision dated 10th June, 2019. Accordingly, for the review of services of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) (Technical/ Technical) Cadre Officers of Maharashtra Education Service in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Reference No. (2) As per the Government decision dated 07.01.2022, a Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary, Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation Department.
Special proposal for review of service of officers of the post of Deputy District Vocational Education and Training Officer/ Assistant Director (Senior), Maharashtra Education Service Group-A (C) (Ata) in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, is being sent to the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide reference No. (3) The following letter has been received. The proposal was submitted to the Departmental Review Committee headed by the Principal Secretary, Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation Department in a circular manner and with the approval of the competent authority.
