As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to retire officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in public interest at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. The procedures and general instructions regarding service review have been prescribed under the Government Decision dated 10/06/2019. Accordingly, for the review of services of officers of the cadre of Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A and Group-B (Technical/ Intellectual) (Gazetted) under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Reference No. 2 here.
