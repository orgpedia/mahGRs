Preamble: Provisional posts under 414 schemes of" Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education" under Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved under the Government Decision at Reference (2). (1) All administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts under the Government decision. As per the Government Decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time, the above Reference No. The said 414 temporary posts have been extended till 28.02.2019. Under the Government Decision (4) in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 30.09.2019. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter dated 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019, extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: ITI-2019/ PR No.37/ Person-3
Government Decision:- Under the scheme of" Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education" under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 414 temporary posts under the scheme in the accompanying Form A have been approved to be continued from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
4) After reviewing the posts, the revised figure will in any case need to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. If the approval of the High-Level Secretary Committee is not taken within this period, then the extension of these posts will not be agreed by the Tribal Development Department.
(5) In case of any question of remuneration of these temporary posts, the responsibility shall be of the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the approved grants under the following heads during the financial year 2019-20.
" Demand No. T-5, Accounts Head 2230 Labour and Employment, Schemes under Five Year Plans, Tribal Area Sub Plan, 03 Training, 796 Tribal Area Sub Plan, (01)- Schemes under Tribal Area Appropriation, (01)- Schemes under State Schemes, (01) Industrial Training Institute, Extension of Industrial Training Institutes in Tribal Areas (22300764) The Director (Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall ensure that the Budget Estimates, Revised Estimates and final revised grant proposals under the said heads are submitted within the stipulated time.
