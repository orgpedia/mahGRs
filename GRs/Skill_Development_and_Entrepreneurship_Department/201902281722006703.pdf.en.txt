The Proposal:
The government has decided to create a total of 52 posts in the first phase for 13 government technical secondary schools/ centres started under the Nashik Development Programme, 2009.
Approved on 30/4/2011. These 52 temporary posts have been extended from 01/03/2012 to 28/02/2019 as per the Government decision issued from time to time. Out of these 52 posts, 28 temporary posts are in 7 Government Technical Secondary Schools/ Centres under Tribal Appointments in Tribal Areas. Since these temporary posts need to be continued, these temporary posts should be given. from 01/03/2019.
The matter regarding grant of extension up to 30/09/2019 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The following total 28 temporary posts sanctioned in the first phase for 7 Government Technical Secondary Schools/ Kendras under Tribal Appointments in Tribal Areas launched under Nashik Development Programme, 2009 are being sanctioned with effect from 01/03/2019 to 30/09/2019 subject to the following conditions.
