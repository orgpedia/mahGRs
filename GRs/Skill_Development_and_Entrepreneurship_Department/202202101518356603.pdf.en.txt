Preamble: The State Government is implementing the Craftsman Training Scheme with the objectives of providing skilled manpower required for various works in the industrial sector in the State and to empower the youth with job oriented training and to enable them to become self-employed in manufacturing and other service sectors by providing skill training, to enhance the quality, quality and production of industrial establishments by providing systematic training to the workers. Under the scheme of Center of Excellence under the World Bank, as well as under the concept of Public-Private Partnership, the status of Industrial Training Institutes has been successfully implemented. In addition to these schemes, all the industrial training institutes run apprenticeship schemes, public service centres, training for the needy, universalization of tribal employment oriented vocational education. It is necessary to recognize and encourage the institutions that are implementing the above schemes and take its example and move towards excellence by competing well with all other institutions. For this purpose, the decision to award the" Best Industrial Training Institute" to the institutions was taken by the relevant government decision. 17.02.2018. Similarly, Rs. 1.00 lakh for 5 categories Rs. 5.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the decision. As on 17.02.2018, the following corrections are being made and Government Corrigendum is being issued:
GOVERNMENT CREDIT: Considering the background mentioned in the Preamble, 5 categories of candidates from the State will be selected for the" Best Industrial Training Institute Award" for Government and Private Industrial Training Institutes in the State as well as for the Central Government-sponsored Kaushalacharya Award.
