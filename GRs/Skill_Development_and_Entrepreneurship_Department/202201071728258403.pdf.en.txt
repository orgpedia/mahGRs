NO. 03/ Entrepreneurship/ Dated 04.01.2021 Preamble: To increase the representation of women in the field of start-ups and entrepreneurship and to provide necessary support to women to become" entrepreneurs," the Maharashtra State Innovation Society has decided to set up a" Women Entrepreneurship Cell" for women. The decision of the Government No. 3 has been approved. A partial change in the composition of the" Women Entrepreneurship Cell" as well as the" Executive and Controlling Committee" of this cell was under consideration of the government.
Government's stand:
In order to increase the representation of women in the field of start-ups and entrepreneurship and to provide necessary support to women to become entrepreneurs, the revised composition of the" Women Entrepreneurship Cell" and the" Executive and Controlling" Committee is being done as follows.
The Women's Entrepreneurship Cell:
The structure of the" Women Entrepreneurship Cell" set up under the Maharashtra State Innovation Society will be as follows.
