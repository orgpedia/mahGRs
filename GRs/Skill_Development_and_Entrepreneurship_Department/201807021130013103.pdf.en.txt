The Proposal:
The government has decided to create a total of 52 posts in the first phase for 13 government technical secondary schools/ centres started under the Nashik Development Programme, 2009.
Approved on 30/4/2011. These 52 temporary posts have been extended from 01/03/2012 to 28/02/2018 as per the Government decision issued from time to time. Out of these 52 posts, 24 temporary posts are in Non-Tribal (General) Government Technical Secondary School/ Centre. Since these temporary posts are required to be continued, the matter regarding grant of extension of these temporary posts from 01/06/2018 to 30/09/2018 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. The following total 24 temporary posts sanctioned in the first phase for 6 Government Technical Secondary Schools/ Kendras started under Nashik Development Programme, 2009 are being sanctioned with effect from 01/06/2018 to 30/09/2018 subject to the following conditions.

Terms and Conditions:
(1) The said posts shall be utilised for the purpose for which they have been created.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
(3) Care should be taken to obtain the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for the review of the sanctioned posts as well as the revised review (including the newly created posts in the figure) during this period.
The expenditure on this post should be met from the grant available under the head of non-tribal (general) sector 6 Government Technical Secondary School, Salary and other collateral allowances Demand No.ZA-1, 2203, Technical Education, 003 Training, (01) Government Technical Secondary School, (01) (05) Creation of posts for various schemes under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, (22033513) 01, Salary.
