Ratan Tata Ratan Tata Maharashtra State Skill University Act, 2021 (State Skill Mahasabha of 2021) In exercise of the powers conferred by section 14, sub-section (2) of the University Act, 2021, the Government of Maharashtra hereby appoints the Registrar to the office of the Registrar in the Schedule appended to this Act.
Specifying qualifications and experience for the purpose.

| A. Required Qualifications |
| 1.                         | the Registrar         | A) Minimum 55% marks in the Master's degree examination of any statutory university or its equivalent in Category B of the 7 point standard of the University Grants Commission. B) Relaxation - I) Condition of minimum 55% marks prescribed in the Master's degree examination is relaxed by 5% for candidates who are already working in the university system. II) Candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes / Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes may be exempted by 5% from the condition of minimum 55% marks prescribed in the Master's degree examination and the limit shall be 50. III) Minimum 55% prescribed in the Master's degree examination for candidates who have held a PhD degree before 19th September, 1991. |
