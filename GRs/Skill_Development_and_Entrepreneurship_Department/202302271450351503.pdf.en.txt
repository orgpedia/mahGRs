The Proposal:
Under the scheme titled 10-Contractual Services for the year 2022-23 of the Department of Skills, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation, the monthly salary of constables and drivers employed by external agencies, retired contractual employees and clerks and typists hired by external agencies is paid. For the financial year 2022-23, under the target of 10-contractual service, Rs. A budget of Rs. 25.00 lakh was sanctioned for the project. As of December 2020, Rs. 20.00 lakh has been disbursed and accordingly 99.99 per cent of the funds disbursed have been spent. Further, to clear the pending payments under the 10-contractual target till December, 2022, Rs. A sum of Rs. 6.96 crore had been earmarked for the purpose. Accordingly, Rs. 6.96 lakh available under this sub-heading for payment of Rs. 5.00 lakh and Rs. The Finance Department was requested to provide additional funds of Rs. 1.96 lakh. As per the instructions of the Finance Department, during the financial year 2022-23, the Department was contemplating to provide funds through redeployment/ reallocation from the savings in other mandatory schemes of the Department for payment of pending dues under the target of 10-contract service under head 2251 0516.
