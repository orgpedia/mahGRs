Out of the 14,963 sanctioned posts, 9896 are permanent and 3,323 are temporary posts in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and 6 regional offices under it, Government Industrial Training Institute, Advanced Vocational Training System, Machine Repair and Maintenance Team, Basic Training and Allied Information Center, Government Industrial Workshop and Similar Center, Evening Class for Industrial Workers, Hi-Tech Training Scheme. Out of these, 1744 non-plan temporary posts have been sanctioned under the review of posts. (1) All administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts under the Government decision. Further, as per the Government Decision issued from time to time by the Finance Department, the above Reference No. The said 1744 temporary posts have been extended up to 28.02.2019. Under the Government Decision (4) in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 30.09.2019. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter dated 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019, extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.

Government Decision:- Under this Government Decision, the extension of 1744 non-plan temporary posts as shown in Annexure-I in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and 06 Regional Offices under the Directorate, Government Industrial Training Institute, Advanced Vocational Training Systems, Machine Repair and Maintenance Team, Basic Training and Allied Information Center, Government Industrial Workshop and Similar Center, Evening Classes for Industrial Workers, Hi-Tech Training Scheme, is being approved under this Government Decision from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 subject to the following conditions. The category-wise details of the posts are given in Form-1.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed time.
4) The revised pattern after reviewing the posts needs to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. In case of non-payment of salary, the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai will be responsible for the same. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the grants to be sanctioned under the following heads during the financial year 2019-20.
