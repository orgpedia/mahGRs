Introduction: The Maharashtra State Board of Vocational Training (presently known as Maharashtra State Skill Development Board) was established in 1986 under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training to meet the need for vocational education for students who have dropped out of secondary education due to the growth of industrialization, changing technology, social and economic changes and to provide short-term vocational courses for students taking special courses (add-on courses) to meet their needs for employment and self-employment. Business oriented courses are run by this board to achieve the goal of employment and self-employment.
Due to the increase in industrialization, changing technology, social and economic changes, etc., the curriculum of the Board has been improved and increased over time.

Accordingly, from the year 2021-22, it was under the consideration of the Government to grant approval to start business courses on self-financing basis to new institutions/ batches of students pursuing courses in the Intellectual Group of Maharashtra State Skill Development Board.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Skill Development Board has recommended that the Maharashtra State Skill Development Board (MSSDB) should approve the introduction of Intellectual Group Courses in private educational institutions that are willing to implement the courses of the Maharashtra State Skill Development Board. The details of these institutions have been attached in the accompanying Form' A' and the Maharashtra State Skill Development Board has decided to introduce courses in the Intellectual Group in the new institutions from 2021-22.
More batches are being approved to be started on self-financing basis subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
The institutions approved to implement the courses of Maharashtra State Skill Development Board will have to comply with the orders issued from time to time by the Government, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State Skill Development Board and the concerned offices under the Directorate.
Students should be admitted to the place where the institutions have been approved to run the courses of the Maharashtra State Skill Development Board. Also, the approval of the same place will be considered, if there is a change in the place, the approval will be cancelled.
