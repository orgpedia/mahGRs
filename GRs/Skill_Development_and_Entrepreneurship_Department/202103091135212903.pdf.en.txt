Preamble: The registration of Maharashtra State Innovation Society under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and the consequential Memorandum of Association (MoA) has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 03/07/2017 at No. Further, the Maharashtra State Innovation Startup Policy, 2018 has been approved under the Government Resolution dated 05/02/2018 at Reference No.5 with the objective of promoting enterprises based on innovative ideas to boost the industrial and economic development of the State and to address the problem of unemployment in the country.
The Maharashtra State Innovation Startup Policy, 2018 has a five-year period from 2018 to 2023, under which 15 incubators will be developed in the next five years to attract investment of Rs 5,000 crore through angel funds and seed funds. It also aims to create 5,00,000 employment/ self-employment opportunities by facilitating 10,000 start-ups. Under this policy, innovative clusters are being developed for start-ups, incubators (including Virtual Incubators), three world-class Accelerators, Scalers and Start-up Parks are being developed in collaboration with higher educational institutions, R& D institutions and industry to provide various services and facilities to start-ups.
