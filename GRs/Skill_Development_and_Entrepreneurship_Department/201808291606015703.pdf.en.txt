The following is the Preamble of the letter No. 07/ CTS/ MSCVT/ 2018/64 dated 30/01/2018:
The Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training (MSCVT) has approved the modalities for affiliation of Vocational Training Institutes and Skill Assessment Agencies for imparting skill training programmes in the state. The procedure in this Government decision, in paragraph 11, provides for verification of infrastructure and training facilities by the supervising agency of the applicant desirous of being affiliated to Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training as Vocational Training Institute/ Skill Development Institute.
Accordingly, the government was considering granting the status of" Inspection Agency" to Government Industrial Training Institutes, Government Technical Schools, Government Technological and Government Engineering Colleges.
Government's decision:
All the Government Industrial Training Institutes, Government Technical Schools, Government Technological and Government Engineering Colleges in the State are being accorded the status of" Inspection Agency" under this Government decision subject to the following terms and conditions.
Government Industrial Training Institutes, Government Technical Schools, Government Technological and Government Engineering Colleges will have to register on the official website of Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training.

(ii) After selecting the inspection agency for physical inspection of the Vocational Training Institute/ Skill Testing Institute through computer system, the concerned institution will be required to give online acceptance of inspection within 48 hours. If the opportunity for inspection is denied or consent is not shown within 48 hours, another institution will be selected for inspection.
(iii) The inspection agency shall also be required to fix the date of inspection while submitting online inspection acknowledgement through computer system. A 30-day grace period is required. If it is not possible to carry out the inspection during the period for unavoidable reasons, the institution concerned may refuse to accept the inspection.
The monitoring agency will verify the information/ details mentioned by the applicant in the online application and the documents uploaded. Further, after physically inspecting the infrastructure and training facilities available with the applicant for Vocational Training/ Skill Test as per the standards prescribed by the Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training, the inspecting agencies shall submit the report along with photo and feedback to the Council online through computer system within 48 hours from the date of inspection.
v. A remuneration of at least Rs.5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousand only) shall be admissible to the Inspecting Institution, however, if the number of businesses is more than one, the remuneration shall be Rs.3,000/- (Rupees Three Thousand only) per business. The Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training (MSVTC) will have the power to make changes in the remuneration.
The expenditure on the honorarium of the inspection institutes will be met from the funds deposited in the bank account of the Maharashtra State Vocational Training Council.
This Government decision is being issued with the consent received under the Informal Reference No. 01/ Tanshi-4 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education, dated 10/04/2018.
