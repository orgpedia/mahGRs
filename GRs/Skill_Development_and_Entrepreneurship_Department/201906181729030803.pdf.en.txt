Preamble: The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retired Additional Chief Secretary, Government of India to revise the pay scales of State Government and other eligible employees, taking into consideration the decisions of the Central Government on the recommendations of the 7th Central Pay Commission. The Government has decided to accept the recommendations of the Committee. The government has taken 3 decisions. Accordingly, in order to implement the revised pay structure for teachers and non-teaching staff in government institutions along with other state government employees with effect from January 1, 2016, the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2018 have been amended vide Reference No. 4 have been announced.
