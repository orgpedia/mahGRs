Preamble: To promote the industrial and economic development of the State and the country
In order to encourage enterprises based on innovative resolutions to address the problem of unemployment, the Department has approved to organize" Business Plan Competition" in the state under the Government Decision dated 21/02/2019 to develop the start-up culture in the state in accordance with the" Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018" approved under the Government Decision dated 05/02/2018. The matter of issuing guidelines regarding the conduct of this competition was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In order to develop the start-up culture in the state, the Department has approved to organize" Business Plan Competition" in the state under the Government Decision dated 21/02/2019.
