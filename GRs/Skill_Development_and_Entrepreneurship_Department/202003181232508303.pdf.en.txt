Under the Combined Peripheral Training Programme-2019 (CPTP-5& 6), Assistant Director in Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Mechanism, 2 in Group-A Cadre and 3 in Group-B Cadre, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Officer have been inducted.
