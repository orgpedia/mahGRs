Increase in the remuneration of the second and third-term contractual craft directors in the Government Industrial Training Institutes
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Skills, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation
Government Resolution No: ITI-3317/ P.S.No.210/ Person-3
Second Floor, Madame Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: July 4, 2021.
Read:- 1) Government Resolution No. ITI-2010/ No. 64/ Vyashi-3, D. 23.08.2010
The Proposal:
For the second and third shifts in the Government Industrial Training Institutes in the Municipal Corporation area of the state and the second shift in the remaining Industrial Training Institutes to start from the August 2010 session, a total of 1500 teaching posts on contract (Rs. 15000/- per month) and Rs. 2480.96 lakh under various heads. Approval has been granted by the Government Decision dated 23/08/2010.
However, in pursuance of the repeated representations made by the said Contract Director to the Government regarding the increase in remuneration, the Cabinet in its meeting held on 16.05.2023 has decided as under:
At present, the remuneration of 297 Directors working in Industrial Training Institutes will be increased from Rs. 15000/- to Rs. 25000/- per month.
Every year, the work of the employees should be reviewed and the department should take appropriate action to continue the service.
3. From time to time, the demand for regularization in government service is made by the contractual art director working in the Government Industrial Training Institute. A committee under the chairmanship of the Minister (Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation) should be formed and appropriate decisions should be taken.
The decision was taken by the cabinet of the government.

Government Resolution No: ITI-2020/ P.S. No.210/ Person-3
Government's decision:
As per the decision taken in the Cabinet meeting as above: 1. Remuneration of Directors working on contract basis is being increased from Rs.15,000/-to Rs.25,000/-per month. The increase in remuneration is effective from 01.04.2023.
Every year, the performance of the employees should be reviewed and the process of continuing the service should be done by the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai.
3. The demand for regularization in Government service is made from time to time by the Contractual Director in the Government Industrial Training Institute in the Cabinet meeting. The government decision on the formation of the committee under the chairmanship of the Minister (Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation) Department will be issued separately.
The expenditure on honorarium of these employees should be covered under the head" Expansion of Industrial Training Institutes for Enhancing Access to Accountancy (22309209) (Programme) (10) Contractual Services."
