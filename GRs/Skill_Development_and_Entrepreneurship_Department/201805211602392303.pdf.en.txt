The Proposal:
The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training (DVET) has initiated a detailed investigation into the irregularities and financial corruption in the procurement of SSSC Lathe All Geard Stock-1500 in the financial year 2011-12, 2012-13 and 2013-14 and in the purchase of SSSC Lathe All Geard Stock-1500 in the year 2013-14. 1 here. In pursuance of the Government decision dated 29.11.2017, an Inquiry Committee under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Industries), Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry, Mumbai has been constituted and the Committee has been asked to submit its report within two months. However, the Member Secretary of the said Committee and Deputy Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training vide letter dated 16.1.2018 in Reference No.2 had requested the Government for extension of time for the functioning of the said Committee. According to reference no. 3 here. The Committee was given extension of two months vide Government Resolution dated 17.02.2018. Now, the Member Secretary of the said Committee and the Deputy Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training have issued a circular in reference no. vide letter dated 31.03.2018

Government's decision:
The Government has now decided to conduct a detailed inquiry into the administrative and financial irregularities and financial corruption in the procurement of SSSC Lathe All Geard Stock-1500 in the financial year 2011-12, 2012 and 2013-14 in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and in the case of procurement of SSSC Lathe All Geard Stock-1500 on tender in 2013-14. The Committee constituted under the Government Order dated 29.11.2017 is being given an extension of two months from the date of this order to conduct an inquiry in this regard and submit a complete report to the Government.
