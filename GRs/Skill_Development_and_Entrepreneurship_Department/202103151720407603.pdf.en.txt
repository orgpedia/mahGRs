The Chairperson of the Scrutiny Committee may invite an expert having in-depth knowledge and experience in the field of skill development to the meeting of the Committee as an invited member. However, an invited member of the Scrutiny Committee shall not have the right to vote.
The term of office of the non-official members of the said Committee shall be two years from the date of issue of this Government Resolution.
The Scrutiny Committee shall look after the following: 1. All proposals received by the Government shall be examined in accordance with the guidelines prescribed by the Government. The matter should be examined in accordance with the matters mentioned in paragraph 6 of the guidelines for the establishment of self-financing universities issued by the Government.
The Committee may request other information from the Sponsoring Board in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 7 (7.4) of the Guidelines.
3. The Committee may suggest changes in the Detailed Project Report, if any, and the concerned Sponsor Board may consider and report the same to the concerned Sponsor Board for compliance within a specified period.
(4) The Committee shall examine the application and report to the Chairman.
It will be submitted to the government within two months.
5. Prepare a summary of the detailed project report.
