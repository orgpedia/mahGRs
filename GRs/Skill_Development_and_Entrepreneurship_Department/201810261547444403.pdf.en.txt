As per the proposal submitted to the Government vide letter (5), the extension of temporary posts from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision:- Under this Government Decision, 417 temporary posts under the schemes in the accompanying Form A approved for the opening of 2 new O.P. Institutes, Mandvi and Nehru Nagar (Kurla) in Brihanmumbai and 2 second/ third shift in the existing Industrial Training Institute in 43 minority dominated areas of the State under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai are to be continued from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
4) The revised pattern after reviewing the posts needs to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. In case of non-payment of salary, the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai will be responsible for the same. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the following heads:
" Minority Development Department- Demand No. ZE-1, 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 200-Other Programmes, Schemes under State Schemes, (00) (16)-Assistant Grants for starting second and third shifts in existing Industrial Training Institutes for Minority Students (2235 A 534)
This Government decision is in accordance with the Informal Reference No. 22/2018/ 8 of the Department of Minority Development, Govt. 26th October, 2018 and issued by the Finance Department, pursuant to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide G.O. No.: 2016/ P.S. No. 8/16/ A.P.C., dated 7th September, 2018.
