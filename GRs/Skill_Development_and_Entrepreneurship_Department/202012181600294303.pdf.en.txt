The Proposal:
The Skill Acquisition and Knowledge Awareness for Livelihood Project (SANKALP) is being implemented by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India under the National Skill Development Mission (NSDM) to strengthen the institutional mechanism at the national and state level, create a pool of quality trainers, centralize all skill training programmes being implemented at the state level, create an effective mechanism for monitoring and evaluation of skill development training programmes, provide opportunities for skill training for socially backward classes, create skilled manpower required for the manufacturing sector and supplement the" Make in India" concept. The duration of the project is 6 years and the Central and State Governments share in the project cost is 60: 40. Administrative approval has been given to implement this campaign in the state under the decision of the Government No. 4.
