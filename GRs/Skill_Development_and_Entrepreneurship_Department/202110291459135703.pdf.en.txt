Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Government Decision No. No. 06/2020/ P.S.2, dated 30th January, 2020 Government Decision: In the cadre of Assistant Commissioner, Group-A and Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Officer, Group-B, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has created 5 supernumerary posts through Government Decision dated 30th January, 2020 for 5 candidates who were selected on the basis of the final results of State Service Examination-1017 and State Service Examination-2018 and who underwent training in Combined Probationary Training Programme-5 and 6.
However, the results of the examination of Assistant Commissioner, Group-A and Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Officer, Group-B Trainee Officers in CPTP-5 and 6 for Session 3 and Session 4 have not been declared yet. It is likely that after the declaration of the result, it will take some time to determine the service quality of the trainee concerned. At present, the divisional cadre of the post has not been allocated yet, and since all these matters are likely to take some time, the government is approving the extension of the 5 supernumerary posts created for the trainees under this purview up to regular posting.
