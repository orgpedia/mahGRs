Preamble: The Government has decided to start the second and third shifts in Government Industrial Training Institutes and the second shift in the remaining Industrial Training Institutes in the Municipal Corporation areas of the State from the August 2010 session. Out of 334 Contractual Directors appointed as on 23/8/2010, a proposal to induct 326 Directors in regular service excluding 8 Directors who have passed away and 5 Directors who have resigned was placed in the Cabinet Meeting held on 17.01.2018. The Cabinet of Ministers discussed the proposal to induct 326 contractual Directors of Government Industrial Training Institutes under the Department of Skill Development into regular government service. After taking into consideration the various judgements of the Supreme Court and considering the number of contractual employees under other administrative departments.
Considering this, it has been decided to approve the appointment of a cabinet sub-committee to study the matter thoroughly and submit a report on the regularization of contractual office holders in the state. Accordingly, the matter of forming a cabinet sub-committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government has decided to recruit 326 Contractual Directors of Government Industrial Training Institutes (GITIs) under the Skill Development Department. Taking into consideration the various judgments of the Supreme Court
