Government Supplementary Paper:
In order to encourage and increase the recruitment of apprentices under the Apprenticeship Training Scheme in Government, Semi-Government and Private establishments, the Government has approved the" Maharashtra Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme" as a supplement to the Apprenticeship Training Scheme.
Under the Maharashtra Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme, the apprentices will be given scholarships in the form of Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) through MahaDBT. This scholarship will be given in addition to the scholarship provided under the Apprenticeship Training Scheme with an additional 75% or less of Rs.5000/-. Also, those candidates who will not be able to avail the benefit of education allowance under the National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme, will be paid an education allowance ofâ‚¹ 5000/- directly under the Maharashtra Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme. For proper implementation of the scheme, a cell has been set up at the Directorate level for which 5% of the funds are being approved to be spent under the head 2230A719.
The approval is being given subject to the following conditions: 1. The exact details of all the beneficiaries and the disbursement of the scholarship in Direct Beneficiary Transfer (Direct Bank Account).
The scheme should be implemented on Maha DBT for the next financial year and planned action should be taken for it.
The pending scholarship for the financial year 2022-2023 should be disbursed offline.
