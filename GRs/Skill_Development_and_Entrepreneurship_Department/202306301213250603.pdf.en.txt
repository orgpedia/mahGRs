(2) Since the transfers of the above officers are administrative, the allowances for the period of appointment and transfer as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Appointment to Service and Payment of Salaries during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981 shall be admissible to them as per the rules of the Government in force.
3. The transferred officers should immediately report to the post of the transferor. The Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai, should submit the information to the government immediately.
4. No leave shall be granted to the concerned officer for any reason without prior permission of the Government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave applications of the officers transferred by the parent office should be accepted. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities by post to the original address.
If the concerned officer does not appear in the post of transfer immediately as per the transfer order or tries to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against him as this action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
