Government's decision:
As per the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries headed by the Hon'ble Chief Secretary, a total of 945 posts in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System have been notified under the aforesaid reference No. 1 It has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 08.09.2006. Out of the total 945 posts, 189 are temporary. These 189 temporary posts are being filled up under reference No. 2 The Government hereby approve the continuation from 01.0902020 to 28.02.2021. Now, as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Order dated 15.02.2021 of the Finance Department, No. 3, a total of 189 temporary posts as indicated in Annexure 1 to 4 in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Mechanism have been created with effect from 01.03.2021. Government approval is being given to continue the same for the period 31.08.2021 (both days inclusive).
The expenditure for the continuation of the above posts shall be met from the budgetary provision of that financial year under the head of the following relevant accounts in Demand No.ZA-1: 1) 2230, Labour and Employment 02, Employment
001 Management and Administration.
