NO.: ESE-2010/ PROCESSOR.29/ PRESSOR.2 dated 6.2.2012 Memorandum:
The procedure has been prescribed under the Government Decision No. 1 in the context of General Administration Department for reviewing the eligibility-ineligibility of the Deputy Commissioner (Group-A), Assistant Commissioner (Group-A) and Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Officer (Group-B) officers of the system of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Commissioner to continue in service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after 30 years of qualifying service.
Under the order at Reference No. 2, a Divisional Review Committee has been constituted to review the cadre of Deputy Commissioner (Group-A), Assistant Commissioner (Group-A) and Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Officer (Group-B) in the subordinate commissionerate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department.
On the recommendation of the Divisional Review Committee, the following officers of the cadre of Deputy Commissioner (Group-A), Assistant Commissioner (Group-A) and Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Officer (Group-B) are being approved to continue their service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
