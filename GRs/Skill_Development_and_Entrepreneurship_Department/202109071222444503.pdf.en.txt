Preamble: Land has been acquired for Industrial Training Institute, Shirur Kasar, G.Beed and the land for Group No. 3.00 hectares of land out of 259. The transfer and possession of the said plot at Shirur Kasar, G. Beed has been completed for the Institute on 7/12. Industrial Training Institute, Shirur Kasar, G. Beed, established in 2006-07, has 11 batches sanctioned for 08 trades viz., Mechanical Automotive, Electrical Instrumentation, Jodari, Sandhata, Telecommunication, Electronics Mechanic, Coppa and Swing Technology. Out of 11 business units sanctioned for lack of space in the institute, only 05 units are functioning. The Industrial Training Institute, Shirur Kasar needs a building urgently so that all units can get affiliation and start business. The total area required as per the requirement of the organization is 1424 square meters. According to the type plan TEDU-5219, G+ 1, the construction area will be 1394.23 square meters, which will be enough for the institution.
