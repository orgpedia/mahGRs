The Proposal:
There is a need to train the staff of the state to become dynamic administration by increasing efficiency at all levels in the state government service. Training at all levels is necessary to meet the challenges arising out of economic, social and technological changes and to achieve the necessary flexibility for change in governance.
Reference No. As per the proposal submitted by the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, the Regional Office, Pune, was under the consideration of the Government to approve the training program of the Industrial Training Institute of its division at IGTR Aurangabad and the expenditure to be incurred for it.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the above, the Regional Office under the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Pune is being given approval to conduct the training programme of the Director of Industrial Training at IGTR Aurangabad under the following terms and conditions.
