Preamble: MoU between the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India and Dassault Skill Academy, France to develop global training and educational initiatives in the country. The MoU was signed on 3rd July, 2019. As per the MoU, the Industrial Training Institute, Nagpur will start three batches of business courses in Aeronautical Structure and Equipment Fitter from August 2019. 3 has been approved by the government. The training will be offered on a self-financed basis for a period of 3 years. For the first 3 years, 3 expert trainers were provided by Dassault Aviation, France.
Since the three-year period for self-financed training in the business course of Dassault Aviation, France Aeronautical Structure and Equipment Fitter was over, the Government was considering to conduct the training through the Government Industrial Training Institute, Nagpur and sanction the posts of 3 craft directors for the course.
Government's decision:
As per the MoU signed by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India with Dassault Aviation, France, Government Industrial Training Institute, Nagpur, 3 batches of business courses on Aeronautical Structure and Equipment Fitter are being approved under this decision.

For 03 batches of Aeronautical Structure and Equipment Fitter Vocational Courses at Industrial Training Institute, Nagpur, 3 posts of 1 Sculptor each are being sanctioned.
In order to make available the required manpower for 03 batches as per DGT New Delhi standards for the said course at Industrial Training Institute, Nagpur, it has been decided to start Skill Development Centres at Gadchiroli and Gondia under Skill Development Programme for the youth of the districts affected by militancy. Out of the total 48 posts sanctioned under the Government Decision, a total of 3 posts of Director of Craft are being approved to be transferred to the Aeronautical Structure and Equipment Fitter course.
Reference No. 2. As per the decision of the Government of India, the period of 3 years for the self-financed Aeronautical Structure and Equipment Fitter course through the establishment of Dassault Aviation, France has expired, the Government Institute of Civil Aviation, Nagpur is being allowed to run the course by charging the regular training fee permissible as per the rules.
