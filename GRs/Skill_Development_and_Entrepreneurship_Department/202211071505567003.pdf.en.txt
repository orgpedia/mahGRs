The Proposal:
A proposal is under consideration to make changes in the various schemes and activities being implemented in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System under the Commissionerate as per the time, as well as to implement some new schemes or activities in the system, considering the current situation, some schemes will be closed or transferred to other departments. A committee has been formed at the district level for the purpose. After considering the work done by this study group on the scheme of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Employment Fair and the difficulties faced in doing this work and the scheme, it has been recommended to implement the scheme of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Employment Fair in a revised form through a letter dated 18.10.2022. In order to achieve the objective of providing maximum employment to the local unemployed residents of the state, the vacancies from various entrepreneurs/ establishments should be notified and these fairs should be organized in collaboration with the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and the vacancies related to apprenticeship should be made available in this fair. Various Government Corporations, Nationalized/ Private Banks/ Financial Institutions, Skill Development, Employment and Self Employment Departments of the Government, etc., providing loans to the candidates seeking self-employment, setting up of stalls for providing guidance/ information to the candidates, besides providing guidance to the candidates regarding employment, self-employment, skill development training, preparation of bio-data, future employment opportunities, preparation of interview etc.
