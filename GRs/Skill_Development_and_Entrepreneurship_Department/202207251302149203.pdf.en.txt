Budget 2022-23 under Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission
The Rs. A sum of Rs. 224.00 crore has been budgeted and the request for disbursement of this fund has been made by Maharashtra State Skill Development Society vide letter No.8.
2. In pursuance of the above request, under the Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission, budgeted for the financial year 2022-23, an amount of Rs. 224.90 crore (Rs. 47.22 crore (Rupees Forty Seven Crore Twenty Two Lakh only) is being approved for disbursement and expenditure.
The Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship shall draw the funds allocated for the scheme by the State Government from the treasury and make the same available for expenditure to the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society. For this, Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Navi Mumbai is being declared as" Controlling Officer" and Assistant Director (Accounts) Konkan Bhawan is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
