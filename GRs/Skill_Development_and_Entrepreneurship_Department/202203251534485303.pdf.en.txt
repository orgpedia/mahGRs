The Proposal:
Department of Forest and Wildlife No. As per the notification dated 01.08.2014, the newly created Palghar district has been created by bifurcating Thane district. The District Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Center, Palghar is functioning under the Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Maharashtra State. The office implements various schemes of the department such as Skill Training Programmes, Job Fairs, Establishment Search, New Establishment Registration, Employment Promotion Programmes, Establishment Inspection etc.
Reference No. Issue No. 3. The manpower for the newly created office of District Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Centre, Palghar in Palghar district has been sanctioned. Therefore, the matter of declaring the Palghar office as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Controller was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: The above Reference No. of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
