Government Decisions: To promote industrial and economic development of the State and the country.
The Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018 has been approved to promote enterprises based on innovative resolutions to address the problem of unemployment. In line with this policy, approval is being given to implement the" Hirkani-Nav Udyojak Maharashtrachi" scheme in the state to encourage women self-help groups to become entrepreneurs through start-ups.
1. the purpose of the program
The main objective of the scheme is to provide necessary guidance to women SHGs to promote their innovative ideas, provide forums at block and district level and provide financial assistance to create infrastructure for excellent innovative ideas.
Beneficiaries of the scheme
National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM) and National Urban Livelihood Mission (NULM)
The beneficiaries of the scheme will be women Self Help Groups (SHGs) established at least one year ago under the Economic Development Corporation (MAVIM) and following the five-point strategy of the National Livelihood Mission.
In addition to this, some other schemes of the State or Central Government through the State and other departments of the Central Government.
Women Self-Help Groups formed at least one year ago and adhering to the five-point formula
The District Innovation Council can take a decision on inclusion.
(3) The implementation system
Implementation of the scheme at district level Dated 14/08/2014
It will be done through the District Innovation Council formed under the decision of the government of the day.
Taluk level committees will be formed in all taluks.

4. the plan of action
Under the scheme, women self-help groups will be provided a platform at the taluka level to showcase their innovative ideas.
2) The ideas will be guided at the taluka level to convert them into industry and the selected ideas will be selected for presentation at the taluka forum within a limit of 100.
3) These concepts will be examined by the Taluk Level Committee.
4) The best innovative ideas will be selected in a limit of 10 from the innovative ideas that have been selected after the examination of the Taluk Level Committee.
Each project will be funded to the extent of Rs.50,000/- to create the necessary infrastructure for realization of the selected best ideas.
6) Innovative ideas selected at the Taluka level will be eligible for presentation at the district level forum and will be given an opportunity to present at the district level.
Successful entrepreneurs, businessmen, investors, banks, etc. of the district will be invited to the district level forum.
8) Innovative ideas at the district level forum will be judged by the above invitees and the District Innovation Council.
9) The best innovative ideas will be selected in a limit of 10 from the innovative ideas that are selected after the test.
