As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to retire officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in public interest at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 here. The Government Decision dated 10/06/2019 prescribes the procedure and general instructions regarding service review. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Education Service under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Group A and B (Technical/ Intellectual) (Gazetted) Cadre, is requested to review the services of the officers under Reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 07/01/2022, a Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of IAS/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship or Innovation.
The Departmental Review Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship or Innovation has approved the proposal to review the services of Manager, Industrial Training Institute/ Assistant Director (Technical), Maharashtra Education Service, Group-B (Technical) (Gazetted) cadre officers at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. Presented in the meeting dated 05/05/2022. As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the officers who have been found eligible to continue in service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
