Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited. In pursuance of the request made for appointment of Shri D.L.Thakare, Assistant Director (Pay Scale 9300-34800, Grade Pay 5000) on deputation in equivalent post, Shri D.L.Thakare, Assistant Director is being appointed on deputation in equivalent post in the establishment of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation.
The tenure of the appointment will be one year. It is also informed that whenever the services of Mr. D. L. Thackeray, Assistant Director, are required by the Department before the expiry of his period of deputation, the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation shall be required to relieve him forthwith.
The terms and conditions regarding the deputation of Mr. D. L. Thackeray, Assistant Director in the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation should be fixed and informed to this department.
These orders are being issued with the concurrence of the Public Works Department vide its informal reference No.2553/ dated 25.07.2019.
