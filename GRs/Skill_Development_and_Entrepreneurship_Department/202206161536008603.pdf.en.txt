Industrial Training Institutes play a major role in creating world-class and business-oriented, skilled manpower in accordance with the demands of the establishment. Industrialization is increasing rapidly in the state due to economic liberalization. With the changing process of industrial technology production, the need for skilled manpower is increasing in the industries. It is also necessary to include new courses as per the demand of industrial establishments based on new technology.
2. Government Industrial Training Institute, Ghansawangi, G. Jalna is a Taluk level Industrial Training Institute and as on date, 10 batches of 05 trades are functioning in the Institute with an average intake capacity of 152 per annum. A total of 216 trainees are undergoing training at the institute.
Jalna district is a leader in the industrial sector and is known as the Steel Capital.
In order to skill the needy students of Jalna district and create employment opportunities, Government Industrial Training Institute, Ghansawangi has selected new batches of 4 business courses for the said OPI which will provide employment and self-employment opportunities related to the future development and cutting-edge technology related to the modern world.
Accordingly, the proposal to start batches of new business courses with high demand was under the consideration of the government to develop the skills of the needy students of Ghansawangi G. Jalna district and create employment.
