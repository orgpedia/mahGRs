Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 02.09.2015, the Department has approved the implementation of Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission to empower the youth of the State through skill development and provide them employment/ self-employment opportunities in industries, services and similar sectors which are in high demand. In accordance with the provisions of section 9.2 of the said Government Decision, the District Level General Scheme of" Minimum Skill Development Programme" has been accorded administrative approval under the Government Decision dated 16.03.2017 of this Department with the objective of identifying the areas of high demand for employment/ self-employment in the district based on the specific geographical and social conditions, traditional occupations, natural resources and resources etc. and to implement specific courses as per the demand for skills of these areas and to assist the trained candidates for employment/ self-employment.
In view of the effective implementation of the Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission, the Government was considering to revise the modalities of implementation of the scheme as prescribed in the Government Resolution dated 16th March, 2017.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In order to ensure effective implementation of Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission, the following paragraph 3 of the Government Decision dated 16th March, 2017 of the Department regarding Minimum Skill Development Programme is being amended:
3. The selection of training institutions for implementing minimum skill development programmes at the district level may be done in the following manner.
The name of the course and the number of trainees will be communicated online to the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society by the District Skill Development Executive Committee.
The list of training institutions required for training of trainees will be made available online by the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society to the District Skill Development Executive Committees, taking into account the intake capacity of the institutions listed in the respective district for the implementation of the said course.
If the training institute required to implement the courses selected by the District Skill Development Executive Committee is not listed with the Maharashtra Skill Development Society, it will be reported online to the District Skill Development Executive Committee by the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society.
In case the Maharashtra Skill Development Society does not have a listed institution, the District Skill Development Executive Committee will seek an EoI from the willing institutions.
In the Letter of Intent, the willing skill training institute will have to mention the rate of reimbursement of training cost and the rate should be within the limit of the rate fixed by the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship from time to time. The rate will include the cost of mobilization, counseling, mentoring, training, providing employment/ self-employment to the candidates and subsequent follow-up.
5) Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Government Decision No. KOUVIU-2015/ No. 37/ Abhi. The eligible institutions meeting the criteria as on 23/11/2015 will be selected by the District Skill Development Executive Committee. The selected training institutes will be required to get empanelled with Maharashtra State Skill Development Society. "
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the consensus reached under the Planning Department Non-official Reference No. 75/1481-NV, dated 26/12/2018.
