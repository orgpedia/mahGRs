Government Resolution No: KAUVU-2017/ PR No.260/ SKILL-1
Preamble: The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India vide letter No. 17 has sanctioned Rs. 11,50,00, 000/- has been disbursed. Out of this amount, Rs. 8,60,20, 000/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the above and as approved by the Department of Finance," Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (100% CSS) PMKVY (2016-20) with an outlay of Rs. 8,60,20, 000/- (Rs. Under this decision, the government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of eight crore sixty lakh twenty thousand only).
The expenditure should be met from the grants available in the year 2022-23 under the head Demand No.ZA-2, 2230 Labour& Service Scheme, 02 Service Scheme Services, 001 Operations& Administration, (01) Directorate of Service Planning, Schemes under Five Year Plans, Schemes under State Schemes, (01) (12) Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (100% Centrally Sponsored Schemes) 2230A307, 31-Subsidiary Grants.
The Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship shall draw the funds allocated for this scheme by the State Government from the treasury and make it available for expenditure to the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, Mumbai. For this, Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Navi Mumbai is being declared as" Controlling Officer" and Assistant Director (Accounts) Konkan Bhawan is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
The Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society should take care of sending the utilization certificate and other details of the expenditure to the concerned ministry of the central government as well as to the government. These accounts should also be made available to the Auditor General at the time of audit.
It shall be mandatory for the expenditure to be made as per the provisions as amended by the Central Government from time to time and for the activities/ projects for which funds have been received from the Central Government.
6. The guidelines/ rules/ terms/ conditions issued by the Finance and Planning Department should be followed while spending the fund.
The said Memorandum is being issued with the concurrence received from the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 02/ Expenditure-6, dated 16th January, 2022.
