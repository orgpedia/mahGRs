Preamble: Provisional posts under 128 Schemes of Higher Level Industrial Training Institutes opened at Divisional level for the children of Scheduled Castes and New Buddhists under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been sanctioned under the Government decision in the Reference (2). As per the Government Decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time, the above Reference No. (3) The said 128 temporary posts have been extended till 28.02.2019 as per the decision of the Government. Under the Government Decision (4) in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 30.09.2019. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter dated 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019, extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: ITI-2019/ PR No.45/ Person-3
GOVERNMENT DECISION:- The Government hereby approve the continuation of 128 temporary posts under the scheme in the accompanying Form A for the Higher Level Industrial Training Institutes for the children of Scheduled Castes and New Buddhists started at the divisional level under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, in cases where the post creation has been approved thereafter, the process of approval of the contours of the posts along with the scrutiny of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
4) The revised pattern after reviewing the posts needs to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. In case of non-payment of salary, the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai will be responsible for the same. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the grants sanctioned during the year 2019-20 under the following heads:
The Demand No. N-3, Head Accounts Head 2225 Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes, 01 Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 277 Education, (01) (04) Establishment of Industrial Training Institute for Scheduled Castes and Newborn Boys and Girls at Divisional Level (VGYO) (22254104)
The Director (Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall ensure that the budget estimates, revised estimates and final revised grant proposals and expenditure reconciliation proceedings under the above head as well as the feedback on the appropriation account report of the Accountant General are submitted within the prescribed time.
(3) This Government Decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 1 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. 1/ Education-1, dated 19.03.2019 and Finance Department, vide Government Resolution No: 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 21.02.2019, as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
