The Proposal:
For the purchase of materials required for government offices, a revised procedure has been prescribed for office purchases to be made by government departments in accordance with the government decision in the above context. In the rule book for the purchase of offices by the government departments with this government decision, paragraph No. 2.9.3 The Administrative Department of the Ministry (itself) has been informed to constitute the respective Procurement Committee for procurement of office supplies.
Accordingly, a procurement committee of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department (itself) was being constituted for procurement of office material for the department (s).
Government's decision:
The Procurement Committee of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department (KHEDU) is being constituted for procurement of office equipments for the Department (KHEDU) as under:
