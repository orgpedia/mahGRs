Introduction: Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MAHALT) has been assigned to implement customized HTE service delivery system in all non-agricultural universities, all aided colleges and other aided institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, which also includes the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. Through this system, the salary and allowances of the employees of non-government funded technical secondary schools and higher secondary schools under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under this department in the vocational courses (primary and higher secondary vocational) are paid online.
The system covers 69000 officers and staff of Higher and Technical Education Department and 9535 officers and staff of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department. The number of officers and staff is 87 per cent of the total strength of the Higher and Technical Education Department and 13 per cent of the total strength of the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
