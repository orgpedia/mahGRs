Preamble: With a view to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government vide order dated 23rd March, 2021 has directed to adopt a revised methodology. Accordingly, it is necessary to appoint a single nodal agency (SNA) for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and open a separate single bank account in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least one officer of the rank of Deputy Secretary as Nodal Officer for coordination of these Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a single nodal agency and nodal officer for centrally sponsored schemes under the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship was under consideration of the government.
Government Resolution: Reference No. As per the instructions issued vide Circular No. 2, the following Single Nodal Agency and Nodal Officer are being appointed for the implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship:
