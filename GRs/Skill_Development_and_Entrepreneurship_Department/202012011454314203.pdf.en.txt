November 18, 2020 Preamble: The then Hon'ble Minister of State for Education, Smt. Sushila Kumari, highlighted the issues of time bound improvement and enhancement of curriculum being implemented by the Directorate of Business Education due to increase in industrialization, changing technology, social and economic changes etc. In the meeting held under the chairmanship of the Minister of Higher and Technical Education, instructions were given to collect tuition fees for courses implemented under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training.
Read on to No. The Education Fee Committee was formed as per the decision of the government. Read No. 2 in the letter. A proposal was received to abolish the Committee on Tuition Fee constituted under the Government Resolution dated 12th August, 2014 and headed by the present Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training.
Accordingly, the government decided to dissolve the committee.
Government's decision:
The government has taken a decision. As on 12th August, 2014, it is being approved to abolish the Committee on Tuition Fees constituted for all courses, professional and non-professional, in the Non-Governmental Organizations under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and the Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the present Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training.
