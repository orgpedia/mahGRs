Preamble: To promote the industrial and economic development of the State and the country
In order to encourage enterprises based on innovative resolutions to address the problem of unemployment, the Department has approved the" Maharashtra State Innovative Start-up Policy, 2018" under the Government Decision dated 05/02/2018. In line with this policy, Maharashtra Start-up Week will be celebrated every year in the state to provide a platform to start-ups to showcase innovative products and services through the government and to address the important social and economic challenges of the state by the government agencies in collaboration with start-ups.
It was under the consideration of the government to issue guidelines regarding the conduct of this Maharashtra Start-up Week.

In line with this policy," Maharashtra Start-up Week" will be celebrated every year in the month of February in the state to provide a platform to start-ups to showcase innovative products and services through the government and government agencies in collaboration with start-ups to address important social and economic challenges of the state.
The Government is approving the guidelines in Annexure-A for the conduct of this Maharashtra Start-up Week.
The Maharashtra State Innovation Society should take action regarding the organization of Maharashtra Start-up Week in accordance with these guidelines.
