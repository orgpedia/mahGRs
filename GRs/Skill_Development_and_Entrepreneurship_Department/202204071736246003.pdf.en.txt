The Proposal:
Profile of posts in Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under Department of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation Reference No. (8) and (13) are hereby approved by the Government. Also Reference No. The posts in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training have been increased and decreased from time to time as per the Government decisions in (1) to (7), (9) to (12) and (14) to (33). Reference No. (34) The Department of Finance, Govt. Under 11/02/2016, instructions have been issued to review the sanctioned posts in the administrative departments and offices under them and fix the revised figure. Also Reference No. (36) Department of Finance, Govt. Guidance Notices have been issued to fix the revised figure under 29/06/2017. Accordingly, a proposal to fix the revised pattern of posts in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training has been mooted. The matter was taken up for consideration in the meeting of the High Level Committee of Secretaries headed by the Chief Secretary dated 23/08/2021. The minutes of the meeting No. (38) As per the communication received by the Department, a total of 15489 regular posts of 13867+ 1622 (new posts) have been sanctioned in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved to take the services through external agencies within the limit of total manpower strength of 5857. As per the approval given by the high-level committee of secretaries, the matter of reviewing the posts in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and approving the revised figure was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A total of 15489 regular posts as shown in the accompanying Form-A (1) and a total of 5857 external agency services as shown in Form-A (2) are being approved for establishment of Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the Department of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation and Government offices/ institutions under it.
The number of posts in the deceased cadre in Form-A (1) in A.No. (9), (13), (14), (17), (18), (24), (28), (30), (35), (37), (38), (44), (45), (47), (48), (51), (52), (54), (55), (59) and (60) shall be deemed to be more than the number of employees currently working. The posts will automatically lapse after the incumbent's post falls vacant due to retirement/ resignation/ death etc. The combined details of these posts are shown in Form-B.
The Committee of Secretaries at the highest level has approved the outsourcing of total manpower of 5857 to the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training as indicated in Form-A (2).
