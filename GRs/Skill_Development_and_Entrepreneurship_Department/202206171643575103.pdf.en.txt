Introduction: Industrial Training Institutes play a major role in creating skilled manpower in the State.
Industrial Training Institutes play a major role in creating world-class and business-oriented, skilled manpower in accordance with the demands of the establishment. Industrialization is increasing rapidly in the state due to economic liberalization. With the changing process of industrial technology production, the need for skilled manpower is increasing in the industries. It is also necessary to include new courses as per the demand of industrial establishments based on new technology.
Government Industrial Training Institute, Jalna G. Taluk level Industrial Training Institute is functioning at Jalna and as on date, 28 batches of 15 trades are functioning in the Institute with an intake capacity of 400 per annum. A total of 600 trainees are undergoing training in the institute.
At present, there is a huge need for manpower for solar-based and related industrial establishments in Marathwada, a total of 4 new batches of business courses have been selected for this UP institute, which meet the local needs and demand of industrial establishments as well as provide employment and self-employment opportunities.
Accordingly, the Government was considering to approve the proposal to start a batch of new business courses in high demand at the Government Industrial Training Institute, Jalna G. Jalna to develop the skills of the needy students of Jalna district and create employment.
Government Decision: Government Industrial Training Institute, Jalna is a Taluk level Industrial Training Institute in Jalna district and at present 28 batches of 15 trades are working in the institute. Approval is being given to start new batches of 4 business courses from 2022-23. Details of the new units are given in Annexure A. Approval to start this course is subject to the following conditions.
Approval of DGT, New Delhi should be obtained for starting business courses from the institutions.
