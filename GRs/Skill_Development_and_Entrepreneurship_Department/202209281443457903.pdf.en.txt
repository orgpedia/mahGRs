The Proposal:
To effectively control the academic, administrative and financial aspects, it is necessary to provide infrastructure and modern technology. Accordingly, to improve the quality of business education in the state, it is necessary to connect all educational institutions and offices in the state through computer system, create Wi-Fi connection for it, implement e-governance projects, strengthen information management and provide maximum state-of-the-art facilities to students.
Further, the Department of Higher and Technical Education vide Government Decision No.VOC-2012/ No. 21/12/ BISHI 2, No. The State Level Scheme for Strengthening of Directorate of Vocational Education and Training has been accorded administrative approval with effect from 05.06.2012. Strengthening of Directorate of Vocational Education and Training (VEDT) in line with the provisions made under the scheme for provision of machinery and equipment for connecting Industrial Training Institutes through networking, e-governance, creation of database and procurement of equipment like computers, interactive panel sets, Xerox machines etc. keeping in view the increasing scope of schemes in the Directorate of VEDT.
