As per the decision of the Government here, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 30.09.2018. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter at (7), the Government was considering extension of 63 temporary posts from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- The Government have decided to continue the posts under 63 schemes (shown in the accompanying Form-1) for starting new/ additional trades in the existing O.P. Institutions under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai from 01.03.2018 to 31.05.2018 subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
4) The revised pattern after reviewing the posts needs to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. In case of non-payment of salary, the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai will be responsible for the same. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the following heads:
Demand No. ZA-1, 2230- Labour and Employment, 03- Training, (003)- Training of Artisans and Supervisors, (02)- Technical and Vocational Training of Artisans, (02 (08) Expansion of Industrial Training Institutes to increase accessibility (2230A 209)
3. The said Government Decision is being issued by the Finance Department in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide Government Decision No: 2016/ P.S. No. 8/16/ A.P.C., dated 05.03.2018.
