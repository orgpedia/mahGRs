Government's decision:
The revised admission procedure proposed by the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training for the admission process to be done under the Craftsman Training Scheme in the Industrial Training Institute in the admission session 2021-22 will be as per the Form-A attached with the Rules and Procedures.
As per the revised admission rules and procedures, the admission process for the 2021-22 session should be carried out under the Craftsmen Training Scheme.
