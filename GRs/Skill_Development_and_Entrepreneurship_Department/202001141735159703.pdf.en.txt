The Proposal:
The World Bank assisted Skill Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE) project will be implemented in all the States of the country to enhance the relevance and efficiency of skill training provided under Industrial Training Institutes and Apprenticeship to the needs of industrial establishments. The period is 5 years and for the period FY 2019-20 to 2022-23, 100% funding from the Central Government i. e. Rs. 226.20 crore will be received through PFMS system.
The government was considering the issue of obtaining new accounting titles and computer codes to budget funds for the World Bank-assisted Skill Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE) scheme.
Government's decision:
The World Bank-aided Scheme for Skill Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE) has been approved with new headings and indicators. The plan is as follows:
