Industrial Training Institute, Malvan, Sindhudurg. A case under Sections 420, 406, 465, 467 and 471 of the Indian Penal Code has been registered at the Malvan police station. Chavan, the then Principal, Industrial Training Institute, Malvan, G. Sindhudurg. 26.05.2021 at 22.15 hrs. was arrested. Accordingly, the matter is sub judice.
U.S. Chavan was suspended under the proviso to Rule 4 Potnium 1 (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, by an order of the office of reference.
3. Therefore, now, in the aforesaid Reference No. (2) In accordance with the provisions of Rule 4 Potnium 5 (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, the suspension of Mr. U.S. Chavan is hereby revoked and subject to the decision thereon, if an appeal is filed in a higher court, he is reinstated in the Government service. Chavan has been posted as vice-principal, Industrial Training Institute, Khamgaon, district Buldhana.
Chavan should join the post of Vice Principal, Industrial Training Institute, Khamgaon, G. Buldhana within 7 days from the date of receipt of this order, otherwise, if he does not join the post on time, this period will be considered as an off-day.
