The state government has decided to upgrade 419 government industrial training institutes in the state in collaboration with Tata Technology and allied entrepreneurs in a phased manner and set up 6 centres of excellence and 3 centres of excellence in agriculture.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship
Government Resolution No: ITI-2020/ PR No.68/ Person-3
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 18, 2021
Government's decision:
In order to meet the growing demand of modern skilled manpower by creating world class skilled manpower for the manufacturing sector in the state, the state government in collaboration with Tata Technology and associate entrepreneurs will upgrade 419 government industrial training institutes in a phased manner and establish 6 Centre of Excellence and 3 Centre of Excellence in Agriculture.
A high-level committee has been constituted to decide on the following:
1) Ma. Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra, Chairman, 2. Additional Chief Secretary, Planning Department, 3. Additional Chief Secretary, Finance Department, 4. Principal Secretary, Industries Department, 5. Principal Secretary, Higher and Technical Education Department, 6. Principal Secretary, Information Technology Department, 7. Principal Secretary, Law and Justice Department, 8. Secretary, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department, 9. Invitees, Technical Experts, 10. Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai Member Secretary.
