The Proposal:
The Government was contemplating to set up Skill Universities in the State on funded/ self-funded basis to provide integrated and holistic skill training to the youth of the State and to create employment/ self-employment opportunities for them by enabling them to get employment through nationally and internationally recognized skill-based higher education.
Government Decision: In order to create a conducive environment for providing integrated and holistic skill training to the youth of the state, the matter of establishing skill universities on funded/ self-financed basis in the state to create employment/ self-employment opportunities by enabling them through higher education in nationally and internationally recognized skills is under consideration at the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department level and the following committee is being formed in this regard.

The mandate of the Committee is to: (i) draft an Act to provide for the establishment of Skill Universities on the basis of self-financing, funded and funded basis in the context of skill development.
(ii) Recognition of certificates/ diplomas/ degrees in the skill development programmes conducted by the institutions covered by this Act.
(3) To make provision for recommending proposals to the University Grants Commission (UGC) for the deemed universities to be established under this Act.
The committee has to submit its report to the government within 15 days.
