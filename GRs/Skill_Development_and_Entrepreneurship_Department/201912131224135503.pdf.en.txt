Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to fix a time-bound programme for online transfer of officers/ staff of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and subordinate offices.
Government's decision:
In the above background, this Government has decided to implement the following time-bound programmes for the periodical transfer of Group-B officers and Group-C staff in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and subordinate offices:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | Stages of action                                                                                                                            | period of time                  |
| 1    | Publication of list of vacancies for transfer on online portal.                                                                             | As of January 15th              |
| 2    | Formulating a policy for equalization of vacancies                                                                                          | As of January 15th              |
| 3    | Publication of list of transferable employees on online portal                                                                              | As of January 31st              |
| 4.   | Submit suggestions and objections to the list of transferable officers and employees published                                              | As of February 7th              |
| 5.   | Publication of final list of transferable officers and employees after suggestions and objections on the published list                     | As of February 10th             |
| 6    | Obtaining transfer preferences from transferable officers and staff through online HRMS portal                                              | From February 11 to February 28 |
| 7.   | Conducting meeting of Civil Services Board, finalizing transfer proposals online and submitting recommendations to the competent authority. | From March 1 to April 15        |
| 8    | Issue of transfer orders by the competent authority on the recommendation of the Civil Services Board.                                      | From April 30 to May 31         |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Director, Vocational Education and Training, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai will be responsible for the periodic transfers of officers/ staff of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and subordinate offices through this computerised procedure as per the above time-bound schedule.
