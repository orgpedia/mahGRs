Government circular:
The provisional seniority list of the officers of the cadre of Deputy Commissioner Group-A in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System as on 01/01/2022 is being published in Form-A vide this circular.
The Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship is advised to bring this provisional seniority list to the notice of all concerned officers and in case of any objections and suggestions of the said officers, the same along with necessary documentary evidence should be communicated to the Government within 15 days from the date of issue of this Government Circular so that necessary corrections in the seniority list can be made and the final seniority list can be published.
