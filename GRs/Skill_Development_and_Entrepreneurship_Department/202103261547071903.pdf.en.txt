The Proposal:
Under the National Skill Development Mission (NSDM), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India, it has been decided to strengthen the institutional mechanism at the national and state level, create a pool of quality trainers, centralize all skill training programs being implemented at the state level, and provide training to the youth.
Creation of an effective mechanism for monitoring and evaluation of skill development training programmes, providing opportunities for skill training for socially backward classes, for the manufacturing sector.
To create skilled manpower and support Make in India.

Government's decision:
