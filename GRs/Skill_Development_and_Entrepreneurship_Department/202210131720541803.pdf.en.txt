Preamble: Development Title Social and Community Services Sub-Development Title Technical Education The Government has decided to strengthen the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training. Administrative approval has been given to implement the scheme from 03.02.2011.
Accordingly, under the scheme of Strengthening of Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, the provision available under the objective of machinery has been budgeted for the purchase of machinery required for the functioning and day-to-day functioning of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and the administrative offices under it. Under this, computers, xerox machines, etc. are required to be purchased for the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Regional Offices, Office of the District Vocational Education and Training Officer, Maharashtra State Skill Development Examination Board. Accordingly, the matter of giving administrative and financial approval to the above purchase was under consideration of the Government.
