The said expenditure shall be met from the provisions approved in the year 2023-24 under the head of Demand No.ZA-2, 2230 Labour& Employment, (01) (14) Maharashtra State Innovation Society (Programme) Accounts Head 2230A 343).
For this, the Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship has been authorized as the Controlling Officer, Assistant Director, Accounts as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Innovation Society as the Nodal Agency.
The Commissioner of Funds, Commissionerate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Navi Mumbai shall transfer the said funds to the Maharashtra State Innovation Society and keep the accounts separate and updated. The Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Innovation Society will be responsible for the expenditure on the basis of which the above funds have been made available.
Also, it should be ensured that the utilization certificate of the actual expenditure is submitted within the prescribed time limit. While spending the said funds, the guidelines/ terms and conditions issued by the Finance and Planning Department from time to time should be followed.
The Fund is governed by the Financial Rules, 1978 Rule No. 27 (2) is being issued with the approval of the competent authority and as per instructions of the Finance Department, Circular No. 2022/ No. 40/ Finance-3, dated 12th April, 2023.
