Government's stand:
The Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Government of India, under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, is implementing a free book bank scheme for pre-exam technical/ vocational education in secondary schools. The administrative approval has been given under the Government Decision dated September 30, 2021.
