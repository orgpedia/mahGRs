Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts for persons with disabilities notified under the notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published in the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading List of posts identification suitable for person with Bench mark Disability Notified on 04/01/2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions regarding ensuring posts in the State Government establishments for the disabled under the Government decision dated 02/02/2021. As per the directions of the said Government decision, the Government was considering to ensure posts in Government service for the disabled persons for the posts in Group-A, Group-B and Group-C cadres on the establishment of the Commissionerate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship under the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship.
Government's decision:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, has notified that the posts of Group-A, Group-B and Group-C, which have been ensured by the Central Government, are in the state government establishments. In respect of such posts, the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are the same as those in the State Government Service, even if the designations are different
