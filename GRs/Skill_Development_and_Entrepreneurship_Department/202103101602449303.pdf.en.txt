The Proposal:
In line with the Government of India's vision of Skill India, the State of Maharashtra has put forward the goal of Skill Maharashtra, Employable Maharashtra and has approved the implementation of" Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Mission" in the State to empower the youth of the State through skill development and provide them employment/ self-employment opportunities in industries, services and similar sectors where there is more demand. In the Annexure-I of the said Government Resolution, 1.7 (b) herein describes the methodology for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL), a prior knowledge recognition model. 8 It has been mentioned here that the long-term goals and objectives related to skill development will be planned in due course. Accordingly, the matter of approval to implement the" Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)" model under the Pramod Mahajan Skill Development Mission in the state was under consideration of the government.

Government's decision:
National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India has launched the initiative" Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)- Prior Knowledge Recognition" under the" Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana" (PMKVY). Similarly, under the scheme" Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission" (PMKUVA), approval is being given to implement" Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)" model in the state of Maharashtra.
This Government Decision is being issued in pursuance of the decision taken in the meeting dated 08/12/2020 of the Empowered Committee on Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship established by Government Decision dated 03/08/2015.
Guidelines for implementation of" Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)" in the State are attached along with the modalities and in accordance with the said guidelines, the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, Mumbai is being entrusted with the responsibility of controlling and implementing the scheme.
