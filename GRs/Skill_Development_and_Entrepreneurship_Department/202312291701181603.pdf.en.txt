Preamble: The Maharashtra State Skill University Act, 2021 (Maharashtra Act No. 7 of 2021) dated 23rd March, 2021 provides for the establishment of a public skill university on a funded basis to provide skill-based employable education to the youth of the state. The said Act has come into force with effect from 14th April, 2021 vide notification dated 6th April, 2021.
The Maharashtra State Skill Development Corporation (MSDSDC) is planning to start a state-of-the-art skill training institute in the state.
