Introduction: - To enhance the employability of the youth of the state by providing them hands-on training after their education. The "Mukhyamantri Yuva Karyakarta Shiksha Yojana" has been implemented under the government decision of 09.07.2024. The financial coverage of the Mukhyamantri Yuva Karyakarta Yojana is extensive. Suggestions were received from the Finance Department that separate headings for general / SC / ST should be proposed under the scheme so that the expenditure thereon can be managed sector wise.
Accordingly, the proposal submitted for getting a separate title of account for the Scheduled Tribe category under the Mukhyamantri Yuva Karyakarta Yojana was approved by the Finance Department as well as the Ministry of Finance. The Accountant General (Accounts and Licensure) has agreed. Accordingly, the government was considering approving a separate head of accounts for the Scheduled Tribe category under the Chief Minister's Youth Work Training Scheme.
Government Decision: Under the Chief Minister's Youth Affairs Training Scheme, the following separate headings are being approved for the Scheduled Tribes component: -
