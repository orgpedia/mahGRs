In order to effectively implement the Skill Development Mission in the State, the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department has been constituted under the Government Decision dated 15/01/2015 of the General Administration Department and the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS), the nodal agency, controls, plans and implements various Centrally and State sponsored schemes/ programmes of skill and entrepreneurship development in the State.
Various schemes/ programmes for skill and entrepreneurship training and capacity building are implemented by various departments of the State Government. A high level committee under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Industries was constituted as per the decision of the Government dated 05/04/2018 of the Department to review the said schemes and convergence of schemes to avoid duplication. In pursuance of the recommendation of the said Committee, a proposal for convergence of schemes related to skill and entrepreneurship training and capacity building being implemented by various departments of the State Government was approved on 13/08/2019. As per the decision taken by the Hon'ble Cabinet in its meeting dated 13/08/2019, the Government was considering to approve the convergence of schemes related to skill and entrepreneurship training and capacity building being implemented by various departments of the State Government.
Government's decision:
As mentioned in the above proposal, the government is implementing skill and entrepreneurship training and capacity building through various departments of the state government.

Approving the following schemes/ programmes for capacity building:
Skill and Entrepreneurship Development implemented by various departments of the State Government.
Schemes related to skill and entrepreneurship development and capacity building will have to conform to the National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF).
It will be mandatory to implement the courses available on the integrated web portal of Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS). However, it will be mandatory for the concerned department to prepare the curriculum and get it approved by the Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training (MSCVT) if it wants to implement the customised courses other than the ones available on the portal.
It will be mandatory to run the courses through the training institutes available on the portal.
However, if the training institute is not available for the relevant course on the portal or the department is willing to conduct the training program through an institute that is not registered on the web portal, then the training institute will have to first register with the Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training (MSCVT).
If the concerned department is willing to implement the training program through the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS), the permissible amount of training and evaluation fee will be required to be deposited in advance with the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS) along with 2% administrative fee.
The department concerned will be allowed to deposit the admissible amount towards training fee with the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS) in advance along with 2% administrative fee or pay it directly to the concerned training institute.
If the concerned department is going to implement the training program, then it will be mandatory to manage the training through the integrated web portal of the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS). Also, 0.5% of the training fee (web portal fee) and assessment fee amount will have to be deposited with the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS).
The right to choose the training institution will remain with the concerned candidate.
