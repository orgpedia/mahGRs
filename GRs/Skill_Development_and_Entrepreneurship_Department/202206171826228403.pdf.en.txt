The Proposal:
Employment and self-employment generation is central to the country's social and economic development and is the most important component of the Human Development Index. In the globalization due to the changing new technology, the nature of employment and self-employment is changing drastically and accordingly the" skill curriculum" and" job roles" are also changing.
2. With rapidly changing technology and skills, industrial establishments are facing shortage of skilled manpower. The Government was contemplating to implement a" Skill Upgradation" policy for timely and up-to-date skill upgradation required for sustainable self-employment and up-to-date skilled employment.
Government's decision
The Government has decided to implement a" Skill Enhancement Policy" for skill upgradation in tune with the changing technology and skills to provide sustainable opportunities of employment and self-employment to the youth of the State.
The background of the" Skill Enhancement" policy will be as per the objectives, target beneficiaries and their eligibility, curriculum, implementation, cost norms and financial aspects, training model, dynamics and expected outcomes, etc.
