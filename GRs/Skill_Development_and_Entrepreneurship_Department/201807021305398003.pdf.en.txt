Preamble: Provisional posts under 37 schemes sanctioned for starting batches of business courses/ new business courses of higher demand in Government Industrial Training Institutes under Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been sanctioned under the Government decision in the context (2). (1) All administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts under the Government decision. As per the Government Decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time, the above Reference No. The said 37 temporary posts have been extended till 31.05.2018. Under the Government Decision (3) in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 30.09.2018. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted to the Government by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter dated 01.06.2018 to 30.09.2018, the extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: ITI-2018/ PR No.73/ Person-3
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have decided to continue the existing 37 temporary posts under the scheme in the accompanying Form A for starting batches of business courses/ new business courses of higher demand in the Government Industrial Training Institutes under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai from 01.06.2018 to 30.09.2018 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
1) The above posts should be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
4) The revised pattern after reviewing the posts needs to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. In case of non-payment of salary, the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai will be responsible for the same. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the following heads:
" Demand No. ZA-1, 2230 Labour and Employment, 03-Training, (003)-Training of Artisans and Supervisors, (02)-Technical and Vocational Training of Artisans, (02) (08) Expansion of Industrial Training Institutes to increase accessibility (2230 A 209)
8/16/ APC, dated 05.03.2018 is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
