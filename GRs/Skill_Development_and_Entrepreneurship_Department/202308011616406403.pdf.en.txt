GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: TRANSFER-2023/ PR No.154/ PRISA-2 (Employment)
(2) This order comes into force with immediate effect and the Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship should relieve the above officers immediately to join the post indicated by the transfer.
(3) Since the transfer is for administrative reasons, the travel allowance, daily allowance admissible to them shall continue to be admissible.
4.The concerned officer should immediately submit a report to the government about the date on which he appeared at the place of posting.
If the transferred officer cancels his transfer or directly or indirectly presses for it, disciplinary action will be taken against him as the act violates Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
During the period prescribed under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Cessation of Posts), Non-Ministerial Services and Suspension, Promotion and Removal from Service Rules, 1981, the concerned officer shall take care to join immediately at the place of posting of the transferor otherwise the period of absence of such transferor shall be deemed to be a non-working day. The transferred officials should take note of this.
No leave shall be granted without prior approval of the officers transferred as above and no leave applications of the officers transferred as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981 shall be accepted by the parent office. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities by registered post at the original address.
