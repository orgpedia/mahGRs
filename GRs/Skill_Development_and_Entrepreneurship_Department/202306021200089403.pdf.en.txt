The Proposal:
In order to implement the Skill Development Mission more effectively, in line with the concept of" Make in Maharashtra," a separate department" Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department" has been established in the state under the Government Decision dated 15.01.2015 and this department is now functioning under the name" Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation Department."

2. The Department has set the vision of" Skilled Maharashtra, Employable Maharashtra" and launched the" Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission" from 2nd September, 2015 to empower the youth of the State through skill development and provide them employment/ self-employment opportunities in industries, services and similar sectors which are in high demand.
The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Commissionerate of Skill, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Maharashtra State Skill, Vocational Education and Training Board, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, Maharashtra State Innovation Society and Maharashtra State Skill University, etc. offices are working to fulfill the mission of" Skill Maharashtra, Rozgar Yukt Maharashtra" of this department.
The administrative and financial autonomy of these offices is summarized below.
The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training was established in 1984 to provide skilled manpower to the industry.
The directorate aims to ensure the flow of skilled workers from different professions, improve the quality of labor with training and increase industrial production. The objectives have been set to equip the educated youth for gainful industrial employment. For this, ITI courses of 1 to 2 years duration are provided to the trainees from Industrial Training Institutes.
At present, 419 Government Industrial Training Institutes and 169 Government Technical Secondary Schools/ Centres are functioning under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training. Also, there are an estimated 1500 buildings for head offices, departmental offices and district offices.
Maharashtra State Skill Development Society has been formed for the implementation of short and long term schemes and programmes related to skill development by the central and state governments.
The Maharashtra State Startup Policy for innovative entrepreneurship in the state is implemented through the Maharashtra State Innovation Society (MSInS).
Maharashtra State Skill, Vocational Education and Training Board has been set up to regulate matters related to imparting skills, vocational education and training and entrepreneurship education and other skill courses in the state as per the National Skill Qualification Framework.
