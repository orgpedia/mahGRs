Government's decision:
Reference No. Under Section 6, a proposal was submitted to the government to give administrative approval to fill up the posts of Class-3 and Class-4 under the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State through an external mechanism by creating fictitious posts. In order to contain the expenditure on administration and to provide adequate funds for development, the work of this post should be done through external mechanisms without creating posts wherever possible. 1 here. Notice dated 27.09.2010 has been issued. Considering these guidelines, a proposal was placed before the High Powered Committee for the creation of 50 posts of fictitious posts in the cadre of Class-3 and Class-4 in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training.
Accordingly, the Committee has approved the creation of 50 notional posts as follows. Accordingly, the government has taken the decision.
