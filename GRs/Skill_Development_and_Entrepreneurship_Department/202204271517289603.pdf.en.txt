Preamble: In pursuance of the decision of the Government in the context (1), 128 temporary posts have been sanctioned under the scheme of Higher Level Industrial Training Institute for the Children of Scheduled Castes and New Buddhists under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai. The said temporary posts were extended till 28.02.2022. Under the Government Decision (2) issued in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter at (3), the matter of extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the scheme of Higher Level Industrial Training Institute for the Children of Scheduled Castes and New Buddhists started at Divisional Level under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 128 temporary posts (attached form A) are to be continued from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: The above posts should be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(1) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: ITI-2019/ PR No. 45/ Special-32) The approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during the period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
(3) In the event of any question of remuneration of the said temporary posts, the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall be solely responsible. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal should be met from the approved grants under the following heads in the financial year 2021-2022 and 2022-23.
" Demand No. N-3, Main Accounts Head 2225 Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes, 01- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 277- Education, (01) (04) Establishment of Industrial Training Institute for Scheduled Castes and Newborn Boys and Girls at Divisional Level (VGYO) (2225 4104)" The Director (Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall take care to submit the Budget Estimates, Revised Estimates and final revised grant proposals under the above headings within the prescribed time.
