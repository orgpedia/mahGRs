The Proposal:
Under the National Skill Development Mission (NSDM), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India is implementing the National Skill Development Mission (NSDM) to strengthen the institutional mechanism at the national and state level, create a pool of quality trainers, centralize all skill training programmes being implemented at the state level, skill development training
The Skill Acquisition& Knowledge Awareness for Livelihood Project (SANKALP) will be implemented with the objective of creating an effective mechanism for monitoring and evaluation of programmes, providing opportunities for skill training for socially backward classes, creating skilled manpower required for the manufacturing sector and supplementing the Make in India initiative. The duration of the project is 6 years
