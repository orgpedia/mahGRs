Applications have been invited from eligible private institutions for the programme.
Accordingly, applications received from private institutions interested in starting courses of the Maharashtra State Board of Skill, Vocational Education and Training have been examined and monitored by the District Vocational Education and Training Officer of the concerned district and the eligible institution has been recommended to the Board. The proposals received from the District Vocational Education and Training Officers will be scrutinized by the Maharashtra State Board of Skill, Vocational Education and Training and the list of eligible institutions will be sent to the Government for approval. 7 are presented here. Accordingly, from the year 2023-24, the Government was considering to grant approval to start business courses on self-financing basis to new institutions/ batches of Maharashtra State Board of Skill, Vocational Education and Training (MSVTEC) which are implementing certificate courses in Intellectual Groups.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Council for Skill, Vocational Education and Training (MSSCVT) has recommended that the Maharashtra State Council for Skill, Vocational Education and Training (MSSCVT) should consider the proposals submitted by private educational institutions willing to implement the courses of the Maharashtra State Council for Skill, Vocational Education and Training (MSSCVT). The details of these institutions are enclosed in the accompanying Form-A and they are being approved to start the Intramural Group Certificate Courses of the Board from the year 2023-24 in the new institution on a self-financing basis subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
The institutions approved to implement the courses of the Maharashtra State Board of Skill, Vocational Education and Training will have to comply with the orders issued from time to time by the government, the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, the Maharashtra State Board of Skill, Vocational Education and Training and the concerned offices under the Directorate.
Students should be admitted to the place where the institutions have been approved to run the courses of the Maharashtra State Board of Skill, Vocational Education and Training. Also, the approval of the same place will be considered, if there is a change in the place, the approval will be cancelled.
