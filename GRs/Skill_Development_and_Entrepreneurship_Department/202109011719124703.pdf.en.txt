Government's decision:
Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation has been allocated Rs. 50.00 crore, out of which 25% i.e. Rs. The government has sanctioned Rs. 12.50 crore for the purpose.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai is being authorized as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Joint Secretary, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Controlling Officer to disburse the above sanctioned funds to Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation, Mumbai. The government is giving approval to sign the payment. This amount should be paid to the corporation in lump sum.
The expenditure in respect of the aforesaid funds shall be covered under the head" Capital Expenditure on ZA-4, 4250, Other Social Services, 00, 190, Investments in Public Sector and Other Enterprises (00) Employment (00) (01) Capital Contribution to Annasaheb Patil Economic Development Corporation (Programme) (42500259, 32 Contributions)" and shall be met from the provision sanctioned during the financial year 2021-2022.
As the funds made available to the Corporation are from the funds under the schemes, the utilization certificate should be submitted to the Government by spending the funds only on the schemes approved by the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship and the Finance Department.
