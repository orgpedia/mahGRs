Preamble:- Provisional posts under 37 schemes sanctioned for starting batches of business courses/ new business courses of higher demand in Government Industrial Training Institutes under Directorate of Business Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved under the Government decision in the context (2). (1) All administrative departments have been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts under the Government decision. As per the Government Decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time, the above Reference No. The said 37 temporary posts have been extended till 30.09.2018. Under the Government Decision (4) issued in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 28.02.2019. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter at (5), the extension of temporary posts from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: ITI-2018/ PR No.73/ Person-3
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- The Government hereby approve the continuation of 37 temporary posts under the scheme in the accompanying Form A for starting batches of business courses/ new business courses of higher demand in the Government Industrial Training Institutes under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
1) The above posts should be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
4) The revised pattern after reviewing the posts needs to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. In case of non-payment of salary, the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai will be responsible for the same. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the following heads during the financial year 2018-19.
" Demand No. ZA-1, 2230 Labour and Employment, 03-Training, (003) Training of Artisans and Supervisors, (02) Technical and Vocational Training of Artisans, (02) (08) Expansion of Industrial Training Institutes to enhance Accessibility (2230A 209)
