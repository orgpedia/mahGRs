Reference:- General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. SRV-2016/ PR No. 290/ Work.12 dated 19/11/2016.
Preamble: General Administration for taking final decisions in the matter of appointments/ postings of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers as well as Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees by direct appointment, promotion or deputation.
The appointing authority has been decided as per the Government decision dated 19/11/2016 in the context of the Department. Also final on the matters of appointments/ postings to be made by direct service, promotion and deputation to the posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D
Instructions have been given to announce the designation of the post-wise appointment authorities by the concerned administrative department under a separate order as to who will be the appointing authority for taking decisions. Accordingly, Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training as well as Government Institutions and Offices under them.
The matter of declaring the appointing authority for making appointments/ postings to the posts in direct service, promotion and deputation was under the consideration of the Government.
Govt. Decision: In direct service to the posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training as well as in the government institutions and offices under them.
The appointing authority for appointments/ postings by promotion and deputation is being declared as follows:
