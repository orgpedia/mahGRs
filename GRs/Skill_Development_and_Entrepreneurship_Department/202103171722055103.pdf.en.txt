The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has decided to extend the second and third term of the existing Industrial Training Institutes in 43 minority-dominated areas of the state and a total of 417 temporary posts in Mandvi and Nehrunagar (Kurla) in Brihanmumbai from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.

Preamble: The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has sanctioned 417 temporary posts under the scheme for opening of 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi and Nehrunagar (Kurla) in the second and third shifts and 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi and Kurla in 43 Minority Concentration Areas of the State.
The said temporary posts have been extended till 28.02.2021 as per the Government decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time. Under the Government Decision (2) issued in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education& Training), Directorate of Vocational Education& Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter at (3), the matter of extension of temporary posts from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 was under consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: ITI-2019/ PR. No. 36/ Special-3 GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, hereby approves the continuation of 417 temporary posts (attached in Form A) under the schemes sanctioned for opening of 2 new Industrial Training Institutes at Mandvi and Nehrunagar (Kurla) in 43 Minority Concentration areas of the State in the second and third shifts and 2 new Industrial Training Institutes in Brihanmumbai under the following terms and conditions with effect from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
(4) In case of any question of remuneration of such temporary posts, the responsibility shall be of the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai.
Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the approved grant under the following head for the financial year 2021-2022.
The Director (Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall take care to submit the Budget Estimates, Revised Estimates and Final Revised Grants proposals under the head" Demand No. ZE-1, 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 02 Social Welfare, 200-Other Programmes, Schemes under State Schemes, (00) (16) Subsidiary Grants for starting second and third term in existing Industrial Training Institutes for Minority Students, (2235 A 534) within the prescribed time.
This Government Decision was taken by the Minority Development Department vide Informal Reference No. 09/2020/ S.8D. 8/16/ APC, dated 15.02.2021 is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
