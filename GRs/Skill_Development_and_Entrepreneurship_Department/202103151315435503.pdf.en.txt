Government's stand:
Government Resolution dated 29th May, 2019, Page No. 4, Issue No. 3 of 2 are as follows.
The amount of reimbursement of business training fees of the students will be deposited in the bank account of the Institute Management Committee and Private Industrial Training Institute set up under the PPP scheme in the Government Industrial Training Institute through the concept of Skill Wallet to be developed by your Government MahaDBT Portal or Directorate of Business Education and Training. The respective Institute Management Committee/ Private Industrial Training Institutes will be able to accept the remaining training fee from the students after deducting the permissible amount of business training fee reimbursement from the training fee prescribed by the Government.

Government Resolution dated 29th May, 2019, Page No. 4, Issue No. 3 of 2 are being improved. The amount of reimbursement of the students' business training fees will be given to the beneficiary students in two installments through the concept of Skill Wallet to be developed by your government MahaDBT portal or Directorate of Business Education and Training. The first instalment will be credited to the Aadhaar seeded bank accounts of the beneficiaries by the end of February and the second instalment by the first week of October of the respective academic year. However, before the above said deadline, the Industrial Training Institutes shall not take the amount of reimbursement of training fee admissible to the students under the said scheme from the concerned students in advance at the time of admission and the amount of reimbursement admissible to the eligible beneficiary students as above through DBT shall be paid by them to their Industrial Training Institutes immediately towards the fee and the entire responsibility of this shall be of the concerned student.
