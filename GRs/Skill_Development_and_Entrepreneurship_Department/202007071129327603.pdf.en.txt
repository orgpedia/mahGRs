Introduction: Considering the growth of industrialization, changing technology, social and economic changes, business education and training to meet the need for business education to promote employment and self-employment by creating short-term vocational courses for students who have dropped out of secondary education as well as for students pursuing special education.
Under Directorate Reference No. 1 decision of the government. The Maharashtra State Board of Vocational Education was established on 23 January 1986.
Reference No. General Administration Department, 2. The Department of Employment and Self Employment was renamed as the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship on 15th January, 2015.
Reference No. 3. Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and its subordinate offices, institutions and their related functions in the field of higher and technical education.
It was approved to bring it under the control of the Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
Since this department is working for skill development, employment and self-employment, there is a need to make the Maharashtra State Board of Vocational Education more empowered. A combination of education and skills is needed. The Maharashtra State Board of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSBSHSE) has decided to change the name of the Maharashtra State Board of Vocational Education (MSBVET) to Maharashtra State Skill Development Examination Board (MSSDE).
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Board of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSBSHSE) has decided to change the name of the Maharashtra State Board of Professional Examination (MSBSHE) to Maharashtra State Skill Development Examination Board (MSSDE).
