Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 03/07/2017 of the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, the Maharashtra State Innovation Society has been approved for registration under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and Memorandum of Association (MoA) of the Society.
The Governing Council of Maharashtra State Skill Development Society currently has a total of 11 members with Additional Chief Secretary Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department as ex-officio Chairman and Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship as ex-officio Member Secretary. The government was considering the inclusion of 6 ex-officio officers as" members" in the Governing Council of Maharashtra State Innovation Society- Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary/ Under Secretary, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
Government's decision:
The following officers have been approved to be inducted as" ex-officio members" in the Governing Council of Maharashtra State Innovation Society.
Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Mumbai- Member (03)
Under Secretary, Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Mumbai Member (03)
Accordingly, the Commissioner, Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship has made necessary changes in the Memorandum of Association (MoA) of the Maharashtra State Innovation Society. Necessary action should be taken to get the approval of the Charity Commissioner, Worli, Mumbai.
