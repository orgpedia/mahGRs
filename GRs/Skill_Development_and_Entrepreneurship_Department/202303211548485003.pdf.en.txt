The Proposal:
The MoU was signed between the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India and Dassault Skill Academy, France to develop world-class training and educational initiatives in the country. As per the MoU signed on 03.07.2019, Industrial Training Institute (ITI), Nagpur has been permitted to start three batches of Aeronautical Structure and Equipment Fitter courses from August 2019. It has been approved by the Government Decision dated 20/09/2019. On the same lines, the government was considering to start batches of business courses in Aeronautical Structure and Equipment Fitter at Industrial Training Institute, Aundh-Pune and Industrial Training Institute, Nashik to develop the skills of needy students in Pune and Nashik districts and create employment.
