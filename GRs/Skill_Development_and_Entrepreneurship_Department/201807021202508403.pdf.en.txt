The Proposal:
In order to remove the regional imbalance in the seats of students of pre-professional courses, the government has decided to create a regular pay scale for 132 posts for the increased batch in the newly created and existing Government Technical Secondary School/ Center in the state and to increase the rank of Headmaster (Group-B) in Government Technical School, Mulund.
Approved on 04/5/2011. The temporary posts under these 133 schemes have been extended from 01/03/2012 to 31/05/2018. With a view to continue the said batch in Government Technical Secondary Schools/ Centres, a total of 133 temporary posts under the schemes were under consideration of the Government for extension from 01/06/2018 to 30/09/2018 as per the Government Decision dated 05/03/2018, vide Finance Department Government Resolution No.
