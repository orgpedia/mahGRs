The Proposal:
While presenting the State Budget 2019-20 on 18th June, 2019, Honble Finance Minister said," Financial stress in the lives of widows, widows, divorced women is often severe. It is the social duty to support these women and make them self-reliant. In this regard, the Government intends to make these women self-employed by providing them skill training in selected areas and provide self-employment to such women by providing self-employment skill development training to build their confidence and ability to stand on their own feet. " He announced.
A scheme on Skill Development Training has been proposed to empower widows, widows, divorced women for self-employment. As per the decision taken in the meeting dated 26/09/2019 of the Empowered Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra, the matter of approval to implement the said scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The financial stress in the lives of widows, widowers, and divorced women is often severe. It is the social duty to support these women and make them self-reliant.
In this regard, the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship has launched the" Self-Employment Skills Development for Widows, Divorced and Divorced Women."

Under this decision, the government is approving the implementation of the' Scheme for Providing Self-Employment through Training'.
Objective of the scheme
To provide self-employment to widows, widows and divorced women by providing them self-employment training to build their self-confidence and ability to stand on their own feet.
Providing skilled training to unskilled manpower. It also provides upskilling training to the existing skilled manpower for career advancement.
Implementation and control of the program
The changing national and international scenario in terms of employment has led the state government to work on the lines of the central government to further promote skill development and entrepreneurship. Separate Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department has been established on 15/01/2015. Maharashtra State Skill Development Society has been registered on 15th February, 2011 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 under the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
Various skill development schemes of the central and state governments are implemented by various departments of the state government. As a result, it has been decided to implement all the schemes related to skill development in the state through the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society in order to bring uniformity in it and to control, plan and effectively implement the national and state level policies on skill development, employment and entrepreneurship. The institute is functioning as the" nodal agency" for the skill development scheme. The State Skill and Entrepreneurship Department has launched the" Pramod Mahajan Skill and Entrepreneurship Development Mission (PMKUVA)" under the Government decision dated 02.09.2015 to implement skill development programmes in the State.
The Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS) will be implementing a skill development training scheme to empower widows, widows and divorced women for self-employment.

The categories of women beneficiaries under the scheme are as follows:

Eligibility and Qualification of Candidates
The eligibility and eligibility criteria for the candidates will be as follows:
Age Limit: 18 to 45 years Educational Qualification: The candidate should have passed the qualifying examination.
Resident: The candidate must be a resident of Maharashtra.
Category: Widows/ Divorced/ Divorced Women Proposed Districts: All districts of Maharashtra
Skills training (areas and courses)
Employment/ self-employment training courses will be selected to implement this skill development training programme. The course must be approved by NCVT/ SSC/ MSCVT approved assessment and certification body.
Selection of training institutes
The selection of training institutes for this scheme will be done in the following manner.
The Skill Training Institute must be a NSQF complaint and must be listed with the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society. Also, if the training institute which is not listed under Maharashtra State Skill Development Society has a VTI Id or is listed on S.M.A.R.T (Skill Management and Accreditation of Training Centre) portal, then such institute will be provided the facility to list on MSSDS portal.
The rates of training cost fixed for different training courses for the skill training institutes listed on the web portal of Maharashtra State Skill Development Society will continue to be applicable.
For imparting skill development training, the concerned institution must have the capacity of NSQF compliant, required infrastructure, adequate manpower of the skill training institution.
The training institute will be required to implement training programmes for skill courses that are compliant with the NSQF (National Skill Qualification Framework) and available on the integrated web portal of the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society.

Planning and implementation
Under the scheme, the objectives of the government are to outline the program according to the policies, control the implementation, conduct regular reviews, take various kinds of policy decisions regarding the scheme as per the need. Accordingly, a committee will be formed under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department to make necessary changes, take decisions from time to time regarding various types of policies and make changes as per the need. The committee will comprise of Additional Chief Secretary (Skill Development and Entrepreneurship), Joint Secretary (Skill Development and Entrepreneurship), Chief Executive Officer (Maharashtra State Skill Development Society), Deputy Secretary (Women and Child Welfare Department) and Under Secretary (Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department).
The scheme will be implemented at the state level through Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS) and Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training (MSCVT). For this, it will be mandatory for the concerned departments to make available the information of the trainees who have benefited from the various skill development schemes being implemented by the concerned departments on the web portal of the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship.
(i) Maharashtra State Skill Development Society will promote and disseminate the scheme at the state level through print, electronic media and social media.
ii) Special awareness campaigns will be conducted for each district by the District Skill Development Executive Committee.
(iii) The scheme will also be promoted by the concerned department.
b) Selection and registration of beneficiaries i) Interested widows/ widows/ divorced women are required to apply online for availing the benefits of the scheme. Candidates will be able to choose the right training institute from the listed training institutes while applying online.

Course structure and training.
The structure of the training and the methodology of training under this scheme will be as follows.
However, if an institution does not have sufficient number of women to start a training batch, the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society will have the power to approve the start of the training batch as a special case if the institution is willing to start the training of the available women.
The Training Batch Number (TBN) will be issued to the batch through MSSDS portal after the training institute uploads the list of trainees for a batch for training.
It will be mandatory for the concerned training institution to notify the schedule of the training program within seven days of receiving the TBN through the MSSDS portal.
Every training institute will be required to provide daily biometric attendance report of trainees and trainers to the concerned stakeholders through the portal of Maharashtra State Skill Development Society.
The training must be completed within the stipulated time.
Officials of the District Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department will make surprise visits to the training institutes during the training period to verify the biometrics attendance and related reports.

Responsibility for the implementation of the plan
The implementation of the scheme will be as follows:
The Maharashtra State Skill Development Society will be responsible for providing the facility of" web-based" planning, implementation and monitoring of the scheme to all the stakeholders, ensuring proper utilization of funds and submitting the utilization certificate to the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department within the prescribed time frame, ensuring that the implementation and monitoring of the scheme is done effectively and in a proper manner, and ensuring effective coordination among various stakeholders working at the field level.
b) Maharashtra State Council for Vocational Training will be responsible for providing VTI to the training institutes, assessing the skills of the trainees and providing certificates to the trainees who successfully complete the training.
The District Skill Executive Committee will be responsible for implementing and controlling the scheme at the district level, encouraging and mobilizing beneficiaries to take advantage of the scheme, encouraging skilled manpower to provide employment by providing establishment resources in the service and industrial sectors of the district, suggesting innovative ideas to achieve the objectives of the scheme and submitting proposals to the government accordingly, and ensuring that district level officials are actively involved in the planning, implementation and control of the scheme.
