The Proposal:
Government Industrial Training Institute, Sindkhedraja, G.Buldhana was established by Government Decision, Higher and Technical Education Department, No. ITI-1097/ (713)/ Person-1, D. Established on 04/08/1997, the Institute has been functioning from August, 1997 in rented premises. The Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government under Reference No. 2 for obtaining administrative approval for the construction of Administrative Building and Workshop for Industrial Training Institute, Sindkhedraja, District Buldhana at a cost of Rs. 1493.20 lakh. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government's decision:
The Budget for the construction of Administrative Building and Workshop of the Institute at Buldhana has been prepared by the Deputy Divisional Engineer, Public Works Sub-Division, Deulgaonvaraja, G. Buldhana based on the tariff list for the year 2019-20. This budget was presented by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Buldhana, Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Akola and on behalf of the Consumer Department, Principal, Government Industrial
