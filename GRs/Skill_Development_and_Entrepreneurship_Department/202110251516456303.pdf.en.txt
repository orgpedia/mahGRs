Mr. R.V. He passed away on 11.01.2021. He was given an order from the office of the Regional Office Amravati. As per Rule No.134 (3) of Part I Sub-section-1 of the Financial Rules, 1959 dated 07.03.2008, a sum of Rs. 2,20,000/- (Rs. Two lakh twenty thousand) house construction advance has been provided. After the recovery of the principal amount of the house construction agreement was completed, the regional office Amravati issued an official order. As on 07.10.2020, Rs. 112880/- (Rupees One Lakh Twelve Thousand Eight Hundred only). Accordingly, an amount of Rs. 9,425/- has been recovered. The remaining amount of Rs. 103455/- (Rupees One Lakh Three Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Five only) was paid to Mr. He passed away on 11.01.2021. Hence, the interest on the remaining Rs. 103455/ (Rupees One Lakh Three Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Five only) could not be recovered. A proposal had been sent to the Government for sanction.
In this regard, the proposal was approved by the Finance Department, Government Resolution No. GHABAN-2012/ Q. No. 30/2012/ Regulations D. As per the provision of Para 10 dated 20.02.2015, the Government is approving the waiver of interest on the house building advance of Rs. 103455/- (Rupees One Lakh Three Thousand Four Hundred and Fifty Five only) on account of the death of Mr. Zelle IV class employee.
