The Proposal:
Under Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department, 417 Government Industrial Training Institutes are functioning under Craftsman Training Scheme, 8 institutions under Advanced Vocational Training Scheme, 1 institution under Hi-Tech Training Scheme, 2 institutions under Mechanization Team. Under the Craftsman Training Scheme, 417 Government Industrial Training Institutes train approximately more than 2 lakh apprentices in 102 different vocational courses. Under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, 10,133 employees are working under the schemes of Craftsmen Training Scheme, Apprenticeship Scheme, Advanced Training Scheme, Hi-tech Training Scheme, Mechanization Team.

Government Decision: In view of the above, the training programme for officers and staff of the office under the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training and officers and staff of Industrial Training Institutes is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
Under this scheme, priority should be given to the staff and teaching staff of the Government Industrial Training Institute.
These training programmes should be conducted at the district/ divisional level and at all the facilities available.
The training programme should include up-to-date knowledge of the relevant business curriculum, use of modern audio-visual media, instructional materials, new teaching methods/ technologies, psychological aspects as well as methods of handling trainees, etc.
4. The training to be imparted to the officers should cover aspects such as leadership development, administrative and financial matters, student welfare, good governance and managerial skills.
