Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of Maharashtra has decided to constitute" Departmental Review Committee" for review of services of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers of Maharashtra Education Service Cadre beyond 50/55 years of age/ 30 years of qualifying service and" Departmental Grievance Committee" for consideration of grievances.

Read:- General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. LPL-2017/ No. 21/ K. 10th June, 2019.
Preamble: As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, the procedure and general instructions regarding the review of service of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, shall be decided by the Government in the context of the General Administration Department. It was released on June 10, 2019. Officers of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) (Technical/ Technical) of Maharashtra Education Service Cadre in the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship above the age of 50/55 years or
The Government was contemplating to constitute a" Departmental Review Committee" to review the service on the basis of prescribed criteria to test their eligibility to continue in service after completion of 30 years of qualifying service and a" Departmental Appeal Committee" to consider the grievances against the decision of premature retirement after review of service.
Government's decision:
The Departmental Review Committee is being constituted to review the service of the officers of the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training in the Maharashtra Education Service (Technical/ Technical) Cadre (1) Group-A (Gazetted) (Senior Pay Scale) Officers in the cadre of 7600 and above but below 10000 (2) Group-A (Gazetted) (Junior Pay Scale) Officers in the cadre of 7600 and below and (3) Group-B (Gazetted) Officers beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service, based on the prescribed criteria to test their eligibility to continue their service:
