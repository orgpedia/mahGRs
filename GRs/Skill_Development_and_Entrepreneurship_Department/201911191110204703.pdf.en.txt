The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Skill Development Society (MSSDS) is the nodal agency for the regulation, planning and implementation of skill and entrepreneurship development programmes in the state. Maharashtra State Skill Development Society has been registered on 15/02/2011 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.
As per the Memorandum of Association (MoA) of Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, there are a total of 9 members in the Governing Council, with Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department as ex-officio Chairman and Mission Coordinator, Maharashtra State Skill Development Society as Member Secretary. As per the provisions of the Maharashtra State Skill Development Society Rules and Regulations, the government was considering the inclusion of 6 officers- Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary/ Under Secretary, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department- as" members" in the Governing Council.
Government's decision:
The following officers have been approved to be included as members in the Governing Council of Maharashtra State Skill Development Society.
Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Mumbai Member (3) Under Secretary, Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Mumbai Member (3)
