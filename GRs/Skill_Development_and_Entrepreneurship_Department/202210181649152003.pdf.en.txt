Preamble:- 128 temporary posts have been sanctioned under the scheme of Higher Level Industrial Training Institute for the Children of Scheduled Castes and New Buddhists under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. These temporary posts have been extended till 31.08.2022. Under the Government Resolution (2) issued in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter at (3), the matter of extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- The Government hereby approve the continuation of 128 temporary posts (attached in Form A) from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 under the scheme of Higher Level Industrial Training Institute for the Children of Scheduled Castes and New Buddhists started at Divisional Level under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: The above posts should be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(1) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
