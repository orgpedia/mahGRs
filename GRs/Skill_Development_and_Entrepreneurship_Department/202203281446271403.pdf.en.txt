Letter from Maharashtra State Skill Development Society No. MSSDS/ CSR/ 2021/1320, dated 13th September, 2021. MSSDS/ CSR/ 2021/1919, dated 30th December, 2021 Preamble: Maharashtra is a leading state in the field of industrialization and business in the country. These sectors contribute significantly to the country's GDP. There is an excellent network of industries and businesses in most parts of the state. The Department is engaged in the development of an ecosystem of entrepreneurship and innovation, creating skilled manpower required from the primary sector like agriculture and animal husbandry to the quaternary sector like research and start-ups.
The offices under the department are Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State Skill Development Institute, Maharashtra State Institute of Innovation, Commissionerate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship and Maharashtra State Board of Skill, Vocational Education and Training. Proposals are received from industrial and commercial establishments for development of office infrastructure, capacity building of manpower, implementation of various employability training and knowledge sharing.
As per the Central Government notification dated 27th February, 2014, a policy framework has been laid down for Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and Voluntary Donation to all establishments registered under the Companies Act to invest at least 2% of their profits in social enterprises. In this regard, the government was considering the adoption of the Social Responsibility Fund (CSR) and Voluntary Donation Policy for various activities.

Government Resolution No: KAUVU-2021/ PR No.140/ SKILL-1
Government's decision:
To adopt CSR and Voluntary Donation funds for projects/ activities related to Skill Development, Employment, Innovation, Entrepreneurship, Vocational Education and Training under the Department of Skill Development, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation, and to put in place an integrated policy framework for speedy, transparent and effective implementation of these activities.
The" draft" and" implementation modalities" have been approved under this government decision.
The CSR and Voluntary Donation Policy covers the background, the premise, the expected benefits to the corporate entities, the scope and objectives of the policy, the participation, the permissible aspects through the fund, the structure, the actual implementation, audit, financial powers, monitoring and control, etc.
3. Composition, functions and responsibilities of State Level Skill Development Social Responsibility Fund and Voluntary Contribution Committee in Annexure 1, Composition, powers and responsibilities of cells under Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation Department in Annexure 2 and Industrial Development Committee in Annexure 3.
The rights and responsibilities of the classroom at the training institution level are mentioned.
4. The outcome of the programme will be reviewed from time to time by the" State Level Skill Development Social Responsibility Fund and Voluntary Contribution Committee" as the case may be. Guidelines in this regard will be issued by Maharashtra State Skill Development Society, Mumbai from time to time with the approval of the Head of the Department. Legislative matters arising in connection with this programme shall be dealt with by the respective offices.
