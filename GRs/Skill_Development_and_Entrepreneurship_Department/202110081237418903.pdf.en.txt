Preamble: Guidelines providing for the establishment of self-financing private skill universities in the State and for regulation of their functioning and functioning and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto. The ruling of 1. 05/02/2021. Accordingly, to scrutinize the project reports/ proposals received by the Government for setting up of Self-Financed Private Skill University from various institutions/ Sponsoring Board. The Scrutiny Committee has been constituted under the Government Decision dated 15/03/2021. The matter of sanctioning honorarium and travel allowance to the non-official members of this scrutiny committee was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government have decided to examine the proposals received for the establishment of self-financing private skill universities in the State. The Government approves the grant of honorarium and travel and daily allowance to the non-official members and invited members of the Scrutiny Committee constituted under 15/03/2021 as follows:
(1) The non-official members and invited members of the Scrutiny Committee shall be paid Rs. 5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousand only).
2) Non-official and invited members who will attend the meeting from Mumbai will be paid Rs. Travel allowance of Rs.1000/- (Rupees One Thousand only) and the amount admissible to the two tier AC status of the Railways will be paid to the members who will come from outside Mumbai.
The expenditure for payment of honorarium and travelling allowance to the non-official members and invited members attending the meetings of the said Scrutiny Committee shall be met by deducting the expenditure from the grant sanctioned for the current financial year under the head' Demand No.Z-2, 2251, Secretariat Social Services, (090) Secretariat (01) Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department (22510516), 13 Office Expenditure'.
