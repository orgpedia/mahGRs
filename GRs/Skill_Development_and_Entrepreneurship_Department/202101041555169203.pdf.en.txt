Introduction: The Government of Maharashtra has announced the" Maharashtra State Innovative Startup Policy-2018" in February, 2018 under the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship. For the effective implementation of this policy and study of the startup and innovation ecosystem in the state and
Maharashtra State Innovation Society for Development was established vide Circular No. The decision was taken on July 3, 2017. The Society implements various schemes and activities related to the start-up and innovation sector for all sections of the state. Some of these initiatives are Maharashtra Startup Week- Startup Week is organised every year to give startups an opportunity to work with the government. The winning start-ups will be awarded work orders worth Rs 15 lakh each from the Innovation Society.
Maharashtra Startup and Innovation Yatra- The Yatra is organized by the Maharashtra State Innovation Society with an aim to provide a platform to budding entrepreneurs to realize their ideas and dreams. All ages are welcome to participate.
The winners are provided with facilities like pre-incubation, incubation support, special financial assistance, etc.
Intellectual Property Rights/ Quality Assessment and Financial Assistance Scheme It is important for early stage entrepreneurs to secure the intellectual property rights of their innovative concept as well as to monitor and certify the quality of their product or services. Initiatives have been taken to secure and enable innovative ideas by startups and to finance the costs incurred for quality monitoring and certification.
