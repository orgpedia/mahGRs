Preamble: Under the Scheme of Establishment of New Industrial Training Institutes in 32 newly created blocks under the Craftsmen Training Scheme in the State under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 90 temporary posts under Tribal Appointments out of 576 sanctioned schemes have been approved under the Government decision of Reference (1). Under the scheme of Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education, 414 temporary posts have been approved as per the decision of the Government under reference (2). The said temporary posts have been extended upto 22.02.2022 as per the Government decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time. Provisional from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022 under the Government Resolution (3) under reference to Finance Department
The administrative departments have been given the power to extend the tenure of the posts. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter at (4), the matter of extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: ITI-2019/ PR No. 37/ Special-3 GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, under the newly created 32 Taluks under the Craftsman Training Scheme in the State, has approved the continuation of 90 temporary posts under the sanctioned schemes for the establishment of new Industrial Training Institutes and 414 temporary posts under the scheme of Universalization of Tribal Employment Oriented Vocational Education (Form A& B attached) from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022, subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
(4) In case of any question of remuneration of such temporary posts, the responsibility shall be of the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the approved grant under the following head for the financial year 2021-2022 and 2022-2023.
" Demand No. T-9, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, 2 Welfare of Scheduled Tribes, 796, Janjati Area Sub-Plan, 31 Schemes under Janjati Area Appropriation, Tribal Component Programme.
The Director (Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall ensure that the Budget Estimates, Revised Estimates and Final Revised Grant Proposals under the above headings are submitted within the prescribed time.
