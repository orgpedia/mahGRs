Preamble: As many as 417 temporary posts have been sanctioned under the scheme for opening of 2 new O.P. Institutes, Mandvi and Nehru Nagar (Kurla) in Greater Mumbai and 2 second/ third shift in existing Industrial Training Institutes in 43 minority dominated areas of the State under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. As per the Government Decision issued by the Finance Department from time to time, the above Reference No. (3) The said 417 temporary posts have been extended till 28.02.2019 as per the decision of the Government. Under the Government Decision (4) in the context of the Finance Department, the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 30.09.2019. Accordingly, as per the proposal submitted by the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide letter dated 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019, extension of temporary posts was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: ITI-2019/ PR No.36/ Person-3
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, 417 temporary posts under the scheme in the accompanying Form A approved for the opening of 2 new O.P. Institutes, Mandvi and Nehru Nagar (Kurla) in the second/ third shift and 2 new O.P. Institutes in Brihanmumbai in the existing Industrial Training Institute in 43 minority dominated areas of the State under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Maharashtra State, Mumbai are to be continued from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 subject to the following conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The above posts shall be continued for the purpose for which they were created. The employees in the existing temporary posts should be considered eligible for salary, allowances etc. as per rules.
(2) It shall be ensured that none of the posts for which extension has been granted remains vacant for a period of more than six months.
3) The approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been taken for the review of the sanctioned posts during this period. However, it was later approved. In such cases, the process of approving the design of the posts along with the evaluation of the newly created posts should be completed within the prescribed period.
4) The revised pattern after reviewing the posts needs to be approved by a high-level committee of secretaries. In case of non-payment of salary, the Director (Vocational Education and Training), Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai will be responsible for the same. Please note that after this period, there will be no extension of time for fixing the figure.
The expenditure on this proposal shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the following heads:
" Minority Development Department- Demand No. ZE-1, 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 200-Other Programmes, Schemes under State Schemes, (00) (16) Subsidiary Grants for starting second and third shifts in existing Industrial Training Institutes for Minority Students (2235 A 534)
This decision was taken by the Government in Informal Reference No. 11/ k-8, d. 7/3/2019, also being issued by the Finance Department, pursuant to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide Government Resolution No: 2016/ Q. No. 8/16/ A.P.C., dated 21st February, 2019.
