Government circular
The provisional seniority of the officers of the cadre of Assistant Director, Group-A in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System, dated 01/01/2017, was issued in the context of the Government Circular dated 14th July, 2017. Accordingly, after considering the representations received, the final seniority of the officers of the cadre of Assistant Director, Group-A in the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship System, as on 01/01/2017, is being published vide this circular.
The Commissioner, Directorate of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship is advised to bring the final seniority list to the notice of all the concerned officers.
