Preamble- The Central Government has focused on career counseling of the emerging youth power so that they acquire the best skills and choose the right career for the future progress of the youth of the country. The National Career Service (NCS) scheme has been approved by the Directorate General of Employment (DGE) and the Ministry of Labour and Employment.
Under this scheme, various employment related services are provided. Under this, a comprehensive" Model Career Center" is proposed to be set up in every state by the central government. Through this" Model Career Center," skill-based job opportunities/ demand will be explored and youth trainees will be guided on training and employment opportunities as per their aptitude and ability, and the concerned will be brought in contact with employers for employment. Under this scheme, the Central Government has decided to convert at least one Employment Guidance Center in every state into a" Model Career Center" and a Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Center has been selected in Yavatmal district of Maharashtra.
Following that. It has also been approved by the Government vide Government Resolution dated 20.1.2016. Now the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India, Directorate General of Employment, Reference No. 2 is mentioned here. As per the letter dated 22.10.2018, the Central Government has released Rs. 43,95,700/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakh Ninety Five Thousand Seven Hundred only) has been sanctioned and out of which Rs. 26,37,420/- (Rs. Twenty-five thousand four hundred and twenty only) grants have been distributed. In pursuance of this decision of the Central Government, the matter of granting administrative approval to convert the District Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Center, Osmanabad into a" Model Career Center" was under consideration of the Government and the decision is being taken accordingly.
The Directorate General of Employment (DGE), Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India, has decided to convert the District Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Centers in Osmanabad district of the state into" Model Career Centers" as per the approval of the Central Government under the" National Career Service" scheme. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an amount of Rs. 43,95,700/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakh Ninety Five Thousand Seven Hundred only).
