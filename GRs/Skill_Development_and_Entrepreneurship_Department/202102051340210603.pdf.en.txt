Preamble: The National Policy on Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, 2015 has been released by the Government of India, which envisages skill training as a key component to address the mismatch between employment opportunities and supply. Also, the youth of the state need new-age skill education to become employable.
The Cabinet has approved the setting up of Skill University in the meeting of the Council of Ministers on the theme of" Skill Maharashtra RozgarYukt Maharashtra" on the lines of Skill India. The government has taken the decision. It was decided to take action to set up a Skill Development University in the state by September 2, 2015. In accordance with this decision. As per the Government decision dated 20th February, 2020, a Committee under the Chairmanship of Commissioner, Skill Development was appointed to draft Acts for setting up Skill Universities in the State on funded/ self-funded basis. The committee comprised former vice-chancellors of universities and principals of reputed colleges. The report submitted by this committee as well as the Higher Technical Education Department issued for the establishment of self-financing universities
The government has taken a decision. In view of the revised guidelines dated 19th January, 2019, the Government was contemplating to issue guidelines for establishment of permanent self-financing skill universities in the State, regulation of their functioning and functioning and for related matters.
Government decision: As per the decision taken in the Cabinet meeting held on January 13, 2021, to establish private skill universities in the state on a permanent self-financing basis, to regulate their functioning and functioning and to provide for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
