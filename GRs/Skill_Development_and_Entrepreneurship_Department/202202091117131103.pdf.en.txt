The Proposal:
The main objectives of the scheme are to provide skilled manpower for various industrial activities through the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the Department of Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship, to empower the youth to get employment in factories by providing employment oriented training, to empower the youth to start self-employment in manufacturing and other service sectors by providing skill training, to enhance the quality, quality and production of industrial establishments by providing systematic training to the workers.
2. Trainees in Industrial Training Institutes, while undergoing training, are enabled to get employment by providing job oriented training. Through training, trainees can easily gain insight into their skills, activities, social knowledge and production methods. Increased confidence and a sense of responsibility to work collaboratively on their own or with others
can be done. Through the Shilpakari Yojana, the hands-on trainees are provided with weapons and equipment of their choice, books as per requirement.
So that the trainee will be able to employ himself and self-employed after passing out.
3. Administrative approval for implementation of the scheme for filling up the shortage of machinery in Industrial Training Institutes for the trainees belonging to Scheduled Castes (including neo-Buddhists) under the Scheduled Castes Component Programme in Industrial Training Institutes under the Industrial Craftsmen Training Scheme to meet the budgetary provisions and expenditure of the said provision under the District Level Scheme under the Labour and Employment (OPE) Development Sector under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training.

The matter was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Decision of the Government: Administrative approval is being given to implement the Shilpakartis Training Scheme, to meet the shortage of machinery in the Industrial Training Institutes for the trainees belonging to the Scheduled Castes (including the neo-Buddhists) under the Scheduled Castes Component Programme under the Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, under the Labour and Employment (O& S) Development Sector, to meet the budgetary provisions and the funds received every year.
The guidelines for the scheme are as follows:
As the scheme is a district-level scheme, the District Vocational Education and Training Officer concerned should ensure that the demand for fixed expenditure under the Head Account Title 2225 Labour and Service Scheme under the demand program of the concerned district and the proposal to be made in the budget of the fund every year should be sent to the District Planning Committee.
(2) The scheme is a district level scheme for vocational education and training.
The officials should request for funds from the District Planning Committee as per the guidelines issued by the Planning Department from time to time.
The Director, Vocational Education and Training, as submitted by the Joint Director, Regional Office, shall examine and give technical approval to the amount and list of machinery to be purchased from the funds available under this scheme, in accordance with the Budget Rules and the Financial Powers Manual.
4. To implement this scheme, according to the reservation provided by the Constitution, the apprentices of the Scheduled Castes category will be trained according to the vocational course.
The list of admitted trainees and the demand for necessary funds should be submitted by the head of the institution to the District Vocational Education and Training Officer within the stipulated time.
5. To meet the shortage of machinery in the Industrial Training Institutes The procurement under this scheme shall be made by the Government as issued from time to time.
Decisions should be made according to the guidelines.
Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai shall ensure that the procurement made under the scheme does not exceed the requirement of equipment in the Industrial Training Institute.
