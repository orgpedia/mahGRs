The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to be appointed to posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts notified under the Notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published under the heading List of posts identification suitable for person with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021 in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification on the website of the Central Government www.disabilityyaffairs.gov.in.
Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government Decision dated 7th October, 2016, instructions have been issued to ensure that the posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In accordance with the directions of the said Government decision, the Government was considering to ensure posts in Government service for the disabled for the post of" peon" in Group-D cadre on the establishment of the Skill Development, Employment and Entrepreneurship Department (itself) and to ensure these posts for the newly disabled category by reviewing the list of seats in Government service ensured for the disabled after a period of not more than 3 years.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi vide its notification dated 04.01.2021, has notified the list of posts for persons with disabilities to be filled up under the State Governments as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The Central Government has notified the posts in Group A to Group D which are in the state government establishments.
