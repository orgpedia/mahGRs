The Proposal -
Various competitive examinations are conducted annually by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC). Although a large number of candidates qualify in these examinations, only a few are recommended for final selection as the number of posts available is limited. As a result, many meritorious and competent candidates do not have the requisite educational qualifications, analytical ability developed through competitive examinations, leadership qualities, and public service mindset, but do not have the expected employment opportunities. The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has developed a "Pratibha Setu" portal for candidates who have qualified the interview round but did not make it to the final merit list. Similarly, the knowledge and skills of the meritorious candidates of the state who have reached the final stage (interview / skill test) of various examinations of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) but have not made it to the final selection list are required to be leveraged for the economic and industrial development of the state. With this view, the Government was contemplating to launch an ambitious initiative "Nipun Setu" under the one-window Maitri 2.0 portal developed by the Department of Industries to make the details of candidates available to the public as well as to prospective employers and entrepreneurs, especially in the private sector in Maharashtra, on a single platform. In this regard, the Cabinet of Ministers, Shri. As per the decision taken in the meeting on 13.04.2026, the following Government Decision is being issued.
