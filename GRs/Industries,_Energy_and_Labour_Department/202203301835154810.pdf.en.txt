Preamble: The Maharashtra State Industrial Complex Development Programme (MSICDP) has been announced vide Government Resolution dated 25/02/2014 for the development of clusters of small clusters in industrially underdeveloped areas of the State thereby promoting holistic development of micro and small units. Under the scheme, the state government provides grant-in-aid for the implementation of capacity building programmes for the overall development and growth of micro and small enterprises as well as for the construction of common facilities. Under this scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared by properly diagnosing the shortcomings in the production process of the industrial group, including the latest technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial groups, updated training programs and study tours, etc. After receiving the Diagnostic Study Report on the recommendation of the District Level Committee, the State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee approved the Diagnostic Study Report proposals and fixed the grant for the capacity building programme under it

The grant for the capacity building programme is disbursed in two phases. The first tranche of 60% subsidy to be disbursed by the Government is disbursed after ensuring the required 10% participation of the stakeholders. The remaining 40% subsidy is disbursed after submission of first stage utilization certificate. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse the second phase grant of Rs. 25.94 lakh to the State Government for the capacity building programme of 10 industrial cluster projects proposed by the Directorate of Industries vide letter dated 22/03/2022.
Government Decision: The Directorate of Industries has issued a proposal regarding the release of Rs. 25.94 lakh as the second phase grant of the State Government for the capacity building programme for 10 industrial cluster projects approved under the Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSI-CDP) of the State Government. 9 submitted vide letter dated 22/03/2022. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the said Scheme, the Government have approved the release of second tranche grant of Rs. 25.94 lakh (Rupees Twenty Five Lakh Four Thousand Nine Thousand only) for the capacity building programme for the 10 industrial cluster projects listed below.
