Preamble: The Government Public Information Officer and Government First Appellate Officer were designated under the Right to Information Act, 2005, in respect of all offices in the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, Government of India vide Government Order dated 25/07/2016.
However, the Assistant Public Information Officer, Public Information Officer and First Appellate Authority in the office of Principal Secretary (Industries/ Power/ Labour) were not designated under the said Government Order. Accordingly, it is proposed to declare Assistant Public Information Officers, Public Information Officers and First Appellate Authorities for these offices.
