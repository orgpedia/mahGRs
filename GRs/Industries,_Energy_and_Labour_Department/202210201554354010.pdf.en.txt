The Election Commission of India (ECI) conducts a special summary revision programme every year to verify and correct the existing electoral rolls. The programme of updating the electoral roll by registering new voters as per the eligibility date of January 1 is implemented. Special Summary Revision Programme, to be launched in 2023. The programme is being organized to revise the electoral roll by registering new voters who will complete 18 years of age on January 1, 2023.
From 2023, citizens who complete 18 years of age on or before the four qualifying dates of January 1, April 1, July 1 and October 1 will be able to register in advance in the electoral roll. Under this special brief revision programme, The draft electoral rolls of all the Assembly Constituencies of the State will be published on 09/11/2022. 09/11/2022 to D. Claims and objections will be accepted from 08/12/2022. Applications can be submitted after 09/11/2022).
All industry groups, companies and institutions in the state under the Special Summary Revision Program, 2023. The Additional Chief Secretary and Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra State have been requested to take up the initiative of enrolling all employees who complete 18 years of age by October 1, 2023 as voters in the electoral roll.
The following activities should be carried out by all the industrial groups, companies and institutions of the state in accordance with the special summary revision program of the electoral roll.
