Preamble: The 150th Birth Anniversary of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, aka Father of the Nation, is being celebrated by the Central and State Governments from 2018-19 to 2020-21. The festival is being held from 2nd October 2018.
Accordingly, under the Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme, a grand exhibition of Mahakhadi and Gramodyog products will be held at the Trimurti premises of the Ministry. It was inaugurated by the Chief Minister on 3rd October, 2018. The exhibition is being held from 3rd to 6th October, 2018. In the exhibition, The Chief Minister also released a book titled" Gandhi Ek Vichar" published by Khadi and Village Industries Board, Maharashtra. The book is available at the headquarters of Khadi and Village Industries Board, 19/21, Manohardas Marg, Fort Mumbai-1.

The government has decided to issue the following circular:
Government Circular: The thoughts of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, aka Mahatma Gandhi, the Father of the Nation, are his true reputation. Therefore, if the 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi is to be celebrated in the true sense, it is necessary to inculcate his thoughts in the minds of the general public. Accordingly, in order to disseminate and propagate the thoughts of Mahatma Gandhi in the book" Gandhi Ek Vichaar," this booklet will be published in the daily correspondence at the government level.
