Preamble: The Central Government has enacted the Unorganized Workers Social Security Act, 2008 on 31/12/2008 for providing social security to the unorganized workers. The Central Government has also decided to implement the Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana (PM-SYM) as a pension scheme with the objective of providing social security to the workers of various 127 business groups on the Central Government's website https:// labour. gov. in/ pm-symya. The registration of the scheme has been started from 15/2/2019. The Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India has fixed the procedure for registration of Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana. The central government has given a target to the state government to register as many workers as possible by giving wide publicity to this scheme through various media. In this regard, the matter of announcing a nodal officer for the effective implementation of the scheme was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Development Commissioner (Unorganized Workers) as Nodal Officer and Assistant Commissioner of Labour (Unorganized Workers) as M.S. Appointments are being made for providing information regarding the e-Samiksha Portal of the Chief Secretary's Office and other matters. They have to submit a report on the action taken on the implementation of the scheme to the state government every month.
