Preamble: The matter specified in column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2020, vide Circular No. 1 of Reference No. 1, has been approved by the Legislature and the Hon'ble Governor of Maharashtra has also given his assent. This Act is known as Maharashtra Act No. 15 Therefore, in the Maharashtra Extraordinary Gazette, Part IV, It was released on March 18, 2020. The circular has suggested that budget estimates in the range of 15 to 25 per cent of the total annual outlay by the end of June, 2020, should be made on the Financial Intended Distribution System (BEMS).
In pursuance of the said circular, the Budget Branch of this Department has disbursed grants to the tune of Rs. 17,44,01, 400/- and Rs. 27,60,900/- respectively under the head Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (2851 0585) under the sub-heading 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaries) within the limit of 25%. Out of this provision, a grant of Rs. 5,81,33, 000/-and Rs. 27 lakh respectively has been released to the Board for meeting the salary, superannuation salary and non-salary expenses for the month of April, 2020, as per the Government Order, Reference No.2.
The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, vide its letters of reference No. 3 and 4, has requested for grant of Rs. 11,62,66, 000/- for meeting the expenditure on salary, retirement salary for two months of May and June, 2020 and Rs. 46,00,000/- for 31 ancillary grants (non-salary).
The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board was under the consideration of the government to provide subsidy to meet the salary, retirement salary and other non-salary expenses for the month of May and June, 2020.

Government's decision:
Under this decision, the Government have approved the release of grant-in-aid to Khadi and Village Industries Board amounting to Rs. 11, 63,26,900/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Sixty Three Lakh Six Hundred and Twenty-Six Thousand Nine Hundred only) for the purpose of payment of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and Rs. 60,900/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Sixty Three Lakh Sixty-Nine Thousand Nine Hundred only) for the purpose of payment of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaries) to Khadi and Village Industries Board (KVIC) to meet the expenditure on salary, retirement salary and contribution for the two months of May and June, 2020.
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2020-21 under the targets of Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (2851 0585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaries) and should be spent under the same head.
The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government and the Drawing and Disbursing Officers within the prescribed time. Also, when the accountant general, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai, is asked about the expenditure of the accounts, the board should submit the necessary documents related to the account to the office of the accountant general.
