PREAMBLE
The second edition of News9 Global Summit conducted by TV9 Network held during the period of 8" October, 2025 to 11 October, 2025 at Stuttgart, Germany.
According to the invitation received from TV9 Marathi the summit bringing together
Industry leaders, policymakers, visionaries and cultural icons from both India and Germany. The summit will be a celebration for the 25 "years of India-Germany strategic partnership. The theme for this year's summit is Democracy / Demography.
Development: The India-Germany Connect.
Government of Maharashtra's participation in the summit will underscore its
existing partnership with Baden-Wurttemberg. The summit will provide an
opportunity to further strengthen these ties by exploring new avenues in advanced
Manufacturing, green mobility and digital innovation. Both regions will benefit from leveraging each other's strengths to foster economic growth, promote technology transfer and create sustainable employment opportunities. The invitation for the
summit also mentions that there will be a dedicated zone where delegates can
experience Maharashtra as a land of opportunities, innovation and culture, while
Reinforcing its position as India's financial capital. The event will provide a platform
for further strengthening business linkages between Germany and Maharashtra.
Hon'ble Chief Minister of Maharashtra has accepted the invitation and
Approved the following delegation under the chairmanship of Hon'ble Dr. Uday Samant, Minister (Industries) to participate in the Summit for the period of 7 October, 2025 to 12 October 2025 (including travel time).
RESOLUTION:-
Government of Maharashtra is now pleased to accord ex-post facto approval to
the visit of the below mentioned State level delegation under the chairmanship of
Hon'ble Dr. Uday Samant, Minister (Industries) to attend "News9 Global Summit" at
Stuttgart, Germany for the period of 7th to 12th October, 2025 (Including travel time).
