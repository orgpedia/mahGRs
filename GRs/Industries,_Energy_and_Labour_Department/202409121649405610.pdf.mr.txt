During the visit 10 members of delegation will attended the "World Economic Forum
Annual Meeting 2024" at Davos-Switzerland organized by World Economic Forum (WEF).
2. The Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi vide their letter dated
19/12/2023 & Q1/01/2024 have approved the visit of delegation of Maharashtra to Davos-,
Switzerland.
3. All the members of the official delegation will be entitled for travel expenses and per diem
allowance as per prescribed norms of Government of India.
4. State Government is further pleased to authorize the absence of Shri Bhushan Gagrani,
Additional Chief Secretary to Hon, Chief Minister from 15/01/2024 to 19/01/2024 (Excluding travel
time), Shri Briyesh Singh, Principal Secretary to Hon, Chief Minister from 15/01/2024 to 19/01/2024
(Excluding travel time), Dr.Harshdeep Kamble, Principal Secretary (Industries) from 15/01/2024 to
19/01/2024 (Excluding travel time), Dr. Vipin Sharma, Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra
Industrial Development Corporation, Mumbai from 15/01/2024 to 19/01/2024 (Excluding travel
time), Shri Vikas Suryawanshi, General Manager (Marketing), Maharashtra Industrial Development
Corporation, Mumbai, Dr Rajesh Kawale, Officer on Special Duty to Hon. Chief Minister from
15/01/2024 to 19/01/2024 (Excluding travel time), Shri Ajay Patne, Officer on Special Duty to Hon.
Minister of Industries from 15/01/2024 to 19/01/2024 (Excluding travel time), Shri Jayraj
Suryawanshi, Officer on Special Duty to Hon. Chief Minister from 15/01/2024 to 19/01/2024 (Excluding travel time) participating as part of delegation to attend " World Economic Forum
Annual Meeting 2024" to be treated as on duty for the above mentioned period until they resume
their duties on return to India from the foreign tour.
5. State Government is pleased to ex-post- facto sanction the processing fees for visa for
Davos-Switzerland, as per actual and medical facilities admissible, under the revised AMA Scheme
continued in the Ministry of External Affairs O. M. No. Q/GA/653/ 1/74 dated 5/3/1979 to them.
The Government is also pleased to post facto sanction the charges for medical insurance for them.
6. The entire expenditure on travel and accommodation etc. for the members of delegation to
be borne by the Maharashtra Industrial development Corporation.
7. The detailed report for the tour and outcomes of the said tour with suggestions if any should
be submitted to the government within one month from the date of returning.
