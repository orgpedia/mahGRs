2. As the Budget Division of this Department has provided subsidy under the head Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (28510585) under the sub-heading 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the extent of 90%, a subsidy of Rs. 5,81,33, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore Eighty One Lakh Thirty Three Thousand only) has been disbursed to meet the expenses of salary, retirement salary, etc. for the month of January, 2021 under the Government decision dated 22nd February, 2021.
3. The Government was considering providing subsidy to meet the post-salary expenses of Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board.
Government's decision:
31 Grants-in-aid (non-salary) to Khadi and Village Industries Board to meet the non-salary expenditure of Rs. 41,41,350/- (Rupees Forty One Lakh Forty One Thousand Three Hundred and Fifty only) is being allowed under this Government decision.
Expenditure on this should be met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2020-21 under the target Demand No. K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (28510585) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) and should be spent under the same head.
