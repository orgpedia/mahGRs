In line with the above, it is informed that the post of Chief Electrical Inspector has been created afresh under the above Government decision. The additional charge of the post has been given to an officer of the rank of Superintending Engineer.
Sandeep Patil is working as Chief Engineer (Power), Public Works Department and in view of his administrative experience, he is being given the additional charge of the post of Chief Electrical Inspector equivalent to this post.
Suhas Bagade, Superintending Engineer, Industries, Energy and Labour Department, Mumbai, has been posted as Superintending Engineer, Industries, Energy and Labour Department, Mumbai in place of Sandeep Patil, Chief Engineer (Electrical), Public Works Department, Mumbai, who has been posted as Chief Electrical Inspector.
