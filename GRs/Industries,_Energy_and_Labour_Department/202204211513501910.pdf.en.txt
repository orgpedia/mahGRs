Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation has submitted a total budget of Rs. 4,49,00, 000/- for the activities of the organization. Project Maps and Budget for Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation
Ramgopal Gupta Co-operative Industrial Estate, Shivaji Nagar, College Road, G.Nanded, has transferred 25% of his shareholding to the executing agency.
Affidavit for deposit, undertaking for maintenance repair and undertaking for opening the road for general public has also been signed with Directorate of Industries on 22/02/2022. Considering all these aspects, administrative approval is being accorded for the maps and budget for the works of the Institute with a total outlay of Rs. 4,49,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Nineteen Fifty Lakhs only).
The increase in the project cost will not be acceptable to the organization. Additional expenses should be borne by the institution.
The said administrative approval is in accordance with the approval given by the Steering Committee in its 8th meeting held on 24/02/2022 as well as the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor Government Decision No. Aupasu-2012/ No.234/ Industries-2, Dy. dated 25/02/2014. The powers conferred under section 6 are being exercised.
