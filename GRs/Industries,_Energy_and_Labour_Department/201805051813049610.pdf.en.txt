Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government dated 03rd May, 2018, the process of supplying 12 hours of 3 phase additional power to the Krishi Pampas has been approved to prevent the loss of crops in the state in the year 2016 and the additional cost incurred by MSEDCL for the supply of this power is Rs. 1154.03 crore has been decided to be reimbursed to MSEDCL as subsidy. Accordingly, the government is approving the reimbursement of this amount to the MSEDCL as a subsidy for the electricity tariff concession of the agricultural pump. Further, the cost of subsidy for the electricity tariff concession, the demand of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor No. K-6,800, Other Expenditure (00) (00) (01) Financial assistance to Power Distribution/ Transmission licensees for reduction of agricultural and machinery tariff (Programme) 33, under the head Financial Assistance (28012615), as approved and available in the Budget 2018-19.
