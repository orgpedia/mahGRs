The Directorate General of Industries has certified that 25% of the shareholding of Marya, Sinner, G. Nashik has already been received by the executing agency. Sinner Taluka Co-operative Industrial Estate Marriyya, Sinner has also signed an agreement with the Directorate of Industries on 11/03/2022 for an affidavit, an undertaking regarding maintenance repairs and an undertaking that the road will be opened to the general public. Considering all these aspects, administrative approval is being accorded for the maps and budget for the works of the Institute with a total outlay of Rs. 99,56,200/- (Rupees Ninety Nine Lakhs Ninety Two Thousand Two Hundred Only).
The increase in the project cost will not be acceptable to the organization. Additional expenses should be borne by the institution.
