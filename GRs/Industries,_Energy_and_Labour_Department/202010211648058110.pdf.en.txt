The Proposal:
On 12.10.2020, power supply in Mumbai, Mumbai Suburban, Thane and Navi Mumbai area and suburban rail was disrupted for about two to two and a half hours. The government was considering setting up a technical audit committee to study the reasons for the power outage and the measures to be taken to prevent such incidents in the future.
The government has taken the following decisions: :
Government decision: It has been decided to constitute a Technical/ Audit Committee to conduct an inquiry/ technical audit into the power outage in Mumbai, Mumbai Suburban, Thane and Navi Mumbai areas on October 12, 2020. The composition of the committee will be as follows:
