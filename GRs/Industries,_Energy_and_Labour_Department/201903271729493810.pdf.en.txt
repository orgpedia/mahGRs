The Proposal:
The Government of India has approved the utilization of the incentive grant of Rs. 961.03 crore received from the Central Government for development of New and Renewable Energy as per the recommendations of the Thirteenth Finance Commission vide Government Resolution dated 15.10.2016. Out of this, Rs.300 crore has been disbursed in 2016-17 and Rs.401 crore in 2017-18. A sum of Rs. 259.53 crore has been provided in the Budget Estimates 2018-19. The High Court has asked for the provision to be removed. As of October 15, 2016
The proposal submitted for reallocation of Rs. 274.53 crore of the sanctioned schemes under the decision was approved by the Hon. The meeting chaired by Chief Secretary No. 5 has been approved.
For the year 2018-19, an amount of Rs. 259.53 crore has been provided in the budget for this programme, as against Rs. 207.6240 crore provided in the revised estimates. Accordingly, the Government was considering to approve reallocation of Rs. 274.53 crore and release of Rs. 207.6240 crore.
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the recommendations of the Thirteenth Finance Commission, the Government of India has approved the utilization of the incentive grant of Rs. 961.03 crore for development of new and renewable energy. Approval of the decision
