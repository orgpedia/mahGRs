The Proposal:
For the purposes of Financial Authority and other financial rules as per Rule 2 (10) of the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959, the Secretary to Government has been declared as the Head of Department for all the Ministries/ Departments in Annexure-II as per Rule 9 (23) of the Bombay Civil Services Rules, 1959.
Thereafter, the Government may, from time to time, declare the Head of Department, Head of Regional Office, as the Head of Office in accordance with Rule 2 (10) and (10-A) of the said Rules and the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I, Roll No. 1,2,3.
As mentioned above, the matter of declaring the concerned under the Industries, Energy and Labour Department as" Administrative Head of Department" for the purpose of Financial Authority of the wards under them was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The following officers are hereby approved to be declared as" Head of Administrative Department" for the purpose of Financial Authority of the wards under the respective Secretaries under the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour on the following conditions:

1. The said officers in the capacity of" Head of the Administrative Department" in respect of their official functions shall, as required, depose before the Public Accounts Committee, as well as other Committees of the Legislatures, Courts, Constitutional or Statutory Commissions, Authorities, etc. and deal with the matters concerned accordingly.
They shall be fully responsible for all matters and matters entrusted to them in respect of financial matters.
As the head of the administrative department, he will be responsible for the responsibility and action to be taken in case of financial irregularities and similar matters in the department.
Subject to the prescribed limits, terms and conditions of the Financial Powers in the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Divisions 1 to 5, the said Administrative Head shall have powers under the provisions of the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959, the Maharashtra Contingent Expenditure Rules, 1965, the Maharashtra Budget Rule Book, the Composite Matters of Expenditure and the Maharashtra Public Works Rules.
(2) The said Government Decision is issued in accordance with the authority vested in the Administrative Department.
