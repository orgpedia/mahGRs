Madam Kama Marg, Martyr Rajguru Chowk,

Government Decision-M. As envisioned by the Prime Minister, the World Audio-Visual & Entertainment Summit (WAVES) 2025 will be held in New Delhi. From May 1, 2025. It was held on 4 May 2025 at Jio World Convention Centre, Mumbai.
In line with WAVES-20R4. The meeting was chaired by the Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra. The meeting was held on 07/03/2025. While a proposal was submitted to the Department of Finance for the creation of a new separate title of accounts for the schedule of the event as per the minutes of these meetings, the Department of Finance has approved the proposal.
Accordingly, the Government is hereby approving the opening of a new separate title for the budgetary allocation for hosting the World Audio-Visual & Entertainment Summit (WAVES) 2025 event.
