The Proposal:
Municipal Corporations, Municipalities, Zilla Parishads and Gram Panchayats in the State
The water supply schemes and street lights have been provided electricity connections by the MSEDCL and the payment of arrears of electricity connections and electricity payments is to be made regularly by the respective offices.
However, due to non-payment of electricity dues by the concerned offices on time, the total outstanding amount at the end of September 2017 is Rs. 4538.47 crore.
Discussing the proposal of the Department of Energy in the Cabinet Meeting held on 21.11.2017
The decision was taken as follows:
" The penalty and interest on the electricity bill due to the municipal corporation, municipality and gram panchayat are the same.
Instead, a proposal should be made to provide 50% of the principal amount from the 14th Finance Commission funds.
In pursuance of the above decision, MSEDCL had submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 02.01.2018. In pursuance of the above proposal, the Cabinet in its meeting held on 08.03.2018 had submitted its comments. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Urban Development Department should transfer 50% of the outstanding bills of MSEDCL on Public Water Supply Schemes and Street Lights in Municipal Corporations, Municipalities and Nagar Panchayats till 31st March, 2018 (after deducting penalty and interest) to MSEDCL from the Grants-in-aid of the Finance Commission and the remaining 50% should be recovered by MSEDCL from the concerned Municipal Corporation/ Municipality/ Nagar Panchayat. If necessary, the MSEDCL should make easy installments to pay the amount.
The Rural Development Department should pay 50% of the amount of electricity bill (minus penalty and interest) due to the Zilla Parishad/ Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran/ Gram Panchayat till March 31, 2018, directly to the MSEDCL from the fund of the Finance Commission. The remaining 50% of the amount should be paid by the concerned Zilla Parishad/ Maharashtra Life Authority/ Gram Panchayat to the MSEDCL. For this, the MSEDCL should plan easy installments of the amount of electricity bill required.
