Preamble: In order to promote the development of small clusters in the industrially underdeveloped areas of the State and thereby promote the overall development of micro and small units, the Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP) has been announced vide Government Resolution dated 25/02/2014. Under the scheme, the state government provides grant-in-aid for the implementation of capacity building programmes for the overall development and growth of micro and small enterprises as well as for the construction of common facilities.
Under the MSICDP scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared by properly diagnosing the flaws in the production process of the industrial group, including the latest technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial groups, updated training programs and study tours, etc. After the diagnostic study report is received on the recommendation of the district level committee, the State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee approves the diagnostic study report proposal and determines the subsidy for the capacity building programme. A Detailed Project Report (DPR) containing details of high quality common facilities and other necessary clearances, no-objection certificates, technical and financial evaluation reports are prepared to enhance the productivity and competitiveness of MSME units. The procurement process is initiated after the final approval of the State Level Steering Committee for the proposal of the Common Facility Centre.
As per the revised guidelines of MSICDP Scheme dated 24/07/2019, after final approval of the proposal of Common Facility Centre by the State Level Steering Committee and completion of the process of redeployment by the Zonal Committee, 20% of the first phase subsidy is disbursed to these projects after receiving the report along with the procurement details. Thereafter, after receiving the physical verification report from the General Manager, District Industries Centre regarding the installation of machinery and equipment at the work site as per the project approval and after receiving the Utilization Certificate of the first phase grant, 60% subsidy in the second phase will be given.

As per proposal dated 24/03/2023 of the Directorate of Industries, the State Level Steering Committee for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Solar Panel Cluster G. Government's decision:
The Directorate of Industries has submitted a proposal for the final disbursement of the state government's grant for the construction of a common facility center of the solar panel cluster G. Latur, which has been approved under the" Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Program (MSI-CDP)" scheme of the state government. 5 is presented here. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the present scheme, the final grant of the State Government amounting to Rs. 1,02,19, 800/- (Rupees One Crore Two Lakh Nineteen Thousand Eight Hundred only) is being approved under this Government decision.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall arrange to transfer the amount of Rs. 1,02,19, 800/- from the Government treasury to the group project of Solar Panel Cluster G. Latur. The powers to draw and disburse this amount are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
