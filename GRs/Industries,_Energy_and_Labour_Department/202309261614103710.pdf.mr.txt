PREAMBLE
Dr. Fukahori Yasukata, Consul-General, Consulate of Japan in Mumbai has invited Hon.
Deputy Chief Minister, Government of Maharashtra to visit Tokyo, Japan for five days from 20th August, 2023 to 25th August, 2023 on behalf of Government of Japan. The Purpose of this
tour is to visit Tokyo, Kyoto Prefecture and Wakayama Prefecture as well as meetings with Japanese Cabinet Ministers, head of local Government and independent administrative
institutions and representatives of major corporations are planned. This visit will provide an excellent opportunity to deepen understanding and strengthen relationship between Government of Maharashtra & Japan.
2. On this background Government of Maharashtra's 6 member state level delegation
under the leadership of the Deputy Chief Minister, Government of Maharashtra had toured abroad from 20.08.2023 to 25.08.2023. The post-facto sanction for the said foreign tour was under the consideration of the State Government.
GOVERNMENT RESOLUTION:

5. State Government is pleased to ex-post-facto sanction the processing fees for visa for Tokyo, Japan per actual and medical facilities admissible, under the revised AMA Scheme continued in the Ministry of External Affairs O.M.No.Q/GA/653/1/74 dtd. 5/3/1979 to them.
