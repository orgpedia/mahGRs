Preamble: In terms of the provisions of Section 7 of the Minimum Wages Act, 1948 and Rule 4 of the Maharashtra Minimum Wages Rules, 1963, vide Reference No. The Maharashtra State Minimum Wages Advisory Board has been reconstituted vide Government Resolution dated 23rd May, 2013. As per Rule 4 of the Minimum Wages Rules, 1963, the tenure of the Board is three years from the date of appointment and expires on May 22, 2016.
