Preamble: To take a decision on the proposals received for the release of funds under the Maharashtra Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development Scheme. The 9th meeting of the Steering Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary was held on 03/02/2023. In the meeting, the committee has approved funds for the infrastructure works of a total of 10 cooperative industrial estates. Out of which, while sanctioning funds for installation of Express Feeder of The Earth Milk Research and Cultural Foundation, Achalpur, G. Amravati, the Committee has proposed to accept the revised proposal at the old rates as per the proposal submitted for the purpose. As per the decision of the Steering Committee, MSEDCL has sanctioned Rs. 3,07,35, 890/- has been submitted and the Directorate General of Industries vide their letter dated 31/10/2023 has requested the Government to approve the proposal. The proposal has been examined and the matter of granting administrative approval for the works of the said Institute amounting to Rs. 3,07,35, 890/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) has submitted a total budget of Rs. 3,07,35, 890/- for the work of Express Feeder of The Earth Milk Research and Culture Foundation Achalpur, G. Amravati, which has been approved by the Steering Committee for the distribution of funds under the scheme of Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development. The details is as follows.

2. A total amount of Rs. 3,07,35, 890/- has been budgeted for the Express Feeder works of the Institute. The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) has approved the project. The Earth Milk Research and Culture Foundation Achalpur, Dist. Amravati has also entered into an agreement dated 10/10/2023 with the Directorate of Industries for an undertaking that the Institute will bear the cost of future maintenance and repair of the Express Feeder.
Considering all these aspects, administrative approval is being accorded for the works of Express Feeder at a total cost of Rs. 3,07,35, 890/- (Rupees Three Crore Seven Lakh Thirty Five Thousand Eight Hundred and Ninety only).
The increase in the project cost will not be acceptable to the organization. Additional expenses should be borne by the institution.
The said administrative approval is in accordance with the approval given by the Steering Committee in its Ninth Meeting held on 03/02/2023 as well as the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor Government Decision No. Aupasu 2012/ S.No.234/ Industries-2, Dy. dated 25/02/2014. The powers conferred under section 6 are being exercised.
