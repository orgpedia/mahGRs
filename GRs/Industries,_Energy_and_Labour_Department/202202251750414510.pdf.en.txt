Introduction: As per the policy of the government, the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) provides subsidy on electricity tariff to industrial consumers in the state and the government reimburses it to MSEDCL. Maharashtra State Power Distribution Company has received a demand for financial assistance to industrial consumers in the financial year 2021-22 under Demand Number K-6 Head Account Head 28015605 for an amount of Rs. A budget provision of Rs. 1200.00 crore has been made. Out of this provision, Rs. 583.18 crore has been disbursed in cash vide Government Decisions dated 29.06.2021 and 16.11.2021 and Rs. Supplementary Demand of Rs. 250.00 crore has been approved for Samayona in the session of December, 2021. The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure in the Winter Session of December, 2021 was submitted to the Legislative Council on 22 nd December, 2021. The Supplementary Appropriation Bill has been approved by the Cabinet for inclusion in the Appropriation for the financial year ending March 31, 2022. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, dated 30th December, 2021, as the Maharashtra (Third Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2021 (Maharashtra Act No. 17 of 2021). Accordingly, the government has allocated Rs. 250.00 crore was under consideration of the Government to be made available to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company in an adjusted manner.
Government Decision: Out of the subsidy amount payable to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the concession given to industrial consumers in electricity tariff for the financial year 2021-22, Rs. 250.00 crore (Rupees Two Hundred and Fifty Crores only) is being allowed to be released by this Government as adjustment instead of cash.
The expenditure for the said purpose should be spent under the head" Demand No.K-6,2801 Electricity, 05 Transmission& Distribution, 104, Power Development System National Load, (01) Electricity Rate Concession, (01) (04) Electricity Rate Concession to Industrial Consumers (General) (28015605), (Programme) 33 Financial Assistance" and it should be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2021-22.
