Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to appoint persons with disabilities for posts under the State Government. The Central Government has notified the list of posts reserved for Divyangjan as per the notification dated 04/01/2021.
As per the Central Government notification (on the lines of Central Government), instructions regarding ensuring posts for Divyangjan in State Government establishments have been issued vide Government Resolution, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department dated 02/02/2021. Accordingly, the matter of ensuring posts for the disabled on the establishment of the Maharashtra Labour Welfare Board was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: 1. In view of the provisions of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, Central Government Notification dated 04/01/2021 and the Government Decision, dated 02/02/2021 issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Maharashtra Labour Welfare Board is ensuring that the posts for persons with disabilities in the establishment of the Maharashtra Labour Welfare Board are as mentioned in the Return-A.
The said Government Order is being issued in accordance with the notification dated 04/01/2021 issued by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India and the Government Order dated 02/02/2021 issued by the Department of Social Justice, Government of India.
