Government order:
As per the provisions of Section 4 (1), 4 (2) and 4 (3) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Electrical Inspector, Group-A (Gazetted) cadre officer of the Electricity Inspectorate Branch is being transferred on administrative grounds and his posting is being done in front of his name in column No. The position shown in 4 is being done:

(2) The Chief Inspector of Electricity, Mumbai shall relieve the transferred officer from his present charge forthwith by making necessary local alternative arrangements to enable him to take charge of his transferred place immediately and shall direct him to join the transferred place immediately. Also, leave should not be granted to the transferred officer.
Further, disciplinary action will be initiated against the concerned Electrical Inspector as per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, if it is found that political pressure/ coercion has been exerted to cancel or modify the transfer made under this order.
