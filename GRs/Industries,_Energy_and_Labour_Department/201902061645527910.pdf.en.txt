Preamble: It has been decided as per the Government Decision dated 15.11.2018 to implement the Mukhyamantri Solar Krishi Pump Scheme in the State and it has been approved to create a new Solar Krishi Pump Scheme by utilising the budgetary allocation of the Department and funds under the Special Component Plan/ Tribal Appropriation to SC/ ST beneficiaries for installation of one lakh solar Krishi Pumps in the State. As per the decision taken by the State Cabinet, the Department of Social Justice and the Department of Tribal Development has to provide the funds required for the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Castes/ Tribes from the amount of the Special Component Plan and the Tribal Sub-Plan out of the fixed cost to the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Castes/ Tribes excluding the State share and the beneficiary share and the remaining funds (including the 5% additional share of the beneficiaries and the remaining amount of the pump). Since the scheme is meant for individual beneficiaries, the subsidy to Scheduled Tribe beneficiaries under the scheme is being provided under the Tribal Appropriation Scheme" Subsidy for Installation of Solar Powered Agriculture Pumps" under the Mukhyamantri Solar Agriculture Pump Yojana" for the purpose of providing subsidy to MSEDCL, vide Demand No. The opening of new head of accounts under T-05, Head of Accounts 2810 New and Renewable Energy was under consideration of the Government.
