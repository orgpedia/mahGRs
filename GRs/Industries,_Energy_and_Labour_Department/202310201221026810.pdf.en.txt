The Proposal:
The State Government has decided to supply electricity to the agricultural pump applicants who are waiting for payment till 31/03/2018. The High Pressure Distribution System (HDPS) Scheme was approved with an outlay of Rs. 5048.13 crore. The scheme involved setting up of 226 power sub-stations to supply power to 2.24 lakh paid agricultural pump applicants and to distribute the power load of existing and potential agricultural pumps. The scheme was approved for 2018-19 and 2019-20. Under the High Pressure Distribution System Scheme, Rs. 2248.13 crore was to be made available by the State Government as subsidy to MSEDCL and the remaining amount of Rs. 2800 crore was approved to be raised by MSEDCL in the form of a loan.
