Cabinet approves setting up of a revised sub-committee to take policy decisions on giving rebate in power tariff to industrial consumers in Vidarbha, Marathwada, North Maharashtra, D& D+ areas.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
No: No: 2022/ P. No. 110/ Energy-5
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madame Cama Road, Mantralaya, Mumbai 400032.
Date: July 7, 2023.
Read:- (1) The Government Resolution 2015/ No. 238/ Energy-5, D. 4th June, 2016.
(2) Government Resolution 2018/ No. 184/ Energy-5, p. 8th March, 2019.
Government decision: The Cabinet in its meeting held on 24th May, 2016 decided to take a policy decision on the proposal submitted by the Department to provide concession in power tariff to industrial consumers in Vidarbha, Marathwada, North Maharashtra, D& D+ areas. It has been decided to constitute a Cabinet Sub-Committee under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Power). Thereafter, the Government vide Resolution No. (2) The following amendments are being considered by the Cabinet Sub-Committee:
Shri Devendra Fadnavis, Deputy Chief Minister and Minister (Power) Chairman 2. Minister (Finance) Member 3. MoS Shri Sudhir Mungantiwar, Minister (Forests) Member 4. MoS Shri Sanjay Dhotre, MoS MoS MoS MoS MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances, Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr. Shri Uday Samant, Minister (Industries) Member 5. Shri Atul Save, Minister (Cooperation) Member
The committee will have the power to take a final decision on the proposal of the department including the modalities and permissibility of the power tariff concession to be given to industrial consumers in Vidarbha, Marathwada, North Maharashtra, D and D+ areas.
