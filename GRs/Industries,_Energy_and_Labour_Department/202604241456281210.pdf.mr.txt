PREAMBLE
The Indian Ports Association has invited the Principal Secretary (Industries) Government of Maharashtra to participate in ‘Singapore Maritime Week 2026'. This event is scheduled to be held in Singapore from 20" April to 24™ April, 2026.
2. The Indian Ports Association (IPA) has invitation to visit an event in Singapore on 21% April and 22" April, 2026, as part of the “Singapore Maritime Week 2026”. This visit is of paramount importance for developing "Green Shipping Corridors" at a global level and for presenting the "Novel Gadchiroli" bio-methanol initiative. This project aligns with India's
ambition to develop a large-scale supply of clean fuel for international green maritime routes and to build high-quality renewable infrastructure for sectors that are difficult to decarbonize.
3. Believing that participation in this program will help attract investment to Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra has accepted the invitation from The Indian
Ports Association (IPA) in Singapore, and with the approval of the Chief Minister of
Maharashtra, has decided to send a state-level delegation to Singapore, which will take place
from April 18, 2026 to April 22, 2026 (including travel time).
RESOLUTION:-
The Government of Maharashtra is hereby granting ex-post facto approval for the visit of a state-level delegation to Singapore from 18" April,2026 to 22™ April, 2026 (including travel time), to participate in the "Singapore Maritime Week 2026".
