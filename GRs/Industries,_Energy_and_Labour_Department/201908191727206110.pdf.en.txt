Periodic transfers of officers of the cadre of Electricity Duty Inspector Group-B (Gazetted) in the Electricity Inspectorate Branch have been made as per the Government Order issued in 2019. Changed by order
Some of the officers have submitted applications to the government seeking extension and partial modification of their transfer orders to accommodate them on account of their family problems and medical reasons. In accordance with section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, with the approval of the competent authority, the following statement of the following officers of the category of Electricity Duty Inspector, Group B (Gazetted) has been issued. In partial modification of the transfer order dated 12th June, 2019, they are now being transferred and posted at the place indicated in column 5 in front of their names:
