The Proposal:
As per the policy of the government, the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) provides rebate in electricity rates to industrial consumers in the state and the government reimburses the same to MSEDCL. Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company has received a demand for financial assistance to industrial consumers in the financial year 2021-22 under the head Demand No. K-6 Main Account Head 28015605 for an amount of Rs. A budget provision of Rs. 1200.00 crore has been made. As per the approval of the Finance Department, Rs. 391.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.
