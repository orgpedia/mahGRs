Government Decision: With the aim of helping the beneficiaries of all sections of the state to increase their income from the beekeeping business, raising their standard of living and developing infrastructure for the development of the honey industry, as well as creating beekeepers who do the business of honey industry on a commercial basis, the Government of this Department has issued a Government Decision No. KVB-2017/ PR.No. Honey Centre Scheme (Beekeeping) has been approved to be implemented in the entire State under 18/06/2019. The programmes covered under the scheme include creation of mechanism for selection of beekeepers and institutions, training of beekeepers, organising camps, providing financial assistance to beneficiaries, supply of subsidy and materials and procurement of honey and wax.
Under this scheme, it has also been approved to provide manpower on a contract basis.
