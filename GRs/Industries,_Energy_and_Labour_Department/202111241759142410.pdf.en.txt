Preamble: As per the policy of the government, the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) provides concession in electricity rates to various categories of consumers in the state and the government reimburses the same to MSEDCL. Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company has received a demand for financial assistance to agricultural pump consumers in the financial year 2021-22 under the head of Demand Number K6 Head Account Heading 28015572. 5300.00 crore
A budget has been set. Of the Rs. 361.00 crore has been adjusted vide order dated 29.06.2021. At present, the Finance Department has sanctioned Rs. 2927.74 crore to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company (MSEDCL).
Government Decision: Out of the subsidy amount payable to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the rebate given to agricultural pump consumers in the financial year 2020-21, Rs. 2927.74 crore (Rupees Two Thousand Nine Hundred Seventy Seven Crore Seventy Four Lakh only) is being allowed to be released by this Government through adjustment instead of cash.
The expenditure for the said purpose should be spent under the head" Demand No. K-6,2801 Electricity, 05 Transmission and Distribution, 104, Power Development System National Load, (01) Electricity Rate Concession, (01) (01) Electricity Rate Concession (General) to Agricultural Pump Customers (28015572), (Programme) 33 Financial Assistance" and it should be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2021-22.
3. Amount to be disbursed by adjusting Rs. 2927.74 Crores (Rupees Two Thousand Nine Hundred and Twenty Seven Crores Seventy Four Lakhs Only) to MSEDCL as further shown without giving cash to MSEDCL. The amount due to the Government should be recovered from them and deposited by adjustment under the head of accounts mentioned below.
