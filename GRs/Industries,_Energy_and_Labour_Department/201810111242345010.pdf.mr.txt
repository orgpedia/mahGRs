Introduction:
The Department of Industrial Policy & Promotion (DIPP) under Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India, has issued a draft Business Reforms Action Plan 2018 for States / UTs. This initiative by the Government of India is taken to improve the Ease of Doing Business across the states in the country. The States will be evaluated based on their compliance with the parameters mentioned in the Action Plan 2018. DIPP will carry out the evaluation based on timely responses for approvals of various applications, the feedback given by investors about their experience of using the facilities and responses provided by the robust grievance redress mechanism in place.
The State Government has also adopted the Ease of Doing Business Policy in accordance to its Make in Maharashtra Policy. The Labour Department has adopted and implemented various business reforms suggested by the DIPP as per the Business Reforms Action Plan.
Circular -
The Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion (DIPP) of Government of India as a part of Business Reforms Action Plan (BRAP-2018) has suggested to mandate that registers under various labour laws to be maintained in electronic form.
Labour Commissionorate, Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health and Directorate of Steam Boilers provide various services/permissions online in stipulated timeframe to facilitate Ease of Doing Business. All the information is received online vide these services and is stored electronically. The Labour department maintains the registers in electronic form as under various laws as mentioned in Annexure I.
This Government Circular of Maharashtra Government is available at the website www.maharashra.gov.in Reference no. for this is 201810111242345010. This Circular has been signed gitally.
