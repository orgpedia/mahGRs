Introduction: To remove the imbalance of agricultural pump energy in different parts of the state. 18 districts have been declared as backward districts by the" Index and Backward Committee" appointed by the Governor. Out of these, physical backlog in 16 districts has been removed by the end of March, 2017. Out of 16,933 and 1,8987 agricultural pumps installed in the remaining two districts of Gadchiroli and Ratnagiri, the number of pumps installed at the end of March 1996 was 471 and 8,300 respectively. The target set for the Gadchiroli district to electrify the agricultural pumps under the backlog scheme has been completed by the end of March 18. In Ratnagiri district, there is a backlog of 7441 agricultural pumps at the end of March 2018 and a target of 2400 agricultural pumps for 2018-19 has been set. In order to remove this backlog as soon as possible, the Government has allocated Rs. 50.00 crore. To remove this backlog, the MSEDCL has taken up a programme. As per the request made vide letter dated 27.12.2018, Rs. 50.00 crore out of Rs. 30.00 (Rupees Thirty Crores only) in cash to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited.
