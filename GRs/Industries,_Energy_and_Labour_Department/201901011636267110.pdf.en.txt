Preamble: As per the decision of the Government of India, a State Government Biofuel Advisory Committee has been constituted to formulate the State Government's Biofuel Policy in line with the National Biofuel Policy announced by the Government of India in June, 2018. The committee consists of two non-government experts in the field. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a non-government expert was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Atul Murlidhar Mule, Pune is being appointed as a non-official member in the Biofuel Advisory Committee of the State Government constituted under the decision of the Government in question.
