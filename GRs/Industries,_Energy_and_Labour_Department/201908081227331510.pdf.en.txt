Preamble: The Accountant General is requested to submit information to the Head of the Department within one month on the inspection report that results from the completion of the audit of each office. Further, on completion of audit of each component, the respondents are requested to file their replies within six weeks from the date of receipt of the report on the findings of the audit.
But on the objections related to the departments, the answers are submitted to the Accountant General's office after a long time. Similarly, the Comptroller and Auditor General of India
The explanatory memorandum on objections is not submitted to the Public Accounts Committee by the department within the stipulated time after being approved by the Accountant General. Hence, the Finance Department. In this letter dated 04/02/2019, instructions have been given to appoint one officer of the rank of Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary in each administrative division as the nodal officer for taking prompt action on the inspection reports of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India, draft paragraphs, audit edits, explanatory memos on CAG reports and recommendations of the Public Accounts Committee.
