Preamble: Proposal received from Directorate of Industries under the Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development Scheme (4875 0025). Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. It was placed for consideration in the 7th meeting of the Steering Committee held on 22/03/2021. After scrutinizing the proposals submitted to the Committee, the Directorate of Industries shall submit the proposals for administrative approval of the approved proposals to the Government immediately and thereafter, based on the cost of project approval approved by the Steering Committee, the Department of Industries shall issue the Administrative Sanction Order/ Technical Sanction from the Implementing Agency/ Fund Disbursement Order as per the prevailing procedure. However, considering the fact that the financial year will come to an end in the meantime, the committee has unanimously decided to disburse the budgetary provision of Rs. 18.75 crore for the current financial year 2020-21 to the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation and pay it to the implementing agencies by following the prescribed procedure. The minutes of the meeting No. 4 is here. Issued vide letter dated 24/03/2021.
Under the Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development Scheme, a budgetary provision of Rs.25 crore was made in the financial year 2020-21. Meanwhile, in the Revised Estimates for FY 2020-21, the budget allocation for the scheme has been increased from Rs.25 crore to Rs. 18.75 crore. Out of this, an amount of Rs. 32,09,584/- has been spent and an amount of Rs. 18,42,90, 416/- is available for distribution on BDS system. The Planning and Finance Departments have approved the release of funds. As per the decision taken in the 7th meeting held on 22/03/2021, a budget of Rs. 18,42,90, 416/- is available for the financial year 2020-21 under this scheme.
