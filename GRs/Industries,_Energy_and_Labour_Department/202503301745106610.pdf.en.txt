Preamble: - The scheme "Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 25/02/2014 to promote cluster development of small clusters in industrially undeveloped areas of the state thereby promoting holistic development of micro and small units. Under the scheme, state government grants are provided for implementation of capacity building programmes for holistic development and growth of micro and small enterprises as well as for setting up of common facilities.
As mentioned in the proposal dated 24/03/2025 of the Directorate of Industries, an amount of Rs. 10,10,52, 000 / - has been given final approval for setting up of Common Facility Centre for the industrial cluster project Sangha Textile Cluster, G. Akola at the 14th meeting of the State Level Steering Committee dated 02/11/2021 chaired by the Additional Chief Secretary (Industries).
