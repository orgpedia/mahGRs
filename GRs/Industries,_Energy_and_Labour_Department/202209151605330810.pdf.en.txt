Introduction: The above reference No. In pursuance of the above letter (2) and (3), the Labour Commissioner, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to amend the Maharashtra Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Rules, 1971 as notified by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under the Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP) 2022.
In view of the original provisions of Rules 23 and 29 of the Maharashtra Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Rules, 1971, these provisions provide for grant of license/ renewal to the contractor within 7 days and a Deemed Approval in case of non-proceeding within the said period. Also, the Maharashtra Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Rules, 1971, do not specify a specific time period for issuing a registration certificate to the principal employer.
However, in the above Reference No. (1) In the Government Resolution dated 24/07/2015, the time period for providing this service is mentioned as 07 days. However, no consequential changes have been made in the computing system for issuance of auto-generated Registration/ Correction Certificate. Therefore, after the expiry of the said period of 7 days, the principal owner/ contractor is required to make the consequential changes in the computer system for issuance of auto generated registration/ renewal/ modification certificate. Following this, the government took decision:
Government's decision:
DPIIT has launched the Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP) 2022. Amendments have been proposed under Sections 130 and 139 regarding grant/ renewal/ modification of license to the contractor/ principal owner. Government Decision dated 24/07/2015 to grant/ renew/ amend the license to the Contractor/ Principal Owner within 07 days

A delivery time is indicated. Considering the amendments suggested by DPIIT and the provisions prescribed in the Government Order, the Government is approving necessary changes in the computer system to make the Autogenerated Registration/ Renewal/ Improvement Certificate available to the Contractor/ Principal Employer under the Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970 after the prescribed period. In accordance with the said Government approval, the Labour Commissioner, Mumbai shall take immediate action to make necessary consequential changes in the relevant computer system from his level.
