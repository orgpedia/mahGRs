Terms and Conditions of the Scheme: -
1. Active (living) construction workers registered with the Board are eligible to take advantage of the scheme.
These eligible workers shall submit a requisition application in the prescribed format to the In-charge Labour Remedies / Assistant Labour Commissioner / Government Labour Officer, District Labour Facilitation Centre (all).
After filling them up, they should be provided with an essential kit.
The Essential Kit to be supplied to eligible construction workers under the Board should include the following items:
(1) Galvanized trunk, (2) Plastic mat, (3) Grain storage tank (capacity 20 kg) (capacity 25 kg), (4) Grain storage tank (capacity 22 kg), (5) Bedsheet, (6) Chaddar, (7) Blanket, (8) Sugar container (1 kg) (SS202) (1 KG) (SS202), (9) Tea powder container (capacity Yoo Gram) (SS202) (Tea container) (500 g) ($202), (10) Water
