The Government's Decisions:
The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board has been asked to meet non-salaried expenses for the months of April-2023 to July-2023 and August-2023 to November-2023 respectively vide Reference No. The approval was granted by the Government Decisions 3 and 4. Now Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board Reference No. The Government is approving the disbursement of grant-in-aid to the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board to the tune of Rs.30,37,000 / - (Rupees Thirty Lakh, Thirty Seven Thousand only) for the purpose of "31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)" to meet the non-salaried expenditure for the month of December-2023 as proposed under letter No.5. The conditions of grant disbursement of the finance department should be followed while making these expenditures.
2. Expenditure on this should be met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2023-24 for the purpose of "Demand No. K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (00) (01) Khadi and Village Industries Board Subsidiary Grants (Dattamat) (2851 0585) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment)" and it should be spent under the same head.
3. The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the appropriation certificate regarding the expenditure of the subsidy to the Government and the Drawing and Disbursing Authorities within the prescribed time. Also, when inquiries are made by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-I, Mumbai regarding the expenditure of accounts, the Board shall submit the required documents to the Accountant General's Office.
