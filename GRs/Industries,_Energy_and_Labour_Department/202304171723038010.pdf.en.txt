The Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2023 (Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2023) has been passed by the Legislative Assembly. The Finance Department, through a circular, informed that the Governor has given his assent to it. The Finance Department has also notified that the Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV dated 29th March, 2023 has been published as Maharashtra Act No. 17 of 2023.
The funds will be disbursed to the administrative departments in accordance with the targets authorized on the basis of the Budget Estimates 2023-24 (White Book) book as approved by the Finance Department through a Government Circular in the context of the Finance Department.
After the monthly fund statements, budget estimates, allocation and control system (BEAMS) of the budgetary provisions for the financial year 2023-24 are recorded by the administrative departments, the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programmed expenditure required for the first nine months of 2023-24 (ending December, 2023) will be distributed through the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEAMS) within the limit of 70% of the total annual provision. Funding will be disbursed at 20% for the first quarter of April to June, 2023, 20% for the quarter of July to September, 2023 and 30% for the quarter of October to December, 2023. The facility of withdrawal of funds for the period April-December 2023 is being provided on the Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System (BEAMS). The Finance Department has issued instructions through the relevant government circulars that the monthly expenditure amounts should be consistent with what is indicated in the monthly cash flow statement while disbursing the funds.
4. All Controllers and Disbursing Officers under the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour shall, subject to the conditions specified in the circular under reference to the Department of Finance, make available the funds made available on the Budget Distribution System before drawing the same from the Treasury and incur expenditure.
