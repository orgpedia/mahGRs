A sum of Rs. 5.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2017-18 for this scheme. In respect of this provision, four industrial estates namely Lonavala, Sinnar, Pachora and Yeole have been allotted land within the limits prescribed in the circular dated 6/2/2018 of the Finance Department. As per the Government decision dated 21/03/2018, a sum of Rs. 3.92 crore was sanctioned. However, the Audit Team of the Accountant General's Office, Mumbai (Audit) raised the issue of non-payment of funds amounting to Rs. 3.92 crore sanctioned by the Government vide Government Resolution dated 21/03/2018 to the concerned executing agency by the Development Commissioner (Industries) (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) till the time of examination. On further enquiry, it was found that the payment of the said amount could not be made as the Accounts and Treasury Office had passed the payment of the said amount on or after 31st March, 2018. Therefore, the Government found that the amount of Rs. 3.92 crore released under the Government Decision dated 21/03/2018 had lapsed. In view of the seriousness of the matter, the Government vide letter dated 10th September, 2018 and letter dated 26th October, 2018 has informed the Development Commissioner (Industries) to take disciplinary action against the Joint Director (Suppro), Member Secretary and the concerned staff handling the work of the scheme in the Directorate of Industries.
