Preamble: As per the Government decision in Reference No. 3, Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai has been approved to disburse a total of Rs. 2113.96 lakh (Rs. The Government decision in Reference No. 3, Government Corrigendum to Reference No. 5, has approved the release of subsidy amounting to Rs. 120.84 lakh under 31 Auxiliary Subsidy (Non-salaried) Objectives.
The government was considering providing grants to the board to meet the salary and non-salary expenses for four months from December 2019 to March 2020.
Government's decision:
The Khadi and Village Industries Board, vide its Reference No.4, has requested for release of total grant-in-aid of Rs. 2253.40 (Rupees Twenty Two Crore Thirty Five Lakh Forty Thousand only) for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 2113.96 Lakhs and 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 139.44 Lakhs for meeting the expenditure for the 4 months from December, 2019 to March, 2020. As the Board has been able to disburse full grants within the limit of 60% of the sanctioned budgetary provision (Rs. 278.88 lakhs) under the target of 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary), the amount under the target of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) has been increased to Rs. 2113.96 lakh (Rupees Twenty One Crore Thirteen Lakh Ninety Five Thousand) is being approved through this Government Resolution.
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2019-20 under the targets Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (28510585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) and should be spent under the same head.
