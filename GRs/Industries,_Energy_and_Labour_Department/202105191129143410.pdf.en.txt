As per the Government Circular, the Deputy Director, Group-A Cadre Officers of the Directorate of Vapour, Dy. 01.01.2015, Dy. 01.01.2016, Dy. 01.01.2017, Dy. 01.01.2018, Dy. 01.01.2019 and D. Provisional seniority as on 01.01.2020 has been notified.
Representations were sought regarding objections/ errors regarding the said seniority. The officers holding the post of Deputy Director in the Directorate have not raised any objections in terms of their temporary seniority. Therefore, the Deputy Director, Group-A Cadre Officers of the Directorate of Vapour, Dy. 01.01.2015, Dy. 01.01.2016, Dy. 01.01.2017, Dy. 01.01.2018, Dy. 01.01.2019 and D. are being published as per the last seniority as on 01.01.2020.
