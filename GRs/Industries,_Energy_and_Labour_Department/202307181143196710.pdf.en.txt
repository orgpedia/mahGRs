Government's decision:
Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation Limited As proposed vide letter dated 20/04/2023, the Government have approved the release of grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 93,60,501/- (Rupees Ninety Three Lakhs Sixty Thousand Five Hundred and One only) to the Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation for the purpose of" 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary)" to meet the non-salary expenditure. While making these expenditures, the conditions for the distribution of grants by the Finance and Planning Departments should be followed.
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2023-24 under the target of" Demand No. K-7, 2851 Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 104 Handicrafts Industry (00) (00) (02) Development Programme of Handicrafts Industry (28510567) 31-Support Grants (Non-Salary)" and should be spent under the same head.
The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government and the Drawing and Disbursing Officers within the prescribed time. Also, when the accountant general, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai, is asked about the expenditure of the accounts, the board should submit the necessary documents related to the account to the office of the accountant general.
