GOVERNMENT DECISION, INDUSTRY, ENERGY AND LABOUR DEPARTMENT NO. OPEN- 0230/2005-2006/ (8605)/ ADMINISTRATION-1, dated 11.07.2005.
Preamble: The Department had approved the introduction of a new sub-heading 0230-Labour and Allowance, 800-Other Deposits, (00) (13) for payment of appointment fee of Standard Surgeons under the Government decision dated 11.07.2005. However, since the title had lapsed, a proposal had been submitted to the Finance Department for obtaining a new title for the registration of certified surgeons and payment of renewal fee. The Accountant General's Office has approved the new head of accounts for the payment of registration and renewal fees of certified surgeons, as suggested by the Finance Department.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following new head of accounts for the payment of registration and renewal fees of Certified Surgeons has been approved by the Finance Department and the Accountant General (Accounts and Licensure)-1, Maharashtra, Mumbai and administrative approval is being given by the Government for the collection and payment of registration and renewal fees of Certified Surgeons under the following head.
0230, Labour and Employment, 104, Fees under Factories Act, (00) (03) Registration and renewal fee of Certified Surgeons (0230 0241)
This Government decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 266/ Finance- 14/18 dated 30.11.2018 of the Finance Department and the Informal Reference No. 1 of the Accountant General (Accounts and Accounts)-1, Maharashtra, Mumbai. 835 dated 05.11.2018 is being issued.
