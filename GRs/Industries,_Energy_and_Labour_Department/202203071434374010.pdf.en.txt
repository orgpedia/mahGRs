Government's decision:
In order to prevent the loss of the grape growing farmers of the state, to encourage the farmers to cultivate grapes, to make dry fruits and to create economically more profitable alternative products from grapes, and to promote the wine industry based on grapes, the government has launched the Grape Processing Industry Policy-2001. Decision of 19/10/2001. As part of the implementation of the policy, the government has decided to provide a wine incentive subsidy to the wine industry equivalent to 16 per cent of the value added tax payable on the sale of wine produced and finally sold in the state. Implemented by the Government Decision of 31/08/2009.
2. A proposal has been submitted to the Finance Department for release of funds to the tune of Rs. 23,26,78, 541/-for settlement of 35 pending claims for the financial year 2019-20 or earlier, out of the budgeted amount of Rs. 7,50,00, 000/-for 2021-22.
Therefore, subject to the condition of ensuring strict implementation of the provisions, criteria, eligibility etc. of the Government Decisions/ Circulars/ Corrigendum mentioned in Reference No. 1 to 4 4, taking into account the funds available for the said Scheme, the claim received from the Directorate of Industries during the financial year 2019-2020 or earlier shall be considered in the following statement: The wine industry component mentioned in section 2 is referred to as Rakana No. This Government resolution approves the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 7,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crore Fifty Lakh only) as mentioned in Rule 5.
