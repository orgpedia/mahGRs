Letter dated 06/11/2018.
Preamble: What measures need to be taken to rationalize the functioning of the Mathadi Board. Similarly, a sub-committee has been constituted on 29th October, 2018 to suggest to the Government the present functioning of the Board, its composition, problems faced by them, issues of Mathadi workers and measures to be taken on it and the committee is required to submit its report within eight working days from the date of its constitution. As per the letter dated 06/11/2018 of the Assistant Labour Commissioner (Mathadi), considering the scope of the work of the sub-committee, the fact that it has to visit every Mathadi Mandal, verify the documents and recommend the remedial measures after thorough discussion, and the work of the upcoming winter session of the Maharashtra Legislative Council, the sub-committee has been requested to extend the time. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the time for submitting the report to the sub-committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the request of Assistant Labour Commissioner (Mathadi), the Sub-Committee is being extended till 15/12/2018 to submit its report to the Government.
