Preamble: As per the decision of the Government dated 14th June, 2017 regarding solarization of agricultural lines in the rural areas of the State, where the rural and agricultural lines of electricity have been isolated, the Chief Minister's Solar Agriculture Line Scheme has been launched in the State. For effective implementation of this scheme, the Mukhyamantri Solar Krishi Vahini Yojana has been amended as per the Government decision dated 17th March, 2018. Under the scheme, the government will allocate Rs. The Government Decision dated 17.03.2018 provides for leasing out of private land/ fallow land for the project to the implementing agency/ private investors on lease basis for a period of 30 years at the rate of Rs. The government has also decided that there will be no need to cultivate the land made available for the project under this scheme.
The Chief Minister reviewed the Solar Agriculture Channel Scheme. As per the decision taken in the meeting held on Wednesday, July 13, 2022, under the chairmanship of the Deputy Chief Minister, a committee was constituted under the chairmanship of the Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue) under the Government decision dated September 15, 2022, to make the land required for solarization of the agricultural channel available through the Revenue Department. As per the recommendations of the committee, the government was considering making available government and private land for the Mukhyamantri Solar Agriculture Channel Scheme and Kusum Yojana easily and quickly.
