Introduction: The increasing number of educated youth in urban and rural areas of the state and new opportunities for self-employment and employment in various fields in the state in the field of industry, business. Considering these new opportunities of self-employment and employment, the comprehensive scheme of' Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme' (CMEGP), which promotes entrepreneurship and gives a time-bound boost to creativity, has been implemented as per the Government decision dated 01/08/2019.
Accordingly, State Level and District Level Controlling Committees have been constituted to provide various opportunities of self-employment to the urban and rural youth of the state and for effective implementation of the scheme.
Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program, the state government will provide financial assistance in the form of grants from the state government's budgetary provision to establish self-employment projects to make the youth of the state self-sufficient/ self-reliant. Accordingly, a new Account Head for General Category and Tribal Category has been opened under the Scheme vide Government Resolution dated 23/01/2020. Also, for the Scheduled Castes Sub Plan, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance provides provision in accordance with the objectives.
The Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme is being implemented through the District Industries Center under the Directorate of Industries under the Industries Department and the District Khadi and Village Industries Office under the Khadi and Village Industries Board. Accordingly, an additional margin money of Rs.7 crore is required to be made available for disbursement of pending claims of SCs under the scheme.

The Director of Industries, Directorate of Industries has made the request vide their letter dated 11/03/2022. Accordingly, for this purpose, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance was under the consideration of the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 7 crore made available to the Department under its Government Resolution dated 30/03/2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance vide its Government Resolution dated 30/03/2022 has approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 7,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores only) for the pending subsidy claims of SCs under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme to the Principal Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department as Head of the Administrative Department on Budgetary Fund Distribution System. Accordingly, the powers to draw and disburse this provision are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
