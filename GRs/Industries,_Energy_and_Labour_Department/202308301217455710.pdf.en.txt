PREAMBLE
London Tech Week 2023 organized at London, UK from 12.6.2023 to 16.6.2023.
Mr. Joshua Market, Trade and Investment Manager-Indo-Pacific Region, Department for Business and Trade, London, UK had invited Hon. Minister (Industries), Government of Maharashtra to visit London Tech Week 2023. Hon'ble Minister (Industries) accepted the invitation & represented the State, in this Programme. London Tech Week 2023 had showcased the UK's world class technology ecosystem which has recently been valued at 1 trillion dollar; the UK is the 3rd country to reach this milestone. LTW may provide the opportunity to promote Maharashtra, as one of the India's largest technological hub.
2. On this background Government of Maharashtra's 5-member state level delegation under the leadership of the Hon'ble Minister (Industries), Government of Maharashtra had toured abroad from 15.6.2023 to 17.6.2023. The post-facto sanction for the said foreign tour was under the consideration of the State Government.
