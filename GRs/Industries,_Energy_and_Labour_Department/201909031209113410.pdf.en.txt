Government Resolution, Industries, Energy and Labour Department, No. Policy-2016/ PR No.520/ Energy-4 dated 31.05.2017 Preamble: As per Section 164 of the Indian Electricity Act, 2003 and Section 10 (d) of the Indian Telegraph Act, 1885 and Government of Maharashtra Order No. Under Section 312/ Energy-4, dated 24.08.2006, the Maharashtra State Power Transmission (Mahatrans) Company is empowered to construct transmission lines and towers. Accordingly, in accordance with the above Reference letter of the Maharashtra State Electricity Transmission Company, for obtaining prior approval of the Government under Section 68 of the Electricity Act, 2003, for the installation and maintenance of transmission lines under the scheme approved by the Maharashtra State Electricity Transmission Company, the reference has been made to the Government of Maharashtra. The proposal has been submitted vide letter dated 08.03.2019. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government Resolution: Under Section 68 of the Indian Electricity Act, 2003, the Maharashtra State Electricity Transmission Company Limited (MSETCL) has been declared as a Power Transmission Company. The Schemes proposed in Annexure A and B as proposed on 08.03.2019 are being approved subject to the following conditions:
The construction of power lines shall be carried out (as the case may be) by the General Transport Company or the concerned private power generation plant holder at their own cost in the prescribed standard manner and under the guidance and supervision of experts. Also, the concerned private power generation plant holder will transfer the transmission lines to the Mahanagar Transmission Company for operation and maintenance work after the completion of the transmission line construction works.
2. The operation and maintenance of the said power lines shall be carried out by the General Transport Company.

(3) It shall be incumbent upon the General Transport Company or the consumer concerned to use the said power lines only for the purpose for which they are intended to be used by the General Transport Company or the consumer concerned.
Prior approval of the competent authority shall be required before commissioning/ commissioning of the said power lines.
If there are any complaints of the concerned land owners/ farmers during the installation or after installation of the said power lines, it shall be the responsibility of the Mahanagar Transmission Company or the concerned consumer to resolve the complaints appropriately in consultation with the competent authority.
The Company shall be bound to obtain all the necessary licenses for the installation and maintenance of such power lines, fulfill the prescribed norms and other prevailing rules of various authorities/ departments of the Government, Electricity Regulatory Commission, Load Transmission Center, etc. as well as the rules and conditions approved by the Government from time to time.
Terms and Conditions of Section 68 of the Electricity Act, 2003 and its modifications from time to time, as well as the terms and conditions of the Government Resolution No. 2016/ No. 520/ Energy-4, dated 31.05.2017 and the High Pressure Transmission of the State Government and the Central Government
The guidelines and policies issued/ to be issued from time to time in respect of the channels shall continue to be applicable to the said transmission channel.
The period of such licence shall be 25 years from the date of issue of the licence, after which such permission shall automatically lapse.
