Government Supplementary Paper: Government Resolution, Industries, Energy and Labour Department No. BUD-2017/ Q.No.262/ Energy-7, Dy. As on 31st March, 2018, under the Non-Residential Energy Sources Funding Programme (NRSE), for the financial year 2017-18, an amount of Rs. 2.20 crore (Rupees Two Crores Twenty Lakhs only) has been sanctioned.
The following paragraphs after Annexure 7 are being inserted in this Government Resolution: 8.Major State Appropriation Act, 2017/ Circular No. Finance-2017/ P.S. 75/ Finance-3, dated 18th April, 2017 certifying the fulfilment of the conditions in the checklist in Annexure No. 7 and 8 and each of the conditions in paragraphs 7 and 20. Also, it is being certified that each of the conditions mentioned in paragraph number 2 to 6 of the circular dated 6/2/2018 of the Finance Department have been fulfilled.
9.Y. In respect of the payment made for disbursement of funds under the head of accounts, it is certified that no summary payment is pending under this head of accounts. This fund has been authorized by the circular dated 6/2/2018 of the Department of Finance to disburse 80% of the funds to the Ministries/ Departments. Accordingly, the said Government Supplementary Letter is being issued with the approval of the Principal Secretary (Power).
