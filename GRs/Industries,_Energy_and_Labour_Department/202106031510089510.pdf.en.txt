The Cabinet also approved the setting up of a high-powered committee to be empowered to take up emergency works to contain the outbreak, provide facilities to the affected patients as well as emergency purchase of medicines and other materials.
In pursuance of the said decision, the revised rule book of procedure for procurement of offices by Government Departments is being amended under paragraph 3.3.1 of the Government Decision dated 01.12.2016 by inserting the revised paragraph 3.3.1.2 in procurement in special circumstances.
3.3.1.2- Emergency purchases and activities to be undertaken in the wake of COVID-19 pandemic:
The government is also approving the formation of empowered high-powered committees for emergency works to prevent the outbreak of the Covid-19 pandemic, facilities to be provided to the affected patients as well as for emergency purchase of medicines and other materials.
