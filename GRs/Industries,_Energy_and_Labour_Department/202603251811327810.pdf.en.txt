The rules of the Guiding Committee shall be as follows:
1. Provide overall policy guidance regarding the Council.
2. Plan, outline, format, schedule and date and time of the conference programme.
3. Coordination among various government departments, central agencies and other stakeholders.
4. To take necessary administrative and technical decisions for the Council.
5. Guidance from the Government of India to the Event Management Institute for organizing the Conference.
Selection and appointment as per instructions.
6. Selection and fixation of presentations and lecturers.
7. Deciding on other matters necessary for the successful conduct of the Conference.
3. Meetings of the Committee:
1. Meetings of the Committee shall be held with the approval of the Chairman, as the case may be.
The Committee shall have the power to invite experts as required.
