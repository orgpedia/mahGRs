Government's decision:
Certified Surgeons in Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health, Group-A Officers Reference No. 3 here. In accordance with the Government Decision dated 26.11.2018. With effect from 1st July, 2012, Dearness Allowance@ 35% of the basic pay as per the pay scale in the 6th Pay Commission has been sanctioned. Accordingly, the total amount of salary plus withholding allowance received by them is Rs. It is limited to Rs 85,000.
Reference No. 2 of the Department of Public Health. 2 As per the decision of the Government here, the officers working in the Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health as Certified Surgeons, Group-A, have been given the status of Specialist Surgeons. from April 1, 2010. The matter of sanctioning the revised rate of Anti Occupational Allowance for the period June 30, 2012 was under consideration of the Government. Now, the government has decided to give the standard surgeon, Group-A, according to the salary category of the Sixth Pay Commission. It has been decided to sanction Dearness Allowance@ 25% of basic pay for the period 1.4.2010 to 30.6.2012. However, the total salary they get plus the combined amount of anti-employment allowance is Rs. Not more than 85000. This is the occupational restriction allowance. This will be applicable to wages actually paid during the period from 1.4.2010 to 30.6.2012.
The Director, Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health, Mumbai shall be responsible for ensuring that the Standard Surgeons, Group-A in the Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health, have not engaged in any private, independent profession during the aforesaid period, as the Occupational Restraint Allowance has been made admissible. Further, the procedure/ mechanism for implementation and control of the said Government Decision is set out in Reference No. 3 here. as prescribed under the Government Order dated 26.11.2018.
