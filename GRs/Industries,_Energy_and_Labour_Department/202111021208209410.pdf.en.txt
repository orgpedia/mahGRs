the prelude
As per Section 12 (2) of the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996, the authorities mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 23.09.2021 have been declared as the registering authority for registration of construction workers. It was under consideration of the Government to include Senior Labour Investigator and Junior Labour Investigator in the Registered Officers.
Government's decision
As per Section 12 (2) of the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996, the authorities mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 23.09.2021 have been declared as the registering authority for registration of construction workers. The Government is also approving inclusion of Senior Labour Investigator and Junior Labour Investigator in the authorized Registered Officers.
Accordingly, the Senior Labour Investigator and Junior Labour Investigator should perform the responsibility of registration of construction workers as per the provisions of the Act.
