The proposal: The Master of Labour Studies (MLS) is a postgraduate degree programme offered by the Narayan Meghaji Lokhande Maharashtra Institute of Labour Sciences, Mumbai and the Regional Labour Institute, Nagpur, both affiliated to the Institute. MLS is a two-year full-time post-graduate program in the industrial sector in the state of Maharashtra to foster cordial relations between employers and workers, create trained business leadership to enhance worker welfare and productivity, and create a center of excellence in labor studies.
With a glorious history of 75 years, thousands of students have studied and held high positions in various fields and many students are working in the field of Human Resource Development in different countries outside India. After completing this course, students can get job opportunities in government and private sectors. The demand is increasing especially in the areas of human resource development, industrial relations, labour law compliance.

Government's decision:
The Government have approved the fee of Rs.62,500/-for Part-I (1st year) and Rs.65,000/-for MLS (Part-II) (2nd year) as per the details in the accompanying annexure.
The revised course fee hike structure will be applicable for both MLS (Part-I) and (Part-II) students from the academic year 2022-23.
