Assistant Labour Commissioner and Chairman, Security Guard Board, Brihanmumbai and Thane District vide letter No. Surman/ Dhakaga/ 2018-348, dated: 27th November, 2018 Preamble: As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Private Security Guards (Regulation of Employment and Welfare) Act, 1981 and the Scheme (Amendment) 2002 thereunder, for the regulation of employment and welfare of security guards in the State, Security Guards Boards have been constituted in the State to implement the said Act. The Board has decided to develop an updated computer system for the office work of the Security Guards in view of the difficulties faced in the day-to-day work due to the old computer system operating in the Brihanmumbai and Thane District Boards. In this regard, the Board has decided to accept the tender of the qualified tenderer by conducting the tender process for upgrading the computer system and as per the proposal submitted by the Assistant Labour Commissioner and Chairman, Security Guard Board, Brihanmumbai and Thane District to the Labour Commissioner vide letter dated 27/11/2018, the Labour Commissioner vide letter dated 29/11/2018 has submitted the proposal to the Government for approval of the said process. The proposal was under consideration of the government for approval as per the decision taken by the board.
Government Decision: All the District Security Guard Boards in the state are autonomous Boards and no subsidy is given to the Boards by the Government. The Board has the statutory responsibility to implement the scheme as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Private Security Guards (Regulation of Employment and Welfare) Act, 1981 and the Maharashtra Private Security Guards (Regulation of Employment and Welfare) Scheme, 2002.
As per the tender process carried out by the Security Guard Board, Brihanmumbai and Thane District for the implementation of the state-of-the-art computer system for the functioning of the Board, the qualified bidders who have been accepted will be eligible to bid for the project. The following terms and conditions are being approved to make the Computer System Security Guard Board applicable to all other District Security Guard Boards including Brihanmumbai and Thane districts:
(1) There shall be an agreement between the Board and the Institute for providing 3 years of service at the primary level in respect of the said computer system.
2) Rs. 88.00 per month (Rs. 7.33) and data entry fee of Rs. 10.00 per data entry will remain the same.
The Board shall have the right to make free modifications in the scope of work on the computer system as required.
The ownership of all types of information (data) of the Board on the computer will remain with the Board. The responsibility of ensuring that the data is not corrupted or stolen rests entirely with the organisation concerned.
The organization will be required to keep a monthly backup of the work of the board.
6.If the organization maintains a data center in the office of the Board, it will be obligated to pay rent for the space used as per the rules of the Board.
The ownership of all the hardware and software in the computer system shall remain with the Board and it shall not be moved outside the Board without its permission.
8. The organization shall be required to provide a certificate of confidentiality of the work.
9. Intellectual Property Rights shall remain with the Board. It will be obligatory to make such an agreement with the board at the beginning.
The Board of Security Guards, Brihanmumbai and Thane District and other Boards shall enter into a service agreement with the said organization with all the above conditions and give work orders in the draft finalized.
It will be necessary to enter into a contract/ memorandum of understanding (MOU) with the said organization for giving work order or taking their services in this regard along with the terms and conditions of service. In case of any dispute arising out of the provision of service or payment of service, it will be possible to proceed in accordance with the terms and conditions of the agreement. Joint Labour Commissioner (Mathadi) and Assistant Labour Commissioner and Chairman, Brihanmumbai and Thane District Security Guard Board will coordinate the entire process.
