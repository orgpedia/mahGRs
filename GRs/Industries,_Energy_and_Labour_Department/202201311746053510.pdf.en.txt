Introduction: In the winter session of the Maharashtra Legislature in 2018, a notice was raised in the Legislative Council regarding irregularities in the functioning of Mathadi Hospital. The proposal was discussed in the Legislative Council. The Minister (Labour) has assured to constitute a committee under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Labour) to inquire into the functioning of Mathadi Hospital (Trust). Under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Labour), further action will be taken as per the assurance given in the Legislative Council. The Committee has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 03.01.2019. The Committee was required to submit its report within one month. The time limit for completion of the work was extended up to 31.07.2019. 01.08.2019 to D. 26.02.2020 and thereafter. 27.02.2020 to dt. The deadline was extended to complete the work by the end of 30.09.2020. The committee could not meet and submit its report due to the COVID-19 situation. The committee In the meeting held on 12.01.2022, it was discussed that the Committee needs to seek extension for completion of the work. The government had sought time to complete the probe and submit a report.
Government Decision: To give the benefit of Mathadi Hospital (Trust) to all Mathadi workers and to investigate the functioning of the hospital. The Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Labour) vide Government Resolution dated 03.01.2019 has recommended the following: In view of the decision taken in the meeting held on 12.01.2022, the committee is being given an extension of six months to investigate the matter and submit a report.
