6) Government Resolution No. Aupasu-2021/ No. 171/ Industry-2, D. 04/01/2022 GOVERNOR'S DECISION: Set up under the scheme of creation and development of industrial infrastructure. The Steering Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary in its 7th meeting held on 22/03/2021 has approved the installation of Express Feeder at Pokhran Lake Small Scale Industries Association, Upvan, Thane. Accordingly, administrative approval has been accorded for the said work vide Government Resolution dated 04/01/2022. The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) is the implementing agency for the project. The Development Commissioner (Industries) has approved the transfer of 100% government share i.e. Rs. 3,61,47, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Sixty-one Lakhs Forty-seven Thousand only) to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL).
