GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: TRANSFER-2023/ PR No.89 (PART-1)/ WORKERS-8
(2) The order shall come into force immediately. The Commissioner of Labour, Mumbai shall take necessary action for relieving the above Assistant Commissioner of Labour forthwith, instruct him to appear at the place of his transfer and submit a report to the Government.
(3) The period of office, travel allowance, daily allowance and other benefits shall not be admissible to the officers transferred on the above request.
It may be noted that non-compliance of the order passed with the approval of the competent authority under the Transfer Act is serious in nature and the officer/ employee who does not comply with this will be liable for suspension under Section 2.1 (2) (v) of Section-2 of the Departmental Inquiry Rules, 1991.
5.The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address.
If any person fails to join the post immediately as per the said order or tries to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
