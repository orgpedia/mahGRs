२. सदर समितीची कार्यकक्षा:-
a) Regularly undertake review of progress under the Scheme under all components from
concerned departments.
b) To facilitate necessary coordination between departments for implementation of the
scheme.
c) To review the status of rooftop solar installation in government buildings and to monitor
and supervise implementation of action plan for saturation of all government buildings in
a time-bound manner.
d) To facilitate convergence of efforts between departments at the field level to maximize
impact of awareness, capacity building and rooftop solar installation. Particularly in
existing beneficiaries of other government schemes.
e) To coordinate with the National Program Implementation Agency (NPIA) regarding
scheme implementation and to suggest measures to improve scheme implementation in
the district.
f) To facilitate and review the implementation of Model Solar Village Component of the
scheme.
