The Proposal:
It has been decided to constitute the Board subject to the prescribed conditions after taking into consideration the number of establishments/ factories availing the services of Maharashtra Private Security Guards in Latur, Osmanabad and Beed districts, the financial capacity of the Board, etc.
The notification has been issued under the Maharashtra Private Security Guards (Regulation of Employment and Welfare) Act, 1981 in the areas of Latur, Osmanabad and Beed districts subject to the provisions of the said Act. It has been implemented w.e.f. 16.08.2017. Similarly, a separate district police force has also been constituted. vide Notification dated 22.08.2017. He also said that the Maharashtra Private Security Guards (Regulation of Employment and Welfare) Scheme, 2002, would be implemented in the district. The Government Notification has been issued on 14.02.2019.
In order to start the work of the Security Guard Board in the district effectively and efficiently, the government was considering to give instructions regarding the implementation of the following conditions.
Government's decision:
The following conditions shall be strictly enforced for the Security Guard Board constituted for the districts of Latur, Osmanabad and Beed.
1.The post of Secretary of the newly formed Board of Security Guards should be given to the Government Labour Officer Latur in addition.
(2) Since the office of the Chairman and Secretary of the Board cannot be available in the Board full time due to the additional charge of the post of Chairman and Secretary of the Board, a personnel officer should be appointed in the Board to carry out the work of the Board smoothly at other times.
