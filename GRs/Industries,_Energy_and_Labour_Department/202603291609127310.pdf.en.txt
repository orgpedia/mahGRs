The Proposal: -
The scheme "Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 25/02/2014 to promote cluster development of small clusters in industrially underdeveloped areas of the state thereby promoting holistic development of micro and small units. Under the scheme, state government grants are provided for implementation of capacity building programmes for holistic development and growth of micro and small enterprises as well as for setting up of common facilities.
The Industrial cluster project General Engineering Cluster, Kadegaon, District-Sangli has been given final approval for setting up of Common Facility Centre in the 13th meeting of the State Level Steering Committee dated 10/03/2021. The special purpose vehicle of the project is the 100% micro component of the industrial cluster project.

The Government's Decisions:
Under the "Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" scheme of the State Government, the cost of grant-in-aid is being sanctioned as per the project approval as well as the provision of MSICDP scheme for setting up of Common Facility Centre of the project at TROT General Engineering Cluster, Kadegaon, Zila-Sangli at a cost of Rs. 2,25,00, 000 / - (Rupees Two Crore Twenty Five Lakhs only) in the State Government's share.
The amount of the grant will be provided to the General Engineering Cluster, Kadegaon, District-Sangli, Group Project in May.
The accounting officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai, will be responsible for the drawing and transmission of payments to Ennovtech Engineering Foundation, Sangli, a special purpose vehicle company. They should deposit the above amount into their personal virtual deposit account (VPDA) by submitting the payment to the treasury.
