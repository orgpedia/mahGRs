MLS is a two-year full-time post-graduate program in the industrial sector in the state of Maharashtra to foster cordial relations between employers and workers, create trained business leadership to enhance worker welfare and productivity, and create a center of excellence in labor studies.
With a glorious history of 75 years, thousands of students have studied and held high positions in various fields and many students are working in the field of Human Resource Development in different countries outside India.
