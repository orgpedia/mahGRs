Preamble: In order to promote the development of small clusters in the industrially underdeveloped areas of the State and thereby promote the overall development of micro and small units, the Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP) has been announced vide Government Resolution dated 25/02/2014. Under the scheme, the state government provides grant-in-aid for the implementation of capacity building programmes for the overall development and growth of micro and small enterprises as well as for the construction of common facilities.
Under the MSICDP scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared by properly diagnosing the flaws in the production process of the industrial group, including the latest technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial groups, updated training programs and study tours, etc. After the diagnostic study report is received on the recommendation of the district level committee, the State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee approves the diagnostic study report proposal and determines the subsidy for the capacity building programme. A Detailed Project Report (DPR) containing details of high quality common facilities and other necessary clearances, no-objection certificates, technical and financial evaluation reports are prepared to enhance the productivity and competitiveness of MSME units. The procurement process is initiated after the final approval of the State Level Steering Committee for the proposal of the Common Facility Centre.
As per the revised guidelines of MSICDP Scheme dated 24/07/2019, after final approval of the proposal of Common Facility Centre by the State Level Steering Committee, the first phase 20% subsidy is disbursed to the projects whose procurement process has been completed by the Zonal Committee after receiving the report along with the procurement details. Thereafter, 60% of the second phase subsidy is disbursed after receipt of physical verification report from the General Manager, District Industries Centre regarding the installation of machinery, equipment at the work site as per the project approval. The remaining 20% subsidy is disbursed after receipt of certificate from the concerned Industry cum Director on successful completion of the Common Facility Centre.

As per proposal of Directorate of Industries dated 31/03/2023, the State Level Steering Committee for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Industrial Cluster Project, Jackfruit& Multifruit Cluster, Kudal G. Sindhudurg has given final approval in its meeting dated 05/08/2022. Accordingly, the Joint Director of Industries, Konkan has submitted a report with the details of the procurement process completed by the Divisional Committee as well as the physical verification report of the installation of machinery worth Rs. 3,51,51, 040/- out of the amount of Rs. 3,72,40, 908/- fixed by the Divisional Committee through the prescribed tender process. The proposal dated 31/03/2023 of the Directorate General of Industries states that the necessary effective steps have been completed to disburse the subsidy as 80% of the State Government's share (20% in the first phase and 60% in the second phase combined). Accordingly, a total grant of Rs. 3,30,48, 800/- (20% in Phase-I and 60% in Phase-II combined) as 80% of the State share for the construction of Common Facility Centre of the project was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Directorate of Industries has proposed to disburse a total grant of Rs. 3,30,48, 800/- as 80% of the State Government's share (20% in the first phase and 60% in the second phase combined) for the construction of a common facility center of the Jackfruit and Multifruit Cluster, Kudal G. Sindhudurg, which has been approved under the scheme" Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Program (MSICDP)" of the State Government. 3 is presented here. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the present scheme, the total grant of Rs. 3,30,48, 800/- (Rupees Three Crores Thirty Lakhs Forty Eight Thousand Eight Hundred only) as 80% of the State Government share is being sanctioned under this decision.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall arrange to transfer the amount of Rs. 3,30,48, 800/- from the Government Treasury to the Jackfruit and Multifruit Cluster, Kudal G. Sindhudurg Group Project. The powers to draw and disburse the above mentioned amount of Rs. 3,30,48, 800/- are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
