The Proposal:
The Government of India had announced Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana on 1.10.2007 for unorganized workers living below the poverty line. Accordingly, as per the guidelines of the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India vide Government Resolution No. CLA 2008/ Q.No.673/ Work. 7A, dated 28.3.2008, the National Health Insurance Scheme was implemented in the State of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the cases/ matters pertaining to the said scheme were being dealt with by this Department.
Thereafter, vide Executive Order dated 17.3.2015 of the Ministry of Labour& Employment, Government of India, New Delhi, as part of National Health Insurance Mission, in public interest, the National Health Insurance Scheme was ordered to be transferred from the Ministry of Labour& Employment to the Ministry of Health& Family Welfare with effect from 1.4.2015 in the as-is-where-is status.
Government's decision:
The Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana was being implemented in the State of Maharashtra as a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Government Decision, Industries, Energy and Labour Department dated 28.3.2008, as per the guidelines of the Ministry of Labour and Employment with a view to providing social security to the workers in the unorganized sector.
The implementation of this insurance scheme was being carried out by the office of the Development Commissioner (Unorganized Workers) through the Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana Samiti, Maharashtra. It was headed by Principal Secretary (Labour) and Member Secretary, Development Commissioner (Unorganised Labour). Name of the Organization State Bank of India, Branch Government Colony, Bandra

Seventy eight thousand five hundred and ninety seven only) is to be used by the Nodal Agency for the administrative expenses of the implementation of the National Health Insurance Scheme.
In the meanwhile, the Ministry of Labour& Employment, Government of India vide its Executive Order dated 17.3.2015, has ordered the transfer of" Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana" from the Ministry of Labour& Employment to the Ministry of Health& Family Welfare with effect from 1.4.2015 in as-is-where-is status. Therefore, the government has decided to transfer the scheme" Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana" to the State Public Health Department along with the liability of the organization, such as the amount collected through the registration fee of the National Health Insurance Scheme Committee, Maharashtra, the passbook of the bank, all the expenses, etc.
In pursuance of the above decision, all cases/ matters, payments etc. relating to the said scheme are being transferred from the Labour Department to the Public Health Department. The Development Commissioner (Unorganized Workers) should immediately hand over all the documents, funds and pending payments related to this scheme to the Public Health Department and send its report to the Labour Department. After this, the department should complete all the further proceedings related to this scheme and the process of making the savings account of the scheme in the name of the Public Health Department.
