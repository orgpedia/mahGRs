Introduction: Maintenance, repair and renovation of existing and high capacity transmission lines of 66 KV and above capacity are carried out by various government and private transmission companies and licensees in the state. While constructing, renovating and repairing these channels, the concerned farmers and land holders were given reference no. 1 and Reference No. The compensation for the land is paid according to the government decision of 2. Reference No. Para 10 of the Government Resolution dated 31.05.2017 is as follows: A committee under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Power, should be constituted to resolve any difficulties, clarifications, if any, arising in the implementation of this decision. The committee will have representatives of the concerned transmission company and the Power Grid Corporation of India. The members of the committee will be the Chief Engineer, State Transmission (Undertakings) in the rank of Secretary.
The matter of forming a committee in accordance with the above paragraph was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A Government Decision dated 31.05.2017 has been issued regarding payment of compensation for the site of towers to be constructed for transmission lines. In accordance with the provisions of paragraph 10 of the said Government Resolution, it is proposed to constitute the following Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Power) to resolve any difficulties arising in the implementation of the said Government Resolution. :
