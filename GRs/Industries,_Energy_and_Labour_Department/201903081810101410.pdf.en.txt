2. Letter from Assistant Labour Commissioner and Mumbai Vegetable Market Unreserved Labour Board No. BHAKAM/ 2019/09, Dated: 11th February, 2019
Preamble: As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Mathadi Hamal and Other Labourers (Regulation of Employment and Welfare) Act, 1969 and the Scheme (Amendment) thereunder, Mathadi Mandals have been constituted in the State for the welfare of the Mathadi and other labour workers of the State and to implement the said Act. Considering the difficulties faced in the day-to-day functioning of the Mumbai Vegetable Market Unreserved Workers Board, the Board has decided to develop an updated computer system for the office work of the Mathadi Board. In this regard, the Board has decided to accept the tender of the qualified tenderer by conducting the tender process for the computer system upgrade and the Assistant Labour Commissioner and Chairman, Mumbai Vegetable Market and Unreserved Labour Board has submitted a proposal to the Government to approve the process through a letter dated 11/02/2019. The proposal was under consideration of the government for approval as per the decision taken by the board.
Government Decision: All the District Mathadi Mandals in the state are autonomous mandals and no subsidy is given to the mandals by the government. The Board has the statutory responsibility to implement the scheme under the scheme of Mathadi Board as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Mathadi Hamal and Other Working Workers (Regulation of Employment and Welfare) Act, 1969 and under the Act.
According to the tender process carried out by the Mumbai Vegetable Market Unreserved Workers Board to implement the state-of-the-art computer system for the functioning of the board, the qualified bidders accepted may be selected by the board. Upgradation of computer system by Serise Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd., Pune and the computer system is also being implemented in Mumbai Vegetable Market Unreserved Labour Board and all other Mathadi Mandals subject to the following terms and conditions:

(1) There shall be an agreement between the Board and the Institute for providing 3 years of service at the primary level in respect of the said computer system.
2. Business charges for computer system upgradation Rs. 10.00 and Rs. 10.00 per data entry will remain the same.
The Board shall have the right to make free modifications in the scope of work on the computer system as required.
4. The Board shall have ownership of all the data on the computer.
The responsibility of ensuring that the data is not corrupted or stolen lies entirely with the organisation concerned. In case of corruption/ theft of data, compensation will be demanded by the Board and it will be binding on the tenderer.
The organization will be required to keep a monthly backup of the work of the board.
6.If the organization maintains a data center in the office of the Board, it will be obligated to pay rent for the space used as per the rules of the Board.
The ownership of all the hardware and software in the computer system shall remain with the Board and it shall not be moved outside the Board without its permission.
8. The organization shall be required to provide a certificate of confidentiality of the work.
9. Intellectual Property Rights shall remain with the Board. It will be obligatory to make such an agreement with the board at the beginning.
10.Mumbai Vegetable and Market Unreserved and Other Labour Board and all other Mathadi Boards shall enter into a service agreement with the said organization with all the above conditions and give work orders in the draft finalized.
11.If the organization is to be given a work order or to take their services in this regard, it will be necessary to make a contract/ memorandum of understanding with them with terms and conditions regarding the service. In case of any dispute arising out of the provision of service or payment of service, it will be possible to proceed in accordance with the terms and conditions of the agreement. Joint Labour Commissioner (Mathadi) and Assistant Labour Commissioner and Chairman, Mumbai Vegetable Market Unreserved Labour Board will coordinate the entire process.
In addition to the above terms and conditions, the necessary conditions should be included by the board in the contract.
