Preamble: The New Industrial Policy, 2019 of Maharashtra has been notified vide Government Resolution dated 07/03/2019 and has come into effect from 01/04/2019. This policy will expire on 30/03/2024. Accordingly, a new Industrial Policy of the State has been prepared vide No. Two study groups have been established. The state government has formed a committee to draft a new industrial policy. The following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: In order to formulate the new industrial policy of the State, the following" Drafting Committee" is being constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Industries).

The mandate of the Drafting Committee will be as follows: 1) To obtain detailed information about the objectives and targets of the New Industrial Policy-2019 of Maharashtra, its outcome and submit its report 2) To study in detail the representations received regarding the formulation of the New Industrial Policy of Maharashtra, the policy/ guidelines of the Central Government on this, the policy of other States and stakeholders in the sector by discussing revenue department wise and studying the relevant matters.
