Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to appoint persons with disabilities for posts under the State Government. The Central Government has published the list of posts ensured for Divyang as per the notification dated 04/01/2021. As per the Central Government notification (on the lines of Central Government), instructions regarding ensuring posts for Divyangjan in State Government establishments have been issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance vide Government Resolution dated 02/02/2021. Accordingly, the matter of ensuring the posts for the disabled on the establishment of the Directorate of Vapors was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: For the posts on the establishment of the Directorate of Steam, the posts for the disabled are being ensured as mentioned in the accompanying returns- A, return B and return C.
