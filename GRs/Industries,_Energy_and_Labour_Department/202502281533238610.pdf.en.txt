Government Decision: - Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board to meet non-salaried expenses for the month of December-2023 vide Reference No. Approval was granted by the Government Decision of 2. Now Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board Reference No. As proposed by letter No. 3, the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board is being sanctioned an expenditure of Rs. 1,91,33, 100 / - (Rupees One Crore, Ninety One Lakh, Thirty Three Thousand, One Hundred Only) for "31 Subsidized Subsidies (Non-Salary)" for meeting the non-salaried expenditure from January 2025 to March 2025. The conditions of grant disbursement of the finance department should be followed while making these expenditures.
2. Expenditure on this should be met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2024-25 for the purpose of "Demand No. K-7, 2851, Village Industries & Small Industries, 105 Khadi & Village Industries (00) (00) (01) Khadi & Village Industries Board Subsidiary Grants (Dattamat) (2851 0585) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment)" and it should be spent under the same head.
3. The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the utilization certificate of the grant to the Government B to the Drawing and Disbursing Authorities within the prescribed time. Also, when inquiries are made by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-I, Mumbai regarding the expenditure of accounts, the Board shall submit the necessary documents relating to the accounts to the Accountant General's office.
4. The said Government Decision, Department of Finance Government Circular No. As per the approval granted under 9th April, 2024 and the Finance Department's No. Reference No. 1250 AD. 18/02/2025 is being issued with the consent given by Anbay.
