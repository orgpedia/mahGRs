The Proposal: -
To train 250 trainees from Ratnagiri district to make various items out of coir, a sum of Rs. 43 lakh to be provided by Reference No. Administrative approval has been granted by Government Decision No. 3. Under the objective of 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) under the Coir Industry Development Programme, the Government was contemplating disbursement of Subsidiary Grants for the non-payment of training expenses to 250 trainees in Ratnagiri district for various coir products for this financial year as requested by the Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation.
The Government's Decisions:
Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation.
