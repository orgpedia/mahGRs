Preamble: All the posts in the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour (itself) were reviewed as per the guidelines of the Finance Department, Government Circular No. 06176/ No. 20/ APUK-1, dated 29.6.2017. As per the review, out of total 282 sanctioned posts in the existing pattern in the respective cadres for the department, 67 posts have been abolished and the revised pattern of total 215 regular posts has been approved by the high level departmental committee and finance department. Accordingly, the revised figure of this Department vide Reference No. 3 has been issued by the government here. As per the revised pattern, the matter of restructuring the posts of assistant cell officer and clerk-typist was under consideration.
