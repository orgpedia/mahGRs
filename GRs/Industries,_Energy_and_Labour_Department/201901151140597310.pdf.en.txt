Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to appoint a Government Hospital in the headquarters of Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation and to certify the working officers/ employees at the district level and their families for 27 emergent diseases and 5 critical diseases under the provisions of the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Care) Rules, 1961.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the provisions of the Maharashtra State Services (Medical Maintenance Rules, 1961), the proposal received from the Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation under letter No. 2 under reference regarding the appointment of the concerned District Surgeon/ Dean/ Medical Superintendent for the officers/ employees working in the headquarters of the Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation at Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital, Mumbai and at the district level is being approved under this Government Decision.
