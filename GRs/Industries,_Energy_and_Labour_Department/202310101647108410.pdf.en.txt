The Proposal:
Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) is conducting a two-year integrated probationary training programme for Group-A cadres through Yashoda, Pune and Group-B posts through Vanamati, Nagpur. The decision is being implemented as per the government's 1. Accordingly, the cadre of Assistant Labour Commissioner (Group-A) and Government Labour Officer (Group-B) in the Labour Commissionerate under the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour will be included under reference No. Under the decision of the Government of 2, it has been approved to conduct a combined probationary training program. As per the order issued by the General Administration Department, the Group-A cadre officers selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination-2021 have been appointed as Trainee Officers and their training will be conducted at Yashda, Pune under the Combined Peripheral Training Programme-9 (CPTP-9). It has started from 03.10.2023.
Instructions have been issued through the Reference No. 4 circular of the General Administration Department regarding the creation of more number of posts within the limit of the number of candidates who appeared for this training. Further, in reference No.5 of YASHADA, Pune, the letter has provided the names of the officers who attended the training at YASHADA under CPTP-9 and the administrative departments have been asked to take action for creation of more number of posts from the date of commencement of training.

Government Resolution No: Appointment-2023/ PR No. 215/ Workers-8
GOVERNMENT ORDER: The Government is approving the creation of a total of 15 supernumerary posts in the pay scale S-18 (49100- 155800) in the revised pay matrix for a period of two years from 3.10.2023 to 29.09.2025 for a total of 15 candidates who have been selected on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination 2021 by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission under the Labour Commissionerate under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour and who have joined Yashoda, Pune for training.
These 15 supernumerary posts are being created on the establishment of Labour Commissioner, Mumbai under the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour.
Expenditure on the salary of the probationary officers for the period of two years from 3.10.2023 to 29.09.2025 for the said 15 supernumerary posts of Assistant Labour Commissioner (Group A) cadre in the establishment of the Labour Commissioner, Mumbai under Demand No. K-8, Head Account Head 3451 003 Training (00) (01) Training Programme for Government Employees (34512891)- 20- Other administrative expenses shall be covered under this head and the same shall be covered by the grants sanctioned for that year.
The General Administration Department has issued guidelines for the Combined Peripheral Training Programme-9 (CPTP-9) through its circular at reference number 4. Accordingly, instructions have been issued to register these trainee officers in the service system, obtain the DCPS number, appoint Drawing and Disbursing Officers for their salary, provide regular salary of the concerned post to the probationers from the first month of training, prepare service books of the probationers, etc. in the first 4 weeks of training. Therefore, the necessary action should be taken by the Labour Commissioner taking into account the instructions in this circular.
This Government Order is being issued by the Finance Department in accordance with the provisions of Government Resolution No. 2016/ No. 7/16/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 20.02.2016.
