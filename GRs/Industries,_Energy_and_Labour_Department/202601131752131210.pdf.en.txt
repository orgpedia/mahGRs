The Proposal: -
The scheme "Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 25/02/2014 to promote cluster development of small clusters in industrially underdeveloped areas of the state thereby promoting holistic development of micro and small units. Under the scheme, state government grants are provided for implementation of capacity building programmes for holistic development and augmentation of micro and small enterprises as well as for setting up of common facilities. Under this scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared comprising of up-to-date technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial clusters, up-to-date training programmes, study tours, etc., by properly diagnosing the defects, deficiencies in the manufacturing process of the industrial group.
2. Once the diagnostic study report is received after the recommendation of the district level committee, it is followed by the approval of diagnostic study report proposals and fixation of grant for capacity building programme etc. by the State Level Industrial Group Control Committee headed by the Development Commissioner (Industries). The grants for the capacity building programme are disbursed in two phases. The first tranche of 60 per cent subsidy to be disbursed by the government is disbursed after ensuring that the required 10 per cent participation of the stakeholders is accumulated. The remaining 40 per cent subsidy is disbursed after the submission of the first phase utilisation certificate.
3. Capacity building programme of 06 industrial cluster projects has been approved in the meeting of State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee held on 12/01/2024, 30/01/2024, 14/10/2024. Accordingly, Phase-II approval orders for these projects have been issued by the Directorate of Industries dated 28/08/2025, dated 15/09/2025 and dated 16/10/2025.
4. In pursuance of the decision taken by the State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee in its meeting held on 12/01/2024, 30/01/2024, 14/10/2024, the said ARH is considering sanction of grant in aid amounting to Rs. 17,87,000 in Phase-II (equivalent to 40) for 06 Industrial Cluster Projects for implementation of Capacity Building Programme as proposed by the Directorate of Industries vide letter dated 27/10/2025.
