Introduction: The Department of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India has come out with the National Policy on Electronics-2019.
The new electronics policy has been announced through a notification dated February 25, 2019. In accordance with this policy
Department of Electronics and Information Technology Modified Electronics Manufacturing Clusters (EMC 2.0) has been notified vide Notification dated 01/04/2020. Announced on 01/06/2020.
Electronics Manufacturing Clusters (EMC) and Common Facility Centres under EMC 2.0
Financial assistance for CFCs will be provided by the Central Government within the limit of 50% of the project cost (upto Rs.70 crore per 100 acres) for EMC projects and the remaining 50% will be provided by the concerned State Government or State Planning Authority. For CFCs, financial assistance will be provided by the Central Government within the limit of 75% of the project cost (up to Rs.75 crore) and the remaining 25% will be provided by the concerned State Government or State Planning Authority. The matter of appointing a State Implementing Agency for the effective implementation of the scheme in the State was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) is being appointed as the State Implementing Agency for the implementation of Modified Electronics Manufacturing Clusters (EMC 2.0) in the State. The MIDC should take all possible measures to ensure that the state gets the benefit of the above scheme of the central government at the earliest.
