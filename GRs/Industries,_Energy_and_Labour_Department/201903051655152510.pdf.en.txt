The Maharashtra Shops and Establishments (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 2017
The Maharashtra Shops and Establishments Act (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Rules, 2018 were published on 23/03/2018. Rule 5 of the said Rules lays down the procedure for registration of an establishment. As per the said procedure, the application in the prescribed format for obtaining the registration certificate shall be digitally signed on the registration form in the form of Form B of the registration form by approving it within seven days of the office working day from the date of receipt on the dashboard of the facilitator.
Section 6 (2) of the Maharashtra Shops and Establishments (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 2017 provides for submission of the application form through online computer system.
Under the Ease of Doing Business policy of the government, the Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion (DIPP) has suggested that the registration certificate issued under the said Act should be issued within a day after receiving the application. As notified by the Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion (DIPP), the issue of registration certificate was under the consideration of the Government within one day of receipt of application for registration under the said Act. Based on this, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under Section 6 (2) of the Maharashtra Shops and Establishments Act (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 2017, the applicant should submit a fully filled application in the prescribed format for taking registration certificate and complete application form along with the necessary documents mentioned in the schedule to the Maharashtra Shops and Establishments Act (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Rules, 2018 online and it should be approved on the day of office work from the date of receipt on the facilitator's dashboard and the registration form in the form of registration form-" B" should be digitally signed.
