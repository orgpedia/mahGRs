The Proposal:
The Ministry of Mines, Government of India has notified the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 2015 vide Government Notification dated 27.03.2015 by amending the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, 1957.
As per the provisions of Section 9 (B) of the present Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 2015, District Mineral Foundations are required to be established in every district of the State for the development of mining affected areas and individuals. In accordance with this provision, the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, Government Notification No. As on 01.09.2016, the rules regarding the establishment, structure and functioning of District Mineral Foundations in the State were prescribed.
However, the Union Ministry of Mines, Government of India vide Reference No. 2 here. As per the order dated 23.04.2021 regarding amendment in the structure of Regulatory Council and Managing Committee mentioned at sub-section 3 of section 9 (B) of the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 2015, the structure of Regulatory Council and Managing Committee under District Mineral Foundation vide reference No. 3 here. This has been amended vide Government Notification dated 06.12.2021.

9. Many components under the ambit of Pradhan Mantri Khanij Kshetra Kalyan Yojana are linked to various government departments and development schemes. Therefore, the work to be undertaken under this can also be scheduled by the concerned departments. Avoiding duplication and misuse of public resources and funds, setting priorities and maintaining inter-departmental coordination are essential.
Therefore, it should be ensured that all the works to be undertaken by the Regulatory Council under the Pradhan Mantri Khanij Kshetra Kalyan Yojana are brought to the notice of the State Level Regulatory Committee before approval.
