Out of this provision, a grant of Rs. 11,62,66, 000/-and Rs. 60,900/-for meeting the salary, superannuation, etc. and non-salary expenses of the Board for the month of May and June, 2020 respectively has been disbursed as per the Government decision dated 2nd July, 2020.
Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, vide its Reference No. 3, has requested to provide a grant of Rs. 17,43,99, 000/- for meeting the expenditure on salary, retirement salary for three months from July to September, 2020 and Rs. 69,00,000/- for 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary).
The Government was considering providing subsidy to Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board to meet the salary, retirement salary and non-salary expenses for the month of July, 2020.
Government's decision:
The Government have approved the release of grant in aid worth Rs. 5,81,33, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores One Hundred and Eighty Three Lakh Thirty Three Thousand only) to Khadi and Village Industries Board (KVIC) towards 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to meet the expenditure on salary, superannuation salary, contribution for the month of July, 2020.
Expenditure on this should be met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2020-21 under the target Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (2851 0585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and should be spent under the same head.
