Preamble: The recommendations of the 6th Pay Commission have been implemented for the employees of Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board as per the decision of the Government under Section No. 1 of the Industries, Energy and Labour Department. Retired employees of the Board. The High Court of Judicature at Mumbai, vide Writ Petition No. 3961/2003 was filed. On this petition. The High Court, Bombay vide its Judgment dated 12th April, 2006 has ruled that the recommendations of the 6th Pay Commission for the period of service from 01.01.2006 to 31.03.2009 should be made applicable to the retired employees of the Board. Accordingly, the amount payable to 481 retired officers/ employees of the Board as per the recommendations of the 6th Pay Commission has been paid by the Government vide order No. 2 dated 01.02.2016 and the arrears of the 6th Pay Commission have been paid to the retiring officers/ employees from time to time. The matter of clearing the salary arrears of 410 employees of Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board for the period from 01.01.2006 to 31.03.2009 of the 6th Pay Commission was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
410 employees of Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board (MSKV& VB) have been paid arrears of 6th Pay Commission for the period 01.01.2006 to 31.03.2009. 6,75,79, 407/- (Rupees Six Crores Seventy Five Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand Four Hundred and Seven only) is being allowed through this Government Resolution.
The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of such amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government and the Drawing and Disbursing Officers within the prescribed time. As well as the cost of books
