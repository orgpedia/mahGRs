The proposal: The Government has come out with a comprehensive" Krishi Pump Power Connection Policy-2020" for the pending payment of electricity connection from 01.04.2018. As per Government Decision dated 18.12.2020, (w.e.f. 01.04.2018, only High Pressure Distribution System Scheme was being implemented to provide power connection to the existing pending agricultural pumps. The government has introduced a new policy. The implementation of this policy is underway through the MSEDCL. Under this policy, agricultural pumps are being provided electricity connection on the short-pressure system through aerial bunch cable if the distance is up to 200 meters from the short-pressure channel. Further, it has been approved to provide electricity connections through high pressure distribution system to agricultural pumps located at a distance of more than 200 meters from the low pressure channel but within 600 meters from the high pressure channel. The solar pumps are being provided to the agricultural pumps located more than 600 meters away from the high pressure channel.

As per the provisions of the policy, the electricity connection to the agricultural pumps located at a distance of 0-200 meters from the small pressure channel through service wire or aerial bunch cable on the small pressure channel as per the requirement will be provided by the MSEDCL through its own funds.
