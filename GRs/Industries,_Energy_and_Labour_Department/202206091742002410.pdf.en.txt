Introduction Regarding the wages and conditions of service of the workers in the sugar factories in the state of Maharashtra. To make recommendations/ suggest measures to the Government and the Tripartite Committee constituted under the Government Resolution dated 12.11.2020. As per the Government decision dated 04.03.2021, a four-member committee has been constituted. The committee's term of office was three months. This period The period ended on 03.06.2021. Pursuant to the Government Decision dated 07.07.2021. 04.06.2021 for a further period of 06 months and thereafter. As per the Government Decision dated 16.12.2021. This has been extended for a further period of 06 months with effect from 04.12.2021. At present, 4 meetings of the committee have been held. The committee could not complete its work due to Covid restrictions and other administrative reasons. The Labour Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has requested for an extension of 02 months from 04.06.2022. He then referred the matter to the committee. The matter of extension of 2 months beyond 04.06.2022 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION The Government hereby approve the extension of time for making recommendations/ suggesting measures to the Government on the demands of the sugar factory workers in the State of Maharashtra and to the tripartite committee constituted under the Government decision dated 12.11.2020, for a period of two months with effect from 04.06.2022. The other provisions of the Government Order dated 04.03.2021 shall continue to remain in force.
