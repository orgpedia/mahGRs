Introduction: Akole, G. Ahmednagar is a tribal and remote taluka and is scattered in villages and villages. At present, the sub-station at Rajur is being demolished. Electricity is supplied to the area. Due to the long distance from the substation, there is a power leakage, due to which the farmers of this area have to suffer from irregular load regulation. In order to provide uninterrupted and proper power supply to the consumers of Akole taluka, it is necessary to set up a power sub-station at Padalne.
In the absence of an electricity sub-station in the area, the power supply is often interrupted and it is necessary to set up a power sub-station there.

The 33/11 KV sub-station at Padalane will provide uninterrupted and sustainable power supply to 910 agricultural pump consumers in 7 villages and 1730 domestic, commercial and industrial and street lights with proper pressure.
Considering the above. The government was considering granting administrative approval for setting up a sub-centre at Akole under a state-level scheme for tribal applications.
Government's decision:
1.Take a bath Administrative approval is being accorded to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 291.708 lakh under State Level Scheme for setting up of 33/11 KV, 5 MVA power sub-station at Akole under Tribal Sub Plan Area.
