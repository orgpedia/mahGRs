Formation of Implementation Committee for day-to-day functioning of Maharashtra State Social Security Board
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour,
Government Resolution No.Assanka/ 2018/ P.S. No.120/ Work-7A
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai- 400032
December 17, 2018
Preamble: The Government of India with the objective of providing social security to the unorganized workers of India through various welfare schemes. In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 6 (4), 6 (6), 6 (7), 10 (1) and Section 14 of the Unorganized Workers Social Security Act, 2008, which came into force on 31/12/2008, the Government of Maharashtra vide Notification No. Maharashtra Unorganized Workers Social Security Rules, 2013 have been made applicable under 30/05/2013.
Maharashtra State Social Security Board under Section 6 (1) of the Unorganized Workers Social Security Act, 2008. It was established on April 3, 2018. The Maharashtra State Social Security Board has decided to constitute an" Implementation Committee" for the day-to-day functioning of the Maharashtra State Social Security Board to provide benefits of welfare schemes implemented by the state government to the unorganized workers registered under the corporation under Section 3 (4) of the Act.
The first meeting was held on 4/8/2018. The government was contemplating to take further steps in this regard.
Government Decision: The Implementation Committee for carrying out the day-to-day activities of the Maharashtra State Social Security Board for providing benefits of various welfare schemes for the unorganized workers registered under the Maharashtra State Social Security Board constituted under Section 6 (1) of the Unorganized Workers Social Security Act, 2008 is being constituted as follows.
Development Commissioner (Unorganized Workers)- Chairman
2) Assistant Labour Commissioner, Office of the Development Commissioner- Member Secretary
3) Government Labour Officer, Office of the Development Commissioner- Member
