Preamble: Reference to General Administration Department regarding direct recruitment of nominated quota vacancies in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D cadres in the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) on the establishment of Directorate of Industries
The consolidated guidelines have been issued under the Government Decision No. 2.
Accordingly, a district, regional and state-level selection committee has been constituted to carry out the recruitment process in the said cadre and the selection committees are to be constituted accordingly. For recruitment to the post of Group-C cadre in the Directorate of Industries under this Department, a Regional Selection Committee has been constituted at the State level under the Chairmanship of Development Commissioner (Industries) and under the Chairmanship of Joint Director of Industries, Zonal Office of the concerned Regional Department, as per the Government decision of Reference No. 1. The matter of reconstitution of the said Committee in accordance with the Government decision at Reference No. 2 of the General Administration Department was under the consideration of this Department.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: By the Government Decision at Reference No. 1 of this Division to fill up the vacancies in the Group C cadre under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board at the establishment of the Directorate of Industries
The State Level Selection Committee and the Regional Selection Committee are being reconstituted on the basis of the suggestions received under the Government Decision in Reference No.2 of the General Administration Department to fill the vacancies in the nomination quota in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D cadres under the purview of the erstwhile Secondary Service Selection Board (outside the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission) directly.
