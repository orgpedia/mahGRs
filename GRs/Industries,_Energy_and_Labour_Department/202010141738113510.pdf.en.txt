The Proposal:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 17.04.2018 has approved the implementation of High Pressure Distribution System (HDPS) scheme by MSEDCL for the agricultural pump applicants who are waiting for payment of electricity connection in the state after 31.03.2018. The project cost of Rs. 5048.13 crore has been approved. Of this, Rs 2, 248.09 crore has been approved as grant-in-aid to MSEDCL for providing electricity connections to pending agricultural pumps in Vidarbha and Marathwada and for setting up of power sub-stations in these areas.
The scheme is being implemented in the state from October, 2018. At present, 90,827 agricultural pumps have been provided electricity connection under this scheme. Also, the state government has disbursed Rs 1, 134.22 crore to MSEDCL at the end of March 2020. Accordingly, the Asian Development Bank (ADB) carried out a field inspection with MSEDCL from 19th to 26th July, 2019 and based on the progress of the scheme, a policy for disbursement of funds has been decided.
The loan is being processed by the Department of Economic Affairs (DEA) of the central government. The Principal Secretary (Financial Reforms) is the nodal officer of the state government in this regard.
The loan will be repaid to the state government and there will be a moratorium of five years on repayment of loan and principal. The repayment period will be from 2025 to 2040. The repayment of loan and principal will be half-yearly i.e. on 15th October and 15th April of every month during the repayment period. The interest rate of the loan will be LIBOR (London Inter Bank Offer Rate+ 0.5% and Commitment Charges 0.15% per annum). The loan will be disbursed after verification of Disbursement Link Indicator by an independent agency.

Funds will be provided by the government. The Asian Development Bank vide letter dated 30.04.2020 has informed that it has sanctioned a loan of US$ 346 million. The matter was under consideration of the government to approve the loan. Accordingly, the following resolution was adopted:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Approval is being given to take a loan of Rs. 2,248 crore (US$ 346 million) from Asian Development Bank through the State Government as reimbursement for implementation of High Pressure Distribution System Scheme of the Department of Energy in the entire State.
Accordingly, the Central Government/ State Government and MSEDCL are being approved to enter into a collateral agreement in the prescribed format with the Asian Development Bank.
Accordingly, it is being approved to authorize the Resident Commissioner, New Delhi to sign on behalf of the State Government on the loan agreement to be made with the Asian Development Bank.
Also, Executive Director-3 (PA) is being authorized on behalf of MSEDCL.
(2) This Government decision was taken with the concurrence of the Finance Department and in its informal reference No. 77/2020/ Finance Department. Issued on 14.10.2020.
