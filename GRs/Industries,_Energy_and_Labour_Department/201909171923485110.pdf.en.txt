The transfer orders of the officers who have completed three years of service as on 31/05/2019 have been issued under the relevant Government Orders. Accordingly, with the approval of the Competent Authority and the senior officer next to him, the officers mentioned in the table below are being posted in the place of the revised posting indicated in column-4 before their names in the transfer posting.
