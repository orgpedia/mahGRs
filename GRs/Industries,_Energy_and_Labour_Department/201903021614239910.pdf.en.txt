The Proposal:
Provision has been made for wide publicity under Rule 2A of the Child and Adolescent Labour (Prohibition and Regulation) Act, 1986 (Amended Act 2016) Rules, 1988. 26/2011 vide order dated 08.02.2018 to strictly enforce the Act.
An amount of Rs. 3.29 crore has been earmarked for the financial year 2018-19 for publicizing the scheme with the objective of reaching out to the general public through advertisements to eradicate the practice of child and adolescent labour and to eliminate this practice through public participation. Out of this, 80% i.e. Rs. 2,63,20, 000/- has been made available for distribution on computer system.
In this regard, for the financial year 2018-19, the following committee was constituted under the chairmanship of the Commissioner (Labour) for issuing work orders to the agencies authorized by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for publishing advertisements through the agencies authorized by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations in accordance with the media plan approved by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for advertising, publicity and publicity to create mass awareness against the evil practice of child labour.
