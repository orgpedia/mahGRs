The Proposal:
The state government will provide subsidy to the consumers of agricultural pumps, machinery, textile and industrial categories.
As per the policy, the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) provides rebate on electricity tariff and the government reimburses the same to MSEDCL. Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company to provide financial assistance to customers of agricultural pumps, machinery, textile and industrial categories in the financial year 2020-21
Under Demand No.K-6 under the head Account Head 28015572, Account Head 28015581, Account Head 28015599 and Account Head 28015605 respectively, an amount of Rs. 5019.9699 crore, Rs. 600.00 crore, Rs. 200.00
crore and Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 600.00 crore has been made.
In accordance with the above approved provision, the MSEDCL will receive about 10% of the total amount of Rs. 640.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
1. Discount on electricity tariff for agricultural pump consumers:
(1) The amount of financial assistance payable to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the rebate given on the electricity tariff to the agricultural pump consumers shall be Rs. The Government has decided to disburse Rs. 500.00 crore (Rupees Five Hundred Crores only) in cash.
(2) The expenditure for the said purpose shall be spent under the head" Demand No.K-6,2801 Electricity, 05 Transmission and Distribution, 104, Power Development System National Load, (01) Electricity Rate Concession, (01) (01) Electricity Rate Concession (General) to Agricultural Pump Customers (28015572), (Programme) 33 Financial Assistance" and shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2020-21.

2. Concession in power tariff to consumers:
(1) The amount of financial assistance payable to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the rebate given on the electricity tariff to the mill consumers shall not exceed Rs. The Government has decided to disburse Rs. 60.00 crore (Rupees Sixty Crores only) in cash.
(2) The expenditure for the said purpose shall be spent under the head" Demand No. K-6,2801 Electricity, 05 Transmission and Distribution, 104, Power Development System National Load, (01) Electricity Rate Concession, (01) (02) Electricity Rate Concession (General) (28015581), (Programme) 33 Financial Assistance to Machinery Consumers" and it shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2020-21.
3. Concession in electricity tariff for textile consumers:
(1) The amount of financial assistance payable to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the rebate given on electricity tariff to textile consumers shall be Rs. The Government has decided to disburse Rs. 20.00 crore (Rupees Twenty Crores only) in cash.
(2) The expenditure for the said purpose shall be spent under the head" Demand No.K-6,2801 Electricity, 05 Transmission and Distribution, 104, Power Development System National Load, (01) Electricity Rate Concession, (01) (03) Electricity Rate Concession to Textile Consumers (General) (28015599), (Programme) 33 Financial Assistance" and shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2020-21.
4. Concession in power tariff for industrial consumers:
(1) The amount of financial assistance payable to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the rebate given on electricity tariff to industrial consumers shall be Rs. The Government has decided to disburse Rs. 60.00 crore (Rupees Sixty Crores only) in cash.
(2) The expenditure for the said purpose shall be spent under the head" Demand Series K-6,2801 Electricity, 05 Transmission and Distribution, 104, Power Development System National Load, (01) Electricity Rate Concession, (01) (04) Electricity Rate Concession to Industrial Consumers (General) (28015606), (Programme) 33 Financial Assistance" and it shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2020-21.
5. The MSEDCL shall ensure that the funds are spent for the purpose for which they were sanctioned.
The Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission (MERC) has disallowed certain power consumption on agricultural pump usage. Care should be taken that the subsidy received from the government is not used for concessions on the use of this rejected electricity.
7. The said amount shall be drawn from the treasury through the Admiralty and Accounts Officer, Mumbai and paid to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Mumbai.
Mr. U.D. Walunj, Joint Secretary (Power-3), Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Mumbai is being authorized as Under Secretary (Power-3) and Controlling Officer. The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited has sanctioned Rs. 640.00 crore (Rupees Six Hundred and Forty only) drawn from the Subordinate and Accounts Officer, Mumbai and payable to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Mumbai by separate cheque.
