The Proposal:
The consumers of different categories in the state are given discounts on electricity rates as per the government policy.
For the financial year 2018-19, Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company has received a total amount of Rs. The total budget outlay is Rs. 6941.41 crore. Out of the budgetary provision, Reference No. 2 and 3, Rs. 1595.76 crore in adjustment and Rs. 1682.79 crore in cash. 3278.55 crore has been disbursed. Of the remaining Rs. 565.41 crore to MSEDCL in cash was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The amount of financial assistance payable to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the rebate given on electricity tariff to agricultural consumers and machinery consumers for the financial year 2018-19 is Rs. The Government hereby approve the release of Rs. 565.41 crore (Rupees Five Hundred Sixty Five Crores Forty One Lakhs Only) in cash.
The expenditure for the said purpose shall be covered under the head" Demand No.K-6,2801 Electricity, 05 Transmission& Distribution, 800 Other Expenses, (00) (01) Financial Assistance to Distribution/ Transmission Licensees for Reduction of Agricultural and Machinery Tariff (28012615), 33 Financial Assistance (Programme)" and shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2018-19.
The said amount is being authorized to be withdrawn from the treasury through the Subordinate and Accounts Officer, Mumbai and paid to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Mumbai with the Under Secretary (Power-3) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Power-3), Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry, Mumbai as Controlling Officer. The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) has been sanctioned Rs. 565.41 crore (Rupees Five Hundred Sixty Five Crores Forty One Lakhs only) drawn from the Authority of Accounts and Accounts, Mumbai and payable to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Mumbai.
