The government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government regarding Mukhyamantri Solar Krishi Pump Yojana, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has provided Rs. 73.6594 crore for the year 2018-19 for the benefit of Scheduled Castes. However, as per Government decision dated 15.11.2018, 2953 SC beneficiaries are available. Accordingly, the amount of Rs. 51.02 crore. Considering the benefit given to the direct scheduled caste beneficiaries, this fund is being made available on the condition that the amount remaining from the above fund will be returned. The Government is approving the release of Rs. 51.02 crore (Rupees Fifty One Crore Two Lakh only) to the Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Power Distribution Company Limited, Prakashgarh, Bandra (East), Mumbai through the Controlling Authorities through the Budget Fund Distribution System.
Walunj, Joint Secretary (Power), Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Mumbai as Controlling Officer and Shri N. R. Dhane, Under Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Mumbai as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The said amount shall be paid in the name of Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Prakashgarh, Bandra (East), Mumbai. The monthly and quarterly reports with information about the expenditure, targets and actual achievement of various schemes under the solar power agri-pump installation programme should be submitted by MSEDCL to the government as well as to the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance by the 10th of the next month. The MSEDCL should ensure that the amount sanctioned under this Government decision is spent only for the implementation of various schemes under the Solar Energy Agri-plantation programme for the financial year 2018-19 for the benefit of Scheduled Castes only.
The documents relating to the accounting of the said fund should be submitted by Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Prakashgarh, Bandra (East), Mumbai to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai, for inspection, as and when required.
