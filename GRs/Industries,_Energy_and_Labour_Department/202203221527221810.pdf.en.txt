Introduction: The increasing number of educated youth in urban and rural areas of the state and new opportunities for self-employment and employment in various fields in the state in the field of industry, business. Considering these new opportunities of self-employment and employment, the comprehensive scheme of' Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme' (CMEGP) has been implemented on the lines of the existing scheme of' Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme' (PMEGP), which promotes entrepreneurship and promotes creativity in a time-bound manner. Accordingly, State Level and District Level Controlling Committees have been constituted to provide various opportunities of self-employment to the urban and rural youth of the state and for effective implementation of the scheme.
Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program, the state government will provide financial assistance in the form of grants from the state government's budgetary provision to establish self-employment projects to make the youth of the state self-sufficient/ self-reliant. Accordingly, a new Account Head for General Category and Tribal Category has been opened under the Scheme vide Government Resolution dated 23/01/2020. Also, for the Scheduled Castes Sub Plan, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance provides provision in accordance with the objectives.

The Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme is being implemented through the District Industries Center under the Directorate of Industries under the Industries Department and the District Khadi and Village Industries Office under the Khadi and Village Industries Board. Under this scheme, a total amount of Rs. 64,87,200/- and Rs. 20,55,600/- has been spent for the online training program implemented by Maharashtra Entrepreneurship Development Center (MCED) and Mitcon Consultancy and Engineering Services Limited in the financial year 2020-21 and 2021-22 respectively. As per the provisions of the said scheme, an amount of Rs. 85,42,800/- required for the online training program implemented by the above-mentioned training institutions and Rs. 2,43,333/- required for the publicity and publicity by the institutions under the Information and Public Relations Office regarding this scheme was under the consideration of the Government to be disbursed from the approved budgetary provision for the financial year 2021-22.
Government Decision: A total amount of Rs. 85,42,800/- (Rupees Eighty Five Lakhs Forty Two Thousand Eight Hundred Only) is required for the online training program implemented by the training institutions under the Chief Minister Employment Generation Program (CMEGP) of the State Government in the financial year 2020-21 and 2021-22 and an amount of Rs. 2,43,333/- (Rupees Two Lakh Thirty Three Thousand Three Hundred Thirty Three Only) is required for the publicity and publicity of the scheme by the institutions under the Information and Public Relations Office since 2019 in accordance with the objectives and effective implementation of the scheme. 2 and 4 are hereby approved by this Government Resolution to be disbursed as per the provisions of the said Scheme.
