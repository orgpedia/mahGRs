the prelude
The government has taken a decision. In pursuance of the amendments proposed in the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 vide order dated 6th March, 2018, the Government at the level of Labour Commissioner, Mumbai, has been requested to hold meetings with the concerned to resolve the representations/ complaints received from various trade unions and submit a final report to the Government. A tripartite committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Minister (Labour). The term of the Tripartite Committee will expire on 6th June, 2018. Therefore, the matter of extending the term of the said tripartite committee was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision
In pursuance of the amendments proposed in section 2A, section 25F (b), section 25K (1)& (1A), section 25N (1) (b), section 25N (9) and section 25O (8) of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, after considering the representations/ complaints received from various trade unions and the Company Management, the Board has decided to hold meetings with all the stakeholders to resolve the grievances and submit a final report to the Government. The Tripartite Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Labour) is being extended for a further period of three months with effect from 6th June, 2018.
The said Tripartite Committee shall consider the various representations/ complaints received/ received on the proposed amendments to the said Act and hold meetings with all the stakeholders and submit a final report to the Government within the stipulated time.
