A committee has been formed to decide on the setting up of a drone city and a manufacturing cluster in Aurangabad.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Resolution No. Asandho-2022/ P.S. No. 182/ Industries-2
Madam Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400032
Date: January 16, 2023
Director, Brahma Research Foundation, Bandra, Mumbai submitted a memorandum to the government demanding setting up of a drone hub and manufacturing cluster at Auric City, Aurangabad, formulation of a policy for manufacturing safety related equipment, creation of a special economic zone for the same and providing related facilities. The Deputy Chief Minister, Shri.
The demands of the Institute were discussed in the meeting on Industrial Investment held at Aurangabad on 16/11/2022 under the chairmanship of Minister (Industries).
It has directed the institute to provide necessary information to the government as per the demands made by the institute. The government had constituted a committee to look into the demands.
Government's decision:
The following Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Development Commissioner (Industries) to consider and decide on the demands put forth by the Director, Brahma Research Foundation, Bandra, Mumbai.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows: 1) A detailed examination of the demands made by the Brahma Research Foundation, Bandra, Mumbai, the provisions of the policy of the Central Government and the State Government in this regard, as well as related matters, shall be made by the Committee and a detailed report shall be prepared by the Committee accordingly.
The committee will submit its report to the government within three months.
