Government's stand:
The State Cabinet in its meeting held on 20.04.2021 had approved the constitution of a empowered High Powered Committee for taking up emergency works to contain the spread of COVID-19 pandemic, providing facilities to the affected patients and for making emergency purchases of medicines and other materials. The government has taken the decision. The order was issued on 23.04.2021. The following amendments are being made in the composition of the Empowered High Powered Committee in this Order.
3.3.1.2 Emergency Procurement and Operations in the Background of COVID-19 Pandemic:
The Government is approving the constitution of a High Powered Committee to be empowered to undertake emergency works to prevent the spread of COVID-19 pandemic, to provide facilities to the affected patients and to make emergency purchases of medicines and other materials.
The composition of the proposed Empowered High Powered Committee is being changed as follows
