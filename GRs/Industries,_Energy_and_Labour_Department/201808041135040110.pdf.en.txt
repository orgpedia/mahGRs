The Proposal:
The government has decided to support investors in accordance with the state industrial policy. Integrated Facilitation Centre Maharashtra Industry, Trade and Investment Facilitation Centre (Maitri) has been established at Mumbai with effect from 27.2.2014. The government has taken several steps to improve the ease of doing business. This will ensure smooth and balanced industrial development of the state and attract more and more investments to the state. In this regard, all the services, information and guidance are made available to the investors at one place under the single window concept through this friendship room. Also, as per the instructions of the Central Government, the work related to the ongoing activities of the Department of Industry under EODB and Doing Business is being done by the Maitri Cell and the government is considering to publish advertisements on television, radio, social media, audio, internet etc. to make the activities of this Maitri Cell popular among the entrepreneurs and investors of the state and outside the state.
Accordingly, Business Today magazine has published a 16 paani special edition in line with Ease of Doing Business with the approval of the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations. In the Business Today Magazine dated 20.05.2018, a two-page special supplement was also published. This was published in the issue of India Today dated 28.05.2018. The proposal for this expenditure was made by the Directorate of Industries under reference No. 6 here. Submitted vide letter dated 06/ 07/2018. As per the proposal, the matter of approving the above advertisement expenditure for the publicity and dissemination of the friendship cell was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In order to popularize and promote the Maitri Cell, the Director General, Directorate General of Information and Public Relations has decided to publish a 16 page special edition in line with the Ease of Doing Business in the magazine Business Today. 5 here. Approval has been given for publication of the said Advtorial vide letter dated 27/04/2018.
