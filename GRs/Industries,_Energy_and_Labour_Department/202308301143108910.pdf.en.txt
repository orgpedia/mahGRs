Preamble: To take a decision on the proposals received for the release of funds under the scheme of Maharashtra Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development. The 9th meeting of the Steering Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary was held on 03/02/2023. In the meeting, the committee has approved funds for the infrastructure works of a total of 10 cooperative industrial estates. Out of which, Rs. 46,26,230/-has been sanctioned by the Committee for installation of Express Feeder of Chakur Cooperative Industrial Estate Limited, Chakur, Latur District. The proposal for grant of administrative approval for the activities of the Institute has been submitted to the Government by the Directorate of Industries vide their letter dated 20/07/2023.
The proposal has been examined and the matter of granting administrative approval for the works of the said Institute amounting to Rs. 46,26,230/- was under consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government: Under the scheme of Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development, the Steering Committee has approved the distribution of funds. The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) has submitted a budget of Rs. 46,26,230/- for the work of Express Feeder of Chakur Cooperative Industrial Estate Limited, Chakur, Latur District. The details is as follows.
