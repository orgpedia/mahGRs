Santosh B. Naikwade, Medical Officer, Group-A, Public Health Department is being appointed as a Certified Surgeon, Group-A, Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health, Mumbai on deputation for a period of 3 years subject to the following terms and conditions:
Dr. Santosh B. Naikwade, Medical Officer, will commence his deputation from the date on which he enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner and shall conclude his service on the date on which he resumes the charge of his original office.
(b) The Government/ Competent Authority i.e. Department of Public Health shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by a non-employee employer, the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, the department must give three months' notice to the Department of Public Health before such repatriation; and
(d) Dr. Naikwade shall be returned to the parent department after giving at least three months notice to the Department of Industries, Power and Labour that he intends to return to the parent department.
