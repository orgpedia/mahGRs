Introduction: The increasing number of educated youth in urban and rural areas of the state and new opportunities for self-employment and employment in various fields in the state in the field of industry, business. Considering these new opportunities of self-employment and employment, the comprehensive scheme' Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme' (CMEGP), which promotes entrepreneurship and promotes creativity in a time-bound manner, has been implemented under the Government Decision dated 01/08/2019. Accordingly, State Level and District Level Controlling Committees have been constituted to provide various opportunities of self-employment to the urban and rural youth of the state and for effective implementation of the scheme.
Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program, financial assistance from the state government will be provided in the form of subsidy from the state government's budgetary provision to establish self-employment projects to make the youth of the state self-sufficient/ self-reliant. Accordingly, a new head of account has been opened for the General Category and Scheduled Tribes under the scheme vide Government Order dated 23/01/2020. Also, for the Scheduled Castes category, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance provides provision in accordance with the objectives.
The Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme is being implemented through the District Industries Center under the Directorate of Industries under the Industries Department and the District Khadi and Village Industries Office under the Khadi and Village Industries Board. Accordingly, the Additional Director of Industries, Directorate of Industries vide their letter dated 01/02/2023 has requested to make available the margin money amount of Rs. 103.06 crore, required for the pending loan cases for the distribution of general category under the scheme, for distribution under the objective of 31-Support Grants (Non-Payment) under the scheme. Accordingly, the general category was approved under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program.
