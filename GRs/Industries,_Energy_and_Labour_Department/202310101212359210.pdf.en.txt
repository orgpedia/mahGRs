The Additional Chief Secretary and Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra State have been requested to conduct the following campaign by the industrial groups, companies and institutions of Nashik and Amravati divisions so that eligible graduate persons of Mumbai and Konkan divisions can register in large numbers as graduate voters in those respective divisions to vote in the above mentioned biennial elections. The concerned Divisional Commissioner is the Electoral Registration Officer and the District Collector is the Assistant Electoral Registration Officer.
Industry groups, companies and institutions in Mumbai and Konkan region should campaign as follows: As the electoral roll for the Graduate Legislative Council constituency is being prepared in Mumbai and Konkan region, eligible graduate employees from the industry groups, companies and institutions in that region (who have held a degree at least 3 years before the qualifying date 1/11/2023) should be invited and encouraged to register in the electoral roll of the respective graduate constituency.
Eligible graduate employees working in industrial groups, companies and institutions in Mumbai and Konkan region will have to fill up Form 18 to register their names in the list of the respective Graduate Legislative Council Constituency. These forms will be available in the office of the concerned Divisional Commissioner (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), concerned District Collector (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), Deputy Divisional Commissioner and Tehsildar as designated by the Divisional Commissioner.
