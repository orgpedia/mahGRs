Preamble: A High Powered Committee under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary has been constituted vide Government Resolution dated 12/09/1984 to approve the budget for participation of State Government in India International Trade Fair and the expenditure to be incurred on it. Accordingly, the committee plans and organizes the program. The expenditure for the programme is to be met from the funds available during the financial year and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer is Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. Thereafter, vide Government Resolution dated 27/08/1996, the said High Powered Committee has been reconstituted and constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary (Trade and Commerce), which was earlier headed by Additional Chief Secretary.
The India International Trade Fair is organised by India Trade Promotion Organisation, New Delhi every year from 14-27 November at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi. In this fair, Maharashtra Pavilion is organized/ set up by Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation as per the theme decided by India Trade Promotion Organization on behalf of the Government of Maharashtra. The theme for this year's India International Trade Fair is" Vocal to Local, Local to Global." The 68th meeting of the High Powered Committee was held under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Industries). The meeting approved an estimated expenditure of Rs. 3,63,43, 000/-for the said purpose. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 3 crore as made available by the Finance Department under the Scheme for Participation in Industrial Exhibitions and Trade Fairs (2851 0398) for organizing Maharashtra Pavilion at India International Trade Fair-2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: For organizing the Maharashtra Pavilion at India International Trade Fair-2022 to be organized by India Trade Promotion Organization, New Delhi at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi from 14th to 27th November, 2022, Shri M. As approved by the High Powered Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Industries), an amount of Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only) is being released to Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai.
