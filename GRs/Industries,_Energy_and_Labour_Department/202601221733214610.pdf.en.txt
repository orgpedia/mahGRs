The Proposal: -
To provide smooth power supply to the state by empowering the transmission lines in the state.
Director (Projects), Maharashtra State Power Transmission Company Limited. , Mumbai added a total of 20 new transmission channels.
The construction work is proposed to be taken up under Section 68 of the Indian Electricity Act, 2003.
The government had received a request to grant prior permission. Accordingly, the proposal was given prior approval.
The issue of payment was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Mentioned in Annexure-A attached by the Transmission Company to various districts of the State.
Section 68 of the Indian Electricity Act, 2003 for the construction of a total of 20 transmission lines as done
Under this, prior permission of the Government is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions: -
