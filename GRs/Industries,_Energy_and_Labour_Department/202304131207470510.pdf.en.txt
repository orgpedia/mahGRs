As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to retire officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in public interest at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 The procedures and general instructions regarding service review have been prescribed under the government decision here. Accordingly, the Grade Pay of Group-A (Gazetted) (ex-officio Senior Grade) in the Electricity Inspectorate has been increased from Rs. (2) Group-A (Gazetted) (Senior Pay Grade) Officers in the cadre of 7600 and above but less than 10000 (3) Group-A (Gazetted) (Junior Pay Grade) Officers in the cadre of 7600 and above and (4) Group-B (Gazetted) Officers in the cadre of 7600 and below. As per the decision of the Government, a Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary (Power)/ Principal Secretary (Power).
The age of the Electrical Inspector, Group-A (Gazetted) Cadre Officers in the Electrical Inspectorate shall be 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
Proposal for review of service It was submitted to the Departmental Review Committee headed by Principal Secretary (Power) in a circular manner. As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee after evaluating the service of the said officers, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the officers who have been found eligible to remain in service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
