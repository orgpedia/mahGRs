GOVERNMENT DECISIONS Regarding the problems of the sugar factory workers in the State of Maharashtra, a committee is being constituted under the Government decision dated 12.11.2020 to recommend to the tripartite committee constituted for the purpose of the sugar factory workers and to the Government/ to suggest measures as follows: Member- 1. Sugar Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Pune.
Commissioner of Labour, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
Managing Director, Maharashtra State Cooperative Sugar Factories Federation Ltd. , Mumbai.
Member Secretary- Deputy Commissioner of Labour (OS), Mumbai.
The Deputy Commissioner of Labour (OS), Mumbai is being appointed as Member-Secretary of the said Committee in addition to his own charge. He will hold a meeting of the committee.
The Committee will make recommendations to the Government on the following subjects: (i) To save the dues of the workers of the closed sugar mills.
2) Action to be taken to save the pending wages of sugar workers.
3) To take decisions on sugar mills that have not implemented the previous tripartite agreement.
The said Committee shall submit a report to the Government with recommendations within a period of 3 months from the date of constitution of the Committee on the issues of sugar factory workers in Maharashtra.
