Government's decision:
32 temporary gazetted and non-gazetted posts in the offices of the Directorate of Steam, Mumbai, Nagpur, Pune, Solapur, Nashik, Ahmednagar and Kolhapur have been notified vide reference No. 2 decision of the government. from 01.03.2018. The Government had approved the continuation till 30.09.2018.
2. After completion of the review of all the sanctioned posts of temporary gazetted and non-gazetted on the establishment in the Directorate of Vapors. With the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries and concurrence of the Finance Department, Reference No. Under 1, the orders regarding the diagram are issued. Some of the posts have been created and some of the posts have been transferred. In the present situation, all the posts are required and all the posts will be used in the future. Accordingly, 32 temporary gazetted and non-gazetted posts on the establishment of Directorate of Vapour, as mentioned in the accompanying prospectus, have been created. w.e.f. 01.10.2018 The Government is granting permission to continue till 28.02.2019.
(3) The office bearers shall be paid allowances as may be sanctioned by the Government from time to time.
4. The expenditure in this regard shall be covered under Demand No. K-4, headings 2230 Labour& Employment (01) Labourers (102) Working Conditions& Safety (00) (02) Directorate of Vaporizers (2230 0532) 01 Salary and shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2018-2019.
