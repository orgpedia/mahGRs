Re-constitution of the Expert Committee under the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour,
Government Order No. Building- 2015/ No.220/ Work- 7.
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is February 28, 2020
Read: Govt. Building-2015/ No.220/ Work-7 Dated: 15th June, 2016.
Government's stand:
As per the decision of the Government dated 15th June, 2016, the reconstituted Expert Committee has been constituted with the Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, Mumbai as its Member Secretary. Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, Mumbai has increased its workload. Accordingly, Secretary and CEO Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, Mumbai is being appointed as Member Secretary of the Expert Committee in place of Deputy Commissioner of Labour.
