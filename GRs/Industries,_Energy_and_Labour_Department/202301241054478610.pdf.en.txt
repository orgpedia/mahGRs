Preamble: In order to promote the development of small clusters in the industrially underdeveloped areas of the State and thereby promote the overall development of micro and small units, the Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP) has been announced vide Government Resolution dated 25/02/2014. Under the scheme, the state government provides grant-in-aid for the implementation of capacity building programmes for the overall development and growth of micro and small enterprises as well as for the construction of common facilities.
Under the MSICDP scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared by properly diagnosing the flaws in the production process of the industrial group, including the latest technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial groups, updated training programs and study tours, etc. After the diagnostic study report is received on the recommendation of the district level committee, the State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee approves the diagnostic study report proposal and determines the subsidy for the capacity building programme. A Detailed Project Report (DPR) containing details of high quality common facilities and other necessary clearances, no-objection certificates, technical and financial evaluation reports are prepared to enhance the productivity and competitiveness of MSME units. The procurement process is initiated after the final approval of the State Level Steering Committee for the proposal of the Common Facility Centre.
As per the revised guidelines of MSICDP Scheme dated 24/07/2019, after final approval of the proposal of Common Facility Centre by the State Level Steering Committee, the first phase 20% subsidy is disbursed to the projects whose procurement process has been completed by the Zonal Committee after receiving the report along with the procurement details. Thereafter, after receiving the physical verification report from the General Manager, District Industries Centre regarding the installation of machinery and equipment at the work site as per the project approval and after receiving the utilization certificate of the first phase grant, 60% of the second phase grant is disbursed. The remaining 20% subsidy is disbursed after receipt of certificate from the concerned Industry cum Director on successful completion of the Common Facility Centre.

As per proposal dated 12/01/2023 of the Directorate of Industries, the State Level Steering Committee for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Steel Furniture and Agri Equipment Cluster, Cage G. Beed, an industrial cluster project, has accorded final approval in its meeting dated 05/08/2022. Accordingly, the joint director of industries, Aurangabad, has submitted a report with the details of the procurement process through the departmental committee. Accordingly, the Government was considering to disburse the first phase grant of Rs. 1,40,59, 600/- from the State Government's share for the construction of Common Facility Centre of the project.
Government Decision: The Directorate of Industries has proposed to disburse the first phase grant of Rs. 1,40,59, 600/- from the State Government for the construction of Common Facility Centre of Steel Furniture and Agri Equipment Cluster, Cage G. Beed Project sanctioned under the scheme" Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" of the State Government. 3 is presented here. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the present scheme, the Government have approved the release of first instalment of subsidy amounting to Rs. 1,40,59, 600/- (Rupees One Crore Forty Lakh Sixty Nine Thousand Six Hundred only).
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall arrange to transfer the amount of Rs. 1,40,59, 600/- from the Government Treasury to the Steel Furniture and Agri Equipment Cluster, Cage G. Beed Group Project. The powers to draw and disburse the above mentioned amount of Rs. 1,40,59, 600/- are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
As per the provisions mentioned in the Government Circular No. 2022/ No. 43/ Finance-3 of the Finance Department, dated 04/04/2022 and the accompanying annexure, as well as the information received from the Directorate of Industries, it is certified that the Government Decision dated 25/02/2014 of the" Maharashtra State Industrial Group Development Program" has been incorporated in the scheme, which has given information about the duration of the scheme, the implementation mechanism, the distribution of grants, various sanctioning committees. Utilization certificates of the grants released earlier in connection with the said project have been made available by the Directorate of Industries. Also, no issues have been raised by the auditors with regard to the grants released earlier for the said project.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall take necessary action to monitor the progress of the said group project and the proper utilization and utilization of the Government shareholding.
The expenditure for this scheme should be spent under the head" Demand No. K-7, 2851- Village Industries and Small Industries, 00 102 Small Industries- Scheme under State Schemes (00) (24) Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP) (2851 6378) 33- Financial Assistance" and it should be met from the budgetary provision approved in the financial year 2022-23.
This Government decision is being issued under the authority conferred on the Administrative Department as the conditions contained in the Government Circular No. Finance-2022/ No. 43/ Finance-3, dated 04/04/2022 of the Department of Finance and its Annexure are being fulfilled.
