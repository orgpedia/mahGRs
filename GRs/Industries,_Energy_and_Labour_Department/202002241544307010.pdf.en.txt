Read:- General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. LPL-2017/ No. 21/ K. 10th June, 2019.
Government Decision: As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, officers of incompetent and doubtful integrity have been given their service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, subject to the procedure and general instructions regarding review of their service. Accordingly, the Grade Pay of (1) Group-A (Gazetted) (ex-officio Senior Grade) in the Electricity Inspectorate under the Department of Power is Rs. 10,000/- and above. (2) Officers of Group-A (Gazetted) (Senior Pay Scale) Grade Pay 7600 and above but below 10000. (3) Officers of Group-A (Gazetted) (Senior Pay Scale) Grade Pay 7600 and below. (4) Officers of Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre above 50/55 years of age or 30 years of qualifying service whichever is earlier.
