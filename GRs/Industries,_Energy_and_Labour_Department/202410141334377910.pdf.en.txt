The functions of the said Committee: -

concerned departments.
b) To facilitate necessary coordination between departments for implementation of the

a time-bound manner.
d) To facilitate convergence of efforts between departments at the field level to maximize

existing beneficiaries of other government schemes.

scheme implementation and to suggest measures to improve scheme implementation in
the district.

scheme.
