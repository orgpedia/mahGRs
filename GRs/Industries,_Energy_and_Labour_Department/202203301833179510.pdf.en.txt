Preamble: In order to support the construction of projects under the Central Government's Micro and Small Enterprises Cluster Development Scheme (MSE-CDP) and to enhance the cost-effectiveness of the projects, a scheme titled" Scheme of Grant-in-Aid to Industry Cluster Projects for Creation of Common State Facility" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 09/06/2010. Further, as per the Government decision dated 22/10/2010, the State Government's participation under the scheme has been fixed at 10% of the project cost (upper ceiling subsidy limit Rs. 1.50 crore).
Under the scheme, the Central Government vide its order dated 06/11/2017 has accorded final approval for setting up of Common Facility Centre at the Industrial Cluster Project, Printing Cluster, Aurangabad, with a State Government contribution grant of Rs. 1.50 crore under the project.
As per the guidelines of the Micro, Small Enterprises-College Development Plan (MSE-CDP), the sanctioned grant of the Central Government is disbursed in the ratio of 40:30:30 to the projects sanctioned under the scheme. Also, for the disbursement of the grant approved by the Central Government, the participation of the special purpose vehicle organization should be 100% of the subsidy and the participation of the state government is proposed by the Central Government.

However, as per the revised guidelines of MSE-CDP scheme, if the participation of the special purpose vehicle entity is 100% as per the project approval and the state government's participation grants are disbursed in high-share, then 50% of the central government's approved grant to the project concerned is disbursed in a single phase.
The group project Printing Cluster, Aurangabad has completed 100% of their participation for the project and if the State Government's participation grant to the project is disbursed in a single phase (one-go), the total sanctioned grant of the Central Government as per the revised guidelines of the scheme will be Rs. 600.00 lakh out of the total sanctioned grant of Rs. 1200.00 lakh. Therefore, for the speedy completion of the project in the state, the government was considering to disburse an amount of Rs 45 lakh to the industrial cluster project Printing Cluster, Aurangabad, as part of the second phase of the state government's participation grant.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Central Government has accorded final approval for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Printing Cluster, Aurangabad under Micro and Small Enterprises-Collective Development Plan (MSE-CDP) under the scheme vide order dated 06/11/2017 at a total approved project cost of Rs. 1633.80 lakh with Central Government grant of Rs. 1200.00 lakh and State Government share grant of Rs. 150.00 lakh. Accordingly, the Government have approved the release of State Participation Grant amounting to Rs. 150.00 lakh to the Printing Cluster, Aurangabad as per the project approval, out of which the second phase grant of Rs. 45.00 lakh (Rupees Forty Five Lakh only) will be disbursed as per the provisions of the said scheme.
(2) The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall arrange to transfer the amount of Rs. 45.00 lakhs (Rupees Forty Five Lakhs only) from the Government Treasury to the Institute of Special Purpose Vehicle, Printing Cluster, Aurangabad. The powers to draw and disburse the above amounts are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall take necessary action to monitor the progress of this special purpose vehicle and the proper utilization and utilization of the Government share capital.
