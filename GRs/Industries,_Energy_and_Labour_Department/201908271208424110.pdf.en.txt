Introduction: Maharashtra Logistic Parks Policy-2018 was announced on 14/2/2018. The key objectives of the policy are to make Maharashtra a part of the global chain, upgrade traditional warehouses into fully integrated and value-added logistics services, improve efficiency and reduce logistics costs.
