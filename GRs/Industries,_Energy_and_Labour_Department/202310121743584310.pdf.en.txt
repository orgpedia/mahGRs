The functions of the Committee are: 1) To ensure that the candidates recommended for appointment to the posts of Deputy Director, Group-A (Gazetted) and Assistant Director, Group-B (Gazetted), as per the rules of service, possess the technical qualification, educational qualification and experience as per the rules of service.
2) To check whether the places where they worked are registered under the Factories Act or not, according to the experience certificate submitted by the recommended candidates.
3) To check whether the recommended candidates have actually worked in the said factories or not.
4) As per the experience certificate submitted by the recommended candidates, it should be checked whether the supervisory and managerial related work is carried out in the machinery repair and maintenance, production and safety and health departments in these factories.
5) All the above matters should be examined and a report should be submitted to the government immediately.
