Government circular:
As per the circular issued by the Finance Department, every administrative department has been asked to prepare an updated list of administrative department heads, department heads, regional office heads and office heads declared by them in the prescribed format and send a copy of the same to the Accountant General (Accounts and Licentiate)-1, Mumbai/ Accountant General (Accounts and Licentiate)-2, Nagpur/ Accountant General (Audit)-1, Mumbai/ Accountant General (Audit)-2, Nagpur/ Director, Accounts and Treasuries, Mumbai/ Subordinate and Accounts Officer, Mumbai/ Treasury Officer and all concerned.
As per the provisions of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I, Roll No.1, an updated list of" Heads of Administrative Department" in the Department of Industries, Power and Labour (itself) has been prepared in the prescribed Form-1.
The circular has been made available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharashtra.gov.in and its code is 202307241146412410. This government circular is being issued by digitally signing.
