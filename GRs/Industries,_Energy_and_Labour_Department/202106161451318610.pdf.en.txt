Preamble: As per Section 33 (i) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for the persons with disabilities to be appointed to the posts in the establishment of Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board. The Central Government No. 2 The list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities has been published as per the notification here. As per the notification of the Central Government (on the lines of Central Government), the instructions regarding ensuring posts for Divyangjan in the State Government establishments are issued under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance vide No. 3 decisions have been made by the government.
Accordingly, the matter of ensuring the posts for the disabled on the establishment of the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. 04/01/2021 Notification and Governance Decision, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. In view of the instructions issued under 02/02/2021, the posts of Divyangjan in the establishment of Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board are being ensured as mentioned in the accompanying annexure.
