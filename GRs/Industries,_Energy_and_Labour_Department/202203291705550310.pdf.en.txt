Introduction: As per the policy of the government, the electricity distribution company is given a discount on the electricity tariff and the government reimburses it to the distribution company.
Distribution Companies are required to provide subsidy of Rs. 28015572/- (Rs. 5300.00 crore out of Rs. 5300.00 crore has been disbursed as adjustment. Now, for this purpose, in the March 2022 session, Rs. Supplementary demand of Rs. 490.15 crore has been sanctioned. Supplementary Statement of Expenditure was presented to the Legislative Council in the Budget Session of March, 2022.
The Supplementary Appropriation Bill has been approved by the Cabinet for inclusion in the Appropriation for the financial year ending March 31, 2022. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, dated 15th March, 2022, as the Maharashtra (Second Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2022 (Maharashtra Act No. 23 of 2022). Accordingly, the Government sanctioned Rs.
490.00 crore to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company.
Government Decision: The amount of financial assistance payable to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the discount given on electricity rates to agricultural pump customers is Rs. This Government resolution approves release of Rs. 490.00 crore (Rupees Four Hundred and Ninety Crores only) in cash.
The expenditure for the said purpose should be spent under the head" Demand No.K-6,2801 Electricity, 05 Transmission and Distribution, 104, Power Development System National Load, (01) Discount in Electricity Rates, (01) Discount in Electricity Rates to Agricultural Pump Customers (General) (28015572), (Programme) 33 Financial Assistance" and it should be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2021-22.
