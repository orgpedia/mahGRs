The Proposal:
To take a decision on the proposals received for the release of funds under the Maharashtra Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development Scheme. The 9th meeting of the Steering Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary was held on 03/02/2023. In the meeting, the committee has approved funds for the infrastructure works of a total of 10 cooperative industrial estates. Among them, Sinnar Taluka Cooperative Industrial Estate, Marri. The Committee has sanctioned Rs. 11,93,86, 100/-for the infrastructure works of the Institute at Sinnar, Nashik. The company has contributed 25 per cent of the amount. 2,98,46, 525/-) has been deposited with the executing agency (Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation). Therefore, a proposal for grant of administrative approval for the activities of the said Institute has been submitted by the Directorate General of Industries to the Government vide their letter dated 31/10/2023. The proposal has been examined and the matter of grant of administrative sanction for the works of the said Institute amounting to Rs. 11,93,86, 100/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the scheme of industrial infrastructure creation and development, the steering committee has approved the distribution of funds. Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation has submitted a total budget of Rs. 11,93,86, 100/- for the infrastructure works of Sinner, G. Nashik. The details is as follows.
