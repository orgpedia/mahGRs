Introduction: As per the policy of the government, the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited gives a discount on the electricity tariff to the consumers of the state and the government reimburses it to the MSEDCL. Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company has received a demand for financial assistance to the millers in the financial year 2021-22 under the head of Demand No. 6 Main Account Heading 28015581. A budgetary provision of Rs. 600.00 crore has been made. From this provision, the above reference No. Under the Government's decision, MSEDCL will receive Rs. 235.00 crore has been provided. Now, as approved by the Finance Department, the MSEDCL will receive Rs. 365.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The amount of financial assistance payable to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the discount given on electricity tariff to the mill customers is Rs. 365.00 crore (Rupees Three Hundred and Fifty Five Crore only) is being approved by this Government.
