Subject: Grant of prior approval of the Government under section 68 of the Electricity Act, 2003 for setting up of transmission new transmission lines belonging to a Mahatransmission Company.
Reference Director (Operations) General Transport Company, vide letter No. Maravipakam/ Sanka/ Pa. Q/ 9024, D. 18.08.2018
the order
I am directed to inform you, in pursuance of the letter of reference of the Mahatransport Company relating to the above subject, that the Government approval is being accorded under the conditions mentioned above under Section 68 of the Electricity Act, 2003 for the installation and maintenance of transmission lines under the scheme approved by the Mahatransport Company and mentioned in the prospectus in the Annexure (Annexure A, B, C).
The Company shall construct the said power lines at its own cost in the prescribed standard manner and under the guidance and supervision of experts.
(2) The company shall operate and maintain such power lines.
(3) The Company shall be bound to use the said power lines only for the purpose for which they are intended to be used.
Prior permission of the competent authority (Chief Electrical Inspector/ Electrical Inspector, as the case may be) shall be required before commissioning/ commissioning of the said power lines.
After the installation of the said power lines, it shall be the responsibility of the concerned company to resolve the grievances of the farmers/ concerned land owners, if any, properly in consultation with the competent authority.
6. The Company shall be bound to obtain all necessary licences for the erection and maintenance of the said power lines, fulfill the prescribed norms and other prevailing rules of various authorities/ departments/ Electricity Regulatory Commission etc. of the Government as well as the rules and conditions approved by the Government from time to time.

The period of such licence shall be 25 years from the date of issue of the licence, after which such permission shall automatically lapse.
