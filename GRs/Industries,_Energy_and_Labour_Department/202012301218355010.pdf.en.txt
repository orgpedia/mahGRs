The Proposal:
The Government of India has approved the utilization of the incentive grant of Rs. 961.03 crore received from the Central Government for development of New and Renewable Energy as per the recommendations of the Thirteenth Finance Commission vide Government Resolution dated 15.10.2016. Out of this, Rs. 909.124 crore has been released to the Department so far and Rs. 51.906 crore is yet to be released. So far, out of the sanctioned amount of Rs. 961.03 crore, Rs. 909.124 crore has been disbursed. Due to non-availability of funds, the utilization certificate of Rs. 51.906 crore required to be submitted to the Central Government could not be submitted to the Central Government.
The Supplementary Appropriation Bill for Supplementary Demands introduced in the Winter Session of December, 2020 was passed by the House. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published as the Maharashtra Third Supplementary Appropriation Act, 2020 (Maharashtra Act No. 29 of 2020) in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, dated 17th December, 2020.
In reference to the above No. 2 of the claim No. K-06, Accounts Titled" 2810 New and Renewable Energy" under the head 28100911 As the Budget Estimate for the Winter Session of December, 2020 is Rs. 1000/- has been budgeted by Supplementary Demand.
Demand No. K-06, under" Accounts Head 28100123, Expenditure shared from Maharashtra Power Development Fund (Programme) 50 Other Expenditure" From the following savings" Incentive Grants (Programme) as recommended by the 13th Finance Commission, 31 Subsidiary Grants, under Account Head 28100911" Under the above Government Decision dated 29.12.2020, a provision of Rs. 51,90,59, 000/- has been made.

Government's decision:
As per the recommendations of the Thirteenth Finance Commission, the Government have approved the release of the balance amount of Rs. 51.9059 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Fifty-one Crore Ninety-nine Lakhs Sixty-nine Thousand only) to the Director General, Maharashtra Power Development Agency, Pune, out of the incentive grant of Rs. 961.03 crore received from the Central Government for the development of New and Renewable Energy.
Walunj, Joint Secretary (Power), Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Mumbai as Controlling Officer and Shri N. R. Dhane, Under Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Mumbai as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
51.9059 crore (Rupees One Crore Ninety-Nine Lakhs Sixty-Nine Thousand only) should be paid in the name of Director General, Maharashtra Energy Development Agency, Pune.
It may be ensured that the amount of funds sanctioned under this Government decision will be spent only for the financial year 2020-21 for the implementation of the schemes sanctioned out of the Incentive Grants received from the Central Government for the development of New and Renewable Energy as per the recommendations of the Thirteenth Finance Commission. Administrative and technical approval for the projects should be obtained from the competent authority before the actual expenditure is incurred. The responsibility of submitting monthly and quarterly reports with information on expenditure, targets and actual achievement on various schemes without fail by the 10th of the preceding month is being entrusted to the officer looking after the charge as Assistant Director (Accounts) dealing with accounting matters. The documents relating to the accounting of the said fund should be submitted by the Maharashtra Energy Development Agency to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai, for inspection, as and when required.
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2020-21 under the head" Demand No. K-6, 2810, New and Renewable Energy, 00, 105, Support Programme (01) Renewable Energy Under Power Grid Construction Programme (KEMPUYO), (01) (01) Incentive Grants (Programme), 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) (28100911) as recommended by the 13th Finance Commission."
