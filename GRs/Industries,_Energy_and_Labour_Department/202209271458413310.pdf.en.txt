Introduction: Since 1971, 8 temporary posts have been created in the office of the Collector, Mumbai City, for the collection of labor dues and labor compensation in the Mumbai City District. These eight posts have been extended from time to time by the government. These positions are listed in Table 1. As per Government Decision dated 01/03/2022 dated 30.03.2022, It was allowed to continue till 31/08/2022.
The government was considering extending the tenure of these posts. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the purpose of recovery of dues under the Labour Act, 1952 and other Acts, the following eight temporary posts under the office of the Collector, City of Mumbai and mentioned below, shall be relieved by the Finance Department from the vacancies mentioned in the circular dated 20/12/1997 and for a period of six months or more, subject to the conditions mentioned in the circular in their number DBB 1097/ PR 26/ Budget-19, dated 20/12/1997: 01/09/2022 to D. The Government hereby approves the continuation up to 28/02/2023:

The expenditure on account of salary and allowances for these posts shall be covered under the head Main Accounts Head 2230-Labour and Employment (01) Labour 111 Social Security for Workers (00) (02) Expenditure in respect of Employees Provident Fund and other Acts (2230 0612) Demand No. K-4 and shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2022-23.
The said Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Resolution No.1004/ PR 12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 4th October, 2004 of the Finance Department.
The above posts shall not be used by the District Collector for any work other than the work related to the Labour Department like works to eradicate child labour and other labour law enforcement activities, except for the work of recovery of pending amounts due to the workers under the Labour Department. As per the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the Government decision dated 18 th August, 2022, the process of creation of the said posts will be completed in due course of time.
