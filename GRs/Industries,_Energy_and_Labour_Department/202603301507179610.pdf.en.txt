The Government's Decisions:
The Central Government has accorded final approval under the Micro and Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Scheme (MSE-CDP) to the project Health Food Cluster, Satara for setting up of Common Facility Centre vide order dated 20/06/2023. Accordingly, the total sanctioned project cost of the project is Rs. 21984.36 lakh, under which the Central Government grant is Rs. 1580.16 lakh and the State Government participation grant is Rs. 150 lakh.
Accordingly, an industrial cluster project Health Food Cluster, Satara is being approved under this Government Decision for an expenditure of Rs. 45,00,000 - (Rupees Forty Five Lakh only) under the final phase out of the State Participatory Grant of Rs. 150 lakh as per the project approval.
