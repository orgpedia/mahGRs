PREAMBLE
HYOSUNG TNC Corporation, Seoul, South Korea has invited Hon. Minister (Industries), Government of Maharashtra to visit headquarters in Seoul, South Korea. South Korea is a country known for its strides in the development of new age technology sphere.
2. On this background Government of Maharashtra’s 07 members state level delegation under the leadership of the Hon. Minister (Industries), Government of Maharashtra for official visit 06/08/2023 to 12/08/2023 at Seoul, South Korea. The post-facto sanction for the said foreign tour was under the consideration of the State Government.
GOVERNMENT RESOLUTION:-
Government of Maharashtra is pleased to accord ex-post-facto approval to the visit of the State level delegation led by Shri Uday Samant, Hon. Minister (Industries), Government of Maharashtra to official visit at Seoul, South Korea for the period mentioned in table below.
