Preamble: In order to ensure smooth power supply to the State by empowering the transmission lines in the State, the Director (Projects) (In-Charge), Maharashtra State Power Transmission Company Limited, Mumbai has issued Reference No. For the Jambhul Sub-station, work is proposed to be taken up on 100 KV Ambernath-Mohone channel through LILO mode and the Government has received a request for grant of prior approval under Section 68 of the Electricity Act, 2003 for the scheme. The proposal was then approved by the government.
For Jambhul Sub-station, the work of laying of 100 KV Ambernath Mohone channel through LILO method will be done as per Annexure-A.
For the construction of new transmission lines/ sub-stations mentioned in Annexure-A, the Maharatna Company is being granted approval by the Government under Section 68 of the Indian Electricity Act, 2003 subject to the following terms and conditions: 1. The installation of power lines/ sub-stations in Annexure-A shall be done by the Maharatna Company at its own cost in the prescribed standard manner and under the guidance and supervision of experts.
2. The operation and maintenance of the power lines shall be done by the Mahatransport Company itself.
(3) It shall be incumbent upon the General Transport Company or the consumer concerned to use the power lines only for the purpose for which they are intended by the General Transport Company or the consumer concerned.
Prior permission of the competent authority shall be required before commissioning/ commissioning of the power lines.
Complaints of the concerned land owners/ farmers, if any, during or after installation of power lines, shall be taken up for consideration with the competent authority.
