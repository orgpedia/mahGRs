The Proposal: -
The Maharashtra Factories (Safety Audit) (Amendment) Rules, 2024 have been notified in the Gazette dated 19.06.2024. As per Rule 6 (1) of the said Rules, examination fee, approval fee and renewal fee will be charged by the applicant for getting the approval of the security auditor. The government was considering providing a new title to the safety auditors for collecting examination fees, approval fees and dance floor fees as demanded by the Director, Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health, Mumbai.
The government's decision:
The following new headings are being approved by the Government for submission of examination fee, approval fee and renewal fee of security auditors. The details of the new title are as follows: -
