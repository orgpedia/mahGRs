Preamble: In order to create awareness through advertisements to eradicate the practice of child labour and to eradicate the evil practice of child labour through public participation, a media plan was prepared by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for the publication of advertisements through audio, audio-visual and daily Marathi newspapers in the financial year 2019-20 with a limit of Rs. 86,40,000/- and work orders were issued to select media through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations to publish the advertisement. The government has decided to pay Rs 40,36,760 (Rs 40 lakh thirty six thousand seven hundred and sixty only) for this advertisement. 24.03.2021. However, during the financial year 2019-20, advertisements in newspapers, radio, billboards, ST Corporation (digital signboards) and auto rickshaw advertisements were released by the media at a total cost of Rs. 22,34,431/- (Rupees Twenty Two Lakhs Thirty Four Thousand Four Hundred and Thirty One only) could not be submitted to the Accounts Office as the payments were received after March 2021 due to the situation arising out of the contagious disease of COVID-19. Hence, the amount was not paid. Now that the payments have been received by the Government, Refer No. According to the letters 1 and 2, the Labour Commissioner's Office has submitted a proposal to the government to pay these dues. The government was considering the issue. A decision in this regard is being taken.
