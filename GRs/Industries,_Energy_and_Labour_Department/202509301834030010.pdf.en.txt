Visit of Dr. Vijay Rathod, Joint Chief Executive Officer, MIDC to Tokyo and Osaka, cities of Japan.
GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASHTRA
Government Resolution No.Del-2025/CR-163/ IND-3.
Industries, Energy, Labour & Mining Department,
Mantralaya, Mumbai-400032.
Date : 30 September, 2025
PREAMBLE
The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) functions as the Investment Promotion Nodal Agency for the Government of Maharashtra. In order to attract investment into the State, various investment conferences, International tours have been organized so far by MIDC.
2. Japan is an important partner of India in terms of trade and investment. Since Entrepreneurs from Japan have made large-scale industrial investments into the State, there is scope to increase such investments through meetings with Japan Entrepreneurs. Accordingly, to encourage new entrepreneurs from Japan to invest into the State, a visit of Dr. Vijay Rathod LA.S., Joint Chief Executive Officer, MIDC has been arranged to Osaka and Tokyo in Japan, for the period of 20th to 26th September 2025 (including travel period).
3. The visit of Dr. Vijay Rathod LA.S., Joint Chief Executive Officer, MIDC consists the programme of one-on-one meetings with entrepreneurs/investors, round table sessions, meetings with industrial associations, and visits to industrial areas.
4. Assessing the Significance of the visit for the promotion of investment Government of Maharashtra have approved the visit and decided to send Dr. Vijay Rathod [.A.S., Joint Chief Executive Officer, MIDC for the period of 20th to 26th September, 2025.
RESOLUTION:-
Government of Maharashtra is now pleased to accord ex-post facto approval to the visit of Dr. Vijay Rathod I.A.S., Joint Chief Executive Officer, MIDC for the period of 20th
to 26th September, 2025 (Including travel time) at Tokyo and Osaka, cities of Japan.
2. The Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi vide their letter dated 19.09.2025 have approved the visit of Dr. Vijay Rathod I.A.S., Joint Chief Executive Officer, MIDC to Tokyo and Osaka, cities of Japan.
3. Dr. Rathod will be entitled for travel expenses and per diem allowance as per prescribed norms of Government of India.
4. State Government is further pleased to authorize the absence of Dr. Vijay Rathod L.A.S., Joint Chief Executive Officer, MIDC from 20.09.2025 to 26.09.2025 UIncluding travel time) be treated as on duty until they resume their duties on return to India from their foreign tour.
5. State Government is pleased to approve the ex-post facto sanction processing fees of visa for Japan, as per actual and medical facilities admissible, under the revised AMA Scheme continued in the Ministry of External Affairs O.M.No.Q/GA/653/1/74 dated 5/3/1979 to him. The Government is also pleased to approve the ex-post facto sanction charges for medical insurance for him.
6. The entire expenditure on travel and accommodation etc. for Dr. Rathod will be borned by the Maharashtra Industrial development Corporation.
7. The detailed report for the tour and outcomes of the said tour with suggestions if any should be submitted to the government within one month from the date of returning.
