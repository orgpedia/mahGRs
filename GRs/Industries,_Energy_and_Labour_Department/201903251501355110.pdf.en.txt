Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government regarding Mukhyamantri Solar Krishi Pump Scheme, the Tribal Development Department has made available Rs.76.241 crore to the beneficiaries of Scheduled Tribes in 2018-19 from the Tribal Sub Plan to the Principal Secretary (Power) Industries, Power and Labour Department as the Head of the Administrative Department on the budgetary fund distribution system. However, as per the Government decision dated 15.11.2018, 2336 tribal beneficiaries are available. Accordingly, the amount of Rs. 40.36 crore. provided directly to tribal beneficiaries.
The fund is being made available on the condition that the amount remaining from the above fund will be returned. The fund of Rs. 40.36 Crores (Rs. Forty Crores Thirty Six Lakh only) to the Chairman& Managing Director, Maharashtra State Power Distribution Company Limited, Prakashgarh, Bandra (East), Mumbai through the Controlling Authorities through the Budget Fund Distribution System.
Walunj, Joint Secretary (Power), Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Mumbai as Controlling Officer and Shri N. R. Dhane, Under Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Mumbai as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The said amount shall be paid in the name of Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Prakashgarh, Bandra (East), Mumbai. The monthly and quarterly reports with information about the cost, objectives and actual achievement of various schemes under the solar power agri-plantation program should be submitted by MSEDCL to the government as well as the Commissioner, Tribal Development, Maharashtra State Nashik, Tribal Development Department by the 10th of the next month. The MSEDCL should ensure that the amount sanctioned under this Government decision is spent only for the implementation of various schemes under the Solar Power Plant Installation Programme for the financial year 2018-19 for the benefit of Scheduled Tribes only.
The documents relating to the accounting of the said fund should be submitted by Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Prakashgarh, Bandra (East), Mumbai to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai, for inspection, as and when required.
