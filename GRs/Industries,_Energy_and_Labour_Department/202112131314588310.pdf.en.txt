A committee will be formed to extend the deadline of the projects to be set up under the Non-Conventional Energy Generation Policy-2020 if they are not completed within the stipulated time.

The Proposal:
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| New and Renewable Energy (Non-Conventional) Policy of the State under Non-Conventional Energy Generation Policy-2020 Part-1           |
| Integrated Policy for Transmission Linked Projects of Power Generation from Energy Sources- 2020 and Part-II                          |
| Transmission of electricity from new and renewable energy sources                                                                     |
| The Consolidated Policy for Projects-2020 has been announced by the relevant Government Decision.                                     |
| To give time limit to the projects to be set up under the policy if they are not completed within the stipulated time.                |
| The government had constituted a committee for the purpose.                                                                           |
| Government's decision:                                                                                                                |
| Non-conventional energy projects to be set up in 2020                                                                                 |
| In case of non-completion of such projects, a committee is constituted to extend the time for completion of such projects.            |
| The members of the Committee shall be:                                                                                                |
| Principal Secretary (Energy)                                                                                                          |
| Chairman and Managing Director, MSEDCL                                                                                                |
| Chairman and Managing Director, Member of the Board                                                                                   |
| Chairman and Managing Director, Member of the Board                                                                                   |
| Consultant, Maharashtra State Electricity Board Host Company Limited Member                                                           |
| Director General, Member Secretary                                                                                                    |
| The committee shall include as members the persons concerned in accordance with the policy as may be required.                        |
| You can participate.                                                                                                                  |
|                                                                                                                                       |
| Committee's mandate:                                                                                                                  |
| Projects to be set up under Non-Conventional Energy Policy-2020 to be completed in stipulated time frame                              |
| It is mandatory to complete the projects which are not completed within the stipulated time.                                          |
| If applied before completion, such projects can be extended up to a maximum period of 2 years depending on the quality of the project |
| However, the deadline will not be extended beyond March 31, 2026.                                                                     |
