The Government's Decisions:
In order to ensure that the grape-producing farmers of the state are not harmed, the government has formulated the Grape Processing Industry Policy-2001 to encourage farmers to cultivate grapes, make dry fruits and produce more economically viable alternative products from grapes, and to promote the grape-based wine industry. The rule of 19/10/2001 was declared by decree. As part of the implementation of this policy, the government has proposed to provide a wine incentive subsidy to the wine industry equivalent to 16 per cent of the value-added tax payable on the sale of finally sold wines produced in the state. The rule of 31/08/2009 has been implemented by decree.
