To review the suspension of Group-A and Group-B officers under the Department of Energy, Reference No. 1 The suspension review committee has been formed under the government decision here.
Suspension Review to review the suspension of the officers shown in the above statement
Committee meeting Completed on 14.07.2022. As per the decision taken by the committee in this meeting, it has been recommended to end the suspension of the above officers and reinstate them in non-executive posts in the government service. In accordance with the recommendation of the Suspension Review Committee, the matter of ending the suspension of the officers in the above statement and reinstating them in government service was under the consideration of the government. Orders have been issued in this regard:
Government order:
As recommended by the Suspension Review Committee and in exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 4 Potnium-5 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, Shri. Chavan, Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Suspended), Mr. Vaibhav Ra. Vaidkar, Junior Engineer (Suspended) and Shri. The suspension of Gite, Branch Engineer (Suspended) is being terminated and reinstated in Government service subject to final decision in the ongoing departmental enquiry and court cases against them.
General Administration Department. As per the provisions of Circular dated 20/04/2013, the said officers are being re-designated as indicated in the following statement.

GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: MOVINI-2022/ PR No.125/ ENERGY-1
The officers who are being reinstated in the government service under this order should join the place of posting within 7 days, otherwise, if they do not join the post on time, the period will be considered as an inactive day. The report should be submitted to the government through the Chief Electrical Inspector, Mumbai. The suspension shall be deemed to have ended on the date on which the said officer joins the office of the post holder.
Orders regarding regularization of the period of suspension of the said officers will be issued as soon as the final decision of the court case and departmental inquiry against them is made.
