Introduction: The increasing number of educated youth in urban and rural areas of the state and new opportunities for self-employment and employment in various fields in the state in the field of industry, business. Considering these new opportunities of self-employment and employment, the comprehensive scheme' Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme' (CMEGP), which promotes entrepreneurship and promotes creativity in a time-bound manner, has been implemented under the Government Decision dated 01/08/2019. Accordingly, State Level and District Level Controlling Committees have been constituted to provide various opportunities of self-employment to the urban and rural youth of the state and for effective implementation of the scheme.
Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program, the state government will provide financial assistance in the form of grants from the state government's budgetary provision to establish self-employment projects to make the youth of the state self-sufficient/ self-reliant. Accordingly, a new Account Head for General Category and Tribal Category has been opened under the Scheme vide Government Resolution dated 23/01/2020.
Also, for the Scheduled Castes Sub Plan, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance provides provision in accordance with the objectives.

The Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme is being implemented through the District Industries Center under the Directorate of Industries under the Industries Department and the District Khadi and Village Industries Office under the Khadi and Village Industries Board. Accordingly, the margin money amount required for pending claims of general category under the scheme is Rs. 17.34 crore which has been requested by the Additional Industries Officer, Directorate of Industries vide their letter dated 23/03/2022 for disbursement under the 31st Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) Objective under the scheme. In pursuance of the above request made by the Additional Industries Officer, Directorate of Industries, it was under the consideration of the Government to disburse the margin money amount of Rs. 17,34,15, 000/- required for the pending claims of general category under the Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme from the approved budgetary provision for the financial year 2021-22.
Government Decision: Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme (CMEGP) of the State Government, it is being approved to disburse the State Government's participation grant of Rs. 17,34,15, 000/- (Rupees Seventeen Crore Thirty Four Lakh Fifteen Thousand Only), as per the provisions of the present scheme, for the margin money included in the approved cases in the general category.
The powers to draw and disburse this amount are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
Government of Finance Decision No. As per the information received from the Directorate of Industries as per the provisions of the Annexure dated 24/06/2021 and subsequent to this, it is certified that the Government Decision dated 01/08/2019 regarding the implementation of the Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme in the State has been issued in which the details of the application process and the action to be taken under the project approval, the mechanism of implementation of the scheme, the distribution of grants and the various sanctioning committees have been given. The funds received under the scheme earlier are being deposited in the authorized bank and the funds are being credited to the beneficiaries' account through the bank. There are no objections from the Auditors/ Accountants General in respect of funds disbursed earlier. The amount will be deposited in your bank account or savings account.
