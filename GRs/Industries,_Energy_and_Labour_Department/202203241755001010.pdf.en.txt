Introduction: Exporters of the state should be recognized and appreciated for their export performance and in future, exporters should export more by doing more substantial work than in the past.
In order to contribute to the export operations, the scheme of conferring of insignia and quality certificates to the exporters of the State for their outstanding performance in exports is being implemented by the Development Commissioner (Industries) since 1971-72. As per the decision of the Government dated 20/01/2003 to select the best exporters for the awards to the small and medium enterprises in the State, the Government has decided to award Rs. A selection committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Industries). Accordingly, the State Export Excellence Awards for the year 2017-18 have been announced. As per the directions of Principal Secretary (Industries), a meeting was held on 30/12/2020 under the chairmanship of Development Commissioner (Industries) through online mode. In the meeting, 34 exporters of different categories have been selected for the financial year 2017-18. Accordingly, a proposal for administrative sanction and disbursement of funds for Rs. 22.5 lakhs for organizing one day Export Excellence Award Distribution Programme and Export Promotion Workshop at World Trade Centre, Mumbai has been submitted by Additional Director Industries, Directorate of Industries vide their letter dated 17/02/2022. The proposal has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 21/03/2022. Accordingly, the matter of disbursing funds for the above mentioned programmes and workshops was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In pursuance of the selection of 34 Exporters in different categories for the award of Best Exporter for the financial year 2017-18, it is hereby approved to disburse the required amount of Rs. 22,50,000/- (Rupees Twenty Two Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) to the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai for the One Day Best Export Award Distribution Programme and Export Promotion Workshop held at World Trade Centre, Mumbai.
