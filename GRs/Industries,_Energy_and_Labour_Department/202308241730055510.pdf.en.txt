Government's decision
The Government is approving the distribution of Rs. 58,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Eight Crores Fifty Lakh only) to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSDDCL) in cash to provide discount on electricity tariff to individual beneficiary agriculture pump holders under the Scheduled Castes Special Component Programme of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
The Energy-5 office at the divisional level as well as the distribution company should ensure that the amount of financial assistance sanctioned under this government decision will be spent only on the beneficiaries under the Scheduled Caste category as a concession in electricity rates. Also, it will be the responsibility of the MSEDCL to ensure that the fund is utilised for the purpose for which it has been sanctioned.
