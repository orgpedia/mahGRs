In accordance with the provisions of the Central Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has decided to ensure reservation of posts for persons with disabilities from Group A to Group D in the establishment of Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board.

Department of Industry, Energy and Labour,
Government Resolution No:- Divyang- 2020/ PR No.120/ Industries-6.

4) Government Decision, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department No. Divyang 2019/ Q. No.251/ D.C.2, D.F.2, 2021.
The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Government of India has notified the list of posts for persons with disabilities. Further, instructions have been issued regarding the action to be taken by the Administrative Department while ensuring posts for the disabled under the Government Decision Read 4 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Accordingly, the matter of ensuring posts for the disabled in the group' A" to' D" cadre on the establishment of the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board under this department was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: For the posts on the establishment of the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, the posts are being ensured for the disabled as mentioned in the accompanying return A and return B.
The said Government Order is being issued in accordance with the notification dated 04/01/2021 issued by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi and the Government Order dated 02/02/2021 issued by the Department of Social Justice, Government of India.
