The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board was established by the Maharashtra Legislative Council Act, 1960. The Central Government is implementing various schemes of the Khadi and Village Industries Commission as well as the State Government to provide employment opportunities to rural artisans and unemployed.
For the upliftment and progress of the potter community in the State, the Finance Minister in the Budget 2018-19 announced the setting up of Shri Sant Shiromani Gorobakaka Matikala Board at Wardha and for the purpose, Shri.
Pursuant to the announcement of providing Rs.10 crore, the State Government has decided to set up Shri Sant Shiromani Gorobakaka Matikala Board at Wardha as per the Government decision dated 8th March, 2019. To implement the decision of the government
Provisional composition of Government/ Non-Government members has been prescribed. The matter of appointing non-official members of this structure was under the consideration of the government.
