An agreement dated 04/02/2022 has also been signed with the Directorate General of Industries for an undertaking that the Institute will bear the cost of future maintenance and repair of the Express Feeder. Considering all these aspects, administrative approval is being accorded for the works of Express Feeder at a total cost of Rs. 31,10,140/- (Rupees Thirty One Lakh Ten Thousand One Hundred Forty Only).
The increase in the project cost will not be acceptable to the organization. Additional expenses should be borne by the institution.
The said administrative approval is in accordance with the approval given by the Steering Committee in its 8th meeting held on 24/02/2022 as well as the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor Government Decision No. Aupasu-2012/ No.234/ Industries-2, Dy. dated 25/02/2014.
