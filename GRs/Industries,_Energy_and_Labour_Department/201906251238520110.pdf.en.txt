The Proposal:
In response to the issue raised in the winter session of the Maharashtra Legislature in 2018, a high-level inquiry will be ordered in connection with the Mathadi Hospital (Trust). During the discussion in the Legislative Council, The Minister (Labour) has assured to constitute a committee under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Labour) to inquire into the functioning of Mathadi Hospital (Trust). Under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Labour), further action will be taken as per the assurance given in the Legislative Council. The Committee has been constituted by the Government Resolution dated 03/01/2019. At the request of the Commissioner of Charity. The composition of the members has been changed as per the Government Resolution dated 26/02/2019.
In view of the broad nature of the subject matter and the scope of the work set before the Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 03/01/2019, the Committee has been appointed. The matter of extension of time for completion of works up to 31/07/2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In view of the broad nature of the subject matter and the scope of work set before the Committee constituted under the Government Resolution dated 03/01/2019 under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Labour), the Committee has been constituted. The deadline for completion of the work is being extended till 31/07/2019.
