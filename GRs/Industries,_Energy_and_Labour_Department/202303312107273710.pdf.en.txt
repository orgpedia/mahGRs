Preamble: The Maharashtra State Industrial Complex Development Programme (MSICDP) has been announced vide Government Resolution dated 25/02/2014 for the development of clusters of small clusters in industrially underdeveloped areas of the State thereby promoting holistic development of micro and small units. Under the scheme, the state government provides grant-in-aid for the implementation of capacity building programmes for the overall development and growth of micro and small enterprises as well as for the construction of common facilities.
Under this scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared by properly diagnosing the shortcomings in the production process of the industrial group, including the latest technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial groups, updated training programs and study tours, etc. After the diagnostic study report is received on the recommendation of the district level committee, the State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee approves the diagnostic study report proposals and determines the subsidy for the capacity building program under it.
The grant for the capacity building programme is disbursed in two phases. The first tranche of 60% subsidy to be disbursed by the Government is disbursed after ensuring the required 10% participation of the stakeholders. The remaining 40% subsidy is disbursed after submission of first stage utilization certificate. Accordingly, vide letter dated 31/03/2023 from the Directorate of Industries, the Government was contemplating to disburse a total grant of Rs. 7,19,000/- in the second phase to the State Government for the capacity building programme of 03 industrial cluster projects.
Government Decision: The Directorate of Industries has issued a proposal for the release of the second phase grant of Rs. 7,19,000/- from the State Government for the capacity building program for the 03 industrial cluster projects approved under the scheme" Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Program (MSI-CDP)" of the State Government.
