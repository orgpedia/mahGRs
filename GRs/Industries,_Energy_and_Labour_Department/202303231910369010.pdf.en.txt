The Proposal:
Regarding obtaining administrative approval for making logo for Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health, the Directorate has issued a letter to the Deputy Commissioner at No. 1. The request was made to the Government vide letter dated 30.11.2022. The College of Applied Arts, Mumbai, after making more than one model of the appropriate logo for the Directorate, the said model of the logo should be submitted to the Government for final approval. The administrative approval has been accorded vide letter dated 05.01.2023. In pursuance of the said stipulation, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to approve the logo recommended by the Directorate out of the samples submitted by the Dean, Sir J. G. College of Applied Arts, Mumbai.
Government's decision:
For the Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health, the Government is approving the following emblem in English and Marathi along with the name of the office in the colour scheme of saffron, green, blue and white.
