Intimation by Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai, dated 1st October, 2018:
The year 2019 marks the 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi.
In order to carry forward the teachings of the Father of the Nation Mahatma Gandhi and to keep his message alive for the future generations across the world, a committee has been constituted at the Central Government level to organize various programs from the year 2018. The Chief Minister of Maharashtra is a member of this committee. As per the decision of the Rural Development Department dated 16.04.2018, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to prepare and implement the schemes to be implemented by the Government of Maharashtra in this year in accordance with the work of Mahatma Gandhi and his ideology. A high-level committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary.
In line with the above, the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board (MSKVB), Mumbai, has submitted a plan for various programmes to be organized in the three financial years 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 to the Government for approval. Accordingly, the Planning Department was requested to provide additional outlay. The Department of Planning's expenditure

Government's decision:
This Government decision approves the draft submitted by the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board under its reference dated 1st October, 2018. The details of the plan are as per the attached prospectus.
