The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2, the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai has been approved to disburse a total grant of Rs. 1103.46 lakhs (Rs. Eleven Crore Three Lakh Forty Seven Thousand only) for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 1056.98 lakhs and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 46.48 lakhs for the two months April and May.
The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai vide letter No. 3 has sanctioned a sum of Rs. 2113.96 lakh has been requested. It was under consideration of the Government to provide subsidy to the Board to meet the expenditure on salary for two months i.e. June and July, 2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Under this Government decision, a grant of Rs. 1056.98 lakh (Rs. 10 crore fifty six lakh sixty eight thousand nine thousand only) is being sanctioned to Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai for meeting the expenditure on salary for two months i.e. June and July, 2019, for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries).
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2019-20 under the targets Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (28510585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaries) and should be spent under the same head.
The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government and the Drawing and Disbursing Officers within the prescribed time. Also, when the accountant general, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai, is asked about the expenditure of the accounts, the board should submit the necessary documents related to the account to the office of the accountant general.
