The Proposal: -
The scheme "Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 25/02/2014 to promote cluster development of small clusters in industrially underdeveloped areas of the state thereby promoting holistic development of micro and small units.
Under the scheme, state government grants are provided for implementation of capacity building programmes for holistic development of micro and small enterprises as well as for setting up of common facilities. Under this scheme, latest technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial clusters, up-to-date training programmes, and study tours are provided to the industrial clusters by properly diagnosing the defects and deficiencies in their production processes.
A Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared which includes, etc.
