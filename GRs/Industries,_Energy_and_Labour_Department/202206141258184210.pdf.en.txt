GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the decision of the Government vide No.1 above, the final service seniority list dated 01.01.2020 for the post of Additional Labour Commissioner, Group-A was published.
However, since there has been no promotion from the cadre of Deputy Commissioner of Labour to Additional Commissioner of Labour, Group-A during the period from 01.01.2020 to 31.12.2020, the final service merit list dated 01.01.2020 is being published as final service merit list dated 01.01.2021 without publishing the provisional service merit list dated 01.01.2021 in the cadre of Additional Commissioner of Labour. The base date for publishing the service seniority list is 1st January every year.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the Government has decided to exclude the names of officers of the cadre of Additional Labour Commissioner who are not working due to retirement, death, promotion, resignation, etc. from the final merit list of the cadre of Additional Labour Commissioner for the year 01.01.2017.
