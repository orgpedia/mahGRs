Introduction: Electricity is a very important service. If there is no communication and coordination between the consumer and the power distribution company, there should not be dissatisfaction among the consumers in case of problems and the administration of the MSEDCL should be transparent and transparent.
Power Distribution Control Committees have been appointed at the district and taluk levels as per the decision of the respective Governments to ensure effective functioning and to provide better and quality services to the consumers. The composition of the Committees is set out in Annexure A and B and the details of the functions of the Committees are set out in Annexure A and D.
As per section 166 (5) of the Electricity Act, 2003, the State Government is empowered to constitute a District Level Committee (DLC) to: (a) co-ordinate and review the expansion of electrification in each district; (b) review the quality of power supply and consumer satisfaction; and (c) enhance and promote the efficiency of electricity.
Accordingly, the government was considering the re-constitution of district and taluk level electricity distribution control committees. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The government has decided to reconstitute the district and taluk-level committees.
The details of the members to be included in the District and Taluk level Electricity Distribution Committees are given in Annexure' A' and' B'.
(3) The details of the functions of the Committee are set out in Annexure' C' and' D'.
In this function, the Committee is expected to perform its functions and assist in the implementation of the Government's policy/ decision on power distribution.
The meetings of the committee should be held every three months and the decisions taken in the meeting should be informed to the government through the Chief Engineer, MSEDCL with feedback.

5. The District Level Committee shall be constituted by the District Collector concerned. The sub-divisional officer (SDO) will form the taluk-level committee. Before issuing the order regarding the formation of the committee, the appointment of non-government members at the district level. The consent of the Guardian Minister should be taken by the concerned Collector in collaboration with the concerned Chief Engineer, MSEDCL and by the Sub-Divisional Officer in collaboration with the Executive Engineer, MSEDCL appointed at the Taluka level. This process should be completed within a month from the date of this order.
Travel expenses and allowances payable to non-government and government members will not be admissible as per the existing rules.
7. Identity cards to the non-official members of the District Level Committee should be issued by the Collector and to the non-official members of the Taluk Level Committee by the Sub-Divisional Officer. It should be clearly mentioned in the relevant order that the appointments made on the committee of non-official members are until further orders of the government. It should also be mentioned in the order that the chairman of the committee reserves the right to appoint or relieve the post. The guardian minister of the district will have the power to relieve the presidents at the taluka level.
All the concerned Chief Engineers, MSEs/ Sub-Divisional Engineers, MSEs should cooperate with the concerned District Collectors and Sub-Divisional Officers in the process of formation of the committee.
The Chairman and Managing Director, MSEDCL, Mumbai shall give appropriate instructions to the concerned Chief Engineer/ Executive Engineer for the formation of the committees at the district and taluka level and take necessary action so that the process of forming the committees through the concerned Collector is carried out within the prescribed time frame. Also, the Chairman and Managing Director, MSEDCL should give appropriate instructions to the concerned Sub-Divisional Engineer regarding the formation of the committee at the taluka level and the concerned Sub-Divisional Engineer Officer should take action so that the process of forming the committee is carried out within the time limit.
10. District/ Taluk level Power Distribution Control Committees constituted earlier as per this Government decision should be reconstituted as per requirement.
