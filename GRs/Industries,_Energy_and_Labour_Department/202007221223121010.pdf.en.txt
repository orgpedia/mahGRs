Memorandum: The government has given a subsidy to the cashew processing industry in the state. The decision has been taken as per the Government Decision dated 15/07/2010. Also the. As per the Government decision dated 13/02/2013, the scheme has been approved to be continued by fixing the revised methodology of allocation of incentive subsidy to cashew processing industry. As per the scheme, after payment of 5% value added tax on sale of cashew nuts finally sold in the State out of the cashew nuts produced and processed in the State, the amount of this tax shall be given to the concerned units as incentive subsidy.
