Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation has submitted a total budget of Rs. 3,68,79, 805/- for the works of the organization. The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) has examined the project map and budget of the said organization and has given technical approval to the same. Considering all these aspects, administrative approval is being accorded for the maps and budget for the works of the Institute at a total cost of Rs. 3,68,79, 805/- (Rupees Three Crore Eighty Six Lakh Eighty Nine Thousand Eight Hundred Five only).
The increase in the project cost will not be acceptable to the organization. Additional expenses should be borne by the institution.
The said administrative approval is in accordance with the approval given by the Steering Committee in its 8th meeting held on 24/02/2022 as well as the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor Government Decision No. Aupasu-2012/ No.234/ Industries-2, Dy. dated 25/02/2014. The powers conferred under section 6 are being exercised.
