The state government is taking various measures to contain the spread of Covid-19 in the state. While addressing the people of the state on April 13, 2021, he announced financial assistance to domestic workers. A notification in this regard has been issued by the Maharashtra government. Maharashtra Domestic Workers Welfare Board constituted under 12/8/2011 was under consideration of the Government to provide one-time financial assistance to eligible domestic workers registered and renewed as per the provisions prescribed in the Domestic Workers Welfare Board Rules, 2010.
Government's decision:
The Office of the Development Commissioner (Unorganized Workers), Mumbai vide Reference No. As demanded under the proposal of the Maharashtra Domestic Workers Welfare Board, a total of 1,05,500 domestic workers with active and live registration in the first phase for registered and renewed domestic workers will be given Rs. 1,500/- (1,05,500 x 1,500) aggregating to Rs. The Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 15,82,50, 000/- (Rupees Fifteen Crore Eighty Two Lakh Fifty Thousand only).
Accordingly, 1,05,500 domestic workers will be directly credited to their bank accounts through DBT. The process of allocating Rs 1,500/- in lump sum should be done by the Development Commissioner (Unorganized Workers).
With a view to expedite the disbursement of the above financial assistance, the office of the Development Commissioner (Unorganized Workers), Mumbai shall guide the zonal offices for the process of allocation of financial funds.
