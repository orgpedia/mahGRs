The Proposal:
The efficiency and financial viability of electricity distribution companies in the country by the Central Government
The UDAY scheme has been approved with an aim to increase. A tripartite agreement (MOU) has been signed between the Ministry of Power, the State Government and MSEDCL for participation in the scheme.
Under the Scheme, the Government has been given approval to reimburse 75% of the total amount of Rs.6,613 crore as on 30th September, 2015 to MSEDCL towards principal amount and interest on Non-SLR/ SDL Bonds amounting to Rs. 4959.75 crore, through Back to Back Arrangement from the MSEDCL.
Accordingly, the State Government has constituted the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL). 4959.75 crore has been provided to MSEDCL for a period of five years and the repayment of the loan amount is to be made in five financial years from 2016-17 to 2020-21.
Accordingly, in the financial year 2016-17, the first installment of Rs. 991.75 crore has been disbursed to MSEDCL as per the Government decision dated 31.03.2017 and the second installment of the loan has been disbursed in the financial year 2017-18 as per the Government decision dated 13.02.2018 and the third installment of the loan has been disbursed in the financial year 2018-19 as per the Government decision dated 13.302.2019. The government was now considering adjusting the fourth installment of the subsidy budgeted for 2019-20 for MSEDCL to repay the loan without disbursing Rs 992 crore in cash.

Government's decision
Approval is being given to disburse an amount of Rs. 992 crore (Rupees Nine Hundred and Ninety Two Crores only) to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) by way of adjustment in the form of subsidy for repayment of loan against Non-SLR/ SDL bonds raised under UDAY Scheme of Government of India.
The Government should ensure that the grant sanctioned under this decision is utilised for the purpose for which it has been sanctioned.
For payment of 4th instalment of loan repayment in 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 992 crore has been budgeted under the head Demand No. K-6 Main Accounts Head 2801- Power, 80- General, 101 Assistance to Power Boards, (00) (01) MSEDCL Subsidiary Grants for repayment of loan under UDAY Scheme of Government of India, 28015507 (Mandatory) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment).
The expenditure for the year 2019-20 should be included in the provisions made under the instant Demand No. K-6 Main Account Head 2801- Power, 80- General, 101 Assistance to Power Boards, (00) (01) MSEDCL Subsidiary Grants for repayment of loans under UDAY Scheme of Government of India, 28015507 (Mandatory) 31 Subsidiary Grants and the same should be adjusted in the following head.
