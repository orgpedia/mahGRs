Preamble: A Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Power) vide Government Resolution No. 2015/ PR No. 480/ URJA-4, dated 29th January, 2016, for taking measures regarding power supply and transmission system in Mumbai area.
The Mumbai Islanding facility has been available since 1981 to maintain the power supply of the city. In such an emergency, the power supply of Mumbai city is cut off from the grid and the power supply in Mumbai remains uninterrupted. However, on October 12, 2020, power supply to Mumbai city was disrupted for some time due to non-functioning of Mumbai Islanding System. On 12th October, 2020, power supply to Mumbai, Mumbai Suburbs, Thane, Navi Mumbai and Suburban Railway was restored in a phased manner at 11:00 AM.
