The Proposal: -
The Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board has been established in the state in 2007 under Section 18 of the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Pension) Act 1996 and a computer system has been developed for registration, submission of renewal benefit applications through the board. The registered construction workers under the Board are given the benefit of various welfare schemes of financial assistance, health, educational assistance and social security.

(c) The meetings of the Committee: -
The Member Secretary shall be responsible for convening at least one meeting of the 193 Committee every month. The Member Secretary shall take the date of the meeting of the Chairman and forward the notice of the meeting to the Chairman and other members of the Committee within 3 days. At least three delegates will be required to attend the meeting.
The Member Secretary shall be responsible for convening a meeting from the 1st to the 15th of the following month to submit the applications received at the end of each month to the Committee. In case the meeting of the Control Committee is not held for 2 consecutive months, it shall be the responsibility of the Member Secretary concerned to scrutinize and process the applications as per the norms during that period. The cases that fit the criteria for this period must be approved and the rejected cases must be presented to the committee at the next meeting.
