The Proposal:
The Government of India has issued guidelines in June, 2015 based on the concept of Housing for All to ensure that every family in the country has access to a rightful home. Accordingly, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) has been implemented in the State by the Housing Department as per the Government decision dated 09.12.2015. The scope of the scheme has been widened to include construction workers in the building and other construction sectors in the state. Maharashtra Construction Workers Housing Scheme for Urban Areas has been announced under the Government decision dated 01.02.2018. Also, reference No. The terms and conditions of the beneficiary have been fixed as per the Government Decision dated 29th October, 2018.

Maharashtra Construction Workers Housing Scheme (Urban): Construction workers registered with Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board on or before 31st May, 2019 can apply for the benefit of this scheme.
