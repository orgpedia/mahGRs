The Proposal:
1) Read here. As per the Government decision dated 31.08.2020, the investment/ employment norms and incentives provided for large and mega projects under the Scheme for Incentive to Agro-based and Food Processing Industries (SAPI), 2019 have been revised. The following is set out in paragraph (a) (1) of this Government Resolution: The benefit will also be available to the units which have invested in agro-based and food processing sector and have obtained the eligibility certificate after 1st April 2020 due to lockdown imposed in the State since 23rd March, 2020 due to COVID-19. These incentives will be applicable till 31.03.2024 of the Scheme period of the Composite Incentive Scheme 2019. "
(2) The Government are pleased to approve the inclusion of the matters contained in paragraphs (a), (b) and (c) below in the said Government Resolution.
(a) Industries based on agriculture and food processing. The investment period for large projects to provide permissible benefits as prescribed under the Government Decision dated 31.08.2020 is Rs. 4 years from the date of issue of the Government Order dated 31.08.2020 i.e. Exemption from the provision prescribed for investment period in Para. 2.9 of the Collective Incentive Scheme-2019 is being provided for the investment of any component for a period of 4 years from 31.08.2016 onwards, as demanded by the component.
(b) Industries based on agriculture and food processing. The investment period for mega projects is the period for giving permissible benefits as prescribed under the Government decision dated 31.08.2020. 5 years from the date of issue of the Government Order dated 31.08.2020 i.e. Any 5 years from 31.08.2015 onwards.
