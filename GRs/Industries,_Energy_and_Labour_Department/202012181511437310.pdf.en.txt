The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Resolution No: SANKIRN-2020/ PR No.121/ ENERGY-5
Madame Cama Road, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: December 18, 2020.
The Proposal:
At present, the High Pressure Distribution System Scheme for providing electricity connection to agricultural pumps is operational as per the Government decision dated 05.05.2018. Payment under this scheme up to 31st March, 2018
The pending agricultural pumps are being given electricity connections.
The scheme is not available for the applicants of agricultural pumps who are waiting for payment of electricity connection after 1st April, 2018. The existing high pressure distribution system
Considering the progress, limitations in providing electricity connection through other means, a comprehensive scheme is being proposed for potential agricultural pump applicants.
The government was considering to include in the agricultural pump policy the issues related to the planning of supplying electricity to agricultural pumps for 8 hours a day through decentralized solar energy/ installation of capacitors to avoid distribution losses as well as to make full use of the available distribution system/ to collect agricultural consumer dues/ to encourage Gram Panchayats, Village Electricity Managers, MSEDCL employees, Farmers Cooperative Societies to collect these dues and metering etc. In this regard, the government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
(1) The proposed Krishi Pump Policy is being approved to provide electricity connection through conventional mode and solar energy to the applicants of Krishi Pumps pending for payment of electricity connection since 01.04.2018.
(2) Under this scheme, the government is approving to give equity to the MSEDCL in the form of an annual estimated cost of Rs 1500 crore for providing electricity connection to agricultural pumps in the traditional way. The approval is being given to the MSEDCL for the next 5 years i. e. till March 2024. A separate account should be opened for this purpose. Additional budgetary allocation is also being approved every year. The Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes category beneficiaries of Krishi Pump are being approved to budget the fund by sanctioning the necessary expenditure every year through the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance and the Department of Tribal Development respectively.
(3) The agricultural pump policy attached to the decision of the government is being approved to be implemented in the state.
This Government Resolution is being issued as per the concurrence and approval received under the Informal Reference No.4398 dated:- 14/12/2020 of the Finance Department.
