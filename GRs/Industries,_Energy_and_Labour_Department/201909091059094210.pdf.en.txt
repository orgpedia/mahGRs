To constitute a Committee to assign additional charge of the post of Chief Electrical Inspector.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Petition No. 34 (Part-II)/ Energy-5
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is September 9, 2019
Dinesh J. Khonde, Superintending Engineer, Regional Electricity Inspection Board, Aurangabad. The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal Bench, Nagpur, in the original application No. 26/2019 has been filed. A committee of officers of the rank of Principal Secretary of the department will be constituted to submit a report to the government for handing over the additional charge of the post. The Minister (Energy) has given instructions. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a committee to hand over the additional charge of the post of Chief Electrical Inspector.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department to submit a report on the assignment of additional charge of the post of Chief Electrical Inspector in the Electricity Inspectorate Branch under the Department of Energy.
Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department-Chairman (2) Additional Chief Secretary (Power), Industries, Energy and Labour Department Member (3) Principal Secretary, Public Works Department-Member (4) Principal Secretary, Law and Justice Department-Member (5) Deputy Secretary (Power), Industries, Energy and Labour Department Member Secretary
The functions of the said Committee shall be as follows:
The Government Circular, General Administration Department No. SRV-22018/ Q.No.208/ Work-12, Dy. Submission of report to the Government considering the guidelines dated 05/09/2018.
The term of the Committee shall be one week from the date of the order, however, the Committee shall submit its report to the Government by meeting immediately after the issuance of this Government Resolution.
