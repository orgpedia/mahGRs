The Government's Decisions:
Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation. According to the letter of the 197/03/2025, under the program 'Development of Handicrafts Industries', Rs. 68,61,234 - Funds were sought for this expense. However, the Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation is being sanctioned an expenditure of Rs.68,41,000 / - (Rupees Sixty-six lakh, forty-one thousand only) for the objectives of "31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment)" under the "Development of Handicrafts Industries" programme, as per this Government decision. Out of the expenditure incurred by the Corporation, the remaining amount is Rs. 20,234 / - to be spent from self-finance.
Expenditure on this should be met from the grant sanctioned for this budget year under the head "Demand No. K-7,28591 Village Industries B Small Industries, 104 Handicrafts Industries (00) (00) (02) Development of Handicrafts Industry (Programme) (2851 0567) 31 - Auxiliary Grants (Non-Payment)" and should be spent under the same head.
3. The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Corporation shall submit the appropriation certificate regarding the expenditure of the subsidy to the Government of India within the prescribed time period to the Drawing and Disbursing Authorities. Also, when inquiries are made by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-9, Mumbai, regarding the leakage of accounts, the Corporation shall submit the required documents to the Accountant General's Office. The corporation will be responsible for spending the funds for the purpose for which it has sought them. It shall be the responsibility of the corporation to spend the said funds only after ensuring that the procedure prescribed in the case is followed. The funds cannot be kept in the back account of their Subordinate Zonal Officers / Boards / Corporations, in the personal register account or outside the government account without any expenditure under any circumstances. If the funds disbursed are not spent in the fiscal year 2024-25, the corporation must SAT the funds.
