Preamble: As suggested by DPIIT under Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP) 2022, the Labour Commissioner, Mumbai vide letter dated 10.06.2022 has submitted a proposal regarding" Certificate of Registration of Establishments under Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation and Conditions of Employment) Act, 1996" to" consider that such service has been accepted after such period as may be decided by the State Government and accordingly modify the computer system for issue of Auto Generated Registration/ Improvement Certificate."
Government Resolution, Industries, Power and Labour Department, dated 24.07.2015 prescribes a period of 7 days for providing the service of" Certificate of Registration of Establishments under the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation and Conditions of Labour) Act, 1996." If the service was not provided during this period, the government was considering making necessary changes in the computer system to issue registration certificates through auto-generated means.
Government's decision:
As per the Government Decision dated 24.07.2015, a period of 7 days has been prescribed under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 to provide Establishment Registration Certificate services in the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation and Conditions of Employment) Act, 1996.
If the certificate of registration/ amendment has not been issued within seven working days from the date of submission of the completed application for establishment registration/ amendment certificate under the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation and Conditions of Employment) Act, 1996, the registration/ certificate of such establishment shall be deemed to have been amended and the certificate of registration/ amendment shall be issued automatically in electronic form.

In this regard, the government is giving approval to make necessary changes in the computer system.
Accordingly, the Labour Commissioner, Mumbai should take immediate action to make necessary changes in the relevant computer system from his level.
While provision has been made for Deemed Approval of Establishment Registration/ Improvement Certificate and automatic issuance of the said certificate in electronic form, the concerned Registrar shall ensure that the Certificate of Registration/ Improvement is issued within seven working days from the date of submission of complete application for Establishment Registration/ Improvement Certificate.
