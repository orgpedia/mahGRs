Introduction: - The Maharashtra SMAI, Visual Effects, Gaming, Comics and Extended Reality (AVGC-XR) Policy-2025 has been formulated. The announcement has been made by a government decision dated 03.11.2025. Financial as well as non-financial incentives have been paid under the policy to eligible AVGC-XR entities covered under the Maharashtra Animation, Visual Effects, Gaming, Comics and Extended Reality (AVGC-XR) Policy-2025.
In line with these incentives, the Government was considering framing of procedures / guidelines under the Animation, Visual Effects, Gaming Comics and Extended Reality (AVGC-XR) Policy-2025.
Government Decision: - Financial as well as non-financial incentives have been paid under the policy to eligible AVGC-XR entities covered under the Maharashtra Animation, Visual Effects, Gaming, Comics and Extended Reality (AVGC-XR) Policy-2025. Accordingly, the procedure / guidelines are being prescribed as per the Annexure attached thereto. These procedures / guidelines are being approved by the Government through this.
2. By making this Government Decision available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in
