Vide letter dated 25/11/2022 Shri. Borge Brende, President, World Economic Forum, 91-93
route de la Capite, CH-1223 Cology /Genva, Switzerland invited Hon'ble Chief Minister, Government of Maharashtra to participate in " World Economic+ Forum Annual Meeting 2023" at Davos-Switzerland from 16th to 20th January, 2023. The Forum is an international organization for
public-private cooperation which wants to work with Government of Maharashtra on the New Vision for Agriculture and other significant initiatives for the Maharashtra and India.
2. World Economic Forum Annual Meeting is the foremost gathering of global business
leaders as also senior leaders from governments, media, media & civil society. Over 3000 heads of the world's largest companies gather in Davos every year to participate in the Meeting. The WEF Annual Meeting therefore, provides a premier platform for policy makers from the Government of India and the State Governments to present their investment promotion policies to a large number of potential investors at one location.
In this background the State Government has decided to send Eight member's State level delegation led by Hon'ble Chief Minister, Government of Maharashtra to attend " World Economic Forum Annual Meeting 2023" to be held on 16th January to 20th January, 2023 at Davos Switzerland.
RESOLUTION:
Government of Maharashtra is pleased to accord ex-post-facto approval to the visit of the State level delegation led by Hon'ble Chief Minister, Government of Maharashtra to participate in "World Economic Forum Annual Meeting 2023" at Davos-Switzerland for the period mentioned in table below.

During the visit 8 members of delegation will attended the "World Economic Forum Annual
Meeting 2023" at Davos-Switzerland organized by World Economic Forum (WEF).
2. The Ministry of Finance, Department of Economic Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi
vide their letter dated 02/01/2023 have approved their clearance and The Ministry of External
Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi vide their letter dated 16/12/2022 have also approved the visit of delegation of Maharashtra to Davos-, Switzerland.
3. All the members of the official delegation will be entitled for travel expenses and per diem
allowance as per prescribed norms of Government of India.
4. State Government is further pleased to authorize the absence of Shri Bhushan Gagrani, Additional Chief Secretary to Hon, Chief Minister from 16/01/2023 to 20/01/2023 (Excluding travel
time), Dr.Harshdeep Kamble, Principal Secretary (Industries and Mining ) from 16/01/2023 to 20/01/2023 (Excluding travel time), Dr. Vipin Sharma, Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, Mumbai from 15/01/2023 to 17/01/2023 (Excluding travel time), Shri Yogesh Mahangade, Private Secretary to Hon. Minister of Industries from 16/01/2023 to 19/01/2023 (Excluding travel time), Shri Abhijit Ghorpade, General Manager (Marketing),
Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, Mumbai, from 16/01/2023 to 20/01/2023 (Excluding travel time), participating as part of delegation to attend World Economic Forum
Annual Meeting 2023" to be treated as on duty for the above mentioned period until they resume their duties on return to India from the foreign tour.
5. State Government is pleased to ex-post- facto sanction the processing fees for visa for Davos-Switzerland, as per actual and medical facilities admissible, under the revised AMA Scheme
continued in the Ministry of External Affairs O. M. No. Q/GA/653/ 1/74 dated 5/3/1979 to them.
The Government is also pleased to post facto sanction the charges for medical insurance for them.
6. The entire expenditure on travel and accommodation etc. for the members of delegation to
be borne by the Maharashtra Industrial development Corporation.
7. The detailed report for the tour and outcomes of the said tour with suggestions if any should
be submitted to the government within one month from the date of returning.
8. This Resolution issues with the concurrence of Additional Chief Secretary, Finance
Department vide dated 11/01/2023 and approval of Hon. Chief Minister.
