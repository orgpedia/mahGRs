The Logistics Policy-2024 of the State of Maharashtra has been announced on the date 26/08/2024.
Three committees namely, State Level Empowerment Committee (SLEC), State Level Control Committee (SLMC) and District Level Coordination Committee (DLCC) have been constituted to monitor the growth of the logistics sector in the state and for smooth implementation of Maharashtra Logistics Policy-2024. In line with compliance with role B responsibilities related to logistics policy, Ms. A State Level Empowerment Committee (SLEC) has been constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. The composition of the committee is Chairman + 18 Members + 1 Member Secretary. The Department of Skill Development, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation will take action to develop "Skills, AGIA, ASH, Startups, Skills University Affiliations, Apprenticeship Courses and Logistics related courses" in line with the Logistics Policy. Also, Innovation Hubs and Skoll Centres are to be set up in all the three categorized zones - Zone-1 (Vidarbha B Marathwada), Zone-2 (Ratnagiri-Sindhudurg, Dhule-Nandurbar), and Zone-3 (rest of Maharashtra) to give special impetus to startups. Accordingly, the inclusion of the Additional Chief Secretary (Department of Skill Development, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation) as a member of the committee was under consideration of the Government.
2. D. As per the provision in the Logistics Policy-2024 of the State of Maharashtra announced by the Government Decision, M / s. It is approving the inclusion of Additional Chief Secretary (Skill Development, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation) as a member of the State Level VAT Committee (SLEC) constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary.
