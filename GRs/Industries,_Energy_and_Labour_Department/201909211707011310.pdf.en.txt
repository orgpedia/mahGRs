Introduction: Industrialization of local people to provide employment to the local people of the state through industrial development.
The policy of giving priority to jobs in enterprises has been adopted through the Government Decision No. 1, and the Government Decision No. 2 to 4 regarding the organization of combined employment fairs of employers, factory workers, educated unemployed to provide employment/ self-employment to the educated unemployed in order to effectively implement the Employment and Self-Employment Policy of the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. the state
In order to provide employment to the local people in the industrial sector and to establish micro and small enterprises in the state with adequate opportunities for self-employment arising from industrial development, the government was considering to give priority to the local people in employment and guidelines for a conducive environment for self-employment by holding employment and self-employment fairs at various places in the state. In this regard, the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor has issued a Government Decision dated 24/4/2018.
As certain shortcomings and deficiencies have been noticed while implementing the said Government Decision and need to be rectified, the Government has decided to revise the said Decision vide Circular No. 24/4/2018.
The revised Government Decision is being issued as follows: Government Decision: Local educated unemployed in the state will get more opportunities for employment and self-employment
District level/ industrial sector-wise employment self-employment fairs should be held to provide employment and employment through self-employment in industrial establishments at local and other levels by making a suitable balance between the industrial sector where employment opportunities are available and the skills of the local educated unemployed.
The government has decided to hold it with the approval of the Minister (Industry).
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries will be responsible for the successful organization, coordination and control of the Employment and Self-Employment Fair and for the decisions, approvals and other related matters regarding the expenditure for the said event. The Development Commissioner (Industries) shall request the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, Mumbai, for the expenditure to be incurred for the fair and accordingly the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, Mumbai, shall make the financial provision for the same and take further action.
