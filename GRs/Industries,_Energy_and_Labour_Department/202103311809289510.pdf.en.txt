Government Decision: The Central Government has decided to increase the efficiency and financial capacity of the electricity distribution companies in the country.
The plan has been approved. A tripartite agreement (MoU) has been signed between the Union Ministry of Power, the state government and MSEDCL to participate in the scheme. The decision was taken on December 27, 2016.
Under the Scheme, the State Government has approved the sale of Non-SLR/ SDL Bonds amounting to Rs. 4959.75 crore, amounting to 75% of the total amount of Rs.6613 crore of MSEDCL as on 30th September, 2015. The MSEDCL has to repay the loan with interest to the government.
