Introduction Maharashtra Industrial Infrastructure Development Committee Reference No. In the meeting of 4th Co-operative Industrial Estate and Integrated Textile Park, Tardal, Avade, Kallapan in Kolhapur district. Industrial infrastructure works in Hatkanangale G. Kolhapur Co-operative Industrial Estate have been approved. The government has to pay 75 per cent of the government share i.e. Rs. 187.38 lakh to the implementing agency. Earlier, 25 per cent of the company's share i.e. Rs. 62.46 lakh was to be deposited by the company with the concerned executing agency i.e. MIDC. The funds were not disbursed as 25 per cent of their share was not deposited by the institution. The Directorate General of Industries (DGI) has informed that the company has deposited 25% of its shareholding with the executing agency as on 09/03/2021. As reported by 17/03/2021. Hence, the government had given administrative sanction for the project.
