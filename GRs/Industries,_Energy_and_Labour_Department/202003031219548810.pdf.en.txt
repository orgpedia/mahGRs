The Proposal:
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in public interest, it is the policy of the Government to review the service of officers/ employees with inefficient and doubtful integrity at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire the ineligible officers/ employees from Government service prematurely.
In accordance with the said policy of the Government, the General Administration Department has issued a consolidated Government decision under Reference No. 1 regarding the action to be taken regarding the review by authorizing various Government decisions, circulars, etc. issued earlier in this regard. In pursuance of the said decision, the Government was contemplating to constitute a Review Committee to review the Group-A and Group-B cadre officers of the Directorate of Industries under this Department at the age of 50/55 years or on completion of 30 years of qualifying service and to constitute a Grievance Committee to consider the grievances against the decision of premature retirement of the officers/ employees who were found ineligible to continue in service by the Review Committee.
Government's decision:
Ministry of Industry, Energy and Labour. 2 The Government hereby supersedes the decision of the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 In view of the provisions of the Government decision, the following Departmental Review Committee is being constituted to review whether the Group A and Group B cadre officers of the Directorate of Industries under this Department are eligible to continue in Government service or not.
