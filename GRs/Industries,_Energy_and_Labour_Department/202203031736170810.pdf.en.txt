The Proposal:
Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) is conducting a two-year integrated probationary training programme through Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur for the posts in Group A and Group B respectively. The decision is being implemented as per the government's 1. Accordingly, Deputy Director of Industries (Technical) Group A and Industry Officer (Technical) Group B cadre under the Directorate of Industries under the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour have been included as reference No. Under the decision of the Government of 2, it has been approved to conduct a combined probationary training program. The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has released the final results of the State Service (Main) Examination-2017 and 2018 for the candidates selected for the Combined Subject Training Programme-5 and 6. 30.9.2019 to D. The maximum number of posts were created on 03.10.2021 for a period of two years. However, since the allocation of cadre to the officers under the purview of the said purview will take some time to be determined and decided as per the service excellence, the Department has been informed of the extension of the said posts vide letter No. 4 of the General Administration Department. Hence, the government was considering to extend the deadline.
Government Decision: 7 posts of Industry Officer (Technical) Group-B and 2 posts of Deputy Director of Industries (Technical) Group-A have been created on the establishment of the Directorate of Industries under the decision of the Government at 3 and 4 respectively. 04.10.2021 to D. The period is being extended for 03.03.2022.
