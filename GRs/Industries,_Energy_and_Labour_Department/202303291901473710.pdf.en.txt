Introduction: The increasing number of educated youth in urban and rural areas of the state and new opportunities for self-employment and employment in various fields in the state in the field of industry, business. Considering these new opportunities of self-employment and employment, the comprehensive scheme of Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme (CMEGP), which promotes entrepreneurship and promotes creativity in a time-bound manner, has been implemented as per the Government decision dated 01/08/2019. Accordingly, State Level and District Level Controlling Committees have been constituted to provide various opportunities of self-employment to the urban and rural youth of the state and for effective implementation of the scheme.
Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program, the state government will provide financial assistance in the form of grants from the state government's budgetary provision to establish self-employment projects to make the youth of the state self-sufficient/ self-reliant. Accordingly, a new head of account has been opened for the General Category and Scheduled Tribes under the scheme vide Government Order dated 23/01/2020. Also, for the Scheduled Castes category, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance provides provision in accordance with the objectives.
The Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme is being implemented through the District Industries Center under the Directorate of Industries under the Industries Department and the District Khadi and Village Industries Office under the Khadi and Village Industries Board. Accordingly, the Additional Director of Industries, Directorate of Industries vide their letter dated 16/02/2023 has requested to provide the required amount of Rs. 1.70 crore for disbursement of margin money for 126 pending proposals of ST category under the scheme.
Accordingly, the Government was considering to disburse an amount of Rs. 1.70 crore made available to the Department by the Tribal Development Department under Government Resolution dated 28/03/2023 for this purpose.
