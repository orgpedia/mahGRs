Government Decision- Exporters of the state should be recognized and appreciated for their export performance and in future, exporters should do more than the past and export more.
In order to contribute to the export operations, the scheme of conferring of insignia and quality certificates to the exporters of the State for their outstanding performance in exports is being implemented by the Development Commissioner (Industries) since 1971-72. As per the decision of the Government dated 20/01/2003 to select the best exporters for the awards to the small and medium enterprises in the State, the Government has decided to award Rs. A selection committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Industries). Accordingly, the State Export Excellence Awards for the year 2017-18 have been announced. As per the directions of Principal Secretary (Industries), a meeting was held on 30/12/2020 under the chairmanship of Development Commissioner (Industries) through online mode. In the meeting, 34 exporters of different categories have been selected for the financial year 2017-18. Accordingly, a proposal for administrative approval and disbursement of funds for organizing one day Export Promotion Workshop and Export Excellence Award Distribution Programme at World Trade Centre, Mumbai has been submitted by the Additional Director of Industries, Directorate of Industries vide their letter dated 17/02/2022. Administrative approval is being granted to the proposal of Rs. 22.5 lakh mentioned in the following statement submitted by the Additional Director of Industries, Directorate of Industries. The Directorate of Industries, Mumbai is the implementing agency for organizing these events and workshops.
