The Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill, 2019 has been passed by the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. This Act has been published as Maharashtra Act No. 2 in the Maharashtra Government Gazette, Part-IV, dated 8th March, 2019. A provision of Rs. 2206.70 lakh has been made. Under this provision, the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board was under consideration of the Government to provide subsidy to meet the salary and non-salary expenses for the month of April, 2019 to May, 2019
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, a total grant of Rs. 1103.46 lakh (Rs. Eleven crore three lakh forty seven thousand only) is being sanctioned to Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai for meeting the expenditure for two months i.e. April and May, for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 1056.98 lakh and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 46.48 lakh.
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2019-20 under the targets of Demand No. K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (28510585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) and should be spent under the same head.
