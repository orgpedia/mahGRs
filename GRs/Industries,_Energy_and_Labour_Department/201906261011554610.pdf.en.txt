Periodic transfers of Superintending Engineer, Group-A (Gazetted) Cadre Officers w.e.f.2019.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
GOVERNMENT ORDER NO-2019/ PR No.79/ ENERGY-5
Ministry of Health, Mumbai 32.
The date: June 2019
As per the provisions of section 4 (1), 4 (2) and 4 (3) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the power of transfer of officers/ employees of the Electricity Inspectorate Branch has been notified and with the approval of the competent authority, Mr. S. R. Nalawade, Superintending Engineer, Pune Regional Electricity Inspectorate Board, Pune is being transferred to the post of Superintending Engineer, Mumbai Regional Electricity Inspectorate Board, Mumbai.
2. Mr. S. R. Nalawade, Superintending Engineer. Pune Regional Electricity Inspection Board, Pune is being relieved from its duties with effect from 26.06.2019 (BCE). Mr. Nalawade, Superintending Engineer should join the new place of transfer immediately and submit a compliance report to the government. The additional charge of the post of Superintending Engineer, Pune Regional Electrical Inspector Board, is being given till further orders.
Further, disciplinary action will be initiated against the concerned Superintending Engineer as per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, if it is found that political pressure/ coercion has been exerted to cancel or modify the transfers made under this order.
