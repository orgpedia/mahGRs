Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to appoint persons with disabilities for posts under the State Government. The central government. The list of posts reserved for Persons with Disabilities as per notification dated 04/01/2021 has been published. As per the notification of the Central Government (on the lines of Central Government), instructions regarding ensuring posts for Divyangjan in State Government establishments have been issued by the Department of Government Decisions, Social Justice and Special Assistance. 02/02/2021. Accordingly, the matter of ensuring posts for the disabled on the establishment of Industrial Court, Maharashtra, Mumbai was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. 04/01/2021 Notification and Government Decision, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. In view of the instructions issued under 02/02/2021, it is being ensured that the posts in the establishment of Industrial Court, Maharashtra, Mumbai for the disabled persons are as mentioned in the accompanying annexure.
