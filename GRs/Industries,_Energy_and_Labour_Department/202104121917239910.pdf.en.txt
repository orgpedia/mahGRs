The Proposal:
Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar is known as the father of freedom and freedom. In adverse circumstances, he has set a new example by getting higher education from home and abroad. It is this education that has revolutionized the lives of the oppressed, marginalized sections and the whole of India and in a way laid the foundation of modern India.
130th birth anniversary of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar celebrated It will be celebrated on 14 April 2021. On the occasion of this birth anniversary, a special campaign for electricity connection was launched to illuminate the lives of people belonging to the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes in the state. from April 14, 2021. The government was considering the implementation of 6 December 2021.
Government's decision:
Objective:
Dr. It has been decided to implement Babasaheb Ambedkar Jeevan Prakash Yojana.
The scheme will also cover redressal of grievances/ problems related to power supply in the unorganized sector of SC/ ST.
Beneficiaries allowed:
The eligibility of the beneficiaries for household electricity connection under the scheme will be as follows: 1. The beneficiary applicant will be required to have a caste certificate from the competent authority.
2. The MSEDCL shall be required to furnish the required documents such as Aadhaar Card, Resident Certificate etc. for applying as per the prescribed format for electricity connection.
3) Applicants should not have any previous arrears at the place of application.
The plan of action:
1.The beneficiary applicant will apply online or offline with documents in the prescribed format of MSEDCL.
The applicant will be required to attach the test report of the electricity setup by the government-recognized electricity contractor with the application.

3.The beneficiary will get Rs. A deposit of Rs. 500/- is required to be made. The amount will be paid in 5 equal monthly installments.
4.After receiving the complete application of the applicants. The power connection will be provided subject to the existing provisions of the Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission in this regard.
As per the application received by MSEDCL, the electricity connection will be provided to the beneficiaries whose house does not have electricity connection in the next 15 working days if the electricity infrastructure is available.
6. Wherever the applicant is required to provide electricity infrastructure for power connection, it shall be taken up and completed by MSEDCL on priority from its own funds or District Planning Development Fund (including Special Component Plan and Tribal Sub Plan) or other funds as may be available.
Further, in order to resolve the power problems in the unorganized industries of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes on priority, a task force will be formed at the level of MSEDCL for their jurisdiction under the chairmanship of the Superintending Engineer in each mandal or district. Such a task force should consist of entrepreneurs representing the Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes in that area. It will be reviewed every month at the level of Chief Engineer.
A dedicated web portal will be created at the MSEDCL level to address the grievances of the scheme.
9. The scheme will be published by MSEDCL.
The Chairman and Managing Director shall submit to the Government every month a report on the results of the monitoring and review of the scheme, as may be required by MSEDCL, but at least quarterly.
The funds for this scheme can be made from MSEDCL/ District Development Committee funds (including Special Component Plan and Tribal Sub Plan)/ Agriculture Contingency Fund.
After the completion of the period of this scheme, if there are success stories and errors in this regard, instructions are being given to submit a result report to the government.
