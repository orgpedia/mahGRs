The Proposal:
As per the decision of the General Administration Department here, instructions have been given to create a maximum number of posts for officers/ employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribe category, but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or have submitted other caste validity certificate, to send a demand letter to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, District Selection Committee to fill the posts that will be vacant in this way. As per the provisions of this Government decision, the services of a total of 6 employees in the Directorate of Industries under this department have been made in a number of posts and the matter of creating a number of posts for them was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Returns in the Directorate of Industries where court cases are pending regarding the caste validity certificate of persons appointed from the Scheduled Tribe category but who have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes
A total of 6 (2 posts of Comment Assistant, 2 posts of Branch Head, 1 post of Industry Inspector (Group-C) and 1 post of Clerk-Typist) additional posts are being created for the employees.
The said supernumerary posts shall continue to be in existence for a period of 11 months temporarily in the office in which they are presently working or till the date on which they would have retired, whichever is earlier, had they been in Government service.
After that, the positions will be eliminated.
