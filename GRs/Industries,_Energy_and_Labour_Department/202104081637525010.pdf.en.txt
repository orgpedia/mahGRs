" All registered active (Jeevit) construction workers will continue to be eligible to avail the benefits of both Maharashtra Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana (Urban) and Atal Nirman Awas Yojana (Rural) under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana. "
