Preamble: Construction workers in rural areas registered with Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board (ACWWWB) will be eligible to apply for construction of new house on their own land or conversion of existing kutcha house into pucca house in rural areas through Atal Construction Workers Awas Yojana (Gramin) Board and Rs. 1,50,000/- has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 14th January, 2019.
In the Atal Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana (Gramin), the beneficiary must have land. The government was considering providing financial assistance to the beneficiaries to reimburse the expenses incurred on the purchase of land and other related matters.
Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board: Construction workers who have completed the construction of a house and submitted a certificate of completion of the house by taking advantage of the Atal Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana (Gramin) will be eligible for financial assistance from the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board.
The Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board will provide Rs. Financial assistance up to Rs. 50,000 will be provided. The amount will be directly credited to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries.
