Introduction: Read on. As per the directions of the Central Government vide Government Order dated 19.05.2018, a State Resource Centre with a three-tier structure under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Labour) was constituted by the Government. However, since the structure of this center is not in accordance with the instructions in the office memorandum at No. 2 under the central government, read the government decision. The matter of issuing a new government decision by superseding 19.05.2018 was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Regarding the proposal. By virtue of the Government Resolution dated 19.05.2018, the Government hereby approves the establishment of the following State Resource Centre under the Chairmanship of the Commissioner (Labour), Labour Commissionerate, Labour Bhawan, Bandra (East) Mumbai-400 051.
