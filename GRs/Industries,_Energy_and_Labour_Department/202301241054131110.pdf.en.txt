Preamble: In order to promote the development of small clusters in the industrially underdeveloped areas of the State and thereby promote the overall development of micro and small units, the Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP) has been announced vide Government Resolution dated 25/02/2014. Under the scheme, the state government provides grant-in-aid for the implementation of capacity building programmes for the overall development and growth of micro and small enterprises as well as for the construction of common facilities. Under this scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared by properly diagnosing the flaws in the production process of the industrial group, including the latest technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial groups, updated training programs and study tours, etc. After the diagnostic study report is received on the recommendation of the district level committee, the State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee approves the diagnostic study report proposal and determines the subsidy for the capacity building programme. A Detailed Project Report (DPR) containing details of high quality common facilities and other necessary clearances, no-objection certificates, technical and financial evaluation reports are prepared to enhance the productivity and competitiveness of MSME units. The procurement process is initiated after the proposal for Common Facility Centre is first approved by the State Level Steering Committee. 60% of the first phase subsidy is disbursed after receiving the physical verification report of the machinery, equipment, workstation as approved. The remaining 40% subsidy is provided after the successful completion of the Common Facility Centre by obtaining a certificate from the Joint Director of Industry concerned. Accordingly, the Common Facility Center of the Industrial Group Project Fabrication Cluster, Paravas G. Satara has become fully operational and the certificate of successful completion of this Common Facility Center has been submitted by the Joint Director of Industries, Divisional Office, Pune through the Directorate of Industries. Accordingly, the Government was considering to disburse the final eligible grant of Rs. 1,90,62, 800/- from the State Government's share for the construction of Common Facility Centre of the project.

Government Decision: The Directorate of Industries has issued a proposal for the final disbursement of the eligible grant of the State Government of Rs. 1,90,62, 800/- for the construction of a Common Facility Centre of the Fabrication Cluster, Paravas G. Satara Project approved under the scheme" Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" of the State Government. 3 is presented here. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the present scheme, the Government is approving the final grant of Rs. 1,90,62, 800/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety Lakh Sixty Two Thousand Eight Hundred only).
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall arrange to transfer the amount of Rs. 1,90,62, 800/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety Lakh Sixty Two Thousand Eight Hundred only) from the Government treasury to the Fabrication Cluster, Paravas G. Satara, a group project. The powers to draw and disburse the aforesaid amount of Rs. 1,90,62, 800/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety Lakh Sixty Two Thousand Eight Hundred only) are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
As per the provisions mentioned in the Government Circular No. 2022/ No. 43/ Finance-3 of the Finance Department, dated 04/04/2022 and the accompanying annexure, as well as the information received from the Directorate of Industries, it is certified that the Government Decision dated 25/02/2014 of the" Maharashtra State Industrial Group Development Program" has been incorporated in the scheme, which has given information about the duration of the scheme, the implementation mechanism, the distribution of grants, various sanctioning committees. Utilization certificates of the grants released earlier in connection with the said project have been made available by the Directorate of Industries. Also, no issues have been raised by the auditors with regard to the grants released earlier for the said project.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall take necessary action to monitor the progress of the said group project and the proper utilization and utilization of the Government shareholding.
The expenditure for this scheme should be spent under the head" Demand No. K-7, 2851- Village Industries and Small Industries, 00 102 Small Industries- Scheme under State Schemes (00) (24) Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP) (2851 6378) 33- Financial Assistance" and it should be met from the budgetary provision approved in the financial year 2022-23.
This Government decision is being issued under the authority conferred on the Administrative Department as the conditions contained in the Government Circular No. Finance-2022/ No. 43/ Finance-3, dated 04/04/2022 of the Department of Finance and its Annexure are being fulfilled.
