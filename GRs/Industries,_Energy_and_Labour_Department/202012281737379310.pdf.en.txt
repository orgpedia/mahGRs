Preamble- The Central Government has decided to provide online services in the context of the above Reference No. 2 here. As directed vide letter dated 19.8.2019. Maharashtra is the leader in the production of vapour and other allied systems in India. The Vapour Directorate under this Department examines and approves/ standardizes the vapour, pressure vessel, pressure parts, fittings, piping at various stages before and during manufacture. However, due to the Covid-19 outbreak, the postal service in Mumbai from outside the district was not running smoothly, due to the lack of permission from the local administration to travel to submit the drawing directly, etc., the government was considering to provide this service online.
