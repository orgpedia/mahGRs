Preamble: A new Textile Policy has been notified vide Government Decision No. 2017/ P.No.6/ Tex-5, dated 15/02/2018 of the Department of Co-operative Marketing and Textiles for the period 2018-2023 which will expire on 31/03/2023. Pursuant to the implementation of Para- 7.9.1 and 7.9.2 pertaining to this section of the said policy, in respect of a mega project in the textile sector, Rs. Textile projects with a capital investment of Rs.100 crore or new or expansion projects generating 250 jobs will be accorded the status and incentives of Mega Projects under the Industrial Policy-2019 and the Collective Incentive Scheme-2019. Also, the status and incentives of mega projects will be given considering the investment or employment of textile projects set up at different places in the same taluka. The Government Decision dated 08/05/2018 has been issued by the Department of Industries fixing such criteria.
Meanwhile, the New Industrial Policy-2019 of Maharashtra was announced on 07/03/2019 and the corresponding collective incentive scheme was announced on 16/09/2019. Government Resolution No: MADHO-2017/ PR No.119/ INDUSTRY-2
In pursuance of Maharashtra New Industrial Policy-2019 dated 07/03/2019, a Mass Incentive Scheme has been announced vide Government Resolution dated 16/09/2019. Under the said Government decision, the criteria for the mega project have been modified as indicated in the above table. Though there is a change in the criteria for mega projects as per the Industrial Policy-2019 and the Collective Incentive Scheme-2019, since the five year period of the Textile Policy-2018 is coming to an end on 31/03/2023, the period of the Textile Policy-2018 has been extended to provide mega project status and incentives to the projects in the textile sector. It was under consideration of the Government to issue a revised Government Resolution to be in accordance with the criteria in the Government Resolution dated 08/05/2018.
Government's decision
Notwithstanding the change in the criteria for grant of Mega Project Status as per the Industrial Policy-2019 and the Collective Incentive Scheme-2019, the criteria for grant of Mega Project Status and incentives to the projects in the Textile Sector will continue to be the same during the period of the Collective Incentive Scheme-2019, as per the Textile Policy-2018 and the Government Decision dated 08/05/2018 issued by the Department of Industries vide its Order dated 31/03/2023.
1) Under the collective incentive scheme implemented by the Industries Department, Rs. Textile projects with a capital investment of Rs 100 crore or new or expansion projects creating 250 jobs will be given mega project status and incentives.
2) The status and incentives of mega projects will be given considering the investment or employment of textile projects set up at different places in the same taluka.
The financial returns/ incentives to textile projects from all sources shall be admissible only to the extent of 100% of the total fixed capital investment made by the entity as per the provisions of Para. No. 10.2.5 of the New Industrial Policy-2019 of Maharashtra.
