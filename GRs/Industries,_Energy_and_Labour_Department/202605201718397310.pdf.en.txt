Office: The office of the Divisional Level Coordination Committee (DLCC) constituted under the chairmanship of the Divisional Commissioner will be as follows.
1) To resolve the issues and queries sent by the District Advisory Committee and District Industry Friends (ZUM) of the Department.
2) To review the coordination of the work of various corporations and other agency bodies concerned with the industrial development of the division.

3) Review the general progress of industrialization in the region.
4) Vata the problems and issues arising in industrial development and guide the planning of industrial development in the region.
5) To coordinate with various government departments for immediate receipt of licenses / clearances, facilities and assistance / grants from the government, corporations, boards and local authorities related to industries.
6) Review of sick industries.
7) To establish strategic coordination for the development of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises by identifying existing and potential industry sectors in the region.
8) Problems faced by industries in the division such as land, seeds, water, pollution, transportation issues, etc.
To coordinate with various government departments in the matter and take measures to resolve the issues expeditiously.
9) To make recommendations to the Governments on developing new industrial estates / parks / clusters and Industry 4.0 in the Department.
10) To coordinate with Skill Development Institutes, Institutes of Industrial Technology, Government / Semi-Government Training Institutes to create employment opportunities for local youth.
11) Coordinate with the relevant agencies to remove export barriers to sectoral export-oriented industries.
12) To regularly review the industrial progress and submit recommendations and reports to the Government for promoting industrial development in the Department.
13) The Departmental Level Coordination Committee (DLCC) will be required to meet at least once every three months.
