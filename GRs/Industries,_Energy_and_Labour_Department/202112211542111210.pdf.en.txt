Preamble: Department of Finance Government Circular No.No. Guidelines for fixing the revised figure were issued on 29.06.2017. Accordingly, a total of 481 posts in Group-A, Group-B, Group-C and Group-D cadres on the establishment of the Directorate of Geology and Mining, Nagpur were under the consideration of the Government.
Accordingly, a high-level committee of secretaries was formed on Tuesday to thoroughly examine the proposals for the creation of new posts, revival of posts and review of posts. The revised pattern of posts in the Directorate of Geology and Mines and its subordinate offices has been approved in the 4th meeting held on 13.07.2021.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, a total of 366 regular posts (including 2 new posts) in a total of 44 cadres as shown in the accompanying prospectus' A,"' B,"' C,"' D,"' E" and' E" in the Directorate of Geology and Mining, Nagpur under the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour and its subordinate offices, and a total of 398 (366 regular posts and 32 posts through external agencies) posts in a total of 5 cadres to be taken up by external agencies.

In accordance with the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries dated 13.07.2021, the Government have decided to fill up the vacant and lapsed posts in the revised figure as on the date of this Government decision. These posts have been removed by the government. Also, the expenditure on salaries and allowances of the employees, which has been fixed as per the revised figure, should continue to be spent under the same head of account as was being spent earlier. After the retirement of the employee, the post will be vacant.
As per the approval given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 13.07.2021, the Government has decided to abolish two posts of Senior Deputy Director (Group-A) and create two new posts of Deputy Director (Group-A) in the establishment of Director, Directorate of Geology and Mining, Nagpur.
5. Expenditure on salaries and allowances of the posts fixed as per the revised figure shall continue to be incurred under the same head of account as was being incurred earlier.
6. The said Government Decision is based on Informal Reference No.378/ APUK, D. being issued pursuant to the approval granted under 20.09.2021.
