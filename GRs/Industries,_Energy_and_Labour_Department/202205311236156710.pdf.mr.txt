PREAMBLE
Vide letter dated 07.05.2022 Shri. Chandrajit Banerjee, Director General, Confederation of Indian Industry (CII), Lodhi Road, New Delhi invited Hon'ble Minister (Industries) of
Maharashtra to participate in World Economic Forum Annual Meeting 2022" at Davos Switzerland from 22th to 26th May, 2022. The Forum is an international organization for public -private cooperation which wants to work with Government of Maharashtra on the New Vision for Agriculture and other significant initiatives for the Maharashtra and India.
2. World Economic Forum Annual Meeting is the foremost gathering of global business
leaders as also senior leaders from governments, media, media & civil siciety. Over 3000 heads
of the world's largest companies gather in Davos every year to participate in the Meeting. The
WEF Annual Meeting therefore, provides a premier platform for policy makers from the
Government of India and the State Governments to present their investment promotion policies
to a large number of potential investors at one location.
In this background the State Government has decided to send nine member's State level delegation led by Hon'ble Shri. Subhash Desai, Minister (Industries) to attend World Economic Forum Annual Meeting 2022" to be held on 22nd May to 26th May,2022 at Davos Switzerland.
