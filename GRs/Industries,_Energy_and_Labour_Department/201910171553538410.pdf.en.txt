Department of Industry, Energy and Labour Government Decision No. EST-2019/ No. 134/ Labour- 8D. 21/8/2019.
As per the decision of the government, the office of the Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Amravati has been approved as the divisional office of the Labour Department for the 5 districts of Amravati, Washim, Buldhana, Akola and Yavatmal under the Amravati revenue division.
The matter of declaring Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Regional Office Head for Amravati Division was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, it is being approved to declare Deputy Commissioner of Labour Amravati as the Head of Regional Office for the Office of the Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Amravati, which has been started as the Divisional Office of the Department of Labour under the Revenue Department.
