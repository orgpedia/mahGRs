The Proposal:
Department of Finance, Govt. 01/07/2021, to adopt the revised methodology prescribed under the Memorandum dated 23/03/2021 of the Government of India for effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more effectiveness in the process of fund allocation. As per the revised procedure, the concerned administrative department shall appoint a single nodal agency (SNA) for the implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme and open a separate single nodal bank account in the authorized bank for the scheme and appoint at least a Deputy Secretary level officer as nodal officer for the coordination of the scheme. Accordingly, a special assistance scheme in the form of a loan for capital expenditure (non-interest loan) to the state to be implemented by this department through central assistance to the state was under the consideration of the government to appoint a nodal officer, Single Nodal Agency (SNA) at the state level for the implementation of this scheme by mapping it on the Public Financial Management System (PFMS), opening a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) authorized bank and opening a Child Account for the implementing agency.
Government's decision:
Scheme for Special Assistance as Loan to State for Capital Expenditure (Scheme Code-3888) Under the scheme of the Central Government, funds will be provided for specific works in various schemes of the State Government for the year 2022-23. The Finance Department has instructed to transfer the funds to the Public Management System (PFMS). Accordingly, separate head of accounts have been opened for the implementation of the scheme and decisions are being taken to open a separate single nodal bank account in the authorized bank and child account for the implementing agency by mapping the scheme with PFMS.
The State Project Management Cell, Mumbai will take action for mapping of these schemes on Public Financial Management System (PFMS).

For the implementation of these schemes, Deputy Secretary (Power-3), Industries, Energy and Labour Department is being selected as State Scheme Manager (SSM) at the administrative department level. It will act as the single nodal agency (SNA) for the scheme. Under Secretary (Power-3), Department of Industries, Power and Labour will act as the First Level Agency.
Under this decision, it is being approved to open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account of the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for the implementation of these schemes and a Child Account for the implementation agency in the Bank of Maharashtra Mantralaya Branch.
4.1) The Bank of Maharashtra will provide the necessary manpower to the field offices for the implementation of the scheme.
4.2) The process of opening a Child Account at the level of Controller Officer (Superintending Engineer) and Drawing and Disbursing Officer (Executive Engineer) for the implementation of the scheme at the regional level will be done by the Bank of Maharashtra at the state level. For this, all the Controlling Officers (Superintending Engineers) and Drawing and Distribution Officers (Executive Engineers) should immediately make their office address available on the e-mail address of Back of Maharashtra IFSC Code- MAHB0001388 and bomb1388@mahabank.
