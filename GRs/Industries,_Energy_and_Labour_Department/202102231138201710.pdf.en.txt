Preamble: The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, vide its Reference No. 3, has requested to provide a grant of Rs. 1744.10 lakh and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 248.48/-for meeting the expenditure on salary, superannuation salary and non-salary for the three months January to March, 2021 respectively.
2. As the Budget Division of this Department has provided subsidy under the head Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (28510585) under the sub-heading 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the extent of 75%, a subsidy of Rs. 11,62,66, 000/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Sixty Two Lakh Sixty Six Thousand only) has been disbursed to meet the expenditure of salary, retirement salary etc. for the two months November, December, 2020 as per the Government decision dated 14.12.2020. Now, the provision is made available under this sub-heading to the extent of 90%.
The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board was under consideration of the government to provide subsidy to meet the expenses on salary, retirement salary, etc. for the month of January, 2021.
Government's decision:
Under this decision, the Government is approving the release of grant-in-aid of Rs. 5,81,33, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore Eighty-one Lakh Thirty-three Thousand only) to Khadi and Village Industries Board (KVIC) towards 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to meet the expenditure on salary, superannuation salary, contribution for the month of January, 2021.
