The Proposal:
The Government of India has issued guidelines in June, 2015 based on the concept of Housing for All to ensure that every family in the country has access to a rightful home.
Accordingly, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana has been implemented in the State through the Housing Department as per the Government Decision dated 09.12.2015. The Maharashtra Construction Workers Housing Scheme for Urban Areas has been announced under the Government decision dated 03.02.2018 to expand the scope of the scheme to include construction workers in the building and other construction sectors in the State. The following terms and conditions have been set to qualify as a beneficiary.
A housing project eligible for benefits under the Maharashtra Construction Workers Housing Scheme should be registered under the MahaRERA Act, 2016.
The beneficiary worker should be registered with the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board and eligible under the scheme.
(a) The eligible workers as per clause (b) above, participating in the housing scheme in any of the projects registered under clause (a) of the Maharashtra Construction Workers Housing Scheme, shall be paid a sum of Rs. 2 lakh will be eligible for the grant.
As per the Government decision dated 03.02.2018, the responsibility of determining the eligibility of construction workers has been entrusted to the concerned Board and Labour Department. Accordingly, the eligibility of the construction worker to apply for a flat in the Maharashtra Construction Workers Housing Scheme in urban areas is being determined as follows.

(a) The area selected for implementation of the scheme and the area to be applied for by the (active) construction worker in this entry.
(1) The Maharashtra Construction Workers Housing Scheme has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 03.02.2018 and the scheme is implemented by the Director, State Mission Directorate (Steering Agency), which is functioning in urban areas.
(2) Under this scheme, the registered (active) construction workers of the same district can demand for the flats in the selected housing project in the urban area. However, registered (active) construction workers in Mumbai, Navi Mumbai and Thane will be able to apply for flats available in projects in the periphery of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA) area.
(b) Details of the project for which flats are available, eligibility verification and selection of beneficiaries.
(1) Developers registered under the MahaRERA Act 2016 have been given exemptions and concessions by various departments of the government for the approved housing projects under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana. Accordingly, under the Maharashtra Construction Workers Housing Scheme, the cost of the apartment in the project in urban areas, the area of the apartment will be verified, and the site of the housing project will be determined and the list will be made available to the Housing Department, Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board. The Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board should submit the names of the construction workers who are willing and eligible for the flats in this list to the Director, State Mission Directorate (Steering Agency).
(2) The Director, State Mission Directorate (Steering Agency) shall take action to transfer the applications of the (active) construction workers from the list of applications received from the Director, State Mission Directorate (Steering Agency) for the flats in the projects sanctioned under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana to the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board for checking the eligibility of the said applications.
(3) The Steering Agency shall have the powers to select the beneficiary from the list submitted by the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board to the Director, State Mission Directorate (Steering Agency) and to carry out the necessary procedures for the same. The construction worker (active) in the register finalized for allotment of flats by the steering agency will be eligible for the Board's grant as a beneficiary.
