Preamble: In order to promote the development of small clusters in the industrially underdeveloped areas of the State and thereby promote the overall development of micro and small units, the Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP) has been announced vide Government Resolution dated 25/02/2014. Under the scheme, the state government provides grant-in-aid for the implementation of capacity building programmes for the overall development and growth of micro and small enterprises as well as for the construction of common facilities.
Under the MSICDP scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared by properly diagnosing the flaws in the production process of the industrial group, including the latest technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial groups, updated training programs and study tours, etc. After the diagnostic study report is received on the recommendation of the district level committee, the State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee approves the diagnostic study report proposal and determines the subsidy for the capacity building programme. A Detailed Project Report (DPR) containing details of high quality common facilities and other necessary clearances, no-objection certificates, technical and financial evaluation reports are prepared to enhance the productivity and competitiveness of MSME units. The procurement process is initiated after the final approval of the State Level Steering Committee for the proposal of the Common Facility Centre.
As per the revised guidelines of MSICDP Scheme dated 24/07/2019, after final approval of the proposal of Common Facility Centre by the State Level Steering Committee, the first phase 20% subsidy is disbursed to the projects whose procurement process has been completed by the Zonal Committee after receiving the report along with the procurement details. Thereafter, after receiving the physical verification report from the General Manager, District Industries Centre regarding the installation of machinery and equipment at the work site as per the project approval and after receiving the utilization certificate of the first phase grant, 60% of the second phase grant is disbursed. The remaining 20% is shared.

As per the proposal of the Directorate of Industries dated 25/03/2023, the State Level Steering Committee for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Bamboo Cluster, Surgana G. As per the project approval, the certificate of successful completion of the Common Facility Center as well as the utilization certificate of the subsidy distributed by the State Government has been submitted by the Joint Director of Industries, Nashik through the Directorate of Industries. Accordingly, the final grant of Rs. 15,36,160/- from the State Government's share for the construction of Common Facility Centre of the project was under consideration.
Government Decision: The Directorate of Industries has submitted a proposal for the final disbursement of the grant of the State Government for the construction of a common facility center of the Bamboo Cluster, Surgana G. Nashik, approved under the scheme" Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Program (MSI-CDP)" of the State Government. 4 is presented here. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the present scheme, the final grant of the State Government amounting to Rs.15,36,160/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs Thirty-six Thousand One Hundred and Sixty only) is being approved under this Government decision.
