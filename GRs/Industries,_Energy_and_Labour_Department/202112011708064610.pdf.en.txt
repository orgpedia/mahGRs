It is the policy of the Government to try the eligibility of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers in Government service beyond the age of 50/55 years or on completion of 30 years of qualifying service as per the prescribed criteria in accordance with Rule 10 (4) and 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982. In accordance with the above reference No. 1 The consolidated procedure is detailed in accordance with the government decision of the General Administration Department mentioned here. As per the provisions of the said Government Resolution, the Government have decided to review and recommend the eligibility of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers, who are eligible for review on establishment of Labour Commissioner under Industries, Power and Labour Department, to continue their service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after 30 years of qualifying service. 2 It was under the consideration of the Government to revise the composition of the Review Committee constituted as per the decision of the Government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The above Reference No. 1 is mentioned here. As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 10.06.2019, the establishment of Labour Commissioners under the Department of Industries, Power and Labour will be eligible for review in Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted).
