PREAMBLE
Hon.ble Deputy Chief Minister of Maharashtra officially visited Mauritius from 28-29 April, 2023. During visit the statue of Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was unveiled in Mauritius by Dy.
Chief Minister in presence of Prime minister of Mauritius Shri. Pravind Kumar
Jugnath.Memorandum of understanding with Mauritius on the subject of Tourism/Culture, Economic, Trade & Industry Co-orporation, Logistics & Stay was also signed by Government of Maharshtra during the visit. The future freindly relations between Mauritius & Maharashtra State in Trade, Tourism & Industry will increase by the said visit.
2. On this background Government of Maharashtra has nominated 4 members State level delegation led by Shri. Devendra Fadnavis, Hon. Dy.Chief Minister for official visit 28-29 April, 2023 at Mauritius.
RESOLUTION:
Government of Maharashtra is pleased to accord ex-post-facto approval to the visit of the State level delegation led by Hon'ble Dy. Chief Minister, Government of Maharashtra to official visit at Mauritius for the period mentioned in table below.

During the visit 4 members of delegation attended the official programmes from 28th to 29th
April, 2023 at Mauritius organized by Republic of Mauritius.
2. The Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi vide their letter
dt. 24/04/2023 has approved the visit of delegation The Ministry of Finance, Department of Economic Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi vide their letter dated 19/05/2023 have also approved their clearance to said Mauritius tour.
3. All the members of the official delegation was entitled for travel expenses and per diem
allowance as per prescribed norms of Government of India.
4. State Government is further pleased to authorize the absence of Shri Saurabh Vijay, Principal Secretary, Tourism & Cultural Affair's Department, 28th to 29th April, 2023 (Excluding
