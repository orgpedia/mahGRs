Introduction: - There is an increasing number of educated youth in the urban and rural areas of the state and new opportunities for self-employment and employment are being created in various sectors in the state in the field of industry, business. Considering these new opportunities for self-employment and employment, a comprehensive scheme 'Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme' (CMEGP), which promotes entrepreneurship and promotes creativity in a timely manner, has been implemented under the Government Decision dated 01/08/2019. Accordingly, State Level and District Level Control Committees have been constituted to provide various self-employment opportunities to the urban and rural youth of the state and for effective implementation of the scheme.
The State Industrial Policy-2019 states that to make the youth of the state self-sufficient / self-reliant under the scheme, more support from the State Government will be provided in the form of grants from the budgetary provision of the State Government for setting up self-employment projects. Accordingly, a new Head of Accounts has been opened under the scheme for General Category and Scheduled Tribe category vide Government Decision dated 23/01/2020. Also, provision is made by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance for the Scheduled Castes category as per the scale of the grievances.

With a view to facilitate smooth implementation of the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme, all processes from loan proposal to subsidy disbursement are being done completely online through the District Industries Centre under the Directorate of Industries under the Department of Industries and the District Khadi and Village Industries Office under the Khadi and Village Industries Board. For this purpose, a special IT and Coordination Cell has been set up under the Directorate of Industries in collaboration with Maharashtra Entrepreneurship Development Centre under the Directorate of Industries. The cell undertakes the creation, maintenance and repair of the programme portal, database and MIS presentation, etc. The cost of the room is met from the provision made for administrative purposes under the provision available under the scheme. Accordingly, a letter dated 38.00 dated 29/03/2025 has been requested by the Directorate of Industries to make available for distribution a total amount of ₹1. 5 lakh towards administrative expenses of the Special IT and Coordination Cell (CMEGP Cell) set up by the Maharashtra Entrepreneurship Development Centre (MCED) at Mumbai and Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar for the current financial year and ₹1. 5 lakh towards stationary expenses for the Special IT and Coordination Cell (CMEGP Cell) set up by the Maharashtra Entrepreneurship Development Centre (MCED) at Mumbai and Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar. Accordingly, the Government was considering sanctioning expenditure of Rs. 38.00 lakh from the target provision of (General) Office Expenditure (13) under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme Scheme for this additional year 2024-25.
