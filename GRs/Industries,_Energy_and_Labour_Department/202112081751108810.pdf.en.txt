Government Decision: Department of Industrial Energy and Labour, Government Decision No:- Seniority-2020/ PR No.34/ Work-8, dated 21.01.2021, the final service seniority list of officers of the post of Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Group-A was published on 01.01.2020. However, since there has been no promotion from the cadre of Assistant Labour Commissioner to Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Group-A during the period from 01.01.2020 to 31.12.2020, the final service merit list dated 01.01.2020 is being published as final service merit list dated 01.01.2021 without publishing the provisional service merit list dated 01.01.2021 in the cadre of Deputy Commissioner of Labour. The base date for publishing the service seniority list is 1st January every year.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the Government has decided to exclude the names of the officers of the cadre of Deputy Commissioner of Labour who are not working due to retirement, death, promotion, resignation, etc. from the final service merit list for the year 01.01.2020 to 31.12.2019. Deputy Commissioner of Labour Cadre issued vide Notification dated 21.01.2021. The final Service Excellence List dated 01.01.2020 is being published as the final Service Excellence List dated 01.01.2021.
