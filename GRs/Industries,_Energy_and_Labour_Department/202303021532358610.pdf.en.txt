Preamble: In order to support the construction of projects under the Central Government's Micro and Small Enterprises-Collective Development Plan (MSE-CDP) and to enhance the cost-effectiveness of the projects, a scheme titled" Scheme of Subsidy to Industry Cluster Projects for Creation of Common Facilities of the State" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 09/06/2010. Further, as per the Government decision dated 22/10/2010, the State Government's participation under the scheme has been fixed at 10% of the project cost (upper ceiling subsidy limit Rs. 1.50 crore). Under this scheme, the Central Government vide its order dated 31/12/2021 has accorded final approval for the construction of Common Facility Centre at Engineering Cluster, Kupwar G. Sangli, an industrial cluster project, with State Government's participation grant amounting to Rs. 1.50 crore under the project.
As per the guidelines of the Micro, Small Enterprises-College Development Plan (MSE-CDP), the sanctioned grant of the Central Government is disbursed in the ratio of 40:30:30 to the projects sanctioned under the scheme. Also, the share of the state government in the grant sanctioned by the central government should be in proportion to the proposed grant by the central government. However, as per the revised guidelines of MSE-CDP scheme, in case of higher share of State Government participation subsidy, 50% of the Central Government sanctioned grant to the project concerned is disbursed in a single phase.
The Engineering Cluster, Kupwar G. Sangli, an industrial cluster project, will receive an amount of Rs. 800.00 lakh out of the total sanctioned grant of Rs. 1600.00 lakh as per the revised guidelines of the scheme, if the State Government's participation grant is disbursed in higher share.

Therefore, for the speedy completion of the projects in the state, the government was considering to disburse an amount of Rs 1,05,00, 000/- to the Engineering Cluster, Kupwad G. Sangli, Industrial Group Project under the first phase with the participation of the state government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Central Government has accorded final approval for the construction of Common Facility Centre under Micro and Small Enterprises- Group Development Scheme (MSE-CDP Engineering Cluster, Kupwar G. Sangli) under the scheme vide order dated 31/12/2021 at a total approved project cost of Rs. 2020.92 lakh with Central Government grant of Rs. 1600.00 lakh and State Government participation grant of Rs. 150.00 lakh.
Accordingly, the Government have decided to disburse the State Participation Grant of Rs. 1,05,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Five Lakh only) to the Engineering Cluster, Kupwar G. Sangli as per the project sanction, out of the total amount of Rs. 150.00 lakh, in the first phase, as per the provisions of the said scheme.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall arrange to transfer the amount of Rs. 1,05,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Five Lakhs only) from the Government Treasury to the Engineering Cluster, Kupwad G. Sangli, a special purpose vehicle society for the project. The powers to draw and disburse the aforesaid amount of Rs. 1,05,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Five Lakh only) are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall take necessary action to monitor the progress of this special purpose vehicle and the proper utilization and utilization of the Government share capital.
Decision of the Finance Department No. 43/ Earth-3, dated 04/04/2022 and as per the provisions of the Annexure thereto and the information received from the Directorate of Industries accordingly certifies that the project at Engineering Cluster, Kupwar G. Sangli has been accorded final approval by the Central Government vide its order dated 31/12/2021. Government Circular No. SME-2010/ (1017)/ Industry-7, D. The ceiling of subsidy on financial contribution by the State Government has been fixed as on 22/10/2010. Subject to the said limitation, the Directorate of Industries has issued grant sanction orders of the concerned project on 23/02/2023.
Funds are also being allocated for the project. Utilization certificates of more than 75% of funds released earlier under the scheme have been received. There are no objections from the Auditors/ Accountants General in respect of the funds disbursed.
