Appointment of Nodal Officer for Champion Portal at State level.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 16th of September 2020
Reference:- Joint Secretary, Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, New Delhi vide No.D.O.No.5 (3)/ 2020-21/ MSME CHAMPIONS' Desk/ General Letter dated 27/07/2020.
Introduction: Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, New Delhi By the Prime Minister.
On 01/06/2020, a" Champion Portal" has been formally launched for grievance redressal in the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises sector.
The" Champion Portal" is a robust, integrated, robust, integrated and technology driven platform that will not only address the grievances of MSMEs but also provide a fillip to the MSME sector in the country.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to appoint a" nodal officer" at the state level on the" Champion Portal" to resolve the grievances of the micro, small and medium enterprises sector in the state.
Government's decision:
The Managing Director, Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation, Mumbai is being appointed as the" Nodal Officer" at the state level for uploading the schemes of the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises sector on the" Champion Portal" and resolving the issues related to the complaint and start-up schemes of the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises sector.
The Managing Director, Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation, Mumbai shall coordinate with all the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises sectors in the state as per the instructions of the Central Government and take necessary action and give a progress report to the Government every 3 months on the action taken.
