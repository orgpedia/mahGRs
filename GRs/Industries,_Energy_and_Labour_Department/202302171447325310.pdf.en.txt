2.2 The functions of the High Level Committee shall be as follows:
To take policy decisions in coordination with various departments of the Government to address the problems faced by migrant workers and their families due to migration.
(ii) To examine, control and guide the work of the District Level Migration Committee at the level of the Collector.
3) To lay emphasis on the completion of the tasks/ tasks assigned to the district-wise Migration Committees and to control the progress of the work related to them.
4) Removing errors in the Maha MTS (Migration Tracking System) application and giving instructions for the improvement of this application.
2.3 The Committee shall meet at least once in three months.
