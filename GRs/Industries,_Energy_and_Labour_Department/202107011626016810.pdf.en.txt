Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to be appointed to posts in the government service. Accordingly, the Central Government vide reference No. 4 is here. The list of posts reserved for Persons with Disabilities as per notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published. Accordingly, the Central Government has decided to ensure the posts for the disabled in the posts of Group-A to Group-D which are in the state government establishment, the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, although the designations of such posts are different. 5 decisions have been made. As per the provisions of this Government decision, out of 23 posts in the Electricity Inspectorate, 13 posts are reserved for the disabled. 6 is confirmed. The remaining posts include (1) Chief Electrical Inspector, (2) Superintending Engineer, (3) Electrical Inspector, (4) Assistant Electrical Inspector, (5) Assistant Engineer Grade-II (6) Branch Engineer/ Junior Engineer

(7) Jeep Driver and (8) Chowkidar are the posts of Central Government. As there is no mention in the notification dated 04/01/2021, the proposal in this regard was placed for consideration in the meeting of the Expert Committee headed by Principal Secretary, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department dated 22/02/2021. As per the recommendations of the expert committee, the matter of ensuring these posts in the Electricity Inspectorate for the disabled was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 2 here. The total number of 13 posts in the list of posts from Group-A to Group-D, which are in the establishment of the Electricity Inspectorate, as per the notification dated 04.01.2021, and whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the establishment of the Electricity Inspectorate, are mentioned in Reference No. 6 are provided for the disabled. The remaining Naiks are being ensured for Divyang as indicated in the prospectus-A attached with the post.
2. An Expert Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department. As per the minutes of the meeting dated 22/02/2021, out of the remaining 8 posts in the Electricity Inspectorate, two posts of (1) Jeep Driver and (2) Chowkidar are being excluded from the reservation for Divyang category as per the recommendation of the Committee and the remaining 6 posts of (1) Chief Electrical Inspector, (2) Superintending Engineer, (3) Electrical Inspector, (4) Assistant Electrical Inspector, (5) Assistant Engineer Grade-II and (6) Branch Engineer/ Junior Engineer are being reserved for Divyang as mentioned in the Return-B.
(3) This Government Resolution, issued by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi. Notification dated 04.01.2021 of the Department of Social Justice& Empowerment, Govt. being issued as per the instructions issued vide Government Decision dated 02.02.2021.
