Read: 1. Director, Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health, Mumbai vide letter No. COMMISSION/ ACCIDENT/ COMMITTEE/ 9990/4 dated 05.09.2019.
The proposal: A massive explosion took place in the factory of Rumit ChemSynth Pvt. Ltd., Group No. 27/6& 7, Balde Road, Subhash Nagar, Shirpur, Dhule on 31.08.2019 during chemical processing. At least 14 people, including eight workers and six outsiders, were killed and 85 others injured in the incident. The building has suffered extensive damage. A part of the factory building was also damaged in the explosion. Similarly, huts outside the compound wall of the factory were also damaged, killing some people and injuring others. The extent of the accident is large and the Government was considering the proposal of the Director, Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health, Mumbai dated 05.09.2019 to constitute an Inquiry Committee to investigate the cause of the accident. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: A massive explosion took place in the factory of Rumit ChemSynth Pvt. Ltd., Group No. 27/6& 7, Balde Road, Subhash Nagar, Shirpur, Dhule on 31.08.2019 during chemical processing. A thorough investigation is required to ascertain the cause of the incident. In order to conduct a thorough inquiry into the accident and to prevent recurrence of such incidents in future, the following Committee of Government officials and experts is being constituted under the powers conferred on the Government under the provisions of Section 90 of the Factories Act, 1948.
