The Proposal:
ANK Pharma Pvt. Ltd., Plot No. M2, Boisar, G. The plant at Palghar started trial production of Orthochlorobenzoic Acid at around 07.00 pm on 11.01.2020. For this, the chemical orthochloro-tolvin is mixed with nitric acid to produce nitration. The process was extremely hot and the pressure inside the reactor was high enough to cause an explosion. Eight people, including three workers and five others, were killed and seven others, including the factory's director, were injured. The building has suffered extensive damage.
A part of the factory building was also damaged in the explosion. The extent of the accident is huge and the government was considering the formation of an investigation committee to find out the cause of the accident. A decision in this regard is being taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: MANK Pharma Pvt. Ltd., Plot No. A massive explosion took place in a factory at M2, Boisar, G. Palghar at around 7.00 pm on 11.01.2020 while chemical processing was going on. A thorough investigation is required to ascertain the cause of the incident. In order to conduct a thorough inquiry into the accident and to prevent recurrence of such incidents in future, the following Committee is being constituted under the powers conferred on the Government under the provisions of Section 90 of the Factories Act, 1948.
