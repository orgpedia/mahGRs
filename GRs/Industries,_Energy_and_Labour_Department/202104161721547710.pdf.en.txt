Preamble: As a temporary measure to meet the urgent committed expenditure for the month of April, the Finance Department has released funds to the Administrative Departments in a limited manner for a total of 8 purposes viz., 01- Salary, 02- Wages, 04- Pension, 06- Telephone, Electricity and Water charges, 10- Contractual Services, 13- Office expenses, 14- Lease and Taxes and 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries).
Under the Government Circular issued in the context of the Finance Department, it was under consideration to disburse funds under the authorized head to the administrative departments on the basis of the Budget Estimates 2021-22 (White Book) book as approved by the Finance Department. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the circular of the Government in the context of Finance Department, under this Government Decision, limited funds are being allocated to the Budget Estimate Allocation and Monitoring System (BEAMS) for the following objectives as a temporary measure for the urgent committed expenditure for the month of April as follows:
