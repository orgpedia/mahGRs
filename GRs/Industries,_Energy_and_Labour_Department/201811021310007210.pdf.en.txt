The Proposal:
According to the state's industrial policy, An Integrated Facilitation Cell to assist investors who are willing to invest in the State has been set up at Maharashtra Industry Trade and Investment Facilitation Centre (Maitri Cell), Mumbai from 27/02/2014. The Directorate of Industries has proposed to reimburse the amount due to the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation for the expenses incurred on professional services for the year 2017-18. submitted vide letter dated 15.02.2018. Reference No. 1. 4.7.2013 and No. 2 here. In pursuance of the reimbursement of the amount of expenses for professional services rendered by the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation as per Government Decision dated 11.02.2016, vide reference No. 7 The Government has decided to provide Rs. 3,19,67, 772/- was approved to be paid to Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation. However, due to some technical glitches, the payment was returned by the Subordinate& Accounts Office and the expenditure could not be incurred during the financial year 2017-18. Now, the Directorate of Industries has decided to reimburse the said expenditure as per reference No. 8 crore to the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation. A proposal has been submitted to the Government for approval for payment of Rs. 2,03,56, 714/-. Accordingly, the matter of approving the expenditure was under the consideration of the government. The government is taking the following steps:
