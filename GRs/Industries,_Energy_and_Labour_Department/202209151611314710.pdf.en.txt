Introduction: The above reference No. In pursuance of the instructions received from the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII), the Labour Commissioner, Mumbai has proposed to make necessary amendments in the Maharashtra Shops and Establishments (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Rules, 2018, in terms of submission of online applications/ undertaking mentioned in Rule 13 (2) (7), Rule 33 and Rule 34.
In view of the substantive provisions contained in Rule 13 (2) (viii), Rule 33 and Rule 34 of the Maharashtra Shops and Establishments (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Rules, 2018, the said provisions do not specifically provide for submission of application/ undertaking through online or offline mode. That is, the contractor/ owner can submit the said application/ guarantee in both ways.
However, in case the facility is not available for submission of application/ undertaking online, the same is required to be made available on the website. For this, it is necessary to make changes to the computer system. Following this, the government took decision:
Government Decision: In view of the suggestions made by the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) regarding making necessary provisions regarding online submission of applications/ undertaking mentioned in Rule 13 (2) (vii), Rule 33 and Rule 34 of the Maharashtra Shops and Establishments (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Rules, 2018, the Government is approving to make the facility available online and make necessary corrections in the relevant computer system accordingly. In accordance with the approval of the said Government, the Labour Commissioner, Mumbai shall take immediate action to make necessary changes in the computer system from his level.
