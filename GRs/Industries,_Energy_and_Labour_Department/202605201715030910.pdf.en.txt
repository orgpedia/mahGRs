Government Decision: - Managing Director, Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation is being appointed as the Nodal Officer for smooth functioning, management and maintenance of PM Ekta Mall.
The Managing Director, Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation is being authorised to accept the transfer of PM Ekta Mall from Cidco Corporation to the Industries Department in the prescribed manner. The Managing Director, Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation should take necessary action for power connection and other ancillary matters for PM Ekta Mall.
The following Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Development Commissioner (Industries) to work out the modalities for operation, management and maintenance of PM Ekta Mall.
