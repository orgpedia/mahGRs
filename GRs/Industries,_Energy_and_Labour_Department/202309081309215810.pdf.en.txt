Preamble: Out of the budgetary provision for training programmes, market development and marketing under Coir Industry Policy-2018 for the financial year 2022-23, Rs. 1,47,00, 000/- was approved for disbursement. Orders were also issued by the Directorate of Industries for the distribution of funds. The amount submitted as per the said Government decision was not recorded in the BDS system due to a technical glitch. Hence, the funds could not be released. Since the funds have been repatriated, the Government was considering to grant administrative approval for disbursement of subsidy amounting to Rs. 70,54,500/- (Rupees Seventy Lakh, Fifty Four Thousand, Five Hundred only) out of the budgetary provision for the purpose of" 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried)" in the year 2023-24 to meet the expenditure incurred in the year 2022-23 for training programmes, market development and marketing under the Coir Industry Policy-2018.
Government Decision: In the limited context of Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation Ltd. As proposed vide letter dated 23/05/2023, this Government has decided to disburse a grant of Rs. 70,54,500/- (Rupees Seventy Lakhs, Fifty Four Thousand, Five Hundred only) from the budgetary provision for the purpose of" 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment)" in the year 2023-24 to meet the expenditure incurred in the year 2022-23 for training programmes, market development and marketing under the Coir Industry Policy-2018. While making these expenditures, the conditions for the distribution of grants by the Finance and Planning Departments should be followed.
