Government Decision: Proposals received by the Directorate General of Industries under the Scheme for Creation and Development of Industrial Infrastructure (4875 0025), Rs. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. It was placed for consideration in the 8th meeting of the Steering Committee held on 24/02/2022. After scrutinizing the proposals submitted to the Committee, the Directorate of Industries shall submit the proposals for administrative approval of the approved proposals to the Government immediately, and thereafter, the administrative approval order/ technical approval/ fund disbursement order from the implementing agency will be proposed by the Department of Industries as per the prevailing procedure, based on the cost of project approval approved by the Steering Committee. However, considering the fact that the financial year will end in the meantime, the committee has unanimously decided that the budgetary allocation for the current financial year 2021-22 should be distributed and handed over to the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation and paid to the implementing agencies by following the prescribed procedure. The minutes of the meeting No. 4 is here. Issued vide letter dated 28/02/2022.
A sum of Rs 30 crore has been allocated for the scheme in 2021-22. In the 8th meeting of the Steering Committee, the Committee has approved the disbursement of Government share of Rs. 16,06,58, 547/- to the Institutions for the works proposed by the total 10 Institutions. As per the decision taken by the Committee, a proposal was submitted to the Planning Department and Finance Department for making available on BDS system for disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 16,06,58, 547/- out of the budgeted funds under the scheme for the financial year 2021-22. The proposal has been approved by the Planning and Finance Ministry. In view of this, Ma. The committee is headed by the Chief Secretary. As per the decision taken in the 8th meeting held on 24/02/2022, the Government is approving to hand over the amount of Rs. 16,06,58, 547/- (Rupees Sixteen Crores Six Lakhs Eighty-Four Thousand Five Hundred and Forty-Seven only) budgeted for the financial year 2021-22 for the scheme of Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development (4875 0025) to the Office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
