Preamble: In order to effectively implement the Electricity Act, 2003 and the rules and regulations made thereunder, the functioning of the Electricity Inspectorate Branch functioning under the administrative control of the Public Works Department is referred to in Reference No. 3 It has been brought under the authority of the Department of Energy by a government decision. As the Electricity Inspectorate Branch has been transferred to the Energy Department, as per the decision of the Government, the option of inclusion of the officers/ employees working in both the departments as per their choice has been provided in the Reference No. 1 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor. 5 and Public Works Department No. 6 The decision has been approved by the government.
The consolidated seniority list of Assistant Engineer Grade-II cadre as on 01.01.2015 was published by the Chief Engineer (Electrical), PWD on 08.06.2015. Now that the Electricity Inspectorate Branch has been transferred to the Department of Power as mentioned in the above paragraph, the officers/ employees of the Electricity Branch of the Public Works Department and the Electricity Inspectorate Branch of the Department of Power have been transferred to that Department on the basis of their service willingness. Therefore, considering the change in the number of cadres, the government was considering to publish a separate ad-hoc provisional seniority list of the assistant engineer category-2 cadre working under the Electricity Inspectorate branch.
Circular: Assistant Engineer Grade-II Engineers working under the Electricity Inspectorate Branch under the Department of Energy. The ad-hoc provisional seniority list as on 01.01.2018 is being published as per Annexure-A annexed.

The Chief Electrical Inspector shall inform all the heads of departments/ offices under his command about the receipt of the copies of the ad-hoc seniority list from the website and the Assistant Engineer under him shall make the copies of the ad-hoc seniority list available to all the engineers of the Grade-2 cadre from the website.

If there are any errors, errors, shortcomings in the seniority list, the concerned should inform the government in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular, along with the proof of birth certificate, copy of caste certificate, copy of record in the service book, etc., through the head of the office concerned and the Chief Electrical Inspector.
