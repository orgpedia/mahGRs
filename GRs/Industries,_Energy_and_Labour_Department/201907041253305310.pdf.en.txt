The Proposal:
Construction workers registered with the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board in rural areas (active) will be eligible to apply for construction of a new house on their own land in rural areas or to convert an existing kutcha house into a pucca house under the Atal Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana (Gramin) and will be eligible for Rs. 1,50,000/- has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 14th January, 2019. Further, Rs. The sanction of financial assistance of Rs.50,000/- has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 07.02.2019.
The condition that the construction worker should be registered with the Board for more than one consecutive year for availing the benefit of this scheme has been made under the Government decision dated 14th January, 2019. To take advantage of the said scheme, the government was considering to fix the date of registration of construction workers by changing this condition.
Government's decision:
Construction workers registered with the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board on and before May 31, 2019, can apply for the benefit of Atal Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana (Gramin).
