the prelude
Under the chairmanship of the Minister (Labour). On 30.1.2019, Shri Radhakrishna Vikhe Patil, Hon. A meeting was held in the presence of the Leader of Opposition, Maharashtra Legislative Assembly to resolve the problems of workers in the film, television and serial sector. The meeting urged the government to form a tripartite committee comprising government representatives, management representatives and labour representatives to discuss the problems of workers in the film, television and serial sectors. A proposal to form a three-member committee was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A tripartite committee is being constituted to study in detail the problems of workers in the film, television and serial sector, labour and welfare laws applicable to them, their working style, facilities available to them, their registration and other issues and submit a report to the government. :
