Government Decision: Government Decision, Industries, Energy and Labour Department No. MLI-1094/ CR-7557/ Workers-6, Dt. As per the order dated 01/07/1996, The Departmental Promotion Committee has been constituted to prepare and revise the selection list for promotion to the posts of Class-I and Class-II gazetted in the establishment of Narayan Meghaji Lokhande Maharashtra Institute of Labour Sciences, Mumbai. The government was contemplating to reconstitute the committee. In this regard, the Government is reconstituting the Committee as mentioned below.
Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Labour), Industries, Energy and Labour Department Chairman 2) Director, KNAM May Lokhande Maharashtra Labour Science Institute, Mumbai Member 3) Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Labour), Industries, Energy and Labour Department, Member 4) Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary/ Under Secretary (Backward Classes), Industries, Energy and Labour Department or Ministerial Administrative Department Member 5) Manager, CAI. N. M. Lokhande Member Secretary of Maharashtra Labour Science Institute, Mumbai
(2) The Departmental Promotion Committee to be set up as aforesaid shall, after taking into consideration the guidelines prescribed for promotion by the Government from time to time, recommend to the Government, the following: The selection list for promotion to Class-1 and Class-2 gazetted posts in Narayan Meghaji Lokhande Maharashtra Labour Science Institute should be prepared and revised.
