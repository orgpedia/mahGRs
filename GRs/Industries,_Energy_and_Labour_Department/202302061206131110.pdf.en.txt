A committee has been formed to prepare a circular economy policy for Maharashtra.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Resolution No: Policy-2023/ No.2/ Industry-2
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 6, 2023
Introduction: The Central Government has given instructions to make a circular economy policy by recycling unnecessary vehicles and other unused goods or tools in the state.
A new strategy needs to be developed. The state government had formed a committee to study the recommendations of the central government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The following Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Development Commissioner (Industries) to make recommendations to the State Government for formulation of circular economy policy.

The committee will have the following functions:
The committee shall prepare a report on the formulation of the state policy after consulting the experts in this field and studying the policies made by other states in this regard, according to the instructions of the central government.
The committee will submit its report to the government within three months.
