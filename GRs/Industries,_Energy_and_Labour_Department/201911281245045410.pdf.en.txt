The Proposal:
The Government of India has implemented two schemes namely Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana (PM-SYM) for unorganized workers and National Pension Scheme (NPS) for small traders. to discuss various aspects of the project. On 19/11/2019, a video conference was held at 11.00 am.
In this TV conference, the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India has directed to immediately form a State Level Controlling Committee and District Level Implementation Committee for two schemes- Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana (PM-SYM) for unorganized workers and National Pension Scheme (NPS-Traders) for small traders. Considering the above directions, the Government was contemplating to constitute State Level Controlling Committee and District Level Implementation Committee for two schemes namely Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana (PM-SYM) and National Pension Scheme for Small Traders (NPS-Traders).
Government's decision:
The composition and functions of the State Level Monitoring Committee for successful implementation of the two schemes namely Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana (PM-SYM) for unorganized workers and National Pension Scheme (NPS) for small traders, such as registration of beneficiaries, publicity of schemes, etc., shall be as follows:

The functions of the State Committee shall be as follows:
To monitor and monitor the progress of online registration of beneficiaries of Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana (PM-SYM) and National Pension Scheme (NPS Traders) at the level of Collector/ District Magistrate.
To control the functioning of the regional level Citizen Facilitation Centres and e-Jan Suvidha Kendras and strive to achieve the targets of Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana (PM-SYM) and National Pension Scheme (NPS-Traders). Focus on meeting the targets of groups of unorganized workers such as MGNREGA Mazdoor Self Help Groups (National Urban Livelihoods Mission and National Rural Livelihoods Mission), Domestic Workers, Brick Kiln Workers, ASHA Workers, Anganwadi Workers, Street Vendors, Rickshaw Drivers, Farm Workers, MGNREGA Workers, Fishermen and other workers, Building and other Construction Workers, Mid Day Meal Workers etc.
Enrolment of beneficiaries of Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana/ Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana in Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana.
4. Control over meeting the target of enrolment of large groups of shopkeepers and small traders in the National Pension Scheme for Small Traders.
(v) To make efforts to enrol members of trade unions/ associations/ civil society organizations working for the welfare of unorganized workers and small traders in the National Pension Scheme.
6. Regular functioning of small and large registration workshops for unorganized workers and small businesses at district level and urban facilitation centres.
To provide information, education and communication (IEC) to the beneficiaries of Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan Yojana (PM-SYM) and National Pension Scheme (NPS-Traders) and to prescribe the modalities for implementation of the scheme through the Labour Commissioner and other government agencies.
Implementation and monitoring of State-wide awareness programmes through seminars, seminars, public meetings, participation of students, universities, colleges, channels, newspapers, workshops, pamphlets, posters, banners, District Labour Officers/ District Industries Centre Help Desks/ Skill Development Centres/ other district level officers.
9. Redressal of grievances of the public coming to this committee.
The Committee shall meet once in three months.
