The Proposal:
The head accounts of Industries, Power and Labour Department (Power Division) are headings 2801 Electricity 2810 New and Renewable Energy 4801 Capital Expenditure on Power Projects. Office of the Principal Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and Finance Department.
In the Entry and Exit Conference held on 23.08.2017, it was suggested to indicate the appropriate sub-heading instead of the sub-heading of 800 other expenditure for the schemes shown under the above headings of this department. The matter of showing the appropriate subtitle instead of this subtitle of 800 other expenses was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Demand No. K-6 Main Account Head 2801 Electricity, 2810 New and Renewable Energy, Demand No. The Government is pleased to approve the following sub-headings for the following schemes to be shown under the head Capital Expenditure on K-11, 4801 Power Projects instead of 800 other headings.
