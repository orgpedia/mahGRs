The memo:
In order to promote the grape-based wine industry in the state, the government has issued the Grape Processing Industry Policy-2001. In pursuance of the scheme introduced on 19/10/2001, the Government plans to provide a wine incentive subsidy to the wine industry equivalent to 16% of the value added tax payable on the sale of wines produced and finally sold in the State, as an incentive to the industry. The pending claims for reimbursement of incentives with the Government have been cleared in a phased manner as the financial provision under the scheme is inadequate.
