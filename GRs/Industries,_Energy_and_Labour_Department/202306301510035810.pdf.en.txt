It may be noted that in case of non-admission to the post as per the said order, or any attempt to transfer and change the post under any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.

Further, the non-compliance of the order passed with the approval of the competent authority under the Transfer Act by the officers transferred as mentioned in the above table is serious in nature and the non-compliant officer shall be liable to be proceeded against under Section 2.1 (2) (v) of Section 2 of the Departmental Inquiry Manual, 1991.
