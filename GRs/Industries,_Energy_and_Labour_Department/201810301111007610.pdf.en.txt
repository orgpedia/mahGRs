The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Government of India, a State Government Biofuel Advisory Committee has been constituted to formulate a State Government Biofuel Policy in line with the National Biofuel Policy announced by the Government of India in June, 2018. The committee consists of two non-government experts in the field. Accordingly, the matter of appointing a non-government expert was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Smt. Shobhatai Madhavrao Fadnavis is being appointed as a non-official member in the Biofuel Advisory Committee constituted by the State Government under the decision of the Government in question.
