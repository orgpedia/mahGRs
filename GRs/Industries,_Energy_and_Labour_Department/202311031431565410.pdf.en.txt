5. The concerned officers should take charge of the new post with minimum time and submit their report to the government without any delay. If the officer does not join the post within the stipulated period of 30 days from the date of this order, the period of his absence shall be deemed to be non-working day. Also, their attention is being drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of the said Rules, they will be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any application for change in the post.
The Chief Inspector of Electricity, Mumbai is hereby informed that the date of assumption of charge by the said officers shall be communicated to the Government immediately on the e-mail address prdip. bhintade@nic.in. If they fail to join within the stipulated period, the same should be immediately communicated to the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken immediately against the concerned.
The concerned officers should give the option of salary fixation within one month from the date of taking up the post of promotion. In case such option is not given within the prescribed time, their salary will be determined as per Rule No. 1 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981. According to 11 (1) (a), the date of promotion should be taken into account.
