Grant-in-Aid to Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation for the year 2017-18.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Resolution No.Lavim-2017/ P.S. 157/ Industries-6
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date- 21/03/2018
Government Decision: A sum of Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore only) has been budgeted for the financial year 2017-2018 under the head Handicrafts Industry Development with a target of 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried). Out of which, the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour (Administration 1 Office) vide Circular dated 5/7/2017 has sanctioned 70% of the cost of Rs. 70 lakh has been handed over to the control officers. The Government have approved the release of grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 70,00,000/- (Rupees Seventy Lakhs only) to meet the expenditure for the financial year 2017-18 as per the Government Resolution dated 16/11/2017.
Now, as per the Government Circular Finance Division dated 6/2/2018, the limit of spending of budgeted funds on all other items excluding the mandatory expenditure items in the revenue account has now been prescribed as 80%. Accordingly, the Maharashtra State Small Industries Development Corporation has been allocated Rs. 1 crore, the remaining 10 per cent (Rs. 10 lakhs) has been sanctioned. Expenditure on Demand No. K-7, 2851 Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, (00) 104 Handicrafts Industries, (00) Scheme under Five Year Plans State Plan (00) (02) Development of Handicrafts Industry (28510567) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) The grants sanctioned for the year 2017-18 under the head' Grants-in-Aid' shall be shared and spent under the same head.
The powers to draw and distribute the said amount are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai and the Corporation shall submit the utilization certificate of the expenditure incurred for the said purpose to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The cost of the account should be reported to the office of the Accountant General (Audit/ Accounts and Licensure), Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and the Accountant General (Audit/ Accounts and Licensure), Maharashtra-2, Nagpur within the stipulated time.
The Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai, shall furnish the figures of the monthly funds spent on the above purpose to the Office of the Commissioner (Administration).
