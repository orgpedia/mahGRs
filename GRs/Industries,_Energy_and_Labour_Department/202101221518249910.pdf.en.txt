The Proposal:
Thane district has been divided. With effect from 01.08.2014, Palghar is a new district comprising of the following taluks: Palghar, Vasai, Dahanu, Talasari, Jawhar, Wada, Mokhada and Vikramgad 2 has been created by notification here. Regarding the establishment of the office of the Inspector of Electricity, Inspection Division, Palghar, of the Electricity Inspectorate under the Department of Energy for this newly created Palghar district and the reference no. 1 Chief Electrical Inspector, Mumbai and Electrical Inspector, Inspection Division No. The proposal to transfer the posts in the office of 1, Thane was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Approval is hereby accorded for the establishment of the office of the Inspector of Electricity, Inspection Division, Palghar of the Electricity Inspectorate under the Department of Power for the newly created Palghar district. Also, 8 Talukas Palghar, Vasai, Dahanu, Talasari, Jawhar, Wada, Mokhada and Vikramgad have been included in the district. 1, Thane is being transferred to the newly created office of the Inspector of Electricity, Inspection Department, Palghar.
Out of the two sanctioned posts of Electrical Inspector in the office of the Chief Electrical Inspector, Mumbai, 1 post mentioned in the following statement-A and the Electrical Inspector, Inspection Division No. A total of 13 posts out of the 12 posts mentioned in the following statement-B in the office of 1, Thane are being permanently transferred to the office of the Inspector of Electricity, Inspection Department, Palghar in the newly created Palghar district.
