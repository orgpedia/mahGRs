The Proposal:
The Planning Department has approved the implementation of a pilot project on resource-based intensive planning and development in Chandrapur and Sindhudurg districts. Under this scheme, the district for the implementation of micro industries, their by-products and honey industry project for Sindhudurg district.
The matter of appointing a level coordinating agency was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the pilot project of Resource-Based Intensive Planning and Development for Sindhudurg and Chandrapur districts, projects for the development of micro-enterprises, their by-products and honey industry will be implemented.
The Maharashtra government has decided to declare the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board (MSKVB) as a district-level coordinating agency.
Since the BDS system is not working with the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, the funds due under this scheme should be paid to the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board by cheque.
This decision was taken by the Planning Department in its informal reference No. Issued pursuant to the approval given under Rule 1418, dated 17/3/2018.
