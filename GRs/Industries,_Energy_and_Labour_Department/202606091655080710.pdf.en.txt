Preamble: - Decided to partially revise the State's New IT & ITeS Policy-2023 to allow incentives for setting up of three (3) Green Integrated Green Data Centre Parks in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai Region. The governance of the 09.10.2024 has been carried out by decision. The policy prescribes 3 green integrated data centre parks to be set up in the Mumbai and Navi Mumbai regions with a capacity of 500 megawatts (MW) and an investment of at least ₹30,000 crore.
Developed by setting up three (3) green integrated green data centre parks in Mumbai B Navi Mumbai Region, Maharashtra-2047 will not achieve the target of developing 30 to 40 GW of green energy-based data centres by 2047 as envisaged by the initiative. Therefore, it is necessary to increase the number of integrated green data center parks. Also, considering the investment and employment generation through integrated green data centre parks in the state and the difficulties in setting up integrated data centre parks to be set up in the state, Dr. The Cabinet Sub-Committee decided to amend the Government Decision of the 09.10.2024 and grant special case incentives. The d. Conducted in meetings concluded on 22.04.2026. Accordingly. The issue of amending the governance decision of the 009.10.2024 was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: - The state government intends to develop Mumbai and Navi Mumbai region as data centre hubs and considering the difficulties faced in setting up integrated data centre parks to be set up in the state with an aim to make Mumbai the "Data Centre Capital of India," the state government has decided to set up data centre hubs in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai. To make the following amendments to the Government Decision on 09.10.2024 and to grant special incentives in respect of the case. It was approved by the Cabinet Sub-Committee.
