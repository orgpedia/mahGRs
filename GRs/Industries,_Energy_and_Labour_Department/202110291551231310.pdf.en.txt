Preamble: In order to consider the demands of the sugar factory workers in the State of Maharashtra, a tripartite committee has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 12.11.2020 in the aforesaid Reference No. Reference No. Under the decision of the Government of Maharashtra, six months have also been extended to submit recommendations on the demands of sugar workers in the state.
In the meetings of this committee, the representatives of the workers and the factory representatives had a thorough discussion and gave information about the demands of the workers in the sugar factories of the state. The MoU was signed on 04.10.2021. Accordingly, the report in the form of an agreement signed by most of the members has been submitted to the government.
After considering the recommendations of the above report, the matter of government's approval to the report in the form of an agreement was under consideration.
The tripartite committee has recommended that it will be mandatory for all the factories in the sugar and by-products industry in the state of Maharashtra to implement the terms of service of this agreement on a cooperative, private, lease and partnership basis. In this regard, a tripartite committee at the state level with the concerned employers' representatives and workers' representatives
Agreement dated 04/10/2021 has been entered into pursuant to the recommendation.

In view of the above, this Government decision provides for immediate implementation of the Agreement in accordance with all the recommendations made by the Tripartite Committee in its report submitted to the Government. The terms and conditions of this Agreement are set forth in the attached Annexure.
The period of implementation of the Agreement as recommended by the Tripartite Committee shall be from 01/04/2019 to 31/03/2024. Both parties are obligated to terminate the agreement and to continue to enforce the terms and conditions of the agreement until a new agreement is reached.
In pursuance of the recommendations of the said Tripartite Committee, the Commissioner of Sugar, Maharashtra State, Pune is requested to issue instructions to all the (Cooperative and Private) Sugar Mills in the State regarding the implementation of the agreement dated 04/10/2021 and submit the compliance report to the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, Ministry of Labour, Government of Maharashtra.
The said Government Decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 07/2021/ KA of the Department of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles. 25-C, dated 29.10.2021 is being issued with concurrence and approval.
