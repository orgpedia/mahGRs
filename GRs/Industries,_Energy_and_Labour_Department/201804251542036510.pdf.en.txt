Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill, 2018 has been passed by the Maharashtra Legislature and the said Act has been published as Act 22 in the Government of Maharashtra Gazette Extraordinary Part IV, dated 27/3/2018.
Further, for the Budget Year 2018-19, 70% of the budgeted subsidy has been approved for expenditure and the same has been handed over to the concerned Controlling Authorities. 1 has been issued by the government.
For the financial year 2018-19, a total of Rs. 5195.07 lakh has been provided in the Budget as 31. Subsidy (Non-Salary) Rs. 278.88 lakh 36. Subsidy (Salary) Rs. 4916.19/-. Out of this provision, Rs.36 crore has been allocated under 70% subsidy limit, Rs. 34,41,33, 300/-31 crore under Subsidiary Subsidy (Salary) Scheme, Rs. 1,95,21, 600/- has been made available to the Board.
The Financial Advisor and Chief Accounts Officer, Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai vide their Reference No. The letter has requested to provide a total of Rs. 865.84 lakh for the two months April and May, 2018 under the target of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 819.36 lakh and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 46.48 lakh. As per the above request of the Board, the Government is approving the release of grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 865.84 crore (Rupees Eight Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Eighty Four Thousand only).
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2018-19 under the targets Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (2851 0585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) and should be spent under the same head.
