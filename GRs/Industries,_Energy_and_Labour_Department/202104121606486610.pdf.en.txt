The Government of Maharashtra

Government Resolution No. IEL-2021/ PR No. 85/ Ministry of Administration-3, Mumbai-400 032.
The date: April 12, 2021.
General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. LPL-2017/ No. 21/ K. 15, dated 10.06.2019.
Preamble: The Group A and Group B Gazetted Officers who have come into Government service before the age of 35 years as per the Government decision dated 10.06.2019 in the context of General Administration Department, shall be re-evaluated only once at the age of 50 years or at the age of 30 years of their qualifying service, whichever is earlier. Also, Gazetted officers who joined the service after the age of 35 should be reviewed at the age of 55. In case of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees, instructions have been issued to review their age at the age of 55 years or on completion of 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
Accordingly, the Department of Industries, Power and Labour (DIPL) was contemplating to constitute a Departmental Review Committee to examine the eligibility of State Government officers/ employees on establishment to continue in service beyond the age of 50/55 years or beyond the qualifying service period of 30 years.
Government Decision: As mentioned in the Preamble above, it is being approved to constitute the Review Committee as follows.
Divisional Review Committee (Group-A to Group-B (Gazetted) Officers) (1) Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary (Labour& Administration) Chairman (2) Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Labour) Member (3) Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Administration-3) Member (4) Under Secretary/ Office Officer (Administration-3) Secretary

Divisional Review Committee (Group-B (Non-Gazetted) to Group-D Officers/ Staff) (9) with Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Labour) as Chairman
(2) Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Administration- 3) Member

(9) The aforesaid Committee shall review the State Government officers/ employees of Group-A to Group-D cadre on Industry, Energy and Labour (itself) establishments. The Under Secretary/ Officiating Officer (Admin-3) in his capacity as Secretary/ Member Secretary of the Committee shall present the matter before the Committee and prepare the records and take further action.
(2) The recommendations of the Group-A officers shall be considered for final decision. be submitted to the Minister.
(3) The recommendations of the Group-B (Gazetted) Officers shall be considered for final decision. be submitted to the Minister of State (Labour).
(8) The recommendations of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) to Group-D officers/ employees shall be submitted to the Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary (Labour and Administration) of Industries, Power and Labour Department for final decision.
(4) In case of disqualification of Group-A to Group-B (Gazetted) officers, such officers shall be reviewed by the Special Review Committee of the General Administration Department.
(6) The recommendations regarding the officers/ employees of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) to Group-D who are eligible/ ineligible should be submitted to the Head of the Department for final decision.
(7) Both the above Committees are to submit recommendations on the eligibility of officers/ employees after taking into consideration the orders issued from time to time by the General Administration Department regarding the review of the continuation of government servants in government service.
Further, to consider the grievances against the decision of premature retirement after review of service, the following Departmental Grievance Committees are being constituted for the employees of Group-A (Gazetted) Grade Pay less than 7600 and Group-B (Gazetted) in the Department and Group-B (Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D in the Ministries/ Departments.
