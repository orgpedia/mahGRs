Government's decision:
Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2018, a total grant of Rs. 1731.68 lakhs (Rs. Seventeen crore thirty one lakh sixty six thousand only) has been provided to Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai for the four months from June to September, 2018 for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 1638.72 lakhs and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 92.96 lakhs.
The Financial Advisor and Chief Accounts Officer, Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai vide their Reference No. In the letters dated 3rd and 4th October, 2018, a total grant of Rs. 865.84 lakh (Rs.8 crore fifty five lakh eighty four thousand only) has been requested for the two months i.e. October to November, 2018 for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 1638.72 lakh, 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 819.36 lakh and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 46.48 lakh. In accordance with the aforesaid request of the Board, a sum of Rs. 865.84 lakhs (Rupees Eight Crores Sixty Five Lakhs Eighty Four Thousand only) is being sanctioned by this Government.
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2018-19 under the targets Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (2851 0585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) and should be spent under the same head.
The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government and the Drawing and Disbursing Officers within the prescribed time. as well
