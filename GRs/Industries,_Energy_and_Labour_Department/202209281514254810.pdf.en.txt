The Proposal:
The Green Energy Fund is being set up to implement renewable and non-renewable energy sources. The Green Energy Fund has been notified by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour with the objective of promoting generation of electricity through non-carbon energy generation projects to reduce carbon emissions in the atmosphere. As per the notification of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour, 4 paisa per unit on the consumption of electricity by industrial and commercial consumers has been fixed as on May 19, 2004. As of May 15, 2008, the green energy fund is being collected and deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the Government at a revised rate of 8 paise per unit of electricity consumption of industrial and commercial consumers.
