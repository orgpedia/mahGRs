Preamble: The office of the Additional Labour Commissioner is functioning at Wakdewadi, Shivajinagar, Pune. The government has given the land. Purchased on 27.02.1979. The land on which the office of the Additional Labour Commissioner, Pune is located has an area of 7400 sq.m. and the construction area of the office is 1, 646.75 sq.m. I.
These include the main building, a small building with a meeting hall for government labour officers, shops, 7 government quarters for class-4 employees and 10 private tenants who were already living before the government bought the land. The building, which houses the office of the Additional Labour Commissioner, Pune, was constructed in 1864 and is 153 years old. The Public Works Department has also opined that since the life of the building is over, it would be appropriate to demolish the building and construct a new building considering the needs of the office. It is proposed to construct the office of the Additional Labour Commissioner and other government offices of the Labour Department in a new building on a plot of 1, 646.75 sq.m.
