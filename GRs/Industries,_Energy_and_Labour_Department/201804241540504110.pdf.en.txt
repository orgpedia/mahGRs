The Proposal:
Policy No. 1 to give preference to local people in jobs in industrial enterprises to provide employment to the local people of the state through industrial development
The decision is taken by the Employment and Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department
In order to provide employment/ self-employment to the educated unemployed in view of the effective implementation of the self-employment policy, it is directed under the Government Decisions No. 2 to 4 to organize joint employment fairs of employers, factory workers, educated unemployed. To provide employment to the local people in the industrial sector of the state
In order to establish micro and small enterprises in the state through adequate opportunities for self-employment arising from development, local people should be provided employment and self-employment fairs at various places in the state.
The matter of giving guidelines for priority in employment and a conducive environment for self-employment was under the consideration of the government. The Government have taken the following decisions in this regard:
Government's decision:
In order to provide more employment and self-employment opportunities to the local educated unemployed in the state, the industrial sector where employment opportunities are available and the skills of the local educated unemployed should be properly matched and employment in industrial establishments at the local and elsewhere level and employment through self-employment should be available at the district level.

The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries will be responsible for the decisions, approvals and other ancillary matters regarding the expenses of the fair. The Development Commissioner shall request the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, Mumbai, for the expenditure to be incurred for the fair and the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, Mumbai, shall make the financial provision accordingly.
The Directorate of Industries and the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Development should provide the necessary information about the urgency of manpower, educational qualifications, necessary skills, etc. in coordination.
The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation will determine the manpower required by the industrial unit in the industrial area of the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation as well as the service/ manufacturing supplier (Vendor). The concerned General Manager, District Industries Centre/ Joint Director of Zonal Industries shall obtain information about the required manpower and related educational qualifications and skills for industrial enterprises/ units and other establishments outside the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation area in coordination with the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
The registration of the youth/ women interested in employment in the Employment and Self-Employment Fair is available on the website www.mahaswayam.in created by the Commissioner, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department. Interested youth/ girls of the district will register on this website. The Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department will be responsible for the proper analysis of the information of the willing unemployed and accordingly invite the big industrial units and other establishments of the district, to organize a physical interview in the fair and take necessary action for recruitment, and to coordinate and control the follow-up of the participating candidates after the fair.
