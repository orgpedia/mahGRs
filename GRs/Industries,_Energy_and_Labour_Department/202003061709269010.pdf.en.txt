The Proposal:
To control the increasing expenditure on government vehicles and to provide government vehicles where necessary, the policy was first decided by the Finance Department in its Government Resolution dated 10.09.2001. According to the policy, it is mandatory to get the approval of the state-level vehicle review committee before allowing and purchasing a new government vehicle.
Accordingly, a detailed proposal after reviewing the vehicles of Industry, Energy and Labour (Self) was submitted to the approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee through the Finance Department. The proposal has been approved by the Vehicle Review Committee in the month of July 2015.
Government's decision:
The State Vehicle Review Committee has approved to allow a total of 7 cars, six cars and one staff car for the following Nos. 1 to 6 posts for the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor (itself).
The vehicles are:

| 7      | The car's staff                  | 1 car (1)                  |
