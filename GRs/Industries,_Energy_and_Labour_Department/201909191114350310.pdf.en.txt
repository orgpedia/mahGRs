Partial modification in the guidelines issued under Pradhan Mantri Khanij Kshetra Kalyan Yojana- Update Paper
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Order No. DMF-0819/ S.No.46/ Industries-9
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date of release: September 19, 2019
Reference: Government Order No.DMF-0615/ PR No. 34/ Industries-9, Dy. 01.09.2016
Government's stand:
The guidelines of Pradhan Mantri Khanij Kshetra Kalyan Yojana have been prescribed as per the decision of the Government in this regard and the actual affected area has been demarcated as 20 km radius from the mine or dump. However, henceforth the actual affected area shall be limited to an area of 5 km from the mine or dump.
