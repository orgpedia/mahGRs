The Government has approved the online availability of Auto Generated/ Auto Renewal Registration/ Renewal/ Improvement Certificate to the Contractor/ Principal Employer under the Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970, Factories Act, 1948 and Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation and Conditions of Employment) Act, 1996 after the prescribed period of 26/09/2022. Accordingly, if the above services are not provided within the prescribed period, the certificate of registration/ rectification of those establishments will be deemed to have been amended and the Deemed Approval is being issued to them in electronic form automatically.
It was also under the consideration of the Government to reduce the time period for obtaining self-generated registration/ renewal/ amendment certificate mentioned in the said paragraph and to form a study group of senior officers of the Labour Department as to how many services/ new services out of the total 41 services notified under the Maharashtra Right to Service Act under the Labour Department can be included as notified by DPIIT under the Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP) 2022 of the Central Government.
Government Decision: Reducing the time period for obtaining self-generated registration/ renewal/ amendment certificate as notified by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under the Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP) 2022 of the Central Government, as well as the Maharashtra Public Service Rights under the Labour Department
