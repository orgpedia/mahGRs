The Proposal: -
The scheme "Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 25/02/2014 to promote cluster development of small clusters in industrially underdeveloped areas of the state thereby promoting holistic development of micro and small units. Under the scheme, state government grants are provided for implementation of capacity building programmes for holistic development and augmentation of micro and small enterprises as well as for setting up of common facilities.

The Government's Decisions:
Under this Government decision, the cost of grant-in-aid is being sanctioned as per the project approval as well as the provision of MSICDP Scheme for setting up of Common Facility Centre for Modular Furniture Cluster, Udgir District Latur, sanctioned under the scheme "Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" of the State Government at a final cost of Rs. 1,51,43, 200 / - (Rupees One Crore One Hundred and Fifty One Lakh Forty Three Thousand Two Hundred Only).
The amount of the grant is from the group project Modular Furniture Cluster, Udgir G. Latur. The accounting officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai, will be responsible for the drawing and transmission of payments to Vihansha Wood Furniture Foundation, Latur, a special purpose vehicle organisation. They should deposit the above amount into their personal virtual deposit account (VPDA) by submitting the payment to the treasury.
The Department is certified as such by the Directorate of Industries vide Government Circular No.Endo-2025 / Q. 44 / Endo-3, dated 07/04/2025, dated 08/10/2025, dated 29/01/2026 and dated 12/03/2026 and the provisions contained in the accompanying Annexure and the information received therewith.
