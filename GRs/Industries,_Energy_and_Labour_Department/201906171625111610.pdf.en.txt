Government Circular As per the prevailing practice, investment period for large/ very large projects is allowed from 1/4/2013, the date of commencement of Group Incentive Scheme-2013/ from the date of Letter of Credit/ MOU/ any qualifying date during the period of SupproYo-2013 or from the date of application to the Government for large/ very large projects. The fixed capital cost of the project includes the cost of land acquired for the project. However, since the land acquisition for the project takes some time and the investment period is permissible as above, the project does not get the full period of 5 years payable. Therefore, the planned investment in the project cannot be made within the prescribed period. In this regard, vide No. 3 By the Government Circular herein, the time limit given to the completion of all the effective stages and the expiry date of the scheme to qualify under the collective incentive scheme of the units as mentioned in paragraph 7.1 of the collective incentive scheme 2013 shall be extended. as on 31.03.2018.
The government has announced the new industrial policy 2019. Accordingly, the government decided to implement the Community Incentive Scheme-2013. The extension granted under 05/06/2018 has expired and the Scheme of Collective Incentive- 2019 has been extended. It will come into effect from 01.04.2019. Therefore, the above
