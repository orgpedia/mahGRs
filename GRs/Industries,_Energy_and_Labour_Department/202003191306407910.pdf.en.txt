Preamble: A total amount of Rs. 720 crore has been allocated for the scheme of providing Agriculture Pump Power Connection under High Pressure Distribution System in Vidarbha and Marathwada region by opening a new account head 28015516 in 2018-19. Out of this, Rs. 230.00 crore has been utilized for the programme under High Pressure Distribution System for providing power connection to new agricultural pumps.
The remaining amount of Rs. 490.00 crore has been utilized for electrification of agriculture pumps in Vidarbha and Marathwada divisions in the traditional manner.
Out of the total sanctioned amount of Rs. 2248.09 crore for Vidarbha and Marathwada region for providing power connection under High Pressure Distribution System to new agricultural pumpsets, a total provision of Rs. 592.59 crore has been released in 2018-19 under Tribal Appropriation Scheme (Rs. 59.43 crore), Special Component Scheme (Rs. 303.16 crore) and General Scheme (Rs. 230.00 crore). The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Prakashgarh Bandra, has submitted a proposal for the distribution of Rs. 902.7199 crore in the budgetary allocation for the year 2019-20. As per the Government Decision dated 11.12.2019, Rs. 541.63 crore has been disbursed and the total outlay for 2019-20 is Rs. 620.90 crore has been sanctioned. A sum of Rs. 79.27 crore was under consideration.
