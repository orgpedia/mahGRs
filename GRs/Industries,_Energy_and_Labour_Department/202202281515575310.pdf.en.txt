Introduction: High Flash Ash Cluster, Chandrapur, a project approved under the Micro, Small Enterprises Cluster Development Scheme (MSE-CDP) of the Government of India. Industrial Boilers Private Limited, Vapi, has filed a claim under Section 18 (1) of the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Development Act, 2006 with Sukrta Parishad, Gandhinagar, Gujarat, for non-payment of their dues under the procurement process. The Tribunal appointed in the matter has passed the final award dated 11/08/2021. In this case, the Tribunal accepted the claim made by the petitioner and determined that the primary responsibility for payment of dues to the petitioner lies with the State Government. Before filing an appeal under Section 34 of the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 against the said award passed by the Arbitrator in this case, the Arbitrator is required to make a pre-deposit of Rs. 1032.50 lakh, which is 75% of the total amount mentioned in the award. Accordingly, as per the request made by the Directorate General of Industries vide letter dated 27/12/2021, an amount of Rs. 1032.50 lakh and professional fee of Rs.5 lakh for Specialist Law Expert in the said case, totalling Rs. 1037.50 lakh was under consideration of the Government for making available on BDS system.
