To provide funds to the Maharashtra State Mining Corporation for the works approved under the Mineral Development Fund.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Resolution No: MDF-1016/ PR No.85/ Industries-9
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date of release: March 29, 2019
Read:- Director, Directorate of Geology and Mining, Nagpur's letter No. Accounts/ Budget/ MSMC/ 419/ Part- 2/2019 dated 13.03.2019.
Preamble- To give directions to the Government regarding the utilization and allocation of Mineral Development Fund. As per the decision taken by the State Level Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Minister in its meeting held on 07.05.2012, a Government Resolution has been issued on 01.08.2012 prescribing revised guidelines regarding utilization and allocation of Mineral Development Fund.
Government decisions have been issued regarding the administrative and financial approval of the eligible works as per the guidelines in this government decision. The Government was considering providing funds to the Maharashtra State Mining Corporation for providing funds to the executing agencies to take up the works approved by the Government.
Government Decision- Under this Order, the Government is providing Rs. 9.00 crore (Rs. Nine crore only) to Maharashtra State Mining Corporation Limited, Nagpur from the Maharashtra Mineral Development Fund, which was budgeted for 2018-19. The Government hereby approve the release of the said amount of Rs. 9.00 crore (Rupees Nine Crores only) to the Directorate of Geology and Mining, Nagpur for meeting the administrative expenses of various matters mentioned in Para 4 of the Government Resolution dated 01.08.2012.
2. Budgetary provision is available for this purpose and out of this provision, an expenditure of Rs. 9.00 crore (Rs.9 crore only) should be met and this expenditure should be met through Demand No. K-7 Principal Accounts Head" 2853, Aloe Mining and Metallurgical Industries- 02 (00) (05) Mineral Development (Non-Plan) (28530132) 31 Expenditure to be made as per revised Cash Flow under the head Subsidy (Non-Payment).
The said Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred under Section 5 (a) of the Maharashtra Mineral Development (Formation and Sub-Plan) Act, 2001.
