2) Department of Finance, Government Decision No. Post No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC dated 11th September, 2019 Preamble: For the purpose of collection of Labour dues and Labour compensation in Mumbai City District, 8 temporary posts have been created since 1971 in the office of the Collector, Mumbai City. These eight posts have been extended from time to time by the government. The said posts were approved to be continued from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 vide Government Order dated 01.04.2019 in the aforesaid Roll No. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approve the continuation of the following eight temporary posts under the office of the Collector, Mumbai City for the purpose of collection of Labour dues and Labour Compensation for the Mumbai City District from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 subject to the conditions mentioned in the circular of the Finance Department in their No.DBB1097/ PR.No.26/ Budget-19, dated 20.12.1997 and excluding the posts lying vacant for a period of six or more than six months.

The expenditure on account of salary and allowances of these posts shall be covered under the head Main Accounts Head 2230-Labour and Employment (01) Labour 111 Social Security for Workers (00) (02) Expenditure in respect of Employees Provident Fund and other Acts (2230 0612) Demand No. K-4 and shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2019-20.
The said Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Resolution No.1004/ PR 12/ (Part-II)/ 2004/ Financial Reforms-1 dated 4th October, 2004 of the Finance Department.
However, the above posts are not to be used by the District Collector for any work other than the work of recovery of pending dues to the workers under the Labour Department, the work related to the Labour Department such as the work to be done to eliminate child labour and other labour law enforcement activities, as well as according to the authority granted to the administrative departments under the Government Decision No. No. Designation 2016/ No. No. 8/16/ P.C.D. of the Finance Department dated 11th September, 2019 and the process of drawing up of the said posts will be carried out within the stipulated time.
