| The Maharashtra Welfare Officers (Duties, Qualifications and Conditions of Service) Rules, 1966 | For the purposes of clause (1) of proviso (1) to rule 3 of the Maharashtra Welfare Officers (Duties, Qualifications and Conditions of Service) Rules, 1966, the Government hereby grants recognition to the course M.A. in Labour Studies and Practices conducted by the Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai (Tata Institute of Social Sciences) as possessing the educational qualification required for appointment to the posts of Welfare Officer, Additional Welfare Officer or Assistant Welfare Officer. |
