Preamble: As per the Government decision in Reference No. 2, the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai has been approved to disburse a grant of Rs. 2113.96 lakhs (Rupees Twenty One Crore Thirteen Lakhs Fifty Five Thousand only) for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to meet the expenditure for the month of June and July. The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai, vide its letters of reference No. 3 and 4, has requested to provide a grant of Rs. 2113.96 lakh (Rupees Twenty One Crore Thirteen Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand only) and 31-Subsidised Grant (Non-Salary) of Rs. 139.44 lakh (Rupees Twenty Two Crore Thirty Three Lakhs Forty Four Thousand only) for meeting the expenditure for four months from August to November, 2019.
It was under consideration of the Government to provide grants to the Board to meet the salary and non-salary expenses for the four months from August to November, 2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Under this Government Decision, a total of Rs. 2253.40 (Rupees Twenty Two Crore Fifty Three Lakh Forty Thousand only) is being sanctioned to Khadi and Village Industries Board for meeting the expenditure for the period of four months from August to November, 2019 for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 2113.96 lakh and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 139.44 lakh.
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2019-20 under the targets Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (2851 0585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) and should be spent under the same head.
