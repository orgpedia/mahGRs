The Proposal:
In order to promote ready-made garments, gems and jewellery and micro-electronics and engineering components, the Department of Industries has notified the" Flatted Sludge Industrial Complexes (Parks) Policy 2018" for ready-made garment manufacturing, gems and jewellery, micro-electronics and engineering components through a relevant Government Resolution. The government was considering issuing an operational order through the Energy Department under the" Power Supply at Industrial Rates" policy. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
(1) Electricity will be provided at industrial rates for activities other than commercial and commercial facilities in the Flatted Sludge Industrial Complexes (Parks) Policy-2018 for readymade garment manufacturing, gems and jewellery, micro electronics and engineering components.
(2) The" Power Distribution Franchisee Model Through MoU Route" can be adopted as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission for industrial complexes with flatted sludge for ready-made garment manufacturing, gems and jewellery, micro electronics and engineering components.
(3) As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission's Open Access Regulations-2016, individual eligible consumers in the Flatted Sludge Industrial Complexes for readymade garment manufacturing, gems and jewellery, micro electronics and engineering components can purchase electricity through open access from non-conventional/ conventional sources of energy. Also, as per the Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission's Net Metering Regulation-2015 and Amended 2017, eligible consumers can generate electricity for self-consumption through renewable energy sources.
(8) Under the Power Distribution Franchisee Model through MoU route, the infrastructure required for the connection of power supply from the working substation/ high pressure channel of MSEDCL/ MET will be constructed by the developer at his own cost under the Dedicated Distribution Facility.
