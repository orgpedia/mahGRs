The Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board has been set up in the state under Section 18 of the said Act.
Workers working in building and other construction sectors in the state are registered with the board and given the benefits of various welfare schemes run by the board. Accordingly, the provision of safety kits for active (living) construction workers in the state registers under the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, vide Scheme No. 5 was approved by a government decision here.

Terms and Conditions of the Scheme: -
1. The active (living) construction workers in the register with the Board shall be eligible to avail the benefit of the scheme. These qualified workers submit a demand form in the prescribed format to the authorized in-charge worker.
Deputy Commissioners / Assistant Labour Commissioners / Government Labour Officers, after filling up with District Labour Facilitation Centres (all), should be provided with safety kits.
The safety kit to be supplied to qualified construction workers should include the following items.
(1) Safety harness, (2) Safety shoes, (3) Ear plug, (4) Mask, (4) Reflective jacket, (6) Helmet, (7) Safety gloves, (8) Safety goggles, (9) Mosquito net, (90) Water bottle, (11) Steel Tiffin box, (22) Solar torch, (92) Passenger kit bag
Government approval is being granted for implementation of e-tendering process for supply of safety kits to active (living) construction workers registered under the Board.
Accordingly, the Board shall submit a proposal to the Government for selection of a registered, nominated and experienced Institute under the e-tendering process. Also, the provisions of the Government Decision dated 1.12.2016 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour should be strictly followed for this entire process.
The prices of the items in the modified safety kit to be distributed to registered construction workers should be fixed along with the supply, transportation, distribution and all taxes.
Before accepting the tender for the safety kit, the quality of the items in the safety kit should be checked and verified by a government-approved testing school and the distribution of such items to the eligible beneficiary should be done only after the certificate is received.
According to the list of construction workers in the eligible register duly attested by the Deputy Commissioner of Labour / Assistant Labour Commissioner / Government Labour Officer, District Labour Facilitation Centre (all).
Safety kits should be supplied within the stipulated time frame.
The Assistant Labour Commissioner / Government Labour Officer in-charge, District Labour Facilitation Centre (all) shall ensure that the safety kit is provided to the eligible construction workers and submit the supply to the Additional Labour Commissioner / Deputy Commissioner of Labour / Regional Executive Officer (Division level) in charge of the supply.
