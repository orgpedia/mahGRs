The proposal: The Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission (MERC) has filed a petition No. 26/2019. In the decision dated 05.01.2022, it has been stated that MSEDCL will advise the State Government to set up a study group to suggest short and long-term solutions to the current financial difficulties of the company. Under the reference letter, as per section 86 (2) of the Electricity Act, 2003, the State Government has been informed to constitute a study group to prepare a roadmap for time bound improvement in the operation and performance of MSEDCL. The matter of appointing such a study group with the approval of the government was under the consideration of the government.
Government decision: In accordance with the advice given by the Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission to the Department of Power under Section 86 (2) of the Electricity Act, 2003, to appoint a study group to prepare a roadmap for improving the performance of the MSEDCL, the study group is being constituted as follows:
