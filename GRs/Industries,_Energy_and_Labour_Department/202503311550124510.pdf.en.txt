The Proposal: -
The scheme "Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 25/02/2014 to promote cluster development of small clusters in industrially underdeveloped areas of the state thereby promoting holistic development of micro and small units. Under the scheme, state government grants are provided for implementation of capacity building programmes for holistic development and augmentation of micro and small enterprises as well as for setting up of common facilities. Under this scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared which includes up-to-date technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial clusters, up-to-date training programmes, study tours, etc., by properly diagnosing the gaps in the manufacturing process of the industrial group.
2. Once the Diagnostic Study Report is received after the recommendation of the District Level Committee, it is taken up by the State Level Industrial Group Control Committee headed by Vikas Ayukt (Udyog) to approve the Diagnostic Study Report proposals and fix the grant for capacity building programme thereunder. The grants for the capacity building programme are disbursed in two phases. The first tranche of 60 per cent subsidy to be disbursed by the government is disbursed after ensuring that the required 10 per cent participation of the stakeholders is accumulated. The remaining 40 per cent subsidy is disbursed after the submission of the first phase utilisation certificate.
3. Accordingly, approval orders have been issued by the Directorate of Industries dated 21/01/2025, dated 13/03/2025 and dated 19/03/2025.
