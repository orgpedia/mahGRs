Introduction: The new industrial policy of the state has been announced through a relevant government decision. The Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Shri. The Global Investment Promotion Council (GIPC) has been set up under the Government Decision dated 23.08.2019. The Council has been asked to appoint heads of major industrial associations of the state, international investment advisory bodies, individuals from home and abroad or representatives of reputed institutions as special invitees as per the requirement. Accordingly, the matter of appointing non-government members as special invitees to the Global Investment Promotion Council in the state was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The government has taken a decision. The following eminent members from the industry are being nominated as special invitees to the Global Investment Promotion Council (GIPC) constituted on 23.08.2019.
