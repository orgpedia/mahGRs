Memorandum to the Government, Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, No. Caprio-2019/ No. 124 (Part-1)/ Industry-2, D. 09/12/2020.
Memorandum: The government has given a subsidy to the cashew processing industry in the state. The decision has been taken as per the Government Decision dated 15/07/2010. Also the. To continue the scheme by fixing the revised methodology of allocation of incentives to cashew processing industry as per Government decision dated 12/03/2013.
It's been approved. As per the scheme, after payment of 5% value added tax on the sale of cashew nuts finally sold in the State out of the cashew nuts produced and processed in the State, the tax amount is being provided to the concerned units as incentive subsidy. The pending claims for reimbursement of incentives with the Government have been cleared in a phased manner as the financial provision under the scheme is inadequate. Rs.5 crore has been allocated for the scheme in 2020-21
Out of this amount, Rs. 1.5 crore is available for distribution on BDS system with a limit of 30%.
Funds have been provided. 23/07/2020 and D. It has been distributed to the industry units under the Government Memorandum dated 09/12/2020. At present, 32 claims are pending with the government and a sum of Rs 1,03,65, 805/- is required to settle these claims. Therefore, the remaining amount of the proposed fund should be provided for distribution through the Finance Department as per the request of the department.
