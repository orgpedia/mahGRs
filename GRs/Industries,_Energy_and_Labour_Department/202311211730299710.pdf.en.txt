(2) The promotion to the post of Senior Industrial Officer, Group-A is being granted to the said officers subject to the assumption that no departmental enquiry is pending or proposed against them.

3. The above officers should be relieved by the Development Commissioner (Industries) within the stipulated time to join the place of posting after promotion. Also, no application for cancellation or modification of the post should be submitted. If this is done, the officer will be liable for disciplinary action.
The officers to be appointed by promotion under the provisions of 2 (d) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2015 shall be required to join within a period of 30 days from the date of issue of the order of promotion.
