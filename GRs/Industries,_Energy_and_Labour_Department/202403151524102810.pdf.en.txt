Preamble: - Pradhan Mantri Vishwakarma Kaushalya Samman Yojana has been implemented throughout the state by a Government decision dated 06.09.2023. The scheme is funded by the Government of India. The scheme will provide eligible beneficiaries with (1) Pradhan Mantri Vishwakarma Certificate and Identity Card, (2) Toolkit Incentive, (3) Skill Development Training Allowance, (4) Discounted and Mortgage-Free Loans, (5) Incentives for Digital Transactions and (6) Marketing Support. Initially, eighteen traditional businesses have been covered under the scheme. Beneficiaries under the scheme are being registered through the biometric-authenticated Aadhaar-based Pradhan Mantri Vishwakarma Portal, through Common Service Centres ("$5"). The beneficiary details are expected to be verified by the head of the Gram Panchayat, the executive head of the urban local body, as per the laid down procedure of the scheme.
A "District Implementation Committee" has been constituted under the chairmanship of the Collector for district-level implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Vishwakarma Kaushalya Samman Yojana and a "State Control Committee" under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Industries) for control of the scheme.
Eligible beneficiaries are registered on the Aadhaar authenticated web portal as per the guidelines of the scheme. The registration is done through biometric authentication through Common Service Centres ("505") of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India (1 / 0 (4)).

2. The Collector shall authorise the Tahsildar to make the second round of recommendations in the urban division of the Nagar Panchayat.
