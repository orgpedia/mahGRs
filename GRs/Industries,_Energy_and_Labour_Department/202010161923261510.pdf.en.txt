The above officer is being given ad-hoc promotion only on temporary basis subject to the following conditions:
(1) The promoted officer shall be eligible for the best of service and other service benefits in the post of promotion after regularization of the said promotion/ selection list with the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.

(2) The above promotion. Pursuant to the final award in the Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 pending in the Supreme Court, the vacancy in the open category is being filled up only on temporary basis.
(3) The above promotion. The original application filed in the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai No. 1192/2019 along with Narrow Application No. 115/2020 as well as Original Application No. 483/2020 along with Narrow Application No. 221/2020 is being issued subject to the final decision.
(4) The ad-hoc promotion to the said officer is subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed to be initiated in any case till the date of the order against him.
(5) The officers so promoted are promoted subject to the assumption that they have passed the requisite professional examination, computer examination, Hindi language examination, Marathi language examination, etc., within the prescribed time and opportunity, and that the exemption from passing is admissible as per rules, and proper records thereof are taken in the service book and maintained as per rules.
(6) If the said officer does not accept the net temporary promotion to the post of Assistant Electrical Inspector, Group-A (Gazetted), the Government Decision, General Administration Department, No. SRV-2015/ PR No. 303/ Office No. 12, D. Action will be taken as per the provisions of 12.09.2016.
(7) The Chief Inspector of Electricity, Mumbai shall, after making local alternative arrangements, relieve the officer appointed by promotion forthwith and instruct him to attend the place of new appointment immediately. Also, the Chief Electrical Inspector, Mumbai, should immediately submit a report to the government that the concerned officer has joined the post of promotion.
(8) The above officer appointed by the promotion shall join immediately at the place of his new appointment. They should not go on leave unauthorisedly or submit a representation/ application for change in posting. In doing so, they will be liable for disciplinary action for violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
(9) The benefits of promotion shall be admissible to the above officers from the date on which they join the office of promotion.
