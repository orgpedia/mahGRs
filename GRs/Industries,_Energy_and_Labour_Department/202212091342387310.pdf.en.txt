Preamble: General Administration Department Reference No. 1 The Appointment Authority hereby determines the appointment of Group' A' and Group' B' (Gazetted) officers in the Government Service as well as Group' B' (Non-Gazetted), Group' C' and Group' D' employees in direct service promotions and deputation. The government has also asked the administrative departments to issue orders to declare the post-wise appointing authority for the posts in Group' B' (non-gazetted), Group' C' and Group' D'.
Reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 21.08.2019, the Divisional Office of the Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Amravati has been started for the Amravati Revenue Division. Also, the reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 15.11.2019, Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Amaravati has been declared as the Head of Regional Office for Amaravati Division. However, he has not been appointed as an official. General Administration Department Reference No. 1 The matter of determining the appointing authority for taking a final decision in respect of appointment/ posting of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees in the Labour Department by direct recruitment, promotion or deputation, as notified under the Government Order, was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Amravati, for the office of the Labour Department under the control of the Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Amravati, under the Amravati Revenue Division, is being declared as the appointing authority under this Government Decision to take the final decision regarding the appointment/ posting of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees by direct service, promotion or deputation.
