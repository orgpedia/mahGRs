Preamble In the 7th meeting held on 22/03/2021 of the Steering Committee under the scheme of Maharashtra Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development, the Committee has approved the work of installation of Express Feeder to Pokhran Lake Small Scale Industrial Association, Thane in Thane district and 100% funds accordingly. An agreement has been signed with the Directorate General of Industries that the repair and maintenance of the express feeder sanctioned to this institute will be done through the institute and the express feeder will be used for public purposes. A warranty is also provided. Therefore, the matter of granting administrative approval to this work of the institute was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the approval given in the 7th meeting of the Steering Committee held on 22/03/2021 under the scheme of Maharashtra Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development and as per the proposal submitted by the Directorate of Industries in their letter dated 11/10/2021 under reference No.5, the administrative approval is being granted to the work mentioned in the following statement of Pokhran Lake Small Scale Industrial Association, Thane. Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Ltd. (MSEDCL) is the operating system. Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Ltd. (MSEDCL) should take further action by giving immediate technical approval on the basis of this administrative approval.
