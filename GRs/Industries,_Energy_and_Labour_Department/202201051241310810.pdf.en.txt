Preamble: In order to promote the development of small clusters in the industrially underdeveloped areas of the State and thereby promote the overall development of micro and small units, the Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP) has been announced vide Government Resolution dated 25/02/2014. Under this scheme, the state government provides grants for the implementation of capacity building programs for the overall development and growth of micro and small enterprises, as well as for the construction of common facilities. Under this scheme, a Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) is prepared by properly diagnosing the flaws in the production process of the industrial group, including the latest technology, market expansion, visits to various successful industrial groups, updated training programs and study tours, etc. District-level study report
After receiving the recommendation of the Committee, the State Level Industrial Cluster Control Committee approves the Diagnostic Study Report proposals and fixes the grant for capacity building programme under it. A Detailed Project Report (DPR) containing details of high quality common facilities and other necessary clearances, no-objection certificates, technical and financial evaluation reports are prepared to enhance the productivity and competitiveness of MSME units. The procurement process is initiated after final approval of the proposal of Common Facility Centre by the State Level Steering Committee. Sadar

After the final approval of the proposal of the Common Facility Center, the first phase 20% subsidy is disbursed after receiving the report with the purchase details to the projects whose procurement process is completed through the zonal committee. 60% of the second phase subsidy is disbursed after receiving the physical verification report from the General Manager, District Industries Centre regarding the installation of machinery and equipment at the work site as per the project approval and the utilization certificate of the first phase subsidy. The remaining 20% subsidy is given after the successful completion of the Common Facility Centre by obtaining a certificate from the Joint Director of Industries. Accordingly, the Leather Cluster, Osmanabad Group Project has been approved for the construction of Common Facility Center and as per the project approval, the physical verification report of the machinery and equipment being installed at the workplace and the utilization certificate of the first phase grant has been submitted by the Joint Director of Industries, Aurangabad through the Directorate of Industries.
Government's decision:
The Directorate of Industries has issued a proposal for the release of second phase eligible grant of Rs. 1,62,02, 400/- from the State Government for the construction of Common Facility Centre of Leather Cluster, Osmanabad Project sanctioned under the scheme" Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSI-CDP)" of the State Government. 4 is presented here. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the present scheme, the Government is approving the release of second phase eligible grant of Rs. 1,62,02, 400/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Two Lakh Two Thousand Four Hundred Only).
