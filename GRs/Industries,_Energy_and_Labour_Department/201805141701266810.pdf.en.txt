Introduction: Re-fixation of Minimum Wage Rates for Beedi Industry Workers in the State The Government vide notification dated 10th November, 2014 has fixed the minimum wage at Rs. The owners of the beedi industry have not listened to them while rescheduling the minimum wage rate and have objected to the need to review the draft notification and the final notification as the rate has been increased drastically and is not justified and the rates of other states have also not been taken into account. The date is April 21, 2018. The Minister of State (Labour) has directed to form a tripartite committee of the government, employer representatives and workers representatives to fix the minimum wage rate by revising the minimum wage rate.
Government Decision: Minimum Wages for Beedi workers in the State to be revised from the Minimum Wages prescribed vide Notification dated 10th November, 2014 and to fix new rates of Minimum Wages
As per Section 5 (1) (a) of the Wages Act, 1948, a Tripartite Committee consisting of Government, Employer's Representative and Worker's Representative is being constituted under the Chairmanship of the Labour Commissioner as follows:
