The Proposal:
As per the government policy, the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) provides subsidy on electricity tariff to the textile consumers of the state and the government reimburses the same to MSEDCL. Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company has received an amount of Rs.100 crore under Demand Number K-6 Head Account Heading 28015599 for the financial year 2021-22 for providing financial assistance to textile consumers. A budgetary provision of Rs. 300.00 crore has been made. From this provision, the above reference No. Under the Government's decision, MSEDCL will receive Rs. 207.00 crore has been provided. Now, as approved by the Finance Department, the MSEDCL will receive Rs. 93.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited has been given a subsidy of Rs. The Government has decided to disburse Rs. 93.00 crore (Rupees Ninety Three Crores only) in cash.
