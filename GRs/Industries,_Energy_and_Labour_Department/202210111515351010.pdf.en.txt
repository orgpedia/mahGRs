The Proposal:
With the increasing number of educated youth in urban and rural areas of the state, new opportunities for self-employment and employment are being created in various fields in the state. or
Promoting entrepreneurship by considering new opportunities for self-employment and employment.
The Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme (CMEGP)
The comprehensive scheme has been implemented as per the Government Decision dated 01/08/2019.
Accordingly, State Level and District Level Controlling Committees have been constituted to provide various opportunities of self-employment to the urban and rural youth of the state and for effective implementation of the scheme.
Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program, the state government will establish self-employment projects to make the youth of the state self-sufficient/ self-reliant.
The financial assistance will be provided from the state budget.
The State Industrial Policy 2019 has been announced. Accordingly, a new accounting head for the general category and tribal category under the said scheme has been issued vide Government Resolution dated 23/01/2020
