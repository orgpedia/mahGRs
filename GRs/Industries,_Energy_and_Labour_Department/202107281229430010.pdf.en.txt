Amendments to the Maharashtra Electricity Tariff Act, 2016 and the Maharashtra Tax on Sale of Electricity Act, 1963
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SRIKH- 2020/ PR No.41/ ENERGY-1
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk
Ministry of Health, Mumbai- 32
Date: July 28, 2021
The Proposal:
Currently, the Maharashtra Electricity Tariff Act, 2016, and the Maharashtra Tax on Sale of Electricity Act, 1963, are in force in the state. In the energy sector, new concepts are emerging, ease of doing business, and in view of the power tariff orders and court orders issued from time to time, it is necessary to have the provisions of the act and rules consistent with it. Further, the Government was contemplating to constitute a Study Group to study the provisions of such Act in other States and make changes in such Act relating to power sector to suit the present situation.
Government Decision: A Working Group is being constituted to study various aspects of the Maharashtra Electricity Tariff Act, 2016, the Maharashtra Tax on Sale of Electricity Act, 1963 and to suggest changes and amendments in the existing Act and Rules to incorporate concepts related to the power sector.

The Committee may include and invite such persons as may be necessary.
3. The Working Group shall study the various provisions of the Act, Rules, Regulations, etc. relating to the power sector and submit a report to the Government within one month, after taking into account the time-bound aspects, for making changes and amendments therein. The working group should be coordinated by the Chief Electrical Inspector, Mumbai.
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
To study the relevant provisions in other States with respect to electricity duty and electricity sales tax and other power sector related provisions in Acts, Rules, Regulations, Codes etc. where the State Government has jurisdiction and make suitable recommendations for its implementation in the State and to suggest amendments in the above Acts, Rules, Regulations, Codes.
To consider the new concepts in the Electricity Tariff Order issued by the Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission from time to time.
3. To study and suggest modifications in the judgment of the court in a judicial matter arising out of the limitation in the relevant provision.
4. Suggest recommendations for incorporation of new concepts in the Act relating to captive power plants and open access consumers.
To make recommendations for changes in outdated Acts, Rules, etc. or provisions thereof as may be relevant from time to time.
