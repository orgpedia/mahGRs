Preamble: In order to support the construction of projects under the Central Government's Micro and Small Enterprises-Collective Development Plan (MSE-CDP) and to enhance the cost-effectiveness of the projects, a scheme titled" Scheme of Subsidy to Industry Cluster Projects for Creation of Common Facilities of the State" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 09/06/2010. Further, as per the Government decision dated 22/10/2010, the State Government's participation under the scheme has been fixed at 10% of the project cost (upper ceiling subsidy limit Rs. 1.50 crore). Under the scheme, the Central Government vide its order dated 06/11/2017 has accorded final approval for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Printing Cluster G. Aurangabad and the State Government's participation grant under the project will be Rs. 150 lakh.
As per the guidelines of the Micro, Small Enterprises-College Development Plan (MSE-CDP), the sanctioned grant of the Central Government is disbursed in the ratio of 40:30:30 to the projects sanctioned under the scheme. Also, the share of the state government in the grant sanctioned by the central government should be in proportion to the proposed grant by the central government. Government Decision: The Central Government has decided to set up a printing cluster under the Micro and Small Enterprises- Group Development Scheme (MSE-CDP).
The final approval for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Aurangabad has been accorded vide order dated 06/11/2017 and the total sanctioned project cost is Rs. 1633.80 lakh with Central Government grant of Rs.1200 lakh and State Government share grant of Rs.150 lakh.
Accordingly, the Government have approved the release of State Participation Grant amounting to Rs. 45,00,000/- (Rupees Forty Five Lakh only) as per the provisions of the said Scheme to the Printing Cluster G. Aurangabad as per the project approval, out of the total amount of Rs.150 lakh.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall arrange to transfer the amount of Rs. 45,00,000/- from the Government treasury to the Printing Cluster G. Aurangabad, a special purpose vehicle society for the project. The powers to draw and disburse the above amount of Rs. 45,00,000/- are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall take necessary action to monitor the progress of this special purpose vehicle and the proper utilization and utilization of the Government share capital.
