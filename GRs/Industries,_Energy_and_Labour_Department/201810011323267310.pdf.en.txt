Preamble: The Ministry of Mines, Government of India has notified the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 2015 vide Government Notification dated 27.03.2015 by amending the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, 1957.
As per the provisions of Section 9 (B) of the present Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 2015, District Mineral Foundations are required to be established in every district of the State for the development of mining affected areas and individuals. In pursuance of the above provisions, the Rules regarding establishment, structure and functioning of District Mineral Foundation have been issued vide Government Notification dated 01.09.2016.
In accordance with the provisions of the said Rules, recommendation has been received from the Guardian Minister of Latur District and Chairman District Mineral Foundation, Latur for appointment of nominated members in the Governing Council of District Mineral Foundation, Latur. In pursuance of these recommendations, the proposal for appointment of nominated members on the Executive Council of District Mineral Foundation, Latur was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In view of the above, it is being agreed to appoint the following members as nominated members to the Governing Council of District Mineral Foundation, Latur in accordance with the provisions of Government Notification dated 01.09.2016.
