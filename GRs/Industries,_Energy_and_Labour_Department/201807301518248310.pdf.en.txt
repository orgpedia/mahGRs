Deputy Director, Group-A, Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health Provisional Common Seniority as on 01/01/2017 is annexed to this Circular. This seniority is determined by General Administration Department, Government Resolution No.
SRV 2011/ Q.No. 284/12, D. As per the instructions dated 21/10/2011 and Government Order No.1011/ PR No.58/ Labour-1, D. 23.4.2018 and Ma. The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, vide its original application No. 400/2017 has been prepared.
The Director-in-charge, Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health is requested to bring this seniority to the notice of all the concerned officers (including officers on leave/ deputation) and to place a copy of their signature on such intimation in the records. Also, the concerned should immediately submit a statement regarding the objections/ errors regarding this seniority. The factual position in the representation so received should be verified from the available records of the concerned and the representations with detailed feedback should be sent to the Government at the earliest (within fifteen days). In case of non-receipt of objections/ information, the final seniority list will be published assuming that there are no objections to the seniority list.
