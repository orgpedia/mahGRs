Preamble: In order to support the construction of projects under the Central Government's Micro and Small Enterprises Cluster Development Scheme (MSE-CDP) and to increase the cost-effectiveness of the projects, a scheme titled" Scheme of Subsidy to Industry Cluster Projects for Creation of Common Facilities of the State" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 09/06/2010. Further, as per the Government decision dated 22/10/2010, the State Government's participation under the scheme has been fixed at 10% of the project cost (upper ceiling subsidy limit Rs. 1.50 crore). Under the scheme, the Central Government vide its order dated 17/05/2022 has accorded final approval for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Industrial Group Project, Textile& Garment Digital Printing Cluster, Miraj G. Sangli with State Government's share of grant-in-aid amounting to Rs. 149.99 lakh under the project.
As per the guidelines of the Micro, Small Enterprises-College Development Plan (MSE-CDP), the sanctioned grant of the Central Government is disbursed in the ratio of 40:30:30 to the projects sanctioned under the scheme. Also, the share of the state government in the grant sanctioned by the central government should be in proportion to the proposed grant by the central government. However, as per the revised guidelines of MSE-CDP scheme, in case of higher share of State Government participation subsidy, 50% of the Central Government sanctioned grant to the project concerned is disbursed in a single phase.
