The Proposal: -
The Maharashtra Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development Scheme has been launched by the Department of Industries to create infrastructure to promote industrial development in Maharashtra. Department of Industry. The procedures and guidelines for allocation of Industrial Infrastructure Funds have been prescribed under the Government Decision dated 25.02.2014. Accordingly, it is empowered to approve proposals received for seeking funds under the scheme. The Steering Committee is headed by the Chief Secretary. Technical approval is granted by the HARA mechanism after 25 per cent of the total project cost is paid to the government as a voluntary contribution by the agencies approved by the Steering Committee, followed by administrative approval at the ministerial level and the remaining 75 per cent is disbursed by the government.
