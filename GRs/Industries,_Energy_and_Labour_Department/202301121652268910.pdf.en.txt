The Proposal:
The Central Government has decided to provide interest free loans to the States for a period of 50 years under the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Expenditure. Under the scheme, Rs. A sum of Rs. 145.00 crore has been sanctioned. This provision has been budgeted under head 68750011 by way of Supplementary Demands in the Winter Session 2022. The Finance Department has also sanctioned Rs. 145.00 crore has been made available by the Department of Finance on the BEAMS system. Give the amount. It is necessary to spend before 31/3/2023.
Accordingly, a total of Rs. 145.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.
Government circular:
Under the Scheme of Special Assistance to States for Capital Expenditure, the Central Government has made a budgetary provision of Rs. 145.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Forty Five Crores only) to Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation for the following projects.

The expenditure for the said purpose shall be covered under the head" Demand No. K-11, 6875- Loans to other industries, 60- Other industries, 190- Loans to Public Sector and other enterprises, (1) Non-interest bearing loan (Central share 100%) under Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Investment by Central Government to States, (1) Non-interest bearing loan for Pradhan Mantri Gatishakti Infrastructure Project (Scheme Part-II) (68750011)" and the same shall be met out of the approved outlay for the financial year 2022-2023.
Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation should ensure that the funds are spent for the same project and its purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned by the Central Government and submit the utilization certificate of the disbursed amount immediately. Also, the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation should ensure that no funds from any other scheme are spent on the above-mentioned projects.
Kishore Motiram Soitkar, Cell Officer, Mantralaya, Mumbai and Sanjay Degaonkar, Joint Secretary, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Controlling Officers.
The said Fund Drawing and Disbursing Officer has been empowered to disburse the funds received by making payments to the Accounts and Accounts Officer, Mumbai, to the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, Andheri, Mumbai-93.
The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation shall maintain accounts in the form prescribed by the Accountant General for the amount of loans received under Part-II under the Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment Scheme of the Central Government.
