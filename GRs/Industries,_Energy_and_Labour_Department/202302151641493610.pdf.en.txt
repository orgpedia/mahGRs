Government Decision: Under the Central Government Scheme for Special Assistance as Loan to State for Capital Expenditure (Scheme Code-3888), funds will be made available for specific works in various schemes of the State Government for the year 2022-23. The Finance Department has instructed to transfer the funds to the Public Management System (PFMS). The mapping of these schemes on Public Financial Management System (PFMS) will be done by the State Project Management Cell, Mumbai.

5. The account at SSM level and the account for the execution mechanism should be linked to the PFMS system.
To disburse the funds of the scheme to the concerned agencies in a centralized manner, the funds received for each scheme from the treasury should first be deposited in the SSM level bank account linked to the PFMS system of the concerned scheme and thereafter the concerned agencies should disburse the funds in a centralized manner to the projects selected from their level.
If the SSM account is closed in the future, the balance of the balance should be matched and the remaining amount should be deposited immediately under the appropriate government account.
