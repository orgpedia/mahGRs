The officers who are being reinstated in government service under this order should join the place of posting immediately and submit the report to the government through the Chief Electrical Inspector, Mumbai. The suspension of the officer shall be deemed to have come to an end from the date on which he enters the office of the post holder.
Orders regarding regularization of the period of suspension of the said officers will be issued as soon as the final decision of the court case and departmental inquiry against them is made.
