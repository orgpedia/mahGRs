GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SRIKH- 2018/ PR No. 214/ ENERGY- 4
The meetings of the said Committee were held on 14.02.2019 and 07.03.2019. As directed by the committee in these meetings, a proposal was under the consideration of the government to appoint a Bid Process Coordinator (BPC) for the construction of new transmission projects in the state under the competitive tender process based on the power tariff.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The meetings of the Empowered Committee were held on 14.02.2019 and 07.03.2019. In the meeting, the Chief Engineer, State Transmission Undertakings (Co-ordinating Member) recommended setting up of some transmission projects under competitive bidding process based on power tariff. It has been approved by the Empowered Committee. The Maharashtra State Power Transmission Company is being recognized as Bid Process Coordinator (BPC) to execute the tender process as proposed by the Empowered Committee for these projects.
