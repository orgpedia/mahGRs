2. The shareholding is distributed by the Government to the above named General Manufacturing, MSEDCL and MSEDCL. The Government was considering opening three new heads of accounts to reflect the capital investment made by the Government in these companies in the financial accounts of the Accountant General's Office as an investment made by the Government's principal investor, the Mahasutradhari Company.
Mahasutradhari, Mahavitaran, Mahanirmiti and Mahatrans have come into existence since 2005 after the bifurcation of the erstwhile Maharashtra State Electricity Board. The government has approved the opening of three new heads of accounts to show the share capital of MSEDCL, DGM and MAHATRADESH in the financial accounts of the Accountant General's office as the capital investment made by the MSEDCL.
