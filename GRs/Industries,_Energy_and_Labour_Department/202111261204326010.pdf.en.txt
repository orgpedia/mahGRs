Preamble: As per the policy of the government, the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) provides concession in electricity rates to various categories of consumers in the state and the government reimburses the same to MSEDCL. For the year 2021-22, MSEDCL has been allocated Rs. 300.00 crore out of Rs. 207.00 crore has been disbursed in cash as per Government Decision dated 29.06.2021 and Rs.
Supplementary Demand of Rs. 500.00 crore has been approved for Samayona in the July, 2021 session. The Supplementary Statement of Expenditure was presented to the Legislative Council in the Monsoon Session of July, 2021. The Supplementary Appropriation Bill has been approved by the Cabinet for inclusion in the Appropriation for the financial year ending March 31, 2022. The Governor has given his assent and the same has been published in the Maharashtra Government Gazette Extraordinary Part-IV, dated 15th July, 2021 as the Maharashtra (Second Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2021 (Maharashtra Act No. 08 of 2021). Accordingly, the Government sanctioned Rs. 500.00 crore was under the consideration of the Government for making available the same to the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company (MSDDC) in an adjusted manner.
Government Decision: Out of the subsidy amount payable to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited for the concession given to textile consumers in electricity tariff for the financial year 2020-21, Rs. This Government resolution approves release of Rs. 500.00 crore (Rupees Five Hundred Crores only) by way of adjustment instead of cash.
