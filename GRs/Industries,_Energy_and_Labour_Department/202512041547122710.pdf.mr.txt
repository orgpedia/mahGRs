Delegation visit to ArcelorMittal
Singapore Pvt. Ltd., Singapore.
GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASHTRA
Industries, Energy, Labour & Mining Department,
Government Resolution No.Trip-2025/CR-227/ IND-3
Mantralaya, Mumbai-400032.
Date : 04 December, 2025
PREAMBLE :
Dr. P. Anbalagan, IAS, Secretary, (Industries), Government of Maharashtra,
Shri.P.Velrasu, IAS, Shri. Deependrasingh Kushwah, IAS and Shri. Kaustubh
Dhavse, chief Advisor (Investment) to Hon.Chief Minister have been invited by
ArcelorMittal Singapore Pvt. Ltd., Singapore between 04‘ December to 07th
December, 2025. The visit aims on understanding Maharashtra Government Policies
regarding investment and developing the ecosystem in order to develop global
competitiveness.
2. Accordingly, ArcelorMittal Singapore Pvt. Ltd., Singapore, this company 15
one of the leading investors in manufacturing space in India. Their investment in
Maharashtra are primarily in their steel plants located at Pune and Khopoli. Thus, the
outcome of this visit can help Maharashtra to improve and increase its global
investment and accelerate Maharashtra’s journey as an_ industry-first
destination.
3. Hon’ble Chief Minister of Maharashtra has approved the above delegation
visit to ArcelorMittal Singapore Pvt. Ltd., Singapore between 04th and 07th of
December, 2025. (including travel time).
RESOLUTION:-
Government of Maharashtra is now pleased to accord approval to the visit of
Dr. P. Anbalagan, IAS, Secretary, (Industries), Government of Maharashtra, Shri.P.
Velrasu, IAS, Shri. Deependrasingh Kushwah, IAS and Shri. Kaustubh Dhavse, chief
Advisor (Investment) to Hon.Chief Minister for the duration of 04 December to
07th December, 2025. (Including travel time).
2. The Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi vide their
letters dated 02.12.2025 have approved the said visit of delegation of Maharashtra to
ArcelorMittal Singapore Pvt. Ltd., Singapore.
# Page 2
3. The members of the official delegation will be entitled for travel expenses and
per diem allowance as per prescribed norms of Government of India.
4. State Government is further pleased to authorize the absence of Dr. P.
Anbalagan, IAS, Secretary, (Industries), Government of Maharashtra, Shri.P. Velrasu,
IAS, Shri. Deependrasingh Kushwah, IAS and Shri. Kaustubh Dhavse, chief Advisor
(Investment) to Hon.Chief Minister from 04‘ December to 07" December, 2025
(Including travel time) and be treated as on duty until they resume their duties on
return to India from their foreign tour.
5. State Government is pleased to approve the sanction for processing fees of visa
for Singapore, as per actual and medical facilities admissible, under the revised AMA
Scheme continued in the Ministry of External Affairs O.M.No.Q/GA/653/1/74 dated
5/3/1979 for the members of the delegation. The Government is also pleased to
approve the sanction charges for medical insurance for them.
6. The entire expenditure on travel and accommodation etc. for the members of
the delegation will be borned by Maharashtra Industrial development Corporation
(MIDC).
7. The detailed report for the tour and outcomes of the said tour with suggestions
if any should be submitted to the government within one month from the date of
returning.
