The Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board has been constituted under Section 18 of the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996. Under Section 7 of the Act, it is mandatory for building and other construction establishments to register on the Maha-online computer system within 60 days. Also, under Section 12, there is a provision to register construction workers working on building and other construction works with the Board. While approving the construction plan by the local bodies, Maharashtra Housing Area Development Authority, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority and Slum Rehabilitation Authority, a cess of 1% of the construction is collected and deposited with the Board under Section 3 of the Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Cess Act, 1996. The cess collected from the Board is being spent on various welfare schemes for registered construction workers.
For building and other construction works, the Local Self-Government Organizations, Maharashtra Housing Area Development Authority, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority, Slum Rehabilitation Authority do not provide the copy of the approval of the building and other construction plans and the copy of the building and other construction orders to the Board. Providing a copy of the plan approval and the work order to the registrar will facilitate the registration of the building and other construction establishment under section 7 of the Act and the registration of the construction workers on the said work with the Board under section 12 of the Act.
3) For this purpose, all the building and other construction sanctioning authorities in the state, municipal corporations, municipalities, other local bodies, Maharashtra Housing Area Development Authority, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority, Slum Rehabilitation Authority, etc.
