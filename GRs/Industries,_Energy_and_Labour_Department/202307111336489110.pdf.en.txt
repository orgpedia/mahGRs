It may be noted that non-compliance of the order passed with the approval of the competent authority under the Transfer Act is serious in nature and the officer/ employee who does not comply with this will be liable for suspension under Section 2.1 (2) (v) of Section-2 of the Departmental Inquiry Rules, 1991.
5) No leave should be granted to the concerned employee without the prior approval of the government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave applications of the transferred employees/ officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address by post.
6. It may be noted that in case of failure to join the post immediately as per the said order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
