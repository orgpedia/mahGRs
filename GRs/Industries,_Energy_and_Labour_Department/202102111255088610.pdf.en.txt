Reference No. As per the circular, necessary instructions have been given to implement the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Service Admission Rules for the employees of Mathadi and Security Guard Board by passing a resolution in the meeting of the Service Admission Rules Board. Also Reference No. As per the decision of the Government of India, the creation of posts of staff in the Mathadi and Security Guard Boards, which were already functioning, has been approved. For equivalent posts in the government, there should be uniformity in the service entry rules while approving the recruitment.

It has also been instructed that the service entry rules for the respective posts should be prepared by the boards with the consent of the Labour Commissioner.
Reference No. The recruitment to the post of Mathadi and Security Guard Board was approved during the period 2003-04 under the Government Decision No. 3 and 4.
Letter No. Under the letters of the Labour Commissioner's Office dated 5th, 6th and 7th, the draft guidelines of the Service Entry Rules have been submitted to the Government. The draft is a draft service rules and guidelines for recruitment of employees for the posts of Chief Accountant (Group-B Non-Gazetted), Personnel Officer (Group-B Non-Gazetted), Superintendent (Group-B Non-Gazetted), Accountant (Group-C), Assistant Accountant (Group-C), Cash Guard (Group-C), Senior Scribe (Group-C), Supervisor (Group-C), Typist (Group-C), Driver (Group-C), Short Typist (Group-C), Inspector (Group-C), Constable (Group-D). In this regard, instructions have been issued to all the Mathadis/ Security Guard Boards to take action to pass a resolution regarding the implementation of the Service Entry Rules vide G.O. No. UWA-2016/ P.S. No. 01/ Work-5, D.O. The letter dated 08.03.2019 has already been issued.
It has come to the notice of the Government that there is no uniformity in the service entry rules of the employees of Mathadi/ Suraksha Rakshak Boards, uncertainty in promotions in the Boards, non-adherence to point numbers, etc. In order to avoid this, it was under consideration of the Government to implement uniform service entry rules for the above mentioned posts in order to maintain equality and uniformity in the appointment, educational qualification, promotion of employees in Mathadi and Suraksha Rakshak Boards and to have uniformity in the service entry rules in all Mathadi/ Suraksha Rakshak Boards.
Accordingly, a) Service Entry Rules are being made applicable to all the Mathadi Boards in the State as per Annexure 1 to 10 attached to them, b) Service Entry Rules are being made applicable to all the Security Guard Boards in the State as per Annexure 11 to 20 attached to them and c) All the employees of Mathadi/ Security Guard Boards (except Group-D employees) are required to comply with the provisions of Government Circular No. 21/02/2009 making it mandatory to submit the statement of opinion and liability to the concerned Mathadi/ Security Guard Board.
