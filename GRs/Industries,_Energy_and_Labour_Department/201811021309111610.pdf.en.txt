The Proposal:
The government has decided to support investors in accordance with the state industrial policy. Integrated Facilitation Centre Maharashtra Industry, Trade and Investment Facilitation Centre (Maitri) has been established at Mumbai with effect from 27.2.2014. Through this Friendship Cell, all the services, information and guidance are made available to the investors at one place under the single window concept. This will ensure smooth and balanced industrial development of the state and attract more and more investments to the state. The government is considering advertising on television, radio, social media, audio, internet, etc. to make this friendship room famous. Accordingly, the Directorate of Industries proposed the expenditure with the approval of the Directorate of Information and Public Relations regarding the action and plan for these advertisements.
