Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,

Read 1) Government Decision No. Department, No. Saproyo-2014/ (Q. No.3) Industry-8/ D. 06.06.2014 Government Decision Subject to Government Decision Department of Industries, Power and Labour dated 06.06.2014, the concession of electricity duty waiver to all industries in Marathwada and Vidarbha region was paid from 01.04.2014 to 31.03.2019.
The industrial development of Marathwada and Vidarbha region is necessary for the overall development of the state and to empower the industries there to survive in the competitive era.
Considering this, the Government has decided to extend the waiver of electricity duty for five years from 01.04.2019 to 31.03.2024 to all industrial units in Vidarbha and Marathwada. Necessary notification in this regard will be issued by the Department of Energy.
This Government decision is in accordance with the opinion of the Finance Department in its informal reference No. Issued pursuant to MAMB 2019/ Q. No.6.
