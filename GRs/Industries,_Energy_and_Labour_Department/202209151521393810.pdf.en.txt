Regarding the formation of a committee in connection with the provision of land by the Revenue Department for solarization of agricultural feeders.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Resolution No: SOURP-2022/ PR No.155/ Energy-7
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madame Cama Road, Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032.
The date is September 15, 2022.
The proposal: The Deputy Chief Minister chaired a review meeting on the Solar Agriculture Channel Scheme at Sahyadri Guest House, Malabar Hill, Mumbai on Wednesday, 13th July, 2022 at 11.30 am. Following decisions were taken in the meeting:
" Action should be taken to make available the land required for solarization of agricultural feeders by the revenue department. The District Collectors of all the districts have been asked to identify the fallow land available with them for the scheme.
It should be done. All District Collectors have been informed by the government in this regard.
MSEDCL should set a target of 30% of agricultural feeders in each district to be solar by the end of this year and take action to provide space in coordination with the Revenue Department. "
2. The Government was contemplating to constitute a Committee under the Chairmanship of A.M.S. (Revenue) to complete the proceedings immediately in accordance with the said decision.
Government's decision:
It is hereby approved to constitute a Committee under the Chairmanship of A.M.S. (Revenue) with the following members to ensure that the land required for solarization of agricultural feeders under the Mukhyamantri Saur Krishi Vahini Yojana is made available by the Revenue Department at the earliest and accordingly to meet the target of 30% of agricultural feeders in each district on solar energy:
A.M.S. (Revenue), Chairman, Principal Secretary (Expenditure), Member Principal Secretary (Power), Member Principal Secretary (Forest), Member Principal Secretary (Agriculture), Member Secretary (Water Resources), Member Chairman and Managing Director, MSEDCL, Member Chairman and Managing Director, Member Director General, MAHATTI, Member Deputy Secretary, Power, Member Director, Projects, Member Secretary, MSEDCL
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: SOURP-2022/ PR No.155/ Energy-7
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows: 1) To make available the land required for solarization of agricultural feeders by the Revenue Department.
(ii) To determine the procedure/ policy for fixing the lease rate for the lease of private land for the Mukhyamantri Solar Krishi Vahini Yojana and other solar power projects, as well as how the lease rate can be linked to the quick count rate of the said land.
3) Coordinate with the Revenue Department regarding the availability of space so that 30% of the agricultural feeders in each district are solarized by March, 2023 through MSEDCL.
4) To make available the unutilized/ fallow land available with the private land, government land, corporations/ agricultural universities/ government departments for the solar channel scheme and other solar energy projects with the help of the Collector.
Term of office of the Committee: The term of office of the Committee shall be until the completion of the aforesaid proceedings.
