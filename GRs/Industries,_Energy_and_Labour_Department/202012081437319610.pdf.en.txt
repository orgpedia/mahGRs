Memorandum: The government has given a subsidy to the cashew processing industry in the state. The decision has been taken as per the Government Decision dated 15/07/2010. Also the. As per the Government decision dated 12/03/2013, it has been approved to continue the scheme by fixing the revised methodology of allocation of incentive subsidy to cashew processing industry. As per the scheme, after payment of 5% value added tax on sale of cashew nuts finally sold in the State out of the cashew nuts produced and processed in the State, the tax amount is being provided to the concerned units as incentive subsidy. The pending claims for reimbursement of incentives with the Government have been cleared in a phased manner as the financial provision under the scheme is inadequate. Out of the budgeted amount of Rs.5 crore for the year 2020-21, Rs.50 lakh has been made available for disbursement on BDS system for the first half of the year subject to a ceiling of 10%.

Funds have been disbursed to the industry units as per the Government Memorandum dated 23/07/2020. At present, 96 claims are pending with the Government and a sum of Rs. 2,03,65, 805/- is required to settle these claims. Therefore, to make the remaining funds available for disbursement out of the proposed fund, it has been allowed to spend within the limit of 30% by the Finance Department as per the request made by the Department. Accordingly, Rs. 1 crore has been allocated for the project.
In view of the funds available for the scheme at present, the Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore only) to 65 cashew processing units as mentioned in the last para of Annexure-A, B, subject to the condition that the provisions, criteria, eligibility etc. of the Government Decision/ Circular mentioned in Reference No. 1& 3 are verified/ ensured to be strictly implemented.
The expenditure for the said scheme should be met from the approved budgetary grants for the financial year 2020-21 under the head" Demand No.K-7, Account Head 2852-Industry (00) (12) Promotion of Cashew Processing Industries (Mandatory) (28520481), 33-Finance Assistance."
