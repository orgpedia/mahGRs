PREAMBLE
Vice President, Primus Strategic Management Consulting LLC, Dubai, has informed
that a roadshow event is being organized in Dubai, UAE, from 8th December to 18th
December, 2025. The Secretary (Industries) of Maharashtra was invited to this event, which
includes a two-day "Maharashtra Investment Roadshow" program during the aforementioned
period.
The event has provided a huge platform for showcase Maharashtra's investment
opportunities, major growth sectors, and strategic initiatives. aim to highlight this event to
curated audience of key Gulf investors, business leaders and institutional stakeholders.
2. The main objective of this program is to present investment opportunities, priority sectors, and key policy initiatives in the state, with a request that senior officials from the Government of Maharashtra lead the program.
It has been suggested that the program include presentations on various sectors (such as manufacturing, data centers, real estate, etc.) with the presence of senior government officials from Maharashtra, and that direct G2B meetings be organized with selected
companies in the UAE. Primus's team has stated that they will provide all necessary support for program planning, stakeholder engagement, and logistics management in Dubai.
3. Believing that participation in this program will help attract investment to Maharashtra and encourage young entrepreneurs to set up industries in the state, the Government of Maharashtra has accepted the invitation from Primus Strategic Management
Consulting LLC of Dubai and has decided to send a state-level delegation under the
chairmanship of Dr. P. Anbalagan, IAS, Secretary (Industries) to the Maharashtra Investment Roadshow program, scheduled for the period of 17 December to 20 December 2025 (including travel time) with approval of Chief Minister of Maharashtra.
RESOLUTION:-
Government of Maharashtra is now pleased to accord ex-post facto approval to the
visit of the State level delegation to attend "Maharashtra Investment Roadshow" held on 17th
Dec, 2025 to 20th Dec, 2025 programmes visits at Dubai, UAE.
