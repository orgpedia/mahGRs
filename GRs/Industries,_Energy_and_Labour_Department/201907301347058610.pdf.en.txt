Appointment of Grievance Redressal Officer in accordance with the provisions of Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour,
Government Order No.Movini-2019/ PR No. 102/ Energy-5
Ministry of Health, Mumbai 32.
Date: 30th of July 2019
Reference: (1) The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (2) along with the letter of the Secretary, Department of Industries, Power and Labour No. 29/07/2019.
the Government Order
As per the provisions of Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 of the Central Government, the offices of the Electrical Inspectorate are designated as Grievance Redressal Officers for receiving, maintaining records and redressal of grievances of persons with disabilities. The officers are hereby appointed as mentioned in the table below as per the instructions in Section 2.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A. No. | The name of the office                                                                                    | the designation of Grievance Redressal Officer                                                                |
| 1.     | Office of the Chief Inspector of Electricity, Mumbai                                                      | Office of the Chief Inspector of Electricity, Mumbai                                                          |
| 2.     | Office of the Superintending Engineer, Regional Electrical Inspectorate, Mumbai/ Pune/ Aurangabad/ Nagpur | the concerned Superintending Engineer, Regional Electricity Inspector Board, Mumbai/ Pune/ Aurangabad/ Nagpur |
| 3.     | All Electrical Inspectors Offices                                                                         | the concerned Electrical Inspector                                                                            |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
All the concerned offices should take action to give due publicity to this order.
