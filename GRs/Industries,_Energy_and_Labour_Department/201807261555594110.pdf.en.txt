2) General Manager, Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation No. 2018-19/474 dated 12th July, 2018
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2018-19, a sum of Rs. 4.29 crore (Rs. Four Crore Thirty-Nine Lakhs only) has been provided in the Budget for 31 Subsidiary Subsidies (Non-Salary) under the head Handicrafts Industry. The Department of Industry, Energy and Labour No. 70% of Rs. 299.60 lakhs (Rupees Two Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) has been made available to the Controlling Authorities.
2. General Manager, Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation, Mumbai vide their Reference No. Since the statement showing the plan of expenditure by letter of 2 is presented in A, B and C, Rs. 299.60 lakh (Rupees Two Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) is being sanctioned through this Government Resolution. This subsidy should be spent as indicated on the budget distribution system.
Expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the budget year 2018-19 under the targets Demand No.K-7," 2851-Village Industries and Small Scale Industries," 104 Handicrafts Industry, (00) (02) Development of Handicrafts Industry 31-Supported Grants (Non-salaried) and should be spent under the same head.
The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government and the Drawing and Disbursing Officers within the prescribed time. Also, when the accountant general, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai, is asked about the expenditure of the accounts, the board should submit the necessary documents related to the account to the office of the accountant general.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the authority conferred on the Administrative Department under Rule No. 149 of Part-I, Sub-Division-1, dated 17th April, 2015, of the Finance Division No.VYPR-2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations.
