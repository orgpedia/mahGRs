The revised pattern of posts in the establishment of the Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health is subject to the decision of the Government. As on 11.02.2021, a total of 316 posts (264 regular posts and 52 supernumerary posts) have been sanctioned.
In order to implement the" The Factories Act, 1948" properly and effectively, it has been decided to set up 2 new offices of Joint Director, Palghar and Deputy Director, Ratnagiri under the Directorate of Industrial Safety and Health. Also, considering the number of factories in the area, it has been decided to convert the office of Deputy Director, Satara into the office of Joint Director, Satara and the office of Joint Director, Akola into the office of Deputy Director, Akola. Accordingly, the reference. Statements A, B and C accompanying the Government Resolution dated 11.02.2021 are being amended.
Read the government decision here. Returns A, B and C with 11.02.2021 are being superseded. The revised prospectus- A, B and C are annexed.
