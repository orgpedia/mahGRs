Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation has submitted a total budget of Rs 2,45,20, 800/- for the works of the organization. The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) has examined the project map and budget of the said organization and has given technical approval to the same. Considering all these aspects, administrative approval is being accorded for the maps and budget for the works of the Institute at a total cost of Rs. 2,45,20, 800/- (Rupees Two Crore Forty Five Lakh Twenty Thousand Eight Hundred Only).
The increase in the project cost will not be acceptable to the organization. Additional expenses should be borne by the institution.
