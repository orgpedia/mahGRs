Introduction: - Reference No. By Government Circular No. 1, the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2024, as specified in column 4 of the Schedule, has been approved by the Legislature of the State of Maharashtra. The Governor has given his assent to it. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2023 was repealed by the Maharashtra Act No. 27 Therefore, in the Maharashtra Extraordinary Gazette Part-IV. Released on July 18, 2024.
Necessary provision has been made to the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board for the purpose of 36-Auxiliary Subsidy (Salary). The Department of Finance has made the subsidy available on the budget distribution system.
The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board was under the consideration of the government to provide grants to meet the expenditure on salaries, pension, gratuity, fractionation, capitalization, etc. of the officers / employees of the board's head office and district office for the months of January 2025 and March 2025 respectively.
Government Decision: - Under this Government Decision, the Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board is approved to disburse a subsidy of Rs. 90,77,73, 000 / - (Rupees Ten Crore, Seventy Seven Lakh, Seventy Three Thousand only) for the purpose of 36 - Auxiliary Grants (Salary) to meet the expenditure on salary, retirement pay, service gratuity, fractionation, capitalization, etc. of the officers / employees of the Head and District Office.
Expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for this budget year under the objectives of Demand No. K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (00) (01) Khadi and Village Industries Board Subsidiary Grants (Dattamat) (2851 0585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and should be spent under the same head.
3. The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the appropriation certificate regarding the expenditure of the subsidy to the Government and to the Drawing and Disbursing Authorities within the prescribed time. Also, the auditor general, Maharashtra-I, Mumbai, will be asked about the leakage of accounts.
