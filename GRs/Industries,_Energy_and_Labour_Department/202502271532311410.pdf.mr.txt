PREAMBLE
Vide letter dated 4/12/2024 Shri. Satish Siwan, Consulate of Dubai is planning to host an India
Week in Ras Al Khaimah from 28-30 January 2025. The 76th Republic Day celebrations of India will also
be organized in Ras Al Khaimah on 28 January 2025. Maharashtra was requested by The Ras Al Khaimah
side is keen on a business delegation led by the Hon'ble Chief Minister of Maharashtra. The preferred
sectors for the business delegation include steel, plastics, engineering, FMCG, automotive, and ports. The Ras Al Khaimah side is eager to sign a MoU with Maharashtra at the government-to- government (G2G) level and is looking forward to facilitating business-to- business (B2B) agreements during India Week.
2. In this background the State Government has decided to send Five member’s State level
delegation led by Hon'ble Minister ( Industries ), Government of Maharashtra to attend "India Week
Celebrations programme" held on 28™ January to 30" January, 2025 at Ras Al khaimah, UAE.
RESOLUTION:-
Government of Maharashtra is pleased to accord ex-post-facto approval to the visit of the
State level delegation led by Hon'ble Minister ( Industries ), Government of Maharashtra to attend
"India Week Celebrations programme" held on 28" January to 30" January, 2025 at Ras Al khaimah,
UAE.
