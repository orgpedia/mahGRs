The Proposal:
Electricity is supplied to the agricultural pump holders of the state at a discounted rate every year and the MSEDCL is given financial assistance to reimburse it. Further, to provide subsidy on power tariff to individual beneficiaries belonging to Scheduled Tribes under the Tribal Component Programme of the Tribal Development Department, the Tribal Development Department has sanctioned Rs. 72.79 crore in respect of the aforesaid Reference No. 5 5 is provided by the government decision here.
The matter of distributing this provision to the MSEDCL was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited has received Rs. 42,79,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Two Crores Eighty Nine Lakhs only) to MSEDCL.
At the divisional level, the Energy-5 and the MSEDCL should ensure that the amount of funds sanctioned under this decision is spent only on the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Tribes in the form of discounts on electricity rates. Also, it will be the responsibility of the MSEDCL to ensure that the funds are utilised for the purpose for which they have been sanctioned.
