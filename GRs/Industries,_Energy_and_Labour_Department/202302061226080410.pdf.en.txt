Preamble: In order to support the construction of projects under the Central Government's Micro and Small Enterprises-Collective Development Plan (MSE-CDP) and to enhance the cost-effectiveness of the projects, a scheme titled" Scheme of Subsidy to Industry Cluster Projects for Creation of Common Facilities of the State" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 09/06/2010. Further, as per the Government decision dated 22/10/2010, the State Government's participation under the scheme has been fixed at 10% of the project cost (upper ceiling subsidy limit Rs. 1.50 crore). Under the scheme, the Central Government vide its order dated 25/04/2022 has accorded final approval for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Precision Metatech Tooling Cluster, Bhosari G. Pune, and the State Government's participation grant under the project will be Rs. 1.49 crore.
As per the guidelines of the Micro, Small Enterprises-College Development Plan (MSE-CDP), the sanctioned grant of the Central Government is disbursed in the ratio of 40:30:30 to the projects sanctioned under the scheme. Also, for the disbursement of the grant sanctioned by the Central Government, the contribution of the Special Purpose Vehicle Society should be 100% and the contribution of the State Government should be distributed in proportion to the proposed grant of the Central Government.
However, as per the revised guidelines of MSE-CDP Scheme, if the participation of the Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) is 100% as per the project approval and if the State Government's participation grants are disbursed in higher share, then 50% of the Central Government's approved grant to the concerned project is disbursed in a single phase.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Central Government has accorded final approval for the construction of Common Facility Centre (CFC) at Precision Metatech Tooling Cluster, Bhosari G. Pune under Micro and Small Enterprises-College Development Plan (MSE-CDP) vide order dated 25/04/2022 at a total approved project cost of Rs.2211 lakh with Central Government grant of Rs. 1768.80 lakh and State Government participation grant of Rs. 149.91 lakh.
Accordingly, the Government have accorded approval for disbursement of State Participation Grant amounting to Rs. 1,04,93, 700/- (Rupees One Crore Four Lakh Ninety-nine Thousand Seven Hundred only) to Precision Metatech Tooling Cluster, Bhosari G. Pune as per the project sanction, out of the total amount of Rs. 149.91 Lakhs under Phase-I, as per the provisions of the said Scheme.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall arrange to transfer the amount of Rs. 1,04,93, 700/- (Rupees One Crore Four Lakh Ninety-nine Thousand Seven Hundred only) from the Government treasury to the Special Purpose Vehicle Institute of the Precision Metatech Tooling Cluster, Bhosari G. Pune. The powers to draw and disburse the aforesaid amount of Rs. 1,04,93, 700/- (Rupees One Crore Four Lakh Ninety Three Thousand Seven Hundred only) are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall take necessary action to monitor the progress of this special purpose vehicle and the proper utilization and utilization of the Government share capital.
Decision of the Finance Department No. 43/ Earth-3, dated 04/04/2022 and as per the provisions of the Annexure thereto and the information received from the Directorate of Industries accordingly certifies that the project of Precision Metatech Tooling Cluster, Bhosari G. Pune has been accorded final approval by the Central Government vide its order dated 25/04/2022. Government Circular No. SME-2010/ (1017)/ Industry-7, D.
