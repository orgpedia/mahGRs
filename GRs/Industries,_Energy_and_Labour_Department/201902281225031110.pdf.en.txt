Preamble: The Central Government has sanctioned Rs. Rs 400.00 crore. The Central Government vide its letter dated 18/10/2017 has approved the installation of a total of 7000 solar agricultural pumps with a total capacity of 2000 nos and 5000 nos vide letter dated 10/11/2017 on new terms and conditions. Considering the changes in the terms and conditions of the approval given by the Central Government for the installation of new solar agricultural pumps, changes in the implementation mechanism, changes in the criteria of the beneficiaries, the reference No. Under the decision of the government here, the implementation of Atal Solar Agriculture Pump Scheme-2 has been approved in the state. The scheme will be implemented by the Director General, Maharashtra Energy Development Agency (MAHAWARGA), Pune and as per the said scheme, the share of subsidy of the State Government in the allocation of solar agriculture pumps is 5% and the amount was considered to be Rs. 11.9963 crore.
Out of the total expenditure of Rs. 32.99835 crore, Rs. 65.50 crore from the Thirteenth Finance Commission and Rs. 42.9265 crore from the funds collected from the Additional Power Sales Tax (TOSE), the Government was considering to disburse the State Government's 5% share of Rs. 7,85,73, 250/- as demanded by the Maharashtra Energy Development Agency, Pune.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Order, the Government is pleased to disburse the State Government's share of subsidy for implementation of Atal Solar Krishi Pump Scheme-II@ 5% to the tune of Rs. 7,85,73, 250/- (Rupees Seven Crore Eighty Five Lakhs Seventy Three Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) to the Director General, Maharashtra Power Development Agency, Pune.
Walunj, Joint Secretary (Power), Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Mumbai as Controlling Officer and Shri N. R. Dhane, Under Secretary, Industries, Power and Labour Department, Ministry of Labour, Mumbai as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The amount should be paid in the name of Director General, Maharashtra Energy Development Agency, Pune.
Similarly, the Director General, Maharashtra Power Development Agency, Pune will be responsible for receiving the central financial assistance of Rs. 32.99835 crore from the Central Government before 31st March, 2019
Monthly and quarterly reports with information on expenditure, targets and actual achievement of the scheme under Atal Solar Krishi Pump Yojana-2 should be submitted by the 10th of the corresponding month. It is to be ensured by the Chief Minister that the amount sanctioned under this Government decision will be spent only as the State Government's share in the Atal Solar Krishi Pump Yojana-II for the financial year 2018-19.
The documents relating to the accounting of the said fund should be submitted by the Maharashtra Energy Development Agency to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai, for inspection, as and when required.
The expenditure in this regard should be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2018-19 under the head" Demand No-K-6, 2810, New and Renewable Energy 02, Solar 102, Light Volt (01) Solar Power Scheme for Installation of Agriculture Pumps (01) (01) Subsidy for Installation of Solar Power Pumps (State Share 5%) (Programme) 33, Financial Assistance (28100902)."
