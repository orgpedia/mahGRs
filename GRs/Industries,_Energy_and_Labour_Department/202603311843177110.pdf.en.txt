The Proposal: -
The Scheme "Scheme of Subsidy to Industry Cluster Projects for Creation of State Common Facility" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 09/06/2010 with a view to contribute to the project construction from the Micro and Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Scheme (MSE-CDP) of the Central Government and to increase the project's cost-effectiveness. Also, as per revised guidelines dated 24/05/2022 of MSE-CDP scheme, the limit of financial contribution grant of the State Government has been revised vide Government Decision dated 25/04/2023. Accordingly, State Government participation subsidy has been permitted for general category industrial cluster projects within a ceiling of 20 per cent of the project cost and for special category projects within a ceiling of 15 per cent.

According to the revised guidelines of the MSE-CDP scheme, the Textile Fashion Printing Cluster, Kagal, Kolhapur, a group project is required to receive the Central Government's first tranche grant of ₹8,95,53, ₹500 / - (50 per cent) with the State Government's participation grant to be disbursed in a single tranche or in proportion to the Central Government's proposed grant. Accordingly, the Directorate of Industries has proposed to disburse 50% of the State Government's first tranche grant of ₹1,91,90, 000 / - to the group project Textile Fashion Printing Cluster, Kagal, Kolhapur. Accordingly, the State Government was considering sanctioning 50 of the first tranche of grant-in-aid of Rs. 1,91,90 000 / - to Textile Fashion Printing Cluster, Kagal, Kolhapur, an industrial cluster project for the purpose of setting up a Common Facility Centre (CFC) for expeditious completion of projects in the State.
Government Decision: - The Central Government has accorded final approval under the Micro and Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Scheme (MSE-CDP) to the Industrial Cluster Project Textile Fashion Printing Cluster, Kagal, Kolhapur for the purpose of setting up a Common Facility Centre (CFC) vide order dated 23/09/2025. Accordingly, the total sanctioned project cost of the project is Rs. 2558.67 lakh, under which the Central Government grant is Rs. 1791.07 lakh and the State Government participation grant is Rs. 383.80 lakh. Accordingly, the industrial cluster project Textile Fashion Printing Cluster, Kagal, Kolhapur is being sanctioned under this Government Decision as per the project approval as well as the State Government participation grant amounting to Rs. 383.80 lakhs as per the provisions of the MSE-CDP Scheme and 50% of the cost of the first phase subsidy i.e. Rs. 1,91,90, 000 / - (Rupees One Crore Ninety One Lakh Ninety Five Thousand Only).
