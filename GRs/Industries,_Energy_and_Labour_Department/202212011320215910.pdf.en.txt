The Proposal:
The Ministry of Mines, Government of India has notified the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 2015 vide Government Notification dated 27.03.2015 by amending the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, 1957.
As per the provisions of Section 9 (B) of the present Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 2015, District Mineral Foundations are required to be established in every district of the State for the development of mining affected areas and individuals. In accordance with this provision, the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor, Government Notification No. As of September 1, 2016, the rules regarding the establishment, structure and functioning of the District Mineral Foundation in the state were prescribed.
