the prelude
The government has taken a decision. In pursuance of the amendments proposed in the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 vide order dated 6th March, 2018, the Government at the level of Labour Commissioner, Mumbai, has been requested to hold meetings with the concerned to resolve the representations/ complaints received from various trade unions and submit a final report to the Government. A tripartite committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Minister (Labour). The term of the said Tripartite Committee has been extended for a further period of three months with effect from 6th June, 2018. The tripartite committee. Government's decision
