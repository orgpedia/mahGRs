The Proposal:
In reference to the above. As per the decision of the Government dated 16/04/2021, the admission process for the course at N. M. Lokhande Maharashtra Institute of Labour Sciences has been revised. The procedure for admission to the Master of Labour Studies (MLS) course mentioned in this government decision has been mentioned.
As per the New Education Policy 2020, the Master of Labour Studies (MLS) has been renamed as Master of Human Capital Management and Employee Relations. The course will be conducted in four semesters spread over two years with the approval of the University of Mumbai and Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University. The new curriculum will be implemented from the academic year 2023-24. The new name of the course of the institute The revised course has been prescribed as per the New Education Policy 2020, so the matter of prescribing the admission process for the students taking admission in this post-graduate degree course was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Government Decision No.MLI-0519/ P.S. 23/ Work-6, Dy. The Director, Na. M. Lokhande Maharashtra Labour Science Institute, Mumbai is being approved by the Government to implement the educational qualification and admission process prescribed in 16/04/2021 for the Post Graduate Degree Course' Master of Human Capital Management and Employee Relations'.
