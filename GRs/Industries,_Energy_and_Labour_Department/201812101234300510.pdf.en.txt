The Proposal:
In order to effectively implement the Electricity Act, 2003 and the rules and regulations made thereunder, the Establishment of the Electricity Inspectorate Branch functioning under the administrative control of the Public Works Department vide Reference No. 3 It has been brought under the authority of the Department of Energy by a government decision. As the Electricity Inspectorate Branch has been transferred to the Energy Department, as per the decision of the Government, the option of inclusion of the officers/ employees working in both the departments as per their choice has been provided in the Reference No. 1 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor. 5 and Public Works Department No. 6 The decision has been approved by the government.
The consolidated Seniority List dated 01.01.2013 of the engineers of the cadre of Graduate Junior Engineer Group-B (Non-Gazetted)/ Branch Engineer Group-B (Gazetted) was published by the Chief Engineer (Electrical), PWD on 30/07/2013. Now that the Electricity Inspectorate Branch has been transferred to the Department of Power as mentioned in the above paragraph, the officers/ employees of the Electricity Branch of the Public Works Department and the Electricity Inspectorate Branch of the Department of Power have been transferred to that Department on the basis of their service willingness. Circular:
Junior Engineer Group-B (Non-Gazetted)/ Branch Engineer Group-B (Gazetted) The ad-hoc provisional seniority list as on 01.01.2018 is being published as per Annexure-A annexed.
The Chief Electrical Inspector shall inform all the heads of departments/ offices under his command about the receipt of the copies of the ad-hoc seniority list from the website and make the copies of the ad-hoc seniority list available to all the engineers of the cadre of Junior Engineer/ Branch Engineer under his command from the website.
The Chief Electrical Inspector shall inform all the heads of departments/ offices under him to bring the ad-hoc seniority list to the notice of all the concerned engineers. Also, if there are any errors, errors, or omissions in this seniority list, give it to the concerned. Before 27/12/2018, along with the proof of proof of birth certificate, copy of caste certificate, copy of record in service book, etc., the concerned office head and the Chief Electrical Inspector should inform the government in writing. Please note that action will be taken to finalize the said seniority list after considering the representations received by the Government till 24/12/2018.
