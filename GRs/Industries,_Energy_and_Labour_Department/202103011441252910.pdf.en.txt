Preamble: New and Renewable (Non-Conventional) Energy of the State under Non-Conventional Energy Generation Policy-2020 Part-I
Under the Integrated Policy for Transmission Linked Projects of Power Generation from Energy Sources-2020 and Part-II, the Integrated Policy for Non-Transmission Linked Projects of Power Generation from New and Renewable (Non-Conventional) Energy Sources of the State 2020 has been announced under the relevant Government Decision. A comprehensive strategy needs to be put in place to ensure smooth and effective implementation of the policy. On this In the meeting held on 16 th February, 2021 under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Power), it was decided to constitute a Committee to work out the modalities of the Policy and to constitute a Sub-Committee to assist the Committee to work out the modalities. Accordingly, the matter of forming a committee and a sub-committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A committee is being constituted to work out the modalities of the Non-Conventional Energy Policy-2020. The members of the committee will be: Principal Secretary (Power)- Chairman Chairman and Managing Director, Mahatrans company- Member Chairman and Managing Director, MSEDCL- Member Chairman and Managing Director, Mahanirmiti company- Member Chairman, Maharashtra State Electricity Board, Sutradhari Company Limited.
