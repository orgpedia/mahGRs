Self Certification-cum-Consolidated Annual Returns Scheme for Factories and Establishments in the State of Maharashtra
GOVERNMENT OF MAHARASHTRA
INDUSTRIES, ENERGY & LABOUR DEPARTMENT
Government Corrigendum No. MISC- 2015/CR- 76/Desk Lab-9
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032.
Date - 25 November, 2020.
Read :- Government Resolution, Department Of Industry Energy and Labour No.Misc-2015/C.R.No.76/Lab-9, dated 23/06/2015.
Introduction: A Self-Certification-Cum-Consolidated Annual Returns Scheme for Factories and Establishments in the State of Maharashtra was introduced vide Government Resolution dated 23/06/2015. In the said Government Resolution Fees and Security Deposits were prescribed. The exclusion of Security Deposit was under consideration of Government of Maharashtra.
Accordingly, following corrigendum in being issued.
Corrigendum:
In the Government Resolution date 23/06/2015, the provision of Security Deposit prescribed under paragraph no 5.2 is hereby deleted.
