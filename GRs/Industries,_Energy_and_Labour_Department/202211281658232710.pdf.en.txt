Preamble: The Central Government has decided to provide interest free loans to the States under the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Expenditure. In this regard, the Central Government vide reference No. 1 here. The guidelines have been issued vide Government letter dated 06/04/2022. Under this scheme, the government was considering the creation of a new accounting head to provide interest-free loans received from the central government for infrastructure projects under PM Gati Shakti.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has approved the creation of separate head of accounts for the expenditure on non-interest bearing loans received for infrastructure projects under PM Gati Shakti under the Central Government's Special Assistance to States for Capital Expenditure Scheme.
