Government's stand:
Paragraph No. 1 It is hereby stated that the implementing agency for the works under the Mineral Development Fund in Nagpur District shall be the Zilla Parishad, Works Department, Nagpur. Instead, the district council will be the implementing agency for the works in Ramtek, Parsivani and Mauda taluka.
