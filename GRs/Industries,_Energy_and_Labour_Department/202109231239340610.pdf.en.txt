Section 12 (2) of the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996, empowers the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board to authorise the registration of construction workers as the Registering Officer. According to reference no. 1 As per the Government decision dated 02.01.2018, the Block Development Officers of Rural Development Department, Panchayat Samitis (all), Ward Officers of Urban Development Department, Mahanagar Palika (all), Chief Officers, Municipal Councils (all), Deputy Engineers (all) of Public Works Department, Deputy Engineers (all) of Water Conservation Department, Deputy Engineers (all) of Water Supply Department and Labour Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Additional Labour Commissioner, Deputy Commissioner of Labour, Assistant Labour Commissioner (all), Labour Development Officer (all), Labour Welfare Officer (all), Government Labour Officer, Labour Investigator and Facilitator (Inspector of Shops) have been declared as Registered Officers. However, with the Board passing a resolution in its 55th meeting dated 13.07.2020 to start construction worker registration through online mode, it has become easier to register construction workers at all levels in the State. 1 The matter of change in the Registering Officers declared under the Government Decision dated 02.01.2018 was under consideration of the Government.
Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board passed a resolution in the 55th meeting of the Board to start the registration of construction workers online. The Government decision dated 02.01.2018 is hereby superseded. As per Section 12 (2) of the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996, the following officers of the Labour Department are being declared as Registration Officers for registration of building and other construction workers.
