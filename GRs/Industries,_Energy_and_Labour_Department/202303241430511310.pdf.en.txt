Introduction: The above reference No. 01 In pursuance of the Government decision, the Government have approved the constitution of Selection Committees for filling up of the posts of Group-C cadre (excluding the posts under the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) in the establishment of the Director, Directorate of Government Printing, Stationery and Publications, Mumbai at the regional and state level with direct service entry.
Reference No. 2. As per the decision of the Government herein, a consolidated guideline has been prescribed for direct recruitment to the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the nomination quota under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board (outside the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission). The government has approved the recruitment process through TCS (Tata Consultancy Private Limited) and IBPS (Institute of Banking Personnel Selection) to fill up the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D cadres in the nomination quota through direct, competitive examination process.
Reference No.3. Under the guidelines issued under the government decision here, the government was considering updating the composition of the state-level selection committees to fill the state-level posts.
Government's decision:
A State Level Selection Committee is being constituted to conduct the recruitment process for direct recruitment to the posts in the nomination quota in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) at the establishment of the Director, Directorate of Government Printing, Stationery and Publications, Mumbai and at the establishment of Government Printing Presses, Government Libraries and Government Stationery Stores under the Directorate.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows: (a) The procedure of selection of candidates for filling up the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board (outside the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission) at the establishment of the Directorate of Government Printing, Stationery and Publications, Mumbai, as well as at the establishment of Government Printing Presses, Government Libraries and Government Stationery Depots under the Directorate, shall be made mandatory in conformity with the provisions of the Government Decision referred to in Reference No. 2 and 3 of the General Administration Department and the orders issued in this behalf from time to time.
(b) The Committee shall complete the process of filling up the posts in the direct service quota under consideration within two months from the date of issue of the said Government Resolution.
