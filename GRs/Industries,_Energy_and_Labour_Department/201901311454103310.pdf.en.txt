Government's decision:
Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2018, a total amount of Rs. 865.84 lakh (Rupees Eight Crores Sixty Five Lakhs Eighty Four Thousand only) has been provided to Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai for the period of two months i.e. October and November, 2018 for 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 819.36 lakh and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 46.48 lakh.
The Financial Advisor and Chief Accounts Officer, Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, Mumbai vide their Reference No. The 3rd letter has requested to provide a total grant of Rs. 1648.05 (Rupees Sixteen Crores Forty Eight Lakhs Five Thousand only) for a period of four months from December 2018 to March 2019 for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 1638.75 lakhs and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 9.30 lakhs. In accordance with the aforesaid request of the Board, a sum of Rs. 1648.05 lakh is being sanctioned to the Board.
Expenditure on this should be met from the budgetary provision approved for the financial year 2018-19 under the targets of Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (01) Subsidiary Grants to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (2851 0585) 36, Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) and 31-Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaries) and should be spent under the same head. 36, the Finance Department has issued an Informal Reference No. 7/2019/ Expenditure- 16 dated 19/1/2019 has been agreed. Accordingly, the remaining 30% of the subsidy has been disbursed on the Budget Distribution System.
The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board has prescribed the utilization certificate to the Government and the Drawing and Disbursing Officers regarding the expenditure of this grant.
