Government Circular -
The Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion (DIPP) of Government of India as a part of Business Reforms Action Plan (BRAP) from 2015-16 has suggested inspection reforms primarily based on the principles of transparency, streamline risk based regulation and automation i.e. uploading procedures, mandating time lines, risk rating of business automating process etc.
As a part of the Business Reforms Action Plan (BRAP) from 2018-19, The Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion has proposed every State to develop a single integrated annual return and mandate its submission along with all the applicable payments under various labour laws online.
The State Government has also adopted the Ease of Doing Business practices in accordance to its Make in Maharashtra campaign. The Labour Department has adopted and implemented various business reforms suggested by the DIPP as per the Business Reforms Action Plan. The registration, license and amendment to the registration certificate or the license are made available online. The office of the Commissioner of Labour has also developed a single integrated annual return under various labour laws. The annual return and the services are deployed on the LMS Portal.
Therefore, all the users are hereby directed to avail all the services online through e-payment and must also submit online the single integrated annual return under various labour laws. The list of Labour Acts under which the single integrated annual return is to be submitted online is enclosed as Annexure-1
