As per the policy, all municipalities, municipal corporations, development authorities, gram panchayats, Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC), zilla parishad, public works department and all government/ semi-government departments will have to install only LED street lights for street lights.
To take stock of this, it is proposed to first pilot the LED street light project for the lighting scheme in the areas of Municipalities and Gram Panchayats falling under Chandrapur and Nagpur districts. A total of 58 villages in Chandrapur district and 31 villages in Nagpur district will be covered under the project. The project is estimated to cost Rs. 53.25 crore.
In 2018-19, Rs. A sum of Rs. 53.25 crore has been provided in the Budget 2018-19 through Supplementary Demands for Grants. To receive the said funds, the proposal to give administrative approval to the pilot project for the implementation of LED street lights for the lighting scheme in the area of 58 and 31 village gram panchayats of Chandrapur and Nagpur districts respectively was under consideration of the government.
