A committee has been formed to prepare a new logistics policy for Maharashtra.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Circular No:- MLP-2023/ PR No. 01/ Industries-2
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 8, 2023
circular
The Logistic Park Policy of the State has been declared under the Government Decision dated 14/02/2018. The Central Government has announced the National Logistics Policy dated 28/09/2022 and as per the policy of the Central Government, it is necessary to formulate a new policy by revising the Logistics Park Policy of the State.
The following Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Development Commissioner (Industries) to study the provisions of the National Logistics Policy of the Central Government and make recommendations to the State Government for preparing a new Logistics Policy of the State.

The mandate of the Committee shall be as follows: 1. The Committee shall examine and prepare its report on the provisions of the National Logistics Policy of the Central Government, the policies and provisions of other States in the logistics sector, the existing provisions of the State Policy and the amendments to be made.
The committee will submit its report to the government within three months.
