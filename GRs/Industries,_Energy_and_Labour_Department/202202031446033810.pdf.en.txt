Pursuant to the Government letter dated 01.06.2021, the Directorate of Industries vide reference No. 3 here. The guidelines have been issued vide circular dated 09.06.2021. In these guidelines, the definition of Liquid Medical Oxygen (LMO)/ Air Separation Unit (ASU) as well as Cylinder manufacturing units mentioned under Fixed Capital Investment (FCI) in paragraph 2 has been included as follows:
i. Liquid Medical Oxygen (LMO)/ Air Separation Unit (ASU) :-Oxygen Plant Manufacturing, Manifold, Tankers, Refilling & Filling Storage Tanks and Cryogenic Transport Tankers.
ii. Cylinder Manufacturing :- Cylinders, Cryogenic Storage Tanks & Cryogenic Transport Tankers.
The, however. In the Government Resolution dated 21.05.2021, vide Para No. (a) Some of the above items are not included in clause (ii), which creates ambiguity. In order to clear the confusion, the No. 1 of the Government Resolution No. a) It is being approved to include some of the above mentioned items in 9- (iii). Reason (a) 9- (iii) should be read as follows:
Plant and Machinery including Manifold, LMO Transport Tankers, Cylinders for Oxygen filling and Refilling, Oxygen Plant Manufacturing, Cylinder Manufacturing: Cylinder, Cryogenic Storage Tanks& Cryogenic Transport Tankers. The storage capacity of oxygen generation plants of tonnage and above shall be 5 times the installed storage capacity.
