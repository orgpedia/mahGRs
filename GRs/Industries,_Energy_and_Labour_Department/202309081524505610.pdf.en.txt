To review the suspension of Group-A and Group-B officers under the Department of Energy, Reference No. 1 The suspension review committee has been formed under the government decision here.
A meeting of the suspension review committee was held to review the suspension of the officials mentioned in the above statement. Completed on 09.08.2023. As per the decision taken by the committee in the meeting, it has been recommended to end the suspension of the above officers and reinstate them in non-executive posts in the government service. GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: MOVINI-2022/ PR No.82/ ENERGY-1
The officers who are being reinstated in the government service under this order should join the place of posting within 7 days, otherwise, if they do not join the post on time, this period will be considered as an inactive day. The report should also be submitted to the government through the Chief Electrical Inspector, Mumbai. The suspension of the officer shall be deemed to have come to an end from the date on which he enters the office of the post holder.
Orders regarding regularization of the period of suspension of the said officers will be issued as soon as the final decision of the court case and departmental inquiry against them is made.
