Minutes of the 17/7/2020 meeting.
A proposal was submitted to the government to create 599 posts in various cadres required for fire stations, fire headquarters, 4 regional offices in 24 industrial estates of the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation. Out of which, 469 posts in various cadres have been created in the first phase with the approval of the Finance Department. The decision was approved by the government.
The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation has submitted a proposal to the government to approve the creation of 130 posts, including the post of Joint Chief Fire Officer, for the remaining 7 fire stations. As informed by the Finance Department, the Sub-Committee of Additional Chief Secretary (Services) and Secretary (Expenditure) constituted under the Finance Department Government Resolution dated 9th June, 2017 in its meeting held on 14th February, 2020 had recommended to place the proposal for creation of the post before the High Level Committee of Secretaries. Accordingly, a high-level committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary was constituted. In the meeting dated 17/07/2020, when the proposal for the creation of this post was presented, the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the creation of 130 posts in various cadres for 7 fire stations in the fire department of Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation. As per the approval given by the high-level committee of secretaries and the finance department, the matter of issuing a government decision regarding the creation of the post was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the creation of the following mentioned 130 posts in various cadres in the second phase required for 7 fire stations under the Fire Department of Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation under the administrative control of the Industries Department.
