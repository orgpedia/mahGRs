The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board (MSKVB) provides 100% subsidy to the Board every year through budgetary provision for recurring and non-recurring expenditure on establishment (including salary allowances of working and retired employees) of the Board.
The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board (MSKVB) Employees' Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 1988, have come into force with effect from 8th December, 1989. The Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 regarding retirement pension applicable to State Government employees have been implemented by the Government with effect from 24th February, 1982. Accordingly, the government was considering to implement the revised pay structure and pension for the employees of the board and the retirement pay holders in line with the Seventh Pay Commission.
The Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 have been made applicable to the State Government employees with effect from 1st January, 2016 vide Government Notification, Finance Department No.Vepur 2019/ PR No. 01/ Service-9, dated 30th January, 2019.
