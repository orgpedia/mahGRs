Preamble: In the designation of the post of Government Labour Officer on the establishment of Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Boards, Reference No. 2 Change in the name of Deputy Executive Officer (Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board) by the Government
has been done. However, since the post of Deputy Executive Officer (Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board) mentioned in the Government decision is a junior post adjacent to the post of District Executive Officer (Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board), the Government was considering changing the designation of the post of Deputy Executive Officer (Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board) to" Deputy District Executive Officer (Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board)." The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The post of Deputy Executive Officer (Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board) on establishment of Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board is being renamed as" Deputy District Executive Officer (Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board)."
