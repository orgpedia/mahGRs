Read:- 1) Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, No. S.B.E- 03/2010/ S.No.8/ Workers-1, Dy. February 8, 2011.
2) Letter from the Director-in-Charge, Directorate of Vapour No. Cell-4/ Accounts Branch/ 2018/ 17014, dated 18.08.2018 and Letter No. SB-4 or GL. 127/2018/22787, dated 20.11.2018.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: To strengthen the Directorate of Vapour and to effectively implement the Vapour Act, under this scheme, an amount of Rs. 11,20,000/- (Rs. 11.20 lakh only), for conducting Seminars in Nashik and Kolhapur Divisional Offices in connection with creating awareness about the Indian Liquor Act, 1923 (as amended in 2007), Rs. 5,60,000/- is being authorized to the concerned Controlling Officers in the Directorate of Vapour.
2.The amount should be spent as a monthly fund distribution letter. Also, financial limits and existing rules and regulations should be strictly followed in this regard.
The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount are being delegated to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the concerned office under the Director, Directorate of Liquor, Mumbai.
4. Expenditure for the above schemes, Demand No. K-4, Head Account head 2230 Labour and Employment, 01 Labour, 102 Working Conditions and Safety, (00) (15) Strengthening of Directorate of Vapour and effective implementation of Vapour Act (22305784).
Also, the certificate of utility should be submitted to the government.
5. No objections by the Accountant General for the purposes hereof. However, this Government decision is in contravention of the provisions of the Finance Department Circular No. Finance-2018/ P. No. 69/ Finance-3, No. The instructions issued under 2.4.2018 are being issued subject to strict compliance.
