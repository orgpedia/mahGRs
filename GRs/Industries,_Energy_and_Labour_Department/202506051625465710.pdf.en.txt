Government decision, d. The Logistics Policy 2024 of the state of Maharashtra has been announced. Under the Logistics Policy 2024 of the State of Maharashtra, an amount of Rs. 7,000 crore for capital subsidy for small, large, mega, ultra-large, integrated truck terminals. 635 crore as well as interest rate subsidy to single entities and incentives for technology support amounting to Rs. 675 crores for the implementation of the present policy by creating two separate heads of accounts under this subject with a total outlay of Rs. 8,310 crore has been allocated. TESST was under the consideration of the government to open two new headings of accounts to make this provision available.
The Government is hereby approving the opening of a new Head of Accounts for providing grants for implementation of infrastructure projects of Logistics ASS as well as Logistics Hubs under the Logistics Policy-2024 of Maharashtra.
