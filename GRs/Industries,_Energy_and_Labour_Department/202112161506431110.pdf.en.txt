Introduction Regarding the wages and conditions of service of the workers in the sugar factories in the state of Maharashtra. To make recommendations/ suggest measures to the Government and the Tripartite Committee constituted under the Government Resolution dated 12.11.2020. As per the Government decision dated 04.03.2021, a four-member committee has been constituted. There was no mention of the chairman of the committee in the resolution. The first term of the committee was three months. This period expired on 03.06.2021. This period has been extended as per the Government Decision dated 07.07.2021. for a period of 06 months from 04.06.2021 i.e. This has been extended till 03.12.2021. The committee 06.05.2021 and D. The meetings were held on 17.05.2021 and due to COVID restrictions and other administrative engagements, the said Committee could not meet. However, the committee has not yet been formed.
Currently, the committee is in session. The Labour Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has requested for an extension for another 6 months with effect from 04.12.2021. Accordingly, the Chairman of the Committee shall be appointed. The matter of extension of 06 months beyond 04.12.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION The Government of Maharashtra has approved the extension of 6 months from 04.12.2021 for submission of report to the Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 04.03.2021 for making recommendations/ suggesting measures to the Government and the Tripartite Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 12.11.2020 regarding the demands of sugar factory workers in the State.
Also, the Sugar Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Pune, who is a member of the said Committee, is being declared as the Chairman of the said Committee. The other provisions of the Government Order dated 04.03.2021 shall continue to remain in force.
