With a vision to make Maharashtra a preferred destination for indigenous/ Indian made manufacturing in Space and Defence sector, promote indigenous and modern technology capability building, create world class skilled manpower and empower Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in Space and Defence sector for a globally competitive environment, the Government of Maharashtra has announced the New Space and Defence Sector Manufacturing Policy-2018 vide Govemment dated 14/02/2018. The policy will be for a period of 5 years ending on 13/02/2023. A new government policy is being drafted to address this issue. A Committee under the Chairmanship of Development Commissioner (Industries) is being constituted to make recommendations to the Government for formulation of new Space and Defence Sector Production Policy of Maharashtra.

The mandate of the Committee shall be as follows: (i) A detailed study of the representations received for formulation of policy on defence production, the policy/ guidelines of the Central Government in this regard and the policy of other States and related matters shall be made by the Committee and a detailed report shall be prepared accordingly.
The committee will submit its report to the government within three months.
