Preamble: Reference No. Under the scheme of" Financial Assistance to Power Distribution and Transmission License Holders" every year under the Government Decision 1 and 2, electricity is supplied at a discounted rate and financial assistance is provided to MSEDCL for its reimbursement. Since the scheme is meant for individual beneficiaries, the demand for grant of financial assistance to MSEDCL for grant of concession in power tariff to the individual beneficiaries belonging to Scheduled Tribes under this scheme from the funds received under the Tribal Component Programme henceforth is vide Demand No. The matter of opening a new account under T-05, head account head 2801 electricity was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Under the Tribal Component Programme, approval is being given for opening of new head of accounts for financing MSEDCL for the scheme" Financial Assistance to Power Distribution and Transmission License Holders for Reduction of Agricultural and Machinery Tariff."
