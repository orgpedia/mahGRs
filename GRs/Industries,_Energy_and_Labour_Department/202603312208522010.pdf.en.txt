The Proposal: -
A High Powered Committee under the Chairmanship of the Additional Chief Secretary has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 12/09/1984 to approve the expenditure budget for the India International Trade Fair with the participation of the State Government. Accordingly, the committee plans and organizes the event. The expenditure for the programme is provided from the funds available in the financial year and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer is the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. Thereafter, as per the Government decision dated 27/08/1996, the said High Powered Committee has been reconstituted and constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary (Trade & Commerce), earlier headed by Additional Chief Secretary.
India International Trade Fair is organized by India Trade Promotion Organisation, New Delhi every year at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi during the month of November. The Maharashtra Pavilion is organised / set up by the Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation on behalf of the Government of Maharashtra as per the theme decided by the Trade Promotion Organization of India. The theme for this year's India International Trade Fair is "Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat" and a meeting of the High Powered Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Industries) was held on the date 28/08/2025 regarding the Maharashtra Pavilion of the Fair. For the said purpose, an estimated expenditure of Rs. 4,95,0, 000 / - has been approved in the meeting.
According to a letter dated 26/02/2026 by the Directorate of Industries, the actual expenditure incurred by the Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation for organizing / setting up the Maharashtra Pavilion at the fair was Rs. 4,08,36, 144 / - and the expenditure incurred by the Corporation for the publicity of the Maharashtra Pavilion at the India International Trade Fair-2024, however, not received by the Government, was Rs. 4,42,201, 345 / - against the total expenditure of Rs. 4,12,78, 345 / -.
It is proposed to provide funds with administrative approval. For this reason, the Government was considering approving the expenditure of the fund of 4,12,78, 345 / - for this purpose.
