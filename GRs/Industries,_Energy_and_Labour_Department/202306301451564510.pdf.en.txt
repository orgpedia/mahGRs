(2) The Chief Inspector of Electricity, Mumbai shall relieve the transferred officers from their present posts immediately by making necessary local alternative arrangements so that they can take charge of their transferred places immediately and they may be asked to join the transferred places immediately.
(3) The officers concerned shall ensure that they join the place of posting within the prescribed period as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Duration of Appointment, Non-Acceptance of Service and Removal of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. If he does not join the post, the period of his absence will be treated as" non-working day." Further, Government Resolution No. 2016/ No. 186/16/ Office- 14, D. The concerned should note that disciplinary action will be taken as per the provisions of 23.12.2016.
The above officers will be paid the period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules.
5. The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government. Also, as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address by post.
(6) If he does not join the post of the post immediately as per the said order or tries to cancel the transfer and change it by putting any kind of pressure, this action will be taken by the Maharashtra Civil Service (Conduct).
