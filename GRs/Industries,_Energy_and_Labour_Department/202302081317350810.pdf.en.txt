The Government of Maharashtra has taken the decision. MODO- 2014 118/ Udyog-2, dated 11/04/2016, the State's Maharashtra Electronics Policy-2016 and the Incentive Policy for FAB Project under it has been announced. The tenure of this policy has been fixed at 5 years and was to expire on 10/04/2021. In the meantime, a decision has been taken to amend this policy and extend the said policy till 31/03/2023. Cabinet of Ministers has been taken up in the meeting held on 06/01/2021. The Government decision in this regard has been issued on 20/01/2021 and the said policy will expire on 31/03/2023. The process of formulating a new electronics policy of the state is underway at the government level. A committee under the chairmanship of Development Commissioner (Industries) is being constituted to make recommendations to the State Government in formulating the new policy.

The mandate of the Committee shall be as follows: 1) A detailed report shall be prepared by the Committee based on the representations received regarding formulation of policy on manufacturing of electronic products, policy/ guidelines of the Central Government on the same and other States on the same and in consultation with the stakeholders in the sector and an in-depth study of the relevant aspects shall be carried out by the Committee.
The committee will submit its report to the government within three months.
