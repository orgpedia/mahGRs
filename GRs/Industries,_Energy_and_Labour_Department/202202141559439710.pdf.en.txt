Regarding constitution of a Departmental Review Committee to examine the eligibility/ ineligibility of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers on establishment of Directorate of Steam, to continue in service beyond the age of 50/55 years or on completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour,
Government Order No.Vaapke-0222/ PR No. 38/ Labourer-1
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: February 14, 2022.
Reference:- General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. LPL-2017/ Q. No.21/ Q. Fifteen, d. 10.6.2019
The order:
As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, it is the policy of the Government to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them prematurely from Government service.
In pursuance of the said policy, the Departmental Review Committee is being constituted as per the provisions of the relevant Government Decision to review and recommend the Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers on establishment of the Directorate of Vapour, beyond the age of 50/55 years or after 30 years of qualifying service, to exercise their eligibility (eligible/ ineligible) to remain in service.
