(4) The ad-hoc promotion to the said officer is subject to the assumption that no departmental inquiry has been initiated or proposed to be initiated against him till the date of the order.
(5) The documents given at the time of appointment of the said promoted officers and thereafter, at the prescribed time and opportunity, the necessary professional examination, computer examination, Hindi language examination, Marathi language examination, etc.
The promotion is being given subject to the assumption that the pass or exemption from passing is permissible as per the rules and proper records are taken in the service book and they are as per the rules.
(6) If the said officer does not accept the net temporary promotion to the post of Assistant Electrical Inspector, Group-A (Gazetted), the Government Decision, General Administration Department, No. SRV-2015/ PR No. 303/ Office-12, D. Action will be taken as per the provisions of 12.09.2016.
(7) The Chief Inspector of Electricity, Mumbai shall, after making local alternative arrangements, relieve the officer appointed by promotion forthwith and instruct him to attend the place of new appointment immediately. Also, the Chief Electrical Inspector, Mumbai, should immediately submit a report to the government that the concerned officer has joined the post of promotion.
(8) The above officer appointed by the promotion shall join immediately at the place of his new appointment. They should not go on leave unauthorisedly or submit a representation/ application for change in posting. In doing so, they will be liable for disciplinary action for violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
(9) The benefits of promotion shall be admissible to the above officers from the date on which they join the office of promotion.
