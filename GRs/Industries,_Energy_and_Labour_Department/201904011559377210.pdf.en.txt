How to apply: (i) The registered women based Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) engaged in the production of products which have participated in the State Level/ International Exhibition shall be required to submit an application in the prescribed format, information regarding the exhibition, self-certified affidavit, etc. to the District Industries Centre of the district in which the unit is functioning, within a maximum period of 3 months from the last date of the exhibition for reimbursement of their dues. The application will be sent to the Directorate of Industries within 7 days from the receipt of the application from the District Industries Center through the concerned Divisional Office.

ii) The specified Organizers who are to organize the Zonal/ State level Exhibition for Women Entrepreneurs are required to submit the application in the prescribed format, information regarding the Exhibition, self-attested affidavit etc. to the Office of the Joint Director of Industries of the Department in which the relevant Exhibition is to be held, at least 3 months before the date of the scheduled Exhibition. The concerned Divisional Office shall submit the application along with suitable recommendation to the Directorate of Industries for further action within 7 days from the date of receipt of application. The application will be placed before a committee headed by the Development Commissioner (Industries). If approved by the committee, the organizer will be eligible for incentives based on the availability of funds.
(ii) The registered women based Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) engaged in manufacturing, which have participated in the State level/ International Exhibition, shall be required to submit an application in the prescribed format, information regarding the exhibition, self-certified affidavit, etc. to the District Industries Centre of the district in which the unit is functioning, within a maximum period of 3 months from the last date of the exhibition, for reimbursement of their dues. The application will be sent to the Directorate of Industries within 7 days from the receipt of the application from the District Industries Center through the concerned Divisional Office.
