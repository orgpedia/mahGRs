Reference: Director (Project) General Transport Company, vide letter No. Maravipakam/ Sanka/ Pa. Q/ 3791, D. 11.04.2017
the order
I am directed to inform you, in pursuance of the letter of reference of the Mahatransport Company relating to the above subject, that the Government approval is being accorded under the conditions mentioned above under Section 68 of the Electricity Act, 2003 for the installation and maintenance of transmission lines under the scheme approved by the Mahatransport Company and mentioned in the prospectus in the Annexure (Annexure A, B, C).
The construction of power lines shall be carried out (as the case may be) by the General Transport Company or the concerned private power generation plant holder at their own cost in the prescribed standard manner and under the guidance and supervision of experts. Also, the concerned private power generation plant holder will transfer the transmission lines to the Mahanagar Transmission Company for operation and maintenance work after the completion of the transmission line construction works.
2. The operation and maintenance of the power lines shall be done by the Mahatransport Company itself.
(3) It shall be incumbent upon the General Transport Company or the consumer concerned to use the power lines only for the purpose for which they are intended by the General Transport Company or the consumer concerned.
Prior approval of the competent authority shall be required before commissioning/ commissioning of the power lines.
It shall be the responsibility of the Mahatransport Company or the concerned consumer to resolve the grievances of the concerned land owners/ farmers, if any, after the installation or installation of the power lines, in consultation with the competent authority.

The Company shall be bound to obtain all necessary licences for the erection and maintenance of the said power lines, fulfill the prescribed norms and other prevailing rules of various authorities/ departments/ Electricity Regulatory Commission, Load Transmission Centre, etc., as approved by the Government from time to time.
The terms and conditions of Section 68 of the Electricity Act, 2003 and its modifications from time to time, as well as the terms and conditions of the Government Decision No. 2016/ PR No. 520/ Energy-4 of this Division, dated 31.05.2017 and the guidelines and policies of the State Government and the Central Government issued/ issued from time to time in respect of High Pressure Transmission Channels shall be applicable to the said transmission channel.
The period of such licence shall be 25 years from the date of issue of the licence, after which such permission shall automatically lapse.
