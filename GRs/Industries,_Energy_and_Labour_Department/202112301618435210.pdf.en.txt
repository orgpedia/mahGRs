Preamble: In order to ensure smooth power supply to the State by empowering the transmission lines in the State, the Director (Projects) (In-Charge), Maharashtra State Power Transmission Company Limited, Mumbai has issued Reference No. In pursuance of the aforesaid letter, the Government has been requested to grant prior approval under Section 68 of the Electricity Act, 2003 for the construction of 132/33 KV MIHAN sub-station in Nagpur district. The proposal was then approved by the government.
Government Decision: The work of construction of 132/33 KV MIHAN sub-station in Nagpur district will be done by Mahatrans company as per Annexure-A attached.
For the construction of new transmission lines/ sub-stations mentioned in Annexure-A, the Maharatna Company is being granted approval by the Government under Section 68 of the Indian Electricity Act, 2003 subject to the following terms and conditions: 1. The installation of power lines/ sub-stations in Annexure-A shall be carried out by the Maharatna Company at its own cost in the prescribed standard manner and under the guidance and supervision of experts.
2. The operation and maintenance of the power lines shall be done by the Mahatransport Company itself.
(3) It shall be incumbent upon the General Transport Company or the consumer concerned to use the power lines only for the purpose for which they are intended by the General Transport Company or the consumer concerned.
Prior permission of the competent authority shall be required before commissioning/ commissioning of the power lines.
It shall be the responsibility of the Mahanagar Transmission Company or the consumer concerned to resolve the grievances of the concerned land owners/ farmers, if any, after the installation or installation of the power lines, in consultation with the competent authority.
