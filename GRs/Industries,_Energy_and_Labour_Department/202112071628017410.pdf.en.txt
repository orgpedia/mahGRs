As per the Government decision dated 12/03/2013, it has been approved to continue the scheme by fixing the revised methodology of allocation of incentive subsidy to cashew processing industry. As per the scheme, after payment of 5% value added tax on the sale of cashew nuts finally sold in the State out of the cashew nuts produced and processed in the State, the tax amount is being provided to the concerned units as incentive subsidy.
At present, there are 03 claims pending with the Government and since a sum of Rs. 16,49,884/- is required to settle these claims, the Department has allowed the expenditure to be incurred by the Finance Department as per the request made by the Department for making available funds to the tune of Rs. 16,49,884/- for disbursement from the budgetary provision during the financial year 2021-22. Considering the funds available for the scheme at present, the Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 16,49,884/- (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Nineteen Thousand Eight Hundred and Forty Four only) to the cashew processing units mentioned in the following table, subject to the condition that the provisions of the Government Decision/ Circular mentioned in Reference No. 1 and 3, the criteria, eligibility etc. are verified/ ensured to be strictly implemented.
