Introduction: As per the policy of the state government, the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company is given a discount on the electricity tariff and the government reimburses it to the MSEDCL.
Under the head Demand No.K-6 MAIN ACCOUNT NO.28012615 for the year 2019-20 Rs. A sum of Rs. 9127.8515 crore has been provided as Supplementary Demand.
The total amount due from MSEDCL towards electricity duty and electricity sales tax for the third quarter of 2018-19 is Rs. 1711.24 crore out of which Rs. 1219.00 crore as per the Government Resolution No. 6 were made.
The balance amount of adjustment for the third quarter of 2018-19 is Rs. 492.24 crore for the first quarter of 2019-20 and Rs. 1491.5471 crore. 1983.7871 crore vide Government Resolution No. 8 was made.
The Department of Finance has not approved the adjustment of the amount due to the Government on account of electricity duty and electricity sales tax for the fourth quarter of 2018-19. The Department of Finance has accorded approval for utilization of approximately Rs. 1589.00 crore for third quarter adjustment and Rs. 256.00 crore for fourth quarter of the financial year 2019-20. As per the concurrence of the Finance Department, the matter of disbursement of Rs. 1845.00 crore to MSEDCL was under consideration of the Government.
