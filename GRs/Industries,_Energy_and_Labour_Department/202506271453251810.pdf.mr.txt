PREAMBLE
The EVS38 Organization has organized EVS38 — The World Largest Electric Mobility Event of the year from 15" to 19 June, 2025 at Gothenburg, Sweden. EVS is highly renowned as the most important conference and meeting place for the EV industry in the world. The event was
a gathering for policymakers, representatives from industry, relevant research communities and
NGO’s and was the most important international networking arena for stakeholders globally.
The event designed as hear from top experts and thought of leaders in the electric
mobility sector and evolution, cutting-edge technology and interactive session that showcases the
future of electric mobility.
2. The Government of Maharashtra has always preferred sustainable development so far and
in particular sustainable electronic vehicle technology. The event was like an opportunity for the
state to adapt new knowledge from EV batteries of the vehicles to upcoming technologies in the
field accordingly for the EV sector to implement new policies and expedite the development
process of the sector into the state, subsequently little contribution towards the positives of climate changes cost of travel.
3. Assessing the Significance of the event Government of Maharashtra have accepted the
invitation and have   decided to send the state   level delegation to attend the “EVS38- The World Largest Electric Mobility Event of the Year” held between 15" to 19 June, 2025 .
RESOLUTION:-
Government of Maharashtra is now pleased to accord ex-post facto approval to the visit
of the State level delegation to attend “EVS38- The World Largest Electric Mobility Event of the
Year” form 14" to 20" June, 2025 (Including travel time) at Gothenburg, Sweden.
