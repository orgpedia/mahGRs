The Government's Decisions:
In order to ensure that the grape-producing farmers of the state are not harmed, farmers are encouraged to cultivate grapes, make dry fruits and produce more and more profitable alternative products from grapes, and to promote STAAL based wine industry, the Government has formulated the Grape Processing Industry Policy-2001. The rule of 19/10/2001 was declared by decree. As part of the implementation of this policy, if 20 per cent value-added tax is paid on the sale price of wine produced in the state and finally sold, 16 per cent of the amount is equal to the wine tax.
The government has announced a scheme to provide incentives to the wine industry. The rule of 31/08/2009 has been implemented as per the decision of the Hon'ble Cabinet of Ministers in its meetings held on 01/04/2024 and 04/02/2024.
