The Proposal:
In order to make it possible for the farmers to irrigate during the day and to achieve the goal of saving the state government on the cost of connecting the traditional agricultural pumps and the subsidy provided by the state government, the" Mukhyamantri Solar Krishi Pump Yojana" has been approved by the government decision dated November 15, 2018 and September 11, 2019 to provide 1 lakh non-transmission solar agricultural pumps to the farmers of the state for agricultural use in a phased manner. " Mukhyamantri Solar Krishi Pump Yojana" is a fully state government scheme under which one lakh solar agricultural pumps are planned to be installed in a phased manner. In the first phase, 25,000 nos of solar agri pumps have been installed and in the second and third phase, a target of installing 75,000 nos of solar agri pumps has been set. The general category beneficiaries will be given 10 per cent of the state's budgetary subsidy, 10 per cent of the beneficiary share and the remaining 80 per cent will be paid from the mutual amount collected from the tax on sale of surplus power in the escrow account of MSEDCL.
As per the decision of the government, the general category beneficiaries of the 75 thousand naga solar agricultural pumps in the second phase will be provided 10 percent of the state government's subsidy.
