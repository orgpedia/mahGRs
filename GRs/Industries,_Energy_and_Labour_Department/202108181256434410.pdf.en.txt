Preamble: In order to ensure smooth power supply to the State by empowering the transmission lines in the State, the Director (Projects) (In-Charge), Maharashtra State Power Transmission Company Limited, Mumbai has issued Reference No. Under the scheme, it is proposed to supply 110 KV power for each 9.3 MW rail traction load of Central Railway and the proposal for grant of prior approval under Section 68 of the Electricity Act, 2003 to the scheme for this transmission line. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Under the scheme, the work of installation of 110 KV supply line for each 9.3 MW rail traction load of Central Railway will be done as per Annexure-A attached.
The Corporation is granting approval for the construction of new transmission lines/ substations mentioned in Annexure-A under Section 68 of the Indian Electricity Act, 2003 subject to the following terms and conditions. The cost of construction of the power line/ substation in Schedule A shall be borne by Central Railway and the power line/ substation shall be constructed in the prescribed standard manner and under the guidance and supervision of experts.
2. The operation and maintenance of the power lines shall be done by the Mahatransport Company itself.
(3) It shall be incumbent upon the Maharatna Company or the Railway concerned to use the power lines only for the purpose for which it is intended by the Maharatna Company or the Railway concerned.
Prior permission of the competent authority shall be required before commissioning/ commissioning of the power lines.
It shall be the responsibility of the Mahatransport Company or the concerned consumer to resolve the grievances of the land owners/ farmers, if any, during the installation or after installation of the power lines, in consultation with the competent authority.
