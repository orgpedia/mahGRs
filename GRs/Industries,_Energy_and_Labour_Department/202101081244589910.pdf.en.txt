The Central Government has enacted the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996 and the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Rules, 2007.
The Government has constituted the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board vide notification dated 4th August, 2007 under Section 18 of the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996. In pursuance of Section 3 of the Central Building and Other Construction Workers (Cess) Act, 1996, the Central Government vide notification dated 26th September, 1996 has made a provision for deposit of 1% Cess (excluding the value of land under construction) by the Assessing Officer to the Board. The cess collected from the Board is spent on various welfare schemes sanctioned to the registered construction workers under the Board as per Section 22 of the Building and Other Construction Workers (Cess) Act, 1996.
As the Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996 does not provide for the financial powers of the Head of Department, Head of Regional Office and Head of Office, the matter of declaring them as Head of Department, Head of Regional Office and Head of Office was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board, the officers shown in the table below are being declared as competent authorities.
