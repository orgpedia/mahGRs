Government Order: Under the Revenue and Forest Department, No. Ajay Keshavrao Patne, Tehsildar, Malvan, G. Sindhudurg have agreed to be appointed on deputation to the establishment of Maitri Cell under the Directorate of Industries under the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour. Accordingly, Mr. Ajay Keshavrao Patne, Tehsildar is being appointed on deputation for a period of three years subject to the following terms and conditions by elevating him to the post of Deputy Tahsildar on the establishment of Friendship Cell under the Directorate of Industries under this Department.
(a) The service of the Deputy Commissioner shall commence from the date on which Mr. Ajay Keshavrao Patne, Tehsildar, joins the office of Deputy Commissioner. After this, the period of deputation will end on the date on which they join their parent department by handing over the charge of the post of deputation after three years.
Provided that if their services are deemed necessary by the parent department in the interest of public service, the parent department/ competent authority shall have the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. But back to
