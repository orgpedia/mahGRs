A total of 1542 posts on the establishment of Labour Commissioner have been fixed as per the government decision mentioned in the above A.No. 2 to 5. As per the Government decision in the aforesaid Act 1 of the Finance Department, it has been notified to fix the revised figure after reviewing the sanctioned posts in the administrative departments and offices under them. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to review the posts on the establishment of the Labor Commissioner and determine the revised figure.
Government Decision The above mentioned No. 7 of the Labour Commissioner. The proposal dated 06.03.2020 and the proposal of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries constituted to thoroughly examine the proposals for creation of new posts, revival of posts and review of posts etc., as per the approval given in the above mentioned Annexure No.6, the revised figure of 985 regular and 212 supernumerary posts in the establishment of Labour Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai under the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour is being approved under this Government decision.

# Services within the limit of manpower shown here are being approved to be taken through external agencies as per requirement. However, the number of posts should be considered as more than the number of employees working in the deceased cadre. Services should be taken from external agencies in proportion to the remaining manpower excluding the posts currently working as supernumeraries. The post of Deputy Commissioner shall become vacant on the date of retirement/ resignation/ death of the incumbent. After that, the services should be taken over by external agencies as per the requirement. While taking services through external agencies, care should be taken to follow the guidelines given by the government from time to time.
