Introduction: The increasing number of educated youth in urban and rural areas of the state and new opportunities for self-employment and employment in various fields in the state in the field of industry, business. Considering these new opportunities of self-employment and employment, the comprehensive scheme of' Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme' (CMEGP), which promotes entrepreneurship and gives a time-bound boost to creativity, has been implemented as per the Government decision dated 01/08/2019. Accordingly, State and District Level Controlling Committees have been constituted to provide various opportunities of self-employment to the urban and rural youth of the state and for effective implementation of the scheme.
Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program, the financial assistance from the state government will be provided in the form of subsidy from the state government's budgetary provision to establish self-employment projects to make the youth of the state self-sufficient/ self-reliant. Accordingly, a new Account Head for General Category and Tribal Category has been opened under the Scheme vide Government Resolution dated 23/01/2020. Also, for the Scheduled Castes Sub Plan, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance provides provision in accordance with the objectives.
The Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme is being implemented through the District Industries Center under the Directorate of Industries under the Industries Department and the District Khadi and Village Industries Office under the Khadi and Village Industries Board. As per the provisions of the scheme, the training will be imparted through the Maharashtra Centre for Entrepreneurship Development (MCED) and Mitcon Consultancy and Engineering Services Limited.

The Additional Director of Industries, Directorate of Industries vide their letter dated 16/03/2022 has requested to make available on BDS system for disbursement of total amount of Rs. 93,52,800/- (Rs. 66,67,200/- and Rs. 26,85,600/- respectively) required for total 57 online training programmes conducted for 1645 beneficiaries during the financial year 2021-22. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 93,52,800/- required for the online training program implemented by the said training institutions was under consideration of the Government from the approved budgetary allocation for the financial year 2021-22.
Government Decision: As per the provisions of the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program, the Government has approved the disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 93,52,800/- (Rupees Ninety Three Lakhs Fifty Two Thousand Eight Hundred Only) for the online training program implemented for the beneficiaries by the Maharashtra Entrepreneurship Development Center (MCED) and Mitcon Consultancy and Engineering Services Limited in the financial year 2021-22.
The powers to draw and disburse this amount are being delegated to the Accountant in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
