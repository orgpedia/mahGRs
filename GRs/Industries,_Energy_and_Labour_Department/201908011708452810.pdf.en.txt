Preamble: Under the Government decision in the context of Urban Development Department, approval has been given to take technical approval from the Public Works Department or Maharashtra Life Authority for architectural works.
As requested by the Managing Director, Maharashtra Small Industries Development Corporation in his letter of reference No. 3 to the Government, the matter of approval of technical approval from the Public Works Department or Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran for the renovation works of Government offices was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra government has decided to seek technical approval from the Public Works Department (PWD) or Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran (MJA) for the renovation of government offices.
