(2) The Chief Inspector of Electricity, Mumbai shall relieve the transferred officers from their present posts immediately by making necessary local alternative arrangements so that they can take charge of their transferred places immediately and they may be asked to join the transferred places immediately.
(3) The officers concerned shall ensure that they join the place of posting within the prescribed period as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Duration of Appointment, Non-Acceptance of Service and Removal of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. If he does not join the post, the period of his absence will be treated as" non-working day." Further, Government Resolution No. 2016/ No. 186/16/ Office- 14, D. The concerned should note that disciplinary action will be taken as per the provisions of 23.12.2016.
Since the above officers are being transferred on their request, they will not be paid the period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance.
5. The concerned officer should not be granted leave on any ground without prior permission of the Government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office shall not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers.
6. It may be noted that in case of non-admission to the post as per the said order, or any attempt to transfer and change the post under any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
7. The concerned officers, after appearing in the transferred post, should immediately submit their status report along with the transfer certificate to the Government through the Chief Electrical Inspector, Mumbai without any delay in the prescribed manner.
