The Proposal:
The Interim Budget 2019-20 was presented to the Legislative Assembly on 27th February, 2019. As this is an interim budget, the Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill, 2019 to meet the expenditure for the four months (April, 2019 to July, 2019) for the period up to the passing of the Appropriation Bill by way of an additional budget. It was under consideration of the government to distribute the accounting grants to the establishments, companies and institutions under the administrative control of the Department of Energy on the BEAMS system.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the disbursement of four months' accounting grant sanctioned to the Office of the Chief Inspector of Electricity, Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company, Maharashtra State Power Generation Company, Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission, Maharashtra Energy Development Agency under the administrative control of this Department on BEAMS system within the limit of 33% of the original budgetary provision sanctioned for the year 2019-20.
The said accounting grant has been sanctioned for a period of four months and the office of the Chief Inspector of Electricity under this department is advised to instruct its subordinate offices to spend within the sanctioned accounting limit.
This Government decision is being issued under the relevant Government Circular of the Finance Department dated 1st April, 2019.
