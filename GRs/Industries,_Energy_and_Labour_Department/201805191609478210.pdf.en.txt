Preamble: The Central Government has approved the implementation of the Child Labour (Prohibition and Regulation) Amendment Act, 2016 and the National Child Labour Project (NCLP). The PENCIL portal has been established on 26.09.2017, and a nodal officer has been appointed from each district of the state.
As per the amendment made under the Central Government Office Memorandum No. 1 in the context, the matter of setting up a State Resource Centre in the State for effective implementation and control of NCLP in the State was under consideration.
The Ministry of Labour and Employment, Govt. The Central Advisory Board (CAB) has been re-established vide Notification dated 24.01.2017 and the State Resource Centre (SRC) has been directed by the Central Government vide letter No. 2 dated 23.01.2017 to accord priority to the suggestions of the said Central Advisory Board.
