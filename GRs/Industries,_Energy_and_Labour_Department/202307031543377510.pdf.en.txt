the prelude
The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited, Mumbai (MSEDCL) has been appointed as the State Nodal Agency for providing charging facilities for electric vehicles in the State vide Government Order dated 21st February, 2019. Department of Environment and Climate Change. The Government of Maharashtra has announced the" Maharashtra Electric Vehicle Policy, 2021" under the Government Decision dated 23 rd July, 2021. The policy has been approved to be implemented in the State by 31st March, 2025. In the decision No. As per 2.4.2, incentives for the creation of infrastructure charging systems will be provided by the Department of Energy. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds to MSEDCL in line with the proposal of MSEDCL for disbursement of incentives for infrastructure charging facilities is under consideration of the Government. Funds are available for disbursement in the limit of 10% of Rs. 7.90 crore. Accordingly, the government was considering to disburse funds to the tune of Rs 79 lakh.
