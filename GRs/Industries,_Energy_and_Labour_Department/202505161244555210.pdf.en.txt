Date: May 16, 2025
Government Decision: - The new Information Technology and Information Technology Supported Services Policy-2023 of the State of Maharashtra has been announced vide Government Decision dated 27th June 2023. The government's decision. 4.3. "The State Governments shall create a separate fund, the Critical Infrastructure Fund for IT and IT-enabled Services Industries, of the amount of overvalue received by the private IT parks as per the provision of this policy towards additional mat area directives, and this fund shall be used only for the creation of infrastructure required by the IT industry." A provision has been made for this.
2. Accordingly, the Government is hereby approving the opening of a separate head of accounts for setting up a "Critical Infrastructure Fund" for the IT and IT-enabled services industries.
