The Superintending Engineer, Regional Electricity Inspection Board, Mumbai/ Pune/ Aurangabad/ Nagpur shall relieve the transferred officer from the present charge immediately by making necessary local alternative arrangements so that he can take charge of his transferred place immediately and he may be asked to join the place of transfer immediately. Also, leave should not be granted to the transferred officer.
Further, disciplinary action will be initiated against the concerned Assistant Electrical Inspector as per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, if it is found that political pressure/ coercion has been exerted to cancel or modify the transfers made under this order.
4. The concerned Assistant Electrical Inspector shall join the new place of appointment immediately and submit the compliance report to the Government.
