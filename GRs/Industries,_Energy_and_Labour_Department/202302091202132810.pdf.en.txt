Preamble: Proposal received from Directorate of Industries under the Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development Scheme (4875 0025). Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. It was placed for consideration in the ninth meeting of the Steering Committee held on 03/02/2023. After scrutinizing the proposals submitted to the Committee, the Directorate of Industries shall submit the proposals for administrative approval of the approved proposals to the Government immediately, and thereafter, the administrative approval order/ technical approval/ fund disbursement order from the implementing agency will be proposed by the Department of Industries as per the prevailing procedure, based on the cost of project approval approved by the Steering Committee.
However, considering the fact that the financial year will be over in the meantime, the Steering Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary has decided that in the current financial year 2022-23, the budgetary allocation available on BDS system should be disbursed to the tune of Rs. 72.52 crore to the Development Commissioner (Industries), handed over to the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation and paid to the implementing agencies by following the prescribed procedure. The minutes of the meeting No. 4 is here. Issued vide letter dated 07/02/2023. As per the decision taken by the Steering Committee, the budgetary provision of Rs. 72.52 crore available for the financial year 2022-23 under the scheme was under consideration of the Government to be placed under the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation.
Government decision: The committee is headed by the Chief Secretary. As per the decision taken in the 9th meeting held on 03/02/2023, the Government is approving to transfer the budgetary provision of Rs. 72.52 crore (Rupees Seventy Two Crores Fifty Two Lakh only) available on BDS system in the current financial year 2022-23 for the scheme of Creation and Development of Industrial Infrastructure (48750025) to the office of Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
