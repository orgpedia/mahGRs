PREAMBLE
The Consulate General of India, Frankfurt had invited Government of Maharashtra for the
event of ‘Maharashtra Business Day’ organized in collaboration with Marathi Katta Germany on 7 June, 2025. Already there are several German companies operating from Maharashtra and
there are several collaborations happening between the start-up ecosystems in India and Germany. Accordingly the event aimed to promote and strengthen business collaborations between Maharashtra and Germany.
The event has provided a huge platform for further strengthening new business linkages
between Germany and Maharashtra. The Maharashtra diaspora also has a lot of professionals and
businessmen who happily supported the event.
2. Along with the event some sessions and panel discussion have been arranged about
building partnership with various sectors. Therefore the event was like an opportune use to encourage some potential entrepreneurs/investors investment in the state of Maharashtra and to address Marathi diaspora directly to build up strong industrial ecosystem as well.
3. Keeping the view to strengthen business networks in Germany; Government of
Maharashtra have accepted the invitation of the Consulate General of India, Frankfurt and have
decided to send the state level delegation under the chairmanship of Hon’ble Dr. Uday Samant, Minister (Industries) to attend the “Maharashtra Business Day” held on 7" June, 2025 and the
G2B meetings and factory visits after the event at Frankfurt, Germany for the period of 5" June,2025 to 10 June 2025 (including travel time).
RESOLUTION:-
Government of Maharashtra is now pleased to accord ex-post facto approval to the visit
of the State level delegation to attend "Maharashtra Business Day" held on 7™ June, 2025 and
accordingly the G2B meeting programmes and factory visits at Frankfurt, Germany.
