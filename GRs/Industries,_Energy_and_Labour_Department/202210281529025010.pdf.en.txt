The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Ordinance, 2015, was promulgated with a view to provide transparent, efficient and timely public service to the eligible persons of the state and to provide prompt and quality service to the people in view of their growing expectations from the local administration. It has been implemented on 28/4/2015. Section 3 of the Ordinance mandates that every public authority shall, within a period of three months from the date of commencement of the Ordinance and from time to time thereafter, provide such services as public servants, designated officers, first and second appellate authorities and such other services as may be notified under the Ordinance.
