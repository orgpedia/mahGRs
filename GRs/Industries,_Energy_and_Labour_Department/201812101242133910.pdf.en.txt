The Proposal:
In order to effectively implement the Electricity Act, 2003 and the rules and regulations made thereunder, the Establishment of the Electricity Inspectorate Branch functioning under the administrative control of the Public Works Department vide Reference No. 3 It has been brought under the authority of the Department of Energy by a government decision. As the Electricity Inspectorate Branch has been transferred to the Energy Department, as per the decision of the Government, the option of inclusion of the officers/ employees working in both the departments as per their choice has been provided in the Reference No. 1 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor. 4 and Public Works Department No. 5 The decision has been approved by the government.
The final consolidated seniority list of Engineers in the cadre of Executive Engineer (Electrical)/ Electrical Inspector as on 01/01/2015 is being prepared by the Public Works Department vide Reference No. 6 was published.
Now that the Electricity Inspectorate Branch has been transferred to the Department of Power as mentioned in the above paragraph, the officers/ employees of the Electricity Branch of the Public Works Department and the Electricity Inspectorate Branch of the Department of Power have been transferred to that Department on the basis of their service willingness. Therefore, considering the change in the number of cadres, the matter of publishing a separate ad-hoc provisional seniority list of the Electrical Inspector Cadre working under the Electrical Inspectorate Branch was under the consideration of the Government.
