The Proposal:
Electricity is supplied to the agricultural pump holders of the state at a discounted rate every year and the MSEDCL is given financial assistance to reimburse it. Further, to provide subsidy on power tariff to individual beneficiaries belonging to Scheduled Tribes under Tribal Component Programme of Tribal Development Department, the Tribal Development Department has sanctioned Rs.
200.00 crore.
As per the proposal of Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company dated 05.12.2022, the Tribal Development Department has allocated Rs. 200.00 crore provided by Tribal Development Department. 70.00 crore has been disbursed to MSEDCL vide Government Resolution dated 19.10.2022. Now, the Finance Department has allocated 45% of the funds available for distribution among the departments on the BDS system. 90.00 crore has been released by the Tribal Development Department vide Government Order dated 31.01.2023 and the same has been made available to the Department of Power as administrative department for disbursement to MSEDCL. The fund of Rs. 90,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ninety Crores only) to MSEDCL was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited has received Rs.
The Government is approving cash disbursement of Rs. 90,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ninety Crores only) to MSEDCL.
At the divisional level, the Energy-5 and the MSEDCL should ensure that the amount of funds sanctioned under this decision is spent only on the beneficiaries of the Scheduled Tribes in the form of discounts on electricity rates. Also, it will be the responsibility of the MSEDCL to ensure that the funds are utilised for the purpose for which they have been sanctioned.
