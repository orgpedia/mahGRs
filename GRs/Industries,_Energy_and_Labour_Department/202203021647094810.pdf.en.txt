circular
The Election Commission of India (ECI) conducts various activities to create electoral awareness among the citizens under its" Systematic Voter Education and Electoral Participation (SVEEP)" programme. Accordingly, the Election Commission of India has decided to increase the participation of all citizens in the elections at the national level. January 25, 2022. The" National Voters Awareness Contest" is scheduled to be held on 15 th March, 2022. Under the National Voters Awareness Competitions, five types of competitions have been organized: a) Question Paper Competition, b) Video Making Competition, c) Poster Design Competition, d) Song Competition, e) Slogan Making Competition. Details of the competition are available at https:// ecisveep. nic. in/ contest/. The Election Commission of India has directed that more and more voter awareness forums in the state should participate in this contest. Accordingly, the Principal Secretary and Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra vide their letter dated 11/02/2022 has requested all the industry groups in the State to encourage the Voter Awareness Forum to participate in the contest.
Accordingly, under this circular, the industry groups of the state should encourage the Voter Awareness Forum as well as their workers/ employees to participate in the National Voter Awareness Contest.
3. The following steps should be taken so that more and more citizens can participate in the National Voters Awareness Contest.
