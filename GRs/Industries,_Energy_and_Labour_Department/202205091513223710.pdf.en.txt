Government Decision To Decide On The Proposals Received For Disbursement Of Funds Under The Maharashtra Industrial Infrastructure Creation and Development Scheme. The 8th meeting of the Steering Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary was held on 24/02/2022. In the meeting, the Committee has approved the release of funds to Lonavala Cooperative Industrial Estate Limited, Nangargaon, G. Pune for the following infrastructure works. The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) is the implementing agency for the project.

Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation has submitted a total budget of Rs 1,49,21, 500/- for the works of the organization. The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) has given technical approval to the project. The Lonavala Cooperative Industrial Estate Limited, Nangargaon, G.Pune has also entered into an agreement with the Directorate of Industries on 28/01/2022 for depositing 25% of their share with the executing agency, guarantee for maintenance repair and guarantee for opening of the road for the general public. Considering all these aspects, administrative approval is being accorded for the maps and budget for the works of the Institute at a total cost of Rs. 1,49,21, 500/- (Rupees One Crore Nineteen Five Lakh Twenty One Thousand Five Hundred only).
The increase in the project cost will not be acceptable to the organization. Additional expenses should be borne by the institution.
The said administrative approval is in accordance with the approval given by the Steering Committee in its 8th meeting held on 24/02/2022 as well as the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor Government Decision No. Aupasu 2012/ S.No.234/ Industries-2, Dy. dated 25/02/2014. The powers conferred under section 6 are being exercised.
