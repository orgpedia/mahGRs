As per the decision of the government, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Jeevan Prakash Yojana. It was implemented from 14.04.2021 to 06.12.2021. During this period, 12,102 household electricity connections have been provided to beneficiaries belonging to SC/ ST category.
In view of the response received from the above beneficiaries and the future demand for electricity connections, the duration of this scheme will be decided. being extended up to 06.12.2022.
