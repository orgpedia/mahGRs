If the officer concerned does not join the post of posting within the prescribed period as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a" non-working day." 5.The concerned officer should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave applications of the transferred officers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned authorities at the original address.
If any person fails to join the post immediately as per the said order or tries to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
