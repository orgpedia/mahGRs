The Proposal:
The Government Circular in Reference No. 1 has been approved by the Legislature of the State of Maharashtra as specified in Column 4 of the Schedule to the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2021 (including the amounts) and has also been assented to by the Hon'ble Governor of Maharashtra. This Act is known as Maharashtra Act No. 15 Therefore, in the Maharashtra Extraordinary Gazette, Part IV, It was released on March 15, 2021. As per the said circular, the budget estimates have been made on the Financial Intended Distribution System (BEMS) in the range of 5 to 10 per cent of the total annual outlay till the end of April, 2021.
In pursuance of the said circular, the Budget Branch of this Department has disbursed a subsidy amounting to Rs. 11,19,65, 850/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Nineteen Lakhs Sixty Five Thousand Eight Hundred and Fifty only) under the head Demand No. K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (28510585) 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) on Budgetary System with a ceiling of 15%.
Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, vide its Reference No. 3, has requested to provide a total grant of Rs. 1289.62/ lakh (Rupees Twelve Crore Ninety-Nine Lakhs Sixty-two Thousand only) for the purpose of 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salary) amounting to Rs. 1244.06/ lakh and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 45.56 lakh for two months April and May, 2021.
In accordance with the provisions of the above circular of the Finance Department and taking into account the provisions distributed by the Budget Branch of the Department on the Budget Distribution System, it was under the consideration of the Government to provide subsidy to the Board to meet the expenditure on salary for the month of April, 2021.
