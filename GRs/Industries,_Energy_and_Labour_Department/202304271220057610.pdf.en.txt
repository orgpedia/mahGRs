Proposal to provide 20 hectares of land of Parbhani Krishi Gou-Samvardhan Limited, a secondary company of Aurangabad, to set up a government medical college and hospital in Parbhani
It was submitted to the Marathwada Development Corporation, Aurangabad, by the department through the Collector, Parbhani. Marathwada Development Corporation, Aurangabad submitted the proposal to the Industries Department for approval.
The Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals has issued a notification dated 2 nd March, 2022 regarding the transfer of land, construction of hospital and hostel, creation of posts, etc. for the establishment of Government Medical College and Hospital at Parbhani. The proposal was approved at a Cabinet meeting.
The book value of the said land has been adjusted by the Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals vide Government Resolution dated 28th March, 2023 under the head" Repayment of Loan for Incentive Schemes by State Government by Marathwada Development Corporation" (6885514201). 2,34,91, 000/- has been processed.
Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals vide Government Resolution dated 17th April, 2023
Necessary action should be taken to hand over the actual possession of the concerned place to the Medical Education Department through the Revenue Department. The transfer of 20 hectares of land belonging to Parbhani Krishi Gau Sanrakshan Limited, a subsidiary of Marathwada Development Corporation, Aurangabad, to the Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department is under consideration.

Terms and Conditions: (1) The Managing Director, Marathwada Development Corporation, Aurangabad, shall give possession of 20 hectares of land to the Director, Medical Education and Research, Mumbai, of Parbhani Krishi Gau Sanrakshan Limited, a secondary company of Marathwada Development Corporation, Aurangabad, under the administrative control of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour.
2) The remaining land of Parbhani Krishi Gau Sanrakshan Limited in the above-mentioned return should be kept in the possession of" Parbhani Krishi Gau Sanrakshan Limited."
(3) The land or any part thereof or any interest therein shall not be transferred by way of sale, gift, exchange, mortgage, lease, private public participation or any other mode except with the prior permission of the Industries Department. No right of any person, institution, company, etc. shall be transferred in such a manner as to create any right of any kind over the said land or any part thereof. Also, the land cannot be subdivided without prior approval of the government's industries department.
The Director of Medical Education and Research, Mumbai shall use the Thematic Land for the approved purpose only.
If the land or any part of it is to be used temporarily or permanently for any other purpose, prior approval of the Industries Department of the Government will be required.
