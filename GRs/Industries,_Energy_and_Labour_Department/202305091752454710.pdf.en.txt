The Proposal:
A total of 09 agency panels have been appointed to carry out works by external agencies under the government decision mentioned here. Since the SOP does not include the procedure to be followed for hiring manpower in a strike situation, the decision has been taken in the interim. Also, some other terms and conditions need to be included in the SOP. The government decision. 2.3 provides for the formulation of necessary terms and conditions (SOPs) from time to time in respect of the panel of service providers. The government was considering amending the terms and conditions (SOP) mentioned here with the government decision as above.
