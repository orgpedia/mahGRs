Date: January 27, 2023.
Introduction: The state government is trying to attract maximum investment in the state of Maharashtra under the collective incentive scheme (Saproyo) policy. The main objective of the industrial policy of the state is to attract investment in the state. As per the prevailing policy, incentives are given to the industrial units that establish in the state and create employment by investing.
To review the various MoUs and other important pending issues in the subordinate departments of the Industries Department. The government was considering setting up a task force under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Industries).
Government Decisions: To review the various MoUs and other important pending issues of the subordinate departments of the Industries Department at the department level weekly with the officials of the industries and the concerned departments. A Task Force is being set up under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Industries).

The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall hold weekly meetings of the Task Force Committee with the industry and the officers of the concerned department on various MoUs and other important matters pending in the subordinate departments of the Industries Department.

In the meeting of the Task Force, the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai, after conducting a rapid review at the department level, decided to conduct a survey every month. A meeting should be held under the chairmanship of the Minister (Industries) and a report on the implementation of the projects should be submitted.
The Task Force will be as follows:
To liaise with the concerned departments and authorities as required for implementation of the decisions to be taken in the meeting of the Task Force regarding difficulties of investors and obstacles in investment.
2) Follow-up of MoUs signed on behalf of the Government.
(3) To identify and resolve the difficulties in implementation of the MoU.

5) To take action regarding the acquisition and allotment of land from the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation as well as outside its area.
6) Review of clearances required for setting up of industries.
The Development Commissioner (Industries) and Additional Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai will be responsible for taking information from all the stakeholders in the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation and the Directorate of Industries and making efforts to take immediate action through the" Maharashtra Industry, Trade and Investment Facilitation Cell (Maitri Cell)" at the level of difficulties.
