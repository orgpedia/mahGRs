the prelude
Solapur (North) taluka of Solapur district has been included in' D' classification and Mohol taluka in' D+' classification under the Collective Incentive Scheme-2019 as per the relevant Government Decision dated 16.09.2019. However, since some parts of Chincholi MIDC area are included in North Solapur Taluka and some parts in Mohol Taluka and there is no separate classification of Chincholi MIDC area in the Collective Incentive Scheme-2019, some industries in the same area are getting the benefit of" D" classification and some industries are getting" D+" classification. To avoid this ambiguity, the government was considering to include the entire Chincholi MIDC area in the" D+" classification. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision
The Government has approved the inclusion of Solapur (North) Taluka, Mohol Taluka and Chincholi MIDC area of Solapur district in the following classification under the Collective Incentive Scheme-2019.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A. No. | Taluka/ Area               | Basic Classification under Collective Incentive Scheme-2019 | Revised classification under Collective Incentive Scheme-2019 |
| 1      | Solapur (North)            | " D."                                                       | D (excluding Chincholi MIDC area)                             |
| 2.     | Mohol                      | " D+"                                                       | " D+" (including the Chincholi MIDC area)                     |
| 3      | Entire Chincholi MIDC area |                                                             | " D+"                                                         |
