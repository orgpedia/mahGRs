Preamble: - The scheme "Maharashtra State Industrial Cluster Development Programme (MSICDP)" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 25/02/2014 to promote cluster development of small clusters in industrially underdeveloped areas of the state thereby promoting holistic development of women and small units. Under the scheme, state government grants are provided for implementation of capacity building programmes for holistic development and growth of women and small enterprises as well as for setting up of common facilities.
As mentioned in the proposal dated 24/03/2025 of the Directorate of Industries, an amount of Rs. 1,28,33, 000 / - has been given final approval in the 15th meeting of the State Level Steering Committee dated 05/08/2022 under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Industries) for setting up of Common Facility Centre for Industrial Cluster Projects at Garment Cluster, Chalisgaon, District Jalgaon.
The project has been extended up to date 31/03/2025 in the meetings of the State Level Steering Committee dated 12/03/2025. The project has a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) as the industrial group project has an industry component in the 100% women category. The Self-Help Women's Association, G. Jalgaon is eligible for State Government grants up to 90 of the project cost as per the Government Decision dated 02/08/2021. Accordingly, the final approval orders have been issued by the Directorate of Industries dated 13/10/2022.
