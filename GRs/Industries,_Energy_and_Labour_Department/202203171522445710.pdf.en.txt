Introduction: The increasing number of educated youth in urban and rural areas of the state and new opportunities for self-employment and employment in various fields in the state in the field of industry, business. Considering these new opportunities of self-employment and employment, the comprehensive scheme of' Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme' (CMEGP) has been implemented on the lines of the existing scheme of' Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme' (PMEGP), which promotes entrepreneurship and promotes creativity in a time-bound manner. Accordingly, State Level and District Level Controlling Committees have been constituted to provide various opportunities of self-employment to the urban and rural youth of the state and for effective implementation of the scheme.
Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Program, the state government will provide financial assistance in the form of grants from the state government's budgetary provision to establish self-employment projects to make the youth of the state self-sufficient/ self-reliant. Accordingly, a new Account Head for General Category and Tribal Category has been opened under the Scheme vide Government Resolution dated 23/01/2020.
Also, for the Scheduled Castes Sub Plan, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance provides provision in accordance with the objectives.

The Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme is being implemented through the District Industries Center under the Directorate of Industries under the Industries Department and the District Khadi and Village Industries Office under the Khadi and Village Industries Board. Accordingly, the Additional Director of Industries, Directorate of Industries vide their letter dated 28/12/2021 has requested to make available the margin money amount required for the pending claims in the general category under the scheme on BDS system for the purpose of 316 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaried) amounting to Rs. 51.12 crore. Further, the Additional Director of Industries, Directorate of Industries, vide his letter dated 04/01/2022, has requested to provide a total amount of Rs. 1.50 crore for the Special IT and Coordination Cell set up in collaboration with Maharashtra Entrepreneurship Development Centre under the control of Directorate of Industries under this scheme.
In pursuance of the above request made by the Additional Director of Industries, Directorate of Industries, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse the margin money amount of Rs. 51,11,60, 000/- required for the pending claims of general category under the Chief Minister Employment Generation Programme and Rs. 1,50,00, 000/- for the Special IT and Coordination Cell established in collaboration with Maharashtra Entrepreneurship Development Center under the control of Directorate of Industries from the approved budgetary provision for the financial year 2021-22.
Government Decision: Under the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme (CMEGP) of the State Government, the State Government's share of the margin money amount included in the approved case of the general category is Rs. 51,11,60, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Fifty Crores Eleven Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) and the fund required for the Special IT and Coordination Cell under the control of the Directorate of Industries is Rs. 1,50,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only). 2 and 4 are hereby approved by this Government Resolution to be disbursed as per the provisions of the said Scheme.
