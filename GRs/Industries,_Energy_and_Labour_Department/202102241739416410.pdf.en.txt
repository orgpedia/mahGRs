GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PROMOTION- 2019/ PR. No.165/ WORKERS- 8 3. The above officers are being given temporary promotion subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) Vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota shall be filled only on a temporary basis.
A Special Leave Petition No. In respect of Filling under 28306/2017, S.P.V./ 16-B has issued a D.O. The approval is being given in accordance with the instructions issued under the Government Decision dated 18.02.2021.
(2) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the officers in the select list and in the promotion list shall not be entitled to any regularity and eminence as a result of this temporary promotion.
(3) The said promotion to the post under the promotion quota is temporary in nature and subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
(8) In respect of the promotion of officers who are eligible but have been denied promotion in accordance with the merit of service in the said selection list, the Action will be taken as per the Government decision dated 12.09.2016.
4. Promoted officers mentioned in the above table are hereby relieved of their duties immediately to join the place of posting after their promotion and to join the place of new posting.
The officials concerned should submit a report to the government in this regard. Also, they should not go on leave. Also, no application for cancellation or modification of the appointment should be submitted. In doing so, the concerned officers will be liable for disciplinary action.
