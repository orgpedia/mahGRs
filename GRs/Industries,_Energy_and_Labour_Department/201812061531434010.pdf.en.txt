(2) Letter from the Commissioner of Labour No. KA/ KIWA/ Board/ Reorganization/ 2018/ Work- 10 dated 16th November, 2018
Preamble: The Maharashtra State Minimum Wages Advisory Board has been reconstituted vide Government Resolution dated 14th November, 2018 in accordance with the provisions of Section 7 of the Minimum Wages Act, 1948 and Section 6 of the Maharashtra Minimum Wages Rules, 1963. In this decision, Deputy Commissioner Labour, Mumbai has been appointed as Member Secretary of the Board. As per Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Minimum Wages Rules, 1963, there is a provision to appoint a secretary to the board. However, the designation as Member Secretary is mentioned in the Government Resolution. Therefore, the Commissioner of Labour vide letter dated 16.11.2018 has requested to change the designation to Secretary and appoint Assistant Labour Commissioner (GRAVI), Mumbai as Secretary of the Board. Accordingly, the following Government Decision dated 14th November, 2018 is being revised:
Corrigendum:
Reference No. 1 The Government Resolution here should read" Secretary Assistant Commissioner of Labour (GRAVI), Mumbai" instead of Member Secretary Deputy Commissioner of Labour.
