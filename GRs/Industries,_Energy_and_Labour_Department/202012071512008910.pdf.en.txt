The Proposal:
Atal Nirman Kamgar Awas Yojana for construction workers in rural areas registered with Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board (ACWWWB) for construction of new houses on their own land or conversion of existing kutcha houses into pucca houses in rural areas.
(Rural) has been approved under the Government Resolution dated 14th January, 2019 and under the Government Resolution dated 7th February, 2019, an amount of Rs. 50,000/- has been sanctioned.
In order to remove the difficulties in availing the benefits of the scheme due to the condition in the government decision dated July 4, 2019, it was under the consideration of the government to fix the date of registration of construction workers by changing the conditions.
Government's decision:
The time limit for availing the benefit of Atal Construction Workers Awas Yojana (Gramin), as decided by the Government vide its decision dated 04.07.2019, is being relaxed and the construction workers registered with Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board till 15.10.2020 can apply under the scheme.
