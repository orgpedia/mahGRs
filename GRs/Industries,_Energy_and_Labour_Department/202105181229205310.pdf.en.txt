Preamble- Subject to the appointment of staff on compassionate grounds in various Mathadi Mandals/ Suraksha Rakshak Mandals in the State. The modalities have been decided as per the Government decision dated 18.04.2013. In order to keep a check on the appointment on compassionate grounds, the Government has now decided to change the procedure as follows.
GOVERNMENT DECISION It was decided to finalize the proposals for appointment of employees on compassionate grounds in various Mathadi Boards/ Suraksha Rakshak Boards in the State with the approval of the Labour Commissioner as per the terms and conditions prescribed under the Government Decision dated 18/04/2013 of the Department.
However, in order to keep control over the appointment on compassionate grounds in the said Board, the Labour Commissioner has issued a notification. Proposals should be finalized with prior approval of the Government by acting in accordance with the prevailing policy of the General Administration Department regarding the Government Decision and Compassionate Principle dated 18.04.2013.
