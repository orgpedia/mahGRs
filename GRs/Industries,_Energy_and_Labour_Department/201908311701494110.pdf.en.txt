Introduction: On the lines of the existing Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) of the Central Government, the Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme (CMEGP) has been implemented as per the Government's New Industrial Policy-2019, Issue No. 5 (2) and 9.2. The government took the decision. has been issued on 1.8.2019.
The government has decided to provide various opportunities of self-employment and employment to the urban and rural youth of the state, the effective implementation of the scheme is being done through the District Industries Center, District Khadi and Village Industries Board under the Directorate of Industries. District Level Scrutiny and Coordination Committee, State Level Controlling Committee have been constituted under the Chairmanship of General Manager, GUK for necessary administrative action.
The Government has decided that in the District Level Scrutiny and Coordination Sub-Committee, representatives of 2 major industrial organizations/ institutions/ industrial groups of the district will be represented as members. It is listed in 6.2.
3. By this Government Order, the Industrial Association" Buddhist Entrepreneurs Association of Commerce and Industry" (Registration No. 8/ P. No. 110784) is being recognized for nominating Industrial Associations to the District Level Scrutiny and Coordination Sub-Committee and State Level Controlling Committee under the" Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme" as follows:
(a) The government's decision. The Chairman of the Committee shall nominate representatives of the Buddhist Entrepreneurs Association of Commerce and Industry or other industrial associations as required to be members of the Sub-Committee constituted under paragraph 6.2 of the Act of 1.8.2019.
(2) The decision of the government. In paragraph 7.1 of 1.8.2019. The Chairman of the Committee shall nominate the representatives of the NAMUDA Association and other industrial associations as invited members in the Control Committee to be constituted under the Chairmanship of the Development Commissioner (Industries).
