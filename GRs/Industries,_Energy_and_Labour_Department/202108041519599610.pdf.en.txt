The Provisional Service Seniority List dated 01/01/2020 of Government Labour Officers, Group-B was published on 14/07/2021. Representations of the concerned officers have been received on the service seniority list. Accordingly, the final service seniority list dated 01/01/2020 of Government Labour Officers, Group-B Cadre as shown in the attached" Form-A" is being published after making corrections.
The base date for publishing the service seniority list is 1st January every year.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the names of Government Labour Officers who are not in service due to retirement, death, promotion, resignation etc. in the final merit list of Government Labour Officer cadre dated 01/01/2017 have been excluded.
