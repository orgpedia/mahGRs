Under the Central Government's Special Assistance to the State for Capital Expenditure Scheme under PM GatiShakti (Part-II), the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) has been allocated Rs. 145.00 crore.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Sanitary Certificate No: IDC-2023/ PR No.13/ Industries-2
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 28, 2023
Government Resolution No. IDC-2022/ P.No. 707/ Industries-14 dated 12/01/2023
GOVERNMENT SUGGESTIONS: The Central Government has released a total of Rs. 145 crore has been sanctioned and the Government decision to disburse the same to Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation has been issued on 12/01/2023. Article 5 of the resolution reads as follows:
" The said Fund Drawing and Disbursing Officer has been authorised to disburse the said funds to the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation, Andheri Mumbai-93 by making payments to the Accounts and Accounts Officer, Mumbai." 5 is amended as follows:
" The Drawing and Disbursing Officer is being empowered to deposit the funds available on the BDS system at the level of State Scheme Manager in a single Nodal Bank Account opened in the name of Bank of Maharashtra, Mantralaya Branch, Mumbai-32 under" PM Gati Shakti (Part-II)" under the Special Assistance to States for Capital Expenditure Scheme by the Central Government. "
