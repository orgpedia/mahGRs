It may be noted that if the concerned officer presses for cancellation of transfer or transfer through the people's representative or other means without appearing in the post of transfer, the concerned officer will be liable to disciplinary action as the act violates rules 3 and 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Also, since the above mentioned officers are being transferred as per their request, the period of assumption of office, travel allowance and related benefits will not be admissible to them.
