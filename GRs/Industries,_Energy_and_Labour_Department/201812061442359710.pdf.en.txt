The Proposal:
In reference to the above. As per the Government Decision dated 21.09.2006. Guidelines have been issued for reporting/ reviewing confidential reports of officers/ staff of Narayan Meghaji Lokhande Maharashtra Institute of Labour Sciences, Mumbai and Regional Labour Institute, Nagpur. However, the present Director, Shri. Narayan Meghaji Lokhande is an officer of the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Labour at the Maharashtra Institute of Labour Sciences, Mumbai. Accordingly, the Government has decided to report/ review the confidential reports of the officers/ employees of the above two offices as follows:
Government's decision:
The confidential report of the officers of the post of Professor/ Superintendent/ Research Officer/ Manager and Deputy Director of the office of Shri Narayan Meghaji Lokhande Maharashtra Institute of Labour Sciences, Mumbai and Regional Labour Institute, Nagpur for the year 2017-2018 and Director, Shri K. The Deputy Secretary (Labour) is being authorised as Reviewing Officer to revisit the confidential reports of the concerned officers for the period up to the appointment of a regular officer to the post of Narayan Meghaji Lokhande Maharashtra Institute of Labour Sciences, Mumbai.
The government has already taken the decision in this regard. Rest of the provisions of 21.09.2006 shall continue to apply as before.
