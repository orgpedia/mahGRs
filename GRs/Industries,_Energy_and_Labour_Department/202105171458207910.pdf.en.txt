Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to be appointed to posts in the government service. Accordingly, the Central Government vide reference No. 2 here. The list of posts reserved for Persons with Disabilities as per notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published. Accordingly, the Central Government has decided to ensure the posts for the disabled in the posts of Group-A to Group-D which are in the state government establishment, the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, although the designations of such posts are different. 4 decisions have been made. Accordingly, the matter of ensuring the posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre on the establishment of the Electricity Inspectorate under the Department of Energy was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 2 here. The posts in the list of posts from Group-A to Group-D, which have been ensured for PwDs as per the notification dated 04.01.2021, are in the establishment of the Electricity Inspectorate and the posts in the said list, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the establishment of the Electricity Inspectorate, are being ensured for PwDs as shown in the return.
