Preamble: The 150th Birth Anniversary of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, aka Father of the Nation, is being celebrated by the Central and State Governments from 2018-19 to 2020-21. The festival will be held from 2nd October 2018. Accordingly, on the occasion of the exhibition organized by the Khadi Village Industries Board of the State of Maharashtra from 3rd to 6th October, 2018, on the sale and other activities of manufactured goods in the State, Shri. PM pays tributes to Mahatma Gandhi on his 150th birth anniversary
Various activities have been planned to mark the year.
In this regard, the Khadi and Village Industries Board of Maharashtra is implementing various activities to celebrate the 150th birth anniversary of the Father of the Nation. As a part of this, the Directorate of Handicrafts, Pune under the Board has developed a new attractive colour scheme for the office use of files, covers, folders and has printed the" Logo" of the 150th Birth Anniversary Year of the Central Government on the product. These products are of" eco-friendly" nature. The government was contemplating to take up the project. Accordingly, it has been decided to issue the following circular: Government Circular: On the occasion of the 150th birth anniversary of the Father of the Nation, Mahatma Gandhi, the files, covers, folders and other products required for daily office use at the government level should be purchased from the Directorate of Handicrafts, Pune under the Khadi and Village Industries Board of Maharashtra State.
2. All the Departments shall give instructions accordingly to the field offices under them for taking action as aforesaid.
