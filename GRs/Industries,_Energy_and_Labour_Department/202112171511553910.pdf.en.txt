Under the Solar Park Scheme, the Solar Power Project Developer (SPD) will provide the solar power to the concerned Solar Park Project Developer (SPPD).
It has been decided to constitute a committee to fix the charges for development of infrastructure, maintenance and repair (O& M) and other charges in the Dayabaya Solar Park.

The Proposal:
Ministry of New and Renewable Energy, Govt. of India. As per the official order dated November 12, 2021, the Solar Power Project Developer (SPD) under the Solar Park Scheme has notified the revised norms regarding the charges for development, maintenance and repair (O& M) and other charges for the solar park infrastructure to be paid to the concerned Solar Park Project Developer (SPPD). As per the provisions of Schedule 2 of the said amended Rules, the matter of constituting a committee for fixing such fee was under consideration of the Government.
