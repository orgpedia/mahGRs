For conversion of temporary posts into permanent posts, the Finance Department has issued Reference No. Under the Government decision here, the administrative departments whose review of the posts on the establishment of the offices has been completed and the government decision has been issued in this regard after the approval of the Chief Secretary Committee on the figure determined after the review, have been empowered to retain the posts within the limit of 80% of the total posts.
