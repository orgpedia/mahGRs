Preamble: The Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board, vide its Reference No. 5, has requested to provide a grant of Rs. 1244.06 lakhs and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) amounting to Rs. 45.56/-lakh respectively for meeting the expenditure on salary, superannuation salary and non-salary for the two months April, 2021 and May, 2021.
2. As the Budget Division of this Department has provided subsidy under the head Demand No.K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (28510585) under the sub-heading 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) to the extent of 75%, a subsidy of Rs. 18,66,09, 000/- (Rupees Eighteen Crore Sixty Six Lakh Nine Thousand only) has been disbursed to meet the expenses of salary, retirement salary, etc. for the month of July, August and September, 2021 under the Government decision dated 29th November, 2021.
3. The Government was considering providing subsidy to meet the post-salary expenses of Maharashtra State Khadi and Village Industries Board.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, a grant of Rs. 40,99,950/-lakh (Rupees Forty Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand Nine Hundred and Fifty only) is being sanctioned to Khadi and Village Industries Board for meeting the non-salary expenditure for the purpose of 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary).
Expenditure on this should be met from the grant sanctioned for the year 2021-22 under the target Demand No. K-7, 2851, Village Industries and Small Scale Industries, 105 Khadi and Village Industries (00) (00) (01) Subsidiary Grant to Khadi and Village Industries Board (Dattamat) (28510585) 31, Subsidiary Grant (Non-salaried) and should be spent under the same head.
The powers of withdrawal and disbursement of the said amount have been delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai. The Board shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government and the Drawing and Disbursing Officers within the prescribed time. Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai
