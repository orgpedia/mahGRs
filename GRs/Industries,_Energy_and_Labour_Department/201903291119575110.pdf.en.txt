The date is March 29, 2019
Read:- Government Resolution No. Saproyo-2015/ No. 133/ Industry-8, D. February 11, 2016
Government's decision: Read the above. Issue dated February 11, 2016. 11 is being amended as follows: 11) In order to enhance the capacity of entrepreneurs belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes, a maximum of 100% financial assistance will be provided by the State Government for providing infrastructure facilities in the said Group Industrial Development Groups. Under this, the following programmes/ activities will be covered: 1. Development of ancillary infrastructure under industrial cluster projects promoted by SC/ ST entrepreneurs, in which more than 50% of the beneficiaries will be from SC/ ST category. Present Group Industrial Development Group Infrastructure such as roads, electricity, water, etc.
Incubation centres, research centres, testing centres, mass processing centres and similar activities introduced for SC/ ST entrepreneurs 3. Development of necessary infrastructure in private/ cooperative industrial estates promoted by SC/ ST entrepreneurs reserved for 100% SC/ ST category.
Financial assistance of the same amount will be provided for the plots reserved for SC/ ST entrepreneurs in the industrial estates and industrial complexes promoted by the Government and Government undertakings such as MIDC, MSSIDC, KVIB etc.
The expenditure for the above will be borne by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance as well as the Department of Tribal Development from the budgetary provisions.
