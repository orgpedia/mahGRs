As per the directions in the letter dated 11.01.2019, the Government had constituted a State Resource Centre with a composition of 8 members under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Labour) vide Government Order No.3 of the said Act. However, due to some errors found in it, the matter of issuing a new government decision by overruling the government decision dated 11.01.2019. A decision in this regard is being taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the amendments made under the National Child Labour Project (NCLP), the Government vide Government Resolution dated 11.01.2019 regarding the establishment of State Resource Centre, hereby approves the establishment of State Resource Centre under the Chairmanship of Commissioner (Labour), Office of the Commissioner of Labour, Labour Bhawan, Bandra (East), Mumbai-400 051 as follows:
