The Proposal:
The Departmental Promotion Committee has been constituted as per the Government decision in Reference No. 1 for promotion to Group A (Gazetted) post in the Electricity Inspectorate. However, in reference to General Administration Department No. The consolidated guidelines on the procedure to be followed for promotion of Government officers/ employees have been prescribed under the Government Decision. As per the guidelines in the said Government decision, the matter of reconstitution of the Divisional Promotion Committee for giving promotion to the Group A (Gazetted) post in the Electricity Inspectorate was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Divisional Promotion Committee constituted for promotion to Group A (Gazetted) post in the Electricity Inspectorate is being reconstituted as follows.
