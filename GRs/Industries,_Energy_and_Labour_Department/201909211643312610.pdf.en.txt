Setting up a tripartite committee to study the problems of workers in the film, television and serial sectors and make recommendations to the government.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
Government Resolution No.Sankiran-2018/ S.No. 113 (Revised)/ Labourers-7A.
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: September 20, 2019.
Read:- Government Decision No. 2018/ No. 113/ Work-7A dated 7.3.2019.
Government's decision:
A tripartite committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 7.3.2019 to study in detail the problems of workers in the film, television and serial sector, labour and welfare laws applicable to them, their working style, facilities available to them and their registration and other issues and submit a report to the Government. The tripartite committee comprises representatives of the management and the workers.
It was under consideration of the Government to include some new representatives in the management representatives and labour representatives.
The following new members are being included in the Management Delegation and Labour Delegation of the Tripartite Committee constituted vide Government Resolution dated 7.3.2019:
