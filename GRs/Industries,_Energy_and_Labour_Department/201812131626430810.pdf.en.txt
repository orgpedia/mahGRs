GOVERNMENT ORDER: As per the provisions of section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the power of transfer of officers/ employees of the Electricity Inspectorate Branch has been notified and with the approval of the competent authority, Mr. V. V. Nagdev, Superintending Engineer, Aurangabad is being transferred to Superintending Engineer, Nagpur. Orders are being issued separately for the immediate dismissal of Dinesh Khonde, Superintending Engineer and the transfer of Mr. Khonde, Superintending Engineer.
Further, if any political pressure/ coercion is found to cancel or modify the transfers made under this order, the concerned Electrical Inspector shall initiate disciplinary action as per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
