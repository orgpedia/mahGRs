Preamble The Government has taken a decision regarding the scheme of supply of electricity through High Pressure Distribution System (HVDS) to the agricultural pump consumers in the State who are waiting for payment of electricity connection at the end of March, 2018. 1 has been approved. In order to benefit the farmers belonging to the Scheduled Tribes (ST) category in the state, it is necessary to energize the agricultural pumps of these farmers through High Pressure Distribution System (HVDS).
The pending agricultural pumps of the Scheduled Tribes will be energized on the HVDS system. Under the HVDS scheme in the state, 633 work orders have been issued under the Partial Turn Key, which includes 1, 30,744 agricultural pumps. Also, 12 work orders have been issued under Full Turn Key, which includes 61,671 agricultural pumps.
