PREAMBLE
Vide Email dated 07.04.2025 Shri. R. K. Jalan, Chairman, Council of Leather Export have
requested to attend The Shoe and Leather Fair arranged during Dt. 15.05.2025 to 17.05.2025 to the
Government of Maharashtra. It is also informed that on the eve of this Fair the Indian Consulate have
planned Buyer-Seller Meetings, Meetings with some anchor Investors and Chinese Machinery
manufacturers as well.
2. Focus Products scheme for footwear and leather sector is the scheme of Government of
India stands at the cusp of enhancing the productivity, quality and competitiveness of India's footwear.
and the leather sector. On this background the state government has decided to send four members'
State Level Delegation of Government of Maharashtra to attend "The Shoe and Leather Fair" to be
held on 15" May to 17" May, 2025 and accordingly before the Fair the meeting programme from 12" May to 14" May, 2025 planned by the Indian Consulate at Guangzhou and Fujian Cities of
China. The preferred programme for the business delegation would be attracting investment to the footwear, footwear components and leather products sector in Maharashtra.
RESOLUTION:-
Government of Maharashtra is pleased to accord ex-post facto approval to the visit of the State level delegation to attend " The Shoe and Leather Fair " to be held on 15th May to 17th
May, 2025 and accordingly before the Fair the meeting programme from 12th May to 14th May,
2025 planned by the Indian Consulate at Guangzhou and Fujian Cities of China.
