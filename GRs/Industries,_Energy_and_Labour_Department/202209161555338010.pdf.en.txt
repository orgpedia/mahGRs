Introduction: In the above reference No. In a letter dated 01.02.2021, the Labour Commissioner, Mumbai has proposed to change the computer system for issuing auto-generated registration/ rectification certificates as suggested by DPIIT under the Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP) 2022 in respect of" Establishment Registration/ Correction Certificate in Inter-State Migrant Workmen (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1979" after such period as may be decided by the State Government. Refer to 2. In the Government decision dated 28.4.2017, a period of 21 days has been prescribed for providing the above services. If the service is not provided during this period, the government was considering making necessary changes in the computer system for issuing registration/ correction certificates through auto-generated means.
Government Decision: As per the Government decision dated 28.4.2017, a period of 21 days has been prescribed under the Maharashtra Right to Service Act, 2015 to provide establishment registration/ amendment certificate services in the Inter-State Migrant Workmen (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1979.
In case the service is not provided within the prescribed period of 21 days, the government is approving the necessary changes in the computer system to issue registration/ improvement certificate through auto-generated after being deemed approved. In accordance with the approval of the said Government, the Labour Commissioner, Mumbai shall take immediate action to make necessary consequential changes in the relevant computer system at his level.
