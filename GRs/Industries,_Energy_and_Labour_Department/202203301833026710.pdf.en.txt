Preamble: In order to support the construction of projects under the Central Government's Micro and Small Enterprises-Collective Development Plan (MSE-CDP) and to increase the cost-effectiveness of the project, a scheme titled" Scheme of Grant-in-Aid to Enterprise Cluster Projects for Creation of Common State Facility" has been announced vide Government Decision dated 09/06/2010. Further, as per the Government decision dated 22/10/2010, the State Government's participation under the scheme has been fixed at 10% of the project cost (upper ceiling subsidy limit Rs. 1.50 crore).
Under the Scheme, the Central Government vide its order dated 08/08/2019 has accorded final approval for setting up of Common Facility Centre at Rice Mill Cluster, Pavani G Bhandara, an industrial cluster project with State Government's share grant of Rs. 1.50 crore.
As per the guidelines of the Micro, Small Enterprises-College Development Plan (MSE-CDP), the sanctioned grant of the Central Government is disbursed in the ratio of 40:30:30 to the projects sanctioned under the scheme. Also, for the disbursement of the grant approved by the Central Government, the participation of the special purpose vehicle organization should be 100% of the subsidy and the participation of the state government is proposed by the Central Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Central Government has accorded final approval for the construction of Common Facility Centre at Rice Mill Cluster, Pavani G. Bhandara under Micro Small Enterprises-Collective Development Scheme (MSE-CDP) project vide order dated 08/08/2019 at a total approved project cost of Rs. 1636.40 lakh with Central Government grant of Rs. 1050.00 lakh and State Government share grant of Rs. 150.00 lakh. Accordingly, the Government have decided to disburse State Participatory Grant of Rs. 150.00 lakh (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) as per the project approval to the Industrial Group Project of Rice Mill Cluster, Pavani G. Bhandara as per the provisions of the said scheme.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall arrange to transfer the amount of Rs. 150.00 lakh (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) from the Government treasury to the Special Purpose Vehicle of the Rice Mill Cluster, Pavani G. Bhandara. The powers to draw and disburse the aforesaid amount of Rs. 150.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) are being delegated to the Accounts Officer in the office of the Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai.
The Development Commissioner (Industries), Directorate of Industries, Mumbai shall take necessary action to monitor the progress of this special purpose vehicle and the proper utilization and utilization of the Government share capital.
