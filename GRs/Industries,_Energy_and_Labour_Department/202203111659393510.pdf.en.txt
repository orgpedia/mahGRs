Preamble: The Department of Labour is mandated to implement the Child and Adolescent Labour (Prohibition and Regulation) Act, 1986 effectively by disseminating and publicizing the provisions of the Act through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations for the purpose of disseminating the Act to the public through advertisements and publicity and to eliminate the evil practice of child labour through public participation. Accordingly, the. To mark the International Day against Child Labour on 12th June, a colourful advertisement was published in 12 Marathi dailies to eradicate the practice of child labour. In accordance with the directions given by the Minister (Labour), the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, Mumbai has been directed to The same was communicated vide letter dated 11.06.2021.
Accordingly, this advertisement has been published in 12 newspapers by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, Mumbai. A proposal has been submitted to the government by the Labour Commissioner's office to pay the advertisement dues. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative and post-facto approval to the proposal for payment of the said advertisement was under consideration of the Government. A decision in this regard is being taken.
Government Decision: On the occasion of International Day against Child Labour on 12th June, an advertisement to eradicate child labour was prepared by Ed-way Design, Pune/ Kolhapur and subsequently published in 12 selected Marathi dailies.
