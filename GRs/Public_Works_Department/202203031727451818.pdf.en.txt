Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of major district roads in Naigaon, Bhokar, Umri, Biloli and Dharmabad talukas of Nanded district as state highways.
Taking into consideration the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded, the following roads in Naigaon, Bhokar, Umri, Biloli and Dharmabad talukas of Nanded district are being included as state highways.
