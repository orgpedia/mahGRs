Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Kage taluka of Beed district as major district roads, vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Beed has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Beed, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Beed, the following roads in Kage Taluka of Beed District are being upgraded.
