Government Circular:
The final seniority of the cadre of Superintending Engineer (Construction) for the period dated 19.2.1982 to 31.12.2003 in Public Works Department has been published vide circular dated 26.2.2018 under reference.
2. Provisional seniority list dated 01.01.2022 for the period 01.01.2004 to 31.12.2021 of the cadre of Superintending Engineer (Civil) in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 attached to this circular “Form -A” is being published.
3. Hon. High Court, Bombay vide judgment dated 04.08.2017 in Petition No. 2797/2015 invalidated reservation in promotion. Hon. Against the decision of the High Court, the State Government filed a special leave petition no. 28306/2017 filed. The said petition is still pending and subject to the final decision of the said petition, the provisional seniority list dated 01.01.2022 for the period of 01.01.2004 to 31.12.2021 of the cadre of Superintending Engineer (Construction) is being published through this circular. S.B. Department Government Order No. POG 1220/P.No.182/Service-1, dt. By October 21, 2021 Mr. c.h. Rajput has been promoted to the post of Superintending Engineer (Civil) and as he has not yet joined the promoted post, his name is omitted from the said seniority list.
4. If there are any mistakes / errors in the details of Superintending Engineer (Civil) like name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority etc. contained in the said seniority list, the same should be reported in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular along with necessary proof. - Your representations / objections / suggestions should be brought to the direction of Govt. If the mistakes/mistakes are not brought to the attention of the Government in writing within the prescribed time, the seniority list of the cadre of Superintending Engineer (Construction) dated 01.01.2022 will be published on the basis that there is no objection.
