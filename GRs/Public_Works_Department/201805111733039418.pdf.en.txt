Introduction:
As per reference letter from Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad, two roads namely 1) Other District Road - 30 Masala - Bhisevagholi Shivarasta to Major District Road - 6 (Pimpri Amba) and 2) Masala to State Road 211 (Tandulja) in Latur Taluka of Latur District. The Government has received a proposal to include the Road Development Scheme 2001-21 as a rural route.
Tandulja is an important market on this road. Also the road is important in terms of sugarcane transportation. The said roads are unpaved roads and no land acquisition is required for this. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Latur to include the said roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the above mentioned road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and demand of Zilla Parishad, Latur, 1) Other District Route-30 - Masala - Bhisevagholi Shivarasta to Principal District Route-6 (Pimpri Amba) in Latur District, Latur Taluka km From 0/00 to 4/00 (4.00 km) this road is Rural Marg No.165
2) Masala to State Highway 211 (Tandulja) Road Km. 0/00 to 4/50 (4.500 KM) This road is rural road no. 166 is therefore being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
