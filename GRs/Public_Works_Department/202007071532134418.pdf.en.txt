Introduction : Department of General Administration, Government Decision no. Matansam/Nasti 04/224/39, dt. dated 26/5/2004
To the Secretary of the concerned Department for posts which do not require computer training in serial number 3
A provision has been made that exemption can be given from Computer Aptitude Test. Accordingly, the Chief Engineer (Electrical), Public Works Department, Mumbai vide reference No. 3 dated vide letter dated November 05, 2019 “Considering the duties of fourteen technical posts in their subordinate offices
It has been requested to exempt the posts from passing the Computer Aptitude Test”. Pursuant to the said request, the matter of exempting the said posts from passing the Computer Aptitude Test was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Reference No. 2 of Directorate of Information Technology. As per the provisions dated 26th May, 2004, the following fourteen outstation technical posts under the Chief Engineer (Electrical), Public Works Department, Mumbai office, considering their duties, are hereby granted exemption from Computer Aptitude Test:
