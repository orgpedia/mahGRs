Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade other district roads in Wardha Taluka of Wardha district as Major District Roads under the reference letter. The Wardha Zilla Parishad has also demanded that the road be upgraded.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Based on the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the following other district roads of Wardha taluka of Wardha district are being upgraded as Major District Roads.
