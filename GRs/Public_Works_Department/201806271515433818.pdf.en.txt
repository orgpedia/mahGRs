Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of 8 rural roads in Nagpur - Rural Taluka of Nagpur District as Major District Roads through the letter under reference. Considering the increasing traffic and industrialization on these roads, the government was considering upgrading these roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Following the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, the following roads in Nagpur Rural Taluka of Nagpur District are being upgraded.
