Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Khatav Taluka of Satara District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said road and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Khatav Taluka of Satara district are being upgraded as rural roads.
