Foreword : Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan vide letter under reference has submitted to the Government the proposal to include the unclassified road connecting Prajima-1 to Ijima-191 in Talasari Taluka of Palghar District as Rural Road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Considering the use of the said road as well as the importance of the road, and taking into consideration the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Palghar, Maharashtra State Human Rights Commission filed Sumoto case no. 3544/13/30/2022 hearing, the said road was under consideration to be included in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21 as a rural road in accordance with the instructions given to take action regarding the construction of a bridge on the said road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Palghar, the following road in Talasari Taluka of Palghar District is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as a rural road.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road                   | Chain no      | Total length to be upgraded) Km. | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Road connecting Prajima-1 to Ijima-191. | 0/00 to 1/800 | 1.800 m.                         | Rural Marg-321      |
| Total | 1.800 km.                               |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
