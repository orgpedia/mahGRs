Preface : To check the quality of the construction projects in Palghar district, to provide quality work and regular construction test results required for quality control of the work, to establish a district laboratory under Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Navi Mumbai, Mauje Manor, District Palghar. It was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision : Office of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan Under Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Thane Sub-Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Palghar, Road Project Sub-Division No.4, Kokan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai Subject to the conditions that there will be no additional financial burden on the Government, approval is being given to operate as District Laboratory, Mauje Manor, District Palghar.
2. Deputy Engineer, Road Projects Sub Division No. 17 posts in various cadres have been sanctioned for the office 4, Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai as per figure. Among them, after providing the necessary posts for District Laboratory, Palghar, the remaining posts should be adjusted under Konkan Regional Division by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan.
3. The Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Navi Mumbai should take action to fill the posts of Laboratory Assistant and Laboratory Attendant required for the District Laboratory, Palghar without creating new posts from the employees equivalent to the said post.
4. District Laboratory, Mauje Manor, Dist. The laboratory at Palghar will be under the direct control of the Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Navi Mumbai. Therefore, the Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Navi Mumbai should supply the required / deficient equipment and materials (structural / electrical) for the District Laboratories.
5. Trained Officers/Employees provided for the District Field Laboratory should not be transferred before the completion of their tenure in the said post, so that the time and expenditure of the Government on training is not wasted.
