Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik vide the letter under reference, owned by the Public Works Department. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marg (Old R. P. D. Road) has been requested to be transferred to the Railway Administration. Also to transfer the aforesaid road to Railway Administration
Approved by Municipal Council, Bhusawal. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to transfer the said road to the railway administration.
Government Decision:
Old National Highway No. 6 (Old R. P. D. Road) passing through Bhusawal city is 5.800 km.
Length of road is being transferred to Railway Administration, Bhusawal subject to the following conditions.
1. Kandari is a village along the said road and this is the only road available for traffic for the said village. Railway Administration, Bhusawal should ensure that the traffic of Sabab Kandari village is not disturbed.
2. The maintenance and repair of the said road will be done through the railway administration. No additional subsidy will be given by the State Government for the said road, nor can the Railway Administration make a demand from the State Government in this regard.
3. All court cases on the said road will be handled by Railway Administration, Bhusawal.
4. The Railway Administration, Bhusawal will be responsible for ensuring that there is no encroachment on the said road.
5. The ownership of the said road will remain with the Public Works Department.
6. In future, if it is decided to hand over the said road again to the Public Works Department as per the requirement, the responsibility of the Railway Administration, Bhusawal will remain the responsibility of making the said road in good condition and handing it over to the Public Works Department and in this regard, the demand for funds/claims/expenditure etc. regarding any works on the said road will not be accepted.
