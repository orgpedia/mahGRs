Introduction : The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to upgrade the length of 3 rural roads in Shirol Taluka of Kolhapur District as Major District Roads. In the General Assembly of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, a demand has been made to upgrade the said roads.
These roads are very necessary for communication and traffic to be safe and fast. Taking into account the traffic volume, number of villages, population and road usage on the said roads as well as the demand of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad Kolhapur, the following rural roads in Shirol Taluka of Kolhapur district are being upgraded as major district roads.
