Government Decision: Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation has been established by order dated 9th July, 1996 and the Board of Directors of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation has been revised on 9th February, 2000.
2. The government has taken a decision to cancel the appointments of the members appointed on the governmental / non-governmental boards and committees established by various laws / acts / rules under the department and reconstitute new boards and committees.
3. Accordingly, the government decision dt. 05 September, 2019 and dated Appointments of the following members as Non-Government Members on Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation with effect from September 13, 2019 are hereby cancelled.
