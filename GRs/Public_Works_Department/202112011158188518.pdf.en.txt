Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Himayatnagar Taluka of Nanded District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Nanded has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded,
The said road was under the consideration of the government to upgrade it as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Himayatnagar in Nanded district considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded
