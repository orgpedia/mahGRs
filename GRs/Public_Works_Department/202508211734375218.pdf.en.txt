Konkan during the upcoming Ganeshotsav period
Toll waiver and other facilities to be provided to the visiting Ganesh devotees on the occasion of Ganeshotsav.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works,
Circular No. Khaksas-2024 / P.No.329 / Roads-8
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032.
Date: August 22, 2025
Circular
The government has decided to provide toll free and other facilities to Ganesh devotees visiting Konkan during the upcoming Ganeshotsav 2025.
Accordingly, all concerned are being informed through this circular as follows.
The government was considering exempting vehicles of devotees going to Konkan for Ganeshotsav 2025 from tax. Following that. 23/08/2025 that is. During this period, vehicles of Ganeshotsav devotees going to Konkan and Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation buses are being exempted from the team tax at MASA Construction Department's and Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation's toll plazas on Mumbai Bangalore National Highway (Ram-48), Mumbai Goa National Highway (Ram-66), etc.
A) For this purpose, stickers in the form of "Ganeshotsav 2025, Konkan Darshan" stickers with the text "Tax-free pass" with the text "Train Number, Driver's Ala" and stickers (Passage pattern with Attached Attachment - A) should be made available to police stations, Traffic Police AM and RTO offices in coordination with Transport Department, Traffic Police / Police, concerned Regional Transport Department (RTO) as per the number required. Passes will also be accepted for return travel.
(b) Passes shall be made available to the Police Department or Regional Transport Department of the district from where the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation buses and other vehicles are coming, as in "A" above. All buses departing from the concerned district for Gauri-Ganapati will depart by affixing / affixing the relevant TSA to the team tax-exempt pass.
The consolidated information regarding the number of passes to be issued by the rural or urban police / Regional Transport Office should be submitted to the Deputy Secretary (Kh1), Public Works Department, 4th Floor, Mantralaya, Mumbai-32 for information.

3. The Police and Transport Department should publish the information about the pass facilities and their availability during Ganeshotsav in the notification / advertisement as required for the public.
