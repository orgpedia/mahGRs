the decision of the government. As on 24.03.2023, the temporary posts in the office of Chief Engineer (Electrical), Public Works Department, Mumbai were continued from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
Now, under this Government decision, a total of 264 posts of 171 posts on regular temporary establishment and 93 posts on converted temporary establishment in the office under the Chief Engineer (Electrical), Public Works Department, Mumbai, are being approved to be continued from September 01, 2023 to February 29, 2024 subject to the condition that they are not vacant for more than six months.
