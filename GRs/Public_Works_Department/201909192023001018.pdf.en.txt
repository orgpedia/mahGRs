Government Order:
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. Q. no Pagrit, Executive Engineer, Nagpur Improvement Trust, Nagpur is being posted in the interest of public service and for administrative reasons, to the vacant post of Deputy Executive Engineer, Nagpur Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Nagpur.
2. Mr. Pagrit should assume charge of his new post promptly and thereafter submit his assumption of office report to the government. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
4. Mr. As Pagrut is being transferred for administrative reasons, he will be paid the joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
5. Mr. Pagrit has been posted on deputation subject to the terms and conditions of deputation prescribed in Appendix II to the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Post-Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 .
6. Mr. Pagrit should inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Co-ordination Centre, Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
