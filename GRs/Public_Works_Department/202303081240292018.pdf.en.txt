See :- 1) Letter of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune Ja.No.Ka-2/Resha/05/474/Year 2022, dated 24/01/2022 Preface :
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Koregaon Taluka of Satara District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has requested to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Satara, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision: Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Koregaon Taluka of Satara District are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
