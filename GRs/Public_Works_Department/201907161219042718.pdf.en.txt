Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of Wagholi Bhavadi Tulapur Road IJIMA 126 and Apti Vadhu Sanswadi to Talegaon Dhamdere Road IJIMA 144 as Major District Roads in Pune District under reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Pune has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said road and the number of villages, population, use of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Pune, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Pune, the following roads in Pune district are being upgraded.

As a result, the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 in Pune district has a total length of 34,260 km of major district road grade roads. By increasing the total length (4247.740+34.260) i.e. 4282.00 km. As well as other district roads, the total length of roads is 34.260 km. By reducing the total length (2281.240 - 34.260) i.e. 2246.980 km. That will be it.
Also, no works or budgetary works will be proposed under any other scheme and no new land acquisition will be done for the said road in the length of which the work has been completed or proposed under the Pradhan Mantri Gramsadak / Chief Minister Gramsadak Scheme and other schemes or there is remaining liability period for the work done. Approval is being given to upgrade the road subject to these conditions.
