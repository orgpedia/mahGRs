Refer to :-1) Letter of Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune Ja.No.MUA (Pune)/ Ka-2/Resha/54/1867/2019, dated 08/04/2019.
Preface: The government has received a proposal to include the unplanned road No.189 Parle Malusrewadi Dhangarwada in Chandgad taluka of Kolhapur district as a rural road in Ravio 2001-21 through the letter under reference from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune.
The demand of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur is to include the use of the said road as well as the road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to include the above road development scheme as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, R.M.No.189 Parle Malusrewadi Dhangarwada in Chandgad Taluka of Kolhapur District is an unplanned road Chain No. 0/00 to 2/500 Length 2.500 km This unplanned road is being included in Road Development Plan 2001-21 as Gramin Marg No-281.
Consequently, the total length of rural roads in Kolhapur district in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 was 2,500 km. By increasing the total length (4351.436 + 2.500) i.e. 4353.936 km. I. That will be it.
