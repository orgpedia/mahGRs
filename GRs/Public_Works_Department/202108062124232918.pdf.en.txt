Government Order:
In accordance with the provisions of Sections 4 (1), 4 (2) and 4 (3) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority, as well as General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 9.7 .2021 as per provision of Public Works Department Smt. Res. Gadekar, Executive Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Aurangabad has been transferred in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience to Shri. no. A. The post of Assistant Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, which is vacant due to the transfer of Poredi. Mr. Poredi&#39;s transfer orders will be issued separately.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Aurangabad.
3. Mrs. Gadekar should assume charge of her new post promptly and submit her assumption of office report to the government without delay. Smt Gadekar m. no Sec. (Period of Joining, Non-Accepted Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 shall continue to require joining the posted post within the given joining period. Otherwise the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of the said rule, they shall be eligible for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. As Smt. Gadekar is being transferred for administrative reasons, she will be paid tenure, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
