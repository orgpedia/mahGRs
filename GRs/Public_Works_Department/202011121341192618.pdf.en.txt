Introduction: As the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is coming to an end next year, it is necessary to take up the road development plan for the next 20 years 2021-2041.
It is necessary to form a committee from the government level to prepare the proposed road development plan for the year 2021-2041. For this, the government has decided to form a committee to prepare the road development plan for the year 2021-2041.
Government Decision: The following committee is being formed to prepare the road development plan for the year 2021-2041.
