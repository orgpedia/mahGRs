Government order
As per the present order, Junior Engineer (Diploma holder) in District Technical Service in Zilla Parishad mentioned in the attached Annexure “A” in the selection list for the year 2020-21 on the post of Deputy Divisional Officer (Construction) in Maharashtra Engineering Service, Group-A in pay scale S-20 Rs.56, 100-1,77,600 are being promoted on a temporary basis in the scale of pay and post promotion to the places shown against their names in Annexure &quot;A&quot;.
2. A Junior Engineer (Diploma Holder) in the District Technical Service of the Zilla Parishad is being temporarily promoted to the post of Deputy Divisional Officer (Construction) subject to the following conditions: (1) Special permission petition filed in the Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court No. 28306 on the basis of seniority on the vacant post in the promotion quota. /2017 is being promoted subject to the decision.
(2) In the said temporary promotion Writ Petition Stamp No.10876/2021 and Writ Petition Stamp No.10878/2021 Hon. As per the decision of the High Court, Bombay dated 25.5.2021, the said writ petition shall be subject to final decision.
(3) As the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, the officers in the select list will not get any right of regularity as a result of this temporary promotion.
(4) Action will be taken as per S.P.V., Government Decision, dated 12.9.2016 in respect of the officers who refuse the promotion in the said promotion order.
3. The said orders of promotion are being issued subject to the condition that there is no Departmental inquiry pending or proposed / Court Case / FIR filed under Rules 8 &amp; 10 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline &amp; Appeal) Rules, 1979 against the above mentioned Engineers. However, if a Departmental Inquiry/Court Case/FIR is filed against any of these Engineers on the date of this order, these orders shall stand automatically in respect of them.

4. In the accompanying Annexure “A” A.No. 29 Engineers are eligible according to seniority and reservation for disabled persons and A.No. Engineers of 71 and 72 are being temporarily promoted to the post reserved for the differently abled category.
5. The concerned Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad is requested to immediately relieve the officers in Annexure “A” assigned to them as per the said promotion order and order them to join at the place of new appointment with minimum joining period without going on leave.
6. The concerned junior engineer (degree holder) should join the post appointed by promotion within 1 month from the date of this order, otherwise, S.P.V., notification, dt. As on 14.7.2021 the period of absence of concerned Junior Engineer (Graduate) shall be treated as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
7. The concerned Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works / Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad is informed that if the promoted engineers do not join within 1 month, the names of such officers should be reported to the Government for further action at the Government level as mentioned in Para.6.
8. As per the said promotion order, the posts posted elsewhere are deputation posts, excluding the officers posted to the post on the establishment of the Public Works Department.
The said appointment is made subject to the terms and conditions of deputation prescribed in paragraph two of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Entry Period, Non-Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981.
