Regarding inclusion of unplanned roads in Ashti Taluka of Wardha District as rural roads in the Road Development Scheme 2001-21.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Govt Decision No: RAVO-2022/P.No.50/Plan-2
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032
Dated - April 22, 2022.
Refer :- 1) Letter of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur Ja.No.PRA/1608, dated 15/03/2022.
Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Ashti Taluka of Wardha District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also Zilla Parishad, Wardha has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said road as well as the resolution of the General Assembly, Zilla Parishad, Wardha, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the following road in Ashti Taluka of Wardha District is being upgraded as a rural road.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road           | Chain no      | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Peth Ahmadpur to Jaitapur road. | 0/00 to 4/500 | 4.500 km.                        | Rural Road - 46     |
| Total | 4.500 km.                       |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Wardha district in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 4,500 km. By increasing the total length (2304.00 +4.500) i.e. 2308.500 km. I. That will be it.
