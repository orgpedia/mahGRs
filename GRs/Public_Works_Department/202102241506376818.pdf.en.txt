Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads in Paithan taluka of Aurangabad district as major district roads vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad, the following roads in Aurangabad district are being upgraded.
