Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan vide letter under reference Uran S. T. A request has been made regarding the transfer of Std Charphata to Bokadvira (Charphata) Police Post Road State Highway No.103 Road to CIDCO. Also, the Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Konkan has approved the transfer of the above road from the Public Works Department to the CIDCO administration.
Accordingly, the said road was under the consideration of the government to be handed over to the CIDCO administration.

is
Uran S. T. The road from Stad Charphata to Bokadvira (Charphata) Police Chowki Road State Road No.103 is being handed over to CIDCO subject to the following conditions.
1) Ownership of road space to be transferred will remain with the department.
2) CIDCO shall obtain all the general approvals of the Chief Engineer of Public Works Department, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan for the technical proposal for improvement/reconstruction of the road through CIDCO and it will be the responsibility of CIDCO administration to work as per the approval. The said work will have to be done as per the standards laid down by the Indian Road Congress (IRC).
3) According to the length of the road to be handed over, no-objection certificates, no-objection certificates and other similar certificates should be issued through the CIDCO administration. CIDCO administration will remain responsible for following the prevailing rules of the government while giving the said certificate.
4) CIDCO administration will remain fully responsible for ensuring that there is no encroachment in the length of the road to be transferred.
5) The Public Works Department shall have the authority to permit billboards to be erected along the road in the length of the road to be transferred and to collect revenue.
6) If it is necessary to deal with widening / reconstruction of bridge in the length of the road, the said works as well as road maintenance and repair works should be done from the funds of CIDCO.
7) After the said road is handed over to CIDCO, appropriate changes should be noted in the annual statement of road length.
