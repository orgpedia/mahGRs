Introduction:
Infrastructure Committee of the Cabinet in its meeting dated 30.11.2015 has approved greenfield alignment of Nagpur-Mumbai Expressway and appointment of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited as implementing agency. Accordingly, under the government decision dated 19.08.2016, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited has been appointed as the implementing agency for this project.
Also, in accordance with the implementation, fund raising and related activities of Nagpur-Mumbai High Speed Expressway (Maharashtra Prosperity Highway) project, Nagpur Mumbai Super Communication Expressway Ltd. It has been approved to establish a special purpose vehicle under this name. Considering the above matters, the issue of approving a tripartite agreement between the Government of Maharashtra, the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited, and the Nagpur-Mumbai Rapid Communications Expressway Limited and the draft agreement for the Nagpur-Mumbai Rapid Communications Expressway (Maharashtra Prosperity Highway) project was under the consideration of the government.
