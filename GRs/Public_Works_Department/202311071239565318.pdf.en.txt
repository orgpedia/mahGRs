Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the following other district roads and rural roads in Kalyan and Thane taluks of Thane district as major district roads under the reference letter. The Zilla Parishad and Thane have also demanded that these roads be upgraded.
Considering the number of villages on these roads, the population, the use of the roads and the approval of the Zilla Parishad, Thane, the government was considering to upgrade these roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Based on the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Thane, the following other district roads and rural roads in Kalyan and Thane taluks of Thane district are being upgraded as major district roads.
