Government Circular:
The revised final seniority scale of Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) in Maharashtra Engineering Service Class-1 for the period from 01.01.2010 to 31.12.2012 has been published vide circular dated 18.04.2015 under reference no.2.
2. In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department at Reference No. 1 and the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, the officers appointed by direct service during the period from 01.01.2013 to 01.01.2019 The provisional seniority list for the post of Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) inclusive is being published as “Form-A” attached to this circular.
3. The said circular should be brought to the immediate notice of the Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) in Form “A” by all the Regional and Zonal Offices under their jurisdiction, and a copy signed by them should be preserved in their records.
4. If any errors / mistakes are found in the details of Assistant Executive Engineer (Construction) like name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., contained in the said seniority list, they must be corrected in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular along with necessary proof. Your representations / objections / suggestions should be brought to the attention of Govt. If the mistakes/defects are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the prescribed time, the provisional seniority list for the period from 01.01.2013 to 01.01.2019 of the cadre of Assistant Executive Engineer (Construction) will be finalised, assuming that there is no objection.
