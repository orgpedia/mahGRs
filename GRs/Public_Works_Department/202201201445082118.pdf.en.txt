Government Circular:
Provisional seniority list dated 01.01.2021 of Engineers working in the cadre of Graduate Deputy Engineer (Electrical) in Maharashtra Electrical Engineering Service in Electricity Organization of Public Works Department of Government of Maharashtra has been published vide circular dated 13.12.2021 under reference number 3.
2. According to the government circular dated 13.12.2021, the government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer (Electricity), Public Works Department regarding the errors in the temporary seniority list, through a letter dated 17.12.2021, and according to the documentary evidence received along with the said proposal. And the errors have been rectified as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021.
3. Hon. High Court, Bombay vide judgment dated 04.08.2017 in Petition No. 2797/2015 invalidated reservation in promotion. Hon. Against the decision of the High Court, the State Government filed a special leave petition no. 28306/2017 filed. The said petition is still pending and subject to the final decision of the said petition and in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, Engineers working in the cadre of Graduate Sub-Engineer (Electrical) in the Maharashtra Electrical Engineering Service in the Electrical Organization of the Public Works Department dated 01.01. .2021 provisional seniority list is being published as “Form-B” attached with this circular. According to the judgments in the above court cases, if there is any change in the selection lists after the year 2004, the seniority list will be changed accordingly.
