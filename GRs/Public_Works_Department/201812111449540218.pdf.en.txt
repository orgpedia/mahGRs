Introduction:
Appointing authority regarding appointment by direct service, promotion and deputation of Group &quot;A&quot; and Group &quot;B&quot; (Gazetted) officers as well as Group &quot;B&quot; (Non-Gazetted), Group &quot;C&quot; and Group &quot;D&quot; employees in the Government Service as per the Government Decision referred to by the General Administration Department. are determined. Also, in that Government Decision, the administrative departments have been notified to issue orders to declare post-wise appointing authority for posts in Group “B (Non-Gazetted), Group “C” and Group “D”. Accordingly, the issue of determining the appointing authority in the Public Works Department was under consideration.
