Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and in the performance of Government duties
As per the provisions of Section 4(4) and 4(5) of the Prevention of Delays Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority and also as per the provisions of the General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 29.7.2021 Shri. Division, Gadchiroli has been transferred as per his request and in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience as Executive Engineer, Public Works Department no. 1, Gondia is being done on this vacant post.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad (S.B.) Division, Gadchiroli.
3. Mr. Labhane should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. Mr. Labhane M. no Sec. (Period of Joining, Non-Acceptance of Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 shall continue to require joining the posted post within the given joining period. Otherwise the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Labhane is being transferred as per his request, he will not be paid joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance.
6. Mr. Labhane to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Co-ordination Centre, Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
