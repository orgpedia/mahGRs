Refer to :- 1) Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur&#39;s letter Ja.No.Ka-Pra/7403, dated 04/09/2019.
Introduction : The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the following unplanned roads in Katol Taluk as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also Zilla Parishad, Nagpur has demanded to include the said roads in the scheme.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision: Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the following roads in Katol Taluka of Nagpur district are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
