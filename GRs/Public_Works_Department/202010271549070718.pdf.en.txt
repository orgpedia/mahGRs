Government order
Smt. Asha Jatal, Assistant Engineer Grade-2 (Civil) from the 2018-19 selection list, is hereby promoted ad hoc to the post of Sub Divisional Engineer (Civil) in Maharashtra Engineering Service Group-A from the cadre of Assistant Engineer Grade-2 (Civil). . After promotion, Mrs. Asha Jatal is being posted under Revenue Division, Konkan-1. Detailed posting of Mrs. Asha Jatal on the post of Deputy Divisional Engineer should be given by the Chief Engineer, S.B.P.V. Division, Kokan to the vacant post in Ratnagiri District.
2. The promotion to be given to Mrs. Asha Jatal is Hon. Subject to the final decision of the Special Leave Petition No.28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court, the vacancy in the open category in the promotion quota is being filled on a purely temporary basis. Also, the said promotion is being given subject to the order issued from time to time by the General Administration Department regarding promotion and reservation.
3. The promotion being given to Smt. Asha Jatal is purely temporary and she will not get seniority in the cadre of Deputy Divisional Engineer as per the said promotion. Also, if necessary, such temporarily promoted engineers will be demoted.
4. The said promotions are being made subject to the condition that no departmental inquiry is pending or proposed / court case / FIR filed against Smt. However, if a departmental enquiry/court case/FIR is filed against them on the date of this order, these promotion orders will automatically stand cancelled.
