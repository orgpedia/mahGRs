Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding inclusion of unplanned roads in Selu Taluka of Parbhani District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also, Zilla Parishad, Parbhani has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the following road in Selu Taluka of Parbhani district is being upgraded as a rural road.
