Introduction:
The Government has taken the following decision regarding the purchase of additional vehicles for the Public Works Department for the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Memorial Project Implementation and Coordination Committee.
Government Decision:
The combined vehicle review of the Public Works Department (self) and its subordinate offices is currently pending. Therefore, as per the Government Decision of Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department, the approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee has been taken for the purchase of the above mentioned vehicle.
2. Purchase of new vehicle for the use of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Memorial Project Implementation and Coordination Committee is being approved in compliance with prevailing government rules and subject to the following terms and conditions.
a) Driver post must be approved / available. Under no circumstances should a new driver post be created.
b) The purchase of the said new vehicle should be made as per the prevailing tariff of the government approved manufacturing company.
c) While purchasing a new vehicle, Government Decision of Finance Department of Reference No. 2 should be strictly followed.
d) The said vehicle shall remain under the jurisdiction of the Executive Engineer, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Memorial Project Department, Mumbai.
