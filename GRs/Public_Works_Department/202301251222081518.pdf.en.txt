Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400032.

Reference : Letter No.MU(Co)/Technical Establishment/P.No.4/2023/165 dated January 13, 2023 from Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan.
Preface : The Sub Divisional Office under Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan, Sub Divisional Office under the command of Public Works Board, Ratnagiri, Executive Engineer, Public Works Division, Sawantwadi, Sub Divisional Office, Malvan is functioning at present. is
Public Works Sub Division, Malvan, though this sub division comes under the divisional office of Public Works Department, Sawantwadi, Malvan Taluka and Malvan Sub Divisional Office are geographically close to Public Works Department, Kankavli, and it will be easy to supervise the scope and work of Malvan Sub Division from this department. Also, Public Works Department, Sawantwadi includes 05 talukas namely Kudal, Sawantwadi, Dodamarg, Vengurla and Malvan, and Public Works Department, Kankavali includes 03 talukas namely Devgad, Vaibhavwadi and Kankavali. Therefore, if Malvan Public Works Sub-Division is added to Public Works Department, Kankavli office, there will be 04 talukas in the scope of both the departments and in order to maintain balance in the geographical area and also in the development works, the matter of classifying Public Works Sub-Department, Malvan under the divisional office of Public Works Department, Kankavli was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan, Superintending Engineer under Regional Division, Executive Engineer under Public Works Board, Ratnagiri, Deputy Engineer under Public Works Division, Sawantwadi, Public Works Sub Division, Malvan, any post approved by Govt. Subject to the condition that there will be no financial burden, approval is being given to the class under the divisional office of Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Kankavli. Accordingly, the revised structure of the Divisional Office is as follows:
