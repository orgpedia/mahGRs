Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Pune, has submitted a proposal to the Government under reference to include the unplanned road in Gadhinglaj Taluka of Kolhapur district as a rural road in the Road Development Scheme 2001-21. The Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, has also demanded that the road be included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as a rural road.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following roads in Gadhinglaj Taluka of Kolhapur district are being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as rural roads.
