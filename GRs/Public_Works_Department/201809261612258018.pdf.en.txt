Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of 6 other district roads and 2 rural roads as major district roads in Nashik district through a reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following roads in Nashik district are being upgraded.
