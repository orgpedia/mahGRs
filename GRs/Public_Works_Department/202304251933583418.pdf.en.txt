Government Order:
According to the Government Order under reference, Shri. Dinesh Budha Bagul, Executive Engineer (Suspended) has been reinstated in Government service, subject to the decision of the criminal case against him and the final decision of the Departmental Inquiry. Accordingly, Mr. Dinesh Budha Bagul is posted on the non-executive post of Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation, Mumbai.
2. Mr. Bagul should assume charge of his new post immediately and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. Mr. Bagul will be required to join the posted post within the tenure provided by the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Joining, Non-Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. Otherwise, their period of absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The Controlling Officers concerned are hereby informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of assumption of charge by the posting officers.
Also, if the concerned officer does not join the place of posting within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government, so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately.
4. Mr. Bagul has been posted on deputation subject to the terms and conditions of deputation prescribed in Appendix II to the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Post-Service and Benefits during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. is
