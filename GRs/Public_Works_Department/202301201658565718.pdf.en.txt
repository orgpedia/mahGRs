Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads in Shevgaon taluka of Ahmednagar district as major district roads through the letter under reference.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar, the following roads in Shevgaon Taluka of Ahmednagar district are being upgraded as major district roads.
