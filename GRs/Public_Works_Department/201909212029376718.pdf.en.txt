Introduction: A proposal for upgradation of other district roads and rural roads in Khatav taluka of Satara district as major district roads has been submitted to the government by Chief Engineer Public Works Regional Division, Pune.
As there is a lot of traffic on the proposed road, the Zilla Parishad Satara has demanded to upgrade the said roads. Considering the importance of the said roads, the proposed roads were under the consideration of the government for upgradation.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Khatav Taluka of Satara District are being upgraded as Major District Roads.
