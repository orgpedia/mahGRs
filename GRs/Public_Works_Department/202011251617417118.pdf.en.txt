Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Kokan has submitted a proposal to the Government for upgrading the road Kharvate Omli Tamhanmala Gulwane Ijima 49 in Chiplun Taluka of Ratnagiri District to the status of Prajima through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the usage of the said road and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, Kharawate Omli Tamhanmala Gulwane Ijima 49 in Chiplun Taluk of Ratnagiri District, chain number 0/00 to 14/950, total length 14.950 km .I And, this road is being upgraded as Major District Road No.-102.
As a result, the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 in Ratnagiri district has a total length of 14,950 km of major district road grade roads. By increasing the total length (1936.525 +14.950) i.e. 1951.475 km. As well as other district roads, the total length of roads is 14.950 km. By reducing the total length (1231.410-14.950) i.e. 1216.46 km. That will be it.
