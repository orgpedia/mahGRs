Foreword : The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide the letter under reference has submitted a proposal for inclusion of the unplanned road from Mudgad - ECOG - Vikram Yuvraj Patil to Madhukar Ramrao Patil to the farm in Nilanga Taluka of Latur District as a rural road in the Road Development Scheme 2001-21. is
Earthwork has been done on the said road under Magrarohoyo and it needs to be included in the road development plan for further maintenance and repair. The total length of the said road is 2.300 km and no land acquisition is required for the road. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Latur in this regard. Considering this fact, the said road was under consideration of the government to include it as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision: Considering the demand of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and Zilla Parishad, Latur, the road to Madhukar Ramrao Patil&#39;s farm is 2.300 km from Mudgad - EKOG- Vikram Yuvraj Patil in Nilanga Taluka of Latur District. Long unplanned road is being included in Road Development Plan 2001-21 as Gramin Marg No.167.
As a result the total length of rural road grade roads in Latur district is 2.300 km. With this increase, the total length is (3651.710 + 3.400) i.e. 3655.110 km. That will be it.
