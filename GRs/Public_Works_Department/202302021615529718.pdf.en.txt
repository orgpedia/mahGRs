Introduction: Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Chamorshi taluka of Gadchiroli district as major district roads.
Taking into account the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and also considering the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Gadchiroli, the following other district roads and rural roads in Chamorshi Taluka of Gadchiroli district are being upgraded as major district roads.
