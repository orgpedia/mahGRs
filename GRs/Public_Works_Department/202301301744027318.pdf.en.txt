Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the major district roads in Yewala, Chandwad, Malegaon and Nandgaon talukas of Nashik district as state highways.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said roads, population, demand for use of the roads, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as state highways.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan, the following major district roads in Yewala, Chandwad, Malegaon and Nandgaon talukas of Nashik district are being upgraded as state highways.
