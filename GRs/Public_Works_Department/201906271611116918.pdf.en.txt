Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Ratnagiri has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of Hedvi - Morewadi - Umrath - Navlai Mandir to Dharwadi - Waddai - Khalchiwadi road in Guhagar Taluka of Ratnagiri district as a major district road. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said road and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following road in Ratnagiri district is being upgraded.
