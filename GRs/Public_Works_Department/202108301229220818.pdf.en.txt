Government order
In accordance with the provisions of Sections 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 of Assistant Engineer Grade-2/ Branch Engineer / Junior Engineer (Civil) in the Public Works Department shown in the attached prospectus. Due to administrative reasons as well as on their request, the pre-term request is being transferred.
2. The Chief Engineer (Electrical), Public Works Department, Mumbai should immediately relieve the transferred engineers. Engineers whose detailed postings are mentioned in the prospectus should join the post of detailed posting immediately. Also, detailed appointment orders of Engineers who have been posted under Public Works Board should be issued immediately by the concerned Chief Engineer.
If the engineer mentioned in the prospectus has been transferred to the level of Chief Engineer, the order of transfer of Chief Engineer shall be deemed cancelled. The Chief Engineer should submit the transfer report of the transferred engineers to the Government within 15 days.
3. The Engineers mentioned in the prospectus should not be absent without authorization after assuming charge of the new post immediately. Otherwise, their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and will be unpaid and the possibility of disruption in service cannot be ruled out, the concerned engineers are informed. Also, their attention is drawn to the General Administration Department, Government Circular no. CDR-1082/2567/28/11, dated 30.08.1982 is being referred to and in view of the instructions given under the said circular, it may also be noted that if they submit any form of application for cancellation of transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
