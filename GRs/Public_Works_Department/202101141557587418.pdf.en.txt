Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Kokan has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Poladpur taluka of Raigad district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Raigad has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
On the said road is the historical Mahipat Gad Kalgai temple. As the place is a tourist spot, there is a large crowd of people. As this road connects to the tourist spot, there is a lot of traffic on this road. There are two English medium and two Marathi medium schools on the said road. Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Raigad, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Raigad, the following roads in Raigad district are being upgraded.
