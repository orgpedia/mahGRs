Refer to :- 1) Letter from Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan Ja.No.MUA (Co)/Drawing Branch/Upgradation/2076, dated 21/06/2022.
Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Kokan has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the following other district roads and rural roads in Ambernath, Murbad and Kalyan talukas of Thane district as major district roads. Also District Planning Committee, Thane has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the approval of the District Planning Committee, Thane, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of District Planning Committee, Thane, the following roads in Thane district are being upgraded.

Also, the total length of rural roads is 112.970 km. By reducing the total length (2444.524- 112.970) i.e. 2331.554 km. That will be it.
