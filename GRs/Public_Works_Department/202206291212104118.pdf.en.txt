Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of major district roads and other district roads in Latur taluka of Latur district as state roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded, the following roads in Latur Taluka of Latur District are being included as State Highways.
