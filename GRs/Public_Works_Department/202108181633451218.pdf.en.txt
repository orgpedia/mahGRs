Government Order:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4(4) and 4(5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority and also as per the provisions of General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 29.7.2021, public Smt Jagruti M from Construction Department. Sankhe, Executive Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Navi Mumbai is transferred as per his request and in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience to Executive Engineer, Road Development Department no. 4, Andheri, Mumbai is filling the vacant post.
2. Smt. Sankhe should assume charge of her new post promptly and submit her assumption of office report to the government without delay. Mrs. Sankhe m. no Sec. (Period of Joining, Non-Acceptance of Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 shall continue to require joining the posted post within the given joining period. Otherwise the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of the said rule, they shall be eligible for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
4. Since Mrs. Sankhe is being transferred as per her request, she will not be paid joining period, traveling allowance and daily allowance.
5. Smt. Sankhe should inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Opp. Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assuming charge of the newly posted post.
