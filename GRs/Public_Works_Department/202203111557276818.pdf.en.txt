Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads in Umarkhed and Pusad talukas of Yavatmal district as roads and other district roads through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the traffic volume on the said road and the number of villages, population, use of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal, the government was considering upgrading the said road as another district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal, the following roads in Umarkhed and Pusad talukas of Yavatmal district are being upgraded.
