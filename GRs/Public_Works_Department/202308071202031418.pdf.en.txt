Notification regarding e-tender publication of Public Works Department.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Government Decision No. : Miscellaneous 2022/P.No.104/Ima-2
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032.
Date:- 07/08/2023
Reference: 1) Public Works Department, Government Resolution No. CAT/ 2017/ Q. No.8/ IMMA-2D. 27/09/2018
Preamble: In order to avoid time constraints in development works due to elections to Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities, Nagar Panchayats, Gram Panchayats, Zila Parishads/ Lok Sabha in 2023-2024, it was contemplated to revise the time period for publication of e-tender in Table No. 2.1 of the relevant Government Decision.
Government Decision: Since the elections to the Municipal Corporations/ Municipalities, Nagar Panchayats, Gram Panchayats, Zila Parishads/ Lok Sabha are scheduled to be held in the year 2023-24, the period of publication of e-tender is being implemented as follows so that development works do not suffer due to time.
-------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Cost of work                 | duration     |
| 1     | Rs. 10 lakhs to 1.5 crores   | 8 days       |
| 2     | Rs. 1.5 crore to Rs.25 crore | 15 days      |
| 2     | Rs. 25 crores to 100 crores  | It's 21 days |
| 3     | Rs. More than 100 crores     | 30 days      |
-------------------------------------------------------
the above mentioned period. It will remain in force till 31/03/2024.
