Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Mahad taluka of Raigad district as major district roads vide letter under reference. Raigad Zilla Parishad, Alibaug has also demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, use of the road as well as the resolution of Raigad Zilla Parishad, Alibaug, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Raigad Zilla Parishad, Alibaug, the following other district roads in Mahad Taluka of Raigad District are being upgraded as major district roads.
