Government Order:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. Patil, Executive Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Pune is transferred on deputation in the interest of public service and for administrative reasons as Deputy Chief Project Manager (Construction), Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Limited (Maha Metro), Pune.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Pune.
3. Mr. Patil should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule - 30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Patil is being transferred on his request, he will not be paid the joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Patil has been posted to the post of deputation and the said posting is Maharashtra Civil Service (tenure of appointment, non-commissioned service and suspension, dismissal and removal from service).
