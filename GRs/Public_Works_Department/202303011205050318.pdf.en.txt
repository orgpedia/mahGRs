Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade major district roads in Chikhali, Mehkar, Lonar and Risod talukas of Buldana and Washim districts as state highways.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said roads, population, demand for use of the roads, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as state highways.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati, the following major district roads in Chikhali, Mehkar, Lonar and Risod talukas of Buldana and Washim districts are being upgraded as state highways.
