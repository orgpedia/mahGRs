Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Hatkanangle taluka of Kolhapur district as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has requested to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following unplanned roads in Hatkanangle taluka of Kolhapur district are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
