Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Kokan has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade other district roads in Poladpur taluka of Raigad district as major district roads through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Raigad, Alibag have demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Raigad, Alibag, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Raigad, Alibaug, the following roads in Raigad district are being upgraded.
