The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they may relieve the said Officers by making necessary local alternate arrangements and ask them to report to their new places of appointment. Also, the concerned officers should be immediately informed to the Government on the e-mail id doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in about the date on which the concerned officers were released from duty to join the place of posting and on which date they appeared at the place of posting.
5. The concerned officers should assume charge of the new post with minimum tenure and submit their assumption report to the government without delay. If the said officer does not join the post on promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of this order, his period of absence shall be treated as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
6. The concerned officers should give the option of pay fixation within one month from the date of joining the promoted post. If such option is not given within the prescribed period, their pay fixation will be as per Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981 under Rule No. 11 (1) (a) should take into account the date of promotion.
7. The concerned officers should inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Co-ordination Centre, Opp. Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the post of the new appointee.
