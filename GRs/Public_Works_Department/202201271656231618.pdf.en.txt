Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of Rural Road Road in Satara Taluka of Satara District as Major District Road vide letter under reference. Also Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the number of villages on the said road, population, usage of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Satara Taluka of Satara District are being upgraded.
