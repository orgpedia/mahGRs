Under the Public Works Department, roads, bridges, buildings of various departments and other public works are done. While preparing the concept of buildings/bridges and making conceptual drawings using other media i.e. computers, as well as the technical skills that are needed while doing the electrification of public buildings and implementing projects and which makes the services of such engineers beneficial to the society in the future, keeping in mind the administrative as well as technical skills of such engineers, their personal It is honored by giving awards. Birthday of Bharat Ratna Sir Visvesvaraiya on 15th September is celebrated as “Engineer&#39;s Day” every year. On that occasion, the engineers who have performed outstandingly are given a badge of honor and a certificate of appreciation.
The announcement of awards for the period 2014 to 2019 was delayed due to some unavoidable reason.
