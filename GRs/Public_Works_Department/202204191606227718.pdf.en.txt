Government order
As per the Government Order under reference, Assistant Engineer Grade-2 (Civil) has been promoted from Assistant Engineer Grade-2 (Civil) to Sub Divisional Engineer (Civil) cadre. In the Government Order under reference Shri. The posting of Rakesh Dagula on promotion to World Bank Project Sub Division No.3, Aurangabad has been changed as per his request and posted as S.B. Sub Division, Ambad, Dist. It is being done at Jalna.
2. As per this order, Shri. In the case of Rakesh Dagula, the terms and conditions of the promotion order under reference will continue.
3. It is informed to the concerned control officers that Mr. Dagula should be relieved immediately by making necessary local alternate arrangements so that he can take charge at the new place of appointment and he should be asked to join immediately at the new place of appointment.
4. Mr. Rakesh Dagula should assume charge of the new post immediately and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The aforesaid Engineers should ensure that the transferred Engineers join the place of transfer within the period of appointment given in the Maharashtra Civil Service (Period of Appointment, Non-Service and Allowances during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, otherwise they may not join the post of transfer during the period of appointment. If so, the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be eligible for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for transfer in posting.
