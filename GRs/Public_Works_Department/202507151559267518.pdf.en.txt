Preamble: - The Scheduled Tribe caste certificate of Mr. Ramchandra Shamrao Koli, Mali was declared invalid by the Executive Magistrate, Kolhapur Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Kolhapur.
Accordingly, the office of the Director, Uplands and Parks, Mumbai, was established by the General Administration Department, Government Decisions, Govt.
As per the provisions of December 21, 2019, the services of Mr. Koli will be terminated. The order dated December 31, 2019, provides for the transfer of the majority position for the next 11 months.
This was followed by the Public Works Department, Government Decisions, D. By 02 August 2024, a majority of the posts were created for Mr. Koli and the post was given to him. The term has been extended till 02.07.2025. As the term of the majority post created for Mr. Ramchandra Shamrao Koli, Mali has expired, the matter of extension of the majority post was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - Shri Ramchandra Shamrao Koli, gardener, working under the office of the Director, Uplands and Parks, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has a maximum tenure of two years. They were superseded by the General Administration Department, Government Decisions, with effect from July 02, 2025.
