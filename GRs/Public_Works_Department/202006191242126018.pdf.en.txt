2. Hon&#39;ble High Court, Bombay in Petition no. 2797/2015 vide decision dated 4.8.2017 has invalidated reservation in promotion. Hon. The State Government has filed Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 in Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court against the High Court. The said petition is still pending. In this regard, the Law and Justice Department has opined that there is no legal problem in promoting the employees in the open category. Accordingly, at present the vacant posts in the open category in the promotion quota are purely temporarily filled according to seniority. To be paid subject to the decision of the Supreme Court. While taking such action, before promoting the backward category officers/employees in the seniority list, they shall be as per the provisions of Government Decision dated 25.5.2004.

General Administration Department has instructed that every administrative department should ensure that they do not get top position in the seniority list by getting the benefit of reservation. vide letter dated 29.12.2017.
Accordingly, the promotion given to Shri.Gurusiddha Shankarao Naik is being done on a purely temporary basis to the vacant post in the open category in the promotion quota subject to the final decision of the Special Leave Petition No.28306/2017 filed in the Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court.
3. The said ad-hoc promotion will remain for 11 months or till the commission sponsored candidates become available, whichever is earlier. In the meantime, if Commission sponsored candidates are received, the ad hoc promoted officers will be demoted. The said ad hoc promotion is temporary and Mr.Gurusiddha Shankarao Naik will not have permanent lien on the said post.
4. The promotion given to Mr. Gurusiddha Shankarao Naik is purely temporary and he will not get any seniority in the cadre of Deputy Divisional Officer in pursuance of the said promotion. Also, if necessary, such temporarily promoted engineers will be demoted. Regarding the acceptance of the said condition, written consent should be obtained from the said engineers before taking up the post of promotion.
5. The said promotions are being given subject to the condition that no departmental inquiry is pending or proposed/Court Case/FIR filed against Shri.Guru Siddha Shankarao Naik under Rule 8 and 10 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979. However, if a departmental enquiry/court case/FIR is filed on the date of this order, these promotion orders will automatically stand cancelled.
6. The promotion to Shri.Gurusiddha Shankarao Naik, Junior Engineer (Civil) will be subject to the provisions of the Government Decision to re-review the age of the concerned after 50/55 years.
7. Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune / Sa. left Board, Pune is requested that Shri. Naik, Junior Engineer (Civil) should be relieved immediately from the date of issue of promotion and detailed appointment order and should be ordered to join at the place of new appointment with a minimum joining period without going on leave or giving notice of cancellation of appointment in any way.
