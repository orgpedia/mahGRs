No. In paragraph 2 of SRV-1094/ Pr.No.4/94/Bara, dated 07.03.1996 “cadre means a group of posts in a service or part of a service sanctioned as a separate unit, permanent as well as temporary posts sanctioned from time to time. will be understood,&quot; it is explained. Rule-4 (1) of the Service Entry Rules dated 07.04.1983 for the post of Executive Engineer (Civil) has detailed the provision of announcing the cadre strength of the post of Executive Engineer (Civil) every year. There is no such provision in the Service Entry Rules for the post of Deputy Engineer (Construction). However, from the point of view of maintaining administrative order, it has been found necessary to determine and announce the cadre strength of the said cadre (permanent sanctioned posts + temporary posts on deputation as per the figure).
Similarly, due to the demand from various departments for the posts in this cadre, Deputy Engineer (Construction) Cadre Dt. The issue of determining and announcing the number of cadres on 01.09.2021 was under consideration of the government.
