Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads in Jalgaon and Dharangaon talukas of Jalgaon district as roads, other district roads as per reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon has demanded to upgrade the said roads as other district roads.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said rural road as another district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon, rural roads in Jalgaon and Dharangaon talukas of Jalgaon district are being upgraded as other district roads.
