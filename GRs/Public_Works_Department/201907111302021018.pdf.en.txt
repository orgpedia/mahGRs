Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Bhiwapur taluka of Nagpur district as major district roads through the reference letter. Also, there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur regarding the upgrading of the said roads.
Considering the traffic volume on the said road, the population of the villages on the road, the use of the road and the demand of the people&#39;s representatives as well as the demand of the Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Taking into account the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the demand of the Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the following roads in Bhiwapur Taluka of Nagpur District &quot;on the length of which works are approved/in progress under the Prime Minister/Chief Minister Gram Sadak Yojana, shall be made public only after the expiry of the liability period of the respective scheme. The works are being upgraded on the condition that works will be taken up by the construction department.
