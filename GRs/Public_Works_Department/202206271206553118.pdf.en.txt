Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032.
Date :- 27 June, 2022.
Reference : Letter No. No.4/Seva-1/ 2780/2021 dated June 09, 2021 from Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik.
Introduction : The Divisional Office is functioning under the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Dhule under Executive Engineer, Employment Guarantee Scheme, Public Works Department, Dhule. Earlier, under the said department, the works were being done under the employment guarantee scheme. However, due to the closure of all employment guarantee schemes under this department, the matter of changing the name of the said departmental office was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: This government decision approves the change in the name of the divisional office of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik under Regional Division, Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Dhule under the authority of Executive Engineer, Employment Guarantee Scheme, Public Works Department, Dhule. And, the name of the said office is being given as &quot;Executive Engineer, Public Works (North) Department, Dhule&quot;.
