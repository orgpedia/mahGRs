2. Hon. The High Court, Bombay has invalidated reservation in promotion vide judgment dated 4.8.2017 in Petition No.2797/2015. Hon. The State Government has filed Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 in Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court against the High Court. The said petition is still pending. In this regard, according to the opinion given by the Law and Justice Department dated 28.12.2017, there is no legal problem in promoting the employees in the open category. Accordingly, at present the vacant posts in the open category in the promotion quota are purely temporarily filled according to seniority. To be paid subject to the decision of the Supreme Court. While taking such action, before promoting the officers/employees in the backward category in the seniority list, it is ensured that they have not been promoted in the seniority list by getting the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of Government Decision dated 25.5.2004.
