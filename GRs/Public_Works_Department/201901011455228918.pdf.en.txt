Foreword : Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Mumbai vide reference letter regarding inclusion of unplanned road from Panchanadi Sapteshwar Mandir to Lakhtarwadi Road in Dapoli Taluka of Ratnagiri District as Rural Road in Road Development Plan 2001-21.
The proposal is submitted. The said road will provide communication facility to the people of Lakhtarwadi. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri regarding the said road. Considering the importance of the said road, it was under the consideration of the Government to include this road as a rural route in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the road from Panchanadi Sapteshwar temple in Dapoli taluka of Ratnagiri district to Lakhatarwadi km. This unplanned road from 0/00 to 1/700 (1.700 km) is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as Rural Marg No.-383.
Consequently, the total length of rural roads in Ratnagiri district in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 is 1,700 km. By increasing the total length (6595.900 + 1.700) i.e. 6597.600 km. That will be it.
