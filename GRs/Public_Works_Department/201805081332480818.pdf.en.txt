Introduction : Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai has submitted a proposal regarding upgradation of other district roads and rural roads in Khed, Dapoli and Mandangad talukas of Ratnagiri district as major district roads vide letters under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the roads. Considering the importance of these roads, the government was considering upgrading these roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following roads in Khed, Dapoli and Mandangad talukas of Ratnagiri district are being upgraded.
