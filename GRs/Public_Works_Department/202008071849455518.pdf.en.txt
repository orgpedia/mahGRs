Government Order: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4(4) and 4(5) of Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority, Shri. Shai. A. Gulvelkar, Senior Architect, Constituent No. - 5, Mumbai is being transferred to the vacant post of Senior Architect, Aurangabad (for Aurangabad) in pursuance of his request and in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience.
2. Another officer on the post of Senior Architect, Constituent No. - 5, Mumbai will be posted separately from time to time.
3. Mr. Gulvelkar should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and they will be without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule - 30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As the transfer of Gulvelkar is in accordance with his request, he will not be paid the tenure of office, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
