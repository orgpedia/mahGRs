2. The Hon&#39;ble High Court, Bombay has invalidated the reservation in promotion in petition no.2797/2015 dated 4.8.2017. Hon. The State Government has filed Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 in Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court against the High Court. The said petition is still pending. In this regard, according to the opinion given by the Law and Justice Department on 28.12.2017, there is no legal problem in promoting the employees in the open category.
Accordingly, the vacant posts in the open category under the promotion quota should be filled on a purely temporary basis subject to the decision of the Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court on the basis of seniority. While taking such action, every administrative department should ensure that before promoting the backward category officers/employees in the seniority list, they have not got the benefit of reservation in the seniority list as per the provisions of the government decision dated 25.5.2004, General Administration Department dated 29.12. 2017 letter is given.

Accordingly, the promotions of the above mentioned Engineers are being made on a purely temporary basis on the vacant post in the open category in the promotion quota subject to the final decision of the Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed before the Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court.
3. As the said promotion is purely temporary, as a result of this temporary promotion, the said officers will not get any right to regularity and seniority.
4. The said promotions are being given subject to the condition that no departmental inquiry is pending or proposed/Court Case/FIR filed against the above mentioned officers under Rule 8 &amp; 10 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline &amp; Appeal) Rules, 1979. However, if a departmental enquiry/court case/FIR is lodged against any of these officers on the date of this order, these promotion orders will stand automatically in respect of them.
5. Government Notification General Administration Department, No.SRV-2015/P.No.156/Work-12, dated 28.4.2015 and dated 16.7.2015 and amended notification, dated 15.6. 2017 as per provision of departmental cadre allocation and details of place/office to which they are posted after promotion are mentioned against their names in paragraph-1.
6. Chief Architect, S.B. Department, Mumbai, is requested to release the concerned Junior Architects posted under them immediately from the date of issue of promotion and detailed appointment orders and join at the new place of appointment with a minimum joining period without going on leave or giving notice of cancellation in any manner. Give orders.
7. If the above officers do not appear at the place of detailed appointment given after promotion by 4.5.2018, their promotion orders will be canceled without any notice, assuming that they are not interested in promotion. Concerned please note that no later representations will be considered in this regard.
8. Chief Architect, S.B. Division, Mumbai is informed that if the promoted officers posted in their Regional Division do not appear till 4.5.2018, their names should be reported to the Government so that further action can be taken at the Government level.
