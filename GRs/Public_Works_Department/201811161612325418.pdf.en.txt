Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads in Hatkanangle taluka of Kolhapur district as major district roads through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population as well as the total usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and Zilla Parishad,
Taking into consideration the resolution of Kolhapur, the following roads in Hatkanangle taluka of Kolhapur district are being upgraded as major district roads.
