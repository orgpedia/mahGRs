2. The Hon&#39;ble High Court, Bombay has invalidated the reservation in promotion in petition no.2797/2015 dated 4.8.2017. The State Government has filed Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 in the Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court against the Hon&#39;ble High Court. The said petition is still pending. In this regard, according to the opinion given by the Law and Justice Department on 28.12.2017, there is no legal problem in promoting the employees in the open category.
Accordingly, the vacant posts in the open category under the promotion quota should be filled on a purely temporary basis subject to the decision of the Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court on the basis of seniority. While taking such action, every administrative department should ensure that before promoting the backward category officers/employees in the seniority list, they have not got the benefit of reservation and got higher position in the seniority list as per the provisions of the government decision dated 25.5.2004, General Administration Department dated 29.12. 2017 letter is given.

Accordingly, the promotion of Mr. M. M. Mathurkar is Hon. Subject to the final decision of the Special Leave Petition No.28306/2017 filed before the Supreme Court, the vacancy in the open category in the promotion quota is being filled on a purely temporary basis.
3. The promotion to be given to Shri.Mathurkar is purely temporary and he will not get seniority in the cadre of Deputy Divisional Engineer in accordance with the said promotion. Also, if necessary, such temporarily promoted engineers will be demoted.
Regarding the acceptance of the said condition, written consent should be obtained from the said engineers before taking up the post of promotion.

5. The promotion granted to Shri.MM Mathurkar, Assistant Engineer Grade-II shall be subject to the provisions of the Government Decision to be reviewed after 50/55 years of age of the concerned.
6. The concerned Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division / Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board is hereby requested to immediately relieve Shri.MM Mathurkar, Assistant Engineer Grade-2 posted under him from the date of issue of promotion and detailed appointment orders. And order to join at the place of new appointment with a minimum tenure without going on leave or giving notice of cancellation of appointment in any way.
7. If the engineer concerned does not appear at the place of detailed appointment given after promotion by 17.11.2018, his promotion orders will be canceled without any notice, assuming that he is not interested in promotion. Concerned please note that no later representations will be considered in this regard.
8. The concerned Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division/Chief Executive Officer is informed that if the concerned Engineer appointed in his Regional Division does not appear till 17.11.2018, he should immediately inform the Government so that further action can be taken at the Government level.
