Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of 1 other district road and 1 rural road in Gondia taluka of Gondia district to the status of major district road. Also, Zilla Parishad, Gondia has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population and the total usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Gondia, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Gondia, the following roads in Gondia Taluka of Gondia District are being upgraded.
