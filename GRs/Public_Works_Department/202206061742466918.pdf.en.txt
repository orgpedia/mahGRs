Introduction: There are 16 talukas in Nanded district, except Umri taluka, all the other taluka headquarters have sub-divisional offices of Public Works Department functioning. Under the jurisdiction of the Public Works Department in Umri Taluka, construction of roads, bridges and buildings, maintenance and repairs etc. are currently executed from the Public Works Sub-Divisional Office in Dharmabad Taluka. Considering the growing population, communication facilities in Dharmabad and Umri talukas, the matter of setting up a sub-divisional office of Public Works Department at Umri was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision : Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded under the Regional Division Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Nanded under the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Bhokar. According to this government decision, the establishment of Sub Divisional Office at Umri, District Nanded is approved and the name of the said office is being given as Public Works Sub Divisional Office, District Umri, District Nanded.
For this, the Superintending Engineer under Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Pune, Sub Divisional Engineer under Public Works Board, Pune, Road Project Sub Division No. 2, Pune is approving the transfer of the vacant post of “Sub Engineer” cadre to Umari Sub Divisional Office. Also, after shifting the post of Sub Engineer cadre to Umri office, the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune should take action to include the posts of other cadres in other Board/Division offices of Pune Regional Division as per the requirement of work.
