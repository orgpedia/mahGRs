Introduction: As the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 has been terminated, a committee was constituted vide reference number (2) dated 12/11/2020 to prepare Road Development Plan for the next 20 years from 2021 to 2041. Due to the incidental change in the functioning of the said committee, the committee has been partially changed under the Government Resolution dated 23/12/2021 under reference number (2). Similarly, the Government was considering appointing the Chief Engineer, Prime Minister/Chief Minister, Gram Sadak Yojana, Maharashtra Rural Road Development Institute, Pune and Chief Engineer, National Highways, Konkan Bhawan, as well as Under Secretary (Planning), Ministry, Mumbai as members to the said committee.
Unplanned/unclassified roads are included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan. The maintenance and repair of rural roads is done through the Village Development Department. So their feedback is expected. Accordingly, the committee formed to prepare the road development plan for the year 2021-2041 consists of Chief Engineer, Prime Minister/Chief Minister, Village Road Scheme, Maharashtra Rural Road Development Institute, Pune and Chief Engineer, National Highways, Konkan Bhavan and Under Secretary (Planning), Ministry , Mumbai is being proposed for appointment as a member.
Government Decision: In the committee formed for the road development plan for the year 2021-2041, the following No. (4), (5) and (9) new members are being included.
