Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Pune vide letter under reference IJIMA NO. 99 to Manerajuri Jamdadevasti Khairatwasti Road 2.750 km. A proposal has been submitted to the Government regarding the inclusion of the length in Rural Route No.69. Also Zilla Parishad, Sangli has requested to include the length of said road.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the request of Zilla Parishad, Sangli, the said road was under consideration of the Government to include it in Rural Route No.69.
Government Decision:
2.750 km of Ijima No.99 to Manerajuri Jamdadevasti Khairatavasti road in Sangli district considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the request of Zilla Parishad, Sangli. Length, chain no. 00/00 to 2/750 being included in Rastagramin Marg No.69.
As a result, the total length of rural roads in Sangli district in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 2.750 km. By increasing the total length (5922.810+2.750) i.e. 5925.56 km. I. That will be it.
