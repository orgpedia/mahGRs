Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding inclusion of unclassified roads in Sengaon Taluka of Hingoli District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also, Zilla Parishad, Hingoli has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads.
Since the villages on the said road are connected to the main road and considering the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to include rural roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli, the following roads in Hingoli district are being upgraded as rural roads.
