Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads in Karad taluka of Satara district as major district roads through a reference letter. Also Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the number of villages on the said road, the population, the road usage and also the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Satara, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Karad Taluka of Satara district are being upgraded as major district roads.
