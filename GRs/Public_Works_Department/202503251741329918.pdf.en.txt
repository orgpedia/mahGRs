Mr. Ravidra Ganpat Baviskar, Branch Engineer, working in the office under the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, has been given an extension of 11 months or till the date he retires or till the final decision of the Government on the recommendations of the Study Group, whichever is earlier, by creating a maximum post for him as "Tokre Koli," a Scheduled Tribe whose caste claims are invalid. Now the caste certificate of Mr. Ravindra Ganpat Baviskar, Branch Engineer (Architecture) "Tokre Koli" (28) of the caste has been issued by the Scheduled Tribes Certificate Committee, Dhule Division, Nashik. Due to the decision, the government was considering abolishing the majority of the posts created for Mr. Ravindra Baviskar.
