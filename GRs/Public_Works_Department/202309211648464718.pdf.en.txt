Circular:
The Service Entry Rules for Assistant Engineer (Architecture) Grade-II Cadre in Maharashtra Engineering Services Group-B have been published by the Water Resources Department vide Government Notification No. MAC-1084/ (61)/ A (Sany) dated 16.06.1997 and for Assistant Engineer (Architecture) Grade-II (Service Entry) (Amendment) Rules, 2009 in Maharashtra Engineering Services Group-B vide Government Notification No. SSC-1008/ (73/2008)/ A (Sany) dated 8.7.2009. In accordance with the provisions of the said rule regarding service superiority and as per the provision laid down in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 under the General Administration Department, No. SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ Work.12, dated 21.06.2021, the provisional seniority of Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Architecture) cadre in the Public Works Department as on 01.01.2023 is being published in Annexure-A.
2. All Chief Engineers are requested to circulate this seniority to all the Circle/ Board/ Division/ Sub-Division offices under them and submit the same to the Government.
(3) If the concerned engineers have any objections in respect of the said seniority, they should submit their objections/ representations to the Government through the concerned Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer within 15 days from the date of publication of the seniority, along with appropriate proof and documents. Submissions received after that will not be considered.
The final decision will be taken after considering the seniority.
