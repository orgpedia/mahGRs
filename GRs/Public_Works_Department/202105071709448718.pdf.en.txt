Foreword: Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation has been established vide Government Decision No.1), Public Works Department No. Khakshes-1096/Pro.No.80/Roads-8, dated 9th July, 1996. Read no. 2) The composition of the Board of Directors of the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation has been changed and modified vide Public Works Department, No. Khakshes 1096/P.No.80(1)/Roads - 8, dated February 9, 2000. So read no. 3) 2 non-governmental members have been appointed on the board of directors as per the decision of the government here.
According to the provisions of Section 149 (4) of the Companies Act, 2013, one-third of the total members of the Board of Directors must be independent non-executive members. Accordingly, the proposal to appoint non-governmental members (Independent Directors) on the board of directors of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: It is necessary to appoint non-governmental members (Independent Directors) on the Board of Directors of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation as per the provisions of Section 149 (4) of the Companies Act, 2013.
Subject to the provisions of Public Works Department No. Khakshes-1096/P.No.80(1)/Roads-8, dated 9th February, 2000 and subject to the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 and with the approval of the competent authorities, the Board of Directors of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation The following persons are being appointed as members (Independent Directors) as well as invited members.
