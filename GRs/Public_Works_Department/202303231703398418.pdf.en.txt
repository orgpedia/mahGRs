Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik vide reference letter submitted to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Satana Taluka of Nashik District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
is Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the use of the said roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik
Meanwhile, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as rural roads.
Government decision
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following unplanned roads in Satana Taluka of Nashik district are being included in Road Development Plan 2001-21 as rural roads.
