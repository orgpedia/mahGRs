Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding upgrading the road from Valani to Tondakhairi Ijima 54 in Kalameshwar Taluka of Nagpur District and including it in Project 39 through the letter under reference.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Nagpur has demanded to upgrade the said road and include it in Project 39.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road and include it in Project 39.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, Valani to Tondakhairi Ijima 54 (length 3.300) road in Kalmeshwar Taluka of Nagpur district is being upgraded and included in Ijima 39. .
