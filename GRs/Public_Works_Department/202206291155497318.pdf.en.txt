Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Kagal Taluka of Kolhapur District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also District
Council, Kolhapur has requested to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following roads in Kagal Taluka of Kolhapur District are being upgraded as rural roads.
