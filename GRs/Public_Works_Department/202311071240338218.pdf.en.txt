Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade other district roads in Akole taluka of Ahmednagar district to the status of major district roads under the reference letter.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, the following roads in Akole Taluka of Ahmednagar district are being upgraded.
