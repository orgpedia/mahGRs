Preface : Considering the total area of Marathwada division and total districts geographically, it was necessary to reorganize the Public Works Board in accordance with operational, supervisory ease and administrative convenience of construction works. Taking this matter into consideration, the issue of establishing two offices of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded and Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Latur was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: On establishment of Public Works Department (self) for Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Nanded by establishing two new offices of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Nanded and Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Latur. It is approved to transfer the post of Engineer and Joint Secretary to Nanded and renaming the post of Chief Engineer and Joint Secretary as Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded.
