Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Kalmanuri Taluka of Hingoli District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Hingoli has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli,
The said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli, the following roads in Kalmanuri Taluka of Hingoli district are being upgraded as rural roads.
