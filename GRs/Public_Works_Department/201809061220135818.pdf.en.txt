Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati vide the letter under reference, in accordance with the demand of Zilla Parishad, Amravati, Chandur Railway - Kurha Tivasa - Riddhapur State Road no. The government has received a proposal to include the unplanned road Kawadgavan Poch Marg connecting to 300 as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021.
If the said road is included in the Road Development Scheme 2001-21, it will be convenient for the students of Kavadgavan village to go to Kurha, Tivasa, Chandur Railway for education. Therefore, the said road was under the consideration of the government to include it as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Amravati, Chandur Railway - Kurha - Tivasa - Riddhapur State Route No. Kawadgavan Pochmarg (length 3.00 km) connecting to 300 is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as Rural Road No.118.
As a result, 3.00 km in length of rural road quality roads in Amravati district in Road Development Plan 2001-21. By increasing the total length (6258.640 + 3.00) is 6261.64 km. I. That will be it.
