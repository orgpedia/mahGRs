Preface : The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune vide letter under reference to include the unplanned road from Bhandarkawathe to Antroli in South Solapur Taluka of Solapur District as a rural road in Ravio 2001-21.
The demand of Zilla Parishad, Solapur is to include the use of the said road as well as the road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to include the above road development scheme as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the unplanned road from Bhandarakawathe to Antroli in South Solapur Taluk of Solapur District Chain No. 0/00 to 8/00 length 8.00 km. This unplanned road is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as Rural Road No-235.
As a result, the total length of rural roads in Solapur district in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 8.00 km. By increasing the total length (6871.13 + 8.00) i.e. 6879.13 km. I. That will be it.
