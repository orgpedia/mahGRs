Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik vide letter under reference, Prarama-8 Vadgaon Gupta R.Ma.58 road to Pimpalgaon Malvi Prajima-35 to Gaware Vasti in Ahmednagar district. Res. Hon. 5 has been demanded to upgrade the road from Jewar to Pimpalgaon Ujjain as a major district road.
Considering the traffic volume and the number of villages on the said road, population, use of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar, Prarama - 8 Vadgaon Gupta R.Ma.58 road to Pimpalgaon Malvi Prajima 35 to Gaware Vasti of Ahmednagar district. R. Hon. 5 from Jeor to Pimpalgaon Ujjain was under the consideration of the government to upgrade it as a major district road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmadnagar, Prarama 8 Vadgaon Gupta Ram 58 Jod Road to Pimpalgaon Malvi Prajima 35 to Gaware Vasti in Ahmednagar District. R. Hon. 5 from Jeor to Pimpalgaon Ujjain road on Pradhan Mantri Gramsadak / Chief Minister Gramsadak Yojana and other schemes in which the work has been completed or proposed or any works or budgetary works for which there is remaining liability period for the work done will not be proposed under any other scheme and new for the said road Land acquisition will not be done. The road is being upgraded under these conditions.
