Introduction:
According to the above referred letters from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, the Kanyadol Jodarsta (Rural Route No. 214) connecting Mohapa, Khumari Road in Kalameshwar Taluka of Nagpur District by upgrading the road from Pipla (K), Tishti (Bu), Dadhera to State Route No. . 335 to Tishti (Khu.), Nandikheda to State Road No. 349, Jirola, Kokarda to State Road No. 366 (Kanyadol) (Major District Road No. 99) has received a proposal from the Government.
The said road has approximately 6000 to 7000 MT per day traffic and there are industrial estates, coal mines, markets, educational institutions in Kalmeshwar area. Dhapewada and Adasa are important pilgrimage and tourist spots in Kalameshwar taluka on this road and this road is connected to the state highway and Nagpur headquarters. Said rural road no. Considering the importance of this road 214, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade this road and include it in the major district road no.99.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, Kanyadol Road connecting Mohapa, Khumari Road in Kalameshwar Taluka of Nagpur District, Rural Road No. 214 is 5.00 km. Upgrading the length of the road from Pipla (K), Tishti (B), Dadhera to Rajyamarg no. 335 to Tishti (Khu.), Nandikheda to State Road No. 349, Jirola, Kokarda to State Road No. 366 (Kanyadol) Major District Road No. 99 is being included in this road.
