Introduction:
Bharat Narayan Thakur, bungalow chowkidar, working in the office under the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Ratnagiri, has not submitted the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribe. The maximum number of posts has been created as on 06.03.2020. Scheduled Tribes Caste Verification Committee, Thane Konkan Division has issued No. 2/529/ SER/ 012018/121384, Dy. Shri Bharat Narayan Thakur has been granted Scheduled Tribe Caste Validity Certificate with effect from 06.04.2023. Therefore, the matter of cancellation of the superannuation post created for Mr. Bharat Thakur was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: A majority of the posts created for Mr. Bharat Narayan Thakur and the bungalow chowkidar working in the office under the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Ratnagiri are being abolished and their services are being transferred to regular posts.
Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Ratnagiri to direct the concerned to take action to pay the annual increment and other benefits withheld by Mr. Bharat Thakur.
Further, the expenditure on the salary of Shri Bharat Thakur shall be covered under the head of salary accounts of the office in which he will continue to work and shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the relevant financial year.
