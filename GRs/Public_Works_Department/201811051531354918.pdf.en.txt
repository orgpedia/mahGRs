Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of the road from Chipri - Jainapur - Kothali to district boundary - Haripur to Rajyamarg-154 in Shirol taluka of Kolhapur district as a major district road. Also Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following roads in Shirol Taluka of Kolhapur District are being upgraded.
