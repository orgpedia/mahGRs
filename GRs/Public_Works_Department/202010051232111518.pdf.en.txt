Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Aurangabad, under reference letter, Ramma- 752 I to Loha to Gopi Tanda- Dhanora- Samtanagar- Bembari- Jambli- Ramma in Bhokar and Hadgaon Taluka of Nanded District. A proposal to upgrade 161A road as a major district road has been submitted to the government. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nanded has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, the population, the road usage and also the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Nanded, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the following road in Bhokar and Hadgaon Taluka of Nanded District is being upgraded.
