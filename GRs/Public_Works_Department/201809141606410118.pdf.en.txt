Regarding inclusion of unplanned road from Khadka to Bargaon in Warood taluka of Amravati district as rural route in Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Government Decision No: RAVO-2018/P.No.134/Planning-2 Ministry, Mumbai - 400 032
Dated- September 14, 2018.
Refer to :- 1) Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati letter no. Why - 2 (2) / Promotion/2018/2457d.19/12/2017.
Preface : The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati vide letter under reference to include the unplanned road from Khadka to Bargaon in Warood Taluka of Amravati District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
The said road is important for the communication of the people of Khadka and Bargaon villages. Also, there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Amravati to include the said road in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the said road was under the consideration of the government to include it as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Amravati, Khadka to Bargaon road (total length 1.500 km) in Warood Taluka of Amravati District as Rural Road No.205 It is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
As a result, the total length of rural road grade roads in Amravati district is 1.500 km. With this increase, the total length is (6258.640 + 1.500) i.e. 6260.140 km. I. That will be it.
