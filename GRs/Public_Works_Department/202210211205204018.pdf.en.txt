Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Panhala and Shahuwadi talukas of Kolhapur district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the said road was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following Ijima and Grama roads in Panhala and Shahuwadi talukas of Kolhapur district are being upgraded as major district roads.
