Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Niphad Taluka of Nashik District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Nashik, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
