Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unclassified road from Ijima 52 to Kirkirewadi Markatwadi in Mokhada Taluka of Palghar District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Considering the usage of the said road as well as the importance of the road, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the importance of the road and the report submitted by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan regarding the readiness of local villagers to provide land for the proposed road, the following road in Mokhada Taluka of Palghar District Road Development Scheme 2001-21 is being included as rural route.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road                    | Chain no      | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Ijima 52 to Kirkirewadi Markatwadi road. | 0/00 to 2/600 | 2.600 km.                        | Rural Marg-118      |
| Total | 2.600 km.                                |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result, the total length of rural roads in Palghar district in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 2,600 km. By increasing the total length (4299.37+2.600) i.e. 4301.97 km. I. That will be it.
