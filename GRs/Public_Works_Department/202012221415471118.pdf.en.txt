Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Kalwan taluka of Nashik district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Number of villages on said roads, population, road usage and district
Considering the resolution of the Parishad, Nashik, the said roads were under consideration of the Government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following roads in Nashik district are being upgraded.
