Circular
Provisional Additional Seniority List of Superintendents working in Regional Offices (Chief Engineer / Superintending Engineer) dated 1.1.2016 under Public Works Department is hereby published.
2. The said seniority list is in accordance with the provisions of Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 as well as Government Decision No. S.P.V. SRV-2011/Pro.No.284/12 dated 21.10.2011 has been prepared as per instructions.
3. The Chief Engineer / Head of Department is requested to bring this seniority to the notice of the concerned Superintendent. If any error is found in the said seniority list or if there is any person to give a statement in this regard, the head of the department should check such statements and send them to the government with their feedback within 30 days from the date of this circular. In case no objection/information is received within the prescribed period, the seniority list will be published in its final form assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority list are correct.
