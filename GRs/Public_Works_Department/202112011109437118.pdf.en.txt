Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Hadgaon Taluka of Nanded District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nanded has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the following roads in Hadgaon Taluka of Nanded District are being upgraded as rural roads.
