As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, government employees who are appointed by promotion are required to join the post of posting within 30 days from the date of issue of the promotion order. If the officer concerned does not join the post of posting by promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of this order, his period of absence shall be deemed to be a non-working day. If the concerned officer does not join within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of order or within the extended period allowed for joining with prior approval, the order of promotion of the concerned officer shall be deemed to be automatically cancelled, considering that he is not interested in promotion.
