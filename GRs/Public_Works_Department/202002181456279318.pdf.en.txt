Accordingly, the said road was under the consideration of the government to be transferred to the Public Works Department.
Government Decision:
Kulgaon-Badlapur municipal council limits from Hope India Company in the eastern part to Kharwai Naka N.P. limits are within the municipal council limits. The said length starts from Hope India Company i.e. the junction of PM 74 and PM 76 belonging to Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation.
Kalyan-Badlapur-Vangani-Done-Karjat road from Juveli bridge to 123/520 to 133/250 km. is under the department. Therefore, Hope India Company km of this road. 121/760 to Juveli Pool Km. 123 / 520 is 1.76 km. Transferring the length will result in contiguousness of RA.76 from Junction (Hope India Company) (Km.121/760) to Jewel Bridge (Km.123/520) of Pragima No.74 in Kulgaon Badlapur Municipal Council limits. The portion of No. 76 is 1.76 km. The road is being transferred to Public Works Department.
