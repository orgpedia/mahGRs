Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government to include a total of 12 unplanned roads in the Nagpur Rural Taluka of Nagpur District as Rural Roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide the letter under reference.
Considering the said proposal, total 12 unplanned roads in Nagpur rural taluka of Nagpur district were under consideration of the Government to be included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal submitted by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, the following 12 roads in Nagpur Rural Taluka of Nagpur District are being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as rural roads.
