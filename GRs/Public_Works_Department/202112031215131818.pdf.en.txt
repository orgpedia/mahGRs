Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade other district roads and rural roads in Latur and Renapur talukas of Latur district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Latur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Latur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Latur, the following roads in Latur and Renapur talukas of Latur district are being upgraded as major district roads.
