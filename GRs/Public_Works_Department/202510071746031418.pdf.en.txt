2. Provisional regular promotions are being made against such officers subject to the assumption that no punishment is pending against them or which would prejudice their promotion to the divisional posts.
3. In the case of officers in the said promotion order who refuse promotion, the General Administration Department shall issue a notification to this effect. Action will be taken in accordance with the government decision of 12.09.2016.
3. Senior officers concerned are requested to relieve the Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Construction) cadre engineers appointed to them with immediate effect as per the order of this promotion and to join the new appointment without going on leave with minimum period of retention.
4. The respective promoted sub-divisional engineers are informed of the position they have been promoted to.
Join within 9 months from the date of this order, otherwise the General Administration Department may notify the same. The period of their absence shall be deemed to be a non-deceiving day as provided in the 14.07.2021. Also, their attention is being drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of this rule, they will be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of office.
7. Senior officers concerned are informed that in case the promoted engineers do not join the post of promotion within 1 month, a proposal for further action at the government level as mentioned in paragraph 4 above should be submitted to the Government forthwith.
