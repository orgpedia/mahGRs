Under the Public Works Department, roads, bridges, buildings of various departments and other public works are done. Birthday of Bharat Ratna Sir Visvesvaraiya on 15th September is celebrated as “Engineer&#39;s Day” every year. On that occasion, the engineers who have performed outstandingly are given a badge of honor and a certificate of appreciation.
