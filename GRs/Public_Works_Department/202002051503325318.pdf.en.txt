Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune vide letter under reference to E.G.M. of Patan Taluka of Satara District. A proposal has been submitted to the government regarding upgradation of village roads together as major district roads. Also Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said road.
The said proposed roads are important in terms of traffic as there is a lot of traffic coming from Maharashtra and other states. Considering the importance of these roads, the government was considering upgrading the roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Satara Taluka of Satara District are being upgraded.
