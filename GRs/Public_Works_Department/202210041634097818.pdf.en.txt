Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Nandura and Malkapur talukas of Buldana district as state roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati, the following roads in Nandur and Malkapur Talukas of Buldana District are being upgraded as State Highways.
