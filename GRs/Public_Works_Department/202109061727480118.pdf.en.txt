Foreword: Hindu Heart Emperor Balasaheb Thackeray on the lines of Maharashtra Prosperity Highway for the development of high-speed highway for the overall development of Konkan Division on March 14, 2020 by the state government. An announcement was made in both the Houses about undertaking the project through the Corporation. The Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation was informed on March 20, 2020 about preparation of technical and financial feasibility report for this project.
Mumbai is the financial capital of India and the state of Maharashtra and is a major commercial and industrial centre. The Konkan region of Maharashtra is rich in tourism, industrial and commercial activities. Also, there is a train for tourists and businessmen traveling from Goa state to Konkan region to Mumbai. Along with this, most of the professional, working class and mainly industrial and tourist class from Konkan region are aligned with Mumbai / Pune city. Sabab, looking at the current infrastructure progress in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai, if a high-speed highway from Mumbai - Konkan Division to Goa is established, the industrial, tourist and daily communication will be accelerated and the overall progress of Konkan Division will be possible. Accordingly, the Government has taken the following decision in the meeting of the Cabinet Infrastructure Committee held on 25.08.2021.
Government Decision: 1) The appointment of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation (MSRDC) as the &#39;Implementing Agency&#39; for the Konkan Expressway project connecting Raigad, Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg districts from Mumbai to Sindhudurg is approved.
2) M.R.R.V. to carry out Drone Survey / Lidar Survey in the entire length for the said projects. The Corporation is being approved.
3) By appointing a technical consultant to prepare a detailed project report with technical engineering and financial feasibility for the said projects and to take further action accordingly. Sanction is being given to the corporation.
