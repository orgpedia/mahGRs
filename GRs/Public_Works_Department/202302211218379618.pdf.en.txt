Introduction:
146.16 Crores (Rupees One Hundred Forty Six Crores Sixteen Lakhs Only) for the work of &quot;Temba Khardi Jawhar Jamsar Bopadri Ruighar&quot; road improvement at Jawhar, District Palghar Rama-77 (Under Hybrid Annuity) (MU 3 Project) vide Government Decision under reference. Administrative approval was given for the estimated cost work.The said budget includes Estimate project cost, escalation, service channel relocation, land acquisition, consultancy fee.
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan vide his letter dated August 03, 2021 accepted the higher rate tender for the proposed project as well as change in the scope of work during construction, price hike, consultancy fee and service and goods tax, etc. A proposal for revised administrative approval has been submitted to the government taking into consideration the increase in the project cost due to the reasons.
Accordingly, the issue of approving the revised budget for the said work amounting to Rs.235.3920 crores (Rupees two hundred and thirty five crores, forty nine lakhs, twenty thousand only) was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision To improve Tembha Khardi Jawhar Jamsar Bopadri Ruighar road. Jawhar, Dist. Palghar Rama-77 (Under Hybrid Annuity) (MU 3 Project) at a cost of Rs.235.3920 Crores (Rupees Two Hundred Thirty Five Crores, Forty Nine Lakhs, Twenty Thousand Only) is being given revised administrative approval as per this Government Decision subject to the following condition. .
1) While implementing the scheme, it will be the responsibility of the department to follow the prevailing government decisions, rules, CVC principles, financial limits, prescribed tender procedures, as necessary, to rectify the errors and instructions given by the State Technical Advisory Committee in accordance with the project and work.
2) The said amended administrative approval shall not be deemed to be approval of technical matters in the report, zonal decision on tender or irregularities. It will be the responsibility of the department to investigate such a case as per the rules prevailing at that time.
3) The said project work should be completed within the scheduled time and within the revised administrative approval provision.
4) If there have been any financial and operational irregularities in the previous period in relation to the said project, any illegal action has been taken or any matter which may lead to any irregularity, the Government agrees.
