the Public Works Department

Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032
Date: 15th March, 2024
Read: 1. Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated 13.03.2024.
Proposal: - A decision has been taken on 13th March, 2024 regarding the construction of Maharashtra State Guest House at Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir. Accordingly, the government was considering setting up a coordination committee to coordinate the work of the Maharashtra State Guest House to be set up by the government of Maharashtra in Jammu and Kashmir.
Government Decision: - The Government of Maharashtra is approving the constitution of a Coordination Committee of the following eminent officers / dignitaries to coordinate the work of the Maharashtra Bhawan to be set up by the Government of Maharashtra in Jammu and Kashmir.
