Preface: The government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad, regarding the inclusion of unplanned road from R.M.-253 to Valur to Valangwadi road in Selu Taluka of Parbhani District as rural road in Ravio 2001-21.
Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani to include the said road in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the above road was under the consideration of the Government to include it as a rural road in the Road Development Scheme 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the road from RM-253 to Walur to Valangwadi in Selu Taluka of Parbhani District is unplanned road chain number 0/00 to 4/700 (4 .700 km) This unplanned road is rural road no. 124 is therefore being included in the Road Development Plan 2001 21.
As a result, the total length of rural roads in Parbhani district in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 is 4,700 km. By increasing the total length (2843.690 + 4.700) i.e. 2848.39 km. I. That will be it.
