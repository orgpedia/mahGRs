Government Order:
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. No. Vs. Bhande, Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Ambejogai, Beed on transfer in the interest of public service and for administrative reasons, Shri. Vs. A. Machale is deputed to the post of Executive Engineer, Office of Sugar Commissioner, Sugar Complex, Shivaji Nagar, Pune, which is vacant from 30.9.2019 (M.No.) due to superannuation.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Ambejogai, Beed.
3. Mr. Bhande assumed charge of his new position. Machale should be accepted after his retirement and after that his assumption of office report should be submitted to the government. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and they will be without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge respectively by the deposed and deputized officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Bhande is being transferred for administrative reasons, he will be paid his joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Bhande has been posted to the post of deputation and the said posting is Maharashtra Civil Service (tenure of appointment, non-commissioned service and suspension, dismissal and removal from service).
