Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding upgradation of 4 other district roads as major district roads in Taloda Taluka of Nandurbar district vide letters under reference. Also, there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar regarding the upgrading of the said roads.
If the said roads are upgraded, the said roads will be useful for the transportation of agricultural goods in the area as well as for health services. These roads connect the tourist and pilgrimage areas of Rokadman Hanuman and Kankeshwar Mahadev and are useful for going to Taloda main market and places in Nandurbar district.
Considering its importance, the government is considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar, the following roads in Taloda Taluka of Nandurbar district are being upgraded.
