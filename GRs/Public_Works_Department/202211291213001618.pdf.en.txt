Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include 2 unplanned roads in Man Taluka of Satara District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said road and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Man Taluka of Satara District are being included in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21 as rural roads.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A. No. | Name of proposed road        | Chain no    | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.     | Vadgaon to Dadswada road.    | 0/0 to 2/00 | 2.00 km.                         | Rural Marg-223      |
| 2.     | Vadgaon to Mahadevdara road. | 0/0 to 3/00 | 3.00 km.                         | Rural Marg-224      |
| Total  | 5.00 km.                     |
