Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government to include 90 unplanned roads in Miraj taluka and 1 in Tasgaon taluka of Sangli district as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 through the letter under reference.
Considering the said proposal, it was under the consideration of the government to include 90 unplanned roads in Miraj taluka and 1 in Tasgaon taluka of Sangli district as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the information mentioned in the proposal submitted by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune, 90 roads in Miraj Taluka and 1 in Tasgaon Taluka of Sangli District are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
