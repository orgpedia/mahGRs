As per the provision dated 21st December, 2019, directions have been issued for creation of supernumerary posts for officers/ employees who are transferred to supernumerary posts on the ground of non-submission of caste validity certificate of Scheduled Tribes. Thereafter, the General Administration Department, Govt. As on 14th December, 2020, the authority to create these additional posts has been delegated to the Administrative Department. According to this, Satyawan Arvind Chavan, who is working under the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Satara, and the driver of the vehicle have been given the responsibility of the Public Works Department, Government Decision, D. The number of posts has been created with effect from April 26, 2021. The term has been extended till May 04, 2023. The government was considering extending the deadline beyond May 6, 2023.
Government Decisions: General Administration Department, Government Decisions, Dy. The maximum number of posts created for Mr. Satyavan Arvind Chavan, Driver, as on 14th December, 2022 is Rs. from May 6, 2023. The extension is till 05.04.2024 or the date on which he would have retired if he had continued in service or till the recommendations of the Study Group are received by the Government and a final decision is taken by the Government, whichever is earlier.
2. As Mr. Satyawan Chavan, Driver is currently working in the office under Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Satara, the expenditure on his salary should be charged under the salary account of that office and met from the sanctioned grant of the relevant financial year.
