Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune, vide letter under reference, combined the major district roads No. 8, 74, 4 and 3 in Shahuvadi and Shirala talukas of Kolhapur and Sangli districts from State Road No. 191 to Nandgaon Ghungur Pishvi Bambwade Sarud Sangav Road as State Highway. A proposal for upgradation has been submitted to the government.
Considering the traffic volume and the number of villages as well as the population and road usage on the said roads, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government decision -
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune, the following roads in Kolhapur and Sangli districts are being upgraded as State Highways.
