Refer to :- 1) Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad letter No. Muau/Ka-14/Pra/Ravio-01-21/4862, dated 29/10/2021.
Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road from Pornabi to Khadala in Purna Taluka of Parbhani District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Parbhani has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the following road in Purna Taluka of Parbhani district is being upgraded as a rural road.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road              | Chain no     | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | The road from Por near to Khadala. | 0/00 to 6/00 | 6.00 km.                         | Rural Route- 146    |
| Total | 6.00 km.                           |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Parbhani district in Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 6.00 km. By increasing the total length (2916.840 + 6.00) i.e. 2922.840 km. I. That will be it.
