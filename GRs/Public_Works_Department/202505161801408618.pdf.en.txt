It is communicated to the Controlling Authorities concerned that they should immediately relieve the aforesaid Architects by making necessary local alternate arrangements so that they can take charge at the new place of appointment and they should be asked to join the new place of appointment immediately.
3. The aforesaid Vastu Vigyan should take charge of the new post forthwith and submit his report of assumption of office to the Government without delay. The aforesaid transferred officers shall take care to join the place of transfer within the time of assumption of office as per the rules of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption, non-acceptance of service and service during periods of suspension, dismissal or removal from service) Rules, 1981, otherwise, if they do not join the post of transfer during the period of assumption of office, their period of absence shall be deemed to be non-deceasing day. Their attention is also being drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules 1979. In view of the provisions of this rule, they may apply for transfer of office in any form whatsoever.
If submitted, they will be subject to disciplinary action.
4. The concerned Controlling Authorities are informed that they should immediately inform the Government of the date of demitting office and assumption of office by the incumbent. the transfer of the officer concerned within the prescribed period.
In case of non-attendance, the same should be communicated to the Government immediately so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned expeditiously. As per Rule 30 (c) of the MNS (Leave) Rules, 1981, leave applications of transferred officers should not be accepted / sanctioned.
5. To make the said Government Order available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharashtra.gov.in.
