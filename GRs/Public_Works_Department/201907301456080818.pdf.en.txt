Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the government to include 3 rural roads in Aurangabad and Gangapur talukas of Aurangabad district in Road Plan No. 59 and 1 unplanned road as rural road in Road Development Plan 2001-21 through the letter under reference. . Also, Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad, the following roads in Aurangabad and Gangapur talukas of Aurangabad district are being upgraded.
