Proposal: - D. The Nagpur-Goa Expressway (Maharashtra Shaktipeeth Highway) from Pavanar, Saat to Patradebi, Sindhudurg District has been extended by a distance of 802.592 km as per the Government Decision dated 07.02.2024. I was approved for the length plan.
According to this plan, the UR notification issued pursuant to the acquisition of land under the Maharashtra Mahamag Act 1955 for the highway was issued for the sanctioned plan of Shirol, Karvir, Hatkanangale, Kagal, Bhudargarh, Ajra taluks of Kolhapur district. Cancelled by notification on 15.10.2024. After that. The Government Decision dated 28.08.2025 had approved the continuation of the plan from Pavanar to Sangli, directing the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation to examine and submit to the Government the available survey options and possible options for the remaining plan.
Related to Ma. Minister B.M. In consultation with the public representatives, the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation issued the guidelines (Indian Road Congress Special Publication No. According to the Evaluation Matrix of 19:2020, various alternative plans have been studied on the basis of physical site inspection and a revised plan has been finalised and proposed which is most suitable in comparison.
According to the proposal, the Maharashtra Shaktipeeth highway will pass through 13 districts viz., Wardha, Yavatmal, Nanded, Hingoli, Parbhani, Beed, Latur, Dharashiv, Solapur, Satara, Sangli, Kolhapur, Sindhudurg, and 21 religious places including the major Shaktipeeths of Maharashtra Mahur, Tuljapur, Kolhapur. The highway is expected to reduce the current 18-hour journey from Nagpur to Goba to about 8 hours.
The government was considering finalising the revised layout of the Maharashtra Goba Shipping Expressway (Maharashtra Shaktipeeth Highway) from Pavanar, Maat to Patradebi, G. Sindhudurg.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
Government Decision: - By retaining the original sanctioned plan in Wardha, Yavatmal, Beed and Latur districts, by amending the original sanctioned plan in Nanded, Hingoli, Parbhani, Dharashiv, Solapur, Sangli, Kolhapur, Sindhudurg districts and by re-inserting the plan in Satara district, Maharashtra Shaktipeeth Highway Digraj, T.G. Wardha to Banda, Dt. Sambatwadi, Dist. Sindhudurg (Chain No. km 0.000 to km. m. 856.765) The revised framework is being approved.
