Introduction:
The government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad to include the road from National Highway-361 to Jamgavan Tanda to Jamgavan road in Kalmanuri Taluka of Hingoli district as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
The said road is important for connecting the villages on this road to the main road. The said road is a gravel road and no land acquisition is required for this. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli to include the said road in Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Therefore, the above road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as a rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli, National Highway-361 to Jamgavan Tanda to Jamgavan Road in Kalamanuri Taluka of Hingoli District Km. 0/00 to 3/600 (3.600 km) this road is rural road no. 103 is therefore being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
As a result, the total length of rural roads in Hingoli district in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 was 3,600 km. By increasing the total length (1905.400 + 3.600) i.e. 1909.00 km. I. That will be it.
