Government Corrigendum: Provisional seniority list dated 01.01.2018 for the period of 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2017 of the cadre of Deputy Divisional Engineer (Civil) in the Public Works Department published vide circular dated 08.06.2001 has been done.
2. Department of General Administration, Government Decision no. Divyang 2015/ P.No.171/16-A, dated 05.07.2021, reservation has been prescribed in promotion to the posts of Group “A” and Group “B” for disabled employees. Therefore, the government has received proposals from the regional offices to register the disabled category in front of the names of the disabled engineers whose names of disabled engineers have not been recorded. Accordingly, the cadre of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Civil) in the Public Works Department for the period of 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2017 has been published vide circular dated 08.06.2021 dated 01.01.2018.
The statement in this regard has been attached as Annexure “C” by noting the category of disabled employees against the name of the disabled employees in the provisional seniority list and making other corrections as per the documentary evidence received by the Government.
