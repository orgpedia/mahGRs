Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Parbhani taluka of Parbhani district as major district roads under the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Parbhani has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the following roads in Parbhani taluka of Parbhani district are being upgraded.
