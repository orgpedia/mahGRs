Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Amravati Taluka of Amravati Taluka as Major District Roads and some of its length to be included in the existing Major District Roads. And according to the demand made by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the said roads have been upgraded.
Taking into consideration the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the roads as well as the demand of the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the demand of Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the following roads in Amravati Taluka of Amravati District are being upgraded as major district roads. And lengths are being added to existing major district routes.
