Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Nanded vide reference letter regarding upgradation of other district roads and rural roads in Parbhani, Jintur, Selu and Gangakhed talukas of Parbhani district as major district roads and inclusion of unclassified roads within municipal/municipal limits in the existing major district roads Submitted to Govt. Also Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, Parbhani City Municipal Corporation, Parbhani and Nagar Parishad, Selu have demanded to include the said roads in the existing major district roads.
Taking into consideration the use of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, Parbhani Municipal Corporation, Parbhani and Nagar Parishad Selu, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as major district roads and to add length to the existing major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani as well as Municipal Corporation, Parbhani and Nagar Parishad, Selu, the following roads in Parbhani, Jintur, Selu and Gangakhed talukas of Parbhani district have been upgraded and included in the major district roads category. are coming Also length is being added to the existing major district route.
