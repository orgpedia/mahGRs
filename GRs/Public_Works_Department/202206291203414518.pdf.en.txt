Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan has submitted a proposal to the Government for upgrading Kherdi Terav Kamthe Road, Ijima 124 in Chiplun Taluka of Ratnagiri District as Major District Road through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following road in Chiplun Taluka of Ratnagiri District is being upgraded as Prajima.
