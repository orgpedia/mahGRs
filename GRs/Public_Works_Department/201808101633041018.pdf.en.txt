Provisional additional seniority list of Superintendents working in Regional Offices (Chief Engineer / Superintending Engineer) dated 1.1.2015 under Public Works Department is hereby published.
2. The said seniority list has been prepared in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 as well as the instructions vide Government Decision No.S.P.V.SRV-2011/P.No.284/12 dated 21.10.2011. (The final seniority list of Superintendents dated 1.1.2014 has been published vide circular dated 26.7.2018. The said final seniority list and the provisional additional seniority list dated 1.1.2015 have been combined to prepare the final seniority list dated 1.1.2015. will come.) 3. The Chief Engineer / Head of Department is requested to bring this seniority to the attention of the concerned Superintendent. If any error is found in the said seniority list or if there is a statement to be made in this regard, the head of the department should verify such statement and send it to the government with his feedback within 15 days from the date of this circular. In case no objection/information is received within the prescribed period, the seniority list will be published in its final form, assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority list are correct.
