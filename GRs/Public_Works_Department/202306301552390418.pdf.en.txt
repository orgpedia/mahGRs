Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade other district roads in Wada taluka of Palghar district as major district roads under the reference letter. The administrator and chief executive officer, Zilla Parishad, Palghar, have also demanded that these roads be upgraded.
Considering the traffic on these roads and the number of villages, the population, the use of the road, as well as the demand of the Administrator and the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Palghar, the government was considering to upgrade these roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan, and the demand of the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Palghar, the following other district roads of Wada and Taluka of Palghar district are being upgraded as Major District Roads.
