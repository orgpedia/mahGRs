Refer to :- 1) Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune letter No.MUA (Pune)/Ka-2/Resha/35/1037/2019, dated 20/03/2019.
Preface: The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune to include two unplanned roads in Karmala Taluka of Solapur District as Rural Roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 through the letter under reference.
The said roads are unpaved roads and no land acquisition is required for this. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Solapur to include the said roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the above mentioned road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the following unplanned roads in Karmala Taluka of Solapur District are being included as rural roads.
1) Awti to Talukahadd (Loni) (Palkhi Marg) Road Km. 0/00 to 7/00 (Length 7.00 km) This road is rural road no. 295 2) Road connecting Pathurdi to Varakute Ra 202.No.M.Km. 0/00 to 5/00 (Length 5.00 km) This road is rural road no. 296
As a result, the total length of rural roads in Solapur district is 12.00 km. By increasing the total length (6916.23 + 12.00) i.e. 6928.23 km. I. That will be it.
