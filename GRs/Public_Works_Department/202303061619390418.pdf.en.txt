Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Chandgad Taluka of Kolhapur District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also District
Council, Kolhapur has requested to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following unplanned roads in Chandgad Taluka of Kolhapur District are being included in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21 as rural roads.
