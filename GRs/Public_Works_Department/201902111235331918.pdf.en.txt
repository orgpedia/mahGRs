Introduction: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Y taluka of Satara district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Y taluka of Satara district are being upgraded.
