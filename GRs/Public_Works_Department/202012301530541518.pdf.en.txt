Introduction:
Norms have been prescribed for various types of buildings in accordance with the above Government Decision Public Works Department No. BDG 2005/P.No.53/Ima 1, dated 25th November, 2005. After that, the cost of construction materials and labor has increased tremendously. However, due to the lack of increase in the financial and physical quality of maintenance repairs, less funds are available for maintenance repairs. The government has decided to form a committee to prepare new standards after technical scrutiny to improve these matters.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the matters mentioned in the preamble, it is decided to form the following committee to prepare new standards in accordance with the maintenance repair.

2) The committee will submit the report to the government within three months.
