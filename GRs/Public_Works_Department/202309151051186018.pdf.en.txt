Introduction:
Various types of service lines are allowed to be laid within the limits of roads under the Public Works Department. the applicant's application for permission
Scrutiny is done at the regional level and submitted to the competent authority for approval, after which the process of granting permission to the applicant is completed.
The idea of making the process faster, more transparent and more accessible has been expressed for a long time. There was a need to bring in more administrative dynamism by reducing the level of clearances by changing the manner in which service channels were allowed to be laid within the road boundaries. Accordingly, the Govt. Considering the directions of the Government Circular dated 30th May, 2017, the matter of appointment of Executive Engineer as Competent Authority in place of Chief Engineer was under consideration of the Government.
Government decision
The following procedures are being laid down for permitting laying of various types of service lines within the limits of roads under the Public Works Department.
At the field level, the Executive Engineer of the concerned Public Works Department is being appointed as the Competent Authority for OFC Cable/ Network and Gas Pipeline.
The Executive Engineer shall inspect the site and traffic arrangements with the applicant or his representative and prepare a report in accordance with the rules.
