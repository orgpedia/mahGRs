Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur, has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade other district roads in Gondia Taluka of Gondia district as Major District Roads under the Reference Letter. The Zilla Parishad, Gondia, has also demanded that these roads be upgraded.
Considering the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, use of the road as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Gondia, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: On the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and considering the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Gondia, the following Ejima roads in Gondia Taluka of Gondia district are being upgraded as Major District Roads.
