Mumbai: Mumbai is the commercial and financial capital of India. The Konkan region of Maharashtra is rich in tourism, industrial and commercial activities.
Also, there is a huge rush of tourists and businessmen coming to Mumbai from the state of Goa to the Konkan region. With the construction of the Mumbai-Konkan section of the expressway, the industrial, tourist and daily communication will be accelerated and the Konkan section will be able to make all-round progress.
The expressway will pass through the districts of Raigad, Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg, Pune, Kolhapur, Satara, Sangli, Solapur, Ahmednagar, Beed, Parbhani, Nanded, Latur, Osmanabad, Nanded, Latur, Parbhani, Osmanabad, Nanded, Latur, Beed, Hingoli, Osmanabad, Nanded, Beed, Palghar, Osmanabad, Nanded, Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg, Palghar, Pune, Ahmednagar, Osmanabad, Ratnagiri, Palghar, Thane, Panvel, Latur, Osmanabad, Ratnagiri, Panvel, Latur, Osmanabad, Nanded, Yavatmal, Washim, Washim, Washim, Wardha, Parbhani, Wardha, Wardha, Nagpur, Gondia, Jalgaon, Jalgaon, Latur, Washim, Parbhani, Jalgaon, Jalna, Washim, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Beed, Jalna, Washim, Nagpur, Ahmednagar, Aurangabad, Jalna, Jalna, Jalna, Washim, Washim, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Jalna, Jalna, This highway will definitely boost the industrial growth of Konkan and provide employment opportunities to the locals.
The proposed Konkan Expressway will provide a faster route to the southern states of the country closer to Mumbai and will connect the southern and northern states. The highway will be connected to the proposed international airport at Navi Mumbai, which will facilitate international travel through the Konkan region.
Accordingly, on 14th March, 2020, a representation was made in both the Houses regarding taking up of the project by Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation (MSRDC) through the State Government for making an expressway for the overall development of Konkan region on the lines of Hindu Hriday Samrat Balasaheb Thackeray Maharashtra Samruddhi Mahamarg. Accordingly, the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation was informed vide Government letter dated 20.03.2020 to prepare the technical and financial feasibility report of the project.
Next. Pursuant to the approval given by the Cabinet Committee on Infrastructure in its meeting held on 25.08.2021, the Government has approved the appointment of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation (MSRDC) as the Implementing Agency for the Konkan Expressway Project from Mumbai to Sindhudurg connecting Raigad, Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg districts. Accordingly, the government has decided to finalize the design of the Greenfield Konkan Expressway as follows.
Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation (MSRDC) has decided to finalize the most priority plan in accordance with the following package after studying the suggestions given to the project consultants, guidelines and various alternative plans after the site inspection.
