The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of Fathiyabad to Asegaon road in Gangapur taluka of Aurangabad district and including it in Project 59 through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad has demanded to upgrade the said road and include it in Project 59.
Considering the usage of the said road as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road and include it in Prajima 59.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Aurangabad, Gangapur in Aurangabad District
The following road in the taluka is being upgraded and included in Project 59.
