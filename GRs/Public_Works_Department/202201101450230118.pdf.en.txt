The said promotion and posting of the above mentioned Deputy Divisional Engineer is being done on a temporary basis subject to the following conditions: i. The said promotion is Hon. Subject to the final decision of the Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 as well as Writ Petition Stamp No. 10876/2021 and writ petition stamp no.
10878/2021 in Hon. High Court, Bombay. As per the decision dated 25.5.2021, the said promotion shall be subject to the decision in the said writ petition.
ii. As the said promotion is purely temporary, they will not have any right to regularity and seniority as a result of the promotion.
3. The Government reserves the right to cancel the said promotion to the above mentioned officers without any prior notice or notice.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they may relieve the said Officers by making necessary local alternate arrangements and ask them to report to their new places of appointment. Also, the concerned officers should be immediately informed to the Government on the e-mail id doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in about the date on which the concerned officers were released from duty to join the place of posting and on which date they appeared at the place of posting.
5. The concerned officials should assume charge of the new post with minimum induction period and submit their induction report to the government without delay. If the said officer does not join the post on promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of this order, his period of absence shall be treated as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
6. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that the date of assumption of charge by the said officer should be intimated to the Government immediately on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in. If they do not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) of his leave application should not be accepted / granted.
7. The concerned officers should give the option of pay fixation within one month from the date of joining the promoted post. If such option is not given within the prescribed period, their pay fixation will be as per Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981 under Rule No. 11 (1) (a) should take into account the date of promotion.
8. A in the above table. No. 2, 3, 4 and 12 above officers have been appointed / posted on the post of deputation. The said appointment is made subject to the terms and conditions of deputation prescribed in Appendix II to the Maharashtra Civil Services (Entry Period, Post-Commission Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981.
9. The concerned officers should inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Co-ordination Centre, Opp. Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the post of the new appointee.
