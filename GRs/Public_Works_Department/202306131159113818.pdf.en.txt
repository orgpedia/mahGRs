Preamble: 11 Divisional Accountants (Subordinate) have been appointed in the office of the Accountant General (Accounts& Entitlements)-II, Maharashtra, Nagpur. The Government was considering the creation of 11 additional posts for the period of probation in accordance with the new inclusion of 11 posts of Divisional Accountants (probationers) in the service system for payment of salary and allowances to these employees during the probation period.
The Central Government has approved the creation of 11 supernumerary posts for the period of probation for the newly appointed Trainee Divisional Accountants (Under-Trial) [Central Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2016 in the Level 6 (Six) in the Pay Matrix] in the Office of the Accountant General (Accounts and Entitlements)-II, Maharashtra, Nagpur.
2. The recruitment process for the post of Divisional Accountant has been done by the Staff Selection Commission and the probationary period of the said post is two years. The service details of the employees who have joined the post of Divisional Accountant (Probationary) are as follows:
