Introduction:
Public Works Department to fund and implement projects for 22 infrastructure development sectors like roads, ports, airports, railways, metro, power etc.
The Maharashtra State Infrastructure Development Corporation (MSIDC) is a state-owned public sector enterprise. The relevant Government decision prescribes the appointment of manpower and composition of the Board of Directors for the Corporation. A partial revision of certain provisions in manpower and appointment of directors was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- The provisions relating to manpower and Board of Directors on Maharashtra State Infrastructure Development Corporation (MSIDC) are being amended as under:
1. Paragraph No. An officer of the rank of Joint Director shall be appointed to the post of" Chief Financial Officer" mentioned in section 7.
2. Paragraph No. An officer of the rank of Assistant Director shall be appointed to the post of" Regional Pay and Accounts Officer" mentioned in Section 7.
