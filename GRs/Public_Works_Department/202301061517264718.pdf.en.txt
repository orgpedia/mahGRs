Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned 1 road in Satara Taluka of Satara District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the use of the said road and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara,
The said road was under consideration of the government to include it as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following road in Satara Taluka of Satara District is being included in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21 as a rural road.
