Government Order:
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4(1), 4(2) and 4(3) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri.
