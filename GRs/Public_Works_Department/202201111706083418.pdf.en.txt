Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government to include the unplanned road from Humbarne to Pandeipani in Patan taluka of Satara district as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said road as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Satara, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following road in Patan Taluka of Satara District is being upgraded as rural road.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road        | Chain no     | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Humbarane to Pandepani road. | 0/0 to 5/500 | 5.500 km.                        | Gramin Marg-339     |
| Total | 5.500 km.                    |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Satara district in Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 5.500 km. By increasing the total length (6732.030 + 5.500) i.e. 6737.530 km. I. That will be it.
