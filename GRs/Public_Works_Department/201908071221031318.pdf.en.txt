Government decision
A total of 68 Junior Engineers (Civil) shown in the annexure under Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai, Nagpur, Pune, Amravati and Nashik are being promoted as Branch Engineers (Civil) with effect from the date shown against their names.
2. According to this government decision, the salary of Junior Engineers appointed as Branch Engineers should be fixed in the pay scale existing on the date from which they are given the rank of Branch Engineer. Revision of Pay Fixation is required by the concerned concerned as per Rule 15 of Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981 as per Note-4 below. If he does not give such a re-option within this period, it will be deemed that he has given the option of salary fixation from the date of appointment to the new post.
3. A person appointed to the post of Branch Engineer (Civil) Group “B” will be required to pass the Departmental Examination and the Marathi and Hindi Examination as per rules, if the person has not already passed these examinations or has not been exempted from passing such examinations. If the Junior Engineers concerned have not passed the Departmental Examination and Marathi / Hindi Language Examination or have not been exempted from passing such examinations, then the pay scale of the post of Branch Engineer should be applicable from the date of granting them the rank of Branch Engineer. However, they should not be given further increments. Junior Engineers who are exempted from passing the departmental examination on completion of 45 years of age will be regular from the date of completion of 45 years of age / from the date of grant of pay scale of the post of Branch Engineer.
