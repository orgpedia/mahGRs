Introduction : A proposal to classify and transfer unclassified roads under the jurisdiction of Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation, Nanded to the Public Works Department has been submitted by the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Nanded. The roads in the said proposal have been transferred to Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation, Nanded vide Government Decision No. TRS 2010/P.No.42/Roads-1, dated 07/03/2011 and Government Decision No.-Ravio- 2017/P.No. 40/Plan-2, dated 02/05/2017 was declassified. Accordingly, the Nanded-Waghala city was under the consideration of the government to classify the unclassified roads of the Municipal Corporation and take them to the Public Works Department.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Nanded and the letter of Commissioner, Nanded Waghala Municipal Corporation, the following unclassified roads in Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation are being classified and handed over to Public Works Department.

A total of 27.80 km as above. The road length is being classified and included with the Public Works Department subject to the following conditions.
1. Roads along with land will remain fully owned by Public Works Department.
2. Nanded-Waghala Municipal Corporation will be responsible for removing the encroachment in the width area owned by the government in this length of the road.
3. Roads to be classified Government Decision no. Traffic rules as per TPS 1819/No-36/19/Navi-13 Urban Development Department dated 05/08/2019 will continue to apply.
4. While the Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation was giving permission for construction adjacent to this road in its jurisdiction, the Urban Development Department, Government Decision no. Govt decision TPS-1819/No-36/19/Navi-13 dated 05/08/2019 should be followed. It should be mandatory to obtain the approval in this regard from the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Nanded, 5. Statues/monuments and similar type of monuments shall not be erected in the roadside area of the road being classified. Also roads cannot be named after dignitaries.
6. Before classifying and transferring the above roads and encroachments/lease constructions/contracts or other related matters on the same Hon. Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation will be responsible for handling court cases. Regarding the above subject Hon. Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation should ensure that if there is a court order, it will not be defied.
7. No fee will be paid by the government for road transfer. The land acquisition cases on this road and the amount due should be transferred to the Road Public Works Department only after finalisation.
In case of any dispute on the above road in future and in line with land acquisition, Hon. Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation shall be fully responsible for any legal case arising.
8. After the classification of the above roads by the Government, the Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation Nanded shall be fully responsible (at its own expense) for the maintenance and repair, improvement and maintenance of these roads till the date of actual transfer. Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation will be responsible for any legal case arising on the above road.
9. Due to some unavoidable reasons in future Hon. The directives / orders / instructions of the court as well as the government will be binding on Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation.
10. The Commissioner, Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation, Nanded should first hand over the land under the above road to the Collector, Nanded. Thereafter, the said Collector of Lands, Nanded vide Rule 5 &amp; 6 of Maharashtra Land Revenue (Distribution of Government Land) Rules-1971 and Circular No.LND-1483/2702/P.No.1109/C-5 dated 04/02/ 1983 and as per the provisions of Government Decision No. LND-1090/P.No.181/J-1 dated 12/10/1990 of the Revenue and Forest Department to be transferred to the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department subject to financial limitations.
