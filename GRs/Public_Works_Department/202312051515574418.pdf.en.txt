Preamble:- In the Monsoon Session of the Maharashtra Legislature in July 2023, a question was raised about the deterioration of rest houses in the state. In response to this question, Mr. The Minister assured that an app is being developed to make public the basic information of government rest houses in the state of Maharashtra (status of rest houses, available facilities, total number of rooms, information about reservation, concerned officials). Accordingly, the matter of creating an app for making reservations of restrooms under the Public Works Department through online mode was under the consideration of the government.
Following this, the government took decision on this issue.
Government Decision: The status of the rest houses under the Public Works Department of Maharashtra, the available facilities, the total number of rooms, the information about the reservation, the concerned officers, etc. are being made available on the website. The government has also decided to make reservations for government restrooms online through the website and mobile app.
The website and mobile app are being prepared by the ITCC Cell, Office of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Nagpur.
The decision has been uploaded on the state government's website and its computer code is 202312051515574418.
