Purification Sheet:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Konkan, has issued a letter to the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Konkan, regarding the construction of a road from Mauje Kadav to Mauje Bhiwapuri in Karjat taluka of Raigad district. The Public Works Department has taken over the 7.00-km-long road under the Tata Power Company and the total length of Vaijanath Bhiwapuri Marg-Grame-66 is 1.600 km. Vaijnath Humgaon Marg- Grama-67 Total length 2.00 Km (7+ 1.600+ 2.00= 10.600) The government has issued a decision to rename the route as Major District Route No. 111. The name of the road mentioned in this government decision is being amended as follows:
" The road is from Mauje Kadav to Mauje Bhiwapuri in Karjat taluka of Raigad district. Regarding taking over of 7.00 km long road under Tata Power Company by Public Works Department. " Instead of
NH 548A to Bhalivadipata Malwadi Mandawane Tata Gate Bhiwapuri Humgaon Vaijanath Gaulwadi Road, Prajima-111, Km. 0/00 to 10/600 m. Karjat, Raigad.
