Government Order:
According to the Government order under reference Shri. Mr. Rm. Katkade, Chief Engineer, Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana, Pune was transferred on deputation as Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Health University, Nashik in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience. However, as the process of sanctioning the post of Chief Engineer (Civil) cadre in the university was not completed, there were administrative difficulties in getting Mr. Katkade to join the said post. Sabab, in accordance with the provisions of Sections 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority, Shri. Mr. Rm. Katkade, Chief Engineer, in the interest of public service and for administrative reasons, is being given revised posting on deputation to the post of Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Life Authority, Mumbai.
2. Mr. Katkade should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. Mr. Katkade M. no Sec. (Period of Joining, Non-Accepted Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 shall continue to require joining the posted post within the given joining period. Otherwise the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. Subject to the terms and conditions of deputation prescribed in Appendix II of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Joining, Post-Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, Shri. Katkade is being posted on the post of deputation.
4. Mr. Katkade will continue to be paid the joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
5. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively.
