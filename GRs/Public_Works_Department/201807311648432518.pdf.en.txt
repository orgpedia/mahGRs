Introduction:
Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik vide letter under reference to include this unclassified road connecting National Highway No. 50 from Nanegaon Kalavasti Nashik Sugar Factory to Bengali Baba Dargah in Major District Route 37 of Nashik Taluka.
Said 4.600 km. The length of the road is owned by Nashik Co-operative Sugar Factory Ltd., Palase and the authorized board of Nashik Co-operative Sugar Factory Ltd., Palase has approved the transfer of ownership of the said road to Public Works Department without compensation. If the length of the proposed road is included in the major district road - 37, the said road will be convenient in terms of communication for Nashik Sugar Factory and the farmers and villages in the area. It is also important for the overall development of the taluka as a connecting route to National Highway No. 50. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the above road and include it in the major district road-37.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, Nanegaon - Kalavasti - Nashik Cooperative Sugar Factory Ltd. in Nashik Taluka. The road connecting National Highway No. 50 from Palase to Bengali Baba Darga is 4.600 km. The unclassified road is being included in the major district route 37. Nashik Cooperative Sugar Factory Ltd., Palase will not get any compensation for land acquisition of the said road. Subject to this condition, the said road is being included in the main district road 37.
