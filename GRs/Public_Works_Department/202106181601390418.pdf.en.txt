Circular
Read the temporary additional seniority scale of superintendents working in field offices (Chief Engineer/Superintending Engineer) under Public Works Department dated 1.1.2018, dated 1.1.2019 and dated 1.1.2020 published vide circular dated 16th February 2021. All concerned were informed to report any objections/errors regarding the same. In this regard, in accordance with the representations/objections received from the field offices, the seniority list has been finalized by amending the provisional additional seniority list published vide circular dated 16 February 2021. The said seniority scale form “A” is attached herewith.
2. The said seniority scale is in accordance with the provisions of Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 as well as S. Q. Vs., has been prepared as per instructions vide Government Decision No. SRV - 2011/P.No.284/12, dated 21.10.2011.
