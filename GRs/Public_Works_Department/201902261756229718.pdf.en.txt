Introduction:
The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference to include 6 unplanned roads in Chachur Taluka of Latur District as Rural Roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021.
These roads are important in terms of communication and transportation. The said roads are unpaved roads and no land acquisition is required for this. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Latur to include roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the above mentioned road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Latur, the following roads in Chachur Taluka of Latur District are being included as Rural Roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
