Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the other district roads and rural roads in Kalameshwar taluka of Nagpur district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nagpur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, the population, the road usage and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the following roads in Kalameshwar Taluka of Nagpur district are being upgraded as major district roads.
