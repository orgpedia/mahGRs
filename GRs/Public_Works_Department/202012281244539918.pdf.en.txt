Government Decision - As per the result of competitive examination conducted by Maharashtra Public Service Commission, Mumbai in 2018 for the recruitment of posts in Maharashtra Engineering Service, Group-A, a total of 16 candidates have been recommended for the post of Assistant Engineer Grade-1 (Civil) under Public Works Department. Accordingly, the 16 candidates mentioned in the table below will join the pay scale S-20 (Rs.56100 - 177500) in the pay structure of the revised pay matrix as per Seventh Pay Commission for the post of Assistant Engineer Grade-1 (Construction), Group-A in the Public Works Department from the date on which they will join. The appointment is for a probationary period of two years. The appointment given to the candidates is being done in the order of merit shown against their name.

7. If required, the above candidates will have to serve in a post in the Defense Services of India or on such terms and conditions as may be decided by the Government of India for a period not less than 4 years subject to a period of training abroad. But
a) They shall not be required to perform such service after the expiry of 10 years after their appointment or b) They shall not be required to perform such service generally after attaining the age of 40 years.
8. The pay of the above candidates in the service of the Government of Maharashtra during the probationary period will be fixed as per Rule 11 of the Maharashtra Civil Service Pay Rules, 1981. As per the first proviso to rule 39.1 of the Maharashtra Civil Services Pay Rules, 1981, they will get increment during the probationary period.
9. Among the above mentioned candidates who have not already passed the Hindi and Marathi Language Examination or have not been exempted from passing, will have to pass the Hindi and Marathi Language Examination rules.

According to the provisions of paragraph-6 of the Government Decision No. TRN-09/P.Cr.39/09/12-A dated 23 September 2011 of the General Administration Department regarding the Maharashtra State Training Policy, the trainee has to write a bond to the competent authority before starting the training. Accordingly, it will be mandatory for every trainee to fill and submit a bond in the prescribed format to the Superintending Engineer and Co-Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Prabodhini, Nashik before joining Meta, Nashik and a copy of the same should be submitted to the Govt.
16. Candidates appearing at Maharashtra Engineering Training Prabodhini, Nashik should submit their attendance report to the Government through the Superintending Engineer and Joint Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Prabodhini, Nashik.
17. The Superintending Engineer and Co-Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Prabodhini Nashik should submit the non-appearance report to the Government for further action regarding the candidates who will not appear for the service.
18. Probationary candidates should write their confidential reports as per rules from the day they appear and in case of transfer they should be sent to the authorities concerned.
19. The Superintending Engineer and Joint Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Prabodhini, Nashik and the concerned officers as soon as the training of the probationary candidates is over, their Special Assessment Report Government Circular, General Administration Department, No. SRV 1062/D, dated 26.4.1962 in triplicate in the prescribed format should be sent to Govt. The said report of each candidate should be sent separately.
20. Out of the above candidates, the joining period will be permissible for the candidates who are currently working in the government service. The concerned controlling officers should release the said candidates sufficiently early to appear before the Superintending Engineer and Joint Director, Maharashtra Engineering Training Prabodhini, Nashik on 1.1.2020.
21. If the above candidates do not achieve the required standard of work during the probationary period or do not pass the departmental examination within the prescribed period and thus their work or conduct is found not suitable or conformable, they will be eligible for reduction from service.
