Refer:- 1) Letter from the Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur No.
Preamble: The Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government under reference to include the unplanned roads of Gondia Taluka of Gondia district as rural roads in the Road Development Scheme 2001-21. The Zilla Parishad, Gondia has also demanded that these roads be included in the road development scheme as rural roads.
Consideration of the use of these roads and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Gondia
However, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to include them as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Government Decision:
After considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Gondia, the following roads of Gondia Taluka of Gondia district are being included in the Road Development Plan 200121 as rural roads.
