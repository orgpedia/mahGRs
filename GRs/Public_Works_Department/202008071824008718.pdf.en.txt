4. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.

6. Smt. Lotane has been posted on deputation in the Maharashtra Civil Service (tenure of recruitment, non-commissioned service and suspension, dismissal and removal from service).
