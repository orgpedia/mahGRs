Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Amravati district as major district roads through a reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Amravati has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population as well as the total usage of the roads as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the following roads in Amravati district are being upgraded.
