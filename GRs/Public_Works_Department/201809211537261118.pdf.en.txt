Revision in the final seniority list published on 24/8/2006 for the period 1/4/1996 to 31/3/2003 of the unqualified Junior Engineer (Civil) cadre in the Public Works Department...
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Government Circular No.Seniority 2017/P.No.56/Establishment-1
Mantralaya, Mumbai 400032
Date : 21 September, 2018
Read : Public Works Department, Circular No. Seniority-2006/77/Aastha-1 dated 24/8/2006.
Corrigendum: Final seniority list of qualified Junior Engineers, (Construction) Cadre as per Public Works Department, Circular No.-Seniority-2006/77/Aastha-1 dated 24/8/2006 for the period from 1.4.1996 to 31.3.2003 has been published. Some junior engineers in the said seniority list have requested for correction of their caste/category records, correction in name and correction in date of birth. Also, some junior engineers have submitted representations regarding inclusion of names in the said seniority list. Accordingly, by revising the information in front of the names of the concerned engineers and adding new names of some junior engineers, the revised information statement is being issued.
