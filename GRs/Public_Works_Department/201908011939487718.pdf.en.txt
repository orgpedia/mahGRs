Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and in the performance of Government duties
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Prevention of Delays Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority, Shri. Shankar Pandurang Darade, Executive Engineer, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation, Mumbai (camp office, Pune) is being transferred to the vacant post of Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Satara as per his request and in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience.
2. Another officer is being posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation, Mumbai.
3. Mr. Darade should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, his focus is Maharashtra Civil Services
Reference is made to Rule-23 of the (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of assumption of charge by the post officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981
30(c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / approved.
5. Mr. Since Darade is being transferred as per his request, he will not be paid the joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Darade to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Co-ordination Centre, Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
