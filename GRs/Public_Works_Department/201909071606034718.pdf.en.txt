Government Order:
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. Arun Bhaidas Chavan, Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad (Zilla Parishad) Division, Thane is transferred in the interest of public service and for administrative reasons to the vacant post of Assistant Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad (S.B.) Division, Thane.
3. Mr. Chavan should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. They should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and will be without pay. Also, the concerned are being made aware that the possibility of interruption in their services cannot be ruled out. Also, Mr. Chavan&#39;s attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of assumption of charge by the post officers.
If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30(c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Chavan is being transferred for administrative reasons, he will continue to be paid his joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance as admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Chavan to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Co-ordination Centre, Opp. Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of his assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
