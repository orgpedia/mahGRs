Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of Velsakhra-Udasa-Virli-Pipla to Major District Road-113 Road in Umred Taluka of Nagpur District as Major District Road. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nagpur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the following roads in Umred Taluka of Nagpur District are being upgraded.
