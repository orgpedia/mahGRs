The Chief Engineer, Public Works, Regional Department, Konkan, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade Guhagar, Khed, Chiplun, Lanja, Sangameshwar and other district roads and rural roads in Ratnagiri district as major district roads.
The district council, Ratnagiri, has also demanded that these roads be upgraded.
