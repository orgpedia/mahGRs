Introduction : The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to upgrade the road Anjanvel - Veldur Navanagar - Dhopave - Jetty Marg - Dhopave - Ranvi Road (Rural Marg-1) in Guhagar Taluka of Ratnagiri District as Major District Road through the letter under reference. .
This road is an important road for the tourists coming through Dabhol and going through Guhagar in Dapoli taluka. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the said road. Considering the importance of the said road, the government was considering upgrading this road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following road in Guhagar Taluka of Ratnagiri District is being upgraded.
