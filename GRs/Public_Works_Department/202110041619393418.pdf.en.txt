Introduction : A proposal for classification and transfer of unclassified roads under the jurisdiction of Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation, Nanded to Public Works Department has been submitted by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad. Many roads within Nanded Waghala City Municipal Corporation Nanded limits. left As the roads owned by the department are linked to Nanded city, it was under the consideration of the government to classify the unclassified municipal roads and take them to the Public Works Department for maintenance and repair of the roads in Nanded city.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the letter of Commissioner, Nanded Waghala Municipal Corporation, the following mentioned unclassified roads in Nanded-Waghala City Municipal Corporation are being classified and transferred to Public Works Department.
