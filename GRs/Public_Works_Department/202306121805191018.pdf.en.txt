Government circular
Superintendents working in the field offices (Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer) under the Public Works Department. The Provisional Seniority Notice dated 01.01.2023 is being issued by the General Administration Department vide Government Notification No. SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/12, dated 21.06.2021 in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021.
The Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer/ Head of the Department is requested to bring this seniority to the notice of the concerned Superintendent, so that his signature is preserved in the records. It will be the responsibility of all concerned offices to provide this access to the government if the government deems it necessary.
If the concerned Superintendent has any objection in the said seniority list, he should submit his written objections/ representations in this regard to the Government through the concerned Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer/ Head of the Department along with appropriate evidence and documents within 15 days from the publication of the said provisional seniority list. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the seniority will be published in the final form, assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
