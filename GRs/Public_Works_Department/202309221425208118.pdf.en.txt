Introduction: The Public Works Department has a major contribution in creating infrastructure like roads/ bridges/ buildings for the development of the state of Maharashtra. The nature of this department is that of technical work and the Conceptual Board is the core of this department. The post of Chief Engineer cadre is not sanctioned in Sankalp Chitra Mandal, Konkan Bhawan, Navi Mumbai, in which two posts of Superintending Engineer, Sankalp Chitra Mandal (Bridge) and Superintending Engineer, Sankalp Chitra Mandal (Buildings) are sanctioned. Of these, the post of Superintending Engineer, Sankalp Chitra Mandal (Bridge), Navi Mumbai is vacant. At present, the work of conceptualizing construction according to new technology is in progress in the Public Works Department, it is necessary to provide experienced officers in this post. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to temporarily upgrade the post of Superintending Engineer, Sankalp Chitra Mandal (Bridge), Navi Mumbai to the cadre of Chief Engineer (Architecture) and post the officers of Chief Engineer (Station) cadre there.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: The Deputy Engineer on Establishment of Public Works Department, SANKALPITRA (Bridge) Component No. The Government is pleased to dedicate one post, Navi Mumbai (Revised Pay Matrix S-20: 56100-177500) and temporarily upgrade the post of Superintending Engineer, Sankalp Chitra Mandal (Bridge), Navi Mumbai (Revised Pay Matrix S-25: 78800 209200) to the rank of Chief Engineer (Architecture), Sankalp Chitra Division, Navi Mumbai (Revised Pay Matrix S-29: 131100-216600).
