Introduction:
A total of 12 roads in Nilanga and Shirur Anantapal taluks of Latur district have been completed under Magrarohoyo and need to be included in the road development plan for further maintenance and repair. Accordingly, the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad submitted a proposal to the government to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Considering the said proposal, a total of 12 roads in Nilanga and Shirur Anantapal talukas of Latur district were under consideration of the government to include them as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad, the following 12 roads in Nilanga and Shirur Anantapal taluks of Latur district are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
