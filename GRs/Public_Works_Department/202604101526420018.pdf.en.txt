Introduction: Kedareshwar Temple B Mahadev Temple at the base of Sajjangarh in Parli, Satara district are Shiva temples in the ancient Hemadampati shell. The temples are believed to date from the 13th to 14th centuries, with beautiful carvings. Approximately 6 to 7 lakh devotees visit Kedareshwar Temple and Mahadev Temple during the year. At present, the temple is in a state of disrepair. ATA of these temples is required and devotees coming to Sajjangarh after Jirnadhdaar will also visit these temples, which will boost the growth of tourism in these places.
"Parli, Ta. B. To preserve and promote the Shiva temples here at Satara G. "The budget for this work has been received by the government. A meeting of the committee constituted to preserve and conserve ancient temples and caves in the state of Maharashtra was held. Done on 07.01.2026.
In pursuance of the proposal received in the meeting, it has been approved to carry out the renovation work of Kedareshwar and Mahadev Temple at Parli through the Subordinate Regional Office of Public Works Department. It was under the consideration of the government to grant administrative approval to the budget for the work.
