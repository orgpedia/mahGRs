Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Lakhandur, Lakhani and Sakoli talukas of Bhandara district under the category of state highways and major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Bhandara has demanded to upgrade and include the said roads.
Considering the usage of the said roads and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Bhandara, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads and include them in the category of state roads and major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Bhandara, the following roads in Lakhandur, Lakhani and Sakoli talukas of Bhandara district are being upgraded and included in the category of state highways and major district roads.
