Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads in Jafrabad and Bhokardan talukas of Jalna district as major district roads through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Jalna has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Jalna, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Jalna, the following other district roads in Jafrabad and Bhokardan talukas of Jalna district are being upgraded as major district roads.
