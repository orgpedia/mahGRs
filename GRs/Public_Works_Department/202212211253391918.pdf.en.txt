Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan has submitted a proposal to the government to include the unplanned road in Sudhagad-Pali Taluka of Raigad District as rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 through the reference letter. The Chief Executive Officer, Raigad Zilla Parishad, Alibaug has requested to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the demand of the Chief Executive Officer, Raigad Zilla Parishad, Alibaug, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Considering the demand of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and Chief Executive Officer, Raigad Zilla Parishad, Alibaug, the following road in Sudhagad-Pali Taluka of Raigad District is being included in the Road Development Plan Year 21 as a rural road.
