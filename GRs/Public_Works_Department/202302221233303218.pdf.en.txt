Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the major district roads in Chikhli, Buldana and Mehkar talukas of Buldana district as state roads.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said roads, population, demand for use of the roads, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as state highways.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati, the following major district roads in Chikhali, Buldana and Mehkar talukas of Buldana district are being upgraded as state roads.
