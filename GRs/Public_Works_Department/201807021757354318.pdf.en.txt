Government Order: In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (one) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. Saleem Gulab Shaikh, Executive Engineer, (Standard) Buildings, Sankalpchitra Mandal, Navi Mumbai is transferred in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience as Executive Engineer, Public Works Special Projects Division, Chembur, Mumbai to Shri. Vr.Pr. Joshi (Nanoti) is being promoted to the post which is falling vacant.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, (Standard) Buildings, Sankalpchitra Mandal, Navi Mumbai.
3. Mr. Shaikh assumed charge of his new position with Shri. After Joshi&#39;s promotion, he should be accepted promptly and his induction report should be submitted to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, Mr. Shaikh&#39;s attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Shaikh is being transferred for administrative reasons, he will be paid tenure, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Shaikh should inform the Government and Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Opposite Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
