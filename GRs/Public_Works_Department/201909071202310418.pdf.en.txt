Government decision
The government plans to honor the officers/employees of class-1 to class-3 technical cadre as well as technical cadre-3 employees of the Public Works Department for the skill they have shown in government work by putting their administrative skills to the test and for their efforts to bring dynamism in government work by giving citations and medals every year. has started
2. Pursuant to the above decision, meritorious officers/employees in the following table have been selected for the year 2015-16. The date and time of the ceremony for awarding the certificate and badge to the said meritorious officer/employee will be intimated separately.
