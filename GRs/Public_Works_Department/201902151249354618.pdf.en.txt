Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of 2 rural roads in Khatav taluka of Satara district as major district roads through a reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, use of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
