Introduction:
By the Government decision under reference S.No. 226/370 (110/5-6) for construction of new railway flyover under Government Decision no. Administrative approval was granted vide PLN-2015/CR/(825) / Ni-3, dated 02.03.2015 for the work at an estimated cost of Rs.35.00 Crores (Rupees Thirty Five Crores, only). The budget included railway flyovers, approaches and miscellaneous items.
Chief Engineer, Mr. left The Regional Division, Aurangabad has submitted a revised estimate of Rs.4649.17 lakhs for the said work. The budget includes railway flyovers, approach roads, construction of additional bridges, construction of retaining walls, price hike, Goods and Services Tax (GST), cost overruns and miscellaneous items.
The issue of approving the revised budget amounting to Rs.4649.17 lakhs (Rupees Forty Six Crores, Fifty Nine Lakhs, Seventeen Thousand Only) for the said work was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Pune-Ahmadnagar-Aurangabad Road in Aurangabad District S.No. 226/370 (110/5-6) for construction of new railway flyover at a cost of Rs.4649.17 lakhs (Rupees Forty Six Crores, Fifty Nine Lakhs, Seventeen Thousand Only) as per the revised estimate for the work RM60 subject to the following condition Revised administrative approval is being provided as per this government decision.
1) While implementing the scheme, it will be the responsibility of the department to follow the prevailing government decisions, rules, CVC principles, financial limits, prescribed tender procedures, as necessary, to rectify the errors and instructions given by the State Technical Advisory Committee in accordance with the project and work.
2) The said amended administrative approval shall not be deemed to be approval of technical matters in the report, zonal decision on tender or irregularities. It will be the responsibility of the department to investigate such a case as per the rules prevailing at that time.
3) The said project work should be completed within the scheduled time and within the revised administrative approval provision.
