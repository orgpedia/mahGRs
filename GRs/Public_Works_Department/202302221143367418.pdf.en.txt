Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also District
Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following road in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara District is being included in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21 as a rural road.
