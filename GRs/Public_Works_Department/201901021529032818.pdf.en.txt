Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to upgrade Patpanhale - Waki Pimpalwat - Aarey to Major Rajya Marg 4 road as Major District Road in Guhagar Taluka of Ratnagiri District vide letters under reference. It has been demanded as per the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri to upgrade the said road. Considering the importance of the said road and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade this road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following road in Guhagar Taluka of Ratnagiri District is being upgraded.
