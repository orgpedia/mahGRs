The Government's Decision
Under the Public Works Department, roads, bridges, buildings of various departments and other public constructions are carried out. Engineers are honoured with individual awards for their administrative as well as technical skills in conceptualizing buildings / bridges and making concept films using other means such as computers, electrification of public buildings and implementing projects that will benefit the community in the future.
In line with the same, the Government has decided to confer the "Best Engineer" award along with the "Best Road," "Best Bridge" and "Best Building" awards annually under the Public Works Department in the run-up to Engineer's Day celebrated on 15th September, the birth anniversary of Bharat Ratna Dr. Visvesvaraya.
3. Accordingly, it has been decided to award "1) Best Road 2) Best Bridge and 3) Best Building" to notable and unique constructions mentioned in the following prospectus of the Public Works Department.
