Government Order:
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. Dayanand Purbhaji Bahatre, Office of the Executive Engineer (vs Chief Engineer (Electricity), Mumbai) is deputed on his request and in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience, to the vacant post of Executive Engineer Nashik (Electricity) Division, Nashik.
2. Another officer will be posted separately in the post of Executive Engineer (Electrical), Office of Chief Engineer (Electrical), Mumbai.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government of the date of assumption of charge by the deputized officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
4. Mr. Bahatre should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent unauthorizedly, otherwise their absence will be considered as unauthorized absence and will be unpaid, and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of the said rule, if they bring any form of political or external pressure to change the posting, they will be liable for disciplinary action.
5. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of assumption of charge by the commissioned officers.
If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30(c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
