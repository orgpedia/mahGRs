Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the other district roads and rural roads in Shindkheda Taluka of Dhule district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Dhule has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the following Ijima and Grama roads in Shindkheda Taluka of Dhule district are being upgraded as major district roads.
