Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads and other district roads in Kadegaon taluka of Sangli district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Sangli has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Sangli, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Sangli, the following roads in Sangli district are being upgraded.

As a result, the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 in Sangli district has a total length of 76,500 km of major district road grade roads. By increasing the total length (3023.720+76.500 ) i.e. 3100.220 km. Soli and other district roads are 20.700 km in road length. By reducing the total length (2009.07-20.700) i.e. 1988.37 km. Also, the total length of rural roads is 55,800 km. Total length (6044.01 - 55.800) i.e. 5988.210 Km. That will be it.
Also, no works or budgetary works will be proposed under any other scheme and no new land acquisition will be done for the said road in the length of which the work has been completed or proposed under the Pradhan Mantri Gramsadak / Chief Minister Gramsadak Scheme and other schemes or there is remaining liability period for the work done. Approval is being given to upgrade the road subject to these conditions.
