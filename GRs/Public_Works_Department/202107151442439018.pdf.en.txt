Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Latur taluka of Latur district as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also, Zilla Parishad, Latur has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the road development plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Latur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Latur, the following roads in Latur taluka of Latur district are being upgraded as rural roads.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road     | Chain no      | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Gangapur to Umbadga road. | 0/00 to 2/500 | 2.50 km.                         | Gramin Marg-167     |
| 2.    | Gangapur to Budhoda road. | 0/00 to 3/00  | 3.00 km.                         | Gramin Marg-168     |
| Total | 5.50 km.                  |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
