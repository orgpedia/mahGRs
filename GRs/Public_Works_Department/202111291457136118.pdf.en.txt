Preface: A new District Field Laboratory has been set up under Vigilance and Quality Control Sub Division under Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Thane to check the quality of construction projects in Thane district. The issue of placing the said laboratory under Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Navi Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision : Superintending Engineer under Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan, Deputy Engineer under Public Works Board, Thane, Vigilance and Quality Control Sub-Division, Kopri, Thane in terms of administrative work and subject to the condition that no additional financial burden will fall on the Government. , is being placed under the supervision of Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Navi Mumbai, and his nomination is approved as District Laboratory, Thane as per this Government decision.
2. The decision regarding inclusion of necessary officers / staff as indicated in Annexure-1 for District Laboratory, Thane should be taken by the Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Navi Mumbai in consultation with the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Thane.
3. Restrictions have been placed on the creation of new posts under Finance Department, Government Decision, dated 04/05/2020. Therefore, the two posts of Laboratory Assistant and Laboratory Attendant required for the District Regional Laboratory should not be newly created in the present situation but the equivalent officers/employees should be trained and the work related to the laboratory should be done.
4. Trained Officers/Employees made available to the District Field Laboratory should not be transferred before the completion of their tenure in the said post.
