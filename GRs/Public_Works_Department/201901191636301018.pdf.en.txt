Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of 4 other district roads in Washim and Mangrulpir talukas of Washim district as major district roads. Also, there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Washim regarding the upgrading of the said roads.
Considering the traffic volume on the said road, the population of the villages on the road, the use of the road and the demand of the people&#39;s representatives as well as the demand of Zilla Parishad, Washim, the said road was under the consideration of the government to upgrade it as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Washim, the following roads in Washim and Mangrulpir talukas of Washim district are being upgraded.
