Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Kokan, vide letters under reference, proposed to upgrade the main state highway-4 to Agvekarwadi- Haldankarwadi shop - Jakadevi temple - Savarewadi - Adivare - Kondsar Khurd - Bengi - Bhalawli to Alwachi Fandi road as district main road in Rajapur taluka of Ratnagiri district. is presented. Also, there is a demand from Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri regarding the upgrading of the said road. Considering the traffic volume on the said road, the population of the villages and the use of the road, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a highway.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following road in Rajapur Taluka of Ratnagiri District is being upgraded.
