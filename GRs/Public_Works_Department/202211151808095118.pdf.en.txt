2. After the merger of the workshops as above, the merged workshops are instructed to take the following actions:
1) The records of the employees of the merged office in the office case register, reference register, informal reference register as well as incoming outgoing register etc. as on 31st December,
It should be done in the name of previous office till 2022.
2) From 1st January, 2023, all documents should be recorded in the name of the merged office.
3) The branch should register the seating arrangement of the merged officers/employees.
4) File numbers should not be changed on old records in the merged department.
But its records should be kept separately in the merged office.
5) Condemned debts of the merged office should be taken care of by the new office.
3. The subject list of the department will be revised according to the reorganization of the workshops as above. Posting orders of additional officers and employees due to reorganization will be issued separately.
