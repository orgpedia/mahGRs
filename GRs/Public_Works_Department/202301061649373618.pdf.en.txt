Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan has submitted a proposal to the government for upgrading the major district roads in Bhiwandi, Kalyan and Ambernath talukas of Thane district as state highways through the reference letter. Also, Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Thane have demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the roads as well as the demand of the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Thane, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as state highways.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the demand of Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Thane, Bhiwandi in Thane district,
The following major district roads in Kalyan and Ambernath taluks are being upgraded as state roads.
