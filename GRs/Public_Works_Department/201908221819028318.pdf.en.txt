Government Order:
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4(4) and 4(5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. Ch. W. Javale, Superintending Engineer, Pune Metropolitan Region Area Development Authority, Pune has been transferred as per his request and in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience, as Superintending Engineer and State Quality Coordinator, Pradhan Mantri Gramsadak Yojana, Pune. The post of deputation is being made vacant due to the transfer of Sontakke.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Superintending Engineer, Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Pune. Mr. Transfer orders of Sontakke will be issued separately.
3. Mr. Javale should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government within eight days from the date of the order. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and they will be without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately.
M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Javale is being transferred as per his request, he will not be paid the joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules.
6. Mr. Javale has been posted on the post of deputation and the said posting is for the duration of the Maharashtra Civil Service (tenure of induction, post-commissioned service and suspension, dismissal and removal from service).
