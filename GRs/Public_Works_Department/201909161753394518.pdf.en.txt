Introduction: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of the road connecting Devasade to Prajima 43 in Nevasa Taluka of Ahmedananagar District and including it in Prajima 185 through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the usage of the said road and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road and include it in Prajima 185.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar, the following road in Nevasa Taluka of Ahmednagar District is being upgraded and included in Project 185.
