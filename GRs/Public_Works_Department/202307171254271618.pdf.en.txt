Introduction:
The office of the Superintending Engineer under the Chief Engineer (National Highways), Navi Mumbai, the Executive Engineer under the National Highways Board, Nagpur, the Deputy Engineer under the National Highways Department, Akola, the Sub-Divisional Office of the National Highways Sub-Division, Amravati is presently functional.
With a view to ensure smooth and timely execution of National Highways works, the Government was considering to attach the office of Executive Engineer, National Highways Division, Akola under the Deputy Engineer, National Highways Sub-Division, Amravati under the Divisional Office of the Sub-Division Executive Engineer, National Highways Division, Amravati.
Government Decision: The Office of the Chief Engineer (National Highways), Superintending Engineer under Navi Mumbai, Executive Engineer under National Highways Board, Nagpur, Deputy Engineer under National Highways Department, Akola, National Highways Sub-Division, Amravati will be transferred to the Divisional Office of the Executive Engineer, National Highways Department, Amravati, subject to the condition that there will be no additional financial burden on the Government including the sanctioned posts. 1, Amaravati is being approved under this government decision. Accordingly, the revised structure of offices under the Superintending Engineer, National Highways Board, Nagpur is as follows:
