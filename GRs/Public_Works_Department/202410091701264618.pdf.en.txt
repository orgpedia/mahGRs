3. Mr. Chakranarayan has been promoted to the post of Special Superintendent from the selection list of the 2021-22. The year 2021-22 is followed by the year 2022-23 and the year 2023-24 is the end of the calendar year. However, Mr. Chakranarayan is yet to join the promotion post. This shows that Mr. Chakranarayan is not interested in promotion. Hence, Reference No. 3 General Administration Department, Govt. As per the provisions in the 12/09/2016, Mr. Sunil A.
