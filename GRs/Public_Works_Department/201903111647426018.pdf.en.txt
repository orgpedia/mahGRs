Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding upgradation of 2 other district roads and 1 unplanned road in Ausa taluka of Latur district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Latur has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Latur, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Latur, the following roads in Ausa Taluka of Latur District are being upgraded.
