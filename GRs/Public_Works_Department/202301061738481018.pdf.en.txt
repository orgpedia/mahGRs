Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati vide reference letter has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding inclusion of unplanned roads in Akot Taluka of Akola District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also
Zilla Parishad, Akola has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Akala,
The said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Akala, the following unplanned roads in Akot Taluka of Akala district are being upgraded as rural roads.
