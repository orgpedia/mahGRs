Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Miraj Taluka of Sangli District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, the Administrator, Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Sangli has requested to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the demand of the Administrator, Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Sangli, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the demand of Administrator, Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Sangli, the following roads in Miraj Taluka of Sangli district are being upgraded as rural roads.
