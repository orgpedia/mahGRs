Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of Vadgaon Mali-Sawangir-Mohodari-Koyali-Babhulkhedate-Rajyamarg-214-Sarangpur (Mehkar Diwan Marg) to Ganpur-Borakhedi road as a major district road in Buldana district through the letter under reference. is presented. Also, Zilla Parishad, Buldana has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the traffic volume on the said road and the number of villages, population as well as the total usage of the road as well as the demand of Zilla Parishad, Buldana, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the following roads in Buldana district are being upgraded.
