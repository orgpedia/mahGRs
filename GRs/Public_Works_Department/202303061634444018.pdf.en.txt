Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of 3 unplanned roads in Koregaon Taluka of Satara District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to include them as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Government Decision: Koregaon in Satara district considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara
The following non-plan roads in the taluka are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
