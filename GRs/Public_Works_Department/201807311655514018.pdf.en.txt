Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Amravati vide letter under reference, Dhamangaon Bade in Motala Taluka of Buldana District - Ridhora Kh. - A proposal has been submitted to the government regarding the upgrading of Pofli - Kolhi Gawli Ava Yunuspur - Dahigaon road as a major district road. Also, Zilla Parishad, Buldana has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population and the total usage of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the following roads in Motala Taluka of Buldana District are being upgraded.
