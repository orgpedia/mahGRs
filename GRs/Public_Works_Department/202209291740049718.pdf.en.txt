5. The concerned officials should assume charge of the new post with minimum induction period and submit their induction report to the government without delay. The said officer shall be promoted to the post of posting
If they do not join within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of the order, their period of absence will be treated as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979
Referring to Rule-23 of Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
