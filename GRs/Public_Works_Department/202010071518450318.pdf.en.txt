Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Kokan vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include an unplanned road in Khed Taluka of Ratnagiri District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the usage of the said road and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the government is considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following road in Khed Taluka of Ratnagiri District is being upgraded as a rural road.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road                                                     | Chain No.    |          | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Mirle Zolai Kelmai Temple to Mirle Dhangarwadi to Sheldi Dhangarwadi Road | 0/00 to 4/00 | 4.00 km. | Rural Marg-488                   |
| Total | 4.00 km.                                                                  |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
