Preface : 5 persons died in an accident on Bandra-Worli Sea Bridge on 05.10.2022. The investigation of this accident is expected to be done independently by the police system as per the provisions of the Motor Vehicle Act. However, the road is a toll road and the government is considering appointing a committee through the Public Works Department to suggest measures to prevent accidents on this road.
Government Decision: In order to take measures to prevent accidents on Bandra-Worli Sagari Setu toll road, the following committee is being formed through Public Works Department.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | name                                                |
| 1     | Additional Chief Secretary, Public Works Department | President        |
| 2     | Secretary (Roads), Public Works Department          | Member           |
| 3     | Secretary (Construction), Public Works Department   | Member           |
| 4     | Joint Secretary (Services), Public Works Department | Member Secretary |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The working class of the said committee will be as follows: 1. To suggest measures to prevent accidents on Bandra-Worli Setu sea route.
2. d. Taking into account the accident that occurred on 05.10.2022, was there a lack of measures to prevent accidents through the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation on the said sea route or how? 3. The committee will be required to report within one month.
