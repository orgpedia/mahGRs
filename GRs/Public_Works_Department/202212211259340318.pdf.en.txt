The transfer of the length of the road to the Public Works Department was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision : Koyna Nagar, Navja to Dicholi State Road no. 148 km. 0 /00 to 15/00 This road is under Koyna hydropower project phase no. 4 is under the possession of Water Resources Department for construction. Currently, the work of Koyna Hydropower Project has been completed and no work has been approved/proposed on the said road through the Water Resources Department as per the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the certificate submitted by Executive Engineer, Satara Koyna Irrigation Department, Koynanagar. Also under reference no. (2) The Irrigation Department has no objection to open the said road for public use and use it for public purposes as per the certificate submitted by the Superintending Engineer, Satara Irrigation Board, Satara. Based on this, the said road owned by the Public Works Department is being transferred from the Water Resources Department to the Public Works Department along with the ownership right.
