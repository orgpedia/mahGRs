Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding inclusion of unplanned roads in Gondia Taluka of Gondia District as Rural Roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also District
Parishad, Gondia has requested to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Considering the usage of the said roads and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Gondia, it was under the consideration of the government to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Gondia, the following unplanned roads in Gondia Taluka of Gondia District are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
