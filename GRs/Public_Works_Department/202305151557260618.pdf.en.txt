The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government to include unplanned roads in Gevrai and Beed talukas of Beed district as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed have requested to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the demand of the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed, the said roads were under the consideration of the Government to include them as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed, the following roads in Gevrai and Beed talukas of Beed district are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
