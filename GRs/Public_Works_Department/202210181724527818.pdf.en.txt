Preamble: Consolidated guidelines for recruitment through direct service to posts in the nomination quota of Group-C cadre under Ex-Secondary Services Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) have been issued by the General Administration Department vide Government Decision dated 04.5.2022. Accordingly, the composition of regional and state level selection committees for the recruitment of the said posts has been mentioned in the said government decision. Accordingly, the administrative department of the ministry has to establish regional and state level selection committees. In this regard, it is necessary to establish selection committees for each department as per the structure given in the General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 04.5.2022. Accordingly, the matter of setting up a state-level selection committee for filling up the posts in the nomination quota in the office of Director, Forests and Parks, Maharashtra State, Mumbai through direct service was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As approved under the Government Decision dated 27.12.2021 and Government Circular dated 15.2.2022 of the Office of the Director, Forests and Parks, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai in Public Works Department, to fill up the posts in the nomination quota of Group-C cadre in the said office through direct service. The composition of the State Level Selection Committee is being done as follows.
