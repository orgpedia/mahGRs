To see: :- 1) Letter from Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune Ja.No.Ka-2/Resha/28/2001/Year 2023, dated 27/03/2023 Foreword:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Jawli Taluka of Satara District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following road in Jawli Taluka of Satara District is being included in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21 as a rural road.
