Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade the rural road road in Karjat taluka of Raigad district as a major district road vide letter under reference. Also, Raigad Zilla Parishad, Alibaug has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, use of the road as well as the resolution of Raigad Zilla Parishad, Alibaug, the said road was under the consideration of the government to upgrade it as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Raigad Zilla Parishad, Alibaug, the following road in Karjat Taluka of Raigad District is being upgraded as a major district road.
