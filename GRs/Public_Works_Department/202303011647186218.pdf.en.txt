Introduction:
Officers/employees working in the office under the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, due to invalidation of Scheduled Tribe caste claims, created surplus posts for them vide Government Decision, Public Works Department, dated June 01, 2021 or if they had remained in service for 11 months. An extension has been granted till the date on which he would have retired or till the final decision of the Government on the recommendations of the Study Group, whichever is earlier. Expiry date of surplus post created for the said officers/employees. As it was terminated on 30th September, 2022, the issue of extending the term of the surplus post was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Due to invalidation of scheduled tribe caste claims of the following officers/employees working in the office under the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, Public Works Department, Government Decision, dated June 01, 2021, General Administration Department, As per Government Decision, Anu No. 2 dated December 14, 2022, extension is being given for 11 months (from October 2, 2022 to September 01, 2023) or till the date of their retirement by giving a technical break of 01 days.
