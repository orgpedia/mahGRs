Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of State Road 264 Shelagoon (Chhatri) Sujlegaon Aural Kothala Dugaon Belkoni Phata Other District Road 161 in Naigaon Taluka of Nanded District as a major district road under reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nanded has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the following roads in Nanded district are being upgraded.
