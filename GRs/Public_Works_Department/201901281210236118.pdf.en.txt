Introduction:
The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference to include four unplanned roads in Mudkhed Taluka of Nanded District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
These roads are important in terms of communication and transportation. The said roads are unpaved roads and no land acquisition is required for this. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Nanded regarding the inclusion of roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the above mentioned road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the following roads in Mudkhed Taluka of Nanded District are being included as Rural Roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
