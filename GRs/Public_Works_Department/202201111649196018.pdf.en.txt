Refer to :- 1) Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad letter No. Muau/Ka-14/Pra/Ravio-01-21/3500, dated 12/08/2021.
Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Chachur taluka of Latur district as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also, Zilla Parishad, Latur has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said road as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Latur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Latur, the following road in Chachur Taluka of Latur District is being upgraded as a rural road.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road                               | Chain no     | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Road from Chachur Pragima-33 to Tirthwadi Grama-51. | 0/00 to 3/00 | 3.00 km.                         | Rural Marg-103      |
| Total | 3.00 km.                                            |
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Latur district in Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 3.00 km. By increasing the total length (3813.060 + 3.00) i.e. 3816.060 km.
