Government order
In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority, Deputy Engineer (Establishment) column no. 4 is being posted by transfer to the post shown against his name here.
2. The Controlling Officers concerned are hereby informed that they may immediately relieve the Deputy Engineer (Est) mentioned in Annexure “A” to take charge at the place of new appointment by making necessary local alternate arrangements and direct him to join immediately at the place of new appointment. However, the officers who have been deputed to the post falling vacant by retirement should be relieved by making necessary local alternate arrangements so that the officers concerned can take charge at the place of new appointment after the retirement of the concerned officer.
3. The Deputy Engineer (St.) mentioned in the attached Annexure “A” should assume charge of the new post promptly and submit his assumption of charge report to the Government without delay. Engineers referred to in Annexure “A” should ensure that the transferred Engineers join at the place of transfer within the period of engagement given in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Service and Allowances during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, otherwise the appointment shall be cancelled. If the replacement post is not taken up within the period, the period of absence will be counted as dies non. Also their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be eligible for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for transfer in posting.
4. The Controlling Officers concerned are informed that they should immediately inform the Government of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge by the discharged and decommissioned officers. If the concerned officer does not join the place of transfer within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government, so that
