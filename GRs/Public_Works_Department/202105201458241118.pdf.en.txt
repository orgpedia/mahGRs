Government Circular:
The final seniority scale dated 01.01.2014 of the cadre of Sub Divisional Engineer (Civil) in the Public Works Department for the period dated 01.01.2001 to 31.12.2013 published vide circular dated 29.01.2015 under reference no.2 is
2. In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department at Reference No. 1 and the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, the cadre of Sub Divisional Engineer (Civil) dated 01.01.2014 to 31.12. Provisional seniority list dated 01.01.2018 for the period 2017 is being published as “Form-A” attached with this circular. Working Seniority List of engineers working on the post of Sub Divisional Engineer (Construction) issued under Government Letter No.Seniority 2020/P.No.28/Aastha-1, dated 12.02.2021 is being validated.
3. Hon. High Court, Bombay vide judgment dated 04.08.2017 in Petition No. 2797/2015 invalidated reservation in promotion. Hon. Against the decision of the High Court, the state government Hon.
Supreme Court Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed. The said petition is still pending and subject to the final decision of the said petition, the provisional seniority list dated 01.01.2018 for the period of 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2017 of the cadre of Sub Divisional Engineer (Civil) is being published through this circular. All regional and zonal offices should immediately bring the said circular to the notice of the Sub-Divisional Engineer (Civil) in Form “A” under their authority, and keep a copy signed by them in their records.
4. If there are any errors/mistakes in the details of the Sub-Divisional Engineer (Civil) contained in the said seniority list such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority etc., the same should be reported in writing within 07 days from the date of issue of this circular along with necessary proofs. - Your representations / objections / suggestions should be brought to the direction of Govt. If the errors / errors are not brought to the attention of the Government in writing within the prescribed time, assuming that you have no objection, the provisional seniority scale of the cadre of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Civil) dated 01.01.2018 will be published.
