Read- Circular dated 5.7.2023.
The Central Government has launched an online portal for training government officers/ employees under the name of" Karmayogi Bharat." Many useful trainings are available free of cost on this training portal. All the officers/ employees of the state are required to undergo various types of useful training using this portal. Accordingly, the circular dated 5.7.2023 has made it mandatory for all officers and employees of the Public Works Department to undergo various trainings on the" Karmayogi Bharat" portal.
All officers/ staff of Public Works Department (including field offices) should regularly undertake at least one training on" Karmayogi Bharat" portal in a week.
All Chief Engineers/ Superintending Engineers of Public Works Department shall direct all concerned to conduct online training of all officers/ staff under them at the place where they are working and submit a report of completion of work every month.
The circular has been made available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharshtra.gov.in and its computer code is 202308031130253518.
