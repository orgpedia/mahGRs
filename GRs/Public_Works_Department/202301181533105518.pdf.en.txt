Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Chandgad taluka of Kolhapur district as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following unplanned roads in Chandgad Taluka of Kolhapur District are being upgraded as rural roads.
