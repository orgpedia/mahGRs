3. The Government reserves the right to cancel the said promotion to the above mentioned officers without any prior intimation or notice.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they may relieve the said Officers by making necessary local alternate arrangements and ask them to report to their new places of appointment. The appointment made to the post vacated by promotion shall take effect after the promotion of the officer concerned. Also, the concerned officers should be immediately informed to the Government on the e-mail id doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in about the date on which the concerned officers were released from duty to join the place of posting and on which date they appeared at the place of posting.
5. The concerned officials should assume charge of the new post with minimum induction period and submit their induction report to the government without delay. If the said officer does not join the post on promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of this order, his period of absence shall be treated as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
