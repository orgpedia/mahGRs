Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade major district roads in Gadchiroli, Kurkheda, Armori and Vadsa talukas of Gadchiroli district as state highways and other district roads and rural roads as major district roads in Gadchiroli district.
Taking into account the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as state highways and major district roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and taking into consideration the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Gadchiroli, the following major district roads in Gadchiroli, Kurkheda, Armori and Vadsa talukas of Gadchiroli district, as state highways and other district roads and rural roads roads major district are being upgraded as roads.

As a result, the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 increased the total length of State Highways in Gadchiroli District to 58,500 km. By increasing the total length (2912.250 + 58.500) i.e. 2970.750 km. So much so that the total length of major district roads is 198.650 km. By increasing the total length (1043.470 + 198.650) i.e. 1242.120 km. That will be it. Also, the total length of other district roads is 123.350 km. By reducing the total length (1374.750 - 123.350) i.e. 1251.400 km. That will be it. Also, the total length of rural roads is 16,800 km. By increasing the total length (38.150 - 16.800) i.e. 21.350 km. That will be it.
