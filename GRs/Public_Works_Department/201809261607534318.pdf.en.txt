Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of Malshiras - Paniv to Major District Road - 91 (Yashwantnagar) road in Malshiras Taluka of Solapur District as a Major District Road. Also, Zilla Parishad, Solapur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the following roads in Malshiras Taluka of Solapur District are being upgraded.
