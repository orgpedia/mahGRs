Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Parola Taluka of Jalgaon District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon has requested to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said road and the resolution of the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon, the following roads in Parola Taluka of Jalgaon District are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-2021.
