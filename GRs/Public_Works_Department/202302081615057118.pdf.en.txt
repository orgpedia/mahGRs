Government Decision: As per the instructions given under the reference Government Decision dated 10/11/2022, in the year 2022-23, the period of publication of e-tenders is up to 31/01/2023 due to the non-announcement of elections in Municipalities/Municipalities, Nagar Panchayats, Gram Panchayats, Zilla Parishads. The following has been implemented.
-------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Cost of work               | duration |
| 1     | Rs. 10 lakhs to 1.5 crores | 8 days   |
| 2     | Rs. 1.5 crore to 100 crore | 15 days  |
| 3     | Rs. More than 100 crores   | 30 days  |
-------------------------------------------------
Now the said notification dt. Will be applicable till the end of 31/03/2023.
