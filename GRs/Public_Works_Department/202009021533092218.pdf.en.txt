Referenced by Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad
According to the letter, a proposal has been submitted to the government to upgrade the major district roads in Ambad and Ghansawangi talukas of Jalna district and include them in the state highway.
Considering the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population as well as the total usage of the roads, the inclusion of the said roads in the national highway is under the consideration of the government.
