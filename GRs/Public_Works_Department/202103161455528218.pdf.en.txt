Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Pune vide letter under reference from Ijima 59 in Radhanagari Taluka of Kolhapur District to Bardwadi Bottom Chandre Kasba Walve Pirachiwadi Savarde Bu. A proposal has been submitted to the government to upgrade the Ijima No. 61 road as Prajima. Also, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, from Ijima 59 in Radhanagari Taluka of Kolhapur District to Bardwadi Bottom, Chandre Kasba Walve Pirachiwadi Sawarde Bu. Ijima No. 61, Chain No. 0/00 to 16/600, Total Length 16.600 km. And, this road is being upgraded as Major District Road No.-113.
As a result, the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 has increased the total length of major district road grade roads in Kolhapur district to 16,600 km. By increasing the total length (1933.616 + 16.600) i.e. 19502.16 km. As well as other district roads, the total length of roads is 16,600 km. By reducing the total length (1677.455 - 16.600 ) i.e. 1660.855 km. That will be it.
