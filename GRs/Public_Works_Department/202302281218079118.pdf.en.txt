Refer to :- 1) Letter of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune Ja.No. M.A. No./P.A./ 3634/Proposal, dt. 26/12/2022
Introduction : The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Renapur taluka of Latur district as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also
Zilla Parishad, Latur has requested to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Latur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Latur, the following road in Renapur Taluka of Latur District is being included as a rural road in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No.            | Name of proposed road  | Chain no    | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| Taluka - Renapur |
| 1.               | Iti to Lakhmapur road. | 0/0 to 3/00 | 3.00 km.                         | Gramin Marg-131     |
| Total            | 3.00                   |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Latur district in Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 3.00 km. By increasing the total length (3600.760 + 3.00) i.e. 3603.760 km.
