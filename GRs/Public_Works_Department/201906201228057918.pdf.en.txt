Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade the following other district roads in Khandala Taluka of Satara District as Major District Roads vide letter under reference.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Satara district are being upgraded.
