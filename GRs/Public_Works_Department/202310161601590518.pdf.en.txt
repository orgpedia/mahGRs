Preamble: Sarvashree Naman, Rajvardhan Singh, Keshav Tiwari and Amarit Das, Divisional Accountants (Subordinate) have been posted as Accountants General (Accounts& Entitlements)-II, Maharashtra, Nagpur. The Government was considering to create 4 additional posts of Divisional Accountants (Sub-Divisional) for the period of probation and to provide salary and allowances to all the officers namely Shri Naman, Rajvardhan Singh, Keshav Tiwari and Amarit Das during the probation period.
Government Decision:
The Central Government has approved the creation of 04 supernumerary posts for the period of probation for the monthly salary and allowances to the newly appointed Shri Naman, Rajvardhan Singh, Keshav Tiwari and Amarit Das, Divisional Accountants (under probation) [Central Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2016 in the Level 6 (Six) in the Pay Matrix] in the office of the Accountant General (Accounts and Entitlements)-II, Maharashtra, Nagpur.
The recruitment process for the post of Divisional Accountant has been done by the Staff Selection Commission and the period of probation for this post is two years.
