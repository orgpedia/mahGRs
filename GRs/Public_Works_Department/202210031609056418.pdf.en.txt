Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan has submitted a proposal to the government to include the unclassified road from Solshet to Kosesari in Vikramgad taluk of Vikramgad district of Palghar district as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 through the letter under reference.
Considering the use of the said road as well as the importance of the road, and taking into consideration the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Palghar, Maharashtra State Human Rights Commission filed Sumoto case no.
467/2022 in the hearing, it was under the consideration of the government to include the said road as a special rural route in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21, as per the instructions given to undertake the work of the said road immediately.
Government decision
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Palghar, the following road in Vikramgad taluka of Palghar district is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as a rural road as a special matter.
