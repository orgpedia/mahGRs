Regarding a high level inquiry into the bridge damaged due to heavy rain in Nagpur Regional Division.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Government Decision No. Inquiry-2020/Q.No.205/ Roads- 4-A
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032
Date :- 04 September, 2020
Foreword: On 29th and 30th August, 2020, the bridge was damaged due to sudden release of water from Khairi dam on Pench river in Parshivani taluka of Nagpur district on 30th August, 2020 during the early hours of the morning, 8 stones in the middle of the bridge collapsed, Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Nagpur. has been informed.
Government Decision: Accordingly, a large bridge has been damaged due to heavy rains on Mahuli-Salai-Bitoli project 126 in Parshivani taluka of Nagpur district. Also, bridges have been damaged at other places in Nagpur regional division. A high level committee is being formed to investigate the matter. The composition of this committee will be as follows.
(1) Chief Engineer and Joint Secretary, Ministry of Public Works Department, Bombay Chairman,
(2) Superintending Engineer, Sankalp Chitra (Building) Member,
(3) Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Nagpur Member, (4) Superintending Engineer, Sankalp Chitra (Pool) Board, Navi Mumbai :- Member Secretary.
2. The Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Nagpur / Chandpur / Gadchiroli should make available the necessary documents and necessary information for the work of the committee for the said enquiry. Also, if bridges are damaged in this way at other places in Nagpur Regional Division, the Chief Engineer, Nagpur should inform the Committee and the same should be inspected by the Committee.
The working class of the committee will be as follows.
(1) To present the general cause of damage to the above bridge, (2) To suggest measures to prevent further damage of this type, (3) To suggest necessary improvements in the method of collection.
This committee should conduct necessary studies and submit its report to the government within two months.
