Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade major district roads in Mahur and Kinwat taluks of Nanded district as state highways under a reference letter.
Considering the number of villages on these roads, population, use of roads and demand, the government was considering to upgrade these roads as state highways.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded, the following major district roads in Mahur and Kinwat taluks of Nanded district are being upgraded as State Highways.
