Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgradation of rural roads in Majalgaon Taluka of Beed District as major district roads through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Beed has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Beed, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Osmanabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Beed, the following road in Majalgaon Taluka of Beed District is being upgraded as a major district road.
