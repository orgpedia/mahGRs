Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Parner taluka of Ahmednagar district as major district roads through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Parner has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parner, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parner, the following roads in Ahmednagar district are being upgraded.
