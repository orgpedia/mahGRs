Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgradation of other District Roads in Bhiwapur Taluka of Nagpur District as Major District Roads vide letter under reference. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur regarding the upgrading of the said road.
Considering the traffic volume on the said road, the population of the villages on the road, the use of the road and the demand of the public representatives as well as the demand of the Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the following roads in Bhiwapur Taluka of Nagpur District are being upgraded.
