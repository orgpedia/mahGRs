Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of Bopegaon - Vitthalwadi - Kenjal - Chandak - Chorachiwadi road (Other District Route-41) in Y Taluka of Satara District as a Major District Road through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, the population and the total usage of the roads as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Satara, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following road in Y taluka of Satara district is being upgraded.
