Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding upgradation of other district roads as major district roads in Sonpeth Taluka of Parbhani district vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Parbhani has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the following roads in Sonpeth Taluka of Parbhani district are being upgraded.
