Refer to :- 1) Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur letter no. Ka-Praa/6325 d. 23/07/2018.
Introduction:
The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur vide letter under reference to include 1) Arjuni to Savari Road and 2) Kolhapuri Bandhara to Batana Road in Gondia Taluka of Gondia District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. is
The said roads are important in terms of communication of the people of the villages on said roads. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Gondia to include the said roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to include them as rural roads in the Road Development Scheme 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Gondia, in Gondia Taluka of Gondia District, 1) Arjuni to Savari Road (Total Length 3.00 KM) This road is rural road no.271 A &amp; 2) Kolhapuri Dam to Batana Road (total length 2.00 km) This road is rural route no. 421 is therefore being included in Road Development Schemes 2001-21 on the condition that “no funds shall be provided for land acquisition of said roads”.
As a result, the total length of rural road grade roads in Gondia district is 5.00 km. With this increase, the total length is (4438.810 + 5.00) i.e. 4443.810 km. I. That will be it.
