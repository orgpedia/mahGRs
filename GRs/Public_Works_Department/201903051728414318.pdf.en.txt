Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the road from National Highway-361 to Sirpur Kharda Bopapur (Vani) Bhandori Ghonigaud Kolona to Rajyamarg-334 in Deoli taluka of Wardha district through the reference letter. .
The said road connects National Highway-361 and State Highway-334 and the road is convenient for the farmers of Wardha district in terms of communication. This road provides close access to famous Ganpati Devasthan, Chandrapur Industrial Area and Tadoba Tiger Reserve in Kalamba taluka. Also the road is useful for visiting Sevagram Ashram. Considering the importance of the said road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision : Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, the following road in Wardha district is being upgraded.
