Introduction:
A proposal to include the road from Other District Road-26 to Gadwalimura-Ahirmura Akalpemura-Uttreshwar Mandir-Lamjmura-Dodani Road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara District through the letter under reference from the Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Pune. has been received.
The said road is convenient for the villagers of Mahabaleshwar taluka as well as Ratnagiri and Raigad districts to go to Uttareshwar Temple, a religious place and thus the communication system in Mahabaleshwar taluka will be easy. The said road is unpaved and does not require land acquisition, so the above road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as a rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Satara, Mahabaleshwar in Satara district from other district route-26 to Gadvalimura-Ahirmura-Akalpemura-Uttareshwar temple-Lamjamura-Dodani road km. 0/00 to 16/00 Total length 16.00 km. This road is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001 21 as Rural Road No.138.
As a result the total length of rural road grade roads in Satara district is 16.00 km. By increasing the total length (7021.130 + 16.00) is 7037.130 km. I. That will be it.
