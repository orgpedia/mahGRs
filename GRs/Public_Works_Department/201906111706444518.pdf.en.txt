Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads and other district roads in Akola and Akot talukas of Akola district as major district roads through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Akola has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Akola, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Akola, the following roads in Akola district are being upgraded.
