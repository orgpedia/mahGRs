Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Kokan has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade 7 other district roads as major district roads in Karjat Taluka of Raigad district vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Raigad, Alibag have demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Raigad, Alibag, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Raigad, Alibaug, the following roads in Raigad district are being upgraded.
