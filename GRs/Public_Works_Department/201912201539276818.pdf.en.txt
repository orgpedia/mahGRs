Preamble: The Infrastructure Committee of the Cabinet in its meeting dated 30.11.2015 has approved the greenfield alignment of Nagpur Mumbai Expressway and appointment of Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited as the implementing agency. Accordingly, under the government decision dated 19.08.2016, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited has been appointed as the implementing agency for this project.
Nagpur-Mumbai Super Communication Expressway (Maharashtra Prosperity Highway) project implementation, fundraising and related activities vide Government Decision dated 22.11.2017 Nagpur-Mumbai Super Communication Expressway (Maharashtra Prosperity Highway) Project Nagpur-Mumbai Super Communication Expressway Limited Approval has been given to establish a company named Special Purpose Vehicle. Concession Agreement for Mumbai-Nagpur High Speed Rail (Maharashtra Prosperity Highway) project has been approved vide Government Decision dated 14.06.2018. This government decision included finalization of the final price of the project to be tendered and the subsequent grace period with the approval of the Finance Department.
Also, according to the government decision dated 01.10.2018, administrative approval and other ancillary matters have been approved for Nagpur-Mumbai High Speed Expressway (Maharashtra Samriddhi Highway) project cost. For this project, the proposal to approve the change in the financial plan of the project by keeping in mind the provisions of Article 25.2 of the concession agreement signed by the government with Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation and Special Purpose Vehicle NMSCEW Ltd was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the Cabinet has approved the said proposal on 11.12.2019, and accordingly the Government has now taken the following decision.

Government Decision: Regarding changes in the financial plan of the Nagpur-Mumbai Expressway (Maharashtra Samriddhi Highway) project, the following approval is given
A) Sustainable Debt should be maintained at Rs.28,000 crore. But approval is being granted to limit its withdrawal to the currently sanctioned Rs.24,500 crore.
b) The remaining part of the loan amounting to Rs.3,500 crores is approved by the government as part capital grant.
E) Even if the loan amount is Rs.24,500/- crore, whether to take the entire amount or not should be decided at that time as per the circumstances. Considering that the lower the debt, the greater the financial viability of this project, the government is approving the additional capital subsidy of Rs.5,000 crore for this project.
e) In accordance with a to e above, approval is being given to make the following budgetary provision.
