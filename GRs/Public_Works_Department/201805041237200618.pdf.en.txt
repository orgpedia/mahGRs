To form a committee for revising the spacing of building lines and control lines in roadside regulations.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Government Decision No.RBD-2018/Pro.No.138/Raste-7
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032
Date :- May 04, 2018
Introduction: The Public Works Department vide Government Decision No.RBD-1081/871/Road-7, dated 9.3.2001 has prescribed the distances of building lines and control lines to bring uniformity in roadside regulations. However, at the state level, proposals are being received by the government for relaxation in the prescribed intervals in the Roadside Rules. A review of the proposals received at the present time shows that there is a need to amend these rules.
For this, the government has decided to constitute a committee to discuss and amend the existing roadside rules regarding the spacing of building/control lines.
Government Decision: The following committee is being constituted to amend the roadside rules.
Principal Secretary (S.B.) Chairman Principal Secretary (Revenue) Member Principal Secretary (Urban Development) Member Secretary (Roads) (S.B.) Member Deputy Secretary (Roads) (S.B.) Member Secretary
Scope of work of the Committee: (1) To conduct a comprehensive study of the building lines, control lines, service roads in the existing Roadside Rules and make recommendations to the Government to improve the same.
(2) Coordination for computerization of online permission of Public Works Department and Urban Development Department as well as Revenue Departments in respect of compliance of road side rule and to suggest the course of action to be taken for the same.
