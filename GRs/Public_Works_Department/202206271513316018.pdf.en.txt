Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Kandahar Taluka of Nanded district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nanded has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the following roads in Kandhar Taluka of Nanded District are being upgraded.
