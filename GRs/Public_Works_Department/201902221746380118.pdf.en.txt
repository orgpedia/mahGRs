Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of RA.76 (Vadavali) to Asal Bhutivali Matheran Ropeway Station road as a major district road from Raigad district through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Alibaug has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Matheran Ropeway Project is proposed to go to Matheran, a famous place of cold weather and the proposed site of ropeway station for the ropeway project is near Bhutawali village. Taking into consideration the future traffic volume on the said road, the government is considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Alibaug, Rajyamarg-76 (Wadavali) in Raigad District to Asal Bhutivali Matheran Ropeway Station road S.No. The road from 0/00 to 2/600 (2.600 km) is being upgraded as Major District Road - 90.
Consequently, the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 has added 2,600 km to the total length of major district road grade roads in Raigad district. By increasing the total length (821.240 + 2.600 ) i.e. 823.840 km. That will be it.
