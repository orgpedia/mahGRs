Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the Palasgaon Dagwadi Anfale Maini Chitali Mahuli Road in Khatav Taluka of Satara District as a major district road. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the government is considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Satara district are being upgraded.
