Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Nevasa taluka of Nashik district as major district roads. Also Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmadnagar, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar, the following roads in Ahmednagar district are being upgraded.
