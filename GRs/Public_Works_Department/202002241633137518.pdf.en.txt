Preface Finance Dept. Reference No. According to the government decision of 2, administrative departments have been given six months to cancel 25 percent of the sanctioned posts in Group-D cadre. Accordingly, the issue of abolishing 25 percent of the posts of Group-D cadre in the offices of Director, Forests and Parks, Maharashtra State, Mumbai under the Public Works Department and under his authority was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision:
For the Director, Forests and Parks, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and the offices under his control under reference no. As per the Government decision of 1, the framework of 218 posts was approved.
2. Finance Department Reference No. As per the provision of paragraph 5 of the Government Decision of 2, 25 percent of the sanctioned posts in Group-D cadre are required to be cancelled. According to this government decision, the abolition of 02 posts namely Sepai-01 and Chowkidar-01 in the offices under Director, Forests and Parks, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and under them is approved.
