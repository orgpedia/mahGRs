Introduction:
Chief Engineer Public Works Regional Division, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of National Highway 146 Vaduj-Ganeshwadi-Yeralwadi Banpuri-Palasgaon-Khatwal-Anphale-Maini to R.M.143 road as a major district road in Khatav Taluka of Satara District.
The proposed road is an important road in terms of communication and for making traffic safe and fast. Also Zilla Parishad Satara has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the importance of the said road, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road.
Government Decision:
