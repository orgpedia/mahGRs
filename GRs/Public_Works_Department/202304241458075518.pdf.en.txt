Government Decision:
In order to take action as per the provisions of Section 7 and Section 8 of the Maharashtra Highways Act, 1955, Govt. read no. 2 The committee has been constituted as per the decision of the government here. The said committee has been directed to submit a report to the government by 23-04-2023.
Meanwhile, the chairman of the said committee has informed that it will be necessary to study the prevailing procedure of the land records department, land acquisition and accordingly submit the report to the government by 23 04-2023 to take action regarding the said subject and requested for an extension of another 4 months to submit the report. As per his request, another 4 months extension is being given to submit the said report to the government.
