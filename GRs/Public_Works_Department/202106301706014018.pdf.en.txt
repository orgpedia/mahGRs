Corrigendum: Public Works Department, Circular no. As per Seniority 2006/77/Establishment 1, dated 24.8.2006, the final seniority list for the period dated 1.4.1996 to 31.3.2003 of unqualified Junior Engineer (Civil) cadre has been published. Proposals have been received by the government regarding inclusion of new name in the said seniority list, correction in the name and correction in the service details against the name in the seniority list. Accordingly, the revision of the final seniority list published on 24.8.2006 for the cadre of Unqualified Junior Engineer (Civil) for the period dated 1.4.1996 to 31.3.2003 has been amended as mentioned in Annexure “A” attached hereto. A revised information statement is being issued.
