Dated - 06th February, 2020.
See :- 1) Letter from Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur. No. Ka Praa/565, dated 17/01/2019.
Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding upgrading Virli / Khurd Etan Road Grama 13 in Lakhandur Taluka of Bhandara District and including it in Prajima 39 under reference letter.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Bhandara has demanded to upgrade the said road and include it in Project 39.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Bhandara, the said road was under consideration of the government to upgrade it as a major district road and include it in Prajima 39.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Bhandara, Virli / Khurd Etan Road Grama 13 in Lakhandur Taluka of Bhandara District is being upgraded and included in Project 39.
The previous total length of Pragima 39 was 21.00 km. It is 4.00 km of village 13.
Total length of Pragima 39 after adding length is 25.00 km. It will happen. Also, Prajima 39 will be renamed as Kitli Masal Virli/Bu.Virli/Khurd Etan.
As a result, the total length of major district road grade roads in Bhandara district in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 4.00 km. By increasing the total length (674.51+4.00) i.e. 678.51 km. That will be it. Also the total length of rural roads is 4.00 km. By reducing the total length (3315.05-4.00) i.e. 3311.05 km.
That will be it.
