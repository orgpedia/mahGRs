Government Circular: -
With regard to the effective implementation of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, Shri. The main
The Public Works Commission chaired a meeting under the chairmanship of the Commissioner, State Right to Service Commission.
To re-engineer the business process of the notified services under the department.
A study group of experts from the department has been directed to be appointed. Sadar
In accordance with the directive, the following Study Groups are being set up.

The scope of the study group will be as follows: -
1) simplification of procedures with a view to providing the notified services more easily to the citizens and
Re-engineering the business process to make it smoother.
2) Create a Common Application Form.
3) Develop procedures for linking the service to Digi-Locker.
4) Setting a time frame for maintaining records regarding notified services.
Duration of submission of report: -
The above study groups should submit their report to the government within three months.
The study group may hold meetings as needed.
