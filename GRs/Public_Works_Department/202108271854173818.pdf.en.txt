Regulation of Transfers of Maharashtra Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 as per the provisions of Sections 4(1), 4(2) and 4(3) and with the approval of the Competent Authority, as well as General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 29.7 .2021 as per provision of Public Works Department Shri. (Period of Joining, Non-Accepted Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 shall continue to require joining the posted post within the given joining period. Otherwise the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Mane is being transferred for administrative reasons, he will continue to be paid his joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Mane to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assuming charge of the newly posted post.
