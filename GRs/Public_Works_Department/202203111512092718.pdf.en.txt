Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Manawat Taluka of Parbhani District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Parbhani has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the following road in Manavat Taluka of Parbhani district is being upgraded as a rural road.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road                                      | Chain no      | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Jodarsta Kharba T. Road from Manawat Ramma-61 to Ijima 28. | 0/00 to 3/500 | 3.500 km.                        | Rural Road – 56     |
| Total | 3.500 km.                                                  |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Parbhani district in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 3,500 km. By increasing the total length (2893.240 + 3.500) i.e. 2896.740 km.
