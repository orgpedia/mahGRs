Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan vide letter under reference, Satara and Jawli in Ratnagiri district,
A proposal has been submitted to the government to upgrade major district roads in Mahabaleshwar and Khed talukas as state roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan, the following major district roads in Javali, Mahabaleshwar and Khed talukas of Satara and Ratnagiri districts are being upgraded as state highways as a special matter.
