Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of the unplanned road in Kadegaon Taluka of Sangli District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, the Administrator, Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Sangli has requested to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the demand of the Administrator, Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Sangli, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
