Introduction:
The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference to include the unplanned roads from Jewli to Takli Road and 2) Jewli Cemetery (water supply well) to Bhuisamudra in Latur Taluka of Latur District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. is
The said roads are important in terms of communication of the people of the villages on said road. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Latur to include the said roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to include them as rural roads in the Road Development Scheme 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Latur, in Latur Taluka of Latur District, 1) Jevali to Takli Road (Total Length 3.00 KM) this road Rural Marg-162 2 ) The road from Jevali Smashanbhumi (water supply well) to Bhuisamudra (total length 4.00 km) is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as Rural Marg-163.
As a result, the total length of rural road quality roads in Latur district in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 was 7.00 km. By increasing by , the total length is (3654.010 + 7.00) i.e. 3661.010 km. That will be it.
