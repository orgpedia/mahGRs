Accordingly, after the approval given by the State Level Vehicle Review Committee, the Government has taken the following decision.
Government Decision:
Considering the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the establishment of the Public Works Department (own) and the subordinate offices, a decision has been taken regarding which posts need to be provided with vehicle facilities and accordingly the vehicle review is being approved. The vehicle review office-wise summary statement is as per the accompanying revised Form “A” and abstract.
Conditions regarding vehicle permit are as follows
1. After the vehicle review of the department is approved by the State Level Vehicle Review Committee, if the number of vehicles currently in the department is more than the number of vehicles permitted therein, new vehicles cannot be purchased to replace these additional vehicles. The said additional vehicles should be promptly transferred to other authorized offices.
2. Equipment Vehicles (Trucks, Tippers, Water Tankers, Road Rollers, Tractors, Mobile Vans, Mini Buses, Dozers, Mobile Bridge Inspection Machines etc.) should be registered on the computer system.
3. The Public Works Department should register 141 registered vehicles on the Mahavahana computer system.
