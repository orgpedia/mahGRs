Preface: Public Works Department under reference no.1, Government Decision dt. As on February 18, 2011, 29 posts of feeder-cadre in this department have been ensured for persons with disabilities. After that, the posts in the promotion chain of these 29 posts and 187 posts with generally similar duties as per designation dt. As per Government Decision dated 10th June, 2021 are ensured for the disabled. As reported by the expert committee headed by Principal Secretary (Social Justice), the remaining 10 such posts ensured by the Central Government for the differently abled. It has been ensured as per the Government Decision dated 20th July, 2021. Expert Committee of Social Justice Department. In the meeting held on 21.06.2021, it was decided to exclude the driver category from the posts ensured for the disabled. Accordingly, the proposal to exclude the post of driver in Group-C cadre in own and zonal offices under this department was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The post of &quot;Driver&quot; in the Group-C cadre in the Public Works Department&#39;s own and regional establishments is being excluded from the reservation made for the disabled.
