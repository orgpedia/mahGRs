Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding upgradation of other district roads in Kamthi taluka of Nagpur district as major district roads under reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nagpur has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, use of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the following roads in Nagpur district are being upgraded.
