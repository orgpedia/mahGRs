Government Order:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4(4) and 4(5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority and also as per the provisions of General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 29.7.2021, public Construction Department Mr. Dadaji no. Gangurde, Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad (1), Nashik has been transferred as per his request and also in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience Shri. Ms. A. The post of Executive Engineer, Public Works (North) Department, Nashik, which is vacant due to the transfer of Kankarej, is being filled. Mr. Kankraj&#39;s transfer orders will be issued separately.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad (1), Nashik.
3. Mr. Gangurde should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. Mr. Gangurde M. no Sec. (Period of Joining, Non-Acceptance of Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 shall continue to require joining the posted post within the given joining period. Otherwise the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Gangurde is being transferred as per his request, he will not be paid joining period, traveling allowance and daily allowance.
