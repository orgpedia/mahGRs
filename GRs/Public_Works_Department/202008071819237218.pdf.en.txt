Government Order:
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4(1), 4(2) and 4(3) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. Rs. Res. Hande, Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Nashik has been transferred in the interest of public service and administrative convenience to Shri. A. Bh. The post of Superintending Engineer, Pradhan Mantri Gramsadak Yojana, Pune, which is vacant due to the transfer of Chavan, is being made on deputation. Mr. Chavan&#39;s transfer orders are being issued separately.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Nashik.
3. Mr. Hande should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule - 30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Hande is being transferred for administrative reasons, he will be paid his joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Hande has been posted on the post of deputation and the said posting is for the duration of the Maharashtra Civil Service (tenure of appointment, post-commissioned service and suspension, dismissal and removal from service).
