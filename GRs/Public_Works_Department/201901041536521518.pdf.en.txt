Preface: The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai, through the under-referenced letter, to include the unplanned road from Kotaluk Kawankarwadi (State Road-173) to Narayan Tepe Road in Guhagar Taluka of Ratnagiri District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. The demand of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri is to include the said road in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the above road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as a rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, Kotaluk Kawankarwadi (State Route-173) to Narayan Tape Road in Guhagar Taluka of Ratnagiri District Km. This road from 0/00 to 0/500 (0.500 km) is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as Rural Marg No.262.
Consequently, the total length of rural roads in Ratnagiri district in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 was 0.500 km. By increasing by , the total length is (6597.600 + 0.500) i.e. 6598.100 km.
That will be it.
