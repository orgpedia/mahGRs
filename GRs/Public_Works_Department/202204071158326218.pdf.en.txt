As per the provisions mentioned in the rules regarding seniority and also as per the provisions laid down in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 vide Common Administration Department, No. SRV-2016/ Pr.No.281/ Karya.12, dated 21.06.2021, in the Public Works Department. Interim Provisional Seniority List dated 01.01.2022 of “Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Civil)” cadre is being published in the attached “Annexure-A”.
2. All the Chief Engineers are requested to distribute the said seniority list to all Circles / Boards / Divisions / Sub-Divisional Offices under their jurisdiction and submit the same to the Government.
3. If the concerned engineers have any objections regarding the said seniority scale, they should submit their objections/reports to the government along with appropriate evidence and documents through the concerned Chief Engineer/Superintendent Engineer within 15 days from the publication of the seniority scale. The representations received thereafter will not be considered and the said seniority scale will be finalized taking into account the representations received.
4. If there are any factual errors in the said seniority list (mistakes in name, surname, date of birth, date of appointment, route of appointment, educational qualification, comments etc.) or if the name of an engineer is not included in the seniority list or if the relevant column next to the engineer&#39;s name is left blank due to lack of information, the said The information should be brought to the notice of the Government along with necessary evidence, accordingly appropriate corrections will be made while finalizing the seniority.
