Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of Solapur Hotgi Auj Tilati station Fata Ingalgi to Jeur road in Dakshin Solapur and Akkalkot talukas of Solapur district as a state highway.
Considering the traffic volume on the said road and the number of villages, population as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune, the following roads in Solapur district are being upgraded as State Highways.
