Circular
As per Government Decision dated 02.07.2002 at Wacha General Administration Department, the posts have been classified according to pay scale. Accordingly, the post of High Grade Stenographer falls under Group-B category.
Also in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2009 the post of High Grade Stenographer has been designated as High Grade Stenographer Group-B (Non-Gazetted). Accordingly, the post of High Class Stenographers working in Regional Offices (Chief Engineer / Superintending Engineer / Executive Engineer etc.) under Public Works Department should be called as High Class Stenographer Group-B (Non-Gazetted).
2. The said circular is being issued by the Common Administration Department vide informal reference No.642/Ka.12 dated 14.6.2018.
