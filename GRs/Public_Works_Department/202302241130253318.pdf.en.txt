Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads in Deola Taluka of Nashik District as roads, other district roads, vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said roads as other district roads.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, rural roads in Devla Taluka of Nashik district are being upgraded as other district roads.
