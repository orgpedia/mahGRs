Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of rural roads in Satana Taluka of Nashik District as Major District Roads vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following roads in Nashik district are being upgraded.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road                                               | Common Road No | Chain no        | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion        |
| 1.    | From R.M.M.752 G to Vdarkadhish Sugar Factory to Shevre R.M.7. road | Gramin Marg-36 | (0/00 to 11/00) | 11.00 km.                        | Major District Road-167    |
| 2.    | Road from R.M.20 to Antapur Dasvel to Pimpalkothe to R.M.752.       | Rural Road- 83 | (0/00 to 12/00) | 12.00 km.                        | Major District Road-1 -168 |
| Total | 23.00 km.                                                           |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result, the total length of major district road grade roads in Nashik district in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 23.00 km. By increasing the total length (4160.16 +23.00) i.e. 4183.16 km. Also, the total length of rural roads is 23.00 km. By reducing the total length (10603.15-23.00) i.e. 10580.15 km. That will be it.
