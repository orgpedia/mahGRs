Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan vide the letter under reference has requested the Public Works Department of Vasai Virar City Municipal Area to transfer the Prajima Roads for maintenance and repairs to the Municipal Corporation. Also Vasai Virar City Municipal Corporation, Vasai has approved the transfer of the above roads to Vasai Virar City Municipal Corporation, Vasai. Accordingly, the said roads were under the consideration of the Government to be transferred to Vasai Virar City Municipal Corporation, Vasai.
