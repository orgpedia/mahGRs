Preamble: - The Sub-Divisional Office of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Superintendent Engineer under Konkan, Executive Engineer under Public Works Board, Sindhudurg, Deputy Engineer under Public Works Department, Kankavali, Sub-Divisional Office of Public Works Department, Malwan is presently functional.
The Public Works Department, Sawantwadi covers 05 Taluks namely Kudal, Sawantwadi, Dodamarg, Vengurla B Malvan. With a view to balance the development works in the geographical area, the government was contemplating to transfer the office of Public Works Sub-Division, Malwan under the Divisional Office of Public Works Department, Sawantwadi.
Government Decisions: - Department of Public Works, Government Decisions, Govt. The office of Public Works Sub-Division, Malvan was brought under the Public Works Department, Kankavli with effect from 25 January 2023. The said Government Decision is being superseded by this Government Decision and the Sub-Division Public Works Sub-Division, Malwan is being approved to be brought under the control of Public Works Department, Sawantwadi subject to the condition that there shall be no additional financial burden on the Government including sanctioned posts in the Sub-Division as before.
