Government Circular:
Public Works Department, Government Circular No. Provisional dated 01.01.2018 for the period from 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2017 of the cadre of Sub Divisional Engineer (Civil) under Seniority-2020/P.No.28/Aastha-1, dated 08.06.2021 The seniority list has been released. It has also been amended as per the Government Corrigendum in the above reference no.1 and 2.
In the provisional seniority list published on 08.06.2021, the date of ad hoc promotion of Sub Divisional Engineers promoted on ad hoc basis between 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2017 was fixed as the date of seniority.
However, since the said ad hoc promotions have been regularized with the approval of the General Administration Department, the seniority of the Engineers who were given ad hoc promotion needs to be changed in the relevant date, the seniority list dated 08.06.2021 and the reference correction sheets are being updated as per this circular. And the revised temporary seniority list of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Construction) cadre in Public Works Department for the period from 01.01.2014 to 31.07.2017 is being published under this circular.
2. The date on which the ad-hoc promotion given in the Sub Divisional Engineer cadre will become regular is being fixed as the date of their seniority. Hon. High Court, Bombay vide judgment dated 04.08.2017 in Petition No. 2797/2015 invalidated reservation in promotion. Against the decision of the Hon&#39;ble High Court, the State Government has filed Special Permission Petition No. 28306/2017 in the Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court. The said petition is still pending and subject to the final decision of the said petition, the provisional seniority list for the period dated 01.01.2014 to 31.07.2017 of the cadre of Sub-Divisional Engineer (Civil) is being published as per the prospectus (&quot;Form A&#39;) attached with this circular. According to the judgments in the above court cases, if there is any change in the selection lists after the year 2004, the seniority list will be changed accordingly.
