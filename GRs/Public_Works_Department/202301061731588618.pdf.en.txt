Foreword : The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Parner Taluka of Ahmadganar District as Rural Roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the road development plan.
The use of the said road as well as the General Assembly, Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar
Considering their resolution, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar, the following roads in Parner Taluka of Ahmednagar District are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-2021.
