According to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005, Shri. Rahul U. Vasaikar, Executive Engineer, as per the reference order, Public Works Special Projects Division, Chembur is now partially modified and his revised posting for administrative reasons and in the interest of public service and with the approval of the competent authority to the vacant post of Executive Engineer, Building Construction Department, Mulund, Mumbai. is being done.
2. Mr. Vasaikar should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are being made aware that they should not be absent unauthorized, otherwise their absence will be considered as unauthorized absence and will be unpaid and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out.
Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule 30(c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
