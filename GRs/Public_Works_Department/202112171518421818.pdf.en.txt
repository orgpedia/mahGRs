2. The terms and conditions of the order under reference shall remain in force in respect of the officers being posted under this order.
3. The officers concerned should assume charge of their new post promptly and submit their assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned officers m. no Sec. (Period of Joining, Non-Accepted Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 shall be required to join the posted post within the given joining period.
Otherwise the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
