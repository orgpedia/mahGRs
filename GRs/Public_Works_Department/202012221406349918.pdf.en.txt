Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Hadgaon and Himayatnagar talukas of Nanded district as major district roads under reference letter.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Nanded has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Number of villages on said roads, population, road usage and district
Taking into account the resolution of the Parishad, Nanded, the said roads were under the consideration of the Government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the following roads in Nanded district are being upgraded.
