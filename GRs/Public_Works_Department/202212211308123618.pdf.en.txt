Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Karanja Taluka of Wardha District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also
Zilla Parishad, Wardha has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said road as well as the resolution of the General Assembly, Zilla Parishad, Wardha, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the following road in Karanja Taluka of Wardha District is being upgraded as rural road.
