The above Coordinating Committee has been decided as follows: (1) To fill up the vacant posts in the quota of Group-C category nominations in all the offices of the State under the Public Works Department, General Administration Department, Government Decision dated 21.11.2022 to enter into MoU with the company selected and other ancillary matters. coordinate.
(2) Constituency wise and post wise options should be invited from the examinee. After the completion of the examination process, the selected candidates should be allotted under the circles/mandals as per the option given by them according to the merit.
(3) The said committee is being formed to coordinate for the combined recruitment. Therefore, the main responsibility of the recruitment is reference no. The state level and regional selection committees established between 05 to 09 will remain.
(4) If any complaints or court cases related to the recruitment process arise after the recruitment process has been carried out by the Coordination Committee, such complaints and court cases should be dealt with by the concerned Selection Committee.
(5) It is necessary to carry out the recruitment process as per the instructions given/issued by the General Administration Department, Finance Department and Public Works Department from time to time. Care should be taken to fill up the posts by taking into account the procedure prescribed by the government, service entry rules, all types of reservation, point list and rules and orders while recruiting.
