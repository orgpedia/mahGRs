Introduction: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade two other district roads in Chandrapur district as major district roads.
The said road will be connected to Itan village in Bhandara district by constructing a big high bridge and embankment on the Wainganga river. Therefore, considering the importance of the said road, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, the following road in Chandrapur district is being upgraded.
