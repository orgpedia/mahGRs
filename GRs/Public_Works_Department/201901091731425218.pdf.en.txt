Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding upgrading the road from Mhasadi-Kalgaon to Rahudbari to district border in Sakri taluka of Dhule district as a major district road. Also, Zilla Parishad, Dhule has demanded to upgrade the said road.
A famous pilgrimage site is the temple of Dhanai Devi at Mhasadi on the Sadar road. This road is important for the transportation of agricultural goods in this area and the two districts of Dhule and Nashik are connected by this road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the following roads in Sakri Taluka of Dhule District are being upgraded.
