Government circular
General Administration Department vide Government Decision No. SRV-1076/12 dated 21.06.1982 Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 as per the provisions laid down in the Public Works Department as “Assistant Engineer Grade-II (Electrical) Group-B ( Gazetted)” Cadre dated 01.01.2021 Provisional Seniority List is being published in Annexure-A herewith.
2. The Chief Engineer (Electrical) is requested to distribute the said seniority list to all Circles / Boards / Divisions / Sub-Divisional Offices under his authority and submit the same to the Government.
3. If the concerned engineers have any objections regarding the said seniority scale, they should submit their objections/reports to the government through the concerned Chief Engineer / Superintending Engineer within 15 days from the publication of this seniority scale along with appropriate evidence and documents. The representations received thereafter will not be considered and the said seniority scale will be finalized taking into account the representations received.
