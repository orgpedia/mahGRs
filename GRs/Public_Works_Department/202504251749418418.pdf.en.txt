As per the order dated 03.08.2024, promotion from the post of Construction Engineering Assistant to the post of Junior Engineer (Construction) with 5% quota was granted. However, it has come to the notice of the government that he is not eligible for promotion to the rank of Junior Engineer (Architecture) as he obtained this promotion on the basis of bogus certificates.
Therefore, Mr. Dilip Babasaheb Gade was referred to the D. By Government Order dated 03.05.2013 and Sarvashri.
Dattatraya Anantrao Kulkarni, Rajendra Murlidhar Rokade, Trimbak Laxman Raut, Devanand Prahlad Patil and Prabhakar Ramrao Darade. The promotion given to the post of Junior Engineer (Construction) under the Government Order dated 03.09.2015 is being cancelled.
Among the above engineers, Mr. Rajendra Murlidhar Rokade and Mr. Prabhakar Ramrao Darade, who are presently working, are being posted in the office of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik in the post of Assistant in Construction Engineering. The office of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, shall make detailed posting of these engineers and apprise the Government of the same.
