Preface: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Shrigonda Taluka of Ahmednagar District as major district roads. It has been demanded.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the demand of Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar, the following other district roads and rural roads in Srigonda Taluka of Ahmednagar district are being upgraded as major district roads.
