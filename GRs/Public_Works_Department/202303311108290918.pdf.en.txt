Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also
Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the use of the said road and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara,
The said roads were under the consideration of the government to include them as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Satara, the following unplanned roads in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara District are being included as rural roads for the year 2001-2021.
