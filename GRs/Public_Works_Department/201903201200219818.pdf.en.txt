Under reference 2 Government Decision S.P.V.No. SRV - 1094/P.No.4/94/Bara, dated 7.3.1996 in paragraph 2 (b) &quot;cadre means posts in a service or part of a service sanctioned as a separate entity, permanent as well as temporary posts sanctioned from time to time.&quot; will be considered as a group&quot;. Rule-4 (1) of the Service Entry Rules dated 7.4.1983 for the post of Executive Engineer (Civil) has detailed the provision of announcing the cadre strength of the post of Executive Engineer (Civil) every year. There is no such provision in the Service Entry Rules for the post of Deputy Engineer (Construction). However, from the point of view of maintaining administrative order, it has been found necessary to determine and announce the cadre strength of the said cadre (permanent sanctioned posts + temporary posts on deputation as per the figure).
Similarly, due to the increasing demand from various departments for the posts in this cadre, Deputy Engineer (Construction) Cadre Dt. The issue of determining and announcing the number of cadres on 01.04.2018 was under the consideration of the government.

In accordance with determining the cadre strength of Deputy Engineer cadre, the cadre strength of Deputy Engineer cadre has been ascertained by analyzing the detailed information regarding regular and seasonal posts from the Public Works Department and Zilla Parishad received from the field offices.
Accordingly, regarding the regular posts under the Public Works Department, Village Development Department and the Zilla Parishad as well as the temporary posts to be filled by deputation under the various boards, authorities and other administrative departments of the Government under the Public Works Department, the cadre of Deputy Engineer (Civil) in Maharashtra Engineering Service Group-B dated 01.04. Cadre number on 2018 is being determined and announced as per the attached prospectus.
2. 986 posts of Deputy Engineer (Civil) are sanctioned as per approved scheme of Public Works Department. Also, apart from the above posts of the department, a total of 291 posts of the Zilla Parishad under the Rural Development and Water Conservation Department have been approved, and these posts are filled through this department. Considering the said posts, total 1277 permanent posts are sanctioned in the cadre of Deputy Engineer (Civil). Apart from this, there are 193 posts of Deputy Engineers on deputation.
Therefore, the cadre strength of the said cadre as on 01.04.2018 is 1470.
It will have 1277 posts as per approved scheme and 193 seasonal posts on deputation. Apart from this, there will be a division of 1470 posts for Assistant Executive Engineer Cadre, 387 for Assistant Engineer Class-1 Cadre, 472 for Sub Divisional Engineer Cadre and 471 for Sub Divisional Officer Cadre.
3. Permanent sanctioned posts as per the scheme of Deputy Engineer (Construction) are shown in “Descriptive A” attached to the said Government decision, regular posts of Deputy Engineers and temporary posts on deputation are shown in “Descriptive B”.
