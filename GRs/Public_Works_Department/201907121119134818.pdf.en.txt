Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of 10 rural roads in Malegaon taluka of Nashik district as other district roads under the reference letter. Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as other district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, the following rural roads in Nashik district are being upgraded.
