Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of the road from Anandavalli-Chandesi Mungsara Dari to Taluka Boundary Road in Nashik Taluka of Nashik District to Other District Road-158 as a major district road. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said road.
The said road is an important road connecting Nashik city and the two talukas of Nashik and Dindori. The said road is useful for transporting agricultural produce to markets in large areas of Nashik taluka. Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said road and the number of villages, population, road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following road in Nashik district is being upgraded.
