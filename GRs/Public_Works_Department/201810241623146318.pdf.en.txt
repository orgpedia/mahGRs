Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of Japatalai - Workeksa - Toyagondi - Modebhatti - Mohli - Bhakarondi - Muska - Khambada - Sursundi road as a major district road in Dhanora taluka of Gadchiroli district under reference letter.
Due to this road, Gadchiroli and Chandrapur are the closest route to the district headquarters. The road is useful for Chandrapur Industrial Area, Tadoba Tiger Reserve, educational areas and pilgrimage sites as well as anti-Naxalite campaign. Therefore, considering the importance of the said road, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision : Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, the following road in Gadchiroli district is being upgraded.
