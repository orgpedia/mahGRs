Government Order:
Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and in the performance of Government duties
As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Prevention of Delays Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority, Shri. Pradeep A. Dalvi, Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, No.2, Thane is being transferred to the vacant post of Executive Engineer, Public Works (Tribal) Department, Thane in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience.
2. Mr. Pradeep A. Dalvi should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are being made aware that they should not be absent unauthorized, otherwise their absence will be considered as unauthorized absence and will be unpaid and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser7.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge respectively by the deposed and deputized officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30(c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
4. Mr. As Dalvi is being transferred for administrative reasons, he will be paid his joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
5. Mr. Dalvi to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
