Introduction:
The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference to include the unplanned road from Gavandgaon to Borgaon in Degalur Taluka of Nanded District as a rural route in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also there is a demand from Zilla Parishad, Nanded regarding the said road.
Said 3.00 km. No land acquisition is required for long roads. Considering the importance of the said road, it was under the consideration of the government to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, Gavandgaon to Borgaon road in Degalur Taluka of Nanded District is being constructed in km. 0/00 to 3/00 Total length 3.00 km. This road is being included in Road Development Plan 2001-21 as Rural Route No.98.
As a result the total length of rural road quality roads in Nanded district under Road Development Plan 2001-21 is 3.00 km. Total length increased by (5156.81 + 3.00) i.e. 5159.81 km. That will be it.
