Madam Kama Marg, Martyrdom Rajguru Chowk,

Date: 10th August, 2020
Mr. Mithtewad, Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Parbhani has been transferred in the interest of public service and administrative convenience.
Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad (ZP) B) Department, Parbhani is being done in this post.
2.The other officer in the post of Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Parbhani will be posted separately.
Mr. Mithtewad should take charge of his new post immediately and submit his report to the Government without delay. They are being made aware that they should not be absent unofficially otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorised absence and will be made without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is being drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of the said Rules, they will be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any application for change in the post.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
As Mr. Mithtewad is being transferred on administrative grounds, he will continue to be paid the period of assumption of charge, travelling allowance and daily allowance as admissible to him as per rules.
The date of assumption of charge of the new post by Mr. Mithtewad should be intimated to the Government as well as to the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, opposite Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune.
