Refer :- 1) Letter of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune Ja.No.Ka-2/Resha/110/7054/Year 2022, dated 12/12/2022
Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Koregaon Taluka of Satara District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also District
Parishad, Satara has requested to include the said road as a rural route in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following road in Koregaon Taluka of Satara District is being included in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21 as a rural road.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A. No.          | Name of proposed road    | Chain no      | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| Taluka - Satara |
| 1.              | Dahigaon Anpatwadi Road. | 0/00 to 3/500 | 3.500 km.                        | Rural Road No. 290  |
| Total           | 3.500 km.                |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Satara district in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 3,500 km. By increasing the total length (6783.330 + 3.500) i.e. 6786.830 km. I. That will be it.
