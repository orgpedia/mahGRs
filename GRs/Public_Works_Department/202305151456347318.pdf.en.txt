Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Balapur taluka of Akola district as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also, Zilla Parishad, Akola has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Akela, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Akala, the following unplanned roads in Balapur Taluka of Akala District are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
