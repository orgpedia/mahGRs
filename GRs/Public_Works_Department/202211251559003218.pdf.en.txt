Introduction:
Referenced by Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nashik
A proposal has been submitted to the government regarding inclusion of unplanned roads in Pachora taluka of Jalgaon district as rural roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also
Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon has requested to include the said road as a rural route under Road Development Scheme 2001-21.
Considering the use of the said roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon
However, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to include them as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon, the following roads in Pachora Taluka of Jalgaon District are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
