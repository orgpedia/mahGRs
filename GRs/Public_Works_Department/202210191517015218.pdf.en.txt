Refer to :- 1) Letter of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune Ja.No.Ka-2/Resha/70/5249/year 2022, dated 26/08/2022.
Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade the rural road road in Karad taluka of Satara district as a major district road vide letter under reference. Also Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the number of villages on the said road, population, usage of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Karad Taluka of Satara District are being upgraded.
