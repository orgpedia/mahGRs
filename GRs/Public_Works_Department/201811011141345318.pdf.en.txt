Circular:
The Government has issued detailed instructions regarding acceptance and approval of tenders as per the Government Decision dated 27/09/2018. Also in paragraph no.6 of the said government decision, it is clearly mentioned that, &quot;Through this government decision, the Government of Maharashtra, Public Works Department Rulebook Sixth Edition 1984 and the various government decisions / circulars issued earlier regarding tenders are being ratified.&quot; While this is the case, taking the decision of tender acceptance at the competent level is being avoided based on the provisions of superseded circulars of 1997 or 2012. The following instructions are being issued to remove the confusion created among the Zonal Officers and to bring about uniformity and consistency in the work and decision-making process of all Chief Engineers / Superintending Engineers / Executive Engineers:
Order: 1) Opening of tender envelope no.1 and opening of tender envelope no.2 should be only three days apart. After opening the tender envelope no.2, the tender acceptance proposal should be submitted within three days at the regional level and within five days at the state level. Competent authorities should decide the updated price as per the prevailing price list on the date on which the tender is to be accepted and the tender should be accepted at the competent level.
2) In case of delay between tender envelope no.1, tender envelope no.2 and acceptance of tender, detailed information regarding the justifiable reasons such as court cases, grievance cases etc. should be kept on record.
