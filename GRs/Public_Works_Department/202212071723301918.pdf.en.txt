Introduction:
General Administration Department has issued consolidated guidelines for direct service recruitment to posts in the quota of nominations in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D cadre under Ex-Secondary Services Selection Board (outside Maharashtra Public Service Commission) vide Government Decision dated 04.5.2022. Accordingly, the composition of regional and state level selection committees for the recruitment of the said posts has been mentioned in the said government decision.
Accordingly, the administrative department of the ministry has to establish regional and state level selection committees. In this regard, as per the structure given in the Government Decision dated 04.5.2022 of the General Administration Department, it is necessary to establish selection committees for each department.
Accordingly, the matter of setting up a state level and regional selection committee to fill up the posts in the nomination quota of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadre in the office of Chief Architect, Maharashtra State, Mumbai was under the consideration of the Government.

Consolidated guidelines for implementing the recruitment process have been issued by the General Administration Department vide Government Decisions dated 04.5.2022 and 21.11.2022. Accordingly, it is necessary to carry out the recruitment process as per the instructions given / given by the General Administration Department, Finance Department and Public Works Department from time to time. Care should be taken to fill the posts by taking into consideration the procedure prescribed by the government, service entry rules, all types of reservation, point list and rules and orders while recruiting. It will be mandatory to take prior approval of the Government from time to time as given in the guidelines.
