Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune vide letter under reference, in Satara-Kas-Bamanoli-Gogve-Tapola Mahabaleshwar Road (Major District Route-26) in Satara Taluka of Satara District, Major District Route-26 to Kasani Phata to Ghatai Devi Road to Major District Road - 26 Road (Other District Road - 59) is 8.00 km.
A proposal has been submitted to the government to upgrade and include the length of the road. Also Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade the said road.
The work of increasing the height of Kas dam in Satara taluka is currently in progress. After increasing the height of the said dam, the length of the main district road-26 from 19/400 to 24/300 will be blocked. Also the continuity of the road will be broken. An alternate route must be made for the length of this obstacle. Considering the above, the distance between Major District Road-26 to Kasani Phata to Ghatai Devi Road to Major District Road-26 (Other District Road-59) is 8.00 km. The length of the road was under the consideration of the government to upgrade it and include it.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Satara, major district in Satara-Kas-Bamanoli-Gogve-Tapola-Mahabaleshwar road (Major District Route-26) in Satara Taluka of Satara District. Route-26 to Kasani Phata to Ghatai Devi Road to Major District Route-26 Road (Other District Route-59) is 8.00 km. Length of roads are being improved and included.
Consequently, the major district route-26 has been renamed as Satara - Yevateswar - Petri - Ghatai Devi Fata - Ghatai Devi Mandir - Kas - Bamanoli - Gogve - Tapola - Mahabaleshwar Road (Major District Route-26) (108.850) km. It will happen.
