Government Order:
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. V. L. Bhirud, Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Jalna has been transferred as Executive Engineer, Maharashtra State Rural Road Development Institute, Hingoli in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience. The post of deputation is being made vacant due to the transfer of Shelgaonkar.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Jalna and Shri. The transfer orders of Shelgoonkar will be issued separately.
3. Mr. Bhirud should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, Mr. Bhirud&#39;s attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30(c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Bhirud is being transferred for administrative reasons, he will continue to be paid his joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance as admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Bhirud has been posted on the post of deputation and the said posting is for the duration of the Maharashtra Civil Service (tenure of appointment, post-commissioned service and suspension, dismissal and removal from service).
