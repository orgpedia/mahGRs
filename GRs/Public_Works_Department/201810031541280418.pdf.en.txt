Introduction:
A proposal to include the unclassified road from Kapustalni (Ratnapur) to Vadner Bhujang (length 4.00 km) in Anjangaon Taluka of Amravati District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati to Govt. has been received.
The said unclassified road is 12.00 m. There is a wide unpaved road and no land acquisition is required for this. The said road is a road connecting two taluks of Anjangaon and Achalpur as well as Vadner Bhujang, a great pilgrimage site. Therefore, the above road was under the consideration of the Government to include it as a rural road in the Road Development Scheme 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the road from Kapustalni (Ratnapur) to Vadner Bhujang (length 4.00 km) in Anjangaon Taluka of Amravati District as rural route no. 129 Therefore the Road Development Plan 2001-21 is being included subject to the condition that “funds shall not be made available for road land acquisition”.
As a result, 4.00 km in length of rural road quality roads in Amravati district under Road Development Plan 2001-21. The total length increased by (6434.140 + 4.00) is 6438. 140 km I. That will be it.
