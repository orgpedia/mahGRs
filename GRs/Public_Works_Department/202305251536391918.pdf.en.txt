In accordance with the provisions of Sections 4(4) and 4(5) of the Maharashtra Government Servants Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority working under the Public Works Department as mentioned in the attached Annexure-A Posting of Engineer (Construction) by transfer as per his request column no. 4 is being done here at the place indicated before their names.
2. The Controlling Officers concerned are hereby informed that they may immediately relieve the Deputy Engineer mentioned in the annexure to take charge at the place of new appointment by making necessary local alternate arrangements and direct him to join immediately at the place of new appointment. However, the officers who have been deputed to the post falling vacant by retirement should be relieved by making necessary local alternate arrangements so that the officers concerned can take charge at the place of new appointment after the retirement of the concerned officer.
3. The Deputy Engineer (St.) mentioned in the attached Annexure-A should assume charge of the new post promptly and submit his assumption of charge report to the Government without delay. Engineers mentioned in the attached Annexure-A should ensure that the transferred Engineers join at the place of transfer within the period of appointment given in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Service and Allowances during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 If the replacement post is not taken up within the period, the period of absence will be counted as dies non. Also their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be eligible for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for transfer in posting.
4. As the officers mentioned in Annexure-A are transferred on their request, they will not be paid joining period, traveling allowance and daily allowance.
