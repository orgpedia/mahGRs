Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade other district roads in Khed taluka of Ratnagiri district as major district roads under reference letter.
Considering the number of villages on the said road, population, road usage, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road as an alternative route to the Parashuram Ghat road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan, the following road in Khed Taluka of Ratnagiri District is being upgraded as a major district road as a special matter.
