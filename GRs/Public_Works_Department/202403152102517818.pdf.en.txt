(1) Actual work should not be started unless there is an adequate provision in the budget.
(2) Work should be carried out in accordance with the prevailing guidelines as the case may be.
(3) The competent authority shall provide for the general insurance required for the construction period, maintenance repair and liability period.
(4) The expenditure shall be subject to the limits of the funds sanctioned for the work.
