Government Circular- The Central Government has launched an online portal for training government officers/ employees under the name of" Karmayogi Bharat." Many useful trainings are available free of cost on this training portal. All the officers/ employees of the state are required to undergo various types of useful training using this portal. Accordingly, it is being made mandatory for all officers and employees of the Public Works Department to undergo various trainings on the" Karmayogi Bharat" portal.
The training for all officers/ staff (including field offices) of the Public Works Department will commence from 11.00 am on Friday, 7th July, 2023. All officers and employees. The training on" Time Management" should be conducted on 07th July, 2023.
However, if it is not possible at 11.00 a.m. due to workload. Training must be done at any time of the day on July 7, 2023 only.
After this training, the process of conducting other training on the portal in the next period should be done regularly. The procedure for conducting training on" Karmayogi Bharat" portal is prescribed in Annexure-A attached.
