Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade 17 other district roads and 18 rural roads in Umred, Bhiwapur and Kuhi talukas of Nagpur district as major district roads. If the said roads are upgraded, it will be convenient for the people of the local villages to go to the taluka and district headquarters, so the government is considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, the following roads in Umred, Bhiwapur and Kuhi talukas of Nagpur district are being upgraded.
