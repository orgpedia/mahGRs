Circular
The provisional seniority of Superintendents working in Regional Offices (Chief Engineer/ Superintending Engineer) under Public Works Department was notified vide Circular dated 12.06.2023. All concerned were informed about the objections/ errors, if any. In view of the representations/ objections received from the field offices in this regard, the seniority of the post of" Superintendent" has been finalized by amending the provisional seniority notice issued vide circular dated 12th June, 2023. The Annexure' A' to this seniority is annexed herewith.
The said seniority has been prepared in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, General Administration Department, Government Notification, No. SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/ Function-12, dated 21.06.2021.
