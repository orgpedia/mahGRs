Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the inclusion of a total of 14 unplanned roads in Kamthi Taluka of Nagpur district in the adjacent rural route and 2 roads under the Road Development Plan 2001-21 through the letter under reference. Zilla Parishad, Nagpur has also demanded in this regard.
Taking into consideration the said proposal and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, it was under the consideration of the government to include 14 unplanned roads in Kamthi Taluka of Nagpur district in the adjacent rural roads and 2 roads as new rural roads in the rural roads under Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into account the information mentioned in the proposal submitted by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Nagpur, the following roads in Kamthi Taluka of Nagpur District are being included in the Road Development Plan 2001 - 21 Rural Road.
