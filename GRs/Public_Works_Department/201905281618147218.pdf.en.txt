Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Kokan vide letters under reference regarding connecting Mauje Nidwe Gawaliwadi Dhangarwadi Gurwawadi Road (Rural Marg-40) and Sakharpa Survewadi Jadhavwadi Road (Rural Marg-407) in Sangameshwar Taluka of Ratnagiri District and upgrading these roads as major district roads. The proposal has been submitted to Govt. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
These roads are important roads for the people of Ratnagiri and adjoining districts to reach Marleshwar, a pilgrimage and tourist destination. Taking into account the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, use of the road as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following roads in Sangameshwar Taluka of Ratnagiri District are being upgraded.
