Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of the road from Nandale Prajima 25 in Dhule Taluka of Dhule District to Borkund Mandal Dhadra Dhadri Purmepada Bendrepada to RMMA 3 as Prajima in the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Dhule has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, the population, the road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, from Borkund Mandal Dhadara Dhadari Purmepada Bendrepada to R.M.M.3 Road Ijima No. 30 from Nandale Prajima 25 in Dhule Taluka of Dhule District, Chain No. 0/00 to 23/300, total length 23.300 km. And, this road is being upgraded as Major District Road No.-49.
Consequently, the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 has increased the total length of major district road grade roads in Dhule district to 23,300 km. By increasing the total length (829.09 +23.300) i.e. 852.39 km. As well as other district roads, the total length of roads is 23.300 km. By reducing the total length (1340.22 - 23.300) i.e. 1316.92 km.
That will be it.
Also, no works or budgetary works will be proposed under any other scheme and no new land acquisition will be done for the said road in the length of which the work has been completed or proposed under the Pradhan Mantri Gramsadak / Chief Minister Gramsadak Scheme and other schemes or there is remaining liability period for the work done. Approval is being given to upgrade roads subject to these conditions.
