Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of major district roads and other district roads in Karjat, Jamkhed and Ahmednagar talukas of Ahmadnagar district as state highways.
Taking into consideration the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, the following roads in Ahmednagar district are being included as State Highways.
