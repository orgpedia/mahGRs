Introduction:
Road (1.600 km) from Mauje Sindgi in Kalamanuri Taluka of Hingoli District to Wapati Vasmat Taluka border (1.600 km) and road (3.800 km) from Mauje Wapati in Vasmat Taluka to Sindagi Kalmanuri Taluka border in Hingoli district as per reference letter from Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad. m.) Government has received a proposal to include these two roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
These roads will connect important markets such as Bolda (Bazar), Waranga (Fata) Kalmanuri, (Akhda) Balapur, Vasmat in Hingoli district. Also, there are important roads in terms of sugarcane transportation and communication.
There is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli to include the said roads in Road Development Scheme 2001-21. Considering the importance of the said road, it was under the consideration of the government to include these roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli, in Hingoli district,
1) From Mauje Sindagi in Kalmanuri Taluka to Wapati - Vasmat Taluka Boundary Road Chain No. 0/00 to 1/60 (1.600 km) this road is Rural Marg No.104
