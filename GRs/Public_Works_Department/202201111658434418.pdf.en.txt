Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road from Chore Pul to Shelke Panand Sakhrwadi in Karad Taluka of Satara District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said road and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
Government decision
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following road in Karad Taluka of Satara District is being upgraded as a rural road.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road                           | Chain no     | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Chore Bridge to Sheleke Panand Sakharwadi road. | 0/0 to 2/400 | 2.400 km.                        | Rural Marg-330      |
| Total | 2.400 km.                                       |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Satara district in Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 2.400 km. By increasing the total length (6737.530 + 2.400) i.e. 6739.930 km. I. That will be it.
