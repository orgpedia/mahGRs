Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade other district roads in Guhagar taluka of Ratnagiri district as major district roads under the reference letter. The district council, Ratnagiri, has also demanded that the road be upgraded.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, use of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Based on the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following other district roads in Guhagar Taluka of Ratnagiri district are being upgraded as Major District Roads.
