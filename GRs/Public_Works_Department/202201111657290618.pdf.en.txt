Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of an unplanned road in Aundha Nagnath Taluka of Hingoli District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Hingoli has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said roads, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded, the following road in Aundha Nagnath Taluka of Hingoli District is being upgraded as rural road.
