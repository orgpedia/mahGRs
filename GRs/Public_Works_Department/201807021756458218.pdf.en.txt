Government Order:
According to the order under reference, Shri. Uttamkumar Chandrakant Mule, Executive Engineer, was transferred to the vacant post of Executive Engineer, Maharashtra State Rural Road Development Institute, Ratnagiri.
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. The posting of Uttam Kumar Chandrakant Mule, Executive Engineer, Maharashtra State Rural Road Development Institute, Ratnagiri is now being partially changed and his revised posting is being made to the vacant post of Administrative Facilities, Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Chiplun.

3. Mr. Mule should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, Mr. Mule&#39;s attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. 4.
