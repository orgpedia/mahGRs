Government Order:
Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and in the performance of Government duties
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Prevention of Delays Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority, Shri. Mr. m. Mangulkar, Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad (S.B.) Division, Aurangabad is being transferred to the vacant post of Assistant Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Aurangabad as per his request and in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience.
2. Mr. Mr. m. Mangulkar should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and they will be without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out.
Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
4. Mr. As Mangulkar is being transferred as per his request, he will not be paid tenure, travel allowance and daily allowance.
5. Mr. Mangulkar to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Opposite Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assuming charge of the newly posted post.
