Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the other district roads and rural roads in Dhule Taluka of Dhule District as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Dhule has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, the population, the road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the following roads in Dhule Taluka of Dhule District are being upgraded as major district roads.
