Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of Pirle - Natepute - Phadtari (E.G.M.-76) road in Malshiras Taluka of Solapur District as a major district road through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Solapur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the following roads in Solapur district are being upgraded.
