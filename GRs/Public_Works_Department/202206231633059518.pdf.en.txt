Government order
Accordingly, Mr. Dinesh Anandrao Patil, Deputy Divisional Engineer, Construction Sub-Division, Ammalner, Dist. Jalgaon, has been charged against him at Ammalner Police Station under Section 7 and 12 of Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988, Case No. 287/2021. The reference was suspended vide Government Order dated 30.8.2021 in pursuance of the offense filed on 6.7.2021.
2. As per the provisions of General Administration Department, Government Decision, 14.10.2011 and dated 9.7.2019, the Government has decided to terminate the suspension of Mr. Dinesh Anandrao Patil, Deputy Divisional Engineer and reinstate him in Government service.
3. Accordingly, Mr. Dinesh Anandrao Patil, Deputy Divisional Engineer, taking into account the provisions of General Administration Department, Government Circular, dated 20.4.2013, he is being reinstated as per this Government Order and his posting as Deputy Executive Engineer, Public Works Department No. 1, Gadchiroli is being filled on this vacant post.
4. The Controlling Officers concerned are hereby informed to ask the above Deputy Engineer to join immediately at the new place of posting.
5. Mr. Patil, Deputy Engineer should take charge of the new post promptly, he should not be absent without permission otherwise his absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay, also possibility of termination of his service cannot be ruled out. The concerned are being made aware of this. Also their attention is drawn to Government Circular, General Administration Department No.CDR-1082/2567/28/Akra, dated 30.8.1982. According to the said circular
It should also be noted that in view of the instructions given, they will be eligible for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for cancellation of transfer.
6. The Controlling Officers concerned are informed that they should immediately inform the Government of the date of taking charge of Shri.Patil, Deputy Engineer.
