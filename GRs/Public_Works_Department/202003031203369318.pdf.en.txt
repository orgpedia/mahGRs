The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads and other district roads in Hatkanangle taluka of Kolhapur district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, use of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following roads in Kolhapur district are being upgraded.
