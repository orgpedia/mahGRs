The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of District Roads and Rural Roads in Bhokar Taluka of Nanded District as Major District Roads through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nanded has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
