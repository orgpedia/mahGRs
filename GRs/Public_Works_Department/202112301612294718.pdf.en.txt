The concerned officers should assume charge of the new post with minimum tenure and submit their assumption report to the government without delay. If the said officer does not join the post on promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of this order, his period of absence shall be treated as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
6. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that the date of assumption of charge by the said officer should be immediately reported to the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in. If they do not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) of his leave application should not be accepted / granted.
7. The officer concerned should give the option of pay fixation within one month of joining the promoted post. If such option is not given within the prescribed period, their pay fixation will be as per Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981 under Rule No. 11 (1) (a) should take into account the date of promotion.
8. The officers in the above table have been appointed / posted to the post of deputation.
The said appointment is made subject to the terms and conditions of deputation prescribed in Appendix II to the Maharashtra Civil Services (Entry Period, Post-Commission Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981.
9. The officer concerned should inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Opp. Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly appointed post.
