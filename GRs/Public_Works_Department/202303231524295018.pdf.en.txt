Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the upgrading of other district roads in Walwa Taluka of Sangli District as Major District Roads vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Sangli has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Sangli, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Sangli, the following other district roads in Walwa Taluka of Sangli district are being upgraded as major district roads.
