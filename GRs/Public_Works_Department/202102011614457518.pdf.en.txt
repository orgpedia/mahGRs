Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Niphad taluka of Nashik district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik as well as the sugar factory near Ranwad, the following roads in Nashik district are being upgraded as roads.
