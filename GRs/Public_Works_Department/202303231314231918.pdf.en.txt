The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Amravati taluka of Amravati district as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide the letter under reference.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Amravati has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the road development plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said road and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Amravati, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Amravati in Amravati district considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Amravati
