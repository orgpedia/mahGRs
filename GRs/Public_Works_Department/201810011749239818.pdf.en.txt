Introduction:
As per Government decision dated 11.08.2003 of Public Works Department, the estimated cost for improvement of transport system in Nandurbar city is Rs. Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation has been announced as the &quot;Entrepreneur&quot; to execute the road and bridge works costing 16.43 crores on &quot;build and transfer&quot; basis. The concession period of the said project is 24 years. As per the Government decision dated 05.2009 under the Nandurbar City Integrated Road Development Scheme, the right to collect the amount of road tax at road toll booths for the period from 19.05.2009 to 31.12.2016 has been granted to the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation (MARYA), Mumbai. The road tax was collected on the project from 06.10.2010 to 10.11.2012 and after that the collection of road tax has been temporarily stopped due to strong opposition from the locals. The actual expenditure on the project is Rs.27.35 crores. The matter of payment of the expenses incurred by the corporation under the scheme and the improvement thereof as well as the amount with interest on the expenses incurred by the corporation is under the consideration of the government.
