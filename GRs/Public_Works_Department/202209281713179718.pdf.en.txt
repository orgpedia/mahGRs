Preamble: Maharashtra Government Gazette Extraordinary Part-4, Maharashtra Act No. 45 of the year 2022 regarding creation of surplus posts in the cadre of civil services and posts of the Government, Government Departments and their secondary offices or authorities and appointing candidates selected and recommended by the Selection Authorities. Provision has been made vide September 08, 2022 and General Administration Department, Government Decision, September 21, 2022. Accordingly, the issue of creating surplus posts for appointing qualified candidates in the regional offices under the Public Works Department was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision:
This Government Decision approves the creation of 22 additional posts for appointing the candidates in the attached prospectus in the Regional Office under Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai / Amravati / Nagpur. Regarding selection and appointment of recommended candidates taking into account the said surplus posts, General Administration Department, Government Decision, dated September 21, 2022, paragraph no. Taking into consideration the instructions from 02 to 06, the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai/ Amravati/ Nagpur should issue appointment orders separately by cadre specifying the fixed tenure of the posts of the said employees.
2. The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the Government Decision dated September 21, 2022 issued by the General Administration Department pursuant to Maharashtra Government Gazette Extraordinary Part-4, Maharashtra Act No. 45 of the year 2022, dated September 08, 2022.
3. Expenditure on salary of surplus post should be charged under the head of salary account of the office of Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Mumbai/Amravati/Nagpur and met from the sanctioned grant of the financial year concerned.
