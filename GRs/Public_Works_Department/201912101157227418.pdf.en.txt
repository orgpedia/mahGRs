4. In Maharashtra Engineering Service, Group A, the above mentioned candidates are being appointed for the post of Assistant Executive Engineer, Group-A in this department as per the provision of service recruitment rules through direct recruitment.
5. As per the recommendation made by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, the seniority of the selected candidates will be as indicated in paragraph no.1. Similarly, General Administration Department, Notification no. SRV 1076/ Bara, dated 21/06/1982 shall remain subject to the provisions and amendments made thereto from time to time.
6. Appointment of the above candidates to the post of Assistant Executive Engineer in Public Works Department in Maharashtra Engineering Service, Group-A will not be changed.
7. If required, the above candidates will have to serve in a post in the Defense Services of India or on such terms and conditions as may be decided by the Government of India for a period not less than 4 years subject to a period of training abroad. But
a) They shall not be required to perform such service after the expiry of 10 years after their appointment or b) They shall not be required to perform such service generally after attaining the age of 40 years.
8. The pay of the above candidates in the service of the Government of Maharashtra during the probationary period will be fixed as per Rule 11 of the Maharashtra Civil Service Pay Rules, 1981. As per the first proviso to rule 39.1 of the Maharashtra Civil Services Pay Rules, 1981, they will get increment during the probationary period.
9. Among the above mentioned candidates who have not already passed the Hindi and Marathi Language Examination or have not been exempted from passing, will have to pass the Hindi and Marathi Language Examination rules.
10. Also, if any of the above mentioned candidates have not passed the computer examination prescribed by the government, they will have to pass the computer examination prescribed by the government as per the rules in this regard.
