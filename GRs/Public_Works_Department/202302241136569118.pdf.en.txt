Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Arvi and Ashti Talukas of Wardha District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Wardha has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said road as well as the resolution of the General Assembly, Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the following roads in Arvi and Ashti Talukas of Wardha District are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
