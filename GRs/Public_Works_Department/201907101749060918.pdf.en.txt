Manoj M. Jaiswal, Executive Engineer, after his promotion as per the reference order, is now partially modified and his revised posting is being done as per his request and in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience, to the vacant post of Superintending Engineer, National Highways Board, Nagpur.
3. Mr. Jaiswal should assume charge of his new post immediately and submit his assumption of office report to the government. The concerned are being made aware that they should not be absent without authorization otherwise their absence will be considered as unauthorized absence and will be unpaid and the possibility of interruption in service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. Since the change in posting of Jaiswal is being done as per his request, he will not be paid the tenure, travel allowance and daily allowance as admissible as per rules.
