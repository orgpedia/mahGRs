Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the inclusion of unplanned road from Katwadi to Ankai Bari in Chandwad Taluka of Nashik District in Prajima-65 vide letter under reference.
Considering the usage of the said roads, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road and include it in Prajima 65.
Government Decision : Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, unplanned road from Katwadi to Ankai Bari in Chandwad Taluk of Nashik District is being included in Prajima 65. The previous total length of Prajima 65 was 25.650 km.
It is like this, out of which Missing Length is 2.250 km. being omitted.) Total length 23.400 km. A 1.400 km stretch of unplanned road from Katwadi to Ankai Bari is being included. After inclusion, the total length of Pragma 65 will be 24,800 km. Also the name of Pragima 65 will be - Rahud Uswad Dugaon Raipur Vadgaon Pangu Katwadi to Ankai Bari road.
