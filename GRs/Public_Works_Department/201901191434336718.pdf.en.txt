Regarding nomination of the post of Chief Engineer and Joint Secretary as Chief Engineer and Project Director (Externally Aided Projects) for Asian Development Bank financed projects.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Government Decision No. Narrow-2018/Pro.No.02 / ADB
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032.
Date : : 19th January, 2019
Introduction:
About three lakh km in the state of Maharashtra. A wide network of roads of this length is spread and the Public Works Department has about 90 thousand km. There are major state highways, state highways and district highways of this length. Maximum funds are required for the construction and improvement of the said roads and bridges and funds must be made available from various sources.
Since the Ministry of Finance (DEA) of the Central Government has approved the availability of financial assistance of 500 million dollars (US $ 500 Million Dollar) from the Asian Development Bank for the first phase for the improvement of the state highways and major district roads in the state, it is necessary to submit a proposal to the Asian Development Bank to take action on the same. The process has been initiated by this department. This is an ambitious project of the government and to implement it in an excellent and effective manner, the issue of appointing a Project Director and Chief Engineer, an officer at the level of Chief Engineer for externally assisted projects by setting up a Project Management Unit at the state level and a Project Implementation Unit for the regional level was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision:
A proposal has been submitted to the Asian Development Bank for providing financial assistance for the construction and improvement of roads and bridges in the state and for the implementation of this project of the government in an excellent and effective manner, for the project funded by the Asian Development Bank (Maharashtra Road Improvement Project) Chief Engineer and Joint Secretary, Department of Public Works, Ministry , Mumbai by changing the designation of Chief Engineer &amp; Project Director (Externally Aided Project) to Chief Engineer &amp; Project Director (Externally Aided Project) as per this government decision.
