Introduction
The final seniority list of Civil Engineering Assistant cadre for the period dated 01.01.1989 to 31.12.2018 has been published vide circular dated 11.12.2020. In the service book of Smt. Sujata Sadhana Rao Bhatkule, she was registered as Civil Engineering Assistant in direct service. Record of appearance on 03.06.2008 appears. However, Civil Engineering Assistant Cadre Dt. In the final seniority list published on 11.12.2020 against the name of Mrs. Bhatkule in column 10 dt. Recorded on 06.03.2008. Therefore, the date of appointment of Mrs. Bhatkule is being changed to 03.06.2008 instead of 06.03.2008 in the final seniority list of Civil Engineering Assistant cadre published on 11.12.2020. A statement regarding the changes made accordingly is attached as Annexure “E”.
