Preface: As per the above government decision regarding e-tendering system under Public Works Department
It is mandatory to hold a pre-tender meeting for construction tenders. But in some cases
A pre-tender meeting will be held to provide tender approval in order to proceed with tenders
Due to non-attendance of the officers, the points made by the tenderers in their presence were not satisfied
It has come to the attention of the government. Therefore, the government is issuing the following instructions.
Government Decision:
Pre-tender meeting of construction regarding e-tendering system required for various building, road and bridge projects under Public Works Department should be held in the presence of the tender sanctioning officers.
