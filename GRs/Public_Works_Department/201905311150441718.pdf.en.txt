Dated 20/02/2019.
Preface : The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference to include the unplanned road from Shivni Bhilwasti Shindewadi to Grama 51 in Beed Taluka of Beed District as Rural Road in Ravio 2001-21.
The demand of Zilla Parishad, Beed is to include the use of the said road as well as the road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the above mentioned road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Aurangabad and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Beed, an unplanned road chain number 0/00 to 4/500 from Shivni Bhilwasti Shindewadi to Grama 51 in Beed Taluka of Beed District is constructed with a length of 4.500 km.
This unplanned road is being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 as Rural Road No-569.
Consequently, the total length of rural roads in Beed district in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 was 4,500 km. By increasing the total length (6764.190 + 4.500 ) i.e. 6768.69 km. I. That will be it.
