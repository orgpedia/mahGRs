Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of Bibi Chikhla Hatta Tambola Saraswati Lonar Hirdav Gundha Bagulkhed Gunjkhed road as major district road in Buldana district through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Buldana has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the traffic volume on the said road and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the following road is being upgraded.
