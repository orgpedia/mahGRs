Regarding shifting the office of Superintending Engineer, Vigilance Squad Board, Mumbai to Pune...
Government of Maharashtra
Public Works Department,
Government Decision No. EST-2019/Q.No.91 / Administration-1
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400 032.
Date :- 07 December, 2019
Preface: The headquarters of the office of Superintendent Engineer, Vigilance Team Board, Mumbai is currently in Mumbai and the matter of shifting the said office to Pune for convenience of administrative work was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: It is approved as per this government decision to transfer the office of Superintendent Engineer, Vigilance Team Board, Mumbai to Pune and nominate him as Superintendent Engineer, Vigilance Team Board, Pune, subject to the condition that no additional cost will be incurred on the Government. The office of the Superintending Engineer, Vigilance Team Board, Mumbai is being shifted to the available space in the office of the Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Pune till an alternative space is arranged at Pune.
The Superintending Engineer, Vigilance Squad Board, Pune for Vigilance Squad Board, Pune office is hereby declared as &#39;Head of Office&#39; by this Government Decision. The Head of Office (Superintending Engineer) will perform all the responsibilities (eg payment of wages, payment of dues, service and financial matters etc.) of the Head of Office in the Vigilance Team Board, Pune Board Office.
