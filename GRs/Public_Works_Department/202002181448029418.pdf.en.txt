Introduction:
The proposal to transfer the road length of Old Pune Nashik Highway in Chakan City from R.M.No.127 km 0/00 to 2/300 under the jurisdiction of Public Works Department to Chakan Municipal Council for maintenance and repair as per the demand of Chakan Municipal Council Chief Engineer, Public It has been received by Govt from Regional Department of Construction, Pune. Accordingly, the transfer of said road was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the provision of Note 5 of the Government Decision No-RBD-1081/871/ Roads-7, dated 09/03/2001 and the following terms and conditions
1) While granting permission from the Municipal Council for the construction of the said road, the permission will be given according to the A, B, C Municipal Council Rules approved by the Government of Maharashtra on November 2013 and as per the prevailing government decision issued by the Urban Development Department from time to time.
2) Vigilance will be taken by the municipal council that encroachment will not take place on the said road.
3) The said road will be properly maintained and repaired and it will be kept traffic-friendly and the entire expenses incurred for this will be borne by the Municipal Council.
4) All court cases regarding the said road will be transferred to Chakan Municipal Council. All those court cases should be handled by Chakan Municipal Council.
