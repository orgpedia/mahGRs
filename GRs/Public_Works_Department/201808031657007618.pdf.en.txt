Government Order: In accordance with the provisions of Sections 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Mr. B.D. of the Public Works Department. Dabhade, Executive Engineer, Jalgaon Municipal Corporation, Jalgaon is being transferred to the vacant post of Executive Engineer, Road Project Department, Jalgaon as per his request and in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Jalgaon Municipal Corporation, Jalgaon.
3. Mr. Dabhade should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, Mr. Dabhade&#39;s attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Dabhade is being transferred as per his request, he will not be paid the joining period, traveling allowance and daily allowance as admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Dabhade to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Opp. Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
