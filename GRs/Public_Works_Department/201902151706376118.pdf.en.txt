Introduction: Nagpur-Mumbai Rapid Transit Project is an ambitious project of the state government. As per the proviso to Section 4 of the Maharashtra Highways Act, the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited is the “Highway Authority” for the said project. The state government has given in-principle approval to develop Navnagars (Agricultural Prosperity Centers) at 24 places on the said expressway. Accordingly, under the Government Notification dated 23.04.2018 from the Department of Urban Development, the land at Mauje Babatara, Lakhganga and Purangaon, Taluka Vaijapur, District Aurangabad and Mauje Dhotre, Taluka Kopargaon, District Ahmednagar as per Section 113 of the Maharashtra Regional and Urban Planning Act, 1966 has been notified for Navnagar. has been done and for this purpose the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation has been declared as “New Town Development Authority”.
The said notification has been published in the Government Gazette dated May 24.03.2018.
The notified land in Navnagar is in Aurangabad and Ahmednagar districts.
The area of Mauje Babatara, Lakhganga and Purangaon, Taluka Vaijapur is under the jurisdiction of Sub Divisional Officer (Revenue), Sub Division Vaijapur, District Aurangabad and the area of Mauje Dhotre, Taluka Kopargaon is under the jurisdiction of Sub Divisional Officer (Revenue), Sub Division Shirdi, District Ahmednagar.
As per the provisions of Land Consolidation Scheme under the Maharashtra Highways (Amendment) Act, 2016, the area covered by the said Navanagar is to be acquired through the consent given by the landowners or interested persons on voluntary participation in the said scheme. For that purpose, under section 2 (d-1) of the said Act, &quot;competent authority&quot; includes the Collector of a revenue district and includes any officer not below the rank of Sub-District Officer appointed by the Government for the purpose of the Land Consolidation Scheme.
The area included in said Navnagar is under the jurisdiction of Sub Divisional Officer (Revenue), Vaijapur, District Aurangabad and Sub Divisional Officer (Revenue), Shirdi, District Ahmednagar. Therefore, in terms of ease of administrative work and consolidation of land
