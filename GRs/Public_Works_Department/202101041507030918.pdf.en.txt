Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road from Pondhwadi to Kadamvasti in Karmala Taluka of Solapur District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Solapur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the usage of the said road and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the government is considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the unplanned road from Pondhwadi to Kadamvasti in Karmala taluka of Solapur district is 5.00 km long. Grama No-272 is being included in Road Development Scheme 2001-21.
Including the said road in Grama 272 makes the total length of Grama 272 (3.0+5.0) 8.0 km. So and Namabhidhan Umrad to Pondhavadi road will be. Chain No. 0/00 to 8/00.
As a result, the total length of rural roads in Solapur district in Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 05.00 km. By increasing the total length (6879.13 +5.000) i.e. 6884.13 km. I. That will be it.
