Refer to :- 1) Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Konkan&#39;s letter Ja.No./MU(Co)/Drawing Branch/Promotion/695, dated 22/02/2022.
Foreword : Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Konkan vide letter under reference to include roads connecting railway stations in Sangameshwar, Rajapur, Chiplun, Khed and Kankavali, Kudal, Sawantwadi talukas of Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg districts as rural roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21 Submitted to Govt. Also, Chief Executive Officer and Administrator, Zilla Parishad, Sindhudurg and Chief Executive Officer and Administrator, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri have demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the road as well as the demand of the Chief Executive Officer and Administrator, Zilla Parishad, Sindhudurg and the Chief Executive Officer and Administrator, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, as well as the authorized access / connecting roads of Konkan Railway connecting the Konkan Railway Station. Konkan Railway Corporation Ltd. for the purpose of improving and maintaining them through Public Works Department. According to the Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) dated 23.01.2023 between (KRCL) and Public Works Department, Government of Maharashtra, the said roads were under consideration of the Government to include them as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Government Decision: Proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and demand of Chief Executive Officer and Administrator, Zilla Parishad, Sindhudurg and Chief Executive Officer and Administrator, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri as well as Konkan Railway Corporation Ltd. (KRCL) and Public Works Department, Government of Maharashtra taking into account the MoU between Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg Districts Roads connecting to Railway Stations in Sangameshwar, Rajapur, Chiplun, Khed and Kankavali, Kudal, Sawantwadi Talukas as a special matter Road Development Scheme as Rural Roads are being included in 2001-21.
