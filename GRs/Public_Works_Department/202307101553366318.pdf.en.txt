The Versova-Bandra sea bridge will be named after Swatantryaveer Savarkar.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Government Resolution No: Khakshes-2023/ PR No. 301/ Roads-8,
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400032
Date: July 10, 2023
Preamble: The Union Cabinet in its meeting held on 28.06.2023 has approved the renaming of Versova-Bandra Sea Bridge as" Swatantryaveer Savarkar."
Government Decision:
The government has decided to rename the Versova-Bandra sea bridge as Swatantryaveer Savarkar sea bridge.
