Circular
Provisional additional seniority list of superintendents working in zonal offices (Chief Engineer/Superintending Engineer) under Public Works Department dated 1.1.2017 is hereby published.
2. The said seniority list has been prepared in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 as well as the instructions vide Government Decision No.S.P.V.SRV-2011/P.No.284/12 dated 21.10.2011.
3. The Chief Engineer / Superintending Engineer / Head of Department is requested to bring this seniority to the attention of the concerned Superintendent. If any error is found in the said seniority list or if there is a statement to be made in this regard, the head of the department should verify such statement and send it to the government with his feedback within 30 days from the date of this circular. In case no objection/information is received within the prescribed period, the seniority list will be published in its final form, assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority list are correct.
