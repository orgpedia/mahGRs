Other District Roads in Badnapur Taluka of Jalna District to be designated as Major District Roads.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Government Resolution No: RAVIO-2023/ PR No. 115/ Planning-2
Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 032
Dated the 13th of June, 2023.
Refer:- 1) Letter from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad. 24/03/2023.
The Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Aurangabad, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade other district roads in Badnapur taluka of Jalna district as major district roads under the reference letter. The Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Jalna has also demanded to upgrade these roads.
Considering the number of villages on these roads, the population, the use of the roads and the demand of the Zilla Parishad, Jalna, the government was considering to upgrade these roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Jalna, the following other district roads of Badnapur taluka of Jalna district are being upgraded as major district roads.
