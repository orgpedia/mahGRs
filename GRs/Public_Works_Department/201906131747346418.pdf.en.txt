Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nashik, as per reference letter, in Ahmadnagar and Rahuri Talukas of Ahmednagar District.
A proposal has been submitted to the government to upgrade the road from 8 Kalewadi to R.M.52 as a major district road. Also Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmedgan, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmadganar, the following roads in Ahmednagar district are being upgraded.
