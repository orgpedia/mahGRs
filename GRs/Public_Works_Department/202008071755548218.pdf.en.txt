Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4(1), 4(2) and 4(3) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. A. Q. Debdwar, Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur has been transferred in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience to Shri. Vs. Ms. Deshpande is being transferred to the vacant post of Chief Engineer, National Highways (S.B.), Navi Mumbai. Mr. Deshpande&#39;s transfer orders are being issued separately.
2. Orders for posting of other officers to the post of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur will be issued separately.

4. It is informed to the concerned controlling officers that they have Shri. Debdwar should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of relinquishment of the present charge as well as the date of assumption of the new charge.

6. Mr. Debadwar to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Opp Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly appointed post.
