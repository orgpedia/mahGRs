3. Government Decision:
The cadre strength of Chief Engineer (Civil), Superintendent Engineer (Civil) and Executive Engineer (Civil) in Maharashtra Engineering Service Group-A in Public Works Department as on 01.04.2018 is fixed and announced as follows
a) Chief Engineer (Civil)
9 posts of Chief Engineer (Civil) have been approved as per the sanctioned figure of S.B. Department. Also 15 posts of Chief Engineers on deputation are being announced total number of cadre strength is 24. There will be 9 posts as per sanctioned figure and 15 seasonal posts on deputation.
b) Superintending Engineer (Civil) -
41 posts of Superintending Engineer (Civil) are sanctioned as per sanctioned figure of S.B. Department.
Also, the total cadre strength of the said cadre is 82 which is 41 posts of Superintendent Engineers on deputation. There will be 41 posts as per sanctioned figure and 41 seasonal posts on deputation.
c) Executive Engineer (Civil)
184 posts of Executive Engineer (Civil) are sanctioned as per the sanctioned scheme of the Department of Public Works.
Also, apart from the above posts of the department, total 44 posts of Zilla Parishad of Rural Development and Water Conservation Department are approved and these posts are filled through this department. Considering the said posts, total 228 permanent posts are sanctioned in the cadre of Executive Engineer (Civil). Apart from this, there are 123 posts of Executive Engineers on deputation. Therefore, the cadre strength of the said cadre as on 01.04.2018 is 351. There will be 228 posts as per sanctioned figure and 123 seasonal posts on deputation.
4. The details of permanent sanctioned posts and temporary posts on deputation of Chief Engineer (Civil), Superintending Engineer (Civil) and Executive Engineer (Civil) are mentioned in the circular attached to this Government Decision.
