Introduction: Mrs. Meena Surendra Kale, who is working as a Room Officer in the Public Works Department, has been temporarily promoted to the post of Under Secretary as per Government Order No-1, dated 23 February 2023 under the reference of the General Administration Department and has been posted in the same department. He has joined the department from 23 February 2023 (AD).
She will retire on May 31, 2023.
2. The revised scheme of 269 posts in various cadres on the establishment of Public Works Department (Self) has been approved vide Government Decision, Public Works Department No.-EST-1117/P.No.13/Administration-2, dated 4th November, 2023. 7 posts have been sanctioned for this department in the cadre of Under Secretary (Technical) and all the posts are filled. Out of 38 sanctioned posts, 6 posts are vacant in Room Officer cadre. At present Mrs. Kale needs to be upgradated one post of Room Officer in the Department to the rank of Under Secretary for posting to the post of Under Secretary. The matter of upgradation of one post of Room Officer in the Sub Division to Under Secretary cadre was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision : One post in the cadre of Room Officer (Pay Scale S-17 : Rs.47,600-1,51,100) in the cadre of Under Secretary (Pay Scale S-23 : Rs.67,700) on the establishment of Public Works Department (Self) 2,08,700) from 23rd February, 2023 (B.C.) to 31st May, 2023 is being approved by the Government.
2. Smt. Meena Surendra Kale, Under Secretary is being posted on this enhanced post with effect from 23rd February, 2023 (M.D.). This graded post will continue to exist as long as the Under Secretary is working in that department. Thereafter this post will automatically be downgraded to the rank of Room Officer.
3. The expenditure incurred in this regard should be met from the sanctioned grant under the appropriate budget head of the Public Works Department.
