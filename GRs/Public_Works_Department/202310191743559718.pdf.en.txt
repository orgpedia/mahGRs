GOVERNMENT Circular: Public Works Department, Government vide Circular No. Seniority-2020/ No. 28/ Aastha-1, dated 08.06.2021, has published the provisional seniority list of Sub Divisional Engineer (Architecture) cadre for the period 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2017, dated 01.01.2018. Also refer to no. The corrections have been made under Government Corrigendum 2 and 3.
In the Provisional Seniority List published on 08.06.2021, the date of ad-hoc promotion of Sub Divisional Engineers who were promoted during the period from 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2017 in ad-hoc manner was fixed as the standard date of seniority.
However, since the said ad-hoc promotions have been regularized with the approval of the General Administration Department, it is necessary to change the date of seniority of the engineers who were given ad-hoc promotions, so the seniority list and the reference sheets dated 08.06.2021 may be superseded by this circular and the revised provisional seniority list of the Sub-Divisional Engineer (Architecture) cadre of the Public Works Department for the period from 01.01.2014 to 31.07.2017 may be published in Reference No. 4 has been issued by the government.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, the period of seniority list in the relevant year must be from January 1 to December 31. The revised Seniority List for the period from 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2016 in the revised Provisional Seniority List dated 12.05.2023 has been finalized as there is no objection to the list. The list for the period from 01.01.2017 to 31.07.2017 will be included in the seniority list as on 01.01.2018 (from 01.01.2017 to 31.12.2017).
