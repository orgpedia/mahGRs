Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati, in the letter under reference, the length of the following rural roads in Amravati Taluka of Amravati District and the length of the roads under the jurisdiction of Municipal Corporation, Amravati from Badnera Bus Stand to Durgapur (Hanuman Temple) municipal limits existing major district road no. A proposal for inclusion in 74 has been submitted to the government. Also, the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati has requested to include the said length in the existing PRJIMA-74.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the roads as well as the demand of the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the length of the said rural road roads and the length within the municipal limits of the main district road no. 74 was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the demand of the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the following Municipalities in Amravati Taluka of Amravati District, Amravati Boundary Length and Rural Road Road Length included in the existing Major District Road No.74 are being done.
