Government Decision:
The cadre strength of Executive Engineer (Civil) in Maharashtra Engineering Service Group-A in Public Works Department as on 01.09.2020 is being fixed and announced as follows
184 posts of Executive Engineer (Civil) are sanctioned as per the sanctioned scheme of the Department of Public Works. Also, apart from the above posts of the department, total 44 posts of Zilla Parishad of Rural Development and Water Conservation Department are approved and these posts are filled through this department. Considering the said posts, total 228 permanent posts are sanctioned in the cadre of Executive Engineer (Civil). Apart from this, there are 123 posts of Executive Engineers on deputation. Therefore, the cadre strength of the said cadre as on 01.09.2020 is 351. It will consist of 228 posts as per sanctioned figure and 123 seasonal posts on deputation.
