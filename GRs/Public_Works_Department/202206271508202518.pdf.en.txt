Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in North Solapur Taluka of Solapur District as Rural Road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also, Zilla Parishad, Solapur has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the road development plan.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the following roads in North Solapur Taluka of Solapur District are being upgraded as rural roads.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No.        | Name of proposed road                           | Chain no     | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.           | Banegaon to Ekrukh lake water supply well road. | 0/00 to 4/00 | 4.00 km.                         | Rural Marg-118      |
| total length | 4.00 km                                         |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Solapur district in Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 4.00 km. By increasing the total length (6690.430 +4.00) i.e. 6694.430 km. I. That will be it.
