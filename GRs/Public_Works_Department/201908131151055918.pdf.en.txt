Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding inclusion of unclassified roads in Latur taluka of Latur district as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also, Zilla Parishad, Latur has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads.
Since the villages on the said road are connected to the main road and taking into account the approval of the Zilla Parishad, Latur, the said road was under consideration of the Government to include it as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
