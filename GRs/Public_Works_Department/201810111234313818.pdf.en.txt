Corrigendum under reference 1 The final seniority list of Civil Engineering Assistant Cadre from 01.01.1989 to 31.12.2014 has been released as per Government Circular dated 02.01.2016. According to the said circular, the statutory criteria for inclusion in the seniority list have been clarified.
2. On perusal of the government circular under reference, working under Nagpur circle (then) Shri.M.G. It is seen that Waghmare, Civil Engineering Assistant has been recorded twice in seniority number A.No.1332 and 1404.
Mr. M.G. Waghmare has been inducted as Civil Engineering Assistant on 22.02.2007. Accordingly, he has been given seniority serial number 1332 by giving seniority date of 22.02.2007. Mr. Waghmare has not passed the qualifying examination but he has been exempted from the condition of passing the qualifying examination at the age of 45 i.e. on 15.05.2008. Reason, accordingly, in the seniority list dated 02.01.2016, the date of seniority has been given as 15.05.2008 and the seniority serial number has been given as 1404. Thus Mr. Waghmare has been entered twice in the seniority list of Civil Engineering Assistants dated 02.01.2016.
3. In the above circumstances Mr. M. G. Waghmare&#39;s seniority number allotted to him under No.1332 in the seniority list dated 02.01.2016 of Civil Engineering Assistant Cadre is cancelled. Reason, Mr. The seniority serial number 1404 assigned to Waghmare in the above seniority list is correct.
