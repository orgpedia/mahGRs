Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Mehkar and Lonar talukas of Buldana district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Bulda has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Buldana, the following roads in Mehkar and Lonar talukas of Buldana district are being upgraded as major district roads.
