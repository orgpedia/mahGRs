Introduction:
Consolidated guidelines for recruitment through direct service to posts in nomination quotas in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D cadres under Ex-Secondary Services Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) have been issued by the General Administration Department vide Government Decision dated 04.5.2022 . Accordingly, the composition of regional and state level selection committees for the recruitment of the said posts has been mentioned in the said government decision. Accordingly, the Administrative Department of the Ministry has to establish regional and state level selection committees. General administration in this regard
It is necessary to set up each department wise selection committees as per the composition given in the Department, Govt Decision dated 04.5.2022. Accordingly, the issue of setting up a state level and regional selection committee for filling up the posts in the nomination quota of group-B (non-gazetted) and group-C cadre in the construction branch through direct service was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision:
Excluding the posts to be filled through Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the quota of nominations in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadre in the Construction Office under the Public Works Department, the state level and regional selection committee is being structured as follows to fill the remaining posts through direct service.
