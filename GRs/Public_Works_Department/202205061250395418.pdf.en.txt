Introduction:
Works of roads, bridges and buildings are done through Public Works Department. At present, the works of roads, bridges and buildings are in progress all over the state. Since March, 2020, the progress of the works has been greatly affected due to the corona epidemic and due to this, most of the works have not been completed within the stipulated time. Also, during this period, the price of construction materials such as cement, steel, asphalt has increased to a large extent. Due to the increase in the said price, various contractor organizations have made demands to the government through oral and written letters from time to time. Due to the situation arising out of the corona situation and the extraordinary increase in the price of construction materials, the issue of compensation to be paid to the contractors was under the consideration of the government.
Government circular: In accordance with it, the following committee is being formed to decide the policy in accordance with the consequences of the increase in the price of construction materials.

The said constituted committee is expected to decide the policy regarding the special price increase to be paid to the contractor as per the requirement and submit the report to the government within 15 days after taking into consideration the provision of price increase and the extraordinary increase in the price of construction materials.
The working class of the said committee will be as follows: 1) The committee will study the price increase clause provisions in the prevailing tenders.
2) To study the situation arising out of the corona epidemic and the extraordinary increase in the price of construction materials.
3) Taking into consideration both the above aspects, to formulate a definite formula for calculating the compensation payable to the contractor as required.
