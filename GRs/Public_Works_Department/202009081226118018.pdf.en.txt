Introduction:
According to the government decision dated July 29, 2020, guidelines have been issued regarding the Enlistment of PWD Contractors working in the Public Works Department. According to this government decision, Maharashtra State Contractors Federation has raised various problems. Also, some contractors have informed about the problems in this regard. Accordingly, it is necessary to take into consideration the matters of this government decision and the difficulties raised by the contractors regarding the implementation of the government decision. It is also in order to amend the government decision in this regard if necessary.
Government circular: According to it, the provisions of the government decision dated July 29, 2020 and considering the obstacles raised by various contractor associations/contractors regarding their implementation, a study committee is being appointed as follows to suggest necessary amendments in the government decision.
