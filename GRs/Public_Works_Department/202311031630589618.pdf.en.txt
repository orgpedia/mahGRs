Introduction:
Though adequate technical expertise is available for the conception, construction and special repairs of roads, bridges and buildings under the Department, sometimes due to inadequate staff or in some cases due to complex technical issues, need for modern technology, environmental studies and similar matters, verification of feasibility, research studies and legal, economic investigations for projects to be taken up under privatization. The procedure for the appointment of a consultant has been decided. The Chief Engineer hereby lays down in detail the procedure/ guidelines to be followed for appointment of nominated Consultants (Financial/ Technical) at his level.
The Government was contemplating to constitute a Committee to suggest amendments in the procedures/ guidelines laid down by the said Government.
Government Circular:
The following Committee is being constituted to study and suggest amendments in the existing procedure/ guidelines regarding appointment of Consultants for Road, Building and Bridge projects to be taken up under PWD

To make recommendations to the Government on the improvements to be made by making a thorough study of the prevailing procedures/ guidelines regarding the appointment of consultants for the projects of roads, buildings and bridges to be taken up under the Public Works Department.
