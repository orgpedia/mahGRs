Introduction: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Amalner and Parola talukas of Jalgaon district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon, the following roads in Jalgaon district are being upgraded.
