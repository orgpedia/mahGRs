Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Government Corrigendum No. : Seniority 2015/P.No.58/Aastha-1, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 032,
Dated- 20.11.2018.
Read- 1) Government Circular, Public Works Department no. Seniority 2015/P.No.58/Aastha-1, dated 02.01.2016.
2) Government Circular No. Public Works Department No. Seniority 2015/P.No.58/Aastha-1, dated 15.07.2017.
Corrigendum According to the information received from the regional offices under reference 1, the final seniority list of Civil Engineering Assistant Cadre for the period from 01.01.1989 to 31.12.2014 has been released as per the Government Circular dated 02.01.2016. According to the said circular, the statutory criteria for inclusion in the seniority list have been clarified. After that, after scrutinizing the objections/statements received in accordance with the said seniority list, on the basis of the information received from the field offices, the government has issued a correction letter on 15.07.2017.
2. However, objections and representations are still being received in accordance with the above seniority list and the relevant Government Corrigendum. In which correction of date of birth, date of appointment, alternatively giving seniority number and date of appointment in revised form, taking note of other ancillary matters (such as project victims, disabled etc.) etc. Matters are covered. Proposals have also been received regarding inclusion of new names in the final seniority list.
3. According to the above, considering the objections/statements received by the government and the supporting documents, the documents and information received from the regional offices/S.B. Parimanda, the correction sheet regarding the amendment in the reference seniority list and the government correction sheet is being published along with the appendices.
