Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of 8 other district roads and 3 rural roads in Shahada taluka of Nandurbar district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population and total usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar, the following roads in Shahada Taluka of Nandurbar district are being upgraded.
