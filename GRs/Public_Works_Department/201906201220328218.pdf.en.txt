Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of Hamdapur Algaon Shivangaon Pahelanpur Palasgaon Sindi (Railway) to district boundary road as a major district road in Wardha district through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Wardha has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the following roads in Wardha district are being upgraded.
