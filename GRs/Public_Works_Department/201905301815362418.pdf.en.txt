Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads and other district roads in Shindkheda taluka of Nashik district as major district roads and inclusion of IJIMA in Prajima No.18. Also, Zilla Parishad, Dhule has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the following roads in Dhule district are being upgraded.
