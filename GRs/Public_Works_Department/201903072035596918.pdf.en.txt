6. Mr. Misal has been posted on deputation subject to the terms and conditions of deputation prescribed in Appendix II of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Post-Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. .
