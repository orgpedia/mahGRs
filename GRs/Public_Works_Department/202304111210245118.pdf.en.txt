Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads in Mandangad and Dapoli talukas of Ratnagiri district as major district roads through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following other district roads in Mandangad and Dapoli talukas of Ratnagiri district are being upgraded as road projects.

As a result the total length of major district road grade roads in Ratnagiri district in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 14,250 km. By increasing the total length (2060.725 + 14.250) i.e. 2074.975 km. As well as other district roads, the total length of roads is 14.250 km.
