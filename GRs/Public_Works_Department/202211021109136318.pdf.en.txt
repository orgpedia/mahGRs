Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of major district roads in Chikhali Taluka of Buldana district to state highways through the letter under reference.
Taking into consideration the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati, the following major district roads in Chikhali Taluka of Buldana District are being upgraded as State Highways.
