Government Decision:
Said Road State Road No.262 (Old R.M.No.213) Passing through City in Pusad Municipal Council (Shivaji Chowk Subhash Chowk, Ambedkar Chowk to Old Umarkhed Naka Pusad) Chain No. Old Km.61/400 to 63/200 New 0/00 to 1/800 Total length 1.800 km. The length is such that, as there is an alternate road (detour) from Pusad to Umarkhed, most of the heavy traffic is carried on the alternate road.
Said 1.800 km. Length has been transferred to Municipal Council Pusad for maintenance and repairs since 08/01/2003. However, as the said length has not been declassified and to avoid further complications the said length is being declassified.
The Nagar Parishad, Pusad has approved the declassification of the said road.

Government Decision No: Ravio-2021/P.No.42/Plan-2
Accordingly, subject to the following conditions, State Road No. 262 (Old R.M. No. 213) (Shivaji Chowk Subhash Chowk, Ambedkar Chowk to Old Umarkhed Naka Pusad) passing through the city within the limits of Pusad Municipal Council, Chain No. Old Km.61/400 to 63 /200 New 0/00 to 1/800 Total Length 1.800 Km. Length is being unclassified.
1) While giving permission from the city council for the construction along the said road, Maharashtra Government approved on November 2013 A. b. c. Permission should be given keeping proper distances as per Municipal Council Rules and prevailing Government Decisions issued by Urban Development Department.
2) Nagar Parishad should ensure that encroachments do not take place on the said road.
3) The said road should be maintained and repaired to keep the road traffic suitable.
4) The said road will be resurfaced as per the need in future. If the decision is taken to hand over the road to the Department, the responsibility of handing over the said road to the Department of Public Works will remain the responsibility of Pusad, Nagar Parishad and in this regard, any demand for funds/claims/expenditure for doing any kind of work on the road will not be accepted.
5) After declassification of the said road, encroachments/lease constructions/contracts or other related matters on it. Pusad Nagar Parishad will be responsible for handling court cases. Regarding the above subject Hon. Pusad Nagar Parishad should ensure that court orders, if any, are not disobeyed.
