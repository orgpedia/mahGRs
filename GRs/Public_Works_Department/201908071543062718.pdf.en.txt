Introduction:
The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Nashik to include the unplanned roads Nashirabad to Varadsima Road and Nashirabad to Bhagpur Road in Jalgaon Taluka of Jalgaon District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-2021.
There is no need for land acquisition as the said roads are on agricultural land. The said roads are convenient for the people of the respective villages, and there is a market at Nashirabad.
Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon to include roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the above mentioned road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon, the following roads in Jalgaon Taluka, Jalgaon District are being included as rural roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of unplanned road        | Chain no     | Length (km) | Proposed New Rural Road No |
| 1.    | Nashirabad to Varadsima road. | 0/00 to 3/00 | 3.00        | Rural Road No-142          |
| 2.    | Nashirabad to Bhagpur road.   | 0/00 to 4/00 | 4.00        | Rural Road No-143          |
| Total | 7.00 km.                      |
