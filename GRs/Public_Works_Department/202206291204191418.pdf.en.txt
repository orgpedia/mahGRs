Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of an unplanned road in Parbhani taluk of Parbhani district as a rural route in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also
Zilla Parishad, Parbhani has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the use of the said road and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani,
The said road was under the consideration of the government to upgrade it as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the following road in Parbhani taluka of Parbhani district is being upgraded as a rural road.

| A.No. | Name of proposed road     | Chain no     | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |

| Total | 5.00 km.                  |
