Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Parner and Ahmednagar talukas of Ahmednagar district as major district roads through the letter under reference.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, the following roads in Parner and Ahmednagar talukas of Ahmednagar district are being upgraded as major district roads.
