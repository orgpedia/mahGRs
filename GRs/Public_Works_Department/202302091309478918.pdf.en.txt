Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. The posting was given at Mumbai (Camp Office, Nagpur). As per his request and in the interest of public service and for administrative convenience, he is being posted on the vacant post of Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad (S.B.) Division, Gondia.
2. Mr. Meshram should assume charge of his new post immediately and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. Mr. Meshram M. no Sec. (Entry period, voluntary service etc
(Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, will be required to join the post on deputation within the prescribed tenure, otherwise the period of absence will be treated as dies non.
