2. The officers concerned should assume charge of their new post immediately and submit their assumption of office report to the government without delay. They should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and will be without pay. Also, the concerned are being made aware that the possibility of disruption in their services cannot be ruled out. Also,
The attention of the said officers is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively.
4. As the concerned officers are being transferred as per their request, they will not be admissible as per the rules on joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance.
