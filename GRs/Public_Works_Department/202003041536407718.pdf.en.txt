Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Ardhapur and Mudkhed Talukas of Nanded District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also Zilla Parishad, Nanded has demanded to include the said roads in the scheme.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nanded, the following roads in Nanded district are being upgraded as rural roads.
