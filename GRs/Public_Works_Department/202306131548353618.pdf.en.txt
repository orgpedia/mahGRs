Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Nanded, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade major district roads in Kinwat taluka of Nanded district as state highways under a reference letter.
Considering the number of villages on these roads, population, use of roads and demand, the government was considering to upgrade these roads as state highways.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded, the following major district roads in Kinwat Taluka of Nanded district are being upgraded as State Highways.
