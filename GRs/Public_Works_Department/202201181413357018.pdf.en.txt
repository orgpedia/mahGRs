Government Decision:
The cadre strength of Chief Engineer (Civil), Superintendent Engineer (Civil) and Executive Engineer (Civil) in Maharashtra Engineering Service Group-A as on 01.09.2021 is announced as follows
a) Chief Engineer (Civil) -
9 posts of Chief Engineer (Civil) have been approved as per the sanctioned figure of Public Works Department. Also 18 posts of Chief Engineers on deputation are being announced total strength of 27 of the said cadre. There will be 9 posts as per sanctioned figure and 18 seasonal posts on deputation.
b) Superintending Engineer (Civil) -
41 posts of Superintending Engineer (Civil) are sanctioned as per sanctioned figure of Public Works Department. Also, 41 Superintendent Engineer posts on deputation, the total cadre strength of the said cadre is 82 is being announced. There will be 41 posts as per sanctioned figure and 41 seasonal posts on deputation.
c) Executive Engineer (Civil)
184 posts of Executive Engineer (Civil) are sanctioned as per the approved scheme of Public Works Department. Also, apart from the above posts of the department, total 44 posts of Zilla Parishad of Rural Development and Water Conservation Department are approved and these posts are filled through this department. Considering the said posts, total 228 permanent posts are sanctioned in the cadre of Executive Engineer (Civil). Apart from this there are 121 posts of Executive Engineers on deputation.
