Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the major district roads in Kalameshwar and Savner talukas of Nagpur district as state highways through the reference letter.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said roads, population, demand for use of the roads, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as state highways.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, the following major district roads in Kalameshwar and Savaner Talukas of Nagpur District are being upgraded as State Highways.
