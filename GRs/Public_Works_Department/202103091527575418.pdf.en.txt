Introduction:
In the review meeting held on 28.01.2021 under the chairmanship of the Hon&#39;ble Minister (Public Works), it was decided to form a committee to discuss and study the new program under Hybrid Annuity. The issue of issuing a government decision in this regard was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision:
Hybrid Annuity Scheme has been implemented in the state on the lines of Hybrid Annuity Model of the Central Government to improve the roads on a large scale for comprehensive development of the roads in the state. A committee will be formed under the chairmanship of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati to study whether new works should be undertaken under the proposed scheme and submit a report to the government within a month. The composition of the present committee will be as follows.
1. Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati : Chairman 2. Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai Member 3. Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad : Member 4. Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Pune Member 5. Knowledgeable Chartered Accountant and Expert Cost Accountant : Members (Mr. Swapnil Gandhi, Mr. Sanjay Mehta)
The said committee will study and submit a report to the government in one month about whether to take up the next program under hybrid annuity.
