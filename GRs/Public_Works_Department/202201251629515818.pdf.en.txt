Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the major district roads in Surgana Peth Dindori, Chandwad and Niphad talukas of Nashik district as state highways.
Taking into consideration the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, the following major district roads in Surgana Peth Dindori, Chandwad and Niphad talukas of Nashik district are being included as state highways.
