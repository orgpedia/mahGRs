Introduction : General Administration Department, Government Decision, dt. As per the provision dated 21st December, 2019, the number of officers and employees who are to be promoted to the supernumerary post on account of non-submission of Caste Validity Certificate of Scheduled Tribes, to the extent that such officers and employees are currently serving in the supernumerary post and before the Government decision of the General Administration Department mentioned above. , the officers and employees who have been relieved of service due to the invalidity of the Caste Validity Certificate of the Scheduled Tribes, have been temporarily retained in service for eleven months by all administrative departments in order to create a majority of the post in which they were working before the termination of their service, from a humanitarian perspective and to remove administrative difficulties. Otherwise, the date on which they would have retired, whichever is earlier, is directed to be appointed to the supernumerary post.
Also General Administration Department, Government Decision, Dt. The administrative department has been empowered to create the said surplus posts by 30th June, 2020. According to Public Works Department, Government Decision, dated September 29, 2020, surplus posts have been created for officers/employees in the office of Chief Engineer (Electrical), Public Works Department, Mumbai. General Administration Department, Government Decision, Dated 27th November, 2020 and Dated 28th October, 2021, the said majority posts were extended. As the term of the said surplus post expired on 30th September, 2022, the issue of extending them further from 01st October, 2022 was under the consideration of the government.
