Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Prevention of Delays Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority, Shri. S. S. Solanki, Executive Engineer, Implementation Cell, Town Planning Department, Nagpur is being deputed on his request and in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience, to the vacant post of Deputy Executive Engineer, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai.
2. Mr. S. S. Solanki should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and they will be without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser7.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge respectively by the deposed and deputized officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30(c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
