Refer to :- 1) Letter No. Ka-PRA/1166 dated 16/03/2017 from Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur.
Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the Government to include a total of 58 unplanned / unclassified roads in Narkhed Taluka as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference.
Considering the said proposal and the number of villages on the said roads, the population as well as the total usage of the roads, it was under the consideration of the government to include the said unplanned / unclassified roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Government Decision: The following roads in Narkhed taluka of Nagpur district are being upgraded by considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the demand of local people&#39;s representatives.
