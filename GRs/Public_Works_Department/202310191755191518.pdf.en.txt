The Provisional Seniority as on 01.01.2018 for the period 31.12.2017 has been prepared after taking into consideration the provisions of M.N.S. (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021.
2. m. The High Court, Bombay vide its judgment dated 04.08.2017 in Petition No. 2797/2015 has declared the reservation in promotion as invalid. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed. The said petition is still pending and subject to the final disposal of the said petition, the provisional seniority of the cadre of Deputy Divisional Engineer (Architecture) for the period from 01.01.2017 to 31.12.2017, as on 01.01.2018, is being published in the prospectus (Form-A) attached to this circular. The circular shall be brought to the immediate notice of the Deputy Divisional Engineer (Architecture) in the attached list by all the Regional and Zonal Offices under their jurisdiction and a signed copy of the same shall be preserved in their own records.
If any error/ error is found in the details of the Deputy Divisional Engineer (Architecture) included in the said seniority list, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., they should bring their representations/ objections/ suggestions to the notice of the Government through e-mail (doest3.pwd@maharashtra. gov. in)/ registered post/ speed post/ hand delivery along with necessary proof in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. Objections received after this period will not be considered. If the engineers included in this seniority list have any objections/ suggestions, they should take care to attach the necessary documents/ records as proof in this regard. Objections/ suggestions received in the absence of any concrete evidence will be rejected, please note this. Also, avoid submitting objections/ suggestions by signing the same points through photocopy. If the errors/ errors are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the stipulated time, the final seniority of the last date of 01.01.2018 for the period from 01.01.2017 to 31.12.2017 for the category of Deputy Divisional Engineer (Architecture) will be published on the understanding that there are no objections.
