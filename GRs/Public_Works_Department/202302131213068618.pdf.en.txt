Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Gondia, Goregaon and Amgaon talukas of Gondia district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Gondia has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, use of the road as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Gondia, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Gondia, the following Ijima and Grama roads in Gondia, Goregaon and Amgaon talukas of Gondia district are being upgraded as major district roads.
