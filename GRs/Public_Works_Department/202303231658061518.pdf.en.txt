Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nashik vide letter under reference
A proposal has been submitted to the Government regarding the inclusion of unplanned roads in Devala Taluka of Nashik District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also Zilla Parishad,
Nashik has demanded to include the said roads as rural roads in the road development plan.
Considering the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik,
The said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into account the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following unplanned roads in Devala Taluka of Nashik district are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
