Government Circular:
Provisional seniority list of these superintendents working in zonal offices (Chief Engineer/Superintending Engineer) under Public Works Department dated 1.1.2021 and dated 1.1.2022, General Administration Department, Government Notification No.SRV-2016/P.No.281 /12, dated 21.6.2021 is hereby published in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority), Rules 2021.
2. The Chief Engineer/Superintending Engineer/Head of Department is requested to bring this seniority notice to the notice of the concerned Superintendent, and preserve his signed acknowledgment on record. It will be the responsibility of all the concerned offices to make the said receipt available to the government if the government deems it necessary.
3. If the concerned superintendent has any objections in the said seniority list, he should submit his written objections/statements through the concerned Chief Engineer/Superintending Engineer/Head of Department along with appropriate evidence and documents to the Government within 15 days. In case no objection/information is received within the prescribed period, the seniority list will be published in its final form, assuming that the details mentioned in the seniority list are correct.
