Introduction Due to the situation caused by Kovid-19 in the entire country. Lockdown was announced from 24.03.2020. Due to this, the availability of transport and labor was also adversely affected. Also, according to the government decision dated May 4, 2020 of the Finance Department, all departments are not to undertake any construction and also in the year 2020-2021
Planning instructions were given subject to the formula that 33% of the budgetary funds would be available. Therefore, as a special matter, all the registrations of the contractors registered with the Public Works Department up to the cost of Rs.1.50 crore, whose registration has expired or will be completed during the period from January to December 2020, were extended up to 31 December 2020.
Government Decision The registration of contractors whose registrations up to Rs.1.50 crores from the Public Works Department has expired or will expire during the period January 2020 to March 2021, is being extended to March 31, 2021.
