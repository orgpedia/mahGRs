3. The Government reserves the right to cancel the said promotion given to the above mentioned officers without any prior notice or notice.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they may relieve the said Officers by making necessary local alternate arrangements and ask them to report to their new places of appointment. Also, the concerned officials should be immediately informed to the Government on the e-mail id doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in about the date on which the concerned officials were released from duty to join the place of posting and on which date they appeared at the place of posting.
5. The concerned officials should assume charge of the new post with minimum tenure and submit their tenure report to the government without delay. If the said officer does not join the post on promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of this order, his period of absence shall be treated as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
6. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that the date of assumption of charge by the said Officers should be immediately reported to the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in. If they do not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) of his leave application should not be accepted / granted.
7. The concerned officers should give the option of pay fixation within one month from the date of joining the promoted post.
