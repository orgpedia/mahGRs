Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees and in the performance of Government duties
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Prevention of Delays Act, 2005 and with the approval of the Competent Authority, Shri. B. UEK, Executive Engineer, Maharashtra Rural Road Development Institute, Bhandara is being transferred to the vacant post of Executive Engineer, Zilla Parishad (S.L.) Division, Gondia in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience.
2. Mr. The UIKE should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the Government within eight days from the date of the order. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and they will be without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (leave)
As per rule-30(c) of the Rules, 1981 leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted/granted.
4. Mr. As the transfer of UIKE is for administrative reasons, they will be paid the joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance as admissible as per rules.
5. Mr. The date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post by the UIKE should be intimated to the Government as well as to the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Opposite Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune.
