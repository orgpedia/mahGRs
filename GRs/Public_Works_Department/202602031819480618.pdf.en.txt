Introduction: - Save No. 194 (b) provides for the proposal for the development of a Government plot here by way of privatisation. Held on 26.09.2001. It was approved by the Cabinet Committee on Infrastructure. Commencement of the work is ordered to the developer. Delivered on 19.08.2004. The original construction period of the project was 4 years.
The project has been extended 4 times. The fourth extension. 31.12.2019 was provided. The project has been delayed due to various reasons. At present, the physical progress achieved by the government buildings in the project is 78.81% and the progress of infrastructure works is generally 30%. The developer did the rest of the project. Completion up to 31.12.2027 is guaranteed. The project needs to be completed in the interest of the government. work on the present project. From 01.01.2020 to d. A proposal for a fifth and final extension to 31.12.2027 was under consideration of the government.
Following that. 09.12.2025 of the day. The proposal has been approved in the meetings of the Cabinet Committee on Infrastructure and accordingly, the Government has taken the following decision.
Government Decision: - 1. Save No. 194 (b) to complete the project of development of the Government plot here by way of privatization. From 01.01.2020 to d. The fifth and final extension up to 31.12.2027 is being granted subject to the following conditions.
2. The developer should appoint a Project Management Consultant to supervise the completion of the project so that it is well planned and quality.
3. The component / case-wise specific time-bound schedule of the remaining work received and submitted by the Chief Engineer, S.B. Regional Division, Mumbai from the developer with a view to complete the remaining work of the project in a time-bound manner is mentioned below AA.
