Preface: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Hinganghat taluka of Wardha district through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Wardha has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Wardha, the following roads in Wardha district are being upgraded.
