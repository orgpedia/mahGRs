The said promotion and posting of the aforesaid Executive Engineer (Architecture) is being made on temporary basis subject to the following: i. This is a promotion. Subject to the final decision of the Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 and also in Writ Petition Stamp No. 10876/2021 and Writ Petition Stamp No.
10878/2021 in Hon. High Court, Bombay. As per the decision dated 25.5.2021, the said promotion shall be subject to the decision in the said writ petition.
(ii) Subject to the final decision of the High Court in the matter pending before it in respect of seniority.
(iii) Since the said promotion is purely temporary in nature, they will not be entitled to any regularity and emolument consequent upon the promotion.
3. The Government reserves the right to cancel the said promotion to the above mentioned officers without any prior notice or notice.
The concerned officers should take charge of the new post with minimum time and submit their report to the government without any delay. As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, government employees who are appointed by promotion are required to join the post of posting within 30 days from the date of issue of the promotion order. If the officer concerned does not join the post of posting by promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of this order, his period of absence shall be deemed to be a non-working day. If the concerned officer does not join within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of order or within the extended period allowed for joining with prior approval, the order of promotion of the concerned officer shall be deemed to be automatically cancelled, considering that he is not interested in promotion.
5. The Controlling Authorities concerned are informed to relieve the said officers by making necessary local alternative arrangements to enable them to join their new place of posting and ask them to report at the new place of posting. Also, if the concerned officer does not join within the prescribed period, it should be immediately informed to the government, so that disciplinary action against the concerned can be taken immediately. The officers to be promoted/ posted under this order shall not accept/ grant leave applications as per Rule 30 (c) of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981.
