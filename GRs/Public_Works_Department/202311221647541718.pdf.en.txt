The ad-hoc promotion given to the vacant post in the direct service quota will be for 11 months or till the candidates are available from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, whichever is earlier. In the meantime, if the Commission-approved candidate is available, the ad-hoc promoted officer will be demoted. This promotion is temporary in nature and the officers concerned will not have the right to hold the post permanently.
The Government reserves the right to cancel the ad-hoc promotions given to the above officers without any prior notice or notice.
The concerned officers should take charge of the new post with minimum time and submit their report to the government without any delay. As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, government employees who are appointed by promotion are required to join the post of posting within 30 days from the date of issue of the promotion order. If the officer concerned does not join the post of posting by promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of this order, his period of absence shall be deemed to be a non-working day. If the concerned officer does not join within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of the order or within the extended period allowed for joining with prior approval, the order of promotion of the concerned officer shall be deemed to have been cancelled automatically on the ground that he is not interested in the promotion.
5. The Controlling Authorities concerned are informed to relieve the said officers by making necessary local alternative arrangements to enable them to join their new place of posting and ask them to report at the new place of posting. Also, if the concerned officer does not join within the prescribed period, it should be immediately informed to the government, so that disciplinary action against the concerned can be taken immediately. The officers who are to be promoted/ posted under this order shall not accept/ approve leave applications as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981.
The attention of the concerned authorities is being drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of the said Rules, they will be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any application for change in the post.
7. The concerned Controlling Authorities are hereby informed that the date on which the said officers were relieved by the promotion to join the place of posting and the date on which they appeared at the place of posting should be immediately communicated to the Government on the e-mail id doser1.pwd@maharashtra. gov. in.
8. The concerned officers should give the option of fixation of salary within one month from the date of assumption of charge of the post of promotion. In case the concerned officers do not give the option regarding fixation of pay within the prescribed time, the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981, Rule No. According to 11 (1) (a), the salary of the post of promotion should be determined by taking into account the date of promotion.
