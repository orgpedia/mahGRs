Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan vide letter under reference, in the existing major District Road No.-38 of Chiplun Taluk of Ratnagiri District, Rural Road No. 203 of 7.00 km. And rural road no. 158 of 4.00 km. Such a total of 11.00 km. A proposal for inclusion of length has been submitted to Govt. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the length of the said rural road was under the consideration of the government to include the length of the said rural road in the existing major district road No.-38.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the length of the following rural roads in Chiplun Taluka of Ratnagiri District are being included in the existing PRJIMA-38.
