Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide letter under reference National Highway no. 211 (New No. 52) A proposal has been submitted to the government for downgrading the total length of 10.09 km left in Beed city (Abandoned Length) of Solapur - Aurangabad road and including it in the major district road and giving it a new registration number.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the transfer of the said length by the Project Director, National Highways Authority of India, Aurangabad to the Public Works Department, National Highway No. 211 (New No. 52) The total length of 10.09 km left in Beed city (Abandoned Length) of Solapur - Aurangabad road was under consideration of the government to downgrade it and include it in the major district road and give a new major district road number.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the transfer of length from Beed city to Public Works Department by the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and Project Director, National Highways Authority of India, Aurangabad, the following lengths in Beed district are being included in the major district road and given new major district road numbers. Even if there is a change in the grade of the said road, there will be no change in the design route of the said road.
