Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of 2 rural roads and 1 other district roads in Malegaon taluka of Nashik district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
The said roads are useful for transporting agricultural produce to markets in large areas of Malegaon taluka. These roads are important for the villages in terms of communication and are important roads connecting the state highways and national highways. Taking into account the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following roads in Nashik district are being upgraded.
