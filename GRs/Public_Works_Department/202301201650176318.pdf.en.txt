Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include 89 unplanned roads in Khanapur taluka of Sangli district as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference. Also, according to the resolution of the Executive Engineer (Department), Zilla Parishad, Sangli, it has been requested to include the said roads as rural roads in the road development plan.
Considering the usage of the said roads as well as the demand of the Executive Engineer (Department), Zilla Parishad, Sangli, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and also the demand of Executive Engineer (Department), Zilla Parishad, Sangli, the following roads in Khanapur Taluka of Sangli district are being included as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-2021.
