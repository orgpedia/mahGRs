Introduction - Lockdown was announced from 24.03.2020 due to the situation caused by Kovid-19 in the entire country. Due to this, the availability of transport and labor was also adversely affected. Therefore, keeping in view the Covid situation, all the registrations of the contractors whose registrations have expired or are due to expire during the period of January to December 2020 up to Rs.1.50 crores with the Public Works Department have been extended as a special matter up to 31st March 2022. was
Govt Decision - Public Works Department registered contractor whose registration has expired by January 2020, March 2022 or all regular contractors whose registration is up to Rs.1.50 Crores up to March 2023, Educated Unemployed Engineers (including Civil &amp; Electrical) to be registered A brief extension is being given till March 31, 2023.
