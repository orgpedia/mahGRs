Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding upgradation of other District Roads/Rural Roads as Major District Roads in Dhule Taluka of Dhule District vide letter under reference. Also regarding the upgrading of the said roads
There is a demand from Zilla Parishad, Dhule.
Considering the traffic volume on the said road, the population of the villages on the road, the use of the road and the demand of the people&#39;s representatives as well as the demand of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the said road was under the consideration of the government to upgrade it as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the following roads in Dhule Taluka of Dhule District are being upgraded.
