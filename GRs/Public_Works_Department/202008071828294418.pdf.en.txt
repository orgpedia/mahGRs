Date: 07th August, 2020
Government Order:
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 4(1), 4(2) and 4(3) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Shri. Vs. L. Kamble, Superintending Engineer, National Rural Health Mission, Mumbai has been transferred in the interest of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience to Shri. Res. T. Patil is filling the vacant post of Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Thane with effect from 31.8.2020 (M.No.) due to superannuation.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on deputation of Superintending Engineer, National Rural Health Mission, Mumbai.
3. Mr. Kamble took charge of his new position. Res. T. Patil should be accepted promptly after his retirement and his assumption report should be submitted to the government without delay. The concerned are informed that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Mr. As Kamble is being transferred for administrative reasons, he will continue to be paid his joining period, travel allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
6. Mr. Kamble to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Opp. Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
