Refer to :- 1) Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune, letter No.Mua(Pune)/Ka-2/Resha/58/2292/2019, dated 28th May, 2019.
Introduction: A proposal for upgradation of P.R.M.No.15 to Mandvi Girvi to Sonmala road as Major District Road in Malshiras Taluka of Solapur District has been received from Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Division, Pune.
Since the proposed road is necessary for communication and traffic safety and speed and also because the proposed road was approved by the Zilla Parishad, Solapur in their general meeting, the upgradation of the said road was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, P.R.M.No.15 to Mandvi Girvi to Sonmala road in Malshiras taluka is being upgraded as a major district road.
