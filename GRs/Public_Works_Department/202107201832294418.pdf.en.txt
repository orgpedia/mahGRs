Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai vide letter under reference has requested to change the layout and length of the existing major District Road-80 in Roha Taluka of Raigad District.
It should be easy for village children to go to school at Birwadi on this road. Also, as the village of Oosar is close to the market for the people on the said road, it was under the consideration of the government to change the route from Birwadi to Tambadiwadi which is missing and replace it with Chanera-Birwadi-Pangloli Oosar to Oosar Fata-Tambadiwadi-Savane-Mohre road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai, the following changes are being made in the design of Prajima-80 in Roha Taluka of Raigad District.
