3. The employee concerned shall not be granted leave for any reason without the prior permission of the Government.
Also M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule - 30 (c) the parent office shall not accept any application for leave from the transferred employee. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the concerned employee by registered acknowledgment of receipt at the original address. If the leave application of the transferred employee is sent to the parent office, the said application will not be considered.
4. In case of non-appearance at the place of transfer immediately as per the said transfer order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by applying any kind of pressure, as such action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, disciplinary action will be taken against them as misconduct.
