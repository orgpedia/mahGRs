Government Order:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4(1), 4(2) and 4(3) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Mrs. Dipali of the Public Works Department. 2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Superintending Engineer, Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Marya., Mumbai.
3. Mrs. Bhaik should assume charge of her new post promptly and submit her assumption of office report to the Government without delay. Mrs. Bhaik m. no Sec. (Period of Joining, Non-Acceptance of Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 shall continue to require joining the posted post within the given joining period. Otherwise the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. 4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and acceptance of charge respectively by the discharged and retiring officers. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.

6. Smt. Bhaik has been posted on deputation in the Maharashtra Civil Service (tenure of recruitment, non-commissioned service and suspension, dismissal and removal from service).
