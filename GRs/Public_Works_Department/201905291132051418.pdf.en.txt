Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads in Sangole taluka of Solapur district as other district roads through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Solapur has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as other district roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the following roads in Solapur district are being upgraded.
