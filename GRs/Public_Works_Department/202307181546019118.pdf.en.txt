Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade major district roads in Khamgaon, Shegaon and Nandura taluks of Buldana district as state highways under a reference letter.
Consider the number of villages, population, road use and demand on these roads.
However, the government was contemplating to upgrade the road as a state highway.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati, the following major district roads in Khamgaon, Shegaon and Nandura taluks of Buldana district are being upgraded as State Highways.
