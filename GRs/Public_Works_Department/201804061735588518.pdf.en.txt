Introduction:
The length of the Thanathuni reach connecting the orange plant at Morshi in Amravati district is 1.400 km. Upgrading this unclassified road to major district road no. 66 has been received by the Government vide the letter under reference from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati.
The planned site of the orange project has been decided near Hiwarkhed village on Amravati-Morshi-Varud-Padhurna road in Morshi taluka. The length of the Thanathuni access road to reach this orange project is 1.400 km. This is the only way available. Upgradation of the said road has been approved by the District Planning Committee, Amravati. Accordingly, by upgrading the Thanathuni access road connecting the said orange project, major district road no. 66 was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the demand of District Planning Committee, Amravati, the length of Thanathuni reach road connecting Orange Project at Morshi in Amravati district is 1.400 km. Upgrading this unclassified road to major district road no. 66 is being included in Consequently major district road no. 66 Named Morshi-Pala-Salbardi-Yawali-Dapori-State Route 47-Thanathuni (Chain No. 0/00 to 16/400) Length 16.400 km. This will happen and 1.400 km in length of major district road grade roads in Amravati district. With this increase, the total length of major district roads in the district is (1980.210 + 1.400) i.e. 1981.610 km. I. That will be it.
