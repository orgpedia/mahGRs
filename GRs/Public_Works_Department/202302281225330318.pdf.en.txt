Introduction : The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of an unplanned road in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also District
Parishad, Satara has demanded to include the said road as a rural road in the Road Development Plan.
Considering the usage of the said road and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, it was under the consideration of the government to include the said roads as rural roads in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Satara, the following unplanned roads in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara District are being included as rural roads for the year 2001-2021.
