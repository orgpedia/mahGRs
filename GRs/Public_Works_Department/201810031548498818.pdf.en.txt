Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding upgradation of 4 other district roads as major district roads in Rahuri Taluka of Ahmednagar district vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population and road usage on the said roads as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar, the following roads in Ahmednagar district are being upgraded.
