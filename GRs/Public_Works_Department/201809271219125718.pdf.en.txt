Introduction
Labor cooperative societies have been given concessions as per the above government decision dated 16.11.2006 regarding construction in Public Works, Rural Development and Water Resources Department.
The question of increasing the said concession was under consideration of the government. As per the government decision dated 23.07.2018, the concession of giving works to labor cooperative societies has been increased. In the said government decision issue no. 2 In (b) of electrical works, the limit of awarding works without tender to the organizations whose work of Electrical and Mechanical Labor Co-operative Society is found satisfactory after 2 years of experience has been increased to Rs.50,000/- instead of Rs.25,000/-. Instead, it was under consideration to increase the limit of awarding works without tender to the organizations whose work of Electrical and Mechanical Labor Co-operative Societies found satisfactory after 2 years of experience from Rs.50,000/- to Rs.3,00,000/-. Accordingly, the following government decision is being issued.
Government Decision 1) Limitation of Award of Works (Construction Works)
a) As per Government Decision dated 26/11/2014 of General Administration Department e-tendering system is to be adopted for works of amount above Rs.3.00 Lakhs.

Institutions should be awarded their quota works up to Rs.3.00 lakhs without calling for tenders in the manner prescribed vide Public Works Department, Government Decision No.LCS 1077/55933/Desk-27 D, dated 30.3.79. Also, these organizations are being increased in the price limit of their quota for the works to be given above Rs.3.00 lakh through e-tendering system.
b) The revised classification of Labor Co-operative Societies for providing works shall be as follows.
Rs.30,00,000 (Rs.30 Lakhs) instead of Rs.15,00,000/- for “A” Class Highest Qualification Rs.15,00,000 instead of Rs.7,50,000 for “B” Class Highest Qualification (Rs.15 Lakhs) c) Labor Co-operative Societies could undertake works up to a total limit of Rs.50 Lakhs in a year. Now this limit is being increased, these institutions can take up works up to a total limit of Rs.100 lakhs in a year. A new labor cooperative can be given 3 or more works up to Rs.30 lakhs for a total of Rs.100 lakhs per annum after successful completion of work worth Rs.15 lakhs in the first instance. The Executive Engineer should make a note in the pass book of the concerned labor cooperative before giving the work order to the labor cooperative and ensure that the total amount of work not exceeding Rs.100 lakh is given in one year from the entry in the pass book of that organization
2) Electrical Works a) Electrical and Mechanical Labor Organizations should now be given work up to Rs.50,000/- instead of Rs.25,000/- initially after registration.
b) The limit for awarding works without calling for tenders to the electrical and mechanical labor cooperatives whose work is found to be satisfactory after 2 years of experience is being increased to Rs.3,00,000/- instead of Rs.50,000/-.
3) Considering the above increase in discount, Public Works Department, Government Decision No.LAB 1098/P.K. 164/ Ima. 2, dated 11th January, 2000, in serial no. 4, the other provision is being amended as follows.
In paragraph 3 (principal amount) instead of Rs.15,00,000/- read Rs.30,00,000/-.
In paragraph 4 (a) (security deposit) instead of Rs.15,00,000/- read Rs.30,00,000/-.
Paragraph 5 (Credit Certificate) should read Rs.30,00,000/- instead of Rs.15,00,000/-.
15,00,000/- should be read instead of Rs.7,50,000/- in paragraph 6 (6) (allocation of work).
