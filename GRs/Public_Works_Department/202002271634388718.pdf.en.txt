Preface: As per the Government decision on the above reference no.1, a total of 79 posts (50 in Amdar Niwas and 29 in Vidhan Bhavan) in various cadres have been created on daily basis for the establishment at Manora and other MLA Residences and Vidhan Bhawan, Mumbai under the Public Works Department. A total of 79 (room attendants-42, laborers-33 and sweepers-4) employees were included. Government Decision Condition No. According to iii) it is stated that the terms and conditions of service as per the collective agreement will be applicable to these employees like the employees of other establishments in the Public Works Department.
After that, as per the provisions of Article 28 of the Kalelkar Agreement, the matter of taking the employees of the qualified daily wage establishments who have served continuously for 5 years to converted temporary establishments, Superintending Engineer, S.B. Board, Mumbai reference no. 2 was under consideration of the Government as per the above proposal. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
GOVERNMENT DECISION As the total eligible 60 employees in the Annexure attached with the said Government Decision are appointed as per paragraph 28 of Kalelkar Sargranthi as they have fulfilled the condition of service for 5 consecutive years from 06.02.2014, they are to be transferred to temporary establishment with effect from 06.02.2019 and accordingly A total financial burden of Rs.42,75,360/- is being sanctioned.
2. The Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Mumbai should take care of correct calculation and not overprovision before paying the dues to the concerned employees and submit the compliance report to the concerned employees by paying their dues immediately.
