Refer to :- 1) Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati letter Ja.No. Ka2(2)/Promotion/2019/1939, dt.09/05/2019.
Introduction:
Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Amravaji has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of unclassified roads in Ner and Darwa talukas of Yavatmal district as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide letter under reference.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as rural roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Yavatmal, the following roads in Yavatmal district are being upgraded as rural roads.
