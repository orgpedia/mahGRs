Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the Government to upgrade the following other district roads in Akkalkuwa Taluka of Nandurbar district as Major District Roads under the reference letter. The Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar, has also demanded that these roads be upgraded.
Considering the number of villages on these roads, the population, the use of the roads and the approval of the Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar, the government was considering to upgrade these other district roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Nandurbar, the following other district roads in Akkalkuwa Taluka of Nandurbar district are being upgraded as Major District Roads.
