Functions of the Committee: -
The Committee shall hold meetings as required to coordinate with various departments of Government of Maharashtra and Government of India to expedite the implementation of the proposed and in-progress projects of MoRT & H and NHAL and remove various bottlenecks in the implementation of the projects.
