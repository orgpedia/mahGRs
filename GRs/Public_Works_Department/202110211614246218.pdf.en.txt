Government order
In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authorities, without posting for administrative reasons, Shri. Shalini Kutemate, Deputy Engineer is posted on the vacant post of Deputy Engineer in the office of Chief Engineer, S.B.P.V., Nagpur.
2. The Controlling Officers concerned are hereby informed to direct Shrim Shalini Kutemate, Deputy Engineer to join immediately at the place of new posting.
3. Shri.Shalini Kutemate, Deputy Engineer should assume charge of his new post promptly, he should not be absent without permission otherwise his absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay, also the possibility of termination of his service cannot be ruled out. Concerned people are being made aware of this. Also their attention is drawn to Government Circular, General Administration Department No.CDR-1082/2567/28/Akra, dated 30.8.1982. In view of the instructions given under the said circular, it should also be noted that if they submit any form of application for cancellation of transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
The Controlling Officers concerned are informed that they should immediately inform the Government of the date of assumption of charge of Shri.Kutemate, Deputy Engineer. If the concerned officer does not join the place of posting within the prescribed period, the Government should be immediately informed about it, so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per order 30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
