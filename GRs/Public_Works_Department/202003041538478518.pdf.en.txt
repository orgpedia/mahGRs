Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Morewadi village of Karveer taluka of Kolhapur district as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 vide the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the usage of the said road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following road in Karveer Taluka of Kolhapur District is being upgraded as a rural road.
