Introduction: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of National Highway 361 from Nanded and Yavatmal District to National Highway 161 A to Ashta Padsa Panganga River Ranidhanora Anjankhed Lonbehal to National Highway 361 in Nanded and Yavatmal District.
Taking into consideration the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad, the following roads in Nanded and Yavatmal districts are being included as state highways.
