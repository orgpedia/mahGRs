Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Nashik, has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade rural roads in Parner taluka of Ahmednagar district as major district roads under a reference letter.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: The following rural roads in Parner taluka of Ahmednagar district are being upgraded as major district roads after considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar.
