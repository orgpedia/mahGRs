As per the provisions of Section 8 (1) (2) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, every department or office is required to publish a citizen's charter. Accordingly, the Citizen's Charter of the Public Works Department was first issued in 2005.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the instructions given by the General Administration Department from time to time, taking into account the reforms and changes made over time, the revised and updated Citizens' Charter of the Public Works Department (itself) is being published along with it. Also, the revised citizen's certificate is available on the website of the Public Works Department www.mahapwd.com and the government's website www.mahapwd.com.
