Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has submitted a proposal to the government regarding upgrading the major district road in Malkapur taluka of Buldana district and making it a state road.
Considering the traffic volume on the said road and the number of villages, population as well as the total usage of the roads, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati, the following roads in Buldana district are being included as State Highways.

The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati has informed that there is no need for land acquisition for the said road, therefore no funds will be made available by the government for land acquisition. Also, the repair / improvement of the said road will be undertaken only after the funds are available to the government. Subject to these conditions, the said road is being upgraded as a state highway.
