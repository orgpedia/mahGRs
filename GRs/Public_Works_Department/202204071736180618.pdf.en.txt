Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the inclusion of the road from Argaon Devmala to Shirvali in Lanja Taluka of Ratnagiri District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21 through the letter under reference.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Konkan and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, the following roads in Lanja Taluka of Ratnagiri District are being upgraded as Prajima.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.No. | Name of proposed road            | Chain no     | Total length to be upgraded (km) | No. after promotion |
| 1.    | Argaon Devmala to Shirvali road. | 0/00 to 5/00 | 5.00 km.                         | Rural Road- 305     |
| Total | 5.00 km.                         |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
As a result the total length of rural roads in Ratnagiri district in Road Development Plan 2001-2021 is 5.00 km. By increasing by , the total length is (6534.00 + 5.00) i.e. 6539.00 km. I. That will be it.
