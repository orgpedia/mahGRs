Circular
The issue of No Objection Certificate for payment of retirement pay and gratuity to Class-1 and Class-2 Technical Officers under Public Works Department has been transferred from Astha-2 office of the department to Service-1 and Service-2 office for administrative reasons. is coming Accordingly, action will be taken to give non-revocation certificate to the retired engineers in the sub-engineer cadre from the Service-2 office and to the Executive Engineer / Superintendent Engineer / Chief Engineer / Secretary from the Service-1 office.
2. After receiving the proposal at the Ministry level, the action at the Ministry level will be completed within 15 days in this matter. The Chief Engineer of the Regional Division should ascertain and issue a certificate and submit a proposal regarding the pending/proposed Divisional Inquiry in the matter. Inquiries should be made to the concerned Divisional Commissioner regarding the non-revocation certificate of Employment Guarantee Scheme (EGS) and a proposal should be submitted accordingly.
The Chief Engineer of the concerned Regional Division will be responsible for the demand for non-removal certificate of the concerned District Officers on deputation.
3. There will be 3 stages of presentation at the Ministry level.
3 days after receipt of proposal in the workshop Under Secretary (Departmental Inquiry) 2 days Joint Secretary (Services) 2 days Secretary (Roads) 2 days Departure 10th day
The said order will come into effect immediately.
