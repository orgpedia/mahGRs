In accordance with the provisions of the Prevention of Delays in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, the Sub-Inspector (Construction) working under the Public Works Department shall be transferred to the post of Deputy Engineer (Construction) mentioned in Annexure-A, in column No. 4 being done here in the place shown before his name.
It is communicated to the Controlling Authorities concerned that they should immediately relieve the Deputy Engineer (Construction) mentioned in the accompanying Annexure by making necessary local alternate arrangements so that he / she can take charge at the place of new appointment / posting and ask him / her to join immediately at the place of new appointment. However, the officers who have been posted to the post of retired / promotional vacancy shall be relieved by making such local alternate arrangements as may be necessary so that the officer concerned may take charge at the place of the new appointment on retirement / promotion.
3. The Deputy Engineer (Architecture) mentioned in Annexure-A to the Annexure shall take charge of the new post forthwith and submit his report to the Government without delay. Also, the transferred engineers shall take care to join the place of transfer within the time of assumption of office as prescribed by the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Retirement, Non-Service and Provisions during Suspension, Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, otherwise, if they do not join the post of transfer during the period of assumption of office, their absence shall be deemed to be non-deceasing. Also, their attention is being drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules 1979. In view of the provisions of this rule, they will be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for transfer of office.
The period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance shall not be admissible to the engineers / officers mentioned in Annexure-A, which have been transferred on their request.
It is communicated to the Controlling Authorities concerned that they should immediately inform the Government of the date of demitting office and assumption of office by the demitting officers. the transfer of the officer concerned within the prescribed period.
