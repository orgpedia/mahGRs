Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Dindori taluka of Nashik district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following roads in Dindori Taluka of Nashik District are being upgraded as Prajima.
