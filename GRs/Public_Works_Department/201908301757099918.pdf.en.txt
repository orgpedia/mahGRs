2. Hon. High Court, Bombay in Petition no. 2797/2015 vide decision dated 4.8.2017 has invalidated reservation in promotion. The State Government has filed Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 in the Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court against the Hon&#39;ble High Court. The said petition is still pending. In this regard, the Law and Justice Department has opined that there is no legal problem in promoting the employees in the open category. Accordingly, at present the vacant posts in the open category in the promotion quota are purely temporarily filled according to seniority. To be paid subject to the decision of the Supreme Court. While taking such action, before promoting the backward class officers/employees in the seniority list, every administrative authority shall ensure that they have not moved higher in the seniority list by getting the benefit of reservation as per the provisions of Government Decision dated 25.5.2004.

Such instructions have been given by the General Administration Department vide letter dated 29.12.2017 to be done by the department.
Accordingly, the promotions of the above mentioned Engineers are being made on a purely temporary basis on the vacant post in the open category in the promotion quota subject to the final decision of the Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed before the Hon&#39;ble Supreme Court.
3. The promotion offered to the above mentioned Engineers will continue for 11 months or till the Maharashtra Public Service Commission sponsored candidates become available, whichever is earlier. In the meantime, if Maharashtra Public Service Commission sponsored candidate is received, since the said ad hoc promotion is temporary, the concerned officers will not have permanent lien on the said post.
4. The promotions of the above mentioned Engineers are purely provisional in nature and they will not get seniority in the cadre of Deputy Divisional Officers as per the said promotion. Also, if necessary, such temporarily promoted engineers will be demoted. Regarding the acceptance of the said condition, written consent should be obtained from the said engineers before taking up the post of promotion.
5. The said promotions are being given subject to the condition that no departmental inquiry is pending or proposed/Court Case/FIR filed against the above mentioned Engineers under Rule 8 &amp; 10 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline &amp; Appeal) Rules, 1979. However, if a departmental enquiry/court case/FIR is filed against any of these engineers on the date of this order, these promotion orders will automatically stand cancelled.
6. Government Notification General Administration Department, No.SRV-2015/P.No.156/Work-12, dated 28.4.2015 and dated 16.7.2015 and revised notification, dated As per provision dated 15.6.2017, the departmental cadre has been allotted and the details of the place/office to which they have been posted after promotion are mentioned against their names in paragraph - 1.
7. The concerned Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works / Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board is requested to release the concerned Junior Engineer (Construction) posted by him immediately from the date of issue of promotion and detailed appointment orders and at the place of new appointment for a minimum period of tenure. Order to join without going on leave or giving notice of cancellation of appointment in any way.
8. If the above Engineers do not appear at the place of detailed appointment given after promotion by 30.9.2019, their promotion orders will be canceled without any notice, assuming that they are not interested in promotion. Concerned please note that no later representations will be considered in this regard.
