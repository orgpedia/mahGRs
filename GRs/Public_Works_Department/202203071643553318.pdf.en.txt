Introduction:
The Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Osmanabad has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Majalgaon taluka of Beed district as major district roads through the letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Beed has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Beed, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Osmanabad and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Beed, the following roads in Majalgaon Taluka of Beed District are being upgraded.
