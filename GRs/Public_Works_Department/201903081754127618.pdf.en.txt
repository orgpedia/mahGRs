Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of National Highway-3 to Arvi Ankawadi to Sonewadi Road and District Road-44 in Dhule Taluka of Dhule District through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Dhule has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume and the number of villages, population, road usage as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the following roads in Dhule Taluka of Dhule District are being upgraded.
