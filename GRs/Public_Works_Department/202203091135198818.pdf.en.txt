Foreword: Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of major district roads in Sengaon, Hingoli, Wasmat, Purna, Kalmanuri and Aundha talukas of Hingoli district to state highways.
Taking into consideration the traffic flow on the said road as well as the overall use of the road, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a state highway.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded, the following roads in Sengaon, Hingoli, Vasmat, Purna, Kalmanuri and Aundha talukas of Hingoli district are being included as state highways.
