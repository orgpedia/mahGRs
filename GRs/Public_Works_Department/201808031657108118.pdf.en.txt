Government Order:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4(4) and 4(5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfers and Prevention of Delay in Performance of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, Smt. V.Pr. Vaidya, Executive Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board,
Amravati is being transferred to the vacant post of Executive Engineer, Special Projects Division, Amravati as per his request and in the interest of public service and administrative convenience.
2. Another officer will be posted separately on the post of Executive Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Amravati.
3. Mrs. Vaidya should assume charge of her new post promptly and submit her assumption of office report to the government without delay. The concerned are aware that they should not be absent without permission otherwise their absence will be treated as unauthorized absence and without pay and the possibility of interruption in their service cannot be ruled out. Also, Mrs. Vaidya&#39;s attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. In view of the provisions of the said rule, they shall be eligible for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30(c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted / granted.
5. Since Mrs. Vaidya is being transferred as per her request, she will not be paid the joining period, traveling allowance and daily allowance admissible as per rules.
6. Mrs. Vaidya to inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Co-ordination Centre, Opp. Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
