Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the road from Winchur Borkund to Nashik district boundary to Zodge in Dhule taluka of Dhule district through the reference letter.
Also, Zilla Parishad, Dhule has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, the population, the road usage and also the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Dhule, the following road in Dhule Taluka of Dhule District is being upgraded as Prajima.
