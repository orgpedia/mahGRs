Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of major district roads as state highways and rural roads as major district roads in Purna taluka of Parbhani district through the reference letter. Also, Zilla Parishad, Parbhani has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the roads as well as the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as state highways and major district highways.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Parbhani, the following roads in Purna Taluka of Parbhani district are being upgraded as state highways as well as major district roads.
