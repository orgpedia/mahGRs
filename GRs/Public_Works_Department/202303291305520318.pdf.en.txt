The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune vide letter under reference has submitted a proposal to the Government to include the unplanned road in Satara Taluka of Satara District as a rural road in the Road Development Plan 2001-21. Also District
Parishad, Satara has requested to include the said road as a rural route in the Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21.
Taking into account the usage of the said roads and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said road as a rural road.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following road in Satara Taluka of Satara District is being included in Road Development Plan for the year 2001-21 as a rural road.
