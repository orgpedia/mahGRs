Government Circular:
Read the above A.No. 1 to 6, the seniority list of the Engineers working in the Assistant Engineer Category-1 (Construction) cadre in the Maharashtra Engineering Service Class-1 and the correction sheets have been published.
Meanwhile Government Notification, General Administration Department, no. The Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 have been promulgated vide SRV-2016/Pro.No.281/ Twelve, dated 21st June, 2021. According to the provisions of the said notification, the matter of publishing the consolidated seniority list of the officers working in the Assistant Engineer Grade-1 (Construction) cadre in the Maharashtra Engineering Service Class-1 was under the consideration of the Government.
2. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the consolidated provisional seniority list of the officers working in the Assistant Engineer Grade-1 (Construction) cadre as on 01.01.2021 is being published as “Annexure-A” attached to this circular. .

3. Errors have been shown in column no.11 against the names of some Assistant Engineers Grade-1 (Construction) mentioned in “Annexure-A”. The responsibility of rectifying the said errors lies with the concerned Regional/ Zonal Office and the concerned Assistant Engineer Grade-1. All errors should be rectified within 8 days from the date of issue of the said circular.
4. The said circular should be brought to the immediate notice of the Assistant Engineer Grade-1 (Civil) under &quot;Annexure-A&quot; by all the Regional and Zonal Offices, who should keep a copy signed by him in their records.
5. Assistant Engineer Category-1 (Construction) included in the said provisional seniority list if any mistakes / errors are found in their particulars in the seniority list like name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority etc., within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. Your representations/objections/suggestions should be brought to the direction of the Govt in writing along with necessary supporting evidence through e-mail (doest1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in) / registered post / speed post / hand bag. If the mistakes/defects are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the prescribed time, the consolidated provisional seniority pay of the officers working in the Assistant Engineer Category-1 (Construction) cadre as on 01.01.2021 will be finalised, assuming that there is no objection.
