4. The responsibility of maintaining the quality of all the works undertaken by the department is primarily that of the Regional Chief Engineer, Superintending Engineer, Executive Engineer, Deputy Engineer and other technical officers. However, the above parameters have been given for inspection by the Vigilance and Quality Control Board in a representative manner and 25 percent of the works are inspected by themselves. Superintending Engineer, Vigilance and Quality Control Board, Konkan Bhavan, Navi Mumbai will be mandatory and it will be necessary to make a clear note on the document.
5 As per the said criteria, the compliance of the points raised by the vigilance and quality control board should be done by the concerned executive engineers of the construction department, it is orderly that the field officers of that department should be directly involved in maintaining the quality of the works. For that, it is the primary responsibility of the field officers to promptly remove the defects pointed out from the inspection conducted by the Vigilance and Quality Control Board or to take necessary action for their redressal and it is mandatory to carry out the same promptly.
6. During the construction of roads, bridges and buildings under various schemes of the Public Works Department, complaints are sometimes received regarding the quality of work or false or excessive measurements. Some of such complaints are investigated by officers of Vigilance and Quality Control Board.
