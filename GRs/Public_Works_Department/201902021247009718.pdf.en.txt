Introduction : Nagpur-Mumbai Rapid Transit (Maharashtra Samriddhi Highway) project is an ambitious project of the state government. Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited is the &quot;Implementing Agency&quot; for the said project. The government has decided to establish Navnagars (Agricultural Prosperity Centers) at 24 places on the said expressway. Accordingly, in accordance with the provisions of Section 113 of the Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966, sites have been notified for new towns to be established at 17 places on the said expressway and for that purpose the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited has been declared as the &quot;New Town Development Authority&quot;.
As per the provision of Land Consolidation Scheme in Chapter III-A of the Maharashtra Highways (Amendment) Act, 2016, there is a provision to acquire the notified land for the said new town through the consent of the landowners or interested persons for voluntary participation in the Land Consolidation Scheme.
Rule 4 of the Maharashtra Highways (Preparation and Implementation of Land Consolidation Scheme) Rules, 2016 prescribes the procedure for land falling under Scheduled Areas. According to sub-rule (1) &quot;If the land intended to be included in the scheme is situated in a scheduled area, the competent authority, before declaring its purpose of the scheme, shall, under the provisions relating to Panchayats (Extension to Scheduled Areas) Act, 1996 (Act No. 40 of 1996), Maharashtra Gram. It shall be repealed as provided in the Panchayat Act (3 of 1959) and the Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Extension to Scheduled Areas Rules, 2014.&quot; There is such a provision.
In Thane and Nashik district, some of the notified land of new towns to be established on the expressway is included in the scheduled area. Taking the matter into consideration, land consolidation scheme for new towns to be established on Nagpur-Mumbai Expressway (Maharashtra Prosperity Highway) as per provisions of Maharashtra Highways (Amendment) Act, 2016 and Maharashtra Highways (Preparation and Implementation of Land Consolidation Scheme) Rules, 2016 Before declaring the intention to include land in Scheduled Areas, the Panchayat Provisions (Extension to Scheduled Areas) Act, 1996 (Act No. 40 of 1996), the Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Act (3 of 1959) and the Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Extension to Scheduled Areas Rules, 2014

According to the provision, the issue of authorizing Additional Collector, Thane for Thane district and Additional Collector, Nashik for Nashik district on behalf of the State Government was under consideration of the State Government to discuss with the concerned Gram Sabha / Panchayati, Panchayat Samiti and Zilla Parishad.
Government Decision:
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Highways (Amendment) Act, 2016 and the Maharashtra Highways (Preparation and Implementation of Land Consolidation Scheme) Rules, 2016, land in the Scheduled Areas should be included in the Land Consolidation Scheme for new towns to be established on the Nagpur-Mumbai Expressway (Maharashtra Samriddhi Highway). Before declaring the purpose, the concerned Gram Sabha / Panchayat as per the provisions of the Panchayat Provisions (Extension to Scheduled Areas) Act, 1996 (Act No. 40 of 1996), the Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Act (3 of 1959) and the Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Extension to Scheduled Areas Rules, 2014 , Additional Collector, Thane for Thane district and Additional Collector, Nashik for Nashik district are being appointed as authorized officers on behalf of the state government to consult with Panchayat Samiti and Zilla Parishad.
Additional Collector, Thane and Additional Collector, Nashik having regard to Panchayat Provisions (Extension to Scheduled Areas) Act, 1996, Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Act, Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Provisions (Extension to Scheduled Areas) Rules, 2014 and orders passed by the Government from time to time in this regard Take action.
The Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Limited should provide the plan of new towns to be established on the Nagpur-Mumbai Expressway (Maharashtra Samriddhi Highway) and related documents to the Additional Collector, Thane and Additional Collector, Nashik for this work.
