Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Amaravati
According to the letter, a proposal has been submitted to the government to upgrade rural roads in Chandur Bazar and Bhatkuli taluks of Amravati district as major district roads. The administrator and chief executive officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati, have also demanded that these roads be upgraded.
Taking into consideration the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the roads as well as the demand of the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Amravati and the demand of the Administrator and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati, the following roads in Chandur Bazar and Bhatkuli taluks of Amravati district are being upgraded as major district roads.
