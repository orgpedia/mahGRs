Government Order:
Reference dt. of Public Works Department. By order dated 21.10.2021 Shri. S. G. Shaikh, Executive Engineer was promoted and post posted to the vacant post of Superintending Engineer, Police Housing and Welfare Corporation, Mumbai (Camp Office, Aurangabad). In the interests of public service and for reasons of administrative convenience, Mr. S. G. Shaikh&#39;s posting is being changed and his revised posting is being made to the newly created post of Superintending Engineer, Public Works Board, Latur. The terms and conditions of the order under reference shall remain in force.
2. Mr. Shaikh should assume charge of his new post promptly and submit his assumption of office report to the government without delay. Mr. Sheikh M. no Sec. (Period of Joining, Non-Accepted Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981 shall be required to join the posted post within the given joining period. Otherwise the period of their absence will be counted as dies non. Also, their attention is drawn to Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
3. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should immediately inform the Government on the e-mail address doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in of the date of resignation and assumption of charge by the deposed and deputized officers respectively. If the officer does not join within the prescribed period, the same should be immediately brought to the notice of the Government so that disciplinary action can be taken against the concerned immediately. M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981 as per rule-30 (c) leave application of transferred officer should not be accepted/granted.
4. Mr. Shaikh will be paid tenure, traveling allowance and daily allowance as admissible as per rules.
5. Mr. Shaikh should inform the Government and Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Opposite Hotel Sagar Plaza, Camp, Pune, of the date of assumption of charge of the newly posted post.
