Purification Sheet:
According to the Public Works Department, Circular Seniority 2010/12/Establishment 1, dated 18.6.2010, the final seniority list of unqualified Junior Engineer (Civil) cadre for the period dated 1.4.2003 to 31.3.2007 has been published. The government has received proposals regarding the inclusion of new names in the seniority list and the inclusion of persons with disabilities.
Accordingly, the final seniority list published on 18.6.2010 for the period of 1.4.2003 to 31.3.2007 of the cadre of Unqualified Junior Engineer (Civil) is accompanied by the relevant statement as Annexure “A”. has been added.
