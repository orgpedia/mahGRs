Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade 8 other district roads and 5 rural roads in Hingana taluka of Nagpur district as major district roads. Considering the increasing traffic and industrialization on the said roads, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Following the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur, the following roads in Hingana Taluka of Nagpur District are being upgraded.
