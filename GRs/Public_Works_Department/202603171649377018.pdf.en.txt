Introduction: - Gadchiroli is a land rich in mineral resources. An "Integrated Steel Project" at this PME is going to create about 15,000 jobs and will also lead to social development of the area. The expansion of the project at Konsari is being completed in a phased manner in the next one or two years and the transportation of equipment from the mine at Surjagad will be substantial for this expansion project.
In order to facilitate the movement of mineral resources to the developed parts of the state at a conveniently and cost-effective cost, the road from Navegaon Mor-Konsari-Mulcherra-Hedri to Surajgarh Mine is required to be taken up for connecting this area with the rest of the state through the Hindu Hriday Samrat Balasaheb Thackeray Maharashtra Samruddhi Highway and the proposed Nagpur-Chandrapur (Seldoh-Navegaon More) Access Controlled Expressway, as well as the Dedicated Mineral Transport Road to connect it with the rest of the country through the proposed Durg-Hyderabad Access Controlled Expressway proposed by the Central Government. The road will help in the all-round development of Gadchiroli district by generating employment and help tribal villages in the region get immediate benefits of other government schemes, including health facilities.
