The Virtual Personal Deposit Account (501) /) system has been implemented for the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and other non-executive authorities for faster disbursement and control of subsidy under 14.02.2024. By this Government decision, the Drawing and Disbursing Officers have been transferred to other Non-Governmental Implementing Authorities through M.C.N.Sample No. Approval has been given for implementation of Virtual Personal Deposit Account (501) /) mechanism B for utilization of funds drawn from the Consolidated Fund of the State for 27-small constructions, 31-subsidized grants (non-salaried), 33-financed, 35-subsidized for construction of capital goods, 50-other expenses, 53-major constructions and other purposes under Object Head as may be announced by the Finance Department from time to time. In this procedure, the disbursement of the above-mentioned Subsidiary Grants, to be fed from the Consolidated Fund of the State, shall be made directly from the Virtual Personal Deposit to the bank account of the respective income, as the case may be, rather than from the current or savings account in the commercial bank of the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Coordinating Mechanism, as the case may be. For this, the Virtual Personal Deposit Account Administrator needs to be activated so that the official payment system in place is used by the Virtual Personal Deposit Account Administrator. Accordingly, the issue of declaring the Chief Financial and Accounts Officer of the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation, under the control of the Public Works Department, as the "Deputy Personal Deposit Accountant" was under consideration of the Government.
