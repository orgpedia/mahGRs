Introduction : The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune vide letter under reference to include seven unplanned roads in Pandharpur taluka of Solapur district as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
These roads are important in terms of communication and transportation. The said roads are unpaved roads and no land acquisition is required for this. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Solapur to include roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21. Therefore, the above mentioned road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Solapur, the following roads in Pandharpur Taluka, Solapur District are being included as rural roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21.
