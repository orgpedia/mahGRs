Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding upgradation of 4 roads in Raver taluka of Jalgaon district as major district roads through letters under reference.
6 villages and 31965 population of Raver and Yaval talukas are connected by said 4 roads.
The said road is under the consideration of the government to upgrade it as a major district road as it is convenient for market, industrial area, transport of agricultural goods, school students, citizens in terms of cultural exchange.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik, the following roads in Raver Constituency of Yaval Taluka are being upgraded.
