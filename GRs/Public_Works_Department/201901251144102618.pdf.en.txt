Introduction : Construction of a new 12-room Rest House at Kolhapur of Public Works Department has been completed recently. During the said construction, there has been an increase in the quality of the rooms as well as the facilities. These cells need to be carefully maintained so that the quality of the work done can be maintained for a long period of time. The proposal to use these rooms for the arrangement of very important persons was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Considering all these matters, the government is ordering that the use of these rooms should be restricted to very important persons. V. to these rooms. i. P room
It should be addressed and reservation of these rooms should be done at the level of Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Kolhapur.
