Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of 2 rural roads and 1 other district road in Mahabaleshwar taluka of Satara district as major district roads as well as the upgrading of Pratapgad Jodarsta Ijima 23 road and including it in Prajima 17. .
Also, Zilla Parishad, Satara has demanded to upgrade and include the said roads.
The said proposed roads are very necessary in terms of communication and to make traffic safe and fast. These roads are very important in terms of traffic as there is a large traffic of tourists and devotees going to and from religious places. Considering the importance of the said roads, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Satara, the following roads in Mahabaleshwar Taluka of Satara District are being upgraded.
