Preface : The Government has received a proposal from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad vide the letter under reference to include three unplanned roads in Ahmedpur Taluka of Latur District as rural roads in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
The said roads are unpaved roads and no land acquisition is required for this. Also there is a demand of Zilla Parishad, Latur to include the said roads in Road Development Plan 2001-21.
Therefore, the above mentioned road development scheme was under consideration of the government to include it as rural road in 2001-21.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Aurangabad and the demand of Zilla Parishad, Latur, in Ahmedpur Taluka, Latur District,
1) Kalegaon to Lanji Road Km. 0 /00 to 3/400 (3.400 km) This road is rural road no.125 2) Kalegaon Boudh Vihar to Chilkha road km. 0 /00 to 5/00 (5.00 km) This road is rural road no. 126 3) Major District Road - 9 Boudh Vihar to Shenkund to Rajyamarg-249 Road Km. 0/00 to 3/600 (3.600 km) this road is rural road no. 127 is therefore being included in the Road Development Plan 2001-21.
