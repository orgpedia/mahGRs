Purchase Committees are to be constituted under each Administrative Department/ Office to enable all the Administrative Departments to carry out the procurement process as mentioned in 2.9.3.
Procurement of spare parts, other than computer sets, printers and other related computer equipment required for office work in various offices under the administrative divisions of the Ministry for the Public Works Department (PWD). The work is done through the purchase of toners, keyboards, mice and mouse pads. Accordingly, the following Departmental Procurement Committees were constituted under the Public Works Department (Computer Room) to procure the above mentioned items.
