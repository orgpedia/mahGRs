Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Chimur taluka of Chandrapur district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Chandrapur has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Number of villages on said roads, population, road usage and district
Considering the resolution of the Council, Chandrapur, the said roads were under consideration of the Government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision:
Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nagpur and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Chandrapur, the following roads in Chimur taluka of Chandrapur district are being upgraded as major district roads.
