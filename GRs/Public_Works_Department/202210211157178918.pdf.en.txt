Other district roads and rural roads in Hingoli, Sengaon and Aundhanagnath talukas of Hingoli district
Regarding upgradation as Major District Road.
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Public Works
Govt Decision No: Ravio-2022/P.No.105/Plan-2
Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 032
Dated - 20th October, 2022
Refer to :- 1) Letter of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded Ja.No./M.A. No./P.A./2266, dated 22/08/2022.
Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of other district roads and rural roads in Hingoli, Sengaon, Aundhanagnath talukas of Nanded district as major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Hingoli has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Taking into account the number of villages on the said road, population, road usage and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli, the government was considering upgrading the said road as a major district road.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nanded and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Hingoli, the following other district roads and rural roads in Hingoli, Sengaon and Aundhanagnath talukas of Hingoli district are being upgraded as major district roads.
