Foreword: The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of rural roads in Niphad taluka of Nashik district as other district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, the road usage and also the resolution of the Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the government was considering upgrading the said roads as other district roads.
Government Decision:
Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following roads in Niphad Taluka of Nashik District are being upgraded as other district roads.
