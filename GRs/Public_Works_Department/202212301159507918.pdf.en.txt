2. The said promotion of the above mentioned officers is being done subject to the following conditions : i. The said promotion is Hon. Subject to the final decision of the Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 and also in Writ Petition Stamp No.10876/2021 and Writ Petition Stamp No.10878/2021 Hon. Subject to the decision of the High Court, Bombay dated 25.5.2021/ in the Writ Petition.
ii. As the said promotion is purely temporary, they will not have any right to regularity and seniority as a result of the promotion.
3. The Government reserves the right to cancel the said promotion to the above mentioned officers without any prior notice or notice.
4. The concerned Comptroller Officers are informed that they should relieve the said officers after promotion by making necessary local alternative arrangements for joining at the place of posting and ask them to appear at the new place of posting. Also, the said officers should be immediately informed on the e-mail id doser1.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in about the date on which the said officers were released to join the place of posting and on which date they appeared at the place of posting.
5. The concerned officials should assume charge of the new post with minimum induction period and submit their induction report to the government without delay. If the officer concerned does not join the post on promotion within the prescribed period of 30 days from the date of this order, his period of absence shall be treated as dies non. Also their attention is drawn to Rule-23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979. Subject to the provisions of the said rule, they shall be liable for disciplinary action if they submit any form of application for change of posting.
6. The concerned authorities should give the option of pay fixation within one month from the date of joining the promoted post. If such option is not given within the prescribed period, their pay will be fixed taking into account the date of promotion as per Rule No.11 (1) (A) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981.
7. All the above mentioned officers have been appointed / posted on deputation posts.
The appointment is made subject to the terms and conditions of deputation prescribed in Appendix-II to the Maharashtra Civil Services (Entry Period, Post-Commission Service and Provisions during Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981.
8. The concerned officers should inform the Government as well as the Director, Information Technology Coordination Centre, Pune, of the date on which they assumed charge on promotion.
