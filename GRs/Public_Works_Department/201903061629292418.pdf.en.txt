Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Kokan has submitted a proposal regarding the upgrading of other district route-112 and including it in the major district route in Rajapur taluka of Ratnagiri district. It has also been demanded by Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri.
Taking into consideration the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population, use of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the Ijima road in Rajapur taluka of Ratnagiri district and include it in the major district route-73.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Ratnagiri, by upgrading Ijima Road in Rajapur Taluka of Ratnagiri District, project no. 73 are being included.
