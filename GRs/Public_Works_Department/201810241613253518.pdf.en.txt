Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government regarding the upgrading of three roads passing through Karveer, Radhanagari, Bhudargarh and Ajra talukas of Kolhapur district as major district roads and including the three roads in the adjacent major district roads. Also, Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur has demanded to upgrade the said roads.
Considering the traffic volume on the said roads and the number of villages, population as well as the total usage of the roads as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the said roads were under the consideration of the government to upgrade them as major district roads.
Government Decision: Considering the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Pune and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Kolhapur, the following roads in Kolhapur district are being upgraded as major district roads.
