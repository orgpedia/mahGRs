Introduction:
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik has submitted a proposal to the government to upgrade the other district roads as major district roads in Niphad Taluka of Nashik district vide letter under reference. Also, Zilla Parishad, Nashik has demanded to upgrade the said road.
Considering the number of villages on the said roads, population, usage of the road as well as the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, it was under the consideration of the government to upgrade the said roads as major district roads.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the proposal of Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Nashik and the resolution of Zilla Parishad, Nashik, the following roads in Niphad Taluka of Nashik District are being upgraded as Prejima.
