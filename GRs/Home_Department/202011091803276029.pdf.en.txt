The proposal:
The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) has been providing concessions in passenger fares to various categories of passengers as per the orders issued by the state government from time to time. Compensation for the cost. 1.04.2016 through Home (Transport) Department. The policy decision has been taken in accordance with the Government Decision dated 25/2/2016. Accordingly, the reimbursement of the travel concession value is being made by the government in cash as well as some amount to the corporation through adjustment of passenger tax.
Due to the lockdown announced in the wake of the Covid-19 pandemic, most of the transport services of the state transport corporation were closed except for essential transport services. As a result, the employees of the corporation have been suffering. In such a situation, the matter of disbursing funds to the corporation was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) has released Rs. An amount of Rs. 79.00 crore (Rupees Ninety-Nine Crores only) is being approved to be provided in cash out of the provision for the financial year 2020-2021. This amount will be adjusted from time to time as an advance from the amount of discounted rates payable to the Maharashtra State Transport Corporation.
