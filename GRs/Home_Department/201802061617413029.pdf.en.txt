Government Circular: The Provisional Common Service Seniority List dated 01.01.2014 of Deputy Superintendent, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail Class-II cadre under the Prison Department of Home Department has been published under Reference No. 2. In response to the objections raised regarding the provisional seniority list, the Additional Director General of Police and Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune, has informed the government about the publication of the final service seniority list of 2014 by amending it under reference number 3.
The objections, errors and retired officers etc. on the provisional service seniority list published under Reference No. 2 of the Deputy Superintendent, Intermediate Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail Class-2 in the Prison Department have been taken into account and revised records have been taken accordingly. Accordingly, the Service Seniority List as on 01.01.2014 is being finalized in Annexure A attached to the Circular. The service seniority list should be sent to all the concerned superintendents immediately.
