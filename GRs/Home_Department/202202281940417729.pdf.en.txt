Mahashivratri 2022 Guidelines
In view of COVID-19, all religious festivals, celebrations and events are being celebrated in a very simple manner this year. As the outbreak of Corona is yet to subside, the following guidelines are being issued in view of the Mahashivratri festival falling on March 1, 2022 this year: 1. Considering the contagious situation of COVID-19, the Government of Maharashtra has issued Circular No. DMU/ 2020/ CR92/ DisM-1, d. 8/1/2022 as well as No.DMU/ 2020/ CR.92/ DisM-1, dt. The instructions given under 31/1/2022 should be strictly followed.
Mahashivratri is considered one of the most important festivals in India. The Hindu festival is celebrated with much fervour across the country. Accordingly, all the temple trustees/ managers need to take special care that there is no crowd in the temple for darshan and social distancing is followed.
3. The concerned Trustees or Managers shall take appropriate measures to ensure social distancing is followed inside every Shiva Temple to prevent the spread of COVID-19. Also, the temple management should ensure that disinfection arrangements, social distancing and hygiene rules (masks, sanitizers, etc.) are followed in the surrounding areas.
The temple manager and the local administration should take special care that there is no crowd of garland and flower sellers in the temple premises on the day of Mahashivratri and the rules of social distancing are strictly followed. Similarly, on the occasion of Mahashivratri festival, limited mandaps should be set up in line with the policy of the concerned local administration regarding mandaps.
5) Devotees who wish to visit the temple in person should use masks and follow social distancing. Senior citizens and children should not be allowed to visit the temple.
6. On the occasion of Mahashivratri, the manager of the Shiva temple should provide the facility of darshan through online, cable network, website and Facebook etc.
To prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus, the rules prescribed by the Government Relief, Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department as well as the concerned Municipal Corporation, Police Administration, Local Administration should be strictly followed. Also, if any other instructions are published after this circular and in the period between the start of the actual festival, they should also be complied with.
