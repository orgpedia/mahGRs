Preface: Mulgaon, Ta. Srivardhan, G. Raigad is a small village where fishing is the main occupation of the locals. The entire stretch of the creek has been filled with silt for the last 40-50 years and the mouth of the creek is narrowing. As a result, it is difficult for fishermen to cross the river. Therefore, fishermen have to wait for the highest tide. This has affected the income of the fishermen. Accordingly, the local fishermen have submitted a memorandum to the Maharashtra Maritime Board to build a wave protection wall at the site and remove the silt from the shipping lane.
