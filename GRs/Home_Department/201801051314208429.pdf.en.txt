Introduction A proposal to make a mobile based game to create awareness on road safety is under consideration and a competition will be organized by NASSCOM and the proposal to award the first 3 developers was under consideration of the government.
The proposal to make a mobile game through NASSCOM (National Association of Software and Service Companies) on road safety awareness and the draft of the agreement to be made with NASSCOM has been examined by the Department of Information and Technology.
Accordingly, financial and administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for making a mobile game on road safety awareness through NASSCOM (National Association of Software and Service Companies) and the draft agreement to be made with NASSCOM and the amount of Rs.2.00 lakh towards management fee to be paid to NASSCOM and the amount of prize money to be given to the first 03 developers at the rate of Rs.5.00 lakh, Rs.3.00 lakh and Rs.2.00 lakh respectively at a total cost of Rs. 12,00,000/- (Rupees Twelve Lakh only).
