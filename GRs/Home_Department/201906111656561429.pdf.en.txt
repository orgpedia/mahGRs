GOVERNMENT ORDER: Mr. S.S. Nimbalkar, Assistant Public Prosecutor, against Mr. A case vide R. No. 3103/2014 was registered under Sections 7, 13 (1) (d) read with 13 (2) of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988. In this crime. Special Judge, Special Court, Buldhana vide order dated 24.05.2018 has acquitted Shri S.S. Nimbalkar. Accordingly, Shri S.S. Nimbalkar has been approved to be reinstated in service vide Government Order dated 20th March, 2019. Now, Shri S.S. Nimbalkar is being appointed to the vacant post of Assistant Public Prosecutor in Beed district.
The appointments are being made with the prior approval of the competent authority in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
3. These orders shall come into force with immediate effect. The Director, Directorate of Grievances should strictly follow the following instructions for the immediate and effective implementation of this order.
(a) Mr. S.S. Nimbalkar, Assistant Public Prosecutor, has been proposed for appointment in Beed district and his posting anywhere in this district shall be done by the Director, Directorate of Grievances.
(b) Mr. S.S. Nimbalkar, Assistant Public Prosecutor shall be called upon to appear at the place of appointment without delay. The report will be submitted to the government soon.
