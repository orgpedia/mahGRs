Preamble: A total of 8 Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been constituted for Mumbai district and a total of 119 posts have been created on the establishment of this Tribunal. The above reference No. A total of 103 posts have been made permanent as per the government decision from 1 to 6. The remaining 16 posts are temporary. 7 As per the Government decision dated 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
These 16 posts have expired on 28.02.2022. The In-Charge Manager, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mumbai has now issued a proposal for extension of the said 16 posts with effect from 01.03.2022 vide Reference No. 8 has been submitted to the government as per the letter here and the matter of extension of these posts was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following 16 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mumbai are being extended from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022. This extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending before the Tribunal are settled expeditiously, the last returns of the settled claims for the month of May, 2022 and August, 2022 are submitted to the Government and if 50 per cent of the pending claims are not found to be settled at the end of 28.02.2022, no further extension will be granted.
