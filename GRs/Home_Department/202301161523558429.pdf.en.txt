Preamble: Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik. 1 here. The Institute is functioning as apex training institute of Maharashtra Police Force and has been granted autonomy vide Government Decision dated 05.02.2016. The Maharashtra Guptavarta Prabodhini, Pune and the Additional Director General of Police/ Director of Communications and Transport, Maharashtra State, Pune (Bintari Sandesh, Pune) are themselves functioning as a specialized training institute. These institutes have been created for technical training, administrative and in-service training in the state police force and are currently functioning as the only such training institutes in the state for a particular category. Therefore, on the lines of the Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik, the Maharashtra Guptavarta Prabodhini, Pune and the training institute of the Additional Director General of Police/ Director of Communications and Transport, Maharashtra State, Pune (Bintari Sandesh, Pune) were under the consideration of the government to be declared as the apex training institute of the Maharashtra Police Force.
The Maharashtra State Intelligence Bureau, Pune and the Maharashtra State Directorate of Information and Communication Technology, Pune (BINTARI SANDESH, Pune) have been approved as apex training institutes of the Maharashtra Police Force.
