Introduction: There has been a persistent demand from the grape farmers and the wine growing sector to waive the excise duty on wine blended from grapes produced in the state. Accordingly, the government has decided to form a committee to discuss the necessary changes in the existing rules to encourage grape growers in the state.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In response to the demands received regarding the waiver of excise duty on wine blended from grapes produced in the state, the following committee is being constituted to consider the necessary changes in the existing rules.

The terms and conditions of the Committee shall be as follows:
(i) To suggest necessary changes in the prevailing rule regarding 100% exemption from excise duty on wine produced by allowing blending of bulk wine produced from grapes in the State with other wineries for making wine.
(ii) To examine the feasibility of granting the exemption granted at Annexure 1 above with retrospective effect (from the year 2006).
The committee has been asked to submit its report within 15 working days.
