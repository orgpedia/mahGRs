Government's decision
48 posts of Non-Mandatory Pediatrician and 20 posts of other different cadres in the establishments of the offices of the Commissioner of Police and Superintendent of Police and other offices under the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, a total of 68 such temporary posts are being approved for extension from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 on the following conditions:
1) The extension will not be applicable to posts lying vacant for more than six months.
2) The terms and conditions under which the said 68 posts have been sanctioned should be strictly complied with.
3) The Director General of Police, Maharashtra should review all the posts and submit the revised figure immediately for the approval of the government. After this, it should be noted that the term of the said posts will not be extended to determine the format.
The expenditure on salary and allowances of the said 68 posts shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the respective heads of accounts for the financial years 2021-22 and 2022-23.
