The Director, Directorate of Forensic Science Laboratories shall strictly follow the following instructions.
a) On receipt of the transfer order, the transferred officers shall be relieved from their present posts. The officers should be recruited without delay after they are relieved from their duties. The officers concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-recruitment of the said officer and payment of salary and allowances by fixing a mandatory waiting period without work.
(b) The date on which the officers concerned were relieved from their duties to appear at the place of new appointment and the date on which they appeared at the place of new appointment shall be intimated to the Government forthwith.
(3) If any officer directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, the action will be considered as misconduct as it is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
