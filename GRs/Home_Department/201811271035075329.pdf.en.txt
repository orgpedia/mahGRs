The Proposal:
INS Viraat was decommissioned from the Indian Navy on 6 March 2017. The ship is currently docked at the Naval Dockyard. Keeping in mind the rich legacy of the Indian Navy, the interest in maritime sector among school/ college youth, maritime training as well as tourism facilities, the Government of Maharashtra was contemplating to acquire INS Viraat from the Indian Navy and convert the warship into a museum and an all-inclusive tourist destination. Accordingly, the following decisions have been taken with the approval of the Cabinet:
Government Decision: Approval is being given to convert INS Viraat into a museum and tourist centre with all facilities and to implement the project on Public-Private Partnership basis.
