Preamble: 1 (one) temporary post in the office of the State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai may be referred to as Reference No. 1 As per the Government decision dated 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. The term of the 1 (one) post of this constable has expired on 28.02.2021. The In-Charge Manager, State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has now issued a proposal for further extension of the 1 (one) temporary post of peon with effect from 01.03.2021 vide Reference No. 2 As per the letter submitted to the Government, the matter of extension of the said post from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1 (one) temporary post of peon in the establishment of State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being extended from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021. This extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending with the State Transport Appellate Tribunal are settled expeditiously, the last statement of claims settled for the month of May, 2021 and August, 2021 is submitted to the government and if 50 per cent of the pending claims are not found to be settled by the end of 28.02.2021, no further extension will be granted.
