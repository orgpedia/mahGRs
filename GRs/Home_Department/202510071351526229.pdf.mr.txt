Government Resolution:
With reference to letter dated 16.09.2025 and 29.09.2025 sent by Candela Technology
AB Sweden to the Hon'ble Minister (Ports and Fisheries), a delegation led by Hon'ble
Minister Ports and Fisheries has been proposed to visit Sweden from 14.10.2025 to
19.10.2025.
2. The objective of the Study Tour is to study / discuss use of electric hydrofoil boats
which is manufactured by Candela using modern technology for sustainable, environment-
friendly and cost-effective water transport system on the coast of Maharashtra State. Thus,
Candela Technology AB, Stockholm Projects has sent invitation to The Hon’ble Minister
(Ports and Fisheries) and deligation of three officers, Government of Maharashtra, Mumbai to visit Stockholm, Sweden between 14“ October to 19" October, 2025. Government
Resolution reffered at No.4 above regarding Hon'ble Minister (Ports and Fisheries) study
tour has been issued seperately
3. The visit will provide an opportunity to explore new avenues of collaboration especially in Maharashtra. The Itinerary from 14" October to 19" October, 2025, including
travelling is provided for Hon’ble Minister (Ports and Fisheries) and his delegation of three
officers. Hon’ble Minister (Ports and Fisheries) has confirmed all the appointments with
various Agencies in the Study Tour.
4. The above mentioned Study Tour to Stockholm, Sweden will allow for valuable discussions with International Maritime Experts along with site visits, Financing Institutions and Industry Leaders to further refine the strategic roadmap for implementing best practices in the State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
5. Considering these potential benefits, the Government of Maharashtra, Mumbai has planned a Study Tour for Hon’ble Minister (Ports and Fisheries) with delegation of three officers to Stockholm, Sweden from 14 October to 19 October, 2025 (Including Travelling).
