The Proposal:
A total of 115 four-wheelers are available at the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Solapur City Commissionerate, 7 police stations and 3 assembly constituencies at the establishment of the Commissionerate.
The Commissioner of Police, Solapur City, had hired 144 private vehicles as the police force did not have enough vehicles to maintain law and order during the Assembly elections. The Deputy Regional Transport Officer, Regional Transport Authority, Solapur, in its meeting dated 10.10.2019, had taken the vehicle on lease from 08.10.2019 to 24.10.2019 as per the rates fixed in the lease deed of the vehicle to be hired for government work. A total of 144 vehicles were seized. 18,90,065/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakh Ninety Thousand and Sixty Five only) was under consideration of the Government for grant of ex-post facto approval and expenditure out of the subsidy available for the financial year 2020-2021.
Government's decision:
For the period from 08.10.2019 to 24.10.2019, the Commissioner of Police, Solapur City, for the purpose of maintaining law and order in the 03 Assembly Constituencies of Solapur City, the Regional Transport Authority, Solapur, has released a sum of Rs. Post facto approval is being accorded to the proposal for release of balance subsidy out of the prescribed head of accounts for the financial year 2020-2021 amounting to Rs. 18,90,065/- (Rupees Eighteen Lakh Ninety Thousand and Sixty Five only).
