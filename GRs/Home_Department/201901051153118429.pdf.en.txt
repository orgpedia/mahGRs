The proposal: The High Court of Judicature at Mumbai, Public Interest Litigation No. 28/2013 (Sh. As per Shrikant Karve v. State of Maharashtra& Ors., a separate programme for revision training of Motor Vehicle Inspectors was conducted in 2016-17.
During the period from April 2017 to till date, some of the Motor Vehicle Inspectors have retired, some officers have been suspended and some officers have been promoted from Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspector Cadre to Motor Vehicle Inspector Cadre. At present, 92 Motor Vehicle Inspectors are working in the Motor Vehicle Inspector Cadre of the Motor Vehicle Department.
Training is still pending. As 80 Motor Vehicle Inspectors out of 92 Motor Vehicle Inspectors are required to be given revision training, excluding the officers who are nearing retirement, it was under the consideration of the Government to provide revision training to these 80 Motor Vehicle Inspectors.
The Government has decided to impart 5-day revision training to 80 officers of Motor Vehicle Inspector Cadre of Motor Vehicles Department at Central Institute of Road Transport (CIRT), Pune and for this, Rs. 3000/- 80 X 5 X 3000= Rs.
