Introduction: Mr. Dattatray Gopal Jadhav, Police Constable Driver No. 3-1517 Additional Commissioner of Police, Motor Transport Department, Nagpada, Mumbai is in the service of MAHA since the date 01.04.1991. Shri Jadhav is drawing salary in the pay scale of S-9 (26400-83600) Pay-41100 in the rank of Police Constable (Driver). Mr. Jadhav has been declared medically unfit for further service by Sir J.G. Group Hospital, Board of Medicine, as he was diagnosed with Paralysis / Paralysis while in Government service. Jadhav from Sir J.G. Group Hospital. A 90% disability certificate has been issued on 10.08.2023. A proposal has been submitted to the government for creation of additional post for Mr. Jadhav as the post equivalent to the post of Police Constable (Driver) is not available in the office of Additional Commissioner of Police, Motor Transport Department, Nagpada, Brihanmumbai. Accordingly, in accordance with the provisions of Section 20 (4) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, a maximum of one post of Police Constable (Driver) in the cadre of Shri Dattatray Gopal Jadhav, Police Constable Driver / 3-1517, with effect from the date 11.10.2021, is liable to superannuation as per his age. The creation has been approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for a period up to 31.05.2036. In pursuance of this approval, the following Government Decisions are being issued.
Government Decision: - The Government is approving the creation of a maximum number of posts in the cadre of Police Constable (Driver) for drawing salary in the pay scale S-9 (26400-83600) of Mr. Dattatray Gopal Jadhav, Police Constable Driver / 3-1517, Additional Commissioner of Police, Motor Transport Department, Nagpada, Mumbai for a period from the date of 11.10.2021 to the date of retirement 31.05.2036 as per their prescribed age.
The post is being created at the establishment of Additional Commissioner of Police, Motor Transport Department, Nagpada, Brihanmumbai.
