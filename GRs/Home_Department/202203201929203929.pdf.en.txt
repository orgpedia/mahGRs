Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti (as per date) 2022 Guidelines
The birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj is celebrated as Maha Shivratri across the country. This year, on Monday. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti (Shivjayanti) will be celebrated on 21st March, 2022 as per the date.
As the corona infection is still going on, the celebration of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti (Shivjayanti), which falls on the same date, should be celebrated by the citizens taking care of their own health and that of others. Following are the guidelines: : 1. Circular No. Maharashtra Government Revenue and Forest, Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation Department. DMU/ 2020/ CR. 92/ DisM-1, Dy. Follow the guidelines as of March 1, 2022.
Covid Appropriate Behaviour rules should be followed while organizing cultural programs, processions and other programs on the occasion of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti.
To prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus, the rules prescribed by the Government Relief, Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department as well as the concerned Municipal Corporation, Police Administration, Local Administration should be strictly followed. Also, if any other instructions are published after this circular and in the period between the start of the actual festival, they should also be complied with.
