Preamble: There are a total of 13 posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune, out of which 9 are permanent and 4 are temporary. These 4 temporary posts are mentioned in Reference No. The period was extended from 01.10.2017 to 31.12.2017 as per the decision of the Government. The said 4 temporary posts have expired on 31.12.2017 and further extension of the said temporary posts with effect from 01.01.2018 has been issued by the Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune vide Reference No. 5 has been requested by the government. The matter of extension of these posts beyond 01.01.2018 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following 4 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune are being extended from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018. The extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending before the Tribunal are disposed of expeditiously, the returns of the settled claims for the month of December, 2017 and February, 2018 are submitted to the Government and if 50 per cent of the claims pending at the end of the month of December, 2017 are not found to be disposed of, no further extension will be granted.
