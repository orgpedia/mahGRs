Semi-Government Letter dated 30-156/2016/ LWE Phase-II dated November, 2018 from Sanjay Singh, Additional Secretary and Administrator, Ministry of Telecommunications, New Delhi
Preamble: The Union Cabinet, chaired by the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, has given its approval to the Scheme for providing mobile services in Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas in the areas identified by the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA). The government had constituted a committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary to look into the issue.
Government's decision:
To provide mobile services in the areas affected by Left Wing Extremism (LWE) in the areas identified by the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) and to finalize the locations before providing infrastructure for mobile towers and to identify the locations for installation of mobile towers and to complete the work within the stipulated time.
