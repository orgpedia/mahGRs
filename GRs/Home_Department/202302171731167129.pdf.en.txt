Read: On the pending work of the Wriston Dedicated Freight Corridor, Ma. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. Meeting held on 15.02.2023.
Preamble: Dedicated Freight Corporation of India Ltd. DFCCIL is the flagship project of Indian Railways. The main objective of the project is to create a robust and efficient rail freight transport system in the country and the Dedicated Freight Corridor consists of two corridors- Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor and Western Dedicated Freight Corridor. The Western Dedicated Freight Corridor (WDFC) will be 1504 km long and will connect Dadri to Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust (JNPT), Navi Mumbai. The 176-km-long corridor will pass through Palghar, Thane and Raigad districts of Maharashtra. For the work of this corridor, it is necessary to expedite the work of land acquisition, payment of compensation for the acquired land, transfer of government, private and forest land, ROB works, shifting of pipelines and electricity poles, etc. in these three districts. To speed up this work. Reviews are conducted from time to time by the Chief Secretary with all the concerned agencies, departments, Collectors etc. Accordingly, Ma. In the review meeting held by the Chief Secretary on 15.02.2023, it was observed that time is being wasted on the works of ROBs and these works need to be done immediately. the Chief Secretary said. To speed up these works, a task force committee will be formed under the chairmanship of Commissioner Konkan Division. the Chief Secretary said. Accordingly, the matter of forming a task force committee was under the consideration of the government. Now the task force is being approved by this government decision as follows.
Government Decision: Under the work of the Versten Dedicated Freight Corridor, the following Task Force Committees are being formed to complete the road works (ROBs) on the bridge in Thane, Palghar and Raigad districts expeditiously and in a time-bound manner.
