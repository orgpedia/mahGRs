The Government has decided to create 15,000 posts of Police Naiks by upgrading the sanctioned posts of constables and 1500 posts of Assistant Police Inspectors by upgrading the sanctioned posts of Sub-Inspectors of Police and these posts have been created with effect from 01/11/1992. These posts were extended from 01/03/ 2019 to 30/09/2019 vide Government Resolution, Home Department No.Police-1915/ PR No.127/ Pol-5A, dated 25/03/2019.
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide the above Reference No. As per the letter, a proposal has been submitted for extension of the said temporary posts with effect from 1/10/2019. The Government hereby grants extension of tenure of the said posts for the period from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020 subject to the following conditions:
(1) None of the above posts has been vacant for more than 6 months and the terms and conditions for which the posts have been sanctioned have been complied with.
2) The terms and conditions laid down in the Government Decision, Finance Department, No. 2016/ Q. No. 8/16/ APC dated 11/9/ 2019 should be scrupulously followed and action should be taken accordingly. Similarly, immediate action should be taken to get the revised format of the office approved by the high-level committee.
(3) If the process of review of posts is not completed within the stipulated time, the temporary posts will not be extended for the next period and if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officers/ employees, the responsibility will be of the concerned establishments.
