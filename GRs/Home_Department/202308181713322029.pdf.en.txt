2. the High Level Committee. As per the minutes of the first meeting in 2023 dated 06/03/2023, a total of 2,650 regular posts in the establishment of the police unit offices of the Additional Director General and Director, Communications and Transport, Maharashtra State, Pune (Motor Transport Department) in the Maharashtra Police Force, and a total of 30 services of manpower to be taken by external agencies, are being approved.
As per the revised figure, the details of regular posts in the establishment of the office of the Additional Director General of Police and Director, Communications and Transport, Maharashtra State, Pune (Motor Transport Department) in the Maharashtra Police Force, as well as the manpower to be recruited by external agencies (designation, pay scale, sanctioned posts) have been attached as shown in Annexure-I to 76.
The revised pattern of posts in the establishment of the office of the Additional Director General of Police and Director, Communications and Transport, Maharashtra State, Pune (Motor Transport Department) in the Maharashtra Police Force is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The limited number of services of manpower referred to in Annexure (b) to this Government Decision are being sanctioned to be taken up by external agencies as per requirement. However, if there are posts currently functioning as dead cadre in that respective cadre, the services should be taken from the external system in proportion to the number of manpower remaining excluding those posts. As the posts in the dead cadre become vacant, the services should be taken by external agencies as per the requirement.
