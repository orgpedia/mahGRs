The Proposal:
The state government has issued instructions to the district administration to curb the sale and consumption of illicit liquor in the state. Effective measures need to be devised to curb the manufacture, transport and sale of illicit liquor.
In this regard, a large number of accidents and human casualties have been reported due to the consumption of alcohol mixed with methanol. Therefore, in order to avoid accidents/ loss of human lives due to misuse of methanol in illicit liquor, it is necessary to determine a suitable mechanism to effectively prevent misuse of methanol by making a comprehensive mapping of methanol produced in the state and imported from foreign countries.
Government's decision:
In order to avoid accidents/ human casualties due to consumption of methanol-laced liquor, the Government has decided to constitute the following committees to determine the mechanism to effectively prevent misuse of methanol by making a comprehensive mapping of methanol produced in the State and imported from foreign countries.

Committee's mandate:
1. To recommend the modalities for ensuring comprehensive mapping of methanol produced in the State and imported from other States, from production point to legal use.
(ii) To recommend consequential amendments in the Toxic Substances Act, 1919, as may be necessary for comprehensive mapping of methanol.
The committee has been asked to submit its report within 2 months.
