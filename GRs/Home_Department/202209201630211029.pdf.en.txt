Date of release: September 20, 2022.
Read: Home Department, Government Resolution No. PRO-0822/ PR No. 139/ VISA-2, Dy. 18th August 2022.
Introduction: Several cases have been registered for violation of legal instructions during the period of Ganpati and Dahi Handi festival in the state. A committee has been formed to take action on the government's decision. The government was considering a partial change in the composition of the committee.
Government Decision: Offences in which there is a violation of legal instructions during the Ganpati and Dahi Handi festival in the state. The following revised committee is being constituted to take action as per the legal provision of withdrawal of cases where chargesheets have been filed till 31/03/2022.
