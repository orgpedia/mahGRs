Preamble: Rajarampuri, Kolhapur district. S. No. 136/2019. Adv. Avdhoot Chimalkar has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor to effectively represent the Government in the judicial matters in the Special MCOCA Court, Pune. Special Judge, Pune. The matter of sanctioning professional fees to Mr. Avdhoot Chimalkar was under consideration of the Government.
Government decision: Adv. Mr. Avdhoot Chimalkar, who is handling the court proceedings on behalf of the Government in the Special MCOCA Court, Pune, is being allowed to pay professional fees as under:
Special MCOCA Court, Pune (a) Effective Court Hearing Fee per day- Rs.30,000/- (Rupees Thirty Thousand only) (b) Discussion Fee (per hour) (without ceiling condition)- Rs.3,000/- (Rupees Three Thousand only) (c) Travel Expenses- Rs.3,000/- (Rupees Three Thousand only)
The payment of professional fees admissible to Mr. Avdhoot Chimalkar should be made after every two months of court proceedings. Effective hearing days of the court proceedings shall not be less than the rank of Superintending Deputy Superintendent of Police of the concerned case. The Deputy Superintendent of Police concerned shall ensure that the same is certified by the Manager of the Court and no additional payment of any kind is made to Advocate Mr. Avdhoot Chimalkar.
