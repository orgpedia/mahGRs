The Home Department of four states- Gujarat, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh and Telangana- had been requested to provide additional police and home guard strength to ensure the conduct of the upcoming Assembly elections 2019 in the state in a safe and peaceful manner. Accordingly, it has been agreed to provide 20,000 Home Guards by these States at a cost of Rs. The matter of approval to make available an amount of Rs. 8,77,09, 700/- as advance was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Home Guards were requisitioned from Gujarat, Karnataka, Telangana and Madhya Pradesh to ensure safe and peaceful conduct of the upcoming Assembly elections in the state.
Accordingly, the concerned states have agreed to provide up to 20,000 Home Guards. They have also been asked to pay the advance amount. The total amount of advance required as per the assessment made by the respective States through this Government decision is Rs. 8,77,09, 700/- (Rupees Eight Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs Nine Thousand Seven Hundred only) are being sanctioned to the respective States.
