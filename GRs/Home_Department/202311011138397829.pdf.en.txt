Subject:- Approval of the Accounting Committee for obtaining interest free loan under Part-5- Housing for Police Personnel above or as part of Police Station in urban areas, for the year 2023-24 under the Special Assistance Scheme for Capital Expenditure to the States of the Central Government.
Reference 1. Letter from Finance Department No. 2023/ Q. No. 09/ Finance Force, dated 12.04.2023.
The date of the decision of the Government of the Synchronous Number of this Department is August 1, 2023.
The Proposal:
Under the Scheme of" Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment," the Central Government is providing financial assistance to the State Governments in the form of 50 years non-interest bearing loan for capital investment projects. Under this scheme, for the year 2023-24, for the Central Government loan available under Part-5- Housing for Police Personnel above or as part of Police Station in urban areas, Rs. A proposal of Rs. 770.15 crore has been submitted. Accordingly, the proposal has been submitted to the Central Government through the Finance Department and accordingly, the Central Government vide order dated 10th August, 2023 has sanctioned Rs. Two works worth Rs 233 crore have been approved. The first instalment of Rs. 116.50 crore has been released by the Central Government vide order dated 29.09.2023.
2. Separate head of accounts has been notified for this scheme. The Central Government has allotted Rs. 116.50 crore is to be disbursed to the implementing agencies. The government was considering opening a new account head with the approval of the Accountant General and the Finance Department to disburse the funds.
Here's what has been decided:
The government's decision:
Under the Scheme of" Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment," the Central Government is providing financial assistance to the State Governments in the form of 50 years non-interest bearing loan for capital investment projects. Under this scheme, the government is approving the opening of separate head of accounts for the Central Government loans available under Part-5- Housing for Police Personnel above or as part of Police Station in urban areas, in the year 2023-24.
