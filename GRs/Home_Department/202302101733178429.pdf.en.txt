Preamble: GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF GENERAL ADMINISTRATION, NO. BCC-2018/ Q.No. 308/16-B, dated 21.12.2019. MCT-0221/ PR No.37/ Para-5, dated 09.02.2022, has created a supernumerary post of Clerk-Typist from Group-C cadre, appointed from Scheduled Tribes category on the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mumbai, an office under this Department, but who could not produce caste validity certificate. General Administration Department Referral No. 4 Paragraph 2 of the Government Decision here states that" Government officers and employees holding superannuation posts shall, from a humanitarian point of view and having regard to the services rendered by them, be given a technical break of 1 day and again continue in service for a period of 11 months till the date of their retirement." As per the provisions of the said Government Order, the matter of extension of tenure of appointment to the office of the Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As mentioned in para-II of the Government Decision dated 14.12.2022 of the General Administration Department, Smt. Alka Sanjay Raikwad, Clerk-Typist, who was appointed to the supernumerary post created in the office of the Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mumbai under the Transport Department, is being approved by this Government Decision to continue her service for a further period of 11 months till the date of her retirement by giving a technical break of 1 day after the expiry of the period of 11 months of her appointment to the supernumerary post.
2. m. The Chairman, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mumbai shall take necessary action as per the instructions given in the Government Order dated 14.12.2022 in the context of General Administration Department and submit a report to the Government.
The said Government Decision, General Administration Department No. BCC-2020/ PR No. 187-O/ 16-B, dated 30.06.2020 and No. BCC-2019/ S.No.581A/ 16-B is being issued under the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide Government Resolution dated 14.12.2022.
