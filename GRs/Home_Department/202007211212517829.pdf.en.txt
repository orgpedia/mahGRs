Closed on 29/02/2020. The decision was taken by the Finance Ministry. The temporary posts included in the figure as on 12/02/2020 and all existing temporary posts created after approval of the earlier review but not included in the figure, will be filled up with effect from 01/03/2020. The administrative departments have been empowered to extend the deadline till 31/08/2020. Accordingly, the temporary post of Deputy Director General in the establishment of the Office of the Director General Home Guards Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has been made vacant with effect from 01/03/2020. The matter of extending the deadline till 31/08/2020 was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: The Pay Scale of Deputy General Manager (Rs. 67,000-79, 000+ HAG) Pay Matrix Level 15 (7th Pay Commission) in the establishment of the Office of the Director General Home Guards, Maharashtra State, Mumbai will be from 01/03/2020 to 01.03.2020. The deadline has been extended to 31/08/2020.
