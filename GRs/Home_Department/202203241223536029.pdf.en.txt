The Proposal:
As requested by the Commissioner of Transport, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, vide letter No. 3 above, the matter of administrative and financial sanction of expenditure for training of 100 newly appointed Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors of the Motor Vehicles Department at the Central Institute of Road Transport, Pune (CIRT, PUNE) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Central Institute of Road Transport, Pune (CIRT, PUNE) has invited applications for the 8 weeks training of the newly recruited 5th batch of 100 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors in the Motor Vehicle Department. Administrative and financial sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 1,84,80, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Four Lakh Eighty Thousand only).
