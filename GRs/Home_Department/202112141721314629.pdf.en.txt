The Proposal:
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has issued the above Reference No. As per the letter dated 16.06.2021, a proposal has been submitted to create a new Urlikanchan Police Station by bifurcating the Loni Kalbhor Police Station under the jurisdiction of the Superintendent of Police, Pune Rural.
Accordingly, reference No. 2 here. In view of the approval given by the Committee in the minutes of the 7th meeting of the High Level Committee of Secretaries held on 05/10/2021, the Government has decided as follows:
Government's decision:
The state government has decided to bifurcate Loni Kalbhor police station under the jurisdiction of the Superintendent of Police, Pune Rural, and create a new police station at Urlikanchan.
2.To create a total of 100 regular posts of various cadres mentioned in the" Annexure-A" with this Government decision for the newly created Urlikanchan Police Station and to provide Rs. Annual recurring expenditure of Rs. 6,48,27, 300/- (Rupees Six Crores Forty Eight Lakhs Twenty Seven Thousand Three Hundred only) is being approved. Also, out of the 100 posts, 25 posts of Police Naik will be filled in future as per the policy decision.
3. Annexure-B to the said Government Decision for the newly created Urlikanchan Police Station
As mentioned, Rs. Non-recurring expenditure of Rs. 35,41,500/- (Rupees Thirty Five Lakhs Forty One Thousand Five Hundred only) is being approved
