The Proposal:
Reference No. Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district for a period of two years. Reference No. A total of 12 new posts have been created under the government decision. Reference No. Under the Government decision, 2 additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals and 12 temporary posts have been extended from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020. Since the extension of these 12 posts is expiring on 29.02.2020 and as per Reference No.
As the Chairman, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur, vide letter dated 14.02.2020, had submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of tenure of two additional Tribunals and a total of 12 temporary posts therein, the matter of extension of tenure of the said posts from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals set up at Nagpur and 12 temporary posts created for the same are being extended from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. The extension is subject to expeditious disposal of the claims pending before the Tribunal, submission of the final returns of the settled claims for the month of May, 2020 and August, 2020 to the Government, and no further extension of time in case 50% of the claims pending at the end of February, 2020 are not found to be settled.
