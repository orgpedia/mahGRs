GOVERNMENT DECISION: As many as 1,101 temporary posts in various District/ City Police Establishments in the Highway Safety Squad of the State were extended till 29.02.2020 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Now, vide this Order, the Government is granting extension of tenure of 1,101 temporary posts in various District/ City Police Establishments in the accompanying prospectus from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020 subject to the following conditions:
The extension will not be applicable to temporary posts (excluding executive posts) lying vacant for more than six months.
2. The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned subject to the terms and conditions to be followed.

If the process of reviewing the posts is not completed within the prescribed time, if the above temporary posts are not extended for a further period and if the question of salary and allowances of the officers/ employees arises, it will be the responsibility of the concerned office.
