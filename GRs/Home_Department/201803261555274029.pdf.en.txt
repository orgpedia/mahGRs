Introduction- A total of 812 temporary posts in the office of the Transport Commissioner and various regional transport offices in the Motor Vehicle Department have already been filled through a separate government decision.
The period was extended till 30.09.2017. These positions are currently available for 2018-2019. 01.01.2018 to D. The matter of extension for the period up to 30.09.2018 is under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
