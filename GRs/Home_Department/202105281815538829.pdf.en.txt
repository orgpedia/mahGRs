The Proposal:
Criminal Writ Petition (Stamp) No.
2934/2021 (Meena D'Sa Vs. State of Maharashtra& Ors.), the Government was considering the matter of providing professional fees to Mr. Anil Sakhare, Special Public Prosecutor appointed to handle the judicial proceedings.
Government decision: Criminal Writ Petition (Stamp) No. 2934/2021 (Meena D'Sa Vs. State of Maharashtra& Others) approving payment of professional fees to Mr. Anil Sakhare, Special Public Prosecutor appointed to conduct the judicial proceedings of the case as under:
High Court of Bombay, Mumbai
Daily court fee of Rs. 3,50,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh Fifty Thousand only) Discussion Fee (Per Hour) and Drafting Fee- Rs. 50,000 (Rupees Fifty Thousand only) and no other benefit other than the said professional fee shall be admissible to Mr. Anil Sakhre. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.
The payment of admissible professional fees to Mr. Anil Sakhre shall be made after every two months of the judicial proceedings of the said case. Effective hearing days of judicial work shall be provided by an officer not below the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case. The Deputy Commissioner of Police concerned should ensure that the court managers are checked and certified and no additional payment is made to Adv. Anil Sakhare.
