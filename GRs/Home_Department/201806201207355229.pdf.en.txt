The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, has submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for repairing the parapet wall of the buildings at Police Colony, Tardeo.
2. Deputy Engineer, Public Works Sub-Division No. 2, Worli, prepared as per the State Rate List for the year 2017-18, Rs. 23,69,846/- has been submitted. These cost estimates have been certified by the Executive Engineer, Central Mumbai (Public Works) Department, Mumbai.
3. Estimates of the said expenditure have been examined by the Public Works Department, Ministry and it is estimated to be Rs. 23,69,846/- has been amended and agreed to grant administrative approval, subject to certain conditions.
4. Accordingly, for repair of parapet wall at Police Colony, Taddeo, Rs. Administrative approval is hereby accorded to the cost estimate of Rs. 23,69,846/- subject to the following conditions: 1) The work should not be started without the availability of funds; 2) The cost estimate should be certified by the Joint Commissioner of Police (Administration), Brihanmumbai.
The cost of the said work shall be met from the grants available under the Home Department- Demand No. B-1, 2055, Police, (02) City Police, (02) (01) City Police-Establishment (Dattamat) (Mandatory) (2055 0097), 27, Small Constructions.
6. This memorandum was submitted to the Informal Reference No. 314/ IMA-2, dated 11.06.2018, is being issued with the concurrence received.
