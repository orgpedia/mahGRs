GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Ministry of Finance, Government of India vide its Office Memorandum dated 23.03.2021 has directed to adopt a revised methodology for effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in more effectiveness in the process of allocation of funds. Pursuant to the Office Memorandum dated 23.03.2021 of the Ministry of Finance, Government of India, the Department of Finance, Government of Maharashtra, Ministry of Finance, Government of Maharashtra, vide Government Circular dated 01.07.2021, has issued revised instructions regarding Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
The Department of Finance, vide Government Circular dated 01.07.2021, has issued instructions to open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the authorized bank for the scheme by appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) as per the revised procedure for implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme. Accordingly, Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, Pol-4 is being designated as Single Nodal Agency (SNA).
