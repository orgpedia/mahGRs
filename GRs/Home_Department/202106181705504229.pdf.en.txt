The cabinet has decided to form a sub-committee to suggest measures to improve the financial condition of the state transport corporation.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Home Department
Government Resolution No. STC-1020/ P.No.370/ Para-1
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: June 18, 2021.
Preamble: The State Cabinet As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 20.04.2021, the Government was considering the formation of a Cabinet Sub-Committee as follows to suggest comprehensive measures to improve the financial position of the State Transport Corporation.
Government Decision: A Cabinet Sub-Committee is being constituted to suggest comprehensive measures to improve the financial position of the State Transport Corporation.
1) Ma. Deputy Prime Minister- President
2) Ma. Minister of Transport- Member
3) Ma. Minister of State (Public Works)
Additional Chief Secretary (Transport)- Member Secretary
2.The Committee shall make a comprehensive review of the financial position of the State Transport Corporation and submit a report on the measures to be taken.
The Vice-Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation will be required to provide documents related to the subject to the Member Secretary during the meeting of the said Sub-Committee.
