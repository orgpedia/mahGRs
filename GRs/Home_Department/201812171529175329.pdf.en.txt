The Proposal:
The seven probationers of the 70th batch of the Indian Police Service (IPS) have been posted in Maharashtra. 12.11.2018 to D. A proposal to create seven supernumerary posts of Assistant Superintendent of Police in the Indian Police Service in the junior pay scale i.e. Level 10 of the Pay Matrix for training in the State of Maharashtra during the period up to 24.05.2019 (6 months 12 days) and to appoint these seven probationary officers has been approved. 12.11.2018 to D. Salary and Allowances for the period 24.05.2019 (6 months 12 days) Rs. 43,17,222/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakhs, Seventeen Thousand, Two Hundred and Twenty Two only) was under the consideration of the Government.
The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following seven probationary officers of the 70th batch of the Indian Police Service have been appointed as probationers in the State of Maharashtra, i.e. 12.11.2018 to D. The Government is approving creation of seven supernumerary posts of Assistant Superintendents of Police in the State of Maharashtra for training up to 24.05.2019 (6 months 12 days).
