Government's decision:
The Departmental Promotion Committee has been reconstituted under the Government decision dated 16.07.2016 to prepare the selection list for promotion to the posts of Group-A and Group-B cadres in the State Excise Department. Shri G. L. Pawra, Joint Secretary, Home Department, who was a member of this committee as a representative of the backward classes, has died prematurely. Therefore, Mr. Pavara as the representative of the backward class in this committee
Shri P. G. Deshmukh, Joint Secretary, Home Department is being appointed in his place. The composition of the committee will be as follows:
Principal Secretary, State Excise, Home Department: Chairman
Commissioner, State Excise, Maharashtra State, Mumbai: Member
Shri P. G. Deshmukh, Joint Secretary, Home Department: Member (representative of Backward Classes) 4) Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Raushu), Home Department: Member Secretary
