Introduction: The State of Maharashtra The Chief Minister has announced on 16.08.2022, on the occasion of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," to allow free travel concession in all types of buses of State Transport Corporations to senior citizens above 75 years of age in the State of Maharashtra." Accordingly, the Vice-Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai issued Reference No. A proposal has been submitted to the government through 9 letters.
Accordingly, the government was considering allowing senior citizens of Maharashtra to travel in all types of buses of the state government.
The Maharashtra government has decided to allow senior citizens of the state to travel in all types of buses of the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation. Accordingly, 1) Senior citizens above the age of 75 years in the state of Maharashtra will be allowed free travel concession in all types of buses of the State Transport Corporation.
50% concession in all types of buses of the State Transport Corporation will be allowed to senior citizens of the state of Maharashtra from 65 years to 75 years.
2) For this, the identity cards of senior citizens like Aadhaar card, election ID card, identity cards issued to retired officers and employees of the central/ state government (but photo, date of birth, residential address is mandatory), passport, driving license and smart card issued by the National Population Register (NPR) will be accepted.
