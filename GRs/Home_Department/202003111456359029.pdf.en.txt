Government Decision: Tribal Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai. As per the Government decision dated 13.02.2020, for the financial year 2019-20, under the scheme of Modernization of Bus Stations of State Transport Corporations in Tribal Areas under Tribal Appropriation, the demand No. T-5, 3055, Road Transport, Schemes under Five Year Plans, 00-Road Transport, 796 Tribal Area Sub Plan, (01) (01) Development, Construction, Repair of ST Depots in Tribal Areas (3055 0043), 31 Subsidiary Grants Rs. 100.00 lakh has been provided in the Budget. The Finance Department's
Approval has been given for disbursement of funds to the extent of 60% of the budgetary allocation for the programme expenditure plans for the financial year 2019-20. In pursuance of the said circular, the Tribal Development Department (30550043), under the 31-Support Grants-in-Aid, has sanctioned Rs. 60.00 lakh (Rupees Sixty Lakh only) has been sanctioned to be released to the Principal Secretary (Transport), Home Department, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai as administrative department on Budgetary Fund Distribution System (BEAMS).
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to disburse the above funds and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in his office shall take action to recover the amount and pay it to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation.
