By dividing the Talbid Police Station on the establishment of the Superintendent of Police, Satara, as well as the Umbraj Police, as per the letter dated 29.01.2021
A proposal has been submitted to convert the Masur remote area into a new Masur police station. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: It is being approved to create a new Masur Police Station by bifurcating the Talbid Police Station on the establishment of the Superintendent of Police, Satara and converting the Masur remote area into Umbraj Police Station.
2. To operationalize the new Masur Police Station at present, it is being approved to provide a total of 30 posts of Police Sub-Inspector-01, Police Constable-03 in Masur remote area and 01 Assistant Police Inspector, 03 Assistant Sub-Inspector, 03 Police Constable, 05 Police Naik, 14 Police Constable in Police Headquarters/ other Police Stations. So there will be no extra cost.

At present, the Police Remote Zone is functioning at Masur and the administrative building of the Masur Remote Zone is available at this place. Approval is being given to start a new Masur police station with the available building and equipment of this far-flung area. So there will be no extra cost.
4. Necessary vehicles for New Masur Police Station should be provided from existing sanctioned vehicles.
5.The expenditure for the remaining necessary equipment for the new Masur Police Station should be met from the available grant of the Superintendent of Police, Satara.
6. The Director General of Police should submit a proposal to the Government immediately.
7. The said Government Decision was passed by the Finance Division No. 1/ 2015/ No. 41/ Finance-1, D. being issued as per the powers conferred on the Government vide 02.06.2015.
