The Proposal:
Home Department As per the Government Decision dated 26.8.2014, various development works in the Naxal affected areas have been taken up by the State Government. The committee is headed by the Chief Secretary. A cabinet sub-committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister and Rs 50 crore has been handed over to this committee. As per the Government Resolution dated 24.10.2017 of the Home Department, the amount sanctioned by the Shakti Parbandhan Committee and the Cabinet Sub-Committee is Rs. 31,89,23, 600 (Rs. The amount is to be paid to various departments of the central government as well as the Public Works Department of the state government. Without the payment of this amount in advance, it will not be possible to complete the sanctioned works within the stipulated time. The matter of payment through abstract bill instead of detailed bill was under the consideration of the government for receiving this amount within the prescribed period. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The amount sanctioned by the Shakti Parbandhan Committee and the Cabinet Sub-Committee for various development works in the Naxal affected areas under the Government decision dated 24.10.2017 of the Home Department is Rs. 31,89,23, 600 (Rupees Thirty One Crore Ninety Nine Lakh Twenty Three Thousand Six Hundred only) out of which the sanction for serial number 3 in the Government decision dated 24.10.2017 has been given.
