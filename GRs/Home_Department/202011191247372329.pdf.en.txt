Theme- Chhath Puja 2020 Guidelines
circular
North India in the midst of Covid-19 pandemic
People should celebrate this year's Chhath Puja in a simple manner.
The guidelines are as follows: :
1) In order to prevent the spread of corona infection, citizens should avoid crowding at the lake/ sea shore and celebrate Chhath Puja in a simple manner by staying at home.
2) Artificial ponds should be created with the help of municipal corporations, public representatives, NGOs, etc. Also, proper measures should be taken in terms of safety and hygiene to prevent the spread of corona.
3) No pandals of any kind should be erected at the place of Chhath Puja celebration. No religious and cultural programmes will be held. Fireworks and fireworks are prohibited.
4) North Indian citizens should use masks and follow social distancing during Chhath Puja, and senior citizens and children should not be brought to the venue. Organisers should create awareness on the issue.
5) Sanitization arrangements should be made at the venue of the Chhath Puja program. Special attention should be paid to social distancing and hygiene rules (masks, sanitizers, etc.).
6) To prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus, the rules set by the Government Relief and Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department, as well as the concerned Municipal Corporation, Police, Local Administration from time to time should be strictly followed. Also, if any new instructions are published after this circular and in the period between the actual day of the festival, they should also be complied with.
