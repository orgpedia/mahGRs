The Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, under the Scheme" Special Infrastructure Scheme (SIS), including construction of 250 Fortified Police Stations in LWE affected States," vide letter dated 15.03.2018, has sanctioned Rs. 3 crore (Rupees Three Crores only) has been disbursed as" ad hoc advance" for the financial year 2017-18. Government Resolution, Home Department, No. PWF-0118/ S.No.58/ Pol-7, dated 23.08.2018 for the said amount of Rs. 3 crore has been approved for disbursement and expenditure, subject to certain conditions.
The State Government's share (40%) under the scheme is Rs. 2 crore is being sanctioned subject to the condition that the said funds shall be spent only for the purposes as approved under the said scheme in the meeting of the Project Clearance Committee (PCC) of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, dated 13.06.2018.
The said expenditure should be met from the grants available during the current (2018-19) financial year for the purpose of" 53-Big Constructions" under the head of Home Department-Demand No.B-10, 4055, Capital Expenditure on Police, (00) 207, State Police (00), (00) (04) Construction of well equipped Police Stations in Naxal affected areas (40% State share) (Mandatory) (40550214).
Deputy Inspector General of Police, Gadchiroli Range, Mukkam-Nagpur, is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the purpose of withdrawing the said amount from the treasury and providing it to the concerned.
