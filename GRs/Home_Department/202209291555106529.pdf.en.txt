The Proposal:
Government Decision As on 07.02.2015, considering the nature and scope of work of the officers of the State Excise Department, as per the recommendation of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee, vehicles have been made admissible for 270 posts. The government also decided. As on 13.02.2019, one new vehicle has been sanctioned for the Superintendent of the newly created Palghar district. According to this, vehicles are permissible for a total of 271 posts in the state excise department. The government then took the decision. As on 05.01.2022, two new Regional Divisions namely Amravati and Nanded have been created in the State Excise Department and 42 new posts have been created in various cadres. Out of these new posts, the government was considering to make vehicles permissible for 4 posts of Divisional Deputy Commissioner, Amravati and Nanded and Inspector, Bharari Squad, Amravati and Nanded. The following decision has been taken:
Government Decision: The Government is giving approval to make vehicles permissible for the following posts in the newly created Divisional Deputy Commissioner Amravati and Nanded offices of the State Excise Department.
