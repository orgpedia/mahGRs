2. The High Level Committee of Secretaries. As per the minutes of the 5th meeting held on 13/12/2022, a total of 68 regular posts in the establishment of the office of Additional Director General of Police and Director, Communications and Transport, Maharashtra State, Pune in the Maharashtra Police Force, and a total of 41 services of manpower to be taken by external agencies, are being approved.

The revised pattern of posts in the establishment of the office of the Additional Director General of Police and Director, Communications and Transport, Maharashtra State, Pune Police Unit in the Maharashtra Police Force is subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) The limited number of services of manpower referred to in Annexure-A, B, C to this Government Decision are being sanctioned to be taken up by external agencies as per requirement. However, if the posts are presently functioning as dead cadre in the respective cadre, the remaining manpower excluding those posts shall be retained.
