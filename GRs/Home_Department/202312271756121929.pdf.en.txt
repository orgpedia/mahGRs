Preamble: As per the aforesaid letter dated 19/06/2023 from the Home Secretary, Government of India, the Central Government has planned to provide bail to the prisoners who are unable to pay the fine and are unable to get bail due to financial constraints. Guidelines and Standard Operating Procedures have been laid down for successful implementation of the scheme. 2 has been sent to the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune for necessary action. Further, as mentioned in the said guidelines, an Oversight Committee has been constituted at the Government level under section 3 of the above mentioned' Readings." However, in view of the confusion being created at the field level regarding the composition of the Empowered Committee at the district level as mentioned in the Guiding Principles, it was under the consideration of the Government to give clear instructions in this regard. This is explained below.
Government Decision: For the successful implementation of the scheme designed by the Central Government to provide bail to prisoners who are unable to pay the fine and are unable to get bail due to financial difficulties, the following" Empowered Committee" should be formed at the district level.
