Mr. Pradeep Gharat, Special Public Prosecutor in this case as follows.
1.Effective hearing fee per day. High Court of Bombay- Rs. Effective hearing fee of Rs.20,000/- per day. Sessions Court, Alibaug/ Panvel- Rs. 15,000/ 3. Discussion Fee Rs. 3,500/ 4. Ma. The cost of travelling to court
