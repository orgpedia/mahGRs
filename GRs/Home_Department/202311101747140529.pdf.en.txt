The Proposal:
The construction of Ro-Ro jetty at Borivali, Mumbai Suburban has been completed and Ro-Ro service is running here. Borivali is a small village in the northern part of Mumbai. At present, the jetty is being used for passenger water transport. Borivali is a popular tourist destination, which attracts a large number of tourists. However, parking of vehicles has become a problem. Hence, vehicles can be parked in the parking lot. Because of the high volume of passengers arriving here, there is a need for a terminal building for passengers. Accordingly, Rs. The Maharashtra Maritime Board has submitted a proposal for administrative approval for the budget of Rs. 11,99,72, 128/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Ninety Nine Lakh Seventy Two Thousand One Hundred and Twenty Eight only) and the proposal was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Construction of Terminal Building adjacent to Ro-Ro Jetty at Borivali, Suburban Mumbai, Construction of Parking Facility, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 11,99,72, 128/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Ninety Nine Lakh Seventy Two Thousand One Hundred and Twenty Eight only).
