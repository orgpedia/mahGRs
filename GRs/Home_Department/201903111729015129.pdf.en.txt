Introduction: Effective measures to prevent violence against women in public places.
The Ministry of Home Affairs, Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India has approved the selection of Mumbai as the city for implementation of the Safe City Initiative for Women. The scheme will be implemented over a period of three years from 2018-19 to 2020-21 with the Central/ State share being 60:40. The Central Government has allotted Rs.
