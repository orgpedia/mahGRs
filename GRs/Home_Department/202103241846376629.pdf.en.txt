Good Friday and Easter Sunday- 2021 Guidelines.
This year is Good Friday for Christians. On 2/4/2021 as well as Easter Sunday
It will be held on 4/4/2021. Special prayers were held in the church on this day.
All religious festivals to be celebrated in a simple manner amid Covid-19 surge
have come. At present, the number of patients is increasing in many states of the country.
As the Covid-19 pandemic is still on, everyone is coming together in large numbers.
It's not appropriate to celebrate. Consider the situation that has arisen. From 28/3/2021
""" 4/4/2021 Celebrate the"""" Good Friday and Easter Sunday"""" during the Holy Week"""
Below are the guidelines for doing so:
1. d. Every prayer meeting during the Holy Week from March 28 to April 4, 2021
The attendance of people should be regulated according to the place in the church at the time. If there is a big church, a maximum of 50 people can attend it and if there is less space in the church, a special prayer meeting should be organized in the presence of 10 to 25 people. So that there is no crowd in the church, social distancing will be maintained.
Organize 4 to 5 special Masses as per the need.
It is necessary to pay special attention to the use of masks and sanitizers by Christian devotees during the prayer meeting. Disinfection should be arranged in the church.
The manager of the church should make arrangements to provide the facility of online broadcasting of the prayer meeting. Also, spread the message through social media platforms like WhatsApp, Facebook, and YouTube.
4) Avoid large gatherings in public places outside the church or on the street.
