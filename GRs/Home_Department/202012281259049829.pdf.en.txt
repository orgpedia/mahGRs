In view of the instructions given under the order, welcome to December 31, 2020 and the New Year
It is expected to be simple. Below are the guidelines for the same:
1. according to the corona. Even though there is no day-long curfew on December 31, 2020, to bid farewell to the passing year. On the occasion of New Year's reception on January 1, 2021, citizens should celebrate New Year's reception at home in a simple way without going out of the house.
On December 31, citizens should pay special attention to the use of masks and sanitizers in public places such as beaches, gardens, roads, etc. without crowding.
3) In view of the Corona outbreak, especially in the large cities of the state including Gateway of India, Marine Lines, Girgaum Chowpatty, Juhu Chowpatty in Mumbai, many people are crowded in many places, so people need to take care of their health.
Citizens above 60 years of age and children below 10 years of age should avoid going out of the house as far as possible for safety and health reasons.
No religious/ cultural program or procession should be taken out on the occasion of New Year's reception.
