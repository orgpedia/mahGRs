Government Circular: -
A "Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)" has been formulated by the government with the objective of ensuring uniformity, transparency and efficiency in the process of licensing of private security guard training institutes operating or newly established in the state with regard to training of private security guards and supervisors. The methodology details the procedure to be followed by the training institutes for obtaining the license, list of required documents, infrastructure, qualification of the trainers, training curriculum, assessment methodologies, as well as the process of renewal.
As per the notification issued by the State Government under the Private Security Systems (Regulation) Act, 2005, the Principal Secretary (Special), Department of Home Affairs, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai shall be the competent controlling authority for granting licences to Private Security Guard Training Institutes.
This procedure should be strictly followed by all the training institutes concerned.
This procedure will essentially be considered in the license approval or renewal process for the training institute. All licenses will then be TART approved for this procedure. It is attached in the form of Annexure 507 and is also being made available on the official website of the Government.
