On the basis of the final results of the State Service Main Examination-2017 and State Service Main Examination-2018, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has recommended candidates in the cadre of Assistant Regional Transport Officer (Group B). Of these, 6 candidates are from Nagpur. The training under Combined Peripheral Training-5 and 6 has been started by the General Administration Department from 04.10.2019. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts for Assistant Regional Transport Officer in the Motor Vehicle Department, Group-B cadre selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission and for candidates undergoing training in Combined Subordinate Training Programme 5 and 6.
Government Decision: For the cadre of Assistant Regional Transport Officer, (Group B), the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has given 2 supernumerary posts in the pay scale S-16: (44900-142400) in the revised matrix for 2 candidates who were selected on the basis of the final result of the State Service Main Examination 2017 and who were directly selected for training. 30.9.2019 to D. 3.10.2021 for a period of two years.
