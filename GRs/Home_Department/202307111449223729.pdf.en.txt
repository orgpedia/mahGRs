Preamble: The State Government has adopted a policy of providing 40% to 50% financial participation in selected projects to expedite the railway projects in rural and underdeveloped areas of the State.
The Union Government has included the construction of 174 km long railway line from New Jalna to Jalgaon in the 2022-23 budget. The railway line will connect Ajanta Caves, a world-class tourist destination in Marathwada, and Rajur Ganapati, a holy pilgrimage site. Stating that if the said railway line is built, it will be a project that will add to the development of the people of Marathwada, who are deprived of sustainable development, the Union Ministry of Railways has decided to approve the financial participation of the state in this project. 1 The above has been requested vide letter dated 27.04.2023.
The detailed project report of the new broad gauge railway project of Jalna-Jalgaon has been prepared and the estimated cost of the project (including land acquisition) is Rs. 7105.43 crore, Central Railway said in its Reference No. The above has been communicated to the State Government vide letter dated 31.05.2023.
As per the Detailed Project Report prepared by Central Railway and as per the request made by Ministry of Railways, the Jalna-Jalgaon new Broad Gauge Railway Line (174 Km) project is being implemented with 50:50 financial participation of Central Government (Ministry of Railways) and State Government. 3552.715 crore was under the consideration of the Government for approval of financial participation of the State Government. Following decisions were taken in the State Cabinet meeting:
Government Decision: For the Jalna-Jalgaon new Broad Gauge Railway line (174 km) project, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to bear 50% of the total project cost i.e. Rs. 7105.43 crore. The financial implication of up to Rs. 3552.715 crore is being approved by the Government.
