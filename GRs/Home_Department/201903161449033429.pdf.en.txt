A Committee under the Chairmanship of Smt.N.Ambika, Deputy Commissioner of Police, Zone-4, Mumbai has been constituted as per the guidelines in the Vishakha Case" Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace and Prevention and Redressal of Complaints of Sexual Harassment Act, 2013" to inquire into the charges 1 to 5 of the charges framed under the Memorandum dated 06.12.2013. The Committee Chairperson, Mrs. N. Ambika State, Pune, has asked the Government to extend the time to submit the report of the Committee on the subject matter. The request has been made vide letter dated 10.01.2019. Accordingly, the government proposed to extend the period of the committee by three months.
Government Decision: Mr. Hiralal Jadhav, Principal (Suspended), Daulatrao Jadhav Training College, Yerawada, Pune against Dy.
