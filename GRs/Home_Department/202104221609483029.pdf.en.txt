Introduction: The functioning of the Prisons Department under the Home Department is carried out in accordance with the provisions of the Prison Rules, 1979. Part of Chapter-9 Staff Uniform& Equipment in the said Rules. the non-statutory rules. According to this, provisions have been made regarding the uniform allowance of all the officers/ employees from the rank of peon in the prison department to the post of Deputy Inspector General of Prisons.
2. The guarding staff of the prison department has to perform urgent and urgent work in the prison. Since the assigned uniform cap often falls off the head while performing such tasks, many employees remove the uniform cap and keep it in their pocket while performing such duties. Moreover, the present cap does not cast a shadow on the eyes of the staff on duty in the tower, bank, circle, guard etc. of the jail during the summer days and they have to suffer from the intense heat. Considering all these factors, the government was considering allowing only the guard cadre personnel to use panel visor cap instead of the cap which is currently part of the uniform.
