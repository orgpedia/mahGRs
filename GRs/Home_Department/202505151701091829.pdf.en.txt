Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai Reference No. Under Section 6, the Office of the Director General of Police, Anti-Terrorist Squad, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has requested for the return of ministerial officers and staff currently working in the office of the Anti-Terrorism Squad, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, as well as the filling up of all the posts of ministerial officers and staff sanctioned by the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, in the office of the Director General of Police, Brihanmumbai, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
The office of the Anti-Terrorism Squad, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is in dire need of ministerial officers and staff for smooth functioning and smooth functioning of the office.
Therefore, the Director General of Police, Anti-Terrorist Squad, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, to fill up the posts of Ministerial Officers and Staff at their establishment from the establishment of Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, the approval to fill up the posts from the establishment of Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the sanctioned post of Administrative Officer in the office of Director General of Police, Anti-Terrorism Squad, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, as both the posts of Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai State, Senior Office Superintendent have the same pay scale (S-15, 41800-132300).
The government's decision:
Since the pay scale (S-15, 41800-132300) of both the posts of the sanctioned Administrative Officer in the Office of the Director General of Police, Anti-Terrorism Squad, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is the same as that of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai Office of the Senior Office Superintendent, the Government is approving the change in the designation of the post of Administrative Officer to Senior Office Superintendent, Mumbai, to be filled up by the City Clerk Establishment of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
Also, Government approval is being accorded for filling up of a total of 20 posts of the following ministerial officers and staff sanctioned as per the framework on establishment of the Office of the Director General of Police, Anti-Terrorism Squad, Maharashtra State, Mumbai by the Office of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
