Government Decision: The revised figure of total 5068 posts on the establishment of offices under the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services has been approved by the Sub-Committee of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department and Principal Secretary (Expenditure), Finance Department. This has been approved in the meeting held on 07.06.2022. As per the recommendations of the Sub-Committee, it is proposed to fix a revised figure of a total of 5068 posts on the establishment of the Prison Department. A high-level committee headed by the Chief Secretary. It was submitted on 14.06.2022. High-level committee In the meeting held on 30.06.2022, it has been approved to fix the revised figure of a total of 5068 regular posts on the establishment of the Prison Department.
The details of 5068 posts approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries are as follows.

In addition to the 4216 posts sanctioned under the Government decision dated 13.01.2006, the Government is approving the revised figure of 5068 regular posts in various cadres, including 848+04 posts created for newly created prisons after establishment of new prisons from time to time.
As approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries, 5068 regular posts of the Prison Department have been shown in Form-A attached with the post-wise details in the revised figure.
4. The Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Pune is being authorized to allocate/ adjust the posts sanctioned under this order office-wise. The expenditure of the posts in the figure approved by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries should be met from the sanctioned grant of the office of the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Pune and the office under him.
