Raja Thakare has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor. The matter of sanctioning professional fees to Vaibhav Bagade was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Case No. 20/2018. Sessions Court, Dindoshi, Mumbai and Ma. Advocate General, High Court of Judicature at Mumbai. Vaibhav Bagade is being approved to pay professional fees as follows:
Sessions Court, Dindoshi, Mumbai and M. High Court, Bombay (A) Daily Court Effective Hearing Fee- Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand only) B) Discussion Fee (per sitting) Rs. 5000/- (Rupees Five Thousand only)
There are no other benefits apart from the professional fees. Splendor will not be allowed. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.
