As on 02.09.2021, a revised figure of 3800 posts has been fixed in various 31 cadres of State Excise Department. Thereafter, reference No. The decision of the Government, 2. As on 05.01.2022, two new Regional Divisions namely Amravati and Nanded have been created by bifurcating the Regional Divisions of Nagpur and Aurangabad in the State Excise Department with a total of 42 new posts of 38 regular posts of 19 each and 4 posts of 2 each by external agencies.
The office of the Under Commissioner of State Excise, State Excise, Maharashtra State is functioning at Mumbai and as per the new pattern, 2 posts of Joint Commissioner (Excise/ Liquor) and Joint Commissioner (Administration) of the rank of Joint Commissioner are sanctioned in the office of the Commissioner, State Excise. It would be desirable to have a separate post of Joint Commissioner rank at Nagpur to ensure proper coordination with the headquarters in the matters of the functioning of the State Excise Department, including proper control over the sale of liquor licenses, emphasis on the collection of revenue from liquor manufacturing units, and to ensure that the excise collection in Vidarbha and Marathwada regions is at an adequate level. Accordingly, the matter of shifting 1 post of Joint Commissioner in the Commissioner, State Excise Office to Nagpur was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1. Commissioner, Joint Commissioner (Excise/ Liquor) in the State Excise Office. Yatin Sawant, Joint Commissioner (Group-A) [Pay Scale S-25 (78,800-2, 09,200)] is being transferred and posted at Nagpur.
The post will be transferred to the office of the Divisional Deputy Commissioner, Nagpur Division, Nagpur. The post will be held by Joint Commissioner (Coordination).
