Guidelines for Mahavir Jayanti 2021.
The Mahavir Jayanti festival is celebrated with great fervor and enthusiasm by the Jain community all over the state of Maharashtra. the current year. Mahavir Jayanti will be celebrated on April 25, 2021. In view of COVID-19, this year all religious festivals/ celebrations as well as all events are being celebrated in a very simple manner. Considering the present situation of the second wave of COVID-19 and the increasing number of Corona patients, the Ministry of Revenue and Forest Department, Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation, Mumbai, vide its order dated 13th April, 2021, has issued the following orders: Mahavir Jayanti celebrations are required to be held this year subject to the provisions mentioned in Sections 1, 2, 7 and 10. In this regard, the following guidelines are being issued: 1. Mahavir Jayanti is celebrated by people in large numbers. But this year, to avoid the spread of Covid-19, people are expected to celebrate Mahavir Jayanti at their homes in a simple manner without gathering.
2) In view of the increasing outbreak of COVID-19, all religious places have been closed and devotees will not be allowed to enter the temple for worship and darshan.
The manager/ trustee of the temple should make the facility of darshan available through online, cable network, web site and Facebook etc. if possible.
4) No procession or procession should be taken out on the occasion of Mahavir Jayanti.
To prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus, the rules prescribed by the Government Relief, Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department as well as the concerned Municipal Corporation, Police Administration, Local Administration should be strictly followed.
Also, if any other instructions are published after this circular and in the period between the start of the actual festival, they should also be complied with.
