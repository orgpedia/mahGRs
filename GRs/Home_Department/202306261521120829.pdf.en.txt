In Maharashtra, cricket matches are played in Mumbai, Nagpur, Pune and Navi Mumbai.
In these cricket matches, special police force is deployed as per the requirement for the match keeping in view the popularity of the team and the local situation. The Government vide Government Resolution No. Parikrama- 0318/9340/ P.S. 92/ VISA-1A dated 12.11.2018 have approved the levy of bulk charges on the basis of face value for the period from 01.04.2018 to 31.03.2019 for cricket matches played at different venues during different periods. This period has ended on 31.03.2019. Therefore, the proposal to fix the rates for the period from 2019-2020 to 2023-2024 was submitted to the government for approval. However, the government is yet to decide on the new rates.
Government's decision:
By this Government decision, the Government letter and Government decision issued earlier regarding the rate of police bandobust fee for cricket matches in Mumbai, Nagpur, Pune and Navi Mumbai in Maharashtra are being superseded. The Government is approving the following charges for police bandobast per match for cricket matches to be held throughout the State:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | The type of cricket            | Fee for each match (per match) |
| 1.   | T20 Internationals and the IPL | Rs 10 lakh                     |
| 2.   | One-day internationals         | Rs 25 lakh                     |
| 3.   | Test match (5 days)            | Rs 25 lakh                     |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Consider the following when making a payment.
A. The amount of this fee is fixed as a lump sum settlement cost on a face to face basis.
b. the additional settlement fee shall be applied only in exceptional circumstances (taking into account threats to the organisation of matches). Under no circumstances should the additional police bandobust fee be charged more than 25% of the prescribed fee. The permission of the Director General of Police, State of Maharashtra should be obtained for the levy of additional bandobust fee.
The total amount also includes security arrangements inside and outside the stadium.
