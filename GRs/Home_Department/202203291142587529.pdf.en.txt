Government's decision:
1085 temporary posts in various cadres in the establishment of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, vide Government Resolution Home Department No.DPP-0921/ PR No. 216/ Pol-10, Dy. 27.09.2021 has been extended till 28.02.2022. Now, under the said decision, the Government is granting extension of tenure from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 for a total of 1484 temporary posts in Annexure-" A, B, C, D, E, F, C, H." The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than six months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts where the government has already issued orders to abolish these posts.
Government Resolution, Home Department No.DPP-0516/ PR No. 136/ Pol-10, D. As on 01.02.2021, a total of 1431 posts have been sanctioned in the establishment of Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
