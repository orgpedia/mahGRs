Accordingly, the proposal to sanction incentive pay to the police officers/ staff working in the Anti-Terrorism Squad as per the pay scale of the Seventh Pay Commission was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: An amount equivalent to 25% of the basic pay in the pay scale of the Seventh Pay Commission is being sanctioned retrospectively to the serving police officers/ staff of Anti-Terrorism Squad.
