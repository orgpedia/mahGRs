Government circular:
The Road Safety Campaign is organized every year throughout the country to effectively curb motor vehicle accidents and to promote and disseminate traffic rules among the citizens. In addition to the central government's road safety campaign. January 11, 2023. Road Safety Mission 2023 is being observed from 17th January, 2023.
Under the Road Safety Campaign, various programs are organized for publicity, awareness and public awareness for road safety in collaboration with the Transport Department, Traffic Police Department, Public Health Department, Public Works Department, School Education Department and all other related organizations/ organizations.
3. The activities to be undertaken by all concerned departments under" Road Safety Campaign" to create awareness about road safety as mentioned above are mentioned in the attached prospectus.
The heads of the concerned departments shall ensure strict implementation of the said action plan/ guidelines.
District Road Safety Committees have been constituted for each district of the State. The committee should hold a meeting of the committee regarding the implementation/ planning of Road Safety Mission 2023 in the district and give directions to the concerned and make all efforts to make the mission a success.
The Commissioner of Transport, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the Additional Director General of Police (Traffic), Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall act as coordinators for the implementation of this programme and submit an action taken report to the Government.
