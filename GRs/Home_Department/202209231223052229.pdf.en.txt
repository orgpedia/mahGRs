GOVERNMENT DECISION 1,127 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai were extended till 31/08/2022 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Now in the attached prospectus, 1,127 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai will be filled up with effect from 01/09/2022. The Government hereby grants its approval to continue till 28/02/2023 subject to the following conditions:
The extension will not be applicable to temporary posts (excluding executive posts) lying vacant for more than six months.
2. The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned subject to the terms and conditions to be followed.

No. 12/2004/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 19th August, 2004, No. No. 8/16/ Fiscal Reform-1, dated 21st September, 2016 and No. The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, is required to complete the process of review of posts as per No. 8/16/ APC, dated 10th February, 2017 immediately and obtain the final approval of the High Level Committee for the revised layout of the office, in any case within the stipulated time, so that necessary action should be taken on priority. The suggestions given from time to time should be considered.
If the process of reviewing the posts is not completed within the deadline, if the above temporary posts are not extended for that further period and if there is a question of salary and allowance of the officer/ employee, then it will be the responsibility of the concerned office.
