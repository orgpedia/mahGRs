The proposal: The Supreme Court in Special Leave Petition No. 3543/2020. Order dated 02.12.2020 and SLP No. 2302/2017 has directed to create an Oversight Mechanism in the State to study CCTV camera footage and publish monitoring reports on it regularly.
The state government has also informed the Centre about the same. Accordingly, the government had constituted the committee.
Maharashtra government's decision As per the directions of the Supreme Court, the Government is constituting a Committee at the level of each Police Commissionerate and at the level of Police District to study the CCTV camera footage and to prepare an Oversight Mechanism for regular publication of the monitoring report.
