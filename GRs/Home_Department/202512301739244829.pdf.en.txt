2) Letter dated 18.12.2025 from the President, Indian Hotel & Restaurant Association, Mumbai.
Introduction: On Christmas and New Year's Eve, various organizations request the state government every year to allow the owners of eateries, hotels, restaurants and orchestra bars to continue their establishments till 05.00 morning on the second day of December 24, 25 and 31.
Sometimes, if the government receives the application late, it is not possible to issue the order within the stipulated time. The government was considering allowing eateries, hotels, restaurants, and orchestra bars to operate until 05.00 on the second day of December 24, 25, and 32, every year, to prevent such a recurrence in the future.
Government Decision - In view of the time lost in the annual demand and government approval from eateries, hotels, restaurant and orchestra bars establishments, Section 33 of the Maharashtra Police Act, 1951 allows the owners of eateries, hotels, restaurant and orchestra bars in the state to continue their establishments on Christmas and New Year's Day every year till 24th, 25th and 31st of December, next morning, subject to the following conditions:
1. The owner of the establishment shall ensure that no law and order problem arises in the premises by deploying adequate number of private security guards inside and outside the establishment.
