The Proposal:
Seven probationary officers of the 69th batch of the Indian Police Service have been transferred to Maharashtra. 11.12.2017 to dt. A proposal has been received by the Government to create seven supernumerary posts of' Assistant Superintendent of Police' rank for training in the State of Maharashtra during the period up to 22.06.2018 (6 months 11 days). The posts are in the pay scale of Level 10 of the Indian Police Service (IPS).
The seven officers mentioned above are: 11.12.2017 to dt. A proposal was under consideration of the Government to meet the expenditure of Rs. 34,02,000/- (Rupees Thirty Four Lakhs Two Thousand only) towards salary and allowances for the period 22.06.2018 (6 months 11 days) from the sanctioned grant.
The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The following officers of the 69th batch of the Indian Police Service have been selected for probation. 11.12.2017 to dt. The Government is approving an expenditure of Rs. 34,02,000/- (Rupees Thirty Four Lakhs Two Thousand only) for the training period upto 22.06.2018 (6 Months 11 Days) at Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik and in the district shown against their name thereafter.
