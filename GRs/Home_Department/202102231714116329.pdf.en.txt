The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. The central government has given. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of this decision, the government was considering to ensure the posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre in the Maharashtra Maritime Board office under the Home (Ports) Department for the disabled category.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. As per the notification, the posts in Group' A" to Group' D" notified by the Central Government for persons with disabilities will continue to be reserved for persons with disabilities, even if the designations of the posts in the list published by the Central Government are different from the posts in the establishment under the State Government, and the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the service under the State Government.
