The matter of payment of professional fee to Mr. Pradeep Gharat, Special Public Prosecutor appointed to look after the judicial affairs of the High Court, Mumbai on behalf of the Government was under consideration of the Government.
The Government Decision has been issued vide G.O.R. No. 29/2019 filed with the Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai. Special Court, Mumbai. the Sessions Court, Mumbai and Ma. Shri Pradeep Gharat, Special Public Prosecutor, High Court, Mumbai, who has been appointed to look after the court proceedings on behalf of the Government, is being allowed to pay the following professional fees for effective day to day hearing.
Apart from this professional fee, no other benefit will be admissible to Mr. Pradeep Gharat.
the Special MPID Court, and Sessions Court, Mumbai 1) Effective hearing fee per day is Rs. 20,000/- (Rupees Twenty Thousand only) 2) Discussion Fee (per sitting) Rs. 3,000/- (Rupees Three Thousand only). High Court, Mumbai 1) Effective hearing fee per day is Rs. 30,000/- (Rupees Thirty Thousand only) 2) Discussion Fee (per sitting) Rs. 3,000/- (Rupees Three Thousand only)

The payment of professional fees admissible to Adv. Mr. Pradeep Gharat shall be made after every two months of court proceedings. The Deputy Commissioner of Police concerned shall ensure that the effective hearing days of the court proceedings are certified by an officer not below the rank of Superintending Deputy Commissioner of Police of the concerned case and no additional payment of any kind shall be made to Mr. Pradeep Gharat.
The expenditure for this purpose will be met from the sanctioned grant for the financial year under the head Demand No.B-1, 2055 Police- (00) 108, State Headquarters Police (02) (01) City Police Establishment- (2055 0097)-28 Professional Services of this Department and as per the monthly fund statement.
