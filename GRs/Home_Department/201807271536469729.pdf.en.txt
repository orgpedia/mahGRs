The government's decision:
To prevent accidents on the Yashwantrao Chavan Expressway, necessary measures are taken to reduce the number of accidents by collecting and analyzing the scientific data of accidents.
A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed on 28.06.2018 between the Department of Home Affairs, Government of Maharashtra and SaveLIFE Foundation, New Delhi (Non-Governmental Organization) for the purpose of implementation.
Projects involving representatives of all concerned departments including SaveLIFE Foundation under the Chairmanship of A.M.S. (Home) for the control of the project as provided in Clause 1.1 of the MoU
The government has decided to form the committee. The composition of the committee will be as follows:
Additional Chief Secretary (Home), Home Department Chairman 2) Principal Secretary (Special), Home Department Member 3) Additional Director General of Police (Traffic), Maharashtra State Member 4) Joint/ Deputy Secretary, Public Health Department Member 5) Joint/ Deputy Secretary, Medical Education Department Member 6) Joint/ Deputy Secretary, Transport Department Member 7) Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Member 8) Superintendent of Police (HQ), Highway Police Member 9) Chief Engineer, Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation Member 10) Chief Operating Officer, BVG 108 Ambulance Service Member 11) Chief Advisor, Save Life Foundation, New Delhi Member 12) Chief Executive Officer, Save Life Foundation, New Delhi Member 13) Joint/ Deputy Secretary

The committee will review the progress of the project as per the MoU signed between the Home Department and SaveLIFE Foundation to reduce accidents on the Yashwantrao Chavan Expressway and give appropriate directions as required. It will also discuss the recommendations submitted by the SaveLIFE Foundation to take necessary measures regarding the compliance of traffic rules on the route in order to avoid accidents on the expressway.
The Committee shall review the progress of the proceedings under the Agreement every three months and issue necessary directions accordingly.
The Committee shall continue to exist until the MoU comes into force.
