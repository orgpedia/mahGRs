(1.79 ha) plus 5,000 sq.ft. It has been informed in the Reference letter dated 13.12.2017 that in case the advance possession of the said land is given to the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Mumbai for the project" Metro Line-4," the 14 buildings i. e. 960 flats in the proposed project will have to be reduced, due to which the said project will not be viable. Therefore, the matter of providing alternative plots instead of the said plots for the project" Metro Line-4" was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision- For the project" Metro Line-4," a plot of land No. 635/ 2B in Vidyavihar, Mumbai Suburban District, in the name of Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, 7,654 sq. I.
Approval is hereby given for giving the possession of the said land in advance to the Commissioner, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Mumbai, for a period of five years.
