The Proposal:
Chandrapur district. Read here for a comprehensive discussion, study and recommendation to the government on the liquor ban implemented from 01.04.2015. The High Level Committee has been constituted under the Government decision dated 12.01.2021 and the Committee has fixed a time frame to submit its report to the Government within one month. After that. As per the Government decision dated 12.02.2021, the said High Level Committee was directed to submit a report to the Government. The date has been extended till 07.03.2021. The committee is in the final stages of its work and has sought an extension of time to submit its report to the government.
Accordingly, the government was considering extending the time to submit the report to the said high-level committee.
Government Decision: The High-Level Committee constituted to study and make recommendations to the Government on the comprehensive implementation of the prohibition of liquor from 01.04.2015 was asked to submit its report to the Government. 10 more days beyond 07.03.2021 (D. 17.03.2021) is being extended.
