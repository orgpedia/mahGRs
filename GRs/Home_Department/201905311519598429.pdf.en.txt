(2) This order shall come into force immediately. The transferred officers should take charge of the post immediately. Action should be taken through the Commissioner, Raushu, so that the transfer order is implemented immediately and efficiently.
3. The transferred officers should be reinstated without delay after they are relieved from their present posts and come to join. The officers concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-recruitment of the said officer and payment of salary and allowances by fixing a mandatory waiting period without work. The date on which the officers concerned were relieved from their duties to appear in place of the new appointees and the date on which they appeared in place of the new appointees should be informed to the government immediately.
If any officer directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned officers considering it as misconduct as the action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
