Government Order:
In the cadre of Inspector (Group-B) from the cadre of Secondary Inspector (Group-C) in the State Excise Department, a total of 53 officers have been selected on the basis of the selection list for the year 2018-19. The promotion has been granted vide Government Order dated 11.09.2019. The officer Mr. Satish Anant Gawde, Secondary Inspector, Raushu has denied the promotion on family grounds. vide letter dated 13.09.2019. Accordingly, Shri. The promotion granted vide order dated 11.09.2019 stands cancelled.
As per the instructions issued by the General Administration Department under the relevant Government Decision, Shri Gawade will not be considered in the selection lists (2019-20 and 2020-21) for the next two years.
Further, if the matter is required to be dealt with in accordance with the orders issued by the Finance Department from time to time regarding withdrawal of benefits, if any, provided under the Assured Progress Scheme to the officers/ employees who have been denied promotion, such action should be taken at the level of Commissioner, State Excise.
