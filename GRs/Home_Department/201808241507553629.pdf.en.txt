Preamble- Since the Special Inspector General of Police, Women Repression Prevention Department has been separated from the Civil Rights Protection Department, the office has not been provided space for office work at the Office of the Director General of Police. This makes it difficult to work. In addition, the office of the Additional Director General of Police, Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai, was also in need of space for office work and hence, the Government was considering to take on rent the space in a private building at World Trade Centre, Cuffe Parade, Mumbai for the offices of the Special Inspector General of Police, Women Repression Prevention Cell and the Additional Director General of Police, Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai.
Decision of the Government: The Director General of Police, State of Maharashtra has sanctioned Rs. 225/- plus incidental charges, as per the rates mentioned in the table below, is being approved as per the terms and conditions.
