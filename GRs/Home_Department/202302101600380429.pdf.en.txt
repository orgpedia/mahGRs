GOVERNMENT DECISION: In a court case arising out of an offence registered under Chikhli Police Station, Buldhana G.R. No. 746/ 2021, Md. Adv. Vinod Phate is being allowed to pay the fee as follows.
the Sessions Court, Buldhana
(a) Daily hearing fee of Rs. 50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only) 2) Discussion Fee per hour- Rs. 15,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Thousand only) (Maximum Rs. 50,000/- per day)
Any other benefits other than the above mentioned. Jokes will not be allowed.
