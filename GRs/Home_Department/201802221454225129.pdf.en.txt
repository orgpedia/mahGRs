A total of 11 specified medical examinations of police personnel and officers of the age 45 years and above (excluding Indian Police Service officers) of the State Police Force from Government Hospitals during the period from 1st July, 2017 to 30th September, 2017 were approved vide Government Resolution, Home Department, No. POQUA-0617/ PR No. 348/ POL-7, dated 27.06.2017.
The date for conducting the said inspections has been extended from 1st October, 2017 to 31st March, 2018 vide Government Resolution, Home Department, No.Pokua-0617/ PR No. 348/ 1/ Pol-7, dated 22.02.2018.
Now, the Commissioner of Police, Nagpur City, has submitted a proposal to the Government to conduct these tests from the Government Hospitals in Nagpur City as well as the Police Polyclinic at the Police Hospital in the Police Headquarters under the jurisdiction of the Commissionerate of Police, Nagpur City.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
