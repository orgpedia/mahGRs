The Proposal:
After the terrorist attack on Mumbai city on 26.11.2008 and the terrorist attack on Dadar, Opera House, Zaveri Bazar, it has been decided to implement the CCTV project in Mumbai city and other cities of the state in view of security. The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai vide letter dated 21.08.2018 requested for installation of more CCTV cameras in Mumbai city. Accordingly, the High Powered Committee chaired by the Hon'ble Home Minister in its meeting dated 05.11.2018 has decided to install additional CCTV cameras as part of the Mumbai CCTV Project as per the provisions of the" U Schedule" of the contract for the Mumbai CCTV Project. In this meeting, a high-powered committee headed by the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai to examine the technical aspects of the project and submit a report.
