the Home Department

Date: December 9, 2022.
Reference:
Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai vide letter No. Rap/ Lekha/ Anvavi/ 2208, dated 28.10.2022.
The Proposal:
Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has requested the Government through a letter under reference to provide funds for payment of salaries of employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of October, 2022. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds for the salary of the employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of October, 2022 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. Approval is being given for providing Rs. 200.00 crore (Rupees Two Hundred Crores only) in cash.
2) Rs. 200.00 crore should be met from the provisions available in the financial year 2022-23 and the expenditure should be spent under the head" Demand No.B-3, Accounts Head 2041, Taxes on Vehicles (00) 001, Operations and Administration (01) Commissioner of Transport (01) (01) Establishment- Commissioner of Transport (Adaptation) (Mandatory) (2041 0018) 33, Financial Assistance."
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to collect and disburse the amount to be paid to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation by the Accountant in his office.
4. Government Decision Informal Reference No. 441/2022/ Expenditure-8, Sec. being issued with the consent received as on 05.12.2022.
