These orders are being issued in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, with prior approval of the competent authority.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The transferred officers should take charge of the post immediately. For the immediate and effective implementation of the transfer order, the Commissioner, Raushu, shall strictly follow the following instructions.
(a) The transferred officers shall be relieved forthwith on receipt of the transfer orders. The transferred officers should be reinstated without delay after they are relieved from their present posts. The officers concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-recruitment of the said officer and payment of salary and allowances by fixing a mandatory waiting period without work.
