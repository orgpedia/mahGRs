Government's decision
Rajendra Madhukar Patil, Seniority No. 28 Assistant Regional Transport Officers are being given temporary promotion from open category to the post of Assistant Regional Transport Officer subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in this order.
(2) The said temporary promotions are being granted subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) The above promotions are temporary in nature and are subject to the following conditions: Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 in the Supreme Court. Subject to the orders of the Supreme Court, S.V./ 16-B has issued a notice. As per instructions issued vide letter dated 29.12.2017. If the order of promotion is violated by this order, the officers promoted under this order will not have the right to claim service merit and corresponding financial benefits.
(b) The promotions to the above officers who are eligible for promotion are temporary in nature. Hence, these officers will have no right to regularity and excellence in service.
(a) The orders of posting of the above officers who have been promoted from the cadre of Inspector of Motor Vehicles to the cadre of Assistant Regional Transport Officer shall be issued separately.
(d) In case of refusal of promotion by the promoted officers, the Government Decision, General Administration Department, SRV-2015/ PR No. 303/ Function-12, D. Action will be taken as per the provisions of 12.9.2016.
