The Proposal:
The Ministry of Finance, Government of India vide its Office Memorandum dated 09.03.2022 has directed to adopt a revised methodology for effective management of funds of Central Sector Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds. Pursuant to the Office Memorandum dated 09.03.2022 of the Ministry of Finance, Government of India, the Department of Finance, Government of Maharashtra, Ministry of Finance, Government of Maharashtra, Mumbai vide letter dated 25.05.2022, has informed to take action in accordance with the revised instructions on Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS).
As per the revised modalities, a proposal for appointment of Central Nodal Agencies (CNAs), Implementing Agencies for implementation of Cyber Crime Prevention against Women& Children (CCPWC) Central Sector Schemes under the jurisdiction of WISHA-6, Home Department is under consideration of the Government.
As per the revised modalities, Central Nodal Agencies (CNAs), Implementing Agencies are being appointed to implement the Central Sector Schemes of Cyber Crime Prevention against Women& Children (CCPWC) under the Department of Home Affairs (DoH).
