Introduction: The camp office functions in 44 of the 50 transport offices in the Motor Vehicles Department. In the camp office the work of learner' s license and permanent license is done.
At present, human trials are being done for learner's license. This system needs to be computerized to make it more transparent and faster. The proposal to purchase tablets from the GeM portal for the camp office under the transport offices was under consideration of the government as it would be possible to test the learner's license through the system developed by the National Informatics Center if the tablet is made available to the actual camp office to fully computerize the learner's license process.
Government Decision: Administrative and financial approval is being given to purchase 15 tablets each for 11 Regional Transport Offices and 10 tablets each for 32 Sub-Regional Transport Offices under the Motor Vehicles Department, 485 tablets and 15 reserved tablets, total 500 tablets from the GeM portal.
