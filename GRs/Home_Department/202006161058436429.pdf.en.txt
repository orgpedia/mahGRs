The proposal: The Supreme Court has dismissed the petition No. 295/2015 to review the road safety issues from time to time. A Road Safety Committee has been constituted to submit a report to the Supreme Court. The committee has directed to create a separate road safety fund for road safety activities. Accordingly, for approval of various projects related to road safety, under reference No. 4 here. As per the Government decision dated 01.09.2016, a" Road Safety Fund Control Committee" has been set up. For this purpose. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. The meeting was held on 03.03.2020 and the minutes of the meeting are as under. 1 here. Issued vide letter dated 20.03.2020. The government was considering issuing a government resolution in accordance with the proposals approved in the meeting.
Government's decision:
