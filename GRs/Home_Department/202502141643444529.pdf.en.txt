Representational representatives (grandmothers / former), various organisations in the state and some citizens had submitted statements on making laws to prevent love jihad and fraudulent or forced conversions. Laws have also been enacted by some states in India to prevent fraudulent or forced conversions to love jihad. Accordingly, the government was considering setting up a special committee to study the prevailing situation in Maharashtra and suggest measures for complaints received regarding love jihad or forced conversions, study the law in other states and draft the law as well as legal matters. Accordingly, a Special Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the composition of the Committee will be as follows: -
