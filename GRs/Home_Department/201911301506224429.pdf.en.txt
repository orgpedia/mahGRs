GOVERNMENT ORDER: Mr. Sanjay Barve, Bhapose (MH: 1987) vide No. 1 hereunder vide Government Order dated 28.02.2019, has been appointed as Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, vide Reference No. As per the order issued herein, Shri Sanjay Barve, Bhapose has been posted as Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, with effect from the date of his superannuation i.e. The period has been extended for the next three months beyond 31/08/2019.
Now, the Union Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi vide Reference No. 3 here. In the public interest, in pursuance of the order dated 29.11.2019 relaxing the provision of Rule 16 (1) of the All India Services (Death and Gratuity of Service) Rules, 1958, Shri Sanjay Barve, presently serving as Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, has been transferred and posted as Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai. The period has been extended by three months beyond 30.11.2019. Accordingly, Sanjay Barve, Bhapose was given the post of Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai. The extension is for a period of three months beyond 30.11.2019.
