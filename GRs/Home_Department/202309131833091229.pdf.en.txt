The Proposal:
As per the aforesaid letter dated 19/06/2023 from the Home Secretary, Government of India, the Central Government has formulated a scheme for grant of bail to the prisoners who are unable to pay the fine and are unable to get bail due to financial constraints.
Providing financial support to pay the fine or helping them get bail will help them to come out of jail and join the mainstream as responsible citizens of the country. Guidelines and SOPs have been laid down for successful implementation of the scheme. As mentioned under the said guidelines, the matter of forming an" Oversight Committee" at the government level was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
For the successful implementation of the scheme designed by the Central Government to provide bail to the prisoners who are unable to pay the fine and are unable to get bail due to financial difficulties, an" Oversight Committee" is being formed at the government level as follows.
