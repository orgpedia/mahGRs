Introduction: Refer No. 1 Motor Accident Claims Tribunal has been set up at Pune under the decision of the Government herein. A total of 13 posts have been created on their establishment under the government decision at 2 and 3. There are 9 permanent and 4 temporary posts. These 4 temporary posts are mentioned in Reference No. 4 As per the Government decision dated 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. Regarding extension of these posts beyond 01.09.2020, Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune vide Reference No. 6 request to the government.
The matter of extension of these posts from 01.09.2020 onwards was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following 4 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune are being extended from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. The extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending before the Tribunal are disposed of expeditiously, the returns of the settled claims are submitted to the Government at the end of the month of November, 2020 and February, 2020 and if 50 per cent of the pending claims are not found to be settled at the end of the month of August, 2020, no further extension will be granted.
