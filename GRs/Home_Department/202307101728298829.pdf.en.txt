The Maharashtra Government Allotment of Revenue Department Rules, 2021 for appointment to Group A and Group B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) posts by direct recruitment and promotion.
As per the order, Mr. Liladhar Vasant Patil has been promoted to the post of Inspector, State Excise, Group-B from the promotion list of 2019-20 and after the promotion, he has been allotted Aurangabad Revenue Division and posted to the post of Inspector, Divisional Excise Squad, District-Aurangabad.
In pursuance of the request made by Mr. Liladhar Vasant Patil, Inspector, Raushu regarding the change of Revenue Department, as per the provision of the Revenue Department Allotment Rules, 2021 for appointment to Group A and Group B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) posts of the Government of Maharashtra by direct service and promotion, he is being allotted the Revenue Department" Nashik" instead of" Aurangabad" and with the approval of the competent authority, his posting in the revised Allotment Revenue Department is being done in the post shown in column (5) below.
