The Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune has a total of 13 posts, out of which 9 are permanent and 4 are temporary. These 4 temporary posts are mentioned in Reference No. The period was extended from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 as per the Government decision dated 02.04.2018. The said 4 temporary posts have expired on 28.02.2019 and further extension of the said posts with effect from 01.03.2019 is being considered by the Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune vide Reference No. 4 has been requested by the government. The matter of extension of these posts beyond 01.03.2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
The following 4 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune are being extended from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019. The extension is for expeditious disposal of the claims pending before the Tribunal and for furnishing of the statement of claims settled every three years.
It will be submitted to the government in a month's time. The details of the posts are as follows:
