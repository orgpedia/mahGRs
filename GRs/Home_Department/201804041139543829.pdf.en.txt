The Manager, State Transport Appellate Tribunal, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai has now issued a proposal regarding extension of 1 (one) temporary post of peon vide Reference No. 2 has been submitted to the Government as per the letter here and the matter was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The 1 (one) temporary post of Manager, State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, peon in the establishment is being extended from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018. This extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending with the State Transport Appellate Tribunal are settled expeditiously, the monthly statement of settled claims is submitted to the government by the end of September 2018 and if 50 per cent of the pending claims are not found to be settled as on 01.03.2018, no further extension will be granted.
