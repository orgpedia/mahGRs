Preamble: The project for modernization of Police Control Rooms in the State will enable immediate availability of vehicles based on Global Positioning System (GPS) or similar system at the place of emergency and will reduce the time taken for providing emergency services to the citizens due to use of improved and updated technology. The facility of contacting the emergency services through mobile application, SMS, e-mail, chat etc. will also be available. Considering these aspects, the proposal for modernization and strengthening of Police Control Rooms in the State has been approved by the Cabinet in its meeting held on 3rd August, 2017. Accordingly, the administrative approval for the modernization project of the police control room in the state and the construction of a control, planning and analysis center in Worli, Mumbai, was decided by the Home Department. 04.09.2017.
