Introduction: VAN 4.0 and SARATHI 4.0 systems in all the offices of the Department of Motor Vehicles
have been made. The staff needs to be trained on the safety and security of the system. Also, the training of the employees in the transport offices was under the consideration of the government as it was necessary to train the concerned staff on the concept of e-challan, DigiLocker/ m-Parivahan and the project to be undertaken by the Motor Vehicles Department in the near future.
The Government has decided to impart 5-day training to 100 staff members, two each from all 50 Transport Offices under the Motor Vehicles Department, on Vahan 4.0 and Sarathi 4.0 computer systems and other related aspects at the Central Institute of Road Transport (CIRT), Pune. 3000/- Rs. 15,00,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs only) is the administrative and financial sanction.
