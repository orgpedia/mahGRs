Government Decision: A total of 1168 posts under the control of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved. Further, as per the Government Decision No.D.P.-2012/ PR No. 112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway. Out of these, 173 temporary posts in 5 District Offices of Assistant Director and Public Prosecutor in Annexure-D along with the control of Deputy Director, Government Grievances, Nashik Range, Nashik have been notified vide Home Department Government Resolution No. The date has been extended upto 29.02.2020 w.e.f 05.10.2019. Now, the Government hereby grants extension of 173 temporary posts in Annexure-D from 01/03/2020 to 31.08.2020. This extension will not be applicable to the posts which are lying vacant for more than 6 months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts that the government has already ordered to eliminate.
The High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the review of the posts in the establishment of Directorate of Complaints, Maharashtra State, Mumbai on 13.01.2005 and the above figure of 1168 posts has been prescribed as per the Government decision dated 12.10.2010 in Reference No. 1. This figure includes these positions. Further, as per Government Resolution No. 2012/ DPP-112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway.
