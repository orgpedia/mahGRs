Provisional seniority of all officers in the cadre of Additional Superintendent, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail, Class-I as on 01.01.2018 has been prepared. The General Administration Department of this seniority has been prepared in accordance with the instructions in Government Decision No. SRV-2011/ PR No. 284/12, dated 21.10.2011.
2. The Additional Director General and Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune is requested to inform the Additional Superintendent, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail, Class-I of the temporary seniority of service as on 01.01.2018 to all the officers of the cadre of Additional Superintendent, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail, Class-I (including officers on leave/ on deputation) and seek their objections/ objections/ objections regarding the same. In case of any objection/ error received from the concerned Additional Superintendent, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail, Class-I, the information regarding the issues in their representation should be examined as per the records in your office and sent to this department with your feedback within a period of one month. In case of non-receipt of objections/ errors within the prescribed period, final seniority shall be published on the assumption that the information/ details of the officers mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
