The Proposal:
The complainant in the case vide G.R. No. 98/2017 of the Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai has registered a case against the accused company. The writ petition filed in the High Court, Mumbai as well as the National Company Law Tribunal, Mumbai has made 5 claims against the accused company. (Insolvency Application No. 4578/2018, CP (B)-514/ MB/ 2018, CP (IB)-3060/ MB/ 2018, CP (IB)-472/ MB/ 2018, CP (IB) 2975/ MB/ 2018). Shri Pradeep Gharat as Special Public Prosecutor in the cases arising out of crime, to effectively argue on behalf of the Government in the High Court, Mumbai and National Law Tribunal, Mumbai. The Department of Law and Justice has approved the appointment of Mr. Pradeep Gharat as Special Public Prosecutor to effectively argue on behalf of the Government during the High Court, Mumbai hearings.
The matter of payment of professional fees to Mr. Pradeep Gharat, who was appointed as Special Public Prosecutor to look after the court proceedings on behalf of the Government in the said justice case in the High Court, Mumbai, was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
98/2017 filed with the Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai. Shri Pradeep Gharat, Special Public Prosecutor, High Court, Mumbai and National Law Tribunal, Mumbai, who has been appointed to look after the court proceedings on behalf of the Government, is being allowed to pay professional fees as follows for effective hearing on a daily basis. Apart from this professional fee, no other benefit will be admissible to Mr. Pradeep Gharat.
