Introduction: Mr. Iqbal Hussain Kasam Momin, Poshi/ 10192 Mr. Momin has been working in the police force since 15/9/1989. The patient was diagnosed on 29/5/2005. He is being treated at the Nagpada Police Hospital. Police Surgeon Nagpada Police Hospital vide letter dated 11/5/2006 referred him to JJ Group of Hospitals, Mumbai for further treatment. As the report was not received from the Group Hospitals, Mumbai and Mr. Momin was still in the hospital, the Senior Police Inspector, Criminal Investigation Branch, gave the report. In a letter dated 20/10/2010, Mr. Momin was directed to appear before the Medical Board. The Medical Board of the Hospital vide letter dated 18/12/2010 reported Mr. Momin as medically totally and permanently unfit for further service. Momin has 100% disability certificate issued by Sir J.J. Group of Hospitals dated 5/1/2011. A proposal has been submitted to the government to create a supernumerary post for Mr. Momin as there is no equivalent post in the office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai. Accordingly, as per the provisions of Section 20 (4) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, in case of disability while in service, the employee shall be given posting in a suitable post in accordance with his disability and shall be given salary protection of the post holding origin. If the post is not available, the employee will be given posting on it by making additional number of postings till the availability of another suitable post or till the date of his retirement as per the prescribed age, whichever is earlier. Considering the above provision, the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved creation of one supernumerary post in the cadre of Police Constable for Mr. Iqbal Hussain Kasam Momin, Police Constable with all benefits including superannuation, with effect from 05/01/2011 till the date of his superannuation as per the prescribed age or for such period as may be earlier.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government hereby approve the creation of one supernumerary post in the cadre of Police Constable for drawing salary in the pay scale of S-8 (25,500-81, 100) for Mr. Iqbal Hussain Kasam Momin, Poshi/ 10192, Special Branch-1, with effect from 05/01/2011.
