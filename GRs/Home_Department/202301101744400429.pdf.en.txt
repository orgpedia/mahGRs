Introduction: The State Excise Department is an important source of revenue for the State. The government needs to strengthen the revenue department. The zonal offices of the department should be located on government land and the government intends to construct new structures where the structures are old/ dilapidated. To finalize the revised type plans of various field offices of the State Excise Department. The meeting was chaired by the Chief Secretary. 17.11.2022. As per the decision taken by the Committee in the said meeting, the Chief Architect, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai vide Reference No. 3 submitted to the Government. Accordingly, in order to maintain the integrity of these structures, the matter of determining the type plan for the field offices of the State Excise Department was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the field offices of the State Excise Department, the construction area limit is being fixed as follows.
