The Proposal:
The Government has decided to construct a total of 19 service quarters, 18 of type-1 and one of type-2, at the Police Station, Alipur, Taluka Hinganghat, on the establishment of the Superintendent of Police, Wardha, in the Home Department, No. CRP-0506/ 1132/ PR No.250/ POL-7, dated 08.03.2010, Rs. Administrative approval was granted for an estimated cost of Rs. 2,55,30, 000/-. However, the work could not be taken up as funds were not released.
As per Para 262 of the Maharashtra Public Works Rules Manual," The administrative approval granted to the construction or the technical approval granted to the estimates of such construction shall ordinarily cease to have effect after a period of five years from the date on which it is granted. " In this case, the said administrative sanction has not been implemented as the period of grant of administrative sanction for the said work is more than five years. Hence, the government took this decision.
Government's decision
Government Resolution, Home Department, No. CRP-0506/ 1132/ PR No.250/ POL-7 dated 08.03.2010 is hereby cancelled.
