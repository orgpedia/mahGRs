Government Decision: A total of 1168 posts under the control of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved. Further, as per the Government Decision No.D.P.-2012/ PR No. 112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway. Out of which, 167 temporary posts of Assistant Director and Public Prosecutor in the 5 District Offices under the control of the Deputy Director, Government Complaints, Konkan Range along with Annexure-C will be filled by the Home Department vide Government Resolution No. 27.03.2018 has been extended upto 30.09.2018. Now, the Government hereby grants extension of 167 temporary posts in Annexure-C from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than 6 months. Also, the extension of tenure will not be applicable to the posts which the government has already ordered to abolish.
