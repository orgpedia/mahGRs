Preamble: As requested by the Commissioner of Transport, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, vide letter No. 3 above, the Government was in the process of giving administrative and financial approval for the expenditure incurred on training of 100 newly appointed Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors of the Motor Vehicles Department at the Central Institute of Road Transport, Pune (CIRT, PUNE).
Government's decision:
Central Institute of Road Transport, Pune (CIRT, PUNE) has invited online applications from eligible and interested candidates for filling Eight (8) weeks training to the newly recruited 100 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors (AMV) in the Department of Motor Vehicles, Government of India through direct recruitment to be posted at Central Institute of Road Transport, Pune (CIRT, PUNE). Administrative and financial sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 1,84,80, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Four Lakh Eighty Thousand only).
