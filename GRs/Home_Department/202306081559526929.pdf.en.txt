The Proposal:
Many political parties and social organizations follow the path of agitation to draw attention to various issues of public interest. Therefore, a case has been registered against them. The Government has taken policy decisions from time to time in the light of the above decisions of the Government regarding withdrawal of cases filed in connection with offences arising out of political and social movements.
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: PRO-0516/ PR No.106/ VISA-2
The government receives a large number of requests from many public representatives to withdraw cases filed due to political and social movements. The government should take a firm decision in this regard. For this, the government was considering the formation of a cabinet sub-committee to take a decision at the government level to give suggestions/ recommendations to the concerned public prosecutor or assistant public prosecutor for withdrawal of cases.
Government's decision:
The date is May 3, 2023. As per the decision taken in the Cabinet meeting, the following Cabinet Sub-Committee is being constituted to take a decision at the government level to advise/ recommend to the concerned Public Prosecutor or Assistant Public Prosecutor in accordance with Section 321 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, on the withdrawal of cases related to political and social movements:
