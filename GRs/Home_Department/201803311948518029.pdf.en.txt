The Proposal:
In the Home Guard Office, 5253 Home Guard were recruited in the year 2017. They need to be provided. Therefore, it is proposed to purchase 15759 meters of cloth to provide 2 uniform sets for Home Guards for three years and the estimated cost for this is Rs. 23,48,091/-. It has been approved by the Government on 12.3.2018.
In this regard, the procurement process has started and the payment to the supplier needs to be made soon. Accordingly, a provision of Rs. 23,48,091/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Office of the Director General, Home Guards, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. Mass/ Work- 5/2018/1022, Dy. As per the proposal dated 17.3.2018, Rs. 23,48,091/- was requested to be drawn on short payment.
An amount of Rs. 23,48,091/-is being approved as a special case to meet the expenditure of the said amount under head B-1, 2070-Other Administrative Services, (00) (107) Hamguard, 00 (00) (01) Home Guards (2070 0281) (Dattamat) 21- Supplies and Materials for the year 2017-18 subject to the following conditions: 1.
