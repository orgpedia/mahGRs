The Christmas Festival 2021
A new strain of coronavirus, Omicron, has been detected in India in the last few days.
A new risk of infection has emerged. There is a strong possibility of transmission of this Omicron virus strain to the general public/ residents with a very high intensity.
In this backdrop, Christians are expected to celebrate Christmas without large gatherings as a health precaution. Below are the guidelines for the same:
Christians should celebrate Christmas this year in a simple way with full precautions from the point of view of health.
In view of COVID-19, the Government of Maharashtra has issued Circular No.DMU/ 2020/ CR.92/ DisM-1 of the Department of Revenue and Forest, Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation. It is imperative to strictly follow the revised guidelines issued under 27/11/2021.
3.On the occasion of Christmas/ Christmas, people will be allowed to attend the church up to 50% of the available seating capacity. Special care should be taken to ensure that there is no crowding in the church, social distancing is maintained and masks and sanitizers are used. Disinfection should also be arranged in the church.
On Christmas Day, scenes from the life of Lord Jesus, Christmas trees, etc. are placed in the church. Appropriate measures should be taken to ensure social distancing and hygiene.
At least four choir singers should be included in the church to sing the praise of Lord Jesus Christ. Social distancing should be followed by using different mikes at that time.
Shops and stalls should not be set up outside the church.
7) Avoid large crowds in public places.
No religious/ cultural events or processions should be organized in any way to attract crowds.
9) Fireworks should not be set off. The rules and regulations regarding noise pollution should be strictly followed.
10.To prevent the spread of COVID-19 and especially the Omicron virus species, the rules prescribed by the Urban Development Department, Relief and Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department of the Government as well as the concerned Metropolitan Municipality, Police, Local Administration will be strictly complied with.
