Preamble: As per the provisions of the Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995, in the Reference No. 1 Home Department, Govt. As on 9.03.2011, suitable posts in the Police Force and Prisons Department establishments have been ensured for persons with disabilities. The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 1995 has been amended and the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 has been enacted.
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure posts in government services for persons with disabilities. The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has issued a notification in this regard. As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, the list of posts to be ensured for Persons with Disabilities has been published on the website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading Policy/ Act/ Rules Notification List of Post identification suitable for Person with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021. According to the list published by the Central Government, the duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale of the posts in the list published by the Central Government in respect of the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which are in the state government establishment, will be in the service of the state government.
