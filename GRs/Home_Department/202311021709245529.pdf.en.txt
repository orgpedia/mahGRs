Preamble: In pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Tamil Nadu based Allen Group, L-69 Manikampalayan Housing Unit, Erode, Tamil Nadu for obtaining permission to introduce smart card phone facility for the inmates of the prison, the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Pune (APOMS) had informed the Divisional Office to submit the feedback. Accordingly, all the Deputy Inspector Generals (Prisons) have given their consent to the proposal of smart card phone facility of Allen Group and expressed their unwillingness to implement it. Accordingly, Additional Director General and Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune also recommended to start smart card phone facility at Yerawada Central Jail on a pilot basis. Accordingly, the proposal received from the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune regarding the approval to start the free Inmate Calling System provided by the Allen Group at Yerwada Central Jail, Pune on a pilot basis. 1 has been approved by the government. A report has also been received from Yerawada Central Jail in this regard. The Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune has issued a circular regarding the implementation of smart card telephone facility in all prisons in the state. The proposal was submitted under letter No. 2 and the matter of approving the proposal was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Smart card phone facility for prisoners has been made operational on a pilot basis at Yerawada Central Jail in the state and the government has approved the implementation of such smart card phone facility for prisoners in all jails of the state.
