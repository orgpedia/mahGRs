government decision.
The Government have approved the extension of 68 temporary posts of Non-Monitorial Paediatricians and 20 posts of other different cadres in the establishment of the offices and other offices of the Commissioner of Police and Superintendent of Police under the Director General of Police, State of Maharashtra, specified in the Government Decision, Home Department, No. Parivartan-0316/ PR No. 198/ Pol-7, dated 30.09.2016, with effect from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019, on the following conditions:
Conditions
1) The extension will not be applicable to posts lying vacant for more than six months.
2) The terms and conditions under which the said 68 posts have been sanctioned should be strictly complied with.
3) Review the posts and submit the revised pattern for the approval of the government immediately. It should be noted that after this, the term of the said posts will not be extended to determine the format.
The expenditure on salary and allowances of the said 68 posts shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the respective heads of accounts for the financial years 2018-19 and 2019-20.
