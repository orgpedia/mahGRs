the Proposal.
The Board of Directors of Mumbai Police Foundation has approved the establishment of a Trust named" Mumbai Police Foundation" through Government Resolution, Home Department, No. In accordance with clause (4) of the said Government Order, the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, vide letter dated 01.11.2017, has submitted the Draft Trust Deed for registration of the said Trust to the Commissioner of Endowments, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai, for approval of the Government.
Further, vide letter dated 22.01.2018, the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, has also proposed the names of three eminent persons from different areas of the city of Mumbai, mentioned at serial number 5 in volume 10 thereof, for appointment of the first trustees of the said Trust.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
Government's decision
Approval is hereby accorded for registration of the Trust named" Mumbai Police Foundation" with the Commissioner of Endowments, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai, in the accompanying Draft Trust Deed, including the names of three eminent persons from different areas of the city of Mumbai to be appointed as the first Trustee mentioned in Annexure 5 of Volume 10.
