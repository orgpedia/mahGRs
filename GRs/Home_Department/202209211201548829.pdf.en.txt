The Proposal:
On the subject of" Police Housing." Under the chairmanship of Chief Minister. As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 27.07.2022 in the presence of the Deputy Chief Minister, the Government was considering the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of the Additional Chief Secretary (Home) to redevelop the police colonies on the land owned by MHADA in Mumbai and Maharashtra through MHADA and provide service quarters to the police from it.
Government's decision:
A committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Home) to redevelop police colonies on the land owned by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region and Area Development Authority (MHADA) through MHADA and provide service accommodation to the police from it.
Additional Chief Secretary (Home), Home Department Chairman 2. Principal Secretary, Housing Department Member 3. Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai Member 4. Managing Director General, Maharashtra State Member Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Limited.
Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai Member 6. Principal Secretary (Special), Home Department Member 7. Vice Chairman, MHADA Member 8. Special Executive Officer, MHADA, Mumbai Circle Member 9. Additional Director General of Police and Joint Managing Member Secretary Director, Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Limited.
The Member Secretary of the said Committee shall convene meetings from time to time as required and the minutes shall be made available to all concerned and the Home Department. Also, the home department should be informed about the action taken from time to time.
The Committee shall take necessary measures to expedite the redevelopment.
