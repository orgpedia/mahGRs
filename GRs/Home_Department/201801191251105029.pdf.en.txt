Government Resolution No.RLI 2017/ PR No.210/ Transport-5 Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: January 19, 2018.
Reference: Minutes of the Cabinet Meeting dated 03.10.2017.
The Proposal:
In view of the accident on the foot overbridge connecting Elphinstone Road and Parel Railway Stations, it has been decided in the Cabinet Meeting held on 03.10.2017 to constitute a Cabinet Sub-Committee to review the safety of railway stations, foot overbridges and other related places and to suggest measures for the safety of the citizens so that such incidents do not occur in future. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a cabinet sub-committee.
Government's decision:
In view of the accident on the foot overbridge connecting Elphinstone Road and Parel railway stations, the following Cabinet Sub-Committee is being constituted to review the safety of railway stations, foot overbridges and other related places and to suggest measures for the safety of the citizens so that such incidents do not occur in future.
1) Ma. Chief Minister.
2) Mr. Vinod Tawde- Ma. Minister (School Education, Sports and Youth Welfare, Higher and Technical Education, Marathi Language, Cultural Affairs, Minority Development and Wakf) and Guardian Minister, Mumbai Suburban District.
3) Mr. Diwakar Raote, Ma. Minister of Transport, Rural Development.
