There are a total of 8 posts of Director General of Police in the Indian Police Service.
The posts include Director General, Home Guards and Director, Civil Defence, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
The government has decided to create a separate post of Director General of Police, Civil Defence, by dividing the posts of Director General, Home Guards and Director, Civil Defence, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
As per the proviso to Section 22 (n) (2) of the Maharashtra Police Act, Smt. Rashmi Shukla, Bhapose, Commissioner, State Intelligence Department, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being promoted to the post of Director, Civil Defence in the rank of Director General of Police.
