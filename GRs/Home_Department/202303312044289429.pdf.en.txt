The Proposal:
The Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has requested the Government through a letter in this regard to provide funds to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for reimbursement of concessional rates for the month of February, 2023. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds to Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for reimbursement of concessional rates for the month of February, 2023 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In the year 2022-23, from the provision made under the head" 2041 0018-33 Financial Assistance" of the Home (Transport) Department, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation has been given Rs. 165.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Sixty Five Crore only) is being approved for providing funds in cash.
2) Rs. 165.00 crore should be met from the provisions available in the financial year 2022-23 and the expenditure should be spent under the head" Demand No.B-3, Accounts Head 2041, Taxes on Vehicles (00) 001, Operations and Administration (01) Commissioner of Transport (01) (01) Establishment- Commissioner of Transport (Adaptation) (Mandatory) (20410018) 33, Financial Assistance."
This support is" unconditional."
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to collect and disburse the amount to be paid to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation by the Accountant in his office.
