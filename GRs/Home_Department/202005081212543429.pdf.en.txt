In the preamble, the government has decided to provide information about Naxalites in the districts of Gadchiroli, Gondia, Chandrapur, Nanded, Yavatmal and Bhandara. The rules regarding the award of cash prizes have been implemented under 24/11/2006.
The amount of cash payable has been increased as per the Government decision dated 8/8/2014. The powers to sanction cash rewards to such informers have been delegated to the Superintendent of Police, Deputy Inspector General of Police, Additional Director General of Police (Special Operations), State of Maharashtra, Mumbai, by fixing the limit of payment.
The Home Department has issued a government order to authorise the sanction of cash to persons who provide information about the activities of Naxalite organizations and Naxals in Naxal-affected districts, as well as other districts and urban areas of the state. The proposal for partial modification was under consideration of the Government on 8/8/2014.
As per the proposal, the state government has decided to give cash rewards to those who provide information about Naxal activities in Gadchiroli, Gondia, Chandrapur, Nanded, Yavatmal and Bhandara districts. Also, the authority to approve the amount of the said prize shall be as mentioned below. :
