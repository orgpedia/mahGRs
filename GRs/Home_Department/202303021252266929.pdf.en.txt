Introduction: Kallappana Avade Co-op, Hatkanangale, Kolhapur. Industrial Estate and Integrated Textile Park Ltd. The proposal received to start a brake test track for the vehicle's fitness certificate on the site of this cooperative society was under the consideration of the government.
Government decision Kallappana Avade Co-op, Hatkanangale, Kolhapur. Industrial Estate and Integrated Textile Park Ltd. Approval is being given for starting of Brake Test Track for vehicle fitness certificate at the site of this Cooperative Society subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) It is expected to start the test of the vehicle on this road 250 m from there. As the distance is steep, crash guards should be installed on those sides to prevent possible accidents.
(b) The gates of the factories/ godowns of the property holders on the side of the said road shall be closed permanently and the movement of people and vehicles through it shall be completely prohibited.
e) The Kallappana Avade Textile Park, which owns the entire public transport on this road, should transfer the road to the Transport Department free of cost.
e) Potential public traffic should be prevented by placing barricading on the side where the brake test passing track will start.
