For the construction/ upgradation and modernization of bus stands of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, the Government provides grant in aid for the construction of capital buildings. Accordingly, under the scheme of Modernization, Construction, Expansion and Upgradation of Bus Stations of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, for the year 2018-19, Rs. 40.00 crore has been provided in the Budget. The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) has issued a notification in this regard. As per the letter dated 16.11.2018, for the year 2018-19, Rs. 40.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Construction/ Modernization/ Extension of Bus Stations of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation Rs. 40.00 crore (Rupees Forty Crores only) is being disbursed.
