Introduction: The above reference No. Two separate Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district as per the government notification. Reference No. A total of 24 temporary posts have been created under the government decision. The 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the said Tribunal are mentioned in Reference No. 3 As per the Government decision dated 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. Since the extension of these 24 posts has expired as on 31.08.2020 and as per Reference No. 4 As the Chairman, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur, vide letter dated 11.08.2020, had submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of the said 24 temporary posts, the matter of extension of the said posts from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Extension of 24 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Nagpur from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. The extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending before the Tribunal are disposed of expeditiously and the statement of claims disposed of is submitted to the Government after every three months.
