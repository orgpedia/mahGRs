(2) This order shall come into force immediately. The transferred officers should take charge of the post immediately after the transfer. The Director, Directorate of Forensic Science Laboratory shall strictly follow the following instructions for the immediate and efficient execution of the transfer order.
a) On receipt of the transfer order, the transferred officers shall be relieved from their present posts. The officers should be recruited without delay after they are relieved from their duties.
