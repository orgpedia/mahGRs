Introduction: The above reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 27.5.2016, the post of Joint Commissioner of Police, Economic Offences, Brihanmumbai has been created and he has been declared as the Head of the Department.
As per this Government decision, there is a need for an independent Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the salary/ accounts/ establishment, leave, retirement, departmental inquiry and other important work of the executive/ non-executive posts in the office of the Joint Commissioner of Police, Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai. 3 here. The Assistant Commissioner of Police (Administration), Economic Offences Wing, Brihanmumbai has been declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" vide Government Order dated 18.02.2019.
The Joint Commissioner of Police (Administration), Office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai vide the above Reference No. 1 here. vide letter dated 24.07.2020, requested for transfer of 586 executive/ non-executive posts from the total of 2074 executive/ non-executive posts in the establishment of the unit office of Joint Commissioner of Police (Crime), Brihanmumbai under the office of Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai to the unit office of Joint Commissioner of Police, Economic Offences, Brihanmumbai under the office of Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai. Following this, the government took decision on this request.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: From the 2074 posts of Executive/ Non-Executive Cadre in the establishment of Joint Commissioner of Police (Crime) on the establishment of Commissioner of Police, Mumbai, in the establishment of the constituent office, in the accompanying statement, column No. Approval is being given to transfer 586 executive/ non-executive posts mentioned in Sections 3 and 4 along with Commissioner of Police (Economic Offences) to this constituent office.
