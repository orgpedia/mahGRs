The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has submitted a proposal to the Commissioner of Police, Nashik City, for obtaining administrative approval for the repair of 60 officers quarters and 160 staff quarters at" Snehbandhan Park" Police Colony, Nashik, under the jurisdiction of the Commissioner of Police, Nashik City.
The Sub-Divisional Engineer, West (Public Works) Sub-Division, Nashik, has prepared the rates for the year 2017-18 for the said work. The cost estimates amounting to Rs. 2,75,06, 462/- have been submitted and certified by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Nashik.
3. Estimates of the said expenditure have been examined by the Public Works Department, Ministry and it is estimated to be Rs. 2,82,05, 778/- has been amended and, on certain conditions, has agreed to grant administrative approval.
4. Accordingly, for the repair of 60 officers quarters and 160 staff quarters at" Snehabandhan Park" Police Colony, Nashik, under the jurisdiction of Commissioner of Police, Nashik City, Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the cost estimate of Rs. 2,82,05, 778/- on the condition that the cost estimate is certified by the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Department, Nashik and the Commissioner of Police, Nashik City.
