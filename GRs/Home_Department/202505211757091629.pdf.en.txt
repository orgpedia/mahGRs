The Proposal: -
The government has approved the procurement of a new system called AMBIS (Automated Multi-Modal Biometric Identification System) for the Maharashtra Police force. The proposal to replace 07 servers of AMBIS system provided at the area level with 05 regional divisions and 02 new police stations in Brihanmumbai with functional ones has been discussed and approved in the High Powered Committee meetings dated 13/02/2025 as the Government has approved to change the levels of Chance Print Verification in the project to match the levels of Central Government NAFIS computer system.
