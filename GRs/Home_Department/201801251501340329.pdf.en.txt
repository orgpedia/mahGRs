Preamble: 1 (one) temporary post in the office of the State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai may be referred to as Reference No. The period was extended from 01.10.2017 to 31.12.2017. The term of office of the 1 (one) peon is expiring on 31.12.2017.
The Manager, State Transport Appellate Tribunal, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai has now issued a proposal regarding extension of 1 (one) temporary post of peon vide Reference No. 2 has been submitted to the Government as per the letter here and the matter was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The 1 (one) temporary post of Manager, State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, peon is being extended from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018. This extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending with the State Transport Appellate Tribunal are settled expeditiously, the last statement of claims settled in the month of February 2018 is submitted to the Government and if 50% of the pending claims as on 01.01.2018 are not found to be settled, no further extension will be granted.
