GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to re-employ the officers/ staff who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category on the establishment of the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories, Mumbai, but who have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or have submitted the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes, pending court cases regarding the caste validity certificate, these 6 superannuated posts are being created under this Government Decision as per Return-1 and Return-2 from the date these posts have been transferred on the establishment of the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories, Mumbai, under the Home Department.
Return No. 1:- A total of 6 officers/ employees who have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or who have submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes, pending court cases regarding the caste validity certificate.
Statement No.2: Number of posts in the Forensic Science Laboratory.
(4) The Government have decided to create a number of posts of officers/ employees who will be transferred to the number of posts in which they are presently working and the number of posts will be temporarily for a period of 11 months or till the date on which the officer/ employee concerned would have retired from service, whichever is earlier.
The expenditure incurred on the salary of the superannuated officer/ employee shall be covered under the head of salary accounts of the department/ office in which the officer/ employee is presently working and shall be met from the grants of the relevant financial year.
