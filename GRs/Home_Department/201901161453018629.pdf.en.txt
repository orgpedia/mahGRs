The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, has submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the construction of the remaining of a total of 41 service quarters for police officers and staff at the Police Station, Amarnath, under the Superintendent of Police, Jalgaon.
Government Decision to construct six construction projects of residential and administrative buildings of the Police Department in Jalgaon District, Home Department, No. Administrative approval was accorded vide CRP-0309/ PR No.171/ Pol. 5-7, dated 08.03.2010. The works included construction of service quarters for police officers and staff (total 43) at Police Station, Amarnath. The estimated cost of the project is Rs. 5,56,39, 000/-. All these works are being carried out by the Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Limited. The decision was taken by the government.
Thereafter, vide Government Resolution, Home Department, No.CRP-0715/ No.246/ Pol-7, dated 02.03.2016, a sum of Rs. Administrative approval was granted for an estimated cost of Rs. 5,56,39, 000/- (superseding the earlier administrative approval). The cost estimates were based on the 2007-08 tariff schedule.
