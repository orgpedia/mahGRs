Preamble: With a view to expedite and expedite the completion of railway projects in rural and underdeveloped areas of the State, the State Government has adopted the policy of providing financial support in the range of 40% to 50% of the project cost in selected railway projects. Accordingly, the state government was considering to provide 50% financial participation in the Kampa-Chimur-Worora Broad Gauge Railway Project.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Cabinet in its meeting held on 10.08.2022 has taken the following decisions regarding financial contribution of the State Government for the new Broad Gauge Railway line project of Kampa-Chimur-Worora.
i) In-principle approval is being given to the new broad gauge rail line project of Kampa-Chimur-Worora.
In this project, according to the Detailed Feasibility Report prepared by the Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Corporation, it is being approved in principle to request the Central Government to bear 50% of the total cost of the project by the State Government and the remaining 50% by the Central Government.
