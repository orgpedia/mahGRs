The Proposal:
As requested by the Transport Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, vide letter No. 3 above, the Government was in the process of giving administrative and financial approval for the expenditure incurred on training of the second batch of 84 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors of the Motor Vehicles Department at Maharashtra Guptavarta Prabodhini, Pune.
Government Decision: The second batch of 84 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors in the Motor Vehicle Department of Maharashtra Guptavarta Prabodhini, Pune will be trained for eight weeks. 56,44,800/- (Rupees Fifty Six Lakh Forty Four Thousand Eight Hundred only) is being given administrative and financial sanction.
