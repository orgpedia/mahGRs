Government Order:
As per the Government Resolution, Home Department No. SPP-2295/ PR No. 149/ Pol-10, dated 20th May, 1997, the posts of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group A have been created in various establishments under the Director, Directorate of Prosecutions, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
Further, 299 new posts have been created vide Government Resolution, Home Department No.DPP-2012/ PR No.112/ Poll-10 dated 28.05.2013. These posts are to be filled directly by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission. Accordingly, out of the 174 candidates recommended for the post by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission vide letter dated 22.09.2016 and dated 28.09.2016, Shri Sheikh Qadeer Usman is being appointed as a substitute for the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group-A under probation as per the provisions of the Revenue Department Allotment Rules, 2015 and Second Amendment Act, 2017 for appointment to Group A and Group B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) posts of the Government of Maharashtra by direct recruitment and promotion.
