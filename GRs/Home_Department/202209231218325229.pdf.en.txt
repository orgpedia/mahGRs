Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government, 1,101 temporary posts in various District/ City Police Establishments in the Highway Safety Squad of the State were extended till 31/08/2022. Now, vide this Order, the Government is granting extension of tenure of 1,101 temporary posts in various District/ City Police Establishments in the accompanying prospectus from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 subject to the following conditions:
The extension will not be applicable to temporary posts (excluding executive posts) lying vacant for more than six months.
2. The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned subject to the terms and conditions to be followed.
Government Resolution, Finance Department, No.Assank-1001/ PR No. 29/2001/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 10th September, 2001, No.Assank-1004/ PR. No. 12/2004/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 19th August, 2004, No. No. 8/16/ Fiscal Reform-1, dated 21st September, 2016 and No. The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is required to complete the process of review of posts as per No. 8/16/ APC, dated 10th February, 2017 immediately and obtain the final approval of the High Level Committee to the revised pattern of the office within the prescribed time frame in any case.
If the process of reviewing the posts is not completed within the prescribed time, if the above temporary posts are not extended for a further period and if the question of salary and allowances of the officers/ employees arises, it will be the responsibility of the concerned office.
