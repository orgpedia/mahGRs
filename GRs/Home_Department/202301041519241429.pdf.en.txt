Under Section 5 of the Maharashtra Police Act (22 of 1951), subject to the provisions of the said Act, the State Government is empowered to prescribe, by general or special order, the various ranks and number of posts, their duties and functions and powers in the police force.
Also, under Section 7 of the said Act, the state government has the power to appoint a police officer as Commissioner of Police, Additional Commissioner of Police, Joint Commissioner of Police. The Commissioner of Police, subject to the control of the Director General of Police, has the power to determine the duties and responsibilities, powers to be exercised, etc. to be performed by such officer.
2. The Government was considering the transfer of the post of Special Commissioner of Police to the rank of Additional Director General of Police to enable the Joint Commissioner of Police to supervise the work of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, more effectively. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision
In order to supervise more effectively the working of the Joint Commissioner of Police under the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, as a matter of administrative urgency, considering the requirement of the post of" Additional Director General of Police" rank, the Maharashtra State Electricity Board has decided to appoint a Joint Commissioner of Police to supervise the work of the Company.
It is being approved to transfer the post of Director, Security and Enforcement, in the rank of Additional Director General of Police, to the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai. The government has decided to rename the post as' Special Commissioner of Police." The Special Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, will control and supervise the work of all Joint Commissioners of Police under the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai.
This Government Order is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Government under Section 5 of the Maharashtra Police Act (22 of 1951).
