The Proposal:
Public transport is a very important and urgent service for the passengers of the state and public transport is used by the passengers on priority for economical travel.
Public transport services are being developed and expanded under various municipal corporations in the state. An agreement has been signed between the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways and the World Bank to develop passenger transport facilities. The Reimbursable Advisory Services (RAS) has been launched in collaboration with the World Bank.
The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, Government of India, has approved the setting up of a Project Implementation Unit in collaboration with the World Bank to develop passenger mobility. The directions have been issued vide letter dated 13.02.2019. Accordingly, the government had constituted a committee for the implementation of the project.
Government's decision:
Implementation of state-level projects to develop passenger mobility in the state
