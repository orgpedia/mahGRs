The proposal:
The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) has suspended most of its services, except for essential ones, due to the Covid-19 lockdown.
The employees of the corporation are suffering as their salaries are getting reduced. Therefore, R.P.
The government was considering sanctioning funds to the corporation to pay salaries to its employees.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) to pay pending salaries of employees
Out of the aforesaid contingency fund of Rs. Rs 120 crore has been allocated for this. The government has decided to transfer the sanctioned funds to the state government.

The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to collect and disburse the amount to be paid to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation by the Accountant in his office.
The Transport Commissioner, State of Maharashtra, in his order regarding handing over of the amount to the Assessing and Disbursing Officer for spending from the Contingency Fund, has issued a Contingency Fund Advance Government Memorandum on the payments to be submitted to the Treasury at the time of spending from the advance amount. This instruction should be underlined with red ink by mentioning clear instructions to be written in red ink as approved under 10.11.2020.
