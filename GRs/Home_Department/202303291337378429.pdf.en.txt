The Proposal:
The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, Government of India has issued Guidelines on 27.11.2020 under the Motor Vehicle Aggregator Guidelines 2020 for the App& Web Based Aggregators to carry on the business of passenger transport. Further, the State Government vide notification dated 09.03.2022 has made it mandatory for the App& Web based aggregators in the State to obtain permission from the competent authority for carrying on the business of passenger transport. In the case The Supreme Court As per the order dated 13.02.2023, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to constitute a committee to frame the Maharashtra Regulation of the Aggregators Rules, 2022 for app-based vehicles in the State in accordance with the Central Motor Vehicle Aggregator Guidelines 2020.
The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
As per the Central Motor Vehicle Aggregator Guidelines 2020, a committee is being constituted to formulate the Maharashtra Regulation of the Aggregators Rules, 2022 for app-based vehicles in the state.
