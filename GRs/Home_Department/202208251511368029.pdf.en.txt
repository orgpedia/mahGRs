Preamble: Decision of the Government. As on 05.01.2022, two new Regional Divisions namely Amravati and Nanded have been created by bifurcating the Regional Divisions of Nagpur and Aurangabad in the State Excise Department with a total of 42 new posts of 38 regular posts of 19 each and 4 posts of 2 each by external agencies.
Also, Divisional Deputy Commissioner, State Excise, Amravati Division, Amravati and Divisional Deputy Commissioner, State Excise, Nanded Division, Nanded have been declared as" Regional Office Heads." The Commissioner, State Excise Office has requested to declare the newly created two offices of Divisional Deputy Commissioner, Raushu, Amravati and Divisional Deputy Commissioner, Raushu, Nanded as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and Divisional Deputy Commissioner as" Controlling Officer." Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Accounts Officer in the office of the Divisional Deputy Commissioner, Raushu, Amravati and Divisional Deputy Commissioner, Raushu, Nanded as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the concerned Divisional Deputy Commissioner, State Excise as the Controlling Officer. The government has taken the following decisions:
As per the provisions of Rule 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the newly created offices of Divisional Deputy Commissioner, State Excise, Amravati and Divisional Deputy Commissioner, State Excise, Nanded are being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officers." Also, the concerned Divisional Deputy Commissioner, State Excise is being declared as the" Controlling Officer." These rights will be limited to financial matters.
