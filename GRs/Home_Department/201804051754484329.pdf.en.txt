Memorandum-
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for conducting structural audit of police quarters buildings at Dharavi and Dadar under the Commissioner of Police, Railway, Mumbai.
The area of the said work is 17, 166.01 sq.m. Sub-Division, Dadar, Mumbai Rs. 74.42 per sq.m. Estimates have been submitted for an expenditure of Rs. 12,77,580/-.
The said cost estimates have been examined by the Department of Public Works, Ministry and, on certain conditions, have agreed to grant administrative approval.
4. Accordingly, for conducting structural audit of buildings of police quarters at Dharavi and Dadar under the control of Commissioner of Police, Railway, Mumbai, Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the cost estimate of Rs. 12,77,580/- through the following conditions: 1. The Commissioner of Police, Railways, shall certify the cost estimate.
2) Work should not be started without the availability of funds.
