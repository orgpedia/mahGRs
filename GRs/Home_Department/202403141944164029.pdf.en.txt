The Proposal: -
The association of one-screen theatres in the state requested the government to waive the licence fee charged for one-screen theatres during the period when theatres were closed due to COVID restrictions. In this regard, the government has decided to give them a concession in the license fee.
The Government's Decisions:
As per the Maharashtra Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, 1966, licensed single screen theatres in the state are exempted from the licence fee as follows.
A) One screen theatres which have renewed film licences till 2019 by paying licence fee - 1) Full exemption for the year 2020.
2) 50% discount for the year 2021.
(b) One screen theatres which have renewed film licences during 2020 and 2021 by paying licence fee - 1) Full capacity for the period 2022.
2. 50% relaxation for the period 2023 2.
