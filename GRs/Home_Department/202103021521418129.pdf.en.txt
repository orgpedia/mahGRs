As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 62 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in the public interest at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them prematurely from Government service. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 here. The Government Decision dated 10.06.2019 prescribes the procedure and general instructions regarding Service Review. Accordingly, for the review of services of 1) Group-A (Gazetted) (Senior Pay Scale) Officers in the cadre of 7600 and above but less than 10000, 2) Group-A (Gazetted) (Junior Pay Scale) Officers in the cadre of 7600 and less than 7600 and Group-B (Gazetted) Officers in the Motor Vehicles Department, Reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 02.08.2019, a Divisional Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Transport).
The Departmental Review Committee headed by the Additional Chief Secretary (Transport) has approved the proposal to review the services of Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Group-A cadre officers in the Motor Vehicle Department at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
