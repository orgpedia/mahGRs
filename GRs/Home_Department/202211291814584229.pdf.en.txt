Government Decision: Reference No. 1 here. A total of 52 officers/ employees of the establishment of the Additional Director General of Police and Director, Binatari Sandesh Vibhag, Maharashtra State, Pune under this Department were temporarily transferred to the above post for 11 months or till the date on which they would have retired from service, whichever is earlier, to re-employ the officers/ employees who were appointed under the Government decision dated 21.12.2019 from the Scheduled Tribes category, but could not produce the caste validity certificate or who had submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes, pending court cases regarding the caste validity certificate. Since the period of 11 months of the officers/ employees who were appointed to the superannuation post had expired, the matter of extension of the superannuation and above appointments to such services was under consideration of the Government.
Reference No. 2. 5 here. As per the Government Order dated 21.12.2020, a total number of 52 supernumerary posts have been created in the establishment of Additional Director General and Director of Police, Binatari Sandesh Division, Maharashtra State, Pune for re-appointment of officers/ staff who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or have submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes, pending court cases regarding the caste validity certificate.
