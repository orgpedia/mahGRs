Read:
The Vice Chairman and Managing Director, M.R.M. RAAP/ ACCOUNTS/ REGISTRATION/ 867 dated 19.05.2021.
The proposal:
Due to the lockdown announced in the wake of the Covid-19 pandemic, most of the transport services of the state transport corporation were closed except for essential transport services. Therefore, the salaries of the employees of the corporation for the month of May, 2021 have been exhausted. Deputy Chief Minister (Finance and Planning) The meeting was held on 09.06.2021. As per the decision taken at the meeting, Rs. 600.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Finance Department has sanctioned Rs. 600.00 crore has been sanctioned.
Out of these, Rs. 300,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Hundred Crores only) is being approved to be provided in cash out of the provision for the financial year 2021-22. The funds are being sanctioned subject to the condition that they will be adjusted against the future liabilities of the Maharashtra State Transport Corporation.
2. The expenditure shall be met from the provisions available in the financial year 2021-22 and the expenditure shall be covered under the head" Demand No.B-3, 2041, Tax on Vehicles 00,001, Operation and Administration (01) Transport Commissioner (01) (01) Establishment- Transport Commissioner, (Endowment) (Mandatory) (2041 0018) 33, Financial Assistance."
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to collect and disburse the amount to be paid to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation by the Accountant in his office.
4. This Government decision is based on the informal reference of the Finance Department No. 248/2021/ Expenditure-8, Dy. being issued with the consent received under 16.06.2021.
