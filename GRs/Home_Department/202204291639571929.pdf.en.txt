Government Order:
Whereas, Mr. Shailesh Baburao Kale, Deputy Superintendent of Police, Palghar (then Sub-Divisional Police Officer, Kurkheda, Gadchiroli) is concerned in the disciplinary matter and a departmental inquiry is being conducted against him as per the procedure laid down in Rule 8 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979.
And therefore, an inquiry officer should be appointed to investigate the charges framed against him. The governor seems to think
So now, Ma. The Governor, in exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 8, Potnium (2) of the said Rules and in accordance with the decision of the Government in question, is appointing the" Regional Divisional Inquiry Officer, Nagpur Division, Nagpur" as the" Enquiry Officer" to inquire into the charges framed against the aforesaid specified officer.
