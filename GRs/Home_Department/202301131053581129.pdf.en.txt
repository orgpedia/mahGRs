The Proposal:
Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has requested the Government through a letter under reference to provide funds for payment of salaries of employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of November, 2022. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds for the salary of the employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of November, 2022 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. An amount of Rs. 300.00 crore (Rupees Three Hundred Crores only) in cash is being approved.
2) Rs. 300.00 crore should be met from the provisions available in the financial year 2022-23 and the expenditure should be spent under the head" Demand No.B-3, Accounts Head 2041, Taxes on Vehicles (00) 001, Operations and Administration (01) Transport Commissioner (01) (01) Establishment- Transport Commissioner (Adaptation) (Mandatory) (2041 0018) 33, Financial Assistance."
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to collect and disburse the amount to be paid to the Maharashtra State Road Corporation by the Accounts Officer in his office.
4. This Government decision is based on the informal reference of the Finance Department No. 3/ 2023/ Expenditure- 8, d. being issued with the consent received as on 09.01.2023.
