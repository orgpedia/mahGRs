Government's decision:
Advocate, Mr. K.S. Dhule, was appointed to conduct the proceedings in the court on behalf of the Government in the case arising out of the crime No. 150/2017, (Gudachor Murder Case) at Dhule City Police Station. Shri Ujjwal Nikam has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor by the Department of Law& Justice vide notification dated 01.11.2017 in the above context. In this regard, the Government is approving the payment of professional fees to Adv. Ujjwal Nikam as follows:
Effective hearing fee per day: Rs.50,000/-
(b) Discussion Fee per hour Rs.15,000/-per day
Cost of the hotel per day: Rs.5,000/-
(d) Cost of travel: The cost of travel by train.
(2) In addition, no inoperative hearing fees or any other fees or allowances shall be admissible to Mr. Ujjwal Nikam. The days of effective hearing shall be certified by an officer not below the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case and shall not provide for any excess.
4. Expenditure to be incurred for this Department vide Demand No. B. 2055 Police- (00) 109, District Police- (00) (01) District Police Force- (2055 0168)-28 Professional Services will be covered under this head from the sanctioned grant for that financial year and as per the monthly fund statement.
