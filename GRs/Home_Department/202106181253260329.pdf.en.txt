The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure posts in government services for persons with disabilities. The central government has given. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government Resolution, 20 posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre in the office of Maharashtra Maritime Board under the Home (Ports) Department have been ensured as per the Government Resolution No. 6. In addition to the 20 posts mentioned in the government decision, the posts included in the central government. The matter of ensuring the remaining 24 posts in the disabled category, which were not included in the notification of 04.01.2021, was under the consideration of the Government.

Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions that the posts in Group A to Group D, which have been ensured for the disabled by the Central Government, will be ensured for the disabled even if the designations of the posts in the list published by the Central Government are different for the posts in the establishment under the State Government, the duties and responsibilities of the posts, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the service under the State Government. As per Government Decision dated 02.02.2021.
2. Accordingly, in terms of this Section, Reference No. 6 Decision of the Central Government. As per the list published for the Group-A to Group-D cadre ensured for the disabled in the notification dated 04.01.2021, 20 posts have been ensured for the disabled under this Government decision for the posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre in the Maharashtra Maritime Board Office. Out of the remaining 24 posts, 23 posts, excluding the post of Driver, are being ensured for the disabled as shown in the return B under this Government decision.
