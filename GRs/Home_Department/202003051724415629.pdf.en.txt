Preamble: For the speedy and effective curbing of anti-terrorist activities in the State, Reference No. 1 As per the Government Order dated 08.07.2004, an Anti-Terrorism Squad was constituted on the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Mumbai. Also, the scheme of incentives to the officers/ staff of Anti-Terrorism Squad has been implemented under the Government Decision, Home Department dated 09th November, 2005. As per the scheme, 50 per cent of the basic pay plus dearness allowance (DA) was sanctioned as incentive to the executive officers/ staff of the team.
After the implementation of the 7th Pay Commission in the State, a proposal was under the consideration of the Government to sanction incentive allowance to the Indian Police Service officers working in the Anti-Terrorism Squad as per the pay scale of the 7th Pay Commission. The government has taken the following decisions:
