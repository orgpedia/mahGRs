Provisional seniority of all officers in the cadre of Superintendent, District Jail Class-II/ Deputy Superintendent, Central Jail has been prepared for the period up to 01.01.2020. The decision was taken by the General Administration Department. The guidelines dated 21.10.2011 have been prepared.
Additional Director General of Police and Inspector General Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune are requested to inform the Superintendent, District Jail Class-II/ Deputy Superintendent, Central Jail of the temporary service seniority for the period up to 01.01.2020 to the Superintendent, District Jail Class-II/ Deputy Superintendent, Central Jail of all the officers of the cadre (including officers on leave/ deputation) and seek information about their objections/ lapses. In case of any objection/ error received from the concerned Superintendent, District Jail Class-II/ Deputy Superintendent, Central Jail, the information regarding the issues in their representation should be examined as per the records in your office and sent to this department with your opinion within a period of one month. In case of non-receipt of objections/ errors within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published assuming the correctness of the information/ details of the officers mentioned in the seniority notice.
