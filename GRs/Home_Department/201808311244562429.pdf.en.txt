" The condition of 1000 mature trees in Mumbai city, Mumbai suburban and Thane districts of Konkan region is being relaxed and approval is being given as a special case to bring and sell toddy from Palghar and Raigad districts of the adjoining Konkan region. While approving toddy shops in Mumbai city, Mumbai suburban and Thane districts, proof of the presence of 1000 mature trees in Palghar and Raigad districts of Konkan division will be required to be submitted. Necessary action in this regard should be taken at the level of Commissioner. Care should be taken that adulterated toddy is not distributed.
