Preamble: Restrictions on the number of autorickshaw/ taxi permits in the cities of Mumbai, Thane, Pune, Nagpur, Solapur, Nashik and Aurangabad in the state are under reference No. 2 here. as per notification dated 17.06.2017. Also, the No. 1 here. The terms and conditions of Autorickshaw/ Taxi permits have been prescribed vide Government Order dated 18.07.2017.
Also, the private autorickshaws registered under licence No. 6 here. Additional licence fee has been prescribed vide notification dated 22.09.2017.
Similarly, No. 5 here. Autorickshaws registered in the private category as per the Government decision dated 22.09.2017, the last date for registration in the transport category has been extended. It was given up to 31.03.2018. However, the extension was under consideration of the government as all private autorickshaws were not registered on licence during this period.
Government's decision:
Autorickshaws registered in the private category shall be registered in the transport category on a new license or as a replacement vehicle on the existing license. It will be extended until December 31, 2019.
