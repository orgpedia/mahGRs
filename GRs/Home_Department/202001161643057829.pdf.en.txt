The Proposal:
Sub Regional Transport Office, Parbhani has been allotted Rs. 43,17,139/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakhs Seventeen Thousand One Hundred and Nineteen only). 1 Administrative and financial approval has been given under the Government Memorandum here. The construction work of this Brake Test Track includes construction of i) 3 nos. of Nalkandi Bridge and 2 nos. of (30 mts.) and 16 mm. Length) Construction of linkages ii) Processing of black soil and digging of open drains adjacent to the track ii) Construction of vehicle sheds adjacent to the track and removal of excess vegetation iv) Additional provision of 12% GST and 1% Labour Insurance have led to increase in cost. Accordingly, the revised budget for the said work has been prepared by the Deputy Divisional Engineer, Public Works Department, Parbhani and certified by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Parbhani. The matter of giving administrative approval to the revised budget was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Rs. Revised Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 66,69,550/- (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Seventy Nine Thousand Five Hundred and Fifty only). The details of the cost of the works as per the revised budget are as follows:
