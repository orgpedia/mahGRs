The Proposal:
The Vice-Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has issued instructions regarding the availability of subsidy and deficit funds for the month of October, 2023 to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation. 1, 2 and 3 have been requested by the Government. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds to Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for reimbursement of concessional rates for the month of October, 2023 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to provide an amount of Rs. 329.94 crore (Rupees Three Hundred and Thirty-Nine Crore and Ninety Four Lakh only) to Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) as cash reimbursement towards the concessional rates for the month of October, 2023.
