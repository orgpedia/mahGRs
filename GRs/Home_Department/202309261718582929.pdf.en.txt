The Proposal:
Railways is a subject in the Central List. The development of the railway network in Maharashtra will add to the social and economic development of the state. For the implementation of this railway project, the state needs to cooperate with the railway department on many aspects. As per the MoU signed between the Centre and the state government, the state government has agreed to provide financial assistance to the Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (MRIDC) for the project. (MRIDC) has been set up and some of the projects have been implemented through MRIDC. Some of the projects have been approved by the State Cabinet and these projects need to be implemented in the near future and completed in a reasonable time frame. He said that the railway projects in Maharashtra should be completed at a fast pace and the problems in the implementation of the railway projects should be resolved quickly. A high-level committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra, was being considered by the government.
Government's decision:
To expedite the implementation of the ongoing railway projects in Maharashtra, the projects approved with financial participation by the State Government, as well as the future projects to be completed and commissioned within the stipulated time, and to coordinate between the Railway Department and the State Government. The government has constituted a high-level committee under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra.

The agenda of the Committee is as follows: 1) To approve the financial model for financial participation of the State Government in new railway projects, cost escalation of existing projects, revised administrative and financial approvals and other financial participation.
2) Konkan Railway Development Corporation Pvt Ltd and National High Speed Rail Corporation Ltd. E.
To decide on the proposals regarding the State Government's share in the project.
3) Prompt completion of land acquisition proposals required for railway projects.
4) Recognition of forest law matters.
(5) Grant of minor mineral mining clearances.
6) Eliminate problems in transmission line connectivity.
7) Removing the difficulties in utility shifting.
8) To give instructions to the administration and the police department regarding law and order.
Committee meetings will be held as needed. The Transport Department will act as a" Coordinating Department" for the consideration of the High Level Committee as well as for submission of approved issues and proposals.
