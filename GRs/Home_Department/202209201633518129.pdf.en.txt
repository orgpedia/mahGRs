The Proposal:
Many political parties and social organizations follow the path of agitation in the form of gherao, rallies, demonstrations, etc. to draw the attention of the government to various issues of public interest. Therefore, a case has been registered against them for violating the law. Crimes arising out of political and social movements. The Government has taken a policy decision to withdraw the cases in which chargesheets have been filed till 31/12/2019. The people's representatives have been demanding the government to extend the period of withdrawal of cases filed due to political and social agitations. It was under consideration of the Government to extend the period of withdrawal of cases filed in political and social agitations and to bring uniformity in this regard, all the relevant Government decisions/ circulars issued earlier should be superseded and a comprehensive Government decision should be issued.
Government Decisions: The Government Decisions/ Circulars mentioned in the context of proceeding on cases of political and social movements are being superseded by this Government Decision and orders are being issued for proceeding as follows.
