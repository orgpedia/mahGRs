The proposal m. Criminal Public Interest Litigation filed in the High Court, Mumbai No. In the year 9/2017. As indicated in the court during the hearing on 23/10/2023, the government was considering the formation of a committee of officers at the level of secretary to facilitate speedy decisions in relation to prison reforms and to coordinate between the Home Department and other departments of the government concerned. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision To facilitate faster decision making in line with the Jail Reforms in the State and to coordinate with the Home Department and other departments of the Government concerned in this regard, a committee of officers of the level of Secretary is being constituted as follows:
