The Proposal:
The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) has decided to pay the salary of its employees for the month of April 2022. 300.00 crore is required for payment of salaries to the employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation. The Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has requested the Government through a letter under reference to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 300.00 crore.
In the Budget Estimates 2022-23 under the head Financial Assistance Accounts, Rs. A provision of Rs. 1450.00 crore has been approved. The Finance Department has released Rs. 300.00 crore has been approved to be disbursed towards the salaries of the employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of April, 2022. Accordingly, Rs. An amount of Rs. 300.00 crore was under consideration of the Government for disbursement of salaries of employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of April, 2022.
Government's decision:
In the year 2022-23, the Government has sanctioned Rs. 1450.00 crore out of which Rs. An amount of Rs. 300.00 crore (Rupees Three Hundred Crores only) in cash is being approved.
