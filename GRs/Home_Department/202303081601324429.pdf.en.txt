The Proposal:
The State Government has adopted a policy of providing 40% to 50% financial participation in such selected projects to speed up the railway projects in rural areas of the State, especially in underdeveloped areas. The Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Company (MahaRail) has prepared the Detailed Feasibility Report (DFR) of the Phaltan-Pandharpur new Broad Gauge Railway line project. 1842 crores. The project will have 50:50 financial participation from the Central Government (Ministry of Railways) and State Governments. The total cost of the project is Rs. 1842 crore i.e. 50% of the cost of Rs. The matter of approving the financial contribution of the state government for the expenditure of Rs 921 crore was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the following decisions have been taken at the Cabinet level.
