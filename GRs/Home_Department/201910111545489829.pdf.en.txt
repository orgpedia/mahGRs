Government's decision:
A total of four supernumerary posts have been created for three officers of the cadre of Deputy Superintendent (Group-B) and one officer of the cadre of Assistant Commissioner, Raushu, (Group-B) who have joined the training of Combined Peripheral Training Programme-4. The establishment of the Commissioner, State Excise, for a period of two years with effect from 01.08.2017. The approval was accorded vide Government Resolution dated 08.08.2017. The said number of posts are being extended till the time the process of posting is completed by allotting Revenue Divisional Cadre to the said Subordinate Officers. The officers under this examination will continue to work at the places where they are currently undergoing training for department-wise district-related work till the departmental cadre is allocated.
Since Assistant Commissioner, Raushu, (Group-B) cadre probationary officer Dr. Priya Vasantrao Dehedkar has been selected for the post of Assistant Commissioner of Sales Tax (Group-A) from the State Service Examination-2017, she will be required to attend the training institute under CPTP-5 for joining the post of the new appointee. He has been relieved from his duties as per the Government Order dated 30.09.2019.
