Preamble: In order to provide protection to the highly protected persons and protected persons of great importance in the State of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra vide Reference No. A Special Protection Group has been constituted under the Commissioner, State Intelligence Bureau, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide order dated 10th November, 1986. The police officers/ personnel of the Special Protection Group are primarily tasked to provide protection to the important, highly protected persons of the state from political, local, terrorist organizations and other situations arising from time to time. The officers/ staff of this team have to perform their duties by risking their lives on occasion. Therefore, in order to facilitate the police officers/ personnel to join the team voluntarily for undertaking such high risk work, the Government vide Government Order dated 10.11.1986 has approved to provide additional incentive of 50% of basic pay plus dearness allowance to the police officers/ personnel of Special Protection Group and 30% for the post of driver. Also, reference No. 2 and Reference No. 4 is reference no. The incentive scheme has been applied to the sanctioned posts in the Special Protection Group created under the government decision.
