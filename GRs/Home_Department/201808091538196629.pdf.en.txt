UBM India Pvt Ltd. Relaxation in Label Registration Fee on imported wines for Food and Hotel India-2018 programme from 5th September, 2018 to 7th September, 2018.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Home Department
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MIS 0718/ PR No. 05/ Shikana/ Raushu-3 5th Floor, Extension, Mantralaya, Mumbai
Date: 9th of August 2018
The proposal-. UBM India Pvt. Food and Hotel India-2018 is being organized from 5th September, 2018 to 7th September, 2018.
The event will not sell wines produced by winemakers, but only offer free drinks for tasting.
The applicant has requested for exemption from label registration fee and special charges thereon for the brand stating that the wines to be displayed in the above programme will be imported from abroad and will be made available by the state's authorized registered FL-1 licensee. Please refer to the Commissioner, State Excise, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
The proposal was submitted for Government approval vide FLR No. 1118/14433/5-B, dated 15.06.2018. In view of this, the state government has taken the following decisions:
Government decision UBM India Pvt. The Government is pleased to grant exemption from registration charges on the labels of imported wines from abroad under the provisions of sub-section (1) (d-1) of section 139 of the Maharashtra Prohibition Act, 2018, in the event of" Food and Hotel India-2018" held from 5th September, 2018 to 7th September, 2018, subject to the following conditions:
1) If a representative of the department is appointed for supervision, the fee for it will have to be paid to the unit.
It will be mandatory for the entity to pay the fee for this license.
2) The unit will have to take one-day and other necessary permissions for this program.
Fee should be paid as per the rules of the government.
