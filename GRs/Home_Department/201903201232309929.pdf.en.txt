Government Decision: A total of 1168 posts under the control of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved. Further, as per Government Resolution No. 2012/ DPP-112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway. Of them, 48 temporary posts in the office of the Regional Deputy Director have been transferred to the Home Department, Government Decision No. DPP-0118/ PR No. 14/ Pol-10, D. 16.10.2018 has been extended till 28.02.2019. The Government hereby approve the extension of the existing 48 temporary posts in Annexure-B from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than six months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts where the government has already issued orders to abolish these posts.
