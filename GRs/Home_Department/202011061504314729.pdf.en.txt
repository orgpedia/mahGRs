The police is the last representative of the government at the village level. The police have the responsibility of maintaining law and order in the village. The Sub-Divisional Officer is the Competent Authority in respect of the appointment of Police Patil as per the provision of Section 3 of the Maharashtra Village Police Act, 1967.
During the recruitment process of Police Patil in the state, it has been found after investigation that at some places during the interview, the candidates of the post of Police Patil have been selected as Police Patil by submitting certificates in the form of sports skills/ computer knowledge as a qualification.
3. Decision of the Department of School Education and Sports, Govt. regarding eligibility for Sports Skill Certificate.
The guidelines were issued on July 1, 2016. Accordingly, the validity of the sports skill certificate submitted by the candidate for the post of Police Patil at the time of interview should be checked/ verified by the appointing authority (Sub-Divisional Officer) and further action should be taken only after ensuring complete verification.
4. General Administration Department, Government Decision on Computer Knowledge Qualification. March 19, 2003 and d. The guidelines were issued on February 4, 2013. Accordingly, the certificate of MS-CIT examination from the Maharashtra State Board of Higher and Technical Education, Mumbai, as well as other computer courses prescribed in the government decision, should be held valid.
However, all the sub-divisional officers as the appointing authority should ensure that there are no irregularities in the police Patil recruitment process. If any irregularity is found in such cases, the concerned appointing authority will be held responsible. Please note them.
