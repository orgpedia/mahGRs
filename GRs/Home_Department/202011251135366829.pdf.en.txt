Subject: Guidelines for the 64th Mahaparinirvan Diwas of His Holiness Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar on 6th December, 2020
circular
Considering the pandemic situation arising out of COVID-19, this year on 6th December, 2020
On the occasion of the 64th Mahaparinirvan Diwas of His Holiness Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar
Below are the guidelines for the event:
1) Corona patients are still found in large numbers in the state and in big cities.
are coming. Considering the outbreak of Covid this year, the last seven
All religious festivals/ celebrations in the eight months are celebrated in a very simple manner and without gathering of people.
celebrated. So this year, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar's date is December 6, 2020
Daily Mahaparinirvan Diwas program to be held with full precautions against the background of Covid
It should be done in a simple way.
2. Due to restrictions on arrival at Chaityabhumi, Dadar on the background of Covid virus and
There is a ban on crowding at Dadar and other railway stations in Maharashtra.
Live telecast of Mahaparinirvan Diwas programme at Chaityabhoomi, Dadar
Since it will be done, all the followers will not come to Chaityabhumi Dadar and come from home.
Salute to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar.
3. Mahaparinirvana Day is a day of sorrow for Indians and should be observed with seriousness.
Today is the day to express our gratitude towards Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar. Therefore
All followers should behave thoughtfully and patiently on the day of Mahaparinirvan considering the Covid infection.
