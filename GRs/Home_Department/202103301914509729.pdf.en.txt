Government Decision: Tribal Development Department, Ministry, Mumbai. As per the Government decision dated 30.03.2021, for the financial year 2020-21, under the scheme of Modernization of Bus Stations of State Transport Corporations in Tribal Areas under Tribal Appropriation, Demand No. T-5, 3055, Road Transport, Schemes under Five Year Plans, 00-Road Transport, 796-Tribal Area Sub-Plan, (01) Development, Construction, Repair of ST Depots in Tribal Areas (3055 0043), 31-Support Grants (Non-Payment) for the year 2020-21 with a target of Rs. 632.00 lakhs is provided in the Budget.
The Finance Department, in line with the proposal of the Home (Transport) Department, has approved the release of funds for 2020-21 within the limit of 33% of the budgetary allocation. Accordingly, Tribal Development Department (30550043), under 31-Support Grants-in-Aid to construct Driver Training Centre at Gadchiroli along with Divisional Office has sanctioned Rs. 80.00 lakh, renovation of Pandharkawada Driver Training Centre at a cost of Rs. 51.56 lakh and modernisation of bus stand at Chikhaldara, G. 77.00 lakhs. 208.56 lakh (Rs.2.88 crore only) has been sanctioned to be released to the Additional Chief Secretary (Transport), Home Department, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai on Budgetary Allocation of Funds (BEAMS) as administrative department.
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to disburse the above funds and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in his office shall take action to recover the amount and pay it to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation.
