Introduction: Political parties and social organizations follow the path of agitation in the form of gherao, rallies, demonstrations, etc. to draw the attention of the government to various issues of public interest. Therefore, a case has been registered against them for violating the law. Crimes arising out of political and social movements.
The Government has already taken a policy decision to withdraw the cases in which chargesheets have been filed till 31/03/2022. In this regard, the Home Department, Government Resolution No. PRO-0921/ No. 282/ VISA 2, D. Order has been issued on 20/09/2022. However, the people's representatives are demanding the government to extend the period of withdrawal of cases filed due to political and social agitation. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the period of withdrawal of cases filed in connection with political and social movements.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government Decision to withdraw the cases of political and social agitations has been issued by the Home Department vide No. PRO-0921/ PR No. 282/ VISA-2, dated 20/09/2022, para. With partial modification in section 2, the withdrawal of cases of political and social agitation in which chargesheets have been filed as on 30/06/2022 is being approved in principle, while retaining all other terms, conditions and provisions of the Government Order.
