The proposal: Pawan Hans Helicopters Ltd., Mumbai and Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai for hiring of helicopters on lease basis for strengthening the police force in Naxal affected areas, transportation of injured persons and jawans, providing immediate medical assistance and food to injured jawans, inspection of suspected areas, etc.
The agreement was signed on 22/2/2016. After the expiry of the said contract, the extension of the helicopter was approved from time to time with the approval of the Finance Department.
At present, the Gadchiroli district police force has a new helicopter purchased from a German-based manufacturer. The helicopter, hired on lease basis from Pawan Hans Limited, Mumbai, was handed over to the Superintendent of Police (Special Action), Office of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, vide No. The matter of extension was under consideration of the Government as per the request made under letter No. 2.
Government Decision: To strengthen the police force in the Naxalite affected areas, to transport the injured persons and jawans, to provide immediate medical assistance to the injured jawans and police officers/ personnel, to provide food items to the jawans, to inspect the suspected areas, etc. The helicopter was procured from Pawan Hans Company Limited, Mumbai. Government approval is being given to extend it from 1/6/2021 to 31/5/2022.
