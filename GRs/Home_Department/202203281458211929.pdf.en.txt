District Judge-1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Mehkar, District Buldhana vide letter No. 601/ 2021 dated 14.07.2021.
Preamble: For the purpose of adjudicating claims for compensation in case of accident involving death or bodily injury to persons or damage to property of a third person or both, under Government Notification, Home Department No. MCT- 0805/1063/136/ Transport-5, dated 25/07/2006, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal has been constituted in all the districts and taluks of the State for the area specified for that revenue range. Besides, the District Judges, Joint District Judges, Additional District Judges and Adhoc Additional District Judges, members equivalent to the officers of that rank, District Judges who are deemed to have assumed the charge of that office from time to time, Joint District Judges and Additional District Judges and Adhoc Additional District Judges have been empowered to deal with the cases of Motor Accident Claims Tribunals for that revenue jurisdiction. Accordingly, the Government was considering declaring District Judge-1 and Additional Sessions Judge, District Court and Additional Sessions Court and Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mehkar, District Buldhana as" Office Head" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
Government Resolution: As per the Government Resolution No. 2013/ Q. No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17th April, 2015 of the Finance Department and the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the Financial Powers, Rule Book, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division No. 3, Rule No. 2 of Sub-Division 1 and Rules 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the District Judge-I and Additional Sessions Judge, District Court and Additional Sessions Court Mehkar and ex-officio members, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mehkar, District Buldhana are being declared as" Office Head" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
The order will be limited to financial matters.
