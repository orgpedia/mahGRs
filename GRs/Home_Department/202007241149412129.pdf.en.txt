Government's decision:
In the wake of COVID-19, various measures have been taken to deal with the traffic problem. As per the Government Decision dated 23.06.2020, A state-level task force has been formed under the chairmanship of the Minister (Transport).
In this regard, a sub-committee is being set up under the State Level Transport Task Force to take measures and implement the problems of parking charges, e-challans etc. of the transporters during the lockdown period.
