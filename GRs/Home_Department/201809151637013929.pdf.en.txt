Preamble: The Finance Department has announced the policy on automobiles under the Government Resolution No. 1. As per the policy, vehicles of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC), offices under him, police stations, etc., are required to be reviewed and approved by the state-level vehicle review committee. In view of this, the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai Police was under the consideration of the Government to submit the status of vehicles to the State Level Vehicle Review Committee and to decide the permissibility of vehicles for all concerned officers/ offices/ police stations of the Brihanmumbai Police Force. Accordingly, after the approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee, the government has taken the following decision.
Government's decision:
The vehicles are being made permissible for the Commissioner of Police, Officers of the Brihanmumbai Police Force/ Police Stations/ Unit Offices/ Special Branch as indicated in returns (a) and (b) along with this Government Order.
