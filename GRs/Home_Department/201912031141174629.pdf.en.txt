Read:- 1) GOVERNMENT DECISION, NO. PRO:- 0516/ PR No.106/ VISA-2, Dy. As of August 30, 2016:
The government was considering setting up a committee to examine and recommend to the government the withdrawal of cases registered against the protesters who participated in the anti-Nayanar project and anti-Aarey Metro Cardepo agitation.
Government has decided to constitute a committee to recommend to the government to withdraw cases related to political and social agitations under Section 321 of the Criminal Procedure Code in connection with the incidents of anti-Ninar Project and anti-Aarey Metro Car Depot agitations.
Additional Chief Secretary (Home) Chairman 2) Director General of Police: Member 3) Commissioner of Police: Member 4) Principal Secretary (Special) Home Department: Member Secretary 5) Principal Secretary (V.S. and No. Department): Invited
After the government's approval of the committee's report, action will be taken to withdraw cases related to political and social movements under Section 321 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973.
(2) The said Government decision is subject to the approval of the Department of Law and Justice and its informal reference No. 2955/ PS D. 2.12.2019 is being issued.
