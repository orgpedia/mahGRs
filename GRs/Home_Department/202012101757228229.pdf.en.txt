By Government Resolution, Home Department, No.PoS-0820/ PR No. 41/ Pol-3, dated 28.08.2020, it has been approved to establish a Police Sub-Headquarter at B-Hampur Village, Taluka-Baramati, District-Pune. The office of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra, has submitted a proposal for administrative approval of a water supply scheme to supply water to the said Sub-Headquarters.
2. Accordingly, for the water supply scheme for water supply to Police Sub-Headquarter, B-Hampur, Rs. Administrative Sanction is hereby granted subject to the following conditions, to the extent of Rs.15 crore:
The water supply scheme is only for the police force (within the limit of 3,000 population).
The scheme will be implemented by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran.
3) Once the said scheme is completed, it should be transferred to the Public Works Department.
4) All permissions/ objections required for the said scheme should be obtained by the Water Supply and Sanitation Department.
5) Work should not be started without the availability of funds for the said scheme.
