The Proposal:
In accordance with the Maharashtra State Reservation of Seats in Educational Institutions and of Appointments or Posts in the Public Services under the control of the State) Act, 2018 for Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC), the Government of Maharashtra has decided to provide reservation of seats in educational institutions in the State. A petition was filed in the High Court. The High Court. Against the decision dated 27/06/2019. In the Supreme Court, Civil Appeal No. 3123/2020 and other petitions have been filed. On the petitions. The Supreme Court Interim stay has been granted vide order dated 09/09/2020.
As per the aforesaid interim stay order of the Supreme Court on SEBC reservation, in the year 2019, police constables, police constables, drivers, jail constables and RAS officers have been given the benefit of reservation. The matter was under consideration to take action in accordance with the advertisement issued regarding the filling of vacancies in the cadre of National Police Force constables.
