The proposal: Writ Petition (Stamp) No. 21699/2021, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation v. Struggle St. According to the Bombay High Court, As per the Government decision dated 08.11.2021, Shri. A three-member committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. The Committee in its report has recommended:" Considering the present financial position of the Corporation, the State Government should make budgetary provision to provide funds for the salaries of the employees of the Corporation for at least next 4 years. The decision to continue this financial assistance should be taken at an appropriate time after reviewing the financial performance of the Corporation. "
2. The Cabinet gave the report of the said tripartite committee. This has been approved in the meeting dated 23.03.2022. Accordingly, in the letter under reference, the Vice-Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has requested to take a decision on the payment of financial assistance to the corporation every month as financial assistance for the salary of the employees/ officers of the corporation.
Accordingly, the matter of providing financial assistance to pay the salary of the employees/ officers of the R.P. Corporation every month was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government decision: In pursuance of the recommendation of the Cabinet, the following instructions are being issued for the salaries of the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation: (1) The amount which is being deducted every month from the monthly income of the RSRTC to meet the expenses on maintenance and salary of fuel vehicles. The Corporation will be provided with financial assistance from the Government under the head of accounts 2041 0018-33.
(2) Provide financial assistance. It will be in force till March 31, 2024.
(3) A decision shall be taken in due course as to whether or not to continue the financial assistance for salaries after reviewing the income and expenditure of the N.P. Corporation every quarter.

(4) The Corporation is required to take immediate measures to meet the deficit of the amount it is incurring to meet its expenditure.
The N.P.C. should not be dependent on the Government for such assistance.
It is the responsibility of the Mahamandal to meet the expenses on salaries and pending dues of the employees of the corporation. The State Transport Corporation should try to make the corporation self-sufficient by increasing the current income.
(5) The expenditure should be incurred under the head" Demand No. B-3, Account Head 2041, Tax on Vehicles (00) 001, Operation and Administration (01) Transport Commissioner (01) (01) Establishment- Transport Commissioner, (Adaptation) (Mandatory) (2041 0018) 33, Financial Assistance."
(6) The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is hereby authorised and the Accountant in his office who is the Procurement and Disbursement Officer shall take necessary action for recovering the said amount and making payment to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation.
3. This Government Decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 161/2023/ Expenditure-8, Dy. is being issued with the consent received as on 24.03.2023.
