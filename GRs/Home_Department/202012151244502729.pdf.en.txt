Under the" Special Infrastructure Scheme (SIS), including construction of 250 Fortified Police Stations in LWE affected States" of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, the construction of 20" Fortified Police Stations" has been approved for the State of Maharashtra. The Government of India has allotted Rs. 7,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Fifty Lakhs only) has been disbursed to the State of Maharashtra as" ad hoc advance," subject to certain conditions. The said Rs. 7,50,00, 000/ has been deposited in the Consolidated Fund of the State.
Administrative approval has been accorded to the construction of the said 20" Fortified Police Stations" vide Government Memorandum, Home Department, No.SIS-1118/ PR No.441/ One-Two-Twenty/ Pol-7, dated 27.11.2020.
Therefore, 75% of the amount of Rs. 5,62,50, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores, Sixty Two Lakhs, Fifty Thousand only) to the Deputy Inspector General of Police, Gadchiroli, on Budget Estimates, Allocation and Control System, is hereby approved, subject to the following conditions: 1) The guidelines of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, on" Special Infrastructure Scheme (SIS), including construction of 250 Fortified Police Stations in LWE affected States" should be complied with.
The conditions contained in the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, F.No. 18015/04/2020-LWE-1, dated 19.03.2020, should be complied with.
