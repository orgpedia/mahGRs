GOVERNMENT CURRENCY: As per the order under reference, orders were issued under Transfer-2023 on the request of the Additional Public Prosecutor, Group-A, on the establishment of the Directorate of Complaints.
In paragraph 2 of this Order, paragraph 2.1 should be read as follows:
2.1 These orders will come into effect immediately. The transferred prosecutors should take charge of the post immediately after the transfer. The Director, Directorate of Grievances should strictly follow the following instructions to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(a) An Additional Public Prosecutor has been transferred and posted in the district and the posting under the concerned district shall be done by the Director, Directorate of Grievances.
(b) On receipt of the transfer order, the transferred Additional Public Prosecutor shall be engaged by the Controlling Officer without any further delay after he comes out of his present post to join. The officers concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of payment of salary and allowances by fixing a mandatory waiting period without work due to non-recruitment of the said prosecutor.
