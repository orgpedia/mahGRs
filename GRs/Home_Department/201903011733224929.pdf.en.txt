Introduction: The above reference No. Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district for a period of two years. Reference No. A total of 12 new posts have been created under the government decision. Reference No. Under the Government decision, 2 additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals and 12 temporary posts have been extended from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019. Since the extension of these 12 posts is expiring on 28.02.2019 and as per Reference No. 4 As the Chairman, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur, vide letter dated 17.01.2019, had submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of tenure of two Additional Tribunals and a total of 12 temporary posts therein, the Government was considering extension of tenure of the said posts from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
Government Decision: Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals at Nagpur and 12 temporary posts created for the same are being extended from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019. The extension is for expeditious disposal of the claims pending before the Tribunal, submission of the final returns of the settled claims for the month of February, 2019 and May, 2019 and September, 2019 to the Government and 50% of the pending claims for the month of December, 2018.
