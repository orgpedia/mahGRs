The matter of payment of professional fee to Advocate Mr. Vaibhav Bagade, who was appointed as Special Public Prosecutor under the contextual notification to look after the court proceedings in the Special MCOCA Court, Mumbai on behalf of the Government was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Crime Disclosure Wing, G.A.V. No. 19/2016 (Chunabhatti Police Station, Gu. R. No.
45/2016). The Government hereby approve payment of professional fees to Mr. Vaibhav Bagade, Advocate, who has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor in the Special MCOCA Court, Mumbai, as under:
Special MCOCA Court, Mumbai (A) Effective hearing fee per day, including consideration fee- Rs. 15,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Thousand only) and no other benefit other than the said professional fee shall be admissible to Adv. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.
The payment of admissible professional fee to Adv. Mr. Vaibhav Bagade shall be made after every two months of court proceedings. The Deputy Commissioner of Police concerned should ensure that the effective hearing days of the court proceedings are certified by an officer not below the rank of Superintending Deputy Commissioner of Police of the concerned case and no additional payment of any kind is made to Advocate Mr. Vaibhav Bagade.
