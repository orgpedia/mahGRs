Preamble: Public Interest Litigation No. 28/2013 (Shrikant Karve Vs. the High Court of Bombay. On 18.02.2016, the Government had issued an order to set up Automated Testing Centres at various places. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to grant administrative approval for setting up of Vehicle Inspection and Certification Centre for various Transport Offices of the Department of Motor Vehicles through Automotive Research Association of India, Pune, an organization recognized under Rule 126 of the Central Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989.
The Automotive Research Association of India (ARAI) has decided to set up inspection and certification centres at Taddeo (Mumbai), Taloja (Panvel), Hingna (Nagpur), Bhosari (Pimpri-Chinchwad), Dive Ghat (Pune), Kolhapur, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nandivli (Kalyan) and Murphy (Thane). Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 136,33,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Thirty Six Crores Thirty Three Lakhs only).
