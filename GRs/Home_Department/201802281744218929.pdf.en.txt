Government's decision
The Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Limited, Mumbai has accorded administrative approval for construction of" Cyber Crime Detection Centre" and nine Type-4 Service Accommodations for Police Officers at Bandra (West) under the aegis of Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai.
2. Further, vide Office Order, Home Department, No. 2018/ PR No. 40/ VISA 3 (A), dated 16.02.2018, the Home Department has sanctioned a sum of Rs. 50 crore has been approved to be diverted for" 53 major constructions."
3. For construction of the said" Cyber Crime Detection Centre" and service quarters for police officers and other similar major constructions, the said Rs. Rs 50 crore has been allocated for this.
The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw the said amount from the Accounts and Accounts Office, Mumbai. The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, Mumbai, shall transfer the said funds from the Office of Accounts and Accounts, Mumbai, to the Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Limited, Mumbai.
The said expenditure is to be met from the grants available in the current (2017-18) financial year under the Home Department- Demand Number B-1, 2055, Police, 115, Modernization of Police Force, (01) Various Technology Development Projects, (01) Various Technology Development Projects (20559032), 53 Major Constructions.
