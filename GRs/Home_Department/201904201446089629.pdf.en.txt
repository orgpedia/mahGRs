Introduction- A total of 812 temporary posts in the office of the Transport Commissioner and various regional transport office establishments in the Motor Vehicle Department have been given. As per the Government Decision dated 22.10.2018, The period was extended till 28.02.2019. These positions are currently available for 2019-2020. 01.03.2019 to D.
