Preamble: - Since the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation needs funds to pay for the modernization and reconstruction works of the bus stands in progress, the budget of the year 2025-26 has allocated Rs. 3055 0052-35. In a reference letter, the Vice-Chairman, Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai, has requested the Government to disburse the funds from the budgeted provision under the "Modernization and Other Facilities Programme of the Corporation." Accordingly, the issue of devolution of funds to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation was under consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation has been allotted a total of Rs. Funds to the tune of ₹1 crore (Rupees Ninety Five Crore Eighty Five Only) are being sanctioned in cash.
