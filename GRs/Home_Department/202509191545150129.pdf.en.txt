The Proposal: -
The temporary post in the cadre of Superintendent of Police in the Office of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, Department of Protection of Civil Rights, Nagpur is at Reference No. 5 The date was extended from 01.03.2025 to 31.08.2025 by a government decision here. The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has issued the order. The letter dated 13.08.2025 requests an extension. The issue of extension of temporary post was under consideration as per Finance Department Government Decision No. Posts-2016 / Q. No. 8/16 APC No. 26.08.2025 where the administrative departments have been empowered to extend the temporary posts.
The Government's Decisions:
By this Government decision, the post of Superintendent of Police, Office of Protection of Civil Rights, Department of Protection of Civil Rights, Nagpur, under the Directorate General of Police, State of Maharashtra, has been transferred to Reference No. 7 Subject to the conditions set out in the Government Decision of the Department of Finance herein, the Government is approving the extension of the date from 01.09.2025 to 28.02.2026.
2. The expenditure for these posts shall be incurred under the head 'Accounts' and shall be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2025-2026.
