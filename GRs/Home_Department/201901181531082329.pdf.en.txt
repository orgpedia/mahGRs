Preamble: An MoU has been signed on 28th June, 2018 between the Department of Home Affairs, Government of Maharashtra and SaveLIFE Foundation, New Delhi (a Non-Governmental Organization) for collecting and analysing scientific data of accidents and taking necessary measures to reduce the number of accidents on the Yashwantrao Chavan Expressway. As per the provisions of Clause 1.1 of the MoU, a Project Steering Committee of members consisting of representatives of all concerned departments including SaveLIFE Foundation under the Chairmanship of A.M.S. (Home) has been constituted vide Notification dated 27.07.2018 for monitoring of the project. The government was considering including some other members in the committee. Accordingly, the Government is now taking the following decisions:
The Steering Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri Yashwantrao Chavan, IAS (Home) to collect, analyse and analyse the scientific data of accidents and to suggest and implement measures to reduce the number of accidents on the expressway, consists of the following members: 1) Commissioner (Health Services)& Managing Director, National Health Mission, Maharashtra 2) Additional Mission Director, National Health Mission 3) Project Director (EMS), National Health Mission, Maharashtra 4) Vice Chairman (Administration& CSR), Mahindra& Mahindra Ltd.
Superintendent of Police, Raigad 6) Deputy Commissioner of Police, Thane 7) Deputy Commissioner of Police (Traffic), Pune
