Conditions:
(1) None of the above posts is vacant for more than 6 months and the terms and conditions for which the posts have been sanctioned should be complied with.
2) Department of Finance, Government Resolution, No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ A.P.C.
The terms and conditions laid down as on 18.08.2022 should be scrupulously followed and action taken accordingly. Similarly, immediate action should be taken to get the revised design of the office finally approved by the high-level committee.
(3) If the process of review of posts is not completed within the stipulated time, the temporary posts will not be extended for the next period and if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officers/ employees, the responsibility will be of the concerned establishments.
4) The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall submit the remaining proposals to the Government for approval of the High-Level Secretary Committee for review and revised review of the sanctioned post during this period. If this is not done, it should be noted that if the question of salary of these temporary posts arises due to the expiration of the deadline, the entire responsibility will be of the Office of the Director General of Police.
The expenditure on this should be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2022-23 under the head of accounts mentioned in statements 1 to 11.
