Preamble: The State Government had decided on 23.01.2013 to provide 50% financial participation in the project for conversion of Nagpur-Nagbhid Narrow Gauge railway line of 116.15 km into Broad Gauge. Subsequently, vide Government Resolution dated 30.07.2020, the said Railway Project was awarded to Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Company (MRIDC) for implementation of the project with 60% loan and 40% equity and an investment of Rs. 1400 crore has been approved.
40% of the project cost of Rs. 280.00 crore from the State Government and the remaining half share of Rs. 280.00 crore has been approved by the Central Government. The State Government's share of Rs. 280.00 crore out of which Rs. 50.00 crore has been sought by Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Company (MRIDC) vide letter dated 05.08.2020.
Accordingly, the matter of disbursing funds to the Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Company was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Company (MRIDC), Mumbai has been allotted Rs. The State Government hereby approves the release of Rs. 12.53 crore (Rupees Twelve Crore Thirty Five Lakh only).
