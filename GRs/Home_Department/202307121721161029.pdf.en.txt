Introduction: The Home (Transport) Department has decided to implement the Motor Vehicles (Expulsion and Registration) Rules 2021. In accordance with the provisions of the Motor Vehicles (Eviction and Registration) Rules, 2021, as notified vide Government Order dated 27.01.2023, the Central Government has issued a notification vide G.O. 16.12.2022 and D. As per the directions given in the letter dated 17.12.2022, vehicles owned by the State Government from the date of registration in the subordinate offices of the Government, semi-government, local bodies and public transport undertakings in the State to the date of the decision of the said Government should be scrapped through the Registered Vehicle Scrapping Facility and the action will be taken by the Central Government. 17.12.2022, it said.
Accordingly, the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai Police has constituted a Central Government agency, May Metal Scrap Trading Corporation (MSTC Ltd.), to dispose of such unsold vehicles, which are 15 years old and above and have been disposed of in various units of the force. The proposal to nominate this corporation to use the e-auction service system of this corporation was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Central Government has approved the sale of defective motor vehicles, spare parts/ materials, tyre tubes, batteries and lubricating oil and other materials of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai Police Force through e-auction. Metal Scrap Trading Corporation (MSTC Ltd.) The Government have approved the appointment of a nominee as an auctioneer for the next five years from the date of this Government decision to exercise the e-auction mandate of the Corporation. The approval shall be subject to the following conditions:
Police vehicles, which have been registered during the financial year 2013-14 to 2018-19 and uploaded for sale through NIC but have not been sold, and which are to be dismantled in future, should be sold at the scrap price in the subsequent e-auction, if not sold in the first e-auction.
2. All unregistered vehicles on the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai for fuel maintenance and repair should be e-auctioned from the same establishment.
3. The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai Office while removing the vehicles, directed the Home (Transport) Department. Action should be taken as per the directions given in the Government Resolution dated 27.01.2023.
Further, the procedure prescribed under other Government Orders issued from time to time by the Government should be strictly followed in carrying out the said dismantling process of vehicles.
