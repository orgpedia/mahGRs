Orders of posting of Mr. A.B. Chaskar, Superintendent, Raushu, Satara will be issued separately.
These orders will come into effect immediately. These officers should be transferred immediately. The officer should submit a report to the government after taking charge and should not go on unauthorised leave. For effective implementation of this order, the salary of the transferred officers for the month of June, 2022 should not be withdrawn by the transfer office under any circumstances.
(4) The transferred officers shall ensure that they join the place of transfer within the period of assumption of office as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Term of Office, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. If he does not join the transferred post during this period, the period of absence shall be deemed to be non-dies.
If any attempt is made to directly or indirectly press for modification of the said transfer order, disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned officers as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
