Government Decision: As per the Government Order dated 10th May, 2018, the post and office of Additional Director General of Police (Economic Offences) has been created. The jurisdiction of the new office will be the entire state except the Mumbai Police Commissionerate. Considering this, the Additional Director General of Police (Economic Offences) is being declared as the head of the department. As Head of the Department, these powers will be limited to financial matters.
This Order is issued pursuant to the Government Decision Finance Division No. 2013/ P. No. 30/2013/ Exchange Part-II, D. 17.4.2015 under Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Division I, Rule No. 2 are issued in accordance with the powers vested in the administrative departments.
