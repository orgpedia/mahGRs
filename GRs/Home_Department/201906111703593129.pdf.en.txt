Government Order:
Smt. Swati Mangesh Shinde, Assistant Public Prosecutor, Mazgaon Court, Sewri, Mumbai, when FIR No. 23/2017 was registered against her under Sections 7, 13 (1) (d) read with 13 (2) of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988. Accordingly, Smt. Swati Mangesh Shinde, Assistant Public Prosecutor was placed under suspension with effect from 28th July, 2017 vide order dated 18th September, 2017. It was also approved to file a complaint against him under serial number 26th October, 2018.
Since more than one year has elapsed since the suspension of Smt. Swati Mangesh Shinde, Assistant Public Prosecutor, the General Administration Department, vide Government Resolution, No. 1314/ Pr. No. 86/11-A, dated 31.01.2015, has decided to suspend the services of Smt. Swati Mangesh Shinde, Assistant Public Prosecutor, vide Government Resolution No. The suspension of Ms. Shinde was reviewed in the meeting of the Committee constituted under OCB-0215/ PR No.84/ Pol-2, dated 09.03.2015 under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Home). After review, the Committee unanimously decided to reinstate Mrs. Swati Mangesh Shinde, Assistant Public Prosecutor (Suspended) to the non-executive post subject to the outcome of the court case filed by the Anti-Corruption Bureau. Now, Mrs. Swati Mangesh Shinde is being appointed to the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor in Ratnagiri district which is vacant.
The appointments are being made with the prior approval of the competent authority in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
These orders will come into effect immediately. Mrs. Swati Mangesh Shinde should take charge as Assistant Public Prosecutor immediately. The Director, Directorate of Grievances should strictly follow the following instructions for the immediate and effective implementation of this order.
(a) Mrs. Swati Mangesh Shinde, Assistant Public Prosecutor has been proposed for appointment in Ratnagiri district and her posting anywhere in this district shall be done by the Director, Directorate of Complaints.
