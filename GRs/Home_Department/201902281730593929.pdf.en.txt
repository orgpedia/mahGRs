the Proposal.
Water supply is provided by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation in the offices of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai. The outstanding as on March 2017 against the original size of payments for water connections in 14 such offices is₹ 15,94,27, 945/-.
Further, the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, provides additional police force to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation for anti-encroachment operations from time to time, in addition to the sanctioned posts for the purpose. An amount of Rs. 15,94,56, 171/-is to be borne by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) towards the cost of additional police bandobust provided to the BMC during the period October 2009 to January 2016.
The Office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, has submitted a proposal to provide the arrears of the said water bill by adjusting the book in the name of arrears of expenditure of additional police bandobust provided to the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation during the period October 2009 to January 2016. The proposal was under the consideration of the government.
