Government Resolution:-
With reference to the invitation letter dated 16.09.2025 sent by Candela Technology
AB Sweden to the Hon'ble Minister (Ports and Fisheries), a delegation led by Hon'ble
Minister, Ports has been proposed to visit Sweden from 04.10.2025 to 08.10.2025.
2. The objective of the Study Tour 15 to study / discuss use of electric hydrofoil boats which
is manufactured by Candela using modern technology for sustainable, environment-friendly
and cost-effective water transport system on the coast of Maharashtra state. Thus, Candela Technology AB, Stockholm Projects has sent an invitation to the Hon'ble Minister (Ports and
Fisheries) and delegation of three officers, Government of Maharashtra, Mumbai to visit Stockholm, Sweden between 4") October to 08" October, 2025.
The visit will provide an opportunity to explore new avenues of collaboration especially in Maharashtra. The Itinerary from 4 "October to 08" October, 2025, including traveling is
Provided for Hon'ble Minister (Ports and Fisheries) and his delegation of three officers.
Hon'ble Minister (Ports and Fisheries) has confirmed all the appointments with various
Agencies in the Study Tour.
4. The above mentioned Study Tour to Stockholm, Sweden will allow for valuable discussions with International Maritime Experts along with site visits, Financing Institutions and Industry Leaders to further refine the strategic roadmap for implementing best practices in the State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
Considering these potential benefits, the Government of Maharashtra, Mumbai has planned a Study Tour for Hon'ble Minister (Ports and Fisheries) with delegation of three officers from Stockholm, Sweden from 4 "October to 08" October, 2025 (Including Traveling).
