The Proposal:
The Additional Director General of Police (ADGP), Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter dated 16/06/2023 has submitted a proposal for approval for the establishment of 5 Police Help Desks at Nelgunda, Kavande, Murewada, Tumarkoti, Pimplibargi for protection of iron mines in Bhamragad and Etapli taluks of Gadchiroli district. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the establishment of 05 Police Help Desks at (i) Nelgunda, (ii) Kavande, (iii) Murevada, (iv) Tumarkoti, (v) Pimplibargi in Bhamragad and Etapalli taluks of Gadchiroli district to protect the iron mines and to provide assistance to the police personnel.
Orders for the creation of posts required for the newly created police help desks at Nelgunda, Kavande, Murewada, Tumarkoti and Pimplibargi will be issued separately with the approval of the Finance Department.
