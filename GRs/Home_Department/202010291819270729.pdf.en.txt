Special MCOCA Court, Sessions Court, Mumbai (Rs. 40,000/- (Rs. 500/- (a) Accommodation Government Rest House/ Actual Accommodation (as per requirement) D) Travel expenses Actual Travel expenses etc.) Security arrangements In the case of a case presented as per Government policy, the Adjournment Hearing Fee and any other allowances will not be admissible to the Advocate.

Effective hearing days of court proceedings are not less than the rank of Supervisory Deputy Commissioner of Police of the concerned case. The Deputy Commissioner of Police concerned shall ensure that the same is certified by the Manager of the Court and no additional payment is made to Adv. Ajay Misar.
