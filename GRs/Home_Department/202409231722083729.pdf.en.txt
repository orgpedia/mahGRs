Preface: Reference No. Two separate Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district under a government notification here. For these Tribunals, Reference No. 2 A total of 24 temporary posts have been created by the government decision here. The 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the said Tribunal have been notified vide Reference No. 3 by Government Decision dated 01.03.2024 at
The deadline was extended to 31.08.2024. As these 24 posts expired on the extension date 31.08.2024, Reference No. As vide letter dated 13.09.2024 4, the Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Nagpur submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of these 24 temporary posts, the Government was considering extension of the said posts from 01.09.2024 to 28.02.2025.
Government Decision: - 24 temporary posts at the establishment of the office of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Nagpur are being extended from 01.09.2024 to 28.02.2025. The extension is being granted subject to the condition that the claims pending with the Tribunal are disposed of expeditiously and the statement of claims settled is submitted to the Government after every three months.
