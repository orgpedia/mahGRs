the prelude
A total of 50 Police officers/ personnel from the Rapid Response Team at the establishment of Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, comprising of eight Police Officers who have completed two consecutive years of service (including Commando Training Period) and 42 Police Personnel who have completed four consecutive years of service (including Commando Training Period) have been awarded Special Service Medals and Commendation Cards every year
Reference No. 1 was approved by the government here. The government was considering raising the limit to 50.
Government's decision
""" It is hereby approved to enhance the limit of award of Special Service Medals and Commendation Cards per annum to the Police Officers who have completed two consecutive years of service (including Commando Training Period) and to the Police Personnel who have completed four consecutive years of service (including Commando Training Period) in the\"" Rapid Response Team\"" at the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, as specified in column (4) of the following heading:"""
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Rapid Response Team (9) | essential services (2)                   | existing restrictions (3) | Extended Limits (4) |
| Police Officer          | Two years (including training period)    | 08                        | 25                  |
| Police personnel        | Four years (including commando training) | 42                        | 175                 |
| Total                   | 50                                       | 200                       |
