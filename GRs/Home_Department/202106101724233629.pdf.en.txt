Introduction: Byculla Post, Mumbai. In the court case arising out of R. No. 82/ 2019, Md. Advocate, who has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor to look after the court proceedings in Sessions Court, Mumbai.
The matter of payment of professional fees of Mr. Vaibhav Bagade was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the court case arising out of offence No. 82/2019 registered at Byculla Police Station, Mumbai, Shri. Advocate Mr. Vaibhav Bagade, who has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor to look after the court proceedings in the Sessions Court, Mumbai, on behalf of the Government, is being allowed to pay professional fees as follows for effective hearing on a daily basis till the disposal of the said judicial matter.
a) Effective hearing fee per day- Rs. 15,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Thousand only) (b) Discussion Fee per sitting- Rs. Rs.2000/- (Rupees Two Thousand Five Hundred only)
Apart from this professional fee, no other benefit will be admissible to Advocate Mr. Vaibhav Bagade. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.
The payment of admissible professional fee to Adv. Mr. Vaibhav Bagade shall be made after every two months of court proceedings. Effective trial days of the court proceedings are not less than the rank of Superintending Investigating Officer/ Deputy Commissioner of Police of the concerned case. The concerned Deputy Commissioner of Police should ensure that the same is certified by the Manager of the Court and no additional payment is made to Adv. Vaibhav Bagade.
