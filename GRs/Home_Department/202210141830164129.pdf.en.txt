Preamble: The Government has decided to implement the Housing Advance Scheme by taking loans from banks for the construction of houses for the officers/ officials of the police force in the state through the Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation. 10.04.2017 has been approved.
Accordingly, 5017 Police Officers/ Amaldars of various Police Unit Offices in the State have been provided with house construction advance till May 2019. Thereafter, the scheme was discontinued and in the future, the Government regular housing advance scheme for the police like other government employees was implemented as per the Government decision dated 07.06.2022.
However, for about 7950 applications expected by 2023-24 for house building advance, a minimum of Rs. 2012 crore will be required and it is not possible for the government to allocate such a large amount in one or two years. That's true. As per the Government decision dated 10.4.2017, it will be appropriate to extend the benefit of the Housing Advance Scheme to the Police Officers/ Officers considering the pending applications, fresh applications and future applications by raising loans from banks/ financial institutions through the Police Housing and Welfare Corporation. In view of this, as per the decision of the Government dated 07.06.2022 to arrange loans through banks/ financial institutions, the Government has decided to withdraw the decision to give advance from the Government under the Government Housing Advance Scheme to the officers/ officials of the Police Force of the State as before and to implement the Housing Advance Scheme through the Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation by taking loans from banks for the construction of houses for the officers/ officials of the Police Force of the State. The matter of re-introduction of Advance Scheme implemented as on 10.04.2017 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to implement the Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation through taking loans from banks for the construction of houses for the officers/ officials of the state police force. 10.04.2017 has been approved. However, it is being approved to withdraw the Government decision dated 07.06.2022 issued in respect of providing funds under the head (Government Regular Housing Advance Scheme (HBA)) Head 7610 as earlier.
Also, the officers/ officials of the police force of the state will be given loans through banks for construction of houses. Approval is being given to restart the scheme which was started under the Government decision dated 10.04.2017.
