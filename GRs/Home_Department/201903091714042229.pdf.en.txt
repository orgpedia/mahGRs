The Proposal:
The Government has taken a decision to study and recommend to the Government for the preparation of an" umbrella scheme" to provide social security to the Police Patil and other employees on honorarium in the State. Meeting 2016/ No.58/ E-10D A Committee of Secretaries under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Finance) was constituted on 18.3.2017. The committee has submitted its report to the state government.
Considering the recommendations made in the report regarding the police officers and the fact that the Police Patil is an important link between the revenue system and the police administration, taking into account their duties and responsibilities, increasing the honorarium of the Police Patil, giving them the benefit of the" State Government Employees Group Personal Accident Scheme,"" Atal Pension Yojana" and" Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana" etc. The Cabinet sub-committee, headed by the Chief Minister, gave the decision to the Home Department, Govt. has been fixed as on 28.2.2019.
The committee With regard to the recommendations made by the Welfare Umbrella Committee in its meeting held on 1.3.2019 and other demands made by the Police Patil Unions from time to time
Following the decision of the Cabinet, the government issued the following orders:
Government's decision:
1) It is being approved to increase the honorarium given to the police patils of the state from the existing Rs 3,000/- per month to Rs 6,500/- per month.
2) It is being approved to establish a Police Patil Welfare Fund (Welfare Fund) and deposit Rs 500/- per month from the increased honorarium of the Police Patil in it. Sadar

The amount deposited should be used to provide the benefit of 1) State Government Employees Group Personal Accident Insurance Scheme 2) Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana and Atal Pension Yojana (Centrally Sponsored) to the Police Patil. However, if the subscription for this scheme is more than Rs.500/-, it should be paid from the honorarium of the Police Patil.
3) In case of death of police Patil in anti-Naxal operations in Naxal-affected areas, the ex-gratia grant of Rs 1 lakh to his heirs is being approved to be increased to Rs 5 lakh (Rs 5 lakh only).
4) It is being approved to increase the amount of award for the Governor's Award to the Police Patil of the state from Rs 5,000/- to Rs 25,000/- (twenty-five thousand only). Read about it.
