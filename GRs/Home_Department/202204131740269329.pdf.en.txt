The proposal: The High Court, Bombay, Bench at Nagpur, in Writ Petition No. 1078/2022 dated 22.03.2022, under Section 14 of the SARFAESI Act, 2002, the District Magistrate has been directed to create a permanent mechanism for taking action.
Accordingly, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to issue a circular to the field level officers under Section 14 of the Securitization and Reconstruction of Financial Assets and Enforcement of Security Interest Act, 2002 (SARFAESI Act) to be implemented by the District Magistrate in a proper and time-bound manner. Accordingly, the District Magistrate shall take action in accordance with the following guidelines under Section 14 of the SARFAESI ACT, 2002.
