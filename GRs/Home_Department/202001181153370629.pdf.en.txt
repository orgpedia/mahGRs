Government's decision:
The Mumbai-Pune National Highway No. 48 A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed on 11th February, 2019 between the Department of Home Affairs, Government of Maharashtra and SaveLIFE Foundation, New Delhi (Non-Governmental Organization) for collecting and analysing scientific data on accidents on this route and to suggest and implement necessary measures to reduce the number of accidents. As per the provisions of Clause 3.2 of the MoU, a Nodal Officer is being appointed for the implementation of the project as follows:
the Superintendent of Police (Headquarters),
Office of the Additional Director General of Police (Traffic), Highway Police Headquarters, D.D. Building, Old Excise Office,
Shaheed Bhagat Singh Marg, Mumbai
E-mail: highwaypolice. computerdiv@gmail. com
Phone: 022/22670313, 22615757
The Coordinating Officer will be responsible for preparing any relevant orders or documents to ensure smooth coordination between the department as per the agreement between the Government of Maharashtra and the SaveLIFE Foundation in this case.
