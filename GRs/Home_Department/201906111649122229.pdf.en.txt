Government Order:
Shri Siddhartha Keshavrao Sable, then Civil Judge, Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate, First Class was approved to hold the post of" Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group-A" vide Government Order, dated 24.10.2018. Now, Mr. Siddhartha Keshavrao
Sable is being appointed to the vacant post of Assistant Public Prosecutor in Washim district.
The appointments are being made with the prior approval of the competent authority in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
These orders shall come into force with immediate effect. Shri Siddharth Keshavrao Sable shall take charge of the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor with immediate effect. The order will be implemented with immediate effect
To become a director, the Complaints Directorate should strictly follow the following instructions.
(a) Mr. Siddharth Keshavrao Sable, Assistant Public Prosecutor, has been proposed for appointment in Washim district and his posting anywhere in this district shall be done by the Director, Directorate of Prosecutions.
(b) Mr. Siddharth Keshavrao Sable, Assistant Public Prosecutor shall be called upon to appear at the place of appointment without any delay. The report will be submitted to the government soon.
In case of non-payment of salary and allowances, the concerned officers will be held responsible.
