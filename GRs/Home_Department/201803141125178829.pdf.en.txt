Government's decision
A total of 1482 posts created for the protection and security of various establishments and individuals in the state have been given the reference serial number 14. In accordance with the Government Decision dated 22.01.2018.
01.01.2018 to D. The period has been extended up to 28.02.2018. The following temporary positions are now available. 01.03.2018 to D. Extension up to 30.09. 2018.
(i) 998 temporary posts of Security Subject as mentioned in Form A in the establishment of various Commissioners of Police/ Superintendents of Police under the control of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State.
(ii) 411 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, in respect of security matters mentioned in Form B.
(iii) A total of 72 temporary posts, 4 each of Police personnel created in Form A, for the protection of 18 Sub-Treasury Offices opened in the newly created Taluks in the State.
(iv) Special Inspector General of Police, Force One, State of Maharashtra.
