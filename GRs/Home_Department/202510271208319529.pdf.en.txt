The Proposal:
The Mobile Forensic Vans project will be implemented in the state of Maharashtra in line with the approval given under the Government Decisions on Reference No. 1 for conducting on-the-spot situational evidence collection procedures by Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratory experts in offences with a sentence of seven years or more to enforce the provision of the Indian Civil Protection Code, 2023.
The Directorate had proposed to create a new separate head of accounting for the Mobile Forensic Vans project as it required coordination, coordination and synergy in the requirement and utilization of funds. In pursuance of the approval given by the Office of the Principal Accountant General (Accounts and Entitlements) -I, Maharashtra, Mumbai for creation of a new separate title for the Mobile Forensic Vans Project under the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratory, the Government was considering the proposal for introduction of a separate title for the Mobile Forensic Vans Project.
The government's decision:
Under this Government decision, the following new separate heads of accounts are being approved for implementation of the entire project under a single Project Account Head for the Mobile Forensic Vans project being implemented by the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories.
