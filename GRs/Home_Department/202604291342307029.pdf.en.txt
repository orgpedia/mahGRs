Government Orders: -
For the purpose, Mr. Rajendra Sadashiv Keskar, Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Presently Appointed Sub-Regional Transport Officer Office, Gondia, District - Gondia v. Gondia Rural Police
A case has been registered at Thane, District Gondia under Section 7 (a) and 22 of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 (Amendment Act 2018), No. 697/2025, dated 05/12/2025.
While a case of criminal offence against Mr. Rajendra Sadashiv Keskar, Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Gondia is under investigation, Mr. Rajendra Keskar was arrested in the said offence on the date 05/12/2025.
Accordingly, in exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 4 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, the said Shri Rajendra Sadashiv Keskar, Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Gondia is hereby suspended from public service with effect from the date of this Government Order, namely, the date 29/04/2026, in terms of the provisions of Section 4 (2) (a) of the said Rules.

Accordingly, orders are also being passed that during the ASMA period, Mr. Rajendra Sadashiv Keskar, Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Gondia, be paid subsistence allowance and allowable dearness allowance as per Rule 68 (1) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption, superannuation and suspension, dismissal, remuneration during removal from service) Rules, 1989.
The Government is also ordering that the office of the Transport Commissioner, Mumbai, shall remain the headquarters of Shri Rajendra Sadashiv Keskar for as long as the suspension orders exist. Mr. Rajendra Sadashiv Keskar cannot leave the headquarters without the prior permission of the Transport Commissioner, Mumbai.
If they leave the headquarters without the prior permission of the Transport Commissioner, Mumbai, it will be their misconduct and they will be liable for a separate disciplinary action for the same.
