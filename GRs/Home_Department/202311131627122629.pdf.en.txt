The Proposal:
Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, as the Central Nodal Agency (CNA) for Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) project of New Delhi through CDAC, Thiruvananthapuram to implement the ERSS project in the State as a Central Sector Scheme. The Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) project is being implemented in the state under Dial 112 and the matter of opening a bank account in the name of the responsible officer in the state for the implementation of the Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) Central Sector Scheme was under consideration.
Government Decision: In the case of the subject matter, a Zero Balance Bank Account should be opened in Canara Bank authorized by the designation of Joint Secretary, Home Department as per the procedure detailed under the letter of reference of the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi for implementation of Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) project following the guidelines of the Central Government. The Additional Director General of Police (ADGP) has been given the responsibility to implement the Emergency Response Support System (ERSS). The government has decided to keep it through the office of Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
