Introduction: The Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, Ministry of Commerce& Industry, Government of India has released the State Reform Action Plan 2020 and District Level Business Reforms Action Plan 2020. Accordingly, an online portal for 18 reforms under the Ease of Doing Business related to the Home Department is to be created and its information is to be uploaded on the Central Government Information System with the help of the Directorate of Industries. Through this, an online portal was under consideration for the entire state for the renewal of licenses and permits issued by the licensing authority under the Maharashtra Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, 1966, the Public Entertainment Rules, 1960 and the Cinematograph Act, 1952.

Government's decision:
The General Administration Department (Information and Technology) Department, Government Resolution, No. As per Para- D- 3.3 of MATS-1716/ PROCESS 286/39, dated 9th August, 2016, Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation was instructed by the Department to submit the project report by doing System Requirement Specifications (S.R.S.) with the help of Director General of Police, Maharashtra State.
The MahaIT Corporation, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government and the Office of the Director General of Police for the creation of a system by preparing System Requirement Specifications (SRS) for the online portal for the renewal of licenses and permits issued under the Public Entertainment Rules, 1960 and the Maharashtra Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, 1966, for a total amount of Rs. 43,41,473/- (Rupees Forty-three Lakhs Forty-one Thousand Four Hundred and Seventy-three only) for the implementation and maintenance of the system.
3. Department of Finance, Financial Powers under Government Decision No. 2013/ No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17/4/2015. Rule 27A, 76 vests the administrative department with full powers of expenditure in terms of financial powers to purchase new computer systems and/ or to approve the upgradation of existing computer systems/ e-governance systems etc. and the installation and maintenance of air-conditioned systems required for the same.
In view of the above, the Office of the Director General of Police, Mumbai, has accorded administrative approval to the proposal submitted by MahaIT Corporation, Mumbai, for the development of an online portal for the renewal of licences and permits issued by the licensing authority under the Maharashtra Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, 1966, the Public Entertainment Rules, 1960 and the Cinematograph Act, 1952, for the entire State, at a cost of Rs. 43,41,473/- (Rs. 30,64,074/- for the creation of the portal and Rs. 12,77,399/- per annum for the continuation of the project).
