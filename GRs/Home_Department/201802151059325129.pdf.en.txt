Government's decision
In the budget for the financial year 2017-18, the Home Department- Demand No.B-10, 4070, Capital Expenditure on Other Administrative Services, (00) (11) Maharashtra State Police Housing Construction of Office Building of Police Department (4070 0211), under the head of this account, a total of Rs. A grant of Rs. 37 crore has been sanctioned. Of these, Rs. 17.60 crore (Rupees Seventeen Crores Sixty Lacs only) has been sanctioned vide Government Resolution, Home Department, No.POPRAMA-0617/ PR.No.345/ II/ POL 7, dated 09.08.2017.
The remaining funds of Rs. 8,75,85, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores, Seventy Five Lakhs, Eighty Five Thousand only) is being sanctioned for disbursement and expenditure.

The said expenditure should be met from the grants available in the current (2017-18) financial year under the head of Home Department- Demand No.B-10, 4070, Capital expenditure on other administrative services, (00) (11) Maharashtra State Police Housing Construction of office building of Police Department (4070 0211).
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the purpose of withdrawal of the said amount from the Accounts and Accounts Office, Mumbai.
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, shall spend the said amount as follows:
(1) The expenditure for completion of incomplete works shall be within the limits of the amounts specified in the administrative/ revised administrative sanction orders.
2) Funds should be provided only after considering the planning and progress of the work.
3) The remaining funds (if any) should be made available for new works, according to their priority.
" Construction of office building of Maharashtra State Police Housing Police Department" is not a new scheme. The scope and nature of the scheme has not changed.
