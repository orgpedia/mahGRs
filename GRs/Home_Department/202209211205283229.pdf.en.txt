Introduction: On the subject of" Police Housing." Under the chairmanship of Chief Minister. As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 27.07.2022 in the presence of the Deputy Chief Minister, the Government was considering the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Transport), Home Department, to develop the Metro Station, ST Depot and City Transport Depot and use the additional space thereon for the Police Service Residence.
Government's decision:
A committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Transport) to develop the Metro Station, ST Depot and City Transport Depot and use the additional space thereon to provide police service quarters.

The Member Secretary of the said Committee shall convene meetings from time to time as required and the minutes shall be made available to all concerned and the Home Department. Also, the home department should be informed about the action taken from time to time.
The Committee shall take necessary measures to expedite the redevelopment.
