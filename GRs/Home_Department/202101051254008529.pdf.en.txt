Read:- Letter No.- 25022/33/2020-F. of Ministry of Home Affairs, Ministry of External Affairs 26.11.2020.
Note: After the expiry of the visa period. The Union Home Secretary chaired a high-level meeting on 4,21,255 foreign nationals staying illegally in India since 01/01/2011. The meeting was held on 20.10.2020. The meeting reviewed the situation of foreign nationals staying illegally in the country. or
In pursuance of the decision taken in the meeting, the Union Ministry of Home Affairs, vide its letter, directed the State Governments/ UTs to constitute a Committee under the Chairmanship of Home Secretary, comprising of the concerned Foreigners Registration Officers (FROs) and State Police Officers, to identify the foreigners residing illegally in the State.
Action should be taken to trace the people. Accordingly, a committee under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary (Special) was constituted.
Government's decision:
After the expiry of the visa period. A Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Special) is being constituted to trace the missing and illegal foreigners residing in the State since 01/01/2011 as under:
