The Commissioner of Transport, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Maritime Board, Mumbai will be present as invited members for the meeting of the Project Implementation Committee in connection with the project related to their sub-division.
The decision of the Committee on e-Governance projects under the Department of Transport and Ports shall be final.
Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Transport) of the department is being declared as the nodal officer for the e-governance project.
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) To decide the policy in line with the e-governance policy of the Directorate of Information Technology.
2) To take a final decision regarding the online functioning of all zonal offices and autonomous bodies under the Home Department (Transport and Ports) and Transport Department. Meetings will also be held as needed.
3) To determine the scope of all e-governance projects to be implemented by the Home (Transport and Ports) Department and to guide the tender process by finalizing the RPF (Request for Proposal) to start the e-governance project for the department.
4) To take decision for procurement of hardware/ software required for implementation of e-Governance projects in Home (Transport& Ports) Department.
5) Review of e-Governance projects in the Home (Transport& Ports) Department. Selection of consultants from reputed organizations as per requirement for e-governance projects to be implemented in the Transport Department or Subordinate Offices.
