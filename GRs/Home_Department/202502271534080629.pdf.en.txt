Bacha: Letter from District Judge and Additional Sessions Judge, Phaltan, District Satara, No. 793/2024, dated 03.10.2024.
Preamble: - The Motor Accident Claims Tribunal has been constituted under Government Notification, Home Department No. MCT- 0805/1063/136 Transport-5, dated 25/07/2006 for the purpose of award of damages in case of an accident in which the use of a motor vehicle causes death or bodily injury to persons or damage to property of a third person or both to the extent indicated for that revenue jurisdiction in all the District B Taluks of the State. Also, District Judges posted in all districts and taluks of the state, Joint District Judge B Additional District Judge and Ad-hoc (Endwalk) Additional District Judge, members equivalent to officers of that rank who are deemed to have assumed charge of the office from time to time, Joint District Judge B Additional District Judge B Ad-hoc (Endwalk) Additional District Judge are empowered to handle cases of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal for that revenue jurisdiction. It was under the consideration of the government to declare him as the "Head of Office" as well as the "Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
Government Decision: - In exercise of the powers conferred by the Government Decision No. 2013 / Q. No. 30/2013 Regulations, Part-II, dated the 17th April, 2015 and the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Section I, Roll No. 3, Rule No. 2 and in exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 15 (3) w.b. 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the District Judge B. Additional Sessions Judge and ex-officio member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Phaltan, District Satara is being declared as the "Head of Office" as well as the "Drawing and Disbursing Officer." The orders will be limited to financial matters.
