the Proposal.
A total of 11 specified medical examinations of police personnel and officers of the age 45 years and above (excluding Indian Police Service officers) of the State Police Force from Government Hospitals during the period from 1st July, 2017 to 30th September, 2017 were approved vide Government Resolution, Home Department, No. POQUA-0617/ PR No. 348/ POL-7, dated 27.06.2017. The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai have requested the Government to grant more time to conduct the said investigations.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
government decision.
In pursuance of the Government Resolution, Department of Home Affairs, No. POKUA-0617/ PR No. 348/ POL-7, dated 27.06.2017, it is hereby approved to extend the date for conducting medical examination of police personnel and officers (excluding Indian Police Service officers) of the age 45 years and above in the State Police Force from 1st October, 2017 to 31st March, 2018.
