Introduction: The above reference No. Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district for a period of two years. Reference No. A total of 12 new posts have been created under the government decision. Reference No. 3 Under the Government decision, 2 additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals and 12 temporary posts were extended from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. Since the extension of the said 12 posts has expired on 28.02.2021, and as per Reference No. 4 vide letter dated 18.02.2021, the Chairman, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur had submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of tenure of two additional Tribunals and a total of 12 temporary posts in them.
Government Decision: Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals set up here and 12 temporary posts created for the same at Nagpur are being extended from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021. The extension is subject to expeditious disposal of the claims pending before the Tribunal, submission of the final returns of the settled claims for the month of May, 2021 and August, 2021 to the Government, and no further extension of time if 50% of the pending claims are not found to be settled by the end of February, 2021.
