The Proposal:
The General Administration Department has been asked to decide which officer should be given additional charge. The guidelines have been issued vide circular dated 05.09.2018 and all the administrative departments are requested to ensure that the provisions of the said circular are implemented. Powers to assign additional charge of Group' B' and Group' C' cadre posts falling vacant on account of transfer, leave and retirement etc. on the establishment of the State Excise Department. Circular dated 09.08.2001 has been issued to the Divisional Deputy Commissioners. As the Government is the appointing authority for Group-A and Group-B cadres in the department, the order regarding the assignment of additional charge of the vacant posts in the said cadre, as well as the order regarding the appointment of additional charge of Group-A and Group-B cadres in the department, shall, subject to the approval of the Government. The matter of issuing the Government decision after taking into consideration all the suggestions in the circular dated 09.08.2001 was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
If the posts of Group' B' and Group' C' cadre officers on the establishment of the State Excise Department become vacant due to transfer, leave and retirement, etc., then the additional charge of these posts will be handed over to other officers. By amending the orders dated 09.08.2001, the competent authority is now being determined to hand over the additional charge of the posts falling vacant due to leave, transfer, promotion or retirement of Group-A and Group-B cadre officers of the department as follows:

(2) The procedure for assignment of additional charge in the cases mentioned in the above table shall be as follows:
2.1 The Commissioner (Roush) shall, with the prior approval of the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Roushu), take action to assign additional charge to the posts falling vacant due to Group-A cadre leave.
2.2 The Commissioner (Raushu) shall take action to assign additional charge to the posts falling vacant due to leave in Group-B cadre.
2.3 Orders of additional charge in respect of Group A and Group B posts falling vacant on account of retirement, transfer and other reasons (excluding leave) shall be issued at the Government level, with the approval of the Government. The information about the vacancies in this regard should be submitted to the government by the Commissioner (Roush) from time to time.
