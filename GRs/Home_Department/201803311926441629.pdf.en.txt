Preamble: To expedite the railway projects in the State, to develop, implement and check the feasibility of railway projects, to increase the project capacity, to complete the projects within the stipulated time frame and to provide relief and rehabilitation to the project victims after land acquisition. The Company has been approved to be incorporated under the Government Resolution dated 24.01.2017, RLY 2015/ PR No.13/ TRANSPORT-5. As per the letter dated 08.06.2017 from Assistant Director (Policy), Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, the Central Government has granted permission to form a company by the name of Maharashtra Rail Marg Infrastructure Development Limited. Accordingly, the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) has been formed.
The state government and the central government each hold 50% stake in the company. In accordance with the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013, for registration of the name of the Company" Maharashtra Rail Marg Infrastructure Development Limited" Rs. 96,32,570/- has been spent. The balance amount is Rs 39,03,67, 430. The Rs. 39,03,67, 430/- was under consideration of the Government to be made available as the State Government's share in the authorised shareholding of Maharashtra Rail Marg Infrastructure Development Limited.
Government Decision: The State Government's shareholding in the authorized shareholding of Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Limited (MRIDL) will be Rs. 50.00 crore out of which Rs. 39,03,67, 430/- is being approved by this Government.
