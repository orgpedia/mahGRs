The government has decided to create a new district of Palghar by combining some talukas of Thane district. It has been in existence since 01.08.2014. Accordingly, the Superintendent of the newly created Palghar District, the referral for the State Excise Office, Dy. The post has been created as per the Government Decision dated 20.11.2015. The post of Superintendent (Group-A), State Excise has been created.
2. Regarding the vehicle admissible for various posts in the State Excise Department. The Government has issued a decision dated 07.02.2015, in which the vehicle has been made admissible to the post of Superintendent (Group-A), State Excise. However, subject to the condition that all working/ unregistered vehicles of the State Excise Department and its subordinate offices are immediately registered on the MahaVahan computer system, the Government is permitting one vehicle (car) for the post of Superintendent (Group-A), State Excise, Palghar in the newly created Palghar district.
3. The said Government Decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 16/19/ Regulations, dt. being issued with the consent received as on 02.02.2019.
