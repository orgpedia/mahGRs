Hanuman Jayanti 2021 guidelines.
Hanuman Jayanti is celebrated with much fervour across the country.
the current year. Hanuman Jayanti will be celebrated on April 27, 2021. In view of COVID-19, this year all religious festivals/ celebrations as well as all events are being celebrated in a very simple manner. Considering the present situation of the second wave of COVID-19 and the increasing number of Corona patients, the Ministry of Revenue and Forest Department, Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation, Mumbai, vide its order dated 13th April, 2021, has issued the following orders: Hanuman Jayanti celebrations are required to be celebrated this year subject to the provisions mentioned in Sections 1, 2, 7 and 10.
In this regard, the following guidelines are being issued: 1. Hanuman Jayanti is celebrated by people in large numbers. But this year, to avoid the spread of Covid-19, people are expected to celebrate Hanuman Jayanti at their homes in a simple manner without gathering.
Every year on the occasion of Hanuman Jayanti, various programs are organized across Maharashtra. However, all religious places will remain closed in view of the COVID-19 pandemic and devotees will not be allowed to visit the temples. No bhajan, kirtan, recitation etc. or any kind of religious, cultural or social programs should be organized in public in the temple this year.
The manager/ trustee of the temple should make the facility of darshan available through online, cable network, web site and Facebook etc. if possible.
4) No processions or processions should be taken out on the occasion of Hanuman Jayanti.
To prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus, the rules prescribed by the Government Relief, Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department as well as the concerned Municipal Corporation, Police Administration, Local Administration should be strictly followed.
Also, if any other instructions are published after this circular and in the period between the start of the actual festival, they should also be complied with.
