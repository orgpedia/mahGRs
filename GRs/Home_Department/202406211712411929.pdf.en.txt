The Proposal: -
Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) operates urban bus services in the state.
Women and senior citizens of Maharashtra are exempted from travelling in Ratnagiri and Sangli divisions.
The matter of making it permissible was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Ratnagiri (10 buses) and Sangli (40 buses) will be run by the corporation.
50% concession for women in urban bus transport in Maharashtra state, 65% in Maharashtra state
50% travel concession for senior citizens between the ages of 15 and 75 years and 75% in the state of Maharashtra.
City buses in Ratnagiri and Sangli districts with 100% free travel concession for citizens above the age of 18
Government approval to make the service permissible subject to the condition that it will be discontinued in a phased manner.
