The Government has given in-principle approval for setting up of a "Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)" with a view to harnessing "Artificial Intelligence" to enable the State Police Force to carry out law enforcement functions more effectively, on the following conditions: -
1) For the purpose of establishing the "SPV" as aforesaid, -
(1) State Government; (2) Indian Institute of Management, Nagpur; and (3) May. Pinaka Technologies Private Limited.
An agreement must be reached between these three parties.
The said "Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)" should be registered as a private limited company under the name "Maharashtra Advanced Research and Vigilance for Enhanced Law Enforcement (MARVEL)" with 100% government share capital.
The Superintendent of Police, Nagpur (Rural), will be the ex-officio Director and CEO of the said company and will work directly under the Zasana (Home Department).
The Director, Indian Institute of Management, Nagpur, shall be the ex-officio director of the said company and the other directors of the company shall be nominated by the Government.
