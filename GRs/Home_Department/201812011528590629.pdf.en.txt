Introduction: Department of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection, Government of India. 16.7.2018 and dt. As per the letter dated 17.7.2018, a committee was set up to study and verify the quantity of water-based liquor produced from three separate sources namely B-Hy, C-Hy mallee and sugarcane juice in order to determine the raw material wise separately. Based on the recommendations of the Committee, the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution, New Delhi vide its Circular No. 40/1/2018- (BP& E) dated 18.9.2018 has issued guidelines to be followed by sugar mills and distilleries for production of ethanol from B-heavy and C-heavy mixtures.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to constitute a committee to determine the exact quantity of liquor produced from B-Hy, C-Hy milli and sugarcane juice from distilleries attached to distilleries and sugar mills in the state and to determine the policy of the state government based on the circular instructions of the central government mentioned above.
Government Decision: The following committee is being constituted to determine the exact amount of liquor produced from B-Hy, C-Hy and sugarcane juice from the distilleries attached to the distilleries and sugar mills in the state and to determine the policy of the state government based on the circular instructions of the central government.
