In order to avoid" Thin Spread of Financial Resources," the Government Circular dated 11.06.2018 has made a provision as to how the total cost ceiling should be considered while granting administrative approval to the construction proposals of residential/ office and other buildings in a particular financial year by the administrative departments.
However, by virtue of the said formula, the M.R. Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Limited. They are facing difficulties in getting new projects. Therefore, it was decided in the meeting of the Board of Directors of the said Corporation dated 06.12.2018 to submit a proposal to the Government (Home Department) regarding exemption from the provisions of the said Government Circular (Resolution No. 1510) and the Managing Director was authorized to take further action in this regard. Accordingly, the Managing Director has requested the Government to issue necessary orders to exempt the said Corporation from the provisions of the said Government Circular.
