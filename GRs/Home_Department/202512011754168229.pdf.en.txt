The Proposal:
The Home Department has issued a circular to periodically review the potential threat to important royalty and other persons in the state and propose a security category for them. Under the 31.03.1986, a high-level committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of the Commissioner, State Intelligence Department. Government circular The committee has been reconstituted under 18.09.2007.
The Home Department has taken a decision to appraise the report submitted by the high-level committee and make recommendations to the government. Under 5.02.2011. A revamp committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. The decision was taken by the Home Department. The final decision will be taken at the level of this Review Committee on the report on Classification Security submitted by the High Level Committee under 01.12.2014. It has been decided that it will not require government approval.
It has been a long time since the formation of the High-Level Committee on Protection of Classified Protected Persons as well as the Review Committee, and since then there have been some significant changes in the police system of the state and some new departments and important posts have been created. Keeping all these in mind, the Commissioner, State Intelligence Department had sent a proposal to the government that the committee should be reconstituted by including some officers in the existing high-level committee as well as the Palacios committee. Read on No. The government was considering reconstituting the High-Level Committee as well as the Review Committee by streamlining the proposed Government Decisions 9 to 3.
