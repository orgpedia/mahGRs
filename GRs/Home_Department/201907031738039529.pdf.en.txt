(2) These orders shall come into force with immediate effect. The officer should take charge of the post immediately after transfer. For the immediate and effective implementation of the transfer order, the following action should be taken by the Commissioner, Raushu.
(a) The transferred officer shall be relieved forthwith on receipt of the order of transfer. The transferred officers should be reinstated without delay after they are relieved from their present posts. In case of non-recruitment of these officers, the officers concerned will be held responsible for the payment of salary and allowances by fixing a mandatory waiting period without work.
(b) The date on which the officers concerned were relieved from their duties to appear in place of the new appointees and the date on which they appeared in place of the new appointees shall be intimated to the Government forthwith.
