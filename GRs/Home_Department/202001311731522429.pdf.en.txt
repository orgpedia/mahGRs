B/ 2019. No. 16428, Dy. 16.11.2019 Preamble: Section 113 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 contains prohibitive provisions for carrying more than the capacity. The Motor Vehicles Department has also been empowered to check overloaded vehicles under Section 114. As per Section 114 of the Motor Vehicles Act, the officer of the Motor Vehicles Department has the power to bring a vehicle to a weigh-down. After the inspection of such a vehicle, there is a provision to unload the vehicle if excess load is found in the vehicle and do not allow the vehicle to move forward without doing so. Accordingly, the detailed procedure has been mentioned in Section 223 of the Maharashtra Motor Vehicles Rules. The government was contemplating to provide suitable portable weights to drivers, including the state's airspeed teams, to prevent overloading of the state by intensifying the implementation of overloading throughout the state.
Government Decision: In order to prevent overloading in the state by intensifying the implementation of overloading in the state, the airspeed teams of the Motor Vehicle Department of the state can purchase 15 portable weight cuts easily on the GeM portal and for this, the per weight cut will be approximately Rs. 25,00,000/- to a total of Rs. Administrative and Financial Sanction for an amount of Rs. 3,75,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Seventy Five Lakhs only) is being granted subject to the following conditions.
