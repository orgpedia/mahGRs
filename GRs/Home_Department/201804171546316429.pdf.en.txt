Introduction: The Government has taken an important decision to implement the concept of" Green Maharashtra" to reduce the intensity and severity of natural disasters such as climate change, rising temperatures, drought and water scarcity. Under the programme, a target of planting 50 crore trees has been set for the period 2017-2019. In addition, in 2016. A unique and ambitious programme of planting 2 crore trees on a single day on 1st July, 2016 has been implemented.
The target is to plant 13 crore and 33 crore trees in 2018 and 2019 respectively.
It is very important that the goal of tree plantation is fully achieved and at the same time the trees planted so far are protected, maintained and nurtured well.
Presentation of 4 crore tree plantation programmes during 1st July to 7th July, 2017 In front of the cabinet. It was held on June 13, 2017. As per the discussion, action will be taken. As agreed by the Council of Ministers, the matter of communicating the suggestions to the administrative departments was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, instructions are being issued through this circular.
The circular:
In meetings at all levels under the Prisons Department, the subject of tree plantation and nurturing should be included as a permanent subject in the agenda at the time of each meeting. In such meetings, review of tree plantation, survival rate of trees planted earlier, planting of new trees in place of dead trees, fixing of sites for plantation as per the target for tree plantation in the coming years, availability of saplings, preparation of pits within the stipulated time as per the required technical parameters, availability of funds, etc. should be reviewed.
