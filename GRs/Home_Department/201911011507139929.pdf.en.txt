Introduction: The corporation reimburses the expenses incurred on account of the discounted value of passenger fares to various social groups. The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) pays 17.5 per cent of the total passenger tax to the government. Reference No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 20.6.2000, it was decided to pay 12 per cent of the passenger tax to the Government and the remaining 5.5 per cent to be made available to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation as capital contribution. Thereafter, No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 04.07.2015, it has been decided to adjust the balance 10% of passenger tax as capital contribution to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) and the balance 7.5% of passenger tax to be adjusted in the books as reimbursement of the concessional rate payable by the Government to the Corporation.
For the year 2019-20, Rs. 1450.00 crore is in the budget.
Of these, Rs. 132,99,22, 615/- for the year 2018-19 was under consideration of the Government for making book adjustments in return of concessional value of passenger tax.
Government Decision: Out of the balance amount to be paid to Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation towards reimbursement of various travel concession value, the total amount of passenger tax to be paid by the corporation to the government is Rs. 132,99,22, 615/- (Rupees One Hundred and Thirty Two Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand Six Hundred and Fifteen only) is being approved.
