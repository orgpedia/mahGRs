The Proposal:
Shri Siddhartha Keshavrao Sable, as per the recommendation of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission vide Government Order No. DPP-2011/ Pr. No.60/ Pol-10, dated 28.06.2011 was appointed as Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A' for a period of probation. Accordingly, Shri Siddharth Keshavrao Sable appeared on 06.07.2011. Shri Siddhartha Keshavrao Sable, having satisfactorily completed the period of probation of two years, is hereby disqualified by the Government Resolution, Home Department, No.DPP-2015/ PR. 33/ Pol-10, dated 24.06.2015 had expired.
Shri Siddharth Keshavrao Sable, Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A', while serving in the Government Service, had submitted an application to the Director, Directorate of Complaints, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, dated 20.11.2015 for continuation of lien and discharge of duty on the ground of having passed the Civil Judge, Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Examination. A letter dated 27.11.2015 from the Director, Directorate of Grievances had submitted a proposal to the Government for relieving Shri Siddharth Keshavrao Sable to join the post of Civil Judge, Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class. 383/ POL-10, dated 11.12.2015 issued by Shri.
The orders have been issued relieving Siddharth Keshavrao Sable, Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group A, of the charge of Civil Judge, Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class with effect from 11.12.2015 (M. No.

206/ Pol-10, thereafter, Shri Siddhartha Keshavrao Sable, through the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Bhandara, had submitted his application for permission to re-join the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A', as on 09.06.2017. Mr.
Appointment of Shri Siddharth Keshavrao Sable as Civil Judge, Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class is under scrutiny and he has not exercised his lien as a permanent employee on the said post. Also, the term of office has not expired. Therefore, since he continues to hold the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A' under the Directorate of Grievances under the Home Department, it has been decided to appoint him to the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A' under this department as per the provisions of Rule 20, 22 and 25 (1) (2) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (General Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981.
Government's decision:
Shri Siddharth Keshavrao Sable had requested for appointment to the substantive post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A' under the Home Department. The Government has decided to appoint Shri Siddhartha Keshavrao Sable as Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A" as he continues to hold the post. Mr.
Siddhartha Keshavrao Sable should immediately join the Director, Directorate of Complaints, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, after retiring from the present post.
Orders for the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A" of Siddharth Keshavrao Sable will be issued separately.
This Government Resolution is being issued as per the concurrence given under the Informal Reference No. 27/18/ Service-6, dated 08.03.2018 of the Department of Finance.
