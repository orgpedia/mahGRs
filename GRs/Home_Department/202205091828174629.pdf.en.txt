Government circular:
As part of the preventive measures to be taken at the Ministerial level to prevent the spread of Corona Virus disease worldwide, the Department of Home Affairs, vide Government Circular No.
As per MIS-0919/ PR No. 226/ VISA-4, dated 16th March, 2020, the process of issuance of Ministry Entry Pass through Visitor Pass Management System (VPMS) system for issuing entry passes to visitors to the Ministry was closed till further orders. Further, the General Administration Department, vide Government Circular No. 1820/ P.S. 12/2020/18-A, dated 30.05.2021, has made arrangements to receive outgoing mail at the entry pass windows located near the Mantralaya entrance for the convenience of the general public in the wake of COVID-19 virus infection. For this, the approval of the Home Department was given subject to the condition that after the VPMS system being implemented to issue entry passes is restored, the possession of these windows should be returned to the Home Department for use.
The order was issued in accordance with the instructions and restrictions issued by the notification, circular and government decision issued from time to time by the Central Government, Health Department and Disaster Management Department during the Corona virus outbreak.
In the meantime, there was a continuous demand from various social organizations, political parties and the general public to open the ministry for the general public. Further, the Department of Revenue and Forest, Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation, Government Order dated 31.03.2022 has exempted the restrictions imposed in the wake of Corona virus in the state of Maharashtra.
In this background, the Visitor Pass Management System (VPMS), which is being implemented for issuing entry passes to the candidates coming to the Ministry, is being restored from May 18, 2022. In this regard, all the terms and conditions prescribed under the Home Department, Government Resolution No:- PES-421/ PR No. 108/ VISA-4 dated 24.12.2021 shall continue to apply.
