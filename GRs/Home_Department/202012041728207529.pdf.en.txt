In order to prevent serious crimes related to immoral human trafficking of women and children in the state, Immoral Human Trafficking Prevention Cell has been established in a total of 12 units. Now the establishment of new Immoral Traffic Prevention Cell in 24 units was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
The state government has set up Immoral Traffic Prevention Cells in 12 civic bodies in Mumbai city, Pune city, Thane city, Nagpur city, Thane rural, Sangli, Navi Mumbai, Solapur, Kolhapur, Ahmednagar, Beed and Yavatmal to prevent immoral human trafficking of women and children.
Now new Immoral Traffic Prevention Cells are being set up in the following 24 units.
