Preamble: The recruitment process for 5297 vacancies in the Police Constable Cadre in the State Police Force in the year 2019 is in progress. During the period from January 2020 to December 2020, about 6726 posts of constables, constables, drivers and armed constables will be available for recruitment on the basis of retirement/ promotion/ resignation etc. and 7231 (6726+ 505) posts of constables will be available for recruitment in the first phase out of 975 newly created posts of constables for Mira-Bhayandar Vasai-Virar Police Commissionerate. At present, a total of 12528 (5297+ 7231) posts are available for recruitment in the Police Constable Cadre.
Meanwhile, in the context of the Department of Finance taking fiscal measures to deal with the impact of COVID-19 on the economy in the financial year 2020-21, the Finance Minister, Shri Arun Jaitley, has given a detailed presentation on the impact of COVID-19 on the economy. As per the Government decision dated 4th May 2020, except for the Public Health Department and Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals Department, no other department is allowed to recruit any new posts.
Since there is a dire need for posts in the police constabulary cadre in terms of law and order, it is necessary to fill all 100% vacancies in this cadre immediately.
In the background above. The Cabinet in its meeting held on 13/09/2020 has decided to fill up 100% of the total 12528 (5297+ 6726+ 505) posts of Police Constables in the cadre of Police Constables in the year 2019 and 6726 posts in the year 2020 and 505 posts of Police Constables in the first phase of 975 newly created posts for Mira-Bhayandar-Vasai-Virar Police Commissionerate. Finance- 2020/ Q.No.65/ Finance-3, D. The Cabinet has approved the proposal for relaxation with effect from May 4, 2020.
