The Proposal:
Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has invited online applications from eligible candidates for recruitment to the post of Group A through direct recruitment and Group B through training programme to be conducted by Yashoda, Pune and Vanamati, Nagpur. The decision is being implemented as per the government's 1. General Administration Department Reference No. 4 In pursuance of the above order, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission has appointed the candidates selected on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination, 2019 as Trainee Probationary Officers for the Combined Training Programme-7. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts in the cadre of Deputy Superintendent Group-B and Assistant Commissioner Group-B in the State Excise Department for the period up to the completion of training (two years) for the candidates selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination 2019 and appointed as Trainee Probationary Officers for the Combined Training Programme 7. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has approved the creation of a total of 9 supernumerary posts of Deputy Superintendent Group-B, Pay Scale S-15 (41,800 1,32,300) and Assistant Commissioner Group-B, Pay Scale S-15 (41,800- 1,32,300) in the State Excise Department, selected on the basis of the final results of the State Service Main Examination-2019 and appointed as Trainee Probationary Officer for Combined Training Programme-7, for a period of two years from the date of commencement of training of these candidates.
