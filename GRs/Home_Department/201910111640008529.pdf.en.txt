In pursuance of the Government Decision, Department of Home Affairs, No.Parivartan-0316/ S.No.198/ Pol-7, dated 30.09.2016, it is hereby approved to extend the term of office of the Director General of Police, State of Maharashtra, 48 posts of Non-Monitorial Pediatrician in the offices of the Commissioner of Police and Superintendent of Police and other establishments and 20 posts of various other cadres, totalling 68 such temporary posts from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 on the following terms: 1. The extension shall not be applicable to the posts lying vacant for a period of more than six months.
2) The terms and conditions under which the said 68 posts have been sanctioned should be strictly complied with.
3) The Director General of Police, Maharashtra should review all the posts and submit the revised figure immediately for the approval of the government. After this, it should be noted that the term of the said posts will not be extended to determine the format.
The expenditure on salary and allowances of the said 68 posts shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the respective heads of accounts for the financial year 2019-20.
