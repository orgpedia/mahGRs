Section 22C of the Maharashtra Police Act (22 of 1951) provides for the establishment of Division Level Police Complaints Authorities. As per Government Resolution, Home Department dated 10/09/2014, various cadre posts have been created in the Authority. Government notification 13/04/2017 and D. As on 15/04/2018, the Division Level Police Complaints Authorities at Pune, Amravati, Navi Mumbai, Nashik, Nagpur and Aurangabad have been reduced. Authorities in Pune and Nashik have become operational and the remaining authorities will be operational soon. A fixed advance of Rs.5,000/-to meet the postage, xerox and other minor expenses required for this office. The authorities have been approved as per the government decision here.
However, since the financial year was mentioned in the said Government decision unnecessarily, the matter of issuing a new Government decision by superseding the said Government decision was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Government Resolution, Serial No., dated 30/01/2018 is being superseded.
