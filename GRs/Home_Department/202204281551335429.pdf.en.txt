Mamebo/ Muka/ Abhi-3/ Janjira Jetty/ 2021-22/255 Dt. 15.03.2022 Preamble: Maharashtra has a large coastline. Along the coast there are many historical and scenic spots. The fort is located on the banks of the Arabian Sea and is surrounded by the fort of Murud-Janjira in Raigad district. To go to this fort from the Rajapuri jetty the domestic and foreign tourists have to go through the ladder boats. There is no jetty equipped for boat landing at Janjira Fort, so tourists do not have the facility to safely board/ disembark from the boat at Janjira Fort. Sometimes accidents can't be avoided. Therefore, it is necessary to provide safe boarding/ disembarkation facility to the passengers/ tourists while entering the Janjira Fort.
