Government Decision: The Government is issuing orders regarding payment of salary and dearness allowance at the rate of one and a half times of the permissible salary to the police officers/ staff working in the sensitive areas of Naxal affected Gadchiroli, Aheri (Police District) and Gondia districts as follows:
1) The police officers/ staff working in the sensitive areas of Naxal affected Gadchiroli, Aheri (Police District) and Gondia districts (including State Reserve Police Force, Non-Terror Messaging Unit, Motor Transport Department, Criminal Investigation Department, Anti-Corruption Bureau, Traffic Department, Special Task Force, Anti-Naxal Operations, State Intelligence Bureau, etc.) should be given basic pay and dearness allowance at the rate of one and a half times the permissible salary. Similarly, in Gondia district, the serial number (4) in the address has been fixed by the Government Decision dated 18.02.2011.

All India Services Officers and State Services Officers/ Staff working in Police Stations/ Police Sub-stations/ Armed Reserve and Offices shall be paid salary and dearness allowance at the rate of one and half times of the basic pay admissible to them. The salary and dearness allowance should be given at the rate of one and a half times the permissible salary for the working period considering the risky work.
(2) The benefit of one-time promotion is admissible to the officers/ employees of the State Intelligence Department, wherever they are working in the State. However, the nature of work of the officers/ staff working in Gondia and Gadchiroli districts is very risky. In this district

Salary at the rate of one and half times of the permissible salary as in the case of officers/ staff working in Sub-stations/ Armed Remote Areas plus Dearness Allowance, whichever is higher.
3) State Reserve Police Force units are operating in the hyper sensitive areas of Gadchiroli, Aheri/ Gondia districts. Therefore, those who are working in sensitive sectors should also be given basic pay plus dearness allowance at the rate of one and a half times the permissible salary. State Reserve Police Force officers/ personnel working in the said sensitive areas will be paid at the rate of 1.5 times.
Dearness Allowance and Dearness Allowance will be applicable.
(4) The concerned officers/ employees working in the Naxal affected areas will not be entitled to any other financial concessions as applicable to the officers/ employees working in the Naxal affected areas by the General Administration Department. The officials concerned should look into the matter and pay the salaries accordingly.
5) The expenditure on salary and Dearness Allowance in this regard shall be covered under the head of accounts under which the salary and allowances of these employees are spent and shall be covered by the approved provision.
These orders will be applicable for the period from 01.04.2018 to 31.03.2020.
These orders are being issued in accordance with the concurrence given in the Informal Reference No. 237/18/ Expenditure-7 dated 21.08.2018 of the Department of Finance.
