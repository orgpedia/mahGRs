Government's decision:
A case has been registered at Pimpri Chinchwad police station under IPC 765/2022. Advocate Ujjwal Nikam, appointed as Special Public Prosecutor to look after the court proceedings in the Sessions Court, Shivajinagar, Pune on behalf of the Government, is being approved to pay the fees as follows.
Court of Sessions, Shivajinagar, Pune
Daily Effective Hearing Fee- Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees One Lakh only) Discussion Fee/ Per Hour Portion- Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand only)
Apart from this fee, no other benefit will be admissible to Adv. Ujjwal Nikam. Also, they will not be provided any fee for any inconclusive hearing.
(2) The effective date of hearing shall be fixed by the officer not below the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case. The concerned Deputy Commissioner of Police/ Superintendent of Police shall ensure that the same is checked and certified by the Court Manager and no excess is provided.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the sanctioned grant and monthly fund statement subject to the availability of budgetary provision for that financial year under the head" Demand No.B-1, 2055 Police- (00) 109, District Police- (00) (01) District Police Force- (2055 0168)-28 Professional Services."
This Government Decision was issued by the Department of Finance vide No. 03/ 2023/ Expenditure-7, No. Issued pursuant to Government approval received under 04/01/2023.
