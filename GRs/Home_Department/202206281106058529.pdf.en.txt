The Lecturers invited for in-service training to be conducted at various levels for the State Police Force are given reference no. 1 here. The honorarium was being paid as per the Government decision dated 11.12.2009. The police force faces various challenges in the changing nature of crime and maintaining law and order. For this, in-service training of officers/ staff of the police force is very important. An experienced and qualified instructor is required to provide quality training. Accordingly, in the honorarium of lecturers invited for training to be given from Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik as well as Police Training Centers and various police units, reference No. 2 here. The amendment has been made under the Government Resolution dated 22.11.2021. Now, the matter of revision of stationery, food, tea and snacks and travel expenses of the invited lecturers was under consideration of the Government.

Government's decision:
The cost of stationery, meals, tea and snacks and travel expenses of the invited lecturers for the training to be imparted from Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik as well as from Police Training Centres and various Police Units is being revised as mentioned below.
(c) Writing Material:- The cost of writing material shall be as per rules.
Food, Tea and Snacks: The Police Training Centre Hostel/ Mess will be used for this purpose. In addition to the basic training, in-service training for one subject workshop for each lecturer and trainee (for Working Lunch) will be provided at a cost of Rs. Rs 300/-. If the cost is being met from the hostel or mess of the training center, then there will be no need to spend separately. But the cost incurred will be paid as mess charges.
(c) Travel expenses of Lecturers:- The travel expenses of the lecturers to be called for imparting training shall be reimbursed as follows.
Economy class air travel will be allowed for the selected lecturers/ experts who will be called for training to be held at Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik. Also, the travel expenses payable to the lecturers other than the nominated lecturer/ expert as per their category will be admissible or as per the category of their last appointment.
2) The fare for air-conditioned second class/ air-conditioned third class/ air-conditioned chair car, as per their category, shall be admissible to the nominated lecturers/ experts to be called for various trainings to be conducted at the Commissioner of Police/ Superintendent of Police Office/ Police Training Centres. Also, the travel expenses payable to lecturers other than the nominated lecturer/ expert as per their category will be admissible.
2.The above revised rates will be applicable from the date of issue of the Government Order. All training expenses must be paid for by the grant.
This Government decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 40/2022/ Expenditure-7 of the Department of Finance. Issued with the consent received as on 25.02.2022.
