Preamble: Public Interest Litigation No. In the case of 28/2013. The Bombay High Court. 18.02.2016, it has been ordered that video cameras should be installed for recording all tests related to transport vehicles and arrangements should be made to preserve video footage of the test of each vehicle. According to In order to implement the High Court orders, the Regional Transport Office, Thane, is required to implement a CCTV system for video recording of vehicle checks. Accordingly, a proposal to purchase CCTV cameras for recording the tests on the brake test track of the office of the Regional Transport Office, Thane, was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: A total of 16 CCTV cameras will be purchased for recording vehicle inspection on the brake test track of the Regional Transport Office, Thane and Rs. 2,95,000/- (Rupees Two Lakh Ninety Five Thousand only) is being given administrative and financial sanction.
