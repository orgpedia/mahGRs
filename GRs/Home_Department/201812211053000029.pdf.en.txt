GOVERNOR'S DECISION: 69th Anniversary of Indian Republic to be celebrated on 26th January, 2019 at Shivaji Park, Dadar, Mumbai. In this program, a tableau on the topic of' Modern Progress of Mumbai Police" will be taken out by the Mumbai Traffic Police. However, the Government is sanctioning an amount of Rs. 14,16000/- (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Sixteen Thousand only) for the conduct of the said Chitraratha.
The Joint Commissioner of Police (Traffic), Worli, Mumbai will disburse a total amount of Rs. 14,16,000/- (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Sixteen Thousand only) as Controlling Officer.
3. Expenditure in the present case is borne by the Home Department vide Demand No. B-01, under the head" 2055 Police, 108 State Headquarters Police, (02) City Police, (02) (01), Establishment,- (20550097), 21 Materials and Supplies" should be shared from the sanctioned grant for the year 2018-19.
4. The procedure laid down by the Government shall be followed while purchasing or hiring the goods and services required for the operation of the tableau. Also, complete details of the expenditure incurred for the tableau parade should be submitted to the government immediately.
This Government decision is being issued in exercise of the financial powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-section 3, Sequence No. 4, Paragraph No. 27 (2) (a).
