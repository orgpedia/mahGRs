The Proposal:
Contextualization of the establishment of Civil Defence Force for Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg districts

These positions have been vacant for more than eight years. Sadar

expired as per the provision dated 10.9.2001. the Directorate of Civil Defence

presented. Accordingly, a proposal to revive these posts has been made by the Finance Department.
