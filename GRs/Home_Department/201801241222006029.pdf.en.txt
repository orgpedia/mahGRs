Preamble: To make available to the investigating agencies within a stipulated period of time, after analyzing the samples seized in various crimes occurring in the society through various laboratories under the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories in a scientific manner, and thereafter, to make the report available to the investigating agencies.
These laboratories have an important role in presenting scientific evidence in court.
The meeting was chaired by Principal Secretary (Special), Home Department. The Committee constituted under the Government Resolution dated 12.5.2015 has submitted its report to the Government. The Chief Minister has accepted it. The recommendations include setting up of five new laboratories and providing state-of-the-art machinery and equipment.
The Directorate of Forensic Science Laboratories has its headquarters at Mumbai and a total of 8 Regional Forensic Science Laboratories are functioning at Nagpur, Pune, Aurangabad, Nashik and Amravati, Nanded and Kolhapur. With a view to provide timely scientific evidence to the investigating agencies and the judicial system in the interest of social welfare, the cases should be disposed of on priority basis by reducing the pendency of cases in the existing Regional Forensic Science Laboratories and to increase the conviction rate in criminal cases.

The government was considering setting up of mini-judicial scientific laboratories in the district.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to set up Mini Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories at the location of Chandrapur district to reduce the burden of pending cases on Regional Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories under the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories in the State.
2. The Government is giving approval to start Biology Department and Toxicology Department in this mini Legal Aid Scientific Laboratory.
3. As a temporary arrangement till a permanent place is made available for this mini lab, Rs. 2.00 lakh will be provided on rent. Thereafter, the proposal for construction of building for this laboratory will be submitted separately if government land is available from various departments of the government.
