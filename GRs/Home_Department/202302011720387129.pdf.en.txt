In order to re-employ the officers/ staff appointed under the Government decision dated 14.10.2022 from the Scheduled Tribes category but who could not produce the caste validity certificate or who had submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes, pending court cases regarding the caste validity certificate, a total of 04 officers/ staff on the establishment of the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories were temporarily transferred to the above number of posts for a period of 11 months or till the date on which they would have retired if they had remained in service, whichever is earlier.
Reference No. 2. 2 A total number of 06 supernumerary posts were created in the establishment of the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories, Mumbai to re-employ the officers/ staff who were appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but could not produce the caste validity certificate or who had submitted the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes, pending court cases regarding the caste validity certificate. Out of the total number of officers/ employees appointed to this post, 2 have retired. At present, 4 numbers of posts are working on the establishment of the Directorate of the Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratory.
(3) The number of posts created for the transfer of services and services transferred to the following number of officers/ staff on the establishment of the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories, Mumbai and the appointments made thereon shall be made on a temporary basis. The extension is being granted for a period of 11 months from 01.10.2022 or till the date on which such officer/ employee would have retired from service, whichever is earlier, and till the recommendations of the Study Group are received and a final decision is taken by the Government.
