Government circular
Since the Fisheries Department does not have the post of Chief Engineer, the technical approval for the work of the Fish Port in Anandwadi Taluka Devgad, District Sindhudurg, to be implemented by the Maharashtra Fisheries Development Corporation under the Department of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries, will be jointly given by the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Maritime Board. Further, the Chief Engineer, Maharashtra Maritime Board should give technical approval to the works of the Fisheries Development Department by the Chief Engineer of the Public Works Department who is on deputation to the Maharashtra Maritime Board establishment to look after the works of the Fisheries Ports, Jetties and other allied works through the Fisheries Development Department and Maharashtra Fisheries Development Corporation.
This Government Circular is being issued as approved under the Informal Reference No. 83978/ Secretary (Roads), Public Works Department, dated 15.11.2019.
