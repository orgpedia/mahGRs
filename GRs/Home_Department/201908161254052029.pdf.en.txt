As per the proposal submitted by the Additional Director General of Police, Anti-Terrorism Squad, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter dated 17.11.2018, the matter was under the consideration of the Government to approve the creation of a total of 2 fictitious posts of 01-Legal Advisor, 01-Legal Officer (Class-A) on the establishment of the Additional Director General of Police, Anti-Terrorism Squad and filling these posts by external agencies. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to create 02 notional posts of Legal Advisor-01 and Law Officer-01 (Class-A) in the establishment of Additional Director General of Police, Anti-Terrorism Squad, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and to fill these posts through external agencies.
The newly created posts will be filled through external agencies for a period of 11 months with a monthly honorarium of Rs. 40,000 for 11 months. 4,40,000/- and Law Officer- 01 (Class-A) with a monthly honorarium of Rs. 35,000 for 11 months. 3,85,000/- is being approved.
The above posts of Legal Adviser and Law Officer (Class-A) are to be filled through outsourcing. Only the age, required educational qualification, experience, duties and responsibilities will be as per the Home Department, Government Decision No. SPP-2005/ PR No. 247/ Pol-10, Annexure' A'.
4. The action to fill up these posts by external agencies should be taken by the Additional Director General of Police, Anti-Terrorism Squad. These positions will be available for one year only.
