Now, the Government hereby approve the extension of tenure of the above mentioned 1,330 temporary posts in various offices under the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai from 1st March, 2018 to 30th September, 2018 subject to the following conditions:
The extension will not be applicable to temporary posts (excluding executive posts) lying vacant for more than six months.
2) The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned, subject to the terms and conditions, should be complied with.
Government Resolution, Finance Division, No.Assank-1001/ PR No. 29/2001/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 10th September, 2001, No.Assank-1004/ PR. No. 12/2004/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 19th August, 2004, No. No. 8/16/ Fiscal Reform-1, dated 21st September, 2016 and No. The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall complete the process of final approval of the High Level Committee of the office by completing the process of review of the posts as per 8/16/ APC, dated 10th February, 2017 immediately and complete the process within the prescribed time in any case. The suggestions given in this regard should be considered.
