The Proposal:
The implementation of the Mumbai City Women Safety Initiative Scheme (Nirbhaya), a Centrally and State sponsored scheme, has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 31.01.2019. Under this scheme, the central government has provided Rs. 252 crore has been approved and the share of Central Government and State Government is 60:40 respectively.
A meeting of the high-power committee formed to implement the Mumbai City Women Safety Initiative Scheme (Nirbhaya) was held in Mumbai. It was held on 05.01.2022 under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Home). The High Power Committee in its meeting held on 05.01.2022 has approved the release of funds to the Mumbai CCTV Surveillance Project under the Surveillance Components in the framework of the Mumbai City Women Safety Initiative Scheme (Nirbhaya). A proposal for sanctioning and spending of funds amounting to Rs. 38,65,54, 447/- (Rupees Thirty Eight Crores Fifty Five Lakhs Fifty Four Thousand Four Hundred and Forty Seven only) is under consideration of the Government.
