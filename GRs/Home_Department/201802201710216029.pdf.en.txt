The Proposal:
The Union Home Ministry has prepared a National Policy and Action Plan to counter Left Wing Extremism. The Union Home Ministry has announced Special Central Assistance for Naxal affected districts of the country for three years from 2017-18 to 2019-20. The Union Home Ministry has issued instructions to implement the scheme in Gadchiroli district of Maharashtra. The main objective of this scheme is to bridge the gap between public infrastructure and services. Committees have been formed at district and taluk levels. Accordingly, the state government had constituted a district-level committee. :
Government Decision: To implement the Special Central Assistance Scheme announced for Naxal affected districts, the following State level and District level Committees are being constituted.
(a) The State Committee:
Additional Chief Secretary (Home), Home Department. The Chairman A.M.S./ Secretary (Forest), Revenue and Forest Department. The Central Government will provide funds to the State every year for three years from 2017-18 to 2019-20 and the various schemes to be taken out of this fund will be submitted to the State Level Committee for approval after approval by the District Level Committee.
