The Proposal:
The Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai has registered a case vide G.R. No. 14/2018. The Department of Law and Justice has approved the appointment of Mr. Pradeep Gharat as Special Public Prosecutor to effectively argue on behalf of the Government during the High Court, Mumbai hearings.
The matter of payment of professional fee to Mr. Pradeep Gharat, Special Public Prosecutor appointed by the Government to look into the judicial proceedings in the said case in the High Court, Bombay, was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In the case of Justice arising out of G.R. No. 14/2018 filed with the Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai, Shri. The Government have approved the payment of professional fees to Mr. Pradeep Gharat, Special Public Prosecutor appointed by the Government to look after the court proceedings in the High Court, Bombay, for effective hearing on a daily basis only. Apart from this professional fee, no other benefit will be admissible to Mr. Pradeep Gharat. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.
In the case of Justice arising out of G.R. No. 14/2018 filed with the Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai, Shri. The professional fee for viewing the court proceedings in the High Court, Mumbai on behalf of the Government will be as follows.
Effective hearing fee per day- Rs.20,000/- (Rs. Twenty thousand only) 2) Discussion fee (per sitting) 2,000/- (Rs. only two thousand.)
