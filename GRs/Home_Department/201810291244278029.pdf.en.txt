Preamble: The Central Level Committee for Infrastructure and other facilities for Gadchiroli district, which is severely affected by Left Wing Extremism, was considering the proposal of the Government to approve a new sub-document under the head 4055-Police for budgeting the funds made available by the Central Government under the scheme" Special Central Assistance Scheme (100% Centrally Sponsored)."
Government's decision:
The Accountant General, Mumbai and the Finance Department have approved the creation of new sub-headings and statistics under the head 4055-Police to budget the funds made available by the Central Government for infrastructure and other facilities for the Naxal-affected Gadchiroli district. Accordingly, the Government has approved the following new sub-headings for the Special Central Assistance Scheme for Infrastructure and Other Facilities for Naxalite Affected Districts with the computer code of the Scheme as 40550279:
Demand No.B-10
4055 Capital outlay on Police
