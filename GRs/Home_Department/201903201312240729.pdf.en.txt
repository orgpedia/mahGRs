Adv. for dealing on behalf of the Government in the court cases arising out of Manpada Police Station, Thane City vide G.R. No- 353/2017. Shri Shishir Hiray has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor vide notification dated 19.01.2018 in the aforesaid context of Department of Law& Justice. Therefore, in the present case, the Government is approving the payment of fees to Advocate Shishir Hiray as follows.
Effective hearing fee per day Rs.15,000/- (b) Professional consultation fee per day Rs.5,000/- (for first 1 to 5 hours)
(a) Actual travel and accommodation expenses (b) Police protection, if required.
In the present case, the ineffectual hearing fee or any other fee or allowance shall not be admissible to Advocate Shishir Hiray. The effective date of hearing shall not be less than the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case.
