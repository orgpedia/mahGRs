The Proposal:
, Akola. In the court case arising out of R. No. 80/ 2020, Md. The matter of payment of professional fees to Mr. Ujjwal Nikam, Special Public Prosecutor, who was appointed by the Government to handle the court proceedings in the Sessions Court, Akot, was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
, Akola. 80/2020, Adv. The Government have decided to allow Mr. Ujjwal Nikam to pay professional fees as under:
the Court of Sessions, Akot
1) Daily Effective Hearing Fee- Rs. 50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only) 2) Discussion Fee (Per Capita) Rs. 15,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Thousand only) (Maximum limit per day is Rs. 50,000/- (per day) Rs. Rs.5000/- (Rupees Five Thousand only) Travel Cost
Apart from this professional fee, no other benefit will be admissible to Mr. Ujjwal Nikam. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.
2. The payment of admissible professional fees to Mr. Ujjwal Nikam should be made after the disposal of the said Adjudicatory Case. Effective days of hearing of court proceedings shall be decided by the officer not below the rank of Superintending Investigating Officer/ Deputy Superintendent of Police of the concerned case as well as by the concerned officer.
The Deputy Superintendent of Police concerned shall ensure that the same is certified by the Manager of the Court and no additional payment is made to Adv. Ujjwal Nikam.
