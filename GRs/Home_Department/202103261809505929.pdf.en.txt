Government Resolution, Home Department No: CTE-2819/ PR No.112/ Pol-3
Now, the Government is approving extension of the above mentioned 100 newly created temporary posts mentioned in the above table at the establishment of 1) Nender and 2) Yelachil for Armed Police Support Centre at Surjagad from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 subject to the following conditions:
1) The remaining posts should be extended by excluding vacancies for more than six months. Also, the terms and conditions under which the posts have been sanctioned should be followed.
2) Government Decision, Finance Department No. ASANK-1001/ Q.No. 29/2001/ FINANCIAL REFORM-1, dated 10th September, 2001 and No. A proposal should be submitted to the Government immediately to complete the process of review of the posts as per Assn.1004/ Q.No. 12/2004/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 19th August, 2004 and final approval of the revised pattern of the office by the High Level Committee of Secretaries.
3) While reviewing the sanctioned posts in the establishment, the increasing use of technology in the functioning of the government, changes in the manpower of the government over time and the need for effective use of manpower should be considered.
4) During this period, in any case, the review of the posts will be done in front of the high-level committee and approval will be taken by the Finance Department under reference No. 4 is hereby notified vide Government Resolution dated 15.02.2021. In view of this, if the process of review of the posts is not completed by the end of 31.08.2021, if the above temporary posts are not extended for that further period and if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officers/ employees, then the responsibility of the concerned office will remain.
The expenditure should be spent under the head" Demand No. B-1, 2055-Police, (00) 109 District Police (00) (01) District Police Force (20550168)" and it should be met from the sanctioned grant for the financial year 2021-22.
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred under Finance Division No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC dated 15.02.2021.
