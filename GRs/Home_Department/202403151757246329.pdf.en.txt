Preamble: - The State Government has adopted a policy of providing financial participation of 40% to 50% in select projects so that railway projects in rural especially underdeveloped areas of the State can be expedited and completed at a faster pace.
The work on the new railway project Jalna-Khamgaon has been included under the Umbrella Scheme of the Central Government subject to necessary approvals in the budget of the year 2021-22. Tourist attractions along this railway line include Lonar Lake (Buldhana district), Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj Temple (Shegaon), Rajmata Jijau Janmasthan (Sindkhedaraja), Renuka Devi Temple (Chikhali), Guru Ganesh Tapodham (Jain Temple, Jalna). The Railway Department has requested Maharashtra to contribute 50 per cent for the railway project stating that the railway line will meet the continuous demand of the people of the region from all over the country and will lead to sustainable development of Marathwada as well as the rural areas of West Vidarbha. 26/12/2023 is done under the letter of the day.
The Detailed Project Report of the new broad gauge railway project Jalna-Khamgaon has been prepared as per which the estimated cost / cost (including land acquisition) of the project is Rs. 4907.70 - Crores as quoted by Central Railway in their reference No. 2 barrels. The letter dated 30/01/2024 has been communicated to the state government.
