31.01.2015 Preamble: A Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary was constituted under Issue No. 2 (i) of the General Administration Department, Government Resolution dated 14.10.2011 regarding reinstatement of a Government servant in Government service after his suspension. In lieu of the said Committee, the decision of the Government, General Administration Department vide Issue No. 7 dated 31.01.2015, has been issued by the concerned Administrative Department's ADM/ PR. To review the suspension of Gazetted Group-A and Group-B officers under the administrative department headed by the Secretary.
Instructions have been given to form a suspension review committee at the following level. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a committee to review the suspension of Group-A and Group-B officers on the establishment of the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratories, Mumbai.
The Decision:
Accordingly, the following committee will be constituted to review the suspension of Group-A and Group-B officers on the establishment of the Directorate of Judicial Assistant Scientific Laboratory, Mumbai.
