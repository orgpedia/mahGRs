The Proposal:
In Baramati taluka of Pune district, due to political, educational, industrial and other reasons, a large number of necessary arrangements for maintaining law and order have to be sought from other places. If the bandobast is extended to the southern border of Pune district, it takes time for the bandobast to reach, during which if something untoward happens, it adversely affects the available manpower. For this, Rs. The creation of the Deputy Commissioner's office in Baramati will ensure that the situation is handled properly by providing manpower quickly to maintain law and order and will save unnecessary time for the arrival of the police. Baramati District- Proposal for establishment of Deputy Superintendent of Police at Pune Reference No. According to the letter submitted by the office of the Director General of Police, the proposal to establish a Deputy Superintendent of Police in Mauje-Bhapur, Taluka-Baramati, District-Pune was under the consideration of the government, following decisions have been taken:
Government's decision:
In view of the law and order situation, it is being approved as a special case to create Deputy Commissioner of Police at Mauje-Bhapur, Taluka-Baramati, District-Pune.
Orders regarding the creation of the necessary post for the Deputy Commissioner of Police in Mauje-Bhapur will be issued separately.
3. Notification declaring the area and limits of the Deputy Commissioner of Police, Mauje-Bhapur shall be issued in due course.
4. Non-recurring expenditure on the administrative building and other ancillary expenses for the Deputy Commissioner of Police, Mauje-Bhapur, to be met from the annual plan with the approval of the competent authority.
5. This Government Decision is based on the informal reference of Finance Department No. 274/20/ Expenditure-7, dated 25.08.2020 is being issued as per the approval given.
