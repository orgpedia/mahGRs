Preface: Reference No. Two separate Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district under a government notification here. For these Tribunals, Reference No. 2 A total of 24 temporary posts have been created by the government decision here. The 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the said Tribunal have been notified vide Reference No. 3 The date was extended from 01.09.2025 to 28.02.2026 by a government decision here. As these 24 posts are expiring on the extension date 28.02.2026 as well as Reference No. The extension of the said posts was under consideration of the Government as vide letter dated 4 05.03.2026, the Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Nagpur submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of these 24 temporary posts.
Government Decision: - 24 temporary posts at the establishment of the office of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Nagpur are being extended from 01.03.2026 to 31.08.2026. The extension is being granted subject to the condition that the claims pending with the Tribunal are disposed of expeditiously and the statement of claims settled is submitted to the Government after every three months.
