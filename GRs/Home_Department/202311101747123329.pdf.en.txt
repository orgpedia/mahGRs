The Proposal:
The village of Shetbandar (Gharapuri) is located on the island of Elephanta. The island is located 9 nautical miles from Mumbai. As there is water everywhere around this small island, it can only be reached by waterways. About 10 lakh tourists visit the temple annually.
Elephanta Caves is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The embankment near the jetty has been damaged and broken at several places. The bridge needs to be repaired and widened for the safety of tourists. Repair and widening of existing embankment at Uran, G. Raigad, Rs. The proposal for administrative approval of the budget amounting to Rs. 38,67,27, 877/- was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision Repair and widening of existing embankment at Uran, G. Raigad, Rs. 38,67,27, 877/- (Rupees Thirty Eight Crores Sixty Seven Lakhs Twenty Seven Thousand Eight Hundred and Seventy Seven only) is getting administrative approval subject to fulfilment of the following conditions.
1.This work should be approved by the Board in the meeting of Maharashtra Maritime Board.
(2) All necessary permissions of the Central Government and the State Government should be obtained for this work.
