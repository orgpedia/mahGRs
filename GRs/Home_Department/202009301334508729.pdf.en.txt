31/08/2020 Government Decision The Government has decided to create the posts of Police Naik by increasing 15,000 posts out of the total sanctioned posts of Police Constables and Assistant Police Inspector by increasing 1500 posts out of the total sanctioned posts of Police Sub-Inspectors and accordingly these posts have been created with effect from 01/11/1992.
These posts have been decided by the Government, Home Department No. Police-3118/ PR No.109/ Pol-5A, dated 25/02/2020 was extended from 01/03/2020 to 31/08/2020.
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide the above Reference No. As per the letter, a proposal has been submitted for extension of the said temporary posts beyond 1/09/ 2020. The Government approves extension of the said posts for the period from 01/9/2020 to 28/02/2021 subject to the following conditions:
(1) None of the above posts has been vacant for more than 6 months and the terms and conditions for which the posts have been sanctioned have been complied with.
(2) Government Resolution, Finance Division, No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ A.P.C.
The terms and conditions laid down as on 31/8/2020 should be scrupulously followed and acted upon accordingly. Similarly, immediate action should be taken to get the revised format of the office approved by the high-level committee.
