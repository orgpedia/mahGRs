The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in the government service for the disabled. The central government has given. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of the said Government decision, the Government was considering the matter of ensuring the posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre for the disabled category on the establishment of the Director, Directorate of Civil Defence under the Home Department.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions that the posts in Group A to Group D, which have been ensured for the disabled by the Central Government, will be ensured for the disabled, even if the designations of the posts in the list published by the Central Government are different from the posts in the establishment under the State Government, the duties and responsibilities of the posts, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the service under the State Government. As per Government Decision dated 02.02.2021.
