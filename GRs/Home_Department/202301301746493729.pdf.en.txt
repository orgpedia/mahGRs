The Proposal:
The Union Cabinet on 25.3.2015 approved the institutional framework for conceptualization and implementation of Sagarmala Programme. Under the Sagarmala project, port-related infrastructure will be developed to harness the country's coastline for economic development. Maharashtra has a coastline of 720 km, which if developed to its full potential, will greatly benefit the overall economic and social development of the state. Therefore, it is in the best interest of the state to implement the Sagarmala programme. Accordingly, a State Sagarmala Committee comprising of representatives of all departments concerned for coordination of Sagarmala programme, policy guidance and prioritization of projects etc. at the State Government level has been constituted. The Secretary, Ministry of Shipping, Government of India had suggested that it should be constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister. Accordingly, vide Government Resolution No.PDE 1114/ PR No. 170/ Ports-1 dated 15.10.2015, the Government has decided to extend the validity of M/ s. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister.
A new committee is now being considered.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Now, as per the Government Decision dated 15.10.2015, Shri. The government has decided to constitute a new committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister.
