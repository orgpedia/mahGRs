The Adjudicating Tribunal, Pune vide letter No. GO No. 123/2018, dated 09.03.2018 Preamble: There are a total of 13 posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune, out of which 9 are permanent posts and 4 are temporary posts. These 4 temporary posts are mentioned in Reference No. The period was extended from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018 as per the decision of the Government. The said 4 temporary posts have expired on 28.02.2018 and further extension of the said posts with effect from 01.03.2018 is being considered by the Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune vide Reference No. 5 has been requested by the government. The matter of extension of these posts beyond 01.03.2018 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following 4 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune are being extended from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018. The extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending before the Tribunal are disposed of expeditiously, the final returns of the settled claims for the month of May, 2018 and September, 2018 are submitted to the Government and if 50% of the pending claims are not found to be settled by the month of February, 2018, no further extension will be granted.
