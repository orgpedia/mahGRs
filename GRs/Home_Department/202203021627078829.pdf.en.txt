For the office and computerization of e-governance system in the office of the Transport Commissioner and 50 transport offices in the Motor Vehicle Department, the government is considering to approve the purchase of a total of 20 computer sets (All in One) with Intel i5/ AMD processor, b) 20 laptops with Intel i5/ AMD processor, c) 160 desktops with Inteli5/ AMD processor, d) 30 Laser MFM Printer+ Scanner, etc.
Government's decision:
For the office work and computerization of the e-Governance system in the Office of the Transport Commissioner and 50 Transport Offices in the Motor Vehicle Department, the following details will be provided for the purchase of computer sets and printers. Administrative and financial sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 2,13,50, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Thirteen Lakhs Fifty Thousand only).
