Government circular:
Road Safety Week is observed every year throughout the country to effectively prevent motor vehicle accidents and to promote and disseminate traffic rules among the citizens. Road Safety Week is being observed in the State. from January 11, 2020. 31st Road Safety Mission 2020 is being observed from 17 th January, 2020.
Under the Road Safety Campaign, various programs are organized for publicity, awareness and public awareness for road safety in collaboration with the Transport Department, Traffic Police Department, Health Department, Public Works Department, School Education Department and all other relevant organizations/ organizations.
3. The details of activities to be undertaken by all concerned departments under" Road Safety Mission" to create awareness about road safety as mentioned above are given in the attached prospectus.
The heads of the concerned departments shall ensure strict implementation of the said action plan/ guidelines.
District Road Safety Committees have been constituted for each district of the State. The committee should hold a meeting of the committee regarding the implementation/ planning of the Road Safety Campaign 2020 in the district and give directions to the concerned and make all-out efforts to make the campaign a success.
The Commissioner of Transport, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the Additional Director General of Police (Traffic), Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall act as coordinators for the implementation of this programme and submit an action taken report to the Government.
