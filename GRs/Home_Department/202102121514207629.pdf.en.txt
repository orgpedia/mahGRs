Read:- Government Resolution, Home Department, No. MIS-0820/ No. 102/ Raushu-3, Dy. 12.01.2021.
The Proposal:
Chandrapur district. Read here for a comprehensive discussion, study and recommendation to the government on the liquor ban implemented from 01.04.2015. The High Level Committee has been constituted under the Government decision dated 12.01.2021 and the Committee has fixed a time frame to submit its report to the Government within one month. The committee has been asked to complete its work and submit a report to the government. Request for extension up to 07.03.2021. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the time to submit the report to the said high-level committee.
Government's decision:
Chandrapur district. The High-Level Committee constituted to study and make recommendations to the Government on the comprehensive implementation of the prohibition of liquor from 01.04.2015 was asked to submit its report to the Government. The period is being extended up to 07.03.2021.
