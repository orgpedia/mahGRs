Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti 2021 Guidelines.
Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti is celebrated as the birth anniversary of the King of Maratha, Shivaji Maharaj.
The Maharashtra government has decided to celebrate his birth anniversary. Maharashtra on February 19.
It is celebrated. Consider the Covid-19 situation.
the year. Celebration of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti on 19th February, 2021 in a simple manner
A decision has been made. Following are the guidelines:
They are:
Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was born on February 19, 1630.
Therefore, many Shiva lovers go to Fort Shivneri or Fort/ Forts and pay obeisance on the date. On February 18, at 12 midnight, they also come together to celebrate Shivjayanti. However, this year, Shivjayanti is expected to be celebrated in a simple manner without large gatherings to prevent the spread of Covid-19.
Every year during the celebration of Shivjayanti, various programs are organized throughout Maharashtra. However, this year, there will be no performances of songs, plays, lectures, and other cultural programmes in public places.
Instead, arrangements should be made to provide cable network or online broadcast of the program.
Also, morning walks, bike rallies, processions should not be taken out in any way.
Instead, a wreath-laying ceremony is being organised at the statue or statue of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and permission is being given to celebrate Shivjayanti in the presence of only 10 people by following social distancing.

Health activities/ camps on the day of Shivjayanti (e.g. Organizing blood donation) should be given priority and through this awareness should be created about diseases like Corona, Malaria, Dengue, etc. and their preventive measures as well as cleanliness.
Special attention should be paid to the observance of social distancing and hygiene rules (masks, sanitizers, etc.) at the venue while organizing health-related activities.
To prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus, the rules prescribed by the Government Relief and Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department as well as the concerned Municipal Corporation, Police Administration, Local Administration should be strictly followed. Also, if any other instructions are published after this circular and in the period between the start of the actual festival, they should also be complied with.
