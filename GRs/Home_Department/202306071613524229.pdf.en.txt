The Proposal:
Ministry of Finance, Government of India 1 The Memorandum hereby directs to adopt improved procedures for effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds.
Accordingly, the Central Nodal Agency (CNA) and the Implementing Agency/ Sub Agency (SA) are required to be appointed for implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to open a separate Single Bank Account in the Authorized Bank for that Scheme. Read No. The Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India has issued guidelines for the Modernization of Prisons project. Also read No. The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) has designated National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), New Delhi as the Central Nodal Agency (CNA) for implementation of the project. Read No. As per the instructions given under Section 3, a proposal to appoint an Implementing Agency/ Sub Agency (SA) for the implementation of the Modernization of Prisons project is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) has appointed the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), New Delhi as the Central Nodal Agency for implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Project of Modernization of Prisons in the State.
