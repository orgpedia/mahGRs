Government's decision:
A total of 1482 posts created for the protection and security of various establishments and persons in the state have been included in the list of 10. In accordance with the Government Decision dated 29.02.2017. 01.10.2017 to D. The period has been extended up to 31.12.2017. The following temporary positions are now available. 01.01.2018 to D. The period is being extended up to 28.02.2018.
(i) 998 temporary posts of Security Subject as mentioned in Form A in the establishment of various Commissioners of Police/ Superintendents of Police under the control of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State.
(ii) 411 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, in respect of security matters mentioned in Form B.
ii) A total of 72 temporary posts, 4 each of Police personnel created in Form C, for the protection of 18 Sub-Treasury Offices started in the newly created Taluks in the State.
(iv) Special Inspector General of Police, Force One, State of Maharashtra.

(2) The extension is subject to the condition that none of the above posts remain vacant for a period of more than six months and the conditions for which the posts have been sanctioned have been complied with and the revised contours of the posts are finally approved by the High Level Committee.
3." In any case, the administrative departments may review the same in accordance with the provisions mentioned in the above Government Decision of the Finance Department. The revised contours are required to be finalised with the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries by 28.02.2018 (including the newly created posts).
Therefore, the necessary proposals were submitted to the government. It should be noted that it will be submitted by 31.01.2018. If this is not done, it should be noted that if the question of salary of these temporary posts arises due to the expiration of the deadline, the entire responsibility will be of the Office of the Director General of Police.
4. The expenditure on salaries and allowances of the posts concerned shall be met from the approved budgetary provision by putting it under the head of accounts under which the expenditure is incurred.
This Government Order is being issued under the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Order No. 2016/ No. 08/ APUC dated 08.01.2018.
