Preamble: The Department has decided to set up a Cyber Cell at Mumbai at the State level to bring about more effective coordination between the State Government and the Police in the context of cybercrimes and to permanently coordinate the detection of cybercrimes. This was approved by the Government in its decision dated 05.01.2016. Also, the scope/ contours of the Cyber Cell were clarified under the said Government decision. However, the decision was taken by the Home Ministry. 26.08.2021, the Cyber Cell under Special Inspector General of Police, Cyber, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has been transferred to Additional Director General of Police, Economic Offences Wing, Mumbai under the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. To reduce the level of decision-making and to speed up the decision-making process, the government was considering bringing the cyber cell in Mumbai under the control of the Additional Chief Secretary, Home Department, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai.
Government Decision: The Government is approving to bring the Cyber Cell under the Special Inspector General of Police, Cyber, Maharashtra State, Mumbai under the Additional Chief Secretary, Home Department from the date of this order.
The number of the department The Government Decision dated 26.08.2021 is hereby superseded.
