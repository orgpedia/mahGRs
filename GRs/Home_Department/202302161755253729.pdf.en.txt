The Proposal:
The Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has requested the Government through a letter under reference to provide funds for payment of salaries of employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of December, 2022 and January, 2023.
Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds for the salaries of the employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of December, 2022 and January, 2023 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) has released Rs. An amount of Rs. 223.00 crore (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Three Crores only) is being approved to be provided in cash.
2) Rs. 223.00 crore is to be met from the provisions available in the financial year 2022-23 and the expenditure is to be incurred under the head" Demand No.B-3, Accounts Head 2041, Tax on Vehicles (00) 001, Operations and Administration (01) Transport Commissioner (01) (01) Establishment- Transport Commissioner (Adaptation) (Mandatory) (2041 0018) 33, Financial Assistance."
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to collect and disburse the amount to be paid to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation by the Accountant in his office.
4. This Government decision is based on the informal reference of the Finance Department No. 83/2023/ Expenditure-8, Dy. being issued with the consent received as on 16.02.2023.
