Introduction: The State Excise Department is an important department that generates a large amount of revenue for the state. The officers/ employees of the State Excise Department are engaged in a large number of activities under the Maharashtra Liquor Prohibition Act, 1949 and the NDPS Act, 1985 in order to increase the revenue of the government. Considering their achievements,/ courage/ courage/ proper investigation etc. in connection with these activities, the matter of awarding medals in the State Excise Department on the lines of the Police Department was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
In order to increase the revenue of the Government, the Government is approving to award the following medals and insignia to the officers/ employees working in the State Excise Department in accordance with their meritorious service/ achievement/ courage/ courage/ due investigation etc. taking into account the activities being carried out under the Maharashtra Prohibition of Liquor Act, 1949 and NDPS Act, 1985.
