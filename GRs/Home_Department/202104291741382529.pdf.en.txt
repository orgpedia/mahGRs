Preamble: General Administration Department. The Maharashtra Civil Services (Requirement of Knowledge of Computer Handling/ Usage) Rules, 1999 have been notified under 25.01.1999.
General Administration Department, Notification Dt. Regulation D dated 03.05.2000. Effective from 01.01.2001. As per the provisions of the said rules, the officers/ employees working in Group-A, Group-B and Group-C cadre posts in various departments/ offices of the government are required to submit a certificate of knowledge of computer handling/ use within the prescribed period.
The educational qualification for the post of Jail peon was 5th pass till 2005. The Home Department has notified. The said educational qualification has been revised as on 20.01.2005 and it has been changed to Higher Secondary School Certificate Examination. As a result, the employees of the Prison Sepoy Cadre who were appointed before 2005 could not appear for the Computer Eligibility Test conducted by the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education. Therefore, such employees could not submit the certificate of passing the computer qualification examination.
The employees who were appointed to the post of Jail Sepoy before 2005 have been promoted to the post of Havildar, Subedar and some of the employees have retired voluntarily or at the prescribed age and some have died. Finance Department, Govt. the State Government as on 31.01.2019

7th Pay Commission for employees. It has come into effect from 01.01.2016. However, due to the absence of computer qualification certificate, the employees of the posts of jail peon, constable and subedar are facing difficulties in paying the revised pension. Therefore, the matter of exempting the employees who were appointed before 2005 in the post of jail peon, constable and subedar from passing computer qualification was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The following 606 employees who were appointed to the post of Jail peon, Havildar and Subedar before 2005 on the establishment of the Prison Department under the Home Department are exempted from submitting the Computer Qualification Certificate as per Rule 6 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Requirement of Knowledge of Computer Handling) Rules, 1999.
