Dividing Daund subdivision under the purview of the Superintendent of Police, Pune Rural, the creation of a new Shirur Sub-Divisional Police Officer Office and the creation of a post for the said office has been proposed in the above context. The decision has been taken 1.
There are two divisional offices under the jurisdiction of the Superintendent of Police, Pune Rural, namely, 1) Additional Superintendent of Police Pune Division and 2) Additional Superintendent of Police Baramati Division. The two Divisional Offices under the Additional Superintendent of Police, Pune Division include the offices of 1) Haveli Sub-Division, 2) Lonavala Sub-Division, 3) Khed Sub-Division, and 4) Junnar Sub-Division. Also, under the jurisdiction of the Additional Superintendent of Police Baramati Division, the offices of 1) Bhor Sub-Division, 2) Daund Sub-Division, 3) Baramati Sub-Division and 4) Shirur Sub-Division are included. Also, there are various police stations under each of these sub-divisional offices.
Additional Director General of Police (Law and Order) & c.), POMANS OFFICE, Mumbai vide the above Reference No. The proposal to include Shirur Sub-Divisional Office under the jurisdiction of Additional Superintendent of Police, Baramati Division and under it, Shikrapur Police Station, Ranjangaon Police Station, Shirur Police Station, along with Sub-Divisional Office, Additional Superintendent of Police, Pune Rural, has been submitted for approval. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
The Shirur Sub-Divisional Office under the Additional Superintendent of Police, Baramati Division, and the Shikrapur Police Station, Ranjangaon Police Station, Shirur Police Station under it, are being approved to be included in the jurisdiction of the Additional Superintendent of Police, Pune Rural.
