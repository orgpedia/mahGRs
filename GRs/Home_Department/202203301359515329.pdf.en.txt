Introduction: The CCTNS project is being implemented in the state under the e-governance scheme of the Central Government. Under this project, in 2015, 1054 police stations and 629 senior police offices in the state were connected with CAS (Core Application Software) software and 2 Mbps network connectivity has been provided in these offices. Under the CCTNS project, 2 Mbps network connectivity has been provided to 1054 police stations and 629 senior offices.
The project was funded by the Centre, but it will not receive funds from the Centre after March 2018. The project cost is borne by the state government for its maintenance and repair.
