The Proposal:
The Vice-Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has issued a notification regarding the release of funds to Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) for reimbursement of concessional rates for the month of July, 2023. 3 has been requested by the government. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds to Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for reimbursement of concessional rates for the month of July, 2023 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In the year 2023-24, out of the provision made under the head" 2041 0018-33 Financial Assistance" of the Home (Transport) Department, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation has been provided Rs. A sum of Rs. 353.32 crore (Rupees Three Hundred and Fifty Three Crores Thirty Two Lakhs only) is being sanctioned as cash assistance.
2) Rs. 353.32 crore is to be met from the provisions available in the financial year 2023-24 and the expenditure is to be spent under the head" Demand No.B-3, Accounts Head 2041, Tax on Vehicles (00) 001, Operation and Administration (01) Transport Commissioner (01) (01) Establishment- Transport Commissioner (Adaptation) (Mandatory) (2041 0018) 33- Financial Assistance."
