GOVERNMENT ORDER: In pursuance of the Government Resolution, Home Department No. SPP-2295/ PR No. 149/ POL-10, dated 20th May, 1997, the posts of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group A have been created in various establishments under the Director, Directorate of Prosecutions, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai. Further, 299 new posts have been created vide Government Resolution, Home Department No.DPP-2012/ PR No.112/ Poll-10, dated 28.05.2013. These posts are to be filled directly by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
Accordingly, out of the 174 candidates recommended for these posts by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission under the aforesaid letters dated 22.09.2016 and dated 28.09.2016, 3 candidates are being appointed as substitutes to the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group-A under probation as per the provisions of the Revenue Department Allotment Rules, 2015 and Second Amendment Act, 2017 for appointment to the posts of Group A and Group B (Gazetted and Non-Gazetted) of the Government of Maharashtra by direct recruitment and promotion.

Government Order No: DPP-0916/ PR No. 243/ Pol-10, 2. The above candidates shall be appointed subject to the following terms and conditions.
(1) The candidate concerned shall be required to report at the place of posting within 30 days from the date of receipt of the order of posting, failing which he shall be liable to be dismissed from service.
(2) The probation period shall be 2 years (including the period of training) from the date of his joining the post of appointment.
(3) They must complete the training prescribed for this post.
(4) It shall be mandatory for the Assistant Public Prosecutor under the said purview to submit to the Head of the Department the necessary certificate of Government approval regarding the handling/ use of computer under the Government Decision, General Administration Department, No-Training 2000/ PR No. 61/2001/39, dated 19.03.2003 within two years from the date of appointment. Otherwise, their services will be terminated.
(5) Notwithstanding the appointment of the said candidates under this Order, their service excellence shall be as per the merit number decided by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
(6) The candidates will not be entitled to travel allowance for the travel required to join the appointment.
(7) The candidates shall be appointed in the cadre of" Assistant Public Prosecutor" on their satisfactory completion of the probation period of two years or the probation period thereafter extended in accordance with the rules prescribed by the Government.
(8) Candidates will be paid Rs. The salary will be in the range of Rs 15600- 39100+ Grade Pay Rs 5400. The said pay scale will be subject to the final decision of the High Court in Writ Petition No. 8474/2010. The first annual increment will be given during the probation period and subsequent increments will be given after satisfactory completion of probation period as per Government Circular, General Administration Department, No. SRV-1370/ D, dated 12.04.1972. Dearness Allowance and other allowances will be admissible to them as per rules.
