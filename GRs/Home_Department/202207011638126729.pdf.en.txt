As per the instructions in the letter dated 13.04.2022, the matter of appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Security Related Expenditure (SRE), Special Infrastructure Scheme (SIS) and Special Central Assistance (SCA) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Umbrella Scheme of 3194-Modernisation of Police Forces under Home (Police) Department, the Joint/ Deputy Secretary at Serial Number (6) is being appointed as the Single Nodal Agency for the schemes at Table (2) and (3).
