Mr. Sanjay Barve, Bhapose (MH: 1987) was referred to as No. 1 He has been appointed as Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai under Government Order dated 28.02.2019.
Now, reference no. 2 In pursuance of the above order, the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi, in the public interest, hereby relaxes the provision of Rule 16 (1) of the All India Services (Death and Service Allowance) Rules, 1958, to appoint Shri Shri Sanjay Barve, Bhapose, as Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, with effect from the date of his superannuation, i.e. The period has been extended for the next three months beyond 31/08/2019. Accordingly, Shri Shri Sanjay Barve, Bhapose has been posted as Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, with effect from the date of his superannuation i.e. The period is being extended for next three months beyond 31/08/2019.
