Introduction: The above reference No. Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district for a period of two years. Reference No. A total of 12 new posts have been created under the government decision. Reference No. 3 The Government hereby extended the tenure of 2 additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals and 12 temporary posts from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. Since the extension of the said 12 posts has expired as on 31.08.2020 and as per Reference No. 4 In a letter dated 11.08.2020, the Chairman, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of tenure of two additional Tribunals and a total of 12 temporary posts in them.
Government Decision: Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals set up at Nagpur and 12 temporary posts created for the same are being extended from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. The extension is for expeditious disposal of claims pending before the Tribunal, submission of final returns of claims settled for the month of November, 2020 and February, 2021 to the Government and 50% of the pending claims settled at the end of August, 2020.
