Government Order:
The services of Mr. Nitin Waychal, Superintendent, District Jail Class-I, Additional Superintendent, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail Class-I, in the Prison Department under the Home Department have been transferred from Ratnagiri Special Jail to Mumbai Central Jail by an order of the Government of Maharashtra for administrative reasons. A meeting of the Civil Services Board was held in Gathit to recommend to the competent authority regarding the transfer of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers. It was held on 18.05.2023. In pursuance of the recommendations made by the Civil Services Board in this meeting, the services of Shri Nitin Waychal, Superintendent, Mumbai Central Jail are being transferred to Ratnagiri Special Jail with immediate effect.
The order comes into force with immediate effect and the said officer should join the Ratnagiri Special Jail immediately and submit the report of joining to the government without fail.
