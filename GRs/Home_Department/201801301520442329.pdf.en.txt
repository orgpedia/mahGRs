The Proposal:
The Supreme Court in Writ Petition No. 295/2015 to review the road safety issues from time to time. A Road Safety Committee has been constituted to submit a report to the Supreme Court. The committee has directed to create a separate road safety fund for road safety activities. Accordingly, for approval of various projects related to road safety, under reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 01.09.2016, a" Road Safety Fund Control Committee" has been set up.
