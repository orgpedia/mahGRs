Preamble: Mr. Vasant Samadhan Bholankar, Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A' has been appointed in Government Service as a Maharashtra Public Service Commission sponsored candidate for the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group' A' on the establishment of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai under the Home Department vide Government Order dated 29.09.2017. Mr. Vasant Samadhan Bholankar, Assistant Public Prosecutor has requested to be relieved from the Government service by giving prior intimation regarding acceptance of resignation under his application dated 28.03.2018 due to his personal family problems and as he has to appear in the examination for the post of Additional District Judge.
The Director, Directorate of Prosecutions, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has certified that no departmental enquiry proceedings were initiated or proposed before the retirement of Mr. Vasant Samadhan Bholankar, Assistant Public Prosecutor and that there was no communication from him to the Government. The Government was considering to relieve Mr. Vasant Samadhan Bholankar, Assistant Public Prosecutor, from Government service by accepting his resignation from the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group-A on the establishment of Director, Directorate of Prosecutions. Orders have been issued by the government in this regard.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: DPP-0518/ PR No.165/ POL-10, GOVERNMENT ORDER
The resignation submitted by Mr. Vasant Samadhan Bholankar, Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group A, Nashik vide his application dated 28th March, 2018 as Assistant Public Prosecutor, Group A, vide letter dated 13th April, 2018 by the Director, Directorate of Complaints, Maharashtra State, Mumbai under Reference No.2 is being accepted and he is being relieved from Government service with effect from 30th April, 2018 (Mid-Day).
