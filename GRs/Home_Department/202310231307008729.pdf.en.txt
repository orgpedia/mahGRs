The Proposal:
As per the approval of the Cabinet, Reference No. 1 By the Government decision mentioned herein, it has been approved in principle to create a new Regional Office at Nashik by bifurcating the Regional Office of Central Division of Prisons, Aurangabad and incorporating the institutions of Nashik Road Central Jail, Dhule, Nandurbar, Jalgaon, Bhusawal, Ahmednagar District Jail, Visapur Open District Jail and Juvenile Correctional Home, Nashik. The post of Deputy Inspector General of Prisons, Pune has been transferred to a separate regional office at Nashik.
According to reference no. 2 As per the request made by the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 01.02.2019, the matter of declaring the office of the Deputy Inspector General of Prisons, Nashik Division, Nashik as the head of the office was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Deputy Inspector General of Prisons, Nashik Division, Nashik is being declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the newly created Regional Office of the Deputy Inspector General of Prisons, Nashik Division, Nashik under the Department of Prisons. He has also been appointed as the divisional head of Nashik division.
Approval is being given for transfer of the posts in the Annexure to the Regional Office of the Deputy Inspector General of Prisons, Nashik Division, Nashik.
