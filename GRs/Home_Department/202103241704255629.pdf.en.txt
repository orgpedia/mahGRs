Now, the Government is approving the extension of the newly created 335 temporary posts for the Police Stations Pardi, Wathoda and Kapilnagar mentioned in the above table on the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Nagpur City from March 01, 2021 to August 31, 2021 subject to the following conditions:
1) The remaining posts should be extended by excluding vacancies for more than six months. Also, the terms and conditions under which the posts have been sanctioned should be followed.
Government Resolution, Finance Division No. Asank-1001/ Q. No. 29/2001/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 10th September, 2001 and No. A proposal should be submitted to the Government immediately to complete the process of review of the posts as per Assn.1004/ Q.No. 12/2004/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 19th August, 2004 and final approval of the revised pattern of the office by the High Level Committee of Secretaries.
3) While reviewing the sanctioned posts in the establishment, the increasing use of technology in the functioning of the government, changes in the manpower of the government over time and the need for effective use of manpower should be considered.
4) During this period, in any case, the review of the posts will be done in front of the high-level committee and approval will be taken by the Finance Department under reference No. 6 notified vide Government Resolution dated 15.02.2021. In view of this, if the process of review of the posts is not completed by the end of 31.08.2021, if the above temporary posts are not extended for that further period and if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officers/ employees, then the responsibility of the concerned office will remain.
