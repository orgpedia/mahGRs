The Government has decided to create 15,000 posts of Police Naiks by upgrading the sanctioned posts of constables and 1500 posts of Assistant Police Inspectors by upgrading the sanctioned posts of Sub-Inspectors of Police and these posts have been created with effect from 01/11/1992. These posts have been decided by the Government, Home Department No. Police- 3118/ PS No.
109/ POL-5A dated 10/10/2019 was extended from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020.
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide the above Reference No. As per the letter, a proposal has been submitted to extend the said temporary posts beyond 1/03/2020. The Government hereby grants extension of tenure of the said posts for the period from 01/3/2020 to 31/08/2020 subject to the following conditions:
Conditions: 1) None of the above posts are vacant for more than 6 months and the terms and conditions for which the posts have been sanctioned have been complied with.
(2) Government Resolution, Finance Division, No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ A.P.C.
The terms and conditions laid down as on 12/2/2020 should be scrupulously followed and acted upon accordingly. Similarly, immediate action should be taken to get the revised format of the office approved by the high-level committee.
(3) If the process of review of posts is not completed within the stipulated time, the temporary posts will not be extended for the next period and if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officers/ employees, the responsibility will be of the concerned establishments.
4) The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall submit an appropriate proposal to the Government for approval of the High-Level Secretary Committee for review and revised review of the sanctioned post during this period. If not, it should be noted that if the question of salary of these temporary posts arises due to the expiry of the deadline, the Director General of Police will be responsible for it.
