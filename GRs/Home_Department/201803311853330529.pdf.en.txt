The proposal: An agreement was signed between Pawan Hans Helicopters Limited, Mumbai and Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai for hiring of helicopters on lease basis for two years from March 22, 2016 to March 21, 2018. Strengthening of police force in Naxal affected areas, transportation of injured persons and jawans, immediate medical treatment to injured jawans.
The proposal received under Reference No. 2 of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai regarding extension of the period for hiring of helicopters at the existing rates for a period of one year from 22.03.2018 to 21.03.2019 for providing assistance and food to the jawans, inspection of suspected areas etc. was under consideration of the Government.

For hiring helicopters for a subsequent period, the procedure should be carried out using the" open tender" method. This process needs to be completed by 31.12.2018.
