Preamble: The National Institute for Transforming India (NITI Aayog), Government of India, has issued instructions in a letter under reference to the formation of a State Level Task Force to formulate a mobility policy in the state by assessing public transport within the state, the impact of transport on the environment, etc.
The State Level Task Force set up as above is expected to study the intra-state traffic trends, their issues, etc., with the objective of improving urban mobility, public transport, impact of transport on environment and submit a report to NITI Aayog.
In view of the above and the guidelines issued by the NITI Aayog from time to time, a proposal to constitute a State Level Task Force comprising of the heads of the administrative departments and sectoral agencies under the Ministry was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
As per the guidelines issued by the National Institute for Transforming India (NITI Aayog), the NITI Aayog has directed to formulate a state mobility policy by assessing the impact of intra-state public transport, urban/ rural mobility, transportation on the environment, etc. Accordingly, a State Level Task Force is being constituted comprising of the heads of the administrative departments and sectoral agencies under the Ministry.
