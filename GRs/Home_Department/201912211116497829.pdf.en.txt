Government's decision:
The 70th Anniversary of the Indian Republic will be celebrated on 26th January 2020 at Shivaji Park, Dadar, Mumbai. In this program, a tableau will be taken out by the Mumbai Traffic Police on the subject of" Awareness about Helmet among the Public." However, the Government is sanctioning an amount of Rs. 15,93,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs Ninety Three Thousand only) for the conduct of this Chitraratha.
Joint Commissioner of Police (Traffic), Worli, Mumbai is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" to draw the said funds.
3. Expenditure on this matter Home Department Demand No. B-01, 2055 Police, 108 State Headquarters Police, (02) City Police, (02) (01), Establishments,- (20550097), 21 Material and Supplies.
4. The procedure laid down by the Government shall be followed while purchasing or hiring the goods and services required for the operation of the tableau. Also, complete details of the expenditure incurred for the tableau parade should be submitted to the government immediately.
