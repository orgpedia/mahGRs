Preamble: 1 (one) temporary post in the office of the State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai may be referred to as Reference No. 1 has been extended from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019. The term of the post of Constable 1 (I) is expiring on 28.02.2019. The Manager, State Transport Appellate Tribunal, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai has now issued a proposal regarding extension of 1 (one) temporary post of peon vide Reference No. 2 has been submitted to the Government as per the letter here and the matter was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
The 1 (one) temporary post of Manager, State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai as a peon in the establishment is being extended from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019. The extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending with the State Transport Appellate Tribunal are settled expeditiously and the statement of claims settled is submitted to the government after every three months.
