The proposal: The Supreme Court has dismissed the petition No. 295/2015 to review the road safety issues from time to time. A Road Safety Committee has been constituted to submit a report to the Supreme Court. The committee has directed to create a separate road safety fund for road safety activities. Accordingly, for approval of various projects related to road safety, under reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 01.09.2016, a" Road Safety Fund Control Committee" has been set up. For this purpose. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. The meeting was held on 22.01.2021. 1 here. dated 02.02.2021. The government was considering issuing a government resolution in accordance with the proposals approved in the meeting.
Government's decision:
As per the directions of the Supreme Court Committee on Road Safety, various proposals regarding road safety have been approved. A Road Safety Fund Control Committee has been set up under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. The said Committee has approved the proposal for expenditure of Rs. 18,09,42, 000/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores Nine Lakhs Forty Two Thousand only) attached with this Government Resolution.
