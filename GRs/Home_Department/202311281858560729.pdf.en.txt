The Proposal:
Mr. Balasaheb Nana Wakchaure, Police Constable No. 04.0215 Ghatkopar Police Station, Mumbai is in Government service since 1/6/2004. Mr. Wakchaure is drawing salary in the pay scale of S-8 (25,500-81, 100) in the rank of Constable. While working in Government service, Mr. Wakchaure suffered 87% disability due to nerve injury in an accident on 2/5/2013. On the request of Mr. Wakchaure, his resignation was approved by the office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai vide order dated 16/2/20015. Thereafter, the request made by Mr. Wakchaure vide his application dated 14/8/2015 for rejection of his resignation was rejected by the Office of the Commissioner of Police. Therefore, he filed an original application in the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai against the said order under Section 47 of the Persons with Disabilities Act, 1995. In this case, The resignation accepted by Mr. Wakchaure pursuant to the order passed by the Tribunal was cancelled by the Office of the Commissioner of Police vide order dated 18/7/2019. Also, a proposal has been submitted to the government to create a supernumerary post for Mr. Wakchaure as there is no equivalent post in the office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai. Accordingly, as per the provisions of Section 20 (4) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, in case of disability while in service, the employee shall be given posting in a suitable post in accordance with his disability and shall be given salary protection of the post holding origin. In case the post is not available, the employee will be given posting on the post by making additional number of postings till the availability of another suitable post or till the date of his retirement as per the prescribed age, whichever is earlier. In view of this provision, the Finance Department has approved the creation of one supernumerary post in the cadre of Police Constable for Mr. Balasaheb Nana Wakchaure, Police Constable for the period from 2/5/2013 to the date of retirement as per his prescribed age or for the period for which the post falls vacant due to other reasons, whichever is earlier.

The government's decision:
The Government have approved the creation of one more post in the cadre of Police Constable for drawing salary in the pay scale of S-8 (25,500-81, 100) of Mr. Balasaheb Nana Wakchaure, Police Constable No. 04.0215, Ghatkopar Police Station, Mumbai with effect from 02/05/2013.
2.The said post is being created on the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai.
Balasaheb Nana Wakchaure, Police Constable, is being created to provide all benefits including service protection, for the period from 02/05/2013 till the date of retirement of Mr. Wakchaure as per the prescribed age or for the period the post falls vacant due to other reasons, whichever is earlier. After that time, the position will be automatically terminated.
