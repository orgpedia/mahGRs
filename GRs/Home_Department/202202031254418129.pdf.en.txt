Government's decision:
General Administration Department Reference No. 1 here. A total of 57 officers/ employees of the establishment of the Additional Director General of Police and Director General of Police, Bintari Sandesh Vibhag, Maharashtra State, Pune under this Department were temporarily transferred to the above post for 11 months or till the date on which they would have retired from service, whichever is earlier, to re-employ the officers/ employees who were appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category under the Government decision dated 21.12.2019 but could not produce the caste validity certificate or who had submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes, pending court cases regarding the caste validity certificate. Since the period of 11 months of the officers/ employees who were appointed to the superannuation post had expired, the matter of extension of the superannuation and above appointments to such services was under consideration of the Government.

Dilip Madhukarrao Nimje, Personal Assistant (Group-B), Mr. Deepak Narhar Kedar, 6. P.
Anil Mohan Bawalekar, Police Constable (Machinery Operator) (Group-C) and Shri Ramesh Janu Salavkar, Police Constable (Electrical Engineer) (Group-C). With effect from 31.10.2021, a number of posts transferred to such services and above are being temporarily extended for a further period of 11 months or till the date on which such officers/ staff would have retired, if they had continued in service, or till the recommendations of the Study Group are received by the Government and a final decision is taken, whichever is earlier.
The expenditure on the salary of the superannuated officer/ employee shall be covered under the head of salary accounts of the department/ office in which the officer/ employee is presently working and shall be met from the grants of the relevant financial year.
