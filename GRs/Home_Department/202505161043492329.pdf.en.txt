Mumbai is the financial capital of the country and an important city of the state capital.
There is a need to strengthen the Mumbai city's intelligence system and continuously monitor the intelligence received. The city of Mumbai has a large number of vital installations, including a large number of Category A sites. The security arrangements at these sites need to be kept invulnerable and closely monitored. Mumbai city has a large number of high-profile individuals with high threat perception.
Also, the number of visits to the city of Mumbai by the President, Vice President, Prime Minister, Heads of State, Kings, etc. of India and other important Union Ministers of the country.
It's big. Their security needs to be strengthened and closely monitored during their travel and stay in and around Mumbai WEL during each such Mumbai visit. Accordingly, the issue of ensuring the post of "Joint Commissioner of Police, Intelligence" under the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai was under consideration of the government. In accordance with that
The government has taken the following decision.
The Government's Decision-
As an administrative urgency, the post of "Special Commissioner of Police" in the category of "Additional Director General of Police," currently lying vacant at the establishment of the USM Commissioner, Brihanmumbai, is "Special."
