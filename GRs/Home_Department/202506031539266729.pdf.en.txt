The Proposal: -
The government was considering opening a separate head of accounts for the Intelligent Traffic Management System (TMS) system being implemented on the Mumbai-Pune Expressway as well as the Intelligent Traffic Management System (ITMS) system / project being implemented on other important national highways in the state for payment of dues to the entrepreneur / project component during 0 and M (Operations and Maintenance) period.
The Government's Decisions:
The Intelligent Traffic Management System (TMS) system being implemented on the Mumbai-Pune Expressway as well as the Intelligent Traffic Management System (ITMS) system / project being implemented on other important national highways in the state are being approved to open new headings for payment of dues to the entrepreneur / project component during 0 and M (Operations and Maintenance) period as follows: -
