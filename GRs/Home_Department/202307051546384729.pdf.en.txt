The Proposal:
The Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has requested the Government through the reference letters to provide funds to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) for payment of salaries to its employees for the month of June, 2023, reimbursement of concessional rates for the month of May, 2023 to the State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) and to meet the shortfall in income and fuel, salary, maintenance and repair expenses for the month of May, 2023. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds for payment of salaries to the employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of June, 2023 and for reimbursement of concessional rates to the State Road Transport Corporation for the month of May, 2023.
Government's decision:
In the year 2023-24, from the provision made under the head" 2041 0018-33 Financial Assistance" of the Home (Transport) Department, an amount of Rs. 33.82 crore and Rs. 321.18 crore. 355.00 crore (Rupees Three Hundred and Fifty Five Crores only) is being sanctioned in cash.
