Introduction: The above reference No. Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district for a period of two years. Reference No. A total of 12 new posts have been created under the government decision. Reference No. 3 The Government hereby extended the tenure of 2 additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals and 12 temporary posts from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. Since the extension of these 12 posts has expired as on 31.0.2023 and as per Reference No. As the Chairman, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur, vide letter dated 07.09.2023, had submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of tenure of two additional Tribunals and a total of 12 temporary posts, the Government was considering extension of tenure of the said posts from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
Decision of the Government:- The two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals set up at Nagpur and the 12 temporary posts created for them are being extended from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024. The extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending before the Tribunal are disposed of expeditiously and the statement of claims disposed of is submitted to the Government after every three months.
