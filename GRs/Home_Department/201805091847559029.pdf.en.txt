The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the repair of 13 residential buildings in the police colony at Devlali police station under the jurisdiction of the Commissioner of Police, Nashik City.
The Sub-Divisional Engineer, Western Public Works Sub-Division, Nashik, has submitted the cost estimates prepared as per the State Rate List for the year 2017-18 for the said work. The said cost estimates have been certified by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Nashik.
3. Estimates of the said expenditure have been examined by the Public Works Department, Ministry and it is estimated to be Rs. 84,70,000/- as amended and agreed to grant administrative approval, subject to certain conditions.
Accordingly, for the repair of 13 residential buildings in the police colony at Devlali police station under the jurisdiction of the Commissioner of Police, Nashik City, Rs. Administrative approval is hereby accorded to the cost estimate of Rs. 84,70,000/- (1) The said cost estimate is required to be certified by the Commissioner of Police, Nashik City; 2) Work should not be started without availability of funds.
5. The expenditure for the said work is borne by the Home Department. B-2055, Police, (00) 109, District Police, (00) (01) District Police Force (Compulsory) (Dattamat) (2055 0168), 27, Small Constructions, to be covered from the grants available under this.
6. This memorandum was submitted to the Informal Reference No. 164/ EMA-2, dated 22.03.2018, is being issued with the concurrence received.
