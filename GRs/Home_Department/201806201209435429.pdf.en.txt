The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, has submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the following electrical works at Police Colony, Sir Pochkhanwala Marg, Worli:
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Job title (9)                                                         | Estimating Cost (2) |
| Providing L.E.D. street lights, cables for 89 buildings and 30 poles. | Rs. 21,96,323/      |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Deputy Engineer, Public Works, Electrical Sub-Division, Worli, Mumbai, prepared the rates for the year 2015-16 for the said work. Estimates have been submitted for an expenditure of Rs. 21,96,323/-. These cost estimates have been certified by the Executive Engineer, Central Mumbai (Public Works, Electricity Department, Mumbai).
3. Estimates of the said expenditure have been examined by the Public Works Department, Ministry and it is estimated to be Rs. 24,24,716/- has been amended and agreed to grant administrative approval, subject to certain conditions.
4. Accordingly, for providing LED street lights, cables for 89 buildings and 30 poles at Police Colony, Sir Pochkhanwala Marg, Worli, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the estimated cost of Rs. 24,24,716/- through the following conditions:
