Preamble: A Government Resolution dated 14.10.2022 has been issued regarding the provision of loans through banks for construction of houses for the officers/ officials of the police force in the State. In this decision, the government was considering the formation of a committee of representatives of various stake holders to streamline the implementation of the scheme and determine the modalities for proper implementation.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the Government Decision issued on 14.10.2022 regarding the provision of loans through banks for the construction of houses for the officers/ officials of the police force in the State, a committee is being constituted as follows to streamline the implementation and to determine the modalities for the proper implementation of the housing advance scheme.
