Preamble: The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 and Rule 8 (2) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979. 01.04.2010) against Shri Hiralal Jadhav, then Principal, Daulatrao Jadhav Training College, Yerawada, Pune. A Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Smt. N. Ambika, Deputy Commissioner of Police, Zone-4, Mumbai, as per the guidelines in the Vishakha Case," Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace and Prevention and Redressal of Complaints of Sexual Harassment Act, 2013" to inquire into the charges 1 to 5 of the charges framed under the Memorandum dated 06.12.2013. The Chairperson of the Committee, Mrs. N. Ambika, Deputy Commissioner of Police, Zone-4, Mumbai, has asked the Government to extend the time to submit the report of the Committee to the Government. The request has been made vide letter dated 18.04.2019. Accordingly, the government proposed to extend the period of the committee by three months.
Government Decision: Mr. Hiralal Jadhav, the then Principal, Daulatrao Jadhav Training College, Yerawada, Pune. As per the guidelines issued in the Vishakha case to inquire into charges 1 to 5 of the charges framed under the memorandum dated 06.12.2013, as per the request of the Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Smt. N. Ambika, Deputy Commissioner of Police, Zone-4, Mumbai, to inquire into the above matter and submit its inquiry report to the Government, as a special case, the Government has decided to constitute a Special Investigation Team (SIT) to inquire into the matter. The Government has approved the extension for a further period of three months with effect from 23.04.2019.
