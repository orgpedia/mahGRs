The Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India vide its letter No. 45020/34/2018-IPS.II dated 16.11.2018, has informed the Government that the officers of All India Police Services are required to upload the Annual Immovable Property Return showing the status as on 31.12.2018 for the year 2018 on the website of Ministry of Home Affairs by 31.01.2019 in accordance with the provisions of Rule 16 (2) of the All India Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1968. This notice is forwarded to all the officers of the Maharashtra State Police Force along with the information and necessary action.
