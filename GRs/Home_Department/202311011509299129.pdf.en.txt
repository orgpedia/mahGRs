the prelude
Considering the increasing number of prisoners in the future, a modern water heater system of 6000 liters capacity will be installed in Yerawada Central Jail so that hot water will be available for preparing food for the prisoners. It will also save a lot of LPG gas. For this purpose, Maharashtra Energy Development Agency (MEDA), Pune Division Office, Pune has sanctioned Rs. 9,63,108/- (Rupees Nine Lakhs Sixty Three Thousand One Hundred and Eight only) has been presented in the Budget. The proposal for administrative sanction of the said expenditure was made by the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra, Pune. The government has received 1. The proposal had received the government's approval.
Government's decision:
Rs. 10,000 crore for installation of Solar Thermal Water Plant (with cold water tank and cold and hot water piping) of 6000 litres per day capacity in Yerawada Central Jail. An amount of Rs. 9,63,108/- (Rupees Nine Lakhs Sixty Three Thousand One Hundred and Eight only) is being administratively sanctioned under this Government decision.
