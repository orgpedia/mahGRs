The Proposal:
The office of the Director General of Police, No. The letter of 1st has requested for the transfer of the posts of Reserve Police Inspector and Reserve Police Sub-Inspector in the Police Training Center under the Additional Director General of Police, Training and Special Teams, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the establishment of Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik. Following this, the government took decision on this request.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to transfer 5 posts of Reserve Police Inspector and 10 posts of Reserve Police Sub-Inspector in the Police Training Center mentioned in the following prospectus under the Additional Director General of Police, Training and Special Teams, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the establishment of Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik.
