The Proposal:
During the period from the notification of the Lok Sabha General Election to the completion of the election process, the police officers and staff deputed for the full-time physical election work had to carry out the sensitive, sensitive and stonewalling work of the election with utmost diligence and responsibility. Accordingly, a proposal for payment of remuneration/ honorarium to the police officers and staff deputed for full time physical election duty for the Lok Sabha Elections 2019 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the payment of basic pay as per the 7th Pay Commission for the month of April, 2019 to the attached Schedule A Police Officers and staff, taking into account the election workload, scope of work and working days etc. of the police officers and staff deputed for full-time physical election work during the period from the notification of the Lok Sabha General Elections 2019 in the State of Maharashtra to the completion of the election process. However, the police officers and staff who have done election duty for less than 30 days i.e. more than one month, will be paid remuneration for the number of days they have worked. Police officers and employees who have worked for more than 30 days in an election will be paid a maximum of one month's salary (basic salary). Such amount shall not be more than one month's salary (basic pay) of any police officer/ employee and the same shall be paid to an officer/ employee for the Lok Sabha General Elections-2019.
