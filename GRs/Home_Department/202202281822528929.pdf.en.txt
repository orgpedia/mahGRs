Further, detailed disciplinary proceedings have been initiated against Mr. Dhiraj Shankarrao Patil, Executive Director, Security and Enforcement, Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company, pursuant to the said matter vide memorandum dated 14/02/2022.
A complaint has been filed with the Anti-Corruption Bureau against Mr. Dhiraj Shankarrao Patil, Executive Director, Security and Enforcement, Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company. Also, in this regard. A complaint has also been made to the police.
Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, under Potnium 1 (i) of Rule 4, the said Mr. Dhiraj Shankarrao Patil, Executive Director, Security and Enforcement, Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company (then Additional Superintendent of Police, Satara) is hereby suspended from the Government service with effect from the date of this Government Order.
Accordingly, it is hereby ordered that during the period of suspension, Mr. Dhiraj Shankarrao Patil shall continue to be paid subsistence allowance and allowable dearness allowance thereon as per Rule 68 (I) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Assignment, Non-Acceptance of Service and Salary during Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981.
The Government further orders that till the suspension orders are in force, the office of the Director General, Home Guards, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall continue to be the headquarters of Shri Dhiraj Shankarrao Patil. They will not be allowed to leave the headquarters without prior permission of the Director General, Home Guards, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. If they leave the headquarters without prior permission of the Director General, Home Guards, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai, it will be their misconduct and for that reason they will be entitled to separate disciplinary action.
