The Proposal:
A case vide G.O.R.No.673/ 2020, Deccan Police Station, Pune City, has been registered against Mohd. As a Special Public Prosecutor to represent the Government in the High Court, Bombay, the aforesaid Reference No. 1 The Law and Justice Department here was under the consideration of the Government to provide professional fees to Mr. Pravin Chavan.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the court case arising out of offence No. 673/2020 registered at Deccan Police Station, Pune City, Shri. Advocate Mr. Pravin Chavan appointed as Special Public Prosecutor to represent the Government in the High Court, Mumbai is being approved to pay professional fees as follows. the High Court, Mumbai
(a) Daily Court Effective Hearing Fee- Rs. 50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only) B) Discussion Fee (per hour)- Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand only) (Maximum Rs. 30,000 per day) Accommodation- Recreation Hall, Police Gymkhana or Government Rest House.
(d) Travel by public transport.
Apart from this professional fee, no other benefit will be admissible to Advocate Mr. Praveen Chavan. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.
