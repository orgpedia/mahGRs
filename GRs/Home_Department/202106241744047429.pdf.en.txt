Preamble: Public Interest Litigation No. 28/2013 (Shrikant Karve Vs. the High Court of Bombay. On 18.02.2016, the Government had issued an order to set up Automated Testing Centres at various places. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to grant administrative approval for the establishment of Vehicle Inspection and Certification Centre for 13 Transport Offices of the Department of Motor Vehicles through Automotive Research Association of India, Pune, an organization recognized under Rule 126 of the Central Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989.
Government Decision: Automotive Research Association of India, Pune has decided to set up Vehicle Inspection and Testing Centre for 13 Transport Offices namely Regional Transport Offices Mumbai (West), Nanded, Latur, Nagpur (Rural) and Sub-Regional Transport Offices, Satara, Buldhana, Shrirampur, Washim, Akola, Parbhani, Solapur, Baramati and Ambejogai. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 151,86,25, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Fifty Five Crores Eighty Six Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand only).
