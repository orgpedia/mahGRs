As requested by the Commissioner of Transport, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter No. 3 above, the Government was considering administrative and financial sanction for the expenditure incurred for training of the sixth batch of 85 newly appointed Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors and 3 Assistant Regional Transport Officers of the Motor Vehicles Department at the Central Institute of Road Transport, Pune.
Government Decision: The Central Institute of Road Transport, Pune, has decided to pay Rs. Administrative and financial sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 1,57,08, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy Seven Lakh Eight Thousand only).
