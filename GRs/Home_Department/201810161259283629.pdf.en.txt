Government Decision: A total of 1168 posts under the control of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved. Further, as per the Government Decision No.D.P.-2012/ PR No. 112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway. Out of which, 125 temporary posts of Assistant Director and Public Prosecutor in the 5 District Offices of the Subsidiary Annexure-C under the control of the Deputy Director, Government Grievances, Amravati Range, Amravati have been transferred to the Home Department vide Government Order, Home Department No. 27.03.2018 has been extended upto 30.09.2018. Now, the Government hereby grants extension of 125 temporary posts in Annexure-C from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than six months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts where the government has already issued orders to abolish these posts.
