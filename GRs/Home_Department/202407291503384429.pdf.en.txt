The Proposal: -
Illegal billboards on Railway Police land in Ghatkopar, Mumbai dated
On May 13, 2024, 16 innocent people tragically died after falling on a nearby payroll pump.
The incident resulted in billboards in the city, especially on government, police, or railway properties.
Many questions have been raised about the safety and legality of billboards.
To conduct a time bound inquiry into the billboard accident at Ghatkopar, Mumbai, "Shri. Dilip
Bhosale, former Chief Justice of India. The High Court, Bombay, presided over on 10th June,
A committee of inquiry has been set up under the 2024 government decision.
A billboard collapses on a payroll pump in payroll pump premises in Ghatkopar, Mumbai.
To inquire into the factors responsible for the incident in the case and such incidents in the future.
The composition and functioning of the committee constituted to make recommendations for the matters of avoidance of recurrence.
The decision was under the consideration of the government.

The functions of the Inquiry Committee shall be as follows: -
(1) The Committee shall examine the event, its causes and the order of its consequences.
(The Committee shall examine the sequence of events/ causes and
consequences)
(2) The involvement of the company responsible for the billboard and payroll pump.
Conducting inspection, prior history of the company, tracking of financial transactions and various offices /
Checking for any evidence of collusion with agency officials.
