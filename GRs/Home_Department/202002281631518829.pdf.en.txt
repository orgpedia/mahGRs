Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for implementation of Student Police Cadet Programme under Modernization of Police Force for students of class 8th and 9th in 354 government schools in the State, the Central share of Rs. 1,77,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy Seven Lakhs only), out of the said amount, vide Reference No. 1 per school to the schools affiliated to Schedule A with the decision of the Government here Rs. Administrative approval has been given to make available Rs 50,000/-.
Now, reference no. 3 as per Request for Proposal No. As some private schools have been found in the schools selected under the government decision, the government is giving approval to include new government schools under Annexure-A instead of the existing private schools.
The Additional Director General of Police, Training and Special Squads, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall take action to disburse funds to the respective unit heads/ schools for spending on the training of the subjects mentioned in the guidelines of the Central Government to the new schools in the Annexure-A.
