As per the provisions of Rule 40 (E) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, the period of remission of service of Mrs. Rajeshwari Kori, Senior Administrative Officer/ Deputy Controller (Group-A) in the Military Service and Civil Service from 01/01/2009 to 24/06/2010 is 1 year 5 months 24 days subject to the following conditions:
(1) Any bonus or gratuity received in lieu of pension by Mrs. Rajeshwari Kori on or since her discharge from the service of the Army shall be returned in monthly instalments as the case may be, taking into account the period remaining from the date of this order till her retirement from service.
(2) The period of the clause between the military service and the civil service of Mrs. Rajeshwari Kori shall not be admissible as qualifying service.
(3) As on the date of this order, the amount of contribution and the interest thereon deposited by the Government in their accounts under the newly defined contributory pension scheme is not admissible to them. Similarly, the contribution made by Mrs. Rajeshwari Kori under the said scheme will be admissible to her with interest.
This order is being issued as a special case under the provisions of Rule 40 (e) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1981 and in concurrence with the Finance Department's informal reference No. 188/2019/ Service-7, dated 23.7.2019.
