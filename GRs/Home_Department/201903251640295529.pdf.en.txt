As per the Government decision dated 18.03.2019, for the financial year 2018-19, under the scheme of Modernization of Bus Stations of State Transport Corporations in Tribal Areas under Tribal Appropriation, Demand No. T-5, 3055, Road Transport, Schemes under Five Year Plans, 00-Road Transport, 796 Tribal Area Sub Plan, (01) (01) Development, Construction, Repair of ST Depots in Tribal Areas (3055 0043), 31-Assistant Grants Rs. 320.00 lakhs (Rupees Three Crores Twenty Lakh only) has been sanctioned to be released to the Principal Secretary (Transport), Home Department, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai as administrative department on Budgetary Allocation of Funds (BEAMS).
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to disburse the above funds and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in his office shall take action to recover the amount and pay it to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation. The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) will have to follow the model code of conduct for the Lok Sabha elections.
