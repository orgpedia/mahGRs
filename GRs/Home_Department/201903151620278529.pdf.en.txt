Introduction: The advertisement was published in various daily newspapers dated March 11, 2017, November 15, 2017 and May 3, 2017. An amount of Rs.27075/- (Rupees Twenty Seven Thousand Fifty Seven only) was spent on this advertisement and the matter of administrative sanction for the expenditure was under consideration of the Government.
