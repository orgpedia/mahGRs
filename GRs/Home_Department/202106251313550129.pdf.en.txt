Introduction: As per the decision taken by the High Powered Committee, it has been decided to implement the CCTV Surveillance Project in Katol city by including Video Surveillance System, Automatic Number Plate Recognition Cameras, Geographical Optical Fiber Network, Public Address System, etc. in order to maintain law and order and to take action against the violators of traffic rules.
Government's decision
Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded for the project at a cost of Rs. 9.75 crore (including GST).
2. As mentioned above, in Katol city, the cost of the CCTV surveillance project is being approved for installation of 109 CCTV cameras at about 38 locations along with 23 public address systems and other essentials.
