Preamble: All the posts in the Photography Department under the office of the Director General of Police and the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai are in Group-C cadre. As per the rules of service, the posts of Junior Technical Assistant in the promotion chain of these posts are filled up by direct service and the remaining posts are filled up by direct service/ promotion. The educational qualification for these cadres is 10th pass and for the rest of the posts, 10th pass is considered as the educational qualification for all the posts for direct service/ promotion. Similarly, the posts of Assistant Photographers and Photographers in the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai and various districts are also filled on the basis of this educational qualification. The posts of Senior Technical Assistant and Assistant Photographer in the Department of Photography and the posts of Photographer and Director of Photography do not have the same pay scale.
In view of the above, a proposal to revise the educational qualification prescribed for appointment by nomination to the post of Junior Technical Assistant (Technical) was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Deputy Commissioner of Police, Mumbai, Shri. The Government have decided to revise the educational qualification prescribed under the notification dated 15.11.1983 as follows:
