Government's decision:
A total of 1482 posts created for the protection and security of various establishments and individuals in the state have been included in the reference serial number 16. In accordance with the Government Decision dated 13.03.2018. 01.03.2018 to D. The period was extended till 30.09.2018. The following temporary positions are now available. 01.10.2018 to D.
The period is being extended up to 28.02.2019.
(i) 998 temporary posts in respect of the matters mentioned in Form A on the establishment of various Commissioners of Police/ Superintendents of Police under the control of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State.
(ii) 411 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, in respect of security matters mentioned in Form B.
(iii) A total of 72 temporary posts, 4 each of Police personnel created in Form A, for the protection of 18 Sub-Treasury Offices opened in the newly created Taluks in the State.
(iv) Special Inspector General of Police, Force One, State of Maharashtra.
