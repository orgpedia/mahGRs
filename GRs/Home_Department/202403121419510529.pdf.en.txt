Preamble: - The Commissionerate of Railway Police, Mumbai was granted permission under the Government Letter dated 15.07.2017 to hire security personnel through external agencies (from Maharashtra State Security Corporation) for day-to-day maintenance of railway premises. However, the posts of these security personnel have been extended from time to time in view of the reasons given by the Commissionerate of Railway Police. The 150 security personnel have been given an extension from the date 12.07.2020 to the date 31.12.2022 under the Government Letter dated 10.07.2023. The security of the Maharashtra State Security Corporation, which is provided for day-to-day maintenance of railway premises, is manned by 150 guards. 01/01/2023 that is. The extension of these posts was under the consideration of the Government on the request made by the Commissioner of Police, Railways, regarding the extension for this period. A decision has been taken in this regard.
Government Decision: - Post facto approval is being accorded to the Government decision dated 10th July, 2023 regarding the extension of the period from 12th July, 2020 to 31st December, 2022 for the period of 150 security personnel available through external agencies (from Maharashtra State Security Corporation) in the Commissionerate of Railway Police, Mumbai for day-to-day maintenance of railway premises.
