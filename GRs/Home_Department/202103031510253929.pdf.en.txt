Introduction: With a view to connecting Navi Mumbai to Alibaug through water transport, Karanja (Ta. Uran) to Reus (Ta. Alibaug) is proposed to develop such Ro-Ro service. The distance between Rewas and Karanja is 86 km by road and 3 km by water. If the Ro-Ro service starts from Rewas to Karanja, passengers will be able to take their vehicles by boat to go to Karanja from Rewas. This will save time and fuel expenses. The project will also be environment-friendly. He said," Rev. Construction of Ro-Ro Jetty and similar facilities at Alibag, G.
