GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the case arising out of an offence registered under section 136/2018 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, Ahmednagar District Kotwali Police Station, Shri. Advocate Mr. Ajay Misar, who has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor on behalf of the Government to conduct the court proceedings in the Sessions Court, Ahmednagar, is being approved to pay the fees as follows.
Sessions Court, Ahmednagar A) Effective hearing fee per day- Rs. 15,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Thousand only) (b) Discussion Fee/ Per Hour- Rs. 2500/- (Rupees Two Thousand Five Hundred only) (Maximum 4 hours per day)
Apart from this fee, no other benefit will be admissible to Advocate Ajay Misar.
Also, they will not be provided any fee for any inconclusive hearing.
(2) The effective date of hearing shall be fixed by the officer not below the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case. The concerned Deputy Commissioner of Police/ Superintendent of Police shall ensure that the same is checked and certified by the Court Manager and no excess is provided.
3. The expenditure incurred for this section is" Demand No. B-1, 2055 Police- (00) (109), District Police- (00) (01) District Police Force- (2055 0168)-28- Professional Services" subject to availability of budgetary provision for that financial year and to be shared from the sanctioned grant and as per the monthly fund statement.
