Not allowing to inquire into the death of a COVID suspect.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Home Department
Government Circular No. Parichay- 0420/ PR No. 52/ VISA 1A
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date is April 7, 2020.
Reference:- (1) Section 174 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973.
The Epidemic Diseases Act, 1897 (Preamble): In the background of the current corona virus, the police system is insisting on the death of a COVID suspected deceased person who has been admitted to the hospital. In the current situation, doctors, nurses, police are more likely to get infected by Corona while interrogating a deceased person. Therefore, the government was considering to issue a directive to the police to not investigate such a dead person.
Circular:
The police system is being given the freedom not to inquire the deceased by taking the opinion of the medical officer based on the immediate situation regarding the Covid suspected deceased brought to the hospital.
These orders will remain in force till the restrictions are in place in the state under the provisions of the Disaster Management Act, 2005 read with Part II of the Epidemic Diseases Act, 1897.
