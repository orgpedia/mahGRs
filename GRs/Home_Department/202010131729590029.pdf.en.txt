Introduction: The CCTNS project is being implemented in the state under the e-governance scheme of the Central Government. Under this project, 1054 police stations and 629 senior police offices in the state have been connected with CAS (Core Application Software) software. The project The inauguration was done by the Hon'ble Governor on 15.9.2015. Under the CCTNS project, 2 Mbps network connectivity has been provided to 1054 police stations and 629 senior offices. The project was funded by the Centre, but it will not receive funds from the Centre after March 2018. The state government has to bear the cost of maintenance and repair of the project.
