Preamble: In order to ensure effective implementation of the Maharashtra Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial Institutions) Act, 1999 and to facilitate investigation of cases filed under the Act, the Government was considering extension of 12 posts created under the Government decision at Vacha as there was a need for an independent competent authority and a team of ancillary staff.
Decision of the Government: As per Section 5 (1) of the Maharashtra Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial Institutions) Act, 1999, the following 12 posts of Competent Authority (2 posts) and other ancillary staff are being extended till 30.06.2020.
