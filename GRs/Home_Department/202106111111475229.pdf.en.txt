Preamble: Sion under the Commissioner of Police, Railways, Mumbai is situated at Dharavi Railway Police Colony, Building No. There are 60 apartments. The building was constructed by MHADA in 1990-91 and was handed over to the Commissioner of Police, Mumbai, in 1993. The Executive Engineer, Central Mumbai (East) Division, Worli, Mumbai, has carried out structural inspection of the said building and as per the structural inspection, the said building is a dangerous residence and not safe for occupants. The building needs to be demolished to prevent further accidents and loss of life. Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Limited. The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) has declared the building as" dangerous to live in." Therefore, Sion Dharavi Railway Police Colony Building No. 7 was under the consideration of the Government.

Commissioner of Police, Railway, Mumbai, Sayan Dharavi Railway Police Colony, Building No. 7 Demolition is permitted subject to the following conditions:
Conditions:
(1) The provisions of Rule 114 of the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959 shall be complied with.
