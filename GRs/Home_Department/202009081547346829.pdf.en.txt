Provisional seniority of service of all officers of the cadre of Additional Superintendent, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail, Class-I for the period from 01.01.2019 to 01.01.2020 has been prepared. The General Administration Department of this seniority Government Decision No. SRV 2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21.10.2011 has been prepared as per the instructions.
2. The Additional Director General and Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune is requested to bring to the notice of the Additional Superintendent of Police, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail, Class-I of temporary service seniority for the period from 01.01.2019 and 01.01.2020, all the officers of the cadre of Additional Superintendent, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail, Class-I (including officers on leave/ on deputation) and seek their objections/ objections/ objections regarding the same. In case of any objection/ error received from the concerned Additional Superintendent, Central Jail/ Superintendent, District Jail, Class-I, the information regarding the issues in their representation should be examined as per the records in your office and sent to this department with your feedback within a period of one month.
In case of non-receipt of objections/ errors within the prescribed period, final seniority shall be published on the assumption that the information/ details of the officers mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
