Preamble: The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs, in its meeting held on 7th September, 2017, has approved the proposal for increase in the authorised shareholding of Konkan Railway Corporation and the enhanced shareholding of the Government. The decision No. RLY 2016/ PR No.46/ Railway Cell, dated 13th September, 2017 has been issued.
As per the Government Resolution dated 13th September, 2017, the Department has allotted an additional amount of Rs. 22% in the proportion of Government shareholding (22%) in the enhanced authorised shareholding of Konkan Railway Corporation. Approval has been given for the purchase of shares worth Rs. 702.5776 crore in a phased manner. The First Right Issue of Rs. 68.20 crore was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Resolution: Chairman& Managing Director, Konkan Railway Corporation Ltd., Konkan Railway, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai The State Government hereby approves the release of Rs. 68.20 crore (Rupees Sixty Six Crores Twenty Lakh only).
The Government has declared the Deputy Secretary (Transport), Home Department as the Controlling Officer and the Under Secretary to Government, Cash Branch, Home Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032 as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and they are being authorized to disburse the amount through this Government Decision.
