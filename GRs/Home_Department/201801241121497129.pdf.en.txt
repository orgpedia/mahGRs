government decision.
48 posts of Non-Monitorial Pediatrician and 20 posts of other different cadres in the establishments of the offices of the Commissioner of Police and Superintendent of Police under the Director General of Police, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and other offices, a total of 68 such temporary posts are being approved for extension from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018 on the following terms.
Conditions 1) The extension will not be applicable to posts lying vacant for a period of more than six months.
2) The terms and conditions under which the said 68 posts have been sanctioned should be strictly complied with.
3) Review the posts and submit the revised pattern for the approval of the government immediately. It should be noted that after this, the term of the said posts will not be extended to determine the format.
The expenditure on salary and allowances of the said 68 posts shall be met from the grants available under the respective heads of accounts for the financial year 2017-18.
