Accordingly, the government has taken the next decision.
government decision.
48 posts of Non-Monitorial Paediatrician and 20 posts of other different cadres in the establishments of the offices of the Commissioner of Police and Superintendent of Police under the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and other offices, a total of 68 such temporary posts are being approved for extension from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 on the following conditions:
Conditions 1) The extension will not be applicable to posts lying vacant for a period of more than six months.
2) The terms and conditions under which the said 68 posts have been sanctioned should be strictly complied with.
3) Review the posts and submit the revised pattern for the approval of the government immediately. It should be noted that after this, the term of the said posts will not be extended to determine the format.
The expenditure on salary and allowances of the said 68 posts shall be met from the grants available under the respective heads of accounts for the financial year 2018-19.
