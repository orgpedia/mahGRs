The Proposal: -
At present, the prevalence of cybercrime has increased across the state of Maharashtra, with all ordinary citizens of Maharashtra falling prey to online frauds, financial scams, identity theft, and digital extortion. Currently, cybercriminals are concentrated in urban areas.
They are increasingly targeting the population in semi-urban and rural areas, without being limited.
The general public in Maharashtra is not adequately aware of cybercrime.
The number is increasing. Cybercrime through social media to curb cybercrime
