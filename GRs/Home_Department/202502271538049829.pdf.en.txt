Read: Letter from District Judge B Additional Sessions Judge, Vita, District Sangli Go to No. 1090/2024, dated 13.03.2024.
Preamble: - For the purpose of award of STAAL award under Government Notification, Home Department No. MCT- 0805/1063/136 Transport-5, dated 25/07/2006 for compensation in case of accident in which death or bodily injury to persons or damage to property of a third person or both due to use of a motor vehicle, a Motor Accident Claims Tribunal has been constituted for the area specified for that revenue jurisdiction in all the District B Taluks of the State. Also, District Judges posted in all districts and taluks of the state, Joint District Judge B Additional District Judge and Ad-hoc (Endwalk) Additional District Judge, members equivalent to officers of that rank who are deemed to have assumed charge of the office from time to time, Joint District Judge B Additional District Judge B Ad-hoc (Endwalk) Additional District Judge are empowered to handle cases of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal for that revenue jurisdiction. It was under the consideration of the government to declare him as the "Head of Office" as well as the "Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
Government Decision: - In exercise of the powers conferred by the Government Decision No. 2013 / Q. No. 30/2013 Regulations, Part-II, dated the 17th April, 2015 and the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Division I, Roll No. 3, Rule No. 2 and in exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 15 (3) b156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the District Judge B Additional Sessions Judge and ex-officio member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Vita, District Sangli is being declared as the "Head of Office" as well as the "Drawing and Disbursing Officer." The orders will be limited to financial matters.
