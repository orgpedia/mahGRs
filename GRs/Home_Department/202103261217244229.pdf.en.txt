Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti (As per the date) 2021 Guidelines.
Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti is celebrated as the birth anniversary of the King of Maratha, Shivaji Maharaj. As of March 31, 2021
will be celebrated. In view of the pandemic situation due to COVID-19, this year's date
Celebration of" Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti" on 31st March, 2021 in a very simple manner
A decision has been made. Following are the guidelines:
On this day, many Shiv lovers go to Fort Shivneri or Fort/ Fort and celebrate Shivjayanti by gathering at 12 midnight on the day before the birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. But this year, Shivjayanti is expected to be celebrated in a simple manner without large gatherings to prevent the spread of Covid-19.
Every year during the celebration of Shivjayanti, various programs are organized throughout Maharashtra.
However, this year, there will be no performances of plays, songs, lectures, and other cultural programmes in public places. Instead, arrangements should be made to provide cable network or online broadcast of the program.
Also, like every year, morning rounds, bike rallies, processions should not be taken out. Instead, a program to offer floral tributes to the statue or image of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj is being organized and permission is being given to celebrate Shivjayanti in the presence of 100 people by following social distancing.
Health activities/ camps on the day of Shivjayanti (e.g. to donate blood)
Priority should be given to spread awareness about diseases like Corona, Malaria, Dengue etc and their preventive measures as well as cleanliness.
Special attention should be paid to the observance of social distancing and hygiene rules (masks, sanitizers, etc.) at the venue while organizing health-related activities.
To prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus, the rules prescribed by the Government Relief, Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department as well as the concerned Municipal Corporation, Police Administration, Local Administration should be strictly followed. Also, if any other instructions are published after this circular and in the period between the start of the actual festival, they should also be complied with.
