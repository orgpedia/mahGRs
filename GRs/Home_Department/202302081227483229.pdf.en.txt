Introduction: The Government has a policy of effectively using computer systems to speed up the functioning of the Government. In view of the same, approval for maintenance of Grievance Redressal System and Mobile App for office functioning of e-Governance System in the office of Transport Commissioner and Regional Offices of Home (Transport) Department and for the maintenance of e-Governance System in the offices of Regional Offices of Transport Commissioners in the State of Maharashtra will be issued. The matter of giving approval to host this system in the cloud through MahaIT is under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Home (for Transport Department for office functioning of e-Governance system in Transport Commissioner's Office and Regional Offices in Transport Department) Grievance Redressal System Mobile App Maintenance for one year Rs. 22,74,657/- (including taxes).
