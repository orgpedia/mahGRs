Proposal: New railway line from Ahmednagar-Beed-Parli-Vaijanath at a cost of Rs. The estimated cost of the project is Rs 2826 crore. The state government has decided to provide 50 percent share of Rs 1413 crore to the Central Railway Board.
Accordingly, the State Government has sanctioned a total of Rs. 716.84 crore has been provided. Further, vide Government Decision dated 22.03.2018, an amount of Rs. 75.00 crore has been disbursed. As on 15th March, 2018, a total amount of Rs. 878.53 crore. In order to maintain the equity of the State Government, the Department of Railways, vide its letter dated 31.03.2018, has sought for the year 2017-18, an amount of Rs. 75.00 crore was under the consideration of the Government.
Financial Advisor and Chief Accounts Officer (Construction) Mumbai CST, Central Railway, Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, Mumbai The State Government hereby approves the release of Rs. 75.00 crore (Rupees Seventy Five Crores only).
The Government has declared the Joint Secretary (Budget), Home Department as the Controlling Officer and the Under Secretary to Government, Cash Branch, Home Department, Ministry, Mumbai 400 032 as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and they are being authorized to disburse the amount through this Government Decision.
