Considering the expenditure involved in procuring medicines, chemicals and other medical equipment for the Police Hospitals and Dispensaries in the city, the financial powers of the Police Surgeon, Police Hospital, Nagpada, as the Head of the Office and the Joint Commissioner of Police (Administration), Brihanmumbai, as the Head of the Department, are very limited. Therefore, proposals are often submitted for the approval of the administrative department (Home Department) for the purchase of medicines, chemicals and other medical equipment of daily use in the said hospitals and clinics. Medicines, chemicals, and other medical devices are often purchased as a matter of urgency. Therefore, for the purpose of procuring the said medicines, chemicals and other medical equipments and food items required for the diet of the patients, the said
The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, has submitted a proposal to increase the limit of financial powers of the head of office and head of department.
Government Resolution, Department of Medical Education and Pharmaceuticals, No. 2014/ No. 178/ PR. 2, dated 06.02.2015, the limits of the financial powers of the Head of Department, Regional Head of Office and Head of Office under the administrative control of the Department of Medical Education and Pharmacy have been increased.
The Government has taken the following decision after considering the proposal of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai, and the above precedent.
government decision.
In the case of financial powers to procure supply of medicinal, surgical and laboratory chemicals and foodstuffs for patients at the rates fixed by the Government under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part II, within the limits of the financial powers of the competent authority in column (2) of the table attached to this clause, the conditions in column (4) shall apply.
