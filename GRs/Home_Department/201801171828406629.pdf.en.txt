Government Decision 1,127 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai were extended till 31.12.2017 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The Government hereby approve the continuation of 1,127 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018 subject to the following conditions:
The extension will not be applicable to temporary posts (excluding executive posts) lying vacant for more than six months.
2. The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned subject to the terms and conditions to be followed.
Government Resolution, Finance Division, No: ASANK-1001/ Q.No. 29/2001/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 10th September, 2001, No: ASANK-1004/ Q. No. 12/2004/ Financial Reforms-1, dated 19th August, 2004, No. No. 8/16/ Fiscal Reform-1, dated 21st September, 2016 and No. 8/16/ PC, dated 10.02.2017 and dated 03.10.2017, the process of final approval of the High Level Committee by the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai to the revised pattern of the office should be obtained by the end of 28.02.2017 in any case. The proposal should be sent to the government immediately.
