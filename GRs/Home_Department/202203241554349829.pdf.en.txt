The Proposal:
As requested by the Transport Commissioner's Office under the above mentioned letter, the administrative and financial sanction for the expenses incurred for the training of the newly appointed 5 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors in the Motor Vehicle Department in various workshops of the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The newly appointed 5 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors in the Motor Vehicle Department have been given training in the workshop of the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation. 42.48/- x 365 days x05 Trainees Rs. 77,526/- (Rupees Seventy Seven Thousand Five Hundred and Twenty Six only) is the administrative and financial sanction for the same.
