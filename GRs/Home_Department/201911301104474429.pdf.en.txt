However, a perusal of the complaints received by the department reveals that a large number of complaints are pending. Therefore, in order to ensure effective implementation of online grievance redressal mechanism, the following suggestions are being given:
1) Nodal officers should be appointed at the office level to handle the grievances of the citizens at the level of each zonal office to regulate the complaints received on the online system.
2) The concerned nodal officer at the level of Regional Office should review the complaints received on the system every month and resolve the complaints within the prescribed time and upload them on the portal.
3) The complaints which have not been resolved within the prescribed time should be directed to take immediate remedial action by seeking explanation from the concerned officers responsible for the redressal. 4) The Head of the Department of all the Regional Offices should review the complaints received on the online system by their offices as well as their subordinate offices and submit a report to the Administrative Department by the 5th of every month in the above-mentioned statement.
5) If the complaints are not being resolved within the stipulated time at the field office level, then a proposal should be submitted to the senior office for guidance/ feedback/ approval on the same and after resolving the complaint accordingly, the complainant should be informed on the portal.
6) In cases where the complaint cannot be resolved within the stipulated time as per the provisions of the rules, government decisions, circulars available, and there are circumstances where the resolution may take some additional time, the complainant should be informed on the portal by stating the appropriate reasons.
7) If the head of the concerned office is not able to resolve the complaint by the regional offices, then the applicant should be informed on the portal by stating the necessary reasons.
8) With a view to redress the grievances within the prescribed time frame, the Grievance Redressal Officer concerned shall personally ensure that all grievances are redressed within the prescribed time frame.
