The action is underway. Out of which, 48 temporary posts in the office of the Regional Deputy Director have been transferred to the Home Department, Government Resolution No. DPP-0220/ PR No. 35/ Pol-10, D. 27.03.2020 has been extended upto 31.08.2020. Now, the Government is granting extension of 48 temporary posts in Annexure-B from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than six months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts where the government has already issued orders to abolish these posts.
The High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the review of the posts in the establishment of Directorate of Complaints, Maharashtra State, Mumbai on 13.01.2005 and the above figure of 1168 posts has been prescribed as per the Government decision dated 12.10.2010 in Reference No. 1. This figure includes these positions. Further, as per the Government Decision No.D.P.-2012/ PR No. 112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway.
The expenditure for this should be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21 under the head Home Department B-1, 2014-Judge 114-Legal Adviser and Counsel, (00) (01) Director Government Grievance Computer Code No.
This Government Decision is pursuant to the Government Decision No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ APUK, D. being issued under the powers conferred on the administrative departments as on 31.08.2020.
