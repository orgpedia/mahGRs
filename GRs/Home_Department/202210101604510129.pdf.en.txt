Preamble: The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has requested the Prisons Department to supply uniform material to the police personnel of the state. Accordingly, the Prison Department will manufacture and supply mosquito nets and cross belt strips (with hooks) to the police units. As the raw material required for the production of the said goods is estimated at Rs. 4,07,77, 342/- (Rupees Four Crores Seven Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Three Hundred and Forty Two only) for obtaining administrative approval, the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune vide Reference No. 1 proposal has been submitted. Accordingly, the matter of administrative sanction of the expenditure was under consideration of the Government.
