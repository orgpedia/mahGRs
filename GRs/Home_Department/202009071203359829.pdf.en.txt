GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1,101 temporary posts in various District/ City Police Establishments in the Highway Safety Squad of the State were extended till 31.08.2020 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. By this Order, the Government is granting extension from 2020 till 28.02, 2021 subject to the following conditions:
The extension will not be applicable to temporary posts (excluding executive posts) lying vacant for more than six months.
