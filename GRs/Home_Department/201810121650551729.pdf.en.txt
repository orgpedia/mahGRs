Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) has announced Special Central Assistance (SCA) to Gadchiroli, a Naxal affected district in Maharashtra, for three years from 2017-18 to 2019-20 to counter Left Wing Extremism (LWE). Out of which, for the financial year 2017-18, the Central Government vide letter dated 20.02.2018 has released Rs. 5 crore has been provided.
