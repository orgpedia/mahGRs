No. Mamebo/ Muka/ Abhi-3/ Murudkhora Jetty/ 2021-22/107, dated 7/1/2022 Preamble: Raigad, Tt. Murudkhora in Murud is a tourist spot and has a passenger jetty.
At present, passenger traffic is running smoothly from the passenger jetty and a large number of passengers/ tourists travel by boat to the Janjira Fort every day to see the Janjira Fort from this jetty.
The Jedi at Murudkhora is very old and is in a dilapidated condition. Passengers had a tough time boarding the bus. The passengers have to face difficulties in boarding and de-boarding from the jetty. Therefore, it is necessary to increase the length of the jetty so that passengers/ tourists can safely board and disembark the boat from the passenger jetty and also at the time of disembarkation.
