The Additional Superintendent of Police, Jalna has been suspended from government service since the date of this government order.
Accordingly, it is hereby ordered that during the period of suspension, Mr. Rahul Khade, Additional Superintendent of Police, shall continue to be paid subsistence allowance as per Rule 68 (I) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Post-retirement Service and Salary during Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981, and allowable dearness allowance thereon.
The government is also ordering that the office of the District Superintendent of Police, Jalna, will remain the headquarters of Mr. Rahul Khade, Additional Superintendent of Police, as long as the suspension orders are in place. They cannot leave the headquarters without prior permission of the Superintendent of Police, Jalna. If they leave the headquarters without the prior permission of the Superintendent of Police, Jalna, it will be their misconduct and they will be liable for separate disciplinary action for that reason.
