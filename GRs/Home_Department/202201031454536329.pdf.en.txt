The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in the government service for the disabled. The Central Government vide Reference No. 4 here. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance Reference No. The above 5. As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. In pursuance of the above suggestion, the order regarding the establishment of Directorate of Civil Defence under the Home (VISA-8) Department with 20 cadre posts has been issued vide Reference No. 6 have been released. The proposal regarding the remaining 22 cadre posts was submitted to the expert committee headed by Principal Secretary (Social Justice). In the meeting of the Expert Committee held on 11.10.2021, it was decided to exclude the posts from the list of vacancies for Divyangjan. Accordingly, as per the minutes of the meeting of the Expert Committee held on 11.10.2021, it was under the consideration of the Government to exclude the posts of Group-A to Group-D in 22 cadres on the establishment of the Director, Directorate of Civil Defence under the Home Department from the post of Differently Abled.
Government's decision:
The posts for the disabled as shown in the prospectus A along with the posts of Group-A to Group-D in 22 cadres on the establishment of the Director, Directorate of Civil Defence under the Home Department are being excluded from the reservation.
