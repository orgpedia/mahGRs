Preamble: As requested by the Commissioner of Transport, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, vide letter No. 3 above, the Government was in the process of giving administrative and financial approval for the expenditure incurred on training of 100 newly appointed Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors of the Motor Vehicles Department at the Central Institute of Road Transport, Pune (CIRT, PUNE).
Government's decision:
The Central Institute of Road Transport, Pune (CIRT, PUNE) has invited applications from eligible candidates for filling Eight (8) weeks training to the newly recruited batch of 100 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors in the Department of Motor Vehicles. Administrative and financial sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 1,84,80, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Four Lakh Eighty Thousand only).
