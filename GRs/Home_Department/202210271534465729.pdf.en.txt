As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 62 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in the public interest at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them prematurely from Government service. As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee, which evaluated the service of the said officers, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the officers who have been found eligible to continue in service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
Government's decision
Pursuant to the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee in its meeting held on 11.10.2022, it is being approved to continue the services of Deputy Regional Transport Officer, Group-A Cadre Officers in the Transport Department at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
The service of the Deputy Regional Transport Officer mentioned in the accompanying Form-A should be recorded in his service book after 50/55 years of service or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
