The Maharashtra Protection and Security of Witnesses Act, 2017 has been published in the Gazette of the Government of Maharashtra as Maharashtra Act No. 9 of 2018. This notification has been issued with the approval of the Governor on 16.01.2018. Further, vide notification dated 25th April, 2018 under Section 1 (3) of the said Act, the date of coming into force of the provisions of the said Act is 1st May, 2018. As per section 3 (1) (a) of the said Act, the matter of constitution of a State Witness Protection Committee for the purposes of the said Act and as per section 3 (1) (b) of the said Act, a District Witness Protection Committee for each district was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: As per Section 1 (3) of the Maharashtra Witness Protection and Security Act, 2017, published on 16.01.2018, the provisions of this Act have been made applicable in the State from 1st May, 2018. For the purposes of the said Acts, the State Witness Protection Committee is being constituted under section 3 (1) (a), the District Witness Protection Committee under section 3 (3) (a) (for metropolitan areas), the District Witness Protection Committee under section 3 (3) (b) (for commissionerate areas other than metropolitan areas) and the District Witness Protection Committee under section 3 (3) (c) (for other districts) as follows:
