The Proposal:
The existing police stations in the State are No. Manpower is employed on the basis of criteria laid down by the Act dated January 23, 1960. As per the decision of the government, proposals for the creation of new police stations are submitted to the government through the office of the Director General of Police from various constituent offices of the state.
2. Such a long period of 62 years has elapsed since the said criteria for the working manpower in the police stations. Increasing population, increasing industrialization and urbanization have led to changes in the way of life, according to which the nature of crime has changed, the spread and use of social media has increased in society and the religious/ political crimes through it, the impact of their understanding, the financial fraud crimes through the Internet have changed drastically.

The manpower is inadequate. For this, the norms of manpower in police stations need to be changed in accordance with time.
Accordingly, the Office of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide reference No. 3 and No. As per letter No. 4, the proposal for revised manpower standard has been submitted to the Government after re-examining the manpower standard/ criteria for the police stations (excluding the Brihanmumbai Police Commissionerate) as per the requirement of the Police Commissionerate and the police stations in the district.
Considering the new technology and the increase in the number of crimes in the present situation, the government was considering to revise the criteria for the creation of police stations created in 1960 and make changes in them according to the time.
Accordingly, the No. 1 The decision of the government here is being overruled, and the government has decided as follows.
Government's decision:
It is hereby approved to fix the revised standards/ norms of manpower for police stations in the Commissionerate of Police (excluding Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai City) in the State as per Annexure-A and for police stations in the districts as per Annexure-B.
2. Revised Standards/ Criteria for manpower The revised standards/ criteria for manpower for police stations (excluding the Brihanmumbai Police Commissionerate) have been prepared as per the requirement of the police stations in the State Police Commissionerate and in the districts, after studying the Government Decision of 1960 and taking into account the changing situation and taking into account the feedback of various police units. For this, the office of the Director General of Police has studied the manpower criteria for urban and rural police stations in the BPR& D report.
Manpower standards/ norms for police stations under the jurisdiction of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai City will be issued separately.
4) Manpower standards/ norms will be issued separately for the newly created headquarters and other branches in the state.
