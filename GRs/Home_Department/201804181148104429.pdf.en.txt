The Government has taken an important decision to implement the concept of" Green Maharashtra" to reduce the intensity and severity of natural disasters such as climate change, rising temperatures, drought and water scarcity. Circular 1. In the field level meetings at Taluk, District and State level under the Motor Vehicles Department, the subject of tree plantation and nurturing should be included as a permanent subject in the agenda at the time of each meeting. In such meetings, review of tree plantation, survival rate of trees planted earlier, planting of new trees in place of dead trees, fixing of sites for plantation as per the target of tree plantation in the coming years, availability of saplings, preparation of pits within the stipulated time as per the required technical parameters, availability of funds etc. should be reviewed.
The decisions taken in such meetings regarding the tree plantation programme should be recorded in the minutes of the meeting. Further, the implementation of the decisions mentioned in the history and the difficulties encountered should be discussed in the next meeting and measures should be taken to solve the problems and difficulties faced by the field officers.
3. The officers at Taluka, District and State level who will visit the field works, a program should be organized to visit the tree plantation sites under those departments.
During such field visits, senior officers should inspect whether the trees planted under the concerned administrative department are properly protected, whether new trees have been planted in place of dead trees, water arrangements and manpower for protection, etc. Also, you need to take steps to solve the problem.
4. Also inspect the sites selected for proposed tree plantation, preparation of pits of required size within the stipulated time and water and protection arrangements for the trees to be planted at such sites, arrangement of funds for tree plantation, etc.
