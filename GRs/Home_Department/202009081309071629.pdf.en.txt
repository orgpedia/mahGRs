Preamble: The Superintendent of Police, Nashik (Rural), in order to maintain law and order during the elections to the Legislative Assembly 2019, a total of 78 private vehicles were acquired on lease as the vehicles available with the police force were insufficient.
The vehicles were acquired on hire basis for government purposes as per the fare chart published in the letter dated 30.04.2019 of Regional Transport Office, Nashik. A total of 78 vehicles were seized. The proposal for obtaining ex-post facto approval for payment of dues amounting to Rs. 3,02,307/- (Rupees Three Lakh Two Thousand Three Hundred and Seven only) was under consideration of the Government for grant of ex-gratia for the financial year 2020-2021.
Nashik (Rural): A total of 78 private vehicles were acquired on lease for the assembly elections 2019 as the police force did not have enough vehicles to maintain law and order during the elections.
The vehicles were acquired on hire basis for government purposes as per the fare chart published in the letter dated 30.04.2019 of Regional Transport Office, Nashik. A total of 78 vehicles were seized. The proposal for payment of balance subsidy under the head of prescribed accounts for the financial year 2020-2021 amounting to Rs. 3,02,307/- (Rupees Three Lakh Two Thousand Three Hundred and Seven only) is being approved post facto.
