The transfers are being made with the prior approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The transferred prosecutors should take charge of the post immediately after the transfer. The Director, Directorate of Grievances should strictly follow the following instructions to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(a) The transfer of Assistant Public Prosecutor has been proposed in the district and the posting anywhere in the district should be done by the Director, Directorate of Complaints. The officers concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of payment of salary and allowances by fixing a mandatory waiting period without work due to non-recruitment of the said prosecutor.
(c) the date on which the concerned prosecutor was relieved to appear at the place of new appointment and the date on which he appeared at the place of new appointment shall be intimated to the Government forthwith.
