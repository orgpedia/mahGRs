Preamble: In pursuance of the above Government decision, administrative and financial approval has been accorded for the cost of Rs. 1,21,30, 500/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty One Lakh Thirty Five Thousand Five Hundred only) for the training provided to the 2nd batch of 196 newly appointed Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors in the Motor Vehicles Department. Government's decision:
For the training of the 2nd batch of 196 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors of the Motor Vehicle Department at the Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik for eight weeks, Rs. 98,21,500/- (Rupees Ninety-eight Lakhs Twenty-one Thousand Five Hundred only) is being provided as administrative and financial sanction.
