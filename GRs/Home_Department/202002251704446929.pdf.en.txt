It was decided to provide funds for the purchase of 700 buses to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation through the state government. Accordingly, the demand for funds through Supplementary Demands was raised in the Winter Session, 2018-19 to the tune of Rs. 50.00 crore has been sanctioned by way of Supplementary Demands and Rs. 50.00 crore has been disbursed to the Corporation.
Further, during the Monsoon Session 2019-20, Rs. 50.00 crore in the Winter Session, 2019 and Rs. 50.00 crore has been sanctioned through Supplementary Demands. Accordingly, while submitting the proposals for disbursement of funds to the Finance Department, the Finance Department approved the disbursement of funds within the limit of 60 per cent of the sanctioned supplementary demand. Accordingly, pursuant to the Government Resolution No. 2, Rs. 30.00 crore and vide Government Resolution No.3, Rs. 30.00 crore has been disbursed, thus so far Rs. 110.00 crore has been disbursed to State Transport Corporations.
Accordingly, now in the Budget Year 2019-20, the amount sanctioned through Supplementary Demand of Rs. 100.00 crore out of Rs. 40.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.
