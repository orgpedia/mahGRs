Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to grant administrative and financial sanction for the expenditure for training of newly appointed Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors in the Motor Vehicles Department and Sub Divisional Assistant Regional Transport Officers in CPTP-4, 5 and 6 in various institutions.
Government Decision: The newly appointed Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors of Motor Vehicle Department, 200 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors of the second batch at Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik, 100 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors of the second batch at Central Institute of Road Transport (CIRT), Pune and 1 of the Combined Peripheral Programme (CPTP)-4 and Combined Peripheral Programme (CPP)-4. P.T.P.- 7 probationary Assistant Regional Transport Officers (A.T.O.) of 5th and 6th grade for training at Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik
A total of Rs. 4,15,94, 000/- out of which Rs. Administrative and financial approval is being accorded for payment of Rs. 2,49,75, 000/- to the concerned institution. Details are as follows.
