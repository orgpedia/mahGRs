The Proposal: -
Mr. Rajendra Singh has filed a complaint against the Government of India and other State Governments for alleged misuse of illegal arms. Special Leave Petition (Criminal) No. 12831/2022 is entered. In pursuance of this Special Leave Petition, Shri. In pursuance of the hearing held on 07.11.2024, Mr. The Supreme Court has ordered the formation of a committee headed by the Chief Secretary to prepare and implement an action plan at the state level by each state for effective implementation of the Arms Act and prevention of misuse of unlicensed arms.
The matter of constitution of the committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, State of Maharashtra was under consideration of the Government as per the orders of the Supreme Court.
