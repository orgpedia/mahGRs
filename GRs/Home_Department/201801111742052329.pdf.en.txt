Preamble: Sub-Regional Transport Office, Pen, Group No. 28/1, wins the game. 3.40-hectare government land in Pen, Raigad. As per the orders of the High Court, a memorandum was submitted to the government for the construction of a 250-metre-long brake test track. As on 03.03.2017 Rs. Administrative and financial sanction has been accorded for an amount of Rs. 62.69 lakh. Accordingly, it was observed that more work would have to be done for the levelling of the said land after the actual work started. Also, the Regional Transport Office, Panvel decided to test the vehicles of the Transport Office at Pen, which would increase the stress on the brake test track at Pen, necessitating the provision of asphalting of that capacity. Accordingly, the cost of construction of the brake test track has increased and the matter of providing revised administrative and financial approval to the increased expenditure budget was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
