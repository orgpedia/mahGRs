Minutes of the 7th Meeting of the Board of Directors to be held on 27th October, 2023 Department of Finance Letter No. 21.11.2023 Preamble: The Commissionerate of Police, Mira-Bhayander-Vasai-Virar has been made functional with effect from 01.10.2020 by bifurcating the Superintendent of Police, Thane Rural and Palghar Division. 2905 posts were sanctioned for Mira-Bhayander-Vasai-Virar Police Commissionerate in the year 2019. In fact, 1741 posts have been filled and 1064 posts are vacant. There are a total of 501 vacancies in the police force. The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai had requested to take the services of a total of 500 manpower from the Maharashtra State Security Corporation for a period of at least 11 months till the process of filling up the posts in a proper manner is completed. In pursuance of the said request, the matter was under consideration to approve the recommendation of the Sub-Committee constituted by the Finance Department to take over the services of a total of 500 manpower from the Maharashtra State Security Corporation for at least 11 months till the process of filling up the posts of Police Constables under Mira-Bhayander-Vasai-Virar Commissionerate is completed in the prescribed manner.
Government Decision: 1. Approval is being granted to take the services of a total of 500 manpower from external agencies (Maharashtra State Security Corporation) under the condition of no further extension for the period from 15/06/2023 to 31/03/2024 till the completion of the process of filling up the posts of police constables under the newly created Mira-Vasai-Bhayander-Virar Police Commissionerate by bifurcating Thane Rural and Palghar units in the prescribed manner.
2. The approval was accorded by the Sub-Committee constituted by the Finance Department. This is in compliance with the recommendation dated 27.10.2023.
