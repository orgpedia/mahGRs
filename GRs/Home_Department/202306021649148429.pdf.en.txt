Preamble: Educational Qualification for the post of Sub-Inspector (SI) in the State Crime Investigation Department. 1 Under the decision of the government here, the" Degree Examination" was fixed. However, the Central Finger Print Bureau (CFPB), under the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), Government of India, has notified the educational qualification for appearing in Finger Print Expert Examination as" Graduate in Science/ Graduate in Forensic Science" vide Reference No. Under Section 2, instructions have been given to the finger currency centers of all the states. Accordingly, the matter of revision of educational qualification for the post of Inspectors/ Sub-Inspectors of Police (Fingerprint) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: For the post of Sub-Inspector of Police (Finger Print) in the Finger Print Department under the State Criminal Investigation Department, the following recruitment will be done. The educational qualification as directed in section 2 is being revised from" Degree Examination" to" Graduate in Science or Graduate in Forensic Science from a recognized University."
