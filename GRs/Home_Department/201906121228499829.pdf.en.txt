These orders are being issued in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, with prior approval of the competent authority.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The transferred officer should take charge of the post immediately after the transfer. For the immediate and effective implementation of the transfer order, the Commissioner, Raushu, shall strictly follow the following instructions.
(a) The transferred officer shall be relieved forthwith on receipt of the order of transfer.
The transferred officers should be reinstated without delay after they are relieved from their present posts. The officers concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-recruitment of the said officer and payment of salary and allowances by fixing a mandatory waiting period without work.
(b) the date on which the officer concerned was relieved of his duties to appear in place of the new appointee and the date on which he appeared in place of the new appointee.
If any officer directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned officers considering it as misconduct as the action is in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
