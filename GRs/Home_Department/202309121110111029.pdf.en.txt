Aghav, Sub-Divisional Police Officer, Ambad Sub-Division, District Jalna are being suspended from government service from the date of this government order.
Accordingly, it is hereby ordered that during the period of suspension, Mr. Mukund Aghav shall continue to be paid subsistence allowance and allowable dearness allowance thereon as per Rule 68 (I) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Non-Ministerial Service and Salary during Suspension, Dismissal, Removal) Rules, 1981.
The government is also ordering that the office of the District Superintendent of Police, Jalna, will remain the headquarters of Mr. Mukund Aghav as long as the suspension orders are in place. They cannot leave the headquarters without prior permission of the Superintendent of Police, Jalna. If they leave the headquarters without the prior permission of the Superintendent of Police, Jalna, it will be their misconduct and they will be liable for separate disciplinary action for that reason.
