Government Resolution No: DPP-0220/ PR No.35/ Pol-10
Government Decision: A total of 1168 posts under the control of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved. Further, as per the Government Decision No.D.P.-2012/ PR No. 112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway. Out of which, 167 temporary posts of Assistant Director and Public Prosecutor in the 5 District Offices under the control of Deputy Director, Government Grievances, Konkan Range along with Annexure-C have been notified vide Home Department Government Resolution No. DPP-1019/ PR No. The date has been extended upto 29.02.2020 w.e.f 05.10.2019. Now, under this Order, the Government is granting extension of 167 non-permanent posts in Annexure-C from 01/03/2020 to 31.08.2020. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than 6 months. Also, the extension of tenure will not be applicable to the posts which the government has already ordered to abolish.
The High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the review of the posts in the establishment of Directorate of Complaints, Maharashtra State, Mumbai on 13.01.2005 and as per the Government decision dated 12.10.2010 in Reference No. 1, the figure of 1168 posts has been prescribed. This figure includes these positions. Further, as per Government Resolution No. 2012/ DPP-112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway.
The expenditure for this should be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21 under the head Home Department B-1, 2014-Judge 114-Legal Adviser and Counsel, (00) (01) Director Government Grievance Computer Code No.
This Government Decision is pursuant to the Government Decision No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ APUK, D. Issued under the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under 12.02.2020.
