The Proposal:
The Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai has requested the Government through a letter under reference to provide funds for payment of salaries to the employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of May, 2023 and for reimbursement of concessional rates to the State Road Transport Corporation for the month of April, 2023 as well as to meet the deficit in income and fuel, salary, maintenance and repair expenses for the month of April, 2023. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds for payment of salaries to the employees of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the month of May, 2023 and for reimbursement of concessional rates to the State Road Transport Corporation for the month of April, 2023.
Government's decision:
In the year 2023-24, from the provision made under the head" 2041 0018-33 Financial Assistance" of the Home (Transport) Department, an amount of Rs. 82.84 crore and Rs. 271.93 crore. 354.77 crore (Rupees Three Hundred and Fifty Four Crores Seventy Seven Lakh only) is being sanctioned in cash.
