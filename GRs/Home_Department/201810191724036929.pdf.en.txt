Government Resolution 07/9/2018
The government has decided to create 15,000 posts of police naiks and 1500 posts of assistant police inspectors.
These posts have been created with effect from 01/11/1992.
These posts were extended from 01/03/2018 to 30/9/2018 vide Government Resolution, Home Department No.Police-3118/ PR No.109/ Pol-5A, dated 6/04/2018.
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide the above Reference No. As per letter no. 1& 2, a proposal has been submitted for extension of the said temporary posts beyond 1/10/2018. The Government approves extension of the said posts for the period from 01/10/2018 to 28/02/2019 subject to the following conditions:
Conditions:
(1) None of the above posts has been vacant for more than 6 months and the terms and conditions for which the posts have been sanctioned have been complied with.

The terms and conditions laid down as on 07/9/2018 should be scrupulously followed and acted upon accordingly. Similarly, immediate action should be taken to get the revised format of the office approved by the high-level committee.
(3) If the process of review of posts is not completed within the stipulated time, the temporary posts will not get extension for that further period and if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officers/ employees, it will be the responsibility of the concerned establishments.
4) The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall submit an appropriate proposal to the Government for approval of the High-Level Secretary Committee for review and revised review of the sanctioned post during this period. If not, it should be noted that if the question of salary of these temporary posts arises due to the expiry of the deadline, the Director General of Police will be responsible for it.
