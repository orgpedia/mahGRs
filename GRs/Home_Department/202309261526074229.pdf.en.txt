The Manager, State Transport Appellate Tribunal, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai has now issued a proposal regarding extension of 1 (one) temporary post of peon with effect from 1.09.2023 vide Reference No. 2 submitted to the Government. The matter of extension of the said post from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION:- The 1 (one) temporary post of peon in the establishment of State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being extended from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024. This extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending with the State Transport Appellate Tribunal are settled expeditiously, the returns of the settled claims are submitted to the Government for the month of November, 2023 to February, 2024 and if 50 per cent of the pending claims are not found to be settled by the end of 29.02.2024, no further extension will be granted.
