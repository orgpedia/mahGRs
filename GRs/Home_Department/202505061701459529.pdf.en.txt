Objectives: - Effective implementation of the decisions taken at the state level NCORD meetings and
Preventing and controlling the illegal sale and increased use of drugs.
The scope of this working group is as follows.
1) Progress of creation of "Anti Narcotics Task Force" in Maharashtra State.
2) Deportation of Foreigners involved in drug trafficking. Creation of State Detention Centre. Involvement of foreigners in drug trafficking with specially reference to African Nationals.
3) Establishing institutional mechanism for regular inspection of chemical factories to present their usage for illicit Drug manufacture Including sick factories.
4) Public Awareness Programmes related to Drug abuse.
5) Counselling of Drug Addicts, Drug Abuse survey (Type of Drug use, Profile of Drug Addict etc). "Drug Harm" Reduction Initiatives: Standardization of De-addiction/Rehab Centers.
6) Scanning of containers at JNPT.
7) Integration of Portal of registration of Leave and License developed by IGR Pune with CCTNS.
