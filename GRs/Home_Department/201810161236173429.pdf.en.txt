Government Decision: A total of 1168 posts under the control of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved. Further, as per Government Resolution No. 2012/ DPP-112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway. Out of which, 173 temporary posts in 5 District Offices of Assistant Director and Public Prosecutor in Annexure-D along with the control of Deputy Director, Government Grievances, Nashik Range, Nashik have been transferred to the Home Department vide Government Resolution No. DPP-0118/ PR No. 14/ Pol-10, D. 27.03.2018 has been extended upto 30.09.2018. Now, the Government hereby grants extension of 173 temporary posts in Annexure-D from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019.
The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than 6 months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts that the government has already ordered to eliminate.
