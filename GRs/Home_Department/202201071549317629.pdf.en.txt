Accordingly, the Government was considering the creation of a maximum number of posts in the cadre of Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Group-B in the Motor Vehicle Department for the period up to the completion of training (two years) for the candidates selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final result of the State Service (Main) Examination 2019 and appointed as Trainee Subordinate Officers for the Combined Training Programme 7.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the creation of a total of 02 supernumerary posts in the cadre of Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Group-B Pay Scale S-16 (44,900- 1,42,400) in the Motor Vehicle Department, who have been selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final results of the State Service Main Examination 2019 and appointed as Trainee Subordinate Officer for the Combined Training Programme-7 for a period of two years from the date of commencement of training of these candidates.
