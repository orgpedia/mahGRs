Introduction: The above reference No. Two separate Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district as per the government notification. Reference No. A total of 24 temporary posts have been created under the government decision. The 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the said Tribunal are mentioned in Reference No. 3 As per the Government decision dated 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
Since the extension of these 24 posts is coming to an end on 30.09.2019, and in reference No. 4 vide letter dated 22.08.2019, the Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur had submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of the said 24 temporary posts.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Extension of 24 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Nagpur from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020. The extension is subject to the conditions of expeditious disposal of the claims pending before the Tribunal and submission of the statement of settled claims to the Government after every three months.
