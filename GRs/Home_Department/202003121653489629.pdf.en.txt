Now, the Government hereby grants extension of tenure to the above mentioned 1,330 temporary posts in various offices under the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020 subject to the following conditions:
The extension will not be applicable to temporary posts (excluding executive posts) lying vacant for more than six months.
(2) The temporary posts shall be subject to the terms and conditions for which they have been sanctioned.
Government Resolution, Finance Division, No.Assn.1001/ Q.No. 29/2001/ Financial Reforms-, dated 10.09.2001, No.Assn.1004/ Q. No. 12/2004/ Financial Reforms-1, dated
19.08.2004, No. Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ Financial Amendments-1, dated 21.09.2016 and No. The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall complete the process of final approval of the High Level Committee for the revised pattern of posts under the office as per No. 8/16/ APC, dated 10.02.2017 immediately and complete the process within the prescribed time in any case. In this regard, the recommendations made in the past should be considered.
(4) If the process of review of posts is not completed within the prescribed time, if the above temporary posts are not extended for a further period or if there is a question of salary and allowances of the officer/ employee, it shall be the responsibility of the concerned office.
