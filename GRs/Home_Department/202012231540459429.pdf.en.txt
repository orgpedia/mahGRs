Government's decision:
The Department of Rehabilitation and Disaster Management has approved the above mentioned orders and issued the standards of operation of the Home (Ports) Department to help the various water sports projects and boating projects being run along the coast of the state with necessary precautions in view of the outbreak of COVID-19.
Accordingly, the Standards of Conduct as attached to this Order have been prescribed and the said Standards of Conduct (SOPs) shall be strictly followed.
