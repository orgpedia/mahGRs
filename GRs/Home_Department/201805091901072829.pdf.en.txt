The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the construction of a new building for the Police Station, Deglur, Taluka-Deglur, District-Nanded, under the jurisdiction of the Superintendent of Police, Nanded.
2. For the said work, the Sub-Divisional Officer, Public Works Sub-Division, Deglur, prepared as per the 2015-16 tariff of Rs. 46,80,400/- has been submitted and the same has been attested by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Deglur and Superintendent of Police, Nanded.
3. Estimates of the said expenditure have been examined by the Public Works Department, Ministry and it is estimated to be Rs. 44.83 lakh has been so amended and, subject to certain conditions, it is informed that administrative approval is not to be entertained.
Accordingly, administrative approval is being accorded for construction of new building for Police Station Deglur under the jurisdiction of Superintendent of Police, Nanded, Taluka-Deglur, District-Nanded, Police Outpost, Haunagaon, on the following conditions:
1) Construction work should be started only after getting the approval of the architect for the design and detailed map.
