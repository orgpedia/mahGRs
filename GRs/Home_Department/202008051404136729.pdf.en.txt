The proposal:
The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) has been providing concessions in passenger fares to various categories of passengers as per the orders issued by the state government from time to time. Compensation for the cost. 1.04.2016 through Home (Transport) Department. The policy decision has been taken in accordance with the Government Decision dated 25/2/2016. Accordingly, the reimbursement of the travel concession value is being made by the Government in cash as well as some amount to the Corporation through adjustment of passenger tax.
Due to the lockdown announced in the wake of the Covid-19 disease, most of the transport except essential transport services of the RMC are closed. As a result, the employees of the corporation have been suffering. In such a situation, the matter of disbursing funds to the corporation was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) has released Rs. 550,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Hundred and Fifty Crores only) in cash from the provision for the financial year 2020-2021. This amount will be adjusted from time to time as an advance from the amount of the concession price to be paid to the Maharashtra State Transport Corporation.
2. The expenditure shall be met from the provisions available in the financial year 2020- 21 and the expenditure shall be covered under the head" Demand No.B-3, 2041, Tax on Vehicles 00,001, Operation and Administration (01) Transport Commissioner (01) (01) Establishment- Transport Commissioner, (Adaptation) (Mandatory), (2041 0018) 33, Financial Assistance."
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to collect and disburse the amount to be paid to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation by the Accountant in his office.
