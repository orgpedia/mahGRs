Preamble: A proposal to set up a separate Sub-Regional Transport Office in Ichalkaranji, G. Kolhapur considering the vehicle registration number and total population was under consideration of the Government.
The government has decided to set up a new office with registration number MH-51 in the rank of Deputy Regional Transport Officer at Ichalkaranji, Kolhapur.
In accordance with the prevailing policy of the Government in this regard, necessary posts will be created for the newly created office of Deputy Regional Transport Officer Ichalkaranji, G. Kolhapur.
The Transport Commissioner should take further action to take the place of Ichalkaranji Municipal Corporation on rent/ lease basis to start the new office.
4. 1 Interceptor Vehicle is being sanctioned for the newly constructed office. Approval of the vehicle review committee should be obtained before taking this vehicle.
The estimated recurring and non-recurring expenditure for the said office shall be provided by way of supplementary demand in the ensuing session. A proposal in this regard will be submitted to the Transport Department. Till then, these expenses should be met from the available office expenses.
6. Ichalkaranji, G. The new Sub-Regional Transport Office in Kolhapur is being given the registration number MH51.
