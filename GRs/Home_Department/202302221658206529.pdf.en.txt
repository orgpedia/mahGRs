Preamble: As per the Government Decision dated 29.10.2001 issued by the General Administration Department in reference No. 1 regarding laying down the procedures for complete computerization of Government work, the procurement of hardware components was done at a cost of Rs. 5.00 lakh and purchase of software components worth Rs. 2.00 lakh, the concerned departments are required to constitute a Project Implementation Committee under the Chairmanship of the Principal Secretary/ Secretary concerned to implement such computerization projects. Also, the Department of Information and Technology has issued Reference No.
3 here. By a Government Resolution dated 16.06.2010, Ministries/ Departments have been directed to reserve half of their budgetary allocation for e-Governance and seek approval of the Project Implementation Committee while spending the said funds. Further, as per the Government Decision dated 23.09.2011 of the Department of Information Technology, the Project Implementation Committee has been entrusted with the responsibility of monitoring the e-governance projects as per the e-governance policy of the State of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the government had constituted a committee for the implementation of the project.
