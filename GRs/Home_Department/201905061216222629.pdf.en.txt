The proposal: The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. 604/2019. As per the court's order, the government was considering a proposal to prescribe guidelines regarding the police guard and the fee to be charged for it during the period of leave granted to prisoners/ detainees in emergency situations.
Government Decision: In case of any emergency like death/ medical emergency etc. of the close relatives of the prisoner/ detainee in the jail. If the prisoner/ detainee is granted leave of absence in accordance with the emergency situation as per the orders of the court or competent authority, he/ she shall be charged for the police guard being provided. The amount of this fee shall be intimated in writing to the concerned prisoner/ detainee before his/ her release on Emergency Promised Leave and preferably in advance. However, considering the emergency situation, the competent authority may extend the time limit for payment of the said fee by a maximum period of 15 days. If the amount is not paid after 15 days, the interest rate will be 9.5 percent. The responsibility of collecting this fee will be of the concerned jail superintendent.
