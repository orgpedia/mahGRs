Introduction: Every year, the education department of the Mumbai Traffic Police conducts several creative awareness activities along with traffic regulation and control in the city of Mumbai. The Education Department of the Mumbai Transport Department has been providing training to school students in Brihanmumbai since 1957. At present, about 60,000 students from 505 schools are being trained annually under Road Safety Force on traffic control and regulation in a systematic manner.
In this training, the students of different schools are systematically trained in traffic control and rules and disciplined conduct. The students will also participate in the Republic Day parade on January 26. The certificates are issued to the students after completion of three years course under Road Safety Force. This training is provided by the officers/ employees of the transport department. Accordingly, the matter of administrative and financial approval of the expenses to implement the program was under the consideration of the government.
The Mumbai Traffic Police conducts various activities every year under the Road Safety Force with the participation of schools in the city. The expenditure incurred for the 2022-2023 annual initiative implemented by the Education Department under the Mumbai Traffic Police Department is Rs. 47,71,078/- (Rs. Forty-seven lakh seventy-one thousand seventy-eight only) is being approved post facto.
The expenditure should be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2022-2023 under the head' Main Accounts B-1, 2055, Police 108, State Headquarters Police (02), City Police (02) (01) Establishment, (2055 0097) 13 Office Expenditure'.
3. Complete details of expenditure incurred on the subject activities should be submitted to the government immediately.
