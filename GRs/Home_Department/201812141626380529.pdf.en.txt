Minutes of the discussion held in the Maharashtra Legislative Council on 29.11.2018.
Kapil Patil, V.P.S., presented the attention-grabbing suggestions regarding seniority, honorable date and promotion of officers in the cadre of Assistant Regional Officer in the Motor Vehicle Department. The matter of appointing a committee under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department, to investigate in accordance with the assurance given during the discussion on 701 is under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Various Tribunals have passed judgements regarding the seniority of officers in the cadre of Assistant Regional Officer in the Motor Vehicle Department, which was created in 2004 and 2009, the date of award of the officers and the promotions given accordingly.
A committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department, to investigate in detail whether the said seniority and the date of appointment were given as per the prescribed rules and procedures. The committee will comprise the chief secretary and the transport secretary.
The Additional Transport Commissioner will assist the committee by providing necessary documents from time to time.
2. m. As per the assurance given by the Minister (Transport) in the Maharashtra Legislative Council, the functions of the above-mentioned committee will be as follows:

2.1. Inquire as to whether there have been any irregularities in the preparation of the service certificate of the Assistant Regional Transport Officer Cadre in the Motor Vehicles Department since 1991.
2.2. To inquire whether the date of award of the award to the officers of the Motor Vehicles Department up to the year 2004 has been given in accordance with the prescribed rules and procedures and whether there has been any irregularity.
2.3. To enquire whether the date of award of the award and the promotions availed up to 2004 have been made in accordance with the prescribed rules and procedures.
2.4) If there is any irregularity in the date of honorarium given to the officers of the Motor Vehicle Department and the promotion made by taking advantage of it, the names of the officers responsible for the matter should be made available.
The committee has to submit its report to the government within a month.
