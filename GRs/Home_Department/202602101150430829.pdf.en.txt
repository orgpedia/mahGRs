Government Decision: - The Chief Security Officer, Western Coalfields Limited has issued the following order. The letter from the 25.07.2024 requests the Director General, Home Guards, to train their security supervisors at the Maharashtra Home Guards Training Centre. Accordingly, the Director General, Home Guards, Shri. 18.07.2025 and d. According to the letter of the 14.10.2025, since the security personnel of Western Coalfields Limited are proposed to be provided paid training through the Home Guard Department and there will be no financial burden on the government, a proposal has been submitted to the government for administrative approval for providing paid training to these security personnel through the Home Guard Department.
The Maharashtra Home Guards Act, 1947 provides for training of Home Guards. Also, since training has been imparted by the Home Guards Department to personnel / volunteers of colleges, Delhi Home Guards, Nashik Municipal Corporation Firemen, Customs, Civil Defence, etc. during the period 1960 to 1995, the security personnel of Western Coalfields Limited, Nagpur are being given administrative sanction for paid training at the Maharashtra Home Guards Training Centre.
The funds received by the Home Guard Office through this training should be credited under the head of revenue collection 0070-Other Administrative Services, 105-Home Guards, 00, (00) (01) -Home Guards 00700082.
