Read: Letter No. 127/2021 dated 15.02.2021 from District Judge and Additional Sessions Judge, Shrigonda, District Ahmednagar.
Preamble: For the purpose of adjudicating claims for compensation in case of accident involving death or bodily injury to persons or damage to property of a third person or both, under Government Notification, Home Department No. MCT- 0805/1063/136/ Transport-5, dated 25/07/2006, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal has been constituted in all the districts and taluks of the State for the area specified for that revenue range. Besides, the District Judges, Joint District Judges, Additional District Judges and Ad-hoc Additional District Judges, who are deemed to have assumed office from time to time, are also empowered to deal with the cases of Motor Accident Claims Tribunals for the revenue jurisdiction. It was under consideration of the Government to declare him as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government of Finance Department vide Decision No. 2013/ Q. No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, dated 17th April, 2015 and the Financial Powers, Manual of Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-I
As per the powers conferred on the administrative departments under Rule No. 3, Rule No. 2 of the Act, the District Judge and Additional Sessions Judge and ex-officio members, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Shrigonda, District Ahmednagar are declared as" Office Head" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." The order will be limited to financial matters.
