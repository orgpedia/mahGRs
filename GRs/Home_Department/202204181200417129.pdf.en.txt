Introduction: In rural areas, after the rainy season, after the completion of agricultural work, village trips begin. To keep the atmosphere of fun and frolic for the entertainment of this journey, the farmers organize bullock carts in the traditional way. This type of horse racing has a long tradition.
Organizing bullock cart races connects small and big businessmen of rural areas with this race, the turnover from it provides a means of income to all of them. Attractive prizes will be given to the winners of the races. This is considered to be a matter of great prestige for the owners of bulls in rural areas. In bullock cart races, a good breed of bullocks used for racing is bought and sold in large numbers. As much of the state is drought-prone, bullock cart racing in the region also promotes domestic tourism.
In respect of permitting bullock cart racing, the Central Government has amended the" Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960" vide Maharashtra Act No. 45 of 2017. It has been published in the Gazette on 31/07/2017. However, animal-friendly organizations in the state have demanded a ban on bullock cart racing in the state as bullock cart owners are cruelly treated for speeding bulls in bullock cart racing.
