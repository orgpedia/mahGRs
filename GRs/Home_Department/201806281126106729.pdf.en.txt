GOVERNMENT DECISION: To look into the matter of Anti-Terrorism Squad, Kalachowki Police Station, Mumbai No. 02/2018 on behalf of the Government, Adv. Mr. Vaibhav Bagade has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor vide order dated 01.03.2018 in the aforesaid context of Department of Law& Justice. Therefore, in this case, the government is approving the payment of fees to Advocate Mr. Vaibhav Bagade on a daily basis as follows.
a) Effective hearing fee per day is Rs. 10,000/- (including travel and accommodation expenses)
(2) The days of effective hearing shall be certified by an officer not below the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case and shall not provide any additional time. Further, in the present case, in addition to the aforesaid fee, no unreasonable hearing fee or any other kind of fee or allowance shall be admissible to Adv. Vaibhav Bagade.
The expenditure will be met from the approved grant for the financial year under the head of Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATG) Heading B-1, 2055 Police- (00) (101), Crime Investigation and Vigilance (00) (05), Anti-Terrorism Squad (20550571)-28 Professional and Special Services, as per the monthly fund statement.
3. This Government decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 166/18/ Expenditure-7, dated 29.05.2018 is being issued as per the consensus arrived at.
