The Proposal:
The implementation of Mumbai City Women Safety Initiative Scheme, a Centrally and State sponsored scheme, has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 31.01.2019. The Central Government has allotted Rs. 252 crore has been approved and the share of Central Government and State Government is 60:40 respectively. Under this scheme, Rs. Administrative Sanction has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 14.05.2021 for procurement of machinery/ equipment worth Rs. 147.48 crore. In pursuance of this administrative approval, Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai vide his letter dated 16.09.2021 has submitted a proposal to the Government for sanction of funds to the tune of Rs. 32.94 crore for purchase of total 4 types of machinery/ equipment.
A request has been made to the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India vide letter dated 03.09.2021 for inclusion of Forensic Evidence Collection System proposed by Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai under Mumbai City-Women Safety Initiative Scheme. The approval of the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India in the matter of Forensic Evidence Collection System is still pending. Therefore, a proposal to approve the disbursement and expenditure of funds amounting to Rs. 31.87 crore for the purchase of the remaining 3 types of machinery/ equipment, excluding the Forensic Evidence Collection System, is under consideration of the Government.
