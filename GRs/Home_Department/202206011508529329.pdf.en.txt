The Proposal:
Read No. 1 The e-governance policy of the state has been announced under the government decision here.
As per para 12.3 of the said Government Decision, the concerned administrative departments have given guidelines for the formation of Project Implementation Committees. Read here. The composition of the Project Implementation Committee is prescribed under Section 2. Accordingly, the Project Implementation Committee has been formed to implement the computerization and e-governance system in the sub-division under the Home Department, the Office of the Director General of Police, the Commissionerate of Police and the field offices under them. 3 has been fixed. Considering the sensitive and urgent nature of the work in the Home Department and the various subdivisions, read about the formation of subdivision-wise project implementation committees in line with the rationalization of work. 4 have been reported. Accordingly, the matter of forming a Project Implementation Committee for the e-governance project for the Jail Sub-Division under the Home Department was under the consideration of the Government.

Powers and functions of the committee
(i) To formulate a policy in line with the e-Governance policy of the Directorate of Information Technology.
To define the scope of all the e-governance projects to be implemented by the Jail Sub-Division and to guide the tender process by finalizing the RPF (Request for Proposal) to start the e-governance project for the department.
(iii) To take decision for procurement of hardware/ software required for implementation of e-Governance projects in the prison sub-division.
4. Review of e-Governance projects in Jail Sub Divisions. Selection of Consultants from reputed organization as per requirement for e-Governance projects to be implemented in the Department or Subordinate Office.
The Project Implementation Committee of the Jail Sub-Division shall have the powers as per the decisions taken by the General Administration Department (Information Technology) from time to time as well as the powers conferred.
