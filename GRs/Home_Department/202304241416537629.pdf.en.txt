Preamble: The Government servants and their family members are entitled to receive Rs.1,000/- as per the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and Government Orders issued from time to time. Reference No. 8 to the Director General of Police and the Additional Director General of Police provides for the powers of the Head of the Administrative Department of the Ministry to approve or take appropriate decisions in cases of reimbursement of medical expenses above Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh only).
The Maharashtra Police is the main force responsible for maintaining law and order in Maharashtra. Department of Home Affairs, Govt.
MIS-1315/ Q.No.358/ Pol-4, Dy. As on 14.03.2016, all Superintendents of Police and Commandants have been declared as" Head of Department" for financial powers. However, the Superintendent of Police and the Sub-Inspector have not been empowered to sanction medical reimbursement. The Government has decided to provide immediate settlement of medical dues to the Government employees and their family members subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and Government Orders issued from time to time thereafter. As on 20.04.2021, the powers of the Head of the Administrative Department of the Ministry to approve or take appropriate decisions in cases of reimbursement of medical expenses above Rs. 3,00,000/- have been delegated to the Director General of Police and Additional Director General of Police. Reference No. The matter of amending the financial powers of the Director General of Police and the Additional Director General of Police to sanction reimbursement of medical expenses as proposed under Section 9 was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: RME 0217/ PR No.40/ Pol-5B
The Government have decided to make the following changes in the financial powers subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and the Government Orders issued from time to time.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | the Competent Authority                                                                             | the rights of the former                             | The amended rights                                                                                    |
| 1.   | Director General of Police (DGP)/ Additional Director General of Police                             | Cases above Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs only) | Cases above Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakhs only)                                                   |
| 2.   | the Special Inspector General of Police/ Commissioner of Police/ Deputy Inspector General of Police | Cases upto Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs only)  | Cases above Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs only) but up to Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakh only) |
| 3.   | the Superintendent of Police/ Commandant                                                            | indifferent                                          | Rs. 3,00,000/- (cases upto Rs. Three lakhs)                                                           |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Government hereby directs that while sanctioning cases at the level of Head of Department (Director General of Police)/ (Additional Director General of Police rank)/ Special Inspector General of Police/ Commissioner of Police/ Deputy Inspector General of Police/ Superintendent of Police/ Commandant as mentioned above, it shall be the responsibility of the competent authority to strictly examine and approve the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and the Government Orders issued from time to time.
(3) This Government decision should also apply to pending cases, but the cases decided earlier should not be revived.
This order is issued by the Department of Public Health. Reference No. 503-A/ Rakavi-2, Dy. being issued with the consent given under 01.03.2023.
