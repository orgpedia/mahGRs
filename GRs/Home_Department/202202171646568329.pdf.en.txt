Introduction: Due to the increasing population of Thane, Mira-Bhayander, Vasai, Dombivali and Kalyan, the traffic in the city is under tremendous pressure and the problem of traffic congestion is increasing. Even today there are places in these districts where one has to travel a long distance by road and in return one has to waste a lot of time and fuel. However, there is an internal waterway and providing water transport services on this route will help in reducing traffic congestion. Also, it can be of great benefit to the coastal citizens/ tourists, providing employment opportunities to the locals. With this vision, an eco-friendly water transport project from Vasai to Kalyan (National Waterway-53) in Vasai creek will be taken up.
Accordingly, the National Highway No. The proposal for the proposed works to start shipping under 53 was submitted by the Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Maritime Board and the matter of approval of the proposal was under consideration of the government.
