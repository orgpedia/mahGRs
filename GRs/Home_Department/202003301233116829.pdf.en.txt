Government Decision: A total of 1168 posts under the control of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved. Further, as per the Government Decision No.D.P.-2012/ PR No. 112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway. Out of which, 129 temporary posts of Assistant Director and Public Prosecutor in 6 District Offices under the control of Deputy Director, Government Grievances, Nagpur Range, Nagpur along with Annexure-B have been notified vide Home Department Government Resolution No. The date has been extended upto 29.02.2020 w.e.f 05.10.2019. Now, the Government hereby grants extension of the period from 01/03/2020 to 31.08.2020 in respect of 129 temporary posts in Annexure-H. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than 6 months. Also, the extension of tenure will not be applicable to the posts which the government has already ordered to abolish.
2. High Level Committee of Secretaries to review the posts in the establishment of Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. Approved on 13.01.2005, the above figure of 1168 posts has been prescribed as per the Government Decision dated 12.10.2010 in Reference No. 1. This figure includes these positions. Further, as per the Government Decision No.D.P.-2012/ PR No. 112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway.
3. The expenditure for the same shall be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2020-21 under the head Home Department B-1, 2014-Judge 114-Legal Adviser and Counsel, (00) (01) Director Government Grievances, Computer Statistics No. 20140 029 01 Salary.
