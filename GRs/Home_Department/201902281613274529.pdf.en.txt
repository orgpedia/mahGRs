the prelude
In pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Commissioner of Police, Mumbai, for the creation of a new unit called Mounted Cops with 30 horses to deal with the law and order situation, it was under the consideration of the Government to create a new unit called" Mounted Cops" from the available manpower and approve the recurring and non-recurring expenditure incurred thereon. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
A new unit of Mounted Police with 30 horses is being approved for the Mumbai Police force. Also, an expenditure of Rs. 82,46,724/- and non-recurring expenditure of Rs. 1,16,83, 864/- is being approved.
A total of 38 officers/ staff including 1 Sub-Inspector of Police, 1 Assistant Sub-Inspector of Police, 4 Constables and 32 Constables required for the Mounted Police Unit are being transferred from the sanctioned manpower at the establishment of the S.P. Naigaon Division, Mumbai to the' Mounted Police' Unit.
The" Mounted Police" component will be under the command of the Deputy Commissioner of Police, Quick Response Team (QRT), Mumbai.
