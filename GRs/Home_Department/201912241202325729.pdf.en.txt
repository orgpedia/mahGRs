The officers of All India Police Services are required to file the Annual Immovable Property Return (IMR) for the year 2019 upto 31.01.2020 as per the provisions of Rule 16 (2) of the All India Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1968 showing the status as on 31.12.2019
The Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India vide its letter No. 45020/22/2019-IPS.II dated 05.12.2019 has informed the Government of India regarding uploading of the same on the website of Ministry of Home Affairs through online mode. This notice is forwarded to all the officers of the Maharashtra State Police Force along with the information and necessary action.
