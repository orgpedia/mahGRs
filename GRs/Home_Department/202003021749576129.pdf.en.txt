Yellowgate Post, Mumbai. In the case arising out of R. No. 20/2018, Hon. Advocate Mr. Mahesh Mulay, who has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor to look after the court proceedings in the Special MCOCA Court, Mumbai on behalf of the Government, is being allowed to pay professional fees as follows for effective hearing on a daily basis. Apart from this professional fee, no other benefit will be admissible to Advocate Mr. Mahesh Mule. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.

Discussion Fee (Rs.500/- per sitting)
