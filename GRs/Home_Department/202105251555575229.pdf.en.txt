Government's decision:
It has come to the notice of the government that the transporters in the state are facing difficulties in obtaining permission to transport ODC goods on trailers or other vehicles with a length of more than 18 meters. The goods transport is getting delayed as the transporters are not getting permission on time. After considering all these aspects carefully, the government has decided to prescribe the following procedures by formalizing the previous directives.
Methods of operation:
The goods carriers who want to transport Odissi goods from one place to another in the state or to another state outside the state of Maharashtra or to bring Dessi goods from another state to the state of Maharashtra or to transport Odissi goods from the state of Maharashtra to another state should self-certify and take action to pay the prescribed fee.
(2) The transporters shall be allowed to transport such goods after payment of prescribed charges.
3) If the transporter is found transporting goods in excess of the prescribed charges after submitting self-certification, he will be liable for penal action.
