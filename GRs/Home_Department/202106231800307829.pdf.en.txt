The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in the government service for the disabled. The central government has given. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. Social Justice and Special Assistance Department As per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021, instructions have been issued to ensure that posts in the State Government establishments are reserved for persons with disabilities. As per the directions of this decision, the government was considering to ensure the posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre in the office of the Director General and Home Guards under the Home Department for the disabled category.
Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts ensured for Divyang has been published as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. As mentioned in the notification, the posts in Group A to Group D, which have been ensured for the disabled by the Central Government, are in the state government's establishment, as well as the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the list published by the Central Government.
