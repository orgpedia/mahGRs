Preamble: A total of 8 Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been constituted for Mumbai district and a total of 119 posts have been created on the establishment of this Tribunal. The above reference No. 3 posts as per the Government Decision of 1, Reference No. 13 posts as per the Governing Decision of No. 7 posts as per the Governing Decision of 3, Refer No. 34 posts as per the Governing Decision of No. As per the Government Decision No. 5 of 31 Posts and Reference No. A total of 103 posts have been made permanent, including 15 posts as per the decision of the government. The remaining 16 posts are temporary. As per the Government decision dated 01.10.2017 to 31.12.2017. The term of these 16 posts is ending on 31st December, 2017. The Manager, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mumbai has now issued a proposal for extension of the said 16 posts vide Reference No. 8 As per the letter here, it was submitted to the government and the matter of extending the term of these posts was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following 16 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Mumbai are being extended from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018. The extension is for expeditious disposal of the claims pending before the Tribunals, submission of the last statement of the month of February, 2018 of the claims settled and pending as on 01.01.2018
