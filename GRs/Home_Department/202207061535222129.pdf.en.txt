The Proposal:
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has issued the above Reference No. 1 vide letter dated 27/5/2022, to the State Reserve Police Force, Group No. 18 (Bharab-5), Katol, District Nagpur and State Reserve Police Force, Group No. 19 A proposal has been submitted to declare the' Samadhikar' of Ahmednagar as' Head of Department' for financial matters and the' Assistant Samadhikar/ Deputy Superintendent of Police' at his establishment as' Drawing and Disbursing Officer'. The government has taken the following decisions in this regard:
Government's decision:
State Reserve Police Force No. 18 (Bharab-5), Katol, G.Nagpur and State Reserve Police Force Group No. 19 The Comptroller and Auditor-General of Ahmednagar has been given the powers under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part-I, Sub-Division I, No. 1, Rule No. As per the provision in section 2, the Government is giving approval to declare as" Head of Department" only for financial matters.
