Government Order:
Whereas, Mr. Vishal Sharad Dhume, Assistant Commissioner of Police, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar City, was suspended under the Government Order No. 1 in the context of the criminal case No. 0027/2023 registered against him at Police Station City Chowk, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar City, being under investigation;
And whereas, the suspension of Mr. Vishal Sharad Dhume, suspended Assistant Commissioner of Police, was reviewed by a committee headed by the Additional Chief Secretary, Home Department, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 2, 3 and 4 in the context of the General Administration Department.
