The Proposal:
The Supreme Court in Writ Petition No. 295/2015 to review the road safety issues from time to time. A Road Safety Committee has been constituted to submit a report to the Supreme Court. The committee has directed to create a separate road safety fund for road safety activities. Accordingly, for approval of various projects related to road safety, under reference No. 3 here. As per the Government decision dated 01.09.2016, a" Road Safety Fund Control Committee" has been set up. For this purpose. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. The Government was considering to issue a Government Resolution in accordance with the proposals approved in the meeting held on 24.04.2019.
Government's decision:
As per the directions of the Supreme Court Committee on Road Safety, various proposals regarding road safety have been approved. A Road Safety Fund Control Committee has been set up under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. The said Committee has approved the proposal for expenditure of Rs. 170,21,98, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Seventy Crores Twenty One Lakh Ninety Eight Thousand only) attached with this Government Resolution.
