As per the provisions of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, the Maharashtra Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989 have been notified by the state government. Section 68 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 prescribes the provision for the establishment of Regional Transport Authority. Similarly, Rule 61 of the Maharashtra Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989 prescribes the procedure (including quorum) for meetings of the Regional Transport Authority. However, it has been observed that the meeting of the Regional Transport Authority is not functioning as per the above mentioned rules. Accordingly, the following circular is issued.
(a) The proceedings of the meetings of the Regional Transport Authority shall be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 and the Maharashtra Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989.
(b) It may be noted that the officers concerned will be liable for disciplinary action if it is found that the meeting of the Authority is not being conducted in accordance with the procedure prescribed above.
