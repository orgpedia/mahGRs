The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is being authorized to disburse the above funds and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in his office shall take action to recover the amount and pay it to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation.
The funds have been distributed on the budgetary fund distribution system. In this case, the Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer has to apply the financial rules in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Financial Rights Rulebook and in the context of the Finance Department, No. 1 The instructions given in the government decision here should be strictly followed. The funding is conditional.
5. The report of expenditure incurred by the Controlling Officer from the provision made available to him shall be sent to the Tribal Development Department by the 10th of every month along with the information about the material objective achieved in the head of accounts/ sub-head of accounts, the utilization certificate of the fund, etc.
6. The Controlling Officer and the Head of the Department shall ensure that the expenditure is recorded in the tribal sub-plan area.
