the prelude
Under the scheme of" Modernization of Prison Security System" it is necessary to have a separate Diesel Generator System for the prisons so that the electricity supply in the prison barracks can be restored through the Diesel Generator as soon as the power supply is interrupted in the prisons of the state. 1 The matter of granting administrative approval to the proposal for purchase of Diesel Generator Set 30 kva received under the letter dated 28.8.2023 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
To modernize the security system in the prisons in the state, under this scheme, Rs. 2,61,63, 900/- (Rupees Two Crores Sixty One Lakh Sixty Three Thousand Nine Hundred only)
