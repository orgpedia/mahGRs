The Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune has informed that a total amount of Rs. 2,20,25, 381/- (Rupees Two Crore Twenty Five Lakh Twenty Five Thousand Three Hundred and Ninety One only) is still due by the Prison Department to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation. The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) was supposed to pay a part of the amount.
As per the report of the Additional Director General of Police and Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune, the Government is approving the payment of Rs 1,69,67, 741/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy-Nine Lakhs Sixty-Six Thousand Seven Hundred and Forty-Eight Only) to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation for the travel expenses of the staff and prisoners traveling for duty in the buses of the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation from the year 2016-17 to 2018-19.
