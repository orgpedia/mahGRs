GOVERNMENT DECISION: Special Government under reference to act on behalf of the Government in court proceedings arising out of an offence registered under Malvani Police Station No. 98/2022
As a prosecutor. the Metropolitan Magistrate, Borivali, Mumbai and Ma. Advocate, Sessions Court, Dindoshi, Mumbai. Pradeep Gharat is allowed to pay professional fees as follows:
Daily Effective Hearing Fee- Rs. 40,000 (Rupees Forty Thousand only) Discussion Fee/ Per-Session Rs.5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousand only) plus any other benefit other than the said fee. Pradeep Gharat will not be allowed. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.
(2) The effective date of hearing shall be fixed by the officer not below the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case. The concerned Deputy Commissioner of Police/ Superintendent of Police shall ensure that the same is checked and certified by the Court Manager and no excess is provided.
3. The expenditure incurred for this section is" Demand No. B-1, 2055 Police- (00) 108, State Headquarters Police- (02) (01) City Police Establishment- (2055 0097)-28 Professional Services" subject to the availability of budgetary provision for that financial year and to be shared from the sanctioned grant and as per the monthly fund statement.
This Government Decision is issued vide No. No. 477/2021/ Expenditure-7 of the Department of Finance. Issued pursuant to Government approval received under 12.07.2021.
