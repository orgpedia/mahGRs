Introduction: The Regional Transport Office, Mumbai (Central) and the Regional Transport Office, Pune have been asked to send the daily office mail to the office of the Regional Transport Office, Pune.
The government was considering to give administrative and financial approval to the proposal to purchase new version 2 digital franking machines, one each for the said transport offices, from the minimum (L1) service provider company from this tender.
Government Decision: The Regional Transport Officer, Mumbai (Central) and the Regional Transport Officer, Pune have been asked to procure new Version 2 digital franking machines for daily office mail. As the rate of GN Automation is the lowest (L1) in comparison to other rates, it has been decided to purchase a new version 2 digital franking machine for the said transport offices. As proposed by the Transport Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter No. 3, an amount of Rs. 2,65,336/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Sixty Five Thousand Three Hundred and Thirty Six only) is being provided as administrative and financial sanction for the purchase of two new digital franking machines.
