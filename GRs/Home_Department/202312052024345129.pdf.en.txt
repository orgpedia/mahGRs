the Proposal.
The Additional Director General of Police, Training and Special Squads, Maharashtra State, has submitted a proposal for creation of eight supernumerary posts of" Assistant Superintendent of Police" for the purpose of providing District Practical Training [DPT] to eight probationers of Maharashtra cadre of 75th Straight Service Batch of the Indian Police Service for appointment as Assistant Superintendent of Police in the State of Maharashtra from the date of their joining as probationers in the State of Maharashtra till the completion of their training period of Phase-III i.e. from 04.12.2023 to 30.08.2024. Following this, the government took decision on this issue.
government decision.
