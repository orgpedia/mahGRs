The matter of payment of professional fee to Advocate Shishir, who was appointed as Special Public Prosecutor under the said notification to handle the court affairs at District and Sessions Court, Yavatmal on behalf of the Government, was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In all the court cases arising out of the offence registered at Aarni Police Station in Yavatmal District vide G.R. No. 737/18, Mohd. The Government hereby approve payment of professional fees to Shri Shishir Hiray, Special Public Prosecutor, who has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor to look after the judicial affairs of the District and Sessions Court, Yavatmal on behalf of the Government as under:
Professional Fee for District Sessions Court, Yavatmal (A) Effective Hearing Fee per day- Rs. 37,500/- (Rupees Thirty Seven Thousand Five Hundred only) b) Consultation- Rs.5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousand only) c) Travel expenses physically d) Accommodation expenses physically and no other benefits other than the said professional fees shall be admissible to Adv. They will also not be provided with professional fees for any inconclusive hearing.
