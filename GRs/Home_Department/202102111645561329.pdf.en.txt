Preamble: Under the" Special Infrastructure Scheme (SIS), including construction of 250 Fortified Police Stations in LWE affected States" of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, an amount of Rs. 1.25 per cent of the total cost as scrutiny fee for the construction cost estimates of 20" Fortified Police Stations" sanctioned for the State of Maharashtra. 61,69,399/ has been communicated by the Superintending Engineer, Public Works Department, Gadchiroli to the Deputy Inspector General of Police, Gadchiroli Range, Mukkam-Nagpur.
The said works will be done with the grant received from the Central Government and the State Government, the buildings to be constructed will be owned by the Government, the said fee will have to be paid by the Government. With the approval of the Finance Department, exemption has been given from payment of scrutiny fee. Considering this fact, the scrutiny fee levied in respect of cost estimates of the above mentioned 20" Fortified Police Stations" is Rs. 61,69,399/- has been requested by the office of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra.
