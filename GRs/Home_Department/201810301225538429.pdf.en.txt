Under the innovative scheme, Rs. 1.25 crore has been provided by the District Planning Committee, Beed for installation of CCTV cameras at important tourist places, religious places, crowded places, major markets, important bank establishments, entry and exit points of the city and other important landmarks in Parli town of Beed district. The Home Ministry has taken the decision. As on 09.01.2017, the matter of approval of the proposal was under consideration of the Government and accordingly, the Government has decided as under:
Government's decision:
CCTV cameras will be installed at important tourist places, religious places, crowded places, major markets, important bank establishments, entry and exit points of the city and other important places in Parli town of Beed district for law and order. Administrative sanction is being given for the work at a cost of Rs. 1.25 crore.
