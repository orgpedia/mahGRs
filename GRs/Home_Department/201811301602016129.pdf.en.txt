Government's decision:
The Anti-Terrorist Squad, Mumbai Division, has registered a case vide G.R. No. 03/2006. Department of Law& Justice to look after the affairs on behalf of the Government in the High Court, Mumbai. As per letter dated 21/07/2018, Advocate Shri Raja Thakare has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor. Therefore, this government decision is being approved to pay the following fees to Advocate Shri Raja Thakre, Special Public Prosecutor in this case.
a) Effective hearing fee per day is Rs.35,000/-
Discussion Fee (per day) Rs.5,000/-
The days of effective hearing shall be certified by an officer not below the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case and shall not provide for any excess. Further, in the present case, no ex-parte hearing fee or any other kind of fee or allowance other than the aforesaid fee shall be admissible to Advocate Shri Raja Thakre, Special Public Prosecutor.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the approved grants for the financial year under the head of Anti-Terrorist Squad Heading B-1, 2055 Police- (00) (101), Crime Investigation and Vigilance (00) (05), Anti-Terrorist Squad (2055 0571) 28 Professional Services, as per the monthly fund statement.
