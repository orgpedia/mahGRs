History.
Ganesh Narayan Shinde, Poupani Driver/ 28616, Additional Commissioner of Police, Motor Transport Department, Nagpada, Mumbai is in Government service since 01.04.1991. Mr. Shinde is drawing salary in the pay scale of S-14 (38600-122800) in the rank of Sub-Inspector of Police (Driver).
Sir J. J. Group Hospital, Medical Board has declared Shri Shinde medically unfit for further service as he has been diagnosed with Paralysis/ Paralysis. Mr. Shinde was admitted to the hospital. 50% disability certificate has been issued as on 23.08.2022. A proposal has been submitted to the government to create a supernumerary post for Mr. Shinde as the post equivalent to the post of Sub-Inspector of Police (Driver) is not available in the office of the Additional Commissioner of Police, Motor Transport Department, Nagpada, Brihanmumbai. Accordingly, as per the provisions of Section 20 (4) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, in case of disability while in service, the employee shall be given posting in a suitable post in accordance with his disability and shall be given salary protection of the post holding origin. In case the post is not available, the employee will be given posting on the post by posting a higher number of posts till another suitable post is available or till the date of retirement as per his prescribed age, whichever is earlier. In view of the above provision, the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved creation of one more post of Sub-Inspector of Police (Driver) in the cadre of Sub-Inspector of Police for the period from 21.07.2018 to 30.04.2023 for providing all the benefits including maintenance of service to Shri Ganesh Narayan Shinde, Poupani Driver/ 28616 as per his age.
