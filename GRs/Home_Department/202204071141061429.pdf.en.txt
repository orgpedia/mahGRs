Introduction: In Palghar district, Murbhe is a village situated on the banks of the Satpati creek. A passenger jetty exists at Murbhe and is used by locals to travel to and from Satpati.
At present, the work of piling jetty has been taken up at Satpati. The existing jetty at Murba is short in length so that boats do not capsize during low tide. Therefore, a pile jetty has been proposed at Murbhe as passengers are facing difficulties in boarding and alighting. For the construction of Pail Jetty at Murbay, T.G. Palghar, Rs.
