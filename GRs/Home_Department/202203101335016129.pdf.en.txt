Under the relevant government circular, guidelines have been laid down regarding the timings of serving food and liquor in hotel establishments in the state as well as the timings of closing hotel establishments. Accordingly, since the tourist destination of Mahabaleshwar, G. Satara is included in the rest of Maharashtra, the time for serving food and liquor to the hotel establishments there till 10.00 pm and closing the establishments at 11.30 pm was regulated.
A review meeting held on 01/02/2020 at Mahabaleshwar in Satara district under the chairmanship of Hon'ble Chief Minister to consider the demand for extension of night life hours

In order to facilitate the tourists coming to Mahabaleshwar for religious and natural beauty, it is being approved to extend the timings of these establishments till 12.00 pm to provide food items and to cover the establishments under the control of the department.
These establishments will have to comply with the order dated 08/01/2022 issued by the Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation Department in the wake of COVID-19 pandemic and the restrictions and guidelines issued by the Government from time to time in this regard.
