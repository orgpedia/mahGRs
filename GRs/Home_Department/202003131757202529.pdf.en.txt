Introduction: A total of 375 computer sets and 125 duplex laser printers with Intel i3 processor for 51 transport offices in the Motor Vehicle Department can be purchased through GeM portal and 375 computer sets will be sold at Rs. 1,50,00, 000/- for 125 duplex laser printers and Rs. 15,00,000/- to Rs. 1,65,00, 000/- as reference no. 1 here. The administrative and financial approval has been accorded as per the Government Resolution dated 31.01.2020. Accordingly, the Transport Commissioner's Office has issued a notification regarding the purchase of 375 computer sets with Intel i3 processor for the Motor Vehicle Department from the GeM portal. Tender proceedings were conducted on 31.01.2020 but the tender process for procurement of 125 duplex printers could not be completed.
Out of the total 4 bidders who have been technically qualified for the purchase of 375 computer sets, L1 bidder has paid Rs. 1,69,25, 625/-. The department has also sent an e-mail stating that it will take 75-90 days to supply the fake computer set with Intel i3 Processor 8 Generation Specification, which has been approved by the administration, instead of Intel i3 Processor 9 Generation Specification computer set.
Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to give revised administrative approval to purchase a total of 375 computer sets with Intel i3 Processor 9 Generation specification only for 51 transport offices in the Department of Motor Vehicles through the GeM portal.
