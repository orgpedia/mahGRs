The terms and conditions laid down as on 07/9/2018 should be scrupulously followed and acted upon accordingly. Similarly, immediate action should be taken to get the revised format of the office approved by the high-level committee.
If the process of review of posts is not completed within the stipulated time, the temporary posts will not get an extension for that further period and if the question of salary and allowances of the officers/ employees arises, then the responsibility will be of the concerned establishments.
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall submit an appropriate proposal to the Government for approval of the High-Level Secretary Committee for review and revision of the sanctioned post during this period. If not, it should be noted that if the question of salary of these temporary posts arises due to the expiry of the deadline, the Director General of Police will be responsible for it.
The expenditure on this should be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2018-19 under the head of accounts mentioned in the statement 1 to 11.
These Government Decisions are being issued in accordance with the authority conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision No. Designation-2016/ Q. No. 8/16/ A.P.C. dated 7th September, 2018 of the Finance Department.
