For the above 6 candidates, Reference No. 5 here. In accordance with the Government Decision dated 26.11.2019. The Regional Transport Officers concerned are being instructed to implement the training programme as follows:
(a) The Regional Transport Officer concerned shall arrange for a Coordinating Officer/ Trainer (Assistant Regional Transport Officer/ Motor Vehicle Inspector) as per the prospectus attached for the Trainee Subordinate Officers during the said training period and submit their assessment report to the Transport Department.
(b) Any difficulty/ technical problem faced by the trainee during the training period should be resolved immediately.
(c) During the training period, the Regional Transport Office concerned shall keep a record of the attendance of the probationary officers and inform the Transport Commissioner, State of Maharashtra about their attendance.
(d) The Assistant Regional Transport Officer under training shall maintain a daily routine during the training period.
e) During this training period, the Assistant Regional Transport Officer under scrutiny will be given the date of the General Administration Department. Leave as prescribed under 13.11.2017 shall be admissible.
(e) The salary of the Assistant Regional Transport Officer under the above said purview during the said training period shall be paid through the office of the Transport Commissioner, State of Maharashtra.
