Preamble As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the General Transfer of State Government Officers and Employees up to 31st May, 2023 has been extended up to 30th June, 2023 by the Government Resolution, General Administration Department, No. On the same lines, the Government was considering extension of time for general transfers of police officers and staff in the Maharashtra Police Force to be made as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Police Act (22 of 1951).
Government's decision
In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Police Act (22 of 1951), the date for effecting general transfers of police officers and staff to be made up to May 31, 2023 is being extended till June 30, 2023.
