The Proposal:
The implementation of Mumbai City Women Safety Initiative Scheme, a Centrally and State sponsored scheme, has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 31.01.2019. The Central Government has allotted Rs. 252 crore has been approved and the share of Central Government and State Government is 60:40 respectively.
The 10 th meeting of the High Powered Committee for implementation of Mumbai City Women Safety Initiative Scheme (Nirbhaya) was held on 03.11.2022. As per the meeting, Rs. A proposal to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 115.37 crore through the BEAMS system was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Mumbai City Women Safety Initiative Scheme Nirbhaya Yojana, Rs. A sum of Rs. 115.37 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Fifteen Crores Thirty-Six Lakhs) is being sanctioned for disbursement and expenditure.
