Effective hearing fee per day: Rs.50,000/- (b) Discussion fee per day: Rs.15,000/- (c) Maximum Rs.50,000/- per day
(c) Hotel charges per day:- Rs.5,000/- (d) Travel charges:- Actual cost of travel by train

Further, in the present case, no inoperative hearing fee or any other allowances shall be admissible to Advocate Ujjwal Nikam.
