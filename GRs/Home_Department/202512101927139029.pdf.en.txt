Preface: - In the wake of the Mumbai terrorist attacks, D. dated July 24, 2011. As per the decision taken in the cabinet meeting, the decision to implement CCTV projects in Mumbai city and elsewhere in the state will be taken to simplify the process. By a Government Decision dated 27th July, 2011, Shri. A "High Power Committee" was set up under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary. The committee was authorised to take all decisions necessary to take security measures under the said Government Decision. However, at that time. As per the instructions of the Chief Secretary, the Government Decision dated February, 2017 of the Home Department has included the issue of operationalization of CCTV projects in different cities under the purview of the High Powered Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Home) to ensure uniformity in the implementation of various projects in the Home Department.
Urbanization and industrialization in the cities of the state of Maharashtra are leading to an increase in population. Hence, the installation of "CCTV systems" becomes important when implementing the concept of a "safe city" considering law and order issues, increasing crime. The Home Department formulated the policy with priorities considering the availability of resources and funds available to implement CCTV projects in the cities of the state. This includes the city of Thane on priority 3.
