Preamble: The Office Memorandum dated 16.02.2023 of the Ministry of Finance, Government of India has directed to adopt a revised methodology for effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds. Pursuant to the Office Memorandum dated 16.02.2023 of the Ministry of Finance, Government of India, the Department of Finance, Government of Maharashtra, Mantralaya, Mumbai, vide Government Circular dated 16.03.2023, has issued revised instructions regarding Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
As per the revised procedures, instructions have been issued by the Department of Finance vide Government Circular dated 16.03.2023 to appoint a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme and to open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the authorized bank for the scheme. Also refer to no. As per letter No. 1, a proposal to appoint a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for the" National Cybercrime Helpline No. 1930" scheme to be implemented under the office of VISA-3 (A) was under consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the revised modalities, instructions have been issued by the Department of Finance vide Government Circular dated 16.03.2023 to open a separate Single Nodal Bank Account in the authorized bank for the scheme by appointing a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme. Accordingly, a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) is being appointed for the projects under the jurisdiction of VISA-3 (A) Home Department.
