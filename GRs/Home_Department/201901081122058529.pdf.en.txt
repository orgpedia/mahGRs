The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, has submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for a new underground drainage scheme in the premises of Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik.
The Sub-Divisional Officer, South (WB) Sub-Division, Nashik, prepared the rates for the year 2016-17 for the said work is Rs. 3,24,02, 409/- has been submitted and the same has been attested by (i) Executive Engineer, PWD, Nashik, (ii) Superintending Engineer, PWD, Nashik and (iii) Director, Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik.
The said cost estimates have been examined by the Public Works Department, Ministry and accordingly, on certain conditions, administrative approval has been agreed to.
Accordingly, for the new underground drainage scheme in the premises of Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik, Rs. Administrative approval is hereby accorded to the estimated cost of Rs. 3,24,02, 409/- (Rupees Three Crores, Twenty Four Lakhs, Two Thousand Four Hundred and Nine only) subject to the condition that the work shall not be started without availability of funds.
