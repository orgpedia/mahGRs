Government circular
Section 25 (3) of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973. Where a regular Assistant Public Prosecutor is not available in the Court of Judicial Magistrate First Class, a Special Assistant Public Prosecutor is temporarily appointed by the concerned District Magistrate. Accordingly, Special Assistant Public Prosecutors have been appointed in various districts of the state on a temporary basis. Special Assistant Public Prosecutor He is required to attend court and other government offices. They were given identity cards.
In some places, it was difficult to identify them. Since the Special Assistant Public Prosecutor is working on behalf of the government, it is necessary for law and security to have a temporary form of identity card. Therefore, the matter of prescribing a pattern for issuing identity cards to Special Assistant Public Prosecutors was under the consideration of the Government.
Therefore, the concerned Collector and District Magistrate shall take action to issue temporary identity cards to the Special Assistant Public Prosecutor appointed temporarily as per Section 25 (3) of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 in the following prescribed format.
