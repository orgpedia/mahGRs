2. The guidelines, terms and conditions prescribed by the Central Government should be followed for utilization of the said loan funds. Also, all terms and conditions should be followed as per the government decision issued under reference number 2 in this regard.
The Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Pol-7) will be the controlling officer for implementation and coordination for the scheme of" Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment" of the Central Government, while the Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation is being appointed as the" implementing agency" by it.
