Government's stand:
Regarding fixing the maximum fare rate for private contract passenger vehicles in the state, Orders have been issued under the Government Decision dated 27/04/2018. The following changes have been made to this Government Decision:
1) Paragraph No. 1 of the Governing Decision dated 27/04/2018 should read as follows:
Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) fares are decided by the state transport authority. Considering this fare, the per km fare for a private contract vehicle for a full bus is the same as the per km fare for a full bus of the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation. The maximum fare which shall not exceed 50% of the fare rate shall be determined in accordance with the provisions of Section 67 (1) of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988. For example, the fare per bus per km for various types of buses of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation and the maximum fare for buses of private contract vehicle of that type are mentioned in the table attached. "
2) Paragraph No. 3 of the Governing Decision dated 27/04/2018 should be read as follows:
" This directive should be strictly implemented by the State Transport Authority and the Regional Transport Authority. In case of overcharging, the licensee shall be liable to be proceeded against by the Transport Commissioner, State of Maharashtra in accordance with Section 86 and other ancillary provisions of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988. "
