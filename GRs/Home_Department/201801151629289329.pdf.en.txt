General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. BCC 2011/ Q. No.1064/ 2011/ 16B, D. As per the provision of 12th December, 2011, the candidates who have been selected in the direct service recruitment on the seats reserved for certain backward classes and who have not submitted the validity certificate to the government before appointment, are being given temporary appointment subject to verification of the validity of their caste certificate. After receipt of the appointment order, such candidate will be required to get his/ her caste certificate validated by the concerned Caste Verification Committee within six months from the date of the appointment order. As soon as all the concerned candidates accept the appointment, the proposal is submitted to the concerned caste verification committee to check the validity of the caste.
The caste verification committee should submit a certified copy of the submission to the appointing authority. If the caste verification committee decides that the caste certificate is invalid, the appointment order issued to the candidate concerned should be cancelled immediately and the appointing authority should take immediate action against the candidate as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes (Regulation of issue and verification of caste certificate) Act, 2000. The Commissioner, State Excise, Maharashtra State, Mumbai will be responsible for taking necessary action in this regard. All the concerned candidates will be required to submit the proposal of caste verification to the caste verification committee as soon as they appear for the post of appointment.
The Commissioner, State Excise, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai shall forthwith submit a report to the Government regarding the candidates who will join the service.
From the date on which the above candidates appear, their Annual Confidential Reports will be written as per the rules as may be issued from time to time.
The Commissioner, State Excise, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai shall immediately make arrangements for sending the candidates appointed as above to in-service training programmes from time to time.
