Introduction- A total of 812 temporary posts in the office of the Transport Commissioner and various regional transport office establishments in the Motor Vehicle Department have been given. As per the Government Decision dated 26.03.2018, earlier, The period was extended till 30.09.2018. These positions are currently available for 2018-2019. 01.10.2018 to D. The matter of extension for the period up to 28.02.2019 is under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
A total of 812 temporary posts, including 798 posts in the office of the Transport Commissioner in the Motor Vehicle Department, various regional transport offices and 14 posts of police constables and police constables of the police department appointed for border check posts, are as follows:
