Introduction: Section 25 (3) of the Code of Criminal Procedure. Where a regular Assistant Public Prosecutor is not available in the Court of Judicial Magistrate First Class, he is temporarily appointed as Special Assistant Public Prosecutor by the concerned District Magistrate. The rates of remuneration to be paid to the Special Assistant Public Prosecutors so appointed have been fixed as per the Government Resolution dated 15.07.2013. However, since the rates of remuneration were fixed in the year 2013, the matter of enhancement of fees/ remuneration of Special Assistant Public Prosecutors was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Special Assistant Public Prosecutors appointed temporarily under Section 25 (3) of the Code of Criminal Procedure, may be appointed as Special Public Prosecutors in accordance with the provisions of Government Decision No. DPP-2012/ PR No. 117/ Pol-10D. Approval is being accorded for the following increase in the honorarium fixed as on 15.07.2013.
