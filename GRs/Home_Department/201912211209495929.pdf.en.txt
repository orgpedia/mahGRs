Introduction: The Education Department under the Mumbai Traffic Police Department conducts a number of creative awareness activities along with traffic regulation and control in the city of Mumbai every year. The Education Department of the Mumbai Transport Department has been providing training to school students in Brihanmumbai since 1957. At present, about 60,000 students from 505 schools are being trained annually under Road Safety Force on traffic control and regulation in a systematic manner.
In this training, the students of different schools are given training on traffic control and rules in a planned manner. They are also taught discipline. The students will also participate in the Republic Day parade on January 26. Certificates are also issued to the students after completion of three years course under Road Safety Force. This training is provided by the officers/ employees of the transport department.
Accordingly, the matter of administrative and financial approval of the expenses to implement the program was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Mumbai Traffic Police conducts various activities every year under the Road Safety Force with the participation of schools in the city. The expenditure incurred for the annual initiative for the year 2019-2020 implemented by the Education Department under the Mumbai Traffic Police Department is Rs. 31,93,650/- (Rs. Thirty one lakh ninety three thousand six hundred and fifty only) is being approved post facto.
