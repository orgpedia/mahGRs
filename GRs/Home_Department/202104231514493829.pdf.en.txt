Government circular:
In view of the shortage of tankers for transportation of oxygen gas for treatment of patients in the state in the wake of COVID-19 disease, it is being ordered that at least 50% of the tankers carrying both nitrogen gas and argon gas should be converted into tankers carrying liquid medical oxygen within 24 hours for immediate transportation of oxygen gas.
To convert such tankers in the state into liquid medical oxygen tankers within 24 hours, 1 Motor Vehicle Inspector and 1 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspector should be appointed for each tanker and action should be taken to convert at least 50% of nitrogen and argon tankers in the state into liquid medical oxygen tankers within 24 hours.
