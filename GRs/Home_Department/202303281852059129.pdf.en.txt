The Proposal:
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has issued the above Reference No. 1 vide letter dated 12/12/2022, to the State Reserve Police Force, Group No. 17 (Bharab-4), a proposal has been submitted to declare the" Commandant" at Chandrapur as" Head of Department" for financial matters and the" Assistant Commandant/ Deputy Superintendent of Police" at his establishment as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." The government has taken the following decisions in this regard:
Government's decision:
State Reserve Police Force No. 17 (Bharab-4), Financial Powers Rules, 1978 Part-I, Sub-Division I, No. 1, Rule No. As per the provision in section 2, the Government is giving approval to declare as" Head of Department" only for financial matters.

Also, the State Reserve Police Force Group No. 17 (Bharab-4), Chandrapur, the Government is pleased to declare the" Assistant Commissioner/ Deputy Superintendent of Police" as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for financial matters as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Clause I, Rules 15 (3) 156 and 178.
