The Proposal:
For the implementation of 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme of' Establishment of Immoral Traffic Prevention Unit (AHTU)/ Strengthening of Cell," Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India vide letter dated 29.05.2019 has sanctioned Rs. 45.48 lakhs and vide letter dated 02.06.2020 Rs. 462.00 Lakhs out of which Rs. 507.48 lakh has been received by the State. As per the Government decision dated 25.08.2021, Immoral Traffic Prevention Cell has been established in 45 Police Units in the State.
For implementation of 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme of' Establishment of Immoral Traffic Prevention Unit (AHTU)/ Strengthening of Cell', Rs. 507.00 lakh has been provided by the Finance Department. A proposal for sanctioning the disbursement and expenditure of Rs. 507.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Establishment of Immoral Traffic Prevention Unit (AHTU)/ Strengthening of Cell, 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rs. 507.00 lakh has been provided by the Finance Department. A sum of Rs. 507.00 lakh is being sanctioned for disbursement and expenditure.
