The Proposal:
There is a need to modernize and improve the medical facilities in the jails. For this, it is necessary to purchase the necessary medical equipment such as wheel chair and stretcher on trolley as demanded by the medical officer of the prison after understanding the health needs of all the prisoners in the jail. The proposal for the purchase of medical equipment for an amount of Rs. 21,96,098/- (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Sixteen Thousand Eighty Eight only) was under consideration of the Government for grant of administrative approval.
