Government's decision:
General Administration Department Reference No. 1 here. A total of 57 officers/ employees of the establishment of the Additional Director General of Police and Director, Binatari Sandesh Vibhag, Maharashtra State, Pune under this Department were temporarily transferred to the above post for 11 months or till the date on which they would have retired from service, whichever is earlier, to re-employ the officers/ employees who were appointed under the Government decision dated 29.12.2019 from the Scheduled Tribes category but could not produce the caste validity certificate or who had submitted the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes, pending court cases regarding the caste validity certificate. Since the period of 11 months of the officers/ employees who were appointed to the superannuation post had expired, the matter of extension of the superannuation and above appointments to such services was under consideration of the Government.
Reference No. 2. 4 here. In respect of caste validity certificate issued by the Additional Director General of Police and Director, Binatari Sandesh Vibhag, Maharashtra State, Pune, under the Government Order dated 21.12.2020, to the candidates who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribe category but have not been able to produce the caste validity certificate or have submitted the caste validity certificate of Other Backward Classes.

A total of 57 supernumerary posts have been created on the establishment of Additional Director General of Police and Director, Binatari Sandesh Vibhag, Maharashtra State, Pune for reinstatement of officers/ staff pending court cases. Anil Madhukar, Assistant Sub-Inspector (Radio Mechanics), Group-C. Retired as on 31.07.2020. Now, in addition to them, the number of other 56 officers/ employees in the accompanying return-1 has been transferred to the number of posts and the number of posts created for the transfer of services has been increased by 11 months. With effect from the date of termination of service on 30.11.2020, a number of posts transferred to such services and above are being temporarily extended for a further period of 11 months or till the date on which such officers/ staff would have retired, if they had continued in service, or till the recommendations of the Study Group are received by the Government and a final decision is taken, whichever is earlier.
The expenditure on the salary of the superannuated officer/ employee shall be covered under the head of salary accounts of the department/ office in which the officer/ employee is presently working and shall be met from the grants of the relevant financial year.
