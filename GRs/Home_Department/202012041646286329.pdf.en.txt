Due to the temporary promotion, the concerned officer will not get any rights of regular promotion i. e. seniority.
The ad-hoc promotion to the posts falling vacant under the direct services quota will be available to the candidates for 11 months or till the date of appointment of the candidates by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, whichever is earlier. In the meantime, if the candidates of the Public Service Commission are available, the officer who has been promoted ad hoc will be demoted. This promotion is temporary in nature and the officer concerned will not have the right of permanent retention in this post.
These orders will come into effect immediately.
