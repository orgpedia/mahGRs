Government's decision:
A large number of terrorists, naxals, organised criminals and those involved in serious crimes are lodged in jails in Maharashtra. They are trying to create a threat to the security inside the jail by conspiring to escape from the jail, attacking the staff. To extract information from an inmate inside such a prison, a secret service fund is needed. For this Rs. 5.00 lakh under the head Head Accounts 2056 Jails (Non-Plan Scheme) (001) Operation and Administration (00) (01) Inspectorate of Jails (20560026) Dattamat 41 Secret Service Expenditure Under this head and 50 other expenses, the Maharashtra State Transport Corporation has been allocated Rs. 91,33,326/- is being approved under this Government decision.
The expenditure will be met from the 2017-18 budget.
Additional Director General of Police and Inspector General, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune is being authorised to collect and disburse the amount.
This Government Decision is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department subject to the conditions mentioned in the Annexure to the Government Circular, Finance Department, No.Finance-2017/ PR.No. 75/ Finance-3, dated 18.04.2017.
