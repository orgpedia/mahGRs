GOVERNMENT DECISION: 48 temporary posts in the office of the Regional Deputy Director of the Directorate of Complaints will be filled by the Home Department, Government Decision No. DPP-0920/ PR No. 114/ Pol-10, D. 15.10.2020 has been extended till 28.02.2021. Now, the Government is granting extension of 42 temporary posts in Annexure-B from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than six months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts where the government has already issued orders to abolish these posts.
