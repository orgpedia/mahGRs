This Government Decision authorizes payment of professional fees to Ajay Misar for conducting court proceedings as Special Public Prosecutor on behalf of the Government in the said case as follows: Special MCOCA Court, Sessions Court, Mumbai A) Daily Court Effective Hearing Fee- Rs. 35,000/- (Rupees Thirty Five Thousand only) (b) Discussion Fee (per sitting) Rs. 7,500/- (Rupees Seven Thousand Five Hundred only) (a) Travel Expenses Actual Travel Expenses (b) Security Arrangements as per Government Policy
