Proposal: - Since the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation needs funds to pay the purchase payments for new simple vehicles, the budget of the year 2025-26 has allocated Rs. 3055 0052-35. In a reference letter, the Vice-Chairman, Managing Director, Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation, Mumbai, has requested the Government to disburse funds from the budgeted provision under the "Modernization of Bus Stand and Other Facilities Programme of the Corporation." Accordingly, the issue of devolution of funds to the Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation was under consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation will have to pay a total of Rs. A sum of ₹1 crore (Rupees Fifty-Nine Crore Twenty Lakhs only) is being approved for providing the funds in cash.
