A comprehensive policy needs to be formulated to prevent adulteration of toddy, to plant toddy trees in a modern way, to fix the price of toddy shops, to determine the criteria for the number of trees and to determine the procedure for toddy license approval and renewal. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a high-level committee to formulate a new comprehensive toddy policy.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, it is being approved to form a high-level committee as follows to formulate a new comprehensive toddy policy of the State.

2. Suggest measures to prevent adulteration in toddy.
3. Suggest measures for modern plantation and growth of palm trees.
4. Determination of minimum and maximum prices of toddy shops.
5. Determination of the number of trees in urban and rural areas for a toddy shop.
6. To determine the criteria for sanctioning a new toddy shop in the jurisdiction of toddy shops where the price is very high.
7. Discuss and suggest measures to be taken to streamline the process of cutting of toddy trees, transportation and sale of toddy etc.
8. Suggest measures to improve the health and income of farmers.
Duration of the Committee: The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within a period of three months.
