The Proposal:
Sitabardi P. St. Nagpur City Gu. In all the court cases arising out of R. No. 676/2019, Hon. Special Mocca Court, Nagpur. The matter of payment of professional fee to Advocate Mr. Pravin Chavan, who was appointed as Special Public Prosecutor to look after the court proceedings in the High Court, Nagpur Bench, Nagpur on behalf of the Government was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Sitabardi P. St. Nagpur City Gu. In all the cases arising out of R.No. 676/2019, Hon. Special Mocca Court, Nagpur. Advocate Pravin Chavan has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor in the High Court of Maharashtra. St., Nagpur. The Government have decided to allow payment of professional fees for effective hearing of all cases arising out of R.No. 676/2019 on daily basis till the disposal of the cases.
