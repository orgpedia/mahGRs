Preamble: As per the provisions of the Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995, 1 Home Department, Govt. As on 09.03.2011, suitable posts in Police Force and Prisons Department establishments have been ensured for persons with disabilities.
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 1995 has been amended and the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 has been enacted.
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to determine the posts in government service for persons with disabilities. The Central Government has published the list of vacancies for Divyangjan as per the notification dated 04.01.2021 on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in. Though the designations of the posts in Group' A' to Group' D' ensured by the Central Government for the disabled are different from those in the State Government establishments or in the list published by the Central Government, the duties and responsibilities of the posts, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government service, the posts in the State Government service are to be ensured for the disabled. Accordingly, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has directed the administrative departments to ensure that the posts in their establishments are reserved for the disabled.
