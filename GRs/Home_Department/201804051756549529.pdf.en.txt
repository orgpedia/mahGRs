The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the repair of the buildings of the government residences of the police personnel in the police station, Yaval, Taluka-Yaval, District-Jalgaon, under the Superintendent of Police, Jalgaon.
The Sub-Divisional Officer, Public Works Sub-Division, Yawal, prepared as per the rates of 2017-18 for the said work, Rs. 66, 60, 633/- has been submitted and attested by (i) the Executive Engineer, Public Works (North) Department, Jalgaon and (ii) the Superintendent of Police, Jalgaon.
3. Estimates of the said expenditure have been examined by the Public Works Department, Ministry and it is estimated to be Rs. 71,51,144/- has been amended and, on certain conditions, has agreed to grant administrative approval.
4. Accordingly, for repairing the buildings of the Government residences of the police personnel in the Police Station, Yawal, Taluka-Yawal, District-Jalgaon, under the Superintendent of Police, Jalgaon, Rs. 71,51,144/- is hereby accorded administrative approval subject to the condition that the work shall not be started without availability of funds.
