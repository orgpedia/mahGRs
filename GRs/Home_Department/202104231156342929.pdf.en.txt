Government Decision: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure posts in government services for persons with disabilities. The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has issued a notification in this regard. The list of posts to be ensured for Persons with Disabilities under the notification dated 04.01.2021 has been prescribed and published on the website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in under the heading Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notification List of Post identification suitable for Person with Benchmark Disability notified on 04.01.2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions to ensure that the posts in Group-A to Group-D, which are in the establishment of the State Government, the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the list published by the Central Government, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government Service (including the posts in the subordinate offices), even if the designations of such posts are different.
