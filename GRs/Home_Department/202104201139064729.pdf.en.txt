Sri Rama Navami 2021 Guidelines.
The festival of Ram Navami is celebrated with great pomp and joy in Maharashtra.
the current year. Sri Rama Navami will be celebrated on April 21, 2021. In view of COVID-19, this year all religious festivals/ celebrations as well as all events are being celebrated in a very simple manner. Considering the present situation of the second wave of COVID-19 and the increasing number of Corona patients, the Ministry of Revenue and Forest Department, Disaster Management, Relief and Rehabilitation, Mumbai, vide its order dated 13th April, 2021, has issued the following orders: The celebration of Sri Rama Navami this year is required to be subject to the provisions mentioned in Sections 1, 2, 7 and 10. Accordingly, the following guidelines are being issued: 1. Sri Rama Navami is celebrated by people in large numbers. But this year, people are expected to celebrate the festival of Shri Ram Navami at their homes in a simple manner without gathering to avoid the spread of COVID-19.
Every year, various events are organized across Maharashtra during the celebration of Shri Ram Navami. However, all religious places will remain closed in view of the Covid-19 pandemic and devotees will not be allowed to visit the temples. This year, there should be no public celebration of bhajan, kirtan, recitation etc. or any kind of religious, cultural or social programs in the temple.
The manager/ trustee of the temple should make the facility of darshan available through online, cable network, web site and Facebook etc. if possible.
4) No procession or procession should be taken out on the occasion of Sri Rama Navami.
To prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus, the rules prescribed by the Government Relief, Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department as well as the concerned Municipal Corporation, Police Administration, Local Administration should be strictly followed. Also, if any other instructions are published after this circular and in the period between the start of the actual festival, they should also be complied with.
