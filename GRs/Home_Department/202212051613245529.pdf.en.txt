Personal Assistant/ Administrative Officer. The Provisional Service Seniority as on 01.01.2022 has been created. The General Administration Department of this seniority has been prepared in accordance with the instructions in Government Decision No. SRV-2011/ PR No. 284/12, dated 21.10.2011.
2. Additional Director General of Police and Inspector General of Prisons, Maharashtra State, Pune are requested to direct the Personal Assistant/ Administrative Officer/ Structure and Procedural Officer to be present on the spot. The temporary seniority of service as on 01.01.2022 should be brought to the notice of the officers in the rank of Personal Assistant/ Administrative Officer/ Structure and Procedure Officer (including officers on leave/ deputation) and sought information regarding their objections/ lapses. In case of any objection/ error received from the concerned Personal Assistant/ Administrative Officer/ Structure and Procedures Officer, the information regarding the issues in their representation should be examined as per the records in your office and sent to this department with your feedback within a period of one month. In case of non-receipt of objections/ errors within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published assuming the correctness of the information/ details of the officers mentioned in the seniority notice.
