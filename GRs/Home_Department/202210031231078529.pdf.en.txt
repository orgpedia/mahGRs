Preamble: Shri Chhaburao Laxman Sanap, Police Naik/ 980010, Armed Police, Tardeo, Mumbai has been in Government service since 01.01.1998. Mr. Sanap was diagnosed with brain tumor while working in government service. He has been under observation since 07.09.2013. He was later shifted to Bombay Hospital. 02.10.2013 and D. Two surgeries were performed on 08.12.2014.
The medical board of Sir J.J. Group of Hospitals, Mumbai, has informed that Sanap has 95 per cent disability. The certificate has been issued on 18.10.2017. Mr. Sanap is unable to move due to his disability. A proposal has been submitted to the government to create a supernumerary post for Mr. Sanap as there is no equivalent post in the office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai.
Accordingly, as per the provisions of Section 20 (4) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, in case of disability while in service, the employee shall be given posting in a suitable post in accordance with his disability and shall be given salary protection of the post holding origin. In case the post is not available, the employee will be given posting on the post by posting a higher number of posts till another suitable post is available or till the date of retirement as per his prescribed age, whichever is earlier. In view of the above provision, the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved creation of one supernumerary post in the cadre of Police Naik for Mr. Chhaburao Laxman Sanap, Police Naik, to provide all benefits including maintenance of service, with effect from 7/9/2013 till the date of his superannuation as per the prescribed age, or for such period as the post falls vacant due to other reasons, whichever is earlier.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approve creation of 1 supernumerary post in the cadre of Police Naik for drawing salary in the pay scale of S-9 (26,400-83, 600) for Shri Chhaburao Laxman Sanap, Police Naik/ 980010, Armed Police, Tardeo, Mumbai with effect from 07/09/2013.
2.The said post is being created on the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai.
3. The said post shall be filled up in accordance with the provisions of Section 20 (4) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, for providing all benefits including service protection to Mr. Chhaburao Laxman Sanap, Police Naik.
