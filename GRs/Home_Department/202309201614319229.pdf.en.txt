GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- The Government have decided to fill up 1661 temporary posts in various cadres in the establishment of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide Government Decision Home Department No.D.P.P. 0223/ PR.No.41/ Pol-10, D.P. 28.02.2023 has been extended upto 31.08.2023. Now, under the said decision, the Government is granting extension of tenure from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 for a total of 1661 temporary posts in Annexure-A, B, C, D, E, F, C and H. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than six months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts where the government has already issued orders to abolish these posts.
