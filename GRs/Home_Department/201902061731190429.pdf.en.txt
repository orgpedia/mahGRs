State of Maharashtra:. General elections were held peacefully in a single phase on October 15, 2014. During the period from the date of notification of General Election to the completion of the election process, the remuneration/ honorarium has been sanctioned to the police officers and staff appointed for full time physical election duty as per the Government decision dated 16.11.2017.
The remuneration of some of these police officers/ personnel has not been drawn for the financial year 2017-18. In order to pay this remuneration to the concerned, a proposal was under the consideration of the Government to spend the said remuneration from the approved expenditure for the financial year 2018-19.
Decision of the Government: To pay remuneration/ honorarium to the police officers and staff appointed for full-time physical election duty during the period from the notification of the General Election to the Legislative Assembly of Maharashtra 2014 to the completion of the election process, dated 16.11.2017.
The decision has been approved by the government. The remuneration/ honorarium of Bhagwat Ramchandra Chaudhary, the then Inspector of Police, Alibaug Police Station, Raigad and Mr. Shankar Nivrutti Kavthekar, Police Naik 31585, Trombay Police Station has not been drawn in the financial year 2017-2018. The Additional Commissioner of Police, Eastern Regional Division, Mumbai has requested the government to pay it from the grant for the financial year 2018-19. Accordingly, the Government have decided to disburse the said honorarium/ remuneration from the available subsidy for the financial year 2018-19.
