The Proposal:
Sick prisoners in the state prisons are treated in the prison hospital and the sick prisoners have to be examined and treated with the advice of the medical officer. This cost is covered by 50 other costs. Also, any other expenditure which cannot be classified under any of the targets is 50- other expenditure is incurred out of this target. As a result, the administration is not getting accurate data of the medical treatment and medical expenses incurred on the prisoners. Due to these reasons, there is confusion in the expenditure on the treatment of prisoners and other aspects while seeking grants. As a result, the inmates are not getting the required subsidy for treatment. Read this article. As per the letter, a proposal to prepare a new target of 21- Supplies and Materials- to meet the cost of treatment and surgery of prisoners has been received by the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune. Read No. 2 As per the proposal of the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune, under the consideration of the Government, it was approved to meet the expenses of medical treatment and surgery of the inmates of Central Jail and District Jail from the objective of" 21- Supplies and Materials."
