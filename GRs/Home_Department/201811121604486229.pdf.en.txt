In the State of Maharashtra, the Signature and Photography Office under the State Crime Investigation Department, Pune is headquartered at Pune and has 3 Zonal Centres at Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur. All the police stations in Maharashtra are located in this zone.
The High Court, Sessions Court, Judicial Magistrate First Class, Local Bodies, Government and Semi-Government Offices examine and photograph the disputed documents in various criminal and civil offences. And the alternative. Handwriting and Photography Department plays a vital role in providing crucial evidence to the judicial system.
The Government was contemplating creation of new posts for disposal of pending and pending cases with a view to provide timely access to vital evidence to the investigating agencies and the justice delivery system in the interest of the society by examining and photographing the disputed documents filed to thwart the tendencies of white-collar criminals.
Government Decision: The Government is approving creation of new posts as mentioned below in the Signature and Photography Office under the Criminal Investigation Department, Pune, at its headquarters in Pune and 3 Divisional Centres at Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur.
