Preamble: All the Police Officers/ Staff in the Police Training Centre of the Maharashtra Police Force.
Training is provided in a variety of services. The in-service training is given to the police personnel who have completed 10 years, 20 years and 30 years of service in the police unit office for a minimum period of two weeks. Special training has also been given to Deputy Superintendents of Police and Sub-Inspectors of Police.
is given. The training is being imparted at Maharashtra Police Academy, Nashik, Crime Investigation Training School, Nashik and 10 Police Training Centres. provided to the officers/ employees
The training fee has to be paid by all the police units to the training centers.
At present, as there is no separate head of account to meet the cost of training fee, the matter of creating a separate head of account for the purpose of this expenditure was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: A new head of account is being created for expenditure on training of officers and staff of the police force as follows.
