Every year, on the occasion of the birth anniversary of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, various programs are organized across Maharashtra. Instead, arrangements should be made to make available the programme through cable network or online.

4) Health related activities/ camps on the occasion of Birth Anniversary of His Holiness Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar (e.g.
Blood donation) can be organized with prior permission of the local administration and through this awareness can be created about diseases like Corona, Malaria, Dengue, etc. and their preventive measures as well as cleanliness.
However, while organizing health related activities, special attention should be paid to the observance of social distancing as well as hygiene rules (masks, sanitizers, etc.) at the venue.
5) To prevent the spread of COVID-19 virus in the state, the rules prescribed by the Government Relief and Rehabilitation, Health, Environment, Medical Education Department as well as the concerned Municipal Corporation, Police Administration, Local Administration should be strictly followed. Also, if any other guidelines are issued by the government after this circular and in the period between the start of the actual birth anniversary program, they should also be followed.
