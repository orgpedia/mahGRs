A proposal has been submitted to the government to create a supernumerary post for Mr. Bachhav as there is no equivalent post in the office of the Superintendent of Police, Nashik Rural. Accordingly, as per the provisions of Section 20 (4) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, in case of disability while in service, the employee shall be given posting in a suitable post as per his disability and shall be given salary protection of the post holding origin. In case the post is not available, the employee will be given posting on the post by posting a higher number of posts till another suitable post is available or till the date of retirement as per his prescribed age, whichever is earlier. In view of the above provision, the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved creation of one supernumerary post in the cadre of Police Constable for Mr. Ananda Vilas Bachhav, Police Constable for the period from 26/11/2015 till the date of his superannuation as per the prescribed age or for the period for which the post falls vacant due to other reasons, whichever is earlier.
