the prelude
The interior areas of the prisons in the state as well as the prison farm areas are vast and it is necessary to keep a close watch on the movement of prisoners in these areas and to maintain security arrangements and to ensure that the lives of prisoners are not endangered. Also, it is necessary to effectively frisk every prisoner entering the main entrance of the prisons in the state, as well as the officers and staff.
