Preamble: Installation of CCTV cameras for security and crime prevention purposes in Osmanabad city, including burial grounds, incidents of theft of ornaments, public street fights, tourist places, important religious places, crowded places, major markets, important events, important banks, business establishments and entry and exit points of the city. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore only) has been provided. A total of 60 state-of-the-art CCTV cameras will be installed at 23 locations.
The Home Department took the decision. As on 09.01.2017, the matter of approval of the proposal was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
In order to maintain law and order in Osmanabad city's graveyards, incidents of burglary, public street fights, tourist places, important religious places, crowded places, major markets, important events, important banks, commercial establishments and entry and exit points of the city, a total of 60 CCTV cameras of ANPR-15, Bullet-43 and PTZ-2 have been installed at 23 places. Administrative sanction is being accorded for works costing Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore only).
