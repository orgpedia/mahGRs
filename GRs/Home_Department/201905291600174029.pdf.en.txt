300 flats in eight buildings constructed by MHADA at Pratiksha Nagar, Sion, Mumbai were purchased for the Police Department in 2013 for use as service quarters. A proposal for obtaining administrative approval for carrying out certain repair works of these buildings has been submitted by the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai.
For the said work, the Deputy Divisional Engineer, North (WB) Sub-Division, Dadar, Mumbai, prepared as per the State e-Rate List of 2017-18, a total of Rs. Estimates for the cost of Rs. 1,78,18, 480/- have been submitted and the same has been certified by the Executive Engineer, Central Mumbai (CB) Division, Worli, Mumbai.
The said cost estimates have been examined by the Department of Public Works, Ministry of Public Works and revised as indicated in column (3) of the following table and, on certain conditions, have agreed to grant administrative approval:
