Preamble: The State Government has adopted a policy of providing 40% to 50% financial participation in selected projects so that the railway projects in rural areas, especially in the underdeveloped areas of the State, can be expedited and completed expeditiously. Central Railway has prepared a detailed project report for Solapur-Osmanabad Tuljapur new Broad Gauge Railway Project. 904.92 crore. The project will have 50:50 financial participation from the Central Government (Ministry of Railways) and State Governments. The total cost of the project is Rs. 904.92 crore i.e. 50% expenditure of Rs. The matter of approving the financial contribution of the State Government for the expenditure of Rs. 452.46 crore was under consideration.
Accordingly, the following decisions have been taken at the Cabinet level.
Government Decision: 1) For the new broad gauge railway line project of Solapur-Tuljapur-Osmanabad, the State Government will bear the entire project cost (Rs. 904.92 crore) 50% expenditure i.e. Rs. Financial participation of Rs. 452.46 crore is being approved. The cost of land (government land or other land) will be included in the state government's account.
The project is being set up by Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Corporation (MRIDC), a special purpose vehicle (SPV).
The project is being constructed by Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Corporation (MRIDC). This has been communicated to the Ministry of Railways, Government of India vide letter dated 09.01.2023. As the project will be implemented by Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Corporation Limited (MRIDC), it is requested to hand over 50% of the Central Government (Ministry of Railways) share of funds to Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Corporation Limited (MRICL). The request has been made to the Central Government (Ministry of Railways) vide letter dated 09.01.2023. After the Ministry of Railways, Government of India, approved the implementation of the project through Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Corporation (MRIDC) and transferred 50% of the Central Government (Ministry of Railways) share to Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Corporation (MRIDC) in a phased manner, the State share of Rs. 452.46 crore is being approved for MRIDC in a phased manner depending upon the availability of funds.
