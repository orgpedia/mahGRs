The Proposal -
Special Central Assistance (WEA) is a 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme under which works are carried out by the Collector, Gadchiroli. The Central Government has decided to disburse funds for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Special Central Assistance (SNA-SPARSH) through SNA-SPARSH during this financial year.
Accordingly, the SNA-SPARSH system was required to be operationalised for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Special Central Assistance. Accordingly, the Department. The Joint Secretary / Deputy Secretary, Visha-Shaw, has been appointed as the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) under the Department of Finance vide Government Decision dated 14/02/2025. An account for the scheme has been opened with RBI Bank in pursuance of the direction given under the Government Decision dated 11/10/2024.
Department of Finance. Under the Government Decision dated 23/04/2025, instructions have been issued for timely disbursement of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes through SNA-SPARSH mechanism. The issue of declaring Joint / Deputy Secretary, Visa-1B, as the Controlling Officer for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Special Central Assistance was under consideration of the Government.
