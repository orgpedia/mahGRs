Ministry of Home Affairs, Foreigners Division Letter No. 25022/33/2020-F-I, dated 28.7.2020.
Preamble: The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) is considering the issue of detection of foreign nationals who have been staying illegally in India prior to 01.01.2011 after expiry of their visas.
The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), in a letter to the States, has asked them to constitute a Task Force under the Chairmanship of senior officers of Home/ Police Departments, along with the concerned Regional Foreigners Registration Officers (FRROs) and State Police Officers, for the Immigration Check Posts (ICPs) in the State to take action for detection of foreign nationals staying illegally in the State. Accordingly, a task force of the concerned under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Special), Home Department was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: A Task Force is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Special) for Immigration Check Posts (ICPs) in the State to detect foreign nationals who are staying illegally in India after expiry of their visa period before 01.01.2011 as follows:
