The Supreme Court of Appeal No. 8928/2015 (Chairman and Managing Director, FCI and others vs. Jagadish Balaram Behera and others) and other petitions. The High Court of Maharashtra, Bombay Bench, Nagpur has ruled that protection in government service is not payable to persons who have joined government service on the basis of reservation for backward castes on 16.07.2017 and whose caste claims have been invalidated thereafter. 3140/2008. vide order dated 28.09.2018. As per the Supreme Court order. In view of the said order of Hon'ble Supreme Court, the Government was contemplating to create more number of posts for the implementation of the officers/ staff appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category on the establishment of Civil Defence Organisation under the Home Department but who have not submitted the caste validity certificate.
Government decision: The Supreme Court in Civil Appeal No. 8928/2015 and other petitions. In order to implement the decision dated 16.07.2017, the Government are approving creation of additional posts for a total of 2 officers and staff who are presently working in the establishment of Civil Defence Organization under the Home Department but have not submitted caste validity certificate appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category.
