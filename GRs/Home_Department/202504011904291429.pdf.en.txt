The state government has launched the Maharashtra Cyber Security Project with state-of-the-art technology, skilled manpower and resources, with a focus on cyber fraud victims, especially women, children and senior citizens. The project will be a state-of-the-art hub for cyber security and cybercrime control in the state. They are being asked to provide guidance and commercial services on the implementation of the project from other states as well as from abroad. The Maharashtra Cyber Crime Security Corporation (MCCSC) is proposed to be set up under the aegis of the Home Department with a view to augmenting the revenue of the state by utilising these technology-based resources.
The establishment of the Corporation will enable the State Government to ensure cyber security of its administrative departments and ultimately citizens by creating a robust cyber infrastructure in the State. Accordingly, the government was considering setting up the Maharashtra Cyber Crime Security Corporation in the state.
The Government's Decisions:
In order to improve the overall cyber security situation and safety of citizens, as well as to guide government departments, public and private organizations, and citizens on cyber security and safe cyber practices, the government is approving the formation of the Maharashtra Cyber Crime Security Corporation.
(a) The nature of the corporation: -
1) The Maharashtra Cyber Crime Security Corporation to be set up under the Companies Act, 2013 will be owned by the 100% state government.
The proposed corporation will have its headquarters (registered office) at Maharashtra State Cyber Office, Global Trade Centre, Cuffe Parade, Mumbai.
3) The authorised equity of the corporation will be Rs 200 crore. The state government will provide paid-up and subscribed share capital to the Maharashtra Cyber Crime Security Corporation.
4) The corporation will be administered in the first year from the initial share capital provided by the government. The revenue income received by the corporation thereafter will be used to meet the ensuing administrative expenses.
5) Since the proposed corporation is in the early stages of business, the company will use the paid-up capital for its URS operations and business growth.
6) The decision of the government. The Maharashtra Cyber Cell set up under 05.01.2016 will continue to function independently.
