Preamble: 1 (one) temporary post in the office of the State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai may be referred to as Reference No. The date was extended from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. The term of office of 1 (one) Constable has expired as on 31.08.2020.
The In-Charge Manager, State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has now issued a proposal for further extension of the 1 (one) temporary post of peon with effect from 01.09.2020 vide Reference No. 2 has been submitted to the Government as per the letter here and the matter was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1 (one) temporary post of peon in the establishment of State Transport Appellate Tribunal, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being extended from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. This extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending with the State Transport Appellate Tribunal are settled expeditiously, the last statement of claims settled for the month of November, 2020 and February, 2021 is submitted to the Government and if 50 per cent of the pending claims as on 01.09.2020 are not found to be settled, no further extension will be granted.
