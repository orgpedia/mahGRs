The Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune has a total of 13 posts, out of which 9 are permanent and 4 are temporary. These 4 temporary posts are mentioned in Reference No. The date was extended from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 as per the decision of the Government. The said 4 temporary posts have expired on 30.09.2019 and further extension of the said posts with effect from 01.10.2019 is being considered by the Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune vide Reference No. 4 has been requested by the government. The matter of extension of these posts beyond 01.10.2019 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following 4 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Pune are being extended from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020. The extension is subject to the conditions of expeditious disposal of the claims pending before the Tribunal and submission of the statement of claims settled to the Government after every 3 months.
