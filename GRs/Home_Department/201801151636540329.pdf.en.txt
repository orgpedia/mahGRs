Introduction: The above reference No. Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district for a period of two years. Reference No. A total of 12 new posts have been created under the government decision. Reference No. Under the Government decision, 2 additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals and 12 temporary posts have been extended from 01.10.2017 to 31.12.2017. Since the extension of these 12 posts is expiring as on 31.12.2017 and as per Reference No. 4 As the Chairman, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur, vide letter dated 28.12.2017, had submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of tenure of two Additional Tribunals and a total of 12 temporary posts therein, the Government was considering extension of tenure of the said posts from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018.
Government Decision: Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals at Nagpur and 12 temporary posts created for the same are being extended from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018. The extension is subject to expeditious disposal of the claims pending before the Tribunal, submission of the final returns of the settled claims for the month of December, 2017 and February, 2018 to the Government, and no further extension of time if 50% of the pending claims are not found to be settled by the end of December, 2017.
