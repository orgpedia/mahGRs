Subject:- Administrative approval for the construction of a new administrative building for the Police Station, Koparkhairane, on the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Navi Mumbai, through CIDCO.
Reference:- Letter from the office of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, No. POMS/ 27-B/ 28-B-2/ Koparkhairane-CIDCO-Navi Mumbai/ 342/ 2018, dated 04.10.2018.
Memorandum for construction of new administrative building for Police Station, Koparkhairane, on the establishment of Commissioner of Police, Navi Mumbai, by CIDCO at Sector 20, Koparkhairane, Navi Mumbai City. 7 (1939.615 sq.m. area) has been allotted on lease to the Police Department. The possession of the said plot has been taken on 06.10.2008 and the Director General of Police, M.R.R., has paid Rs. 1,05,826 has been paid.
CIDCO has constructed administrative buildings of Police Station, Kamothe, Police Station, Kharghar and NRI Marine Police Station on the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Navi Mumbai, at its own cost. Similarly, the Board of Directors of CIDCO in its meeting held on 17.12.2014 decided to construct a new administrative building for the Police Station, Koparkhairane.
The Director General of Police, M.R., has therefore submitted a proposal to obtain administrative approval for the construction of a new administrative building for the Police Station, Koparkhairane.
Accordingly, approval is hereby accorded for the construction of a new administrative building for the Police Station, Koparkhairane, on the establishment of the Commissioner of Police, Navi Mumbai, by CIDCO, on the following conditions: 1) No funds or resources will be provided by the Government (Home Department) for the said construction.
2) The said construction is being carried out by Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Limited. It should be done according to the map and standards.
