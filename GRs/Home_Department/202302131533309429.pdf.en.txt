The Proposal:
The Ministry of Finance, Government of India vide its Office Memorandum dated 09.03.2022 has directed to adopt a revised methodology for effective management of funds of Central Sector Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds. Pursuant to the Office Memorandum dated 09.03.2022 of the Ministry of Finance, Government of India, the Department of Finance, Government of Maharashtra, Ministry of Finance, Government of Maharashtra, Mumbai vide letter dated 25.05.2022, has informed to take action in accordance with the revised instructions on Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS).
Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (VISA-6), Home Department, Ministry of Home Affairs, Mumbai has been designated as the Single Nodal Agency for implementation of the 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Establishment/ Strengthening of Women Help Desk in Police Stations under Nirbhaya Fund as per revised modalities, vide Government Resolution dated 02.08.2022.
Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi vide GOs dated 19.10.2022 and 06.01.2023, regarding appointment of Nodal Implementing Agency and Subordinate Agency for the 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Establishment/ Strengthening of Women Helpline in Police Stations under Nirbhaya Fund.
