The Proposal:
As per the request of the Union Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways, the formation of a Coastal Shipping Promotion Cell to promote coastal shipping in the state and to coordinate with the Central Government on matters related to coastal shipping was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In order to promote coastal shipping in the State and to coordinate with the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways on matters related to coastal shipping, the following Coastal Shipping Promotion Cell is being set up.

The functions of Coastal Shipping Promotion Cell are as follows: i. To review the current status of Coastal Shipping in the State of Maharashtra.
To study the policies adopted for coastal shipping at the international level as well as in other coastal states of the country.
To study how the various measures/ concessions announced by the Central Government to promote coastal shipping can be used for the growth of coastal shipping in the State of Maharashtra and to suggest further measures/ concessions therein.
To coordinate with the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways, Government of India in all matters relating to Coastal Shipping.
The Chief Executive Officer, Maharashtra Maritime Board is being nominated as the nodal officer to coordinate with the Union Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways on matters related to Coastal Shipping.
