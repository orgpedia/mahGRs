Preamble: In order to effectively curb the incidents of violence against women in public places and to take permanent measures regarding their safety, the Ministry of Home Affairs, Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India has approved the implementation of the scheme of Women Safety Initiative under the Safe City Project by selecting the city of Mumbai in the country and implementing the scheme. The scheme will be implemented over a period of three years from 2018-19 to 2020-21 with the Central/ State share being 60:40. The Central Government has allotted Rs. A sum of Rs. 252.00 crore has been sanctioned. Accordingly, Rs. 252.00 crore, out of which 60% is Central share of Rs. 151.20 crore and 40% is State share of Rs. 100.80 crore. Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India vide Reference No. In view of the guidelines issued under Section 3 of the Mumbai City Women Safety Initiative Scheme, Instructions have been given to constitute a state-level apex committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the government has decided to constitute the committee.
Government's decision:
To implement and monitor the Mumbai City Women Safety Initiative Scheme. It is hereby approved to constitute a State Level Apex Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra as follows:
