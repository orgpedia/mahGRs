As per letter No. 1, the Government had received a proposal to revive the post of" Assistant Manager" in the office of Maharashtra State Human Rights Commission, Mumbai, which had lapsed. He gave it. As per the approval given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 16.09.2022, the matter of revival of the post of" Assistant Manager" in the office of Maharashtra State Human Rights Commission, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
The Government has approved the revival of the post of Assistant Manager in the office of Maharashtra State Human Rights Commission, Mumbai.
