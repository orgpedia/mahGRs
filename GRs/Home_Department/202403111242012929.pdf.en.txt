The Proposal: -
A Government Decision dated 05.04.2021 mandates payment of remuneration equivalent to one month's basic pay as per the 7th Pay Commission to Police Officers / Personnel directly deputed for 30 days or more than 30 days in full time for the conduct of the General Elections to the Lok Sabha-2019. Accordingly, as per the proposal received from the Head of the Component, most of the Police officers / employees have been paid remuneration honorarium as one month's basic pay as per the Sixth Pay Commission, the difference in basic pay as per the Seventh Pay Commission being payable to them is Rs. 45,43,929 - The government was considering approving such an expenditure.
