A total of 299 posts have been created. The process of filling these posts is underway. Out of which, 125 temporary posts of Assistant Director and Public Prosecutor in 5 District Offices of Sub-Annexure-C under the control of Deputy Director, Government Grievances, Amravati Range, Amravati have been transferred to the Home Department vide Government Resolution No. 27.03.2020 has been extended upto 31.08.2020. Now, the Government hereby grants extension of 125 temporary posts in Annexure-C from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than six months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts where the government has already issued orders to abolish these posts.
The High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the review of the posts in the establishment of Directorate of Complaints, Maharashtra State, Mumbai on 13.01.2005 and the above figure of 1168 posts has been prescribed as per the Government decision dated 12.10.2010 in Reference No. 1. This figure includes these positions. Further, as per the Government Decision No.D.P.-2012/ PR No. 112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway.
The expenditure for this should be met from the grant sanctioned for the financial year 2020-2021 under the head of Home Department B-1, 2014-Judge 114-Legal Advisor and Counsel, (00) (01) Director, Government Grievances, Computer Statistics No. 2014 0029 01 Salary.
This Government Decision is pursuant to the Government Decision No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ APUK, D. being issued under the powers conferred on the administrative departments as on 31.08.2020.
