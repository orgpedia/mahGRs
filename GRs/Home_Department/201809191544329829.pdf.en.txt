GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the case of G.R. No. 281/ 2016 (Shubham Mahakalkar murder case) at Ambazari Police Station, Nagpur, Md. Adv. Ujjwal Nikam has been posted as District Judge, District Court, Nagpur. He has been appointed as Special Public Prosecutor vide notification dated 04.05.2018. Therefore, in this case, the government is approving to pay the following fees to Advocate Ujjwal Nikam every day.
A. Effective hearing fee per day-₹ 50,000/-. Discussion Fee (Per Month)-₹ 15,000/ Maximum per day-₹ 50,000/
Hotel Lodging Boarding (per day)-₹ 5,000/-. Airfare- The cost of airfare.
The days of effective hearing shall be certified by an officer not below the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case and shall not provide for any excess. Further, in the present case, no ex-parte hearing fee or any other kind of fee or allowance other than the aforesaid fee shall be admissible to Advocate Ujjwal Nikam.
3. Expenditure to be incurred for this Department vide Demand No. B-2055 Police- (00) (109), District Police (00) (01), District Police Force (20550168)- 28 Professional Services, will be covered under this head from the sanctioned grant of that financial year and as per the monthly fund statement.
