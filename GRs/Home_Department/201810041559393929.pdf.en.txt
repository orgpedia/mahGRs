Introduction: Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) provides regular passenger transport services to the people of the state, especially in rural areas. The financial cost of these travel concession schemes was initially borne by the State Transport Corporation. After this, due to a large increase in the amount of expenditure on passenger concession schemes, the expenditure on various passenger concession schemes started to be reimbursed by the government from 1997-98, at that time this expenditure was being reimbursed by the Home (Transport) Department. Thereafter, as per the Government decision dated 5th December, 2005, it was decided to reimburse the travel concession value paid to various social groups in the bus fare of the State Road Transport Corporation by the concerned departments. However, it was decided to modify the decision and give the amount of travel concession value to the State Road Transport Corporation through book adjustment from the tax received through the Home (Transport) Department as before. The Government decision dated 26/07/2006 was taken and since then the amount of travel concession value was being paid to the Corporation as per the said decision. After that. Pursuant to the Government Decision dated 10/02/2014. From 01.04.2014 onwards, reimbursement of travel concession value shall be made by the respective administrative departments issuing the concession. It was also decided that the policy decision to modify, amend, discontinue or introduce a new travel concession scheme should be taken by the concerned administrative department. However, the order.
