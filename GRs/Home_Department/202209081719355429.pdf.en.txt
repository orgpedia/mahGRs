The Maharashtra Sustainable Climate Resilient Coastal Protection and Management Investment Project (MSCRPMIP) has been approved with an investment of Rs 643.5 crore.
As per the request of the Director (ADB), Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance, Government of India, the matter of forming an Empowered Coastal Committee for inter-departmental cooperation and coordination of the State Government for the implementation of" Maharashtra Sustainable Environmentally Sound Coastal Protection and Management Project" was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: To implement the Asian Development Bank sponsored" Maharashtra Sustainable Coastal Protection and Management Project" Ma. The Empowered Coastal Committee/ Shaktiparan Sarak Committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary as follows:
