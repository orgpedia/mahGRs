Introduction: Under the decision of the government in the context, the food allowance is sanctioned to the undertrial prisoners in the police custody of the state as follows.
1) Brihanmumbai Railway Zone and other Municipal Corporations- Rs. 50/- per day for a raw prisoner in a police custody in the rest of Maharashtra. The rate of 40/- has been fixed in 2010. However, in view of the rising inflation in the last 8 years, the government was considering to revise the rates of food allowances for the prisoners in police custody.
