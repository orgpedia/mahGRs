Government's decision:
A case has been registered at the Nirmal Nagar police station. 42/2012, in the case arising out of this offence. Advocate Mr. Vaibhav Bagade, Special Public Prosecutor, Department of Law and Justice, to conduct the proceedings on behalf of the Government in the Sessions Court, Mumbai. Appointed as Special Public Prosecutor vide notification dated 16/06/2017. Therefore, in the present case, Advocate Mr. Vaibhav Bagade, Special Public Prosecutor, has been appointed as the Special Public Prosecutor in the Government Resolution No: 2011/ 249/ D-14 of the Department of Law and Justice. Under this Government decision, payment of professional fees at the Government rate prescribed as on 15/02/2014 is being approved.
The days of effective hearing shall be certified by an officer not below the rank of Deputy Commissioner of Police supervising the case and shall not provide for any excess. Further, in the present case, the ineffectual hearing fee or any other kind of fee or allowance to Adv. Mr. Vaibhav Bagade, Special Public Prosecutor shall not be admissible.
