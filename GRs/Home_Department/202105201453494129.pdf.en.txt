GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the goods transport vehicles available with the State Transport Corporations, it has been directed under the Government Decision dated 20th May, 2021 that 25% of the goods transport work to the State Transport Corporations should be given to the private transporters selected through the tender process at the rate permissible.
As per the decision taken by the Cabinet in the matter of freight of Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department, the following committee is being constituted.
Additional Chief Secretary (Finance)- Chairman
Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue)- Member
Additional Chief Secretary (Transport) Member
Secretary (Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection)
5) Managing Director, M.S.M.P. Corporation Member Secretary
The committee will submit a report to the government within a month on the feasibility of 25 per cent of the freight of the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department to be carried by the State Transport Corporation.
