Introduction: The above reference No. Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been constituted for Nagpur district for a period of two years. Reference No. A total of 12 new posts have been created under the government decision. Reference No. 3 The Government hereby extended the tenure of 2 additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals and 12 temporary posts from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019. Since the extension of the said 12 posts is expiring on 30.09.2019 and as per Reference No. 4 In a letter dated 22.08.2019, the Member, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur had submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals and 12 temporary posts.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Two additional Motor Accident Claims Tribunals at Nagpur and 12 temporary posts created for the same are being extended from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020. The extension is subject to the condition that the claims pending before the Tribunal are settled immediately and the statement of claims settled is submitted to the Government after every three months.
