Government's decision
Under the Government of India sponsored (100%) Nirbhaya Yojana, the budgetary allocation for the year 2018-19 is Rs. 20,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Crores only) i.e. 70% of the total grant of Rs. 14,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores only) to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Legal Services Authority, Mumbai, is hereby approved.
The Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Legal Services Authority, Mumbai, has approved the said Rs. 14,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores only) to be spent for the purposes of" Compensation Scheme for Victims of Rape in Maharashtra, 2014."
The said expenditure should be met from the grants available in the current (2018-19) financial year under the Home Department-Demand No.B-1, 2055-Police, 108 State Headquarters Police, (08) Victim Compensation Fund, (08) (02) Compensation under Nirbhaya Scheme (100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme) (2055 A079) (Programme) to the victims of loss or injury or their dependents, 50 other expenditure.
This Government Resolution is being issued with the approval of the Finance Department vide Informal Reference No.276/ 18/ Expenditure-7 dated 23.10.2018.
