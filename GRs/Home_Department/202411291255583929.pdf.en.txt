The Proposal:
Nilkanth Laxman Jagtap, Police Constable / 1358, was a civil servant. Joined on 23.11.1994. Mr. Jagtap was serving in the government service when D. He suffered a disability due to an accident on 19.04.2020. The Civil Surgeon, General Hospital, Ahmednagar has certified Mr. Jagtap to be 70% disabled. Mr. Jagtap, as per the rules, is the Deputy Commissioner. Mr. Jagtap is due to retire on 30.04.2031. As he has a permanent disability, a proposal to create a majority post for him was under consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
As per the provision of Section 20 (4) of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, a High-Level Committee of Secretaries has been constituted to create a maximum of one post of Police Constable under the Home Department till Shri Nilkanth Jagtap, Police Constable, Office of the Superintendent of Police, Ahmednagar is able to do the job or till the date of retirement, whichever is earlier. Approval has been given in the meetings of the 08.10.2024.
As per the present approval, the Government is approving the creation of one more post in the Subordinate Police Constable cadre of the Office of the Superintendent of Police, Ahmednagar with effect from the date 19.04.2020 to draw the salary of Mr. Nilkanth Laxman Jagtap, Police Constable / 1358 in the pay scale S-9 (26400-83600).
