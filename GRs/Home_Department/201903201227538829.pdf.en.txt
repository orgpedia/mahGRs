Government Decision: A total of 1168 posts under the control of Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been approved. Further, as per Government Resolution No. 2012/ DPP-112/ POL-10 dated 28.05.2013, 299 posts of Assistant Public Prosecutors have been created on the establishment of the Directorate. The process of filling these posts is underway. Out of these, 31 temporary posts of various cadres in the headquarters of the Director, Directorate of Complaints, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, have been transferred to the Government Resolution Home Department No.DPP-0118/ PR No. 14/ Pol-10, Mumbai. 16.10.2018 has been extended till 28.02.2019. The Government hereby approve the extension of temporary posts from 01/03/2019 to 30.09.2019. The extension will not be applicable to posts which are lying vacant for more than six months. Also, the extension will not be applicable to the posts where the government has already issued orders to abolish these posts.
The High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the review of the posts in the establishment of Directorate of Complaints, Maharashtra State, Mumbai on 13.01.2005 and the above figure of 1168 posts has been prescribed as per the Government decision dated 12.10.2010 in Reference No. 1. This figure includes these positions.
