3. Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter No. - Poms/ 30/ VP (C-60)/ Gadchiroli/ Commando Allowance/ 73/2021, Dy. 31/12/2021.
The C-60 team of the state police is tasked with conducting day and night operations in the Naxal-affected areas of the state. They have to face death every moment while doing this work. In view of this situation, the government was considering to increase the commando allowance currently being given to the police officers/ officials of the C-60 team.
Government Decision: The Commando Allowance currently being paid to the Commandos of Special Operations Group (C-60) in Naxal affected areas is Rs. 4,000 per month to Rs. 8,000/- (Rupees eight thousand only). The extension is subject to the following terms and conditions:
The allowance will be admissible to the officers/ officials working in the Special Operations Group (C-60) Gadchiroli during the period they are posted in Gadchiroli.
Commando allowance will be admissible to the officers/ officials of the Special Operations Group (C-60) working in areas other than Gadchiroli for the period of their posting in Gadchiroli.
The Commando Allowance is being enhanced as a special case to the officers/ officials of the Special Operations Group (C-60) deployed in the Naxal affected district of Gadchiroli. The hike will not be applicable to other categories. This case cannot be taken as a precedent.
