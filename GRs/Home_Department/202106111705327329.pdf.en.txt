Preamble: As per the decision taken by the High Powered Committee, Baramati CCTV Surveillance Project will include Video Surveillance System, Automatic Number Plate Recognition Cameras, Geographical Optical Fibre Network, Public Address System, etc.
Government's decision
Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for the project at a cost of Rs. 28.56 crore (including GST).
The High-Powered Committee has approved the proposal for installation of 316 CCTV cameras at 113 locations and 30 public address systems and other essentials in Baramati city within the cost of the CCTV surveillance project.
