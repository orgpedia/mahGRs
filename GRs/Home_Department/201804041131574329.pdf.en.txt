Introduction: The above reference No. Two separate Motor Accident Claims Tribunals have been set up for Nagpur district as per the government notification. Reference No. A total of 24 new posts have been created under the government decision. The 24 temporary posts on the establishment of the said Tribunal are mentioned in Reference No. 3 As per the decision of the Government, the date has been extended from 01.01.2018 to 28.02.2018. The extension of these 24 posts is due to expire on 28.02.2018. 4 As the Chairman, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur, vide letter dated 06.02.2018, had submitted a proposal to the Government for extension of the said 24 temporary posts, the matter of extension of the said posts from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 24 temporary posts in the establishment of Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Nagpur are being extended from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018. This extension is for expeditious disposal of the claims pending before the Tribunal and for the disposal of the same.
The statement of claims is being issued subject to the condition that it is submitted to the government after every three months. The details of the posts are as follows:
