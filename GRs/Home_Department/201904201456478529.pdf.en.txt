Preamble: The office of the Deputy Regional Transport Office, Akola is a Marathi Girls School owned by Akola Municipal Corporation, Akola. 21, near railway station.
It has been functioning on lease from 19.11.2015. In accordance with the above lease deed of the Sub Regional Transport Office, Akola, Mr. Girdhar Lakshmandas Harwani has given a letter to Shri. Public Interest Litigation in the Bombay High Court, Nagpur Bench No. 102/2017 was filed. In the petition, Md. High Court Sub Regional Transport Office. Orders have been issued regarding shifting to other places on or before 15.05.2019 and non-renewal of the said lease.
In pursuance of the order of the Hon'ble High Court, a private building was sought to be shifted to the Sub Regional Transport Office, Akola, as per which proposals for three buildings were received. The rent of these buildings was fixed by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Akola. Among them, Mr. Jankilal Bansilal Somani has given consent to the rent fixed by the Public Works Department for the building (ground floor and first floor) owned by him. Accordingly, it was decided to lease out the said space for the office of the Deputy Regional Transport Office, Akola, at the rate of its monthly rent and to shift the said office to the proposed building.
