The Proposal:
The Additional Director General and Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune-1 has issued orders in this regard. 01 here. As per the letter dated 17.03.2018, a proposal was submitted to the Government for creation of new posts for the office of the Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Mumbai and accordingly sanction of recurring expenditure for the said post. The proposal was submitted to the high-level committee of secretaries with the approval of the finance department. On this occasion, Ma. The High Level Committee of Secretaries in its 7th meeting held on 11.09.2019 has approved creation of total 04 regular posts for the office of Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and filling up of 02 posts of Drivers through external sources. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: A total of 4 regular posts are being created for the office of Director General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and 2 posts of drivers are being approved by the government through external sources.
