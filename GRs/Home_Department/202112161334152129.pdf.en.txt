The office of the Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, has issued a notification for the construction of residential buildings for the police officers and police officers for Dindori Police Station on the establishment of the Superintendent of Police, Nashik Rural. A proposal has been submitted for administrative approval for an estimated cost of Rs. 14,98,94, 000.00.
The said cost estimates have been prepared by the Sub-Divisional Officer, PWD, Nashik as per the State Rate List for the year 2021-22 and have been certified by (i) the Executive Engineer, PWD (East) Department, Nashik and (ii) the Superintending Engineer, PWD, Nashik.
The Chief Engineer, Public Works Department, Nashik has accorded technical approval to the said cost estimate and has agreed to grant administrative approval. From the point of view of urban design, it should be ensured that construction is permissible on this plot.
