Also Read: Maharashtra Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) Special Inspector General of Police Letter dated 23.01.2023
Preamble: The Office of the Special Inspector General of Police, Anti-Terrorism Squad, conducts in-service training for police officers and police officials, Kashmiri language training sessions, Personal Security Officer training sessions and Urdu language revision training. It was under consideration of the Government to create a new head of accounts for the training to be provided to the said officers/ employees.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has approved the introduction of a new head of accounts for training (compulsory) of officers/ staff under the Special Inspector General of Police, Anti-Terrorism Squad.
