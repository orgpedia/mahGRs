Government circular:
Sub-Division No. 2 The Government hereby directs the Deputy Regional Transport Officer, Group-A cadre officers of the Transport Department to submit their report to the Chief Minister. The Provisional Seniority Notification dated 01.01.2023 is being superseded by this Government Circular.
2. The aforesaid No. 2 In response to the objections received by the Government regarding the provisional seniority list of officers of the cadre of Deputy Regional Transport Officer, Group-A, published by the Government Circular here, the Deputy Regional Transport Officer, Group-A, has been appointed as the Deputy Regional Transport Officer. 01.01.2022 to D. The matter of publishing the revised provisional seniority for the period 31.12.2022 was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the matters considered while preparing the said Seniority List are as per Annexure-A attached and based on the same, the revised Provisional Seniority List is being published as per Annexure-B attached therewith.
3. The present amendment of temporary seniority is only temporary in nature.
4. The Transport Commissioner is requested to temporarily amend the said circular.
The names included in the seniority list should be brought to the notice of all the concerned officers who are retired and working in the government service.
5. The officers in the revised provisional seniority list should obtain a copy of this seniority list from the government website.
