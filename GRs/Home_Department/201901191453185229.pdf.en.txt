Introduction: Regional Transport Officer, Mumbai (Central). On 05.08.2018, all the records, 30 computer sets, printers, servers, etc. in the new licensee section in the" C-Block" of the office were completely gutted in the fire. The vehicle licensing operations in the office are carried out through web based VAHAN/ SARATHI 4.0 computer system developed by National Informatics Centre. Therefore, a proposal to purchase 30 computer sets from GeM portal for licensing operations at Regional Transport Office, Mumbai (Central) was under consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government: Administrative and financial approval is being given for the purchase of 30 computer sets from the GeM portal for the office of Regional Transport Office, Mumbai (Central) and the cost of Rs. 12,00,000/- (Rupees Twelve Lakh only).
