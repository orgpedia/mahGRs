The Proposal:
The updation of National Population Register (NPR) is a 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme under sub-rule (4) of rule 3 of the Citizenship Act, 1955 and the Citizenship (Registration of Citizens and National Identity Cards) Rules, 2003. Under this scheme, the work of filling up the information recorded in the National Population Register in the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation area in 2016-17 has been done by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation in the computer system.
