The Proposal:
As the Pragati Building, which houses the staff in the area of Byculla District Jail, is dangerous to live in, the engineer, Worli, Mumbai, has taken action to repair the building. 1,20,81, 146/- Proposal for Administrative Sanction and Funding Proposal The Budget proposes to provide Rs. 4,99,220/- if the amount is shown as excess in the Centage Charges, the amount is reduced from the original amount to Rs. 1,15,81, 926/- has been recommended by the Additional Director General of Police, Inspector General of Prisons and Correctional Services, Pune.
The rates for the above works are based on the schedule of 2017-18 and administrative sanction for the works is proposed at a budget of Rs. 115.82 lakhs. The audited and certified budget of Rs. 115.82 lakhs by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Worli, Mumbai has been submitted by the Additional Director General and Inspector General of Police, Prisons and Correctional Services, Maharashtra State, Pune for administrative approval of the Government. The proposal has been considered by the Public Works Department after including contingency cost, centage charges and GST. It has been agreed to give administrative approval for the expenditure of Rs. 1,20,81, 146/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Lakh Eighty One Thousand One Hundred Forty Seven only).
