The proposal-
The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai has submitted a proposal to open a separate head of account for the scheme" Maharashtra Police Family Health Scheme" through a reference letter. Accordingly, the Finance Department was considering a proposal to provide separate head of accounts for the Maharashtra Police Family Health Scheme, for the office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai and the Director General of Police. The government has already taken the decision in this regard.
Government's decision:
The Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai has submitted a proposal to open a separate head of account for the scheme" Maharashtra Police Family Health Scheme" and accordingly, the Finance Department has provided a separate head of account for the" Maharashtra Police Family Health Scheme" for the office of the Commissioner of Police, Brihanmumbai and the Director General of Police.
