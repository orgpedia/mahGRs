The proposal-
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, vide letter dated 22.03.2017, had submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for construction of Child Care Centre for Women Police Officers/ Staff at Police Station, Andalgaon under the Superintendent of Police, Bhandara. Accordingly, the Government Memorandum, Home Department, No. Poprama-0417/ Q.No.250/ Pol-7, dated 08.08.2017, Rs. Administrative approval was accorded for an estimated cost of Rs. 25,94,645/-.
However, the Maharashtra State Police Housing and Welfare Corporation Limited, Mumbai is in the process of constructing a new administrative building for the Police Station, Andalgaon.
Considering the aforesaid facts, the Government has taken the following decision.
government decision.
Government Memorandum, Home Department No. POPRAMA-0417/ Q.No.250/ POL-7 dated 08.08.2017 is hereby cancelled.
