Government circular:
Inspector of Motor Vehicles Department. The final service attendance list of 01.01.2013 has been submitted to the Office of the Transport Commissioner. The circular dated 11.04.2014 has been published. After that, the seniority of the proposed cadre was not published and the government decided on the service matters of the proposed cadre. As per 14.11.2008, being dealt with at the Government level, the Motor Vehicle Inspector Cadre of the Govt. 01.01.2014, Dy. 01.01.2015, Dy. 01.01.2016, Dy.
01.01.2017, 01.01.2018 and The Government is contemplating to publish the year wise seniority as on 01.01.2019. Accordingly, the matters considered while preparing the said provisional seniority list are as per Annexure-" A" annexed hereto and the said provisional seniority list is being published on the basis of Annexure-" B."
2. The present seniority is temporary in nature.
The Transport Commissioner is requested to bring this circular to the notice of all the concerned officers who are involved and working in the accompanying seniority notice.
If there are any objections regarding this seniority list, then submit your representation to the Transport Commissioner and the government within a month. Any submissions received after this time will not be considered.
5.The officers working in the seniority list should get a copy of the seniority list from the government website.
