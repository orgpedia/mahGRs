Government circular:
Road Safety Week is observed every year throughout the country to effectively prevent motor vehicle accidents and to promote and disseminate traffic rules among the citizens. Along with the Road Safety Week of the Central Government, the" Road Safety Fortnight" is also celebrated in the state. With the slogan" Road Safety- Jeevan Raksha." from April 23, 2018. The 29th Road Safety Mission-2018 is being observed in the State from 05.05.2018.
Under the Road Safety Campaign, various programs are organized for publicity, awareness and public awareness for road safety in collaboration with the Transport Department, Traffic Police Department, Health Department, Public Works Department, School Education Department and all other related organizations/ organizations.
2. The details of activities to be undertaken by the concerned departments under the Road Safety Mission to create awareness about road safety as mentioned above are given in the attached prospectus.
The heads of the concerned departments shall ensure strict implementation of the said action plan/ guidelines.
District Road Safety Committees have been constituted for each district of the State.
The committee should hold a meeting of the committee on the implementation/ planning of the Road Safety Campaign-2018 in the district and give directions to the concerned and make all-out efforts to make the campaign a success.
The Commissioner of Transport, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and the Additional Director General of Police (Traffic), State of Maharashtra, Mumbai shall act as coordinators for the implementation of this programme and submit an action taken report to the Government.
