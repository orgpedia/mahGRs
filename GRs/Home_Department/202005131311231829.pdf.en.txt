(1) General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. LPL-2017/ PR No. 21/ K. 15, dated 10.06.2019 2) Home Department Government Resolution No. MVD 0719/ P.No. 171/ Para-4, Dy. 02.08.2019 3) Meeting of the Departmental Review Committee. 13.02.2020 Preamble- As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 62 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, it is the policy of the Government to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them from Government service prematurely. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 here. The Government Decision dated 10.06.2019 prescribes the procedure and general instructions regarding Service Review. Accordingly, for the review of services of 1) Group-A (Gazetted) (Senior Pay Scale) Officers in the cadre of 7600 and above but less than 10000, 2) Group-A (Gazetted) (Junior Pay Scale) Officers in the cadre of 7600 and less than 7600 and Group-B (Gazetted) Officers in the Motor Vehicles Department, Reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 02.08.2019, a Departmental Review Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Transport).
The Departmental Review Committee under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Transport) has approved the proposal to review the services of Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Group-B Cadre Officers in the Motor Vehicle Department at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. Presented in the meeting dated 13.02.2020. As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee, which evaluated the service of the officer, the government was considering to approve the officers who have been found eligible to remain in service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.

GOVERNMENT DECISION As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee in its meeting held on 13.02.2020, the services of Assistant Regional Transport Officer, Group-B Cadre in the Motor Vehicles Department, as mentioned in the attached form, are approved to be continued at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
The service book of the officers mentioned in the attached form should be taken on record that they have been approved to continue their service after 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
