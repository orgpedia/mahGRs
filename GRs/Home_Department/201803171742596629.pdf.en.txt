Memorandum-
The Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has constituted a State Reserve Police Force, Group No. A proposal has been submitted to get administrative approval for repairing internal roads in 11, Balegaon, Taluka-Thane, District-Thane.
Assistant Engineer, Grade-I, Public Works Sub-Division No. 2. 5, Thane, prepared as per the 2017-18 tariff of Rs. 2,51,06, 511/- has been submitted to the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department No. 1, Thane, has testified.
3. Estimates of the said expenditure have been examined by the Public Works Department, Ministry and it is estimated to be Rs. 2,37,14, 972/- as amended and accordingly agreed to grant administrative approval, subject to certain conditions.
Accordingly, the State Reserve Police Force, Group No. 11, Balgaon, Taluka-Thane, District-Thane, Rs. 2,37,14, 972/- is hereby accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions: 1) The Superintendent Engineer, Public Works Department, Thane, and the Commandant, State Reserve Police Force, Group No. 11. They should certify the cost estimates; 2) Work should not be started without availability of funds.
