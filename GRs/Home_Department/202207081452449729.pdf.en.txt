Preamble: For effective implementation of the Maharashtra Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial Institutions) Act, 1999 and to assist in the investigation of cases filed under the Act, a total of 12 posts of 2 independent competent authority and 10 ancillary staff under the administrative control of the Collector and District Magistrate Office, Mumbai City. 5 have been created by the decisions of the government. As these posts are required for the implementation of the Maharashtra Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial Institutions) Act, 1999, the following posts are required to be filled up: The extension was granted in accordance with the government's decisions No. 6-8 and No. 10. As there was a need for these 12 posts, the proposal for their extension was submitted to the high-level secretary committee. As per the approval given by the high-level secretary committee, the matter of extending the tenure of these 12 posts was under consideration of the government.
