The order has been issued in pursuance of the said Government decision dated 29th June, 2019 regarding ad-hoc promotion from the cadre of Superintendent, Jail Division, District Jail Class-II/ Deputy Superintendent, Central Jail to the cadre of Superintendent, District Jail, Class-I/ Additional Superintendent, Central Jail.
