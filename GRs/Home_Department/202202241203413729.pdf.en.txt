The Proposal:
As requested by the Transport Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, vide letter No. 3 above, the Government was in the process of giving administrative and financial approval for the expenditure incurred on training of the first batch of 150 newly appointed Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors in the Motor Vehicles Department at Maharashtra Guptavarta Prabodhini, Pune.
Government's decision:
The first batch of 150 Assistant Motor Vehicle Inspectors of the Motor Vehicle Department at Maharashtra Guptavarta Prabodhini, Pune will be trained for eight weeks. 84,00,000/- (Rupees Eighty Four Lakhs only) is the administrative and financial sanction.
