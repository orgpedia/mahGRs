The Proposal:
The programme of construction of cement embankments in taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under these programmes, funds are provided to taluks/ villages in scarcity situations and river rejuvenation programmes and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan by sanctioning cement embankments.
In pursuance of the demand for funds for the cement embankment completed under River Rejuvenation in Osmanabad district, a total of Rs. A sum of Rs. 91.23 lakhs (Rupees Ninety One Lakh Twenty Three Thousand) was under consideration for disbursement.
Government's decision:
During 2018-19, the Cement Dam Programme Account head was 4402 2799 with an outlay of Rs. A sum of Rs. 80.00 crore (Rupees Eighty Crore) has been budgeted. Under the River Rejuvenation Programme, the completed cement dam in Osmanabad district of the sanctioned embankment has been constructed at a total cost of Rs. 91.23 lakhs (Rupees One Ninety Five Lakh Twenty Three Thousand) is being sanctioned as follows:

The Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune has been declared as the Controlling Officer and the Executive Engineer of the concerned district as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending the funds being made available for the construction of the cement dam. The Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune shall take care of the expenditure on the targets allocated and the Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune is being authorized to disburse the funds to the concerned.
The funds being disbursed are subject to the following conditions:
3.1 If the above Cement Bond is funded by another instrument, the funds being distributed by the said Government Decision shall not be withdrawn or distributed.
3.2 The amount should not be more than the administrative approval or the price approved by the government.
3.3 If the funds provided remain unutilized for any reason, the spent funds should not be used for any other purpose.
3.4 Cement Bonds- If the funds being made available to it remain unutilized, it should be returned to the government immediately.
3.5 The full utilization certificate and the certificate of completion of the work given so far should be submitted to the government immediately.
The approved funds should be spent under the following head of accounts.
" Demand No. ZH-5, 4402 Capital Expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation, 102 Soil Conservation, (02) Cement Dam (Check Dam) Programme, (4402 2799), 53 Major Constructions
The said Government Decision is being issued pursuant to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide Circular dated 02.04.2018 in the context of Finance Department.
