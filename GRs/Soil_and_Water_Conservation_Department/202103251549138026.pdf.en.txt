Introduction Cement concrete embankments with 8 gates are proposed on a local drain in Dindori taluka of Nashik district. The total cost of the project is estimated at Rs 8 crore. With an inflow of Rs. 2.5291 crore, these works will create a reservoir of 201.66 mm and submerge 84 hectares. Accordingly, a proposal for granting administrative status to the cement works with gates was submitted to the government through a reference letter. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: The total cost of the 8 Door Cement Builder Schemes in Dindori Taluka of Nashik District is Rs. 2,52,91, 019/- (Rupees Two Crores, Fifty Two Lakhs Ninety One Thousand Nineteen only), subject to the following terms and conditions.
