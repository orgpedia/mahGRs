According to the proposal submitted to the government, this scheme is being implemented in Burhanpur. Akkalkot is proposed to be constructed on a flowing local canal at a distance of 1.5 km from Solapur. The actual Pazar Lake is 460 m long and 8.75 m high. This Pazar lake has been constructed by ROHO in 1985. The proposed storage capacity of the reservoir is 418 cubic feet and the irrigated area is 98 hectares. Accordingly, a proposal for granting administrative status to the converted storage tank was submitted to the government through a letter. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The total cost of the converted storage tank at Mauje Burhanpur in Akkalkot Taluka of Solapur district is Rs. 6,50,60, 170/- (Rupees Six Crores, Fifty Lakhs Sixty Thousand One Hundred and Seventy only).
