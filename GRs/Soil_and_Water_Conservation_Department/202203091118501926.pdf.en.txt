In order to restore the irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented under the relevant Schedule No. 1. Pazar Lake Yelavan Jugai, Pazar Lake Panudre Tekoli, Pazar Lake Talavade, Ta. Shahuwadi and Pajar Lake.
Panhala G. Kolhapur 4 schemes have been completed through District Council Kolhapur.
All plans are in Priority 3. The water is being drained out of the lake due to heavy rains. A large number of trees have also been planted on the site. The plans need to be revised in view of dam safety. Therefore, the matter of giving administrative approval to these repair works was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: Under the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme, Pazar Lake Yelvan Jugai, Pazar Lake Panudre Tekoli, Pazar Lake Talavade, Ta. Shahuwadi and Pajar Lake. Proposal for revision of 4 schemes of Panhala G. Kolhapur Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the proposal at a cost of Rs. 2,94,92, 150/- (Rupees Two Crore Four Nine Lakh Ninety Two Thousand One Hundred and Fifty only) subject to the following conditions. The Scheme is being presented in Annexure A with sub-heading provisions as per the Budget.
