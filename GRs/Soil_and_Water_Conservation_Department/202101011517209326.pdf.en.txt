Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works on progress/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 300.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-5, head 4702A019 in 2020-21.
Out of this, Rs. 30.00 crore is for eradicating the irrigation backlog of Hingoli district. At present, 50% of the budgeted funds are available for disbursement. It has been decided to provide funds for the work recommended as per the proposal in the context of the Marathwada region.
Decision: Under the Marathwada Development Programme approved and the then National Agriculture Development Scheme, as per reference No. 1 Some of the funds granted pursuant to the order herein shall be credited to the C.P.B.
Government approval is hereby accorded for payment of balance liability of Rs. 9.65 lakh (Rupees Nine Lakh Sixty Five Thousand only) for Ambad, G. Jalna project.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) the funds being distributed through the Water Conservation Corporation, the District Planning Committee or any other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above projects, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) the funds shall not be spent beyond the cost of administrative approval as well as revised administrative approval approved by the Government; (c) if funds are not required for the works of the above projects, the funds shall not be distributed for other projects under any circumstances; and (d) if the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the funds shall be returned to the Government forthwith.
