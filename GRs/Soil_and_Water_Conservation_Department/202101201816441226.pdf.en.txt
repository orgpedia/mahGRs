Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune vide letter No. Question- 1/1/2039 of the year 2020, d. 10 NOVEMBER 2020 Preamble It is proposed to revamp the water conservation treatment structures in order to restore the irrigation potential of the completed water conservation schemes in the State by conserving and repairing the schemes which require maintenance and repair. A total of 178 schemes will be repaired under the Pune Circle Office, and the total amount for repair is Rs 23.49 crore. After the restoration of these projects, 6765 hectares of irrigated area and 30249.22 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for administrative approval of these works is given above. As per the letter dated 10/11/2020, the Superintending Engineer and Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune have submitted to the Government. The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune has scrutinized/ verified all the estimates at its level and certified the same. Considering the above, administrative approval was under consideration for the amendment proposal of 178 works mentioned. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The total cost of 178 repair works in Pune Regional Water Conservation Department is estimated at Rs. 23.49 crore (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
