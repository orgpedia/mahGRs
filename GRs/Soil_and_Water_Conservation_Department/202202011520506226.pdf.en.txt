Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 1 is being implemented. A total of 4 schemes in Latur district under Aurangabad Circle Office will be repaired. 41.849 lakhs. The total water storage available in these 4 schemes is 267 sq.mtrs and total irrigation potential is 86 hectares.
The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 2 submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. According to the information submitted by the Board Office, these 4 schemes were in the first priority in the reform plan and administrative approval was under consideration for the amendment proposal. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
