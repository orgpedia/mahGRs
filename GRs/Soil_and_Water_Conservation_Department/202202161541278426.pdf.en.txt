Preamble: The Government was considering the issue of disbursement of funds for the augmentation of Deogaon Upasana Irrigation Scheme, which has created 219 hectares of water in Trimbakeshwar Taluka of Nashik district, as approved in the order at Reference No. 2. The government has taken the following decisions:
Decision: Upsa Irrigation Scheme Devgaon Ta. 150.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Fifty Lakh only) for the project at Trimbakeshwar, G.Nashik is being disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions: (a) If funds have been received from the Water Conservation Board or any other source for the works of the above project, the amounts being disbursed by it shall not be withdrawn or disbursed.
(b) the expenditure shall not exceed the cost of the administrative sanction as well as the revised administrative sanction approved by the Government.
(c) If funds are not required for the above project, the amount should not be distributed among themselves for any other project under any circumstances.
d) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
e) The directions in the relevant order of the Tribal Development Department should be strictly complied with,
