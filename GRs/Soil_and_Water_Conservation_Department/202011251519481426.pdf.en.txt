The Proposal:
The programme of construction of cement embankments in taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
During the year 2020-21, Cement Dam Programme Account Head 4402 2799 involving an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) has been budgeted. Accordingly, it has been decided to disburse funds for the cement works completed vide letter dated 09.10.2020.
The Decision:
Under River Rejuvenation Programme, Rs. Government approval is hereby accorded for disbursement of Rs. 8.62 lakhs (Rupees Eight Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand only) as follows:
