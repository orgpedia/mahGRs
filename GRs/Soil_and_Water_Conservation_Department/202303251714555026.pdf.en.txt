Decision: Yavatmal District Reference No. 2 Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse a total amount of Rs. 188.70 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Eighty Eight Lakh Seventy Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues of eligible and completed survey works in the proposals.
