Proposal As per the budget submitted to the government, the converted storage tank at Hannur Ta. Akkalkot G. Solapur The scheme is proposed on a local drain and is located about 2 km from the village. I'm far away.
Storage pond Hannur Ta. Akkalkot G. Solapur was completed in about 1972 AD. The lake has been drying up for the past 10 years. As a result, the water does not flow in the channel. This is the demand of the people and the villagers. Converting the tank into a storage tank will stop the leakage from the waterproofing trench and the main wall, thereby creating the envisaged water storage and benefiting the farmers of the area. Therefore, it has been proposed to convert the Pazar lake into a storage lake. Storage of the lake
The capacity is 330 c.m. and the irrigated area is 65. Accordingly, a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for this converted storage pond has been submitted to the Government under the relevant letter.
The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
