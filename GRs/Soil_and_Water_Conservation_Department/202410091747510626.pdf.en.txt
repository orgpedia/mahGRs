Introduction: - The Adarsh Gaon scheme is based on the concept of village development through public participation. Various schemes of the government are implemented in these villages for the overall development of the villages. It mainly covers a total of 59 schemes of 12 different departments of the government. Further watershed development work is classified as core area and other departmental work as non-core area.
