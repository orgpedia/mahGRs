Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 150.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9201. Out of which, it has been decided to fund the survey works in the proposal dated 23/11/2021 as follows:
The Decision:
Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 158.49 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Thirty-eight Lakhs Fifty-nine Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues of eligible and completed survey works in the context of Aurangabad district.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) Approval of survey works, commissioning orders, survey reports, maps, field reports, etc. It should be ensured that all documents are available and all works are done as per the rules, b) It should also be ensured that the trial pit/ core logging report is completed, c) The records in the measurement book should be checked to ensure that the amount demanded is reasonable and the funds are not disbursed twice for the related works, d) No amount beyond the sanctioned cost should be disbursed, e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the unutilized amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad and shall be promptly disbursed to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them.
