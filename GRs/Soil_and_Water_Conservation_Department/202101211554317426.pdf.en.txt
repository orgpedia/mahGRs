The Proposal:
Under various schemes under the department, soil and water conservation works are carried out as per the procedures of the government's prevailing e-tendering system. However, various fiscal measures have been taken in accordance with the relevant Government decisions dated 04.05.2020 regarding the impact of COVID-19 on the economy. No further construction work will be allowed till further orders. Subsequently, under the Contextual Government Decision dated 10/11/ 20, it was approved to disburse 75% of the budgeted funds for capital expenditure, provided that
No exemption was granted for undertaking the above mentioned constructions.
Now, as per the decision dated 01/12/20 of the Government of India, the capital expenditure has been approved to be met from 50% of the budgeted fund of 75% of the budgeted fund in the financial year 2020-21 to first clear the pending dues on priority, thereafter to complete the incomplete construction/ construction works from the remaining fund and to take up the new construction/ construction works within the limit of remaining 25% of the fund of 75%. Therefore, the government was considering to issue a short-term tender notice related to the tender process with a view to expedite all the necessary procedures for the immediate and timely completion of the development works of public interest under the capital expenditure under the department. The government has taken the following decisions:
The Decision:
Considering the balance period of the financial year 2020-21, the Government hereby grants approval to reduce the time limit for inviting e-tender to the extent of 25% of the budgeted funds in the current financial year so as to expedite the process of completion of all the works required for taking up new construction/ construction works under the capital expenditure schemes and to ensure proper utilization of the available funds.
