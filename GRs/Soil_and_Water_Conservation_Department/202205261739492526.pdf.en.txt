According to the proposal submitted to the government, the Pazar Lake Rs. Barshi G. Solapur North Solapur was completed in 1972. The reservoir has a storage capacity of 259 cusecs and an irrigation area of 60 cusecs. That's so much. The planned converted storage pond is Rs.
The project is located on a local drain near Rui village in Barshi taluka of Solapur district. The lake has been in spate for the past decade. During the inspection, large quantity of water was found to have leaked from the well. As a result, the water supply to the lake will be affected. The villagers and the people's representatives have demanded that the pond be converted into a reservoir. Therefore, if the pond is converted into a storage pond, the leakage from the waterproof trench and the main wall will be stopped, which will benefit the farmers of the beneficiary area. Accordingly, a proposal has been submitted to the government under the relevant letter for administrative approval of the converted storage pond. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for conversion of storage tank at Mauje Rui in Barshi Taluka of Solapur district at a total cost of Rs. 4, 97, 42, 273/- (Rupees Four Crore Ninety Seven Lakh Forty Two Thousand Two Hundred and Seventy Three only) subject to the following conditions.
