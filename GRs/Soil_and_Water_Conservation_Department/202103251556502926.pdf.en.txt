The amendment of the water conservation treatment structures is proposed in line with the restoration of irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state. A total of 27 schemes will be repaired under the Nashik Circle Office and the total amount for repair is Rs. 7.12 crore. After the restoration of these projects, 665.98 ha of irrigated area and 3619.81 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for administrative approval of these works has been submitted to the government by the Additional Commissioner and Chief Engineer, Water Conservation, Aurangabad. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik has scrutinized/ verified all the estimates at his level and certified the same. Considering the above, administrative approval was sought for the amendment proposal of the 27 works mentioned. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
