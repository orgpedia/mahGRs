GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 475.35 lakhs (Rs. Four Crore Seventy Five Lakh Thirty Five Thousand only) for 13 works of 12 storage dams and 1 K.P.Bandhara in the contextual proposals taken up under the Hingoli District Irrigation Residue Eradication Programme.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions: a) The funds being disbursed through the Water Conservation Corporation, District Planning Committee or other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above projects, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed. b) The funds shall not be spent beyond the cost of administrative sanction and revised administrative sanction approved by the Government. c) If there is no need for funds for the above projects, the funds shall not be disbursed for other projects under any circumstances. d) If the funds remain unspent for any reason, the funds shall be immediately returned to the Government. e) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds shall be strictly followed.
