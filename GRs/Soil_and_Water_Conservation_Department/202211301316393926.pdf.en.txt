Milind Sukhdev Pawar, Assistant Engineer Grade-1, Shravan Udaybhan Shende, Sub-Divisional Engineer, and Shri. Ankita Ambadas Waghmare, Assistant Engineer Grade-I, is being included in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as per the" Statement-A" attached with this Government Resolution. The induction of these 8 officers will be subject to the following terms and conditions:
The seniority list of these 8 officers will be after the officers of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer (Architecture) Group-A cadre who were earlier given the option to the Department of Soil and Water Conservation.
The 8 officers will be next in seniority to the last seniority of the Sub Divisional Water Conservation Officer (Architecture) Group A cadre of the Soil and Water Conservation Department.
3. The said 8 officers have been posted in the cadre of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer (Architecture) Group-A. As per the order dated 30/6/2021, the Sub Divisional Water Conservation Officer (Architecture) will be next to the officers promoted to the post of Group-A.
4. The seniority of the proposed 8 officers after all the above officers shall be in the order in which they have submitted their belated options to this Department, losing their respective seniority.
