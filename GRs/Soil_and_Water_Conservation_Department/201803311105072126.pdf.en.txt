The Superintending Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Board, Aurangabad shall draw the above funds and hand them over to the Executive Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Department, Aurangabad immediately for further disbursement.

(c) If funds are not required for any work, such funds should not, under any circumstances, be distributed among themselves for any other work.
d) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the funds should be returned to the government immediately.
