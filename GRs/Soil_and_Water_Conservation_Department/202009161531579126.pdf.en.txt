The Proposal:
The programme to construct cement embankments in taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
As per the demand for funds for the cement embankment completed under the River Rejuvenation Programme in Solapur district, a total of Rs. A sum of Rs. 119.24 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Nineteen Lakhs Twenty Four Thousand) was under consideration.
Government's decision:
During the year 2020-21, Cement Dam Programme Account Head 4402 2799 involving an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) has been budgeted. Under the River Rejuvenation Programme, the cement embankment of the embankment sanctioned in 2016-17 in Solapur district has been completed at a total cost of Rs. 119.24 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Nineteen Lakhs Twenty Four Thousand only) is being sanctioned as follows:

The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Officer concerned are being declared as Controlling Officer and District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending the funds being made available for cement construction.
The Controller should ensure that the funds are spent on the objectives for which they are distributed.
The funds being disbursed are subject to the following conditions:
3.1 If the above Cement Bond is funded by another instrument, the funds being distributed by the said Government Decision shall not be withdrawn or distributed. The District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district should ensure that the expenditure is not misused.
3.2 No amount shall be spent in excess of the administrative sanction or the price agreed to by the Government.
3.3 If the funds provided remain unspent for any reason, the unspent funds shall not be used for any other purpose. The money should be immediately returned to the government.
3.4 The full utilization certificate and the certificate of completion of the work given so far should be submitted to the government immediately.
The approved funds should be spent under the following head of accounts.
" Demand No. ZH-5, 4402 Capital Expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation, 102 Soil Conservation, (02) Cement Dam (Check Dam) Programme, (4402 2799), 53 Major Constructions
5. The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by Circular No. 2 of the Finance Department.
