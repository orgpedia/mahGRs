The Proposal:
The work of water conservation under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme is of great importance. As per the Government decision dated 5th December, 2014, Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan has been launched in the State with a target to make 5000 villages water scarcity free by 2019-20. The target is to make 5000 villages drought-free every year. For this, public participation is very important and it is very important to campaign and publicize through various media to increase public participation in the campaign.
As per the relevant Government Decision dated 25/8/2008, Watershed Development Movement Programme is being implemented in the State to make the objectives and importance of soil and water conservation known to the general public. Under this, a joint tableau based on the schemes of Planning (ROHYO) Department and Soil and Water Conservation Department was included in the Republic Day Parade held on 26th January, 2019. Through this tableau, the important scheme of soil and water conservation was promoted and the matter of reimbursement of the expenses incurred for it was under the consideration of the government.
