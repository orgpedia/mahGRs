Preamble- The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 under Section 33 of the Act and the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi As per the provisions of the notification dated 04/01/2021, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance of the State Government has been directed to ensure the posts under the State Government for the disabled. All the administrative departments have been notified by the Government Decision dated 02/02/2021. As per the provisions of Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the matter of ensuring posts for Divyangjan in Group C and D cadres in the subordinate offices of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation was under consideration of the Government as per the notification of the Central Government.
Government's decision
As per the provisions of Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and notification of the Central Government, the appointment of persons with disabilities in Group C and D cadre in the subordinate offices of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation is being made as mentioned in the Statement A accompanying this Government Resolution. This Government has taken the above decision under Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi As per the provisions of the notification dated 04/01/2021 and issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. As per the instructions in the Government Resolution dated 02/02/2021, the same is being issued with the approval of the Head of the Administrative Department.
