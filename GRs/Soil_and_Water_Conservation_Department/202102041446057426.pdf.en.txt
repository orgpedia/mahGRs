Preamble: The programme for construction of cement dams in taluks in the State in the drought situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
During the year 2020-21, Cement Dam Programme Account Head 4402 2799 involving an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) has been budgeted. The District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." The Controller should ensure that the funds are spent on the objectives for which they are distributed.
The funds being distributed are subject to the following conditions: Cement bond at 3.1. If funds are available from other sources, the funds being distributed by the said Government decision shall not be withdrawn or distributed. The District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district should ensure that the expenditure is not revised.
3.2 No amount shall be spent in excess of the administrative sanction or the price agreed to by the Government.
3.3 If the funds provided remain unspent for any reason, the unspent funds shall not be used for any other purpose. The money should be immediately returned to the government.
3.4 The full utilization certificate and the certificate of completion of the work given so far should be submitted to the government immediately.
The approved funds should be spent under the following head of accounts.
" Demand No. ZH-5, 4402- Capital Expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation, 102 Soil Conservation, (02) Cement Dam (Check Dam) Programme, (44022799), 53 Major Constructions."
