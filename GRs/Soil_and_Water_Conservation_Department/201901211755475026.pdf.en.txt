As per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 31.5.2017, if the officers of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation are available in the above cadre on a permanent basis by promotion or direct service or otherwise, the services of the above officers posted through the Water Resources Department will be returned to the Water Resources Department immediately. Subject to this condition, the said officers are being given postings under this department only on temporary basis as indicated against their names.
