Preamble: Demand No. 1 for conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 619.41 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046. Out of the funds provided, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal for seeking funds with reference date 26/10/2021.
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse a total amount of Rs. 98.81 lakhs (Rupees Ninety Eight Lakhs only with One Thousand only) towards payment of repair works of the projects in the reference proposals in Akola District subject to the following conditions and conditions.
a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring completion of the repair work, b) The amount should not be paid more than the sanctioned cost, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
(f) The works have been completed 100% and no complaints have been received and the quality of the work should be ensured and payment should be made and the compliance report should be submitted to the government.
The amount so disbursed should be collected by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Amaravati and distributed to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them immediately.
