Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 1 is being implemented. A total of 12 schemes will be repaired under the Pune Mandal Office. 518.91 crore. The irrigation potential of these works is 407.2 MW. The total water storage available in the reservoir is 1401.96 mm. The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 4 and 5 have been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. Considering the above, the administrative approval of the proposal was under consideration as the 12 works mentioned in the revised plan of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme were included in the second priority. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Samvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 12 repair schemes in the Pune Regional Water Conservation Department is Rs. 518.91 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
