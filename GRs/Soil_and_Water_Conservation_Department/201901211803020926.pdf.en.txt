2.The concerned officers should immediately join the place of the detailed posting indicated above and submit a report to the government through the Commissioner. No request for change in post will be entertained.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision dated 31.5.2017, if the officers of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation are available for permanent service in the above cadre by promotion or direct service, the services of the officers who have not opted for this department out of the above officers posted by the Water Resources Department will be returned to the Water Resources Department, subject to the condition that the said officers are being given postings under this department as indicated against their names.
