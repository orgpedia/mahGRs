Preamble: A sum of Rs. 40.00 crore has been provided for the year 2017-18 for the construction of new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential ranging from 0 to 100 hectares under L-3, Account Head Index No. 2702 4754.
The funds will be distributed under the programme. The Governor has to do the legislative development in accordance with the formula prescribed by the Board.
As per the order dated 13/09/2017, orders have been issued to transfer the pending liabilities under Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme to different schemes. Of these, 0 to 100 are index numbers.
The Government has decided to provide funds for the demand proposals in respect of schemes under VIIDP transferred under 2702 4754 as follows:
Decision: The Government hereby approve the release of Rs. 332.00 lakh (Rs. Three Crore Thirty Two Lakh only) for minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of 0 to 100 hectare irrigation potential in Chandrapur, Amravati and Wardha districts as per Form-A attached to the approved provision for the financial year 2017-18.
2. The aforesaid funds are being distributed subject to the following conditions and conditions.
a) No funds shall be disbursed for projects other than those transferred in accordance with the relevant Government Decision dated 13/09/2017.
