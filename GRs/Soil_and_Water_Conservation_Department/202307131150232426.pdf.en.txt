Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 20.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2023-24 under ZH-3, Account Head 27029219. Out of which, the Government have decided to provide funds for payment of Water Availability Certificate Consultation Fee in the proposal dated 20/06/2023 from the funds made available through the Reference Circular.
Decision: The Government hereby grant approval to disburse an amount of Rs. 7,70,200/- (Rupees Seven Lakh Seventy Thousand Two Hundred only) towards payment of consultation fee for obtaining Water Availability Certificate for the project of Storage Tank Vishvi, comprising 101 to 250 hectares in Mehkar Taluka of Buldhana district subject to the following terms and conditions.
The above funds are being distributed subject to the following conditions and conditions: a) The District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall re-verify the amount demanded from the Government before actual disbursement of funds so that no more than the reasonable amount is disbursed for the water availability certificate work, b) Water availability certificate for the said project shall be verified to ensure that the funds are not disbursed twice for the work, c) If there is no need for funds due to any reasons, the disbursed amount should not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances, d) No amount in excess of the sanctioned cost should be disbursed, e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the unutilized amount should be immediately returned to the Government, f) In case of non-disbursement of funds.
