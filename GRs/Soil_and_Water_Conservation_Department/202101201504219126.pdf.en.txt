(a) The amounts so distributed shall not be withdrawn or distributed if funds have been made available from other sources for the joint enumeration of the lands being acquired for the above projects.

(a) In case of non-availability of funds due to any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government; and (d) Guidelines in the circular dated 10/11/2020 of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds should be strictly followed.
The amount so made available shall be appropriated by the concerned Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department and disbursed expeditiously to the concerned officers subordinate to him.
