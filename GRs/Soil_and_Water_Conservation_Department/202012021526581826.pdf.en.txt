The Proposal:
The programme of construction of cement embankments in taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
During the year 2020-21, Cement Dam Programme Account Head 4402 2799 involving an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) has been budgeted. Accordingly, it has been decided to disburse funds for the construction of cement works completed as per the letter dated 21.08.2020 and 11.09.2020.
The Decision:
Under River Rejuvenation Programme, Rs. Government approval is hereby accorded for disbursement of Rs. 102.24 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Two Lakh Twenty Four Thousand only) as follows:

The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Officer concerned are declared as" Controlling Officer" and the District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for spending the funds being made available for cement construction. The Controller should ensure that the funds are spent on the objectives for which they are distributed.
The funds being disbursed are subject to the following conditions:
3.1 If the above Cement Bond is funded by another instrument, the funds being distributed by the said Government Decision shall not be withdrawn or distributed. The District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district should ensure that the expenditure is not revised.
3.2 No amount shall be spent in excess of the administrative sanction or the price agreed to by the Government.
