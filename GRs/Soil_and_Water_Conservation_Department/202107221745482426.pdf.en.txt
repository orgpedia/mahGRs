GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF VILLAGE DEVELOPMENT AND WATER CONSERVATION, No. 2016/ PR No. 88 (Part-9)/ WATER-2, dated 31st May, 2017.
Preamble: In pursuance of the approval given by the Cabinet in its meeting dated 25/04/2017 to the proposal for reorganization of Zonal Zonal Bodies including establishment of Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation, a Government Resolution dated 31/05/2017 has been issued in this regard. in section 15. Accordingly, the administrative department has been empowered to determine the headquarters of the office under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation and to make changes in the location of these headquarters according to the scope of work and the urgency of the department. To make the work of water conservation schemes in 11 talukas of Beed district more effective, the government was considering to shift the headquarters of the sub-divisional office and the taluk jurisdiction.
Government's decision:
In order to make the work of water conservation schemes in Beed district under the soil and water conservation department more effective and for the convenience of the people and communication, approval is being given to relocate/ restore the place of headquarters of the sub-divisional office and the area of the taluka as follows.
