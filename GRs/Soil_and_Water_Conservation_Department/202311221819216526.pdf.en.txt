Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 251 to 600 hectares. A sum of Rs. 5.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2023-24 under ZH-3, Account Head 27029228. Out of which, the Government have decided to fund the survey works proposed as on 04/07/2023 from the funds made available through the Reference Circular.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to disburse a sum of Rs. 36.61 lakh (Rupees Thirty Six Lakhs Sixty Seven Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues for survey works of Laghu Bandhare Talab Megholi Rebuild (Right Side) and Laghu Bandhare Talab Megholi Rebuild (Left Side) in Bhudargad Taluka of Kolhapur district and Laghu Bandhare Talab Savarde in Radhanagari Taluka.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) to ensure that the documents relating to approval of survey works, commissioning orders, survey reports, maps, field reports, etc. are available and that all works are carried out as per rules;
b) It should also be ensured that the trial pit/ core logging report is fulfilled, c) The records in the measurement book should be checked to ensure that the amount demanded is reasonable and the funds are not disbursed twice for the related works.
(d) No amount shall be paid in excess of the approved price.
(e) if for any reason the fund remains unutilized, the unutilized amount shall be returned to the Government forthwith.
