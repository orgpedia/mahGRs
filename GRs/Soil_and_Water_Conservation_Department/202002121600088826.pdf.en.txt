Cement storage embankment schemes with this gate are proposed on a local drain in Chikhli district, Buldhana. A total of 250 hectares of land will be brought under the project. The proposed water storage capacity of these 6 cement dams is 450.01 cusecs. Accordingly, a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the cement drain embankment works connected to the said gate was given above. The letter dated 8/11/2019 was submitted to the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
Satgawan Bhusari No. 1, No. 2, Bhokar No. 1, Bhokar No. 2, No. 1, Waghapur No. 1, T. A total of Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 468.77 lakhs (Rupees Four Crores Sixty Eight Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand only) and other incidental expenditure of Rs. 26.28 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Six Lakhs Sixty Eight Thousand only) aggregating to Rs. 495.04/- (Rupees Four Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Four Thousand only) subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The report on the pier details should be authenticated by the geologist by taking the pier details on the alignment of the gated cement embankment. Also, the geologist should certify that the gated cement dam site is suitable for storing water.
2) The Superintending Engineer/ Regional Water Conservation Officer for the project is required to visit the work site and identify and certify the site and alignment.
