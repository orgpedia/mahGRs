The Proposal:
A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been budgeted for the survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of 101 to 250 hectares for the financial year 2017-18 under headings 2702 3391, out of which Rs. 8.00 crore is available for direct disbursement within the limit of 80%. It was under the consideration of the government to disburse this fund to the eligible works of the survey according to the statutory development board-wise criteria in accordance with the demands submitted by the zonal machinery. Reference letter No. The government has decided to fund the survey work recommended under 2 and 3 as follows.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby grant approval for disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 251.96 lakhs (Rupees Two Crores Fifty One Lakh Ninety Six Thousand only) for the survey works of 47 Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Projects in Form-A covering an irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares subject to the following conditions.

(a) To ensure that no more than the reasonable amount for the survey is disbursed, the Executive Engineer concerned shall, before disbursement of the actual funds, again ensure that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable; (b) The work above the survey shall be verified by the concerned Sub-Divisional Office to ensure that the funds are not disbursed twice; (c) If for any reason the funds are not required, the amount so disbursed shall not, under any circumstances, be diverted for any other purpose; (d) Any amount in excess of the sanctioned cost shall not be disbursed; (e) If for any reason the funds remain unutilized, the said unutilized amount shall be immediately returned to the Government; and (e) The guidelines in the relevant circulars of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds shall be strictly complied with.
The amount so distributed shall be drawn by the concerned Superintending Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Board and shall be distributed expeditiously to the concerned officers subordinate to him.
