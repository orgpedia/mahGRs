Preamble: Under Demand No.ZH-5, Account Head 47029019, a sum of Rs. 50.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2018-19 for minor irrigation schemes with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. Out of which, funds to the extent of 70% amounting to Rs. 35.00 crore have been made available for disbursement as per the relevant circulars. The government was considering to distribute the funds in the form of statutory development board.
Accordingly, the Government has decided to consider the proposal recommended under the Reference Paper for funding of the projects sanctioned under the Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme and transferred under the State Plan for availability of funds.

The funds being distributed as above are being distributed subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) If funds are available from the Water Conservation Corporation, District Planning Committee or other sources for the above project works, the amount being disbursed through this scheme should not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) The amount should not be spent beyond the cost of administrative sanction and revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) If there is no need for funds for the above project works, the amount should not be disbursed for other projects under any circumstances; (d) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government; (e) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
(f) The works have been completed 100% and no complaints have been received and the quality of the work should be ensured by making payment and submitting a compliance report to the government.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the concerned Regional Water Conservation Officer and disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under him.
