Preamble: Village/ Taluk should not be neglected despite their outstanding contribution under Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan and such outstanding contribution should be considered for the award. In point 3 (3) of the sectional inspection No. The matter of amending 3.1) and 3.2) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Jalmitra Award, the procedure for inspection/ scrutiny of villages, taluks, districts, which have done excellent work in the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan, has been fixed. 3 (3) in the middle section level inspection issue no. 3.1) and 3.2) are being amended as follows:
(1) The Block Level Inspection/ Scrutiny Committees shall also inspect the number one villages in all the districts of the division to which they are going to visit for scrutiny/ inspection and the first three villages in the order of merit in that division (which may in case include the first three villages of the same district) and conduct scrutiny and scoring accordingly.
(2) The Division Level Scrutiny/ Scrutiny Committee shall also inspect the number one taluk and the first two talukas in that division (which may also include the first two talukas of the same district) in the respective districts in which it is to visit for inspection/ scrutiny.
