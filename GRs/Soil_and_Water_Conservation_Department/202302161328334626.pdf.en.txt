Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 4 is being implemented. A total of 16 schemes in Solapur district will be repaired under the Pune Mandal Office. 553.94 lakh. After the restoration of these projects, 518 hectares of irrigated area and 2191.35 cft of water storage will be restored. The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 5 has been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. In view of the above, these 16 schemes are included in the second priority category. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to these 16 works was under the consideration of the government.
