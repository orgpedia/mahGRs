Formation of Departmental Review Committee for premature retirement of Government officers/ employees after 30 years of qualifying service/ beyond 50/55 years of age.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Soil and Water Conservation
Government Resolution No. Establishment-2019/ PR No.298/ Water-2
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date: November 28, 2019
Government Resolution, General Administration Department No. LPL-2017/ PR No.21/ KA-15 dated 10.06.2019.
The government's decision:
As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government in the public interest to review the services of officers of incompetent and doubtful integrity at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them from Government service prematurely. Accordingly, the Departmental Review Committee is being constituted under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation to review and recommend on the basis of prescribed criteria for the eligibility of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers to continue in service beyond the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
