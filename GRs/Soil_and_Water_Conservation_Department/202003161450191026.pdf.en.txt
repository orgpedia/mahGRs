Proposal for the L.P. Scheme, Kankavli G. Sindhudurg Project Budget Reference No. 1 has been accorded the original administrative sanction. The project was completed in 2002. This plan The Superintending Engineer Dam Safety Organization has conducted an inspection and according to the inspection," Major leakages observed through downstream side head wall of outlet." The water is leaking from the D/ S Head Wall (UCR) along the outlet conduit of this scheme. The budget has been prepared with the provision of laying iron pipe of 1100 mm diameter in the entire length along with the well in the upstream and downstream system, coating this pipe with HDPE from inside and outside, filling cementitious polymer grout in the iron pipe and system cavity, installing two sluice valves of 800 mm diameter at the downstream of the dam, etc. This budget has a provision to remove the gate, stemrod, hoist, embedded parts, etc. from the well. New headwalls have been proposed in the embankment adjacent to the upstream and downstream headwalls of the dam. After the repair work, the storage capacity of the dam will be restored. Under this project, 124 hectares of land will be brought under irrigation. Janwali L.P. Scheme, Ta. Kankavli G. Sindhudurg has a proposed water storage capacity of 2541.00 cusecs.
