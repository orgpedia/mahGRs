Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 8.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2019-20 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9219. Out of this, Rs. 4.80 crore has been made available for disbursement through circulars subject to a ceiling of 60%. Among them, the government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal submitted by the zonal agency for the fund for Marathwada division as follows.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 69.99 lakh (Rupees Seventy-nine Lakhs Ninety-nine Thousand only) as per the attached Form-A for the survey work of 26 minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of irrigation capacity ranging from 101 to 250 hectares in Nanded and Jalna districts of Marathwada region.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the Executive Engineer concerned shall, before the actual disbursement of funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable.
b) The concerned sub-divisional office should verify that the above work of the survey is not being distributed twice.
