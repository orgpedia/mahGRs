Government Circular: A confidential report is a fundamental and very important document for the evaluation of government officers/ employees and their further progress in terms of service matters. Therefore, it is very important to write confidential reports within the stipulated time and preserve them properly.
A new Administrative Division, Soil and Water Conservation, has been created vide Government Resolution dated 31.05.2017. In order to ensure consistency in the evaluation of the work done by the officers of Group-A and B (Gazetted) cadre at Regional/ Zonal level under this Department on the basis of confidential reports, the Report and Review Officer has been appointed by the Government Circulars dated 29.09.2018, 02.11.2019 and 29.12.2021 of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation.
The Reporting and Reviewing Officer of the confidential reports of the Gazetted Officers/ Employees, other than Group A (Gazetted) and Group B (Gazetted/ Non-Gazetted) Cadre Officers, who are working in the Regional or Divisional Office, shall be the senior officer next to them in that Office. The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad has been appointed to preserve and process confidential reports (performance appraisal reports) of officers of Group A (gazetted) and Group B (gazetted/ non-gazetted) cadres. However, confidential reports on service matters such as promotions, vigilance checks, permanency certificates, etc. are not made available at the government level from time to time. Therefore, it is being amended to preserve confidential reports of Group A and Group B officers.
(4) In order to preserve and streamline the confidential reports properly, the officers of the cadre of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer and other similar posts shall be posted in the office of the Additional Commissioner (Original Copy) and the concerned Regional Office (Secondary Copy) in the subordinate office of the Office of the Additional Commissioner (Water Conservation).

Confidential reports will be kept. In this regard, the following instructions should be strictly followed by the field offices.
(1) Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officers and other similar posts shall preserve the original confidential reports of the officers of this cadre at the level of the office of the Upper Commissioner Water Conservation in which they are working and the secondary confidential reports at the Regional Office. Also, the confidential report of the concerned officer should be transferred to the office where he is transferred within the stipulated time.
2) Confidential reports and information about the opinions and responsibilities of the officers of the cadre of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer and other similar posts at the government level are preserved. Their confidential reports and information about their opinions and responsibilities will be sent to the office of the Additional Commissioner. Also, the office in which the officers of this cadre will be working should receive the confidential reports of the concerned officers from their previous office. So that their updated Confidential Report Sheet will be available in the office where the officer is working for their promotion, benefit of Assured Progress Scheme, vigilance, stability certificate.
(3) When the said officer is promoted to the post of District Water Conservation Officer and similar posts, his updated original and second confidential report should be sent to the Government without delay.
(4) Confidential reports and statements of opinions and liabilities of the District Water Conservation Officer and his officers shall be preserved and processed at the government level. The information about the powers and responsibilities of the sub-divisional water conservation officer and other similar posts should be preserved at the level of the additional commissioner.
The list of the officers who have been preserved and processed in the confidential report office of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer/ Engineer, Deputy Engineer, Assistant Executive Engineer and Assistant Engineer Grade-1 Cadre Officers (Original and Secondary) should be submitted to the Government.
