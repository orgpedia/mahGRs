The Proposal:
Under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, a total of four posts have been sanctioned in the cadre of Additional Commissioner Water Conservation/ Chief Vigilance Officer/ Chief Engineer and ex-officio Joint Secretary in the state. The Government has decided to provide technical assistance to the Commissioner of Soil and Water Conservation. The Government has now decided in this regard that it would be more convenient administratively and geographically to hand over the control of Nashik and Aurangabad Regional Offices to an officer of the rank of Additional Commissioner, Water Conservation, created as on 31st May, 2017, in addition to his responsibilities in the Commissionerate.
Government's decision:
The control and jurisdiction of the Regional Offices under the office of the Additional Commissioner of Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area Pune and Nagpur under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation is being changed as follows:

| 3    | Additional Commissioner Water Conservation, Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad | Aurangabad and Nashik Regional Division |

Since the control of Nashik and Aurangabad regional divisions under the office of the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation, Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area, Pune has now been handed over to the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation, Aurangabad Commissionerate, the office of the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation, Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area, Pune should immediately hand over all the documents related to Nashik and Aurangabad regional divisions to the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation, Soil and Water Conservation Commissionerate, Aurangabad.
The duties and responsibilities assigned to the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation, Soil and Water Conservation Commissionerate, Aurangabad under the Government Order dated 31st May, 2017 of the Department shall continue to be the same.
