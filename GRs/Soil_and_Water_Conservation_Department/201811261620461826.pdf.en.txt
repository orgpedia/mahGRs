The Proposal:
A sum of Rs. 300.00 lakh has been provided in the Budget Estimates 2018-19 for the conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with an irrigation potential of 101-250 hectares. Out of which, Rs. 210.00 lakh has been made available for disbursement under the relevant circulars of the Finance Department, subject to a ceiling of 70%. Reference No. The following decisions were taken on the above proposal.
Government's decision:
K. P. B. Mauje Ooty, Ta. The Government hereby approves disbursement of Rs. 2.50 lakh (Rupees Two Lakh Fifty Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair work for the gates of the 101-250 hectare irrigation project at Mahagaon, G. Yavatmal.
The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Amravati, shall draw the above funds and disburse them to the concerned authorities expeditiously.
The above expenditure shall be covered under the head of accounts and shall be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2018-2019.
