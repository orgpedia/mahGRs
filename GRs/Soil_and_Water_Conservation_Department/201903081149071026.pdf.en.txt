The Proposal:
The expert committee for determining the life span of soil and water conservation treatment has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 03.10.2018. The government had sought extension of time for the committee to submit its report.
Government's decision
As per the Government Decision dated 3rd October, 2018, the Expert Committee constituted to determine the life span of soil and water conservation treatment is being extended till 30th June, 2019 as demanded by the Committee Chairman and Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad to submit the report to the Government.
