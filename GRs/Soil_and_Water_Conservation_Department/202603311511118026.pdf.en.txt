The Proposal:
Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan / Government Decision dated 02.01.2018 regarding Water Augmentation Machinery (Earthmovers) Interest Subvention Scheme through Department of Soil & Water Conservation, Department of Cooperation and Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation has been launched by the Government for providing machinery for soil and water conservation works in the State to educated unemployed youth / unemployed cooperatives / registered group agriculture / farmer producer companies / various executive cooperatives.
The duration of the scheme is 5 years and the liability of interest on loan provided by the financial institution to the beneficiaries for purchase of excavators (earthmowers) under the scheme (maximum interest return of Rs. 5.90 lakh per month or Rs. 9833 / -) is paid by the Government. The Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation is responsible for making available to the beneficiaries the interest reimbursement amount admissible by the government from time to time.
The term of the scheme has expired. Out of 318 beneficiaries selected under the scheme, 233 beneficiaries have been given full benefit and the remaining 85 beneficiaries have been asked for grants by the Government of Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune.

The Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune has issued Reference No. For payment of interest instalment to the savings account of the beneficiary concerned as demanded under letter No. 03, Rs. The issue of distribution of funds to the tune of 25,21,177 - (literal twenty-five lakhs twenty-one thousand one hundred and seventy-seven rupees only) was under consideration of the Government.
The Government's Decisions:
For financial AT 2025-26, the water augmentation earthmovers equipment interest subvention scheme under Account Head 2402 AVN is Rs. 25,21,177 - (Rupees twenty-five lakhs twenty-one thousand one hundred and seventy-seven only) is being approved for disbursement to Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune subject to the condition that the funds will be spent in the prescribed manner.
The Commissioner (Cooperation) will be the controlling officer for the scheme. The "Accountant in the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation" is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for drawing this fund from the treasury. The Controlling Officer and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall ensure that the expenditure is incurred in accordance with the terms and conditions as laid down by the Government decision in respect of this provision.
3. The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the budgetary provision in the financial year 2025-26 under the head Demand No. -ZH-3, Heading- 2402 (01) (05) Exploration (Earthmovers) Equipment Interest Subvention Scheme (Programme) 33, Financing (2402A237).
The Managing Director, Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune, will be responsible for making available the interest repayment amount admissible by the government on the loans provided by the financial institutions to the beneficiaries to the savings account of the beneficiary concerned.
