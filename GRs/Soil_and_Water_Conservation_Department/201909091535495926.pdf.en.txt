GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Office of the Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer on the field establishment of the Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad, has been transferred to the Office of the Controller of Regional Vigilance and Quality Control Offices, Aurangabad. It has been closed under the restructuring made by the Government Decision of 1.3.2019.
Accordingly, now the Deputy Commissioner (Finance), Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation, Valmi, Aurangabad is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Commissioner Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad as Controlling Officer for all Regional Vigilance and Quality Control Officers offices under the Soil and Water Conservation Department for the matters relating to pay fixation and ancillary matters as well as retirement pay.
