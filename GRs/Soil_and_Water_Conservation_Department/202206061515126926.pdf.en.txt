Preamble: Preliminary of the proposed sub-irrigation schemes on the reservoir/ river of Mula Dam and on the left and right canal of Nilwande Dam in Sangamner Taluka of Ahmednagar District
A total of 17 budgets of survey works have been approved for administrative approval through Reference Letter No. 3 submitted to the Government. The Benefit Area Development Authority, Nashik has obtained permission to lift water for the purpose of providing water through sub-irrigation. This area is in a drought-prone zone. Drinking water and irrigation facilities are available in the area.
The question arises. Therefore, the matter of providing administrative approval to the budgets of the survey works was under the consideration of the government. The decision is as follows:
Government's decision:
A total of 17 sub-irrigation schemes are proposed on the left and right canal of the Mula dam in Sangamner taluka of Ahmednagar district. 2,37,31, 546/- (Rupees Two Crores Thirty Seven Lakh Thirty One Thousand
Five hundred and forty seven only) budgetary amount is being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions. The sub-heading of these schemes has been attached along with the table of provisions.
A detailed project report should be prepared with the concept and submitted with the approval.
2. A detailed concept of duct system should be submitted for certification by the competent authority.
