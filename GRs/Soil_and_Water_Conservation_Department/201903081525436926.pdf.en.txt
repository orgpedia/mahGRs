Memorandum: During the financial year 2018-19, out of the budgeted amount for expenditure on installation of soil and water conservation systems, a total amount of Rs. 345.02 crore has already been disbursed. The Finance Ministry has approved the revised estimates for 2018-19. Accordingly, the mandatory revised allocation for the remaining period of 2018-19 has been made available to the administrative departments for disbursement. Accordingly, as indicated in the accompanying" Form-A," Rs. 7,65,15, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Sixty Five Lakhs Fifteen Thousand Only) as Subject-wise Grants. being placed by this Notice under the Controlling Authorities referred to in Section 7.
This provision should be immediately distributed to the drawing and disbursing officers under the authority.
The grants sanctioned under this Memorandum are being released subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in the Government Decisions issued by the Finance Department from time to time.
The terms and conditions of circulars dated 2/04/2018 and 25/01/2019 should be strictly complied with while utilising the above funds.
The said memorandum is being issued as per the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the relevant Government Circular dated 2/04/2018.
