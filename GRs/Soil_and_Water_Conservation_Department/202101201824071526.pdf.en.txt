The amendment of the water conservation treatment structures is proposed in line with the restoration of irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the schemes that require maintenance and repair from the completed water conservation schemes in the state. A total of 121 schemes will be repaired under the Nashik Circle Office and the total amount for repair is Rs. 30.02 crore. After the restoration of these projects, 3535.84 hectares of irrigated area and 15435.85 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for administrative approval of these works is given above. 22/10/2020, Dy. 10/09/2020 and D. As per the letter dated 27/11/2020, the Superintending Engineer and Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik and Additional Commissioner and Chief Engineer Water Conservation, Aurangabad have submitted to the Government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik has scrutinized/ verified all the estimates at his level and certified the same. Considering the above, administrative approval was under consideration for the proposed amendment of 121 works. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
