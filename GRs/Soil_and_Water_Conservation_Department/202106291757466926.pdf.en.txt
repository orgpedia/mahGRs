GOVERNMENT ORDER: In accordance with the provisions of Rules 4 and 5 of the Maharashtra Prevention of Transfers of Government Officials and Delays in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005, on request/ administrative grounds, with the approval of the competent authority, Shri Ravindra Sonu Khode, District Water Conservation Officer, Latur, who is working on temporary deputation in this department, is being transferred and posted as District Water Conservation Officer, Dhule.
The concerned officers shall relieve Mr. Khode immediately to join his place of posting.
Mr. Khode should take charge of his new post immediately and inform the government about it.
4. The services of Mr. Khode shall be transferred to the Water Resources Department with immediate effect, if the officer is available by promotion from the cadre of Sub Divisional Water Conservation Officer to the post of District Water Conservation Officer.
If Mr. Khode does not appear at the place of posting given to him as per the order of the said posting or if he tries to change the post by exerting any kind of pressure, he shall be deemed to have committed an act in violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979 and disciplinary action shall be taken against him.
