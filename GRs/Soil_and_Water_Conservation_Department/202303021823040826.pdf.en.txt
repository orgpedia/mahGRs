Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to disburse a total amount of Rs. 1467.55 lakhs (Rupees Fourteen Crores Sixteen Lakhs Fifty Five Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues for the 3 projects namely, Conversion Tank Rui and Conversion Tank Saundare in Akkalkot Taluka of Solapur District, which have generated 91 Heasinks and 120 Heasinks in Konhali and Barshi Talukas respectively:
The aforesaid funds are being distributed subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) The funds being distributed through the Water Conservation Corporation, District Planning Committee or other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above projects, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed. b) The funds shall not be spent beyond the cost of administrative approval as well as revised administrative approval approved by the Government. c) If funds are not required for the works of the above projects, the funds shall not be disbursed for any other project under any circumstances.
