Preamble: Demand No. 1 for conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 450.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2022-2023 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046. Now, as per the revised estimates, the proposal for seeking funds from the funds made available under the scheme as on 27/01/2023 has been decided as under.
Decision: In the Akot Taluka of Akola district, K.P. Bandhara Lamkani, Telhara Taluka Village Panchgavhan-3 and Barshitakali Taluka Ko. The Government hereby grants approval for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 114.89 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Fourteen Lakhs One Hundred and Ninety Nine Thousand only) for payment of maintenance and repair works of 04 projects namely Rs. 18.75 lakhs, Rs. 18.52 lakhs, Rs. 54.35 lakhs and Rs. 23.27 lakhs respectively.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions: a) The funds are to be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without being disbursed for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
