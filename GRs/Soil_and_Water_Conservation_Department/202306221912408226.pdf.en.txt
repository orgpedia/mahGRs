2) Letter from the Sub Divisional Engineer, Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad No. Le- 1/1313, Dy. 06.06.2023
Preamble: A sum of Rs. 2100.00 crore has been budgeted for the financial year 2023-24 as equity contribution for Maharashtra Jal Sanrakshan Corporation. As per the order dated 12.04.2023 of the Department of Finance, the funds are permissible within the budgetary limit for the first nine months. Out of which, Rs. 210.00 crore has been made available for disbursement to Administrative Departments for the first quarter. The Government has decided to disburse the following funds to the Corporation.
Decision: The Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 210.00 crore (Rupees Two Hundred and Ten Crores only) as share capital contribution to the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation.
The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad is being authorized to draw the above funds from the treasury and distribute the same in the name of Managing Director, Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad.
The expenditure on this should be spent under the following heads of accounts and should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2023-24.
Demand No.ZH-5, Head of Accounts 4402,

The instructions in the relevant circulars of the Finance Department should be followed for the utilization of the said funds.
In case of non-compliance or any violation of the directions of the Government regarding the distribution of funds and in case of any complaint or court case arising out of it in future, the entire responsibility will be of the Managing Director and Chief Accounts and Audit Officer (CAFO), Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad.
