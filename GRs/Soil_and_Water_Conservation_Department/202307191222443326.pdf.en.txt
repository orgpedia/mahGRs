Preamble: A sum of Rs. 2100.00 crore has been provided in the Budget Estimates for the financial year 2023-24 as share contribution for Maharashtra Jal Sanrakshan Nigam. As per the order dated 12.04.2023 of the Department of Finance, the funds are admissible within the budgetary limit for the first nine months.
Out of this, Rs. 420.00 crore is allowed as first quarter fund with 20% limit on expenditure.
Of these, Rs. 210.00 crore. As per the Government decision dated 22.06.2023, Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad has been allotted. Out of this, the remaining amount of Rs. 210.00 crore has been made available for disbursement to the administrative departments. The Government has decided to disburse the following funds to the Corporation.
Decision: The Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 210.00 crore (Rupees Two Hundred and Ten Crores only) as share capital contribution to the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation.
The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad is being authorized to draw the above funds from the treasury and distribute the same in the name of Managing Director, Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad.
The expenditure on this should be spent under the following heads of accounts and should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2023-24.
