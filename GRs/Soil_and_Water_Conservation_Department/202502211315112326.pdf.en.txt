The Proposal:
The Government has decided to provide interest subvention scheme on water conservation machinery (Earthmovers) to educated unemployed youth / unemployed cooperatives / registered group agriculture / farmer producer companies / various executive cooperatives through the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, Department of Cooperation and Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation for providing machinery for undertaking Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan / Soil and Water Conservation works in the State. It was launched on 02 January 2018.
The duration of the scheme is 5 years and the government is responsible for the interest on loans provided by the financial institution to the beneficiaries to purchase excavators (earthmovers) under the scheme. The Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation is responsible for making available to the beneficiaries the interest reimbursement amount admissible by the government from time to time.
