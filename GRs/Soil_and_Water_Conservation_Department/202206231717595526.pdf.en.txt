Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 1 is being implemented. A total of 199 schemes under the Nashik Circle Office will be repaired. 1123.37 Lakhs. With the completion of these projects, 3465.2 ha of irrigated area and 14546.7 sq.m. of water supply will be restored.
The water supply will be restored. A proposal to get administrative approval for these works has been submitted to the government under the above reference letter. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik has scrutinized/ scrutinized all the budgets at his level.
According to the information submitted by the Board Office, the administrative approval of the amendment proposal of the 199 schemes was under consideration. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
