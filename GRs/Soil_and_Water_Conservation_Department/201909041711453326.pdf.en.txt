Preamble The Adarshgaon Resolution and Project Scheme has been launched under the Government Resolution dated 20th February, 1992. This scheme is based on the concept of village development through public participation.
Various schemes are being implemented by the government for the development of villages. These include 59 schemes of 12 government departments. Among them, watershed development work has been classified as core area and work of other departments as non-core area. A State-level committee has also been formed to implement the scheme. The guidelines of the scheme dated 13th August, 2010 have been amended vide Government Resolution dated 10th March, 2015 and Government Sanction Letter dated 24th March, 2015.

Government's decision
In order to honour the best performing villages, best project implementing agencies, best village functionaries, best performing village level staff who have been participating under the Adarsh Gaon Yojana since 2009-10, by giving appropriate awards at the government level, the Maharashtra Adarsh Gaon Bhushan Award under Adarsh Gaon Yojana has been instituted by the Government under the scheme. The recognition has been granted under the Government Decisions dated 1st November 2017 and 20th February 2018. A committee has been formed to select the awardees.
