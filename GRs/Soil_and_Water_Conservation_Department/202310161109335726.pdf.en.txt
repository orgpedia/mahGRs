The Proposal:
The new website is www. swcd. maharashtra. gov. in. Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Ltd. (MKCL) has been created and the website has been made operational. The government was considering the formation of a Website Content Management Team to ensure that the information on the website is updated from time to time, new information is included, outdated information is removed from the website on time, new government decisions, circulars, etc. are published on the website and other ancillary works are done quickly and effectively.
Government's decision:
A Website Content Management Team is being constituted to regularly update the Website of Department of Soil and Water Conservation as under:
