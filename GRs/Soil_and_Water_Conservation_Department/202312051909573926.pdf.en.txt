Preamble: Demands for works in non-tribal areas under Jal Samriddhi Cement Dam (Check Dam) Programme No. A sum of Rs. 50.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2023-24 under ZH-5, Account Head 4402 2799. Out of which, the proposal for seeking funds from the funds currently made available as per the relevant circulars of the Finance Department, dated 07/11/2023, has been decided as follows:
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse a total amount of Rs. 11.00 crore (Rupees Eleven Crores only) towards payment of pending dues for the works of deepening and widening of nullahs in Arvi, Karanja and Ashti taluks of Wardha district.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds being disbursed through the above deepening and widening works should not be withdrawn or disbursed, if funds are available from Water Conservation Corporation, District Planning Committee or other source. b) The funds should not be spent beyond the cost of administrative sanction and revised administrative sanction approved by the Government.
