As per the directions of the Governor, the government has received a proposal to grant administrative approval to 12 gates of cement storage embankments in Aundha and Kalamnuri taluks under the Hingoli district irrigation backlog. The water storage capacity of the proposed Cement Nala Dam is 205.96 cusecs and the total cost of these works is Rs. 2.93 crore. The irrigation potential of these works is 80.21 hectares and the matter of administrative approval of these works was under consideration of the government. Based on this, the following decision was taken.
Decision of the Government: Based on the list of the year 2017-18 of the Soil and Water Conservation Department, the budget of the 12 gate cement storage embankment works in Aundha and Kalamnuri taluks of Hingoli district has been increased to a total of Rs. 2,92,99, 161/- (Rupees Two Crores Ninety Two Lakhs Nine Thousand One Hundred and Sixty-One only). 2,69,78, 862/- (Rupees Two Crores Seventy-nine Lakhs Seventy-eight Thousand Eight Hundred and Eighty-two only) and Goods and Services Tax and Insurance Expenditure of Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 23,20,299/- (Rupees Twenty Three Lakhs Twenty Thousand Two Hundred and Ninety Nine only).
The details of the 12 schemes are as follows: The existing limit for these schemes is Rs. 1,46,114/- per unit.
The administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
1.1 Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining at the competent level the plans/ concepts, externalities and transmission gaps envisaged in the budget as per the requirement.
