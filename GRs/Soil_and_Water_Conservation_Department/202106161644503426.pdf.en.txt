2. Letter from the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation and Chief Engineer, Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area, Pune No.
PRESS- 2/1026 D 16-03-2021 Preamble In order to restore the irrigation potential of the completed water conservation schemes in the State by preserving and repairing the schemes requiring maintenance and repair, it is proposed to repair the water conservation treatment structures. A total of 8 schemes in Sangli district will be repaired under the Pune Circle Office and the total amount for repair is Rs. 1.1498 crore. With the restoration of these projects, 82.85 hectares of irrigated area and 560.72 sq.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for administrative approval of these works is given above. As per the letter dated 16.03.2021, the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation and Chief Engineer, Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area, Pune have submitted to the Government. All these 8 schemes are under the jurisdiction of Pune Regional Board and the proposals for amendment have been scrutinized/ scrutinized at the level of Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune as per the instructions in Government Letter No. Lipayo-2020/ PR No.-6/ Jal-1, dated 11.08.2020 for an amount of Rs. 50.00 lakhs. According to the information submitted by the Board Office, out of these 8 schemes, 5 schemes are in the first priority category and the remaining 3 schemes are in the first priority category in the reform plan.
In view of the above, administrative approval was sought for the amendment proposal of the 8 works mentioned. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The total budget of 8 repair schemes in Sangli district of Pune Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 1.1498 crore (Rs.
