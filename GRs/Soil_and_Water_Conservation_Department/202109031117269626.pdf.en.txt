Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 251 to 600 hectares. A sum of Rs. 5.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-3, head 2702 9228 in 2020-21. Of this, only 5% has been allocated for distribution. Out of this, the government has decided to fund the survey work in the contextual proposal as follows.
Government's decision:
Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 16.00 lakhs (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs only) towards payment of consultation fee to Central Sankalp Chitra Sangathan, Nashik for strengthening of sub-irrigation schemes on Mula Danai Canal in Nevasa Taluka of Ahmednagar District.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
The District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall re-verify the amount demanded from the Government before actual disbursement of funds so that no amount is disbursed beyond the reasonable amount for the purpose of survey.
