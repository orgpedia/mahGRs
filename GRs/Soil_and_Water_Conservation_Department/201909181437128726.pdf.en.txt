The Proposal:
The Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan is being implemented in the state as per the Government Decision dated 5 December 2014 under Water Scarcity Free Maharashtra for All-2019. Under the Mission, out of 22590 villages identified during 2015-16 to 2018-19, a total of 17135 villages have been covered.
As per the Government decision dated 1st March, 2019, the date for completion of works in selected villages under Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan 2018-19 has been extended till 31st December, 2019. Therefore, the matter of providing funds for the works in the selected villages in 2018-19 was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
For Jalyukt Shivar Scheme, the head of accounts is 4402 2781 for the year 2018-19. A provision of Rs. 1500.00 crore has been made in the Budget. Out of this, a total provision of Rs. 500.00 crore has been released under Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan for the scheme of Sludge Free Dams, Sludge Free Shivar as per letter dated 12th April, 2019 and Rs. 340.00 crore as per Government Resolution dated 10th May, 2019.
2. Out of the provisions now available, the selected villages under Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan 2018-19 have been provided with additional funds of Rs. The Government is pleased to approve the release of 50.00 crore (Rupees Fifty Crores only).
3. The provisions are being distributed to the Collector and the Divisional Commissioner is declared as the Controlling Officer and the Collector as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
Care should be taken to comply with the following terms and conditions when spending from the provision being distributed.
