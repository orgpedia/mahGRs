Under the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme, it is proposed to repair the water conservation treatment structures in order to restore the irrigation capacity by preserving and repairing the schemes that require maintenance and repair of the completed water conservation schemes in the state. A total of 5 schemes under Nashik Circle Office will be revised and all the schemes are included in the revised plan on second priority. After the repair of these projects, 12 hectares of irrigated area and 39.00 c.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for administrative approval of these works has been submitted to the government by the Additional Commissioner and Chief Engineer, Water Conservation, Aurangabad. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik has scrutinized/ verified all the estimates at his level and certified the same. In view of the above, administrative approval was sought for the amendment proposal of the 5 works mentioned. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The total budget of 5 repair schemes of Nashik Regional Water Conservation Department is Rs. 6.24 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
