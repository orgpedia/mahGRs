Sharad Parasram Uikey, Chowkidar passed away on 25.5.2018. In case of death of a member in service under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme/ National Pension Scheme, Reference No. Under the 3rd application, the wife of the applicant, Mrs. Meenal Sharad Uikey, has applied for a grant of Rs. 10,00,000/-. Following this application, the Commissioner of Soil and Water Conservation (MRW) Aurangabad, has issued a notification. Sharad Parasram Uikey has submitted a proposal to the Government for sanction of ex-gratia grant of Rs. 10,00,000/- to the nominee i.e. his wife Smt. Meenal Sharad Uikey. Department of Finance As per the powers vested in the Head of the Administrative Department vide Government Resolution No.Anneo-2017/ PR No. 29/ Service-4 No.5 dated 29.9.2018, the late Shri. Grant of ex-gratia of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh only) to the person nominated by Sharad Parasram Uikey, his wife Smt. Meenal Sharad Uikey.
The ex-gratia grant is being sanctioned to the Government employees who died on 25.5.2018 before completing 10 years of service. This order shall remain in force for a period of one year from the date of issue of this order.
