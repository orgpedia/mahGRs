The Proposal: -
In the state of Maharashtra, the government decided to carry out 3558 mapping and ground truthing of soil and water conservation works / structures in the catchment areas under various schemes by various departments in the last year. The guidelines have been issued as of March 12, 2025. The programme is scheduled to be completed by May 2025 and will help in effective planning of Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan based on the information available from the programme.
2. In line with the implementation of the programme, a virtual round of action has been initiated in all the districts by appointing team heads on a day-to-day basis. The mobile app developed by MRSAC, Nagpur requires high speed mobile internet to be used along with GPS while performing ground truthing. For this, the government is taking the following decisions regarding the cost of mobile internet as the officers / staff appointed as team heads have to bear the cost of mobile internet on their own.
The Government's Decisions:
For villages where the number of soil conservation and water conservation structures required for physical site verification is Roo or less than 200, it is Rs. 9000 / - and for villages where the number of soil conservation and water conservation structures required for physical site verification is more than 200, Rs. 2000 / - for mobile internet is being sanctioned by the Government to the Head of the Team.
