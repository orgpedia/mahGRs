Order dated 12th February, 2018: Mr. S.S. Salunke, Executive Engineer is an officer of the Basic Water Resources Department and is working on deputation in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as Executive Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation Department, Washim).
In accordance with the provisions of paragraph 1 (d) of the Government Order dated 12th February, 2018, the Government have decided to terminate the deputation of Mr. S.S. Salunke, Executive Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Department, Washim and return his services to his parent department, Water Resources Department.
3. The Commissioner, Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad, shall make arrangements to hand over the additional charge of the post of District Water Conservation Officer, Washim, to the District Water Conservation Officer, Akola and relieve Mr. Salunke forthwith.
