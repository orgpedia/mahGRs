Preamble: Mauje Supa-2 under Zilla Parishad Parbhani, Ta. Pazar Lake at Gangakhed, G. Parbhani was breached on 24/07/1989 due to heavy rainfall. As per the final judgment dated 17.04.2023 passed by the High Court of Bombay, Bench Aurangabad vide Reference No. Proposals for funding under 2 and 3 have been submitted to the government. In fact, the Department of Soil and Water Conservation does not have any provision to pay any kind of compensation for the projects approved by the Zilla Parishads. Therefore, the District Councils are required to meet the liability of compensation from their own financial sources. However, in this case, the government has already provided funds. Since the High Court's order is final, the reference received for compliance of the court order No.
The following decisions have been taken after considering the suggestions in the relevant Government Decisions of the Finance Department on the proposals at 2 and 3.
Decision: Mauje Supa-2 under Zilla Parishad Parbhani, Ta. The total amount of difference in compensation for the loss of land due to breach of Pazar Lake at Gangakhed, G. Parbhani on 24/07/1989 is Rs. 62.84 lakhs (Rupees Sixty Two Lakhs Eighty Four Thousand only). Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to deposit the same in the High Court at Bombay, Bench at Aurangabad subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) the amounts so disbursed shall not be withdrawn or distributed if funds have been made available from other sources for the purpose of payment of the aforesaid compensation.
(b) in any case, the funds should not be distributed among themselves for any other purpose, unless there is a need for the above funds being made available as aforesaid.
(a) if for any reason the funds remain unutilized, the amount shall be returned to the Government forthwith;
