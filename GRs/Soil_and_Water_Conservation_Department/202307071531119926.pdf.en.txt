Government Circular: Reference No. 2 General Administration Department, Government Notification dated 21.06.2021 In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the temporary seniority of the cadre of Additional Commissioner Water Conservation/ Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer/ Chief Engineer and Designated Joint Secretary (Architecture) in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as on 01.01.2022 is being published as" Form-A" attached to this circular.
2. The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list in Form-A annexed to this circular shall be the date of seniority mentioned in Reference No. The Government Notification No. 281/12, General Administration Department No. SRV-2016/ PR No. As per the provision of 21.06.2021, the seniority of the cadre of Additional Commissioner Water Conservation/ Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer/ Chief Engineer and Designated Joint Secretary (Architecture) will be considered for determining the seniority.
3. The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar shall bring the said circular in Form A under his charge to the immediate notice of the Additional Commissioner, Water Conservation/ Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer/ Chief Engineer and the Designated Joint Secretary (Architecture).
If any errors/ errors are found in the details of the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation and Chief Engineer Officers included in this seniority list, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., they should bring their representations/ objections/ suggestions to the Government through registered post/ speed post/ hand delivery with necessary proper proof in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. If the mistakes/ errors in the written form are not brought to the notice of the Government within the stipulated time, the seniority of the cadre of Additional Commissioner Water Conservation/ Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer/ Chief Engineer and Designated Joint Secretary (Architecture) working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation will be finalized as on 01.01.2022.
