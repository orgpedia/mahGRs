Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 30.00 crore was budgeted in 2022-23 under ZH-3, head 27029201. It has now been decided to fund the survey works proposed in the proposal dated 07/11/2022 from the funds made available as per the revised estimates as under:
Decision: Reference No. 2 Approval of the Government is hereby accorded for disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 68.83 lakhs (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Eighty Three Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues of eligible and completed survey works in the proposals.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions: a) Approval of survey works, commissioning orders, survey reports, maps, field reports, etc., to ensure that all documents are available and all works are done as per rules, b) Trial Pit/ Core Logging Report should also be ensured to be completed, c) Records in the measurement book should be checked to ensure that the amount demanded is reasonable and the funds are not disbursed twice for the related works, d) Amounts in excess of the sanctioned cost should not be disbursed, e) In case of non-availability of funds due to any reason, the said amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
