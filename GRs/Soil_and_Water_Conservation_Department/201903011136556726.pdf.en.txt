Introduction: Under the Water Scarcity Free Maharashtra for All- 2019, the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan is being implemented in the state as per the government decision dated December 5, 2014 to permanently overcome the scarcity situation. Under the mission, soil and water conservation works are started after the end of the rainy season. Also, there is a delay in starting the work as the land is under cultivation. The time limit for completion of works in the selected villages under Sabbaj Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan during the campaign years 2015-16, 2016-17 and 2017-18 has been extended from time to time. Accordingly, additional time will be required after March 2019 to complete the works in the selected villages in 2018-19. Considering this. The Empowered Committee, headed by the Chief Secretary, has given its approval for completion of works in selected villages in 2018-19 at least by December 2019. In the above background, the matter of determining the implementation period of the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government Decision dated 05.12.2014 in the context of Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan specifies the period of implementation of Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan up to 2019. However, now under this decision of the government, the implementation period of the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan is being fixed till December 2019. The Government has decided to extend the time limit for completion of works in the selected villages for 2018-19 by one month till December, 2019.
