The Proposal:
The Expert Committee for determining the life span of soil and water conservation treatment has been constituted vide Government Resolution dated 03.10.2018.
The committee has already submitted its report. Decision of the Board of Directors dated August 13, 2020. The deadline has been extended to September 30, 2020. However, the Deputy Commissioner (Administration), Soil and Water Conservation (MRW), Aurangabad, in his reference, directed the DC to take necessary action. The extension has been sought as more time is required to submit the report vide letter dated December 29, 2020. Hence, the government decided to extend the term of the committee.
Government's decision
As per the demand of the Deputy Commissioner (Administration), Soil and Water Conservation (M& W), Aurangabad, the expert committee constituted to determine the life span of soil and water conservation treatment under the Government Decision dated 3rd October, 2018 is being extended till 30th June, 2021.
