Preamble Government Resolution No. Establishment-2016/ PR No. 88 (Part-9)/ Water-2, D. The field machinery of the Department has been restructured with the establishment of Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation with effect from 31st May, 2017. As per para 5 (ii) of the said Government Resolution, an office of the Additional Commissioner, Water Conservation, Aurangabad has been created to provide technical assistance to the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad. Further, in view of the changed sectoral regulation under the order at Reference No. 4, the matter of declaring the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation, Aurangabad under the department as Drawing and Distribution was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Additional Commissioner Water Conservation, Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area, Aurangabad under the Soil and Water Conservation Department is being declared as the Drawing and Distribution Officer.
