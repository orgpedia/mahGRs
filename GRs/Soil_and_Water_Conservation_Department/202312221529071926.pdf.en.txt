Introduction Among the completed water conservation schemes in the state, it is necessary to restore irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the schemes that need maintenance and repair. A total of 2 schemes under the Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar Mandal Office will be repaired and a total amount of Rs. 67.01 lakh. The total capacity of the project is 42 acres. The total water storage available in the dam is 201.98 mm. The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 3 submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. Trees have grown on the walls of the dam and water is leaking from the foundation of the earthen dam. Considering the above, administrative approval was under consideration for the safety of the 2 works mentioned.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The total cost of 2 repair schemes in Beed district of Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar Regional Water Conservation Division is estimated at Rs. 67,00,380/- (Rupees Sixty Five Lakhs Three Hundred Eighty Five only). These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
