Introduction: Savargaon, a storage tank with an irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares in Niphad taluka of Nashik district, has been proposed as a reference No. 1 As per the decision of the Government, administrative sanction of Rs. 11.74 crore has been obtained under the State Plan Account Head 4702A028 (Old Account Head 47023813). At the moment, the project is still in development. The people's representatives have strongly demanded that the project be transferred to the Water Conservation Corporation to help solve the problem of irrigation and drinking water in Niphad and Chandwad taluks by completing the work of this scheme as soon as possible. Accordingly, considering the funds available to the Water Conservation Corporation and the above demand of the people's representatives, the Corporation has decided to transfer the project from the State Plan to the Corporation. 2 has been requested. The government has taken the following decisions:
Decision: Under the State Plan" Small Irrigation Schemes from 101 to 250 Hz" (Leshisanker 4702 A028), the storage tanks approved by the government under the decision of the relevant government will be constructed in Savargaon. Approval of the Government is hereby accorded for transfer of Niphad G. Nashik Project along with all other liabilities to Maharashtra Jal Sanrakshan Nigam Limited (LESHI SANKRA 4402 2823).
