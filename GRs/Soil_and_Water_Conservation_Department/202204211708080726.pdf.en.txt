Government circular:
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department and the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, the temporary seniority of the cadre of Water Conservation Officer Group-B (Gazetted) in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as on 01.01.2019 is mentioned in the above Reference No. 2 has been issued by the government.
Objections received from the concerned water conservation officers have been taken into consideration. The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has invited applications from eligible candidates for recruitment to the post of Water Conservation Officer, Group-B (Gazetted) cadre. It has been objected that in the seniority list of 01/01/2019, the graduate junior engineers of the Zilla Parishad have been given place from the date of their original appointment without giving place from the date of their promotion to the rank of Assistant Engineer Grade-2.
Accordingly, the date from which the Chief Executive Officer of the Zilla Parishad has promoted the graduate junior engineers of the concerned Zilla Parishad to the rank of Assistant Engineer Grade-2 has been considered by the Government to determine their service excellence and accordingly the transfers have been made in the seniority list.
Further, in accordance with the provisions of the Government Notification dated 21.06.2021 of the General Administration Department, the Water Conservation Officers in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation are designated as Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre Officers in Form A and B respectively. The final seniority list as on 01.01.2019 is being published vide this circular
