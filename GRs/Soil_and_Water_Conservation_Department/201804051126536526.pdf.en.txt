Date of Birth: April 5, 2018
Read:- 1) Government Resolution No. Establishment 2016/ S.No.88 (Part-9)/ Water-2, Dy. 31st May, 2017.
2) General Administration Department, Government Notification No. The September 26, 2017
Introduction: Department of General Administration. As per the notification dated 26th September, 2017, a new Department of Soil and Water Conservation has been created.
The Department of Finance has approved the part two of the budget provision of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation for the year 2018-19 under the demand number ZH. Funds for mandatory expenditure on schemes and programmes of the newly created Soil and Water Conservation Department will be distributed to all field offices through BEAMS system by this department.
3. It was under consideration of the Government to make available on BEAMS system a Controlling Officer for the new Demand Number and Account Head.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Programme and Budget) concerned for the schemes of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation is being declared as the Chief Controlling Officer by this Government Decision.
The Chief Controlling Officer and the Cost Coordinating Officer shall be as indicated in the accompanying Forms" A" and" B" for the schemes of the Soil and Water Conservation Department. Necessary entries will be made on the BEAMS system accordingly.
