2.The above approval is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) Technical approval of the project should be granted only after obtaining the approval of the Regional Water Conservation Officer concerned regarding the alignment and concept of the K.P. Dam before the tender process.
2) The project should be completed within the project-wise approved cost limit as above.
(3) Government Decision No. Hidden- 1099/ PR No. 347/ JAL- 1 dated 31st July, 2000 and Government Resolution No. Hide- 2018/ P.No. 176/ Water- 1D. After completion of the project as on July 20, 2018, the beneficiary water utility should be established.
(4) On completion of the project, the project shall be transferred to the Water Utilization Agency established as above for maintenance repairs.
5) After the transfer of the project as above, the cost of repair should be borne by the beneficiaries.
The decision was taken by the Rural Development and Water Conservation Department. Strict compliance of the provisions as prescribed under the Act No.1 of 2011/ S.No.192/ Nidhi Jal-1 dated 02.02.2012 shall be ensured.
The above expenses should be met from the following headings.
Account Head Demand No. L-3 (02) Minor Irrigation Works Grants under Schemes to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961 (2) (02) Minor Irrigation Works State Integrated Fund Scheme 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) (2702 4754) (New Account Head Demand No. L-54702-A019)
