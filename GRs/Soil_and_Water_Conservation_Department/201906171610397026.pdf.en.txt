The proposal:
Jamthi and Pangarkhed. Jamthi in Buldhana district, Pangarkhed No. 1, No. 2, No. The government has received a proposal to construct a total of 4 Kolhapur-style dams. These dams have been proposed on local drains. The storage capacity of the dam is 62.42 cusecs, 52.22 cusecs, 62.56 cusecs and 69.99 cusecs respectively and a total area of 54.00 ha is planned to be irrigated under the project.
