Preamble: The programme of construction of cement dams in the taluks in the State in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan. In the year 2021-22, Cement Dam Programme Account head 4402 2799 has been allocated Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) was budgeted. Out of these, the proposal for seeking funds from the existing funds has been decided as follows.
Government Decision: The Government hereby grants approval for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 2.41 lakh (Rupees Two Lakh Forty-One Thousand only) towards payment of dues for the cement embankment completed out of the cement embankment sanctioned in 2015-16 in Sangli District under River Rejuvenation Programme as under:

The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Officer concerned are declared as" Controlling Officer" and the District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for spending the funds being made available for cement construction. The Controller should ensure that the funds are spent on the objectives for which they are distributed.
The funds being disbursed are subject to the following conditions:
(a) If funds are available from other sources for the above activities, the amounts being disbursed through this scheme should not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) The amount should not be spent beyond the cost of administrative sanction as well as revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) If funds are not required for the above activities, the amount should not be disbursed for any other purpose under any circumstances; (d) If funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government; (e) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
f) Ensure quality of work, make payments and submit compliance report to the government.
The amount so distributed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune and shall be distributed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under him.
