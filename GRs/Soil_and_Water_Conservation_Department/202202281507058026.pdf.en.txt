Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. It is being implemented. A total of 79 schemes under the Nagpur Circle Office will be repaired. 1069.66 lakh.
After the restoration of these projects, 2258.34 hectares of irrigated area and 7072.15 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. A proposal to get administrative approval for these works has been submitted to the government under the above reference letter. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nagpur has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. Considering the above, the proposal was under consideration for administrative approval as 79 works mentioned in the revised plan of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme were included in the second priority. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Sanvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 79 repair schemes in Nagpur Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 1069.66 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
