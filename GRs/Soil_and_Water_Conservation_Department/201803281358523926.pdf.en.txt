Introduction: In order to see the results of watershed development in a short period of time, Dhadak program was taken to complete 1 watershed in each of the 353 talukas of the state in 2007-08. On the same lines, incomplete watersheds have been completed in a project-based manner under the Integrated Watershed Development Programme from 2008-09 to 2016-17.
Under this programme, 3925 micro watersheds have been completed in a dynamic manner since 2007-08.
Now, for the financial year 2017-18, the Government was considering to give administrative and financial approval for the completion of incomplete watershed projects under the Integrated Watershed Development Programme in a project-based and dynamic manner.
Government Decision: Development of land through soil conservation measures Integrated Watershed Development Programme (ICDP) is being given administrative and financial approval for implementation during 2017-18. Under this programme, as per the terms and conditions of the Government decision dated 30th November 2007, approval is being given to complete the incomplete watershed projects in the state in a progressive manner on the lines of the Accelerated Watershed Development Programme. For this, DPDC General, Scheduled Castes Component Programme and Tribal Component Programme and Tribal External Component Programme and State level funds have been budgeted.
