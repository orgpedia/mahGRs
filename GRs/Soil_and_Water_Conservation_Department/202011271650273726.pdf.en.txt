Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works on progress/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 300.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-5, head 4702A019 in 2020-21. Out of this, Rs. 30.00 crore is for eradicating the irrigation backlog of Hingoli district. At present, 50% of the budgeted funds are available for disbursement.
The following decisions have been taken regarding the allocation of funds for the Vidarbha region for the works recommended as per the relevant proposals:
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 153.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Three Lakhs only) towards payment of dues for the works done on Nimbhora, Kolhapur method embankment in Akola District subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) The funds being distributed through the Water Conservation Corporation, the District Planning Committee or any other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above projects, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) The funds shall not be spent beyond the cost of administrative approval as well as revised administrative approval approved by the Government; (c) If there is no need for funds for the works of the above projects, the funds shall not be disbursed for any other project under any circumstances; and (d) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the funds shall be immediately returned to the Government.
