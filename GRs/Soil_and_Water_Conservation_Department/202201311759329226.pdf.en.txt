Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 4 is being implemented. A total of 7 schemes under the Nashik Circle Office will be repaired. 432.22 lakh. After the restoration of these projects, 189 hectares of irrigated area and 1027 sq.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 5 has been submitted to the government.
