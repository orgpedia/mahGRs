Preamble: Demand for works in non-tribal areas under Cement Dam (Check Dam) Programme No. A sum of Rs. 50.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2023-24 under ZH-5, Account Head 44022799. Out of which, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal for seeking funds from the funds made available as per the relevant circulars of the Finance Department, dated 11/08/2023.
Decision: The Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 59.20 lakhs (Rupees Fifty-nine Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) for the payment of pending dues of 11 dams namely Moradi-1, 2, 3 in Telhara Taluka and Umra- 1,2,3, Belura-1, 2 in Akot Taluka and Belura- 1,2,3, 3 composite Gabian dams in Akot Taluka as proposed in the context of Akola District.
The aforesaid funds are being distributed subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) If funds have been made available from the Water Conservation Corporation, the District Planning Committee or any other source for the works of the above projects, the funds being distributed through this Fund shall not be withdrawn or distributed,
(b) No amount shall be spent in excess of the cost of administrative sanction as well as revised administrative sanction approved by the Government.
