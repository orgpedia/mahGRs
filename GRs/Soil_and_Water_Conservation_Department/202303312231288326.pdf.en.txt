Under the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme, it is proposed to repair the water conservation treatment structures in order to restore the irrigation capacity by preserving and repairing the schemes that require maintenance and repair of the completed water conservation schemes in the state. A total of 16 schemes of Jalna district under Aurangabad Circle Office will be revised and all these schemes are in Priority 2. After the restoration of these projects, 730 hectares of irrigated area and 3294.36 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal has been sent to the government for administrative approval. The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad has scrutinized/ verified all the estimates at his level and certified the same. In view of the above, administrative approval was under consideration for the amendment proposal of the 16 works mentioned.
Government Decision: The total budget of 16 repair schemes of Aurangabad Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 476.79 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
