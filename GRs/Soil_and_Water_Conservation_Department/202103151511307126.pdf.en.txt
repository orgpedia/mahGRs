Preamble: A sum of Rs. 400.00 crore was budgeted in 2020-21 for conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. As per the revised estimate, Rs. 300.00 crore is available. Reference from it.
The matter of payment of the obligations assumed as on 16/05/2017 was under consideration of the Government.
The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
The Decision:
With reference to the sanctioned liability of the projects having 0 to 100 HAFCs where the repair works have been completed and the pending payments have been examined by the tertiary system and the reports have been submitted to the Government. Reference No. 2. The Government hereby approve disbursement of total amount of Rs. 263.50 lakh (Rupees Two Crore Sixty Three Lakh Fifty Lakh only) for 27 works as per Form-A attached for payment of pending liabilities of eligible and completed works as per Clause 3, subject to the following conditions and conditions.

A) Funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring completion of repair work, b) amount should not be paid more than the sanctioned cost, c) if funds remain unutilized due to any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without distributing it for any other purpose.
d) Follow the guidelines given in the circular.
The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad, shall draw the above funds and disburse the same to the concerned District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department expeditiously.
