The Proposal: -
The Government decision on implementation of Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan 2.0 has been issued on 3rd January, 2023. Accordingly, Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan 2.0 is being implemented in the state. As per the Government Decision dated 3rd January, 2023, for review, control and effective implementation of Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan 2.0, District Collectors at the district level have been directed to ensure that the programme is implemented effectively.
A district-level committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of the District Collector and a taluk-level committee under the chairmanship of the Sub-Divisional Officer (Province) at the taluk level. The Member Secretary of the District Level Committee is the District Water Conservation, Soil and Water Conservation Department and the Member Secretary of the Taluk Level Committee is the Sub Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department. The post of Member Secretary of the District Level and Taluk Level Committee should be transferred to the District Superintendent Agriculture Officer and the post of Member Secretary of the Taluk Level Committee should be handed over to the Taluk Agriculture Officer and accordingly, the revised District Level and Taluk Level Committee should be transferred to the Taluk Level Committee.
The matter of formation was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Review of Jalyukt Shivar Mission 2.0 under paragraph 7 of Government Decision No. JASIA-2022 / Q. 302 / JAL-7, dated 3rd January, 2023 The following are the District Level Committees and Taluk Level Committees constituted at the district level under the Chairmanship of the Collector and at the Taluk level under the Chairmanship of the Sub-Divisional Officer (Province) for effective control and implementation of the programme.
