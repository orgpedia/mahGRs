Declaration of Deputy Commissioner (Finance) Commissioner Office, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Soil and Water Conservation
Government Resolution No: Establishment-2020/ PR No.75/ Water-2
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date:- 06.03.2020
Government Resolution No: Establishment-2016/ PR No.88 (Part-9)/ Jal-2, D. Government Notification General Administration Department No. Shakani 2017/ Q. No. 120/18 (R. And why ). 26.9.2017 Government Decision-:
The Deputy Commissioner (Finance), Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer in respect of all the officers and employees of all the offices under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation to handle all the cases like preservation of their service books, updating of records therein, encashment of leave, sanction of annual increment, sanction of advance in provident fund, sending of retirement cases to the Accountant General.
(2) On the appointment of a regular officer to the post of Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad, the said drawing and distribution powers conferred on the Deputy Commissioner as aforesaid shall stand automatically terminated.
