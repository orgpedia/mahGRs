Introduction: The above reference No. 1 The Government hereby vide Government Resolution dated 31.05.2017 has approved the creation of the Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation along with the restructuring of the field machinery of the Department with a total strength of 16479 posts including Group' A,"' B" and' C" cadre posts in Maharashtra Engineering Service in Water Conservation System.
The above reference No. 2 As per the notification dated 26.09.2017, a separate Department by the name of Soil and Water Conservation Department has been notified.
Further, in the meanwhile, with the approval of the High Powered Committee and subsequently the Council of Ministers, the aforesaid Reference No. 3 here. As per the Government decision dated 01.03.2019, out of total 16479 posts, 205 posts have been abolished and 149 posts have been restored.
However, the matter of classification and number of posts in the cadre of Maharashtra Engineering Service Group' A,"' B" and' C" in the water conservation system was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Now, the Government is determining the rank, classification and number of posts in the cadre of Maharashtra Engineering Services Group' A',' B' and' C' on the basis of the number of posts in the above approved diagram for the Soil and Water Conservation Department through this Government Decision as follows:
