Preamble: Maharashtra Jal Sanrakshan Nigam has a pending liability of Rs. 3490.91 crore at the end of March, 2022 for projects in progress. Further, 4324 new schemes have been accorded administrative approval during the period from 01.04.2022 to 31.05.2022 at a cost of Rs. 6191.00 crore. Out of which, 4037 works costing Rs. 5020.74 crore are at various stages of tendering. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Decision: Tenders for all works at all levels of the tender process for the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation are hereby cancelled. Accordingly, tenders for any work in the tender process should not be finalised. Also, work orders should not be issued for any work for which tenders have been finalised.
2. Taluk-wise list of tenders cancelled as above (project-wise) should be submitted by all concerned District Water Conservation Officers to the respective Government.
The decision of the Government of Maharashtra has been digitally signed.
