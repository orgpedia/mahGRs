Government Order: In the meeting held on 22/03/2022, after reviewing the suspension of Shri Ramesh Hiralal Gupta, District Water Conservation Officer, the General Administration Department has issued a notification regarding suspension of Shri Ramesh Hiralal Gupta, District Water Conservation Officer. It is recommended to be reinstated in the Government service in a non-executive post subject to departmental/ judicial enquiry as per the provisions of the Government Order dated 09.07.2019. Accordingly, the Government has decided to revoke the suspension of Mr. Gupta, District Water Conservation Officer and reinstate him in Government service subject to departmental/ judicial enquiry against him.
2. The Government hereby reinstate Mr. Gupta, District Water Conservation Officer, to a non-executive post in the Government service subject to the departmental/ judicial enquiry pending against him. After reinstatement, Mr. Gupta is being posted as Associate Professor and District Water Conservation Officer, Valmi, Aurangabad.
The proceedings of the departmental/ judicial enquiry against Mr. Gupta, District Water Conservation Officer shall continue even after his reinstatement. Mr. Gupta, the suspension of the District Water Conservation Officer will actually end from the date he actually joins the place of appointment.
