Preamble: Under the Government Decision, Rural Development and Water Conservation Department No. 2016/ PR No. 88 (Part-9)/ Jal-2, dated 31.5.2017, the field machinery was restructured with the establishment of the Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation and a total of 16479 posts were earmarked for this purpose.
