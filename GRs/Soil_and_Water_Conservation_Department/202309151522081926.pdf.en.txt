Government circular
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department and the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, the temporary seniority of the cadre of the District Water Conservation Officer in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as on 01.01.2021 is mentioned in the above Reference No. 2 was issued by the government. In this regard, Refer No. A corrigendum has been issued under Section 3.
Now reference no. The provisional seniority and corrigendum issued under Sections 2 and 3 are being regularized vide Government Notification General Administration Department No.SRV-2016/ PR No. 281/12, No. The revised Temporary Seniority as on 01.01.2021 for the cadre of District Water Conservation Officer as per the provisions of 21.06.2021 is being published as" Annexure-A" annexed to this Circular.
3. The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list mentioned in Annexure-A annexed to this circular shall be the date of issue of the Government Notification to the General Administration Department No. SRV 2016/ Q.No. 281/12, D. As per the provision of 21.06.2021, the seniority in the cadre of District Water Conservation Officer will be considered for determining the seniority.
4. The said circular shall be brought to the immediate notice of the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad, the District Water Conservation Officer under him in Annexure A and a copy of the same shall be preserved in his own records and made available to the Government.

In case any error/ error is found in the details of District Water Conservation Officers included in the said seniority list in respect of name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., then bring your representations/ objections/ suggestions to the Government through registered post/ speed post/ hand delivery along with necessary proof in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. If the mistakes/ errors in writing are not brought to the notice of the Government within the stipulated time, the temporary seniority of the District Water Conservation Officer working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation will be finalized as of 01.01.2021.
