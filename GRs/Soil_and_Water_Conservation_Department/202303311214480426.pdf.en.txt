Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works on progress/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with an irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 150.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2022-23 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A028. Now, as per the revised estimates, the proposal for seeking funds from the funds currently made available under the scheme as on 16/03/2023 has been decided as under.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 230.14 lakhs (Rupees Two Crores Thirty Lakhs Fourteen Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues for the construction of storage tank Ajni (Phulamala) project in Nandgaon Khandeshwar Taluka of Amravati district with an irrigation potential of 249 hectares.
The aforesaid funds are being distributed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds being distributed through the Water Conservation Corporation, District Planning Committee or other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above projects, shall not be withdrawn or distributed. b) The funds shall not be spent beyond the cost of administrative approval as well as revised administrative approval approved by the Government. c) If there is no need for funds for the above projects, the funds shall not be distributed for other projects under any circumstances. d) If the funds remain unspent for any reason, the funds shall be immediately returned to the Government.
