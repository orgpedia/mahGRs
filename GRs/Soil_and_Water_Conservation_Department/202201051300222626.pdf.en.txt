Preamble: The Government is implementing the programme for removal of irrigation backlog in Hingoli district from 2017-18. For this, 6785 HCX has to be created, which is currently about 1794. Construction projects are underway. While 157 projects with a capacity of 2349 HCP are under consideration, another 2642 are under approval. The objective of CX formation is to be achieved.
Therefore, the government has decided to transfer the projects approved by the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation in Hingoli district, which have not yet started work, to the state plan.
Decision: 04 of Hingoli district with a capacity of 0 to 100 H.C. which has been given administrative approval under the order of Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation dated 20th January, 2021

Government approval is hereby accorded for the sub-division under C. S. Small Irrigation Schemes (Head 4702A019).
Necessary action should be initiated immediately to implement all the projects transferred to the State Plan as above.
