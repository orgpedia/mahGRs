In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 under 21.06.2021, a provisional interim seniority of the category "District Water Conservation Officer (Construction) and other equivalent posts" in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation dated 01.01.2025 is attached to this circular.
Being published as "Annexure-A."
The date of seniority mentioned in front of the names of the officers in the seniority list mentioned in "Form-A" attached with this circular is as per Government Notification No. SRV-2016 / PR No.281 / Bara, D. As per the provision in the 21.06.2021, District Water Conservation Officer (Architecture) and other equivalent posts will be considered for determining seniority in this cadre.
3. The said circular shall be brought to the immediate notice of the District Water Conservation Officer in Form 'A' under the Commissioner, Mrid B. Water Conservation, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar.
Save their signed copy in their own archives and make SATA and a copy available to the government.
If any errors or omissions are found in the particulars of the District Water Conservation Rights included in this seniority list, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste, category, place of seniority, etc., bring your statements / objections / suggestions to the notice of the Government by registered post / speed post / hand-delivery, along with the requisite proof in writing, within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. Subject to the Department of Soil and Water Conservation assuming that no objection is raised if the errors / omissions are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the prescribed time.
