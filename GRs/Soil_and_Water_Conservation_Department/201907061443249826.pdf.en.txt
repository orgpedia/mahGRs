Government's decision
A Special Investigation Team (SIT) is being constituted to conduct a thorough investigation into the loss of lives and property due to the breach of Tiware Dam in Chiplun Taluka of Ratnagiri district on 02.07.2019.
(1) Mr. Avinash Surve, Secretary, Water Resources and Executive Director, Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation, Nagpur- Team Head
(ii) Chief Engineer (Minor Irrigation) Water Conservation- Member (iii) Collector Ratnagiri- Member (iv) District Superintendent of Police, Ratnagiri- Member
The functions of the Special Investigation Team shall be as follows:
(i) To find out the cause of the breach of the Tiware Dam and fix responsibility on the culprits and conduct a supplementary inquiry accordingly.
(ii) to suggest measures to prevent such incidents in future in respect of minor irrigation tanks, Pazar tanks, village tanks, Malguzari tanks and other similar tanks.
The Special Investigation Team should submit its report to the government within two months.
