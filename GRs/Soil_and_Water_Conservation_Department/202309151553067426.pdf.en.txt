Government circular:
The General Administration Department has issued a notification. 21.06.2021 in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the Regional Water Conservation Officer in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation has been appointed as Regional Water Conservation Officer with effect from 01.01.2020. The aforesaid Reference No. of Provisional Seniority dated 01.01.2021. 2 was issued by the government.
2. The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list in Form-A annexed to this circular shall be as per the Government Notification General Administration Department No. SRV-2016/ PR No.
281/ 12, Dt. As per the provision of 21.06.2021, the seniority in the cadre of Regional Water Conservation Officer will be considered for determining the seniority.
3. The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad shall bring the said circular to the immediate notice of the Regional Water Conservation Officer in Form A under him and shall preserve a signed copy of the communication in his own records and make a copy of the same available to the Government.
Therefore, now as per the accompanying' Form A', the Regional Water Conservation Officer of the cadre will be appointed.
The seniority of 01.01.2021 is being finally published through this circular.
