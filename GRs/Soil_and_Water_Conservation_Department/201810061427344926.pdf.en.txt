Introduction- Mauje Godegaon Pazar Lake in Jalgaon district. Jamner, G. Jalgaon regarding granting administrative approval to the scheme. Proposal from the Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune vide letter No. P.S.- 3/6592/ D. 30.12.2016 was submitted to the Government. The reservoir has an irrigation potential of 58 hectares and a storage capacity of 284 cubic metres. This water will be used for irrigation. Considering the possibility of the above scheme reducing the permanent scarcity of water in the concerned areas, the plan, which does not fit into the financial parameters, is proposed to be given administrative approval as a special case without the approval of the Expenditure and Priority Committee. The Cabinet gave. The decision was taken at the meeting held on 29.08.2018.
