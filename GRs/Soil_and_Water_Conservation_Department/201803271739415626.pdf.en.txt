Preamble: A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been budgeted for the survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares for the financial year 2017-18 under Demand No. L-3, Account Head 2702 4745, out of which Rs. 8.00 crore within 80% limit has been made available for disbursement through the Circular. It was under the consideration of the government to disburse the eligible works in the fund demand proposals submitted by the regional system within the limits of the statutory development board-wise standard. In this regard, the Government has decided to provide funds for the works recommended under Reference No. 2 as follows:
Government's decision
For the financial year 2017-18, the Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 14.97 lakhs (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand only) for the survey works of 16 Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Projects in Aurangabad District in Form A, with irrigation potential ranging from 0 to 100 hectares, subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the Executive Engineer concerned shall, before the actual disbursement of funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable.
(b) No amount in excess of the approved price shall be disbursed, etc.) The Sub-Divisional Office shall ensure that no funds are disbursed twice for the same survey work.
If there is no need for funds for any reason, the amount distributed should not be spent for mutual work under any circumstances.
(a) If for any reason the fund remains unutilized, the unutilized amount shall be immediately returned to the Government.
f) Strictly follow the guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds.
The amount so disbursed shall be appropriated by the Superintending Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Board, Aurangabad and distributed to his subordinates expeditiously.
