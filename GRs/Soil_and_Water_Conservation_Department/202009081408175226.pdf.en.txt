Introduction: The Small Business Plan. Out of the approved cost of Rs. 20.58 crore for Jawhar, G. Palghar project, Rs. 6.06 crore has been disbursed so far. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 2.00 crore has been sought under the Reference Proposal for payment of dues for the work done on this project which is in progress. Based on the available funds, the following decisions have been taken:
The Decision:
Approval is hereby accorded to the Government for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 27.00 lakh (Rupees Twenty Seven Lakhs only) for the Kapricha Pada Minor Irrigation Scheme in Palghar District having an irrigation potential of 126 hectares subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) The amount being disbursed by the Water Conservation Corporation or any other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above project, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) No amount shall be spent in excess of the cost of administrative sanction as well as revised administrative sanction approved by the Government.
(d) If for any reason the funds remain unutilized, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
e) Strictly follow the guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Thane and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under him.
