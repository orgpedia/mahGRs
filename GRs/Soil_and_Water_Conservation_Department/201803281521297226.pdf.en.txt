Introduction: Water conservation work under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme is of great importance. As per the Government decision dated 5th December, 2014, Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan has been launched in the State with a target of making 5000 villages water scarcity free by 2017-18. The target is to make 5000 villages drought-free every year. For this, public participation is very important and it is very important to campaign and publicize through various media to increase public participation in the campaign.
As per the relevant Government Decision dated 25/8/2008, Watershed Development Movement Programme is being implemented in the State to make the objectives and importance of soil and water conservation known to the general public. Under this, in the parade organized on the occasion of Republic Day on 26th January, 2018, a joint tableau based on the theme of" Samruddha Maharashtra Janakalyan Yojana" of ROHYO Department and" Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan" of Soil and Water Conservation Department was included. Through this tableau, the important scheme of soil and water conservation was promoted and the matter of reimbursement of the expenses incurred for it was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the work of tableaux for the Republic Day Parade, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, through the Government Decision dated 12th October, 2017 of the General Administration Department, has included M.R.W.Promotions Private Limited in the list of advertising agencies. Mumbai was chosen as the venue. For this, the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations had issued a work order to the advertising agency on January 20, 2018.
