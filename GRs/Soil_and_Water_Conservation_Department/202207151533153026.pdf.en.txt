Additional Commissioner, Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, under Amravati Sub-Division, Chandur Bazar. Vijay Sudharkar Dandge passed away on 03.09.2019. In case of death of a member in service under the Defined Contribution Pension Scheme/ National Pension Scheme, Reference No. Under the 3rd application, Smt. Sheela Sudhakar Dandge, mother of the applicant, has applied for grant of ex-gratia of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakhs). Following this application, the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation (MRW) Aurangabad, has issued a notification to this effect. Vijay Sudharkar Dandge has submitted a proposal to the Government for sanction of ex-gratia grant of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakhs) to the nominee i.e. his mother Smt. Sheela Sudhakara Dandge.
Department of Finance As per the powers vested in the Head of Administrative Department vide Government Resolution No.Anneo-2017/ PR No. 29/ Service-4 dated 29.9.2018, as per the provision of Roll No.5, the late Shri. The ex-gratia of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh only) is being sanctioned to the legal heir of Vijay Sudharkar Dandge, i.e. his mother Smt. Sheela Sudhakara Dandge.
The ex-gratia grant is being sanctioned to the Government employees who died on 03.09.2019 before completing 10 years of service. This order shall remain in force for a period of one year from the date of issue of this order.
