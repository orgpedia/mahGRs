The programme of construction of cement embankments in taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
As per the demand for funds for the cement embankment completed under the river rejuvenation programme in Ahmednagar district, a total of Rs. A sum of Rs. 17.53 lakhs was sanctioned.
Government's decision:
During the year 2020-21, Cement Dam Programme Account Head 4402 2799 involving an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) has been budgeted.
Under the River Rejuvenation Programme, the cement embankment of the embankment sanctioned in the year 2016-17 in Ahmednagar district has been completed at a total cost of Rs. 17.53 lakh only) is being approved for disbursement as under:
