Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 30.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2020-21 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9201. Of this, 10% of the funds have been made available for distribution. Out of which, the government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal from the fund for Marathwada division as follows.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount equivalent to Rs. 56.25 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs only) currently available for payment of eligible and completed survey works in the reference proposals.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions: a) The District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall re-verify before the actual disbursement of the funds to the Government that the amount demanded is reasonable, b) The sub-divisional office concerned shall verify that the funds are not being disbursed twice for the above survey work, c) If there is no need for funds due to any reasons, the disbursed amount should not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances, d) Any amount in excess of the sanctioned cost should not be disbursed, e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the unutilized amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
