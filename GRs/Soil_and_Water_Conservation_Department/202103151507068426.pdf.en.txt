Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 30.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2020-21 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9201. Out of this, 35% of the funds have been made available for distribution. The Government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal from the funds for the rest of the Maharashtra Division as follows.
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse an amount of Rs. 108.66 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Eight Lakh Sixty Six Thousand only), currently available for payment of eligible and completed survey works in the relevant proposals of Nashik District.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the concerned District Water Conservation Officer shall again verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable before disbursing the actual funds.
