Preamble: A sum of Rs. 100.00 crore has been budgeted for new/ ongoing projects of irrigation potential of 101-250 hectares in FY 2020-21 under Demand No. ZH-5, Account Head 47029028. Of this, 10% has been made available for disbursement. The government has decided to make the following recommendations from the funds for the Marathwada region.
Decision: The Government hereby grant approval to disburse the balance liability in the limit of approved cost of 08 works in the reference fund demand proposal for Nanded district aggregating to Rs. 73.00 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Seven Lakhs only) subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) the amount being disbursed by the Water Conservation Corporation or any other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above project, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) no amount shall be spent in excess of the cost of administrative sanction as well as revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) if funds are not required for the works of the above project, the amount shall not be disbursed for any other project under any circumstances; and (d) in case of non-availability of funds for any reason, the amount shall be returned to the Government forthwith.
