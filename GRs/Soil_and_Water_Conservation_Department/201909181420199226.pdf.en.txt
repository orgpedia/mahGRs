Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 8.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-3, head 27029219 for the year 2019-20. Out of this, Rs. 4.80 crore has been made available for disbursement through circulars subject to a ceiling of 60%. Among them, the government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal submitted by the regional body for the fund for Vidarbha division as follows.
The Decision:
The Government hereby grant approval for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 1.23 crore (Rupees One Crore Twenty Three Lakh only) for survey work of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of 101 to 250 hectares of irrigation potential of Chandrapur Division in the relevant proposal subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the Executive Engineer concerned shall, before the actual disbursement of funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable.
