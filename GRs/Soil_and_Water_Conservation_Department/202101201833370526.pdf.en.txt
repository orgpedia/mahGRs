The amendment of the water conservation treatment structures is proposed in line with the restoration of irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the schemes that require maintenance and repair from the completed water conservation schemes in the state. A total of 2 schemes will be repaired under the Thane Circle Office and the total amount for repair is Rs. 0.1812 crore. After the restoration of these projects, 55 hectares of irrigated area and 535 sq.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for administrative approval of these works is given above. As per letter dated 6/11/2020, Assistant Chief Engineer, Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area, Pune has submitted to the Government. The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Thane has scrutinized/ verified all the budgets at his level and certified the same. In view of the above, administrative approval was sought for the amendment proposal of the above mentioned 2 works. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The total cost of 2 repair schemes in Thane Regional Water Conservation Division is estimated at Rs. 0.1812 crore (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
