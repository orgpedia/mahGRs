The programme of construction of cement embankments in taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
As per the demand for funds for the cement embankment completed under the river rejuvenation programme in Osmanabad district, a total of Rs. 103.02 lakh (Rupees One Crore Three Lakh Two Thousand only) was under consideration.
Government's decision:
During the year 2020-21, Cement Dam Programme Account Head 4402 2799 involving an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) has been budgeted. Under the River Rejuvenation Programme, the cement embankment of Osmanabad district, which was completed in the year 2017-18, has received a total amount of Rs. 103.02 lakh (Rupees One Crore Three Lakh Two Thousand only) is being sanctioned as follows:

The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Officer concerned are declared as Controlling Officer and the District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending the funds being made available for cement construction. The Controller should ensure that the funds are spent on the objectives for which they are distributed.
The funds being disbursed are subject to the following conditions:
3.1 Cement bond- If funds are available from other sources, the amount of funds to be distributed by the said Government Decision or
should not be distributed. The District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district should ensure that the expenditure is not misused.
3.2 No amount shall be spent in excess of the administrative sanction or the price agreed to by the Government.
3.3 If the funds provided remain unspent for any reason, the unspent funds shall not be used for any other purpose. The money should be immediately returned to the government.
3.4 The full utilization certificate and the certificate of completion of the work given so far should be submitted to the government immediately.
The approved funds should be spent under the following head of accounts.
