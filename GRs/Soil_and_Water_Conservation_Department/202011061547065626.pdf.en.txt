Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 251 to 600 hectares. A sum of Rs. 5.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-3, head 2702 9228 in 2020-21. Out of which Rs. 50.00 lakhs is available for disbursement of funds within the limit of 10%. The government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal submitted by the zonal machinery for the Marathwada division of the state as follows.
The Decision:
In response to the proposal for survey and exploration of 03 storage tanks of Pimparwada, Rohtwadi and Disalya Wadi in Beed district, the Government hereby grants approval for disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 6.42 lakh (Rupees Six Lakh Forty Two Thousand only) subject to the following conditions and conditions.
The District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall re-verify the amount demanded from the Government before actual disbursement of funds so that no amount is disbursed beyond the reasonable amount for the survey, b) The sub-divisional office concerned shall ensure that the funds are not disbursed twice for the above survey work, c) If there is no need for funds for any reason, the amount disbursed should not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances, d) Any amount in excess of the sanctioned cost should not be disbursed, e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the unutilized amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
f) Strictly follow the guidelines in the relevant circulars of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad and shall be promptly disbursed to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them.
