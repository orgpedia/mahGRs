The Proposal:
The Central Land Resources Department, Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India has issued Common Guidelines 2021 for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Watershed Development Component 2.0. Accordingly, the Prime Minister's Agriculture Irrigation Scheme Watershed Development Component 2.0 will be implemented in the state. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 27.04.2022. The Centre and the state share in the scheme is 60:40. Under the letter of 2, the Central Government has allotted Central share under the general component of Rs. 4240.00 lakhs has been provided. Accordingly, the matter of distribution of Central share funds and corresponding 40% State share funds was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Central share of 60% under the general component (head 44022743) for implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Watershed Development Component 2.0 amounting to Rs. 42,40,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Four Crores Forty Lakhs only) and State Share of 40% (Account Head 44022752) amounting to Rs. The Government is approving disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 28,26,33, 333/- (Rupees Twenty Eight Crores Twenty Six Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Three Hundred Thirty Three only).
The Deputy Commissioner (Finance), Office of the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Chief Executive Officer, Vasundhara Watershed Development System, as the Controlling Officer to spend this fund.
While utilising the above funds, care should be taken to comply with the procedures, guidelines and terms and conditions issued from time to time by the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Rural Development of the Central Government.
