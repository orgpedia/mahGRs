The Proposal:
Department of Industry, Energy and Labor. Revised Manual of Procedure for procurement of offices by Government Departments has been issued on 01/12/2016. The composition of the Procurement Committees to be constituted immediately by the respective Procurement Departments/ Offices at the level of Administrative Division (itself)/ Division level/ District level of the Ministry as mentioned in paragraphs 2.9.3 and 2.9.4 of the said Manual. Soil and Water Conservation Department was earlier a Sub-Division of Rural Development and Water Conservation Department. It has been operational since April 1, 2018. Accordingly, the matter of constitution of Purchase Committee for procurement of goods/ services required by the Department was under consideration.
Government Decision: Department of Industry, Energy and Labour Government Decision. Pursuant to the provisions contained in Para 2.9.3 of the Rules, dated 01/12/2016, the following Procurement Committees are being constituted for procurement of goods/ services required for the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, Ministry (itself):

(2) The Procurement Committee while procuring essential goods/ services for the Soil and Water Conservation Department (itself) shall proceed in accordance with the procedure laid down in the Government Decision in the context of the Department of Industries, Energy and Labour. If the annual value of the procurement is less than Rs 10 lakh, the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary, Soil and Water Conservation Department as the head of the department will have the right to take a decision regarding the procurement without convening a meeting of the procurement committee.
