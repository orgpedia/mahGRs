Circular: The general duties and responsibilities of Group-A (Director General, Managing Director, Additional Commissioner, Water Conservation, Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer, Regional Vigilance and Quality Control Officer, Regional Water Conservation Officer, District Water Conservation Officer and Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer) in and under the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation Office, Government of Maharashtra are being determined as mentioned in Form" A" attached herewith.
