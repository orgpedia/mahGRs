Preamble: Demand No. 1 for conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 450.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2022-23 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046. Out of which, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal for seeking funds from the funds made available as per the relevant circular of the Department of Finance, dated 07/11/2022.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 25.33 lakhs (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of Laghupatbandhare Talab Kinhi in Ballarpur Taluka of Chandrapur District.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) No amount in excess of the sanctioned cost should be paid, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for any other purpose, d) Guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
