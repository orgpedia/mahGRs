Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works on progress/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 300.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-5, head 4702A019 in 2020-21. Out of this, Rs. 30.00 crore is for eradicating the irrigation backlog of Hingoli district. At present, only 10% of the budgeted funds are available for disbursement.
Out of this, the following decisions have been taken on the reference proposal from the funds allocated for the remaining Maharashtra divisions.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 93.40 lakhs (Rupees Ninety Three Lakhs Forty Four Thousand only) towards payment of dues for the works done on 09 Cement Nala embankments in Sangli District subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) The funds being distributed through the Water Conservation Corporation, the District Planning Committee or any other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above projects, shall not be withdrawn or distributed; (b) The funds shall not be spent beyond the cost of administrative approval as well as revised administrative approval approved by the Government; and (c) If funds are not required for the works of the above projects, the funds shall not be distributed among themselves for any other project.
