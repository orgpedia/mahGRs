Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works on progress/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with an irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 100.00 crore was budgeted in 2020-21 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702 9028. As per the revised estimate, Rs. 75.00 crore is available for disbursement. The following decisions have been taken in this regard:
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a sum of Rs. 385.00 lakhs (Rupees Three Crores Eighty Five Lakhs only) towards payment of pending dues for the work done on the scheme of Shishupatbandhare Talav Anusharap, which will create an irrigation potential of 165 hectares in Bhudargad Taluka of Kolhapur district, subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) If funds are available from the Water Conservation Corporation or other sources for the above project works, the amount being disbursed through this scheme should not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) The amount should not be spent more than the cost of administrative sanction and revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) If funds are not required for the above project works, the amount should not be disbursed for other projects under any circumstances; (d) If funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government; (e) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
(f) The works have been completed 100% and no complaints have been received and the quality of the work should be ensured and payment should be made and the compliance report should be submitted to the government.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under him.
