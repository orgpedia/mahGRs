Preamble: Project Management Consultants (PMCs) have been suggested as per the relevant Government Decision dated 06.01.2022 for project management of 0-600 Hs of projects under water conservation treatment structures till completion of construction, maintenance and repair works and their scope, scope, modalities and guidelines have been prescribed. Accordingly, the matter of payment of consultation fee of the Project Management Consultants (PMC) suggested for Aurangabad Regional Area was under the consideration of the Government. Reference No. 3 The following decisions have been taken on the funding request proposals.
Decision: May, proposed for Aurangabad Regional Area. Shrikhande Consultants Pvt. Ltd., Project Management Consultants, is hereby accorded approval for disbursement of total amount of Rs. 36.24 lakhs (Rs. Thirty Six Lakhs Twenty Four Thousand only) towards demand of Rs. 10.16 lakhs, Rs. 20.30 lakhs, Rs. 2.70 lakhs and Rs. 3.08 lakhs for the works/ services completed under Mukhyamantri Jal Sanvardhan Yojana in the districts of Aurangabad, Latur, Beed and Jalna respectively.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) The project management consultants (PMCs) must make separate additional provision in the budget of the repair works undertaken under the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme for the consultancy rate as per the cost of the project repair work.

b) The expenditure of the above disbursed funds should be shown separately on the repair works undertaken under the Mukhyamantri Jal Sanvardhan Yojana in which the Project Management Consultants (PMC) have provided services.
(c) No amount shall be paid in excess of the administrative/ revised administrative sanction cost of the project concerned at the time of disbursement of the said fund. Funds should be distributed on the basis of payment.
d) The total amount of consultation fee payable for the services rendered by the Project Management Consultants (PMC) shall not exceed 2% of the total cost of the project (excluding ownership charges and incidental costs).
e) Reference No. 1 regarding services rendered by Project Management Consultants (PMC). 2 It should be ensured that the procedures, guidelines and timelines in the government decision here have been complied with.
f) If the fund remains unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for any other purpose, c) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of the fund should be strictly followed.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them.
The expenditure on this should be covered under the following heads and should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2022-23.
