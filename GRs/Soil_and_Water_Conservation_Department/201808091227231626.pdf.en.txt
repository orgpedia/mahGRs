GOVERNMENT Circular: The Department of Soil and Water Conservation has included the officers/ employees working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as per the Government Decision dated 07.06.2018 and 22.06.2018.
In the statement A accompanying this Government decision, the officers who have been given the option to be permanently attached to the Soil and Water Conservation Department have been included and their services have been permanently transferred to the establishment of this department. The said Government Decision provides that the service excellence lists of Group' A' and Group' B' cadres on the establishment of this Department shall be published separately from the Government level after examining the service excellence lists of all the officers/ employees mentioned in the said Return' A'.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department, No. 1, and the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, the temporary interim seniority of the cadre of District Water Conservation Officer (erstwhile designation Executive Engineer in the Water Resources Department) in the Soil and Water Conservation Department, dated 01.01.2018, is being published as per the" Form-A" attached to this circular.
