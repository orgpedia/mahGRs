The Proposal:
K. P. Bandhara Brahmpuri Ta. Hingoli G. Hingoli scheme is proposed on a local drain near Brahmapuri village. The total storage capacity of the scheme is 136.85 cubic metres and 51 villages. It is planned to provide irrigation benefits to the area.
K. P. Bandhara Brahmpuri Ta. Hingoli G. 140.30 lakh was originally approved by the Government vide Reference Government Resolution No. 1 based on the data of 2013-14.

The First Amendment Approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
4.1 New elements that create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without government approval.
4.2 On completion of the project, the project will be funded by the Department of Rural Development vide G.D. 31/7/2000 and the Department of Soil and Water Conservation vide G.D. 31/7/2000. 4.3 It shall be the responsibility of the concerned field functionaries to ensure that the quality and standards are maintained while implementing the said work.
