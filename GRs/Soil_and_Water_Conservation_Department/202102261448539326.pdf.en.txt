Introduction: The Central Government has passed the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and the Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Full Participation and Protection of Rights) Act, 1995. The Maharashtra State Policy for Persons with Disabilities has been approved under the Government Resolution No. 4 of the General Administration Department, prescribing 4% reservation for Physically Handicapped persons in government service posts.
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Central Government has notified the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities as per notification dated 4.1.2021 in Reference No. Accordingly, the instructions regarding the reservation of posts in the State Government have been issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance vide Government Resolution dated 2.2.2021 in Reference No.6.
As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 2.2.2021 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, the matter of ensuring equivalent posts in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation in the list accompanying the notification dated 4.1.2021 of the Central Government was under the consideration of the Government for the Divyang category.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the provisions of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the posts in Group-A and Group-B cadres in the Maharashtra Engineering Services under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation are being ensured for the disabled as shown in the table attached to it as per the instructions in the Government Decision dated 2.2.2021 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, in comparison to the posts in the list ensured for the disabled under the notification dated 4.1.2021 by the Central Government.

As per the provisions of the Government Order dated 29.5.2019 of the General Administration Department, 4% reservation will be applicable for recruitment by direct service/ nomination to the posts of disabled persons in the direct service/ nomination quota in Group A and Group B under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation mentioned in Annexure-A.
(3) The physical eligibility criteria for Divyang candidates for the posts to be filled by direct service/ nomination for Group-A and Group-B through Maharashtra Public Service Commission shall be as per Annexure-A. After receiving the recommendation of the candidates from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission, only after such disabled candidate is certified by a medical board consisting of experts in the subject concerned regarding physical qualification, the disabled person will be appointed in the government service in the relevant cadre taking into account the following criteria:
(a) if qualified in accordance with the provisions of the Service Entry Rules for the respective posts, (b) if so empowered by the Medical Board having regard to the duties and responsibilities of the posts in the respective cadre,
