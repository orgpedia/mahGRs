Distribution of funds The legislative development has to be done as per the board-wise formula prescribed by the Governor. Government's decision:
The Government is hereby granting approval for disbursement of an amount of Rs. 40.00 lakh (Rupees Forty Lakh only) for detailed survey of 450 hectares of Tukai lift irrigation scheme benefit area in Ahmednagar district under Kukadi Project.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the Executive Engineer concerned shall, before the actual disbursement of funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable.
b) The concerned sub-divisional office should verify that the above work of the survey is not being distributed twice.
