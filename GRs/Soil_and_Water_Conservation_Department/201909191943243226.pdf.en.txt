Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 8.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2019-20 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9219. Out of this, Rs. 4.80 crore has been made available for disbursement through circulars subject to a ceiling of 60%. The Government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal submitted by the zonal machinery for the remaining funds for the Maharashtra division as follows.
Government's decision:
Survey of Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Projects with an Irrigation Capacity of 101 to 250 Hectares. In the proposals dated 07/09/19, a total amount of Rs. 2.50 crore (Rs.2.50 crore only) for Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg districts and Rs. 50.00 lakh for Satara district is being handed over to Regional Water Conservation Officers (Soil Conservation), Thane and Pune respectively for disbursement subject to the following conditions.
(a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the Executive Engineer concerned shall, before the actual disbursement of funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable.
b) The concerned sub-divisional office should verify that the above work of the survey is not being distributed twice.
(c) If the funds are not required for any reason, the amount disbursed should not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances.
(d) No amount shall be paid in excess of the approved price.
