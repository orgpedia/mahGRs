Introduction: The Kolhapur Dam. The proposal for obtaining first revised administrative approval for the Akole, G. Ahmednagar scheme has been submitted to the Government in the above context.
The District Council Ahmednagar, Water Management and Sanitation Committee Meeting Resolution No. 369 dated 20/9/2011 amounting to Rs. 42,90,930/- has been accorded administrative approval based on the Tariff Schedule 2009-10.
The revised administrative approval budget is increasing as compared to the original administrative approval price, mainly due to changes in tariff, increase in land acquisition cost, acceptance of higher rate of tender, increase in yield etc.
Under this scheme, an area of 39 hectares will be irrigated by a dam with a storage capacity of 141 cubic feet.
The matter of granting revised administrative approval to the scheme was under consideration to ensure that the beneficiaries of the scheme get the benefit of irrigation.
