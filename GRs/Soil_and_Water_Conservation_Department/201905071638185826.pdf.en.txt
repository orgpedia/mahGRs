The Proposal:
In order to complete the soil and water conservation treatment works within the stipulated time, it was under the consideration of the government to extend the short tender period as before.
Government's decision:
The Government have decided to extend the period of tender (Short Term Tendering) for the works of soil and water conservation treatment under Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan and other places other than Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan, which was reduced under the Government decision dated 19th September, 2017, till 30th June, 2019.
2.Take this order to the notice of all the concerned officers under your command and ensure that these works are completed immediately.
