The Proposal:
The programme of construction of cement dams in the taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan. In the year 2021-22, Cement Dam Programme Account head 4402 2799 has been allocated Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) was budgeted.
Out of these, the proposal for seeking funds from the existing funds has been decided as follows.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby grants approval for release of funds to the tune of Rs. 188.42 lakh (Rupees One Crore Eighty Eight Lakh Forty Two Thousand only) for payment of dues towards completion of cement embankments in Latur District sanctioned during 2017-18 under River Rejuvenation Programme.
