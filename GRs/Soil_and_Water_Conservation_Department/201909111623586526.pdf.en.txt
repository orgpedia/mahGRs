Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 8.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-3, head 2702 9201 for the year 2019-20. Out of this, Rs. 4.80 crore has been made available for disbursement through circulars subject to a ceiling of 60%. Out of which, it has been decided to fund the survey work in the contextual proposals submitted by the Regional Mechanism for the fund for Vidarbha Division as follows.
Government's decision:
Under the survey scheme of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectare. Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse an amount of Rs. 1.28 crore (Rupees One Crore Twenty Eight Lakh only) for the works in the districts of Gadchiroli, Chandrapur and Wardha as demanded under letter No. 1, subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the Executive Engineer concerned shall, before the actual disbursement of funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable.
b) The concerned sub-divisional office should verify that the above work of the survey is not being distributed twice.
(c) If the funds are not required for any reason, the amount disbursed should not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances.
