Preamble: A sum of Rs. 17.00 crore has been budgeted in 2017-18 for the scheme of conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares, out of which Rs. 13.60 crore is available for disbursement as per the relevant circulars in the 80% limit. The government was considering to distribute the funds in the form of statutory development board. In this regard, the government has decided to disburse the funds for the repair work recommended under the reference letter as follows.
Decision: P.T. T. Suktale, Ta. The Government hereby grant approval for providing funds to the tune of Rs. 24.84 lakh (Rs. Twenty Four Lakh Eighty Four Thousand only) for restoration of 125 hectares of irrigation potential of the project by repairing Surgana, G. Nashik projects subject to the following conditions and conditions.
A) Funds should be disbursed on the basis of the payment of the work done after completing the repair work, b) The amount should not be paid more than the approved tender price, c) If the fund remains unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for the project for any other work, d) The guidelines of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds should be strictly followed.
The Superintending Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Board, Nashik shall draw the above funds and disburse the same to the concerned authorities expeditiously.
The above expenditure shall be covered under the following heads and shall be met out of the approved provision for the financial year 2017-18.
