A sum of Rs. 100.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A055. Now, as per the revised estimates, 100% limit of funding is permissible under the scheme. Out of the funds provided, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal dated 25/03/2022.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 15.32 lakhs (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs Thirty Two Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of the project at Kop Bandhara Belwade in Kadegaon Taluka of Sangli district.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions.
a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring completion of the repair work, b) The amount should not be paid more than the sanctioned cost, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) The works have been completed 100% and complaints have not been received, and the quality of the work should be ensured by making payment and submitting a compliance report to the government.
