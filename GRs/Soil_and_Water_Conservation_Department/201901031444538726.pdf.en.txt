General Administration Department As per the Government Notification dated 26.09.2017, a new Administrative Division, namely," Department of Soil and Water Conservation" has been created. Further, the Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 31.05.2017, has created a new Department viz. It has become operational from 01.04.2018. As per the requirement of the new administrative division, new offices have been created in Jal-5 (Establishment itself), Jal-6 (Registration Branch), Jal-9 (Valmy), Jal-14 (Cash Branch), Jal-15 and Jal-16 (Vigilance). Under the Office Order dated 10.09.2018, the structure and designation of the offices of the newly created Soil and Water Conservation Department have been done. While the Department of Soil and Water Conservation was a subdivision of the Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation, the subject allocation list was made as per the 2006 list of offices in the Water Conservation subdivision.
Now, with the formation of a new administrative division, the Soil and Water Conservation Department, the office-wise agenda of the department is being prescribed as follows:-
