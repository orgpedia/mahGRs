Preamble: Demand No. 101 for Conservation and Rehabilitation of Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Projects with Irrigation Capacity of 250 Hectares. A sum of Rs. 100.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A055. Now, as per the revised estimates, 100% limit of funding is permissible under the scheme. Out of the funds provided, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal dated 28/03/2022.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 70.43 lakhs (Rs. Seventy Lakhs Forty Three Thousand only) for the maintenance and repair of 06 minor irrigation schemes and for the payment of the works done for the installation and removal of 03 K.P. embankments in the referential proposals ranging from 101 to 250 hectares in Kolhapur district.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions: a) The funds are to be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring completion of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) In case the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without disbursement for any other purpose, d) Guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) The works have been completed 100% and complaints have not been received, and the quality of the work should be ensured by making payment and submitting a compliance report to the government.
