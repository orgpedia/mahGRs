GOVERNMENT Circular: The Department of Soil and Water Conservation has included the officers/ employees working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as per the Government Decision dated 07.06.2018 and 22.06.2018.
The Statement-A accompanying the said decision of the Government includes the officers who have been given the option to be permanently attached to the Soil and Water Conservation Department and their services have been permanently transferred to the establishment of this department. The said Government Decision provides that the service excellence lists of Group' A' and Group' B' cadres on the establishment of this Department shall be published separately from the Government level after examining the service excellence lists of all the officers/ employees mentioned in the said Return' A'. Further, the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, vide Government Order dated 22.06.2018, has provided the option of Soil and Water Conservation Department to the officers/ employees working in the technical cadre of Group-A and Group-B working under the Water Resources Department. The services of the officers and employees mentioned in the eligible returns A have been transferred to the Soil and Water Conservation Department permanently and they have been declared as officers/ employees of the Soil and Water Conservation Department.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department at Reference No. 1 and the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, the provisional interim seniority of the cadre of Regional Water Conservation Officer (erstwhile designation of Superintending Engineer in the Water Resources Department) in the Soil and Water Conservation Department as on 01.01.2018 is being published in the" Form-A" attached to this circular.
The basis for preparing this seniority list has been taken from the seniority list published under the aforesaid Reference No. 2 of the Water Resources Department.

The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad shall bring the said circular to the immediate notice of the Regional Water Conservation Officer in Form A under him and shall preserve a signed copy of the communication in his own records and make a copy of the same available to the Government.
If any error/ error is found in the details of Regional Water Conservation Authorities included in the said seniority list, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., they should bring their representations/ objections/ suggestions to the Government through registered post/ speed post/ hand delivery with necessary proper proof in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. In case the errors/ omissions are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the stipulated time, the temporary seniority of the Regional Water Conservation Officer working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as on 01.01.2018 will be finalized.
