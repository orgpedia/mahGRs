Memorandum: In the financial year 2022-23, Demand No. Out of the funds budgeted in the year 2022-23 under ZH-5, Reconstruction and Strengthening of Old Malguzari Lakes (Leishanker 4702 A073), a sum of Rs. 3 from the funds made available under the scheme as per the circular at Reference No. 2 The funds amounting to Rs. 42.00 lakhs (Rupees Forty Two Lakh only) towards payment of dues for the repair works of the erstwhile Malguzari Lake in the proposals herein are being handed over to the Nagpur Regional Office for further disbursement.
The following conditions should be fulfilled for the proper utilization of the above funds.
a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring completion of the repair work, b) The amount should not be paid more than the sanctioned cost, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for other works, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that no funds are distributed twice for any work.
The amount so disbursed should be collected by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nagpur and distributed to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers immediately.
