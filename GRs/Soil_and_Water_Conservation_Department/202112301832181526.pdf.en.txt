Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 150.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9201. It has been decided to fund the survey works in the referential proposals from the funds made available to them as follows.
The Decision:
The Government hereby grants approval for disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 156.26 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Fifty Six Lakhs Twenty Six Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues of eligible and completed survey works in the reference proposal of Nanded district.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions: a) Approval of survey works, commissioning orders, survey reports, maps, field reports, etc. It should be ensured that all documents are available and all works are done as per rules.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad and shall be promptly disbursed to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them.
