Preamble: A sum of Rs. 80.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2018-19 under Demand No.ZH-5, Account Head 47029028 for minor irrigation schemes with an irrigation potential of 101-250 hectares. Out of which, funds to the extent of 70% amounting to Rs. 56.00 crore have been made available for disbursement as per the relevant circulars. The government was considering to distribute the funds in the form of statutory development board. Accordingly, the Government has taken the following decisions on the proposal recommended under the Reference Letter.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government hereby approve the disbursement of the following amount out of the sanctioned amount for the financial year 2018-19, namely Rs. 287.00 lakh (Rupees Two Crores, Eighty Eight Lakh only) for the acquisition of land and Rs. 57.00 lakh for the construction of 4 minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of 101 to 250 hectares in non-tribal areas of Nanded district.

The funds being disbursed are subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) If funds are available from the Water Conservation Corporation or other sources for the above project works, the amount being disbursed through this scheme should not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) The amount should not be spent more than the cost of administrative sanction and revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) If funds are not required for the above project works, the amount should not be disbursed for other projects under any circumstances; (d) If funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government; (e) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the concerned Regional Water Conservation Officer and disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under him.
The expenditure on this should be covered under the head of accounts below and should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2018-19.
