A sum of Rs. 450.00 crore was budgeted for 2022-23 under ZH-5, head 4702A046. Now, as per the revised estimates, the proposal for seeking funds from the funds made available under the scheme as on 29/03/2023 has been decided as under.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 259.16 lakh (Rs. Two Crore Sixty-nine Lakhs Sixteen Thousand only) for payment of maintenance and repair works of total 15 Pazar Lakes namely 06 Pazar Lake of Reference Proposals and 09 Pazar Lake of Reference Proposals of Kej Taluka of Beed District.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) In case the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without being disbursed for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
