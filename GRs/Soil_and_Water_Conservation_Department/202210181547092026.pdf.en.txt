The Proposal:
For survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares, vide Demand No. A sum of Rs. 36.24 crore has been budgeted for the year 2022-23 under ZH-3, Account Head 27029219. The Government have decided to fund the works proposed in the proposal dated 20/09/2022 as follows:
The Decision:
The Government hereby grant approval to disburse a total amount of Rs. 18.79 lakh (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand only) for the purpose of waiting for the concept of laying of pipeline from Chanakapur Extended Right Bank Canal to Pimpalgaon (Va) and Meshi village in Nashik district subject to the following terms and conditions.
The above funds are being distributed subject to the following conditions and conditions: (a) The District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall re-verify before the actual disbursement of funds that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable, (b) The sub-divisional office concerned shall verify that the funds are not being disbursed twice for the work above the concept, (c) If there is no need for funds for any reason, the amount disbursed shall not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances, (d) Any amount in excess of the sanctioned cost shall not be disbursed, (e) If for any reason the funds remain unutilized, the unutilized amount shall be immediately returned to the Government.
