It is proposed to be constructed on a flowing local drain, 1.5 km from Sangli. The actual Pazar Lake is 170 m long and 9.00 m high. This Pazar lake has been constructed by ROHO in 1972. The proposed storage capacity of the reservoir is 374 cubic feet and the irrigated area is 82 hectares. Accordingly, a proposal for granting administrative status to the converted storage tank was submitted to the government through a letter. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for conversion of storage tank at Mauje Umbargaon in Atpadi Taluka of Sangli district at a total cost of Rs. 4,97,49, 608/- (Rupees Four Crores, Ninety Seven Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand Six Hundred Eight Only) subject to the following conditions.
