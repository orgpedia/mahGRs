Preamble: It provides for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with an irrigation potential of 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 150.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9201. Now, as per the revised estimates, 75% of the funds have been made available under the scheme. Out of which, it has been decided to fund the survey works in the proposal dated 11/03/2022 as follows:
The Decision:
Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 67.28 lakhs (Rupees Sixty Seven Lakhs Twenty Eight Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues of eligible and completed survey works in the referential proposals in Beed district.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions: a) Approval of survey works, commissioning orders, survey reports, maps, field reports, etc.
It should be ensured that the documents are available and all the work is done as per the rules, b) It should also be ensured that the necessary aspects of the investigation (Trial Pit/ Core Logging Report) are fulfilled, c) The records of the measurement book should be checked to ensure that the amount demanded is reasonable and the funds are not distributed twice for the related works, d) The amount beyond the sanctioned price should not be distributed, e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the unutilized amount should be immediately returned to the government.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them.
