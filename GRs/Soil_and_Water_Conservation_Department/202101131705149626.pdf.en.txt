Preamble: The Comptroller and Auditor General of India in its Report No. 3 of 2020 on General and Social Sector and Public Enterprises for the year ended 31st March, 2019 has referred to the issues raised by the Accountant General regarding the works done under Jalyukt Shivar Engineering. The cabinet has decided to conduct an open inquiry into the works done under Jalyukt Shivar. Just to find out which works need to be selected for open inquiry as the number of works is more, the Govt. As per the Government Decision dated 1st December, 2020, a Committee under the Chairmanship of Shri Vijay Kumar, Retired Additional Chief Secretary, has been constituted and the mandate of the Committee has been fixed. Accordingly, the matter of laying down the terms, conditions and other matters with regard to the appointment of Mr. Vijay Kumar retired Additional Chief Secretary was under the consideration of the Government.
The terms and conditions and other matters relating to the appointment of the Chairman, Government Resolution Mr. Vijay Kumar (Retd.
Salary and Allowances The amount received by Mr. Vijayakumar after deducting his pension (including Dearness Allowance and Dearness Allowance) from the total salary (including Basic Pay and Dearness Allowance) received before his retirement is being determined as monthly remuneration.
Other allowances and facilities admissible to the Additional Chief Secretary shall be admissible to Mr. Vijayakumar.
2.Vehicle arrangement and telephone facility will be provided to Mr. Vijay Kumar as per the rules of the government, as per the vehicles and drivers permissible to the Additional Chief Secretary. A telephone is also available for use in the office.
3.Pravas Allowance shall be entitled to travel allowance, daily allowance and other incidental expenses at the rate applicable to similar category of officers in the Indian Administrative Service.
4. Hallway and manpower for office work- Mr. Vijay Kumar will be provided with separate hallway and required manpower for this purpose.
The expenditure on the salary, allowances and other expenses of Mr. Vijay Kumar, Committee Chairman will be incurred from the fund earmarked for contingency expenses out of the provision made under heading 44022681 of the accounts relating to Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
