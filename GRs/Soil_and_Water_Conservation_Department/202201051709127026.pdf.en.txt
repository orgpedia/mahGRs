Raigad work has been completed on the local drain of Sadar village in the year 1979 through the Rural Water Supply Department Alibag, District Panchayat Raigad. This scheme is in priority 1 in the repair program plan under the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme. The total water storage capacity of the project is 882.98 cusecs and the total irrigation potential of the project is 37 cusecs. 37 after the change. Irrigation capacity will be restored. There is a large amount of seepage in the main reservoir and the bottom of the dam. Due to heavy rains in the catchment areas, there is a possibility of flood in the low-lying areas. Therefore, the matter of administrative approval of the repair works was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: L.P. Scheme Solanpada-Jamrung, Ta.
