The order:
Vinod Narayan Nirwal, Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Sub-Division, Partur has been given additional charge of newly created Sub-Divisional Office of Soil and Water Conservation Sub-Division Selu and Pathri G. Parbhani. As per the order dated 28/6/2022, the additional charge of the newly created posts of Soil and Water Conservation Sub-Division, Selu and Pathri, assigned to Mr. P. B. Kachchhwa, Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Sub-Division, Parbhani, is being terminated under this order.
2. Mr. Nirwal should immediately start the newly created Sub-Divisional Office of Selu and Pathri, which has been given additional charge, and take action to connect the office to the service system by writing to the Accountant General's Office and the Treasury Directorate in order to make this office operational.
3. Mr. Nirwal will look after the work of the post of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Sub-Division, Selu and Pathri with additional charge.
(4) The order shall come into force immediately.
