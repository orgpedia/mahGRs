Government's decision:
Ancharwadi No. 1, Kolar No. 2, Shelgaon Jehangir No. 1 Shelgaon Atoll No. 1, No. 1 th. A total of Rs.
Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the project at a total cost of Rs. 436.34/-lakh (Rupees Four Crores Thirty Six Lakhs Thirty Four Thousand only) amounting to Rs. 397.95/-lakh (Rupees Three Crores Ninety Seven Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand only) and other incidental expenses amounting to Rs. 38.39/-lakh (Rupees Thirty Eight Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand only) subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The report on the pier details should be authenticated by the geologist by taking the pier details on the alignment of the gated cement embankment. Also, the geologist should certify that the gated cement dam site is suitable for storing water.
2) The Superintending Engineer/ Regional Water Conservation Officer for the project is required to visit the work site and identify and certify the site and alignment.
3) Viewer map should be certified by the Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune.
4) Before starting the tender process, the resolution of the ZP Sanitation and Water Management Committee should be passed and the Naharakat certificate should be obtained accordingly.
