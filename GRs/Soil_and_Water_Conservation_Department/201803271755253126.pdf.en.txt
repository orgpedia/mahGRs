The Proposal:
A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been budgeted for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of 0 to 100 hectare irrigation potential for the financial year 2017-18 under Demand No.L-3, Account Head 2702 4745, out of which Rs. 8.00 crore within 80% limit has been made available for disbursement as per the circular. It is a matter of disbursing the funds to eligible works in the fund demand proposals submitted by the field machinery within the limits of the statutory development board-wise criteria.
It was under the consideration of the government. In this regard, the government has decided to provide funds for the works recommended under the relevant Acts No. 2, 3 and 4 as follows:
Government's decision:
6 Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) in Sindhudurg and Ratnagiri districts in Form-A with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares for the financial year 2017-18

e) If there is no need for funds for any reason, the amount disbursed should not be spent for any other purpose under any circumstances,
(a) If for any reason the fund remains unutilized, the unutilized amount shall be immediately returned to the Government.
