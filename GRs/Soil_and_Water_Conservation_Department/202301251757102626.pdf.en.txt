The Proposal:
The Union Ministry of Land Resources, Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India has issued the Common Guidelines 2021 for the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Watershed Development Component 2.0. Accordingly, Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Watershed Development Component 2.0 will be implemented in the state. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 27.04.2022. Under this scheme, the state has prepared 144 preliminary project reports covering an area of 5.65 lakh hectares. The total cost of the project is Rs. 1335.56 crore. The Annual Action Plan of Rs. 807.14 crore for the year 2022-23 has been submitted to the Government of India for approval. The Centre and the state share is 60:40. The Central Government has received Rs. 50.08 crore for the scheme, out of which Rs. 83.55 crore has been disbursed as State share amounting to Rs. 33.39 crore.
There is a large extent of barren land in the state. The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Watershed Development Component 2.0 is being implemented by the Department of Soil and Water Conservation with an objective to enhance the economic status of rural people by utilising this fallow land and to increase their productivity by undertaking appropriate soil and conservation works. The Central Government has set a time limit for the completion of work in various departments in this regard. A review of the plan. The meeting was held on 12.01.2023 under the Chairmanship of Union Secretary, Department of Land Resources, Government of India. In the meeting, it was pointed out that the second installment will not be received unless the amount of the first installment received from the central government is spent. Therefore, considering the short period of balance in the current financial year, the demand for further funds can be made to the Central Government only after spending the available funds.
