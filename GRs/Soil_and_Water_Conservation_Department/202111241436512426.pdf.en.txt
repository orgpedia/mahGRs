Government circular:
Reference No. 2 General Administration Department, Government Notification dated 21.06.2021 In accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the temporary seniority of the cadre of Additional Commissioner Water Conservation/ Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer/ Chief Engineer and Designated Joint Secretary (Architecture) in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as on 01.01.2021 is being published as" Form-A" attached to this circular.
2. The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list in Form-A annexed to this circular shall be the date of seniority mentioned in the above Reference No. The Government Notification No. 281/12, General Administration Department No. SRV-2016/ PR No. As per the provisions of 21.06.2021, the seniority in the cadre of Additional Commissioner Water Conservation/ Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer/ Chief Engineer and Designated Joint Secretary (Architecture) will be considered for determining the seniority.
3. The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad shall bring the said circular in Form A under his charge to the immediate notice of the Additional Commissioner, Water Conservation/ Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer/ Chief Engineer and the Designated Joint Secretary (Architecture) and shall preserve a copy of the same in his own records and make a copy of the same available to the Government.

If any errors/ errors are found in the details of the Additional Commissioner Water Conservation and Chief Engineer Officers included in the seniority list, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., they should bring their representations/ objections/ suggestions to the Government through registered post/ speed post/ hand delivery along with necessary appropriate proof in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. If the mistakes/ errors are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the stipulated time, the seniority of the cadre of Additional Commissioner Water Conservation/ Chief Vigilance and Quality Control Officer/ Chief Engineer and Designated Joint Secretary (Architecture) working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation will be finalized on 01.01.2021.
