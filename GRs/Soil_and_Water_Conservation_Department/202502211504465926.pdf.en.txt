With a view to expedite the completion of works in incomplete catchments, the Government took the decision in this regard. As on 30th November, 2007, it has been decided to implement Dhadak programme for dynamic completion of 353 watersheds with at least 1 in each of the 353 taluks of the state.
Under the scheme, 12,344 watersheds have been approved by the Steering Committee since the year 2007-08 and 10,695 watersheds have been completed. As requested by the Director, Soil Conservation and Watershed Management, Pune, the government was considering disbursement of funds for the completion of incomplete watershed projects in an accelerated manner under the Integrated Watershed Development Programme for the financial year 2024-25.
