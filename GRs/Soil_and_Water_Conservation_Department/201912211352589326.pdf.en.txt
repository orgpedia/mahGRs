Government Circular: In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department and the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation (Designation of Water Resources Departmental Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture) (Saral Seva)/ Sub-Divisional Engineer (Architecture) (Graduate) (State+ GP)/ Sub-Divisional Officer (Architecture) (Diploma holder) (State+ GP)) has been appointed as the Deputy Director of Water Resources Department. Government Circular No: Seniority-2019/ PR No.317/ JAL-2
Provisional Interim Seniority as on 01.01.2019 in Part-I, Part-II and Part-III, respectively, is being published as" Form-A" Part-I, Part-II and Part-III annexed to this Circular.
The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list in Form-A Part-I, Part-II and Part-III attached to this circular shall be taken into consideration for determining seniority in the cadre of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer only as per the provisions of this Government Notification General Administration Department No. SRV- 1076/12, dated 21.06.1982.
The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad shall bring the said circular to the immediate notice of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer in Form A Part-I to III under him and shall preserve a signed copy of the communication in his own records and make a copy of the same available to the Government.
If any error/ error is found in the details of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officers included in the said seniority list, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., they should bring their representations/ objections/ suggestions to the notice of the Government through registered post/ speed post/ hand delivery along with necessary proper proof in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. If the errors/ errors are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the prescribed time, the temporary seniority of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation will be finalized as on 01.01.2019.
