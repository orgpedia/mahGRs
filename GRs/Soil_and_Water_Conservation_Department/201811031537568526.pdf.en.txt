Introduction Valmi Coordination Committee was constituted on 06.09.2000 to coordinate the training and research programmes of Institute of Water and Land Management (Valmi) Aurangabad, Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute (MERRI) Nashik, Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy (META) Nashik and Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development (IRD) Pune.
Further, the revised" Coordination Committee for Training and Research" was re-established vide Government Resolution dated 25.02.2014. The 14th meeting of the Training and Research Coordination Committee was held on 07.10.2016 at the Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development, Pune. Under Section 5, the committee has been reconstituted and the revised proposal has been submitted to the government for approval. Earlier, the agency was under the Water Resources Department. However, as per the Government decision dated 31.05.2017, the Valmy Institute has been transferred to the Department of Soil and Water Conservation and brought under the administrative control of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation without affecting the autonomy of the Institute.
Accordingly, the Valmiki Institute has decided to reconstitute the Chairman, Co-Chairperson, Members of the Training and Research Coordination Committee to support the training and research program between the Water and Land Management Institute (WALMI), Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute, Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development, Maharashtra Engineering Training Academy, Hydrology Project, Maharashtra Water Resources Development Center and the Department of Soil and Water Conservation. Proposal 4 is under consideration of the Government.
The government decision was taken by the Water and Land Management Institute (WALMI) Aurangabad, Maharashtra Engineering Research Institute (MERI) Nashik, Directorate of Irrigation Research and Development, Maharashtra Engineering Academy (META).
