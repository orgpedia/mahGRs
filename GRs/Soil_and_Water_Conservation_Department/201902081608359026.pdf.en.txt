Preamble: In respect of the projects under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation which have been physically completed but the land acquisition funds are yet to be paid, instructions have been issued vide Circular dated 21/02/2018, after deciding that the compensation ordered by the Court shall be paid subject to obtaining revised administrative approval from the competent authority for this enhanced expenditure and within the limit of funds available in every financial year under the scheme/ head concerned.
The Government was contemplating to implement the decision taken only to comply with the court decisions as above, to provide relief to the project victims and to fulfill the obligation of the Government by paying all kinds of compensation (final settlement, direct purchase by private negotiation, pending land rent, interest under Section 28A, rehabilitation, forest land transfer etc.) of the lands acquired for the welfare of the people. The government has taken the following decisions:
The Decision:
Government approval is hereby accorded for spending of all land acquisition funds only within the limits of the approved budgetary provisions within the limits of the proposed revised administrative approval cost of the ongoing projects under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation and within the scope of the earlier approved administrative approval/ revised administrative approval report.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: BHUSPA-2017/ PR. No. 233 (Bha-2)/ JAL-4. Circular dated 21/02/18 is hereby superseded.
The decision shall be limited to the land acquisition fund in the revised administrative approval proposal submitted to the competent authorities.
The relevant authorities shall ensure compliance of the existing Government decisions/ circulars regarding land acquisition, the terms, conditions and guidelines of the Revenue and Finance Departments as well as the proposals for seeking funds in accordance with the aforesaid S.P.M.A. Strict adherence to the procedures/ guidelines in the circular dated 08/06/18 will be required.
It shall be the responsibility of the competent authority concerned as well as the requisitioning authority to issue orders for revised administrative sanction of the said enhanced expenditure within a period of six months from the date of the order of disbursement of the said funds.
(In case of non-compliance of court orders, the time limit for issuing revised administrative approval shall be 01 month)
