Proposal: - A sum of Rs. 2349.00 crore has been budgeted in this financial year as equity contribution for Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation. Under the Government Decision dated 05.12.2025, a sum of ₹1409.40 crore has been made available for carrying out the functions of the Corporation under Section 6 Section 26 of the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation Act, 2000, subject to a ceiling of 60 per cent of the total annual provision cost. Out of the remaining 40 per cent, 10 per cent of the total annual outlay i.e. Rs. 234.90 crores has been made available by the Finance Department. The Government has decided to disburse the funds so made available to the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation as follows.
