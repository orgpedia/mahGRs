Government order:
Mr. Mangesh Manikrao Khairnar, Secretary, Water Resources Department, Govt. 1 above. As per the order dated 21.06.2018, the post of Executive Engineer was shown as promoted and requested to be posted in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation.
Mangesh Manikrao Khairnar has been posted as District Water Conservation Officer, Zilla Parishad, Minor Irrigation Department, Nashik (East). After considering the objections raised by the lower cadre officers of the department, the government is cancelling the inclusion of Mr. Mangesh Manikrao Khairnar in the department. As per the provision mentioned in Para 8 of the Government Decision dated 31.05.2017, the services of the deputationists will continue to be with the department for a very temporary period and after filling up the posts of District Water Conservation Officer cadre, the services of Mr. Khairnar will be reverted to the Water Resources Department.
