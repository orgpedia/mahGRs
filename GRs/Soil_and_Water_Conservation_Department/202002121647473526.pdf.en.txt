As per the directions of the Governor, a proposal has been received by the Government to grant administrative approval to the works of 10 gates cement storage embankments in Aundha taluka under the Hingoli district irrigation backlog. The water storage capacity of the proposed cement nala embankment is 328.48 cusecs and the total cost of these works is Rs. 4.78 crore. The irrigation potential of these works is 82 hectares and the matter of administrative approval for these works was under consideration of the government. Based on this, the following decision was taken.
Government Decision: Based on the 2017-18 schedule of the Soil and Water Conservation Department, the budget for the 10 gate cement storage embankment works in Aundha Taluka of Hingoli district has been increased to a total of Rs. 4,78,03, 151/- (Rupees Four Crores Eighty Eight Lakhs Three Thousand One Hundred and Fifty One only). 4,40,18, 192/- (Rupees Four Crores Forty Lakhs Eighteen Thousand One Hundred and Ninety Two only) plus Goods and Services Tax and Insurance expenses amounting to Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 37,84,959/- (Rupees Thirty Seven Lakh Eighty Four Thousand Nine Hundred and Nineteen Sixty only). The details of the 10 schemes are as follows: The existing limit for these schemes is Rs. 1, 46, 114/- per unit.
The administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
1.1 Technical approval of the budget should be provided by examining at the competent level the plans/ concepts, externalities and transmission gaps envisaged in the budget as per the requirement.
