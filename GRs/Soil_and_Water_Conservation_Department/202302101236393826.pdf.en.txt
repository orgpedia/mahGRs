Preamble: Demand No. 1 for conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 450.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2022-23 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046. Out of which, the following decisions have been taken on the fund demand proposals for the reference date 02/11/2022 from the funds currently made available as per the reference circulars of the Department of Finance.
Decision: The decision was taken at the meeting of the Jalna district administration in Ambad taluka. 1 and Pazhar Lake No. 1 Approval of the Government is hereby accorded for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 45.73 lakhs (Rs. Forty Five Lakhs Seventy Three Thousand only) amounting to Rs. 13.34 lakhs, Rs. 12.32 lakhs and Rs. 20.07 lakhs respectively towards payment of maintenance and repair works of these projects.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) In case the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without being disbursed for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
