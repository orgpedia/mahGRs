Government's decision:
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the determination of posts in Government Services for Persons with Disabilities as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. The list of posts reserved for PwDs as per the notification dated 04.01.2021 has been published. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions to ensure that the posts in Group-A to Group-D posts, which are in the state government establishments, are in the list published by the Central Government, the duties and responsibilities of the posts, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the state government service, even if the designations of such posts are different.
