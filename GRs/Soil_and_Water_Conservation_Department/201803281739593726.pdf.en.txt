The Proposal:
The Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan is being implemented in the State as per the Government decision dated 5th December, 2014. Under this Mission, the Government has issued a decision dated 8th December, 2015 to promote the River/ Stream/ Drain Rejuvenation Programme taken in the villages where the residents have taken the initiative to revive the river/ stream/ nullah by doing the work of desilting, simplification and deepening of the river/ stream/ nullah through public participation.
