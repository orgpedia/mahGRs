GOVERNMENT DECISION Water Conservation Department: JALSAM-2011/ PR No. 127/ JAL-7 dated 3rd July, 2013.
The Proposal:
Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan is a major ambitious programme being implemented in the state. So far, more than 5 lakh works have been completed under the mission. Determining the life span of the works will help in effective implementation of the program.
Accordingly, as per the Government Decision dated 3rd July 2013, it was under consideration of the Government to appoint an expert committee to review the life expectancy of soil and water conservation treatment and to determine the life expectancy of new soil and water conservation treatment.
Government's decision
In order to ensure effective implementation of soil and water conservation programme, the Government have decided to appoint an Expert Committee as per the Government Decision dated 3rd July, 2013 to review the prescribed life span of soil and water conservation treatment and to determine the new life span of soil and water conservation treatment.
