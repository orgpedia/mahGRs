Appointment of an expert committee to find a method for determining the number of farmlands in the watershed.

Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg
The Ministry, Mumbai 400 032
Date: 12th of June 2019
The Proposal:
In pursuance of Public Interest Litigation No. 154/2015 filed in the Hon'ble High Court of Bombay. As per the Government decision dated 31st May, 2017, Shri.
The government has decided to implement the recommendations made by the expert committee headed by former Chief Secretary Johny Joseph. The High Court issued the order on February 18, 2019. As per the recommendations of the committee, the government was considering the appointment of an expert committee to ensure the procedures for determining the number of farmlands in the watershed.
Government's decision
An expert committee is being appointed to ensure the methodology for determining the number of farm ponds in a particular watershed based on scientific factors like rainfall, runoff, cropping pattern etc.
