In the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan Account Head 4402 2681, the matter of disbursement of funds as per the demand of the Collector, Buldana was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As the Finance Department has made 100% funds available for distribution in the head of accounts 44022681, Rs. The Government is approving the disbursement of the provision of Rs. 4.00 crore (Rupees Four Crores only).
The fund is being distributed to the District Collector, Buldana and the Divisional Commissioner is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Collector as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Care should be taken to observe the following terms and conditions while spending the provisions being distributed.
2.1 Funds released during the financial year 2017-18, which are lying unutilized as on 31st March, 2018, cannot be withdrawn from the BEAMS system.
2.2 The funds being disbursed through this Government Decision should be made available for the works completed in the selected villages in 2016-17 and 2017-18 respectively. Village-wise and year-wise statements of expenditure from the funds made available should be submitted to the government.
3. The fund should be spent under the head Demand No. L7,4402 Capital expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation, 00 102 Soil Conservation, 03 Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan, (03) (01) Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan (44022681), 3 Major Constructions.
