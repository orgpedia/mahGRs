Preamble: Out of the sanctioned cost of Rs. 55.13 crore for the Small Scale Infrastructure Scheme Kamare, Taa and G. Palghar, Rs. 47.10 crore has been disbursed so far. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 75.00 lakhs has been sought under the Reference Proposal for payment of the work done on this project which is in progress. Based on the available funds, the following decisions have been taken:
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 26.00 lakh (Rupees Twenty Six Lakh only) for the Kamare Small Irrigation Project in Palghar District with an irrigation potential of 247 hectares subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) If funds are available from the Water Conservation Corporation or other sources for the above project works, the amount being disbursed through this scheme should not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) The amount should not be spent more than the cost of administrative sanction and revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) If funds are not required for the above project works, the amount should not be disbursed for other projects under any circumstances; (d) If funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government; (e) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Thane and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under him.
