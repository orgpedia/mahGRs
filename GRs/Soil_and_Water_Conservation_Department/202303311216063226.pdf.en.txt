Preamble: Demand No. 1 for conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 450.00 crore was budgeted for 2022-23 under ZH-5, head 4702A046. Now, as per the revised estimates, the fund demand proposals as on 24/02/2023 from the funds made available under the scheme have been decided as under.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 7.51 lakh (Rs.7 lakh fifty one thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of two projects namely, Satawan Bandhara Matkuth-4 and Satawan Bandhara Matkuth-3 in Shahada Taluka of Nandurbar district for an amount of Rs. 3.80 lakh and Rs. 3.71 lakh respectively.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) No amount in excess of the sanctioned cost should be paid, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for any other purpose, d) Guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
