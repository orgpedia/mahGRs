Preamble: The programme for construction of cement dams in taluks in the State facing scarcity has been going on since 2012-13. Under these programmes, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in river rejuvenation programmes and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan. The matter of disbursement of funds to the cement embankment completed under river rejuvenation in Nashik district was under consideration.
Government Decision: In the year 2018-19, the Cement Dam Programme Account head was 4402 2799 with an outlay of Rs. A sum of Rs. 80.00 crore (Rupees Eighty Crore) has been budgeted. Under the River Rejuvenation Programme, a total amount of Rs. 620.66 crore (Rupees Six Crores Twenty Lakhs Sixty Thousand) is being sanctioned for the completion of cement embankments in Nashik District as per the demand.

The Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune is declared as the Controlling Officer and the District Water Conservation Officer (Executive Engineer) of the concerned district is declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending the funds being made available for the construction of the cement dam. The Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune shall take care of the expenditure on the targets allocated and the Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune is being authorized to disburse the funds to the concerned.
The funds being disbursed are subject to the following conditions:
3.1 If the above Cement Bond is funded by another instrument, the funds being distributed by the said Government Decision shall not be withdrawn or distributed.
3.2 No amount shall be spent in excess of the administrative sanction or the price agreed to by the Government.
3.3 If the funds provided remain unutilized for any reason, the spent funds should not be used for any other purpose.
3.4 Cement Bonds- If the funds being made available to it remain unutilized, it should be returned to the government immediately.
3.5 The full utilization certificate and the certificate of completion of the work given so far should be submitted to the government immediately.
The approved funds should be spent under the following head of accounts.
" Demand No. ZH-5, 4402- Capital Expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation, 102 Soil Conservation, (02) Cement Dam (Check Dam) Programme, (4402 2799), 53 Major Constructions.
