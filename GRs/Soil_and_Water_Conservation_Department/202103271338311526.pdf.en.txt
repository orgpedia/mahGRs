Memorandum: In the thematic case, a sum of Rs. 30.00 crore has been earmarked for the financial year 2020-21 for the removal of irrigation backlog in Hingoli district.
As per the revised estimates for the current financial year, out of Rs. 225.00 crore under the scheme" Small Irrigation Schemes (0 to 100 Hs)," Rs. 30.00 crore (Rs.30 crore only) is being handed over to District Water Conservation Officer (Soil& Water Conservation) Nanded (Hingoli) through Aurangabad Regional Divisional Office for further disbursement.
The following conditions should be fulfilled for the proper utilization of the above funds: a) The funds should not be spent more than the administrative/ revised administrative approval cost agreed by the government, b) If there is no need for funds for the above projects, the funds should not be distributed to other projects under any circumstances, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the funds should be immediately returned to the government, d) The works are 100% completed and no complaints are received, and the payment should be made after ensuring the quality of the work and the compliance report should be submitted to the government.
e) Utilization certificates of the above expenses should be submitted to the Accountant General's Office and its counter-signed copies as well as certificates of project completion should be submitted to the Government in the prescribed manner.
