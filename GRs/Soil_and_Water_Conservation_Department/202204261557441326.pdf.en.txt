the prelude
" Jal Shakti Abhiyan- Catch the Rain" is a time bound campaign. The duration of the mission is from 29 March 2022 to 30 September 2022. It is a joint initiative of various Ministries of Government of India and State Governments.
" Jal Shakti Abhiyan" is primarily based on the following five activities:
1) Water conservation and rain water harvesting, 2) Renovation of traditional and other water sources as well as main water bodies, 3) Reuse and recharge of water structures, 4) Development of catchment areas, 5) Intensive afforestation.
Under the Jal Shakti Mission, the main objective is to conserve every drop of water available from the rains in the year 2022 and the main focus of this mission is expected to be on the following.
Water conservation and rainwater harvesting: Construction of rainwater harvesting structures on buildings to store rainwater falling on rooftops, construction of soak pits in the building premises to store rainwater, maintenance and upkeep of existing water conservation structures, construction of new storage dams, ponds, rejuvenation of traditional rainwater harvesting structures, removal of encroachments in water bodies and their catchment areas, desilting of ponds, recharging and reuse of borewells, watershed development, minor rivers, nullahs, streams and streams.
