Preamble: L.P. Scheme Nanivade (Mahajanwadi), Ta. Vaibhavwadi, G. Sindhudurg was completed in the year 2000 on the local drain of Sadar village. This scheme is in priority 2 in the repair program plan under the' Mukhyamantri Jal Sanvardhan Yojana'. The total water storage capacity of the project is 1827 cubic feet and the total irrigation capacity is 140 cubic feet. After 140 years of repair. Irrigation capacity will be restored.
Due to the leakage of water from the well and gate of the remitter of this scheme, water has been formed on the D/ S side and water is also leaking from the canal wall. The side walls and headwall of the conduit on the upstream side of the irrigation feeder are in masonry and the doors of the masonry wall have been filled. Therefore, the matter of administrative approval of the repair works was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: L.P. Scheme Nanivade (Mahajanwadi), Ta. Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 1,16,21, 266.00/- (Rupees One Crore Sixteen Lakhs Twenty One Thousand Two Hundred and Sixty Six only) subject to the following conditions.
