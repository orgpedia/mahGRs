The Controlling Authority concerned shall meet the expenditure for the year 2018-19 only from the provision made under this head and shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the sanctioned grant.
The concerned Controlling Officer shall ensure that the terms and conditions of the prevailing Government Order are fulfilled or how, while granting advance to the concerned Government officer/ employee, and only then grant the advance. It may be noted that the concerned officers will be held responsible in case of wrong advance being disbursed to an employee.
6. While sanctioning advance for repayment of loan taken from a financial institution, the advance should be sanctioned after assessing the present status of the loan as on that date.
7.After providing the advance amount to the concerned, the details (bill number and standard number) should be sent to the government immediately.
8. The Controlling Officer concerned shall take the details of advance disbursement and the records up to full recovery separately, employee wise. After the recovery of the advance is complete, necessary action should be taken at your level as the controlling officer.
The grants sanctioned under this Memorandum are being released subject to the terms and conditions prescribed in the Government Resolution issued by the Finance Department from time to time.
While utilising the said fund, the terms and conditions issued by the Finance Department from time to time as well as the guidelines in the circular dated 02/04/2018 and 25/1/2019 should be strictly complied with.
The said memorandum is being issued as per the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the relevant Government Circular dated 2/4/2018.
