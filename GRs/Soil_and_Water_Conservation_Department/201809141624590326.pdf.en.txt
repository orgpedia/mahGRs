Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works on progress/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 50.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2018-19 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A019. Out of which, funds to the extent of 70% amounting to Rs. 35.00 crore have been made available for disbursement through the relevant circulars. Distribution of funds The legislative development has to be done as per the board-wise formula prescribed by the Governor. Accordingly, the Government has decided to provide funds for the project in the reference proposal recommended by the Nashik Circle Office from the funds allocated for the remaining Maharashtra divisions as follows.
Decision: The Government hereby approve the release of an amount of Rs. 1596.21 lakh (Rupees Fifteen Crores Nine Lakhs Twenty One Thousand only) as per the attached Form-A for the construction of 24 Gated Cement Dams in Dhule District with an irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares from the sanctioned provision for the financial year 2018-19.
2. The Rain Fund is being distributed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) The amount being disbursed by the Water Conservation Board, the District Planning Committee or any other source, if funds are available for the works of the above project, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) No amount shall be spent in excess of the cost of administrative sanction as well as revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) If funds are not required for the works of the above project, the amount shall not be disbursed for any other project under any circumstances.
