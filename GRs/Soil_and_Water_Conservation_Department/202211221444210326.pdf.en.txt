A sum of Rs. 200.00 crore has been budgeted for 2022-23 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A055. Out of which, the proposal for seeking funds from the funds made available as per the relevant circular of the Finance Department, dated 25/08/2022, has been decided as follows.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a sum of Rs. 291.76 lakhs (Rupees Two Crores Ninety One Lakh Seventy Six Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of the project at Kop Bandhara Jambarkheda in Vaijapur Taluka of Aurangabad district as follows.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions: a) The funds are to be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without being disbursed for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them.
The expenditure on this should be covered under the following heads of accounts and should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2022-23.
