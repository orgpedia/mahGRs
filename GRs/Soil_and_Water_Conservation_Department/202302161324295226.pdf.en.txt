Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 1 is being implemented. A total of 17 schemes under the Pune Mandal Office will be repaired and a total amount of Rs. 394.44 crore. The irrigation potential of these works is 283. The total water storage capacity is 1350 cusecs. The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 4 has been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. Considering the above, the proposal was under consideration for administrative approval as the 17 works mentioned in the revised plan of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme.
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Samvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 17 repair schemes in the Pune Regional Water Conservation Department is Rs. 394.44 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
