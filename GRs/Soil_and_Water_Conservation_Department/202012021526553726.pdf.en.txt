Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
During the year 2020-21, Cement Dam Programme Account Head 4402 2799 involving an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.00 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) has been budgeted. Out of which, the following decisions have been taken regarding the release of funds for the cement works completed under the letter dated 29th June, 2020 and 14th September, 2020.
