Preamble: Demand No. 1 for conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 619.41 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046. Out of the funds provided, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal of the reference date 04/03/2021.
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 11.82 lakhs (Rupees Eleven Lakhs Eighty Two Thousand only) for payment of maintenance and repair works of Koppa Bandhara Pendur projects in Malvan Taluka of Sindhudurg district subject to the following terms and conditions.
a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring completion of the repair work, b) The amount should not be paid more than the sanctioned cost, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) The works have been completed 100% and complaints have not been received, and the quality of the work should be ensured by making payment and submitting a compliance report to the government.
The amount so distributed should be collected by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Thane and distributed to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them immediately.
