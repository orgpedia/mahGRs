Preamble: A proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the scheme near the storage pond (Barav), T. Jamkhed, G. Ahmednagar has been received by the Government. Under this scheme, an area of 37 hectares will be irrigated by a dam with a storage capacity of 140 cubic meters. The matter of giving administrative approval to the said scheme was under the consideration of the government so that the beneficiaries of this scheme could get the benefit of irrigation.
The scheme does not fit into the three parameters of profitability ratio, cost per share and rate of return. However, considering the possibility of reducing the permanent water scarcity in the area, this scheme, which does not fit into the financial parameters, has to be given administrative approval as a special case without the approval of the Expenditure and Priorities Committee. The Council of Ministers. The decision was taken in the meeting held on 29.8.2018.
