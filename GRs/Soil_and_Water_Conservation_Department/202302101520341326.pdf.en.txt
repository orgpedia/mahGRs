Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 648.77 lakhs (Rupees Six Crores Forty Eight Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues for construction of 98 Heasinks in Akkalkot Taluka of Solapur district.
The aforesaid funds are being distributed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds being disbursed through the above projects should not be withdrawn or disbursed if funds have been made available from the Water Conservation Corporation, District Planning Committee or other sources for the works of the above projects, b) The funds should not be spent beyond the cost of administrative approval as well as revised administrative approval approved by the Government, c) If there is no need for funds for the above projects, the funds should not be disbursed for other projects under any circumstances.
(f) The works have been completed 100% and no complaints have been received and the quality of the work should be ensured by making payment and submitting a compliance report to the government.
