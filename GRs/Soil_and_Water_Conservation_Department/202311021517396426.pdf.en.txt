The Proposal:
The Department of Soil and Water Conservation in the State undertakes various types of water conservation treatment structures with irrigation potential ranging from 0 to 600 hectares. Out of these, the works of Kolhapur method dams/ vented cement storage dams/ cement storage dams/ cement drain dams with irrigation capacity of 0 to 100 hectares are being taken up by the Government/ Corporation/ Zilla Parishad/ District Annual Plan etc.
The fund is being implemented by the Water Conservation/ Zilla Parishad Departments. Such structures are constructed in river or drain basins where land acquisition is not required, keeping in view the local demand and geographical conditions.
At present, it has been observed that land acquisition is being done without examining the technical aspects while undertaking such projects. During the hearing of the case. As per the order issued by the Upalokayukta, the matter of issuing guidelines in the form of a government circular was under the consideration of the government.
Government Circular: The following guidelines/ instructions should be followed before implementing the Kolhapur system of embankments/ vented cement storage embankments/ cement storage embankments/ cement drain embankments to be constructed on rivers/ drains/ private sector drains.

1. Kolhapur type of embankments/ vented cement storage embankments/ cement storage embankments/ cement drain embankments should be taken up at the project site where land acquisition is not required.
Since the Second Order and Third Order drains are mostly privately owned, prior approval/ notarization of such drains on stamp paper from the interested landowners of the area affected by the project should be done by taking action as per Act No. 3 before proposing the Kolhapur method of embankments/ gate cement storage embankments/ cement drainage embankments on such drains.
3. While preparing the proposal for the said works, the resolution of the concerned Gram Panchayat/ Gram Sabha should be taken and attached with the proposal.
4. While determining the location of cement concrete drain embankment i. For the accuracy of the location of the dam, maps of 1:25000 (Topo Sheet) should be used.
The drain slope should be less than 2%.
