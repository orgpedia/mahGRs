Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 4 is being implemented. A total of 46 schemes will be repaired under the Aurangabad Circle Office.
584.32 lakh. After the restoration of these projects, 923 hectares of irrigated area and 4767.12 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. A proposal to get administrative approval for these works has been submitted to the government under the above reference letter. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad scrutinized/ scrutinized all the budgets at his level
Considering the above, the proposal was under consideration for administrative approval as the 46 works mentioned in the revised plan of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
