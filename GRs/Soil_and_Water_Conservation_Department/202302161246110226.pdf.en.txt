Preamble: Demand No. 1 for conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 450.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2022-23 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046. Out of which, the following decisions have been taken on the fund demand proposals at Reference No. 02 from the funds currently made available as per the Reference Circular of the Department of Finance.
Decision: The Government hereby approves disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 46.77 lakh, Rs. 809.99 lakh and Rs. 29.89 lakh for payment of maintenance and repair works of 34 projects viz., K.P.Bandhara Shendi in Ahmednagar Taluk of Ahmednagar District, 31 Pazar Talao in Parner Taluk and 02 Pazar Talao in Jamkhed Taluk of Ahmednagar District.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) In case the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without being disbursed for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
