Preamble: In order to motivate the officers and staff to perform their duties efficiently and effectively and to boost their morale, an innovative scheme" Mukhyamantri Sukarmi Puraskar Yojana" has been launched from the financial year 2018-19 under the decision of the Government in the context. The scheme is being implemented at the Ministry level initially for the financial year 2018-19. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the relevant Government Decision, the Government was contemplating to constitute a Selection Committee at the level of the Ministries/ Departments to select the awardees as Vibhag Ratna from amongst the eligible officers/ staff as per the score in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation for the" Vibhag Ratna Award" to be given at the Administrative Department level.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A Selection Committee is being constituted at the level of Ministries/ Departments to select the awardees as Vibhag Ratnas from amongst the eligible officers/ staff of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation for the" Vibhag Ratna Puraskar" to be conferred at the level of Ministries/ Departments.
