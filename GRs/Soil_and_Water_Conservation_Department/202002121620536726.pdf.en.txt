As on 09/09/2019 As per the directions of the Governor, the proposal for granting administrative approval to 15 gates of cement storage embankment works in Sengaon and Hingoli taluks under Hingoli district irrigation backlog has been received by the Government. The Cement Storage Barrage works in the proposal have a combined water storage capacity of 339.71 cusecs with a total budget of Rs. 4.93 crore. The irrigation potential of these works is 110 hectares and the matter of administrative approval for these works was under consideration of the government. Based on this, the following decision was taken.
Government Decision: Based on the list of the year 2017-18 of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, the budget of the 15 gate cement storage embankment works in Sengaon and Hingoli taluks of Hingoli district has been increased to a total of Rs. 4,93,43, 944/- (Rupees Four Crores Ninety Three Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty Four only) and Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 39,15,460/- (Rupees Forty-nine Lakhs Fifteen Thousand Four Hundred Sixty only).
The details of the 15 schemes are as follows: The existing limit for these schemes is Rs. 1, 46, 114/- per unit.
The administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
