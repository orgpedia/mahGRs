Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 30.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2020-21 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9201. Of this, 10% of the funds have been made available for distribution. Out of which, the Government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal from the fund for Vidarbha Division as follows.
The Decision:
Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 69.09 lakhs (Rupees Seventy Seven Lakhs Nine Thousand only), which is presently available for payment of eligible and completed survey works in the reference proposals.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) In order to ensure that no more than the reasonable amount for the survey is disbursed, the Executive Engineer concerned shall again verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable before the actual disbursement of funds; (b) The concerned Sub-Divisional Office shall verify that the funds are not disbursed twice for the above survey work.
(c) If the funds are not required for any reason, the amount disbursed should not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances.
(d) No amount shall be paid in excess of the approved price.
(e) if for any reason the fund remains unutilized, the unutilized amount shall be returned to the Government forthwith.
