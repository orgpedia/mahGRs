Introduction: Under the National Agriculture Development Programme, the number of H.C.E.s. from 0 to 100 has been increased from 0 to 100.
The Government has taken over the liability of 204 villages/ Pazhar ponds in respect of which no land acquisition has been proposed for the year 2017-18, to the extent of Rs. 30.27 crore. For this, the funds available during 2017-18 have been made available to the Regional Mechanism as per the order at Reference 2. Accordingly, the funds have been demanded as per letter at reference 4 to pay the remaining liability by taking necessary action. The government has decided to provide funds for this.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: To clear the pending dues of villages/ Pazar ponds which are not liable for acquisition of land having irrigation potential of 100 hectares, a total amount of Rs. 6.56 crore (Rs. 6 crore and Rs. 2.56 crore respectively) is being handed over to Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad for further disbursement.
The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Aurangabad shall draw the above funds and immediately hand over the same to the District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Jalna and Aurangabad for further distribution.
The District Water Conservation Officer, Jalna and Aurangabad shall comply with the following terms and conditions while clearing the pending dues of the works for which the above funds have been earmarked:
