Government circular:
A confidential report is a fundamental and very important document for the evaluation of government officials and their further progress in the field of service. Confidential reports need to be written within a stipulated period of time and maintained properly. Therefore, in order to facilitate the evaluation of the work done by the officers of Group-A and B (Gazetted) cadre at the regional/ zonal level under this department on the basis of confidential reports, the Report and Review Officers have been fixed by the Government Circulars dated 29.09.2018, 02.11.2019 and 29.12.2021 of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation.
The General Administration Department has revised the existing pattern of performance appraisal report to ensure uniformity in the performance appraisal report of all State Government officers/ employees vide Government Resolution No. CFR-1211/ Q. No. 257/ XIII dated 7th February, 2018 and implemented it from the year 2017-18.
In view of the above, the performance appraisal reports of all Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers in the offices under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation are required to be recorded through computer system" MahaPAR." For this, the NIC e-mail IDs of all Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted Officers) in the field offices under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation have to be created, for this, the following information has to be filled by clicking on Login with eforms on the website eforms. nic. in. The form is available at mahapar@nic.in. It needs to be sent to this email. Please note that this information should be filled in correctly, in case of incorrect information, the concerned officer will be responsible.
