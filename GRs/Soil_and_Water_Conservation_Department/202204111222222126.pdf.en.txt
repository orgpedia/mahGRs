Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. It is being implemented. A total of 23 schemes in Aurangabad, Latur and Osmanabad districts will be repaired under the Aurangabad Circle Office. 477.61 crore. After the restoration of these projects, 755.7 ha of irrigated area and 2788.56 sq.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 5 to 9 have been submitted to the government.
Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. Out of the 23 schemes mentioned above, 19 are in the first priority category and 4 are in the second priority category. The following decisions are being taken:
