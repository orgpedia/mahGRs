Introduction The Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is a reference scheme for restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state. It is being implemented. A total of 89 schemes will be repaired under the Aurangabad Circle Office. 1446.54 lakhs. After the completion of these projects, 2138.74 hectares of irrigated area and 106.94.70 sq.m. of water supply will be restored.
The water supply will be restored. A proposal to get administrative approval for these works has been submitted to the government under the above reference letter. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. Considering the above, the proposal was under consideration for administrative approval as the 89 works mentioned in the revised plan of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme were included in the second priority. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Samvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 89 repair schemes in Aurangabad Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 1446.54 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
