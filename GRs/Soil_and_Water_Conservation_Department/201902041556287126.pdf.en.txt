The Proposal:
Naganachowki Ta. Proposal for grant of administrative approval to Pazar Lake at Jamner, Jalgaon District Reference No. 1 has been submitted to the government. Jamner, G. Jalgaon Pazar Lake is proposed on local drain. (10.63 LH).
Under the project, a total area of 96 hectares is planned to be brought under indirect irrigation.
The cost of the project is Rs. 4,67,20, 232/- (Rupees Four Crores, Rupees Twenty Thousand Two Hundred and Thirty Two only) and considering the prevailing parameters of the Government, the project is successful. Administrative approval for the project was pending with the government.
