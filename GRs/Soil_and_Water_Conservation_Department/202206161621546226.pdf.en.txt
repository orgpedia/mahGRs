Preamble: As per the Government Resolution dated 1st March, 2019, the office of District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Gadchiroli has been created under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation.
Department of Soil and Water Conservation. As per the Government decision dated 27.04.2022, the office of District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation, Gadchiroli has been made functional with effect from 01.05.2022.
Soil and Water Conservation Department. The office of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation, Sub-Division Wadsa has been made functional with effect from 26.05.2022.
The government was considering declaring drawing and distribution officers for the office of the District Water Conservation Officer, Mrid and Water Conservation, Gadchiroli, and for the office of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Mrid and Water Conservation, Sub-Division Wadsa. Based on this, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
The District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Gadchiroli under Soil and Water Conservation Department is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
In addition, the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation, Sub-Division Wadsa are being declared as Drawing and Distribution Officers.
