Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works on progress/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with irrigation potential of 251 to 600 hectares. A sum of Rs. 50.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2023-24 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A037. Out of which, the following decisions have been taken on the fund demand proposals for the reference date 06/10/2023 from the funds currently made available as per the reference circulars of the Finance Department.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 261.93 lakhs (Rupees Two Crores Sixty-one Lakhs Ninety-nine Thousand only) for payment of dues towards pump house control room and overhead pipe distribution tank and distribution system works of Upasa Irrigation Scheme Tukai, which has an irrigation potential of 599 hectares in Karjat Taluka of Ahmednagar district, amounting to Rs. 135.33 lakhs and Rs. 126.60 lakhs respectively.
The aforesaid funds are being distributed subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) If funds have been made available from the Water Conservation Corporation, the District Planning Committee or any other source for the works of the above projects, the funds being distributed through this Fund shall not be withdrawn or distributed,
(b) the expenditure shall not exceed the cost of administrative sanction as well as revised administrative sanction as may be approved by the Government.
(c) If funds are not required for the above projects, the funds should not be diverted for any other project under any circumstances.
d) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the funds should be immediately returned to the Government.
