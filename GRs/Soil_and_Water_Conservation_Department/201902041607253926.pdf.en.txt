Introduction: Proposal for grant of administrative approval to Pazar Lake at Jamner, Jalgaon District Reference No. 1 submitted to the Government. According to the proposal, Jamner, G. Jalgaon Pazar Lake is proposed on local drain. (2.9 LH).
A total area of 20 hectares is proposed to be brought under indirect irrigation under the project.
The cost of the project is Rs. 1,08,46, 880/- (Rupees One Crore, Eight Lakhs, Seventy-Six Thousand Eight Hundred Eighty-Eight Only) and considering the prevailing government parameters, the project is successful. Administrative approval for the project was pending with the government.
