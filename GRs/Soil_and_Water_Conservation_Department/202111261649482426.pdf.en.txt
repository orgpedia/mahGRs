Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
The date is November 25, 2021.
Additional Commissioner and Chief Engineer, Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area, Aurangabad vide P.S. 2/2059, dated 21.06.2021.
Preamble 4 gated cement embankments namely, Gated Cement Embankment Dudhondi (Nalwade field), Gated Cement Embankment Dudhondi (near Gym School), Gated Cement Embankment Dudhondi (near Canal Bridge) and Gated Cement Embankment Dudhondi (cremation ground) in Palus taluka of Sangli district are proposed on local drains. The total cost of the 4 works is estimated at Rs. 1,40,02, 229/- which will create 130.37 MW of water storage and submerge an area of 52.14 ha. Accordingly, a proposal for administrative recognition of these 4 gated cement embankment works was submitted to the Government through a letter of reference. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
The government has decided to construct 4 gated cement dams in Palus taluka of Sangli district- Gated Cement Dam Dudondi (Nalwade field), Gated Cement Dam Dudondi (near Gymnasium School), Gated Cement Dam Dudondi (near Kanal Bridge) and Gated Cement Dam Dudondi (cremation ground).
Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 1,40,11, 206/- (Rupees One Crore Forty Lakh Eleven Thousand Two Hundred and Six only) subject to the following terms and conditions.
