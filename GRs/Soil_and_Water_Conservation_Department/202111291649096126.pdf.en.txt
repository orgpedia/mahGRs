Letter from the Additional Commissioner and Chief Engineer, Water Conservation, Pune No. Q. 2/ 3346, Dated 15-11-2021.
In order to restore the irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Plan was issued in the context of Government Decision No. 4 is being implemented. A total of 298 embankments/ schemes will be repaired under the Pune Circle Office. 3672.55 lacs. After the restoration of these projects, an irrigated area of 11390 hectares and 31562.14 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 5 has been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at Mandal level. According to the information submitted by the Circle Office, these 298 embankments/ schemes were in the second priority in the repair plan and the administrative approval of the amendment proposal was under consideration. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Samvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 298 repair schemes in the Pune Regional Water Conservation Department is Rs. 3672.555 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
