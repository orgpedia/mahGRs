Preamble: An additional amount of Rs. 3.00 crore was sanctioned through the Supplementary Fund for payment of survey works for Kolhapur system of embankments in Parbhani district with a total irrigation potential of 100 hectares in the year 2019-20. However, due to the lapse of this fund, the government was considering to complete this pending charity on priority from the budgeted fund in 2020-21. The government has taken the following decisions:
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse a sum of Rs. 3.00 crore (Rupees Three Crores only) towards payment of eligible and completed survey works in the reference proposals subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) Ensure that all documents like administrative approvals, commissioning orders, survey reports, maps, field reports, etc. are available and all works are done as per the rules; (b) Ensure that the trial pit/ core logging report is complied with; (c) Check the records in the measurement booklet to ensure that the amount demanded is reasonable and that the funds are not disbursed twice for the works concerned; (d) Amounts in excess of the sanctioned cost should not be disbursed; (e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the unutilized amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
