Introduction: Watershed based soil and water conservation treatment has been taken up for many years under various centrally sponsored schemes of Soil and Water Conservation Department, State Schemes, CSR funds, Social Institution Funds and also under Employment Guarantee Scheme.
Technical and economic parameters of various soil and water conservation treatments were determined by the Government Decision dated 16 July 2007. In this, the economic parameters were revised as per the government decisions dated March 5, 2013 and October 10, 2013.

Now, as per the decision dated 16th April, 2018, under the Mahatma Gandhi National Employment Guarantee Scheme, the minimum wage of Rs. 162/- to Rs. 203/- has been increased. Considering this increase, the government was considering to improve the financial parameters of various soil and water conservation works being carried out under various schemes of watershed development.
Government's decision:
the decision of the government. As per the Mahatma Gandhi National Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), the minimum wage was fixed at Rs. 162/- The financial parameters for treatment of soil and water conservation under various schemes of watershed development were determined after taking into consideration. Now, as per the decision dated 16th April, 2018, under the Mahatma Gandhi National Employment Guarantee Scheme, the minimum wage of Rs. 162/- to Rs. increased to 203. Therefore, as per the proposal submitted by the Director, Soil Conservation and Watershed Management, Pune to the Government regarding the improvement of financial parameters for carrying out various watershed based soil and water conservation treatment works through labourers, the Government is approving to revise the financial parameters for the below mentioned 1 to 16 treatments of soil and water conservation as per Statement A.
1) The dam shutters
2) Farm Bunding (Bunding of Compartments)
3) Paddy Khachre Bandh Bandishti (Majgi)
4) Paddy Bunding
5) Repair of old paddy fields
6) Paddy Khachre Bandhishti (Majgi with stone bundh under Padakai programme)
7) Chibber land reform (open chars)
8) The Gabon Dam
9) Ardan structure (earthen dam)
10) A stone wall

The criteria set will be applicable to all the schemes implemented by the Employment Guarantee Scheme Department and the Soil and Water Conservation Department. The government decision will also be applicable for watershed development works carried out by the Social Forestry Department.
The Government decisions issued earlier regarding the concept of soil and water conservation treatment will remain in force.
The revised financial parameters under this Government decision shall come into force from the date of issue of this decision.
