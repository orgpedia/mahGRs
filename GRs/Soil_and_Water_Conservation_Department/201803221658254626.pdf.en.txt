Introduction: The Central Government had issued the Common Guidelines for Watershed Development, 2008 with effect from 01.04.2008. These guidelines have been revised and implemented by issuing Common Guidelines 2011. Accordingly, the Department of Land Resources, Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India has announced a Centrally Sponsored Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP). This programme has been included in the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana since 2015. The ratio of Central and State funding for Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana has been increased to 60:40 from 2015. The scheme is being coordinated by Vasundhara State Level Watershed Development System, Pune. As per the decision of the Government dated 21st May, 2008. The State level watershed development mechanism has been constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Water Conservation) and registered under the Societies Act, 1860.
