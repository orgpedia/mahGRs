Fund Distribution Order/ Compulsory Expenditure for the year 2018-19 No: ASANK-2018/ PR No.61/ JAL-11 Demand No. ZH- 1,3,4, 6 Department of Soil and Water Conservation, Mantralaya, Mumbai-400 032.
The date: April 9, 2018.
Subject: Financial year 2018-19
Approval for disbursement of funds and expenditure within the limit of 70% of the annual budget allocation for the year 2018-19 of mandatory expenditure of the field establishment of Soil and Water Conservation Department.
Reference:- Government Circular Finance Division No: Finance-2018/ PR No.69/ Finance-3, dated 02.04.2018.
According to the Finance Department. The Government Circular dated 2/4/2018 has approved the disbursement of funds as per the Maharashtra Appropriation Act, 2018.
In accordance with the directions given in this circular, the authority conferred on the Administrative Department to disburse and spend funds on schemes in the accompanying prospectus within the limit of 70% of the annual allocation in the mandatory expenditure budget for the first nine months of the financial year 2018-19 (upto December, 2018) for the mandatory expenditure of the field establishment of the Soil and Water Conservation Department is being approved by this Government Notification subject to the following terms and conditions and 70% of the budgetary allocation is being disbursed on the Budget Computer Distribution System.
Terms and Conditions
1) The provision so distributed has been placed under the control of the Controlling Authorities indicated at column No. 5 of the Return. The said provision should be immediately distributed to the drawing and disbursing officers under the authority.
2) The following terms and conditions must be strictly adhered to in the application of the above-mentioned funds.
1) Zila Parishads shall maintain separate accounts for subsidized expenditure, 2) Zila Parishads shall spend the grants for the purpose for which they are sanctioned, 3) The prevailing terms and conditions regarding financial powers shall be strictly adhered to, 4) The Accountant General's Office shall have the right to inspect the books and accounts of the Zila Parishads for audit. The concerned district councils will be responsible for providing assistance and facilities for this.

The Zila Parishads are required to examine the expenditure statements from their Finance Department and submit the report to the Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Board Office by the 10th of every month, the Board Offices to submit the consolidated information report of expenditure of the Zila Parishads in their division to the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad by the 15th of every month, and the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad shall submit the quarterly report of expenditure to the Government by the 20th of the next month after the end of every quarter. The District Councils should send the expenditure statements on time and the same should be strictly monitored by the concerned Regional Water Conservation Officer.
4. The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad and the Controlling Officer shall take all necessary measures to discharge the responsibility of cost coordination and coordination of other financial matters.
The said Memorandum is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the relevant Government Circular dated 2/04/2018.
