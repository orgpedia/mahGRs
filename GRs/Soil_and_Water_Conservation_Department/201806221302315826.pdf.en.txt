Order: As per the order dated 7.5.2018 of the Water Resources Department referred above, the Executive Engineer has been promoted to the post of Superintending Engineer and has been allotted Revenue Department. Since Shri Sharad Neelkanthrao Tayde, one of the officers who has been promoted, is due to retire on 30.6.2018 as per the prescribed age, it has been decided to engage him in this department only to ensure that his retirement benefits are not adversely affected in connection with the promotion and he is being given the following posting under the Revenue Department allotted to him.

2. Mr. Tayde shall immediately join the place of posting as indicated above and submit a report to the Government through the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad.
As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 31.5.2017, the post given to Mr. Tayde on promotion in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation will be for a temporary period. The Department will also continue to have the option for the period till the promotion of the officers to the cadre of Regional Water Conservation Officer or till the retirement of Mr. Tayde, whichever is earlier. Mr. Tayde is being given posting as indicated in column 4 against his name under this department subject to the condition that if officers are available on promotion from the officers given option before his retirement, they will be sent back to their parent Water Resources Department.
