Memorandum: During the financial year 2017-18, out of the budgeted amount for expenditure on installation of Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Systems, a sum of Rs. 345.26 crore has been disbursed. The Finance Ministry has now approved the revised estimates for 2017-18. Accordingly, the revised plan allocation required for the remaining period of 2017-18 has been made available to the administrative departments for disbursement. Accordingly, as per the accompanying' Form-A', Rs. 1,46,99, 200/- (Rupees One Crore Forty-Seven Lakhs Ninety-Nine Thousand Two Hundred only) is being distributed through this Subject wise Grant.
The terms and conditions of the Memorandum dated 03/05/2017 and Circulars dated 18/04/2017, 30/06/2017 and 06/02/2018 should be strictly complied with while utilising the above funds.
The said Memorandum is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Circular dated 18/04/2017.
