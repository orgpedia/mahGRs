Preamble: A sum of Rs. 80.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2018-19 under Demand No.ZH-5, Account Head 47029028 for minor irrigation schemes with an irrigation potential of 101-250 hectares. Out of which, funds to the extent of 70% amounting to Rs. 56.00 crore have been made available for disbursement as per the relevant circulars. The government was considering to distribute the funds in the form of statutory development board. Accordingly, the Government has taken the following decisions on the proposal recommended under the Reference Letter.
Decision: Minor Irrigation Lake Kuduk (Khurd) Ta. Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 231.96 lakhs (Rupees Two Crores, Thirty One Lakhs, Ninety Five Thousand only) for the construction of a project on Pragatipath in Mandangad, G. Ratnagiri with an irrigation potential of 120 hectares subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) the amount being disbursed by the Water Conservation Corporation or any other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above project, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) no amount shall be spent in excess of the cost of administrative sanction as well as revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) if funds are not required for the works of the above project, the amount shall not be disbursed for any other project under any circumstances; and (d) in case of non-availability of funds for any reason, the amount shall be returned to the Government forthwith.
