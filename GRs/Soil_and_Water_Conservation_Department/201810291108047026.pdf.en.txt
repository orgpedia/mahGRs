Water Conservation Department, Government Resolution, No. 2017/ Q. No.522/ Water-7, dated 2nd January, 2018.
Preamble: In order to provide machinery for Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan/ soil and water conservation works in the state, the government has started the Water Conservation Machinery (Earthmovers) Interest Subvention Scheme through the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, Department of Cooperation and Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation through the Government Decision dated January 2, 2018.
As per the discussion held in the meeting held on May 22, 2018 under the chairmanship of the Minister of Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles, the following amendments in the beneficiary selection process, composition of the beneficiary selection committee, etc. were under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: To provide machinery for Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan/ Soil and Water Conservation works, it is being approved to amend the" Water Enriched Machinery (Earthmovers) Interest Subvention Scheme" introduced vide Government Decision dated 02.01.2018 as under:
(1) The revised selection committee to select the beneficiaries shall be as follows:

(2) Henceforth, the priority of the beneficiaries is being cancelled and the beneficiary list and waiting list should be prepared by excluding all eligible applicants from the list without considering the priority.
The selected beneficiary will have a period of 1 month for disbursement of loan by the financial institution. Also, 1 month time is being given to the previously selected beneficiaries to complete the process of loan disbursement. However, the Managing Director, Maharashtra Co-operative Development Corporation, Pune will be empowered to take a final decision on the matter regarding the time limit for disbursement of loans to the beneficiaries.
(3) A bilateral agreement between the Government and the beneficiary. However, a bilateral Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between the financial institution and the beneficiary will be optional.
The other matters in respect of the decision of the Government in the context dated January 02, 2018 shall remain unchanged.
The selection committee will take the necessary action.
