The Proposal:
Pazar Lake in Nashik district. Malegaon, District Nashik regarding grant of Second Amendment Administrative Sanction to the Scheme. Proposal from the Executive Engineer, Minor Irrigation Department (East) Zilla Parishad, Nashik vide Reference No. 1 submitted to the government. The proposal is in the frequency-prone areas of Nashik division. The irrigation potential of the lake is 25.74 hectares. This water will be used for irrigation.
Government's decision:
Pazar Lake, Gigaon The Second Revised Administrative Sanction for the Pazar Talab Scheme at Malegaon, Dist. Nashik is being granted at a total cost of Rs. 25,01,675/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs, One Thousand Sixty Five Seventy Five) and at an incidental cost of Rs. 1,25,084 (Rupees One Lakh Twenty Five Thousand Four Eighty Four only) 5% (excluding Land Acquisition and Vesting Charges) amounting to Rs. 26,26,759/- (Rupees Twenty Six Lakh Six Thousand Seven Hundred and Sixty Nine only).
Administrative approval is granted subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) Detailed survey/ investigation of the project as per Annexure 165 of the Public Works Manual, 1984 is required to be carried out and approved at the competent level.
2) The project should be completed within the revised cost limit as mentioned above.
3) The beneficiary water use agency should be established in accordance with the Government Decision dated July 31, 2000.
(4) On completion of the project, the project shall be transferred to the Water Utilization Agency established as above for maintenance repairs.
