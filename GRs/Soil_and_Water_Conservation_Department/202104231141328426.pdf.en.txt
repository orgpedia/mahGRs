Government circular:
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department and the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, the cadre of District Water Conservation Officer in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation was created on 01.01.2020.
Provisional seniority is being published as" Form-A" attached to this circular.
2. The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list in Form-A attached to this circular.
As per the provisions of SRV- 1076/12, dated 21.06.1982, the District Water Conservation Officer shall be considered for determining seniority in the cadre.

3. The said circular shall be brought to the immediate notice of the District Water Conservation Officer in Form A under him by the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad, and a copy of the same shall be preserved in his own records and made available to the Government.
(4) In case of any error/ omission in the particulars of the District Water Conservation Officer included in the said seniority list, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., they shall be informed in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular, by registered post/ speed post/ hand with proper proof as required.
Bring your objections/ suggestions to the government. If the errors/ errors are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the stipulated time, the temporary seniority of the District Water Conservation Officer working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation will be finalized as on 01.01.2020.
