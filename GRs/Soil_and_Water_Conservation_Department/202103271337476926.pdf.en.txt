Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2020-21 under ZH-3, Account Head 27029219. Out of this, funds have been disbursed under the order of Reference-2 for the proposal on Reference-1 of Marathwada Division. Now, as per the revised estimates for the financial year 2020-2021, the Government has decided to disburse the balance funds out of the total fund of Rs. 2.50 crore received under the scheme.
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse funds to the extent of Rs. 149.69 lakhs (Rupees One Crore One Hundred and Fifty Five Lakh Seventy Seven Thousand only), currently available for payment of balance eligible and completed survey works in the relevant proposals of Aurangabad district.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) In order to ensure that no more than the reasonable amount for the survey is disbursed, the District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall again verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable before disbursing the actual funds; (b) The concerned Sub-Divisional Office shall verify that the funds are not being disbursed twice for the above survey work.
