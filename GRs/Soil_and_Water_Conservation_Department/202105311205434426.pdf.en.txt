Seniority in the cadre of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer will be taken into consideration.
The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad shall bring the said circular to the immediate notice of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer in Form A Part-I to III under him and shall preserve a signed copy of the communication in his own records and make a copy of the same available to the Government.
If any error/ error is found in the details of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officers included in the said seniority list, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., they should bring their representations/ objections/ suggestions to the Government through registered post/ speed post/ hand delivery along with necessary proper proof in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular. If the errors/ errors are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the prescribed time, the temporary seniority of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as on 01.01.2020 will be finalized.
