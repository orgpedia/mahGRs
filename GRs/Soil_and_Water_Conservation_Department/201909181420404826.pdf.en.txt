Preamble: The Government has taken over the liability of Rs. 30.27 crore for 204 villages/ Pazhar ponds which were not included in the proposal in Reference 1 and some of them have been completed as per the orders in Reference 2 and 3. Certificates of appropriation of the said expenditure have been submitted to the Government vide letter No. 5 with reference to the list of works. Accordingly, photographs with latitude and longitude of the remaining 85 works, copies of the cross-checked measurement book, documents confirming the absence of land acquisition liability, copies of technical approvals and documents stating that the demands were being made since 2012-13 have been made available to the government. Accordingly, the Government has decided to pay the balance liability of the sanctioned works as follows.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government hereby approve the release of Rs. 12.62 crore (Rupees Twelve Crore Sixty Two Lakhs only) as per the attached Form A for meeting the pending liabilities by clearing the dues of the works done in 85 villages/ Pazar Tals of Aurangabad District having capacity 0 to 100 which are not liable to be acquired by the Government.
