GOVERNMENT ORDER: Whereas the General Administration Department, Govt. Pursuant to the provision dated 14/10/2011, Shri Hrishikesh Prahladrao Deshmukh, Tatka. After reviewing the suspension of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Sub-Division, Vaijapur and the in-charge Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad, the competent authority has approved the reinstatement of them in non-executive posts in government service subject to departmental/ judicial inquiry. Accordingly, Mr. Deshmukh, Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer was transferred in the context of the department subject to departmental/ judicial enquiry against him. His suspension has been revoked and reinstated in Government service vide order dated 12/07/2023.
Accordingly, the Government hereby appoint Shri Hrishikesh Prahladrao Deshmukh, Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer, to the vacant post of Assistant District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik after re-establishment of the post.
3. Mr. Deshmukh should immediately attend the office of the post. It may be noted that if the above officers do not appear at the place of posting given to them as per this order or if they try to change the post by exerting any kind of pressure, then disciplinary action will be taken against them as the act is in violation of Rule 23 of the M.N.S. (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
