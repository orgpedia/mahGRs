Preamble: The scheme of Silt Free Dams and Sludge Free Sewers was implemented by the Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation, Government of India vide Government Resolution dated 06.05.2017. Since the term of the scheme expired at the end of March, 2021, the proposal to implement the scheme in its present form was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: This Government Decision approves the implementation of the' Silt Free Dam and Siltation Sewer Scheme" for the next 3 years for de-silting of water bodies under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation and for use in the field.
