The Proposal:
The Adarshgaon Resolution and Project Scheme has been launched under the Government Resolution dated 20th February, 1992. This scheme is based on the concept of village development through public participation. Various schemes are being implemented by the government for the development of villages. These include 59 schemes of 12 different government departments. Out of these, watershed development work has been classified as core area and work of other departments as non-core area.
A State-level committee has also been formed to implement the scheme. The guidelines of the scheme have been revised with reference to the Government Decisions dated 13th August, 2010 and 10th March, 2015.
