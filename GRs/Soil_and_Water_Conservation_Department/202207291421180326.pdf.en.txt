Government order:
Shri V.B.Nath, Additional Commissioner, Water Conservation, Soil and Water Conservation, Regional Area, Aurangabad has been given the additional charge of the post of Managing Director, Maharashtra State Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad till further orders of the Government. Shri Nath will hold the additional charge of the said posts in addition to his original charge.
The additional charge of the post of Managing Director, Maharashtra State Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad in the capacity of Additional Commissioner, Water Conservation, Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area, Pune is hereby terminated.
These orders will come into force with immediate effect.
