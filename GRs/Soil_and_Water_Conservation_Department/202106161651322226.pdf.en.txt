Introduction: The plan of action. Umred, G.Nagpur is on the local canal of the village and was completed in 1994-95. This scheme is in priority 1 in the repair program plan under the' Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme'. The project has a capacity to store 250 cubic metres of water and irrigate 64 acres. It has been amended to 64. Irrigation capacity will be restored. Therefore, the matter of administrative approval of the repair works was under consideration of the government.
