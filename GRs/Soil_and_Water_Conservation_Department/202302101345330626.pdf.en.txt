The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds are to be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without disbursement for any other purpose.
d) Strictly follow the guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
