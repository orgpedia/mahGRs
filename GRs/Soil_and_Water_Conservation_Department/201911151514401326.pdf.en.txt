Introduction: The context. As per the order dated 12/09/18, funds were disbursed for 24 gated cement embankments in Dhule district in 2018-19 within the then sanctioned price limit. Thereafter, the matter of disbursement of funds within the revised approved price limit was under consideration of the Government as it had accorded revised administrative approval for the enhanced expenditure on these projects. In this regard, the government has taken the following decision on the proposal of the Nashik Circle Office.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: For the year 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 446.12 lakh (Rupees Four Crores Forty Six Lakhs Twelve Thousand only) as per Form B attached for the work of 24 Gated Cement Embankments with irrigation capacity of 0 to 100 hectares in Dhule District from the sanctioned provision. Government approval is hereby accorded for distribution subject to the terms and conditions as on 12/09/18.
Accordingly, the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik shall draw the above amount and disburse the same to the District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Dhule expeditiously and the same shall be met from the sanctioned provision for the financial year 2019-2020 by spending the same under the following head of accounts.
