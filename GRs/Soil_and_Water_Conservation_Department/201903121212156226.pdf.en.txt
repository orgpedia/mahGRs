Preamble: It is proposed to create a Geo Portal of the Department for collating village-wise data of works done in the catchment areas under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation along with the latitude and longitude. In this, the work done before and under the campaign in the villages, which are mainly involved in the Jalyukt Shivar campaign, will be marked with the help of satellite images. Accordingly, administrative approval has been accorded under the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan (JYM) for marking of soil and water conservation works in 22,598 identified villages during four years from 2015-16 to 2018-19 by external agencies using satellite-based maps.

Under the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan, the work of marking the soil and water conservation treatment completed before and during the campaign using satellite based maps is to be done by an external agency. For this, the government was considering to disburse the required amount of Rs. 8.70 lakh (Rs. 8 lakh seventy thousand only) for the first phase.
As per the agreement in the Request for Proposal regarding the marking of soil and water conservation treatment done by the Soil and Water Conservation Department using satellite based maps before and after the selection of villages selected under the campaign period under the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan, since the work order for the identification of 6000 villages in the first phase has been given to an external agency, the organization is required to complete the marking work within one month and for the first phase, the amount of Rs. 8.70 lakh is required. The Director, Soil Conservation and Watershed Management, Pune vide letter dated 1st March, 2019 has sought Rs. 8.70 lakh (Rupees Eight Lakh Seventy Thousand only) is being approved.
2. To draw this fund from the treasury, the Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Commissioner (Agriculture), State of Maharashtra, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer.
The expenditure in this regard should be shown under the head Demand No.ZH-3, Main Accounts Title-2402, Control and Evaluation of Soil and Water Conservation Schemes (Programme) 27, Small Constructions (2402 A201).
The Director, Soil Conservation and Watershed Management, Pune will be responsible for submitting the utilization certificate of the said fund to the Government.
