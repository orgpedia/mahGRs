Accordingly, the following decisions have been taken on the reference proposal for payment of pending dues of the work done on this project which is in progress.
Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 30.00 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Lakhs only) for the projects at Shirval, Taka Kankavali, G. Sindhudurg subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) If funds are available from the Water Conservation Corporation, the District Planning Committee or other sources for the works of the above projects, the funds being distributed through it should not be withdrawn or distributed; (b) The funds should not be spent beyond the cost of administrative approval as well as the revised administrative approval approved by the Government; (c) If there is no need for funds for the above projects, the funds should not be distributed to other projects under any circumstances; (d) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the funds should be immediately returned to the Government; e) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds should be strictly followed.
(f) The works have been completed 100% and no complaints have been received and the quality of the work should be ensured by making payment and submitting a compliance report to the government.
