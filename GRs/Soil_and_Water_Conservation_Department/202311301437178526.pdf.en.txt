Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse an amount of Rs. 5.60 lakh (Rupees Five Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) for the purpose of payment of fees for the work of concept and project viewer map of the project at K.P. Bandhara Lakhonda in Akola District and Taluka 0 to 100 H.S.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) To ensure that no more than the reasonable amount is disbursed for the aforesaid conceptual work, the concerned District Water Conservation Officer shall re-verify before the actual disbursement of funds to the Government that the amount demanded is reasonable, b) to ensure that the funds are not disbursed twice for the conceptualization and project mapping of the project, c) if there is no need for funds for any reason, the disbursed amount should not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances, d) no more than the sanctioned amount should be disbursed, e) if the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the unutilized amount should be immediately returned to the Government, f) for the purpose of disbursement of funds.
