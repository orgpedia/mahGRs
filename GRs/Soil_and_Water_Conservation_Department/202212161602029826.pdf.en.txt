The Proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has invited applications for the post of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer (Architecture) Group-A in the Maharashtra Engineering (Architecture) Service Examination, 2019.
Out of the recommended candidates, 72 candidates who have qualified the examination are presently eligible for the post. In accordance with Section 1& 2, Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer (Architecture) Group-A has been given temporary appointment subject to satisfactory completion of two years probationary period and has been deputed to Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad and sent to Valmi, Aurangabad and thereafter to Yashda, Pune for 10 weeks basic training. Accordingly, the Government was considering the creation of 72 supernumerary posts on the establishment of the office of the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad, so that the salary and allowances for the period of training under the examination of the candidates of the cadre of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer (Architecture) Group-A and other equivalent posts recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final results of the Maharashtra Engineering Examination-2019 could be paid from the Commissionerate.

Government's decision:
The 72 candidates recommended by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final result of the Maharashtra Engineering Examination-2019 have been provisionally appointed under the Government Order No. 1 and 2, subject to satisfactory completion of the probation period of two years, and they have been sent to Valmi, Aurangabad initially for 10 weeks of basic training from 05/12/2022 to the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad and have joined Valmi Aurangabad. These candidates are from Aurangabad, Maharashtra. The training has started from 05/12/2022. After the training at Valmy, the candidates will be sent to Yashoda, Pune for administrative training.
The pay scale in the revised pay matrix for 72 candidates of the cadre of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer (Architecture) Group-A and other equivalent posts selected by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission on the basis of the final results of the Maharashtra Engineering Examination-2019 and engaged for training has been increased to S-20 (Rs. 56100- 177500/-) A total of 72 supernumerary posts have been given. 05/12/2022 to D. Government approval is being granted for construction for a period of 10 weeks from 18/02/2023.
The said 72 supernumerary posts are being created on the establishment in the office of the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad.
