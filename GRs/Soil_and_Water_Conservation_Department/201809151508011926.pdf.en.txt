Women employees working in this department are hereby informed that they should submit any complaint regarding sexual harassment to the Chairperson or Member Secretary of the Committee or the Internal Complaints Committee.
The functions of the Women's Complaints Committee shall be as follows:
(a) The Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 23.04.2013 and under Sec. The Rules have been published on 09.12.2013. To take necessary measures to prevent sexual harassment of women and to provide a safe working environment.
(b) to examine the complaints received at the level of the Department for redressal within a stipulated time and submit proposals for further action to the appropriate authorities in accordance with the complaints.
c) To create awareness to prevent incidents like sexual harassment and to ensure creation of safe environment by holding periodic meetings of the Committee.
d) To prepare an annual report in accordance with the provisions of this Act.
