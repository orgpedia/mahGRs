Preamble: Under the 6th Census of Centrally Sponsored Small Irrigation Projects, the State is in the process of enumerating all small irrigation projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 2000 hectares completed during 2017-18 to 2019-20. For this, the government has decided to disburse the funds available as per the letter of reference from the central government as follows.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approve the release of funds amounting to Rs. 4.78 lakh (Rupees Four Lakh Seventy Eight Thousand only) out of the budgeted amount of Rs. 200.00 lakh for the Census Programme for the financial year 2018-19. The fund is being handed over to the Commissioner (Soil and Water Conservation) Aurangabad for utilisation.
(2) The above funds shall be utilized by the Central Government. For the matters mentioned in the letter dated 06/12/18, the Government has issued a notification regarding the disbursement of funds. The terms and conditions of the circular dated 02/04/18 should be complied with.
The expenditure shall be covered under the following heads of accounts and shall be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2018-19.
