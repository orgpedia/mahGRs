The Maharashtra government has decided to extend the term of the review committee set up to review the report of the Special Investigation Team probing the Tiware dam breach in Ratnagiri district.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Soil and Water Conservation
Government Resolution No: Navin-2019/ PR No.180/ Ministry of Water-4, Mumbai-400 032
Date: January 13, 2021.
Read: Department of Soil and Water Conservation, Government Decisions Dated 06th July, 2019 and 20th October, 2020
Government's decision:
A Review Committee has been constituted vide the relevant Government Resolution dated 20.10.2020 to review the report submitted by the Special Investigation Team appointed for in-depth investigation into the loss of lives and property due to the breach of Tiware Dam in Chiplun Taluka of Ratnagiri district.
Approval is being given for extension of time for submission of the report to the said Review Committee by another two months i.e. till the end of 20th February, 2021.
