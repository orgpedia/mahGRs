A sum of Rs. 450.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2022-23 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046. Out of which, the proposal for seeking funds from the funds made available as per the relevant circular of the Finance Department, dated 05/12/2022, has been decided as follows.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 21.17 lakhs (Rupees Twenty One Lakh Seventy Seven Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of 16 Pazar tanks in Akole Taluka of Ahmednagar district.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions: a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work; b) The amount should not be paid more than the sanctioned cost; c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for any other purpose; d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
