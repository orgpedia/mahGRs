The Proposal:
In order to motivate the officers and staff to perform their duties efficiently and effectively and to boost their morale, an innovative scheme" Mukhyamantri Sukarmi Puraskar Yojana" has been launched from the financial year 2018-19 under the decision of the Government in the context. The scheme is being implemented at the Ministry level initially for the financial year 2018-19. Accordingly, the Government was considering to constitute a committee to award marks to the officers/ employees of Soil and Water Conservation Department for their subjective performance in the selection of" Vibhag Ratna Puraskar" to be given at the administrative department level as per the provisions of the relevant Government Decision.
Government's decision:
The following Committee is being constituted to award marks to the officers/ staff of Soil and Water Conservation Department for their subjective aspects in the selection of" Vibhag Ratna Puraskar" to be given at the level of Administrative Department in the Ministry.
