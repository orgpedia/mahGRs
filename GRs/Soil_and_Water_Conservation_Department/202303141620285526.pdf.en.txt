Introduction The Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is a reference scheme for restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state. N. No. 1 is being implemented. Under the Aurangabad Circle Office, 20 schemes will be repaired and a total amount of Rs. 356.44 crore. The irrigation potential of these works is 465. The total water storage is 2064 cusecs.
The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 4 has been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. Considering the above, the 20 works mentioned in the revised plan of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme were under consideration for administrative approval of the proposal. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Samvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 20 repair schemes in Aurangabad Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 356.44 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
