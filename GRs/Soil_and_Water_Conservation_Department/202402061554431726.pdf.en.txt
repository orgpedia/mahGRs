The Proposal -
Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation, Govt. Adarshgaon Scheme under the Government Decision of 10.03.2015.
New guidelines have been issued and the Adarshgaon Sankalp and Project Programme has been extended to the entire state.
It is implemented at various levels at the state, district, and village levels.
Committees have been formed. To formulate Model Village Resolution and Project Programme in Adarsh Gaon Yojana and to organize suggestions on how it should be implemented.
The State Level Committee has been constituted as follows. The government has taken the decision. 10.03.2015
The "State Level Committee" in paragraph 29 in A.No. 1 includes a non-official member in the position of Prof. Dr. Purushottam Vayal, "Swimmers" Project Coordinator, Art of Lichtvag, Maharashtra
The matter was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision - By this Government Decision in paragraph 29 No. 1 of the Government Decision-Referred State Level
The committee was "amended as follows to include a non-official member in the post of Prof. Dr. Purushottam."
Viaal, "Swimmers" Project Coordinator, Art of Lichtvag, Maharashtra is being appointed.
After the appointment of Prof. Dr. Purushottam Vyal, the revised composition of the Adarshgaon State Level Committee will be as follows.
