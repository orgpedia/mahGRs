Committee's mandate:
Before submitting the proposal for administrative/ revised administrative approval of minor irrigation projects (new and repair) above the cost of Rs.10 crore to the State Government, it will be mandatory to obtain the approval of the said Committee.
Proposals for administrative approval/ revised administrative approval of minor irrigation projects (new and amendment), costing more than Rs.10 crore, should be first sent to the State Technical Advisory Committee for technical scrutiny by the concerned Regional Water Conservation Officers, Soil and Water Conservation Departments. The State Technical Advisory Committee will be responsible for in-depth technical scrutiny of the proposal. The State Technical Advisory Committee shall thoroughly scrutinize the proposals for administrative approval/ revised administrative approval as per the provisions of Government Decisions/ Government Circulars/ Notices/ prevailing policy etc. issued from time to time and report to the concerned Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department as per the functioning of the Technical Advisory Committee of the Water Resources Department. The proposal for administrative approval/ revised administrative approval should be submitted to the government as per the prevailing rules after incorporating the amendments suggested by the State Technical Advisory Committee.
The headquarters of the committee shall be the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad.
