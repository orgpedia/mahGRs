The Government hereby approves disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 496.50 lakh (Rupees Four Crore Ninety Six Lakh Fifty Thousand only) for land acquisition and Rs. 23.57 lakh for construction of the project subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) the amounts being disbursed by it shall not be withdrawn or disbursed if funds have been made available from other sources for the construction and land development of the aforesaid project.
(b) If for any reason the funds remain unutilized, the amount shall be returned to the Government forthwith.
(c) If the funds provided for the above purposes are not required, the funds shall not be distributed among themselves for any other purpose. Funds for land acquisition should not be spent on construction.
d) Guidelines in the circular of the Finance Department dated 18/04/17 and 30/06/17 regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
The amount so disbursed shall be appropriated by the Superintending Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Board, Pune and shall be distributed expeditiously to the concerned officers subordinate to him.
The expenditure on this should be covered under the following heads of accounts and should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2017-18.
