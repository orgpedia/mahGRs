2 Kolhapuri type dams at Ahmedpur, G. Latur are proposed on local drains. The total estimated cost of the 2 works is Rs. 2,86,33, 900.00/- which will create 196.99 cusecs of water storage and submerge 56 hectares of area. Accordingly, a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the said 2 crore dam works was submitted to the Government under the reference letter.
