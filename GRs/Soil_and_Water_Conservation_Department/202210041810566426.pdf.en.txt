Preamble: In order to provide machinery for Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan/ soil and water conservation works in the state, the government has decided to provide Water Resources Machinery (Earthmovers) Interest Subvention Scheme to educated unemployed youth/ cooperative societies of unemployed/ registered group farms/ farmer producer companies/ various executive cooperative societies through the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, Department of Cooperation and Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation. It was launched on January 2, 2018.
Under this scheme, the amount of interest reimbursement allowed by the government for excavators (earthmovers) is credited to the beneficiary's savings account by the government after the beneficiary submits a demand for interest reimbursement by submitting proof of payment of instalments. The Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation is responsible for providing the interest reimbursement amount admissible by the government to the beneficiaries from time to time. Accordingly, as demanded under letter dated 28.06.2022 in respect of Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune, an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 2.10 crore (Rs. two crore ten lakhs only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Jal Samriddhi Machinery (Earth Movers) Interest Subvention Scheme launched under the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan/ Government Decision dated 02.01.2018 to provide machinery for soil and water conservation works, 318 beneficiaries in the State have received machinery. The maximum amount of interest payable is Rs. 9833.33 (Rupees Nine Thousand Eight Hundred and Thirty Three Paisa Thirty Three only) per month for 318 beneficiaries as interest instalment for the last week of June, 2022 to be credited to the savings account of the respective beneficiaries.
