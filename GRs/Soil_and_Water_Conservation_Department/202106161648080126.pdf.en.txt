Introduction The Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is a reference scheme for restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state. It is being implemented. Pazar Lake under Nashik Mandal Office, Mirishankarwadi, Dist. The Pathardi G Ahmednagar scheme will be repaired at a total cost of Rs 13.32 lakh. The project will restore 84 hectares of irrigated area and 406 sq.m. of water storage. The proposal for administrative approval of these works has been submitted to the government by the Additional Commissioner and Chief Engineer, Water Conservation, Aurangabad. The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik has scrutinized/ verified the budget at his level. In view of the above, the proposal for amendment of the said work was under consideration for grant of administrative approval.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The estimated cost of this scheme is Rs.1331843/- (Rupees Thirteen Lakhs Thirty One Thousand Eight Hundred and Forty Three only). This Price Budget is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions:
