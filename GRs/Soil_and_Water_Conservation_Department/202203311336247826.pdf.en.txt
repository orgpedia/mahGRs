Preamble: Demand No. 1 for conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 619.41 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046. Now, as per the revised estimates, 100% limit of funding is permissible under the scheme. Out of which, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal for seeking funds from the funds made available as on 28/03/2022.
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse a total amount of Rs. 95.17 lakhs (Rupees Ninety Five Lakhs Seventeen Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of 13 projects in the reference proposal in Akola district as follows.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions: a) The funds are to be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring completion of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) In case the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without disbursement for any other purpose, d) Guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
(f) The works have been completed 100% and no complaints have been received and the quality of the work should be ensured and payment should be made and the compliance report should be submitted to the government.
