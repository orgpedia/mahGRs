The Proposal:
The programme of construction of cement embankments in taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
A total of Rs. 190.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Ninety Lakhs) was under consideration.
Government's decision:
During the year 2019-20, Cement Dam Programme Account Head 4402 2799 involving an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 60.00 crore has been provided. In respect of the demand made under Letter No. 4 under River Rejuvenation Programme, the cement embankment of the embankment sanctioned in the year 2017-18 in Chandrapur district has been completed at a total cost of Rs. 190.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Ninety Lakhs) is being sanctioned as follows:

The Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Executive Engineer/ District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending the funds being made available for the construction of the cement dam. The Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune, shall take care of the expenditure on the targets allocated.
The funds being disbursed are subject to the following conditions:
3.1 If the above Cement Dam is funded by other funds, the funds distributed by the said Government Decision shall not be withdrawn or distributed.
The Executive Engineer/ District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district should take care that the expenditure is not deducted.
3.2 No amount shall be spent in excess of the administrative sanction or the price agreed to by the Government.
3.3 If the funds provided remain unspent for any reason, the unspent funds shall not be used for any other purpose. The money should be immediately returned to the government.
3.4 The full utilization certificate and the certificate of completion of the work given so far should be submitted to the government immediately.
The approved funds should be spent under the following head of accounts.
" Demand No. ZH-5, 4402 Capital Expenditure on Soil and Water Conservation, 102 Soil Conservation, (02) Cement Dam (Check Dam) Programme, (4402 2799), 53 Major Constructions
The said Government Decision is being issued pursuant to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide Circular dated 08.07.2019 in the context of Finance Department.
