Introduction The Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is a reference scheme for restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state. N. No. 4 is being implemented. A total of 278 schemes under the Nashik Circle Office will be repaired. 5233.21 Lakhs. The irrigation potential of these works is 6801. The total water storage available in the reservoir is 31303.10 mm.
The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 5 has been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik has scrutinized/ scrutinized all the budgets at his level. Considering the above, the proposal was under consideration for administrative approval as the mentioned 278 works were included in the revised plan of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Samvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 278 repair schemes in the Nashik Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 5233.21 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
