Introduction: The Plan of Action Kankavli, Sindhudurg, has been completed in the year 2012 on the local drain of the village. This scheme is in priority 2 in the repair program plan under the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme. As per the original estimate, the catchment area of the project was 1.19 sq. The total water storage capacity is 2460 cusecs and the primary irrigation capacity is 178 cusecs. After the 178. Irrigation capacity will be restored. Water is leaking from the well and gate of the insulator of this scheme. Further, 253.50 m of earth has been removed from the earth fill of the dam on the other side (D/ S) of the main reservoir. Here it is estimated at 108.50 m R. L is water. Therefore, the matter of administrative approval of the repair works was under consideration of the government.
