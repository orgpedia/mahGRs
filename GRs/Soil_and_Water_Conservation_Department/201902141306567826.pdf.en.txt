The Proposal:
There are 7 Minor Irrigation Sub-Divisions functioning under Amravati Zilla Parishad. Of them, the Amaravati Zilla Parishad passed a resolution regarding the reorganization of the jurisdiction of the Minor Irrigation Sub-Divisions of Amaravati and Tivasa. As per the proposal submitted to the Government by the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amaravati vide letter dated 11.01.2016, the matter of reorganization of the place and jurisdiction of Minor Irrigation Sub-Division at Amaravati and Tivasa under Amaravati Zilla Parishad was under the consideration of the Government. Following this, the government took decision:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The proposal of the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Amravati, to reorganize the jurisdiction of the Minor Irrigation Sub-Division of Amravati and Tivsa under the Amravati Zilla Parishad as follows.
