Executive Engineer, Water Resources Department, Mr. M. B. Dhokchawale is working as District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Ahmednagar on deputation.
The Water Resources Department had transferred the services of Mr. Dhokchawale to this department on deputation. In this regard, reference No. Under Section 2, Mr. M.B. Dhokchawale, Executive Engineer has been posted on deputation in this department. Thereafter, on the request of Mr. Dhokchawale, the said Reference No. As per the order of the 3rd Chief Minister, the post of District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Ahmednagar has been transferred.
Now, the Water Resources Department has requested to repatriate the services of Mr. Dhokchawale to the Water Resources Department. Therefore, in view of the request of the Water Resources Department, the government has decided to terminate the deputation of Mr. Dhokchawale under this order and return his services to his parent department of Water Resources.
4. The services of Mr. Dhokchawale are being repatriated to the Water Resources Department by this order and he is being relieved from this administrative department with immediate effect.
As a result, the additional charge of the vacant post of District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Ahmednagar, has been given to Mr. Pandurang Bajrangrao Gaisumudre, District Water Conservation Officer, Zilla Parishad (ZP). The department is being handed over to Ahmednagar till further orders. Shri Gaisamudre will hold the additional charge of the post of District Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Ahmednagar.
