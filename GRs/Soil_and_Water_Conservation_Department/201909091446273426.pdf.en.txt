The proposal-
The Adarsh Gaon Sankalp and Project Committee Scheme is based on the concept of village development through public participation. Various schemes are being implemented by the government for the development of villages. Among them, watershed development work has been classified as core area and work of other departments as non-core area. A State-level committee has also been formed to implement the scheme.
This State Level Executive Committee has been empowered to select 100 new villages in the first phase in addition to the total 201 villages as per the Government decision dated 01.08.2010. Government's decision
As per the decision of the Government dated 10th March, 2015, revised guidelines of Adarsh Gaon Yojana have been issued and the Government has empowered to identify 100 new villages in the first phase by constituting a State Level Executive Committee for the overall development of the villages. Under this decision, 22 villages selected under this authority will be included in the Adarsh Gaon Resolution and Project Committee.
