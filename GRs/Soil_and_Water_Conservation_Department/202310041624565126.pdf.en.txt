Out of which, as per the relevant circular of the Finance Department, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal for seeking funds from the funds available under the current scheme, as on 24/03/2023.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 38.73 lakhs (Rupees Eighty Eight Lakhs Seventy Three Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of Irrigation Tank Kinhala and Irrigation Tank Lyahari in Hadgaon Taluka of Nanded District at a cost of Rs. 0.43 lakhs and Rs. 38.30 lakhs respectively.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds are to be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work.
(b) shall not be paid in excess of the agreed price;
(a) In case the fund remains unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without distributing it for any other purpose; (d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
