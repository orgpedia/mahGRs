Preamble: As per the proviso to Rule 4, sub-rule 5 (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, the Government officers/ employees who have been suspended for the commission of a criminal offence in respect of disproportionate assets, moral turpitude, bribery, murder, attempt to murder, rape and such other serious cases shall be liable for periodic review of the suspension. Under the above Government decision, Suspension Review Committees have been constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary for Group-A and Group-B officers and under the Chairmanship of the concerned Divisional Commissioner for Group-C and Group-D employees. In accordance with the policy of the Government to delegate authority and speed up the decision-making process by simplifying the decision-making process, under the General Administration Department, No. 3 Under the above Government decision, instructions have been issued to constitute a Suspension Review Committee at the level of the Administrative Department instead of the Committee headed by the Chief Secretary to review the suspension of officers with criminal cases registered and suspended in Group-A and Group-B cadres. Accordingly, the matter of setting up a suspension review committee at the administrative division level in respect of Group-A and Group-B gazetted officers of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation was under consideration.
