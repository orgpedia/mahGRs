Preamble: In order to provide machinery for Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan/ soil and water conservation works in the state, the government has decided to provide Water Resources Machinery (Earthmovers) Interest Subvention Scheme to educated unemployed youth/ cooperative societies of unemployed/ registered group farms/ farmer producer companies/ various executive cooperative societies through the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, Department of Cooperation and Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation. It was launched on January 2, 2018.
Under this scheme, the amount of interest reimbursement allowed by the government for excavators (earthmovers) is credited to the beneficiary's savings account by the government after the beneficiary submits a demand for interest reimbursement by submitting proof of payment of instalments. The Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation is responsible for providing the interest reimbursement amount admissible by the government to the beneficiaries from time to time. Accordingly, as per the letter dated 15th April, 2021 issued in respect of Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune, an amount of Rs. 400.00 lakhs (Rupees Four Crores) was under consideration of the Government.
