Introduction: About the Water Resources (Earthmovers) Interest Subvention Scheme. As per the discussion held in the meeting held on 22nd May, 2018 under the chairmanship of the Minister (Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles), the beneficiary selection process, composition of beneficiary selection committee etc. have been amended as per the relevant Government decision dated 29th October, 2018. As per the decision taken in the meeting held under the chairmanship of Hon'ble Minister (Cooperation, Marketing and Textiles) and in the meeting held on 19th November, 2018 with the representatives of the State Level Banking Committee, it was under the consideration of the Government to revise the interest reimbursement system in the Water Augmentation Machinery (Earthmovers) Interest Subvention Scheme.
Government Decision: A Government Decision dated 02.01.2018 has been issued in respect of" Jal Samriddhi Machinery (Earth Movers) Interest Subvention Scheme" for providing machinery for Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan/ Soil and Water Conservation works. In the Government Resolution No.
Section 2 (2) is amended as follows:
" The financial institutions have to recover the entire amount from the beneficiaries. The amount of interest reimbursement admissible by the Government for excavators (earthmovers) will be credited to the beneficiary's savings account by the Government every 3 months after the beneficiary submits the demand for interest reimbursement to the Government by submitting proof of payment of instalment. The maximum limit of interest refund is Rs. 5.90 lakh and it will be paid in the same week. "
