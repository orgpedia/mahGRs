Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department
The amendment of the water conservation treatment structures is proposed in line with the restoration of irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the schemes that require maintenance and repair from the completed water conservation schemes in the state. A total of 231 schemes will be repaired under Aurangabad Circle Office and the total amount for repair is Rs. 35.5151 crore. After the repair of these projects, 6422.91 hectares of irrigated area and 32788.51 sq.m. of water storage will be restored. A proposal to get administrative approval for these works has been submitted to the government under the above reference letter. The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad has scrutinized/ verified all the budgets at his level and certified the same. Considering the above, administrative approval was under consideration for the amendment proposal of the 231 works mentioned. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
