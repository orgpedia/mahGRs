In order to restore the irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented under the relevant Schedule No. 1. Pazar Lake Bati-Sangde, Ta.
Khalapur, G. Raigad has been completed in 1981 by the Rural Water Supply Department. Since that time there was a huge and constant leakage from the main release and the bottom of the dam.
Since there are leakages in these Pazar lakes, the scheme needs to be revised for the safety of the dam. This scheme is in priority 2 in the framework of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Plan.
With the revision of the scheme, the original envisaged water storage is 866.84 cft and the original irrigation potential is 48 hectares. Therefore, the matter of giving administrative approval to these repair works was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:

1. Conditions to be imposed on the contractor for setting up a water utility.

a) Before starting the work b) After completion of 25%.
c) 50% of the work is completed.
d) After completion of 75% of the work.
d) 100% of the work is completed.
Geotag video shooting should be done in five stages in this work. The duration of each video will be at least 3 minutes.
i) Before starting the work, the shooting should be started from one end and the entire shooting should be done till the other end.
(ii) The presence of the Water Conservation Officer and the contractor/ his trained technical staff at all times during the video shooting is mandatory. At least twice the presence of the contractor himself is required.
(iii) At least two stages of shooting, the presence of Deputy Engineer and one stage of Executive Engineer is mandatory.
