In order to restore the irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented under the relevant Schedule No. 1. The Yadava-era Rang Mahal Lake is located at Mauje Mawsala. The construction of this lake of Khuldabad, Aurangabad is of the Yadava period. A large amount of water is being wasted due to the collapse of the stone wall of the lake. The water level is rising due to heavy rains in the catchment areas of the district. The storage capacity of the lake is 567.99 cc.
The proposed amendment would result in an approximate cost of Rs 96.30 crore for the scheme. Irrigation will be restored in the area.
Therefore, the matter of giving administrative approval to these repair works was under the consideration of the government.
