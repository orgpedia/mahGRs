The Proposal:
Under the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan/ Water Conservation Mission, the government has provided financial assistance to educated unemployed youth/ cooperative societies of unemployed/ registered group farms/ farmers producer companies/ various executive cooperative societies for providing machinery for soil and water conservation works. The 1st Governing Decision has been initiated as of January 2, 2018.
The Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation is responsible for making available the interest reimbursement amount admissible by the Government on the loans made available by the financial institutions to the beneficiaries to the respective financial institutions from time to time.
Accordingly, the Maharashtra Co-operative Development Corporation, Pune vide No. 2 for payment of interest instalment to the concerned financial institution on demand under letter of Rs. A sum of Rs. 125.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Twenty Five Lakhs) was under consideration of the Government.

Government's decision:
Under the Water Efficient Machinery (Earth Movers) Interest Subvention Scheme launched under the Government Decision dated 02.01.2018 to provide machinery for Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan/ Soil and Water Conservation works, 200 beneficiaries in the State have been provided with machinery. In view of the maximum interest subvention being provided by the Government, it is being approved to disburse a sum of Rs. 125.00 lakhs (Rs. One Crore Twenty Five Lakhs) to Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune for payment of 6 months interest subvention to 200 beneficiaries at an interest subvention of Rs.9, 833.33 per month.
Commissioner (Cooperation) will be the controlling officer for the scheme. The Managing Director, Maharashtra Co-operative Development Corporation, Pune is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw funds from the treasury.
The Controlling Officer and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer have made this provision vide No. It should be ensured that the expenditure is incurred in accordance with the procedures, terms and conditions set out under the Government Decision No. 1.
The expenditure in this regard should be shown under the head Demand No.ZH-3, Head of Accounts- 2402, (01) (05) Excavation (Earthmovers) Machinery Interest Subvention Scheme (Programme) 33, Subsidy (2402 A237).
The Managing Director, Maharashtra Co-operative Development Corporation, Pune shall be responsible for making available to the concerned financial institutions the amount of interest reimbursement admissible by the Government on the loans made available to the beneficiaries by the financial institutions.
The utilization certificate of the disbursed fund and the monthly progress report of the interest subvention scheme of the Maharashtra Co-operative Development Corporation, Pune should be submitted to the government by the Controller by the 10th of the next month.
