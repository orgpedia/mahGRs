Letter from the Additional Commissioner and Chief Engineer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune No. QUESTION- 3/3374, D. 22.11.2021 Preamble The Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is a reference scheme for restoration of irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state. N. No. 1 is being implemented. A total of 11 schemes under the Thane Mandal Office will be repaired and a total amount of Rs. 126.91 lakh. All the 11 schemes belong to the ZP system. The irrigation potential of these works is 91.84%. The total water storage available in the dam is 537.44 mm. The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 4 and 5 have been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Thane has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. Considering the above, the administrative approval of the proposal was under consideration as the mentioned 11 works were included in the framework of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Plan. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Samvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 11 repair schemes in the Thane Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 126.91 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
