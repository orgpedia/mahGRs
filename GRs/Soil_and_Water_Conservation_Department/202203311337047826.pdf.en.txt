Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-3, Account Head 27029219. Now, as per the revised estimates, funds to the extent of 75% have been made available for disbursement as per the reference order. Out of which, the Government has decided to fund the survey works proposed in the proposal dated 04/01/2022 as follows.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government for disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 9.30 lakh (Rupees Nine Lakh Thirty Thousand only) towards payment of consultation fee for the preparation of concept plan of the scheme for 101 to 250 Hrs. of Palghar district subject to the following terms and conditions.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions: a) Approval of survey works, commissioning orders, survey reports, maps, field reports, etc. It should be ensured that all documents are available and all works are done as per the rules, b) It should also be ensured that the trial pit/ core logging report is completed, c) The records in the measurement book should be checked to ensure that the amount demanded is reasonable and the funds are not disbursed twice for the related works, d) No amount beyond the sanctioned cost should be disbursed, e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Thane and shall be promptly disbursed to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them.
