A proposal for granting administrative approval to the Malvan G. Sindhudurg scheme has been received by the Government. The proposed Minor Irrigation Scheme has a total water storage capacity of 985.84 cusecs with a total budget of Rs. 22.12 crore. The irrigation potential of these works is 80 hectares and the matter of administrative approval for this work was under consideration of the government. Based on this, the following decision was taken.
Government Decision: Based on the 2017-18 schedule of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, the total budget of the Malvan G. Sindhudurg works is Rs. 22,11,87, 755/- (Rupees Twenty Two Crores Eleven Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty Five only) for the work and Rs. 21,17,99, 261/- (Rupees Twenty One Crores Seventeen Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand Two Hundred and Sixty One only) for the work and Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 93,88,494/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Ninety Eight Thousand Four Hundred and Ninety Four only). The details of these schemes are given in the Annexure. The existing limit for these schemes is Rs. 2,27,347/- per unit. The total cost of the project is Rs. 2,24,364.
