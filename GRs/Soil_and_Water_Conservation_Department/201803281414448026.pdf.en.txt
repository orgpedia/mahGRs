Preamble: Proposal for grant of administrative approval for the work of repair of minor irrigation (Zilla Parishad) Pazar Lake with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares at Mauje Nagadevi (Sawkheda Sim) in Yawal taluka of Jalgaon district. 1 submitted to the Government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: For the work of minor irrigation (Zilla Parishad) Pazar Lake Repair Administrative Sanction Projects with irrigation capacity from 0 to 100 hectares in Mauje Nagadevi (Sawkheda Sim) in Yawal taluka of Jalgaon district, Rs. 46,30,060/- (Rupees Forty Six Lakhs Thirty Thousand Sixty Six only) and incidental expenditure of Rs. 48,04,977/- (Rupees Forty Eight Lacs, Four Thousand Nine Hundred Seventy Seven only) aggregating to Rs. 94,350,37/- (Rupees Ninety Four Lacs, Thirty Six Thousand, Thirty Seven Only) is being accorded administrative approval by the Government.
The administrative approval being granted as above is subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The project should be completed within the approved project cost.
The beneficiary water use agency should be established as per the government decision dated July 31, 2000.
(3) On completion of the project, the project shall be transferred to the Water Utilization Agency established as above or to the concerned Gram Panchayat for maintenance repairs.
