The amendment of the water conservation treatment structures is proposed in line with the restoration of irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state. A total of 97 schemes will be repaired under the Nagpur Circle Office and the total amount for repair is Rs. 17.26 crore. With the restoration of these projects, 3298.69 ha of irrigated area and 11555.23 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for administrative approval of these works is given above. 03/11/2020 and dt. As per the letter dated 09/11/2020, the Superintending Engineer and Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nagpur have submitted to the Government. The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nagpur has scrutinized/ verified all the estimates at his level and certified the same.
In view of the above, administrative approval was under consideration for the amendment proposal of the 97 works mentioned. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: A total of 97 improvement schemes in Nagpur Regional Water Conservation Division have been budgeted at Rs. 16.4889 crore (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
