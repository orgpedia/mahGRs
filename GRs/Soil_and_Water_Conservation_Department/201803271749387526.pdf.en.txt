Preamble: A sum of Rs. 260.00 lakh has been provided in the Budget Estimates 2017-18 for the conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with an irrigation potential of 101-250 hectares. Out of which, Rs. 208.00 lakh has been made available for disbursement under the relevant circulars of the Finance Department, subject to a ceiling of 80%. Reference No. 2 The Government was considering providing funds for the installation/ erection of gates/ slabs of Kolhapuri Barrage in the above Fund Demand Proposals. Till 2016-17, the expenditure on repair works was non-plan. Therefore, the distribution of funds in this regard was" integral" for the entire state. However, from 2017-18 onwards, the expenditure on repairs has been treated as" programme expenditure." So the funds are distributed. It has to be done according to the Governor's formula. Accordingly, the following resolution was adopted.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 3.92 lakhs (Rupees Three Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand only) for the payment of project wise works like installation/ erection of gates/ slabs of the next five Kolhapuri method dams in Kolhapur district having an irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares.
