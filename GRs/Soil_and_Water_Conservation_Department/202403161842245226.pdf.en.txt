Proposal
According to the budget presented to the government, the scheme was launched in Mauje Khairat Ta. It is proposed on a local drain at Akkalkot District Solapur. The lake was sanctioned under ROHYO in the year 1972-73. At present, the lake is leaking water and the sewage has collapsed considerably.
