Introduction: The concept and the project. It was established on February 20, 1992. This scheme is based on the concept of village development through public participation.
Various schemes are being run by the government for the development of villages.
Yashwantrao Chavan Integrated Rural Development Training Centre, Mauje Hiwarebazar, under Adarshgaon Scheme. The construction of Nagar, G. Ahmednagar has been completed. A total of 120 students are being trained at the centre. Facilities are being upgraded.
Since the present centre is new and it will be possible to take other training in addition to the training in the Adarshgaon scheme, the government is considering the formation of study groups for setting up the establishment, determining the source of funding, determining the maintenance policy, determining the program of short and long-term training and determining the course of training.
Government's decision:
Since it will be possible to take other training along with the training in the Adarshgaon scheme, it will be necessary to create an establishment to run the training center, determine the source of funding, maintain the policy.
