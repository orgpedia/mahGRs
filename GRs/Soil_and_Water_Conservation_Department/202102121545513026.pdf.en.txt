Administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
a) The work must be conceptualized by the competent authority before the tender proceedings are initiated.
(b) The work shall be completed within the limit of the sub-heading of the project and as per the plan.
(c) New elements which create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without the approval of the Government.
d) Government Decision dated 31st July, 2000 and d. Beneficiary Water Utilization Societies should be set up as on 20th July, 2018.
e) The project should be transferred to the Water Utilization Agency established as above for maintenance repairs.
f) The cost of maintenance and repairs after the transfer of the project should be borne by the beneficiaries.
(g) The concerned field functionaries shall be responsible for ensuring quality and excellence in the execution of the work.
h) In order to store water in the concept, the Executive Engineer and the District Water Conservation Officer and the Superintending Engineer and the Regional Water Conservation Officer must certify that the proposed water storage has been done after the completion of the work and inform the same to the Government.
i) The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad shall submit quarterly progress report to the Government till the completion of the project works.
After the completion of the work, the certificate should be provided to the government immediately.
j) Government Decision Village Development and Water Conservation Department No. Strict compliance of the provisions as prescribed under the Act No.1 of 2011/ S.No.192/ Nidhi Jal-1 dated 02.02.2012 shall be ensured.
k) Government Circular No. Prama- 2013/785/13/ SINVYA (Works), Dy. It is mandatory to maintain records and take action as per the guidelines dated 01.01.2015.
4) The decision of the government. The expenditure is being released as per the decision taken in the meeting of the Priority Committee held on 20.08.2019.
(5) The decision of the Government shall be binding from the date of issue.
6. The said orders are being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments.
