The Proposal -
It is under consideration of the Government to constitute a Committee at the Government level to suggest appropriate action by appointing a Committee of the Additional Chief Secretary Finance, Additional Chief Secretary Planning, Secretary Soil and Water Conservation to investigate the detailed reasons for delay in completion of works in progress under the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation and to plan for immediate completion of the works and to consider the new works to be taken up and the available funds for the incomplete works in progress under the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation.
The Government's Decisions:
It has been decided to constitute a Committee at the Government level to look into the detailed reasons for the delay in completion of works on progress under Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar and to suggest appropriate measures considering the incomplete works on progress and the new works to be taken up as well as the funds and to determine the working of the Corporation and the composition of the Committee will be as follows.
