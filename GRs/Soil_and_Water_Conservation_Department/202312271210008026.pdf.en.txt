S.M./ Water Supply/ Demand for Funds/ 901/2023, dated 07/08/2023 Preamble:
The government has decided to provide the Water Resources Machinery (Earthmovers) Interest Subvention Scheme to the educated unemployed youth/ unemployed cooperative societies/ registered group farms/ farmer producer companies/ various executive cooperative societies for providing machinery for the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan/ soil and water conservation works in the state. It was launched on January 2, 2018.
Under this scheme, the interest reimbursement amount admissible by the government for excavators is credited to the savings account of the beneficiary after the beneficiary submits the demand for interest reimbursement to the government by submitting proof of payment of instalment. The Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation is responsible for providing the interest reimbursement amount admissible by the government to the beneficiaries from time to time. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds for payment of interest instalments to the concerned beneficiaries as demanded under the letter dated August 07, 2023 in the context of Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: Jalsam-2023/ P. No. 181/ JAL-7
Government Decision: Under the Jal Samriddhi Machinery (Earth Movers) Interest Subvention Scheme launched under the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan/ Government Decision dated 02.01.2018 to provide machinery for soil and water conservation works, 318 beneficiaries in the State have received machinery. The maximum amount of interest payable is Rs. 9833.33 (Rupees Nine Thousand Eight Hundred Thirty Three Paise Thirty Three only) for 318 beneficiaries, the amount of Rs. 2.10 crore (Rupees Two Crore Ten Lakh only) is being sanctioned to be disbursed to Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune for payment of interest for the last week of June, 2022 to the concerned beneficiaries.
Commissioner (Cooperation) will be the controlling officer for the scheme. " The Accountant in the office of the Commissioner of Cooperation is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw this fund from the treasury. The Controlling Officer and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall ensure that the expenditure is incurred in accordance with the procedure, terms and conditions prescribed under the provisions of this clause.
The expenditure in this regard should be shown under the head Demand No-ZH-3, Main Account Head 2402 (01) (05) Excavation (Earthmovers) Machinery Interest Subvention Scheme (Programme) 33, Subsidy (2402A237).
The Managing Director, Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune will be responsible for making available the interest reimbursement amount admissible by the government on the loan provided by the financial institutions to the beneficiaries on the savings account of the concerned beneficiary.
The utilization certificate of the distributed fund and the monthly progress report of the interest financing scheme of the Maharashtra Cooperative Development Corporation, Pune should be submitted to the government by the controlling officer by the 10th of the next month.
