The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) If funds are available from the Water Conservation Corporation, the District Planning Committee or any other source for the above projects, the funds being disbursed through this Fund shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) The funds should not be spent beyond the cost of administrative sanction and revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) If there is no need for funds for the above projects, the funds should not be disbursed for other projects under any circumstances; (d) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the funds should be immediately returned to the Government; e) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds shall be strictly followed.
(f) The works have been completed 100% and no complaints have been received and the quality of the work should be ensured by making payment and submitting a compliance report to the government.
The expenditure on this should be covered under the following heads of accounts and should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2019-20.
