GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. The Government decision issued on 31.5.2017 regarding the restructuring of the field machinery of the Department along with the establishment of the Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation had fixed the figure of 16479 posts in the field offices of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation. However, out of these posts, excluding 205 posts which were abolished vide Government decision dated 01.03.2019 and the regular posts created for the newly created offices:- Taking into account the total number of 149 posts namely 113+ 9 newly created notional posts in Driving Cadre+ 27 notional posts created in Driving Cadre, the revised figure of total 16423 posts in the field establishment of the Department has now been fixed vide Government Decision dated 01.03.2019. Out of the said 16423 posts, all the temporary posts are being extended from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 as per the decision of the Government.
