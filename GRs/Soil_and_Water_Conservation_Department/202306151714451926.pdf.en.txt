The Proposal:
The Union Ministry of Land Resources, Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India has issued the Common Guidelines 2021 for the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Watershed Development Component 2.0. Accordingly, Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Watershed Development Component 2.0 will be implemented in the state. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 27.04.2022. Under this scheme, the state has prepared 144 preliminary project reports covering an area of 5.56 lakh hectares. The total cost of the project is Rs. 1335.56 crore. The Annual Action Plan of Rs. 807.14 crore for 2022-23 has been submitted to the Government of India for approval. The Centre and the state share is 60:40. A total of Rs. 108.55 crore has been received from the Central Government for this scheme, out of which Rs. 180.86 crore has been disbursed as State share amounting to Rs. 72.31 crore.
There is a large extent of barren land in the state. The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana Watershed Development Component 2.0 is being implemented by the Department of Soil and Water Conservation with an objective to enhance the economic status of the rural population by utilising this fallow land and increasing their productivity, as well as enhancing the productivity of the land by undertaking appropriate soil and conservation works using this scheme. The Central Government has set a time limit for the completion of work in various departments in this regard.
Therefore, considering the short period of balance in the current financial year, the Public Works Department, Government has decided to go for e-tendering process to spend the available funds. The consolidated revised instructions have been issued under 27.09.2018 and as per the provisions therein, action is taken while carrying out works under Soil and Water Conservation Department. In the Government Resolution No. The time period for publication of e-tender has been fixed in 1. Therefore, the government was considering to give exemption to publish short-term tender notices under the e-tendering system for the works to be done under the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana Watershed Development Component 2.0.
