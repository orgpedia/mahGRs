Memorandum: Subject: Distribution of Account Grants for mandatory expenditure of Department of Soil and Water Conservation (itself) and Regional Establishments for the financial year 2019-20 for four months from April 2019 to July 2019.
Reference:- Government Circular Finance Division No: Finance- 2019/ PR No.44/ Finance- 3 dated 01.04.2019.
According to the Finance Department. In pursuance of the Circular dated 1/4/2018, the Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation (Payment of Accounts) Act, 2019 to meet the expenditure for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019 and the said Bill has now been enacted and published as Maharashtra Act No. 2 in the Official Gazette of the Government of Maharashtra, Extraordinary Part IV dated 08.03.2019. Accordingly, it has been approved to disburse the Account Grants for meeting the expenditure for four months from April 2019 to July 2019 of the financial year 2019-20.
2 As per the directions given in this circular, the authority conferred on the Administrative Department has sanctioned the expenditure of Rs. The approval to disburse and spend the funds amounting to Rs. 129,54,27000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Nineteen Crores, Fifty Four Lakhs, Twenty Seven Thousand only) is being accorded by this Government Memorandum subject to the following terms and conditions and the same is being distributed on the Budget Computer Distribution System.
The provision as distributed by these terms and conditions 3 is placed under the control authorities indicated at column number 6 of the prospectus. This provision should be immediately distributed to the drawing and disbursing officers under the authority.
4 While making use of the above funds, care should be taken to strictly comply with the terms and conditions of the relevant circular as well as the following terms and conditions.
1) Zila Parishads shall maintain separate accounts for subsidized expenditure, 2) Zila Parishads shall spend the grants for the purpose for which they are sanctioned, 3) The prevailing terms and conditions regarding financial powers shall be strictly adhered to, 4) The Accountant General's Office shall have the right to inspect the books and accounts of the Zila Parishads for audit. The concerned district councils will be responsible for providing assistance and facilities for this.

The Zila Parishads are required to examine the expenditure statements from their Finance Department and submit the report to the Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Board Office by 10th of every month, the Board Offices to submit the consolidated information report of the expenditure of the Zila Parishads in their division to the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad by 15th of every month, and the Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad, to submit the quarterly report of the expenditure to the Government by 20th of the next month after the end of every quarter. The District Councils should send the expenditure statements on time and the same should be strictly monitored by the concerned Regional Water Conservation Officer.
The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad and the Controlling Officer shall take all necessary measures to discharge the responsibilities of cost coordination and coordination of other financial matters.
The said Memorandum is being issued as per the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Circular dated 01/04/2019.
