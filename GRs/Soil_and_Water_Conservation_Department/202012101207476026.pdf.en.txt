Preamble: A sum of Rs. 100.00 crore has been budgeted in 2020-21 for the non-tribal areas for the development of progressive/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with an irrigation potential of 101-250 hectares, with a ceiling of 50% of the funds available for disbursement.
It has been decided to provide funds for the work recommended as per the proposal in the context of the Marathwada region.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 112.04 lakh (Rs.1 crore, twelve lakh four thousand only) for storage tanks Yeldari Tanda and Ramachi Wadi in Loha Taluka of Nanded district at a cost of Rs. 12.15 lakh and Rs. 99.79 lakh respectively subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) the amount being disbursed by the Water Conservation Corporation or any other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above project, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) no amount shall be spent in excess of the cost of administrative sanction as well as revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) if funds are not required for the works of the above project, the amount shall not be disbursed for any other project under any circumstances; and (d) in case of non-availability of funds for any reason, the amount shall be returned to the Government forthwith.
