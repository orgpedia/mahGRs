Preamble: Preparation of Geo Portal of the Department for collating village-wise data of works done in the catchment areas under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation along with the latitude and longitude
It is proposed. In this, the work done before and under the campaign in the villages, which are mainly involved in the Jalyukt Shivar campaign, will be marked with the help of satellite images. Accordingly, administrative approval has been accorded under the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan (JYM) for marking of soil and water conservation works in 22,598 identified villages during four years from 2015-16 to 2018-19 by external agencies using satellite-based maps.

Under the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan, an e-tendering system has been implemented for selection of agencies and fixing of rates by adopting e-tendering process so that the work of marking the soil and water conservation treatment completed in the villages before and during the campaign can be done by an external agency using satellite based maps. For this purpose, vide letter dated 19.01.2019, an advertisement was published in newspapers in all the districts at the state level through e-tender system for selection of external agencies by the Assistant Director (Advertisement). The matter of disbursement of funds for payment of the advertisement dues was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Under the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan, the Director, Soil Conservation and Watershed Management, Pune, as demanded under the letter dated 1st March, 2019, has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 3.00 lakh (three lakhs) is being approved for disbursement.
2. To draw this fund from the treasury, the Assistant Director (Accounts-1), Commissionerate of Agriculture, Pune is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Commissioner (Agriculture), State of Maharashtra, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer.
3. The responsibility of making this fund available to the district-wise newspapers as per the prevailing policy of the government on advertising and according to the government approved rate shall be borne by the Director, Mridsandhan and
Watershed Management, Pune.
The expenditure in this regard should be shown under the head Demand No.ZH-3, Main Account Head-2402,102, Soil Conservation (01) (01) Watershed Development Programme, Publicity and Reward Scheme (Programme) (2402 A193).
