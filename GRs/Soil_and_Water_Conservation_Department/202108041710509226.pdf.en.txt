Government circular:
Reference No. 8 General Administration Department, Government Notification dated 21.06.2021 issued vide Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021, the provisional seniority of the cadre of Regional Water Conservation Officer in Soil and Water Conservation Department as on 01.01.2020 is being published in the form of" Form-A" attached to this circular.
2. The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list in Form-A annexed to this circular shall be the date of seniority mentioned in the above Reference No. The Government Notification No. 281/12, General Administration Department No. SRV-2016/ PR No. As per the provision of 21.06.2021, the Regional Water Conservation Officer will be considered for determining seniority in the cadre.
3. The Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad shall bring the said circular to the immediate notice of the Regional Water Conservation Officer in Form A under him and shall preserve a signed copy of the communication in his own records and make a copy of the same available to the Government.

(4) If any errors/ omissions are found in the details of Regional Water Conservation Authorities included in the said Seniority List, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., they should bring their representations/ objections/ suggestions to the Government within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular in writing, by registered post/ speed post/ hand delivery, along with necessary proper proof. If the errors/ errors are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the stipulated time, the seniority of the Regional Water Conservation Officer working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation will be finalized as on 01.01.2020.
