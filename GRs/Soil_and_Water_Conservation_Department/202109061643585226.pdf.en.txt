Date of release: September 6, 2021
Read:- Government Decision, Soil and Water Conservation Department No. Establishment-2021/ No. 97/ Water-2, Dy. 22.07.2021.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the government, it was approved to revive/ re-establish the headquarters of the sub-divisional offices and the taluk jurisdiction of Beed district. In this Government decision, it is being approved to restore the two Sub-Divisional Offices of Soil and Water Conservation Survey Sub-Division, Patoda and Soil and Water Conservation Sub-Division, Patoda by making partial changes in the place of headquarters and taluk jurisdiction in view of administrative convenience as mentioned above.
