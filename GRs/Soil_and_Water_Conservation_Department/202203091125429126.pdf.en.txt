In order to restore the irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented under the relevant Schedule No. 1. Pazhar lake.
Gadhinglaj, Pazar Lake, Kamlehatti. Gadhinglaj, Pajar Lake Mahagav Ramtirtha Ta. Gadhinglaj, Pazar Lake Majgaon Tt. Radhanagari G. Kolhapur has completed 4 schemes through Zilla Parishad Kolhapur. All plans are in Priority 3. The water is being drained out of the lake due to heavy rains. A large number of trees have also been planted on the site.
The plans need to be revised in view of dam safety. Therefore, the matter of giving administrative approval to these repair works was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
