In order to restore the irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented under the relevant Schedule No. 1. Pazhar Lake
Chandwad, G. Nashik is in Phase 1 of the scheme. The construction of the scheme was done in 1967-68 and according to the original budget, the water storage of the Pazar lake was 270.17 cusecs and 57 cusecs. There is irrigation potential. Sewers and flank walls of Pazar lake have been constructed and signs of waterlogging have been seen at places. Trees have also been planted on the left and right sides of the dam. Some parts of the dam are completely damaged, so the proposed project needs to be repaired in order to store water. Therefore, the matter of giving administrative approval to these repair works was under the consideration of the government.
