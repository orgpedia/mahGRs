The Proposal:
K.P. Bandhara Jamrun Ta. Sengaon G. Hingoli scheme is proposed on a local drain near Jamrun village. The total storage capacity of the scheme is 135.40 lakh cubic feet. It is planned to provide irrigation benefits to the area.
