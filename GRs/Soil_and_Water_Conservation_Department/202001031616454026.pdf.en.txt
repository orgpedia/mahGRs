Preamble: A sum of Rs. 450.00 lakh has been provided for the enumeration programme of Centrally Sponsored Minor Irrigation Schemes during the current financial year. For this, the budgeted funds are distributed within the limits of the funds approved by the central government programmatically and actually deposited in the consolidated fund of the state government. Accordingly, as per the letter of the Central Government at Reference No. 2, administrative approval has been granted for the continuation of the Statistics Cell made operational as part of the" Rationalization of Minor Irrigation Statistics (RMIS)" under the Census Scheme.
The Decision:
A sum of Rs. 15,04,212/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs Four Thousand Two Hundred and Twelve only) is being handed over to the Commissioner (Soil and Water Conservation), Aurangabad for further utilization to meet the expenditure for the Statistics Cell, which has been made operational under the Enumeration Programme for the Minor Irrigation Schemes in the State.
