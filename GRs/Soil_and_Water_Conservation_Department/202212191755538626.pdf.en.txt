Preamble: The Commissionerate of Soil and Water Conservation at Aurangabad under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation has been established vide Government Resolution dated 31.05.2017 and the contours of the Department have been decided vide the said Government Resolution by reorganizing the field machinery. With a view to remove the administrative difficulties arising out of the merger/ closure of some of the field offices in the implementation of the scheme of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation. The offices have been revived/ restored/ newly created as per the Government decision dated 01.03.2019. In order to double the income of farmers, to provide them with essential civic amenities and necessary infrastructure in rural areas, the government is considering special recruitment for posts related to important services in various departments. The State Government has approved special recruitment to the post of Water Conservation Officer, (Architecture) Group-" B" (Non-Gazetted) as per the existing pattern of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation. Accordingly, in order to maintain uniformity, impartiality and uniformity in the work of filling up the posts of Water Conservation Officer, (Architecture) Group-" B" (Non-Gazetted) category in the nomination quota on the field establishment in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, and to provide equal opportunities to the candidates, the General Administration Department has issued a notification regarding the procedure of recruitment to the post vide No. 5 It was under the consideration of the Government to constitute a State Level Selection Committee after taking into consideration the consolidated orders issued under the Government decision.
