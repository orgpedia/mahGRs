Introduction The Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is a reference scheme for restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state. After the restoration of these projects, 3278 hectares of irrigated area and 16051.61 cu.m. of water storage will be restored.
