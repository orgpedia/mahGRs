Preamble: Demand No. 1 for Conservation and Rehabilitation of Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Projects with Irrigation Capacity of 0 to 100 Hectares. A sum of Rs. 250.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2023-24 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046 and from the funds available under the present scheme, the following has been decided on the proposal for seeking funds as on 30/03/2023.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 38.21 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Eight Lakhs Twenty One Thousand only) within the limit of administrative sanction granted for payment of maintenance and repair works of Pazar Talav Bajrangwadi in Karjat Taluka of Ahmednagar District.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) In case the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without being disbursed for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
