Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works on progress/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with an irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 100.00 crore was budgeted in 2021-22 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A028. Out of the funds provided, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal for seeking funds with reference date 18/01/2022.
Decision: The Government hereby grant approval for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 13.00 Lakhs (Rupees Thirteen Lakhs only) for the work done on the project at G. Gondia subject to the following terms and conditions.
(a) If funds have been made available from the Water Conservation Corporation or other sources for the above project works, the amounts being disbursed through this scheme shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) The amount should not be spent beyond the cost of administrative sanction and revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) If funds are not required for the above project works, the amount should not be disbursed for other projects under any circumstances; and (d) If funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
e) Strictly follow the guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds.
