Memorandum: In the financial year 2018-19, the accounts for the Institute of Water and Land Management (WALMI), Aurangabad, have already been provided with 70% of the first nine months expenditure out of the budgeted funds under the Subsidiary Grants for WALMI (Non-salaried) (2702 9166) and Subsidiary Grants for WALMI (Non-salaried) (2702 9175) under Benefit Area Development Authority. 2 have been distributed.
Now the Department of Finance. As per the Circular dated 25/1/2019, for the year 2018-19, 80% of the budgeted funds have been provided for disbursement for expenditure up to the end of March in respect of programme expenditure in the revenue account. Accordingly, the Institute of Water and Land Management (WALMI), Aurangabad has been allotted the remaining 10% i.e. Rs. 63,60,000/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakhs Sixty Thousand only) is being disbursed through this head and head subsidy.
