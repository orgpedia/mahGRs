Preamble: Demand No. 1 for Conservation and Rehabilitation of Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Projects with Irrigation Capacity of 0 to 100 Hectares. A sum of Rs. 250.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2023-24 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A046. Out of which, as per the relevant circular of the Finance Department, the proposal for seeking funds from the funds available under the current scheme as on 01/07/2023 has been decided as follows.
Decision: The Government hereby grants approval for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 373.86 lakhs (Rupees Three Crores Seventy Three Lakhs Eighty Six Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of the British Village Lake Akhtwara in Akola Taluka of Akola District.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work, b) The amount should not be paid in excess of the sanctioned cost, c) In case the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government without being disbursed for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
