According to the budget submitted to the government, cement storage dams with gates in Buldhana district's Buldhana taluka are proposed on local drains. The estimated cost of the work is Rs.
1,41,60, 670/- and these works will create 80.6 mm of water storage and benefit 25 hectares of irrigation area. Following this, the government took decision on:
Government Decision: The total cost of cement storage dam in Buldhana district is Rs. Administrative Sanction is being granted to the proposal for a cost of Rs. 1,41,60, 670/- (Rupees One Crore Forty One Lakh Sixty Six Thousand Six Hundred and Seventy only) subject to the following terms and conditions. The Scheme is being presented in Annexure A with sub-heading provisions as per the original Budget.
1) The contractor should be made obligatory by putting conditions in the tender for setting up a water use agency. 10% of the tender amount should be deducted from the contractor's payment until the WUA is established. The amount should be disbursed after the establishment of the institution. If an institution in exceptional situation cannot be established, the 10% of the deducted funds should be disbursed after submitting a proposal to the government with detailed work plan and approval of the government. The government has decided to set up the agency. Remuneration to be paid as per provisions of 11-02-2022.
2) The Superintending Engineer/ Regional Water Conservation Officer for the project is required to visit the work site and identify and certify the site and alignment.
