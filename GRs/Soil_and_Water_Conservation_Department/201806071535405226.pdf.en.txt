Date: 7th of June 2018
Read: 1) Government Decision Rural Development and Water Conservation Department No. Establishment 2016/ PR No.88 (Part-9)/ Jal-2, dated 31st May, 2017. Establishment 2016/ PR No.88 (Part-9)/ Water-2, dated 31st May, 2017.
Preamble: Creation of a new Administrative Division of Soil and Water Conservation. The above decision was taken by the government. The officers/ employees of the Water Resources Department and the Agriculture Department who want to become permanent members of the new administrative department by determining the sanctioned posts of Soil and Water Conservation Department. The options were invited before 15/07/2017. Further, all the officers and staff of the Water Resources Department who have been working in the Water Conservation Department on temporary deputation to the Soil and Water Conservation Department since 1992 till the last date of submission of options, have been given the option to either continue in the Soil and Water Conservation Department or go back to their parent Water Resources Department. Accordingly, considering all the objections received, the matter of final publication of the said list and posting of the concerned officers/ employees by inclusion was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, since the establishment of the Department (1992), the options set out for submission of the The officers/ staff of Water Resources Department were on temporary deputation as on 15/07/2017. After the formation of the Soil and Water Conservation Department, the options have been obtained from all the officers/ employees working under this temporary deputation and the final list of those options has been shown in the statement' A' and statement' B' of this government decision. Whereas, the list of officers and employees who have opted out of permanent adjustment in Soil and Water Conservation Department is shown in the return' B'.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: Establishment 2018/ PR No.91/ JAL-2 dated 07.06.2018
Therefore, all the concerned officers/ employees of FORM A are being permanently transferred to the establishment of Soil and Water Conservation Department at the place where they are presently working. Also, all the officers and employees in the return A are being declared as officers/ employees of the Soil and Water Conservation Department. The names and designations of all these officers and employees are mentioned in the above reference No. The above dates shall be as per the Government decision dated 31st May, 2017.
The service performance lists of Group' A' and' B' cadre on the establishment of this department will be published separately from the government level after examining the service performance lists of all the concerned officers/ employees. The seniority lists of Group' C' and' D' cadres will be kept at the level of Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad as the appointing and disciplinary authority. The service books and service records of all the officers and employees of the department should be preserved in the office in which the concerned officers and employees are working. Also, in the service book of the officers and employees who have been transferred to this department permanently, the relevant record should be taken if the services of the concerned are transferred to the Soil and Water Conservation Department permanently.
The officers/ employees who are presently working on the establishment of Soil and Water Conservation Department have been given the option to go to their parent Water Resources Department. The list of all such officers/ employees is shown in the statement' B' accompanying this Government Decision and all the officers/ employees will be provided with the facility of repatriation of their services to the parent Water Resources Department in a phased manner over the next 4 years from 31st May, 2017. Until then, they will continue to work in this department and will not be relieved from the establishment of this department without the permission of this department.
The Government Decisions issued pursuant to the approval given by the Cabinet are being issued by the Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation as per the powers vested in it vide Para 8 dated 31/05/2017 and with the approval of the competent authority.
