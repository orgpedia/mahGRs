Preamble: A sum of Rs. 1563.00 crore has been provided in the Budget Estimates for the financial year 2021-22 as share contribution for the construction of projects undertaken for Maharashtra Jal Sanrakshan Corporation. Out of which 60% of the funds are available for disbursement and out of which 30% of the funds up to Rs. 469.00 crore has been disbursed as per the Government decision dated 11/11/2021.
Meanwhile, under the Government Order dated 14/10/2021 in the context of the Finance Department, funds to the extent of 50% of the funds in the budget have been made available to the administrative departments. It was under consideration of the Government to spend the said fund for meeting the pending liabilities of the Corporation. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Decision: The Government hereby grants approval for disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 312.00 crore (Rupees Three Hundred and Twelve Crores only) as equity contribution for the projects in progress of Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation.
The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad is hereby authorized to draw and disburse the aforesaid funds from the treasury and distribute the same in the name of Managing Director, Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad.
The expenditure on this should be covered under the following heads and should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2021-22.
