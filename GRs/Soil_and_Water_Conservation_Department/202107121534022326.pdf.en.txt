The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Ordinance, 2015 was promulgated on 28/04/2015. As per the provision mentioned in Section 3 of this Ordinance, 8 services have been provided by the Department of Soil and Water Conservation under the Government Decision dated- 30/10/2018 of this Department. The Chief Commissioner (Maharashtra Public Service Commission), Mumbai has given instructions to reflect the services of the department on the portal. In view of this, it was under consideration that the services provided by the Department of Soil and Water Conservation would be reflected on the portal by our government through MahaIT.
Decision: 1) The following 8 services are required to be reflected on your government portal under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act 2015.
a) Grant of water rights due to Water Utilization Agency b) Grant of Water Lease Arrears to Water Utilization Agency c) Grant of Non Irrigation Water Lease Arrears d) Grant of Water Lease Grievance Redressal e) Grant of Benefit Area e) Grant of Household Water Utilization Permits to Gram Panchayats, Zila Parishads, Nagar Parishads, Nagar Panchayats, Cantonment Boards.
(a) Grant of domestic/ industrial water use licences to Municipal Corporations, Private Developers, Special Urban Development Projects.
a) Licensing the use of water for industrial purposes.
In this regard, the Government vide letter dated 03/12/2020 has informed to reflect the above 8 services of this Department on the Aapka Sarkar Portal. The proposal includes the following costs:
