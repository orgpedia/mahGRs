The Proposal:
The government has taken a decision to complete the incomplete works at the earliest. It has been decided to implement the Dhadak programme to complete 353 watersheds in 353 talukas of the state by November 30, 2007.
Under this scheme, 7934 watersheds have been completed since 2007-08.
As requested by the Director, Soil Conservation and Watershed Management, Pune vide letter dated 18.10.2022, the matter of administrative and financial sanction for expeditious completion of incomplete watershed projects under Integrated Watershed Development Programme for the financial year 2022-23 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Integrated Watershed Development Programme is being approved for implementation in the financial year 2022-23. Under this programme, As per the terms and conditions of the Government Resolution dated 30th November, 2007, the incomplete watersheds in the State are being expeditiously completed.
