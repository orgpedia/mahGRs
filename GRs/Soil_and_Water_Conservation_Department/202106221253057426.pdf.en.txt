The amendment of the water conservation treatment structures is proposed in line with the restoration of irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the schemes that require maintenance and repair from the completed water conservation schemes in the state. A total of 51 schemes under the Nagpur Circle Office will be repaired and the total amount for repair is Rs. 10.4763 crore. After the restoration of these projects, 2326 hectares of irrigated area and 7987.04 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for administrative approval of these works is given above. As per the letter dated 12.02.2020, Additional Commissioner Water Conservation and Chief Engineer, Soil and Water Conservation Regional Area, Nagpur has submitted to the Government. The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nagpur has scrutinized/ verified all the estimates at his level and certified the same. Considering the above, administrative approval was under consideration for the amendment proposal of the above mentioned 51 works. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: The total budget of 51 improvement schemes in Nagpur Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 10.4763 crore (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
