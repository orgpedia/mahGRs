Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-3, head 2702 9219 for the year 2020-21. Of this, 10% of the funds have been made available for distribution. Out of which, the Government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal from the fund for Vidarbha Division as follows.
The Decision:
Small-scale planning, Government approval is hereby accorded for disbursement of an amount of Rs. 9.05 lakh (Rupees Nine Lakh Five Thousand only) for the conceptualization of the project at Telhara, G. Akola.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) In order to ensure that no more than the reasonable amount for the survey is disbursed, the District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall again verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable before disbursing the actual funds; (b) The concerned Sub-Divisional Office shall verify that the funds are not being disbursed twice for the above survey work.
(c) If the funds are not required for any reason, the amount disbursed should not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances.
(d) No amount shall be paid in excess of the approved price.
