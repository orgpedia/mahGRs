Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 800.00 lakhs has been budgeted for the year 2018-19 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9201. Out of which, Rs. 560.00 lakh has been made available for disbursement through the relevant circulars, subject to a ceiling of 70%. Distribution of funds The legislative development has to be done as per the board-wise formula prescribed by the Governor. Among them, the government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal submitted by the zonal agency for the fund for Marathwada division as follows.
The Decision:
The Government hereby approve the release of Rs. 105.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Five Lakh only) as per the attached Form-A for the survey work of 37 minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of irrigation capacity 101 to 250 hectares in Aurangabad and Jalna districts of Marathwada region.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the Executive Engineer concerned shall, before the actual disbursement of funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable.
b) The concerned sub-divisional office should verify that the above work of the survey is not being distributed twice.
