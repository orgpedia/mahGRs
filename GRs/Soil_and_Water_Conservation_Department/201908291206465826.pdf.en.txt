Preamble: Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Repair, Renovation and Restoration of Water Bodies through Local Involvement (0 to 100H)," a detailed survey of works and DPR was prepared. The proposal has been sent to the central government for approval. In this regard, in a meeting held on 16th March, 2016 under the chairmanship of the Minister of State (Water Conservation), it was decided to allocate this fund from the State Plan. Accordingly, the Regional Mechanism was informed about the release of funds from the available funds for 2015-16. But due to some technical issues, the funds could not be disbursed. Hence, the following decision has been taken to clear the pending dues:
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approve the release of an amount of Rs. 52.65 lakh (Rupees Fifty Two Lakhs Fifty Five Thousand only) for the survey works under the Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) scheme of water bodies with local participation subject to the following conditions and conditions.
a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the concerned District Water Conservation Officer shall again verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable before disbursing the actual funds.
b) The concerned sub-divisional office should verify that the above work of the survey is not being distributed twice.
