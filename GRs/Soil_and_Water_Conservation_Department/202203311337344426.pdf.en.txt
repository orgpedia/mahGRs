Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 150.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-3, Account Head 27029201. Now, as per the revised estimates, a limit of 75% of the funds has been made available under the scheme. Out of which, it has been decided to fund the survey works in the proposals dated 22/03/2022 as follows.
The Decision:
Nagpur District Reference No. 2 Approval of the Government is hereby accorded for disbursement of a total amount of Rs. 547.36 lakhs (Rupees Five Crores Forty Seven Lakhs Thirty Six Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues of eligible and completed survey works in the proposals.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions: a) Approval of survey works, commissioning orders, survey reports, maps, field reports, etc.
It should be ensured that the documents are available and all the work is done as per the rules, b) It should also be ensured that the necessary aspects of the investigation (Trial Pit/ Core Logging Report) are fulfilled, c) The records of the measurement book should be checked to ensure that the amount demanded is reasonable and the funds are not distributed twice for the related works, d) The amount beyond the sanctioned price should not be distributed, e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the unutilized amount should be immediately returned to the government.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nagpur and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under him.
