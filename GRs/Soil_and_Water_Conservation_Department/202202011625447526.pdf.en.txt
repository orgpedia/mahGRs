Preamble: Demand No. 101 for Conservation and Rehabilitation of Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Projects with Irrigation Capacity of 250 Hectares. A sum of Rs. 100.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2021-22 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A055. Out of the funds provided, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal of 03/06/2021.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a sum of Rs. 33.97 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Three Lakhs Ninety Seven Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of 03 projects of L.P. Talav Nanivade, Janvali and Kokisare in Vaibhavwadi and Kankavali taluks of Sindhudurg district subject to the following conditions and conditions.
a) The funds should be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring completion of the repair work, b) The amount should not be paid more than the sanctioned cost, c) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for any other purpose, d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds should be strictly followed.
e) The works have been completed 100% and complaints have not been received, and the quality of the work should be ensured by making payment and submitting a compliance report to the government.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Thane and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under him.
