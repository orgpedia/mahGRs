Appointment as" Enumeration Commissioner" for the 6th Enumeration Programme
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Soil and Water Conservation
Government Resolution No.Pragna-2018/ P.No.24/ Jal-4
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
The date: May 23, 2018.
Read: Ministry of Water Resources, New Delhi's letter No. V- 57/1/2017-MI (Stat)/ 1066-1184 dated 23rd November, 2017.
Preamble: The Central Government has approved the 6th Census of India programme for enumeration of all minor irrigation projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 2000 hectares for the period 2017-18 to 2019-20. Accordingly, the Government has decided to appoint an" Enumeration Commissioner" for the effective implementation of this national task in the State as follows.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded for appointment of Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation Commissionerate, Aurangabad as" Enumeration Commissioner" of the Centrally Sponsored 6th Enumeration Programme.
Commissioner (Soil and Water Conservation) Aurangabad should take charge of the above post immediately and start the implementation of the enumeration programme.
