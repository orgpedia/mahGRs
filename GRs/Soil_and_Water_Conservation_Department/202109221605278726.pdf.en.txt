(a) the amount being disbursed by the Water Conservation Corporation or any other source, if funds have been made available for the above activities, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) any amount in excess of the cost of administrative sanction and revised administrative sanction approved by the Government shall not be spent; (c) if funds are not required for the above activities, the amount shall not be disbursed for any other project under any circumstances; and (d) if funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount shall be immediately returned to the Government.
