Government circular:
The new administrative division of Soil and Water Conservation has been created by the Government Decision of the Department of Rural Development and Water Conservation dated 31st May, 2017. The pattern of sanctioned posts of the newly created Administrative Department of Soil and Water Conservation was finalised and options were sought before 15.07.2017 from the officers/ employees of the Water Resources Department who wanted to be transferred permanently to this new Administrative Department. The officers/ employees of the Water Resources Department who have opted for permanent inclusion in the Soil and Water Conservation Department have been included under the Soil and Water Conservation Department by the Government Decisions dated 07.06.2018 and 22.06.2018.
While determining the contours of the sanctioned posts under the Soil and Water Conservation Department, the offices and designations at the regional/ zonal level have been restructured. Accordingly, the level of Reporting and Review Officers will be as mentioned in the following statement to ensure consistency in the assessment of work done by Group-A (Gazetted) and Group-B (Gazetted/ Gazetted) cadre officers at the regional/ zonal level under this department on the basis of the Confidential Report (Performance Appraisal Report).
