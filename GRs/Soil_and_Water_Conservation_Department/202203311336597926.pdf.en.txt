The decision was taken at a meeting held at Sinnar taluka in Nashik district, where a 151-hectare irrigation potential was created.
Approval of the Government is hereby accorded for disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 75.08 lakhs (Rupees Seventy Five Lakhs Eight Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues for construction of Bandhara Nimgaon (Deopur) Project subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) The amount being disbursed by the Water Conservation Corporation or any other source, if funds have been made available for the above project works, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) The amount should not be spent beyond the limit of administrative sanction as per rules and the revised administrative sanction cost as approved by the Government; (c) If funds are not required for the above project works, the amount should not be disbursed for other projects under any circumstances; (d) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the Government; (e) The guidelines in the Reference Circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds shall be strictly followed.
f) Ensure quality of work, make payments and submit compliance report to the government.
