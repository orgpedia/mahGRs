The amendment of the water conservation treatment structures is proposed in line with the restoration of irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the schemes that require maintenance and repair from the completed water conservation schemes in the state. A total of 217 schemes under the Amravati Circle Office will be repaired and the total amount for repair is Rs. 57.36 crore. After the repair of these projects, 11484 hectares of irrigated area and 65707.83 cu.m. of water storage will be restored. The proposal for administrative approval of these works is given above. 06/11/2020 and D. As per the letter dated 08/10/2020, the Superintending Engineer and Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Amravati has submitted to the Government. The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Amravati has scrutinized/ verified all the budgets at its level. Considering the above, administrative approval was under consideration for the amendment proposal of 217 works mentioned. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
