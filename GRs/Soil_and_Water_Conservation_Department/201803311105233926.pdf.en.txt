Preamble: A sum of Rs. 17.00 crore has been budgeted in 2017-18 for the scheme of conservation and restoration of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares, out of which Rs. 13.60 crore is available for disbursement as per the relevant circulars in the 80% limit. The government was considering to distribute the funds in the form of statutory development board. In this regard, the government has decided to disburse the funds for the repair work recommended under the reference letter as follows.
Government Decision: , Ta. The Government hereby grant approval to disburse an amount of Rs. 39.21 lakhs (Rupees Forty Nine Lakhs Twenty One Thousand only) for the repair of Sinnar, G. Nashik Project subject to the following conditions.
a) Funds should be disbursed based on the completion of repair work and payment of work done.
(b) No amount shall be paid in excess of the approved tender price, (c) In case the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount shall be immediately returned to the Government without disbursement for any other purpose, and (d) The guidelines of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds, dated April 18, 2017 and February 6, 2018, shall be strictly followed.
The concerned Superintending Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation) Board shall draw the above funds and disburse the same to the concerned authorities expeditiously.
The above expenditure shall be covered under the following heads and shall be met out of the approved provision for the financial year 2017-18.
