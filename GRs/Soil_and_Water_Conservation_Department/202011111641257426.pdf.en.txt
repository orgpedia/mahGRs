Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-3, head 2702 9219 for the year 2020-21. Of this, 10% of the funds have been made available for distribution. Out of which, the Government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal from the fund for Vidarbha Division as follows.
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse an amount of Rs. 14.29 lakhs (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Thirty-one Thousand only) towards payment of 14 survey works in Wardha District in the reference proposal subject to the following conditions and conditions.
a) Ensure that the documents of approval of survey works, commissioning orders, survey reports, maps, field reports, etc. are available and all the works are done as per the rules; b) Ensure that the trial pit/ core logging report is fulfilled; c) Check the records in the measurement book and ensure that the amount demanded is reasonable and that the funds are not distributed twice for the related works; d) Amounts in excess of the sanctioned cost should not be disbursed; e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the unutilized amount should be immediately returned to the Government.
