Government's decision:
As per the decision taken by the Hon'ble Cabinet in its meeting held on 14/10/2020, the Government has decided to conduct an open inquiry into the works done under the Jalyukt Shivar Yojana. In order to find out which works are required to be selected for open enquiry only because the number of works is more, the following committee has been constituted under the Government decision dated 01/12/2020.
Member (iii) Shri Sanjay Belsare, Chief Engineer Water Resources Department Member (iv) Working Director Soil Conservation and Watershed Management, Pune Member
(2) On the recommendation of the said Committee, the Acting Director, Soil Conservation and Watershed Management, Pune is being appointed as Member Secretary in the said Committee.
