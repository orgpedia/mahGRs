The programme of construction of cement embankments in taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
A total of Rs. 2.91 lakh (Rupees Two Lakh Ninety One Thousand) was under consideration.

The Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer and the Executive Engineer of the concerned district as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending the funds being made available for the construction of cement. The Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune shall take care of the expenditure on the targets allocated and the Chief Engineer Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune is being authorized to disburse the funds to the concerned.
The funds being distributed in accordance with paragraph 2 above are subject to the following conditions:
4.1 If the above Cement Bandha is funded by any other Stotra, the funds being disbursed by the said Government Resolution shall not be withdrawn or disbursed.
4.2 No amount shall be spent in excess of the administrative sanction or the price agreed to by the Government.
4.3 If the funds provided remain unutilized for any reason, the spent funds should not be used for any other purpose.
4.4 Cement Bonds- If the funds being made available to it remain unutilized, it should be immediately returned to the government.
4.5 The full utilization certificate and the certificate of completion of the work given so far for this work should be submitted to the government immediately.
