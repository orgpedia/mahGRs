Out of which, as per the relevant circular of the Finance Department, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal for seeking funds from the funds available under the current scheme as on 04/07/2023.
Decision: Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar District. 1, Tanda No. 2, No. Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse a total amount of Rs. 61.98 Lakhs (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Ninety Eight Thousand only) towards payment of maintenance and repair works of 2nd and 04th projects.
The aforesaid funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: a) The funds are to be disbursed on payment basis, ensuring quality of repair work.
(b) No payment shall be made in excess of the sanctioned amount, (c) In case the fund remains unutilized for any reason, the amount shall be immediately returned to the Government without being distributed among themselves for any other purpose, and (d) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds shall be strictly followed.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
