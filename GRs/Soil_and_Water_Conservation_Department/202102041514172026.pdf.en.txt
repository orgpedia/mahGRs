The revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions:
4.1 The work shall be completed within the limit of the sub-heading approved cost of the project and as per the plan.
4.2. New elements that create financial liabilities should not be included in the project without government approval.
4.3. The Water Utilization Agency at the project should be set up by the contractor. 10% of the amount should be deducted from the payment of the contractor till the establishment of the water utility and the amount should be disbursed to the contractor after the establishment of the water utility.
If an institution in exceptional situation cannot be established, the reduced 10% fund should be disbursed after the approval of the government by submitting a proposal to the government with detailed reasons. If the contractor establishes an institution, the contractor should be paid honorarium as per the guidelines of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation dated 20-07-2018. The District Water Conservation Officer concerned will be responsible for setting up the Water Utilization Agency. 4.4. The cost of maintenance and repair after the transfer of the project should be borne by the beneficiary.
