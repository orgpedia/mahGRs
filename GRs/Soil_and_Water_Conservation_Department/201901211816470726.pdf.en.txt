the Government Order
Officers of the cadre of Water Conservation Officer who have been given the option of this Department have been declared as Officers of this Department vide order dated 22.6.2018 and 7.6.2018 and as per order dated 22.6.2018, the officers/ staff of Water Resources Department have been given detailed postings in Soil and Water Conservation Department. However, since the post of Water Conservation Officer is not vacant, as per the provisions of the Transfer Act, 2005, on administrative grounds, with the approval of the competent authority, the posting of Mr. Uday Singh Nanusingh Thakur, Water Conservation Officer vide order dated 22.6.2018 is being changed to the post of Water Conservation Officer, Office of Sub Divisional Water Conservation Officer, Survey Sub Division, Latur.
