Preamble: Pazhar Lake, Dhangarwadi, Ta. The project at Rajapur, Ratnagiri has been given original administrative approval and prior funding from the Zilla Parishad. This fund has been provided under the district level account head 2702 5142. Thereafter, the revised administrative approval for the enhanced expenditure on this account has been accorded by the Government under the same head. However, funds were not available from the district level scheme for this expenditure and there was a constant demand from the farmers who had lost their land and houses in the submerged areas of the project to provide funds before the rainy season. Meanwhile dated 19/03/2018 Ma. In another meeting chaired by the Minister (Soil and Water Conservation), the local people's representatives brought the matter to the attention of the Government.
Decision: Pazar Lake, Dhangarwadi, Ta. Rs. Government approval is hereby accorded for disbursement of an amount of Rs. 119.81 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Nineteen Lakh Eighty One Thousand only).
