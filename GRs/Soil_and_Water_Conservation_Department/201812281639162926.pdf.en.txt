Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 800.00 lakhs has been budgeted for the year 2018-19 under ZH-3, Account Head 2702 9201. Out of which, Rs. 560.00 lakh has been made available for disbursement through the relevant circulars, subject to a ceiling of 70%. Distribution of funds The legislative development has to be done as per the board-wise formula prescribed by the Governor. The Government has decided to fund the survey work in the contextual proposals submitted by the field machinery for the remaining funds for the Maharashtra Division as follows.
Government's decision:
Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 207.27 lakh (Rupees Two Crore Seven Lakh Twenty Seven Thousand only) as per the attached Form-A for survey works of 92 minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of irrigation capacity 0 to 100 hectares in the remaining Maharashtra division subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) In order to ensure that the amount disbursed does not exceed the reasonable amount for the survey, the Executive Engineer concerned shall, before the actual disbursement of funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable.

f) Strictly follow the guidelines in the relevant circulars of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds.
The amount so disbursed shall be appropriated by the concerned Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them.
