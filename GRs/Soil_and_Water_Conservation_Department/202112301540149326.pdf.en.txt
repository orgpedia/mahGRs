Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 251 to 600 hectares. A sum of Rs. 5.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-3, head 2702 9228 in 2020-21. Out of which, it has been decided to fund the survey work in the proposal dated 16/09/2021 as follows:
Government's decision:
The Government hereby grant approval for disbursement of an amount of Rs. 18.30 lakh (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs Thirty Thousand only) towards payment of survey work for strengthening of sub-till irrigation schemes on Mula Danai Canal in Nevasa Taluka of Ahmednagar District.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) In order to ensure that no more than the reasonable amount for the survey is disbursed, the District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall, before disbursement of the actual funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable; (b) The above work of the survey shall be verified by the concerned Sub-Divisional Office to ensure that the funds are not disbursed twice; (c) If there is no need for funds for any reason, the amount disbursed shall not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances; (d) Any amount in excess of the sanctioned cost shall not be disbursed; (e) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount so unutilized shall be immediately returned to the Government.
