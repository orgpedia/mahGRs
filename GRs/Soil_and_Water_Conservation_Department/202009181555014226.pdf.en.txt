Preamble: Demand for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects with irrigation potential of 101 to 250 hectares. A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-3, head 2702 9219 for the year 2020-21. Of this, 10% of the funds have been made available for distribution. Out of which, the government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal from the fund for Marathwada division as follows.
Decision: Approval of the Government is hereby accorded to disburse an amount of Rs. 18.75 lakhs (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand only) available at present for payment of eligible and completed survey works in the reference proposals.
The above funds are disbursed subject to the following conditions and conditions:
(a) In order to ensure that no more than the reasonable amount for the survey is disbursed, the District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall, before disbursement of the actual funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable; (b) The sub-divisional office concerned shall verify that the funds are not being disbursed twice for the above survey work; (c) If there is no need for funds for any reason, the disbursed amount should not be diverted for any other work under any circumstances; and (d) No more than the sanctioned cost should be disbursed.
