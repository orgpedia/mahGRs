Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works on progress/ new minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 300.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-5, head 4702A019 in 2020-21. Out of this, Rs. 30.00 crore is for eradicating the irrigation backlog of Hingoli district. At present, 50% of the budgeted funds are available for disbursement.
Out of this, the following decisions have been taken on the reference proposal from the funds allocated for the remaining Maharashtra divisions.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 57.19 lakhs (Rupees Fifty Seven Lakhs and Nineteen Thousand only) towards payment of dues for the work done on Pazar Talav Bharudkheda, a project with an irrigation potential of 22 hectares in Jamner Taluka of Jalgaon District, subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) the funds being distributed through the Water Conservation Corporation, the District Planning Committee or any other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above projects, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) the funds shall not be spent beyond the cost of administrative approval as well as revised administrative approval approved by the Government; (c) if funds are not required for the works of the above projects, the funds shall not be distributed for other projects under any circumstances; and (d) if the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the funds shall be returned to the Government forthwith.
