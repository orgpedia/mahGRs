Preamble: The Chief Engineer, Minor Irrigation (Water Conservation), Pune Office vide letter No. Pursuant to letter dated 09.09.2019 with P.S. 4/4219/ As per the directions of the Governor, a proposal has been received by the Government to grant administrative approval to the works of 11 gates of cement storage embankments in Aundha Taluka under the Hingoli district irrigation backlog. The water storage capacity of the proposed Cement Nala Dam is 268.44 cusecs and the total cost of these works is Rs. 3.91 crore. The irrigation potential of these works is 70 hectares and the matter of administrative approval for these works was under consideration of the government. Based on this, the following decision was taken.
Government Decision: Based on the list of the year 2017-18 of the Soil and Water Conservation Department, the budget of the 11 gate cement storage embankment works in Aundha Taluka of Hingoli district, a total of Rs. 3,90,99, 797/- (Rupees Three Crores Ninety Lakhs Nine Thousand Seven Hundred and Ninety Seven only) towards the cost of the work amounting to Rs. 3,60,05, 288/- (Rupees Three Crores Sixty Lakhs Five Thousand Two Hundred and Eighty Eight only) and Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 30,94,509/- (Rupees Thirty Lakhs Ninety Four Thousand Five Hundred Nine only). The details of these 11 schemes are as follows: The existing limit for these schemes is Rs. 1,46,114/- per unit.
