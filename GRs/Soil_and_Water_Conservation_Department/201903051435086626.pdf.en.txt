Introduction: The Central Government has launched the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana from 2015-16. Accordingly, from the year 2015-16, funds are being received for soil and water conservation works by the Agriculture Department of the Central Government under the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana- Other Initiatives. The funds are being utilised for taking up soil and water conservation treatment in the projects under the Integrated Watershed Management Programme with the approval of the State Level Sanctioning Committee. Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Other Initiatives The ratio of Central and State funding for the scheme has been fixed at 60:40 from 2015-16.
On the 25th of May 2018. In the 6th State Level Sanctioning Committee meeting held under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, a resolution has been passed regarding the implementation of these components and the planning and spending of the funds received by the Agriculture Department. It has been decided to use the funds received for this scheme for supplementing water management by linking it to micro irrigation component included under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana Per Drop More Crop (PMKSY-PDMC) being implemented by Director Agriculture, Horticulture.
However, the process of making budgetary allocations for the financial year 2018-19 was completed at that time. Therefore, no provision has been made by the Agriculture Department in this financial year.
Necessary funds for both micro-irrigation and supplementary water management components of the Central share have been released from the fund meant for micro-irrigation. However, for supplementary water management, the required funds from the matching State share may not be available from the District Development Fund.

The Department of Soil and Water Conservation has allocated Central and State share for Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana- Other activities in the current financial year. Accordingly, it was decided in the meeting of the State Level Sanctioning Committee of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana held on 28th January, 2019 to implement the sub-component of Supplementary Water Management in the financial year 2018-19 by the Department of Soil and Water Conservation.
As per the decision taken by the State Level Committee, the Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 80 crore from the Central Government. 16.32 crore (General Category- Rs. 12.73 crore, Scheduled Castes Rs. 1.96 crore and Rs. 1.63 crore) and equivalent State share to be disbursed to the implementing agency and requested to take further necessary action to implement the said component. Agriculture and Forestry Department. 14.02.2019 As per the decision of the Government, the Department of Agriculture has decided to implement the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) for providing supplementary water management under per drop more crop component, under reference for general category. As per the Government Decision dated 19.06.2018, Rs. 1273 lakh (Rs. 12 crore 377 lakh only) has been withdrawn from the Budget Management System and the Department of Soil and Water Conservation has decided that the funds released by the Central Government for Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana- Per Drop More Crop (Supplementary Water Management Matters) will be disbursed to the Commissioner (Agriculture) on BDS system along with the required matching State share. Accordingly, the distribution of central and state share for supplementary water management activities under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana-Other Activities (General Category) was under consideration of the Government.
