Introduction The Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is a reference scheme for restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state. N. No. 1 is being implemented. A total of 20 bundhs in Satara district will be repaired under the Pune Mandal Office. 755.80 lakhs. The total water storage available in these 20 schemes is 4347.20 cusecs and irrigation potential is 1579.86 ha.
The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 2 and 3 have been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Pune has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. According to the information submitted by the Board Office, these 20 schemes are in the second phase of the reform plan and the administrative approval of the amendment proposal was under consideration. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Sanvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 20 repair schemes in the Pune Regional Water Conservation Department is Rs. 755.80 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) Each of the above schemes is being given separate administrative approval. The order is assembled.
