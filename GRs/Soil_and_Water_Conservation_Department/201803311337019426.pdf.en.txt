The Proposal:
The Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan is being implemented in the State as per the Government decision dated 5th December, 2014. Under this campaign, the work of desilting, simplification and deepening of rivers/ streams/ nullahs is being done with the participation of the people and the residents of the villages who have taken the initiative to revive the rivers/ streams/ nullahs and have contributed or are contributing in large numbers through public participation/ labour.
