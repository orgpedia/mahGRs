Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 4 is being implemented. A total of 244 schemes under the Nashik Circle Office will be repaired. 3941.37 lakhs. The irrigation potential of these works is 6021. The total water storage is 31099.11 cusecs.
The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 5 has been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik has scrutinized/ scrutinized all the budgets at his level. Considering the above, 244 works mentioned in the revised plan of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme were under consideration for administrative approval of the proposal. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Samvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 244 repair schemes in Nashik Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 3914.37 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
