Preamble: Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyaan is being implemented in the State as per the decision of the Government dated 5th December, 2014 to permanently overcome the scarcity situation under" Water Scarcity Free Maharashtra for All-2019." The state cabinet has directed to prepare a booklet showing the social impact and cost benefit analysis of various measures taken by the government regarding the Jalyukt Shivar scheme and the successful campaign implemented by the state government in this regard. Accordingly, as per the MoU between Yashada and Rambhau Mhalgi Prabodhini, the task of preparing a brochure on the success of the Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan and its impact on social life has been entrusted to Rambhau Mhalgi Prabodhini through Yashada. As per the project agreement, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 15.00 lakhs (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs only) to YASHADA, Pune towards the cost of preparation of brochure.
Government Decision: Under the supervision of Director, Yashoda, Pune, for the work of preparing a booklet showing the social impact and cost benefit analysis of the various measures taken by the government regarding the Jalyukt Shivar scheme and the successful campaign implemented by the state government in this regard
