Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 4 is being implemented. A total of 52 schemes will be repaired under the Aurangabad Circle Office.
