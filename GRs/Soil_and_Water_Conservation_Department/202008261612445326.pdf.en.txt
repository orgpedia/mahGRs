The Proposal:
The programme of construction of cement embankments in taluks in the state in the scarcity situation has been going on since 2012-13. Under this programme, funds are provided by sanctioning cement dams in taluks/ villages in scarcity situations as well as in the River Rejuvenation Programme and Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan.
A total of Rs. 68.62 lakh (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand) was under consideration.
Government's decision:
During the year 2020-21, Cement Dam Programme Account Head 4402 2799 involving an amount of Rs. A sum of Rs. 110.0 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores) has been budgeted. The demand made under letter No. 3 in respect of the cement embankment completed out of the embankment sanctioned in 2016-17 in Parbhani district under the River Rejuvenation Programme.

2. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department concerned to control the expenditure of funds being made available for the construction of the cement dam.
The District Water Conservation Officer of the district is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Controller should ensure that the funds are spent on the objectives for which they are distributed.
The funds being disbursed are subject to the following conditions:
3.1 Cement Bandha above- Withdrawal or disbursement of funds being disbursed by the said Government, if funds are available from other sources.
It shouldn't be done. The District Water Conservation Officer of the concerned district should ensure that the expenditure is not misused.
3.2 No amount shall be spent in excess of the administrative sanction or the price agreed to by the Government.
3.3 If the funds provided remain unspent for any reason, the unspent funds shall not be used for any other purpose. The money should be immediately returned to the government.
3.4 The full utilization certificate and the certificate of completion of the work given so far should be submitted to the government immediately.
