For this, the funds are allocated in the budget within the limits of the funds approved by the central government programmatically and actually deposited in the consolidated fund of the state government. The Decision:
A sum of Rs. 150.00 lakhs (Rupees One Crore and Fifty Lakh only) is being handed over to the Commissioner of Enumeration and Commissioner, Soil and Water Conservation, Aurangabad for further utilization to meet the expenditure under the 6th and 1st Water Storage Enumeration Programme of Minor Irrigation Schemes in the State.
While utilising the above funds, it should be ensured that the procedures, guidelines and conditions issued by the Ministry of Finance and Ministry of Jal Shakti of the Central Government from time to time are complied with.
