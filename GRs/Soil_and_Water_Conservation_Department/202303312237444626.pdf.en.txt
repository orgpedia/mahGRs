Introduction: As per the budget presented to the Government, the Gated Storage Dam No. 1 th. Miraj, Gated Storage Barrage Santoshwadi (Khot Mala) Ta. Miraj, Gated Cement Barrage Jalihal No. 1 th. Gated Cement Barrage Khandanal No. 2 th. Jats are proposed on local drains in G. Sangli.
The estimated cost of the proposed 4 works based on the department's schedule 2020-21 is Rs. With 4,08,88, 630 inflows, these works will create 236.29 mm of water storage and submerge 82 hectares of land. Accordingly, a proposal for administrative approval of these four dams was submitted to the government through a letter of reference. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
