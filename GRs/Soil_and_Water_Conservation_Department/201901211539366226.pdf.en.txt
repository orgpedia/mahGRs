Preamble: A sum of Rs. 80.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2018-19 under Demand No.ZH-5, Account Head 4702A028 for minor irrigation schemes with an irrigation potential of 101-250 hectares. Out of which, funds to the extent of 70% amounting to Rs. 56.00 crore have been made available for disbursement as per the relevant circulars. The government was considering to distribute the funds in the form of statutory development board. Accordingly, the Government has taken the following decisions on the proposal recommended under the Reference Letter.
Government Decision: 247 crore for the construction of a Rs. A sum of Rs. 910.00 lakhs (Rupees Nine Crores Ten Lakhs only) aggregating to Rs. 8.10 crores and Rs. 1.00 crores for land acquisition is hereby approved by the Government subject to the following conditions.
(a) The amount being disbursed by the Water Conservation Corporation or any other source, if funds are available for the above project, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed; (b) No amount shall be spent in excess of the cost of administrative sanction as well as revised administrative sanction approved by the Government; (c) If there is no need for funds for land acquisition and construction of the above project, the amount shall not be disbursed for any other project under any circumstances; (d) If the funds remain unutilized for any reason, the amount shall be immediately returned to the Government; (e) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds shall be strictly followed.
