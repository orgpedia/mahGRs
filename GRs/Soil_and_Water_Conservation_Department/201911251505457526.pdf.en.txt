Government's decision:
In order to honour the best performing villages, best project implementing agencies, best village functionaries, best performing village level staff who have been participating under the Adarsh Gaon Yojana since 2009-10 with appropriate awards from the government level, the Maharashtra Adarsh Gaon Bhushan Award for the year 2017-18 under Adarsh Gaon Yojana has been instituted by the Government of Maharashtra under the Ministry of Rural Development. As per the Government Decision dated 04.09.2019, Rs. 15.00 Lakhs (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs only) (Prize money Rs. 12.20 lakh and Rs. Rs 2.80 crore.
