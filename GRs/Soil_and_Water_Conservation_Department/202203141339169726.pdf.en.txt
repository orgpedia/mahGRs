Proposal L.P. Scheme Juuthi, Ta. Rajapur, G. Ratnagiri are on the local drain, which has accumulated 2234 cusecs of water and 160 cusecs of water. Irrigation is proposed. The side walls and headwall of the system on the upstream side of the irrigation insulator of this scheme are in masonry and the masonry walls of the system have gone out of doors. The construction of the dam is outdated in terms of safety. The side walls and headwalls of the vertical conduit system of the irrigation insulator of this scheme are in masonry. The dam is prone to danger if water seeps through the stone wall on the side of the conduit. An amendment has been proposed. Therefore, the matter of administrative approval of the repair works was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: L.P. Scheme Juuthi, Ta. Rajapur, Rs. 2,06,88, 504/- (Rupees Two Crores Six Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Five Hundred and Four only) as per the proposal of the Finance Department, Govt. The administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions, taking into consideration the notification dated 10th November, 2020:
1. Conditions to be imposed on the contractor for setting up a water utility.
10% of the tender amount should be deducted from the contractor's payment until the WUA is established.
The amount should be disbursed after the establishment of the institution. If the institution cannot be established in an exceptional situation, the 10% of the deducted funds should be disbursed after submitting the proposal to the government with detailed reasons.
