Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a sum of Rs. 12.68 lakh (Rupees Twelve Lakh Sixty Six Thousand only) towards payment of pending maintenance and repair works of Pazar Talav Chungi (Kotamale Khet) in Akkalkot Taluka of Solapur district.

(b) shall not be paid in excess of the agreed price;
(c) If for any reason the fund remains unutilized, the amount shall be immediately returned to the Government without being distributed among themselves for any other purpose.
d) Strictly follow the guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of funds.
e) It should be ensured that the procedures and instructions prescribed under the government decision of the Chief Minister Water Conservation Scheme are complied with.
