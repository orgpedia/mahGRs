According to the proposal submitted to the government, the budget of Umri K.P. Bandhara No. 1 and Nagpur (B) No. 1. The dams are proposed on a tributary of the Sindphana River. The total cost of the two works is estimated at Rs. 2,74,89, 900/- which will create a storage of 188.436 cubic feet of water and submerge 65 hectares of land. Accordingly, a proposal for granting administrative status to the two Kolhapur-style embankments was submitted to the government through a reference letter. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government Decision: The total cost of two Kolhapur type dams- 1 and Nagpur (B) Co. Ltd. No. 1- is Rs. The administrative approval is being granted to the proposal at a cost of Rs. 2,74,89, 900/- (Rupees Two Crores only) subject to the following terms and conditions. The Scheme is being presented in Annexure A with sub-heading provisions as per the original Budget.
1) The contractor should be made obligatory by putting conditions in the tender for setting up a water use agency. 10% of the tender amount should be deducted from the contractor's payment until the WUA is established. The amount should be disbursed after the establishment of the institution. If an institution in exceptional situation cannot be established, the 10% of the deducted funds should be disbursed after submitting a proposal to the government with detailed work plan and approval of the government. The government has decided to set up the agency. Remuneration to be paid as provided in 11-02-2022.
2) The Superintending Engineer/ Regional Water Conservation Officer for the project is required to visit the work site and identify and certify the site and alignment.
