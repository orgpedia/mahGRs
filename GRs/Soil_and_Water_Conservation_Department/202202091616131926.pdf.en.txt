Introduction In the context of restoring irrigation potential by preserving and repairing the completed water conservation schemes in the state, the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme is being implemented. N. No. 4 is being implemented. A total of 58 schemes will be repaired under the Aurangabad Circle Office. 1065.07 lakhs. The irrigation potential of these works is 1368. The total water storage available in the dam is 6043 cusecs. The proposal for obtaining administrative approval for the above works is contained in the above Reference Letter No. 5 has been submitted to the government. Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad has scrutinized/ examined all the budgets at his level. Considering the above, the proposal was under consideration for administrative approval as the above mentioned 58 works were included in the revised plan of the Chief Minister's Water Conservation Scheme. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under the Mukhyamantri Jal Samvardhan Yojana, the budget of a total of 58 repair schemes in Aurangabad Regional Water Conservation Division is Rs. 1065.07 lakh (Rs. These price budgets are being accorded administrative approval subject to the following terms and conditions.
