As per the guidelines/ directions issued under the Government decision dated 21.09.2012, the following Project Implementation Committee is being constituted for implementation of computerization and e-governance system in the Soil and Water Conservation Department and all the field offices, mandals, etc. under the department. The decision of the committee on e-governance projects under the department will be final. Also, Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Establishment) of the Department is being declared as the Nodal Officer for the e-Governance project.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) To decide the policy in line with the e-governance policy of the Directorate of Information Technology.
2) To take a final decision regarding the online functioning of the Soil and Water Conservation Department and all the field offices, boards, etc. under the department. Also, hold meetings as needed.
3) To determine the scope of all e-governance projects being implemented by the Soil and Water Conservation Department and to guide the tender process by finalizing the RPF (Request for Proposal) to start the e-governance project for the department.
4) To take decision for procurement of necessary hardware/ software for implementation of e-Governance projects in Soil and Water Conservation Department.
5) Review of e-governance projects in Soil and Water Conservation Department, selection of consultants from reputed organizations as per requirement for e-governance projects to be implemented in the department or for subordinate offices.
The Committee shall have the powers to implement the project in accordance with the decisions taken by the Department of Information Technology (General Administration Department) from time to time as well as the powers conferred.
