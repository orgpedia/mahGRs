A sum of Rs. 5.00 crore has been budgeted under ZH-3, head 2702 9228 in 2020-21. Out of which Rs. 50.00 lakhs is available for disbursement of funds within the limit of 10%. The government has decided to fund the survey work in the reference proposal submitted by the zonal machinery for the Marathwada division of the state as follows.
The Decision:
Storage Lake Ghatewadi- Mengdewadi, Ta. Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse an amount of Rs. 2.96 lakhs (Rupees Two Lakhs Sixty Nine Thousand only) towards payment of the works done for the Benefit Area Survey of the project at Patoda, G.Beed subject to the following conditions and conditions.
(a) In order to ensure that no more than the reasonable amount for the survey is disbursed, the District Water Conservation Officer concerned shall, before disbursement of the actual funds, re-verify that the amount demanded from the Government is reasonable; (b) The above work of the survey shall be verified by the concerned Sub-Divisional Office to ensure that the funds are not disbursed twice; (c) If there is no need for funds for any reason, the amount disbursed shall not be diverted for any other purpose under any circumstances; (d) Any amount in excess of the sanctioned cost shall not be disbursed; (e) If for any reason the funds remain unutilized, the unutilized amount shall be immediately returned to the Government; (f) The guidelines in the relevant circulars of the Finance Department regarding the disbursement of funds shall be strictly complied with.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad and shall be promptly disbursed to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under them.
