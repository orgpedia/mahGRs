Preamble: A sum of Rs. 80.00 crore has been budgeted for the year 2018-19 under Demand No.ZH-5, Account Head 47029028 for minor irrigation schemes with an irrigation potential of 101-250 hectares. Out of which, funds to the extent of 70% amounting to Rs. 56.00 crore have been made available for disbursement as per the relevant circulars. The government was considering to distribute the funds in the form of statutory development board. Accordingly, the Government has taken the following decisions on the proposal recommended under the Reference Letter.
