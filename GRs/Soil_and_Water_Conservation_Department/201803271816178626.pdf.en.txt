INTRODUCTION: Under Demand No. L-3, Account Head 27023391, a sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been budgeted for the survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of 101-250 hectares for the financial year 2016-17, out of which Rs. 8.00 crore is available for direct disbursement within the limit of 80%. It was under the consideration of the government to disburse this fund to the eligible works of the survey according to the statutory development board-wise criteria in accordance with the demands submitted by the zonal machinery.
