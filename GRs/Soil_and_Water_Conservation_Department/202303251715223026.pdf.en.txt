Preamble: Demand No. 1 for works of new/ progressive minor irrigation (water conservation) projects in non-tribal areas with irrigation potential of 0 to 100 hectares. A sum of Rs. 175.00 crore was budgeted for the year 2022-23 under ZH-5, Account Head 4702A019. Now, as per the revised estimates, from the funds made available under the scheme, the following decisions have been taken on the proposal for seeking funds with reference date 25/01/2023.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded to the Government to disburse a total amount of Rs. 540.11 lakh (Rupees Five Crore Forty Lakh Eleven Thousand only) towards payment of pending dues of 70 works of 61 storage dams and 09 K.P.Bandhs in the reference proposals taken up under the Hingoli District Irrigation Residue Eradication Programme.
The aforesaid funds are being distributed subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) The funds being distributed through the Water Conservation Corporation, District Planning Committee or any other source, if funds have been made available for the works of the above projects, shall not be withdrawn or disbursed.
