The Proposal:
An amount of Rs. 2100.00 crore has been provided in the Budget Estimates for the financial year 2022-23 as share contribution for Maharashtra Jal Sanrakshan Corporation. As per the order dated 04.04.2022 of the Department of Finance, for the first nine months, funds within the limit of 60% of the budgeted amount are admissible. Out of which, Rs. 544.00 crore has been made available for disbursement to Administrative Departments for the first six months. The following decision has been taken to disburse the funds to the Corporation.
Decision: Approval is hereby accorded for disbursement of funds amounting to Rs. 544.00 crore (Rupees Five Hundred Forty Four Crores only) as share capital contribution to Maharashtra Jal Sanrakshan Corporation.
The Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Aurangabad is hereby authorized to draw and disburse the aforesaid funds from the treasury and distribute the same in the name of Managing Director, Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation, Aurangabad.
The expenditure on this should be covered under the head of accounts below and should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2022-23.
