GOVERNMENT Circular: The Department of Soil and Water Conservation has included the officers/ employees working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as per the Government Decision dated 07.06.2018 and 22.06.2018. In the statement A accompanying this Government decision, the officers who have been given the option to be permanently attached to the Soil and Water Conservation Department have been included and their services have been permanently transferred to the establishment of this department. The said Government Decision provides that the service excellence lists of Group' A' and Group' B' cadre on the establishment of this Department shall be published separately from the Government level after examining the service excellence lists of all the officers/ employees mentioned in the said Statement' A'. Further, the Department of Soil and Water Conservation, vide Government Order dated 22.06.2018, has provided the option of Soil and Water Conservation Department to the officers/ employees working in the technical cadre of Group-A and Group-B working under the Water Resources Department. The services of the officers and employees mentioned in the eligible returns A have been transferred to the Soil and Water Conservation Department permanently and they have been declared as officers/ employees of the Soil and Water Conservation Department.
In accordance with the provisions of the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department at Reference No. 1 and the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982, the designation of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer in the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as Assistant Engineer Grade-I (Architecture) (Direct Service)/ Sub-Divisional Engineer (Architecture) (Graduate) (State+ GP)/ Sub-Divisional Officer (Architecture) (Diploma holder) (State+ GP). The Provisional Interim Seniority Notification dated 01.01.2018 is being published in Part-I, Part-II and Part-III of this Cadre respectively.
The seniority list prepared by the Department of Water Resources has been taken on the basis of the seniority list published under the aforesaid Reference No. 2, 3, 4 and 5.

The date of seniority mentioned against the names of the officers in the seniority list mentioned in the Form-A Part-1, Part-2 and Part-3 attached with this circular shall be the date of such seniority mentioned in the Government Notification issued by the General Administration Department No. SRV 1076/12, d. As per the provision of 21.06.1982, seniority in the cadre of Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer will be considered only.
All Regional Water Conservation Officers shall bring this circular to the immediate notice of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer in Form A Parts-1 to 3 under him, and shall preserve a copy of his signature in his own records and make a copy of the same available to the Government.
If any error/ error is found in the details of the sub-divisional water conservation officers included in the seniority list, such as name, date of birth, date of appointment, caste category and place of seniority, etc., they should bring their representations/ objections/ suggestions to the government through registered post/ speed post/ hand delivery with necessary proper proof in writing within 15 days from the date of issue of this circular.
If the errors/ errors are not brought to the notice of the Government in writing within the stipulated time, the temporary seniority of the Sub-Divisional Water Conservation Officer working under the Department of Soil and Water Conservation as on 01.01.2018 will be finalized.
