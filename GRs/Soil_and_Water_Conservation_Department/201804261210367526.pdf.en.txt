Preamble: In addition to the Jalyukt Shivar Mission and the Jalyukt Shivar Mission, instructions have been issued under the Government Circular dated 4th May, 2016 to provide funds for diesel for the works of deepening and widening of rivers/ nullahs/ rivulets, deep CCT, desilting of farmlands and ditches etc. carried out by non-governmental organizations in other villages.
Along with the above work, the construction of the dam, compartment carting are being carried out by non-governmental organizations. Therefore, the matter of issuing instructions for providing funds for diesel for these works was under the consideration of the government.
Government Circulars- Non-government organizations are doing works like deepening and widening of rivers/ drains/ streams, deep CCT, desilting of farmlands and ponds, construction of embankments, compartment bunding etc. It is being approved to provide funds to non-governmental organizations for the expenditure of fuel/ diesel for the construction of barrage and compartment bunding works.
All Government circulars/ Government decisions/ guidelines issued from time to time on how to consider and make available funds for fuel/ diesel shall continue to be applicable.
