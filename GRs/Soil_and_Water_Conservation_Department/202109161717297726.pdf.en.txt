Department of Soil and Water Conservation. As per the Service Entry Rules dated 24.06.2021, Junior Engineers Group-B (Non-Gazetted) of the Zila Parishad System who have been inducted permanently by giving option from the Water Resources Department, have been prescribed criteria for promotion to the post of Group-B (Gazetted). Accordingly, the government was considering to form a committee to check the eligibility of the junior engineer concerned for granting" Group-B (Gazetted)" status to the junior engineer of the Soil and Water Conservation Department.
