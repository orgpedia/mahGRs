The Proposal:
The Central Land Resources Department, Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India has issued Common Guidelines 2021 for Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Watershed Development Component 2.0. Accordingly, the Prime Minister's Agriculture Irrigation Scheme Watershed Development Component 2.0 will be implemented in the state. The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 27.04.2022. The Centre and the state share in the scheme is 60:40. As per letter No. 2, the Central Government has released Central share of Rs. Rs 250.00 crore. Accordingly, the Tribal Development Department has issued a notification. As per the Government decision dated 28.11.2023, the Central Share for Scheduled Tribes category under the head 44022618 is Rs. 250.00 lacs and State share under head 44022627 Rs. 167.00 lakhs has been provided. The issue of distribution of central share funds and state share funds was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana- Watershed Development Component 2.0 under Tribal Area Sub-Plan for STs, Central Share of 60% (Account Head 44022618) of Rs. 2,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Fifty Lakhs only) and State Share of 40% (Account Head 44022627) amounting to Rs. The Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 1,67,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Sixty Six Lakh only).
