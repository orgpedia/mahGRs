Preamble: Project Management Consultants (PMCs) have been suggested as per the relevant Government Decision dated 06.01.2022 for project management of 0-600 Hs of projects under water conservation treatment structures till completion of construction, maintenance and repair works and their scope, scope, modalities and guidelines have been prescribed. Accordingly, the matter of payment of consultation fee of the Project Management Consultants (PMC) suggested for the Nashik regional area was under the consideration of the government.
Reference No. The following decisions have been taken on the funding request proposals at 3 and 4.
Decision: TPF Engineering Pvt.
The Project Management Consultants are hereby accorded approval to disburse a total amount of Rs. 56.33 lakhs (Rupees Fifty Six Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand only) for the works/ services completed under Mukhyamantri Jal Sanvardhan Yojana in Nashik, Ahmednagar and Dhule districts as per demand amounting to Rs. 18.20 lakhs, Rs. 34.36 lakhs and Rs. 3.77 lakhs respectively.
The above funds are being disbursed subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) The Project Management Consultants (PMCs) have rendered services in respect of the repair works taken up under the Mukhyamantri Jal Sanvardhan Yojana.

The budget must contain separate additional provisions for the consultation rate in accordance with the cost of project repair work.
b) The expenditure of the above disbursed funds should be shown separately on the repair works undertaken under the Mukhyamantri Jal Sanvardhan Yojana in which the Project Management Consultants (PMC) have provided services.
(c) No amount shall be paid in excess of the administrative/ revised administrative sanction cost of the project concerned at the time of disbursement of the said fund. Funds should be distributed on the basis of payment.
d) The total amount of consultation fee payable for the services rendered by the Project Management Consultants (PMC) shall not exceed 2% of the total cost of the project (excluding ownership charges and incidental costs).
e) Reference No. 1 regarding services rendered by Project Management Consultants (PMC). 2 It should be ensured that the procedures, guidelines and timelines in the government decision here have been complied with.
f) If the fund remains unutilized for any reason, the amount should be immediately returned to the government without distributing it for any other purpose, c) The guidelines in the relevant circular of the Finance Department regarding the distribution of the fund should be strictly followed.
The amount so disbursed shall be drawn by the Regional Water Conservation Officer, Soil and Water Conservation Department, Nashik and shall be disbursed expeditiously to the concerned District Water Conservation Officers under him.
