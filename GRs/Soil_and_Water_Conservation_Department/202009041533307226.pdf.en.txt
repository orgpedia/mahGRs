Preamble: Chief Engineer, Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme, Nagpur According to the proposal received under Section 1, the Kolhapur-style dam is being constructed at Kadholi, Ta. Kurkheda, G. Gadchiroli under Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme (VIDP). Administrative approval was granted under 2.
Mauje Kadholi, Taluka Kurkheda, Dist. As Gadchiroli village is in the approved plan of Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan 2015-16 and due to non-availability of funds under Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme, the administrative approval was taken for this work under Gap Funding and since the expenditure on this scheme has not been incurred under Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme, the proposal to cancel the administrative approval of this scheme under Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme has been received under reference 4 of the Chief Engineer, Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme, Nagpur.
Mauje Kadholi, Taluka Kurkheda, Dist. As Gadchiroli village is in the approved plan of Jalyukt Shivar Abhiyan 2015-16 and funds are not available under Vidarbha Intensive Irrigation Development Programme, the matter of cancellation of the original administrative approval of the dam was under consideration of the government.
Accordingly, the government has decided to cancel the original administrative approval of the scheme as follows.
