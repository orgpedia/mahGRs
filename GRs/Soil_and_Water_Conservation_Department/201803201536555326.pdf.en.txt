The Proposal:
A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been budgeted for survey of minor irrigation (water conservation) projects of 0 to 100 hectare irrigation potential for the financial year 2017-18 under Demand No.L-3, Account Head 2702 4745, out of which Rs. 8.00 crore within 80% limit has been made available for disbursement as per the circular. It was under the consideration of the government to disburse the eligible works in the fund demand proposals submitted by the regional system within the limits of the statutory development board-wise standard. In this regard, the government has decided to provide funds for the works recommended under the relevant Acts No. 2, 3 and 4 as follows:
