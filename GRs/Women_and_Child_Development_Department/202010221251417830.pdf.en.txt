The Proposal:
To tackle the problem of malnutrition in the state, a scheme to set up Village Child Development Centre (VCDC) for severely malnourished children in non-tribal areas has been approved by the State Government vide Notification No. 3 It has been started as per the Government Decision dated 26.9.2017. The Government was considering to release Rs. 3.30 crore out of the budgeted amount of Rs. 10.00 crore for the financial year 2020-21 to implement the scheme.
Government's decision:
The Government has approved the release of Rs. 3.30 crore (Rs. Three Crore Thirty Lakh only) out of the budgeted fund of Rs. 10.00 crore for the purpose of 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) under the head 2236 Poshan Aahar, 101, Special Nutritional Feeding Programme, (09) Treatment of Malnourished Children, (01) Gram Bal Vikas Kendra Programme (2236-1992) during the financial year 2020-21 for the scheme of Village Child Development Centre (VCDC) for severely malnourished children in non-tribal areas established to tackle the problem of malnutrition in the State.
2. The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai shall take care of spending the said fund as per the guidelines issued by the Government and submit the utilization certificate of the said fund to the Government.
This Government Resolution is being issued as per the approval given by the Finance Department vide Informal Reference No. 162/ Expenditure-6, dated 7.10.2020.
