Government circular
As per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Service Excellence) Rules, 1982 and the Government Notification dated 21.6.2021 of the General Administration Department, it is necessary to publish the service excellence list by considering January 1 as the base date every year.
Government Circular No.MBA-2021/ Q. No.46/ Q. As on 02.06.2021, the final service seniority list of 01.01.2021 in the cadre of Deputy Commissioner Group-A in the cadre under the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development has been issued.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune handles the administrative and institutional/ service matters in the case of officers/ staff working under the office of the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai.
The respective ranks of the officers of these two Commissionerates are being published in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 and the final seniority list in respect of the said officers as per the Government Decision/ Government Circular/ Notification issued in this regard, along with the revised Combined Provisional Service Seniority List of the Deputy Commissioner, Group-A Cadre up to the end of 01.01.2022 as per Annexure-I respectively.
