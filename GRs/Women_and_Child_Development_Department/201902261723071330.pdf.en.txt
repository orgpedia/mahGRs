Government Decision: The Government of Maharashtra has approved a Government Decision dated 29th December 2018 to provide free Baby Care Kit Bags to women who are enrolled in the Government Primary Health Center/ Government Hospital during pregnancy and give birth there, for their newborn child (boy or girl) at the time of first delivery, up to an amount of Rs. The scheme has been implemented across the state from 26th January 2019.
The following head of accounts have been approved for the scheme with the approval of the Planning Department, Finance Department and the Accountant General and the Government is approving the budgeting of funds under this head for the year 2018-19.
