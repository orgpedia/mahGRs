Government's decision:
The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. For the financial year 2020-21, a total amount of Rs. 5300.31 lakh (Rupees Fifty Three Crores Thirty One Thousand only) is being sanctioned as per the following statement for the purpose of payment of salaries to the officers/ staff under Integrated Child Development Services Scheme under Demand No. X-1, Head Accounts Head 2236 Poshan Aahar as per the Head of Accounts/ Objectives of Raka No. 2, Raka No. 5.
