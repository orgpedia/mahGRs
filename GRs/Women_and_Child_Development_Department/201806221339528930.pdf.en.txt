The Proposal:
According to the Finance Department. As per the Circular dated 02.04.2017, the budgetary allocation for mandatory and programme expenditure required for the first nine months of the current financial year 2018-19 (ending December, 2018) has been made available for disbursement within the limit of 70% of the total annual provision.
2. Child Care Scheme is implemented to bring up orphaned, destitute and destitute children in non-institutional, family environment. For the year 2018-19, Rs. 1500.00 lakh has been provided and Rs. 1050.00 lakhs is being made available for disbursement for the first nine months of the financial year. Of these, Rs. 525.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Now, in pursuance of this Government decision, the Government is approving the disbursement of the following out of the budgetary provision of 2018-19 for the Child Care Scheme:
