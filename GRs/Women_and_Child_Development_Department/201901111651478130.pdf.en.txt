Preamble: Reference No. As per the decision of the Government of India, 30% seats have been reserved for women in government, semi-government and government-aided institutions. However, in order to clarify certain aspects of the said Government decision, the Government Decision dated 15.12.2017 of No. 4 referred to above has amended the points No. 1 (10), 2 (a) (3), 2 (a) (4), 2 (7) of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2001. The proceedings in respect of other matters have been stayed in accordance with the Government Order dated 25.05.2001.
However, in the case of 30% reservation for women. The matter of issuing clarificatory instructions in respect of certain provisions of the Government Resolution dated 15.12.2017 was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
(1) The provisions of the Government Decision dated 25.05.2001 as amended by the Government Decision dated 15.12.2017 shall come into force with effect from the date of issue of the Government Decision i.e. 15.12.2017.
(2) The provision relating to consideration of marital status of a woman mentioned in paragraph No. 2 of the Government Decision dated 15.12.2017 is omitted.
