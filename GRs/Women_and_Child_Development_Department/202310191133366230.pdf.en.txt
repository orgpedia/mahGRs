(2) The order shall come into force immediately. The concerned officers should immediately join the place of partial transfer and submit a report to the government in the prescribed way.
Confidential reports, service books, and final salary certificates of the above officers should be sent immediately to the new office where they have been transferred.
The above officers shall not grant any leave without prior permission or their application for leave shall not be accepted by the parent office as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981.
(5) When the above officers join the post of transfer, it should be confirmed by the office who will depute them on posting as to how they were on leave/ absent.
If the officer is absent on medical grounds, he should be sent before the medical board before being produced. If others are found to be on leave, they should fill the leave application without fail and then submit it. Also, the guidelines issued by the government from time to time regarding leave should be followed.
(6) The above officers shall ensure that they join the place of transfer within the period of assumption of office as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Non-Resignation and Removal of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. Otherwise, if he does not join the place of transfer during this period, the period of absence shall be deemed to be non-dies.
Since the transfer of the above officers is on request, travel allowance and other incidental benefits will not be admissible to them.
