Government circular:
The guidelines issued by the Central Government under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme" Mission Shakti" vide letter dated 14 th July, 2022 are being implemented in the State from the financial year 2022-23. The guidelines issued by the Central Government are attached with this circular and all the concerned agencies should take necessary action according to these guidelines. Further, as per the guidelines under Mission Shakti, the scheme-wise government decisions of the schemes should be issued by the concerned offices after due approval from the appropriate level. This governance decision should include instructions regarding the implementation of the schemes, head of accounts, SNA accounts, methodology of availability of funds and manpower, etc.
