Preamble: The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has completed the tender process for the supply of 554 computers and multifunctional printers to 553 Regional Offices and 1 Head Office under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme from the e-Governance Fund, subject to the administrative approval of the Department of Women and Child Development dated 24th January, 2018, as per the procedures of Government e-Market (GeM), Government of India. Technical bids have been opened on Government e-Market (GeM) portal on 8/2/2018 and 9/2/2018.
The Commissionerate has verified the photocopies of all the bidders and the proposal of the eligible bidders namely MINITEK SYSTEMS (I) PVT LTD, L1 bidder for supply of computers and ACE BRAIN SYSTEMS AND SOFTWARE PRIVATE LIMITED, L1 bidder for printing of printers was submitted to the Government for approval vide letter dated 15th February, 2018.
The Project Implementation Committee for the Department of Women and Child Development has been constituted vide Government Order dated 24th May, 2013 under reference date to decide on the proposals for computerization of all the field offices for implementation of computerization/ e-governance projects for the Department of Women and Child Development and its subordinate offices, Corporations, Commissions, etc. A meeting of the Committee was held on 15/03/2018 at 12.00 noon under the Chairmanship of the Committee Chairman and Secretary (Women and Child Development Department) and in his chamber (New Administration Building, 3rd Floor). As one computer and one multifunctional printer each for 553 Regional Offices and 1 Head Office functioning under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme are required for the functioning of Line Listing and MIS, the proposal to purchase from the L1 tenderer through the Government e-Market (GEM) system from the Government e-Governance Fund was placed before the Committee for approval. Necessary action has been taken as per the suggestions made in the meeting by the Department of Information and Technology, Department of Industrial Energy and Department of Labour, Department of Planning. Since the orders for the purchase of computers and printers for the financial year 2017-18 could not be issued after March 31, 2018 due to administrative reasons, the matter of approval for the purchase of computers and printers for the financial year 2018-19 was under consideration of the Government.
