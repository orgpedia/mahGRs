Preamble: The Women's Economic Development Corporation was established under the Companies Act, 1956. It was established on February 24, 1975. Considering the importance of women's overall development and the expertise of the corporation in this work, Government Decision No. Mavim-2001/ P.S. 10/ K-10, D. As on 20th January, 2003, the Corporation has been declared as a State Level" apex body" for women development. The appointment of the Chairperson of the Corporation was under the consideration of the Government as per the provisions of Rule 66 (1) of the Articles of Incorporation and the Establishment Rules.
Government Decision: Smt. Jyoti Deepak Thackeray as the Chairperson of Women's Economic Development Corporation. Chinchaghar, Pt. Kadus, Ta. Wada, G. Palghar-421 312 are being appointed.
The appointment will be for a period of three years from the date of issue of the order or until further orders of the Government, whichever is earlier.
