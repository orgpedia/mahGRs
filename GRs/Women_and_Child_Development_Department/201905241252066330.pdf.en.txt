The Proposal:
The Interim Budget 2019-20 was presented to the Legislative Assembly on 27th February, 2019. As this is an interim budget, the Legislature has passed the Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill, 2019 to meet the expenditure for the period (April, 2019 to July, 2019) till the Appropriation Bill is passed by presenting the additional budget. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has granted permission to meet the expenditure for four months from April 2019 to July 2019.
Accordingly, as per the Government Circular, Department of Women and Child Development, No. BGT-2019/ PR No. 14/ KA-4, dated 10th April, 2019 read with Annexure, the Account Grant has been sanctioned to meet the expenditure for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019 for the financial year 2019-20. Accordingly, the matter was under consideration for disbursement of funds admissible as per the sanctioned accounts under the head 2235 Social Security and Welfare under Women and Child Development Department.
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of the Circular dated 1.4.2019 of the Department of Finance, the Government is approving to disburse the funds under the head of Demand No.X-1 for the financial year 2019-20 under the head of Accounts Head 2235-Social Security and Welfare for meeting the expenditure for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019 as mentioned in column No. 6 of the Statement of Accounts accompanying the said Government decision under the respective headings.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune will be the Controlling Officer for this fund. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune shall strictly follow the checklist items mentioned in the circular dated 1.4.2019 in reference No.1 of the Finance Department, the guidelines of the Central Government as well as the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and the Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing norms, the delegation of financial powers etc.
