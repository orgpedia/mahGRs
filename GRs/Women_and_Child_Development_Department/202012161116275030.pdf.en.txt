Government circular:
As per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Excellence in Service) Rules, 1982 and the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department, the merit list is required to be published every year with 1st January as the base date.
The final list of Group-A Cadre Officers working under the office of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai is being published as per Annexure-I along with the final list of service up to the end of 01.01.2020.
The objections raised by the officers in respect of the list have been resolved and the serviceability list has been revised accordingly.
The date of appointment of these Group-A officers as all the objections received have been resolved. The final service as on 01/01/2020 is being published as per the prospectus displayed along with the seniority list.
