Department of Women and Child Development, Government Resolution No. 2018/ No. 59/ K-5, dated 1.2.2020.
Preamble: To develop and empower all out-of-school beneficiary girls in the age group of 11 to 14 years across the districts of the State by providing them access to nutrition, improving their nutritional and health status, creating awareness among the beneficiary girls about health, hygiene, nutrition, reproductive and sexual health, family and child care, as well as helping the beneficiary girls to improve home skills, life skills and vocational skills, mainstreaming the out-of-school girls in the age group of 11 to 14 years through formal/ non-formal education, popular public services e.g. The Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) for providing information and guidance on Primary Health Centres, Posts, Banks, Police Stations etc. is being implemented for out of school girls in the age group of 11 to 14 years in all the districts of the State.
The scheme is being implemented in the state by the office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. Under this scheme, the ratio of central and state share of expenditure on nutrition is 50: 50. The government has earmarked Rs. 2021-22 crore for the scheme.
Government Decision Under this Government decision, a total amount of Rs. 290.74 lakh (Rupees Two Crore Ninety Four Lakh Seventy Four Thousand only) is being sanctioned for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rajiv Gandhi Yojana for Empowerment of Adolescent Girls (SAG) as per the headings/ objectives of Rakana No.-4 in Rakana No.-2.
