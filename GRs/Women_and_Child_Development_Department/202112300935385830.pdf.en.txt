Provisional promotion to the post of Deputy Commissioner of the said officers is being made subject to the following conditions: 1. The said promotion has been made in public interest as an administrative exigency in a very temporary manner.
2. The officers concerned will not be entitled to any regular promotion i. e. seniority due to this temporary promotion.
3. The promotion of the above officers shall be subject to the final seniority of the Deputy Commissioner Group-A cadre and consequential changes therein and the promotion shall not entitle the above officers to seniority from the date of such promotion.
Since the promotion is purely temporary in nature, the officers in the selection list will not be entitled to any regularisation or promotion as a result of this temporary promotion.
The Government reserves the right to cancel the temporary promotion given to the said officers without any prior notice or notice.
The promoted officers are informed that they are required to report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the order of promotion/ posting. If they do not appear at the place of appointment within the prescribed time after being discharged, then they are not interested in the promotion, the Government Decision, General Administration Department, D. 30/04/1991 and D. As per the provision mentioned in 12/09/2016, their promotion from the government level will be cancelled. Also, it may be noted that the officer who refuses promotion should be stopped immediately if he has been given the benefit of 12/24 years of the post of Deputy Commissioner under the Assured Progress Scheme and he will be given this benefit after direct promotion to the cadre of Deputy Commissioner.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The Controlling Authorities shall relieve the officer who has been given the said appointment/ posting immediately to appear in place of the new appointment. Further, the date on which the concerned officer was relieved to appear in place of the new appointee and the date on which he appeared in place of the new appointee has been communicated to the Government by the concerned Controlling Authorities on wcd- desk07@gov.in.
