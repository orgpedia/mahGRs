GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Now, for the year 2017-18, all the agencies under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme as well as the government and non-government organizations, demand No. X-01 Head 2235, Social Security and Welfare, 199, Assistance to Non-Governmental Organizations, (00) (01) Integrated Child Protection Scheme (2235 A919), 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) amounting to Rs. 373.60 lakh to the Maharashtra State Child Protection Society, Pune through the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate.
Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune is being authorized as the Controlling Officer to control the said fund.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune shall ensure that the above amount is spent for the matters covered under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme. Utilization certificates for the above provision under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme should be submitted by the Maharashtra State Child Protection Society to the Central Government and the State Government through the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune.
4. The Controlling Authority shall ensure that no expenditure is incurred in excess of the provisions made available under the Government decision. While spending these amounts, the rules of business of the state government, financial rules and instructions issued by this department and the Finance Department from time to time, as well as all the terms and conditions of the circular issued under the Finance Department, should be followed.
5. This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the circular in the context of the Finance Department.
