Government's decision:
In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 27.9.2018 passed at Reference No.4, 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai were extended from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019. Accordingly, the proposal for extension of these 43 temporary posts has been submitted by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 22.2.2019 at Reference No.5.
2. Under the Government Order dated 21.2.2019 in Reference No.6 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 22.2.2019 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the aforesaid Government Order of the Finance Department, 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai are being extended from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
