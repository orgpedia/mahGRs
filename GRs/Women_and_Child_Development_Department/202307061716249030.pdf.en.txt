GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MAVIM-2023/ P.S. NO. 73/ K-10 4. District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of this fund to Women Economic Development Corporation.
5. Payments made under the signature of District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban and counter-signed by Deputy Secretary, Women and Child Development Department should be submitted to the Adhiveshan and Accounts Officer. The accounts officer and the creditor must pay the amount by cheque. The Deputy Secretary, Women and Child Development Department has been authorised to counter-sign the payment submitted by the District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban.
After disbursement to the Women's Economic Development Corporation (WEDC), the amount should be reimbursed by the Central Government from the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD).
7. The funds distributed under the said Government Decision shall be spent during the current financial year and the unspent funds shall be paid. Dedication before 31.3.2024 should be taken care of by the Managing Director, Women's Economic Development Corporation, Bandra, Mumbai.
8. This Government decision is based on the informal reference No. 122/ 1472 of the Planning Department. 6.6.2023 and the Finance Department's Informal Reference No. 169/ Expenditure-6, D. are being issued as per the concurrence given by the departments as on 22.6.2023.
