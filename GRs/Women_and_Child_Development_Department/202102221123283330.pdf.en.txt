A total of 1590 posts including 1542 on pay and 48 on honorarium in the establishment of Women and Child Development Commissionerate and a total of 2306 temporary posts including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms were extended from 01/09/2020 to 28/02/2021.

Under the Government Resolution dated 15.02.2021 in Reference No.5 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The letter dated 03.02.2021 in Reference No.4 certifies that 1590 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune and 716 posts filled by the external system have not been vacant for more than six months.
