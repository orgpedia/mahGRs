In places, schemes/ laws such as Sexual Harassment Act, Prevention of Domestic Violence Act, etc. are advertised and publicized through electronic media and print media.
The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations has launched a publicity campaign to publicize various schemes of the Department of Women and Child Development for the financial year 2020-21. Accordingly, the Directorate of Information and Public Relations had assigned the task of advertising publicity to the official agencies on its panel, taking the help of various media. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune has recommended to the concerned agencies to clear the dues of the advertisements. Accordingly, the matter of approving these payments was under consideration of the government.
