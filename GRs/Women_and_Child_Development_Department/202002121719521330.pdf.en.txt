The Proposal:
The Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 came into force from January 2016. Section 54 of the Act provides for the constitution of inspection committees at the state and district level for inspection of institutions registered and recognized under the Act. Accordingly, as per the provisions of Rule 43 of the Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018, vide reference No. 1 decision of the government. As on 21st March, 2018, the State and District level Screening Committees have been constituted. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, requested to make changes in the state level inspection committee formed under this government decision as there are some problems in its functioning. This is according to the semi-official letter dated October 3, 2019. Accordingly, the matter of changing the composition of the State Level Scrutiny Committee was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015, for the purpose of periodic inspection of Government and Non-Governmental Organisations providing residential services to children as defined in Section 2 (21) for children in conflict with law and children in need of care and protection, the following guidelines have been issued: The composition of the State Level Screening Committee constituted under the above Government decision is being changed as follows.
