Introduction: Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for payment of Central and State share of 25: 75 for the posts of Child Development Project Officer, District Programme Officer, Statistical Assistant, Headmistress/ Supervisor. Also, the remaining posts under the scheme (all posts in the commissionerate, scribes, peons, etc.) are paid 100 per cent additional state share.
Accordingly, the matter of providing funds for regular salaries of officers and employees working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, salary hike, retirement cases, payment of the first and second installment of the 7th Pay Commission was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
Regular salary, increment, retirement cases, accounts for payment of 1st and 2nd instalment of 7th Pay Commission under the head 2236-2137, 2236-2146 under the head 01-salary and 36 ancillary grants under the head 2236-2155, 2236-2164 and 2236-2173 under the head Salary. Under this Government decision, a sum of Rs. 116.40 crore has been approved for disbursement and expenditure through Budget Distribution System.
