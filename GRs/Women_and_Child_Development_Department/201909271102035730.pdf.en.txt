Government Decision: Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 174 temporary posts, which are becoming redundant but need to be continued, a total of 5479 temporary posts.
Accordingly, now the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, has sanctioned a total of 5478 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 5074 temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the figure fixed by the Central Government, 221 regular temporary posts in the district cell, 10 posts related to Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 173 temporary posts which are pending but need to be continued.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MBAA-2018/ PR No.7/ KA-7A
A total of 176330 honorarium posts of 81943 Anganwadi workers, 81943 Anganwadi helpers and 12444 Mini Anganwadi workers working under the scheme have been given to a total of 5478+176330= 181808 posts. Proposal for extension from 01.10.2019 to 29.2.2020 has been submitted vide letter dated 18.9.2019.
Under the Government Resolution dated 11.9.2019 in Reference No.9 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, State of Maharashtra, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 18.9.2019 at Reference No.8 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the above decision of the Finance Department, the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has created 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 173 additional temporary posts, which are pending but need to be continued, a total of 5478 temporary posts and a total of 176330 honorary posts of 81943 Anganwadi workers and 81943 Anganwadi helpers and 12444 mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme Extension from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 11.09.2019 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
