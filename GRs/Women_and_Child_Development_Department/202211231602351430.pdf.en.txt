The Proposal:
The International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women's Enterprise Development Project" has been approved to be implemented by the Women Economic Development Corporation (Mavim) on behalf of the Department of Women and Child Development for a period of six years from 2020-21 to 2025-26.
Approval has been given to provide a loan amount of$ 51.40 million (â‚¹ 334.10 crore) from IFAD to implement the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nav Tejaswini- Maharashtra Rural Women Entrepreneurship Development Project."
The Women's Economic Development Corporation (WEDC) has been requested to allocate a sum of Rs. 57.35 crore out of the budgeted amount of Rs. 80.00 crore for the implementation of International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women Entrepreneurship Development Project" in the budget year 2022-23. The request was made vide letter dated 13.10.2022. Accordingly, the Planning and Finance Department was requested to approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 57.35 crore. In pursuance of this proposal, the Planning Department should submit the utilization certificate of the fund of Rs. 22.65 crore released earlier, as well as how much amount has been received from IFAD for this scheme so far, how much loan is going to be received this year, how much has been communicated to IFAD or how? Comments have been made on this information. The Finance Department has also given its feedback on submission of utilization certificate of funds to the tune of Rs. 22.65 crore released earlier and has approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 10.95 crore.
