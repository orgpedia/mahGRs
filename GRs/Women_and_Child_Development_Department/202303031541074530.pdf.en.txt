GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: AHOPU-2021/ PR. No. 09/ KA-10 2. Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for this purpose. While spending the allocated funds, the guidelines issued earlier by the Planning and Finance Department, the prevailing norms, the delegation of financial powers and the instructions of the Finance Department from time to time should be followed as mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook and the Financial Rights Rulebook. Also, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune should take care to submit the utilization certificate of the funds distributed under this government decision to the government.
