Government's decision:
As per the figures fixed for the urban projects under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, a total of 32214 temporary/ honorarium posts in 104 urban projects in the regular pay scale of 1070 temporary posts and 15572 anganwadi workers and 15572 anganwadi helpers in 15572 anganwadis were extended from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 10.08.2021 in Reference No.7 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls.
3. Vacancies in regular pay scale posts and honorarium posts working under Civil Projects in the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme are not vacant for more than six months.
