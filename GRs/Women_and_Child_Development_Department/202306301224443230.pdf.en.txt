Preamble: With a view to ensure effective management of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has issued a notification vide Reference No. 1 here. It is directed to adopt the revised procedures under the memorandum dated 23.3.2021. Accordingly, the Finance Department vide No. 2 here. The circular dated 1.7.2021 provides revised instructions on Public Finance Management System (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, it has been directed to appoint a single nodal agency for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and at least a deputy secretary-level officer as nodal officer.
Accordingly, the matter of appointing a single nodal officer and a single nodal agency for the component schemes of" Shakti Sadan" in the" Shakti" application of the centrally sponsored" Mission Shakti" umbrella scheme was under consideration of the government.
