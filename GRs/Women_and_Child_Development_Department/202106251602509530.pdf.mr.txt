Government Order:
1. The State Government of Tripura, vide letter under reference at sr. no. 4 above, informed that there is no objection to deputation to the equivalent post from the Government of Maharashtra to Mrs. Mamta Shinde, Deputy Director, Information and Cultural Affairs Department Government of Tripura.
2. As per the provisions mentioned in the notification at reference no.1 above, Mrs.
Mamta Shinde is being appointed on deputation for a period of three years as Assistant Commissioner (Begging Prevention Branch), in the office of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune under the Department of Women and Child Development, Government of Maharashtra.

3. The service on deputation of the above officer will be subject to the following conditions A) The deputation service will start from the date on which the above officer will join the post of deputation. The term of deputation will be expired when she will resume in her parent department after handing over the charge of deputed post.
B) If her services are deemed necessary by the Government of Tripura in the interest of public service, the competent authority shall reserve the right to recall her services any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
C) If her services is not deemed necessary by the Government of Maharashtra. The Government of Maharashtra reserve the right to repatriate her services to Government of Tripura.
D) She will be allowed to return to the original department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government / Competent Authority stating that She intend to return to the original department.
