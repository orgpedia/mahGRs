Preamble: The Department of Women and Child Development has been mandated to provide immediate assistance to women in distress.
In pursuance of the order dated 19/8/2014, the Government has decided to launch Chief Ministers Helpline with the help of SAAR IT RESOURCES PVT. LTD. in the State with the toll free number' 181' to provide 24 hours information to women in distress. Approved by order dated 19/8/2014. This helpline has been launched in the state with the help of SAAR IT RESOURCES PVT. LTD, Mumbai.
Monthly telephone charges are paid to BSNL, Mumbai for the operation of 181 helpline. Accordingly, reference No. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune has been given approval to pay the dues from May, 2019 to March, 2020. As per the letter dated 27.02.2020, the 181 helpline for women in distress has been merged with the Chief Minister's Helpline with the consent of the Department of Women and Child Development. The aforesaid Commissioner Women and Child Development, Pune vide letter dated 10.11.2020 has directed BSNL, Mumbai to disburse the entire amount of Rs. 12,023/- (Rupees Twelve Thousand and Twenty Three only) is under consideration for approval by the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the above background, in respect of the Chief Ministers Helpline on the toll free number 181, BSNL, Mumbai has received a total amount of Rs. 12,023/- (Rupees Twelve Thousand and Twenty Three only) is being approved by the Government from the financial year 2020-21.
The expenditure will be incurred during the financial year 2020-21, under the schemes of 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, Social Support, 001 Operation and Administration, (01) (05) Awareness, Publicity, Training and Evaluation (2235-A-901). X-1, 20- Other administrative expenses to be incurred under this head.
Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune is hereby declared as Drawing, Disbursing and Controlling Officer. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune should ensure that the expenditure will be as per the rules.
This Government Decision is based on the Government Circular No.Finance-2020/ No.64/ Finance-3, dated
16.04.2020 and G.D.No.: Finance- 2020/ PR No. 65/ Finance-3 dated 04.05.2020.
