Introduction: Supplementary Nutrition Scheme under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is being implemented in rural and urban areas of the State by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. Under this scheme, hot fresh food and THR are provided to the beneficiaries in the age group of 6 months to 3 years and 3 to 6 years, underweight children, pregnant and lactating mothers in 104 urban and 449 rural projects in the state in proportion to the number of beneficiaries. The scheme is 50% funded by the central government and 50% by state governments.
