9) Commissioner, Integrated Child Welfare Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai vide letter No. Abavicio/ Camp Office/ 2022/28, d. Order dated 27.12.2022:
Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme, the Anganwadi Centres in the rural projects which are under the purview of the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) are being integrated into the urban projects and the urban projects are being restructured. The decision was approved by the government.
The government had given five days" time to implement the order. However, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, has proposed to extend the deadline as it would not be possible to implement the government decision within this period. Submitted vide letter dated 27.12.2022. Accordingly, the government has decided to implement the order. It is being extended till 15.2.2023. during this period. The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai shall ensure that the provisions of the Government Order dated 14.12.2022 are implemented by all concerned.
