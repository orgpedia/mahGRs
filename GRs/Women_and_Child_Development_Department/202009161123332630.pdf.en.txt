Government's decision:
A total of 1590 posts including 1542 on pay and 48 on honorarium in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development and a total of 2306 temporary posts including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms were extended from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020 as per the Government decision dated 13.03.2020.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 31.08.2020 in Reference No.5 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls.
The letter dated 03.09.2020 in Reference No.4 certifies that 1590 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune and 716 posts filled by external agencies have not been vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provision of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, a total of 1590 posts, including 1542 posts on salary and 48 posts on honorarium in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development Department, and 716 posts filled through external mechanisms, have been filled.
