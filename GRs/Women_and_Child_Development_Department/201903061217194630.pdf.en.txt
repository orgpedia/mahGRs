GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Figure after reviewing the posts in the field offices of the Department of Women and Child Development Government Decision, Department of Women and Child Development No.MBAA-2002/ No.No. 5.4.2003 has been approved. According to the figure, a total of 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-3 and Class-4 in the district cadre of the Women and Child Development Department of the Zilla Parishad have been removed. The government's decision 5. Continuation was approved from 1.10.2018 to 28.2.2019.
2. Under the Government Order dated 21.02.2019 in Reference No.7 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 1.03.2019 to 30.9.2019 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls.

The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune has certified that out of the total 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-III and Class-IV in the district cadre of Women and Child Development Department of Zilla Parishad, not a single post has remained vacant for more than 6 months as per the letter dated 15.1.2019 at Reference No.6. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, a total of 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-3 and Class-4 of the District Cadre of the Women and Child Development Department of the Zilla Parishad are being extended from 1.03.2019 to 30.9.2019. The cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale of these posts have been indicated in the statement.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 21/2/209 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ P.S. No. 8/16/ A.P.C.
