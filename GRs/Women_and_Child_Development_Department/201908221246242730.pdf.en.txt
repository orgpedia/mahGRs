The Proposal:
As per the circular dated 08.07.2019 issued in the context of Department of Finance, the Budgetary allocation for Compulsory and Programme expenditure for the financial year 2019-2020 covering 9 months upto December, 2020 has been approved to be released through Budget Distribution System within the limit of 60% of the total annual allocation. Accordingly, the matter was under consideration for release of funds admissible under the head 2235-Social Security and Welfare under the Women and Child Development Department.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2019-20, for meeting the expenditure for nine months upto December, 2019, Demand No. X-1 Main Accounts Title 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02 Social Welfare, 001 Operation and Administration (01) (03) Under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, the Government is approving to disburse funds to the Zilla Parishads under the head Establishment Grants (Local Area) (Compulsory) (2235-2321), 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Pay) within the limit of 60% of the budgeted funds as mentioned in column No.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune will be the Controlling Officer for this fund. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune shall strictly follow the checklist items mentioned in the circular dated 8.7.2019 in the context of Finance Department and the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing norms, delegation of financial powers etc.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune shall ensure that the funds made available on the budget distribution system under the above head for the year 2019-20 are spent and submit the utilization certificate to the Government.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the provisions of Circular No. 1 dated 8.7.2019 issued in the context of the Department of Finance.
