The Proposal:
The Department of Women and Child Development has released Central share of Rs. 7850.42 Lakhs and Rs. 5760.10 Lakhs as against Rs. 13610.52 lakh has been provided in the Budget and the Central share of Rs. 80.20 crore, State share Rs. 49.11 crore. 4.68 crore and Flexi Fund of Rs. 13.40 crore. A proposal of Rs 147.40 crore has been submitted to the Centre. The proposal is yet to be approved by the central government and the salary of the employees working under this scheme is also paid from this fund.
2. Due to the present situation of COVID-19 pandemic in the country, the Central Government is delaying the release of funds under the scheme. However, even during this epidemic, the children's homes, observation homes, etc. working under this scheme are serving as essential services in the institutions working for the care and protection of children, so the proposal submitted to the Central Government under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme for the year 2020-21 to pay the salaries of these employees within the prescribed period will be approved every year.
Government's decision:
For Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS), 20% of the budget outlay of Rs. 255.00 lakhs. This Government Decision approves the distribution to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune as indicated in Section 4.
