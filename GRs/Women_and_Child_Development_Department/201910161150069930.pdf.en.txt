Preamble: In order to inculcate brotherhood and team spirit among all the children of the state and the orphan, destitute and destitute children admitted for rehabilitation in the government/ non-government organizations working in the field of child welfare, every year in 36 districts and 6 divisional levels, in the second week of November, Chacha Nehru Bal Mahotsav is being organized. The 1. As per Government Decision dated 04.12.2012, Rs. 2,09,95000/- has been approved. In the financial year 2019-20, for organizing Chacha Nehru Bal Mahotsav for children, under the head 2235B-325, 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried), Rs. A sum of Rs. 2,15,00, 000/- has been earmarked, out of which, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse 60% of the amount disbursed by the Finance Department on BDS system to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: In order to inculcate the spirit of brotherhood and team spirit among the orphan, destitute and destitute children admitted for rehabilitation in all the children of the State and Government/ NGOs working in the field of child welfare on priority, in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision dated 04.12.2012, the Chacha Nehru Bal Mahotsav will be organized. The Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 1,29,00, 000 (Rupees One Crore, Thirty-nine Lakhs only) to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune.
Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune will be the Controlling Officer. The Controller shall make the said fund available for withdrawal to the concerned Divisional Deputy Commissioner, Women and Child Development and District Women Development Officer as per the prescribed norms and submit its utilization certificate to the Government.
