Introduction: The Government of Maharashtra. To know the background of the 3rd Women Policy announced on 01.03.2014 and to know the details of the policy announced by Hon. Minister of Women and Child Development. The meeting was held on 25.11.20. In the meeting, it was decided to constitute a drafting committee under the chairmanship of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune to review the provisions of the Women Policy 2014, make changes in it in accordance with the time and make the policy more people-oriented. Accordingly, the matter of forming a drafting committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In order to review the provisions of the Women Policy 2014 and to make it more people-oriented, the following Drafting Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune.

Functions of the Committee:- The" Drafting Committee" of the aforesaid Women's Policy will consider and recommend the following issues:
To review the provisions of the Women's Policy 2014 and make changes in it from time to time and to draft a women's policy to make it more people-oriented.
The draft policy for women should be submitted to the Government by the end of 31.12.2020.
