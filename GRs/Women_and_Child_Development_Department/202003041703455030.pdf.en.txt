Government's decision:
A total of 1590 posts including 1542 on pay and 48 on honorarium in the establishment of Women and Child Development Commissionerate and a total of 2306 temporary posts including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms were extended from 01/10/2019 to 29/02/2020 as per the Government decision dated 27.09.2020.
