Government's decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Purak Poshan under Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS), hot fresh meals and THR are distributed to beneficiaries in Anganwadis. The ratio of central and state share is 50: 50.
As per the decision of the Government, a total amount of Rs. 339.62 crore (Rupees Three Hundred and Forty-Nine Crore and Sixty-two Lakh only) out of the budgetary provision of Rs. 144.33 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Forty-four Crore and Thirty-three Lakh only) for the month of April to June, 2020 and Rs. 195.29 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Ninety-five Crore and Thirty-nine Lakh only) for the year 2019-20 for meeting the pending liabilities, is being sanctioned for this scheme during the financial year 2020-21 as per the head of accounts/ targets in Raka-2, Raka-5.
