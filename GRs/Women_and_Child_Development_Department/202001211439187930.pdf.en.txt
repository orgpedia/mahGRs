Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, implemented by the Office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services, Navi Mumbai. The Department of Finance has approved 100% spending of the budgeted funds in 2019-20 as mentioned below, Scheme wise/ head wise. Since the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, the Finance Department is advised to spend the funds in proportion to the funds of the Central Schemes when they are actually made available to the State Governments by the Central Government. The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has considered the funds received from the Central Government as well as the priority for disbursement of funds and has issued the following statement in column No. 5 The Government is approving the disbursement of the subject-wise funds of the scheme/ programme as mentioned herein to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme for spending the Central Share Fund and State Share Fund.
