Preamble: Under the Centrally Sponsored Swadhar Yojana, a new Swadhar Grih Scheme for destitute, destitute women in the State, who have been rendered homeless due to natural calamities/ domestic violence, has been launched by the Department of Women and Child Development, Government of India, vide Government Resolution dated 23rd March, 2018. Under this scheme, women in distress, women in family conflict and their children are provided food, clothing, shelter and medical assistance, legal counseling and guidance through helpline. As per the guidelines of the new" Swadhar Greh Scheme 2015" of the Central Government, the central share for this scheme has been reduced to 60% and the state share to 40%.
