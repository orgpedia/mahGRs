GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The table below is a summary of the positions in the Regional Offices of the Department of Women and Child Development approved under the Government Decision, Department of Women and Child Development No.MBAA-2002/ PR.No. 95/ K-7, dated 05.04. 2003. According to the figure, a total of 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-3 and Class-4 in the district cadre of the Women and Child Development Department of the Zilla Parishad have been removed. The continuation was approved from 1.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 as per the decision of the Government of India.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 10.08.2021 in Reference No.7 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The Deputy Commissioner (Administration), Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune has informed that out of a total of 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-3 and Class-4 in the district cadre of Women and Child Development Department of Zilla Parishad, not a single post has been vacant for more than 6 months.
