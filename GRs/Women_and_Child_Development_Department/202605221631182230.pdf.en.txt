The Proposal: -
Social, economic, legal, educational, health issues of single women in the state of Maharashtra
And the issues of rehabilitation need to be considered in detail. These women are the beneficiaries of various departmental schemes.
There is a need to suggest measures to effectively achieve and empower them. With this in mind. The Chief Minister, in his budget speech, said, "At present, all the districts of the state have single women.
The survey is ongoing and once completed, it will be properly analysed and a suitable policy for the welfare of single women will be brought out, "it has been announced. To draft a single women's policy accordingly.
The formation of a single women's drafting committee was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
Officers from various departments, single to draft a comprehensive policy for single women
Representatives and Commissioners of State NGOs working for women, Women and Child Development.
The Government is approving the constitution of a Drafting Committee to be headed by:

02. The aforesaid Drafting Committee provides for the provision of benefits of various departmental schemes to single women.
The following factors should be considered when deciding on a policy.
(1) To provide detailed benefits of the existing schemes and programmes of various departments with regard to single women.
Accordingly, a comprehensive single women's policy should be drafted.
(2) In this policy, single women will be able to implement and take advantage of various schemes.
Problems and errors should be addressed.
(3) The Committee shall submit its final draft and recommendations to the Government.
(4) The Chairman may, if necessary, appoint other Government officials to assist him in the affairs of the said Committee.
Members as well as NGOs working in the context of single women in the state.
Delegates should be nominated.
(5) The Chairman shall, if necessary, constitute a sub-committee.
The functioning and coordination of the committee will be done through the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune.
The Government decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
