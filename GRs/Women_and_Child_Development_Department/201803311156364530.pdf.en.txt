Now in the financial year 2017-18, all the systems under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme, as well as government and non-government organizations, will get Rs. 1314.69 lakhs, Demand No. The Government have approved the distribution and expenditure to be borne by the Maharashtra State Child Protection Society, Pune through the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate from the grants under heading 2235, Social Security and Welfare, 199, Assistance to other Non-Government Organizations, (00) (01) Integrated Child Protection Scheme (2235 A919), 36 Auxiliary Grants (Salaries).

The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune shall ensure that the above amount is spent for the matters covered under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme. Utilization certificates for the above provision under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme should be submitted by the Maharashtra State Child Protection Society to the Central Government and the State Government through the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune.
4. The Controlling Authority shall ensure that no expenditure is incurred in excess of the provisions made available under the Government decision. 6. This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the concurrence received under the authority of the Administrative Department through a circular in the context of the Finance Department.
