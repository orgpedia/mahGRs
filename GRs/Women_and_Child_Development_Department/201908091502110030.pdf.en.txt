Government's stand:
Subject to Government Resolution No. Section 3. (c) Mahila Shakti Kendras (Block/ Taluka level) Mahila Shakti Kendras (MSK Block) are being included in the sub-sections as under:
2. Mahila Shakti Kendras will be set up in less than half of the total selected block level committees in the district. Such Mahila Shakti Kendras will focus on creating self-employment opportunities through skilling of women by mobilizing their organizations. Priority should be given to rural women, especially to manual scavengers, bonded labourers, destitute and differently-abled women, and women rescued from prostitution.
Eligibility Criteria for NGOs: Proposals from only those NGOs which have registered on NITI Aayog's DARPAN portal will be considered. Also, the following NGOs will be considered:
An institution or association registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 or the Indian Trusts Act, 1882 (not for profit) or under specific laws.
the Co-operative Society
The Center for Agricultural Science
The institution must have existed for at least 3 years.
Special priority should be given to institutions that have taken up skill-integration initiatives for livelihood development and entrepreneurship development.
At the time of filling the application, the NGO should have at least 2 years' worth of cash/ equity net.

The applying NGO will be required to submit documentary proof of the work done for livelihood development as well as for persons belonging to disadvantaged sections. The proof of this work will be verified from the Annual Accounts and Annual Audit Report in accordance with Appendix IV.
Once the project is approved, the institute should start a livelihood training centre. The cost of training per woman will be Rs 6,000 to Rs 8,000.
The NGO will help the women members to access funds from banking and non-banking financial institutions like Rashtriya Mahila Kosh for the activities undertaken by them after they have specialized in the chosen field. This will help these women to set up marketing outlets in the local area and sell their products through e-commerce portals like Mahila e-Haat.
Selection of NGOs:
The District Collector of the concerned district will publish an advertisement for the proposal of the NGOs. Applications must be submitted within two weeks from the date of publication of the advertisement. Applications will be accepted at the District Office.
The submitted applications will be verified by District Level Task Force (DLTF) as per the checklist in Appendix.
The proposals recommended by the District Level Task Force (DLTF) will be forwarded to the State Level Task Force (SLTF) within 2 weeks from the date of receipt of the proposal.
The proposals recommended by the State Level Task Force (SLTF) should be sent to the Ministry of Women and Child Development within two weeks from the date of receipt of the proposal as per Appendix III.
A Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) will be set up under the Ministry of Women and Child Development. The committee will comprise the chairman and other members.
Financial provision for sub-component" Capacity Building of Women Groups through Mahila Shakti Kendra Scheme":
Funds for implementation of the sub-committee will be provided to voluntary organizations selected by the District Level Task Force (DLTF). The funds proposed to be provided to the NGO should be" IEC materials, Flexi Fund (as prescribed by DLTF)," including programme/ activity expenditure- meetings, workshops, training, consultancy, exchange, resource persons association, etc.
The funds will be provided by the District Collector.
