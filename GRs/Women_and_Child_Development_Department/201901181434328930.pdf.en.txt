Preamble: Writ Petition No. 1763/2018, District Inspection and Maintenance Organization, Pune v. State of Maharashtra As per the decision dated 22.6.2018, the matter of shifting of the inmates of the observation home/ children's home in Pune of the District Inspection and Maintenance Organization, Pune is under consideration. Girls belonging to the institutions of District Nursing and Maintenance Organization, Pune can be admitted to Government Observation Home/ Special Home (Girls), Mundhwa, Pune. However, this Government institution is presently recognized as an Observation Home/ Special Home and it is recommended that the institution should be recognized as a Children's Home along with the Observation Home/ Special Home. dated 26.11.2018.
2. Matimand admissions in Government Observation Home/ Special Home (Girls), Mundhwa, Pune are not allowed in Mundhwa at present as they are admitted in Government Kripa Women Hostel, Karjat, G. Raigad.
In view of the above facts and the recommendation of the Commissionerate, the matter of recognition of Government Observation Home/ Special Home (for Girls), Mundhwa, Pune as Government Observation Home/ Special Home/ Children Home was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Government Observation Home/ Special Home (Girls), Mundhwa, Pune is being recognized as Government Observation Home/ Special Home/ Children Home (Girls) under this Government Decision.
However, in case of admission in the said institution under the observation home/ special home, the accommodation of the concerned shall be maintained separately and care shall be taken by the concerned institution to ensure that the admission in the children's home and admission in the observation home/ special home is not contacted.
