The supply order should be issued subject to the following terms and conditions for providing smart Anganwadi kits.
1) The terms and conditions of the e-tender should be strictly adhered to while making the purchase. Also, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai should ensure that the supply is made as specified in the e-tender.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai shall be responsible for the expenditure of funds received from time to time in accordance with the prescribed policy and procedure.
3) The supplier should supply the above" Smart Anganwadi Kit" materials and the work of converting the Anganwadi into a model Anganwadi should be done at the Anganwadi level. The last date of supply of" Smart Anganwadi Kit" material from the supplier to the concerned to be received within the prescribed period should be made binding on the supplier.
4) The supply of" Smart Anganwadi Kit" material should be made by following the prescribed procedure from the available provision. The supplier has supplied the" Smart Anganwadi Kit" material to the Anganwadi level as per the prescribed quality, in the prescribed quantity as per the demand. This should be confirmed by the office of the Commissioner through the concerned Child Development Project Officer. Also, the supply of" Smart Anganwadi Kit" material should be stamped or labelled as supply by Government/ for use under Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be responsible for any irregularity in the distribution/ supply and expenditure of Smart Anganwadi Kit.
5) The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai should issue appropriate guidelines regarding the entry of the Smart Anganwadi Kit after it reaches the Anganwadi Centre in the Inventory Collection Register of the concerned Child Development Project Officer as well as in the register of the concerned Anganwadi Centres.
