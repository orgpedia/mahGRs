The Proposal:
The government often receives complaints of delay in payment of Shiv Bhojan scheme dues to Shiv Bhojan centre operators. In view of this, it is necessary to set a standard operating procedure in order to expedite the payment of Shiv Bhojan centers. The government was considering setting up a committee to study and submit a draft of the standard operating procedure to the government.
Government Decision: A committee is being constituted to determine the standard operating procedure for the speedy payment of the Shiv Bhojan scheme.
