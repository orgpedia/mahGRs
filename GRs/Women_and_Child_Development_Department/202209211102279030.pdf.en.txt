Read: Letter from Commissioner, Women and Child Development No. Mbawia/ Bawai/ Ka-5/ Balsam/ Grants/ 2022-23/3704, Dy. 14/06/2022 Introduction:
Demand No. 4182.35 lakh has been budgeted for disbursement of grants-in-aid under Child Care Scheme under the Department of Women and Child Development for the financial year 2022-23 under the head Ex-I, Main Accounts Head 2235, Social Security and Welfare, 02, Social Welfare, 102, Child Welfare, (15) Child Care Scheme (Care and Care), (01) Subsidiary Grants to NGOs for Child Care Scheme (Programme) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) (2235B906). The Government was considering to disburse the first half grant to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune in the financial year 2022-23 from the funds budgeted under the above head of accounts for the beneficiaries of the child care scheme from the voluntary organizations as well as the district level.
Government's decision:
Demand No. 2022-23 1, 2235, Social Security and Welfare, 02, Social Welfare, 102, Child Welfare, (15) Child Care Scheme (Care and Care), (15) (01) Subsidiary Grants to NGOs for Child Care Scheme (Programme) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary) (2235 B906) as agreed by the Finance Department under the heading of Accounts. 3634.48 lakhs (Rupees Thirty Six Crores Thirty Four Lakhs Forty Eight Thousand only) to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune for disbursement of Auxiliary Grants under Child Care Scheme.
