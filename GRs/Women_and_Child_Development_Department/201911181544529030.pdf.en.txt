Introduction: The Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) is being implemented in all the districts of the State for out of school girls in the age group of 11 to 14 years. The scheme is being implemented by the office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. Under this scheme, the ratio of central and state share of funds for expenditure other than nutrition has been fixed at 60%: 40%.
The Central share of 60% (Account Head 2236 1829) in the State Budget for the financial year 2019-20 for the expenditure on foodgrains under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) is Rs. 3,24,32, 000.00, plus State Share of 40% (Account Head 22361838) Rs. A sum of Rs. 2,16,22, 000.00 has been provided in the Budget.
The Department of Finance vide circular dated 08.07.2019 has approved the release and utilization of 60% of the budgetary provision to meet the expenditure on the schemes upto the month of December, 2019. Accordingly, the government was considering to approve the allocation and expenditure for this scheme in the budget for the financial year 2019-20.
