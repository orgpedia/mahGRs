Government's decision:
As per the figures fixed for urban projects under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, a total of 32134 temporary/ honorary posts in 104 urban projects in the regular pay scale of 1070 temporary posts and 15532 Anganwadi workers and 15532 Anganwadi helpers in 15532 Anganwadis were extended from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020.
2. Under the Government Decision dated 31.08.2020 in Reference No.7 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls.

3. Vacancies in regular pay scale posts and honorary posts working under Civil Projects in the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme are not vacant for more than six months.The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 11.09.2020 has certified the same. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the aforesaid Government Decision of the Finance Department, as per the figure fixed for urban projects under Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, a total of 31064 honorarium posts (total of all 32134 temporary/ honorarium posts) of 1070 working temporary posts in regular pay scale in 104 urban projects and 15532 Anganwadi workers and 15532 Anganwadi helpers in 15532 Anganwadis are being extended from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 31.08.2020 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
