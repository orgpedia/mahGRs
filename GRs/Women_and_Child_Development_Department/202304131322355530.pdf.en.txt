GOVERNMENT DECISION: A Child Development Advisory Committee of Government/ Non-Government members having expertise in the field of Child Development is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune to thoroughly investigate the cases of child abuse and fraud reported in the media in the last 12 months and submit a detailed report to the Government regarding the problems, measures taken thereon and other related matters.

2. The aforesaid" Child Development Advisory Committee" will consider and recommend the following: a) To study the schemes of the Central Government and the State Government regarding the issues of development of the weaker section of the society, policies, laws, welfare activities through the Department of Women and Child Development, to suggest necessary changes in them, and to recommend measures for effective implementation of these schemes.

Government Resolution No:- Committee-2022/ P.S. No. 123/ K-03
(b) The said Committee being an Advisory Committee on Child Development, no remuneration shall be admissible to the members of the Committee and their jurisdiction shall be limited to making recommendations.
The meeting of the said" Child Development Advisory Committee" will be held as per requirement.
The committee will conduct a thorough investigation into the cases of child abuse and fraud in the state in the last 12 months and submit a detailed report to the government in two months regarding the problems, measures taken and other related matters.
