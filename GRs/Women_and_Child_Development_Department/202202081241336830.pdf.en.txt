Preamble: Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru Udyog Kendra (Children's Correctional Home), Yerawada, Pune is a children's home, the buildings of which are very old and some of the buildings are in ruins. These children homes/ observation homes are functioning under the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. Also, the buildings of various government institutions under the Women and Child Development Department are being repaired, including the building repair works of eight government children homes/ observation homes, government beggary homes, etc. Renovation of hostel building at Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Administration Training, Pune is also incomplete. Hence, the government was considering providing funds for the repair and renovation of the buildings of these institutions.
Government's decision
27 minor constructions under heading 2235-2241 (mandatory) for strengthening of buildings in Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru Udyog Kendra, Yerawada, Pune under the Department of Women and Child Development, repair of buildings of various government institutions of the State of Women and Child Development Department and renovation of hostel building of Mahatma Gandhi Administrative Training Institute, Pune. 37.50 lakh is being approved for disbursement and expenditure through Budget Distribution System.
