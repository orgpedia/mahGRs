Government's decision:
As per the figures fixed for the urban projects under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, a total of 32134 temporary/ honorary posts in the regular pay scale of 1070 working temporary posts in 104 urban projects and a total of 31064 honorary posts of 15532 Anganwadi workers and 15532 Anganwadi helpers in 15532 Anganwadis were extended from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021.
2. Under the Government Order dated 15.02.2021 in Reference No.7 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the figure.

3. Vacancies in regular pay scale posts and honorary posts working under Civil Projects in the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme are not vacant for more than six months.The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 04.03.2021 has certified the same. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the aforesaid Government Decision of the Finance Department, a total of 31144 honorarium posts (total of all 32214 temporary/ honorarium posts) of 1070 working temporary posts in regular pay scale in 104 urban projects and 15572 Anganwadi workers and 15572 Anganwadi helpers in 15532 Anganwadis as per the figure fixed for urban projects under Integrated Child Development Services Scheme are being extended from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 15.02.2021 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
