The Proposal:
For the financial year 2019-20, a provision of Rs. 59,93,000/- (Rupees Sixty-nine Lakhs Ninety-nine Thousand only) has been approved for 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under head 2235B942. The reference No. 3 In the Budget, a sum of Rs. 20,25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakh Twenty Five Thousand only) has been released to the Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board for payment of salaries to the employees of the Social Welfare Board establishment up to July, 2019. The government was considering to allocate the remaining funds in the budget within the limit of 60% of the original budgetary allocation.
Government Decision: A sum of Rs. 15,70,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs Seventy Thousand only) is being disbursed towards the salary of the employees of the establishments under the Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board.
2. The expenditure incurred during the financial year 2019-20 under Demand No. Ex-1, Post-Plan, 2235, Social Security and Welfare 02 Social Welfare 102, Child Welfare (02) (04) 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the head Subsidiary Grants to Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board (2235B942).
