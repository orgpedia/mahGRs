The Proposal:
Section 41 of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 makes it mandatory for residential institutions taking care of children to have a registration certificate issued by the state government. The validity period of this certificate has been extended to 5 years. Also, the Registration Certificates issued under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2000 and the Amended Act, 2006 are deemed to be valid under the Act, 2015.
(2) The State Government may, in accordance with the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2000 and the Amended Act, 2006, provide for the establishment of residential institutions for children in the State.

Government's decision
Based on the application for registration certificate by the NGOs interested in taking care of children under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 and the report received after physical inspection of the concerned institutions, it is decided to grant registration certificate to 7 NGOs mentioned in Annexure-A under Section 41 of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 for a period of five years from the date of issue of this Government decision and the admitted capacity building of a Government institution is approved.
The recognition of registration certificate issued to the institutions in Schedule-B is being cancelled for not properly complying with the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015.
The Commissioner, Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune shall be responsible for issuing institution-wise registration certificates and taking necessary action in respect of institutions whose registration certificates have been cancelled.
Attention is drawn to the provisions of Rule 28 and particularly Rule 28 (6) of the Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018 of all the concerned institutions mentioned in Annexure-A and the concerned institutions are required to take care of compliance of the said rule.
