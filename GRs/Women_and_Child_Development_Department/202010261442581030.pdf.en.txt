As per the provisions of Section 17 and 18 of the Commission for Protection of Child Rights Act, 2005, a three-member selection committee has been constituted under the decision of the Government in this regard to make recommendations for appointment to the post of Chairperson and six non-official members of the Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights.
Since the term of office of Chairperson and five non-official members of the Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights has expired on 19/05/2020, the Government was considering to constitute a three-member Selection Committee to recommend to the Government for appointment to these posts.
Government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights (MSPCR) has constituted a three-member selection committee to recommend candidates for the posts of chairperson and members of the commission.
