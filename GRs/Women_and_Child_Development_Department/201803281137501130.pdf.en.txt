Government's decision:
A total of 1591 posts including 1543 on pay and 48 on honorarium in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development and 716 posts filled through external mechanisms, a total of 2307 such temporary posts were extended from 1/1/2018 to 28/2/2018 as per the Government decision dated 7.2.2018 at Reference No.3.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 5.3.2018 in Reference No.5 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 1.3.2018 to 30.9.2018 for the temporary posts included in the figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The letter dated 28.2.2018 in Reference No.4 certifies that 1591 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune and 716 posts filled by the external agencies have not been vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, in accordance with the provisions of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, a total of 1591 posts, including 1543 posts on salary and 48 posts on honorarium in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development Department, and a total of 2307 temporary posts, including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms, are being extended from 1.3.2018 to 30.9.2018.
Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
