6) These appointments are being made subject to verification of character and medical examination report of the candidates.
7) The candidates who are working in the government service before being appointed to this post, their appointment is being made subject to the inspection of confidential records.
(8) The candidates shall not be allowed to remarry while one wife/ husband is alive. They should also submit the certificate of small family as per the Government Circular, General Administration Department No-SRV 2000/ PR No. 17/2000/ 12, dated 1st July, 2005.
9) The candidates are required to declare their residence within six months from the date of appointment. After that, if they declare their hometown, it cannot be changed.
10) Candidates selected to the posts reserved for Backward Classes who have not submitted the Caste Validity Certificate are required to submit the Caste Validity Certificate to the Government within a period of six months from the date of their appointment as per the provisions of Government Decision, General Administration Department, No. BCC 2011/ PR No. 1064/2011/16 B, dated 12.12.2011.
11) If the candidate's caste validity certificate mentions the issue of validity certificate subject to the fulfilment of certain technical issues, it will be mandatory for the candidates to fulfil those technical issues immediately. All the candidates should note that any delay in this will lead to the cancellation of the candidate's appointment to the said post.

12. Appointment of selected candidates to the posts reserved for sportspersons is being made subject to the report of the Director, Directorate of Sports and Youth Affairs, Pune.
13) The candidates selected against the posts reserved for the disabled are being appointed subject to the report of the Commissioner (Disabilities), Social Welfare Commissioner, Pune.
14) It is mandatory for the appointed candidates to pass the Hindi/ Marathi language examination prescribed by the government within the stipulated time.
15) The candidates who have been appointed as above are required to pass the departmental examination conducted by the department within a period of 2 years of the probation period as per the service admission rules.
If the probationary officer does not achieve the expected standard of work or does not pass the prescribed departmental written examination within the probation period or his conduct is found to be improper, suspicious, he/ she shall be eligible for reduction from service without any prior notice.
17) If the candidates want to resign from their service, they must give at least 1 month's advance notice to the Government as per the provision in the Government Circular General Administration Department, No.NRV 1077/ XII, dated 20.5.1977. Also, if the service of this candidate is to be terminated, then such a one-month advance notice will also be given by the government to the concerned officer. However, the government will not be obliged to give notice to the candidate at the time of termination of service for reasons such as indiscipline, violation of service rules, etc. However, action will be taken subject to changes in these rules from time to time while giving notice to the concerned.
18) The new defined contribution pension scheme will be applicable to the officers/ employees who are appointed to the government service on or after November 1, 2005, as per the provisions of Government Decision, Finance Department, No-Anio- 1005/126/ Service-4, dated 31.10.2005.
The existing pension scheme i. e. Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 and Maharashtra Civil Services (Cessation of Pension) Rules, 1984 and the existing General Provident Fund Scheme will not be applicable to these candidates. However, the candidates are government employees. The Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982 and the General Provident Fund Scheme shall be applicable to those who are employed in Government service in a pensionable establishment before 1st November, 2005 subject to fulfilment of the conditions mentioned in the Government Circular, Finance Division No.-Anio 1006/87/ Services, dated 12.01.2007.
19) The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune should immediately submit a report to the government regarding the candidates who will appear in the service.
