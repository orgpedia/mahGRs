Under the Government Resolution dated 21.2.2018 in Reference No.9 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, State of Maharashtra, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 22.2.2019 at Reference No.8 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the above decision of the Finance Department, a total of 5,479 temporary posts, including 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 174 additional but necessary temporary posts, and a total of 1,76,897 honorary posts of 8,1943 Anganwadi workers, 8,1943 Anganwadi helpers and 13011 mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. The period is being extended from 1.3.2019 to 30.9.2019.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 21.2.2019 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
