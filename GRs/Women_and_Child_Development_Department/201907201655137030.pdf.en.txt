The Proposal:
As per the circular issued by the Department of Finance, after the monthly budgetary provisions for the financial year 2019-20 are reported by the administrative departments on the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEMS), the budgetary allocations for the first nine months of 2019-20 (ending December, 2019) will be made on the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEMS) within the limit of 60% of the total annual allocation.
For the financial year 2019-20, a provision of Rs. 10,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crore only) has been approved for 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) under head 2235-3177.
As per the circular of the Finance Department under the head 2235-3177, out of the funds available for non-salary matters, a proposal was under the consideration of the Government to disburse a sum of Rs. 3,81,22, 444/- (Rupees Three Crores Eighty One Lakh Twenty Two Thousand Four Hundred and Forty Four only) for the Counselling Centre Scheme as demanded by the Deputy Commissioner (Women Development), Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune.
Government's decision:
As per the circular issued by the Finance Department, out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 10,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores only) for the Counselling Centre Scheme for the financial year 2019-20, the Government hereby approves the release of Rs. 3,81,22, 444/- (Rupees Three Crores Eighty One Lakh Twenty Two Thousand Four Hundred Forty Four only) as demanded by the Deputy Commissioner (Women Development), Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune, to meet the expenditure from April, 2019 to March, 2020 on the remuneration of the Coordinators and Counsellors working in the Counselling Centre as well as the administrative expenses and training of the Counselling Centre.
