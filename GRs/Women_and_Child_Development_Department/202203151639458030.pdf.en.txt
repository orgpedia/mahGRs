Preamble: PFMS to be paid to all Anganwadi workers, helpers and mini Anganwadi workers in the State
Orders regarding the introduction of the procedure of direct deposit in the bank account of the maid/ helpers through the system from the year 2017 are under reference No. 1 has been issued by the Government. However,
Due to technical difficulties, some Anganwadi workers, helpers and mini Anganwadi workers have lost their jobs in April, 2021.
It has not been possible to pay the honorarium through PFMS system from the commissionerate level. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, has decided to provide anganwadis in the financial year 2021-22.
The honorarium of maids/ helpers could not be paid through PFMS due to technical reasons.
