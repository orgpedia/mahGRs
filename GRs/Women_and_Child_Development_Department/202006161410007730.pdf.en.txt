Preamble: The Women's Economic Development Corporation was established under the Companies Act, 1956. It was established on February 24, 1975. Considering the importance of women's overall development and the expertise of the corporation in this work, Government Decision No. Mavim-2001/ No. 10/ Q-10, D. As on 20th January, 2003, the Corporation has been declared as a State Level" apex body" for women development. As per the provisions of Rule 85 (1) and Rule 66 (1) of the Constitution and Establishment Rules of the Corporation, the matter of cancellation of appointments made under the Government decision in respect of the post of Vice-Chairman and Member of the Corporation was under consideration of the Government.
