From the above fund, the pending grants for the period 2015-16 and 2016-17 should be disbursed to the children's homes run by government and non-government organizations in all the districts except six districts of Beed, Nanded, Latur, Osmanabad, Ahmednagar and Parbhani. Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune should ensure that while disbursing the pending funds, the assessment is finalized and no new pending funds are demanded from the remaining districts except the above 6 districts.
3. The Government shall forthwith submit to the Government, in the prescribed format, the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed under the said Government decision.
4. This Government Resolution was issued by the Finance Department, Informal Reference No. 158/ Expenditure-6, D. being issued pursuant to the consent given by that Department under 09/10/2020.
