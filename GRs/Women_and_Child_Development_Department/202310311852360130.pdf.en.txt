The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 has come into force with effect from 01.07.2006.
As per the provisions of Section 4 (1), 4 (2) and 4 (3) of the said Act, the administrative transfers of transferable officers of Group-B cadre of District Women and Child Development Officer/ Child Development Project Officer (Rural)/ Superintendent, Government Institutions/ Inspectors, Certified Schools and Institutions and similar posts under the department with the approval of the competent authority shall be ordered. It was issued on 30.6.2023. Accordingly, the following officers are being posted as shown in Column No. 4 against their names mentioned in the table below, subject to the approval of the competent authority by the respective senior authorities, by making partial changes in their postings.

(2) The order shall come into force immediately. The concerned officers should immediately join the place of partial transfer and submit a report to the government in the prescribed way.
Confidential reports, service books, and final salary certificates of the above officers should be sent immediately to the new office where they have been transferred.
The above officers shall not be granted any leave without prior permission or their application for leave shall not be accepted by the parent office as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981.
(5) While appointing the above officers to the post of transfer, it should be verified by the office deputing them on deputation as to how they were on leave/ absence.
If the officer is absent on medical grounds, he should be sent before the medical board before being produced. If they were found to be on leave, they should apply for leave without fail.
