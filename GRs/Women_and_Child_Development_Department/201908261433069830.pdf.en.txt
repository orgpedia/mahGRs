Preamble: The Women's Economic Development Corporation was established under the Companies Act, 1956. It was established on February 24, 1975. Considering the importance of women's overall development and the expertise of the corporation in this work, Government Decision No. Mavim-2001/ P.S. 10/ K-10, D. As on 20th January, 2003, the Corporation has been declared as a State Level" apex body" for women development. The appointment of the members of the Corporation was under the consideration of the Government as per the provisions of Rule 66 (1) of the Articles of Incorporation and the Establishment Rules of the Corporation.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following persons are being appointed as members of the Women's Economic Development Corporation under this Government Decision.
Shailja Garje, Ashti Member 11) Sou. Archana Dehankar, Nagpur Member
The appointment shall be for a period of three years from the date of issue of this order or until further orders of the Government, whichever is earlier.
