Preamble to the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. The Juvenile Justice Committee under the High Court of Bombay had suggested to the Government to develop a comprehensive computer system for effective implementation of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. Accordingly, a computerized system has been created to make all the activities online, collect information, control information in line with the effective implementation of the Juvenile Justice Act. Conflict of law in the present system
Two separate modules have been developed for the Juvenile Justice Board and Child Welfare Committee to work out the mechanism for children and children in need of care and protection respectively.
