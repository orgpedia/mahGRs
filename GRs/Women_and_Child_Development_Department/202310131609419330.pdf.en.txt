Preamble: The Central Government is implementing the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Mission Shakti." 1 here. The Guidelines issued vide letter dated 14.07.2023 vide Reference No. 2 here. The Government Circular dated 03.01.2023 has been implemented in the State from the financial year 2022-23.
As per the guidelines, an" Apex Committee" has been constituted under the chairmanship of Secretary, Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, to regularly review the implementation of the scheme with the State Governments and monitor the quality.
Keeping in view the guidelines of the Central Government's" Mission Shakti," the Ministry of Rural Development is implementing the scheme, controlling the schemes and preparing the annual action plan for the State under various components of the schemes. Under Mission Shakti, the Government was contemplating to constitute a State Level Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary and a Project Management Committee under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Department of Women and Child Development at the State level on the lines of Project Sanctioning Board of the Central Government.
Government Decision: Taking into consideration the guidelines of the Central Government's scheme" Mission Shakti," the Ministry has decided to implement the scheme, regulate the schemes and approve the annual action plan for the State under various components of the schemes. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary.
