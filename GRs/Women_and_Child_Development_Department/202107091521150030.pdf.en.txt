3. District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of the said fund to Women Economic Development Corporation.

5. Payments made under the signature of District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban and counter-signed by Deputy Secretary, Women and Child Development Department should be submitted to the Adhiveshan and Accounts Officer.
