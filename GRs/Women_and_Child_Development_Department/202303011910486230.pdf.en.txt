Introduction: Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme program under the Department of Women and Child Development in the state of Maharashtra
Women who have registered their pregnancy in the government primary health centre/ government hospital and are going to give birth there, as well as the name registered in the government primary health centre/ government hospital.
However, in exceptional cases, women admitted for delivery in private hospitals for some reason are provided with a" Baby Care Kit" for their newborns (boy or girl) at the time of first delivery. Accordingly, the Government was considering to release funds to pay the dues of baby care kits supplied in the budget year 2022-23 for this purpose.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 21.06.2022 under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) Programme under the State Scheme of" Baby Care Kits" under the Department of Women and Child Development, a total amount of Rs. 16.80 crore (Rupees Sixteen Crores Eighty Lakhs only) as shown in Racket No. 4 for the headings/ targets in Racket No. 1 of the following prospectus is being sanctioned for payment of the" Baby Care Kits" supplied during the financial year 2022-23.
