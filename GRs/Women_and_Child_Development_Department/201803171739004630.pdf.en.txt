Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a centrally sponsored scheme of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, implemented by the Office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services, Navi Mumbai.
The decision to increase the honorarium given to Anganwadi workers/ helpers/ mini Anganwadi workers with effect from 1st October, 2017 has been taken as per the decision of the Government dated 23rd February, 2018. A supplementary demand proposal for payment of enhanced honorarium amounting to Rs. 12607.00 lakh was presented in the March-2018 session. The supplementary demand proposal has been approved and the Department of Finance has accorded approval to spend the said funds vide Government Circular dated 17th March, 2018. Accordingly, the Government is approving the release of funds to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai to the extent of Rs. 12607.00 lakh as follows:
