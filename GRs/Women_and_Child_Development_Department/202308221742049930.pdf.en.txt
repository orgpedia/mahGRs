Preamble: Guidelines issued by the Central Government under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Mission Shakti" vide Government Circular dated 14 th July, 2022. 03.01.2023 have been implemented in the State from the financial year 2022-23. Under the scheme, the component schemes are implemented for the safety and protection of women under" SAMBHAL" Appropriation in the ratio of 100% Central Share and 60:40 Centre: State Share for" SHAKTI" Appropriation. The government was considering opening of new heads of accounts for all components of the Centrally Sponsored Umbrella Scheme" Mission Shakti."
