Preamble:- The" Scheme for Welfare of Working Children" for children in need of care and protection sponsored by the Central Government was being implemented in various districts of the State. Under the said scheme, the beneficiaries such as child labourers, out of school children, mules working on vehicle repair/ garage, children working as housemaids, etc. are brought into the mainstream of the society by providing non-formal education, business and training. Under this scheme, during the training period, the beneficiaries of the institute are provided with facilities like snacks, medical facilities, entertainment, sports facilities etc. And they're being restored. Under the scheme, the central government and the concerned NGOs share 90:10 and the state government used to recommend the concerned institutions to the central government.
Accordingly, the Centre was directly disbursing the grant to the concerned institution in two installments (Rs 4,65,680/- each).
However, as per Reference No. 2 letter, the scheme has been discontinued with effect from 1st April, 2017. Also Reference No. The grant-in-aid (total Rs. 1,67,83, 128/-) to the institutions functioning during the year 2016-2017 has been made available to the State Government for disbursement at the State level. Accordingly, the Central Govt. Payable to 18 institutions in the received list out of 4 (Total Rs. 1,67,83, 128/-) was under consideration of the Government for disbursement to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune under the head 2235 C 116. The government has taken the following decisions:

Government Decision No: WWC-2018/ Q.S. No. 01/ Q. 03 Government Decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Scheme for Welfare of Working Children," 18 institutions in Form-A attached under head 2235 C 116 are required to pay a total amount of Rs. 1,67,83, 128/ (Rs. One crore, six hundred and sixty lakh, eighty three thousand, one hundred and twenty eight only) is being approved by the Government to be distributed to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune.
Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune is being declared as Controller cum Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the above purpose. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune shall disburse the said funds as per the prescribed norms. Also, the utilisation certificate of the fund should be submitted to the government.
Terms and Conditions of Grant Distribution:- The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune shall be bound to follow the following conditions and comply with the guidelines and prescribed provisions of the Central Government while disbursing the funds of the above scheme.
1.The eligibility criteria must be fulfilled in accordance with the Child Labour Welfare Scheme.
The project should be working in the same area as mentioned in the guidelines.
3. The components for which subsidy has been sanctioned. should be used for the same items.
4. Utilization certificate of previous grant will be required to be submitted.
The guidelines of NITI Aayog's NGO PS-Portal should be followed.
The amount of grant sanctioned under this scheme should be deposited directly through ECS (online) in the bank account of the concerned NGO from the level of Commissioner, Women and Child Development.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune shall ensure that the list of NGOs sanctioned grants under this scheme is made available to the Government within one week of the issuance of the Government decision. Also, the utilization certificate and details of expenditure should be submitted to the government by keeping the accounts separately.
While disbursing the funds due to the institutions under the Child Labour Welfare Scheme, it should be ensured that the institutions do not make funds available for the same beneficiaries from other schemes.
9. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development shall ensure that the remaining funds are repatriated by disbursing the funds payable to the said institutions as per the actual expenditure.
The expenditure should be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2018-19 under the head Demand No.X-1, 2235, Social Security and Welfare, 02, Social Welfare, 199 Assistance to Non-Governmental Organizations, (00) (04) Child Labour Welfare Scheme (Central Share 90%), (2235 C 116) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary).
