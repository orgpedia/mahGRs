The Proposal:
43 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai will be filled up with effect from 01.03.2022. The extension was granted vide Government Resolution dated 21.03.2022 at Reference No.4 to continue till 31.08.2022. Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, State of Maharashtra, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 19.07.2022 at Reference No.5 has submitted a proposal for extension of 43 temporary posts.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 18.08.2022 in Reference No.6 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.

The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, State of Maharashtra, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 19.07.2022 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, the matter of extension of temporary posts on the establishment of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government have decided to extend the existing 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023. The details of the posts along with the pay scale and cadre-wise details are given in the prospectus.
The said Government Order is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Order dated 18.08.2022 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
