The Proposal:
For the implementation of the schemes implemented by the Women and Child Development Department, a total of 6 Divisional Deputy Commissioner Offices, District Women and Child Development Officer Office in each district, Child Development Project Officer (Civil) Office in each district are functioning at different places/ locations in the state. Various schemes, acts, government decisions, circulars etc. implemented by the Women and Child Development Department cause many difficulties in reaching the benefits of government schemes to the public and making information about the schemes available to the general public. Considering all these aspects, the government was considering to start" Mahila and Bal Vikas Bhawan" at the district level to make information about all the schemes of the Women and Child Development Department available to the public at one place.
Government's decision:
In order to make information about all the schemes of the Department of Women and Child Development available to the public at one place," Mahila and Bal Vikas Bhawan" has been started on a pilot basis at Zila Parishad locations in all the districts from 15th August 2020. A meeting of the officers/ staff of the department has been arranged in this Women and Child Development Bhawan to provide immediate appropriate services/ guidance to the affected women and their accompanying children under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005 and the beneficiaries/ citizens of various schemes being implemented by the department. Through them, information about schemes and acts of the Department of Women and Child Development is provided, services are provided/ guided. The district programme officer, representative of the district women and child development officer, staff of the Women Economic Development Corporation, representative of the State Women's Commission, etc. have been appointed in this office.
