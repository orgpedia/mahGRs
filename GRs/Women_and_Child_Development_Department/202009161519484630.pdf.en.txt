Government Decision: Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 173 temporary posts that are becoming redundant but need to be continued.
Accordingly, now the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, has sanctioned a total of 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 temporary posts which are pending but need to be continued. A total of 5472 temporary posts and a total of 176897 honorarium posts of 81943 Anganwadi workers and 81943 Anganwadi helpers and 13011 mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. Proposal for extension of the period from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 has been submitted vide letter dated 11.09.2020.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MBAA-2020/ P.No.16/ K-7A
Under the Government Resolution No.9 of 31.08.2020, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls.
The Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 11.09.2020 at Reference No.8 has certified that the said posts are not lying vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has created 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 additional temporary posts which are due to be continued but need to be continued. The period is being extended from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 31.08.2020 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
