A total of 5,494 temporary posts, including 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department and 189 temporary posts which are due to be added but need to be continued, as well as a total of 1,76,595 honorary posts of 8,1943 Anganwadi workers, 8,1943 Anganwadi helpers and 12,709 mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme were extended from 1/3/2018 to 30/9/2018 under the Government decision dated 31.3.2018.
Accordingly, now the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, has sanctioned 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme.

A total of 5494 temporary posts and a total of 1,76,630 honorarium posts of 81943 Anganwadi workers, 81943 Anganwadi helpers and 12744 Mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme have been proposed to be continued as per the letter dated 21.9.2018.

Under the Government Order dated 7.9.2018 in Reference No.9 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls.
The Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 21.9.2018 at Reference No.8 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the above decision of the Finance Department, a total of 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 189 additional temporary posts, which are pending but need to be continued.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments by the Government Order dated 7.9.2018 in Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
