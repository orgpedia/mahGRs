Introduction: Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, health, nutrition, pre-primary education are provided. For this, it is necessary to convert the existing Anganwadi Centre into a model Anganwadi Centre. In these centers, children will be provided pre-school education, health, nutrition in a pleasant atmosphere. Also, adolescent girls and women can be trained under skill development programmes. For this, in the current financial year 2017-18, in the first phase, 5000 Anganwadis in the state and every year thereafter, in a phased manner, the quality of Anganwadis and their infrastructure will be improved and they will be converted into model Anganwadis.
