Preamble: To increase the rent of Anganwadi Centre buildings in urban and rural/ tribal areas under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Strengthening and Restructuring of Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS). The Department of Women and Child Development, Government of India vide Government Resolution No. 307/ Q.6 dated 15th May, 2017 has granted permission for the same.
However, since the terms and conditions mentioned in the said Government Order regarding the provision of basic facilities in the Anganwadis functioning on rent under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme need to be amended, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, has issued a notification under reference No. Proposals 3 and 4 have been submitted.
The Central Government has revised the rates of various aspects of Anganwadi services. 2 Approval has been granted pursuant to the Government Resolution dated 30th June, 2018. Accordingly, in pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, vide reference No. The Government was contemplating to issue a revised Government Resolution regarding increase in the rent of Anganwadi Centre building in urban, rural and tribal areas under the Centrally Sponsored Integrated Child Development Services Scheme by superseding the Government Resolution dated 15th May, 2017.
Government Decision: Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme, the Anganwadi Centre in urban and rural/ tribal areas will increase the building rent. The Government approval is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions by superseding the Government Resolution dated 15th May, 2017.
