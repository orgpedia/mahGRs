District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of the said fund to Women Economic Development Corporation.
5. Payments made under the signature of District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban and counter-signed by Deputy Secretary, Women and Child Development Department should be submitted to the Adhiveshan and Accounts Officer. The accounts officer and the creditor must pay the amount by cheque. The Deputy Secretary, Women and Child Development Department has been authorised to counter-sign the payment submitted by the District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban.
After disbursement to the Women's Economic Development Corporation (WEDC), the amount should be reimbursed by the Central Government from the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD).
7. The funds distributed under the said Government Decision to be spent during the current financial year and the funds remaining unspent. The dedication before 31.3.2023 should be done by the Managing Director, Women's Economic Development Corporation, Bandra, Mumbai.
8. This Government decision is an informal reference to Planning Department No. 45/1472, dt. 15.2.2023 and the Finance Department's Informal Reference No. 73/ Expenditure-6, D. being issued as per the concurrence given by the departments under 10.3.2023.
