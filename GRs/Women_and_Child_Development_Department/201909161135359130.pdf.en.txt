Reference: Commissioner, Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune Letter dated 4.7.2019 No. Mbawia/ Construction/ Ka- 10/2019-20/ 3375
Preamble: Minor girls rescued under the Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act, 1956 at the Government Girls Special Rehabilitation Centre, Deonar, Mumbai, are admitted for care and protection as per the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015, by order of the Child Welfare Committee.
Therefore, it is necessary to build a strong and high protective wall around the institution for the protection of girls. Accordingly, the proposal submitted by the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune, for the construction of the 348-metre-long and 4.20-metre-high RCC retaining wall under the reference letter is under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune has released Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 99,69,030/- (Rupees Ninety-nine Lakhs Seventy-nine Thousand Thirty only).
