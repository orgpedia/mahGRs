Government's decision
The Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 250.00 lakhs (Rupees Two Crore Fifty Lakh only) to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai for the scheme of Village Child Development Centre (VCDC) for severely malnourished children in non-tribal areas established to tackle the problem of malnutrition in the State, as per the heading/ target for the scheme (5) in Raka (2) of the return.
