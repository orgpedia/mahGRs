A total of 176761 honorarium posts of 81875 Anganwadi workers and 81875 Anganwadi helpers and 13011 Mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme have been filled up. The extension of the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 was granted vide Government Order dated 30.08.2022.
Accordingly, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, has sanctioned a total of 5464 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 162 temporary posts which are becoming redundant but need to be continued, and a total of 176761 honorary posts of 81875 Anganwadi workers and 81875 Anganwadi helpers and 13011 mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. A proposal for extension of the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 has been submitted to the Government vide letter dated 13.02.2023.

Under the Government Order dated 08.02.2023 in Reference No.9 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls.
The Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 13.02.2023 at Reference No.8 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services scheme, regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the central government, temporary posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and additional temporary posts that are pending but need to be continued, as well as the honorarium posts of Anganwadi workers and Anganwadi helpers and mini Anganwadi workers under the Integrated Child Development Services scheme were under consideration of the government.
