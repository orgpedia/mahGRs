GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: CHISO-2017/ PR No.114/ WORK-8 dated 6th July, 2017
Preamble: In order to address the issues of frequent visits to various institutions under the jurisdiction of The Children's Aid Society, Mumbai, and to attend meetings from time to time in connection with the proposed development of the institution in liaison with other authorities concerned, the matter was transferred to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai vide Government Resolution No.
Since the Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) has to do the work of National Nutrition Mission, Line Listing and Aadhaar Linking on priority, it is not possible to pay enough attention to the work of The Children's Aid Society, Mumbai, so the matter was under consideration of the government to transfer the work to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Maharashtra State Pune.
Government's decision:
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: CHISO-2017/ PR No. 114/ ACTIVITY-8, dated 6th July, 2017, vide Government Order, all the matters relating to The Children's Aid Society, Mumbai are being transferred to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune will henceforth look after the affairs of all children's homes under the Children's Aid Society, Mumbai as before. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune should take care of the following aspects regarding the functioning of the institution.
a) Take immediate action and inform the government from time to time about the encroachments on the strategic places of the institution and the measures to be taken.
