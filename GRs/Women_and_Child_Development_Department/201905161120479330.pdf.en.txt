The Proposal:
Finance Department No. In Circular No. 1, all the departments have been informed that the Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill, 2019 has been passed by the Legislature to meet the expenditure for four months (April, 2019 to July, 2019) for the financial year 2019-20. Accordingly, the Department of Finance, Department of Women and Child Development has released funds on Budget Distribution System (BDS) for meeting the expenditure for four months from April, 2019 to July, 2019 for the year 2019-20 under Demand No. The matter of administrative approval to disburse and spend the fund to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of the funds of the schemes in Annexure-A, Pillar-2 attached with the circular No. 1, under reference to the Finance Department and the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune should take care of the following while spending these funds.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall strictly follow the guidelines mentioned in paragraphs 2 to 17 in the circular No. 1, dated 01.04.2019, issued by the Finance Department in this regard and the prevailing norms, delegations of financial authority issued by the Planning Department and the Finance Department.
While spending the funds for the year 2019-20, the rules mentioned in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook and the Financial Powers Rulebook and the orders, instructions, circulars, terms and conditions issued by the Finance and Planning Department from time to time in this regard should be strictly followed.
