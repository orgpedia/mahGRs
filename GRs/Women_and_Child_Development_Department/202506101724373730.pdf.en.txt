Read - Department of Women and Child Development, Government Decision No. Baha-2006 / Q. No. 138 / Ka-3, D. 24/07/2007.
Preamble: - The Commission for Protection of Child Rights, Central Government, under the provisions of sections 17 and 18 of the Act, 2005, has constituted a three-member Selection Committee under the relevant Government Decision to make recommendations for appointment to the post of Chairman and 6 non-official members of the Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights. The composition of this selection committee is as follows.
Hon'ble Minister, Women and Child Development - President (Posted)
Hon'ble Minister of State, Women and Child Development - Member (ex-officio)
Noted social worker member in the field of children
With the term of the current chairperson and 6 non-official members of the Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights coming to an end, the government was considering appointing a designated social worker in the field of children as the third member on a three-member selection committee to select the chairperson and members on the commission.
Government Decision: - To make recommendations for appointment to the posts of Chairman and Members of the Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights. Shri Vikas Raoji Sawant is being included as a member on the three-member selection committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 24/07/2007.
The term of office of Shri Vikas Raoji Sawant, Member, on the said Committee shall automatically come to an end on the constitution of the Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights (with the appointment of Chairman and Members on the Commission).
The government decision is available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharashtra.gov.in.
