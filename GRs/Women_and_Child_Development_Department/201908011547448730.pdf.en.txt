The Proposal:
As per the circular issued by the Department of Finance, after the monthly budgetary provisions for the financial year 2019-20 are reported by the administrative departments on the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEMS), the budgetary allocations for the first nine months of 2019-20 (ending December, 2019) will be made on the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEMS) within the limit of 60% of the total annual allocation.
For the financial year 2019-20, a total amount of Rs. 3,17,57, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Seventeen Lakhs Fifty Seven Thousand only) has been sanctioned for 36 Auxiliary Anusandhan (Salary) items and Rs. 5,66,50, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores Sixty Six Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) for 31 Auxiliary Anusandhan (Non-Salary) items under head 2235-2161. As per the circular of the Finance Department under the head 2235-2161, the proposal was under the consideration of the government to disburse to the Maharashtra State Women's Commission within the limit of 60% of the funds available for salary and non-salary matters.
