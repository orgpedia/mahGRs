The proposal-
The Maharashtra State Women's Commission was set up by the state government. It was established on January 25, 1993. Section 17 of the Maharashtra State Commission for Women Act, 1993 provides that the accounts of the Maharashtra State Commission for Women shall be audited annually by the office of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai. Section 19 (3) of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India (Duties, Powers and Conditions) Act, 1971 provides for the terms and conditions for conducting an independent audit of the accounts of the Maharashtra State Commission for Women every year.
Maharashtra State Commission for Women No. 3 The letter herein requests for the audit of the Maharashtra State Commission for Women by the Comptroller and Auditor General of India with the concurrence of the Accountant General and for the issuance of such orders. The Maharashtra State Women's Commission was to be audited annually by the Comptroller and Auditor General of India.
Government's decision
The Government of Maharashtra has decided to conduct annual audit of the accounts of the Maharashtra State Women's Commission from 2018-19 onwards through the Accountant General, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
