Introduction: Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme program under the Women and Child Development Department, the name of the pregnant woman registered in the government primary health center/ government hospital in the state of Maharashtra and
The name of the women who give birth there as well as the government primary health center/ government hospital has been registered but, in exceptional cases, the private hospital for some reason.
Women who are admitted for delivery are provided with a" baby care kit" for their newborns (boy or girl) at the time of first delivery. Accordingly, the Government was considering to release funds to pay the dues of baby care kits supplied in the budget year 2022-23 for this purpose.
Government Decision:" Baby Care" under Integrated Child Development Services Scheme Programme under Department of Women and Child Development
