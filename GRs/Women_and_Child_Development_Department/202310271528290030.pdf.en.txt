Preamble: Guidelines issued by the Government of India vide letter dated 14th July, 2022 regarding implementation of Centrally Sponsored Umbrella Scheme" Mission Shakti." 03.01.2023 have been implemented in the State from the financial year 2022-23.
The Government has decided to implement the component scheme of" Women Empowerment Centre" under the Centrally Sponsored Umbrella Scheme of" Mission Shakti." Approval has been granted as on 26.07.2023. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments. The government was considering opening a new account head for this fund in the budget.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Approval is being given for opening of new head of accounts for budgeting of funds for Women Empowerment Centre component under the Centrally Sponsored Umbrella Scheme of Mission Shakti namely SHAKTI
