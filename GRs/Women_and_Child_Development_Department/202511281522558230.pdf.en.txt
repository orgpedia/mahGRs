According to the government notification dated 21.06.2021 of the General Administration Department, the service availability list is required to be published every year with 1 January as the base date.
The date 01.01.2015 of the officers serving as Deputy Commissioner of Group-A Cadre under the office of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai is at the end of the provisional service availability list at No. 2 was published by the Government Circular here.
If there are any errors or actual errors in this list of services or if there are any errors with respect to this list of services, the concerned officer should submit a detailed statement in writing to the government within 15 days along with the appropriate documents. The aforesaid Government Circular had stated that the statement received by the Government in this manner would be considered in order to take an appropriate decision and the Service Eligibility List would be published in the final form thereafter.
Regarding this list of services, Shri Sanjay Mane said. An objection has been lodged in the letter dated 15.05.2025.
With this objection rejected, the date of completion of the last date of posting of officers in the rank of Deputy Commissioner is being published as Annexure-I respectively along with the final merit list.
