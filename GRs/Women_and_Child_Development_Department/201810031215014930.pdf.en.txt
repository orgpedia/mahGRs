Preamble: The Department of Women and Child Development is organizing a State Level Fair on 14th November, 2017 at Balewadi, Pune on the occasion of National Children's Day, jointly organized by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services, Navi Mumbai and the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune. However, due to lack of time to organize the program, the program was to be celebrated on 3rd January 2018, on the occasion of Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule Jayanti, at Balewadi, Pune under the name of My daughter Bhagyashree Awareness Campaign-State Level Fair-2018. The Department of Finance has approved to limit the expenditure on this programme to Rs. 352.58 crore and to withdraw the funds on short payment basis.
My Daughter Bhagyashree Awareness Campaign- State Level Meet-2018 program has been planned to be organized by putting up pendol in about 2 lakh sq.m. area of Balewadi Sports Complex. An event management committee was appointed to manage the event. Accordingly, approval for publication of e-tender was accorded to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated November 29, 2017. The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai had submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 22/12/2017 for appointment of Angle Advertising, Mumbai as Event Management with L1 fixed by issuing E-Tender.
The Government had approved the expenditure of Rs. 1,24,88, 680.00 (Rupees One Crore Twenty Four Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Six Hundred Eight only) for the purpose of organizing the program of My Daughter Bhagyashree Awareness Campaign State Level Fair-2018 at Balewadi, Pune on the occasion of Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule Jayanti on 3rd January 2018 by the Department of Women and Child Development.
