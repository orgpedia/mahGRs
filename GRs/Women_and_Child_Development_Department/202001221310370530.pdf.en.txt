The Proposal:
Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Award is being given since 1996-97 to the social workers and organizations of the state who are doing excellent work in the field of women and child development to appreciate the work of social workers and organizations and to inspire other social workers/ organizations to come forward for the upliftment of women and children.

As per Circular dated 8.7.2019 vide Demand No. Considering the" Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2019" made available to the Department under X-01, the Government was considering to allocate a sum of Rs. 25,20,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) out of the existing Rs. 65,77,000/- in the budget for the Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Award.
Government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government, a sum of Rs. 25,20,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs Twenty Thousand only) is being sanctioned for the Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Award.
For this, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune shall ensure that the terms, conditions and rules laid down by the Finance Department from time to time are strictly followed while spending the funds distributed. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed should be submitted to the government.
