No.33/ K-4, dated 23rd April, 2018 (3) Letter from the Joint Director (Finance& Accounts) Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai No.3/ K-3/ Salary Provision/ 17-18/, dated 16th May, 2018 (4) Department of Women and Child Development Govt. 348/ KA. 6 dated 30th June, 2018 The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented by the Department of Women and Child Development through the office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai.
The Budget Cell vide its letter dated 23rd April, 2018 has informed that the Finance Department has approved the expenditure of funds budgeted for the year 2018-19 on plan/ head basis. Since the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, the Finance Department is advised to spend the funds in proportion to the funds of the Central Schemes when they are actually made available to the State Governments by the Central Government. Considering the funds received from the Central Government as well as the priority reported by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai for the disbursement of funds, the subject-wise funds of the scheme/ programme in the attached statement are the Central Share Fund and State Share Fund expenditure.
The Commissioner for Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS), the Government is approving the scheme.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai shall immediately make available the funds disbursed to all concerned subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Order/ Circular of the Finance Department and the Department mentioned hereinabove.
The Central Government has changed the ratio of Central and State share of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) and some activities have been discontinued. The decision was taken on June 30, 2018. The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai should instruct all the concerned officers to spend the funds as per the decision of the government.
This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the provisions of Finance Department, Government Circular No: 2018/ No. 69/ 3 dated 2nd April, 2018.
