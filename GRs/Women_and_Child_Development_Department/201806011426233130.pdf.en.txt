the Government Order
Mr. C. S. Kekan, Block Development Officer, Panchayat Samiti Patoda, District Beed was appointed on deputation to the post of Women and Child Development Officer, Zilla Parishad, Latur under the Women and Child Development Department for a period of two years initially or till the appointment of an officer on direct service to the said post, whichever is earlier. This deputation is being terminated on administrative grounds and in pursuance of this order, Shri C.S. Kekan is being appointed as Women and Child Development Officer, Zila Parishad Beed on deputation.
The offer is subject to the following conditions:
(a) The deputation service shall commence from the date on which the above officers join the post of deputation. Thereafter, the period of deputation shall expire on the date on which they join their parent department after handing over the charge of the post.
(b) The competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of their deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority, and
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
All other administrative matters of the above officers will be handled by the Rural Development Department as well as the Women and Child Development Department.
(3) As the said deputation is on request, the deputation allowance shall not be admissible to the said officers.
Also, the rules of their parent service will be applicable in respect of service matters such as salary allowances, travel exemption, medical exemption, group insurance scheme, etc.
4.The concerned officers should immediately appear at the place of appointment and submit a report to the government.
5.The order will come into effect immediately.
