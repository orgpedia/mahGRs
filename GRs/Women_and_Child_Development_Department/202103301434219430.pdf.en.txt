The Proposal:
Criminal Appeal No. 135/2010 (Budhadev Karmaskar v. State of West Bengal& Ors. 21.9.2020 and dt. As per the order dated 28.10.2020, instructions have been received to provide financial assistance to women who earn their livelihood through prostitution during the period of COVID-19 outbreak. Accordingly, financial assistance to women who earn their livelihood by working as prostitutes was made available to them under the Government Decision, Women and Child Development Department dated November 26, 2020, for the period October-2020 to December-2020. Covid-19 cases have been on the decline in recent months. Also, as the daily life of the people seems to have come to the previous position, the government was considering to suspend the supply of cash financial assistance to women who earn a living by prostitution until further orders.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under this Government decision, the supply of cash financial assistance to women who earn their livelihood by engaging in prostitution in the State has been suspended with effect from 31st December, 2020 till further orders.
