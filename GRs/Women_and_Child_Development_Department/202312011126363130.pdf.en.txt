The Proposal:
Decision to merge Centrally Sponsored Schemes Ujjwala and Swadhar under Mission Shakti with Shakti Sadan Component Read No. 3 has been taken as per the guidelines here and the decision to implement this scheme in the state Read No. 6 decision has been made.
Procedure for disbursement of funds under Centrally Sponsored Schemes Ujjwala and Swadhar through Single Nodal Agency (SNA) Account. 4 has been issued by the Central Government. Accordingly, an SNA account has been opened for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Shakti Sadan by the Department of Women and Child Development and the funds of Swadhar and Ujjwala schemes are required to be disbursed from this SNA account. Accordingly, Rs. 9,37,289/- (Rupees Nine Lakh Thirty Seven Thousand Two Hundred and Ninety Nine only) and Rs. 90,816/- (Rupees Ninety Thousand Eight Hundred and Sixteen only). Under the decision of the government here, Shakti Sadan has been transferred to the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) account of the scheme.
