Introduction: Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS) was launched by the Government of India in 2009-10. The name of the scheme was changed to" Child Protection Services" in 2017-18 and now it has been transferred to" Mission Vatsalya" from 2021-22. The Central Government has issued guidelines for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Mission Vatsalya under reference letters and as per the said guidelines, the Government was considering to constitute a State Level Child Welfare and Protection Committee to prepare and submit a proposal to the Central Government under Mission Vatsalya.
Government Decision: As per the guidelines of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Mission Vatsalya" issued by the Central Government under the Reference Letters, the" State Level Child Welfare and Protection Committee" is being constituted as follows.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) The annual proposal of the state government to be sent to the central government under the" Mission Vatsalya" scheme should be prepared and submitted to the central government after approving it.
2) To check the proper implementation of the scheme" Mission Vatsalya" in the state.
(3) To uphold the rights of children in the State and to take various measures for their welfare.
4) To coordinate the schemes of various departments of the state and take measures to provide maximum benefits of those schemes to the children.
