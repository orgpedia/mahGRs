The Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018.
Preamble: The State Child Fund was established under the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2000 and the Maharashtra Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2002. As per the Government decision in 1st, a Board of Management was constituted to give statutory shape to the Fund.
However, the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2000 has been repealed and the Juvenile Justice Act, 2015 has come into force. The Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018 have been enacted to implement this Act. Section 105 of the Juvenile Justice Act and Rule 85 of the Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice Rules, 2018 provide for the creation of a Children's Fund by each State in the name of" Juvenile Justice Fund" or such other name as the State may deem fit. Accordingly, the State Child Fund and Management Board constituted under the Juvenile Justice Act, 2000 has lapsed.
Therefore, the matter of changing the nomenclature of the Fund and declaring it as" Juvenile Justice Fund" under the Juvenile Justice Act, 2015 was under consideration of the Government.
The State Children's Fund constituted for the purpose of the provisions of Section 61 of the Juvenile Justice Act, 2000 and Rule 84 of the Maharashtra Juvenile Justice Rules, 2002 shall be deemed to be constituted for the purpose of the provisions of Section 105 of the Juvenile Justice Act, 2015 and Rule 85 of the Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice Rules, 2018 and the nomination of the Fund shall be deemed to be the" Juvenile Justice Fund" constituted under the Juvenile Justice Act, 2015. At present, the State Child Fund is functioning under the name of Deputy Commissioner (Child Development), Women and Child Development, Pune and Member Secretary State Child Fund. The amount deposited on this account will be credited to the same account by the Deputy Commissioner (Child Development). It will function as a Child Justice Fund in the name of Women and Child Development, Pune and Member Secretary and Treasurer.
The purpose of the Juvenile Justice Fund shall be (i) to provide assistance for the welfare and rehabilitation of children and to implement programmes which are not covered under any Central Government or State Government or any other scheme.
(ii) Provision of medical assistance or surgery for major ailments of children.
(iii) Provision of entrepreneurial support, skill development training or vocational training.
(iv) Provision of special vocational services, counsellors, translators, interpreters, special educators, social workers, mental health workers, vocational education trainers for children covered under the Juvenile Justice Act.
(v) Remedial care for children with cancer and residential facilities for their parents.
(vi) any other programme or activity for the purpose of providing assistance for the overall growth, development and welfare of children covered under the Juvenile Justice Act and Rules.
(viii) To support innovative programmes for the welfare of children in institutions working for children.
(8) to do all other things incidental and useful to the aforesaid purposes.
Separate orders will be issued regarding the management board, budgetary provisions and other terms and conditions of the Juvenile Justice Fund.
