Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune has been directed to clear the pending dues of the Aided Children Homes run by NGOs under the Women and Child Development Department for the financial year 2015-16 and 2016-17. As on 31.04.2020, a total amount of Rs. 3798.53 lakh is being disbursed as per the provisions available for the financial year 2019-20.
