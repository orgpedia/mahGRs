Preamble: In order to tackle the problem of malnutrition in the State, a scheme for setting up of Village Child Development Centres (VCDCs) for severely acute malnourished children in non-tribal areas has been launched vide Government Resolution dated 26.9.2017. For the financial year 2019-20, Rs. An amount of Rs 24,07,40, 000/- has been provided in the State Budget. Out of the said fund, the Government was considering to release funds for the scheme within the limit of 60% of the funds allocated by the Department of Finance as per the above-mentioned Read No. (2) for the month of December, 2019. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government Decision: For the scheme of establishing Village Child Development Center (VCDC) in Anganwadis in non-tribal areas in the State, the State Budget for the financial year 2019-20 has allocated Rs. 24,07,40, 000/- for the first nine months (i.e. from April, 2019 to end of December, 2019) as per the limit prescribed by the Department of Finance. The Government is approving the disbursement and expenditure of Rs. 14,44,44, 000/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores, Forty Four Lakhs, Forty Four Thousand only) to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai.
