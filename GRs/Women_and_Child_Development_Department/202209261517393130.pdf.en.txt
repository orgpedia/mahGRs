Introduction: Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme, a total of 32271 temporary/ honorarium posts have been sanctioned for 1073 working temporary posts in the regular pay scale in 104 urban projects and a total of 31198 honorarium posts of 15599 Anganwadi workers and 15599 Anganwadi helpers in 15599 Anganwadis. The extension of the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 was granted vide Government Resolution dated 22.03.2022 at Reference No.5.
