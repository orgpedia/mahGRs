The Proposal:
Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Award is being given since 1996-97 to the social workers and organizations of the state who are doing excellent work in the field of women and child development to appreciate the work of social workers and organizations and to inspire other social workers/ organizations to come forward for the upliftment of women and children.
