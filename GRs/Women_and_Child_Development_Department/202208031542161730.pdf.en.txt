Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS) was launched by the Government of India in 2009-10. The scheme has been renamed as" Child Protection Services" in 2017-18 and has now been transferred to" Mission Vatsalya" from the financial year 2021-22. The Central Government has issued guidelines for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Mission Vatsalya under Reference Letters and these guidelines are being implemented in the State from the financial year 2022-23.
The guidelines issued by the Central Government are annexed to this circular and the ratio of Central share of Mission Vatsalya to State share will be 60:40 as decided under the said guidelines and the financial implications mentioned under these guidelines will be applicable to all employees, institutions and beneficiaries of Mission Vatsalya.
