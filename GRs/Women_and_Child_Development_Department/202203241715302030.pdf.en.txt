The government's decision:
The Government has decided to make available at least 3% of the funds under the District Annual Plan (General) to the Department of Women and Child Development for the" Women and Child Empowerment Scheme." Issued on 31.1.2022. In this government decision, the Public Works Department was set as a single mechanism for the construction of Women and Child Development Buildings and all the constructions under this fund. However, for the construction of Women and Child Development Buildings as well as for all the constructions covered under the said fund.
Along with the Public Works Department, the District Council is also being approved as a mechanism under this government decision. The District Planning Committee will have the freedom to choose the mechanism between the Public Works Department and the District Council.
