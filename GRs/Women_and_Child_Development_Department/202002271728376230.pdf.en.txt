The Proposal:
The Women and Child Development Department has been directed to provide immediate assistance to women in distress. As per the order dated 19.8.2014, the Government has decided to launch Chief Ministers Helpline with toll fee number 181 to provide 24-hour information to women in distress with the help of SAAR IT RESOURCES PVT. LTD. This has been approved vide order dated 19.8.2014. This helpline has been launched in the state with the help of SAAR IT RESOURCES PVT. LTD., Mumbai.
Monthly telephone charges are paid to BSNL, Mumbai to keep the 181 helpline operational. Accordingly, Government Resolution, Department of Women and Child Development, No. 2013/ No. 290/ K-2, D. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune has been approved to disburse the dues upto February, 2019 by 29.3.2019. Now, the government was considering the payment of honorarium for a total of 12 months from March 2019 to February 2020.
