The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 has been implemented in the state. Section 9 (2) of the Act provides that the head of every office or department shall prescribe three levels of officers, as may be prescribed, to be submitted to him for taking a final decision on all matters relating to that office.
Accordingly, three levels of presentations are being set up for taking decisions on issues related to the Office of Women and Child Development-10 as follows: However, if there is a direction from the higher authorities or if there is a need in some exceptional cases, appropriate changes will be made on a case to case basis. Also in case of policy matters, there will be more than three levels as mentioned in the circular dated August 11, 2014.
