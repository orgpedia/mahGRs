The Proposal:
The International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women's Enterprise Development Project" has been approved to be implemented by the Women Economic Development Corporation (Mavim) on behalf of the Department of Women and Child Development for a period of six years from 2020-21 to 2025-26.
It has been approved to provide a loan amount of$ 51.40 million (â‚¹ 334.10 crore) from IFAD to implement the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women Entrepreneurship Development Project."
The Women's Economic Development Corporation (WEDC) has requested for release of funds to the tune of Rs. 40.00 crore out of the budgeted fund of Rs. 80.00 crore for implementation of International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women Entrepreneurship Development Project" in the budget year 2022-23. The request was made vide letter dated 19.4.2022. Accordingly, a request was made to the Planning and Finance Department to approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 40.00 crore. The proposal has been approved by the Planning Department and the Finance Department has approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 22.65 crore for the first quarter of 2022-23 in line with the plan outlay.
