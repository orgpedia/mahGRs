Introduction: Women and Child Development Department, Government of Maharashtra has decided to provide" Baby Care Kit" worth Rs. 2000/- to the women who have registered their pregnancy in the Government Primary Health Center/ Government Hospital and are going to give birth there at the time of first delivery. The decision was taken on December 29, 2018. The scheme has been implemented with effect from 26th January, 2019.

The supply order of the baby care kit should be issued subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The terms and conditions of the e-tender should be strictly adhered to while making the purchase. Also, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai should ensure that the supply is made as specified in the e-tender.
Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be responsible for spending the funds received from time to time in accordance with the prescribed policy and procedure.
3) The supplier has supplied the" Baby Care Kit" materials to the project level as per the prescribed quality, in the prescribed quantity as per the demand. This should be confirmed by the office of the Commissioner through the concerned Child Development Project Officer. Also, the" Baby Care Kit" should be stamped or labelled as" Supply by Government/ for use under Integrated Child Development Services Scheme." Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be responsible for any irregularity in the distribution/ supply and expenditure of" Baby Care Kit" materials or any accounting objections.
4) The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai shall issue appropriate guidelines regarding the entry of the" Baby Care Kit" after it reaches the Anganwadi Centre and the same shall be recorded in the Inventory Collection Register of the concerned Child Development Project Officer.
(5) Only after ascertaining the actual supply as per the said order, the payments for the respective projects shall be made from the subsidy received for as many districts as may be specified in the tender and the invoice for the supply of the same shall be received. The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be responsible for this.
(6) The provisions of the procurement policy of the Government Decision dated December 1, 2016 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labor should be strictly followed.
