Introduction: There are a total of 43 government agencies working under the Department of Women and Child Development. Out of these, 18 Children Homes, 19 Observation Homes and 06 Care Homes are functional. These institutions admit children in need of care and protection under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015, as well as children in conflict with law, under the orders of the Child Welfare Committee and Juvenile Justice Board. Sadar
During the period of stay, efforts are made to rehabilitate the children by providing them food, clothing, shelter, education, training, etc. A total of 20 government institutions are working in this rented building and the government was considering providing funds for renting out these institutions through reallocation as the rent of these institutions was less than the target of leasing and taxes.
Government's decision:
Grants to Maharashtra State Commission for Women, 31 under Computer Code 2235-2161, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) are being provided from the savings available in the target 2235-3032, 14 under Computer Code 2235-3032, by way of reallocation for the purpose of lease and taxes as follows.
