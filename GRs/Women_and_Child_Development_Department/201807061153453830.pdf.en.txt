Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, implemented by the Office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services, Navi Mumbai. The Government was considering to disburse the funds budgeted in 2018-19 for payment of honorarium to Anganwadi workers/ helpers/ mini Anganwadi workers.
Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme with funding ratio of 60: 40 between Centre and State. There are instructions of the Finance Department that the budgeted shadow provision should not be spent without receiving funds from the Central Government.
Due to the non-receipt of funds from the central government, it is not possible to pay the honorarium of Anganwadi workers on time. In this case, the amount of remuneration to be deducted on the minus authorization letter
The Finance Department had been asked to approve the proposal. A meeting was held by the Finance Department on 2nd June, 2017 under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Finance). In the meeting, it was decided to allow the department to spend the budgeted amount of the salary of the servitors even if it does not receive funds from the central government.
The Government has decided to disburse 70% of the funds allocated in 2018-19 to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai for payment of honorarium to Anganwadi workers/ helpers/ mini Anganwadi workers as per Government decision dated 7th April, 2018.
