The Proposal:
The Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS), a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, is being implemented in the State of Maharashtra by the Maharashtra State Child Protection Society since 2012-13 as per the guidelines of the Government of India, with the objective of enhancing and strengthening the quality of operational mechanisms related to child protection.
2. Decision of the Government of Women and Child Development Department. Under Umbrella Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) Scheme, the rates of various components of the Scheme have been revised with the introduction of new names of Appropriations under the Scheme w.e.f. 30.06.2018. However, in this government decision, the central government has decided to use the" Child Protection Service." The revised rates as per letter dated 08/12/2017 are not mentioned. As a result, there were difficulties in disbursing the amount to the agencies working under the revised Child Protection Services Scheme of the Central Government.
3. the central government. As per the letter dated 8.12.2017, the following changes have been made in the financial parameters of the units covered under the scheme.
In view of the increase in inflation, the rate of maintenance subsidy applicable to children admitted in Children Homes/ Observation Homes, Open Homes and Special Adoption Centres has been increased by 8% per annum to Rs.2,000/- per child per month.
The meeting of the Child Welfare Committee (CWC) has been increased from 12 to 20 per month and the meeting and travel allowance of the Committee and the Juvenile Justice Board (JJB) has been increased from Rs 1,000 to Rs 1,500.

Government's decision:
Cabinet of Ministers. As per the decision taken in the meeting dated 13/01/2021, the Government have approved to increase the rate of maintenance grant applicable to the children admitted in the Children Home/ Observation Home, Open Home and Special Adoption Centre under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme by 8% per annum, increase the number of meetings of Child Welfare Committee (CWC) from 12 to 20 per month and increase the meeting and travelling allowance of Child Welfare Committee and Juvenile Justice Board (JJB) from 1,000 to 1,500 per meeting. This will be applicable from 2021-22 onwards.
