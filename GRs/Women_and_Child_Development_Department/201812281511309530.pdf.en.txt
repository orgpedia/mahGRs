Government's decision
Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, implemented by the Office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services, Navi Mumbai. The matter of disbursement of funds budgeted in 2018-19 for payment of honorarium to Anganwadi workers/ helpers/ mini Anganwadi workers was under consideration.
Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme with a funding pattern of 60:40 between the Centre and the States. As per the Government decision dated 7th April, 2018, funds have been released to Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai for spending 70% of the budgeted funds in 2018-19 for payment of honorarium to Anganwadi workers/ helpers/ mini Anganwadi workers. Due to the shortage of funds for payment of honorarium, the funds budgeted under the head of State Accounts have been disbursed with the approval of the Finance Department as per the Government Decision dated 6th July 2018. The supplementary demand proposal was presented in the monsoon session of July-2018 due to the shortage of funds for the honorarium. The Finance Department had approved the expenditure of 70% of the sanctioned supplementary demand funds. Accordingly, the balance 30% of the funds budgeted under the head of Central and State Accounts were released as per the Government Decision dated 30th August, 2018, as approved by the Department of Finance to be spent in proportion to APIP. In view of the shortfall of funds for payment of honorarium for the month of December, 2018, the Government is approving to spend 100% of the budgeted amount under the next head of accounts amounting to Rs. 23788.93 lakh (Rupees Two Hundred Thirty Seven Crore Eighty Eight Lakh Three Thousand Nine Thousand only).
