INTRODUCTION: The Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme (Feeding Expenditure) (Head of Accounts 2236 1927 and 2236 1936) is being implemented by the Office of the Commissioner of Centrally Sponsored Schemes, ICDS, Navi Mumbai through the Department of Women and Child Development. The Centre and the state have decided to work on a 50:50 ratio.
During the year 2018-19, a supplementary demand proposal was submitted in the second (monsoon) session of 2018 for the expenditure on hot fresh feed and THR diet to the beneficiaries of 104 urban and 449 rural projects in the state, namely 0 to 3 and 3 to 6 years, underweight children, pregnant and lactating mothers. Accordingly, under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (feeding cost), the Central share of 50% (Account Head 2236 1927) is Rs. 250.00 crore and State Share of 50% (Account Head 2236 1936) Rs. The Department of Finance vide its circular dated 27.07.2018 has informed the Department that the Supplementary Demand for Funds amounting to Rs. 250.00 crore has been approved by the Legislature. Thus, the total amount of the sanctioned supplementary demand for the scheme (Central and State share of Rs. 500.00 crore) was under consideration of the Government for approval for disbursement and expenditure. Accordingly, for the time being, the Department of Finance has approved the release of funds to the extent of 70% of the sanctioned supplementary demand. Following is the government's decision:
Decision of the Government: Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme (Feeding Expenditure) for the Financial Year 2018-19, the Central Share of 50% (Head of Account 22361927) is Rs. 175.00 crore and State Share of 50% (Account Head 2236 1936) Rs. 175.00 crore. 350.00 crore (Rupees Three Hundred and Fifty Crores only) is being sanctioned by the Government to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai for disbursement and expenditure.
