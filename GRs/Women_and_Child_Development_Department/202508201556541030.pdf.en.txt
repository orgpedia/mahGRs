The Proposal -
Rule 232 (v) of the General Financial Rules, 2017 of the Government of India provides for devolution of funds to the State Governments and regulation of allocation of funds through Public Financial Management System (PFMS).
The principle of "Just In Time Release" as provided under Rule 230 (7) of the General Financial Rules, 2017 of the Government of India is intended to be applied to the distribution of all contributions to the extent practicable. In order to achieve greater efficiency in cash management at both central and state levels, it has been decided to propose SNA-SPARSH (Real Time System of Integrated Quick Transfers), an alternative fund flow mechanism for disbursal of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes through the Integrated Framework of Public Financial Management System (PFMS), State Integrated Financial Management Information System (State IFMIS) and Reserve Bank of India's e-Kuber System, in a phased manner vide the office memorandum dated 01.04.2017 dated 01.04.2017 of the Department of Public Financial Management, Department of Expenditure, Government of India, vide vide Notification No.
