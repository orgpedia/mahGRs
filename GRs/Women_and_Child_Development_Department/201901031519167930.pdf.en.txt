Government Decision: As per the Government Decision dated 15th January, 2010, the plan for construction of Anganwadi Centre buildings has been finalised. As per the said scheme, the Government has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 8.50 lakh (including GST) for construction of one Anganwadi Centre building for non-tribal areas and Rs. 9.40 lakh (including GST) for construction of one Anganwadi Centre building for tribal areas from the financial year 2018-19.
2. The Public Works Department has approved additional funds to the tune of 10%+ 20% (Naxal affected areas)= 30% of the Tentative List for tribal areas, hilly areas, extremely difficult areas (notified tribal areas/ hilly areas/ extremely remote areas) in Gadchiroli district and 20% of the Tentative List for all Naxal affected areas in the State as per requirement. Accordingly, the government is giving approval to prepare and approve the budget while constructing the Anganwadi Center. (A table of contents is attached.) The new tariff rates will be applicable from September 19, 2018. GST has been imposed on this amount.
The old rates will continue to be applicable for the completion of previously approved/ incomplete constructions.
