Anganwadi Centres are run by the Department of Women and Child Development under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS). The type plan for the construction of the Anganwadi Center building has been fixed as per the government decision dated January 15, 2010.
According to the type plan of the construction of the Anganwadi Center, the government has increased the financial limit of the construction cost and the government has decided to increase the cost of construction. As per EBV 2018/ PR No.76/ KA.6, dated 11th October, 2018, from the financial year 2018-19, an amount of Rs. 8.50 lakh (including GST) and Rs. 9.40 lakh (including GST) has been approved.
As per the recommendations of NITI Aayog, the Central Government has decided to share the cost of all activities between the Centre and the States in the ratio of 60:40 from 2015-16. Accordingly, for budgeting the funds from the year 2017-18, the new accounting head for construction of Anganwadis under Integrated Child Development Services Scheme has been approved as per the Government Decision No.Ebavi-2015/ PR No. 289/ K.S.6 dated 16th February, 2017 for additional funds for Central and State share of 60% and 40% respectively.

However, the Planning Department had suggested that the Anganwadi Centre construction be taken up under Revenue Account head instead of Capital Account head. Accordingly, the matter of revoking the capital account head mentioned above and approving 3 new revenue account head for this scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the opening of Revenue Account Head as mentioned in the following statement for the year 2019-20 for the construction of Anganwadi Centre building under the main scheme of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme.
