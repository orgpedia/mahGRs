GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government had, vide its decision dated 20.1.2018, extended the period of continuation of 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai from 1.3.2018 to 30.9.2018. Accordingly, the proposal for extension of these 43 temporary posts has been submitted by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 21.9.2018 at Reference No.6.
2. Under the Government Order dated 7.9.2018 in Reference No.7 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, State of Maharashtra, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 21.9.2018 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the above decision of the Finance Department, 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai are being extended from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
