Government Decision: Poshan Abhiyaan is an important programme based on holistic nutrition being implemented under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. The Centre and state share in the scheme is 80:20. The Commissioner and State Project Director, State Nutrition Resource Cell, POSHAN Abhiyaan, Navi Mumbai has informed that as per the directions of the Government of India, funds are required to be released in advance for various activities under POSHAN Abhiyaan for the year 2020-21 and necessary funds are available from the Central Government for the same and the same are required to be provided at the field level for spending during this financial year.
(1) In the next financial year (FY 2020-21), all Anganwadi Centres will be provided Rs. 500/- should be sanctioned in advance.
(2) Under Nutrition Engineering, mobile phones have been provided to all Anganwadi workers, Headmistress and technical manpower. It should be approved to provide advance subsidy to all Anganwadi workers/ head maids and technical manpower for the next financial year (2020-21) for recharging mobile phone data.
As the above advance grants are to be released for the year 2020-21, accordingly, the Central share (80%) for implementation of POSHAN Abhiyaan for the financial year 2019-20 has been increased from Rs. 137.49 crore and State share (20%) of Rs. 34.37 crore. 171.86 crore.
