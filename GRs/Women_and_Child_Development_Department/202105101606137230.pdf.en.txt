The Proposal:
For the year 2020-21, the Central and State share of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Integrated Child Protection Scheme under the Department of Women and Child Development is Rs. 7990.20 Lakhs and Rs. 5922.28 lakh.
A sum of Rs. 13912.48 lakh has been provided in the Budget.
Under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme, the salaries of employees in child care institutions and permanent and contractual employees working in the state machinery for the same are also paid.
At present, even during this pandemic, these employees are providing their services as it is an essential service to serve in the institution of care and protection of children. However, since the salaries of these essential service employees also need to be paid on time, the government is considering to disburse funds for salaries as a special case under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme, assuming that the funds will be received under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme from the central government as every year.
