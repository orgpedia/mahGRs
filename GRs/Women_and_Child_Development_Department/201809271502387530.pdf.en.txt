""" Introduction:- The scheme"""" Majhi Kanya Bhagyashree"""" which was implemented from 1st April 2016 to increase the birth rate of girls, prevent sex selection, promote and ensure education of girls, improve the health status of girls, has been modified and implemented as"""" Majhi Kanya Bhagyashree Revised New Scheme"""" from 1st August 2017 for all sections of the society whose annual income is up to Rs. 7.50 lakh (Seven lakh fifty thousand only).""" Considering the difficulties faced in implementing the scheme at the district level, the government was considering amending some of the terms and conditions of the scheme.
Government Decision: My Daughter Bhagyashree Modified New Scheme has been implemented from 1st August 2017 for all sections of the society whose annual income is up to Rs. 7.50 lakh (Seven Lakh Fifty Thousand only). Considering the difficulties faced in implementing the scheme at the district level, the Government is approving the following amendments in the terms/ conditions of the scheme. The remaining terms and conditions shall remain as specified in the Government Resolution dated 1st August 2017.
