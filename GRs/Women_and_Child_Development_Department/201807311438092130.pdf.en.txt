The Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018 have been notified vide Notification dated 13.3.2018. In view of the provisions of the said Rules, the Non-Governmental/ Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) who are willing to run child care institutions, may be given the opportunity to apply for registration. Till 20.05.2018, instructions were issued through advertisements and circulars on the website of the Department of Women and Child Development regarding online facility and offline mode of application.
Accordingly, a total of 713 online and 180 offline registration certificate applications have been received from the Commissionerate, Women and Child Development Department from the state. The applications have been scrutinized at the Commissionerate level and a photocopy of the institution-wise scrutiny letter has been submitted to the Government under Reference 3.
3. At the end of the scrutiny of the application received, the following instructions are now being issued through this circular:
A copy of all the institution-wise scrutiny letters received from the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Department is being made available on the website of Women and Child Development Department- womenchild.maharashtra.gov.in under the heading News and Information.
2) To provide the relevant agencies to meet the errors noted in the said Scrutiny Letter. From 30.7.2018 to 21 working days (d. 30.8.2018) is being extended. Errors will not be reported separately to the institutions. All applicants should check the official website of the concerned department.
3) The concerned agencies should complete the necessary errors and submit a compliance report within the said period. It should be submitted to the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune by post or by hand till 30.8.2018.
