The Government has decided to implement the scheme" Permanent Rest House, Birsi" under the Department of Women and Child Development in the budget year 2021-22. For the purpose of 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) under the head Ex-1, 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 104-Welfare of Elderly, Disabled and Destitute Persons, (01) (03) Permanent Rest House, Birsi (2235-2896), Rs. 2,00,000/- (Rs. 2 lakh only). 30,000/- (Rs. Thirty thousand only) is being distributed to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune.
The concerned should ensure that the fund is spent for the purpose for which it is meant and the utilization certificate of the spent fund is submitted to the government immediately.
