Government's decision:
The 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai were extended from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 vide Government Order No.4 dated 24.08.2021. However, as per the revised figure approved vide Government Resolution dated 05.09.2014, Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, State of Maharashtra, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 03.03.2022 has submitted the proposal for extension of 43 temporary posts.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 22.02.2022 in Reference No. 6 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts which have been created after the approval of the previous review but which have not been included in the figure.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 03.03.2022 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months.
