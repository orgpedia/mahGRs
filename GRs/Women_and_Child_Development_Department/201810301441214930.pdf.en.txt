The Proposal:
As per the circular issued by the Finance Department, the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018 has been passed by the state legislature. As per the circular, the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programme expenditure for the first nine months of 2018-19 (ending December, 2018) will be distributed through the system (BEAMS) within the limit of 70% of the total annual allocation.
For the financial year 2018-19, a provision of Rs. 10,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores only) has been approved for 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) under head 2235-3177.

Accordingly, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse a sum of Rs. 1,27,12, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Seven Lakh Twelve Thousand only) towards the remuneration of Counsellors and Coordinators for the period August, 2018 to November, 2018.
Government's decision:
As per the circular issued by the Department of Finance, out of the remaining budgetary allocation of Rs. 10,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crore only) for the Counselling Centre Scheme for the financial year 2018-19, the Government hereby approves the release of grant-in-aid of Rs. 1,27,12, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty-Seven Lakh Twelve Thousand only) towards the remuneration of Counsellors and Coordinators for the month ending November, 2018.
Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune has been authorized to disburse a sum of Rs. 1,27,12, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Seven Lakh Twelve Thousand only) to the concerned.
