The proposal: In the meeting dated 12/6/2018 of the Juvenile Justice Committee of the High Court of Bombay, the Committee has given instructions for the Structural Audit of the Children's Home/ Observation Home Building. A total of 43 Government Children Homes/ Observation Homes are functioning under the Department of Women and Child Development. Out of these, 20 institutes are running in their own building and the rest are running in rented buildings. The Children Home/ Observation Home is functioning under the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 and caters to children in need of care and protection and children in conflict with law. The Act provides for the rehabilitation of children by providing them with food, clothing, shelter, education and training. The Child Welfare Committee, The High Court, Bombay, as per the above directions, has issued orders for the Structural Audit of 20 Children Homes/ Observation Homes at a cost of Rs. 2.50 lakh per Children Home in its own building. As additional provision is required under X-1 (Non-Scheme), the proposal for supplementary demand as proposed in the month of July, 2018 would have been Rs. The proposal has been approved for an amount of Rs 30 crore. For this purpose, approval has been taken to disburse the said fund for the financial year 2018-19 as prescribed under Circular No.2 of the Finance Department. Accordingly, under the head Accounts Head 2235 2241 Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 800-Other Expenditure (01) (04) Repair of Buildings, 27-Small Construction under the Women and Child Development Department, the matter of disbursement of funds admissible for the Structural Audit of the building" 20 Government Children Home/ Observation Home" under this head was under consideration.
