The order:
Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme, the Anganwadi Centres in the rural projects which are under the purview of the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) are being integrated into the urban projects and the urban projects are being restructured. The decision was approved by the government. The government had given five days" time to implement the order.

After that. As per the Government Order dated 11.1.2023. To implement the Government Decision dated 14.12.2022. The period was extended till 15.2.2023.
However, it was found that the process was not completed within the stipulated time period and accordingly, a proposal for extension was submitted to the government by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. Accordingly, the government has decided to implement the order. The period is being extended till 31.3.2023. during this period. The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai shall submit a report to the Government on the implementation of the Government Resolution dated 14.12.2022.
