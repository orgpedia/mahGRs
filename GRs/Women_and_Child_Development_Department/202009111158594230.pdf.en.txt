Government circular:
As per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Excellence in Service) Rules, 1982 and the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department, the merit list is required to be published every year with 1st January as the base date.
The final seniority list of 01.01.2019 in the cadre of Deputy Commissioner Group-A in the Cell under the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development has been issued as per Government Decision No. MBA-2019/ PR No. 23/ K.7 dated 10.05.2019.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune handles the administrative and organisational/ service matters in the case of officers/ staff working under the office of the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai.
The respective ranks of the officers of these two Commissionerates are being published in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 and the final seniority list in respect of the said officers, as per the relevant Government Decision/ Government Circular issued in this regard, in accordance with the revised consolidated provisional service list of the Deputy Commissioner Group-A cadre up to the end of 01.01.2020 along with Annexure-I.
All the concerned officers should examine the Service List and submit their detailed statement regarding any objection/ error or amendment in this Service List to the Government in writing within 7 days with appropriate documents. In this way, appropriate decisions will be taken after considering the representations received by the government in order of merit and the service list will be published in the final form. If no representation is received within 7 days, the final list of service will be published, assuming that the concerned officers do not want to give any statement in this regard.
