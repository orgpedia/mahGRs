the Government Order
Smt.Snehlata Naravane, Superintendent, State Excise, Sindhudurg is being appointed on deputation for a period of three years as Administrative Officer in the office of Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights, Worli under the Department of Women and Child Development subject to the following conditions:
(a) The service of the Deputy Commissioner shall commence from the date on which Smt. Snehlata Naravane assumes charge of her office as Deputy Commissioner. After three years, the period of deputation will end on the date on which they join their parent department after handing over the charge of the post of deputation.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-recipient employer, the non-recipient employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. Provided that before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ competent authority; and d) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The post of Administrative Officer in Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights is equivalent to that of Under Secretary in the Ministry with pay scale S-23 67700-208700. However, the pay scale of Mrs. Snehlata Naravane, Superintendent, State Excise is S-20 56100-177500.
Therefore, subject to the approval of the Finance Department to temporarily reduce the pay scale of the said post, Smt. Snehlata Naravane, Superintendent is being appointed on deputation to the said post.
After the repatriation of the deputation, Smt. Snehlata Naravane, Superintendent will be required to join her parent department i.e. (State Excise) Home Department.
The terms and conditions of the appointment of Smt.Snehlata Naravane will be decided in consultation with the Home Department after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the (Period of Incumbency, Post Graduate Service, etc.) Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Smt. Snehlata Naravane will be handled by the Home Department and this Department.
