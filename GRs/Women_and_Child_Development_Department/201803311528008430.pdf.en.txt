Introduction: The Department of Women and Child Development is implementing various schemes for the development of women and children.
Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) is a scheme for development of children in the age group of 0 to 6 years.
The sponsored scheme is being implemented by the office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The Government of India has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 5488.95 lakh for providing School Education Kits (PSE Kits) for Anganwadi children in the year 2017-18. It is proposed to purchase pre-school education kits: intellectual toys, puzzle games, educational toys, etc.
Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 14th March, 2017 has published e-tender for procurement of Intellectual Toys, Puzzle Games, Educational Toys, etc. Approval for purchase of Intellectual Toys, Puzzle Games, Educational Toys, etc. from Vaishnavi Industrial Co-operative Society Limited, Ahmednagar for an amount of Rs. 5,25,29, 267/- was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 14th March, 2017 has published e-tender for procurement of Intellectual Toys, Puzzle Games, Educational Toys, etc. The Government is approving the purchase of the following intellectual toys, puzzle games, educational toys, etc. from Vaishnavi Industrial Co-operative Society, Limited, Ahmednagar for an amount of Rs. 5,25,29, 267/- (Rupees Five Crore Twenty Five Lakh Thirty Nine Thousand Two Hundred Sixty Six only).

The following terms and conditions apply:
1) The terms and conditions of the e-tender should be strictly adhered to while making the purchase. Also, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai should ensure that the supply is made as specified in the e-tender.
2) The funds will be spent as per the policy and procedure prescribed by the government.
3) The supplier shall supply the above items up to the project level. The last date of supply should be made binding on the supplier for the material to be received by the concerned within the prescribed period.
4) The supply of the above items should be made by following the prescribed procedure from the available provision. The supplier has supplied the above items up to the project level as per the prescribed quantity, prescribed quality as per the demand. This should be ensured. Also, the item supplied should be stamped or labelled as supply by/ for use under ICDS. In case of irregularities in the distribution/ supply and expenditure of the above items and in case of accounting objections, the responsibility will be of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai.
5) To ensure that the benefits of this scheme reach the beneficiaries, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai should give appropriate guidelines regarding keeping a proper record of the date of production/ total supply/ total utilization in the self-explanatory information anganwadi registry, etc.
6) Payment should be made only after confirming that the order has actually been delivered.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai will be responsible for this.
