The Proposal:
A sum of Rs. 5,66,50, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores Sixty Six Lakh Fifty Thousand only) is sanctioned for 31 Assistant Anusandhan (Non-Payment) cases under Computer Code 2235-2161 for the financial year 2019-20. Out of this fund, reference No. 2 Under the Government decision, a sum of Rs. 3,39,90, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Forty-nine Lakhs Ninety Thousand only) has been released within the limit of 60% of the budgetary allocation. Out of the original budget provision under this head, the remaining amount of Rs. 2,26,60, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Twenty Six Lakh Sixty Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government to be disbursed to the Maharashtra State Women's Commission.
The Government hereby approve the disbursement of funds to the tune of Rs. 2,26,60, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Twenty Six Lakh Sixty Thousand only) to the Maharashtra State Commission for Women for non-payment of salaries.
District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of this fund to Maharashtra State Women's Commission.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall disburse this fund to the Maharashtra State Women's Commission.
However, without drawing the said funds from the treasury immediately, the District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) shall examine the expenditure/ fund requirement of the Maharashtra State Commission for Women and submit the payment of expenditure to the treasury. It should be ensured that the funds are not left with the Maharashtra State Women's Commission. Also, the member secretary, Maharashtra State Women's Commission and the commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune, should take care of submitting the utility certificate to the Accountant General's office. If the Maharashtra State Women's Commission deposits the amount in their bank account without the purpose of expenditure, the entire responsibility of this irregularity will be of the Controlling Officer as well as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
