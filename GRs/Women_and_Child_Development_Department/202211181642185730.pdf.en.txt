The Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 and Amended 2021 2. Adoption Regulations, 2022 3. 21st March, 2018
Preamble Under the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection) Act, 2015, there is a provision for adoption of such children to willing parents for rehabilitation of orphaned, abandoned, abandoned children and there is a provision for setting up of Central Adoption Resource Authority (CARA) at the central level and State Adoption Resource Authority (SARA) at the state level to regulate the matter of adoption.
Accordingly, an Adoption Resource Authority has been constituted in the State.
The Adoption Regulation, 2022 is being implemented in the state to implement the adoption process through the Adoption Resource Authority. The Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 and the Adoption Regulations, 2022. Subject to the provisions of section 35, the Governing Body of the State Adoption Resource Authority (SARA) shall perform its functions and responsibilities.
Rule No.3 of the Adoption Regulations, 2022. The Member Secretary and Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune shall be responsible for convening the meeting of the Governing Body of the State Adoption Resource Authority (SARA) at least once in three months to review the work of simplifying the adoption process in accordance with the provisions of Article 35 (f), suggesting amendments therein, etc.
The term of office of the non-official members of the Governing Body shall be three years from the date of such decision of the Government.
