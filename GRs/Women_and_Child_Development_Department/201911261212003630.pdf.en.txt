Preamble: As per the administrative sanction of the Central Government, the Government Resolution on Poshan Abhiyaan, No. The scheme is being implemented throughout the State vide EBV-2018/ Q.No.27/ Q.6 dated 11/07/2018.
The Prime Minister's Nutrition Mission is an ambitious project. The Poshan Abhiyan is being implemented in all Anganwadi centres in 553 projects in 36 districts of the state.
Various activities are being organized under the Poshan Abhiyan to reduce the malnutrition rate in the state. As per the decision of the Government, the implementation of the initiative of Common Application Software under Information Computers Technology Enabled Real Time Monitoring of Schemes (ICT-RTM) has been approved.
As per the administrative approval of the Central Government, various types of Disbursement Linked Indicators have been decided to achieve the targets set under POSHAN Abhiyaan, out of which payment of incentive allowance to Anganwadi workers and helpers who fully use the Common Application Software is a part of it. Guidelines for payment of Incentive to Anganwadi Workers and Helpers using Common Application Software have been received from the Central Government vide letter dated 31st October, 2019. Further, the Central Government vide letter dated 27/09/2018 has laid down guidelines/ norms for payment of incentive allowance of Rs.500/- per month and Rs.250/- per month to Anganwadi workers and helpers respectively. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, issued orders vide No. A proposal in this regard has been sent to the government. As per the proposal, the government was considering to allow payment of incentive allowance to Anganwadi workers and helpers who meet the criteria prescribed by the central government in the Poshan Abhiyan.

Decision of the Government: To achieve the Disbursement Linked Indicators set by the Central Government under the Poshan Abhiyaan, the monthly allowable incentive to Anganwadi workers and helpers who qualify as per the criteria set by the Central Government is Rs. 500/- and Rs. 250/- is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) Anganwadi worker: (a) Anganwadi worker shall be required to undertake growth monitoring of at least 60% of the children below the age of 2 years enrolled in the Anganwadi centre every month.
(b) Anganwadi worker shall be required to make at least 60% of the house visits of beneficiaries who are pregnant women and children below the age of 2 years as per the prescribed schedule of house visits in each month.
(e) The above criteria are the basic criteria relating to the essential services that anganwadi workers are expected to provide to pregnant women, lactating mothers and children below the age of 2 years. The achievement of this criterion will need to be reported by Common Application Software.
Anganwadi Helpers: Anganwadi centres are required to be opened for at least 21 days in a month.
(b) The criteria shall be determined on the basis of photographs of anganwadis, anganwadis opened and children present in the anganwadis uploaded through the Common Application Software.
3) Anganwadi workers should be fully trained in the Common Application Software.
4) The Anganwadi worker will have to fill the information of all the beneficiaries on the system of Common Application Software.
5) Anganwadi workers will be required to submit a report every month through the Common Application Software system.
6) The list of Anganwadi workers and helpers who meet the above-mentioned criteria for receiving the incentive allowance should be automatically reported on the Common Application Software system at the end of each month.
The report should be made available to the concerned Headmistress/ Supervisor and concerned Child Development Project Officer through the dashboard.
8) The concerned Headmistress/ Supervisor/ Child Development Project Officer shall certify that the incentive allowance is admissible to the eligible Anganwadi workers and helpers and submit a report to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai in the prescribed manner.

9) It will be required to be deposited in the bank account of the eligible Anganwadi workers and helpers by the 15th of every month. For this, the amount of incentive allowance should be transferred to the District Programme Officer, Zilla Parishad through BDS according to the number received from the district level every month. From the district level, the amount should be withdrawn and transferred to the bank account of the concerned Child Development Project Officer and from the project level, the amount should be deposited in the bank account of the Anganwadi worker and helper by the 15th of every month.
2. The above Government approval is being accorded subject to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai verifying the fulfillment of the terms and conditions mentioned above under the said purpose.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be responsible for ensuring that the funds are spent as per the policy and procedure prescribed by the Government and as per availability.
4. The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the provision made available in the respective headings generated under the head" POSHAN Abhiyaan" during the financial year 2019-20.
