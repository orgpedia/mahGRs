Government circular:
As per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Service Excellence) Rules, 1982 and the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department, it is necessary to publish the service excellence list by considering 1st January as the base date every year.
The provisional list of officers of Group-A cadre working under the office of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai up to the end of 01.01.2021 was published on 6.4.2021.
No objections or representations have been received in this regard. Therefore, the final service availability list upto the end of 01.01.2021 is being published as per the Annexure attached.
