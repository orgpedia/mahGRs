Introduction: The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme which was started in the State of Maharashtra in 1975. Gradually, the number of projects under the scheme has increased and projects have been implemented in various urban, rural and tribal areas of the state. Under this scheme, 6 important services such as Anganwadi services for children, pregnant women and lactating mothers will be provided. The services of immunization, pre-primary education, supplementary nutrition, referral services, health check-up, health and nutrition education to women in the age group of 15 to 45 years, etc. are provided.
The Department of Women and Child Development, Government of India vide letter dated 30.12.2017, has been informed to make available to each Child Development Project Office a set of Aadhaar enrolment material for enrolling children in the age group of 0-5 years and all children below 5 years in Anganwadi Centres under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme and all beneficiaries of the scheme to be benefitted through DBT of the Department of Women and Child Development. Accordingly, the following funds will be provided for the distribution of Aadhaar enrolment material in the ratio of 60% for the Centre and 40% for the State. There are 553 Child Development Project Offices in the State and the Government was considering providing Aadhaar Enrolment Kits to each Project Office in 3 ways per project.

Government's decision:
The Government is providing Aadhaar Enrolment Kits to 553 Child Development Project Offices (3 Aadhaar Enrolment Kits per Child Development Project Office) in the State for enrolling children in the age group of 0-5 years and all children below 5 years in Anganwadi Centres under Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme and beneficiaries of all schemes to be benefitted through DBT of WCD. The terms and conditions for the purchase of Aadhaar Enrolment Set will be as follows:
1) Aadhaar enrolment material kits for enrolment of beneficiaries in Anganwadis should be procured on GEM Portal by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. And it should be supplied to the Child Development Project Officer's office. Procurement of Aadhaar enrolment material should be done by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai in coordination with them as per the standards prescribed by the Aadhaar office.
The Aadhaar enrolment kit will include: 1 computer (desktop)/ laptop/ tablet (for child Aadhaar enrolment) 1 scanner, 1 printer, 1 slap finger print scanner, 1 eye scanner, 1 GPS device.
3) All the above materials should be purchased within a maximum limit of Rs. 1.50 lakh 4) The expenditure of the Centre and the State will be in the ratio of 60:40.
5) The Aadhaar enrolment kit should be made functional in the office of each Child Development Project Officer.
The Child Development Project Officer will act as the Registering Officer. Aadhaar registration will be done under your control.
7) One-time funding will be provided by the Centre for Aadhaar enrolment kit.
8) The funds received for Aadhaar Enrolment kit will be received through the Reserve Bank of India, Nagpur office. Information in this regard should be sent to the Accountant General, Nagpur Office/ Central Government from time to time.
