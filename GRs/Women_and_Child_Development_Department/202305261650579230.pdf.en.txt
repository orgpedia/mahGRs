Under the Mission Vatsalya Yojana, the salaries of employees of child care institutions and permanent and contractual employees working in the state machinery for the same are also paid. At present, these employees are providing their services as it is an essential service in the care and protection of children. However, the salaries of these employees should be paid on time. Under this scheme, the central government has been releasing funds every year to pay the salaries of the salaried employees, but due to non-receipt of funds from the central government for 2023-24, the salaries of these employees have not been paid.
Under this scheme, a proposal has been submitted to the Central Government through the State Government for the year 2023-24 and the proposal is under consideration to distribute the funds made available for the salary of these employees to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Child Protection Society, Pune and the Commissioner, Women and Child Development.
Government Decision: Under Mission Vatsalya Yojana, the head of accounts/ objectives in the statement of return is given as Rakkana No. 4 as well as
The decision is being circulated to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Child Protection Society, Pune and Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune.
