Government's decision:
Under Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme, 1070 working temporary posts in regular pay scale and 15532 in 104 urban projects as per the specifications fixed for urban projects.
A total of 31064 honorarium posts of 15532 Anganwadi workers and 15532 Anganwadi helpers in the Anganwadis, a total of 32134 temporary/ honorarium posts were extended from 01/10/2018 to 28/02/2019 as per the Government decision dated 27.9.2018 at Reference No.5.
2. Under the Government Order dated 21.2.2019 in Reference No.7 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
3. Vacancies in regular pay scale posts and honorarium posts working under Civil Projects in the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme are not vacant for more than six months.
