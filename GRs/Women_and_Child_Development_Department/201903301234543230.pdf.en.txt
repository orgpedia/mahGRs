Government Decision: As per the administrative approval of the Central Government under the Poshan Abhiyaan, various activities have been approved to be organized in 85452 Anganwadi centers in 30 districts of the state under Phase 1 and Phase 2. Information Computers Technology Enabled Real Time Monitoring (ICT-RTM) initiative is being implemented under POSHAN Abhiyaan and under the implementation of this initiative, it has been approved to make available Android based mobile phones and SIM cards with data plan for Anganwadi workers, mini Anganwadi workers, headmistress, supervisors and technical manpower in all Anganwadi centres and district level and project offices.
Mobile phones have been made available to all the Headmistresses/ Supervisors/ Anganwadi workers and technical manpower for the implementation of ICT-RTM in the State. The Government has approved to disburse an advance of Rs. 800/- for 6 months as against Rs. 400/- quarterly for SIM cards and Internet services (4G network) for availing SIM cards with data plan under POSHAN Abhiyaan.
