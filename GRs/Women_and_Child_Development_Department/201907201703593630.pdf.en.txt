Introduction: The Children's Aid Society, Mumbai is a British-era organisation, which runs children's homes/ observation homes in Mumbai (Dogri, Matunga) and Mumbai suburbs (Mankhurd), primarily for children in the age group of 0 to 18 years, under the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015.
The children's homes run by this institution primarily accommodate children affected by legal conflicts and children in need of care and protection (orphans, destitute, street children, victims of sexual abuse). These children are provided with facilities like food, clothing, shelter, education, vocational training, etc., to rehabilitate and rehabilitate them socially. The staff structure of the children's homes of this institution is approved by the Department of Women and Child Development and 100 percent salary as per the pay scale for the staff salary is provided to the institution by the Assistant Grants Department. as well as entering the institution
Monthly stipend of Rs. 1500/- is provided as subsidy.
The Industrial Training Institute at Mankhurd, a project of The Children's Aid Society, Mumbai, has been permanently approved by the Government (Department of Higher and Technical Education) on a non-grant basis since 1989. Private industrial training institutes are not given subsidy by the government. However, due to financial constraints, the institute was closed down in 2012.
The Industrial Training Institute. Public Interest Litigation filed in the High Court, Mumbai No. (l) The said Industrial Training Institute has been re-opened by the Society as per the directions given in 81/2015.
The admission to this Industrial Training Institute has been approved by the competent authority in 2016, 2017 and 2018 till further orders and at present, the admissions are being made under the Fitter, Wireman and Electronic Mechanic courses. In this Industrial Training Institute, admission is given mainly to those admitted in the children's home of the institute. Children from outside the institution can also enter here. Industrial Training Institute is a very useful project in line with the rehabilitation of the admitted under the Juvenile Justice Act.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: This Government Resolution approves the operation of the Industrial Training Institute at Mankhurd of The Children's Aid Society, Mumbai to be run by the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship.
Further necessary action will be taken by The Child Aid Society, Mumbai in respect of the Industrial Training Institute at Mankhurd being run by the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department. Admission to the children's home/ observation room will be free of charge. The Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship will be able to decide on the admission and fee of other children in other places along with these admissions. The Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship will be able to run other courses in addition to the course in the Industrial Training Institute while running the Industrial Training Institute by the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship.
