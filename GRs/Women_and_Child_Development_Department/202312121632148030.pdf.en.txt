As per Section 41 of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015, all government and non-government organisations taking care of children are required to obtain a registration certificate.
Accordingly, the No. Under the scheme, 34 government institutions providing child care in the state have been given registration certificates for a period of five years. It has also been decided to convert the Government Osteopathic Children Home, Chandrapur in Chandrapur district as Government Girls Observation Home/ Children Home, Chandrapur.
The matter of declaring the Superintendent, Government Girls Observation Home/ Children Home Chandrapur as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for obtaining new DDO code for Government Girls Observation Home/ Children Home Chandrapur was under consideration.
The government's decision:
The Superintendent for Government Girls Observation Home/ Children Home Chandrapur, Government Girls Observation Home/ Children Home Chandrapur is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
