The Proposal:
Anganwadis are run by the Women and Child Development Department under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) scheme. The scheme for construction of Anganwadi buildings has been fixed as per the Government Decision dated 15th January, 2010 and an amount of Rs. 6.00 lakh has been earmarked for construction of an Anganwadi for non-tribal areas and Rs.
A sum of Rs. 6.60 lakh had been sanctioned for the project. As per the approved plan for construction of Anganwadi Centre Buildings as per the decision of the Government dated 11th October, 2018, an amount of Rs. 8.50 lakh (including GST) has been sanctioned for construction of one Anganwadi Centre Building for Non-Tribal areas and Rs. 9.40 lakh (including GST) for construction of one Anganwadi Centre Building for Tribal areas from the financial year 2018-19.
With the approval of the Planning Department, Finance Department and the Accountant General, the following heads of accounts have been approved for the construction of Anganwadi under the Government Decision dated 16th February 2017 and the following funds have been budgeted for 2018-19.

The scheme for construction of Anganwadi Centres is being implemented in the District Annual Plan in DPDC and the Government decision to change the name of the scheme has been issued on 15th December 2017. Accordingly, the proposal to change the name of the scheme was submitted to the Planning Department, the Finance Department and the Accountant General's Office.
The Planning Department has notified the construction of Anganwadi Center under the revenue head and it has been approved by the Accountant General's Office.
