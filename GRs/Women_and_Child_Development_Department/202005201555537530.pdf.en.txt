Government Decision: Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. In order to save the honorarium of Anganwadi workers due to non-receipt of funds from the Central Government within the stipulated period, it has been decided in the meeting held on 2nd June, 2017 under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Finance) to allow the department to spend the budgeted amount of honorarium of Anganwadi workers even if the expected central assistance is not available.
The Department of Finance vide circular dated 16th April, 2020 has approved the expenditure of budgetary provisions of essential and programme expenditure for the financial year 2020-21 in the range of 15-25% of the total annual outlay for the first three months (ending June, 2020).
3. Under the said Government decision, the payment of honorarium to Anganwadi workers, Helpers and Mini Anganwadi workers for the month of March, 2020 is being approved.
