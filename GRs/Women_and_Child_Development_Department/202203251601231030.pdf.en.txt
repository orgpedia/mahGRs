As per the Government Resolution dated 31.3.1999, a combined Authorized Share Capital contribution of Rs. 400.00 lakh has been sanctioned by the Central Government and the State Government. Out of this, a total of Rs. 386.37 lakh has been disbursed as capital contribution to the Women Economic Development Corporation (WEDC) out of which Rs. 46.65 lakh has been released by the Central Government and Rs. 339.72 lakh by the State Government. Therefore, out of the 400.00 lakh sanctioned to the Women's Economic Development Corporation, the Government was considering the issue of disbursement of the remaining portion of Rs. 13.63 lakh to the Ashandan Women's Economic Development Corporation.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, a total amount of Rs. 13.63 lakhs (Rupees Thirteen Lakhs Sixty Three Thousand only) is being sanctioned to the Women's Economic Development Corporation as share capital contribution for the head of account/ target in Rakaana No. (2) of the return.
