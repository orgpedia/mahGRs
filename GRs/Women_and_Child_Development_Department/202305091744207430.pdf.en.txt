The proposal-
The programme is aimed at providing information to women about the schemes being implemented by various departments of the government for women in the state as well as providing a platform to solve their local problems. Suggestions were received from the Minister, Women and Child Development. Accordingly, it was under consideration to implement a special program for women, Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Stree Shakti Samadhan Shivir, at the ward level in every taluka level of the state and in the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation area in the month of May 2023.
Government's decision
In order to provide information to women about the schemes being implemented by various departments of the government for women in the state and to solve their local problems, the government is approving the implementation of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Stree Shakti Samadhan Shivir in every ward of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation.
Accordingly, the said camp program should be organized at the taluka level in the state under the chairmanship of the Tehsildar and in the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation area under the chairmanship of the Chief Officer of the Municipal Corporation of the concerned ward.
For the implementation of this programme, the Department of Women and Child Development, through the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune, will provide funds to the tune of Rs. 50.00 thousand for each Taluka and Ward of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation. The funds will be used to fund the publicity and related expenses of the programme.
The guidelines of the said programme shall be as per Annexure-A attached. Accordingly, all the concerned authorities should ensure the successful implementation of this program.
The Collector should give necessary instructions to the concerned officers of various departments at the district/ taluka level for the implementation of this program, and ensure that this camp program is implemented properly. In some cases, direct inspection should be carried out in selected places.
