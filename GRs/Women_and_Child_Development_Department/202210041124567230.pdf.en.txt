Government Decision: Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. To make it possible to pay the honorarium of Anganwadi workers due to non-receipt of funds from the Central Government within the stipulated time, the Additional Chief Secretary (Finance) has been directed to ensure that the payment of honorarium to Anganwadi workers is made within the stipulated time.
In the meeting held on 2nd June, 2017, it was decided to allow the Department to spend the budgeted amount of Anganwadi workers' remuneration even if the expected Central assistance is not available.
