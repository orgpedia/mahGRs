GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MAVIM-2022/ PR. No.57/ K-10 2. District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of the said fund to Women Economic Development Corporation.
The Managing Director, Women's Economic Development Corporation, Bandra, Mumbai shall ensure that the expenditure on salary and non-salary matters of the Women's Economic Development Corporation is met from the funds distributed under this Government Decision.
The Managing Director, Women's Economic Development Corporation, Bandra, Mumbai shall take care of submitting the utilization certificate of the funds distributed under this Government decision to the Accountant General's Office and the Government.
5. Payments made under the signature of District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban and counter-signed by Deputy Secretary, Women and Child Development Department should be submitted to the Adhiveshan and Accounts Officer.
The accounts officer and the creditor must pay the amount by cheque. The Deputy Secretary, Women and Child Development Department has been authorised to counter-sign the payment submitted by the District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban.
(6) The funds disbursed under the said Government Decision shall be spent during the current financial year and the funds remaining unspent. The dedication before 31.3.2023 should be done by the Managing Director, Women's Economic Development Corporation, Bandra, Mumbai.
7. This Government Resolution was issued by the Finance Department, Informal Reference No. 72/ Expenditure-6, D. 6.3.2023 is being issued as per the concurrence given by the Department.
