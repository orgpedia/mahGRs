The Proposal:
As per the circular issued by the Finance Department, the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018 has been passed by the state legislature. As per the circular, the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programme expenditure for the first nine months of 2018-19 (ending December, 2018) will be distributed through the system (BEAMS) within the limit of 70% of the total annual allocation.
For the financial year 2018-19, a provision of Rs. 15,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifteen Crores only) has been approved for 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under head 2235-1316.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the circular issued by the Department of Finance, the Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 10,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crore Fifty Lakh only) to the Women's Economic Development Corporation (WEDC) to the extent of 70% of the available budgetary provision of Rs. 15,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifteen Crore only) for the financial year 2018-19 for 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under head 2235- 1316.
