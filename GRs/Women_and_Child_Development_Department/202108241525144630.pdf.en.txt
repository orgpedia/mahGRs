Government Decision: Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 temporary posts that are becoming redundant but need to be continued. The extension of the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 was granted vide Government Decision dated 9.3.2021.
Accordingly, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has now created a total of 5469 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 temporary posts that are pending but need to be continued, and a total of 176681 honorary posts of 81876 Anganwadi workers, 81876 Anganwadi helpers and 12929 mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. Proposal for extension of period from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 has been submitted vide letter dated 06.08.2021.

Under the Government Resolution No. 9 dated 10.08.2021 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office of temporary posts included in their rolls as well as all existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
The Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, State of Maharashtra, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 06.08.2021 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has created 5071 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 additional temporary posts, which are pending but need to be continued, a total of 5469 temporary posts and a total of 176681 honorary posts of 81876 Anganwadi workers, 81876 Anganwadi helpers and 12929 mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. The extension is being extended from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 10.08.2021 in Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
