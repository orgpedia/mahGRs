Introduction: The Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) is being implemented in all the districts of the State for out of school girls in the age group of 11 to 14 years. The scheme is being implemented by the office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The funding pattern for the scheme is 50%: 50%.
The Central share of 50% (Account Head 2236 1802) in the State Budget for the financial year 2019-20 for feeding expenses under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) is Rs. 8,15,14, 000.00 plus State Share of 50% (Account Head 22361811) Rs. A provision of Rs. 8,15,14, 000.00 has been made in the Budget.
The Department of Finance vide circular dated 08.07.2019 has approved the release and utilization of 60% of the budgetary provision to meet the expenditure on the schemes upto the month of December, 2019. Accordingly, the Government was considering to approve the allocation and expenditure for the scheme in the budget for the financial year 2019-20.
Decision of the Government: The Central share of 50% of the budget for the financial year 2019-20 (Demand No. X-1 Account Head Statement 2236 1802) for feeding expenses under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) has been increased to Rs. 8,15,14, 000.00 i.e. 60% of the provision of Rs. 4,89,08, 400.00 and State share of 50% (Demand No. X-1 Account Head Statement 22361811) of the provision amounting to 60% i.e. Rs. 4,89,08, 400.00 totalling to Rs. A sum of Rs. 9,78,16, 800.00 (Rupees Nine Crores Seventy Eight Lakhs Sixteen Thousand Eight Hundred only) is being approved for disbursement and expenditure.
