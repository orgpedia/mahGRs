The Proposal:
As per circular No.1 of the Finance Department under the head 2235-Social Security and Welfare under the Department of Women and Child Development, in the financial year 2018-19, the matter of sanctioning the remaining admissible funds of the annual budgetary allocation of mandatory and programmatic expenditure through the budget distribution system was under consideration.
Government's decision:
As per the Government Circular dated 25.1.2019 of the Department of Finance, for the financial year 2018-19, the limit of expenditure of the Budgetary Fund has been prescribed as 90% on the items of mandatory expenditure in the Revenue Account and 80% on the items of programme expenditure, 90% on the items of mandatory expenditure in the Capital Account and 100% on the items of programme expenditure. Accordingly, under the head of 2235-Social Security and Welfare under the Department of Women and Child Development, under various headings of the head of accounts, this Government decision is accompanied by a statement in column No. The government is being allowed to disburse through the budget distribution system as mentioned in section 5.
Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune will be the controlling authority for the fund.
The funds are being given to them. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune shall ensure strict compliance of the checklist items mentioned in the circular dated 25.1.2019 in Reference No.1 of the Finance Department and the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing norms, delegation of financial powers etc.
