Government's decision:
In pursuance of the said decision, it has been decided to grant registration certificate on non-subsidised basis for a period of five years from the date of issue of this decision to the proposal of 26 NGOs mentioned in Annexure-A under Section 41 of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 on the basis of the application for registration certificate made by the NGOs willing to take care of children under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 and the report received after physical inspection of the concerned institutions. Direct institution-wise registration certificates will be issued at the level of Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune.
""" 2. Attention is invited to the provisions of Rule 28 and in particular Rule 28 (6) of the Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018 for all the concerned institutions mentioned in Annexure-A and the compliance of the said rule shall be ensured by the concerned institutions as well as the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015, the Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018 and the rules made by the Central and State Governments from time to time."""
The institutions mentioned in Annexure-A shall be inspected every year and recommended to the Central Government for grant-in-aid annually under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme to those institutions which are functioning satisfactorily in compliance with the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. Under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme, grants will be provided to only those institutions which receive grants from the Central Government. Subject to these conditions, the institutions in" Annexure-A" should be given registration certificate.
As per the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 and the Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018, the number of admitted capacity/ sanctioned intake as per the type of department/ district and institution recognized in the state shall be as per" Annexure-B" and" Annexure-C."
