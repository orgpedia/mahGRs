Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, implemented by the Office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services, Navi Mumbai. The total expenditure on account of enhanced grant-in-aid to the employees will be Rs. 152265.81 lakh per annum as Central/ State and State share. Accordingly, the total expenditure on account of remuneration is expected to be Rs. 126.87 crore per month, on Central/ State and State share. The matter of disbursement of funds budgeted in the year 2019-20 for payment of honorarium to Anganwadi workers/ helpers/ mini Anganwadi workers was under consideration.
Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme with a funding pattern of 60:40 between the Centre and the States. There are instructions of the Finance Department that the budgeted shadow provision should not be spent without receiving funds from the Central Government. Due to the non-receipt of funds from the central government, it is not possible to pay the honorarium of Anganwadi workers on time. Approval to withdraw the amount of honorarium on minus authority
A request was made to the Department of Finance. Under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Finance) dated 2
The meeting was held in June 2017. In the meeting, it has been decided to give permission to the department to spend the budgeted amount of the salary of the servitors even if it does not receive funds from the central government.
