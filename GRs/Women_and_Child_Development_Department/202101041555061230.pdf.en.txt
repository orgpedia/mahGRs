The period of implementation of International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women Entrepreneurship Development Project" was under consideration of the Government to approve the loan and interest taken and the change in the repayment period.
Government's decision:
The following changes are being approved in the period of implementation of International Agriculture Development Fund assisted" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women's Enterprise Development Project," the loans taken and the interest thereon as well as the repayment period.
The Government has approved the implementation of" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women's Enterprise Development Project" through Mahila Economic Development Corporation (Mavim) under the Department of Women and Child Development for a period of six years from 2020-21 to 2025-26.
2) To make available funds amounting to$ 51.60 million (₹ 335.40 crore) as loan from IFAD for implementation of International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nava Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women Entrepreneurship Development Project" and to provide funds amounting to$ 29.86 million (₹ 193.15 crore) towards administrative expenses for implementation of the said programme with State Government share aggregating to$ 81.46 million (₹ 528.55 crore). 2 here. The approval was granted in accordance with the Government Resolution dated 8.1.2019. However, in respect of change in the cost of International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women Entrepreneurship Development Project," it is being approved to make available funds as loan from IFAD to the tune of$ 51.40 million (₹ 334.10 crore) against the cost of$ 81.46 million (₹ 528.55 crore) and for administrative expenses for implementation of the said programme, a total amount of$ 80.60 million (₹ 523.00 crore) including State Government share of$ 29.20 million (₹ 188.88 crore) is being provided.
3). Since India is covered under the Ordinary Terms (Market Rate) category of IFAD since 1.1.2018, the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) assisted" Nav Tejaswini-Maharashtra Rural Women Entrepreneurship Development Project" is being approved to take loans from IFAD on Ordinary Term (Market Rate) basis instead of Blend Term basis.
4) Repayment of loan from International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) commences after completion of moratorium period of 5 years.
