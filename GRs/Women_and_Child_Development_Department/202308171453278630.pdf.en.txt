Terms and Conditions: (a) Rural/ Tribal Areas: The fixed monthly rent of Anganwadi/ Mini Anganwadi buildings in rural and tribal areas is Rs.
2,000/- will be accepted. The monthly rental will be determined by local taxes, maintenance, other incidental taxes and electricity bill.
