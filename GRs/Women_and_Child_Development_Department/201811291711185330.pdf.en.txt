GOVERNMENT ORDER: As the number of vacancies in the posts of Child Development Project Officer (Rural) in Group-B cadre under the Department of Women and Child Development is high, the officers interested in making deputation to the vacant posts in the direct service quota out of the said posts are requested to submit their proposal. The letter was sent to all the ministries and the regional offices under them. Accordingly, Mr. Uddhav Namdev Sanap, Agriculture Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed (Group-B Cadre) (Pay Scale Rs. 9300-34800 Grade Pay Rs.4400) has been recommended for appointment as Child Development Project Officer (Rural) in the Department of Women and Child Development with concurrence from the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed and Rural Development Department.
Child Development Project Officer (Rural), Ashti, G. Beed (Group-B Cadre), Women and Child Development Department of Agriculture Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed (Group-B Cadre) (Pay Scale Rs. 9300-34800 Grade Pay 4400) is appointed on deputation for a period of two years initially or till the appointment of an officer on direct service to the said post, whichever is earlier.
The services of Sanap on deputation as Child Development Project Officer (Rural), Ashti, G. Beed will be subject to the following conditions:
(a) The service of the Deputy Commissioner shall commence from the date on which the said officer enters upon the office of Deputy Commissioner. Thereafter, the period of deputation shall end on the date on which they assume charge of the post of deputation and join their parent department.
