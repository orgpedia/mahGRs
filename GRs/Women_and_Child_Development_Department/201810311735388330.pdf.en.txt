The services of Dr. Manjusha Kulkarni, Assistant Professor, Government Vidarbha Institute of Knowledge Sciences, Amravati, are being rendered by deputation as Administrative Officer in the office of Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights, Worli, Mumbai for a period of one year subject to the following conditions:
(a) Dr. Manjusha Kulkarni shall commence the service of the Deputy Commissioner from the date on which she enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner, and shall, on the date on which she enters upon the office of the Deputy Commissioner, cease to hold the office of the Deputy Commissioner.
(b) The Government/ competent authority shall reserve the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(e) If their services are not required by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be entitled to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the Government/ Competent Authority
and
(e) they shall be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent department.
The terms and conditions for the deputation of Dr. Manjusha Kulkarni will be decided in consultation with the Department of Higher and Technical Education after taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the Rules, 1981.
