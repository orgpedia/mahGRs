The Proposal:
Read about the Department of Revenue and Forestry. In accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision dated 25/7/2018, the Department is required to provide funds for the implementation of the scheme" Tree Plantation and Maintenance" in each administrative division within the limit of 0.5 percent of the overall budgetary allocation available to the Department for the scheme" Expenditure on the Programme." For the year 2019-20, Rs. An amount of Rs. 1 crore has been earmarked for this.

Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government's decision:
For the tree plantation scheme, in the state budget for the year 2019-20, under the heading of 2406 A 435 Tree Plantation and Cultivation, Rs. 1 crore has been earmarked. Out of this, Rs. 25 lakh and Rs. 75 lakh has been provided. Out of this provision, 60 per cent has been made available by the Department of Finance on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System. Accordingly, Rs. 15 lakh and Rs. 45 lakh has been provided.
For the financial year 2019-20, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai have been allocated a total amount of Rs. A target has been set to plant 2,09,750 trees. 25/- has been fixed as the rate of cost of tree plantation. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai were given Rs. 26,81,250/- (Rupees Twenty Six Lakhs Eighty One Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) and Rs. 25,62,500/- (Rupees Twenty Five Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand Five Hundred only) aggregating to Rs. 52,43,750/- (Rupees Fifty Two Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty only) is being approved by the Government to be disbursed and spent as follows.
