The Proposal:
The Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. The Juvenile Justice Committee constituted under the aegis of the Hon'ble High Court of Judicature at Bombay had suggested to the Government to develop a comprehensive computer system for effective implementation of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015.
Accordingly, a computerized system has been created to make all the activities online, collect information, control information in line with the effective implementation of the Juvenile Justice Act.
