The decision is being taken based on a High Court order.
Provisional promotion from Group-B cadre to the posts of District Women and Child Development Officer and similar vacancies in Group-A cadre is being made subject to the following conditions: 1. The said promotion has been made in public interest as an administrative exigency in a very temporary manner.
2. The officers concerned will not be entitled to any regular promotion i. e. seniority due to this temporary promotion.
(3) The promotion of the above officers shall be subject to the final seniority of the Group A cadre and consequential changes therein and the promotion shall not entitle the above officers to seniority from the date of such promotion.
Since the promotion is purely temporary in nature, the officers promoted as a result of this temporary promotion shall not be entitled to any regularisation and extension of service.
The Government reserves the right to cancel the temporary promotion given to the said officers without any prior notice or notice.
The promoted officers are informed that they are required to report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the order of promotion/ posting.
If they do not appear at the place of appointment within the prescribed time after being discharged, the government decides that they are not interested in the promotion.
