Government's decision:
As per the figures fixed for the urban projects under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, a total of 32134 temporary/ honorarium posts, including 1070 working temporary posts in the regular pay scale in 104 urban projects and a total of 31064 honorarium posts of 15532 Anganwadi workers and 15532 Anganwadi helpers in 15532 Anganwadis, were extended from 1/1/2018 to 28/2/2018 as per the Government decision dated 20.1.2018.
2.
