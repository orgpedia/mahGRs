The Proposal:
During the financial year 2017-18, the budgetary provision available for 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under head 2235-2161 was Rs. 1,82,01, 600/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Two Lakh One Thousand Six Hundred Only) subject to 80% of the budgetary provision of Rs. 2,27,52, 000/- (Rupees Two Crore Twenty Seven Lakh Twenty Two Thousand Only). As per the Government decision dated 23rd May, 2017 and 31 Supplementary Grants (Non-Payment), the budgetary provision available for this subject is Rs. 3,96,55, 000/- (Rupees Three Crore Sixty Five Lakh Fifty Thousand only) within the limit of 70% of the budgetary provision of Rs. 5,66,50, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore Sixty Six Lakh Fifty Thousand only). It has been circulated to the Maharashtra State Women's Commission vide Government Resolution dated 22nd August, 2017. Finance Department No. 5 As per the Circular herein, for the financial year 2017-18, out of the available budgetary provision of Rs. 2,27,52, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Twenty Seven Lakhs Twenty Two Thousand only) for 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under the head 2235-2161, 20% of the available undistributed provision of Rs. 45,50,400/- (Rupees Forty Five Lakhs Fifty Thousand Four Hundred only) and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaries) under the head 2235-2161 is available out of the available budgetary provision of Rs. 5,66,50, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores Sixty Six Lakh Fifty Thousand only).

Government Resolution No. Rama- 2017/ PR No.62/ Ka-10 (Rupees One Crore Thirteen Lakh Thirty Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government for disbursement to the Maharashtra State Commission for Women.
Government's decision:
During the financial year 2017-18, the Government hereby approve to disburse to the Maharashtra State Commission for Women the undistributed amount of Rs. 45,50,400/- (Rupees Forty Five Lakhs Fifty Thousand Four Hundred only) and Rs. 1,13,30, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Thirteen Lakh Thirty Thousand only) for 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under head 2235-2161 and 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salaries).
The Maharashtra State Women's Commission is being given approval for the distribution of funds subject to the condition that the utilization certificate of the entire funds distributed under the said head of accounts should be submitted to the government.
District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of the said fund to Maharashtra State Commission for Women.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall disburse this fund to the Maharashtra State Women's Commission. However, without drawing the said funds from the treasury immediately, the District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) shall examine the expenditure/ fund requirement of the Maharashtra State Commission for Women and submit the payment of expenditure to the treasury. It should be ensured that the disinvestment funds are not left with the State Women's Commission. Also, the Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Commission for Women, Mumbai and the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune should take care of submitting the utility certificate to the government.
