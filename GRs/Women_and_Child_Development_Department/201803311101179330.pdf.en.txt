Government's decision:
Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district level, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district levels under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 189 additional temporary posts that are pending but need to be continued.
A total of 1,76,897 posts of Anganwadi Workers were extended from 1/1/2018 to 28/2/2018 as per the Government decision dated 20.1.2018 at Reference No.7.
Accordingly, now the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has sanctioned 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the figure fixed by the Central Government, Women and Child Development for Zilla Parishad under Palghar district.

Under the Government Order dated 5.3.2018 in Reference No.9 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 1.3.2018 to 30.9.2018 for the temporary posts included in the figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 27.3.2018 at Reference No.8 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the above decision of the Finance Department, a total of 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 189 additional temporary posts, which are due but need to be continued, a total of 5,494 temporary posts and a total of 1,76,595 honorary posts (total of 182089 temporary/ honorary posts) of 8,1943 Anganwadi workers, 8,1943 Anganwadi helpers and 12,709 mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments by the Government Order dated 5.3.2018 in Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
