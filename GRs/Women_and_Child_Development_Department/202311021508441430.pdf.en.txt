The Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 and the General Administration Department. As per the Government decision dated 21.10.2011, it is mandatory to publish the Service Excellence List every year with 1st January as the base date.
The final seniority list of Group-A cadre officers of the class under the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development has been issued as per Government Circular No. MBAA-2022/ PR No. 74/ K. 7, dated 31.10.2022.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune handles the administrative and organisational/ service matters in the case of officers/ staff working under the office of the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The respective ranks of the officers of these two Commissionerates are being published in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 and the final seniority list in respect of the said officers as per the relevant Government Decision/ Government Circular issued in this regard.
