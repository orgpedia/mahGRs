Under the Mission Vatsalya scheme, employees of childcare institutions are also paid salaries for the same. At present, these employees are providing their Par since it is an essential service to serve in a childcare institution.
However, the salaries of these essential service employees also need to be paid on time.
Under this scheme, the government is considering disbursing the required funds for the salaries of 37 government and non-government children's homes / observation homes from the budgeted funds for the year 2025-26 to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Child Protection Society, Pune and Commissioner, Women and Child Development.
Government Decision: - Raising the title / objectives of the statement of accounts under Mission Vatsalya Scheme No. 5 is being distributed by this Government Decision to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Child Protection Society, Pune and Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune.
