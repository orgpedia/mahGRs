GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: A total of 1590 posts including 1542 on pay and 48 honorary posts in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development and a total of 2306 temporary posts including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms have been extended from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 as per the Government Decision dated 13.09.2021 at Reference No.3.
2. Under the Government decision dated 22.02.2022 in Reference No.5 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
It has been certified vide letter dated 04.03.2022 at Reference No.4 that 1590 temporary posts in the establishment of Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune and 716 posts filled by external agencies are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the aforesaid Government Order of the Finance Department, a total of 1590 posts including 1542 on pay and 48 honorary posts in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development Department and a total of 2306 temporary posts including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms are being extended from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
