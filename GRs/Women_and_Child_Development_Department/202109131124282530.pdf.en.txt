Government's decision:
A total of 1590 posts, including 1542 on salary and 48 on honorarium, have been created in the establishment of the Women and Child Development Commissionerate. 01.03.2021 to dt. The extension was granted vide Government Decision dated 22.02.2021 at Reference No.3 to continue till 31.09.2021.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 10.08.2021 in Reference No.5 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls.
The letter dated 20.08.2021 in Reference No.4 certifies that 1590 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune and 716 posts filled by the external system have not been vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provision of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, a total of 1590 posts, including 1542 on-pay and 48 honorary posts, have been filled in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development Department. The date is being extended from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
