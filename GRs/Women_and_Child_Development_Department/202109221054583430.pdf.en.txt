3. Regarding promotion of Divyang officers to Group A and Group B posts. High Court of Bombay. As per the decision dated 22.7.2021, all the vacancies for promotion including Divyang reservation will be filled up by the General Administration Department, Govt. 5.7.2021 and D. The promotion of Divyang officers is being done as per the approval received in accordance with the instructions given under the Government letter dated 23.7.2021.
Provisional promotion of the said officers from Group-B cadre to the posts of District Women and Child Development Officer and similar vacancies in Group-A cadre subject to the following conditions:
1. The said promotion has been granted in public interest as an administrative exigency in a very temporary manner.
2. The officers concerned will not be entitled to any regular promotion i. e. seniority due to this temporary promotion.
(3) The promotion of the above officers shall be subject to the final seniority of Group A cadre and consequential changes therein and the promotion shall not entitle the above officers to seniority from the date of such promotion.
Since the promotion is purely temporary in nature, the officers promoted as a result of this temporary promotion shall not be entitled to any regularisation and extension of service.
The Government reserves the right to cancel the temporary promotion given to the said officers without any prior notice or notice.
The promoted officers are informed that they are required to report at the place of posting immediately on receipt of the order of promotion/ posting. If they do not appear at the place of appointment within the prescribed time after being discharged, then they are not interested in the promotion, the Government Decision, General Administration Department, D. 30/04/1991 and D. As per the provision mentioned in 12/09/2016, their promotion from the government level will be cancelled.
Also, if the officer who refuses promotion is given the benefit of Group-A cadre post under the Assured Progress Scheme, it should be stopped immediately and he should be directly transferred to Group-A cadre.
Please note that this benefit will be given after the promotion.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The Controlling Authorities shall relieve the officer who has been given the said appointment/ posting immediately to appear in place of the new appointment. Further, the date on which the concerned officer was relieved to appear in place of the new appointee and the date on which he appeared in place of the new appointee has been communicated to the Government by the concerned Controlling Authorities on wcd- desk07@gov.in. Inform us immediately by e-mail.
