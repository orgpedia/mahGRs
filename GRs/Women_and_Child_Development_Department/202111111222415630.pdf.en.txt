Decision of the Government dated 14.10.2021: Poshan Abhiyaan, a flagship programme based on holistic nutrition, is being implemented under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. The Centre and state share in the scheme is 80:20. The Commissioner and State Project Director, State Nutrition Resource Cell, Poshan Abhiyan, Navi Mumbai has informed that as per the directions of the Central Government, the advance amount for various activities under Poshan Abhiyan for the year 2021-22 is required to be disbursed and the necessary funds have been made available by the Central Government for this purpose and it is required to be provided for various purposes at the field level for spending in this financial year.
