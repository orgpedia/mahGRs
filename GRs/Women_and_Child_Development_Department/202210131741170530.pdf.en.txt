Government Resolution, Department of Women and Child Development, No. Children's Home-2012/ No. 164/ K-3, dated 04.12.2012.
Preamble:- It has been approved to organize Chacha Nehru Bal Mahotsav in the second week of November every year in 35 districts and 6 divisional levels in order to develop brotherhood and team spirit among the orphan, destitute and destitute children admitted for rehabilitation in all the children of the state and government/ non-government organizations working in the field of child welfare on priority. 2,09,95, 000/- has been approved. The matter of disbursement of funds to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune for organizing Chacha Nehru Bal Mahotsav for children in the financial year 2022-23 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In order to inculcate the spirit of brotherhood and team spirit among the orphan, destitute and destitute children admitted for rehabilitation in all the children and government/ non-government organizations working in the field of child welfare in the state, the Government is approving the disbursement of a sum of Rs. 2,09,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crore Nine Lakhs only) to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune under the head 2235-B-325, 31 Auxiliary Grants (Non-Paid) from the funds to be made available under this scheme as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 04.12.2012.
For this, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune shall disburse the said funds as per the prescribed norms. Also, the utilisation certificate of the fund should be submitted to the government.
