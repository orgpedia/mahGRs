The proposal-
The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retired Additional Chief Secretary to take into consideration the decisions of the Central Government on the recommendations of the 7th Central Pay Commission and to revise the pay scales of State Government and other eligible employees.
The Government has decided to accept the recommendations of the Committee with certain modifications. The government has taken 1 decision. As per the decision of the Government, the scope of the recommendations on pay scales and their corollaries in the State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 Report Vol. 4.13." shall be applicable to eligible State Government employees, Zilla Parishads, Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat, non-judicial employees of courts, aided school employees, employees of agricultural and non-agricultural universities and their affiliated non-aided colleges and employees of other aided institutions." The government has decided to implement the recommendations of the 6th Pay Commission for the employees of other subsidized institutions. Accordingly, the matter of implementing the revised pay scale as per the Seventh Pay Commission for the employees of the Maharashtra State Inspection and Maintenance Organization, Pune office was under the consideration of the government.

Government's decision
The revised pay structure is being sanctioned to various cadres under the office of Maharashtra State Pariksha and Maintenance Organization, Pune as mentioned in the accompanying Annexure-A in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission.
Regarding fixation of pay and payment of arrears in the revised pay structure, Reference No. The Rules for fixation of pay in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 notified under Section 3 and Reference No. Action should be taken as per the instructions issued in the circular regarding fixation of salary and payment of arrears under sections 4, 5 and 6.
(2) Reference No. 1 regarding fixation of pay and payment of arrears in the revised pay structure. As per Annexure-III of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 notified under Section 3, the option required for fixation of pay in the revised pay structure shall be taken within two months from the date of issue of the said Government decision and the undertaking shall be taken in accordance with Annexure-IV.
3. The additional expenditure on account of the revised pay structure implemented under the said Government Decision vide Demand No. Grants to NGOs (02) (03) Subsidiary Grants to NGOs running institutions for children in need of care and protection (2235-3041).
