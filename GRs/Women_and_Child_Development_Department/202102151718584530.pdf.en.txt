Introduction: The Government of Maharashtra. To know the background of the 3R Women Policy announced on 01.03.2014 and to know the details of the policy announced by Hon. Minister of Women and Child Development. This was reviewed in the meeting held on 25.11.2020. In this meeting, the government decided to form a drafting committee under the chairmanship of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune, to review the provisions of the Women Policy 2014, make changes in it according to the time and make it more people-oriented. As per 17.12.2020, a Drafting Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune. As per the decision, the committee constituted for the purpose of selecting representatives of UNICEF, Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai and the organization working in the field of women, seeking nominations from the concerned organization, as well as studying the policy/ charter adopted by the Government of India at the national level, at the international level, in other countries of the world, at the national level, and other states of the country, etc. on women, the situation in the state of Maharashtra is being studied thoroughly and the process of drafting a time-bound and people-oriented women's policy for women in the state is being done from the Commissionerate level. An extension is requested under section 2.
In view of the above facts, the matter of extending the time for submission of the said draft to the Government was under consideration.
Government Decisions: The Government has taken decisions to review the provisions of the Women Policy 2014, make changes in it in accordance with the times and to make it more people-oriented. 17.12.2020 to the Drafting Committee headed by Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune. The government has extended the deadline till February 28, 2021.
