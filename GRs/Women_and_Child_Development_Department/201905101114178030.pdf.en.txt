As per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Excellence in Service) Rules, 1982 and the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department, the merit list is required to be published every year with 1st January as the base date.
Provisional list of officers of the cadre of Deputy Commissioner Group-A working under the office of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai up to the end of 01.01.2019 It was published on 08.04.2019.
No objections or representations have been received in this regard. Therefore, the final service availability as on 01.01.2019 is being released as per the prospectus attached with the list.
