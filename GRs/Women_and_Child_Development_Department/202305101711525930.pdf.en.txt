Government Supplementary Letter: Orphans should be provided reservation of 1% of the available posts in education and recruitment in government (semi-government and government-aided institutions) posts on the lines of disabled persons, and the posts available from the orphan reservation should be divided into two categories of institutional and non-institutional, the eligibility criteria for the orphan reservation, the nature of the reservation, the terms and conditions, the guidelines for the implementation of the reservation, the procedure for issuing the certificate for orphans and the sample of the orphan certificate. 8 The decision has been made.
Issue No. 2 (Sub-issue No. 4) in the form of reservation is being added as follows:" In a recruitment process, if the seats available for one category of orphans through the orphan reservation are not adequately available to the orphan candidates of that category according to the merit, then in that recruitment process, those posts should be filled up from the candidates of the second category of orphans according to the merit. " This is the provision. The new exams will be held after 31/10/2022.
