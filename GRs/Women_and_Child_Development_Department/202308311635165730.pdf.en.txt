The Proposal:
With a view to ensure effective management of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 1 here. dated 23.3.2021
Instructions have been given to adopt improved procedures. Accordingly, the Finance Department vide No. 2 here. The circular dated 1.7.2021 provides revised instructions on Public Finance Management System (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
Accordingly, it has been directed to appoint a single nodal agency for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and at least a deputy secretary-level officer as nodal officer.
Accordingly, the government was considering the appointment of a single nodal officer and a single nodal agency for the component schemes of the centrally sponsored umbrella scheme" Mission Vatsalya"-" Institutional Care/ Sponsorship/ Maintenance";" Creation of Capital Assets";" Swachhata Action Plan" and" Child Helpline."
