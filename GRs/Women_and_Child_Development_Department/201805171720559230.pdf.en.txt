The Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Award is being given to social workers and institutions of the state under the Department of Women and Child Development since 1996-97 with a view to recognize and encourage the work of social workers and institutions doing excellent work in the field of women and child development.
A proposal for the selection of State, Divisional and District level Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Awardees for the year 2013-14 and 2014-15 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The following are the State, Divisional and District level Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Awardees for the year 2013-14 and 2014-15.
