The Proposal:
The 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai were extended from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2022 vide Government Order No.4 dated 30.08.2022. Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 13.02.2023 at Reference No.5 has submitted a proposal for extension of 43 temporary posts.
(ii) Under the Government Order dated 08.02.2023 in Reference No.6 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier Review but not included in the rolls.
The said posts are not vacant for more than six months, as stated by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide No.5 dated

Government's decision:
The Government have decided to extend the term of 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. The details of the posts along with the pay scale and cadre-wise details are given in the prospectus.
The said Government Order is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Order dated 08.02.2023 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
