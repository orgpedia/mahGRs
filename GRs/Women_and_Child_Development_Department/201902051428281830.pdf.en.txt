The Proposal:
In accordance with the circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, it is under consideration to approve the disbursement of the remaining 30% of the annual budgetary allocation of mandatory and programme expenditure in the financial year 2018-19 and the remaining 20% of the budgetary allocation of the funds admissible under the head 2235-Social Security and Welfare through the Budget Distribution System.
Government's decision:
Demand No. for Expenditure for the Financial Year 2018-19 X-1 Main Accounts Head 2235-Social Security and Welfare (01) Directorate of Women and Child Welfare Mandatory (2235 2072), and (01) (03) Establishment Grants to Zilla Parishads under Section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961 (Local Area Mandatory (2235 2321)) 36- Salaries. The government is being allowed to disburse through the budget distribution system as mentioned in section 5.
Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune will be the controlling authority for the fund. The funds are being given to them. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune shall ensure strict compliance of the checklist items mentioned in the circular dated 25.1.2019 in Reference No.1 of the Finance Department and the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing norms, delegation of financial powers etc.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune shall ensure that the funds made available on the Budget Distribution System under the above head for the year 2018-19 are spent and submit the utilization certificate to the Government.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the provisions of Circular No. 1 dated 25.1.2019 issued in the context of the Department of Finance.
