Introduction: - The Manordharya Scheme for rehabilitation of victims of rape, child sexual abuse, as well as acid attacks has been launched in the state with effect from the date 21.10.2023. Thereafter, the scheme was revised to Manodhairya Yojana. 21.10.2013, d. 25.11.2013, d. 26.08.2014, d. 01.08.2017, d. 30.12.2017, d. 08.02.2018, d. 16.02.2018 and d. 16.10.2018 All these government decisions were superseded by a single comprehensive government decision on implementing the revised ATs scheme. Issued on 01.01.2024. In this Government decision, victims of attacks caused by flammable / flammable substances (petrol, diesel, rockel, etc.) have also been included in the Manodhairya Yojana in the same manner as victims of acid attacks / injuries in the revised Manodhairya Yojana. The Prevention of Immoral Traffic Act, 1956 also allows the benefit of the mental health scheme to victims under the age of 18 who have been released from police custody.
The central government replaced the Indian Penal Code (IPC) with the Indian Penal Code (898), 2023, and the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 with the Indian Civil Protection Code, 2023 (BNSS). have been implemented since 01.07.2024.
