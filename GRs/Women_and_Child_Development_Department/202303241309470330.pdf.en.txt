The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune vide letter dated 23.02.2023 has certified that the said posts are not lying vacant for more than six months.
Accordingly, the matter of extending the temporary posts of Class-3 and Class-4 in the district cadre of the Women and Child Development Department of the Zilla Parishad was under the consideration of the government.
