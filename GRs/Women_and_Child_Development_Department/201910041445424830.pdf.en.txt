The Proposal:
The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, implemented since 1975-76. The Government of India has allocated Rs. 2019-20 crore for the scheme. 319.78 crore has been approved for APIP, out of which the Central Government has released Rs. 79.94 crore. The proposal for Supplementary Demand for Wages for the year 2019-20 has been approved in the June, 2019 session for an amount of Rs. 213.01 crore. The Finance Department has approved the release of 60% of the amount. Accordingly, funds were allocated for the purpose.
Government's decision:
For the financial year 2019-20, vide Demand No.X-1 Main Account Head 2236-POSHAN AHAAR, the Government is approving to disburse the funds for the salaries of the officers/ staff working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as mentioned in Column No. 6 of the Statement accompanying the said Government Resolution under the respective head of accounts through the Budget Distribution System.
