Government's decision:
The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. In order to make it possible to pay the honorarium of Anganwadi workers due to non-receipt of funds from the Central Government within the stipulated period, it has been decided in the meeting held on June 2, 2017 under the chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Finance) to allow the department to spend the budgeted amount of honorarium of Anganwadi workers even if the expected central assistance is not available.
