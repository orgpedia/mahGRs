The Proposal:
Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) programme of the Department of Women and Child Development, hot fresh food and THR are distributed to beneficiaries in anganwadis under the centrally sponsored scheme of" Purak Poshan Aahar." Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) scheme, the central and state share was to be shared in the ratio of 50: 50 for the centrally sponsored scheme of" Purak Poshan Aahar."
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government have approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 224.80 crore (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Four Crores Eighty Lakhs only) for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Purak Poshan Aahar" under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Programme under the Department of Women and Child Development as per the head of accounts/ targets in Racket No. 2 of the Statement of Objects for Central and State share.
