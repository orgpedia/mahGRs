The Proposal:
Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) scheme of the Women and Child Development Department, free' Baby Care Kits' are provided to women who are enrolled in the government primary health centres/ government hospitals during pregnancy and delivery at the time of their first delivery. Accordingly, for this purpose, the matter of release of funds for payment of Baby Care Kits ordered in the Budget Year 2019-20 and completed by the end of September, 2020 was under consideration.
