PUNE: Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune has been directed to clear the pending 80% grant for the current year 2022-23 of the institutions run by NGOs for children under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 under the Women and Child Development Department. 1 here has been distributed as per the government decision.
2. Read paragraph no. 3 is as follows.
" The Supreme Court in its order in Writ Petition No. In order to avoid undue delay in disbursement of funds as per the directions issued vide Order No. 4/ 2020, the said funds shall be disbursed from the Commissionerate level to the concerned District Women and Child Development Officer within seven days from the date of the decision of the Government and the concerned District Women and Child Development Officer shall ensure that the funds are disbursed to the concerned institution within seven days from the date of receipt of the same from the Commissionerate. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development and the concerned District Women and Child Development shall be responsible for disbursement of funds within the prescribed time frame as above. "
Instead of the above paragraph, read as follows.
" The Supreme Court in its order in Writ Petition No. As per the directions given in 4/2020, to avoid unnecessary delay in the disbursement of funds, the said funds should be immediately disbursed to the concerned institution from the Commissionerate level. "
