Introduction: The scheme of" Hostels for Working Women" is being implemented to provide safe and comfortable accommodation to working single women, unmarried, widows, divorcees, separated, out of wedlock women at the place of employment. Central government. As per the guidelines in the decision dated 22.11.2017, the revised scheme is to be implemented in the ratio of 60:15:25 between Centre: State: Non-Governmental Organisations by making available on lease the hostel building for employed women by making partial changes in the existing scheme. dated 11.08.2021. The decision was taken by the Women and Child Development Ministry. Approval has been granted as on 23.08.2021.
The government was considering opening a new account for this scheme.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the approval given by the Accountant General, Office, Mumbai, Finance Department and Planning Department, the Government is approving the opening of new head of accounts as follows for making budgetary provision for Central share (60%) and State share (15%) for the revised scheme of Hostels for employed women.
