The Proposal:
As per the circular issued by the Finance Department, the Maharashtra Appropriation Bill, 2018 has been passed by the state legislature. As per the circular, the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programme expenditure for the first nine months of 2018-19 (ending December, 2018) will be distributed through the system (BEAMS) within the limit of 70% of the total annual allocation.
For the financial year 2018-19, a provision of Rs. 10,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores only) has been approved for 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) under head 2235-3177.
As per the circular of the Finance Department under the head 2235-3177, out of the funds available for non-salary matters, a proposal was under the consideration of the Government to disburse a sum ofâ‚¹ 1,27,12, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Seven Lakh Twelve Thousand only) for the Counselling Centre Scheme as demanded by the Deputy Commissioner (Women Development), Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune.
Government Decision As per the circular issued by the Finance Department, out of the budget of Rs. 10,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crore only) for the Counselling Centre Scheme for the financial year 2018-19, the Deputy Commissioner (Women Development), Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune, has been requested to release an amount of Rs. 1,27,12, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy Seven Lakh Twelve Thousand only) as Auxiliary Grant for the remuneration of Counsellors and Coordinators by the end of July, 2018.
Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune is being authorized to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 1,27,12, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Seven Lakh Twelve Thousand only).
