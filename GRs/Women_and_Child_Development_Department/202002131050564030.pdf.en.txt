the decision
As per the provisions of Rule 10 (4) and 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, a committee is being constituted to examine the eligibility of Group-A and Group-B cadre officers at the zonal level in the Women and Child Development Department to remain in service beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completing 30 years of qualifying service.
