Government's decision:
Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, a total of 5071 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 temporary posts that are pending but need to be continued, a total of 5469 temporary posts and a total of 176681 honorary posts of 81876 Anganwadi workers, 81876 Anganwadi helpers and 12929 Mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. The extension of the period from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2021 was granted vide Government Decision dated 24.08.2021.
Accordingly, now the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, has created 5071 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme.

Under the Government Resolution dated 22.02.2022 in Reference No.9 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 for the temporary posts included in the figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the figure.
The Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 03.03.2022 at Reference No.8 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the above decision of the Finance Department, the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has created 5071 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 temporary posts that are pending but need to be continued. The extension is being extended from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 22.02.2022 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
