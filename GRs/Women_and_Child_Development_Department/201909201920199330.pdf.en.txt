The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retired Additional Chief Secretary, to revise the pay scales of State Government and other eligible employees, taking into consideration the decisions of the Central Government on the recommendations of the Seventh Central Pay Commission. The Government has decided to accept the recommendations of the Committee with certain modifications. The government has taken 1 decision. As per the decision of the Government, the scope of the recommendations on pay scales and their corollaries in the State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 Report Vol. 4.13." shall be applicable to eligible State Government employees, Zilla Parishads, Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat, non-judicial employees of courts, aided school employees, employees of agricultural and non-agricultural universities and their affiliated non-aided colleges and employees of other aided institutions." The government has decided to implement the recommendations of the 6th Pay Commission for the employees of other subsidized institutions.
Accordingly, the matter of implementing the revised pay scale as per the Seventh Pay Commission for the officers/ employees of the Maharashtra State Women's Commission was under the consideration of the Government.
The revised pay structure is being sanctioned to the officers/ employees of Maharashtra State Commission for Women under the Department of Women and Child Development as mentioned in the accompanying Annexure-A in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission.
