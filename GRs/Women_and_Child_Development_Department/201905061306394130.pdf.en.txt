No. 14-4/2018-CD-II (e-66710), dated 25/3/2019 Decision: The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme which was started in the State of Maharashtra in 1975. Gradually, the number of projects under the scheme has increased and projects have been implemented in various urban, rural and tribal areas of the state. Under this scheme, 6 important services such as Anganwadi services for children, pregnant women and lactating mothers will be provided. The services of immunization, pre-primary education, supplementary nutrition, referral services, health check-up, health and nutrition education to women in the age group of 15 to 45 years, etc. are provided. Child Development Project Officers and other necessary machinery are also working at every project level for the implementation of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme.
The rates and parameters of various aspects of Anganwadi services were revised as per the Government decision dated 30th June, 2018. The revised rates were applicable for the period from 1st April, 2017 to 30/11/2017 and from 1/12/2017 to 30/11/2018. The Central Government vide its letter dated 14th November, 2018 has notified that the revised rates will be applicable till 30th March, 2019 and the revised rates will be applicable till 31st March, 2020.
The Government is approving the revised rates of various activities/ schemes being implemented under the Umbrella Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) till 31st March, 2020, as mentioned in the statement attached with the Government Resolution dated 30th June, 2018. Separate orders regarding the increase in the honorarium of Anganwadi workers/ mini Anganwadi workers/ helpers by the Central Government will be issued in due course.
