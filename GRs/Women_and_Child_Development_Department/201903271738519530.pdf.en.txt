Government's decision:
In the current financial year under the Child Care Scheme, Demand No. Ex-01, 2235, Social Security and Welfare, 02, Social Welfare, 102, Child Welfare, (15) (01) Subsidiary Grants to NGOs for Child Care Scheme (Programme) (2235 B906), 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) Rs. A sum of Rs. 15.00 crore has been allocated for this purpose. 12.00 crore has been made available for expenditure at the field level. In view of the demand for the current financial year from the grants available in the current financial year for the Child Care Scheme, the request for approval to pay the pending grants of the schemes from the balance fund is being considered in Reference No. 3 has been written. In view of the above request, the pending grants of the Scheme prior to March, 2018 are being allowed to be spent from the balance of the current financial year subject to the following condition: Since the grants of the Child Care Scheme are to be disbursed under the Subsidiary Grants, the utilization certificate of the expenditure of the Scheme is required to be submitted to the Government. In this utility certificate, the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer will be required to submit self-explanatory information about the number of beneficiaries of the scheme in the current financial year (institution level and District Women and Child Development Officer level) and the funds spent accordingly, and the number of beneficiaries (institution level and District Women and Child Development Officer level) and the funds spent accordingly in the relevant financial year in accordance with the pending grants. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development will be the controlling authority for obtaining the Utilization Certificate. If the above information is not mentioned in the utility certificate, the utility certificate will not be accepted and the responsibility will be fixed on the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
