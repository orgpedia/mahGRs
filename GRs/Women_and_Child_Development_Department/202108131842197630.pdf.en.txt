Introduction: The Government of Maharashtra. To know the background of the 3R Women Policy announced on 01.03.2014 and to know the details of the policy announced by Hon. Minister of Women and Child Development. This was reviewed in the meeting held on 25.11.2020. In this meeting, the government decided to form a drafting committee under the chairmanship of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune, to review the provisions of the Women Policy 2014, make changes in it over time and make the policy more people-oriented. As per 17.12.2020, a Drafting Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune.
It will take time to draft a time-bound and people-oriented women's policy for women in the state. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune has requested for extension till 30.09.2021. dated 20.07.2021.
In view of the above facts, the matter of extending the time for submission of the said draft to the Government was under consideration.
Government Decisions: The Government has taken decisions to review the provisions of the Women Policy 2014, make changes in it from time to time and to make it more people-oriented. 17.12.2020 to the Drafting Committee headed by Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune. The government has decided to extend the deadline till September 30, 2021.
The extension is final and will not be extended further. The reason The responsibility of submitting the draft women policy to the government by September 30, 2021, will be of the chairperson of the drafting committee and the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune.
