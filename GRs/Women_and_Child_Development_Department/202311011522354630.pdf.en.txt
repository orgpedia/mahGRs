The Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 In accordance with the decisions taken on 9.12.2013 and the Government Decision and Circular, action should be taken as mentioned below regarding publicity, publicity and public awareness programs.
The Protection of Women from Sexual Harassment at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. Publicity and awareness programs should be implemented in all the districts of the state in accordance with the decisions taken on 9.12.2013 and the rules and government decisions and circulars.
The programme will be conducted by the Women and Child Development Officer of each district in the state with the help of the District Collector, Superintendent of Police, District Legal Services Authority.
