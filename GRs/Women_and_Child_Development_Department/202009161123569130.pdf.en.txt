Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 03.09.2020 has certified that out of the total 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-III and Class-IV in the District Cadre of Women and Child Development Department of Zilla Parishad, not a single post is lying vacant for more than 6 months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, a total of 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-3 and Class-4 of the District Cadre of Women and Child Development Department of the Zilla Parishad are being extended from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. The cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale of these posts have been indicated in the statement.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 31.08.2020 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
