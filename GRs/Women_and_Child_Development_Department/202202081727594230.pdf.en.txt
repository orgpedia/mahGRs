Preamble: The Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Scheme for Welfare of Working Children" for children in need of care and protection was being implemented in various districts of the State. Under the said scheme, the beneficiaries such as child labourers, out of school children, children working on vehicle repair/ garage, children working as domestic help, etc. are brought into the mainstream of the society by imparting non-formal education, business and training. Under this scheme, during the training period, the beneficiaries of the institute are provided with facilities like snacks, medical facilities, entertainment, sports facilities etc. And they're being restored. Under the scheme, the central government and the concerned NGOs share 90:10 and the state government used to recommend the concerned institutions to the central government. Accordingly, the Centre was directly disbursing the grant to the concerned institution in two installments (Rs 4,65,660/- each).
However, as per Reference No. 2 letter, the scheme has been discontinued with effect from 1st April, 2017.
