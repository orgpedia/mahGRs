The Proposal:
Under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS), the salaries of staff in child care institutions and permanent and contractual employees working in the state machinery for the same are also paid. At present, it is an essential service to serve in an organization that cares for and protects children.

However, since no proposal has been received from the Central Government for the year 2022-23, the salaries of these employees have not been paid.
At present, the fund is sanctioned and disbursed by the Central Government every year to pay the salaries of the employees of the Government Children's Home who are receiving salaries under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme.
Considering that the funds will be received by the State after getting approval from the Centre, the Government is considering to disburse the funds to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development for the salaries of the employees of Government and Non-Government Children Homes/ Observation Homes.
