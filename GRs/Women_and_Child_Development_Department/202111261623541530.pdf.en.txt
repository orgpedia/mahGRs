Preamble: Under the Department of Women and Child Development, the allocation made in the budget for the financial year 2021-22 for various development schemes being implemented for the development of women and children was under consideration to be disbursed as per the direction of the Finance Department. Government Decision: In accordance with the subject matter under this office, the Government is approving the distribution and expenditure of the following provisions in the budget for the year 2021-22 for various schemes related to women and child development in the state being implemented by the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune.
