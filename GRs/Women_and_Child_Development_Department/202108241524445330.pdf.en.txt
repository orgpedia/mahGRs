GOVERNMENT DECISION: 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai will be filled up from 01.03.2021. The extension was granted vide Government Decision dated 9.3.2021 in Reference No.4 to continue till 31.08.2021. Accordingly, the proposal for extension of these 43 temporary posts has been submitted by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 06.08.2021 at Reference No.5.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 10.08.2021 in Reference No.6 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, State of Maharashtra, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 06.08.2021 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the aforesaid Government Decision of the Finance Department, 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai are being extended from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
