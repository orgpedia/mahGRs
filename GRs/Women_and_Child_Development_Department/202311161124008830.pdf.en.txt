Government Resolution, Department of Women and Child Development, No. Children's Home-2012/ No. 164/ K-3, dated 04.12.2012.
In order to inculcate the spirit of brotherhood and team spirit among the orphan, destitute and destitute children admitted for rehabilitation in all the children of the State and Government/ NGOs working in the field of child welfare on priority, it has been approved to organize Chacha Nehru Bal Mahotsav in 35 districts and 6 divisional levels in the second week of November every year at a cost of Rs. 2,09,95, 000/-. Accordingly, the Government was considering to distribute funds to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune, for organizing Chacha Nehru Bal Mahotsav for children in the financial year 2023-24.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given to disburse a sum of Rs. 2,09,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Nine Lakhs only) to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune for the financial year 2023-24 in the State.
The expenditure is to be met from the provision approved for the financial year 2023-24 under the head Demand No.X-1, 2235, Social Security and Welfare, 02, Social Welfare, 102, Child Welfare, (05) (04), Organizing Chacha Nehru Bal Mahotsav for Children (2235B325) 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payable).
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune shall disburse the said funds as per the prescribed norms. Also, the utilisation certificate of the fund should be submitted to the government.
This decision was taken by the Finance Ministry vide reference No. 298/ Expenditure-6 dated 3/11/2023 is being issued with the consent received.
