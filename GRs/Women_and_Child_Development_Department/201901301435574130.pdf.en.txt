Introduction: Every administrative department in the Ministry is working for the overall development of the people of the state as per the Government Business Rules. A separate Tribal Development Department has been created as per the government rules for the overall development of tribals. The programme is being implemented by the Women and Child Development Department under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) scheme. Food and Civil Supplies Department is supplying foodgrains to the people of the State and Rural Development Department is providing health related facilities to the people of the State through schemes of Rural Development and Health Department. The Government was considering the formation of a review committee under the chairmanship of Mr. Vivek Pandit, former Member of Legislative Assembly, to review whether the schemes implemented by the Government are providing adequate benefits to the tribals.
Government decision:
All the schemes being implemented in the tribal areas of the state are in the lifestyle of the tribal brothers
To make recommendations to the Government on how the impact has been felt so far, and what measures can be effectively implemented to bring about a drastic change in their lives
The government has decided to set up a committee under the chairmanship of former MP Vivek Pandit. The functions of the Committee shall be as follows: The committee should focus mainly on the tribal areas of the state.
1) To solve the problem of employment of tribals in tribal areas.
2) To study how much salary the tribals need to earn every day, how much salary they are getting now.
3) To study and recommend to the government what can be done to provide employment opportunities to the tribals.
4) To suggest measures to ensure that children in tribal areas do not remain deprived of education and do not drop out of school.
5) To motivate the tribals to come out of the old traditions and adopt a scientific approach and family planning by emphasizing the importance of family planning.
