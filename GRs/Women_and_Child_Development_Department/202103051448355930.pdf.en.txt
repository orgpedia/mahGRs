The proposal-
As per Rule 10 (4) and 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, it is the policy of the Government to review the services of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in the public interest at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, and retire them from Government service prematurely. Accordingly, the General Administration Department vide Reference No. 1 here. The Government Decision dated 10.6.2019 prescribes the procedure and general instructions regarding Service Review. Accordingly, for the review of services of Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers in the Department of Women and Child Development, Reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 13.2.2020, Principal Secretary (M. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the District Collector.
In the case of officers of Group-B cadre of Child Development Project Officer (Rural)/ Superintendent/ Inspector Certified School/ District Women and Child Development Officer and similar posts in the Women and Child Development Department, who have attained the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, it was under the consideration of the Government to take a decision in accordance with the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee regarding their service review.
Government's decision
Child Development Project Officer (Rural)/ Superintendent/ Inspector of Certified Schools/ District Women and Child Development Officer and similar posts in Group-B cadre officers of Women and Child Development Department who have attained the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service in the accompanying form have been qualified in the service review and are being approved to continue their services.
