Introduction: Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai had submitted a proposal for obtaining administrative approval for supply of Energy Dense Nutritious Food (EDNF) to SAM children under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. The State Government has decided to publish e-tender for supply of special Energy Dense Nutritious Food (EDNF) feed prepared scientifically to overcome malnutrition in SAM children under Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) scheme and to prevent malnutrition among children in the State. The approval was granted vide letter No.130/ KA-5 dated 14th July, 2017. Accordingly, the e-tenders have been published by the Commissionerate on 17.07.2017 in online mode as per the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and the prevailing procurement policy of the Government.
As per the procedure of the e-tender, Mahalaxmi Women Industrial and Child Development Multipurpose Institute has been declared as the tender holder L1 for the revenue division of Amravati, Konkan and Pune. Venkateshwara Mahila Industrial Producers Society has been declared L1 bidder for Aurangabad and Nagpur revenue divisions and Maria Mahila Self Help Group has been declared L1 bidder for Nashik revenue division.

The Document Verification Committee report mentions that the financial turnover of the tenderer, Maria Mahila SHG, is eligible for only one revenue division and their rates are also L1 for only one revenue division (Nashik). In the e-tender, it has been proposed that a maximum of 2 revenue department works should be given to a single tenderer and in the interest of the beneficiaries and for the smooth implementation of the scheme, it was proposed to give the work of EDNF food supply to the tenderer, Mahalaxmi Women Industrial and Child Development Multipurpose Institute, for the 3 revenue departments fixed as L1, considering their financial capacity. As on 4/8/2018, 81 bidders were informed to appear before the State Level Administrative Department Procurement Committee for negotiation of rates. Accordingly, the rates were negotiated in the meeting and out of the rates submitted by the three bidders, the minimum rate received was Rs. All the bidders have consented to supply Energy Dense Nutritious Food (EDNF) meal at the rate of Rs. 26.80 (Rupees Twenty Six and Eighty paise inclusive of all taxes). The government was considering handing over the work of supplying Energy Dense Nutritious Food (EDNF) to the concerned institutions in the interest of the beneficiaries and timely supply of food.
Government Decision:- To supply special Energy Dense Nutritious Food (EDNF) feed prepared in a scientific manner to overcome the acute malnutrition of SAM children under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme and to prevent malnutrition among children in the state, the following organizations have been given the responsibility of supplying food to the revenue department for 3 years. The Government approval is being given subject to the conditions that the provisions of the procurement policy in the Government Order dated 1/12/2016 of the Department of Industrial Energy and Labour will be strictly followed.
