The Proposal:
The Supreme Court in Petition No. 116/1998 in the case. The provisions of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 are required to be complied with in accordance with the order dated 7.5.2019 of the Supreme Court. The provisions of Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 need to be strictly implemented. Accordingly, the government was considering issuing instructions at the department and district level.
Government circular:
As per the provisions of Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai and all the offices under them shall appoint Grievance Redressal Officers in the office of the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai and in all the offices under them to receive and maintain records of the complaints of persons with disabilities and to redress such complaints.
In order to comply with Section 23 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the provisions of the above policy in respect of women and children with disabilities must be strictly implemented. Accordingly, all concerned offices should take necessary action to strictly implement the provisions of this policy and take policy decisions where necessary.
All the concerned offices should take action to give due publicity to this circular.
