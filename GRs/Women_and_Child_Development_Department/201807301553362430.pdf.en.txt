Introduction: 50 meritorious girls from the state of Maharashtra will be selected for IIT/ JEE and International Olympiad etc.
Expenditure on providing free training through IITians PACE for the preparation of competitive examinations has been approved vide Government Resolution No.213/ Q-03 dated 09.07.2013. An agreement has been signed between the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and IITians PACE, Andheri, Mumbai for imparting the training.
As per Government Circular, Department of Finance, dated 02.04.2018, funds have been made available for disbursement within the limit of 70% of the budgeted amount for the first nine months of 2018-2019.
