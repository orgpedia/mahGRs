Preamble: The scheme" Purak Poshan Aahar" is implemented under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) through the Department of Women and Child Development to eradicate malnutrition in the State. Under this Centrally Sponsored Scheme, hot fresh food and THR are distributed to beneficiaries in Anganwadis. For the purchase, distribution, quality control and redressal of grievances received under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Purak Poshan Aahar" under Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme, the following activities have been undertaken for rural/ tribal and urban areas: District Supply Committees have been formed as per 1 and 2. Central Government No. As per the guidelines received under Section 4, the matter of reconstitution of district level Nutritional Committee was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the new guidelines received from the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, the following District Level Nutrition Committees for rural and urban areas are being reconstituted.
