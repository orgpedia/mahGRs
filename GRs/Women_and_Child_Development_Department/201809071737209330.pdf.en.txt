Preamble: As per circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, the Budgetary allocation for Compulsory and Programme Expenditure for the financial year 2018-19 covering 9 months upto December 2018 has been approved to be released through Budget Distribution System within the limit of 70% of the total annual allocation. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds admissible under the head of Women and Child Development Department was under the head of 2235 Social Security and Welfare.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2018-19, to meet the expenditure for nine months up to December 2018, vide Demand No. X-1 MAIN ACCOUNT TITLE 2235- SOCIAL SECURITY AND WELFARE, 02 SOCIAL WELFARE, 001 OBJECTION AND ADMINISTRATION (01) (03), the Government is pleased to approve the release of funds to the District Councils under section 183 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishad and Panchayat Samiti Act, 1961, under the head Establishment Grants (Local Area) (Mandatory) (2235-2321), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Munitary), to the extent of 70% of the budgeted funds as mentioned in column No. 5 of the Statement accompanying the said Government Decision.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune will be the Controlling Officer for this fund. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune should strictly follow the checklist items mentioned in the circular dated 2.4.2018 in reference No.1 of the Finance Department and the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing norms, delegation of financial powers etc.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune shall ensure that the funds made available on the Budget Distribution System under the above head for the year 2018-19 are spent and submit the utilization certificate to the Government.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the provisions of Circular No. 1 dated 2.4.2018 in the context of the Department of Finance.
