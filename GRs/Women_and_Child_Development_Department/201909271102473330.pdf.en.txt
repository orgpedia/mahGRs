According to the figure, a total of 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-3 and Class-4 in the district cadre of the Women and Child Development Department of the Zilla Parishad have been removed. The government's decision 5. Continuation was approved from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
2. Under the Government Order dated 11.09.2019 in Reference No.7 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier figure but not included in the figure.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune has certified that out of the total 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-III and Class-IV of the District Cadre of the Women and Child Development Department of the Zilla Parishad, none of the posts is vacant for more than 6 months as per the letter dated 17.9.2019 at Reference No.6. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, a total of 136 temporary posts in the cadre of Class-3 and Class-4 of the District Cadre of Women and Child Development Department of the Zilla Parishad are being extended from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020. The cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale of these posts have been indicated in the statement.
