The Proposal:
With a view to ensure effective management of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 1 here. dated 23.3.2021
Instructions have been given to follow the revised procedure. Accordingly, the Finance Department vide No. 2 here. The circular dated 1.7.2021 provides revised instructions on Public Finance Management System (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, it has been directed to appoint a single nodal agency for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and at least a deputy secretary-level officer as nodal officer.
Accordingly, the government was considering appointing a single nodal officer and a single nodal agency for the women's helpline 181, one-stop centre, women empowerment centre and Sakhi Nivas, which are part of the" Sambal" and" Shakti" applications under the centrally sponsored" Mission Shakti."
