Introduction: Beti Bachao-Beti Padhao is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the Department of Women and Child Development, Government of India, implemented by the Office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai for a period of 3 years from 2014-15 to 2016-17. Thereafter, from the financial year 2017-18, the funds are disbursed by the Central Government to the respective districts through the Collector's Office. However, for the 6 districts of Hingoli, Solapur, Pune, Parbhani, Latur and Nashik, a total of Rs. 32.505 lakh (Rs. 5.4175 lakh) was" revalidated" by the Central Government in the subsequent financial year as the amount remained unspent. This amount was not disbursed to the above districts as it was not available on the Budget Distribution System. The amount will be disbursed to the respective districts.
The Central Government had issued instructions to this effect. Accordingly, the Central Government was requested to" revalidate" the above amount for utilization in the financial year 2019-20.
Accordingly, out of the above 6 districts, the Central Government has released Rs. A total of Rs 5.4175 crore. 27.0875 Lakhs for the financial year 2019-20 and transfer the same to the concerned districts.
However, in the case of Nashik district, the utilization certificate for the year 2018-19 has not been submitted by Nashik district to the Central Government. 5.4175 lakh has not yet been" Revalidated" by the Central Government. Accordingly, the matter of disbursing funds to these 5 districts was under the consideration of the government.

The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai shall immediately make available the funds disbursed to all the concerned districts subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Decision/ Circular of the Finance Department and the Department mentioned above.
In the case of Nashik district, since the utilization certificate for the year 2018-19 has not been submitted to the Central Government by Nashik district, the amount of Rs. 5.4175 lakh has not yet been" Revalidated" by the Central Government. Therefore, after the Nashik district submits the utility certificate of the previous year, the central government will take a decision on" revalidating" the funds of Nashik district. Accordingly, after receiving the order of" Revalidation" of this fund, a decision will be taken regarding the distribution of funds to Nashik district. The matter should be brought to the notice of the concerned Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai and the concerned should be informed immediately to take necessary action regarding the" Revalidation" of the said fund.
This decision was taken by the Planning Department, Informal Reference No. 05/20/1472, dated 7/01/2020 and Finance Department, Informal Reference No. 07/2020/ Expenditure-6, dated 31/01/2020 as agreed.
