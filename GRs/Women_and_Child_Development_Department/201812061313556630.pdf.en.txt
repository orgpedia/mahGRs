The Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 (Maharashtra Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018).
Preamble: The State Child Fund was constituted under the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2000 and the Maharashtra Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2002 and the Board of Management was constituted to give statutory shape to the Fund under the Government Order dated 31st December, 2005 of the Department of Women and Child Development.
However, the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2000 has been repealed and the Juvenile Justice Act, 2015 has been made operative. The Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018 have been enacted to implement this Act. Accordingly, the State Child Fund and Management Board constituted under the Juvenile Justice Act, 2000 has lapsed. Section 105 of the Juvenile Justice Act, 2015 and Rule 85 of the Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice Rules, 2018 provide for creation of a State Child Fund by each State in the name of" State Child Fund" or such other name as the State may deem fit. Accordingly, vide Government Order dated 28.06.2018 of the Department of Women and Child Development, it has been approved to rename the State Child Fund as" Child Justice Fund" constituted under the Juvenile Justice Act, 2015. The matter of determining the criteria regarding the functioning of the Child Justice Fund was under the consideration of the government.
