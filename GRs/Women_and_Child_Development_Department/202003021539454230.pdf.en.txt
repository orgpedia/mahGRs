Ms. Manjusha Molavane, who was working as a Deputy Secretary in the office of Maharashtra State Women's Commission, has been appointed to join her parent office after completing her deputation period. It will be effective from March 2, 2020.
Shri L.R. Gujjar, Joint Secretary, Department of Women and Child Development, Ministry of Women and Child Development, Mumbai is being given additional charge of the post of Deputy Secretary in the office of Maharashtra State Commission for Women, which is going to be vacant due to the retirement of Mrs. Manjusha Molavane.
