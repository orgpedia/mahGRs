The Proposal:
In order to encourage women who are doing excellent work in the field of women and child development, the Gram Panchayats should provide two such women in each Gram Panchayat/ Block Gram Panchayat.
On the occasion of the birth anniversary of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar in the office, the matter of awarding the" Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Mahila Samman Award" on May 31 was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
On the occasion of the birth anniversary of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar, every year on 31st May, two women achievers in the field of women and child development from each Gram Panchayat/ Block Gram Panchayat will be honoured with" Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Mahila Samman Puraskar."
The award is to be given every year on the birth anniversary of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar. The nature of the award, the eligibility criteria, the procedure and the mechanism for selecting the awardee woman will be as follows:
Form of Award: 1. Certificate of Honour 2. Medal of Honour 3. Shawl and Srifal (Coconut) 4. Cash (Rs. 500/- for women)

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MASPU-2023/ PR. No. 142/ WORK-2 2. Eligibility Criteria: 1. Women who are doing excellent/ notable work in the field of Women and Child Development, 2. The woman should be a resident of the same Gram Panchayat/ Block Gram Panchayat, 3.
Women who have worked for at least 3 years in the field of women and child development, 5. Women awardees will be eligible for the award again after seven years, 6. Awareness and sensitivity towards women's issues and concerns, 7. Active participation in social sector, 8. No involvement in women abuse, 9. Promoting proactively in activities like prevention of child marriage, dowry harassment, gender therapy, domestic violence prevention, women empowerment, women self-help groups, health, literacy, initiatives for girls education.
Procedure for selection of awardee woman: Interested women should apply to the concerned Gram Panchayat with their personal details and details of the work done.
Mechanism: (a) At the district level, the District Programme Officer, the District Women and Child Development Officer and the District Coordinating Officer of the Women's Economic Development Corporation should coordinate and ensure that the above activities are carried out in a proper manner in each Gram Panchayat of the district.
(b) The Child Development Project Officer and the Protection Officer (Junior) at the Taluk level should ensure that the above activities are implemented in the appropriate manner in every Gram Panchayat of the Taluk.
(c) A committee of Gram Sevaks, Police Patil, Primary School Headmaster, Anganwadi Sevika and ASHA workers should be constituted under the chairmanship of the Sarpanch of the concerned Gram Panchayat to select two women achievers at the Gram Panchayat level. In Gram Panchayats where the term of the appointed representative of the people has expired/ the Gram Panchayat has been dissolved, a committee should be formed under the chairmanship of the administrator.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune, with the approval of the government, should make the sample of the award letter available to every gram panchayat through the officers at the district level. Further action will be taken at the gram panchayat level.
