Duties and Responsibilities of the District Coordinating Committee:
The duties and responsibilities assigned to the Coordination Committee under the relevant Government Decision include the following duties and responsibilities regarding the rehabilitation of beggars:
1. After getting the information of Bhikshaks (women/ men/ children) in the entire taluka, such Bhikkus are called Ma. Admitted to the nearest almshouse on the orders of the court. Also, avail the benefits of various government schemes.
2. To make available information about the mentally ill, disabled, ill beggars found in the entire taluk.
3. To take necessary measures for rehabilitation of drug addicts and other addicts.
4. Action to be taken for admission of children in children's homes by producing them before the Child Welfare Committee.
The Tahsildar as the Chairman of the Committee shall be responsible for holding the meeting of the Taluk Level Committee twice every month i.e. once in fifteen days and to submit the report of the action taken in this meeting to the Task Force constituted under the Chairmanship of the Collector.
The action taken by the Taluk Level Committee will be reviewed in the meeting of the task force formed under the chairmanship of the Collector.
7.To direct all the hospitals in the taluk level area to provide medical treatment to the beggars who are in need of medical help/ medication in the hospitals immediately.
