The proposal-
The Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Award is being given to social workers and institutions of the state under the Department of Women and Child Development since 1996-97 with a view to recognize and encourage the work of social workers and institutions doing excellent work in the field of women and child development.
The Government had been considering the proposal for selection of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Awardees for the years 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 at State, Divisional and District level.
Government's decision
The names of the awardees for the years 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 at the State, Divisional and District levels are being announced as under: :
