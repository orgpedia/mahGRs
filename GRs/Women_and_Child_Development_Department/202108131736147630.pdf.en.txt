The funds available under the scheme will be utilised for providing medical assistance, entrepreneurial support, skill development and vocational training and other related services and training for major ailments/ surgeries of orphaned, destitute and abandoned children covered under the Juvenile Justice Act, 2015. For this scheme, Rs. A sum of Rs. 2.00 crore has been earmarked for the project. 30% is Rs. Rs. 0.60 crore allocated

Government's decision:
In line with the above background, out of the budgeted funds for State Juvenile Justice Fund, Rs. 60,00000/- (Rupees Sixty Lakh only) lacs is being approved.

Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune will be the Controlling Officer. The Controlling Officer shall submit the utilization certificate to the Government as per the prescribed rules regarding the disbursement of the said funds.
