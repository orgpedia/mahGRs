The Proposal:
During the financial year 2018-19, the budgetary provision available for 31 Subsidiary Grants (non-salaried) under head 2235-2161 is Rs. 1,98,27, 500/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty-Four Lakhs Twenty-Seven Thousand Five Hundred Only) subject to a ceiling of 70% of Rs. 5,66,50, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore Sixty-Six Lakhs Fifty Thousand Only) and 50% of Rs. 3,96,55, 000/- (Rupees Three Crore Sixty-Six Thousand Fifty-Four Thousand Only). It has been circulated to the Maharashtra State Women's Commission vide Government Resolution dated 21st September, 2018. A proposal was under the consideration of the Government to disburse the remaining 50% of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 1,98,27, 500/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty-Four Lakhs Twenty-Seven Thousand Five Hundred Only) to the Maharashtra State Women's Commission within the limit of 70% of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 5,66,50, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore Sixty-Six Lakh Fifty Thousand Only).
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approve the release of the remaining 50% i.e., Rs. 1,98,27, 500/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty-Four Lakh Twenty-Seven Thousand Five Hundred Only) of the budgetary provision of Rs. 5,66,50, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore Sixty-Six Lakh Fifty Thousand Only) to the Maharashtra State Women's Commission out of the 70% limit of Rs. 3,96,55, 000/- (Rupees Three Crore Ninety-Six Lakh Fifty-Five Thousand Only) available for 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Salary) under head 2235-2161 for the financial year 2018-19.
District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for disbursement of this fund to Maharashtra State Women's Commission.
The Drawing and Disbursing Officer shall disburse this fund to the Maharashtra State Women's Commission.
However, without drawing the said funds from the treasury immediately, the District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) shall examine the expenditure/ fund requirement of the Maharashtra State Commission for Women and submit the payment of expenditure to the treasury. It should be ensured that the funds are not left with the Maharashtra State Women's Commission. Also, a certificate of use
