The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai should relieve the transferred officers from their present charge and ask them to join immediately in place of their appointment. The concerned should ensure that their next salary and allowances will be withdrawn on the post they hold. Offices whose heads are transferred should submit a proposal to the government for disciplinary action against those officers who do not relieve the transferred officers within the stipulated time.
4.The concerned officers should report at the place of new appointment and inform the government of the date of taking charge. Disciplinary action should be proposed to the government against the officers who do not join the transferred post within the stipulated time after appointment.
(5) The transfer benefits shall be admissible to the officers who are being posted as above as their posting is being done on administrative grounds.
6. Confidential reports, service books and final salary certificates of the transferred officers along with the posting order as per the above should be sent to the new office immediately.
No leave shall be granted to the officers posted as above without prior approval or any leave applications of the officers posted as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, shall be accepted by the parent office.
8. The office to which the officer is being posted as above shall verify whether or not he was on leave/ absence while being posted to the post to which he is being posted. If the officer is absent on medical grounds, he should be sent before the medical board before being produced.
