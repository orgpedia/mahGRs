Preamble: Under the Centrally Sponsored Swadhar Yojana, a new Swadhar Grih Scheme for destitute, destitute women in the State, who have been rendered homeless due to natural calamities/ domestic violence, has been launched by the Department of Women and Child Development, Government of India, vide Government Resolution dated 23rd March, 2018. Under this scheme, women in distress, women in family conflict and their children are provided food, clothing, shelter and medical assistance, legal counseling and guidance through helpline. As per the guidelines of the new" Swadhar Greh Scheme 2015" of the Central Government, the central share for this scheme has been reduced to 60% and the state share to 40%. Read the revised Swadhar Scheme as per the changed criteria of the Central Government. 1 has been implemented.
2 Central Government Read No. Under the Swadhar Yojana, the funds due for the year 2019-20 have been re-financed in the current financial year. From this fund, the government was considering to disburse the funds due under the Swadhar scheme to 27 institutions for 2019-20.
Government Decision: For the year 2019-20, under the scheme" New Self-Help Home Scheme 2015" for" Support and Rehabilitation of Distressed Women in distress," the Central share is Rs. 1,43,96, 887/-and State share is Rs. 98,78,622/- aggregating to Rs. 2,42,75, 509/- is being sanctioned to Commissioner Women and Child Development, Pune.
These funds are to be spent from the funds budgeted in the financial year 2021-22 under the heads of Social Security and Welfare, 02 Social Welfare, 103, Women Welfare, 02, Assistance to Non-Governmental Organizations (02) (09) Swadhar Yojana (2235C081) (Central share) and (02) (10) Swadhar Yojana (State share) 2235C092 respectively.
