Preamble: A Task Force under the Chairmanship of Shri Deepak Sawant, former Minister (Public Health and Family Welfare) was constituted to review the causes of malnutrition in the tribal areas of the State and to pay special attention to these cases. The matter of appointment/ amendment of members in the said Government Resolution was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Mrs. Sujata Saunik, Additional Chief Secretary (Home) is being appointed as a member of the Task Force constituted under the decision of the Government in the context.
The appointments of Mridula Phadke, Senior Consultant, UNICEF and Dr. Simone Irani, Senior Consultant, Pediatrics, Wadia Children's Hospital, Mumbai as members are being cancelled at the request of this government and instead of them, an expert in health or nutrition is being included as an invited member. The Director, Health Services, Pune and Member Secretary will invite the concerned expert members as per requirement during the meeting.
