Government Decision: Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in accordance with the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 temporary posts that are pending but need to be continued. The extension of the period from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 was granted vide Government Order dated 16.09.2020.
Accordingly, now the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has sanctioned 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 temporary posts which are pending but need to be continued. Proposal for extension of the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 has been submitted vide letter dated 04.03.2021.

Under the Government Resolution dated 15.02.2020 in Reference No.9 of the Department of Finance, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 04.03.2021 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the above decision of the Finance Department, the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai has created 5074 temporary posts in 449 rural and tribal projects in the district cell under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, 221 regular temporary posts in 33 district cells under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the figure fixed by the Central Government, 10 posts related to the Women and Child Development Department for the Zilla Parishad under Palghar district and 167 additional temporary posts which are pending but need to be continued, a total of 5472 temporary posts and a total of 176817 honorary posts of 81903 Anganwadi workers and 81903 Anganwadi helpers and 13011 mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. The extension is being extended from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 15.02.2021 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
