Government order:
Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme, the Anganwadi Centres in the rural projects which are under the purview of the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs), have been integrated into the urban projects and the urban projects have been restructured. The decision was approved by the government. As per Statement 1 of the said decision, 17 urban projects have been completely closed and 17 new projects have been constructed. As per Statement 3, 5 rural projects have been closed and 5 new projects have been created. Therefore, the officers of Group-A and Group-B cadre of completely closed projects need to be adjusted in other projects. Accordingly, options were sought through the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The options received from the officers and the proposal for adjustment accordingly are given in Reference No. 6& 7 has been received from the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The proposal for the post has been received from the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The Civil Services Board headed by the Principal Secretary has been approved in the prescribed manner. The proposal for the postings to be made on the recommendation of the Civil Services Board has been approved by the competent authority. Accordingly, the Group A and Group B cadre officers of the fully closed projects are being posted in the post of the project mentioned in column 3 of the head as follows:
