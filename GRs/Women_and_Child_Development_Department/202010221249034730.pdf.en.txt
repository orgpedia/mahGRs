The Proposal:
To develop and empower all out-of-school beneficiary girls in the age group of 11 to 14 years in all the districts of the State and to provide them the benefit of nutrition, nutritional and health status.
Improving, creating awareness among the beneficiary girls about health, hygiene, nutrition, reproductive and sexual health, family and child care, as well as helping the beneficiary girls to upgrade home skills, life skills and vocational skills, mainstreaming the school-going girls in the age group of 11 to 14 years by providing them formal/ non-formal education, public services as applicable. The Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) for providing information and guidance on Primary Health Centres, Posts, Banks, Police Stations etc. is being implemented for out of school girls in the age group of 11 to 14 years in all the districts of the State.
The scheme is being implemented in the state by the office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. Under this scheme, the ratio of central and state share of expenditure on nutrition is 50: 50. The scheme will be implemented from 2020-21.
The government was considering to disburse funds in accordance with the central assistance received from the central government for the year.
