Central funds are available. The state government was considering to build toilets and provide drinking water facilities in its own Anganwadis.
Government's decision:
The government is giving permission to build toilets and provide drinking water facilities in the Anganwadis owned by the state as per the norms set by the central government.
The conditions/ conditions regarding construction of toilets and provision of drinking water facilities to Anganwadis will be as follows.
1) Construction of a toilet in the existing Anganwadi Centre building owned by the self. (Rs. 12,000/- per Anganwadi Centre).
2) Provide drinking water. (Rs.10,000/- per Anganwadi Centre).
3) After the construction of toilets/ providing drinking water facilities, their photos should be sent to the central government.
The cost will be shared between the Centre and the states in the ratio of 60:40.
5) While constructing toilets in anganwadis, care should be taken to ensure that children can use the toilets easily. In this regard, toilets should be constructed in anganwadis taking into account the guidelines of the sanitation action plan of the sanitation department.
When building a toilet, care should be taken to ensure that children can easily use the toilet.
6) If the construction cost is more than the amount sanctioned under this government decision, funds should be made available from the Gram Panchayat or MNREGA scheme.
During 2018-19, work is to be taken up for providing drinking water facility in 2969 Anganwadi Centres and construction of toilets in 7365 Anganwadi Centre buildings.
8) One-time fund will be provided by the Central Government for the above work.
