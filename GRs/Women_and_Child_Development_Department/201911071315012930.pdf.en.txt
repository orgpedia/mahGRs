Preamble: Under the General Administration Department No. 5 here. Guidelines to be considered while assigning additional charge have been issued vide Government Circular dated 05.09.2018.
Most of the posts are vacant in Group-A cadre under the Commissioner of Women and Child Development Department, Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissionerate of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The process of filling up these posts through direct recruitment/ promotion/ deputation is in progress.
However, in the overall Women and Child Development Department, majority of the posts in Group-A cadre will remain vacant for a longer period. Now, considering the increased number of vacancies of Group-A cadre officers in the department and the time taken by the department to fill up the vacancies, it has been decided to give additional charge of Group-A cadre vacancies. 6 Guidelines have been issued here by circular.
However, in spite of the provision that the additional charge in the form of temporary arrangement in the post vacated on account of transfer, retirement, suspension etc. for a maximum period of 15 days may be given to the concerned Commissioner from his level on case to case basis and the additional charge given under temporary arrangement will automatically lapse after a period of 15 days, the proposals for regular assignment of additional charge after a period of 15 days are not being submitted to the Government within the stipulated time. It is the responsibility of all the offices under the concerned administrative department to strictly implement all the relevant government decisions/ circulars of the General Administration Department. Accordingly, the General Administration Department. In order to implement the guidelines of the Government Circular dated 05.09.2018, vide Circular No. 6 is here. The matter of issuing revised orders by superseding the circular dated June 1, 2019 was under consideration of the government.

Government Circular No: MBAA 2018/ PR No.166/ Function-7
Government Circulars: All the relevant government decisions/ circulars of the General Administration Department are strictly implemented by the respective administrative departments as well as the offices under the administrative department. Therefore, all the government decisions, orders, circulars issued earlier regarding the assignment of additional charge of the vacant posts of Group-A cadre officers under the Women and Child Development Department are being superseded and the procedure is being laid down as follows:
(1) The Department of Women and Child Development, Commissionerate, Pune and Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Commissionerate, Navi Mumbai, under the General Administration Department, have issued the following orders: In view of the guidelines given by the Government Circular dated September 5, 2018, the details regarding the additional charge of the vacant post after the vacancy in the Group-A cadre under them should be submitted to the Government immediately with all the necessary information.
(2) If, at the time of submission of such particulars, the officer on whom the departmental inquiry is being conducted is likely to influence his own departmental inquiry or obstruct the departmental inquiry, such officer shall not be proposed for additional charge.
Further, the orders regarding the grant of additional charge will be issued from the level of Administrative Department as per the guidelines of the Government Circular dated September 5, 2018 of the General Administration Department.
After considering the information submitted as above, the process of giving additional charge of the vacant post of Group-A cadre will be done at the level of the administrative department as per the guidelines of the Government Circular dated September 5, 2018 of the General Administration Department. Thereafter, it should be ensured that no orders for giving additional charge are issued from the Commissionerate level without the approval of the Administrative Department and the provisions of the Government Circular dated 5th September, 2018 of the General Administration Department are not violated. Considering the urgency/ difficulty of administrative work and the vacancies, it will be mandatory for both the Commissionerates to follow up with the Government regarding the matter of giving additional charge.
