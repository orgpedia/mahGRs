The Proposal:
Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) programme of the Department of Women and Child Development, hot fresh food and THR are distributed to beneficiaries in anganwadis under the centrally sponsored scheme of" Purak Poshan Aahar." Under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) scheme, the central share of 50: 50 was to be shared between the Centre and the state in accordance with the central assistance received from the Centre for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Purak Poshan Aahar."
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In pursuance of the Central assistance received from the Government of India for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of" Purak Poshan Aahar" under the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Programme under the Department of Women and Child Development, the Government hereby approves the release and expenditure of funds to the tune of Rs. 127.82 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Seven Crores Eighty Two Lakh only) as per the Account Head/ Objectives Racket No.5 in Racket No.2 of the Statement of Profit for Central and State Governments.
