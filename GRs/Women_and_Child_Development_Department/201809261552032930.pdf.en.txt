GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the figures fixed for the urban projects under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, a total of 32134 temporary/ honorary posts in 104 urban projects in the regular pay scale of 1070 working temporary posts and 15532 Anganwadi workers and 15532 Anganwadi helpers in 15532 Anganwadis were extended from 01/03/2018 to 30/09/2018 as per the Government decision dated 31.3.2018.
2. Under the Government Order dated 7.9.2018 in Reference No.8 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
3. Vacancies in regular pay scale posts and honorary posts working under Civil Projects in the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme are not vacant for more than six months.The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 07.09.2018 has certified the same.
