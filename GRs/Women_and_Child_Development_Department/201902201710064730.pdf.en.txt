Read: 1. The High Court of Bombay at Bombay, vide Petition No. 1763/2018, District Inspection and Maintenance Organization, Pune v. State of Maharashtra 22.6.2018 Raji Directions.
The Department of Women and Child Development is implementing the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. Under the Act, voluntary organisations willing to provide residential and other ancillary services to" children in conflict with law" and" children in need of care and protection" are given government approval to run children's homes/ observation homes. Accordingly, the District Inspection and Maintenance Organization, Pune, an NGO, was granted permission to run Observation Home/ Children Home for Boys at Shivajinagar, Pune and Observation Home/ Children Home for Girls at Nana Peth, Pune. However, as per the resolution passed in the meeting of the members of the concerned NGO, the NGO has filed a writ petition No. 1 to order the government to close down the observation home/ children's home in Shivajinagar, Pune and Nana Peth, Pune and relocate the children admitted in the institution under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015.
1763/2018 was filed. The petition has been disposed of vide order dated 22.6.2018. Accordingly, the matter of de-recognition of the observation home/ children's home of the NGO District Inspection and Maintenance Association, Pune, was under the consideration of the government as the children/ girls admitted in the institution were admitted in other institutions.
Government's decision:
The recognition granted under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 to three observation homes/ children homes, two in Shivajinagar, Pune and one in Nana Peth, Pune, run by the District Inspection and Maintenance Association, Pune, an NGO, is being cancelled under this Government decision.
