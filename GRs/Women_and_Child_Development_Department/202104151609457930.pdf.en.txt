The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retired Additional Chief Secretary, Government of India to make recommendations for revision of pension/ family pension of State Government and other eligible pensioners/ family pensioners based on the decisions of the Government in respect of pension/ family pension of Central Government employees based on the recommendations of the 7th Central Pay Commission. The Government has decided to accept the recommendations of the Committee with certain modifications. The government has taken 2 decisions. As per the decision of the Government, in the Report of the State Pay Revision Committee, 2017, in Volume-1, regarding the recommendations regarding pension and collateral benefits, para. 6.10 All benefits to pensioners/ family pensioners. It will be effective from 1.1.2016. They should be given benefits in the shortest possible time.
The State Government has accepted the recommendation that all the reforms mentioned in the Seventh Pay Commission should be applicable to the State Government Family Pensioners also with appropriate modifications. Accordingly, the matter of implementing pension/ family pension as per the Seventh Pay Commission for retired employees of Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board and retired employees of the scheme was under consideration of the Government.
The Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board (MSSWB) has decided to grant pension/ family pension to retired employees of the Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board (MSSWB) and retired employees of the scheme working under the Women and Child Development Department.
Regarding fixation of pay and payment of arrears in the revised pay structure, Reference No. As per the instructions in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 notified under Section 1 and Reference No. Action should be taken according to the government decisions from 2 to 4.
