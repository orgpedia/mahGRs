The Proposal:
As per the order of Reference-3 of the Department of Finance, the Administrative Department has been authorized to disburse funds on Budget Distribution System (BDS) to meet the expenditure for nine months upto December, 2018 within the limit of 70% of the total annual provision budgeted under the head Demand No.X-1 Main Accounts in the Department of Women and Child Development for the financial year 2018-19.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In the above background, the schemes in Pillar-I of Annexure-A attached herewith for meeting the expenditure for the month of December, 2018 for the year 2018-19
The funds are being given by the government to meet the expenses for nine months with a limit of 70%.
In this regard, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune, shall strictly follow the checklist items mentioned in the order dated April 2, 2018 in reference No. 3 of the Finance Department, as well as the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and the Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing criteria, the delegation of financial powers.
In this regard, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune should take care of spending the funds made available on the budget distribution system under the above heading for the year 2018-19 at the end of December 2018 and submit the utility certificate to the government.
This Government Resolution is being issued in pursuance of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department vide circular dated 02.04.2018 in the context of Finance Department.
