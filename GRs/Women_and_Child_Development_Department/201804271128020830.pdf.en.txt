Rajiv Gandhi Scheme for Empowerment of Adolescent Girls (SABLA) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the Department of Women and Child Development, Government of India, implemented by the Office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The scheme is being implemented on a 50:50 sharing pattern between the Centre and the state.
In the Budget Estimates 2018-19, the Central Share of Rs.50 (TOP 2236 1802) has been allocated for meeting the dietary expenditure under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rajiv Gandhi Scheme for Empowerment of Adolescent Girls (SABLA). 5033.54 lakh and State share of 50% (Head of Accounts 22361811) Rs. 5033.54 lakh.
The Finance Department has approved the release and utilization of 70% of the budgetary allocation to meet the expenditure up to December, 2018. Accordingly, Rajiv Gandhi Scheme for Empowerment of Adolescent Girls (SABLA) has been allocated in the Budget Estimates 2018-19 with a Central Share of Rs. 5033.54 Lakhs to 70% of Rs. 3523.478 Lakhs and corresponding State share of Rs. 5033.54 Lakhs to 70% of Rs. 3523.478 Lakhs out of which Rs. 7046.956 lakh was under the consideration of the Government.
