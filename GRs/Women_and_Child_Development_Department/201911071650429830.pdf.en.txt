Introduction: The Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India has launched the' One Stop Centre" scheme to provide immediate assistance to women in distress. Accordingly, one" One Stop Centre" has been started in every district of the state. The One Stop Centre provides medical facilities, police help desk, counselling centre and legal aid to victims of domestic violence, sexual abuse, human trafficking, acid attack and other forms of abuse. For the successful implementation of the" One Stop Centre" scheme, the Central Government has prepared guidelines in December 2017, according to which the work of the centers in the state is going on.
The Central Government's guidelines on the One Stop Centre Scheme provide for the appointment of an implementing agency for the day-to-day operations of the One Stop Centre and staff on a remuneration basis for the day-to-day operations. However, regarding issue of detailed guidelines for functioning of One Stop Centres, Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune vide reference No. A proposal has been submitted to the government. Accordingly, the matter of issuing detailed guidelines for the Management Committee/ Task Force constituted under the Chairmanship of the Collector in connection with the appointment of Implementing Agency (IA) staff for the effective implementation of the One Stop Centre Scheme was under the consideration of the Government.
In line with the guidelines of the Central Government regarding the One Stop Centre Scheme, the following guidelines are being issued regarding the appointment of implementing agencies for the day-to-day operations of One Stop Centres and staff on honorarium basis to run the day-to-day operations:

Guidelines for Implementation of One Stop Centre Scheme 1. ANNEXURE VII of the Government of India guidelines dated December 2017 provides detailed provisions regarding the posts, educational qualifications and experience of the employees to be appointed for the scheme. Accordingly, the Management Committee/ Task Force shall ensure that the educational qualifications and experience of the personnel to be appointed by the Implementing Agency (IA) are duly verified.
The Management Committee shall agree to appoint staff to the said Implementing Agency only after the Management Committee is satisfied that the educational qualifications and experience of the staff appointed by the Implementing Agency (IA) are as per the guidelines.
The list of proposed employees to be appointed by the Implementing Agency (IA) shall be submitted to the committee constituted under the chairmanship of the Collector. This should include verifying the employee's educational qualifications, experience, ensuring there is no conflict of interest with the board of directors of the implementing agency, no criminal charges, etc. Thereafter, the agreement/ contract between the implementing agency and the proposed employee should be examined.
4. The District Women and Child Development Officer shall ensure that the agreement/ contract between the implementing agency and the employees is signed after obtaining the approval of the Management Committee.
The District Women and Child Development Officer shall ensure the payment of honorarium, management of TDS and other taxes every month as per the guidelines of the Central Government and the post-wise rates prescribed by the State Government and the same shall be communicated to the Management Committee from time to time.
The functioning of the implementing agency should be reviewed every three months by the Collector to ensure that the functioning of the agency is satisfactory. Also, the activities/ procedures of the agency should be reviewed every month by the District Women and Child Development Officer and action should be taken to pay the honorarium after receiving the consent of the Collector after submitting a proposal in the prescribed format.
If the women coming to the One Stop Centre have any cash, valuables and jewellery, then the responsibility of providing lockers for such women will be of the concerned agency. All such items should be recorded by the order of the District Women and Child Development Officer and a receipt should be given to the concerned.
If an employee working under this scheme is to be removed from the post for any reason, the concerned implementing agency will have to take prior permission from the management committee appointed under the chairmanship of the Collector.
The monthly remuneration paid to the employees appointed under the One Stop Centre scheme by the implementing agency will be required to be paid to the bank account of the concerned employee every month at the rate fixed by the government. It will be necessary to ensure from time to time that the management committee appointed under the chairmanship of the Collector receives the said honorarium to the concerned employee.

The District Women and Child Development Officer will be responsible for the district level control of this scheme.
11.A District Women and Child Development Officer shall be responsible for ensuring that all One Stop Centres are functional as per the guidelines of the Central Government from time to time. Accordingly, the management committee formed under the chairmanship of the Collector should ensure that the institution implementing the scheme is financially sound.
13.The management committee constituted under the chairmanship of the Collector should control the payment of service charges, TDS, etc. from the implementing agency. Also, no agency should be appointed for a period of more than three years at a time.
14.The draft of the contract to be entered into between the implementing agency and the employee should be prepared by the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and should be made available to all the District Collectors and the Chairperson, Management Committee.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune shall review the implementation of the scheme twice a year.
16. Accreditation of the institution failing to comply with the guidelines of the Central Government and the above instructions shall be cancelled in accordance with the prescribed procedure.
