Preamble: The Government was considering to disburse funds under the headings 2235-3041 (36-Support Grant Salary) and (31-Support Grant Salary) for the purpose of payment of salary dues and non-salary dues of the officers/ employees of The Children Aid Society, Mumbai and Maharashtra State Parivaksha and Maintenance Association, Pune.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: To pay the regular salary of the officers/ employees working at the establishment of The Children's Aid Society, Mumbai for the period from October, 2022 to January, 2023 and the arrears of Dearness Allowance at the rate of 3% for the period from July, 2021 to February, 2022 and at the rate of 4% for the period from July, 2022 to December, 2022. 407.47/- (Rupees Four Crores Seventy Four Lakhs Eighty Nine Thousand only) is being approved under this Government Resolution to be disbursed and spent as per the approval of the Finance Department:
