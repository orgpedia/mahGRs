The Maharashtra Civil Services (Service Excellence) Rules, 1982 and the General Administration Department.
As per the Government decision dated 21.10.2011, it is necessary to publish the Service Excellence List every year with 1st January as the base date.
MBAA-2021/ P.S. No.47/ Q.7, dated. As on 21.6.2021, the final seniority list dated 1.1.2021 has been issued in the cadre of Class Group-A under the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune handles the administrative and organisational/ service matters in the case of officers/ staff working under the office of the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The respective ranks of the officers of these two Commissionerates are being published in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 and the final seniority list in respect of the said officers as per the Government Decision/ Government Circular/ Government Notification issued in this regard.
All the concerned officers shall examine the Service List and submit their detailed report in writing to the Government within three weeks along with appropriate documents, if there are any objections/ errors or corrections in this Service List. In this way, appropriate decisions will be taken after considering the representations received by the government in order of merit and the service list will be published in the final form.
