Introduction: The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented since 1975-76. Through this scheme, various services are provided to improve the nutritional and health status of children. The Central Government vide order dated 8/12/2017 has decided to share the Central and State share in respect of wages under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in the ratio of 25:75. Earlier, the ratio between the Centre and the states was 60:40.
The Central Government has paid 25 per cent of the salary as above to the posts of Child Development Project Officer, District Programme Officer, Statistical Assistant, Headmistress/ Supervisor, etc. under this scheme. Remaining cadre posts are to be paid 100% from the State share. The matter was considered in the Cabinet meeting held on 5th June, 2018. To that Ma. The Cabinet has approved it. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government of India to approve a new head of account/ target for payment of salaries to the officers/ employees working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the changed ratio.
