Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India Letter dated 17th May, 2022
Preamble: Under the Centrally Sponsored Swadhar Yojana, a new Swadhar Grih Scheme is being implemented for destitute destitute women in the State who have been rendered homeless due to natural calamities/ domestic violence. Ujjwala scheme is being implemented by the central government to prevent illegal trade. Read about these two centrally sponsored schemes. The process of disbursement of funds under the scheme has been initiated through the Single Nodal Agency (SNA).
2. Central Government Read No. Under the Swadhar and Ujjwala Yojana, a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) account has been opened in HDFC Bank for the distribution of funds for the centrally sponsored Swadhar and Ujjwala schemes through the Department of Women and Child Development.
Government Decision: The Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 9,37,289/- (Rupees Nine Lakhs Thirty Seven Thousand Two Hundred and Ninety Nine only) and Central share of the Consolidated Fund of the State for Centrally Sponsored Ujjwala Yojana is Rs. 90,816/- (Rupees Ninety Thousand Eight Hundred and Sixteen only). The funds will be transferred to the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) account.
