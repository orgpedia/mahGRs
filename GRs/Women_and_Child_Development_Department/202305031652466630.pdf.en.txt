Preamble: Guidelines issued by the Central Government under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme" Mission Shakti" vide Government Circular dated 14th July, 2022. 03.01.2023 have been implemented in the State from the financial year 2022-23. The state government has decided to launch a toll-free helpline" 181 Mahila Helpline" to provide immediate information and assistance to women in distress. Issued on 12.01.2023. Under the" Mission Shakti" scheme of the Central Government, 100% funding will be provided by the Central Government for the component scheme" 181 Women Helpline." The government was considering opening a new account head for this fund in the budget.
Government's decision:
The following approval is being given to open a new head of account for budgeting funds for the component scheme" 181 Women Helpline" to ensure that the distressed women of the state get immediate necessary information and assistance.
