6) Government Circular Finance Department, No.Finance-2019/ No. 92/ Finance-3, D. As of January 22, 2020:
The Finance Department vide Circular No.4 dated 8.7.2019 has accorded approval to disburse the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programme expenditure to meet the expenditure for nine months upto the end of December in the month of 2019-20 within the limit of 60% of the total annual allocation. Thereafter, vide Circular No.6 of the Department of Finance dated 22.1.2020, funds have been allowed to be disbursed within the limit of 80% of the budgetary allocation for essential items. Accordingly, subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the said circular of the Finance Department and taking into consideration the guidelines issued by the Finance Department and the prevailing norms, the matter of disbursement of funds admissible for essential items under the head of accounts head 2235-Social Security and Welfare under the Department of Women and Child Development was under consideration.
Government's decision:
Demand No.X-1 for the financial year 2019-20 for meeting the expenditure on essential items under the head Social Security and Welfare, as per the provisions of the circular dated 22.1.2020 of the Finance Department. The Government is approving the disbursement of funds through the Budget Distribution System as mentioned in Section 6.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune will be the Controlling Officer for this fund. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune should strictly follow the checklist items mentioned in the circular under the Finance Department, the guidelines of the Central Government as well as the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and the Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing norms, the delegation of financial powers.
