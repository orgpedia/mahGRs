The proposal: A" Consultative Committee" has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Minister, Women and Child Development with 14 Government/ Non-Government, Special Invited Members having expertise in various fields for in-depth study and implementation of issues, policies, laws, welfare initiatives and ongoing schemes for the development of weaker sections of women and children. The government is considering appointing three more members to the committee.
Government Supplementary: Ma. The Mentoring Committee will consider and recommend the following issues: a) To study the schemes of the Central Government and the State Government regarding the issues of development of women and children, policies, laws, welfare activities of the weaker sections of the society through the Department of Women and Child Development, to suggest necessary changes in them and to recommend measures for effective implementation of these schemes.
(b) The Committee being a Consultative Committee shall not be entitled to any remuneration and shall be limited to making recommendations.
(b) The Board of Directors shall meet once in three months.
