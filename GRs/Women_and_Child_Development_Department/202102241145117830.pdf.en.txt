November 25, 2019 Read the Preamble. The training policy of the state has been decided under Section 1. As per the decision, the training institutes will be registered under the Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950 and the Institute Registration Act, 1860. Read as follows. As per the decision of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Governance Training, Pune has been granted in-principle approval as an autonomous institute.
Read No. The Constitution and Rules of the Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Administration Training, Pune have been approved by the Government under the Government Letter No. 4. Similarly, Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Administration Training, Pune has been registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 vide Registration Certificate No. 1501/2019, Pune, Dy. 4th October, 2019 and Mumbai Public Trust Act 1950 Registration Certificate No.F.53564, Pune, Dist. It was released on November 13, 2019. Considering this, a proposal was under consideration of the government to make the Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Administration Training, Pune, autonomous to increase its efficiency.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of this Government Decision, it is decided to recognize Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Administration Training, Pune as an autonomous Institute under the Department of Women and Child Development.
The objectives and objectives of the Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Governance Training will be as follows: 1) Types of Training- Technical training and in-service training will be conducted by preparing an annual plan of Group-A, Group-B, Group-C and Group-D of the Department of Women and Child Development.
As per the State Training Policy 2011, administrative training will be conducted for the officers and staff of Group-A, Group-B, Group-C and Group-D cadres in the Department of Women and Child Development.
3) Foundation Training- The Foundation Training Institute will conduct classes 1 to 4 in the department. Training will also be imparted in revision training, post-promotion training, post-transfer training, face recognition training of new subjects and so on.

4) The Deputy Commissioner (Training) with the approval of the Member Secretary shall have the power to refer to the training conducted by other training institutions to the officers and staff of the department.
5) This training institute will establish a research and documentation center related to the work of the department. The Centre will evaluate the schemes and laws under the Department of Women and Child Development. Based on the data collected, the study will evaluate and recommend the target policies to the government.
Remote Training System Anganwadi workers, helpers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme of the Department of Women and Child Development, as there is a large number of staff under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme, they will be trained through video conferencing.
7) After evaluating the schemes of various departments being implemented by the Government of Maharashtra, the necessary measures will be suggested to the government. This will include various schemes of the state and central government.
8) In addition, all the goals/ activities/ objectives set out in the constitution and regulations approved by the Charity Commissioner will be implemented.
Training of officers and staff of other departments in the government service:- In the day-to-day administration of the Department of Women and Child Development, mainly the departments of Police, Education, Judiciary, Health, Skill Development, etc. are constantly involved.
10) The elected representatives of the people will be trained on the laws and schemes related to women.
Training will be provided to all stakeholders under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme.
Training to NGOs:- Various NGOs are working through the Department of Women and Child Development in the state. Purpose-oriented training will be provided to the office bearers and workers of this organization to enhance the understanding between the Women and Child Development Department and the NGOs

Powers of the Executive Board: (1) The Executive Board shall have the power to approve the annual budget.
(2) The Executive Board shall review every three months whether the Annual Training Calendar is operating effectively or not.
The Executive Board shall have the power to approve the conduct of various research projects and sponsored programmes of other departments in the Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Governance Training.
(4) The Executive Board shall have the power to approve all manpower required for the implementation of the training of the Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Governance Training.
(5) The Executive Board shall have the power to appoint employees on regular basis/ on contract basis/ externally, to fix their remuneration and allowances, etc.
(6) The Executive Board shall have the power to determine the limits of financial sanction as well as administrative sanction.
(7) The Executive Board shall have the power to train or take over the training of the department by entering into a common agreement with other training institutions for taking or giving training.
8) The Executive Board shall have the final authority to take over all other facilities required for residential, non-residential training.

Other Programmes: Skill Development Training/ Vocational Training will be provided to children in the age group of 16-21 years in the institution of Department of Women and Child Development.
2) Pre-marital counseling training will be provided to adolescent girls in the projects under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. Training programmes will be held at the district level.
(3) To prepare and train the adolescents in all the projects under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) for imparting gender equality training to adolescents.
4) Training will be provided to the Headmistress/ Anganwadi worker/ Anganwadi helpers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. The girls will also be given life skills training.
5) Gender equality training will be provided to officers in various departments working mainly for women in the government. Also, the officials who prepare and plan the annual budget will be trained on gender budgeting.
6) Gender equality training will be provided to the officers and employees of the General Cell working in various departments of the government.
Representatives of NGOs working under the State Women's Commission and Maharashtra State Commission for Protection of Child Rights will be trained in the laws and schemes of the Women and Child Development Department.
8) Women's Counselling, Child Counselling Diploma/ Certificate course will be prepared in association with Savitribai Phule Pune University, Bharati University, SNDT University Yashwantrao Chavan Open University, Nashik.
c) CSR of various private companies. Technical training will be provided as required.
The newly elected women office bearers of Maharashtra will be trained in the schemes and laws of the Women and Child Development Department.
11) Training will be conducted for women belonging to self-help groups under the Women's Economic Development Corporation.
The Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Governance Training will be able to undertake training programmes, research programmes, evaluation work of other government departments and private institutions. The cost of the training will be borne by the institute.
Similarly, till the institution becomes self-sufficient, the maintenance of the building, construction/ extension of the building, development and empowerment and other expenses will be borne by the Government. Ex-gratia of Rs.1,235, Social Security, 02-Social Welfare, 109 Pre-Vocational Training, (00) (01) will be provided to Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Governance Training, Pune under the head Extension and Development and Empowerment (Programme) (2235 I 732). Also, 50% of the amount in the development fund account will be used for daily transactions, 25% for fixed deposits and 25% for other welfare funds. The Board shall have the power to make such changes as it deems necessary.
