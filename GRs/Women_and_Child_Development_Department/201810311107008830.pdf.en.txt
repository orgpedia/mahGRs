Preamble: 1. Department of Finance. As per Circular dated 02.04.2018, 70% of the budgeted amount for the current financial year 2018-19 is available for expenditure.
As per the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015, the government grants permission to NGOs to run children's homes to provide residential and other essential services for the purpose of rehabilitation and resettlement of children in need of care and protection. In the financial year 2018-19, the Government was considering to disburse funds to the NGOs running institutions for children in need of care and protection under the heads of Accounts (2235 3041), 31, Auxiliary Grants (Non-Salaried), and- Non-Institutional Services for Destitute Children (2235 1147), 31, Auxiliary Grants in the proportion of 70% of the budgeted funds and Auxiliary Grants (2235 3041), 36.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Now, in pursuance of this decision, the Government hereby approve the disbursement of funds as under:
