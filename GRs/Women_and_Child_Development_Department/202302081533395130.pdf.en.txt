Preamble The Residential Institutions of The Children's Aid Society, Mumbai at Mankhurd, including the Mentally Retarded Children's Home, New Children's Home, Additional Children's Home, Primary and Secondary School, Bal Kalyan Nagar and Government Children's Rehabilitation Centre are functioning under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 and need to be reformed. The Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai has prepared 16 works for the construction of internal and external walls, painting, repair of doors and windows, installation of new sheets on the barracks, repair of toilets, renovation of ladders, construction of basketball grounds, construction of playgrounds, installation of nets and grills at required places, repair of roads and other ancillary works. 3 The above letters have been submitted for administrative approval. Finance Department No. 1 As per the powers conferred on the administrative departments under the above Government decision, the matter of administrative approval of the budgets of these 16 works was under consideration.
Government Decision: Construction of Mandabuddhi Children's Home, New Children's Home, Additional Children's Home, Primary and Secondary School, Child Welfare City and Government Children's Rehabilitation Centre in the premises of these residential institutions as per the State List for 2022-23
