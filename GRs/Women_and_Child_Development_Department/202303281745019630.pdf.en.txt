Government's decision:
A total of 1590 posts including 1542 on pay and 48 on honorarium in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development and a total of 2306 temporary posts including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms were extended from 1/09/2022 to 28/2/2023 as per the Government decision dated 20.09.2022.
(ii) Under the Government Order dated 08.02.2023 in Reference No.5 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and also for the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls.
The letter dated 230.2.2023 in Reference No.4 certifies that 1590 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune and 716 posts filled by external agencies have not been vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, in accordance with the provisions of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, a total of 1590 posts, including 1542 on pay and 48 honorary posts, in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development Department and a total of 2306 temporary posts, including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms, are being extended from 1.03.2023 to 1.03.2023. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
