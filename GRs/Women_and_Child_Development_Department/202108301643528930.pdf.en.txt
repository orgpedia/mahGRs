Preamble: With a view to ensure effective management of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has issued a notification vide Reference No. 1 here. It is directed to adopt the revised procedures under the memorandum dated 23.3.2021. Accordingly, Department No. 2 here. The circular dated 1.7.2021 has provided revised instructions on Public Finance Management System (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, it is necessary to appoint a single nodal agency for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and open a separate account in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least a Deputy Secretary level officer as the nodal officer for coordination of these centrally sponsored schemes.
Accordingly, the matter of appointing a nodal officer and a single nodal agency for the centrally sponsored schemes of the Women and Child Development Department was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In the context of Finance Department No. 2 here. Funding of Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Department of Women and Child Development as per instructions issued vide Circular No. 1.7.2021
