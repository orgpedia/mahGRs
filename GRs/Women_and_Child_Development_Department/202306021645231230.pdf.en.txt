Government's decision:
The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. Demand No.X-1 for the Financial Year 2023-24 Head of Accounts Head 2236-Rakana No.-2 of the following statement for the month of April and May, 2023 for the salary of officers/ employees under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme under Poshan Aahar
Under this Government decision, a total amount of Rs. 13460.80 lakhs (Rupees One Hundred Thirty Four Crores Sixty Lakh Eighty Thousand only) is being approved to be disbursed and spent as per Rakaana No.5 for the head/ target.
