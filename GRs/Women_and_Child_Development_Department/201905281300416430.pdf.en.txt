Read:- (1) The Government Resolution Cumannck Petition 2019/ Q. No.5/ Q-5, dated 03.04.2019.
Preamble: The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai vide order dated 30.3.2019 has issued an order to the Maharashtra State Cooperative Consumers Federation Limited for supply of raw foodgrains and groceries to be supplied to the beneficiaries under the Integrated Child Development Scheme. Mumbai has been given. However, due to lack of clear instructions regarding the level from which the payment of raw grains and groceries supplied by Maharashtra State Cooperative Consumers' Federation, Marriott, Mumbai should be made, the payment of supplies could not be made. Therefore, the matter of issuing a new order for payment of these dues was under the consideration of the government. The government is taking the following steps in this regard:
Government decision: In order to implement the Supreme Court's order, the Maharashtra State Cooperative Consumer Federation Limited, Mumbai has been appointed as the temporary Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, to provide kits of raw food grains and groceries to the beneficiaries under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme.
Under this scheme, the Government is approving the payment of raw grains and groceries supplied to the beneficiaries by the Maharashtra State Cooperative Consumers' Federation, Mumbai through the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai in the following manner.
Rural and tribal areas
(a) The payments should be submitted at the project level after the federation supplies raw grains and groceries at the Anganwadi level.

(b) The payments are to be verified by the Project Officer and submitted to the Deputy Chief Executive Officer (CEO) by making a pass-for-payment.
(c) The Deputy Chief Executive Officer (CEO) shall examine and sign the said payments and submit the same to the Commissionerate.
Approach to urban areas
The Child Development Project Officer should check the payment and submit the pass for payment at the Commissionerate level.
The above dues of Maharashtra State Cooperative Consumers Federation Limited, Mumbai should be paid by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai.
