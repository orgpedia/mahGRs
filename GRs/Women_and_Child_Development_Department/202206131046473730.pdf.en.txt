The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for this fund. The funds are being given to them. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai shall strictly follow the checklist items mentioned in the circular under the Finance Department, the guidelines of the Central Government as well as the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and the Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing norms, the delegation of financial powers.
