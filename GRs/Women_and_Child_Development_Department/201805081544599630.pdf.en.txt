The Maharashtra State Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2018 have been notified vide Notification dated 13.3.2018. The rule provides for a period of one year from the date of commencement of the rule for the certificate of registration of institutions registered under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2000/ 2006. In view of this provision, all Government and Non-Governmental Organizations under the provisions of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 are requested to develop online facility for applying for re-registration within the expiry of the prescribed time limit for children homes. 2 letters are provided.
According to the Women and Child Development Department, The online application process has been started by all the working and interested NGOs till 20.5.2018.
In line with the online process of registration certificate, it is now clarified that in case of difficulties arising due to some unavoidable reasons while applying online from the interested non-government/ non-governmental organizations, the concerned organizations will be able to submit the application in the offline mode in the prescribed format along with the required documents. However, the offline application should be submitted to the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune, by the District Women and Child Development Officer of the concerned district after ensuring that all the necessary aspects are fulfilled and certified accordingly.
