The Proposal:
Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The Central Government vide order dated 8/12/2017 has decided to share the Central and State share in the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme in the ratio of 25:75 in respect of wages. For the year 2018-19, the Central Government has released Rs. 319.78 crore has been sanctioned, out of which the Central Government has released Rs. 79.94 crore. The Central Government has paid 25 per cent of the salary as above to the posts of Child Development Project Officer, District Programme Officer, Statistical Assistant, Headmistress/ Supervisor, etc. under this scheme. Remaining cadre posts are to be paid 100% from the State share. The matter was considered in the Cabinet meeting held on 5th June, 2018. To that Ma. The Cabinet has approved it. The Central Government is in the process of taking a new accounting head for payment of salaries of officers/ employees working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme as per the changed scale. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds for salaries from the previous head of accounts was under the consideration of the government till the creation of the new head of accounts.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: To meet the expenditure for the financial year 2018-19 for nine months upto December, 2018, the Government is approving the release of funds under Demand No.X-1 MAIN ACCOUNT Head 2236-POSHAN AHAAR from the budgeted funds as per the revised guidelines of the Government of India and the provisions of the circular dated 2.4.2018 of the Department of Finance, as mentioned in column No.6 of the statement accompanying the said decision.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for this fund. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai shall strictly comply with the checklist items mentioned in the circular dated 2.4.2018 in Reference No.1 of the Finance Department, the guidelines of the Central Government as well as the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and the Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing norms, the delegation of financial powers.
