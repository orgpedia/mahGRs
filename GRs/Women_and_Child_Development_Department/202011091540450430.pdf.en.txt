The Child Marriage (Prohibition) Act, 2006.
The Child Marriage (Prohibition) Rules, 2008.
Preamble: The Child Marriage (Prohibition) Rules, 2008 have been issued to implement the Child Marriage (Prohibition) Act, 2006 of the Central Government. Since the Child Marriage (Prohibition) Rules need to be amended in a time-bound manner, it was under the consideration of the Government to constitute a Drafting Committee of Experts to make recommendations on the amendments.
Government's decision:
The following Drafting Committee of Experts is being constituted by this Government to make recommendations on necessary amendments in the Child Marriage (Prohibition) Rules, 2008.
1.Mr. Nirmala Samant Prabhavalkar President 2. Shri. Santosh Shinde, Member, Vidhayak Bharati Shri Nishidh Kumar, Member, Social Behaviour Change Communication Shri. Divesh Marathe- District Child Protection Officer Buldhana Member 10. Mr. Ravi Patil, Deputy Commissioner, Child Development, Pune Member Secretary
The committee will submit its recommendations to the government within 15 days for necessary amendments in the Child Marriage (Prohibition) Rules, 2008.
