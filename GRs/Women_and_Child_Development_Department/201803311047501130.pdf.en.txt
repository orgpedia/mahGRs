The Government hereby approve the release of 80% of the said provision amounting to Rs. 54,40,000/- (Rupees Fifty Four Lakh Forty Thousand only) to the Women's Economic Development Corporation (MAWIM), Mumbai as capital contribution for the financial year 2017-2018. The Women's Economic Development Corporation should reconstruct the Savli hostel building from this fund.
District Women and Child Development Officer, Mumbai Suburban is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. They should collect this amount from the accounts and accounts office and give it to the Women's Economic Development Corporation. The Women's Economic Development Corporation should ensure strict compliance with the terms, conditions and rules laid down by the Finance Department from time to time while spending the funds distributed. Also, the utilization certificate of the funds disbursed should be submitted to the government.
