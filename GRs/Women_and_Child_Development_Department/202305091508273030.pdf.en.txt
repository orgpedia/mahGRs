Preamble: For the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Mission Vatsalya under the Department of Women and Child Development, a total amount of Rs. 10521.38 lakh has been budgeted for the year 2023-24 as Central and State share of Rs. 6320.62 lakh and Rs. 4200.76 lakh respectively.
Under the Mission Vatsalya Yojana, the Central Government has released central share of Rs. 5006.44 lakh. The matter of disbursement of these funds and their corresponding State share to the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune is under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In respect of Central Assistance received under Mission Vatsalya, the head of accounts/ objectives of the Statement of Objects has been changed to No. 4 and 6 respectively to the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune.
