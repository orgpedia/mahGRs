Preamble: For the financial year 2019-2020, revised estimates for 36 Auxiliary Grants (Pay) amounting to Rs. 1,07,41, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Seven Lakh Forty-Eight Thousand only) have been fixed under Computer Code 2235B 942. Out of this fund, the Government was considering to disburse the amount of Rs. 31,78,000/- (Rupees Thirty One Lakh Seventy Eight Thousand only) required to pay the salary of the employees of the Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board and the difference amount as per the Seventh Pay Commission to the Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board.
Government Decision: A sum of Rs. 31,78,000/- (Rupees Thirty One Lakh Seventy Eight Thousand only) is being disbursed to the Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board for payment of salary and difference as per the Seventh Pay Commission.
(2) The difference of the 7th Pay Commission of the employees of the Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board should be paid only after receiving the difference from the Central Social Welfare Board.
The Secretary, Maharashtra State Social Welfare Board, Mumbai shall take care to submit the utilization certificate of the funds distributed under the said Government decision as well as earlier to the Government.
