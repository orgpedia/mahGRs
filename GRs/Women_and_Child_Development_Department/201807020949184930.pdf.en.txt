Introduction: The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme which was started in the State of Maharashtra in 1975. Gradually, the number of projects under the scheme has increased and projects have been implemented in various urban, rural and tribal areas of the state. Under this scheme, 6 important services such as Anganwadi services for children, pregnant women and lactating mothers will be provided. The services of immunization, pre-primary education, supplementary nutrition, referral services, health check-up, health and nutrition education to women in the age group of 15 to 45 years, etc. are provided. Child Development Project Officers and other necessary machinery are also working at every project level for the implementation of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme.
