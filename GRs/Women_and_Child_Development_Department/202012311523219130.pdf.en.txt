Government's decision:
A" Consultative Committee" of the following Government/ Non-Government, Special Invited Members with expertise in various fields is being constituted under the chairmanship of the Minister of Women and Child Development to study in depth the issues, policies, laws, welfare initiatives and ongoing schemes of the development of women and children of the society.
