Preamble: As per circular No. 1 of the Department of Finance, the Budgetary allocation for Compulsory and Programme Expenditure for the financial year 2018-19 covering 9 months upto December 2018 has been approved to be released through Budget Distribution System within the limit of 70% of the total annual allocation. Accordingly, the matter was under consideration for release of funds admissible under the head 2235-Social Security and Welfare under the Women and Child Development Department.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: To meet the expenditure for the financial year 2018-19 for nine months upto December, 2018, the Government is approving to disburse funds to the extent of 70% of the budgeted funds under the head Demand No.X-1 MAIN ACCOUNT 2235-SOCIAL SECURITY AND WELFARE through the Budget Distribution System as mentioned in column No.V in the accompanying statement.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune will be the Controlling Officer for this fund. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune should strictly follow the checklist items mentioned in the circular dated 2.4.2018 in reference No.1 of the Finance Department and the guidelines issued by the Planning Department and Finance Department in this regard, the prevailing norms, delegation of financial powers etc.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune shall ensure that the funds made available on the Budget Distribution System under the above head for the year 2018-19 are spent and submit the utilization certificate to the Government.
