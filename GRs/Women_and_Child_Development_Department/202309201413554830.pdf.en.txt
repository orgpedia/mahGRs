Introduction: Empowerment of women is fundamental and essential for the overall development of the country.
Various schemes are being run by the central and state governments for the development of women. Though many schemes are being implemented for women empowerment, special efforts are needed for their social and economic upliftment. Also, the process of" women empowerment" needs to be made people-oriented and involve public participation. Accordingly, the government was considering the implementation of the" Chief Minister Women Empowerment Campaign" in the state with the aim of organizing women, training them, making them self-reliant, accelerating the implementation of government schemes related to women by making them people-oriented, representatives of all government agencies implementing schemes related to women under one roof and providing benefits of various schemes to the beneficiaries and organizing a felicitation ceremony for those beneficiaries.
Government's decision
Under this decision, the state government has decided to implement the' Mukhyamantri Mahila Sashaktikaran Abhiyan" to speed up the implementation of government schemes related to women in the state. Under this campaign, representatives of all the government agencies implementing schemes related to women will come together under one roof and provide the benefits of various schemes to the concerned women beneficiaries.

The outline of the campaign will be as follows. :
(1) Collection of information on Shakti Groups/ Women Self Help Groups.
To collect information about Shakti Groups/ Women Self Help Groups registered with various Government agencies at Taluk/ Municipality/ Municipal Corporation level.
Determination of Active Power Groups/ Women Self Help Groups.
To determine the necessary aspects for the empowerment of women.
(2) Bringing women into the mainstream. Connecting 1 crore women of the state to the stream of Shakti Groups/ Women Self Help Groups.
To connect at least 20 lakh women in the revenue department with women self-help groups/ Shakti groups during the campaign period.
To connect at least 2,50,000 women in each district through Shakti Groups/ Women Self Help Groups.
To connect at least 30,000 women in each block through Shakti Groups/ Women Self Help Groups.
To connect at least 200 women in every village through Shakti Groups/ Women Self Help Groups.
