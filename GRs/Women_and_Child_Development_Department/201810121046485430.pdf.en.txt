Rajiv Gandhi Scheme (SABLA) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for the empowerment of adolescent girls in the State implemented by the Office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai through the Department of Women and Child Development. The scheme is being implemented on a 50:50 sharing pattern between the Centre and the state.
The Central share of 50% (Account Head 2236 1802) in the budget for the financial year 2018-19 for meeting the dietary expenditure under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rajiv Gandhi Scheme for Empowerment of Adolescent Girls (SABLA) has been increased to Rs. 5033.54 lakh and State share of 50% (Head of Accounts 22361811) Rs. 5033.54 lakh.
