Introduction: Under the Mission Vatsalya Yojana, the salaries of employees in child care institutions and employees working in the state machinery for the same are also paid.
At present, these employees are providing their services as it is an essential service to serve in an organization that cares and protects children. However, the salaries of these employees should be paid on time.
Under this scheme, a proposal has been submitted to the Central Government through the State Government for the year 2023-24 and the proposal is under consideration of the Government of Maharashtra to disburse the funds made available for the salary of the employees working in 37 Government Children Homes, Observation Homes and to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Child Protection Society, Pune and Commissioner, Women and Child Development.
Government Decision: Under Mission Vatsalya Yojana, the head of accounts/ objectives in the statement of return is given as Rakkana No. 4 as well as
The decision is being circulated to the Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Child Protection Society, Pune and Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune.
