Introduction: The Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented by the Office of the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai through the Department of Women and Child Development. The Department of Finance vide Circular No. 1 dated 2nd April, 2018 has approved the scheme wise/ head wise budgetary allocation for 2018-19. As the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, the Finance Department has issued instructions to disburse funds accordingly after the receipt of funds from the Centre directly to the State Government. Considering the funds received from the Central Government as well as the component wise details of the funds disbursement by the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, the matter of disbursement of funds admissible under the Head Account Head 2236-POSHAN AHAAR under the Department of Women and Child Development was under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2018-19, the Government is approving to disburse funds on Budget Distribution System as mentioned in Column No. 5 of the Statement accompanying the said Government Decision within the limits of the funds budgeted under the respective objectives under headings 2236 1909 and 2236 1918 under Demand No.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer for this fund. The funds are being given to them. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai, in the circular dated 2.4.2018, issued vide No.1 of the Department of Finance, has issued the following instructions:
