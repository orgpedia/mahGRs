Government's decision:
A total of 1590 posts including 1542 on pay and 48 on honorarium in the establishment of Women and Child Development Commissionerate and a total of 2306 temporary posts including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms were extended from 01/03/2019 to 30/9/2019 as per the Government decision dated 06.03.2019.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 11.9.2019 in Reference No.5 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 for the temporary posts included in their figure and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the figure.
The letter dated 17.09.2019 in Reference No.4 certifies that 1590 temporary posts in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Maharashtra State, Pune and 716 posts filled by the external system have not been vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provision of the above Government Decision of the Finance Department, a total of 1590 posts, including 1542 on-pay and 48 honorary posts, have been filled in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development Department. The period is being extended from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
