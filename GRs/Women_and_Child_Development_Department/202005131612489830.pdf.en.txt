Government's decision:
The Department of Finance has issued a reference regarding the release of funds on mandatory and program expenses included in the state budget. However, as the funds released for the purpose of salary are insufficient, the Government have decided to increase the amount of salary from Rs. The headings in column No. 2. Out of the total budget of Rs. 16260.13 lakh, Rs. 1155.70 lakh is being disbursed to the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, Pune as shown in Figure 5.
