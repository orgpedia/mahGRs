INTRODUCTION: The Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme is being implemented by the Department of Women and Child Development with Central Share of 50% (Apex 2236-1927) and State Share of 50% (Apex 22361936) by the Office of the Commissioner, ICDS, Navi Mumbai. The ratio of central and state share is 50: 50.
The Central share for the Supplementary Nutrition Scheme for the financial year 2019-20 is 50% (Account Head 22361927) Rs. 605.6072 crore and State share of 50% (Head of Accounts 22361936) Rs. 605.6072 crore. 1211.2144 crore APIP has been approved.
In the State Budget 2019-20, the Central share under Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (Feeding Expenditure) is 50% (Head of Accounts 22361927) of Rs. 548.76 crore and State share of 50% (Head of Accounts 22361936) Rs. 548.76 crore. A sum of Rs. 1097.52 crore has been provided in the Budget. Out of this provision, as per Annexure-A annexed to the Government Circular No. BGT 2019/ PROCESS 14/ Q-4, dated 10th April, 2019, the Account Grant has been sanctioned for meeting the expenditure for the period April, 2019 to July, 2019 for the financial year 2019-20. Accordingly, the Central share of Rs. 175.00 crore and State share of Rs. 175.00 as against a total of Rs. A sum of Rs. 350.00 crore has been provided from the accounts. The matter of disbursement and approval of expenditure was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Decision of the Government: Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (Feeding Expenditure), the Central share under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (Feeding Expenditure) has been increased by 50% (head 22361927) to Rs. 548.76 crore and State share of 50% (Head of Accounts 22361936) Rs. 548.76 crore.
