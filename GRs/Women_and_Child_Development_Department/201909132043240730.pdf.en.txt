Preamble: In the State of Maharashtra, the Government has decided to provide a" Baby Care Kit" worth Rs.2000/-for the newborns (boy or girl) at the time of their first delivery to the women who have registered their pregnancy and are going to deliver at the Government Primary Health Center/ Government Hospital. As per the approval of the Cabinet, the Department of Women and Child Development, Govt. of India vide Decision No. The decision was taken on December 29, 2018. The scheme has been implemented with effect from 26th January, 2019. The Government has invited online e-tender for procurement of Baby Care Kits for the financial year 2019-20 for an amount of Rs. 80.00 crore. Approval has been granted as per the provision of revised procurement policy dated 1/12/2016.
Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai had published an e-tender dated 13/08/2019 for providing" Baby Care Kit" to the pregnant women at the time of first delivery for their newborns (boy or girl) registered and delivered at Government Primary Health Centres/ Government Hospitals in the State under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. Under this e-tendering. Indo Allied Protein Foods Pvt. Ltd., Dadar, Mumbai has announced a price of Rs. They have been declared L1 bidders as the lowest bidder was 1995.63.
According to the L1 tenderer, May. Indo Allied Protein Foods Pvt. Ltd., Dadar, Mumbai has supplied a total of 400,875 Baby Care Kits for the financial year 2019-20 at a cost of Rs. 79,99,98, 176/- (Rupees Nineteen Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Eighty Eight Thousand One Hundred and Seventy Six only). The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government Decision: Under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, women who have registered their pregnancy in the government primary health center/ government hospital in the state and are going to give birth there will be given the first delivery.

The supply order of the baby care kit should be issued subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The terms and conditions of the e-tender should be strictly adhered to while making the purchase. Also, the Commissioner, Integrated News Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai should ensure that the supply is made as specified in the e-tender.
2) The funds should be spent according to the policy and procedure prescribed by the government and according to the availability. A total of 400875 baby care kits are to be procured in a phased manner during the financial year 2019-20 and the above mentioned rates will be applicable for a period of 3 years in future (2020-21 to 2022-23). The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be responsible for spending the funds received from time to time as per the prescribed policy and procedure.
3) Supplier should supply the above" Baby Care Kit" materials at the project level.
The last date of supply should be made mandatory for the supplier to receive the" Baby Care Kit" materials from the supplier to the concerned within the prescribed period.
4) The supply of" Baby Care Kit" materials should be made by following the prescribed procedure from the available provision. The supplier has supplied the" Baby Care Kit" materials to the project level as per the prescribed quality, in the prescribed quantity as per the demand. This should be confirmed by the office of the Commissioner through the concerned Child Development Project Officer. Also, the" Baby Care Kit" should be stamped or labelled as" Supply by Government/ for use under Integrated Child Development Services Scheme." Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai will be responsible for any irregularity in the distribution/ supply and expenditure of" Baby Care Kit" materials or any accounting objections.
After the" Baby Care Kit" reaches the Anganwadi Centre, it should be recorded in the Inventory Collection Register of the concerned Child Development Project Officer, appropriate guidelines in this regard should be issued by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai.
