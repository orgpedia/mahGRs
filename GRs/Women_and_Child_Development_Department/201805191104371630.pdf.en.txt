The Proposal:
A total of 2,07,961 Anganwadi workers, 97,475 Anganwadi helpers and 13011 Mini Anganwadi workers have been provided financial assistance of Rs. The payment of Bhai Dooj gift of Rs.1000/- has been approved under the Government Decision dated 24th November, 2017.
As per the Government decision dated 23rd February, 2018, the amount of Bhai Dooj gift to the servitors has been increased from Rs.1000/-to Rs.2000/-from the year 2017-18.
The matter of paying the increased gift amount to the servitors was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS), an honorarium of Rs.2000/- has been given to Anganwadi workers, helpers and Mini Anganwadi workers from 2017-18. Earlier, the payment of Bhai Dooj gift of Rs.1000/- was allowed as per the Government decision dated 24th November, 2017. As per the increase in the Bhai Dooj gift amount, the Government is approving the enhanced Bhai Dooj gift amount of Rs. 1000/- each to Anganwadi workers/ helpers/ mini Anganwadi workers.
Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for the payment of Bhai Dooj to Anganwadi workers, helpers and mini Anganwadi workers working under urban, rural and tribal projects. Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai should gift Bhai Dooj to Anganwadi workers, helpers and mini Anganwadi workers.
