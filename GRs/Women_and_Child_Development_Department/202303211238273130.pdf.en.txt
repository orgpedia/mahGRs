The Proposal:
The Project Implementation Committee has been constituted as per the Government Resolution dated 24th May, 2013 of the Department to decide on the computerization/ e-governance proposals for all the field offices for implementation of the computerization/ e-governance project for the Department of Women and Child Development and its subordinate offices, Corporations, Commissions etc. The Committee has approved the appointment of 5 Advisors (1 Executive Advisor, 2 Senior Advisors and 2 Consultants) of KPMG for a period of 3 years with review of performance every year.
