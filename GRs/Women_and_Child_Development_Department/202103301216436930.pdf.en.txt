The Head of the Department has the power to sanction rent up to Rs.50,000/- and the administrative department has the full power to sanction rent above that. The matter of granting administrative approval to the building rent fixed by the Public Works Department Amravati at Rs 60,370/- (Rupees Sixty Three Hundred and Seventy only) is under the consideration of the Government.
Kirti Nitin Kolhatkar Ignorant Nurturer Mother Sou. Madhuri Nitin Kolhatkar Amravati owned Kolhatkar Memorial Hall, Datta Palace, Gandhi Chowk, Amravati. Subject to the condition that the Superintendent, Government Children's Home/ Children's Home (Senior) Amravati has entered into a lease agreement with the approval of the Senior Office through the Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune to take the building" Kolhatkar Memorial Hall" on lease basis. Under this order, the Government is approving the building rent of Rs. 60,370/- (Rupees Sixty Thousand Three Hundred and Seventy only) per month for a period of three years from 01/04/2019 to 31/3/2022.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune shall ensure that the rent provided under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme for the rent of the said building, the rent provided under the Integrated Child Protection Scheme for the rent of the Child Welfare Committee/ Juvenile Justice Board offices in Imari and the additional rent to be provided by the State Government shall be spent within the rent limit by preparing an account-wise statement. The funds required for the lease of the building should be immediately made available to the concerned District Women and Child Development Officer at the level of Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune.
