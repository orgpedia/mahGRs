Preamble: A Task Force was constituted under the decision of the Government of India to review and suggest measures to reduce malnutrition in Palghar district.
Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to constitute this Task Force to review and suggest measures to reduce malnutrition in all tribal areas of the State.
Government Decision: A Task Force is being constituted under the chairmanship of Mr. Deepak Sawant, former Minister (Public Health and Family Welfare) to review the causes of malnutrition in the tribal areas of the state and to pay special attention to these cases.
