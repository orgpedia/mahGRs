The Central Government has sanctioned a total of 110486 Anganwadi Centres (97475 Anganwadi Centres and 13011 Mini Anganwadi Centres) in the State of Maharashtra, out of which a total of 109603 Anganwadi Centres (97377 Anganwadi Centres and 12226 Mini Anganwadi Centres) have been operationalized.
Therefore, a proposal to grant permission to start the remaining 98 Anganwadi Centres and 785 Mini Anganwadi Centres totalling to 883 Anganwadi Centres has been approved by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai under reference No. 4 was submitted to the Government vide letter dated 30/03/2019.
Vacancies of Anganwadi workers, mini Anganwadi workers and helpers should not be filled up till the revised number of Anganwadis in the State is fixed under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme.

Along with this proposal, a proposal was submitted for the recruitment of Anganwadi workers, helpers and mini Anganwadi workers in 98 Anganwadi centers and 785 mini Anganwadi centers to be operated as above. Accordingly, the matter of allowing the opening of this Anganwadi Center and Mini Anganwadi Center and recruitment of posts there was under the consideration of the government.

Government Resolution No: ABVI-2019/ P.S.No.305/ K.S.6
Government Decision: In order to ensure effective implementation of Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme, the Government is giving approval to start 98 Anganwadi Centres and 785 Mini Anganwadi Centres which have been approved by the Central Government but are not operational. Also, the recruitment of Anganwadi Sevika, Helper and Mini Anganwadi Sevika in these Anganwadi Centres is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. While filling up the vacant posts of Anganwadi Sevika, Helper and Mini Anganwadi Sevika, the procedure prescribed in the Government Decision dated 13th August, 2014 and Government Decision dated 22nd September, 2016 should be strictly followed.
The amendments made by the Government from time to time in the recruitment process of Anganwadi workers/ helpers/ mini Anganwadi workers will be applicable to all newly recruited workers.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai should ensure that the above instructions are implemented properly.
