The said posts are not vacant for more than six months, said the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune vide reference No. 4 hereunder vide letter dated 24.08.2022. Accordingly, the matter of extending the term of the posts on salary, honorary posts and temporary posts filled by external agencies on the establishment of the Women and Child Development Commissionerate was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: A total of 1590 posts, including 1542 on pay and 48 honorary posts, in the establishment of the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development, and a total of 2306 temporary posts, including 716 posts filled through external mechanisms, are being extended from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale of these posts have been given in the statement.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under the Government Decision dated 18.08.2022 in the Finance Department Designation-2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC.
