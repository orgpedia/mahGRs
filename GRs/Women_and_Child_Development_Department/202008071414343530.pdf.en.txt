Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Raigad Bhawan, Navi Mumbai are hereby informed that necessary further action in accordance with the above-mentioned postings should be taken immediately. The office heads should relieve the transferred officers immediately and the said officers should join their transferred posts immediately. The concerned should ensure that their next salary and allowances will be withdrawn on the post they hold.
3.The concerned officers should report at the place of new appointment and inform the date of assumption of charge. Disciplinary action should be proposed to the government against the officers who do not join the transferred post within the stipulated time after appointment.
(4) The transfer benefits shall not be admissible to the officers who are being posted as per their request.
5. Confidential reports, service books and final salary certificates of the officers being transferred along with the order of posting as above should be sent to the new office immediately.
No leave shall be granted to the officers posted as above without prior approval or any leave applications of the officers posted as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, shall be accepted by the parent office.
7. While admitting the officers who are being posted as per above to the post to be given by the post, they should verify whether they were on leave/ absent or not.
