Introduction: - Under the umbrella scheme of "Midan Shakti," a centrally sponsored sub-scheme, "One Stop Centre" * component of "Sambal" sub-scheme, the affected women in distress, in need of care and protection in the state are provided psychological and counselling, medical, police assistance, temporary shelter, legal advice, etc. under one roof through these centres. Under this component scheme, 100% funding will be provided by the central government. Under this scheme, an account titled 2235B998 has been opened to budget funds for the construction of a One Stop Centre. Also, the government was considering opening a new accounting head to budget funds for the day-to-day management of the One Stop Centre.
Government Decision: - Under the Centrally Sponsored Umbrella Scheme of "Midan Shakti," a new Head of Accounts is being approved to be opened for budgeting funds for day-to-day management of One Stop Centres under the "One Stop Centre *" component scheme of the "Salary" sub-scheme.
