The Proposal:
According to the Finance Department. In pursuance of the circular dated 1.4.2019, the Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2019 has been passed and the schedule of proposed appropriations for the year 2019-2020 has been published in this Budget Publication in column No. The amounts shown in 5 and 6" are being allocated to the departments on the Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS) and the monthly fund statements from April to July, 2019 will be recorded on the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System by the administrative departments after the mandatory and programmatic expenditure fund (contribution) required by the department will be disbursed. Guidelines 5 to 17 are to be strictly followed.
For the financial year 2019-20, a provision of Rs. 24,45,61, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Forty Five Lakhs Sixty One Thousand Only) has been approved for 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under head 2235-1316. Considering the contribution made available to the Department, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 8,15,00, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Fifteen Lakhs only) to the Women's Economic Development Corporation (WEDC) for wage matters as per the circular in reference to Finance Department under head 2235-1316.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the contribution made to the Department for the financial year 2019-20, as per the circular issued in the context of the Finance Department, the Government hereby approve the release of Rs. 8,15,00, 000/- (Rupees Eight Crores Fifteen Lakhs Only) out of the budgetary provision of Rs. 24,45,61, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Four Crores Forty Five Lakhs Sixty One Thousand Only) for 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) under head 2235-1316 to the Women's Economic Development Corporation.
