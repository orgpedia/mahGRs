Government circular:
As per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Excellence in Service) Rules, 1982 and the Government Order dated 21.10.2011 of the General Administration Department, the merit list is required to be published every year with 1st January as the base date.
The final seniority list of 01.01.2018 has been issued in the category of Group-A in the class under the Commissionerate of Women and Child Development as per Government Circular No. MBAA-2018/ PR No. 14/ K.7, dated 06.03.2018.
The Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune handles the administrative and organisational/ service matters in the case of officers/ staff working under the office of the Commissioner, Women and Child Development, Pune and Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The respective ranks of the officers of these two Commissionerates are being published in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 and the final seniority list in respect of the said officers as per the relevant Government Decision/ Government Circular issued in this regard.
All the concerned officers after perusing the Service Enlistment List and if there are any objections/ errors or corrections regarding this Service Enlistment List, they should submit their detailed statement in writing to the Government within three weeks along with appropriate documents. In this way, appropriate decisions will be taken after considering the representations received by the government in order of merit and the service list will be published in the final form.
