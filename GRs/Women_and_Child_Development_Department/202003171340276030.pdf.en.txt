GOVERNMENT DECISION: 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai will be filled up with effect from 01.10.2019. The extension was granted vide Government Decision dated 27.09.2019 at Reference No.4 to continue till 29.02.2020. Accordingly, the proposal for extension of these 43 temporary posts has been submitted by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 16.03.2020 at Reference No.5.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 12.02.2020 in Reference No.6 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and also for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the Review earlier but which have not been included in the rolls.
The Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, State of Maharashtra, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 16.3.2020 has certified that the said posts are not vacant for more than six months. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the aforesaid Government Decision of the Finance Department, 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai are being extended from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
