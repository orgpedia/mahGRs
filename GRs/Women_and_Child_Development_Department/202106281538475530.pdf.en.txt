The proposal: As per the directions of the Juvenile Justice Committee of Judges of the Supreme Court, New Delhi, Reference No. Under the above Government decision, a task force has been constituted at the district level under the chairmanship of the Collector to take necessary measures to ensure that the children in the institutions working for the care and protection of children in the state during the COVID-19 outbreak, as well as the children who have lost both parents due to COVID-19, get their rightful rights and get proper care.
The matter of increasing the members of this task force was under the consideration of the government to make the work of this task force more dynamic and effective.
Government Supplementary Letter: Reference No. The following officers are being included in the task force formed under the above government decision.

The duties and responsibilities of the said officers in the said Task Force shall be as follows:
Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad- (1) To direct the concerned to provide detailed information about the children who have lost both parents to COVID-19 in the Zilla Parishad area to the Coordinating Officer.
2) Instruct all hospitals in the Zilla Parishad area to fill in the information from the patient about who should be given custody of his child during the period of admission to the hospital for COVID 8-19.
3) Ensure that an information board about Child Helpline 1098 is put up on the facade of all hospitals in the Zilla Parishad area.
Appointment of a separate medical team to provide immediate treatment to children's homes/ observation homes functioning in the Zilla Parishad area in the wake of COVID-19.
Education Officer: (i) To take steps to ensure that the right to education of children (0 to 18) of parents who have lost both their parents in their workplace or both parents are hospitalized during the period of COVID-19 outbreak is maintained.
2) All teachers are in online contact with all students during the period of COVID-19 outbreak, through them, all kinds of educational information about the children of parents who have lost both parents or both parents are admitted in the hospital and give it to the coordinator/ member secretary.
Principal Municipality/ Municipal Council: (i) To direct the concerned to provide detailed information about the children who have lost both parents to COVID-19 in the Municipality/ Municipal Council area to the Coordinating Officer.
2) To direct all hospitals in the municipal/ municipal council area to fill up the information from the patient as to who should be given the custody of his child during the period of admission to the hospital for COVID-19.
3) Ensure that the information board about Child Helpline 1098 is displayed on the facade of all hospitals in the municipality/ municipal council area.
Appointment of a separate medical team to provide immediate treatment to children's homes/ observation homes operating in the municipality/ municipal council area in the wake of COVID-19.
