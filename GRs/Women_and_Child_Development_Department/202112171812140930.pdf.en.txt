Preamble to the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. The Juvenile Justice Committee under the High Court of Bombay had suggested to the Government to develop a comprehensive computer system for effective implementation of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. Accordingly, a computerized system has been created to make all the activities online, collect information, control information in line with the effective implementation of the Juvenile Justice Act. Two separate modules have been developed for the Juvenile Justice Board and the Child Welfare Committee, which are functional mechanisms for children in conflict with law and children in need of care and protection, respectively. Out of the above two modules, the module on conflict children in the Juvenile Justice Information System developed by the Department of Women and Child Development for effective implementation of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 is Reference No. It is implemented as 01.
