Government Decision: Integrated Child Development Services Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai. The pay scale for the posts of Child Development Project Officer, District Programme Officer, Statistical Assistant, Headmistress/ Supervisor in the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme was 25:75 between the Centre and the State. Apart from this, the rest of the posts under the scheme were being paid 100 per cent from the state account.
While approving the APIP for 2023-24, the Central Government has further closed its accounting fund for wages. Accordingly, the salaries of the officers/ employees under the scheme are to be paid from the State Account Fund. Accordingly, the proposal. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. This has been approved in the meeting of the High Level Committee held on 8.8.2023.
In view of the above, the Principal Account Head/ Sub-head/ Objective in Racket No. 2 of the following Return is being reduced as shown in Racket No. 5 and increased by re-allocation as in Racket No. 6, in order to pay the salary of all the officers and employees of the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme from the 100% State Account Head. Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 22097.99 lakhs (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand Only) is being approved to be released from the existing provision after reallocation, as indicated in Rakkana No.8.
