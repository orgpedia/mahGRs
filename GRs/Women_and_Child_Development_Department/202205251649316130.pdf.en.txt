The Proposal:
With a view to ensure effective management of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in greater efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has issued Reference No. 1 here. dated 23.3.2021
Instructions have been given to follow the revised procedure. Accordingly, Department No. 2 here. The circular dated 1.7.2021 has provided revised instructions on Public Finance Management System (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, it is necessary to appoint a single nodal agency for the implementation of centrally sponsored schemes and open a separate account in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least a Deputy Secretary level officer as the nodal officer for coordination of these centrally sponsored schemes. Accordingly, the Department of Women and Child Development, Govt. 3 here. Commissioner Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai and Commissioner Women and Child Development, Pune have been appointed as Single Nodal Agency (Single Coordinating Mechanism) vide Government Decision dated 25.8.2021. The Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary concerned has been appointed as the Nodal Officer.

Government Resolution No: BGT-2018/ P.S. No.57/ Ka-4
Since the central share received for the centrally sponsored schemes of the Department of Women and Child Development will be deposited in the SNA (Single Nodal Account), it was decided to open the SNA (Single Nodal Account) for the Department of Women and Child Development at the administrative department level in order to effectively manage the fund and bring more effectiveness in the process of fund allocation. Accordingly, SNAs (Single Nodal Account) for Centrally Sponsored Schemes have been opened at the administrative block level. However, for ease of functioning, the matter of transfer of SNA (Single Nodal Account) accounts to Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Raigad Bhawan, Navi Mumbai and Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The functions of SNA (Single Nodal Account) opened at administrative division level for Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Women and Child Development Department are being transferred to Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Raigad Bhawan, Navi Mumbai and Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune. The scheme-wise list of SNA (Single Nodal Account) is annexed as Annexure A.
Funds for Centrally Sponsored Schemes will be received as per the revised methodology. Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Raigad Bhawan, Navi Mumbai and Commissioner, Women and Child Development Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune have issued instructions to all the District Collectors and Municipal Commissioners in this regard. Necessary action should be taken immediately as per the instructions given in the circular dated 21.7.2021 and the accompanying annexure.
