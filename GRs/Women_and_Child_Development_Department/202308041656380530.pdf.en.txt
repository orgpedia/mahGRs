The Task Force has been constituted with effect from 20.06.2023. The government was considering giving the status of minister to the chairman of the committee, Deepak Sawant.
Government's decision:
The Chairman of the Task Force constituted to review the causes of malnutrition in the tribal areas of the state and to take measures to pay special attention to these cases, Ma Shri Deepak Sawant ji is being given the status of Minister from the date of this government decision.
