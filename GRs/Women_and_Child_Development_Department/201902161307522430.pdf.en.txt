The Proposal:
As per circular No.1 of the Department of Finance, for the financial year 2018-19, (23) (01) Defence Officers have been allocated Rs.100 crore to meet the expenditure on schemes for women development under headings 2235-A-857, 01- Salary, 11- Travel Expenditure, 13 Office Expenditure Objectives. 215030 was budgeted. Of these, Rs. 150520 through Budget Distribution System within the limit of 70% as per reference No. 3 has been distributed as per the government decision here. However, Rs. 64510 (Rupees Thousand) is required to be provided.
However, to meet the expenditure for the financial year 2018-19, the Government Circular dated 25/1/2019 of the Department of Finance has prescribed the limit of expenditure of the Budgetary Fund to be 90% on account of mandatory expenditure in the Revenue Account and 80% on account of programme expenditure and 90% on account of mandatory expenditure in the Capital Account and 100% on account of programme expenditure.
