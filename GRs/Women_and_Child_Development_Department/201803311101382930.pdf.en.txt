Government's decision:
In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 20.1.2018 passed at Reference No.3, 42 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai were extended from 1.1.2018 to 28.2.2018. However, the proposal for extension of 43 temporary posts as per the revised figure approved under Government Resolution dated 5.9.2014 at Reference No.3 has been submitted by the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide letter dated 27.3.2018 at Reference No.5.
2. Under the Government Resolution dated 5.3.2018 in Reference No.6 of the Finance Department, all the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the term of office from 1.3.2018 to 30.9.2018 for the temporary posts included in their rolls and all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the rolls.
The said posts are not vacant for more than six months as per the letter dated 27.3.2018 addressed to the Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai vide No.5
has been certified. Accordingly, as per the provisions of the aforesaid Government Order of the Finance Department, 43 temporary posts in the establishment of Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Navi Mumbai are being extended from 1.3.2018 to 30.9.2018. Cadre-wise details of the total number of posts and the pay scale for these posts have been indicated in the accompanying statement.
