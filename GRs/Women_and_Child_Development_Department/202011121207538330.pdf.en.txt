Introduction: The context. As per the Government decision dated 23.2.2018, the Government was considering payment of Bhai Dooj gift amount@ Rs.2,000/- each to the Anganwadi workers, Anganwadi Helpers and Mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme for the financial year 2020-21.
Government's decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS), an amount of Rs. 3860.84 lakh (Rupees Thirty Eight Crore Sixty Four Lakh Eighty Four Thousand only) is being disbursed to the Anganwadi workers, Helpers and Mini Anganwadi workers for payment of Bhai Dooj gift amount of Rs.2,000/- each for the financial year 2020-21.
Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai is being declared as the Controlling Officer for the payment of Bhai Dooj to Anganwadi workers, helpers and mini Anganwadi workers working under the Integrated Child Development Services Scheme.
Expenditure on this shall be met from the budgetary allocation of Rs. 4122.50 lakh for the purpose of 31 Subsidiary Grants (Post-Payment) under the head Demand No.X-1, 2236, Nutritional Food, Distribution of 02 Nutritional Food and Beverages, 101 Special Nutritional Feeding Programme, (08) Integrated Child Development Services Scheme Project, (08) (06) Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (Bhau Beej Gift) (Additional State Share 100%) (Programme) (2236 1954) during the financial year 2020-21.
8. Commissioner, Integrated Child Development Services Scheme, Maharashtra State, Navi Mumbai should take immediate action to gift Bhai Dooj through PFMS system to Anganwadi workers, helpers and mini Anganwadi workers.
5. This Government decision is based on the informal reference of Finance Department No. 175/ Expenditure-6, dated 12/11/2020 is being issued as per the concurrence given by the Department.
