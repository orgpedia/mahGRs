The proposal:
As the building of Savitri Devi Phule Women's Hostel, Mumbai is not repairable, as per the memorandum dated 14/07/2023 in Reference No. 1, demolition of the said hostel building has been approved. In this regard, a letter dated 13/03/2023 addressed to the Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Mumbai, for the reconstruction of the hostel at Savitri Devi Phule Women's Hostel, Charni Road, Mumbai has been submitted. This budget has been scrutinized by the Public Works Department and the cost of the work has been considered in the budget. Also dated 12/09/2023 Ma. As per the consensus reached at the meeting of the high-power committee chaired by the Chief Secretary, the government was considering granting administrative approval for the reconstruction of Savitri Devi Phule Women's Hostel, Charni Road, Mumbai.
