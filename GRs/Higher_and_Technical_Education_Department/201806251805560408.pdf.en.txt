Introduction: The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 has been published in the Gazette dated 28th December, 2016. Section 32 of the Act provides as follows:
9) All Government institutions of higher education and other higher education institutions receiving aid from the Government shall reserve not less than five per cent seats for persons with benchmark disabilities.
2) The person with benchmark disabilities shall be given an upper age relaxation of five years for admission in institutions of higher education.
As per the provisions of the Act, 5% seats are required to be reserved for persons with disabilities in various Government and non-Government aided institutions imparting undergraduate and postgraduate education under the Directorate of Technical Education.
