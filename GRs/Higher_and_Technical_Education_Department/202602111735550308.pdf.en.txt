The Proposal: -
The Director of Higher Education, State of Maharashtra, Pune vide letter dated 1) dated 20.02.2025 has proposed water leakage prevention measures, dyeing, fixing of windows and doors, measures for regular water supply to toilets / washrooms, various repair and renovation works in the building of Elphinstone College, Mumbai.
Expenditure budgets for the work have been submitted to the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Mumbai and the technical consent for the proposed work has been given by the Chief Engineer, Public Works, Mumbai. Accordingly, the proposal was under consideration of the Government for approval.
