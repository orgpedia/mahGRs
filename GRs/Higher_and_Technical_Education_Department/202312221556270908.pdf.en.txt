Preamble: Considering the need for a separate university for the disabled students, the government was considering the formation of an expert committee in this regard to facilitate their higher education by considering the specific methods and facilities required for the study, teaching and evaluation of the disabled students and to effectively implement the policy in this regard and complete their education effectively.
The government's decision:
The following expert committees are being formed in connection with the establishment of a separate university for the education of disabled students in the state.

Functions of the Committee:
Students with disabilities are scattered throughout the state and are enrolled in a variety of courses. And they're learning with the students. In such a situation, it is necessary to recommend to the government with detailed details as to whether or how a single form separate Divyang University is required.
(ii) to recommend the establishment of a University for the disabled, taking into account its location, required land, construction, requirement of land, etc.
3. To make recommendations with detailed information regarding the cost estimates, faculty and non-faculty staff, and the financial burden on the state in connection with the establishment of the university.
Details of possible non-recurring, recurring expenditure etc. while setting up the University.
A detailed plan of the various departments of the university and the general structure of the university.
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and the concept of Inclusive Education in the Act, establish a separate university for students with disabilities
They can't get the same education as normal children. Also, to study in depth the possibility of developing a sense of alienation among students with disabilities and make recommendations.
7. To make recommendations as to how the students studying in the university will get employment opportunities along with education.
Establishment of a separate departmental centre for students with disabilities in a traditional university instead of a separate university.
Presenting comparative information on the qualitative difference.
9.To consider and recommend whether the nature of the university will be single or affiliated to other colleges.
10. To study the performance of the university in other States where there is a separate university for the disabled and suggest measures to address the issues and difficulties faced by the university.
To make recommendations with a view to aligning the courses taught in the proposed University with the new National Education Policy of the Central Government/ University Grants Commission.
12.Determining the category of EWS students, studying the courses for it, the criteria of the University Grants Commission and making detailed recommendations.
Considering the recommendation made in the National Education Policy, 2020 regarding multidisciplinary education and research-oriented university, how can a university be established only for the disabled? If so, to make detailed recommendations on the research programmes, multidisciplinary courses and teaching methodologies to be proposed for the implementation of the said policy.
The Committee will submit a report to the Government within two months with all inclusive details in consultation with various experts and institutions on the above agenda items as well as matters which may be considered appropriate for setting up of the University.
Non-official members of this committee will be entitled to travel allowance. The provision for this should be made from the funds available for the office expenses of the Joint Director, Higher Education, Pune.
