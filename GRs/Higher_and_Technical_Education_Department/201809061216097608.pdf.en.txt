Three separate committees have been constituted under the Government Resolution No. 1 under the context of publishing the literature of Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu. Under the Government Resolution No. 2, it has been approved to search the literature of these great men, to make tours in the state/ outside the state if necessary, to examine the literature, to carry out detailed and research process of the literature, to print and reprint the literature and to bring out the edition.
In accordance with the Government Resolution No. 3, The date has been extended upto 23.11.2016. Proposal for further extension The Prime Minister agreed. Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide Reference No. The next volume of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar published under the letter of 4 was printed and constructed at a total cost of Rs. 20,20,003/- (Rupees Twenty Lakhs Twenty Thousand Three only) has been certified as yet unpaid and requested to approve the said expenditure. Accordingly, the matter of approving the expenditure was under the consideration of the government.
