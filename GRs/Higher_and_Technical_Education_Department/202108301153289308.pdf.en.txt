The government's decision:
The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 has come into force with effect from 01.03.2017. Keeping in view the increasing pass percentage in higher secondary schools and the inclination of students towards a particular course, so that no student is deprived of the desired higher education, Section 109 (8) of the Act provides for expeditious recognition of additional fresh batches of specified faculties in exceptional circumstances. In the academic year 2021-22, the non-agricultural universities of the state had submitted proposals to the government for additional new batches of specific faculties. The proposals were examined in accordance with the criteria laid down under the relevant Government decisions. After this inspection, the universities recommended to the government the proposals for the rapid addition of new batches of specific faculties of the colleges mentioned in the following statement.
As the name suggests, they are being rejected for not meeting the specified criteria.
