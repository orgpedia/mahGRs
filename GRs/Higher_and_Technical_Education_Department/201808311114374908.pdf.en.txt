The proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 has come into force with effect from 01.03.2017. Keeping in view the increasing pass percentage in higher secondary schools and the inclination of students towards a particular course, so that no student is deprived of the desired higher education, Section 109 (8) of the Act provides for expeditious recognition of additional fresh batches of specified faculties in exceptional circumstances. In the academic year 2018-19, the non-agricultural universities of the state had submitted proposals to the government for additional new batches of specific faculties. The Government Resolution No. The inspection was carried out as per the norms prescribed under NGC-2017/ (128/18)/ Mashi-4, dated 04.07.2017. After this inspection, the proposals of the universities recommended to the government, the proposals of the fast-track additional new batches of specific faculties of the colleges mentioned in the following statement are being rejected as they do not meet the criteria as indicated in their names.
