Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's ideals of activism and political and social experiments are important.
The biography and work of this king, who brought progressive changes in Indian governance and social reforms, is an inspiration for the new generation and rulers, but the experience, experiments and documents of the Maharajas political career can be of great use to the civil servants, advanced scholars of management, architects, legal scholars and economists.
The appointment of the members of the committee was under the consideration of the government.
The following eminent personalities, who have studied the biography of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, are being appointed as nominated members on the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti:
