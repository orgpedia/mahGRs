Preamble: To implement the National Mission on Higher Education (NMHE), Government of India, to ensure quality, universal access and equity in higher education in the State of Maharashtra. The decision has been taken in the Cabinet meeting held on 21/04/2015. In accordance with the decision taken by the State Cabinet, the scheme will be implemented effectively in the state. The State Project Directorate has been established by a Government Resolution dated 20th May, 2015.
The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India has created 14 components under the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan 2.0. These 14 elements are: 5 includes the component" New Government Model Degree College." NITI Aayog has identified 70 aspirational districts across the country. The two districts are Washim and Nandurbar.
The Project Approval Board of the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) has been constituted to ensure the availability of educational facilities in the district on the basis of Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER), number of girls and population of backward classes. In the 12th meeting held on 25th May, 2018, approval has been given for setting up of new Government Model Degree Colleges in two districts of Maharashtra, namely Nandurbar and Washim.
Under the scheme, Rs. 1200.00 lakh has been sanctioned for non-recurring expenditure. The scheme is being implemented on a 60:40 sharing basis between the Centre and the state. 720.00 lakhs and Rs. 480.00 lakhs.
As per the pre-requisites for the establishment of the Model Degree College, the District Collector, Nandurbar has sanctioned 4.00 Ha. for the proposed Model Degree College at Nandurbar in Block No. 65/1 A at Mauje Tokartale. The land has been given in the name of Higher and Technical Education Department.
The Public Works Department has agreed to the construction of the hostel building at Government Model Degree College, Nandurbar after technical scrutiny of the budget.
