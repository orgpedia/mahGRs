The proposal:
To ensure that the teaching/ non-teaching staff in the non-government aided arts institutions get the same benefits as the teachers/ non-teaching staff in the non-government aided schools under the control of the School Education Department. The High Court of Bombay at Petition No. 2817/2014 was filed. In the petition, Md. The Court has disposed of the petition by passing the following order dated 5th October, 2017:" The service benefits to the teachers/ non-teaching staff in the non-government aided arts institutions should be made permissible on the same lines as the service benefits to the teachers/ non-teaching staff in the non-government aided schools under the School Education Department. "
02. Ma. In pursuance of the orders of the Court, the following Government decisions have been issued to allow in-service benefits to the teaching/ non-teaching staff of Non-Government Aided Arts Institutions:

03. Contempt Petition No. 55/2019. In view of the orders of the High Court of Bombay, the Government was considering to extend the direct benefit of the above Government decisions to the teaching/ non-teaching staff of Non-Government Aided Arts Institutions with effect from 5th October, 2017. Also, the matter of allowing the benefit of" compassionate appointment" to the next of kin of the teachers/ non-teaching staff of government aided arts institutions as demanded under Writ Petition No. 2817/2014 was also under consideration of the government. Accordingly, the proposal was placed before the Cabinet in its meeting held on 17 th November, 2022. The proposal has been approved by the state cabinet. Accordingly, the following decision was taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: ODR-2711/ PR No. 50/ TANSI-6 dated 3.10.2018 to implement the general provisions (with future amendments) of the Maharashtra General Provident Fund Rules, 1998 to the teaching/ non-teaching staff of non-government aided arts institutions. Further, to: (i) implement time bound promotion scheme for Class-III and Class-IV employees; (ii) implement two-tier/ three-tier pay scale for teaching posts; (iii) make service rendered in a recognized non-aided arts institution eligible for senior and selection category; (iv) implement accrued leave encashment for non-teaching staff; (v) implement maternity leave provisions for women teachers/ non-teaching staff; (vi) implement the provisions of Schedule III of the M.N.S. 3.The information regarding the application for employment by the relatives of the concerned employees and the documents to be submitted along with it shall be as mentioned in Annexure' B'.
4.In case of death or premature retirement of the employee, the concerned officer shall provide the necessary information (Annexure C) to the relatives within three months or within fifteen days of submission of the family pension document and submit the application of the candidate in the prescribed form to the Inspector, Painting and Crafts.
In case of non-government aided art institutions, the information in Annexure D attached by all the Divisional Assistant Inspectors, Painting and Sculpture should be sent to the Director, Directorate of Arts through the Inspector, Painting and Sculpture.
Since the Compassionate Policy for Teachers/ Non-Teaching Staff in Non-Government Aided Arts Institutions has been implemented with effect from 5th October, 2017, the condition 5A and 5B of the Rules in Annexure A shall not be applicable to the relatives of the teachers/ non-teaching staff who have died/ retired prematurely or have been rendered incapacitated/ ineligible for service from 5th October, 2017 till the date of this Government decision. However, such relatives will be required to apply for appointment on compassionate grounds within 3 months from the date of the said Government decision.
Compassionate appointment can be made to all posts up to the pay scale of Rs. 38,600-1, 22,800/- (S-14) (Rs. 5500-9000 as per Fifth Pay Commission) in Aided Art Institutions. Also, these orders will be applicable for appointments to posts which have been approved by the government.
In case of any future changes in the policy/ rules made under the Government Decision dated 31st December, 2002 of the School Education Department, the changes will be applicable to the teachers/ non-teaching staff of the non-government aided arts institutions.
These orders have been issued in concurrence with the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 374/22/ Service-3, dated 05.09.2022, Informal Reference No. 214/ Services, dated 06.09.2022 and Informal Reference 889/2022/ Expenditure-5, dated 13.09.2022.
