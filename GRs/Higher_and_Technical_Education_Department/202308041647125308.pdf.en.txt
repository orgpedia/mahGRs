The government order:
The State Government officers, as per the decision of the Government under the General Administration Department, and
Consolidated guidelines on the procedure to be followed for promotion of employees have been issued. In paragraph (ii) of the said Government Resolution, the posts in accordance with the 6th Pay Commission having Grade Pay of Rs. Provision has been made to constitute a Divisional Promotion Committee to recommend to the appointing authority regarding promotion/ grant of date of promotion for all Group A posts which are Rs. 7599/- or less.
Accordingly, all the offices/ institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education, whose grade pay as per the 6th Pay Commission is Rs. 7599 or less.
A Divisional Promotion Committee is being constituted as under to recommend to the appointing authority regarding grant of date of promotion/ posting for such Group A cadre posts (outside the purview of the Establishment Board)
