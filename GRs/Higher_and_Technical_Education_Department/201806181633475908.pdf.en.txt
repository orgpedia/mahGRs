As per the suggestion of the Cabinet Sub-Committee headed by the Minister of Revenue, Relief and Rehabilitation, Public Works (excluding Public Undertakings), the income limit of the parents in both the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and the Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Living Allowance Extension Scheme was required to be the same. Therefore, it was under consideration of the Government to implement the scheme of extending Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Living Allowance to the students belonging to economically weaker sections (excluding marginal farmers/ registered labourers) having annual income up to Rs. 8.00 lakh.
The government's decision:
The Government has approved the implementation of Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Allowance Extension Scheme from the academic year 2018-19 for the students belonging to economically weaker sections whose family (both parents together) has an annual income up to Rs. 8.00 lakh who take admission through centralized admission process for professional courses prescribed by the Government in recognized Government, Government aided, Government unaided and permanent unaided colleges/ technical institutions in the State. With this decision, the maximum number of beneficiary students fixed for each district for students with an income limit of Rs. 1.00 lakh to Rs. 8.00 lakh will remain unchanged at 500. The other terms and conditions of the scheme as per various government decisions will remain unchanged.
