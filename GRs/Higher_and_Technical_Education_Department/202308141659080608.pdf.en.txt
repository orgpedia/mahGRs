Preamble: The Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi has approved the opening of new institutions of Diploma Pharmacy course on permanent non-aided basis in the State from the academic year 2023-24. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has submitted a proposal in this regard to the Government under the above reference letter.
Government's decision
In pursuance of the approval granted by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi from the academic year 2023-24, the institution mentioned in the attached Form-A is being granted approval by the Government to start new institutions of Diploma Pharmacy course on permanent non-grant basis subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions 1) The approval shall be on a perpetual basis without subsidy.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the Government of India in this regard as well as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education/ Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi dated 21st May, 1983.
3) The intake capacity of the institution for the said course shall be limited as mentioned against their name in the prospectus.
(iv) The Institute shall be required to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education/ Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
5) The institution concerned must be affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education for these diploma courses.
6) The Principals of the respective institutions shall submit an undertaking to the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai that the institution will be fully responsible for providing all the facilities as per the standards of Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. Only after this, the directorate has to give consent to these institutions to start the course.
