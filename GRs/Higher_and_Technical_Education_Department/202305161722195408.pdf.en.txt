Introduction To make India a knowledge superpower, to provide necessary skills and knowledge to students and to address the shortage of manpower in science, technology, education and industry, to provide quality education, innovative teaching and research facilities to the Indian population, the Government of India has implemented the New National Education Policy-2020. The New Education Policy envisions a holistic and multidisciplinary education system that aims to develop all human potential in an ethically-integrated manner.
The directions regarding the curriculum and ranking framework were issued in accordance with the report submitted by the sub-committee headed by Dr. Ravindra Kulkarni, former Pro-Vice-Chancellor, University of Mumbai, Mumbai, constituted under the Government Decision dated 26.04.2022. issued pursuant to the Government Decision dated 06.12.2022.
To suggest and guide measures for redressal of grievances arising out of review of implementation of the recommendations of the Sub-Committees constituted in pursuance of the implementation of National Education Policy, 2020 in the State. The Steering Committee has been constituted as per the Government Decision dated 26.012.2022. The Steering Committee has submitted its interim report on the curriculum and rating framework. 19th and 20th April 2023

The revised instructions and directions have been issued vide Government Order dated 20.04.2023 for uniform implementation of syllabus and grading scheme in the State after fruitful discussions in the seminar held on 20.04.2023.
The Steering Committee has made recommendations vide letter dated 09.05.2023 regarding the distribution of ranks for 2 year Post Graduate courses with exit option/ one year Post Graduate courses. The government was considering issuing similar instructions and directions for the implementation of these recommendations on a uniform scale in the state.
The government's decision:
Revised instructions regarding syllabus and grading pattern for 2 years Post Graduate courses with exit option/ 1 year Post Graduate courses as per Annexure attached are being issued and these instructions are to be implemented from the year 2023-24.
These directions are being issued in accordance with the provisions of Section 5 (81) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
The implementation of these directives will be reviewed by the government through the Steering Committee from time to time.
