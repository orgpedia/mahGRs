Maharshi Dhondo Keshav Karve established the Shrimati Nathibai Damodar Thackersey Women's University (the first women's university in the country) in 1916 to bring women into the mainstream of education in Maharashtra. Through this university, different faculties were started for women and started providing higher education to women. The University is in its post-centenary journey and will complete 106 years in 2022. Keeping in view the objective of empowering women through education, the University had selected Ballarpur, G. Chandrapur district for the purpose of providing employment to local women by starting skill development courses for women with low higher education enrolment rate in rural and tribal areas as per the scheme of the Government and proposed to set up a knowledge campus of the University to empower women with quality and modern education. Maharshi Karve Women Empowerment Knowledge Complex at Visapur, Ballarpur, G.Chandrapur of Women's University has been constructed at a cost of Rs. 4265.98 lakh in the Budget Reference No. 1 here. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 02.06.2023. This work is being done under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" and the entire funding for this work is being provided by the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs.
