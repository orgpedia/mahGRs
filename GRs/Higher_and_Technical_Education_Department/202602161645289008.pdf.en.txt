To conduct a comprehensive study regarding grant-in-aid to women's colleges on permanent non-aided basis in Naxal-affected tribal areas. On constitution of a 5-member expert committee under the chairmanship of the Minister (Higher and Technical Education).
Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
GOVERNMENT RESOLUTION NO. ANNUDA-2026 / E-732110 / MASHI-4
Hated Extension Building, Mumbai 400032.
Date: February 96, 2026.
The Proposal: -
Considering the fact that the enrolment ratio of women in education in the Naxal-affected areas is found to be lower than the state average, the grant to these colleges will increase the education rate of girls in these areas, Ms. The formation of a 5-member expert committee headed by the Minister (Higher Education) was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
To conduct a comprehensive study on grant-in-aid to women's colleges recognized on a permanent non-subsidised basis in Naxal-affected tribal areas. A 5-member expert committee is being constituted under the chairmanship of the Minister (Higher and Technical Education) as follows.
