Introduction: Fr. Baby Apte has played an important role in the student movement. He trained many students. He also developed leadership qualities in the youth and worked away from the limelight, doing every work with loyalty and hard work. Today's youth need to be inspired for the national youth power.
The students and youth movement has been a major contributor to the constructive and agitational aspects of social change. A permanent study center is needed. Through this course, the new generation needs a centre that can be effective in bringing about change through a movement of political, social and economic change.
The course is running successfully in many universities abroad and it was intended to run the course from at least one university in the state and it should be done from Mumbai University. In this regard, the University of Mumbai. Bal Apte Centre for Studies in Students and Youth Movement. On the 4th December 2017. A meeting was held under the chairmanship of the Minister (Higher and Technical Education). As per the decision taken in the meeting, Prof. The university has decided to start a centre in the name of Baby Apte. It has been approved by the Governing Council and the Discussion Council held on 11th December 2017.
University of Mumbai. Bal Apte Centre for Studies in Students and Youth Movement to be set up from the academic year 2019-20.
