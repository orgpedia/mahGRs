The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
(5) The institution concerned shall meet other legal requirements by depositing the requisite consolidated deposit with the Director, Technical Education in a joint account.
6) Admission to the said Diploma/ Degree/ Post Graduate Degree Courses will be done as per the rules/ procedure of admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
If the fees charged by the institutions concerned are more than the tuition fees prescribed by the fee regulatory authority, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
8) The institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the government rules. Salaries and allowances should be paid to the employees as per the rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
9) The library in the institute is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students. Also, the students are provided with all the educational facilities as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). laboratories, libraries, classrooms, etc.
10) In the context of ragging. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999, need to be strictly implemented.
Other terms and conditions as laid down by the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, Director, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai and the University concerned shall continue to apply.
The Director shall recover the processing fee from the institutions which have not paid to the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State and the Director, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai, before giving final approval to the same and the Director, Technical Education and the Director, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai shall issue necessary orders within the next 10 days for the inclusion of the course as per the approval of this Government in the centralized admission process.
