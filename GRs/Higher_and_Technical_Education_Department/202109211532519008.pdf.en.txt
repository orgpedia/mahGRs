The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi vide its letter dated 01.01.2020, has prescribed a Sandwich Pattern for the courses offered at Government Institute of Technology, Mumbai from the academic year 2019-20. Further, as per the letter of Reference No. 2 and 3, the Government Institute of Technology, Mumbai has extended the deadline for the implementation of this Diploma Course as a Sandwich Pattern for the academic year 2020-21 and 2021-22.
Accordingly, the proposal for grant of Government approval has been submitted by the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide their letter No. 4 in the above context.
The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government Decision: The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has been granting recognition to the courses listed in Column 2 in the table below from the academic year 2019-20 in Government Technological Institute, Mumbai as per the recognition given as Sandwich Pattern as mentioned in Column 3.
