Introduction:- The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has approved the above-mentioned No. 2 here. Pursuant to the approval given vide letter dated 10.06.2023 and issued by the Director, Technical Education vide their aforesaid No. 3 here. The Government was contemplating to start a new Government Engineering College at Kolhapur with 5 courses with an intake capacity of 60 each as proposed vide letter dated 19.06.2023.
GOVERNMENT DECISION:- In accordance with the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education and as proposed by the Director of Technical Education, Government approval is being given to start a new Government Engineering College at Kolhapur from the academic year 2023-24 subject to the following terms and conditions as indicated in the table:

Terms and Conditions: 1) The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in the said matter.

4) Regarding the ragging. The Supreme Court's directions and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999, should be strictly implemented.
