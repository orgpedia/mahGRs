Preamble: In pursuance of the notification dated 19th May, 2018 notified vide Section 2 (d) read with Section 3 (1) and (2) of the Maharashtra Unaided Professional Institutions (Regulation of Admission and Fee) Act, 2015, the professional diploma courses in Schedule A, namely Diploma in Engineering and Technology at Roll No. 1, Diploma in Pharmacy at Roll No. 2 and Diploma in Hotel Management and Catering Technology at Roll No. 3, are excluded from professional courses vide Government Notification No.
Accordingly, as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 3, the admission process of diploma level courses for the academic year 2018-19 has been directed to be continued by the Director, Technical Education, excluding the scope of the Admission Regulatory Authority.
In pursuance of the said Government Decision, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai as the" Competent Authority" for implementing the matters relating to admission process to Diploma Courses and to confer all powers to the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai (Competent Authority) to spend from the Director's personal register for the matters mentioned in the Government Decision at Reference No. 1 for effective implementation of the admission process to various Diploma Courses in accordance with the provisions of the Government Recognized Admission Manual.

Government Decision: The Director, Technical Education shall be empowered to take all decisions regarding the admission process of various Diploma level courses under the Directorate of Technical Education from the academic year 2018-2019 in online mode, keeping the necessary confidentiality, in a proper manner, keeping in view the interest of the students and parents, by promoting, disseminating and creating awareness. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being declared as the" Competent Authority" for admission to Diploma in Engineering and Technology, Diploma in Pharmacy and Diploma in Hotel Management and Catering Technology.
Further, the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government Decision No.PLA-2015/ (P.S. 168/2015)/ TANSHI-3, D.C., on all matters relating to diploma admission process and appointment of service providers as per the prevailing procedures of the Government for the purpose of developing the online computer system required for effective implementation of admission process of various diploma courses in accordance with the provisions of the Government Recognized Admission Rules and developing the necessary software for the purpose of maintaining the sensitivity and confidentiality of the online admission process. As per the provisions dated 1st January, 2018, the powers to take action on expenditure from the personal accounts of the Directors are being delegated to the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai (Competent Authority).
