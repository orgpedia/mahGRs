Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was the founder of the Maratha Empire in India. Chhatrapati Shivaji
Maharaj built a powerful and progressive state on the strength of a disciplined army and a well-organized administrative system. The thoughts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj should be awakened in everyone's mind and information about him should reach everyone. Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University
Proposal for grant of approval to start Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Adhiveshan Kendra Reference No. 1 is presented here. The following activities are planned to be carried out through this Adhiveshan Kendra:- To disseminate and propagate the life work of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, to conduct research and study on the life work of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, to produce literary works related to the life work of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
The policy regarding the establishment of chair centres in the name of eminent personalities in various non-agricultural universities in Maharashtra has been prepared in the context of reference No. 2 here. Approval at Cabinet meeting
has come. Therefore, in pursuance of the said proposal and policy, the matter of granting administrative approval for the establishment of" Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Adhyasan Kendra" at Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision:- The thoughts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj should be awakened in everyone's mind.
With a view to disseminate information about Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, administrative approval is being given to start Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Adhyasan Kendra in Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University subject to the following conditions:
(1) No new post shall be created for the said Centre.

Government Resolution No: Adhyasan-2021/ PR No. 100/ Vishy-2
2. Total amount for the establishment of this Chair is Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only).
The subsidy received from the Government will be invested in the fixed deposits of the nationalised bank and the interest earned from this amount will be used for the activities of the Foundation.
No additional funds will be provided to the University. The university should provide more funds.
5. The income of the Foundation shall be raised from the interest received on fixed deposits. This will be supplemented by revenues generated from the fees for courses of study and income generated from running training programmes and mentoring/ counselling projects.
It shall be the responsibility of the University concerned to submit the information of the expenditure incurred on the course to the Government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune from time to time.
Every 2 years, each Adhyasan Kendra will be inspected and a report in this regard will be submitted to the government by the Director of Higher Education, Pune. If the report shows negative progress of the centres in terms of quality of teaching, research performance, number of students, teaching classes conducted and training classes conducted, then the centres will be considered as centres of excellence
It will be closed and it will be mandatory for the university concerned to transfer the funds held by the Chair to the government.
The expenditure should be met from the approved provision under the head" Demand No.W-2, Principal Head- 2202- General Education, 03- University and Higher Education, 102- Assistance to Universities,- (00) (18) Development of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University (Compulsory) (22023539), 31 Subsidiary Grants."
