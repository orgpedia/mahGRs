The proposal:
Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, as a temporary arrangement, has been permitted to take on lease the 2nd, 6th, 4th and 3rd Floor and Hostel Rooms at MTNL, Powai. As per the government decision, a lease agreement has been signed between Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai and MTNL, Powai, Mumbai. As per the agreement, MTNL will acquire 26, 280.10 sq ft of 2nd and 6th floor land at Powai for Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai. ft. and 4 th floor area of about 13,150 sq. ft. of space and the 3rd floor is about 6868 sq. ft space has been provided at Rs. 240/- each. The rate is fixed. A total of 225 rooms are available for rent. 17,200 per sq. feet have been taken at this rate. At present, the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, is functioning temporarily on the 2nd, 6th and 4th floors of MTNL, Powai.
It will be possible to provide the land to the Maharashtra National Law University in Pahadi Goregaon, Mumbai, only after the Central Government decides on the CZMP. The work is being carried out by the Urban Development Department. Therefore, for continuation of the above lease agreement by renovating the above garlands in the campus of MTNL at Powai to run the affairs of the Law University, Reference No. As per the decision taken at the meeting here, the matter of forming a Rent Negotiation Committee of technical/ non-technical members to determine the tenancy was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai has been given temporary lease of 2nd, 4th and 6th Floor and Hostels at MTNL, Powai, Mumbai.
