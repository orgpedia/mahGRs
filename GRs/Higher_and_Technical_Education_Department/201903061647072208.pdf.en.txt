Introduction: Recognizing the importance of public libraries, the Government of Maharashtra has passed the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 to provide information to the people of rural and urban areas of the state on various aspects and subjects in the fields of social, economic, political, educational, information technology, agriculture, sports, art and entertainment etc. Under this Act, the work of spreading the reading/ library movement to the rural areas of the state by cultivating the reading habit among the people of the state and developing the public library system is being carried out by the government-owned public libraries. The Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 was passed on 1st May, 1968. Since the Act needed to be amended from time to time, the government was considering setting up a committee to suggest amendments to the Act.
Government Decision: A Committee is being constituted to suggest amendments in the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 as follows

The jurisdiction of the said Committee shall be the State of Maharashtra. The term of office of the Committee shall be three months from the date of its constitution.
The Chairman shall have the power to call a meeting of the Committee and to fix the date, time and place of such meeting.
The Committee shall prepare its report after thorough discussion on the amendments to be made in the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 in accordance with the changing circumstances and submit it to the Government within 3 months with its suo motu opinion.
Travel allowance, daily allowance will be admissible to the non-official members of this committee as per the prevailing rules of the Finance Department.
