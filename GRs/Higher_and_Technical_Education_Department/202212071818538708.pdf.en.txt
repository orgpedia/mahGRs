Letter from the Director, Directorate of Art No. DOO-2022/ 5/ 2319, dated 6 th December, 2022 Preamble: Grant of Certificate Courses in Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International College of Music from the academic year 2022-23. The approval has been given under the Government Resolution dated 23 rd September, 2022. The Cabinet also approved the creation of the post of" Principal" in the pay scale of 13A1 (starting salary Rs 1,31,400) for Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College.
As per the letter dated 6 th December, 2022 issued by Directorate of Arts, Mumbai, a proposal was under consideration to declare the post of" Principal" in Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International College of Music as Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The Principal of the Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College, Bharat Ratna Lata Dina is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer under the powers conferred by Rule 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. The order will be limited to financial matters.
