The proposal:
The MahaDBT portal developed by the State Government to deliver the benefits of government schemes directly to the beneficiaries has been made operational in the month of August, 2018. Since the 14 scholarship schemes being implemented by the Directorate of Higher Education in 2018-19 have been implemented online for the first time through the MahaDBT portal, the services of IT Assistants at the district and block levels were taken by the MahaIT office for the period from October 2018 to March 2019 to resolve the technical difficulties faced during the process of inviting applications for scholarships from eligible students, scrutinizing the applications from college and department level, providing allotment for payment of scholarship sanctioned students and disbursement of scholarship in Aadhaar linked bank account of eligible student through PFMS system. The matter of approving the expenses of the salary of this IT assistant was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
In order to address the technical issues faced by eligible students in accessing the benefits of 14 scholarship schemes being implemented by the Directorate of Higher Education in 2018-19 through MahaDBT portal, MahaIT has made available a monthly stipend of Rs. 52,89,533/- (Rupees Twenty Two Lakhs Ninety Nine Thousand Five Hundred Thirty Three only) is being approved to be paid out of the sanctioned amount for the year 2019-20.
