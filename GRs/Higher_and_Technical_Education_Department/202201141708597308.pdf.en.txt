The memo:
I am directed to inform the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai vide his letter No.17/ TANSIST/ YASHADA TRAINING PAYMENTS/ 2021-22/ (68 dated 9/9 2021) that the officers of Group A cadre working in the institutions/ offices coming under the jurisdiction of Directorate of Technical Education
During the financial year 2021-22, online training programmes in the form of foundation training, revision classes have been conducted by Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini, Pune in five sessions from 28/04/2021 to 09/08/2021. A grant of Rs. 34,35,000/- (Rupees Thirty-four Lakhs and Thirty-five Thousand only) is being provided to Director Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to reimburse the expenditure incurred on this training programme to Yashwantrao Chavan Development Administration Prabodhini, Pune.
The expenditure should be met from the available provision of 2021-22 under the head of Department of Higher and Technical Education, Demand No. W-6, 2251, Secretariat-Social Services 003, Training (00) (00) (01) Training to Government Employees (22510964), 50, Other expenses.
The above expenses should be made according to the prevailing procedure of the government.
