Preamble: The committee has been constituted as per the decision of the Government dated 11th July, 2023 regarding the establishment of Marathi Language University in the State. A proposal to extend the term of the committee by including expert members in the committee was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
The committee constituted on July 11, 2023, in connection with the establishment of Marathi Language University is being expanded to include the following members.

| 7   | Dr. Chaya Mahajan, Aurangabad        | the members |
------------------------------------------------------------
The Committee will consider the agenda mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 11th July, 2023 in this regard and submit a report to the Government within two months from the date of issue of the said Government Resolution with all inclusive details after consulting various experts and institutions on the matters that may be considered appropriate for the establishment of the University.
