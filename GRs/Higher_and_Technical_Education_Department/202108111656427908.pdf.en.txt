Government Decision: The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) has issued a notification regarding the same. Pursuant to the approval given vide letter dated 15/7/2021 and as proposed by the Director Technical Education, the opening of a new Government Engineering College at Ratnagiri from the academic year 2021-22 is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions as indicated in the Table:
