Government's decision
The translation, editing and publication works of the committees formed to publish the literature of great personalities like Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu are in progress. Since these books have a great demand in Maharashtra, in the country and abroad, all the unpublished literature, writings, research works related to these great men are being completed by Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jotirao Phule and others.
