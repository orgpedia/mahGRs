Preamble: The APH 2019-20 published by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi provides for merger of institutions in a locality run by the same management as per 2.10. (page 47). As proposed by the Director, G.H. Raisoni Institute of Business Management, Jalgaon has been permitted to merge the institutions of the same area running under the same management vide G.H. Raisoni Institute of Business Management, Jalgaon vide Government Letter No. TEM-2019/ Q.No. 75/1/ Tanshi-4D. Government No Objection Certificate has been issued under 20/3/2019. Accordingly, the All India Council for Technical Education vide letter dated 30/4/2019 has approved the merger of the two institutions. Accordingly, the Director, Technical Education vide letter dated 24/6/2019 has requested the Government to approve the merger of these two institutions.
