The proposal:
As per Section 72 and 74 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the government receives various statutory approvals from all non-agricultural universities in the state, as well as from Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik and Kavi Kulguru Kalidas Sanskrit Vishwavidyalaya, Ramtek. However, the subject/ subject approved by a university through a Statutory Ordinance must be the same as the subject/ subject in another university. In order to avoid disparity in similar matters between the two Universities and to ensure that the rules, provisions etc. of the other University are not affected, a Committee comprising of experienced and expert persons under the Chairmanship of Director, Higher Education, Pune has been constituted to examine/ scrutinize the Statutes and Ordinances before approving the Statutes and Ordinances. The committee was formed as per the decision of the government.
Then reference No. 2 The appointments of non-official members on the committee appointed to examine the ordinances and regulations under the government decision here have been cancelled.
However, before approving the statutes and ordinances to be received from the non-agriculture universities of the state as well as Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik, Kavikulaguru Kalidas Sanskrit University, Ramtek, the government was considering to approve the proposal by reconstituting the following committee to make recommendations to the government by examining/ scrutinizing the first statute/ order of the self-financing universities. Based on this, the following decision is taken.
The government's decision:
As per Section 72 and 74 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, a committee is being reconstituted under the chairmanship of the Director, Higher Education, Pune, consisting of the following experienced and expert persons to examine/ scrutinize the statutes and ordinances received from all non-agricultural universities of the state as well as Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik and Kavikulaguru Kalidas Sanskrit University, Ramtek before approving them and to make recommendations to the government after examining the first statute/ order of the self-financed universities.

Keeping in view the number of references received by the committee, the committee should hold meetings and give its opinion to the government on the basis of the statutes, ordinances, orders and government decisions.
The Committee shall hold regular meetings. The meeting of the committee should be conducted by the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune at the university at his level. And that university should arrange this meeting. Also, the university should pay the necessary honorarium to the members present for this meeting. Also, travel allowance to non-official members should be paid from the directorate level.
