The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
4. In order to ensure immediate implementation of the transfer order, the concerned Head of Office shall strictly follow the following instructions.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
(2) On receipt of the transfer orders, the concerned head of the office shall relieve the transferred administrative officers immediately to join the place of transfer without waiting for the appearance of an alternate administrative officer in place of the transferred administrative officers. Also, the concerned administrative officer should join the office immediately after his transfer from the current office.
3.The transferred administrative officers should be reinstated without delay after they are relieved from the office concerned and come to join. The head of the office concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-appointment of an administrative officer.

(4) No request for change in post will be entertained. If any administrative officer directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, the concerned administrative officer will be liable to take disciplinary action.
5. No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
Since these transfers are required to be effected with immediate effect, the salaries of the transferred administrative officers up to the date of retirement or maximum date i.e. 15th October, 2020, shall be drawn by the office in which they are presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the office before transfer under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the post-transfer office after the concerned administrative officer joins the transfer office. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from October 16, 2020 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the office where they were working before the transfer.
7.The responsibility of relieving the transferred administrative officers immediately will be personally borne by the head of the department concerned. The Director of Higher Education, Pune should submit the report to the Government before 16th October, 2020.
