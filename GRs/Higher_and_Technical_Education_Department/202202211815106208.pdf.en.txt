The proposal: Finance Minister of Maharashtra. In the Budget Speech made on 06.03.2020, it has been announced to provide a special grant of Rs.5 crore to Mumbai Marathi Granth Sangrahalaya, Mumbai for digitization of rare books and manuscripts in their possession.
Accordingly, the reference was made. The administrative approval has been granted vide Government Resolution dated 27.05.2021.
For this purpose, a supplementary demand of Rs.5 crore has been approved in the December, 2021 session of the Legislature. Accordingly, the Government was considering to sanction 60 per cent of the funds sanctioned through supplementary demand i.e. Rs. 3 crore for digitization of rare books and manuscripts held by the Mumbai Marathi Granth Sangrahalaya.
Government Decision: Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only) is hereby approved by the Government. For this purpose, the District Library Officer, Office of the District Library Officer, Mumbai City is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and the Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being declared as" Controlling Officer."
(2) The instructions contained in the Government Decisions/ Circulars issued by the Finance Department from time to time should be followed while making the above expenditure.
