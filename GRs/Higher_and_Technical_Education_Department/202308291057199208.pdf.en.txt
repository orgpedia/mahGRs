Preamble With a view to implement the National Education Policy-2020 in the State, a Task Force Group was constituted under the Chairmanship of Dr. Raghunath Mashelkar, former Director General, Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, New Delhi, vide Government Resolution dated 16.10.2020, to study the National Education Policy-2020 and submit a report to the State Government. The committee sent its report to the government. It was released on June 30, 2021.
The report recommends that an e-BoSF shall be set up with representation from the Chairpersons of respective Boards of Studies of all Universities regarding the design of courses for the three/ four year multidisciplinary undergraduate and postgraduate programmes. Accordingly, the government is considering the formation of a coordination committee of vice-chancellors to coordinate among universities and guide the proposed e-study platforms in order to ensure uniformity in the curriculum and ranking framework in the universities of the state.
Government Decision As per the guidelines given by the Sookanku Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 26.12.2022, instructions have been issued under the Government Decision dated 20.04.2023 and under the Government Decision dated 16.05.2023 regarding the Curriculum Framework and Ranking Framework.
In order to create a uniform curriculum in the state and to establish an e-learning platform as recommended by the Dr. Mashelkar Task Force Group for exchange of courses in the universities, the following coordination committee is being constituted.
