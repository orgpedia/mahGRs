The Proposal: -
The general policy of the government is to ensure balanced educational development in all the departments of the state.
In a Government Decision dated 17 June 1995, Zasna approved the grant-in-aid for colleges in 10 taluks which did not have any aided college. Thereafter, a permanent non-subsidized policy has been adopted in the state from the academic year 2009-2002. With the increase in the number of new taluks in the state, there was a demand for grant-in-aid in taluks that did not have any aided college or faculty. In pursuance of this demand, sanction of one college / faculty in each taluk on a 100% grant basis has been accorded vide Government Decision dated 04.02.2008. In pursuance of the above Government decision, the Cabinet of Ministers has given its approval for grant in aid to the existing Universities on grant in aid 100% dated 26th May, 2022 as per the approval given in the Annexure dated 28.4.2022.
Under the said decision, the Science Branch of the College, Adivasi Pragati Mandal, Sanchalan, Comrade Godavari Shamrao Parulekar College of Arts, Commerce and Science, Talasari, G. Palghar, received 100% grant under Taluk Only Scheme. Therefore, considering the number of students admitted in the academic year 2021-22, the issue of the government approving the recruitment of teaching and non-teaching posts in the science stream on the basis of the number of students admitted on the date 09.90.2029 was under consideration.

If the retention is held by an employee serving in a teaching and non-teaching cadre position, no new appointment will be made to the vacant position under any circumstances until the retention is terminated, and the principal and the institution will be solely responsible for making such appointments.
The recruitment should be done by strictly adhering to the qualifications, experience, professional qualifications as prescribed by the Government of Maharashtra from time to time while filling up the posts in the teaching and non-teaching cadre.
