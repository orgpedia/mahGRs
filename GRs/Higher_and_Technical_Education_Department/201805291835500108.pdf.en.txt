The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 has come into force with effect from 01.07.2006. The competent authority for transfer has been declared under Section 6 of the said Act.
The proposal for transfer of the following officers on administrative grounds was submitted to the Civil Services Board in accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and Rule 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005.
On the recommendation of the Civil Services Board, as per the approval of the competent authority, the officers mentioned in the following statement shall be transferred and posted in front of their names in column No. The post is being posted in the post shown in 4.

The officer should take charge of his new post immediately and should not go on unauthorised leave. His attention was drawn to the General Administration Department No. CDR/ 1082/2567/28/ XI, dated 30th August, 1992. Those concerned should note that if they submit any form of application for cancellation of transfer in view of the instructions given in it, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.

It should be ensured that the transferred officers join the given place of posting within the stipulated time and submit a report to the government.
