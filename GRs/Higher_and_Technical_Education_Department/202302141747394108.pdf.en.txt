The proposal:
All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. 1 here. Pursuant to the approval given on 30.12.2019, the Director, Technical Education vide the aforesaid No. 2 here. As proposed vide letter dated 17.11.2022, Shri Vasant Naik Education Society's Pratap Institute of Management and Technology, Washim was under consideration for grant of Government approval for closure of the institution with retrospective effect from the academic year 2017-18.
The government's decision:
In accordance with the approval of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government hereby grants approval for the closure of Shri Vasant Naik Education Society's Pratap Institute of Management and Technology, Washim, a professional course institute with retrospective effect from the academic year 2017-18, subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) The institution will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in the institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the concerned university.
(2) The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters relating to the alternative arrangements for teachers and non-teaching staff appointed for the relevant courses in the institution as well as judicial matters in respect of service matters and other matters.
