The proposal m. Minister of Higher and Technical Education. 07.03.2019 and D. A meeting of the All India National Education Federation was held on 17.05.2019. As decided in the meeting, a committee has been directed to consider the various demands of the teachers of non-agriculture universities and affiliated colleges and make recommendations to the government. Accordingly, the government had constituted the committee.
Government's decision
The government has constituted a committee to look into the issues raised by the teachers in the Seventh Pay Commission. The composition of the committee is as follows:
Shri Murlidhar Chandekar, Vice-Chancellor, Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University- President; Joint Secretary (VC), Department of Higher and Technical Education- Member; Director, Higher Education Maharashtra State- Member Secretary
The representatives of the following teachers' organizations shall present their views before the said Committee in a comprehensive manner and with due support.
Shekhar Chandratre- General Secretary, Education Federation of Maharashtra State, Dr. Kalpana Pandey- Member, Education Federation of Maharashtra State,
The Committee shall hold meetings at the University of Mumbai or Savitribai Phule Pune University, as the case may be, to study the above mentioned demands and make suitable recommendations after taking into consideration the prevailing laws, rules, acts and regulations and submit a detailed report within 3 months.
