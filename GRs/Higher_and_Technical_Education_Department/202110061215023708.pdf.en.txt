Introduction: The scheme is implemented with the joint participation of the contribution of Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata, functioning under the Department of Cultural Affairs, Government of India and the contribution of the State Government. Under this scheme, books, computers, books, etc. will be provided to the libraries of the state.
Funds are provided for expenditure on UPS, Xerox machines, carpets, symposiums and building construction etc.
Raja Rammohan Roy Library Foundation Scheme for the Financial Year 2021-22
A total amount of Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only) has been provided in the Budget towards State Government's contribution. From the library directorate. As per the proposal dated 17.08.2021, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the expenditure to be incurred by disbursing the budgeted funds for the Equal Fund Scheme of the Foundation.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2021-22, Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only) is hereby approved to be spent.
The Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, Maharashtra State, Nagar Bhavan, Mumbai is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to spend this fund.
