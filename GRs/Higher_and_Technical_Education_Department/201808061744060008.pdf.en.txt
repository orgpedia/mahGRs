Approval is proposed to be granted for introduction of a new Master's degree course in Master of Architecture (Environmental Architecture) at D.Y. Patil School of Architecture, Pune with an intake capacity of 20.
As per the approval given by Council of Architecture, New Delhi and as proposed by Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, the Government was considering to start a new post-graduate degree course with 20 intake capacity, Master of Architecture (Environmental Architecture) at D. Y. Patil School of Architecture, Pune.
Government Decision: In view of the approval given by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi, the Government is granting approval to start a new Post Graduate Degree Course with 20 intake capacity, Master of Architecture (Environmental Architecture) at D.Y. Patil School of Architecture, Pune, as proposed by the Director, Technical Education under the above mentioned letter, from the academic year 2018-19 subject to the following terms and conditions:

1) The grant will be non-recurring.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed by the Council of Architecture in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The eligibility criteria for the course will be as mentioned in the admission prospectus.
(3) The Institution shall enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
4) For the affiliation of the said course, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra State University Act, 1994.
(5) The institution concerned shall meet other legal requirements by depositing the requisite consolidated deposit with the Director, Technical Education in a joint account.
6) Admission to the said Degree Course shall be made as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
If the institutions charge more than the tuition fee prescribed by the Tuition Fee Committee, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
8) The institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teachers and other staff.
9) The books, periodicals required by the library in the institution must be purchased in accordance with the standards of the Council of Architecture. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students. as well
Students are provided with all educational facilities (e.g., a library) as per the standards of Council of Architecture. laboratories, libraries, classrooms, etc.
