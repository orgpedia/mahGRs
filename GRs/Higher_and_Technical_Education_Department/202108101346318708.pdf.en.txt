Preamble: The Director, Technical Education vide letter dated 28/7/2021 has proposed for grant of Government approval for closure of courses in existing Government, Government aided, University run Engineering, Management, Degree and Post Graduate institutions of the course, reduction in intake capacity of the existing course and merger of courses etc.
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, from the academic year 2021-22, the government was considering to approve the closure of courses in the existing government, government-aided, university-run engineering, management, undergraduate and postgraduate degree institutions as indicated against the names of the institutions in Annexure" A," reduction in the intake capacity of the existing courses and merger of courses, etc.
The government's decision:
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, pursuant to the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the following courses of the existing Government, Government aided, University run Engineering, Management courses in the Under Graduate and Post Graduate institutions as shown in Annexure' A' from the academic year 2021-2022, closure of courses, reduction in intake capacity of the existing courses and merger of courses, etc. are being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: TEM-2021/ PR No. 122/ TANSI-4
1) The institution will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the concerned university.
2) If there are any pending dues of the government from the institution, it will be mandatory for the head of the institution to pay it.
(3) The institution shall be obliged to provide educational facilities and make arrangements for the admission of students and alumni of the institution to sit for the examination until the completion of the course. If the institution does not provide these educational facilities, action will be taken against the institution as per the rules.
