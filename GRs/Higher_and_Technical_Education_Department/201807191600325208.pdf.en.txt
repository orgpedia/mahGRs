GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval accorded by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is granting approval for starting of institutions of Bachelor of Pharmacy and Bachelor of Architecture and Master of Management Diploma courses in the State as indicated in Annexure A subject to the following terms and conditions from the academic year 2018-19.
1) The approval will be on a non-recourse basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. For this course
Accessibility will remain as mentioned in Annexure A.
3. The institution shall have to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
For the affiliation of the said courses, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
The institution concerned will have to take affiliation of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education for diploma courses in pharmacy.
