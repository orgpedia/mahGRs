The proposal:
The Director, Technical Education vide letter dated 25/08/2020 has proposed to discontinue the PGDM course in the existing institution and introduce MBA course.
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the government was considering to approve the opening of MBA course by closing down the PGDM course in Aditya College of MBA, Beed, of Aditya Education Trust from the academic year 2020-21.
The government's decision:
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, pursuant to the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is pleased to grant approval to discontinue the PGDM course in the existing institution as indicated against the name of the institution from the academic year 2020-21 and replace it with MBA course subject to the following terms and conditions:
