As per the letter dated 15th June, 2020, a Bachelor of Fine Arts (B.F.A.) degree was awarded to Vasantdada Patil Pratishthan's College of Engineering and Visual Arts, Sion-Chunabhatti, Mumbai, a private unaided institution.
The new course, Painting, with an intake capacity of 30 students, has been approved to start from the academic year 2020-21 on a non-subsidised basis. Drawing and Painting (B.F.A.)
The facilities required to start this course have been examined by the Directorate of Arts and the proposal along with the recommendations are given in Reference No. (4). The letter was dated July 15, 2020. Accordingly, the Government was considering to grant approval to start a degree course in Drawing and Painting (B. F. A. Painting) in the College of Engineering and Visual Arts of Vasantdada Patil Pratishthan, Sion-Chunabhatti, Mumbai. The following decision has been taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide letter dated 15th June, 2020 in the aforesaid Reference No. (3), the Government has decided to grant B.F.A.Painting Degree in Engineering and Visual Arts to Vasantdada Patil Pratishthan's College of Engineering and Visual Arts, Sion-Chunabhatti, Mumbai, a private educational institution.
