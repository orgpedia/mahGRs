Assistant Inspectors who are transferred for administrative reasons will be entitled to the period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of the government.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) This order shall take effect immediately.
(2) On receipt of the order of transfer, the Assistant Inspector concerned shall immediately join the office of his post-transferred posting from the office in which he is presently working. Also, the head of the concerned office should relieve the transferred assistant inspector immediately while waiting for the appearance of the alternative assistant inspector.

The head of the office concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to forced waiting period.
(4) No request for change of posting shall be entertained.
If any attempt is made to directly or indirectly pressurize the concerned Assistant Inspector to modify the transfer order, the concerned Assistant Inspector will be liable to disciplinary action.
(5) No leave of the transferred Assistant Inspector shall be granted without the prior approval of the Administrative Department until he joins the place of transfer.
(6) Since the said transfers are required to be effected effectively and the said order comes into force with immediate effect, the date of superannuation of the transferred Assistant Inspectors shall be extended up to the date of retirement or until further orders. Salaries up to May 31, 2019 should be drawn at the establishment where they are currently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer establishment under any circumstances. The salary shall be drawn by the post transfer establishment after joining the institution of transfer of the concerned Assistant Inspector.
(7) It shall be the responsibility of the head of the office concerned personally to relieve the transferred assistant inspectors immediately. This should be followed up by the Director, Directorate of Arts. The committee will have to submit its report to the government within 15 working days.
