Preamble: The Government has issued guidelines with a view to encourage private interested institutions to engage in providing opportunities for higher education to the students of the State. The government was considering setting up a committee to scrutinise the proposals received. The following committee will be formed for the next year.

Committee's mandate
The Committee shall examine all proposals received by the Government in accordance with the guidelines prescribed by the Government.
The Committee may seek other information from the Sponsoring Board as per the provisions of the Guidelines.
(3) The Committee may suggest changes, if any, in the Detailed Project Report and the same may be considered and communicated to the Sponsoring Board for compliance within a specified period.
The term of office of the Committee shall be one year from the date of issue of the Government Resolution.
The members of the committee shall not have voting rights.
The Government shall submit its report to the Government within two months from the date of issue of the decision.
7. The Scrutiny Committee shall prepare a consolidated sheet of reports on the project report.
8. Allowance to members other than ex-officio members of the Committee shall be admissible as per the prevailing rules prescribed by the Government and shall be paid from the sanctioned grant.
