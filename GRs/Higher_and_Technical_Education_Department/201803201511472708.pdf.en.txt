The college is required to appoint faculty/ staff as per the norms prescribed by the concerned University and as per the educational qualification prescribed by the University Grants Commission and provide all other necessary infrastructure and the same shall be ensured by the Joint Director of the Department. 3 certificate from the university.

A new college recognized as per the provisions of the Legal Education Act, 2008 and the relevant rules, shall take up the process of seeking approval/ affiliation of the Bar Council of India (B.C.I.) on a case to case basis within the stipulated period.
10. 100% admission to the said Law College shall be made on the basis of merit at the institution level from the All India Quota on the basis of the marks obtained in the State Common Entrance Test.
The college must be started in the academic year for which permission has been granted. In case the college is not able to start in that academic year due to delay in getting affiliation or permission from other apex bodies/ central governing bodies, it will be mandatory to start the college in the next academic year of that academic year. Otherwise, the permission granted will be automatically cancelled.

GOVERNMENT DECISION NO: APPROVAL-2018/ (08/18)/ MASHI-4
Considering the nature, functioning, scope and noble intentions of the institution and the specific beneficiary student community (children of soldiers) of the college and their educational interests, the Government is pleased to grant 100% admission to the said Law College from All India Quota on the basis of marks obtained in the State Common Entrance Test by exempting them from the provision of Centralized Admission Procedure made under the Admission and Fee Regulation Act, 2015.
The concerned University shall not initiate the process of granting affiliation without first perusing the Government approval orders and obtaining the certificate in Condition No. 2 of the concerned Divisional Joint Director before taking further action as may be required for grant of affiliation. The universities should send the compliance report regarding affiliation to the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and the concerned Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education.
After periodically reviewing the new colleges approved by the concerned university and the departmental joint director, if 50% or less of the approved number of students are found in these colleges, the departmental joint director should take orders to cancel the recognition of these colleges with the knowledge of the government and the university.
