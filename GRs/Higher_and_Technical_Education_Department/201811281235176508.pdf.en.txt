Government Resolution: Director, Technical Education, Mumbai As per the letter dated February 15, 2018, a proposal was submitted to the Government Technological Institute, Achalpur for permission to open a new Personal Accounts (PLA) account. Accordingly, the Government is approving the opening of a new Personal Accounts (PLA) Account in the name of the Principal for Government Technological Institute, Achalpur.
The Personal Liability Account (PLA) will include the following items: :
1) Security Deposit (2) Gymkhana Fee (3) Gathering Fee (4) Training and Establishment Fee (5) Student Development Fund (6) Library Fee and Internet Fee (7) Scholarship for SC/ ST Students
The Principal Accountant General (Accounts and Accounts), Maharashtra-1, Mumbai has granted permission to open this account subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) It is necessary to conduct financial transactions from time to time in this personal loan account. Also, the concerned administrator/ office should ensure that the account is not closed.
2) The administrator/ office concerned will be responsible for the reconciliation and examination of the transactions of this accounting department as per rules.
(3) The Personal Accounts (PLA) will be regularly audited.
4) The Personal Liability Account (PLA) should be handled properly by the account administrator.
The Personal Accounts (PLA) Account should be opened by the Accountant General (Accounts and Accounts), Maharashtra II, Nagpur after authorization from the concerned Treasury.
All the provisions of the Maharashtra Treasury Act, 1968 and the Accounting Rules will be applicable to this Personal Accounts (PLA) Account.
