The proposal:
As per the decision of the Government in this regard, a merit-based special scholarship scheme has been launched to increase the number of officers from Maharashtra in the All India Services selected by the Union Public Service Commission. The scheme is to provide financial assistance to meritorious candidates of the State who have qualified the main examination of the Union Public Service Commission and have reached the interview round, but have not been finally selected in the Indian Administrative Service, through scholarships for training in reputed private training institutions in Delhi for preparing for the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) examinations.
The matter of selecting a reputed private training institute in Delhi for this scholarship was under the consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
The reference No. Shri Ram IAS, Delhi is being recognized by the Government for the calendar year 2017 and 2018 for imparting training under the Quality Based Special Scholarship Scheme implemented as per the provisions of the Government Order.
2. Refer No. 1 The criteria, qualifications, terms and conditions prescribed in the Government decision herein shall continue to apply to the merit-based special scholarship scheme.
The Director, State Institute of Administrative Vocational Education, Mumbai is being appointed as the Coordinating Officer for the effective implementation of the said scheme and is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the said scholarship scheme.
Eligible candidates can apply online through the website www. siac. org. in and contact the Director, State Institute of Administrative Vocational Education, Mumbai (Phone No. 022-22070942).
