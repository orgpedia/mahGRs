Preamble: The Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 30/10/2023 has submitted a proposal for the repair and renovation of the structure of the verandah slab, beams etc. work of the hostel building and various renovation works to provide adequate accommodation facilities to the girl students in the hostel building of Telangana Memorial Girls Hostel, Churchgate, Mumbai. The cost estimates have been submitted to the Executive Engineer, City, Public Works Department, Mumbai and the proposed work has been accorded work-wise technical consent by the Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Mumbai vide letter dated 20/09/2023. The proposal was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: For various repair and renovation works in Telangana Memorial Girls Hostel, Churchgate, Mumbai, as shown in the following statement, a total of Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimates amounting to Rs. 2,23,91, 904/- (Rupees Two Crores Twenty Three Lakhs Ninety One Thousand Nine Hundred and Four only) subject to the following conditions.

Conditions: 2.
1) The construction work should be carried out in accordance with the detailed budget, with proper planning of the incidental provisions that are assumed in the rough form during the completion of the work.
2) Before starting the work, this budget should be attested by the concerned Superintending Engineer and Chief Engineer of the Public Works Department as well as the competent officer of the Consumer Department.
3) Special care should be taken to ensure that no revised administrative approval is required for this work.
4) While carrying out the actual work, the Department of Environment should take action according to the guidelines mentioned in the Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No. 177/ No. 1, dated January 10, 2014.
5) The issues related to the said work should be dealt with in accordance with the Government Decision No. BDG-2017/ PR No. 60/ Buildings-2 of the Public Works Department, dated 07.07.2017.
6) Construction should not be undertaken without making a budgetary provision for the work.
