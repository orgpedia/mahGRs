Introduction: The" Student Accident Insurance Scheme" is an important and necessary matter from the point of view of the health and life of university and college students in the state. The Directorate of Technical Education, under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government of Maharashtra, offers all professional courses in the state of Maharashtra. 2 Amartya Education Insurance Scheme and Student Accident Insurance Scheme have been implemented.
The' Student Accident Insurance Scheme" should be implemented more efficiently and strictly in universities, colleges and other educational institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education. The government was also considering setting up a committee of experts to study the insurance scheme and make suitable changes in the scheme from time to time, with a view to formulating a new student accident insurance scheme and ensuring that the" Student Accident Insurance Scheme" or the new scheme to be introduced through this scheme in all non-agricultural universities and colleges of the state should be the same for all students. The government has formed a committee for the purpose.
The government's decision:
An expert committee is being constituted to study the" Student Accident Insurance Scheme" applicable to non-agricultural university and college students in the state and determine the new form of the scheme in accordance with the time.

The Committee will examine the following:
1) The fees currently charged under the head" Group Insurance" in the educational fees for students at the university and college level.
2) Study the current prevailing scheme. Matters presently covered under Group Insurance or Accident Insurance.
3) To study how the proposed insurance scheme will be inclusive. This plan
To study the schemes offered by the insurance company for implementation/ facilitation and prepare a plan suitable to the University. Also study the insurance policy.
4) To study the benefits of accident insurance as well as epidemic prevention insurance in the changing times.
5) Study and recommend how to keep insurance premiums to a minimum.
6) To study and recommend how the process of claim settlement can be simplified.
7) Important and other aspects to be included in the student insurance plan according to the changing times.
8) Incorporating technology-based insurance in the insurance scheme.
9) Creation of a system to ensure that the benefits of the insurance scheme reach the students.
10) Uniformity of insurance scheme in all non-agricultural universities and colleges in the state
Consider and recommend a plan.
The committee will submit its report to the government within three months.
