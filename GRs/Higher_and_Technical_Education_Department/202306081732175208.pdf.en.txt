GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the decision of the Government in question, Dr. Nipun Vinayak (B.S.) has been appointed to conduct a high level inquiry into the incident that took place on 6th June, 2023 at Savitri Devi Phule Hostel, Mumbai. A one-member committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Project Director, State Project Director (RUSA), Mumbai. The government has decided to constitute the committee. Accordingly, Dr. Nipun Vinayak (B. Project Director, National Mission on Higher Education, Mumbai. Ujjwala Chakradev, Vice-Chancellor, Shrimati Nathibai Damodar Thackersey Women's University, Mumbai will be the members.
The remaining instructions shall be as per the decision of the Government in question.
