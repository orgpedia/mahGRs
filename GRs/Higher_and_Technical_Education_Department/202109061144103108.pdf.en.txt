Preamble: Pursuant to the approval given by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi vide letter dated 23/8/2021 dated 01.07.2021 and as proposed by the Director, Technical Education vide their letter dated 26/8/2021 dated 02.07.2021, from the academic year 2021-22, PCI Index-3258-Ratnadeep College of Pharmacy (DRC) has been approved by the Council of Medical and Dental Colleges, New Delhi. The matter of government approval to start a new B. Pharmacy 100 entry capacity course in the institution of pharmacy course was under consideration of the government.
Approval of the Government is being given to start a new B.Pharmacy 100 entry capacity course in the institution of pharmacy course on permanent non-subsidized basis subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
(2) The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi in the said case as well as prescribed under the Government Order dated 21st May, 1983. The institution's intake capacity for this course will be as mentioned above.
(3) The institution shall be required to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
4) The institution concerned shall meet the other legalities by depositing the required accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education.
5) Admission to the said Degree and Post Graduate Degree Courses will be done as per the rules/ procedure of admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
