The proposal:
The Government has decided to implement the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) programme launched by the Central Government in the State to ensure access to higher education to all sections, make higher education inclusive and create high quality standards in higher education. It was approved by the government on 20 May 2015. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments. Maharashtra State Assembly. It was completed on May 9, 2018. The meeting considered the proposal of Fergusson College under the component" Creation of Universities by way of upgradation of existing autonomous colleges."
The central government's P.A.B. At the 12th meeting of 25th May, 2018, Component No. 1 Ferguson University has been approved to be established through the State Public University Act under the" Creation of Universities by way of upgradation of existing autonomous colleges." Accordingly, the proposal to convert Fergusson College, Pune into Fergusson University, Pune and the Fergusson University, Pune Ordinance, 2019 were approved. It was approved in the Cabinet meeting held on January 22, 2019.
As per the directions of the Hon'ble Minister (Higher and Technical Education), the matter of forming a committee consisting of expert persons to finalize the draft of the ordinance of Fergusson University was under the consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
A committee comprising of following experts from the field of higher education is being constituted to exchange views on finalizing the draft of the Ferguson University Ordinance 2019 for the establishment of Ferguson University.
