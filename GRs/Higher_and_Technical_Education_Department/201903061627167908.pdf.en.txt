Committee's mandate
The Committee shall examine the proposal received by the Government of Shri Balaji University, Pune as per the guidelines prescribed by the Government. The self-financing guidelines issued by the Government should be examined in accordance with the matters mentioned in paragraphs 5 and 6, 7 (3).
The Committee may request other information from the Sponsoring Board in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 7 (4) of the Guidelines.
(3) The Committee may suggest changes, if any, in the Detailed Project Report and the same may be considered and communicated to the Sponsoring Board for compliance within a specified period.
The term of office of the Committee shall be one year from the date of issue of the Government Resolution.
The members of the committee shall not have voting rights.
(6) The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within two months from the date of its meeting with the Chairperson.
The representative of KPMG should prepare the project report.
8. Allowance to members other than ex-officio members of the Committee shall be admissible as per the prevailing rules prescribed by the Government and shall be paid from the sanctioned grant.
