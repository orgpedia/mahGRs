Preamble: Reference No. Under the decision of the government here, the Tuition Fee Committee has been set up to fix the fees for pedagogy courses in unaided and permanent unaided colleges in the state. Also, a total of 14 posts have been created for the office of the committee, out of which 10 posts of clerks, bookkeepers, peons and sweepers have been created on contract basis. For the posts of clerk and scribe on contract basis, Rs. 7,000 per month and Rs. The remuneration is Rs 4,000 per month. However, in view of the fact that the remuneration payable to the employees of the said posts is subject to change from time to time, and considering the difficulties in payment of remuneration to the said posts, the contractual nature of the said 10 posts and the monthly salary fixed for the said posts are hereby cancelled and the said posts are transferred to the concerned No. 2 As per the guidelines in the circular of the Finance Department here, the matter of payment by external agency (through outsourcing) was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Having regard to the facts set out in Proposal No. 1 The Government hereby cancel the contractual nature of the 10 posts of Clerk, Short Writer, Constable and Safai Karmachari created for the office of the Education Fee Committee constituted for the determination of fees for the course of Education Science in the unaided and permanent unaided colleges in the State and cancel the monthly salary fixed for the same. 2 The government has decided to fill it according to the guidelines in the circular of the Finance Department here. Accordingly, 10 posts are in reference No. 2 in the Finance Department.
