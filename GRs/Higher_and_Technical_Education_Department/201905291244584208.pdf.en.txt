The proposal:
The Government has implemented the policy of transfer of Group-B officers and Group-C and Group-D employees through counseling under General Administration Department No. SRV-2017/ PR No. 415/ Office 12, dated 9.4.2018. Accordingly, the process of transfer of Group-B officers will be carried out. The list of vacant posts and the list of transferable officers is to be published as mentioned in the Government Decision dated 9.4.2018 of the General Administration Department. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The list of transferable officers and vacancies in Group-B cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (College Branch) under the Department of Higher and Technical Education is being attached with this government decision.

However, the transferable officers shall submit the details of the course of preference in the prescribed format as per Form No. 1 to the Director, Higher Education, Pune by 31st May, 2019. The Director, Higher Education, Pune should submit the consolidated information along with the order of preference to the Department of Higher and Technical Education on 3rd June, 2019.

Officers who do not give preference to the Director, Higher Education, Pune by 31st May, 2019 will be given posting as per the urgency to fill up the vacant posts in the administration. Also, such officers will not be able to apply for premature transfer till the completion of the tenure of the post. Also, applications submitted by such officers will not be considered.
The officials who will specify the priority will have to attach the proof of certificates and documents given by the competent authority. If the said evidence is not attached, the priority of such officers will not be taken into account.
