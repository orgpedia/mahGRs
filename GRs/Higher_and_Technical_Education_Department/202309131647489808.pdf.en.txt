The proposal:
The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (Amendment) Act, 2023 has been passed to fix the fees of the institutions imparting technical education run by the unaided private technical institutes in the state and the institutions affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education. It was published on 26.04.2023. Section 24A (1) of the said Act empowers the Government to specify the financial and other allowances, tenure and conditions of service of the Fee Fixation Committee and the Chairman and Members of the Committee for determining the actual cost of imparting technical education at the diploma level in every unaided private technical institution or institution affiliated to the Board. Further, Section 24 (2) provides that the functions, powers and functions of the Fee Regulatory Authority, the procedure to be followed by the Fee Regulatory Authority and the factors for determining the fee structure as provided in Sections 13, 14 and 15 of the Maharashtra Unaided Private Professional Educational Institutions (Regulation of Admission and Fee) Act, 2015 shall be followed, with appropriate modifications, by the Fee Fixation Committee constituted under sub-section (1) while determining the actual cost of imparting each such education.
2. In accordance with the said provisions, the Fee Fixation Committee under reference No. (4) Here. It has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 08/06/2023. As per this decision, the composition of the committee, the term of office of the chairman and members and their service conditions, the functions, powers and procedures of the committee, the processing fee, the place for the office of the committee, the salary and allowances of the committee chairman, etc. have been decided.
3. In accordance with the above decision of the Government, the applications received for the selection of the Chairman of the Fee Fixation Committee shall be scrutinized and recommended to the Government. The Chief Secretary presided over the meeting. The Selection Committee meeting was held on 02/08/2023. The appointment of the chairman and members of the committee was under the consideration of the government.

In exercise of the powers conferred by section 24 (a) of the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (Amendment) Act, 2023, the following appointments are being made to the Chairman and Members of the Fee Fixation Committee constituted to fix the tuition fees for courses offered by unaided private technical institutions affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Institutes of Pharmacy and Government recognized institutions offering short-term courses: The fees for these courses will be decided by the committee.
