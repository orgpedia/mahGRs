Introduction: Considering the industrial expansion in Kolhapur district in the fields of automobile, automation, telecommunication, power generation, etc. as well as the participation of spinning mills, sugar mills, textile mills, foundries, machine shops, etc., the feasibility of starting a new government engineering college in Kolhapur district, the necessary criteria for the government engineering college, the resources required for it, the available equipment, etc. September 11 and 12, 2022. The meeting was chaired by the Minister of Higher and Technical Education. 1 here. The directions were issued vide letter dated 21.09.2022. Accordingly, the Director, Technical Education, vide reference No. 02 here. As per the letter dated 22.11.2022, a detailed report regarding the establishment of a new Government Engineering College in Kolhapur was submitted to the Government. Accordingly, a proposal to start a new Government Engineering College in Kolhapur. It was discussed in the cabinet meeting. Accordingly, the No. 03 here. As per the decision taken in the Cabinet Meeting held on 17.03.2023, the following decisions are being taken regarding the establishment of a new Government Engineering College at Kolhapur.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, approval is being given to start a new Government Engineering College at Kolhapur in the state from the academic year 2023-24.
Approval is being given to start the following courses from the academic year 2023-24 in the new Government Engineering College in Kolhapur.
