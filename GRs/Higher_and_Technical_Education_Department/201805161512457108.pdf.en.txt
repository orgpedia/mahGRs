Government Circular: In the context of the General Administration Department. As per the Government Circular dated 15.12.2017, the performance appraisal reports of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre Officers are proposed to be recorded online in MahaPAR system from the year 2017-18.
2.D. As mentioned in Para-5 of the Government Circular dated 15.12.2017, the appointment of Primary Custodian, Additional Custodian, Office-wise Unit and Par-Manager for online recording of performance evaluation reports of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers in the subordinate offices of the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and all Government Engineering Colleges and Government Pharmacy Colleges will be done at the Government level and will be as per Annexure' A' attached.
The Additional Conservator shall appoint one or more Par Managers depending upon the number of officers in the OU to prepare performance appraisal reports of the officers in the Organization Unit (OU) under his control.
