The government order:
Maruti Mahadev Khot, Assistant Professor, Mechanical Engineering, Walchand Engineering College, Sangli. She has been selected for the course" Visiting Research Fellow in Refrigeration and Cryogenics Laboratory (Post Doctoral Program)" at IT Mumbai.
Khot is granted 12 months study leave to complete the course subject to the following conditions as per the provisions of the M.N.S. (Leave) Rules, 1981.
The period of deputation of Mr. Khot shall commence from the date he is relieved from the post.
After returning from the study leave, Mr. Khot should immediately submit to the government through the Director of Technical Education a complete report on the work done during the study leave period as well as the certificates of the passed examinations/ special courses. Also, the date of commencement and end of the course should be indicated in it along with the opinion of the officer in charge of the course.

E. Mr. Khot cannot leave India without prior permission of the Government.
