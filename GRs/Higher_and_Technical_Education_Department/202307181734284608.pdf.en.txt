2. Librarian and Physical Education Director transferred by the concerned Principal/ Director.

3. The Principal/ Director concerned shall make the transferred Librarians and Director of Physical Education join without any further delay after they are relieved from their duties in the respective college/ institution. The Principal/ Director concerned will be held responsible in case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of Librarian and Physical Education Director by the Principal/ Director concerned. Also, the concerned Librarian and Director of Physical Education will not be granted any leave during the period from the time of discharge to the time of joining the transferred place.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune should immediately submit an executive report to the government regarding this transfer order.
