Government circular
The Brihanmumbai State Government Employees' Association and the State Government Employees' Association, Maharashtra have given a memorandum regarding various pending demands of the state government employees. The strike notice has been served to the government in connection with the state-wide" indefinite strike" agitation from March 14, 2023. It is the constitutional responsibility of the government to complete its work on time and the government has an important role in this. In order to ensure smooth functioning of colleges/ universities/ technical colleges/ engineering colleges/ libraries/ arts colleges during the strike period, it is directed as follows:
All colleges/ universities/ technical colleges/ engineering colleges/ libraries/ arts colleges shall remain closed during the period of strike.
Alternate arrangements should be made in place of striking employees.
â€¢ Colleges/ Universities/ Technological/ Engineering Colleges/ Libraries/ Arts in the State
Necessary instructions should be given by holding a meeting through video conference with the principals/ registrars concerned of the colleges.
It should be ensured that the day-to-day functioning of colleges/ universities/ technical/ engineering colleges/ libraries/ arts colleges in the state is not affected during the strike.
The Head of the Department/ Head of the Office shall not grant any leave to any employee from the date of this order till the end of the strike and shall consider every case where the employee is on leave and cancel his leave and call him to work immediately or decide how.
The employees should be made aware that the state government is also following the" no work, no pay" policy of the central government regarding the strike of government employees.
The Director, Higher/ Technical/ Arts/ Libraries shall submit compliance report of all colleges/ universities/ technical/ engineering colleges/ libraries/ arts colleges functioning smoothly during the strike period to the Department of Higher and Technical Education by 01.00 pm on e-mail: do. asthahte- mh@gov. in.
