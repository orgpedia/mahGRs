Preamble: Social and Educational Activities under the National Service Scheme.
In order to involve the youth and to promote the schemes of the Government, the Government was considering the appointment of Smt. Priya Patil as the Goodwill Ambassador of the National Service Scheme for the State of Maharashtra for the academic year 2022-23 on a non-monetary basis. Following is the government's decision:
Government Decision: In order to make the social work done by Smt. Priya Patil an inspiration for the youth, Smt. Priya Patil is being appointed as a Goodwill Ambassador under the National Service Scheme, for the academic year 2022-23 on a non-honor basis.
Smt. Priya Patil will assist in organizing lectures by the Goodwill Ambassador, organizing programs of interaction with students and youth and in the program to achieve the various schemes of the Government as well as the activities and goals undertaken by the National Service Scheme as per the Government Decision dated September 26, 2017, and the responsibility will be of the concerned University/ Directorate. In consultation with Smt.Priya Patil, all the Universities/ Directorates will interact with the students of the subordinate colleges and promote the same.
The programme coordinator and the Director, National Service Scheme will be responsible for conducting the actual social reform activities. The Programme Coordinator and Director, National Service Scheme will be responsible for planning and execution of the programme. The State Liaison Officer and Officer on Special Duty, National Service Scheme, Department of Higher and Technical Education, Mantralaya Cell will act as Controllers.
