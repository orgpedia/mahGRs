The Annexure B to the Government Resolution dated 4th October, 2018 is being amended as under:
(1) In paragraph 4" Educational Qualification," in clause (b)" However, a student holding the qualification of Master's Degree of a foreign University shall be eligible to apply for Ph.D.
If the selection committee is of the opinion that the Ph.D. programme of a student applying for a foreign scholarship for a course is a special research and specialization course of changing times, the Government will consider sanctioning the scholarship in the case recommended by the selection committee. " This line is being added.
2) The words" Director, Higher Education, Pune" in para-10 (2) are being omitted.
(3) The Scrutiny Committee referred to in paragraph 15 shall consist of the Secretary, Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Pune or his representative and the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune or his representative as its members.
