Deccan College is one of the three oldest institutions in India, which came into existence on 06.10.1821 as Hindu College. The college was renamed as Deccan College on 15.11.1864. Deccan College has entered its thirtieth year. Deccan College is well known for its postgraduate studies and research in Ancient Indian History, Culture and Archaeology, Linguistics and Sanskrit Lexicology in India and abroad. Providing modern equipment to the various departments of this institution will have a positive impact on the academic, research, innovation and ranking of the university. Therefore, the University has decided to procure the equipment and machinery through the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal of the Central Government. 1 is reported here. This includes the following types of equipment: The equipment will be used for research students such as digital radiograph machine, smart tech storage and safety solution, ground penetration radar GSSI, particle size analyzer, X-ray fluorescein, etc. Accordingly, the tariff has been submitted for approval for purchase of various equipment. As per the proposal, the matter of administrative approval of the procurement process was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision:- The total estimated amount for the purchase of necessary academic and scientific equipments, various machinery etc. at the two academic departments and library of Deccan College Post Graduate and Research Institute, Pune, as proposed by the University is Rs.
