the order
The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006.
In accordance with the provisions of Section 3 (1) of the said Act, the officers of the post of Assistant Professor (Pedagogy), Group-A in Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 5 against their names with the approval of the competent authority.
2. The concerned Principal/ Director shall relieve the transferred officers immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned officers should immediately join the college/ institution where they have been transferred from the existing college/ institution.
3. The Principal/ Director concerned shall make the transferred officers join without any further delay after they are relieved from the respective college/ institution. The Principal/ Director concerned will be held responsible in case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of the officer by the Principal/ Director concerned. Also, no leave of absence will be granted during the period from the date of discharge to the date of joining the place of transfer.
The concerned head of the office shall take action to relieve the officers transferred by this order by June 15, 2023. Date of transfer of officers
