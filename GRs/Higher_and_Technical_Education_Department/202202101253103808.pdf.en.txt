Read:- Higher and Technical Education Department Government Decision No. ADR-2020/ Q. No. 90/ TANSI-6 dated January 28, 2021.
Preamble: A Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 28th January, 2021 to study and submit a report to the Government regarding the establishment of Government Master Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College with well-equipped and state-of-the-art facilities for the scientific study, development, dissemination and propagation of music. Meanwhile, a study committee constituted in this regard has recommended to rename the proposed music college as" Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College." The following decisions are being taken:
Government Decision: A Government Music College with well-equipped and state-of-the-art facilities is being set up for the scientific study, development, propagation and propagation of music. The proposed Government Music College will be named as" Bharat Ratna Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar International College of Music."
