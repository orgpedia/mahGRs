If the fees charged by the institutions concerned are more than the tuition fees prescribed by the fee regulatory authority, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
8) The institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teachers and other staff.
9) The books, periodicals required by the library in the institution must be purchased in accordance with the standards of the Council of Architecture. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students. Also students are provided with all educational facilities according to the standards of the Council of Architecture (e.g. laboratories, libraries, classrooms, etc.
10) In the context of ragging. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999, need to be strictly implemented.
11) If the institution wants to close the course, it can be closed with the approval of the competent authority in a phased manner by following the prescribed procedure. However, if all the classes of this course are closed at the same time, the accumulated deposit kept by the institute in the joint account will be deposited to the government.
12) Apart from this, other terms and conditions set by the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the concerned university will remain applicable.
If the institute has not paid the processing fee to the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, the same should be collected from the institute before final approval by the Director.
On receipt of the proposal of the Institute after completion of 3 rounds of Centralized Admission Process (CAP) for the academic year 2018-19, the admission for the academic year 2018-19 will be done under the supervision of Joint Director, Technical Education, Divisional Office, Pune till the last date 14.08.2018 as per the rules laid down at the institute level. Also, the benefit of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship, Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance and other scholarship schemes will not be paid to the students admitted at the institution level in the academic year 2018-19.
