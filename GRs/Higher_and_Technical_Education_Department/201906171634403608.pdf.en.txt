Introduction: The Department of Finance. As per the Government decision dated 14.01.2016, 25% of the sanctioned posts in Group D cadre are required to be filled up. Accordingly, the Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, Mumbai, has issued a notification to this effect. As per the letter dated 04.05.2019, it is proposed to fill up 33 vacancies in Group-D cadre in the Directorate of Libraries and its subordinate offices. Accordingly, the matter of developing 33 posts in Group-D cadre was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: 33 vacancies in Group-D cadre mentioned in the prospectus attached with the Directorate of Libraries and its subordinate offices are being filled up.
