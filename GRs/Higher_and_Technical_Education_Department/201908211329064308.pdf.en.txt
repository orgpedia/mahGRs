Introduction: The Sponsoring Board of Shri Ramdevbaba Sarvajanik Samiti, Nagpur had submitted a proposal to the Government for the establishment of a self-financed university named" Ramdevbaba University, Nagpur."
Since the land proposed by the Sponsoring Board is occupied by the government and the encroachment is regulated, respectively, while establishing the proposed self-financed university, Ma. As per the decisions of the Cabinet of Ministers held on 16th June, 2019 and 13th August, 2019, the issue of issuance of Government Resolution was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The Government has approved the establishment of a self-financed university," Ramdevbaba University, Nagpur," subject to the following terms and conditions:
1. The Sponsoring Board of" Shri Ramdevbaba Sarvajanik Samiti, Nagpur" has invited applications from eligible and interested candidates for filling numerous vacancies to the post of General Manager and Deputy General Manager through direct recruitment to be posted at Mauje Zingabai Takli, Tehsil Nagpur City, Khasra No. 11.02 acres at 292,293 and Khasra No. While setting up a self-financed university" Ramdevbaba University, Nagpur" on the 5.75-acre land at 291, 10% of the unearned amount should be deposited with the Collector, Nagpur.
The government's proprietary rights will remain intact on the 11.02-acre land leased for the proposed university and the 5.75-acre land that has been encroached upon.
3. Mauje Jhingabai Takli provided to" Shri Ramdevbaba Sarvajanik Samiti, Nagpur," Tehsil- Khasra, Nagpur City No. 11.02 acres at 292,293 and Khasra No. The terms and conditions laid down in the order of grant of 5.75-acre land at 291 and the order of regularisation of encroachment will remain in force.
Since the university will be established on the land provided by the government, the fee should be charged from the students considering the interest of the society.
This Government decision is being issued with the concurrence received under the Informal Reference No.6824 of the Department of Revenue and Forest, dated 20th July, 2019.
