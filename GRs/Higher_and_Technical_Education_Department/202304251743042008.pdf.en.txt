Introduction: Section 22 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 provides for the Director, Department of Lifelong Learning and Extension. The educational qualification and salary benefits are as recommended by the University Grants Commission and accepted by the State Government. These posts have been sanctioned by the University Grants Commission from time to time for the university and thereafter the financial responsibility of their salary has been taken over by the state government. Then the government took a decision. As on 18.11.2004, the pay scale was applied to the above mentioned posts on par with the teaching posts. The government has taken the decision. 12.08.2009 and D. As on 08.03.2019, the pay scales of the 6th and 7th Pay Commissions respectively have not been applied to the teaching posts.
The university has a department of lifelong learning and extension, which has the equivalent posts of director, assistant director and project officer. Therefore, in order to apply the pay scale to these posts as the teaching posts, as per the instructions of the Finance Department, these posts will be paid by the Pay Commission of the non-teaching posts. By the notification dated 07.10.2009, the Government vide Notification (Amendment) No. 01.08.2022 are excluded. The pay scale of the sixth and seventh pay commission should be applied to the employees working in these posts.
