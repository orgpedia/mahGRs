(1) For the construction of the building mentioned in the above table, Rs. 9515.97 lakh will be provided by the Government in a phased manner. If more funds are required for the construction, the Law University should raise funds from other sources. The State Government will not be responsible for the funds raised.
(2) The Law Universities should start innovative courses/ certificate courses in various areas of law for raising funds. Funds should be raised through CSR, donors etc.
(3) The said fund shall be utilised only for the construction of buildings approved under this Government decision.
(4) While dealing with the provision made in a lenient manner, the work should be taken up only after the approval of the detailed budgetary rates.
(5) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No. 177/ T. C. 1, d. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
(6) To comply with the provisions of the Financial Rights Manual. Also, the existing rules and conditions of the government should be taken into consideration.
(7) All the documents and accounts are to be examined by the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1 or the Accountant General-2, Nagpur, as the case may be, and if so requested, all the documents are to be submitted to him by the University for examination.
The funds sanctioned for the purpose for which the university is to be set up should be used for the same purpose. The utilization certificate should be submitted to the government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune within the prescribed time.
(8) D. All other terms and conditions of the Government Order dated 27.03.2018 shall remain unchanged.
The Executive Council shall be responsible for administering the grants sanctioned by the Government as per the Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014 by depositing them in the University Fund.
Also, the university should take action to approve the expenditure incurred from the grant by exchanging the statement of accounts of the expenditure before the General Council.
