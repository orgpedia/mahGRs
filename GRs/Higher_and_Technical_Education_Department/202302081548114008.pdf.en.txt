Government circular
Provisional Service Merit List for the post of Administrative Officer (Group-A) under Directorate of Higher Education has been prepared as on 01.01.2023 and is being published as per Annexure-A annexed to this circular. This seniority has been created as per the Government Notification dated 22.06.2021 of the General Administration Department.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1982 have been used in the preparation of this seniority list.
The Director, Higher Education, Pune is being informed to bring this seniority to the notice of all concerned administrative officers (including employees on leave/ deputation). In this regard, if the concerned Administrative Officer (Group-A) has any objection regarding the said seniority list, it should be submitted to the Director of Education, Directorate of Higher Education, Pune within 2 weeks from the date of this circular and the Director of Education should send it to this department along with the feedback on the said objection within 1 week thereafter. In case of non-receipt of objections/ information within the prescribed period, the final seniority will be published on the assumption that the details mentioned in the seniority notice are correct.
