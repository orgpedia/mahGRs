Introduction: - Reference No. As per the Government Decision dated 1 11/03/2024, 1656 full-time and 06 part-time temporary posts on establishment of Directorate of Higher Education have been approved to be continued. Reference No. The Government Decision dated 06/09/2024 of the Department of Finance herein empowers the Administrative Departments to extend the tenure of temporary posts included in their profile as well as all existing temporary posts created after earlier approval of the Review but not included in the profile from the date 01/09/2024 to the date 28/02/2025. The Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune is No. The letter dated 3rd 23/10/2024 proposes to extend the tenure of 1656 full-time B 06 part-time temporary posts at the establishment of the Directorate of Higher Education. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to allow the continuation of these temporary posts from the date 01/09/2024 to the date 28/02/2025.
