(2) The period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to the teachers transferred on administrative grounds as per the rules of the Government. As per the government rules, the teachers who have been transferred will not be entitled to the period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The following instructions shall be strictly followed by the Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution so that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.

Government Order No: Transfer-2019/ PR No.81/ Masi-1
(2) On receipt of the transfer order, the Principal/ Director concerned shall relieve the transferred faculty immediately to join the place of transfer without waiting for the replacement of the transferred faculty. Also, the concerned faculty should join the college/ institution immediately after their transfer from the existing college/ institution.
3. The transferred faculty should be reinstated without any delay after they are relieved from their duties and join the respective college/ institution. The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be held responsible in case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of a faculty member due to compulsory waiting period.
4.No request for change in post will be entertained.
If any professor directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, the concerned professor will be liable for disciplinary action.
5. No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
Since these transfers are required to be effected with immediate effect, the salary of the transferred faculty up to the date of superannuation or upto the maximum date of June 15, 2019, shall be drawn by the college/ institution where they are presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the college/ institution before transfer under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the college/ institution after the transfer of the concerned professor after joining the college/ institution. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from June 16, 2019 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the college/ institution where they were working before the transfer.
The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be personally responsible for the immediate release of the transferred faculty. The Director, Higher Education, Pune shall submit the report to the Government within 7 days from the date of this order.
