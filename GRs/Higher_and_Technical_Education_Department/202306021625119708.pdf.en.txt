Preamble The Government have approved the introduction of Paris-Sparsh Scheme for NAAC assessment of un-aided colleges in the State on the lines of PARAMARSH Scheme of University Grants Commission vide Government Resolution dated 19.04.2023. The State level committees, University level committees and district level committees should be constituted for the implementation of the scheme. Accordingly, the matter of constituting a state-level committee and prescribing the composition of university-level committees and district-level committees is under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
A State Level Committee as mentioned below is being constituted for implementation of the NAC-Sparsh Scheme for NAAC Assessment of Colleges in the State on the lines of PARAMARSH Scheme of University Grants Commission.
