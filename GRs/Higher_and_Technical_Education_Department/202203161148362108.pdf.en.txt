Preamble: A Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution dated 28th January, 2021 to study and submit a report to the Government regarding the establishment of Master Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College with well-equipped and state-of-the-art facilities for the scientific study, development, propagation and propagation of music. Further, vide Government Resolution No. ADR-2022/ No.14/ TANSI-6 dated 9.2.2022, the proposed Music College has been renamed as" Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College."
The Government of Maharashtra has decided to establish a music college in the name of Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar International College of Music. In this regard, the Cabinet in its meeting held on 9 th February, 2022 has decided as follows:
Government Decision: (1) Approval is being given to establish a Government Music College in the name of" Bharatratna Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar International College of Music" equipped with state-of-the-art facilities for the scientific study, development, promotion and propagation of music.

(2) Approval is being given for setting up of" Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Museum" comprising of Indian Folk Music, Classical Music, Musical Instruments and other items related to the life of Bharat Ratna Lata Mangeshkar under the college of" Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International College of Music."
(3) A site has been identified at Kalina Complex, Mumbai under the control of Directorate of Libraries, Government of Maharashtra for setting up of" Bharat Ratna Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar International Music College" and" Bharat Ratna Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar Museum." Necessary action in connection with the transfer of this place to the Directorate of Arts should be taken immediately in coordination with the Director, Directorate of Libraries and the Director, Directorate of Arts.
The expenditure for the construction of the college is being approved under the head Demand Number H-8, 4202 Capital Expenditure on Education, Sports, Art and Culture, 04, Art and Culture, 101- Fine Arts Education, (00) (01) Major Constructions, (Programme) 42020201. Further, the expenditure on the objectives of salary, overtime allowance, telephone, electricity and water, contractual services, travel expenses, office expenses, lease and taxes, publications etc. of the teacher/ non-teaching posts to be created in the future for the said college is being approved from the grants available under the head Demand No.W-4, 101, Fine Arts Education, (02) Government Art Institute (02) (01) Government Art Institute (Compulsory) (22050037).
