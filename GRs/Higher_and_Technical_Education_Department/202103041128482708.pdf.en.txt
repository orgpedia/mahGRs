Introduction: The review of posts in various administrative offices and government and non-government aided educational institutions functioning under the Directorate of Technical Education in the state has been taken as per the government decision mentioned in Reference No. 1. Under the decision of the Government of the Finance Department mentioned in Reference No. 2, it has been made mandatory for all the administrative departments to approve the revised figures by taking the arhtiyas of the sanctioned posts in the concerned department and the divisional offices under them.
As per the decision of the Government referred to in Reference No. 3, the administrative departments which are yet to approve the final designations shall not be allowed to create and fill up new posts. Also, until the positions have been converted

It will not be allowed to do so. As per the instructions given under this government decision, no action has been taken to fill the vacant teaching posts in government and non-government aided degree and diploma institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education. As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department referred to in Reference No. 4, guidelines have been issued for demarcation of sanctioned posts in the administrative departments and offices under them.
As per the orders issued by the Finance Department under various Government decisions mentioned at Reference No. 2 to 4, it was necessary to fix the revised figure of sanctioned posts before the creation and filling up of the posts. Accordingly, the proposal to fill up the vacancies in the institutions related to technical education in the state and to approve the creation of new posts as per the norms of the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for the year 2019-20 was placed before the 14th meeting of the sub-committee constituted on 16th July, 2019 for making recommendations after thoroughly examining the proposals for creation of new posts/ revival of posts and review of posts etc. In the meeting, it was decided as follows:" The proposal for the delineation of teaching posts in Government, Non-Government aided Engineering, Pharmacy, Vastu Shastra and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University under the Department of Higher and Technical Education was submitted for the consideration of the sub-committee. The sub-committee recommended that the proposal for fixing revised pattern, abolition of certain posts and creation of certain new posts as per AICTE norms for the year 2019 be placed before the high-level committee of secretaries. The sub-committee has also approved the filling up of vacant teaching posts out of the sanctioned posts. The posts proposed to be created can be filled only after the approval of the High-Level Committee of Secretaries. Accordingly, the proposal to create constitutional posts, degree and diploma level posts in the Government Engineering and Pharmacy Degree Colleges, Non-Government Engineering and Pharmacy Degree College, Non-Government College of Vastu Shastra and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere under the Directorate of Technical Education in the State. The recommendations were submitted in the meeting of the High Level Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary on 29.08.2019. The minutes of the meeting have been issued vide letter No. 8 and the revised format has been approved for creation of teaching posts in the institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education under the Department of Higher and Technical Education.
As per the approval given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries, orders have been issued to approve the revised review and creation of academic and constitutional posts in Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 9. Accordingly, subject-wise and designation-wise details of the posts mentioned in the review finalized as per the decision of the government in question have been submitted by the university to the government under reference number 10 and requested to issue a government decision regarding the subject-wise and designation-wise diagram.
In view of the above, the matter of approving the pattern of teaching posts at Diploma, Degree, Post Graduate level in Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University in terms of syllabus/ subject wise and designation wise and constitutional posts was under consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The teaching posts at Diploma, Degree and Post-Graduate level in Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere and the constitutional posts in the University administration are being sanctioned as per the review of the cadre-wise teaching posts sanctioned under the Government Decision mentioned at Reference No. 9 and as per the assignment mentioned in the Approval Process Handbook of the All India Council for Technical Education for the year 2019-20.
This order has been issued with the concurrence of the Finance Department vide their Informal Reference No: 456/ APUK, Dt. 13/09/2019 and No. 371/2019/ SERVICE-9, dated 16/09/2019.
