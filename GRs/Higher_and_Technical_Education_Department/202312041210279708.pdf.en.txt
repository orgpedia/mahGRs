The proposal:
The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSEB) has been constituted under the Maharashtra Act No. 2023 with a view to make fresh appointments of Government nominated members to the Board of Governors. The composition of the Governing Body has been amended as per the provisions of Article 22 (An Act to amend the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education Act, 1997).
The Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Government of Maharashtra, Shri. In pursuance of the aforesaid letter, a proposal for fresh appointment of Government nominated members to the Board of Governors of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education has been submitted to the Government for approval. In pursuance of this proposal, the matter of appointment of Government nominated members to the said Regulatory Board was under consideration of the Government.
Order: Government nominated members are being appointed to the Governing Board of the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education as follows.
