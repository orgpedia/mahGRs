Preamble: Regarding the proposal submitted by the Fee Regulatory Authority (FRA) to the Government regarding education fees due to the outbreak of COVID-19, Hon. The meeting was held on 23 rd March, 2021 under the chairmanship of Minister, Higher and Technical Education through video conferencing. In the meeting, it was discussed in the context of the proposal submitted by the Fee Regulatory Authority to the government regarding the education fees due to the outbreak of COVID-19 and after discussion, it was decided to form a committee under the chairmanship of Secretary, Fee Regulatory Authority, Mumbai.
The following decisions are taken:
The government's decision:
As per the decision taken in the above meeting, a committee is being constituted to study and recommend to the government regarding the various fees charged from the students as follows:

Against this backdrop, it is necessary to study whether or how the financial burden on students and parents regarding various fees charged from students can be reduced, and in doing so, educational institutions should not be affected too much. To recommend the fee structure (including tuition fee, examination fee, development fee and other charges, etc.) in Universities and Government/ Aided/ Unaided Institutions/ Colleges for traditional and professional courses during the period of COVID-19 outbreak.
