With a view to imparting training for competitive examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), such as UPSC, etc., thereby increasing their success rate and, in turn, providing adequate exposure to the students of Maharashtra, the Government has established State Institute of Administrative Business Education, Mumbai and Indian Administrative Service Pre-Examination Training Centre, Kolhapur, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Nashik and Amravati.
