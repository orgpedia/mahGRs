The University of Gondwana, Gadchiroli was established on 27th September, 2011 with the noble objective of educational development of tribal and poor students of Gadchiroli and Chandrapur districts.
For Gondwana University, Gadchiroli, it is 78.17 in Mauja, Adapalli, T.G. Gadchiroli. The process of acquiring private land is underway. Of this, 50.66 per cent has been acquired so far. The land has been acquired through private negotiations at a total cost of Rs. 58,08,08, 260/-. A total of Rs. 56,19,35, 502/- has been disbursed. At present, the Government has released Rs. 1,88,72, 758/- has been released by the University vide Reference No. 2 has been requested. Reference No. Under the circular, 10% of the budgeted funds have been disbursed on the BEAMS system. The Finance Department has given its approval to provide necessary funds for this work. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of difference for acquisition of private land for Gondwana University, Gadchiroli was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: For Gondwana University, Gadchiroli University, out of 78.17 ha of private land in Mauja, Adapalli, T.G. Gadchiroli, 50.66 ha of land acquired. The difference in land cost is Rs. 1,88,72, 758/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Eight Lakh Seventy Two Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty Eight only) is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:-
(1) No land shall be purchased unless provision is actually made for it.
The University should use the grant for the purpose for which it has been sanctioned and should take care to submit the Utilization Certificate to the Government immediately through the Director (Higher Education), Pune.
The progress of the land acquisition process should be informed to the government from time to time.
The Director (Higher Education), Maharashtra State, Pune shall be solely responsible for providing the said sanctioned provision to Gondwana University, Gadchiroli immediately. All the documents and accounts of the above grant are entitled to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the Accountant General-2, Nagpur, as the case may be, and if so requested, all the documents are to be submitted to him by the University for examination.
