In view of the conversion of Government Technological Institute, Yavatmal (Old) into Government Engineering College, Yavatmal, students who are eligible for admission to the second year (fourth semester) in the diploma course in the institution will be allowed to migrate to other institutions from the academic year 2019-20.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education,
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO. APPROVAL-2020/ (P.S. 27/20)/ TANSI-5
Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai - 400 032.
The date is March 09, 2020.
Read:- 1) Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter No. 10/ NGP/ Student Migration/ 2019/149, dated 17th February, 2020.
Introduction: The Director, Directorate of Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government under reference letter regarding the transfer of students who have been eligible for admission to the second year (fourth semester) of the diploma course in the Government Engineering College, Yavatmal from the academic year 2018-19 onwards to another institution. Accordingly, the matter of approving the transfer was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Government has approved the transfer of two students, Prajwal Ashok Thakare and Nilesh Ramrao Koram, who were eligible for admission to the second year (fourth semester) of the diploma course in Government Engineering College, Yavatmal, after the Government Diploma Course Institute, Government Technological College, Yavatmal (Old) was converted into a Degree Course Institute in Government Engineering College, Yavatmal from the academic year 2018-19, within the limit of admission capacity of the relevant course in other institutions as shown in the table below.
