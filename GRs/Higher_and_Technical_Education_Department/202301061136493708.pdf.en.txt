The proposal:
Under the Directorate of Libraries, there are 6 Government Divisional Libraries functioning at each District and one at each Division. Subscriber members and institution members are registered in these government libraries. Membership and deposit fees are charged for library membership. These offices have opened their own Personal Accounts Accounts (PLA) to deposit the reserve amount. After the subscriber's membership is terminated, the balance amount is refunded to the subscriber from their Personal Property Account (PLA).
The District Library Officer's Office, Mumbai Suburban, Bhandara, Kolhapur, Hingoli, Chandrapur, Jalgaon, Mumbai City, Aurangabad, Amravati, Thane, Nashik and Raigad have been closed by the respective District Treasury Offices as there has been no transaction in their Personal Property Accounts (PLA) for one year. As the offices are required to open their own Prapanji Account, the proposal received from the Directorate of Libraries was under the consideration of the government to approve the opening of the Prapanji Account of the Office of the District Library Officer, Mumbai Suburban, Bhandara, Kolhapur, Hingoli, Chandrapur, Jalgaon, Mumbai City, Aurangabad, Amravati, Thane, Nashik and Raigad.
The government has approved the opening of self-registration accounts of the office of the District Library Officer, Mumbai Suburban, Bhandara, Kolhapur, Hingoli, Chandrapur, Jalgaon, Mumbai City, Aurangabad, Amravati, Thane, Nashik and Raigad subject to the following conditions:
1) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
2) The administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3) Deposits from Library members should be credited in the account and expenditure out of the Personal Ledger Account should be limited to the refund of the deposit to concerned member after the termination of their membership 4) No Revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions shall be booked in this PLA.
5) Either on monthly or quarterly basis, balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the Administrator with the concerned Treasury officer/ sub Treasury officer.
6) All rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this PLA
