2. The following instructions shall be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is executed promptly and efficiently.

2) On receipt of transfer orders, the concerned superiors should immediately transfer from the existing institution to the institution of the next posting. 3) The transferred domiciles should be re-employed without any delay after they are relieved from their respective organization and come for re-employment.
(4) No leave of the transferred designations shall be granted without prior approval of the Administrative Department.
(5) This order shall come into force with immediate effect. The directorate should inform this department about the transferred officers being relieved of their duties and joining the place of transfer.
