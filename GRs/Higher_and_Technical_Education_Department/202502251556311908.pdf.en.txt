The Proposal: -
The Union Ministry of Human Resource Development has decided to set up an Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) in the country on a Not for Profit Public Private Partnership (N-PPP) basis to promote the Indian IT industry in the country. It provides for three partners: the Union Government (Ministry of Human Resource Development), the respective State Governments, and the entrepreneurial partners. Accordingly, the State Government has decided to set up the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT, Pune) at Pune vide Order Reference No. (1) Here. 08.12.2015à¤š are issued by order. As per the said Government Order, the capital Ws (non-recurring expenditure) for Indian Institute of Information Technology, Pune is Rs. 128 crores, out of which the Central Government - 50% (Rs. 64 crores), State Government - 35% (Rs. 45 crores), Entrepreneur Partners (Third Partner) - 15% (Rs. 199 crores) is required to be paid.
The land in Pune has been allotted for the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Pune and the Central Government has released the entire amount of their share (Rs. 64 / - crore) has been disbursed to the Institute and the State Government (Rs. 45 / - crore) out of which Rs. 26.65, and a third entrepreneurial partner's share of Rs. 6.40 crores (total Rs. 33.05 crore) has been disbursed to the institute. The utilization certificate for such funds has been submitted by the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Pune, in their letter dated December 1.
