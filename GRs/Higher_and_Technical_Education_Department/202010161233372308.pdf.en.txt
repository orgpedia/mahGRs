Introduction: Due to the success of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan and Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan, the Central Government has decided to implement the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) in the 12th and 13th Five Year Plans to strengthen the quality and foundation of higher education in the state. The National Mission on Higher Education (NMHE), a flagship programme of the Government of India, aims to ensure quality, equity and opportunity in higher education in Maharashtra. The decision was taken at the Cabinet meeting held on 21.04.2015.
In pursuance of the decision taken by the State Cabinet, a State Project Directorate has been set up for effective implementation of the scheme in the State. Under the scheme, the Centre and the state have decided to share 60:40 subsidy for non-agriculture universities and government institutions.
