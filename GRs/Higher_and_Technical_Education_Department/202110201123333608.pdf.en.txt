Preamble: In pursuance of the approval given by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi vide letter dated 11/08/2021 dated 01.07.2021 and as proposed by the Director, Technical Education vide letter dated 16/09/2021 dated 02.07.2021, the Government was considering to grant approval for enhancement of intake capacity in the undergraduate course in the existing Institute of Architecture mentioned in Annexure' A' from the academic year 2021-22.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval given by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi and as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the Government is granting approval to increase the intake capacity of B.Arch. course in Vivekananda Education Society's College of Architecture, Chembur, Mumbai as indicated in Annexure A from the academic year 2021-22, subject to the following terms and conditions, on a permanent non-subsidized basis:
Terms and Conditions: 1) The grant will be made on a non-contributory basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed by the Council of Architecture in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The institution's intake capacity for this course will be as mentioned above.
3) The institution will have to sign an agreement on the acceptance of all terms and conditions prescribed by the government and prescribed by the Council of Architecture. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.

4) The institution concerned shall meet the other legalities by depositing the required accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education.
5) Admission to the said Degree Course shall be made as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the relevant course as approved by the Government.
If the fees charged by the institutions concerned are more than the tuition fees prescribed by the fee regulatory authority, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
7) The institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the norms.
Salaries and allowances should be paid to the employees as per the rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
8) Books, periodicals required by the library in the institution must be purchased in accordance with the standards of the Council of Architecture. Also, in order to make full use of the library for the students, it is necessary to provide courses to the students in the library and all educational facilities to the students as per the standards of the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). laboratories, libraries, classrooms, etc.
9) Regarding the ragging. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999, need to be strictly implemented.
10) Apart from this, other terms and conditions set by the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and the concerned university will remain applicable.
