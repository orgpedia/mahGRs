circular
The post of Principal is a standalone post in the category of Head of Office in the Non-Government Aided Colleges under the Directorate of Higher Education. A regular principal is required to be employed for the day-to-day administrative and academic functioning of the college as well as for obtaining National Assessment and Accreditation. After considering all these aspects, the Finance Department, as per the agreement reached to fill the vacant posts as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 09.6.2017, is giving approval to fill the vacant posts of Principals in the Non-Government Aided Colleges under the Directorate of Higher Education and the posts of Principals falling vacant after the expiry of the five-year tenure of the post of Principal.
(2) It shall be ensured that while filling up the vacant posts of principals, the existing Acts in this regard, the Government decisions/ circulars, orders and instructions issued from time to time, as well as the prescribed procedures, are strictly followed.
3. This Circular is an informal reference to the Department of Finance No. Issued vide Consent received vide No.100/ 2018 dated 15.03.2018.
