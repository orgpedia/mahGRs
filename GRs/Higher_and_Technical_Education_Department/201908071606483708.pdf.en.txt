No. 20/2016/ APUC, D. 29th June, 2017 Preamble: Restrictions have been imposed on creation of new posts and recruitment till final approval of the modalities as per the Government Resolution, dated 25th May, 2017, in the Finance Division, Reference No.
Further, with regard to the matters to be considered for fixing the revised figure, vide Reference No. 3 Guidelines have been issued by the government circular here. Accordingly, the process of revising the figure of teaching posts in the agricultural university under the control of the department is underway. As the revised pattern is yet to be finalised and there is a restriction on fresh recruitment since 2017, vacancies arising out of retirement and other reasons could not be filled up during this period.
Considering the academic interest of the students, the reference No. 2 of the Subcommittee constituted by the Government here. In the meeting held on 16.07.2019, the Sub-Committee has approved the filling up of posts in 15 non-agriculture and deemed universities under the Department within the limit of 80% of the sanctioned posts. As per the approval given by the said sub-committee, the matter of government approval to fill the vacant posts of teachers and teacher equivalents in non-agriculture universities and deemed universities in the state under the department was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, the Government is permitting 15 non-agriculture Universities under the Department to recruit up to 80% of the total sanctioned posts for Government recognized Deemed Universities. The details are given in Annexure-1.
2.The above appointment will be subject to the following conditions.
(i) Non-agricultural Universities shall, while filling up the vacancies, give priority to filling up the posts in the same academic department as are required to be filled up.
(ii) The maximum number of posts of Assistant Professor should be filled up from the posts approved for recruitment.
(iii) Non-agricultural Universities shall ensure that the posts are filled up to the extent of 80% of the total sanctioned posts for the University.
