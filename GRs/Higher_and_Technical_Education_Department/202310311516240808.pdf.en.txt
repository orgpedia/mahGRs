Gokhale Institute of Political Science and Economics (Deemed to be University), Pune, established in 1930, is one of India's leading institutions for teaching and research in economics. The University is committed to provide quality teaching and research facilities to the students and faculty since its inception. The institute is a premier centre of excellence for research and teaching in the field of economics, politics and social sciences. The building is old and needs to be renovated. Also, it is difficult to accommodate the increasing number of students as the institute has limited space available.
Therefore, considering the increasing number of students, it is proposed to construct additional floors on the working buildings. Accordingly, the budget of the said work has been prepared by the Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Pune, with technical consent in terms of Reference No. 1 has been submitted to the Government as per the letter here.
The cost of repair and renovation works is Rs. 15 crore, as the No. The matter of giving administrative approval to the renovation work was under the consideration of the government as per the authority of the administrative department under the government decision at 2 and 3.
