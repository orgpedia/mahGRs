The Junior Advocate who is transferred on administrative grounds will be entitled to the period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of the Government.
For immediate implementation of the transfer order, the concerned principals should strictly follow the following instructions.
(1) On receipt of the transfer order, the transferred Junior Advocate shall be relieved immediately to join the place of transfer without waiting for the replacement of the transferred Junior Advocate by an alternate Junior Advocate.
No request for change in the post will be entertained. Any attempt by a Junior Advocate, directly or indirectly, to press for modification of the transfer order shall entitle such Junior Advocate (Group-B) to disciplinary action.
3) No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining the transferred post.
Since these transfers are required to be effected effectively, the salaries of all the transferred Junior Advocates up to the date of their superannuation or up to the maximum date of 30.06.2018 shall be drawn by the colleges where they are presently working. Subsequent salary shall not be deducted in any case in the pre-transfer college, the salary shall be deducted by the post-transfer college after the concerned Junior Resident joins the transfer college. If the salary from 01.07.2018 onwards is withdrawn from the college functioning before the transfer, the Collection and Disbursement Officer of the college will be held personally liable.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The Director of Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune shall submit a report to the Government before 01.07.2018 regarding the release of the transferred Junior Resident and joining the place of transfer.
