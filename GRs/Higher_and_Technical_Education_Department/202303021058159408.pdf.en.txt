Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur University was established on 4th August 1923. The campus of the university is spread over 373 acres. Renowned as the oldest university in Central India, the university is entering its centenary year in August, 2022. The academic year 2022 will be celebrated as the centenary year. The University has designed policies as per the need, aligning with global challenges and opportunities. The policy envisages implementation of new projects to mark the centenary year of the university with a view to empowering, skilling, conscientizing students and providing new employment opportunities to the youth. Accordingly, the University has sanctioned Rs. 107.07 crore has been proposed.
