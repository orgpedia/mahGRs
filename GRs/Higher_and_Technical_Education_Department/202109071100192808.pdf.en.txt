Introduction: Due to economic conditions, meritorious children of the open category are deprived of higher education in reputed educational universities abroad. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance and the Department of Tribal Development have been providing scholarships to SC/ ST students for higher studies abroad. The scheme was under consideration of the government to be implemented in the academic year 2021-22 with some modifications.
Government Decision: The Government has decided to increase the annual income limit of the student and his/ her parents to Rs. 8.00 lakh from the academic year 2021-22 for the benefit of the scheme" Grant of Scholarship to Meritorious Children for Higher Education Abroad."
