(a) Other conditions of the office of the Presiding Officer.
(1) The salary of a person appointed to the office of the Presiding Officer shall be the same as that of a Judge of a High Court. However, if a retired High Court Judge is appointed to this post or a retired person who is qualified to be appointed as a High Court Judge, his salary shall be determined in accordance with the provisions contained in Rule 157 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Service Pension) Rules, 1982.
(2) The Presiding Officers shall be governed by the High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954 (Act No. Hospitality allowance, travelling allowance and daily allowance and other service concessions as per the High Court Judges (Travel Allowance) Rules, 1956 shall continue to be admissible.
