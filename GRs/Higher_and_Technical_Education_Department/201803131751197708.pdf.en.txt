The proposal:
Vice-Chancellor, Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik As per the letter dated March 31, 2017, Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane has been given the letter of intent to start some courses. As per the provisions of the said Letter of Intent, three Diploma level courses namely 1. Diploma in Essential Skills, 2. Diploma in Business Skills and 3. Diploma in Industrial Skills run by Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University in the state will be implemented under the initiative" Learn and Earn" in collaboration with Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane.
Under the Skill India Mission of the Central Government, new skill training courses are being started to create more and more skilled workers. Participation of students already working in the commercial, retail, industrial, etc. sectors or trainees who want to be absorbed in the industrial sector.

The' Learn and Earn' scheme will be implemented in collaboration with Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University and Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane. Under this scheme, Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane will sign Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with some prominent industries. Through this, diploma level courses will be imparted to poor students across the state by providing on-the-job training while they are studying. Scholarships will also be provided during this period. The poor students will get free basic technical education, skill education, accounting education, etc. and students will also get scholarships. The entire cost incurred under this initiative will be borne by the entrepreneurs as consumers.
So, there will be no financial burden on the government.
On the same lines, a proposal to provide government support to the' Learn and Earn' scheme jointly run by Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik, and Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane, was under consideration of the government. After considering the proposal, the government has taken the following steps:
The government's decision:
Under the" Learn and Earn" initiative between Yashwantrao Chavan Open University and Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane, the Government is approving the Letter of Intent given by the University to the Institute to run three courses for diploma level training: 1. Diploma in Essential Skills, 2. Diploma in Business Skills, and 3. Diploma in Industrial Skills.
This Government approval is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions: 1) On-the-job training is provided to all students participating under the" Learn and Earn" initiative in industry and business. After completion of the course, he will be awarded a diploma certificate from Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University. Taking this into consideration, trainees should be called as Apprentices during the course of the course subject to the condition of strict compliance with the provision in the" Apprenticeship Act, 1961."

The University shall ensure that the period of training of the said trainees shall not exceed 2 years. Also, if the students are willing to pay more than the minimum apprenticeship or entrepreneurial remuneration permissible under the provisions of the Apprenticeship Act, 1961, the remuneration shall be paid by the respective entrepreneurs.
The cost of equipment, textbooks and materials etc. for the students and all other expenses on training will be borne by the entrepreneurial establishment or Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane, in whose factory the students are undergoing training.
4) All participating students should be insured and necessary measures should be taken regarding their safety and medical care.
5) The decision to participate in the scheme will be taken by other institutions with the consent of the university considering the facilities available. For this, the institutions should sign a separate agreement with Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane, as per the requirement.
6) Guarantee of good conduct should be taken from the office bearers of this innovative Institute of Technology and Management, Thane. Also, the university should ensure that the quality and standard of these courses is maintained and regulated by the Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane. The Government shall have the power to cancel the recognition granted to these courses on the ground of malpractice, non-maintenance of standards and such other valid reasons.
These facilities shall be limited to the period of approval granted by Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik to Abhinav Institute of Technology and Management, Thane for running the course.
8) In case of any complication/ court case arising in future in connection with the recognition/ functioning of these courses, the entire responsibility will be of Yashwantrao Chavan Open University.
9) New terms and conditions will be imposed by the government as and when required.
10) The institution shall be required to submit to the University and the Government a bond confirming acceptance of all the above terms and conditions.
