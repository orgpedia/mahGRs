The proposal:
The Government of Maharashtra has decided to file a writ petition in the Bombay High Court to apply the benefits available to teachers/ non-teaching staff in non-government aided schools under the control of the School Education Department as per various 23 Government decisions. 2817/2014 and the High Court, Bench at Aurangabad in Petition No. 3872/2005 was filed. Accepting the demands of the petitioners, the said petitions were disposed of by the Hon'ble Supreme Court vide its orders dated 5th October, 2017 and 26th February, 2018 respectively. The government was considering to implement the orders of the Supreme Court regarding cash payment of earned leave to eligible teaching/ non-teaching staff in non-government aided arts institutions, maternity leave to women employees, leave allowance to paralysed employees. This was discussed in the State Cabinet meeting held on 31st March, 2022 and the following decisions have been taken:
Government Decision: The following benefits are being extended to the teaching/ non-teaching staff of Non-Government Aided Arts Institutions with effect from the date of this Government Decision.
1) Cash payment of earned leave: Government Decision No. 2401/7/ Service-9 of the Department of Finance. Exemption of repatriation of accrued leave to Government employees as on 15th January, 2001. It has been closed since February 1, 2001. Application- 2401/8/ Service-9, Dy. The limit for cash withdrawal of accrued leave for government employees has been increased to 300 days with effect from January 15, 2001.
Further, vide Government Resolution No. 2009/ No.21/ Service-6, D. 27th August, 2009 and Government Supplementary Letter No. Cash payment of leave accrued as on 10th November, 2009 has been approved under the revised pay structure. the Finance Department

The provisions of the aforesaid Government Decision, along with the future amendments to be made by the Finance Department, are being made applicable to the non-teaching staff in the Non-Government Aided Arts Institutions from the date of this Government Decision.
2) Maternity leave to women teachers/ non-teaching staff: Government Resolution No. 1409/ No. 8/09/ Service-6 of Finance Department. As of August 24, 2009, the maternity leave limit for women employees of the state government has been increased to 180 days. The provisions of the said Government Decision are being made applicable to women teaching/ non-teaching staff of Non-Government Aided Arts Institutions from the date of this Government Decision along with the amendments to be made by the Finance Department in future.
3. Leave Relaxation to Cancer/ Paralytic Staff: The provisions of Rule 79, Schedule III of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, for cancer/ paralytic teaching/ non-teaching staff in non-government aided arts institutions are being implemented from the date of this Government decision.
