Three separate committees have been constituted under the Government Order No. 1 in the above context to publish the literature of Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu. Under the Government Resolution No. 2, it has been approved to search the literature of these great men, to make tours in the state/ outside the state if necessary, to examine the literature, to carry out detailed and research process of the literature, to print and reprint the literature and to bring out the edition. In accordance with the Government Resolution No. 3, The date has been extended upto 23.11.2016.
Proposal for further extension The Prime Minister agreed. The following volumes of Dr. B. R. Ambedkar published under the 4th Letter were printed at a total cost of Rs. 1,19,024/ (Rupees One Lakh Nineteen Thousand Twenty Four only) has been certified as yet unpaid and requested to approve the said expenditure. The government had agreed to bear the cost.
Memorandum:- It is informed to the Sub-Collector and Accountant that the expenditure incurred for printing the following books of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar is Rs. 1,19,024/- (Rupees One Lakh Nineteen Thousand Twenty Four only) is being approved by the Government.
