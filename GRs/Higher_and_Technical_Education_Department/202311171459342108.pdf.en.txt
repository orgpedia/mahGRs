Read- Government Circular, Finance Department, Number: Salary 2023/ Q. No.13/ Service-3, D. 28.06.2023.
Preamble- The aforesaid Government Circular of the Finance Department has issued guidelines for fixation of retirement pay to the State Government employees who have retired/ would retire on 30th June, taking into account the notional increment of 1st July. The matter of applying this instruction to teachers and non-teaching staff in aided educational institutions under the control of the Higher and Technical Education Department was under the consideration of the government, in which the following decision has been taken.
Babasaheb Ambedkar University of Technical Education- Lonere, COEP University of Technology, Aided Art Institute, I. C. T.
The university, LIT and LIT University of Innovative Technologies are being implemented for teaching and non-teaching staff. Be sure to follow these instructions exactly.
This Government Circular is being issued pursuant to the approval received under the Finance Department's Informal Reference No. 430/23/ Service-3 dated 9th November, 2023. Government of Maharashtra
