Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur University was established on 4th August 1923. The campus of the university is spread over 373 acres. Renowned as the oldest university in Central India, the university has entered its centenary year. The academic year 2022-23 will be celebrated as the centenary year. On this occasion, the University has started to build some new projects by taking into account the material needs and future development prospects of the population in the university's periphery, along with the university's adopted knowledge generation and skill development.
The University has designed policies as per the need, aligning with global challenges and opportunities. Accordingly, the University has proposed to set up a world-class inter-sports complex to enhance the participation of the students of the University in national and international sports competitions and also to benefit the schools and other institutions and individuals. It will have facilities for basketball, badminton, archery, boxing, gymnastics, kabaddi and Rs. 5581.82 lakh in Reference No. 1 has been requested.
