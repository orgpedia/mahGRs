Accordingly, the Member Secretary of the Task Force Group and Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has requested for an extension of 3 months beyond the original deadline for submission of the final report. 2 letter to the government. Accordingly, the matter of extending the tenure of this Task Force Group was under consideration of the Government.
