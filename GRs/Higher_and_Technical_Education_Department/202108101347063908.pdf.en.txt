Preamble: In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, the Director, Technical Education vide letter dated 26/07/2021 has proposed to change the name of the Institute/ College of Engineering, HMCT, Management Science, MCA course on existing permanent non-aided basis with effect from the academic year 2021-22 and to change the name of the course currently running in the existing institution.
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, from the academic year 2021-22, the Government was considering to change the name of the existing permanent unaided institutions/ colleges of Engineering, HMCT, Management Science, MCA course as indicated against the names of the institutions in Annexure A and Annexure-B and to approve the change in the name of the existing course in the existing institution.
The government's decision:
In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is approving the change in the name of the Institute/ College of the existing permanent unaided course in Engineering, HMCT, Management Science, MCA and the name of the course currently being offered in the existing institution as indicated in Annexure-A and Annexure-B respectively, with effect from the academic year 2021-22, as proposed by the Director, Technical Education.
