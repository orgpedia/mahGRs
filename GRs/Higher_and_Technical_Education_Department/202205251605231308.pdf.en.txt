The matter of giving administrative approval to the budget of Rs. 74,90,750/- (Rupees Four Seventy Seven Lakh Ninety Nine Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak Memorial Centenary initiative, the Government Divisional Library, Ratnagiri, will be modernized and digitized. Administrative Sanction is being granted as per Form A along with budget of Rs. 74,90,750/ (Rupees Seventy Four Lakh Ninety Seven Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty only) subject to the following conditions:
1) Ensure that no expenditure is incurred beyond the administrative sanction for the actual work.
2) Proceed according to the rules of the government for matters related to the purchase of the proposal.
2. Cost of the work. W-4, 2205, Art and Culture, 105, Public Libraries, Schemes under Five Year Plans, (03) (08) Computerization of the offices of the Directorate of Libraries and Assistant Director of Libraries (2205 3054).
The said Administrative Sanction was issued vide Government Resolution, Finance Division, No. 2013/ No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part-II, D. 17.04.2015 Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-III, No. 4, Paragraph No. 27 (2) (a) as per the powers conferred on the administrative department.
