Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government Decision No.-DBA-2008/ (86/08)/ Coordination, Dy. 30.09.2008 2) Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government Decision, No. Committee-1111/ (91/16)/ Coordination, Dy. 27.01.2020 Preamble: Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj has contributed to the ideological, social, political and educational fabric of Maharashtra. Even today, a sample of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj's work and political and social experiments can be useful. The Government of Maharashtra should take up a special action-project to raise the identity of this great leader, who inspired the state's progress, for this purpose, the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti has been formed. The decision was taken by the government. The term of the Committee ended on 27.01.2020. In the coming days, the government was considering to reconstitute the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti to collect, edit and publish other literature based on the life of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj.
The Government have approved the reconstitution of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti, Mumbai for publishing the books of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj.

The tenure of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti will initially be for three years.
The objectives of the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti and the functions of the Member Secretary shall be as follows:
(a) To publish Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj's complete poetry and publish it in Hindi, English etc.
(b) To write and publish an up-to-date biography of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj in Hindi, English etc.
a) To publish literature on the life and works of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj.
d) To collect and edit the unpublished literature of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj.
e) To research the published literature of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj.
