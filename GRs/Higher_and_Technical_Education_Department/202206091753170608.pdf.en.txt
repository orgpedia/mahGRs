As per the provisions of Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, the General Administration Department has laid down a consolidated procedure for reviewing the services of officers/ employees at the age of 50/ 55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. As per the provisions of the said Government decision, the Government was contemplating to constitute a" Divisional Grievance Committee" to consider the grievances of the concerned after reviewing the services of Group-A and Group-B officers of the Directorate of Technical Education (itself) and the institutions/ colleges/ offices under them at the age of 50/ 55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: In accordance with the provisions contained in the Government Order dated 10th June, 2019 issued in the context of General Administration Department, the Department of Technical Education (DTE) is constituting a" Departmental Grievance Committee" to consider the grievances of the officers of Group A and Group B cadre of Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) and institutions/ colleges/ offices under them who have been declared ineligible for continuation of their services beyond the age of 50/ 55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, after reviewing their services on the basis of the criteria prescribed for eligibility to continue their services:

4. Functions of the Departmental Complaints Committee:
(a) To consider the representation of the officers/ employees who have been served notices and who are undisputedly disqualified from continuing their services after review, the record of the confidential reports of the said officers/ employees shall be taken for consideration by the Grievance Committees.
(b) The recommendations of the Departmental Grievance Committee shall be submitted to the appropriate authorities as mentioned in Annexure-D to the Government Resolution dated 10.6.2019 in the context of General Administration Department for final decision.
5.The proceedings in relation to the said petition shall be carried out in accordance with the procedures and instructions set forth in the Government Decision dated June 10, 2019 in the context of the General Administration Department.
