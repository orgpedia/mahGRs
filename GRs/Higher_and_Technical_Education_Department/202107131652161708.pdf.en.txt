Introduction: In the above reference No. 1 Periodic transfers of Principals and Heads of Departments in Government Technical Institutions have been made in the year 2020. However, the matter of cancellation of transfer of Shri Sunil Dutt Shamrao Prabhune, Head of Department, Nuclear Engineering, who was transferred, was under consideration of the Government as there was no vacancy available to fill up his place of transfer.
Government Decision: 01 As per the decision of the Government here, Mr. Sunil Dutt Shamrao Prabhune, Head of Department, Nuclear Engineering, Government Technological University, Jintur was transferred to Government Technological University, Pune. As there is no vacancy available to join the place of transfer, his transfer to Government Technician, Pune is being cancelled on administrative grounds with the approval of the competent authority.
Mr. Prabhune, Head of Department, Nuclear Engineering should immediately join the institution before his transfer i.e. Government Technological Institute, Jintur.
The said Head of Department is being sanctioned as compulsory waiting period under Rule 14 (f) (1) of Maharashtra Civil Services Rules, 1981 (General Conditions of Service) Rule 09 from the date of discharge from the parent organization before transfer till the date of joining the place of posting after cancellation of transfer by this order.
In order to implement this order immediately and effectively, the following instructions should be strictly followed by the concerned.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
2) The concerned Principal/ Head of the institution should make Mr. Prabhune join without delay. The Principal of the institution concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment.
3) No leave of the concerned should be sanctioned without prior approval of the administrative department.
(4) The Director, Directorate of Technical Education shall submit the compliance report to this Department within 15 days from the date of this order without fail.
