The Government Order:
Muralidhar Prabhakar Wadekar, Associate Professor (Chemistry), Government Vidarbha Knowledge Science Institute, Amravati was caught red-handed by the Anti-Corruption Bureau, Amravati on 30.06.2022 while accepting a bribe and a case No. 735/2022 has been registered under Section 7 of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 at Gadgenagar Police Station Amravati. In pursuance of the Government Order at Reference No. 1, Dr. Muralidhar Prabhakar Wadekar has been placed under suspension with effect from 30.06.2022, the date of his detention, in accordance with the provisions of Potnium (2) of Rule 4 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979.
As per the provisions laid down in the Government Resolution of the General Administration Department at Reference No. 2 and 3, the Suspension Review Committee in its meeting held on 09.01.2023, terminated the suspension of Dr. Wadekar and reinstated him in service. However, it has been recommended that they should be given postings outside their parent department and in non-executive positions. Accordingly, Dr. Wadekar's suspension has been terminated and he is being reinstated in Government service and posted as Associate Professor, Government Institute of Science, Nagpur.
The proceedings of the case under the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 and the departmental inquiry against Dr. Wadekar will continue even after his reinstatement. Dr. Wadekar should immediately join the place of above posting i.e. Government Institute of Science, Nagpur. The suspension of Dr. Wadekar will be effective from the date of his joining.
