Preamble: The Director, Technical Education vide letter dated 28/7/2021 has proposed for grant of Government approval for closure of existing permanent unaided undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Engineering, HMCT, Management, MCA courses, reduction in intake capacity of existing courses and merger of courses etc.
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the Government was considering to grant approval for closure of courses in the existing permanent unaided Engineering, HMCT, Management, MCA courses in undergraduate and postgraduate institutions as indicated against the names of the institutions in Annexure' A' from the academic year 2021-22, reduction in the intake capacity of the existing courses and merger of courses etc.
Government's decision
In pursuance of the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is granting approval for closure of courses in existing permanent unaided undergraduate and postgraduate institutions of Engineering, HMCT, Management Science, MCA courses as indicated against the names of the institutions in Annexure A from the academic year 2021-2022 as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, subject to the following terms and conditions:

Government Resolution No: TEM-2021/ PR No. 125/ TANSI-4
1) The institution will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the concerned university.
(2) The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters which are sub judice in respect of service and other matters of the faculty/ staff appointed for the concerned course. In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.
3) If there are any pending dues of the government from the institution, it will be mandatory for the head of the institution to pay it.
(4) It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay the loans taken by it from the Government or any other financial institution in respect of the immovable property required for running the course in the Institute.
5) While closing the courses in the institution in a phased manner, it will be mandatory for the institution to provide educational facilities to the admitted students and alumni of the institution till the completion of the course and to make arrangements for them to sit for the examination. If the institution does not provide these educational facilities, action will be taken against the institution as per the rules and the accumulated deposit with the government will be deposited by the government.
(6) It shall be obligatory on the part of the institution to pay the salary, salary compensation and other financial allowances payable as per rules to the teachers and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
The institutions which have not paid the processing fee to the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai, shall recover the same from the institutions before the final approval of the Director and necessary orders shall be issued by the Director, Technical Education within the next 15 days in accordance with the approval of this Government.
