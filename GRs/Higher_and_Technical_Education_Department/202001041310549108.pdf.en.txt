Letter No.19/ TANSIS/ Foreign Scholarship/ 2019/172 dated 29th July, 2019 from the Director, Technical Education
Preamble: As per the Government Decision, Higher and Technical Education Department No. 2018/ PR No. 218/ TANSI-4, dated 4th October, 2018, the scheme" Scholarship for meritorious boys and girls for higher education abroad" has been implemented from the academic year 2018-19 for the students who have taken admission in Master's Degree/ Master's Diploma and Ph.D. courses in the universities within THE/ QS Ranking 200 abroad. The selection committee, headed by Secretary, Higher and Technical Education, had recommended a total of 13 candidates for foreign scholarships, 3 for PhD courses and 10 for post-graduation/ post-graduation diploma courses. Accordingly, Government vide its decision dated 28th May, 2019 had approved grant of foreign scholarship to a total of 13 candidates for the academic year 2018-19. However, in view of non-compliance of various provisions of the Government Decision dated 4th October, 2018, the matter of cancellation of selection of 6 candidates for scholarship was under consideration of the Government as per the recommendation of the Selection Committee headed by Secretary, Higher and Technical Education.
