Three separate committees have been constituted under the Government Order No. 1 of the said Act to publish the literature of Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu. Under the Government Resolution No. 2, it has been approved to search the literature of these great men, to make tours in the state/ outside the state if necessary, to examine the literature, to carry out detailed and research process of the literature, to print and reprint the literature and to bring out the edition.
In accordance with the Government Resolution No. 3, The deadline has been extended till 01.11.2021. Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide Reference No.
The next volume of Mahatma Jotirao Phule and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar published under the letter of 4 was printed and constructed at a total cost of Rs. 28,45,401/ (Rupees Twenty Eight Lakh Forty Five Thousand Four Hundred and One only) has been submitted for approval. It has also been requested to certify that the payment has not yet been made and approve the expenditure. Accordingly, the matter of approving the expenditure was under the consideration of the government.
