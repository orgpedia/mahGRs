Preamble: The Director, Technical Education vide letter dated 23/7/2021 has proposed to the Government to approve closure/ reduction in the intake capacity of the courses in the existing Degree/ Post Graduate Institute of Pharmacy.
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the matter of approval of reduction/ closure of existing intake/ courses as indicated against the names of institutions in Annexure" A" with effect from the academic year 2021-22 was under consideration of the Government.
Decision of the Government: In pursuance of the approval given by Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi, as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the following conditions have been prescribed for phasing out/ reduction in the course intake in the existing Degree/ Post Graduate Institute of Pharmacy as indicated against the name of the institutions in Annexure' A' with effect from the academic year 2021-2022.
Government approval is being given subject to the condition:
Terms and Conditions:
1) The institution will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the concerned university.
(2) The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters which are sub judice in respect of service and other matters of the faculty/ staff appointed for the concerned course. In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.
3) If there are any pending dues of the government from the institution, it will be mandatory for the head of the institution to pay it.
(4) It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay the loans taken by it from the Government or any other financial institution in respect of the immovable property required for running the course in the Institute.

5) While closing the courses in the institution in a phased manner, it will be mandatory for the institution to provide educational facilities to the admitted students and alumni of the institution till the completion of the course and to make arrangements for them to sit for the examination. If the institution does not provide these educational facilities, action will be taken against the institution as per the rules and the accumulated deposit with the government will be deposited by the government.
(6) It shall be obligatory on the part of the institution to pay the salary, salary compensation and other financial allowances payable as per rules to the teachers and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
The Director shall recover the processing fee from the institutions which have not paid the same to the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra before giving final approval to the same and the Director, Technical Education shall issue necessary orders within the next 15 days for the inclusion of the course as per the approval of this Government in the Centralized Admission Process.
