The contribution of social activists like Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj, Mahatma Jotiba Phule and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar in the ideological, social, political and educational formation of Maharashtra is important. The Government of Maharashtra has decided to set up Biographies of these great men with the aim of undertaking special action projects to awaken the identity of these great men who have inspired the progressive fabric of the state.
