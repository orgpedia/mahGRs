As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure teaching/ non-teaching posts in non-government aided colleges for persons with disabilities. For this, the notification of the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India at Reference No. 2 has been published on its website prescribing the list of posts to be ensured for Divyangjan. It is stated in the order that the duties and responsibilities in the list published by the Central Government in respect of the posts in Group-A to Group-D for the disabled in the state government establishment, the nature of work and the prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the government service (including posts in subordinate offices), although the designations of such posts are different. As the same is the case, the policy for ensuring disability as mentioned in the statement (Returns A and B) attached with the Government decision at Reference No. 4 and 5 shall continue to be applicable to the teachers/ non-teaching staff of the Non-Government Aided Colleges.
