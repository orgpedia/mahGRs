Preamble: An Act to provide for the reservation of seats for admission to educational institutions in the State and for the reservation of appointments and posts in the public services under the control of the State and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto for the advancement of the Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) in the State of Maharashtra. It was published on November 30, 2018. Issue No. Under Section 4 (1) (b), 16% of the total number of appointments to the civil services and posts under the control of the State have been reserved for the Socially and Educationally Backward (SEBC) category of citizens, including the Maratha community.
Orders have also been issued regarding the reservation of 10% seats for admission in government services and educational institutions for economically weaker sections in the open category in the state of Maharashtra. Accordingly, the concerned administrative departments have been directed to issue immediate orders. Accordingly, the No. 5 Instructions have been issued regarding admission to non-agricultural universities and educational institutions under the purview of the Department of Public Administration.

Government Circular No: Parichay-2019/ PR No.73/ Vishy-1
The General Administration Department was under the consideration of the Government to issue directions to all non-agricultural universities under this Department to reserve 16% seats for Socially and Educationally Backward (SEBC) category including Maratha community and 10% seats for economically weaker sections in the open category for appointment in educational institutions in the State of Maharashtra as directed under the above Government Decision.
Government Circular: As per reference number 1 to 4, 16% seats should be reserved for Socially and Educationally Backward (SEBC) category including Maratha community for appointment in all non-agricultural universities and educational institutions under the purview of Higher and Technical Education Department in the state of Maharashtra. Also, 10% seats should be reserved for the economically weaker sections in the open category.
The reservation for appointment in educational institutions prescribed by the Government shall be applicable to all non-agricultural universities, aided deemed universities, law universities and all non-government aided colleges and autonomous colleges/ institutions affiliated to them, except minority educational institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education of the State. It will be the responsibility of the concerned universities to ensure effective implementation of this order.
(3) If the Universities/ Institutions have framed rules/ regulations for appointment in educational institutions and reservations of various social categories have been indicated in them, the necessary amendments in the rules/ regulations in accordance with this reservation shall be made immediately by the concerned as per the prescribed procedure.
4. All Universities are hereby informed to bring this circular to the notice of all educational institutions under them.
5. Department of General Administration. The instructions regarding recruitment under the letter dated 25.02.2019 should be strictly followed.
