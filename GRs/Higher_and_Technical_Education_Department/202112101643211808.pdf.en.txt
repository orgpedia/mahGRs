The proposal:
Section 76 (1) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 provides for the establishment of the" Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission." As per the provisions of the Act, the Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission will be the state government authority in charge and responsible for higher education. Further, the Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission will be the planning, controlling, coordinating and evaluating authority in the state and will act as an expert body for higher education, including emerging areas of education such as technical education, medical education, management education, vocational education, bioscience and technology, which will emerge on the horizon of knowledge in the future. The Commission will create synergies among various stakeholders such as State Governments, Public and Private Universities, Private Skill Education Provider Ecosystem and Industry.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the matter of setting up the" Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission" was under consideration of the Government, in this regard, the Government has taken the following decision.
The government's decision:
As per the provisions of Section 76 (1) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the" Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission" is being constituted.
