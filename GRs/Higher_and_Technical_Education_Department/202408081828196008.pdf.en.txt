Read: Letter from Director, Technical Education No. 2 / PCI-approved / 2024/482, d. 11.07.2024.
Preamble: The Director, Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government in a letter to read out the change in the address of the institute of the existing Pharmacy course from the academic year 2023-24 as approved by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. Accordingly, the issue of government approval was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval given by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi in the academic year 2023-24 and as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the Government is approving the change in address of the existing institution namely, Adarsh College of Pharmacy, Vita, G. Sangli of Loknete Hanamantrao Patil Charitable Trust with effect from the academic year 2023-24 as follows.
