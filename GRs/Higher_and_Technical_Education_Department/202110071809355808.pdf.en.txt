Under the Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak Memorial Centenary initiative, a proposal has been submitted for administrative approval for the repair, renovation, beautification and modernization of the office building of the Government Divisional Library, Ratnagiri.
The budget for this work has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, North Ratnagiri, Public Works Department, Ratnagiri and the Chief Engineer, Public Regional Department, Konkan, Mumbai has provided Rs. Technical Approval has been accorded for a budget of Rs.4, 25,00,626/- (Rupees Four Crores Twenty Five Lakhs Six Hundred and Twenty Six only).
The budget is based on the 2020-21 budget.
The government's decision:
Renovation, Renovation and Modernisation of Government Divisional Library, Ratnagiri As per Form A along with the Budget Estimate of Rs. 4,25,00, 626/- (Rupees Four Crore Twenty Five Lakh Six Hundred and Twenty Six only), Administrative Sanction is being granted subject to the following conditions: 1. Care should be taken to ensure that no expenditure beyond the administrative sanction is incurred while carrying out the actual work.
2) Get the necessary approvals before you start working.
3) While working on the provision made in a rough form, the work should be taken up only after approving the detailed budgets.
4) The Department of Industries, Energy and Labour has adopted the e-tendering system for the procurement related matters in the proposal. Action should be taken in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision dated 24.08.2017.
5) If the work falls under a heritage building, it will be necessary to obtain a no-objection certificate from the concerned.
