The proposal:
The Director, Directorate of Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government through the above letter regarding grant of Government approval as per the permission given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for starting new institutions of diploma courses on non-subsidized basis in the State from the academic year 2023-24, starting new courses on non-subsidized basis in existing unaided and non-government aided institutions, increasing the intake capacity, etc.
