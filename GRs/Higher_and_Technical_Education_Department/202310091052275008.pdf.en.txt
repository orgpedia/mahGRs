The Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur have been directed to provide funds to the tune of Rs.5 crore per university per year in four instalments to each law university for the next five years from the financial year 2023-24 for meeting their day-to-day administrative and academic expenses, salaries of posts filled as per the approved figure, and honorarium of contractual staff paid by the universities. 1 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. In accordance with this Administrative Sanction Reference No. 2 As per the letter herein, in the first instalment of 2023-24 (Mahe APIL, 2023 to June, 2023), Rs. 1,25,00, 000/- is requested to be released.
Accordingly, Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Nagpur and Aurangabad will be provided Rs. 1,25,00, 000/- (Rs. 3,75,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Seventy Five Lakhs only) was under consideration of the Government.

The Executive Council will be responsible for the management of the grant to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur by depositing the first installment of the total amount sanctioned by the Government in the University Fund. Also, the registrar/ vice-chancellor of the concerned Maharashtra National Law University should take action to exchange the expenditure made out of this amount and approve it by placing the statement of accounts of expenditure before the General Council with the approval of the Finance Committee of the university.
(2) The Vice-Chancellors of the Law Universities shall be responsible to see that the total amount sanctioned by the Government is spent for the purpose for which it was sanctioned. In this regard, they should submit its utility certificate and detailed expenditure information to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education within the prescribed time after obtaining the approval of the Executive Council as well as the Finance Committee. Please note that the next tranche of funds will not be disbursed to the Law Universities.
(3) All the documents and accounts of the aforesaid bulk amount are to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur on a case to case basis.
