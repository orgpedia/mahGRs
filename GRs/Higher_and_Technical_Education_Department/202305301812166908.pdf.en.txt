the order
The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006. In accordance with the provisions of Section 3 (1) of the said Act, the following officers of the post of Principal, Group-A in Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 5 against their names, with the approval of the competent authority for transfer:

(3) No leave of absence shall be granted to the officer concerned during the period from the date of his retirement to the date of his joining the place of posting.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune shall take action to relieve the officers transferred by this order by June 15, 2023. Also, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers to ensure that the salary of the transferred officers from July 1, 2023, will not be withdrawn from the office before the transfer.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune shall submit the report to the Government by 25th June, 2023.
