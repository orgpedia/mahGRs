Introduction:- A proposal was received from the University regarding the establishment of the Poetess Bahinabai Chaudhari Study and Research Center at the Poetess Bahinabai Chaudhari North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon. The centre will offer courses on Khandshi culture and dialect, women empowerment, Marathi women's poetry and Indian language women's poetry. As per the proposal, administrative approval has been given to set up a Centre for Study and Research on Poetess Bahinabai Chaudhari at the Bahinabai Chaudhari North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon.
Funds were budgeted through Supplementary Demands for Grants for this Chair Centre. The Department of Finance has agreed to allocate the budgeted funds to the Centre. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds to the Poetess Bahinabai Chowdhury Study and Research Center was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- The Centre for Study and Research of Poetess Bahinabai Chaudhari, Uttar Maharashtra University, Jalgaon has been sanctioned an amount of Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
(1) No new post shall be created for the said Centre.
2. The subsidy received from the Government shall be invested in the fixed deposits of the nationalised bank and the interest received from this amount shall be utilised for the activities of the Foundation.
No additional funds will be provided to the University. The university should provide more funds.
4. The income of the Foundation shall be raised from the interest received on fixed deposits. Course fees, training programs, and counseling projects.
This will be supplemented by revenues generated from operating income.
