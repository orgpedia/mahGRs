has been given. Reference No. The University has requested the letter here. As per the request, the Finance Department had approved the release of funds for the construction of the building.
Government Decision:- For the construction of subway and service road to permanently connect the eastern and western part of Shivaji University, Rs. Administrative Sanction amounting to Rs. 250.00 Lakhs (Rupees Two Crores Fifty Lakhs only) is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions: 1. The University shall be utilized for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned and the Utilization Certificate shall be submitted to the Government immediately by the Director (Higher Education), Pune.
The progress of construction should be informed to the government from time to time.
Special care should be taken to ensure that the subject work does not require revised administrative approval.
All the documents and accounts of the above grant shall be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the Accountant General-2, Nagpur, as the case may be, by the officer authorised by him for the said purpose.
