Janani Natarajan, Professor of Nuclear Engineering, St. Xavier's Technical Institute, Mumbai (Aided Institute) has been selected for higher education (Ph.D. course) in National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli under Quality Improvement Scheme for a period of 03 (three) years from the academic year 2019-2020.
This Government Decision allows Smt. Janani Natarajan to depute to the institution selected by her for higher education subject to the following terms and conditions, provided that there is no academic loss to the students of the institution concerned.
(1) The term of office shall be for a fixed period.
(2) Mrs. Janani Natarajan shall be entitled to the following conditions: 7. Salaries and allowances payable during the period of their deputation as per rules.
3) They are being allowed to accept the scholarship provided to them by the institution to which they were sent on deputation in addition to their salary.
(4) They shall not be allowed to travel to the place of deputation or to return to the headquarters during the period of deputation as well as after the expiry of deputation.
Janani Natarajan will not be entitled to any study leave during the remainder of her service and the bond will have to be furnished to her office before proceeding for training.
6) If they apply for extension beyond the contractual period for the course concerned, the extended period of deputation shall be deemed to be their paid and admissible leave.
7) After completion of training, a bond of 6 consecutive years of service of the working organization will have to be given before they go for training. This bond is issued by the General Administration Department
