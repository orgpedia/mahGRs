The transfer is being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) This order shall take effect immediately.
(2) On receipt of transfer orders, the concerned superiors shall join the college from which they are presently working immediately. Also, the concerned college heads should relieve the transferred officials immediately without waiting for them to appear before the alternate administration.

(3) The transferred post-graduates shall be absorbed by their respective colleges without any further delay after they are relieved from their duties and join. The Dean of the college concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-appointment of a domicile.
(4) No request for change of posting shall be entertained.
If any advocate directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, such advocate will be liable to disciplinary action.
(5) No leave of the transferred designations shall be granted without the prior approval of the Administrative Department.
(6) It shall be the responsibility of the respective Deans personally to relieve the transferred officials immediately. This should be followed up by the Director, Directorate of Arts. The order also issued an executive report in this regard.
The Director, Directorate of Arts, should inform this department without fail within 1 month from the date.
