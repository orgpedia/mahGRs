Introduction: Government College of Engineering, Pune has been granted autonomy. The Governing Body of the Institute in its 50 th meeting held on 11 th November, 2021 has approved the draft of the proposed Act for granting the status of Standalone Technical University to the" Government Engineering College, Pune (CoEP)." The government had constituted a committee to examine the proposal.
Government Decision: The following Committee is being constituted to examine the Act approved by the Governing Body of the Institution to grant the status of a Unitary Technical University to the Government Engineering College, Pune

The committee will submit its report along with the recommendations to the government by December 20, 2021.
(3) The work of the committee
The committee will be functioning at the office of the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. The Director, Directorate of Technical Education shall make appropriate arrangements for the functioning of the Committee.
Meeting and Travel Allowances:
Retired officers to get Rs. 7000/- per sitting as sitting allowance and actual cost of travel will be admissible. The expenses will be met from the Personal Pension Account (PLA) of the Government Engineering College, Pune.
