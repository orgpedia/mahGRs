The Proposal:
Reference No. 1 to the Department of Finance mentioned herein. As per the Government decision dated 03.03.2025, a total of 219 temporary posts, namely 14 temporary posts on the establishment of this Department (itself) and 7 temporary posts created for the National Service Planning Cell, have been given extension as per the Government decision at No. 2 in the aforesaid context. Expires on 31.08.2025 (M.No.).
Now refer to No. 3 of the Department of Finance mentioned here. Provisional posts in the department are given under the Government Decision of 26.08.2025. Administrative divisions have been empowered to extend the deadline up to 28.02.2026. In view of this, a total of 21 temporary posts are under consideration for extension, namely 14 temporary posts on the Ministry of Higher and Technical Education (in-house) establishment and 7 temporary posts created for the National Service Planning Cell.
The Government's Decisions:
A total of 21 temporary posts have been created at the Ministry of Higher and Technical Education's (in-house) establishment as indicated in the accompanying co-signer, 14 temporary posts and 7 temporary posts created for the National Service Planning Cell. From 01.09.2025 (BC) to D. Government approval is being given to continue till 28.02.2026 (M.No).
2. Expenditure for posts at the Ministry (in-house) establishment of the Department shall be apportioned from the sanctioned provision under the head "Demand No. W-6,2251 - Secretariat-Social Services-090-Secretariat (01) (01), Department of Higher and Technical Education-01 - Pay (Datta) (2251 0409)" and for posts created for the National Service Cell "Demand No. W-6,2251 - Secretariat-Social Services-090-Secretariat, (02) (National PA Scheme) (03) (01) National Service Scheme-01 - Pay (2251 0688) (Non-Scheme) (Datta Mat) (Central Share)."
3. The said Government Decision, Finance Department, No.: Designation-2016 / Q. 8/16 APC, D. are being issued under the powers conferred on administrative departments under the 26.08.2025 rule.
