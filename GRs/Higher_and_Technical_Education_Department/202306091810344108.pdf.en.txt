GOVERNMENT ORDER: As per the Government decision at Reference No. 1, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC)-recognized candidates have been appointed as Junior Administrative Officers/ Superintendents, Group-B on the establishment of Directorate of Higher Education for a probation period of two years and they have been enrolled in the post of appointment. In view of the fact that the said officers have undergone personal service test as per the checklist and are found to be satisfactory in their work during the period of probation, it is hereby approved to continue their appointment as on the date mentioned in column No. 7 by terminating the probationary period of the said officers mentioned in column No. 2 of the accompanying prospectus. Accordingly, the necessary records should be taken in the service records of the concerned.
