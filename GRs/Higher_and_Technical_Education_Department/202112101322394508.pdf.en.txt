Introduction: The scholarship scheme has been implemented from the academic year 2018-19 for meritorious students pursuing higher education abroad. 20 meritorious students who have been admitted to the Post Graduate/ Post Graduate Diploma and Ph.D. courses are selected for this scholarship scheme. The scheme and selection process is under reference. 1 here. The decision was taken on October 4, 2018. Applications are invited online for the academic year 2021-22.
Accordingly, 57 applications were received on the online portal of the Directorate of Technical Education till the prescribed deadline. Review the applications received. It was presented at the Scrutiny Committee meeting on 16 November 2021. The Scrutiny Committee, after scrutinizing the applications and examining the eligibility conditions, submitted the list of students to the Selection Committee for its decision along with the recommendation of the Committee.
Accordingly, a selection committee was constituted under the chairmanship of the Principal Secretary, Higher and Technical Education. The meeting was held on 03.12.2021. As per the decision taken in the meeting, the matter of allowing scholarships to the students who have qualified for the academic year 2021-22 was under the consideration of the government.
