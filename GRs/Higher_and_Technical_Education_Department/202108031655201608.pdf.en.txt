(2) Only eligible admitted students in the Centralized Admission Process should be given the benefit of Academic Fee Scholarship. (3) The Director, Technical Education and Joint Directors of all Divisional Technical Education shall visit the sample institutions under their jurisdiction and inspect the implementation of the scheme from time to time and submit a report to the Government.
(4) The Director of Technical Education shall take necessary steps to rectify any errors/ irregularities found in the previous inspection report.
