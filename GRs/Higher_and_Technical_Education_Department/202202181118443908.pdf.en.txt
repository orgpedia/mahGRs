Introduction: Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik University was established by the Maharashtra Legislative Council Act No. 20 respectively. 01.07.1989. The University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has jurisdiction over the entire state of Maharashtra. The university has 8 regional centres located at Amravati, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Mumbai, Nashik, Pune, Kolhapur and Nanded. 1600 study centers are controlled by 8 departmental centers of the university. About 6 million students study at the university. There are a total of 267 study centres in Pune, Satara and Solapur under the Pune Divisional Centre, with 160, 36 and 71 respectively, with a total intake of up to 1 lakh students. Considering the accessibility of the students and to decentralize the work, there is a need for a sub-center to provide facilities to the students, so it was proposed to construct a separate sub-center and library building at Baramati for the existing Satara and Solapur districts under the Pune Divisional Center. Accordingly, Rs. 906.57 lakh has been accorded administrative approval under the relevant Government decision subject to certain conditions.
The State Government has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 86,95,000/- (Rupees Sixty Eight Lakh Ninety Five Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nasik, has sanctioned Rs. 86,95,000/- (Rupees Sixty Six Lakhs Ninety Five Thousand only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
The fund should be disbursed and withdrawn within the limits of administrative approval.
(2) No expenditure shall be incurred in excess of the work-wise provision brought to the notice of the Legislature during the current financial year.
Under no circumstances should the concerned Superintending Engineer spend more than the amount of administrative sanction on the works.
The concerned Executive Engineer shall arrange to send a report on the progress of the work and the expenditure incurred to the Registrar and Director of Higher Education of the concerned university.
