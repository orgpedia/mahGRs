The proposal:
The MahaDBT portal, developed by the State Government to provide the benefits of government schemes directly to the beneficiaries, has been operationalized in August 2018 and through this portal, the process of inviting applications for scholarships from eligible students, scrutinizing the applications from the college and department level, providing allotment for payment of scholarships to the approved students, and disbursement of scholarships in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the approved student through PFMS system, etc. is done through MahaIT and MahaOnline. The government has approved the budget for 2018-19.
Government Decision: For the period August, 2018 to January, 2019, a sum of Rs. 65,29,074/-for the month of February, 2019 to March, 2019. 14,42,805/- (Rs. 79,71,879/- (Rupees Ninety-nine Lakhs One Hundred and Seventy-One Thousand Eight Hundred and Nineteen only) is being approved to be paid out of the sanctioned amount for the year 2019-20.
