2. The Principal/ Director concerned shall relieve the transferred officers immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned officers should immediately join the college/ institution where they have been transferred from the existing college/ institution.
3. The Principal/ Director concerned shall make the transferred officers join without any further delay after they are relieved from the respective college/ institution. The Principal/ Director concerned will be held responsible in case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of the officer by the concerned Principal/ Director. Also, no leave of absence will be granted during the period from the date of discharge to the date of joining the place of transfer.

The concerned head of the office shall take action to relieve the officers transferred by this order by June 15, 2023. Also, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers to ensure that the salary of the transferred officers from July 1, 2023, will not be withdrawn from the office before the transfer.
