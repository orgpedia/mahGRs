The Project Approval Board of the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) has been constituted to ensure the availability of educational facilities in the district on the basis of Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER), number of girls and population of backward classes. In the 12th meeting held on 25th May, 2018, approval has been given for setting up of new Government Model Degree Colleges in two districts of Maharashtra, namely Nandurbar and Washim.
Under the scheme, Rs. 1200.00 lakh has been sanctioned for non-recurring expenditure. The scheme is being implemented by the Centre and the State in the ratio of 60: 40. 720.00 lakhs and Rs. 480.00 lakhs.
