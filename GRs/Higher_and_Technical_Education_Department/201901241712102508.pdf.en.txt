Maharashtra State Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology and Research Centre Society, Pune has decided to start undergraduate courses in Hotel Management and Catering Technology on self-help basis from the academic year 2003-04. PEC-2001/ (361/2001)/ Tanshi-1, D. Approved on 30/10/2002.
The government decision stipulates the formation of a company or society to run the degree department on a self-help basis and the appointment of a managing board. Accordingly, a registered society in the name of Maharashtra State Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology and Research Centre Society, Pune has been formed and the society document (Memorandum of Association) of the said Institute has been approved by the Department of Law and Justice vide Government letter dated 08/12/2008. According to the memorandum of association, the government was considering the formation of an 11-member governing body of the institute.
The Governing Board of Maharashtra State Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology and Research Centre Society, Pune is being constituted as follows:
