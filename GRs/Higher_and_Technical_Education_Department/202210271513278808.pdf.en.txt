The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has been conducting the above mentioned examinations since the academic year 2007-08. Under Section 1, it is approved to change the name of the institution of Diploma course in Pharmacy.
Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to change the name of the institution of the existing Diploma course in Pharmacy under the above reference letter. The proposal had been approved by the government.
The government's decision:
In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is approving change in the name of the institution of Diploma course in Pharmacy as mentioned in the following" Form-A" from the academic year 2022-23.
