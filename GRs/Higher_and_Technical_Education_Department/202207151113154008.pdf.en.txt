Government Decision: As per the procedure prescribed under Section 109 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and the Government Decision dated 15.09.2017 under reference, all the non-agricultural universities in the State have recommended proposals for obtaining Intent Letter of Intent to start new colleges for the academic year 2022-23 for the approval of the Government. Reference No. 1 here has been examined as per the government decision. At some points in the investigation, more than one proposal met the criteria. Section 109 (3) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 provides as follows:
The State Government may, in its fair and discretionary decision, issue Intentions to such establishments as it deems fit and proper, after examining the suitability of the management seeking Intentions. This has been said. In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 109 (3) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the following Statement of Intent is being granted to the colleges mentioned in the Statement on a perpetual non-grant basis subject to the following terms and conditions prescribed by the Government:

Terms and Conditions: 1. The letter of intent to start a new college as per the prevailing policy of the Government shall continue to be valid for starting a college on a non-subsidized basis.
Since the said Letter of Intent has been issued by the Institute with a view to fulfil the prescribed terms and conditions before obtaining final approval for the new college, the said Letter of Intent shall not enable the Institute to start the college without obtaining final approval of the Government for starting the new college.
The letter of intent issued by the government to start a new college will be valid until January 31 of the nearest next year. During this period, it will be mandatory to meet the terms and conditions on the basis of which the letter of intent has been issued and submit a compliance report to the university along with the necessary documents for final approval.
In view of the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, the Government has decided to provide Rs. The procedure for final approval as on 15th September, 2017 has been clearly spelt out. In accordance with the provisions of the Act and the procedure, the institutions receiving the Letter of Intent fulfilling the terms and conditions of the Letter of Intent shall apply to the University for final approval with all the documents and the Universities shall submit the applications of all the eligible applicant institutions along with the compliance report to the Government along with the recommendation of approval in Annexure' C' and Form' B' and the accompanying documents.

While providing facilities in the college, it should be ensured that facilities are created for students with disabilities as per the provisions of The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and the norms prescribed from time to time in this regard and it will be mandatory to submit a certificate in this regard while submitting the completion report for final approval.
