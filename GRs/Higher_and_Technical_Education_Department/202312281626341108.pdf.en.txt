Memorandum: In pursuance of letter No. 52/ Granthotsav 2023/ 2023-24/ 4257-58 dated 08.09.2023 from the Director of Libraries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, I am directed to inform that under the Maharashtra State Cultural Policy, 2010, a sum of Rs. 60 lakh in the budget. 18 crore has already been released. In order to ensure timely implementation of the Granthotsav-2023 across the State, the remaining Rs. 42,00,000/- (Rupees Forty Two Lakh only) to the Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
2. The expenditure to be incurred for organizing the book festival should be within the limits of the funds allocated. Also, the above sanctioned funds should be spent for the same matters for which they have been sanctioned and the utilization certificate should be submitted to the government immediately through the Director of Libraries.
3. Expenditure on Demand No. W 4, 2205, Art and Culture, 105, Public Libraries, (02) (03) Organizing Book Festival, Mandatory (22053582) 50, Other expenditure to be met from the funds available under this head in the year 2023-24.
