The government's decision:
Under the General Administration Department. As per the Government Circular dated 15.12.2017, it is proposed to register the performance appraisal reports of all the State Government officers of Group A and Group B (Gazetted) cadre through online mode.
Primary Custodian and Organization Unit are being appointed to record the performance appraisal report of the Gazetted Officers of the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State and their subordinate offices in the online system as mentioned in paragraph 5 of this Government Circular and it will be as per Annexure' A' attached.
3. Primary Protectors to appoint additional Protectors. The Alternate Custodian shall appoint one or more Par Managers depending upon the number of officers in the unit under his control to prepare performance appraisal reports of the officers in the unit under his control.
4. The designated Primary Custodian, Organization Unit, Alternate Custodian and Parmanager shall be responsible for the implementation of the relevant Government Circular, General Administration Department. To perform the duties and responsibilities as per instructions dated 15.12.2017.
