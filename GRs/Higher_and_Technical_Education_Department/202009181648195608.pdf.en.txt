Bengaluru: The entire country has been put under a lockdown due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Due to the lockdown, administrative work is being carried out in the presence of limited staff.
Various writ petitions have been filed challenging the guidelines dated 6.07.2020 issued by the University Grants Commission regarding the conduct of examinations for final year students and the decision of the State Disaster Management Authority and the State Government not to conduct the final year examinations. It was submitted to the Supreme Court. In these petitions, The Supreme Court The decision was taken on August 28, 2020, according to which the final year/ final semester examinations of the university are to be conducted in the state.
