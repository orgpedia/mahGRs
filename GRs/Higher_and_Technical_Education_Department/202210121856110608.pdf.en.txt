Government Circular: In order to preserve the memory of Dr. A.P.J. 15th October is celebrated as" Reading Inspiration Day." To develop interest and motivation in reading among students and other sections of the society and to understand the importance of reading. A circular has been issued in this regard to observe October 15 as" Reading Inspiration Day." Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government Circular No: Meeting- 2413/ (Q. No. 140/15)/ Coordination, Dy. In addition to the compliance of the instructions dated 9th October, 2015, the following activities should also be undertaken to implement this initiative:
1) Bouquet not book- Implementation and dissemination of the government's decision to gift a book instead of a bouquet to guests at a public event.
(2) The experiment.
3) Lectures/ Seminars/ Symposiums/ Workshops on topics related to reading motivation or reading culture.
4) The original thoughts of great people should be read collectively.
5) Free books should be distributed to the needy and interested students.
6) Useful books should be presented to the staff and officers in the administration.
7) The author should conduct a public interview with the authors about their writings, their work, or their work under his visit-interaction with the authors.
8) Read newspapers and magazines.
9) E-books should be read collectively.
(10) Collective reading of important/ selected pages of a well-known book should be done.
(11) There should be an invitation to gift books to each other on birthdays.
Schools, colleges, libraries and other institutions should set up" Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Reading Room" in their premises.
