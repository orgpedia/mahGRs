(2) The period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to the teachers transferred on administrative grounds as per the rules of the Government. As per the government rules, the transfer of teachers will not be allowed during the tenure of the post, travel allowance and daily allowance.
In order to ensure the immediate and effective implementation of this transfer order, the following instructions should be strictly followed:
(a) the head of the institution concerned shall forthwith relieve the transferred teacher to attend his new place of posting and the teacher concerned shall immediately report to the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai and the Government at the place of posting assigned to him.
The heads of the concerned institutions should make the transferred teachers join without any delay after they are relieved from their duties. In case of non-recruitment of a teacher, the principal of the institution concerned will be held responsible for the non-payment of salary and allowances.
(a) As the said transfer order is required to be effectively implemented, all the teachers transferred shall be given full time leave up to the date of their retirement or until further orders. Salaries up to July 15, 2019, should be paid by the institution in which they are currently working. Further action will be taken by holding the drawing and disbursing officer of the transferor institution personally liable for payment of salary from July 16, 2019 onwards.
No leave should be granted to the transferred teachers otherwise disciplinary action will be taken against the competent authority sanctioning leave.
The transferred teachers should join the new post during the period of permissible postings after they are relieved from their duties. Absence of teachers who do not comply with this time limit and remain mutually absent without being present at the place of posting" Unofficial"
