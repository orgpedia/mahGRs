Reference: - Government Resolution No. USG-2006 / (254/06) / Vishy-4 (Part-II), dated 29-05-2013.
Dr. D.Y. Patil Pratishthan, Pune-411044, the Sponsoring Board for the establishment of Patil International University, Pune University, has been approved by the Government. The letter of intent was issued on October 3, 2017. The Sponsoring Board took action in accordance with the letter of intent and submitted a compliance report to the Government. Published on October 11, 2017.
As per the provisions of paragraph 7 (2) of the guidelines issued pursuant to the relevant Government decision, the Sponsoring Board shall constitute a Compliance Report Monitoring Committee to verify the compliance report after submitting the compliance report to the Government. As per the said provision, the Sponsoring Board has submitted a compliance report to the Government pursuant to the letter of intent after the letter of intent is issued. The government was considering setting up a monitoring committee to verify the compliance report.
Accordingly, the following Oversight Committee is being constituted to verify the compliance report submitted by the Sponsoring Board.

Committee's mandate
The Committee has attached a list of actions and responsibilities to be taken to verify and monitor the compliance report. The committee should take action according to the provisions of this list.
The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within a period of one month from the date of its constitution. This period can be extended up to three more months.
The members other than the ex-officio members of the Committee shall be paid an honorarium of Rs.5000 / - (Rupees Five Thousand only) per sitting.
