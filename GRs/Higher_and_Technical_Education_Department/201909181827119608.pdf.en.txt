(2) The period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to the Director of Physical Education who is transferred for administrative reasons as per the rules of the Government. The Director of Physical Education who is transferred on request will not be allowed to hold office for the period, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the government rules.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The following instructions shall be strictly complied with by the Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution so that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
(2) On receipt of the transfer order, the Principal/ Director concerned shall relieve the transferred Director of Physical Education immediately to join the place of transfer without waiting for the replacement of the transferred Director of Physical Education. Also, the Director of Physical Education concerned should immediately join the college/ institution where he is transferred from the present college/ institution.
(3) The transferred Director of Physical Education shall be reinstated without delay after he/ she retires from the concerned college/ institution. Salary and allowances without work due to compulsory waiting period due to non-appointment of a Physical Education Director

The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be held responsible if the occasion arises.
(4) No request for change in post will be entertained.
Any attempt by the Director of Physical Education to directly or indirectly pressurize the Director of Physical Education to modify the transfer order shall entitle the Director of Physical Education concerned to take disciplinary action.
5) No leave of absence will be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
Since these transfers are required to be effected with immediate effect, the salary of the transferred Director of Physical Education up to the date of superannuation or till the maximum date of September 30, 2019, shall be drawn by the college/ institution where he is presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the college/ institution before transfer under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the college/ institution after the transfer of the concerned Director of Physical Education after joining the college/ institution. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from October 1, 2019 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the college/ institution where they were working before the transfer.
The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be personally responsible for the immediate release of the transferred Director of Physical Education. The Director, Higher Education, Pune shall submit the compliance report to the Government within 15 days from the date of this order.
