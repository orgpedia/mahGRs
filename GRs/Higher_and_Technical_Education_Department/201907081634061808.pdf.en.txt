As per the government rules, the transfer of assistant professors will not be allowed during the tenure of the post, travel allowance and daily allowance.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The principals of the concerned colleges shall strictly follow the following instructions so that the transfer order is implemented immediately and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.

(2) On receipt of the transfer order, the Principal concerned shall relieve the transferred Assistant Professor immediately to join the place of transfer without waiting for the replacement of the transferred Assistant Professor. Also, the concerned assistant professors should join the college immediately after their transfer from the existing college.
3.The transferred Assistant Professors should be recruited without delay after they are relieved from the concerned college and come to join. The Principal of the concerned college will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of an Assistant Professor.
4.No request for change in post will be entertained.
If any assistant professor directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, the concerned assistant professor will be liable for disciplinary action.
5. No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
Since the transfers are required to be effected with immediate effect, the salary of the transferred Assistant Professors up to the date of retirement or till the maximum date i.e. 15th July, 2019 shall be drawn by the college where they are presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the college before transfer under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the college after the transfer of the concerned assistant professor after joining the college of the transfer. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from July 16, 2019 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the college where they were working before the transfer.
The Principal of the concerned college will be personally responsible for the immediate release of the transferred Assistant Professors. The Director, Higher Education, Pune shall submit the report to the Government within 7 days from the date of this order.
