The Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014.
The Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai has temporarily resumed its operations on the 2nd, 4th and 6th floor of MTNL, Powai. As per the lease agreement, the rent payable for the month of October, 2021 to December, 2021 as well as the rent payable for the period January, 2022 to March, 2022 for the financial year 2021-22 is to be paid as per the terms of reference no. A letter of 1 is requested. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai for payment of rent was under the consideration of the Government.
Maharashtra Government has decided to temporarily start MTNL at 2nd, 4th and 6th Floor, Powai for Maharashtra National Wardhi Vidyapith, Mumbai. The rent payable for the period from October, 2021 to December, 2021 for the financial year 2021-22 is Rs. 4,65,92, 112/- (Rupees Four Crores Fifty Lakh Ninety Two Thousand One Hundred and Twelve only) and the amount of rent payable for the period January, 2022 to March, 2022 is Rs. 4,71,99, 612/- (Rupees Four Crores Seventy One Lakh Nine Thousand Six Hundred and Twelve only) aggregating to Rs. 9,37,91, 724/- (Rupees Nine Crores Thirty Seven Lakhs Nine Thousand Seven Hundred and Twenty Four) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
(1) The Executive Council shall be responsible for administering the grants sanctioned by the Government as per the Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014 by depositing them in the University Fund. Also, the university should take action to approve the expenditure incurred from the grant by exchanging the statement of accounts of the expenditure before the General Council.
(2) The Vice-Chancellor shall be responsible to see that the grant is spent for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned by the Government to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai. In this regard, they should submit its utility certificate to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education with the approval of the Executive Council.
(3) All the documents and accounts of all the above grants shall be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as the case may be.
