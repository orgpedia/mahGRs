Government Resolution, Department of Education and Youth Services No.- LIB- 2575/7956/ (705)/ Tera, dated 29.7.1977 2) Government Resolution, Department of Higher and Technical Education, No. Maragran-2524/ PR No. 36/2015/ Sashi-5, dated 30.03.2015 3) Government Resolution, Department of Higher and Technical Education, No. MARAGRAM-2524/ Q.No. 36/2015/ (2)/ Sashi-5, Dy. 30.03.2015 4) Letter from the Directorate of Libraries, vide No.31/ SLPL Meeting/ 2018-19/5292, dt. 22.06.2018 Preamble: The Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Calcutta was established in 1972 with financial assistance from the Government of India. The objective of the Foundation is to promote universal development of public library services in the country. The Foundation provides matching grants to the States in the ratio of 50:50 to effectively implement and enhance library services in the States and Union Territories. In order to implement these schemes of the Foundation, the State Library Planning Committee was formed by the Government Decision dated 29th July, 1977 as per the guidelines contained in the book" Books for all" of the Raja Rammohan Roy Library Foundation.
Under the' Granth Bhet' scheme of the Foundation, books are purchased and distributed to selected public libraries. All the books published annually in Marathi language are kept for the consideration of the State Library Planning Committee of Raja Rammohan Roy Library Foundation. In order to facilitate the selection of selected books from all these books, a Sub-Committee of the State Library Planning Committee under the Chairmanship of Shri. The government was reconstituted by the Decision of 3. Accordingly, the term of the said Committee has expired on 29.03.2018. Accordingly, the matter of reconstitution of the sub-committee of the State Library Committee was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is pleased to reconstitute the Sub-Committee of the State Library Planning Committee under the Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation as follows:
