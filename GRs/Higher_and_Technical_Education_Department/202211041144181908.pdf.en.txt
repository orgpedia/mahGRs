Preamble: It is likely to take some more time to finalize the recruitment of candidates to the posts of Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education) and Joint Director (Administration) on the establishment of the Directorate of Higher Education. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the Government to constitute a new Selection Committee by dissolving the Selection Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 04.01.2021 to hand over the additional charge of 10 Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education) and 2 Joint Director (Administration).
Government Decision: The Selection Committee constituted vide Government Decision dated 04.01.2021 to recommend eligible candidates for additional charge of 10 posts of Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education) and 2 posts of Joint Director (Administration) on establishment of Directorate of Higher Education is hereby dissolved and a new Selection Committee is being constituted as under:

(a) to review the performance of the officers working in the posts of Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education) and Joint Director (Administration) on the establishment of the Directorate of Higher Education in accordance with their duties and responsibilities and to recommend to the Government that their charge may be continued or terminated and additional charge may be assigned to a new officer on administrative grounds.
(b) to call for applications along with their service details from the interested candidates who are holding the post of Associate Professors and Professors in Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education and fulfilling the prescribed conditions for the post of Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education), whose charge is required to be handed over to another officer on administrative grounds.
(c) Higher education for the post of Joint Director (Administration) where the charge of the incumbent is required to be handed over to another officer for administrative reasons.

To call for applications from interested candidates for the post of Senior Administrative Officer and Administrative Officer in the Directorate and Subordinate Offices and fulfilling the prescribed conditions along with their service details.
(d) Educational Qualification, Experience and Age Limit prescribed for appointment to the 10 posts of Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education) with additional charge as follows:
(i) At least 55% marks in the Master's degree.
(2) Ph.D. degree.
Professor/ Associate Professor, 12 years teaching experience.
It is clarified that the post of Professor/ Associate Professor is required to be filled. Candidate should have a minimum of 12 years of teaching experience in the field of teaching at degree/ postgraduate degree colleges.
3 years of administrative experience.
At least 3 publications after holding Ph.D.
(b) Maximum age is 55 years.
(e) The following qualifications have been prescribed for appointment to the 2 posts of Joint Director (Administration) with additional charge: (1) Senior Administrative Officer, with at least three years experience in the cadre of M.Sc. Group A.
(2) At least six years' experience in the cadre of Administrative Officer, Group-A of M.Sc.
(f) To seek from the Directorate of Higher Education, after examining the applications received, complete information about the applicants, whether departmental inquiry is going on/ proposed against them or how, a copy of the confidential report of the last 5 years and information about the assessment of grants of colleges in their jurisdiction and pending cases, if any, by the Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education) and Joint Director, who are presently working.
Classification of 50 marks of interview as prescribed in GO No: SRIKARIYA-2014/ (257/14)/ MASHI-1, dated 06.04.2015, taking into account educational qualification, experience, publications, confidential reports and interview.
(h) to submit to the Government, after interviewing the candidates who qualify in accordance with the educational qualification, experience and age limit prescribed for the post of Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education), as well as the qualification and score prescribed for the post of Joint Director.
The committee should complete the above process within one month.
(4) The term of the Committee shall be for the purpose of conducting only one selection process i.e. till the list is submitted to the Government on merit.
Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance to the Chairman and Members of the Committee (Non-official) The provisions of the Bombay Civil Services Rules, Clause 2, Annexure 42 (O), Section-1, relating to Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance to the Chairman and Non-official Members of the Committee, are applicable. Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance at the rate of Rs.8900/- and above will be admissible to the employees.
