Introduction: The context. In a letter dated 8th July, 2020, the Director, Technical Education has submitted a proposal for grant of Government approval for increase in intake capacity and introduction of new PG courses in Government and Government aided colleges from the academic year 2020-2021 on permanent non-subsidized self-financing basis as per the approval of All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. It was pending approval of the government.
Decision of the Government: The Government has approved to increase the intake capacity of the existing seats of 02 PG courses as shown against their names in the Annexure' A' for the academic year 2020-2021 as per the list of approval received from All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and to start 03 new PG courses in 02 Government and Government aided colleges from the academic year 2020-2021.
This Government approval is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions: 1) The approval shall be on a perpetual basis without subsidy.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The institution's eligibility for this course will remain as mentioned above.
(3) The institution shall have to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. as well as related documents.
