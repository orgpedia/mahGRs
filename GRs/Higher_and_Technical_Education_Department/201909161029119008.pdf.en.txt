The Central Government has decided to revise the pay scales of Central Government employees with effect from 1st January, 2016, based on the recommendations of the 7th Central Pay Commission. The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted by the Department of Finance to revise the pay scales of State Government and other eligible employees based on the decisions of the Central Government. The Committee had submitted Volume 1 of the Report to the Finance Department. The Department of Finance has issued an order for acceptance of recommendations on pay scales and annexures in Volume 1 of the Report of the State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 under the Government Resolution No. 06.
The Government have decided in respect of para 4.13 of the report in the said judgment as follows: The scope of recommendations in respect of para 4.13 of the report in clause 1 is as under:" The recommendations in this case shall be applicable to eligible State Government employees, Zilla Parishads, Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat, Judicial Employees of Courts, Aided School Employees, Agricultural and Non-Agricultural Universities and their affiliated non-aided colleges and employees of other aided institutions. It is recommending that the date of implementation of these recommendations for employees of other offices/ institutions should be the same as that of State Government employees. " The government has taken a decision. These recommendations should be implemented by the government for the employees of the 6th Pay Commission and other grant-in-aid institutions receiving salary subsidy.
The Department of Finance has implemented the pay structure of the 7th Pay Commission vide Government Notification dated 30th January, 2019. The pay structure of the officers/ staff under the Directorate of Technical Education is given in the Notification on pages 110 to 115.
4. The Department of Finance vide Circular No.8 has issued instructions regarding the date from which the employees are to accept the revised pay structure and fixation of pay in the revised pay structure.
The Finance Department has issued a clarification regarding payment of arrears to the employees covered under the National Pension Scheme or Definitive Contribution Pension Scheme, vide Circular No. 9.
As per the orders/ instructions issued by the Department of Finance in pursuance of the 7th Pay Commission, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai was apprised of the proposal to submit to the Government for implementation of the 7th Pay Commission pay structure for non-teaching staff under the Directorate. The proposal was submitted to the government through letters No. 10, 11 and 12. According to the proposal, the government was considering the implementation of the 7th Pay Commission for non-teaching staff of non-government aided, government degree and diploma institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education/ Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar University of Technology, Lonere and Institute of Chemical Technology, Matunga, Mumbai.
Government Decision The recommendations of the Seventh Pay Commission are being implemented under this Government Decision to the full-time non-teaching staff working in the non-teaching posts approved by the Government as per the review of the posts in the Non-Government Aided Degree and Diploma Institute under the Directorate of Technical Education, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere and Chemical Technology Institute, Matunga, Mumbai.
The decision will not be applicable to the following employees:
(a) employees who are not in full-time employment;
(b) an employee on consolidated pay,
(a) the employee;
(d) employees who are paid by way of contingency expenses,
(a) daily wage workers;
(f) Employees who retired on or before 31.12.2015 and who were re-employed on that date after retirement and whose period of re-employment after retirement has been extended beyond that date.
Separate revised orders, as may be necessary, will be issued with the concurrence of the Finance Department in respect of the non-teaching posts in Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar University of Technology, Lonere and Institute of Chemical Technology, Matunga, Mumbai and other institutions which are covered by the pay scale or other clauses in clause 2 to be issued by the Finance Department.
The revised pay scales in the Annexure annexed to this Government Resolution shall come into effect from 1st January, 2016. The Directorate of Technical Education shall take action to implement the Government Decisions, Government Notifications and Government Circulars issued by the Finance Department at Reference Nos. 06, 07, 08 and 09 as mentioned in the Government Circular in respect of the fixation of salary in the revised pay scale, options to be taken from the concerned employees and payment of arrears of salary, arrears of employees covered under the National Pension Scheme or Definitive Contribution Pension Scheme and other matters.
The additional expenditure arising out of these revised pay scales shall be covered under the head of accounts under which expenditure is incurred in respect of salaries and allowances of teachers and non-teaching staff and shall be met from the grants sanctioned under that head. the Director, Technical Education
