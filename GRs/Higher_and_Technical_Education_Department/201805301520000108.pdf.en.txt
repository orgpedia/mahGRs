the order
Pursuant to the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Principals/ Directors of Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education and Principals/ Directors of Principal Cadre of Government Teachers Colleges whose tenure has exceeded the period prescribed in Section 3 (1) of the Transfers Act, are being transferred as a result as indicated in the following statement.

Shanti Kumar Gupta has been given additional charge of the post of Director, Institute of Juridical Sciences, Aurangabad. Hemlata Wankhede, Director, Institute of Science, Aurangabad is being entrusted with the task till further orders.
The Principal/ Director who is transferred for administrative reasons will be entitled to the period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of the Government.
For immediate implementation of the transfer order, the Director, Higher Education should strictly follow the following instructions.
(1) On receipt of the transfer order, the transferred Principal/ Director shall be relieved of his duties immediately to join the place of transfer.
No request for change in the post will be entertained. If any Principal/ Director tries to exert direct or indirect pressure to modify the transfer order, such Principal/ Director will be liable to disciplinary action.
3) No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining the transferred post.
(4) The said transfers are required to be effected so as to be effective till the date of retirement of the transferred Principal/ Director or at the maximum.

The salary as on 30.6.2018 should be drawn by the college/ institution where they are presently working.
Subsequent salary shall not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer college/ institution under any circumstances, the salary shall be drawn by the post-transfer college/ institution after the concerned Principal/ Director joins the transferor college/ institution. If the salary drawn from the college/ institution where the salary was drawn from 01.7.2018 onwards is withdrawn from the college/ institution where it was working before the transfer, the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of that college/ institution shall be held personally liable.
The Director of Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune shall submit a report to the Government before 01.7.2018 regarding the transfer of the transferred Principal/ Director.
