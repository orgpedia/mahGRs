Preamble: Out of the large number of vacant posts of principals and teachers/ non-teaching staff in the recognized non-government aided colleges under the Directorate of Higher Education, the recruitment of a total of 4738 posts (Assistant Professor- 3580, Librarian- 163, Director of Physical Education 139 and Laboratory Assistant- 856) approved by the High Level Secretary Committee has been approved with the concurrence of the Finance Department under the Government decision of Reference No. 6. Out of which, 1492 posts of Assistant Professor, 42 posts of Librarian, 37 posts of Physical Education Director and 103 posts of Laboratory Assistant have been filled. However, in view of the economic situation of the State due to the outbreak of COVID-19, the remaining posts could not be filled up due to restrictions imposed by the Government in Reference No. 7 of the Finance Department. However, in the interest of the students and the urgency of the department, the recruitment of the remaining 2088 posts by deducting the 1492 posts filled out of the 3580 posts approved by the Higher Level Secretary Committee in the cadre of Assistant Professor only, has been approved by relaxing the conditions of the Government Decision dated 04.5.2020 and 24.6.2021 of the Finance Department under the Government Reference 10, with the consent of the Finance Department. The Finance Department has approved the recruitment of Librarian-102 and Physical Education Director-121 posts.

The recruitment of total 223 posts of Librarian- 102 and Director of Physical Education- 121 in the cadre of Director of Physical Education- 139, excluding the posts of Librarian- 37 and Director of Physical Education- 42 already filled, is being approved by relaxing the conditions of the Government Decision dated 04.5.2020 and 22.6.2021 of the Finance Department subject to the following terms and conditions:
Approval is being accorded with the concurrence of the Finance Department subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) Firstly, the vacant posts of Additional Librarian and Director of Physical Education should be adjusted immediately. The posts of Librarian and Director of Physical Education for the concerned subject can be advertised only after the Divisional Joint Director certifies that there is no vacancy.
(b) In pursuance of the reservation policy framed by the Centre, the Cadre-wise reservation policy for teaching posts in non-agriculture universities and affiliated colleges/ institutions in the State as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers' Cadre) Act, 2021 has been implemented as per the notification dated 01.4.2022 of the General Administration Department. Accordingly, the Department has issued a notification dated 08.4.2022 for its implementation. Accordingly, guidelines have been issued under the Government Decision dated 11.4.2022 of the Department on how the exact functioning of the reservation policy should be. Reservation will be applicable for the above posts.
e) The new reservation policy will not be applicable to the colleges which have published advertisement before the implementation of the new reservation policy as mentioned in point number 4 of the letter dated 24.6.2022 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education. Therefore, if the recruitment process for the posts advertised after prior approval is suspended/ pending, a proposal for extension should be submitted for the same.
e) The number of posts to be filled should be as per the departmental pattern. In addition, the responsibility of payment of salary to such candidates in case of recruitment to the posts will be of the concerned institution.
A) The contours of the teacher and non-teaching posts in the non-government aided colleges in the state have not yet been finalised as the high-level committee of secretaries has directed to fulfill certain aspects. Therefore, while relaxing the restrictions on recruitment, the vacancies permissible on the student strength as on 01.10.2017 have been made admissible on the basis of the approval of recruitment as above. However, since the framework has not been finalised, the design should not be considered final till the final approval of the Finance Department.
(f) The Director of Higher Education, Pune shall take action without delay to prepare and approve the revised pattern of the above posts.
(e) The provisions of the University Act shall be strictly complied with while making recruitments to the permissible posts in the non-government aided colleges affiliated to the Agricultural University. Appointment orders should be issued only after the completion of the due process.
(2) The said Government Decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department. Anusam 127/ APUC dated 02.03.2023 as well as Sec. 279/ Expenditure-5, being issued with the concurrence received vide Dated 06.3.2023.
