Preamble —
The State Government, vide Government Resolution under reference No.
1 above, notified the implementation of the Career Advancement Scheme (CAS)
in accordance with the AICTE clarifications/additional clarifications issued
from time to time. After the issuance of the said Government Resolution, certain
# Page 2
faculty members approached the Hon’ble Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal,
Mumbai, Bench Nagpur seeking a stay on the implementation of the above
Government Resolution, particularly praying for exemption from the
applicability of API/PBAS and other related conditions retrospectively for the
period from 17.10.2015 to 10.09.2019.
The matter regarding the implementation of the AICTE clarifications/additional
clarifications contained in the Government Resolution dated 03.06.2022 was
under consideration of the Government.
Corrigendum -
The provisions relating to API/PBAS contained in the Government
Resolution No: MISC 1111/28/16/TE-2, dated 03.06.2022, referred to at Sr. No.
1, regarding implementation of the Career Advancement Scheme (CAS), stand
amended as under:
1. The entry at Serial No. 1 in the Tables listed in Page 3 and Page 6, of the above
said Government Resolution dated 03.06.2022, titled as “/.3 Effective date of
application of Service Conditions regarding API/PBAS” would stand deleted.
2. In consonance with the above amendment, the related provisions in Annexure
I, Uf and III shall stand amended mutatis mutandis.
3. The entry at serial no. 7 in Annexure — II of the said G.R. No: MISC
1111/28/16/TE-2 dated 03.06.2022 in the column no. 4 titled as “ Decision taken
by the state government” shall be read as follows:
“Lecturer/Assistant Professor completing three years of teaching in the grade
of Rs.8000 (stage 3) shall be eligible to move to the Pay Band of Rs.37400-67000
with next higher grade of Rs.9000 (stage 4) and to be designated as Lecturer
(Selection Grade )/Associate Professor subject to the condition of fulfilling the
qualifying conditions and whereas in the case of those that have joined the
Service after 5" March 2010 shall possess Ph. D in addition to all other
requirements to move to the stage 4.”
# Page 3
4. "The conditions of qualification, experience, recruitment and promotion etc. for
CAS proposals up to 10.09.2019 shall be processed in accordance with the
provisions of the Fifth Pay Commission, proposals from 11.09.2019 to
31.12.2024 shall be processed in accordance with the provisions of the Sixth Pay
Commission and thereafter shall be processed in accordance with the provisions
of the Seventh Pay Commission."
