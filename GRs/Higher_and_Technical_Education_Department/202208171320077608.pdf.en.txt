As per the procedure laid down under the Government Decision dated 13.09.2017, the non-agriculture universities in the State have submitted proposals for new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties for the academic year 2022-23 to the Government for approval. The proposals were examined in accordance with the norms laid down under the relevant Government decisions. As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the existing colleges are being sanctioned the new faculty as per the existing policy of permanent non-granting principle of the government, subject to the following terms and conditions, keeping in view the demand for higher education and academic urgency in the college premises.
