(2) The benefit of scholarship should be given only to the eligible admitted students of the centralized admission process.
The amount will be directly credited to the bank accounts of the eligible students through the MahaDBT portal. However, appropriate action can be taken as per the court's decision.
3) If it is found that the education fee has been received from the government by filling in wrong or false information, such amount should be recovered and deposited with the government. Also, if the institution is found guilty, it should be recommended to the All India Council for Technical Education or other competent authority to withdraw the recognition of the course of the institution/ file a case of fraud and such strict action should be taken.
4) The funds should be immediately allocated for the scholarship and the utilization certificate should be submitted to the government.
3. Expenditure for the said scheme" Demand No. W-3, Main Account Head 2203-Technical Education, Technical Education 107, Scholarship (00), (00) (06) Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme, (Programme) 2203 0748" should be shown under the head of account and should be shared from the sanctioned provision in the current financial year.
It is being approved to disburse the pending amount of scholarship amount to the students who have become eligible under this scheme in the academic year 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23 from the fund disbursed under this order. Also, Rs. 499.08 crore, if the balance is released, it should be spent for the scholarship of the beneficiary students for the academic year 2023-24.
5. This Government decision is subject to the Finance Department. It is being issued as per the authority received through Government Circular dated 12/04/2023.
