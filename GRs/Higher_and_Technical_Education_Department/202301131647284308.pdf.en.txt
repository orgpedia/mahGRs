In pursuance of the orders dated 05.03.2020 of the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India, instructions issued vide letter dated 15.03.2022 issued by Pharmacy Council of India and the approval given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 30.06.2022, it was under consideration of the Government to adopt the norms and standards of Pharmacy Council of India for undergraduate courses in Pharmacy in the State and to approve institution-wise and designation-wise configuration of teaching posts in the Non-Government Aided Pharmacy (Degree) Colleges.
Government decision: The Supreme Court passed the judgment in" Transferred Petitions (Civil) Nos. 87-101 of 2014 in Pharmacy Council of India and Dr S K Toshniwal Educational Trusts and Others etc. " In. In pursuance of the orders dated 05.03.2020 and instructions received from Pharmacy Council of India vide letter dated 15th March, 2022, the following decisions are being taken:
The institutions running Diploma/ Degree/ Post-Graduate courses in Pharmacy in the State shall follow the standards and norms prescribed by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI).
In accordance with the norms and standards of the Pharmacy Council of India, recognition of teaching posts in the Non-Government Aided Pharmacy (Degree) Colleges under the Directorate of Technical Education in the State is being granted as per the forms attached with the revised pattern.
These orders have been issued with the concurrence of the Finance Department vide their Unofficial Reference No: 680/ APUK, Dt. Is being issued as agreed under 1/12/2022.
