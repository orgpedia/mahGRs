As per the orders issued in this regard, the posts of Assistant Professor (Group-A), Assistant Professor (Group-A) and Director of Physical Education (Group-A) in Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education have been transferred. The transfer of the officers mentioned in the accompanying statement A of the said orders is hereby cancelled.
Further, the officers mentioned in the accompanying statement B of the said orders are being posted to the posts indicated in column No. 6 in front of their names, with partial changes in their postings.
