2.The said orders shall come into force with immediate effect.
3. The Principal / Director concerned shall immediately relieve the transferred faculty to join the place of transfer. Also, the faculty concerned should immediately transfer from the existing college / institution to the college / institution in which they are posted.
4. The Principal / Director concerned shall appoint the transferred faculty without delay on their retirement from the concerned college / institution. The Principal / Director concerned shall be held liable in case of non-working of salary and allowances as a mandatory waiting period due to non-acquisition of faculty by the Principal / Director concerned. Also, no leave of absence shall be granted to such teachers during the period from discharge to joining the place of transfer.
# Page 2
The period of assumption of office shall be admissible to the transferred faculty as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, non-acceptance of service and Provisions for period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981, as per the extant rules of the Government, for joining the place of transfer.
6. The Director of Higher Education, Pune should ensure that the next month's (June) salary of the transferred teachers is drawn from the transferred college itself.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune, shall submit the performance report on the transfer order to the Government within 15 days.
8. The said Government Order shall be issued by the Government of Maharashtra.
