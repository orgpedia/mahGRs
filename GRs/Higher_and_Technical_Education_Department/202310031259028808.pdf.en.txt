The proposal:
The Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 12/06/2023 has submitted a proposal for the work of cementing and painting of the ground floor to sixth floor living rooms, halls, passages, toilets, staircase in the building of Matoshree Government Hostel (Boys), Churchgate, Mumbai. Executive in charge
The cost estimates have been submitted to the Engineer, City, Public Works Department, Mumbai and the proposed work has been accorded technical approval by the Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Mumbai vide letter dated 02/05/2023. The proposal was under consideration of the government.
