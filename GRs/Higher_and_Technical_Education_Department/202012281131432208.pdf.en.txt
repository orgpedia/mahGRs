The matter of government approval to start a new course M.Pharm Regulatory Affairs (Entry Capacity-15) at Patel Institute of Pharmaceutical Research, Shirpur was under consideration of the government.
Government approval is being accorded to start M.Pharm Regulatory Affairs course at Patel Institute of Pharmaceutical Research, Shirpur with intake capacity of" 15" subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. This recognition shall be on a perpetual basis without subsidy.
The said post-vaccination intake capacity shall be as approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for the academic year 2020-21.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.
The institution's eligibility for this course will remain as mentioned above.
