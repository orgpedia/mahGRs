The L. S. Raheja School of Art, affiliated to the Bombay Suburban Art and Craft Education Society, is an arts college affiliated to the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The Institute had written to the Government vide letter dated 7th February, 2018 requesting to discontinue the existing courses in a phased manner as the courses are to be started as per ICTE norms. The institute is not able to provide adequate space for diploma courses in basic courses, drawing and painting and applied arts. Also, the institute is finding it difficult to run the course on a funded basis. The Honorary Secretary, Bombay Suburban Art and Craft Education Society has informed that there is no objection to transfer the management of L. S. Raheja School of Art, Bandra to any other institution. The teachers, non-teaching staff and teachers' organizations of the college have requested the government from time to time to transfer the present arts college on a non-subsidized basis to a competent arts institution without reversing or closing it.
The students and their parents have also been informed.
In order to avoid academic loss to the students studying in L.S. Raheja Arts College and to protect the service matters of the teaching/ non-teaching staff working in this institution, a detailed proposal was submitted to the Government through a letter dated 2nd July, 2018 from the Director, Directorate of Arts stating that there is no other option but to transfer the college.

The government's decision:
Considering the facts mentioned in the Preamble, the Government has taken the following decisions with regard to transfer and transfer of management of L. S. Raheja Arts College. .
(a) The management of L. S. Raheja School of Art, Bandra, an Aided Art Institute run by Bombay Suburban Art and Craft Education Society, is being transferred to Indian Institute of Rural Reconstruction, Wadala, Mumbai, with sanctioned endowment, sanctioned strength of students and teaching/ non-teaching staff.
Approval is being given to relocate to Knowledge Centre, Nadkarni Park, Near BPT Hospital, Wadala (East), Mumbai- 400 037.
(c) L. S. Raheja School of Art, Bandra. The change to' K. Academy of Art and Design" is being approved.
The transfer and relocation is allowed subject to the following conditions:
(1) The transferor shall give an undertaking that its rights and obligations will cease to exist after the transfer.
2) The institution taking over the management shall give an undertaking that it will be responsible for the management of the concerned Arts College after the proposed transfer.
3) Both the above managing bodies should give an undertaking to the government regarding the transfer.
(4) The Board of Directors, Trustees, Directors and Committees of the old management will not have any right in the affairs of the new management.
5) The new management will be required to run the transferred institution in accordance with government rules.
6) The old management should inform the new management about the names of the students of the institution, the results of the examinations and the fees according to the course.
7) The old management should inform the new management about all the details related to the service of the employees, their provident fund, gratuity, leave, salary, terms and conditions of service, etc.
