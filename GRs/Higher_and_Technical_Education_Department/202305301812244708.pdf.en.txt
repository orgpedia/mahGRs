Regarding the periodic transfers of officers in the post of Junior Advocate (Group-B) in Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education in the year 2023.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Order No: Transfer-2023/ PR No.85/ Masi-1
Ministry Extension, Madame Cama Road
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: 30th of May, 2023.
the order
The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006. In accordance with the provisions of Section 3 (1) of the said Act, the officers in the accompanying returns in the post of Junior Resident (Group-B) in Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education, with the approval of the competent authority for transfer, are being transferred and posted to the post indicated in column No. 5 against their names:
2. The concerned Principal/ Director shall relieve the transferred officers immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned officers should immediately join the college/ institution where they have been transferred from the existing college/ institution.
3. The Principal/ Director concerned shall make the transferred officers join without any further delay after they are relieved from the respective college/ institution. The Principal/ Director concerned will be held responsible in case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of the officer by the concerned Principal/ Director. Also, no leave will be granted to the concerned officers during the period from the date of discharge to the place of posting.
The concerned head of the office shall take action to relieve the officers transferred by this order by June 15, 2023. Also, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers to ensure that the salary of the transferred officers from July 1, 2023, will not be withdrawn from the office before the transfer.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune shall submit an executive report to the Government by 25th June, 2023.
