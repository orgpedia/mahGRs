GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: POLICY-3011/ (175/19)/ TANSHI-2
The government's decision:
As per the provisions of the notification dated 16.07.2019 of the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, a person holding the degree of' Pharm-D' shall be deemed to be qualified as a person possessing higher qualification for all the posts related to the course of Pharmacy in the educational institutions under the control of the Directorate of Technical Education in the State for which educational qualification is required to have passed the course of Diploma in Pharmacy/ Bachelor of Pharmacy/ Master of Pharmacy. Form for appointment of candidates holding educational qualification of B.Pharm./ M.Pharm. for the posts under the Directorate. D. Candidates possessing educational qualification will be eligible.
The Directorate of Technical Education will submit a proposal to the Government to revise the service admission rules for all the posts related to the pharmacy course in the educational institutions under the control of the Directorate of Technical Education in the state for which educational qualification is required to pass the Diploma in Pharmacy/ Bachelor of Pharmacy/ M-Pharm course.
The Government shall take action as mentioned in point no. 1 of this Order during the period till the said Rules of Entry into Service are amended and notified.
