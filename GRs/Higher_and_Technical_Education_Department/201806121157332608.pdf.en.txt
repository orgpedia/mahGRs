Preamble: In pursuance of the list received by the Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide email dated 14.05.2018 from All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi regarding recognition of new institutions of professional courses in the State from the academic year 2018-19, the Director, Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government vide Reference letter. As per the provisions of Approval Process Handbook 2018-19 of All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, there is a provision to start Diploma in Pharmacy in Degree Institute of Pharmacy and Degree Course in Pharmacy in Diploma Institute of Pharmacy. As per Annexure A No. 2, the institution concerned is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi to start diploma courses along with the degree course in Pharmacy and their application ID is also the same, so the decision of the government to approve this institution must be the same. Therefore, the diploma courses in this new institution have been included in the accompanying Annexure A. The Government was contemplating to issue a Government Resolution for grant of Government approval for establishment of new Diploma and Degree Courses respectively to the 2 institutions mentioned in Annexure A.

GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Director, Technical Education, as proposed under the aforesaid Reference letter, hereby grants approval to the 2 institutions mentioned in Annexure A to start new Pharmacy Degree Courses as indicated against their names and to the 2 institutions mentioned in Annexure A to start Diploma Courses along with Pharmacy Degree Courses from the academic year 2018-19 subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The institution's eligibility for admission to this course will be as mentioned in Annexure A.
3. The institution shall have to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
For the affiliation of the said courses, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
The institution concerned will have to take affiliation of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education for diploma courses in pharmacy.
In view of the notification dated 10/12/2014 issued by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi, the newly recognized institutions of Diploma and Degree courses in Pharmacy shall not admit students unless they obtain the requisite approval of the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi as per the provisions of Section 12 of the Pharmacy Act, 1948.
The Bachelor of Pharmacy (B. In Chapter-|| of the Pharm) Course Regulation, 2014, No. 9 The following provisions shall apply.
Person, institution, society, trust or university shall start and conduct B. Pharm Program without the prior approval of the Pharmacy Council of India. "
