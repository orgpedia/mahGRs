The proposal:
The office of the Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education), Jalgaon Division, Jalgaon does not have its own building and is presently functioning in a rented building. 6478 of Jalgaon S.No. space has been provided. Accordingly, the Director, Higher Education, Pune vide letter dated 15/12/2022 has submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining administrative approval for construction of new building for the proposed office. The proposal had been approved by the government.
The government's decision:
The Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining administrative approval for the construction of a new building for the office of the Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education), Jalgaon Division, Jalgaon. The Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nashik has given technical consent to the construction budget. Accordingly, for the construction of new building for the office of Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education), Jalgaon Division, Jalgaon, as per the revised tariff schedule of 2022-23, Rs. The estimated cost of Rs. 7,30,00, 000/- (Rupees Seven Crores Thirty Lakhs only) is being accorded administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
Conditions: 1. Before starting the work, the ownership of the land should be fulfilled.
2. During the period of completion of the construction work, the work should be taken up by taking further action according to the detailed budget after proper planning regarding the ancillary provisions that have been assumed in a rough manner.
Before starting the work, this budget should be attested by the concerned Superintending Engineer and Chief Engineer of the Public Works Department as well as the competent officer of the Consumer Department.
4. The competent authority of the electricity department should be informed about the electrification of the building within the period of construction and complete the electrification work.
