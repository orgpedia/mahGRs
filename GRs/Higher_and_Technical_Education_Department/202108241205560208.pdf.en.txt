The Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award for the years 2018 to 2020 was under consideration of the Government for conferring Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award to Maharashtra dignitaries who have done great work in the field of Sanskrit language such as promotion, dissemination, research, publication, study, teaching and other mentions of Sanskrit.
The Government has decided to confer Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Puraskar for the year 2018-2020 on the following awardees as per the selection of the Award Committee at the Government level regarding the announcement of Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Puraskar for the year 2018-2020:
