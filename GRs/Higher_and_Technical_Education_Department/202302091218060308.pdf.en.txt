Preamble:- The policy regarding the establishment of a chair in the name of eminent personalities in various non-agricultural universities in Maharashtra has been prepared in Reference No. 1 here. This was decided at the Cabinet meeting. Read as follows. 2 here. The said policy has been approved by the Government vide Government Resolution dated 07.06.2022. Accordingly, the University had submitted a proposal to the Government to establish a Chair in the University named after Ahilyadevi Holkar to conduct research on her life and work, to undertake various activities keeping in view the contemporary relevance of her work and to collect and preserve historical documents, texts, crisp literature, rare objects etc. related to her. Following this proposal, After considering the approval of the Cabinet, read No. 3 here. As per the Government decision dated 27.06.2022, administrative approval has been accorded for the establishment of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Adhyasan Kendra at Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University, Solapur. As per the sanction of Rs. 22,54,000/- to the Fund. 4 The decision has been made.
The funds were budgeted through Supplementary Demands for Grants to provide the remaining funds for this Chair Centre. The Department of Finance has agreed to allocate the budgeted funds to the Centre. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds to the Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Adhyasan Kendra was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- The Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Chair Centre at Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University, Solapur has been sanctioned Rs. 2,77,46, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs Forty Six Thousand only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
(1) No new post shall be created for the said Centre.
2. The subsidy received from the Government shall be invested in the fixed deposits of the nationalised bank and the interest received from this amount shall be utilised for the activities of the Foundation.

No additional funds will be provided to the University. The university should provide more funds.
4. The income of the Foundation shall be raised from the interest received on fixed deposits. This will be supplemented by revenues generated from fees for courses of study and income generated from running training programmes and mentoring/ counseling projects.
It shall be the responsibility of the University to submit the information of the expenditure incurred for the course to the Government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune from time to time.
6. The funds are being placed at the disposal of the Director, Higher Education for disbursement to the University.
Every 2 years, the Adhyasan Kendra will be inspected and a report in this regard will be submitted to the government by the Director of Higher Education, Pune. If the report indicates negative progress of the centres in terms of quality of teaching, research performance, number of students, teaching and training classes conducted, the centres will be closed and the university will be obliged to transfer the funds to the government.
2. The expenditure should be met from the funds available in the financial year 2022-23 under" Demand No-W-2, Principal Title-2202-General Education, 03-University and Higher Education, 102-Assistance to Universities,- (00) (17) Development of New University at Solapur (Mandatory) (2202 3521), 31 Subsidiary Grants."
