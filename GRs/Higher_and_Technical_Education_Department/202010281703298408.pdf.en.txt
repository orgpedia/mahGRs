The Principal and the Head of the Department who have been transferred for administrative reasons shall be entitled to the period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of the Government. The Principal and Head of the Department, who are transferred on request, will not be allowed to hold office for the period of the appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance.
The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
(2) On receipt of the transfer order, the concerned Principal and Head of the Department should immediately join the institution from which they are presently working. The principals of the institutions should immediately relieve the transferred principal and head of the department without waiting for the appearance of the alternate principal and head of the department.
3) The transferred principals and heads of departments should be recruited without delay after they are relieved from their respective institutions and come to join. without a principal or head of department.

The principal of the institution concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of payment of salary and allowances without work due to forced waiting time.
4) No leave of the transferred Principal or Head of Department should be sanctioned without prior approval of the Administrative Department.
5) It will be the responsibility of the respective institution heads personally to relieve the transferred principals and department heads immediately. If the principal does not relieve the concerned, the director, technical education, should initiate administrative action against them and submit the proposal to the government for approval as needed. Further, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education shall submit the compliance report in this regard to this Department within 08 days from the date of this order without fail.
