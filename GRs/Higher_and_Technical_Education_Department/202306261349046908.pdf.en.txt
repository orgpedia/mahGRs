Introduction: Reference No. 1) As per the Government Memorandum dated 21/03/2023, the office of Directorate of Higher Education, Pune has been provided with the building which will be vacant due to the shifting of the offices of Technical Education Divisional Office, Pune and Maharashtra State Technical Board, Divisional Office, Pune. Before shifting the office to this building, it is necessary to make arrangements for the meeting of officers/ staff, construction of a record room, electrification, Internet LAN cable, interior and exterior painting of the building and minor staining. Director of Higher Education, Pune. The Executive Engineer, Public Works Building Department, Pune vide letter dated 24/05/2023 and 29/05/2023 has prepared the budget for the renovation and electrification of the building and submitted the proposal to the Government for administrative approval. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government Decision: The Director, Higher Education, Pune has sanctioned Rs. 2,10,44, 739/- (Construction and Furniture works Rs. 1, 56, 82, 183/+ Electrical Works Rs. 53,62,556/-) has been proposed for administrative approval. The project has been approved by the Chief Engineer (Civil/ Electrical), Regional Department of Public Works, Pune. Accordingly, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 2,10,44, 739/- (Rupees Two Crore Ten Lakh Forty Four Thousand Seven Hundred and Nineteen only).
