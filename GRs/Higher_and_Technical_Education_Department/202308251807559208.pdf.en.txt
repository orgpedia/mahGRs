The government's decision:
A 14-member Steering Committee under the Chairmanship of Dr. Nitin Karmalkar, Former Vice-Chancellor, Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune has been constituted vide Government Resolution dated 26.12.2023 to suggest measures and provide guidance for redressal of grievances arising out of the review of the implementation of the recommendations of the report of the Sub-Committees constituted in pursuance of the National Education Policy, 2020 in the State. Also, the reference. As per the Government decision dated 26.04.2023, three new expert members have been added to the Steering Committee.
Pursuant to the letter dated 14th August, 2023. As requested by the Director of Education, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune, the following members are being included in the said Steering Committee.
