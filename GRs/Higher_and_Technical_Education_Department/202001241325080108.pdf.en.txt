Preamble: Various types of committees have been constituted by the state government under the above mentioned government decisions to study various issues and submit a report to the government. Apart from the executive members, non-executive members have also been appointed to these committees. Among them, the government was considering to cancel the appointment of non-official members appointed to the committees formed by the state government. Following this, the government took decision:
The government's decision:
The appointments of the non-official members indicated in the following table to the various Committees constituted under the Government decision referred to above are hereby cancelled:
