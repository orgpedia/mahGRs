Preamble- As per the Government decision dated 30.3.2021 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti has been reconstituted. However, the post has been lying vacant due to the death of member secretary Dr Krishna Kamble. The matter was under consideration of the government to fill the vacant post.
Government Decision: Vacant Post of Member Secretary of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti
The government has approved the appointment of Pradeep N. Agalave. There is no change in the Chairman/ Vice-Chairman/ Invitee/ Member appointed on Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti as per the Government decision dated 30.03.2021 of the Department.
