The proposal:
The 33rd meeting of the Governing Council of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education held on 23/02/2021 discussed the proposal regarding construction of Board building in the premises of Government Institute of Technology, Mumbai. After this discussion, the work of constructing a separate building for the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Fee Regulatory Authority, State Common Entrance Test Cell and Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University in the premises of Government Technological University, Mumbai should be taken up with the financial participation of all the beneficiaries. Minister (Higher and Technical Education) and Chairman, Governing Council, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education. The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE) has sent a proposal in this regard to the state government.
The government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Fee Regulatory Authority, State Common Entrance Test Cell and Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University will construct a separate building in the premises of Government Technological University, Mumbai.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
(9) The aforesaid Committee shall determine the modalities for development and utilization of the space within the premises of Government Technological Institute, Mumbai, taking into account the requirements of the future administrative office spaces of the beneficiary institutions.
(2) The construction of the separate building shall be undertaken with the financial participation of all the concerned beneficiary institutions. Therefore, the committee should determine the space required by each of the systems participating in the present independent building, taking into account their future needs, and the contribution of funds of each system to the total project cost.
(3) The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within a period of three months.
