The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has issued its Reference No. Letters 1 to 3 are accepted. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide their aforesaid Reference No. 4 The proposal has been sent to the government. As per the proposal, the Government was considering to approve the change in the name of the institutions of the existing Diploma courses from the academic year 2020-21.
