Introduction: Mumbai University had conducted the recruitment process for the posts of Deputy Registrar and Assistant Registrar from 2009 to 2013. For a thorough investigation of this recruitment process, Reference No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 28th August, 2023, a three-tier committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Prof. Shri R.S. Mali, former Vice-Chancellor. The committee has been asked to submit its report within a month's time. However, the report is yet to be submitted to the government.
" Additional information and documents are required from the University of Mumbai. Also, the case is complicated and requires a lot of paperwork. The Member Secretary of the Committee has requested for extension of time for submission of the report vide Reference Letter No. 2 has been made. Accordingly, the matter of extension of the said Inquiry Committee was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Mumbai University is extending the date for submission of enquiry report to the above said Inquiry Committee constituted to inquire into the recruitment process for the posts of Deputy Registrar and Assistant Registrar from 2009 to 2013 till 15th February, 2024. The committee has to submit its report to the government by February 15, 2024.
