Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg
Ministry, Mumbai: 400032.
The date: April 12, 2021.
The proposal:
The revised guidelines for making provisions for establishment of self-financing universities in the State, regulation of their functioning and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto have been issued vide Government Decision dated 19th January, 2019. As per the provisions of the said guidelines, a self-financing university is established by a separate Act. The self-financed university functions under the Act.
Self-financed universities set up in the state are facing problems in academic functioning and since the self-financed universities have been repeatedly demanding the government to solve these problems, the government was considering setting up a committee to study the problems faced by self-financed universities and suggest solutions.
Government Decision: As mentioned in the Preamble, it has come to the notice of the Government that self-financing universities are facing difficulties in carrying out their academic activities. A committee has been formed to look into the issues and suggest solutions.

Functions of the Committee:
The committee shall study all the sections of the Act of all self-financing universities existing at present.
2. Self-financing universities face difficulties in complying with the provisions of any of the Articles of University Regulation.
3) Study the problems faced by the teachers and students of all self-financing universities in the state.
Steps should be suggested on how to solve the problems of all self-financing universities established in the state.
The committee has to submit its report to the government within three months.
