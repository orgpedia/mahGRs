The proposal:
All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) As per the notification dated 1/3/2019 issued by the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. The revised pay structure of the 7th Pay Commission has been made applicable to the posts of teachers and teacher equivalents in Government and Non-Government aided Engineering Degree and Diploma Institutions as per the Government decision dated 11th September, 2019 (two separate). However, in this regard, vide letter dated 25th November, 2020 addressed to the Directorate of Accounts, Treasury, it has been brought to the notice that in view of the pay structure of the teaching posts in the Directorate of Technical Education, the salary of the said employee has been increased from Rs. The Pay Matrix as mentioned in Annexure-1 to the Government Decision dated 11th September, 2019 has reached the extreme cell. Therefore, in addition to the above decisions of the Government, the Directorate of Accounts, Directorate of Higher and Technical Education, Department of Higher and Technical Education, in agreement with the Department of Finance, has been asked to issue a corrigendum to increase the maximum number of cells in Cell no/ Level-13 A1 and 14 in the Pay Matrix for Degree Institutions and Cell no/ Level-12, 13 A1 and 14 in the Pay Matrix for Diploma Institutions to be registered in the e-Services system. According to this, the matter of amending the government decision here in the context of the department was under the consideration of the government.
Cleaning paper:
In the above background, the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government of India has decided to implement the revised pay structure of the 7th Pay Commission for the posts of teachers and teachers equivalent in Government and Non-Government aided Engineering Degree and Diploma Institutions. The Annexure-1 attached to the (two separate) Government Decision dated 11th September, 2019 is hereby repealed and further action as per the amended Annexure-1 attached to the said Corrigendum shall be taken.
