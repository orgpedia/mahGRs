The provision of temporary seniority has been made in accordance with the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021.
The Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is hereby informed to bring the said temporary seniority to the notice of all the concerned authorities.
If there are any suggestions and objections regarding the seniority list, the concerned officers should submit a written statement regarding it to the Director of the Library within 15 days from the date of issuance of this circular, after which the Director of the Library should submit the relevant statement to the Government with their feedback and revised proposal within 7 days.
