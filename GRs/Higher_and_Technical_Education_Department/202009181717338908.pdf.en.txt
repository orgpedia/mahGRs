The proposal:
2.10 of APH (Approval Process Handbook) 2020-21 published by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi provides for merger of institutes running under the same management. The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi has approved the merger of Diploma, Degree and Post Graduate Diploma courses in Engineering and Management in Form A. Accordingly, the Director, Technical Education vide 2 separate letters dated 14/7/2020 and 04/09/2020 at No.3 mentioned above has requested the Government to approve the merger of these institutions.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the merger of the institutions as mentioned in the accompanying Form A is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. The approval shall be on a perpetual basis without subsidy.
The said post-vaccination intake capacity shall be as approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for the academic year 2020-21.
3. The above approval is subject to payment of processing fee by the institution.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.
The institution's eligibility for this course will remain as mentioned above.
