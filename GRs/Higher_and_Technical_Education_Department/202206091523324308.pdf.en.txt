Introduction: Presenting the State Budget for the financial year 2022-23, Shri. On the 11th of March 2022. As per para 48 of the Budget Speech of the Deputy Chief Minister and Finance Minister titled" Renovation of various colleges affiliated to the Department of Higher and Technical Education,"" Sydenham College of Commerce and Economics, Mumbai has been allotted Rs. Rs. 5.00 crore will be provided. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the necessary renovation work in the said college was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
Principal, Sydenham College of Commerce and Economics, Mumbai
As per the revised rates for the year 2021-22, Rs. 4,99,15, 579/- (Construction works Rs. 3,09,00, 000/- and electrical works Rs. 1,90,15, 579/-) towards administrative expenses.
A proposal has been submitted for approval. Chief Engineer of the project
(Architecture/ Electrical), Technical Approval has been granted by the Public Works Department, Mumbai. In accordance with this
The Sydenham College of Commerce and Economics, Mumbai, has been allocated Rs. 4,99,15, 579/- (Rupees Four Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Fifteen Thousand Five Hundred and Nineteen Only)
Administrative approval is being given to the budget of this amount.

The above approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
1.The construction work should be taken up only after making a detailed plan regarding the incidental provisions that have been assumed in a rough form during the completion of the construction work.
Before starting the work, this budget should be attested by the concerned Superintending Engineer and Chief Engineer of the Public Works Department as well as the competent officer of the Consumer Department.
Special care should be taken to ensure that no revised administrative approval is required for this work.
The Department of Environment, while carrying out the actual work, should take action according to the guidelines mentioned in the Government Decision, No. ENV-2013/ Q. No. 177/ T. No. 1, dated January 10, 2014.
The issues related to the said work should be dealt with in accordance with the Government Decision No. BDG-2017/ PR No. 60/ Buildings-2 of the Public Works Department, dated 07.07.2017.
6. Renovation work should not be taken up unless provision is made in the budget for the said work.
