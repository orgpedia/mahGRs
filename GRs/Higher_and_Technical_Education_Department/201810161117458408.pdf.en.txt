Preamble:
The World Bank Assisted Project, Technical Education Quality Improvement Program (TEQIP) is being implemented in the State since 2002-03. State has performed very well in the 1st and 2nd phase of the TEQIP. TEQIP-III project, which is 100% funded by the Central Government, is being implemented in the country for Focus States (Low Income States) and Non-Focus States(Special Category) from 1st April 2017. NPIU has selected following 12 institutes/Universities (under Sub-component 1.3: Twinning Arrangements to Build Capacity and Improve Performance of Participating Institutions and ATUs) from the State for participation in the 3rd phase of TEQIP.
1. College of Engineering, Pune (COEP) 2. Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad 3. Government College of Engineering, Karad 4. Government College of Engineering, Jalgaon 5. Veermata Jijabai Technology Institute, Mumbai 6. Institute of Chemical Technology, Mumbai 7. Sardar Patel College of Engineering, Mumbai 8. Shri Guru Govind Singh Institute of Engineering and Technology, Nanded 9. Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli 10. Department of Technology, Shivaji University, Kolhapur 11. University Department of Chemical Technology, North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon 12. Dr.Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere (Affiliating State Technological University).
As suggested by the National Project Implementation Unit (NPIU), New Delhi, vide email dated 19/07/2018, formation of State Project Implementation Unit (SPIU) was under consideration of the Government.
Resolution -
1. Now the Government hereby approves State Project Implementation Unit (SPIU) for implementation and monitoring of TEQIP-III project.
2. As recommended by the NPIU, SPIU of Non-focus States will work under the
guidance/ supervision of Additional Chief Secretary/Principal Secretary/Secretary, Higher and
Technical Education Department and Director, Directorate of Technical Education (DTE).
3. In view of this, following academicians and officials with present and project
designations are nominated in SPIU :
