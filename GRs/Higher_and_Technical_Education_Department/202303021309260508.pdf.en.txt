Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 14.09.2022 in Reference No. 1, 1656 full time and 07 part time temporary posts in the establishment of Directorate of Higher Education have been approved to be continued from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023. As per the Government decision dated 08.02.2023 of the Finance Department in Reference No. 2, the Administrative Departments have been empowered to extend the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 for the temporary posts included in their rolls as well as for all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the earlier review but not included in the rolls. The Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter No. 3 has submitted a proposal for extension of tenure of 1656 full time and 07 part time temporary posts in the establishment of Directorate of Higher Education from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning the continuation of these temporary posts till 31.08.2023 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the proposal submitted by the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter No. 3 dated 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023, the following 1656 full-time and 07 part-time posts in the Directorate of Higher Education and its subordinate Government Offices/ Government Colleges/ Institutions/ Government Teachers Colleges/ Economically Backward Classes Hostels/ Indian Administrative Service Pre-Training Centres/ Savitri Devi Phule Women Hostels, Telangana Memorial Girls Hostels/ Government College Hostels/ Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jyotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti, Mumbai are being continued.
