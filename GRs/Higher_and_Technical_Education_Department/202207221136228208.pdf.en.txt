Preamble: As per the instructions of the Central Government under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav," detailed instructions regarding the implementation of the" Har Ghar Tiranga" initiative have been given in the Government Circular in the above text. Accordingly, instructions are being issued accordingly regarding hoisting of the National Flag at Government/ Semi-Government offices, educational institutions, hostels, residences, etc. buildings under the Department of Higher and Technical Education under the" Har Ghar Tiranga" initiative.
Government Circular: As per the instructions of the Central Government, the initiative of" Har Ghar Tiranga" (House to House Tiranga) has been launched. It will be implemented from August 13 to August 15, 2022. All the concerned officers should ensure that the national flag is hoisted on all government/ semi-government offices, all universities, colleges, hostels, residences, etc. buildings under the Higher and Technical Education Department. While implementing this initiative, it should mainly include the following: 1) The tagline" Tiranga at home" and the picture of the tricolor should be displayed on the front of the website of every department/ sub-department, university/ college, office.
2) All government/ semi-government officials/ employees/ teachers/ non-teaching staff/ students/ parents to display pictures, messages on the tricolor through social media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, WhatsApp, etc.).
3) Under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, morning rounds should be organized by college students to publicize, spread and create awareness/ awareness among parents and citizens about this initiative. It can be organized on a large scale by involving volunteers of the National Service Scheme.
4) All officers/ employees should take the initiative to hoist the tricolor on government/ semi-government buildings as well as private buildings or residential houses. This will help create awareness among other citizens.
5) The Department of Cultural Affairs has produced jingles, songs, audio-video, etc. for the awareness campaign of this initiative. For more information, visit https:// mahamrut. gov. in/ The website is available at www. org. These jingles, songs, posters, etc. should be used by the government officials/ employees as well as the general public for the promotion and dissemination of this initiative.

6) For this initiative, instructions have already been issued from the government level to make the national flag available through the revenue department as well as other departments up to the taluka/ village level.
The national flags will be available for purchase by all at the rates assured by them.
7) The process of uploading the photos and videos of the programs being implemented by the department under the" Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" on the website of the Department of Cultural Affairs is underway through the State Liaison Officer, National Service Scheme. Similarly, photographs, videos, etc. of the" Gharo Gharo Tiranga" program will be uploaded on the website on a large scale by the State Liaison Officer, R. SEOs should form a special team and take action to upload it through them. So that maximum information about the initiative in the state reaches the country level.
8) While implementing all the above activities, it is necessary to follow the Flag Code of India and understand that everyone needs to take care that the national flag is not insulted inadvertently. Therefore, it is necessary to be aware of this.
The registrar of all the universities should give instructions to all the colleges affiliated to their university regarding this initiative.
