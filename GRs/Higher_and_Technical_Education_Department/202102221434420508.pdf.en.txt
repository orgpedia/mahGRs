As per the decision of the government, a committee has been formed to improve the training centers at the State Administrative Vocational Education Institute, Mumbai and the Indian Administrative Service Pre-Training Center, Nagpur, Amravati, Nashik, Aurangabad and Kolhapur. The issue of reforming the committee was under the consideration of the government.
The state government has decided to reform the committee formed to improve the training centers of the State Administrative Vocational Education Institute, Mumbai and the Indian Administrative Service Pre-Training Center, Nagpur, Amravati, Nashik, Aurangabad and Kolhapur.
Accordingly, Dr. Abhay Wagh, Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being included in the committee as a member. Also, Dr. Pramod Lakhe, Associate Professor, Vasantrao Naik Government Arts and Science Institute, Nagpur is being included in the committee as Member Secretary instead of Director, State Institute of Administrative Business Education, Mumbai.
(3) The functions and other provisions of the Committee shall be as provided in the relevant Government Decision.
