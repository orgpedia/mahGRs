The proposal:
The Director, Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government under the above mentioned letter to start a 3-year Diploma course in Vocational-Jewellery Design under the Community College in Government Technological Institute, Kolhapur from the academic year 2019-20, as approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. In pursuance of the said letter, the Government was considering to grant approval to start a 3-year Diploma course in Vocational-Jewellery Design under the Community College in Government Technological Institute, Kolhapur.
The government's decision:
In accordance with the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide its letter No. 1, 2 and 3 dated 01.04.2021, the following conditions and conditions are being accorded for the three years Diploma Course in Vocational Jewellery Design (NSQF Level 3 to Level 5) under the Community College at Government Technological College, Kolhapur with an intake capacity of 30 from the academic year 2021-22.
