The government's decision:
As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2 of the Public Health Department, it has been decided to amend the power of sanction and increase the ceiling limit of reimbursement of medical expenses incurred by teaching/ non-teaching staff and their families working in non-government aided colleges under the Directorate of Higher Education as follows:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| the right                      | the right of the former  | The amended rights   |
| the Divisional Joint Director  | Rs 40,000/-              | Up to Rs. 1,50,000/- |
| the Director, Higher Education | Rs.40,001/- to 3,00,000/ | Rs. 3,00,000/        |
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Head of the Administrative Department shall have full powers to approve the cases of reimbursement of medical expenses above Rs. 3,00,000/- subject to the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and Government Decisions issued from time to time thereafter.
The approval of cases for reimbursement of medical expenses at the level of Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State and the concerned Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education) as mentioned above shall be accorded after due scrutiny in strict compliance with the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Maintenance) Rules, 1961 and the Government Decisions issued from time to time.
The said Government decision shall also apply to the cases which are pending as on the date of the decision.
The said order is issued vide Government Resolution No.2, Public Health Department, No.Vaipr-2011/ PR No. 16/2016/ RACVI-2D. The limit is being released as on 16th March, 2016.
