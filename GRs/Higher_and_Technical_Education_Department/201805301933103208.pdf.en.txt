(2) The period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to the teachers who have been transferred as per the rules of the Government. As per the government rules, the transfer of teachers will not be allowed during the tenure of the post, travel allowance and daily allowance.
3. The transferred officers should be relieved immediately by the respective heads of the institutions as the said transfer order needs to be effectively implemented. This should be followed up by the Joint Director of Technical Education of the concerned department. Further, a compliance report in this regard should be submitted to the government through the Director, Technical Education within 1 month from the date of this order.
The salary of the concerned officer up to the date of retirement or up to the maximum date of 30/06/2018 shall be drawn by the organization in which he is presently working. The salary from 01/07/2018 should not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer organization under any circumstances, the salary should be withdrawn by the post-transfer organization after the concerned officer joins the transfer organization. These officers will be held personally liable if the salary due to them from 01/07/2018 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the organisation where they were working before the transfer.
The transferred officer should not go on unauthorised leave. He also focused on General Administration Department No. CDR- 1082/2567/28/ XI, Dy. Referred to Circular dated 30/8/1982. In view of the instructions given in it, they should note that if any form of application is submitted to cancel the transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
