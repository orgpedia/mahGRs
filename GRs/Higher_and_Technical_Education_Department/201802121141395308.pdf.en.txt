Due to the increase in the number of colleges affiliated to the university as well as the number of students, it is becoming difficult to complete the academic and administrative work of the university within the stipulated time. The newly created Gondwana University is located in Gadchiroli and the jurisdiction of the university extends over Gadchiroli and Chandrapur districts. The university has jurisdiction over tribal and Naxal-affected areas, most of which are inhabited by tribals and backward classes. Hence, quality education should be provided to the students.
The University must have 12 (B) status to receive grants from the University Grants Commission. For this, at least 5 academic departments of the university must be fully developed.
