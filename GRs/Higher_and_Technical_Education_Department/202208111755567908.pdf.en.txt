The proposal:
The State Government has decided to commemorate the Partition Anniversary every year as" Partition Vibhishika Smriti Din" to pay homage and salute the people who lost their lives during the Partition and those who were displaced from their native villages/ places. Accordingly, the following instructions are being issued to all the Universities/ Colleges under the Department of Higher and Technical Education.
Government Circular: On the occasion of Partition Horrors Remembrance Day, to ensure that the present and future generations remember the pain and suffering of the citizens during the Partition, the date of 14th August is observed as Partition Horrors Remembrance Day with appropriate events (e.g. Speakers' speeches, symposiums, felicitation meetings, etc.) should be planned in the university and college so that the tradition of sacrifice is remembered forever.
