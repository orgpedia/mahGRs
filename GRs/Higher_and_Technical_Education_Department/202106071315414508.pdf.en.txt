The proposal:
The University Grants Commission (UGC) vide its notification dated 18.7.2018 has prescribed the criteria for appointment to the post of Teachers and equivalent in Central Universities and Colleges and Institutions, Educational Qualification etc. In addition, the modalities for payment of pay scales, collateral benefits, incentive pay hikes etc. to teachers have been prescribed. The decision to apply the provisions of this notification to the posts of Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Professor, Director of College Physical Education in the state has been taken under the above reference No. 2 decision has been made by the government. Accordingly, the No. Under the decision of the government here, a screening committee and a selection committee have been formed to ensure the placement of teachers in government colleges/ institutions in the state under the Career Advancement Scheme. The government was considering the formation of a new selection committee by amending the selection committee formed to determine the position of associate professor/ professor.
The government's decision:
