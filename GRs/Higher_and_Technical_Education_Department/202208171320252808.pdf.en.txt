As per the procedure laid down under the Government Decision dated 13.09.2017, the non-agriculture universities in the State have submitted proposals for new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties for the academic year 2022-23 to the Government for approval. The proposals were examined in accordance with the norms laid down under the relevant Government decisions. As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the Government has been empowered to grant approval to the proposals which have been qualified on the basis of the government's scrutiny at the government level in accordance with the provisions of section 109 (4) (d) and under the existing policy of permanent non-granting of grant to the existing college as mentioned in the following statement in view of the demand for higher education and academic urgency in the college premises.
