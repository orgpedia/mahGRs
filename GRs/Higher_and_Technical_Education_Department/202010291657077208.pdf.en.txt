Preamble: A proposal was sent to the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) to close down the ITM College of Engineering, Nagpur, an engineering course institute affiliated to the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) in the state from the academic year 2018-19. Accordingly, the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide its letter dated 02.04.2018, has approved the closure of the said Institute in a phased manner. In pursuance of the above approval, 3) As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, vide letter herein, to close down the ITM College of Engineering, Nagpur in a phased manner and the academic year of the institution
The Government was considering to approve adjustment in the limit of permissible intake capacity of 23 students who have not passed the final year till 2019-20 in the permanent unaided institutions under Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur as per the academic merit and preference of the students.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In pursuance of the approval accorded by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide their aforesaid letter, (iii) As proposed by the Director, Technical Education vide their letter, the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi is pleased to extend the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Government of India, to the State of Telangana w.e.f. 2018-19.
