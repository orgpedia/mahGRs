Regarding non-requisition of original documents by students applying for scholarships on MahaDBT portal.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Circular No. Parichay-2018/ PR No. 278/ Tanshi-4
Mantralaya Extension Building, Madame Cama Road,
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: January 7th, 2019
Circular:
At present, various schemes of scholarships are being implemented through the MahaDBT portal in the state.
The facility of uploading different documents for different schemes of different departments has been provided on the MahaDBT portal. Regarding MahaDBT Portal dated 29th December, 2018
During the review meeting held by the Chief Secretary, it has been pointed out that the officers of some departments are demanding some original documents from the students/ institutions.
It is informed that since the primary responsibility of checking the uploaded documents lies with the institution, the institutions are required to do the same on the basis of various documents submitted by the students at the time of admission. Except in very exceptional circumstances.
No original documents should be demanded by the institutions or departmental offices from any student.
