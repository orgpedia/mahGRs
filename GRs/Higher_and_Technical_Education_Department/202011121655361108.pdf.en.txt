Preamble: As per Rule 27 (1) of the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University Act, 2014, a provision has been made for the constitution of an Executive Council for the University. Accordingly, the State Government, vide letter No. 1 and 2, referred to the Executive Council of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University
Four members were appointed. As the three-year term of the representatives appointed by the government on the executive council of the university has expired, the government has cancelled the appointments of these representatives under letter No. 3 under reference. Therefore, the proposal to appoint government representatives on the executive council of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University Act, 2014 has come into force with effect from 1st March, 2016. The Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University Act, 2014
