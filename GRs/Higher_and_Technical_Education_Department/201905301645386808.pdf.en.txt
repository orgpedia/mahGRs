4. In order to ensure immediate implementation of the transfer order, the concerned Head of Office shall strictly follow the following instructions.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
2.After receiving the transfer order, the concerned office without waiting for the replacement of the transferred administrative officers.
The head should immediately relieve the transferred administrative officers to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned administrative officer should join the office immediately after his transfer from the current office.
3.The transferred administrative officers should be reinstated without delay after they are relieved from the office concerned and come to join. The head of the office concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-appointment of an administrative officer.
(4) No request for change in post will be entertained. If any administrative officer directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order to change the order of transfer.
Administrative officials will be liable for disciplinary action.
5. No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
(6) Since it is necessary to effectively implement these changes and the order is effective immediately, the transferred administrative officers
The salary up to the date of retirement or maximum up to 31st May, 2019 shall be drawn by the office in which he is presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the office before transfer under any circumstances.
This salary should be drawn by the post-transfer office after the concerned administrative officer joins the transfer office. If the salary due from June 01, 2019 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the office where he was working before the transfer, he will be held personally liable.
7. The responsibility of relieving the transferred administrative officers immediately shall be personally borne by the Head of the Department concerned. The Director, Higher Education, Pune shall submit the report to the Government within 7 days from the date of this order.
