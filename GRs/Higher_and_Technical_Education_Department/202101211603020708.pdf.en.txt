1) The grant will be non-recurring.
The course will be of one-year full-time course. The number of seats for each course will be limited to 120.
3) The institute should appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses and the salary and allowances should be given to the employees as per the government rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
4) The institute must be affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education for this short term course. Also, if the courses approved by this Government are decided to be implemented by the All India Council for Technical Education, it will be mandatory for the institution to get the approval of the Council for such courses.
Although the Government has a fee reimbursement policy for professional courses, the fee reimbursement scheme is not applicable to any non-AICTE courses.
Hence, the fee reimbursement scheme for the above courses will not be applicable to any category of students. The fee reimbursement/ concession scheme will also not be applicable to students who are economically backward.
6) The institution will have to sign an agreement on the acceptance of all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as the Technical Education Examination Board.
7) The Director, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai should take an affidavit regarding the appointment of regular teaching and non-teaching staff, providing machinery, infrastructure and up-to-date laboratory and library facilities to the students as per the standards. The concerned Deputy Secretary, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Divisional Office will be responsible for the fulfillment of these requirements.

8) The books, journals required by the library in the institution must be purchased as per the standards of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Bandra, Mumbai. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students.
The Institute will be under the control of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai and the examinations for the respective courses will be conducted by Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Bandra, Mumbai.
10) Admission to these short-term courses will be done as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the relevant course as approved by the Government.
The course approved under the said Government Decisions shall be required to be started by the Institute from the academic year 2020-21. If the institution does not start the course, the recognition given to the institution for the course will be automatically cancelled.
If the institution charges more than the prescribed tuition fee, action will be taken against the institution under the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
13) In the context of ragging. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999 should be strictly implemented.
14) Apart from this, other terms and conditions set by the Director, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education will be applicable to the institute.
The Government shall have the right to modify the above terms and conditions or to insert new terms and conditions to enhance the quality of education.
