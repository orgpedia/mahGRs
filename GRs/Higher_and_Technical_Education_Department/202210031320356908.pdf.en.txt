The proposal:
Telang Memorial Girls Hostel, Mumbai is a five-storeyed building constructed in 1951-52. The building has been under construction for more than 70 years. The hostel will accommodate 250 girls who are pursuing higher education in Mumbai. The hostel needs to be repaired and renovated to provide accommodation facilities to the girl students. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval for the building repair and renovation works of the hostel was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 04/07/2022 has sanctioned Rs. A proposal has been submitted for obtaining administrative approval for a budget of Rs. 1,53,70, 000/-. The project has been approved by the Chief Engineer, Regional Public Works Department, Mumbai. Accordingly, Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 1,53,70, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Fifty Three Lakh Seventy Thousand only) subject to the following conditions:
