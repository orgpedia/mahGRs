Preamble: Shri Bhagwan College of Pharmacy, Aurangabad was de-recognized by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi from the academic year 2018-19 and an order was passed to adjust the students of the institution to an All India Council for Technical Education recognized institution within 30 days. Renuka Sanjay Ambekar and other students protested against this order. Writ Petition No. 11064/2018 filed in the High Court, Bombay, Bench Aurangabad.
The High Court on 02/11/2018 had directed the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, State Government, concerned managements and teaching and non-teaching staff to arrive at a comprehensive solution by the next academic year. The High Court during the hearing on 13/03/2019 had directed the concerned management not to file an application for the continuation of the institution for the academic year 2019-20 so that the government could take effective steps in this regard without waiting for the response of the institution.
