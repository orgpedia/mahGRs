Writ Petition (Stamp) No.938877/ 2020 challenging the Government Order dated 16/3/2020 was filed by Mr. Amol Jagdish Baviskar and others. The case was filed in Bombay High Court. In pursuance of this writ petition, the Government vide its decision dated 16/3/2020 has decided that the decision regarding exclusion of ATMA, XAT, MAT and GMAT for admission to Post Graduate courses in MBA/ MMS should be implemented from the academic year 2021-22 instead of being applicable to the academic year 2020-21. According to the decision of the Hon'ble Bombay High Court, the matter of making a decision regarding admission to MBA/ MMS courses in the academic year 2020-21 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Pursuant to the judgment dated 11/12/2020 passed by the High Court of Bombay in Writ Petition (Stamp) No. 93887/2020, the Government is approving the acceptance of GMAT, MAT, ATMA and XAT in the All India Candidate category for admission to MBA/ MMS courses in the academic year 2020-21 in the State. The above Government decision dated 16/3/2020 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education should be implemented from the academic year 2021-22.
The Commissioner, State Common Entrance Test Cell and the Secretary, Admission Regulatory Authority, Mumbai should take immediate action in this regard.
