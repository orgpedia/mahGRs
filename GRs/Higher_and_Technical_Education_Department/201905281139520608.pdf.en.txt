Letter No.19/ DTE/ Foreign Scholarships/ 2018/32 dated 1st March, 2019 from the Director, Technical Education
Preamble: As per the Government Decision, Higher and Technical Education Department No. 2018/ PR No. 218/ TANSI-4, dated 4th October, 2018, the scheme" Scholarship for meritorious boys and girls for higher education abroad" has been implemented from the academic year 2018-19 for the students who have taken admission in Master's Degree/ Master's Diploma and Ph.D. courses in the universities within THE/ QS Ranking 200 abroad. The Directorate of Technical Education had issued an advertisement on October 8, 2018, inviting online applications under the scheme. The last date for submission of online application is 31st December 2018.
A total of 48 applications were received by the Directorate of Technical Education after examining the documents of the concerned students received by the divisional offices till the deadline. Out of the total 48 applications received, the Screening Committee headed by Director, Technical Education has found 27 applications eligible for foreign scholarship. Accordingly, the Scrutiny Committee has prepared a consolidated merit list of 3 candidates for PhD courses and 24 candidates for Post Graduate Degree/ Diploma courses.
