Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to apply the procedure prescribed under the Government Decision dated 12.02.2013 of the General Administration Department issued under this heading regarding sanction for filing of complaints under the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 to the non-government aided colleges in the State.
