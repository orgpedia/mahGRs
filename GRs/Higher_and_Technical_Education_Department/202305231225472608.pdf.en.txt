Preamble: A Drafting Committee was constituted under the Chairmanship of Joint Director, Department of Higher Education, Mumbai, vide letter dated 13/10/2022, to draw up a list of various types of unusable, non-functional, obsolete, non-repairable, corroded, corroded, corroded, corroded, corroded metal materials and unusable/ damaged furniture etc. in the Laboratory School of Elphinstone College, Mumbai. The proposal for de-recognition has been submitted for approval vide letter dated 05/04/2023 addressed to the Director, Higher Education, pursuant to the report submitted by the De-recognition Committee after conducting physical inspection/ inspection. Accordingly, a proposal to approve the disposal of useless/ waste materials in Elphinstone College, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Director of Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune is approving the de-recording to be done on the basis of the report submitted by the de-recording committee constituted as mentioned in the preamble for the de-recording of useless/ waste materials in the college. The amount collected from the accounts after the due process of the said accounts shall be credited to the accounts of the Government under the head (0202 0349), income from the sale of inventory, waste paper and other items.
