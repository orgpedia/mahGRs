Preamble: Member Secretary, All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. As per the letter dated 09.06.2020, a new course B.F.A. Painting at Viva Institute of Applied Art, Virar, a private unaided institution, with an intake capacity of 30 students, has been approved to start from the academic year 2020-21. The Directorate of Arts has examined the facilities required to start the course of B.F.A. Painting in the Institute and the proposal for the same has been submitted along with the recommendations at Reference No. (4). The letter was dated October 22, 2020. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to grant permission to start the degree course in Drawing and Painting (B.F.A. Painting) at the Viva Institute of Applied Art, Virar, on a permanent basis without subsidy. The decision has been taken in this regard.
Decision of the Government: In accordance with the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide letter dated 09.06.2020 in the aforesaid Reference Number (3), B.F.A.Painting Degree (Under Graduate) in Viva Institute of Applied Art, Virar, a Private Educational Institution
