Preamble: In exercise of the powers conferred by section 58 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 1994, the Government hereby order to constitute the University and College Tribunals for non-agricultural Universities vide Reference No. 1 has been issued by the government here. The said Act has since been amended under the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016. Section 80 of the 2016 Act provides for the appointment of Presiding Officer.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the appointment of a new Presiding Officer to the vacant post of Presiding Officer at Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the decision of the Government, the post of the Presiding Officer of the University and College Tribunal at Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune is vacant.
Retired Justice Md. The High Court of Judicature at Bombay is appointed for a period of three years from the date on which he enters upon his actual office.
