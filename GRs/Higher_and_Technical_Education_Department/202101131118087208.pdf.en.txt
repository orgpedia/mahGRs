As Adarsh College is a constituent college of the concerned University, the teaching and non-teaching staff required for those colleges shall be the employees of that University. Accordingly, for the approval of the posts and office bearers of the teaching and non-teaching staff of Adarsh College, the concerned universities should prepare proposals and submit them to the Director, Higher Education and the Director should examine the proposal of the university and submit it to the government. It shall be the responsibility of the University concerned to appoint teachers and non-teaching staff holding qualifications prescribed by the Government and the University Grants Commission from time to time in the said University Constituent College and only those teachers and non-teaching staff who are qualified and selected in the prescribed manner shall be eligible for grant of salary.
