The admission rules for Diploma in Pharmacy, Hotel Management and Catering Technology, Surface Coating Technology and Direct Second Year Post SSC Diploma Engineering Courses have been approved vide Government Decision dated 17th May, 2019.
However, in view of the outbreak of COVID-19 and the consequent lockdown, the schedule announced by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, for the Centralized Admission Process, as well as the measures to contain the spread of COVID-19, requires adherence to the important instructions of maintaining social distancing, avoiding crowding of students and parents. Accordingly, the Directorate of Technical Education vide its letter dated 12th May, 2020 has proposed to make necessary changes in the Admission Rules of the Government approved Diploma Courses for the academic year 2020-21. The government was considering revising the admission rules for Diploma in Pharmacy, Hotel Management and Catering Technology, Surface Coating Technology and direct second year Post SSC Diploma Engineering courses.
