Preamble: The Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi has approved the introduction of new unaided diploma courses in the existing unaided degree pharmacy course institutions in the State from the academic year 2022-23 and change in the name of existing unaided diploma institutions in pharmacy.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval given by Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi, the institutions mentioned in attached Form A and Form B are being granted approval by the Government to start new unaided diploma courses in the existing unaided degree institution of pharmacy course respectively and to change the name of the institutions of existing unaided diploma courses of pharmacy course from the academic year 2022-23 subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.
In this case, the institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education/ Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi as well as under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.
The intake capacity of the institution for the said course shall be limited as mentioned against their names in the prospectus.
4. The institution shall have to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education/ Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi.
The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
The concerned institution must be affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education for these diploma courses.
The Principals of the newly approved Diploma in Pharmacy course will be required to obtain the approval of the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi before admitting the students. In the absence of this approval, if some court cases arise in the future, the relevant institution in this case is fully
