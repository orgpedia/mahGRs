Read: Letter from Director, Technical Education No. 2 / PCI-approved / 2024-25/1004, d. 19.12.2024.
Preamble: The Director, Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government under the above mentioned letter for Government approval to merge the Institute of Diploma Courses with the existing Institute of Pharmacy Degree Courses with effect from the academic year 2024-25 as approved by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. Accordingly, the issue of government approval was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval given by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi and as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, Government approval is being accorded for the amalgamation of the Institute of Diploma Courses with the existing Institute of Degree Courses as indicated in the accompanying Form-A from the academic year 2024-25 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: - 1) The approval shall be on a non-grant basis for the time being.
2) As prescribed by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi in the present case, as well as the The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed under the Government Decision dated 21st May, 1983.
3) The Institute shall enter into an agreement with the Director, Technical Education regarding acceptance of all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. Also, the required documents and formalities will be complied with satisfactorily with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra.
4) The institution concerned should meet other legal requirements by depositing the required accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education.
5) Admissions to the said course shall be made as per the rules / procedures for admission to the concerned course as approved by the Government.
6) If the fee charged by the concerned institutions is more than the tuition fee prescribed by the fee regulatory authority, action will be taken against those institutions as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation) Act, 1987.

7) The Institute shall appoint teachers, non-teaching and other technical staff to run the course as per the norms of Pharmacy Council of India, Nabi Delhi. They should be paid salaries and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institute if complaints are received from teachers and non-teaching staff as well as students against the institute to the government regarding salaries and allowances, etc.
8) The institution must purchase the required books, journals as per the Pharmacy Council of India standard. Also, the library needs to provide students with a curriculum so that students can make full use of the library. Also, all educational facilities as per Pharmacy HAT of India standard (e.g. laboratories, libraries, classrooms, etc.) are required to be done.
9) The offered post-merger accessibility is offered by the Pharmacy Council of India, Nabi Delhi for the academic year. will remain as allowed for 25-2024
10) In the context of MTT. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999 will have to be strictly enforced.
11) Government Decisions, Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. If the registration fee prescribed under 22.02.2018 has not been paid by the college / institution concerned, it should be paid by the Joint Director, Technical Education Divisional Office, Pune from the college / institution concerned.
12) The college / institution shall be required to obtain the affiliation of the university concerned for the said course.
In addition, the other terms and conditions laid down by the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai and the University concerned shall be binding on the Institute.
3. The Director of Technical Education shall issue such orders as may be necessary for the inclusion of the courses in the said Institute / College in the Centralized Admission Process as may be approved by this Government.
