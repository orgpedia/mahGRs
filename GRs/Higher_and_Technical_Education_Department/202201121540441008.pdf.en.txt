Tuition fees for short term (full time/ part time) courses under the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, which are not under the purview of All India Council for Technical Education, run under the Department of Technical Education, have been increased from the academic year 2012-13 to the reference No. 1 The dates hereof have been fixed in accordance with the Government Decision dated 20/03/2012.
Reference No. 2 As per the Government decisions dated 3/5/2021, the approval process for starting the courses not under the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) but under the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education in the state from 2021-22 has been approved by the Government.
