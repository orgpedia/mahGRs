The proposal:
A detailed roadmap for the development of College of Applied Arts and Sir J.G. College of Architecture has been prepared for three years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22. The plan includes educational development, infrastructure, historical heritage and enhancing the people's environment. A supplementary grant of Rs.5 crore has been sanctioned in the Additional Budget Session of June, 2019 for appointment of expert Architects and for ancillary matters for making renovation plans for the buildings of College of Applied Arts and Sir J.G.Vastu Shastra College. Out of this, 60 per cent i. e. Rs. 3 crore has been sanctioned. The following decisions have been taken regarding the allocation of funds:
The government's decision:
Approval is hereby given to disburse 60% of the supplementary grant of Rs. 5.00 crore, i.e. Rs.3 crore, sanctioned in the Budget Session, 2019 for appointment of expert Architects and other ancillary matters for making renovation plans of buildings of College of Applied Arts and Sir J.G.Vastu Shastra College. This fund should be made available to the Director, Directorate of Arts, Mumbai through the BDS system.
