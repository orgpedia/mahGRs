In order to ensure that the eligible candidates of Nashik and Amravati divisions as well as the eligible teachers of Aurangabad, Nagpur and Konkan divisions are enrolled in large numbers as graduate/ teacher voters in the respective divisions, the Universities and affiliated colleges of Nashik, Amravati, Aurangabad, Nagpur and Konkan divisions have been requested by the Additional Chief Secretary and Chief Electoral Officer, Maharashtra State to conduct the following campaign. The concerned Divisional Commissioner is the Electoral Registration Officer for these elections and the District Collector is the Assistant Electoral Registration Officer.
The universities and colleges under Nashik, Amravati, Aurangabad, Nagpur and Konkan divisions should carry out the campaign as follows:

Eligible graduate teachers of colleges in Nashik and Amravati divisions will have to fill up Form 18 (Form 18) to register their names in the electoral roll of the respective Graduate Legislative Council Constituency. These forms will be available in the office of the concerned Divisional Commissioner (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), concerned District Collector (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), Deputy Divisional Commissioner and Tehsildar as designated by the Divisional Commissioner. Similarly, the format is also available on the website of the Chief Electoral Officer, State of Maharashtra https:// ceo.maharashtra.gov.in at Downloads/ DownloadForms/ Form- 18.pdf. The form can be downloaded from the website. The duly filled Form 18 (FORM 18) along with necessary proof of educational qualification should be submitted to the above mentioned concerned office. If the application is submitted in person, the concerned designated officer will have to show the original degree certificate/ mark sheet or other prescribed documents. In case of sending Sample 18 by post, the proof/ documents indicating the educational qualification concerned must be attested by the Designated Officer/ Assistant Designated Officer/ Gazetted Officer/ Notary Public of the concerned district.
(b) Teachers Constituency In Aurangabad, Nagpur and Konkan divisions, as the electoral rolls are being prepared for the Teachers Legislative Council Constituency, the State Government shall, with the approval of the Election Commission, allot seats in educational institutions not lower than Secondary School, as determined under Section 27 (3) (b) of the Representation of the People Act, 1950, in that division. Teachers who have been working as teachers for at least 3 years in the six years immediately preceding the qualifying date 1-11-2022 should be urged and encouraged to enrol themselves in the electoral rolls of the respective Teachers Constituencies.
Eligible teachers of Aurangabad, Nagpur and Konkan divisions will have to fill Form 19 (FORM 19) along with the certificate issued by the head of the concerned educational institution in the prescribed form to register their names in the electoral list of the concerned Teachers Legislative Council constituency. The sample 19 and the sample of the prescribed certificate will be available in the office of the concerned Divisional Commissioner (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), the concerned District Collector (Electoral Registration Officer of this election), Deputy Divisional Commissioner and Tehsildar as designated by the Divisional Commissioner and the duly filled applications can be filled in that office.
