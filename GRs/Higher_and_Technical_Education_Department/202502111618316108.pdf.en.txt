Introduction: - Government Diploma Institutes functioning under the Directorate of Technical Education in the State.
The profile of teaching positions was earlier referred to in Reference No. 1 has been approved under the Government Decision mentioned herein.
As the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) has currently fixed the ratio of teachers to students at 1: 25, a total of 43 government technical institutes functioning under the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), B02 Pharmacy Diploma Institutes have been notified with the revised figure of faculty posts in Diploma Institutes vide Reference No. 8 here. The letter of 28.06.2022 has been submitted to the government by the Director, Technical Education.
2. Reference No. 6 of the Department of Finance. As per the guidelines of the Government Decision of 29.06.2017, a proposal for revised profile of teaching posts in Government Diploma Institutes functioning under the Directorate of Technical Education was submitted to the Sub-Committee of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department and Additional Chief Secretary (Expenditure), Finance Department constituted for preliminary scrutiny, following which the Committee recommended submission of the proposal to the High-Level Committee of Secretaries.
The proposal was made on the recommendation of the sub-committee. It was presented before a high-level committee of secretaries headed by the chief secretary.

3. High-Level Committee of Secretaries. The Government was under consideration to approve the revised structure of teaching positions in Government Diploma Institutes functioning under the Directorate of Technical Education as per the approval given in the 4th meeting of the Board in the year 2023.
The Government's Decisions:
The High-Level Committee of Secretaries As per the approval given in the meetings held on 13.07.2023, a total of 2251 faculty positions in B2 Pharmacy Diploma Institutes in total 43 Government Technical Institutes functioning under Directorate of Technical Education are being approved as per the attached prospectus.
2. As per the revised diagram, information on institute-wise regular posts at the establishment of 43 Government Technical B2 Pharmacy Diploma Institutes as well as information on dead cadre posts (designation, pay scale, sanctioned posts) will be as shown in the returns-1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8.
3.
