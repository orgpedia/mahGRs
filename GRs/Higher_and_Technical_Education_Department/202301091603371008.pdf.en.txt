Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is pleased to grant approval to start a new" Standalone" PGDM course at MET Institute of Post Graduate Diploma in Management, Bandra, Mumbai from the academic year 2021-22, as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, and to start PGDM (E-Business) course at the said Institute from the academic year 2021-22 with an intake capacity of 120 and PGDM (Media& Entertainment) course from the academic year 2022-23 with an intake capacity of 60, subject to the following conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.

8) The library in the institute is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students.

If the institute has not paid the processing fee to the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, the same shall be recovered by the Director before giving final approval.
