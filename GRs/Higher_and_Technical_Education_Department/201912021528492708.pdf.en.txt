Government Decision, Department of Higher and Technical Education, No. 2019/ No. 15/ Special-2, D. 08th March, 2019 Introduction: Poetess Bahinabai Chaudhary inaugurated the North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon. The Chief Minister announced that a research and teaching centre in the name of poetess Bahinabai Chaudhary would be set up in the university. To provide information about the life and poetry of Bahinabai, to provide a rich library that will be useful for researchers, to promote the study of Levaboli society and social life, to encourage the study and research of poetry and other literature of Khandesh, to organize the necessary initiative for the empowerment of women, to organize workshops related to Bahinabai's poetry and poetry production in the form of small booklets, to organize poetry symposiums and poetry conferences, to develop the poetic taste of the society, to make Bahinabai's literature reach the world level by signing agreements with foreign universities. Cabinet of Ministers In the meeting held on 28.08.2019, Shri. The Cabinet approved it. Proposal for creation of posts required for this Chair and Research Centre. This has been approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 11.09.2019.
Accordingly, the matter of issuing a government decision regarding the creation of necessary posts by approving the opening of Poetess Bahinabai Chowdhury Chair and Research Center was under the consideration of the government.
