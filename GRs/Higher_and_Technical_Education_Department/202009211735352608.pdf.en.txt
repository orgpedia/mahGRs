Mantralaya, Mumbai 400 032
Date is September 21, 2020.
Reference: Government Resolution, Department of Higher and Technical Education, No. Parikh 2020/ Q. No. 178/ Vishy-3, dated 18th September, 2020.
Preamble: The Government has issued a decision on September 18, 2020 regarding 100% attendance of teachers and non-teaching staff in all non-agricultural universities, affiliated colleges, deemed universities and self-financing universities in the State. Statements have been received from various teachers and non-teaching organizations in this regard. Hence, the government was contemplating to issue a clarification in this regard.
The government's decision:
In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 18th September, 2020 in the above context, the following clarificatory disclosures are being made:
(1) The attendance of teachers and non-teaching staff associated with the examination and examination work will be required as per the decision of the Government dated 18th September, 2020. However, this presence will be applicable online or in person, as the case may be.
(2) Universities/ colleges shall take necessary decisions at their level regarding attendance so that the work related to examination and examination is not disturbed. Necessary action in this regard should be taken immediately by all the concerned Universities/ Educational Institutions.
