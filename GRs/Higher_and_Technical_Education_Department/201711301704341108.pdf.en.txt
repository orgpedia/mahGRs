The objectives of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) To lay down the guidelines for the start-up of students in public universities.
2) To lay down guidelines for start-up for various faculties.
3) Setting the frame work for the inclusion of industries in the start-up in the university.
(iv) laying down guidelines for inclusion of other beneficiaries.
5) To lay down guidelines for the preparation of entrepreneurship courses for start-ups.
6) To direct the other aspects, if any, in line with the Startup at the University.
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
The Chairman of the Committee shall convene meetings with other members as may be necessary.
The travel allowance and the required honorarium to be paid to the members will be paid by the Directorate of RUSA.
The committee will prepare a draft policy in consultation with all the stakeholders (beneficiaries) and submit it to the government after taking public opinion on it. The committee will have a six-month term.
