Preamble: As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2, the policy of transfer of Group-B officers and Group-C and Group-D employees through counseling has been implemented. As per this policy, the list of transferable officers/ employees and the list of vacant posts should be published. In the year 2022, the process of transfer of officers in the cadre of Junior Advocate, Group-B and Junior Administrative Officer/ Superintendent/ Manager, Group-B will be carried out, for which the government was considering to publish the list of transferable officers and vacancies as mentioned in the government decision at Reference No. 2.
The government's decision:
The list of transferable officers and vacancies in Group-B cadre of Maharashtra Education Service (College Branch) under the Department of Higher and Technical Education is being published as follows:
