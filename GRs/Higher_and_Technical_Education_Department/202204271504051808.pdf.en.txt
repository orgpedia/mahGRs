The proposal:
The Government intends to organize the Youth Festival on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of Independence with the objective of providing a platform to the students of the colleges of the state to get exposure to the talents and built-up skills and to inspire them by organizing various competitions at the college/ university level across the state.
The government's decision:
The following committees are being constituted for organizing and implementing various programmes, competitions under the Youth Festival in the colleges of the state.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) To decide the format of various sports competitions and cultural programs to be organized for the college graduating students of the state, to choose the type of sports, to select the art works, to decide the program wise calendar, to organize the program.
2) The festival should be called' Bahar'. Also to decide the motto and motto of the youth festival. Also to jointly display the logo of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav and the logo of the Youth Festival.
3) To take decisions by forming a sub-committee under the said committee at the divisional level for the selection of various programs, sports, and art works in the college.
4) Financial planning to match the expenses under the festival, financial planning to be compatible with the university-wise expenditure.
(vi) Organizing sports events usually in the month of November and cultural programmes before the last week of December.
The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE) should also organize a youth festival for the students of diploma courses on the lines of the proposed youth festival. The cost should be borne by the MSBTE.
