Reference: 1. Government Resolution No: Parichay-2010/ No. 128/ Vishy-4 dated 11th July, 2023.
Government Resolution No: Narik 2010/ P.No. 128/ Vishy-4 dated 30th August, 2023.
Government Decision Corrigendum: A committee has been formed under the Government Decision dated July 11, 2023 to establish a Marathi language university in the state. The committee will have the Joint Director, Higher Education, Amravati Division, Amravati as its member secretary.
Instead, the Director, Higher Education, Pune is being appointed as the Member Secretary of the committee.
In the government decision, a provision was made to provide travel allowance to the non-official members of the committee from the funds available for the office expenses of the Joint Director, Higher Education, Amaravati Division, Amaravati. Instead, it is being amended to provide that the expenses of meeting, honorarium and travel allowance etc. to the non-official members of the committee will be paid by the Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune.
