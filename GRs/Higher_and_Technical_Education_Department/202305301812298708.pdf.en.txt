3. The Principal/ Director concerned shall make the transferred officers join without any further delay after they are relieved from the respective college/ institution. The Principal/ Director concerned shall pay the salary and allowances of the officer who has come to join the department without waiting for the mandatory period of joining.
They will be held responsible. Also, no leave of absence will be granted during the period from the date of discharge to the date of joining the place of transfer.
The concerned head of the office shall take action to relieve the officers transferred by this order by June 15, 2023. Also, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers to ensure that the salary of the transferred officers from July 1, 2023, will not be withdrawn from the office before the transfer.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune shall submit an executive report to the Government by 25th June, 2023.
