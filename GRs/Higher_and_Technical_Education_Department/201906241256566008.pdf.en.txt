Preamble: In exercise of the powers conferred by section 23 of the Act, the Maharashtra Unaided Private Professional Educational Institutions (Regulation of Admission to Full-time Professional Law Undergraduate Courses) Rules, 2017 have been published in the Gazette vide notification No. 2 of the Act. Accordingly, under the decision of the Government at Reference No. 3, it has been approved to conduct admissions to law courses in Government/ Non-Government Aided/ Unaided/ Permanent Unaided/ University Departments/ University Managed Institutions in the State through State Level Common Entrance Test and Centralized Admission Process. As per the notification at Reference No. 4, the Maharashtra Unaided Private Professional Educational Institutions (Regulation of Admission to Full-time Professional Law Undergraduate Courses) Rules, 2017 have been amended. The amendment was under consideration of the government for admission to the law degree departments/ courses in government/ non-government aided/ unaided/ permanent unaided and non-agriculture universities under the Directorate of Higher Education.
The government's decision:
As per the notification at Reference No. 5, the Government has decided to implement the amendments made in the Maharashtra Unaided Private Professional Educational Institutions (Regulation of Admission to Full-time Professional Law Undergraduate Courses) Rules, 2017 for the State Level Common Entrance Examination for Law Courses in Government/ Non-Government Aided/ Unaided/ Permanent Unaided/ University Departments/ University Managed Institutions in the State and for the Centralized Admission Process.
Accordingly, the said reforms will come under the Directorate of Higher Education from the academic year 2019-20
