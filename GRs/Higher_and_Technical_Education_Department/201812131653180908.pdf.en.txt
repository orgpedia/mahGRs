Government Order: Dr. Rajesh Metkar was appointed as Associate Professor, Mechanical Engineering, Government Engineering College, Maharashtra Engineering College Teachers Service, Group-A in Government Engineering College, Jalgaon, as per the decision of the relevant government. As per his request, the post is being changed and he is being given the post of Assistant Professor, Mechanical Engineering at Government Engineering College, Amravati.
The head of the institution concerned should immediately relieve Dr. Metkar to appear at the place of posting given to him and the concerned teachers should immediately appear at the place of posting given to them and submit a report to the government through the Director of Technical Education.
(3) This order shall be issued with the approval of the competent authority and shall come into force with immediate effect.
