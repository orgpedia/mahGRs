The objective of the Foundation is to promote universal development of public library services in the country. The Foundation provides matching grants to the States in the ratio of 50:50 to effectively implement and enhance library services in the States and Union Territories. Raja Rammohun Roy Library has been established as per the guidelines of the' Books for All" book of the Foundation. The State Library Planning Committee was formed as per the decision of the government. Also in reference No. The committee was re-constituted following a government decision. Accordingly, the term of the said Committee has expired on 29.03.2018. Therefore, the matter of reconstitution of the State Library Planning Committee was under the consideration of the Government.
