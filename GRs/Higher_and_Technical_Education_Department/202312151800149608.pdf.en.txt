Letter from Shri Hridaynath Mangeshkar dated 25th October, 2023.
Introduction: Reference No. 1 to start certificate courses in Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College from the academic year 2022-23. It has been approved by the Government Resolution dated 23rd September, 2022. The Cabinet also approved the setting up of an Advisory Board to ensure that the quality and utility of the Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International College of Music is maintained to the highest standards. Shri Hridaynath Mangeshkar has informed that he is resigning from the post of President vide letter dated 25th October, 2023 at Reference No. He has also been informed about the release of important responsibilities of the post of president. Therefore, the matter of appointment of Chairperson on the Advisory Board of Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International College of Music is under consideration.
Government Decision: Shri Hridaynath Mangeshkar has been appointed as the Chairman of the Advisory Board of Lata Mangeshkar International College of Music. Ushatai Mangeshkar has been appointed.
