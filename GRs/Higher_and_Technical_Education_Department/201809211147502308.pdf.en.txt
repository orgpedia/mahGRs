Provision of practice paper sets for trainees undergoing training at State Institute of Administrative Vocational Education, Mumbai and Indian Administrative Service Pre-Training Centres.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Resolution No: PRI-5418/ PR No.155/ Mashi-2
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
The date is September 21, 2018.
The proposal:
With a view to imparting training to bright students of Maharashtra for competitive examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) and to increase their success rate and to provide adequate exposure to the students of Maharashtra, the Government has set up State Institute of Administrative Business Education, Mumbai and Indian Administrative Service Pre-Examination Training Centre, Kolhapur, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Nashik and Amravati.
In his Budget Speech for 2018-19, Shri. The Finance Minister has announced to procure practice paper sets available with reputed private coaching institutes, get those papers solved from the students and get them examined by expert examiners to provide more facilities to the students in line with their studies to increase the ratio of intelligent budding candidates in Maharashtra to the services in the Union Public Service Commission. Accordingly, the Government was considering providing practice paper sets from reputed private training institutes to the trainees undergoing training at State Institute of Administrative Business Education, Mumbai and Indian Administrative Service Examination Pre-Training Centre, Kolhapur, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Nashik and Amravati.
The government's decision:
In order to provide more facilities to the trainees undergoing training at the State Institute of Administrative Business Education, Mumbai and Indian Administrative Service Pre-Examination Training Centre, Kolhapur, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Nashik and Amravati for preparing for the examinations to be conducted by the Union Public Service Commission, the Government has decided to purchase practice paper kits from reputed private training institutes, distribute them to the trainees, get them examined by expert examiners and constitute a committee to select a nominated institute for the purpose.
Accordingly, it is being approved to constitute a Committee under the Chairmanship of Director, Higher Education, Pune to select a private institution nominated for procuring practice paper kits for the trainees undergoing training in 6 training centres under the Directorate of Higher Education for the Preliminary Examination and Main Examination of the Indian Administrative Service Examination of the Union Public Service Commission as follows: (1) Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune- Chairman (2) Director, State Institute of Administrative Business Education, Mumbai- Member (3) Director, Indian Administrative Service Preliminary Training Institute, Nagpur Member Page 1 of 2
