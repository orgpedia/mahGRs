Date: January 24, 2023
Reference: 1) Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. 14.01.2019 2) Department of Public Health, Government Decision No. 17.01.2023.
Introduction: The Department of Public Health is located at No. 2. As per the Government Decision dated 17.01.2023, the ceiling limit and sanctioning authority for reimbursement of medical expenses of Government employees has been revised. The government decision No. Under section 4, the administrative department of the concerned ministry has been instructed to take a decision at their level, taking into account the approved designations in the figure, as to what will be the holders of the posts of head of department and regional head of department in the offices under them for the approval of reimbursement of medical expenses in accordance with the provisions set out in this Government Decision. Accordingly, the government was considering declaring department heads and regional department heads to approve reimbursement of medical expenses of teachers and non-teaching staff of non-agriculture universities and affiliated aided colleges under the department.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following Heads of Departments and Regional Heads of Departments are being declared to approve reimbursement of medical expenses of teaching and non-teaching staff of non-agricultural universities and affiliated aided colleges under the Department.

Cases above Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakhs only) will be required to be submitted to the Administrative Department of the Ministry for approval.
The competent authority concerned will be responsible for approving the cases of reimbursement of medical expenses after strictly examining the provisions of the Maharashtra State Service (Medical Care) Rules, 1961 and the Government Decisions issued from time to time.
