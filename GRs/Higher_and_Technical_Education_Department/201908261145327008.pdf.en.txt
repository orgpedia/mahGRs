The scheme Unnat Maharashtra Abhiyan has been approved for implementation from 13th January, 2016.
Unnat Maharashtra Abhiyaan through a new item proposed in the Monsoon Session of the Legislature in June, 2019 for Rs. A sum of Rs. 100.00 lakh has been allocated for the project.
