Preamble: In order to revise the pay scales of State Government and other eligible employees, the Department of Finance had constituted the State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 vide Government Resolution dated 17.01.2017. The Committee had submitted Volume 1 of the Report to the Finance Department. The Department of Finance, vide Government Order dated 1st January, 2019, has issued orders for acceptance of recommendations on pay scales and annexures in Volume 1 of the Pay Revision Committee Report.
The scope of the recommendations in respect of para 4.13 of the Report of the Pay Revision Committee in Volume 1 is as follows:" The recommendations in this matter shall be applicable to eligible State Government employees, Zilla Parishads, Maharashtra Legislature Secretariat, Judicial Employees of Courts, Aided School Employees, Government Employees of the State and Government Employees of the State.

This will be applicable to the employees of agricultural and non-agricultural universities and their affiliated non-agricultural colleges as well as employees of other aided institutions. It is recommending that the date of implementation of these recommendations for employees of other offices/ institutions should be the same as that of State Government employees. " The government has accepted the recommendation.
The Department of Finance has implemented the pay structure of the 7th Pay Commission vide Government Notification dated 30th January, 2019 mentioned at Reference No.3. Further, the Department of Finance vide Circular No.44 dated 20th February, 2019 has issued instructions regarding the date from which the revised pay structure is to be accepted by the employees and fixation of pay in the revised pay structure. Thereafter, the Department of Finance vide Circular No.5 dated 30th May, 2019 has issued clarification regarding payment of arrears to employees covered under National Pension Scheme or Definitive Contribution Pension Scheme.
Considering the orders/ instructions issued by the Finance Department in accordance with the 7th Pay Commission, the matter of implementing the revised pay structure of the 7th Pay Commission to the non-teaching staff of Tilak Maharashtra University, Pune, a government-aided deemed university under the Department of Higher Education, was under the consideration of the Government.
The revised pay structure in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission is being applied to the non-teaching cadres approved by the Government as per the review of posts in the Government-aided Tilak Maharashtra Abhimant, Pune University under the Department of Higher Education.
This decision shall not apply to the following employees:
(a) employees who are not in full-time employment;
(b) an employee on consolidated pay,
(a) Contract workers.
(d) Employees who are paid salary out of contingency expenses, (e) Daily wage workers,
(f) Employees who retired on or before 31.12.2015 and who were re-employed on that date after retirement and whose period of re-employment after retirement has been extended beyond that date.
(3) In Annexure-II to this Government Resolution, No. The revised pay structure mentioned in Section 5 will come into effect from January 1, 2016.

The procedure prescribed by the Finance Department for Government employees to pay arrears admissible on account of implementation of the revised pay scales of the Seventh Pay Commission should be applied in respect of employees of these deemed universities.
The primary responsibility of determining the salary of the employees as per the revised pay structure as per the Seventh Pay Commission shall be of the deemed university concerned. The deemed university should fix the salary and submit the proposal to the Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education.
Thereafter, the Divisional Joint Director shall examine such proposals in accordance with the rules and certify that the wage determination is correct and thereafter the process of payment of salary should be taken up.
7. The additional expenditure arising out of this revised pay structure shall be covered under the head of accounts under which expenditure is incurred in respect of salaries and allowances of non-teaching staff and shall be met from the grants sanctioned under that head. The Director, Directorate of Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune shall take action to provide additional funds for expenditure on the said matter.
