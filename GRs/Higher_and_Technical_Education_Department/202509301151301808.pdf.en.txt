The Proposal: -
Reference No. The annual income of the family (combined of both parents) for the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme is Rs. Up to 8.00 lakh has been fixed. Under this scheme, the benefit of tuition fee and examination fee amount charged for the course in which the student is studying is given to the boy / girl. The sanctioned amount of the scheme is credited to the students (in their Aadhaar linked bank account) / institutions through the DBT portal. Also, the cost of the scheme shall be calculated from the head of the account No. W.2,2202 General Education, 80 General, 800, Other Expenses (02) (09) whose or whose parents' annual income is less than Rs. Students not exceeding 8,00,000 are being funded under this initiative through Fau Wai (2202 1456) 34 Scholarships / Scholarships. As directed by the Hon'ble Chief Secretary, instructions have been given to propose separate head of accounts for implementation of this scheme. In pursuance of the directions of the Hon'ble Chief Secretary, the proposal for providing a separate title of account for implementing the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme was received by the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide Reference No. 2 The proposal has been submitted to the government under the letter here. Accordingly, the government is considering opening a new title account for the scheme.
