Preamble In order to conduct NAAC assessment of the unapproved colleges in the State on the lines of PARAMARSH Scheme of University Grants Commission (UGC), the Government vide its decision dated 19.04.2023, has prescribed the constitution of University Level Committee and District Level Committees by constituting a State Level Committee under its decision dated 02.06.2023 for implementation of the scheme. Accordingly, it is under consideration of the Government to include experts on NBA rating in the State Level Committee as proposed by the Directorate of Technical Education under reference dated 04.07.2023 and to prescribe the composition of University Level Committee and Zonal Level Committee for this University.
Government's decision
In order to conduct NAAC/ NBA assessment of the un-approved colleges in the State on the lines of PARAMARSH Scheme of University Grants Commission, the State Level Committee constituted vide Government Resolution dated 02.06.2023 for implementation of PARIS-SPARSH Scheme consists of the following members.
