Preamble: The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India vide its letter dated 17.05.2019, has informed the State Governments to prepare an Action Plan (100% Day Action Plan) for 100% recruitment of teaching posts in Government and Non-Government Aided Colleges under State Governments and send it to the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India immediately. Accordingly, in reference No. 2, Mr. The Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra has apprised the Central Government of the action being taken in connection with the recruitment of vacancies in higher educational institutions in the State.
In pursuance of the directions issued by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India vide its letter dated 17.05.2019, the proposal for filling up of vacancies in the institutions related to technical education in the State and approval for creation of new posts as per the norms of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), 2019-20, was placed before the Sub-Committee constituted for making recommendations after thorough examination of the proposals for creation of new posts/ revival of posts and review of posts etc. It was decided in the meeting as follows:" The Sub-Committee approved the filling up of 37 teaching vacancies in the Government aided Deemed to be University, Institute of Chemical Technology, Mumbai. It was also recommended that a proposal for creation of 67 regular posts, including 4 constitutional posts and 63 teaching posts, be submitted to the high-level committee of secretaries. "
