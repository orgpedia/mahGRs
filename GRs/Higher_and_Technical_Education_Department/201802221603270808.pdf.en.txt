Introduction: No. 1 Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. 11/09/2014 has approved the establishment of" Dhatu Tantra Prabodhini" in the Government Institute of Technology, Kolhapur and the constitution of a regulatory committee to run the regular functioning of the Dhatu Tantra Prabodhini. The term of office of the Board was two years. The said period has expired with effect from 10/09/2016. The above reference No. 2 In a letter dated 20/05/2017, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai has requested for Government approval to extend the tenure of the Governing Body of the Metallurgical Academy at Government Technological Institute, Kolhapur for a period of two years.
Government Decision: The Government have approved the constitution of a Regulatory Committee for the regular functioning of the Metallurgical Institute at Government Technological Institute, Kolhapur for a period of two years as per the Government Decision dated 11th September, 2014.
