Introduction: The Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata, under the Ministry of Culture, Government of India, is implementing a common fund scheme for the promotion of library movement and overall development of public libraries. The Maharashtra government's contribution to the scheme is equal to the foundation's share (50: 50). Reference No. The State Government's contribution is Rs. Rs. 1.50. The amount has been increased to Rs 2.50 crore. Now, under the equal fund scheme of Raja Ram Mohan Roy Library Foundation, the state government's contribution has increased from Rs. 2.50 crore to Rs. The matter of raising up to Rs. 3.00 crore was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
The State Government's contribution to the Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata, under the Equal Fund Scheme has been increased from Rs. 2.50 crore in 2018-19 to Rs. 3.00 lakh has been sanctioned by the Government.
The expenditure shall be met from the provisions under the head" Demand No.W-4, 2205-Arts and Culture, 105, Public Libraries, (03) Assistance to Central District and Taluk Libraries, (03) (02) Contribution to Raja Rammohan Roy Library Foundation (Compulsory) (2205 0304) 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Payable)."
