The proposal:
The MahaDBT Portal, developed by the State Government to deliver the benefits of government schemes directly to the beneficiaries, has been operationalized in the month of August, 2018 and through this portal, the process of inviting applications for scholarships from eligible students, scrutinizing the applications from college and department level, providing allotment for payment of scholarships to the approved students and disbursement of scholarships in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the approved student through PFMS system, etc. is done through MahaIT and MahaOnline. It was under consideration of the Government to approve the expenditure incurred by MahaIT for the services rendered during the period from August, 2020 to October, 2021 to provide the benefit of all scholarship schemes under the Directorate of Higher Education directly to the beneficiaries through the MahaDBT portal.
The government's decision:
In order to provide the benefit of scholarship schemes implemented by the Directorate of Higher Education directly to the beneficiaries through the MahaDBT portal, MahaIT has provided Rs. 98,13,878/- (Rupees Thirty Eight Lakhs Thirteen Thousand Eight Hundred and Seventy Eight only) is being approved subject to the following conditions.
The Directorate of Higher Education shall ensure that the said payments are correct while making payments for the services availed from MahaIT.
