The proposal:
Mumbai University had conducted the recruitment process for the posts of Deputy Registrar and Assistant Registrar from 2009 to 2013. The government had received complaints regarding the recruitment process. Information and Documents relating to the Director, High
Education, Pune dated 22nd May, 2023. The government was considering a thorough probe into the matter. The following decisions have been taken:
The University of Mumbai had conducted the recruitment process for the posts of Deputy Registrar and Assistant Registrar from 2009 to 2013. A three-member probe committee has been formed to investigate the matter.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) The recruitment process for the posts of Deputy Registrar and Assistant Registrar by the University of Mumbai from 2009 to 2013 should be thoroughly investigated on an individual basis.
2) Submit self-explanatory individual feedback on whether the prescribed procedure was followed for recruitment.

(3) The candidates who have qualified in the recruitment process should submit self-explanatory individual feedback as to whether they were holding the qualifications required for the post concerned, such as educational qualification, experience, age limit etc. at the time of appointment.
4) Candidates who have qualified in the recruitment process should submit self-explanatory feedback on whether the appointment is valid.
5) Whether there were irregularities in the recruitment process, individual, self-explanatory feedback in this regard should be submitted to the government.
6) If there are irregularities in the recruitment process, the officials responsible for such irregularities should submit information to the government regarding the names of the employees and their role in the recruitment process.
The committee will have to submit its report to the state government in a month's time.
The meetings of the Committee shall be convened by the University of Mumbai. The honorarium and travel allowance of the non-official members of the committee should be paid by the Mumbai University.
