Since the said transfers are required to be effected effectively, the salary of the transferred Assistant Professor up to the date of his superannuation or up to the maximum date of 30.06.2018 shall be drawn by the Government Teachers College where he is presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the Government Teachers College before the transfer under any circumstances, the salary should be withdrawn by the college after the transfer of the concerned assistant professor after joining the Government Teachers College of the transfer. If the salary drawn from the Government Teachers College, which was functioning prior to the transfer from 01.07.2018, the Collection and Disbursement Officer of the Government Teachers College shall be held personally liable.
These orders will come into effect immediately. The Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune shall submit a report to the Government before 01.07.2018 regarding the transfer of the transferred Assistant Professor.
