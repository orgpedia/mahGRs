The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has given its approval for introduction of B. Vocational under NSQF in the existing institution, increase/ decrease in intake capacity, closure of courses, etc. The Director has submitted a proposal to the Government through the aforesaid letter for grant of Government recognition to the institutions recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. A decision in this regard was pending with the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the approval of the Government is being accorded for starting of B. Vocational Degree Courses under NSQF in the existing institutions of the State as indicated in Annexure-A and Annexure-B to this Government Decision from the academic year 2021-2022, increase/ decrease in intake capacity of the courses being run, closure of courses, etc. subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
(2) It shall be mandatory for the respective institution and skill knowledge provider to comply with the special rules, terms and conditions framed by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for the implementation of B.Voc (NSQF Level VII) Degree Courses under the Community College Scheme.

(3) The institution concerned shall apply to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission for affiliation to the said course.
As per the provisions of the Universities Act, 2016, it will be necessary to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university.
Admission to this course is conducted by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi.
Their approval process should be done at the institutional level as mentioned in the Handbook 2021-2022.
(5) The institution shall take steps in accordance with the rules for obtaining approval of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for the extension of the said course.
6) Since the course is being implemented under the Community College Scheme and the admission of students is being done at the institution level, the benefit of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme will not be permissible to the students admitted to this course.
The institutions which have not paid the processing fee to the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, shall recover the same from the institutions before the final approval of the Director and the Director, Technical Education shall issue necessary orders within the next 15 days for the inclusion of the course as per the approval of this Government in the Centralized Admission Process.
