The proposal:
The Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur have been directed to provide funds to the tune of Rs.5 crore per university per year in four instalments to each law university for the next five years from the financial year 2018-19 for meeting their day-to-day administrative and academic expenses, salaries of the posts filled as per the approved figure, and honorarium of the contractual staff paid by the universities. 1 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision.
Accordingly, Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Nagpur and Aurangabad have issued Reference No. 2 In pursuance of the proposal made vide letter dated 01.02.2022, the total amount of the fourth instalment of 2021-22 (i.e. January, 2022 to March, 2022) is Rs. 1,25,00, 000/- (per University) i.e. Rs. 3,75,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Seventy Five Lakhs only) was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur will be provided a total amount of Rs. 1,25,00, 000/- (per University) i.e. Rs. 3,75,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Seventy Five Lakhs only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
(1) The Executive Council shall be responsible for the management of the grant to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur by depositing the second installment of the total amount sanctioned by the Government in the University Fund. Also, the statement of accounts of expenditure by exchanging the expenditure made out of this amount and approving it by the Finance Committee of the University.

The registrar/ vice-chancellor of the concerned Maharashtra National Law University should take action to get it approved before the Mahaparishad.
(2) It shall be the responsibility of the Vice-Chancellor of the Law Universities to see that the amount sanctioned by the Government is spent for the purpose for which it has been sanctioned. (3) All the documents and accounts of all the aforesaid amounts are to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as the case may be.
(4) The University shall use the sanctioned amount for the purpose for which the grant is made.
(5) As per the provisions and conditions of the Government Decision, No.:-BHANKSHA2014/ P.K.82/ PART-III/ INDUSTRY-4, dated 01.12.2016 issued by the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour and the Financial Powers Rule Book, the expenditure should be made by the respective universities as per the rules of each subject. Government Decision The Government Decision No. 2016/ 215/ Udyog-4 dated 24.08.2017 issued by the Department of Industries, Power and Labour has revised the procedures for office procurement by Government Departments and approved the procedures for the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal developed by the Central Government for the State Governments for procurement of goods and services. However, it will be mandatory to purchase goods and services from the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal. Also, the terms and conditions of the Financial Rights Manual should be strictly adhered to. Also, the registrar/ vice-chancellor of the concerned Maharashtra National Law University should take action after considering the prevailing terms and conditions of the government.
The Director (Higher Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune shall be responsible for providing the said sanctioned provision to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur immediately. All the documents and accounts of the above grant are entitled to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the Accountant General-2, Nagpur, as the case may be, by the officer authorised by him for the purpose. The grant sanctioned to the institution should be used for the same purpose and the detailed expenditure information and utilization certificate should be submitted by the Director (Higher Education), Pune in the relevant government decision within the prescribed time as per the schedule given in No. 2 (9) of the relevant Maharashtra National Law University.
