Introduction: Entry into service rules have been prescribed for the post of Director, Directorate of Arts vide notification dated 7th June, 2014. Further, the said Rules have been amended vide notification dated 21st October, 2016. Rule No. 2 (a) (i) (3) prescribes that for the post of Director, Directorate of Arts," the candidate should have not less than 17 years of collective or exclusive experience of teaching in a Government recognized Art Institute or administrative work in a Government recognized Art Institute." However, it is not clear whether the experience was after acquiring an educational qualification. The government was contemplating to bring in a legislation in this regard. Accordingly, the following decision was taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of this Government Decision, it is hereby ordered that the Director of Arts, Maharashtra Fine Arts Education Service, may, in accordance with Rule No. 2 (a) (i) (3): The candidate should have not less than 17 years of combined or non-exclusive experience of teaching in a Government recognised institute of arts or administrative work in a Government recognised institute of arts.
The process of amendment of the above subject in the service entry rules for the post of Art Director will be carried out as soon as possible.
The decision was taken by the General Administration Department.
