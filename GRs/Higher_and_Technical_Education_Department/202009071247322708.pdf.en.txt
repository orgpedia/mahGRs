Preamble: The Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of Government approval for starting new Diploma Engineering Courses in Government and Government Aided Technical Institutions from the academic year 2020-21 and to restore the earlier reduced intake capacity of the existing courses as per the permission granted by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide Reference No. In pursuance of this proposal, the Government was considering to grant approval to start new Diploma Engineering courses in Government and Government aided technical institutions and to restore the earlier reduced intake capacity of the existing courses.
Government Decision: The Government is granting approval to start new Diploma Engineering Courses in Government and Government Aided Technical Institutions listed in Annexure A from the academic year 2020-21 and to restore the previously reduced intake capacity of the existing courses subject to the following terms and conditions.
The admission capacity of the institution for the said course shall be limited as mentioned against their name in the prospectus.
(2) The institution shall be required to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, State of Maharashtra.

The concerned institution must be affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education for these diploma courses.
The Principals of the newly approved Diploma Courses in Engineering will be required to obtain the approval of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi before admitting the students. In the absence of this approval, the concerned institutions will be fully responsible in case some court cases arise in the future. The undertaking has to be submitted by the head of the institution concerned to the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai. After that, the institutes will have to get the approval of the Directorate to start the courses.
5. The institution concerned shall complete other legal formalities by depositing the requisite processing fee and accumulated deposits in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
6. Admission to the said Professional Diploma Courses shall be made as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the respective courses approved by the Government.
If the fees charged by the institutions concerned exceed the tuition fees prescribed by the Fee Regulatory Authority, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
The Institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the Government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
The library of the institute is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students.
