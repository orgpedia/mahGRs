The proposal:
Promotion of eligible officers/ employees for promotion in State Government Service
Consolidated Guidelines on Procedure to be followed for granting General Administration Department. It was released on 01/08/2019. As per the said decision, the Secretary/ Principal of the Administrative Department of the Ministry concerned shall be eligible for promotion to all the posts in Group-A, which are not in the jurisdiction of the Establishment Board in the non-Ministerial cadre.
Provision has been made for a Divisional Promotion Committee under the Chairmanship of Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary. Accordingly, the matter of forming a departmental promotion committee was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
All the offices/ colleges/ institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education as per the instructions given in the General Administration Department, dated 01/08/2019

There will be one member representing the Backward Classes in the Divisional Promotion Committee. If such member is not one of the nominated members, one officer shall be included as an invited member to represent the Backward Classes with the concurrence of the Chairman of the Committee.
The purview of the said Departmental Promotion Committee shall be: (i) to consider proposals for recommending promotions and to make recommendations for promotion to the appointing authority by preparing a list of officers eligible for promotion in accordance with the provisions of the Service Admission Rules and in accordance with the orders/ guidelines issued from time to time by the General Administration Department.
(ii) To consider the proposal for the date of promotion and recommend to the appointing authority to grant the date of promotion in accordance with the provisions of the Service Admission Rules and in accordance with the orders/ guidelines issued by the General Administration Department from time to time.
3.The decision of the Government comes into force with immediate effect and shall be strictly enforced.
