Preamble: Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi has published the following on its website: [http:// www.pharmacy.nic.in]
Decisions _ EC _ CC. html] The list of approved courses in Pharmacy in the country for the academic year 2020-21 has been released. The Supreme Court in Transfer Petition (Civil) No. 87-101/2014. The Adjudication dated 05.03.2020 has ruled that the recognition of Pharmacy courses is under the authority of Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. All the courses offered by the college are approved by the Pharmacy Council of India and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi.
Accordingly, the State Government has proposed to grant recognition to the institutions of Pharmacy course from the academic year 2020-2021 on the basis of recognition by Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. The matter was under consideration for government approval.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government is granting approval for starting new Diploma and Degree courses in Pharmacy in the academic year 2020-2021 as indicated in Annexure A subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
(2) The institution shall be required to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi/ Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
(3) The institution concerned shall meet other legal requirements by depositing the required accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education.
4) Admission to the said Degree and Post Graduate Degree Courses will be done as per the rules/ procedure of admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
