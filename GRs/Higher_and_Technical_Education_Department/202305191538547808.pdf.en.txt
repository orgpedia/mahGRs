Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 16.03.2022 in Reference No.1, approval has been accorded for establishment of Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Museum and for earmarking of site for the said college at Directorate of Libraries, Kalina Complex. Accordingly, the Collector, Mumbai Suburban vide order dated 31.03.2023 in Reference No.2 has transferred 7000 sq.m. of open land at Kalina Complex to the Director of Arts, Directorate of Arts, Maharashtra State, Mumbai for establishment of" Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Museum." A new well-equipped building is planned to be constructed on the 7000 sq.m. land allotted by the Government for Bharat Ratna Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Museum. For this, the Ideas Design Competition of renowned architects/ architectural institutions of the country will be implemented by the Public Works Department. The government was considering setting up a committee to finalise a design from the designs to be submitted in the Ideas Design Competition. Accordingly, the following decision was taken.

A new building will be constructed on the site. Ideas Design Competition of reputed Architects/ Architectural Institutions of the country should be organized by the Public Works Department to determine the possible design of this building. This Government has decided to constitute a selection committee as follows to finalize a design from the designs to be submitted in this Ideas Competition.

An idea competition should be held before the above selection committee in connection with the construction of a new building for Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Museum. Expression of Interest (EOI) should be processed by the Public Works Department for the idea competition. Also, the Public Works Department should organize this idea competition immediately.
The architect/ architectural firm should submit the design for the Ideas Design Competition to the selection committee. Choose the first, second and third numbers. Also, the first Rs. 2,00,000/- for the second position Rs. 1,50,000/- and Rs. Rs 1,00,000. The award money is being sanctioned by the Public Works Department.
The meeting allowance of Rs.5000/- and travelling allowance of Rs.2000/- to the non-official members of the said Selection Committee and the expenditure thereon as provided in the Government Resolution No.PLA-2015/ (P.S. 168/2015)/ TANSI-3, dated 1st January, 2018 in Annexure-I, at para. Under section 8" Expenditure to be incurred with the approval of the Administrative Department in case of additional expenditure," the Government is approving the expenditure from the personal provident fund of the Director, Directorate of Arts.
