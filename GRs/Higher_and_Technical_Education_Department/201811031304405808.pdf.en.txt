Government Circular: Reference No. Under the decision of the government here, a scheme has been implemented to waive the examination fee of the university and examination boards for the students in the scarcity-hit areas of the state. This includes university examinations. Now, in the case of No. The Revenue and Forest Department has declared a drought-like situation in the taluks where the 2018-19 Kharif season Trigger 2 has been implemented. Under this decision, the government has implemented the concession of waiving the examination fee of college students as part of the measures taken to address the drought-like situation. Therefore, in the context of the waiver of examination fees for college students in drought-like taluks, No. 1 Immediate action should be taken in accordance with the provisions of the government decision here. The Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune should immediately submit a proposal for the provision of funds, if required.
