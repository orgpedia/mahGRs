Ministry Extension, Madame Cama Road,
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: July 20, 2023.
the order
The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006. In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) of the said Act, the officers of the accompanying prospectus in the Government Colleges/ Institutions/ Offices under the Directorate of Higher Education are being transferred and posted to the posts indicated in column No. 5 against their names, with the approval of the senior authorities competent to make the transfers.
The concerned Principal/ Director/ Head of Office shall relieve the transferred officers immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned officers should join the college/ institution/ office immediately after their transfer from the existing college/ institution/ office.
3. The concerned Principal/ Director/ Head of the Office shall make the transferred officers join without any delay after they are relieved from the respective colleges/ institutions/ offices. The Principal/ Director/ Head of the Office concerned may not appoint the officer who has come to join, due to which there is an occasion to pay salary and allowances without working for a mandatory waiting period.
The chief will be held responsible. Also, no leave of absence will be granted during the period from the date of discharge to the date of joining the place of transfer.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune should immediately submit an executive report to the government regarding this transfer order.
