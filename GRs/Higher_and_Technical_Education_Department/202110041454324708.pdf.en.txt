The Government was considering to grant approval to establish a new Institute of Architecture, Ratnagiri, with B.Arch. (intake capacity- 40) course from the academic year 2021-22 on a permanent non-subsidized basis.
Government approval is being given to start a new Institute of Architecture, Ratnagiri with B.Arch. (intake capacity 40) course on permanent non-aided basis subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. This recognition shall be on a perpetual basis without subsidy.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the Council of Architecture in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Decision dated 21st May, 1983. The institution's intake capacity for this course will be as mentioned above.
3. The institution shall enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the Council of Architecture. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.

The Institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the norms. Salaries and allowances should be paid to the employees as per the rules.
Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
The books, periodicals required by the library in the institution must be purchased in accordance with the standards of the Council of Architecture. Also, the library should be made accessible to all students.
