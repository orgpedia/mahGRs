The proposal:
After receiving the allotment from the Maha Online Office after 31.03.2019, the students who have been approved for the pending scholarship applications for the year 2018-19 will be allowed to distribute the scholarship for the year 2019-20. The approval has been accorded vide Government letter dated 14.05.2019. Accordingly, as per the decision of the Government dated 14.06.2019, 30.07.2019 and 14.11.2019, the amount of scholarship under Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme has been approved to be paid from the sanctioned amount in the year 2019-20. Now, as per the letter dated 02.12.2019 addressed to the Director, Higher Education, the matter of payment of remaining scholarship amount for the year 2018-19 under the proposed scheme was under consideration of the Government.
