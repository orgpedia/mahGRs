The proposal:
The Chairperson and Members were appointed by the State Government in accordance with the Government decision in Reference No. (1) to the Governing Board of the Government Engineering College, Pune, an autonomous institution functioning in the State, which has been granted autonomy in accordance with the Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme (TEQIP) Phase 1 and 2. The said Governing Body is constituted as per the provisions of the notification issued by the University Grants Commission. Further, in place of the Chairman of the Board of Regulators appointed under the Government decision in Reference No. (1), the Chairman of the Board of Regulators in the said institution has been re-appointed by the Government Corrigendum in Reference No. (2).
The term of office of the Board appointed under the Government Resolution referred to in (1) above shall expire at the end of 2020. As the matter of appointment of members and chairpersons of the Board of Governance (BoG) of 6 Government Engineering Colleges, 2 Government Owned 100% Aided Engineering Colleges and 1 Hotel Management and Catering Technology College in the State is under consideration, the appointment of members appointed by the State Government on the Board of Governors of Government Engineering Institutions under Government Decision, Reference No. (1) and (2) has been cancelled under Government Decision dated 10.01.2020.
