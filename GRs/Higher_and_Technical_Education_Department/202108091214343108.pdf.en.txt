The Principal and the Head of the Department who have been transferred for administrative reasons shall be entitled to the period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of the Government. The Principal and Head of the Department, who are transferred on request, will not be allowed to hold office for the period of the appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance.
The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
2) After the transfer order is issued, the concerned principal and head of the department should join the institution immediately after their transfer from the existing institution. The principals of the institutions should immediately relieve the transferred principal and head of the department without waiting for the appearance of the alternate principal and head of the department. The transferred officer/ employee shall ensure that he/ she joins the place of transfer within the period of his/ her appointment as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Duration of Appointment, Non-Owner Service and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. If he does not join the post of the replacement during this period, the period of his absence shall be deemed to be a non-working day.
3) The transferred principals and heads of departments should be recruited without delay after they are relieved from their respective institutions and come to join. The Principal of the institution concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-appointment of a Principal or Head of Department.
4) No leave of the transferred Principal or Head of Department should be sanctioned without prior approval of the Administrative Department.
5) It will be the responsibility of the respective institution heads personally to relieve the transferred principals and department heads immediately. If the principal does not relieve the concerned, the director, technical education, should initiate administrative action against them and submit the proposal to the government for approval as needed. Further, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education shall submit the compliance report in this regard to this Department within 10 days from the date of this order without fail.
