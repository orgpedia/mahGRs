Government Resolution, Department of Higher and Technical Education, No. Committee-2017/ Q. No. 553/2017/ Special-3, D. December 12, 2017. Order dated February 6, 2018.
In view of the delay in the results of the first semester of the academic year 2017-18 of the University of Mumbai, in the interest of the students, a detailed investigation of the On-Screen Marking System (OSM) of the results of the University of Mumbai has been initiated. A three-member committee has been constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Ministry of Information and Technology, Mumbai vide Government Resolution dated 12.12.2017. The committee has to submit its report to the government. The deadline has been extended to May 31, 2018.
