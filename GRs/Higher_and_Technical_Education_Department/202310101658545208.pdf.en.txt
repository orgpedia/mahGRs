Introduction: Department of Higher and Technical Education As per the decision of the Government on 15th November 2007, a figure of 352 teaching posts has been sanctioned for Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University. In this framework, 62 teaching posts have been created for the Lakshminarayan Technical Institute run by the university. The post of Director was not created in the approved format. The report submitted by Dr. Ganapati Yadav Committee regarding granting autonomy to this institution. Following a public interest litigation filed in the High Court, funds have been provided by the government to strengthen the institution. A full-time director is required to work in the organization to complete the various projects to be started with this fund within the time limit. Accordingly, out of 62 sanctioned teaching posts for Lakshminarayan Technical Institute, 1 out of 10 posts in the faculty cadre has been approved to be converted into" Director" cadre vide Government Resolution dated 30.12.2022. The approval has been accorded ex-post facto approval in the meeting dated 13.09.2023 of the" Sub-Committee constituted to thoroughly examine and recommend proposals for creation of new posts/ revival of posts and review of posts etc. and to approve creation of notional posts and filling up of vacancies" of the Finance Department. Accordingly, orders are being issued as under: Government Supplementary Letter: Out of 62 sanctioned teaching posts for Lakshminarayan Technical Institute run by Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur, 1 post out of 10 posts in the faculty cadre is being issued.
