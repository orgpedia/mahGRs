Preamble: It was under consideration of the Government to appoint a supplier for supply of Recycling Toner for printers for the period from 06.01.2021 to 05.01.2022 (1 year) for the Department of Higher and Technical Education (H& T), Mantralaya, Mumbai. Accordingly, following the prevailing procedure, the sealed tariff sheets were called for on 22.12.2020 from the website of Government of Maharashtra. Accordingly, a total of 8 tariff sheets received by the Department were considered in the meeting of the Departmental Procurement Committee held on 01.01.2021. All the members of the Committee and their representatives attended the meeting. In the meeting, it was decided to appoint a supplier for the supply of printer's recycling toner for this section from among the tenderers qualified in the technical examination as follows.
Government Decision: As per the recommendation of the Departmental Procurement Committee, out of the eligible bidders, Rs. Active Enterprises, 9, Savitribai Sadan Chawl, Carter Road No. Rideongri, Borivali (East), Mumbai- 400 066 are the lowest rates. Accordingly, the Department of Higher and Technical Education (DTH), Ministry of Education, Govt. 06.01.2021 to dt. 05.01.2022 (1 year) for supply of Recycling Toner of printers required during this period. Active Enterprises (M/ s Active Enterprises), Borivali (PU), Mumbai is being appointed as supplier at the rate mentioned in the following statement.
