Government Decision: In the financial year 2022-23, the outstanding preservation grant of Government Recognized Public Libraries for the year 2021-22 is Rs. 33,70,41, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Three Crores Seventy Lakhs Forty One Thousand only) to the Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai. Accordingly, the concerned District Library Officer/ Head of Office is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
