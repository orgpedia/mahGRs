By a notification dated 13.02.2019, the Dr. Homi Bhabha Group of Universities, Mumbai has been constituted with the existing Institute of Science, Mumbai as the main college and the constituent colleges being Elphinstone College, Mumbai, Sydenham College, Mumbai and Secondary Training College, Mumbai. It is now proposed to equip the University with state-of-the-art machinery/ equipment. What kind of equipment can be purchased? The committee was constituted under the chairmanship of Director, Institute of Science, Mumbai. The committee has recommended the equipment to the university. According to this, the state-of-the-art equipment proposed to be purchased are Semiconductor Parameter Analyzer with Probe Station, Vector Network Analyzer with Accessories, Psychometric Instruments, Battery Cycler with Accessories, Gas Chromatography Mass Spectrometry, XRD Floor Model and Workstation. Accordingly, the University vide Reference No. 1 The proposal has been submitted here. The research scholars will be benefitted if these essential facilities/ equipments are made available. These devices will also be used to speed up the separation of samples of various substances under research. As per the proposal, administrative approval for the purchase of equipment was under the consideration of the government.
