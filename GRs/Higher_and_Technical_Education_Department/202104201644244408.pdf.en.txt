Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has invited online applications from eligible candidates for the post of Assistant Secretary (Technical) in Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education. Accordingly, the orders have been issued in this regard. In paragraph 05 of the said order, the Assistant Secretary (Technical Education) shall automatically cease to hold the charge of the post of Assistant Secretary (s), Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (Head Office and all concerned Divisional Offices) of the teachers of the teaching cadre in the Government Technicians working on deputation/ ad-hoc arrangement in the concerned office from the date on which the above candidates appear at the place of their appointment. The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), Maharashtra State Council of Technical Education (MSSCTE) and all the concerned departmental offices have been relieved of the duties of the teachers working in the government technical colleges. At present, the matter of posting the teachers to the vacant post in their parent cadre was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: With the approval of the competent authority for transfer of Maharashtra Government employees, the following posts of the Maharashtra Technician Teachers Service Group-A under the Directorate of Technical Education, Government Technicians and similar institutions are being transferred and posted as per Pillar-4.
