Read:- 1) Government Circular, General Administration Department No.CFR-1217/ Q.No. 131/13, d. December 15, 2017.
Government Decision: In the context of the General Administration Department. As per the Government Circular dated 15.12.2017, the performance appraisal reports of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre Officers are proposed to be recorded online in MahaPAR system from the year 2017-18.
2.D. The Director, Directorate of Arts, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being appointed as the Primary Custodian for online recording of performance appraisal reports of all Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers in the office of the Director, Directorate of Arts, Maharashtra State, Mumbai as mentioned in Para-5 of the Government Circular dated 15.12.2017.
The Organization Unit (OU) is being set up for all Government Arts Colleges under the Directorate of Arts to register the performance evaluation reports of all Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers of that office in the online system. :

4. Deputy Director, Directorate of Art, Maharashtra State, Mumbai is being appointed as an Additional Custodian for the offices of Inspector, Painting and Sculpture, Mumbai, Assistant Inspector, Painting and Sculpture, Nagpur and Aurangabad Divisional Office under the Directorate of Art and Directorate of Art, for online recording of performance appraisal reports of all Group A and Group B (Gazetted) cadre officers of that office.
For all Government Arts Colleges under the Directorate of Arts, the Deans of the respective colleges are being appointed as additional custodians to record the performance appraisal reports of all Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers of the office in the online system.
6. Subject to submission of performance appraisal report online in the Mahapar system. In view of the instructions in the Government Circular dated 15.12.2017, the Department of Art& Culture has been advised to provide updated information (EMD (Employee Master Data) in the form of Annexure D) of all Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers in the offices under the Directorate of Arts. The same has been communicated to the Directorate of Arts vide letter dated 15.01.2018. The additional conservators shall update the details of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers in their subordinate offices in Excel format in the form of Annexure D. EMD
The mobile number mentioned in the application should be linked to the Aadhaar number, so that it is easy to use the e-sign facility in the system. Additional custodians may declare one or more Par Managers under their control as per requirement.
