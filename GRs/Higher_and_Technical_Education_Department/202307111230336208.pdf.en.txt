Preamble: Dr. Homi Bhabha State University, Institute of Science forming part of the University of Mumbai, approves the demolition of the dilapidated Nuclear and Radiation Laboratory building in the Mumbai premises, to disburse funds for the demolition of the building Reference No. 1 has been requested. The building of the Nuclear and Radiological Laboratory dates back to 1984 and has been in existence for 39 years. The building is scheduled for completion in January 2023. As per the BMC's assessment, the building falls under the C-1 (dangerous/ collapsible) category.
The expenditure required for the demolition of the said building is provided in the Budget Reference No. Technical approval has been given under the letter here and Rs. Price Rs 11,11,049. In view of the PRB value of the building, the following sequence of financial powers in Annexure 42 of the Maharashtra Public Works Rules, 1984, is provided in paragraph 46. 368 page 39.40 (a), the administrative department is authorized to demolish dangerous or non-degradable buildings. Accordingly, the matter of granting approval for the demolition of the said structure and administrative approval for the necessary expenditure is under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: This Government has approved the demolition of the building of the Nuclear and Radiation Laboratory in the premises of the Institute of Science under Dr. Homi Bhabha State University, Mumbai and the construction of the building for the purpose of Rs. Administrative Sanction is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions as per the attached budget of Rs. 11,11,049/- (Rupees Eleven Lakhs Eleven Thousand and Nineteen Fifty Five only).
Terms and Conditions: 1. When dealing with a provision made in a lump-sum manner, the work should be taken up only after making a detailed budget.
If the work falls under the jurisdiction of the Department of Antiquities, prior permission of the concerned department as well as the Municipal Corporation and similar authorities should be obtained.
