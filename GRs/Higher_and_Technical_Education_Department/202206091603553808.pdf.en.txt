As per the provisions of Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, the General Administration Department has laid down a consolidated procedure for reviewing the services of officers/ employees at the age of 50/ 55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. As per the provisions of the said Government decision, the Government was contemplating to constitute a' Divisional Review Committee' to review the services of Group-A and Group-B officers of the Directorate of Technical Education (itself) and the institutions/ colleges/ offices under it at the age of 50/ 55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
Government Order:
""" A\"" Departmental Review Committee\"" is being constituted for reviewing the services of Group A and Group B officers of the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) and its subordinate institutions/ colleges/ offices on the basis of criteria prescribed for determining their eligibility to continue in service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier:"""

The Director, Technical Education, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall submit a proposal to the Government regarding the review of services of Group-A and Group-B cadre officers in the Directorate of Technical Education (itself) and institutions/ colleges/ offices under it. The recommendation of the officers who will be eligible/ ineligible to be retained in service as per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee will be submitted to the appropriate authorities as mentioned in Annexure-A along with the Government decision dated 10.6.2019 in the context of General Administration Department for final decision.
The functions of the Departmental Review Committee:
(a) The Committee shall commence its review proceedings in the month of August every year and complete the proceedings by the end of that year on 31st December.
(b) During the review, the record of physical fitness, physical fitness, unquestionable integrity and not less than good record of the officers to test their eligibility for continuing their government service shall be maintained in a confidential record. Also note that confidential reports from 2017-18 should have a performance evaluation score of 4 or higher.
(c) The officers who are found to be undisputedly ineligible to continue in service on the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee shall be reviewed by the" Special Review Committee" of the General Administration Department mentioned in Annexure-A to the Government Resolution dated 10.6.2019.
