Preamble:- The Director of Technical Education, New Delhi, as per the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, has approved the grant of permanent recognition to the institutions of unaided professional courses for increasing the intake capacity of the courses in the existing unaided institution, starting new courses, merging the existing courses with another course, etc. Proposed vide letter dated 03.08.2023.
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the matter of granting Government approval as indicated against the names of the institutions in Annexure A from the academic year 2023-24 was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
In pursuance of the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is granting approval on permanent non-grant basis subject to prevailing terms and conditions for increasing the intake capacity of the courses in the existing unaided institution mentioned in Annexure-A from the academic year 2023-2024, starting new courses, merging existing courses with other courses, etc.
Terms and Conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
2) In this case, as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.
(3) The institution shall be required to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
(4) The private institution concerned shall meet the other legal requirements by depositing the requisite consolidated deposit with the Director, Technical Education, in a joint account.
