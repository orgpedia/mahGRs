Preamble: The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has given its approval for introduction of B. Vocational under NSQF in the existing institution, increase in intake capacity of the existing courses, closure of courses, etc. Regarding grant of Government Recognition to the Institutions recognized by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Director referred to Reference No. The proposal has been submitted to the government through a letter here. A decision in this regard was pending with the government.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the approval of the Government is being given for starting of B. Vocational degree course under NSQF in the existing institutions of the State as indicated in Annexure-A, Annexure-B and Annexure-C in the academic year 2020-2021 along with this Government Decision, subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
(ii) B.Voc. degree by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi under Community College Scheme.
(NSQF Level VII) It will be mandatory for the concerned institution and skill knowledge provider to comply with the special rules, terms and conditions created for the implementation of the degree course.

3) For the affiliation of the said course, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
4) Admission to the said course shall be made at the Institute level as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi in its Approval Process Handbook 2020-2021.
(5) The institution shall take steps in accordance with the rules for obtaining approval of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for the extension of the said course.
6) Since the course is being implemented under the Community College Scheme and the admission of students is being done at the institution level, the benefit of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme will not be permissible to the students admitted to this course.
