The proposal:
The Maharashtra CoEP University of Technology Act, 2022 has been notified vide Gazette dated 10th May, 2022 in the above context, converting the erstwhile Government College of Engineering, Pune into CoEP University of Technology, Pune. As per Section 22 (1) of the said Act, the Governing Body of the University is required to be constituted and Rule 23 (4) specifies the composition of the Governing Body of the University.
The matter of appointment of the Chairman of the Governing Body of the University was under consideration of the Government as per the provision of Section 86 (1) (2) of the Maharashtra CoEP University of Technology Act, 2022. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The government's decision:
In accordance with the provisions of Section 9 (1) of the Maharashtra CoEP University of Technology Act, 2022, the following dignitaries are being approved by the State Government to be appointed as the Chairman of the Governing Body of the Maharashtra CoEP University of Technology, Pune for a period of five years:
