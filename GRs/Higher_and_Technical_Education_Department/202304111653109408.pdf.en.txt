No. 434/15/ Masi-2, Dy. 19.1.2016 Preamble: In pursuance of the above order, a Committee has been constituted to fix the tuition fees of the unaided and permanent unaided colleges of Pedagogy and Physical Education in the State. The matter of sanctioning honorarium to the non-official members of this committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Non-government members are appointed on the committee formed to determine the tuition fees of unaided and permanent unaided pedagogical and physical education colleges in the state. The members attend the meetings of the committee on a daily basis. In view of this, the Government is approving payment of honorarium of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousand only) per sitting to the non-official members in lieu of travel allowance/ sitting allowance.
