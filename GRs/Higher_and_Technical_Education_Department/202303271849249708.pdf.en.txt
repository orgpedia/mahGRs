The proposal:
The President and members of the Board of Governors of the Autonomous Engineering Colleges of the State are appointed by the Government. - (4) The Government hereby revoke the decision. A regulatory body needs to be constituted to run the present autonomous engineering college in an orderly manner.
In this regard, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education had submitted a proposal to the government under reference number (5) regarding the appointment of chairperson and government-appointed members on the governing board of the autonomous government engineering colleges of Government Engineering College, Jalgaon, Karad, Amravati, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar and Chandrapur. Accordingly, the matter of forming a new governing body of these institutions was under the consideration of the government. For this purpose, the following decisions are taken:
The government has decided to reduce the number of boards in the autonomous government engineering colleges of Jalgaon, Government Engineering College, Karad, Government Engineering College, Amravati, Government Engineering College, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar and Government Engineering College, Chandrapur.
Further, the period of appointment of members other than ex-officio members on the Board of Governors of the above mentioned bodies shall be reckoned from the date of such decision of the Government and the period of such members shall ordinarily be two years.
The Chairman and Members of the Board of Management shall be entitled to travel concessions, travel allowance and meeting allowance payable to an officer of the level of Secretary to the State of Maharashtra for the meetings of the Board of Directors and other related business of the Institute, which have been curtailed under this Government Decision.
