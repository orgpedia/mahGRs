(2) The term of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall not be admissible to the assistant professors who have been transferred on request.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The following instructions shall be strictly complied with by the Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution so that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
(2) On receipt of the transfer orders, the Principal/ Director concerned shall relieve the transferred Assistant Professors to join the place of transfer immediately without waiting for the replacement of the transferred Assistant Professors. Also, the concerned Assistant Professors should join the college/ institution immediately after their transfer from the existing college/ institution.
3.The transferred Assistant Professors should be recruited without delay after they are relieved from the concerned college/ institution and come to join. The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be held responsible in case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-appointment of the concerned Assistant Professor.

4.No request for change in post will be entertained.
If the concerned Assistant Professor directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the concerned Assistant Professor to modify the transfer order, the concerned Assistant Professor will be liable to take disciplinary action.
5. No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
Since the transfers are required to be effected with immediate effect, the salary of the transferred Assistant Professors up to the date of retirement or up to the maximum date of 31st January, 2021 shall be drawn by the college/ institution where they are presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer college/ institution under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the college/ institution after the transfer of the concerned assistant professor to the transferor college/ institution. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from February 1, 2021 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the college/ institution where they were working before the transfer.
The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be personally responsible for the immediate release of the transferred Assistant Professors.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune should submit the report to the government before February 1, 2021.
