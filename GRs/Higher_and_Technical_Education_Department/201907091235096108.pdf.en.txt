Preamble: An Act to provide for the reservation of seats for admission to educational institutions in the State and for the reservation of appointments and posts in the public services under the control of the State and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto for the advancement of the socially and educationally backward classes of citizens (SEBC) in the State of Maharashtra. 1 of the Government of Maharashtra. It was published on November 30, 2018.
Issue No. Under Section 4 (1) (a), a total of 16% of the seats for admission in aided and unaided educational institutions in the state have been reserved for the socially and educationally backward (SEBC) category of citizens, including the Maratha community. Also Reference No. As per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra, orders have been issued to reserve 10% seats for admission in government services and educational institutions for the economically weaker sections of the open category in the state of Maharashtra. Also, the decision No. Under Section 4, it has been ordered that the necessary orders regarding admission in educational institutions should be issued by all the concerned departments immediately. Accordingly, 16% seats will be reserved for the Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) including the Maratha community and 10% seats for the economically weaker sections in the open category for admission to all public non-agricultural university educational institutions except minority educational institutions in the state. 3 here. The government circular was issued on March 8, 2019.

However, in order to further amend the Maharashtra State Reservation of Seats for Admission in Educational Institutions in the State and of Appointments and Posts in the Public Services under the Control of the State) Act, 2018 for the Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBCs) in the State of Maharashtra, the said Act may be brought in the aforesaid Reference No. 4 of the Government of Maharashtra. It was published on July 3, 2019. Issue No. Under Section 2 (c) of the Act, 12% of seats in aided and unaided educational institutions in the state have been reserved for the Socially and Educationally Backward (SEBC) category, including the Maratha community, instead of the total 16%. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to issue a circular in this regard to direct all non-agricultural universities under this Department to reserve 12% seats for the Socially and Educationally Backward (SEBC) category of citizens for admission in educational institutions in the State of Maharashtra.
Government Circular: Reference No. As per Rule 4, 12% seats should be reserved for the socially and educationally backward (SEBC) classes, including the Maratha community, for admission to educational institutions in all public non-agricultural universities in the state of Maharashtra instead of 16%.
10% of the seats reserved for economically weaker sections in the open category will continue as notified by the Government Circular at Reference No. 3.
The reservation in admission to educational institutions prescribed by the government will be applicable to all non-agricultural universities, deemed universities, law universities, self-financing universities, all aided and unaided colleges and autonomous colleges affiliated to them, except minority educational institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education in the state.
It will be the responsibility of the concerned universities to ensure effective implementation of this order.
If the universities/ institutions have framed rules/ regulations for admission to educational institutions and reservations of various social groups have been indicated in them, then in accordance with this reservation, the concerned should make necessary amendments in the rules/ regulations as per the prescribed procedure.
5. All the Universities are advised to bring this circular to the notice of all the educational institutions under them.
