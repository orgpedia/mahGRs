Preamble: The Government Decision in Reference No. (1) dated 31st August, 2007 regarding making it mandatory for students admitted to Diploma in Art Education Training (ATD) and Basic Course (Foundation) Courses run by Art Institutions under the control of Directorate of Art to pass the Intermediate Drawing Examination in the academic year in which they are admitted has been issued. However, in view of the outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic in the State, it is proposed not to conduct the Government Drawing (Elementary and Intermediate Drawing Grade) Examination, 2020 in the interest of the health and safety of the students. Therefore, it is proposed to relax the condition of passing the Government Drawing Examination for the students admitted to Diploma in Art Education Training and Basic Courses, as per the letter in Reference No. 5 of the Directorate of Arts.
In view of the outbreak of the COVID-19 epidemic in the state, as well as considering the health and safety of the students, the following decisions are being taken regarding the conduct of Government Drawing Examinations, 2020.
The government's decision:
Due to the outbreak of the global epidemic of COVID-19 in the state, the Government Drawing Exam, 2020 should not be conducted considering the health and safety of the students.
Students appearing for the Secondary School Certificate (Class 10) examination in the academic year 2020-21 should not be given additional marks of concession in the examination to the government line.
The students who have passed the Government level examination in the CET examination to be conducted for admission to undergraduate/ postgraduate courses in visual arts for the academic year 2021-22 shall not be given concessional marks.

Two courses namely Diploma in Art Education Training (ATD) and Basic Course (Foundation) are being exempted from the condition of passing the Intermediate Drawing Grade Examination for the students admitted for the academic year 2020-21.
The present relaxation shall be limited to the students who have taken admission in two courses namely, Art Education Training Diploma (ATD) and Basic Course (Foundation) in the academic year 2020-21 and for the academic year 2020-21 only.
The fact that the students admitted to the diploma courses under the control of the Directorate of Technical Education for the academic year 2021-22 have passed the government examination should not be taken into account at the time of admission.
