The date: March 23, 2018.
Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University Vice-Chancellor Dr. B.A. Complaints have been made to the President and the Prime Minister. His statement is in the annexure attached to this order.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: 1. Dr. S. F. Patil, Retired Vice-Chancellor is being appointed to conduct a preliminary inquiry in respect of the complaint in Annexure A.
2. Dr. S. F. Patil, Inquiry Officer, should be provided with the documents required during the inquiry by the University.
3. The Inquiry Officer may record the statements of the concerned as required for the purpose of inquiry.
It shall be the responsibility of the Registrar of the concerned University to provide all the necessary documents in accordance with the above enquiry. This will be assisted by the Deputy Registrar/ Joint Registrar of the University.
Registrar Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad will be responsible for providing vehicle/ guest house and other necessary facilities to Patil and the investigating officer.
Separate orders will be issued regarding the honorarium and other incidental allowances to Dr. S. F. Patil, retired Vice-Chancellor.
The investigating officer should submit his preliminary fact-finding report directly to the government within 2 months.
