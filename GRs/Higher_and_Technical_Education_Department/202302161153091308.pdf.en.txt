About 853 acres of Shivaji University campus is located on the east and west side of the old Pune-Bangalore highway under the control of the Kolhapur Municipal Corporation. At present, the old Pune-Bangalore highway is being widened by the Kolhapur Municipal Corporation to four lanes and a median divider and pedestrian footpath of 33.00 m in general. The road widening work is underway. In the western part of the university, the main administrative building and the buildings of various departments, sports complex etc. built in the last fifty years have been installed. Also in the eastern part are the university's Faculty of Technology, School of Nano Science and Technology, Shahu Research Center, Pedagogy, etc. In the future, it is proposed to construct buildings like Yashwantrao Chavan School of Rural Development, Journalism and Mass Communication Center, Convention Center etc. in the eastern side area.
Around 2,000 to 3,000 people, including students, parents, teachers, citizens and visitors, pass through this main road every day. As the road divides the university from the Pune-Bengaluru expressway, it is unsafe for students and faculty members travelling from the eastern side of the university to the western side due to the ever-increasing traffic of high-speed vehicles coming to and going from Kolhapur city.
