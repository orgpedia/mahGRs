The proposal:
The Vice-Chancellors of all non-agriculture universities in the state have been asked to review the final year/ final semester examinations of all non-agriculture universities in the state. A meeting was held on 23 rd October, 2020 through video conferencing under the chairmanship of Minister, Higher and Technical Education. A detailed discussion was held by all the members present in the meeting. A committee should be formed to inquire into the technical errors regarding the online examination of the universities, with the Director, Higher Education, Director, Technical Education and Deputy Secretary (VC), experts in technical subjects, as well as the Vice-Chancellor, Registrar and Director of the concerned university, the Board of Examinations and Evaluation as invited members. Minister of Higher and Technical Education.
Accordingly, the Department of Higher and Technical Education, No. (1) Under the decision of the Government here, a committee was formed under the chairmanship of the Director, Directorate of Information Technology, Mantralaya, Mumbai, to investigate the technical errors regarding the online examination of non-agricultural universities in the state. However, since the mandate of the said Committee is to inquire into technical glitches, it is requested that the said Committee may be partially modified and Mr. Mukesh Somkunwar, Cell Officer (Technical) may be appointed in place of the Director, Directorate of Information Technology, Mantralaya, Mumbai. 2 is written here. Accordingly, the government was considering the matter of reconstituting the committee by making partial changes in the committee.
