Introduction: The Maharashtra State Art Exhibition has been organized by the Directorate of Art since 1955-56. The exhibition will be held in two sections- Art Department and Art Department. The vision behind the exhibition is to promote the talent among the students and artists of the state and to provide a platform to their talent power. The practice of awarding prizes to works of art exhibited was started from 1962 with the aim of attracting emerging artists to the exhibition and getting maximum response.
The matter of increasing the amount of these awards and honorarium in accordance with the time was under the consideration of the government, accordingly, the following decision has been taken.
Government Decision: The rates of prizes and honorarium given under the Student Section and Artist Section in the Maharashtra State Art Exhibition have been revised as follows.
