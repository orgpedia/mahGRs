Preamble:- Construction of a pedestal, landscape and beautification and construction of a tower for the memorial of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar in the campus of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University. 14,24,92, 238/- in the Budget Reference No. 1 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. In accordance with the said Administrative Sanction Reference No. A total of Rs 2. 2,73,91, 000/- has been disbursed. Since funds are required for further construction, the University has decided to disburse the remaining funds as per Reference No. 3 has been requested. Reference No. 4 of the Finance Department. Circular dated 12.04.2023 provides for release of funds for 2023-24. The matter of providing funds for the construction of the building was under the consideration of the government.
Decision of the Government:- Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar University, Solapur will construct a memorial and tower for Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar at a cost of Rs. 4,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
The fund should be disbursed and withdrawn within the limits of administrative approval.
(2) No expenditure shall be incurred in excess of the work-wise provision brought to the notice of the Legislature during the current financial year.
Under no circumstances should the concerned Superintending Engineer spend more than the amount of administrative sanction on the works.
The concerned Executive Engineer shall arrange to send a report on the progress of the work and the expenditure incurred to the Registrar and Director of Higher Education of the concerned university.
(5) No revised administrative approval will be required for the subject work. Special attention should be paid to this.
