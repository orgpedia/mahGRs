Government Resolution, Department of Higher and Technical Education, No. SCP-2008/ (143/08)/ Mashi-2, dated 28th January, 2009.
Preamble: If the Government Junior College under the Directorate of Higher Education, under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, cannot provide the required workload for a part-time teacher for a subject, then a professor is appointed on a clock-hour basis to teach such subject. Under the decision of the government, the government was considering to revise the rate of honorarium applied to the superiors working on a clock-hour basis in the government junior colleges under the Directorate of Higher Education.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Approval is being given to revise the rates of honorarium of the superintendents working on a clock-hour basis in the Government Junior Colleges under the Directorate of Higher Education as follows.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A.                      | the Junior Advocate     | Permissible Rates (Per Hourly) as on 28/01/2009 | Revised Rate (per hour) |
| 1                         | Trained Junior Advocate | Rs.72/                                          | Rs. 150/-               |
| Untrained Junior Advocate | Rs.54/                  | Rs. 120/-                                       |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The revised rates will be applicable from October 1, 2021.
