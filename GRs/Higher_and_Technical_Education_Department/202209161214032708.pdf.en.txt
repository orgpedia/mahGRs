Introduction: The Council of Architecture, New Delhi. Pursuant to the approval given vide letter dated 02.09.2022, the Director, Technical Education has decided to conduct the D. Satish Misal Educational Foundation's Brick School of Architecture, as proposed vide letter dated 13.09.2022. The Government was considering to grant approval to start M.Arch (Design and Project Management) course in Pune from the academic year 2022-23 with an intake capacity of 20.
Pursuant to the approval given by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi and as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the Brick School of Architecture, Satish Misal Educational Foundation will be opened from the academic year 2022-23. The Government is granting approval to start M.Arch (Design and Project Management) at Pune from the academic year 2022-23 on permanent non-grant basis with an intake capacity of 20 subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1) The grant will be made on a non-contributory basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed by the Council of Architecture in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The institution's intake capacity for this course will be as mentioned above.
3) The institution will have to sign an agreement on the acceptance of all terms and conditions prescribed by the government and prescribed by the Council of Architecture. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
4) The institution concerned shall meet the other legalities by depositing the required accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education.
5) Admission to the said Post Graduate Degree Course shall be made as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the relevant course as approved by the Government.
