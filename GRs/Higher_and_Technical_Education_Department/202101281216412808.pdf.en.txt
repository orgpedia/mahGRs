The proposal:
Well-equipped and state-of-the-art facilities for the scientific study, development, dissemination and propagation of music
The government is planning to set up a Master Deenanath Mangeshkar International College of Music under the University of Mumbai. The report will be submitted to the government after examining all aspects.
The government had constituted a committee for the purpose.
Government Decision: A committee is being constituted to study and submit a report to the Government regarding the establishment of Master Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College under the University of Mumbai for the scientific study, development, promotion and propagation of music.

The functions of the Committee will be as follows: 1) To study the infrastructure required for the establishment of the Music College, such as premises, building, hostel, recording studio, practice room, equipment, etc., and to submit an estimate of the cost.
2) To study the courses taught in the Music College, their duration and the faculty required for it and to present an estimate of the expenditure incurred for it.
3) To study the professional opportunities available to the students who have studied in the Music College and recommend to the government.
4) To determine the stages of work to be undertaken in connection with the establishment of the Music College and to make recommendations to the Government.
The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within a period of three months after considering all relevant aspects of the matter.
The meetings of the said Committee shall be convened by the University of Mumbai. The University of Mumbai should also take action to pay the honorarium and travel allowance of the non-official members of the committee.
