The proposal:
The Director of Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the government under a letter of reference to discontinue two courses (Diploma in Computer Application)- 60 and ADSTM (Advanced Diploma in Software Technology Management) 60. Accordingly, the matter of approving the closure of this course was under consideration.
The government's decision:
The government has decided to discontinue Diploma in Computer Application (DCA)-60 and Advanced Diploma in Software Technology Management (ADSTM)-60 from the academic year 2019-20.
This Government approval is being granted subject to the terms and conditions prescribed by the Kavaitri Bahinabai Chaudhary Uttar Maharashtra University while issuing a No-Objection Certificate in this regard on 21/2/2018.
