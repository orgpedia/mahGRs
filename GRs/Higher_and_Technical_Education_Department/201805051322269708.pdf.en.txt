Under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Phule, Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj, Sahityaratna Lokshahir Annabhau Sathe and Maharaja Sayajirao Gaekwad Charitra Sadhan Publication Committees have been formed. The literature published by these committees all over the world
The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has provided funds to create a separate web portal of the department to make it accessible to all the people by digitizing it. The Department of Information and Technology has been entrusted with the task. As per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 01/07/2014, a Consultancy Company was to be selected through PIC. According to the KPMG has been selected as Consultant Company in the PIC meeting held on 15/03/2018 to prepare the RFP and to undertake the control activities related to the implementation of the project in the pre-tender and post-tender approvals. Accordingly, the issue of issuing a government decision of the web portal was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
Under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Phule, Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj, Sahityaratna Lokshahir Annabhau Sathe and Maharaja Sayajirao Gaekwad Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samitis have been formed. KPMG, a consultancy firm, has been roped in to prepare the RFP for the purpose of digitising the material published by these committees and creating a separate web portal of the department for the public to access the same.
