The proposal:
To strengthen primary education in the state.
With the success of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan and Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan, the Government has decided to implement Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) as an umbrella scheme in the 12th and 13th Five Year Plans to empower the quality and foundation of higher education in the form of Mission mode. The National Mission on Higher Education (NMHE), a flagship programme of the Government of India, aims to ensure quality, equity and opportunity in higher education in Maharashtra. The decision was taken at the Cabinet meeting held on 21.04.2015.
In pursuance of the decision taken by the State Cabinet, a State Project Directorate has been set up for effective implementation of the scheme in the State. Under the scheme, the Centre and the state have decided to share 60:40 subsidy for non-agriculture universities and government institutions.
