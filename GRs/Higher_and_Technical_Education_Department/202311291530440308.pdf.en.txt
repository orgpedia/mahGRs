Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur University was established on 4th August 1923. The University has started building some new projects keeping in view the physical needs of the population and future development prospects of the university campus. Accordingly, for various constructions in the University, Reference No. 1 The administrative approval has been given in principle as per the decision of the government here. The construction includes the Technology and Energy Park building.
As per the approval of the High Level Secretary Committee for the construction of the Technology and Energy Park building, Rs. 4412.43 lakh in the Budget Reference No. 2 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. Now that funds need to be released for this work, the Finance Department was requested to release funds for this work. The Finance Department has agreed to release funds for this work. Accordingly, the matter of disbursing funds for the construction of the Technology and Energy Park building was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision:- Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur The construction of the Technology and Energy Park building to be built in Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University will cost Rs. The Government is pleased to approve the release of funds amounting to Rs. 20,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Crores only) subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. The fund shall be disbursed and withdrawn within the limits of administrative sanction.
(2) No revised administrative approval will be required for the subject work. This should be done with special attention.
Further, all the documents and accounts of the above grant are to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General-2, Nagpur, as the case may be.
The grant should be used for the same purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned to the University.
Also, it should be ensured that the expenditure is fully met within the stipulated period and the utilization certificate should be submitted to the government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune within the prescribed time.
The progress report of the above work should be submitted to the government from time to time. Further funds will be released based on the progress of the work.
