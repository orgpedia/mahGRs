The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has decided to change the name of the Institute of Diploma in Engineering from the academic year 2020-21. 1 has been approved. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide their aforesaid Reference No. A proposal has been submitted to the government in this regard. In pursuance of the proposal, the Government was considering to approve the change in the name of the institution of the existing Diploma in Engineering course from the academic year 2020-21.
The government's decision:
The following" Form-A" Named Institutions are being approved by the Government to change the name of the Institution for Diploma in Engineering course as per the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi from the academic year 2020-21.
