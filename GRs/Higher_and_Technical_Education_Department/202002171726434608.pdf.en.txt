Circular:
In order to inculcate the spirit of nationalism in the minds of young students pursuing higher education in Maharashtra, special efforts are required to be made by educational institutions to inculcate the spirit of national integration and to inculcate the spirit of patriotism in the younger generation. For this, the following action should be taken: (1) All non-agricultural universities, all deemed universities, all self-financing universities and all government/ non-government, aided/ unaided/ permanent unaided and model degree colleges and institutes under the Directorate of Higher Education, as well as government, non-government, aided, unaided institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education and the Directorate of Arts, shall recite the National Anthem before the commencement of daily classes of students.
(2) The programmes such as Sneh Sammelans, anniversaries, valedictory functions, group activities, seminars, camps, symposiums on various subjects, debate competitions, etc. should be started with the singing of the National Anthem.
(3) All the Directors and the Universities concerned shall immediately communicate the above instructions to the colleges/ technical institutes/ institutes under them at their level. All colleges/ institutions/ institutions should take appropriate action in this regard.
