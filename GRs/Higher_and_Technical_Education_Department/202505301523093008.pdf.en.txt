In case of payment of allowances, the principal concerned will be held responsible. Also, no leave of absence shall be granted to such teachers during the period from discharge to joining the place of transfer.
The period of assumption of office shall be admissible as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption, non-acceptance of service and period of suspension, dismissal or removal from service) Rules, 1981 for the transfer of transferred faculty to join the place of transfer.
6. The Director of Higher Education, Pune should ensure that the next month's (June) salary of the transferred teachers is drawn from the transferred college itself.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune, shall submit the performance report on the transfer order to the Government within 15 days.
8. The said Government Order shall be issued by the Government of Maharashtra.
