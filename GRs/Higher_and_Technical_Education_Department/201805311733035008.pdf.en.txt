GOVERNMENT ORDER NO.: ODR 2724/ PR No. 72/18 (Part-II)/ TANSI-6
(1) This order shall take effect immediately.
(2) On receipt of the transfer order, the concerned superiors shall join the institution from which they are presently working immediately. Also, the heads of the concerned institutions should give the transferred advocates without waiting for them to appear before the alternate advocate. to be discharged in any case by 30.06.2018.
(3) The transferred officials shall be reinstated without any further delay after they have been relieved from their duties by the institution concerned. The Dean of the institution concerned shall be held liable in case of non-payment of salary and allowances without work due to compulsory waiting period for non-recruitment of a domicile.
(4) No request for change of posting shall be entertained.
If any advocate directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order to be modified, such advocate shall be liable for disciplinary action.
(5) No leave of the transferred designations shall be granted without the prior approval of the Administrative Department.
(6) Since the said transfers are required to be effected effectively, all the transferred posts shall remain vacant till the date of their retirement or till the maximum date of their retirement.
Salaries up to 30.06.2018 are to be drawn at the establishment where they are presently working.
Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the establishment before the transfer under any circumstances. The salary shall be drawn by the post-transfer establishment after joining the institution of transfer to the concerned domicile.
(7) The period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to those who are transferred for administrative reasons as per the rules of the Government. The transferred personnel should be immediately transferred to their place of posting. The Director, Directorate of Arts, Government of Maharashtra, Mumbai should submit a report to the government.
