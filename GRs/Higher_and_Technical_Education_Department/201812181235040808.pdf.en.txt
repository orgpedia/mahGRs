In this regard, Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award has been given to 08 eminent personalities from the state of Maharashtra and 1 for the rest of the country for their great work in the field of Sanskrit language, such as promotion, dissemination, research, publication, study, teaching and other references related to Sanskrit. The order was issued on July 27, 2012. Accordingly, the Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award for the year 2016 and 2017 was under consideration of the Government.
The Government has decided to confer the Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Puraskar for the year 2016 and 2017 to the following awardees as per the selection committee of Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Puraskar at the Government level:
