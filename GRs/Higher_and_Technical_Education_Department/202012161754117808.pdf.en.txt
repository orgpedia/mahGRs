The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) has issued a notification dated 01.03.2019 regarding the implementation of 7th Pay Commission for teaching and equivalent posts in technical education institutions. Accordingly, the government and non-government aided teachers in the state. As per the decision taken by the Cabinet in its meeting held on 9th September, 2019, the pay structure of the 7th Pay Commission has been implemented as per the Government Decision dated 11th September, 2019 at Reference No.5.
The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), in its notification dated 1st March, 2019, has prepared the pay matrix as per the grade pay in the 6th Pay Commission. Grade pay of Rs 5400, Rs 6000, Rs 7000, Rs 8000, Rs 9000. In the notification, the grade pay of Rs. 5400 has been mentioned and accordingly the pay matrix has been finalized.
However, the teachers in government and non-government aided technical colleges in the state will be paid Rs. 5400/- as Grade Pay has not been made applicable and accordingly there is difficulty in implementing the pay matrix of the 7th Pay Commission and since the 7th Pay Commission cannot be made applicable to the said teachers, they have been given pay scale of Rs. 15600-39100 with Grade Pay of Rs. 5000/- instead of Rs. 15600-39100 with Grade Pay of Rs. 5400/- was submitted to the Government by the Director, Directorate of Technical Education vide letter No. 6 under reference.
