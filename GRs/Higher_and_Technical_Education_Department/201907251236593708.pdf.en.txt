The proposal:
In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi to increase the intake capacity of Diploma Courses in the existing Private Diploma Courses in the State from the academic year 2019-20, the Director, Technical Education has decided to increase the intake capacity of Diploma Courses in the Institute from the above mentioned reference No. 2 As proposed in the letter here, the matter of granting Government approval to the Institute to increase the intake capacity of the course as indicated against its name in the attached Form-A was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval granted by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Institute of Private Diploma in Pharmacy mentioned in the attached Form-A is being granted approval by the Government to increase the intake capacity of the course as indicated against its name from the academic year 2019-20 subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The eligibility criteria for admission to this course
