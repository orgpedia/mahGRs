Vasudev Gaitonde Kala Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar The Minister of Finance and Planning presented the state's 2019-20 budget in his budget speech. Accordingly, the matter of prescribing the criteria for selecting suitable nominees for this award was under the consideration of the Government, following decisions are being taken.
The Government has decided to confer the" Vasudev Gaitonde Kala Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar" every year to the senior artists working in the field of visual arts in the State. The award of Rs. 5.00 lakh (Rupees Five Lakh only), memento and citation. The award will be presented annually and in order to one of the senior and eminent artist in the following field of visual arts: 1. Department of Drawing and Painting.
2. art department.
Department of Applied Arts.
Department of Arts and Crafts. (Interior decoration, textile work, pottery work, metal work) If the artists are not selected for the award in the respective art category, the artists of the subsequent category will be considered for the award.
The details of the terms and conditions prescribed for the award, composition of the Search Committee and State Level Selection Committee and schedule of the selection process for the award shall be as per Annexure A annexed to this Government Resolution.
The decision of the State Selection Committee in respect of the award shall be final. The Government shall have the power to revoke the award given to an artist on specific grounds. Also, the prize will not be divided.
