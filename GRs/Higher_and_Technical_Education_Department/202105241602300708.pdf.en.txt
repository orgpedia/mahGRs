Preamble: The revised guidelines for establishing self-financing universities in the State, regulating their functioning and making provisions for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto have been issued vide Government Resolution dated 19th January, 2019. Accordingly, detailed project reports are being received by the Government for setting up of self-financing universities from various sponsoring bodies.
In the revised guidelines for scrutiny of Detailed Project Reports of Sponsoring Bodies received by the Government, vide Notice No. As per Section 7 (1), the formation of the Scrutiny Committee was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The following Scrutiny Committee is being constituted to scrutinize the Detailed Project Reports of the Sponsoring Boards to be received by the Government regarding the establishment of Self-Financed University.

At present, the term of the working committee is being extended and the term of this committee will end after receiving further orders from the government.
Committee's mandate
(1) The Committee shall examine all proposals received by the Government in accordance with the revised guidelines prescribed by the Government. The revised guidelines for the establishment of self-financing universities issued by the Government should be examined in accordance with the matters mentioned in paragraph 6.
The Committee may request other information from the Sponsoring Board in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 7 (7.4) of the Guidelines.
(3) The Committee may suggest changes in the Detailed Project Report, if any, and the Sponsor Board may consider and report the same to the Board for compliance within a specified period.
(4) A member of the Board shall not be entitled to vote.
(5) The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within two months from the date of its meeting with the Chairperson-designate.
6) Prepare a detailed project report.
Non-official members of the Screening Committee will be paid an honorarium of Rs.5000/- each for the meeting and Rs.1000/- for the non-official members who will attend the meeting from Mumbai and the non-official members who will come from outside Mumbai will be paid the amount admissible to the two tier AC status of the Railways.
The expenditure shall be borne by Demand No.W.6,2251, Secretariat-Social Services, 090-Secretariat (01) Department of Higher and Technical Education, (01) Department of Higher and Technical Education, (22510409), 50 other expenditure as per the provision available under this head.
