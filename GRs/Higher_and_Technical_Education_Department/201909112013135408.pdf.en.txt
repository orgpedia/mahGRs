Preamble: The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India vide its letter dated 17.05.2019, has informed the State Governments to prepare an Action Plan (100% Day Action Plan) for 100% recruitment of teaching posts in Government and Non-Government Aided Colleges under State Governments and send it to the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India immediately. Accordingly, in reference No. 2, Mr. The Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra has apprised the Central Government of the action being taken in connection with the recruitment of vacancies in higher educational institutions in the State.
In pursuance of the directions issued by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India vide its letter dated 17.05.2019, the proposal for filling up of vacancies in the institutions related to technical education in the State and approval for creation of new posts as per the norms of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for the year 2019-20, was placed before the Sub-Committee constituted for making recommendations after thorough examination of the proposals for creation of new posts/ revival of posts and review of posts etc. and approval for creation of notional posts and filling up of vacancies in the State in its fourteenth meeting held on 16th July, 2019. At the meeting, the following resolution was passed:
" The proposal for the delineation of teaching posts in government, non-government aided engineering, pharmacy, architecture and Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University under the Department of Higher and Technical Education was submitted for the consideration of the sub-committee. The sub-committee decided to fix the revised pattern, abolition of certain posts and creation of new posts as per the AICTE norms of 2019.

The proposal has been sent to a high-level committee for approval. The sub-committee has also approved the filling up of vacant teaching posts out of the sanctioned posts. The posts proposed to be created can be filled only after the approval of the High-Level Secretary Committee. "
In pursuance of the above decision of the Sub-Committee, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education and the Vice-Chancellor, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere have been informed about the process of filling up the vacant posts in the technical educational institutions through a letter in Reference No. 4.
Also, a new position is proposed. The recommendations of the high-level committee headed by the Chief Secretary were made at a meeting held on July 16, 2019.
Accordingly, the proposal to create constitutional posts, degree and diploma level posts in Government Engineering and Pharmacy Degree Colleges, Non-Government Engineering and Pharmacy Degree College, Non-Government College of Architecture and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere under the Directorate of Technical Education in the State. The recommendations were submitted in the meeting of the High Level Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary on 29.08.2019. The minutes of the meeting have been issued vide note no. 5 and the High Level Committee has approved the creation of teaching posts in the institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education under the Department of Higher and Technical Education.
In view of the above facts and in view of the approval given by the High Level Committee, it was under consideration of the Government to issue orders approving the creation of graduate level teaching posts in the non-government engineering and pharmacy colleges under the Directorate of Technical Education in the State.
