Introduction:- Gondwana University was established in 2011 with the objective of improving the quality of education, human development pathways and critical skills for the inclusive development of remote areas and tribal communities in Gadchiroli and Chandrapur districts. Gondwana University aims to provide equal opportunities to the tribal community and ensure human development of the marginalized tribal community. Accordingly, a Tribal Chair Centre is planned to be set up at Gondwana University, Gadchiroli. To promote and strengthen the engagement of the tribal community through the Adivasi Adhasan Kendras so that educational courses and programmes can be developed to meet the needs of the tribals in the region.
To make the tribals understand the Constitution (5th Schedule and 6th Schedule) and the relevant land and forest laws of the tribals. 2. To understand the laws of the tribals 3. To understand the budgetary allocation for tribal development schemes. 4) Understanding of international and domestic laws and international opportunities. 5.Educate an understanding of the issue of customary governance and customary rights (common property resources). 6.Ad develop the field of reporting of local law enforcement. The University has proposed to set up a Tribal Chair Centre at Gondwana University, Gadchiroli for in-depth study on the subject etc. The proposal has been sent to the government. The policy regarding the establishment of chair centres in the name of eminent personalities in various non-agricultural universities in Maharashtra has been prepared in the context of reference No. 2 here. This was decided at the Cabinet meeting. Therefore, in pursuance of the said proposal and policy, the matter of granting administrative approval for the establishment of Tribal Chair Centre at Gondwana University, Gadchiroli was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision:- In order to preserve the culture, art, language, folk music of the tribals of Gadchiroli and Chandrapur districts, to conduct research based on anthropology and history of these areas, and to develop the potential and community-based skills of tribal youth, administrative approval is being given to start a Tribal Chair Centre at Gondwana University, Gadchiroli, subject to the following conditions:
(1) No new post shall be created for the said Centre.
2. Total amount for the establishment of this Chair is Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only).
The subsidy received from the government will be invested in the fixed deposits of the nationalized bank and the interest earned from this amount will be used for the activities of the Foundation.

No additional funds will be provided to the University. The university should provide more funds.
5. The income of the Foundation shall be raised from the interest received on fixed deposits. This will be supplemented by revenues generated from fees for courses of study and income generated from running training programmes and mentoring/ counseling projects.
It shall be the responsibility of the University to submit the information of the expenditure incurred for the course to the Government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune from time to time.
Each centre will be inspected every two years. A report in this regard will be submitted to the government through the Director of Higher Education, Pune. If the report shows negative progress of the Chair centres in terms of quality of teaching, research performance, number of students, teaching and training classes conducted, the Chair centres will be closed and the University concerned will be obliged to transfer the funds with the Chair centres to the Government.
The expenditure should be met from the approved provision under the head" Demand No.W-2, Principal Head- 2202- General Education, 03- University and Higher Education, 102- Assistance to Universities,- (00) (22) Development of Gondwana University (Mandatory) (2202 H581), 31 Subsidiary Grants."
