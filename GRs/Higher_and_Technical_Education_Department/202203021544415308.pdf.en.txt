A committee has been formed to recommend to the government about the management, use, maintenance, etc. of Vande Mataram Hall, Aurangabad.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education,
Government Resolution No: Committee-2022/ No. 26/22/ Mashi-3,
Madama Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya Extension, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date of release: March 2, 2022
The proposal:
Hyderabad: The construction of the Vande Mataram Hall, Aurangabad, which is being constructed in memory of the freedom fighters who participated in the freedom struggle, is in the final stages and after the completion of the construction, the government was considering to form a committee under the chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, to study all the ancillary issues and recommend to the government for its proper management, application and maintenance.
The government's decision:
As mentioned in the preamble, the following committee is being constituted to make recommendations to the government.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| A.A. | Name of the committee members                                      | Designation          |
| 1.   | Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad Division.                      | Chairman             |
| 2.   | District Collector, Aurangabad.                                    | the members          |
| 3.   | Commissioner, Aurangabad Municipal Corporation.                    | the members          |
| 4.   | Chief Administrator (No.Sh.), CIDCO, Aurangabad.                   | the members          |
| 5.   | Joint Director, Higher Education, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad. | The member-secretary |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The committee constituted as above should study all the issues related to the proper management, use, maintenance of the Vande Mataram Hall, Aurangabad and in this regard, there will be no financial burden on the government in the future and submit a report to the government within a period of 1 month.
