Preamble: Review of posts in the offices under the control of Directorate of Arts as well as in Government Art Institutions has been approved vide Government Resolution dated 30th December, 2006. As many as 135 faculty posts in the cadre of Dean, Professor and Dean were sanctioned for four Government Arts Colleges viz., Art College, Mumbai, 2) Sir J. G. College of Applied Arts, Mumbai, 3) Government College of Art and Design, Nagpur and 4) Government College of Art and Design, Aurangabad.
As per the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) Act, 1987, courses related to visual arts have been declared as technical education. All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) is the apex body of the Central Government for all institutions related to technical education. The Institute has laid down norms and standards for institutions imparting technical education from time to time. According to this standard, it is mandatory to appoint the required number of teachers for the above courses. The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) has released a notification regarding the same. It was submitted to the government on February 1, 2019.
As per the norms of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for the year 2019-20, the proposal for creation of posts for Government Arts Colleges, filling up of vacancies, creation of new posts/ revival of posts and review of posts etc. The meeting discussed the revised figure of 159 teaching posts in government arts colleges.
