Preamble: In pursuance of the list received by the Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter dated 30.04.2018 from All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi regarding recognition of new institutions of Diploma courses in Professional Pharmacy in the State from the academic year 2018-19, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government vide Reference letter. It was under consideration of the Government to grant approval to the institution mentioned in Annexure A to start a new Diploma course in Pharmacy.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Institute mentioned in Annexure A is being granted approval by the Government to start the institution of new Diploma course in Pharmacy from the academic year 2018-19 subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The institution's eligibility for admission to this course will be as mentioned in Annexure A.
3. The institution shall have to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
