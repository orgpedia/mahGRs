Terms and Conditions - 1. The approval granted to short term courses approved by the Government under the Board shall be subject to the approval of the Government. In accordance with the Government Decision of 21.05.1983, the courses will be on a perpetual non-subsidized basis and will not be funded by the Government.
The duration, accessibility, semester, etc. of these courses shall be as specified in the Annexure to the Government Decision.
The institute should appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the course and pay salaries and allowances to the staff as per rules. Also, strict action will be taken against the institute regarding the salaries and allowances of the teachers and other staff, etc. and if there are complaints from the students to the government.
will be done.
For this short-term course, institutes are required to take affiliation from the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education. Also, if the All India Council for Technical Education decides to implement the courses approved by this government decision, it will be mandatory for the institutes to get the approval of the All India Council for Technical Education.
Although the government has a fee reimbursement policy for professional courses, it does not have a fee reimbursement scheme for any short-term courses. Hence, the fee reimbursement scheme for the above course will not be applicable to any category of students. The fee reimbursement / rebate scheme will also not be applicable to students who are economically backward.
Institutions shall be obliged to submit to the Board a letter of assurance on the fifth stamp paper in the prescribed form stating that they agree to all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as those prescribed by the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education.
The institution concerned shall meet the other legal requirements by filling in the required balance in the joint account with the Director, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai.
The Director of Technical Education, Maharashtra State Technical Education Board, Mumbai, should take note of the fact that the institute provides regular teaching and non-teaching staff appointments, equipment and state-of-the-art laboratories and library facilities to the students as per the standards. The Deputy Secretary, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Zonal Office concerned will be fully responsible for meeting these requirements.
The books, journals required by the library in the institute are required to be purchased as per the standard of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Bandra, Mumbai. Also, the library must provide students with a study material so that students can make full use of the library.
The institute will be under the control of the Director, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education and examinations for the respective courses will be conducted by Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Bandra, Mumbai.
