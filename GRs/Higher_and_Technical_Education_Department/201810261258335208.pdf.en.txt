The proposal:
Department of Science& Technology (DST), Government of India has launched an umbrella programme" NIDHI (National Initiative for Development and Harnessing Innovations)" in 2016 to develop ideas and innovations (knowledge-based and technology-driven). The programme will focus on creating an environment conducive to innovation and entrepreneurship to provide direction to the youth and make a positive impact on the socio-economic development of the country. It is also aimed at creating new avenues for economic growth and job creation. In order to fulfil the Prime Minister's vision of Startup India and to bring speed and quality to the Startup ecosystem in the country, Department of Science and Technology (DST) is planning to invest Rs. Rs 500 crore will be allocated for this. So far, the Department of Science and Technology has established more than 100 technology professional incubators in the academic and research fields. These institutions include IITs, IIMs, NITs and other academic institutions. Each of these incubators is focused on a technology domain and all of them together bring together more than 2000 startups and provide an area of about 7 lakh square feet for startups. The Chairman, Board of Governors, Virmata Jijabai Technology Institute, Mumbai vide their letter dated 26.05.2017 mentioned in Reference No. 1 has informed that Virmata Jijabai Technology Institute, Mumbai, an autonomous institute of the State Government has been included in the list of institutions selected by the Department of Science& Technology. NIDHI (National Initiative for Development and Harnessing Innovations), Centre to set up Incubation Centre in the institute under this programme.
