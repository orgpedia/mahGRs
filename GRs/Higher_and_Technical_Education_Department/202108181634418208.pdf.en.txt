Preamble: In pursuance of the approval accorded vide letter dated 29th September, 2020 by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi in the above mentioned Reference 1 and as proposed vide letter dated 20th October, 2020 by the Director, Technical Education in Reference 2, the Government was considering to grant approval for starting a new course" M. Arch Project Management" (intake capacity-20) at Aditya College of Architecture, Borivali from the academic year 2020-21.
Government Decision: The Government is granting approval to start the course" M. Arch Project Management" at Aditya College of Architecture, Borivali, an institution affiliated to Council of Architecture, New Delhi, from the academic year 2020-21 on permanent basis with intake capacity of" 20" subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. This recognition shall be on a perpetual basis without subsidy.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the Council of Architecture in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Decision dated 21st May, 1983. The institution's eligibility for this course will remain as mentioned above.
3. The institution shall enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the Council of Architecture. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
