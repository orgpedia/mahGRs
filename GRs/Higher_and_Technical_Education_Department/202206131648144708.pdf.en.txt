The Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014.
The proposal:
The Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai has temporarily resumed its operations on the second, fourth and sixth floors of MTNL, Powai. As per the lease agreement for the said site, the payment of rent payable for the period April, 2022 to June, 2022 for the financial year 2022-23 is to be made in terms of Reference No. A letter of 1 is requested. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai for payment of rent was under the consideration of the Government.
Maharashtra Government has decided to temporarily start MTNL at 2nd, 4th and 6th Floor, Powai for Maharashtra National Wardhi Vidyapith, Mumbai. The rent payable for the period April, 2022 to June, 2022 for the financial year 2022-23 is Rs. 4,71,99, 612/- (Rupees Four Crores Seventy One Lakh Nine Thousand Six Hundred and Twelve only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
(1) The Executive Council shall be responsible for administering the grants sanctioned by the Government as per the Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014 by depositing them in the University Fund. Also, the university should take action to approve the expenditure incurred from the grant by exchanging the statement of accounts of the expenditure before the General Council.
(2) The Vice-Chancellor shall be responsible to see that the grant is spent for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned by the Government to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai. In this regard, they should submit its utility certificate to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education with the approval of the Executive Council.
(3) All the documents and accounts of all the above grants have been authorized by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as the case may be.
