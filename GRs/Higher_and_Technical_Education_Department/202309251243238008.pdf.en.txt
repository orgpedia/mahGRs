Government circular:
The State Government has decided to provide free hostel facility to the students admitted in Matoshree Government Boys Hostel, Churchgate, Mumbai and Telang Smriti Girls Hostel, Churchgate, Mumbai.
This free facility should be made available to the students till the time they join the State Institute of Administrative Business Education, Mumbai.
