Government Circular: Education Fee Committee has been constituted under the Government Decision dated 19.1.2016 to fix the fees for unaided/ permanent unaided pedagogy and unaided/ permanent unaided physical education courses in the State. The committee will examine the fee proposals of the colleges. The Government has approved the levy of processing fee equivalent to 0.5% of the total cost of colleges for examining the proposal for fee revision with retrospective effect from the year 2019-20.
