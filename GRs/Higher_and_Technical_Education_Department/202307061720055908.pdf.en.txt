The Principal and the Head of the Department who have been transferred for administrative reasons shall be entitled to the period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of the Government. The Principal and the Head of the Department who have been transferred will not be allowed to hold office for the period of the appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance.

The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) This order shall come into force at once.
2) After the transfer order is issued, the concerned principal should immediately join the place of posting after the transfer, without waiting for the appearance of the substitute principal. Also, the department heads who have been transferred should be relieved immediately to join the place of posting after their transfer.
The Head of the Department concerned should join the posted institution immediately after his transfer from the existing institution. The transferred teacher shall ensure that he joins the place of transfer within the period of his appointment as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Owner Service and Provisions for Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981.
3) The transferred principals and heads of departments should be reinstated without delay after they are relieved from their respective institutions and join. The Principal of the institution concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of a teacher.
4) No leave of the transferred Principal or Head of Department should be sanctioned without prior approval of the Administrative Department.
5) It will be the responsibility of the respective institution heads personally to relieve the transferred principals and department heads immediately. If the principal does not relieve the concerned, the director, technical education, should initiate administrative action against them and submit the proposal to the government for approval as needed. Further, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education shall submit the compliance report in this regard to this Department within 10 days from the date of this order without fail.
