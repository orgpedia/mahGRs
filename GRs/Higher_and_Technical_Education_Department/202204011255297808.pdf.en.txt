Government Decision: Lokneta Balasaheb Desai was a non-people's personality. He played a major role in the formation of modern Maharashtra. Balasaheb, who held the posts of President of Satara District Local Board, Construction Minister, Education Minister, Agriculture Minister, Revenue Minister and Home Minister, etc. in the pre-independence era, used the power in hand for the welfare of the people and left a mark on Maharashtra's politics. He was also instrumental in the establishment of Shivaji University. He has also worked in theatre, film, television and theatre. It would be appropriate to call Balasaheb Desai the architect of modern Maharashtra, given his work in the political, social, educational and cultural fields. Therefore, with a view to commemorate his unparalleled work, the Government of Maharashtra has sanctioned the establishment of a Chair in his name, providing an opportunity to the University to conduct research on his work and thoughts.
Accordingly, the university had submitted a proposal to the government with detailed details about the non-recurring expenditure of building, materials required for setting up the Loknete Balasaheb Desai Chair Centre at Shivaji University, Kolhapur. Accordingly, the establishment of Lokneta Balasaheb Desai Chair Centre at Shivaji University, Kolhapur was approved vide Reference No. 1 Administrative approval has been granted by the government here. Also, the policy regarding the establishment of the Chair Centre has been referred to as Reference No. 2 here. This was decided at the Cabinet meeting. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds to the Centre was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Rs. 36,75,000/- (Rupees Thirty Six Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand only) is being approved to be disbursed subject to the following conditions: No new post can be created for the Centre.
2. The subsidy received from the Government shall be invested in the fixed deposits of the nationalised bank and the interest received from this amount shall be utilised for the activities of the Foundation.
No additional funds will be provided to the University. The university should provide more funds.
4. The income of the Foundation shall be raised from the interest received on fixed deposits. This will be supplemented by revenues generated from fees for courses of study and income generated from running training programmes and mentoring/ counseling projects.

It shall be the responsibility of the University to submit the information of the expenditure incurred for the course to the Government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune from time to time.
Every 2 years, each Adhyasan Kendra will be inspected and a report in this regard will be submitted to the government through the Director of Higher Education, Pune. If the report shows negative progress of the centres in terms of quality of teaching, research performance, number of students, teaching and training classes conducted, the centres will be closed and the university will be obliged to transfer the funds to the government.
The Director (Higher Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune shall be responsible for providing the said sanctioned funds to Shivaji University, Kolhapur immediately. All the documents and accounts of the grant are to be examined by the Accountant General, Mumbai, Maharashtra-1 or the Accountant General-2, Nagpur, as the case may be.
The expenditure should be met from the funds available in the financial year 2021-22 under the head" Demand No-W-2 Principal Head 2202, General Education 03, University and Higher Education 102, Assistance to Universities, Schemes under Five Year Plans, (00) (26) Development of Shivaji University, Kolhapur (2202H812), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-salary)."
