Introduction: Refer No. (1) Under the decision of the Government herein, admissions to all professional courses in the unaided private professional courses institutions in the State under the Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai, for which the Common Entrance Test of the State has been implemented and/ or whose admissions are to be made through the Centralized Admission Process through a single window system are made through the Common Entrance Examination and Centralized Admission Process through the Commissioner, State Common Entrance Examination Cell as the competent authority in accordance with Section 10 of the Maharashtra Unaided Private Professional Educational Institutions (Regulation of Admission and Fee) Act, 2015. Similarly, reference No. (2) Under the decision of the Government herein, admissions to all professional courses in Government and Government aided institutions to which the State Common Entrance Test has been applied and/ or whose admission is to be made through the Centralized Admission Process in a single window manner, are made through the Commissioner, State Common Entrance Examination Cell through the Common Entrance Test and the Centralized Admission Process in a single window manner. Reference No. (3) PGDM and PGDBM Post Graduate Diploma Courses have been excluded from the list of professional courses under the Directorate of Technical Education under the Government Corrigendum here to which the State Common Entrance Test and the Centralized Admission Process will be applicable and whose entire admission process will be conducted through the Commissioner, State Common Entrance Examination Cell in a single window manner. Therefore, the state

Admission to PGDM and PGDBM courses in any private unaided institution is not conducted through the Common Entrance Test Cell.
However, reference No. (4) As PGDM and PGDBM courses are equivalent to MBA/ MMS post-graduate courses as proposed by the Director, Technical Education under this letter, the admission to PGDM and PGDBM courses in Government and Government aided institutions is required to be done through Common Entrance Test through Commissioner, State Common Entrance Test Cell and through Centralized Admission Process in a single window system.
The Centralized Admission Process will be applicable for PG Diploma courses including PGDM and PGDBM and the admission process for these courses will be conducted through Centralized Admission System through the Commissioner, State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra State, Mumbai on the basis of the Common Entrance Test scores used for MBA/ MMS Postgraduate Degree Courses.
Government Decision: Reference No. (4) As proposed by the Director of Technical Education under the letter herein, PGDM and PGDBM are being included in the professional courses in Government and Government aided institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, for which the State Common Entrance Test and Centralized Admission Process are applicable. Since the courses are equivalent to MBA/ MMS, the admission process for these courses should be conducted through a centralized admission process through the Commissioner, State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra State, Mumbai on the basis of the Common Entrance Test scores used for MBA/ MMS Postgraduate Degree Courses.
