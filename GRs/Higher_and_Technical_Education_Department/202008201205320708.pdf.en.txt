The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi has decided to reduce the intake capacity of Diploma courses of permanent unaided institutions in the State from the academic year 2016-17.
The proposal for grant of Government approval pursuant to the permission granted vide Reference No. 1 has been submitted by the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai to the Government vide their Reference No. 2. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Decision of the Government: The Government is granting approval to reduce the intake capacity of the following non-teaching diploma courses in permanent unaided technical courses run by Vasundhara Multi-Purpose Social Institute, Siddhivinayak Technical Campus, Shegaon-Khamgaon Road, Shirsagaon Blue, Shegaon, District Buldhana-444203, from the academic year 2016-17, subject to the following terms and conditions.
