Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award Criteria Selection and Procedure- Reconstitution of Scrutiny Committee.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: SANSAPU-2020/ PR No.60/ AASTA-2
Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai- 400 032
Date is January 22, 2021
Reference 1) Department of Higher and Technical Education Government Resolution No.: SASAPU-0512/ PR No.78/ Coordination dated 27th July, 2012 2) Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government Resolution No. Award- 2613/ PR No. 124/ Coordination, dated- 15th October 2013 3) Higher and Technical Education Department Government Resolution No: SASAPU-0512/ PR. No.78/ Coordination Dated- 27th January 2020 Preamble The Government of Maharashtra honours Sanskrit Pandits with the Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Puraskar every year. In this regard, all the orders issued earlier in connection with the said awards have been superseded and all the modalities, contours and ancillary aspects of the said award have been decided under the Government Decision at Reference No. 1. As per this decision, a screening committee has been formed to select Sanskrit scholars for the Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award. The Scrutiny Committee was reconstituted pursuant to the Government decision at Reference No. 2. Appointment of Non-Official Members to the Scrutiny Committee Reference No. 3 have been cancelled by the government.
The matter of reconstituting the Scrutiny Committee for the purpose of examining and rating the applications received for the determination and distribution of Mahakavi Kalidasa Sanskrit Sadhana Awards every year was under the consideration of the Government.
The Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award Criteria Selection and Procedure Scrutiny Committee is being reconstituted to scrutinize and score the applications received for the determination and distribution of Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Awards every year.
