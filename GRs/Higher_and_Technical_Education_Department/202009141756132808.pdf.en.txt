Date is September 14, 2020.
The proposal:
To increase the number of candidates in Maharashtra in the Indian Administrative Service, the State Administrative Vocational Education Institute, Mumbai and the Indian Administrative Service Pre-Training Center, Nagpur, Amravati, Nashik, Aurangabad and Kolhapur have been started in the state. The total intake capacity of the training centre is 540. Various facilities are being provided to the students by the government. The government was considering the formation of a committee to improve the training centre.
The government's decision:
The state government has decided to set up a committee to improve the training centers of the State Administrative Vocational Education Institute, Mumbai and the Indian Administrative Service Pre-Training Center, Nagpur, Amravati, Nashik, Aurangabad and Kolhapur.
