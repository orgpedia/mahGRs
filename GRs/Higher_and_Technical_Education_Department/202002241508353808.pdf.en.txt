The Maharashtra State Faculty Development Academy (MSFDA) was set up. Therefore, the following decisions are being taken by making partial changes in the contextual government decision.
The government's decision:
The Government is approving the formation and operation of a company named" Maharashtra State Faculty Development Academy" under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 and the following structure under the Companies Act:
The Government of Maharashtra (Department of Higher and Technical Education) will have 40% share in the scheme.
40% of all non-agricultural universities in the state.
5% of the business/ industry entities (including CSR activities).
Educational institutions and voluntary organisations will get 10 per cent each.
