It was published on November 30, 2018. Issue No. Under Section 4 (1) (b), a total of 16% of the total number of seats in the civil services and posts under the control of the state have been reserved for the socially and educationally backward (SEBC) category of citizens, including the Maratha community. Also Reference No. As per the decision of the Government of Maharashtra, orders have been issued to reserve 10% seats for admission in government services and educational institutions for the economically weaker sections of the open category in the state of Maharashtra. Accordingly, 16% seats will be reserved for the Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) including the Maratha community and 10% seats for the economically weaker sections in the open category for appointment in all public non-agricultural university educational institutions except minority educational institutions in the state. 3 here.
