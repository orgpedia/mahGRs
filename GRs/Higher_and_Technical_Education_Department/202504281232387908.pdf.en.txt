The Proposal: -
"The HTE CAA system is a customized service system for the salaries of teaching / non-teaching staff in aided colleges and non-agricultural universities under the Department of Higher and Technical Education. The system comprises 87 per cent of the staff of the Higher and Technical Education Department and 13 per cent of the staff of the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
MahaIT has been entrusted with the responsibility of developing and maintaining the HTE servicing system as directed by the Department of Finance. The development and maintenance of this system is carried out under the aforesaid Reference No. The date 1.4.2024 to 31.3.2025 was assigned to the MahaIT by Government Decision 2. This order has expired on 31.3.2025. The government was also considering handing over the development and maintenance of the HTE service system to MahaIT for a further period of time to enable it to operate on a regular basis.
The Government's Decisions:
For the development and maintenance of the "HTE Sevaart" system, it is being approved by the Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation Limited (MahaIT), under the General Administration (Information Technology) Department, to provide a separate, 1 Senior Software Developer and 1 IT Office Assistant for the Department of Higher and Technical Education at a rate of 20 per cent for 1 Senior Manager Technical, 1 Business Analyst, 1 Network Specialist, 9 Database Administrators, 1 Software Tester B1 IT Office Assistant.
In the HTE service system, MahaIT should develop and make available new reports as per the requirement of the users. Also, make this report available for download.
