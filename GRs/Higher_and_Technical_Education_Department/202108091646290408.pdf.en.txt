(3) The period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to the officers transferred as per the rules of the Government. As per the government rules, the period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance will not be admissible to the transferred officers.
4. The transferred officers should be relieved immediately by the respective Head of Institutions/ Principals as this transfer order needs to be implemented effectively. The responsibility of relieving the transferred officer will be personally borne by the head of the office concerned. This should be followed up by the Joint Director of Technical Education of the concerned department. Also, the executive report in this regard should be submitted to the government through the Director, Technical Education within 15 days from the date of this order.
(5) After the orders of transfer are issued, the transferred officers shall ensure that they join the place of transfer within the period prescribed under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. Otherwise, if he does not join the post of replacement during this period, the period of his absence will be considered as non-working day.
