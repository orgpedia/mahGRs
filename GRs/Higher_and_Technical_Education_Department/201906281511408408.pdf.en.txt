The proposal:
The Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter No. 2 dated 15.05.2019 has submitted a proposal to the Government regarding the opening of new institutions of Diploma courses in Pharmacy in the Institute of Degree Course in Pharmacy in the State from the academic year 2019-20 by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. It was under consideration of the Government to grant approval to start a new Institute of Diploma Course in Pharmacy in the Institute of Degree Course in Pharmacy to the institution mentioned in the attached Form-A.
The government's decision:
In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is granting approval to start a new Institute of Diploma Course in Pharmacy in the Institute of Degree Course in Pharmacy mentioned in the attached Form-A from the academic year 2019-20 subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.

If the fees charged by the institutions concerned exceed the tuition fees prescribed by the Fee Regulatory Authority, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
The Institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the Government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
The library of the institution is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students.
All the students are trained as per the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) norms.
