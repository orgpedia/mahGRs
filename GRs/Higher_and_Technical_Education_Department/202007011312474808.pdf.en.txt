Functions of the Committee:
(1) To determine the factors such as availability of space, creation of posts, infrastructure, fund requirement, etc. for the establishment of a separate government college for Marathi-speaking students in the border areas of Kolhapur district.
(2) Considering the possibility that the process of establishing a separate government college for Marathi-speaking students may take some time, the Government/ local bodies/ educational institutions/ university buildings available in Chandgad or Gadhinglaj taluka of Kolhapur district or in Kolhapur city or Rajaram Government
To recommend whether or how a temporary sub-station can be made operational at College, Kolhapur.
(3) To recommend how and what will be the nature of the course and how some selected courses for Marathi speaking students can be started in online mode based on the minimum available manpower from the academic year 2020-21 in the proposed sub-centre.
The committee will soon submit its report to the government.
