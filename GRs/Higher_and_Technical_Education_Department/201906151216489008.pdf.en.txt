the Government Order
In accordance with the procedure laid down under Section 109 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and the decision of the Government in question, the non-agricultural universities in the State have submitted proposals for new additional batches for the academic year 2019-20 to the Government for approval. The proposals were examined in accordance with the norms laid down under the relevant Government decisions. Proposals that have been found to be eligible under the scrutiny at the Government level in accordance with the recommendations of the Universities are being approved on permanent non-grant basis in the existing colleges as per the authority vested in the Government under Section 109 (4) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and the proviso thereto, subject to the following terms and conditions.
