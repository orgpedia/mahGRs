The proposal:
Government Technological Institute, Gondia has started two new Diploma courses in Electrical Engineering and Civil Engineering from 2014-15 with an intake capacity of 60 each. A proposal to start 21 new teaching posts at Government Technical College, Gondia, as per the norms of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), was under consideration of the government. Accordingly, a proposal for the creation of 21 teaching posts in the institute was approved by the Finance Department. The committee was headed by the Chief Secretary. Accordingly, the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the regularization of 21 teaching posts at Government Technological University, Gondia vide No. 26/10/2017. It has been approved in the 4/2017 th meeting. The government has taken the following decisions:
The government's decision:
As per the approval given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in their meeting dated 26/10/2017, the Government is hereby giving approval for filling up of 21 teaching posts in Government Technological Institute, Gondia as under:
