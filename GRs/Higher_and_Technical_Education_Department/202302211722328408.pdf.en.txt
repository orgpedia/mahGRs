Government Decision- Government Decision, d. Shri Murlidhar Pandharinath Bendre, Pune was appointed as a member of the Board of Trustees of Deccan College Post Graduate and Research Institute, Pune on 11th January, 2016. Following the resignation of Mr. Bendre, the matter of appointment to the vacant post of trustee in the Board of Trustees of Deccan College Postgraduate and Research Institute, Pune was under the consideration of the Government. Pandurang Namdevrao Balkawade is being appointed to the Board of Trustees of Deccan College, Pune by the Government in accordance with the powers conferred by clause (c) of Schedule-I of the Decree 1939 of the Deccan College Postgraduate and Research Institute, Pune. This order shall come into force with immediate effect.
