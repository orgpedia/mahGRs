Memorandum: - A Centrally Sponsored Scheme Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) is being implemented in the state. Under the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) scheme, grants are distributed in the state as mentioned below.
(1) Universities / Government / Non-Government Aided Colleges have been sanctioned funds under various heads. The ratio of the distribution of funds by the Centre to the state is 60:40.
(2) The MMER (Management, Monitoring, Evaluation and Research) component is directed to use 1 per cent of the total sanctioned funds for RUSA office expenses. The proportion of this fund distribution is 100% (central share).
(3) The ratio of Central share to State share has been fixed at 2 / 3 for the Erst time Model Degree College component.
Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance, Government of India to ensure timely disbursement of funds in respect of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. 2/5-50 An alternative fund flow mechanism has been developed. This mechanism is being implemented through the integration of State Treasuries, Public Financial Management System (PEMS) and Kuber System of Reserve Bank of India. Detailed instructions regarding this new mechanism have been issued by the Central Government vide Memorandum dated 13.07.2023. In the state, this fund distribution system is being used on a pilot basis for the centrally sponsored scheme of Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan.
