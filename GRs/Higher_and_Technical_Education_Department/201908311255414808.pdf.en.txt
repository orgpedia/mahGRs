The proposal:
The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 has come into force with effect from 01.03.2017. Keeping in view the increasing pass percentage in higher secondary schools and the inclination of students towards a particular course, so that no student is deprived of the desired higher education, Section 109 (8) of the Act provides for expeditious recognition of additional fresh batches of specified faculties in exceptional circumstances. In the academic year 2019-20, the non-agricultural universities of the state had submitted proposals to the government for additional new batches of specific faculties. The Government Resolution No. The inspection was carried out as per the norms prescribed under NGC-2017/ (128/18)/ Mashi-4, dated 04.07.2017. After the inspection, the government was considering to approve the proposals of new batches in addition to the specific faculties that qualified from the proposals recommended by the universities to the government in an expeditious manner. The government has taken the following decisions:
The government's decision:
In the academic year 2019-20, the non-agricultural universities of the state will be allowed to recruit new batches of faculty in addition to the fast track faculty who have qualified from the proposals recommended to the government, as per the powers vested in the government under Section 109 (8) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016. The existing colleges are being sanctioned on permanent non-grant basis subject to the following terms and conditions as mentioned in the following statement subject to the terms and conditions laid down under the Government Decision No. 2:

7. Accelerated Additional batch If a student is not admitted in the batch for the academic year for which it is sanctioned and the batch is not started due to some other reasons, the recognition of such batch shall be deemed to be automatically cancelled.
It should be ensured that 50 per cent of the sanctioned strength of students is admitted in the fast track additional batch which has been sanctioned.
The students will be admitted to such fast-track additional batch only through the procedure prescribed by the Government for admission of students in the concerned faculty. Also, as per the prevailing policy of the government, the recognition of the additional fast track platoon will always be on a non-grant basis.
The colleges which have provisionally arranged for the additional batch approved by the fast track, the guarantee that the criteria prescribed for the regular batch by the Government will be fulfilled in the next 6 months is attached with the application.
The Universities shall not initiate the process of granting affiliation without first going through the Government approval orders and obtaining the certificate in Condition No. 2 of the Divisional Joint Director before taking further action as may be required for grant of affiliation. The universities should send the compliance report regarding the affiliation to the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and the concerned Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education.
If the additional batches approved by the respective Universities and the Divisional Joint Director are found to be having 50% or less of the sanctioned strength of students, the Divisional Joint Director shall issue orders to the Government and the University to cancel the recognition of such additional batches.
