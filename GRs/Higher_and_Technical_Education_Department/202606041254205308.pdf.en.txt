The Proposal:
Subject to conditions and conditions, the Government decision dated 3.2.2025 in this regard has approved the grant-in-aid to Nabalbhau Pratishthan, Nabalnagar, VN Patil Law College, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar run by Dhule. Accordingly, a symbolic Supplementary Demand Resolution was introduced in the Winter Session of December, 2025 to include the total salary of 16 posts of 08 teachers and 08 non-teachers permissible for 3 years and 5 years Law course in the said college under the Commitment Expenditure and to bring to the notice of the Legislature the expenditure incurred for payment of salary in lieu of the posts filled up from the date mentioned in the relevant Government Decision. The figurative supplemental demand was accepted and the government was considering approving the expenditure.
Government Decision: By the relevant Government Decision, the salary of a total of 16 posts of 08 teachers and 08 non-teachers permissible for 3-year and 5-year law courses in V.N. Patil Law College, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Nabalbhai Pratishthan, Nabalnagar, Dhule is being approved for inclusion under the Committed Expenditure, subject to the following conditions. The cost of salary and allowances for these posts is being allowed as per the Government Decision in reference as 25 per cent of the cost sanctioned from the date 18.07.2017, 50 per cent of the cost sanctioned from the date 18.07.2018, 75 per cent of the cost sanctioned from the date 18.07.2019 and 100% of the cost sanctioned from the date 18.07.2020.
Conditions: -
1. Referral before payment of salary. Pay the salary subsidy thereafter by complying with the terms and conditions in the Government Decision dated 3.2.2025.
