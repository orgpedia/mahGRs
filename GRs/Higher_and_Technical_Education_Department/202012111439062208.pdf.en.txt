Preamble: The Government have decided to implement a new contributory pension scheme for the employees joining the service of the State Government on or after 1st November, 2005. Further, the procedure for refund of contribution amounts of the new Defined Contribution Pension Scheme has been prescribed vide Government Order dated 12.11.2010 of the Department of Finance.
Procedure for implementation of the defined contribution pension scheme for teachers and non-teaching staff in 100% aided posts in non-agricultural universities and affiliated non-government aided colleges/ colleges of technology, pharmacy, architecture, technical institutes/ industrial training institutes/ aided technical schools/ colleges of vocational education and junior colleges and government aided deemed universities where the retirement age scheme is applicable.

Annio-2010/ (678/10)/ Vishy-1 dated 25th March, 2011. In pursuance of the said Government decision, the Vishy-1 Office has issued clarifications vide their affidavits dated 29th October, 2012, 13th March, 2013 and 30th August, 2013.
The Department of Higher and Technical Education has approved the implementation of the defined contribution pension scheme prescribed under the Government Decision No. 2010/ (678/10)/ Vishy-1 dated 25th March, 2011 in respect of teaching and non-teaching staff in 31 non-government aided arts institutions under the control of the Directorate of Arts. However, as per the aforesaid Government decisions, the Controlling Authority and Deputy State Records Maintenance Agency have not been declared for the Aided Arts Colleges under the control of Directorate of Arts. The matter of declaring the said Controlling Officer and Deputy State Records Maintenance Agency was under consideration of the Government.
The procedure for reimbursement of contribution under the new" Defined Contribution Pension Scheme" to the teaching and non-teaching staff of 100% aided posts in non-agricultural universities and affiliated non-government aided colleges/ colleges of technology, pharmacy, architecture, technical education and government aided deemed universities where the superannuation scheme is applicable has been prescribed by the Department of Higher and Technical Education vide Government Decision No. Annio 2010/678/10/ VISHI-1 dated 21.12.2018. The matter of applying this procedure to non-government aided art institutions under the control of the Directorate of Arts was also under the consideration of the government, accordingly, the following decision has been taken in both the above cases.
For the implementation of the defined contribution pension scheme in respect of teaching and non-teaching staff appointed on or after 1st November, 2005 in the non-government aided art institutions under the control of the Directorate of Arts, the Inspector, Painting and Sculpture, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall act as the Controlling Officer and the Assistant Inspector, Painting and Sculpture, Divisional Office, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur shall act as the Deputy State Records Maintenance Agency.
