Preamble to the Meeting of the Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission (MAHED) held on 10.9.2018: As per Section 76 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to constitute the Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Council (MAHED). The Maharashtra State Higher Education Development Commission (MAHED) under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister has also made a provision for the five-year master plan and point director annual plan to be prepared by every public university under section 107. The Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission (MSHEDC) has to approve these plans for all public universities.
As per this provision, the meeting of MAHED on 10th September, 2018. It was presided over by the Chief Minister. As per the decision taken in the meeting, it has been approved to appoint a new committee for implementation, follow-up and time-bound evaluation of the five-year master plans as well as annual plans.
The implementation of the approved five-year master plan and the annual point plan has started and the need to form a special committee as decided in the commission meeting and the formation of a committee accordingly was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the constitution of the following Committees for implementation, follow-up and time bound evaluation of the Five Year Plan and Annual Plans.

Objectives and Functions of the Committee 1. The objectives of the five year master plans have been defined under Section 107 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act and to guide the universities in fulfilling such objectives.
To guide and follow up the proper implementation of the five-year master plans and annual point plans approved by the President.
3. To make a time bound assessment of the action plans prepared in this regard by the public universities.
To inspire awareness, positive participation and conduct workshops, seminars, etc. regarding the five-year master plan among all sections of the university and college education sector.
To guide and follow up the effective implementation of the proposed schemes to meet the various objectives of the five year master plans.
To suggest changes, corrections, amendments in the five-year master plan prepared by the universities or in the proposed point-wise annual plan.
The term of the Committee shall be five years.
The annual report of the Committee shall be submitted to the Commission.
The Committee may, as the case may be, hold meetings, seminars, workshops, etc., at the University level or at the combined State level and the University in which such meetings are to be held shall pay the requisite honorarium and allowances to the members of the Committee from its University Fund.
