At present, the construction of the boys and girls hostel is in progress and due to lack of funds, the work has been stopped. The University has requested the letter here.
In pursuance of the above request, the Maharashtra National Law University, Nagpur has sanctioned Rs. 30 crore out of the remaining Rs. 12,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crores only) was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Maharashtra National Law University, Nagpur has decided to construct a permanent campus at Mouza Waranga for construction of residential building. As per the administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 17.12.2021, Rs. 12,00,00, 000/ (Rupees Twelve Crore only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
The Executive Council shall be responsible for administering the grants sanctioned by the Government as per the Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014 by depositing them in the University Fund. Also, the university should take action to approve the expenditure incurred from the grant by exchanging the statement of accounts of the expenditure before the General Council.
The Vice-Chancellor shall be responsible to see that the grant is spent for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned by the Government to the Maharashtra National Law University, Nagpur. In this regard, they should submit its utility certificate to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education with the approval of the Executive Council.
The Director (Higher Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune shall be responsible for handing over the said approved provision to Maharashtra National Law University, Nagpur immediately.
Further, all the documents and accounts of the above grant are entitled to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General-2, Nagpur on a case to case basis. The grant granted to the university should be used for the purpose for which it was sanctioned. Also, it should be ensured that the expenditure is fully met within the stipulated time period and the utilization certificate should be issued.
