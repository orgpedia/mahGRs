The proposal:
As per the decision, the state government was considering to conduct a common entrance test for the State Institute of Administrative Business Education, Mumbai and the Indian Administrative Service Pre-Training Centre, Kolhapur, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati and Nagpur, as well as for the Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (Yashda), Pune, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti), Nagpur and Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation.
The government's decision:
The state government has approved the conduct of the common entrance test for the State Institute of Administrative Business Education, Mumbai and the Indian Administrative Service Pre-Teaching Centre in Nagpur, Amravati, Nashik, Aurangabad and Kolhapur, as well as for the Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (Yashda), Pune, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti), Nagpur and Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation.
The following steps should be taken to conduct the exam:
(1) The decision regarding the nature of the entrance test and interview as well as the examination center should be discussed by all the centers.
(2) For all candidates Rs. 600/- and Rs. 400/- is to be paid. This amount should be deposited in the personal account of the State Administrative Vocational Education Institute, Mumbai and spent for the incidental matter of the examination.

(3) The colleges and staff required for the examination center should be made available by the concerned Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education.
(4) The Director, State Institute of Administrative Vocational Education, Mumbai is being appointed as the Nodal Officer for the said examination.
(5) The examination will be conducted in online mode.
(6) The merit list shall be prepared on the basis of the marks obtained by the candidates in the written test and interview and the choice given by them for the training centre and the list shall be circulated to the concerned institutions.
(7) If feedback is received from Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Tribal Research and Training Institute (TARTI) and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI) for inclusion in the present Common Entrance Test (CET) process, they should also be included in this process.
