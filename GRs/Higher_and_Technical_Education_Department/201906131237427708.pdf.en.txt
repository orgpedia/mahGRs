2) Letter from the Director, Technical Education No. 2/ NGC/ Accreditation/ 2019/604, dated 4/6/2019.
Patil College of Architecture, Pune, was under the consideration of the government to grant approval for increasing the intake capacity of B.Arch.
Approval is being given by the Government to increase the intake capacity of B.Arch. course of Patil College of Architecture, Pune from 80 to 120 subject to the following conditions
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed by the Council of Architecture in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.
3) The admission capacity of the institution for the said course shall be as mentioned in the prospectus.
(4) The Institution shall enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.

5) For the affiliation of the said course, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra State University Act, 2016.
(6) The institution concerned shall meet other legal requirements by depositing the required accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education.
7) Admission to the said Degree Course shall be made as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
8) If the fees charged by the institutions concerned are more than the tuition fees prescribed by the Tuition Fee Committee, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
9) The institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teachers and other staff.
10) Books, periodicals required by the library in the institution must be purchased in accordance with the standards of the Council of Architecture. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students. Also students are provided with all educational facilities according to the standards of the Council of Architecture (e.g.
laboratories, libraries, classrooms, etc.
11) In the context of ragging. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999, need to be strictly implemented.
Other terms and conditions as laid down by the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and the University concerned shall also apply.
3. The institutions which have not paid the processing fee to the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, shall recover the same before final approval by the Director.
