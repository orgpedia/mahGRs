The Cabinet Sub-Committee chaired by the Minister (Revenue) in its meeting held on 28/8/2018 has decided to grant permission at the level of District Collector to a registered institution, if it is willing to run a hostel in its own building for students belonging to economically backward classes, by following the procedure prescribed in the Government Decision dated 13/7/2018 and dated 10th August, 2018.
Necessary government orders were being issued in this regard.
The Punjab Government has decided to allow the institutions registered under the Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme to run hostels in their own buildings for the students belonging to economically backward classes in such places where hostels are not available for rent for running under the Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme.
