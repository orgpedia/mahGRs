(2) The period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to the principals transferred for administrative reasons as per the rules of the Government. The Principal who is transferred on request will not be allowed to hold office for the period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance.
The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) On receipt of the order of transfer, the Principal concerned shall immediately retire from the existing institution and join the institution transferred. All the Principals should immediately join the place of the transferred institution by handing over the charge to the senior department head/ faculty of the institution without waiting for the appearance of the alternate principal.
(2) No request for change of posting shall be entertained. If any principal directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, such officers will be liable for disciplinary action.
(3) No leave of the transferred Principal shall be sanctioned without prior approval of the Administrative Department.
(4) In order to ensure effective implementation of these transfers, the salaries of all the transferred Principals up to the date of retirement or upto the maximum date i.e. 15th July, 2019 shall be drawn by the institution in which they are presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer institution under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the post-transfer institution after the concerned principal is transferred to the institution of the transferor. The drawing and disbursement officer of the institution where the salary from July 16, 2019 onwards was paid before the transfer will be held personally liable.
