The Proposal:
For the academic year 2022-23 Ramtek, T.G. Nagpur, Pragati Multipurpose Education Society's Birsa Munda Tribal Art, Commerce and Science College, Devlapar, Ta.
Ramtek, District Nagpur has been granted a letter of intent under Government Decision dated 15.07.2022. Against this, Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Institute of Multipurpose Education, Nagpur, founded by Ma. The High Court had filed Writ Petition No. 8346/2022 at Bombay, Bench Nagpur.
The Hon'ble High Court of Bombay, Bench Nagpur in Writ Petition No. In the 8346/2022. passed by the High Court. Considering the order dated 02.04.2025, for the academic year 2022-23, the PRAGATI Multipurpose Education Society has established Birsa Munda Tribal Art, Commerce and Science College, Devlapar, Ta. The cancellation of the letter of intent provided for the opening of the new college, Ramtek, G. Nagpur, was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Ma. The High Court Bombay, Bench Nagpur filed Writ Petition No. In the 8346/2022. In view of the orders passed by the High Court, the PRAGATI Multipurpose Education Society has established Birsa Munda Tribal Art, Commerce and Science College, Devlapar, for the academic year 2022-23. The letter of intent provided for starting a new college, Ramtek, G. Nagpur, is being cancelled by this.
