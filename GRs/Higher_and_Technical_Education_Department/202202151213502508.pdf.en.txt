Introduction: The Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata was established in 1972 with financial assistance from the Government of India. The objective of the Foundation is to promote universal development of public library services in the country. The Foundation provides matching grants to the States in the ratio of 50:50 to effectively implement and enhance library services in the States and Union Territories. Raja Rammohun Roy Library has been established as per the guidelines in the book" Books for the Maligneurs at their Doorstep" of the Foundation. The State Library Planning Committee has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 02.12.2021.
