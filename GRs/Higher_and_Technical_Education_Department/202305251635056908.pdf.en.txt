The High Court, Bombay, Bench at Aurangabad in Petition No. 6111/2018 has been filed. In this case, The High Court, Bombay Bench at Aurangabad on 24.01.2020 heard the case. The court has given the following orders:
1. The Writ Petition is partly allowed.
2. The impugned communication issued by respondent no.1 - State dated 15.03.2018, is quashed and set aside.
3. Respondent no.1 State is directed to consider the proposal of the petitioner Institute afresh and decide it expeditiously, and not later than eight weeks from receipt
of this order. Respondent no.1 shall communicate its decision to the petitioner Institute within two weeks from the date of decision. We make it clear that respondent
no.1 shall not reject the proposal of the petitioner, raising the same ground again. We also direct respondent no.1 - State Government to decide the proposal on the backdrop of the situation prevailed, when the proposal was submitted. Respondent no.1 shall not reject the proposal on the ground that in the perspective plan, area Sailu does not find place.

Terms and Conditions: 1. The Intent Letter to start a new college as per the prevailing policy of the Government shall remain valid for starting a college on non-subsidized basis.
Since the said Letter of Intent has been issued by the Institute with a view to fulfill the prescribed terms and conditions before obtaining final approval for the new college, the Institute shall not start the college without obtaining final approval of the Government to start the new college.
The letter of intent issued by the government to start a new college will be valid till January 31, 2024. During this period, it will be mandatory to submit a compliance report to the university along with the necessary documents for final approval on the basis of the terms and conditions on which the letter of intent is issued.
In view of the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, the Government has decided to provide Rs. The procedure for final approval as on 15th September, 2017 has been clearly spelt out. In accordance with the provisions of the Act and the procedures laid down thereunder, the institutions receiving the Letter of Intent fulfilling the terms and conditions of the Letter of Intent shall apply to the University for final approval along with all the documents and the Universities shall submit the applications of all eligible applicant institutions along with the compliance report to the Government along with the recommendation for approval in Annexure' C' and Form' B' and the accompanying documents.
The desirous institution seeking final approval to start a new college should submit a compliance report to the concerned university stating that the terms and conditions of the letter of intent have been fulfilled. Along with this, the documents mentioned in Annexure' C' should be attached.
