The matter of approving the pay structure as per the 7th Pay Commission and the" Pay Matrix Level" to the Dean Cadre in Government Arts Colleges under the control of the Directorate of Arts was under the consideration of the Government, accordingly, the following decision has been taken.
The Government is pleased to approve the implementation of revised pay structure for the Dean in Government Arts Colleges under the control of Directorate of Arts with effect from 1st January, 2016 as per the Seventh Pay Commission as indicated in the following statement. The fixation of pay in the revised pay structure shall be in accordance with the Government Notification dated 30th January, 2019 and Government Circular dated 20th February, 2019 and orders issued thereunder.

The employees of the post of Dean in the Government Arts College shall, within one month from the date of issue of this order, give an option in the form of Annexure-I attached to this Government Order to accept the fixation of salary as per the revised pay structure. Also, the bond will be presented in the form of a pair of bonds. If the option is not received within the prescribed time, the employee will be deemed to have opted for the revised pay structure on and from January 1, 2016. In such a case, the head of the office shall obtain from such employee a bond in the form of an addendum to this government decision.
In case of any doubt regarding the revised pay structure, the matter should be referred to the Director of Arts, Directorate of Arts, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. Art directors should submit such cases to the government, if necessary.
The additional expenditure on account of implementation of revised pay scales as per the Seventh Pay Commission should be included under the head Demand No.W4,2205-Arts and Culture, 101 Fine Arts Education, (02) Government Art Institute, (02) (01) Government Art Institute (Compulsory), 01-Salary (2205 0037) and should be met from the grants sanctioned under that head. The Director of Art, Directorate of Art, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall take into account this additional expenditure while preparing the revised estimates.
