Introduction: As per the Government decision dated 23.05.2006, 29 posts of Assistant Library Director/ Librarian Class-II cadre have been sanctioned after reviewing the posts under the Directorate of Libraries and Directorate. The reference As per the Government decision dated 27.04.2009, 10 posts of Librarians in Gazetted Group-B cadre have been sanctioned.
Also referenced. As per the Government decision dated 07.12.2010, 5 posts of Librarians in Gazetted Group-B cadre have been sanctioned. At present, there are 44 sanctioned posts in Group B of Maharashtra State Library Service (Assistant Library Director/ District Library Officer, Group-B).
The post of District Library Officer (Group-B Gazetted) is in the offices of District Library Officers in 28 districts of the state under the Directorate of Libraries. However, in 7 districts of Vidarbha namely Nagpur, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Yavatmal, Wardha, Akola and Buldhana, the post of District Library Officer (Group-B Gazetted) is not in existence and the work there is run by the employees of Class-3" District Librarian" (Group-C Gazetted). Since the functions and services of all the District Library Officer offices in the state are of the same nature, it is necessary to have the post of District Library Officer in these districts also, so the High Level Committee of Secretaries has approved the creation of 7 posts in the cadre of" District Librarian" (Group-C Non-Gazetted, Pay Scale 9300-34800 Grade Pay 4300) in the revised figure of the Directorate of Libraries and instead of it, 7 posts in the cadre of" District Library Officer (Group-B Gazetted, Pay Scale 9300 34800 Grade Pay 4400).
