The following instructions shall be strictly complied with by the Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution so that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
2. On receipt of transfer orders, the transferred Assistant Professors are transferred by the Principal/ Director concerned without waiting for the replacement faculty to appear.
The assistant professors should be relieved immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned Assistant Professors should immediately join the college/ institution where they have been transferred from the existing college/ institution.
(3) The transferred Assistant Professor shall be relieved from the charge of the concerned college/ institution.
They should be made to join free of cost after joining. The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution shall be liable to pay salary and allowances in case of non-employment of an Assistant Professor due to compulsory waiting period.
will be held responsible.
4.No request for change in post will be entertained.
If any assistant professor directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, the concerned assistant professor will be liable for disciplinary action.
5. No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
Since these transfers are required to be effected with immediate effect, the salary of the transferred Assistant Professors up to the date of retirement or maximum upto 15th October, 2020 shall be drawn by the college/ institution where they are presently working. any subsequent payment.
In the circumstances, the transfer should not be from the previous college/ institution. This salary should be drawn by the college/ institution after the transfer of the concerned assistant professor to the college/ institution of the transferor. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from October 16, 2020 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the college/ institution where they were working before the transfer.
The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be personally responsible for the immediate release of the transferred Assistant Professors.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune should submit the report to the Government before 16th October, 2020.
