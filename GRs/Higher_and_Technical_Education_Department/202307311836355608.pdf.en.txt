The proposal:
The Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi has approved to start a new Institute of Diploma Pharmacy course on non-aided basis in the State from the academic year 2023-24 and to start a Diploma Pharmacy course in the existing Institute of Degree Pharmacy course. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide the aforesaid Notification No. A proposal in this regard has been sent to the government.
The government's decision:
In pursuance of the approval granted by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi from the academic year 2023-24, the Institute mentioned in the attached Form-A& B is being granted approval by the Government to start a new Institute of Diploma Pharmacy course on non-aided basis in the State and to conduct Diploma Pharmacy course in the existing Institute of Degree Pharmacy course subject to the following terms and conditions.
