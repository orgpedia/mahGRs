GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the Government of India, Department of Youth Affairs and Sports, vide letter dated 16.8.2016 and 9.9.2016, with effect from April, 2016, the Central shareholding in National Service Scheme (NSS) has become 100% and the scheme has become Centrally Sponsored. A separate savings account has been opened in the bank in which funds have been received from the Centre through ECS. According to the above letter of the central government, the funds have been deposited in the separate savings account of the National Service Scheme without directly going to the state. According to the government decision, this fund is distributed to the universities for the programs of the National Service Scheme. For the year 2020-2021 (1st instalment 75% Central Share for all categories), the funds for disbursement of Subsidiary Grants (conditional) to the respective Universities from ICICI Bank Savings Account of National Service Scheme as mentioned in Annexure A and B are being sanctioned as per the decision of the Government.

The amount of grant-in-aid is disbursed by ECS through Public Fund Distribution System (PFMS) to the Registrar/ Director/ Controller of the concerned University by the State National Service Scheme Officer (State Liaison Officer/ Officer on Special Duty) of the National Service Scheme with the approval of the Principal Secretary, Department of Higher and Technical Education. Universities should be careful to spend according to the guidelines of the central government. Also, the universities should allocate this grant amount to the colleges under their control through Public Fund Distribution System (PFMS) category wise and submit it to the government immediately with the signature of the Chartered Accountant along with the Assistant Grant Allocation Statement and Utilization Certificate letter. The University shall also submit to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai and to the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur and to this Department or its representative all accounts of grants under the National Service Scheme for inspection.
