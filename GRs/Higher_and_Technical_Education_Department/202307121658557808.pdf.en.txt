GOVERNMENT DECISIONS, GENERAL ADMINISTRATION DEPARTMENT NO. LPL-2017/ Q. No.21/ K. 15, dated 10th June, 2019 2. Circular No. 50-55/ Service Review/ 2023/81 dated 17th February, 2023 4. Date of meeting of the Departmental Review Committee 23rd May, 2023
Government Order:
A meeting of the" Departmental Review Committee" under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Higher and Technical Education) was held on 23rd May, 2023 regarding continuation of services of officers working in offices under Directorate of Technical Education who have completed 50/55 years of age or 30 years of qualifying service as on 01st August, 2020, beyond the age of 50/ 55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
As per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee, the officers of Group-A cadre in FORM A and FORM B as well as Group-B (Gazetted) cadre in FORM C and FORM D are fulfilling the criteria prescribed by the General Administration Department, the Government hereby approves the continuation of their services after they attain the age of 50/ 55 years or after completing 30 years of qualifying service.
