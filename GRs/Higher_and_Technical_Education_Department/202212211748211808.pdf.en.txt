Preservation grant is sanctioned to Government recognized public libraries as per the provisions of Maharashtra Public Libraries (Recognition of Subsidiary Grants and Grants for Building and Equipment) Rules, 1970. Keeping in view the continuous increase in the cost of books and periodicals and other expenses, and with a view to increase the salary of the employees of the Government recognized public libraries, the Government in the context of the decision has increased the preservation grant by 50%. In view of the increasing inflation, it is necessary to get additional assistance to meet the expenses of the necessary aspects related to the maintenance of libraries.
In pursuance of the assurance given in this regard, a proposal to enhance the existing preservation grant of Government Recognized Public Libraries was placed for consideration in the Cabinet Meeting held on 13.12.2022. The proposal The Cabinet has approved it. Accordingly, the matter of increasing the existing maintenance grant of Government recognized public libraries was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Government is approving an increase in the existing subsidy rates of Class A, B, C and D Libraries as indicated in Annexure-A to increase the conservation grant admissible to Government recognized Public Libraries by 60% with effect from 2022-23.
The grant to Government recognized public libraries is based on the permissible expenditure incurred by the library in the previous year. Government recognized libraries will continue to receive 60% of the prescribed/ permissible grant-in-aid, subject to the ceiling of the preservation grant, at the end of 31st March, 2022.
