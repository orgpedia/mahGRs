The proposal:
Pursuant to the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, the Director, Technical Education vide letter dated 26/07/2021 has proposed to change the name of the existing Government, Government Aided, University Managed Institutions/ College from the academic year 2021-22 and to change the name of the course currently being offered in the existing institution.
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, from the academic year 2021-22, the change in the name of the existing government, government-aided, university-run institution/ college as indicated against the names of the institutions in Annexure A Annexure B was under consideration of the government and approval was given to change the name of the course currently running in the existing institution.
The government's decision:
In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government has approved the change in the name of the existing Government, Government aided, University run institution/ college as indicated in Annexure-A and Annexure B respectively and change in the name of the course being run in the existing institution from the academic year 2021-22, as proposed by the Director, Technical Education.
