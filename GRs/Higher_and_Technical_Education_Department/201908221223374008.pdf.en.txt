The proposal:
The MahaDBT portal, developed by the State Government to provide the benefits of government schemes directly to the beneficiaries, has been operationalized in August 2018 and through this portal, the process of inviting applications for scholarships from eligible students, scrutinizing the applications from college and department level, providing allotment for payment of scholarships to the approved students, and disbursement of scholarships in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the approved student through PFMS system, etc. is done through MahaIT and MahaOnline. For the year 2018-19, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the expenditure incurred by MahaIT for the services rendered during the period April, 2019 to July, 2019 to provide the benefits of all the scholarship schemes under the Directorate of Higher Education directly to the beneficiaries through the MahaDBT portal.
Government Decision: For the period April, 2019 to June, 2019, a sum of Rs. 17,74,387/-for the month of July, 2019 and Rs. 2,47,031/- (Rs. 20,21,418/- (Rupees Twenty Lakh Twenty One Thousand Four Hundred and Eighty Eight only) is being approved to be paid out of the sanctioned amount for the year 2019-20.
