Circular:
The All India Council for Technical Education vide its letter dated 26/4/2019 has informed that the Hon'ble Supreme Court vide its order dated 3/11/2017 has suspended the open and distance courses in Engineering from the following 4 Deemed Universities which were admitted after the academic year 2001-2005.
J.R.N. Rajasthan University, Udaipur (Rajasthan) 2. Allahabad (Uttar Pradesh), V.M.R.I., Salem (Tamil Nadu)
(2) Even if the degrees of these open and distance courses are suspended, the examination for re-awarding the degrees to the students admitted to these courses shall be ordered. The Supreme Court dated 22/1/2018. As per the All India Council for Technical Education
The University Grants Commission (UGC) had conducted the examination in June and December 2018. The results are available on the website of the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), www. aicte-india. org.
The All India Council for Technical Education has requested to check the results through this letter. All the Administrative Departments are hereby directed to issue suitable instructions to all the concerned authorities/ offices under them to examine the results and take further appropriate action in this regard.
