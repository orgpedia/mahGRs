Government circular
The provisional seniority as on 1.1.2017 for the post of Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) Aided Professor, Group A cadre in the colleges/ institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education is being published as per Annexure-A attached to this circular.
The Director, Higher Education, Pune is hereby directed to bring this seniority to the notice of all the concerned faculty members and if any genuine errors or omissions are found in it, they should be reported to the Government within 15 days from the date of issue of this Government Circular. Any claims/ objections received after the due date will not be entertained.
The Director, Higher Education shall collate all the representations received within the prescribed period and forward them to the Government along with their feedback.
