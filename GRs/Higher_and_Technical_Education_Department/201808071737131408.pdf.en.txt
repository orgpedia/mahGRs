The government's decision:
In the Government Resolution dated 07/10/2017, the condition under the heading" Terms and Conditions" for eligibility of benefit under Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme vide No. 22, 30 and 31 are being approved by the Government as follows:
