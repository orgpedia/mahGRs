Decision of the Government:- Maharashtra National Vadiyapeeth, Aurangabad will be given Rs. 13,91,66, 000/- and Rs. Rs 5,00,00, 000. 18,91,66, 000/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores Ninety One Lakh Sixty Six Thousand only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
(1) The Executive Council shall be responsible for administering the grants sanctioned by the Government as per the Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014 by depositing the grants in the University Fund.
Also, the university should take action to exchange the expenditure made from this grant and keep a statement of the accounts of the expenditure before the General Council.
(2) It shall be the responsibility of the Vice-Chancellor to see that the grant is spent for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned by the Government to the Maharashtra National Law University, Aurangabad. In this regard, they should submit its utility certificate to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education with the approval of the Executive Council.
(3) All the documents and accounts of all the above grants are to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur on a case to case basis and if so requested, all the documents should be submitted to him by the Law University for examination.
(4) The University shall use the grant-in-aid sanctioned for the purpose for which the grant-in-aid is sanctioned.
(5) The provisions of the Financial Powers Rules in this regard shall be strictly complied with. Also, the existing rules and conditions of the government should be taken into consideration.
The Director (Higher Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune shall be responsible for handing over the said approved provision to Maharashtra National Law University, Aurangabad immediately. Further, all the documents and accounts of the above grant are entitled to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or by the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General-2, Nagpur on a case to case basis. The grant granted to the university should be used for the purpose for which it was sanctioned.
