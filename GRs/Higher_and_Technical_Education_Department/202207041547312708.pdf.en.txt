The proposal:
All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. 1 here. Pursuant to the approval given on 18.10.2021, the Director, Technical Education vide the aforesaid No. 2 here. As proposed vide letter dated 01.12.2021, it was under consideration of the Government to grant approval for closure of Dattakala Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology, Pune, a professional course institute, from the academic year 2021-22.
The government's decision:
As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, pursuant to the approval of All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the closure of the Institute of Vocational Courses, Dattakala Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology, Pune from the academic year 2021-22 is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) All the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institute such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. are related to the institute.
It must be submitted to the university.
(2) Responsibility for all matters relating to the judicial and other matters relating to the alternative arrangements for teachers and non-teaching staff appointed for the relevant courses in the institution.
The head of the institution will remain. In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.

3) If the government's recovery from the institution is still pending, it will be mandatory for the institution head to pay it.
(4) It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay the loans taken by it from the Government or any other financial institution in respect of the immovable property required for running the course in the Institute.
5) During the phased closure of the institution, the institution will take the responsibility of providing educational facilities to the admitted students and alumni of the institution till the completion of the course and to make arrangements for them to sit for the examination. If the institution does not provide these educational facilities, action will be taken against the institution as per the rules and the accumulated deposit with the government will be deposited by the government.
(6) It shall be obligatory on the part of the institution to pay the salary, salary compensation and other financial allowances payable as per rules to the teachers and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
