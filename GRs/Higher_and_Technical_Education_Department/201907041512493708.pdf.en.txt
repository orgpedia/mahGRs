Preamble: In pursuance of the list received by the Director, Technical Education vide e-mail dated 15.05.2019 from the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi regarding recognition of new institutions for Diploma Courses in Professional Pharmacy in the State from the academic year 2019-20, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education vide note No. The proposal has been submitted to the government through a letter. The matter of granting Government approval to start a new Institute of Diploma Course in Pharmacy to the 01 Institute mentioned in the attached Form-A was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
In pursuance of the approval granted by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Institute mentioned in the attached Form-A is being granted approval by the Government to start the institution of the new Diploma course in Pharmacy from the academic year 2019-20 subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The eligibility of the institution for admission to this course shall be as mentioned in Form-A.
For affiliation of the said course, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.

6. The institution concerned shall complete other legal formalities by depositing the required accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education.
7. Admission to the said Diploma Course shall be made as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
If the institutions concerned charge more than the tuition fee prescribed by the Fee Regulatory Authority, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
The institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the rules of the government. Salaries and allowances should be paid to the employees as per the rules.
Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
The library of the institution is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students.
