Preamble: The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retd. The decision to accept the recommendations of the said Committee with certain modifications has been taken under the Government Decision Vepur-2018/ PROCESS 44/ SERVICE-9 dated 1st January, 2019 of the Finance Department.
The Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 have been notified vide Finance Department Notification No. Vepur-2019/ PROCESS 1/ SERVICE-9 dated 30th January, 2019 for implementation of 7th Pay Commission to the State Government employees with effect from 1st January, 2016. On page no. 115-116 includes designations on the establishment of the Art Directorate. However, due to confusion regarding the proposed pay scales in the 7th Commission corresponding to the pay scales in the 6th Pay Commission for the posts of Dean and Faculty, these posts were not included in the said notification dated 30th January, 2019.
The detailed procedure for fixation of pay has been prescribed vide Circular No. Vepur-2019/ PROCESS 8/ SERVICE-9 dated 20th February, 2019 of the Finance Department. Further, the circular dated 30th May, 2019 clarifies the payment of arrears to the employees who are covered under the National Pension Scheme or Defined Contributory Pension Scheme.
The matter of approving the salary structure as per the 7th Pay Commission for the faculty cadre in the Government Arts Colleges under the control of the Directorate of Arts was under the consideration of the Government, accordingly, the following decision has been taken.

Government Resolution No: ADR-2019/ No.36/ Tanshi-6,
The Government have approved the revised pay structure for the post of Professor in Government Arts Colleges under the control of Directorate of Arts with effect from 1st January, 2016 as per the Seventh Pay Commission as indicated in the following statement. The fixation of pay in the revised pay structure shall be in accordance with the Government Notification dated 30th January, 2019 and Government Circular dated 20th February, 2019 and orders issued thereunder.

| Professor     | 15600-39100     | 8000                                      | Academic Levl 12 (79800 211500) with rationalized entry pay of Rs.79800/ (UGC) |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The employees of the post of Professor in the Government Arts College shall, within one month from the date of issue of this order, give an option in the form of Annexure-I attached to this Government Order to accept the fixation of salary as per the revised pay structure. Also, the bond will be presented in the form of a pair of bonds. If the option is not received within the prescribed time, the employee will be deemed to have opted for the revised pay structure on and from January 1, 2016. In such a case, the head of the office shall obtain from such employee a bond in the form of an addendum to this government decision.
In case of any doubts regarding the rules for payment of salary in the revised pay structure, the cases should be submitted to the Director of Arts, Directorate of Arts, Maharashtra State Mumbai.
Art directors should submit such cases to the government, if necessary.
The additional expenditure on account of implementation of revised pay scales as per 7th Pay Commission should be included under the head Demand No.W4,2205-Arts and Culture, 101-Line Art Education, (02) Government Art Institute, (02) (01) Government Art Institute (Compulsory), 01-Salary (2205 0037) and should be met from the grants sanctioned under that head. The Director of Art, Directorate of Art, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall take into account this additional expenditure while preparing the revised estimates.
The payment of arrears of salary in respect of all employees in service as on 1st January, 2016 shall be dealt with in accordance with the provisions of Rule 14 of the circular dated 20th February, 2019 of the Finance Department and Circular Vepur-2019/ PR No.8/ Service-9 dated 30th May, 2019.
This decision was taken by the Government with the concurrence of the Finance Department in its Informal Reference No. 369/2019/ SERVICE-9 dated 18th September, 2019.
