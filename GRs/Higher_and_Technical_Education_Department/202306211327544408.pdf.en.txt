Preamble: The Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi and the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi have decided to change the names of the existing unaided Diploma Pharmacy Courses in the State. 1 to 3 are accepted. Further, pursuant to the said approval, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide their aforesaid Reference No. 4 The proposal has been sent to the government. In pursuance of the proposal, the Government was considering to approve change in the names of the existing unaided Diploma in Pharmacy courses from the academic year 2023-24.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval given by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi from the academic year 2023-24, the Government is approving change in the name of the institutions of the existing unaided Diploma course in Pharmacy as mentioned in the following" Form-A."
