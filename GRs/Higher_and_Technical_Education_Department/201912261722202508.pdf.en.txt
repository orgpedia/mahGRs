In exercise of the powers conferred by section 58 of the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994, the Government hereby make an order for the constitution of a University and College Tribunal for non-agricultural Universities. The decision was taken by the government. Reference No. Orders have been issued regarding the appointment of the Presiding Officer of the University and College Tribunal of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad. The said Act of 1994 has since been amended by the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016. Section 80 of the Act provides for the appointment of a Presiding Officer.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the appointment of a new Presiding Officer to the post of Presiding Officer of the University and College Tribunal at Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad was under consideration of the Government.
The post of the Presiding Officer of Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University and the University and College Tribunal in Aurangabad is vacant.
