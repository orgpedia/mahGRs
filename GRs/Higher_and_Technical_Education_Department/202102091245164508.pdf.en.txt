Introduction: The Director, Directorate of Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government through a reference letter regarding the transfer of students studying in the diploma course in Government Engineering College, Yavatmal from the academic year 2018-19 to another institution from the academic year 2020-21. Accordingly, the matter of approving the transfer was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: With the conversion of Government Diploma Institute, Government Technological College, Yavatmal (Old) into Government Engineering College, Yavatmal from the academic year 2018-19, the Government is approving the migration of the students who are eligible for admission to the second and third year of the diploma course in the said institution beyond the limit of intake of the relevant course in other institutions as shown in the table below from the academic year 2020-21.
