The proposal:
The book titled" Treasures of Indian Knowledge" published by Snehal Prakashan and written by Mr. Prashant Paul was released on the occasion. The Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Shri. It was held on 14.05.2017 at Pune. The Hon'ble Chief Minister has directed to supply the book to all Government aided libraries in the State. The Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide Reference No. Under the letter, a proposal has been submitted to the government to make the book" Treasure of Indian Knowledge" available free of cost to all government-recognized public libraries in the state. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to provide administrative as well as financial assistance for making available the book" Treasure of Indian Knowledge" free of cost to the Government recognised public libraries.
Government Decision: Administrative approval is being given to the scheme of making available the book" Treasure of Indian Knowledge" published by Snehal Prakashan and written by Mr. Prashant Paul to Government recognized public libraries.
