The proposal:
In order to provide a separate government building to the Government Teachers College, Nanded and to provide basic facilities to the students in the college, the Divisional Joint Director, Nanded has proposed the construction of a new building for the college on the land of Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded campus.
Accordingly, the Director, Higher Education, Pune vide letter dated 24/11/2020 has submitted a proposal to the Government for obtaining administrative approval for the construction of the proposed Teachers College building. The proposal had been approved by the government.
The government's decision:
Director, Higher Education, Pune has submitted a proposal to the Government seeking administrative approval for the construction of Government Teachers College, Nanded in the premises of Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada Vishwavidyalaya, Nanded.
