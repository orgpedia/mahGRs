The concerned Director/ Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education should relieve the transferred officers immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned officers should join the office immediately after their transfer from the existing office.
The concerned Director/ Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education should make the transferred officers join without delay after they are relieved from the concerned office and join. The Director/ Joint Director of Departmental Higher Education shall be held responsible in case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of the officer by the concerned Director/ Joint Director of Departmental Higher Education. Also, no leave of absence will be granted during the period from the date of discharge to the date of joining the place of transfer.
