1) The terms of the original administrative approval order will continue to apply.
2) The work should be completed within the limits of the revised administrative amount.
3) The Cidco shall furnish an undertaking as to the time within which the said construction shall be completed.
The management of Vande Mataram Hall will be under the control of Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad.
However, the Department of Higher and Technical Education will be given priority for the use of the hall.
