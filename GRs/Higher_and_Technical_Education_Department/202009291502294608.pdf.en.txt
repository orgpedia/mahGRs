The proposal:
As per the 7th Pay Commission, the post of Professor in Government Arts Colleges under the control of Directorate of Arts," Academic Levl 12 (79800-211500) with rationalized entry pay of Rs. 79800/- (UGC)" The revised pay structure has been approved with effect from 1st January, 2016 vide Government Resolution dated 18th September, 2019. Further, it has been decided that the fixation of pay in the revised pay structure shall be in accordance with the Government Notification dated 30th January, 2019 and the Government Circular dated 20th February, 2019 and the orders issued thereunder.
In order to determine the salary of the post of Professor in Government Arts Colleges as above, the reference number 6 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education for this post is attached herewith as Annexure No. 1 at Rs. The matter of sanctioning" Pay Matrix Level" on the lines of Pay Matrix Level mentioned for 15600-39100+ Grade Pay 8000 was under consideration of the Government.
