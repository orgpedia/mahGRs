The proposal:
Reference No. 1 Under the Government decision dated 22/2/2018, detailed orders have been issued regarding the levy of registration fee and fixed deposit in the joint account for various proposals of institutions of professional courses at the level of Bachelor's, Master's Degree/ Diploma, Post-Diploma Diploma on non-subsidized basis. Since the apex body All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has newly included some proposals in the approval process for the academic year 2019-2020, the Director has submitted a proposal to the Government to include these in the Governing Decision dated 22/2/2018.
The government's decision:
The Government is approving the fixation of registration fee for various proposals in the institutions/ colleges of professional courses at the level of Bachelor's, Master's Degree/ Diploma, Post-Graduate Diploma on non-subsidized basis in the State from the academic year 2019-2020 as follows:
