The proposal:
Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar is known in the history of the country as a good administrator, valiant fighter, justice-loving person, as well as a people's mother who did innumerable public welfare works. Solapur University has been named after this great woman.
At present, the university. As there is a demand from the people's representatives, local social organizations and various sections of the district regarding the construction of a memorial for Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar in the North Solapur area, the university has been asked to set up a memorial for her. A committee was formed to build a memorial for Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar as per the decision of the government. The budget of Rs. 14,24,92, 238/- has been accorded administrative approval as per the decision of the Government in this regard.
For the construction of memorial and tower of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar at Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University, the budget approved by the Supplementary Demand for the year 2021-22 in the December, 2021 session of the Legislative Assembly is Rs. 1,73,91, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy Three Lakhs Ninety One Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government.
The government has decided to build a memorial and tower of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar at Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University. As per the administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 07.12.2021, Rs. 1,73,91, 000/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy Three Lakhs Ninety One Thousand only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
