Terms and Conditions: 1. As the aided colleges which have been sanctioned new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties in the above statement are on permanent non-grant basis, such colleges shall not be able to demand teachers and non-teaching staff on grant basis in accordance with this recognition.
The colleges which have been sanctioned new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties as above, will have to submit an undertaking to their Divisional Joint Directors that they will not demand any grant in future. 100/- on non-judicial stamp paper.
The University concerned should not start the process of affiliation without the college submitting an undertaking or obtaining the certificate of the Divisional Joint Director.
The colleges are required to appoint faculty/ staff as per the norms prescribed by the concerned university and educational qualification prescribed by the University Grants Commission and provide all other necessary infrastructure and the same shall be ensured by the Joint Director of the Department and only then the aforesaid condition No.
2 certificate from the university.
The college shall not, under any circumstances, grant admissions in excess of the sanctioned intake capacity.
The University shall not proceed to grant affiliation to the colleges concerned unless they meet the criteria prescribed for granting affiliation to the colleges in the University Grants Commission (Affiliation of Colleges by Universities Regulations 2009).
3. Universities shall not initiate the process of granting affiliation without first going through the Government approval orders and obtaining the certificate in Condition No. 2 of the Divisional Joint Director before taking necessary further action on the issue of affiliation. The universities should send the compliance report regarding the affiliation to the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and the concerned Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education.
The new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties are reviewed periodically by the respective Universities and the Joint Director of the Department in accordance with the approved new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties, if 50% or less of the approved student strength is found in the new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties.
