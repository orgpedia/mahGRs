The government's decision:
Scheme of waiver of examination fee for the academic year 2018-19 for the courses under Directorate of Technical Education vide Reference No. 4 This is being implemented for the students who are residents of villages in 268 revenue circles which have been declared as drought affected as per the Government of Revenue and Forest Department decision dated 6th November, 2018. The scheme of examination fee waiver for the students of such villages will be applicable if the Revenue and Forest Department declares drought in other taluks/ mandals in the year 2018.
2. Further orders are being issued in this regard.
Students who are residents of villages declared as drought affected by the State Government in the current year will be required to apply to the concerned institution for examination fee waiver along with proof that they are residents of that drought affected village. The concerned institution will be required to scrutinize such applications and forward them to the concerned University/ Board along with their recommendation.
2) The concerned university/ autonomous body/ Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education should not charge any examination fee from the students of the villages where drought has been declared by the state government.
(3) If the examination fee for the current academic year has already been charged by the concerned University/ Autonomous Body/ Board from the students who are eligible for the scheme of examination fee waiver in drought affected areas, such University/ Autonomous Body/ Board shall pay the examination fee to such students.
(a) 50% of the examination fee shall be refunded to the student if he/ she is eligible under the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme.
b) If the student is not eligible under the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme, i.e. if the student's admission is at the institution level, then 100 percent of the examination fee should be refunded to that student.
Students who will receive 50% of the examination fee under the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and have not yet paid the examination fee to the university/ board, will be required to deposit this amount to the respective universities/ board/ autonomous body within 15 days.
