The proposal:
The Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi has approved the opening of new institutions of Diploma Pharmacy course on permanent non-grant basis in the State from the academic year 2023-24. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide the aforesaid Notification No. A proposal in this regard has been sent to the government.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval granted by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi from the academic year 2023-24, the institutions mentioned in the attached Form-A are being given approval by the Government to start new institutions of Diploma Pharmacy course on permanent non-aided basis subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.
(2) In this case, as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education/ Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi and as prescribed by the Government Order dated 21st May, 1983.
It will be mandatory for the institution concerned to meet the conditions.
The intake capacity of the institution for the said course shall be limited as mentioned against their names in the prospectus.
(4) The institution shall be deemed to have complied with all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education/ Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi.
