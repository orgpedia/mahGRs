Introduction: Mr. Yogesh Kantilal Chandarana, Head of the Department, Applied Mechanics, Government Technological University, Nagpur was appointed as Deputy Secretary (Technical) by a decision of the Government in this regard. The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), Mumbai, has released a notification regarding the recruitment. However, the post of Deputy Secretary (s) is in the administrative technical cadre and not in the teaching cadre. Chandarana to cancel the transfer orders dated 31/05/2017. Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai Bench at Nagpur in original application No. 344/2017 was filed. According to the original application. The Tribunal has passed the following orders:" The impugned order of transfer of the applicant dated 31/05/2017 stands quashed and set aside.
Since the applicant has completed his tenure and his present post at Nagpur, the respondents are directed to post the applicant anywhere in the available post in the teaching department.
In pursuance of the said order, the matter of cancellation of the transfer of Mr. Yogesh Kantilal Chandarana, Head of the Department, Applied Mechanics, by the decision of the Government in question, and his transfer to a post in the teaching cadre, was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the general transfers in the year 2017 in the cadre of Head of Department, Government Technician and similar institutions in the Maharashtra Technician Teachers Service Group-A, Mr. Yogesh Kantilal Chandarana, Head of Department, Applied Mechanics, Deputy Secretary of Government Technician, Nagpur, has been appointed as the Deputy Secretary (Technical). The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), Mumbai has cancelled the recruitment exam.
Further, with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005, he is being transferred to the post of Head of Department, Applied Mechanics, vacant at Government Technician, Jalna on administrative grounds, as per rules 4 (4) and 4 (5).
Since the transfer was made for administrative reasons, Mr. Chandarana will be entitled to the period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of the government.
The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) This order shall come into force at once.

(2) On receipt of the transfer order, the Head of the Department concerned shall be transferred from the existing institution to join the institution indicated in the posting. Also, the heads of the concerned institutions should relieve the transferred department head immediately without waiting for the appearance of the alternate department head.
3) The transferred department heads should be recruited without delay after they are relieved from their respective organization and come to join. In case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-appointment of the head of the department, the principal of the institution concerned will be held responsible.
(4) No request for change in post will be entertained. If the concerned person directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, the officer will be liable for disciplinary action.
5) No leave of the transferred department head should be sanctioned without prior approval of the administrative department.
(6) The transfer must be effected effectively and the date of retirement of the transferred Head of the Department must be extended to the date of superannuation or till the date of superannuation. Salaries up to 31st December, 2017 should be drawn by the institution/ establishment where they are presently working. Subsequent remuneration should not be withdrawn from the pre-transferred institution/ establishment under any circumstances. The salary shall be drawn by the post-transfer institution/ establishment after the concerned officer joins the transferee institution.
7) It shall be the responsibility of the head of the institution concerned personally to relieve the transferred head of the department immediately. This should be followed up by the concerned Divisional Joint Director Technical Education, and the Director, Directorate of Technical Education should submit an executive report in this regard to the government without fail within 15 days from the date of this order.
