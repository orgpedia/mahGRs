GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the Government of India, Department of Youth Affairs and Sports, vide letter dated 16.8.2016 and 9.9.2016, with effect from April, 2016, the Central shareholding in the National Service Scheme has become 100% and it has become a Central Sector Scheme. A separate savings account has been opened in the bank in which funds have been received from the Centre through ECS. According to the above letter of the Central Government, the funds have been deposited in the separate savings account of the National Service Scheme without directly going to the state. As per the decision of the government, this fund is distributed to the universities for the programs of the National Service Scheme. For the year 2021-2022 (75% of the first instalment of grant-in-aid (Central Share) for all categories) to 25 Universities as mentioned in Annexure A, the disbursement from the savings bank account of National Service Scheme in Bank of Maharashtra, Mantralaya Branch, Mumbai is being approved under this Government decision.
Utilization certificates for the year 2020-2021 have not yet been received from 4 of the total 29 universities/ directorates in the state. Accordingly, for the year 2021-2022, (1st installment@ 75% for all categories), the remaining 25 Universities/ Directorates are being provided financial assistance of Rs.3,79,18,311/-for regular programme and Rs. 78,40,521/-for special camp programme, aggregating to Rs. 4,57,58, 832/- (Rupees Four Crores Fifty Seven Lakhs Forty Seven Thousand Eight Hundred Thirty Two only). Universities which have some funds left at the end of the year 2020-2021 are being approved to disburse the remaining funds by adjusting the balance funds from the dues of the year 2021-2022.
As the Government has not yet received the utilization certificate for the year 2020-21 from the 4 Universities viz., Mumbai University, Mumbai, 2.S. NDT Women's University, Mumbai, 3. Shivaji University, Kolhapur and 4. Mahatma Phule Agricultural University, Rahuri, the first installment of the grant-in-aid for the year 2021-22 has not been disbursed under this order.
The amount of grant-in-aid is disbursed by ECS through Public Fund Distribution System (PFMS) to the Registrar/ Director/ Controller of the concerned University/ Directorate.
