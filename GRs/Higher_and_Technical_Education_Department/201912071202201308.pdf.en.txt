Introduction: The review of the posts in various administrative offices and government and non-government aided educational institutions functioning under the Directorate of Technical Education in the state has been taken as per the government decision mentioned in reference number 1. As per the decision of the Government of the Finance Department mentioned in Reference No. 2, it is mandatory for all the administrative departments to review the sanctioned posts in the concerned department and the divisional offices under them and approve the revised figure.
Reference No. 3 hereby directs that the administrative departments which have not approved the final designations under the said Government Decision shall not be allowed to create and fill up new posts and also not to convert the posts till such time. As per the instructions issued under this government decision, vacant teaching posts in government and non-government aided degree and diploma institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education have not been filled. As per the Government decision mentioned at Reference No. 4, guidelines have been issued for demarcation of sanctioned posts in the administrative departments and offices under them.
The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India vide its letter dated 17.05.2019 mentioned in Reference No.5 has informed that an Action Plan (100% Day Action Plan) for 100% recruitment of teaching posts in Government and Non-Government Aided Colleges under the State Government will be prepared and sent to the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India immediately.
Accordingly, in reference No. 6, Mr. The Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra has apprised the Central Government of the action being taken in connection with the recruitment of vacancies in higher educational institutions in the State.
As per the orders issued by the Department of Finance under various Government decisions mentioned at Reference No. 2 to 4, it was necessary to fix the revised figure of sanctioned posts before the creation and recruitment of posts. Accordingly, the proposal for filling up of vacancies in the institutions related to technical education in the State and approval for creation of new posts as per the norms of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for the year 2019-20, creation of new posts/ revival of posts and review of posts etc. was placed before the Sub-Committee constituted for making recommendations after thorough examination of the proposals and approval for creation of notional posts and filling up of vacancies in the State in its fourteenth meeting held on 16th July, 2019. It was decided in the meeting as follows:" The proposal for delineation of teaching posts in Government, Non-Government aided Engineering, Pharmacy, Vastu Shastra and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University under the Department of Higher and Technical Education was submitted for the consideration of the sub-committee. The Sub-Committee recommended that a proposal for fixing revised pattern, abolition of certain posts and creation of certain new posts as per AICTE norms for the year 2019 be placed before the High-Level Committee of Secretaries. The sub-committee has also approved the filling up of vacant teaching posts out of the sanctioned posts. The posts proposed to be created can be filled only after the approval of the High-Level Secretary Committee. '

Accordingly, the proposal to create constitutional posts, degree and diploma level posts in Government Engineering and Pharmacy Degree Colleges, Non-Government Engineering and Pharmacy Degree College, Non-Government College of Vastu Shastra and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere under the Directorate of Technical Education in the State. The recommendations were submitted in the meeting of the High Level Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary on 29.08.2019. The minutes of the meeting have been issued vide letter No. 8 and the revised pattern has been approved for creation of teaching posts in the institutions under Directorate of Technical Education under the Department of Higher and Technical Education.
As per the approval given by the High Level Committee of Secretaries, orders have been issued to approve the revised pattern of creation of teaching posts in the institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education as per the Government decision in Reference No. 9. Accordingly, the Directorate has submitted the institution-wise and subject-wise details of the posts mentioned in the figure finalised as per the decision of the Government in this regard to the Government in the letters of reference No. 10 and 11 and requested to issue institution-wise, subject-wise and designation-wise Government decisions.
Considering the above facts, the Government was considering to approve institution-wise, course/ subject-wise and designation-wise alignment of teaching posts in Government Engineering (Degree, Post Graduate), Government Pharmacy (Degree, Post Graduate, Pharm D), Non-Government Aided Engineering (Degree), Non-Government Aided Vastu Shastra (Degree) colleges under the Directorate of Technical Education in the State.
Government Decision: In the Government Engineering (Bachelor's Degree, Master's Degree), Government Pharmacy (Bachelor's Degree, Master's Degree, Pharm-D), Non-Government Aided Engineering (Degree), Non-Government Aided Vastu Shastra (Degree) Institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education in the State, according to the review of the cadre-wise teaching posts sanctioned under the Government Decision mentioned at Reference No.9, as well as according to the workload mentioned in the Approval Process Handbook for the year 2019-20 of the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and as allocated by the concerned institutions, institution-wise, course/ subject-wise and designation-wise, as per the Annexure No. 08 in the form attached.
The approval is being accorded to the posts mentioned in the Annexure No. 07 as per the assignments mentioned in the Approval Process Handbook of All India Council for Technical Education for the year 2019-20 and as per the recommendations made by the concerned institutions.
