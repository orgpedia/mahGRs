The government order:
Dr. Ramdas Atram, Director, Government Institute of Legal Sciences, Nagpur (Professor Grade) has been appointed as Chancellor, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar University of Social Studies, Mhow for a period of 5 years or upto the age of 70 years, whichever is earlier. Atram, vide letter No. 2, requested to be relieved of his duties as Chancellor, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar University of Social Studies, Mhow.
Ramdas Athram has been appointed as the Vice-Chancellor of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar University of Social Studies, Mhow. They are hereby allowed to be relieved by keeping the lien till 28.02.2026. Further action will be taken by the Director, Higher Education, Pune.
The said lien shall automatically terminate on the date of his retirement as per his tenure. Dr. Atram may enter into Government service or retire from Government service before the lien period expires.
