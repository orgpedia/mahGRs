The government has decided to implement the Integrated University Management System (IUMS) system in all non-agricultural universities in the state. Under the IUMS project, the universities will be equipped with a robust ICT backbone to provide a seamless experience to students, faculty, university officials, staff, affiliated colleges and other stakeholders etc.
Accordingly, Reference No. 2 here. Orders have been issued vide Circular dated 3rd April, 2018 regarding constitution of IUMS Project Steering Committee (PSC) under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Higher& Technical Education) and Project Monitoring Committee (PMC) under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (H& T). General Administration The department has proposed to set up a Core Implementation Team in all colleges affiliated to non-agricultural public universities as well as all public universities in the state for the implementation of this project.
Accordingly, the following recommendations are made. Core Implementation Team should be formed in every college affiliated to the public university in the state as follows.
----------------------------------------------------------------------
| Principal/Vice Principal of Affiliated College | Head              |
| Administration Cell                            | 1 Representative  |
| IT Cell                                        | 2 Representatives |
| Lecturer                                       | 2 Representatives |
| Students                                       | 2 Representatives |
----------------------------------------------------------------------
