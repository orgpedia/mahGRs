Introduction: The Scheme of Equal Fund is implemented with the joint contribution of the State Government and the contribution of the Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata, functioning under the Department of Cultural Affairs, Government of India. Under this scheme, the libraries of the state are provided funds to spend on books, computers, xerox machines, cupboards, seminars and building construction.
