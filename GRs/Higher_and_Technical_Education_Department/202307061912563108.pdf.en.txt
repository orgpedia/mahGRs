(2) The order shall take effect immediately.
(3) The period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall not be admissible to the above officers as per the rules of the Government.
4. The transferred officers should be relieved immediately by the concerned head of office as this transfer order needs to be effectively implemented. The responsibility of relieving the transferred officers will be personally borne by the concerned Joint Director, Technical Education Divisional Office.
5. The transferred officers should be reinstated without delay after they are relieved from their duties and join. If there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances without work due to non-recruitment of an officer, the concerned officer will be held responsible.
6. The concerned Joint Director, Divisional Technical Education Office shall follow up the implementation of the said order effectively. Further, the executive report in this regard should be submitted to the government through the Director, Technical Education within 15 days from the date of this order.
7. No leave of transferred officer shall be sanctioned without prior approval of the Government.
(8) After the orders of such transfers have been issued, the Maharashtra Civil Services (Duration of Appointment, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, as amended, shall come into force.
