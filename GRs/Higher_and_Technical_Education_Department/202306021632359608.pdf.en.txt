Administrative approval for construction of Maharshi Karve Women Empowerment Knowledge Complex at Visapur, Ballarpur, G.Chandrapur of Women's University.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Supplement No:- Meeting-2022/ P. No. 123/ Special-2
Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai- 400 032.
Dated: June 2, 2023.
Reference: Department of Education. Government Resolution dated 13.03.2023.
Construction of Maharshi Karve Women Empowerment Knowledge Complex at Visapur, Ballarpur, G.Chandrapur of Women's University at a cost of Rs. 4131.65 lakhs (Rupees One Crore Thirty One Lakh Sixty Five Thousand only) has been accorded administrative approval as per the decision of the Government in this regard. In the said report, the Public Works Department was requested to make provision for centage charges for construction and electrification work as there was no provision for labour insurance. The Public Works Department has approved the inclusion of these items.
Accordingly, as per the Recapitulation Sheet attached with Rs. 4265.98 lakhs (Rupees Forty Two Crores Sixty Five Lakhs Ninety Eight Thousand only) is being included in the relevant Government Resolution through this Supplementary Paper and the same is being administratively approved. Further action will be taken according to this.
