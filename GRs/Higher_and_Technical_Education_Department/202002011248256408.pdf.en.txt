The government was considering to implement the revised pay structure in accordance with the 7th Pay Commission for the non-teaching staff of the Institute of Economics, Pune, a government-aided deemed university.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the review of posts in Gokhale Institute of Political Science and Economics, Pune, a government-aided deemed university under the Department of Higher Education, the revised pay structure in accordance with the Seventh Pay Commission is being applied to the non-teaching cadres approved by the Government.
