The proposal:
Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik University was established by the Maharashtra Legislative Council Act No. 20 respectively. 01.07.1989. The University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has jurisdiction over the entire state of Maharashtra. The university has 8 regional centres located at Amravati, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Mumbai, Nashik, Pune, Kolhapur and Nanded. 1600 study centers are controlled by 8 departmental centers of the university. About 6 million students study at the university. There are a total of 267 study centres in Pune, Satara and Solapur under the Pune Divisional Centre, with 160, 36 and 71 respectively, with a total intake of up to 1 lakh students. At present, the Pune Zonal Centre is being run from the Shaheer Annabhau Sathe School under the Pune Municipal Corporation, which has been given on lease by the Metropolitan Municipality on a temporary basis.
