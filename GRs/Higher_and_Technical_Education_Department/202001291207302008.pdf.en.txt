Introduction: The General Administration Department. Instructions have been given to create more number of posts for the officers/ employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribe category under the Government decision dated 21.12.2019, but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate or have submitted the other caste validity certificate, and to send a requisition to the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to fill the vacancies of the officers/ employees appointed to the post of the said Scheduled Tribe. Accordingly, a proposal has been made to create a supernumerary post for transfer of the services of Smt. Smita Sanjay Nagpure, Short Writer Lower Category (Marathi), Group-C on the establishment of the Library Directorate, due to non-submission of caste validity certificate of Scheduled Tribes. Submitted vide letter dated 27.12.2019.
Accordingly, the matter of creating a supernumerary post on the establishment of the library directorate is under the consideration of the government.
