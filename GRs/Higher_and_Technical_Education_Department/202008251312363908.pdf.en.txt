The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi has approved the implementation of Diploma in Engineering (D.Eng.) courses in Government Technical Colleges in the State recognized as Second Term Courses for the academic year 2019-20 and earlier as Regular Courses from the academic year 2020-21. In pursuance of this approval, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of Government approval under the aforesaid Reference No. 2. The government was contemplating issuing a notification in this regard.
The government's decision:
In Annexure A, along with the recognition of second-term courses for the academic year 2019-20 and earlier, the Government is approving the implementation of Diploma courses in Engineering in the second-term in Government Technical Colleges in the State as regular courses from the academic year 2020-21 subject to the following terms and conditions.
