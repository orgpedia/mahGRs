Appointment of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar as a member of the Publication Committee of Charitra Sadhan. ..
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Resolution, No: Parichay-1116/ PR No.91/ Aastha-2
Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai- 400032
Date is November 15, 2021
Reference:- Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. Decision, No: Complex-1111/ Q.No. 91/16/ Coordination, dated 30.03.2021& D. 22.06.2021 Proposal-
As per the Government decision dated 30.03.2021 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti has been reconstituted. Pradeep N. Agalawe has been appointed as Member Secretary. The government was considering the appointment of Dr. Chandrashekhar Kamble as a" member" in this committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Prof. Babasaheb Ambedkar as" Member" of Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti. The government is approving the appointment of Mr. Chandrashekhar Kamble. There will be no change in the Chairman/ Vice-Chairman/ Invitee/ Member and Member Secretary appointed on Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti as per the Government decision dated 30.03.2021 and 22.06.2021 of this Department.
