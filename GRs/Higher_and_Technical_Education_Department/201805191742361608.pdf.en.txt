GOVERNMENT Circular, General Administration Department No.CFR-1217/ Q.No. 131/13, dated 15th December, 2017
The government's decision:
The online performance appraisal reporting system, MahaPAR, is proposed to provide online reporting of performance of all Group A and Group B (Gazetted) cadre officers from 2017-18 onwards. Accordingly, the General Administration Department issued a circular. Under 15.12.2017, Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, who controls the concerned Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers for recording performance appraisal report in the Mahapar system, has been declared as" Coordinating Officer," as well as" Cadre Controlling Officer." Further, the Under Secretary/ Office Officer of the concerned office who controls the concerned service has been declared as" EMD Manager." Accordingly, Deputy Secretary Higher and Technical Education Department and Office Officer (Sashi-5) have been declared as" Coordinating Officer" and" Information Manager" respectively.
