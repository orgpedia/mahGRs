The Proposal: -
The recruitment rules for the posts of Divisional Joint Director (Higher Education) and Joint Director (Administration) at the establishment of the Directorate of Higher Education are likely to be finalised and it is likely to take some additional time for the candidates to be appointed by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission to this post. In view of this, the Departmental Joint Director (Higher Education) and 02 Joint Director (Administration), respectively, have been given additional charge. The Selection Committee was constituted by a Government decision dated 03.11.2022 prescribing the educational qualifications, experience, terms and conditions for appointment with additional charge. With the expiry of the tenure of this selection committee, the government was considering the formation of a new selection committee to give additional charge of the posts of Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education and Joint Director (Administration).
The Government's Decisions:
