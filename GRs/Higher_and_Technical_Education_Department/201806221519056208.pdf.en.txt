Introduction: Refer No. As per the approval of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) under the Government Decision No. 1-6, starting of new institutions of Diploma/ Degree/ Post Graduate Degree courses in Pharmacy from the academic year 2018-19, starting or reversing of Degree Pharmacy courses in the institution of existing Diploma Pharmacy courses, increasing the accessibility of the ongoing Degree/ Diploma Pharmacy courses, etc. While granting approval to this government, the condition of approval of Pharmacy Council of India has been prescribed while admitting students. Since the process of admission to undergraduate/ diploma courses in the state has started, it is not possible for the institutions to comply with this condition immediately. the Pharmacy Council
It is the experience of the Directorate of Technical Education that it takes a long time to get approval from the Council of India. So if the state does not relax this condition, the students

Medicine will be excluded from the curriculum. Therefore, considering the interest of education, the matter of amending this condition was under consideration of the government.
The government has taken the following decisions:
In the academic year 2018-19, the institutions whose Diploma/ Degree/ Post Graduate courses in Pharmacy are not recognized by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi, subject to the condition of obtaining approval of the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi within six months, and in case of any court case arising out of the absence of such approval, the concerned institutions will be fully responsible for the matter.
(2) This condition shall also apply to institutions which have been recognized by the Government up to the previous academic year and have not yet received the approval of the Pharmacy Council of India. This will exclude institutions that have been given specific orders by the court.
(3) If the institution does not obtain the approval of the Pharmacy Council of India within the prescribed time, admission to the institution for the next academic year shall not be granted. "
The Director of Technical Education should take necessary action in this regard immediately.
