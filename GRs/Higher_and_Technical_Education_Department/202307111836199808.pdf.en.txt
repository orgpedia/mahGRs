Terms and Conditions- 1. The approval shall be on a perpetual basis without subsidy.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.
The intake capacity of the institution for the said course shall be limited as mentioned against their names in the prospectus.
4. The institution shall have to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
The concerned institution must be affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education for these diploma courses.
In this regard, it shall be mandatory for the concerned institution to fulfill all the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi from time to time and guidelines prescribed by the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai from time to time.
The fees for the courses will be fixed by the Fee Regulatory Authority, Maharashtra State, Mumbai/ Authority as directed by the Government from time to time.
8. The institution shall take action as per rules for obtaining approval of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for extension of these courses.

The Principals of the newly approved Diploma Courses in Engineering will be required to obtain the approval of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi before admitting the students. In the absence of this approval, the concerned institutions will be fully responsible in case some court cases arise in the future. The undertaking has to be submitted by the head of the institution concerned to the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
Only after this, the directorate has to give consent to these institutions to start the course.
10.The concerned institution shall complete the other legal matters by depositing the requisite processing fee and accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
11.Sadar Admission to Professional Diploma Courses will be done as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the respective courses approved by the Government.
12.If the fees charged by the institutions concerned are more than the prescribed tuition fees by the School Regulatory Authority/ Authority specified by the Government from time to time, action will be taken against those institutions as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
13.The Institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per Government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
14.The library in the institution is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students. Also, the students are provided with all the educational facilities as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). laboratories, libraries, classrooms, etc.
15.Sadar Institute shall be under the control of Director, Technical Education and the examinations for the respective courses shall be conducted by the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Bandra, Mumbai.
16.R. In respect of Ragging. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999, need to be strictly implemented.
The institutions concerned will be responsible for any situation arising in the future due to change in the course/ discontinuation of the course. Such a guarantee has to be given by the concerned agencies.
Other terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai and the University concerned shall continue to apply.
Institutions which have not paid the prescribed registration fee to the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, shall recover the same before final approval by the Director. The Director, Technical Education should expedite the process of inclusion of new courses/ increased intake capacity in the admission process for the academic year 2023-24 as per this Government approval.
