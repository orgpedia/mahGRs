Preamble: The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide its letter No. 1 to 2 has approved the change in the pattern of Professional Engineering Diploma Course (conversion from VT to I) in the State from the academic year 2019-20. In pursuance of this approval, the Director, Technical Education has submitted his proposal to the Government vide letter No. 3. In pursuance of the proposal, the Government was considering to approve the change in the pattern of Professional Engineering Diploma Course (conversion from first shift to second shift) from the academic year 2019-2020.
The government's decision:
As per the approval of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi from the academic year 2019-20 onwards, the following Form-A is being approved by the Government for change in the pattern of Professional Engineering Diploma Courses (conversion from first shift to second shift).
