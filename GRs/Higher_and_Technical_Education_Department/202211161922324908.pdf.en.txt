Preamble: Restrictions have been imposed on the creation and appointment of new posts till the final approval of the modalities as per the Government Resolution, dated 25th May, 2017, in the Finance Division, Reference No. 1. Further, with regard to the matters to be considered for fixing the revised figure, vide Reference No. 3 Guidelines have been issued by the government circular here.
Accordingly, the process of revising the figure of teaching posts in the agricultural university under the control of the department is underway. As the revised pattern is yet to be finalised and there is a restriction on fresh recruitment since 2017, vacancies arising out of retirement and other reasons could not be filled up during this period. Considering the academic interest of the students, the reference No. 2 of the Subcommittee constituted by the Government here. In the meeting held on 16.07.2019, the Sub-Committee had approved filling up of posts in 15 non-agriculture and deemed universities under the Department within the limit of 80% of the sanctioned posts. As per the approval of the Sub-Committee, No. The government had approved the filling up of 659 vacant posts of teachers and teacher equivalents in non-agriculture universities and deemed universities in the state under the department. However, the Department of Finance, under reference No. 5 The Government hereby imposed restrictions on recruitment as part of the economic measures in the wake of the outbreak of COVID-19. As a result, the posts of teacher and assistant teacher could not be filled. The proposal to approve the filling of these posts was again submitted to the Finance Department. Accordingly

A sub-committee was formed by the Finance Department to approve the recruitment. The proposal was placed for approval in the meeting dated 18.10.2022. The sub-committee has approved the filling up of the above 659 posts. Accordingly, the government issued the order.
Decision: The government. As on 07.08.2019, a total of 659 posts have been sanctioned for 15 Non-Agriculture Universities under the Department and Government recognized Deemed Universities, with a ceiling of 80% of the total sanctioned posts of teachers and teacher equivalents. The university-wise details of the posts approved for recruitment are shown in Annexure-1 to the said Government Resolution. Accordingly, the government has decided to fill up 659 vacancies. Approval is being granted to complete the same as per the conditions mentioned in 07.08.2019.
