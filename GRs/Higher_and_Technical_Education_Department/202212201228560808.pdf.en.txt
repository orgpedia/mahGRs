The post of Joint Director in the pay scale of 7th Pay Commission in the office of State Project Director, Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA), Mumbai, under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, is being demoted to the pay scale of S-20: 56100-177500 of the 7th Pay Commission and Mr. Rahul Mhatre, Block Development Officer (Class-I) is being appointed on deputation to the said pay scale of S-20: 56100-177500 for a period of three years subject to the following conditions.
(1) Mr. Rahul Mhatre, Block Development Officer (Class-I) shall commence his deputation service on the date on which he enters upon the office of deputation and shall cease to hold office on the date on which he resumes charge of his original Government post.
(2) The Government/ competent authority shall have the right to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation, if their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service.
(3) If their services on deputation are not found necessary by the non-recruiting employer, the non-recruiting employer shall be at liberty to send them back to the parent department. However, before such repatriation, the non-recipient employer shall give a notice of three months to the Government/ competent authority.
