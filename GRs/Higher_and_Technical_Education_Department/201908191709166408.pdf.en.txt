Introduction: Periodic transfers of teachers in Government Engineering Colleges in the State were made in 2018 under the relevant orders as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005. Some of the teachers who were transferred from the College of Engineering, Pune, under this transfer order. High Court, Mumbai and subsequently Special Leave Petition No. 13751-13752/2019 through Smt. It was challenged in the Supreme Court. In this case, The Supreme Court In pursuance of the Judgement dated 09.07.2019, the Government was contemplating to take an appropriate decision to stay the transfer orders of the teachers who were transferred from College of Engineering, Pune to other Government Engineering Colleges under the relevant orders and who were the petitioners in the said court case.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: On the basis of the representations received from the teachers who were transferred from College of Engineering, Pune to other Government Engineering Colleges under the orders in question and who are the petitioners in the said court case, the transfer of the following women teachers to Pillar-3 is stayed till further orders.
