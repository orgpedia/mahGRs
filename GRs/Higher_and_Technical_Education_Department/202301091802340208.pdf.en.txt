Preamble: Reference No. 1 As per the decision of the Government here, the Reporting Officer, Reviewing Officer, Revision Officer have been prescribed to report and review the confidential reports of the teachers and non-teaching staff in the Directorate of Higher Education, Divisional Joint Director's Offices, Government Colleges/ Institutions under this Department. Reference No. 2 As per the Government notification, Dr. Homi Bhabha State University, Mumbai has been constituted as a group university with Government Institute of Science, Mumbai as the main college and constituent colleges of Elphinstone College, Mumbai, Sydenham College, Mumbai and Government Teachers College, Mumbai. Reference No. The plea has sought directions from the government to send the self-assessment report of the teaching and non-teaching staff under Homi Bhabha State University to the vice-chancellor, Dr Homi Bhabha State University, Mumbai, for review. Accordingly, the matter of determining the Report, Review and Versioning Officer for reporting, reviewing and processing the confidential reports of the teachers and non-teaching staff of the main and constituent colleges of Dr. Homi Bhabha State University was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In view of the Government Notification and Letter No. 2 and 3, the following officers are being approved as Reporting Officers, Reviewing Officers and Editing Officers for the purpose of reporting, reviewing and editing the confidential reports of the teaching and non-teaching staff of the main and constituent colleges of Dr. Homi Bhabha State University:
