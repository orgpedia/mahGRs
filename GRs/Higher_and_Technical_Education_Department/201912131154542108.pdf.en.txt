(c) Senior and junior employees should be of the same cadre and there should be a provision for appointment by promotion and nomination in that cadre as per the rules of induction into service.

(c) No demand for enhancement of pay shall be made for the promoted employees of the cadre in which there is no provision for appointment by nomination.
The salary of the junior employee must be determined in accordance with the provisions prescribed in the Maharashtra Non-Agricultural Universities and Affiliated Colleges Standard Code (Revised Salary of Non-Teaching Staff) Rules, 2009.
f) Senior employees cannot demand a pay hike if the junior employee has been granted an advance pay hike at the time of joining the service.
(g) Senior employees promoted on ad-hoc or ad-hoc basis shall not be entitled to demand a salary hike.
