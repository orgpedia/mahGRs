Government Circular No.: SRIKH- 2017/ PR No. 315/17/ VISHI- 1
It has been observed that the non-agriculture universities under the control of the Department of Higher and Technical Education, the Finance Department and the General Administration Department are not complying with the orders issued from time to time. In view of the above, it is advised that all the non-agricultural universities should strictly follow the Government decisions and orders issued by the Government in letter and spirit as well as the orders issued from time to time. Disciplinary action will be taken against the concerned university as well as the concerned officer/ authority if it is found that the relevant government decisions and letters as well as the orders issued in this regard from time to time have not been complied with.
