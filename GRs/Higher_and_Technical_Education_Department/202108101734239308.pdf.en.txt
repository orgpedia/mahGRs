(a) The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1. Review the policy to be appointed on the prevailing hourly basis and check whether there are any errors in it or how.
(ii) to examine whether the educational qualifications, qualifications, experience and conditions required for appointment on hourly basis are as prescribed by the University Grants Commission and the State Government from time to time or how etc.
3. Review the problems faced in appointment on hourly basis as per the prevailing policy and recommend how they can be resolved.
4. To examine whether or not any alternative policy can be prescribed for appointment on hourly basis.
