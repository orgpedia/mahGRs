Preamble: As per the list provided by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi to Directorate of Technical Education vide e-mail dated 14/06/2018 and 20/06/2018 regarding approval for increase/ decrease in intake capacity of professional courses from the academic year 2018-19, the Director, Technical Education has proposed to the Government to approve closure of courses and increase/ decrease in intake capacity of the existing courses in the Degree, Post Graduate Institute of Engineering, Pharmacy, HMTCT, Management courses under the aforesaid reference letter.
In pursuance of the list made available by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide e-mail dated 14/06/2018 and 20/06/2018, the Director, Technical Education, as proposed under the aforesaid letter, was under consideration of the Government to grant approval for restoration of accessibility and reduction of accessibility of the existing courses as indicated against the name of the institution in Annexure A and Annexure B respectively from the academic year 2018-19.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, as proposed by the Director, Technical Education vide the aforesaid Reference letter, the Government is pleased to grant approval for restoration of accessibility of existing courses and reduction in accessibility of existing courses as indicated against the name of the institution in Annexure A and Annexure B for the academic year 2018-19 subject to the following terms and conditions:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: TEM-2018/ PR No.210/ TANSI-4
1.The terms and conditions prescribed for these courses in the earlier government approval will remain.
The institution will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the concerned university.
The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters which are sub judice in respect of service and other matters of the faculty/ staff appointed for the concerned course.
In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.
4. It shall be incumbent upon the head of the institution to pay the arrears, if any, of the government's collection from the institution.
It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay the loans taken by it from the Government or any financial institution in respect of the immovable property required for the conduct of the said course in the Institute.
After the closure of the relevant courses in the institution, it will be obligatory on the institution to make alternative arrangements for the admitted students and former students of the institution if they want to sit for the examination.
The institution shall be bound to pay the salary, remuneration and other financial payments due to the faculty and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
