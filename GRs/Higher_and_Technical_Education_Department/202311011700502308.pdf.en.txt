the order
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 86 (1) of the Lakshminarayan Innovative Technology (LIT) University, Nagpur Act, 2023, the Government of Maharashtra hereby appoints Dr. Raju Mankar, Director, Laxminarayan Institute of Technology, Nagpur as the first Vice-Chancellor of Lakshminarayan Innovative Technology (LIT) University, Nagpur.
The appointment of Shri Raju Mankar as Vice-Chancellor is being made for a period of one year from the date he assumes charge of the office of the first Vice-Chancellor of the University or until the regular appointment of the Vice-Chancellor by the Chancellor whichever is earlier.
Separate orders will be issued regarding salary, allowances and other service conditions for the post of Vice-Chancellor, Laxmi Narayan Innovative Technology (LIT) University, Nagpur.
