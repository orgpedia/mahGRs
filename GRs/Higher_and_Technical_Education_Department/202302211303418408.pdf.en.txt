The proposal: The Cabinet As per the decision taken in the meeting dated 08.12.2021, Maharashtra National Law University, Nagpur has been given the approval for construction of new residential buildings at Mouza Waranga, G.Nagpur vide reference No. 2 here. As per the Government Decision dated 17.12.2021, Rs. Administrative Sanction has been accorded for an amount of Rs. 9515.97 lakhs.
In accordance with the above administrative approval, these constructions have been sanctioned vide reference No. A total of Rs 3. A total of Rs 78.36 crore has been provided to the university. Now, the University has decided to provide funds for the construction of the remaining buildings such as 1 BHK staff quarters, residential buildings and infrastructure. 4 have been requested. In pursuance of the said request, funds have been budgeted for this work through Supplementary Demand. The Finance Department has agreed to disburse the budgeted funds. In pursuance of the said approval, the Government was considering the issue of release of funds for construction of the remaining buildings in the permanent campus of Maharashtra National Law University, Nagpur.
Government Decision:- Maharashtra National Law University, Nagpur has decided to construct a building in its permanent campus at Mouza Waranga. As per the administrative approval under the Government decision dated 17.12.2021, for the construction of the remaining building, Rs. 10,79,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores Eighty Nine Lakhs only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
The Executive Council shall be responsible for administering the grants sanctioned by the Government as per the Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014 by depositing them in the University Fund. Also, the university should take action to approve the expenditure incurred from the grant by exchanging the statement of accounts of the expenditure before the General Council.
The Vice-Chancellor shall be responsible to see that the grant is spent for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned by the Government to the Maharashtra National Law University, Nagpur. In this regard, they should submit its utility certificate to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education with the approval of the Executive Council.
