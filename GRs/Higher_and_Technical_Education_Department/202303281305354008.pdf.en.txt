Government Decision: Under the Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme, the amount of hostel maintenance allowance for the academic year 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23 for the eligible students has been increased from Rs. 4.00 crore and Rs. 15.94 crore. The Director of Technical Education has proposed to disburse funds to the tune of Rs. 19.94 crore. Submitted vide letter dated 09/03/2023. The government was planning to allocate funds for the purpose.
