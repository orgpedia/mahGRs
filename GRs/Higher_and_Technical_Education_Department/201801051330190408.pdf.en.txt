Regarding transfer of the subject" Matters/ Matters relating to Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited" to General Administration Department.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Resolution No: MKCL-2016/ (249/16)/ Tanshi-5
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai-400032.
Date: January 5, 2018.
Reference:- Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government Resolution No. Matantra 3700/ (132/2000)/ Part-D/ Tanshi-3, dated: 6th June, 2001
Preamble: In pursuance of the aforesaid Government Resolution dated 6/6/2001, the Government have accorded approval for the formation of a company named" Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited" under the Companies Act, 1956. Accordingly, the cases related to this company are being handled by this department. The matter of transfer of cases/ matters related to Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited to General Administration Department was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has decided to transfer the subject" Matters/ Matters relating to Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited" to the General Administration Department. Accordingly, all the cases/ matters relating to the said company shall henceforth be dealt with by the General Administration Department. Also, the subject should be kept under the purview of the Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department.
(2) The detailed supplementary orders required to be issued in accordance with this Government Order shall be issued by the General Administration Department in accordance with the Government Order dated 6th June, 2001.
