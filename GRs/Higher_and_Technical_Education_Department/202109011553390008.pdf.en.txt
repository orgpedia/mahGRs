The proposal:
The Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi has approved the change in the name of the institution of the existing Diploma in Pharmacy course from the academic year 2021-22. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to change the name of the institution of the existing Diploma course in Pharmacy under the above reference letter. The proposal had been approved by the government.
The government's decision:
As per the approval given by Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi from the academic year 2021-22, the Government is approving change in the name of the institution of Diploma course in Pharmacy as mentioned in the" Form-A" below.
