Preamble: As per the provisions of Section 51 to 53 of the Maharashtra University Act, 1994, Reference No. 3 Under the decision of the Government here, a committee consisting of experienced and expert persons has been reconstituted under the chairmanship of the Director, Higher Education, Pune to examine/ scrutinize the statutes/ ordinances received from all non-agricultural universities in the state before approving them and recommend them to the Government.
However, the state. The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 has come into force with effect from January 11, 2017 by repealing the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994. Accordingly, the post of Director of the University, College and University Development Board no longer exists.
Besides all non-agricultural universities in the state, the government was considering giving approval to Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik, and Kavikulaguru Kalidas Sanskrit University, Ramtek, to submit proposals to the committee constituted to examine/ scrutinize and recommend to the government before approving the statutes/ ordinances. Accordingly, the Government is now taking the following decisions by superseding the Government Decision in Reference No. 3.
Government Decision: As per section 72 and 74 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act 2016, a committee consisting of the following experienced and expert persons is being reconstituted under the chairmanship of the Director, Higher Education, Pune to examine/ scrutinize the statutes and ordinances received from all non-agricultural universities of the state as well as Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik and Kavikulaguru Kalidas Sanskrit University, Ramtek before approving them and making recommendations to the government.

Keeping in view the number and priority of references received by the committee, the committee should hold meetings and give feedback to the government on the basis of statutes, ordinances, orders and government decisions as soon as possible.
The Committee shall hold regular meetings. The meeting of the committee should be held by the Director, Higher Education, Pune at the university at his level and the university should pay the necessary honorarium to the members for this meeting and the travel allowance to the non-official members should be paid from the directorate level.
