Preamble: Civil Service Boards have been constituted to make recommendations to the competent authority regarding the posting and transfer of officers/ employees belonging to Group-A, Group-B and Group-C, as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 and 2. Accordingly, Civil Service Boards have been constituted to recommend to the competent authority for the posting and transfer of officers/ staff belonging to Group-A, Group-B and Group-C in the Directorate of Higher Education, Directorate of Technical Education, Directorate of Arts, Directorate of Library and Vocational Education under the control of the Department of Higher and Technical Education and the offices of Divisional Joint Director under them, Government Colleges and Government Institutions. The Directorate of Vocational Education and Training under the control of the Department of Higher and Technical Education has been transferred to the Department of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship as per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 4. Accordingly, the matter of reforming the Civil Service Boards formed under the Government Decision at Reference No. 3 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The following revised Civil Service Boards are being constituted to recommend to the competent authorities for the posting and transfer of all the existing Government Decisions relating to the posting and transfer of Group A, Group B and Group C officers/ employees of the Directorate of Higher Education, Directorate of Technical Education, Directorate of Arts and Library under the control of the Department of Higher and Technical Education and the offices of Divisional Joint Directors under them, Government Colleges and Government Institutions:
