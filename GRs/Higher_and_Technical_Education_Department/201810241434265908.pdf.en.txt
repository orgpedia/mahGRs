Preamble: Under this scheme, 20 students from the open category students of the state will be admitted to foreign educational institutions within the QS and THE World Ranking 200 for post-graduate and PhD courses. Under the Government decision dated 04.10.2018, a new scheme" Grant of Scholarship to Meritorious Students for Higher Education Abroad" has been launched. The following decisions are being taken regarding the opening of the head of accounts to deal with the budgetary aspects of the scheme.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: To make budgetary provision for the scheme" Grant of Scholarship to meritorious students for higher studies abroad," approval is being accorded for opening of new account titles as under:
