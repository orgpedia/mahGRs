Preamble: The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide its letter No. 1 to 4 above has approved the change in the name/ address of the institutions offering Diploma courses in Pharmacy/ Engineering from the academic year 2019-20. In pursuance of this approval, the Director, Technical Education has submitted his proposal to the Government vide letter No. 5. In pursuance of the said proposal, the Government was contemplating to approve change in name/ address of the existing institutions of Pharmacy/ Diploma courses of Engineering from the academic year 2019-20.
The government's decision:
As per the approval of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi from the academic year 2019-20, following Form-A approved institutions are being given approval by the Government to change the name of the existing institutions of Diploma in Pharmacy/ Engineering courses and change the address of the institution of Diploma in Engineering courses named in Form-B.
