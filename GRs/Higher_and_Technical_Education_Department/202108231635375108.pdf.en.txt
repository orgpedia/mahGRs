The proposal:
Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar is known in the history of the country as a good administrator, valiant fighter, justice-loving person, as well as a people's mother who did innumerable public welfare works. Solapur University has been named after this great woman.
At present, the university. In view of the demand from the people's representatives, local social organizations and various sections of the district, the university has requested to form a committee of the following persons in connection with the construction of a memorial for Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar in the premises of Solapur University in joint participation with the university and the government. A committee of the following persons is proposed to be appointed.
The government's decision:
A committee is being constituted to decide on the plan and design of the memorial for the memorial of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar.
