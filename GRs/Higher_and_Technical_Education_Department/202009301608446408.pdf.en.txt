(2) The period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall not be admissible to the junior superiors transferred on request.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The following instructions shall be strictly complied with by the Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution so that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
(2) On receipt of the transfer order, the Principal/ Director concerned shall relieve the transferred Junior Resident immediately to join the place of transfer without waiting for the replacement of the transferred Junior Resident.
Also, the concerned Junior Advocates should immediately join the college/ institution where they have been transferred from the existing college/ institution.

3. The transferred Junior Resident should be reinstated without any delay after being relieved from the concerned college/ institution. The Principal/ Director of the college/ institution concerned shall be held responsible in case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of a junior post.
4.No request for change in post will be entertained.
Any attempt by a junior advocate to directly or indirectly press for modification of the transfer order shall entitle the junior advocate concerned to take disciplinary action.
5. No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
Since the transfers are required to be effected with immediate effect, the salary of the transferred Junior Advocates up to the date of superannuation or upto the maximum date i.e. 15th October, 2020 shall be drawn by the college/ institution where they are presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the college/ institution before transfer under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the college/ institution after the transferor joins the college/ institution of the junior domicile concerned. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from October 16, 2020 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the college/ institution where they were working before the transfer.
The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be personally responsible for the immediate release of the transferred junior superiors. The Director of Higher Education, Pune should submit the report to the Government before 16th October, 2020.
