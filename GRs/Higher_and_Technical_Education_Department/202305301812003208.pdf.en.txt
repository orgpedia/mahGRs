Government Order No: Transfer-2023/ No.68/ Masi-1
2. The concerned Head of Office shall relieve the transferred officers immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned officers should join the office immediately after their transfer from the existing office.
3. The Head of the concerned office shall make the transferred officers join without any delay after they are relieved from the concerned office and come to join. The head of the concerned office will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances without working as a mandatory waiting period due to non-recruitment of the officer who came to join the office. Also, no leave of absence will be granted during the period from the date of discharge to the date of joining the place of transfer.
The concerned head of the office shall take action to relieve the officers transferred by this order by June 15, 2023. Also, it will be the responsibility of the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officers to ensure that the salary of the transferred officers from July 1, 2023, will not be withdrawn from the office before the transfer.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune shall submit an executive report to the Government by 25th June, 2023.
