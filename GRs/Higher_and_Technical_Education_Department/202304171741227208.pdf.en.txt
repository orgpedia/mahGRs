Introduction The Engineering/ Architecture/ Pharmacy/ Hotel Management and Catering Technology/ Management Colleges/ Technicians under the Directorate of Technical Education are required to invite experts from various fields in these institutions and avail their services for teaching/ guidance from time to time. At present, it is becoming difficult to maintain the quality of the course by attracting experienced, expert dignitaries from these fields on a low remuneration basis. Accordingly, the matter of revision of remuneration of visiting faculty to be invited for courses under the Directorate of Technical Education was under consideration of the Government.
Government decisions are being taken in the context of government decisions.
2. Approval is being given to apply the revised rates as indicated below for the remuneration to be paid to the visiting faculty invited for various courses in the institutions under the Directorate of Technical Education under the Department of Higher and Technical Education.

(1) The Principal/ Director of the institution concerned shall ensure that all the working teachers of the concerned faculty in the institution are given academic load as per the norms before inviting the visiting faculty.
(2) The retired faculty or persons having experience in the industry/ profession associated with the course should be invited to teach on hourly basis. However, the visiting faculty shall be required to possess minimum educational qualification and experience as prescribed under the provisions of the Service Admission Rules.

(3) Appointment of teachers on hourly basis may be made on temporary basis for a maximum period of 9 months for an academic year. After the expiration of this period, the contract will automatically terminate.
(4) It shall be the responsibility of the Principal of the concerned college/ technical college to ensure that no candidate on hourly basis is required to pay more than the amount of the starting salary in the permissible pay scale of his rank in a single month.
(5) No additional funds shall be provided for the expenditure incurred for the purpose. Therefore, the expenditure to be incurred will have to be met from the funds available with the Institute/ from the grants sanctioned.
(6) Appointment on an hourly basis is a temporary appointment and does not entitle any employee to regular service. Therefore," in the future will not demand continuation in the service. Also, I will not demand any rights of regular service. The guarantee should be taken on a stamp paper of 100/-.
(7) If any irregularity is found in the actual workload and payment of honorarium on hourly basis, the principal/ director of the concerned college will be fully responsible for it.
The revised rates of remuneration of visiting faculty will be effective from 01.04.2023.
