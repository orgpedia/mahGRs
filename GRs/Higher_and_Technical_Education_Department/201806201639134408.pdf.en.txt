10) The institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
The library of the institute is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, the library is fully accessible to students.
