Introduction: Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar is known in the history of the country as a good administrator, valiant fighter, justice-loving person, as well as a people's mother who did innumerable public welfare works. Solapur University has been named after this great woman.
At present, the university. The construction of the main administrative building and the Pariksha Bhavan building in the North Solapur area is underway and there is a demand from the public representatives, local social organizations and various sections of the district to build a memorial for Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar in this building. The Committee was constituted under the Government Resolution dated 23.08.2021. Give it now. The matter of reconstituting the Committee of the following persons by cancelling the appointments of non-official members in the Government decision dated 23.08.2021 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision:- 1) Regarding the construction of a memorial for Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar, Dy. The appointments of non-official members of the committee constituted under the Government decision dated 23.08.2021 are being cancelled under this Government decision.
2. Approval is being given to reconstitute the committee for planning, design and design of memorial for the memorial of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar as follows:
