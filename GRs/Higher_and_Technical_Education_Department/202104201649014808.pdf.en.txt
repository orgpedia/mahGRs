Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has recommended 10 candidates for the post of Assistant Director (Technical), Directorate of Technical Education/ Deputy Secretary (Technical), Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education.
Accordingly, the orders have been issued in this regard. In paragraph 05 of the said Order, the Assistant Director (Technical Education)/ Deputy Secretary (Technical Education) of the post of Assistant Director (Technical Education), Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Directorate of Technical Education of the teachers of the teaching cadre in the Government Technicians working on deputation/ ad-hoc arrangement in the concerned office shall automatically cease to be in charge of the post from the date on which the above candidates appear at the place of their appointment and shall be relieved. Deputy Secretary (s). The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE) has released the list of teachers who have been transferred/ deputed to the post. Now the matter of posting the said teachers to the vacant post keeping in view the urgency of their parent cadre/ administrative cadre was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT ORDER: With the approval of the competent authority for transfer of Maharashtra Government employees, the following Head of Department, Government Technician and similar institutions in the Maharashtra Technician Teacher Service Group-A service under the Directorate of Technical Education are being transferred and posted as per Pillar-4.

(2) On receipt of the order of posting in the new institution/ office, the Head of the Department concerned should immediately join the institution of the next posting.
3) The concerned Principal/ Head of the institution should make the newly posted Head of the Department join without any delay after he comes to join. 4) No leave of the Head of the Department posted in the new institution/ office should be sanctioned without prior approval of the Administrative Department.
(5) The Director, Directorate of Technical Education shall submit the compliance report to this Department within 15 days from the date of this order without fail.
