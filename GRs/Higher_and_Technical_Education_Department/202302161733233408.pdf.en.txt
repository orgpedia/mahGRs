In pursuance of the said approval, the Government is granting approval for complete closure of the Institute of Diploma Course named in the above form from the academic year 2022-23 subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The institute will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in the institute such as marksheets, answer sheets, result sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education.
(2) The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters which are sub judice in respect of service and other matters of the faculty/ staff appointed for the concerned course. In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.

3) If there are any pending dues of the government from the institution, it will be mandatory for the head of the institution to pay it.
(4) It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay the loans taken by it from the Government or any other financial institution in respect of the immovable property required for running the course in the Institute.
5) After the closure of the relevant courses in the institution, it will be mandatory for the institution to make alternative arrangements for the admitted students and alumni of the institution if they wish to sit for the examination.
(6) The Institute shall be bound to pay the salaries, remuneration and other financial payments due to the teaching and non-teaching staff of the Institute appointed for the respective courses as per rules.
The entire responsibility of this is of the organisation.
