Three separate committees- Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti- have been formed to publish the literature of great personalities like Jotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu. The three committees were referred to. According to the government's decision. The period has been extended by 3 years with effect from 1.11.2018. Reference No. Under Section 6, the matter of cancellation of the appointments of non-official members/ member secretaries appointed to the committees of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: 6 The appointments of the following non-official members and member secretaries to the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti, which were constituted/ reconstituted under the Government decision, have been cancelled under this Government decision.
