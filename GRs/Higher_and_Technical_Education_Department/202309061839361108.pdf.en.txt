The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi has approved the setting up of a new institute with three diploma engineering courses.
The institute has been established to impart training to the personnel of the Indian Army. The institute has prepared various courses for the Indian Army. The Institute has been awarded three Diploma Courses by the All India Council for Technical Education vide Letter No. 2 has been approved. Since the institute is under the control of the central government, it will not get any grant from the state government. As the students are serving in the Indian Army, admission to these courses is done at the institute level by the institute. The college is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune. However, since the newly received Diploma Engineering courses need to be approved by the State Government as per the University Rules for obtaining affiliation of Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has decided to grant affiliation to the University. The proposal has been submitted vide letter dated 14.08.2023.
