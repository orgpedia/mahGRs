Introduction: - Department of Higher and Technical Education. The Department of Finance has directed to propose separate head of accounts for budgetary provision for free vocational education for girls in line with the Government Decision dated 8th July, 2024 to regulate expenditure on free vocational education for girls separately. In pursuance of this direction, the Government was contemplating opening a new separate title of account for the implementation of "Free Vocational Education Scheme-Technical Education (Programme) for Girls" under the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme.
Government Decision: - Ma. Office of the Accountant General, Mumbai, Department of Planning and Department of Finance
