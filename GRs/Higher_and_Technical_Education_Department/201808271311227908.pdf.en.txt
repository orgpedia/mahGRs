Introduction The system of giving awards under the National Service Scheme was started in 1993-94 from the silver jubilee year of the state level based on the central government system. Accordingly, proposals were invited from the universities and the decision was taken on the recommendation of the State Level Award Selection Committee.
Government Decision: The State Government has decided to give State Level Awards as mentioned below to those who have served the society selflessly and loyally in the year 2017-18 under the National Service Scheme, to encourage them in their committed work and to give due recognition to their selfless service.
