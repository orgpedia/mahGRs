As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Libraries (Grant-in-Aid and Grant-in-Aid for Building & Equipment Grants) Rules, 1970, the maintenance grant to government recognised public libraries is generally given in two instalments per annum, 90 per cent of the expenditure incurred on approved matters out of the expenditure incurred by them in the previous year or the ceiling of the grant permissible for the quality / class of the library, whichever is lower. This requires the library to annually submit an annual report and an audited statement and balance sheet of the previous year's savings and expenses certified by a chartered accountant. Accordingly, the Directorate of Libraries vide the said letter had requested for disbursement of funds for the first instalment of grant to the Government recognized public libraries.
