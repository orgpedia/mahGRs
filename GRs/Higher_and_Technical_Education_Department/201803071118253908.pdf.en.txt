Introduction: The courses have been started in various Government approved institutions (NON-AICTE) which are not under the purview of All India Council for Technical Education but under the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education. However, the institutions have requested the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai, to close the courses being run by these institutions and transfer the fixed deposits deposited by the institutions to the accounts of the institution. Accordingly, the Board has proposed to close the courses run by the said institutions (NON-AICTE) and transfer the fixed deposits deposited by the institutions to the accounts of the institution.
Government Decision: The Government has approved the closure of courses in the following listed institutions which are not under the jurisdiction of All India Council for Technical Education but under the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education subject to the following terms and conditions.
