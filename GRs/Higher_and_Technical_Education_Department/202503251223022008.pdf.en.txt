The Proposal: -
Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune vide letter dated 01.01.2016, stated that the hostel premises of Government College of Teachers, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar have been overpopulated due to the increase in grasses. As a result, children's lives are at risk. To avoid this, paver blocks must be installed in the hostel premises. It has also been suggested to repair the broken windows of the building, rain-hosting the building to increase the water level of the borewell, etc. The Principal, MAHA Faculty College, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar has proposed various renovation and renovation works in the college.
Expenditure budgets from the Executive Engineer, Aesth Construction Department, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar have been submitted for the work and the technical consent for the proposed work has been given by the Chief Engineer, Public Works, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar. Accordingly, the proposal was under consideration of the Government for approval.
The Government's Decisions:
The Government College of Teachers, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, for building repair and renovation works, as shown in the following statement, has a total outlay of Rs. 42,18,026 / - (Rs. The expenditure budget of forty-two lakh eighteen thousand twenty-seven rupees) is being approved by the Administrator subject to the following conditions.
