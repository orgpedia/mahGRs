Preamble: In view of the outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic in the State, in order to safeguard the health of the students, a proposal was submitted to the Government under the above mentioned letter (4) of the Directorate of Arts that Government Drawing (Elementary and Intermediate Drawing Grade) Examination, 2020 should not be conducted. Accordingly, in view of the outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic in the State and considering the health and safety of the students, the Government has decided not to conduct the Government Examinations, 2020.

Accordingly, it was necessary to issue a revised Government Decision by superseding the Government Decision dated March 26, 2021 regarding the conduct of Government Drawing Examination (Elementary and Intermediate Drawing Grade), 2020 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education so that the students who had passed the Intermediate Drawing Grade Examination earlier could be given concessional marks as per the prevailing policy.
Further, as per the Government Order dated 31st August, 2007 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education, a student admitted to the courses of Diploma in Art Education Training and Basic Course in Visual Art is required to pass the Intermediate Drawing Grade Examination in the academic year in which he/ she is admitted to the course. However, since it would not be possible to conduct the Government Drawing Examination-2020 as mentioned above, for the academic year 2020-21, the students admitted to Diploma in Art Education Training and Basic Courses were required to be exempted from the condition of passing the Intermediate Drawing Grade Examination.
In view of the above, it was under consideration of the Government to issue a revised Government Decision by superseding the Government Decision dated March 26, 2021 regarding the conduct of Government Drawing Art (Elementary and Intermediate Drawing Grade) Examination, 2020.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. The Government Decision dated 26th March, 2021 in the aforesaid Reference No.6 regarding the conduct of Government Drawing Art (Elementary and Intermediate Drawing Grade) Examination, 2020 is being superseded.
2) Due to the outbreak of the global epidemic of COVID-19 in the state, the government should not conduct the Government Drawing Exam, 2020 considering the health and safety of the students.
3) Students who have previously passed the Elementary and Intermediate Drawing Grade Examinations will be awarded the Certificate of Secondary School Certificate (ECS) in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision of the Department of School Education and Sports dated November 24, 2017, and the Government Certificate dated December 20, 2017. 10th) Additional marks can be given in the exam.
Since the Government Drawing Exam 2020 will not be held in the academic year 2020-21, two courses namely Art Education Training Diploma (ATD) and Basic Course (Foundation) are being exempted from the condition of passing the Intermediate Drawing Grade Examination for the students admitted for the academic year 2020-21.
The present relaxation will be limited to the students who have taken admission in two courses namely Diploma in Art Education Training (ATD) and Basic Course (Foundation) in the academic year 2020-21 and for the academic year 2020-21 only.
