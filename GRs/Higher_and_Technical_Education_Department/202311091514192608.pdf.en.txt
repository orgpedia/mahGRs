The Gondwana University was established in 2011 with the objective of improving the quality of education, human development pathways and critical skills for inclusive development of remote and tribal communities in Gadchiroli and Chandrapur districts. Proposal for grant of approval to start Tribal Chair Centre in the said University. 1 letter was received. The policy regarding the establishment of the Chair in various non-agricultural universities in Maharashtra has been prepared in Reference No. 2 here. This was decided at the Cabinet meeting. In pursuance of the said policy and proposal, a Tribal Chair Centre at Gondwana University, Gadchiroli is proposed to be established vide Reference No. 3 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. Now, as the Finance Department has approved to provide funds to the Tribal Adhyasan Kendra, the matter of disbursing funds to the Tribal Adhyasan Kendra at Gondwana University, Gadchiroli was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: In order to promote tribal culture, art, language, folk music, conduct research based on anthropology and history of the region and to develop the potential and community-based skills of tribal youth, the Tribal Chair Centre established at Gondwana University, Gadchiroli has been sanctioned Rs. 3,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores only) is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions: 1. No new post can be created for the said Centre.
The subsidy received from the government will be invested in the fixed deposits of the nationalized bank and the interest earned from this amount will be used for the activities of the Foundation.
No additional funds will be provided to the University. The university should provide more funds.
4. The income of the Trust shall be raised from the interest received on fixed deposits. This will be supplemented by revenues generated from fees for courses of study and income generated from running training programmes and mentoring/ counseling projects.
It shall be the responsibility of the University to submit the information of the expenditure incurred for the course to the Government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune from time to time.

Every 2 years, the Adhyasan Kendra will be inspected and a report in this regard will be submitted to the government through the Director of Higher Education, Pune. If the report shows negative progress of the centres in terms of quality of teaching, research performance, number of students, teaching and training classes conducted, the centres will be closed and the university will be obliged to transfer the funds to the government.
The grant should be used for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned to the University.
The Director (Higher Education), Maharashtra State, Pune shall be responsible for providing the said sanctioned provision to Gondwana University, Gadchiroli immediately.
3. The expenditure should be incurred under the head" Demand No-W-2, Principal Head-2202-General Education, 03-University and Higher Education, 102-Assistance to Universities,- (00) (22) Development of Gondwana University (Mandatory) (2202H581), 31 Subsidiary Grants" from the approved allocation for the financial year 2023-24.
