Introduction: The Department of Finance. As per the Government Circular dated 29.06.2017, posts in the senior category in addition to Group-D cadre are also required to be developed. Accordingly, the Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai, has issued a notification to this effect. As per the letter dated 04.05.2019, it is proposed to develop 1 vacant post of Short Writer in Group-C cadre in Government Divisional Library, Nagpur. Accordingly, the matter of developing 1 post of Short Writer in the said Group-C cadre was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government Divisional Library, Nagpur under the Directorate of Libraries is developing 1 post of Short Writer in Group C cadre.
