The Proposal:
In pursuance of the appointments of the Chairman and Government Appointed Members on the Governing Board of the Autonomous College of Engineering and Technology in the State, vide Notification No. (5) The Governing Bodies of the said institution have been constituted by a Government decision hereunder and the State Government has also made appointments of Chairman and Members to such bodies. The members have been appointed for a period of 2 years from the date of the decision of the Government.
In this regard, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education in a report submitted under Reference No. (6)
