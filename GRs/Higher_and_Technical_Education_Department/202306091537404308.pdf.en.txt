Preamble: Construction of Main Administrative Building and Examination Hall in the campus of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar University. The government has decided to pay Rs 1. Administrative approval has been accorded for the same at a cost of Rs 54,19,25, 453/-. In accordance with the said Administrative Sanction Reference No. 2 The University has requested the release of funds for this work through a letter here. In pursuance of this request, the Finance Department was requested to provide funds for this work. Finance Department Reference No. 3 It has been agreed to provide funds in accordance with the circular here. The government was planning to allocate funds for the project.
Government Decision: Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University, Solapur, Rs. 11,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Eleven Crores only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
The University should be used for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned and the utilization certificate should be submitted to the government immediately through the Director (Higher Education), Pune.
The progress of construction should be informed to the government from time to time.
Special care should be taken to ensure that the subject work does not require revised administrative approval.
All the documents and accounts of the above grant are entitled to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the Accountant General-2, Nagpur, as the case may be.
The Director (Higher Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune shall be responsible for providing the said sanctioned provision to Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University, Solapur immediately.
