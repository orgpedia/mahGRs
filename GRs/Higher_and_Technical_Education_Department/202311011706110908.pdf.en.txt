Section 23 (1) of the said Act provides for the constitution of the Governing Body of the University and Section 23 (4) lays down the composition of the Governing Body of the University.
The matter of appointing the Chairman of the Governing Body of the University as per the provision of Section 9 (1) of the Lakshminarayan Innovative Technology (LIT) University, Nagpur Act, 2023 was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of Section 9 (1) of the Lakshminarayan Innovative Technology (LIT) University, Nagpur Act, 2023, the following persons are being appointed by the State Government as the Chairman of the Governing Body of Lakshminarayan Innovative Technology (LIT) University, Nagpur for a period of five years:
