Memorandum: Under the new Government Model Degree College component under Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan 2.0, the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India has approved the establishment of new Government Model Degree Colleges in two districts of Nandurbar and Washim in Maharashtra in 12 meetings of the Project Approval Boards held on 25.05.2018.
The Public Works Department has allotted Rs. 1480.71 lakh for construction of hostel building and Rs. 626.19 lakh and Rs. 1485.86 lakh has been agreed.
The Union Cabinet in its meeting held on 15.01.2019 has approved the establishment of new Government Model Degree Colleges at Nandurbar and Washim under RUSA at a cost of Rs. 2400.00 lakh has been sanctioned, 40% of the State share of non-recurring expenditure amounting to Rs. 960.00 lakhs and the additional expenditure to be borne by the State Government on the construction of college and hostel is Rs. 1927.39 lakhs out of which Rs. Non-recurring expenditure to the tune of Rs. 2887.39 lakhs should be approved. It has been decided and approved to provide additional non-recurring expenditure at the State level as per the Government decision dated 12.02.2019.
The Government Model Degree College, Nandurbar, has been sanctioned Rs. 1200.00 Lakhs (Rs. 12 crore) Grants Centre Share of Rs. 720.00 lakhs and State share of Rs. 480.00 lakhs has been provided.
Additional funds to be made available at the State level for the construction of building and hostel building of Government Model Degree College, Nandurbar. 9,06,90, 000/- (Rs. 9 crore, 6 lakh, ninety thousand only) are being sanctioned for distribution and procurement.
