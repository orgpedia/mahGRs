Introduction: In the first phase of the World Bank-assisted Central Government Technical Education Quality Improvement Program, the governing bodies of the Government Engineering College, Pune, Government Engineering College, Amravati, Government Engineering College, Karad, Government Engineering College, Jalgaon, Government Engineering College, Chandrapur, and Veermata Jijabai Technical Institute, Matunga, Mumbai, have been constituted under the Government decision mentioned in Reference No. 1.
However, the term of appointment of members other than ex-officio members of the Governing Body constituted under the Government Resolution at Reference No. 1 is 2 years. Accordingly, the term of office of the members other than the ex-officio members of the Governing Body of the said institutions shall be deemed to be The Director, Directorate of Technical Education vide letter dated 13.01.2018 has requested the Government to constitute a new Board of Regulators and extend the term of the existing Board of Regulators till the new Board of Regulators is constituted. Accordingly, the matter of re-constituting the Governing Body of the institutions mentioned in the above paragraphs participating under the Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Governing Bodies of the Autonomous Engineering Colleges functioning in the State, namely, College of Engineering, Pune, Government Engineering College, Amravati, Government Engineering College, Karad, Government Engineering College, Jalgaon, Government Engineering College, Chandrapur, and Veermata Jijabai Technical Institute, Matunga, Mumbai, are being reconstituted as per the provisions of Annexure A, B, C, D, E respectively.
Further, the period of appointment of members other than ex-officio members of the Governing Body of the above mentioned institutions shall be reckoned from the date of such decision of the Government and the period of such members shall ordinarily be 2 years. Further, the Board of Governors of the above-mentioned Institutions have been given extension from 13th January, 2018 till the date of this Government decision i.e. 20th November, 2018.
