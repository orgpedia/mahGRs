Preamble: As per the Government Decision, Higher and Technical Education Department No. 2018/ PR No. 218/ TANSI-4, dated 4th October, 2018, the scheme" Scholarship for meritorious boys and girls for higher education abroad" has been implemented from the academic year 2018-19 for the students who have taken admission in Master's Degree/ Master's Diploma and Ph.D. courses in the universities within THE/ QS Ranking 200 abroad. The Directorate of Technical Education had issued an advertisement on 22nd June, 2019 inviting online applications under the Scheme for the academic year 2019-2020. The last date for submission of online application was 15th August, 2018.
A total of 60 applications, which were determined by examining the documents of the concerned students received by the divisional offices till the deadline, were received by the Directorate of Technical Education. Out of the applications received, the Screening Committee headed by the Director, Technical Education has found 39 applications eligible for foreign scholarships. Accordingly, the Scrutiny Committee has prepared a consolidated merit list of 01 candidate for Ph.D. course and 38 candidates for Post Graduate Degree/ Diploma courses.
The Selection Committee under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Higher and Technical Education constituted to prepare the final selection list for foreign scholarships vide Government Resolution dated 4th October, 2018, in its meeting held on 7th September, 2019, selected 01 for Ph.D. courses and 12 for Post Graduate/ Post Graduate Diploma courses.
