the order
The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 came into force with effect from 01.07.2006. In accordance with the provisions of Section 3 (1) of the said Act, the officers of the accompanying prospectus in the Government Colleges/ Institutions/ Offices under the Directorate of Higher Education are being transferred and posted to the posts indicated in column No. 5 against their names, with the approval of the competent authority.
The concerned Principal/ Director/ Head of Office shall relieve the transferred officers immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned officers should join the college/ institution/ office immediately after their transfer from the existing college/ institution/ office.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune should immediately submit an executive report to the government regarding this transfer order.
