Introduction:- The Institute of Science which is a part of Dr. Homi Bhabha State University, University of Mumbai, the Nuclear and Radiation Laboratory building in the Mumbai premises is in a dilapidated condition and falls under the C-1 (hazardous/ demolishable) category as per the norms of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation. 2 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. The Finance Department was requested to disburse the cost required for the demolition of the said structure. Finance Department agrees to disburse funds
is displayed. As per the said approval, the matter of disbursement of funds required for demolition of the said structure to Dr. Homi Bhabha State University, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision:- The Institute of Science under Dr. Homi Bhabha State University, Mumbai, required Rs. 11,11,049/- (Rupees Eleven Lakhs Eleven Thousand and Nineteen Fifty Five only), subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: 1. The University shall use the grant for the purpose for which it has been sanctioned and take care to submit the Utilization Certificate to the Government immediately through the Director (Higher Education), Pune.
The progress of the work should be informed to the government from time to time.
Special care should be taken to ensure that the subject work does not require revised administrative approval.
