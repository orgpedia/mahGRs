Munde was appointed as the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC)-approved candidate for the post of Advocate by the Government of Maharashtra in accordance with the Government Order dated 10th September, 2001 of the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government of Maharashtra.
He has been appointed to the College of Art, Mumbai. Accordingly, Shri Munde has joined the said post on 6th November, 2001.
Munde worked as an Assistant Resident at L.S. Raheja School of Art, Bandra, Mumbai from September 5, 1994 to November 5, 2001. The pension scheme has been implemented for the teachers and non-teaching staff of recognized and aided non-government arts institutions under the Government Decisions dated 16th November, 1996 and 10th December, 1999 respectively of the Department of Higher and Technical Education and Employment. Therefore, Shri Munde's service during the period from 5th September, 1994 to 5th November, 2001 is eligible for pension.

Munde's service in L.S. Raheja School of Art, Bandra, Mumbai, a non-government aided art institution, from September 5, 1994 to November 5, 2001, was under consideration of the Government for the purpose of pension.
He is an Assistant Professor at L. S. Raheja School of Art, Bandra, Mumbai. from September 5, 1994. The Government is allowing the services rendered up to 5th November, 2001 to be counted as pensionable services.
