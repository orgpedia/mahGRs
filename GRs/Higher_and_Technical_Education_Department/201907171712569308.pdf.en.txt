Preamble: In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for the academic year 2019-20, the Director, Technical Education vide his letter dated 12/7/2019 mentioned above has proposed to the Government to approve the merger of the direct second year batch of the existing Engineering, Technology/ MCA course in the first year and the merger of the course with the second course. It was pending approval of the government.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, the Government is approving the merger of the second year batch directly into the first year and the merger of the course into the second course in the institutions mentioned in the accompanying form. The accessibility indicated in the accompanying form of this Government Decision is not independent. included in the Governing Decision dated June 10, 2019. For this, the terms and conditions of the government decision dated June 10, 2019 will remain in force.
Other terms and conditions as laid down by the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and the University concerned shall also apply.
