The proposal:
The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE) has requested the Government under the aforesaid letter No. 2 and 3 to close the short-term course in a Government recognized Institute (Non-AICTE), which is not under the purview of the All India Council for Technical Education but under the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), and transfer the fixed deposits deposited by the Institute to the accounts of the Institute. As per the request, the government was considering to close the non-AICTE course in the institute and transfer the fixed deposits deposited by the institute to the accounts of the institute.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Approval of the Government is being given subject to the following terms and conditions to close the courses in the following' Form-A' named institutions which are not under the purview of All India Council for Technical Education but under the purview of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education.

The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters relating to the service and other matters of the faculty/ staff appointed for the concerned course. If any court case arises in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.
3. It shall be incumbent upon the head of the institution to pay the arrears, if any, due to the Government from the institution.
(4) It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay any further loans taken by it from the Government or any financial institution in respect of movable property required for the conduct of the said course in the Institute.
After the closure of the relevant courses in the institution, it will be mandatory for the institutions to make alternative arrangements for the admitted students and alumni of the institution if they wish to appear for the examination.
The Institute shall be obliged to pay the salary, salary compensation and other financial payments due to the teachers and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
7.The institution concerned should take further action to transfer the fixed deposit deposited with the government for starting the above courses to the accounts of the institution.
