Introduction: The Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi has approved the opening of a new M.Pharmacy course in the existing Pharmacy course of New Montfort Institute of Pharmacy, Ashti, Wardha by Bahwan Educational and Social Team. The Directorate of Technical Education, vide its letter dated 31.01.2023, has approved the proposal for grant of affiliation to the courses offered by the said Institute. The same has been submitted to the Government vide letter dated 09.02.2023. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to grant approval on permanent non-subsidized basis to start new M. Pharmacy courses in the existing pharmacy course institute of Bahwan Educational and Social Team, New Montfort Institute of Pharmacy, Ashti, Wardha from the academic year 2022-23.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval given by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi and as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the Government is granting approval for starting of new M.Pharmacy courses in the existing Institute of Pharmacy course of New Montfort Institute of Pharmacy, Ashti, Wardha of Bahwan Educational and Social Team from the academic year 2022-23, subject to the following terms and conditions as shown in the table below:
