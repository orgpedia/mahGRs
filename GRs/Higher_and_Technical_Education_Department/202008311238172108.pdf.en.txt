Government's decision
The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 has come into force with effect from 01.03.2017. Keeping in view the increasing pass percentage in higher secondary schools and the inclination of students towards a particular course, so that no student is deprived of the desired higher education, Section 109 (8) of the Act provides for expeditious recognition of additional fresh batches of specified faculties in exceptional circumstances. In the academic year 2020-21, the non-agricultural universities of the state had submitted proposals to the government for additional new batches of specific faculties. The proposals were examined in accordance with the criteria laid down under the relevant Government decisions. After this inspection, the proposals of the universities recommended to the government, the proposals of the fast-track additional new batches of specific faculties of the colleges mentioned in the following statement are being rejected as they do not meet the criteria specified in their names.

On the occasion, poetess Bahinabai Chaudhary of North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon inaugurated the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar College of Social Work, Morane (Nakane), Dhule, Ta. The proposal for additional batches of W (1st year) and MSW (1st year) has been forwarded to the Government. However, as all the functioning Social Work Colleges are presently under the Department of Social Justice and their entire control and grant-in-aid activities are being carried out by that Department, the above proposal submitted by the University to this Department has not been approved and has been transferred to the Department of Social Justice for further action.
