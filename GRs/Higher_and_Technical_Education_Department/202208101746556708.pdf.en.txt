The Government has taken a decision under Section 109 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016. As per the procedure laid down under the Government Decision dated 13.09.2017, the non-agriculture universities in the State have submitted proposals for new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties for the academic year 2022-23 to the Government for approval. The proposals were examined in accordance with the norms laid down under the relevant Government decisions. As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, new courses/ subjects are being approved in the existing colleges as per the existing policy of permanent non-grant policy of the Government, subject to the following terms and conditions, keeping in view the demand for higher education and academic urgency in the college campus.

B. Terms and Conditions:
Since the Government Colleges mentioned in the above statement have been sanctioned new courses/ subjects which are on permanent non-subsidized basis, the demand for salary of the faculty/ non-teaching staff appointed for such courses/ subjects in accordance with this approval will not be taxed by the Government. The government has also not sanctioned any post for this course.

2. The Principals/ Directors of the concerned colleges/ institutions should give an undertaking to the Director of Higher Education that since the said courses are permanently non-aided in nature, no demand for salary subsidy will be made in future. Apart from this, the concerned university should not start the process of affiliation.
The colleges are required to appoint faculty/ staff as per the norms prescribed by the concerned university and educational qualifications prescribed by the University Grants Commission and provide all other necessary infrastructure and the same shall be ensured by the Director of Higher Education.
Under no circumstances should the college admit more than the sanctioned intake capacity.
The University shall not proceed to grant affiliation to the colleges concerned unless they meet the criteria prescribed in the University Grants Commission (Affiliation of Colleges by Universities Regulations, 2009) for granting affiliation to colleges.
Affiliation proceedings should be carried out as per 108 (1) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
3. The Universities shall not initiate the process of affiliation without first perusing the Government approval orders before taking further action as may be required for grant of affiliation. The universities will have to submit a compliance report to the government through the Director of Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune.
The Director of Higher Education shall issue orders with the knowledge of the Government and the University after periodical review by the concerned University and the Director of Higher Education regarding the new courses/ subjects approved and if the number of students admitted in the new courses/ subjects is less than 50% of the sanctioned intake capacity, the recognition of the new courses/ subjects shall be deemed to be cancelled.
