Preamble Due to the success of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan and Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA), which have been implemented to empower primary education in the State, the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) is being implemented by the Central Government from the 12th and 13th Five Year Plans in the form of an umbrella scheme mission to empower the quality and foundation of higher education. The National Higher Education Mission (NHEM) is a flagship programme of the Government of India to ensure quality, equity and opportunity in higher education.
The State Project Directorate has been set up in pursuance of the decision taken by the State Cabinet for implementation. Ministry of Human Resource Development, New Delhi
The State Level Quality Assurance Cell was created under the Government decision dated 10.10.2016 to streamline the quality of education in each State by maintaining the quality of education and simplifying the method of assessment as mentioned in the guidelines under the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Yojana. Now, in the New Education Policy-2020 also, quality of education has been given priority. Accordingly, the matter of setting up a new state-level Quality Assurance Cell is under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision Under this Government Decision, the Government Decision dated 10.10.2016 is being superseded and the State Level Quality Assurance Cell (SLQAC) is being constituted as mentioned below.

The functions of State Level Quality Assurance Cell are as follows.
1.Increase the NAAC rating of colleges in line with the enhancement of educational quality standards in the state.
Review of academic audit of the University and affiliated colleges.
Review the functioning of the Internal Quality Assurance Cell at the university and college level.
To guide the Internal Quality Assurance Cell at the university and college level to streamline the process of academic auditing and capacity development.
To monitor the progress of assessment in the State and to suggest measures to expedite the progress thereon.
6. To guide the colleges regarding the difficulties being faced in conducting NAAC assessment.
Suggest other measures to improve the quality of education.
Individuals/ Institutions having expertise in the field may be invited for evaluation and quality enhancement of the State Level Quality Assurance Cell.
The services rendered and expenditure incurred by the non-official members of the State Level Quality Assurance Cell for the functioning of the Cell shall be reimbursed by the State Project Directorate, National
Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan will be given from the Preparatory Grants to Maharashtra.
As the work of the State Level Quality Assurance Cell increases, the structure of the cell can be further expanded to include experts in this field.
