Maharshi Dhondo Keshav Karve founded the Shrimati Nathibai Damodar Thackersey Women's University (the first women's university in the country) in 1916 to bring women into the mainstream of education in Maharashtra. Through this university, different faculties were started for women and started providing higher education to women. The University is in its post-centenary journey and will complete 106 years in 2022. Keeping in view the objective of empowering women through education, the university has started skill development courses for women in rural and tribal areas with low enrolment rates and with the aim of providing employment to local women in Ballarpur and Jichandrapur. This district has been selected and it is proposed to set up a knowledge campus of such a university to empower women by providing them quality and modern education.
