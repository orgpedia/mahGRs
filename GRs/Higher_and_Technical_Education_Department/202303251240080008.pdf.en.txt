Conditions: 1) Government Resolution, Department of Higher and Technical Education, No.TEM-2015/ No. 501/ Tanshi-4, D. 13th October, 2016, No. TEM-2016/ Q. No.559/ TANSHI-4, Dt. April 15, 2017 and No. SAMARKHA 2017/ Q.No.332/ TANSHI-4, Dy. The terms and conditions mentioned in the order dated 7th October, 2017 and other orders in this regard shall be strictly complied with.
(2) Only eligible admitted students in the Centralized Admission Process should be given the benefit of Academic Fee Scholarship. The amount will be directly credited to the bank accounts of the eligible students through the MahaDBT portal. However, appropriate action can be taken as per the court's decision.
(3) The Director, Technical Education and Joint Directors of all Divisional Technical Education shall visit the sample institutions under their jurisdiction and inspect the implementation of the scheme from time to time and submit a report to the Government.
(4) The Director of Technical Education shall take necessary steps to rectify any errors/ irregularities found in the previous inspection report. If it is found that the education fee has been received from the government by filling up wrong or false information, such amount should be recovered and deposited to the government. Also, if the institution is found guilty, it should be recommended to the All India Council for Technical Education or other competent authority to withdraw the recognition of the course of the institution/ file a case of fraud and such strict action should be taken.
5) The funds should be immediately allocated for the scholarship and the utilization certificate should be submitted to the government.
There should be no delay in disbursement of funds. The Director of Technical Education will be responsible for this.
3. It is being approved to disburse the pending amount of scholarship amount to the students who have been eligible for the academic year 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 under this scheme from the fund disbursed under this order. Also, Rs. 426.45 crore, if the balance is released, it should be spent for the scholarship of the beneficiary students for the academic year 2022-23.
