Preamble: For the construction of" Pariksha Bhavan" and" Administrative Building (1st and 2nd Floor)" of Gondwana University established for two districts Chandrapur and Gadchiroli in the state, Rs. 4571.93 lakh. 1 here. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 29.03.2017. The government had earmarked Rs. 3,20,00, 000/- has been approved. After that, considering the progress of this construction, Reference No. A total of Rs 2. 26,94,35, 000/- has been disbursed. Now Gondwana University has issued Reference No. (3) Under this letter, the government has been requested to release the remaining funds for the construction of the examination hall and administrative building. Accordingly, the Finance Department was requested to release funds from the provision budgeted through Supplementary Demand for the said construction. The Finance Department has agreed to release funds for this work. Accordingly, the matter of disbursement of funds to Gondwana University, Gadchiroli for construction of examination hall and administrative building was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Construction of" Pariksha Bhavan" and" Administrative Building (1st and 2nd Floor)" at Gondwana University, Gadchiroli Pursuant to Government Decision dated 29.03.2017, Rs. 4571.93 lakhs (Rupees Forty Five Crores Seventy One Lakh Ninety Three Thousand only), the establishment of Gondwana University in the financial year 2022-23 under the head' Establishment of Gondwana University (2202H 581)'.
