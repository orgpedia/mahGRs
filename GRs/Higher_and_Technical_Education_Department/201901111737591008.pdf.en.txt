Preamble: To scrutinize the proposals of self-financing universities received by the Government in accordance with paragraph 6 (1) of the guidelines prescribed by the Government. There is a provision to constitute a Scrutiny Committee under the Government decision dated 10th September, 2013. Accordingly, the following Scrutiny Committee is being constituted to examine the proposal of the Sponsoring Board of Strive India Education Foundation for a self-financed University namely" Universal Management University, Karjat, Raigad."

Committee's mandate
1. The Committee shall examine the proposals received by the Government for" Universal Management University, Karjat, Raigad" as per the guidelines prescribed by the Government. The self-financing guidelines issued by the Government should be examined in accordance with the matters mentioned in paragraphs 5 (2) and 6 (3).
The Committee may request other information from the Sponsoring Board in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 6 (4) of the Guidelines.
(3) The Committee may suggest changes, if any, in the Detailed Project Report and the same may be considered and communicated to the Sponsoring Board for compliance within a specified period.
The term of office of the Committee shall be one year from the date of issue of the Government Resolution.
The members of the committee shall not have voting rights.
(6) The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within two months from the date of its meeting with the Chairperson.
The representative of KPMG should prepare the project report.
8. Allowance to members other than ex-officio members of the Committee shall be admissible as per the prevailing rules prescribed by the Government and shall be paid from the sanctioned grant.
