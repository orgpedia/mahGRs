The proposal:
All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. 1 here. Pursuant to the approval given on 14.03.2022, the Director, Technical Education vide the aforesaid No. 2 here. As proposed vide letter dated 13.09.2022, it was under consideration of the Government to grant approval for closure of Pravara Centre for Management Research and Development, Pune, a professional course institute of Shri Vikhe Patil Foundation, from the academic year 2021-22.
The government's decision:
In accordance with the approval of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government has approved the closure of Pravara Centre for Management Research and Development, Pune, a professional course institute of Shri Vikhe Patil Foundation, from the academic year 2021-2022, subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) The institution will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in the institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the concerned university.
(2) The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters relating to the alternative arrangements for teachers and non-teaching staff appointed for the relevant courses in the institution as well as judicial matters in respect of service matters and other matters.

The institution will take the responsibility of providing educational facilities and arranging for the students to sit for the examination till the completion of the course. If the institution does not provide these educational facilities, action will be taken against the institution as per the rules and the accumulated deposit with the government will be deposited by the government.
7) The institution shall be bound to pay the salary, salary compensation and other financial contributions due as per rules to the teachers and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
