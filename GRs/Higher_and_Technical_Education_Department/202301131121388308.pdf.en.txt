the Government Order
As per the Government Order No. 1& 2, the post of Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A and Joint Director (Administration), Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A, the faculty/ administrative officer was temporarily posted with additional charge for a period of 2 years from the date of the said order or till the receipt of the candidate from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission or until further orders for any other reason, whichever is earlier. Also, under the government order at Reference No. 3 to 6, some teachers were temporarily assigned additional charge of the post of Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra Education Service, Group-A, until further orders.
2. As per the decision of the Government at Reference No.7, a Selection Committee was constituted to review the functioning of the working Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education and Joint Director (Administration) and to recommend to the Government to temporarily hand over the additional charge of this post to new officers. As per the recommendations of the Committee, it has been decided to continue the additional charge of some officers in the rank of Joint Director, transfer the additional charge of some officers by changing the office, terminate the additional charge of some officers and hand over the additional charge to some new officers.

3. The additional charge of Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education and Joint Director (Administration), as above, is being continued, the details of which are annexed in Form A. Also, the details of the Divisional Joint Director who is being given additional charge by changing the office, is attached in" Form-B." Apart from this, the details of the officers who are being given additional charge of the post of Divisional Joint Director have been added in the" Form-C." Accordingly, as new orders regarding additional charge are being issued, the details of the Divisional Joint Directors whose additional charge is being brought to an end through this, are attached herewith in Form D.
The officers mentioned in Form-A, Form-B and Form-C above are being temporarily posted in the post mentioned against their names with additional charge for a period of 2 years from the date of this order or till the availability of candidates from the Maharashtra Public Service Commission or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
The additional charge of the posts of Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education, as well as Joint Director (Administration) as above shall be subject to the following conditions:
(1) The Professor/ Associate Professor/ Administrative Officer appointed to the said post shall be entitled to the same pay and allowances as are being paid to him by his parent institution.
2) Department of Finance Government Decision No.: Pay No. 1311/ PR No. 17/ Service-3, dated 27.12.2011 and the Government Corrigendum dated 28.08.2013 and 01.06.2015 have fixed the additional pay/ special pay for the period of appointment to two or more posts, according to which the salary of the additional workload will be admissible to the said teacher/ administrative officer.
(6) On receipt of the said order, the officers who have been given additional charge of the post of Joint Director of the department should be relieved immediately by the head of their office and the concerned officers should take charge of the post assigned additional charge immediately.
The Director of Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune should submit a report to the government in this regard.
7. The Director Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune shall forthwith relieve the officers of" Form-D" and submit a report to the Government regarding their joining the post at their parent institution.
