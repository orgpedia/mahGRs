The Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur has been directed to provide funds to the tune of Rs.5 crore per university per year in four instalments to each law university for the next five years from the financial year 2018-19 for meeting their day-to-day administrative and academic expenses, salary of the posts filled as per the approved figure, and honorarium of the contractual staff paid by the universities. 1 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision.
Accordingly, Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Nagpur and Aurangabad issued Reference No. 2 In pursuance of the proposal made vide letter dated 01.02.2022, the second tranche of funds for the year 2022-23 (i.e. from July, 2022 to September, 2022) is proposed to be Rs. 1,25,00, 000/- (per University) i.e. Rs. 3,75,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Seventy Five Lakhs only) was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur will be provided a total amount of Rs. 1,25,00, 000/- (per University) i.e. Rs. 3,75,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crores Seventy Five Lakhs only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
(1) The Executive Council shall be responsible for the management of the grant to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, Aurangabad and Nagpur by depositing the second installment of the total amount sanctioned by the Government in the University Fund. Also, the registrar/ vice-chancellor of the concerned Maharashtra National Law University should take action to exchange the expenditure made out of this amount and approve it by placing the statement of accounts of expenditure before the General Council with the approval of the Finance Committee of the university.
(2) It shall be the responsibility of the Vice-Chancellor of the Law Universities to see that the amount sanctioned by the Government is spent for the purpose for which it has been sanctioned. In this regard, they should submit its utilization certificate and detailed expenditure information to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education within the prescribed time according to the schedule given in Article 2 (9) of the relevant Government Decision.
