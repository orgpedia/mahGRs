Introduction: The scheme is being implemented as an equal funding scheme with the joint contribution of the State Government and the contribution of the Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata under the Ministry of Culture, Government of India. In the Budget 2018-19, the State Government has budgeted a total amount of Rs. 2,50,000, 00/- (Rupees Two Crore Fifty Lakh only) for the scheme of Raja Rammohan Roy Library Foundation. Under this scheme, the libraries of the state are provided funds to spend on books, computers, xerox machines, cupboards, seminars and building construction.
As per the circular of the Finance Department in Reference No. 1, the funds are allowed to be spent within the limit of 70% of the budgeted provision. Accordingly, Rs. The matter of approving the expenditure by disbursing Rs. 1,75,000, 00/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy Five Lakh only) was under consideration of the Government.
The Government's Decision:
A total amount of Rs. 2,50,000, 00/- (Rupees Two Crore and Fifty Lakh only) has been budgeted as State Government's contribution for the year 2018-19 for the Equal Fund Scheme of Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata. Department of Finance As per the circular dated 02.04.2018, the limit for spending the mandatory funds is 70%. Accordingly, Rs. 1,75,000, 00/- (Rupees One Crore Seventy Five Lakh only) is being approved for disbursement.
