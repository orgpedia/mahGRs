Reference: 1) Letter No.10/ NGP/ New/ Approval/ 2019/524 dated 17.05.2019 of the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
Preamble: The Director, Directorate of Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government through a Reference letter vide email dated 15.05.2019 regarding recognition of new institutions of Diploma Courses in Professional Pharmacy in the State from the academic year 2019-20 by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. The Government was considering to grant approval to start new Diploma courses in Pharmacy to the 02 institutions mentioned in the attached Form-A.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the institutions mentioned in the attached Form-A are being granted approval by the Government to start the institution of new Diploma course in Pharmacy from the academic year 2019-20 subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The eligibility of the institution for admission to this course shall be as mentioned in Form-A.
For affiliation of the said course, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
The institution concerned will be required to take affiliation of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education for the Diploma courses in Pharmacy.
