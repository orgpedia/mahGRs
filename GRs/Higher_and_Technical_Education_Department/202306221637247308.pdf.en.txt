Preamble: Regarding closure of short term courses in Indira College of Business Management, Pune run by Shri Chanakya Education Society, Pune under Savitribai Phule Pune University, the Directorate of Technical Education vide letter No. 1 to 3 are requested. Savitribai Phule Pune University has issued a circular regarding the closure of short-term courses in the institution. No-objection certificate has been issued under Section 4. Following the request, the government was considering to approve the closure of short-term courses in the institute.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In the following" Form-A" issued by the Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune, the following courses of the said Institute are being approved by the Government for closure from the academic year 2023-24 subject to the following terms and conditions.

1.The Institute will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institute, such as marksheets, answer sheets, result sheets, addresses of the students, fee receipts, etc.
The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters relating to the service and other matters of the faculty/ staff appointed for the concerned course. If any court case arises in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.

3. It shall be incumbent upon the head of the institution to pay the arrears, if any, due to the Government from the institution.
(4) It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay any further loans taken by it from the Government or any financial institution in respect of movable property required for the conduct of the said course in the Institute.
After the closure of the relevant courses in the institution, it will be mandatory for the institutions to make alternative arrangements for the admitted students and alumni of the institution if they wish to appear for the examination.
The institution shall be bound to pay the salary, remuneration and other financial payments due to the faculty and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
7.The institution concerned should take further action to transfer the fixed deposit deposited with the government for starting the above courses to the accounts of the institution.
