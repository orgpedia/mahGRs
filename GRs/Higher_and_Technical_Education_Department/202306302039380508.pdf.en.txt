3.The order will come into effect immediately.
4. The period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of the Government shall be admissible to the teachers who have been transferred as per the above. As per the government rules, the transfer of teachers will not be allowed during the tenure of the post, travel allowance and daily allowance.
5. The principals concerned should relieve the transferred teachers immediately as this transfer order needs to be implemented effectively. It will be the responsibility of the concerned principals personally to relieve the transferred teacher.

6. The transferred teachers should be engaged by the concerned Principal without any delay after they are relieved from their respective institutions and come to join. In case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of a teacher, the concerned principal will be held responsible.
7. The concerned Joint Director, Divisional Technical Education Office shall follow up the implementation of the said order effectively. Further, the executive report in this regard should be submitted to the government through the Director, Technical Education within 15 days from the date of this order.
8. No leave of a transferred teacher shall be sanctioned without prior approval of the Government.
(9) After the transfer orders are issued, the concerned teachers shall take care to join the place of transfer during the period of their appointment as provided under the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incorporation, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981.
