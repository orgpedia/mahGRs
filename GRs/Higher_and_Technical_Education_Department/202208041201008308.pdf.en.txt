Terms and Conditions: 1. The Intent Letter to start a new college as per the prevailing policy of the Government shall remain valid for starting a college on non-subsidized basis.
Since the said letter of intent has been issued by the institution with a view to fulfil the prescribed terms and conditions before obtaining final approval for the new college, the institution shall not start the college without obtaining final approval of the Government for starting the new college.

The letter of intent issued by the government to start a new college will be valid until January 31 of the nearest next year. During this period, it will be mandatory to fulfill the terms and conditions on which the letter of intent has been issued and submit a compliance report to the university along with the necessary documents for final approval.
In view of the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, the final approval procedure has been clearly explained in the Government Resolution dated 15th September 2017.
In accordance with the provisions of the Act and in accordance with the said procedure, the institutions receiving the Letter of Intent fulfill the terms and conditions of the Letter of Intent and they should apply to the University for final approval with all the documents and the universities should submit the applications of all eligible applicant institutions and the compliance report to the Government along with the recommendation of approval in Annexure-C and Form-B and the accompanying documents.
