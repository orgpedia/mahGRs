The Proposal: -
As per the provision of Section 109 (4) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, Swami Ramanand Tirtha Marathwada University Nanded and Shivaji University, Kolhapur have not approved the proposal for new courses / subjects, additional batches, new faculties as recommended by them, the applicant institutions have decided not to apply. A petition was filed in the High Court. In this case, Mr. The issue of recognition of new courses / subjects, additional batches, new faculty in the existing college for the academic year 2024-25 was under consideration of the Government as per the decision of the High Court.
The Government's Decisions:
Swami Ramanand Tirtha Marathwada University, Nanded and Shivaji University, Kolhapur, as per the provisions of Section 109 (4) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and the procedure prescribed under the Government Decision in Reference, have recommended proposals for Government approval regarding introduction of new courses / subjects, additional batches, new faculty in the academic year 2023-24.
2. The proposals received from the universities have been scrutinized as per the norms prescribed for recognition of new courses / subjects, additional batches, new faculty as per the relevant Government decision. Accordingly, proposals meeting the criteria are required to be submitted to the Government under Section 109 (4) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and the following proviso:

In exercise of the powers conferred by the Act, a total of 12 colleges mentioned in the accompanying Statement are being permitted to start new courses / subjects, additional batches, new faculty on a perpetual non-grant basis with effect from the academic year 2024-25.
3. For the aforesaid new courses / subjects, additional batches, new faculties, the following terms and conditions shall apply.
1. The approval for new courses / subjects / speciality courses, additional batches, new faculty to the colleges in the prospectus is always on a non-subsidized basis and in pursuance of this approval, such colleges cannot demand teaching and non-teaching staff on a subsidized basis.
2. The colleges in the prospectus which have been sanctioned new courses / subjects / special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties shall submit to their Divisional Joint Directors a letter of undertaking stating that they will not be seeking grants under any circumstances in the future. Submit on a non-judicial stop paper of 100 / -.
3. The university concerned should not initiate the process of affiliation unless the college submits a certificate of assurance or so receives the certificate of the Divisional Joint Director.
The colleges are required to appoint faculty / staff as per the norms prescribed by the respective university and the academic qualification prescribed by the University Grants Commission and all other necessary infrastructure should be provided and ensured by the Divisional Joint Director and only after that the aforesaid Condition No. The certificate in 3 should be given to the university.
4. The college shall not, under any circumstances, grant admissions in excess of the sanctioned intake capacity.
6. The University shall not take action for affiliation of the colleges concerned unless it meets the criteria prescribed by the University Grants Commission (Affiliation of Colleges by Universities Regulation 2009) for affiliation of the colleges.
7. Proceedings for affiliation as per 108 (1) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
4. Universities should not initiate the process of affiliation without going through the Government approval orders and obtaining a certificate from the Divisional Joint Director in Condition No. 3 before taking the necessary further action regarding affiliation. Universities should send a compliance report on affiliation to the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and the respective Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education.
The respective Universities / Subjects / Special Level Subjects, Additional batches, New faculties approved shall be periodically reviewed by the respective Universities and Divisional Joint Directors and orders shall be issued by the respective University / Divisional Joint Directors with the approval of the Government and the University to cancel the recognition of the new courses / subjects / Special Level Subjects, Additional batches, New faculties if 50 per cent or less of the sanctioned intake capacity is enrolled in these new courses / subjects / Special Level Subjects, Additional batches, New faculties.
