Preamble: Section 3 of the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 made under section 9 (2) (a) (a) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Libraries Rules, 1970 provides for the establishment of a Book Selection Committee as per Section 4 Rule 22 (b) and in Schedule Sample B (5). The committee is tasked with selecting and listing useful books on the subject in Marathi language published in the state. Accordingly, the government has taken the decision. The selection committee was constituted on 30.12.2016. The government took the decision. The said Committee has been dissolved with effect from 27.01.2020. Hence, the government decided to re-constitute the committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have approved the re-constitution of the Book Selection Committee as follows:
