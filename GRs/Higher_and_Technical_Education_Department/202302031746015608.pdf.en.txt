The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has decided to introduce the above mentioned No. Under Section 1, it has been approved to change the name of the institution of the Diploma course in Engineering. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval of change in the name of the institution of the existing diploma course under the above reference letter.
The government's decision:
In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is approving change in the name of the institution of Diploma course in Engineering as mentioned in the following" Form-A" from the academic year 2022-23.
