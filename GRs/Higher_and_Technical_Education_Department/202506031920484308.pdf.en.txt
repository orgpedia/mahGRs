The Government's Decisions:
Section 109 (4) (a) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and the Government Decision in Reference, dated. As per the procedure prescribed under 13.09.2017, non-agricultural universities in the state have submitted proposals for new courses / subjects / special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties for the academic year 2025-26 to the government for approval. The proposals were scrutinized as per the norms laid down by the Government decision in the context. A total of 08 new courses / special level subjects / subjects mentioned in Annexure-A *, a total of 01 faculty mentioned in Annexure-B and a total of 01 additional batch mentioned in Annexure-C * are being approved on a perpetual non-grant basis in the existing college as per the powers vested in the Government under the proviso to Section 109 (4) (d) b of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
Aided colleges which have been sanctioned new courses / subjects / special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties, on a permanent non-aided basis.
