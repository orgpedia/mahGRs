The Maharashtra Unauthorised Establishments and Unauthorised Courses of Study in Agriculture, Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Health Sciences, Higher, Technical and Vocational Education (Prevention) Act, 2013.
The Maharashtra Unauthorised Establishments and Unauthorised Courses in Agriculture, Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Health, Higher, Technical and Vocational Education (Prevention) Act, 2013 has been enacted to effectively prohibit and take action against persons who establish and run unauthorised institutions and unauthorised courses in agriculture, animal and fisheries sciences, health sciences, higher, technical and vocational education in the state of Maharashtra and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto. Section 5 of the Act provides for the appointment of a competent authority for the purposes of this Act.
In pursuance of the said provisions of the Act, vide notification dated 7th November, 2013, the Director, Directorate of Higher Education, Pune, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai and the Director, Directorate of Vocational Education and Training, Mumbai have been declared as the competent authority for the educational institutions and courses in higher education, technical education and vocational education respectively. The government has been receiving complaints of unauthorised institutions and courses being run in the state.
Directorate of Higher Education, Pune and Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai have been declared as competent authorities for the purpose of the Maharashtra Unauthorized Institutions and Courses of Unauthorized Study in Agriculture, Animal and Fisheries Sciences, Health Sciences, Higher, Technical and Vocational Education (Prevention) Act, 2013 by notification dated 7th November, 2013.
