The proposal:
J.M. Patel College, Bhandara, run by Gondia Education Society, Gondia, has been offering Computer Science on a non-subsidised basis since 1988. Petition No. 4550/2016 was filed in the Nagpur Bench of the Bombay High Court. Therein, vide order dated 19.01.2018, a total of 04 posts have been made eligible for grant on the basis of student strength as on 01.10.2010 subject to certain conditions under Government Decision dated 02.01.2019 and these posts have been approved to be covered under the committed expenditure of salary as per Government Decision dated 09.7.2020. At present, under the Government decision dated 12.11.2021, 2088 posts have been approved to be filled. The said 04 posts have not been sanctioned due to non-inclusion of the said posts and restriction on recruitment as per the Government Decision dated 04.5.2020 of the Department of Finance. Therefore, Gondia Education Society has again filed the second petition No. 1948/2020.
dated 02.12.2021. The Court has ruled as follows:" In view of the above, we allow the petition and direct the respondent No. 4 to issue NOC to the petitioners within four weeks from the date of the order. "
In pursuance of the said Judgment, a proposal for filling up of 04 posts of Computer Science subject was placed before the Sub-Committee of the Finance Department, which approved the filling up of 04 posts of Computer Science subject and the matter of issuing orders at the Government level is under consideration.
The Government is granting approval to fill up 04 vacancies of Computer Science in J.M. Patel College, Bhandara run by Gondia Education Society, Gondia subject to the following terms and conditions:
