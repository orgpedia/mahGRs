The proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to fix the posts in the government service for persons with disabilities and to review the seats ensured for persons with disabilities at regular intervals of not more than three years. Accordingly, the Central Government vide notification dated 04.01.2021 has prepared and published the list of posts for persons with Benchmark Disabilities notified on 4.1.2021 under the heading' List of Posts Identified suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities notified on Policy/ Acts/ Rules-Notification' on the Central Government website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in. Further, instructions regarding ensuring posts in State Government establishments for Divyangjan on the pattern of Central Government have been issued by the Department of Social Justice Special Assistance vide Government Resolution dated 2.2.2021. Accordingly, the matter of ensuring posts for the disabled in the government service on the establishment of the Directorate of Higher Education and Regional Offices was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: With regard to reservation for persons with disabilities in the posts of Group A, B, C and D in Government service in the establishment of Directorate of Higher Education, the Government hereby order that in accordance with the provisions of the Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and the instructions contained in the Government Resolution dated 2.2.2021 of the Department of Social Justice, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, for the posts of Group-A to Group-D cadre in the establishment of Directorate of Higher Education and Regional Offices in the Directorate of Higher Education, New Delhi, the Government may, in accordance with the provisions of the said Act, provide for reservation for persons with disabilities in the posts of Group-A to Group-D in the establishment of Directorate of Higher Education and Regional Offices.
