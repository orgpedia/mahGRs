The government order:
As per the Government decisions at Reference No. 1, the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC)-recognized candidates have been appointed as Junior Administrative Officers/ Superintendents, Group-B on the establishment of Directorate of Higher Education for a probation period of two years. In view of the fact that the said officers have been personally examined in service as per the Check List and their performance during the period of probation is found to be satisfactory, it is hereby approved to continue their appointment as on the date of Pillar No.7 by terminating the probationary period of the officers mentioned in Pillar No.2 of the Table below. Accordingly, the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune should take necessary records in the service records of the concerned.
