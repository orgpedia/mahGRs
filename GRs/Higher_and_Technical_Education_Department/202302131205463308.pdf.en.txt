The proposal:
The Director, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the government to increase the intake capacity of Umashankar Social Welfare and Education Foundation, Aurangabad in Rajshri Shahu Institute of Fire and Safety Management, Aurangabad, a government-recognized short-term diploma course institute under the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education. The matter was under consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
The Government hereby grants approval to Umashankar Social Welfare and Education Foundation, Aurangabad to increase the intake capacity of Rajshri Shahu Institute of Fire and Safety Management, Aurangabad, a Government recognized short term Diploma Courses in Institutions not under AICTE and under Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education from the academic year 2023-24 subject to the following conditions.

1) The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE) is the state board of education in Maharashtra. As per the Government Decision dated 21.5.1983, the courses are on permanent non-grant basis and no grant from the Government will be payable for these courses.
(2) The duration of the course/ semester etc. shall be decided by the Government. 20.01.2022 as mentioned above. Also, the accessibility of the courses will be as mentioned in the above table.

3. The Institute shall appoint full time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses and pay salaries and allowances to the staff as per Government rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
For this short-term course, the institute must take affiliation of Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education. Also, if the courses approved by this Government are decided to be implemented by the All India Council for Technical Education, it will be mandatory for the institution to get the approval of the Council for such courses.
Although the Government has a fee reimbursement policy for professional courses, the fee reimbursement scheme is not applicable to any non-AICTE courses. Hence, the fee reimbursement scheme for the above courses will not be applicable to any category of students. The fee reimbursement/ concession scheme will not be applicable to economically backward students as well.
The institution will be obliged to submit a guarantee letter on a stamp paper of Rs.500 in the prescribed format to the Board regarding the acceptance of all terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education.
The concerned institution shall complete the other legal matters by paying the required term deposit in the joint account with the Director, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai.
