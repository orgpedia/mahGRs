A sum of Rs. 122,51,25, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Two Crores One Hundred and Twenty Five Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand only) has been provided in the Budget. To disburse the budgetary allocation, the No. Decisions 2 and 3 have been approved. In the Revised Estimates 2022-23 for the grant in aid to Government Recognized Public Libraries, Rs. 212,91,28, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Twelve Crore Ninety-one Lakh Twenty-eight Thousand only) has been sanctioned. Now, for payment of 2nd/ Consolidated installment of Grants-in-Aid to Government Recognized Public Libraries, Rs. The matter of sanctioning expenditure of Rs. 12,18,72, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Eighteen Lakhs Seventy Two Thousand only) to the Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
For the year 2022-23, for payment of 2nd/ Consolidated Installment of Grants-in-Aid to Government Recognized Public Libraries, an amount of Rs. 12,18,72, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Eighteen Lakhs Seventy Two Thousand only) to the Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
Accordingly, the concerned District Library Officer/ Head of Office is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." They are also being provided with powers to counter-sign as" Controlling Officer" on payments under the 31st Auxiliary Grants (Non-Payment) Target.
