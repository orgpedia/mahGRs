The proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 13.09.2019, considering the changes in the modern education system at the national and international level, it has been approved to set up a Teacher Training Institute under the Department for regular development of professional competence of the faculty of the universities and the teachers under the Department of Higher and Technical Education.
The matter of opening a new head of account for recurring and non-recurring expenditure of the institution was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
Approval is being given to open new Head of Accounts for the Teacher Training Institute established under the relevant Government decision as follows:
