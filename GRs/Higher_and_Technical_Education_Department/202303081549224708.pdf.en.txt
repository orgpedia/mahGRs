Introduction: The Asiatic Society, Nagar Bhavan, Mumbai was founded in 1804. The Institute works towards making the library a major centre of academic learning, enriching the bibliography, organizing lectures/ seminars by scholars, preserving rare manuscripts, rare numismatic collections, ancient maps and reference works and making them available to the readers for research.

The Asiatic Society, Mumbai shall be required to comply with the following conditions- 1) While incurring expenditure as aforesaid, the Society may, if required, incur expenditure to the extent of 50% for any of the items mentioned in the table, however, the total expenditure shall not exceed Rs. 1 crore should be the limit.
2) The funds received from the state government cannot be spent on the matter of salaries and allowances, the expenses of the salaries of the employees of the organization should be borne by the concerned organization.
(3) The funds received from the State Government shall be utilised in the manner prescribed for the purpose for which the funds have been sanctioned. The Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai will act as the controller.
4) The Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai should submit a report to the government after examining the work done from the funds distributed to the institution.
5) The institution should submit the utilization certificate of the spent funds to the government through the library directorate within the prescribed time.
The funds received from the Central and State Governments should not be used for the same purpose.
It will be mandatory for the Asiatic Society to make available the rare books digitized from the government fund, the relevant information free of charge to the Directorate of Libraries, Subordinate Government Libraries and Government Recognized Public Libraries (A, B, C, D category wise) in a phased manner.
8) The Government should provide the Asiatic Society space on priority and free of cost whenever required.
9) The Asiatic Society, Mumbai shall be bound to comply with the directions issued by the Government from time to time.
