4. The principals concerned should relieve the transferred teachers immediately as the transfer order needs to be implemented effectively. The principals will be personally responsible for relieving the transferred officials.
5. The transferred teachers shall be engaged by the concerned Principal without any delay after they are relieved from their duties and join the respective institution. In case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of a teacher, the concerned principal will be held responsible.
6. The concerned Joint Director, Divisional Technical Education Office shall follow up the implementation of the said order effectively. Further, the executive report in this regard should be submitted to the government through the Director, Technical Education within 15 days from the date of this order.
7. No leave of a transferred teacher shall be sanctioned without prior approval of the Government.
(8) After the transfer order is issued, the concerned teachers shall ensure that they join the place of transfer during the period of their appointment as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Term of Appointment, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981. Otherwise, the period of absence will be considered as non-working day.
