Government Decision: The Cabinet in its meeting held on 09.02.2022 has decided to take a decision on the proposal of the Government, Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. Under 16.03.2022, approval has been given to set up a Government Music College in the name of" Bharat Ratna Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar International College of Music" and" Bharat Ratna Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar Museum" equipped with state-of-the-art facilities for scientific study, development, propagation and propagation of music. For this purpose, the Directorate of Libraries, Kalina Complex, (Kole Kalyan), Santa Cruz, Mumbai, has allotted land No. 7000 square meters. The Government is approving the handing over of the open space to the Revenue and Forest Department for setting up of Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Museum to the Director of Arts, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
The Director of Libraries shall hand over the possession of the said land to the Collector, Mumbai Suburban District, Bandra, Mumbai and the Collector shall submit a proposal to the Government (Revenue and Forest Department) for handing over the said land to the Director of Arts, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
The conditions prescribed by the Revenue and Forest Department in this regard shall be binding on the Art Director, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
