Preamble: To set up a self-financed" Dr. D. Y. Patil University, Ambi-Talegaon" in the State. The Government has issued Letter of Intent dated 27th July, 2018 to the Sponsoring Board of D.Y. Patil Education Academy, Talegaon, Pune. The Sponsor Board has acted upon the Letter of Intent and submitted a compliance report to the Government vide letter dated 21st January, 2019.
As per the provisions of Para 7 (2) of the Guidelines issued vide Government Order dated 29th May, 2013, the Sponsor Board shall constitute a Compliance Report Monitoring Committee to verify the compliance report after submission of the compliance report to the Government. As per the said provision, the Sponsoring Board after issuance of letter of intent submits compliance report in terms of letter of intent to the Government. The government was considering setting up a monitoring committee to verify the compliance report.
Governance: The following Oversight Committee is being constituted to verify the compliance report submitted by the Sponsoring Board.

The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within a period of one month from the date of its constitution. This period can be extended up to three more months.
The members other than the ex-officio members of the Committee shall be entitled to a remuneration of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousand only) per sitting.
Non-government members who will attend the meeting from Mumbai will be paid a travel allowance of Rs.1000/- (Rs.1000 only) per meeting and members who will come from outside Mumbai will be paid the amount admissible to the two-tier AC status of the Railways.
5. Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance at the rate of Rs.8,900/-and above has been made admissible to Government employees as per Government Decision dated 3rd March, 2010.
