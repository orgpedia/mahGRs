Regarding the transfer of teachers in Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Order No: Transfer-2023/ PR No.110/ Mashi-1
Ministry Extension, Madame Cama Road
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mumbai 400 032.
The date is November 22, 2023.
The Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharging Official Duties Act, 2005.
the order
In accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) and 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, with the approval of the competent authority for transfer, the teachers in the accompanying prospectus in Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education are being transferred and posted to the posts indicated in column No. 5 against their names.
(2) The order shall come into force immediately.
3. The concerned Principal/ Director shall relieve the transferred teachers immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned teachers should join the college/ institution immediately after their transfer from the existing college/ institution.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune should immediately submit an executive report to the government regarding this transfer order.
(5) As per the provisions of Rule 28 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Appointment, Non-Acceptance of Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Removal and Removal from Service) Rules, 1981, the period of appointment shall not be admissible to the teachers concerned on the ground of their being transferred on request. Travel allowance and other incidental allowances will not be admissible.
6) The concerned teacher should not be granted leave without prior permission of the government. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, the parent office should not accept any leave applications of the said teachers. If such applications are received, they should be returned to the original address of the concerned officer by post.
7. It may be noted by all concerned that in case of failure to join the post immediately as per the said transfer order or any attempt to cancel the transfer by exerting any kind of pressure, disciplinary action will be taken against them considering this misconduct as a violation of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979.
