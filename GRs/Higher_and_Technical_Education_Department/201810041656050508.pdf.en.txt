The proposal:
Due to economic conditions, meritorious children of the open category have to be deprived of higher education in reputed educational universities abroad. In line with the scheme of scholarships provided by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance and the Department of Tribal Development to the students of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes for studies abroad, a proposal was placed for the consideration of the Cabinet to provide foreign scholarships to the children of open category students of the state for admission to courses in reputed universities abroad with certain modifications. Accordingly, in pursuance of the decision taken by the Cabinet on August 21, 2018, it was contemplated to issue detailed orders in this regard.
The government's decision:
The Maharashtra government has decided to grant foreign scholarships to 20 meritorious students from the open category in the state every year who will take admission in foreign educational institutions within THE (Times Higher Education)/ or QS (Quacquarelli Symonds) Ranking 200 for postgraduate degree, postgraduate diploma and PhD courses.
2. The scheme titled" Grant of Scholarship to Meritorious Students for Higher Education Abroad" under the Department of Higher and Technical Education should be implemented from the academic year 2018-19.
