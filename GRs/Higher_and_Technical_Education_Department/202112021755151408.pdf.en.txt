Introduction: Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation, Kolkata was established in 1972 with financial assistance from the Government of India. The objective of the Foundation is to promote universal development of public library services in the country. The Foundation provides matching grants to the States in the ratio of 50:50 to effectively implement and enhance library services in the States and Union Territories. To implement these schemes of the Foundation, the Raja Rammohun Roy Library has been established as per the guidelines of the Foundation's book" Books for the Melineers at their Doorstep" under reference no. The committee was reconstituted following a government resolution.
Accordingly, the term of the said Committee has expired on 02.11.2021. Hence, the matter of forming a State Library Planning Committee was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has approved the constitution of State Library Planning Committee under Raja Ram Mohan Roy Library Foundation as follows:

The non-official members of the committee appointed by the government and nominated by the chairman of Raja Ram Mohan Roy Library Foundation will have a tenure of three years. The Director of Libraries will be required to submit a draft plan of the approved funds for the Equal Assistance Fund and other assistance schemes to be implemented by the Foundation and submit the proposal to the Committee for final approval.
