As per the above decision, necessary teaching and non-teaching posts have been sanctioned from time to time for the following Universities: (i) Gondwana University, Gadchiroli (ii) Shivaji University, Kolhapur (iii) Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University, Solapur (erstwhile Solapur University, Solapur) (v) Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (v) Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravati (vi) Kavitree Behnabai Chaudhary Uttar Maharashtra University, Jalgaon (erstwhile Uttar Maharashtra University, Jalgaon). The salary of these posts is being collected under different headings created for the university. For the salaries of the employees under the same university, the work of demanding salary grants under different headings, making budgetary provisions, disbursing the said provisions, submitting the payments to the treasury and reconciling the expenses incurred, etc. has to be done separately. It was also observed that salaries of some employees were pending as limited funds were being budgeted under the respective heads. Therefore, in order to avoid the above mentioned things and to bring uniformity in the administrative affairs, the matter of spending the salary of the employees in the said university through a single account head was under the consideration of the government, accordingly, the following decision has been taken.
Government Decision: The expenditure of salary of all teachers and non-teaching staff in the following universities will be increased from the financial year 2023-24. Expenditure is being allowed to be deducted from the head of accounts in 3.
