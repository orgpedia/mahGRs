Introduction:" HTE service system is a customized service system for the salary of teachers/ non-teaching staff in aided colleges and non-agricultural universities under the Department of Higher and Technical Education. The system covers 69000 (87%) employees of Higher and Technical Education Department and 9535 (13%) employees of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department.
As per the directions of the Finance Department, MahaIT has been entrusted with the responsibility of developing and maintaining the HTE servicing system. The work of development and maintenance of this system is carried out under the above mentioned No. As per the Government decision dated 01.02.2019, the period from 1.1.2019 to 31.12.2019 was allotted to MAHAIT. It has been extended from time to time. Reference No. 8 This is the period of the decision of the government. This has been extended up to 31.5.2023.
The period of the aforesaid order has expired on 31.5.2023. It was also under consideration of the Government to appoint MahaIT for the next period so that the work of development and maintenance of the HTE service system could continue regularly.
