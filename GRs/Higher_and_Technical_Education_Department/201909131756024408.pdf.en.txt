Preamble: The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retd. The decision to accept the recommendations of the said Committee with certain modifications has been taken under the Government Decision Vepur-2018/ PROCESS 44/ SERVICE-9 dated 1st January, 2019 of the Finance Department. Recommendation No. 11 of the report (para 4.13 of the report) proposes to extend the same to the employees of aided schools.
It was also proposed that the date of implementation of these recommendations should be the same as that of state government employees. Accordingly, the government has decided to implement the 7th Pay Commission for the employees of the 6th Pay Commission and grant-in-aid institutions.
The Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 have been notified vide Finance Department Notification No. Vepur-2019/ PROCESS 1/ SERVICE-9 dated 30th January, 2019 for implementation of 7th Pay Commission to the State Government employees with effect from 1st January, 2016. Further, the detailed procedure for fixation of pay has been prescribed vide Circular No. Vepur-2019/ PROCESS 8/ SERVICE-9 dated 20.02.2019 of the Finance Department. The government was considering to implement the revised pay structure for teachers and non-teaching staff in government-recognized non-government aided arts institutions in accordance with the 7th Pay Commission. Accordingly, the following decision was taken.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government has approved the revised pay structure for teaching and non-teaching staff in Government recognized Non-Government Aided Arts Institutions under the control of Directorate of Arts as shown in Form A attached to the Seventh Pay Commission with effect from 1st January, 2016.

is being provided. Fixation of pay in the revised pay structure for Government employees should be done as per the orders issued by the Department of Finance dated 20th February, 2019 and accordingly.
Employees of Government recognized Non-Government Aided Art Institutions shall, within one month from the date of issue of this order, give an option to accept fixation of pay in accordance with the revised pay structure in the form of Annexure-I attached to this Government Order. Also, the bond will be presented in the form of a pair of bonds. If the option is not received within the prescribed time, the employee will be deemed to have opted for the revised pay structure on and from January 1, 2016. In such a case, the head of the office shall obtain from such employee a bond in the form of an addendum to this government decision. The category wise designation of officers and staff in Government recognized Non-Government Aided Art Institutions is as given in Form A along with the details of pay scale and level.
In case of any doubt regarding the revised pay structure, the matter should be referred to the Director of Arts, Directorate of Arts, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. Art directors should submit such cases to the government, if necessary.
The additional expenditure on account of implementation of revised pay scales as per 7th Pay Commission should be included under the head Demand No.W4,2205-Arts and Culture, 101-Line Arts Education, (03) Assistance to Non-Government Art Institutions, (03) (01) General Recurring Grants (Compulsory), 36-Assistant Grants-in-Aid (2205 0055) and should be met from the grants sanctioned under that head. The Director of Art, Directorate of Art, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall take into account this additional expenditure while preparing the revised estimates.
The payment of arrears of salary in respect of all employees in service as on 1st January, 2016 shall be processed in accordance with the provisions of Rule 14 of the Circular dated 20th February, 2019 of the Department of Finance as well as the following applicable provisions of the Department of Finance Circular Vepur-2019/ PR No.8/ Service-9 dated 30th May, 2019.
This decision was taken by the Government with the concurrence of the Finance Department in its Informal Reference No. 360/ Service 9 dated 13.9.2019.
