Preamble: With the success of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan and Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan, the Government of India has decided to implement Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) as an umbrella scheme, mission mode, to empower the quality and foundation of higher education in the State.
The National Mission on Higher Education (NMHE), a flagship programme of the Government of India, aims to ensure quality, equity and opportunity in higher education in Maharashtra. The decision was taken at the Cabinet meeting held on 21.04.2015.
In pursuance of the decision taken by the State Cabinet, a State Project Directorate has been set up for effective implementation of the scheme in the State. Under this scheme, the ratio of central and state share of subsidy to non-agricultural universities and government institutions is 60:40 and for the Erst Time Model Degree Colleges in RUSA 10, the central share is 1/3 rd and the state share is 2/3 rd.
The Central share under the Research and Innovation component to non-agricultural universities in the State for the State Project Directorate is Rs. 31,21,62, 500/- and component wise State share aggregating to Rs. 21,25,58, 333/- aggregating to Rs. 52,47,20, 833/- (Rupees Twenty Two Crores Forty Seven Lakhs Twenty Eight Thousand Eight Hundred Thirty Three only) to the State Project Director, Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan.
