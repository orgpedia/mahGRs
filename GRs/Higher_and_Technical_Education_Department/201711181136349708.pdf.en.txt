GOVERNOR'S DECISION:- The Director, Technical Education, Mumbai, under the supervision of Dy. As per the letter dated September 26, 2016, a proposal was submitted to the Government for obtaining permission to open a new Personal Accounts (PLA) account for the Government College of Engineering, Nagpur. Accordingly, the Government is approving the opening of a new Personal Accounts (PLA) account in the name of the Principal for Government Engineering College, Nagpur.
The Personal Liability Account (PLA) will include the following items: :
1) Security Deposit (2) Gymkhana Fee (3) Gathering Fee (4) Training and Establishment Fee (5) Student Development Fund. Student Development Fund (6) Library Fee and Internet Fee (7) Scholarship for SC/ ST Students
The Principal Accountant General (Accounts& Licensure), Maharashtra-1, Mumbai has granted permission to open this account subject to the following terms and conditions.
1) The Personal Accounts (PLA) account will be audited.
2) The Personal Accounts (PLA) account should be handled properly by the administrator.
The Personal Accounts (PLA) Account should be opened by the Accountant General (Accounts and Accounts), Maharashtra II, Nagpur after authorization from the concerned Treasury.
All the provisions of the Maharashtra Treasury Act-1968 and the Accounting Rules will be applicable to this Personal Property Account (PLA) account.
Monthly/ quarterly balance of Personal Accounts (PLA) account should be reconciled with the Nagpur Treasury Office.
