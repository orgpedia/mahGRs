Government Decision dated 19.10.2020 constituted a Committee to amend the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
Three meetings of the committee have been held. The committee has to incorporate some provisions of the National Education Policy, 2020 in the draft to amend the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016. The government is considering extending the deadline by four more months.
The committee constituted to amend the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 has four more to submit its report to the government.
The deadline has been extended to November 16, 2021.
