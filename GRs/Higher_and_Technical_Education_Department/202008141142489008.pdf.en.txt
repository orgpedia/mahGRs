The proposal:
The Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi has approved the introduction of Diploma in Pharmacy courses in the existing Under Graduate Institute of Pharmacy courses in the State from the academic year 2020-21 and to restore the earlier reduced intake capacity of the ongoing Diploma courses. The matter regarding grant of Government approval to the affiliated institutions to start Diploma Pharmacy course in the existing Degree Pharmacy course institution as indicated in Annexure A before their names and to restore the earlier reduced intake capacity was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval given by the Pharmacy Council of India, the institutions mentioned in Annexure A annexed herewith, are being granted approval by the Government from the academic year 2020-21 onwards to start Diploma Pharmacy courses in the existing Degree Pharmacy course institution as indicated against their name and to restore the earlier reduced intake capacity, subject to the following terms and conditions.
In case of unaided institutions, the approval shall be on a permanent basis without subsidy.
In this case, the institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education/ Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi as well as under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. For this course
