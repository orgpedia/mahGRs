Three separate committees have been constituted under the Government Order No. 1 in the above context to publish the literature of Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mahatma Jotirao Phule and Rajarshi Shahu. Under the Government Resolution No. 2, it has been approved to search the literature of these great men, to make tours in the state/ outside the state if necessary, to examine the literature, to carry out detailed and research process of the literature, to print and reprint the literature and to bring out the edition. In accordance with the Government Resolution No. 3, The date has been extended upto 23.11.2016.
Proposal for further extension presented to the Chief Minister. The following volumes published by Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar and Rajarshi Shahu under the letters 4, 5 and 6 were printed and for the scanning, printing and binding of the Janata weekly, respectively, Rs. 16,94,020/- (Rupees Sixteen Lakhs Fourteen Thousand Twenty Only), Rs. 48,26,500/- (Rupees Forty Eight Lakhs Twenty Six Thousand Five Hundred only) and Rs. 3,11,250/- (Rupees Three Lakhs Eleven Thousand Two Hundred and Fifty only) aggregating to Rs. 68,31,770/- (Rupees Sixty eight lakh thirty one thousand seven hundred and seventy only) has been certified as yet unpaid and requested to approve the said expenditure. The government had agreed to bear the cost.
The expenses incurred for printing the following books of Babasaheb Ambedkar and Rajarshi Shahu as well as the Janata weekly run by Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar were made available by Nagpur University.
