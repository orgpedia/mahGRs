Preamble: As per the list provided by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide email dated 14.05.2018 regarding recognition of professional courses from the academic year 2018-19, the Director, Technical Education, vide the aforesaid communication, proposes to grant Government approval for closure of courses in the existing Engineering, Pharmacy, HMTCT, Degree, Post Graduate Institute of Management courses and reduction in the intake capacity of the existing courses as per the approval of All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi.
The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has approved closure of 68 courses in Annexure A/ reduction in intake capacity of the existing courses from the academic year 2018-19. It has been approved to reduce the intake capacity of 189 existing courses in Annexure' B'. Therefore, a total reduction of 10,295 seats in the current intake is expected in the academic year 2018-19.
In pursuance of the list made available by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi vide e-mail dated 14.05.2018, the Director, Technical Education, as proposed under the aforesaid letter, was under consideration of the Government to approve the closure of courses and reduction in the intake capacity of existing courses as indicated against the name of the institution in Annexure A and Annexure B for the academic year 2018-19.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As proposed by the Director of Technical Education in the aforesaid Reference letter, the academic year as approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi

Government Resolution No: TEM-2018/ PR No.210/ TANSI-4
Closure of courses and reduction in accessibility of existing courses as indicated against the name of the institution in Annexure A and Annexure B from 2018-19
Approval is subject to the following terms and conditions:
1. All the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc.
It must be submitted to the university.
The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters which are sub judice in respect of service and other matters of the faculty/ staff appointed for the concerned course. In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.
3. It shall be incumbent upon the head of the institution to pay the arrears, if any, due to the Government from the institution.
4. The Institute shall be fully responsible for repayment of any loan taken by it from the Government or any financial institution in respect of immovable property required for the conduct of the said course in the Institute.
After the closure of the relevant courses in the institution, it will be mandatory for the institution to make alternative arrangements for the admitted students and former students of the institution if they want to sit for the examination.
The institution shall be bound to pay the salary, remuneration and other financial payments due to the faculty and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
