Section 9 (2) (i) of the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 provides for the functions of the Directorate of Libraries to provide training in library science to interested candidates for the purpose of acquiring basic knowledge of library science and to conduct examinations for candidates of recognized library training courses.
The Librarianship Certificate Examination is conducted from the inception of the Directorate of Libraries. The examination is conducted annually at the district level by the State/ Division Library Association recognized by the Government. The syllabus of the examination was revised in 1973, 1985 and 1996.
At present, the examination is being conducted as per the revised syllabus of 1996. There is an urgent need to revise the curriculum of the Certificate Course in Librarianship in order to create trained manpower to provide quality library services in the present and future, and to provide trained manpower to the public, school, college, university, private and similar libraries in the state. Accordingly, the matter of forming a committee to revise the curriculum, duration and general procedure of the Librarian Certificate Examination etc. was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government is approving the constitution of a committee of the following members to suggest time-bound reforms in the course of the Certificate Course in Librarianship

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows: The Committee shall determine the syllabus, duration, general procedure etc. in the course of the Certificate Course in Librarianship.
Make all the necessary changes in the curriculum.
The Committee shall submit a draft curriculum with its self-explanatory recommendations to the Government within two months from the date of the decision.
Functions of the Committee: The functions of the Committee shall be held in the office of the Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai or elsewhere as per convenience. The Director of the Library shall make necessary arrangements for the work of the Committee.
Term of the Committee: The term of office of the Committee shall be two months from the date of its constitution.
Meeting Allowance and Travel Allowance: The non-official members appointed in the said committee will be paid by the Finance Department vide Government Resolution No. Travel 1010/ Q.
