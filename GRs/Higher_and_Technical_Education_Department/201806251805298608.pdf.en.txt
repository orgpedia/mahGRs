Preamble: The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has provided the list of approved professional courses for the academic year 2018-19 to the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai through e-mail dated 07.05.2018 and 14.05.2018. As per the list made available, the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide his letter dated 19/05/2018, has submitted a proposal to the Government for approval to increase the intake capacity of Bachelor of Computer Engineering from 60 to 120 from the academic year 2018-19 in the (Government) College of Engineering, Pune. In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government was considering to increase the intake capacity from 60 to 120 in the Bachelor of Computer Engineering course in the Government College of Engineering, Pune, as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, vide the aforesaid letter.
As per the approval of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, the Government is granting approval to increase the intake capacity of Computer Engineering from 60 to 120 from the academic year 2018-19 as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, Pune.
The institution shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.
