Savitri Devi Phule Women's Hostel, Mumbai has a six-storey building with a capacity of 450 girls. The building has been under construction for more than 70 years and is located on the seashore, due to the saline environment of the sea, the building's RCC columns, beams slabs, curtains have been cracked and the iron rods used in the construction of the building have been corroded. Also, the roof of the building is leaking water and the doors and windows of the building are in a bad condition. In this regard, the Public Works Department has suggested the protective strengthening of the hostel building.
Since it has become very necessary to carry out repairs with a view to maintain the building in order to maintain the building in good condition, it has been decided to strengthen and renovate the building of the proposed hostel under the letters dated 04/03/2022 and 27/09/2022, and accordingly, the necessary electrical set layout etc.
The matter of administrative approval of the works budget was under consideration of the government.
