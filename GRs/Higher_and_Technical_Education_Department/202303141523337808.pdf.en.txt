Introduction: Recognizing the importance of public libraries, the Government of Maharashtra has passed the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 to provide information to the people of rural and urban areas of the state on various aspects and subjects in the fields of social, economic, political, educational, information technology, agriculture, sports, art and entertainment etc. Under this Act, the state government aims to promote reading habit among the people of the state and to develop public library system in rural areas of the state.
The work of delivering the reading/ library movement is being done through the government-owned public libraries. The Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967. It was adopted on May 1, 1968, and has completed 50 years. The functions of the Committee shall be as follows: The Committee shall draft an all-encompassing legislation in the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 by making necessary amendments in the draft Act as per the changing situation of the time.

The Committee shall submit a revised draft of the Act along with its self-explanatory recommendations to the Government within one month from the date of the Government decision.
The functions of the Committee shall be carried out in the office of the Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai. The Director of Libraries shall make necessary arrangements for the functioning of the Committee.
Term of the Committee: The term of the Committee shall be one month from the date of constitution of the Committee.
Meeting and Travel Allowances:
The non-official members of the Committee will be entitled to a sitting allowance and travel allowance of Rs.7,000/-per sitting.
