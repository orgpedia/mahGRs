M- 12012/1/2023-NSS, Dated: 21.06.2023 Preamble: The system of conferring awards under the National Service Scheme at the State level based on the Central Government system was introduced in 1993-94 from the Silver Jubilee Year of the State. The application process was conducted online. After the online process, the government received information about the proposals of the program coordinator and director, program officer and volunteers released by the respective university in the first order, along with the hard copy. For the process of screening and selection of proposals received by the State Level Award Selection Committee, the selection committee decided to award marks for scrutiny/ evaluation as per the guidelines of the Central Government instead of online marks, as there is a huge gap of marks/ unreasonable marks/ meagre marks/ negative marks in the online proposals of the Programme Coordinator and Director, Programme Officer and volunteers. Accordingly, based on the marks given by the State Level Award Selection Committee and as per the recommendation of the State Level Award Selection Committee as mentioned below, the matter of awarding the awards for the year 2021-22 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The State Government has decided to give State Level Awards as mentioned below to those who have served the society selflessly and loyally in the year 2021-22 under the National Service Scheme, to encourage them in their committed work and to give due recognition to their selfless service.
