Preamble: The Cabinet in its meeting held on 24th March, 2021 has decided to start a new Government Engineering College at Ratnagiri from the academic year 2021-22. According to reference no. The government has approved the setting up of a new government engineering college in Ratnagiri, where courses in Mechatronics Engineering, Civil and Infrastructure Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Electrical Engineering, and Electrical Engineering will be started.
Accordingly, the Directorate of Technical Education has issued a Government Order regarding the creation of year-wise, course-wise and designation-wise posts in Government Engineering College, Ratnagiri. Proposals 3 and 4 have been submitted to the government. The High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 7 th January, 2022 has approved the creation of teaching and non-teaching posts in Government Engineering College, Ratnagiri in a phased manner. Accordingly, the government was considering to sanction teaching and non-teaching posts for the new Government Engineering College in a phased manner in the next four years.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 07/01/2022 has approved the opening of a new Government Engineering College in Ratnagiri district from the academic year 2021-22 and creation of necessary teaching and non-teaching posts for the same in a phased manner.
