The Proposal: -
As per the provision in Section 109 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, a letter of intent is issued to institutions to start a new college. Accordingly, the issue of issuing a letter of intent for the academic year 2026-27 was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
As per the procedure prescribed under Section 109 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and the Government Decision in Reference, all public universities in the state have been recommended for Government approval through the New College Accreditation System (NCPS) for obtaining Letter of Intent for starting a new college for the academic year 2026-2027.
The proposals received from the universities were thoroughly vetted by the AGM College Accreditation System (NCPS). Accordingly, a total of 38 institutions, namely, 34 institutions mentioned in Annexure-A * and 4 institutions mentioned in Annexure-B as per the proviso to Section 109 (3) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, are being granted Letter of Intent on a perpetual non-grant basis for starting a new college by way of proposals meeting the criteria, in exercise of the powers vested in the Government under Section 109 (3) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
The 34 institutions mentioned in the prospectus-A * are under Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur "Amgaon, Ta. Amgaon, G. Gondia.
