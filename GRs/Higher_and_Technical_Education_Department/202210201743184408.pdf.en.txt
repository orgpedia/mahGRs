Introduction: Lakshmi Narayan Institute of Technology, Nagpur, established in 1942 at Nagpur, is a premier institute of chemical technology in Central India, working in the field of chemical technology from undergraduate level to research level. Reference No. 1 for construction of Laboratory Building, Protection Wall, Entrance Gate and Renovation of the Institute premises. 1 The letter requested approval of the budget. Based on the request, the construction of laboratory building, protection wall, entrance gate and other ancillary renovation works of the institute will be taken up at a cost of Rs. 770.37 lakh in the Budget Reference No. 2 here. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 10.06.2022. An immediate demand of Rs. A budget of Rs. 770.37 lakh has been allocated for the project. The Finance Department had been asked to provide the funds. The Finance Department has agreed to provide funds.
Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to provide funds for the construction of laboratory building, protection wall, entrance gate and other ancillary renovation works of Lakshmi Narayan Institute of Technology, Nagpur.
Government Decision: For the construction of laboratory building, protection wall entrance and other ancillary renovation work of Lakshmi Narayan Institute of Technology, Nagpur, Rs. 4,16,25, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Sixteen Lakhs Twenty Five Thousand only) is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions.
