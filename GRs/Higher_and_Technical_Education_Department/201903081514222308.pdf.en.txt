Sinhagad Institute of Management and Computers from the academic year 2016-2017
All India Institute of Technical Education, Pune
Diploma/ Post Graduate Diploma courses of the above mentioned short duration, which are not under the purview of the Council but are under the purview of Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune, are closed
This is subject to the following conditions being approved by the government.
1.All the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc.
The institution will be required to hand over the same to the university.
(ii) the services of teachers/ non-teaching staff appointed for the concerned course; and
In other cases, the head of the institution will be responsible for all cases that are sub judice.
In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.
3. It shall be incumbent upon the head of the institution to pay the arrears, if any, due to the Government from the institution.
4. The immovable property required for running the course in the institution.
If it is further found that the institution has taken a loan from the government or any financial institution, it will be the responsibility of the institution to repay it.
