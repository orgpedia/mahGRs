Preamble: The Government has decided to implement the ongoing Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme in the State vide Government Resolution No.TEM-2016/ PR No. 501/ Tanshi-4, D. As on 13th October, 2016, the scheme was called" Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Reimbursement Scheme." Thereafter, Government Resolution, Department of Higher and Technical Education No.TEM-2016/ PR No. 559/ Tanshi-4, D. With effect from 15th April, 2017, the name of the scheme has been changed to Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme. Under reference As on 15th March, 2018, the Director, Technical Education had submitted a proposal for change in the head of accounts of the scheme. Accordingly, the proposal to amend the head of accounts of the scheme was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The title of the account of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme is being changed as follows. :
