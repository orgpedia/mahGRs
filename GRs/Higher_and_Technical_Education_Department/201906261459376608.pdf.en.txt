Preamble: The Director has submitted a proposal to the Government through the aforesaid letter regarding grant of Government approval for enhancement of accessibility in the institutions of Bachelor of Architecture course for the academic year 2019-2020, subject to approval of Council of Architecture, New Delhi. It was pending approval of the government.
Decision of the Government: In view of the approval given by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi, an apex body, to increase the accessibility of institutions offering architecture courses in the State for the academic year 2019-2020, the Government is granting approval to increase the accessibility of institutions in the Annexure subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed by the Council of Architecture in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The eligibility criteria for the course will be as mentioned in the admission prospectus.

(3) The Institution shall enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
4) For the affiliation of the said course, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra State University Act, 1994.
(5) The institution concerned shall meet other legal requirements by depositing the requisite consolidated deposit with the Director, Technical Education in a joint account.
6) Admission to the said Degree Course shall be made as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
If the institutions charge more than the tuition fee prescribed by the Tuition Fee Committee, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
8) The institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
9) The books, periodicals required by the library in the institution must be purchased in accordance with the standards of the Council of Architecture. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students. Also students are provided with all educational facilities according to the standards of the Council of Architecture (e.g. laboratories, libraries, classrooms, etc.
10) In the context of ragging. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999, need to be strictly implemented.
Other terms and conditions as laid down by the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and the University concerned shall also apply.
