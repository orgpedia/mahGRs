In accordance with the procedure laid down under Section 109 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and the decisions of the Government in question, the non-agricultural universities in the State have decided to introduce new courses/ subjects, extra-curricular activities and extra-curricular activities for the academic year 2019-20. Proposals of batches and faculties have been submitted to the government for approval. The proposals were examined in accordance with the norms laid down under the relevant Government decisions. Proposals that qualify as per the scrutiny at the Government level in accordance with the recommendations of the Universities, may be accepted by the existing colleges as per the authority vested in the Government under the proviso to Section 109 (4) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, for admission to the new courses/ subjects, as mentioned in the following statement. The batches and faculties are being sanctioned on permanent non-grant basis subject to the following terms and conditions.
