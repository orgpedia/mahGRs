GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION, NO. TED-2008/ (183)/ 08/ TANSHI5, dated 5th November, 2008.
Preamble: The rates of honorarium to be paid to hourly as well as technical superiors for the courses taught by the Government Arts Colleges under the control of the Directorate of Arts have been fixed as per the decision of the relevant Government. The matter of revising the rates of honorarium implemented under this government decision was under the consideration of the government. The following decisions have been taken in this regard:
Government Decision: The rates of remuneration payable to the Specialist Apprentices/ Hourly Teachers for the Degree/ Diploma/ Post Graduate Diploma/ Post Graduate Degree courses taught by the Government Arts Colleges under the control of Directorate of Arts are being increased as follows.

The above revised rates are being approved subject to the following conditions:
(1) The Dean/ Principal of the college shall ensure that all the teachers working in the concerned faculty and administrative posts in the institution are given academic load as per the standard before inviting the faculty/ expert candidates on hourly basis.

(2) The Dean and the Director of the Government Arts College concerned shall ensure that no remuneration in excess of the amount of the starting salary in the permissible pay scale of his grade shall be paid to a candidate on hourly basis in a single month.
(3) A retired teacher or an expert in the profession connected with the course concerned shall be appointed to teach on an hourly basis. " Back Door" in any position
" Such candidates should not be called for this purpose.
(4) The Arts College shall select the teachers through a local selection committee by giving an advertisement with the approval of the Director, Directorate of Arts. The selection committee shall consist of the Dean/ Principal of the concerned college, Head of the Department and an external subject expert.
(5) A temporary appointment of teachers on hourly basis may be made for a maximum period of 9 months in an academic year. The appointment must be approved by the Directorate of Arts. The appointment will be automatically terminated after the expiry of the aforesaid period of 9 months.
(6) Only two full-time faculty members may be appointed to a full-time position. A maximum of 9 hours can be assigned to a teacher.
(7) Appointment on an hourly basis is a temporary appointment and does not entitle to regular service. Hence, there will be no demand for continuance in service in future and no demand for any right to regular service.
Such a guarantee should be obtained from the concerned teacher on a stamp paper of Rs 100/-.
(8) Full-time teachers working in the college should not be given additional appointment on an hourly basis.
(9) If any irregularity is found in the actual workload and payment of honorarium on hourly basis, the principal/ dean of the concerned college will be fully responsible for it.
(10) These revised rates shall come into force from the date of issue of this Government Order.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the concurrence received as per the Finance Department's Informal Reference No. 301/2022/ Expenditure-5 dated 9th March, 2022.
