The State of Maharashtra has inherited the legacy of various glorious great personalities and the contribution of Sahityaratna Lokhatir Annabhau Sathe, Maharaja Sayajirao Gaekwad, Lokmanya Tilak and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in the ideological, social, educational and political fabric of Maharashtra is important. The Maharashtra Government has constituted Biographies of Great Men Publication Committees as per the relevant Government decisions with the objective of undertaking special action projects to create awareness about these great men who have inspired the progress of the state. These committees have been reconstituted. The member-secretaries of the publishing committees have to publish quality literature. The Member Secretary is also responsible for the collection, research, publication, etc. of literature/ information. Considering the nature, quality and importance of the work of the Biographical Instruments Publication Committee, the matter of sanctioning honorarium to the members and secretaries of the committees was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the aegis of the department, the Sahitya Ratna Lokhandir Annabhau Sathe, Maharaja Sayajirao Gaekwad, Lokmanya Tilak and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Charitra Sadhan Publication Committee
The Members-Secretaries are being approved to be paid an honorarium of Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand) subject to the condition that they publish at least 2 volumes in a year.
The Director, Directorate of Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune is being authorized as the Controlling Officer for the above expenditure. Also, Annabhau Sathe, Sahityaratna Loktantrik,
