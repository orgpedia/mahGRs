Introduction: Recognizing the importance of public libraries, the Government of Maharashtra has passed the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 to provide information to the people of rural and urban areas of the state on various aspects and subjects in the fields of social, economic, political, educational, information technology, agriculture, sports, art and entertainment etc. Under this Act, the work of spreading the reading/ library movement to the rural areas of the state by cultivating the reading habit among the people of the state and developing the public library system is being carried out by the government-owned public libraries. Since the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967 needs to be amended from time to time, to suggest amendments in the said Act, the above reference No. 1 Here the committee has been formed as per the decision of the new government. The government has also decided to amend the rules to implement the Act. Accordingly, the Government have decided to amend the Act. The extension of the term of the Committee constituted on 6th March, 2019 and the extension of the term of the Committee was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: To suggest amendments in the Maharashtra Public Libraries Act, 1967, Reference No. 1 the government's decision. The term of the Committee constituted on 6th March, 2019 is being extended by six months from the date of issue of the said Government Resolution.
