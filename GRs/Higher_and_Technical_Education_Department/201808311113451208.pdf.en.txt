Government Resolution No: NGC-2017/ (128/18)/ Mashi-4, dated 04th July, 2017.
The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 has come into force with effect from 01.03.2017. Keeping in view the increasing pass percentage in higher secondary schools and the inclination of students towards a particular course, so that no student is deprived of the desired higher education, Section 109 (8) of the Act provides for expeditious recognition of additional fresh batches of specified faculties in exceptional circumstances. In the academic year 2018-19, the non-agricultural universities of the state had submitted proposals to the government for additional new batches of specific faculties. The Government Resolution No. The inspection was carried out as per the norms prescribed under NGC-2017/ (128/18)/ Mashi-4, dated 04.07.2017. After the inspection, the government was considering to approve the proposals of new batches in addition to the specific faculties that qualified from the proposals recommended by the universities to the government in an expeditious manner. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision: In the academic year 2018-19, the non-agricultural universities of the state will be able to recruit new batches of eligible faculty from among the proposals recommended to the government under Section 109 (8) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016. The existing colleges are being sanctioned on permanent non-grant basis subject to the following terms and conditions as mentioned in the following statement subject to the terms and conditions laid down under the Government Decision No. 2:

Terms and Conditions: 1. The existing colleges in which new batches of grant-in-aid have been sanctioned so expeditiously shall give an undertaking to their Divisional Joint Directors that they will not demand any grant-in-aid in future. 100/- on non-judicial stamp paper.
The University concerned should not start the process of affiliation without the college submitting an undertaking or obtaining a certificate from the Joint Director of the department concerned.
The colleges are required to appoint faculty/ staff as per the norms prescribed by the concerned university and educational qualification prescribed by the University Grants Commission and provide all other necessary infrastructure and the same shall be ensured by the Joint Director of the Department and only then the aforesaid condition No. 2 certificate from the university.
Under no circumstances should the college admit more than the sanctioned intake capacity.
The University shall not proceed to grant affiliation to the colleges concerned unless the criteria prescribed for granting affiliation to the colleges in the University Grants Commission (Affiliation of Colleges by Universities) Regulations, 2009 are fulfilled.
6. The additional batch approved in this manner will continue with the natural growth.
However, if such a batch does not receive regular government recognition in the next academic year, the student will not be admitted to the first year in this batch. For such regular additional batch for the next academic year, it will be necessary to submit a fresh application and get government approval as per the criteria and procedures prescribed by the government.
7. Accelerated Additional batch If a student is not admitted in the batch for the academic year for which it is sanctioned and the batch is not started due to some other reasons, the recognition of such batch shall be deemed to be automatically cancelled.
It should be ensured that 50 per cent of the sanctioned strength of students is admitted in the fast track additional batch which has been sanctioned.
The students will be admitted to such fast-track additional batch only through the procedure prescribed by the Government for admission of students in the concerned faculty. Also, as per the prevailing policy of the government, the recognition of the additional fast track platoon will always be on a non-grant basis.
The colleges which have made temporary arrangements for the additional batch approved by the fast track, the colleges which have attached an undertaking with the application form that they will meet the criteria prescribed by the Government for the regular batch within the next 6 months, shall meet the criteria after the completion of the next 6 months.

The universities should send the compliance report regarding the affiliation to the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and the concerned Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education.
If the additional batches approved by the respective Universities and the Divisional Joint Director are found to have less than 50% of the sanctioned strength of students, after periodic review of the additional batches approved by the respective Universities and the Divisional Joint Director, the orders should be issued by the Divisional Joint Director with the knowledge of the Government and the University, considering the cancellation of the recognition of the additional batches.
