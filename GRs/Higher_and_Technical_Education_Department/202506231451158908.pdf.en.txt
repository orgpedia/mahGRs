Ministry Extension, Madam Kama Marg,

Date: June 23, 2025.
the Government Order
With the approval of the competent authorities as per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (ii) b4 (5) of the Maharashtra Prevention of Delay in discharge of Government Duties and Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees Act, 2005, officers of the Government Colleges / Institutions / Offices under the Directorate of Higher Education are being transferred and posted at the place indicated in Column No. 4 in front of their names in the corresponding prospectus.
2.The said orders shall come into force with immediate effect.
Mr. Vilas Kadam, Administrative Officer, No. 1 in the return accompanying this order, shall be admissible to join the place of transfer in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption, non-acceptance of service and Provisions for suspension, dismissal or removal from service) Rules, 1981, as per the rules in force. The rest of the officers will not be entitled to the tenure of office of the officers.
4. The Principal / Director / Head of Office concerned shall relieve the transferred officers forthwith to join the place of transfer. Also, the officers concerned should join the post immediately after their transfer from the office they are currently working in.
5. The Principal / Director / Head of Office concerned shall depute the transferred officers without any delay on their discharge from the respective colleges / institutions / offices. The Principal / Director / Head of Office concerned shall be held liable in case of non-working of salary and allowances due to non-receipt of attendance by the concerned Principal / Director / Head of Office. Also, no leave of absence will be granted for the period from discharge to joining the place of transfer.
6. The Director of Higher Education, Pune, shall forthwith submit to the Government an operational report on the transfer order.
