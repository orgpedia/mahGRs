Various organizations and individuals such as the All India Sahitya Parishad, Akhil Bharatiya Mahanubhav Sahitya Yuva Manch, Vishwa Mahanubhav Wasnik Parishad have demanded the establishment of a Marathi language university in the state.
in the 2023 budget session. The Deputy Chief Minister and Finance Minister presented the State Budget for the year 2023-24 in the Legislative Assembly on 9th March, 2023. A Marathi language university will be set up in Riddhpur, Amravati.
In the above background, the matter of forming a committee for the establishment of Marathi Language University in the state was under the consideration of the government.
The government has decided to set up a Marathi language university in the state.

The mandate of the committee is: 1) To recommend the location of the Marathi Language University, taking into account all the necessary land, construction-site requirement, eligibility.
2) To submit detailed information regarding the estimate of expenditure in connection with the establishment of the university, what should be the number of faculty and non-teaching staff, how much financial burden will be on the state.
3) Detailed plan of the various departments of the university and the general structure of the university.
4) To make recommendations as to how the students studying in the university will get employment opportunities along with education.
5) To present comparative data on the qualitative difference in the preservation of Marathi language due to the establishment of Marathi University, when the Marathi Language Department is functioning in the traditional university.
6) To examine the possibility of taking policy decisions to provide options for higher education in the mother tongue in the future and make recommendations on the necessary aspects, mechanisms and related recommendations for teaching courses like engineering, medicine in Marathi.

To make recommendations with a view to aligning the courses taught in the proposed University with the new National Education Policy of the Central Government/ University Grants Commission.
The Committee shall submit a report to the Government within two months with all inclusive details after consulting various experts and institutions on the above agenda items as well as on the matters which may be considered appropriate for the establishment of the University.
Non-official members of this committee will be entitled to travel allowance. The provision should be made from the funds available for the office expenses of the Joint Director, Higher Education, Amaravati Division, Amaravati.
