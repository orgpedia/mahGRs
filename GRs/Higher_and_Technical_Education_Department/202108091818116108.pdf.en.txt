(2) The period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to the assistant professors transferred on administrative grounds as per the rules of the government. As per the government rules, the transfer of assistant professors will not be allowed during the tenure of the post, travel allowance and daily allowance.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The principals of the respective colleges shall strictly follow the following instructions so that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
2.After receiving the transfer order, the assistant transferred by the concerned principal without waiting for the replacement of the transferred assistant professor.
The teachers should be relieved immediately to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned assistant professors should join the college immediately after their transfer from the existing college.
3.The transferred Assistant Professors should be recruited without delay after they are relieved from the concerned college and come to join. The Principal of the concerned college will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of an Assistant Professor.
4.No request for change in post will be entertained.
If any assistant professor directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, the concerned assistant professor will be liable for disciplinary action.
5. No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
Since the transfers are required to be effected with immediate effect, the salaries of the transferred Assistant Professors up to the date of retirement or till the maximum date of 31st August, 2021, shall be drawn by the college where they are presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the college before transfer under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the college after the transfer of the concerned assistant professor after joining the college of the transfer. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from September 01, 2021 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the college where they were working before the transfer.
