Introduction: Government College of Engineering, Pune has been granted autonomy. The Governing Body of the Institute in its 50 th meeting held on 11 th November, 2021 has approved the draft of the proposed Act for granting the status of Standalone Technical University to the" Government Engineering College, Pune (CoEP)." To examine the draft of this Act, under reference No. The Committee was constituted under the Government Resolution dated 2nd November, 2021. However, the preparation of a comprehensive Act will be facilitated as proposals for setting up a Technical University are likely to be received from other autonomous Government/ Government Aided Engineering Institutions like College of Engineering, Pune. Keeping this in view, the government was considering drafting a comprehensive law to set up a separate technical university for autonomous engineering colleges in the state. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Reference No. (2) The Government Decision dated 30th November, 2021 is hereby superseded.
A committee is being constituted to draft a comprehensive act for the establishment of a separate technical university for autonomous government/ aided engineering colleges in the state.

The mandate of the Committee shall be to draft an all inclusive Act to enact a separate Technical University Act for the autonomous and future autonomous Government/ Aided Engineering Colleges in the State on the basis of the draft Act approved by the Governing Body of the Government Engineering College, Pune.
To examine the draft of the proposed Act taking into consideration the provisions contained in the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016; Indian Institutes of Technology (IIT) Act; University Grants Commission Act, 1956; All India Council for Technical Education Act, 1987; Dr.
(a) Drafting of the proposed legislation
The committee will submit a revised draft of the Act along with its recommendations to the government by December 31, 2021.
(3) The work of the committee
The committee will be functioning at the office of the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai. The Director, Directorate of Technical Education shall make appropriate arrangements for the functioning of the Committee.
Meeting and Travel Allowances:
Retired officers to get Rs. 7000/- per sitting as sitting allowance and actual cost of travel will be admissible. The expenses will be met from the Personal Pension Account (PLA) of the Government Engineering College, Pune.
