As per the procedure laid down under the Kavikulaguru Kalidas Sanskrit Vishwavidyalaya, Ramtek, Act, 1997 and the relevant Government Decision, the Kavikulaguru Kalidas Sanskrit Vishwavidyalaya, Ramtek, Nagpur, has submitted its proposal to the Government for approval regarding the demand for new batches and courses in the existing colleges under expansion for the academic year 2021-22. The proposals have been scrutinized as per the norms laid down under the Government Resolution dated 12.10.2017.
On the recommendation of the University and on the basis of the criteria met at the Government level, the proposals for expansion of Kavikulaguru Kalidasa Sanskrit University, as per the powers conferred on the Government under Section 62 (5) of the Ramtek Act, 1997 and the proviso thereto, as mentioned in the Statement A below, are being approved on permanent non-grant basis subject to the terms and conditions thereunder.
