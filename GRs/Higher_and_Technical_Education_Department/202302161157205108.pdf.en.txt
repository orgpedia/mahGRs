Deccan College is one of the three oldest institutions in India, which came into existence on 06.10.1821 as Hindu College. The college was renamed as Deccan College on 15.11.1864. Deccan College is a post-graduate and research institution in the Deccan region of India. The government started on 17.08.1939. Subsequently, the Deccan College Poona Trust was formed as per the transfer deed and the ownership of the institute's property was handed over to the trust. The State Government has assured to provide the necessary funds for the development of Deccan College. The transfer was done through a High Court order. The institute is funded by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. It was granted deemed university status on 05.03.1990. This institution has completed 200 years. Considering the status of this prestigious institution with historical heritage, the work of basic research and teaching in the field of linguistics, as well as the work of basic research in the field of Maratha history and Sanskrit, the buildings of the university have been constructed more than 125 years ago. 1 needs to be renewed.
