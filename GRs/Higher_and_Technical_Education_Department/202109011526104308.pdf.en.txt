The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi has given its approval for introduction of new courses, increase/ decrease in intake capacity, closure of courses, extension of PIO/ NRI seats, change in the name of the ongoing course, etc. in the existing Government and Government aided Diploma Institutes in the State from the academic year 2021-22. Accordingly, the Director, Technical Education has submitted a proposal to the Government through his letter in the above context.
The government's decision:
In accordance with the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for starting new courses, increase/ decrease in intake capacity, closure of courses, extension of seats of PIO/ NRI, change in the name of the ongoing course, etc. in the existing Government and Government aided Diploma Institutes in the State from the academic year 2021-22, the institutions named in Annexure A to D as mentioned in the table below are being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions as indicated against their names.
