The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, Section 8 (8) provides that: The State Government may conduct regular test audit or complete audit of the University, College, Faculty or Institution within such period as it deems fit. Accordingly, the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune had invited proposals through e-tendering after determining the criteria, terms and conditions. The proposal is being examined by the Scrutiny Committee and to form a panel of Chartered Accountants. 1 submitted to the government. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the government to appoint a panel of auditors to audit all non-agricultural universities, affiliated colleges and autonomous institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education.
In accordance with the provisions of Section 8 (8) of the Maharashtra Universities Act, 2016, the following panel of auditors are being approved to audit all non-agricultural universities, affiliated colleges and autonomous institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education.

As per the provisions of Section 8 (8) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the state government will order an audit of all non-agricultural universities, affiliated colleges and autonomous institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, at which time they will have to pay the fees decided by negotiating with the said 03 firms regarding the fees and take appropriate action regarding the audit.
