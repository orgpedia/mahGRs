The government order:
The consolidated guidelines on the procedure to be followed for promotion of State Government officers/ employees have been issued vide Government Order dated 01.08.2019 of the General Administration Department. The Government has decided to constitute a Divisional Promotion Committee to recommend to the Appointing Authority for grant of date of promotion/ posting for the posts in Group A, whose grade pay is Rs. 7599/- or less as per the 6th Pay Commission.
Accordingly, the following Departmental Promotion Committee is being constituted to recommend to the appointing authority for grant of date of promotion/ posting for Group A posts (outside the purview of Establishment Board) in the Directorate of Arts, whose grade pay as per the 6th Pay Commission is Rs.
