Government Decision: Maharashtra National Vardhividyapeeth, Aurangabad has been given Rs. 12,04,00, 000/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Four Lakhs only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.
(1) The Executive Council shall be responsible for administering the grants sanctioned by the Government as per the Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014 by depositing them in the University Fund. Also, the university should take action to approve the expenditure incurred from the grant by exchanging the statement of accounts of the expenditure before the General Council.
(2) The Vice-Chancellor shall be responsible to see that the grant is spent for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned by the Government to the Maharashtra National Law University, Aurangabad. In this regard, they should submit its utility certificate to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education with the approval of the Executive Council.
(3) All the documents and accounts of all the above grants are to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as the case may be, and if so demanded, all the documents shall be submitted to him by the Law University for examination.
(4) The University shall use the grant-in-aid sanctioned for the purpose for which the grant-in-aid is sanctioned.
(5) The provisions and conditions of the Financial Powers Rules book shall be strictly complied with in this regard.
Also, the existing rules and conditions of the government should be taken into consideration.
The Director (Higher Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune shall be responsible for handing over the said approved provision to Maharashtra National Law University, Aurangabad immediately.
Further, all the documents and accounts of the above grant are entitled to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General-2, Nagpur on a case to case basis. The grant granted to the university should be used for the purpose for which it was sanctioned. Also, it should be ensured that the expenditure is fully met within the stipulated period and the utilization certificate should be submitted to the government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune within the prescribed time.
