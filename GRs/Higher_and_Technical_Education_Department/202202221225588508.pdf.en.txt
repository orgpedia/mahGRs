Pursuant to the approval given on 21.09.2020, the Director, Technical Education vide the aforesaid No. 2 here. College of Engineering of Dhamangaon Education Society, Dhamangaon Railway, G.Amaravati Institute of Vocational Course as proposed vide letter dated 02.09.2021
The decision to close the school for the academic year 2020-21 has been taken by the state government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, pursuant to the approval of All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the closure of Dhamangaon Education Society's College of Engineering, Dhamangaon Railway, G. Amravati Vocational Courses from the academic year 2020-21 is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
1) The institution will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the concerned university.
(2) The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters relating to the alternative arrangements for teachers and non-teaching staff appointed for the relevant courses in the institution as well as judicial matters in respect of service matters and other matters. In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.
3) If the government's recovery from the institution is still pending, it will be mandatory for the institution head to pay it.

(4) It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay the loans taken by it from the Government or any other financial institution in respect of the immovable property required for running the course in the Institute.
5) Students and alumni admitted to the institution at the time of its phased closure
The institution will take the responsibility of providing educational facilities and arranging for the examination till the completion of the course. If the institution does not provide these educational facilities, action will be taken against the institution as per the rules and the accumulated deposit with the government will be deposited by the government.
(6) It shall be obligatory on the part of the institution to pay the salary, salary compensation and other financial allowances payable as per rules to the teachers and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
