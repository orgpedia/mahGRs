GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION, NO. ANUDA-2011/ (200/11) RESTRICTION-6/ MASHI-4, dated 26th May, 2022.
The proposal:
It has been decided to sanction 100 per cent grant-in-aid to one college/ faculty in each taluka. Accordingly, under the Government decision dated 26/05/2022, a total of 18 colleges have been approved to bring the respective faculties on 100% grant subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government decision. Form-B, Roll No.1 of the said Government Resolution has approved a symbolic supplementary demand presented in the 4th (Winter) Session of December, 2022 for inclusion of the salary expenditure of teaching and non-teaching posts admissible to the Faculty of Science in the Vasantrao Naik College of Arts and Commerce, Mhasala, G. Raigad, mentioned herein. Accordingly, the matter of approving the inclusion of the salary expenses of these posts under the committed expenditure was under consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
As mentioned in the Preamble, the Government of Maharashtra hereby approves inclusion of the salary expenditure of the teacher/ non-teaching posts of 15 teachers and 05 non-teaching posts mentioned in the following statement to be admissible for the Science stream in the Vasantrao Naik College of Arts and Commerce, Mhasala, G. Raigad, subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the relevant Government Decision, dated 26/05/2022 and the following conditions.
