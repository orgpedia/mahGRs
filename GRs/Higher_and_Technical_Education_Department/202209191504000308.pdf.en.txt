Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University was established on 17th September 1994.
The university campus has 14 campuses, sub-campuses, 4 campuses at Latur and sub-campus at Parbhani and there are 357 colleges affiliated to the university. Due to lack of adequate facilities for research in the campus, sub-campuses and affiliated colleges of the university, research scholars and faculty members face difficulties. There is an urgent need for a Central Instrumentation Centre for Multidisciplinary Research and Innovation at Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University to provide impetus to interdisciplinary research. 1 is reported here. The centre will be equipped with state-of-the-art equipment. Such as electron microscope, Raman spectroscope, fluorescence spectroscope, density and sound speed meter, viscometer, refectometer, CADD software and fluorescent microscope, etc., these instruments are useful for industry, manufacturing, technology sector along with research students. The centre will be used to provide quick and low-cost access to samples of various substances under research. Accordingly, budgetary approvals have been submitted for purchase of various equipment. Administrative approval for the procurement process was under consideration in pursuance of this proposal.
