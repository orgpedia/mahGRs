2.10 of APH (Approval Process Handbook) 2020-21 published by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi provides for merger of institutes running under the same management. The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi has approved the merger of Diploma and Degree courses in Engineering from Vilasrao Deshmukh Foundation Group of Institutions, Latur. Accordingly, the Director, Technical Education vide separate letter dated 11/09/2020 at No.2 mentioned above has requested the Government to approve the merger of the Institute.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the merger of the institutions as mentioned in the table below is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions:
