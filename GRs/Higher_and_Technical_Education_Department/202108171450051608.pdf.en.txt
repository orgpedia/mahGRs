Preamble: As per the notification dated 14.01.2019 at Reference No.1," The Maharashtra Public Universities (Standards on Grant of Autonomy to Affiliated Colleges, Recognized Ecosystems and Operating or Constituent Colleges) Consolidated Statutes, 2018" has been notified. Rule 12 of the said Statute provides for and prescribes the constitution of Statutory Boards and Committees in the Autonomous Colleges to ensure proper management of academic, financial and general administrative affairs of the Autonomous Colleges.
The University Grants Commission has granted academic autonomy to the Government Institute of Science, Nagpur for the academic year 2021-22 to 2030-31. Accordingly, Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University has also granted academic autonomy to Government Institute of Science, Nagpur for the academic year 2021-22 to 2030-31 as per the notification at Reference No.3.
Since the University Grants Commission and Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University have granted academic autonomy to the Government Science Institute, Nagpur, the matter of appointment of the Chairman and Members on the Governing Board of the Government Science Institute, Nagpur by nomination was under consideration of the Government as per Rule 12 (3) (ii) of the Maharashtra Public University (Standards on Grant of Autonomy to Affiliated Colleges, Recognized Ecosystems and Operating or Constituent Colleges) Consolidated Statutes, 2018.
