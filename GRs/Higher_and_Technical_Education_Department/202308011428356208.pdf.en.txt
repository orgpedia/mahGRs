The institute will be required to submit all the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institute, such as marksheets, answer sheets, result sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education.
The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters which are sub judice in respect of service and other matters of the faculty/ staff appointed for the concerned course. In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.
3. It shall be incumbent upon the head of the institution to pay the arrears, if any, due to the Government from the institution.
4. The Institute shall be fully responsible for repayment of any loan taken by it from the Government or any financial institution in respect of immovable property required for the conduct of the said course in the Institute.
After the closure of the relevant courses in the institution, it will be mandatory for the institution to make alternative arrangements for the admitted students and former students of the institution if they want to sit for the examination.
The Institute shall be obliged to pay the salary, salary compensation and other financial payments due to the teaching and non-teaching staff of the Institute appointed for the respective courses.
