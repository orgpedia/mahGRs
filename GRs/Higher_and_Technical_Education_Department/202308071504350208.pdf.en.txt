Government Resolution- As per the provision of Section 76 (1) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the" Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission has been constituted by the Government Resolution dated 10/12/2021.
As per the provisions of Section 76 (2) (j) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the posts of Members on the" Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission" shall be filled by the Hon'ble Members of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. Nominated by the President, Mr. Sunil Shelke, Member of Parliament, Shri. Member of Legislative Assembly, Shri Shirish Chaudhary is being appointed.
As per the provisions of Section 76 (2) (j) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the posts of members on the Maharashtra State Higher Education and Development Commission shall be filled by the members of the Maharashtra Legislative Council. Nominated by the Chairman, Mr. Satish Chavan, Member of Legislative Council. Rajesh Rathod has been elected as a member of the Legislative Council.
