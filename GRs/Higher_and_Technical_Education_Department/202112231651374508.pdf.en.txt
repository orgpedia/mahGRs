The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi vide its Notification dated 2.08.2021 has approved the increase in the intake capacity of Diploma Courses in the existing Government Aided Institutions viz., K. J. Somaiya Technological University, Vidyanagar, Vidyavihar (East), Mumbai from the academic year 2021-22. Accordingly, Director, Technical Education, Shri. vide letter dated 26.08.2021, a proposal has been submitted to the Government to increase the intake capacity of diploma courses in K. J. Somaiya Technological University, Vidyanagar, Vidyavihar (East), Mumbai on non-subsidized basis. The following decisions have been taken:
Government Decision: The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has given the above decision. In accordance with the approval given vide Corrigendum dated 02.08.2021, the Government is granting approval to extend the eligibility for admission to Diploma Courses in K. J. Somaiya Technological University, Vidyanagar, Vidyavihar (East), Mumbai on permanent non-grant basis from the academic year 2021-22, as mentioned in the table below, subject to the following terms and conditions:

7. The institution shall take action as per rules for obtaining approval of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for extension of the said courses.
8. The institution concerned shall complete other legal formalities by depositing the requisite processing fee and accumulated deposits in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai.
Admission to the said Professional Diploma Course shall be made as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the respective courses approved by the Government.
10.If the fees charged by the institutions concerned exceed the tuition fees prescribed by the School Regulatory Authority, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
11.The Institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per Government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
12.The library in the institution is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education.
