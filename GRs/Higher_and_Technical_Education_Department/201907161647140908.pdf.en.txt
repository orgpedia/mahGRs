the prelude
The e-Governance Policy of the Government of Maharashtra has been notified vide Government Resolution dated 23rd September, 2011 of the General Administration Department. Paragraph 12 of the said Government Decision contains provisions relating to access to information technology products and services. Accordingly, a Project Implementation Committee needs to be set up to oversee e-governance projects. Accordingly, a Project Implementation Committee for procurement of software/ hardware and implementation of e-governance policies for the Directorate of Arts, Government Arts Colleges and Regional Offices was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following decision was taken.
Government's decision
Project Implementation Committee is being constituted to implement the project of computerization of Directorate of Arts, Government Arts Colleges and Regional Offices, to provide citizen centric e-services and to monitor the e-governance projects under e-Governance Policy.
