Preamble: - The National Education Policy, 2020 has been implemented for undergraduate and postgraduate courses in autonomous colleges from the academic year 2023-24 and in other colleges affiliated to public universities from the academic year 2024-25. In order to effectively implement this educational policy, colleges are required to fill vacancies in the Assistant Professor cadre, taking into account the directions issued by the University Grants Commission from time to time regarding recruitment.

As per the Government Decision, Higher B. Technical Education Department dated 03.11.2018 and dated 12.11.2021, the Government has approved the filling up of 3580 vacancies (40 of total vacancies) in the category of Assistant Professor as permissible considering the workload calculated on the basis of the student strength as on 01.10.2017 in any unaided college affiliated to the Directorate of Higher Education. Accordingly, the recruitment process has been initiated. After this approval, the posts in the Assistant Professor cadre are lying vacant on a large scale due to retirement, death, voluntary retirement, resignation, etc. Therefore, the proposal for filling up the posts of RAD in the Assistant Professor cadre was submitted to the Sub-Committee headed by the Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department.
As per the approval of the Sub-Committee to this proposal, the matter of filling up the vacancies in the Assistant Professor cadre in any aided college was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: - The recruitment of 5012 vacancies out of the total vacancies in the Assistant Professor cadre is being approved as per the workload calculated as on date 01.10.2025 in the number of students in any of the recognized Unaided Colleges under the Directorate of Higher Education affiliated to a Public University in the State.
2. The acceptance of recruitment to the above post shall be subject to the following terms and conditions.
It should be ensured that the workload is determined according to the number of students admitted to the college in 2025 and certified by the university and the posts are distributed equally among the vacancies and according to the subject. In this regard, the Director, Higher Education should submit a detailed proposal regarding deletion of sanctioned posts of Assistant Professors after thorough examination for approval.
The recruitment process should be completed within this time frame by fixing the time limit for the recruitment. Further, the Director, Higher Education, Pune B, concerned Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education, will be responsible for the transparent implementation of the recruitment process as per the prescribed procedure, within the prescribed time frame.

The Director, Higher Education, Pune and the concerned Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education shall be solely responsible for ensuring that there is no irregularity / malpractice in the recruitment.
The recruitment process should be carried out transparently from the qualified candidates only by following the guidelines issued by the University Grants Commission from time to time as well as the criteria laid down for faculty positions.
The posts exceeding the said limit shall not be filled under any circumstances.
The Director, Higher Education, Pune and the concerned Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education shall be responsible for strictly following the instructions of the State Election Commission regarding the Model Code of Conduct, if applicable in the State.
Steps should be taken immediately to fix the workload by appointing a committee at the level of Director, Higher Education, Pune to fix the workload in line with the New Education Policy and accordingly approve the revised format. Apart from this, the recruitment of the remaining vacancies will not be approved.
No new posts shall be created for an educational institution without the approval of the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Designations should be proposed in proportion to the B attendance of the student body.
2.9 The Director, Higher Education, Pune shall examine and submit a proposal to the Government for formulation of a policy / scheme for grant of grace period of 3 years to the colleges which are under rated by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) to give them an opportunity to enhance the assessment.
