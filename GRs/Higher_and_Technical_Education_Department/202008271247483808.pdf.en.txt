The proposal:
The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi has approved the implementation of Diploma in Engineering courses in the second term in private unaided institutions in the State, which were approved as second-term courses during the academic year 2019-20 and earlier, as regular courses from the academic year 2020-21, based on the decision of the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. In pursuance of this approval, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has submitted a proposal to the Government for grant of Government approval under the aforesaid Reference No. 2.
The government's decision:
The Government is granting recognition to the Engineering Diploma Courses running in the second semester in the Private Unaided Institutions in the State which were approved as Second Term Courses during the academic year 2019-20 and earlier, to be made functional as Regular Courses from the academic year 2020-21, based on the decision of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) Admission to the course is based on the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) approved merit list.
