Preamble: The National Service Scheme Office under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Ministry of Higher and Technical Education, Government of Maharashtra is implementing various activities and programs for the students participating in the Regional Units at the level of 39 Non-Agricultural Universities, Agricultural Universities, Deemed Universities and Private Universities/ Institutions and from the Subordinate Colleges in the State. Accordingly, the university-wise programme coordinators of the state and the programme officers and volunteers of the Raseo Unit College under them are recognized for their work and awarded.
Substantial provision of Rs. 7.05 crore has been approved in the budget for the year 2022-2023 under the target of 05-Business for RASEO. Under the said provision, the Government was contemplating to constitute a Financial Planning Committee for the formulation of various activities and programmes of the RASEO, for the formulation and planning of various activities and programmes, for the formulation of possible expenditures etc.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: RASEO Economic Planning Committee is being constituted as mentioned below to plan various activities and programmes under RASEO

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows: (9) Substantial provision has been made for the various activities and programmes of the National Service Scheme under the Department of Higher and Technical Education and accordingly the Committee shall plan and recommend the programmes to the Government at the Government level, at the University level and at the District level.
(2) The Central Government and the State Government shall, after considering and harmonizing the suggestions issued from time to time for the implementation of this scheme, discuss and recommend various positive aspects in a better manner.
(3) It shall also prepare a proposal after planning the financial aspects to be taken up at various stages in accordance with the said decision.
Duration of the Committee: The Committee shall submit its report to the Government within two months.
The daily allowance and travel allowance payable to the government officials appointed as members of the above committee as well as to the persons in the government-funded educational institutions as per their designation from their parent institution shall not be admissible to them as per this order. All non-official members will be entitled to a sitting allowance of Rs.250/- per sitting as per the Finance Department, Government Resolution No. 2012/ No. 1/ Service-5, dated 24th May, 2012, which is admissible to the State Level/ Departmental Non-Constitutional Committee.
