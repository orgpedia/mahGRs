Maharshi Dhondo Keshav Karve founded the Shrimati Nathibai Damodar Thackersey Women's University (the first women's university in the country) in 1916 to bring women into the mainstream of education in Maharashtra. Through this university, different faculties were started for women and started providing higher education to women. Today, the university's affiliated colleges are spread across seven other states, including Maharashtra.
