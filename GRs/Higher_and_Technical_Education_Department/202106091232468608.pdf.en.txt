2) Government Order, Department of Higher and Technical Education, No.Saseni-2020/ PR No. (98/20)/ Tanshi-1, Dy. 07.01.2021.
Mr. Jadhav Maruti Rajaram and Mr. Madavi Shrikant Kavadu have been appointed as Assistant Secretary (Technical), Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Maharashtra Engineering Administrative Service, Group-A, by the Government Order No. 2 under reference, at Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Divisional Office, Pune and Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai respectively. The above appointment order states that the person should join the place of appointment within one month from the date of issue of the order of appointment, otherwise the order of appointment will be cancelled after one month.
The appointment of Mr. Jadhav Maruti Rajaram and Mr. Madavi Shrikant Kavadu as Assistant Secretary (Technical), Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Maharashtra Engineering Administrative Service, Group A has been cancelled by this order.
The said Order, Government Resolution, General Administration Department No. SRV-2018/ VIP67/ Q. No.207/ Works.12, Dy. Issued under the provisions of 28/05/2018 and 26/09/2018 and with the approval of the competent authority.
