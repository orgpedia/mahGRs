The Maharashtra government has decided to reserve seats in all non-agriculture universities in the state for the socially and educationally backward classes (SEBC), including the Maratha community, and economically weaker sections in the open category.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Circular No. Parichay-2019/ PR No. 22/ Vishy-3
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Vistar Bhawan, Mumbai-400 032.
The date: March 8, 2019.
Reference: 1) Maharashtra Act No. 62 of 2018 of the Department of Law and Justice, d. 30th November, 2018.
2) General Administration Department Government Resolution No. 4019/ No. 31/16-A, D. February 12, 2019.
Preamble: An Act to provide for the reservation of seats for admission to educational institutions in the State and for the reservation of appointments and posts in the public services under the control of the State and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto for the advancement of the socially and educationally backward classes of citizens (SEBC) in the State of Maharashtra. It was published on November 30, 2018. Issue No. Under Section 4 (1) (a), a total of 16% of the seats for admission in aided and unaided educational institutions in the state have been reserved for the socially and educationally backward (SEBC) category of citizens, including the Maratha community.
Further, as per the Government decision of Reference No. 2, orders have been issued to reserve 10% seats for admission in Government services and educational institutions for the economically weaker sections in the open category in the State of Maharashtra.
Also, the decision No. Under Section 4, it has been ordered that the necessary orders regarding admission in educational institutions should be issued by all the concerned departments immediately.
The General Administration Department was under the consideration of the Government to issue a circular in this regard to direct all public non-agricultural universities under this department to reserve 16% seats for the Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) including the Maratha community and 10% seats for the economically weaker sections in the open category for admission in educational institutions in the State of Maharashtra as directed under the above Government decision.

Government Circular No: Parichay-2019/ PR No.22/ Vishy-3
Government Circular: In accordance with the above Reference No. 1 and 2, 16% seats should be reserved for Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) including Maratha community and 10% seats for Economically Weaker Sections in the open category for admission to educational institutions in all public non-agricultural universities in the state of Maharashtra.
The reservation in admission to educational institutions prescribed by the government will be applicable to all non-agricultural universities, deemed universities, law universities, self-financing universities, all aided and unaided colleges and autonomous colleges affiliated to them, except minority educational institutions under the Department of Higher and Technical Education in the state. It will be the responsibility of the concerned universities to ensure effective implementation of this order.
(3) If the universities/ institutions have framed rules/ regulations for admission to educational institutions and reservations of various social categories have been indicated in them, the necessary amendments in the rules/ regulations in accordance with this reservation should be made immediately by the concerned in the prescribed procedure.
4. All Universities are hereby informed to bring this circular to the notice of all educational institutions under them.
