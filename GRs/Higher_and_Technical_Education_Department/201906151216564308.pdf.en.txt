Terms and Conditions: 1. The existing colleges in which new faculty have been sanctioned shall submit an undertaking to their Divisional Joint Directors that they will not demand grant-in-aid under any circumstances in future. 100/- on non-judicial stamp paper.
The University concerned should not start the process of affiliation without the college submitting an undertaking or obtaining a certificate from the Joint Director of the department concerned.
The colleges are required to appoint faculty/ staff as per the norms prescribed by the concerned university and educational qualification prescribed by the University Grants Commission and provide all other necessary infrastructure and the same shall be ensured by the Joint Director of the Department by 30th August and only after that the aforesaid condition no. 2 certificate from the university.
Under no circumstances should the college admit more than the sanctioned intake capacity.
The University shall not proceed to grant affiliation to the colleges concerned unless the criteria prescribed for granting affiliation to the colleges in the University Grants Commission (Affiliation of Colleges by Universities) Regulations, 2009 are fulfilled.
6. The concerned Universities/ Colleges of the above prospectus may follow up for conducting case wise NAAC assessment/ re-evaluation.
3. Universities shall not initiate the process of granting affiliation without first going through the Government approval orders and obtaining the certificate in Condition No. 2 of the Divisional Joint Director before taking necessary further action on the issue of affiliation. The universities should send the compliance report regarding the affiliation to the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and the concerned Divisional Joint Director, Higher Education.
Periodic review of the faculties approved by the respective universities and the departmental joint director and if 50% or less of the approved number of students are found in those faculties, the recognition of those faculties will be considered canceled.
