The date is November 21, 2022.
Government Circular: As per the provisions of the Department of Education Gazetted Officer (Under Director of Education) Departmental Examination Rules, 1979, a one-week training programme for the year 2022 departmental examination of Junior Administrative Officer/ Superintendent/ Manager of Group-B cadre of the Superintendent, Maharashtra Education Service, who is appointed by direct service, shall be held for two days in the first week of December, 2022 and two papers on one day in the first week of January, 2023
Applications are invited from eligible candidates for the first time for this training and examination.
The circular should be immediately brought to the notice of all concerned Superintendents, Maharashtra Education Service, Group-B officers (including Junior Administrative Officers/ Superintendents/ Managers) (on deputation/ leave) and their signatures should be taken and the documents should be preserved in the archives. The concerned officers will be able to take advantage of the training classes of this departmental examination only once.
It should be pointed out. The Directorate of Higher Education will be responsible for this.
There are 4 question papers of 100 marks for this departmental examination, which is linked to the syllabus.
4. The pattern of application to be submitted for the said training and examination shall be as per the accompanying Form A. For the first time in the year 2022, the Departmental Examination of the Junior Administrative Officer/ Superintendent/ Manager in the Directorate of Higher Education and Subordinate Offices J Superintendent, Maharashtra Education Service, Group-B Cadre
The Directorate of Higher Education shall certify the applications in the prescribed format of the officers who are eligible to sit and who have not yet been trained and submit them to the Government by 25 th November, 2022.
Applicants must fill in the application form with their full name, address, date of birth and address. The Directorate of Higher Education should ensure that the information filled by the candidate in this application is correct.
The training for the said Departmental Examination is scheduled to be held in the first week of the month of December, 2022 and the Departmental Examination is scheduled to be held in the first week of January, 2023.
