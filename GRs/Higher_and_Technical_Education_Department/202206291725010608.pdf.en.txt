Introduction: The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India has implemented the National Education Policy-2020. Therefore, in order to implement the National Education Policy-2020 in the state, the policy will be studied and a report will be submitted to the state government. As per the Government decision dated 16th October, 2020, a Task Force Group was constituted under the Chairmanship of Dr. Raghunath Mashelkar, former Director General, Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, New Delhi. The committee sent its report to the government. It was released on June 30, 2021. In order to facilitate the implementation of the policy in the State, the recommendations of the report have been categorized into three phases, taking into consideration factors such as their urgency, impact, ease of implementation, investment and funding requirement, priority in action, as follows: (i) Programme to be taken up immediately/ requiring minimum resources; (ii) Programme to be taken up in the medium term/ requiring medium resources; (iii) Programme to be taken up in the long term/ requiring large scale funding.
