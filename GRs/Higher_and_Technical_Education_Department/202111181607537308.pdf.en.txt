The salary of these candidates should be fixed as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981.
10) The retirement age of the above candidates will be as per the rules of the government.
11) The above candidates will not be given travelling allowance to attend the place of appointment.
12) If the above-mentioned candidates want to resign from the government service, then it should be sent to the Government Circular, General Administration Department No. SRV-1092/ 1033/ Q.No.33/ 92/8, Dy. As per the provision dated 02/12/1997, the government will be required to give at least one month's prior notice or one month's salary.
13) As per the provision of educational qualification required for the said post, the candidate must hold at least first class at the graduation level. However, as per the UGC and AICTE policy, some universities/ autonomous bodies award grades to candidates instead of grades. The concerned Head of Office shall ensure that the degree of such candidates is first class or equivalent as per the provisions of the Service Admission Rules.
14) The appointment will depend on the authenticity of the certificates submitted by the candidates. If the certificate is found to be false or forged after the appointment, the appointment of these candidates will be automatically terminated.
15) As soon as the order is received, the concerned candidates should appear at the place of appointment and submit a report to the concerned office head. The head of the office should submit a report to the government through the director, Directorate of Technical Education.
