Preamble: In pursuance of the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for the academic year 2019-20, the Director, Technical Education vide his letter dated 24/6/2019 mentioned above has proposed to the Government to approve the merger of the direct second year batch of the existing Engineering, Technology/ MCA course in the first year and the merger of the course in the second year. It was pending approval of the government.
The government's decision:
In accordance with the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, the Government has approved the merger of the second year batch directly in the first year of the institutions mentioned in the accompanying form, as well as the merger of the course into the second course. The accessibility indicated in the accompanying form of this Government Decision is not independent. included in the Governing Decision dated June 10, 2019. For this, the terms and conditions of the government decision dated June 10, 2019 will remain in force.
Other terms and conditions as laid down by the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and the University concerned shall also apply.
