Preamble: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to fix the posts in the government service for persons with disabilities and to review the seats ensured for persons with disabilities at regular intervals of not more than three years. Accordingly, the Central Govt. As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities has been prepared and published on the website www.disabilityafairs.gov.in. Also, instructions to ensure posts for the disabled in the state government establishments have been issued by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Here are the circulars dated February 2, 2021. Considering this, the matter of ensuring posts for the disabled in Group A, B, C and D cadres on the establishment of Directorate of Arts and Regional Offices was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:

GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the provisions of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Central Government, vide notification dated 4th January, 2021, has prepared and published the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities on the website www.disabilityafairs.gov.in. In comparison to the said list, for the posts to be filled by direct recruitment in the cadre of Group-" A" to Group-" D" under the Directorate of Arts under the Department of Higher and Technical Education (taking into account similar posts in the Central Government list as well as similar responsibilities and duties), the posts have been earmarked for persons with disabilities as shown in the table attached. These orders are being withdrawn by superseding the earlier order dated 14.01.2011 issued in this regard.
This decision was taken by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance Circular No. Divyang-2019/ S.No.251/ D. C. 2d. being issued as per the directions issued under 2.2.2021.
