Read: 1) Government Decision, Department of Higher B. Technical Education, No.Judicial-2023 / Q.No.157 / MASHI-4, dated 20.08.2024
The Proposal:
Subject to the terms and conditions, the Government decision dated 20.08.2024 under reference has approved the grant-in-aid to Shri Ankarnath Malpani Law College, Sangamner District Ahmednagar, which is run by Education Broadcasting Institute, Sangamner.
Accordingly, a symbolic Supplementary Demand Resolution was introduced in the Budget Session of March, 2025 to include the salaries of a total of 19 teaching and 8 non-teaching posts in the college, which would be admissible for a 3-year B-5-year law course, under the Commitment Expenditure and to bring to the notice of the Legislature the expenditure incurred on payment of arrears of salaries of the posts filled up from the date mentioned in the relevant Government Decision. The figurative supplemental demand was accepted and the government was considering approving the expenditure.
Government Decision: By the relevant Government Decision, the salaries of a total of 19 posts of 19 teachers and 8 non-teachers, which are admissible for 3 years B 5 years Law course in Shri Ankarnath Malpani Law College, Sangamner District Ahmednagar, run by Education Broadcasting Institute, Sangamner, are being approved to be included under the Commitment Expenditure, subject to the following conditions. The cost of salary and allowances for these posts is being allowed as per the Government Decision in reference as 25 per cent of the cost sanctioned from the date 18.07.2017, 50 per cent of the cost sanctioned from the date 18.07.2018, 75 per cent of the cost sanctioned from the date 18.07.2019 and 100% of the cost sanctioned from the date 18.07.2020.

Conditions: -
1. Referral before payment of salary. Pay the salary subsidy thereafter by complying with the terms and conditions in the Government Decision dated 20.08.2024.
If the concerned Divisional Joint Director is found to have paid the salary without checking the criteria in the relevant Government decision, action should be taken against him.
3. The approval of the Government in the prescribed manner should be obtained for filling up the vacancies. Apart from this, vacancies should not be filled.
4. The Director shall be responsible for ensuring that the institution complies with the terms and conditions laid down by the Government from time to time while disbursing the grants.
