As per the decision taken in the meeting, the academic management of the Sansthan Peeth for running various certificate/ diploma/ degree/ post-graduate courses related to Indian tradition and culture, Sant Sampradaya, Sant Sahitya, Kirtan, Pravachan, Philosophy in Paithan Sansthan Peeth is being handed over to Dr.Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad for a period of 5 years or until further orders, whichever is earlier and the approval to operationalize the Sansthan Peeth is subject to the following conditions:
