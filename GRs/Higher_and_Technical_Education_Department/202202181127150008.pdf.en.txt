Introduction: Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik University was established by the Maharashtra Legislative Council Act No. 20 respectively. 01.07.1989. The University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has jurisdiction over the entire state of Maharashtra. The university has 8 regional centres located at Amravati, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Mumbai, Nashik, Pune, Kolhapur and Nanded. 1600 study centers are controlled by 8 departmental centers of the university. About 6 million students study at the university. In view of the curriculum being implemented by the university, various activities, efforts being made by the university to provide higher education to all the deprived sections of the society, etc., it was proposed to establish a sub-center of the university for Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg districts under Kolhapur division with the aim of controlling the study centers of the university and to provide study materials in the form of books to the students taking admission in various courses in the university.
For this sub-centre, the B.Ed college is located in the south-north corner of the boarding area. In Survey No. 259/1 (c. AD 860.860/1 to. Area in 860/6 4.3800 Hrs. The plot area near the water tank in Block D of this revenue is 5,000 sq. A place has been provided. The Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Library and Research Centre will be constructed at a cost of Rs. 1239.43 lakh has been accorded administrative approval as per the decision of the Government in this regard. Now, the expenditure for the construction of the building for the establishment of the University's Sub-Centre and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Library and Research Center has been approved by the Supplementary Demand for the year 2021-22 in the December, 2021 session of the Legislative Assembly. 86,95,000/- (Rupees Sixty Eight Lakh Ninety Five Thousand only) was under consideration of the Government.
