Introduction: The father of Jal Kranti, late Shankarraoji Chavan, former Chief Minister of Maharashtra. His birthday is July 14, 1920. from July 15, 2019. As of 14th July 2020, his birth centenary will be celebrated through various programmes to commemorate his work. 5 The decision has been approved by the government. Accordingly, the concerned administrative departments have proposed to take up various programmes to mark the birth centenary of late Shankarraoji Chavan, former Chief Minister. It has been proposed to set up a library, competition examination guidance centre and hostel for boys and girls in Nanded city in memory of former Chief Minister late Shankarrao Chavan. In such a campus, bright and meritorious students of rural areas of Nanded district and adjoining districts will be able to get expert guidance at a very nominal fee, so that the students of rural areas can enter the administrative service in the future and be available for the service of the country.
Accordingly, the government was considering granting administrative approval for the construction of Dr. Shankarrao Chavan Study Complex in the premises of Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded.
