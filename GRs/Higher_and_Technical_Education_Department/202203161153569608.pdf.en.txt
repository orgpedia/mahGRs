The Government has decided to rename the proposed Music College as" Bharatratna Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar International Music College" on 9 th February, 2022.
In order to expedite the process of setting up of Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Museum, the Government was contemplating to constitute an Expert Advisory Committee to discuss with all the members constituted under the above decision dated 28th January, 2021 and submit a report to the Government.
The following decision has been taken:
Government Decision: An Expert Advisory Committee consisting of the following members is being constituted for the establishment of" Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Museum."
