the order
Shri Harivijay Ganpatrao Shinde, Administrative Officer (presently Joint Director, Education Fee Committee, Mumbai) is being approved for appointment as Administrative Officer, Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority on deputation for a period of 3 years with effect from the date he assumes charge of his office, subject to the following terms and conditions:
1. Mr. Harivijay Shinde, the service of the deputation shall commence on the date on which the Administrative Officer enters upon the office of the deputation and the service shall end on the date on which he resumes the office of his Administrative Officer, RANA.
If their services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the Government / Competent Authority shall have the power to recall them at any time before the expiry of the period of deputation.
3.If their appointment is not deemed necessary by the non-self, they will be allowed to be sent to the parent department without the consent of the Mace. Provided that the non-commissioned officer shall give three months' notice to the Government / Competent Authority before such repatriation; and
They will be allowed to return to the parent department after giving at least three months' written notice to the government / competent authority that they intend to approach the parent department.
Terms and Conditions regarding the deputation of Shri Harivijay Shinde having regard to the terms and conditions laid down in Rule 40 (Annexure-II) of the MNS (Period of assumption of office, non-self-service and period of suspension, dismissal, etc.) Rules, 1981, in consultation with the Department of Higher and Technical Education and the Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority, Mumbai.
to decide.
3. All other administrative matters of Mr. Harivijay Shinde shall be handled by the Department of Higher and Technical Education.
