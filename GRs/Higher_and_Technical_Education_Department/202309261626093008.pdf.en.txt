the prelude
The Government has conferred powers under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Divisions 1 to 5, to the Ministerial Departments of the Government and the Department Heads, Regional Office Heads and Office Heads under their control, subject to the prescribed limits of financial powers, terms and conditions, as provided in the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959, Maharashtra Contingent Expenditure Rules, 1965, Maharashtra Budget Rule Book, Composite Matters of Expenditure and Maharashtra Public Works Rules. At present, these powers are being exercised by the Additional Chief Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary as the head of the administrative department. The Financial Powers Rulebook provides that certain powers of financial powers conferred on the Head of the Department may be reassigned to other Gazetted Officers in the office and the Deputy Secretary/ Joint Secretary concerned. Accordingly, the matter of delegation of financial powers to the department for the purpose of ease of doing business was under consideration.
Government's decision
The powers conferred on the Administrative Department in the Financial Powers Rules Book in respect of the matter mentioned in the Annexure annexed to this Government Resolution are set out in Annexure No. 7 are being re-submitted to the Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Registration Branch) for his own office as indicated in the Annexure. The authorities concerned shall consider the terms, conditions and conditions of the financial powers conferred on them and settle the proposals for financial sanction at their level.
After this revision, the relevant government decided. The powers conferred on" Head of Department,"" Head of Regional Office" and" Head of Office" as on 17.04.2015 shall remain unaffected.
