(2) The period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to the Head of the Department transferred for administrative reasons as per the rules of the Government. Period of appointment, travel allowance and daily allowance will not be admissible to the Head of the Department who is transferred on request.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) This order shall take effect immediately.
(2) On receipt of the transfer order, the Head of the Department concerned shall immediately transfer the person from the existing institution to the institution of the next posting. Also, all the heads of the institutions should relieve the transferred department heads immediately without waiting for the appearance of the alternate department head.

3) The transferred head of the department should be contacted without delay after he is relieved from his duties by the concerned organization. In case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-appointment of a Head of Department, the Principal of the institution concerned will be held responsible.
(4) No request for change in post will be entertained. Any officer who directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order shall be liable for disciplinary action.
5) No leave of the transferred department head should be sanctioned without prior approval of the administrative department.
6) The responsibility of relieving the transferred department heads immediately will be personally of the head of the concerned institution. This should be followed up by the concerned Divisional Joint Director, and the Director, Directorate of Technical Education should inform this department without fail within one month from the date of this order.
