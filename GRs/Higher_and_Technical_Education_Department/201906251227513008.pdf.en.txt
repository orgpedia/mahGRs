Preamble: All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has approved the introduction of B. Vocational degree course under NSQF in 14 existing institutions.
Regarding grant of Government recognition to 14 institutions recognized by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Director referred to Reference No. The proposal has been submitted to the government through a letter here.
A decision in this regard was pending with the government.
Government Decision: In pursuance of the approval given by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, 11 Institutions as mentioned in the Annexure attached are being given Government approval to start B. Vocational courses as indicated against their names from the academic year 2019-2020 subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
(ii) B.Voc. degree by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi under Community College Scheme.
(NSQF Level VII) It will be mandatory for the concerned institution and skill knowledge provider to comply with the special rules, terms and conditions created for the implementation of the degree course.

3) For the affiliation of the said course, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
Admission to the said course shall be made at the Institute level as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi in its Approval Process Handbook 2019-2020.

6) Since this course is being implemented under the Community College Scheme and the admission of students is being done at the institution level, the students admitted to this course will be given Rajarshi.
The benefit of Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme will not be admissible.
