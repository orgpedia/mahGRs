The proposal:
The MahaDBT portal, developed by the State Government to deliver the benefits of government schemes directly to the beneficiaries, has been operationalized in the month of August, 2018 and through this portal, the process of inviting applications for scholarships from eligible students, scrutinizing the applications from the college and department level, providing allotment for payment of scholarships to the approved students, disbursement of scholarships in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the approved student through PFMS system, etc. is done through MahaIT and MahaOnline. It was under consideration of the Government to approve the expenditure incurred by MahaIT for the services rendered during the period from August, 2019 to July, 2020 to provide the benefits of all the scholarship schemes under the Directorate of Higher Education directly to the beneficiaries through the MahaDBT portal.
The government's decision:
In order to provide the benefits of the scholarship schemes implemented by the Directorate of Higher Education directly to the beneficiaries through the MahaDBT portal, MahaIT has released Rs. 83,40,315/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakhs Forty Three Hundred and Fifteen only) is being approved.
