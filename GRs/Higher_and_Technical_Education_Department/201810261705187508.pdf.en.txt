Government's decision
The grant in aid is sanctioned to 19 Aided Art Institutions functioning under the Directorate of Arts in four quarterly installments. Reference No. 2 herein refers to the issues in the checklist mentioned in the Annexure to the Circular dated 02.04.2018 of the Department of Finance. Accordingly, the Government have decided to disburse the second instalment of Rs. 5,62,000/- (Rupees Five Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand only) of the said post-salary subsidy in the month of September, 2018. 3 mentioned here. The Government approval is being granted subject to compliance of the conditions contained in the Government Resolution dated 4.1.1988.
The expenditure for the same should be shown under the head Demand No. 4, 2205-Arts and Culture, 101 Fine Arts Education, (101) (03) Assistance to Non-Government Art Institutions, (03) (01) All Ordinary Recurring Grants (22050055), 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Munerations). A provision of Rs. 22,47,000/- has been made under this head in the current financial year. Costs must be covered by this provision.
These orders are being issued in accordance with the authority conferred on the Administrative Department under the Circular No. Finance-2017/ PR No. 69/ Finance-3 dated 02.04.2018 of the Finance Department.
