Preamble: In pursuance of the autonomy granted by the Government in pursuance of the Programme for Improvement of Quality of Technical Education Phase-I& II, a Board of Management was constituted in Sri Guru Gobind Singh Ji Institute of Engineering& Technology, Nanded vide Government Resolution dated 23/10/2016 mentioned at Reference No.
The term of appointment of members other than ex-officio members of the Governing Body constituted under the Government decision at Reference No. 1 is 2 years. Accordingly, the term of office of the members other than the ex-officio members of the Board of Governors of Sri Guru Gobind Singh Institute of Engineering and Technology, Nanded has been extended. expired on 23.10.2018. The Director, Directorate of Technical Education vide letter dated 19/01/2018 has requested the Government to consider the guidelines issued by the University Grants Commission (UGC) for constitution of a new Governing Body and grant extension to the existing Governing Body till the new Governing Body is constituted. Accordingly, the Government was considering the issue of re-constitution of the Governing Board of Sri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology, Nanded, which was involved in the Quality Improvement Programme in Technical Education.
Government Decision: It is being approved to constitute the Governing Board of Sri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology, Nanded, one of the autonomous engineering colleges functioning in the state as follows:
