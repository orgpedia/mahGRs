Preamble: The Deputy Director, Directorate of Arts, vide letter Reference No. 3, has submitted a proposal for issuance of orders for disbursement of funds out of Demand No. 4,2205 Art& Culture, 101 Fine Arts Education, Vocational Services (28) (Mandatory).
Government Decision: Professional service (28) covers the expenses of hourly remuneration of teachers, remuneration and travelling allowance of teacher supervising during examination, lawyer's fees etc. Department of Finance, Circular No. 2022/ No. 43/ Finance-3, No. 2. Orders have been issued on 08/02/2023 for disbursement of funds for the year 2022-2023. It has fixed a percentage for disbursing funds for some of the targets. Accordingly, a limit of 85 per cent has been fixed for the target of" 28 professional services." But for the purpose of 28 professional services under some head, 70% of the funds have been released by the Finance Department.
Accordingly, it is being approved to disburse funds under the following headings from the target of Demand No. 4,2205 Arts and Culture, 101 Fine Arts Education, Professional Services (28). Details are as follows.
