2.The said orders shall come into force with immediate effect.
3. The Head of the Office concerned shall relieve the transferred officers forthwith to join the place of transfer. Also, the officers concerned should immediately join the office in which they were posted after their transfer from the existing office.
# Page 2
4. The Head of the Office concerned shall immediately depute the transferred officers on their discharge from the office concerned. The head of the office concerned shall be held liable in the event of non-payment of salary and allowances as a mandatory waiting period due to non-appointment of officers by the head of the office concerned. Also, no leave of absence shall be granted to such officers during the period from discharge to joining the place of transfer.
The period of assumption of office shall be admissible to the transferred officers as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, Non-Service and Provisions during suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981, as per the extant rules of the Government, for joining the place of transfer.
6. The Director of Higher Education, Pune should ensure that the next month's (June) salary of the transferred officers is drawn from the transferred office only.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune, shall submit the performance report on the transfer order to the Government within 15 days.
8. The said Government Order shall be issued by the Government of Maharashtra.
