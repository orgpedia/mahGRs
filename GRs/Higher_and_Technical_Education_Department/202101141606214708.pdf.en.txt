The Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014.
Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai has started functioning temporarily on the 2nd, 4th and 6th floor of MTNL, Powai. As per the lease agreement for the said premises, for the period from October, 2020 to December, 2020 for the financial year 2020-21, the Finance Officer, Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, has approved the payment of rent for 225 rooms of the hostel at Bhaderakam and CETTM, MTNL, Powai for the period from October, 2020 to December, 2020. A letter of 1 is requested. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai for payment of rent was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The amount of rent payable for the period October, 2020 to December, 2020 for the financial year 2020-21 for the 2nd, 4th and 6th floor space at MTNL, Powai and 225 (100+50+ 50+25) rooms of the hostel at CETTM, MTNL, Powai on temporary basis for Maharashtra Rashtriya Vardhi Vidyapeeth, Mumbai is Rs. 4,71,99, 612/- (Rupees Four Crore Seventy One Lakh Ninety Nine Thousand Six Hundred Twelve only).
