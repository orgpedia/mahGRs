1) As per the prevailing policy of the Government, the Letter of Intent to start a new college shall remain for the purpose of starting a college on an unaided basis.
2) Since the letter of intent is issued by the institution with a view to fulfilling the prescribed terms and conditions before obtaining final approval for the new college, the college cannot be started until the institution receives the final approval of the Government to start the new college.
3) The letter of intent issued by the government to start a new college will be valid till January 39 of the nearest next year.
