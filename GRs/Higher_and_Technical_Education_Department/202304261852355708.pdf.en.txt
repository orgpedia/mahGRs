Government Circular: As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2, the policy regarding transfer of Group-B officers and Group-C and Group-D employees through counseling has been implemented. As per this policy, the list of transferable officers/ employees and the list of vacant posts should be published. The process of transfer of officers in the cadre of Junior Resident, Group-B and Junior Administrative Officer/ Superintendent/ Manager, Group-B under the Department of Higher and Technical Education will be carried out in April/ May 2023, for which the list of transferable officers and the list of vacancies is being published as follows:
