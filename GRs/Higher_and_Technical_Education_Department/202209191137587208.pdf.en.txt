The Central Department of Higher Education, Government of India has issued a Letter of Intent to the State Government dated 27th October, 2021 for setting up of De-Novo type Deemed to be University with three Government Colleges namely College of Applied Arts and Sir J. G. Vastu Shastra Mahavidyalaya. It has been decided to constitute a Task Force Committee to submit a proposal to the Central Government for final approval after fulfilling the points mentioned in the Letter of Intent.
The following Task Force Committee is being constituted to fulfill the objectives stated in the Letter of Intent dated 27.10.2021 received from the Central Government for setting up De-Novo type Deemed to be University comprising of three Government Colleges namely College of Applied Arts and Sir J.G. The functions of the Task Force Committee shall be as follows: (1) To carry out the matters specified in the Letter of Intent.
2) To suggest measures to the Government regarding the action to be taken regarding the establishment of deemed university.
3) Fulfillment of the plans and proposals in the Project Report (DPR) prepared by the Directorate of Arts regarding De-Novo University.
4) To resolve the issues arising in connection with the establishment of De-Novo type Deemed to be University by holding meetings from time to time.
5) Report to the government on the action taken.
The Task Force Committee shall have the power to direct the concerned authorities to fulfil the objectives set out in the Letter of Intent. Also, it will be mandatory for the concerned officers to follow the instructions given by the committee.
Non-official members of the Task Force Committee to attend the meeting physically Rs. 7000/- plus sitting allowance and travelling allowance. A sitting allowance of Rs.5000/- is being allowed. The Government of the Department vide its Resolution No.PLA-2015/ PR No. 168/2015/ TANSI-3 dated 1st January, 2018 in the" Letter of Association-1" vide item' D" of the Department has decided to deduct the expenditure on sitting allowance and travelling allowance from the personal provident fund of the Director, Directorate of Arts. 8 The expenditure to be incurred with the approval of the Administrative Department is being approved.
