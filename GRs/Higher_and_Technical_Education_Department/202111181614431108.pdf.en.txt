Introduction: India is a young nation. Our youth power is huge, wonderful and energetic and can give direction to the country. Today's youth power is the future of our motherland and as every social institution and individual, we have to channelize the energy of the youth in a positive direction. So every youth should be committed. The foundation of the youth's future takes direction in college life. This is the time when we can provide them with the right education and the necessary platform for social, physical and intellectual development for a better future.
The Hindustan Scout and Guide Society is an organization recognized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, for Scouting and Guiding activities throughout the country. The government was considering to implement the activities of Ranger and Rover units of Hindustan Scout and Guide in all colleges of the Higher Education Department.
Government's decision
The activities of the Hindustan Scout and Guide Ranger and Rover Unit will be carried out on a voluntary basis in all the colleges of the Department of Higher Education on a non-financial basis, subject to the following terms and conditions:
is being allowed.
Terms and Conditions- 1. The initiative will be voluntary for Government/ Non-Government aided and Perpetually Unaided Self-Financed Colleges and Non-Agriculture Universities under the Directorate of Higher Education.
This initiative is being approved on the condition that there will be no financial and administrative burden on the government (on a non-financial basis).
The activity should be carried out at a time/ period other than the academic schedule in the college.
