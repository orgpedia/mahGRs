Preamble: Under the authority of the Government No. 2. It has been approved to admit girl students of Sarathi and Mahajyoti Corporations in the hostel for girls from economically weaker sections built in Nashik under the Yashwantrao Chavan Birth Centenary Celebration Scheme.
Accordingly, the matter of giving approval to start this hostel through this corporation was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Birth Centenary Festival Scheme, Plot No. 23, No. Government approval is being given to start a hostel by Sarathi and Mahajyoti Corporations in the building constructed for the hostel for girls belonging to economically weaker sections on ULC land at 24/1-D/ 1-A, Kasbe, Nashik subject to the following terms, conditions and conditions.
The Sarathi and Mahajyoti Corporations should admit 75 girls each from their target groups in this hostel and 50 girls from economically weaker sections should be admitted through the Department of Higher and Technical Education.
The Sarathi and Mahajyoti Corporations shall bear the expenditure for the girl child of their target groups and the expenditure for 50 girls belonging to economically weaker sections shall be shared by both the Corporations at the rate of 50% each. Therefore, the corporations will not be required to pay rent to the government.
3. Approval should be given to run the said hostel for a period of five years. For this, a legal agreement should be signed between the District Collector, Nashik, Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune and Sarathi and Mahajyoti on behalf of the government.
4. The abode should be run according to the rules of Sarathi and Mahajyoti. The day-to-day affairs of the Basti Ghar will be under the interim control of Sarathi and Mahajyoti Corporations and the final control will be of the Collector, Nashik and the Director, Higher Education, Maharashtra State, Pune.
No financial provision will be made by the Department of Higher and Technical Education for this hostel. The staff required for the hostel should be provided by the Sarathi Corporation. The Sarathi Corporation should also bear the cost of maintenance of the township.
