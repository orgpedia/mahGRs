Keeping in view the trend towards curriculum, so that no student is deprived of the desired higher education, Section 109 (8) of the Act provides for expeditious recognition of new batches in addition to specified faculties in exceptional circumstances. In accordance with this provision, in the academic year 2023-24, the non-agricultural universities of the state had submitted proposals to the government for additional new batches of specific faculties. Reference No. 2 The inspection was carried out in accordance with the norms laid down by the government here. After this inspection, the proposals of the universities recommended to the government, the proposals of the colleges mentioned in the following statement, for the rapid addition of new batches of specific faculties, are being rejected as they do not meet the prescribed criteria indicated in their names.
