GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the Government of India, Department of Youth Affairs and Sports, vide letter dated 16.8.2016 and 9.9.2016, with effect from April, 2016, the Central shareholding in National Service Scheme (NSS) has become 100% and the scheme has become centrally administered. A separate savings account has been opened in the bank in which funds have been received from the Centre through ECS. According to the above letter of the central government, the funds have been deposited in the separate savings account of the National Service Scheme without directly going to the state. According to the government decision, this fund is distributed to the universities for the programs of the National Service Scheme.
