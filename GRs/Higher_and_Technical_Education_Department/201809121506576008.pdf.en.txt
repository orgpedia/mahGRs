Government Papers:
Paragraph 1 of the Circular dated 6th September, 2018 read as under:
" Since 50 per cent tuition fee scholarship is admissible to students admitted through centralized admission process for professional courses under Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme in the academic year 2018-19, if the institutions charge 100 per cent tuition fee at the time of admission from the students, the 50 per cent tuition fee amount should be refunded to the student immediately. "
