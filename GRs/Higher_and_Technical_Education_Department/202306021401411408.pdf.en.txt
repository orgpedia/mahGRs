GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION, NO. ANUDA-2011/ (200/11) RESTRICTION-6/ MASHI-4, dated 26th May, 2022.
Preamble: It has been decided to sanction 100% grant-in-aid to one college/ faculty in each taluka. Accordingly, under the Government decision dated 26/05/2022, a total of 18 colleges have been approved to bring the respective faculties on 100% grant subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government decision. Form-A, No. 11 of the said Government Resolution. A symbolic supplementary demand presented in the 4th (Winter) Session of December, 2022 for inclusion of salary expenditure of teaching and non-teaching posts admissible for Science stream in Mehkar, G. Buldhana College under committed expenditure has been approved. Accordingly, the matter of approving the inclusion of the salary expenses of these posts under the committed expenditure was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As mentioned in the Preamble, Smt. Sindhutai Jadhav Arts and Science College, Th. Mehar, G. Buldhana, to include the salary expenditure of the teacher/ non-teaching posts of 20 teachers and 09 non-teaching posts mentioned in the next prospectus to be admissible for the science stream in the college, subject to the conditions mentioned in the relevant Government Decision, dated 26/05/2022 and subject to the following conditions.
