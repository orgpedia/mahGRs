The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately. Since the above transfers have been made on request, they will not be allowed to hold office during the period, travel allowance and daily allowance.
(2) On receipt of the transfer order, the Head of the Department concerned shall immediately transfer the person from the existing institution to the institution of the next posting. Also, the heads of the concerned institutions should relieve the transferred department head immediately without waiting for the appearance of the alternate department head.
3) The transferred head of the department should be contacted without delay after he is relieved from his duties by the concerned organization.
4) No leave of the transferred department head should be sanctioned without prior approval of the administrative department.
5) The directorate should inform this department about the transferred officers being relieved of their duties and joining the place of transfer.
