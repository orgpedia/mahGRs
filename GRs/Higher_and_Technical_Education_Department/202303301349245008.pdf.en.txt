The proposal:
Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur University was established on 4 August 1923. The campus of the university is spread over 373 acres. Renowned as the oldest university in Central India, the university has entered its centenary year. The academic year 2022-23 will be celebrated as the centenary year. On this occasion, the University has started to build some new projects by taking into account the material needs and future development prospects of the population in the university's periphery, along with the university's adopted knowledge generation and skill development.
The University has designed policies as per the need, aligning with global challenges and opportunities. Accordingly, the University proposed to set up a world-class inter-sports complex to enhance participation of the students of the University in national and international sports competitions and to benefit the schools and other institutions and individuals. Accordingly, Rs. 4441.38 lakh. 2 here. The administrative approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 13.03.2023. Reference No. 1 to make available funds pursuant to the said Administrative Sanction. 1 has been requested. As per the request, the matter of providing funds for the construction of an inter-sport complex was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
For the construction of a world-class inter-sports complex in Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Rs. 20,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Crores only) is being sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions.

Terms and Conditions: 1. The fund should be disbursed and withdrawn within the limits of administrative sanction.
(2) No revised administrative approval will be required for the subject work. Special attention should be paid to this.
Further, all the documents and accounts of the above grant are to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General-2, Nagpur, as the case may be.
The grant granted to the University shall be used for the purpose for which it was sanctioned.
Also, it should be ensured that the expenditure is fully met within the stipulated period and the utilization certificate should be submitted to the government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune within the prescribed time.
The progress report of the above work should be submitted to the government from time to time. Further funds will be released based on the progress of the work.
The Director (Higher Education), Maharashtra State, Pune shall be responsible for handing over the said approved provision to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur immediately.
The expenditure for this work should be spent from the approved provision of" Demand No-W-2, Principal Title-2202- General Education, 03- University and Higher Education, 102- Assistance to Universities,- (00) (07) Grants to Universities for General Education (Mandatory) (22020745), 31 Subsidiary Grants" in the year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision, Maharashtra Appropriation Act 2022, Finance Department, Government Circular No. being issued under the authority conferred on the Department vide order dated 04.04.2022. The conditions set out in Annex No. 9 to this Government Decision are being met.
