Preamble: In pursuance of the Government decision dated 16.03.2022 in Reference No.1, approval has been accorded for establishment of Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Museum and for earmarking of site for the said college at Directorate of Libraries, Kalina Complex. Accordingly, the Collector, Mumbai Suburban vide order dated 31.03.2023 in Reference No.2 has transferred 7000 sq.m. of open land at Kalina Complex to the Director of Arts, Directorate of Arts, Maharashtra State, Mumbai for establishment of" Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Museum." A new well-equipped building is planned to be constructed on the 7000 sq.m. land allotted by the Government for Bharat Ratna Lata Dinanath Mangeshkar International Music College and Museum. For this, the Ideas Design Competition of renowned architects/ architectural institutions of the country has been implemented by the Public Works Department. The idea is to finalise a design from the designs to be submitted in the Ideas Design Competition. The Committee has been constituted as per the decision of the Government dated 19th May, 2023 and it was under the consideration of the Government to approve the proposal for declaring the winners of the Ideas Design Competition and awarding their prizes and to assign the work of preparing detailed maps, plans and budget for the construction of the new building of Bharat Ratna Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar International College of Music and Museum to one of these contestants/ winners. Accordingly, the following decision was taken.
