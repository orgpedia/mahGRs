General Administration Department The comprehensive order has been issued by consolidating all the government decisions issued under the government decision of 21.09.2017. General Administration Department. As per the Government decision dated 27.09.2021, the policy of compassionate appointment to Government employees in Group-A and Group-B cadres has been implemented.
As per the decision of the General Administration Department, employees of non-agriculture universities under the department, aided non-government colleges, non-government aided colleges/ institutions under the control of the Directorate of Technical Education and the Directorate of Arts, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technical Education University-Lonner, COEP University of Technology, Laxminarayan Innovative Technology University-Nagpur, Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University-Nashik, and aided deemed universities will be given government jobs.

It was under consideration of the government to issue comprehensive orders for the implementation of the compassionate appointment scheme and the policy of compassionate appointment to the employees of Group-A and Group-B cadres in these educational institutions. The following decision has been taken:
Government Decision: The provisions of the Government Decisions issued earlier regarding the implementation of the policy of compassionate appointment to the employees of the Government recognized posts in non-agricultural universities, aided non-government colleges, non-government aided colleges/ institutions under the control of Directorate of Technical Education and Directorate of Arts, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technical Education University- Lonere, COEP University of Technology, Laxminarayan Innovative Technology University Nagpur, Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University Nashik, aided deemed universities are being overridden.
Non-agriculture universities, aided non-government colleges, non-government aided colleges/ institutions under the control of Directorate of Technical Education and Directorate of Arts, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technical Education University- Lonere, CoEP University of Technology, Laxminarayan Innovative Technology University Nagpur, Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University- Nashik, Aided Deemed to be Universities will be covered as per Annexure-A along with the provisions regarding grant of compassionate appointment to employees in Government recognized Group-A to Group-D posts.
3. The application to be made by the family of the deceased employee and the related information shall be as per Annexure-B.
4. The format of the waiting list to be maintained by the appointing authority for compassionate appointment shall be as per Annexure-C.
5. Give the benefit of this scheme to the employees of Group-A and Group-B in the wake of Covid. It will be effective from 01.01.2020. The time limit for eligible families of such deceased employees to apply for compassionate appointment shall be one year from the date of this Government decision.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the consensus reached through the General Administration Department's Informal Reference No. 91/ Q8/ dated April 5, 2023.
