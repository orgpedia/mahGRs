Director, Directorate of Technical Education, State of Maharashtra vide letter No. 4/ Aastha/ Drawing and Disbursing Officer/ 2023/464 dated 11th July, 2023
Preamble: As per the Government decision dated 27.03.2023, Government Engineering College, Kolhapur has been approved to start from the academic year 2023-24. Also, posts have been sanctioned for this college in a phased manner.
The Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State vide Reference No. As per the letter, a proposal was submitted to declare a Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the newly created institution, Government Engineering College, Kolhapur. Accordingly, the matter of declaring the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the Government Engineering College, Kolhapur, which was started from the academic year 2023-24, was under the consideration of the government.
The government's decision:
The Principal, Government Engineering College, Kolhapur is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer as per the powers conferred under Rule 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rights Rules, 1968.
