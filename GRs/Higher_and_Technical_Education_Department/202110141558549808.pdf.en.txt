Introduction: National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under which the number of students is sanctioned by the Central Government every year to the Universities/ Institutions for two activities, namely, regular programmes and special camps. As per the reference letter for the year 2021-22, the Central Government has sanctioned the total number of students for the State of Maharashtra as 3,27,700 for regular programme and 1,63,850 for special camp programme by dividing the total number of students as 11.8 per cent for SC, 9.4 per cent for ST and 78.8 per cent for others. Proposals have been received for approval of the total number of students of the newly created universities in the year 2021-22, who have been approved from the approved number of students of their parent universities. Accordingly, the total sanctioned strength of students for Maharashtra and its university/ institution wise allocation was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As mentioned in the above Preamble, as per the approved provision for the National Service Scheme 2021-22, a total of 3,27,700 students for regular programme and 1,63,850 students for special camp programme have been approved and are being allocated University/ Institute wise as shown in the table below:

The University/ Directorate/ Institute shall provide the N.S.O. as indicated in the table to them by the Government.
No student should be enrolled under this scheme in excess of the sanctioned number of students, category-wise. Also, the category wise number of students for the year 2021-22 should be allotted by the University/ Directorate/ Institute to the concerned. The university/ directorate/ institution should allocate the number of students allotted to them, category-wise to all the colleges affiliated to them and send a copy to the government. The number of students actually enrolled by the universities should be intimated to the government by 31st October, 2021.
It may be noted that the grant for the year 2021-22 will be sanctioned based on the number of students sanctioned as per this order.
