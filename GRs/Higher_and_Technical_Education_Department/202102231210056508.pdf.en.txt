The proposal:
The University Grants Commission, vide its notification dated 18.7.2018, has prescribed the criteria for appointment, educational qualification etc., to the post of Teachers and equivalent in Central Universities and Colleges and Institutions. In addition, the modalities for payment of pay scales, incidental benefits, incentive pay hikes etc. to teachers have been prescribed. The decision to apply the three provisions of this notification to the posts of Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Professor, Director of College Physical Education in the state has been taken under the aforesaid reference No. The decision has been taken by the government. Accordingly, it is proposed to constitute a screening committee and selection committee to determine the placement of teachers in government colleges/ institutions in the state under the Career Advancement Scheme. The letter has been received by the government. Accordingly, for determining the position as per the 5th Pay Commission, the reference No. 1 It was under the consideration of the Government to dissolve the Committees constituted under the Government Decision dated 8.3.2019 and to constitute new Committees as per the University Grants Commission Notification dated 18.7.2018.
The government's decision:
