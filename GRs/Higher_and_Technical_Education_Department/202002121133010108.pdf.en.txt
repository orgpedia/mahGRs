GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the Government of India, Department of Youth Affairs and Sports, vide letter dated 16.8.2016 and 9.9.2016, with effect from April, 2016, the Central shareholding in the National Service Scheme (NSS) has become 100% and the scheme has become centrally administered. A separate savings account has been opened in the bank in which funds have been received from the Centre through ECS. According to the above letter of the Central Government, the funds have been deposited in the separate savings account of the National Service Scheme without directly going to the state. As per the decision of the government, this fund is distributed to the universities for the programs of the National Service Scheme. The funds for disbursement of grants-in-aid (conditional) to the respective Universities for the year 2018-19 (final instalment for all categories) and 2019-20 (first instalment for all categories) from the ICICI Bank savings account of National Service Scheme as mentioned in Annexure A and B are being sanctioned under this Government decision.
Out of 26 Universities/ Directorates, 15 Universities have been notified by the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government Resolution No. Orders for disbursement of Subsidiary Grants have been issued under 15/11/2019.
Submission for sanction of final instalment of grant-in-aid for the year 2018-19 to the remaining 10 Universities/ Directorates by adjusting their balance for the year 2018-19 out of the due amount for the year 2019-20, excluding the said Agricultural Universities, due to non-receipt of Utilization Certificate and Annual Accounts Payee for the year 2018-19 from 01 Agricultural University (Vasantrao Naik Marathwada Agricultural University, Parbhani), (Details of the amount due to the Universities are given in Annexure A and B). (a) Appendices A and B are attached. The proposal for sanction of funds to 10 Universities/ Directorates for disbursement of final instalment for the year 2018-19 and first instalment amounting to 75% of the grant-in-aid for the year 2019-20 to all the Universities/ Directorates amounting to Rs. 3,03,06, 387/- (Rupees Three Crore Three Lakh Six Thousand Three Hundred and Eighty Seven only) for regular programme and Rs. 2,85,70, 734/- (Rupees Two Crore Fifty Eight Lakh Seventy Thousand Seven Hundred and Thirty Four only) for special camp programme, amounting to Rs. 5,88,77, 121/- (Rupees Five Crore Eighty Eight Lakh Seventy Seven Thousand One Hundred and Twenty One only).
The amount of grant-in-aid shall be disbursed by the ECS to the Registrar/ Director/ Controller of the concerned University by the State National Service Scheme Officer (State Liaison Officer/ Officer on Special Duty) of the National Service Scheme with the approval of the Additional Chief Secretary, Department of Higher and Technical Education.
