Preamble: The Government has decided to implement the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) programme launched by the Central Government in the State to ensure access to higher education to all sections, make higher education inclusive and create high quality in higher education. Rusayo-2013/ Q.No.418/ Vishy-3, Dy. It was approved by the government on 20 May 2015. The scheme is 60% funded by the central government and 40% by state governments. Maharashtra State Assembly. It was completed on May 9, 2018. In the meeting, the draft of the Cluster College Bill was discussed. The Maharashtra State Legislative Assembly has passed the bill. As per the decision taken in the RUSA Council meeting, the Director, RUSA has submitted the draft of the Act to be promulgated for the establishment of Cluster University in the State to the Government.
The government was considering setting up a committee comprising of individuals to discuss the draft of the proposed law and make changes accordingly.
The Government has decided to constitute a Committee to discuss and make changes in the draft Maharashtra Cluster Universities Act, 2018 for setting up Cluster Universities in the State of Maharashtra.
