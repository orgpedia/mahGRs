The proposal:
Dr. Bandal Vithal Shrirang, an awardee candidate of Maharashtra Public Service Commission, was appointed as Professor, Electrical Engineering, Government Engineering College, Maharashtra Engineering College Teacher Service (Group-A) at Government Engineering College, Karad, as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The appointment order also stated that the candidate should join the concerned post within one month from the date of the order. However, he was allowed to join the above post as per the request made by Mr. Bandal. The last date has been extended till 30/08/17. Accordingly, Mr. Must be completed by 30/08/17. However, he has not been appointed as yet.
Therefore, the matter of cancelling the appointment of Mr. Bandal as Professor, Electrical Engineering was under the consideration of the Government.
" The appointment of Shri Bandal Vitthal Shrirang, an awardee candidate of Maharashtra Public Service Commission, as Professor, Electrical Engineering, Government Engineering College, Maharashtra Engineering College Teacher Service (Group-A), Government Engineering College, Karad, has been cancelled under this order," the government said in a statement.
