The proposal:
The term of office of Shri Ashok Ambadas Bhatkar, who was appointed as the Presiding Officer of the University and College Tribunal at Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur vide Reference No. 1, has expired on 17/9/2021. As per Section 80 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, a new Presiding Officer has been appointed to the vacant post of Presiding Officer of the University and College Tribunal at Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur.
The matter of appointing them was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The post of Presiding Officer on the Tribunal of Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur, which is vacant, has been given to Ma. Justice Swapna S. Joshi, High Court, Bombay is being appointed for a period of three years with effect from the date she assumes charge of her office.
The provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 as well as Reference No. 2 The service conditions mentioned in the government decision here. Justice Swapna S. Joshi will continue to apply to the High Court of Bombay.
