The said officers shall assume charge of their new post forthwith and shall not proceed on unauthorised leave. It should be noted that if any form of application is submitted to cancel the transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
The Director of Libraries, Directorate of Libraries, Maharashtra State, Mumbai shall, in accordance with this order, relieve the said officer from his present charge with immediate effect and shall ensure that the transferred officer shall join the place of posting given within the prescribed period and submit a report to the Government within fifteen days.
