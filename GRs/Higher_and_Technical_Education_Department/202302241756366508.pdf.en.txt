Department of Higher and Technical Education

Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Mantralaya Extension, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 24, 2023.
Reference:- 1) Government Resolution, Department of Higher and Technical Education, No. PRASHAMA-2022/ No. 37/ Mashi-3, Dy. 19/10/2022.
2) Letter from the Chief Engineer, Public Works Regional Department, Mumbai No. Mua (M)/ Ka-2/ Maa/ 4179/2022 dated 02/12/2022.
3) Director, Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute, Mumbai vide letter No. VJTI/ SED/ CON/ 6158/2023 dated 14/02/2023.
Introduction: Reference No. 1) As per the Government decision dated 19/10/2022, a total of Rs. Administrative Sanction has been accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 8,43,09, 025/-. In the meantime, as per the letter dated 02/12/2022 addressed to the Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Mumbai, a report has been submitted regarding the immediate demolition of the hostel building as it belongs to the C-1 category. Further, vide letter dated 14/02/2023 addressed to the Director, Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute, Mumbai, a protective inspection report of the said hostel building has been submitted and as per the said report, the condition of the hostel building is not repairable and hence, the demolition of the building has been suggested. Accordingly, the matter of cancellation of administrative approval granted under the Government Decision dated 19/10/2022 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As mentioned in the Preamble, for strengthening/ renovation of hostel building at' Savitri Devi Phule Women's Hostel, Mumbai" and for renovation of electrical set up required for the same, a total amount of Rs. The administrative approval granted under the Government Decision dated 19/10/2022 regarding the budget of Rs. 8,43,09, 025/- is being cancelled.
