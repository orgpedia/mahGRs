Taking up the submissions made before the Tribunal, the Government/ Aided Engineering/ Pharmacy/ Vastu Shastra Colleges/ Technicians under the Directorate of Technical Education under the Department of Higher and Technical Education of the State, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar University of Technology and Institute of Chemical Technology have proposed to increase the retirement age of librarians from 58 to 60 years. It was placed for consideration in the meeting of the Cabinet of Ministers dated 20.02.2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In view of the orders of the All India Council for Technical Education No. 01& 02, the Government/ Aided Engineering/ Pharmacy/ Vastu Shastra Colleges/ Technological Colleges under the Directorate of Technical Education under the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Govt.
The Government hereby approves enhancement of the age of retirement of the equivalent post of Librarian in Babasaheb Ambedkar University of Technology and Institute of Chemical Technology from 58 years to 60 years subject to the following conditions: (i) The Librarians who have retired during the period from 01.01.2006 to the date of this Government decision and have been retired for more than 2 years shall be given retirement benefits and all other incidental benefits on the assumption that they have retired at the age of 60 years.
(ii) The Librarians who have retired in the intervening period from 01.01.2006 to the date of this Government Decision and who have completed less than 2 years, should be re-employed. They should be given all the benefits due to them.
(ii) Librarians who retire after the publication of this Government Order shall be retired at the age of 60 years.
This Government decision was taken with the concurrence of the Department of Finance in its Informal Reference No. 02/19/ SERVICE-4, Dt.
The approval is being issued in accordance with the approval given on January 10, 2019.
