Introduction: The revised pattern of Directorate of Arts, its field offices and Government Arts Colleges. It was approved by the Government Decision of 30 December 2006.
Accordingly, one post of driver is sanctioned for the office of the Director, Directorate of Arts. The staff working at the site. He retired from service on June 30, 2012 and has been serving since then.
Approval has been given to purchase a new vehicle for the office of the Director, Directorate of Arts. A driver's license is required to operate this vehicle. However, as the post of driver on the establishment of Directorate of Arts remained vacant for a period of more than six months, it has lapsed as per the Government decision dated 10th September, 2001 of the Finance Department. The proposal has been sent to the finance ministry. Accordingly, a sub-committee of the Finance Department (DFC) has been constituted to create a fictitious post of driver and fill it up through an external agency. Sub-Committee constituted by the Government Order dated 9th June, 2017. It was approved at the January 8, 2019 meeting. The following decision has been taken:

Government Decision- It is being approved under the following terms and conditions to create 1" fictitious post" in the Directorate of Arts in place of the vacant post of driver and fill it by external agencies.
a) The work of the driver should be done by the contractor/ company/ institution by inviting tender in the manner prescribed by the government and with the approval of the competent authority.
b) Contract with the contractor/ company/ organization to whom the work is to be assigned.
The agreement should state that the government will not be responsible for the services of the employees working in the contractor/ company/ institution.
(c) The remuneration for the work of a driver to be performed by an external agency shall be shown under the heading" Office Expenditure" instead of under this heading.
d) The cost of operating a vehicle through an external agency must be saved by 10 to 25 per cent as compared to the cost of filling up the post on a regular basis. The Director, Directorate of Arts will be responsible for making these savings and keeping a record of it in the prescribed form.
