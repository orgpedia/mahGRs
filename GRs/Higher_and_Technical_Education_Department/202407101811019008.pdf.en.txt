The Proposal: -
Research Development and Evaluation Training, Armoured Corps Centre and School, Ahmednagar U1 ha Gafood GRA ARIAT has been awarded to start a new institute with four diploma engineering courses by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the apex authority for engineering courses.
2. These institutes have been set up to train personnel of the Indian Army. The institute has designed various courses for the Indian Army. The institute has been approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) to start four diploma courses from the academic year 2024-25. Since the institute is under the control of the Central Government, it will not receive any kind of grant from the State Government. Since the students enrolled in the institute are personnel serving in the Indian Army, admission to these courses is done by the institute at the institute level. The course is permanently affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune. However, since the newly received diploma engineering courses need to be approved by the state government as per the university rules in order to get the affiliation of Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune, the Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has issued a notification to this effect. The proposal has been submitted under the letter of 25.06.2024.
