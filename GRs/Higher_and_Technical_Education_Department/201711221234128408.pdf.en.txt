The above mentioned 18 candidates are being appointed to the post of Adhivakta, Maths, Government Technician, Maharashtra Technician Teacher Service Group-A on a temporary basis only by nomination from the date of their physical appearance subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The pay scale of the said employee is 15600-39100 and the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government Decision No. SPC-2010/ (34/10)/ Tanshi-2, Dy. 20/08/2010 will remain in the AGP mentioned.
(2) In addition to the said pay scale, dearness allowance, house rent allowance and local supplementary allowance shall be admissible at such rates as may be determined by the Government from time to time.
The first increment will be given to the candidates after completion of one year of probation period and the subsequent increment will be given after satisfactory completion of probation period.
(4) If the performance or conduct of such candidates during the probation period is found to be improper, substandard or unsatisfactory, their service shall be terminated without any prior notice or they shall be liable to be dismissed from service.
