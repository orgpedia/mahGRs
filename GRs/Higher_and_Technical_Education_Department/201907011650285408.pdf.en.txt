the prelude
In reference to above No. As per the decision of the Government, the Sahityaratna Lokshahir Annabhau Sathe Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti has been constituted to publish the biographical literature of Sahityaratna Lokshahir Annabhau Sathe initially for a period of 3 years. The following revised orders are being issued with partial changes in them.
The Government has decided to publish the books of Shri Annabhau Sathe and to complete all the unpublished literature, writing, research work related to these books in Maharashtra, in the country and abroad. from 16.06.2016. The period is being extended up to 28.02.2019.
2. In addition, the Sahitya Ratna Lokshahir Annabhau Sathe Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti is being reconstituted as follows. The Government is pleased to grant extension for a further period of 3 years with effect from 01.03.2019:
