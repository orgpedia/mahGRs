(1) The Management Council shall be responsible for administering the grants sanctioned by the Government as per the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 by depositing them in the" University Fund." Also, the university should take action to exchange the expenditure made from this grant and place the statement of expenditure accounts before the general council and get it approved.
(2) The Vice-Chancellor of Dr. Homi Bhabha State University, Mumbai University shall be responsible to see that the grant is spent for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned by the Government. In this regard, they should submit its utility certificate to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education with the approval of the Management Council.
