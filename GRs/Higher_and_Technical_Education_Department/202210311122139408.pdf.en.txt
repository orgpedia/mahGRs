Preamble: In exercise of the powers conferred by section 58 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 1994, the Government hereby order to constitute the University and Colleges Tribunal for non-agricultural Universities vide Reference No. 1 has been issued by the government here. The Act has been amended by the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
Section 80 of the 2016 Act provides for the appointment of Presiding Officer.
As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the appointment of a new Presiding Officer to the vacant post of Presiding Officer at Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As per the decision of the Government, Shri Shrikant Dattatraya Kulkarni, Hon. Justice Ma. The High Court, Bombay is appointed for a period of three years from the date on which he assumes charge of his office.
Shri Shrikant Dattatraya Kulkarni, Justice Shri. The High Court of Bombay vide Reference No. The service conditions mentioned in the Government Decision 1 and 5 will continue to apply.
