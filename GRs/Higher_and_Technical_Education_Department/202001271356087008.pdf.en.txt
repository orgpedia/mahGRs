Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award is given annually by the Government of Maharashtra to Sanskrit scholars. In this regard, the modalities, contours and all the ancillary aspects of the award have been decided under the Government Decision in Reference No. 1 by superseding all the orders issued earlier in respect of the said awards. As per the decision of the government, a screening committee has been formed to select Sanskrit scholars for the Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award. The Scrutiny Committee was reconstituted in pursuance of the Government decision at Reference No. 2. The government was contemplating to cancel the appointments of non-official members appointed to this scrutiny committee.
The appointments of the following non-official members of the screening committee for the Maharashtra State Mahakavi Kalidas Sanskrit Sadhana Award constituted under the Government Decision Reference No. 1 and 2 are being cancelled under this Government Decision.
