The Principal/ Director who is transferred for administrative reasons will be entitled to the period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance as per the rules of the government.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
In order to ensure that the transfer orders are implemented promptly and efficiently, the Director, Higher Education shall strictly follow the following instructions: 1. The orders shall come into force with immediate effect.
On receipt of the transfer order, the concerned Principal/ Director should immediately join the college/ institution from which he is presently working, to which he has been transferred and posted.
3. The transferred Principal/ Director shall be reinstated without any further delay after he/ she retires from the respective college/ institution and comes back to join. The Director, Higher Education, Pune will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-appointment of a Principal/ Director, due to compulsory waiting period.
4.No request for change in post will be entertained.
If any Principal/ Director tries to pressurize directly or indirectly to change the transfer order, the concerned Principal/ Director will be liable to take disciplinary action.
5. No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
(6) Since the said transfers are required to be effected effectively and the said order is effective immediately, the date of retirement of the transferred Principal/ Director shall be extended up to the date of retirement or till the date of retirement of the Principal/ Director. The salary up to August 31, 2021 should be drawn by the college/ institution where they are currently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the college/ institution before transfer under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the college/ institution after the transfer of the concerned Principal/ Director after joining the college/ institution. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from September 01, 2021 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the college/ institution where they were working before the transfer.
7. The responsibility of relieving the transferred Principal/ Director with immediate effect shall be personally borne by the Director, Higher Education, Pune. The Executive Report in this regard should be submitted by the Director, Higher Education, Pune to the Government before September 1, 2021.
