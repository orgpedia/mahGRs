Government Order - Subject to reference. Dr. Rajendra Bharud, State Project Director, RUSA, Mumbai has been appointed as Municipal Commissioner, Kolhapur Municipal Corporation, Kolhapur, vide letter A.S.A.
In view of this, under this order, the additional charge of the vacant post of State Project Director, RUSA, Mumbai is being temporarily transferred to Shri Vinod Mohitkar, Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai.
Mr. Vinod Mohitkar, Director, Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai shall hold the additional charge of the said post in addition to the duties and responsibilities of his present office.
This additional charge shall be until the appointment of a Regular Appointment Officer to the post of State Project Director, RUSA, Mumbai or until further orders of the Government, whichever is earlier.
