Introduction:- The process of providing land in Pahadi Goregaon for Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai is underway and it will take more time to start the university after construction on this land. At present, the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai has been approved to start its operations on a temporary basis on 2nd, 3rd, 6th and 4th floor of MTNL, Powai on lease basis. However, since the said lease agreement has expired, the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, as a temporary arrangement, has been permitted to renew the lease agreement by taking on lease the 2nd, 3rd, 4th and 6th floors of MTNL, Powai and hostel rooms (295) for student accommodation. 1 has been requested. Accordingly, the Executive Engineer, North Mumbai, was requested to examine the proposed lease agreement. In response to the request No. 2 The letter here has submitted to the government a certificate and a list of reasonable rates of rent, according to the list in reference No. 3 It was under consideration of the Government to approve the proposal for this lease as per the powers vested in the Administrative Department under the Government decision.
Government Resolution:- (1) The Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai, may, on a temporary basis, lease out the 2nd, 3rd, 4th and 6th floor and hostel rooms (295) for student accommodation at MTNL, Powai, Mumbai.
