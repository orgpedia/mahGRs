Preamble: The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has approved the conversion of Government Technical Niketan, Yavatmal into a Degree level Institute vide letter No. Pursuant to the said approval, the matter of grant of Government approval for conversion of the said Diploma Institute into a Degree level institution as proposed by the Director, Technical Education vide letter no.
