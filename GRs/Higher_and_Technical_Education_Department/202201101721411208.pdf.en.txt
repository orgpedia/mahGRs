The proposal:
As per the provisions of Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, the General Administration Department has laid down a consolidated procedure for reviewing the services of officers/ employees beyond the age of 50/55 years or after completion of 30 years of qualifying service.
As per the provisions of the said decision, the Government was considering to constitute a" Divisional Review Committee" for reviewing the services of the officers of the Directorate of Higher Education (itself) and the Divisional Joint Director's Offices/ Colleges/ Institutions/ Hostels under it beyond the age of 50/55 years/ 30 years of qualifying service, and to consider the representation in this regard.
Government Order:
The following Departmental Review Committee is being constituted to review the services of the officers of Maharashtra Shiksha Seva Group-A (Administration Branch- Clerical Cadre), Maharashtra Shiksha Seva Group-A (College Branch) and Maharashtra Shiksha Seva Group-B (Administration Branch- Clerical Cadre) in the offices/ colleges/ institutions/ hostels of the Directorate of Higher Education (itself) and the Divisional Joint Director under it, based on the criteria laid down for their eligibility to continue in service beyond the age of 50/55 years/ 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier:

The Director, Higher Education, Pune shall submit a proposal to the Government regarding the review of services of officers of the cadre of Maharashtra Shiksha Seva Group-A (Administration Branch- Clerk Cadre), Maharashtra Shiksha Seva Group-A (College Branch) and Maharashtra Shiksha Seva Group-B (Administration Branch- Clerk Cadre) in the offices/ colleges/ institutions/ hostels of the Directorate of Higher Education (itself) and Divisional Joint Director under it.
The recommendation of the officers who will be eligible/ ineligible to be retained in service as per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee will be submitted to the appropriate authorities as mentioned in Annexure-A along with the Government decision dated 10.6.2019 in the context of General Administration Department for final decision. Further, as per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee, the officers who would be ineligible to continue in service shall be reviewed by the" Special Review Committee" of the General Administration Department as mentioned in Annexure-A to the Government decision dated 10.6.2019.
