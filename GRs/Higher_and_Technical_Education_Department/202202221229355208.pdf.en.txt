The necessary procedure for implementation of Level-1 of the Pension Scheme within the State has been laid down in the Government Resolution at Reference No.8. The new defined contribution pension scheme applicable to non-agriculture universities and affiliated non-government aided colleges under the Department, as per the State Government employees, has been included in the National Pension Scheme and the necessary procedure for implementing the scheme has been issued vide Notification No. 10 has been prescribed by the government here.
As per the agreement entered into by the State Government with the Central Record Keeping Agency (CRA), the services provided by the agency under this scheme are provided by the concerned No. 11 It has been approved to prescribe the following fees under the letter of the government.
1. Annual Maintenance Charge-Rs.69/
2. Account Opening Charges-Rs.40/- (Rs.30/- if you use online PRAN Generate Module)
3. Transaction Charges-Rs. 3.75/
The matter of sanctioning a new head of accounts was under consideration of the government to provide subsidy to all DTOs for payment of service charges as above.
Government Decision: Under the National Pension Scheme, the Central Record Keeping Agency is approving the opening of new head of accounts for payment of quarterly charges for services rendered to all DTOs as follows.
