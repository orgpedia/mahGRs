GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION, NO. ADR-2021/ PR No. 89/ TANSHI-6, dated 29th April, 2022.
Preamble: The rates of remuneration payable to the hourly and visiting faculty for the courses taught by the Government Arts College under the control of the Directorate of Arts have been fixed as per the relevant Government decisions. The matter of revising the rates of honorarium implemented under this government decision was under the consideration of the government. The following decisions have been taken in this regard:
Government Decision: The rates of remuneration payable to the Expert Visitors/ Hourly Teachers for the Degree/ Diploma/ Post Graduate Diploma/ Post Graduate Degree Courses taught by the Government Arts Colleges under the control of Directorate of Arts are being increased as follows.

The above revised rates are being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) The Dean/ Principal of the college should ensure that all the teachers working in the concerned faculty and administrative positions in the institution are given academic load as per the standard before inviting the faculty/ expert visitors on an hourly basis.
(2) Ensure that a candidate on hourly basis is not paid more than the amount of the initial pay scale in the allowable pay scale of his rank in a single month

The Dean and Director of the concerned Government Arts College, Directorate of Arts should take it.
(3) A retired teacher or an expert in the profession related to the relevant course should be invited to teach on an hourly basis. In no case should candidates be invited for this purpose if a" backdoor appointment" situation arises.
4) The Arts College shall select the faculty through a local selection committee by issuing a notification with the approval of the Director, Directorate of Arts. The selection committee will consist of the Dean/ Principal of the concerned college, Head of the Department and an external subject expert.
(5) A temporary appointment of teachers on hourly basis may be made for a maximum period of 9 months in an academic year. The appointment must be approved by the Directorate of Arts.
The appointment will be automatically terminated after the expiry of the aforesaid period of 9 months.
Only two full-time teachers can be appointed for a full-time post. A maximum of 9 hours can be assigned to a teacher.
7) Since the appointment on hourly basis is a temporary appointment, it does not entitle any regular service. Hence," no demand for continuance in service in future and no demand for any right to regular service. Such a guarantee should be obtained before joining from the concerned teacher on a stamp paper of Rs.
8) Full-time teachers working in the college should not be given additional appointment on an hourly basis.
9) If any irregularity is found in the actual workload and payment of honorarium on hourly basis, the principal/ dean of the concerned college will be responsible for it.
The revised rates will be effective from April 1, 2023.
