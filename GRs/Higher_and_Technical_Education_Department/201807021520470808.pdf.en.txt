The government order:
The Government, with the approval of the Authority competent to transfer under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 and in accordance with the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) of the said Act, hereby order the transfer of the following officer in the cadre of Assistant Professor, Nuclear and Telecommunication Engineering, Government Engineering Colleges, Maharashtra Engineering Teachers Service (Group-A). After the transfer, the concerned officer is being posted at the place indicated in column-3 in front of his name.

The salary of the concerned officer up to the date of his retirement or up to the maximum date of 31/07/2018 shall be drawn by the organization in which he is presently working. The salary from 01/08/2018 should not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer organization under any circumstances, the salary should be withdrawn by the post-transfer organization after the concerned officer joins the transfer organization.
The officer shall be held personally liable if his salary as on 01/08/2018 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer in the institution where he was working before the transfer.
The transferred officer should not go on unauthorised leave. Their attention is also being drawn to the circular of the General Administration Department No. CDR- 1082/2567/28/ XI, dated 30/8/1982. In view of the instructions given therein, they should note that if any form of application is submitted for cancellation of the transfer, they will be eligible for disciplinary action.
