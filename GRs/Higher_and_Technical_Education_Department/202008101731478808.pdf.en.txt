GOVERNMENT DECISIONS, Department of Higher and Technical Education No. 363/ TANSI-4, Dated 16/3/2020 GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In pursuance of the report of the Director of Technical Education dated 12/12/2019, the Government has decided to accept only MAH-MBA/ MMS (CET), CMAT and CAT in the All India Admission Test (GMAT), (MAT), (ATMA) and (XAT) for admission to Master of Management Studies (MBA/ MMS) courses recognized in the State from the academic year 2020 to 2021.
A committee is being constituted under the aegis of the Additional Chief Secretary (Higher and Technical Education) to further examine the representations received from the government regarding these entrance examinations and to submit appropriate recommendations to the government. Commissioner, State Common Entrance Test Cell, Mumbai and Director, Technical Education, Mumbai will be the members of the committee.
The Director of Technical Education shall provide the information and documents in connection with the convening of the meeting of the Committee. The committee has been asked to submit its report to the government within 10 working days.
