Introduction: The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has published its Notes No. 2 here. Pursuant to the approval given on 10.04.2017, the Director, Technical Education vide the aforesaid No. 3 here. As proposed vide letter dated 07.10.2019, the Government was considering to grant approval for closure of Jai Mahakali Shikshan Sanstha, Shri Babulalji Agnihotri College of Engineering, Nagthana Road, Sindi Meghe, Wardha, a professional institution from the academic year 2017-18.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As proposed by the Director, Technical Education, pursuant to the approval of All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, from the academic year 2017-18, Shri.
The closure of Babulalji Agnihotri College of Engineering, Nagthana Road, Sindi Meghe, Wardha as an Institution of Professional Courses is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The institution will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in this institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the concerned university.
(2) The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters relating to the alternative arrangements for teachers and non-teaching staff appointed for the relevant courses in the institution as well as judicial matters in respect of service matters and other matters.
