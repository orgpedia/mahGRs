Dr AP Jamkhedkar, who was the chairman of Deccan College Post Graduate and Research Institute, Pune, has ended his tenure as the chancellor. Therefore, the matter of appointing the" Chancellor" of the deemed university Deccan College Post Graduate and Research Institute, Pune was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the provisions of Rule 24 (b) of the Amendments in the UGC (Institutions Deemed to be Universities) Regulations, 2010 to the Deccan College Post Graduate and Research Institute, Pune Rules, 2000, the Selection Committee constituted for selection of Vice-Chancellors has submitted its report to the Government on 15th June, 2022. Of the three persons named in the report submitted by the Selection Committee to the Government, Mr. Vivek Devrai (Mr.) has been selected as per the provision of Rule 24 (A) of the Amendment to the UGC (Institutions Deemed to be Universities) Regulations, 2010 in the Rules 2000 of Deccan College Postgraduate and Research Institute, Pune. Bibek Debroy has been appointed as the Chancellor of Deccan College Post Graduate and Research Institute, Pune for a period of five years. This period shall be for a period of five years from the date of assumption of charge by Shri Vivek Devrai. Shri Vivek Devrai will continue as the Chancellor till the new Chancellor is appointed after the expiry of the tenure of Shri Vivek Devrai.
