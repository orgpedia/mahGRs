Madavi and Mrs. Ganakwar, Mrs. Bhosale and Mr. Solanki have been medically qualified for government service by the Medical Board. This should be recorded in their service book.
The medical reports of Lilhare, Matolkar, Pardeshi and Gade are still awaited.
The said candidates are being appointed to the said post as per Rule 11 (1) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (General Conditions of Service) Rules, 1981, subject to submission of medical certificate by the concerned candidate within six months.
The character and antecedents verification reports of all the above candidates are yet to be received by the Government. Therefore, all the candidates should apply to the General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. CHARACTER-2117/ Q.No.483/ 2017/16-A, Dy. The character and antecedents verification certificate as on 28th August, 2017 is required to be submitted within six months from the date of appointment order. Also, if the information submitted by the candidate is found to be untrue, his temporary appointment order will be cancelled and criminal action will be taken under the Indian Penal Code as well as permanent disqualification from government service.
The following candidates will be appointed subject to the following conditions:
1) The pay scale of the above candidates (as per the 6th Pay Commission) is Rs. 15600-39100 plus Grade Pay Rs. 5400/-).
2) In addition to this pay scale, dearness allowance, house rent allowance and local supplementary allowance will be admissible to them at such rates as may be decided by the Government from time to time.

The salary of these candidates should be fixed as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981.
12) The retirement age of the above-mentioned candidates will be as per the rules of the government.
13) The above candidates will not be given travelling allowance to attend the place of appointment.
14) If the above-mentioned candidates want to resign from the government service, it should be sent to the Government Circular, General Administration Department No. SRV 1092/1033/ Q.No. 33/92/8, Dy. According to the provisions of 02/12/1997, the government will be required to give at least one month's prior notice or one month's salary.
15) Those candidates who are employed in the government service are being appointed without seeing the confidential report of such candidates. The heads of the offices appointed by them are being entrusted with the responsibility of verifying the confidential reports of these candidates in their previous five years of government service.
16) As per the provision of educational qualification required for the said post, the candidate must hold at least first class at the graduation level. However, as per the UGC and AICTE policy, some universities/ autonomous bodies award grades to candidates instead of grades. The head of the concerned office should ensure that the degree of such candidates is first class or its equivalent as per the provisions of the Service Admission Rules.
17) The appointment will depend on the authenticity of the certificates submitted by the candidates. If these certificates are found to be false or forged after the appointment, the appointment of these candidates will be automatically terminated.
18) As soon as the order is received, the concerned candidates should appear at the place of appointment and submit a report to the head of the office concerned. The head of the office should submit a report to the government through the director, Directorate of Technical Education.
From the date on which the above candidates appear at the place of their appointment, the charge of the post of Assistant Secretary, Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (Head Office and all concerned Divisional Offices) of the teachers of the teaching cadre in Government Technicians working on deputation/ ad-hoc arrangement in the concerned office will be automatically terminated and they will be relieved.
Orders for posting of the concerned teachers in the said teaching cadre shall be issued separately and the period from the date of termination of their charge in the said office till they join the new place of posting shall be passed as mandatory waiting period as per the provisions of the M.N.S. (Pay) Rules, 1981.
