B. Terms and Conditions:
1) The grant will be granted on a grant-in-aid basis.
The said post-vaccination intake capacity shall be as approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for the academic year 2020-21.
3. The above approval is subject to payment of processing fee by the institution.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.
The institution's eligibility for this course will remain as mentioned above.
The institution shall have to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government as well as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. The formalities and documents will be completed satisfactorily with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, State of Maharashtra.
6. The institution concerned shall complete other legal formalities by depositing the required accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education.
7. Admission to the said Degree and Post-Graduate Degree Courses shall be made as per the rules/ procedure of admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
If the institutions concerned charge more than the tuition fee prescribed by the Fee Regulatory Authority, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
The Institute shall appoint full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the norms. Salaries and allowances should be paid to the employees as per the rules. Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
10.The library in the institution is required to procure the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, in order to make full use of the library to the students, it is necessary to provide courses to the students in the library and also to provide all educational facilities to the students as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). laboratories, libraries, classrooms, etc.
11.R. Regarding Ragging. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999 need to be strictly implemented.
