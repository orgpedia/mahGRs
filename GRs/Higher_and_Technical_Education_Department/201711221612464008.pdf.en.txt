3) Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj's literature.
Rajarshi Shahu Gaurav Granth was first published in 1976 on the occasion of Rajarshi Shahu Chhatrapati Centenary by the Government of Maharashtra. The volume is 628 pages+ 12 photographs. The third revised edition of the same book was in the process of being prepared by the Rajarshi Shahu Charitra Sadhan Prakashan Samiti of the Government of Maharashtra for the last two and a half years. The third edition of the book, which runs into more than 1350 pages, includes some contemporary writers, historians, litterateurs, intellectuals etc. The book also contains the orders, decrees, proclamations and some social laws made by Shahu Maharaj, as well as some rare photographs of Shahu Chhatrapati.
4) Literature of Sahityaratna Lokshahir Annabhau Sathe.
A total of 32 rare novels, story collections, folk-dramas, dramas, ballads, and poems of Sahityaratna Lokshahir Annabhau Sathe have been collected. Of these, 19 books have been published. In the first volume, in the valley of Varna, Chitra, Fakira,
Vyjayanta, Chandana, Avadi, Monkey's Wreath, Vair, and Gulam are the 9 novels and the second volume contains 10 novels, namely, Ranaganga, Pazar, Halguj, Master, Kurup, Tara, Warane's Tiger, Murti, Agnidivya, and Mayura.
