In accordance with the provisions contained in Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Director of Physical Education in Government Colleges/ Institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education is being transferred as a resultant of the following statement.

In order to ensure immediate implementation of the transfer order, the concerned Principal/ Director should strictly follow the following instructions.
(1) On receipt of the transfer order, the Principal concerned shall relieve the transferred Director of Physical Education immediately to join the place of transfer without waiting for the replacement of the transferred Director of Physical Education.
No request for change in the post will be entertained.
The Director of Physical Education shall be liable for disciplinary action if any person directly or indirectly tries to pressurize him to modify the transfer order.
3) No leave of absence shall be granted during the period of joining the transferred post.
Since the transfers are required to be effected effectively, the salary of the transferred Director of Physical Education up to the date of superannuation or upto the maximum date of 30.06.2018 shall be drawn by the college/ institution where he is presently working. Subsequent remuneration in any case from the college/ institution before the transfer.
