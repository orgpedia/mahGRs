Government circular:
Under the General Administration Department. As per the Government Circular dated 15.12.2017, the performance appraisal reports of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre Officers are proposed to be recorded online in MahaPAR system from the year 2017-18.
2.D. As mentioned in Para-5 of the Government Circular dated 15.12.2017, the appointment of Primary Custodian, Additional Custodian, Office Unit and Parmanager for online recording of performance evaluation reports of Group-A (Gazetted) cadre officers in Government Technological/ Government Pharmacy (Diploma)/ Hotel Management and Catering Technology/ Printing System Institutions will be done at the Government level and will be as per Annexure' A' attached.
The Additional Conservator shall appoint one or more Par Managers depending upon the number of officers in the OU to prepare performance appraisal reports of the officers in the Organization Unit (OU) under his control.
The Primary Custodian, the Alternate Custodian, the Organization Unit and the Parmanager so appointed shall, subject to the circular of the Government, submit a report to the General Administration Department. To perform the duties and responsibilities as per instructions dated 15.12.2017.
