Introduction: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to ensure posts in government services for persons with disabilities. Accordingly, the Central Government has published the list of posts ensured for Divyang as per the notification dated 04.01.2021. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions regarding ensuring the posts in the State Government establishments for the disabled persons as per the Government decision dated 02.02.2021. Accordingly, posts in 59 out of 65 cadres from Group-A to Group-D on the establishment of field offices of the Directorate of Higher Education and Directorate of Higher Education under the control of this Department have been ensured for the disabled persons as per the Government decision dated 18.02.2021 and Government Sanction Letter dated 19.03.2021.
