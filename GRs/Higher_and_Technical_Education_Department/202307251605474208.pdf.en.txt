Introduction: The All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi has published its Notes No. 1 here. Pursuant to the approval given on 13.02.2023, the Director, Technical Education vide the aforesaid No. 2 here. As proposed vide letter dated 30.05.2023, the Government was considering to grant approval for closure of Dr. D.Y. Patil School of Business Management, Worle, Pune, a professional institution from the academic year 2022-23.
As per the approval of All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the closure of Dr. D. Y. Patil School of Business Management, Worle, Pune from the academic year 2022-23 is being approved by the Government subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
1) The institution will be required to hand over all the documents related to the courses to be closed in the institution such as marksheets, answer sheets, results sheets, addresses of students, fee receipts, etc. to the concerned university.
(2) The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters relating to the alternative arrangements for teachers and non-teaching staff appointed for the relevant courses in the institution as well as judicial matters in respect of service matters and other matters.
(3) The Company shall be responsible for any future legal matters arising out of this Agreement.

4) If the government's recovery from the institution is still pending, it will be obligatory on the institution head to pay it.
(5) It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay the loans taken by it from the Government or any financial institution in respect of the immovable property required for the conduct of the course in the Institute.
6) During the phased closure of the institution, the institution will take the responsibility of providing educational facilities to the admitted students and alumni of the institution till the completion of the course and to make arrangements for them to sit for the examination. If the institution does not provide these educational facilities, action will be taken against the institution as per the rules and the accumulated deposit with the government will be deposited by the government.
7) The institution shall be bound to pay the salary, salary compensation and other financial contributions due as per rules to the teachers and staff of the institution appointed for the course concerned.
