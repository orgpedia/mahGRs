In order to reduce the intensity and severity of natural calamities such as climate change, increasing temperature, drought and water scarcity, the Government of Maharashtra has taken up the" Harit Maharashtra" programme with a target of planting 50 crore trees between 2017 and 2019. The target is to plant 13 crore and 33 crore trees in 2018 and 2019 respectively. Suggestions are being issued to ensure that this objective is fully achieved and also the trees planted so far are protected, maintained and nurtured well.
Circular: The subject of tree plantation and nurturing should be included as a permanent subject in the agenda of every taluk, district and state level meetings at the field level under the Department of Higher and Technical Education. It should review the tree plantation, survival rate of these trees planted earlier, planting of new trees in place of dead trees, fixing of sites for plantation as per the target of tree plantation in the coming years, availability of saplings, preparation of pits within the stipulated time as per the required technical parameters, availability of funds, etc.
The decisions taken regarding the tree plantation programme should be recorded in the minutes of the meeting.
Also, measures should be taken to resolve their problems and difficulties by being sensitive to the implementation of the decisions mentioned in the history and the difficulties faced by the field officers.
