The proposal:
In pursuance of the aforesaid letter, the Director, Technical Education has submitted a proposal for grant of Government approval for the academic year 2020-2021 for starting new institutions of Post Graduate Degree (MBA) courses and Post Graduate Diploma (PGDM) courses in the State by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. It was pending approval of the government.
The government's decision:
Approval of the Government is being accorded for starting new institutions of Post Graduate Degree (MBA) courses and Post Graduate Diploma (PGDM) courses as indicated in Annexure A for the academic year 2020-2021 subject to the following terms and conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education in this regard as well as under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983. The institution's eligibility for this course will remain as mentioned above.
(3) The institution shall have to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
4) The institution concerned shall meet the other legalities by depositing the required accumulated deposit in the joint account with the Director, Technical Education.

5) Admission to the said Post Graduate Degree/ Diploma Course shall be made as per the rules/ procedure for admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
If the institutions charge more than the tuition fee prescribed by the Fee Regulatory Authority or the concerned university, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
(7) The Institute shall appoint a full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the Government rules. Salaries and allowances should be paid to the employees as per the rules.
Strict action will be taken against the institution if complaints are received from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teaching staff and other employees.
8) The library in the institute is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students.
Also, all the students are trained as per the standards of the All India Council for Technical Education.
