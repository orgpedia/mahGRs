Appointment of Smt.Naveli Deshmukh as the Goodwill Ambassador for Village Reforms in the State.

Mantralaya Extension Bhawan, Madame Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: January 31, 2019
In order to involve the youth in the social and educational activities carried out under the National Service Scheme and to promote and disseminate the schemes of the Government, Smt. Naveli Deshmukh, as the Village Improvement Goodwill Ambassador of the National Service Scheme of the Department of Higher and Technical Education, Government of Maharashtra, has been appointed as the District Education Officer (DSE) for the academic year 2017-18 on a non-monetary basis. As on 06.02.2018, the appointment was made for the first time.
However, the proposal to extend the tenure of Mrs. Naveli Deshmukh as Village Reforms Goodwill Ambassador was under consideration. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision:
Government Decision: In order to make the social work done by Smt. Naveli Deshmukh an inspiration for the youth, Smt. Naveli Deshmukh is being appointed as the Village Improvement Goodwill Ambassador under the National Service Scheme, on a non-remunerative basis, for the academic year 2018-19.
Mrs. Naveli Deshmukh will be responsible for organizing lectures by Mrs. Naveli Deshmukh, the Goodwill Ambassador for Village Reforms, organizing programs for interaction with students and youth, and in that program, Mrs. Naveli Deshmukh will cooperate in achieving the various schemes of the government and the activities and objectives undertaken by the National Service Scheme as per the government decision of this office dated September 26, 2017. The Programme Coordinator and Director, National Service Scheme (NSS) will be responsible for the communication, promotion and dissemination of village improvement activities to the students of the subordinate colleges of all the universities in consultation with Smt. Naveli Demukh. The programme coordinator and director, National Service Scheme will be responsible for the planning. The State Liaison Officer and Officer on Special Duty, National Service Scheme, Department of Higher and Technical Education, Mantralaya Cell will act as Controllers.
