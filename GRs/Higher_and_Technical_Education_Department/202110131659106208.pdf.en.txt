The proposal:
As per the provisions of Section 80 (9) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, there is a provision to appoint a Presiding Officer in the University and College Tribunal. However, the post of Presiding Officer to be appointed in the University and College Tribunal at Mumbai University, Mumbai, is currently vacant.
Therefore, the matter of appointing the post was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision
The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016
The University and Colleges of the University of Mumbai, Mumbai as per the provisions of Section 80 (9)
The vacant post of Presiding Officer on the Tribunal is held by Mr. A.M. Dhawale, Hon. Retired
The judge holds office for a period of three years.
Appointments are made from the day.
Mr. A.M. He requested the retired judge to take charge of the post.
The report should be sent to the government immediately.
Mr. A.M. Dhawale, Shri. Retired Judge is appointed as Judge of Government Judgment, Higher and Technical and Service Division No. The terms of service set forth in USG 1391/ (2089)-VISHI-4, dated August 19, 1995, shall apply.
