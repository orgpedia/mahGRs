Read: - 1) Government Decision No. Appointment of Department of Higher and Technical Education - 2010 / (Q. No.337 / 2010) / MC 5, dated 23rd August, 2010.
2) GOVERNMENT RECOMMENDANCE NUMBERS-2018 / Q. No. 56/18 VISION-1, dated 8th March, 2019.
3) Letter from the Director, Higher Education No. UNI / E 2516516 / VISHI-1, dated 23rd December, 2024.
Preamble: - There should be uniformity in the lien of teaching / non-teaching staff in non-agricultural universities, aided / unaided colleges and in what form the lien holder should be appointed to the post during the tenure of the lien. The instructions in this regard have been issued vide the above Government Decision dated 23rd August, 2010. As per point number (3) of this Government decision, the lease can be granted for a maximum period of two years. Also, in case of appointment to a statutory post in the University Act, the lien is granted for a maximum period of 5 years. Employees appointed to the above statutory post may face issues with their lien if the service period of retirement at the prescribed age is more than 5 years. Therefore, the government was considering revising the tenure of the lease.
Government Decision: - In case of appointment of teaching / non-teaching staff in non-agricultural universities, aided / unaided colleges, to a statutory post in the University Act, they are granted a lien for a period of 5 years under Issue No. (3) of the Government Decision dated 23rd August, 2010. This is being amended to make the tenure of the lease 10 years.
This Government decision is available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharashtra.gov.in.
