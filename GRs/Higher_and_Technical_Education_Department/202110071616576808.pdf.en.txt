Preamble: In response to the pending questions of the teachers and non-teaching staff of non-agricultural universities and affiliated colleges in the state, Ma. Minister of Higher and Technical Education. The meeting was held on 17.08.2021. In the meeting, The Minister has directed to constitute a committee comprising of public representatives and representatives of teachers and non-teaching staff associations to review the pending issues of the teachers and non-teaching staff of the universities and colleges and accordingly make recommendations to the Government. Accordingly, the government had constituted a committee to look into the issue.
Government decision: As per the directions given by the Minister (Higher and Technical Education) in the meeting dated 17.08.2021, the Committee is being constituted as follows:
