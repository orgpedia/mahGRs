Shrikant Londhe, Associate Professor, Mechanical Engineering, Government College of Engineering, Chandrapur has applied for transfer to Government College of Engineering, Amravati. Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai, Bench at Nagpur in the original application No.

" The O. A. is allowed. The respondent no. 1 is directed to take appropriate decision on the proposal of respondent No. 3 Dated 11/05/2016 and to issue transfer order in respect of applicant in any of the posts lying vacant at Amravati. In pursuance of the aforesaid order of the Tribunal, the matter of issue of transfer order of Mr. Londhe, Associate Professor, Mechanical Engineering was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
In accordance with the aforesaid order of the Tribunal and with the approval of the competent authority for transfer under Rule 4 (4) (ii) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, Mr. Londhe is being transferred as per the request mentioned in column-3 below.
