Introduction: Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar is known in the history of the country as a good administrator, valiant fighter, justice-loving person, as well as a people's mother who did innumerable public welfare works. Solapur University has been named after this great woman. Construction of pedestal, landscape and beautification and construction of tower for the memorial of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar in the campus of Solapur University. 14,24,92, 238/- in the Budget Reference No. 1 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. In accordance with the said Administrative Sanction Reference No. 2 The Government has decided to provide Rs. 1,73,91, 000/- has been disbursed. The university has requested under the reference letter to disburse the remaining funds as funds are required for further construction. The Finance Department was requested to release the budgeted funds for this work. The Finance Department has agreed to release the budgeted funds for this work. The government was contemplating to provide funds for the purpose.
The government has decided to construct a memorial and tower of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar at Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University. As per the administrative approval vide Government Resolution dated 07.12.2021, Rs. 1,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Crore only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
The fund should be disbursed and withdrawn within the limits of administrative approval.
