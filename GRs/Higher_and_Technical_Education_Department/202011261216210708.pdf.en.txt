A committee has been formed to investigate the irregularities in the evaluation work of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Resolution No: VIPA 2020/ No.141/ VISHI-3
Ministry Extension Building, Mumbai-400032.
Date is November 26, 2020
Introduction: In the Second Budget Session of the year 2020, the Legislative Council Starred Question No. 38" Dr. " A case has been registered against Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University for alleged irregularities in the evaluation process. In reply to the starred question in the Legislative Council on 13.03.2020, Hon. The Minister of Higher and Technical Education has given a time-bound assurance. The government was considering setting up the committee.
Government Decision:- In accordance with the above assurance, it is being approved to constitute a committee of the following members under the chairmanship of Deputy Secretary, Higher and Technical Education to investigate the irregularities in the evaluation work in Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad.
