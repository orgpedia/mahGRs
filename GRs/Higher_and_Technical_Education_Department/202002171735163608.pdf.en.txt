Circular: All educational institutions under the Directorate of Higher Education, Technical Education and Arts have been provided with name boards and boards with information about the institution or various other aspects. It has been noticed that in many educational institutions, the boards are in English language and the boards are not in Marathi language. However, this circular is being notified as follows. - (1) The name boards to be installed in all non-agricultural universities, all deemed universities, all self-financed universities and all government/ non-government, aided/ unaided/ permanent unaided and model degree colleges and institutes under the Directorate of Higher Education, Directorate of Technical Education and Directorate of Arts.
(2) Wherever the name boards are displayed in the English language, the same shall be in Marathi along with the English language.
(3) All the Directors and the Universities concerned shall immediately communicate the above instructions to the colleges/ technical institutions/ institutes under them at their level. All colleges/ institutions/ institutions should take appropriate action in this regard.
