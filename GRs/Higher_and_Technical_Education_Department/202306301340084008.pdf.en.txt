Final approval has been granted to the new colleges as per the recommendation of the University under the relevant Government Order, as well as new subjects/ courses, additional batches, new faculties under the expansion. However, the concerned university as well as the institution have requested to get the said recognition order amended. Accordingly, the recommendations and documents of the universities were examined as per the available documents. Accordingly, the Government is giving its approval to make the corrections mentioned in the following statement as the corrections are required to be made in accordance with the request made by the concerned universities as well as the concerned institutions.
