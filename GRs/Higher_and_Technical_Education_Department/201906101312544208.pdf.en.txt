The above committee should submit its report to the government regarding the amendment of the accounting code within 1 month as required.
The above Committee should hold its meetings in non-agricultural Universities as per the convenience of the Committee members. The notice should be given to the concerned university at least 8 days in advance.
The necessary honorarium and allowances of the members of the committee should be paid immediately by the respective universities.
