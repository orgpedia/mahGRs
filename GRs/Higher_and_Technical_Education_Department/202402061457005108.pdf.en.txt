Introduction: - To fill up the posts of Non-Teaching by the University of Mumbai. The advertisement was released on 28 March 2013. The qualifications for these positions were prescribed by the university. As many as 146 employees have been selected by the university under the recruitment process implemented accordingly. The qualifications prescribed by the university as per the provision in Section 5 (11) of the Maharashtra University Act, 1994, are required to be recognised by the government. However, the university has reported that it was inadvertent in obtaining such recognition. Also, the request for post-facto approval of SA Sea was made by the Director, Higher Education and University of Mumbai and the following decision has been taken in the present case.
Government Decision: - To fill up non-teaching posts from Mumbai University. The advertisement was released on 28 March 2013. The qualifications for the posts mentioned in this advertisement are being given post facto recognition as per the provisions of Section 5 (11) of the Maharashtra University Act, 1994 as follows: -
