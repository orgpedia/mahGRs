Lakshmibai National Institute of Physical Education, Gwalior is an institution declared as Deemed to be University by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India vide notification dated 21.9.1995 under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956. The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate and diploma courses in physical education and sports education. The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, has issued instructions to the Education and Sports Departments of all the States to consider the Diploma and Post Graduate Diploma courses of the Institute as equivalent to the Diploma courses of Netaji Subhas Institute of Sports, Patiala.
Accordingly, the Department of Sports, Government of Uttar Pradesh has approved the Physical Education courses of Lakshmibai National Institute of Physical Education, Gwalior as equivalent to the Diploma courses of Netaji Subhas Institute of Sports, Patiala.
