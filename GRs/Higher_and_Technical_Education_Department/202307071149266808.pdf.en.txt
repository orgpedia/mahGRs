Terms and Conditions:
1) The grant will be non-recurring.
2) In this case, as prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education. The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfil the conditions prescribed under the Government Resolution dated 21st May, 1983.
(3) The institution shall be required to enter into an agreement to accept all the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government and prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. The formalities and documents will be completed with the consent of the Director, Technical Education, Maharashtra State.
(4) The private institution concerned shall meet the other legal requirements by depositing the requisite consolidated deposit with the Director, Technical Education, in a joint account.
5) Admission to the said Degree and Post Graduate Degree Courses will be done as per the rules/ procedure of admission to the respective course as approved by the Government.
If the fees charged by the institutions concerned are more than the tuition fees prescribed by the fee regulatory authority, action will be taken against them as per the Maharashtra Educational Institutions (Prohibition of Capitation Fee) Act, 1987.
(7) The Institute shall appoint a full-time faculty and other technical staff to run the courses as per the Government rules. They should be given salary and allowances as per the rules, and strict action will be taken against the institution if there are complaints from the students to the government regarding the salary and allowances of the teachers and other employees.
8) The library in the institute is required to purchase the required books, journals as per the standards of All India Council for Technical Education. Also, the library should be made fully accessible to the students. as well

9) Regarding the ragging. The directions of the Supreme Court and the Maharashtra Prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999, need to be strictly implemented.
The present post-merger intake capacity of the courses in the merging institutions under the same management shall be as approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for the academic year 2023-24.

12) In case it is found that the Institute has taken loans from the Government or any financial institution in respect of immovable property required for running the course in the Institute, it shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay the same.
13) While closing the relevant courses in the institution in a phased manner, it will be mandatory for the institution to provide educational facilities to the admitted students and alumni of the institution till the completion of the course and to make arrangements to sit for the examination. If the institution does not provide these educational facilities, action will be taken against the institution as per the rules and the government will deposit the accumulated deposit with the government.
The Institute shall be obliged to pay the salary, salary compensation and other financial contributions due as per rules to the teachers and staff of the institution appointed for the relevant closing course.
15) All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi under Community College Scheme for B.Voc. Voc. (NSQF Level VII) It will be mandatory for the concerned institution and skill knowledge provider to comply with the special rules, terms and conditions created for the implementation of the degree course.
16) For affiliation of B.Voc. course, the concerned institution will be required to obtain the approval/ affiliation of the concerned university as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
17) Admission to B.Voc. course shall be made at the institution level as prescribed by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi in its Approval Process Handbook 2023-24.
18) The institution shall take action as per the rules for obtaining approval of the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi for extension of B.Voc. course.
Since the course is being implemented under the Community College Scheme and the admission of students is being done at the institution level, the benefit of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme will not be permissible to the students admitted to this course.
