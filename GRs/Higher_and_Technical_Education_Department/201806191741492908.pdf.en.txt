(2) The period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall be admissible to the Principal who is transferred for administrative reasons as per the rules of the Government.
The following instructions should be strictly followed to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) On receipt of the order of transfer, the Principal concerned shall immediately retire from the existing institution and join the institution transferred. All the Principals should immediately join the place of the transferred institution by handing over the charge to the senior department head/ faculty of the institution without waiting for the arrival of the substitute officer.
(2) No request for change of posting shall be entertained. If any officer directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the transfer order, such officer shall be liable to disciplinary action.

(3) No leave of the Principal shall be sanctioned without prior approval of the Administrative Department.
(4) In order to ensure effective implementation of these transfers, the salaries of all the principals transferred up to the date of retirement or up to the maximum date of 30th June, 2018 shall be drawn by the institution in which they are presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the pre-transfer institution under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the post-transfer organization after the concerned officer joins the transfer organization. The drawing and disbursing officer of the institution where the salary from 01.07.2018 onwards was paid before the transfer will be held personally liable.
(5) This order shall come into force immediately. The Directorate of Technical Education should inform this department about the transferred principal being relieved of his duties and joining the place of transfer.
