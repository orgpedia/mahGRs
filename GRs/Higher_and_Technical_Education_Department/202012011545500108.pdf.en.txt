GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the approval given by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, the Government is granting approval for starting the Post H.Sc. Diploma Courses of Defence Institute for Geo-Spatial Information and Training, Pune from the academic year 2020-21 as shown in Annexure A.
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.

The intake capacity of the institution for the said course will be limited as indicated against their name in Annexure A.
The course will require affiliation/ equivalency from the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education.
