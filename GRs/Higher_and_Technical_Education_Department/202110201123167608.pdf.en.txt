Introduction: The Council of Architecture, New Delhi has referred to the above. Pursuant to the approval given vide letter dated 12/08/2021, the Director, Technical Education vide his Reference No. As proposed vide letter dated 16/09/2021, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the reduction in the intake capacity of undergraduate courses in the existing Institute of Vastu Shastra mentioned in Annexure" B" from the academic year 2021-22.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the approval of Council of Architecture, New Delhi and as proposed by the Director, Technical Education, the Government is granting approval for reduction in intake capacity of B.Arch. course in Pillai HOC College of Architecture, Rasayani, Mahatma Education Society from the academic year 2021-22 as mentioned in Annexure" B" subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions:
(1) The Head of the Institution shall be responsible for all matters relating to the service and other matters of the faculty/ staff appointed for the concerned course. In case of any court case in the future, the institution will be responsible for it.
2) If the government's recovery from the institution is still pending, it will be mandatory for the institution head to pay it.
(3) It shall be the sole responsibility of the Institute to repay the loans taken by it from the Government or any other financial institution in respect of the immovable properties required for the conduct of the course in the Institute.
(4) The institution shall be obliged to provide educational facilities and make arrangements for the admission of students and alumni of the institution to sit for the examination until the completion of the course. If the institution does not provide these educational facilities, action will be taken against the institution as per the rules.
