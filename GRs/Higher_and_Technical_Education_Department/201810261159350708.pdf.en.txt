Introduction: Section 32 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 provides for reservation of seats for students with significant disabilities in government institutions of higher education and in institutions receiving grants from the government.
1) All Government institutions of higher education and other higher education institutions receiving aid from the Government shall reserve not less than five per cent seats for persons with benchmark disabilities.
2) The person with benchmark disabilities shall be given an upper age relaxation of five years for admission in institutions of higher education.
As per the above provision, the matter of reserving seats for students with significant disabilities in the admission of all courses run by Government Arts Colleges and Non-Government Aided Arts Institutions was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Admission to all courses run by Government Arts Colleges and Non-Government Aided Art Institutions under the control of Directorate of Arts.
5% seats are reserved for students with disabilities.
b) The upper age limit for admission is being relaxed by 5 years for students with significant disabilities.
For this, the director, art director should make necessary amendments in the admission rules of various courses.
