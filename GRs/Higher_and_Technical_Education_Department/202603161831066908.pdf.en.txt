The Proposal: -
The Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and various Government Educational Institutions functioning under it has issued a notification vide Government Reference No. 3 has been approved by the Government here. The Director of Technical Education, Directorate of Technical Education, Mumbai has issued a circular to the Government of India vide Reference No. According to the letter, a total of 4,016 posts under his jurisdiction have been filled. Government Decision: - The Directorate of Technical Education, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and various Government Educational Institutions functioning thereunder, has sanctioned a total of 4016 temporary posts of officers and staff in Administrative Offices. The continuation from 01.03.2026 to 31.08.2026 is being approved by this government decision.
