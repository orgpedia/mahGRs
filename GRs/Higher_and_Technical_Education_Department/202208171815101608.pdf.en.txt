As per the procedure laid down under the Government Decision dated 13.09.2017, the non-agriculture universities in the State have submitted proposals for new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties for the academic year 2022-23 to the Government for approval. The proposals were examined in accordance with the norms laid down under the relevant Government decisions. As per the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, the existing colleges are being sanctioned new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties as per the existing policy of the Government under the principle of permanent non-grant of grant subject to the following terms and conditions.

Terms and Conditions: 1. As the aided colleges which have been sanctioned new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties in the above statement are on permanent non-grant basis, such colleges shall not be able to demand teachers and non-teaching staff on grant basis in accordance with this recognition.
The colleges which have been sanctioned new courses/ subjects/ special level subjects, additional batches, new faculties as above, will have to submit an undertaking to their Divisional Joint Directors that they will not demand any grant in future. 100/- on non-judicial stamp paper.
The University concerned should not start the process of affiliation without the college submitting an undertaking or obtaining the certificate of the Divisional Joint Director.
The colleges are required to appoint faculty/ staff as per the norms prescribed by the concerned university and educational qualification prescribed by the University Grants Commission and provide all other necessary infrastructure and the same shall be ensured by the Joint Director of the Department by 30th August and only after that the aforesaid condition no. 2 certificate from the university.
