Intimation: The Institute is granted Intentions under the provision of Section 109 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016. Accordingly, the issue of issuance of Intent Certificate for the academic year 2023-2024 was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: As per the procedure prescribed under Section 109 of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016 and the decision of the Government in this regard, all the non-agricultural universities of the State have recommended for the approval of the Government to get the Intent Letter of Intent for starting new colleges in the academic year 2023-24.
The proposals received from the Universities have been examined as per the norms prescribed for grant of Intentions as per the decision of the Government in question. Accordingly, in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 109 (3) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, a total of 117 institutions mentioned in the accompanying Statement-A are being granted Intentions to start new colleges on a perpetual non-grant basis. Further, in exercise of the powers conferred by the following proviso to Section 109 (3) (d) of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016, a total of 04 institutions mentioned in the accompanying Statement-B are being granted Intentions on a permanent basis on a non-grant basis for starting new colleges.
The following terms and conditions will be applicable for the aforesaid Intentions: 1) The Intentions to start a new college as per the prevailing policy of the Government shall be for starting a college on a permanent non-subsidized basis.
2) Since the said Letter of Intent has been issued by the institution to the new college to meet the prescribed terms and conditions before obtaining final approval, the institution shall not start the college without obtaining final approval of the Government to start the new college.
The letter of intent issued by the government to start a new college will be valid till January 31, 2024. During this period, it will be mandatory to submit a compliance report to the university along with the documents required for final approval, after fulfilling the terms and conditions on the basis of which the letter of intent has been issued.
