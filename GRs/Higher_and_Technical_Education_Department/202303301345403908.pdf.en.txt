The proposal:
The Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai has temporarily resumed its operations at 2nd, 3rd, 4th and 6th Floor of MTNL, Powai.
Also, 295 rooms have been provided on lease for hostel accommodation for students. Reference No. 2 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. The rent payable for the period April, 2022 to June, 2022 for the financial year 2022-23 as per the old lease agreement is Rs. 4,71,99, 612/ Payable to Referral No. The decision has been taken 1. Now the reference No. The University has requested for release of funds for payment of rent to MTNL for the remaining 09 months (July-2023 to March-2023) as per the new lease agreement vide letter No. As per the said request, the matter of providing funds to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai University for payment of rent was under consideration of the Government.
The Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai has temporarily started MTNL at 2nd, 3rd, 4th and 6th Floor, Powai. The rent payable for the period July, 2022 to March, 2023 for the financial year 2022-23 is Rs. 18,44,51, 310/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores Forty Four Lakhs Fifty One Thousand Three Hundred and Ten only) is being sanctioned subject to the following conditions.

(1) The Executive Council shall be responsible for administering the grants sanctioned by the Government as per the Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014 by depositing them in the University Fund. Also, the university should take action to approve the expenditure incurred from the grant by exchanging the statement of accounts of the expenditure before the General Council.
(2) It shall be the responsibility of the Vice-Chancellor to see that the grant is spent for the purpose for which the grant has been sanctioned by the Government to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai. In this regard, they should submit its utility certificate to the government through the Directorate of Higher Education with the approval of the Executive Council.
(3) All the documents and accounts of all the above grants are to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-2, Nagpur, as the case may be, and if so demanded, all the documents shall be submitted to him by the Law University for examination.
(4) The University shall use the grant-in-aid sanctioned for the purpose for which the grant-in-aid is sanctioned.
(5) The provisions and conditions of the Financial Powers Rules book shall be strictly complied with in this regard. Also, the existing rules and conditions of the government should be taken into consideration.
The Director (Higher Education), State of Maharashtra, Pune shall be responsible for handing over the said approved provision to the Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai immediately. Further, all the documents and accounts of the above grant are entitled to be examined by the Accountant General, Maharashtra-1, Mumbai or the officer authorised for the purpose by the Accountant General-2, Nagpur on a case to case basis. The grant granted to the university should be used for the purpose for which it was sanctioned. Also, it should be ensured that the expenditure is fully met within the stipulated period and the utilization certificate should be submitted to the government through the Director, Higher Education, Pune within the prescribed time.
