The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016
The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016. It is effective from 1st March 2017. Section-15 of the said Act provides for the creation of the statutory post of Dean of Faculty. There is a provision of one Dean post for each faculty in every university and it is fully paid. The appointment will be made on the basis of the recommendations of the selection committee. The term of office of the Dean is equal to the term of office of the Vice-Chancellor or till he attains the prescribed age, whichever is earlier. The qualifications and experience for the purpose of selection to the said post shall be the same as that of the post of Professor or Principal and shall not be less than fifteen years of total teaching and research experience. As per Section 34 (2) of the said Act, the University shall consist of the following faculties:
Faculty of Science and Technology 2. Faculty of Commerce and Management 3. Faculty of Humanities 4. Faculty of Inter-Faculty Studies
Section 16 of the Act prescribes the powers and duties of the office of the Dean of the Faculty. Considering the duties, responsibilities and educational importance of the post of Dean in the university, the government was considering the creation of the posts of Dean in the universities.
