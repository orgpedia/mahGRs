GOVERNMENT DECISION, DEPARTMENT OF HIGHER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION, NO. ANUDA-2011/ (200/11) RESTRICTION-6/ MASHI-4, dated 26th May, 2022.
The proposal:
It has been decided to sanction 100 per cent grant-in-aid to one college/ faculty in each taluka. Accordingly, under the Government decision dated 26/05/2022, a total of 18 colleges have been approved to bring the respective faculties on 100% grant subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in the Government decision. Form-A, Roll No.2 of the said Government Resolution has approved a symbolic supplementary demand presented in the Fourth (Winter) Session of December, 2022 for inclusion of salary expenditure of teaching and non-teaching posts admissible to the Faculty of Science of Anjuman Islam Janjira Degree College of Science, Murud Janjira G. Raigad, mentioned herein. Accordingly, the matter of approving the inclusion of the salary expenses of these posts under the committed expenditure was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As mentioned in the Preamble, in the next prospectus to be approved for the Science stream in Anjuman Islam Janjira Degree College of Science, Murud Janjira G. Raigad, a total of 23 posts of 12 teachers and 11 non-teaching staff with prescribed qualifications and duly appointed in the prescribed manner, the salary expenditure of the teachers/ non-teaching staff to be included in the committed expenditure.
The decision, dated 26/05/2022 is subject to the following terms and conditions.
