(2) The period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance shall not be admissible to the transferred Librarian and Director of Physical Education as per the rules of the Government.
The transfers are being made with the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfers of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.
The following instructions shall be strictly complied with by the Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution so that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently.
(1) The order shall come into force immediately.
2) After receiving the transfer order, the replacement of the transferred librarian and the director of physical education will not wait for the appearance of the alternate librarian and the director of physical education.

In view of this, the concerned Principal/ Director should immediately relieve the transferred Librarian and Director of Physical Education to join the place of transfer. Also, the concerned Librarian and Director of Physical Education should immediately join the college/ institution where they have been transferred from the existing college/ institution.
(3) The transferred Librarians and Physical Education Directors shall be reinstated without any further delay after they have been relieved from their duties by the respective colleges/ institutions. The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be held responsible in case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-appointment of a Librarian and Director of Physical Education.
(4) No request for change in post will be entertained.
If any librarian or physical education director tries to directly or indirectly pressurize the transfer order, the concerned librarian and physical education director will be liable to take disciplinary action.
5) No leave of absence will be granted during the period of joining at the place of transfer.
Since these transfers are required to be effected with immediate effect, the salary of the transferred Librarians and Physical Education Directors up to the date of retirement or upto the maximum date i.e. 15th October, 2020 shall be drawn by the college/ institution where they are presently working. Subsequent salary should not be withdrawn from the college/ institution before transfer under any circumstances. This salary should be drawn by the college/ institution after the transfer of the concerned librarian and the Director of Physical Education after joining the college/ institution. They will be held personally liable if the salary due from October 16, 2020 is paid by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the college/ institution where they were working before the transfer.
The Principal/ Director of the concerned college/ institution will be personally responsible for the immediate release of the transferred Librarian and Director of Physical Education.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune should submit the report to the Government before 16th October, 2020.
