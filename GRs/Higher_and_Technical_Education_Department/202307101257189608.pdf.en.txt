Preamble: As per the letter dated 23rd June, 2023 received from the Member Secretary, All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Sanjeevan Engineering and Technology Institute, Panhala, Kolhapur has been approved to start a new degree course in Applied Arts (BFA) and Drawing and Painting (BFA-Painting) from the academic year 2023-24 on a non-subsidized basis with an intake capacity of 30 students. The facilities required to start this new course have been examined by a two-member committee at the level of the Directorate of Arts and the proposal for the same has been received under the letter of reference (4) along with the recommendations. Accordingly, the government was considering to approve the introduction of a new degree course in Applied Arts (BFA) and Drawing and Painting (BFA-Painting) in the private unaided institution Sanjeevan Engineering and Technology Institute, Panhala, Kolhapur. The following decision has been taken:
As per the approval accorded vide letter dated 22.06.2023 from the Member Secretary, All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Sanjeevan Engineering and Technology Institute, Panhala, Kolhapur, a private unaided institution is being approved to start BFA (Applied Arts) and BFA (Painting) courses from the academic year 2023-24 with an intake capacity of 30 students on a non-subsidized basis subject to the following conditions. :
1) The grant will be on a grant-in-aid basis.
The institution concerned shall be bound to fulfill the conditions prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education as well as prescribed under the Government Decision dated 21st May, 1983.
The maximum intake capacity for this course will be 30 students.
