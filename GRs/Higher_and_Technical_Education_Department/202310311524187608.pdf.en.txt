Gokhale Institute of Political Science and Economics (Deemed to be University), Pune, established in 1930, is one of India's leading institutions for teaching and research in economics. Providing various equipments for the research and teaching purposes of this institution will have a positive impact on the academic, research, innovation and ranking of the university. Also the equipment is required to upgrade the range of library and information center. Therefore, the University has decided to procure the equipment and machinery through the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal of the Central Government vide Reference No. 1 is reported here. This includes the following types of equipment: These devices like Firewall Fortinet, Power Backup, Coupler Machine, Audio System, Online UPS for Digital Library etc. will be useful for the students. Accordingly, the tariff has been submitted for approval for purchase of various equipment. As per the proposal, the matter of administrative approval of the procurement process was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS:- The total estimated cost for the procurement of the necessary academic and digital equipment, various machinery etc. for the Gokhale Institute of Political Science and Economics (Deemed to be University), Pune, as proposed by the University, is Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for procurement of 159.30 Lakhs (Rupees One Crore Sixty-Nine Lakh Thirty Thousand only):

The purchase of such equipment shall be subject to the following conditions:
1) The requirement/ demand for the said equipment and material should be ascertained. Thereafter, the tender process will be initiated. This procedure should be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, No. Bhankhas-2014/ No. 82/ Part III/ Industry-4, dated December 1, 2016.
2) Ensure that the specifications of the equipment and materials are as per requirement and up to date. The technical specifications of the items to be procured should be as per the specifications agreed by the concerned experts (Technical Committee/ Officials).
Also, it should be ensured that the technical specification is not tailored in a manner that will benefit any particular company.
After that, you have to buy them.
(3) The prescribed procurement procedure should be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the revised rule book of procedures for office procurement to be carried out by government departments published under the Government Decision, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, No. 2014/ PR No. 82/ Part III/ Industry-4, dated December 1, 2016.
4) The rates of the proposed procurement are estimated at the time of granting administrative approval. Therefore, the price should be fixed on the basis of the GEM portal and the procurement process of other states. It should be ensured that the price is less than the market price and not more than the rate at which the supplier has supplied the item to other places.
5) Pre/ post dispatch random sample inspection of the items to be purchased should be done to ensure that only the items with proper and correct specifications are purchased.
6) Storage and distribution of the items to be procured, as well as supply chain management and inventory management, should be done in a maintenanceable manner to ensure that these items do not remain unused and are used within the stipulated time.
7) Reconciliation Certificate should be submitted by the Director, Higher Education, Pune.
8) Also, it should be ensured that there is no duplication in the purchase of these items.
The Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) guidelines should be strictly followed while carrying out the entire procurement process.
