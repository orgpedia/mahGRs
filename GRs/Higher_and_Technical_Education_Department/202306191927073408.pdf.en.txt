the Government Order
Dr Varsha Andhare, Assistant Professor, Government Institute of Science, Mumbai, has been given the additional charge of Superintendent, Savitri Devi Phule Women's Hostel, Mumbai.
""" A very unfortunate and shocking incident has taken place at Savitri Devi Phule Women's Hostel on June 6, 2023." As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2, a two-member committee has been constituted to conduct a high-level inquiry into the incident. In the interim report submitted by the two-member committee, objections have been raised about the functioning of the superintendent and in the next inquiry, the statements of the girls living in the hostel and others concerned will be taken. To ensure that the investigation of this committee is not hindered or pressured, Dr. Varsha Andhare is being suspended until the investigation of this incident by this two-member committee.
Further, it is being ordered that during the period this order is in force, the headquarters of Dr. Varsha Andhare will remain in Mumbai and she will not be able to leave the headquarters without prior permission of the government.
The Director, Higher Education, Pune shall forthwith entrust the additional charge of the post of Superintendent, Savitri Devi Phule Women's Hostel, Mumbai, to another competent officer and submit a report to the Government.
