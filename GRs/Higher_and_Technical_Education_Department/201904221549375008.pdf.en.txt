Preamble: The Project Approval Board of the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) has been constituted to provide educational facilities in the district on the basis of Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER), number of girls and population of backward classes. In the 12th meeting held on 25th May, 2018, approval has been given for setting up of new Government Model Degree Colleges in two districts of Maharashtra, namely Nandurbar and Washim.
While approving the proposal to set up new Government Model Degree Colleges in two districts of Nandurbar and Washim under RUSA, the Cabinet directed the department to take measures to improve the performance of the 7 Model Degree Colleges approved earlier. Accordingly, the government had constituted a committee to look into the matter.
The government has decided to set up two new government model degree colleges in Nandurbar and Washim districts under RUSA. Pursuant to the direction given by the Cabinet in its meeting held on 15th January, 2019, it is being approved to constitute a Committee under the Chairmanship of Deputy Secretary (Mashi) to suggest measures to improve the performance of 7 Model Degree Colleges approved earlier under this Department.
