The proposal:
10% reservation for Economically Weaker Section (EWS) in direct admission to Engineering and Pharmacy courses for the academic year 2019-20. The writ petition No. 4965/ 2019 was filed before the Nagpur bench of the Bombay High Court. In response to this petition, The Nagpur Bench of the Bombay High Court has given the judgment dated 30/7/2019. The All India Council for Technical Education vide its letter dated 25/7/2019 has clarified and clarified the reservation of seats for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) for the direct B.Ed. Considering the orders of the High Court, the Directors have submitted a proposal to the Government vide letter dated 1/8/2019. The government was considering issuing an order in this regard.
