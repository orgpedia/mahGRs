Regarding the appointment of a committee for the establishment of an online university in the state of Maharashtra.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Higher and Technical Education
Government Resolution No: Parikh-2021/ P.No.74/ Vishy-4
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg
Ministry, Mumbai: 400032.
Date: December 9, 2021.
Preamble: In the wake of COVID-19 pandemic in the country, the traditional public universities/ colleges etc. in the State are imparting education to the students through online mode using various online portals and applications. Students who live far away from the educational institution and are unable to attend the university/ college regularly, will not incur additional expenses in terms of student accommodation, travel, etc., in addition to the university fees, as it is not mandatory for such students to show physical attendance due to the establishment of the online university.
For candidates who want to pursue online education, it is financially affordable and easy to make a significant difference in the lives of students with the establishment of online universities. Also, it will be possible to continue academic activities without interruption during emergencies like COVID-19.
The National Education Policy 2020 has highlighted the importance of online and digital education. Considering this, the government was considering to form a committee to conduct a comprehensive study in connection with the establishment of an online university to provide online/ digital education to students at affordable prices.
Government Decision: The following committee is being formed to study the National Education Policy 2020 and online universities established in the developed countries of the world and recommend to the government to establish online universities in the state.

Functions of the Committee:
The above committee will submit a report to the government after considering the following aspects: 1) To study the online universities established in the world advanced countries and determine the policy of online university in the state on the same lines.
(2) It shall be appropriate to establish the University on the basis of the rules prescribed by the University Grants Commission from time to time as well as in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016.
To make recommendations in regard to imparting education to the students of the State, taking into account the provisions of online education in the National Education Policy, 2020.
4) Details of the structure and functioning of the online universities set up in other states or how.
5) Detailed information about the basic facilities, manpower, equipment required for the online university, its cost.
6) Determining the quality criteria of teachers and staff teaching in the online university.
7) Study and submit reports on online university courses, examinations, results of those examinations, degrees.
8) To make recommendations regarding the time, duration, fees, etc. of online university education.
9) To make clear recommendations on what courses will be taught in the online university, the existing traditional universities, open universities, their scope, courses, demonstrations and degrees.
10) Details on how to manage the administration of the online university.
11) To make recommendations in line with the need for online universities to nurture ethics and values like a traditional university.
12) Detailed information on the cost of digital infrastructure and content for the online university, considering the rapid change in technology in the education sector.
To study and report on the National Education Policy, 2020.
14) Details of how the courses that are taught through demonstrations will be taught online.
15) To submit a detailed report with details on whether or not it would be appropriate to provide online education to students through non-agricultural universities in the state instead of setting up an online university.
16) To make recommendations on the basis of some other important points, if deemed necessary, in addition to the above.
The committee will submit its report to the government within six months.
