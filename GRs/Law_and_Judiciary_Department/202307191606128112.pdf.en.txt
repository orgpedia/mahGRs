Government Order No. 1020/296/ PR No.110/ KA-16, dated 05.10.2020
Permanent employees of Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi, are reimbursed for the educational fees of their wards. On the same lines, the Government Order No. (2) Accordingly, the Government is approving the reimbursement of educational fees of all the contractual employees working in various departments of Shri Saibaba Sansthan for two academic years at an estimated cost of Rs. 50,00,000/-for the year 2020-2021 and Rs. 50,00,000/-for the year 2021-22.
The contractual employees of the Institute shall not be entitled to claim any other service or establishment benefit or other permanent benefits from the Institute on the basis of this order. For this, written assurance will be taken from all the contractual employees by Shri Saibaba Sansthan.
The Chief Executive Officer, Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi shall submit a performance report along with the statement of expenditure incurred in implementing the above Government Order to the Government.
