Read:- Office of the Charity Commissioner Letter No. Property Branch/ 2604/2022, Dy. 10.05.2022 Preamble: The Public Trust Registration Office, Thane is presently functioning in a total area of 3300 sq.ft. on the first (1650 sq.ft.) and second floor (1650 sq.ft.) of the building at Lily Apartments, Tembhi Naka, Thane West 400601. The building is very old and there is no separate toilet for officers, employees or women in the office. Also, there is no waiting room or sitting area for lawyers. The entrance of the building is very small and the building is not allowed to breathe freely, which is creating health problems for the officials, staff, visitors, lawyers. There is no room for records in the office. For the last one to one and a half decades, efforts were being made to get government land for the Thane office from the Collector's office. However, the Collector's office has informed the Thane office about finding a private place for the office, saying that there is no government space available. Accordingly, the matter of renting space for the Public Trust Register, Thane office was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision- The following decisions have been taken regarding the registration of public trusts, renting of space for the Thane office.
The Government is pleased to grant approval for payment of additional GST at the rate of Rs. 4,26,300/- (Rupees Four Lakh Twenty Six Thousand Three Hundred only) per month at the rate of Rs. 70/- per sq. ft. for a total area of 6090 sq. ft. of carpet area on the 3rd, 4th, 5th and 6th floor of the new building" Westview," near Gajanan Maharaj Mandir, Shree Ram Maruti Road, Thane (West)- 400602 for the first three years from the date of taking possession of the site and after three years at the rate of 15%+ GST.
