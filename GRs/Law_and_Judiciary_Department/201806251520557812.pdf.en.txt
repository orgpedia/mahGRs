As per Section 17 (2) (k) of the Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust (Shirdi) Act, 2004, it is provided that the Committee shall acquire or purchase such land and buildings as may be necessary for the purpose of carrying out the schemes of the Trust and for carrying out the purposes and objects of the Trust.
Accordingly, the land is purchased through the institute. The Chief Executive Officer, Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trustee System, Shirdi, vide Reference Letter No. (1), had requested for guidance on the criteria to be followed for the purchase of land reserved for various purposes for Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trustee System as well as land other than reserved land. Further, in order to avoid delay in acquisition of land by direct purchase of private land under the Revenue and Forest Department, Land Acquisition Act, 2013, the Government was considering the matter of determining the land purchase policy for acquisition of private land for Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust Management, Shirdi, considering the procedure to be adopted and the request of the CEO as per Government Decision dated 12/5/2015 and the Government Decision dated 25/1/2017 in the updated context.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government is formulating the following policy for the purchase of private land for Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi after taking into consideration the area, distance, contiguity of the land, consent of the landholders, valuation, procedure of purchase, etc.
