Preamble- In pursuance of the aforesaid resolution, the Department has accorded administrative approval to the proposal for permanent transfer of 24 posts and creation of 1 new post in various cadres from the Headquarter, Brihanmumbai and Thane Charitable Offices under the Government Resolution dated 22.10.2018 for the establishment of a new Charitable Office for the newly created Palghar District.
As per Section 3 of the Act, the office of the Registrar of Public Trusts at Palghar has started functioning from 13.9.2019. Accordingly, pursuant to the request made by the Office of the Charity Commissioner, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai vide letter dated 29th August, 2019, the matter of declaration of Assistant Charity Commissioner, Palghar as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
The Government have decided to declare the Assistant Charity Commissioner, Palghar as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the Public Trust Registration Office established for the newly created Palghar district under Rule No. 15 (3)& 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. The order will be limited to financial matters.
