The Proposal:
The Court of Civil Judge Senior Level has been functioning at Ambad, District Jalna since 21.02.2021 vide notification dated 17.02.2021 dated 17.02.2021 under reference No. As per the Government Order dated 22.04.2021, Civil Judge Senior Level, Ambad has been declared as" Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer." Principal District and Sessions Judge, Jalna vide letter dated 04.05.2021 has requested for approval to open a Personal Register Account for the Court of Civil Court Senior Level, Ambad. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of a Personal Accounts Account for the Civil Judge Senior Level, Ambad.
Government's decision:
In accordance with Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Clause 1 in the name of Civil Judge Senior Level, Ambad, District Jalna, approval is being given to open a Civil Private Accounts Account for depositing the amounts lying with the Civil Court Senior Level, Ambad, District Jalna, subject to the following conditions:
1) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
