The Proposal:
The revised figure of 17 regular and 6 (5+ 1) external posts to be filled in the office of General Manager and Government Trustee, Maharashtra State, Mumbai under the Department of Law and Justice has been fixed by the Government Decision dated 18.04.2022. As per the decision of the government, the driver of the office of the General Manager and Government Trustee, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has been declared dead by the cadre. However, since one of the drivers working in the said office is about to retire, a driver is required. Therefore, the High-Level Committee of Secretaries has approved to take the service of a driver for this office through a permanent external agency, accordingly, the Government was considering to amend the Statement-B of the Government Decision dated 18.04.2022.
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the approval given by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries through this Government Decision, it is being approved to take the service of 1 driver for the office of the General Manager and Government Trustee, Maharashtra State, Mumbai through a permanent external agency. Accordingly, in the Government Resolution dated 18.04.2022 of this Department," Return B" has been attached with the revised" Return B" by making necessary amendments.
