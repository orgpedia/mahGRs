Ministry of Finance No. 1/3/2008-E (b) A copy of the office memorandum dated 28.03.2018, Government of India, Ministry of Finance, Department of Expenditure, forwarded to the Judicial Officers of the Subordinate Courts in the State of Maharashtra for information and appropriate action.
According to the above-mentioned Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Finance of the Central Government. The Dearness Allowance increase of 3% (139% to 142%) with effect from 01.01.2018 and other provisions mentioned in the Memorandum shall continue to be applicable to Judicial Officers and retired Judicial Officers of Subordinate Courts in the State of Maharashtra. Accordingly, now the Judicial Officers of the Secondary Court of Maharashtra State as well as retired Judicial Officers of the State of Maharashtra will be given the status of Judicial Officers. Dearness Allowance at the rate of 142% will be admissible with effect from 01.01.2018. The increase in dearness allowance should be paid in cash.
