Preamble In pursuance of the above, the Government have accorded approval for establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Vaibhavwadi in Sindhudurg district vide Government Notification dated 24.01.2014 No. In pursuance of the above mentioned Government Decision dated 13th February, 2012 mentioned in A.No.2, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. The District Court Sindhudurg has requested to declare the Magistrate for Vaibhavwadi Gram Nyayalaya, Gram Nyayalaya Vaibhavwadi (Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Kankavali) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Vaibhavwadi, District Sindhudurg as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for the Gram Nyayalaya Vaibhavwadi, Sindhudurg.
The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya Vaibhavwadi, District Sindhudurg is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Gram Nyayalaya established under the Gram Nyayalaya Act, 2008 at Vaibhavwadi, District Sindhudurg. The order will be limited to financial matters.
