For the purpose of" Strengthening the Justice Sector" recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, a fund of Rs. 1014 crore has been earmarked for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, a sum of Rs.228 crore has been earmarked for the renovation of the functional courts for a period of five years. A High Level Steering Committee has been constituted on 9th March, 2016 for effective implementation of the recommendations of the Commission. At the meeting of the committee. Accordingly, the proposal for the renovation of the courts at Karanja Ghadge under the control of District and Sessions Court Wardha in the Schedule of 2015-16 has been approved by the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India vide letter No. 3. The Bombay High Court has given its verdict.
