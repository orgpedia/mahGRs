The Proposal:
As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, the Government had a policy to review the service of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in the public interest at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
Accordingly, Reference No. The departmental review committees have been formed under the government decisions 1 and 2.
Now the General Administration Department has issued Reference No. The decision of the combined government is made by superseding all previous orders under the government decision mentioned in section 3. The main provisions of this law are as follows:
Group-A and Group-B Gazetted Officers: (a) Officers/ Employees who have come into Government service before the age of 35 years: As per the revised policy of the General Administration Department, the Gazetted Officers of Group-A and Group-B who have come into Government service before the age of 35 years are to be reviewed once on the completion of their 50 years of age or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
(b) Officers/ Employees who have joined the Government service after 35 years of age: Gazetted officers who have joined the Government service after 35 years of age are to be reviewed at the age of 55 years.
(ii) Group B (Non-Gazetted), Group C and Group D employees: In case of Group B (Non-Gazetted), Group C and Group D employees, they are to be re-employed at the age of 55 years or on completion of 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
(iii) While reviewing the eligibility of Government officers/ employees to continue in Government service, after reviewing them on the basis of the prescribed criteria, only competent and efficient officers/ employees are to be continued in Government service in public interest and officers/ employees with inefficient and doubtful integrity are to be retired prematurely.
