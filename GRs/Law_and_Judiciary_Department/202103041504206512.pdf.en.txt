GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- In pursuance of the Government decision dated 14th September, 2020, 126 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court Wardha have been extended till 28th February, 2021. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 126 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
