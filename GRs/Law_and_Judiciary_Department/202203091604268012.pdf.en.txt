Preamble: In pursuance of the above decision dated 05.02.2021, the Government have accorded administrative approval for the establishment of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Vadgaon-Maval in Pune district. In pursuance of the Government Order dated 26.03.2021 in Reference No. 2, the funds required for the said Court have been sanctioned. As per the notification dated 31.01.2022, dated 31.01.2022, the Court of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Vadgaon-Maval has become functional with effect from 06.01.2022. As per the request made by the Civil Court, Vadgaon-Maval vide letter dated 4, 5& 6 dated 01.04.2019, the matter of declaring Civil Judge (Senior Level) Vadgaon-Maval District Pune as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
Government Judgments: Civil Judge (Senior Level) for Civil Court (Senior Level) Vadgaon-Maval, G. Pune, Vadgaon Maval, G. Pune is being declared as" Head of Office" as well as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." They are being authorised to deposit all the court dues in the treasury. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, dated 17th April, 2015, in Part I, Sub-Division No. 3 of Sub-Division 1 and in exercise of the powers conferred under Rules 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
