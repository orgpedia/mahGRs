Government's decision:
As per Rule 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Act, 1968, the Resident Commissioner, Government of Maharashtra, Resident Commissioner Office, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi, for the office of the Public Prosecutor, Supreme Court, New Delhi, is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for all purposes for the current financial year 2022-2023.
(2) This Government have made the following provisions in the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. 156 and is being issued with the approval of the Principal Secretary and Senior Legal Advisor, Department of Law and Justice.
