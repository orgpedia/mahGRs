Preamble: To dispose of the cases pending in the courts of the State as soon as possible. from April 1, 2016. 100 Fast Track Courts have been extended for a period of 5 years up to 31st March, 2021. A total of 500 posts, including that of ad-hoc district judges and their support staff, are required for these courts. It was approved to continue till 29.02.2020. The request for continuation of these temporary posts has been made by the District and Sessions Court, Amravati, Nashik, Nagpur and Akola under the proposal at the reference serial number 2 to 5. Reference No. 6 decision of the Finance Department. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts from 1.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. Accordingly, the matter of continuation of these posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: 500 temporary posts as shown in the following table in 100 fast track courts in the state. from 1st March 2020. This Government decision is being given the go-ahead to continue till 31st August 2020.
