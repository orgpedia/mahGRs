Ministry of Finance, Government of India, Ministry of Finance, Department of Expenditure, dated 11.09.2018 for information and appropriate action in respect of Judicial Officers of Subordinate Courts in the State of Maharashtra.
According to the above-mentioned Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Finance of the Central Government. Dearness Allowance increased by 6% (142% to 148%) with effect from 01.07.2018 and other provisions mentioned in the memorandum shall continue to be applicable to Judicial Officers and retired Judicial Officers of Subordinate Courts in the State of Maharashtra. Accordingly, now the Judicial Officers of the Secondary Court of Maharashtra State as well as retired Judicial Officers of the State of Maharashtra will be given the status of Judicial Officers. Dearness Allowance@ 148% will be admissible with effect from 01.07.2018. The increase in dearness allowance should be paid in cash.
