Preamble- In pursuance of the above reference, the Government have approved the establishment of the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Akot, District Akola at Akot, District Akola and creation of a post for this Court. As per the Government Notification dated 14.12.2018, the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Akot, District Akola has become functional with effect from 16.12.2018. Accordingly, vide letter dated 14.01.2019 addressed to the Manager, Observations (1), Smt. The matter of declaring Civil Judge Senior Level, Akot, District Akola as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government on the request made by the High Court of Bombay.
The Civil Judge Senior Level Akot, District Akola has been declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. The order will be limited to financial matters.
