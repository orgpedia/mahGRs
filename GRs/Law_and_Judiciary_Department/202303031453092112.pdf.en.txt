The Hon'ble High Court, Bombay has passed an order dated 07.02.2023 in Criminal Writ Petition No. 1874/2017, wherein the Chief Secretary has been directed by the Hon'ble High Court to issue instructions to the Administrative Departments, through which the Coordinating Officers of the Administrative Departments have been directed to take necessary steps to ensure the compliance of the orders of the Hon'ble High Court. The office of the Public Prosecutor/ Public Prosecutor in the High Court and its Benches as well as in the District Courts are to be informed of their responsibilities to be effectively contacted.
The Hon'ble High Court, Bombay has also observed that despite court orders and despite being contacted by the Public Prosecutor/ Public Prosecutor's Office in the High Court, the administrative departments in the Ministry are not responding or are not contacting the Public Prosecutor/ Public Prosecutor's Office. As a result, in the absence of a response from the government, cases have to be adjourned from time to time and courts are forced to issue summons or impose punishment in the case.
In paragraph 6 of the impugned order, the Hon'ble High Court has observed and directed as under:" It is for the Principal Secretary of the concerned department who is required to effectively
supervise the process of giving appropriate instructions to the office of Public Prosecution or Government Pleader in the pending matters before the High Court or other Courts. What has been noticed is for want of such Nodal Officer from each Administrative department process of giving instructions to the officers is delayed and at times the Administrative department, even are not communicating with the office of the Public Prosecution or Government Pleader. As a sequel of which the Courts are constrained to saddle costs or summon the Officers in the Court. In the aforesaid background, we hereby direct that the Chief Secretary of the State shall issue appropriate instructions to the Administrative department, thereby intimating the Nodal Officer who shall be solely responsible for effectively communicating to the office of Public Prosecutor or Government Pleader in the High Court and its Benches, so also the District Courts or in alternate evolve a mechanism to effectively deal and address the above issue."
Accordingly, in pursuance of the aforesaid order of the Hon'ble High Court, the Additional Chief Secretaries/ Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries of all the Administrative Departments are hereby directed to ensure that the concerned Coordinating Officers appointed by the respective Administrative Departments are appointed by the respective Administrative Departments. the High Court and the Benches and District
