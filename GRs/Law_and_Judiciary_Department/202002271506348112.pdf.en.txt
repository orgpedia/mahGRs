The Proposal:
As per the proposal of the Hon'ble High Court, the Government has decided to establish a Civil Judge (Senior Level) at Vita in Sangli District vide Government Resolution No. Composite No. 2019/968/ Q.No.3/ Work-11, D. It was approved on July 23, 2019. Further, the reference to this Court is at Annexure 3. The post has been created in accordance with the Government Decision dated 25/2/2020. In this case, a symbolic supplementary demand was submitted to the Legislature through the Finance Department in the December-2019 session. It's been approved. Thus, the proposal to provide funds for this court was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The following symbolic supplementary demands for the Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court set up at Vita in Sangli district have been approved by the Legislature.
