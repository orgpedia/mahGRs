The Proposal:
The court buildings in Andheri, Bandra, Borivali, Esplanade, Vikhroli and Kurla, which are under the jurisdiction of the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai, have been put on high alert. The administrative approval has been granted under the Government Decisions dated 1/9/2018, 10/9/2018 and 26/9/2018.
A total of Rs. The Supplementary Demand for Rs. 48,76,000/- was submitted to the Legislative Council by the Finance Department in the Monsoon Session of June, 2019. This has been approved by the cabinet. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to make available funds for meeting the expenditure for fire fighting measures in the court buildings at Andheri, Bandra, Borivali, Esplanade, Vikhroli and Kurla under the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai.
Government's decision:
The Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai, has sanctioned Rs. Supplementary Demand for 48,76,000/- (Rupees Forty Eight Lakh Seventy Six Thousand only) has been approved by the Legislature.
