The Proposal:
A proposal was submitted to the government to allow the District Sessions Court, Pune to open a new targeted bank account in State Bank of India on a trial basis to accept the fine and other amounts to be deposited in the court in accordance with the" Ease of Doing Business." In pursuance of the said proposal, the office of the District Sessions Court, Pune was permitted to open a new Targeted Bank Account in State Bank of India for sanction of fine and other amounts to be deposited in the Court vide Government Order dated 18.04.2018. The Central Project Coordinator, e-Courts Project, High Court, Appellate Division, Mumbai, vide Reference No. As per the letter, a request was made to open a separate targeted bank account for all court establishments in Pune district on the lines of the District Sessions Court, Pune, to accept the fine and other amounts to be deposited in the court in accordance with the" Ease of Doing Business." Accordingly, the government was considering allowing the opening of separate targeted bank accounts for all establishments (including court of law) in Pune district.
Government's decision:
The opening of a separate Targeted Bank Account in State Bank of India for all Court establishments in Pune District for accepting the amount of fine and other amounts to be deposited in the Court using e-payment is being approved subject to the following terms and conditions as mentioned in the Government Order dated 18.04.2018: 1.
