Reference No. 2. General Administration Department in 3, the government decision. As on 10/06/2019, directions have been issued regarding consolidated action for re-evaluation of Government officers/ employees in the age group of 50/55 years. The composition of the Review Committees is set out in Annexure A to this Government Resolution. In Schedule 3, instructions have been issued to constitute a committee of 4 members, including the Chairman and 3 members, to advise the Head of the Department on the review of the employees of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D. Further, Annexure-D provides for constitution of Grievance Committees to consider the grievances against the decision of premature retirement after review of service and also to constitute a Grievance Committee of appropriate authorities for final decision on the recommendation of the Committee. The instructions are as follows:
Zonal/ Regional Review Committee (1) Chairman:- Senior officer of Group-A in addition to the Head of the Department in the office of the Head of the Department.
Member:- Senior officer of the other Group-A in the department.
Member:- Senior officer of Group-A in other Divisional Offices of the District under the administrative control of the same Ministerial Department (if such officer is not available, the officer nominated by the Secretary of the Administrative Department in any other Division of the District).
Member Secretary:- Senior officer of Group-B (Gazetted/ Non-Gazetted) dealing with administrative matters in the office of the Head of the Department.
Note:- The recommendations regarding the officers/ employees of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D who are eligible/ ineligible as per the recommendation of the Review Committee, should be submitted to the concerned Head of Department for final decision.
