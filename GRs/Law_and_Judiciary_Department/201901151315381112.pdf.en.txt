As per the notification dated 10.08.2018, the Court of Senior Civil Judge at Malkapur, District Buldhana has become functional with effect from 16.08.2018. As per the Government Order dated 26.10.2018, Civil Judge Senior Level Malkapur has been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. On 27.09.2018, Principal District and Sessions Judge, Buldhana has requested to approve opening of Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Malkapur. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of a Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Malkapur.
As per Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Clause 1, the opening of a Civil and Criminal Private Partnership Account in the name of Civil Judge Senior Level, Malkapur, District Buldhana for depositing the amounts is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3) The amount ordered by the Court to be deposited as Civil Court Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA.
4) No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions will appear in this PLA.
