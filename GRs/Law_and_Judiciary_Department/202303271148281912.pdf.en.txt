4) If the cost of the computer to be purchased by the officer/ employee is less than the standard advance, the amount of the computer should be approved to the applicant in advance and the same should be informed to the government immediately. Also, if the advance is not being approved or rejected for any reason, the government should be informed immediately. Otherwise, please note that the entire responsibility in this regard will be on the concerned regulatory authorities.
To ensure that the actual expenditure of each month determined by each office as per the target is exactly in line with the expenditure shown in the Accountant General's account, the monthly expenditure meeting should be made online on the website http:// agmaha.cag.gov.in and the monthly statement of verified expenditure should be sent to the office of this department by the 10th of every month along with the reconciliation completion slip. If the amount certified is not utilized within the prescribed period, the certified amount should be returned to the government within 15 days from the issuance of the government decision. The government should be informed immediately about how the money was spent.
(6) No advance shall be payable to any officer/ employee other than the said officer/ employee.
7) All the Controlling Officers shall plan the monthly expenditure sub-account wise and target wise and allocate the provision through Computerized Budget Distribution System (BDS) to the Drawing and Disbursing Officers. Expenditure from this grant should be spent in the same months every month through the Computational Budget Distribution System (BDS) as per the Monthly Fund Statement and within the limits of the sanctioned provisions.
8) If the concerned officer has shifted from his office, the details should be informed to the government before taking out the Authorization Slip on the Budget Distribution System (BDS). Accordingly, the concerned office should take further action regarding the preparation of payment only after the necessary changes are made by the government in the amount authorized on the system.
9) The officers/ employees to whom the personal computer purchase agreement has been sanctioned should be duly recorded in their service books. The photocopy of the page in the registered service book should be submitted to the government.
(10) This is Expenditure No. J-5, 7610- Loans to Government Employees etc. (Post-Plan/ Datta) 204 (00) (01) Advances for purchase of Personal Computers (7610 184 2) 55 Loans and advances to be spent under this head and to be met from the grants sanctioned for the financial year 2022-23.
