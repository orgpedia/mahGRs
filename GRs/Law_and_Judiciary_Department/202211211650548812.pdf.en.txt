In pursuance of the Government decision dated 11th August, 2009, referred to in clause (1) of the said proviso, administrative approval has been accorded for the establishment of the Court of Principal District and Sessions Judge and Chief Judicial Magistrate at Gondia and creation of a post for the same. As per the decision, 22 new posts of Principal District and Sessions Judge, Gondia have been created and 12 posts have been transferred from the erstwhile District Court, 1 Gondia. However, the District and Sessions Court Gondia has requested for creation of more posts citing that these posts are insufficient. As per letter dated 22nd February, 2016, referred to in Reference No. (2) above. The Bombay High Court has recommended the appointment. Accordingly, the matter of creating a post for the District Court Gondia was under the consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Government have approved creation of 15 more posts for District and Sessions Court, Gondia as mentioned below
