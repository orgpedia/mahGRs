Ministry of Finance, Government of India, Ministry of Finance, Department of Expenditure, dated 25.10.2019, forward a copy of the office memorandum dated 25.10.2019 to the Judicial Officers of the Subordinate Courts in the State of Maharashtra for information and appropriate action.
According to the above-mentioned Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Finance of the Central Government. The Dearness Allowance hike of 10% (154% to 164%) with effect from 01.07.2019 and other provisions mentioned in the memorandum will continue to be applicable to judicial officers of subordinate courts as well as retired judicial officers in the State of Maharashtra.
Accordingly, now the Judicial Officers of the Secondary Court of Maharashtra State as well as retired Judicial Officers of the State of Maharashtra will be given the status of Judicial Officers. Dearness Allowance at the rate of 164% will be admissible with effect from 01.07.2019. The increase in dearness allowance should be paid in cash.
