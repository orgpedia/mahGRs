The Proposal:
The Court of Civil Judge Sr. Level Malkapur at Malkapur, District Buldhana has been functioning on regular basis since 16.08.2018 as per the notification dated 10.08.2018 under reference No. As per the Government Order dated 26.10.2018, Civil Judge Sr. Level Malkapur has been declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." Principal District and Sessions Judge, Buldhana has requested for administrative approval for opening of Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Malkapur. The High Court of Bombay has requested the Government vide letter dated 20th May, 2022 mentioned in the aforesaid Reference No.
Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of a Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Malkapur, District Buldhana.
Government's decision:
In accordance with Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Clause 1 in the name of Civil Judge Senior Level, Malkapur, District Buldhana, approval is being given to open a Civil Private Accounts Account for depositing the amounts lying with the Civil Court Senior Level, Malkapur, District Buldhana, subject to the following conditions:

Government Resolution No: PLA 2022/ P.No.98/ Q.12
1) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3) Amount relating to decretal deposit by the respondents and other judiciary matters as per court order, may be deposited in the PD. Expenditure as per the court order may be made out of the personal deposit account.
4) No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions will appear in this PLA.
5) Either on quarterly or monthly basis, balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the Court.
6) All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this PLA.
7) Office of the Accountant General (A&E)II, Nagpur will issue an authority to open PLA on receipt of Govt. orders.
The following shall be the classification which may be recorded on every challan while depositing the amount in Treasury / Sub-Treasury.
For Civil Court Deposit 8443 Civil Deposit 104 - Civil Court Deposits 01 - Court's Name
