In pursuance of the Government Order dated 10th April, 2018, dated 01.04.2018, the Government have approved the creation of the post of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class in the Court of Koraput. In the table mentioned under the heading" Creation of posts for the Court of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class at Korpana, District Chandrapur" in paragraph 2 of this Government Order, the pay scale of the post of peon at A. No. 7 should be read as" 4440-7440+ Grade Pay 1300" instead of" 4400 7400+ Grade Pay 1300."
