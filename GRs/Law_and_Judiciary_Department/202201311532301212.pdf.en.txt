Under the Finance Department, Government Decision dated 05.05.1970, all government departments, semi-government district councils and municipalities, corporations, government factories, etc. all the institutions in which the government has direct or indirect control/ interest are involved, have to insure the buildings owned by them to the Government Insurance Fund. Accordingly, the District and Sessions Court, Akola, vide its letter dated 21.11.2019, has requested for grant of administrative approval for payment of provisional annual insurance premium amounting to Rs.96,667/- (Rupees Sixteen Thousand Six Hundred Sixty Six only) including terrorism insurance risk for the District and Sessions Court, Akola and the Court Buildings under its control and Judge's Residences for insuring the same at a total insurance risk amount of Rs. 13,64,37, 993/- (Rupees Thirteen Crores Sixty Four Lakhs Thirty Seven Thousand Nine Hundred and Ninety Three only). The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, the government was considering to grant permission to insure the District and Sessions Court Akola and the court buildings and judges' residences under its control.
