Government's decision:
171st Akhand Harinaam Saptah of Yogiraj Shri Gangagiri Ji Maharaj was celebrated at Shri Kshetra Shirdi on behalf of the villagers of Shirdi. It will be held from 16.08.2018 to 23.08.2018. This week is celebrated in a very grand manner and due to its grandeur and devotional nature, it is the most grand event of the Samadhi Centenary Year. For this Akhand Harinaam Week, Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi has sanctioned Rs. 75 lakh is being approved under this Government decision subject to the following conditions:
1) The Week Committee shall consist of all the office bearers, ex-officio members/ members of the Management Committee of Shirdi Sansthan.
2) On the dais, in the facade, flex photos of Shri Saibaba in full size approximately 545 feet in size should be put and mention the Shri Sai Centenary Year under it.
(3) The name boards of all the members of the Management Committee shall be placed beside the place where the name boards/ boards of the members of the Week Committee shall be placed.
4) Welcoming the Chief Guest and other dignitaries, the President, Vice-President, CEO and other members of the Management Committee of Shirdi Sansthan will be given a reception.
5) After getting the approval from the government, before the distribution of the subsidy, the Shirdi Sansthan should fulfill the above-mentioned things and only then the subsidy should be distributed.
