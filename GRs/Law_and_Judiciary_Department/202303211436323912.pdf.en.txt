As per Section 18 (2) and Section 18 (2) (i) of the Shree Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) Act, 1980, after making adequate provisions for the purposes referred to in Section 18 (1) and making similar provisions for the purposes of the Trust for its immediate future objectives, needs, requirements and improvements, it is provided that part of such excess balance may be used for developing the assets of the Trust and acquiring movable or immovable properties for the purposes of the Trust. Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai had requested the Government to approve the policy for purchase of land/ land of the Trust under their referral letter.
Considering the request made by Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) and the provisions of Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) Act, 1980, it was under consideration of the Government to decide the" Land/ Immovable Property Purchase Policy" of the Trust.

While taking this decision, land/ immovable property should be purchased after taking into consideration the interests of the Devasthanam. When buying private land/ immovable property, if there is a demand for a price higher than the market rate (market value), it should be negotiated and taken at the market rate or even lower.
