Preamble: Department of Law and Justice, Govt. The design of 13 Public Prosecutor Offices under the jurisdiction of the Office of the Prosecutor-8 has been approved as of April 18, 2023. Accordingly, a total of 12 posts of various cadres have been approved for the office of the Advocate General, High Court, Mumbai and 1 post of driver has been approved.
2. m. Advocate General, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai vide Reference No. 2 As per the decision of the Government here, the status of" Minister of State" has been conferred. Frequent visits are required for the functioning of the High Court. Since they have to travel frequently, the stress falls on the only driver available in the office. Therefore, the Superintendent, Advocate General's Office, Mumbai has requested to approve the services of an additional driver for the office of the Advocate General through an external agency. 3 was written here. Accordingly, a proposal for hiring a driver for the said office through external agencies was submitted to the Finance Department, Government Resolution, D. The Sub-Committee of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department and Principal Secretary (Expenditure), Finance Department, constituted under 9.6.2017. It was submitted for approval at the meeting on June 7, 2023. The proposal has been approved by the cabinet.
Government Decision: As per the approval given in the meeting held on June 7, 2023, the Government is approving to take the services of one driver post for the office of the Advocate General, High Court, Mumbai through an external agency for a period of 11 months.
(2) The guidelines issued by the Finance Department from time to time through Government Circulars regarding the provision of services by external agencies should be strictly followed. Also, the head of the office should ensure that the prevailing law is not violated while providing services from external agencies.
