C./ 264/2019-20 D. 12.02.2020 Preamble: As per the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission, as well as the proposal submitted by the Central Project Coordinator, e-Courts Project, High Court, Appellate Division, Mumbai, technical manpower support under the 14th Central Finance Commission has been provided to the Ministry of Finance. A total of 199 posts of Senior System Officer (40 posts) and System Officer (159 posts) to be appointed for High Courts and other Courts are proposed to be created and filled up by external agencies by 31st March, 2020 (till the period of 14th Finance Commission). The administrative approval has been given under the government decision of 2.
dated 30.08.2016. The meeting of the Dedicated Cell of the High Court decided to approve the posts to be filled under the technical manpower support component under the 14th Central Finance Commission for a period of five years from their appointment.
The posts under Technical Human Resource Support will expire after March 31, 2020 and will not have a full tenure of 5 years as of now. For these posts to get a full term of 5 years, they need to be approved for a further period of 5 years from the date of appointment.
