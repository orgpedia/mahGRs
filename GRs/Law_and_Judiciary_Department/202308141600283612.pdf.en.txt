The Proposal:
Reference No. 1 to the establishment of Civil Court, Senior Level Court at Belapur, Navi Mumbai in Thane District and creation of necessary posts therefor. 1 The decision has been approved by the government. Also Reference No. The Court by notification No. 2. Started on 7/4/2023. Civil Judge, Senior Level as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the said Court vide Reference No. 3 has been announced by the government here. Civil Court, Senior Level, Belapur vide Reference No. Letter No. 4 has requested for approval to open a personal provident account for the said Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of a personal provident fund account for the said Court
Government's decision:
As per Rule 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the opening of a personal provident fund account for deposits and repayment of amounts in the Civil Court, Belapur G.Thane is allowed subject to the following conditions:
i. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
ii. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit Account.
iii. Receipt into the PD account will be the Deposit, Maintenance Amount, rent, Decretal amount, Expenditure may be done as per the order of the Court.
