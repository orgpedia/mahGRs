For the purpose of" Strengthening the Justice Sector" recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, a fund of Rs. 1014 crore has been earmarked for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, a sum of Rs.228 crore has been earmarked for the renovation of the functional courts for a period of five years. A High Level Steering Committee has been constituted on 9th March, 2016 for effective implementation of the recommendations of the Commission. In the meeting held on 28.03.2016, Shri. The action plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle. The High Level Committee meeting held on 28.02.2019 has approved a sum of Rs.50 lakh.

Under the Government Order dated 31st July, 2019, the power to grant administrative approval for the repair and renovation works of the outer court buildings has been delegated to the Department of Law and Justice subject to conditions as an administrative department. Administrative Sanction has been accorded for the renovation of Courts at District and Sessions Court Wardha for an amount of Rs. 58,68,981/- (Rupees Fifty Eight Lakh Sixty Six Thousand Nine Hundred Eighty One only) vide Government Order dated 12.02.2018. Thereafter, vide letter dated 19.01.2019, referred to in clause (4), the Government has received the proposal on the Tariff Schedule for the year 2018-19 for installation of an elevator in the building of the District and Sessions Court, Wardha. The budget has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Divisional Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer and Principal District and Sessions Judge Wardha. The proposal has been approved by the High-Level Steering Committee at its meeting held on 28.02.2019. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval for the renovation works under the 14th Central Finance Commission of the court buildings of the District and Sessions Court Wardha was under consideration of the Government. The above approval is being granted subject to the following conditions: i. The Maharashtra Public Works Department, the rules for granting administrative approval in the rule book and the related rules should be complied with.
(ii) The repair work shall be carried out by the Public Works Department by appointing a contractor through the Administrative Departments, if the post of Executive Engineer or above is held by the concerned Administrative Departments, depending on the tender method prescribed.
(iii) Budgetary provisions should be available.
4. The cost of renovation of court buildings at District and Sessions Court Wardha shall be met from the approved provision under the head Demand No. J-1, 2014 JUDGMENT, (00) JUDGMENT, 102-High Courts, (11) RENEWAL OF FUNCTIONAL COURTS, (11) RENEWAL OF FUNCTIONAL COURTS (2014 0548).
5. Before inviting tender for the said work. It shall be the responsibility of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Wardha to ensure that the above works fall within the scope of the action plan fixed by the High Court of Bombay and the works fixed by the Dedicated Cell. The e-tendering process should be carried out by the Public Works Department following the prevailing procedure and other ancillary proceedings should be completed in accordance with this administrative approval.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the Public Works Department vide No. No. 74/2019/ Ima-2, dated 16.02.2019 and with the concurrence of the Department and subject to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Government Resolution, Law and Justice Department, dated 31.07.2019.
