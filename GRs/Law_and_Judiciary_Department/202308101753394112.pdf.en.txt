The revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions:
1) Agreeing to the revised administrative approval means that the technical aspects need to be examined by the Public Works Department.
(2) All other conditions mentioned in the original administrative approval shall also apply to the revised administrative approval.
3) Before starting the construction, the sample map, layout map and detailed map should be started only after getting approval from the architect.
4) While working on the provision made in a rough form, the work should be undertaken only after making a detailed budget.
5) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in ENV 2013/ Q. No. 177/ T.K.1, dated 10th January, 2014.
Before starting the work, the approval of all the concerned local bodies/ authorities should be taken.
7) Department of Public Works, Government Decision No. Action to be taken under BDG 2017/ PR No. 60/ Building-2, dated 7th July, 2017.
8) The tender notice for the work should be published after receiving the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the consumer department.
9) Don't change your budget.
10) The scope of work should not be changed or new works should not be included on the basis of oral instructions given by the engineers of the Public Works Department.
11) If new works are to be included or the scope is to be changed, then action should be taken to get approval for these works in the prescribed time and manner by obtaining a written order in this regard. Otherwise, the responsibility will be fixed on the concerned officials.
11) The Public Works Department should take care of the completion of the present scheme in the revised administrative approval price. Also, the department should not spend beyond the limit.
