Preamble: In terms of the order dated 08.07.2019 of the Department of Law and Justice, the post of Superintendent (Non-Gazetted) in the District and Sessions Court attached to the office was declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the District Government Advocate Office (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) for the period upto 31.12.2019 as a special case. Now, for the interim period till the gazetted officer is declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the District Government Advocate Office, the Government was considering to re-assign the responsibility of the post of Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the District Government Advocate Office (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) to the Superintendent (Non-Gazetted) of the District and Sessions Court attached to that office.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Accordingly, the post of" Superintendent" (Non-Gazetted) in the District and Sessions Court attached to the office of the District Government Advocate (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the period up to 30th June, 2020 as a special case. to submit all the payments of this office to the treasury.
