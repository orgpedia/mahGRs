Reference-M. Letter No.B (W) A-1908 of 207 dated 19.09.2017 of the High Court of Bombay
Gram Nyayalaya has been in existence at Korpana, District Chandrapur since 2nd October, 2009. Civil Judge Junior Level Rajura visits the Gram Nyayalayas at Korpana, District Chandrapur from time to time. Observing that the required number of pending cases and infrastructure are meeting the standards, the Gram Nyayalaya Gadchandur, Th. It has been decided to set up a regular court of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class in Korpana, District Chandrapur instead of Gram Nyayalaya. The Establishment of Courts Committee was set up at the level of the Bombay High Court. In this regard, the manager has been asked to take necessary action. The High Court of Bombay vide its letter dated 24.11.2017.
Accordingly, the matter of setting up a Civil Court Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class in Korpana, District Chandrapur in place of the Gram Nyayalaya at Korpana and sanctioning the creation of the necessary post for the same was under the consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
The government has decided to set up a court of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class in Chandrapur district instead of the existing Gram Nyayalaya. Orders for the creation of posts will be issued separately.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be finally spent for the purposes under the head" Demand No. 1, 2014- Adjudication (00) (105) Civil and Sessions Courts (02) District and Sessions Judge (02) (01) District and Sessions Judge (20140172) Datta Mat" by making provision of funds in the relevant financial year.
