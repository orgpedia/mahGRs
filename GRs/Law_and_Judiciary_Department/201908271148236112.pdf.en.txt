Preamble: With a view to provide facilities to the courts in the State, the" Infrastructure Policy for Courts" has been issued vide Government Resolution No. 1. But Ma. The High Court of Judicature at Bombay, while passing judgment on Public Interest Litigation No. 156/2011, has suggested amendments in paragraph No. 10.2 of the said policy. In pursuance of the said amendments made in Para No. 10.2 of the Infrastructure Policy for Courts, the matter of conferring financial powers to the Principal District Judge, Chief Justice Small Causes Court, Mumbai and the Principal Judge, City Civil and Sessions Judge, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government decision: As per the decision taken by the Cabinet, the Government hereby approve the conferment of financial powers to the Principal District Judge, Chief Justice Small Causes Court, Mumbai, Principal Judge, City Civil and Sessions Judge, Bombay under the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-II, as indicated in Annexure A annexed to this Government Resolution and subject to specified terms and conditions and financial limits therein.
