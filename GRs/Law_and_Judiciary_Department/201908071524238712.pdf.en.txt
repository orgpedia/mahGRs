The Proposal:
The Family Court, Mumbai was allowed 3 vehicles as per the vehicle review of family courts in the state. There were 3 vehicles available at the Family Court, Mumbai, however, the vehicle used for the Chief Justice was No. 02-JP-8705 has been deprecated. Therefore, the proposal to purchase a new government vehicle in lieu of the said vehicle. 1 was received by the Government from the Family Court, Mumbai, vide letter No. Accordingly, the proposal to purchase a new government vehicle in lieu of the old vehicle for the Family Court, Mumbai was under the consideration of the government for administrative approval.
Government's decision:
Government approval is being accorded for the purchase of a new Government vehicle for the use of the office of the Chief Justice of the Family Court, Mumbai subject to the following conditions:
(a) Department of Industry, Energy and Labour. As per the Government decision dated 24.08.2017, the modalities of the Government-e-Marketplace (GeM) portal developed by the Central Government have been approved to be adopted by the State Governments for procurement of goods and services. Accordingly, it is mandatory for the state government to consider the modalities of GeM Portal for procurement of goods and services. As per the Government decision dated 01.12.2016, the procedure in the Revised Purchase Manual needs to be considered. Action should be taken as per the government's directions.
(b) Department of Finance Reference No. 3 As per the Government Decision dated 21/01/2017, other officials who have been permitted vehicles with the approval of the State Level Vehicle Review Committee, Rs. The vehicle should be purchased within the limit of Rs. 6.00 lakh. The subsidy sanctioned for the purchase of a vehicle includes all taxes in the price of the vehicle.
