Shri Vitthal Rukmini Mandir Samiti, Pandharpur has extended financial assistance to the families of the martyred jawans in the terrorist attack in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir.

The Faith, 114. 18.02.2019
The proposal: On 14.02.2019, a dastardly terrorist attack was carried out on Indian soldiers in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir valley. Shri Vitthal Rukmini Mandir Samiti, Pandharpur has donated Rs. 10 lakh each to the families of the deceased and Rs. Request for Rs. 20 lakh This has been done by the Executive Officer, Shri Vitthal Rukmini Mandir Samiti, Pandharpur vide letter dated 18.02.2019.
The Government was of the view that the proposal should be approved as a" special case" as it was necessary to provide assistance from a humanitarian angle to the families of the soldiers who were martyred in the dastardly terrorist attack on Indian soldiers in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir Valley on 14.02.2019.
Government's decision:
An ex-gratia of Rs. 1 lakh each to the next of kin of the martyred jawans of Maharashtra who were martyred in the terrorist attack in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir Valley on 14.02.2019. 10 lakh to each of the families. 20 lakh from Shri Vitthal Rukmini Mandir Samiti, Pandharpur, subject to approval in the ensuing meeting of Shri Vitthal Rukmini Mandir Samiti, Pandharpur.
