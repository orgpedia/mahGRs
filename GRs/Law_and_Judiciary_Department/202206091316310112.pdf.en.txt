Preamble In pursuance of the Government notification dated 29th February, 2016, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Trimbakeshwar in Nashik district. As per the notification dated 26.12.2019 in Reference No.2, the Joint Civil Judge and Judicial Magistrate First Class, Court No.1, Nashik has been approved to be appointed as the Presiding Officer of Gram Nyayalaya, Trimbakeshwar. In pursuance of the above mentioned Government Decision dated 13th February, 2012 mentioned in Act No. 3, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. The District Court Nashik has requested to declare the Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya Trimbakeshwar (Joint Civil Judge Junior Level and First Class Judicial Magistrate Nashik, Court No. 1) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for Trimbakeshwar Gram Nyayalaya. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court.
Accordingly, the matter of declaring Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya, Trimbakeshwar, District Nashik as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya, Trimbakeshwar, Nashik was under consideration of the Government.
# Page 2
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: DDO 2022/ Q. No.97 (4)/ Q.12
The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya, Trimbakeshwar, District Nashik is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Gram Nyayalaya established under the Gram Nyayalaya Act, 2008 at Trimbakeshwar, District Nashik. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 read with Finance Division No. 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated 17.04.2015.
