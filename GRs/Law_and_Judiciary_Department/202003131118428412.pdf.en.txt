the prelude
The Government Decisions dated 29.10.2001 and 23.09.2011 of the General Administration Department prescribe the modalities for complete computerization of Government activities. Accordingly, it has been provided that the concerned departments shall constitute a Project Implementation Committee to implement the computerization projects under the chairmanship of the concerned Principal Secretary/ Secretary or an officer of the rank of Secretary as may be directed by the Secretary. Accordingly, the Project Implementation Committee for computerization of Charity Commissioners Association was formed under the Government decision dated 3rd March, 2012 of the Department. However, the government was contemplating to revise the order.
Government Decision: A Project Implementation Committee (PIC) is being constituted under the chairmanship of Principal Secretary to the Department to implement the computerization project of the Regional Office of the Department with a view to computerization.
