In paragraph 3 of the Government Resolution referred to above, The following is as follows:" The materials required for the implementation of LAN based Bio-metric Attendance System should be made through GeM portal as per the Government decision dated 24.08.2017 and 08.12.2017 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour in the prescribed manner. In place of-
The materials required for the implementation of LAN based Bio-metric Attendance System should be done in the prescribed manner by the Government as per the Government Decisions dated 24.08.2017 and 08.12.2017 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour. However, in case the goods are not available on GeM portal or the supplier is not willing to supply the same, such goods shall be purchased by the buyer by following the procedure and provisions as prescribed in the Government Order dated 01.12.2016.
