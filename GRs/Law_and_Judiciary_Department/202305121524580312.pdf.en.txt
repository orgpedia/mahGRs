The administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions: 1) Before starting the construction, the design, layout and detailed map should be started with the approval of the architect.
2) As the said budget is a sample budget, while technically approving the detailed budgets of these works, the The provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Parbhani.
3) The tender notice for the work should be published after receiving the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the consumer department.
(4) In the process of tendering the work, one tender should be issued for all the works mentioned together without separate tenders for each work.
5) Approval of all concerned local bodies/ authorities should be obtained before starting the work.
6) When working directly with the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No.177/ T. Action shall be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in the Notice dated 10th January, 2014.
7) Department of Public Works, Government Decision No. Action to be taken under BDG 2017/ PR No. 60/ Building-2, dated 7th July, 2017.
8) Special care should be taken to ensure that no revised administrative approval is required for this work.
9) Don't change your budget.
10) The scope of work should not be changed or new works should not be included on the basis of oral instructions given by the engineer in the Public Works Department.
11) If new works are to be included or the scope is to be changed, then action should be taken to get approval for these works in the prescribed time and manner by obtaining a written order in this regard. Otherwise, the responsibility will be fixed on the concerned officials.
