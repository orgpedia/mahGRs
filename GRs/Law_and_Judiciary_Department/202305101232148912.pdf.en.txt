Preamble: Under Section 3 of the Family Courts Act, 1984. the High Court
It provides for establishment of Family Courts for urban/ peri-urban areas with a population of 10 lakh or more. Accordingly, reference No. 1 here. As per the Government decision dated 06.01.2009, it was approved to establish 1 Family Court at Amaravati. The court said. It has been operational since 29.11.2009. Considering the number of pending cases of family disputes in this court, The High Court of Bombay vide Reference No. As per the letter, a proposal was submitted to the government to set up an additional family court in Amravati.
The proposal was approved by a high-level committee. The Cabinet has approved it.
Accordingly, the matter of setting up 1 additional family court in Amravati and creating a post for it was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Approval is being given for setting up of 1 additional Family Court at Amravati.
