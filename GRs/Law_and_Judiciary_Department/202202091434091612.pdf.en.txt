Preamble: Two Combined Courts, namely, the Court of the District and Additional Sessions Judge and the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level, at Mangrul Peer, District Washim have been functioning since 27.10.2013. However, posts of judges and staff were not created for these twin courts. At present, judges who have been posted at Washim have to go to Mangrul Pir for 15 days with their own staff. Considering the number of pending claims. The Establishment of Courts Committee, constituted at the level of High Court of Bombay, has recommended to the Government to set up regular courts at the level of District and Additional Sessions Court, Mangrul Peer and Civil Court, Senior Level, Mangrul Peer instead of the existing District and Additional Sessions Court and Civil Court, Senior Level, at Mangrul Peer, District Washim. Government's decision
The Government have decided to establish regular District and Additional Sessions Court and Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Mangrul Peer, District Washim in place of existing Combined Court of District and Additional Sessions Judge and Civil Judge Senior Level.
