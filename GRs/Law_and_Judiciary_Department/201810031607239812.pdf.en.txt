The Proposal:
In pursuance of the above decision dated 23rd May, 2018, the Government have approved the establishment of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Chalisgaon in Jalgaon district. In pursuance of the Government Order dated 09.08.2018 in Reference No.2, the funds required for the said Court have been sanctioned. As per the notification dated 21.08.2018, the Court of Civil Judge (Senior Level), Chalisgaon has become functional with effect from 26.08.2018. Therefore, in pursuance of the request made by the District and Sessions Court, Jalgaon under letter No. 4, the matter of declaring Civil Judge (Senior Level) Chalisgaon, District Jalgaon as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Civil Judge (Senior Level) Chalisgaon is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the Civil Court (Senior Level) Chalisgaon at Chalisgaon, District Jalgaon. The order will be limited to financial matters.
