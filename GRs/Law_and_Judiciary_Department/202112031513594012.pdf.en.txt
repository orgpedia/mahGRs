This administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
(a) Construction shall commence with the approval of the architect of the design, layout and detailed design before commencing construction.
(b) Since the said budget is a rough model budget, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nanded, while technically approving the detailed budgets of these works.
(c) Tender notice for the said work should be published after obtaining the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the Consumer Department.
d) When tendering the work, one single tender should be issued for all the mentioned works together without separately tendering each work.
Before starting the work, the approval of all the concerned local bodies/ authorities should be taken.
(fs) While doing the actual work, the Department of Environment should take action according to the guidelines mentioned in the Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q.No. 17/ T.C. 1, dated January 10, 2014.
