Preamble- In pursuance of the above decision, the Government have approved the establishment of a Court of Civil Judge Senior Level at Savner, District Nagpur and creation of a post for this Court. Accordingly, vide letter dated 16th November, 2022, the Manager, Observations (1), Ma. The matter of declaring Civil Judge Senior Level Savner, District Nagpur as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under the consideration of the Government on the request made by the High Court of Bombay.

The Government have decided to declare the Civil Judge Senior Level, District Nagpur as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level, established at Savner, District Nagpur. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 read with Finance Division No. 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated 17.04.2015. .
