Preamble- The number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be at least 500, along with the infrastructure e.g. The availability of court buildings, etc. All the necessary criteria as per the policy of the High Court appear to be fulfilled. The High Court of Bombay has recommended the establishment of a Court of District and Additional Sessions Judge at Paranda, District Osmanabad. Accordingly, the Government was considering the establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court at Paranda, District Osmanabad. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The government has decided to set up a district and additional sessions court at Paranda in Osmanabad district. Also, the Government is approving the creation of posts and hiring of manpower by external agencies for this Court as indicated below:
