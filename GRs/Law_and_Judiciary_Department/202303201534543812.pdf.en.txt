The proposal: As per the notification of High Court vide No. 3& 4, the designation of District& Sessions Judge has been changed to" Principal District& Sessions Judge" and that of Additional District Judge, Satara to" District Judge 1& Additional Sessions Judge." According to As per the request made by the High Court, Bombay and the District and Sessions Court, Satara under section 1 and 2, the matter of declaring the Principal District and Sessions Judge and District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Satara as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The designation of the Drawing and Disbursing Officers of the District and Sessions Court Satara is being changed and declared as the new Drawing and Disbursing Officer as follows. The order will be limited to financial matters.
