On the same lines, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to implement the provisions prescribed in the Central Government Notifications dated 4.8.2016 and 12.5.2017 regarding revision of pension and family pension of retired Judges of Bombay High Court who retired before 1.1.2016 as per 7th Commission. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Resolution- The provisions prescribed in the Central Government's Notification dated 4.8.2016 and dated 12.5.2017 regarding revision of pension and family pension of retired Judges of the Bombay High Court who retired before 1.1.2016 as per the 7th Commission are being implemented under this order.
The following action should be taken while revising the pension/ family pension of a retired Judge of the Bombay High Court who retired before 1.1.2016.
1) Pensioners/ family pensioners who retired before 1.1.2016, the notional pay fixation in the pay matrix as per the 7th Pay Commission is to be done by the head of the concerned office, taking into account the pay in the pay scale they were drawing on the date of retirement/ demise. With effect from 1.1.2016, 50% of the wages will be revised pension and 30% will be revised family pension. The amount of revised pension/ family pension falling under this head is to be taken in the next integer of Re 1.
The pension/ family pension of retired Judges of the High Court of Judicature at Bombay, who retired before 1.1.2016, shall be deemed to be the revised pension/ family pension with effect from 1.1.2016 in excess of the amount of pension/ family pension as revised and computed in accordance with the Central Government Memorandum dated 4.8.2016 and the amount calculated in accordance with the Central Government Memorandum dated 12.5.2017.
(3) No arrears shall be admissible for the period prior to 1.1.2016 as per the determination of the monthly salary made before 1.1.2016. However, for the period from 1.1.2016 onwards, the amount due for revision of pension/ family pension is to be paid in lump sum to the pensioner/ family pensioner.
4) In this regard, the stages of work and responsibilities are prescribed as follows.
