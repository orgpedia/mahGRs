The Proposal: -
The Department of Law and Justice, Government Decision, 27th November, 2024 has fixed the revised structure of 13 Public Prosecutor Offices under Executive 8.
Accordingly, 113 regular posts in various cadres have been sanctioned for the Office of Public Prosecutors, High Court, Appellate Branch (Writ Cell), Mumbai and 33 posts in Group-D cadre have been approved to be taken over by the administration. The Office of the Public Prosecutor, High Court, Appellate Division (Writ Cell), Mumbai is functioning under the Ministry of Law and Justice. The High Court is attached to Mumbai and insufficient manpower is likely to hinder the functioning of the court.
Hence, the office has proposed to take over the services of a total of 23 vacancies in the direct service quota, namely, Short Writer (Lower Class) -3 posts and Clerk-Typician -20 posts, through the Service Mechanism Reference No. 3 was submitted under the letter here. The proposal was submitted to the Finance Department for approval.
