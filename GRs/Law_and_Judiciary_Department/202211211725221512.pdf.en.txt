Increase in Dearness Allowance from 9% (203% to 212%) with effect from 01.07.2022 and other provisions mentioned in the memorandum has been approved by the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India in the State of Maharashtra. shall apply to judicial officers retired before 01.01.2016.
Accordingly, the High Court of Maharashtra. Retired Judicial Officers as on 01.01.2016. Dearness Allowance@ 212% will be admissible with effect from 01.07.2022. The increase in dearness allowance should be paid in cash.
