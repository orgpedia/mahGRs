The Proposal:
As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is mandatory for persons with disabilities to hold posts in the government service. In this regard, the Central Government vide notification dated 04.01.2021 has published the list of posts for persons with Benchmark Disabilities notified on 04.01.2021 under the heading List of Posts Identified suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities notified in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notifications of the Central Government on the website www.disabilityaffairs.gov.in. As per the said notification, the posts in Group' A' to Group' D', which have been ensured by the Central Government for Divyangjan, in respect of posts in the State Government establishments (including posts in subordinate offices) or in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to those in the State Government service, even if the designations of such posts are different, will continue to be ensured for Divyangjan and accordingly the Administrative Department shall ensure for the vacancies in their establishments at their level. 4 decisions have been made. Accordingly, the matter of ensuring these posts was under the consideration of the government.
