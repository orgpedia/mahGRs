Jaisinghpur. Proposal for providing furniture for the first floor of the court building at Shirol, Kolhapur. The Bombay High Court has asked the government. Accordingly, administrative approval for the work is under consideration.

Jaisinghpur. Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded by this Government to the works (as per details mentioned below) at an estimated cost of Rs. 1,07,86, 243/- (Rupees One Crore Seven Lakh Eighty Six Thousand Two Hundred and Forty Three only).
