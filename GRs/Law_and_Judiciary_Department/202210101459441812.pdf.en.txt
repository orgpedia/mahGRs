Preamble: For the Gram Nyayalayas Scheme of the Central Government, the Accounting Year 2014 0477 has been provided under various objectives. An SNA (State Nodal Agency) account has been opened in a nationalized bank under the PFMS system for the Gram Nyayalayas scheme of the Central Government. The Government was considering the creation of 31 Subsidiary Grants for the purpose of operationalizing this Account and to meet the expenditure, including salary, of the Gram Nyayalayas in the State.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government have accorded approval for opening of 31 Subsidiary Grants for provision of funds in the Nationalized Bank under PFMS system to the State Nodal Agency (SNA) accounts of Gram Nyayalayas (Gram Nyayalayas) of the Central Government under the head Objective Demand No. J-1, 2014-Judgment, (00) 105-Civil and Sessions Courts, (02) District and Sessions Judges, (02) (02) Establishment of Gram Nyayalayas (Central Assistance) 2014 0477 under Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008). Further, a supplementary demand for Rs. 702.05 lakh (Rs. Accordingly, a total amount of Rs. 702.05 lakhs (Rupees Seven Crores Two Lakhs Five Thousand only) is being disbursed under 31 Auxiliary Grants. The expenditure required for the Gram Nyayalayas should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2022-23 under the head of accounts mentioned above.
The District and Sessions Courts concerned should take steps to implement the PFMS system on a priority basis and ensure that the funds are spent in a timely manner.
