The Proposal:
The Department. As per the Government decision dated 20.12.2016, the vehicle of the family courts functioning in the State has been reviewed. Accordingly, 2 vehicles are permissible for Family Court, Nagpur as per the review. The Family Court, Nagpur has submitted the proposal to the Government for approval vide letter dated 13.03.2015 mentioned at para (1). Therefore, the matter of granting administrative approval to the proposal for purchase of 1 vehicle for Family Court, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government has approved the purchase of one Government vehicle for the use of the office of the Judge, Family Court, Nagpur.
