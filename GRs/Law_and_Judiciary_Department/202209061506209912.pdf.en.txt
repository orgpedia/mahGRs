Pursuant to the Government decision dated 04.03.2022, 192 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Nanded have been extended till 31.08.2022. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 192 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023.
