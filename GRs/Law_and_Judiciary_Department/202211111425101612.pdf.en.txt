Preamble The Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai vide letter dated 09.06.2022 has requested to provide funds for payment of salary for services rendered on contract basis by an external agency to the Court of Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai. Accordingly, the proposal to provide funds for this Court was under consideration of the Government as the Supplementary Demand submitted to the Legislature in the Monsoon Session of August, 2022 for provision of funds was approved.
The Legislature has approved the Supplementary Demand under the head 2014 0234 under the Objectives of" 10 Contractual Services" for the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai for payment of services rendered by external agencies to the Court, totalling to Rs. 60,66,000/- (Rupees Sixty Lakh Sixty Thousand only), as mentioned in the following table in para.
