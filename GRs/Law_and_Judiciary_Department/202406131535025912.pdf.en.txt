By the Office Order dated 11" June, 2024 the Co-ordination Committee has been constituted to facilitate organizing the conference to be held on the Criminal Laws viz. The Bhartiya Nyay Sanhita, 2023,The Bhartiya Nagrik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023 and The Bhartiya Saksh Adhiniyam, at Mumbai. The following additional members are nominated in co-ordination committee for effective co-ordination.
1. The Collector, Mumbai City.
2. The Additional Commissioner (City), Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai.
3. The Director (Information) (News and Public Relations), Mumbai.
