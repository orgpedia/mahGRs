Preamble: Figure of 13 Public Prosecutor's Offices under Office of the Commissioner 8 Reference No. 1 decision of the government. It was approved on 15.2.2007. According to the figure, a total of 11 posts of various cadres have been sanctioned for the office of the Public Prosecutor, Supreme Court, New Delhi. There are 4 vacancies in the post of Clerk. There are 2 regular employees. Also, 1 vacancy in Clerk Typist cadre has been filled through external recruitment and 1 vacancy is currently vacant. Considering the increasing workload in the office of the Public Prosecutor, Supreme Court, New Delhi, the Government Pleader, Office of the Public Prosecutor, Supreme Court, New Delhi has requested for approval to fill up 1 post in the cadre of Clerk-Typist by external agencies vide Reference No. 3 was written here. Accordingly, a proposal was submitted to the Finance Department for taking over the services of one of the posts of Clerk Typist Cadre in the said office through an external agency. As per the approval of the Finance Department, the proposal for taking the services of a post in the cadre of Clerk-Typist by an external agency has been sent to the Finance Department, Govt. 9.6.2017 in the presence of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department and Principal Secretary (Expenditure), Finance Department. The proposal has been approved by the Sub-Committee in its meeting held on 7 th June, 2022.
