Preamble In pursuance of the Government notification dated 28th February, 2014, vide No. 1 of the above mentioned Notification, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Mandangad in Ratnagiri district. In pursuance of the above mentioned Government Decision dated 13th February, 2012 mentioned in A.No.2, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. The District Court Ratnagiri has requested to declare the Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya Mandangad (Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Dapoli) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for Mandangad Gram Nyayalaya. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Mandangad, District Ratnagiri as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Mandangad, Ratnagiri.
""" For the Gram Nyayalayas established under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008 at Mandangad, District Ratnagiri, the Magistrate, Gram Nyayalayas, Mandangad, District Ratnagiri is being declared as"""" Head of Office"""" and"""" Drawing and Disbursing Officer"""" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.""" The order will be limited to financial matters.
