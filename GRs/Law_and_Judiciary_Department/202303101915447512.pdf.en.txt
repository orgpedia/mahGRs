Government's decision
Subject to the Government decision dated 08.09.2022, 138 temporary posts on the establishment of District and Sessions Court Washim have been extended till 28.02.2023. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 138 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
