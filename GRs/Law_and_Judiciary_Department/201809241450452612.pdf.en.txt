(1), (2), (3) and (5), the District and Sessions Courts in the State have been prescribed the mandatory conditions to be followed while providing services on contractual basis through external mechanisms. As per letter dated 11.08.2017, Assistant Manager, Ma. The High Court of Bombay has requested the District and Sessions Court Aurangabad to create fictitious posts of Evacuees for the 7 Evacuees in the new building in District Aurangabad and make their services available on contractual basis through an external mechanism. The government has taken the following decisions:

Government Decision The Government has approved the creation of 7 temporary posts of Evacuees for the new building of District and Sessions Court Aurangabad and District Aurangabad and to provide their services on contract basis through external mechanism.
While making the above 7 posts available on contract through external agencies, it will be mandatory to comply with all the terms and conditions of the Government Decision No. 2512/ PR.S. 157/ K.A.12 dated January 27, 2015 and the corresponding Government Decisions.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be provided under the head Demand No. J-1, 2014-Judgment, (00) 105 Civil and Sessions Courts, (02) District and Sessions Judge, (02) (01) District and Sessions Judge, (20140172) Datta Mat, 13 Office Expenditure.
This Government Resolution is being issued with the approval of the Department under the Informal Reference No. 405/18/ Expenditure-5 of the Department of Finance dated 04.08.2018 and Informal Reference No. 660/ Appuc of the Department of Finance dated 04.08.2018.
