Circular
The Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Assistant Charity Commissioner on Establishment, Mumbai, fixes the seniority number and date of seniority of Group-A cadre officers. Temporary Service Excellence List dated 01.01.2024, Reference No. 9 was published by the government circular here.
No objections have been received as of the date 13.03.2024 pursuant to this tentative Service Excellence List. Considering this, the present Service Excellence List is being finalised. This final merit list is mentioned in Annexure-A to this circular.
A copy of this final merit list is being brought to the notice of all officers of the Assistant Charity Commissioner cadre at the establishment of the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
This Government Circular is available on the Maharashtra AAT www.maharashtra.gov.in website.
