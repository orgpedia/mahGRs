No. 8/16/ IPC, dated 21.02.2019 6 GOVERNMENT JUDGMENT, LAW& JUSTICE DEPARTMENT NO. 0816/625/ PR. 101/ 11, dated 05.01.2019 Gram Nyayalayas have been established in the State under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008. In order to ensure smooth functioning of these Gram Nyayalayas, as per the decision of the Government under reference dated 09.10.2018, a total of 110 temporary posts in 22 Gram Nyayalayas have been extended for the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019. As it has been decided to set up Civil Court Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class in place of Gram Nyayalaya Bodwad, District Jalgaon, a total of 5 posts created for Gram Nyayalaya Bodwad have been lapsed as per the Government decision dated 05.01.2019. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the remaining 21 Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 105 temporary posts in the following 21 Gram Nyayalayas are being extended for the period from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
