This administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
1) The work of installation of the lift should be started only after getting approval from the architect for the model map, layout and detailed map.
2) When working on a loosely held provision, the work should be undertaken by making a detailed budget.
3) The Department of the Environment in the actual work, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No.177/ T. Action shall be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in the Notice dated 10th January, 2014.
4) Approval of all concerned local bodies/ authorities should be obtained before starting the work.
5) Public Works Department, Government Decision No. Action should be taken as per BDG 2017/ Q.No.60/ Building-2, dated 7th July, 2017.
