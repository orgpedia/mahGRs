Preamble: The number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be at least 500, along with the infrastructure e.g. The availability of court buildings, etc. All the necessary criteria as per the policy of the High Court appear to be fulfilled. The High Court of Bombay has recommended setting up of a senior level court of Civil Judge at Ausa, District Latur. Accordingly, the matter of setting up a court of Civil Judge Senior Level at Ausa, District Latur was under the consideration of the Government.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The government has decided to set up a civil court in Latur district of Maharashtra. Also, the Government is being permitted to create posts for this Court as indicated below, transfer from other Courts and take manpower within the limits of notional posts:
