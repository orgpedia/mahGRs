The proposal: Public Interest Litigation filed in the High Court, Mumbai No. In the year 216/2010. In pursuance of the orders passed by the High Court, Mumbai, fire audit of all court buildings in the State is being carried out and fire fighting/ fire safety measures are being taken. In this regard, a proposal for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the court building of the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai Esplanade Court (Fort Court) and Borivali Court under his jurisdiction has been approved. The High Court has submitted as per Reference No. 1. Maharashtra Fire Service, Mumbai. As directed in letter No. 3, in accordance with the measures suggested by the local fire officer, the budget for the year 2014-2015 has been prepared by the concerned Branch Engineer, Police Beat, CB Division, Mumbai and the concerned Deputy Engineer and Executive Engineer have been appointed as the Executive Engineer. The case was heard by the Bombay High Court and the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate.
The Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has examined the said budget and agreed to the administrative sanction. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai Esplanade Court (Fort Court) and the budget for the construction of fire fighting system/ fire preventive measures in the court building under his jurisdiction was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai Esplanade Court (Fort Court) and the court buildings under his jurisdiction at Borivali Court for installation of fire extinguishers/ fire safety measures. 3,00,289/- and Rs. 3,81,442/-, totaling to Rs.
