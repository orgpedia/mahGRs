In pursuance of the request made by the Office of the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai vide letter No. 1 in the above context, the matter of declaring the Deputy Commissioner of Charities, Jalgaon as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION Under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, for the Office of the Registrar of Public Trusts, Jalgaon, the Deputy Commissioner of Charities, Jalgaon is hereby declared to be the" Head of Office" and the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
