The availability of court buildings, etc. All other criteria as required by the policy of the High Court appear to be fulfilled. The High Court of Judicature at Bombay has recommended the establishment of a Court of District and Additional Sessions Judge at Aheri, District Gadchiroli, as a special case. Accordingly, the Government was considering the establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court at Aheri, District Gadchiroli. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The state government has decided to set up a district and additional sessions court in Gadchiroli. Also, the Government is approving the creation of posts and hiring of manpower by external agencies for this Court as indicated below:
