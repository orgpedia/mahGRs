Preamble: The Central Project Coordinator, e-Courts Project, Appellate Division, High Court, Mumbai has submitted a proposal for procurement of new computer content for District and Sessions Court, Raigad-Alibag and its subordinate courts vide Reference No. 1 submitted to the Government. The proposal has received the opinion of the Directorate of Information Technology. Reference No. Under Section 2, it was asked to submit a proposal for the purchase of computer content according to the revised specifications.
Accordingly, reference No. Under Section 3, a proposal for the purchase of computer materials according to the revised specifications was submitted to the government. The proposal was accepted by the Department of Information Technology. Accordingly, the approval was accorded in the meeting of the Project Implementation Committee of the Department held on 27.09.2023. Accordingly, the proposal for the purchase of new computer equipment for the District and Sessions Court, Raigad Alibag and its subordinate courts was under the consideration of the Government for administrative approval.
Government Decision:- For the purchase of new computer materials for the District and Sessions Court, Raigad-Alibag and their subordinate courts, Rs. A proposal for an amount of Rs. 40,77,485/- (Rupees Forty Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Four Hundred and Eighty Five only) is being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution.
