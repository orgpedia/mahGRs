Introduction: Many cases filed by and against the government are pending in various courts and tribunals. The government was considering the development of a computerized case management system to control all court cases filed by and against the government.
Government's decision:
To select the most suitable system from the off the shelf Case Management System available in the market to control all the court cases filed and pending against the government as well as the government in various courts and tribunals. The Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Department of Information Technology, Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India.
