Preamble In pursuance of the above, vide Government Order dated 26.07.2018 in A.S. (1), administrative approval has been accorded for construction of fire fighting system/ fire fighting measures for District and Sessions Court, Nanded and buildings under its control at Mukhed, Kandhar, Bhokar, Biloli, Kinwat, Hadgaon, Deglur, Loha, Naigaon, Mahur, Umri, Dharmabad, Himayatnagar and Ardhapur. As per the Government Order dated 24.08.2018, administrative approval has been accorded for the construction of fire fighting/ fire safety measures in the District and Sessions Court, Chandrapur and the buildings under its control and the court buildings at Warora, Bhadravati, Chimur, Rajura, Pombhurna, Ballarpur, Gondpimpri, Mool, Savli, Sindewahi, Brahmapuri and Nagbhid. As per the Government Order dated 15.10.2018 in Reference A. No. (3), administrative approval has been accorded for setting up of Fire Fighting System/ Fire preventive measures in District and Sessions Court Bhandara and Courts at Sakoli, Lakhandur, Pavani, Mohadi and Tumsar. The proposal to provide funds to this Court was under consideration of the Government as the Supplementary Demand presented to the Legislature in the Winter Session of November, 2018 for provision of funds for these works was approved.
