Preamble: Under the 14th Finance Commission, a total of 18 additional courts have been constituted and the posts for these 18 additional courts have been created as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 18 temporary posts for additional courts. The extension is required for the next period from 01.03.2022. Accordingly, the proposals have been received by the concerned District and Sessions Courts.
As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department in Reference No. 8, 01.03.2022 to D. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2022. Accordingly, the extension of temporary posts for 18 additional courts was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following temporary posts for 18 Additional Courts constituted under the 14th Finance Commission have been sanctioned by the Government. 01.03.2022 to D. The period is being extended up to 31.08.2022.
