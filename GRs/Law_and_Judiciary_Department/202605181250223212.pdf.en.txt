Date: May 18, 2026
See: Department of Law and Justice, Government Decision No. CHA-2025 / Q. No.81 / Ka-1D. 01.10.2025
The Proposal:
The aforesaid Government decision had approved the revival of the post of ROS in Group-D declared dead cadre in the Office of Public Trust Registration Office, Akola of the Charitable Trust to appoint Shri Vinod Mohan Sankat on compassionate grounds. However, the office of the Collector, Akola. By order of 30.09.2025, Mr. Sankat was given the charge of Collector.
A post of Swachhak (Group-D) (Pay scale 8 - 1: 15000-47600) has been compassionately appointed at the establishment of Office, Akola and Mr. Sankat has been posted as Swachhak at Collectorate, Akola. Since Mr. Sankat has joined the establishment of the Collectorate, Akola, the revived post of Sepoy in the Public Trust Registration Office, Akola serves no purpose. For this reason, the abolition of the revived post of sepoy was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The revived post of Sepoy, Group-D cadre in the Office of Public Trust Registration Office, Akola of the Charitable Trust is being abolished.
The Government decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
