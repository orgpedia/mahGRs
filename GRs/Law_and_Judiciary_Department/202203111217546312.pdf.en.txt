As per the Government decision dated 27.08.2021, 132 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court Aurangabad have been extended till 28.02.2022. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 132 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
