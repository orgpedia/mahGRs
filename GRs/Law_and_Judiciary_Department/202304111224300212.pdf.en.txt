Preamble In pursuance of the above, the Government have approved the establishment of a District and Additional Sessions Court at Paranda, District Osmanabad and creation of a post for this Court. The District and Additional Sessions Court at Paranda, District Osmanabad has been made functional with effect from 19th March, 2023 vide Government Notification dated 24th February, 2023. Accordingly, vide letter dated 30.03.2023, the Manager, Observations (1), Ma. On the request of the High Court of Bombay, the Government was considering declaring District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Paranda, District Osmanabad as Drawing and Disbursing Officers.

Government Resolution No: DDO 2023/ Q. No.66/ Q.12
The District and Additional Sessions Court, District Paranda, District Osmanabad, has been declared as the" Office Head" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 read with Finance Division No. 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated 17.04.2015.
