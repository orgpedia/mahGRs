Gram Nyayalayas were established in the State under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008.
In order to ensure smooth functioning of these Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 115 temporary posts in 22 Gram Nyayalayas have been extended for the period from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022 under the Contextual Government Resolution, Department of Law& Justice, dated 06.09.2021. In order to ensure smooth functioning of all 22 Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 115 temporary posts in the following 22 Gram Nyayalayas are being extended for the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
