Preamble: High Court of Judicature at Aurangabad. Considering the non-availability of government accommodation in accordance with the status of a judge, Mr. Justice S.M. Gavhane was allotted Plot No. 356, Sector N-1, Cidco, Aurangabad, to take on lease a private bungalow as a government accommodation. The approval has been granted under the Government Decision dated 31/07/2017. He signed an 11-month contract. Expires on 30/06/2018. At present, Aurangabad. The High Court administration has decided to extend the lease of the said private bungalow for the next 11 months as there is no government accommodation available for the judge according to his status. The Divisional Commissioner and Chairman Government Accommodation Allotment Committee, Aurangabad Division, Aurangabad, under the As per letter dated 27/06/2018, Hon'ble High Court of Judicature at Aurangabad. Bhartendu Vats, the owner of the private bungalow, has approved the extension as per the provisions of Section 14 and 15 of the lease agreement. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the lease of the private bungalow for the next 11 months. Accordingly, the court made the following decision.
