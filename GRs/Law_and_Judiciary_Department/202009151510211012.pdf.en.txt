Government's decision
Subject to the Government decision dated 02.03.2020, 134 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Aurangabad have been extended till 31.08.2020. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 134 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021.
