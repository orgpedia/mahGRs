The Proposal:
In pursuance of the above, vide Government Order dated 17.10.2018, No. 1, it has been approved to establish a District and Additional Sessions Court at Panvel in Raigad District. In pursuance of the Government Order dated 04.01.2019 in Reference No.2, the funds required for the said Court have been sanctioned. As per the notification dated 18.07.2019, District and Additional Sessions Court, Panvel, District Raigad has become functional with effect from 27.07.2019. In pursuance of the request made by the District Court, Raigad-Alibag vide letter No. 4, the matter of declaring District Judge-I and Additional Sessions Judge, Panvel, G. Raigad as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
District Judge-I and Additional Sessions Judge, Panvel, G. Raigad have been declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the District and Additional Sessions Court, Panvel, Raigad.
The order will be limited to financial matters.
