Government Resolution No. Parivartan- 2816/ PR No. 191 (Part-I)/ 16/ KA-8 The Committee in its meeting held on 13.12.2022 reviewed the sanctioned posts in the establishment of 13 Public Prosecutor Offices and approved the revised figure of total 581 posts. Accordingly, the matter of issuing administrative sanction was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government are approving the revised figure of 581 posts (418 regular+ 163 external) as shown in Annexure A attached on the establishment of 13 Public Prosecutor Offices under the Department of Law and Justice, Office of the Registrar General.
The posts in Group' D' cadre as indicated in Annexure A in 13 Public Prosecutor's Offices under this office have been declared as dead cadre. In the said deceased cadre, it is being approved to take the services through external agencies as mentioned in' B" of Annexure A. This service is provided by the Finance Department. Guidelines issued vide Government Resolution dated 27.04.2022 are to be followed.
Services should be taken from external agencies in proportion to the number of vacant manpower excluding the number of employees currently working in the posts designated as" dead cadre" in Group' D."
The post will be automatically abolished/ cancelled after the post falls vacant due to retirement/ resignation/ death etc. of the incumbent. After that, the services of that post should be taken by external agencies as per the requirement.
The guidelines issued by the Finance Department from time to time through government circulars regarding the provision of services by external agencies should be strictly followed. Also, the head of the office should ensure that the prevailing law is not violated while providing services from external agencies.
The decision was taken by the High-Level Committee of Secretaries. As per the approval given in the meeting dated 13.12.2022 and the Finance Department has issued the following. Reference No. 93/ Apuq, D. is being issued pursuant to the consent given under 14.02.2023.
