Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated 04.03.2022, 1230 temporary posts of Judicial Officers in District and Sessions Courts have been extended till 31.08.2022. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 1232 temporary posts of Judicial Officers as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
