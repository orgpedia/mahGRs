For the purpose of" Strengthening the Justice Sector" recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, a fund of Rs. 1014 crore has been earmarked for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, a sum of Rs.228 crore has been earmarked for the renovation of the functional courts for a period of five years. The above approval is being granted subject to the following conditions: i. The Maharashtra Public Works Department, the rules for granting administrative approval in the rule book and the related rules should be complied with.
(ii) The repair work shall be carried out by the Public Works Department by appointing a contractor through the Administrative Departments, if the post of Executive Engineer or above is held by the concerned Administrative Departments, depending on the tender method prescribed.
(iii) Budgetary provisions should be available.
3. The cost of renovation of court buildings at District and Sessions Court Wardha shall be met from the approved provision under the head Demand No. J-1, 2014 JUDGMENT, (00) JUDGMENT, 102-High Courts, (11) Renovation of functional courts, (11) (01) Renovation of functional court (2014 0548).
4. Before inviting tender for the said work. It shall be the responsibility of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Wardha to ensure that the above works fall within the scope of the action plan fixed by the High Court of Bombay and the works fixed by the Dedicated Cell. The e-tendering process should be carried out by the Public Works Department following the prevailing procedure and other ancillary proceedings should be completed in accordance with this administrative approval.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the Public Works Department vide No. No. 74/2019/ Ima-2, dated 16.02.2019 and with the concurrence of the Department and subject to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Government Resolution, Law and Justice Department, dated 31.07.2019.
