The Proposal:
In pursuance of the above decision, administrative approval has been accorded for establishment of Civil Court (Senior Level) at Wai in Satara district. As per the Government Resolution dated 09.01.2023 in Reference No. 2, the approval of the Legislature has been obtained for the funds required for the said Courts. The Civil Court (Senior Level), YG, Satara Court has become functional with effect from 16th January, 2023 vide notification dated 10.01.2023. The Civil Court, Y.G., on the request made under the letter dated the 4th and 5th A.N. The government was considering declaring Civil Judge (senior level) Y. G. Satara as the head of the office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the Satara court.
For Satara, Civil Judge (Senior Level) Y. G. Satara is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." They are being authorised to deposit all the court dues in the treasury. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, dated 17th April, 2015, in Part I, Sub-Division No. 3 of Sub-Division 1 and in exercise of the powers conferred under Rules 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
