Reorganisation of cadre wise/ post wise/ district wise posts sanctioned and classification of permanent/ temporary posts and classification of manpower to be taken up by external agencies as per the revised pattern of the office of the Commissioner of Charitable Endowments, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai and offices under its jurisdiction are being approved subject to the following terms and conditions: (a) Services to be taken up by external agencies as per requirement within the limits of manpower indicated district wise in Annexure-A of this Government Order.
