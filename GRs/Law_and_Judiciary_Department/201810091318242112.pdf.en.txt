No. 8/16/ IPC, dated 7th September, 2018 6 Government Resolution, Law and Justice Division No. No.55/ 12 dated 10.04.2018 Gram Nyayalayas have been established in the State under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008. In order to ensure smooth functioning of these Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 115 temporary posts in 23 Gram Nyayalayas have been extended for the period from 01.03.2018 to 30.09.2018 as per the decision of the Government in this regard dated 16.03.2018. As the Court of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class was established on 23.06.2018 in place of the Court of Gram Nyayalaya Korapana, District Chandrapur, a total of 5 posts created for Gram Nyayalaya Korapana have lapsed as per the Government decision dated 10.04.2018. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the remaining 22 Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 110 temporary posts in the following 22 Gram Nyayalayas are being extended for the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019.
