खालील अटींच्या अधीन राहून मान्यता देण्यात येत आहे.
1. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit Account.
3. For Civil Court Deposit, the credit to the Personal Deposit will be the Civil Deposit,
Decreetal Amount, Rent, Maintenance and etc. and the expenditure out of the Personal
Deposit Accounts will be as per courts order.
For the Criminal Court Deposit, the credit to the PD account will be Criminal Fine
Amount, Criminal Cash surety, Maintenance Amount, Penalty and the expenditure out of
the Personal Deposit will be as per courts order.
4. No Revenue receipt/Revenue expenditure transactions shall be booked in this PD.
5. The balances in this Personal Deposit Account shall be reconciled by the Court on
Monthly basis with the District Treasury Office.
6. The balances have to be reconciled with AG office and confirmation of Balances is to
be submitted by the Treasury Officer to AG office at the end of the Financial Year.
7. The Finance Department GR dated 05.09.2023 regarding operation of the PD/PLA
should be followed.
8. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this Personal Deposit
Account.
