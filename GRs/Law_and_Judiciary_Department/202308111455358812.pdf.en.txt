The Proposal:
Proposal for construction of 10 residential quarters for District Judge and Ad-hoc District Judge, 5 for Civil Judge (V.S.) and 5 for Chief Judicial Magistrate at Justice Nagar, District Buldhana. The High Court, Mumbai has submitted to the Government under the above reference letter. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government's decision:
A total of 10 residential quarters, 5 for District Judge and Adhoc District Judge, 5 for Civil Judge (ST) and Chief Judicial Magistrate, will be constructed at Justice Nagar, District Buldhana. Administrative Sanction is being accorded by this Government Resolution for the works estimated to cost Rs. 7,86,02, 431/- (Rupees Seven Crores Eighty Six Lakhs Two Thousand Four Hundred and Thirty One only).
