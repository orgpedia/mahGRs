The Proposal:
In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has issued Reference No. The order dated 23 rd March, 2021 has directed to adopt a revised methodology for disbursement of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes through Public Finance Management System. Accordingly, Finance Department vide Reference No. 2 The government circular here has given instructions regarding the use of Public Finance Management System for the distribution of funds for centrally sponsored schemes.
Accordingly, for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes under Department of Law& Justice, Reference No. A Nodal Officer and a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for each Centrally Sponsored Scheme has been appointed under the Office Order. It was under consideration of the government to issue a comprehensive government decision regarding the opening of accounts of these Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Enforcement Agency (IA) in nationalized banks and the appointment of Nodal Officer and Single Nodal Agency (SNA).

The following authorities are being declared for the implementation and utilization of Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for disbursement of funds under Centrally Sponsored Schemes:
Nodal Officer:- For the implementation of PFMS, Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (KA-13) at the State level will be the nodal officer for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes of the Department of Law and Justice and will coordinate the Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
(b) Single Nodal Agency: The Office bearers of the scheme for implementation of PFMS, Department of Law and Justice will be the single nodal agency for the Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
(c) Checker and Maker:- The officers/ employees looking after the affairs of the Accounts Department in the Regional Office implementing the schemes of the Department of Law and Justice will perform the responsibility as Maker. Also, the head of the office will work as a checker.
