In pursuance of the Government decision dated 09.10.2018, 1243 temporary posts of Judicial Officers in District and Sessions Courts have been extended till 28.02.2019. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 1216 temporary posts of Judicial Officers as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
