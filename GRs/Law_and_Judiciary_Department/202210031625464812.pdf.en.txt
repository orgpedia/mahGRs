Preamble- In pursuance of the Government notification dated 01.02.2014, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Goregaon in Gondia district. 2 As per the Government Decision dated 27th September, 2011 and 3rd February, 2012, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been constituted in the State. District Court Gondia has requested to declare Magistrate Gram Nyayalay Goregaon as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the scheme as the fund distribution will be done through PFMS system for Gram Nyayalay. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Magistrate Gram Nyayalay Goregaon as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalay Scheme for Gram Nyayalay Goregaon at Gondia.
The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya, Goregaon, District Gondia is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Gram Nyayalaya established under the Gram Nyayalaya Act, 2008 at Goregaon, District Gondia. The order will be limited to financial matters.
