Introduction- The main account of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Sironcha in the form of a personal deposit account in the form of Civil No. 8443-106 has been closed due to non-transaction of accounts with effect from 01.04.2015. As there is no provision for re-opening of the closed self-employment account as per the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Gadchiroli, vide letter dated 2nd August, 2018, referred to in para. The proposal was submitted to the government through the High Court of Bombay.
Accordingly, the Government was considering to approve the opening of a Civil Private Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Sironcha, District Gadchiroli.
Government Resolution- The Government Resolution issued earlier regarding the opening of a civil private limited account in the name of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Sironcha, District Gadchiroli, to deposit the amounts due to Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Sironcha, District Gadchiroli, as per the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Clause 1 Rule No. 512, is being superseded and the revised Government Resolution is as follows:
# Page 2
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PLA 2018/ PR No. 182/ K. 12
In accordance with the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, clause 1, rule no. 512, to deposit the amount of the Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Sironcha, District Gadchiroli, in the name of the Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Sironcha, District Gadchiroli.
The opening of a Civil Private Account is allowed subject to the following conditions: 1) P.L.A. is to be kept operational and to be continued.
2) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
3) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
4) Receipt an account of Civil Deposits, Decreetal amount, Consideration money only be deposited / Credited in PLA.
5) No receipt / Expenditure transaction which is to be made through regular Budget Provision shall be incurred from this PLA.
6) The administrator shall carry out reconciliation as per schedule.
7) All provisions under the MTR will be applicable to this P.L.A.
8) This Personal Account will be opened after A.G.(A&E)II, Nagpur issues an authority to open Personal Ledger Account in Nagpur Treasury.
This Government Resolution has been issued vide Informal Reference No. 366/2018/ Co. P. 5, dated 28.09.2018 and Informal Reference No. 2/ UOR/ L& JD/ 2018-19/830 dated 02.11.2018 and being issued with their concurrence.
