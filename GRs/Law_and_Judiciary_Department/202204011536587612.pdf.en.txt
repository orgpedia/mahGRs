The proposal: Public Interest Litigation filed in the High Court, Mumbai No. In the year 216/2010. In pursuance of the orders passed by the High Court, Mumbai, fire audit of all court buildings in the State is being carried out and fire fighting/ fire safety measures are being taken. Accordingly, a proposal for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the new court building of District and Sessions Court, Dhule has been submitted vide letter No. 1 of the Reference No. In this case
The Budget for the year 2019-2020 has been prepared by the concerned Branch Engineer (Electrical) and witnessed by Deputy Engineer (Electrical), PWD, Dhule and Executive Engineer (Electrical), PWD, Nashik and Chief Engineer (Electrical), PWD, Mumbai and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Dhule. In this regard, the Chief Fire Officer, Malegaon Municipal Corporation, Malegaon has informed that it is necessary to carry out these works as per the provisions of the Fire Prevention Measures Plan.
Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of the budget for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the new court building at District Court, Dhule was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the construction of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the new court building at District Court, Dhule, a total amount of Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded for the budget/ expenditure of Rs. 1,27,08, 238/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Seven Lakh Eight Thousand Two Hundred and Thirty Eight only).
