Preamble: The number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be at least 500. Availability of court buildings etc. It appears that all the necessary criteria as per the policy of the High Court are being fulfilled. The High Court of Judicature at Bombay has recommended the establishment of a Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Umarkhed, District Yavatmal and creation of a post for this court and the office of Assistant Public Prosecutor Umarkhed. Accordingly, the matter of setting up a court of Civil Judge Senior Level at Umarkhed, District Yavatmal and creation of a post for this court as well as for the office of Assistant Public Prosecutor Umarkhed was under the consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, it is being approved to establish a Civil Judge Senior Level Court in Umarkhed, District Yavatmal. As well as this Court and the Assistant
