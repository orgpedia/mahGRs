The Proposal:
Public Interest Litigation filed in the High Court, Mumbai No. In the year 216/2010. In pursuance of the orders passed by the High Court, Mumbai, fire audit of all court buildings in the State is being carried out and fire fighting/ fire safety measures are being taken. Accordingly, a proposal for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the District and Sessions Court, Ahmednagar and the court buildings under its jurisdiction has been submitted vide letter No. 1 and 2. In this case, the budget for the year 2018-2019 has been prepared by the concerned Branch Engineer (Electrical) and witnessed by Deputy Engineer (Electrical), Public Works Department, Ahmednagar and Executive Engineer (Electrical), Public Works Department, Nashik and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Ahmednagar. In this regard, the Chief Fire Officer, Ahmednagar Municipal Corporation, Ahmednagar, does not mind doing such work as per the provisions of the Fire Prevention Measures Plan. Also, 15 projects are under review. This is stated in the letter No. 3. The Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has examined the said budget and agreed to the administrative sanction. Accordingly, the government was considering granting administrative approval to the budget for the construction of fire fighting system/ fire preventive measures in the court buildings of Rahuri, Shevgaon, Nevasa, Pathardi, Karjat, Parner, Sangamner, Akole, Jamkhed, Shrigonda, Shrirampur, Kopargaon, Rahata, Senior Court, Ahmednagar and Junior Court, Ahmednagar.
