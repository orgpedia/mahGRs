Preamble- In pursuance of the Government notification dated 29th September, 2009, vide No. 1 of the above, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Sakri in Dhule district. 2 As per the Government Decision dated 27th September, 2011 and 3rd February, 2012, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been constituted in the State. District Court Dhule has requested to declare Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Sakri as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the scheme as the fund will be distributed through PFMS system for Gram Nyayalayas. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Sakri as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Sakri at Dhule.
Government Decision- For the Gram Nyayalaya established under the Gram Nyayalaya Act 2008 at Sakri, District Dhule, under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules 1968

The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalay Sakri, District Dhule is being declared as the" Head of Office" as well as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." The order will be limited to financial matters.
