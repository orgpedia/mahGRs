Under Section 140, full powers have been delegated to the Administrative Department, Head of Department, Regional Head of Department, Head of Office/ Head of Institutions under the condition that they approve the purchase of reference books, publications for day-to-day office use.
With the change in information technology over time, the medium of the above mentioned things has also changed. Books, publications, reports, journals are available in electronic form and are available in database form. The Bombay High Court Library.
They are purchased at the residence of the judge. Such databases have to be renewed from time to time, without having to purchase them every year. Accordingly, a proposal to amend the aforesaid financial powers in a time-bound manner was under consideration of the Government. The government is taking the following steps:
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As approved by the Committee on Administrative and Financial Powers, the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part I, Sub-Division II, Roll No. 43-A, Rule No. 140 The Government is granting approval to modify the financial powers subject to the prescribed terms and conditions and the prescribed financial limits as indicated in the Annexure annexed to this Government Resolution. If the expenditure is expected to be more than the said financial limit and the conditions laid down or any of the conditions or conditions are not met, the proposal will require the approval of the Government.
The exercise of the financial powers conferred by this Government Decision shall be subject to the terms and conditions contained in the rules and regulations contained in the various financial statements and
