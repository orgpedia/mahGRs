The Proposal:
The design of 13 Public Prosecutors Offices under Office of the Commissioner-8 has been approved by the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its meeting held on 13.12.2022. As per the revised figure, 58 regular posts of various cadres have been sanctioned for the office of the Public Prosecutor, High Court, Bench Nagpur and the services of 18 posts have been approved to be taken through external agencies. Due to vacancies in the office, the law officers are facing difficulties in filing replies to the claims before the court, communicating with the concerned government departments and delaying the disposal of cases. Therefore, the Office of the Department of Law and Justice, Nagpur has issued Reference No. The letter had submitted a proposal to allow filling up of vacancies in the office of the Public Prosecutor, High Court, Bench Nagpur by external agencies. Accordingly, a proposal has been made to approve the services of a total of 4 posts, namely, Short Writer Lower Grade-1, Short Writer-1 and Clerk Typist-2 in the office of the Public Prosecutor, High Court, Bench Nagpur, which are vacant in the direct service quota, from external agencies. It was presented at the meeting on June 27, 2023. The proposal has been approved by the cabinet.
