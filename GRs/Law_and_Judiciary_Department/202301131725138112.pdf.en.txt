Preamble: Decision of the Government. Relaxation in reimbursement of medical expenses has been made applicable to retired judicial officers as on 10.08.2022 at par with serving judicial officers. However, in pursuance of the said Government decision, there are difficulties in sanctioning reimbursement of medical expenses of retired judicial officers. 2 letters have been sent to the government. Accordingly, a standard operating procedure (SOP) for reimbursement of medical expenses of retired judicial officers was under consideration of the Government.
Government circular: As per the Government decision dated 10.08.2022, reimbursement of medical expenses to all retired judicial officers in the State has been made admissible on the pattern of serving judicial officers. The decision has come into effect from the day it was taken. Accordingly, the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for reimbursement of medical expenses of retired judicial officers will be as follows.
Retired judicial officers can be given the option of either of the two options for reimbursement of medical expenses at the place where they have settled or at the place from where they have retired.
(2) The Public Health Department, as per the option given by the retired judicial officer, vide Government Resolution No. The Competent Authority as mentioned in 06.06.2019 shall have the powers to approve reimbursement of medical expenses of retired judicial officers.

The existing pattern of application for reimbursement of medical expenses will be applicable to retired judicial officers as well as serving judicial officers.
(3) The concerned retired Judicial Officers of Mumbai City and Mumbai Suburban District, who have opted for medical reimbursement, shall submit the application to the Municipal Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai. After the competent authority thereof approves reimbursement of medical expenses, the said expenditure shall be reimbursed in accordance with the provisions of" Demand No. J-1, (01) Mumbai City Civil and Sessions Judge (01) (01) Mumbai City Civil and Sessions Judge (Datta Mat) (Mandatory) 20140163."
4) The concerned retired judicial officers, who have opted for districts other than Mumbai city and Mumbai suburban districts, shall submit the application for reimbursement of medical expenses to the District and Sessions Court of those districts. After the competent authority there approves the reimbursement of medical expenses, the expenditure will be released to the district under" Demand No. J-1, 2014-Judgment, 105 Civil and Sessions Court 02 District and Sessions Judge (02) (01) District and Sessions Judge (Datta Mat) (Compulsory) 20140172."
