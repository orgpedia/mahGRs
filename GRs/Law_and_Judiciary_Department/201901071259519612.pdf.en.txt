For the purpose of" Strengthening the Justice Sector" recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, a fund of Rs. 1014 crore has been earmarked for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, a sum of Rs.228 crore has been earmarked for the renovation of the functional courts for a period of five years. A High Level Steering Committee has been constituted on 9th March, 2016 for effective implementation of the recommendations of the Commission. In the meeting held on 28.03.2016, Shri. The action plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle. As per the Government decision dated 01.01.2019, a total of 13 buildings beyond the limit of Rs.50 lakhs, which were approved in the meeting of the High Level Control Committee held on 06.08.2018, have been taken up for repair and renovation. With the approval of the Chief Minister, the power to grant administrative approval has been given to the Department of Law and Justice as an administrative department subject to conditions.

The above approval is being granted subject to the following conditions: i. The Maharashtra Public Works Department, the rules for granting administrative approval in the rule book and the related rules should be complied with.
(ii) The repair work shall be carried out by the Public Works Department by appointing a contractor through the Administrative Departments, if the post of Executive Engineer or above is held by the concerned Administrative Departments, depending on the tender method prescribed.
(iii) Budgetary provisions should be available.
3. The cost of renovation of court buildings at Kuhi, Kamthi and Parsivani under the control of District and Sessions Court, Nagpur should be met from the provision approved under the head" Demand No. J-1, 2014 Nyaydan, (00) Nyaydan, 102-High Courts, (11) Renovation of Working Courts, (11) (01) Renovation of Working Court (2014 0548)."
4. Before inviting tender for the said work. It shall be the responsibility of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur to ensure that the above works fall within the scope of the action plan fixed by the High Court of Bombay and the works fixed by the Dedicated Cell. The e-tendering process should be carried out by the Public Works Department following the prevailing procedure and other ancillary proceedings should be completed in accordance with this administrative approval.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the Public Works Department No.No. 727/2017/ Ima-2, dated 12.01.2018 and with the concurrence of that Department and subject to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Government Resolution, Law and Justice Department dated 01.01.2019.
