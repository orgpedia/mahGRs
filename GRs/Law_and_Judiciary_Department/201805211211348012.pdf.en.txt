Government circular
For appointment of Notaries under the Notaries Act, 1952 and the Notaries Rules, 1956, in the following Talukas of Kolhapur District, candidates having the qualifications prescribed in Rule 3 under the Notaries Rules, 1956 may apply in the manner and in the form prescribed in Rule 4 of the said Rules. or form. ); are being sought by the competent authority, Department of Law and Justice, Ministry, Mumbai. Apart from the documents prescribed in the Notary Rules, the candidates are required to attach a certificate of domicile (domicile certificate), a certified copy of the ration card (ration card) or Aadhaar card; a certificate from the president/ secretary of the Taluk Bar Association, that the applicant is a member of the Bar Association; and a copy of the PAN card, if there is a PAN number, with the application in the prescribed format. A photo is also required to be submitted.
According to the circular, eligible candidates/ applicants who had earlier sent their applications to the government on their own will have to apply again. Applications submitted in advance will not be considered.
All eligible candidates are required to send their application in the prescribed format only by Registered Post/ Speed Post to the competent authority who will sign below.
