The Proposal:
As per the Government Order dated 23.03.2011 in Reference A. No. (1), administrative approval has been granted for opening of Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil and Criminal Court, Aheri, District Gadchiroli. As per Maharashtra Treasury Rule 495, the said Civil Deposit Account has been closed with effect from 01.04.2014 due to non-transaction. The establishment of this Court is permanent and for the smooth functioning of the Civil and Criminal Courts, the Court is requested to be allowed to open a new Private Portfolio Account. The High Court of Bombay vide its letter dated 08.03.2022 referred to in the aforesaid Reference No. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of a Private Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge and Judicial Magistrate First Class, Aheri, District Gadchiroli.
