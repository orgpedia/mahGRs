Preamble: For establishment of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Vita, District-Sangli, Hon. As per the policy of the High Court, the minimum number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts in various places in the State should be 500, along with the infrastructure, e.g. There should be availability of court buildings and residences of judges, etc. The" Court Establishment Committee" of the High Court has approved the establishment of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Vita G. Sangli. Accordingly, Ma. The High Court. On 19/1/2019, a proposal for establishment of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Vita G. Sangli and creation of posts for the said Court has been submitted to the Government vide letter No. Accordingly, the matter of setting up the said court and creating a post for it was under the consideration of the Government.
The government has decided to set up a civil judge court at Vita in Sangli district for the four taluks of Vita, Palus, Kadegaon and Atpadi.
Orders for creation of posts will be issued after the approval of the sub-committee and the high-level secretary committee to create the necessary posts for this court.
