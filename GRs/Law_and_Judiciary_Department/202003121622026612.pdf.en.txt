Preamble: Reference No. 11 in 11 places where more than 1000 cases related to persons with disabilities, senior citizens and marginalized sections of the society are pending. As per the decision of the government, 11 special courts have been set up and 55 temporary posts have been created for this court. These are temporary positions. The request for continuation from 01.03.2020 has been made by the District and Sessions Court, Nagpur, Thane and Nashik vide its proposal at Reference No.2 to 4.
Reference No. 5 decision of the Finance Department. 01.03.2020 to dt. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2020.
Accordingly, the matter of continuation of these posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A total of 55 temporary posts in 11 special courts set up at 11 places where more than 1000 cases related to disabled persons, senior citizens and marginalized sections of the society are pending. 01.03.2020 to dt. This Government has decided to extend the period up to 31.08.2020.
