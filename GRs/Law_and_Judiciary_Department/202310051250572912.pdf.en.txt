Accordingly, the matter of approval for opening of Personal Deposit Account for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level, Sinnar, G. Nashik was under consideration of the Government.
In accordance with Rule 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the opening of a personal deposit account for deposits and repayment amounts in favour of Civil Judge Senior Level, Sinner, G. Nashik is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit Account.
3. The amount as per order passed by the Court in the matter will be deposited in this PLA and the amount as ordered by the Court will disbursed from this PD.
