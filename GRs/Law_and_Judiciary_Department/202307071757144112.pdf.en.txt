As it was taken by the committee of judges, the manager, Ma. The High Court of Bombay vide its letter dated 18th November, 2021. Accordingly, the government was considering setting up of Gram Nyayalayas at Harsul, Taluka Trimbakeshwar and District Nashik.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The government has decided to set up Gram Nyayalayas in the villages of Harsul, Trimbakeshwar, Trimbakeshwar, Nashik district, which fall under the limits of Trimbakeshwar Gram Nyayalay and Harsul police station. Also, the Government is approving the creation of posts for this Court as indicated below:
