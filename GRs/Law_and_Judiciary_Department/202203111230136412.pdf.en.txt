Pursuant to the Government decision dated 27.08.2021, 126 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court Wardha have been extended till 28.02.2022. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 126 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
