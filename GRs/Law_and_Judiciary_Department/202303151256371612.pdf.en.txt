Preamble: Under the 14th Finance Commission, a total of 24 Fast Track Courts have been set up for a period of 5 years. 1 The post has been created as per the decision of the government. Provisional posts for these 24 Fast Track Courts. After 28.02.2023, it is necessary to give an extension for the next period. Reference No. 3 decision of the Department of Finance. 01.03.2023 to Dy. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2023. Accordingly, the matter of extension of temporary posts of the 24 fast track courts was under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following temporary posts for 24 Fast Track Courts set up under the 14th Finance Commission have been sanctioned. 01.03.2023 to Dy. The period of extension is upto 31.08.2023 or till the completion of 5 years of tenure of the said Courts, whichever is earlier.
