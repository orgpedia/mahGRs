Preamble In pursuance of the Government notification dated 29th September, 2009, vide No. 1 of the above mentioned Notification, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Wardha (Sevagram) in Wardha district. Further, in pursuance of the aforesaid Government Decision dated 13th February, 2012, mentioned at Annexure-II, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. District Court Wardha has requested to declare Magistrate for Wardha (Sevagram) Gram Nyayalaya Wardha (Sevagram) (Civil Judge Junior Level and First Class Judicial Magistrate Wardha) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Principal District and Sessions Judge Wardha as Controlling Officer of the scheme. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to declare the Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Wardha (Sevagram) District Wardha as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Wardha (Sevagram) at Wardha and Principal District and Sessions Judge Wardha as the Controlling Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Wardha (Sevagram) at Wardha.
