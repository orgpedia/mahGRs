The Proposal:
Writ Petition No. 12882/2025 (Mr. Akshay Sanjay Dhumal v. Registrar Partnership Institute / Sub-Registrar Partnership Institute, Pune. The High Court of Bombay. The order dated 19.12.2025 directs that all pending cases in the Registrar Partnership Office be disposed of within 06 months. The manpower of the Registrar Partnership Institute, Mumbai and its Subordinate Office is falling short of compliance with this order. Hence, more manpower is required to dispose of pending cases. For this, the Department of Finance, Government Decisions, Govt. The Sub-Committee constituted on 09.06.2017 At the meeting held on 21st May, 2026, Reference No. 3 The Department's proposal to appoint a contractor who will complete the disposal of pending cases within 06 months under the chronology herein has been approved. It was under the consideration of the government to issue a government decision accordingly.
The government's decision:
The Registrar Partnership Institute, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and its administrative control offices at Pune, Nagpur and Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar are hereby authorised to appoint contractors / agencies through external agencies for disposal of pending cases, subject to the following conditions.
1. Instead of hiring manpower for disposal of pending cases, a contractor / agency should be appointed who can complete the pending work in 06 months. A service purchase agreement should be made for this.
For this, the entire pending work should be tendered.
