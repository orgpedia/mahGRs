The Proposal- M. As per the proposal of the High Court, the Government has decided to establish a District and Additional Sessions Court and Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Ambad in Jalna district and to create a post for the same. The approval was granted on October 13, 2020. In this government decision, it has been mentioned about spending the funds by making provisions in the respective financial year. Accordingly, the proposal to provide funds for this Court was under consideration of the Government as the symbolic supplementary demand submitted to the Legislature in the winter session of December, 2020 was approved.
