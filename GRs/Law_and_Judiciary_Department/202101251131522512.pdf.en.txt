The administrative approval has been granted at a price of Rs 4,24,14, 899.00. But as construction costs increased, so did the cost of construction. The High Court, vide letter No. 2, has submitted to the Government a proposal for revised administrative sanction for the construction of the said building. Accordingly, the proposal was under the consideration of the government to give administrative approval for the amendment.
Government Decision: Rs. Revised administrative approval is being accorded by this Government to the works (as per details indicated below) at an estimated cost of Rs. 5,79,29, 754/- (Rupees Five Crore Eighty-Nine Lakhs Thirty-Nine Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty Four only).
