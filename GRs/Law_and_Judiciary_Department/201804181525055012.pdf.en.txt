Accordingly, the proposal on the 2015-2016 tariff list for the repair/ renovation of the Court Building at Chiplun under the District Court, Ratnagiri, has been approved vide the aforesaid Reference No. 3 in the letter. The Bombay High Court has given its verdict. Providing Furniture, Braille Symbol& Renewals to approach road& name board. In the absence of work approved by the Dedicated Cell of the High Court and the High-Level Control Committee, administrative approval has been considered for other works. The Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has examined the said budget and agreed to the administrative sanction.
Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the following work budget was under consideration.
Government's decision:
Renovation of the Court Building at Chiplun under District Court, Ratnagiri under the scheme of Renovation of the Court functioning as per the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission.
