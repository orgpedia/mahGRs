The court building and the residence of the judges should be available. As all the criteria required as per the policy of the High Court are being fulfilled, the Government has decided to set up a District and Additional Sessions Court at Chiplun, Ratnagiri. The decision was approved by the Supreme Court Committee. Accordingly, the matter of approving the establishment of this court and the creation of 25 posts required by it was under consideration of the government.
The Government have decided to establish District and Additional Sessions Court at Chiplun, District Ratnagiri and to create 25 posts (23 regular and 2 external agency posts) for the same from the date of commencement of the Court as indicated in the following prospectus.
