The proposal m. In Public Interest Litigation No. 216/2010 filed in the High Court of Bombay. As per the orders of the High Court of Bombay, fire audit of all court buildings in the state is being done and fire fighting/ fire prevention measures are being taken. Accordingly, vide letter dated 13.02.2019, the Manager (Observations 2), Smt. The High Court of Bombay has submitted a proposal to the Government for installation of fire extinguishers/ fire safety measures in the old (1925 and 1867) court buildings of the District and Sessions Court, Akola. The concerned officers of the Maharashtra Fire Service have suggested the fire prevention measures mentioned in the proposal and the Director, Directorate of Maharashtra Fire Service has agreed to carry out the work of installing the fire fighting system in the court building as per the recommendations given by them. In accordance with the measures suggested by the local fire officials, the budget for 2018-19 has been prepared by the concerned Divisional Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Akola. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to grant administrative approval for installation of fire extinguishers/ fire safety measures in the old (1925 and 1867) court buildings of the District and Sessions Court, Akola.
