Government's decision:
Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trustee System, Shirdi as the Chairman of the Committee vide Notification No. Mr. Ashutosh Ashokrao Kale has been appointed as per Section 2 of the Act.
2. Shri Ashutosh Ashokrao Kale, Chairman, Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi is being given the status of Minister of State in this Government with effect from the date of this Government decision.
3.
