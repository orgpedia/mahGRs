Circular
The Joint Commissioner of Charities, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, on the establishment of the Commissioner of Charities, Group-A, shall fix the seniority number and date of seniority of the officers of the cadre. Temporary Service Excellence List dated 01.01.2026, Reference No. 1 was published by the Government Circular here. The present Service Excellence List is being finalised after meeting the objections received in pursuance of this provisional Service Excellence List. This final merit list is mentioned in Annexure-A to this circular.
A copy of this Final Service Excellence List is being brought to the notice of all officers of the Charity Joint Commissioner cadre at the establishment of the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
The circular is available on the Government of Maharashtra's www.maharashtra.gov.in website.
