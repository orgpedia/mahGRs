The Proposal- M. In Public Interest Litigation No. 216/2010 filed in the High Court of Bombay. As per the orders of the High Court of Bombay, fire audit of all court buildings in the state is being done and fire fighting/ fire prevention measures are being taken. Accordingly, vide letter dated 28.11.2016, the Deputy Manager, Shri. The High Court of Bombay has submitted a proposal to the Government for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the court buildings of District and Sessions Court Nagpur and Suyog Building Nagpur and Parsivani. The concerned officers of the Maharashtra Fire Service have suggested the fire prevention measures mentioned in the proposal and the Director, Directorate of Maharashtra Fire Service has agreed to carry out the work of installing the fire fighting system in the court building as per the recommendations given by them. In accordance with the measures suggested by the local fire officials, the budget for the year 2017-18 has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the government to grant administrative approval for installation of fire fighting system/ fire preventive measures in the court buildings at District and Sessions Court Nagpur and Suyog Bhawan Nagpur and Parsivani.

Government Decision The District and Sessions Court, Nagpur and the courts under its control have been directed to set up fire extinguishers/ fire prevention measures. In pursuance of the proposals received from the High Court of Bombay, administrative approval is being accorded for the construction of fire fighting system/ fire fighting measures in the District and Sessions Court, Nagpur and Court Buildings at Suyog Bhawan, Nagpur and Parsivani at a cost of Rs. 2,06,24, 648/- (Rupees Two Crore Six Lakhs Twenty Four Thousand Six Hundred and Forty Eight only) including 4% contingency cost and 12% provision for Goods and Services Tax.
