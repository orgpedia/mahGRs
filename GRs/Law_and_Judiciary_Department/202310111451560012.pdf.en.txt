The proposal: Under the e-Courts project of the Bombay High Court, the High Court Cases Scanning and Digitization Centre, which has been taken on lease, will be set up on the first floor of the CTO building, about 5000 sq. Proposal for grant of administrative approval for electrical and architectural works to be established in ft. of land, Reference No. According to the letter, the High Court administration has submitted to the government that the Bombay High Court Building Committee has approved the proposal. A total of Rs. 15,53,47, 711/- has been submitted along with the proposal by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Mumbai. These budgets have the conditional technical approval of the Chief Engineer (Power), Public Works Department, Mumbai and the technical approval of the Chief Engineer (Architecture), Public Works Department, Mumbai.
These budgets were submitted to the Public Works Department for examination. The budgetary allocation of Rs. 14,48,76, 841/- has been agreed. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
Government decision: Under the e-Courts project of the Bombay High Court, a High Court Case Scanning and Digitization Centre, about 5000 sq ft of the first floor of the leased CTO building, has been set up. As per the scrutiny of the Public Works Department regarding the electrical and architectural works to be carried out in connection with the installation of foot space, a total of Rs. Administrative Sanction is hereby accorded to the Budget Estimates amounting to Rs. 14,48,76, 841/- (Rupees Fourteen Crore Forty Eight Lakh Seventy Six Thousand Eight Hundred and Forty One only).
