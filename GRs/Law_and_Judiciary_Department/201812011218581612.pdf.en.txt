In accordance with the provisions of Section 21 (2) (2) of the Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust (Shirdi) Act, 2004, the Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi has issued a circular to the Shirdi Nagar Panchayat, Shirdi to carry out day-to-day sanitary and hygienic cleaning activities in the area falling under the jurisdiction of Shirdi Nagar Panchayat as approved by the Scrutiny Committee of the Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust. From 01.12.2017 onwards, Rs. 42,51,599/-for the next five years.
