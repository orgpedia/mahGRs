Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated 06.09.2022, 148 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court Yavatmal have been extended till 28.02.2023. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 148 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 1st March, 2023 to 31st August, 2023.
