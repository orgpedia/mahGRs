Introduction: A total of 14 Family Courts have been set up under the 14th Finance Commission. 5 posts have been created as per the Government decision here and the remaining 9 posts have been created as per the Government decision at Reference No.6 for Family Court. The temporary posts for all these 14 Family Courts are required to be extended for further period. As per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 1 regarding the temporary posts of Family Court, the Department of Finance, Govt. The provision has been omitted from the Government Decision dated 10.9.2001. Further, pursuant to the decision of the Government of Finance Department in Reference No. 10, 1.03.2021 to dt. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2021. Accordingly, the matter of continuation of the temporary posts for the said 14 District Courts was under consideration of the Government.
