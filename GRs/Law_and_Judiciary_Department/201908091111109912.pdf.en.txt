The Administrative Department has been empowered to grant administrative approval to repair works of the court building under the commission up to a limit of Rs 50 lakh.
As per the letter of reference No.1 of the Hon'ble High Court, the budget for the repair/ renovation of the Court Building at Chalisgaon under the District and Sessions Court, Jalgaon has been received. This is for the period 2018-2019. The budget in this proposal has been prepared by the Assistant Engineer, Public Works Department, Chalisgaon and has been attested by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Jalgaon and the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Jalgaon. The Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has examined the said budget and agreed to the administrative sanction. Accordingly, the matter of giving administrative approval to the budget of the work at Chalisgaon under the District and Sessions Court, Jalgaon was under the consideration of the Government.
