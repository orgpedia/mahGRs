The Commissioner of Charities, Deputy Commissioner of Charities on Establishment, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, fixes the seniority number and date of seniority of Group-A cadre officers. Temporary Service Excellence List dated 01.01.2024, Reference No. 1 was published by the Government Circular here. The objections received in pursuance of this provisional merit list as on date 29.02.2024 (M.No) are being processed and the present merit list is being finalised. This final merit list is mentioned in Annexure-A to this circular.
2. The Service Excellence List. The Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Bench filed the original application No. In 155/2024. It is being finalised subject to the final order of the Tribunal.
3. A copy of this Final Service Excellence List is being brought to the notice of all officers of the Charity Deputy Commissioner cadre at the establishment of the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
The Government Circular is available on the www.maharashtra.gov.in website of the Government of Maharashtra.
