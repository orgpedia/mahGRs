For the purpose of" Strengthening the Justice Sector" as recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, an amount of Rs. 1014 crore has been earmarked for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-16 to 2019-20. The component of upgradation of functional courts under the 14 Central Finance Commissions has been earmarked at a cost of Rs.228 crore for a period of 5 years. A High Level Steering Committee has been constituted on 9th March, 2016 for effective implementation of the recommendations of the Commission. At the meeting of the committee. The Action Plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle and under the Government Order dated 12th June, 2017, the Administrative Department has been empowered to grant administrative approval for the repair works of the Court Building under the 14th Central Finance Commission with the concurrence of the Finance Department to the extent of Rs. Accordingly, the proposal for the upgradation of the Court at Bhadravati under the control of District and Sessions Court, Chandrapur in the Schedule of 2018-19 has been placed at the above mentioned Reference No. 3 is mentioned here. The court has asked the Mumbai government. The budget has been prepared by the concerned Assistant Executive Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer as well as the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Chandrapur.
