३. उपरोक्त हार्डवेअर सामग्नीची खरेदी १८६ वी उच्चाधिकार समितीने दि.०९.०१.२०२६ रोजीच्या
बैठकीत खालीलप्रमाणे दिलेल्या मान्यतेनुसार करावी
The High-Power Committee approved the technical specifications of the proposed
hardware and accorded administrative approval for procurement of All-in-One computers,
printers, MFD printers, Digital Signature USB tokens and basic infrastructure for live streaming
for 14 new courtrooms at the Hon'ble Bombay High Court, Mumbai and its Benches at Nagpur,
Aurangabad and Kolhapur, at a total estimated cost of Rs. 5,53,81,382/- (Rupees Five Crore Fifty-
Three Lakh Eighty-One Thousand Three Hundred Eighty-Two only).
The purchase was approved to be made from the existing Rate Contract of the Hon. High Court.
