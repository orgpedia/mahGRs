The proposal: As per the proposal of the High Court, the Government has decided to establish a Civil Judge (Junior Level) Court in Bodwad, G. Jalgaon instead of the Gram Nyayalaya and to create a post for it. Narrow- 0816/625/ P.No. 101/ Ka-11, Dy. Approved on 05/01/2019. Accordingly, a symbolic supplementary demand for this Court was submitted to the Legislature through the Finance Department in the February, 2019 session. It's been approved. Accordingly, the proposal to provide funds for this court was under consideration of the government.
