To constitute a Committee to study whether the presentation made by DAKSH on" Challenges and Reforms in Tribunals" can be implemented in Tribunals in the State.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Law and Justice
Government Resolution No: Mumans 2018/ Q. No.3/ Q.7
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry of Health, Mumbai- 32
Date:- 21/06/2018
Introduction: DAKSH has worked to improve the functioning of tribunals in other states of the country. This work is also being done free of cost. The presentation made by DAKSH on the topic" Challenges and Reforms in Tribunals" was under consideration of the government to form a committee under the chairmanship of Joint Secretary, Law and Justice Department by including the managers of various tribunals in the state such as Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, School Tribunal, etc. and other such tribunals as members to study whether they can be implemented in the tribunals of the state.
Government Decision: A Committee is being constituted to study whether the presentation made by DAKSH on" Challenges and Reforms in Tribunals" can be implemented in the Tribunals of the State.
