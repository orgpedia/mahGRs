The Proposal:
For the purpose of Strengthening the Justice System recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, an amount of Rs. 1014 crore has been recommended for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Renovation of the Court functioning under the 14th Finance Commission for a period of 5 years for this component. 228 crore. Implementation of the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission
A High Level Committee has been constituted on 9/3/2016. At the meeting of the committee. The action plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle.
Also with the approval of the Department of Finance. 14th Finance Commission by Government Resolution dated 12/6/2017
The Administrative Department has been empowered to grant administrative approval to repair works of the court building under the commission up to a limit of Rs 50 lakh.
As per the letter of reference No. 1 of the Hon'ble High Court, the budget for the repair/ renovation of the court building at Karad under the District and Sessions Court, Satara has been received. This is for the period 2018-2019. The budget of the proposal has been prepared by the Deputy Divisional Engineer, Public Works Department, Karad and has been attested by the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Satara as well as the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Satara. The Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has examined the said budget and agreed to the administrative sanction. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the work budget at Karad under the District and Sessions Court, Satara was under consideration.
