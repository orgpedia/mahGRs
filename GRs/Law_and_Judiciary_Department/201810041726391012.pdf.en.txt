Government's decision
Subject to the Government decision dated 13.03.2018, 146 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Chandrapur have been extended till 30.09.2018.
In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 158 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019.
