Preamble In pursuance of the Government notification dated 06.11.2015, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Devala in Nashik district. In pursuance of the above mentioned Government Decision dated 13th February, 2012 mentioned in A.No.2, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. The District Court Nashik has requested to declare the Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya Devala (Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class) as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the Devala Gram Nyayalaya. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court.
Accordingly, the matter of declaring Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Devala, District Nashik as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Devala, Nashik was under consideration of the Government.
The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalay, Devala, District Nashik is being declared as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Gram Nyayalay established under the Gram Nyayalay Act, 2008 at Devala, District Nashik. The order will be limited to financial matters.
