Preamble- A request for creation of a notional post of driver for Gram Nyayalaya Vaibhavwadi, District Sindhudurg and sanction for taking services of these posts through external agencies was made by the Assistant Manager, (Inspections), Ma. Reference was made by the High Court of Bombay vide letter dated 02.04.2016. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of a fictitious post of driver for the Gram Nyayalaya Vaibhavwadi, District Sindhudurg and the government's approval to provide the services of this post on contract basis through an external agency. The government has taken the following decisions:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No. 2016/ P. No.62/ K.12
Government's decision
The government is giving approval to create a fictitious post of a driver for the Gram Nyayalay Vaibhavwadi in Sindhudurg district and to make the services of these posts available through external agencies on a contract basis. The expenditure for this purpose shall be met under the head Demand No. J. 1, 2014 Adjudication, (00) 105 Civil and Sessions Courts, (02) District and Sessions Judges, (02) (02) Establishment of Gram Nyayalayas under Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008, (2014 0477) Dattamat.
(2) In the case of filling up of these posts on contractual basis by external agencies, it shall be mandatory to comply with the guidelines issued in this regard from time to time.
The said Government Resolution is being issued in concurrence with the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 479/2020/ Expenditure-5 dated 27th July, 2020 and Informal Reference No. 167/2019/ Appuc dated 16th December, 2019.
