This administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
a) Before starting the work, the design, layout and detailed map should be started with the approval of the architect.
(b) While dealing with the provision made in a rough manner, the work should be taken up only after making a detailed budget.
(c) While working directly with the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No.177/ T. C. 1, d. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
Approval of all concerned local bodies/ authorities should be obtained before starting the work.
(c) Public Works Department No. BDG 2017/ S.No.60/ Building-2, D. Action to be taken as on 07.07.2017.
e) Tender notice for the said work should be published after obtaining the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the Consumer Department.
f) There will be no change in the budgetary work.
c) The scope of work should not be altered or new works should not be included on the basis of oral instructions given by the engineers of the Public Works Department.
If new works have to be included or the scope has to be changed, then the action should be taken to get the approval of these works in the prescribed time and manner by obtaining a written order in this regard, otherwise the responsibility will be fixed on the concerned officer.
