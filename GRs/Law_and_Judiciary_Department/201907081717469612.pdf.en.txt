Date: 8th of July 2019
Preamble: There is no Gazetted post in the office of the District Government Advocate in the District and Sessions Court of the State (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai).
The Public Prosecutor and the Public Prosecutor have been declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officers for the office. This system has been in place for a long time. Now, the Accountant General's Office, Mumbai, in its Treasury Inspection for 2015-16, has objected to the appointment of Drawing and Disbursing Officer to the rank of Gazetted Officer as the post of District Public Prosecutor and Public Prosecutor is not a Gazetted Officer as per Rule 15 (3) of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
Accordingly, a proposal is under consideration to create a new post of gazetted rank for all the District Government Advocate Offices to hand over the charge of the post of Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the District Government Advocate Office. Meanwhile, to assign the charge of the post of Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the District Government Advocate's Office (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) to the Superintendent of the District and Sessions Court attached to that office.
The matter of declaring him as an officer was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Accordingly, for the period from the date of the order till 31st December, 2019, the post of Superintendent of the District and Sessions Court attached to the office of the District Public Prosecutor as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the office of the District Public Prosecutor (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer as a special case. They are being authorized to deposit all the payments in this office in the treasury.
The order will be limited to financial matters. All the District Government Advocate Offices should take action to obtain Drawing and Disbursing Officer Number (DDO Code) from the Finance Department/ Accountant General's Office.
2. The decision of the Government shall be taken by the Finance Department. Reference No. 259/2019/ Koshas being issued pursuant to the agreement dated 04.07.2019.
