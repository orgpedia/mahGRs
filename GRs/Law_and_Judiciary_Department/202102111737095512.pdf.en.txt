Preamble As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, it is necessary to fix the posts in the government service for persons with disabilities. In this regard, the Central Government has published the list of posts for persons with Benchmark Disabilities notified on 04.01.2021 under the heading' List of Posts Identified suitable for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities notified on 04.01.2021' in the Policy/ Act/ Rules-Notifications of the Central Government on the website www.disabilityairs.gov.in. As per the said notification (on the lines of Central Government), the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions under the Government Order dated 02.02.2021 regarding the posts in Group A to Group D, which are in the establishment of the State Government, or the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the posts in the State Government Service, even if the designations of such posts are different, the posts in the State Government Service will be ensured for the disabled and the Administrative Department has to ensure the posts in their establishment at their level for the disabled. Accordingly, the matter of ensuring the post of peon in Group-D cadre in the Ministry of Law and Justice (itself), (including Nagpur and Aurangabad) Division was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi, vide notification dated 04.01.2021, has notified the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities for the purpose of determining the posts under the State Government. In the notification, as the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities specifies the category of persons with disabilities suitable for the post of" peon" in Group D cadre, the Department of Law and Justice (itself) (including Nagpur and Aurangabad) is ensuring the category of persons with disabilities in Group D cadre as indicated in the Disability Type Statement-A.
