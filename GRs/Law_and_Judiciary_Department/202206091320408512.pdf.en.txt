Preamble In pursuance of the Government notification dated 30th September, 2015, dated 01.04.2015, Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Higanghat (Alipur village) in Wardha district. Further, in pursuance of the aforesaid Government Decision dated 13th February, 2012, mentioned at Annexure-II, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. District Court Wardha has requested to declare the Magistrate for Hinganghat (Alipur Village) Village Court, Village Court Hinganghat (Alipur Village) (Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Hinganghat) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Principal District and Sessions Judge Wardha as Controlling Officer of the scheme. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Hinganghat (Village Alipur), District Wardha as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Hinganghat (Village Alipur), Wardha and Principal District and Sessions Judge Wardha as Controlling Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Hinganghat (Village Alipur), Wardha.

The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya, Hinganghat (Village Alipur), District Wardha is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Gram Nyayalaya established under the Gram Nyayalaya Act, 2008 at Hinganghat (Village Alipur), District Wardha. Also, Principal District and Sessions Judge Wardha is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" for these Gram Nyayalayas. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 read with Finance Division No. 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated 17.04.2015.
