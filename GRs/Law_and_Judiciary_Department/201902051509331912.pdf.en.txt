Introduction- The electricity supply in the new building of the District and Sessions Court Beed is interrupted many times. Since the functioning of the Court is being hampered, therefore, the request for grant of administrative sanction for installation of a Noiseless Generator of suitable capacity to supply power to the building for smooth functioning of the Court has been made to the Manager vide letter No. The High Court of Bombay. The detailed budget of the work has been prepared by the Deputy Engineer, Public Works Department Beed on the schedule for the year 2015-16 and the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department Aurangabad as well as the Principal District and Sessions Judge Beed have testified. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to install a noise-free generator for the new building of the District and Sessions Court in Beed was under the consideration of the government.
Administrative Sanction is being accorded for installation of Noiseless Generator in the new building of District and Sessions Court, Beed at a cost of Rs. 57,87,692/- (Rupees Fifty Seven Lakhs Eighty Seven Thousand Six Hundred and Ninety Two only). The details are as follows:
