Preamble- The number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be at least 500, along with the infrastructure, e.g. The court buildings and residences of the judges should be made available. Since it is evident that all the criteria required as per the policy of the High Court are being fulfilled for setting up of Civil Court (Senior Level) at Akot, District Akola, the Hon'ble High Court has decided to set up a Civil Court at Akola, District Akola. The Establishment of Courts Committee constituted at the level of High Court of Bombay has approved the establishment of senior level Civil Courts for Akot and Telhara Taluks in Akola District. Earlier, under the Government decision dated 04.03.2011, referred to in Reference No.1, the matter of revival of the post of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Akot, District Akola at Akot, District Akola and approval for creation of the post required for this Court was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following Government Decision is being issued: Government Decision Administrative approval is being given for the establishment of Civil Judge Senior Level Court for Akot and Telhara Taluks in Akot, District Akola and creation of the following posts for this Court from the date of commencement of the Court:
