Preamble: Under the National Mission for Safety of Women, the Central Government has made a provision for setting up of Special Fast Track Courts for speedy disposal of pending cases of rape and POCSO Act involving women and children. Accordingly, reference No. 1 and 2 here. According to the proposals received under the letters of the High Court, Reference No. 3 Department of Law& Justice, Government of India has approved establishment of 138 Fast Track Special Courts (FTSCs) in the State for a period of one year and creation of a total of 1104 temporary posts of 8 temporary posts for each court. Out of these 138 courts, 30 courts will function as Exclusive/ Designated POCSO Act Courts.
Reference No. 3 The list of Special Fast Track Courts to be set up in the State is appended in Annexure-A of the Government Resolution. Accordingly, approval has been given to set up 1 Fast Track Court each at Buldhana, Nandurbar and Raigad-Alibag in Buldhana, Nandurbar and Raigad districts respectively. However, considering the number of pending cases. The High Court vide Reference No. As per the letter, it has been proposed to set up one fast track court each in Mehkar, Shahada and Panvel taluks instead of Buldhana, Nandurbar and Raigad-Alibag district headquarters.
