The Proposal:
The Supreme Court Computer Committee has developed a Mobile Application for Electronic Services and Processes, National Service& Tracking& Electronic Process (NSTEPS). The National Service& Tracking& Electronic Process (NSTEPS) Mobile Application is useful for serving court orders through bailiffs working in courts, which require internet connectivity. Central Project Coordinator The High Court, Mumbai had submitted a proposal to the government under the reference letter for recurring expenditure on 4G internet connectivity for 2162 bailiffs working in the subordinate courts in the state on a monthly basis 195. Accordingly, the government was considering approving the recurring expenditure for providing 4G internet connectivity to bailiffs working in the courts of the state.
Government's decision:
2162 bailiffs working in the High Court of Bombay and the subordinate courts of the state will have to pay Rs. 195 (+ 18% GST) and Rs.
4,97,476/- (Rs. Under this government decision, administrative approval is being given to the monthly expenditure of four lakh ninety nine thousand four hundred and seventy six only).
