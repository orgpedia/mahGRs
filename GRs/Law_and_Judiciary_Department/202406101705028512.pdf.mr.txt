OFFICE ORDER
The Ministry of Law and Justice, Government of India, New Delhi requested the
Government of Maharashtra to organise a conference on Criminal Laws viz. The Bhartiya Nyay
Sanhita, 2023, The Bhartiya Nagrik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023 and The Bhartiya Saksh Adhiniyam,
2023, on 29-30 June 2024, at Mumbai. It has requested the Government of Maharashtra to
facilitate holding conference. I, the undersigned constitute the co-ordination committee for
effective co-ordination among different Government departments, agencies and district
administration, Mumbai District. The officers are nominated as chairperson and members as
under
