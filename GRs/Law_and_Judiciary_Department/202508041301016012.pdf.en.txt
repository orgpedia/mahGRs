Introduction: The Registrar Partnership Institute, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and its Regional Offices at Pune, Nagpur and Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar under its aegis have revised structure of posts with reference no. 2 has been approved by the Government here. Accordingly, at present, there are 19 posts of Clerk-Typographer in Group-C cadre in the office of Registrar Partnership Institute, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, 4 posts are sanctioned in the office of Deputy Registrar Partnership Institute, Nagpur. Also, 4 posts are sanctioned in the cadre of Clerk-Typographer, Group-C in the office of Deputy Registrar, Partnership Institute, Pune. The scope and pendency of work in the Pune office is more and considering the urgency of the administrator of the office, the proposal for temporary transfer of 4 posts in the total Clerk-Typician cadre, viz., Clerk-Typician cadre, to the Office of the Registrar Partnership Institute, Mumbai Office and Deputy Registrar Partnership Institute, Nagpur, in the office of the Deputy Registrar Partnership Institute, Pune, vide Reference No. 3 is presented under the letter here. The government was considering taking action accordingly.
Government Decision: It is hereby approved to temporarily transfer 4 posts of Clerk-Typographer cadre, namely Clerk-Typographer in the Office of Registrar Partnership Institute, Mumbai B Deputy Registrar, Partnership Institute, Nagpur, Group-C (Pay Scale: -S-6: 19900-63200) 2 posts each out of sanctioned posts to the Office of Deputy Registrar, Partnership Institute, Pune.
