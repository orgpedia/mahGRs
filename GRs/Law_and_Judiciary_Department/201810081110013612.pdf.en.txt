Pursuant to the Government decision dated 13.03.2018, 90 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Akola have been extended till 30.09.2018.
As per the Government decision dated 25.05.2018, mentioned in Reference No.4, one post of Superintendent Cadre has been revived. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 91 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019.
