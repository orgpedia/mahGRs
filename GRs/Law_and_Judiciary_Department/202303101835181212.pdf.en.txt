Government's decision
Subject to the decision of the Government dated 08.09.2022, 104 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Beed have been extended till 28.02.2023. District and Additional Sessions Judge, Kej, District Beed has been accorded administrative approval vide Government Order dated 02.02.2023. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 125 temporary posts mentioned in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
