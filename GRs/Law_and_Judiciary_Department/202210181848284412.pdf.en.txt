A copy of the Office Memorandum No. 1/3 (1)/ 2008-E-II (B) dated 03.10.2022 of the Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance, Government of India, forwarded to the Judicial Officers of the Subordinate Courts in the State of Maharashtra for information and appropriate action.
According to the above-mentioned Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Finance of the Central Government. The increase in Dearness Allowance by 4% (from 3 4% to 38%) with effect from 01.07.2022 and other provisions mentioned in the Memorandum shall continue to apply to Judicial Officers of Secondary Courts as well as retired Judicial Officers in the State of Maharashtra.
Accordingly, now the Judicial Officers of the Secondary Court of Maharashtra State as well as retired Judicial Officers of the State of Maharashtra will be given the status of Judicial Officers. Dearness Allowance at the rate of 38% will be admissible with effect from 01.07.2022. The increase in dearness allowance should be paid in cash.
