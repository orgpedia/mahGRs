1. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit Account.
3. Receipt into the PD account will be the depositing L.A.R. amounts and other amounts in civil proceeding by the concerned parties as per the court order. Expenditure out of the Personal Deposit Account will be as per orders of the Court.
4. No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions shall be booked in this PD.
5. On a Monthly basis, the balance in this Personal Deposit Account shall be reconciled with the Treasury officer and yearly reconciliation with AG office for all types of Personal Deposits.
6. At the end of each financial year, the balance in the PD may be forwarded by the Treasury office to AG for confirmation.
7. The Finance Department GR dated 5.09.2023 regarding operation of the PD/PLA should be followed.
8. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this Personal Deposit Account.
