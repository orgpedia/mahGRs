The government has decided to translate the High Court verdict into Marathi. According to the AI Assistant Legal Translation Committee of the Bombay High Court. Pursuant to the decision taken in the meeting dated 14/03/2023, the High Court Administration has requested the Government through the reference letters to approve the availability of the required manpower for examining the copies translated into Marathi with the help of judicial decision software by the High Court through external agencies from retired judicial officers, advocates recommended by Bar Council of Maharashtra and Goa and experts from Universities/ Colleges/ Law Colleges in the State. Accordingly, a proposal was submitted to the Sub-Committee for approval to create notional posts of Junior Translator and Interpreter on the establishment of the High Court and make its services available through external agencies, which has been approved by the Committee. Accordingly, the proposal has been approved by the government. Following this, the government decided:
Government decision Translation of Judgments of Supreme Court and Bombay High Court in Marathi with the help of Software created 300 fictitious posts of Junior Translator and Interpreter cadre on the establishment of Appellate Division for the work of examination by the Service External of the said post (retired Judicial Officers, Advocates recommended by Bar Council of Maharashtra and Goa and experts from Universities/ Colleges/ Law Colleges in the State).
