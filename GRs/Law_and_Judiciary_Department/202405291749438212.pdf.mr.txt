१. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
२. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal
Deposit Account.
३. Receipt into the PD account will be the Deposit, Maintenance
Amount, Rent, Decreetal Amount(Civil). Expenditure out of the
Personal Deposit Account will be as per orders of the Courts.
४. No Revenue Receipt/Revenue Expenditure transactions shall be
booked in this PD.
५. On a monthly basis, the balances in this Personal Deposit
Account shall be reconciled with the Treasury Offices and yearly
reconciliation with A G office for all types of Personal Deposits.
६. At the end of each financial year, the balance in the PD may be
forwarded by the Treasury office to AG for confirmation.
७. The Finance Department GR dated ०५.०९.२०२३ regarding
operation of the PD/PLA should be followed.
८. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury will be applicable to this
Personal Deposit Account
९. This office will issue an authority for opening of the Personal
Deposit Account on receipt of Government Orders.
