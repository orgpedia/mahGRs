The administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions: (i) The design, layout and detailed design of the building shall be approved by the Architect before commencement of construction.
2) Since the said budget is a rough sample budget, while technically approving the detailed budget for the said work, the Government of India has decided to provide a detailed budget for the said work. The provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Principal Chief District and Sessions Judge, Ahmednagar.
3) The tender notice for the work should be published after receiving the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the consumer department.
(4) In the process of tendering the work, one tender should be issued for all the works mentioned together without separate tenders for each work.
5) Approval of all concerned local bodies/ authorities should be obtained before starting the work.
6) While doing the actual work, action should be taken according to the guidelines mentioned in the Department of Environment, Government Decision, No. ENV-2013/ Q. No. 177/ T.K.1, dated January 10, 2014.
7) Government Decision of the Public Works Department, No. Action to be taken under BDG 2017/ PR No. 60/ Building-2, dated 7th July, 2017.
8) Special care should be taken to ensure that no revised administrative approval is required for this work.
9) Make sure that there is no change in the budget work.
10) The scope of work should not be changed or new works should not be included on the basis of oral instructions given by the engineers of the Public Works Department.
