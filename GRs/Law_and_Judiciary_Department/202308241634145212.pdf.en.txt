As per the Government Notification No. 2 to 5. The said Court has become functional with effect from 22.07.2023. In reference No.6. In pursuance of the request made by the Manager, High Court, Bombay vide letter dated 01.08.2023, the Government was considering to declare District Judge-I and Additional Sessions Judge, Aheri, G. Gadchiroli as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
The District and Additional Sessions Judge-I and Additional Sessions Judge, Aheri, G. Gadchiroli have been appointed as the Office Head and Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the District and Additional Sessions Court, Gadchiroli.
