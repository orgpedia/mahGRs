Government Resolution District and Sessions Court Latur appointed Manager, Observations-1, Ma. As per the proposal sent by the High Court of Bombay, the District and Sessions Court Latur and the court buildings under its control at Nilanga, Udgir, Chakur, Renapur, Ausa, Ahmedpur, Deoni and Jalakote have been provided with firefighting equipment. The proposals received from the High Court of Bombay have been approved under the Government Order dated 04.07.2017. Of these, construction of a new building has been started in Ausa, District Latur. A proposal for a new building at Deoni, District Latur is under consideration. Also, the Gram Nyayalay at Jalakot is functional and it meets only once every week. Therefore, it is not necessary to take fire preventive measures in these three buildings, the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Latur has informed in a letter dated July 23, 2018. The administrative approval granted for installation of fire fighting/ fire safety measures in court buildings at 8 Deoni and No. 9 Jalakote is being cancelled.
In the Government Resolution dated 04.07.2017, the administrative approval is being accorded for works costing Rs. 1,44,82, 190/- (Rupees One Crore Forty Four Lakh Eighty Two Thousand One Hundred and Ninety only) by making a provision in line 7 of paragraph 1. Instead, administrative approval is being granted for works costing Rs. 1,06,88, 355/- (Rupees One Crore Six Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Three Hundred and Fifty Five only). This should be read.
