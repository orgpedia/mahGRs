The Proposal:
The Court of Civil Judge Senior Level has been functioning at Vaijapur, District Aurangabad with effect from 16.10.2010 vide notification dated 07.10.2010 under reference No. As per the Government Order dated 08.11.2010, Civil Judge Senior Level, Vaijapur has been declared as" Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer." The Principal District and Sessions Judge, Aurangabad vide letter dated 12.02.2021 has requested for approval to open a Personal Register Account for the Court of Civil Court Senior Level, Vaijapur. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of a Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level, Vaijapur.

Government's decision:
Maharashtra Treasury Rules in the name of Civil Judge Senior Level, Vaijapur, District Aurangabad,
Civil Court Senior Level, Vaijapur, District Aurangabad vide Rule No. 512 of 1968
The following conditions apply for opening a civil personal loan account to deposit the amounts due to them:
Approval is granted subject to:
1) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3) The amount ordered by the Court to the concerned / opposite parties to be deposited as Civil Court Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA and payment due may be made as per the order of the Court.
4) No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions will appear in this PLA.
5) Either on quarterly or monthly basis, balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the Court.
6) All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this PLA.
7) O/o the A.G. (A&E) II, Nagpur will issue an authority to open PLA on receipt of Govt. orders.
