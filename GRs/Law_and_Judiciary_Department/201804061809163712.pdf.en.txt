Preamble: For the purpose of Strengthening the Justice System recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, an amount of Rs. 1014 crore has been recommended for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Renovation of the Court functioning under the 14th Finance Commission for a period of 5 years for this component. 228 crore. Implementation of the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission. A High Level Monitoring Committee has been constituted on 9/3/2016. At the meeting of the committee. The action plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle.
Also with the approval of the Department of Finance. As per the Government decision dated 12/6/2017, the administrative departments have been empowered to grant administrative approval for repair works of court buildings under the 14th Finance Commission to the extent of Rs.
In this regard, the proposal on the 2016-2017 tariff list for the repair/ renovation of the District Court, Solapur and the court building at Malshiras under its jurisdiction has been placed at the above mentioned Reference No. 3 mentioned here. The Bombay High Court has given the government. The estimates have been prepared by the concerned Assistant Engineer, Public Works Department, Solapur and have been attested by the concerned Executive Engineer and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Solapur. The Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has examined the said budget and agreed to the administrative sanction.
Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the following work budget was under consideration.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission, under the Scheme for Renovation of a Working Court, administrative approval is being accorded to the budget of Rs. 40,89,000/- (Rupees Forty Lacs Ninety Nine Thousand only) for the repair/ renovation of the Court Building at Malshiras under the District Court, Solapur.
