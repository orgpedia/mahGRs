To apprise the concerned Minister before filing an affidavit in a court case which is a financial burden on the State Government and is likely to affect the policy of the Government

circular
There are many policies of the government. It is being challenged in court. Some court cases can put a financial burden on the government's treasury.
However, all the departments are advised that before filing of affidavit in a court of law matter which is a financial burden on the State Government and is likely to affect the policy of the Government, the Minister concerned should be apprised of the proposed affidavit and the draft affidavit should be finalised with his approval.
