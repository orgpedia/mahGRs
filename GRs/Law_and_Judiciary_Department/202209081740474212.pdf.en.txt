Preamble: In order to expeditiously dispose of cases of atrocities against women in the State, Reference No. 14 special courts have been set up as per the decision of the government. A total of 99 temporary posts in these 14 Special Courts have been notified vide Reference No. The Government's Decision 2. This has been extended up to 31.08.2022. Proposals for further extension of these 99 temporary posts have been received by the Government. Reference No. 4 decision of the Finance Department. 01.09.2022 to dt. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 28.02.2023. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of a total of 99 temporary posts in 14 special courts.
A total of 99 temporary posts have been sanctioned for the establishment of special courts in 14 districts of Mumbai, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Dhule, Jalna, Latur, Nanded, Nashik, Osmanabad, Parbhani, Sangli, Satara, Solapur and Wardha to deal with cases of atrocities against women. 01.09.2022 to dt. The period is being extended up to 28.02.2023.
