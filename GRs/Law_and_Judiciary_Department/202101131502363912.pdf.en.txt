Government's decision:
Personal Pension Account in the name of the Judge, Family Court, Yavatmal for the amount deposited in the Family Court, Yavatmal as per Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Vol.
Approval is subject to the following conditions:
I. The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
II. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
III. The amount ordered by the Court to be deposited as Civil Court Deposits should only be deposited in this P.L.A. and payment may be made as per the order of the Court.
IV. No Revenue Receipt/Revenue Expenditure transactions shall be booked in this P.L.A.
V. The balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the Administrator either on monthly or on quarterly basis.
VI. All provisions under the Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this P.L.A.
VII. This office will issue an authority to open PLA on receipt of Govt. Orders.
The transactions will be classified under the following major Head of Account.
8443 - Civil Deposit 00 - 104 - Civil Court Deposits 01 - Personal Ledger Account of Judge, Family Court, Yavatmal.
2. This Government Decision is issued in accordance with the Informal Reference No. 248/20/ CoPR-5 of the Department of Finance.
