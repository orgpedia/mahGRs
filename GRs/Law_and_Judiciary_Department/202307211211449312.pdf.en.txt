Preamble: Administrative approval has been accorded for the purchase of 372 laptops for judicial officers under the Government Resolution No. 1. Accordingly, the Central Project Coordinator, e-Courts Project, Appellate Branch, High Court, Mumbai, vide Reference No. A proposal was submitted to the government to purchase anti-virus licenses for 372 laptops. About the proposal
The views of the Directorate of Technology have been taken and the same have been approved by the Project Implementation Committee of the Department in its meeting held on 20.07.2023. Accordingly, the proposal to purchase anti-virus licenses for 372 laptops made available to judicial officers was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
For the purchase of anti-virus licenses for 372 laptops made available to judicial officers, Rs. A proposal for an amount of Rs. 10,06,535/- (Rupees Ten Lakh Six Thousand Five Hundred Thirty Five only) is being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution.
