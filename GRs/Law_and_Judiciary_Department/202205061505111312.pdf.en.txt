The Proposal:
Proposal for construction of a residence for Civil Judge (C.S.) at Akole, District Ahmednagar. The High Court, Mumbai has submitted to the Government under the above reference letter. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government's decision:
For construction of a residence for Civil Judge (C.S.) of Akole, District Ahmednagar, Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded by this Government Resolution for the works estimated to cost Rs.62,46, 533.00 (Rupees Sixty Two Lakhs Forty Seven Thousand Five Hundred and Thirty Three only).

This administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
1) Before starting the work, the sample map, layout map and detailed map should be started only after getting approval from the architect.
2) Since the said budget is a rough sample budget, while technically approving the detailed budgets of these works, the The provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Ahmednagar.
3) The Department of Environment, Government Decision No. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in ENV-2013/ Q.No. 177/ T.K.1, dated 10th January, 2014.
4) Approval of all concerned local bodies/ authorities should be obtained before starting the work.
5) Government Decision No. Action to be taken under BDG 2017/ PR No. 60/ Building-2, dated 7th July, 2017.
6) The tender notice for the work should be published after receiving the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the consumer department.
(7) At the time of tendering of the said work, one tender shall be issued for all the works mentioned together without separate tenders for each work.
8) Ensure that there is no change in the budget.
9) The scope of work should not be changed or new works should not be included on the basis of oral instructions given by the engineers of the Public Works Department.
10) If new works are to be included or the scope is to be changed, then action should be taken to get approval for these works in the prescribed time and manner by obtaining a written order in this regard. Otherwise, the responsibility will be fixed on the concerned officials.
