Introduction - Indapur, District Pune at Reference No. 1 vide Notification No. Since 25.01.2025, the civil court, the senior-level court, has become operational. The Civil Judge, Senior Level, Indapur, District Pune has been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer by the Government Decision at Reference H.2. Reference No. Letters 3 and 4 request permission to open a separate personal deposit account for civil claims amounts for the Civil Court, Senior Level, Indapur, District Pune. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to approve opening of a separate Personal Deposit Account for civil claims amounts for the Civil Court, Senior Level, Indapur, District Pune.
Government Decision: Names of Civil Judge, Senior Level, Indapur for Civil Court, Senior Level, Indapur, District Pune, as per the court order vide Rule 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, opening of Personal Deposit Account for deposits and other dues made by the parties in a civil case is being permitted subject to the following conditions.
