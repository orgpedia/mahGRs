Government Decision: As per the budget finalised by the Public Works Department for the Family Court, Dhule, Rs. 5,14,349/- (Rupees Five Lakhs, Fourteen Thousand, Three Hundred and Nineteen Fifty Five only) and Rs. 2,45,811/- (Rupees Two Lakhs, Forty Five Thousand, Eight Hundred and Eleven only) is being granted administrative approval subject to the following conditions.
i. Furniture required for Family Court, Dhule should be of prescribed standard size. They shall be determined on the basis of the quantity of furniture prescribed in Annexure 26 of the Maharashtra Public Works Department rule book and taking into account the number of physical staff.
The usual procedures and other general directions should be followed in this regard.
