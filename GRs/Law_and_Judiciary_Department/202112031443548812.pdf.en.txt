Preamble- The Court of the District and Additional Sessions Judge at Ambad, District Jalna has become functional with effect from 21.02.2021 vide notification dated 17.02.2021 in the Annexure (1). As per the Government Order dated 15.04.2021, District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Ambad have been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Principal District and Sessions Judge, Jalna vide letter dated 21.05.2021 has requested for approval for opening of Personal Register Account for the Court of District and Additional Sessions Judge, Ambad. Accordingly, the matter of approval to open a personal account for the court of District and Additional Sessions Judge Ambad was under the consideration of the government.
The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to audit in accordance with Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Clause 1 of the said Rules, for depositing the amounts due to the District and Additional Sessions Court, Ambad, District Jalna, subject to the following conditions: 1.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3) The amount ordered by the Court to the concerned / opposite parties to be deposited as Civil Court Deposits and Criminal Court Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA and payment due may be made as per the order of the court.
