The Proposal- M. The High Court administration had submitted a proposal to the government through reference letters to take the services of 50 employees for doing e-filing and related technical works in the Bombay High Court and its Nagpur and Aurangabad benches through external agencies and approve the estimated expenditure for the same. Accordingly, the proposal was submitted to the Sub-Committee as well as the High-Level Secretary Committee for approval. The proposal was approved by the state government in the meeting. Following this, the government decided:
Government decision The services of 50 staff (Main Branch-15, Appellate Branch-15 and Nagpur and Aurangabad Bench 10 each) for the purpose of e-filing and related technical works in the High Court of Bombay and its Benches at Nagpur and Aurangabad will be made available through external agencies for a period of 11 months. The estimated cost of Rs. 1,86,38, 400/- is being sanctioned by the Government.
2. Subject to the decisions of the Government in respect of taking the services of the employees through external agencies in this behalf, the Finance Department, d. The procedure prescribed under 27/04/2022 should be followed. This will save 20 to 30 per cent of the government's revenue.
