According to the above-mentioned Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Finance of the Central Government. The increase in Dearness Allowance from 25% (164% to 189%) with effect from 01.07.2021 and other provisions mentioned in the memorandum shall continue to apply to judicial officers of subordinate courts as well as retired judicial officers in the State of Maharashtra.
Accordingly, now the Judicial Officers of the Secondary Court of Maharashtra State as well as retired Judicial Officers of the State of Maharashtra will be given the status of Judicial Officers. Dearness Allowance at the rate of 189% will be admissible from 01.07.2021. The amount of increase in Dearness Allowance should be paid in cash. 01.01.2020 to Rs.
