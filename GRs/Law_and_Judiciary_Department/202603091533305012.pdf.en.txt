The Government Judgment, Department of Law & Justice, No. Sankarna - 2009/323 Q. 41/09 Ka-22, dated 30.22.2008 has created 15 posts of Secretaries on the establishment of Maharashtra State Legal Services Authority.
Government Judgments, Law & Justice Department, No. Under LAB 1313/214 Q.69 / Ka-22, dated 21.11.2017, 19 posts of Secretaries have been created on the establishment of Maharashtra State Legal Services Authority.

Thus, a total of 37 posts of Secretaries have been created on the establishment of Maharashtra State Legal Services Authority.
