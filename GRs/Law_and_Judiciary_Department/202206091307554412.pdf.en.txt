Preamble In pursuance of the Government notification dated 29th September, 2009, dated 01.04.2016, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Jawhar in Thane district. In pursuance of the above mentioned Government Decision dated 13th February, 2012 mentioned in A.No.2, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. The District Court Thane has requested to declare the Magistrate, Gram Nyayalay Jawhar (Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for Jawhar Gram Nyayalay. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court.
Accordingly, the matter of declaring Magistrate Gram Nyayalay Jawhar, District Thane as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalay Scheme for Gram Nyayalay Jawhar, Thane was under consideration of the Government.
""" For the Gram Nyayalaya established under the Gram Nyayalaya Act, 2008 at Jawhar, District Thane, the Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya Jawhar, District Thane is being declared as"""" Head of Office"""" and"""" Drawing and Disbursing Officer"""" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.""" The order will be limited to financial matters.
