Preamble The Court of the District Judge and Additional Sessions Judge at Malkapur, District Buldhana, Buldhana has become functional with effect from 16.08.2018 vide notification dated 10.08.2018, as mentioned in Annexure (1). As per the Government Order dated 26.10.2018, District and Additional Sessions Judge, Malkapur has been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. On 30.08.2018, Principal District and Sessions Judge, Buldhana has requested for approval to open a Personal Accounts Account for the Court of District and Additional Sessions Judge, Malkapur. Accordingly, the matter of approval to open a Personal Accounts Account for the Court of District and Additional Sessions Judge Malkapur was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION As per Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Clause 1 in the name of the District and Additional Sessions Judge, Malkapur, District Buldhana, the opening of a Civil and Criminal Personal Accounts Account for depositing the amount in the District and Additional Sessions Court, Malkapur, District Hinganghat is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3) The amount ordered by the Court to be deposited as Civil Court Deposit / Criminal Court Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA.
