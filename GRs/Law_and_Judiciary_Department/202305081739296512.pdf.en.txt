The Civil Judge (Senior Level), Belapur, is being declared as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the Civil Court (Senior Level) at Belapur, Navi Mumbai in Thane district. This Government has decided to amend the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, dated 17/4/2015, in Part-I, Sub-Division I, No.3, Rule No. 2 issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments and under Rule 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
