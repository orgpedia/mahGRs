Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated 14th September, 2020, 99 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court Gadchiroli have been extended till 28th February, 2021. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 99 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
