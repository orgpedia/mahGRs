the prelude
In Public Interest Litigation No. 216/2010 filed in the Hon'ble High Court of Bombay. As per the orders of the High Court of Bombay, fire audit of all court buildings in the state is being done and fire fighting/ fire prevention measures are being taken. Accordingly, vide letter dated 20.06.2016, the Deputy Manager, Shri. The Bombay High Court has submitted a proposal to the government for setting up of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the District and Sessions Court Chandrapur and the buildings under its control, as well as in the court buildings at Warora, Bhadravati, Chimur, Rajura, Pombhurna, Ballarpur, Gondpimpri, Mool, Savli, Sindewahi, Brahmapuri and Nagbhid. The concerned officers of the Maharashtra Fire Service have suggested the fire prevention measures mentioned in the proposal and the Director, Directorate of Maharashtra Fire Service has agreed to carry out the work of installing the fire fighting system in the court building as per the recommendations given by them. In accordance with the measures suggested by the local fire officials, the budget for 2017-18 has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer. Accordingly, the District and Sessions Court at Chandrapur and the buildings under its control at Warora, Bhadravati, Chimur, Rajura, Pombhurna, Ballarpur, Gondpimpri, Mool, Savli, Sindewahi, Brahmapuri and Nagbhid
