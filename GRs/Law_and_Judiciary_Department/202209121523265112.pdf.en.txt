Pursuant to the Government decision dated 07.03.2022, 95 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Osmanabad have been extended till 31.08.2022. Department of Law and Justice dated 26 th July, 2022 has approved the establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court and Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Kalamb, District Osmanabad and creation of post for this court. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 126 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
