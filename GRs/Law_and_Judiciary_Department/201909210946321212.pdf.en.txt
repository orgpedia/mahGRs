Gram Nyayalayas have been established in the State under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008. In order to ensure smooth functioning of these Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 105 temporary posts in 21 Gram Nyayalayas have been extended for the period from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The Gram Nyayalaya Deoli under the control of District and Sessions Court Wardha has become functional from 10th August, 2019. Accordingly, for the smooth functioning of 22 Gram Nyayalayas, the following 22 Gram Nyayalayas have been established:
