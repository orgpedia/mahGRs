Government's stand:
Department of Law and Justice, Govt. CHA 2017/ Q. No.133/ K. 1, D. As on November 25, 2019, the Deputy Commissioner of Charitable Endowments, Jalgaon has been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The first paragraph of this judgment is as follows:
The Deputy Commissioner of Charitable Endowments, Jalgaon is hereby declared as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the Office of the Registrar of Public Trusts, Jalgaon under Rule No. 15 (3)& 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. The order will be limited to financial matters. "
Instead, read as follows:
" For the office of the Public Trust Register Office, Jalgaon, under Rule No. 15 (3)& 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the Deputy Commissioner of Charitable Endowments, Jalgaon is hereby declared as the"" Head of Office"" and"" Drawing and Disbursing Officer"" in place of the Assistant Commissioner of Charitable Endowments, Jalgaon." The order will be limited to financial matters. "
