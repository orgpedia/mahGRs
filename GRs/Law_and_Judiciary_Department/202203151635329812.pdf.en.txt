The administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions: (a) Construction shall commence only after obtaining approval from the architect for the design, layout and detailed design before commencing the construction.
(b) Since the said budget is a rough model budget, the provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Principal District and Sessions Judge while technically approving the detailed budgets of these works.
(c) Tender notice for the said work should be published after obtaining the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the Consumer Department.
d) When tendering the work, one single tender should be issued for all the mentioned works together without separately tendering each work.
Before starting the work, the approval of all the concerned local bodies/ authorities should be obtained.
f) In the actual working of the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q. No.17/ T. Action shall be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in the Notice dated 10th January, 2014.
