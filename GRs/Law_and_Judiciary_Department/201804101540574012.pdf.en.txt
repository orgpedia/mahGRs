Government's decision
In pursuance of the above notification, Gram Nyayalayas Poladpur, District Raigad, Alibag have become functional with effect from 17.01.2016. The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya Poladpur, District Raigad, Alibaug is being declared as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the purpose of disbursement of salaries and allowances of judicial officers and staff of the said Court and for payment of other office and court expenses. The order will be limited to financial matters.
The said Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2, Schedule 3 to Sub-section 1, Part I of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, dated 17th April, 2015.
