Preamble: General Administration Department, Reference No. As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, a consolidated procedure has been prescribed for premature retirement of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity by reviewing their service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier.
As the Government is required to constitute a Committee to advise the Head of the Department on the subject of review as per Schedule 3 (a) of Annexure-A of the said Government Decision, the Office of the Registrar Partnership Societies vide Reference No. 2 The letter here requests the government to set up a review committee. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to constitute a Committee to review the services of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees in the Office of Registrar Partnership Institutions beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after 30 years of qualifying service.
Government Decision: A Review Committee is being constituted to review the services of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees of the Office of the Registrar Partnership Institutions beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after 30 years of qualifying service.
