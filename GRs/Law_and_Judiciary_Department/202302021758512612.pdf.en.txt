Preamble- The number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be at least 500, along with the infrastructure e.g. The availability of court buildings, etc. All the necessary criteria as per the policy of the High Court appear to be fulfilled. The High Court of Bombay recommended the establishment of a District and Additional Sessions Court at Kej, District Beed and creation of a post for this court and the office of Public Prosecutor Kej. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to establish a District and Additional Sessions Court at Kej, District Beed and to create a post for this Court as well as for the office of Public Prosecutor Kej. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The state government has decided to set up a district and additional sessions court in Kej, Beed district. Also, the Government is approving the creation of posts and hiring of services by external agencies for this Court and the Office of the Public Prosecutor, as indicated below:
