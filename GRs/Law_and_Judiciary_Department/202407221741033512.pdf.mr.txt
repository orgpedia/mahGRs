3. Receipt into the PD account shall be the Amount of Rent, Recovery, Cost, Witness
Bhatta and other amount deposited by the respondent for the petitioner as per the Court Orders. Expenditure out of the Personal Deposit Account will be as per orders of the Court.

with the Treasury Office and yearly reconciliation with A G Office for all types of
Personal Deposits.

7. The Finance Department GR dated 5/9/2023 regarding operation of the PD/PLA should
be followed.
8. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this Personal Deposit
Account.
