The Director, Union Ministry of Finance and Department of Expenditure, Government of India, New Delhi, has issued a No. 1/4/2023-E-II (B), Dy. 20.10.2023 to the Judicial Officers of the Subordinate Courts in the State of Maharashtra for information and appropriate action.
According to the above-mentioned Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Finance of the Central Government. The increase in Dearness Allowance from 4% (42% to 46%) with effect from 01.07.2023 and other provisions mentioned in the memorandum shall continue to be applicable to serving judicial officers as well as retired judicial officers of the subordinate courts in the State of Maharashtra.
Accordingly, now the judicial officers working in the secondary courts of the state of Maharashtra as well as retired judicial officers will be given the benefit of this scheme. Dearness Allowance at the rate of 46% will be admissible from 01.07.2023. The increase in dearness allowance should be paid in cash.
