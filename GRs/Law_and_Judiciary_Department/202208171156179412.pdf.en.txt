Preamble: 26th Conference of District Judges of the State. It will be held on 27.08.2022 at Maharashtra Judicial Academy and Indian Mediation Centre and Training Institute, Uttan, Dist. A request has been made to approve the necessary expenditure for the conference and make available the funds through a short payment. by the High Court. The matter was under consideration at the government level.
Government decision: A total of Rs. 17,50,000/- (Rupees Seventeen Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) and the said amount is being allowed to be withdrawn on short payment subject to the following conditions:
(9) Government Circular No.Munwini-1016/ P.S. No. 38/17/ Regulations, D. As per Notice dated 18.12.2017 (20140207) under the heading, The detailed payments before 30.06.2022 are not pending.
(2) D. All overdue payments drawn on short payment after 30.06.2022 should be settled promptly.
(3) The detailed payment of this brief payment should be submitted within one month.
(8) It is not possible to meet the expenses by regular payment, but it is mandatory to withdraw the amount on short payment.
(4) It shall be the personal responsibility of the Deputy Director and Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Maharashtra Judicial Academy, Uttan, G.Thane to submit the detailed payment of this brief within the prescribed time.
(6) Funds are available for this purpose as per the monthly fund statement.
(7) The amount shall be withdrawn on payment of a sum of money within a maximum period of 10 days before the meeting.
(8) Such expenditure shall be incurred only for the purpose for which it is sanctioned in paragraph 2.
