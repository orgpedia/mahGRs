Accordingly, a proposal was received vide letter No. 1. The proposal has been submitted to the Finance Department. In the meeting held on 8/7/2019, approval has been received for the establishment and creation of the court. Government's decision:
The Government have approved the establishment of Civil Judge (Junior Level) and Judicial Magistrate, First Class Court at Rajur in Ahmednagar district and creation of 16 posts (14 posts regular and 2 posts by external agencies) for the same from the date of commencement of the Court as indicated in the following prospectus.
