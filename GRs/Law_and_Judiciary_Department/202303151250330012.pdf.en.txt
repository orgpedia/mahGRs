Preamble: Under the 14th Finance Commission, a total of 18 additional courts have been constituted for a period of 5 years and the posts for these 18 additional courts have been created under the Government decision at Reference No.
18 temporary posts for additional courts. The extension is required for the next period from 01.03.2023. Accordingly, the proposals have been received by the concerned District and Sessions Courts. As per the decision of the Finance Department in Reference No. 3, 01.03.2023 to Dy. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2023.
However, out of the above 18 courts, 12 courts have a tenure of 5 years. expired on/ before 28.02.2023. Accordingly, the extension of temporary posts for the remaining 6 additional courts, excluding the said 12 additional courts, was under consideration.
Government Decision: Out of 18 Additional Courts set up under 14th Finance Commission, the following temporary posts for 6 Additional Courts at Achalpur (District, Amravati), Nanded, Pune, Motor Vehicle Accident Claims Authority, Thane, Motor Vehicle Accident Claims Authority, Thane and Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai will be filled. w.e.f. 01.03.2023 The period of extension is upto 31.08.2023 or till the completion of 5 years of tenure of the said Courts, whichever is earlier.
