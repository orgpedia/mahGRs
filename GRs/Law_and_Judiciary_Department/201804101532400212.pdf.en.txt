the prelude
Gram Nyayalaya has been in existence since 2nd October, 2009 at Korpana, District Chandrapur.
Civil Judge Junior Level Rajura visits the Gram Nyayalayas at Korpana, District Chandpur from time to time. Observing that the required number of pending cases and infrastructure are meeting the standard, the Gram Nyayalaya Gadchandur, Th. It has been decided to set up a regular court of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class in Korpana, District Chandrapur instead of Gram Nyayalaya. The Establishment of Courts Committee was set up at the level of the Bombay High Court. Earlier, under the Government Order dated 23rd August, 2007, referred to in section 1, it was decided to establish Civil Court Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Courts in the non-court taluks of the State. Korpana under Chandrapur district of Maharashtra State.

The court was involved. As per the Government Order dated 26.10.2007, the necessary posts for 53 Taluk Courts were created. Accordingly, under the Government Order dated 23rd November, 2007 in Reference No. 3, it was approved to start 15 posts for a limited period for the court of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class at Korpana, District Chandrapur. However, under the Government Resolution dated 21st June, 2008 in Reference No. 4, the Government Resolution dated 26th October, 2007 was cancelled and a revised Government Resolution was issued. There was no creation of a post for the court at Korpana, District Chandrapur. As per the notification dated 29th September, 2009 issued under section 5 of the said Act, approval was accorded to start Gram Nyayalayas at Korpana, District Chandrapur. The posts for Gram Nyayalayas have been created vide Government Order dated 27th September, 2011 in Reference No. 6. In pursuance of the Government Order dated 23rd March, 2018, dated 07.09.2018, the Government have approved the establishment of Civil Court Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class in place of the existing Gram Nyayalaya at Korpana, District Chandrapur. The matter of creating a post for this court was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision
Government Resolution, Department of Law and Justice dated 23rd November, 2007 is being superseded.
The Government have accorded administrative approval for creation of 13 temporary posts for Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class at Korpana, District Chandrapur from the date of commencement of the Court. The work of the posts of watchman/ security guard and sweeper should be done by external agencies.
