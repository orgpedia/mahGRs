GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- In pursuance of the Government decision dated 15th September, 2020, 64 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court Jalna have been extended till 28th February, 2021. As per the Government Order dated 13th October, 2020 dated 13th October, 2020, administrative approval has been accorded for establishment of Senior Level District and Additional Sessions Court and Civil Court at Ambang, District Jalna and for revival of posts/ creation of posts for these two courts. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 80 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
