Introduction: As it has been observed that several transformative steps are required to be taken in the direction of administrative reforms in the functioning of the Charity Commissioners to provide support to the public trusts and control over the charitable institutions in the state, a review meeting was held under the chairmanship of Shri Ratnakar Gaikwad, former Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra, to make necessary recommendations in this regard. The High Level Study Committee has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 12.11.2018. The committee has been given three months to submit its report. The deadline expires on 11.02.2019. Hence, the government sought extension of time to submit the report. Rajendra Sawant, legal advisor and joint secretary, was transferred from the establishment of the Law and Justice Department to the post of Chief District Sessions Judge, Satara, so the matter of appointing other members in his place, as well as appointing additional 2 members on the said committee, was under the consideration of the government.
