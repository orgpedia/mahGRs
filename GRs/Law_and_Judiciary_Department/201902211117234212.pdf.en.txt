The Shri Siddhivinayak Ganpati Mandir Trust has decided to provide financial assistance to the families of the soldiers who were martyred in the terrorist attack in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Law and Justice,
Government Resolution No. Parivartan-1019/ P.S. No.33/ K.16
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai 400 032.
Date is February 20, 2019.
Reference:- The Executive Officer in charge, Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai vide letter No. Savigam/ 1581/2019, Dy. 16.02.2019
The proposal: On 14.02.2019, a dastardly terrorist attack was carried out on Indian soldiers in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir valley. Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple Trust Management Committee pays homage to the families of the Indian Army personnel martyred in the attack. At the meeting held on 15.02.2019, Rs. A sum of Rs. 51 lakh had been earmarked for the purpose. Accordingly, Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai, has decided to donate Rs. The proposal has been received vide letter dated 16.02.2019. The proposal was under consideration of the government to approve it as" a special case" as it was necessary to provide assistance from a humanitarian point of view to the families of the soldiers who were martyred while protecting the country.
Government's decision:
Financial assistance of Rs. 51 lakh from Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai.
