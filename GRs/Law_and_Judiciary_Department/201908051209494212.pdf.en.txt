Date: August 5, 2019
Read:- Synchronized Government Resolution dated 12.11.2018 and dated 01.06.2019
Introduction: As it has been observed that many transformative steps are required to be taken in order to bring about administrative reforms in the functioning of the Charity Commissioners in order to provide support to public trusts and control over charitable institutions in the state, a meeting was held under the chairmanship of Shri Ratnakar Gaikwad, former Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra, to make necessary recommendations in this regard. The High Level Study Committee has been constituted as per the Government decision dated 12.11.2018.
The term of office of the said Committee shall be as per the Government Decision dated 12.11.2018. With effect from the close of business on 11.02.2019, the said Committee has been superseded by the said Committee. The three-month extension has been granted under the government decision of June 1, 2019. The deadline has expired on 11.05.2019.
Hence, the government sought extension of time to submit the report.
The sixth meeting of the high-level study committee on government decisions was held on Saturday. It was held on 11.05.2019 at Sahyadri Guest House, Malabar Hill, Mumbai. After the meeting, the committee submitted its report. It will be presented to the Chief Minister. However, the term of the committee is yet to be extended.
To submit a report to the High Level Study Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 12.11.2018. 3 months from 12.05.2019. The deadline is being extended till 11.08.2019.
