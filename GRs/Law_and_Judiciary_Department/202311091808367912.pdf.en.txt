While passing the order of disbursement of the amount of housing advance, the concerned controlling officers should thoroughly scrutinize the proposal in accordance with the provisions of the prevailing government decisions and rules of the Finance Department. Only after this, the order for the distribution of funds should be withdrawn and the amount withdrawn from the treasury.
(2) Before disbursing the certified advance to the applicant concerned, the applicant shall ensure that all the items/ documents are completed and are in order. Also, the concerned Controlling Officer should ensure that the entire advance sanctioned by the applicant before his retirement is recovered along with interest.
(3) The amount of the advance shall be withdrawn from the treasury by approving this advance as long as the subsidy for the financial year is available on the budget distribution system. Also, after withdrawing the amount from the treasury, the amount should be given to the concerned applicant within three days.
(4) In cases where advance has been taken for the purpose of purchase of an under-construction flat, the Controlling Officer shall ensure that the construction of the applicant's house is completed up to the prescribed stage and only then the proposal for seeking subsidy for the second and third phase should be submitted to the Government. Applicants who have been certified for the 3rd installment/ final installment should complete all the necessary documents as per the rules and then pay the actual amount.
(4) Before recommending the sanction of the second instalment after sanction of the first instalment of the advance for the purpose of" purchase of land and construction of house," the Controlling Officer shall preserve in his records the approved plans of the applicant's house, a copy of the sanction order of the local authority and a copy of the registered mortgage form furnished by the applicant in Form B-1 in Annexure 26 under the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959. The Controlling Officer shall preserve a copy of the mortgage application filed by the applicant in Form B-2 in Annexure 26 under the Bombay Finance Rules, 1959 in his/ her records before the actual sanction of the advance certified under this Memorandum for the purpose of construction of the house. Recovery of advance, interest thereon from the applicants who have been certified in advance in respect of instalments shall be subject to the provisions of Annexure 26 under the Bombay Finance Rules, 1959.
(6) The amount of advance shall be actually paid by the applicants who have been certified in advance for the purpose of" purchase of ready house" only after filling up the copy of the agreement in Form A-2 in Annexure 26 under the Bombay Financial Rules, 1959. Also, the Controlling Officer should ensure that while accepting the contract of purchase from the officers/ employees regarding the old ready house/ new ready house, they have registered the same with the Secondary Registrar, Revenue and Forest Department by paying the appropriate stamp duty.
(7) The applicants who have been certified in advance for the purpose of" Repayment of Loan taken from Financial Institution for purchase of house/ construction of house" shall, before the advance approval, get a recent certificate issued by the concerned Financial Institution regarding their loan and balance and accordingly grant an advance equal to the actual balance loan.
(8) If for any reason the advance is not granted, it shall be notified to the Government forthwith. Also, if the amount sanctioned is less than the certified advance, the same should be informed immediately with proper details and the remaining amount of the certified advance should be returned to the department immediately through the Budget Distribution System (BEAMS).
(9) While issuing the house building advance approval order, it should be mentioned in the order that the insurance of the flat or house should be insured with the Government Insurance Fund and it should be strictly followed. A copy of the sanction order should also be forwarded to the Director of Insurance, Maharashtra State, Housing Building, MHADA, 264, 1st Floor, Bandra (East) Mumbai- 400 051 for further information.
(10) In order to ensure that the actual expenditure of each month determined by each Controlling Authority in terms of the target is exactly in line with the expenditure shown in the Accountant General's account, the monthly expenditure meeting should be made online every month on the website http:// agmaha.cag.gov.in and the monthly statement of verified expenditure should be sent to the Office of this Department by 10th of every month along with the Reconciliation Completion Slip. 5/24.
(11) The sanctioning authority shall cause the original copy of the" Appropriation Certificate" for the purpose for which the advance has been sanctioned to the applicant, the utilisation of the amount for the same purpose, to be kept in its records and send a second copy to the Government. Also, if the amount certified is not utilized within the prescribed period, the certified amount should be returned to the government immediately.
(12) Only after ascertaining on the basis of the service book or caste certificate that the applicants in List No. 2 and 4 belong to the backward class, the orders for the sanction of house construction should be issued. If the matter is not completed within 15 days, the certified amount should be returned to the government. This amount can be transferred to other eligible applicants. If for some reason the advance is not being approved, it should be informed to the government immediately. If not, please note that the concerned" Controlling Authority" will be responsible for any irregularity arising in this regard.
(13) If the concerned officer has shifted from his office, before taking out the authorization slip on the Budgetary Financial System (BEAMS), the details about it to the government.
