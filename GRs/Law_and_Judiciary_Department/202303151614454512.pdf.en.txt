Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated 12.09.2022, 126 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Osmanabad have been extended till 28.02.2023. Department of Law and Justice dated 11th November, 2022 has approved the establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court at Paranda, District Osmanabad and creation of post for this court. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 144 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
