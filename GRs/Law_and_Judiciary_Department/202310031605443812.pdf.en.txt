Government's decision
Gram Nyayalayas have been established in the State under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008. In order to ensure smooth functioning of these Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 135 temporary posts in 26 Gram Nyayalayas have been sanctioned as per the decision of the Government in this regard dated 15.03.2023.
The period has been extended from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the 28 Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 147 temporary posts in the following 28 Gram Nyayalayas are being extended for a period from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024.
