According to Rule 512 of the Treasury Rules, 1968, civil, criminal and motor accident claims
To open a personal deposit account for deposits and repayment amounts in this case.
Approval is being granted subject to the following conditions.
1. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit Account.
3. For the Civil Court Deposit, the credit to the Personal Deposit account will be the
Maintenance amount and other amounts in Civil proceeding by the concerned parties as per the court order. Expenditure out of the Personal Deposit Account will be as per courts order.
4. For the Criminal Court Deposit, the credit to the PD account will be the Maintenance
amount and other amounts in Criminal proceeding by the concerned parties as per the court order.
5. For the Motor Accident Claim Tribunal, the credit to the Personal Deposit account will
be the MACT amount and other amounts in MACT proceeding by the concerned parties as per the court order.
6. No Revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions shall be booked in this PD.
7. The balances in this Personal Deposit Account shall be reconciled by the Court on
Monthty basis with the District Treasury office.
8. On a yearly basis, the balance has to be reconciled with AG office and confirmation of
Balances is to be submitted by the Treasury officer to AG office at the end of the financial year.
9. The Finance Department GR dated 5.09.2023 regarding opreation of the PD/PLA should
be followed.
10. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this Personal Deposit
Account.
