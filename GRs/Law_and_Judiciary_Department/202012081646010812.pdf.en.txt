Preamble: The Central Project Coordinator, e-Courts Project, High Court, Appellate Division, Mumbai, referred No. A proposal was submitted to the government for the purchase of 1232 (2 in 1) convertible laptops.
The proposal has been approved by the High Powered Committee in its meeting dated 21.02.2019 and a Government Resolution has been issued under Reference No. 2. Reference No. 3 in the month. The High Court had submitted a proposal to the government to purchase a total of 389 (2in 1) convertible laptops to replace the old laptops of 277 judicial officers as well as laptops for 112 judicial officers to be appointed. In the meeting of the High Powered Committee dated 14.02.2020, it has been approved to purchase 389 (2 in 1) Convertible Laptops from the GeM portal. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the government to approve the proposal to purchase 389 (2 in 1) convertible laptops to the judicial officers of the state.
Government's decision:
Judicial officers in the state have to pay Rs 389 (2 in 1) to buy convertible laptops. 3,51,38, 440/- (Rs. A proposal for an amount of three crore fifty one lakh thirty eight thousand four hundred and forty only) is being given administrative approval under this government decision.
The procurement should be done through GeM portal as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 24.08.2017 and 08.12.2017 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour as well as the decision taken in the meeting of the High Powered Committee.
