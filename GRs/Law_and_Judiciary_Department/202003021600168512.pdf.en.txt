Gram Nyayalayas were established in the State under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008.
In order to ensure smooth functioning of these Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 110 temporary posts in 22 Gram Nyayalayas have been extended for the period from 20th September, 2019 to 30th September, 2019 as per the decision of the Government in this regard. For Gram Nyayalayas Korchi and Mulchera under the control of District and Sessions Court Gadchiroli, one fictitious post of driver each has been created vide Government Order dated 30.10.2019
Accordingly, to ensure smooth functioning of 22 Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 112 temporary posts in the following 22 Gram Nyayalayas are being extended for the period from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020.
