Preamble: The National Green Tribunal has been established under the National Green Tribunal Act, 2010 to deal with important issues relating to environment protection, protection of forests and civil matters. In such cases, the government is included as a party. Therefore, as an interim arrangement to conduct the case on behalf of the Government in the National Green Tribunal, Pune, the matter was under consideration to appoint/ nominate from among the" Assistant Public Prosecutors and Additional Public Prosecutors" working in Pune district on a temporary basis.
Government Resolution: The Government of Maharashtra at the National Green Tribunal (West Zone Bench), Pune is temporarily appointing the following" Assistant Public Prosecutors and Additional Public Prosecutors" working in Pune District and Sessions Court in the said Tribunal till the process of fresh appointment of Public Prosecutors by the concerned Revenue and Forest Department and Administrative Department of Environment and Climate Change for conducting court cases is completed.
