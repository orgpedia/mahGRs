Preamble: Under the order dated 25.06.2020 issued in the context of Department of Law and Justice, the post of" Superintendent" (Non-Gazetted) in the District and Sessions Court attached to the office of District Government Advocate (excluding City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) was declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" as a special case for the period up to 31.12.2020.
Now, it is under consideration of the Government to continue the charge of the post of" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" assigned to the" Superintendent" (Non-Gazetted) of the District and Sessions Court attached to the office of the District Public Prosecutor (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) for the intervening period till the Gazetted Officer is declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the District Public Prosecutor's Office or another Gazetted Officer is given the charge.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Accordingly, the responsibility of the post of" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" assigned as a special case to the post of" Superintendent" (Non-Gazetted) in the District and Sessions Court attached to the District Government Advocate Office (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) is being continued till 31st March, 2021. They are being authorized to deposit all the payments in this office in the treasury. The order will be limited to financial matters.
2. The decision of the Government shall be taken by the Finance Department. Reference 9/21/ Co. Q-5, dated 01.02.2021 is being issued pursuant to the approval given.
