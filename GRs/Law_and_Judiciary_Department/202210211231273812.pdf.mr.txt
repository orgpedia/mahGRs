The following shall be classification which may be recorded on every challan while depositing the amount in Treasury/Sub-Treasury.
For Civil Court Deposit ८४४३ Civil Deposit १०४ - Civil Court Deposit ०१ District Judge- १ & Additional Session Judge, Vadgaon Maval Dist – Pune.
For Criminal Court Deposit
८४४३ - Civil Deposit १०५ – Criminal Court Deposit ०१ – District Judge-१ & Additional Session Judge, Vadgaon Maval Dist – Pune.
1. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3. Amount relating to decretal deposit by the respondents and other judiciary matters as per the court order, may be deposited in the
Personal Deposit Account. Expenditure as per the court order may be made out of the Personal Deposit Account.
4. No Revenue Receipt/Revenue Expenditure transactions will appear in this Personal Deposit Account.
5. Either on Quarterly or Monthly basis, balances in this Personal
Deposit Account shall be reconciled by the Court.
6. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this Personal Deposit Account.
