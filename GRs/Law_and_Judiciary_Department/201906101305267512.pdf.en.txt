Preamble: The proposal submitted by the High Court to grant approval to the designated service provider through e-tendering process and the cost incurred thereon for carrying out the work of cleaning, maintenance and management of the gardens in the premises of the Government residences of the Judges of the Nagpur Bench of the Bombay High Court through external agencies for a period of 2 years was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Decision of the Government: The Department of Cleaning, Maintenance and Management of the gardens in the vicinity of the official residences of the Judges of the Nagpur Bench of the Bombay High Court. 1/06/2019 to D. 31/05/2021 for a period of two years to be carried out by external agencies from the service provider M/ s Om Sai Roses, Bharat Nagar, Nagpur and accordingly Rs. 1,66, 203.66 including all taxes amounting to Rs. 39,88,888/- (Rupees Forty-nine Lakhs Eighty-eight Thousand Eight Hundred and Eighty-eight only) is being approved by the Government under this Order.
The said approval is being granted subject to the condition that copies of the documents submitted by the aforesaid service provider for tendering process are attested by the competent authority at Bench Nagpur. It shall be mandatory for the above service provider as well as the High Court Administration to comply with all the terms and conditions laid down by the Government from time to time regarding the work to be done by the external agency while doing this cleaning work through an external agency.
The Manager (Administration), High Court, Bombay, Bench, Nagpur, shall, within 15 days, enter into an appropriate agreement with the concerned service provider and submit a copy thereof to the Government. the above service. It will automatically expire on 31/05/2021. before the expiry of this Agreement.
