Pursuant to the Government decision dated 08.10.2018, 91 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Akola have been extended till 28.02.2019.
In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 91 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
