Introduction: Refer No. 1 here. As a temporary arrangement for 14 Family Courts at Latur, Osmanabad, Beed, Jalna, Parbhani, Sangli, Raigad-Alibag, Jalgaon, Yavatmal, Ahmednagar, Satara, Dhule, Buldhana and Bhandara established under the 14th Finance Commission under the Government decision dated 23.09.2022, the Controlling Officers of the District and Sessions Courts of the respective districts were declared as the Controlling Officers of the respective Family Courts.
Also, the Chief Judges/ Judges of the respective Courts have been declared as Controlling Officers and Drawing and Disbursing Officers for the Family Courts established in 11 places in the State before 2016, namely Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Thane, Amravati, Akola, Nashik, Solapur, Kolhapur and Nanded. However, the Controlling Officer and Drawing and Disbursing Officer are expected to be separate officers and the Controlling Officer is meant to be the senior officer to the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
The expenditure of all the Family Courts in the State including the 14 Family Courts established under the 14th Finance Commission from the financial year 2023-24 is being met from a single head 2014 0341. Therefore, there should be uniformity in all family courts in the State with respect to Controlling Officers as well as Drawing and Disbursing Officers. In view of the above, the Chief Justice/ Judges of all the Family Courts in the State are requested to provide feedback regarding declaration of Controller Officers and Managers as Drawing and Disbursing Officers. When the High Court, The High Court vide Reference No. 2 has agreed to this. Accordingly, the government has taken the decision.
