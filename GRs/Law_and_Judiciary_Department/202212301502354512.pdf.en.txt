Preamble: The consolidated guidelines for direct recruitment to the nominated quota posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres in the establishment of the office of the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and offices under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Service Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) have been issued by the General Administration Department under the Government Decisions dated 04.05.2022 and 21.11.2022. Accordingly, the composition of district, regional and state level selection committees for the recruitment of the mentioned posts has been mentioned in the said government decision. Accordingly, the administrative department of the ministry has to form regional and state-level selection committees. In this regard, it is necessary to constitute each department-wise selection committees as per the structure given in the General Administration Department, Government Resolution, dated 04.05.2022. Accordingly, the Government was considering the formation of a State Level Selection Committee to directly fill the posts in the nomination quota in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C cadres on the establishment of the Office of the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai and offices under its control.

The consolidated guidelines regarding the recruitment process have been issued by the General Administration Department under the Government Decisions dated 04.05.2022 and 21.11.2022.
Accordingly, the recruitment process must be carried out in accordance with the instructions given/ issued from time to time by the General Administration Department, Finance Department and Law and Justice Department. While filling up the posts, care should be taken to fill up the posts only after considering the procedures prescribed by the government, rules and orders regarding entry into service, reservation of all kinds, point list. It will be mandatory to take prior approval of the government from time to time regarding the prescribed matter as given in the guidelines in the government decision.
