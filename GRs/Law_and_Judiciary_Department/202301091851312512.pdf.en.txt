Preamble: In pursuance of the Government Resolution dated 21.06.2018 under the Department of Law and Justice and the Corrigendum dated 19.10.2018 under the Department of Law and Justice, the Advocate General, State of Maharashtra has been conferred the status of" Minister of State" and accordingly the services to be provided to the Advocate General, State of Maharashtra have been provided.
Now, the Department of Law and Justice, under reference, vide Government Notification dated 15.12.2022, has appointed Shri Dr. Birendra Saraf as Advocate General, State of Maharashtra.
Accordingly, the matter of granting the status of" Minister of State" and permissible facilities to Mr. Dr. Birendra Saraf, Advocate General, State of Maharashtra was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Shri Dr. Birendra Saraf has been appointed as Advocate General, State of Maharashtra vide Government Notification dated 15.12.2022 by the Department of Law& Justice, Govt.
Accordingly, Dr. Birendra Saraf, Shri. The Advocate General, State of Maharashtra is being given the status of" Minister of State" under this Government decision.
