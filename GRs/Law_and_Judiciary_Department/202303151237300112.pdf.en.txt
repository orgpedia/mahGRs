Preamble: For immediate redressal of cases of atrocities against women in the State, Reference No. 14 special courts have been set up. A total of 99 temporary posts in these 14 Special Courts have been notified vide Reference No. 2 decision of the government. The period was extended till 28.02.2023. Proposals for further extension of these 99 temporary posts have been received by the Government. Reference No. 4 decision of the Finance Department. 01.03.2023 to Dy. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2023. A total of 99 temporary posts in 14 special courts were under consideration for extension.
A total of 99 temporary posts have been sanctioned for the establishment of special courts in 14 districts of Mumbai, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Dhule, Jalna, Latur, Nanded, Nashik, Osmanabad, Parbhani, Sangli, Satara, Solapur and Wardha to deal with cases of atrocities against women. 01.03.2023 to Dy. The period is being extended up to 31.08.2023.
