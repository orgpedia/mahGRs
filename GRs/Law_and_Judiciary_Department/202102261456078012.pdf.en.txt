Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated 09.09.2020, 95 temporary posts on the establishment of District and Sessions Court Osmanabad have been extended till 28.02.2021. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 95 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021.
