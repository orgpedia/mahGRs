The Proposal:
More than 1000 cases related to persons with disabilities, senior citizens and marginalized sections of the society are pending at 11 places. Under the decision of the government, it has been approved to establish 11 special courts and create 55 temporary posts for these courts. Provisional posts for these 11 Special Courts. The extension is required for the next period from 01.09.2022. Accordingly, the proposals have been received by the concerned District and Sessions Courts. Reference No. 4 decision of the Finance Department. 01.09.2022 to dt. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 28.02.2023.
Accordingly, the matter of extending the tenure of these posts was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: A total of 55 temporary posts in 11 special courts set up for persons with disabilities, senior citizens and marginalized sections of the society have been sanctioned. 01.09.2022 to dt. The period is being extended up to 28.02.2023.
