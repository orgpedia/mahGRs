Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated 05.09.2022, 99 temporary posts on the establishment of District and Sessions Court Gadchiroli have been extended till 28.02.2023. In pursuance of the Government Order dated 02.02.2023, the Government have approved the establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court at Aheri, District Gadchiroli and creation of post for this Court.
