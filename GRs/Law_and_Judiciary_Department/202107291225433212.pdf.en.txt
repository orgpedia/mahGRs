The Proposal:
The Chief Justice, City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai has been declared as the Head of the Department vide Government Order No.1 of the said Act. The City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai, has been allocated Rs. 2500/- was sanctioned under the Government Resolution No. 2.
At present, there are more than 75 Courts and more than 10 Divisions in the Municipal Civil and Sessions Courts, both in Mumbai and Dindoshi. Therefore, the scope of the court's work is very wide. At present, Rs. In the limit of 2500/-, the payments submitted to the Subordinate and Accounts Office are delayed in filing the return after approval and many payments remain pending. This leads to delay in payment of dues. Accordingly, the said Court vide letter No. 4 has requested for enhancement of amount of Permanent Advance for the Court of City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai. Accordingly, the matter of increasing the amount of the permanent advance of this court was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai Rs. 2500/- instead of Rs. It is hereby approved to increase the amount of fixed advance to Rs.5000/-.
