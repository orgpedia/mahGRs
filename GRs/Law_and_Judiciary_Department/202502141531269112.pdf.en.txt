Circular
The Commissioner of Charities, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, shall fix the seniority number and date of seniority of the officers of the Group-B (Gazetted) cadre as Personal Assistant on Establishment. Temporary Service Excellence List dated 01.01.2025, Reference No. 9 was published by the government circular here.
No objection has been received as on date 08.02.2025 in respect of this provisional merit list. Considering this, the present Service Excellence List is being finalised. This final merit list is mentioned in Annexure-A to this circular.
A copy of this final merit list is being brought to the notice of the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, all officers of the Group-B (Gazetted) cadre, Personal Assistant on Establishment.
This Government Circular is available on the www.maharashtra.gov.in website of the Government of Maharashtra.
