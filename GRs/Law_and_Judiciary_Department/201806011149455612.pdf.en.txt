The government has taken the following decisions:

Government Resolution No: Contract No.28/ Q.12 of 2018
The government has approved the creation of 12 temporary posts of excavators and 3 garden workers for the new building of District and Sessions Court Sangli, District Sangli and their services will be made available on a contract basis through an external system.
While making available the above 15 posts on contract basis through external agencies, it will be mandatory to comply with all the terms and conditions of the Government Decision No. 2512/ No. 157/ Q. 12, dated January 27, 2015 and the relevant Government Decisions.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be provided under the head Demand No. J-1, 2014-Judgment, (00) 105 Civil and Sessions Courts, (02) District and Sessions Judge, (02) (01) District and Sessions Judge, (20140172) Datta Mat, 13 Office Expenditure.
This Government decision is in accordance with the Informal Reference No. 274/ Expenditure-5 of the Department of Finance, dated 15.03.2018 and the Informal Reference No. 174/ Appuq of the Department of Finance, dated 15.03.2018 and the Informal Reference No. 331/2018/ Ka of the Department of General Administration. 12 dated 31.03.2018 with the approval of the Department.
