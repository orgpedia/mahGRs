In pursuance of the Government decision dated 15.10.2018 in A.No. (4) dated 30.09.2018, 169 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Buldhana have been extended. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 169 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019.
