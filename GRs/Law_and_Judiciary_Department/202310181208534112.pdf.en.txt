Preamble: The Central Project Coordinator, e-Courts Project, Appellate Division, High Court, Mumbai has submitted a proposal for procurement of new computer content for Civil Court, Senior Level, Mangaon Court vide Reference No. 1 submitted to the Government. The proposal has received the opinion of the Directorate of Information Technology. Reference No. Under Section 2, it was asked to submit a proposal for the purchase of computer content according to the revised specifications. Accordingly, reference No. Under Section 3, a proposal for the purchase of computer materials according to the revised specifications was submitted to the government. This has been approved by the Project Implementation Committee of the Department in its meeting held on 27.09.2023. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the proposal for the purchase of new computer materials for the Civil Court, Senior Level, Mangaon Court was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For purchase of new computer material for Civil Court, Senior Level, Mangaon Court, Rs. 26,99,559/- (Rupees Twenty Six Lakhs Nine Thousand Five Hundred and Nineteen Sixty-Nine only) is being accorded administrative approval under this Government Resolution.
