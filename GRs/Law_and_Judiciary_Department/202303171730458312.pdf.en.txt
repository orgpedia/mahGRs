Reference:- Department of Law and Justice, Government Resolution No.- Special No.1318/ PR No.57/ K-9, 15.03.2023.
Government Preamble: Paragraph 1 of the Government Resolution referred to in the Reference makes the following observations:
" Out of 18 additional courts set up under the 14th Finance Commission, the following temporary posts for 6 additional courts at Achalpur (District, Amravati), Nanded, Pune, Motor Vehicle Accident Claim Authority, Thane, Motor Vehicle Accident Claim Authority, Thane and Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai have been sanctioned. w.e.f. 01.03.2023 The period of extension is upto 31.08.2023 or till the completion of 5 years of tenure of the said Courts, whichever is earlier. "
Instead, it should be read as follows:
" Out of the 18 additional courts set up under the 14th Finance Commission, the following temporary posts for 6 additional courts at Achalpur (Dist-Amravati), Nanded, Pune, Motor Vehicle Accident Claims Authority-Nagpur, Motor Vehicle Accident Claims Authority-Thane and Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai will be filled. w.e.f. 01.03.2023 The period of extension is upto 31.08.2023 or till the completion of the period of 5 years of the said Courts, whichever is earlier. "
