Preamble: Reference No. 1 for construction of new court building for District Judge-I at Biloli, Dist-Nanded. 1 The government has decided to provide Rs. The original administrative approval was accorded for the work at an estimated cost of Rs. 9,15,16, 033/-. However, since the Goods and Services Tax (GST) came into effect from July 2017, the amount of tax was not paid. The High Court, Mumbai has made the request vide letter No. 2 under reference. Therefore, the matter of granting revised administrative approval for the construction of this building was under the consideration of the government.
