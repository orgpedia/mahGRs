The Proposal:
The minimum number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be 500, along with infrastructure e.g. There should be court buildings and residences of judges. As all the criteria required as per the policy of the High Court are being fulfilled for setting up of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Chalisgaon in Jalgaon district, the Hon'ble High Court has decided to set up a Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Chalisgaon in Jalgaon district. The" Court Establishment Committee" of the High Court has approved the establishment of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Chalisgaon in Jalgaon district. It was also informed that 18 posts will be transferred from the establishment of Civil Court (Junior Level) Chalisgaon for this court.
Accordingly, the matter of approving the establishment of this court and the creation of the remaining 11 posts required for it was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approve the establishment of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Chalisgaon in Jalgaon district for the Chalisgaon Taluka area and creation of the remaining 11 posts (9 regular and 2 external agency posts) for the same from the date of commencement of the Court as indicated in the following prospectus.
