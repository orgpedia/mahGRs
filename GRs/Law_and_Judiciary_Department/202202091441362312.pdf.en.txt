Preamble- The number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be at least 500, along with the infrastructure, e.g. The court buildings and residences of the judges should be made available. As it is evident that all the criteria required as per the policy of the High Court are being fulfilled for the establishment of Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Savner, District Nagpur, Shri. The High Court of Bombay has recommended the establishment of a Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Saoner, District Nagpur.
Accordingly, the matter of setting up a Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Saoner, District Nagpur was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision
The government has decided to set up courts of senior civil judges for the taluks of Savner and Kalmeshwar in Savner, Nagpur district. Also, the government is being allowed to create posts, transfer from other courts and create notional posts for this court as shown below:
