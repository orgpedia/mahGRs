As per the Government decision dated 19.11.2016, the Minister in charge of the department concerned has been declared as the appointing authority in respect of the appointment of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers on their establishment. Further, it has been directed that separate orders be issued by the concerned administrative department to decide who among the Secretary/ Head of Department/ Head of Office of the concerned department will be the appointing authority in respect of the appointment/ posting of officers and employees of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C, and Group-D by direct service, promotion or deputation.
Reference No. 2. As per the Government Order No. 2, the officers of the Non-Judicial Administrative Office under the Department of Law and Justice have been declared as Head of Department, Head of Office. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the appointment of Appointment Officer for the posts of Group-B (Gazetted), Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D in 13 offices under the administrative control of Office-Session-8 of the Department of Law and Justice by direct service, promotion or deputation.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Appointment Authorities are being declared as indicated in the accompanying Annexure-A under this Government Decision for making appointments/ postings by direct service, promotion or deputation to the posts of Group-B (Gazetted), Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D in 13 offices under Section-8 of the Act.
The appointing authority at Annexure-A shall be the competent authority to take final decision on matters of appointments/ postings to be made by direct service, promotion or deputation. However, the final decision regarding the transfer will be taken by the competent authority notified under the Transfer Act.
