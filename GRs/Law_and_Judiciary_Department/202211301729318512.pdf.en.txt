Preamble In pursuance of the above, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Parner in Ahmednagar District vide Government Notification dated 29th September, 2009 at No. Further, in pursuance of the Government decision dated 27th September, 2011 and the Corrigendum dated 13th February, 2012, referred to in Reference No. 3, in the aforesaid Annexure, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been constituted in the State.
The District Court Ahmednagar has requested to declare the Magistrate, Gram Nyayalay Parner (Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Parner) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the Gram Nyayalay Parner. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court.
Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare Magistrate Gram Nyayalay Parner, District Ahmednagar as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalay Scheme for Gram Nyayalay Parner at Parner.

Government Resolution No: DDO 2022/ Q. No.238 (2)/ Q.12
The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya Parner, District Ahmednagar is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Gram Nyayalaya established under the Gram Nyayalaya Act, 2008 at Parner, District Ahmednagar. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 read with Finance Division No. 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated 17.04.2015. .
