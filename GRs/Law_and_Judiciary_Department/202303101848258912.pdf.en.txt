Government's decision
Subject to the decision of the Government dated 05.09.2022, 133 temporary posts on the establishment of District and Sessions Court Gondia have been extended till 28.02.2023. As per the Government Order dated 21.11.2022, 15 new posts have been created for District and Sessions Court Gondia. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 148 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
