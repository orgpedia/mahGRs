Preamble- In pursuance of the Government notification dated 24th June, 2014, vide No.1 above, Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Salekasa in Gondia district. 2 As per the Government Decision dated 27th September, 2011 and 3rd February, 2012, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been constituted in the State. District Court Gondia has requested to declare Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Saleksa as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the scheme as the fund will be disbursed through PFMS system for Gram Nyayalaya. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Salekasa as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Salekasa at Gondia.
The Gram Nyayalayas at Salekasa, Distt. Gondia constituted under Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008 under Rule 15 (3)& 156 of Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968
