Preamble: The minimum number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be 500, along with infrastructure e.g. There should be court buildings and residences of judges. As all the criteria required as per the policy of the High Court are being fulfilled, the Government has decided to set up District and Additional Sessions Court at Shrigonda, G. Ahmednagar. The" Court Establishment Committee" of the High Court has approved it as a special case. Accordingly, the matter of approving the establishment of this court and the creation of 20 posts required by it was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Government have decided to establish District and Additional Sessions Court at Shrigonda, Ahmednagar District for the Taluks of Srigonda, Karjat and Jamkhed and to create 20 posts (13 regular and 7 extern posts) for the same from the date of commencement of the Court as indicated in the following prospectus.
