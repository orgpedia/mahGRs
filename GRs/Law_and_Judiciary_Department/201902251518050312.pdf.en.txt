Preamble- The Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Hinganghat has been functioning at Hinganghat, District Wardha from 03.04.2017 as per notification dated 01.04.2017 under reference No. As per the Government Order dated 20.06.2017, District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Hinganghat and Civil Judge Senior Level Hinganghat have been declared as Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. On 18.12.2018, the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Wardha has requested for approval to open a Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Hinganghat, District Wardha. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Hinganghat, District Wardha.
The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to audit in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, Clause 1 of Rule No. 512, for depositing the amounts in the name of Civil Judge Senior Level, Hinganghat, District Wardha, subject to the following conditions: 1.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
