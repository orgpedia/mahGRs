Subject to the decision of the Government dated 08.09.2022, 142 temporary posts on the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Nagpur have been extended till 28.02.2023. As per the Government Order dated 15 th November, 2022, administrative approval has been accorded for establishment of Civil Court Senior Level at Ramtek District Nagpur and creation of post for this Court. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 146 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
