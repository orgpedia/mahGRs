The Proposal:
Reference No. Vehicle review of 11 family courts in the state has been approved under the government decision here. As per this vehicle review, 2 vehicles are permissible for the office of Family Court, Nagpur.
Reference No. As per the Government Order, the Chief Justice, Family Court, Nagpur was given administrative approval to purchase a new Government vehicle for office work. However, due to the lack of registration of the Family Court, Nagpur on the GeM (Government e-Market) system, it was not possible to carry out the process of vehicle purchase. Presently, the office of Family Court, Nagpur has been registered on GeM (Government e-Market) system.
The High Court has once again requested the Government to sanction the purchase of a new Government vehicle for the office of the Chief Justice, Family Court, Nagpur. According to reference no. As per the proposal herein, the matter of granting administrative approval to the proposal for purchase of 1 new Government vehicle for the office of the Chief Justice, Family Court, Nagpur was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Approval is hereby accorded to the Chief Justice, Family Court, Nagpur for purchase of 1 new Government vehicle for official purposes.
