Government Circular: In order to provide facilities to the courts in the State," Infrastructure Policy for Courts" has been issued under the Government decision in the above context. Issue No. 11 of the Policy in the Policy Policy, in the Annexure No. 11.2 of" Mode of Correspondence," provides that" a separate e-mail ID shall be created for the said Proposal Handling Cell of the High Court and the District Courts." Accordingly, for dealing with the proposals of High Court and District Court," so13.lnjd-mh@gov. An email address has been created. However, correspondence in respect of the proposals mentioned in the Infrastructure Policy for Courts should be sent to the said e-mail ID.
