In order to strengthen the juvenile justice system in the state, the High Court administration had submitted a proposal to the government through a reference letter to create two posts of one cell officer/ assistant and one clerk in order to provide separate secretarial facilities for the High Court Juvenile Justice Committee. Accordingly, the matter of approving the post was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision- To strengthen the child justice system in the state. In order to provide separate secretarial facility for Juvenile Justice Committee of Bombay High Court, an Assistant Cell Officer (Pay Band-2 as per 6th Pay Commission, Rs. 9300-34800+5000/- Grade Pay) and a Clerk- Typist (Pay Band-2 as per 6th Pay Commission, Rs. A total of 2 posts (Rs. 5200-20200+ Rs. 2800/- Grade Pay) are being sanctioned by the Government for creation and expenditure on establishment of Appellate Branch.
2) The posts should be filled up as per the norms of the government.
3. The process of including the said posts in the High Court's draft, finalizing the draft and submitting it to the High-Level Committee of Secretaries for approval should be completed.
4. Both the posts are temporary in nature and shall be for a fixed term. It will remain till 31/08/2023. Thereafter, action should be taken as per the directions issued from time to time regarding the extension of temporary posts by the Finance Department.
