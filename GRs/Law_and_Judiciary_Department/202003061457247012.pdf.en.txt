The Proposal:
As per the proposal of the Hon'ble High Court, the Government have decided to establish and create the post of Civil Judge (Junior Level) and Judicial Magistrate First Class at Rajur in Ahmednagar district. Narrow 1114/1153/ No.210/ Work-11, Dy. Approved on 13/9/2019. In the said government decision, it was stated that the orders regarding the availability of funds would be issued separately. In this regard, a symbolic supplementary demand was submitted to the Legislature through the Finance Department in the December-2019 session. It's been approved. Thus, the proposal to provide funds for this court was under the consideration of the government.
The Legislative Assembly has passed the following symbolic supplementary demands for the Civil Judge (Junior Level) and Judicial Magistrate First Class Court established at Rajur in Ahmednagar district.
