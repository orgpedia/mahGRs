Pursuant to the Government decision dated 19th September, 2019, 136 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Nagpur have been extended till 29th February, 2020. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 136 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020.
