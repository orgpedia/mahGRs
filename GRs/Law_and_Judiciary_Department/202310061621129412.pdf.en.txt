1 The Government hereby approve the establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court and creation of a post for this Court in place of the District and Additional Sessions Court at Mukhed, G. Nanded. As per the Government Notification No. 2 and No. 3. The said Court has become functional with effect from 01.09.2023. On the request made by the Manager, High Court, Bombay and District and Sessions Court, Nanded, the Government was considering declaring the District and Additional Sessions Judge, Mukhed, G. Nanded, as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
The District and Additional Sessions Judge, Mukhed, G. Nanded, has been declared as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the regular District and Additional Sessions Court at Nanded. The order will be limited to financial matters.

In exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, dated April 17, 2015, in Part I, Sub-Division I, No. 3, dated 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated April 17, 2015, the office of the Head of the Department is being declared. Also, the Drawing and Disbursing Officer is being declared in accordance with the provision made in Rule 15 (3) of the Maharashtra Treasury Act, 1968.
