Gram Nyayalayas have been established in the State under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008. In order to ensure smooth functioning of these Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 112 temporary posts in 22 Gram Nyayalayas have been extended for the period from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021 under the Contextual Government Resolution, Department of Law& Justice, dated 18.09.2020. For Gram Nyayalaya Devalaya and Trimbakeshwar under District and Sessions Court Nashik and for Gram Nyayalaya Vaibhavwadi under District and Sessions Court Sindhudurg, one notional post of driver each has been created vide Government Order dated 11.11.2020. Therefore, to ensure smooth functioning of 22 Gram Nyayalayas.
