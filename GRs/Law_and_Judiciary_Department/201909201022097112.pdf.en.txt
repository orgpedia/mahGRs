Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated 01.03.2019, 104 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Beed have been extended till 30.09.2019. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 104 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020.
