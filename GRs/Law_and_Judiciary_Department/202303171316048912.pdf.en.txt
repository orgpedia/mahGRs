Saoner, District Nagpur, with effect from 24th September, 2022, the date mentioned in Annexure No. As per the notification dated 25 th August, 2022 and corrigendum dated 07 th September, 2022, the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Savner has become functional on regular basis. The Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur, has requested the Civil Judge Senior Level Saoner to grant administrative approval for opening a personal provident account for the court.
