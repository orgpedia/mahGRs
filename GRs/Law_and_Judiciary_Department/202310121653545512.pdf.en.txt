As per the Government Order dated 14th March, 2011, the officers of the Non-Judicial Administrative Offices under the Department of Law and Justice have been declared as Head of the Departmental Head Office as indicated in the prospectus' A' accompanying the Government Order. In the accompanying prospectus' A' at No. 20, for the office of the Principal Secretary and Legal Adviser, Department of Law and Justice, Ministry of Law and Justice, Mumbai (itself), at column No. 4, the Principal Secretary and Legal Adviser have been declared as the head of the department. The order is being partially modified to declare the Principal Secretary/ Secretary and Senior Legal Advisor as' Head of Department' for the Department of Law and Justice (itself) in pursuance of the approval of the proposal regarding the financial matters of the offices entrusted to the Principal Secretary/ Secretary and Senior Legal Advisor.
