Preamble- Request for administrative approval for installation of generators for the main server of the court buildings at Vadvani and Shirur under the control of the District and Sessions Court, Beed. The High Court of Bombay. The detailed budget of the work has been prepared by the Deputy Engineer, Public Works Department Beed on the schedule for the year 2015-16 and the Executive Engineer, Public Works Department Aurangabad as well as the Principal District and Sessions Judge Beed have testified. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval for installation of generators for servers for court buildings in Wadhwani and Shirur under the control of District and Sessions Court Beed was under consideration of the government.
Administrative Sanction is being accorded for installation of Generators for Servers in Court Buildings at Vadwani and Shirur under the control of District and Sessions Court, Beed at a cost of Rs. 13,37,940/- (Rupees Thirteen Lakhs Thirty Seven Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty Only).
