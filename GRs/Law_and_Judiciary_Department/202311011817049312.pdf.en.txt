The Proposal:
High Court of Justice. As per the Standard Format dated 04.04.2007, the minimum number of pending cases/ cases for opening of new courts at various places in the State should be 500, along with the infrastructure e.g. There should be availability of court building and residence of judges. or ma. As all the necessary criteria are being fulfilled as per the policy of the High Court, the Government has decided to set up a District and Additional Sessions Court in Ahmednagar. The approval has been given by the" Court Establishment Committee" of the High Court. High level committee and Secretary. As approved by the Cabinet, the matter of setting up the said Court and issuing orders regarding the creation of the post accordingly was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: This Government has approved the establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court at Rahata, Ahmednagar and creation of 19 regular posts and taking over the services of 6 human resources by external agencies from the date of commencement of the Court as indicated in the following statement.
