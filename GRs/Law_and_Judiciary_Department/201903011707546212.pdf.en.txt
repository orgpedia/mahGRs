Subject to the Government decision dated 15.10.2018, 109 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Beed have been extended till 28.02.2019.
In order to ensure smooth functioning of the concerned Courts, 104 temporary posts (excluding 5 contractual posts of Watchmen) as mentioned in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019.
