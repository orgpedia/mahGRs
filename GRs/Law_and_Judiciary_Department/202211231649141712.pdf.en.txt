The said Supplementary Demand has been approved under the heading Demand No. J-1, 2014, Adjudication, 105, Civil and Sessions Courts, (02) District and Sessions Judge, (02) (01) District and Sessions Judge (Compulsory) (20140172). Accordingly, out of the administrative sanction for the above works, the remaining 80% of the fund amounting to Rs. 2,51,85, 895/- (Rupees Two Crore One Lakh Fifty Five Thousand Eight Hundred and Ninety Five only) is being disbursed to the Controlling Officer, the Principal District and Sessions Judge concerned. The expenditure required for these courts should be met from the grants sanctioned for the year 2022-23 under the head of the aforesaid accounts.
The said Government Resolution is being issued in accordance with the approval given under the Informal Reference No. 1080/2022/ Expenditure-5 of the Department of Finance, dated 15th November, 2022.
