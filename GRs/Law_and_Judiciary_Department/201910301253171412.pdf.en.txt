Preamble- Request for creation of notional posts of one driver each for Gram Nyayalayas Korchi and Gram Nyayalayas Mulchera, District Gadchiroli and sanction for taking services of these posts through external agencies. The reference was made by the High Court of Bombay vide its letter dated 6th April, 2017. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of one fictitious post of driver each for Gram Nyayalaya Korchi, District Gadchiroli and Gram Nyayalaya Mulchera, District Gadchiroli and the government was approving the services of this post to be made available on contract basis through external agencies. The government has taken the following decisions:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No: Designation 2017/ P.No. 126/ K.A.12
The government has decided to create one post of driver each for Gram Nyayalayas Korchi and Mulchera in Gadchiroli district and provide services of these posts on contract basis through external agencies. The expenditure for this purpose shall be met under the head Demand No. J. 1, 2014 Adjudication, (00) 105 Civil and Sessions Courts, (02) District and Sessions Judges, (02) (02) Establishment of Gram Nyayalayas under Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008, (2014 0477) Dattamat.
(2) In the case of filling up of these posts on contractual basis by external agencies, it shall be mandatory to comply with the guidelines issued in this regard from time to time.
This Government Resolution is being issued with the concurrence of the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 354/ Expenditure-5, dated 10th June, 2019 and Informal Reference No. 268/ Appuc, dated 30th August, 2019.
