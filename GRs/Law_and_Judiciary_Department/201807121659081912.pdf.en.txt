29/16/ Fiscal Reforms-1, dated 02.08.2016, guidelines have been issued for providing services on contract basis through external agencies. Accordingly, the Maharashtra Judicial Academy and the Indian Mediation Centre and Training Institute, Uttan, Thane, have decided to construct their respective buildings.
After getting the area certified by the Public Works Department, a tender process has been implemented to get the work of the sweeper done on a contract basis by an external agency. And the proposal of L1 tenderer vide letter dated 15.05.2018 mentioned in Annexure (5) has been submitted to the Government for approval.
Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
