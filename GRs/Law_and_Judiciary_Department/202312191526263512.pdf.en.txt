District and Sessions Court, Thane As requested under letter No. 4, the matter of declaring District Judge-I and Additional Sessions Judge, Bhiwandi and Civil Judge, Senior Level, Bhiwandi as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
District Judge-1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Bhiwandi, for the District and Additional Sessions Court, Bhiwandi in Thane district are being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." They are being authorized to deposit all kinds of payments in this court in the treasury. The order will be limited to financial matters.
Further, the Civil Judge Senior Level, Bhiwandi, has been declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the Civil Court Senior Level, Bhiwandi, Thane District. They are being authorized to deposit all kinds of payments in this court in the treasury. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government has decided to amend the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, dated 17/4/2015, in Part-I, Sub-Division I, No.3, Rule No. 2 issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments and under Rule 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
