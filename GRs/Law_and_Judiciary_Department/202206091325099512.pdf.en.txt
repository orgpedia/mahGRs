Preamble In pursuance of the Government notification dated 05.08.2019, vide No.1 of the above, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Deoli in Wardha district. Further, in pursuance of the aforesaid Government Decision dated 13th February, 2012, mentioned at Annexure-II, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. District Court Wardha has requested to declare Magistrate for Deoli Gram Nyayalaya, Gram Nyayalaya Deoli (Civil Judge Junior Level and First Class Judicial Magistrate Wardha) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Principal District and Sessions Judge Wardha as Controlling Officer of the scheme. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Deoli, District Wardha as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Deoli, Wardha and Principal District and Sessions Judge Wardha as Controlling Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Deoli, Wardha.
The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya, Deoli, District Wardha is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Gram Nyayalaya established under the Gram Nyayalaya Act, 2008 at Deoli, District Wardha. Also, the Principal District and Sessions Judge for these Gram Nyayalayas
