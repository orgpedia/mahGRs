According to the Family Court, Belapur, the following conditions apply for opening a personal deposit account for deposits:
Subject to approval.
The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to audit.
The administrator should ensure proper maintenance of personal
Deposit Account.
Receipt into the PD account will be the Deposit, Maintenance
Amount, Rent, Decreetal Amount(Civil). Expenditure out of the
Personal Deposit Account will be as per orders of the Courts.
No Revenue Receipt / Revenue Expenditure transactions shall be
booked in this PD.
On a monthly basis, the balances in this Personal Deposit
Account shall be reconciled with the Treasury Offices and yearly
reconciliation with A G office for all types of Personal Deposits.
At the end of each financial year, the balance in the PD may be
forwarded by the Treasury office to AG for confirmation.
The Finance Department GR dated 05.09.2023 regarding
operation of the PD/PLA should be followed.
All rules in Maharashtra Treasury will be applicable to this.
