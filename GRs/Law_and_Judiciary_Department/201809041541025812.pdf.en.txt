Government's decision:
Under the 14th Finance Commission Technical Manpower. The Government has accorded administrative approval for creation of 199 notional posts of Senior System Officers (40 posts) and System Officers (159 posts) for High Courts and other Courts to be filled up through external agencies by March, 2020 (till the end of the 14th Finance Commission).
A total of 199 vacancies of 40 Senior System Officer posts and 159 System Officer posts are to be filled.

(4) Conducting training of new Judges/ staff.
