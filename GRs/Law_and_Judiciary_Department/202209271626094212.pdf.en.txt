KEMPUYO-3621/ Q.Sr55/ Ka-13 dated 16th November, 2021 Preamble: With a view to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has issued Reference No. The order dated 23 rd March, 2021 has directed to adopt a revised methodology for disbursement of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes through Public Finance Management System. Accordingly, Finance Department vide Reference No. 2 The government circular here has given instructions regarding the use of Public Finance Management System for the distribution of funds for centrally sponsored schemes.
2. As per the said instructions, for the implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes under the Department of Law and Justice, Reference No. Under the decision of the government, a nodal officer and a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) has been appointed for every centrally sponsored scheme and their Single Nodal Agency (SNA) account has been opened in Stent Bank of India.
Further, as per the said instructions, it is mandatory to deposit the unutilized State funds and interest thereon at the end of the financial year in the account of the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme. Accordingly, the matter of opening a new accrual ledger for depositing the unaccounted funds and interest was under the consideration of the Government.
