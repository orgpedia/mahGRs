Pursuant to the Government decision dated 04.03.2022, 159 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Amaravati have been extended till 31.08.2022. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 159 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023.
