The Proposal:
The Hon'ble High Court of Delhi vide its judgment dated 22/09/2017 has directed to update the information of State Acts and Statutory Acts on the India Code Portal. In this regard, in a video conference held on 06/03/2020, the Cabinet Secretary, Rashtrapati Bhavan has directed the Director, Information Technology and Chief Nodal Officer to coordinate with all the nodal officers and take action on high priority and submit a performance report to the Government of Maharashtra in this regard. In this regard, Ma. The High Court vide order dated 24/01/2022 and 07/03/2022 has directed to take immediate action in the matter.
Accordingly, Ma. According to the affidavit submitted in the court, the functioning of the India Code portal needs to be made operational immediately. The posts of IT Assistant to be filled for the work of India Code Portal are classified as Skilled posts. Hence, the Finance Department.
As per the guidelines issued vide Government Resolution dated 27.04.2022, a proposal for creation of 10 notional posts for hiring of IT Assistant from external agencies for uploading of Acts, Rules etc. on India Code Portal was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In the above background, the Government is giving approval to create 10 notional posts of IT Assistant on the establishment of the Department of Law and Justice (itself) and take the services of these posts through an external mechanism for a period of 11 months.
