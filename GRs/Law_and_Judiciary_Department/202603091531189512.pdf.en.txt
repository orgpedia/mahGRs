The Proposal: -
Under the Maharashtra State Legal Services Authority, 792 temporary part-time posts have been created on the establishments of Taluka Legal Services Committees and these posts were extended for a period from 01.09.2025 to 28.02.2026 under Government Decision, Department of Legal Services, No.
In pursuance of the request made by the Member Secretary, Maharashtra State Legal Services Authority, Mumbai vide their letter dated 04.02.2026 regarding extension of the above mentioned posts, the Government was under consideration for extension of 792 temporary part-time posts in the establishments of Taluka Legal Services Committees under Maharashtra State Legal Services Authority. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision.
The Government's Decisions:
The Department of Finance vide Government Decision HUST 2016, / Q. 8/16 S.P.C., dated 28.02.2026 has issued a notification to all the Administrative Departments providing for temporary posts included in their profile as well as all existing temporary posts created after prior review approval but not included in the profile. The powers to extend are given from 01.03.2026 to 31.08.2026. Accordingly, 792 temporary part-time posts on the establishments of Taluka Legal Services Committees under Maharashtra State Legal Services Authority have been created. The deadline is being extended from 01.03.2026 to 31.08.2026.
