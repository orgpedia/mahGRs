The Proposal:
All India Judges Association Vs. Union of India, in Writ Petition (Civil) No. Interlocutory Application No. 1022/1989 279/2010. The Supreme Court vide its order dated 02.08.2018 has directed to provide basic and important facilities to all the court complexes.
Also, Ma. As per the directions issued by the Supreme Court, a status report is being prepared for the preparation of the development plan of the courts. The Chief Secretary of the state has to form a committee to present it to the court. The Supreme Court has given the verdict. Accordingly, a committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary was constituted.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the Supreme Court's order dated 02.08.2018. Preparation of Court Development Plan and Status Report as per the directions of the Supreme Court.
A Committee under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Government of Maharashtra is being constituted to submit to the Court:
