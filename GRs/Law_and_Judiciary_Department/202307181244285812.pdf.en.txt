Preamble: The Central Project Coordinator, e-Courts Project, Appellate Division, High Court, Mumbai, has issued a letter to the High Court of Bombay and its Bench regarding the procurement of 70 Zoom Licenses for running Courts through video conferencing. A proposal was submitted to the government. The proposal has been received from the Directorate of Information Technology and approved by the Project Implementation Committee of the Department in its meeting held on 30.06.2023. Further, non-recurring expenditure for the period from 03.09.2023 to 31.03.2024 on 70 Zoom Licenses for running Courts through Video Conferencing for the High Court of Bombay and its Benches has also been provided for under reference No. 5 Administrative approval has been given by the government decision here. Accordingly, the administrative approval for 70 Zoom Licenses from 01.04.2024 to 31.03.2025 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 70 Zoom Licenses for running Courts through Video Conferencing for Bombay High Court and its Benches for the period from 01.04.2024 to 31.03.2025 amounting to Rs. 15,23,474/- (Rs. Only 15 lakh twenty three thousand four hundred and seventy four) expenditure is being given administrative approval under this government decision.
