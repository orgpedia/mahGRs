Preamble- Mehkar, District Buldhana has two Joint Courts viz., the Court of the District Judge and the Court of Additional Sessions Judge and Civil Court at Senior Level with effect from 18.08.2013. However, posts of judges and staff were not created for these twin courts. At present, judges who have been posted in Buldhana have to go to Mehkar with their own staff for 15 days. Considering the number of pending claims. The Establishment of Courts Committee, constituted at the level of High Court of Bombay, has recommended to the Government to set up regular courts at District and Additional Sessions Court Mehkar and Civil Court Senior Level Mehkar instead of the existing District and Additional Sessions Court and Civil Court Senior Level at Mehkar, District Buldhana.
Accordingly, the District and Additional Sessions Court and Civil Court Senior Level at Mehkar, District Buldhana have been converted into regular District and Additional Sessions Courts instead of the existing Joint Courts.
