Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated March 11, 2022, 148 temporary posts on the establishment of District and Sessions Court Yavatmal have been extended till August 31, 2022. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 148 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023.
