The proposal: In Public Interest Litigation No. 216/2010 filed in the High Court, Bombay, Hon. In pursuance of the orders passed by the High Court, Mumbai, fire audit of all court buildings in the State is being carried out and fire fighting/ fire safety measures are being taken. In this regard, a proposal for the renovation of the firefighting system in the old and new building of the Small Disputes Court, Mumbai. High Court Referral No. 1 submitted to the Government. Maharashtra Fire Service, Mumbai. In pursuance of the measures suggested by the local Fire Officer as directed in letter No. 3, the budget for the year 2014-2015 has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Engineer, Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer and the Chief Justice, Small Causes Court, Mumbai. The Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai and the Finance Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai have agreed to the administrative approval of this budget. The Small Cause Court has informed that the cost can be met from the available grants as per Article 4. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the proposal for expenditure for implementing fire safety measures in the old and new buildings of the Small Causes Court, Mumbai, was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Fire fighting measures in old and new building of Small Cause Court, Mumbai Rs. Administrative approval is being accorded for expenditure/ budget amounting to Rs. 14,07,109/- (Rupees Fourteen Lakhs Seven Thousand One Hundred and Nine only). The details of the building are as follows:
