The Proposal:
Administrative approval has been accorded for establishment of Civil Court, Senior Level Court at Igatpuri, Dist. Nashik vide Government Order dated 09.11.2022. In pursuance of the Government Resolution dated 09.01.2023 in Reference No. 2, the approval of the Legislature has been obtained for the expenditure required for the said Court. The Civil Court, Senior Level, Igatpuri has become functional with effect from 22nd April, 2023 vide Notification No.3 dated 17.04.2023. As per the letter dated 4th and 5th A. As per the request made by the High Court, Bombay and District and Sessions Court, Nashik, the matter of declaring Civil Judge, Senior Level, Igatpuri, G. Nashik as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under the consideration of the Government.

Civil Judge, Senior Level, Igatpuri, G. Nashik is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for this Court. They are being authorised to deposit all the court dues in the treasury. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, dated 17th April, 2015, in Part I, Sub-Division No. 3 of Sub-Division 1 and in exercise of the powers conferred under Rules 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
