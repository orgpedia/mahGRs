Preamble: Reference No. 11 in 11 places where more than 1000 cases related to persons with disabilities, senior citizens and marginalized sections of the society are pending. Under the decision of the government, it has been approved to establish 11 special courts and create 55 temporary posts for these courts. Provisional posts for these 11 Special Courts. The extension is required for the next period from 01.03.2023.
Accordingly, the proposals have been received by the concerned District and Sessions Courts. Reference No. 4 As per the decision of the Government of Finance Department, w.e.f. 01.03.2023. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2023. Accordingly, the extension of these posts was under consideration.
Government's decision:
A total of 55 temporary posts in 11 Special Courts for Persons with Disabilities, Senior Citizens and Marginalized Sections of the Society have been created with effect from 01.03.2023. The period is being extended up to 31.08.2023.
