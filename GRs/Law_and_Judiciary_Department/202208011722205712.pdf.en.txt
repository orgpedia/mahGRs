Introduction: The cook. In order to avoid inconvenience to the Chief Justice of the Bombay High Court, the High Court Administration had submitted a proposal to the Government through a reference letter to create two posts of cooks on the establishment of the Appellate Division for the work of cooking at his residence. The proposal was referred to the Sub-Committee. It has been approved in the meeting dated 15/07/2022. Accordingly, the matter of approving the creation of the post was under the consideration of the government.
Government decision The Government hereby approve the creation of two posts of Cooks in the pay scale of S-1: 15000 47600 in the Appellate Division for the purpose of cooking at the official residence of the Chief Justice, High Court of Bombay.

4. Further, since the said two posts are temporary in nature, action should be taken accordingly as per the directions of the Finance Department from time to time regarding extension of temporary posts.
