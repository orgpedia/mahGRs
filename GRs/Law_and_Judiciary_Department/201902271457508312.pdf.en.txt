The Proposal- M. In Public Interest Litigation No. 216/2010 filed in the High Court of Bombay. As per the orders of the High Court of Bombay, fire audit of all court buildings in the state is being done and fire fighting/ fire prevention measures are being taken. Accordingly, vide letter dated 04.04.2018, Deputy Manager, Ma. The High Court of Bombay has submitted a proposal to the Government for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the District and Sessions Court Gondia and court buildings at Amgaon, Deori, Morgaon, Sadak Arjuni and Tiroda. The concerned officers of the Maharashtra Fire Service have suggested the fire prevention measures mentioned in the proposal and the Director, Directorate of Maharashtra Fire Service has agreed to carry out the work of installing the fire fighting system in the court building as per the recommendations given by them. In accordance with the measures suggested by the local fire officials, the budget on the table for the year 2017-18 has been testified by the concerned Executive Engineer of the Public Works Department and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Gondia. Accordingly, the matter of grant of administrative approval for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in District and Sessions Court Gondia and court buildings at Amgaon, Deori, Morgaon, Sadak Arjuni and Tiroda was under consideration of the Government.

GOVERNMENT DECISION The District and Sessions Court, Gondia and the Courts under its control have been directed to provide necessary infrastructure for installation of fire extinguishers/ fire safety measures. In pursuance of the proposals received from the High Court of Bombay, administrative approval is being accorded for the construction of fire fighting system/ fire fighting measures in the court buildings of District and Sessions Court Gondia and Amgaon, Deori, Morgaon, Sadak Arjuni and Tiroda at a cost of Rs. 1,14,42, 325/- (Rupees One Crore Fourteen Lakhs Forty Two Thousand Three Hundred and Twenty Five only) including 4% contingency cost and 12% provision for Goods and Services Tax.
