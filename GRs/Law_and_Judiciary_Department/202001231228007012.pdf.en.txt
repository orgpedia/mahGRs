The Proposal:
The Central Government under the National Mission for Safety of Women has made a provision for setting up of Special Fast Track Courts for speedy disposal of pending cases involving women and children. As per the action plan of the Mission, a total of 1023 Special Fast Track Courts are proposed to be set up across the country and out of these 1023 Special Fast Track Courts, 138 Special Fast Track Courts are proposed to be set up in the State of Maharashtra.
The scheme of" Setting up of Special Fast Track Courts" to implement the Action Plan of National Mission for Safety of Women is centrally sponsored and the share of Central and State Governments is fixed at 60:40 respectively.
Separate new head of accounts for expenditure for proposed 138 Special Fast Track Courts
Central Government Reference No. 1 has been reported. Accordingly, the matter of opening new separate head of accounts for the Central and State accounts was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The Government have decided to open new separate head of accounts for Central and State accounts for expenditure incurred on 138 Special Fast Track Courts under the National Mission for Safety of Women, as approved by the Planning Department, Finance Department and the Accountant General's Office.
