In pursuance of the above decision, the Government have approved the establishment of a District and Additional Sessions Court at Mangrulpir, District Washim and creation of a post for this Court. The government was considering declaring Justice 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Mangrul Peer, District Washim, as Drawing and Disbursing Officers.
The District and Additional Sessions Court at Mangrul Peer, District Washim has been declared as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 read with Finance Division No. 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated 17.04.2015.
