The Proposal:
In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government vide Memorandum dated 23.03.2021 has been directed to adopt revised procedures. Accordingly, revised instructions have been issued vide Finance Department, Government Circular dated 01.07.2021 regarding Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. As per the amended Notice No. 11 in Annexure A of the said Government Circular, the Central Government and the State Governments are required to have separate budget lines. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of separate accounts of the Central Government and the State Government under the programme for the Centrally Sponsored Gram Nyayalaya Scheme.
Government's decision:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Gram Nyayalayas (Gram Nyayalayas), separate accounts of the Central Government and the State Government are being opened as under:
Grant No. J-01 2014 Administration of Justice
105 Civil and Sessions Courts
(02) Civil and Sessions Judges

The two-digit indicative target headings under the said headings shall be as per the list of target headings in Annexure A vide Government Resolution dated 10.08.2010 at Reference No.1.
