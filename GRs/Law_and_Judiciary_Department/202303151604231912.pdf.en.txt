Gram Nyayalayas have been established in the State under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008. In order to ensure smooth functioning of these Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 115 temporary posts in 22 Gram Nyayalayas have been extended for the period from 1st September, 2022 to 28th February, 2023, as per the decision of the Government in this regard.
