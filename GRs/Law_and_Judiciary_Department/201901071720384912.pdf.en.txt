The above approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
(i) Compliance with the rules for granting administrative approval in the Maharashtra Public Works Department, rule book and the corresponding rules.
(ii) The repair work shall be carried out by the Public Works Department by appointing a contractor through the Administrative Departments, if the post of Executive Engineer or above is held by the concerned Administrative Departments, depending on the tender method prescribed.
(iii) Budgetary provisions should be available.
3. The cost of renovation of court buildings at District and Sessions Court Osmanabad and at Tuljapur, Umarga, Bhooma and Paranda under their control should be met from the approved provision under the head Demand No. J-1, 2014 Adjudication, (00) Adjudication, 102-High Courts, (11) Renovation of Working Courts, (11) (01) Renovation of Working Court (2014 0548).
3) Before the tender of the work. It shall be the responsibility of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Osmanabad to ensure that the above works fall within the framework prescribed by the High Court of Bombay and the works prescribed by the Dedicated Cell. The e-tendering process should be carried out by the Public Works Department following the prevailing procedure and other ancillary proceedings should be completed in accordance with this administrative approval.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the Public Works Department vide No. No. 254/2017/ Ima-2, dated 13.12.2017 and with the concurrence of the Department and subject to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Government Resolution, Law and Justice Department dated 01.01.2019.
