The Proposal:
High Court of Justice. As per the Standard Format dated 04.04.2007, the minimum number of pending cases/ cases for opening of new courts at various places in the State should be 500, along with infrastructure e.g. As all the criteria required as per the policy of the High Court are being fulfilled, the Government has decided to set up a Civil Court, Senior Level Court at Daund, G. Government's decision:
This Government Resolution approves establishment of Civil Court, Senior Division at Daund, District Pune and creation of 16 regular posts and taking over of 4 manpower services through external agencies from the date of commencement of the Court as indicated in the following statement.
