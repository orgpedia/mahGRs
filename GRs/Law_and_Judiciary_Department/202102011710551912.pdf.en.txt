The Proposal:
There are a total of 75 courts of the Metropolitan Magistrate under the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai and they are functioning in 16 court centres in Mumbai. Out of these, PLA accounts are operational in 13 judicial centres. The PLA did not open its account in the three judicial centres. In such a situation, the amount of bail, fine, and other complex amounts collected daily in the three court centers of Shindewadi, Vile Parle, and Children's Court, Dongri, are accepted in large amounts in cash. Therefore, the government was considering to approve the opening of PLA account in three court centers- Shindewadi, Vile Parle and Children's Court, Dongri.
Government's decision:
Metropolitan Magistrate, Shindewadi Court, Vile Parle Court and Juvenile Court, Dongri, Mumbai
As per Rule 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the opening of a Civil Private Partnership Account for the amount of bail, fine, and other miscellaneous amounts deposited in the three courts of the Metropolitan Magistrate, Shindewadi, Vile Parle and Juvenile Court, Dongri, Mumbai is being approved subject to the following conditions:
i. The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
ii. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
iii. The amount ordered by the Court to the concerned / opposite parties to be deposited as Civil Court Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA and payment due may be made as per the order of the court.
iv. No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions will appear in this PLA.
v. Either on quarterly or monthly basis, balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the court.
vi. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this PLA.
vii. PAO Mumbai will issue an authority to open PLA on receipt of Government orders.
