Introduction: Refer No. As per the decisions of the government here, guidelines have been issued regarding the implementation of the competitive examination process in the online mode (Computer Programme Based Test/ Examination) to fill the posts of Group B (Non-Gazetted), Group C and Group D in the nomination quota under the purview of the Ex-Secondary Selection Board (outside the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission). As per the decisions of the Government, it has been advised to open a separate bank account by selecting a bank as per the guidelines of the Finance Department for depositing the fees received from the candidates for the competitive examination.
Accordingly, reference No. As per the letter, the government has received a proposal to approve the opening of a current account in the name of the Chairman, State Level Selection Committee and Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai in the State Bank of India, Nirlon House, Dr. Annie Besant Road, Worli, Mumbai branch to deposit the examination fees received from the candidates for conducting competitive examinations through the company Tata Consultancy Services Limited to fill the vacancies in the nomination quota outside the purview of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the offices under the charitable organization under this department. Action was being taken accordingly.
Government's decision:
The Chairman, State Level Selection Committee to deposit the examination fee received from the candidates for conducting competitive examination through Tata Consultancy Services Limited for filling up the vacancies in the nomination quota in the purview of Ex-Secondary Selection Board (outside the purview of Maharashtra Public Service Commission) in the offices under charitable organizations.

The Commissioner of Charitable Endowments, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is permitted to open a current account in the name of the Commissioner of Charitable Endowments, State Bank of India, Nirlon House, Dr. Annie Besant Road, Worli, Mumbai, subject to the following conditions:
1) The current account opened under the charitable organization should be used only for depositing the amount of examination fee received from the candidates in connection with the competitive examination conducted by the TCS company.
(2) The amount of interest in the bank account must be adjusted by the government.
3) Monthly/ quarterly reconciliation of transactions in the said bank account and audit will be mandatory.
4) The funds should be withdrawn from the treasury as soon as necessary for immediate utilization so that the funds do not remain unutilized for a long time in the said bank account.
5) In case of closure of the said bank account, the balance should be reconciled and the balance should be deposited immediately under the appropriate government account head.
6) Follow the rules of finance.
2. This Government Decision is based on the Informal Reference No. Anusam 197/23/ COP. being issued as per the approval received on 02.08.2023.
