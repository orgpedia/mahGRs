The Proposal- M. In Public Interest Litigation No. 216/2010 filed in the High Court of Bombay. As per the orders of the High Court of Bombay, fire audit of all court buildings in the state is being done and fire fighting/ fire prevention measures are being taken. Accordingly, vide letter dated 26.08.2015, the Deputy Manager, Shri. The Bombay High Court has submitted a proposal to the government for setting up of firefighting system/ fire prevention measures in the court buildings of District and Sessions Court Osmanabad and Chief Judicial Magistrate Osmanabad, District Law and Services Authority Osmanabad, Bhoomi, Umarga, Tuljapur, Kalamb, Paranda and Vashi. The concerned officers of the Maharashtra Fire Service have suggested the fire prevention measures mentioned in the proposal and the Director, Directorate of Maharashtra Fire Service has agreed to carry out the work of installing the fire fighting system in the court building as per the recommendations given by them. In accordance with the measures suggested by the local fire officials, the budget for 2017-18 has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer and Principal District and Sessions Judge, Osmanabad. Accordingly, the District and Sessions Court Osmanabad and Chief

GOVERNMENT DECISION The District and Sessions Court, Osmanabad and the Courts under its control have been directed to take steps for installation of fire extinguishers/ fire safety measures. In pursuance of the proposals received from the High Court of Bombay, administrative approval is being accorded for the construction of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the court buildings of District and Sessions Court, Osmanabad and Chief Judicial Magistrate, Osmanabad, District Legal Services Authority, Osmanabad, Bhoom, Umarga, Tuljapur, Kalamb, Paranda and Vashi at a cost of Rs. 4,01,34, 640/- (Rupees Four Crores One Lakh Thirty Four Thousand Six Hundred and Forty Four only) including 4% contingency cost and 12% provision for Goods and Services Tax.
