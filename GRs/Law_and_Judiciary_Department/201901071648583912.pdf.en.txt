Introduction- Chalisgaon, District Jalgaon. With reference from 26/8/2018 No. As per the notification dated 21/8/2018, the Court of Civil Judge, (Senior Level), Chalisgaon, G. Jalgaon has become functional. Reference No. 2 is mentioned here. As per the Government Order dated 3/10/2018, Civil Judge (Sr. Level) Chalisgaon, G. Jalgaon has been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. On 30/8/2018, the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Jalgaon has requested for approval to open a Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Chalisgaon, G. Jalgaon. Accordingly, the matter of approval for opening of Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge, (Senior Level) Chalisgaon, G. Jalgaon was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra Treasury Rules, in the name of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Chalisgaon, G. Jalgaon.
As per Rule No.512 of 1968, opening of Civil Private Accounts Account for deposits and repayments from the Court of Civil Judge (Senior Level), Chalisgaon, G. Jalgaon.
Acceptance is subject to the following conditions:
(i) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
(ii) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
(iii) The amount ordered by the Court to be deposited as Civil Courts Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA.
(iv) No revenue receipt/Revenue expenditure transactions will appear in this PLA.
(v) Either on quarterly or monthly basis, balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the court.
