२. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit
Account.
३. For Civil Court Deposit, the credit to the Personal Deposit Account will be
the amounts deposited by a party to the litigation as per the order of the Court, in
Civil matters and the expenditure out of the Personal Deposit Accounts will be as
per courts order.
For the Criminal Court Deposit, the credit to the Personal Deposit Account
will be the amounts deposited by a party to the litigation as per the order of the
Court, in Criminal matters and the expenditure out of the Personal Deposit
Accounts will be as per courts order.
४. No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions shall be booked in
this PD.
५. The balance in this Personal Deposit Account shall be reconciled by the
Court on Monthly basis with the District Treasury office.
६. The balance have to be reconciled with AG office and confirmation of
Balances is to be submitted by the Treasury officer to AG office at the end of the
financial year.
७. The Finance Department GR dated ५.०९.२०२३ regarding operation of the
PD/PLA should be followed.
८. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this Personal
Deposit Account.
