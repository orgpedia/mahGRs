Preamble: In pursuance of the above, the Government have accorded administrative approval for establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court at Vadgaon-Maval, Pune vide Government Order dated 05.02.2021 in A. No. In pursuance of the Government Order dated 26.03.2021 in Reference No. 2, the funds required for the said Court have been sanctioned.
As per the notification dated 31.01.2022, District and Additional Sessions Court, Vadgaon-Maval has become functional with effect from 06.01.2022. As per the request made by the District Court, Vadgaon-Maval vide letters dated 4, 5 and 6 dated 01.04.2016, the matter of declaring the District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Vadgaon Maval, Pune as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
The District Judge-1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Vadgaon Maval, Pune for District and Additional Sessions Court, Vadgaon-Maval, G. Pune are being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." They are being authorised to deposit all the court dues in the treasury. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Resolution is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, dated 17th April, 2015, in Part I, Sub-Division No. 3 of Sub-Division 1 and in exercise of the powers conferred under Rules 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
