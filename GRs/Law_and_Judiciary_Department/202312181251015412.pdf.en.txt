Preamble: General Administration Department, Reference No. As per Rule 10 (4) and Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, in the public interest, a consolidated procedure has been prescribed for premature retirement of officers/ employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity by reviewing their service at the age of 50/55 years or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier. Accordingly, the office of the Registrar Partnership Society, Maharashtra State, Mumbai has requested to constitute a Review Committee and a Grievance Committee in respect of the review of services of the employees of the office of the Registrar Partnership Society, Maharashtra State, Mumbai under this department. 2 letter to the government. According to reference no. Under the Government decision, a Review Committee has been constituted to review the service of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees of the Office of the Registrar Partnership Institutions beyond the age of 50/55 years/ after 30 years of qualifying service.
Now the reference No. As per the recommendation of the Review Committee as per Schedule 4 of Annexure-D of the Government Resolution here, the Government was considering the formation of a Committee to consider the grievances against the decision of premature retirement after review of service.
Government Decision: A Grievance Committee is being constituted to consider the grievances of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees of the Office of the Registrar Partnership Institutions against the decision of premature retirement after review of service beyond the age of 50/55 years/ 30 years of qualifying service.
