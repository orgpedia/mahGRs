For the purpose of" Strengthening the Justice Sector" recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, a fund of Rs. 1014 crore has been earmarked for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, a sum of Rs.228 crore has been earmarked for the renovation of the functional courts for a period of five years. A High Level Steering Committee has been constituted on 9th March, 2016 for effective implementation of the recommendations of the Commission. At the meeting, The Action Plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle and under the Government Order dated 12th June, 2017, the Administrative Department has been empowered to grant administrative approval for the repair works of the Court Building under the 14th Central Finance Commission with the concurrence of the Finance Department to the extent of Rs. Accordingly, the proposal for renovation of courts at Arni and Kalamb under the control of District and Sessions Court, Yavatmal, in the Schedule of 2017-18 is placed at Reference No.3 above.

Accordingly, the administrative approval for the renovation works under the 14th Central Finance Commission of the court buildings in Arni and Kalamb, District Yavatmal under the control of the District and Sessions Court Yavatmal was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision- Redesigning of Existing Courts in the State under the 14th Central Finance Commission. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for the renovation of Court Building at Arni under the control of District and Sessions Court, Yavatmal at a cost of Rs. 48,08,000/- (Rupees Forty Eight Lakhs Eight Thousand only) and for the renovation of Court Building at Kalamb at a cost of Rs. 48,08,000/- (Rupees Forty Eight Lakhs Eight Thousand only).
