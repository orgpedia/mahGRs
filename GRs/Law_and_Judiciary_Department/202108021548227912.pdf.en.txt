The Proposal:
In pursuance of the aforesaid letter No. 1, a proposal has been submitted for renovation of Jail Court at Barrack No. 12C on the ground floor of Arthur Road Jail, Mumbai. He is lodged in Mumbai Central Jail. Administrative approval was sought for renovation of 12 Court Halls (Adjudication Hall, Toilet, Drainage Line, Paint Works etc.) at an estimated cost of Rs. 49,49,578/- and construction of Furniture Supply and Leakage Prevention Shed for Adjudication Hall at an estimated cost of Rs. 32,98,918/-.
