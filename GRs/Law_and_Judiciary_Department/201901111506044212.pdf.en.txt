Establishment of the court is recognized as a" special case." Accordingly, a proposal was received vide letter No. 1. When the proposal was submitted to the Finance Department, a high-level committee of secretaries approved the establishment and creation of the court in a circular manner. Therefore, it was under the consideration of the government to set up the said court and create the necessary 20 posts (18 posts regular and 2 posts through external agencies) for it.
Government Decision: Approval is being given to establish a Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court for the Taluka area residing at Rahta in Ahmednagar district and to create 20 posts (18 posts regular and 2 posts by external agency) for the same from the date of commencement of the Court as indicated in the following prospectus.
