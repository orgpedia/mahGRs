Preamble: For the renovation of the court building at the District and Sessions Court, Thane, under the Government Order No. 1 referred to above, Rs. The administrative approval has been granted at a price of Rs 3,29,47, 340.00. But as construction costs increased, so did the cost of construction. The High Court, vide letter No. 2, has submitted to the Government a proposal for revised administrative sanction for the construction of the said building. Accordingly, the proposal was under the consideration of the government to give administrative approval for the amendment.
Government's decision:
Rs. The revised administrative approval is being accorded by this Government Resolution for the works estimated to cost Rs. 4,89,30, 669/- (Rupees Four Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Thirty Three Thousand Six Hundred and Seventy-Nine only) (as per details enclosed below).

The revised Administrative Sanction is being granted subject to the following conditions: (a) The conditions contained in the original Administrative Sanction shall continue to apply.
(b) When dealing with a provision made in a lump-sum manner, the work should be taken up only after making a detailed budget.
c) Necessary permissions before and during the work should be obtained from the local bodies.
d) In the actual working of the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013/ Q.No.177/ T.C.1, D. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in January 10, 2014.
e) No further changes to the work under the proposal shall be made.
e) Changes/ additions to the work under the proposal should be made in the prescribed manner. Otherwise, action will be taken against the concerned officials.
3. Expenditure on this request No. Capital Expenditure on H-8, 4059 Public Works- 01- Office Buildings- 51 Construction- (03) NYAYDAN Centrally Sponsored Schemes (State Share)- (03) (01) Large Constructions (Programme)-53 Large Constructions (40590672) and (21) NYAYDAN Centrally Sponsored Schemes (Central Share)- (21) (01) Large Constructions (Programme)-53 Large Constructions (40591222). However, the work should not be started without making a provision in the budget.
