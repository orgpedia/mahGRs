The above approval is being granted subject to the following conditions i. The Maharashtra Public Works Department, the rules for granting administrative approval in the rule book and the related rules should be complied with.
(ii) The repair work shall be carried out by the Public Works Department by appointing a contractor through the Administrative Departments, if the post of Executive Engineer or above is held by the concerned Administrative Departments, depending on the tender method prescribed.
(iii) Budgetary provisions should be available.
3. The cost of renovation of court buildings at Badnapur under the control of District and Sessions Court Jalna shall be met from the provision approved under the head" Demand No. J-1, 2014 Nyaydaan, (00) Nyaydaan, 102-High Courts, (11) Renovation of functional courts, (11) (01) Renovation of functional court (2014 0548)."
3) Before the tender of the work. It shall be the responsibility of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Jalna, to ensure that the above works fall within the framework prescribed by the High Court of Bombay and the works prescribed by the Dedicated Cell. The e-tendering process should be carried out by the Public Works Department following the prevailing procedure and other ancillary proceedings should be completed in accordance with this administrative approval.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the Public Works Department vide No. No. 76/2017/ Ima-2, dated 05.01.2018 and with the concurrence of the Department and subject to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Government Resolution, Law and Justice Department dated 01.01.2019.
