The Proposal:
Municipal Civil and Sessions Court, Dindoshi, Goregaon, Mumbai. A self-financed account has been opened on 27/4/2010. Criminal and civil deposits are being paid into this account. However, having a single account makes it difficult to balance the cost of criminal and civil deposits. Also, the number of transactions has increased. the Civil and Criminal
Two separate accounts are required for smooth functioning of the administration. The current account no. 6184121014 to be allowed to be used only for City Civil Court, Dindoshi and to allow opening of a separate self-financing account for City Sessions Court, Dindoshi
The request was made by the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai, through a reference letter. Accordingly, the matter of opening a separate criminal private limited account for the Municipal Sessions Court, Dindoshi was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
As per Rule 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the opening of a criminal personal loan account for the deposit of criminal deposits in the Court of City Civil and Sessions Court, Didonshi, Goregaon, Mumbai, in the name of the Manager, Sessions Court, Dindoshi, Goregaon, Mumbai, is being approved subject to the following conditions:
i. The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
ii. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
iii. The amount ordered by the Court to the concerned / opposite parties to be deposited as Civil Court Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA and payment due may be made as per the order of the court.
iv. No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions will appear in this PLA.
v. Either on quarterly or monthly basis, balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the court.
vi. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this PLA.
vii. PAO Mumbai will issue an authority to open PLA on receipt of Government orders.
