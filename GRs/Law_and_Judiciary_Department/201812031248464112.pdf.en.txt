As per the notification dated 01.04.2017, the Court of the District Judge and Additional Sessions Judge at Hinganghat, District Wardha has been functioning with effect from 03.04.2017. As per the Government Order dated 20.06.2017, District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Hinganghat and Civil Judge Senior Level Hinganghat have been declared as Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. On 08.08.2018, Principal District and Sessions Judge, Wardha has requested for approval for opening of Personal Accounts Account for the Court of District Judge and Additional Sessions Judge. Accordingly, the matter of approval to open a Personal Accounts Account for the court of the District and Additional Sessions Judge was under consideration of the Government.
The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to audit in accordance with Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Clause 1, for depositing amounts in the District and Additional Sessions Court, Hinganghat, District Wardha subject to the following conditions: 1.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3) The amount ordered by the Court to be deposited as Civil Court Deposit / Criminal Court Deposits / MACT Court Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA.
