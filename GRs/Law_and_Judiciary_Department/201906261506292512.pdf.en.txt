The Proposal:
For the purpose of Strengthening the Justice System recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, an amount of Rs. 1014 crore has been recommended for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Renovation of the Court functioning under the 14th Finance Commission for a period of 5 years for this component. 228 crore. Implementation of the recommendations of the 14th Finance Commission. A High Level Monitoring Committee has been constituted on 9/3/2016. At the meeting of the committee. The action plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle. Also with the approval of the Department of Finance. 12/6/2017
As per the decision of the Government of the day, the Administrative Department has been empowered to grant administrative approval to the extent of Rs 50 lakh for the repair works of the court building under the 14th Finance Commission.
Reference No. 1 of the Hon'ble High Court vide letter No. 1 has received the budget for the repair/ renovation of the Court Building at Mazgaon (Sewri) under the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai. This is for the period 2018-2019. The budget in this proposal has been prepared by the Branch Engineer, Public Works Department, Parel Division and has been attested by the concerned Deputy Engineer as well as the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai. And Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has agreed to the administrative approval after examining the said budget. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the said work budget at Mazgaon (Sewri) under the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai was under consideration.
