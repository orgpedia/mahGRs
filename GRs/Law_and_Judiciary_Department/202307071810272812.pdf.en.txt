Preamble: The number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be at least 500. Availability of court buildings etc. It appears that all the necessary criteria as per the policy of the High Court are being fulfilled. The High Court of Bombay has recommended the establishment of a court of District and Additional Sessions Judge at Warud, District Amravati. Accordingly, the Government was considering the establishment of a District and Additional Sessions Court at Varud, District Amravati.
Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
The government has decided to set up a district and additional sessions court in Warud, Amravati district. Also, the Government is approving the creation of posts and hiring of manpower through external agencies for this Court as indicated below:
