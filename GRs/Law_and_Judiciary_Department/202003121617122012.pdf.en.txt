A total of 99 temporary posts in the Special Court have been notified vide Reference No. 13 decision of the government. The period was extended till 29.02.2020. Proposals for further extension of these 99 temporary posts have been received by the Government. Reference No. 14 decision of the Department of Finance. 01.03.2020 to dt. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2020. Accordingly, the government was considering extending the tenure of a total of 99 temporary posts in 14 special courts.
Government's decision:
A total of 99 temporary posts have been created in the establishment of special courts in 14 districts of Mumbai, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Dhule, Jalna, Latur, Nanded, Nashik, Osmanabad, Parbhani, Sangli, Satara, Solapur and Wardha to deal with cases of atrocities against women. 01.03.2020 to dt. The period is being extended up to 31.08.2020.
