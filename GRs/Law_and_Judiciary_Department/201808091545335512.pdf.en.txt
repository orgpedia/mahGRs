The Proposal:
As per the proposal of the Hon'ble High Court, the Government have decided to establish a Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Khed in Ratnagiri district and to create a post for the same. CRC- 0417/318/ Q. No.135/ Ka-11, Dy. Approved on 9/5/2018. The orders regarding the availability of funds in this government decision have been issued separately.
It was said that it would be done. Accordingly, a symbolic supplementary demand for this Court was submitted to the Legislature through the Finance Department in the Monsoon Session of July 2018.
It's been approved. Accordingly, the proposal to provide funds for this court was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Civil Judge (Senior Level) at Khed in Ratnagiri District
