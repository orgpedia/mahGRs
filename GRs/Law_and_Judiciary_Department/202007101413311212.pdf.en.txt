Introduction: The Structural Audit of the building of the Charity Commissioner's Office was conducted by VJTI (Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute), Matunga, Mumbai in connection with the dilapidated and dangerous building of the Charity Commissioner's Office. 01.09.2018. According to the report, the present condition of the building of the Charity Commissioner's House is dangerous and the building needs to be redeveloped and the office of the Charity Commissioner's House should be shifted to another suitable place within the next six months. When the Charity Commissioner's Office requested the General Administration Department to provide 25,000 square feet of government vacancies, the Government Building in the Mumbai area did not have enough space at present. Space cannot be provided. However, the administrative department has been given full powers regarding the approval of renting and providing rent for the office as per the Financial Powers Rules. vide letter dated 25.03.2019. Also, Mr. Khushru Ratanshah Zaiwala has filed a complaint against the Maharashtra Government and others regarding the dilapidated and dangerous building of the Charity Commissioner's Office. Public Interest Litigation in the High Court of Bombay (No. 49/2019 (St. (a) has been entered. Accordingly, the matter of renting a place for the office of the Charity Commissioner was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNOR'S DECISION- The following decision has been taken regarding the renting of space for the office of the Charity Commissioner. Office of the Charity Commissioner at Ramnord House, 77, Dr. Annie Besant Marg, Worli, Mumbai- 400 018 (C. S. No. 936) to make available to the owners of Ramnord House, a total area of 25500 square feet including the ground floor, first floor and second floor staircase and elevator in this building for a period of three years from the date of possession and for which a monthly allowance of Rs. Government approval is being granted for an amount of Rs. 51,00,000/- (Rupees Fifty One Lakh only)+ GST. An agreement will be signed with the government in this regard.
