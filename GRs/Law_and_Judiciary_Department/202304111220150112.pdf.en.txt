Preamble- In pursuance of the above decision, the Government have approved the establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court and Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Kalamb, District Osmanabad and creation of posts for these courts. The District and Additional Sessions Court at Kalamb, District Osmanabad has been made functional with effect from 05.03.2023 vide Government Notification dated 01.03.2023 in respect of S. No. 2, 3, 4& 5. Accordingly, vide letter dated 29th March, 2023 to the Manager, Observations (1), Ma. Based on the request made by the High Court of Bombay, the Government was considering declaring District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Kalamb, District Osmanabad as Drawing and Disbursing Officers.

The District and Additional Sessions Court, District Kalamb, District Osmanabad is being declared as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 read with Finance Division No. 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated 17.04.2015.
