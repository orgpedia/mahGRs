INTRODUCTION A request for creation of a notional post of Driver for the vehicle meant for District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Sindhudurg, District Sindhudurg and sanction for availing the services of these posts through an external agency was made by the Assistant Manager, (Inspections), Ma. The reference was made by the High Court of Bombay vide letter dated 03.08.2019. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of a fictitious post of a driver for District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Sindhudurg and the government's approval to make the services of this post available on a contract basis through an external agency.
The government has taken the following decisions:

Government Decision Government Decision The Finance Department vide its order dated 09.06.2017 has decided to grant Rs. In the fourteenth meeting of the sub-committee of the Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department and Secretary (Expenditure) Finance Department held on August 27, 2021, at 11 am, a proposal to make the services of a driver for the District and Sessions Court Sindhudurg available through an external mechanism was put for consideration. As per the approval of the sub-committee, the government is approving the creation of a fictitious post of a driver for District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Sindhudurg District Sindhudurg and making the services of these posts available through external agencies on a contractual basis. The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the head Demand No. 1, 2014 Adjudication, (00) 105 Civil and Sessions Courts, (02) District and Sessions Judge, (02) (01) District and Sessions Judge, (2014 0172) Datta Mat, 10 Contractual Services.
(2) In the case of filling up of these posts on contractual basis by external agencies, it shall be mandatory to comply with the guidelines issued in this regard from time to time.
The said Government Resolution is being issued under the Informal Reference No. 245/2021/ APUC, dated 10th August, 2021 of the Department of Finance with the concurrence of the Department.
