Preamble In pursuance of the Government notification dated 29th September, 2009, vide No. 1 of the above mentioned Notification, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Haveli in Pune district. Further, as per the Government Notification dated 17th August, 2015 in Reference No. 2, the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Mulshi Male in Pune district has been approved. In pursuance of the above mentioned Government Decision dated 13th February, 2015 mentioned in A.No.3, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. District Court Pune has requested District Judge 3 Pune to declare the Controlling Officer of the Scheme and Magistrate Gram Nyayalay Ulikanchan Haveli and Male Mulshi (Court No. 7) District Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the Scheme as the Fund will be disbursed through PFMS system for Gram Nyayalay. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare District Judge 3, Pune as Controlling Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for both Gram Nyayalaya Mulshi Male and Ulikanchan Haveli at Pune; and Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Ulikanchan Haveli and Male Mulshi (Court No.7), District Pune as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme.
