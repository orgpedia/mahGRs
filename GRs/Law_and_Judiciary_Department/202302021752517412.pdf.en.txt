Introduction- District and Sessions Court Beed has requested to create eight fictitious posts of conductors for the court building and to allow the services of these posts to be taken on contract basis by an external agency. The High Court of Bombay vide its letter dated 20th May, 2022 has made the reference. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of eight temporary posts for the building of the District and Sessions Court, Beed, and the government's approval to take the services of these posts on a contract basis through an external agency. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision
The Government has approved the creation of a total of eight temporary posts of Evacuees for the building of District and Sessions Court, Beed and to make available the services of these posts through external agencies on contract basis. The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the head of account" Demand No. J1, 2014 Adjudication, (00) 105 Civil and Sessions Courts, (02) District and Sessions Judge, (02) (01) District and Sessions Judge, (20140172) Datta Mat, 10- Contractual Service."
