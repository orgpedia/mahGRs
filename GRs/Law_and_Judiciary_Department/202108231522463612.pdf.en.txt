Government's decision:
Civil Judge-Senior Level, Karad, G. Satara Named as Maharashtra Treasury
As per Rule No. 512 of the Rules, 1968, the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level, Karad, G. Satara, has been designated as the Criminal Court for the purpose of collection of deposits and repayment of dues.
The opening of a PPF Account is allowed subject to the following conditions:
(i) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
(ii) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
(iii) The amount ordered by the Court to the concerned/opposite parties
to be deposited as Civil /Criminal Court Deposit should only be
deposited in this PLA and payment due may be made as per the order of the court.
