The revised administrative approval is being granted subject to the following conditions: 1) The sample map, layout map and detailed map should be approved by the architect before starting the construction.
2) As the said budget is a sample budget, while technically approving the detailed budgets of these works, the The provisions should be finalised with the concurrence of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Yavatmal.
3) The tender notice for the work should be published after receiving the certificate that the planned place is in the possession of the consumer department.
(4) In the process of tendering the work, one tender should be issued for all the works mentioned together without separate tenders for each work.
Before starting the work, the approval of all the concerned local bodies/ authorities should be taken.
6) When working directly with the Department of Environment, Government Decision, No. ENV-2013/ Q. No.177/ T. Action shall be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in the Notice dated 10th January, 2014.
7) Public Works Department, Government Decision, No. Action to be taken under BDG 2017/ PR No. 60/ Building-2, dated 7th July, 2017.
8) This work should be completed at the cost of revised administrative approval.
9) Don't change your budget.
10) The scope of work should not be changed or new works should not be included on the basis of oral instructions given by the engineer in the Public Works Department.
11) If new works are to be included or the scope is to be changed, then action should be taken to get approval for these works in the prescribed time and manner by obtaining a written order in this regard.
Otherwise, the responsibility will be fixed on the concerned officials.
The existing government decisions, rules, CVC principles, financial limits, prescribed tender procedures should be followed while implementing the scheme.
The Public Works Department will be responsible for fulfilling the directions and shortcomings given by the State Technical Advisory Committee in connection with the project and work, as per the requirement.
(14) Technical aspects of the revised administrative approval shall not be taken into account for the approval of regional decisions or irregularities in the tender. It shall be the responsibility of the Public Works Department to investigate such matter in accordance with the prevailing government decision of the time.
