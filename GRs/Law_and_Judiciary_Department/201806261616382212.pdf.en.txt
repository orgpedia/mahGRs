This is requested. In pursuance of this request, the Government was considering sanction of grant to Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar through Shri Saibaba Sansthan, Shirdi for construction of new classrooms in Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar.
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of Section 17 (2) (n) of the Shree Saibaba Sansthan Trustee System (Shirdi) Act, 2004, a grant of Rs.10 crore is being sanctioned in the first phase to the Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar for construction of new school rooms by the Shree Saibaba Sansthan subject to the following conditions:
1) The above sanctioned funds should be made available to Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar through Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi.
The construction of schools under the Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar should be done by the Public Works Department, Ahmednagar.
2) The funds should be transferred to the Public Works Department, Ahmednagar, as per the progress of the work through the Zilla Parishad, Ahmednagar.
The selection of schools from the list submitted by the District Collector, Ahmednagar should be done by the local committee of Shri Saibaba Sansthan.
