The proposal-
For the purpose of" Strengthening the Justice Sector" recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, a fund of Rs. 1014 crore has been earmarked for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, a sum of Rs.228 crore has been earmarked for the renovation of the functional courts for a period of five years. A High Level Steering Committee has been constituted on 9th March, 2016 for effective implementation of the recommendations of the Commission. In the meeting held on 28.03.2016, Shri. The action plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle. As per the Government decision dated 12.01.2018, a total of 91 buildings have been taken up for repair and renovation works to the extent of Rs.50 lakhs which were approved in the meeting of High Level Control Committee held on 08.12.2017. With the approval of the Chief Minister, the power to grant administrative approval has been given to the Department of Law and Justice as an administrative department subject to conditions.

The above approval is being granted subject to the following conditions i. The Maharashtra Public Works Department, the rules for granting administrative approval in the rule book and the related rules should be complied with.
(ii) Financial provisions should be available.
3. The cost of renovation of court buildings at Ambajogai, Parli, Kej, and Gevrai under the control of District and Sessions Court Beed should be met from the approved provision under the head Demand No. J-1, 2014 Nyayadan, (00) Nyayadan, 102-High Courts, (11) Renovation of Working Courts, (11) (01) Renovation of Working Court (2014 0548).
3) Before the tender of the work. It shall be the responsibility of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Beed, to ensure that the above works fall within the scope of the action plan fixed by the High Court of Bombay and the works fixed by the Dedicated Cell. The e-tendering process should be carried out by the Public Works Department following the prevailing procedure and other ancillary proceedings should be completed in accordance with this administrative approval.
This Government Resolution is being issued vide No. No. 216/2017/ Ima-2, Public Works Department, dated 07.08.2017 and with the concurrence of the Department and subject to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Government Resolution, Law and Justice Department, dated 12.01.2018.
