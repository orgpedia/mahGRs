NO. 10/ K. 9, dated 12th January, 2018 Letter No. B (W) 6049 of 2010 1923/17170/21- 7-16 dated 21st July, 2016 to the Manager (Observations 2) Preamble- The 14th Central Finance Commission has recommended a fund of Rs.1014 crore for the State of Maharashtra for the period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020 for the purpose of Strengthening the Justice Sector. Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, a sum of Rs.228 crore has been earmarked for the renovation of the functional courts for a period of five years. A High Level Steering Committee has been constituted on 9th March, 2016 for effective implementation of the recommendations of the Commission. In the meeting held on 28.03.2016, Shri. The action plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle. As per the Government decision dated 12.01.2018, a total of 91 buildings have been taken up for repair and renovation works to the extent of Rs.50 lakhs which were approved in the meeting of High Level Control Committee held on 08.12.2017. With the approval of the Chief Minister, the power to grant administrative approval has been given to the Department of Law and Justice as an administrative department subject to conditions.

Proposal for renovation of courts at Gevrai, Parli, Ashti, Kej, Dharur, Patoda and Ambajogai under the control of District and Sessions Court, Beed in the year 2015-16. The Bombay High Court has given its verdict.
The budget has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Divisional Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer and Principal District and Sessions Judge Beed. The proposal has been approved by the High-Level Committee in its meeting held on 08.12.2017 as the proposals of the said Courts on other posts are being considered and are exceeding the limit of Rs.50 lakhs. Accordingly, the Government was considering granting administrative approval for the renovation works under the 14th Central Finance Commission of the court buildings in Gevrai, Parli, Ashti, Kej, Dharur, Patoda and Ambajogai, District Beed under the control of District and Sessions Court Beed.
Government Decision- Redesigning of Existing Courts in the State under the 14th Central Finance Commission. 31,20,000/- (Rupees Thirty One Lakh Twenty Thousand only) for renovation of Court Building at Gevrai, District and Sessions Court, Beed, Rs. 52,05,643/- (Rupees Fifty Two Lakh Five Thousand Six Hundred and Forty Three only) for renovation of Court Building at Parli, Rs. 53,07,066/- (Rupees Fifty Three Lakh Seven Thousand Sixty Six only) for renovation of Court Building at Ashti, Rs. 53,32,083/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakh Thirty Two Thousand Eighty Three Only) for renovation of Court Building at Kej, Mumbai and Rs. 53,07,066/- (Rupees Thirty Three Lakh Six Thousand Sixty Six only) for renovation of Court Building at Gevrai, Beed.
