Preamble: A total of 24 Fast Track Courts have been set up under the 14th Finance Commission. For these 24 fast track courts, the post has been created as per the decision of the Government at Reference No. 2. The temporary posts for these 24 fast track courts are required to be extended for a further period. As per the Government decision in reference No. 1 regarding temporary posts of the court, the Finance Department has issued a notification. The provision has been omitted from the Government Decision dated 10.9.2001. Further, pursuant to the decision of the Government of Finance Department in Reference No. 14, 01.03.2020 to dt. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2020. Accordingly, the government was considering the continuation of the temporary posts for the 24 fast-track courts.
