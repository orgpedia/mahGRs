Preamble - Junnar, District Pune at Reference No. 1 vide Notification No. The District and Additional Sessions Court has come into operation with effect from 16.02.2025. The District Judge-I and the Additional Sessions Judge, Junnar, District Pune have been declared as the Head of Office and the Drawing and Disbursing Officer by the Government Decision at Reference H.2. Reference No. Letters 3 and 4 request permission to open a separate personal deposit account for criminal claims amounts for the District and Additional Sessions Court, Junnar, District Pune. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to allow the District and Additional Sessions Court, Junnar, District Pune to open a separate Personal Deposit Account for criminal claim amounts.
Government Judgment: The District and Additional Sessions Court, Junnar, for District and Additional Sessions Judge, Junnar, for District and Additional Sessions Judge, Junnar, Pune, vide court order vide Rule 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, is approving the opening of a personal deposit account for the amounts and other dues deposited by the parties in a criminal case subject to the following conditions.
