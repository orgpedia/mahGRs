Preamble: The General Administration Department has been declared the appointing authority under the Government decision dated 19.11.2016 in respect of the matters relating to the final decisions to be taken in the matter of appointment/ posting of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers as well as Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees by direct service, promotion or deputation.
Further, the concerned administrative departments have been informed to declare the designation of the post wise appointing authorities through separate orders as to who will be the appointing authority to take final decision on the matters of appointments/ postings to be made by direct service, promotion and deputation to the posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D.
The procedure followed by the General Administration Department in respect of appointment of officers/ staff under its administrative control till date is as follows:
Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has invited applications for recruitment to various posts through direct recruitment. After receiving the recommendation from Maloya, the allocation of officers/ staff is made by the Secretary as per the demand of the departments. Further, the process of filling up the posts of promotion shall be carried out by the General Administration Department/ Department of Works, through the General Administration Department as the appointing authority. 12, as per the provisions of the Government Resolution dated 19.11.2016.
Further, as per the provisions of Section 6 of the Transfer Act, the General Administration Department acts as the competent authority to take final decisions on matters of appointments/ postings/ transfers etc. to be made by direct service, promotion or deputation of all posts.
