Ministry of Finance, Government of India, Ministry of Finance, Department of Expenditure, dated 08.03.2019 for information and appropriate action in respect of judicial officers of subordinate courts in the State of Maharashtra.
According to the above-mentioned Office Memorandum of the Ministry of Finance of the Central Government. The increase in Dearness Allowance by 6% (from 148% to 154%) with effect from 01.01.2019 and other provisions mentioned in the Memorandum shall continue to be applicable to Judicial Officers of Subordinate Courts as well as retired Judicial Officers in the State of Maharashtra.
Accordingly, now the Judicial Officers of the Secondary Court of Maharashtra State as well as retired Judicial Officers of the State of Maharashtra will be given the status of Judicial Officers. Dearness Allowance@ 154% will be admissible with effect from 01.01.2019. The increase in dearness allowance should be paid in cash.
