Circular
The Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Assistant Charity Commissioner on Establishment, Mumbai, fixes the seniority number and date of seniority of Group-A cadre officers. Temporary Service Excellence List dated 01.01.2026, Reference No. 1 was published by the Government Circular here. No objection has been received within the prescribed period pursuant to this provisional merit list. Considering this, the present Service Excellence List is being finalised.
This final merit list is mentioned in Annexure-A to this circular.
This final merit list is being brought to the notice of all officers of the Assistant Charity Commissioner cadre at the establishment of the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
The circular is available on the Government of Maharashtra's www.maharashtra.gov.in website.
