As per rule 512 of 1968, the following is required for opening of Personal Deposit Account (PDA) for deposit and repayment of amounts for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level, Mangaon, District Raigad-Alibag:
Acceptance is subject to conditions.
1. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit Account.
3. The amount as per the order passed by the Court in the matter will be deposited and disbursed in the Personal Deposit Account.
4. Receipt into the PD account will be the Civil Court Deposits only.
5. No Revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions shall be booked in this PD.
