The Proposal:
As per the recommendations of the 11th Central Finance Commission, a total of 187 fast track courts had been set up in the State since April 2001 for expeditious disposal of cases pending in the State courts under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme. The scheme was funded by the central government till 2011.
Considering the need and utility of Fast Track Courts, out of 187 Fast Track Courts, 100 Fast Track Courts have been notified vide Reference No. Under the Government Decisions 1, 2 and 3, it has been approved to extend the period from time to time and to meet the expenses from the state fund. Reference No. 3 here. Extension of tenure of 100 Fast Track Courts for a period of 1 year from 2021-2022 vide Government Order dated 22.04.2021. Proposal for further extension as they are expiring on 31.03.2022. The High Court vide Reference No. 4 submitted to the Government. To extend the tenure of 100 fast track courts for 5 years. The Cabinet has approved it. Accordingly, the government was considering to extend the tenure of these 100 fast track courts for the next 5 years.
Government Decision: 100 Fast Track Courts set up for speedy disposal of cases pending in the courts in the State are being extended for a period of 5 years from 1st April, 2022 to 31st March, 2027.
