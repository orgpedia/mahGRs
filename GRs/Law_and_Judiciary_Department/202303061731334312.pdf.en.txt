This administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
1) Before starting the construction, the design, layout and detailed map should be approved by the architect.
2) Approval of all concerned local bodies/ authorities should be obtained before starting the work.
3) In the course of actual work, the Department of Environment, Government Decision, No. ENV-2013/ No. 177/ T. Action shall be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in the Notice dated 10th January, 2014.
4) Public Works Department, Government Decision, No. Action to be taken under BDG 2017/ PR No. 60/ Building-2, dated 7th July, 2017.
5) The work should be completed at the cost of revised administrative approval.
6) Don't change your budget.
7) The scope of work should not be changed or new works should not be included on the basis of oral instructions given by the engineer in the Public Works Department.
8) If new works are to be included or the scope is to be changed, then action should be taken to get approval for these works in the prescribed time and manner by obtaining a written order in this regard. Otherwise, the responsibility will be fixed on the concerned officials.
9) The existing government decisions, rules, CVC principles, fiscal limits, prescribed tender procedures should be followed while implementing the scheme.
10) The Public Works Department will be responsible for fulfilling the directions and shortcomings given by the State Technical Advisory Committee in connection with the project and work, as per the requirement.
(11) Technical aspects of the revised administrative approval shall not be taken as an endorsement of the regional decision or irregularity in the tender. The Public Works Department will be responsible for investigating such cases in accordance with the then prevailing government decision.
