For the purpose of" Strengthening the Justice Sector" recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, an amount of Rs. 1014 crore has been earmarked for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, a sum of Rs.228 crore has been earmarked for the renovation of the functional courts for a period of five years. A High Level Steering Committee has been constituted on 9th March, 2016 for effective implementation of the recommendations of the Commission. At the meeting, The Action Plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle and under the Government Order dated 12th June, 2017, the Administrative Department has been empowered to grant administrative approval for the repair works of the Court Building under the 14th Central Finance Commission with the concurrence of the Finance Department to the extent of Rs. Accordingly, the proposal for the renovation of Courts at Korpana under the control of District and Sessions Court, Chandrapur, on the Schedule for the year 2019-20 has been approved by the Hon'ble Chief Justice of India vide letter No. 3 above. The Bombay High Court has given its verdict. The budget has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Divisional Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer as well as the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Chandrapur. those
