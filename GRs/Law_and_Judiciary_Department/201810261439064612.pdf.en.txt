The matter of declaring Civil Judge Senior Level, Malkapur, District Buldhana as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government on the request made by the High Court of Bombay.

The Civil Judge Senior Level, Malkapur, District Buldhana is being declared as the" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Civil Court Senior Level, established at Malkapur, District Buldhana. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 read with Finance Division No. 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated 17.04.2015. .
