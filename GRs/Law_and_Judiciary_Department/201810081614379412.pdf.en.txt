Proposal for administrative approval of the estimated expenditure for the construction of modern furniture in the inner side of the office of the Municipal Corporation of Mumbai, renovation of toilets, wall scaffolding, modern renovation of the entire office by installing floor tiles and cutlery etc. 1 The Government have received from the office of the Nagarpal (Sheriff), Mumbai, under the letter mentioned here. The budget has been prepared by the Executive Engineer, Municipal Corporation, Mumbai, Public Works Department. Suburban Police, Office of the City Magistrate (Sheriff), Mumbai has testified. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of the estimated expenditure on modern renovation of the office of the Municipal Corporation of Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
The government has decided to renovate the office of the Municipal Corporation of Mumbai in a modern way. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimates amounting to Rs. 55,75,675/- (Rupees Fifty Five Lakhs Fifty Seven Thousand Six Hundred and Seventy Five) received from the Sub-Collector, Nagarpal (Sheriff) Office, Mumbai.
To meet the cost of modern renovation of the Municipal Corporation of Mumbai, funds will be provided through supplementary demand in the current financial year (2018-2019) under the target of 27-small constructions.
This Government Decision has been issued vide No. No. 574/2017/ IMMA 2 dated 22.05.2018 of the Public Works Department and with the concurrence of the Department as well as vide No. No.623/ 18/ Expenditure. 5, d. 03.07.2018 as also in the Financial Powers Rules Book Part I, Sub-Division 3A No. 4, para. 27 (2) is being issued subject to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department.
