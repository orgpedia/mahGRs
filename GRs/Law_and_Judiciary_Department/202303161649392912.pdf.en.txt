Provisional Service Merit List dated 01.01.2023 issued by General Administration Department. As per Rule 3 (2) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 notified on 21.06.2021, the date of seniority and the number of seniority in the Service Excellence List shall be determined by the Government of Maharashtra. It was published on 09.01.2023. The objections received in respect of the said Provisional Service Merit List as on 23.02.2023 (M.No.) are being considered and the said Service Merit List is being finalized. This final service excellence list is mentioned in Annexure' A' and' B' along with this circular.
A copy of this final service excellence list is being brought to the notice of all the Charity Deputy Commissioner Officers in the establishment of the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.
