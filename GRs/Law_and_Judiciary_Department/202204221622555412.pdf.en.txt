Proposal: Decision to be made. A set of 13 Public Prosecutor Offices under Section 8 has been sanctioned under 15.2.2007. At present, there are 27 sanctioned posts in the office of the Public Prosecutor, Municipal Civil Court, Mumbai. 2 posts in Short Writer (Lower Grade) Cadre, Clerk Typist Cadre-2 in the office of the Public Prosecutor, Municipal Civil Court, Mumbai
There are a total of 8 vacancies for the posts and 4 posts in Group-D cadre. Also, since the functioning of this office is connected with the High Court, affidavits have to be filed in the court within the prescribed time.
Therefore, the Government Pleader, City Civil Court, Mumbai, has requested for approval to fill up these posts through external agencies vide Reference No. There were 2 letters. Accordingly, a proposal was submitted to the Finance Department for approval to fill up a total of 8 vacancies in the said office through external agencies
As per the approval of the Finance Department, a total of 8 vacancies in the post of Short Writer (Lower Grade), 2 posts of Clerk-Typist and 4 posts of Constable are to be filled through external system. The proposal has been approved by the Sub-Committee in its meeting held on 24.03.2022.
Government Decision: As per the approval given in the meeting held on 24.03.2022, the Government is approving to create and take over the services of the following 8 notional posts mentioned in the table below in the office of the Public Prosecutor, Municipal Civil Court, Mumbai for a period up to the approval of the revised pattern of the office of the Public Prosecutor or one year, whichever is earlier.

The Sub-Committee has approved the creation of the above notional posts subject to the following conditions: (1) The procedure for filling up the vacancies shall be taken up within six months after the approval of the revised pattern of the office of the Public Prosecutor by the High Level Committee of Secretaries.
(2) Since there are no posts from the lower cadre available for promotion to the posts in the said office, the Department shall make separate service entry rules for the posts in the office of the Public Prosecutor.
The guidelines issued by the Finance Department from time to time through government circulars regarding the provision of services by external agencies should be strictly followed. Also, the head of the office should ensure that the prevailing law is not violated while providing services from external agencies.
