Government circular:
Special Counsel/ Senior Counsel is sometimes appointed by the Government to deal with judicial matters in the Supreme Court, New Delhi. Such judges are not part of the government. Therefore, the remuneration of the lawyers appointed in such a particular case has to be paid from the budgetary provision of the concerned department of the government. It has come to the notice of the government that the honorarium of some such lawyers is pending. Therefore, all the departments are advised to pay the dues of the special counsels appointed in the case of your department immediately. Also, Ma. As per the instant attached with the details regarding payment of Special Counsel appointed by his Department in the Supreme Court, New Delhi, Shri. Instructions should be given to the Chief Minister's Office to submit it immediately.
