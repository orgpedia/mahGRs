In pursuance of the above, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Ratnagiri vide Government Notification dated 29th September, 2009.
In pursuance of the above mentioned Government Decision dated 13th February, 2012 mentioned in A.No.2, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been created in the State. District Court Ratnagiri has requested to declare the Magistrate for Ratnagiri (Pali) Gram Nyayalaya Ratnagiri (Pali) (Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Ratnagiri) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to declare the Judicial Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Ratnagiri, District Ratnagiri as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for the Gram Nyayalaya at Ratnagiri.
""" For the Gram Nyayalayas established under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008 in Ratnagiri, District Ratnagiri, the Magistrate, Gram Nyayalayas, Ratnagiri, District Ratnagiri is being declared as"""" Head of Office"""" and"""" Drawing and Disbursing Officer"""" under Rule No. 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.""" The order will be limited to financial matters.
