Preamble: In terms of the order dated 27.01.2020 of the Department of Law and Justice, the post of Superintendent (Non-Gazetted) in the District and Sessions Court attached to the office of District Government Advocate (excluding City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the period up to 30th June, 2020 has been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer as a special case.
Due to the serious health problems caused by the corona virus spread around the world, there is a very low staff presence in the ministry and other offices. It will take some time for the situation to normalise.
Hence, it was under the consideration of the Government to continue the responsibility of the post of Drawing and Disbursing Officer assigned to the Superintendent (Non-Gazetted) of the District and Sessions Court attached to the office of the District Government Advocate (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) for the intervening period till the gazetted officer is declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the District Government Advocate Office.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Accordingly, the responsibility of the post of Drawing and Disbursing Officer assigned as a special case to the post of" Superintendent" (Non-Gazetted) in the District and Sessions Court attached to the District Government Advocate Office (excluding the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai) is being continued till 31st December, 2020. They are being authorized to deposit all the payments in this office in the treasury. The order will be limited to financial matters.
2. The decision of the Government shall be taken by the Finance Department. Reference No. 141/2019/ KOSHA is being issued pursuant to the agreement dated 10.06.2020 under Section-5.
