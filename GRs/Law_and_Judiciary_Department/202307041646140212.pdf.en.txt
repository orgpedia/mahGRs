Government's decision:
The sub-committee. As per the approval given in the meeting held on June 7, 2023, a total of 4 posts of Short Writer-Lower Grade-II and Clerk-Typist-2 for the office of Public Prosecutor, Municipal Civil Court, Mumbai will be filled. From 1st May, 2023 onwards, the Government is permitting recruitment through external agencies for a period of 11 months or till the availability of regular candidates in direct service, whichever is less.
The guidelines issued by the Finance Department from time to time through Government Decisions/ Circulars regarding the provision of services by external agencies should be strictly followed. Also, the head of the office should ensure that the prevailing law is not violated while providing services from external agencies.
