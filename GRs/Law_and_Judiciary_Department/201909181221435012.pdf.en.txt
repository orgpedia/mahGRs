As per the Government Resolution dated 4.08.2008, the structure of Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai has been approved. The number of devotees visiting this temple is rising day by day. As per the demand made by the Trust, in order to strengthen the security of the temple for security reasons as well as in view of law and order as the temple is a target of extremists and to provide basic facilities to the devotees visiting the temple, an additional figure of 133 posts has been approved under the Government decision dated 25th June, 2018.
The President of Shree Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai in his address No. 3, vide letter dated 20/7/2019, has requested that the services of the employees who have been working in the service of the Trust for the last 3 to 15 years on daily basis be recognized as" a special case." Considering the services rendered by the employees to the Trust on daily wage basis and the request made by the President of Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai, the Government was considering regularization of the services of 129 daily wage employees working in Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai.
Government Decision: The employees of Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai, who have been appointed on daily wages for the last several years and who are still in the service of the Trust, are being recognized as a special case under this Government Decision to regularize their services in the rank and pay scale as indicated in Annexure A attached herewith and this Government Decision will not be taken as a precedent.

The vacancies existing since the date of issue of this Government Order or the vacancies arising in future should be filled up in the manner prescribed in accordance with the rules of service.
The date of the first appointment of these employees shall be the date of this Government decision. The salary of these employees should be fixed in the first phase of the pay scale admissible to them.
Further, all appointments and promotions will be strictly in accordance with the rules of service.
These employees will be on probation for a period of one year. These employees can be given regular appointment only after satisfactory completion of the probation period. The Committee shall have the power to extend the probation period for a further period of one year in consultation with the Executive.
This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the provisions of Section 12 (2) and Section 14 (2) of the Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple Trust System (Prabhadevi) Act, 1980.
The expenditure on these matters should be met from the management fund of the trust system. The Executive Officer of Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai shall ensure that the expenditure is within the limits prescribed under the provisions of Section 19 (2) of the Act.
