Preamble In pursuance of the above decision, the Government have accorded administrative approval for the construction of building at Daryapur under the control of District and Sessions Court, Amravati at a cost of Rs. 19,39,729/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Forty-Nine Thousand Seven Hundred and Thirty-Nine only) under the Redesigning of Existing Courts under the 14th Central Finance Commission. The Chief Justice of District and Sessions Court, Amravati has now informed that the building at Daryapur does not require renovation and requested to exclude the same from the Government Order dated July 21, 2017.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS In the above context, in the Government decision at A. No. (3), the building at Daryapur under the control of the District and Sessions Court, Amravati, for which administrative approval for repair/ renovation has been granted under the 14th Finance Commission at a cost of Rs. 19,39,729/- (Rupees Nineteen Lakhs Forty-nine Thousand Seven Hundred and Thirty-nine only) is no longer required, the" Court Building at Daryapur" and the table thereunder mentioned in paragraph No. 1 of the said Government Decision are omitted.
