This administrative approval is granted subject to the following conditions:
a) Before starting the work, the design, layout and detailed map should be started with the approval of the architect.
(b) While dealing with the provision made in a rough manner, the work should be taken up only after making a detailed budget.
(c) While working directly with the Department of Environment, Government Decision No. Action should be taken in accordance with the guidelines mentioned in ENV-2013/ Q.No. 177/ T.K.1, dated 10th January, 2014.
d) Approval of all concerned local bodies/ authorities should be obtained before starting the work.
