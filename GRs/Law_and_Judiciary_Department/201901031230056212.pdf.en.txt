Preamble: Construction of Maa Bhavani Hospital has started in Pardi area under the aegis of Shri Bhavani Mata Seva Samiti. A total of 110 beds are being arranged in this hospital to provide state-of-the-art facilities to the poor. The project will be funded by donations and community support. The equipment for the Eye, Radiology, Cardiology Department is Rs. 2.35 crore has been requested by the President, Shri Bhavani Mata Seva Samiti, Pardi, Nagpur. Management Committee dated 02.05.2018 and the Scrutiny Committee. Maa Bhavani in the meeting held on 22.08.2018
The hospital will be provided with" Equipment for Eye, Radiology, Cardiology Department" worth Rs. A sum of Rs. 2.35 crore has been sanctioned through Shri Saibaba Sansthan, Shirdi. Accordingly, the Chief Executive Officer, Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust Management, Shirdi, has submitted a proposal to the government and the matter of approval of this proposal was under consideration.
Government's decision:
As per the provisions of Section 21 (2) (i) of the Shree Saibaba Sansthan Trustee System (Shirdi) Act, 2004, a sum of Rs. Under this Government decision, Government approval is being given to release financial assistance of Rs. 2.35 crore from Shri Saibaba Sansthan subject to the following conditions:
1) The machinery proposed to be purchased from the grant of Shri Saibaba Sansthan should be purchased in a transparent manner through a competitive tender process. The prevailing market

Don't buy at a higher price. A copy of the records of the procurement process should be submitted to the institution as information.
(2) If any amount is saved from the subsidy to be provided by Shirdi Sansthan for the purchase of the proposed machinery, the amount shall be refunded to Shirdi Sansthan. However, if the amount spent is more than the subsidy, the committee will have to bear the additional expenditure.
3) The grant given by the Shirdi Sansthan will be chargeable if the beds are not reserved for the economically weaker and low income group patients as per the government rules for charitable hospitals. The certificate of the Charity Commissioner in this regard will have to be submitted to the Shirdi Sansthan every year.
The said equipment shall not be sold or transferred in any manner to Sri Bhavani Mata Seva Samiti for at least 10 years. A certificate regarding the operation of the equipment and the number of patients who have benefited from the equipment during the year will have to be submitted to the Shirdi Sansthan in April every year.
Before allocating the grant, Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi shall ensure that the basic facilities required for the use of the above machinery and the staff handling the said machinery are in place.
6) It is necessary to use the funds for the same work for which the said Shri Bhavani Mata Seva Samiti has given the funds.
7) The Maa Bhavani Seva Samiti must take responsibility for the repair and maintenance of this machine.
8) The machinery to be purchased through the financial assistance provided by Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi will be installed at the place where the board" Financial assistance from Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi" will be displayed on the facade.
9) After purchasing the machinery from the said grant, the utility certificate of the expenditure will be required to be submitted to Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trustee System, Shirdi by Shri Bhavani Mata Seva Samiti.
10) It shall be mandatory for Shri Bhavani Mata Seva Samiti's Maa Bhavani Hospital, Nagpur to enter into an agreement with Shri Saibaba Sansthan Trust Management, Shirdi within a period of one month containing the above terms and conditions and other ancillary terms and conditions to be enforced by the Shirdi Sansthan. A copy of the agreement should be sent to the government.
