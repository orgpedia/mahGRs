Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated 08.09.2022, 96 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court Latur have been extended till 28.02.2023. In pursuance of the Government Order dated 18.10.2022, the Government have accorded approval for establishment of Civil Court Senior Level at Ausa, District Latur and creation of post for this Court. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 99 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023.
