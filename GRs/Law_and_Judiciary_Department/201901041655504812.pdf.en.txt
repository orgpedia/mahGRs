The proposal: As per the proposal of the High Court, the Government has decided to establish a District and Additional Sessions Court at Shrigonda in Ahmednagar district and to create a post for it. CRC-0413/ 466/ P.No.87/ Work.11, Dy. It was approved on August 10, 2018. In the said government decision, it was stated that the orders regarding the availability of funds would be issued separately. In this regard, a symbolic supplementary demand was submitted to the Legislature through the Finance Department in the November 2018 session. It's been approved. Thus, the proposal to provide funds for this court was under the consideration of the government.
