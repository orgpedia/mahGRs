The Proposal- M. In Public Interest Litigation No. 216/2010 filed in the High Court of Bombay. As per the orders of the High Court of Bombay, fire audit of all court buildings in the state is being done and fire fighting/ fire prevention measures are being taken. Accordingly, vide letter dated 11.09.2015, the Deputy Manager, Shri. The Bombay High Court has submitted a proposal to the state government for setting up of firefighting system/ fire safety measures in the court buildings of the District and Sessions Court Gadchiroli and the courts at Armori, Desaiganj, Kurkheda, Chamorshi, Aheri and Sironcha. The concerned officers of the Maharashtra Fire Service have suggested the fire prevention measures mentioned in the proposal and the Director, Directorate of Maharashtra Fire Service has agreed to carry out the work of installing the fire fighting system in the court building as per the recommendations given by them. In accordance with the measures suggested by the local Fire Officer, the budget for the year 2017-18 has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer and Principal District and Sessions Judge Gadchiroli. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to grant administrative approval for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in the District and Sessions Court Gadchiroli and court buildings at Armori, Desaiganj, Kurkheda, Chamorshi, Aheri and Sironcha.
