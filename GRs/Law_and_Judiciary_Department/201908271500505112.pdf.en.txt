The Proposal:
The Sangam Bungalow (basement, ground floor and first floor) of the residence of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Pune has been constructed in 1876. The structure is of load bearing type and has Mangalorean cowls. The structural audit of the bungalow has been done by the Public Works Department and it has been suggested that it needs to be repaired. The Rs. 39,58,045/-. The High Court had submitted to the Government for administrative approval under the letter at the reference serial number 1. The documents and the report of the structural audit were submitted by the Principal District Judge, Pune, vide letter No. 2. Accordingly, the matter of administrative approval of this expenditure was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A total of Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget/ Expenditure amounting to Rs. 42,11,537/- (Rupees Forty Two Lakhs Eleven Thousand Five Hundred and Thirty Seven only) as follows.
