The Proposal:
Department of Law and Justice, Govt. The Office of the Public Prosecutor, Supreme Court of India, New Delhi was established on 10th May, 1984. Reference No. 2 As per the decision of the Government herein, the Special Commissioner, Government of Maharashtra, New Delhi was declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the said office. Thereafter, reference No. 6 The Resident Commissioner, Government of Maharashtra, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi was declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the financial year 2022-2023 for the said office.
(2) The office of the Public Prosecutor, Office of the Public Prosecutor, Supreme Court, New Delhi shall not be deemed to be a gazetted officer. The Office of the Public Prosecutor, Supreme Court of India, New Delhi has now issued Reference No. 7 The office is requested to declare Resident Commissioner, Government of Maharashtra, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the financial year 2023-2024. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to declare" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" for the Office of the Public Prosecutor, Supreme Court, New Delhi.
As per Rule 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Act, 1968, the Resident Commissioner, Office of the Resident Commissioner, Maharashtra Sadan, New Delhi for the Office of the Public Prosecutor, Supreme Court of India, New Delhi is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for all purposes for the current financial year 2023-2024.
