Preamble- In reference to No. 1. Administrative approval has been accorded for creation of Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Ausa, Latur District vide Government Order dated 18.10.2022. Pursuant to the request made by the District and Sessions Court, Latur vide Government Order dated 03.04.2023 dated 01.04.2023, Civil Judge Senior Level, Ausa has been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. In reference No. 3. On 27.04.2023, the District and Sessions Court, Latur has requested for approval to open a separate self-registration account in the name of Civil Judge Senior Level, Ausa. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to approve the opening of Personal Deposit Account for Civil Judge Senior Level, Ausa Court.
In accordance with Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 Clause 1, the opening of a Personal Deposit Account for depositing amounts in the Civil Court Senior Level, Ausa District Latur is being approved subject to the following conditions:

The following shall be classification which may be recorded on every challan while depositing the amount in Treasury / Sub-Treasury.
For Civil Court Deposit 8443 Civil Deposit 104 Civil Court Deposit 01- Civil Judge, (Senior Division) Ausa, Dist-Latur.
Conditions 1. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit Account.
3. Receipt into the PD account will be the Civil Court Deposit, Maintenance rent, decretal amount, Expenditure should be made as per the order of the court.
4. No revenue Receipt/Revenue Expenditure transactions shall be booked in this PD.
5. Either on quarterly or monthly basis, the balances in this Personal Deposit Account shall be reconciled by the Court.
6. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this Personal Deposit Account.
7. On receipt of the Goverment Order office of the Pr. Accountant General (A&E) II Nagpur Maharashtra will issue an authority to open the above PD.
