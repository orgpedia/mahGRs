Preamble In pursuance of the above, the Government have approved the establishment of Gram Nyayalayas at Tamsa, Taluka Hadgaon in Nanded District vide Government Notification dated 12th March, 2014 at No. Further, in pursuance of the Government decision dated 27th September, 2011 and the corrigendum dated 13th February, 2012, referred to in Reference No.33, in the aforesaid Annexure No.2, a total of 25 Gram Nyayalayas have been constituted in the State.
The District Court Nanded has requested to declare the Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya Tamsa, Taluka Hadgaon, (Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class Hadgaon) as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the Gram Nyayalaya Tamsa, Taluka Hadgaon. The case is being heard in the Mumbai High Court. Accordingly, the government was considering the matter of declaring Magistrate Gram Nyayalaya Tamsa, Taluka Hadgaon District Nanded as Drawing and Disbursing Officer of Gram Nyayalaya Scheme for Gram Nyayalaya Tamsa, Taluka Hadgaon, Nanded.

Government Resolution No: DDO 2022/ Q. No.238 (1)/ Q. 12
The Magistrate, Gram Nyayalaya, Tamasa, Taluka Hadgaon, District Nanded is being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" under Rule 15 (3) and 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 for the Gram Nyayalaya established under the Gram Nyayalaya Act, 2008 at Tamasa, Taluka Hadgaon, District Nanded. The order will be limited to financial matters.
This Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments under Rule No. 2 of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978 read with Finance Division No. 2013/ PR No. 30/2013/ Regulations, Part II, dated 17.04.2015. .
