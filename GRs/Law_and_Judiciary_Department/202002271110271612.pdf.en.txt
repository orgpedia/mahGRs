For the purpose of" Strengthening the Justice Sector" recommended by the 14th Central Finance Commission, a fund of Rs. 1014 crore has been earmarked for the State of Maharashtra for a period of 5 years from 2015-2016 to 2019-2020. Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, a sum of Rs.228 crore has been earmarked for the renovation of the functional courts for a period of five years. A High Level Steering Committee has been constituted on 9th March, 2016 for effective implementation of the recommendations of the Commission. In the meeting held on 28.03.2016, Shri. The action plan received from the High Court has been approved in principle. The proposal for the renovation of the court buildings at Latur and Nilanga under the control of the Chief Judicial Magistrate and the Chief Judicial Magistrate in the year 2018-19 on the basis of the letter mentioned in the above Reference No. 3 has been approved by the Hon'ble Chief Judicial Magistrate. The Bombay High Court has given its verdict. The budget has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Divisional Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer as well as the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Latur. The proposal has been approved by the High Level Steering Committee in its meeting held on 16.01.2020. Accordingly, the administrative approval for the renovation of the court buildings of the District and Sessions Court Latur and the Chief Judicial Magistrates Latur and Nilanga under the 14th Central Finance Commission was under the consideration of the Government.

The above approval is being granted subject to the following conditions: i. The Maharashtra Public Works Department, the rules of administrative approval in the rule book and the related rules should be complied with.
(ii) The repair work shall be carried out by the Public Works Department by appointing a contractor through the Administrative Departments, if the post of Executive Engineer or above is held by the concerned Administrative Departments, depending on the tender method prescribed.
(iii) Budgetary provisions should be available.
3. The cost of renovation of court buildings of the Chief Judicial Magistrate Latur and Nilanga under the control of District and Sessions Court Latur (Main and Extension Building) and District and Sessions Court Latur shall be met from the approved provision under the head" Demand No. J-1, 2014 Nyaydaan, (00) Nyaydaan, 102-High Courts, (11) Renovation of functional courts, (11) (01) Renovation of functional court (2014 0548)."
3) Before the tender of the work. It shall be the responsibility of the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Latur, to ensure that the above works fall within the framework prescribed by the High Court of Bombay and the works prescribed by the Dedicated Cell. The e-tendering process should be carried out by the Public Works Department following the prevailing procedure and other ancillary proceedings should be completed in accordance with this administrative approval.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the Public Works Department No. No. 248/2019/ Ima-2, dated 24.09.2019 and with the concurrence of that Department and subject to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under the Government Resolution, Law and Justice Department dated 01.01.2019.
