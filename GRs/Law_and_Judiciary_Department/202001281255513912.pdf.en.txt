The Proposal:
In pursuance of" Ease of Doing Business," the Government vide its decision dated 18.04.2018 has approved the opening of a new Targeted Bank Account in State Bank of India in the office of the District Sessions Court, Pune on a pilot basis for sanctioning the amount of fine and other amounts to be deposited in the Court.
As per the Government decision dated 28.08.2018, all the court establishments in Pune district have been allowed to open a self-gazetted bank account in State Bank of India for accepting the amount of fine and other amounts to be deposited in the court using e-payment.
As per the letter from the Central Project Coordinator, E-Court Project, High Court, Appellate Division, Mumbai, under the' Ease of Doing Business', the government was considering to open a targeted bank account in all district and taluka courts of the state to accept the fine amounts and other amounts to be deposited in the court and to allow the draft tripartite agreement regarding the same. Accordingly, the following decisions have been taken:

Approval is being granted subject to the following terms and conditions as laid down in the Government Resolution dated 28.08.2018: 1) The Bank Scroll should be submitted to the Treasury in the form of an Account Head and Account Payable.
(2) The Court shall first take the balance of the list of amounts deposited with it and the actual amount deposited in the SBI and then take the balance with the Treasury. While taking final entries in the PLA of the courts, the amounts included in the treasury notes should be included. The amount deposited with SBI in case of unsuccessful transactions will not be included in the treasury account.
(3) The terms and conditions of Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 shall be complied with.
4) There will be no change in the method of monthly account report submitted by the Treasury Office to the Accountant General's Office.
5) It should be ensured that the amount collected by the government is deposited immediately in the Consolidated Fund/ Personal Principal Account.
The draft tripartite agreement on opening of a separate targeted bank account in State Bank of India for sanction of fines and other amounts to be deposited in the court for all district and taluk court establishments in the state. The Maharashtra government has approved the proposal.
This Government Resolution is being issued as per the concurrence of the Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries, Mumbai and the concurrence given by the Finance Department under the Official Reference No. 356/2019/ Treasury No. 5 dated 04.10.2019.
