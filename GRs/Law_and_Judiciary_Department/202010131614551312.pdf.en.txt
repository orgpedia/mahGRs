Preamble- The number of pending cases/ cases for opening of courts at various places in the State should be at least 500, along with the infrastructure, e.g. The court buildings and residences of the judges should be made available. As all the criteria required as per the policy of the High Court are being fulfilled for the establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court and Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Ambad, District Jalna, Ma. The High Court of Bombay recommended the establishment of 1) District and Additional Sessions Court and 2) Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Ambad, District Jalna. Earlier, in pursuance of the Government decision dated 03.02.2010 in the aforesaid regard, the Government had approved the creation of necessary post for the establishment of Additional District and Sessions Court and Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Ambad, District Jalna. However, since the posts had lapsed, the matter of revival/ creation of the posts and establishment of 1) District and Additional Sessions Court and 2) Civil Judge Senior Level Court at Ambad, District Jalna was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:

Government's decision
The Government have decided to establish 1) District and Additional Sessions Court and 2) Civil Judge Senior Level Court for Ambad and Ghansawangi Taluks in Ambad, Jalna District.
