Introduction - Indapur, District Pune at Reference No. 1 vide Notification No. The District and Additional Sessions Court has come into operation with effect from 25.01.2025. As per the Government decision in Reference H.2, District Judge-1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Indapur, District Pune have been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. Reference No. Letters 3 and 4 request permission to open a separate personal deposit account for criminal claims amounts for the District and Additional Sessions Court, Indapur, District Pune. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to allow the District and Additional Sessions Court, Indapur, District Pune, to open a separate Personal Deposit Account for criminal claim amounts.
Government Decision: The District and Additional Sessions Court, Indapur, for District Pune, in the name of District Judge-1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Indapur, District Pune, as per the court order vide Rule 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, is approving the opening of a Personal Deposit Account for the amounts and other dues deposited by the parties in a criminal case subject to the following conditions.
The following shall be classification which may be recorded on every challan while depositing the amount in Treasury / Sub-Treasury.
For Criminal Court Deposit 8443 - Criminal Deposit
105 - Criminal Court Deposit
(001 - District Judge-1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Indapur, Dist. Pune.
1. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit Account.

6. The balances have be reconciled with AG office and confirmation of Balances 15 to be submitted by the Treasury Officer to AG office at the end of the financial year.
