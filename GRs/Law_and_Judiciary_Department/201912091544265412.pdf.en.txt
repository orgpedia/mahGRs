As per notification dated 24.01.2019, the Court of the District and Additional Sessions Judge, Mehkar, District Buldhana has become functional with effect from 01.02.2019. As per the Government Order dated 14.02.2019 dated 14.02.2019, District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Mehkar have been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. On 12.06.2019, Principal District and Sessions Judge, Buldhana has requested for approval to open a Personal Accounts Account for the Court of District and Additional Sessions Judge, Mehkar. Accordingly, the matter of approval to open a Personal Accounts Account for the court of District and Additional Sessions Judge Mehkar was under the consideration of the government.
Government Resolution- As per Rule No. 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, Clause 1, the District and Additional Sessions Court, Mehkar, District Buldhana, in the name of the District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Mehkar, District Buldhana, the opening of Civil and Criminal Personal Accounts Account is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.

3) The amount ordered by the Court to the concerned / opoosite parties to be deposited as Civil Court Deposit / Criminal Court Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA and payment due may be made as per the order of the Court.
4) No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions will appear in this PLA.
5) Either on quarterly or monthly basis, balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the Court.
6) All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this PLA.
7) O/o the A.G. (A&E)II, Nagpur will issue an authority to open PLA on receipt of Govt.orders.
The following shall be the classification which may be recorded on every challan while depositing the amount in Treasury / Sub-Treasury.
