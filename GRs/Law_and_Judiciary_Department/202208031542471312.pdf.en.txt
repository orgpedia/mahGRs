Introduction: The Old Secretariat building of the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai was constructed in 1874 and is about 165 years old. The building is an old structure. The building has undergone a structural audit and needs immediate repairs.
According to The High Court of Bombay No. Under the letter of 1, the budgets of Providing weather shed and external pointing and structural repairs and repairs to M.T. roof and water proofing were submitted to the government for approval. The estimates have been prepared by the Deputy Engineer, South Sub-Division, Mumbai and prepared by the concerned Executive Engineer and the Principal Judge, City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai. The Chief Engineer, P.W.
Region, Mumbai has given the recommendation and technical approval. Accordingly, the matter of providing administrative approval to the budget for the works of providing weather shed and external pointing and structural repairs and repairs to M.T. roof and water proofing in the court building of the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: A sum of Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimate of Rs. 5,07,56, 917/- (Rupees Five Crore Seven Lakh Fifty Six Thousand Nine Hundred Seventeen only).
