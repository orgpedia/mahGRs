In a letter dated 06.12.2019, the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nashik, requested for creation of one fictitious post of driver each for Gram Nyayalaya Devala and Gram Nyayalaya Trimbakeshwar, District Nashik and to allow the services of these posts to be availed through an external agency. Accordingly, the government was considering the creation of one fictitious post of driver each for Gram Nyayalaya Devala, District Nashik and Gram Nyayalaya Trimbakeshwar, District Nashik and the government was approving to provide the services of this post on contract basis through external agencies. The government has taken the following decisions:
# Page 2
Government Resolution No. 2012/ No.58/ No.12
Government's decision
The government is giving approval to create one fictitious post of driver each for Gram Nyayalayas and Trimbakeshwar in Nashik district and to make the services of these posts available through external agencies on a contract basis. The expenditure for this purpose shall be met from the head of account, Demand No. 1, 2014 Adjudication, (00) 105 Civil and Sessions Courts, (02) District and Sessions Judges, (02) (02) Establishment of Gram Nyayalayas under Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008, (20140477) Dattamat.
(2) In the case of filling up of these posts on contractual basis by external agencies, it shall be mandatory to comply with the guidelines issued in this regard from time to time.
This Government Resolution is being issued with the concurrence of the Department of Finance vide Informal Reference No. 682/ Expenditure-5 dated 13th November, 2018, Informal Reference No. 459/2018/ Regulations dated 13th December, 2018 and Informal Reference No. 194/ Appuq dated 20th August, 2020.
