GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- In pursuance of the Government decision dated 06.09.2021 in Reference No. (3), 201 temporary posts in the establishment of District and Sessions Court, Buldhana have been extended till 28.02.2022. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 201 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
