Public Interest Litigation filed in the High Court, Mumbai No. In pursuance of the orders dated 216/2010, fire audit of all court buildings in the State is being conducted and fire fighting/ fire safety measures are being taken. In this regard, a proposal for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in court buildings at Vikhroli and Kurla under the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai has been approved. The High Court has submitted as per Reference No. 1. Maharashtra Fire Service, Mumbai. As directed in letter No. 3, in accordance with the measures suggested by the local Fire Officer, the budget for the year 2014-2015 has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Engineer, Kurla (S.B.Division), Kurla and the concerned Executive Engineer, S.B.Division, Kurla. The case was heard by the Bombay High Court and the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate. The Public Works Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has examined the said budget and agreed to the administrative sanction. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval to the budget for the construction of fire fighting system/ fire preventive measures in the court buildings at Vikhroli and Kurla under the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Rs. 3,85,466/- and Rs. 9,06,151/- aggregating to Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an amount of Rs. 12,91,617/- (Rupees Twelve Lakh Ninety One Thousand Six Hundred Seventeen only).
