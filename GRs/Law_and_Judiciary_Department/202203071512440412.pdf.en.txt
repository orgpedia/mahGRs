Government's decision
Subject to the decision of the Government dated 06.09.2021, 104 temporary posts on the establishment of District and Sessions Court Beed have been extended till 28.02.2022. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 104 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
