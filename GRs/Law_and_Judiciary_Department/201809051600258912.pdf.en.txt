As per the proposal of the High Court, the establishment of Civil Judge (Senior Level) Court at Akot in Akola district and creation of post for the same has been approved vide Government Resolution, Law& Justice Division No. In the said government decision, it was stated that the orders regarding the availability of funds would be issued separately. Accordingly, the proposal to provide funds for this Court was under consideration of the Government as the symbolic supplementary demand submitted to the Legislature in the Monsoon Session of July, 2018 was approved.
