Accordingly, approval for opening of Personal Deposit Account for Civil Court, Senior Level, Igatpuri, G. Nashik was under consideration of the Government.
In accordance with Rule 512 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, the opening of a personal deposit account for deposits and repayment amounts in the Civil Court, Senior Level, Igatpuri, Nashik District, is being approved subject to the following conditions:
1. The said Personal Deposit Account shall be subject to Audit.
2. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Personal Deposit Account.
3. The amount as per order passed by the Court in the matter will be deposited and disbursed in this Personal Deposit Account.
4. Receipt collected into the PD account will be the Civil Court Deposit only.
