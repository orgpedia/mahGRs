Preamble: A total of 16 Dedicated Commercial Courts have been established at the establishment of the Municipal Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai, 11 at the Main Branch and 5 at the Dindoshi Branch. A total of 112 temporary posts, including 16 judges and 96 support staff, have been created. These are temporary positions. The request for continuation from 01.03.2021 onwards has been made by the City Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai vide its Preamble at Reference No.2. Reference No. 3 decision of the Department of Finance. 01.03.2021 to dt. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 31.08.2021. Accordingly, the matter of continuation of these posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A total of 112 temporary posts of 16 Judges and 96 supporting staff in 16 Dedicated Commercial Courts, 11 in the Main Branch and 5 in Dindoshi Branch on the establishment of Municipal Civil and Sessions Court, Mumbai. 01.03.2021 to dt. This Government has decided to extend the period up to 31.08.2021.
