The Proposal- M. In Public Interest Litigation No. 216/2010 filed in the High Court of Bombay. As per the orders of the High Court of Bombay, fire audit of all court buildings in the state is being done and fire fighting/ fire prevention measures are being taken. Accordingly, vide letter dated 20.06.2016, the Deputy Manager, Shri. The High Court of Bombay has submitted a proposal to the Government for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in District and Sessions Court Bhandara and court buildings at Sakoli, Lakhandur, Pavani, Mohadi and Tumsar. The concerned officers of the Maharashtra Fire Service have suggested the fire prevention measures mentioned in the proposal and the Director, Directorate of Maharashtra Fire Service has agreed to carry out the work of installing the fire fighting system in the court building as per the recommendations given by them. In accordance with the measures suggested by the local fire officials, the budget for 2017-18 has been prepared by the concerned Deputy Engineer of the Public Works Department and witnessed by the concerned Executive Engineer. Accordingly, the matter of granting administrative approval for installation of fire fighting system/ fire safety measures in District and Sessions Court Bhandara and court buildings at Sakoli, Lakhandur, Pavani, Mohadi and Tumsar was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision District and Sessions Court Bhandara and the courts under its control for the construction of fire fighting system/ fire prevention measures. District and Sessions Court Bhandara and Sakoli, Lakhandur, Pavani, Mohadi, as per the proposals received from the High Court of Bombay.
