The Proposal:
The fire audit of the small court's Bhaskar building has suggested measures to create an alternative electricity system from a separate sub-station in case of an emergency.
Accordingly, for providing power backup to the courts and offices on the third to eighth floor of the miniature court, building eruptions, fire fighting systems, light fittings in the genia, etc., by the emergency services, Rs. 6,85,416/- in the Budget. The High Court has submitted to the Government for administrative approval under the letter of reference No. 1. Accordingly, the proposal has been sent to the government for approval.
Government Decision: To provide power backup by emergency services to the courts and offices on the third to eighth floor of the Bhaskar Building of the Small Courts Court, Mumbai, building eruptions, fire fighting systems, light fittings in the genia, etc. 7,29,667/- (Rs. The budget/ expenditure of Rs. 7 lakh, Rs. 19 thousand, Rs. 6 hundred and sixty six only) is being given administrative approval as follows.
