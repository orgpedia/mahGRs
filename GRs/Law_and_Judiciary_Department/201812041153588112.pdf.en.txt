Reference No. 3 decision of the Department of Finance. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the tenure of temporary posts from 01.10.2018 to 28.02.2019. Accordingly, the matter of continuation of these posts was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
A total of 55 temporary posts in 11 special courts set up at 11 places where more than 1000 cases related to disabled persons, senior citizens and marginalized sections of the society are pending. from October 1, 2018. The government has extended the deadline till February 28, 2019.
