Preamble: In pursuance of the orders of administrative sanction mentioned in A. No. (1) to (10) in the above context, the District and Sessions Court of Jalgaon, Pune, Gadchiroli, Buldhana, Gondia, Beed and Aurangabad in the State have sought funds.
