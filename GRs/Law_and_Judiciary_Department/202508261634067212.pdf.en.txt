The Proposal: -
The Government decided to create a total of 821 posts (633 regular and 188 external) of 13 offices under the jurisdiction of the Department of Law and Justice, Executive 8. Defined under 27.11.2024. As per this figure, 113 regular posts in various cadres have been sanctioned for the Office of the Government Advocate, Appellate Branch (Writ Cell), High Court, Bombay and 33 posts in Group-D cadre have been sanctioned to be taken over by the external agencies.
The Office of the Public Prosecutor, Appellate Branch (Writ Cell), High Court, Bombay is functioning under the Ministry of Home Affairs. The High Court is attached to Bombay. It is necessary to clean all the rooms in the office as well as the dust, doors, windows, fans and toilets accumulated on court cases from time to time. Hence, the Government Plea, Office of the Government Pleader, Appellate Branch (Writ Cell), High Court, Bombay, vide Reference No. An additional 4 posts of part-time sweepers working 4 hours per day for the 3rd High Court office and MTNL office at Cooperage have been requested to be taken over by the external agency.
According to the Finance Department, the administrative department has the power to approve the work of unskilled posts to be done by external agencies. Accordingly, the issue of approval to take over the services of the 4 part-time SHSM post through an external agency was under consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
The Government is approving the acquisition of 4 part-time SHS controllers working 4 hours daily for the Office of the Government Advocate, Appellate Branch (Writ Cell), High Court, Bombay, Office of the High Court at MTNL Cooperage.
