3) Department of Finance, Government Decision No. 2016/ No. 08/16/ APC, D. 18.08.2022 Preamble: Under the 14th Finance Commission, a total of 24 Fast Track Courts have been set up for a period of 5 years. The temporary posts for these 24 fast track courts are required to be extended for a further period. Reference No. 3 decision of the Department of Finance. 01.09.2022 to dt. The Administrative Department has been empowered to extend the temporary posts till 28.02.2023. Accordingly, the government was considering the extension of temporary posts for the 24 fast track courts.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The following temporary posts for 24 Fast Track Courts set up under the 14th Finance Commission have been sanctioned. 01.09.2022 to dt. The period is being extended till 28.02.2023.
