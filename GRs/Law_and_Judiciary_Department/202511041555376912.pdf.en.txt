Preamble: - General Administration Department, Reference No. The Government Decision 1 of 1982 prescribes a consolidated procedure for premature retirement of officers / employees of inefficient and doubtful integrity in the public interest by revoking their services in the 50/55 year of service or 30 years of qualifying service, whichever is earlier, in accordance with Rule 10 (4) w.e.f. Rule 65 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982.
As per the instructions in the said Government Decision, Reference No. 2 As per the Government decision, a "Re-Volocation Committee" has been constituted to review the services of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees of the Sub-Registrar Partnership Institute, Maharashtra State, under this Department beyond the 50/55 years / 30 years of qualifying service. Also, Reference No. 2 Under the Government Decision herein, a 'Representation Committee' has been constituted to consider the grievances raised by the civil servant against the decision of premature retirement after service review.
It was under the consideration of the Government to reconstitute the said Committees by altering the composition of the said Committees.
