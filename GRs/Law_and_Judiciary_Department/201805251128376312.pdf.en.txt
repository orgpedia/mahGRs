General Administration Department, vide Government Resolution No. SRV-2016/ PR No. 290/ WORK-12 dated 19.11.2016.
Preamble: The Department of General Administration has declared the appointing authority under the Government decision dated 19.11.2016 regarding the final decision to be taken in the matter related to the appointment/ posting of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers as well as Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees in direct service, promotion or deputation. Further, the concerned administrative departments have been informed to declare the designation of post wise appointment authorities through separate orders as to who will be the appointing authority to take final decision on the matters of appointments/ postings to be made by direct service, promotion and deputation to the posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D. Accordingly, it was contemplated to declare the appointing authority for appointment to the posts of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D on establishment of the Department of Law and Justice (itself) by direct service, promotion or deputation.
The General Administration Department is the appointing authority for all other posts in Group-B (non-gazetted) and Group-C (except driver) in the administrative division of the Ministry. Therefore, the General Administration Department shall be the appointing authority for all other posts in Group-B (Non-Gazetted) and Group-C (except Drivers) on the establishment of Law and Justice Department (itself).
