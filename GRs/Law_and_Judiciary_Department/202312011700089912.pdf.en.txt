The Proposal:
Department of Law and Justice, Govt. The revised figure of the posts on the establishment of 13 Public Prosecutor Offices under the jurisdiction of the Office of the Attorney General has been fixed as of April 18, 2023. As per the revised pattern, 5 regular posts of various cadres have been sanctioned for the office of the Chief Submitting Officer, Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Nagpur and 1 post of Group-D cadre has been approved to be taken over by an external agency. Due to inadequate staff in the office, which often creates problems in office and court work, it is proposed to create a total of 6 new posts for this office- 2 posts of Short Writer, 2 posts of Clerk-Typist and 2 posts of Constable. 2 As per the letter here, the Chief Submitting Officer, Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Nagpur had submitted to the Government. Accordingly, as per the approval of the Finance Department, the proposal for creation of 2 new posts of Short Writer-1 and Clerk-Typist-1 and taking over the services of peon-02 through external agencies was approved by the Deputy Committee of Sub-Committee. It was introduced at the October 27, 2023 meeting. The proposal has been approved by the cabinet. Accordingly, the proposal to create 2 new posts- 1 post of Short Writer and 1 post of Clerk-Typist- is being worked out independently.
