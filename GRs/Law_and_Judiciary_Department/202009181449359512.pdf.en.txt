Government's decision:
Gram Nyayalayas have been established in the State under the Gram Nyayalayas Act, 2008. In order to ensure smooth functioning of these Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 112 temporary posts in 22 Gram Nyayalayas have been extended for the period from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020 as per the decision of the Government in this regard dated 02.03.2020. Accordingly, to ensure smooth functioning of 22 Gram Nyayalayas, a total of 112 temporary posts in the following 22 Gram Nyayalayas are being extended for the period from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021.
