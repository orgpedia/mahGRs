According to the above-mentioned office memorandum of the Ministry of Finance of the Central Government. The increase in Dearness Allowance from 7% (189% to 196%) with effect from 01.07.2021 and other provisions mentioned in the memorandum shall continue to apply to judicial officers of subordinate courts as well as retired judicial officers in the State of Maharashtra.
Accordingly, now the judicial officers of the secondary courts of the state of Maharashtra as well as the retired judicial officers will be given the benefit of this scheme. Dearness Allowance at the rate of 196% will be admissible with effect from 01.07.2021. The increase in dearness allowance should be paid in cash.
