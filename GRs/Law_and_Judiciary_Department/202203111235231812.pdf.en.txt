Government's decision
As per the Government decision dated 25th August, 2021, 148 temporary posts on the establishment of District and Sessions Court Yavatmal have been extended till 28th February, 2022. In order to ensure smooth functioning of the respective Courts, 148 temporary posts as indicated in the table below are being extended for the period from 1 st March, 2022 to 31 st August, 2022.
