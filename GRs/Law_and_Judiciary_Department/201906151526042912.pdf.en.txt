Government Decision Under the 14th Central Finance Commission, the work of Redesigning of Existing Courts in the State. The Administrative Sanction for the renovation of Court Building at Ausa, District Latur, under the control of District and Sessions Court, Latur, received from the High Court of Bombay, at a cost of Rs. 48,95,000/- (Rupees Forty Eight Lakh Ninety Five Thousand only) has been accorded vide Government Order dated 29th August, 2018 in the Reference No. The Principal District and Sessions Judge, District and Sessions Court, Latur vide letter dated 09.03.2019 has informed that since the administrative approval for the construction of the new building at Ausa, District Latur has been granted vide Government Order dated July, 2017 in the context of the aforesaid Act No. Accordingly, the administrative approval granted for the repair/ renovation of the court building at Ausa, District Latur vide Government Order dated 29.08.2018 is hereby cancelled.
