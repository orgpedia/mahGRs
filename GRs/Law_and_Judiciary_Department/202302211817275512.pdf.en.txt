The Proposal:
1 As per the Government Order dated 09.11.2022, administrative approval has been accorded for establishment of District and Additional Sessions Court at Wai, G. Satara. In pursuance of the Government Resolution dated 09.01.2023 in Reference No. 2, the approval of the Legislature has been obtained for the expenditure required for the said Court. The District and Additional Sessions Court, Waii has become functional with effect from 16th January, 2023 vide Notification No.3 dated 10.01.2023. As per the request made by Satara, the matter of declaring District Judge-1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Y, G Satara as Office Head and Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under the consideration of the Government.
For Satara, District Judge-1 and Additional Sessions Judge, Y. G. Satara are being declared as" Head of Office" and" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." They are being authorised to deposit all the court dues in the treasury. The order will be limited to financial matters.
