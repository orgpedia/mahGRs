The proposal: As per the proposal of the High Court, the Government has decided to establish a District and Additional Sessions Court at Chiplun in Ratnagiri district and to create a post for it. SAMKIND 2018/742/ P.No.134/ WORK-11, Dy. It was approved on July 23, 2019. In the said government decision, it was stated that the orders regarding the availability of funds would be issued separately.
In this regard, a symbolic supplementary demand was submitted to the Legislature through the Finance Department in the December 2019 session. It's been approved. Thus, the proposal to provide funds for this court was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The following symbolic supplementary demands for the District and Additional Sessions Court at Chiplun in Ratnagiri district have been approved by the Legislature.
