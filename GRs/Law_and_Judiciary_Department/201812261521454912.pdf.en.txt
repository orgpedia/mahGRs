As per the notification dated 29.11.2017, the Court of Senior Civil Judge at Umred, District Nagpur has become functional with effect from 02.12.2017. As per the Government Order dated 16.01.2018, Civil Judge Senior Level Umred has been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
On 07.05.2018, the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Nagpur has requested to approve the opening of Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge Senior Level Umred.
Accordingly, the matter of allowing Civil Judge Senior Level Umred to open his own private accounting account for the court was under the consideration of the government.
The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to audit subject to the following conditions: 1. The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to audit.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3) The amount ordered by the Court to be deposited as Civil Court Deposits should only be deposited in this PLA.
4) No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions will appear in this PLA.
