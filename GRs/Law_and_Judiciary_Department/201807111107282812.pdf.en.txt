Proposal- Reference No. 3 Government Decisions, Industry, Energy and Labor Department, D. 24/08/2017 has made it mandatory for all the departments of the State Government and all the government offices under the departments to make office purchases from the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal of the Central Government. However, since the A4 size White Bond Paper (90 GSM) and A3 size White Bond Paper (90 GSM) required for office use by the Appellate Division of the High Court are not available on the said portal and the said portal is not in good condition, the said Government has decided to procure the paper as per the provision mentioned in para 12 of the said Government Order, instead of GeM Portal vide Reference No. 2 the decision of the government. The proposal of the High Court to go through the e-tendering process as prescribed under 1/12/2016 and to approve the tenderer appointed through the e-tendering process and the expenditure incurred thereon was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government decision Purchase of A4 size White Bond Paper (90 GSM) and A3 size White Bond Paper (90 GSM) required for office use of the Appellate Division of the High Court of Judicature at Bombay is being done through Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal instead of Reference No. 2 the decision of the government. Approval is being granted as a special case to be done through e-tendering process prescribed under 1/12/2016.
