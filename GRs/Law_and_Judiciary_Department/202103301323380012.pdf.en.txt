The Proposal:
The Public Prosecutors Association at the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Aurangabad has filed the original application No. 502/2017 has been filed. It has raised the issue of grant of additional allowance to all the Assistant/ Additional Public Prosecutors who are conducting criminal cases in the morning/ evening courts under the 13th Finance Commission. The matter is being investigated in this regard. It was submitted to the court. The Maharashtra Morning Courts Rules, 2010 provide for additional allowance to be paid to the Additional Public Prosecutor/ Assistant Public Prosecutor who has worked in the morning court established under the 13th Finance Commission. Therefore, as per the guidelines of the 13th Finance Commission, the Finance Department has approved the proposal of the High-Level Control Committee for payment of additional allowances to the Additional Public Prosecutors/ Assistant Public Prosecutors who have worked in the morning court subject to the examination and verification of the validity of the proposal and the amounts due. It was approved in the meeting dated 28/2/2019. Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Grievances, Maharashtra State, Mumbai, has directed the As per the letter dated 2/12/2020, a total of 244 Assistant Public Prosecutors in the case have been paid Rs. 77,95,062/- and the details have been verified to be correct.
