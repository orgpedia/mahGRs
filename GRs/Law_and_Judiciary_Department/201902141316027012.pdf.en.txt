Preamble- In pursuance of the above decision, the Government have approved the establishment of a regular District and Additional Sessions Court in Mehkar, District Buldhana in place of the Joint Court of District and Additional Sessions Court at Mehkar, District Buldhana and creation of a post for this Court.
As per the Government Notification dated 24.01.2019, the Court of District and Additional Sessions Judge, Mehkar, District Buldhana has become functional with effect from 1.02.2019. Accordingly, vide letter dated 13th February, 2019 addressed to the Manager, Observations (1), Smt. On the request of the High Court of Bombay, the matter of declaring District Judge 1 and Additional Sessions Judge Mehkar, District Buldhana as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
