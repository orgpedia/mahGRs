The Proposal:
The reference No. 1 and 2 respectively. August 17, 1973. As per the Government decision dated 19th June, 1972, a framework has been approved for the management of Devasthanas under Devasthan Prabandhak Samiti, Western Maharashtra, Kolhapur. The Devasthanam Management Committee manages about 3067 temples in Kolhapur, Sangli and Sindhudurg districts.
In the meantime, the work of the Devasthan Management Committee has increased tremendously.
About 25 acres of land is under the control of the committee. There is a dire need of staff for the functions of inspection, auction, collection of ground rent, management of land, counting of donation boxes, planning of various festivals, controlling the work of sub-committees, accounting of donations and physically inspecting the land under the control of the committee, searching the records of such lands, etc. In this regard, vide No. 3, d. In pursuance of the request made by the Secretary, Devasthan Management Committee, vide letter dated 09.08.2014, to approve the revised layout, the matter of approval of the revised layout of Devasthan Management Committee, Kolhapur, Head Office and Sawantwadi Sub Office of the Committee was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The revised layout for the head office of the Devasthan Management Committee, Western Maharashtra, Kolhapur and the Sawantwadi sub-office is being approved as follows.

The period shall be terminated and the relevant pay scale shall be applied after the expiry of the period under review.
(v) It shall be mandatory for the officers and employees working in all the posts included in the figure to write and preserve the annual confidential report as per the prescribed procedure.
(vi) The pension scheme will not be applicable to the posts sanctioned by this Figure as government employees. While promoting employees to the post of promotion, the committee should consider the qualifications of such post and the confidential reports of the employees, efficiency, performance and character etc.
(vii) While appointing employees to the Devasthan Nidhi, the Committee shall make appointments taking into consideration the duties and responsibilities of the post concerned and the urgency of the service of the employees. It will be mandatory for the secretary and the temple management committee to take full care that unnecessary and additional appointments of employees will not be made on the temple fund.
