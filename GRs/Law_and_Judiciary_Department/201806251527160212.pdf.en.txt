The Proposal:
Department of Law and Justice, Govt. As on 04.08.2009, a figure of 158 posts has been sanctioned for the Trust. Since 2009, the activities of the Trust have increased manifold with the increase in the number of devotees visiting, income of the Trust, welfare activities of the Trust, registration of worship, types of worship, laddu production, etc. Therefore, the services of additional staff are required for the smooth functioning of the daily work of the Trust, to provide easy darshan to the devotees and to ensure that the various welfare activities of the Trust are carried out within the prescribed time frame. The Executive Officer, in his letters of reference, had requested for creation of additional posts due to increase in work on the trust establishment. As per the request made by the Executive Officer, Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai, the matter of sanctioning the revised contours by creating additional posts on the establishment of the Trust was under consideration of the Government.
In view of the substantial increase in the functioning of Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust, Prabhadevi, Mumbai, under Section 14 (2) of the Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir Trust (Prabhadevi) Act, 1980, the Government hereby approve the creation of additional 133 posts on the establishment as follows:
