The financial powers conferred under the Maharashtra Contingent Expenditure Rules, 1965 in Part I, Sub-Division II of the said rule book are reserved for the administrative department, head of department, head of office. Accordingly, the proposal of the High Court to increase some of the financial powers conferred on the Bombay High Court as Head of Department was under consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: As per the approval of the Administrative and Financial Empowerment Committee, Ma. The Manager, Original Branch and the Manager, Appellate Branch of the High Court of Bombay shall be entitled to receive such remuneration as may be specified in the Annexure annexed to this Government Order, subject to the prescribed terms and conditions and financial limits as may be specified therein.
Subject to the grant of financial powers, the government is being given approval.
