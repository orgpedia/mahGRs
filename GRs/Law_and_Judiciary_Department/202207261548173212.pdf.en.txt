The Proposal:
The Court of Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class has been functioning at Bhandara, District, Lakhandur since 09.08.2008 vide notification dated 05.08.2008 under reference No. As per the Government Order dated 20.08.2008 referred to in Reference No. (2), the Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class, Lakhandur has been declared as the Head of Office and Drawing and Disbursing Officer. On the request made by Principal District and Sessions Judge Bhandara, the Civil Judge Junior Level and Judicial Magistrate First Class, Lakhandur, has requested for administrative approval to open a personal provident account for the court. The High Court of Bombay vide its letter dated 22nd October, 2019 referred to in the aforesaid Reference No. Accordingly, the Government was considering to approve the opening of a Personal Accounts Account for the Court of Civil Judge and Judicial Magistrate First Class, Lakhandur, District Bhandara.

The opening of a self-financing Account is allowed subject to the following conditions:
1) The said Personal Ledger Account shall be subject to Audit.
2) The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
3) The amount ordered by the Court to be deposited as Civil Court Deposit, Decretal Deposit and Consideration Deposit may be deposited in the PD/PLA and expenditure on account of Civil Court Deposit, Decretal Deposit and Consideration Deposit shall be made through this PLA.
4) No revenue receipt / Revenue expenditure transactions shall be booked in this PLA.
5) The PD/PLA shall not become inoperative.
6) Either on quarterly or monthly basis, balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the Court.
7) All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this PLA.
8) Office of the Accountant General (A&E)II, Nagpur will issue an authority to open PLA on receipt of Govt. orders.
