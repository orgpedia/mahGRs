The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 13.11.2018, the revised development plan for creation of basic amenities on Sri Kshetra Dehu-Alandi, Bhandara Hills, Pandharpur and Palkhital route has been approved at a cost of Rs. 1427.85 crore has been sanctioned. It was under the consideration of the Government to make available the funds budgeted for the financial year 2018-19 to the Divisional Commissioner, Pune.
