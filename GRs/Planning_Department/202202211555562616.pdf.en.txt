Read 1) Government Decision Synopsis dated 25/01/2022 2) Government Decision Planning Division No.MagroRo-2021/ PR No.29/ ROHYO-10A, dated 11/11/2021
GOVERNMENT RECOGNITION: Under the" Matoshree Gram Samruddhi Khet/ Panand Road Scheme," the above mentioned Government Decision dated 25/01/2022 has approved the scheme of Farm/ Panand Road for the financial year 2021-22 subject to certain conditions. Condition No. 2) Read the following.
Before commencing the work on the farm/ watery roads mentioned in Annexure-A, it should be ensured that the farm/ watery roads are included in the relevant village map of the Revenue Department or that the farm/ watery road has come into existence under the orders of the concerned competent authority.
Also, in cases where the farm/ watery road is not included in the relevant village map of the Revenue Department or there is no order of the concerned competent authority regarding the farm/ watery road, the written consent of the owner of the farm/ land through which the farm/ watery road passes should be obtained.
The consent letter should be mentioned in the property register/ road register of the Gram Panchayat in connection with the work of the farm/ panand road.
