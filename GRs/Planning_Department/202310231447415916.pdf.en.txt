The proposal: At the level of the Prime Minister, the progress of 28 agenda points and 13 welfare schemes is reviewed from time to time. The 28 points of the No. 5 Procedures to be followed for review of welfare schemes at State level
Just as the 13 welfare schemes are being reviewed at the level of the Hon'ble Prime Minister, the State also needs to plan time bound programmes to ensure that the benefits of their welfare schemes reach the last mile beneficiaries. Therefore, the government was considering the formation of a committee to formulate a time-bound programme for the schemes and implementation of the administrative departments such as Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Agriculture and Padum Department, Tribal Development Department, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Women and Child Development Department.
Government Decision: The following committees are being formed for the time-bound implementation of welfare schemes in the state.
