Read the above statement. The Maharashtra Institution for Transformation (MITRA) has been established under the control of the Planning Department in the state of Maharashtra. Also read the above. 2 As per the decision of the Government here, a new head of account has been introduced for the expenses of the' Mitra' organization. Read above. 3 As per the decision of the Government herein, in order to pass the payments of the expenses of the newly created institution' Mitra', the Cell Officer (Cash Branch Office-1422) was declared as the' Drawing and Disbursing Officer' and the Deputy/ Joint Secretary (Office Office-1444-A) as the' Controlling Officer' for the said institution on temporary basis till further orders.
Now that the office of' Mitra' has been opened regularly, the Research Officer on the establishment of' Mitra' has been asked to approve the proposal for declaring' Drawing and Disbursing Officer' and Joint Secretary to the Chief Executive Officer as' Controlling Officer'. 3 It was under consideration of the Government to cancel the order issued by the Government here to declare' Drawing and Disbursing Officer' and' Controlling Officer' temporarily.
The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Research Officer on Establishment of' MITRA' for Maharashtra Institution for Transformation (Pay Scale S-16 Rs. 44900-142400) to' Drawing and Disbursing Officer' and Joint Secretary to CEO (Pay Scale S-27 Rs. 118500-214100) are being declared as' Controlling Officer'.
