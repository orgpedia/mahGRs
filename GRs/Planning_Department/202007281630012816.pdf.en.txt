A total of 51 temporary posts, 17 each in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of Maharashtra Development Board and 6 temporary posts created in the Planning Department (itself) were extended from 1/5/2020 to 30/6/2020 under the Planning Department, Government Decision No. EST-2020/ PR No. 32/ K. 1426, dated 28/5/2020.
The final decision on extension of the period beyond 30th April, 2020 for Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and remaining Maharashtra Development Boards is yet to be taken. Therefore, a total of 57 temporary posts, namely 51 temporary posts in the establishment of these development boards and 6 temporary posts in the planning department, could not be extended for a period beyond 30/6/2020. In the above background, a proposal was under consideration of the Government for extension of 57 temporary posts from 1/7/2020 to 31/8/2020 in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and Rest of Maharashtra Development Board to complete 17 temporary posts each and 6 temporary posts in the Planning Department (itself) for keeping the records of the offices of these development boards updated till the final decision on the extension beyond 30/4/2020, Utilization Certificate regarding the utilization of special funds allocated to the development boards, audit objections and other ancillary works.
