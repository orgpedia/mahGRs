The Proposal:
As per the Government decision dated 13.11.2018, the development plan for creation of basic amenities on Shri Kshetra Dehu-Alandi, Bhandara Hills, Pandharpur and Palkhital route has been approved at a cost of Rs. 1427.85 crore has been approved for Government participation. It was under the consideration of the Government to make available the funds budgeted for the financial year 2020-21 to the Divisional Commissioner, Pune for this scheme.
Government's decision:
Under the development plan for the construction of basic facilities on the Shree Kshetra Dehu-Alandi, Bhandara-Dongar, Pandharpur and Palkhital route, the Divisional Commissioner, Pune, has been allocated a total of Rs. 14,60,61, 650/- (Rupees Fourteen Crores Sixty Lakhs, Sixty One Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty only) is being disbursed.
