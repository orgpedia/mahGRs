Preamble- A Screening Committee has been constituted on a pilot basis for Aurangabad Division under the Reference Government Resolution dated 17th June, 2016 to scrutinize the proposal before giving administrative technical approval to the works under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme-Maharashtra. The Committee has been given extension from the Government Resolution dated 29th March, 2019 to 17th March, 2020. During the recent review meeting held by the Hon'ble Minister (ROHYO) on 10th November, 2020, it has come to notice that the regular and timely meetings of the Scrutiny Committees are not taking place leading to delay in sanctioning of works and adversely affecting the implementation of the scheme. Therefore, the Scrutiny Committee constituted for Aurangabad Division on a pilot basis under the Government Decision dated June 17, 2016, stands cancelled
The matter was under consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
