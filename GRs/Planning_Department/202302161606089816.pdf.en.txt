As 80 to 90 per cent of the total work under MNREGA in the district is being done by the panchayat system, the government was considering to give the district programme coordinator and the chief executive officer with the authority to establish all the employees of the district council, panchayat committee and panchayat system.
Government's stand:
The Government Circular dated 25 th August, 2021 of the Department of Planning (ROHYO) has laid down the policy for management of manpower made available on contract basis for the Department of Planning (ROHYO) through CSC SPV e-Governance Services India Limited, a Centrally Sponsored Enterprise. It is proposed that the proposal for providing new manpower on contract basis as mentioned in para 2 of the said Government Decision should be submitted through the Joint District Programme Coordinator and Chief Executive Officer instead of the District Programme Coordinator and Collector.
Further, the powers of appointment of contractual staff mentioned in the Government Resolution dated 29th September, 2017 of the Planning (ROHYO) Department have been delegated to the District Programme Coordinators and Collectors, which are being partially modified to include the Joint District Programme Coordinators and CEOs in place of them only in respect of contractual staff working in the Panchayat system.
Similarly, the powers regarding the discipline and service of these contractual employees will remain with the District Programme and Chief Executive Officer.
