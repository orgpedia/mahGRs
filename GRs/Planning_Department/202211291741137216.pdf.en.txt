1) Ma. The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. 2797/2015 dated 4/8/2017 has invalidated the reservation in promotion and the Govt. The Supreme Court As the High Court has not yet stayed the decision, the decision to fill all the vacancies in the promotion quota with the status of 25/4/2004 was taken under the Government Decision dated 7/5/2021 of the General Administration Department.
2) This promotion is only temporary. The Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court shall be subject to final disposal.
3) The promotion is subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
4) The officer should be promoted immediately.
(5) The officers to be promoted under this order shall not be entitled to any regular promotion i.e. seniority.
6) Under the Government decision dated 12/9/2016 of the General Administration Department," If an officer refuses to accept the post of promotion after or before being selected for promotion to the upper cadre, his name will be removed from the selection list of officers eligible for promotion and the eligibility of the concerned officer for promotion will be checked in the third year selection list without considering the name of the concerned officer in the next two years selection lists. If the concerned officers are found eligible for promotion as per the merit of that time, they will be included in the regular selection list. A promotion cannot be denied after the appointment.
7) The officers in this promotion order who have not been given the benefit of Assured Progress Scheme under Time-bound Promotion/ Assured Progress/ Revised Service should be fixed in the pay scale of the post of promotion as per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019.
8) Take charge of the post of promotion within 30 days from the date of issue of this order. Employees who do not accept promotion within the prescribed period after receipt of this promotion order or who refuse to accept posting under various sub-sections, will be deprived of the benefits sanctioned under the scheme of advancement under the revised service as per the Seventh Pay Commission as per the Finance Department, Government Resolution No. Further, after withdrawal of such benefits, the salary of the concerned will be determined as per the provisions of Rule 12 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981, considering that they were not given the benefit of this scheme.
9) Ma. The officers concerned are being promoted subject to the final award in respect of original application No. 694/2018 filed at Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Bench Aurangabad.
