Letter No. GENI/ KASSANAS/ KA 2/2018-19/ KAVI- 2552, dated 28/12/2018 GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the provisions of Section 12 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998, a provision has been made in Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 regarding the reduction of an" Executive Committee" headed by the Chairman of the District Planning Committee to assist the District Planning Committee. Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, it is necessary to appoint 2" nominated members" out of the total" nominated members" under paragraph 2 (b) of section 3 (3) of the said Act and 2" special invitees" out of the total" special invitees" members as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3).
As per the provisions of Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and in accordance with paragraphs 4 (a) and (f) of Section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, the District Planning Committee, Nanded is being constituted as follows, with the nomination of the following Hon'ble Members of Parliament and Members of Legislative Assembly of Nanded District, as recommended by the Chairman and Guardian Minister of the District Planning Committee, Nanded.
