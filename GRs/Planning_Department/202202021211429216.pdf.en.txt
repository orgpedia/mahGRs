Preamble Planning Division, vide Government Resolution No: 2015/ P. No.6/ K. 1444 dated 4th June, 2015, has prescribed the" Policy Guidelines for Finalization and Implementation of Pilgrimage, Tourist Destination and Area Development Plan in the State." As per the policy, the development plan for pilgrimage sites and areas in rural areas will be implemented by the Rural Development Department, the development plan for pilgrimage sites and areas in urban areas will be implemented by the Urban Development Department and the development plan for tourist places will be implemented by the Tourism Department. Also, the Planning Department, Government Decision No.
1444 dated 19th July, 2016 specifying the provisions and criteria in accordance with the" Policy Guidelines for Finalization and Implementation of Pilgrimage, Tourist Destination-Environment Development Scheme in the State."
The matter was under consideration of the Government for inclusion of the development plan of the pilgrimage/ religious places and localities to be implemented by the Minority Development Department in the" Paragraph No. e" of the Planning Department, Government Resolution No.: T.
GOVERNMENT REFERENCE: Planning Department, Government Decision No: TEE- 2015/ No.6/ No.1444, dated 4th June, 2015, is being amended in Paragraph No. e and in this Government Decision, Paragraph No. e shall be read as follows: (e)" Considering the scope and nature of the development plans of important sites of international/ national importance, pilgrimage sites and precincts mentioned in the Budget Speech, the relevant plans shall be implemented by the Planning Department as per the directions of the Government. The development plans of the pilgrim centres and localities in the urban/ rural areas will be implemented under the respective Urban Development/ Rural Development departments. Tourist Sites
