Government's decision:
The Government is pleased to approve the appointment of 2 members of the nominated members of the Legislative Assembly/ Members of Parliament, 4 nominated members having knowledge of district planning and 14 persons ordinarily resident in the area of district planning committee as special invitees to the District Planning Committee of Satara district as indicated in the prospectus A attached herewith in accordance with the provisions of clause (3) (2) (b) and (3) (c) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000 and clause (4) (f) of clause (3) of the Act.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Satara should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments made by the above-mentioned nominees and special invitees to the Satara District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
