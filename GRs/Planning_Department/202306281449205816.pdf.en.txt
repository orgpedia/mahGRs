As per the provisions of the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 and the Government Resolution of the Department of Women and Child Development in Reference No.1, an Internal Women's Grievance Committee was constituted under the Department of Employment, under Reference Order No.2, to inquire into complaints of sexual harassment of women. However, Member of the Women's Grievance Redressal Committee of the Department, Shri. Manjusha A. Karande, Under-Secretary, being promoted to the post of Deputy Secretary and posted to the Second Division, and other members of the Committee, Sh. Neelima P. Chaudhary, Cell Officer and Mr. Nanda A. Parab, Cell Officer are presently under consideration for transfer under the Maharashtra General Transfer Act-2005 and are expected to be transferred to other departments. Accordingly, the Committee is reconstituted as follows:
1) Mrs. Charushila. Chaudhary, Joint Secretary to the President 2) Smt. Dr. Vaishali Kolhe, Associate Professor Non-official Member (Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai) Smt. Sonawane, Member Secretary, Cell Officer
