Government circular
The provisional seniority of the officers who have been appointed in the cadre of" Joint Director, Group-A" in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics during the period from 1/01/2022 to 31/12/2022 through regular promotion, direct service or other prescribed route is being published as per" Annexure-A" attached to this Government Circular. Provisional promotions in this seniority list are being published subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.

The officers concerned shall submit a representation to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai within a period of 15 days from the date of this circular, stating any factual errors, omissions or objections with regard to the temporary seniority of the" Joint Director, Group-A" cadre in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics as on 01/01/2023.
