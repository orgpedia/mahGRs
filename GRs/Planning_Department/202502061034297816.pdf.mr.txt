२. खालील अटी व शर्ती आभासी वैयक्तीक ठेव लेखा प्रशासक [Virtual Personal Deposit
Account Administrator (VPDAA)] यांना बंधनकारक राहतील :
(१) The said VPDA shall be subject to Audit.
(२) The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms, and conditions
of the relevant GR have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
(३) No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds.
(४) VPDA is applicable only for State Scheme and not for Central sector and
centrally sponsored schemes.
(५) The Receipt to the said VPDA shall be only such grants drawn under MTR ४४
for ३१- Grants in Aid (Non Salary) & ३५-Grants in Aid for creation of Capital Assets
under the CRC २२२५F५२१. No amount under Capital heads of expenditure shall
be transferred to VPDA. The Administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation
Certificate after incurring expenditure from the VPDA.
(६) The Expenditure from the VPDA shall be as per the Sanction order of the
respective Grant.
