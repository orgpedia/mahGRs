1) Take charge of the post of promotion in accordance with this order and the certificate of transfer of charge for doing so should be submitted to the government. A copy of the same should be forwarded to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai. If you do not want to accept the promotion, you should inform the government in writing.
2) Ma. The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. 2797/2015 dated 4/8/2017 has invalidated the reservation in promotion and the The Supreme Court Since the High Court has not yet stayed the decision, the decision to fill all the vacancies in the promotion quota with the status of 25/4/2004 was taken under the Government Decision dated 7/5/2021 of the General Administration Department.
3) This promotion is only temporary. The Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court shall be subject to final disposal.
4) This promotion is being given subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
5) These officers should be promoted immediately.
(6) The officers to be promoted under this order shall not be entitled to any regular promotion i.e. seniority.

7) As per the Government decision dated 12/9/2016 of the General Administration Department," If an officer refuses to accept the post of promotion after or before being selected for promotion to the upper cadre, his name will be removed from the selection list of officers eligible for promotion and the eligibility of the concerned officers for promotion will be checked in the third year selection list without considering the names of the concerned officers in the selection lists to be held for the next two years. 8) The officers in this promotion order who have not been given the benefit of the assured progress scheme under the time-bound promotion/ assured progress/ revised service should be fixed in the pay scale of the promoted post as per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019.
9) Take charge of the post of promotion within 30 days from the date of issue of this order. Employees who do not accept promotion within the prescribed period after receipt of this promotion order or who refuse to accept posting under various sub-sections will be deprived of the benefits sanctioned under the scheme of promotion under the revised service as per the Seventh Pay Commission as per the Finance Department, Government Decision No. However, such benefits will not be recovered. Further, after withdrawal of such benefits, the salary of the concerned shall be determined as if they had not been given the benefit of this scheme, as per the provisions of Rule 12 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Salaries) Rules, 1981.
10). The officers concerned are being promoted subject to the final decision in respect of original application No. 694/2018 filed at Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Bench Aurangabad.
