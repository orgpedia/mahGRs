Government circular:
The provisional seniority of the officers of the cadre of Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted) in the Directorate of Finance& Statistics as on 01/01/2023 (from 01/01/2022 to 31/12/2022) is being published as per Annexure-A annexed to this circular. The notification has been issued by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC).
2. m. The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. Against the decision dated 04/08/2017 in 2797/2015, the State Govt. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed in the Supreme Court. The General Administration Department vide its letter No. BCC-2017/ PR No. 312A/ 16-B dated 29/12/2017, with the concurrence of the Department of Law& Justice, has decided to fill the vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota on purely temporary basis as per the merit of service. Instructions have been issued to be filled subject to the decision of Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court. In view of the aforesaid instructions, promotion to the vacant posts in the open category in the promotion quota in the cadre of" Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted)" has been granted only on temporary basis. So Ma. After the final decision of the Supreme Court, the said seniority of the" Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted)" may be revised.
(3) The officers concerned shall submit a representation to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai, within a period of 15 days from the date of this circular, regarding any factual errors, omissions or objections with regard to the temporary seniority of the Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted) cadre in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics as on 01/01/2023. The Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai should examine the facts of the representation received from the available records of the concerned and send the representation along with detailed comments to the Government at the earliest.
4. The Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai may obtain a copy of this circular and the accompanying provisional seniority list from the website and send the same to the Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group- (B Gazetted) Cadre.
