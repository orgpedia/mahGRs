Government's decision:
In pursuance of the provisions of clause (3) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, vide Government Resolution No. DAP 2016/ PR No. 88/ K. 1481, dated 15/7/2016, 4 special invitees were appointed to Wardha District Planning Committee. The Government is pleased to approve the appointment of Shri Milind Dadaji Bhende as a" Special Invitee" to the District Planning Committee of Wardha district in the vacancy caused by the death of Shri Ramesh Motiramji Varkad.
