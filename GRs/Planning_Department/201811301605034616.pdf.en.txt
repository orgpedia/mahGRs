November 14, 2018 Preamble: Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) As per the Government Circular dated 9th October, 2012, the works of continuous leveling chars, cattle barrier chars, rough stone bunds, compartment bunding, slab bunding, gabion bunds, earthen drain bunds, etc. are being carried out by the Agriculture Department. As per the Government decision dated 3rd September, 2016, the Agriculture Department has been empowered to issue, fill, pass and provide skilled/ unskilled inputs as Programme Officer under the Fruit Plantation Programme to be implemented on the farm of individual beneficiaries. As per the Government Decision dated 14.12.2016, the competent authorities in the Agriculture Department have been empowered to issue, fill, pass and provide skilled/ unskilled inputs in respect of NADEP composting, vermi composting, farm and nursery works. In the current year, considering the scarcity-like situation, there is likely to be a huge demand for labour work at the regional level. Therefore
