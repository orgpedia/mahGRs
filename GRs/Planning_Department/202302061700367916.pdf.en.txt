4. Regional Rural Banks are involved in the economic development of their areas of operation. Therefore, in order to facilitate the active participation of all the members in the discussions in this regard, the Regional Rural Banks are appointed as the government representatives on the said bank. It is, therefore, mandatory for the Government appointed representatives to attend the meetings of the Board of Directors on occasions other than the most urgent and important matters/ meetings in their office. Absence for prohibited purposes shall be required to be intimated in writing to the Government as well as to the concerned Regional Rural Bank.
This Government decision is based on the provisions of the Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976 vide Chapter No. It is being issued in exercise of powers vested in the Government under Section 9 (1) (e) of Section 3.
