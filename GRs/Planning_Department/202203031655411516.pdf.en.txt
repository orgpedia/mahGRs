Guarantee Scheme- A total of 1073 posts in Maharashtra Commissionerate were extended from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022. The said 1073 posts are required for effective implementation of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. Therefore, a total of 1073 posts including 1052 posts of Divisional Commissioner, District Collector, Zilla Parishad and Taluka level are being considered for extension from 01.03.2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the powers conferred on the Ministerial Administrative Departments under the Government Decision No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 22.02.2022, to extend the tenure of the temporary posts in the offices under them, the Government has approved the extension of a total of 1073 posts under the Employment Guarantee Scheme from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022, including a total of 1052 posts in the offices of Divisional Commissioner, Collector, Zilla Parishad and Taluk level offices and 21 posts created for the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Maharashtra, Nagpur Commissionerate. The details of the posts are given in Annexure No. are shown in Figures 1 to 5.
Vacancies of more than six months in the sanctioned posts will be excluded.
