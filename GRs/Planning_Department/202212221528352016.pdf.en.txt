See:- (1) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998 (2 of 1998) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, 2000 (3 of 2000) Letter from the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Kolhapur No. 21.12.22.
Government's decision:
The Government is pleased to approve the appointment of 02 members of the nominated members from amongst the Members of Legislative Assembly/ Members of Parliament, 04 nominated members having knowledge of district planning and 14 persons ordinarily resident in the area of district planning committee and having experience of district planning as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Kolhapur district as indicated in the prospectus attached herewith as per the provisions of clause (3) (2) (b) (c) and clause (3) (4) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Kolhapur should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominated members as well as special invitees to the Kolhapur District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
