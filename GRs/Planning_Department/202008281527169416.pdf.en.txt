Under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, the works of new irrigation wells should be sanctioned in each Gram Panchayat in proportion to the population of the Gram Panchayat as mentioned below.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Gram Panchayat Population | Number of irrigation wells to be sanctioned |
| up to 1500.               | 05                                          |
| Between 1500 and 3000     | 10                                          |
| Between 3000 and 5000     | 15                                          |
| The next 5000.            | 20                                          |
