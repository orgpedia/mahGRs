Under the" Support for Statistical Strengthening (SSS)" programme being implemented by the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, Government of India, it is proposed to develop a mechanism at the State level for regular compilation and monitoring of indicators for implementation of 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 169 targets. Further, the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, Government of India is expected to create a State level mechanism for the" Collection and Compilation of Statistics for Local Area Planning including Strengthening Monitoring Framework for Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)" as per the 20 Core Statistical Activity specified under the" Support for Statistical Strengthening (SSS)" programme of the Government of India.

On 22nd January, 2018, the Control Room" Action Room to Reduce Poverty" in collaboration with the United Nations adopted 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 169 targets to achieve the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. Completed by March 31, 2020. In this, the Department has completed the important preparatory planning to align the various (i) State Level Schemes and (ii) District Level Schemes implemented by the administrative departments with the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 169 targets. In this context, the government was considering the establishment of a" Sustainable Development Goals-Implementation and Coordination Center" under the Directorate of Finance and Statistics to achieve 17 Sustainable Development Goals and 169 targets in the state by 2030.

Continuing the groundwork laid by the Action Room to Reduce Poverty, established in collaboration with the United Nations on 22nd January, 2018, the Action Room to Reduce Poverty, set up by the Department of Planning earlier, has been set up to achieve the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 169 targets adopted by the United Nations (UN) during the Decade of Action 2020-2030, at the state level, with the objective of (i) effective implementation, (ii) concurrent monitoring and insightful evaluation.
The Sustainable Development Goals-Implementation& Co-ordination Centre (SDG-ICC), to be set up under the Directorate of Finance& Statistics, will act as a link between the Ministry of Statistics& Programme Implementation, Government of India and NITI Aayog and the respective Ministerial Administrative Divisions of the State Governments.
The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Implementation and Coordination Centre (SDG-ICC), a Cell under the Directorate General of Finance and Statistics, will adopt a five-pillar strategy to achieve the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 169 targets:
Comparative Analysis of Outcomes and Policy Advice (b) Knowledge Aggregation and Implementation of Strategies (c) Data Collection and Statistical Design Framework for SIF and DIF (d) Information, Education, Outreach and Capacity Building Activities (e) Organisational Partnership and NGO, Civil Society Involvement (f) Finance and Statistics Assange-1319/ 485/ Q.No. 69/ Ka-1417, D. (i) State Level Committees constituted as on 9th October, 2020

The officers of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics as specified in Annexure-A for the Cell" Sustainable Development Goals-Implementation& Co-ordination Centre" under the Directorate of Finance and Statistics shall continue to function in the Cell assuming the duties and responsibilities of their respective posts as" Additional Responsibilities." It will be done by the State Implementation Committee constituted as on 9th October, 2020.
The Sustainable Development Goals-Implementation& Co-ordination Centre (SDG-ICC) Cell under the Directorate of Finance& Statistics will function from the office of the present Additional Director (Evaluation), Directorate of Finance& Statistics, New Administrative Building, 7th Floor, Mantralaya, Mumbai. A well-equipped campus and state-of-the-art IT facility will be provided for the Sustainable Development Goals-Implementation& Co-ordination Centre (SDG-ICC) Cell.
The expenditure for the Control Room of the Sustainable Development Goals-Implementation& Co-ordination Centre (SDG-ICC) under the Directorate of Finance and Statistics will be met from the funds available under the Central Government's Support for Statistical Strengthening (SSS) and the State Government's Demand No. O-9," Main Account Head-3454- Census, Survey and Statistics, (03) (15) Implementation of State Statistical System (State Sponsored Schemes) (Programme) (34540483)" will be met from the funds available under the scheme.
