The Government has decided to provide administrative technical sanction powers under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in Maharashtra. The committee was constituted on June 17, 2016. The committee has been constituted for a period of nine months. The term of the committee. The Committee adopted a resolution on 16 March 2017.
