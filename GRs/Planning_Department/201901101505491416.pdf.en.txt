The Proposal:
" Chandrapur and Sindhudurg, at the two ends of Maharashtra, are blessed with mineral and natural resources. A special programme of intensive planning and implementation will be implemented to ensure optimum utilisation of natural resources in these districts. Following the announcement, the state government has selected two districts- Chandrapur and Sindhudurg- for micro-planning and implementation.
