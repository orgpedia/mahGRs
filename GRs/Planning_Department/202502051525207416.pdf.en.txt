The Proposal -
On December 9, 2024, Ma. The meeting chaired by the Chief Minister with the Secretaries of all the Administrative Departments has been directed to take immediate action as per the instructions given in the chronicle and all the Administrative Departments have been directed to submit their at least 100 days' report of results.
In this regard, the Department's website is updated, maximum information of the Department is published on the website in terms of information, the information of the best performing website is kept in the cabinet, the Dedication Mechanism is prepared in coordination with the Administrative Department and the Information and Technology Department. The Chief Minister has directed to take action. Accordingly, a committee is under consideration at the government level to take action on various matters such as updating the website of Employment Guarantee Scheme Ward in the Planning Department, publishing maximum information of the department on the website in terms of information, keeping the information of the best performing website before the cabinet, creating a Dedication Mechanism in coordination with the Administrative Department and the Information and Technology Department, as follows.
Decisions of the Government: -
A committee is being constituted at the government level to take action on various matters like updating the website of the Employment Guarantee Scheme Ward under the Planning Department, publishing maximum information of the department on the website in terms of information, keeping the information of the best performing website before the cabinet, preparing the Dedication Mechanism in coordination with the Administrative Department and the Information and Technology Department, as follows.
