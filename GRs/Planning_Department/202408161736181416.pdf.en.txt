The following conditions shall be applicable to the Virtual Personal Deposit account (VPDA)
(1) The said VPDA shall be the subject to audit.
(2) The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms, and conditions of the
relevant GR have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
(3) No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds.
(4) The Receipt to the said VPDA shall be only such grants drawn under MTR 44 as
mentioned in the Finance Department GR dated 14.02.2024. No amount under capital
heads and Loan Heads of expenditure shall be transferred to VPDA. The Administrator shall
timely submit the Utilisation Certificate after incurring expenditure from the VPDA.
(5) The expenditure from the VPDA shall be as per the sanction order of the respective
Grant.
(6) The unspent balances under the VPDA shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund.
after completion of one year from the date of drawal of the funds/date of credit of
amount to the VPDA or the timeline mentioned in the sanction order whichever is
earlier.
(7) The Administrator with the District Treasury Officer shall reconcile the balances under
the VPDA at the end of every calendar month.
(8) The balances shall be reconciled with the AG office by the Treasury Officer who shall
submit Balance Certificate to A.G. Office for confirmation of their balances in the books of
A.G at the end of the Financial Year.
(9) All the conditions / instructions in the Finance Department G.R.No. Narrow / 2023
Q. No. 110/2023 Treasuries - 5 dated 14/02/2024 shall follow.
(10) All rules including Rule 495 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968 shall be
applicable to the VPDA.
