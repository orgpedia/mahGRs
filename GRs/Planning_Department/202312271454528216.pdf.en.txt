The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 2000.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the provisions of clause (3) of clause (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve to nominate 03 persons as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Hingoli District, who are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and have experience in district planning.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Hingoli should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The nomination of special invitees to the Hingoli District Planning Committee mentioned in the prospectus' A' shall remain in force until further orders or until such nomination is cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
