The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, New Delhi has issued a notification for the determination of posts under the State Government as per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016. As per the notification dated 04.01.2021, the list of posts reserved for Divyang has been published. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has issued instructions to ensure that the posts of Group' A' to Group' D' posts in the state government establishments, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale in the list published by the central government are similar to the posts in the state government service, even if the designations of such posts are different. Issued on 02.02.2021.
Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, New Delhi. The posts are ensured for the disabled through the notification dated 04/01/2021. As decided for the post of peon in Group-D cadre in that list, the posts are being ensured through this Government decision for the disabled as shown in the prospectus-A in the Planning Department (itself).
Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, New Delhi. This Government decision is being issued in accordance with the notification dated 04/01/2021 and dated 02/02/2021 of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
