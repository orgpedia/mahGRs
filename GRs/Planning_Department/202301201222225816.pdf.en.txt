The Maharashtra government has decided to declare an Assistant Accounts Officer in the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur, as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS).
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Planning (ROH)
Government Resolution No: Aastha-2023/ P.No.
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: January 20, 2023
Read:- Government Decision, Planning Department No. Establishment-2016/ No. 29/ Mgr.O-3, D. 28.12.2023.
Under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), the State government has created posts of divisional commissioners, district collectors, zilla parishads and taluk panchayats. After the approval of the high-powered committee constituted by the Finance Department in this review, the revised pattern of posts has been approved under the relevant government decision. As per the revised format, the post of Divisional Commissioner, Accounts Officer in Nagpur office has been abolished. Government Resolution, Planning Division No. Establishment-2013/ No.6/ MGR-3, Dy. As on 20.2.2013, the Accounts Officer in the office of Divisional Commissioner was declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The question of declaring a new Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur was under the consideration of the Government as the post of the Accountant in that office had lapsed. The government has taken the following steps in this regard:
Government's decision:
In order to facilitate the work of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme in the state, under this government decision, the Assistant Accounts Officer in the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur is being declared as the" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and he is being given the powers of this post.
This Government Resolution is being issued under the powers conferred on the Department under Section 156 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968, Clause-1.
