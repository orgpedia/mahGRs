The proposal-
As per the Government Decision No. 2021/ PR No. 33/ KA-1411 dated 28/03/2022 of the Planning Department, the scheme for providing civic services and facilities to the" Urban Local Bodies" has been approved and guidelines have been issued for the works to be done under it.
In 2021-22, Rs. 800 crore had been released to the State government.
Government's decision
As there is a short time left for the end of the financial year 2021-22, it is not possible to provide information about the works of civic services and facilities and the funds required for it by various urban local bodies. Therefore, as a special case, the funds made in the budget are being deposited in the account of District Planning Officer, Pune under the Collector, Pune, in Canara Bank, Pune.
(2) After separate orders are issued from the government level regarding the list of works to be done urban local bodies and the expenditure on it, the funds should be distributed to the local bodies concerned by the District Planning Officer, Pune under the Collector, Pune.
3. Funds are being disbursed for the schemes' Civic Services and Amenities in Municipal Corporations' and' Civic Services and Amenities in Municipalities/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats' as under:
