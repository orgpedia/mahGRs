On being nominated as a special invitee to the Nashik District Planning Committee...
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Planning
Government Resolution No: DAP-2023/ PR No.99/ KA-1481-A
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: December 26, 2023.
The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 2000.
Government's decision:
In pursuance of the provisions of clause (3) of clause (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government is pleased to approve the nomination of 07 persons to the District Planning Committee of Nashik District as" Special Invitees" who are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and have experience in district planning.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Nashik should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The nomination of special invitees mentioned in the prospectus' A' to the Nashik District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such nomination is cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
