Preamble: As per the Government decisions dated 17.12.2009 and 27.1.2010 of the Planning Department, it has been decided to provide funds to the tune of Rs. 3.00 crore per financial year to the District Coordination Committee for Gadchiroli, Gondia, Chandrapur and Bhandara districts under the Special Action Programme for immediate resolution of local level problems in Naxal affected areas. Accordingly, as per the concurrence of the Finance Department, the matter of providing funds to the District Collectors of Gadchiroli, Gondia, Chandrapur and Bhandara for the financial year 2022-23 was under consideration of the Government.
The decision was taken at a meeting of the District Coordination Committee (DCC) headed by the Collectors of Chandrapur, Gadchiroli, Bhandara and Gondia districts. In order to enable prompt resolution of local issues by selecting schemes/ facilities of collective benefit based on the guidelines given under the relevant Government decision dated 17.12.2009, the following funds are being provided to Gadchiroli, Gondia, Chandrapur and Bhandara districts for the financial year 2022-23:
