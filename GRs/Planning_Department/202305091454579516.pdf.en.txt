PLAN-RA-1/ Special. / 2022-23/195, Dated 21.1.2023.
Government's decision:
The Government is pleased to approve the appointment of 9 persons as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Ratnagiri district, who are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and have experience in District Planning, as indicated in the statement A attached herewith under clause (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000.
District Collector, District Planning Committee, Ratnagiri should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments made by the above-mentioned nominees and special invitees to the Ratnagiri District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
