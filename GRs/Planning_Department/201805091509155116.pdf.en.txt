Government's decision
The Government Circular dated 15/12/2017 of the General Administration Department has detailed the procedure for online submission of performance appraisal reports of Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) cadre officers using MahaPAR system from the year 2017-18. For Group-A and Group-B (Gazetted) officers of the concerned service, the Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary controlling that service has been declared as" Co-ordinating Officer" and" Cadre Controlling Officer" for recording performance appraisal report in MahaPAR system.
Further, the Under Secretary/ Office Officer of the concerned office who controls the concerned service has been declared as" EMD Manager." Accordingly, Deputy Secretary (Office Post-1427) and Office Officer (Office Post-1427) have been declared as" Co-ordinating Officer" and" EMD Manager" respectively.
As mentioned in the Government Circular No.CFR-1217/ PR No. 131/ XIII of the General Administration Department, dated 15th December, 2017, the following officers are required to be declared for the office of the Directorate of Economics and Statistics under the Department of Planning for the implementation of the MahaPAR system and to be delegated/ entrusted with the powers in the said system: 1) Primary Custodian:- O. U.
The officers in the department will have the right to see the performance appraisal report and remove the wrong performance appraisal report before it is self-appraised.
2) Alternate Custodian:- Preparation of performance appraisal report of the officers after preparing the work-flow of the officers and the information in Part-1 thereof.
