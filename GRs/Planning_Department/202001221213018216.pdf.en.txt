The MLA Local Area Development Programme (MLALAD) for the financial year 2019-20 has been enhanced to Rs. 50.00 lakhs.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Planning
Government Resolution No. Sthavika- 1119/ PR No. 139/ KA- 1482
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is January 22, 2020
Read:- Government Resolution, Planning Department, No. 0716/ PR No. 96/ KA-1482 dated 12.7.2016
An amount of Rs. 2.00 crore is admissible to each Assembly Constituency for each financial year under MLA Local Area Development Programme. Prior to the General Elections to the Legislative Assembly held in October, 2019, an amount of Rs. 2.00 crore per Assembly Constituency was approved for spending under the MLA Local Area Development Programme for the financial year 2019-20. After the general elections held in October, 2019, a new Legislative Assembly has been formed and 288 members have been elected. All these MLAs are required to make available funds for development works under MLA Local Area Development Programme in their constituencies for the remaining period of 2019-20. Accordingly, Rs. 50.00 lakh was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
During the financial year 2019-20, Rs. 2.00 crore has been made available to all the 288 members of the Legislative Assembly of the State under the MLA Local Area Development Programme permissible per Assembly Constituency wise. In addition to this fund, an additional fund of Rs. 50.00 lakh is being made available to the newly elected members of all 288 Assembly Constituencies constituency-wise, for a total amount of Rs. 144.00 crore.
The details are attached.
Further disbursement of this fund should be made immediately subject to the provisions of the Reference Government Decision dated 12.7.2016. The District Collector should approve the works that fit into the guidelines prescribed for this programme by the government. The District Collector should ensure that the duration of the code of conduct of the general elections at various levels and the funds released by the Election Commission are fully utilized. Also, all the necessary procedures should be completed within the stipulated time.
