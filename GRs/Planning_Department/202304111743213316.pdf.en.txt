Preamble: With a view to improve the implementation of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) and to ensure effective implementation of the scheme in the State, to ensure optimum utilization of Central funds for maintaining speed and quality of work, etc. As per the decision of the government here, the power to give administrative and technical approval for work under MNREGA was decentralized to the taluka level with financial limits. Since then, two computer systems, NREGA Soft and Secure, have been used in the implementation of MGNREGA. At present, the Secure Computer System has been implemented only in the Panchayat Operational System. Apart from this, the Secure Computer System is not applicable to other operating systems.

Also, since the ratio of unskilled and skilled should be maintained at 60:40 at the district level, it has become necessary for the government to instruct the gram panchayats to take some work as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Gram Panchayat Act and the need to maintain the ratio. Since the objective of MGNREGA is to" strengthen the means of livelihood of the poor," in pursuance of this objective, reference No. The Government has decided to implement the" Mission for Well-Being Families and Sarvagina Gram Samriddhi Yojana" under MNREGA.
In order to achieve the objective of the Government to complete the works under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme by increasing the speed and quality of works in the above background and to make the policy regarding the right to administrative and technical sanction more clear and relaxed, the relevant Government Decision No. Revised Government Orders are being issued in batches of 9, 11 and 12.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The powers of administrative and technical sanction for all works taken up under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme are being revised as follows.
