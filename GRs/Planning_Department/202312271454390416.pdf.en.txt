GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of clause (3) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the District Planning Officer, who is ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee, as shown in the attached statement A, shall be deemed to be a member of the District Planning Committee.
The Government is also giving approval to nominate 04 persons with experience as" Special Invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Buldhana district.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Buldhana should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The nomination of special invitees to the District Planning Committee, Buldhana, mentioned in the return" A" shall remain in force until further orders or until such nomination is cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
