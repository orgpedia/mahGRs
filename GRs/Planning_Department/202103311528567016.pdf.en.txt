the prelude
Department of Planning, Government Resolution No: SLBC-1616/ P.No.49/ KA-1417, D. As on 14th June, 2016, a District Level Committee under the Chairmanship of the District Collector has been constituted to promote, disseminate and coordinate the Central Government schemes to ensure that maximum number of youth get the benefit of Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY). The District Level Committees are being provided funds for the promotion and publicity of Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) from the financial year 2016-17 as per the demand of the respective District Collectors. There is a need to effectively publicize, disseminate and coordinate the banking related schemes of the Central and State Governments among the people in rural and remote areas of the State to make them aware of the financial inclusion initiatives implemented by the Central Government as well as other banking related schemes of the Central and State Governments and to maximize the benefits of all these public welfare schemes to the needy and needy persons. Therefore, the Government was contemplating to issue a new Government Resolution for the effective implementation of the scheme by expanding the scope of the scheme by reconstituting the" Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana District Level Coordination Committee" under the chairmanship of the Collector at the district level and providing funds to these committees as per the requirement and to promote, publicize and coordinate all Central and State Government banking related schemes through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations by superseding the Planning Department, Government Resolution No. The government has taken the following decisions:
The Planning Department, Government Resolution No: SLBC-1616/ PR No.49/ K-1417 dated 14th June, 2016 issued earlier in respect of District Level Coordination Committee of Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana under the Chairmanship of Collector is being superseded by this order.
