Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, 2" Special Invitees" out of the total number of" Nominated Members" under paragraph 2 (b) of section 3 (3) and 2" Special Invitees" out of the total number of" Nominated Members" under paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3) of the said Act are required to be appointed.
As per the provisions of Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and out of the total number of nominated members under paragraph 2 (b) of section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998 and the total number of special invitees as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3), the District Planning Committee, Satara is being constituted as follows with the nomination of the following Hon'ble MPs and MLAs of Satara district as recommended by the Chairman and Guardian Minister of the District Planning Committee.
