GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of clause (3) (2) (b) and clause (3) (3) (c) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve the appointment of two members of nomination from amongst the Members of Legislative Assembly/ Parliament, as well as 4 members having experience in district planning and 14 persons ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee, as' special invitees' to the District Planning Committee of Nagpur District, as indicated in the Annexure A attached herewith in clause (4) (f) of clause (3).
District Collector and Member Secretary District Planning Committee, Nagpur should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominees and special invitees to the Nagpur District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
