Government's decision:
As per the provision of clause (3) (4) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government is approving the appointment of 9 persons as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Hingoli district, who are residents of the area of the District Planning Committee in general and have experience in district planning as indicated in the attached statement A.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Hingoli should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments made by the above mentioned special invitees to the Hingoli District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
