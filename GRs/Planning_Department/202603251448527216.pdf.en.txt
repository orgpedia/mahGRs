The Proposal:
Department of Finance. In accordance with the Government Decision of 26.08.2025, all administrative divisions are granted temporary posts included in their designations, as well as all existing temporary positions created after the previous review has been approved but not included in the designations. An extension to 28.02.2026 was authorized. However, the process of fixing the revised contours of all administrative divisions is yet to be completed. Considering this, the government has taken the following decision.
Government Decision - All Administrative Departments are empowered to extend the term of office of temporary posts included in their designations as well as all existing temporary posts created after prior review approval but not included in the designations from 01.03.2026 to 31.08.2026.
For effective implementation of the Special Action Plan and Supplementary Plan for the rapid development of the Naxal affected area, a total of 6 posts at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur (3 posts at the level of Deputy Director-1, Research Officer-1 and Statistical Assistant-1) and 3 posts at the state level (Deputy Secretary-1, Research Officer-1B Clerk Typist-1) have been created under Government Decision No. : EPD-2510 / Q. No.294 / Ka-1421, dated 22nd July, 2010 and Supplementary Notes with serial numbers dated 18th August, 2010. As per the Government Decision dated 7th October, 2025, the temporary posts were extended for the period from 01st September, 2025 to 01st February, 2026. The government was considering extending these posts beyond March 1, 2026.
A total of 6 temporary posts, namely 3 created at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur and 3 created at the state level, for effective implementation of the Special Action Programme and Supplementary Programmes for the speedy development of the Naxal affected area, are being approved under the present Government Decision for extension for the period from 01.03.2026 to 03.03.2026 or till the finalization of the draft of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics, whichever is earlier.
