The Cabinet Committee on Infrastructure was constituted under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister. The Cabinet Infrastructure Committee has since been renamed GAIA from time to time.
At present, the Planning Department is under Government Decision No. Government Decision No. Sankarna-2022 / Q. No.5 / Ka. 1461, D. There exists a Cabinet Infrastructure Committee constituted under 28.08.2023.
The matter of reconstituting the Cabinet Infrastructure Committee was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government has taken the following decision in the matter.
