Introduction: The Maharashtra Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme is implemented in the state of Maharashtra under the Act of 1977 and the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme is implemented in the state under the Act of 2008. Under this scheme, 100 days of wages are provided by the central government and 100 days of wages are provided by the state government. Under this scheme, water conservation works, soil treatment works, road works, personal works, nursery, social forestry, and other value-added works are taken up.
Accounts head 2505 0022 (02) wages are spent on establishment grants since the formation of the department, regular land acquisition cases, honorarium of attendance assistants as per the orders of the court from time to time, skilled and unskilled funds for the work in the area of C class municipalities and town panchayats, etc.
Head of Accounts 2505 0022 (02) The matter of determining the various items to be spent from the wages was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the Government Decision dated 10/08/2010 of the Department of Finance, the expenditure figures of the Budget Estimates have been accounted for. Accordingly, the Government is approving the expenditure under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) under head 2505 0022.
