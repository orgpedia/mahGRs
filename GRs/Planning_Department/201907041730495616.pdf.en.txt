The matter of giving approval to take up farm works by converging the Employment Guarantee Scheme was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
Further, the Government is approving the convergence of the works to be taken up under the Farming Sector Scheme with the revised Form A and Form B of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) and Farming Sector Scheme (MGNREGS).
The following terms and conditions shall be applicable for implementation of the Farmlands Programme: (i) The initial 0.50 m depth of farmlands approved by the Taluk Level Committee in the scheme" Farmlands to be provided to eligible and interested beneficiaries as per the norms of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme is to be dug through labour from the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The excavation of this 0.50 m depth is mentioned in Table 7 of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The amount will be payable as per the approved rate list of 2018 as per the Government decision dated 16.4.2018.
The remaining 2.50-metre deep farmlands are to be dug under the' Magel to Farmlands" scheme.
The permissible payment for digging of 2.50 m in this way will be in proportion to the subsidy payable under the scheme of" Farming to the needy."
2) While converging the works of the farmlands under the above mentioned" Magel to Farms" scheme and" Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme," it will be necessary to start the work of the" Magel to Farms" scheme after completing the work of 0.50 m from the" Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme" by tagging its GEO and uploading its completion certificate in the Nrega soft system. Tagging the GEO of the scheme" Farming in the future" will have to be done separately before the payment of this subsidy.
(3) The guidelines of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme shall be followed while digging up to 0.50 m of farmlands through convergence.
