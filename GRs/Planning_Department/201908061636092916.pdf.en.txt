Government to implement State Employment Guarantee Scheme in the State
Detailed instructions in this regard are given under the Government Resolution dated 3rd March, 2014.
Accordingly, the State Employment Guarantee Scheme is being implemented in the" C" category Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats in the State. Further, in the State, where some Gram Panchayats have been converted into Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats and some Gram Panchayats have been included in Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats, instructions have been issued vide letter dated 21st May, 2015 to convert the works under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme in such Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats into State Employment Guarantee Scheme and to take action in respect of such works.
Due to the expansion of the boundaries of many municipal corporations in the state, some gram panchayats have been included in the municipal corporation. Under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), private and public works have been taken up. As these gram panchayats have been included in the corporation, the incomplete works cannot be funded under the MGRHOI. The concerned beneficiaries who worked on these works did not receive the funds
He has appealed to the government. It has come to the notice of the government that due to the inclusion of these gram panchayats in the municipal corporation, there is a problem in getting funds to complete the incomplete works in the respective gram panchayats.

Instructions have been issued to the Commissioner NREGA, Nagpur vide letter dated 3rd June, 2019 that such village works should not be given fresh administrative approval under MGRoHIO.
Since the funds cannot be released under MGNREGA, the Commissioner, NREGA, Nagpur is required to submit a demand proposal to the Government along with a recommendation to release the funds under the State Employment Guarantee Scheme.
Accordingly, funds will be provided by the government to complete the incomplete works in the gram panchayat as demanded by the Commissioner NREGA. The Commissioner NREGA should immediately sanction and distribute this fund as per the demand of the concerned District Collector and District Programme Coordinator.
The Commissioner NREGA, Nagpur shall ensure that the said funds are credited through FTO to the account of the beneficiaries who have made the demand. As soon as the proposals are received from the Commissioner, Nagpur, the funds will be disbursed to the Commissioner, NREGA, Nagpur. The Commissioner NREGA, Nagpur should take immediate action to sanction the funds for the completion of the incomplete works in the concerned district. All of the above recommendations should be followed.
