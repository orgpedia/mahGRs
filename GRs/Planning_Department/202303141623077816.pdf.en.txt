Government Decision: 3 temporary posts created for the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Pune Division, Pune on the establishment of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics for the control and implementation of the works under the Shree Kshetra Dehu, Alandi, Pandharpur Bhandara Hills, Palkhital, Nevasa, Area Infrastructure Development Plan and for the control and implementation of the works under the 2 Shrines Development Plan namely, Creation of Infrastructure in Shegaon Area for the Centenary Celebrations of Shri Gajanan Maharaj Samadhi and Creation of Infrastructure at Vandania Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Mozari, District Amravati
The establishment of the Directorate has been sanctioned for the continuation of 6 temporary posts created for the office of" Divisional Commissioner, Amravati Division, Amravati" till 28.02.2023. Now, the Government is approving the continuation of these 9 temporary posts for the period from 1st March, 2023 to 31st August, 2023.
