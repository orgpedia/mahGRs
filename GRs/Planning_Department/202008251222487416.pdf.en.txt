Read:- Government of Social Justice and Special Assistance Department Decision No. III-2018/ No. 26/ SAMASU, D. 13/12/2018.
Preamble: Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, vide Government Resolution No. III, 2018/ P. No. 26/ SAMASU, dated 13/12/2018, has approved the establishment of" Divisional Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board" at the divisional level for the purpose of protecting the welfare and rights of transgenders in the State. Senior officers at the field level under the Planning Department are required to be nominated as per the structure of the Zonal Tertiary Rights Protection and Welfare Board. Therefore, the matter of nominating senior officers at the field level under the Planning Department to the" Departmental Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board" was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The Deputy Commissioner (Planning), Divisional Commissioner's Office, who is working as a senior officer at the field level under the Planning Department, is being nominated to the" Divisional Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board." They should attend the meetings of the Departmental Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board from time to time on behalf of the Planning Department.
