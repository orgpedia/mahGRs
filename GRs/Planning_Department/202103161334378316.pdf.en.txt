See:- (1) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998 (2) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, 2000 (3) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Holding of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 (4) Letter from the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Gadchiroli No. 18/01/2021.
The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018, in Rule 6-A, provide for constitution of an Executive Committee under the Chairmanship of the Chairman of the District Planning Committee to assist the District Planning Committee in accordance with the provisions of Section 11 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998.
Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, 2" nominated members" out of the total" nominated members" under paragraph 2 of section 3 (3) of the said Act and 1" special invitee" out of the total" special invitee" members as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3) are required to be appointed.
As per the provisions of Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and the total number of nominated members under paragraph 2 of Section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998 and the total number of special invitees as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of Section 3 (3), the District Planning Committee, Gadchiroli is being constituted as follows, with the nomination of the Hon'ble MLA of Gadchiroli District, as recommended by the Chairman and Guardian Minister of Gadchiroli District.
