The functions of the Committee are: 1) To review, co-ordinate and publish the State Level Index of Industrial Production (State Level IIP) from time to time and to regulate the regular publication of the State Level Index of Industrial Production (State Level IIP).
(ii) To review from time to time the computer system of the Web Portal developed for the State Level IIP, if it needs to be improved/ modified.
3) To undertake and implement the State Level IIP in the context of comparative analysis and best practices with other Governments/ States/ Centre.
(iv) To revise the index of goods and accordingly to verify the revised weight of goods and suggest amendments/ changes, if required, in the circumstances of non-response from the closed factory, change in the process of production, shifting of factory, etc., while publishing the" State Level IIP."
