Circular:
The Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai has taken action to update the comprehensive database of all the employees of the Government of Maharashtra every year.
The database will collect information on all categories of employees, including regular establishment employees and non-regular establishment employees (working establishment, daily wage, part-time, voluntary etc.) as well as those who have been appointed on ad-hoc basis. Based on this, a comprehensive database containing information about all the filled and vacant posts will be created.
The Employees Master Database (EMDb) is being updated. Details such as name, date of birth, date of joining the service, date of retirement, gender, social class, religion, family background, disability, etc. will be collected. Further, the details of the entire month of July, 2019 will also be collected. For registration
The Directorate of Finance and Statistics will make available the computerized online command and log-in ID and password and instructions regarding filling up of information and use of command book to the concerned offices. The Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the State Government should consider the following schedule and instructions and act accordingly to update the Employees Master Database-2019 (EMDb-2019) within the stipulated time and to carry out its proceedings properly.

e) In case of employees who have taken voluntary retirement in the month of July, if they were State Government employees as on 1st July, 2019, such employees should also be included in the database as the Government fund was spent on them in the month of July.
