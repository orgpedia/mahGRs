As per the order, an" Executive Committee" has been constituted under the chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Amravati Division, Amravati. Further, an Executive Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Amravati Division, Amravati under the decision of the Government in Reference No. 2 for" Development Plan for providing basic facilities by developing the Samadhi Sthal of Saint Gadge Baba at Valgaon, Taluka and District Amravati." Further, as per the decision of the Government in Reference No. 3 for" Development Plan for providing basic civic amenities in Sri Kshetra Kaundanyapur, Taluka Tivasa, District Amaravati," an" Executive Committee" has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Divisional Commissioner, Amaravati Division, Amaravati.
All the Committees constituted under the above said Government Decision relating to the Pilgrim Development Plan to be implemented by the Planning Department shall continue to function till the implementation of the respective Pilgrim Development Plan is completed. These committees have been appointed by the Government from time to time as" Non-official Members"/" Special Invitees." The matter of cancellation of the appointment of these" non-official members"/" special invitees" was under consideration of the government.
The appointments of' Non-official Members"/' Special Invitees" named in Annexure' A" appointed by the Government on all the Committees constituted under the relevant Government Decisions relating to the Pilgrimage Development Plan to be implemented by the Government Decision Planning Department are hereby cancelled with immediate effect.
The Divisional Commissioner, Amravati Division, Amravati and Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur Division, Nagpur shall bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
