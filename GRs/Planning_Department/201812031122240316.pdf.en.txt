Government circular
Preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in Government service every year.
A comprehensive policy has been prepared in this regard. Accordingly, after final publication of seniority in the previous year, a provisional additional seniority is to be created in the next year consisting only of officers/ employees who have been appointed through regular promotion, direct service or other prescribed route. The provisional seniority of the cadre of Additional Director in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics dated 01/01/2017 has been notified vide Circular.
Considering the appointments of officers appointed by promotion or nomination in the cadre of Additional Director in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics during the period from 01/01/2017 to 31/12/2017, a provisional seniority of 01/01/2018 is being prepared in Annexure A subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission in the selection list for the post of Additional Director.
3. The Directorate of Finance and Statistics should obtain a copy of the provisional seniority list from the Government website. Also, all the officials should be informed about making the copy of the seniority list available from the government website. The Directorate of Finance and Statistics should arrange for the officers who have retired to send a copy of this seniority list to their address.
