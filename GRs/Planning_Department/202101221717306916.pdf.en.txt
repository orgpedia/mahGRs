Preamble: The Planning Department, Government of India has taken a decision to develop the Dargah premises of Hazrat Baba Tajuddin at Tajbagh in Nagpur city. As on 30th September, 2013 Rs. Administrative approval has been given to the" Tajbagh District Nagpur Development Plan" at a cost of Rs. 132.49 crore. The matter of providing funds to the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur for this scheme in the financial year 2020-21 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: The Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur Division, Nagpur has issued orders for the works under the" Tajbagh District Nagpur Development Plan." The Finance Department, as sought under letter dated November 26, 2020, vide Government Circular, has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 3.015 crore (Rupees Three Crores One Lakh Fifty Thousand only) is being disbursed as per the following targets.
