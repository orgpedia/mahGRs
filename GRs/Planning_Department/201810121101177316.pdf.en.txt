The Proposal:
For the effective implementation of the Special Action Plan and Supplementary Plan for the rapid development of the Naxal affected areas, a total of 6 posts at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur (Deputy Director-1, Research Officer-1, and Statistical Assistant-1) and at the state level (Deputy Secretary-1, Research Officer-1 and Clerk Typist-1) have been created vide Government Decision No. EPD-2510/ PR No. 294/
1421 dated 22nd July, 2010 and Supplementary Paper of Schedule dated 18th August, 2010. Provisional Posts under Reference Government Resolution No.Naxal-2417/ PR No. 142/ KA-1417 dated 15th March, 2018 from 1st March, 2018 to 30th September, 2018
This period has been extended. The Government was considering extension of the said posts for the period from 1st October, 2018 to 28th February, 2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: For the effective implementation of Special Action Programme and Supplementary Programmes for speedy development of Naxal affected areas, the following 6 temporary posts, 3 at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur and 3 at the State level, have been sanctioned for the period from 1st October, 2018 to 28th February, 2019.
