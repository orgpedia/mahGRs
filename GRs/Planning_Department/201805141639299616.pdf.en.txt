Introduction: Chanda to Banda is a pilot project for resource-based intensive planning and development in the districts of Chandrapur and Sindhudurg, initially for a period of four years from 2016-2017 to 2019-2020. The reference No. Under the scheme, a State Level Committee at the State level and a District Level Committee at the district level have been constituted for implementation of the scheme. Article 2 of the Constitution provides for the appointment of special invitees to both the Committees, to be nominated by the Chairman, as the case may be.
Accordingly, the matter of appointing 7 persons as special invitees nominated by the President, District Level Committee, Sindhudurg was under the consideration of the Government.
The ruling:
The following 7 Special Invitees are being approved by the Government to be appointed on the District Level Committee as nominated by the Chairman, District Level Committee, Sindhudurg to implement the scheme from Chanda to Banda at the district level for Sindhudurg district.
