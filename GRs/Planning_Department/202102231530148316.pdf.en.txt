Decision of the Finance Department No. In exercise of the powers conferred on the Administrative Departments vide Notification No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ PC, dated 15/2/2021, a total of 255 such temporary posts are being extended for the period from 1/3/2021 to 31/8/2021 in respect of 59 temporary posts in the Headquarters of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics as shown in the accompanying' Return-A', 48 temporary posts in all Regional Offices of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics as shown in' Return-B' and 148 temporary posts in all 36 District Statistical Offices as shown in' Return-A'. Expenditure on salary and allowances of these 255 temporary posts" Demand No. The expenditure under the respective sub-headings under O-9 3454, Calculation, Survey and Statistics, 02 Survey and Statistics, 112 Financial Advice and Statistics shall be met from the grant sanctioned under the objective of" (01) Salary" for the relevant financial year.
