The approval for" Development of Mangroves Park at Gorai" was given by the District Planning Committee, Mumbai Suburban in its meeting held on 30/6/2018.
Accordingly, the proposal for grant of administrative approval for the said project with a budget of Rs. 25.3010 crore was submitted by the Divisional Forest Officer, Mumbai (Kandhalvan Component) to the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Mumbai Suburbs. However, as it was found that the Collector had no power to grant administrative sanction for works above Rs. 5 crore as per the provisions of the Government Resolution No. 1 under reference, the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Mumbai, have submitted a proposal to the Government for administrative sanction for the said project. Therefore, the proposal was under consideration for administrative approval from the Planning Department as an administrative department.
Government's decision
