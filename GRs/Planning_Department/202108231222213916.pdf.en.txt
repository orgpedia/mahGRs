A total of 51 temporary posts, 17 each in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of Maharashtra Development Boards and 6 temporary posts created in the Planning Department (itself) have been extended from 1/5/2021 to 31/7/2021 under the Planning Department, Government Decision No. EST-2021/ PR No. 15/ K. 1426, dated 29/4/2021.
The final decision on extension of the period beyond 30th April, 2020 for Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and remaining Maharashtra Development Boards is yet to be taken. Therefore, a total of 57 temporary posts, namely 51 temporary posts at the establishment of these Development Boards and 6 temporary posts at the Planning Department, could not be extended beyond 31/7/2021. In the above background, a proposal was under consideration of the Government for extension of 57 temporary posts from 1/8/2021 to 3 1/8/2021 in Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and Rest of Maharashtra Development Board to complete 17 temporary posts each in Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and Rest of Maharashtra Development Board and 6 temporary posts in Planning Department (itself) to complete the works related to the utilization of special funds distributed to the development boards, utility certificates, audit objections and other ancillary works.
