A total of 51 temporary posts, 17 each in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of Maharashtra Development Boards and 6 temporary posts created in the Planning Department (itself) have been extended from 1/9/2021 to 28/2/2022 under the Planning Department, Government Decision No.
The final decision on extension of the period beyond 30th April, 2020 for Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and remaining Maharashtra Development Boards is yet to be taken. Therefore, a total of 57 temporary posts, namely 51 temporary posts at the establishment of these Development Boards and 6 temporary posts at the Planning Department, have not been extended for a period beyond 28/2/2022. In this background, the proposal was under consideration of the Government to extend the term of 57 temporary posts from 1/3/2022 to 31/8/2022 for a total of 51 temporary posts of 17 each in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and Rest of Maharashtra Development Board and 6 temporary posts in the Planning Department (itself) to complete the utility certificate, audit objections and other ancillary works related to the utilization of special funds allocated to the development boards.
