Preamble:" Chanda to Banda" scheme is being implemented in Chandrapur and Sindhudurg districts to implement the pilot programme of" Resource Based Intensive Planning and Development." Under this scheme, a program on planning and implementation is being implemented in the districts of Chandrapur and Sindhudurg, which are blessed with mineral and natural resources, to ensure optimum use of natural resources.
The scheme has set the target for the planned coverage of Chandrapur and Sindhudurg districts by 2019-20.
2. District Project Management Cells have been set up in Chandrapur and Sindhudurg districts for implementation of the scheme" Chanda to Banda." The effectiveness and results of the programme are being monitored. For this, the office has to contact the various regional agencies of the district. Therefore, the issue of sanctioning a separate telephone system to the District Project Management Cell, Chandrapur and Sindhudurg District Offices is under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Under this Government decision, two" Separate Telephones" (with STD facility), one each for" District Project Management Cell" Chandrapur and Sindhudurg district offices are being sanctioned by 2019-20.
