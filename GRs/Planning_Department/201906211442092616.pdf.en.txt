The functions of the State Level Coordination Committee shall be as follows:
(1) The preparation for the implementation of the seventh economic census in the state as per the instructions of the Central Government, regular control by assessing the current situation and emerging problems.
2) Verify the full scope of the information collected by verifying it with the State Business Register.
3) To check the accuracy of the data by preparing reports of the work done by the supervisors of the second level state level (Directorate of Finance and Statistics) and the Department of Regional Affairs, National Sample Survey Organization.
4) To sensitize the business organizations and common citizens of the state to cooperate in the seventh economic census.
5) To control and resolve the suggestions/ grievances received from the District Level Coordination Committee.
6) To approve the State Level Provisional Report of the Seventh Economic Census.

The functions of the District Coordination Committee shall be as follows:
1) Preparation for the implementation of the seventh economic census in the district, regular control by analyzing the current situation and emerging problems.
(ii) To assist the State Level Coordination Committee in effectively carrying out its roles and functions at the district level.
3) Common Service Centre (CSC-SPV) for smooth conduct of 7th Economic Census.
Provide guidance to local administration and panchayats from time to time.
4) Provide ward-wise and village-wise maps to the Common Service Centres (CSC-SPV) and to assist the concerned offices in the smooth conduct of the 7th Economic Census.
5) To make the State Level Coordination Committee aware of the work of economic calculation.
6) To make necessary publicity for the field work of economic calculation.
7) Approval and dissemination of district level reports of financial accounts.
