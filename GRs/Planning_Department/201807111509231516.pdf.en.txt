In order to ensure that the benefits of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme reach the workers, there should be transparency in it, the grievances of the workers should be resolved and injustice should not be done to them, in accordance with Section 27 (1) of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, the Central Government has directed to appoint a Grievance Redressal Authority at the place of every district by an order dated 7th September, 2009. A Grievance Redressal Authority is appointed in each district as per the guidelines of the Central Government. If the applicant does not accept the decision of the Grievance Redressal Authority or is not satisfied with the decision of the Grievance Redressal Authority, there is a provision to appeal against the decision of the Grievance Redressal Authority. The government has decided to appeal against the verdict. A three-tier Appellate Authority has been constituted vide Order dated 21.7.2014.
Government circular
As per Chapter V (Annexure) Para 15 and 15.1 of the revised guidelines dated 28th August, 2017 issued in the context of Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India, New Delhi, this Government Circular declares all District Grievance Redressal Authorities in the State of Maharashtra as State Public Information Officers under Section 5 (1) and 5 (2) of the Right to Information Act, 2005 and Chairman, Appellate Authority, Mumbai as Appellate Authority under Section 19 (1).
The order will come into effect immediately.
