circular
The provisional seniority of the" Joint Director, Group-A" cadre in the Directorate of Finance& Statistics vide Circular dated 01/01/2018 is being revised.
2. Provisional Seniority of the Joint Director, Group-A cadre in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics for the period from 01/01/2017 to 31/12/2017 is being published as per Annexure-A annexed to this Government Circular. The notification has been issued by the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC).
The Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai is requested to obtain a copy of this circular and the accompanying provisional service merit list from the website and bring it to the notice of all the concerned officers and place on record the signature of the concerned officers on such intimation.
(4) If there are any genuine errors, omissions or objections with regard to the said seniority, the concerned officers shall submit a representation in this regard to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics within a period of 15 days from the date of this circular in accordance with paragraph 3 (2) (b) of the Government Resolution No. 2 in the context of the General Administration Department. The Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics shall examine the facts of the representations received from the available records and forward the representations along with detailed comments to the Government at the earliest.
