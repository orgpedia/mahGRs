the Department of Planning

Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date of release: May 12, 2022
See:- (1) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998 (2 of 1998) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, 2000 (3 of 2000) Letter from the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Kolhapur No. Planning-Ka-18/ Things/ 1743/2020-21, Dy. 01/11/2021
Government's decision:
The Government is pleased to approve the appointment of 02 members of the nominated members from amongst the Members of Legislative Assembly/ Members of Parliament, 04 persons having knowledge of district planning as" nominated members" and 14 persons having experience of district planning and ordinarily resident in the area of district planning committee as" special invitees" to the Kolhapur District Planning Committee as indicated in the prospectus A attached herewith in accordance with the provisions of clause (3) (2) (b) and (3) (c) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000 and clause (4) (f) of clause (3) of the said Act.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Kolhapur should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominees and special invitees to the Kolhapur District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
