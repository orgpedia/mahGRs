If the members of the Legislative Assembly recommend funds for the purchase of medical machinery and materials as well as medicines and equipment for the financial year 2021-22 under the MLA Local Development Program, the Collector shall give" administrative approval" and thereafter the head of the district level operational system (1) District Civil Surgeon (2) District Health Officer, Zilla Parishad or as required (a) Commissioner, Municipal Corporation and (b) Chief Officer, Municipal Councils) may purchase" medical machinery and materials as well as medicines and equipment" under the following conditions:
Under the MLA Local Development Programme," Medical Machinery and Materials as well as Medicines and Equipment" in the context of COVID-19 should be procured as per the policy and prescribed procedure fixed by the Public Health Department or the Medical Education and Medicines Department.
Under the MLA Local Development Programme, funds cannot be made available for" maintenance and repair" to the district level operational machinery for" medical equipment and materials" in the context of" COVID-19." Also, no other" recurring expenditure" for such" medical equipment and materials" will also be admissible under the MLA Local Area Development Programme.
