4. Ma. In pursuance of the above announcement made by the Minister (Finance& Planning) in the Budget Speech 2017-18, 27 blocks in 13 districts out of the total 125 blocks in 23 districts covered under the Human Development Programme have been selected for implementation of special schemes for skill development and employment generation. This was done on 22/01/2018. Accordingly, special schemes for skill development and employment generation were under the consideration of the government in 27 selected talukas.

Government's decision:
The Control Room" Action Room to Reduce Poverty" has been set up in collaboration with the United Nations to achieve the objectives of skill development and employment generation in 27 selected blocks of 13 districts under the Human Development Programme.
Under the Human Development Program, employment will be provided to the maximum number of people in the selected 27 talukas of 13 districts by developing their skills taking into account their geographical, economic and social conditions.
The District Collectors have been asked to formulate special plans at the taluk level. The detailed proposals should be submitted to the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur/ Nashik/ Amravati/ Aurangabad by 30th June, 2018. The Divisional Commissioner forwarded the proposal to the Commissioner, Human Development along with his recommendation. Final approval is due by July 15, 2018. The Commissioner, Human Development has issued the detailed proposal received from the concerned Collector as per the recommendation of the Divisional Commissioner. District-wise funds should be made available with final approval by July 31, 2018.
The powers of administrative approval of the schemes to be taken in 27 talukas of 13 districts under the Human Development Program will be given for only 1 year at the beginning.
