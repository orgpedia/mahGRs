3. Planning Department, Government Resolution No: SLBC-1618/ P. No.20/ K-1417, D. 11th October, 2018
Preamble: As per the decision of the Government in the context of the Department of Planning, the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana Coordination Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of the Collector to effectively disseminate, disseminate and coordinate the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana among the masses in rural and remote areas so as to effectively disseminate information in rural, remote and extremely remote areas and to ensure that the benefits of this scheme reach the needy, the meritorious and the unemployed. In view of this decision, a proposal was sought from the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana District Coordination Committee regarding the necessary funds. Accordingly, for the financial year 2018-19, 50% of the funds admissible to all 36 districts for promotion, dissemination and coordination of Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana have already been disbursed. The government was considering providing the remaining 50% funds to 34 districts.
Government Decision: The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana Coordination Committee, constituted under the chairmanship of the Collector, has been mandated to promote, disseminate and coordinate the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana, subject to the guidelines in the relevant Government Decision, with a total outlay of Rs. 6,35,90, 000/- (Rupees Six Crores Thirty Five Lakhs Ninety Thousand only) is being provided.

The functions of the Coordination Committee under the Chairmanship of the Collector for the promotion and co-ordination of the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Scheme have been mentioned in the Planning Division, Government Resolution No: SLBC 1616/ PR No.
The guidelines of the government decision in the context of the planning department and the instructions given by the finance department in the relevant government circular should be strictly followed while spending the funds according to the matter/ head as approved by the district level plan approved by the coordination committee chaired by the collector.
The above funds should be placed under the control of the concerned District Collector. The Collector concerned will be the" Controlling Officer" and the District Planning Officer concerned will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw and disburse the funds from the Treasury Office. The District Collector will be responsible as the controlling officer to keep a tab on the expenditure of this fund and to inform the government about the expenditure from time to time.
5.Take care to take appropriate action after taking into account the orders of the State Election Commission issued from time to time regarding the election code of conduct.
6" Since 12 heads/ subject-wise works of publicity and publicity identified at the state level are being implemented by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, these items should not be excluded in the publicity plan to be implemented at the district level," it has been stated in the fund distribution order. This condition is being relaxed under this order and with the approval of the District Level Coordination Committee and the Planning Department, Government Decision No: LLBC-1616/ PR No.49/ K-1417, Dy. Promotional and publicity works should be completed immediately as per the procedures laid down by June 14, 2016.
The aforesaid funds are required to be deposited under the head Demand No.O-7,3451, Secretariat-Financial Services, 090, Secretariat (01), Planning Division, (01) (11) Promotion, Publicity and Co-ordination of Mudra Bank Scheme (Under Schemes) (Dattamat), 26 Advertisement and Publicity (3451 O 001) for the financial year 2018-19.
