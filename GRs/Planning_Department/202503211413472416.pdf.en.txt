The Proposal: -
Read Finance Department No. 3 Read B Planning Section No. 4 The Government decision hereby revises the rate of allowable remuneration for excess work to be done by officers / staff associated with budgetary activities with effect from the financial year 2022-23. On the same lines, the Government was considering revising the rate of remuneration paid to the officers / staff associated with the budgetary functions of the Electronic Data Dissemination Centre (EDS), a wing of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics, for additional work related to preparation of the Annual Programme, which forms an integral part of the annual budget, and making the data available to the Finance Department.
The Government's Decisions:
The Government has now decided to revise the allowable remuneration rate for additional work to be done in line with the budget for the officers / staff associated with budgetary activities in the category of Electronic Data Dissemination Centre (EDS) in the Directorate of Statistics, Hl as follows.
