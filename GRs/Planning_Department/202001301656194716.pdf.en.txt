Preamble:" Chief Minister's Fellowship Programme" General Administration Department Government Decision No. SRV 2014/ Q. No. 400/2014/12, D. It was launched on 29.05.2015. Since then, this program is being implemented every year and the criteria, terms and conditions for the selection of Chief Minister Fellows have been set.
There is a need to review the performance of the work done under the Chief Minister's Fellowship Programme and" improve/ change" the way it is designed and implemented. As the proposal to revise the' Chief Minister's Fellowship Programme" was under the consideration of the Government, the proposal to terminate the ongoing' Chief Minister's Fellowship Programme" was under the consideration of the Government. The following decisions are being taken in this regard:
Government's decision:
" Planning Department Government Decision No. Mufeka 1019/ Q. No. 53/ Q. 1426, c. The tenure of appointment of all Chief Minister Fellows appointed by the Directorate of Finance& Statistics in pursuance of the" Chief Minister's Fellowship Programme 2019-20" mentioned in 24.05.2019 has been fixed for a period of 11 months. This is now being changed and all the Chief Minister Fellows have been appointed. It expires on March 31, 2020.
