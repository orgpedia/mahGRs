In the Cabinet meeting dated 27.09.2022, it has been decided to reconstitute the Vidarbha Development Board, Nagpur, Marathwada Development Board, Sambhaji Nagar, and the remaining Maharashtra Development Board, Mumbai. Therefore, a proposal was under consideration of the Government for extension of a total of 57 temporary posts from the date 01.03.2025 to the date 31.08.2025 in Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of Maharashtra Development Board offices for 17 temporary posts each for up-to-date maintenance of records in these Development Board offices, utilization certificate for disbursement of special funds to Development Boards, audit objections and other ancillary works.
