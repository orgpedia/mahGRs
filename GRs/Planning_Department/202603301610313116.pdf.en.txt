The Proposal -
Reference No. As per section 07, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to make every family a millionaire through proper planning of MGNREGA. Under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, irrigation works have been made permissible. Also, guidelines have been issued from time to time from the government level regarding the proposal of irrigation wells as well as the approval process. However, Maharashtra is a large state and the topography of each region is different. Therefore, since it is not possible for the state to determine the same size or scale of the well, a district level committee has been constituted to determine the technical and economic aspects related to the work of the well and the committee issues necessary instructions regarding the technical, additional parameters and resolution picture of the well within the district as per the requirement.
The composition of the District Level Committee is 1) Collector and District Programme Coordinator-Chairperson 2) CEO and Sahajlila Programme Coordinator-cum-Chairperson 3) Deputy Collector (MGNREGA) -Member 4 Deputy Executive Officer / Block Development Officer (MGNREGA) -Member

Reference No. According to 08. As per the recommendation of the committee headed by Divisional Commissioner Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, the government was considering reconstituting the 507 (Standard Operating Procedure) District Level Committee for expeditious completion of irrigation well works under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme with the objective of making every family in the state a millionaire.
Government Decisions - Government Decisions. Irrigation well works have been permitted under MGNREGA as on 4th November, 2022. As per this government decision, the District Collector is the chairman of the District Level Committee.
However, the Collector is the head of the revenue administration and is responsible for planning, implementing and reviewing the schemes of all the departments of the state government, chairing various committees at the district level, law and order, disaster relief, elections, royal discipline, visits of high-profile persons, etc. As per the recommendation of the committee headed by Divisional Commissioner Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, the chairmanship of the committee is being given to the CEO, Zilla Parishad and Joint District Programme Coordinator.
