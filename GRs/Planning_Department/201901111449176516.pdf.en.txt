Preamble: State Level Conference of Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana to be held on 19th December, 2018 The meeting was chaired by the Minister (Finance and Planning). The meeting was attended by the heads of various government departments, district magistrates and senior officials of the state government. The office of the District Collector, Mumbai Suburban, has done the decoration and ancillary works for this conference and the book printing of success stories has been done by the office of the District Collector, Thane. The matter of providing additional funds for the above mentioned matters to the two districts was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana Coordination Committee constituted under the chairmanship of the Collector, Mumbai Suburban and Thane, has been entrusted with the following activities as per the guidelines issued in the relevant Government Decision, for the State Level Conference held on 19th December, 2018. 17,57,504/- (Rupees Seventeen Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand Five Hundred Four only) is being provided as additional funds.
