The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 2000.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the provisions of clause (3) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government is pleased to nominate 03 persons to the District Planning Committee of Washim District as" Special Invitees" who are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and have experience in district planning as indicated in the attached statement A.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Washim should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The nomination of special invitees mentioned in the prospectus A to the Washim District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such nomination is cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
