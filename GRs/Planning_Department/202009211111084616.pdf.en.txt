GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Under the Planning Department, Government Decision No.NIVIA-1720/ No. 80/ 1421, dated 9th March, 2020, a total of 40 temporary posts indicated in the statement" A,"" B,"" C,"" D,"" E,"" F" and" C" accompanying this Government Decision on the establishment of the Planning Department (itself) were extended till 31.08.2020. This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers vested in the Department of Finance, Government Decision, No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 31st August, 2020.
