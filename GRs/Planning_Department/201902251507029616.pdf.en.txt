Under the 13th Finance Commission Grants-in-Aid to the States, each district of the State has been allocated an amount of Rs. 20 lakh for 5 years Rs. 1.00 crore for 5 years Rs. 35 crore was required for the purpose. The scheme is 90% funded by the government and 10% by the public. The fund is to be used for filling gaps in local infrastructure and for works not funded from existing funds/ sources. 1750 crore. The Central Government has released on 29.4.2013.
The district has been allocated Rs. 1750.00 lakhs as per the Utilization Certificate received from all the concerned districts. 1327.18 lakhs and Rs. 422.82 lakhs have been dedicated. The same has been submitted to the Department of Finance vide letter dated 17.11.2017. The government was contemplating to direct the district collectors to take action to spend the remaining funds as per the Centre's guidelines for the state without returning the same to the central government. Accordingly, the following decisions are taken:
Government's decision:
For the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of District Innovation Schemes under the 13th Finance Commission, for a period of five years from 2010-11 to 2014-15, 90% of the funds received by the State from the Central Government, with Government share, will be provided as per the above No. 2 For the works sanctioned under the letter herein, the same are being distributed as mentioned in the following statement. The list of proposals approved by the High Level Committee vide No. 2 has been sent to the concerned District Collector.
