Introduction: The Planning Department has taken a decision to develop the area of the dargah of Hazratbaba Tajuddin in Nagpur city. As on 30th September 2013, Rs. Administrative approval has been given to the" Tajbagh District Nagpur Development Plan" at a cost of Rs. 132.49 crore. For this scheme, the government was considering to provide the funds budgeted in the financial year 2019-20 to the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur.

The Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur Division, Nagpur, has issued orders for the works under the' Tajbagh District Nagpur Development Plan."
