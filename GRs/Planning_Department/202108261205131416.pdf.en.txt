The Government have approved the appointment of two members of nomination from the Legislative Assembly/ Members of Parliament, two persons having knowledge of district planning as nominated members and 11 persons having experience of district planning and ordinarily resident in the area of district planning committee as special invitees to the District Planning Committee of Chandrapur District as indicated in the prospectus A attached herewith under clause (3) (2) (b) and (3) (c) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000 and clause (4) (f) of clause (3) of the said Act.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Chandrapur should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominees and special invitees to the Chandrapur District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
