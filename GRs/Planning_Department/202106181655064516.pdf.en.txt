The Proposal:
Under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), the Forest Department has been empowered to grant technical and administrative approvals for afforestation and nurseries as well as soil and water conservation works. It was handed over to the Forest Department by a Government Resolution dated September 29, 2016. Also, a screening committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Collector to ensure that the works are in the labour budget while taking up these works, to approve the list of completed works before administrative approval for the works to be in the approved plan at the village/ taluk/ district level and to prepare the shelf.
The government has taken the decision. It is valid until March 31, 2018. This was referred to the scrutiny committee. Decision of the Government of June 30, 2018. through September 30, 2018. Decision of the Government of 25 March 2019. The deadline has been extended to March 31, 2020. After that. The decision was taken by the government on May 20, 2020. The deadline has been extended to March 31, 2021.
At present, the government was considering extending the term of the committee. Following this, the government took decision:
Government's decision:
Approval of the list of completed works before administrative approval for afforestation and plantation works to be prepared by the Forest Department under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, ensuring that the works are in the labour budget while taking up soil and water conservation works, works in the approved plan at the village/ taluk/ district level.
