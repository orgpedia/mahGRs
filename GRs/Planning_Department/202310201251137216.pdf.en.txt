Government circular
The provisional seniority of the officers who have been appointed in the cadre of" Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted)" in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics during the period from 01/01/2021 to 31/12/2021 through regular promotion, direct service or other prescribed route is being published as per" Annexure-A" attached to this Government Circular. Provisional promotions in this seniority list are being published subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
2. m. The High Court of Bombay, vide Petition No. Against the decision dated 4/8/2017 in 2797/2015, the State Govt. Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 has been filed in the Supreme Court. The General Administration Department vide its letter No. BCC-2017/ PR No. 312A/ 16-B dated 29/12/2017, with the concurrence of the Department of Law& Justice, has decided to fill the vacancies in the open category in the promotion quota on purely temporary basis as per the merit of service. Instructions have been issued to be filled up subject to the decision of Special Leave Petition No. 28306/2017 filed in the Supreme Court. In view of the aforesaid instructions, promotion to the post of" Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted)" in the cadre of" Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted)" in the open category vacancies in the promotion quota has been made only on temporary basis. So Ma. The said seniority of the" Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted)" may be revised after the final decision of the Supreme Court.
Any factual errors, omissions or objections with respect to the temporary seniority of the Research Officer/ Statistical Officer, Group-B (Gazetted) cadre in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics as on 01/01/2022 (dated 01/01/2021 to 31/12/2021), as may be notified in the notification.
