Government Decision: Planning Department Reference No. The Government of Maharashtra has approved the implementation of the Maharashtra Strengthening Institutional Capabilities in Districts for Enabling Growth Project (MahaSTRIDE) to promote World Bank-funded district-level development. The project is being implemented through the Maharashtra Institution for Transformation (MITRA). Out of the total budgeted outlay for the year 2025-26 under the three headings created for the project, a total of 135.43 crore has been allocated under Reference No. 2 has been requested by the Taras government here.
