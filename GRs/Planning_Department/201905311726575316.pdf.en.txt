(2) The head of the office concerned shall immediately relieve the officers of the post of the concerned officers by entrusting the charge of the post of the concerned officers to another officer to join the post of the transferor and after the retirement, the concerned officers shall immediately join the post of the transferred officers and take charge of that post.
(3) The transferred officers shall immediately contact the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer after joining the transferred office and take action to update the e-profile within seven days. The salary payment of the concerned officer should not be passed by the treasury office unless the updated e-profile is submitted to the treasury office along with the salary payment of the concerned officer for that month. Also, copies of the updated e-profile should be sent to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics and the Government. It will be the responsibility of the concerned office heads to update the e-profile.
4.The concerned head of the office shall forward the copy of the order regarding the release of the transferred officer and the order of his appointment to this department.
