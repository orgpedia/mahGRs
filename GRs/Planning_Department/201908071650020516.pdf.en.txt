Government's decision:
The Government is pleased to approve the appointment of 9 persons as" Special Invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Bhandara District, who are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and have experience in District Planning, as per the provision contained in clause (3) (4) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Bhandara should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The appointments made by the aforesaid Special Invitees to the District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
