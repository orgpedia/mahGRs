A total of 51 temporary posts, 17 each in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of Maharashtra Development Boards and 6 temporary posts created in the Planning Department (itself) have been extended from date 01.03.2025 to date 31.08.2025 under the Planning Department, Government Decision No.
2. Ma. The Cabinet in its meeting dated 27.09.2022 has decided to reconstitute Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and SANT Maharashtra Development Board. Therefore, a proposal was under consideration of the Government for extension of total 57 temporary posts from date 01.09.2025 to date 28.02.2026 in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and rest of Maharashtra Development Board for completion of 17 GY temporary posts each and 6 temporary posts in the Planning Department (itself) for up-to-date maintenance of records in the offices of these Development Boards, Utilization Certificate regarding disbursement of special funds to Development Boards, Audit Objections and other ancillary works.
