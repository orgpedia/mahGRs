The Proposal:
The Revenue & Forest Department, Government Decision No: S. 2022 / Q. 84 / F-9, dated 27/07/2022 has approved the plan for an amount of ₹369.7877 crore. Due to the budget deficit of the project of this plan, in accordance with the approval given to the increased cost of the sanctioned works in the plan, No. The revised plan has been approved at a cost of ₹434.6177 crore by the Government of India.
Due to the increase in the cost of some works under the conservation, conservation and development plan of Lonar Lake and the savings due to the decrease in the cost of some works, the government was considering giving the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati, the right to use one of the works within the limits of government participation, for other works of higher cost, subject to the fact that additional funds would not be sought in future in the saving works, and the authority to use the funds of one of the works approved in the plan for other works.
