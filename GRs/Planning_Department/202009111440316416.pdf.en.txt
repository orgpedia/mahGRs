A total of 1073 posts at the level of Collector, Zilla Parishad, Taluk level and Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) Maharashtra Commissionerate were extended from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. The said 1073 posts are required for effective implementation of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. Therefore, a total of 1073 posts of Divisional Commissioners, Collectors, Zila Parishads and Taluk level posts are being considered for extension from 01.09.2020 onwards.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the powers conferred by the Government Decision No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 31.08.2020 of the Finance Department, regarding extension of temporary posts in the offices under the administrative departments of the Ministry, under this Government Decision, a total of 1052 posts working in the offices of Divisional Commissioner, Collector, Zilla Parishad and Taluk level offices under the Employment Guarantee Scheme and 21 posts created for the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme-Maharashtra, Nagpur Commissionerate, a total of 1073 posts are being approved for extension from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. The details of the posts are given in Annexure No. are shown in Figures 1 to 5.
Vacancies of more than six months in the sanctioned posts will be excluded.
