Preamble: 1. The company" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (SARATHI), Pune" has been registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 on 25th June, 2018.
Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune has established 6 divisional offices (Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Aurangabad and Pune) for the effective and timely implementation of various welfare schemes for the economic, social and educational development of Marathas, Kunbis, Kunbis-Marathas and Marathas. For this, the government was considering creating 21 posts for each divisional office with the necessary officers and staff for each office (08 regular posts and 13 manpower services to be taken by external agencies). A high level committee of secretaries has been constituted for 6 divisional offices (Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Aurangabad and Pune) under the Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune. Approved in the meeting of 29/4/2022. Accordingly, the Government was contemplating to create 08 regular posts for each of the 06 Divisional Offices (Mumbai, Nashik, Amravati, Nagpur, Aurangabad and Pune) under the Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune and issue orders for creation of 13 posts for hiring manpower services through external agencies.
