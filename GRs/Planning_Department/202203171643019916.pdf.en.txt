INTRODUCTION The draft report of the Time, Work Speed Study by CWE, NIRD& PR Hyderabad on the time, work and speed study completed by the State of Maharashtra under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme has been made available as per the above reference. Instructions have been received to form a committee of architectural engineers at the Maharashtra state level to review and verify the report. The government will take a decision on it.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the constitution of a Committee of Architectural Engineers at the State level to review and verify the draft report of the Time, Work Speed Study received from CWE, NIRD& PR Hyderabad in accordance with the time, work and speed study completed by the State of Maharashtra under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme.
