Preamble: For the effective implementation of the Special Action Plan and Supplementary Plan for the rapid development of Naxal affected areas, a total of 6 posts at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur (Deputy Director-1, Research Officer-1 and Statistical Assistant-1) and at the State level (Deputy Secretary-1, Research Officer-1 and Clerk Typist-1) have been created vide Government Resolution No. EPD-2510/ PR No. 294/ K-1421 dated 22nd July, 2010 and Supplementary Letter of Schedule dated 18th August, 2010. As per the decision of the Government of India dated 6th March, 2023, the said temporary posts were extended for a period from 1st March, 2023 to 31st August, 2023. The extension of these posts from 1st September, 2023 onwards was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: For effective implementation of Special Action Programme and Supplementary Programmes for speedy development of Naxal affected areas, a total of 6 temporary posts at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur (Deputy Director-I, Research Officer-I and Statistical Assistant-I) and at the State level (Deputy Secretary-I, Research Officer-I and Clerk Typist-I) are being approved under the decision of the present Government for extension of tenure from 1st September, 2023 to 29th February, 2024.
