Government's decision:
The Government is pleased to approve the appointment of 15 persons as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Nanded district, who are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and have experience of District Planning as indicated in the statement A attached herewith as per the provision of clause (3) (4) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Nanded should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The appointments made by the aforesaid Special Invitees to the District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
