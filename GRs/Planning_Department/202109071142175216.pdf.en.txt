A total of 51 temporary posts, 17 each in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of Maharashtra Development Boards and 6 temporary posts created in the Planning Department (itself) have been extended from 1/8/2021 to 3 1/8/2021 under the Planning Department, Government Decision No. EST-2021/ PR No. 15/ K. 1426, dated 23/8/2021.
The final decision on extension of the period beyond 30th April, 2020 for Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and remaining Maharashtra Development Boards is yet to be taken. Therefore, a total of 57 temporary posts, namely 51 temporary posts at the establishment of these Development Boards and 6 temporary posts at the Planning Department, could not be extended for a period beyond 31/8/2021. In the above background, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to extend the tenure of 57 temporary posts from 1/9/2021 to 28/2/2022 in Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and Rest of Maharashtra Development Board till the final decision on extension beyond 30/4/2020.
