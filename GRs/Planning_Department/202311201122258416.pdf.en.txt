Government's decision:
The Selection List (Preparation and Revision) Rules, 1977 for promotion to the cadre of Deputy Director, Group-A in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics have been issued by the Planning Department vide Government Notification No. STW-1173/ A-129/ MDM-3, dated 17th August, 1977. As per Rule-3 of the said Rules, the" Selection Committee" has been constituted as per the decision of the Government in question. 3, Shri Sanjay Krishna Bhosale, Deputy Secretary (Backward Classes Representative) is retired. Therefore, the committee should be reconstituted. Accordingly, in pursuance of the decision of the Government in this regard, a Selection Committee is being constituted to prepare the list of candidates for promotion to Group-A cadre as Deputy Director in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics as under:
