District Collector, District Planning Committee, Gadchiroli should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominees and special invitees to the Gadchiroli District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
