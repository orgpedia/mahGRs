circular
Under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, the works of the incomplete wells in the state should be completed immediately considering the current drought situation in the state. While planning in this regard, new wells should be sanctioned taking into account the wells in progress. It should be ensured that the work of 5 wells at a time in a Gram Panchayat is in progress (e.g. If the work of 3 wells is in progress in a Gram Panchayat, then the work of 2 new irrigation wells should be taken up. If more than 5 wells are incomplete, the Chief Executive Officer should ensure that administrative approval is not given to new wells in that Gram Panchayat.
2. When taking such actions, the government in question should take a decision. December 17, 2012 and Govt. Comply with the provisions of August 21, 2014.
