Preamble: As per the decision of the above Government, the Amrit Mahotsav Fruit Garden/ Tree/ Phulpik plantation scheme is being implemented in the State. Bamboo tree is included in this government decision. Bamboo plantation will bring a lot of change in the lives of farmers and will help in reducing environmental degradation. Through this project, the people-oriented work of making the river channels green and flowing perennially will stand out, which will be a direction to the entire country. For this, it is necessary to plant bamboo trees on a large scale in the state.
Under MGNREGA, it is necessary to take guidance from experts on various aspects such as propagating bamboo cultivation through tree plantation, creating a positive attitude among farmers, creating awareness, training bamboo farmers, preparing a bamboo cultivation budget and studying the available market for it. A government committee is being constituted for this purpose.
Government Decision: The following committee is being constituted for the cultivation of bamboo trees under MGNREGA under the chairmanship of A.M.S. (Ro.

The committee will have the following functions:
To prepare a roadmap for promoting bamboo plantation on a large scale under MGNREGA.
Encourage farmers to take up bamboo cultivation, impart training on bamboo cultivation, environmental importance of bamboo cultivation and economic benefits of bamboo cultivation.
Discussing the budget of bamboo cultivation.
To study the market available for bamboo and other important aspects.
The committee will guide the government agencies for large-scale bamboo cultivation.
The Committee shall meet at least once every three months. The travel allowance to the non-official members attending the meeting will be admissible from the fund under MNREGA.
