the Department of Planning

Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032

(1) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998 (2 of 1998) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, 2000 (3 of 2000) Letter from the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Mumbai Suburban No. 14.10.2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the provisions contained in sub-clauses (3) (2) (b) and (3) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve the appointment of 2 members of the nominated members from amongst the Members of Legislative Assembly/ Members of Parliament, as mentioned in the attached prospectus' A', and 07 persons ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and having experience in district planning, as' Special Invitees' to the District Planning Committee for Mumbai Suburban District.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Mumbai Suburban should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominated members as well as special invitees to the Mumbai Suburban District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
