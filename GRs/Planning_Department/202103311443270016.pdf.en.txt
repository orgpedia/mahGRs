GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of clause (3) (2) (b) and clause (3) (3) (c) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve the appointment of two members of the nomination from amongst the Members of Legislative Assembly/ Parliament, as well as 9 persons having experience in district planning and ordinarily residing in the area of the District Planning Committee, as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee for Gondia district, as indicated in the Annexure A attached herewith in clause (4) (f) of clause (3).
District Collector, District Planning Committee, Gondia should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominees and special invitees to the Gondia District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
