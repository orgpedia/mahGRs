Government's decision:
Under the District Annual Plan (General) 2014-15 of Gondia District, Rs. 89,69,530/- was approved as a" one time special case" under the Government decision dated 03.03.2018 for spending upto 31.03.2018 as an exceptional case.
However, under the innovative scheme under District Annual Plan (General) 2014-15 of Gondia district, the Government has sanctioned Rs. 330 regarding acceptance of tender from the general body meeting of Zila Parishad, Gondia for beautification work vide Resolution No. The approval under 3/5 dated 08.06.2018 has been granted. In this regard, an amount of Rs. 89,69,530/- (Rupees Ninety-nine Lakhs Seventy-nine Thousand Five Hundred and Thirty only) dated 31.03.2019 is being extended for the last time as a special case.
