Guarantee Scheme- A total of 1073 posts in Maharashtra Commissionerate will be filled up from 01.03.2021. The deadline has been extended to 31.08.2021. The said 1073 posts are required for effective implementation of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. Therefore, a total of 1073 posts of Divisional Commissioner, District Collector, Zilla Parishad and Taluka level posts are being considered for extension from 01.09.2021.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the powers conferred on the Ministerial Administrative Departments under the Government Decision No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 10.08.2021 of the Department of Finance, to extend the tenure of temporary posts in the offices under them, the Government has approved the extension of a total of 1073 posts under the Employment Guarantee Scheme from 01.09.2021 to 28.02.2022, including a total of 1052 posts in the offices of Divisional Commissioners, Collectors, Zilla Parishads and Taluk level offices and 21 posts created for the Commissionerate of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, Maharashtra, Nagpur. The details of the posts are given in Annexure No. are shown in Figures 1 to 5.
Vacancies of more than six months in the sanctioned posts will be excluded.
The Commissioner, Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, Maharashtra, Nagpur, Divisional Commissioner (ROHYO), District Collector and District Programme Coordinator and CEO, Zilla Parishad and Joint District Programme Coordinator should review all the total posts in their establishment and submit the information to the Department before 28.02.2022.
