Government Decisions In order to accelerate the implementation of government schemes by making them people-oriented, Government Decisions have been issued on April 13, 2023 and May 4, 2023 to implement the" Government at Your Doorstep" campaign. As per the decision of the above Government, instructions have been issued to implement this campaign from 15th April 2023 to 15th June 2023. The duration of the mission is being extended till 30 th August, 2023.
