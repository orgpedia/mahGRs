The Proposal:
The Department of Planning serves as the" nodal department" for achieving the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 169 targets set by the United Nations by 2030. The Standardization, Rationalization and Integration of Statistical Data is being carried out by the Sustainable Development Goals-Implementation and Coordination Centre (SDGIC), set up under the Directorate of Economics and Statistics. Under this, the planning department has completed the process of mapping (i) State Level Schemes and (ii) District Level Schemes with 17 Sustainable Development Goals and 169 targets.
""" Monitoring& Evaluation"" of progress under the"""" State Indicator Framework"""" and"""" District Indicator Framework"""" to achieve the 17 Sustainable Development Goals and 169 targets set by the United Nations by 2030.""" In the above background, the government was considering issuing guidelines to the administrative departments to take into account the above 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 169 targets in the Cabinet Note and Government Resolution to be submitted by the administrative departments while determining the policy of the state government.
Government's decision:
17 Sustainable Development Goals and 169 targets set by the United Nations to be achieved by 2030

Government Resolution No: Parichay-2019/ P.S.No.61/ KA-1412,
In determining the policy process, it will be mandatory for all the administrative departments to include the new programs/ schemes and projects proposed by all the administrative departments from the date of this Government Decision in the" Cabinet Note" and Government Resolution submitted by them as follows:
" The implementation of this programme/ scheme and project will contribute to the achievement of Sustainable Development Goal No. Target No. This will be achieved by 2030.
The Administrative Department should take the help of the Sustainable Development Goals-Implementation and Coordination Centre (SDG-ICC) established under the Directorate of Finance and Statistics to incorporate the above text in the Cabinet Note to be submitted by them.
This will then be confirmed by the Planning Department in the cabinet comments and feedback on the government decision.
