Preamble: The Government has decided to issue, fill, pass and provide skilled/ unskilled attendance sheets in the works of NADEP composting, vermi composting, farms and nurseries being implemented as an agriculture department system under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The decision has been handed over to the Department of Agriculture on December 14, 2016. It was fixed up to 31/03/2018. After that. As per the Government decision dated 18th September, 2019, the powers conferred on the Department of Agriculture in respect of issue, filling, passing and skilled/ unskilled matters in respect of works of NADEP Composting, Vermi Composting, Farms and Nurseries being implemented as an Agriculture Department Mechanism under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) have been withdrawn. The deadline has been extended to March 31, 2021. The government was considering extending the deadline. Following this, the government took decision on:

Government Resolution No: MGRRO-2016/ No.120/ MGRRO-1
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The powers conferred on the Agriculture Department to issue, fill, pass and issue attendance sheets in respect of works of NADEP Composting, Vermi Composting, Farms and Nurseries being carried out as an Agriculture Department machinery under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) are being extended till further orders.
Also, 2. The activities of the nurseries in the Government Decision of 14/12/2016 should only be in the official premises of the Department of Agriculture.
Further, the guidelines in this regard have been issued vide Government Resolution No.120/ 2016/ No.120/ 2016/ No. It will be held on December 14, 2016.
