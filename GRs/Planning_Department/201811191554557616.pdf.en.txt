Preamble: As per the decision of the Government in the context of the Department of Planning, the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana Coordination Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of the Collector to effectively disseminate, disseminate and coordinate the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana among the masses in rural and remote areas so as to effectively disseminate information in rural, remote and extremely remote areas and to ensure that the benefits of this scheme reach the needy, the meritorious and the unemployed. In view of this decision, a proposal was sought from the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana District Coordination Committee regarding the necessary funds. Accordingly, proposals have been received from the District Collectors, Mumbai Suburban, Jalgaon, Osmanabad, Gadchiroli and Pune for providing funds for the promotion and coordination of the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Scheme for the financial year 2018-19.
Government Decision: The Prime Minister Mudra Bank Scheme Coordination Committee constituted under the chairmanship of the District Collectors of Mumbai Suburban, Jalgaon, Osmanabad, Gadchiroli and Pune has been given the mandate to promote, disseminate and coordinate the Mudra Bank Scheme under the guidelines of the relevant Government Decision. 98,00,000/- (Rupees Ninety Eight Lakhs only) is being provided.

The functions of the Coordination Committee under the Chairmanship of the Collector for the promotion and co-ordination of the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Scheme have been mentioned in the Planning Division, Government Resolution No. With a view to bring uniformity in the publicity and publicity programme of the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana in all the districts, the 12 items of publicity and publicity will be implemented at the state level through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, Department of Contextual Planning, as mentioned in the Government Resolution No.
The guidelines of the government decision in the context of the planning department and the instructions given by the finance department in the relevant government circular should be strictly followed while spending the funds according to the matter/ head as approved by the district level plan approved by the coordination committee chaired by the collector.
The above funds should be placed under the control of the concerned District Collector. The Collector concerned will be the" Controlling Officer" and the District Planning Officer concerned will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw and disburse the funds from the Treasury Office. The District Collector will be responsible as the controlling officer to keep a tab on the expenditure of this fund and to inform the government about the expenditure from time to time.
5.Take care to take appropriate action after taking into account the orders of the State Election Commission issued from time to time regarding the election code of conduct.
6 Since the 12 heads/ subject-wise works of publicity and publicity fixed at the state level are being implemented by the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations, these 12 items should not be excluded in the publicity and publicity plan implemented at the district level.
