(3) The transferred officers shall immediately contact the concerned Drawing and Disbursing Officer after joining the transferred office and take action to update the e-profile within seven days. The salary payment of the concerned officer should not be passed by the treasury office unless the updated e-profile is submitted to the treasury office along with the salary payment of the concerned officer for that month. Also, copies of the updated e-profile should be sent to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai and the Government. The head of the concerned office will be responsible for updating the e-profile.
4. The Head of the concerned office shall forward to this Department a copy of the order of posting and relieving the transferred officer.
