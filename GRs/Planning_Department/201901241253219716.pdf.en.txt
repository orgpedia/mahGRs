4) Letter from the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Hingoli No. Jinginh/ Jivayo/ 18-19/1685, Dt. 21/12/2018
The Government Decision:" As per the provisions of Section 12 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, a provision has been made in Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 regarding the reduction of an" Executive Committee" under the chairmanship of the Chairman of the District Planning Committee to assist the District Planning Committee. Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, 2" nominated members" out of the total number of" nominated members" under paragraph 2 of section 3 (3) of the said Act and 2" special invitees" out of the total number of" special invitees" as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3) are required to be appointed.
As per the provisions of Rule 6-A of the" Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018" and as per the provisions of paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of Section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, the" Executive Committee" of the District Planning Committee, Hingoli is being constituted as follows, with the nomination of the following Hon'ble Members of Parliament and Members of Legislative Assembly of Hingoli District, as recommended by the Chairman and Guardian Minister of the District Planning Committee, Hingoli, out of the total number of" Special Invitees."
