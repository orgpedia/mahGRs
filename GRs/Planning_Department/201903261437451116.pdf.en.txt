The Proposal:
Under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), the powers of technical and administrative approval in respect of afforestation and nurseries, soil and water conservation works carried out by the Forest Department were delegated to the Forest Department by a Government Resolution dated 29th September, 2016. Also, a scrutiny committee was constituted under the chairmanship of the Collector to ensure that the works are in the labour budget while taking up these works, to approve the list of completed works before administrative approval for the works to be in the approved plan at the village/ taluk/ district level and to prepare the shelf.
