Preamble Under" Manav Vikas Karyakram," for empowerment of women SHGs and SC/ STs in 125 backward blocks of 23 districts, Rs. A sum of Rs. 2.00 crore is under consideration for formulation and implementation of a" Special Scheme" with a special focus on" Employment Generation." Due to the limitation of 20% of the total funds received by each district under the" Human Development Programme," equal amount of funds were not available to implement the special scheme for" Employment Generation" to 125 backward taluks of 23 districts under the District/ Taluka Specific Plan. Therefore, the government was considering to implement a special scheme under the" District/ Taluka Specific Scheme" for the financial year 2022-23 to provide more opportunities for" employment generation" to the citizens of each of these backward taluks.
Government Decision For the year 2022-23, under the" Human Development Programme,"" Guidelines" are being prescribed for the preparation and implementation of" Special Schemes" under" District/ Taluka Specific Schemes" to give more emphasis on" Employment Generation" for empowering women SHGs and SC/ STs in 125 backward taluks of 23 districts as follows:
(9) Under the" Human Development Programme," 125 talukas of 23 districts have been selected taking into account the local economic conditions and considering their utility and sustainability while preparing a special plan under the" District/ Taluka Specific Plan."

The District Collectors and District Human Development Committees will prepare a special scheme with the objective of" generating employment" for the people of these categories with the help of maximum number of women self-help groups, farmer producer groups, SC/ ST farmer producer companies, Van Dhan Kendras of tribals, Gram Sanghs of Livelihood Mission, Atma Groups, Tribal Cooperative Societies, Fishermen Cooperative Societies and other cooperative societies, Gram Sabhas in tribal areas, and women self-help groups formed by other organizations other than Umeed and Mavim.
(2) During the implementation of this scheme, Rs. Proposals related to livelihood and employment generation sector in the limit of Rs. 2.00 crore will be submitted to the Commissioner, Human Development through the Collector. Taluka-wise proposals for the" Special Plan" should be prepared and submitted to the Commissioner, Human Development, Aurangabad, with the recommendation of the Collector. Thereafter, the Commissioner, Human Development, Aurangabad, shall in principle approve the taluk-wise proposal recommended by the Collector, after ascertaining that it is in consonance with the objectives of the Human Development Programme and will boost the local economy. The District Collectors concerned have been given Rs. Rs. 2.00 crore will be allocated for the project. There should not be any duplication in other programmes/ schemes of the State Government for" employment generation" while formulating" special schemes" under" district/ taluka specific schemes" related to" employment generation."
(3) While preparing proposals for implementation of special schemes under" District/ Taluka Specific Schemes" in FY 2022-23 under" Human Development Programme," priority will be given to women SHGs and SC/ STs with focus on local economy. While preparing the proposal, the poor class included in the" Socially and Economically Backward Classes" Census (SECC) can be considered besides utility and sustainability in increasing income. A" special scheme" should be implemented under the" district/ taluka specific scheme" by the farmer producer groups and women self-help groups of this category. No large financial outlay should be proposed for a single scheme/ project. Special plans under" District/ Taluka Specific Plan" in each taluk should be submitted to the" Commissioner, Human Development, Aurangabad" by the Collector in different villages and at least four separate proposals for each taluk. In addition, if it is necessary to take proposals on health and education according to local needs, the Collector should consider everything and submit such proposals.
