""" For the purpose of assisting the District Planning Committee in accordance with the provisions of Section 11 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, a provision has been made in"""" Rule 6-A"""" of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 regarding the reduction of an"""" Executive Committee"""" headed by the Chairman of the District Planning Committee.""" Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, 2" nominated members" out of the total number of" nominated members" under paragraph 2 of section 3 (3) of the said Act and 2" special invitees" out of the total number of" special invitees" as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3) are required to be appointed.
As per the provisions of Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and the total number of nominated members under paragraph 2 of Section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998 and the total number of special invitees as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of Section 3 (3), the District Planning Committee, Buldhana is being constituted as follows with the nomination of the Hon'ble MP and MLA of Buldhana district as recommended by the President and Guardian Minister of Buldhana.
