Introduction: The Tibetan Rehabilitation Policy, as per the policy of the Central Government, provides for the benefit of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme to the Tibetan refugees. Accordingly, in pursuance of the implementation of the" Tibetan Rehabilitation Policy-2014" in the State, the Government was considering how the benefits of various facilities and schemes could be extended to the Tibetan refugees under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme.
In pursuance of the above, the Government of India has decided to extend the benefits of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme to Tibetan refugees under the Tibetan Rehabilitation Policy, as under:
Government circular:
The Government of India has decided to implement the Central Government's" Tibetan Rehabilitation Policy-2014" in the State. Accordingly, in order to ensure that Tibetan refugees get the benefits of various facilities and schemes under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, and to implement the policy more effectively, the following procedures are being ensured.
1) Provide information about the villages/ Gram Panchayats in the state where Tibetan refugees live and provide job cards to Tibetan refugees.
(ii) Incorporating Tibetan refugees in the work placement process under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme in the village/ Gram Panchayat.
