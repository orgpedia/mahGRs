A total of 40 temporary posts indicated in the statement A, B, C, D, E, F and C accompanying this Government Decision on the establishment of the Planning Department (itself) under the Government Decision Planning Department, Government Decision, No.Nivya-1718/ No.Nivya-449/ No.Ka-1421, dated 21st September, 2018 were extended till 28.02.2019. This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers delegated to the administrative departments of the Ministry by the Department of Finance, Government Decision, No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 21st February, 2019.
