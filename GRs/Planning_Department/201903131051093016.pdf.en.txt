A total of 1073 posts including 1052 posts at the level of Commissioner, Collector, Zilla Parishad and Taluk level and 21 posts created under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS)-Maharashtra Commissionerate, Nagpur are under consideration of the Government for extension from 01.03.2019.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the powers conferred on the Ministerial Administrative Departments under Government Decision No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 21.02.2019 of the Finance Department regarding the extension of temporary posts in the offices under them, the Government has approved the extension of a total of 1073 posts under the Employment Guarantee Scheme from 01.03.2019 to 30.09.2019, including a total of 1052 posts in the offices of Divisional Commissioners, Collectors, Zilla Parishads and Taluk level offices and 21 posts created for the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Maharashtra, Nagpur Commissionerate.
The details of the posts are given in Annexure No. are shown in Figures 1 to 5.
Vacancies of more than six months in the sanctioned posts will be excluded.
The Commissioner, Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme-Maharashtra, Nagpur, Divisional Commissioner (ROHYO), District Collector and District Programme Coordinator and CEO, Zilla Parishad and Joint District Programme Coordinator should review all the total posts in their establishment and submit the information to the Department before 31.03.2019. So that the revised figure may be approved by the High Level Committee before 30.09.2019.
