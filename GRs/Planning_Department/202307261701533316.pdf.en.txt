The Proposal:
Allowances have been sanctioned to the officers/ employees of the Finance Division in the budgetary activities like Budget Room, Budget Speech Room, Resource Room, Taxation Room etc. under the Government Resolution No. 1. Accordingly, the amount of remuneration is paid in addition to the salary as per the hierarchy of the posts. As per the decision of the Government No. 2 in the context of the Finance Department, the coordination of expenditure on the program in the budget has been entrusted to the Planning Department. Therefore, the matter of sanctioning allowances to the officers/ employees involved in the process of budget preparation in the Planning Department was under the consideration of the Government. Accordingly, allowances have been sanctioned to the officers/ employees related to budgetary activities under the Government Resolution No. 3 of the Planning Department.
