The Proposal:
The state government is preparing a District Development Plan (DDP) for each district of the state. Various committees have been formed at the district and state level under the Planning Department, Government Decision No.DAP-1023/ PR No.98/ K1481, dated April 20, 2023, to prepare the District Development Plan and provide qualitative support to it.
However, as per the demand from various agencies/ units and considering the nature of work of the District Development Plan, the Member Secretary of the District Level Executive Committee
Therefore, the matter of appointing the District Planning Officer as Member Secretary instead of the Resident Deputy Collector was under the consideration of the Government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government's decision:
In order to prepare the District Development Plan and provide qualitative support to it, the Government has approved the appointment of District Planning Officer as Member Secretary in the District Level Executive Committee constituted under Decision No. DAP-1023/ PR.No. 98/ K. 1481, dated April 20, 2023, instead of Resident Deputy Collector. The District Collector will be a member of the committee.
