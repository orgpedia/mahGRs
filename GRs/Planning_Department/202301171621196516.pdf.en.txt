Appointment of nominated members and special invitees to the Pune District Planning Committee.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Planning
Government Resolution No: DAP-2022/ P.S. No.73/ KA-1481-A
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: January 17, 2023.
(1) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998 (2 of 1998) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, 2000 (3 of 2000) Letter No. Jinsinpu/ Ni-10/ Nasvinis/ 2022-23/2691, dated 17.10.2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: In pursuance of the provisions of clause (3) (2) (b) and (c) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000 and clause (3) (f) herein, the Government hereby approve the appointment of 02 members of the nominated from amongst the Members of Legislative Assembly/ Parliament, 04 nominated members having knowledge of district planning and 14 persons ordinarily resident in the area of district planning committee and having experience of district planning as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee for Pune district.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Pune should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominated members as well as special invitees to the Pune District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
