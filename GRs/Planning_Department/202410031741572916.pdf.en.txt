In order to provide and raise the level of finance, it has been decided in the Cabinet meeting dated 23rd September, 2024 to set up Veer Shiromani Maharana Pratap Economic Development Corporation. Accordingly, Veer Shiromani Maharana Pratap Economic Development Corporation is being set up as a Government guaranteed and non-share capital company under Section-8 of the Companies Act, 2013.
