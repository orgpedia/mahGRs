Government order:
Smt.Megha Hanumant Ingale, Administrative Officer-cum-Research Officer, Vidarbha Statutory Development Board, Nagpur is being appointed on deputation to the post of Statistical Officer, Fifth Maharashtra State Finance Commission, Mumbai for a period of 11 months initially subject to the following terms and conditions:
(a) Mrs. Megha Hanumant Ingale, Administrative Officer-cum-Research Officer, will commence her service as a Deputy on the date she assumes charge of her office and shall cease to hold office on the date she assumes charge of her office.
(b) If his services are deemed necessary by the Government in the interest of public service, the competent authority shall have the power to recall him at any time before the expiry of the period of his deputation.
(c) If their services are not required by the non-employer, the non-employer shall have the right to send them back to the parent office. However, the non-employee employer shall give three months notice to the competent authority before such repatriation.
And,
d) They will be allowed to return to the parent office after giving at least three months' written notice to the Government/ competent authority that they intend to return to the parent office.
The Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai shall determine the terms and conditions of the deputation taking into consideration the provisions of Rule 40 (Annexure II) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Services, etc.) Rules, 1981.
All other administrative matters of Mrs. Ingale, Administrative-cum-Research Officer shall be handled by the Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai.
