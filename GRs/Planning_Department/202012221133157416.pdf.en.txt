Subject:- Relaxation of Marathi and Hindi language for the examination of the Board of Education to the candidates appointed by nomination to the post of" Statistical Officer/ Research Officer, Group-" B" (Gazetted)" cadre on the establishment of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics.
Reference:- Planning Department Government Order No.NAM-1218/ PR No. 05/ KA-1427 dated 12/02/2019
Government order:
The candidates appointed to the post of" Statistical Officer/ Research Officer, Group-" B" (Gazetted) on establishment of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics, by nomination under the relevant Government Order, are required to pass the language test within the stipulated time.
The following officers appointed in the cadre of" Statistical Officer/ Research Officer, Group-" B" (Gazetted) on the establishment of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics have passed the examination in Secondary School with Marathi (Higher Level) and Hindi-Hindi-Sanskrit. They are, therefore, exempted from passing the Marathi Language Examination and Hindi Language Examination of the said Board as per the provisions of General Administration Department Government Notification No. M.B.A.P.- 1087/14/ CR- 2/87/20 dated 30th December, 1987 and General Administration Department Government Decision No. HIMB.A.P. 1083/1448/ 20 dated 1st December, 1984.
