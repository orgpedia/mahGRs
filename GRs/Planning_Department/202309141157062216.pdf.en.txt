A total of 51 temporary posts, 17 each in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of Maharashtra Development Board and 6 temporary posts created in the Planning Department (itself) have been extended from 01.03.2023 to 31.08.2023 under the Planning Department, Government Decision No.
2. m. The Cabinet in its meeting held on 27.09.2022 has decided to reconstitute Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the remaining Maharashtra Development Board.
Therefore, a proposal was under consideration of the Government to extend the period of 57 temporary posts from 01.08.2023 to 29.02.2024 for keeping the records of the offices of these development boards updated, utility certificates regarding the use of special funds allocated to the development boards, audit objections and other ancillary works.
