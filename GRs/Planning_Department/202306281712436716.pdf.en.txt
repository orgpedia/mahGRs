Government circular:
As per the decision of the government in reference No. 1 of the Planning Department, the revised design of the posts has been prepared and the posts have been sanctioned for two new posts in the department under the decision of the government- Office 1425-A and Office 1444-A. As per the Government Circular No. 2 of the Planning Department, the revised schedule of the department has been published by including two new offices- Office Station-1425-A and Office Station-1444-A. In the revised schedule, the subject of Hazratbaba Tajuddin Dargah, Tajbagh, Nagpur Development Plan at No. 11 of Office 1443 is being handed over to Office 1444-A. The topics of Office- 1443 and Office- 1444-A in the Planning Department are being revised as follows.
