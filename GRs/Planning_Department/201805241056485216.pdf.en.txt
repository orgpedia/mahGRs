Provisional promotion to the cadre of" Joint Director" (Group-A) in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics is being made subject to the following terms and conditions: (i) The concerned officers promoted to the post of" Joint Director" (Group-A) shall assume charge of the post assigned to them immediately on promotion and submit the transfer certificate to the Government and forward a copy thereof to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai.
If you do not want to accept the promotion, you should inform the government in writing.
(ii) If the concerned officers promoted to the post of" Joint Director" (Group-A) try to bring any kind of political pressure to change the post given after promotion, it will be considered serious and disciplinary action will be taken against them.
(iii) The concerned officers promoted to the post of" Joint Director" (Group-A) will not accept the posting given after receipt of the promotion order or will refrain from accepting the posting under various sub-sections.
(iv) If the concerned officer promoted to the post of" Joint Director" (Group-A) does not join the post of promotion after promotion, his name will be excluded from the selection list as per the standing order of the General Administration Department. After that time, your request will not be accepted.
(v) This temporary promotion to the concerned officer promoted to the post of" Joint Director" (Group-A) shall not mean that the concerned officer is eligible for regular promotion to the post of promotion and shall not be entitled to any regular promotion.
(vi) The provisional promotion granted to the concerned officer promoted to the post of" Joint Director" (Group-A) shall be subject to the approval of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
(3) The concerned officers who have been promoted to the cadre of" Joint Director" (Group-A) in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics should be relieved immediately by all the concerned heads of offices to enable them to join the promoted post by handing over the charge to other officers. If the head of an office refuses to engage the concerned officers in the post assigned to them and the question of regularizing the mandatory waiting period arises, the Government Decision of the Department of Finance, No. Seniway- 1001/130/ Service 4, D.
