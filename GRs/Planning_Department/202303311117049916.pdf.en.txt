The Proposal:
The Government have approved the following schemes for providing civic services and amenities in the jurisdiction of Urban Local Bodies, namely," Civic Services and Amenities in Municipal Corporations" and" Civic Services and Amenities in Municipalities/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats." As per the decision of the Government under Schedule No. (2), the technical and administrative approval of the works under this scheme is given by the implementing agency and the Collector respectively after the works are determined by the Urban Development Department. Also, the sanction orders are issued by the Urban Development Department after ensuring that the technical and administrative approval is given. As per the approval order of the Urban Development Department, the funds available in the account of the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Worli, Mumbai are made available to the concerned implementing agency by the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Worli, Mumbai. As per the above provision, in the year 2022-23, the budget allocated for the above schemes is Rs. The government had earmarked Rs. 1,500 crore for the purpose.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: For the financial year 2022-23, funds are being released to the Commissioner and Director, Directorate of Municipal Administration, Worli, Mumbai for the schemes" Civic Services and Amenities in Municipal Corporations" and" Civic Services and Amenities in Municipalities/ Municipal Councils/ Nagar Panchayats."
