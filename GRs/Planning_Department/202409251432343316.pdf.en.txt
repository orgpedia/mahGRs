The Proposal:
For effective implementation of the Special Action Plan and Supplementary Plan for the rapid development of the Naxal affected area, a total of 6 posts at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur (3 posts at the level of Deputy Director-1, Research Officer-1 and Statistical Assistant-1) and 3 posts at the state level (Deputy Secretary-1, Research Officer-1B Clerk Typist-1) have been created under Government Decision No. : EPD-2510 / Q. No.294 / Ka-1421, dated 22nd July, 2010 and Supplementary Notes with serial numbers dated 18th August, 2010. As per the Government Decision dated 14th March, 2024, the temporary posts were extended for the period from 19th March, 2024 to 31st August, 2024. The government was considering extending the tenure of these posts beyond September 19, 2024.
Government Decision: - For the effective implementation of the Special Action Programme and Supplementary Programmes for the speedy development of the Naxal affected areas, a total of 6 temporary posts namely 3 created at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur and 3 created at the state level are being approved under the present Government Decision for extension for the period from 1st September, 2024 to 28th February, 2025.
