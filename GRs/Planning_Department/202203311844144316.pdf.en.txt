The Proposal:
Allowances have been sanctioned to the officers/ staff of the Finance Division in the budgetary activities like Budget Room, Budget Speech Room, Resource Room, Taxation Room etc. under the Government Resolution No. 1. Accordingly, the amount of remuneration is paid in addition to the salary as per the hierarchy of posts. As per the decision of the Government No. 2 in the context of the Finance Department, the coordination of the expenditure on the program in the budget has been entrusted to the Planning Department. Therefore, the matter of sanctioning allowances to the officers/ employees involved in the budget preparation process in the Planning Department was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The preparation of the Annual Programme, which forms an integral part of the Annual Budget, and the furnishing of relevant data to the Finance Department, is undertaken by the Planning Department from October to March every year. Considering the constitutional importance and responsibility of this work as well as the obligation to complete it within the stipulated time, the work in the Planning Division Program Division i.e. 1. Officers/ staff of 1411, 1412 to 1414, 2) Work related to the district annual program. 1481, the work. 1481 (A), K. 1482, c. 1482 (a) to the officers/ employees and 3) to the officers/ employees of Development Area Operations, etc., for the additional work to be done, allowances as shown in the table below are being made admissible:
