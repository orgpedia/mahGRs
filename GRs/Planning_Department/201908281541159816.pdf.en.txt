GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the provisions contained in clause (3) (4) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve the appointment of 09 persons as" Special Invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Gadchiroli District, who are persons having experience in District Planning and are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee, as indicated in the Annexure A.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Gadchiroli should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The appointments made by the aforesaid Special Invitees to the District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
