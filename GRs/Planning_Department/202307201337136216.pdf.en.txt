The Proposal:
Students of Maratha, Kunbi, Kunbi-Maratha, Maratha-Kunbi castes in the state of Maharashtra are not able to take admission in a reputed university abroad for higher education due to financial conditions.
Students are deprived of higher education due to their quality and financial situation. The government was considering to implement a foreign scholarship scheme for the children of Maratha, Kunbi, Kunbi-Maratha, Maratha-Kunbi caste to take admission in courses in reputed universities abroad. Accordingly, Ma. Detailed Government Decision in this regard is being issued as per the decision taken by the Cabinet on 04.07.2023.

Government's decision:
The Government of Maharashtra has approved the grant of foreign scholarship to 75 meritorious students belonging to Maratha, Kunbi, Maratha-Kunbi and Kunbi-Maratha categories in the State of Maharashtra who will take admission in educational institutions/ universities ranked within 200 in the QS World Ranking for postgraduate degree/ diploma and PhD courses.
The scheme will be implemented by Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Institute of Human Development (Sarathi), Pune, a Government Institution, from the academic year 2023-24 under the name" Sayajirao Gaikwad-Sarathi Scholarship Scheme for Meritorious Boys and Girls for Higher Education Abroad."
The scope of the scheme will be as follows: The scheme will be applicable to students belonging to Maratha, Kunbi, Maratha-Kunbi and Kunbi-Maratha castes in Maharashtra. Applications from eligible students will be accepted online.
