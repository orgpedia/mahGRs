The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 2000.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the provisions contained in clause (3) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve the nomination of 04 persons as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Yavatmal district, who are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and have experience in district planning, as indicated in the attached statement A.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Yavatmal should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The nomination of the special invitees mentioned in the return' A' to the Yavatmal District Planning Committee shall remain intact until further orders or until the cancellation of the said nomination by the Government, whichever is earlier.
