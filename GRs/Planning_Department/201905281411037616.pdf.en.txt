Preamble: A total of 15 posts, 13 vacancies in the peon (Group D) cadre and 2 vacancies in the near future, on the establishment of the Planning Department, were under the consideration of the Government to fill these posts through external agencies by creating notional posts until they are filled by regular appointments.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Sub-Committee of Additional Chief Secretary (Services), General Administration Department and Secretary (Expenditure), Finance Department constituted under the Finance Department, Government Resolution, dated 9th June, 2017 has approved creation of 7 out of 11 vacancies in the cadre of Constables (Group D) in the establishment of Planning Department on 5th March, 2019 as notional posts and filling up of the same through external agencies. In view of this approval, the Government have decided to create 7 notional posts in the cadre of Constables (Group-D) in the establishment of the Planning Department.
