circular
A comprehensive policy for preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in Government service every year has been laid down. Accordingly, after final publication of seniority in the previous year, a provisional seniority list consisting of only those officers/ employees who have been appointed in the next year through regular promotion, direct service or other prescribed route is to be prepared. The last date of seniority for the post of Joint Director in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics dated 1/1/2017 has been notified vide Circular No.
The post of Joint Director includes the names of local/ ad-hoc/ promoted officers for 11-11 months only and their names will be included in the seniority list in the post of" Joint Director" as per the date on which their promotion will be regularized after the approval of Maharashtra Public Service Commission.
The Director, Directorate of Finance& Statistics, Mumbai is requested to bring this circular and the accompanying provisional service statement to the notice of all concerned authorities.
