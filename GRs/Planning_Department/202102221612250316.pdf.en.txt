GOVERNMENT DECISION: Under the Planning Department, Government Decision, No.NIVIA-1720/ No. 80/ No. 1421, dated 18th February, 2020, a total of 40 temporary posts indicated in the statement A, B, C, D, E, F and C accompanying this Government Decision on the establishment of the Planning Department (itself) were extended till 28.02.2021. The total number of 40 posts indicated in the return for the period from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021 is being approved under this Government decision and the expenditure on these 40 posts should be included under the head of accounts shown in the accompanying statement (Returns A to C) and should be met from the grant sanctioned under the head for the year 2021-2022.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers delegated to the Administrative Departments of the Ministries under the Department of Finance, Government Decision, No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated February 15, 2021.
