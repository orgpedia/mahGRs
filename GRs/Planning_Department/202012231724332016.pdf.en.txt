Preamble: The Department of Rural Development, Government of India vide its resolution No. GRASSO-2015/ PR. No.12/ Scheme-9 dated 28th October, 2015 has decided to implement the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana in the State on the lines of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana for upgrading the existing rural roads and other rural roads in the State to connect the unconnected villages. Accordingly, the Planning Department, vide Government Resolution No: DAP-1015/ PR No.347/ KA-1481, dated 28th January, 2016, had issued a decision to provide 15% of the total funds available for the District Annual Plan (General) for the scheme" Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana" for the period 2016-17 to 2019-20. " It will not be possible to provide adequate funds to the Rural Development Department at the state level in the financial year 2020-21 for the Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana. In this background, the government was considering providing funds as a" special case" for the repair of rural roads that have been damaged due to heavy rains, storms and other natural causes.
Government's decision:
District Annual Plan (General) 10% of the total allocation to be made to the districts for the financial year 2020-21 is being approved for the scheme" Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana" and up to 5% of the total allocation is being made as" special case" for the immediate repair of rural roads which have been damaged due to heavy rains, storms and other natural causes.
