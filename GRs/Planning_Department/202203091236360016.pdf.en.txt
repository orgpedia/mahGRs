As per Section 133 of the Memorandum of Association and Articles of Association of the" Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation," the" Board of Directors" of the" Annasaheb Patil Economic Development Corporation" shall be competent to perform administrative duties and financial responsibilities under their supervisory control. As per the provision of the Companies Act, 1956, the Managing Director shall make arrangements for the regular holding of meetings of the Company Committees, which are mandatory for a registered company under section 10 (26) (b), to be established at the level of Managing Director with the approval of the Board of Directors. Also, the annual general meeting of the corporation" Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation" should be held regularly.
7. The Registrar of Companies, Mumbai, in respect of the Corporation of Bombay, under the Companies Act, 1956 and rules/ notification/ circular thereunder, has issued instructions and instructions regarding the business and policy and day-to-day affairs of the Company, as per the provisions of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India (Audit)-II (Comptroller and Auditor General of India Act-1971), to the Principal Accountant General (Auditor General), Mumbai/ Nagpur, State of Maharashtra, in respect of the financial management and audit of the Corporation and to the rules and regulations of the concerned administrative department of the State Government.
The Managing Director will be responsible for submitting the action taken report in all matters of directions and suggestions from the competent authority regarding implementation, with the approval of the Board of Directors within the prescribed period.
Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai will be autonomous to implement the activities and welfare schemes with the approval of the Board of Directors to achieve the 5 main objectives and 51 incidental or ancillary objectives mentioned in the" Memorandum of Association" of the company. The Board of Directors, taking into account the funds available from the Government in the financial year, may select and implement the necessary activities and welfare schemes out of the 5 main objectives and 51 incidental or ancillary objectives mentioned in the Memorandum of Association and the financial criteria to be determined regarding similar activities and welfare schemes to be implemented by the concerned administrative department.
Appropriate decision can be taken at the level of the Board of Directors, taking into account the administrative procedures etc. In this regard, the Managing Director, Annasaheb Patil Economic Backward Development Corporation, Mumbai will not be required to take prior consent from the concerned administrative department.
