The Proposal:
General Administration Department, in accordance with the Government Decision No. 308/16-B, dated 21.12.2019, Planning Department, Government Decision No. 1019/ PR. A total of 22 supernumerary posts have been created in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics under the Department of Planning vide No. 129/ KA-1426 dated 3.9.2020 for various cadres of officers/ employees belonging to Group B (Gazetted), Group B (Non-Gazetted) and Group C who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but have not been able to produce the caste certificate. The matter of extension of the first 11 months of service of the officers/ staff who have been transferred to the higher post after the expiry of their service period on 30.11.2020 was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: As per the General Administration Department, Government Decision No. BCC-2018/ P. No. 308/16-B dated 21.12.2019, a total of 22 officers/ staff have been created in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics. Under this, a total of 22 officers/ staff of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics have been appointed for the first 11 months from 1.1.2020 to 30.11.2020.
A total of 22 officers and staff who have been transferred/ appointed to the supernumerary posts in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics are being given extension of tenure in the superannuation for the next 11 months after the expiry of the period of 11 months in the superannuation or till the date on which they would have retired had they continued in service or till the recommendations of the Study Group are received by the Government and the Government takes a final decision, whichever is earlier.
Necessary action in respect of extension of tenure of officers/ staff of Directorate of Finance& Statistics in superannuation will be taken by the Planning Department as well as the Director, Directorate of Finance& Statistics, Mumbai.
The expenditure on the salary of 22 supernumerary posts in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics shall be borne by the Drawing and Disbursing Officer of the concerned office under the head of salary accounts and shall be met from the sanctioned grant of the concerned financial year.
This Government Resolution is being issued as notified under the General Administration Department, Government Resolution No. 308/16-B, dated 27.11.2020.
