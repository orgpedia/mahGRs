The Proposal:
Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) are established under the Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976. Currently, Maharashtra Gramin Bank and Vidarbha Konkan Gramin Bank are the two regional rural banks in the state of Maharashtra, in which the central government has 50 per cent stake, sponsored banks 35 per cent and the state government 15 per cent. Letter No. 3/9/2020 of Additional Secretary, Department of Financial Services, Ministry of Finance, Government of India- RRB, D. Instructions have been received from the Government of Maharashtra to disburse their 15% stake by March 29, 2023.
Accordingly, Maharashtra Gramin Bank will be given 15% share of the state government in 2022-23.
