Public Health Department Notification No. 2020/ p. In accordance with the provisions of Sections 2, 3 and 4 of the Epidemic Diseases Act, 1897, vide No.58/ Health-5, dated 14th March, 2020, the Government of Maharashtra has framed the Maharashtra COVID-19 Measures Rules, 2020 for prevention, containment and management of communicable diseases caused by COVID-19 virus. In this backdrop, as a one-time" special case" to strengthen the district level action against COVID-19 virus, it is necessary to provide immediate medical equipment and materials in case of emergency arising out of COVID-19 under MLA Local Area Development Programme 2019-20 and 2020-2021.
2. The Government is pleased to grant one time" special case" funds to the extent of Rs.50 lakh to the Members of Legislative Assembly for the purchase of the following" Medical Equipment and Materials" for the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 under the" MLA Local Area Development Programme" for taking effective preventive action at the district level during the emergency situation arising out of COVID-19 virus.

If the members of the Legislative Assembly recommend funds for the purchase of" Medical Machinery and Materials" for the year 2019-20 and 2020-21 under the MLA Local Development Program, the Collector shall give administrative approval and thereafter the head of the district level operational system (1) District Civil Surgeon (2) District Health Officer, Zila Parishad or Commissioner, Municipal Corporation and Chief Officer, Municipal Councils as per the requirement, may purchase medical machinery and materials as per the following conditions.
Under the MLA Local Development Program, medical equipment and facilities in connection with" COVID-19" (COVID 19) should be purchased according to the policy and procedures prescribed by the Department of Public Health or the Department of Medical Education and Medicines.
Funds for maintenance and repair in respect of medical equipment and facilities in the context of" COVID-19" shall not be provided under the MLA Local Development Programme. Also, no" recurring expenditure" for such medical infrastructure facilities will also be admissible under the MLA Local Area Development Programme.
