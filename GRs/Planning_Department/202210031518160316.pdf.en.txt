Mr. Dilip Sadashivrao Rajurkar has been appointed as a Member of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of Incumbency, Non-Ministerial Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981 vide Rule 40, Annexure 2, Clause No. According to section 18, promotion is being given in the form of temporary. The extension period shall be 6 months from the date of this order. After this, the officers will have to appear in the parent department.
The promotion is granted subject to the following terms and conditions:
(1) Take charge of the office of promotion in accordance with the said order and the certificate of transfer of charge for doing so shall be submitted to the Government and a copy thereof shall be forwarded to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai. If you do not want to accept the promotion, you should inform the government in writing.
2) Ma. Special Leave Petition No. The said promotion is being granted only on temporary basis subject to the decision dated 28306/2017.
