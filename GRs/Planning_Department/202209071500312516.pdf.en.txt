A total of 51 temporary posts, 17 each in the offices of the Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of the Maharashtra Development Board, and 6 temporary posts created in the Planning Department (itself) have been approved by the Planning Department, Government Decision No. EST-2021/ Q. No.15/ K. 1426 dated 03/03/2022 has been extended from 01/03/2022 to 31/08/2022.
The final decision on extension of the period beyond 30th April, 2020 for Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and remaining Maharashtra Development Boards is yet to be taken. Therefore, a total of 57 temporary posts, namely 51 temporary posts on the establishment of these Development Boards and 6 temporary posts in the Planning Department, have not been extended for a period beyond 31/08/2022. In this background, a proposal was under the consideration of the Government to extend the tenure of 57 temporary posts from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023 for a total of 51 temporary posts of 17 each in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and Rest of Maharashtra Development Board and 6 temporary posts in the Planning Department (itself) to complete the utility certificate, audit objections and other ancillary works related to the utilization of special funds allocated to the development boards.
