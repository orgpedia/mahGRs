The Government has decided to provide administrative technical sanction powers under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS). The committee was constituted on June 17, 2016. The committee has been constituted for a period of nine months. The term of the committee. The Committee adopted a resolution on 16 March 2017. As of August 29, 2017. The deadline has been extended to March 17, 2018. Therefore, the Divisional Commissioner, Aurangabad, has requested to extend the term of this committee. The letter dated March 28, 2018. Considering the urgency of the work in Aurangabad division, the matter of extension of the committee was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In order to implement the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme in Aurangabad division and the" Samruddha Maharashtra Janakalyan Yojana" under it effectively and considering the scope of the newly started works in Aurangabad division, the screening committee has been tasked to examine the proposals of various public and personal benefit works of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme and recommend necessary work. The deadline has been extended from March 18, 2018 to March 17, 2019. The decision was taken by the committee. As reported on June 17, 2016
