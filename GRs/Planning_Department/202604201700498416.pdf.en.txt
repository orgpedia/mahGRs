The Proposal: -
It was decided in the Cabinet meetings held on 7th June, 2006 to constitute a Cabinet Sub-Committee to study the scope and modalities of the works under the MLA Local Development Programme and take a final decision for the work to be done in a more dynamic manner. Accordingly, the No. The Cabinet Sub-Committee has been constituted by Government Decision 1, 3, 4B5. The government was contemplating the reconstitution of such a Cabinet Sub-Committee.
