Government's decision:
Under the MLA Local Development Program, the Planning Department, Government Circular No. 1121/ S.No.101/ K. 1482 dated 15th December, 2021 has been approved to provide necessary funds under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) for works under Matoshree Gram Samriddhi Khet/ Panand Road Scheme through convergence under MLA Local Area Development Programme. Further, it is now being approved to provide funds under MLA Local Area Development Programme through Convergence for works of public nature which are permissible under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) and which are likely to generate permanent government revenue, subject to the following conditions:
I. The MLA fund can be made available through circulation only for public works proposed under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, which will generate permanent government revenue. However, under no circumstances shall MLA funds be allowed for personal benefit activities.
The skilled share (Part C) out of 40% of the State Government's share in the MNREGA scheme in the ratio of 60:40 can be made available from the MLA fund.
|| The proposed works should be included in the Convergence Plan by the District Level Convergence Committee constituted for this purpose by the Employment Guarantee Division.
The MLA fund has been allocated for works under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The administrative and technical approval of those works and the implementation of the convergence plan were discussed. It will be mandatory to comply with the prescribed procedures and terms/ conditions as of March 30, 2021.
