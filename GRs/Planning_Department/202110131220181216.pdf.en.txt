Government's decision
The Selection List (Preparation and Revision) Rules, 1977 have been issued for promotion to the post of Deputy Director, Group-A and Joint Director, Group-A in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics vide Notification No. STW-1173/ O-129/ MDM-3 dated 17th August, 1977. As per Rule-3 of the said Rules, Planning Department constituted a" Selection Committee" vide Government Resolution No: EST-1017/ Q. No.30/ Q. 1427 dated 06/04/2017. The selection committee needs to be reconstituted as the above Mr. V.F. Vasave, Deputy Secretary has been transferred and posted in the Tribal Department. Accordingly, the Planning Department vide Government Resolution No: EST-1017/ Q.No.30/ Q. 1427 dated 06/04/2017 is constituting the Selection Committee as follows:
