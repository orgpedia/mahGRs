The Project Implementation Committee of the Planning Department shall have the power to decide on the selection of suppliers/ consultants and other ancillary matters for providing necessary hardware/ software for the implementation of IT projects and computerization. After the approval of the Project Implementation Committee constituted as per the decision of the Government, computerization and related matters i.e. purchase of necessary equipment like computers etc. and development of computer system should be done as per the Government decision in the above context and as per the Government decision/ circulars issued by the concerned administrative department from time to time and as per the Government policy regarding procurement.
