The Proposal:
The Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI), Government of India has started the work of compiling 'Environmental Accounts' following the internationally recognized 'System of Environmental Economic Accounting (SEEA)'. Accordingly, the publication 'Envistats India Vol Il: Environment Accounts' is published from the year 2018 under the overall guidance of the Inter-Ministerial Group (IMG) on Environmental-Economic Accounting-India constituted under the chairmanship of the Director General (Statistics).
Therefore, since the Commissionerate of Statistics is the nodal agency for all statistical activities in the state, the government was considering the setting up of a state-level inter-departmental group under the chairmanship of the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Finance and Statistics to coordinate the process of obtaining information for easy collection of 'Environmental Accounts'.
The Government's Decisions:
As per the guidelines of the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MOSPI), the following State Level Inter-Departmental Group is being constituted by the Commissionerate of Finance and Statistics for collection of Environmental Accounts: -

The work of the group:
1.समन Ease the exchange of information and participation of member departments and coordination for timely availability of information from various departments regarding the framework, models, TAD required for 'Environmental Accounting'.
To act as a forum for coordination between the Commissionerate of Finance and Statistics and other departments.
2. Quality of information, timelines and comparability: To check the quality of information for "Environmental Accounting" collection, as well as to provide guidance to reduce errors / discrepancies / deficiencies in the information and to assess the accuracy.
To review and validate the data in the publication 'Maharashtra State Environmental Statistics'.
3. Defining Forms / Tables / Samples: To identify errors or duplications in the form / tables / samples required for collection of 'Environmental Accounts' and suggest corrections. Also guide the preparation of forms / charts / samples.
Implementation of the System of Environmental Economic Accounts Framework (SEEA): - To ensure that the accounting process is compliant with the United Nations' SEEA-Central Framework (SEEA-CF) and SEEA Ecosystem Accounting (SEEA-EA).
