The Government Decision:" As per the provisions of Section 11 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, a provision has been made in Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and 2019 regarding the reduction of an" Executive Committee" headed by the Chairman of the District Planning Committee to assist the District Planning Committee. Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, 2" Special Invitees" out of the total number of" Nominated Members" under paragraph 2 (b) of section 3 (3) of the said Act and 2" Special Invitees" out of the total number of" Special Invitees" under paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3) are required to be appointed.
As per the provisions of Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and the total number of nominated members under paragraph 2 (b) of section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998 and the total number of special invitees as per paragraph 4 (a) of section 3 (3), the District Planning Committee, Yavatmal is being constituted as follows, with the nomination of the following Hon'ble Members of Parliament and Members of Legislative Assembly of Yavatmal District, as recommended by the Chairman and Guardian Minister of Yavatmal.
