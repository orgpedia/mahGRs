The Proposal: -
As per the Government Decision dated 06.02.2019 of the Planning Department (ROHYO) Ward, 6 districts of Nagpur Division namely Gadchiroli, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Gondia, Nagpur and Wardha have been targeted for additional 13000 irrigation wells under the Dhadak programme of irrigation wells.
With reference to the above programme, No. The extension has been extended from time to time by Government Decisions 3 to 7. Under Dhadak programme of irrigation wells in Nagpur division. The extension granted until May 31, 2026, has expired.
There is a huge scope for completion of irrigation well works considering the possibility of rain delay due to Elnino impact in the state. This will result in flooding of the irrigation area and provide sustainable irrigation facilities to the beneficiaries. As a result, the income of the farmers will increase. In view of all this, the government was considering extending the irrigation wells programme. Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
The Government's Decisions:
Under the Dhadak programme of "Gadchiroli, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Gondia, Nagpur and Wardha districts of Nagpur division," the completion date of 110 incomplete irrigation wells out of 386 irrigation wells in progress is being extended up to 31st December, 2026. "
