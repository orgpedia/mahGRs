The Proposal:
Detailed instructions have been provided under the Government Decision dated 12th April, 2018 regarding the implementation of tree plantation programme by the Social Forestry Wing of the Forest Department on the farm lands of farmers and on the farm lands of individual beneficiaries under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. Now the above reference no. (2) Orders have been issued regarding the implementation of tree planting program under the government decision. This Government decision is being amended and revised orders are being issued as follows:
Government Circular: In Issue No. 1.7, the officers of the Social Forestry Branch of the Forest Department
Approval and administrative approval should be given by the concerned authorities.
The said conditions are being amended by repealing the above mentioned conditions and stating that" the technical approval of the Agriculture Officer in the Panchayat Samiti for the implementation of the tree plantation programme through the Gram Panchayat under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme should be given by the Gram Development Officer and administrative approval should be given by the Group Development Officer" to get these works done by the Gram Panchayat
