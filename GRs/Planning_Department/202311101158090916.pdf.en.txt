Preamble: The Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav is being celebrated in the State on the lines of the Central Government's initiative- Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav- to mark the 75th year of Indian Independence. Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs. 1 As per the decision of the Government dated 28.09.2023, under this initiative, various programmes have been approved to be organized in the State and funds will be provided under the umbrella scheme for the programmes/ schemes to be implemented by various departments on the occasion of the Amrit Mahotsav of Swatantra.
During the celebration of' Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav', a proposal was submitted to the Department of Cultural Affairs by the Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai under the Planning Department to compile and publish the data/ information available in various publications since the formation of the State of Maharashtra with a focus on post-independence achievements. Under the said Mahotsav, the Core Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Minister, Cultural Affairs, in its meeting held on 5.12.2022, has decided to compile and publish the Economic Survey of Maharashtra and State Statistics on the above subject of the Planning Department. Rs. 2 crore for the project
The request for funds has been approved. The minutes of the meeting have been obtained from the letter dated 17.02.2023 in Reference No.3 of the Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs. Accordingly, a detailed proposal in this regard has been submitted to the Government by the Directorate of Finance& Statistics, Mumbai vide letter dated 2.11.2023. The proposal had been approved by the government.
