(2) The transferred officers shall ensure that they join the place of transfer within the period of assumption of office permissible as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Duration of Appointment, Post-retirement Service and Provisions in respect of Suspension, Dismissal and Removal) Rules, 1981. If he does not join the post of a replacement during this period, the period of absence shall be deemed to be a non-working day.
In view of the provisions of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct of Business) Rules, 1979, the above officers will be liable to disciplinary action if they submit any kind of application for change in their postings after their transfer.
(4) As the officers mentioned in the above table are being transferred for administrative reasons under this Government Order of the Planning Department, they will be paid the period of office, travel allowance and daily allowance as permissible under the rules. Also, the officers mentioned in the above table are being relieved from duty from the date of this order. The date of demitting office and assumption of charge by the officers mentioned in the above table should be immediately communicated to the Cell Officer, Planning Department at the e-mail address balaso. gaikwad@nic. in as well as to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Mumbai.
