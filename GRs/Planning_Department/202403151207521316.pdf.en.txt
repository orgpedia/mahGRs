The Proposal: -
The state aims to achieve a US $1 trillion economy by 2028 for a developed India. Achieving this goal requires focusing on districts as catalysts for economic growth and state gross state income using a "bottom up" approach. Also, the focus on districts as "centres of development" will enable the development of a thoughtful and specific approach to economic development, which will be inclusive and will also help achieve the Sustainable Development Goals.
In view of the above, the State Government has issued guidelines to all the districts of the State for preparation of District Development Plan. Chief Secretary The guidelines have been circulated through a letter dated March 28, 2023. As well as the Planning Department, Government Decisions. April 20, 2023 and D. Various committees have been constituted at the district and state level to prepare the District Development Plan as on 21st January, 2024 and provide qualitative support to it. It is essential that the District Development Plans / Projects / Schemes prepared by the districts of the state with the approval of the District Planning Committee for the economic development of the district are implemented from this financial year. Accordingly, the State Cabinet approved the implementation of the District Development Plan. As per the decision taken in the meetings on 11th March, 2024, the issue of approval of District Development Plans along with Annual Action Plan was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken.
Government Decisions: - All the District Collectors of the state have prepared a "District Development Plan" with the approval of the District Planning Committee in consultation with the strengths and weaknesses of their districts, potential investment opportunities, planning development plan, agriculture plan of the district, export policy and various policies of the state government as well as representatives of various industry sectors in the district. The District Development Plans will be implemented from the financial year 2024-25.

1) All the District Collectors of the State have submitted to the Government their District Development Plan approved by the District Planning Committee for a period of five years along with the State Development Plan for each year.
