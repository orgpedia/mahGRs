Government circular
A comprehensive policy for preparation and publication of seniority of officers/ employees in Government service every year has been laid down. Accordingly, after final publication of seniority in the previous year, a provisional additional seniority is to be created in the next year consisting only of officers/ employees who have been appointed through regular promotion, direct service or other prescribed route. The last date of seniority of the cadre of Additional Director in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics as on 1.01.2016 has been notified vide Circular No. 1.
