The Government Decision:" In accordance with the provisions of Section 11 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, a provision has been made in Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 regarding the reduction of an" Executive Committee" under the chairmanship of the Chairman of the District Planning Committee to assist the District Planning Committee. Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, 2" nominated members" out of the total number of" nominated members" under paragraph 2 of section 3 (3) of the said Act and 2" special invitees" out of the total number of" special invitees" as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3) are required to be appointed.
As per the provisions of Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and out of the total nominated members under paragraph 2 (a) of section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998 and the total special invitees as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3), the District Planning Committee, Gondia, as recommended by the Chairman and Guardian Minister, is being constituted as follows:
