the prelude
With regard to the revised administrative approval, on Tuesday, The meeting of the Expenditure Priority Committee was held on 5.9.2023. In the minutes of the meeting, suggestions were given on the action to be taken on the revised administrative sanction proposals to be submitted to the Expenditure Priority Committee.
It suggested that the Planning Department should take action to determine the ratio of the cost of administrative approval/ revised administrative approval proposals from the Water Resources and Soil and Water Conservation Departments to the cost of land acquisition. Accordingly, the Department of Water Resources and Soil and Water Conservation was under the consideration of the government to form a committee in order to determine the policy regarding the cost of land acquisition out of the project cost while undertaking new projects.
Government's decision
As indicated by the Expenditure Priority Committee, the Department of Water Resources and Soil and Water Conservation is forming the following committee to decide the policy regarding the cost of land acquisition out of the project cost while taking up new projects
