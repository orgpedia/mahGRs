The date is November 24, 2022.
(1) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998. Jignana/ Jivayo 2022-23/ Ka- 8/2022/602, dated 04.11.2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Government hereby approve the appointment of 2 members of the nominated members from amongst the Members of Legislative Assembly/ Parliament, 4 nominated members having knowledge of district planning and 14 persons ordinarily resident in the area of district planning committee and having experience of district planning as" Special Invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Nagpur District as indicated in the prospectus attached herewith as per the provisions of clause (3) (2) (b) and (c) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30 of 2000 and clause (3) (f) hereof.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Nagpur should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominated members as well as special invitees to the Nagpur District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
