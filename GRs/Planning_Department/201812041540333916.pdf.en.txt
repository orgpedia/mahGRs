No.20/ K-1417, D. 11th October, 2018 Preamble: The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana (PMMBY) Scheme under the decision of the Government in the context of the Department of Planning has been formulated by the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana Coordination Committee (PMMBY) for effective dissemination, dissemination and coordination among the masses in rural and remote areas with a view to effectively disseminate information in rural, remote and extremely remote areas and to benefit the needy, the meritorious and the unemployed. As per the Government decision dated 14.06.2016, a District Level Coordination Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of the District Collector. Every year, funds are being provided to the district level committees as per the demand of the districts for the promotion and publicity of Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana. An amount of Rs.20 crore has been budgeted for the scheme in the current financial year 2018-19 under the head 3451 O 001-26 (Advertisement and Publicity). For the financial year 2018-19, it was under consideration of the Government to provide funds to the districts for the promotion and coordination of Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana.
The decision was taken by the coordination committee of the PMMBY headed by the district collectors of Ahmednagar, Akola, Bhandara, Buldana, Gondia, Jalna, Kolhapur, Mumbai city, Nanded, Nandurbar, Nashik, Palghar, Parbhani, Raigad, Ratnagiri, Satara, Thane and Yavatmal. 3,43,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Crore Forty Three Lakhs only) is being provided.

The guidelines of the government decision in the context of the planning department as well as the instructions given by the finance department in the relevant government circular should be strictly followed while spending the funds according to the matter/ head according to the district level plan approved by the coordination committee chaired by the collector.
The above funds should be placed under the control of the concerned District Collector. The Collector concerned will be the" Controlling Officer" and the District Planning Officer concerned will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw and disburse the funds from the Treasury Office. The District Collector will be responsible as the controlling officer to keep a tab on the expenditure of this fund and to inform the government about the expenditure from time to time.
