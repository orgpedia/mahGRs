Regarding extension of tenure of 57 temporary posts from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021, including 51 temporary posts in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and remaining Maharashtra Development Boards and 6 temporary posts in the Planning Department (itself).
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Planning
Government Decision No.EST-2020/ P.S. No.32/ K.1426
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date:- 11/09/2020.
Reference:- Planning Department, Government Resolution No.EST-2020/ PR No. 32/ K. 1426 dated 28/7/2020.
A total of 51 temporary posts, 17 each in the offices of the Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of the Maharashtra Development Board, and 6 temporary posts created in the Planning Department (itself) have been approved by the Planning Department, Government Decision No. EST-2020/ Q. No.32/ K. 1426 dated 28/7/2020 was extended from 1/7/2020 to 31/8/2020.
The final decision on extension of the period beyond 30th April, 2020 for Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and remaining Maharashtra Development Boards is yet to be taken. Therefore, a total of 57 temporary posts, namely 51 temporary posts in the establishment of these development boards and 6 temporary posts in the planning department, could not be extended for a period beyond 31/8/2020. In the above background, a proposal was under consideration of the Government for extension of 57 temporary posts from 1/9/2020 to 28/2/2021 in the offices of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and Rest of Maharashtra Development Board, totaling 51 temporary posts of 17 each in Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and Rest of Maharashtra Development Board and 6 temporary posts in Planning Department (itself) to complete the utility certificate, audit objections and other ancillary works related to the utilization of special funds allocated to the development boards.
