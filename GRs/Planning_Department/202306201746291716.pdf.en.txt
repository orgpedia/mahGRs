Government's decision:
In the context of the Government decision dated 19.07.2016 relaxing the ceiling price limit of Rs.25 crore, the Government is approving the proposed development plan of' Pandharpur Devasthan Mandir Development Plan' at a cost of Rs. 7385.95 lakh.
District Collector, Solapur is being declared as the" Controlling Officer" for actual implementation of works under' Pandharpur Devasthan Mandir Development Plan." The Collector, Solapur will not be provided with a separate staff to perform the duties of" Controlling Officer." The functions of the Committee shall be as set out in the Governing Decision as follows: Page 2 of 12

1) The works proposed at" Annexure A" under' Pandharpur Devasthan Temple Development Plan" should be completed by 31/03/2025. The Collector, Solapur will be responsible as the" Controlling Officer" to ensure that the implementation and execution of the works as per the presentation made by the Collector, Solapur is completed within the time frame prescribed by the implementing agencies.
(2) The" Pandharpur Devasthan Temple Development Plan" shall not include any works other than the approved works of the development plan worth Rs. 7385.95 lakh.
The Collector, Solapur, as the Controlling Officer, shall also be responsible for ensuring that the works in the" Pandharpur Devasthan Temple Development Plan" are not duplicated and that such works are not proposed or approved or in progress in any other Government scheme.
(a) Obtaining the necessary permissions for the design:
The Collector, Solapur will be responsible as the" Controlling Officer" to take necessary approvals/ necessary no-objection permissions from the concerned authorities before implementing the works under" Pandharpur Devasthan Temple Development Plan."
(b) Technical approval of works under the scheme:
1) Before starting the work on the" Pandharpur Devasthan Temple Development Plan," technical approval should be obtained from the" competent authority" as required for the plan and budget of the related works. Necessary approval of local bodies like Zilla Parishad and Nagar Palika should also be taken before starting these works.
(2) For the completion of the works related to the" Pandharpur Devasthan Temple Development Plan" within the prescribed time frame, a" District Level Technical Committee" comprising of the district heads of all the implementing and executing agencies related to the plan should be constituted under the chairmanship of the Collector, Solapur as the" Controlling Officer." The district planning officer will be the member secretary of the committee.
The District Technical Committee shall meet at least once a month. The District Collector, Solapur, as the" Controlling Officer" shall have the power to invite other district heads to such meetings.

(a) Formation of District Level Committee and Executive Committee for Control and Implementation of the Plan: Planning Department, as per the instructions in the Government Resolution No: Tirthavi-2015/ PR No. 6/ K. 1444, dated 4th June, 2015, to implement and control the Pandharpur Devasthan Temple Development Plan approved by the Summit Committee. The district level committee will have the complete power to control and implement the Pandharpur Devasthan Temple Development Plan.
(d) Appoint consultants as required for the design of:
The project reports for the" Pandharpur Devasthan Temple Development Plan" should be prepared by the designated implementing agencies. Only if absolutely necessary, approval should be obtained from the" District Level Committee" for appointment of independent and competent consultants for specific and technical works. Such consultant should be capable and committed to complete the work independently as per the design/ blueprint prepared by the architect. The consultant appointed should have sufficient experience in this kind of work. The fee to be paid to the concerned Consultant as per the Planning Department, Government Resolution, No. 2015/ Q. No. 6/ Q. 1444, dated 19th July, 2016, should be in the form of lump sum and reasonable and should be spent from the available funds.
