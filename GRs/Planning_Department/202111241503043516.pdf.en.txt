Government circular
The Department of Planning, Government Circular No: JIHTASU-1121/ PR No.48/ KA.1427 dated 23/7/2021 has issued the provisional seniority of the cadre of" Deputy Director, Group-A" in the Directorate of Finance and Statistics as on 01/01/2021.
2. In the circular dated 23/7/2021 issued in the context of the Department of Planning, it has been observed that the temporary seniority of the officers of the cadre of" Deputy Director, Group-A" as on 01/01/2021 is not in accordance with the instructions issued under the General Administration Department's Government Resolution No: SRV-2011/ PR No. 284/12 dated 21/10/2011.
The Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021 notified by the General Administration Department have been prepared on the basis of the suggestions given by the General Administration Department vide Circular No: SRV-2016/ PR No.281/ WORK-12, dated 13/07/2021. However, since the" Maharashtra Civil Services (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 2021" are to be implemented with effect from 21/6/2021 as notified in Rule 1 (2), the same shall be applicable for the cadre of" Deputy Director, Group-A" for the period of seniority to be notified with effect from 01/01/2022.
