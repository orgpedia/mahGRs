Preamble: The office of the Commissioner, Planning (ROHYO) Department, Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme, Maharashtra State, Nagpur is functioning at Administrative Building No. 2, B Wing, Civil Lines, Nagpur, which has been completely destroyed due to fire in the early hours of 02.05.2021. The Executive Engineer, Public Works Department, Sadar, Nagpur was informed through a letter dated May 10, 2021, to renovate the office of the Administrative Building No. 2, B Wing, Civil Lines, Nagpur, which was destroyed in the fire, and submit the budgetary estimates for the same. Accordingly, Ma. The Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur has sanctioned Rs. The budget of Rs. 1,84,52, 050/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Four Lakh Fifty Two Thousand Fifty only) has been submitted to the Administrative Sanctioning Department. The matter of administrative and financial approval of the budget was under consideration of the government.
Government decision: The Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur has sanctioned Rs. The budget of Rs. 1,84,52, 050/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Four Lakh Fifty Two Thousand Fifty only) has been submitted to the Administrative Sanctioning Department. The Commissioner, Nagpur, Shri. The Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Nagpur presented Rs. Administrative and Financial Sanction is being accorded to the Budget Estimates for an amount of Rs. 1,84,52, 050/- (Rupees One Crore Eighty Four Lakh Fifty Two Thousand Fifty only).
The following conditions must be observed while carrying out the renovation work: 1) The newly made furniture, wooden dividers and other wooden items should be fire proofed.
2) Ensure that there is no leakage of water on the ground floor.
