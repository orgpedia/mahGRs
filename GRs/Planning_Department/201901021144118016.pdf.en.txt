District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Kolhapur
His letter No. Planning-Ka.18/ Works Committee/ 2978/2018-19, dated 17/12/2018 and vide No. Planning- 18/ Working Committee/ 3058/2018-19, Dy. 26/12/2018 GOVERNMENT DECISION:" In accordance with the provisions of Section 12 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998, a provision has been made in Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 regarding the reduction of an" Executive Committee" under the Chairmanship of the Chairman of the District Planning Committee, to assist the District Planning Committee. Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, 2" nominated members" out of the total number of" nominated members" under paragraph 2 (b) of section 3 (3) of the said Act and 2" special invitees" out of the total number of" special invitees" under paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3) of the said Act are required to be appointed.
As per the provisions of Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and out of total nominated members under paragraph 2 (b) of section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998 and total special invitees as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3), the District Planning Committee, Kolhapur is being constituted as follows.
