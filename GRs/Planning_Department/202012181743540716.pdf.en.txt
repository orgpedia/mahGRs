The funds under the Hill Division Development Programme should be utilized as per the following terms and conditions:
(1) The funds disbursed under the Hill Division Development Programme should be immediately disbursed to all the concerned agencies as per the guidelines in the relevant Government Decisions and Government Circulars.
(2) Funds should be made available from the funds distributed under the Hill Division Development Programme, first for the completion of the works started in the previous financial year and now incomplete.
(3) Control of expenditure under the Hill Division Development Programme. to be carried out in accordance with the instructions in section 155. Also, the consolidated expenditure should be reconciled with the accounts of the Accountant General every month. Every quarterly expenditure should be verified with the sample register kept in the office of the Accountant General, within two months from the end of the quarter and a consolidated report should be submitted to the government.
(4) The financial and material achievements (work-wise) of the expenditure made under the Hill Division Development Programme should be submitted to the Government before the 5th of every month. Also, the completed work, the completion certificate and the transfer of work should also be submitted to the government. pending
