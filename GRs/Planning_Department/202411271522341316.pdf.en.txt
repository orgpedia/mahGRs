It is an important tool for measuring growth rates in various sectors such as services, industry, services, etc. Similarly, state income data is used to take holistic measures to increase the growth rate in these various sectors.
At present, the state income data is being prepared on the basis of base year 2011-12 under the guidance and methodology of the central government. The Central Government has decided to change these base years and the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, National Statistical Office, New Delhi, Government of India has reconstituted the STATIS (Extraordinary 09296) "National Accounts Advisory Committee * dated 27th June 2024 to take a decision on the same. On the same lines, the formation of an "Advisory Committee on State Income" at the state level was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the government is taking the following decisions.
Government Decisions: An "Advisory Committee on State Income" is being set up to determine the appropriate base year for estimating gross state income at the state level on the lines of the Central Government, as well as to suggest appropriate procedures for calculating gross state income and district income in a more accurate manner.
