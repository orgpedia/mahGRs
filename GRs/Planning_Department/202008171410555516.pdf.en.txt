The Proposal:
The e-Governance policy of the State has been announced under the Government Resolution No. 1 under the context to make all government services available to the general public at affordable, transparent and fast through information technology. Paragraph 12.3 of the said Government Decision
Accordingly, the concerned administrative departments have issued guidelines regarding the formation of the Project Implementation Committee. Also the structure of the project implementation committee is prescribed in paragraph 6 of the governing decision of reference number 2. Accordingly, the government was considering the formation of a Project Implementation Committee to implement computerization and e-governance of the Planning Department and all field offices under the department.
Government's decision:
General Administration Department, dated 23/09/2011 and 21/09/2012
As per the guidelines/ directions given under the Government decision of the day, the Project Implementation Committee (Project Implementation Committee) is as follows to implement computerization and e-governance system in all the field offices under the Planning (ROHO) Department and the Department.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
1) To decide on the policy in line with the e-governance policy of the Directorate of Information Technology 2) To take a final decision regarding the online functioning of all the field offices under the Planning (ROHYO) Department. Also, hold meetings as needed.
3) To define the scope of all e-governance projects to be implemented by the Planning (ROHYO) Department and guide the tender process by finalizing the RFP (Request for Proposal) to start the e-governance project for the Department.
4) Decision-making for the purchase of hardware/ software required for the implementation of e-governance projects in the Planning (ROHYO) Department.
Review of e-governance projects in the Planning (ROHYO) Department, selection of consultant from reputed organization as per requirement for e-governance projects to be implemented in the department or for subordinate offices.
The Committee shall have the powers to implement the project as per the decisions taken by the Department of Information Technology (General Administration Department) from time to time as well as the powers conferred.
