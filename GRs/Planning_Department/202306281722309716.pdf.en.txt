Preamble Under the Government decision dated 14 th December, 2022, it has been decided to implement the Suvidha Sampoorna Mission and Sarvottam Gram Samriddhi Yojana through convergence of various schemes of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme and other departments. A provision has been made to provide the services of a retired officer of the Indian Administrative Service in the post of Mission Director General, MGNREGA for the implementation of the scheme. Considering the experience and efficiency of Shri Nand Kumar Verma, IAS (Retd.), as Additional Chief Secretary, RoHo, the Department would be benefitted, it was under consideration of the Government to appoint him as Mission Director General, MGNREGA on contract basis.
Nand Kumar Verma, IAS (Retd.) has been appointed as Mission Director General, MGNREGA. From the date of this order, appointment to the post of Mission Director General, MGNREGA is being made on contractual basis for a period of 1 year.
The terms and conditions of appointment on contractual basis shall be as under: 1. The amount payable to Mr. Nand Kumar Verma after deducting his present pension (including Dearness Allowance and Dearness Allowance) from the total salary (including Basic Pay and Dearness Allowance) received before his retirement, shall be fixed as monthly remuneration.
2. As long as Mr. Nand Kumar Verma is employed on contract basis, all service concessions and allowances admissible to him for the post of Secretary shall be admissible.
The terms and conditions as may be determined by the Government from time to time shall be binding on Mr. Nand Kumar Verma.
During the period of such appointment, Shri Nand Kumar Verma, who is discharging the duties of the post of Mission Director General, MGNREGA, shall be governed by the rules of conduct, discipline and appeal applicable to officers in the State Government service.
