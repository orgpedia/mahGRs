4) Letter from the District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Bhandara No. GISIN/ BH/ GVO/ 21-22/2022/ 224, Dy. 16.02.2022
The Government Decision:" As per the provisions of Section 11 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, a provision has been made in Rule 6-A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 regarding the constitution of an" Executive Committee" under the chairmanship of the Chairman of the District Planning Committee to assist the District Planning Committee.
Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, 2" Special Invitees" out of the total number of" Nominated Members" under paragraph 2 of Section 3 (3) of the said Act and 2" Special Invitees" out of the total number of" Special Invitees" under paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of Section 3 (3) are required to be appointed.
As per the provisions of Rule 6A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and the total number of nominated members under paragraph 2 of section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998 and the total number of special invitees as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3), the' Executive Committee' of the District Planning Committee, Bhandara is being constituted as recommended by the Chairman and Guardian Minister of the District Planning Committee, Bhandara.
