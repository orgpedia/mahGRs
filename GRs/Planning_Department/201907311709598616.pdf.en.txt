The Proposal:
The Department of Finance, Government of India vide its Resolution No. Vepur-2018/ S.No.44/ Service-9, dated 19.09.2017, has approved the recommendations contained in Volume-I of the Report on 7th Pay Commission submitted by the State Pay Revision Committee, 2017. Under 1/01/2019, the" Revised Pay Matrix" has been implemented for State Government officers and employees in the service of the Government. The Governing Council of Maharashtra Remote Sensing Deployment Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur in its 47th meeting held on 19th June, 2019 had recommended for implementation of the revised pay matrix of 7th Pay Commission for the officers and staff of the technical/ administrative posts in the Maharashtra Remote Sensing Deployment Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur, an autonomous organization of the Department of Planning. As the Finance Department has agreed to implement the revised pay matrix of the 7th Pay Commission to the officers and employees of the Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur, an autonomous organization under the Department of Employment, the government was considering to implement the revised pay matrix of the 7th Pay Commission to the officers and employees of the Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur.

The instructions issued The Government is pleased to approve the applicability of the Annexure to Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur, an autonomous organization, in the technical and administrative posts as indicated in the Annexure to this Government Resolution, with suitable modifications.
2. The officers/ staff of the technical and administrative posts in the autonomous organization" Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur" shall submit the necessary options for determining the salary as per the revised pay matrix of the" 7th Pay Commission" to the" Director, Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur" within 1 month from the date of issue of this Government decision. Once selected, the option will be considered final.
As the post of Director at Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur is being filled up by deputation of scientists from ISRO, Government of India and the pay scale of scientists is comparatively higher, the post was proposed to be at a relatively higher pay scale (S-29: 131100-216600) as per the revised pay matrix of the 7th Pay Commission. However, if a scientist from ISRO is appointed on deputation to the post of Director (S 27:118500-214100) of Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur, in a higher pay scale, action will be taken at the government level for temporary elevation of the post.
Accordingly, at present, the regular pay scale of lower pay scale (S 27:118500-214100) is being applied for the post of Director of Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur as per the revised pay matrix.
4. On receipt of options from the Director, Maharashtra Remote Sensing Deployment Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur, the pay fixation of the concerned technical and administrative officers/ staff shall be subject to scrutiny by the Pay Fixation Unit. In case of any future change in the fixation of pay, the difference in the amount of additional payment to the concerned officer/ employee on account of fixation of remuneration will be recovered. In this regard, it will be mandatory for all the officers/ employees to fill the' Undertaking' in' Addendum-4' in the prescribed format. " The"" Revised Pay Matrix"" in Annexure-II to the Maharashtra Civil Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 2019 notified by the Department of Finance, Government of Maharashtra vide Notification dated 30.1.2019 specifies the revised pay levels equivalent to the unchanged pay structure of all the posts, which should be verified in person by the"" Accountant"" of the Maharashtra Remote Sensing Application Centre (MRSAC), Nagpur."
