As on 23/4/2021, for the construction of the building of the Headquarter of the Company" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi)," Pune, at TP-1, Mauje Bhamburda, Shivajinagar, Pune, a building has been constructed at Town Geo Assessment No. The land measuring 4163 sq.m. in 173-B/ 1 has been transferred from the School Education Department to" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (SARATHI), Pune" on 30/04/2021. As per the approval given in the meeting of the High Powered Committee of Secretaries constituted on 30.06.2021 regarding the budget of expenditure for the construction of the headquarters building of the company" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune" and the construction of the government building, the planning department has decided to provide a budget of Rs. 42,70,00, 000/ (Rupees Forty Two Crores Seventy Lakhs only) for the work.
