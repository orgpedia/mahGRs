GOVERNMENT DECISION: In accordance with the provisions of clause (3) (2) (b) and clause (3) (c) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve the appointment of two members of nomination from amongst the Members of Legislative Assembly/ Parliament as shown in the Annexure A and 9 persons having experience in district planning and ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee for Washim district.
The District Collector, District Planning Committee, Washim should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The appointments made by the above-mentioned nominees and special invitees to the Washim District Planning Committee shall be made until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier. It will remain intact till then.
