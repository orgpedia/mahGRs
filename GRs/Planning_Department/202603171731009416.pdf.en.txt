Circular: - General Administration (Information and Technology Sub-Division) Read No. 2 The Government Circular here provides instructions on the functioning of the e-office system to speed up the functioning of the government by making maximum use of computers in government work, streamlining the functioning, securing documents and information, and facilitating decision-making processes at a faster and faster pace.
2. General Administration Department Read No. The Government Circular herein directs that every mail received by the Ministries / Departments and their subordinate offices shall be routed through e-Office during correspondence with all the Administrative Departments / Zonal Offices of the Ministry with effect from 01.02.2023 so as to avoid wastage of time.
3. In the Planning Department, correspondence is provided by the District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee regarding District Annual Plan (General), MLA Local Development Programme, MP Local Development Programme, Hill Division Development Programme and other subjects. Submission of proposals to the Planning Department through e-Office through this correspondence will help in streamlining the process, securing documents and information, and facilitating the decision-making process. Therefore, the District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee shall strictly follow the following procedure for sending every mail / proposal through e-office while communicating with the Planning Department.
(1) The District Planning Committee Offices, while communicating with the Planning Department, shall submit every post / proposal along with necessary documents to the Additional Chief Secretary / Principal Secretary / Secretary and Development Commissioner, Planning Department through e-office (in the form of receipt) signed by the Collector and Member Secretary District Planning Committee. (4) The officers / staff of the Planning Department shall process the proposals received from the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee in the form of Receipt through e-Office System through e-Office System only.
All District Planning Committee offices in the state will be required to strictly follow the above procedure while submitting proposals to the government. Please note this ATA.
