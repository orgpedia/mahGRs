The proposal-
As per the Government Decision, Rural Development Department No. Mugrayo-2021/ PR No. 165/ Construction-4, dated 06/01/2022," Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2" has been started on the lines of" Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-1" to upgrade the existing and dilapidated roads in the state of Maharashtra and this scheme will be implemented in phases. As per point number 3 of this decision, instructions have been given that 10 percent of the funds available to the District Planning Committee annually will be made available to the District Planning Committee for" Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2" in addition to the funds made available for the road sector. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds under the District Annual Plan (General) for the" Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-2" was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
For the" Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II," a target of 10,000 km has been set for the upgradation of rural roads. As per the circular dated 21/04/2022 issued by the Department of Rural Development, the length of the road has been allocated district-wise as per the target fixed under" Mukhyamantri Gram Sadak Yojana Phase-II." As per the district-wise allocation of road length by the Rural Development Department, the Government is approving to provide district-wise funds under the District Annual Plan (General) to the tune of Rs.1,000 crore per annum for two financial years 2022-23 and 2023-24 as mentioned in the Annexure. This fund is in addition to the funds made available for road schemes implemented under the District Annual Plan (General).
