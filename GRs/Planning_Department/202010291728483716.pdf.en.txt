Regarding the distribution of funds to the Mudra Bank Scheme District Coordination Committee formed at the district level for the promotion, dissemination and coordination of the scheme so that the maximum number of youth can get the benefit of the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Scheme.

Planning Division, Government Resolution No: SLBC 1616/ PR No.49/ KA-1417D 14th June, 2016.
Preamble: As per the decision of the Government in the context of the Department of Planning, the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana Coordination Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of the Collector to effectively disseminate, disseminate and coordinate the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana in rural, remote and extremely remote areas so that the benefits of the scheme reach the needy, the meritorious and the unemployed. In pursuance of the above decision, funds are being provided to the District Level Coordination Committee every year from the financial year 2016-17, as per the demand and population of the districts for the promotion and publicity of Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana.
Out of the funds disbursed during 2019-20, 6 districts namely Jalgaon, Hingoli, Chandrapur, Aurangabad, Sangli and Gadchiroli have not been able to submit any payments to the Treasury since 22nd March, 2020 due to technical glitches in the BEAMS system. Therefore, these 6 districts have requested the Government to provide funds from the available funds for 2020-21 to clear the pending dues of publicity and publicity works for 2019-20. Accordingly, the matter of providing funds to these 6 districts was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana District Level Coordination Committee, which was formed under the chairmanship of the District Collectors of Jalgaon, Aurangabad, Chandrapur, Hingoli, Sangli and Gadchiroli, has been given the following total amount of Rs. 91,84,000/- (Rupees Ninety One Lakh Eighty Four Thousand only) is being provided.

1.The guidelines of the relevant government decision should be strictly followed while spending the above funds.
The above funds should be placed under the control of the concerned District Collector. The District Collector will be the Controlling Officer and the District Planning Officer will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer. The Controller shall be responsible for keeping a tab on the expenditure of the Fund and for furnishing information to the Government from time to time.
The District Collector will be the nodal officer.
The aforesaid funds are required to be deposited under the head Demand No.O-7,3451, Secretariat-Financial Services, 090, Secretariat (01), Planning Division, (01) (11), Promotion, Promotion and Co-ordination of Mudra Bank Scheme (Under Schemes) (Dattamat), 26 Advertisement and Publicity (3451A 001) for the financial year 2020-21.
