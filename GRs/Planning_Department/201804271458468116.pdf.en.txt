Reference No. (4) As per the circular dated 2.4.2018 of the Department of Finance, the budgetary allocation of mandatory and programme expenditure for the first nine months of 2018-19 (ending December 2018) has been allocated to the BEAMS system within the limit of 70% of the total annual allocation. Accordingly, Rs. 665000.00 thousand (70%) has been provided by the Department of Finance on Budget Distribution System. Terms and Conditions:-
(1) The said fund shall be immediately disbursed to all the concerned agencies as per the guidelines in the relevant Government decisions and Government Circulars.
(2) The funds allocated should be made available primarily for the completion of works started in the previous financial year and now incomplete, i.e., for spill over.
(3) The expenditure under this programme shall be governed by the provisions of clause (1) of section 155 of the Maharashtra Budget Rules Book. Also, the total expenditure should be reconciled with the accounts of the Accountant General every month. Every quarterly expenditure should be reconciled with the sample register maintained in the office of the Accountant General within two months from the end of the verification quarter and a consolidated report should be submitted to the Government.
(4) The financial and material achievements (work-wise) of the expenditure made under this programme shall be submitted to the Government before the 5th of every month. Also, the completed work, the completion certificate and the transfer of work should also be submitted to the government. Pending utility certificates should be submitted to the Accountant General's office immediately, and a copy should be sent to the government.
