The Proposal:
For effective implementation of the Special Action Plan and Supplementary Plan for the rapid development of the Naxal affected area, a total of 6 posts at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur (3 posts at the level of Deputy Director-1, Research Officer-1 and Statistics Assistant-1) and 3 posts at the state level (Deputy Secretary-B-1, Research Officer-1B Clerk Typist-2) have been created under Government Decision No. : EPD-2510 / Q. No.294 / Ka-1421, dated 22nd July, 2010 and supplementary letters of serial number dated 18th August, 2010 have been generated. The provisional posts were extended for the period from 01.03.2025 to 31.08.2025 as per the Government Decision dated 22.03.2025. The government was considering extending these posts beyond September 19, 2025.
Government Decision: - For the effective implementation of the Special Action Programme and Supplementary Programmes for the speedy development of the Naxal affected areas, a total of 6 temporary posts namely 3 created at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur and 3 created at the state level are being approved under the present Government Decision for extension for the period from 01.09.2025 to 28.02.2026.
