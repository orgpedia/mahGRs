Planning Department Synchronized Government Decision D. 10.1.2023.
Government Resolution: As per the provisions of clause (3) (2) (a) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, 2000, the Government is approving the appointment of 2 members having knowledge of district planning as shown in the attached statement A and 7 persons ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and having experience of district planning as" Special Invited Members" to the District Planning Committee of Mumbai Suburban District.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Mumbai Suburban should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominated members as well as special invitees to the Mumbai Suburban District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
