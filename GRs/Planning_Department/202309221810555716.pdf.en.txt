The Proposal:
Reference No. 2 here. As per the Government decision dated 24.03.2023, the pattern of posts in the Planning Department (including Khud-Rohyo Ward) has been fixed. Accordingly, a total of 260 posts, including 225 regular posts and 35 manpower to be filled through external sources, have been sanctioned. Reference 3 No. 3 here. As per the Government decision dated 17.08.2023, 260 sanctioned posts were categorized as 209 permanent posts and 51 temporary posts. However, reference No. 3 The Government Resolution herein has been amended by superseding the Government Resolution Reference No. As per the decision of the government, 180 permanent and 45 temporary posts have been categorized.
Out of the above 45 temporary posts, action is taken separately for the extension of tenure of 6 temporary posts created for the functioning of Vidarbha Development Board, Marathwada Development Board and the rest of Maharashtra Development Board and 3 temporary posts created at the state level for the effective implementation of the Special Action Plan and Supplementary Plan for the rapid development of Naxal affected areas. The remaining 36 vacancies were under consideration for extension.
Government's decision:
Reference No. 4 of the Finance Department. in the Government Resolution dated 31.08.2023 and d. As per the provisions of the Appropriation Order dated 06.09.2023, a total of 36 temporary posts as indicated in the attached returns A, B, C, D, E and F of the Department of Employment (including Khud-Rohyo Ward) for the period from 01.09.2023 to 29.02.2024 are being approved under this Government decision.
The expenditure for this purpose shall be covered under the head of accounts referred to in the accompanying statement (Returns A to F) and the expenditure shall be met from the grants sanctioned under the respective head of accounts for the financial year 2023-2024.
The said Government Decision is being issued in exercise of the powers conferred on the administrative departments of the Ministry by the Department of Finance, Government Decision, No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC dated 31st August, 2023.
