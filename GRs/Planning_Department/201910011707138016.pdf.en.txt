The Proposal:
For the effective implementation of the Special Action Plan and Supplementary Plan for the speedy development of Naxal affected areas, 3 posts at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur (Deputy Director-1, Research Officer-1, and Statistical Assistant-1) and 6 posts at the state level (Deputy Secretary-1, Research Officer-1 and Clerk Typist-1) have been created vide Government Resolution No. As per the Government Resolution No. Navika 2418/1627/ P.No. 148/ KA-1417 dated 11th March, 2019, the temporary posts were extended for the period from 1st March, 2019 to 30th September, 2019. The Government was considering extension of the said posts for the period from 1st October, 2019 to 29th February, 2020.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: For the effective implementation of Special Action Programme and Supplementary Programme for speedy development of Naxal affected areas, the following 6 temporary posts, 3 at the level of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur and 3 at the State level, have been sanctioned for the period from 1st October, 2019 to 29th February, 2020.
