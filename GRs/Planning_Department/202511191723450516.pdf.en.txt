For the fulfillment of pending assurances, the said Assurance Committee shall act as per the instructions of the respective Executive of Section a: - 1. The Chairman of the Assurance Committee shall hold a regular review meeting of the pending assurances of the Department and other Parliamentary arms once in every 15 days.
2. The Coordinating Officers of the Assurances Committee of the Department shall take all the preliminary action by fixing a time limit for the settlement of the pending promises so that a list of the promises pending for more than two years may be submitted at the review meetings of the pending arsenals of the Department.
3. Invite field officers to the meeting whose ARATE reports are pending from the field offices and get information about the pending reports.
