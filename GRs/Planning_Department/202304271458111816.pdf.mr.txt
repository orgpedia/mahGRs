ii. The Administrator should ensure proper maintenance of Account.
iii. The credit to the PD/PLA may be done as per the GR dated 27.7.2022 and the expenditure may be made as per the approved works under Lonar Crater Development Plan. Expenditure from the PD/PLA may not be done for any other project iv. The procedure of Nil Bill may be followed as per Finance Department GR dated 27.09.2016 v. Financial transaction must be done from time to time.
vi. Either on quarterly or monthly basis, balances in this PLA shall be reconciled by the Department.
vii. All Rules in Maharashtra Treasury Rules will be applicable to this PLA.
viii.Specific internal control measures and arrangements should be put in place to monitor the operation of the PD/PLA and establish a system for continued timely reconciliation and audit of PD/PLA.
ix. Office of the Accountant General(A&E) II Nagpur will issue an authority on receipt of Government orders.
