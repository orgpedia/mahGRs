(1) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, 2000.
3) Letter from the District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee Ratnagiri
No. Planning/ Ratna/ Works-1/ Items/ 427/18.D. 23/01/2018
The Government is pleased to approve the appointment of 9 persons as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Ratnagiri district, who have experience in District Planning and are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee, as per the provision contained in sub-section (3) (4) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Ratnagiri should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments made by the above-mentioned special invitees to the Ratnagiri District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
