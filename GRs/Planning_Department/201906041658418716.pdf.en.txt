" Government Resolution: As per the provisions of Section 11 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, a provision has been made in"" Rule 6-A"" of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 regarding the reduction of an"" Executive Committee"" headed by the Chairman of the District Planning Committee to assist the District Planning Committee." Accordingly, in the aforesaid Executive Committee, 2" nominated members" out of the total number of" nominated members" under paragraph 2 of section 3 (3) of the said Act and 2" special invitees" out of the total number of" special invitees" as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3) are required to be appointed.
As per the provisions of Rule 6A of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 and out of the total number of nominated members under paragraph 2 (a) of section 3 (3) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998 and the total number of special invitees as per paragraph 4 (a) and (f) of section 3 (3), the following Executive Committee of the District Planning Committee, Nagpur is being constituted with the nomination of the following Hon'ble Members of Parliament and MLAs of Nagpur District, as recommended by the Chairman, District Planning Committee, Nagpur and the Guardian Minister.
