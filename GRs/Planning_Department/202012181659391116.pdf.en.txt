Preamble: Reference No. The Government has approved the establishment of a Government Company under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 for the Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Institute of Research, Training and Human Development (SARATHI), Pune. Also, the Government of the Bahujan Welfare Department No. SARATHI 2020/ S.No.38 (Part-II)/ Corporations D. 14 September 2020 and the General Administration Department's Government Decision No. 295/16-B dated 9th October, 2020 has been transferred to" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (SARATHI), Pune" Planning Department. Accordingly, the matter of declaring" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and" Controlling Officer" for the company" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune" was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
Sarathi-2019/ PR No.117 (Part II)/ Corporations dated 4th November, 2020 and as per the decision of the Department of Planning, the Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune has been appointed as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Managing Director, Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune has been appointed as Controlling Officer.
