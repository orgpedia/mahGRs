Preamble: It was the consideration of the Government to constitute a Study Group Committee for the following activities in the Department of Planning (ROHYO).
(a) NREGA SAPAT is a computerised system used for implementation of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS).
In such a situation, does the department need the posts of top clerks, short writers in the sanctioned pattern for the planning (ROHO) department as well as the manpower appointed to various posts on contractual basis by external agencies? Review it.
Under the Employment Guarantee Scheme, a large number of private and public works are being undertaken through the Gram Panchayat and Panchayat Samiti. Therefore, the creation of the post of Additional Block Development Officer at the Panchayat Samiti level in the entire state only for the functioning of ROHAs.
(c) To prepare the revised design of the Department using modern technology without any change in the design approved to the Planning (ROHYO) Department (by making redundant old designations obsolete and creating new designations within the limit of the total sanctioned designations).
(d) To prepare a report on the measures to be taken for the effective implementation of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme in the State and to improve the quality of work.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving the formation of a Study Group Committee in the Planning (ROHYO) Department as mentioned below to study the above mentioned activities and submit a report to the Government.
