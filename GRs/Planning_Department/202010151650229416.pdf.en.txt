There will be autonomy in the implementation of activities and welfare schemes with the approval of the" Board of Directors" to achieve incidental or ancillary objectives. After taking into account the funds available in the financial year, the Board of Directors may select and implement the necessary activities and welfare schemes out of the 3 main objectives and 82 incidental or ancillary objectives mentioned in the Memorandum of Association and take appropriate decisions at the level of the Board of Directors after taking into account the financial criteria and administrative procedures etc. determined for similar activities and welfare schemes implemented by the concerned administrative department. In this regard, the Managing Director Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi) will not need prior consent from the concerned administrative department.
