Preamble: As per the decision of the Government dated 19.07.2011 of the Planning Department, it has been decided to implement the" Human Development Programme" in 125 Atimagas Taluks of 23 districts of the State. District Planning Officer/ Assistant District Planning Officer/ Research Assistant/ Statistical Assistant in the District Level Office created for Human Development Programme
Such posts are approved and the post of' peon' cadre is not approved. As a result, district level offices are facing difficulties in carrying out similar tasks like cleaning, opening/ closing of offices, putting up files, distribution of mails, etc. Therefore, the matter of appointing multi-purpose unskilled staff in the district level offices of the Human Development Program was under the consideration of the government. As a result, the following decisions are made:
Under the" Human Development Programme," 125 backward taluks in 23 districts of the state have been included. Accordingly, in order to facilitate the regular functioning of the District level offices under the" Human Development Programme," it is being approved to appoint 23 multi-purpose unskilled staff from external agencies in a total of 23 districts, one for each district, subject to the following terms and conditions:
The multi-purpose unskilled staff to be appointed for 23 districts under the Human Development Programme should be recruited by making available the manpower at the district level from external agencies.
2) The head of the office shall be responsible for ensuring that the expenditure for this purpose is minimal and proportionate.
(3) The rules and procedures laid down by the Government for carrying out works by external agencies (contractor/ contractor/ company/ institution) shall be followed.
