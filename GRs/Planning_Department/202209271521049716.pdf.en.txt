The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998. The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) (Amendment) Act, 2000.
The Proposal:
As per the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, there is a provision to constitute District Planning Committee for each district. Accordingly, District Planning Committees have been constituted for various districts.
As per the provisions of Section 2, Section 3 (3), 2 (b) and (c) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 1998, appointments are made by the State Government to the District Planning Committees of all the districts as" nominated members." Further, in accordance with the provisions of Section 3 (3) (f) of the said Act, the State Government in consultation with the Chairman of the District Planning Committee nominates persons who are residents of the area and have experience in district planning as" Special Invitees."
As per Para 7 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) Rules, 2018, there is a provision to constitute an" Executive Committee" under the chairmanship of the Chairman of the District Planning Committee to assist the District Planning Committees of all the districts of the state. Accordingly, the state government has constituted various district executive committees.
Since the appointments of" nominated members" and" special invitee members" on the district planning committees and executive committees of the district planning committee have been made until further orders and now it is proposed to renew these appointments, the government was considering to cancel the appointments of" nominated members" and" special invitee members" on the district planning committee and executive committee of all the districts made by the state government as per the provisions of the above Act and Rules.

Government's decision:
The appointments made by the State Government as Nominated Members and Special Invitees to all the District Planning Committees in the State under the provisions of Section 3 (3) (2) (b) and (c) and Section 3 (3) (4) (f) of the Maharashtra District Planning Committees (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, 2000 and as Nominated Members and Special Invitees to the Executive Committees of the District Planning Committees in the State under the provisions of Paragraph 7 (Rule 6-A of the principal Act) of the notification of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Conduct of Meetings) (Amendment) Rules, 2018 are hereby cancelled with immediate effect by this Government
2. All Collectors and Member Secretaries of District Planning Committees shall bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
