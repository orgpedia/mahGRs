Preamble: In order to effectively disseminate, disseminate and co-ordinate the Mudra Bank Scheme in rural, remote and extremely remote areas under the decision of the Government in the context of the Planning Department and to ensure that the benefit of the scheme reaches the needy, the meritorious and the unemployed, a Mudra Bank Scheme Co-ordination Committee has been constituted at the district level under the chairmanship of the Collector. As per the decision of the government, proposals are sought from the Mudra Bank Scheme District Coordination Committee regarding the expected expenditure and funds are provided to the districts as per the demand.
The Central Government, while presenting the Budget 2018-19, has allocated Rs. A target of Rs. 3 crore has been set. Against this backdrop, the enhanced target under Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana for the State for the financial year 2018-19 has also been achieved. In order to provide loans under the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana to the maximum number of needy people as per the increased target for the financial year 2018-19 by the nationalized and private banks and financial institutions in the state, it is necessary to publicize the scheme in all the districts of the state, especially in rural and remote areas. In view of the above, the matter of preparing a state-level plan for the promotion and publicity of the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Scheme and implementing it in the financial year 2018-19 through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations was under the consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: 1. Administrative approval is being given to take up the following works related to the promotion and publicity of the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Scheme at the state level through the Directorate General of Information and Public Relations.
