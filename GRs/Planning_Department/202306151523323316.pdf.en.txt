The proposal m. The Chief Minister has announced the formation of a separate" Konkan Area Development and Planning Authority" for the overall and inclusive development of the Konkan region. A high-level committee has been constituted by the government to finalise the modalities of the authority. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government decision The Chief Minister has announced the formation of a separate" Konkan Area Development and Planning Authority" for the overall and inclusive development of the Konkan region. The proposed planning authority will be responsible for the implementation and coordination of irrigation projects, infrastructure, tourism, orchards, fisheries and other ancillary services in the Konkan region. A High Level Committee is being constituted to decide the composition, nature and terms of reference of the said Authority.
