Government's decision:
To regulate and implement the works under the Pilgrim Development Plan for" Creation of Infrastructure in the area of Shri Kshetra Dehu, Alandi, Pandharpur Bhandara Hills, Palkhital, Nevasa,"
3 temporary posts created for the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Pune Division, Pune and Gajanan Maharaj Samadhi Shatabdi on the establishment of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics
A total of 9 temporary posts have been approved to be continued till 28th February, 2022 for the following 6 temporary posts created for the office of the Divisional Commissioner, Amravati Division, Amravati on the establishment of the Directorate of Finance and Statistics for the control and implementation of the works under the 2 Tirtha Kshetra Development Plan titled" Creation of Infrastructure in Shegaon Area on the Occasion of Mahotsav" and" Creation of Infrastructure at Vandania Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Mozari, District Amravati." Now, the Government is approving the continuation of these 9 temporary posts for the period from 1 st March, 2022 to 31 st August, 2022.
