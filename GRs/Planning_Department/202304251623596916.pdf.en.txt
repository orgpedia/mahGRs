Government's decision
In order to empower women SHGs and SCs/ STs in 125 backward taluks of 23 districts of the State, it is being approved to fix the ratio of public participation in the implementation of special schemes under district/ taluka specific schemes at 90:10 (State Government share and beneficiary group share respectively) for all sections/ beneficiary groups.
