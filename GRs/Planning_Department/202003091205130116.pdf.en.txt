GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Under the Planning Department, Government Decision No.NIVIA-1719/ PR.No. 137/ K.A.1421, dated 5th October, 2019, a total of 40 temporary posts indicated in the statement A, B, C, D, E, F and C accompanying this Government Decision on the establishment of the Planning Department (itself) were extended till 29.02.2020. A total of 40 posts indicated in the return are being approved to be continued from 1.03.2020 to 31.08.2020 and the expenditure on these 40 posts should be included under the head of accounts shown in the accompanying statement (Returns A to C) and should be met from the grant sanctioned under the head for the year 2020-2021.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments of the Ministry by the Department of Finance, Government Decision No. 2016/ No. 8/16/ APC, dated February 12, 2020.
