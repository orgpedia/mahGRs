GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the provisions of clause (3) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government is pleased to nominate 04 persons to the District Planning Committee of Palghar district as" Special Invitees" who are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and have experience in district planning as indicated in the attached statement A.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Palghar should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The nomination of special invitees to the Palghar District Planning Committee mentioned in the return" A" shall remain in force until further orders or until the cancellation of the said nomination by the Government, whichever is earlier.
