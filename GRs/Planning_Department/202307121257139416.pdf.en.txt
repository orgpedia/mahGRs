Government circular:
The e-governance policy of the Government of Maharashtra has been fixed under Reference No. 1 of the General Administration Department. The e-governance policy of the Government of Maharashtra aims to provide all government services to the general public through information technology in an effective, transparent and fast manner at affordable prices. Accordingly, greater transparency and ease of access to information on governance processes is the main focus of the state e-governance.
In order to make maximum use of e-governance by the Ministries/ Departments and their subordinate offices, Boards, Corporations, etc., to ensure that the facilities provided by the Government to the people are provided expeditiously, in a hassle-free and cost-effective manner, instructions have been issued under Government Resolution No. 2 to allocate half of the total allocation made by each Ministry/ Department for e-Governance under its schemes and schemes from 2010-11. The list of updated e-facilities being provided to the citizens by the Ministries/ Departments is given in Annexure A of the said Government Order. The list of Planning Department at Annexure-A No. 11 is as follows.
11. Development of Maharashtra Plan Schemes Information Management System (MP-SIMS)
Integration with various applications with State Data Bank and provision of BI Dashboards for policy makers
Expanding the role of MRSAC; training various officials across departments and districts in use of Maps; releasing many Maps and GIS data in public domain Web based application for MP/MLA funds.
