Government Decision: The outline of the posts on the establishment of the Planning Department has been approved under the Government Decision at Article No. 1. Accordingly, the Planning Department, Government Resolution, No. 1720/ Q. No. 80/ Ka. 1421 dated 15th March, 2022, a total of 40 temporary posts indicated in the statement" A,"" B,"" C,"" D,"" E,"" F" and" C" accompanying this Government decision on the establishment of the Planning Department (itself) were extended till 31.08.2022.
The total number of 40 posts shown in the return for the period from 01.09.2022 to 28.02.2023 is being approved under this Government Decision and the expenditure on these 40 posts should be included under the head of accounts shown in the accompanying return (Returns A to C) and should be met from the grants sanctioned under this head for the year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers delegated to the administrative departments of the Ministry by the Department of Finance, Government Decision, No. 2016/ Q. No. 8/16/ APC, dated 18 August 2022.
