The Proposal:
For effective implementation of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), the Government vide Decision No. 2017/ PR No. 30/ MGRRO-3, dated 11.09.2020, had extended a total of 1073 posts of Divisional Commissioners, Collectors, Zilla Parishads, Taluk level and Commissionerates of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Maharashtra from 01.09.2020 to 28.02.2021. The said 1073 posts are required for effective implementation of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. Therefore, a total of 1073 posts of Divisional Commissioner, District Collector, Zilla Parishad and Taluka level posts are being considered for extension from 01.03.2021.
Government's decision:
In accordance with the powers conferred on the administrative departments of the Ministry under the Government Decision No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 15.02.2021 of the Finance Department regarding the extension of the temporary posts in the offices under them, the Government has approved the extension of a total of 1073 posts under the Employment Guarantee Scheme from 01.03.2021 to 31.08.2021, including a total of 1052 posts in the offices of Divisional Commissioners, Collectors, Zilla Parishads and Taluk level offices and 21 posts created for the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme- Maharashtra, Nagpur Commissionerate. The details of the posts are given in Annexure No. are shown in Figures 1 to 5.
Vacancies of more than six months in the sanctioned posts will be excluded.
The Commissioner, Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme Maharashtra Nagpur, Divisional Commissioner (ROHYO), District Collector and District Programme Coordinator and CEO, Zilla Parishad and Joint District Programme Coordinator should review all the total posts in their establishment and submit the information to the Department before 31.08.2021. So that the revised figure may be approved by the High Level Committee before 30.09.2021.
