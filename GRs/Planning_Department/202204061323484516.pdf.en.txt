The main objective of the programme is to cross the State Average of" Key Performance Indicators" fixed by the Central Government in the respective districts in the next 1 year and to bring it at par with the National Average in 2 years.
Aspirational District Programme (ADP) includes 4 districts of Maharashtra Gadchiroli, Osmanabad, Washim and Nandurbar. Out of these, 5 districts namely 2 districts Gadchiroli and Nandurbar and 3 districts Hingoli, Yavatmal and Sindhudurg have been selected for the new programme" Mission for Upliftment of Selected Districts." These 5 districts have been selected for various programmes/ schemes under the Ministries/ Departments of the Government of India.
