Nomination as a special invitee to the Mumbai City District Planning Committee
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Planning
Government Resolution No: DAP-2023/ PR No.77/ KA-1481-A
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: December 26, 2023.
The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 2000.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the provisions of clause (3) of clause (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve to nominate 06 persons as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Mumbai City District, who are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and have experience in district planning, as indicated in the attached statement A.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Mumbai City should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The nomination of special invitees mentioned in the prospectus' A' to the Mumbai City District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such nomination is cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
