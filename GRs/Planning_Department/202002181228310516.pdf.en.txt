A total of 1073 posts at the Collector, Zilla Parishad, Taluka level and Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) Maharashtra Commissionerate were extended from 01.10.2019 to 29.02.2020. The said 1073 posts are required for effective implementation of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. Therefore, a total of 1073 posts of Divisional Commissioners, District Collectors, Zila Parishads and Taluk level posts are being considered for extension from 01.03.2020 onwards.
Government's decision:
As per the powers conferred on the administrative departments of the Ministry under the Government Decision No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 12.02.2020 of the Finance Department, a total of 1052 posts working in the offices of Divisional Commissioner, Collector, Zilla Parishad and Taluk level offices under the Employment Guarantee Scheme and 21 posts created for the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS)-Maharashtra, Nagpur Commissionerate, a total of 1073 posts have been approved for extension from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020. The details of the posts are given in Annexure No. are shown in Figures 1-5.
