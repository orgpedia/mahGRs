The Proposal: -
NITI Aayog is promoting economic growth for select sectors across the country through this concept called G-HUB.
A policy has been formulated. Accordingly, the Pune Metropolitan Region will prepare an Economic Master Plan and
Implementation of the Plan at various levels as per Government Decision dated 15th September, 2025.
Committees have been formed.
The Government's Decisions:
Pune Metropolitan Region to prepare and plan an economic macro-plan.
The date for implementation is as per the Government Decision dated 15th September, 2025. Presided over by the Chief Minister

The committee consists of A. No. 1 followed by A. No. 1 (a). The Minister of State (Urban Development) is being included.
