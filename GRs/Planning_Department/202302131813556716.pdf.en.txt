Government's stand:
" The officers who have been newly empowered to grant administrative sanction for works under MGNREGA under the Government Resolution dated 03.01.2023 are also being empowered to issue commissioning orders for the works accorded administrative sanction by them. Further, the NREGA Cell at the Panchayat Samiti will provide action/ assistance for issuing attendance sheets, taking measurements and making payments. "
The above paragraph should be read as follows:" The Deputy CEO (NREGA) shall make available to the concerned the services of the staff (Assistant Programme Officer, Panel Technical Assistant and Clerk cum Data Entry Operator) available in the NREGA Cell at Panchayat Samiti level for the purpose of issuing attendance sheets, payment of dues etc. in accordance with the administrative approval given by the officers who have been newly empowered to give administrative approval for works under MNREGA under the Government decision dated 03.01.2023. Further, no change has been made in the policy for determining the supplier and the matter will be as per the provisions of the earlier Government Decisions dated 14th February, 2022 and 15th March, 2022 and the letter issued by the Government on 6th April, 2022, according to which an amount of Rs. For works within the limit of 25 lakhs, supply orders should be issued at the level of the supplier by identifying them at the level of the Deputy Engineer. "
