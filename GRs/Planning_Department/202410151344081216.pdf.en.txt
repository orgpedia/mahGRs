The Government's Decisions:
Records and registers of the Mahatma Gandhi Employment Guarantee Scheme are maintained at the gram panchayat level. The responsibility of this work lies with the Gram Panchayat i.e. Sarpanch and Gram Sevak. The services of a Gram Rozgar Sevak are taken as assistants to assist the Gram Sevak in this work and to fill in computer information etc. However, it is the responsibility of the Gram Rozgar Sevak to assist and do the actual work.
The government was considering changing the designation of Gram Rozgar Sevak to Gram Rozgar Sevak as there was a demand from the organizations of the state regarding the change in the designation of Gram Rozgar Sevak.
Hence, the designation "Gram Rozgar Sevak" is being changed to "Gram Rozgar Sahayak." However, The guidelines shall continue to be in force without any change in the guidelines with respect to duties and responsibilities of the above-mentioned post and their appointment as per the Government Decision dated 2nd May, 2011.
