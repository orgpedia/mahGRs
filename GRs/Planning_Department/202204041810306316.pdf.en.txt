The Proposal:
The state government has decided to implement the Sindhuratna Samriddhi Yojana for the development of Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg districts. Paragraph (5.2) of the said Government Decision provides that two special invitees to the Sindhuratna Executive Committee shall be appointed by the Planning Department. As per the said provision, the proposal for appointment of two members as" Special Invitees" on the" Sindhuratna Executive Committee" was under consideration of the Government. Based on this, the following decisions are taken:
Government Decision: As per the provision of paragraph (5.2) of the Government Decision regarding the implementation of" Sindhuratna Samriddhi" Scheme, the following two" Special Invitees" are being appointed on the" Sindhuratna Executive Committee."
Shekhar Nikam, Member of Legislative Assembly, Chiplun, Ratnagiri
2 Shri Prafulla Sudrik, Shri. Patient Pride, Kudal, G. Sindhudurg
