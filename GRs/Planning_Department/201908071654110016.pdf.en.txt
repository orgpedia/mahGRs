Government's decision:
As per the provision of clause (3) (4) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government is approving the appointment of 9 persons as" special invitees" to the District Planning Committee of Gondia district, who are shown in the attached statement A, having experience in district planning and are ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Gondia should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The appointments made by the aforesaid Special Invitees to the District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
