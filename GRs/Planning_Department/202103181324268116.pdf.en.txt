Government Decision: To complete all the works sanctioned under the following development plans being implemented by the Planning Department for the development of pilgrimage sites and provision of basic civic amenities, the date is being extended till 31st March, 2022: 2.1" Development Plan for creation of infrastructure facilities in the Mozari area on the occasion of Birth Centenary Celebrations of Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj."
2.2 Development Plan for providing basic facilities by developing the Samadhi Sthal of Saint Gadge Baba at Valgaon, Taluka and District Amravati.
2.3 Development Plan for providing basic civic amenities in the areas of Sri Kshetra Kaundanyapur, Taluka Tivsa, District Amaravati.
2.4" Development Plan for creation of basic facilities for Shri Gajanan Maharaj Samadhi Centenary Mahotsav Shegaon Shrine."
2.5 Development Plan for Shri Kshetra Ghrishneshwar, Verul, Taluka Khultabad, District Aurangabad.
