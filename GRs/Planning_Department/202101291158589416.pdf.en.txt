The Proposal:
The subject of the company" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi)" Pune has been transferred to the Planning Department under the decision of the Government in the context of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department No-1. Also, under the order of the Planning Department at No. 2, the subject has been handed over to the 1425 office of the department. Accordingly, under the decision of the Government at Reference No. 3, approval has been given to open a new Account Head for the scheme" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi)" under Demand No. O-2, Head Account Head 2225. The Managing Director, Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune has been declared as the Controlling Officer. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to declare the Deputy Secretary of the Planning Department, under whom the subject is under, as the" Controlling Officer" at the Government level.
Government's decision:
