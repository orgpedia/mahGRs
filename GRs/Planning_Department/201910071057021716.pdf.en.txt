GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Under the Planning Department, Government Decision, No.Nivya-1719/ GOVERNMENT DECISION, No. 137/ 1421, dated 14th March, 2019, a total of 40 temporary posts indicated in the statement A, B, C, D, E, F and C accompanying this Government Decision on the establishment of the Planning Department (itself) were extended till 30.09.2019. The total number of 40 posts shown in the return for the period from 1.10.2019 to 29.02.2020 is being approved under this Government decision and the expenditure on these 40 posts should be included in the head of accounts shown in the accompanying statement (Returns A to C) and should be met from the grant sanctioned under the head for the year 2019-2020.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers vested in the Department of Finance, Government Decision, No. 2016/ PR No. 8/16/ APC, dated 11th September, 2019.
