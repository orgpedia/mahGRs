As per the" State Training Policy" issued on 23rd September, 2011, the training institute Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune has been declared as" Apex Training Institution" and all other training institutes in the state have been affiliated to it. The training programmes of the Divisional Administrative Training Institute at the headquarters level of the Revenue Department and the District Administrative Training Institute at the district level are being controlled by the Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune. Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune has been imparting training to district level officers through training institutes at all levels. In this backdrop, the conscious participation of the officers and employees of the administration in the state is essential to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) announced by the United Nations.
For this, training of government and semi-government officials/ employees is necessary. It is also expected to create awareness among the representatives of the local bodies. It also requires the participation of academicians, researchers and students at the university level. Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradhikaran Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune has submitted a fresh proposal to the Planning Department for the necessary training of all these stakeholders in a time-bound manner.
The Department of Planning and the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI), New Delhi have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for implementation of the Central Government Scheme" Support for Statistical Strengthening." The" Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)" has been signed on 16 th March, 2020. Accordingly, considering the proposal of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Prabodhini (YASHDA), Pune regarding" Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)," the Planning Department, Government Resolution, No. The" Sustainable Development Goals- Implementation and Coordination Centre" (SDG-ICC) was established on 03.12.2020. The Centre for Sustainable Development Goals (SDG-Centre) was set up under the Central Government Scheme of Support for Statistical Strengthening to implement the 5 Pillar Strategy of the Centre.
