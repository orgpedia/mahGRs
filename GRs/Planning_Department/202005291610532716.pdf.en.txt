Government circular:
The Finance Department vide Circular No.2 dated 16.04.2020 has approved the release of funds in the range of 15-25% of the total annual budget outlay for the first quarter of the financial year 2020-21, taking into account the situation arising out of the Corona virus outbreak in the State. Accordingly, under the District Annual Plan (General) for the year 2020-2021, a total of Rs. 9800.00 crore has been disbursed to all the concerned District Collectors as shown in the Statement attached with the Government Resolution dated 28.04.2020, referred as 1st instalment of 2020-21 under the head Revenue and Capital Accounts under Demand No. O-14 to O-85, amounting to Rs. 981.35 crore (Rupees Nine Hundred Eighty One Crore Thirty Five Lakh only).
2. From the funds disbursed on the Budget Distribution System under the District Annual Plan (General) 2020-2021, the Collector and the Controlling Officer are directed to take the following actions while spending the funds in this disbursement as the financial condition of the State is likely to be critical due to the emergency situation arising out of the virus" COVID 19."
(a) The original budgetary provisions made for the District Annual Plan (General) 2020-21 and the 33% revised provisions available are shown district-wise as per the demand number in the Annexure.
