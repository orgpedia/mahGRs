Regarding the appointment of nominated members and special invitees to the Akola District Planning Committee.
The Government of Maharashtra
the Department of Planning
Government Resolution No: DAP-2022/ P.No.74/ K-1481-A
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: December 2, 2022.
(1) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) Act, 1998 (2 of 1998) The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, 2000 (3 of 2000) Letter from the Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Akola No. 17.10.2022.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the provisions contained in sub-clauses (3) (2) (b) and (3) (f) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve the appointment of 2 members nominated from amongst the Members of Legislative Assembly/ Members of Parliament and 7 persons ordinarily resident in the area of the District Planning Committee and having experience in district planning as" Special Invitees" to the District Planning Committee for Akola District.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Akola should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
The appointments of the above-mentioned nominated members as well as special invitees to the Akola District Planning Committee shall remain in force until further orders or until such appointments are cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
