Preamble: 1. The company" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (SARATHI), Pune" has been registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 on 25th June, 2018.
2. A Sub-Centre was established at Kolhapur for timely and effective implementation of various welfare schemes for economic, social and educational development of Marathas, Kunbis, Kunbi Marathas and Maratha Kunbis through the company" Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune." For this, a proposal of 21 posts for the sub-station with the necessary officers and staff (8 regular posts and 13 manpower services to be taken by external agencies) was under the consideration of the government. A high-level committee for the Kolhapur Sub-Centre under the Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune, has approved the proposal. Approved in the meeting of 29/4/2022. Accordingly, the government was considering issuing orders for the creation of 8 regular posts for the Kolhapur Sub-Centre under the company' Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune' and 13 manpower services through external agencies.
GOVERNMENT DECISION 1. The following 8 regular posts are being sanctioned for Kolhapur Sub-Centre under Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Institute of Research, Training and Human Development (Sarathi), Pune and 13 manpower services are being taken through external agencies.
