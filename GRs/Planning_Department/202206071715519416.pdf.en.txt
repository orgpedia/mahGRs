Read: 1. Government Resolution No. 169/ Water No.1 of Soil and Water Conservation Department, 2011 26th April, 2022. Under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary. Minutes of the meeting held on 28/3/2022
Government Circular: The decision of the Department of Soil and Water Conservation. On 26 th April, 2022, under Jal Shakti Abhiyan- Catch the Rain, a total of Rs. from March 29, 2022. It has been decided to undertake various activities during the period from 30 th September, 2022. Plantation of trees in the area and efforts to build roof top rainwater harvesting structures have been included.
Also, 2. On 28 th March, 2022, under the Centrally Sponsored Jan Shakti Abhiyan (JAS) programme, the Government of India launched the Catch the Rain Campaign 2022 (29 th March to 30 th September). In the minutes of the meeting held under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, it has been directed that soak pits for rainwater harvesting should be constructed at appropriate places and other water conservation works should be taken up under the employment guarantee scheme. Accordingly, instructions are being issued to construct soak pits for rainwater harvesting in government buildings, schools, colleges, universities, educational institutions, primary health centres, anganwadis, etc. premises and wherever possible, water conservation works under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The District Collector, who is appointed as the chairman of the coordination and control committee formed under the government decision in connection with soil and water conservation, should plan the work in this regard.
