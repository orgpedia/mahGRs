The promotion is granted subject to the following conditions:
(1) The office of the Director of Finance and Statistics, Bombay shall be taken over by the said order and the certificate of transfer of charge for doing so shall be submitted to the Government and a copy thereof shall be forwarded to the Director, Directorate of Finance and Statistics, Bombay. In case of non-acceptance of promotion, the same should be informed to the government in writing.
(2) Ma. Special Leave Petition No. The said promotion is being granted only on temporary basis subject to the decision dated 28306/2017.
(3) Writ Petition Stamp No. 10876/2021 and Writ Petition Stamp No. 10878/2021. The said promotion shall be subject to the decision in the said writ petition as per the decision dated 25/5/2021 passed by the High Court, Bombay.
(4) The said temporary promotion shall be for a period of 11 months from the date of joining or order of promotion, whichever is earlier, of the outgoing senior officer on deputation.
(5) The temporary promotion of officers who have been temporarily promoted shall be terminated on the repatriation of the senior officer on deputation.
