The Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Functions) Act, 2000.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the provisions of clause (3) of section 3 of the Maharashtra District Planning Committee (Composition and Works) (Amendment) Act, No. 30/2000, the Government hereby approve to nominate 04 persons to the District Planning Committee of Dhule District as" Special Invitees" who are ordinarily resident in the area of District Planning Committee and have experience in district planning as indicated in the attached statement A.
District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Dhule should bring the above orders to the notice of all concerned.
(3) The nomination of special invitees to the Dhule District Planning Committee mentioned in the return" A" shall remain in force until further orders or until such nomination is cancelled by the Government, whichever is earlier.
