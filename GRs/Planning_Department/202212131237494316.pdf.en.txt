The components of the permissible innovation schemes include the inclusion of various innovative and start-up related schemes/ activities in the ambit of the mission-mode policy of the Department of Skill, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation. The decision has been taken in the meeting of the Empowered Committee chaired by the Chief Secretary dated July 12, 2022. Accordingly, the matter of inclusion of various innovative and start-up related schemes/ activities in the list of permissible innovative schemes in the Statement-1 along with Annexure-A of the Government Resolution dated 27th August, 2014 at Reference No. (1) of the Planning Department was under consideration of the Government. Government Supplementary Paper:

The committee is working for the development and dissemination of innovative ideas in the district.
