Preamble: As per the decision of the Government in the context of the Department of Planning, the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana Coordination Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of the Collector to effectively disseminate, disseminate and coordinate the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana among the masses in rural and remote areas so as to effectively disseminate information in rural, remote and extremely remote areas and to ensure that the benefits of this scheme reach the needy, the meritorious and the unemployed. In view of this decision, a proposal was sought from the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana District Coordination Committee regarding the necessary funds. Accordingly, for the financial year 2019-20, it was under consideration of the Government to provide 50% of the funds admissible to all 36 districts for the promotion, dissemination and coordination of Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana.
Government Decision: The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Bank Yojana Coordination Committee, constituted under the chairmanship of the Collector, has been mandated to promote, disseminate and coordinate the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana, subject to the guidelines in the relevant Government Decision, with a total outlay of Rs. 6,95,80, 000/- (Rupees Six Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Eighty Thousand only) is being provided.

The guidelines of the Government decision in the context of the Planning Department as well as the instructions given by the Finance Department in the relevant Government circular should be strictly followed while spending the funds according to the matter/ head as approved by the District Level Plan approved by the Coordination Committee chaired by the Collector.
The above funds should be placed under the control of the concerned District Collector. The Collector concerned will be the" Controlling Officer" and the District Planning Officer concerned will be the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to draw and disburse the funds from the Treasury Office. The District Collector will be responsible as the controlling officer to keep a tab on the expenditure of this fund and to inform the government about the expenditure from time to time.
The above amount is to be met from the grant-in-aid available for the financial year 2019-20 under the head Demand No.O-7,3451 Secretariat-Financial Services, 090, Secretariat (01) Planning Division, (01) (11) Promotion, Publicity and Co-ordination of Mudra Bank Scheme (Under Schemes) (Dattamat), 26 Advertisement and Publicity (3451A 001).
