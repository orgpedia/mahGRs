Preamble: The procedure for budgeting, disbursing and giving administrative approval to the works under the District Annual Plan (General) has been prescribed by the Government Decision dated 16th February, 2008 in the context of the Planning Department. Accordingly, administrative approval was accorded for the construction of District Planning Building, Nandurbar at a cost of Rs. 599.59 lakhs vide order dated 21/10/2016 in the presence of District Collector and Member Secretary, District Planning Committee, Nandurbar. Subsequently, due to delay in the period from 16/12/2017 to 17/10/2018 due to judicial process, increased prices of construction materials, GST rate hike of 12% and additional funds required for internal and external electrification, internal furniture, fire prevention measures and asphalt roads in the premises, a request for revised administrative approval for an amount of Rs. 953.75 lakhs has been made under Reference No. 3.
The proposal of Rs. 953.75 lakhs submitted for the construction of District Planning Building, Nandurbar was under consideration of the Government for" Revised Administrative Sanction."
Government's decision:
The Public Works Department after examining the revised administrative approval proposal of the District Planning Building, Nandurbar and as per the approval given in the meeting of the Expenditure Priority Committee held on 24/02/2022, the construction of the District Planning Building, Nandurbar

Revised Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an additional expenditure of Rs. 953.75 lakhs subject to the following conditions:
(i) There shall be no change in the resolution plan/ budget of the approved works.
2) The executing agencies shall start the work only after obtaining technical approval from the competent authorities 3) The prevailing government decisions, following the prescribed tendering procedures, fulfilling the directions and shortcomings given by the State Technical Advisory Committee in connection with the project and work, and the changes made from time to time will be binding on the executing agencies.
(4) The work shall be completed within the prescribed time limit.
5) Before starting the work, the work should be started by taking all the necessary permissions as per the government rules.
After the completion of the work, the savings from the sanctioned amount will not be diverted to other works or new works/ proposals will not be approved from the savings.
7) Funds under the District Annual Plan (General) cannot be sanctioned for the maintenance, repair and electricity payment of the Planning Building.
