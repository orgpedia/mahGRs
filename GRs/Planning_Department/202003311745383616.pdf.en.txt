The Proposal:
The Maharashtra government has proposed to provide special funds to Vidarbha, Marathwada and the rest of the Maharashtra Development Board for the financial year 2019-20. As per the directions of the Governor, Rs. 50.00 crore. 150.00 crore for the financial year 2019-20. The planning department here has been taken under the Government Decision No. 2018/ PR No. 33/ K. 1416, dated July 23, 2019. In accordance with the decision of the present government and with the" administrative approval" given by the concerned collector, the matter of distributing funds to the selected talukas under the" Human Development Program" was under the consideration of the government.

The funds are to be used as supplementary resources for the district/ taluka specific schemes under the" Human Development Programme" and the Commissioner, Human Development, Aurangabad and the District Collectors, Yavatmal, Nagpur, Gondia and Chandrapur shall strictly follow the guidelines of the relevant Government decision while spending the funds.
