Proposal: As per the Planning Department, Government Decision No. ROC 2012 / Q. 142 / JAL-7 dated 10th December 2012, to increase the availability of irrigation through watershed and water conservation of WRI based dryland agriculture in the state, a scheme for taking up agriculture on subsidized basis on the lines of Individual Beneficiary Scheme (on the lines of Jawahar Well and Horticulture Scheme) under MGRROHYO was under consideration. Under the scheme, the government has set a target of 30400 farmlands per year in the entire state (excluding Mumbai city and suburban districts) for the year 2009-10, with 200 farmlands per taluk per year in the state. The plan was thus planned for the next five years. The government was considering fixing the terms, conditions and guidelines of the scheme. Under the said Government decision, the Government is now laying down the terms, conditions and guidelines of the scheme.
