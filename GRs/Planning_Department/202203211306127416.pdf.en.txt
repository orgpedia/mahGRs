Government's decision:
The outline of the posts on the establishment of the Planning Department has been approved by the government decision in Article No. 1. Accordingly, the Planning Department, Government Resolution, No. NIVIA-1720/ PR No. 80/ Ka. 1421 dated 8th September, 2021, a total of 40 temporary posts indicated in the statement" A,"" B,"" C,"" D,"" E,"" F" and" C" accompanying this Government decision on the establishment of the Planning Department (itself) were extended till 28.02.2022. A total of 40 posts indicated in the return are being approved to be continued from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.22 and the expenditure on these 40 posts should be included under the head of accounts shown in the accompanying statement (Returns A to C) and should be met from the grants sanctioned under this head for the year 2022-2023.
This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers delegated to the administrative departments of the Ministry by the Department of Finance, Government Decision, No. 2016/ Q. No. 8/16/ APC, dated February 22, 2022.
