The government has taken a decision to plant trees on the farm lands of farmers and on the farm lands of individual beneficiaries under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS). It was approved on April 12, 2018. The government was considering some amendments to the bill. The following improvements are being made:
2. Guidelines in the Government Resolution No. 2.1 out of 2 lists the tree species to be planted on the bund/ farm of the farmers. In the list, serial number 20 mentions" cashew (Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg)." The reference to Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg has been omitted. The serial number is 31) Moliya Dubia, followed by" 32) Kokum 33) Karwand 34) Tuti (Mulberry)."
Further, three species namely Kokum, Karavand and Mulberry (Mulberry) have been included in the Sample No. It is being done in 01.
