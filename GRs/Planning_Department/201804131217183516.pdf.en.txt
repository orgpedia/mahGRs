The Proposal:
Under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), the Forest Department was empowered to issue, fill, pass attendance sheets and make payment for skilled/ unskilled aspects of works to be carried out by the Forest Department under the scheme till 31st March, 2018. The government was considering extending the deadline.
Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government's decision:
The Forest Department has been given the power to issue, fill, pass attendance sheets and make payment for skilled/ unskilled aspects of works such as plantation, afforestation works, soil and water conservation works, etc. to be carried out by the Forest Department under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. The deadline has been extended to March 31, 2020. The guidelines in this regard have been issued vide Government Resolution No.Magro-2016/ No. 102/ Magro-1, D. It will be on September 29, 2016.
