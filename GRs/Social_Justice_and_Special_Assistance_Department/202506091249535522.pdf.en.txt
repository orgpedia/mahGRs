The Proposal: -
The Government Hostels Scheme is implemented for the education of backward class boys and girls under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Under the scheme, a total of 443 government hostels are functioning in the state. 230 government hostels for children are functioning in these government hostels with 23,208 students enrolled. A total of 43,858 students, including 20,650 girls, are availing the benefits of the government hostel scheme, while 213 girls hostels are functioning. Facilities such as accommodation, breakfast, two meals, educational materials, school uniforms, subsistence allowance for daily expenses, additional allowance for hygiene and grooming for girls are provided to the students admitted to the government hostels under the department. Accordingly, the Government was considering the purchase of iron coats, office tables, computers, water purifiers, dining tables, wooden tables for students, wooden chairs with hands, fire extinguishers, CCTV related materials, etc., required to provide necessary facilities to the admitted students of the newly opened Government Hostel for 1000 Children, Chembur, Mumbai.
