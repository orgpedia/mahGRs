Prateek Industrial Cooperative Societies, Limited under the Scheme for Grant of Financial Assistance to Scheduled Castes Cooperative Societies. Andalgaon, Ta. Shirur, District Pune, approved the first tranche of Government shareholding amounting to Rs. 50.00 Lakh and long-term loan amounting to Rs. 50.00 Lakh amounting to a total amount of Rs. 100.00 Lakh. 2 has been approved by the Government Decision.
The organization spent the amount on the matters mentioned in the lifting order. The utility certificate of the Collector, Pune has been submitted. Accordingly, the matter of sanctioning the second / final instalment to the institution was under consideration of the Government.
The government's decision:
Prateek Industrial Co-operative Society, Limit. Andalgaon, Ta. 3. Mother of the institution. It should be ensured that the petition is not filed in the court.
4. Field level officers will conduct physical site inspection of the project before disbursement of funds to these institutions. The funds should be disbursed to these institutions only after the submission of the site report.
The process of determining the interest rate on government loans provided under the scheme is underway at the government level. The interest rate determined by the government will be the interest rate applicable to the loan.
6. Repayment of government loans to these institutions should be initiated after two years from the payment of 70 of the project value due to the institution. The repayment period of the loan will be 6 years. The loan (principal) should be paid in 16 equal quarterly installments within 4 years and then the interest should be recovered in 8 equal quarterly installments in two years.
The amount of equity sanctioned under the scheme is required to be returned by the institution to the government in 10 equal installments within 10 years from the date of completion of the project.
8. The Controller shall spend the funds specified in the aforesaid statement. Once the funds are spent, the statement and utilization certificate should be submitted to the government without fail.
9. The Commissioner (Social Welfare) shall, after drawing up the aforesaid funds, subordinate them. The funds should be disbursed only after the prescribed conditions and defects are met.
10. Commissioner Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the purpose. They are also being empowered to sign copies of the payment to them or to the officer authorised by them.
