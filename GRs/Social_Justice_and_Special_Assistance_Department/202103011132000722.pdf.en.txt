Introduction: Under the Scheme of Scholarship to the Children of Parents working in Unhygienic Occupations, the scholarship is provided to the students whose parents work in unclean occupations. Earlier, the scheme was being implemented with 50% Central share and 50% State share as per the guidelines issued by the Central Government dated 30th October, 2003. Subsequently, the Central Government revised the guidelines of the scheme vide letter dated 7th May, 2013. Under this scheme, 100% of the expenditure on the state's committed expenditure is received by the state government from the central government. Under this scheme, the expenditure of the last year of the previous five-year plan period was fixed by the central government as a binding obligation. But in the new guidelines of the Central Government dated 02.04.2018, the scheme was changed.
