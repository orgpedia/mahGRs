Government's decision
Under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, Shirsagaon Band Mauje Jamapur Taluka in Amravati district. A list of 54 individual house site beneficiaries of Achalpur has been submitted to the Government under Reference No. 5. Therefore, in pursuance of the said proposal, it is ordered to inform that in the year 2021-22, in pursuance of the amendments made under the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme for the sections of the community belonging to the Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes under the Government Decision dated 24/1/2018 in Reference No. 1 and the Government Papers dated 8/1/2019, 8/3/2019 and 11/9/2019, the Government of Maharashtra has decided to extend the benefit of the scheme to the people of the State of Andhra Pradesh. A total of 54 individual houses at Achalpur have been constructed for 5 colonies for eligible beneficiaries (mentioned in Annexure A attached) at a cost of Rs. 02,39,27, 400/- for purchase of private land and Rs. 28.00 lakh for construction of 05 colonies for 54 beneficiaries.
