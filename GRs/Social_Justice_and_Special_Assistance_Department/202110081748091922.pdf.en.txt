Preamble: To develop the habitations/ villages of the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist communities in the urban and rural areas of the State and to provide basic amenities to these communities. The Government has decided to implement Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana at the State level from the year 2018-19 to provide recognition to the works suggested by the people's representatives at the government level. The people's representatives and local body officials are being repeatedly asked to amend this government decision. Also, in view of the outbreak of COVID-19, the Government was considering to amend the Government Resolution dated 09.03.2018 to implement the said scheme effectively.
Government's decision:
The Government decision dated 09.03.2018 regarding introduction of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme is being amended as under:
Representation of the people:" Only the proposal of the current MP, MLA will be considered as a representative for this scheme" instead of" The proposal of the MP, MLA, all the members and office bearers of the local body as a representative for this scheme will be considered. General Terms and Conditions: 2. The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance shall have the power to modify works sanctioned at the Government level in exceptional circumstances. Instead, the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance will take necessary action on the written request of the people's representative who has suggested that the work be changed in exceptional circumstances in the work approved at the government level:

The government's decision also includes the following:
Considering the current outbreak of COVID-19 and the limited funds available with the government, the works suggested by the people's representatives under this scheme should be approved in principle at the government level and the administrative and technical approval of the competent authority should be taken for these works.
All the work related to technical inspection, measurement of work can be done by the concerned administrative department through the same agency/ system through which the concerned administrative department wants to do it. In case of any problem, the responsibility will be of the concerned agency/ agency.
After the completion of the said works, the executing agency/ machinery of the concerned administrative department shall submit the payment to the concerned Assistant Commissioner. After receipt of payment for the said work, the Commissioner shall, in any case, demand the funds required for the same within eight days.
After the completion of the work of the scheme from each district, the Assistant Commissioner will receive the demand for providing funds from the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, the Commissioner, Social Welfare Office will take a first record of the payment in a separate register. After that, the districts that have received the payments first. Those districts will be provided funds for the respective district as per this principle and availability of funds. This action must be taken within ten days of receipt of payment.
The Commissioner of Social Welfare shall, after receipt of the funds from the Assistant Commissioner of the concerned district, disburse the same to the agency/ agency concerned within eight days.
Disciplinary action will be taken against all the concerned officers if the proceedings are not completed within the time prescribed by the government in this process or if there is a deliberate delay.
