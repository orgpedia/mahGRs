Preamble: Maintenance of Buildings and Roads Under the heading 2225 F488, repairs are carried out in the hostels under the Department of Social Justice. These repairs were being included in the Public Works Department's" Part-II Expenditure Detailed Budget Estimates (White Book)" publication under heading 42250318 for the year 2019-20. However, the reference. 25/2/2020 These works have been included in the publication" Detailed Budget Estimates of Part-II Expenditure" of the Department of Social Justice under the heading" Maintenance of Buildings and Roads, Accounts 2225 F488" from the financial year 2020-21. Accordingly, the matter of declaring Controller and Drawing and Disbursing Officer under this head was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the heading 2225 F488, the repair of hostels under the Social Justice Department is carried out. For this purpose, Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune has been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department as Controlling Officer.
