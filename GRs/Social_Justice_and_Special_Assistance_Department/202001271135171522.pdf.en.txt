the prelude
There are a total of 54 recognized social work colleges by the Social Justice Department in the state of Maharashtra, out of which 1 is closed, 3 unaided and 50 aided colleges are working at present.
The department receives various demands, problems and other problems from the teachers and non-teaching staff of this social work college.
However, in order to expedite the resolution of these demands, problems and ancillary matters, the government was considering the formation of a committee under the chairmanship of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune.
Government's decision
In order to resolve the demands of the teachers and non-teaching staff of the Social Work College under the Department of Social Justice and Welfare, the following committee is being formed under the chairmanship of the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune.

The functions of the Committee
(a) To give advice in respect of redressal of demands/ problems relating to the Social Work School/ staff under the Department of Social Justice.
(b) to make recommendations as to the changes, if any, in the existing policy of the State Government.

(a) The Committee shall make a comparative study of various Acts/ Rules/ Government Decisions/ Codes/ Circulars, UGC Guidelines, Standard Operating Procedures etc. applicable to the colleges of the Department of Higher and Technical Education and recommend the implementation of the above Acts, Rules, Government Decisions, Codes/ Standard Operating Procedures, Circulars including customization to the colleges of the Department of Social Work in this department and mention the same in the report.
(d) To study/ learn about the functioning of Social Work Colleges in other States and to prepare an action plan to devise suitable policy for the change.
(e) to ensure that the orders issued by the State Government from time to time are properly implemented;
(e) The Committee shall study the issues/ issues related to the College/ College of Social Work and submit a complete report to the Government within three months.
(a) The Committee shall perform such other duties in relation to the College of Social Work as may be specified by the State Government.
(3) The meeting of the Committee shall be held at such place as the Chairman of the Committee may indicate.
(4) The decision as to whether or not to accept the recommendations of the said Committee shall be entirely that of the Government.
The Committee shall meet at least once a month as required. The allowances admissible to non-official members for this meeting shall be applicable as per rules.
