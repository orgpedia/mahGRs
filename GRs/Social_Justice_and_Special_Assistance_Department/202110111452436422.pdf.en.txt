Government Decision- Grama Bharathi Education Society The District Social Welfare Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed, inspected the school, Pujya Babasaheb Paranjape Residential Disabled School, Ambajogai, G. Beed.
Grama Bharati School. In Pujya Babasaheb Paranjape Residential Disabled School, Ambajogai, G. Beed, there is no competent executive and there is a lot of internal dispute. The institution cannot run the school. Apart from the educational, social and professional rehabilitation of the disabled students, there are serious disputes of the institution's management and staff creating differences among themselves, quarreling, criminal proceedings against each other. Similarly, the purpose of the special schools for the disabled, which were given to the said institution to run activities in the disabled area, was not being achieved, the investigation has found. Also, the dispute over the functioning of the institution is currently sub judice with the Charity Commissioner.
(2) The Government shall take a decision regarding the appointment of administrators for the management of special schools/ workshops for the disabled run by NGOs. (a) until the management of such institution takes over and improves the functioning of the school, until it is satisfied that the management is competent to provide facilities to the students with disabilities as per the provisions of the Persons with Disabilities Act, 1995, as amended from time to time; or

Provided that the period of office of the Administrator shall not exceed three years, until the differences in management are resolved, whichever is earlier, the Government shall have the power to appoint Administrators to the institution.
3. Therefore, as per the recommendation of the Commissioner, Disabled Welfare, Pune, the District Social Welfare Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed has been appointed as the Administrator of the said institution. He was appointed on 29.05.2018 for a period of 3 years. The term of the said Administrator is being extended for a further period of 3 years in accordance with the decision of this Government.
In the meantime, if the government receives a report from the Commissioner, Disabled Welfare, Pune, regarding the smooth functioning of the institution, an appropriate decision will be taken regarding the administrator appointed on the said school.
Commissioner, Disabled Welfare, Pune and Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed and District Social Welfare Officer, Zilla Parishad, Beed should take appropriate action so that the day-to-day functioning of the school is carried out smoothly.
