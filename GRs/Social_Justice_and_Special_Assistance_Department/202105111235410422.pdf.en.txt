The Proposal:
The Department of Social Justice has been mandated to study the social, educational, economic, cultural and political status of the Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes and suggest measures to the Government. The Maharashtra State Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission was constituted by the Government in its decision dated 01/03/2005. The term of office of the 27 non-permanent members of the Commission was to expire on February 28, 2021. Accordingly, 27 non-permanent posts of the Commission have also been given extension from time to time as per the decision of the Government in this regard. These 27 temporary posts were extended from 1st September, 2020 to 28th February, 2021 as per the Government decision in Reference-12. It was also under the consideration of the government to extend the date from March 1, 2021 to August 31, 2021.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the Government Decision dated 2nd December, 2006, out of the 53 sanctioned posts of officers/ staff for the State Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes Commission, 27 posts indicated in the prospectus are to be filled up in the first phase.
