Government's decision:
In the financial year 2021-22, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 45,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Five Crores only) has been approved. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 22,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Two Crores Fifty Lakhs only) has been released by the Finance Department. Accordingly, for the Centrally Sponsored Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme, the amount sanctioned for the period April, 2022 to October, 2022 is Rs. 22,50,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Two Crores Fifty Lakhs only) has been disbursed to all the District Collectors on Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) System as per the statement attached under the Government Decision.
All the Collectors are informed that the funds have been disbursed to the respective districts in the accompanying returns on PFMS system. The subsidy disbursed by them should be distributed to the talukas of their districts on the PFMS system in proportion to the beneficiaries. The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
The expenditure from the said fund should be spent under the head" Demand No.N-3, Main Account Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 104 Welfare of Elderly, Disabled and Destitute Persons, (08) (11) Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (Centrally Sponsored Scheme) (Programme), 50 Other Expenditure (2235B 031)."
