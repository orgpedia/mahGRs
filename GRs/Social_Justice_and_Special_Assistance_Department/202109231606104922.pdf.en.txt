In order to ensure that the transfer order is implemented promptly and efficiently, the following instructions shall be strictly followed:- (1) On receipt of the transfer order, the Commissioner Social Welfare, Pune shall relieve the transferred officer forthwith to join the transferred post.
2) When the transferred officer comes to join after being relieved from the office concerned, he should be recruited without delay. Establishment officers concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of an officer by fixing a mandatory waiting period.
(3) If the officer concerned directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the officer to change the transfer order, such officer shall be liable for disciplinary action.
