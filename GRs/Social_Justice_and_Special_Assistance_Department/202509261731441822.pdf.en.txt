Preamble: - Under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, the backward class boys and girls should be promoted educationally, socially and economically and should lead a life at par with other sections of the society. Also, Government Hostels under the Department is implemented for the education of backward class boys and girls with the objective of providing all kinds of education and higher education opportunities. A total of 441 government hostels are functioning in the state under the scheme, out of which 230 are for boys and 2119 are for girls. The hostels have an intake capacity of 20,320 girls and 23,570 boys. Government hostels under the department thus have a total intake capacity of 43,890 students. Shapkop SSAA provides accommodation for students, breakfast, two square meals, subsistence allowance for daily expenses for students, educational materials, additional allowance for hygiene and grooming for girls, school uniforms, Aaa materials, raincoats, Wa, painting for students of arts stream, educational tours, workshops, allowance for amusement / entertainment arrangements, money for purchase of crudas, colour TV for each government hostel, Inquired and Botter coolers, fire fighting facilities, etc. Reference No. The Government Decision No. 2 provides that the rates of the facilities shall be revised every five years. However, there was no increase in these allowances even after a long time. Accordingly, the facilities provided to the students have been increased. The government is being constantly followed up by public representatives, parents, student organisations. Accordingly, the government was considering allowing an increase in the rates of subsistence allowances to be paid to students admitted to government hostels under the department, taking into account the inflation index.
