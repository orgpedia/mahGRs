As per the Government decision dated 06.09.2019, 1) To provide financial relief to the youth of Dhangar community belonging to nomadic tribe-C category in the fee for competitive examination. 2) To provide residential coaching to the students of this category for the examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission/ Maharashtra Lok Seva Commission. 3) The government has approved three schemes for the Dhangar community to recruit unemployed youth in the army and provide necessary training for the recruitment of police in the state. A new budget has been proposed for 2020-21. The scheme is to be implemented through Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti), Nagpur. The matter of transferring the provision of all these schemes to Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti), Nagpur and making the same available through the Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Nagpur was under the consideration of the Government.
