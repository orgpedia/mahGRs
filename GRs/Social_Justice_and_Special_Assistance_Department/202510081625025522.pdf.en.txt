The Government's Decisions:
The Government hereby approves the continuation of a total of 1422 temporary posts from 01/09/2025 to 28/02/2026 subject to the condition that none of the temporary posts at the establishment of the Collector in the 35 districts in the accompanying prospectus for implementation of the Sanjay Gandhi Nirbhadra Grants Scheme and other special assistance scheme is lying vacant for more than six months. (District-wise returns are attached)
The expenditure for this matter shall be met from the provision approved for that financial year under the head Demand No. N-2, Head of Accounts-2053, District Administration (093) District Establishment (01) General Establishment.
3. This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the authorities received by the Administrative Division under the Government Decision No. 1 with respect to the Finance Department.
