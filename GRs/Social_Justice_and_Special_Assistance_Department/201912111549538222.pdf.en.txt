GOVERNMENT DECISION-: In pursuance of the proposal submitted under the letter of reference No. 5 for 2039 individual household beneficiaries in Yavatmal district under the Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme, it is ordered to inform that in pursuance of the amendments made under the Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme for the sections of the community belonging to the Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes under the Government Decision dated 24/1/2018 and the Government Papers dated 8/1/2019, 8/3/2019 and 11/9/2019 in Reference No. 1 for the year 2019-20, out of the proposals submitted by 2039 individual household beneficiaries in Yavatmal, only Rs. 1.00 crore has been released.
The benefit of the said scheme will be admissible by selecting the beneficiaries according to the criteria and priority order from the beneficiaries qualified and finalized by the Yavatmal District Level Committee within the limit of Rs. 1.00 crore only.
