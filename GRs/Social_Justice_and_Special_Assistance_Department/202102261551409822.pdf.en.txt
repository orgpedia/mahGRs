Chapter No. 3, Rule No. 3.5 of the Ashramshala Code for Primary, Secondary and Higher Secondary Ashramshalas, Vidyaniketans and Schools for the Children of Sugarcane Workers belonging to the Deprived Castes, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes.
The Proposal:
For the primary, secondary and higher secondary ashramshalas, vidyaniketans and schools for the children and daughters of sugarcane workers, run by NGOs, the government was considering adding by-laws v) then by-laws vi) to Rule No. 3.5 of Chapter III of the Ashramshala Code. Accordingly, the Government are approving the addition of Bye-Laws v) and vi) as follows:
Government's decision:
On the other hand, if the recognition of any school run by the voluntary organizations under the Bahujan Welfare Department for the primary, secondary and higher secondary ashramshalas, vidyaniketans and children of sugarcane workers and sugarcane workers of the Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes is withdrawn or cancelled, then it will be given to that institution or any ashramshala in the future.
