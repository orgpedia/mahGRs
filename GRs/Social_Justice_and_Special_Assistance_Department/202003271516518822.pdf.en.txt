The Proposal:
To develop the habitations/ villages of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist communities in urban and rural areas of the State and to provide basic amenities to these communities. The Government has decided to implement the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme from the year 2018-19 to give recognition to the works suggested by the people's representatives at the government level. According to The matter of sanctioning works in the habitations of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist communities in urban and rural areas suggested by the people's representatives (MLAs/ MPs) was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
In order to provide basic amenities in the habitations of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhists in the urban and rural areas of the State, the Government has approved the works mentioned in Annexure A along with this decision in the budget provision for the year 2019-20 under the' Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana' and administrative approval is being granted for an amount of Rs. 26732.18 lakh. A sum of Rs. 10692.87 lakh is being sanctioned for the year 2019-20 as 40% of the total cost of the project.
