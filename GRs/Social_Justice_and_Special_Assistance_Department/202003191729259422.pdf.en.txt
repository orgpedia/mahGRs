Introduction: The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance has been mandated to study the social, educational, economic, cultural and political status of the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes and suggest measures to the Government. The Maharashtra Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission was constituted under the Government Resolution dated 1/3/2005. The term of the Commission along with the Chairperson was to expire on 30th June, 2009. However, the Commission has to submit a report to the government. The deadline has been extended by the government. Accordingly, the terms of reference of the Commission have also been extended from time to time. The period of extension of these temporary posts was from 1st October, 2019 to 29th February, 2020. Also, the matter of extending the period from March 1, 2020 to August 31, 2020 was under consideration of the government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 2nd December, 2006, approval has been accorded for filling up of 27 posts in the first phase out of the sanctioned 53 posts of officers/ staff for the State Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes Commissions. A total of 27 vacancies have been announced. 01/10/2019 to D. 29/02/2020 or until further orders of the Government whichever is earlier. The decision was approved by the government. Now, the 27 temporary posts of the Commission are being filled up vide Reference No. 10 the government's decision. 01/03/2020 to dt. It is hereby approved to continue till 31/08/2020 or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
