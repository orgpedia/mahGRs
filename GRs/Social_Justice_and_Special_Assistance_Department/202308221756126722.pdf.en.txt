Letter from the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune No. SAKA/ EDUCATION/ 1000 Capacity Children's Shrine-Aubad/ Naming of Dr. B.A./ 2022-23/ Ka-5A/ Pune dated 13.3.2023
Preamble:- The scheme of Government Hostel is implemented in the state for the convenience of education of children of backward classes under the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department. Under the scheme of Government Hostels, under the Government decision dated 28th June, 2007 at Reference No.1, a total of 7 new Government Hostels with a capacity of 1000 students (4 units each with a capacity of 250 students) each have been approved to be opened at Divisional Headquarters at Mumbai, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nagpur and Latur. In Aurangabad, a government hostel for backward class children with a capacity of 1000 students at the divisional level has been started and the matter of nominating the government hostel was under the consideration of the government. The government has taken the following decisions:
Government Decision:- The Government Hostel for Backward Class Children at the divisional level in Aurangabad under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Kileark, Aurangabad is being nominated as" Mahamanav Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Government Hostel for 1000 Children, Kileark, Aurangabad."
Necessary action should be taken by the Commissioner, Social Welfare Commissionerate, Pune.
