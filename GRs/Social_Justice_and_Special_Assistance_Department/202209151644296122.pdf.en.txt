Proposal- Department of Finance, Government of India. As on 14.01.2016, the Administrative Departments have been directed to take action for abolition of 25% of sanctioned posts in Group-D cadre. Accordingly, a total of 2186 posts have been sanctioned in Group-D under the Commissionerate of Social Welfare. As much as 25 per cent of these posts, 546 posts need to be abolished. Accordingly, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune vide Reference No. As per letter No. 2, a proposal has been submitted to abolish 25 per cent of the sanctioned posts out of the vacant posts in Group-D. Finance Department, Govt. dated 14.01.2016. As per the provisions of Section 5 of the Act, the Government was contemplating to abolish 546 posts in Group-D of Government Hostels under the Commissionerate of Social Welfare.
Government Decision: As per the decision of the Government regarding the creation and classification of posts in the subordinate office of the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, a total of 2186 posts have been sanctioned in Group-D cadre. Out of these, 25% posts i. e. total 546 posts are being abolished as mentioned in the attached prospectus.
2.If the employee is working in the post that has been abolished, he should be transferred to an equivalent post in that district or department as per the rules. Also, to exclude the abolished post from the service system, the detailed information of the post should be submitted to the government by all the office heads through the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune immediately. The Commissioner, Social Welfare, should follow up with all the Assistant Commissioners, Social Welfare Office in this regard and the proposal to extend the temporary posts should not include the abolished posts.
