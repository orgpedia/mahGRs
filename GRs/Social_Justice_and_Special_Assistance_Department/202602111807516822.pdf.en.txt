Preamble: - To facilitate the further education of the children of sugarcane farmers by increasing their faith in education, the Government vide the relevant Decision No. Under Section 1, "Sant Bhagwan Baba Sarkar Ko Hostel Scheme" has been started for the sons and daughters of migrant sugarcane farmers. Under the scheme, a total of 82 (YUU student capacity each) government hostels for boys and 41 for girls have been approved to be set up at 41 taluks in the state with a high concentration of sugarcane workers. Accordingly, a total of 20 government hostels, 10 for boys and 10 for girls, have been approved to be set up in the first phase. The 82 Government TAPS approved under the scheme are in the form of Government Decision No. 1 regarding opening of 62 Government hostels except 20 MAHA hostels which have been started in the first phase. 2 has been approved.
Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. 15.06.2021 and Government Decisions. Under the "Sant Bhagwan Baba Sarkoi Hostel Scheme" for the sons and daughters of migrant sugarcane harvesters under the 10.01.2024 scheme, a total of 22 government hostels with 02 (1 boys / 1 girls) (capacity of 100 students each) have been sanctioned in each taluka of Beed district.
All these government hostels are to be started in the government building. At present, only 3 MAHA seats are available and construction of hostel is in progress at those places. Meanwhile, the government was considering giving administrative approval to the construction budget of 191 available hostels out of the remaining 19 hostels.
