The Proposal: -
Government residential schools have been started for the education of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist boys and girls under the Social Justice Department. The students enrolled in this Government Residential School are provided with facilities such as accommodation, food arrangements, educational materials, subsistence allowance for daily expenses, etc. Accordingly, the Government Residential School for Scheduled Caste and neo-Buddhist children under the department was established. The government was considering procuring necessary items like sheets, bedsheets, blankets, pillows, pillow covers and mattresses for the admitted students of Man G Satara.
