The Proposal:
The government has proposed to implement the Public Financial Management System (PFMS). Babu Jagjivan Ram Hostel Scheme is implemented by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India. The scheme has been implemented for the Scheduled Castes girls from the 3rd Five Year Plan (1961-66) and for the Scheduled Castes boys from 1989-90. Under the scheme, Central grant is provided in the ratio of 90:10 (Central Government: State Government: Non-Governmental Organizations) for construction of new Government/ Aided Hostels for girls and 45:45:10 (Central Government: State Government: Non-Government Organizations) for construction of new Government/ Aided Hostels for boys under the Babu Jagjivan Ram Hostel Scheme. The government was also considering the appointment of a single nodal agency (SNA) and a nodal officer for the scheme.

For the implementation and coordination of the scheme for Babu Jagjivan Ram Hostel Scheme, Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Construction), Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Ministry, Mumbai is being appointed as the single nodal agency (SNA) and Joint Commissioner (Education) at the level of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune as the nodal officer and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare at the district level as the nodal officer.
As the concerned Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare (District Level) is implementing the scheme directly, he is being designated as the Implementing Agency (IA). The Finance Department has also taken a decision. As on 13.3.2020, the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare (concerned district) should open a separate bank account with Zero Balance Subsidiary Accounts at their level.
For this, you have to open an account in Bank of Maharashtra.
