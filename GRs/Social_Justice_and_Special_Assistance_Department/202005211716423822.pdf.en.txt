GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: PRAGAYO-2019/ P.K.103/ AJAK, dated 26th August, 2019.
Preamble: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India has implemented the Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAY) on a pilot basis since 2009-10. Under this scheme, works of basic amenities like water supply, roads, etc. and other activities in the villages with more than 50% of the population of the Scheduled Castes in the state and in such a state are approved in convergence with other schemes of the state government. The main objective of this scheme is to implement the welfare schemes implemented by the various departments of the state government in the villages and to develop these villages in a holistic manner. The main objective of this scheme is to provide basic facilities to the people of the village and to improve the living standards of all the citizens.
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment of the Central Government has issued detailed guidelines for the implementation of this scheme. In order to implement this scheme effectively in the state of Maharashtra, the formation of committees at various levels and detailed guidelines to the field officers have been issued under the above Government Decision, dated 26th August, 2019. Accordingly, the matter of providing administrative approval and accounting title to the scheme was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Union Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment is providing administrative approval and accounting for the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAY) in the State of Maharashtra, which was implemented on a pilot basis to the States since 2009-10. This scheme should be effectively implemented in the state as per the guidelines of the above government decision, dated August 26, 2019.
The expenditure on this scheme should be spent under the following heads: Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities.
1, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 789, Scheduled Castes Sub-Plan (08), Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana.
