Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government Order No. 2023/ No. 68/ Aastha-2, Dy. 29.05.2023 Government Order Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune issued against the Assistant Commissioner on Establishment, Social Welfare and similar, Mrs. Sangita Dawkhar of Group-A cadre, Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune. The request was made vide letter dated 29.03.2023. Accordingly, the Government has issued orders for the transfer of Mrs. Sangita Dawkhar, Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune. As per the order dated 29.05.2023, the research has been done at the Office of the District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Sindhudurg.
Thereafter, as per the request of Mrs. Sangita Dawkhar and Mr. On the recommendation of the people's representative, a proposal was submitted for the approval of the competent authority under the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Performing Government Duties Act, 2005. In pursuance of this proposal, as approved by the competent authority, Mrs. Sangita Dawkhar, Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, was appointed as the Deputy Commissioner. The transfer orders made vide order dated 29.05.2023 are being partially modified and posted at the place indicated in column 3 as under: :
