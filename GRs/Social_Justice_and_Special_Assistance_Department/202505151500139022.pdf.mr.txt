I. The said VPDA shall be subject to Audit.
Il. The Administrator shall ensure that all rules, regulations, terms and conditions of the relevant GR have been followed in respect of the said VPDA.
Hf. No amount shall be transferred to the VPDA to avoid lapse of funds.
IV. VPDA 15 applicable only for State Schemes and not for Central Sector and Centrally Sponsored
Schemes.
V. The Receipt to the said VPDA through Nil Bill procedure, shall be only for the bills drawn
under:
e MTR-31B for the object heads 37- Minor Works and 53- Major Works.
e MTR-45 B for the object heads of expenditure 33- Subsides and 50- Other
Charges;
e MTR-44 for the object heads of expenditure 31- Grants- in -Aid (Non Salary) and 35- Grants in Aid for creating of Capital Assets.
e MTR-29 for the object head of expenditure 52- Machinery and Equipment shall
be applicable from 1 April 2025.
VI. The Administrator shall timely submit the Utilisation Certificate in the prescribed format after
incurring expenditure from the VPDA to the offices viz. O/o. PAG (A&E)-I, Mumbai, O/o
PAG(AX&E)-II, Nagpur or O/o PAO, Mumbai as per the respective jurisdiction for the Bills drawn under MTR-44 for object heads of expenditure 31-Grants-in-Aid (Non Salary), 35-
Grants-in-Aid for creation of Capital Assets.
For bills drawn under MTR-31B, MTR-45B, MTR-29 the Utilisation Certificate shall be
submitted to the concerned administrative departments.
VIL. The Expenditure from the VPDA shall be as per the Sanction order of the respective Grant.
पा. The unspent balances under the VPDA shall be credited back to the Consolidated Fund after completion of one year (365 days) from the date of drawl of the funds/date of credit of amount to the VPDA or the timeline   mentioned in the sanction order whichever 1s earlier.
IX. The Monthly Plus Minus Memo shall be forwarded to this office by the respective treasury officer.
The balances under the VPDA shall be reconciled monthly by the Administrator with the
District Treasury Officer.
XI. The balances shall be reconciled with the AG office by the Treasury Officer who shall submit the Balance Certificate to A.G. office for confirmation of their balances 11 the books of A.G at
the end of the Financial Year.
