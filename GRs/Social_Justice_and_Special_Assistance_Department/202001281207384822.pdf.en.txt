Government's decision:
A budgetary provision of Rs. 25,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores only) has been approved for implementation of Centrally Sponsored National Family Benefit Scheme for the financial year 2019-20. As per the Government Circular No.2 of the Department of Finance, 60% of the total outlay of Rs. 15,00,00, 000/- for the period April-December, 2019 for the National Family Benefit Scheme has been released, out of which Rs. 15,00,00, 000/- has been released for the period April-October, 2019 for the period April-October, 2019 as per the Government Decision in Reference No.4 and 5. Now, the Finance Department has approved to disburse 20% of the remaining 40% of the amount of Rs. 5,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Crore only) i.e. 80% of the total provision. Accordingly, the Government hereby approves the release of balance amount of Rs. 5,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Crores only) for the period November, 2019 to March, 2020 for the National Family Benefit Scheme as per the accompanying statement under this Government decision. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
