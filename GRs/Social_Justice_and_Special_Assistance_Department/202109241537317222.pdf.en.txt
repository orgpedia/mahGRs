The 36-Additional Grant (Salary) amounting to Rs. 2,34,39, 000/- (Rupees Two Crores Thirty Four Lakhs Forty Nine Thousand only) is being approved to be disbursed subject to the following conditions.
1. The norms prescribed by the Government for the expenditure of funds under the schemes should be strictly implemented. Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2. Streamline the operations to limit the cost of management.
3. Detailed information about the amounts spent on various items and the expenditure incurred on them should be submitted to the government.
4) Cut down on office expenses.
5. The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the debt recovery rate.
6. The funds of the scheme are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
