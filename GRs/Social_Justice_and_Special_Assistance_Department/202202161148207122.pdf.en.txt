Government circular:
In view of the continuous demand for reopening of schools from areas where the spread of COVID-19 virus is not uniform in all parts of the state, the Commissioner of Municipal Corporation in the Municipal Corporation area and District Disaster Management Authority and CEO, Zilla Parishad in other parts of the state have been provided with the authority to reopen schools for classes 1 to 12 in the state in the academic year 2021-22 from January 24, 2022, considering the local situation.
It is informed to the Sub-Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune that now considering the local situation on the same lines, appropriate action should be taken to start special schools/ workshops for Divyangs under the department from March 1, 2022, as per the directions of the Municipal Commissioner in the Municipal Corporation area and the local authorities such as the District Disaster Management Authority and the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla Parishad in other parts of the state.
The guidelines prescribed by the Government from time to time regarding the opening of special schools/ workshops in this regard should be strictly followed.
