The functions of the Departmental Review Committee: (1) The State Government officers/ employees of Group-A to Group-D cadre on the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department (Self) establishment shall be reviewed by the above Committee. The Under Secretary/ Officiating Officer (Aastha-1) in the capacity of Secretary/ Member Secretary of the Committee shall submit the proposal to the Committee and prepare the records thereof and take further action.
2) The recommendations of the Group-A officers will be considered for final decision. be submitted to the Minister (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare).
3) The recommendations of the Group-B (Gazetted) officers will be considered for final decision. be submitted to the Minister of State (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare).
4) The recommendations of the officers/ employees of Group-B (Non-Gazetted) to Group-D should be submitted to the Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Chief Secretary of the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department for final decision.
If Group-A to Group-B (Gazetted) officers are found ineligible, such officers will be reviewed by the Special Review Committee of the General Administration Department.

The above two committees have been constituted in the context of the General Administration Department to review the continuation of government servants in government service. The recommendations regarding the eligible disqualification of officers/ employees are to be submitted in accordance with the procedures and instructions laid down in the Government Decision dated 10/06/2019 and considering the orders issued from time to time.
Further, as per the recommendation of the Departmental Review Committee, the Departmental Grievance Committee is being constituted to consider the grievances of Group-A (Gazetted) (Junior Pay Scale) employees with less than 7600 Grade Pay and Group-B (Gazetted) Cadre and Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees of the Ministries/ Departments against the decision of premature retirement after reviewing their services.

Functions of the Departmental Grievance Committee: (1) In case of Group-A (Gazetted) officers whose grade pay is less than 7600 as per the Sixth Pay Commission, the recommendation of the Grievance Committee shall be considered by the Government for final decision. It should be presented to the Prime Minister.
(2) In the case of Group-B (Gazetted) Officers, the recommendation of the Grievance Committee shall be considered for final decision. Minister (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare).
3) The recommendation of the Grievance Committee in the case of Group-B (Non-Gazetted), Group-C and Group-D employees will be considered by the Committee for final decision. submitted to the Chief Secretary.
