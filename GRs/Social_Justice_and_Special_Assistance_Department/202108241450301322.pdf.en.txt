Preamble: In order to expeditiously dispose of the pending departmental enquiry cases in the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, the Government was considering the appointment of two officers retired from the service of the State Government with in-depth knowledge of establishment matters.
Government's decision:
In view of the educational qualification and work experience required from the applications received in pursuance of the above advertisement, the following retired officers are being appointed on contract basis for a period of 11 months from the date of their joining as contractual staff to handle the pending departmental enquiry cases in the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Those concerned should immediately report to the office.

(1) On being appointed on contract basis, the concerned Government shall not have any right/ entitlement regarding induction/ absorption of service in any cadre of the Government or any other benefit of regular service.
(2) The competent authority for appointment shall have the power to terminate the contractual service of such officer at any time under special circumstances.
(3) The person appointed under the contract shall not be engaged in any commercial activity which may cause disruption in the performance of the services rendered.
(iv) The person appointed under the contract will be required to declare the conflict of interest involved.
5) Contractual appointees shall be required to maintain confidentiality of documents/ information and supporting materials received by them.
(6) The persons appointed under the contract shall be required to complete the work assigned to them within the stipulated time. The competent authority for appointment in respect of their functioning shall review and evaluate the work from time to time.
(4) The facilities of honorarium and other allowances for the period of appointment of the said retired officer by contract. shall be determined in accordance with the Government Decision dated 17.12.2016.
