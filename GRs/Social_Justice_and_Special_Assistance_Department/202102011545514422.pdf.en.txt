Government Circular: To implement the Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995, vide Circular No. 02 As per the decision of the Government here, the formation of the District Disability Committee under the chairmanship of the concerned Collector has been approved. However, the existing Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995 has been repealed vide Section 102 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.
Therefore, in accordance with Section 72 of the newly enacted Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the Central Government has decided to constitute the committee at the district level or until further orders from the Government. The term of office of the District Differently Abled Committees established under the Government Order dated 23.09.1999 is being extended.
The Committees are mandated to implement the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and to regulate and effectively implement schemes for the welfare of persons with disabilities. The terms and conditions of the said Committee are also as under. 2 Here the government will remain as decided.
