The Proposal:
It is seen that the society is suffering a lot due to many types of misunderstandings rooted in some evil, Aghori, inhuman customs that are rooted in Indian society. In order to save the lives of millions of people, to stop their exploitation and harassment and to improve the mental health of the society, the Government has issued the Maharashtra Prevention and Eradication of Human Sacrifice and Other Inhuman, Evil and Aghori Practices and Witchcraft Act, 2013 on December 20, 2013.
The anti-magic law will save the lives of millions of people from harassment, exploitation and exploitation.
For this, it is necessary to remove the misconceptions in the minds of the general public of Maharashtra by educating the society. Because of the misunderstandings rooted in culture, things that are dangerous to humanity such as racism, high expectations, mutual hatred between groups, gender inequality, poverty still exist in society. The Programme Implementation and Monitoring Committee (PIMC) has decided to change this. It was re-launched on January 5, 2017. Subsequently, seven members of the committee were appointed. 4 is here. It was cancelled on March 17, 2020. However, the committee needs to be reconstituted with the inclusion of new non-official members. The matter of reconstitution of the" Anti-Witchcraft Act Awareness Campaign and Dissemination Programme Implementation Committee," constituted on 5th January, 2017, was under consideration of the Government.
