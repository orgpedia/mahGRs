Preamble: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India has implemented the Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAY) on a pilot basis since 2009-10. Under this scheme, the works of basic amenities like water supply, roads, etc. in the villages having more than 50% population of Scheduled Castes and other activities are sanctioned in convergence with other schemes of the state government. The main objective of this scheme is to implement the welfare schemes implemented by the various departments of the state government in the villages and to develop these villages in a holistic manner.
The main objective of this scheme is to provide basic facilities to the people of the village and to improve the living standards of all the citizens. While spending the funds sanctioned under this scheme, it is expected to implement the scheme/ program by combining the funds received from other schemes of the Central Government and the State Government and the funds received under the Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana.
In the backdrop of the above facts, detailed guidelines to the field officers and constitution of various level committees for effective implementation of the scheme in the State of Maharashtra have been issued vide Government Resolution dated 26.08.2019.
The following villages have been selected by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India during the year 2018-19.
1. Kharab Budruk, T.G. Akola
2. Hingna Mysore, T.G. Akola
Both the villages fall under the jurisdiction of Akola City Municipal Corporation.

However, since the scheme is being implemented for rural areas of the State, the matter of taking a decision to exclude these two villages was under the consideration of the Government vide letter dated 16.12.2020 of the Commissionerate of Social Welfare.
Government Decision: If the villages/ hamlets selected by the Central Government under the Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana are included/ have been included in the limits of Municipal Corporation, Municipal Council and Nagar Panchayat, then such villages/ hamlets should be excluded and the funds sanctioned for those villages/ villages should be processed as per the existing order of the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India.
