The Proposal:
In the ashramshalas/ Vidyaniketan for the children/ daughters of the Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes/ Scheduled Tribes/ Other Backward Classes/ Other Backward Classes/ Other Backward Classes/ Bahujan Welfare Department in the State, the necessary basic facilities are provided by the Government to the needy institutions as mentioned in the decision of the Government in the context of the educational needs of the students. Under this government decision, it has also been ordered that newcomers in the education sector and other matters that are beneficial for the interest of students will also be included with the approval of the government at that time. Accordingly, in view of the current outbreak of global Corona (COVID 19) infection, the government was considering to increase the facilities in all ashramshalas under this department and to include some more aspects in order to prevent this disease.
Government's decision:
The Government is approving to increase the facilities provided in the ashramshalas under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department by including the following essential basic services as per the decision of the Government in order to prevent the spread of global corona infection.
1. First Aid Kit/ Covid Kit 2. Excellent type of Max.
3. Gloves 4. Temperature measuring devices 5. Oxygen measuring devices 6. Sprayers (Power sprayers 2 stroke)
