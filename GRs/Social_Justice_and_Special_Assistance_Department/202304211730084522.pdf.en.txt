Government Decision: In the financial year 2023-24, for the implementation of Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme for general beneficiaries, Rs. A budget provision of Rs. 1530,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One thousand Five hundred and thirty crore only) has been approved. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 153,00,00, 000/- has been released by the Finance Department. Accordingly, the amount sanctioned for the Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme for general beneficiaries for the period April, 2023 to March, 2024 is Rs. 153,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Fifty Three Crores only) is hereby approved by the Government to be distributed as per the accompanying prospectus under this Government Order. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
2. All the Collectors are informed that in the accompanying statement, Column No. 3 Funds have been distributed to the respective districts on the new CO Code. The grants distributed by them should be distributed to the taluks of their districts in proportion to the beneficiaries.
The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
The expenditure from the said fund should be spent under the head" Demand No.N-3, Main Account Head 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 104-Welfare of Elderly, Disabled and Destitute Persons (08) (07) Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme (Programme) (22353112), 50-Other Expenditure."
4. All the District Collectors are hereby informed to ensure that these payments are deposited in the treasury. Also, they should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the subsidy given to them under any circumstances.
All the District Collectors are also informed that the expenditure incurred by them from the grants handed over to them is equal to the expenditure recorded in the office of the Accountant General.
