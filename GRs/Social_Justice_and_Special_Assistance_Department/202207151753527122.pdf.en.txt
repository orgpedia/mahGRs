Preamble: To provide basic facilities to the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist communities for the development of their habitations/ villages in urban and rural areas of the State. The Government has decided to implement the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme from the year 2018-19 to give recognition to the works suggested by the people's representatives at the government level. The activities to be taken up under the scheme will include development of places of cultural and historical importance, monuments and cultural development of Scheduled Castes and New Buddhists (e.g. Library, Study Centre, Dhamma Kendra, Vipassana Kendra, Training Centre) have been included in the Government Decision dated July 28, 2021. According to The matter of sanctioning works in the habitations of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist communities in urban and rural areas suggested by the people's representatives was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
In order to provide basic amenities in the habitations of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist communities in urban and rural areas of the State, the Government has approved the works mentioned in Annexure A along with this Government decision and the funds to be disbursed for the same through the provision in the Budget for the year 2022-23 under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme.
2. m. These works should be carried out through the working mechanism suggested by the people's representatives, for example, Public Works Department, District Council Construction Department, Municipal Corporation, Municipality, Nagar Panchayat, Gram Panchayat or other departments of the government.

3. The executing agency shall prepare detailed maps/ plans and budgets of the works at the estimated cost approved in principle and submit them to the Collector, Thane for administrative approval. The District Collector, Thane should give administrative approval to these works. The working system should implement the tender process of the works as per the prevailing rules of the government.
It should be ensured that these works are completed within the approved budgetary limit. If administrative approval is given for works costing more than the approved budgetary cost, no additional funds will be provided in any form.
(5) The Government shall have the power to cancel or modify the works if the people's representatives who have suggested such works make a request in writing, confirming that they are to be changed or that they cannot be done due to some unavoidable reasons.
Before starting the work, the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Thane must ensure that the work is done in the Scheduled Castes and New Buddhists community.
It should be ensured that the terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated March 9, 2018 and July 28, 2021 of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme are strictly followed.
The Collector, Thane should ensure that the code of conduct for any general election/ by-election is not implemented in the area of the works approved before starting these works.
The cost of these works shall be met from the funds made available by the Finance Department for the said scheme in the budgetary provision for 2022-23 and shall be spent under the following head:
