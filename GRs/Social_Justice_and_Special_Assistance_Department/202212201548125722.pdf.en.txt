Preface: The Sowing of the Fruit, p. Koregaon Bhima Vijaystambh Shaurya Diwas is celebrated every year on 1st January in Haveli, Pune. Every year on 1st January, more than 5 lakh people from all over the country gather to pay homage to Vijay Stambh.
Considering this, every year. A permanent administrative committee under the chairmanship of the District Collector, Pune, was under the consideration of the government to make arrangements for the Koregaon Bhima Vijaystambh Shaurya Diwas celebrations on January 1.
Government decision: Every year in Pune, India. For administrative convenience to celebrate Koregaon Bhima Vijay Stambh Shaurya Diwas on 01st January, a Permanent Administrative Committee under the Chairmanship of District Collector, Pune is being constituted as follows.
