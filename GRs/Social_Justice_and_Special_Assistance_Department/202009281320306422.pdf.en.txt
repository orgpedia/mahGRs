Government Decision, No.: General Administration Department, dated 11th June, 2020 Preamble: In view of the increasing prevalence of Corona across Maharashtra at present, the General Administration Department has already issued instructions to keep the daily attendance in government offices in rotation to a limit of a few percent. Government Circular: In view of the increasing prevalence of corona across Maharashtra, the General Administration Department has already issued instructions to limit daily attendance in government offices by rotation. However, in offices where 100% attendance has been made mandatory, Divyang officers/ staff are exempted from attending the office till the public transport facility is restored.
However, the concerned departments/ establishments shall ensure that the functioning of the offices is not adversely affected by these holidays.
