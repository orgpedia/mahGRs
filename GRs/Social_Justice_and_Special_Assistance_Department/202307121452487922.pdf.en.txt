Regarding the approval to purchase items under the facilities for students in government hostels and residential schools under the Department of Social Justice.

Sanitation: Administrative approval has been granted under the Government Decision dated 4th July, 2023 for the purchase of items under the facilities for the students of Government Hostels and Government Residential Schools through the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Paragraph 2 (b) of this Government Resolution shall read as follows:
The supplier shall submit the samples of the goods to be supplied to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune. The Commissioner, Social Welfare shall finalize the samples after checking the quality of the samples from the expert organization in the concerned field. The details of the samples should be sent to the Home Guard/ Superintendent of the hostel where the goods are to be supplied and they should certify that the goods have been received as per the specifications of the sample.
(ii) The Department of Industries, Power and Labour, as per the Government Decision, dated 1.12.2016, may take action to pay the dues after verifying 10% samples of quality randomly from a recognized institution as prescribed under Annexure-12 of the Rules of Procedure for Office Purchases to be made by the Government Departments.
