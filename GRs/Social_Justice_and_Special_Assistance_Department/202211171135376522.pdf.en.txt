Government's decision:
Social Justice and Special Assistance Department
As a special case, provision has been made to fill up 10% of the seats falling vacant every year in all the government hostels except the hostels of meritorious students as per the decision of the government dated 18th June, 2015.
As per the provisions of this Government decision, the list of students to be admitted as private students in the Government Hostels for the year 2022-23 in the Backward Classes is being approved under this Government decision.
Students in the above list should be admitted subject to the following conditions:
The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, concerned should ensure that no more students than the sanctioned student capacity of the hostel are allowed to enter the hostel.
The students are required to submit the Bonafide Certificate of the current year of the school/ college in which they are studying to the Home Guard of the hostel concerned.
All the Assistant Commissioners, Social Welfare should complete the process of admission to private sector immediately and submit the compliance report to the government through the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune within 30 days.
If any seats remain vacant after the admission of the above-mentioned private students or the seats remain vacant due to the non-admission of the student in the said list, the concerned Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, shall take action according to the reservation and merit mentioned in the prevailing government decision regarding hostel admission.
