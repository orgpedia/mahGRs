As per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Government Employees Regulation of Transfer and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, the Government, with the approval of the competent authority, issued orders for the administrative reasons of the Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Group-A and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare and similar, Group-A cadre officers. The changes have been made with effect from 14.07.2023.
