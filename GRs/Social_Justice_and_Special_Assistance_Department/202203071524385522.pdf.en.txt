Government Decision: Sant Rohidas Tannery and Tannery Development Corporation (SRODC) Mumbai is implementing various schemes for the economic upliftment of the tannery community. A budget provision of Rs 7,60,00 lakh has been made.
2. Out of this, the balance proposed to be disbursed by the Finance Department on the Beams system to the extent of 95% is Rs. The Government is approving the release of funds amounting to Rs. 2,66,00 thousand (Rupees Two Crore Sixty Six Lakh only) subject to the following conditions:
1) Limit the cost of operations.
2) The details of the amount spent on various matters and the expenditure incurred on it should be submitted to the government.
3) The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the rate of recovery of loans.
4) Cut down on your expenses.
The Deputy Secretary (Corporations), Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Mantralaya, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer in respect of this managerial grant. The amount is being handed over to the Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Navi Mumbai for further action. They are being empowered to disburse this grant to Sant Rohidas Tannery and Tannery Development Corporation through payment in the form of M.C.N.44.
The expenditure on these items should be met from the approved provision for the financial year 2021-2022 by meeting the expenditure under the head" Demand No. N-3, 2225- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes, 1 Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 800 Other Expenditure (09) (03) Subsidiary Grants to Sant Rohidas Tannery and Tannery Development Corporation (2225 3592) 36- Subsidiary Grants (Salaries)."
