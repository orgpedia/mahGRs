The proposal: and V.S.V. On 29.09.2020, a review meeting of Gopinath Munde Usdod Labour Corporation was held. In the meeting, it was decided to register all the workers of the state and give them identity cards.
So that the benefits of the schemes can be given to them, the sugarcane workers who are residing in the place and who are continuously working as sugarcane workers for the last three years are expected to be given an identity card by the Gram Sevak (in the respective village, hamlet, tandas and paddies and others). The government was considering the formation of a committee to ensure that the work is completed on time, solved and if there is any problem at the local level, it can be solved immediately.
Government Decision: All the striking workers of the state must be registered and given an identity card. For this, the following committees have been formed at the district level.
