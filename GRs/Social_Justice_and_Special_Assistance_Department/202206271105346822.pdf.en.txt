2. In the context of Finance Department. As per the circular dated 4.4.2022, 60% of the budgetary allocation will be released for the first nine months (i.e. by the end of December 2022). The funds will be disbursed in a phased manner and the Finance Department will bear 21 per cent of the amount for the three months of April, May and June i.e. Rs. 491.50 lakh has been disbursed on BEAMS system. Accordingly, under the Fund Distribution Policy of the Finance Department, starting a poultry business through the rearing of 1000 meatless poultry birds has been included in the Scheduled Castes Measures Scheme as per Section No. (2403D 731) for the financial year 2022-23. The Government is approving the disbursement of Rs. 2340.33 lakh on Budget Fund Distribution System to the Principal Secretary, Padum Division as Head of Administrative Department.
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner.
It should be ensured that the funds will be spent this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made, account-wise/ sub-account-wise and material objective-wise information achieved, the utilization certificate of the fund, the concerned field officer should ensure that it is sent to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, the concerned Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.
