Cabinet approves constitution of a Cabinet Sub-Committee for construction of a world class memorial for Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar at Indu Mill.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance
GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: MEETING-2020/ PR No.40/ CONSTRUCTIONS
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Extension, Mumbai 400 032.
Date: 9th of May, 2022.
Read:- Urban Development Department, Government Resolution, No. MRD-3320/ No. 52/ NV-7, Dy. 10.09.2020.
The Proposal:
State Cabinet on Wednesday. In the meeting held on 22.1.2020, it has been decided that a Cabinet Sub-Committee will be constituted to look into the International Memorial of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar at Indu Mill. Urban Development Department As per the Government decision dated 10.9.2020, for the speedy functioning of the memorial of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar at Indu Mill, the Hon. It has been decided that the Minister (Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance) will be the controller. It has also been decided that the cost of the project will be reimbursed by the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department. Accordingly, the matter of forming a cabinet sub-committee regarding the international memorial of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: In the meeting held on 22.1.2020, it has been decided that a Cabinet Sub-Committee will be constituted to look into the International Memorial of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar at Indu Mill. Accordingly, the following Cabinet Sub-Committee is being constituted for overall control of the project.
