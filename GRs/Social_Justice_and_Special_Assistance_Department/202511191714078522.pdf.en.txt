-: Government Corrigendum: -
The Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Government of Andhra Pradesh, under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, decided to budget for the construction of residential quarters for the working teachers / non-teaching staff of Government Residential Schools functioning in the State for the children of Scheduled Castes and FTAAs of Amravati Regional Division. Administrative approval has been granted under 11.06.2025 and the condition number (s) in the said Government decision is as follows: -
f) The Integrated Construction Management Cell, Tribal Development Department / Social Justice and Special Assistance Department established under the Tribal Development Department dated 13.02.2025 will be operational as the operational mechanism for the present constructions.
The above Condition No. (o) is being amended as follows.
The operational mechanism for the present constructions will be Maharashtra-State Infrastructure Development Corporation (MSIDC), Mumbai.
(2) The other provisions of the Government Decision shall remain in force.
The Government Corrigendum is available on the Government of Maharashtra website Www.maharashtra.gov.in with its computer code No. 202511191714078522. The certificate is being digitally signed and authenticated.
