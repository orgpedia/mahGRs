Government's decision:
For the financial year 2022-23, a budgetary provision of Rs. 111,85,73, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eleven Crore Eighty Five Lakh Seventy Three Thousand only) has been approved for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Subsidy Scheme for District Establishments and Rs. 42,51,22, 000/- (Rupees Forty Nine Crore One Lakh Twenty Two Thousand only) for the establishments on the scheme for providing financial assistance to the old landless agricultural labourers. 10% of the budget. 3 For the purpose of the Government Decision 01-Wage herein, Reference No. 4 As per the decision of the Government here, 15% of the budget allocation for the target of 01-Salary, 14% each for the target of 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water, 11 Travel expenses and 13 Office expenses and Reference No. Under the government decision here, 7 percent of the amount for the purpose of 01-salary has been distributed to all the divisional commissioner's offices.
