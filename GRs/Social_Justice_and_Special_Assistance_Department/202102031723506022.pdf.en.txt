Opening of new head of accounts for the Vocational Training Fee Reimbursement Scheme for the students belonging to Scheduled Castes who have taken admission in the seats available under the PPP Scheme in Government Industrial Training Institutes as well as in Private Industrial Training Institutes through Central Online System.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance
Government Resolution No: Vyaprasu-2020/ PR No.267/ Education-1
Ministry Extension, Madame Cama Road,
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Mumbai 400 032
Date: February 3, 2021
Reference: Government Resolution No., Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, No. Vyaprasu 2019/ Q.No.173/ Education-1, dated 1st August, 2019
Preamble: The Scheme of" Business Training Fee Reimbursement" for SC students who have taken admission in Government Industrial Training Institutes under PPP Scheme and in Private Industrial Training Institutes through Central Online Mode has been implemented as per the decision of the Government in the above context. The government was considering opening a new account for this scheme.
Government Decision: Approval is being given to open new accounting heads for the scheme of" Business Training Fee Reimbursement" for the students belonging to Scheduled Castes who are taking admission in the seats available under the PPP scheme in Government Industrial Training Institutes as well as in Private Industrial Training Institutes through the Central Online System.
