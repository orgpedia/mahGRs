Government's decision
Raje Sambhaji Multipurpose Service Institute, Dagdisahjahanpur Sanchali Rukhmai Govind Mukabdheer Residential School, Beed, T.G. Beed has been changed to Matimand category of Special Mukabdheer Class School. Government approval is being given for shifting to Ambegaon, G. Pune subject to the following conditions.
(1) The Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune shall take further action as per the rules for change of category within the limit of the number of approved students 50 residential students and the number of funded students.
(2) It shall be mandatory for the institution to obtain the permission of the Commissioner, Differently Abled Welfare, Pune, to run a school for the mentally retarded category by providing all necessary facilities.
3) The above schools will continue to be on grant basis as before. Also, no incremental posts and additional subsidy will be paid to the employees due to the change in category.
4) Since the school of the Mukabdheer class is being converted into a school of the Matimand class, the training of the employees who are already working should be completed in the next three years.
(5) The present and future orders of the Government issued from time to time shall apply to the said institution.
(6) The existing rules and regulations of the scheme of recognition and grant of Divyang Special School to the said institution shall be binding from time to time.
7) The institution concerned will have to bear all the expenses related to the relocation of the said special school. A refund will not be issued.
