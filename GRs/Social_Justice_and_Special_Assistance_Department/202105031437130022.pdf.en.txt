4) Government Decision of General Administration Department, dated June 11, 2020 Preamble: In view of the increasing outbreak of Corona across Maharashtra, the General Administration Department has already issued instructions to keep the daily attendance in government offices in rotation to a limit of a few percent. Further, to ensure that the absence of disabled officers/ employees does not adversely affect the functioning of the office, it has been decided to exempt them from attending the government offices by taking care of the concerned establishments. 1 has been issued by the government. At present, various departments have asked whether this circular will be applicable or how, and various organizations have also sent representations to this department regarding its implementation. Considering this, the matter of issuing a revised circular was under the consideration of the government.
Government Circular: In view of the increasing prevalence of corona across Maharashtra, the General Administration Department has already issued instructions to limit daily attendance in government offices by rotation. Since the immunity of the disabled persons is not as good as that of the general public, it is difficult and troublesome to face the difficulties, all the disabled employees should be exempted from attending the office and they should be allowed to work from home. These guidelines shall be applicable to all the concerned departments/ establishments till further orders.
