Foreign Scholarship Scheme has been implemented since 2003 to provide opportunities to the students of Scheduled Castes to study in reputed educational institutions abroad. The Rules of the Scheme have been amended from time to time and the revised Rules of the Scheme have been laid down vide Government Order dated 27.06.2017.
A total of 75 students belonging to Scheduled Castes from Maharashtra will be admitted to foreign educational institutions within the 300 QS World Rankings for postgraduate and PhD courses. Accordingly, the Government was considering the matter of selecting eligible students under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme for the year 2022-23 and sanctioning foreign scholarships to them.
Government Decision: As per the Government Decision dated 27th June, 2017 regarding the Overseas Scholarship Scheme for the children belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes, a total of 56 students of Form A-1, A-2, A-3, A-4, A-5, A-6, A-7, A-8, A-9, A-10, A-11 and A-12 selected on the recommendations of the Selection Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Department of Social Justice, Ministry of Education, Mumbai and as per the terms and conditions of the Government Decision, are being sanctioned this scholarship.

The Selection Committee under the Chairmanship of the Chief Justice of India (CJI) is approving the grant of scholarship to 19 students in Form B-1 and B-2, who have been selected through an inter-change process.
3.1 Further, as per the provisions of the Government Decision dated 10.3.2021, after the selection of eligible students as per the sanctioned strength under the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Foreign Scholarship Scheme, if some students seek cancellation of their selection under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme of the State Government on various grounds, the State Government has the power to select the eligible students in the same selection year from the waiting list of eligible students in the order of merit as recommended by the Selection Committee. In pursuance of this provision, a general waiting list of a total of 38 students is being prescribed under Form C.
Commissioner, Social Welfare Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune is being authorized to grant admissible scholarships to the students selected for overseas scholarships. They should take action to sanction the scholarship admissible to the student concerned only after taking the guarantees prescribed under the government decision from the students selected for foreign scholarship under this government decision and examining the original documents of the students. The Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune is being empowered to sanction scholarships to the concerned students for the courses and duration mentioned in the government decision and also to sanction scholarships payable accordingly if there is an increase or decrease in the tuition fees of a university during the period of study abroad of the students.
5. Students who have been sanctioned foreign scholarships by the Government are required to serve the country after completing their education and benefit from their knowledge. In this regard, it will be mandatory for the concerned students to write a letter of guarantee (BOND) before approving the scholarship.
The students selected for the foreign scholarship must have completed their Master's degree or PhD course abroad within the prescribed time period. Under no circumstances will the student be given an extension or increased cost.
The amount of scholarship will be recovered in full from the student selected for the foreign scholarship if the student changes the place or course of the educational institution, university or course approved for higher education.
8. (b) Information certified by the University regarding semester-wise tuition fees, living allowance and other incidental expenses for the subsequent year, attendance and satisfactory academic performance of the student.
