Preamble: The Government was considering to disburse the grant received for the scheme from the Finance Department on Budget Distribution System (Share Contribution to BEAMS Sant Rohidas Tannery and Tannery Development Corporation) to the Controlling Officer (Joint Secretary (Budget), Social Justice Special Assistance Department).
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS- The share contribution to Sant Rohidas Tannery and Leather Development Corporation, which was budgeted for the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department for the financial year 2022-2023 and received from the Finance Department on the BEAMS system, is Rs. 560.00 lakhs to the Controlling Officer (Joint Secretary (Budget), Social Justice Special Assistance Department).
2. Expenditure for" Demand No. Head of Accounts, 4-N. 4225. Capital expenditure on welfare of Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Castes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 190 (01), Investments in Sectors and other activities, Welfare of Scheduled Castes (03) (01), Share Capital Contribution to Sant Rohidas Tannery Development Corporation (42250042) (Programme) 54- Investments to be met from the budgetary provision in the year 2021-22.
The Joint Secretary (Budget), Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Mumbai will be the controlling authority in respect of this shareholding. The said share contribution amount is being handed over to Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Navi Mumbai for further action. They are being authorized to disburse the grant to Sant Rohidas Tannery and Tannery Development Corporation Limited, Mumbai in the form of payment in M.C.N.44. After the funds are spent, the statement of expenditure and utility certificate should be submitted to the Accountant General's office and the government without fail.
The above funds made available by the Finance Department on the BEAMS system in the financial year 2022-2023. It is being distributed subject to the terms and conditions of the Government Decision dated 4.4.2022.
