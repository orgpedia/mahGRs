Introduction: As per the decision of the government here, Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development
Under the scheme, in-principle approval has been granted for the construction of a social hall at a cost of not less than Rs.15 crore on the available land/ land in the Mumbra-Kalwa area of the local body of Thane Municipal Corporation, Thane. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government Decision: Thane Municipal Corporation, Thane under Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme
Administrative approval is being accorded to the proposal for construction of Social Hall on available land/ plot in Mumbra-Kalwa area of the local body at a cost of not less than Rs.15 crore. Also, 10% of the project cost i. e. Rs. 1.50 crore is being released in the first phase.
The expenditure on this project should be met from the funds budgeted for the year 2022-23 by covering the expenditure under the following heads:
