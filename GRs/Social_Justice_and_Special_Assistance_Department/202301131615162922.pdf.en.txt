The Proposal:
The Union Cabinet in its meeting held on 29th November, 2022 has approved the creation of a separate Department for the Welfare of Persons with Disabilities by separating the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance from the Department of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities and creation of a total of 2063 posts of officers/ staff at the Ministerial level, Commissioner, Welfare of Persons with Disabilities, Regional level, District level and Taluka level. Accordingly, a Government notification dated 15.12.2022 of the General Administration Department has created a" Department of Disabled Welfare."
Further, the High Level Committee of Secretaries in its 6 th meeting held on Thursday, 29 th December, 2022 has approved creation of a total of 42 (32 regular posts+ 10 externally recruited posts) posts in the first phase for the" Department of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities." Accordingly, the matter of providing temporary staff officers to the newly started" Divyang Kalyan" department was under the consideration of the government.
The High-Level Committee for the newly formed" Department of Disabled Welfare" in the state has approved the creation of 42 (32 and 10 external sources) posts in the first phase. It will take some time to get these jobs. In view of this, the following officers and staff on establishment of Social Justice and Special Assistance Department are being made available to the Department of Disabled Welfare only on temporary basis.
