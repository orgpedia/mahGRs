The process of loan disbursement:
The district level office will be fully responsible for the selection of eligible beneficiaries for the loan schemes and the completion of the necessary legal documents in the approved cases and the recovery of the loans given by the corporation within the stipulated time and the regional office will have control over them. Basically, the process is as follows. The beneficiaries will have to apply afresh to get the loan under this revised scheme.
1. the decision of the government. The beneficiaries will be selected by the District Beneficiary Selection Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Collector under 06/07/2019.
The entire process of selection of beneficiaries and recovery of loans under this scheme will be done from the concerned district level office of the corporation and it will be controlled by the concerned regional office.
The District Manager concerned shall be the implementing officer of the scheme and the Regional Manager concerned shall be the controlling officer.
4. The District Offices of the Corporation will publish advertisements for loan cases in the local newspapers and on the notice board of the main government/ semi-government offices and at the same time, the application form and the list of documents will be made available in the office for public viewing.
The concerned District Manager shall prepare a list of eligible and ineligible beneficiaries after thorough scrutiny/ examination of the application received and submit the complete application to the concerned Regional Manager for examination. Thereafter, the concerned Regional Manager will recommend the beneficiary wise funds to the Headquarters, for which it will be mandatory to consider the following aspects: a) Enhancement of Industry/ Business, b) Capacity of Beneficiaries/ Knowledge of Business, c) Capacity for Repayment/ Capacity of Guarantors.
After the approval of the District Selection Committee, the error-free complete loan proposal of the eligible beneficiary will be submitted to the headquarters for approval/ demand of funds by the concerned District Manager through the concerned Regional Manager. The loan proposals of eligible beneficiaries will be approved by the Headquarter Beneficiary Selection Committee under the Chairmanship of the Managing Director.

The process of loan recovery:
The repayment of the loan will be initiated after 90 days of disbursement.
Monthly instalments of loan repayment should be fixed and in order to recover the loan, advance cheques of the following dates should be taken from the beneficiaries as well as through ECS (Electronic Clearance System).
3. If not recovered by doing so, the loan should be recovered by the mortgage and the guarantors kept with the corporation.
If it is not possible to recover the loan from the guarantor, the loan should be recovered by submitting complete information to the Collector under Section 221 of the Land Revenue Code (RRC).
Beneficiaries who have not repaid the loan taken earlier will not be able to avail the benefit of the new loan till the full repayment is made. Also, the beneficiaries who repay the entire loan amount within the repayment period will be eligible to avail the benefit of this scheme again to grow their business.
