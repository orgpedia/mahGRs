Introduction: The date for the repair, maintenance and upkeep of the xerox machine in this section. For the period from 1/1/2020 to 31/12/2020. The Service Contract Agreement was entered into with Sahil Copier Services, Mazgaon, Mumbai vide Memorandum dated 17/1/2020. Now, in this regard, the government has decided as follows: Government Decision: Date for the repair, maintenance and upkeep of xerox machines in this section. For the period from 1/1/2020 to 31/12/2020. The Service Contract Agreement was entered into with Sahil Copier Services, Mazgaon, Mumbai vide Memorandum dated 17/1/2020.
As per the provisions of the said memorandum, the supplier Sahil Copier Services, Mazgaon, Mumbai is bound to comply with all the terms and conditions of the contract for supply of toner for Xerox machines to the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment. However, it has been observed that there has been non-compliance with the terms and conditions by Sahil Copier Services, Mazgaon, Mumbai. Accordingly, since the Government has the power to cancel the tender agreement at any stage of the acceptance of the tender, the Government Memorandum No. 17/1/2020, the contract with the Government of Sahil Copier Services, Mazgaon, Mumbai is hereby cancelled.
Sahil Copier Services, Mumbai has not complied with the agreement with this department.
Therefore, all the departments of the ministry should take note that no other department should enter into an agreement with this company anymore.
