Government's decision:
In the financial year 2022-22, for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme, Rs. A budget provision of Rs. 450,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Hundred and Fifty Crores only) has been approved. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 225,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Five Crores only) has been released by the Department of Finance. Accordingly, for the Centrally Sponsored Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme, the amount sanctioned for the period April, 2022 to October, 2022 is Rs. 225,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Five Crores only) has been disbursed to all the District Collectors on Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) System as per the statement attached under the Government Decision.
All the Collectors are informed that the funds have been disbursed to the respective districts in the accompanying returns on PFMS system. The subsidy disbursed by them should be distributed to the talukas of their districts on the PFMS system in proportion to the beneficiaries. The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
