In order to provide the necessary skills and knowledge to the students of the Scheduled Castes (including neo-Buddhists) and to prepare them for the competitive era in various fields, the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship Scheme for higher education in reputed educational institutions of the country has been implemented under the Government Decision Social Justice, Cultural Affairs, Sports and Special Assistance Department No. EBC-2003/ PR No. 323/ MAVK-2, dated 11 June 2003. By superseding all the earlier Government decisions in respect of the Rules of the Scheme, the revised Rules of the Scheme have been brought into force with effect from the Government decision dated 08.11.2017. In this decision, the selection committee headed by the NMU Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, was under the consideration of the government to conduct the selection process for the year 2020-21 for the students admitted in the reputed educational institutions of the country and to sanction scholarships to the students selected on the terms and conditions specified in the applications received through it.
Government Decision: The Selection Committee constituted under the Government Decision, dated 08.11.2017, for Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for SC students to pursue higher education in reputed educational institutions in the country, in its meeting held on 5.01.2021, has approved the sanction of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for the year 2020-21 to 25 eligible students in Form A-1 to A-3 and Form B-1 to B-2 attached with this Government Decision.
2.The students who have been approved the scholarship, the full tuition fee and examination fee fixed by the respective educational institution will be given to the institution through the Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune. The students will have to pay the entire hostel and food charges as per their choice.
