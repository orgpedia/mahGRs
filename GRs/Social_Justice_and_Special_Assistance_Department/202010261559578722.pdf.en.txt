Preamble: With a view to protect the rights and welfare of transgenders in the State, the Government have decided to constitute a Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board at the State level and at the Divisional level. The decision was taken on December 13, 2018. The composition, functions and objectives of the Board have been prescribed by this Government. Accordingly, the Maharashtra State Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board has been constituted. The members have been appointed as per the Government Resolution dated 8th June, 2020.
The High Court in Public Interest No. In the case of CJ-LD-VC-38 of 2020, the petitioner Vikram Ramesh Shinde has filed a writ petition regarding the demands of transgenders in the wake of COVID-19. In pursuance of the memorandum dated 1 st July, 2020, the Government has been directed to decide on their various demands and take appropriate action. Accordingly, it is necessary to establish a Grievance Redressal Committee/ Cell regarding the problems and grievances of transgenders in the state.
According to the directive, the government was considering the formation of a grievance redressal committee at the district level to take action on the complaints received from transgenders in the state.
Government's decision
Grievances/ problems of all transgenders in the state need to be redressed at the district level. For this, a district-level grievance redressal committee will be set up.

The objectives of the Committee are: To provide effective control and redressal of grievances of transgenders in the State expeditiously.
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
(a) to take necessary action in accordance with the complaints received.
(b) To redress the grievances received by the District Level Committee within the prescribed time limit.
c) Verify the complaint and recommend to the Departmental Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board, as required.
(d) The Committee shall study in depth the problems/ grievances faced by transgenders and suggest suitable measures to the Government.
