The Proposal:
The Government Hostels Scheme is implemented for the education of backward class boys and girls under the Department of Social Justice. A total of 442 MAHA hostels are functioning in the state under the scheme, out of which 230 are boys' hostels and 292 are girls' hostels. These government hostels have an intake capacity of 23,570 boys and 20,220 girls and thus a total of 43,890 students are admitted to the government hostels under the division subject to prescribed conditions. The students admitted to these government hostels are provided with facilities such as accommodation, food arrangements, educational materials, living allowance for daily expenses, etc. Accordingly, on the occasion of the 125th birth anniversary of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar under the department, Girls Government Hostel, Amba Ta. In order to provide facilities to the students admitted in the Government Hostel at Patrur District Jalna, the Government was considering the purchase of essential items like pillows, pillow covers, bedsheets, mattresses, bed sheets, blankets, sattanji, iron coats, offoser tables, offoser chairs, high back chairs, office wooden tables, office large iron cupboards, printers, computers, wooden benches, water purifiers, dining tables, dining benches, wooden tables for students, wooden chairs for students without hands, CCTV sets, cameras, UPS for offices, etc.
