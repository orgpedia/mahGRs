Letter from the Collector, Kolhapur dated May 12, 2022.
Preamble: The Collector, Kolhapur, has submitted a proposal to get funds for the beautification and other works of the Samadhi Sthal of Shrimant Rajshri Shahu Maharaj in the limits of Kolhapur Municipal Corporation under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme.
Accordingly, the matter of providing funds for the beautification and other works of the Samadhi Sthal of Shrimant Rajshri Shahu Maharaj in the limits of Kolhapur Municipal Corporation was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
For the beautification and other works of the Samadhi Sthal of Shrimant Rajshri Shahu Maharaj, the Government is approving a budget of Rs. 9,40,56, 108/- based on the tariff list of the State Public Works Department for the year 2021-22.

Government Resolution No: Savio-2021/ P.S. No.132/ Ajak
The said budget has been examined by the Public Works Department (Regional) and the same has been approved by the concerned Chief Engineer i.e. Chief Engineer Architecture and Chief Engineer Power, Regional Department of Public Works, Pune, who is the highest technical officer at the zonal level. Accordingly, pursuant to the countersignature on the RECAPULATION SHEET made by him, the amount of Rs. 9,40,56, 108/-crore is being sanctioned as per condition number 9 in para 134 (1) of the Financial Powers conferred under the Financial Powers Rules Book, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-V, Maharashtra Public Works Rules Book.
The approval is being granted in accordance with the financial powers conferred under paragraphs 134 (1) of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-V, Maharashtra Public Works Rules, 2019, under the Government Resolution, Finance Department, dated 17th April, 2015 and 20th September, 2019.
