The Prohibition of Employment and Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers Act, 2013 (2 of 2013) Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government Notification No. 31/07/2019.
Preamble:" The Central Government has enacted the Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act, 2013" (25 of 2013). This law The Rules made by the Central Government under this Act, which came into force with effect from 6th December, 2013, have been published in the Gazette of India, dated 12th December, 2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 26 (1) of the said Act, 2013, the Central Government hereby makes provision for the establishment of a State Level Control for the purposes of implementation of this Act, vide notification dated 31st July, 2019. A State-level committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister had been formed for the purpose.
There is a provision for appointment of 2 representatives of Scheduled Castes nominated by the State Government as" members" of the State Level Control Committee.
Accordingly, vide Notification No.6 dated 31/7/2019, the matter of inclusion of 2 members belonging to the Scheduled Castes category of the State Legislature in the said Committee was under consideration of the Government.

Government Resolution No: HAMAYU-2020/ PS No.115/ AJAK
Government's decision:
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 26 (1) of the Central Government Act, 2013 (25 of 2013) with respect to prevention of recruitment and rehabilitation of manual scavengers, the Government, vide notification dated 31/7/2019, hereby make provision for regulation in relation to the implementation of the said Act. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister. The following two members belonging to the Scheduled Castes category of the Legislature are being included in the Control Committee at No. 6:
