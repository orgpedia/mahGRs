Preamble:- On the occasion of World Disability Day, the Central Government every year awards National Awards to outstanding employees with disabilities, outstanding entrepreneurs with disabilities, outstanding employers of persons with disabilities and officers of service schemes, excellent institutions working for persons with disabilities, etc. for their work. Since February 9, 1982, the Social Justice Department of the Government of Maharashtra has been giving awards annually to disabled employees, self-employed and outstanding employers under the scheme of State Awards for the Welfare of the Disabled. The Divyang Welfare State Award will be conferred on 3rd December. However, the General Administration Department has The circular dated 23rd November, 2016 has suggested that all departments should constitute a single selection committee at the department level/ ministry level to consider the recommendations received for the award. Reference No. A State Level Selection Committee has been constituted to make recommendations to the Central Government for National Awards under Sections 1 and 2. Accordingly, the matter of reconstitution of screening committee and state level committee for final selection of awardees and institutions for state awards for disabled welfare was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: As per the circular dated 23rd November, 2016, the Government has decided to recommend the awardee to the Central Government under the scheme of National Awards for the Welfare of Persons with Disabilities, in accordance with the procedure prescribed for the sanction and distribution of awards and the time schedule for the distribution of awards.
