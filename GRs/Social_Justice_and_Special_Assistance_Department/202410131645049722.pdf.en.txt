The Proposal:
To provide basic facilities to these units for development of SC / ST habitations / villages in urban and rural areas of the state. The Government has decided to implement a State Level Scheme namely "Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme" with effect from the year 2018 2018-19 to give Government approval to the works suggested by the people's representatives, vide the above Government Decision dated 09.03.2018. The activities to be taken up under the scheme include development of sites associated with eminent persons working for the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist sections, as well as places of cultural and historical importance, their monuments, and cultural development of the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist sections (e.g. Libraries, Vidhyakas, Dhamma Kendras, Vipassana Kendras, Training Centres) have been included under the Jashan Decision, dated 28th July, 2021. According to Ma.
The government was considering sanctioning works in urban and rural areas, in the habitations of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist communities, suggested by the representatives of the people's representatives.

The Collector should grant administrative sanction for these works. The executing agency should carry out the tendering process of the works as per the prevailing rules of the government.
4. It should be ensured that these works are completed within the approved budgeted cost limit.
No additional funds will be made available in any form if administrative approval is granted for works costing more than the approved budgeted cost.
5. The Government shall have the power to cancel or modify the said works by way of confirmation of the works which, if so requested in writing, are to be altered or not to be done for some unavoidable reason by the same public representatives who have suggested the said works.
6. Before commencing the works, it is mandatory for the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the concerned district to ensure that the works are within the Scheduled Caste and neo-Buddhist communities. The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the concerned district will be held responsible and disciplinary action will be taken if funds are distributed for works which are outside the habitations of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist elements.
7. Ensure that the Government decision of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme, dated 09.03.2018, dated 28.07.2021 and Adland dated 8.10.2021 is strictly adhered to.
8. The District Collector concerned shall ensure that no code of conduct for any general election / by-election is in force in the area of the sanctioned works before commencing these works.
9. The cost of these works shall be apportioned from the funds made available by the Finance Department for the scheme in the financial year 2024-25, subject to the following head of accounts.
