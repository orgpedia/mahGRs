GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to implement the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grant Scheme and other special assistance schemes, the Government hereby approves the continuation of a total of 1422 temporary posts from 01/09/2023 to 29/02/2024 subject to the condition that none of the temporary posts in the establishment of 35 District Collectors in the accompanying returns has been vacant for more than six months and has not expired. (Attached is the report)
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the provision made under the head Demand No.N-2, Head Accounts-SHIP-2053, District Administration (093) District Establishment (01) General Establishment, as approved for that financial year.
3. This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the powers conferred on the Administrative Department under Government Decisions No. 1 and 2 in the context of the Finance Department.
