Government's decision:
In the financial year 2021-22, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 550,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Hundred and Fifty Crores only) has been approved. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 110,00,00, 000/- has been disbursed by the Finance Department. Accordingly, for the Centrally Sponsored Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme, the amount sanctioned for the period April, 2021 to March, 2022 is Rs. The Government hereby approve the release of Rs. 110,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Ten Crores only) under this Government Resolution as per the accompanying prospectus. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
2. All the Collectors are informed that in the accompanying statement, Column No. 3 Funds have been distributed to the respective districts on the new CO Code. The grants distributed by them should be distributed to the taluks of their districts in proportion to the beneficiaries. The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
