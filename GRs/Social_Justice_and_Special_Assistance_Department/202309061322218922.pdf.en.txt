Introduction: The Overseas Scholarship Scheme has been implemented since 2003 to provide opportunities to Scheduled Caste students to study in reputed educational institutions abroad. The Rules of the Scheme have been amended from time to time and the revised Rules of the Scheme have been laid down vide Government Order dated 27.06.2017.
A total of 75 students belonging to Scheduled Castes from Maharashtra will be admitted to foreign educational institutions within the 300 QS World Rankings (QSWR) for postgraduate degree and PhD courses. Accordingly, the Government was considering the matter of selecting eligible students under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme for the year 2023-24 and sanctioning foreign scholarships to them.
Government Decision: Under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme for the Children of Scheduled Castes and Newly Weds, a total of 60 students of the selected form A-1, A-2, A-3, A-4, A-5, A-6, A-7, A-8, A-9, A-10 are being sanctioned this scholarship as per the recommendations of the selection committee constituted under the chairmanship of Secretary, Department of Social Justice, Ministry of Education, Mumbai, as per the decision of the Government dated 27th June, 2017.
