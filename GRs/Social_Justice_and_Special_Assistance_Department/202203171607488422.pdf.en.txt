GOVERNMENT DECISION: In pursuance of the Government Decision dated 27.10.2021, 353 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune have been sanctioned for continuation from 1.09.2021 to 28.02.2022.
2. Government Decision, Department of Finance dated 22.02.2022 has empowered all the Administrative Departments to extend the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 for the temporary posts included in the administrative department chart and all the existing temporary posts which were created after the approval of the previous review but have not been included in the chart.
Accordingly, the 353 temporary posts indicated in the accompanying prospectus A-to-C on the establishment of the Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune are being approved to be continued from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022 subject to the following terms and conditions: 1. The said extension is being granted subject to the assumption that other temporary posts, excluding executive posts, are not vacant for more than six months.
2. The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned subject to the terms and conditions to be followed.
(3) As from the date on which the officer/ employee holding the post of Superintendant is absorbed in the other posts, the Superintendents post shall stand automatically abolished.
