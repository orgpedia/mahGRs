Preamble: The Scholarship Scheme for Divyang students pursuing Post-Matric Education in Schools run by the Commissionerate of Disabled Welfare is being implemented through DBT system and the amount of scholarship has been approved to be deposited in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the students. The development of this DBT system through Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MAHAIT) has also been approved under the decision of the Government in the context.
For this scheme, the processing fee is charged from MahaIT for the process of filling up the online form from the students, verification and examination of the form from the district office, approval and then forwarding the payment to the login of the Commissionerate of Disabled Welfare, passing the payments and depositing the benefit of scholarship in the account of the beneficiaries. However, at present, there is no accounting head available for payment of processing fee. Therefore, the processing fee to be charged for the implementation of the Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Divyang students through the DBT Portal is being collected through Demand No. N-3, (08) (06) Scholarships to Physically Handicapped and Training in Industries (Programmes), 34 Scholarships/ Scholarships under the head 2235 2733.
Government Decision: As per the Government decision dated 02.08.2018, the Scholarship Scheme for Divyang students pursuing Post-Matric Education in Schools, being implemented by the Commissionerate of Welfare of Persons with Disabilities, is being implemented through DBT system and the amount of scholarship has been approved to be deposited in the Aadhaar linked bank account of the students. Further, the development of DBT system through Maharashtra Information Technology Corporation (MAHAIT) has been approved under the decision of the State Government.
