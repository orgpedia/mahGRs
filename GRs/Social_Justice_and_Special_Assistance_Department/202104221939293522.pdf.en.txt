GOVERNMENT DECISIONS, SOCIAL JUSTICE AND SPECIAL ASSISTANCE NO. VISSIO-2018/ No.62/ VISSIO, D. August 20, 2019.
Government Decision: In the financial year 2021-22, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 531,01,80, 000/- (Rupees Five Hundred Thirty One Crores One Lakh Eighty Thousand Only) has been approved. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 330,00,00, 000/- has been disbursed by the Finance Department. Accordingly, the Government hereby approves disbursement of the amount sanctioned for the period April, 2021 to March, 2022 for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme for general beneficiaries as per the accompanying statement under the Government decision of Rs. 330,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Three Hundred and Thirty Crores only). Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
2. All the Collectors are informed that in the accompanying statement, Column No. 3 Funds have been distributed to the respective districts on the new CO Code. The grants distributed by them should be distributed to the taluks of their districts in proportion to the beneficiaries. The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
The expenditure from the said fund should be spent under the head" Demand No.N-3, Main Account Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 104-Welfare of the Aged, Disabled and Destitute Persons (08) (09) Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grant Scheme (Programme) (2235 O 294) 50-Other Expenditure."
8. All the District Collectors are hereby informed to ensure that the said payments are deposited in the treasury. Also, they should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the subsidy given to them under any circumstances.

All the District Collectors are also informed that they should send a copy of the reconciliation statement to the Audit Office of this Department and to this Office by matching the expenditure made from the grants handed over to them with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's Office.
