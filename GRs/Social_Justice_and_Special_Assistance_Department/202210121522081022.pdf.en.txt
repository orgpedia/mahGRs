The State Level Project Appraisal-cum-Convergence Committees (PACCs) will play a key role in the successful implementation of the scheme as per the guidelines of the Central Government and will be responsible for the evaluation of the Annual Action Plan, its monitoring with convergence at all levels. Section No. For the schemes mentioned in Section 2, the concerned Regional Offices under this Department shall prepare an Annual Action Plan in the prescribed manner as mentioned in the guidelines. The Annual Action Plan will be submitted to the Project Appraisal-cum-Convergence Committees (PACC) at the state level for approval.
Based on the guidelines of the Project Appraisal-cum-Convergence Committees (PACC). The Annual Action Plan (AAP) 2 and 3 will be approved.
The Annual Action Plan approved by the Project Appraisal-cum-Convergence Committees (PACC) will be sent to the Central Government for approval and availability of funds.
