The proposal for grant of recognition to Sumangal Matimand School for Boys and Eklavya Vocational Training Centre in Sanchali, Raigad, on grant-in-aid basis as a special case, was under consideration of the government.

The terms and conditions are as follows: 1) The terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time shall apply to the said School/ Office.
2) The School/ Workshop is being recognized on grant-in-aid basis subject to the approval of the Finance Department and the High Level Committee of Secretaries on Post-Graduation Grants and Posts.
3) The School is affiliated to the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. The terms and conditions as per paragraphs 1 to 27 of the Government Order issued under 8.4.2015 shall continue to apply.
4) The government decided to inspect this special school. As per Form-B dated 8.4.2015, it should be done every year and as per Form-C, the Divyang school should be evaluated and scored and accordingly the concerned special school should be made eligible for the grant.
(5) If it is found that the special schools/ workshops for the disabled are receiving regular grants from the Central Government, local bodies or other institutions, the institution will be ineligible to receive the grant from the State Government.
(6) If it is found that the terms and conditions and procedures prescribed by the Government from time to time have not been complied with, the recognition of the institution shall be cancelled.
7) The school is being recognized on a grant basis with a sanctioned strength of 75 Non-Residential Students. The proposal regarding the staff required as per the figure 18.8.2004 should be submitted through the Finance Department for the approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries. Without this recognition, these posts should not be given recognition.
