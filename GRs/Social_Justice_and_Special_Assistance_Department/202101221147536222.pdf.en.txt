The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. As the financial position of the State is likely to be very critical due to COVID-19, the Controlling Authority should take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner while spending the funds. It should be ensured that the funds will be spent this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made, account-wise/ sub-account-wise and material objective-wise information achieved, the utilization certificate of the fund, the concerned field officer should ensure that it is sent to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, the concerned Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department and Agriculture and Padum Department by the 10th of every month.
