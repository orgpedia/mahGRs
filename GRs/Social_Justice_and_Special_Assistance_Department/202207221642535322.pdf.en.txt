The memo:
Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is informed that the proposal for purchase of books under Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme of the Government for self-sufficiency along with self-sufficiency for Scheduled Castes and New Buddhists in the State has been approved under the Memorandum of Government in the context of 36.00 crore (Rs.
At present, an expenditure of Rs. 18.00 crore (Rs.18 crore only) was approved for the financial year 2021-22. Only 21 districts have collected Rs. 10.50 crore and 15 districts have not. Therefore, the remaining amount of Rs. 25.50 crore is being made available for the scheme of purchase of books under Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana in the financial year 2022-23.
The Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer and Drawing and Controlling Officer for the expenditure of this fund.
The expenditure on this scheme should be met from the funds budgeted for the year 2022-23 by incurring expenditure under the following heads:
Demand No.N-3 Main Account Head 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities
Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme (04) (01) Bharat Ratna Dr. J.O. (Program) 27, Small Constructions 2225 F085
The Controlling Officer shall be responsible for spending the funds subject to verification by the Controlling Officer of the utilization certificates of the previous year's expenditure under the above scheme.
