Introduction: Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute, Pune (BARTI), has invited applications for admission to 200 students. The exam was conducted on 25th December 2021.
The application of 3500 students who needed training had come to Aurangabad for this screening test. Around 2800 students appeared for the exam. This shows that there is a dire need for training for students belonging to the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist sections.
The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) conducts various competitive examinations for the state services, combined and class C. Around 8 to 10 lakh students from Maharashtra are appearing for the examination. At least 15 per cent of the students belonging to the Scheduled Castes (SCs) constitute at least 1 lakh students. At present, the only centre in the institute is the Sambodhi Academy run by Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute, Pune (BARTI) in the state- the Sambodhi Competition Pre-Examination Training Centre at Aurangabad.
