Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as Controlling Officer and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the concerned district as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending the said fund.
The Controlling Officer shall spend the funds (Rs. 17.50 crore) in Raka 4 of the aforesaid statement for the said scheme. Once the funds are spent, the details and utilisation certificates should be submitted to the government without fail.

The expenditure on this scheme should be met from the funds budgeted for the year 2022-23 by incurring expenditure under the following heads:
Demand No.N-3 Main Account Head 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities
Welfare of Scheduled Castes (04) Financial Assistance for Tools and Machinery (04) (01) Financial Assistance to Self Help Groups for procurement of Mini Tractors (Programme) 31, Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payable) 2225 D 331
The Controlling Officer shall be responsible for spending the funds subject to verification by the Controlling Officer of the utilization certificates of the previous year's expenditure under the above scheme. Further, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune shall be responsible for ensuring that no financial irregularity takes place by spending the said funds as per the terms and conditions issued by the Finance Department from time to time as per the Financial Rules, Maharashtra Budget Rulebook.
The said Memorandum is being issued pursuant to the powers conferred on the Administrative Department by the Department of Finance, Government Resolution No: Finance-2022/ PR No. 43/ Finance-3, dated 04.04.2022.
