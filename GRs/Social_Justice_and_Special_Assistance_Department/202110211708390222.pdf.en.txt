The Proposal:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Atal Vayu Abhyudaya Yojana (AVYAY), implementing agencies are required to be selected for starting/ setting up of new old age homes/ continuing care homes in the State. The selection of the agency for setting up the old age home (SrCH) will be determined by the selection. For this, the government was considering to form a committee at the state level as per the guidelines of the central government.
Government Decision Under Atal Vayu Abhyudaya Yojana, a State Level Committee is being constituted to select implementing agencies for starting/ setting up of new old age homes/ Continuous Care Homes for senior citizens in the State as follows.

| 6    | Dr. Ajay Dudhane, (Aged Care Specialist/ Distinguished Social Worker) | the members          |
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
The Committee will examine the proposals received as per the guidelines of the Central Government and select implementing agencies for new old age homes/ continuing care homes for senior citizens.
