Read:- Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government Decision No. Aasthaap 2017/ No.264/ Aastha-1, dated 25th June, 2019.
Introduction: Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi) was established by the Government of India. The Company has been registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 on 25/06/2018.
Under the government decision, the Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi) and the subjects and schemes related to the Maratha community were transferred from the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department as well as the Higher and Technical Education Department to the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department.
The government was considering transferring all welfare schemes related to the Sarathi and Maratha community (SEBC category) from the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department to the Planning Department.
Government Decision: The welfare schemes related to the Maratha community, including the following two welfare schemes related to the Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi) and the Maratha community (SEBC category), are being transferred from the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department to the Planning Department. (Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Scheme) 2. The budgetary provisions available in respect of the above transferred subjects will be immediately disbursed by the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department after the proposal is submitted by the Planning Department. However, the planning department should immediately take a separate accounting title for this. The Planning Department will henceforth handle the other aspects like taking the head of accounts for making provisions and making necessary budgetary provisions under it, making supplementary demands, disbursement of funds. All the documents and documents related to this subject should be taken over by the Planning Department from the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department.
