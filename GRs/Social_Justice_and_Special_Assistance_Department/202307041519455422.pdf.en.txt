Introduction: The Social Justice Department runs government hostels for backward class students and government residential schools for scheduled caste and neo-Buddhist boys and girls in the state. Under the scheme for providing facilities to educational institutions of backward classes, the government was considering giving administrative approval for the purchase of essential items for the government hostels and hostels attached to residential schools.
Government's decision:
As per the proposal submitted by the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune under reference No. 5 and 6, under the facilities to be provided to the students admitted in the Government Hostels for Backward Class Children under the Department of Social Justice and the Hostels Department attached to the Government Residential Schools, a total of Rs. Administrative Sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 52,35,56, 243/- (Rupees Fifty Two Crores Thirty Five Lakhs Fifty Six Thousand Two Hundred and Forty Three only).
