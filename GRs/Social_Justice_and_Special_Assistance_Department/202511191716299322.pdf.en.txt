-: Government Corrigendum: -
The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government of Chhattisgarh, under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, decided to budget for the construction of residential quarters for the children of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar Regional Division for the teaching / non-teaching staff of Government Residential Schools functioning in the State. Administrative approval has been granted under 11.06.2025 and the condition number (s) in the said Government decision is as follows: -
f) The Integrated Construction Management Cell, Tribal Development Department / Social Justice and Special Assistance Department established under the Tribal Development Department dated 13.02.2025 will be operational as the operational mechanism for the present constructions.
The above Condition No. (o) is being amended as follows.
The operational mechanism for the present constructions will be Maharashtra-State Infrastructure Development Corporation (MSIDC), Mumbai.
(2) The other provisions of the Government Decision shall remain in force.
The Government Corrigendum is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
