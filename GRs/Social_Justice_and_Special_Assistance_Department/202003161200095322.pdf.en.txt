The Proposal:
Ashramshalas are run by giving grants to voluntary organizations through this department. In such ashramshalas, employees are appointed by voluntary organizations. Also, the appointed employees work in the ashramshala of the same institution from the time of appointment till retirement. Considering this, the employees of the ashramshalas of the department are not transferable.
2) Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Incentive Allowance announced by the Government under the Government Decision dated 3/12/2007 has been made applicable to the teaching/ non-teaching staff of all Ashramshalas for the children belonging to Vimukt Jati/ Nomadic Tribes in the Naxal affected areas and their districts. However, since the employees of the ashramshalas of the department are not transferable, the matter of reviewing the benefit of incentive allowance/ one-tier promotion given to such employees was under the consideration of the government. Accordingly, the School Education and Sports Department, the General Administration Department and the Finance Department were consulted.
Government's decision:
School Education Dept. As per the Government Decision dated 14/8/2008, the Government from time to time

And one-time promotion/ incentive payable to Naxal affected areas cannot be applied. " The comments said.
(3) In view of the provisions of the Government Decision of the School Education and Sports Department and the above-mentioned views of the Finance Department and the General Administration Department, the employees of the Ashramshalas working in the Primary/ Secondary and Higher Secondary/ Vidyaniketan/ Ashramshalas for the children of the workers of this Department are not transferable and the one-time promotion/ incentive payable to the employees of tribal and Naxalite affected areas is not admissible to them. Accordingly, appropriate action should be taken by the concerned Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare and Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare within the next 30 days and a report should be submitted to this department.
