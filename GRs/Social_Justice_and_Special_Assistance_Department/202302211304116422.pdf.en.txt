Government Decision: In the financial year 2022-23, for the implementation of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme for general beneficiaries, Rs. 540,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Five Hundred and Forty Crores only) and sanctioned by way of Supplementary Demands in the Monsoon Session 2022 amounting to Rs. 780,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Seven Hundred Eighty Crores only) aggregating to Rs. 1320,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Thousand Three Hundred and Twenty Crores only) has been sanctioned. For the Rs. 1100,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Eleven Hundred Crores only) has been distributed to all the District Collectors as per the Government decision in Reference No. 2 and 3.
Now the Finance Department has sanctioned Rs. 220,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Crores only) has been approved. Accordingly, for the period April, 2022 to March, 2023, an amount of Rs. 220,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred and Twenty Crores only) is hereby approved by the Government to be distributed as per the accompanying prospectus under this Government Order. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
2. All the Collectors are informed that in the accompanying statement, Column No. 3 Funds have been distributed to the respective districts on the new CO Code. The grants distributed by them should be distributed to the taluks of their districts in proportion to the beneficiaries.
The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
