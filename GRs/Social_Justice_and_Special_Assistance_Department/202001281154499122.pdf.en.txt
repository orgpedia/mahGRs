Government Decision: A budgetary provision of Rs. 200,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred Crores only) has been approved for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme for the financial year 2019-20. As per the Government Circular in Reference No. 2 of the Department of Finance, 60% of the total outlay of Rs. 120,00,00, 000/- for the period April to December, 2019 for the Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme has been released under the Government Decision in Reference No. 4 and 5. Now the Finance Department has approved the release of 100 percent i.e. the remaining 40 percent funds.
Accordingly, for the period from November, 2019 to January, 2020, an amount of Rs. 80,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Eighty Crores only) is hereby approved by the Government to be distributed as per the accompanying prospectus. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
