The Proposal:
The Bill provides for constitution of a Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board at the State level and at the Divisional level for the purpose of protecting the rights and welfare of transgenders in the State. The decision was taken on December 13, 2018. The composition, functions and objectives of the Board have been prescribed by this Government. Accordingly, the matter of appointing members to the Maharashtra State Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision
The Bill provides for constitution of a Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board at the State level and at the Divisional level for the purpose of protecting the rights and welfare of transgenders in the State. The decision was taken on December 13, 2018. The following members are being appointed to the Transgender Rights Protection and Welfare Board at the State level as per the Board structure prescribed under the said Government Decision.
