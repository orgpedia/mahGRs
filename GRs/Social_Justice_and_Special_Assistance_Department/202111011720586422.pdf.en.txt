Government Decision: Sahityaratna Loktantra Annabhau Sathe Development Corporation (MIRYA. Various schemes are implemented for the economic upliftment of the Matang community through Mumbai. The Corporation has been sanctioned 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) for the financial year 2021-2022 for meeting the expenditure on salaries. A budgetary provision of Rs 20,00,00 thousand has been made.
Out of this, the Finance Department has made available 60% limit on the Beams System of Rs. The Government is approving the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 12,00,00 thousand (Rupees Twelve Crores only) subject to the following conditions:
1) Restrictions set by the government for spending in non-plan funds should be strictly implemented.
2) Limit the cost of operations.
3) The details of the amount spent on various matters and the expenditure incurred on it should be submitted to the government.
4) The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the rate of recovery of loans.
5) Reduce the cost of living.
The Deputy Secretary (Corporations), Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Mantralaya, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer in respect of this managerial grant. The amount is being handed over to the Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Navi Mumbai for further action. They are being empowered to disburse this grant to Sahityaratna Lokshahir Anna Bhai Sathe Vikas Nigam through payment in the form of M.C.N.44.
