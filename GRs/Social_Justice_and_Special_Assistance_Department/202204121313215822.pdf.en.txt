Government Decision: For the financial year 2022-23, a budgetary provision of Rs. 111,85,73, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eleven Crore Eighty Five Lakh Seventy Three Thousand only) has been approved for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Subsidy Scheme for District Establishments and Rs. 42,51,22, 000/- (Rupees Forty Two Crore One Lakh Twenty Two Thousand only) for the Establishments on the Scheme for Financial Assistance to Old Landless Agricultural Labourers. Out of which, 10% of the approved budgetary provision by the Department of Finance has been disbursed on BIMS system for the purpose of 01-Salary. Accordingly, the Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 10,80,11, 100/- (Rupees Ten Crore Eighty Eight Lakhs Eleven Thousand Hundred only) for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grants Scheme District Establishment and Rs. 4,21,97, 300/- (Rupees Four Crore Twenty One Lakh Ninety Nine Thousand Three Hundred only) for the establishment on the scheme for old landless agricultural labourers to all the Divisional Commissioner's Offices for the period of May, April and May, 2022 for the purpose of 01-salary, as per the statement accompanying this Government decision.
All the Divisional Commissioners are hereby informed that they should allocate the grants distributed as per the prospectus A and B attached herewith to the districts under their jurisdiction as per their requirement and the District Collectors should allocate the grants to the taluks of their districts as per their requirement.
