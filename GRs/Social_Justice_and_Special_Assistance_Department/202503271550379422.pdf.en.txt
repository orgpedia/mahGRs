The Proposal:
The Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Quality Scholarship Scheme for higher education in reputed educational institutions in the country has been implemented by Government Decision Social Justice, Cultural Affairs, CRODAB Special Assistance Department No.EBC-2001 / P.No.323 / Mavak-II, dated 19th June, 2003 to enable SC (including neo-Buddhists) students to acquire the necessary skills and knowledge and prepare them for the competitive era in various fields. The revised Rules of the Scheme have been implemented vide Government Decision dated 08.11.2017 superseding all earlier Government Decisions regarding the Rules of the Scheme.
The Selection Committee headed by the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, as mentioned in the said Government Decision, conducted the selection process for the students admitted to reputed educational institutions in the country for the year 2024-25 and accordingly, as per the terms and conditions prescribed in the application received by it, a total of 29 students have been awarded Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship.

The Government's Decisions:
Government Decision for UST Shahu Maharaj Quality Scholarship for SC Students to pursue higher education in reputed educational institutions in the country, dated 08th November, 2017 A total of 32 eligible students selected in the meetings of the Selection Committee dated 13.01.2025 and dated 28.02.2025 are being approved under Forms-A-1, A-2 and Form-A-3 and Forms-B-1 and B-2 attached with the Government Decision for Rajshi Shahu Maharaj Quality Scholarship for Year 2024-25.
The full tuition fee and examination fee fixed by the respective educational institution for the students for whom the scholarship has been sanctioned shall be paid to the institution through the Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune. Students will be paid in full by the educational institution as per their hostel and meal charges. The amount of this expense will be paid only once in a year. A total of Rs.10,000 / - will be given every year for course books, educational materials and other educational expenses. The cost of their course should be paid to the college / institution concerned by seeking information from the university / institution concerned regarding tuition fees, examination fees, hostel rent, subsistence allowance, and other allowable expenses for the year 2024-25 and obtaining a certificate of student's progress / attendance.
