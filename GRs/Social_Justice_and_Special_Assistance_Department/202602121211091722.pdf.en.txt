Terms and Conditions of Distribution of Ration: -
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1. | The beneficiary should be a sugarcane farmer and the concerned Gram Sevak should have an identity card / certificate.                                                                                                                                             |
| 2. | In order to avail the benefit, a requisition form in the prescribed format will have to be filled up with the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare Department.                                                                                                  |
| 3. | At the district level, there will be an Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare Coordinating Officer (Nodal Officer).                                                                                                                                              |
| 4. | The committee headed by the District Collector should include District Social Welfare Officer, Zilla Parishad as well as Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, etc.                                                                                             |
| 5. | The e-tendering / GEM system should be adopted from the headquarters level of the Social Justice Department or the corporation to provide ration for the sugarcane workers and their families.                                                                    |
| 6. | The quality of the item in the ration kit will need to be checked by a government-approved laboratory before a tender for ration distribution can be accepted.                                                                                                    |
| 7. | All expenses like supply, transportation, storage, insurance, distribution, photo, manpower supply, etc., except for Goods and Services Tax, will be included in the rate of ration.                                                                              |
| 8. | It will be necessary to have the name of the corporation engraved on the items in the ration kit itself.                                                                                                                                                          |
| 9. | After the selected beneficiary receives the ration itself, the payments will be made to the head office of the corporation after receiving a receipt from the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, with a proposal to pay the payment on their recommendation. |

The procedure to be followed
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1. | The eligible beneficiaries will be selected through a committee constituted at the district level under the chairmanship of the District Collector constituted by the Government Decision dated 4th August 2021. |
| 2. | The beneficiary selection committee will consist of Assistant Commissioner, Member Secretary Social Welfare and Social Welfare Officer as Zila Parishad member.                                                  |
| 3. | Since the corporation does not have a separate district office at the district level, the scheme will be implemented through the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare.                                         |
| 4. | Benefits of the scheme will be distributed in the form of commodities to the beneficiaries selected by the Eligible Beneficiary Selection Committee.                                                             |
| 5. | The Assistant Commissioner Social Welfare concerned will be responsible for ensuring that there is no irregularity in the distribution of ration itself.                                                         |
