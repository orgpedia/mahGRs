Constitution of a Committee to recommend policies for redevelopment of housing societies under the Backward Classes Housing Scheme (PWR-219).
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance
Government Resolution No: Parichay-2020/ PR No.125/ AJAK
Ministry Extension, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date is September 11, 2020.
Reference: Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government Resolution No. BCH-2008/ No. 187/ Mavak-2, dated 26th June, 2009.
Preamble: Regarding Backward Classes Housing Scheme (PWR-219). In the meeting held on 09.09.2020 under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Social Justice), the Committee was constituted to recommend the policy for redevelopment of the said housing societies. The Minister has given instructions. Accordingly, the government had constituted the committee.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: A Committee is being constituted to recommend the policy for redevelopment of the said housing societies under the Backward Classes Housing Scheme (PWR-219) as under:

In respect of redevelopment of Backward Class Housing Societies sanctioned under the scheme in Maharashtra, the societies will be given enhanced floor area (FSI) or transferable development rights (TD) for redevelopment. The Committee shall recommend the proportion of members belonging to Backward Classes and Other Backward Classes in respect of the increased area received as per the DR and the additional flats/ flats etc. received for sale accordingly and other ancillary matters.
