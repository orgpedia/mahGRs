A total of 54 social work colleges functioning under the Social Justice Department have been approved in the state of Maharashtra, out of which 1 is closed, 3 are unaided and 50 colleges are functioning. Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance
The post of principal in most of the social work colleges is vacant and proposals have been received by the Commissionerate for getting No Objection Certificate to fill it. It has also been directed that since the post of regular principal is not filled, the college cannot apply to the NAAC committee and therefore, they are not being evaluated. Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune
In a letter dated 4 to 6, the Finance Department had proposed to fill the vacant posts of principal in Dhanaji Nana Chaudhary Vidya Prabodhini Sanchalan, Public Service Madhukar Rao Chaudhary College of Social Work, Jalgaon, Nirmala Niketan College of Social Work, Mumbai. The posts of Principal of Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru College of Social Work, Amarnath, G. Jalgaon and Kumbhalkar College of Social Work, Wardha have also been filled.

A proposal was submitted for approval. When the proposal was presented to the sub-committee of the Finance Department, it was under the consideration of the government to fill up 2 vacant posts of principals and post-facto approval of 2 posts in the Social Work College as per the approval of the sub-committee.
Government's decision:
As per the approval of the Sub-Committee constituted to review the sanctioned posts in the Administrative Department and the offices under it and to fix the revised figure, the Government is giving ex-post facto approval to 2 posts of Principals and filling up of 2 vacancies in the following nominated Social Work Colleges functioning under the Department of Social Justice.
(a) Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru College of Social Work, Amarnath, Dist. Dr. Dilip Sukhlal Bhavsar, who has been serving as the Principal since December 13, 2017. Retired on 19th May, 2019 (Post facto ratification of appointment).
Kumbhalkar College of Social Work, Wardha. As of March 27, 2018, Dr.
Chandu Kashinath Popatkar, was appointed (giving ex-post facto approval to the appointment).
