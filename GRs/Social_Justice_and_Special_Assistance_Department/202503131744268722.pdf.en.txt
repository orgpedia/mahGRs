Government Decision: - The Government hereby approves the continuation of a total of 1422 temporary posts from date 01/03/2025 to date 31/08/2025 subject to the condition that none of the temporary posts at the establishment of the Collector of 35 districts in the accompanying return for implementation of the Sanjay Gandhi Nirbhadra Grants-in-aid Scheme and other Special Assistance Scheme has become vacant for more than six months. (Added to the statement)
The expenditure for this matter shall be met from the provision approved for that financial year under the head Demand No. N-2, Head of Accounts-2053, District Administration (093) District Establishment (01) General Establishment.
3. This Government Decision is being issued in accordance with the authorities received by the Administrative Division under the Government Decision No. 1 with respect to the Finance Department.
The decision is available on the Government of Maharashtra's www.maharashtra.gov.in website.
