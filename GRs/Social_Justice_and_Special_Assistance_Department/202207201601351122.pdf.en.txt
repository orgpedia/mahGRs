Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made, account head-wise/ sub-account head-wise and material objective-wise information achieved, fund utilization certificate, the concerned field officer should ensure that it is sent to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, the concerned Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department and Revenue and Forest Department by the 10th of every month.
The funds should be deposited in the SNA account through the PFMS system and the account department should ensure this.
The guidelines issued by the Central Government from time to time in respect of Centrally Sponsored Schemes being implemented through PFMS system should be followed.
