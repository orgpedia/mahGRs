Preamble: In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring in more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government has been directed to adopt a revised methodology as per the Office Memorandum dated 09.03.2022 under reference. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued revised instructions on Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Circular No. 2. Accordingly, a Central Nodal Agency (CNA) is required to be appointed for the implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and a separate single bank account is required to be opened in the authorized bank for that scheme. It has also been directed to appoint at least a Deputy Secretary level officer as the nodal officer for coordination of these centrally sponsored schemes. Accordingly, the Government was considering the appointment of a Central Nodal Agency (CNA) and a Nodal Officer for the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Scheme for Implementation of Persons with Disabilities Act 2016 (SIPDA).
Government Decision: Joint/ Deputy Secretary (Welfare of Persons with Disabilities), Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance is being appointed as the Central Nodal Agency (CNA) for implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Scheme for Implementation of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (SIPDA).
