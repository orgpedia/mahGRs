Under 08/08/2019, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (MAHAJYOTHI) has been established on the lines of SARATHI to implement various activities for the youth belonging to Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes.
2. Read the Government Resolution No. For the financial year 2020-21, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti), Nagpur has been allotted a total of Rs. A sum of Rs. 10.00 crore has been sanctioned. In this decision, it was mentioned that prior approval of the government is required to implement the activities/ schemes.
As per the provisions of the Memorandum of Association and the Articles of Association of the company, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti), it was necessary to take decisions regarding the implementation of various schemes/ projects and spending of funds for them with the approval of the Board of Directors. Accordingly, the Planning Department has issued a notification for the institution. The matter of issuing appropriate orders on the lines of 15/10/2020 for the organization Mahajyoti was under consideration of the Government.
The Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti) will have autonomy in implementing activities and welfare schemes with the approval of the" Board of Directors" to achieve the objectives set out in the" Memorandum of Association" of the company. After taking into account the funds available in the financial year, the necessary activities and welfare schemes can be selected and implemented from the incidental or ancillary objectives mentioned in the Memorandum of Association with the approval of the Board of Directors and similar activities and welfare schemes can be implemented by the concerned administrative department.
