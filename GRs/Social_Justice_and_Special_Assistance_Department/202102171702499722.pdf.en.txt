Preamble: A dedicated helpline has been developed to deal with drug-related crimes/ abuse etc. in the state, as well as to provide assistance to the general public and counseling by experts.
2. Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Govt. As per the letter dated May 8, 2020, the existing National Toll Free Helpline No. 1800-11-31 (2407) for Maharashtra related Alcoholism and Abuse Prevention calls have been agreed to be diverted/ used for Mumbai based call centre.
Accordingly, in coordination with the Department of Social Justice, Narcotics Control Bureau, Mumbai Division, a call center will be set up to address complaints related to drug addiction in the state of Maharashtra. A State Level Standing Committee was proposed to be constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Maharashtra State.
Government Decision In coordination with the Department of Social Justice and the Narcotics Control Bureau, Mumbai Division, to start and properly operate a call center to address complaints related to drug addiction in the state of Maharashtra. The next State Level Standing Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of Chief Secretary, Maharashtra.
