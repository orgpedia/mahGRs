2. The Managing Director, Loknayak Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Harvesting Workers Welfare Corporation, Pune, on receipt of the said order, shall issue immediate supply orders to the Purbathadara.
3. Supplies of required materials / items shall be made at their own cost within the time limit specified in the tender from the date of receipt of supply order. If the supplier does not deliver within the stipulated time, penal action will be taken against the supplier as per the terms and conditions of the tender.
4. Within 8 days of the receipt of the Purbatha order by the Supplier Organization, the Managing Director, Lokneta Gopinath Munde signed an agreement with Sugarcane Harvesting Workers Corporation, Pune for payment of Rs. It will be mandatory to sign the contract on stamp paper of 500 / -.
5. Supplier agencies shall submit samples to the Managing Director, Lokneta Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Workers Corporation, Pune as per the requirement of the goods to be supplied. Whether the material / item required for the office of the corporation is as per the standard in this Act, they should get the items inspected by the approved agency as per the provision prescribed under the Government Decision, dated 1.12.2016 of the Industries, Power and Labour Department.
6. No amount shall be paid for the State Level Office of Lokneta Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Harvesting Workers Corporation, Pune, for the Divisional Office at Wab Parli, District Beed, other than the sanctioned price as the rates of materials / articles required are inclusive of all taxes and expenses. Therefore, WASNT will be responsible for paying government taxes as determined by the government, including GST. Submission of invoices for supply of materials / goods will be required to separately mention the cost of goods as well as GST and the amount of all taxes, if admissible. Also, the cost per item / material includes the cost of installation / repair, transportation, etc.
7. Supplier shall take delivery challan of receipt of goods / materials after delivery of the above mentioned materials to the respective offices along with the dated signature and stamp of the Head of the concerned office.
