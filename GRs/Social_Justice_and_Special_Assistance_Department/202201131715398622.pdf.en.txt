AMRUT Society Resolution No. 1, d. 21.12.2021 Preamble: The Maharashtra Research and Development and Training Academy (AMRUT) has been established for the development of students, youth and other candidates belonging to economically weaker sections of the open category through various activities, programmes, etc. It was established on 22.08.2019. The Institute is an autonomous body with its head office at World Trade Centre, Mumbai.
