Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI), Pune provides pre-examination training to the brightest and hardworking candidates belonging to the Scheduled Castes of Maharashtra State under the scheme of UPSC, MPSC, IBPS and similar competitive examinations. In this regard, Reference No. Under Section 2 of the Act, the Government was contemplating to implement the e-tendering process for selecting the service provider agency required to conduct the entrance examination for conducting the UPSC, MPSC and IBPS Competitive Examination Preparatory Training for the year 2021.
The decision was taken by Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI), Pune. In the 29th Governing Council Meeting held on 22/01/2021, administrative approval is hereby accorded for e-tendering of Service Provider for conducting UPSC, MPSC and IBPS Competitive Examination Preparatory Training for the year 2021 for pre-preparatory training of MPSC, UPSC, IBPS and similar competitive examinations.
