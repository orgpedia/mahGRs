Preamble: To develop the habitations/ villages of the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist communities in the urban and rural areas of the State and to provide basic amenities to these communities. The Government has decided to implement Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana at the State level from the year 2018-19 to provide recognition to the works suggested by the people's representatives at the government level. Under this scheme, the government was considering to give in-principle approval for the construction of a cultural building, Vipassana Center, Multi-purpose center at Mauje Ghodbunder Survey No. 233 by Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, District Thane under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme.
Government Decision: Under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme, the Mira Bhayandar Municipal Corporation, District Thane, is giving in-principle approval for the construction of a cultural building, Vipassana Center Multi-Purpose Center at Mauje Ghodbunder Survey No. 233, subject to the recommendation of the concerned Chief Engineer (Architecture) and Chief Engineer (Electrical).
The work is being sanctioned at a cost of Rs. 13,85,23, 900/-. The said expenditure is being sanctioned as per the financial powers conferred under paragraphs 134 (3) of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-V, Maharashtra Public Works Rules, dated 20th September, 2019 and Government Circular, Public Works Department, dated 25th September, 2019.
After receiving the recommendation of the Chief Engineer (Architecture) and Chief Engineer (Electricity) Konkan Division regarding the budget of the said work, the Assistant Commissioner, Thane, through the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, should submit a proposal to the government.
