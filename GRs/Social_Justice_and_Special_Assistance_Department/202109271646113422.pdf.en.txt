Introduction: In order to provide stable life to the people of the Vimukta Jati Nomadic Tribe community of Maharashtra State, to raise their living standards and to make them economically self-sufficient, Reference No. Vasantrao Naik Tanda Habitat Improvement Scheme is being implemented under 01. Also Reference No. According to 03, the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme is being implemented. The works to be taken up under these schemes are submitted to the government through the committee of the concerned collector. Such works are given administrative approval and funding from the government level. However, in respect of prioritization of works to be taken up under the said scheme, Reference No. Under Section 4, a committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the concerned Taluk Public Fund. Similarly, to implement the scheme more effectively, a committee under the chairmanship of a person from the Banjara community is being demanded to be formed to suggest to the District Collector the works to be undertaken under the Tanda Vasti Sudhar Yojana and Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasant Yojana in 11 Banjara-dominated districts of the state (Yavatmal, Jalna, Nanded, Parbhani, Beed, Solapur, Aurangabad, Latur, Hingoli, Buldana and Washim). Therefore, to suggest works to be taken up under Vasantrao Naik Tanda/ Vasti Sudhar and Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme, Reference No. The matter of reforming the committee formed under section 4 was under the consideration of the government.
The government has decided to form a committee under the chairmanship of a person belonging to the Banjara community in 11 Banjara-dominated districts (Yavatmal, Jalna, Nanded, Parbhani, Beed, Solapur, Aurangabad, Latur, Hingoli, Buldana and Washim) of the state to suggest to the committee headed by the concerned collector the works to be undertaken under the Vasantrao Naik Tanda/ Vasti Sudhar and Yashwantrao Chavan Mukt Vasant Yojana.

The proposal regarding the election of the chairman and members of the said committee should be submitted to the government by the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the district.
The above committee will take action according to the prevailing policy of the government while selecting the works to be undertaken.
The work has not been completed as yet. It should not be started or finished. Such projects should not be funded by any other scheme. The work to be undertaken in the schemes should not be of a particular type. While selecting the works, it should be ensured that there is a balanced development of the habitation/ market. The works to be undertaken should be submitted to the committee of the concerned Collector through the above committee. Also, the works approved by the committee as per the government policy should be considered on priority by the committee of the Collector.
The decision was taken in 11 districts. The Committee constituted as on 23.02.2021 stands abolished. The government has also decided to implement the same in other districts. The Committee will be as per the provisions mentioned in 23.02.2021.
