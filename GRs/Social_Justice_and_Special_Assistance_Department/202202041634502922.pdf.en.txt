Government circular:
Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department has been established under Government Notification General Administration Department dated 09/03/2017. Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department is the department implementing the social scheme. The amount of subsidy to be disbursed under the social service and the amount to be deposited must be declared by the scheme-wise Drawing and Disbursing Officer as well as the Controller.
Under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, the scheme and sub-accounts for the financial year 2022-23 are being declared as per Form-A.
