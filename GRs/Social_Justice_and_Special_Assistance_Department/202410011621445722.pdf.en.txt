The Proposal: -
The Overseas Scholarship Scheme has been implemented since 2003 to enable Scheduled Caste students to pursue education in an institution of higher education abroad.
The Rules of the Scheme have been amended from time to time, read No. The revised rules of the scheme have been prescribed by Government Decisions 1 and 6.
Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Overseas Scholarship has been sanctioned to a total of 75 students belonging to Scheduled Castes of Maharashtra who will be admitted to an educational institution abroad within 200 QS World Rankings for postgraduate degree and PhD courses, as proposed by the recommendation of the Selection Committee under the Government Decision dated 30.08.2024. In addition, the provision in the Government Decision dated 10.3.2021 provides that if, after the number of eligible students approved under the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Overseas Scholarship Scheme has been selected, some students seek cancellation of their selection under the State Government Overseas Scholarship Scheme for various reasons, the State Government shall have the power to select the eligible students in the same selection year in order of merit from the waiting list of eligible students as recommended by the Selection Committee for such vacancies.

The Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune vide his letter dated 13.09.2024 proposes to cancel the selection of a total of 4 students selected from the government level for the scholarship scheme on the grounds of furnishing false information in the Government Decision dated 27th June, 2017. As the selection of 4 students from the general waiting list is in order for these 4 vacancies to be filled, it is proposed to cancel the selection of 4 students mentioned in the table below and issue a government decision for the selection of 4 students from the general waiting list.
In pursuance of this proposal, the Government was contemplating to cancel the selection of a total of 4 students as proposed by the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune and select the names of the students from the general waiting list under Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Overseas Scholarship Scheme (ID1) for special studies abroad for children belonging to SC / ST / neo-Buddhist category.

The government should take action to sanction the permissible scholarship to the student concerned only by taking the guarantees prescribed under the decision and checking the original documents of the students.
The Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune is also being empowered to grant scholarships to the students concerned for the duration of Course B mentioned in the Government Decision and to sanction scholarships payable accordingly in case of a drop or decrease in tuition fees of a university during the period of study abroad of the students.
4. Students who have been granted foreign scholarships by the government are required to serve the country after completing their education and make the most of their knowledge. Accordingly, it will be mandatory for the student concerned to write a letter of guarantee (301S) before sanctioning the scholarship.
A student selected for a foreign scholarship must complete a master's degree or PhD abroad within the prescribed time. Under no circumstances will the student be paid an extension or an additional cost.
6. The scholarship amount shall be fully recovered from the student concerned if the student interchangeably changes the place or course of study at the educational institution, university or course in which the student is approved to pursue higher education.
(a) The amount of the total cost of the course for which the scholarship has been sanctioned and the amount of fees, maintenance allowance and other incidental charges for the first year or the second year of the students for whom the scholarship has been sanctioned shall be paid by the Commissioner, Social Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune, after obtaining certified information from the university concerned, to the university concerned and the amount of maintenance allowance and incidental expenses shall be paid to the back account of the students concerned.
The Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune shall pay the amount of scholarship admissible for the respective years in accordance with paragraph 6 (a) above, as decided by the Government, on receipt of certified information, attendance and satisfactory academic progress report of the student from the University regarding semester-wise tuition fee, subsistence allowance and other incidental expenses for the subsequent year.
