Appointment of Anna Bhai Sathe Research and Training Institute (RTI), Mumbai as Controller was under consideration at the Government level with Joint Secretary (Budget), Department of Social Justice Special Assistance, Ministry of Social Justice, Mumbai as Managing Director, APACS, Mumbai.
The Government's Decisions:
As a result of this Government decision, Anna Bhai Sathe Research and Training Institute (RT), Mumbai is being declared as the Managing Director and Anna Bhai Sathe Research and Training Institute (RT), Mumbai as the Controlling Officer.
