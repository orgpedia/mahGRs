Terms and Conditions for Supply of Household Items Packages: -
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1.  | The beneficiary should be a sugarcane farmer and the concerned Gram Sevak should have an identity card / certificate.                                                                                                                                                        |
| 2.  | In order to avail the benefit, it will be necessary to fill the requisition form in the prescribed format with the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare Department.                                                                                                        |
| 3.  | At the district level, there will be an Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare Coordinating Officer (Nodal Officer).                                                                                                                                                         |
| 4.  | The committee headed by the District Collector should include District Social Welfare Officer, Zilla Parishad as well as Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, etc.                                                                                                        |
| 5.  | The e-tendering / GEM system should be adopted from the headquarters level of the Social Justice Department or the corporation regarding the supply of household kits for sugarcane workers and families.                                                                    |
| 6.  | The quality of the item in the home set will need to be checked by a government-approved testing school before the tender for the home set is accepted.                                                                                                                      |
| 7.  | All expenses including supply, transportation, storage, insurance, distribution, photo, manpower supply, etc., except Goods and Services Tax, will be included in the rate of household goods.                                                                               |
| 8.  | It will be necessary to engrave the name of the corporation on the items in the set of household items.                                                                                                                                                                      |
| 9.  | The payments will be made after the selected beneficiary receives the kits, receives a receipt from the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, and after receiving the proposal from the head office of the Corporation for payment of the payment on their recommendation. |
| 10. | It will be mandatory to take a photograph of the sugarcane harvest worker beneficiary while distributing the home kit.                                                                                                                                                       |
| 11. | Household kits should be distributed through the office of the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, either by camping or by visiting places during the off-season.                                                                                                        |
| 12. | The company which has fixed the process of stocking the household items will be obliged to supply within the prescribed time.                                                                                                                                                |
| 13. | This benefit will be given to only one sugarcane farmer in the family once.                                                                                                                                                                                                  |

Procedure to be followed for supply of household kits: -
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1. | Eligible beneficiaries will be selected through a committee constituted at the district level under the chairmanship of the District Collector, constituted by a Government decision dated 4th August 2021, for supply of the proposed kits of household items. |
| 2. | The beneficiary selection committee shall consist of Assistant Commissioner, Member Secretary Social Welfare and Social Welfare Officer as Zila Parishad member.                                                                                                |
| 3. | Since the corporation does not have a separate district office at the district level, the scheme will be implemented through the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare.                                                                                        |
| 4  | In case applications are received in excess of the target set by the Corporation, the beneficiary will be selected through lottery in a committee constituted under the chairmanship of the District Collector.                                                 |
| 5. | Benefits of the scheme will be distributed in the form of commodities to the beneficiaries selected by the Beneficiary Selection Committee.                                                                                                                     |
| 6. | The Assistant Commissioner Social Welfare concerned will be responsible for ensuring that there is no irregularity in the allotment of household items.                                                                                                         |
