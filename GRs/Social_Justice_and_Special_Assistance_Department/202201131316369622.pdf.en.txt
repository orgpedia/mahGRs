The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be the controlling officer for this scheme. The Controlling Officer shall spend the expenditure from the prescribed purpose within the limits of the provision made as aforesaid. The Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Directorate, Pune shall ensure that the details of expenditure (district-wise expenditure/ number of beneficiaries) and utilization certificate are submitted to the Central Government immediately.
Under the above scheme, the Controller will be responsible for spending the funds subject to verification by the Controller of Utilization Certificates of the current financial year as well as the previous year's expenditure. Also, the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Directorate, Pune will be responsible for ensuring that no financial irregularity takes place by spending the said fund as per the government decisions, terms and conditions issued by the Finance Department from time to time in accordance with the Financial Rules, Maharashtra Budget Rulebook.
The Controlling Officer shall ensure that the conditions contained in the Government Decision/ Circular issued by the Finance Department from time to time are fulfilled while spending the funds.
