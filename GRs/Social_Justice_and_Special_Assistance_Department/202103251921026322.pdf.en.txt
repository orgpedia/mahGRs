Government's decision:
A budgetary provision of Rs. 45,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Five Crores only) has been approved for implementation of Centrally Sponsored National Family Benefit Scheme for the financial year 2020-21. As per the Government Circular at Reference No.2 of the Finance Department, an amount of Rs. 15,75,00, 000/- has been disbursed to all the District Collectors towards expenditure for the period April to June, 2020 for the National Family Benefit Scheme. Now the Finance Department has approved the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 18,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores only). Accordingly, the Government hereby approves the release of Rs. 18,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Eighteen Crores only) towards expenditure for the period July, 2020 to March, 2021 under the National Family Benefit Scheme as per the accompanying statement under this Government decision. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
2. All the Collectors are informed that in the accompanying statement, Column No. 3 Funds have been distributed to the respective districts on the new CO Code. The grants distributed by them should be distributed to the taluks of their districts in proportion to the beneficiaries. The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
