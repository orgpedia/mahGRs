Government Decision: Under the Centrally Sponsored Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Other Backward Classes Students of the State, the Central Government has allocated Rs. Rs 13,00,50, 000. has been distributed to the State vide letter dated 26/08/2021. Accordingly, the funds received under the Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme of the Government of India for the students belonging to Other Backward Classes is Rs. 13,00,50, 000/- as per Demand No.
The expenditure under the head Post Matric Scholarship (100% Centrally Sponsored) Programme (2225 E991) for the students belonging to Other Backward Classes (OBCs) is hereby approved under the head ZG-3, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, Welfare of 03 Backward Classes, 277, Education, (02) Scholarships/ Scholarships.
The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune will be the controlling officer for the scheme. The Controlling Officer shall spend the amount within the limits of the provision made as aforesaid for the prescribed purpose only. After the funds are spent, the director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate, Pune, will be responsible for submitting the details of the expenditure (district-wise expenditure/ number of beneficiaries) and the utility certificate to the government without fail.
Under the above scheme, the Controlling Officer will be responsible for spending the funds subject to verification by the Controlling Officer of the utilization certificates of the current financial year as well as the previous year's expenditure. The Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate, Pune will be responsible for ensuring that no financial irregularities take place by spending the said funds as per the terms and conditions issued by the Finance Department from time to time as per the Financial Rules, Maharashtra Budget Rulebook.
4. The Controlling Officer, while spending the funds, shall be appointed by the Finance Department. The funds should be disbursed after verifying that the conditions in the circular dated 26.08.2021 have been fulfilled.
