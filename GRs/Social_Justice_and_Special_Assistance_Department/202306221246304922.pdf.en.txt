(2) The Government of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance vide Decision No. 2020/ No. 100/ Visyo, D. 03.05.2021.
Preamble: Under the Special Assistance Schemes under the Government decision dated 20.08.2019 of the Department, there is a provision to take income certificate from the beneficiaries of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Anudan Yojana and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme from April to June after the end of the financial year every year. Considering the difficulties of the beneficiaries of this scheme above the age of 50 years, the government was considering not to take the income certificate every year.
Government Decision: It is hereby approved to take income certificate for the beneficiaries of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grant Scheme and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme for the age group of 50 years and above not every year but once in 5 years.
