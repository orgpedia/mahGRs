2. Admission of Backward Class Children in Government Hostels under the Department. Representations were submitted by the public representatives and other dignitaries. Accordingly, the Government have decided to grant admission to the students mentioned in the attached Form A in the Government Hostel for the year 2019-20 in the Government Hostel for Backward Classes Boys and Girls subject to availability of space for admission in the particular subject in the respective Hostel.
The terms and conditions of the Government decision dated 4th September, 2019 regarding admission of Backward Class students in Government Hostels shall remain in force.
