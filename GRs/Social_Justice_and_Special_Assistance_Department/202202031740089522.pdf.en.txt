The proposal: The Supreme Court in Writ Petition (Civil) No. 980/2019. In the decision dated 04.03.2021, vide para No. 12 the government notification. 29.06.2021, the Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission has been declared as a Dedicated Commission to carry out specific works related to reservation in local bodies. This has been decided as per the Government Notification dated 27.12.2021. The Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission (MSBCC) has prepared a tentative budget for the expenditure of manpower and office expenses for carrying out specific tasks assigned to them. The same has been submitted to the Government vide letter dated 28.07.2021.
The Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission was under the consideration of the government to provide funds for office expenses and ancillary matters.
The government's decision:
The Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission is being provided funds to carry out specific works related to reservation in local bodies as follows:
