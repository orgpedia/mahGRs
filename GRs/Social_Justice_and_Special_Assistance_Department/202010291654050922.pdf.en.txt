In view of the limited availability of funds in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, the matter of cancellation of administrative approval of all works sanctioned under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme, which have been given administrative approval but have not yet issued the commissioning order, was under consideration of the Government.

Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme Demand No. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana (04) (01) Implementation of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana, State Level 27 Small Constructions 50 Other Expenditure, 2525 F085 Administrative Sanction of all the works which have been given administrative approval under this scheme but the work order has not been issued yet.
