The Government of India has enacted the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989. In exercise of the powers conferred by section 23 of the said Act, the Central Government may, in accordance with the provisions of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Rules, 1995, by notification in the Gazette Extraordinary,

As per the provisions of Rule 16 of the said Rules, the Department of Social Welfare, Cultural Affairs and Sports vide No. Rouse 1015/ PR No. 93/ Mavak-2, dated 06.01.1996, has issued a circular to the concerned authorities to implement the Act and Rules. A high-level committee under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister had been formed for the purpose. The Committee has been reconstituted from time to time by the Government Circulars and Government Decisions No. 2 to 9. Reference No. As per the Government decision dated 10th July, 2020, the appointments of non-official Chairpersons/ Members appointed to Commissions/ Committees established by various Acts/ Acts under the Department of Social Justice have been cancelled. Therefore, the matter of reconstitution of the Committee constituted under the Government Resolution dated 8th January, 2015 was under consideration of the Government. Accordingly, the earlier orders in this regard are being superseded and the State Level High Powered Vigilance and Control Committee is being reconstituted as mentioned below.

The functions of the Committee shall be as follows:
To review the implementation of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989 (Amended Act, 2015) and the Amended Rules, 2016 in the State in a proper and strict manner and to review the cases in this regard.
Review of cases under the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989 (as amended) and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) (Amendment) Rules, 2016, as to whether or not the financial assistance/ relief provided to the victims of atrocities is in accordance with the rules.
To review the cases of how the rehabilitation of the oppressed Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes persons etc. is being carried out as per rules and with immediate effect.
Review of cases registered with the Police/ Courts under the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989 (as amended) and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 2016 (as amended).
To review whether and how these Acts are strictly enforced by the concerned agencies/ authorities/ offices.
To review various cases received by the Government regarding violation or enforcement of rules under this Act.
Travel Allowance and Daily Allowance to non-official members shall be admissible subject to such Government decisions as may be issued by the Finance Department from time to time in this regard.
(4) The Committee shall continue to exist until further orders in respect of the Committee are issued.
The Committee shall hold two meetings in a calendar year in the months of January and July, 1-1.
(6) The appointment of members of the House of the People, the House of the People and the House of the People shall continue until they become members of the House of the People, the House of the People and the House of the People, or until further orders of the Government in respect of their appointment to the House of the People.
