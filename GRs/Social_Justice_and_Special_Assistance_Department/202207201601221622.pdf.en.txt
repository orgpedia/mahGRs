Preamble: The Central share of funds for the Centrally Sponsored Pradhan Mantri Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Scheme for Scheduled Castes has been increased from Rs. 206.00 lakh has been provided by the Central Government. 25.3.2022 has been approved and the State share of 40% is Rs. 137.33 lakh. Since the provision for this scheme was not made in the 2022-23 budget due to lack of accounting, the government was considering to disburse funds from the central share 2210 G618 and state share 2210 G627 under the approved funds of the central government under the National Urban Health Mission.
Government Decision- Since the Finance Department has approved the proposal on the basis of the recommendation of the Public Health Department, the Central Government has released Rs. 206.00 lakhs and corresponding State share of Rs. 137.33 lakh crore for the financial year 2022-23 under the National Rural Health Mission (Central share 60%) Account head 2210G618 and State share 40% Account head 2210G627.
