35/ D.C.- 2, dated May 3, 2021.
Government circular:
During the lockdown period declared as a preventive measure against the spread of COVID-19 virus in the State, all the disabled employees were exempted from attending office till the public transport facility was restored vide circular dated 25th September, 2020 and vide circular dated 3rd May, 2021, considering the fact that the immunity of the disabled persons was not good enough and it was becoming difficult and cumbersome to face the difficulties.
At present, with the decline in the prevalence of the Covid-19 virus in the state, the ministerial departments and various government offices have become operational at full capacity. Public transport is also running smoothly. In view of this, the exemption granted to the disabled officers/ employees under the said circular from attending office is being discontinued from the date of this order.
