Government of Maharashtra

Government Decision No: - BCH-2016 / Q. No.293 / Education-9
Mantralaya, Mumbai - 400032
Date: August 13, 2025
Reference: - Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government Decision No: - BCH-2016 / Q. No.293 / Education-2, D. December 26, 2024
Preamble: - The Government has decided to directly deposit the amount required for self-acquisition of food, accommodation and other educational facilities to students enrolled in professional and non-professional courses after Class 19B, 12 and 12B in SC / ST and neo-English categories who are eligible for admission to Government Hostel in Social Justice and Special Assistance Department but are not admitted to Government Hostel due to lack of hostel capacity, directly into the Aadhaar linked back account of the students. The "Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Yojana" has been launched on 6 January 2017. Extension of the scheme to the Taluk level and amendments in the scheme and terms and conditions have been made under the Department of Social Justice, Government Decision, dated 26th December, 2024.
A No. 2A (9) and A No. 2B (2) of the Government Decision in Reference provide that the student shall not be a resident of the city / taluka in which the institution in which the student is admitted is located. There were demands from various quarters to amend these provisions to make the student a non-resident of the city / taluka in which the educational institution in which the student is enrolled. Accordingly, the government was considering amending the provisions of the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Swadhar Yojana.
Government Decision: - Bharat Ratna Dr. Under this Government decision, it is approved to amend No. 2A (9) and 2B (2) of the Government Decision to provide that the student should not be a resident of the city / taluka in which the educational institution in which the student is enrolled for the Babasaheb Ambedkar Swadhar Yojana is located, and not of the taluka.
