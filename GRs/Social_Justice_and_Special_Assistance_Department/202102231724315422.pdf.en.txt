GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: Department of Information and Public Relations to publicize and disseminate special assistance schemes
Subject to the post-facto approval of the Media Plan Planning and Implementation Committee and availability of funds, Rs. 11,43,46, 000/- to the media plan. It was approved by the government on August 22, 2019. Directorate General of Information and Public Relations
