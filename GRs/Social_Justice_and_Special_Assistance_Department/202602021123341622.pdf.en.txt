The Proposal: -
The Government Hostels Scheme is implemented for the education of backward class boys and girls under the Social Justice Department. A total of 449 government hostels are functioning in the state under the scheme. It has 231 boys' and 218 girls' government hostels. These government hostels have an intake capacity of 24,958 girls and 21,715 boys. Thus, the government hostels under the division have a total intake capacity of 46,673.
The students of this hostel are provided with facilities like accommodation, food arrangements, educational materials, living allowance for daily expenses, etc. Accordingly, the 125th birth anniversary under the department was celebrated at the new Girls' Government Hostel, Shirval, Ta. In order to provide facilities to the students admitted in the Government Hostel at Khandala, District Satara, the Government was considering the purchase of necessary items like pillows, pillow covers, bedsheets, mattresses, sheets, blankets, sattanji, iron coats, offoser tables, officer chairs, highback chairs, office wooden tables, office large iron cupboards, wooden benches, water purifiers, dining tables, dining benches, wooden tables for students, wooden chairs for students without hands.
