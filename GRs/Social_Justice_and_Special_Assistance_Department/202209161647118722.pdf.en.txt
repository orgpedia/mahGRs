Government Decision: None of the temporary posts on the establishment of 35 District Collectors in the accompanying statement for the implementation of the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grant Scheme and other special assistance schemes has been vacant for more than six months and has lapsed. Subject to these conditions, the Government hereby approve the continuation of a total of 1422 temporary posts from 01/09/2022 to 28/02/2023. (Attached is the report)
The expenditure for this matter shall be met from the provision approved for the respective financial year under the head Demand No.N-2, Head of Accounts-2053, District Administration (093) District Establishment (01) General Establishment.
