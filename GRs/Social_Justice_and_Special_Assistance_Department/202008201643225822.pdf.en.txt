Government's decision
For the financial year 2020-21, a budgetary provision of Rs. 96,29,36, 000/- (Rupees Ninety-six Crores Thirty-nine Lakhs Thirty-six Thousand only) for the month of April to June, 2020 for the District Establishment and Rs. 36,48,10, 000/- (Rupees Thirty-six Crores Forty-eight Lakhs Ten Thousand only) for the District Establishment for payment of salary and other administrative expenses of the District Establishment has been approved.
As per the Government Circular, for the period April, 2020 to June, 2020, 10% of the sanctioned amount for the expenditure of Reference No. 3 (Salary, Telephone and Office Expenditure), 15% (Salary), 4% and 10% has been disbursed to all the Divisional Commissioner's Offices under the Government Resolution of Reference No. 5. Now, 10% of the budgetary allocation is sanctioned for the District Establishment Subsidy Scheme (01-Salary), 06 Telephones, Electricity and Water and 13 Office Expenses amounting to Rs. 9,52,76, 800/- (Rupees Nine Crores Fifty Two Lakhs Seventy Six Thousand Eight Hundred only) and the Financial Assistance Scheme for Old Landless Agricultural Labourers (Rupees Three Crores Sixty Three Lakhs Ninety Four Thousand Eight Hundred only). Accordingly, the Government hereby approve the release of funds as per the accompanying statement under this Government Resolution for the period August and September, 2020.
