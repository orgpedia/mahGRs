Proposal- Reference No. As per the decision of the Government dated 24.01.2018, it has been decided to implement the revised Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme for the Vimukta Jati Nomadic Tribes. The government also issued a notification in this regard. As on 08.01.2019, certain amendments have been made in the said Scheme.
The government was considering giving priority to widows/ widowers/ disabled/ orphans/ widows/ elderly persons while selecting beneficiaries for the scheme. The government has taken the following decisions:
The government has decided to include widows/ widowers/ disabled/ orphans/ widows/ elderly persons on priority while selecting beneficiaries under the Yashwantrao Chavan Open Settlement Scheme launched for the category of Vimukt Jati Nomadic Tribes.
