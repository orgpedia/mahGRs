Government's decision
Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation for the financial year 2021-22. 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-salaried) Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 14,50,000/- (Rupees Fourteen Lakh Fifty Thousand only) has been made. The Finance Department has approved the disbursement to Vasantrao Naik Vimukta Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation within the limit of 30% of the original budgetary allocation.
Accordingly, for the Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation, 30% of the original budgetary provision of Rs. 31-Assistant Grants (Non-Payment) amounting to Rs. 4,35,000/- (Rupees Four Lakh Thirty Five Thousand only) are being sanctioned subject to the following conditions:
1. Restrictions prescribed by the Government for expenditure under the scheme should be strictly implemented. Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2. Streamline the operations to limit the cost of management.
3. Detailed information about the amounts spent on various items and the expenditure incurred on them should be submitted to the government.
4) Cut down on office expenses.
5. The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the debt recovery rate.
6. The funds of the scheme are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
7. General Financial Rules for the concerned authorities while spending the aforesaid funds. The funds should be utilised by complying with the terms and conditions of the scheme, CVC guidelines, CAG observations, etc.
The Deputy Secretary (Finance), Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai shall be the Controlling Officer in respect of the above grants. The Cell Officer (Cash Branch), Drawing and Disbursing Officer, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has been announced to collect the amount. They are being empowered to disburse the said grant to Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation (Limited), Mumbai through payment in the form of M.C.N.44.
