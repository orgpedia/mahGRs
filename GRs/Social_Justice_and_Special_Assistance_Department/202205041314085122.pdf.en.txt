Government Decision: For the financial year 2022-23, a budgetary provision of Rs. 111,85,73, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eleven Crore Eighty Five Lakh Seventy Three Thousand only) has been approved for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Subsidy Scheme for District Establishments and Rs. 42,51,22, 000/- (Rupees Forty Two Crore One Lakh Twenty Two Thousand only) for the Establishments on the Scheme for Financial Assistance to Old Landless Agricultural Labourers. 10% of the budget. 3 As per the decision of the Government here, 01-Salary has been distributed to all the Divisional Commissioner's Office for this purpose. Now, the Department of Finance has released 15% of the approved budgetary allocation for the target of 01-Salary and 14% each for the target of 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water, 11-Travelling Expenditure and 13-Office Expenditure.
Accordingly, the Government hereby approves release of funds to the tune of Rs. 16,73,97, 930/- (Rupees Sixteen Crores Seventy Three Lakhs Ninety Seven Thousand Nine Hundred Thirty Only) for District Establishments and Rs. 6,37,36, 810/- (Rupees Six Crores Thirty Seven Lakh Thirty Six Thousand Eight Hundred Ten Only) for the Establishments on the scheme for the period of May and June, 2022 to all the Divisional Commissioner's Offices for the purpose of payment of 01-Salary, 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water, 11-Traveling Expenditure and 13 Office Expenses as per the statement accompanying this Government decision.
All the Divisional Commissioners are hereby informed that they should allocate the grants distributed as per the prospectus A and B attached herewith to the districts under their jurisdiction as per their requirement and the District Collectors should allocate the grants to the taluks of their districts as per their requirement.
