To reform the quasi-judicial system created for redressal of grievances or injustices of the employees of Government recognized aided Ashramshalas/ Residential Schools/ Vidyaniketans in the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department.
The Government of Maharashtra
Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department
Government Resolution No. Vibhasha-2022/ No. 18/ Vijabhaj-1
Ministry Extension Building, 1st Floor,
Hutatma Rajguru Chowk, Madam Cama Marg,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: February 10, 2022.
Reference:- Government Decision Department of Health, Family Welfare Department No.Vibhasha-2017/ No.P.S. 266/ Vijabhaj-2, Dy. October 3, 2017.
Government Decision: A quasi-judicial mechanism has been created to provide for filing of appeals/ reviews to the competent authority under the department for securing justice at the administrative level before filing appeals before the competent court or tribunal in all kinds of service matters and other personal grievances/ grievances etc. of the teachers and non-teaching staff appointed in the government-recognized aided ashramshalas/ residential schools/ vidyaniketans of this department.
If the decision given at the level of Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Directorate, Pune under the said quasi-judicial system is not acceptable, then there is a provision for the appellant to file a revision appeal against the decision at the level of Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ A.M.B.C.V. After that Ma. There is a provision to file a review appeal to the Minister (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare). But under the quasi-judicial system, the appellant is delayed in getting justice as there are two levels above the government in the appeal/ review to be filed with the government against the decision at the field level.
(3) In view of the above, the Secretary/ Principal Secretary/ Additional Secretary may be appointed from the quasi-judicial mechanism created under the decision of the Government in this regard for redressal of grievances/ grievances etc. relating to personal services of teachers/ non-teaching staff of Government recognized aided Ashramshalas/ Residential Schools/ Vidyaniketan existing under the Other Backward Classes Welfare Department. MS (EMBAKV) is being excluded by this government decision.
(4) From the date of this order, if the appellant is required to file an appeal/ review petition before the Government against the order passed by the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Directorate, Pune in quasi-judicial system, the appellant shall file an appeal to the Government. File a quasi-judicial appeal/ review petition with the Minister (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare).
