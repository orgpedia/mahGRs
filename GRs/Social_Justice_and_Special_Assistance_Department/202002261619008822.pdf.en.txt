2. All the Collectors are informed that in the accompanying statement, Column No. 3 Funds have been distributed to the respective districts on the new CO Code. The grants distributed by them should be distributed to the taluks of their districts in proportion to the beneficiaries. The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector. As per the circular at reference no. 1, it is allowed to draw the authorization letter in the minus manner.
Expenditure from the said fund should be spent under the head" Demand No.N-3, Main Account Head 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 104-Welfare of the Aged, Disabled and Destitute Persons (08) (09) Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme (Programme) (2235 O 294) 50-Other Expenditure."
4. All the District Collectors are informed that they have issued Reference No. 2 Care should be taken to submit the payments to the treasury by fulfilling the terms and conditions of the circular of the Finance Department here. Also, they should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the subsidy given to them under any circumstances.
5. All the District Collectors are also informed that they should send a copy of the reconciliation statement to the Audit Office of this Department and to this Office by matching the expenditure made from the grants handed over to them with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's Office. Utilization certificate should be sent to the Accountant General's office immediately and its copy should be sent to the planning office of this department and this office. If the expenditure reconciliation work is not done properly and the utilization certificate is not submitted to the Accountant General's office within the prescribed time, the entire responsibility will be on the concerned district collector.
6. This Government decision was taken by the Planning Department vide Informal Reference No.15/ Q. 1413 dated 24.01.2020 and vide Finance Department Informal Reference No. 96/2020/ Expenditure 14 dated 05.02.2020.
