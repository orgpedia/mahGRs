-: Government Corrigendum: -
The Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government of Nashik Regional Division under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance decided to budget for the construction of residential quarters for the children of SCs and OBCs working in Government Residential Schools / Non-Teaching Staff working in the State. Administrative approval has been granted under 11.06.2025 and the condition number (s) in the said Government decision is as follows: -
f) The Integrated Construction Management Cell, Tribal Development Department / Social Justice and Special Assistance Department established under the Tribal Development Department dated 13.02.2025 will be operational as the operational mechanism for the present constructions.
The above Condition No. (o) is being amended as follows.
The operational mechanism for the present constructions will be Maharashtra-State Infrastructure Development Corporation (MSIDC), Mumbai.
Other provisions of the Government Decision Provisions shall remain in force 2. This Government Correctional Certificate is available on the Government of Maharashtra website www.maharashtra.gov.in.
