Thereafter, a new Department of Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes was created. Reference No. 4 is here. As per the Government decision dated 9/6/2017, 29 posts in this department have been transferred. Accordingly, a total of 117 posts have been sanctioned for the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance (DoSAT), a ministerial establishment, out of which 6 temporary posts have been made available for the functioning of the National Integration, Universal Brotherhood and Thought Promotion Committee established under this department. The period is being extended up to 31.08.2023.
