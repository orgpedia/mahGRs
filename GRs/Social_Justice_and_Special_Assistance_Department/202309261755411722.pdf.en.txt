Government Order Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, Deputy Commissioner on Establishment, Social Welfare and Equality, Mrs. Chhaya Gadekar of Group-A cadre, Deputy Commissioner and Member District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Solapur have completed their three-year tenure in the post and the Government has issued orders to them as per their request.
The said post has been extended by one year with effect from 14.07.2023. However, as per the complaint received against Mrs. Chhaya Gadekar, Deputy Commissioner and Member, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Solapur, she is being transferred and posted as per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Servants and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005, with the approval of the competent authority at the place indicated in column 3 as under: :
