the prelude
In the 125th birth anniversary year of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, 50 new government hostels have been approved for the backward class girls in the state. Under this decision, approval has been given to start 7 girls hostels of 250 capacity at the divisional level and 43 hostels of 100 capacity at the taluk level. As per the decision of the government, approval has been given to start a hostel for girls of backward classes in Vadgaon (Maval) in Pune district. However, since the location of the hostel was inconvenient, the matter of changing the location of the hostel was under the consideration of the government.
Government Resolution: Commissioner, Social Welfare Commissionerate, Pune has informed the Government through a reference letter that there is no rent or government land available to start a hostel for backward class girls in Vadgaon (Maval) G. Also, in order to facilitate the travel of students from Talegaon Dabhade to other colleges i.e. Akurdi, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Talegaon-Dabhade being the central place and to effectively implement the government's hostel scheme for backward classes, the location of the hostel sanctioned at Maval has been changed to Talegaon-Dabhade, located at a distance of 5 km from the nearest college. Approval is being given to start a hostel for girls of backward classes in Talegaon-Dabhade, Pune district by changing the place.
