The Proposal:
The Department of Social Justice has been mandated to study the social, educational, economic, cultural and political status of the Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes and suggest measures to the Government. The Maharashtra State Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission was constituted by the Government in its decision dated 01/03/2005. The term of office of the 27 non-permanent members of the Commission was to expire on August 31, 2021. Accordingly, 27 non-permanent posts of the Commission have also been extended from time to time as per the decisions of the Government in this regard. Reference-4 for these 27 temporary posts. As per the Government decision dated 13th September, 2021, the date has been extended from 01st September, 2021 to 28th February, 2022. The 27 vacancies are temporary. Since the said 27 temporary posts are going to expire on 28.02.2022, it was under consideration of the Government to extend the period from 01.03.2022 to 31.08.2022.
Government's decision:
Out of the 53 sanctioned posts of officers/ staff for the State SC/ ST Commission, 27 posts indicated in the prospectus have been approved for filling up in the first phase as per the Government decision dated 2nd December, 2006. A total of 27 vacancies have been announced. from September 1, 2021. This will continue till 28 th February, 2022 or until further orders, whichever is earlier. The decision was approved by the government. Now, the 27 temporary posts of the Commission are being filled up vide Reference No. 5 here. As per the decision of the Finance Department dated 22 nd February, 2022. from 1st March 2022. The Government is permitting the same to continue till 31st August, 2022 or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
