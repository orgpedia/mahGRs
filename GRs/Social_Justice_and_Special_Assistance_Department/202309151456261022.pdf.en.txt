Government Decision: For the financial year 2023-24, a budget provision of Rs. 117,18,62, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Seventeen Crores Eighteen Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand only) has been approved for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grants Scheme for District Establishments and Rs. 46, 74,70,000/- (Rupees Forty Six Crores Seventy Four Lakhs Seventy Thousand only) for the Establishments on the Scheme for Financial Assistance to Old Landless Agricultural Labourers. Reference No. 3 Under the government decision here, 30 percent of the amount has been distributed to all the divisional commissioner's offices. Now, out of the approved budgetary provision of the Finance Department, an amount of Rs. 9,34,22, 660/- (Rupees Nine Crore Thirty Four Lakh Twenty Two Thousand Six Hundred only) for District Establishments and Rs. 3,73,68, 600/- (Rupees Three Crore Seventy Three Lakh Sixty Six Thousand Six only) for Establishments on Old Landless Agricultural Labourers for the purpose of 01-Salary, 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water, 11-Travel Expenditure and 13-Office Expenditure has been disbursed on BIMS system. The said funds are being sanctioned to all the Divisional Commissioner's Offices for the period from August to September, 2023 for the purpose of 01-Salary, 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water, 11-Travel and 13-Office expenses as per the accompanying prospectus under this Government decision.
All the Divisional Commissioners are hereby informed that they should allocate the grants distributed as per the prospectus A and B attached herewith to the districts under their jurisdiction as per their requirement and the District Collectors should allocate the grants to the taluks of their districts as per their requirement.

All the District Collectors are informed that they should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the grant handed over to them under any circumstances. They should send a copy of the reconciliation statement to the audit office of this department and to this office by matching the expenditure made from the grant handed over to them with the expenditure recorded in the office of the Accountant General. Utilization certificate should be sent immediately to the office of the Accountant General and its copy should be sent to the planning office of this department and this office. If the expenditure reconciliation work is not done properly and the utilization certificate is not submitted to the Accountant General's office within the prescribed time, the entire responsibility will be on the concerned district collector.
5. This Government Decision is based on the Government Circular of the Department of Finance, No. Finance-2023/ P. No. 40/ Finance-3, No. It is being issued as per the administrative authority given to the departments as on April 12, 2023.
