The Proposal:
The names of many villages, hamlets and roads in many cities and rural areas of the state are caste-based. It was approved by the Cabinet on December 2, 2020. Accordingly, the Government have issued a Government Resolution in this regard dated 11th December, 2020.
(2) As per the decision of the Government, the procedure for changing the caste names of villages, hamlets and roads in the State shall be determined by the Urban Development Department for urban areas and the Rural Development Department for rural areas.
