The Proposal:
The National Trust for the Welfare of Persons with Autism, Cerebral Palsy, Mental Retardation and Multiple Disabilities has been established under the National Trust Act, 1999 (National Trust) and is implementing various schemes and programmes for such persons across the country. Section 13 of the said Act provides for constitution of local level committees. Accordingly, local level committees have been formed in all the districts of Maharashtra. As per the National Trust Act, 1999, a State Level Coordination Committee has been constituted under the Government Resolution No. 2. However, under the Central Govt. As per the letter, the National Trust, New Delhi has selected Samvedna Cerebral Palsy Development Centre Laxmi Nagar, Barad Vasti, Harangul Budruk, T.G. Latur, operated by Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh Jankalyan Samiti, as the nodal agency for the State of Maharashtra. Therefore, the matter of reconstitution of the State Level Coordination Committee under the National Trust Act, 1999 was under the consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
The National Trust Act, 1999 (NTA) provides for the supervision of the Trust at the state level. The Committee constituted vide Government Resolution dated 26.08.2015 stands dissolved and a State Level Coordination Committee is being constituted as under:

Duration of State Level Coordination Committee: The Committee will deal with the issue of guardianship under the National Trust Act. However, the tenure of non-official members appointed by the Government shall be for three years from the date of their appointment. The State Government may, if necessary, terminate the membership of the members appointed by it before the expiry of the period of three years after hearing them.
Disqualification:- The membership of the said Committee shall be disqualified on the following grounds.
(a) if any non-official member has been declared bankrupt by a court of law while being a member of the Committee, or (b) if he has been declared mentally ill by a competent court or (c) if the non-official member has been convicted by a competent court for offences like moral turpitude, or (e) if he has been punished in accordance with any law for the time being in force.
Vacancy of Seat:- If a member of the State Level/ Coordination Committee is disqualified due to any of the disqualifications specified in the said paragraph, his seat shall become vacant.
Meeting of State Level Coordination Committee: The State Level/ Coordination Committee shall meet at least once in every six months.
Administrative Department:- The State Level Coordination Committee under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance will continue to function and the said Department will be an administrative department.
Functions of State Coordination Committee:- Subject to the provisions of the National Trust Act, 1999, this State Coordination Committee shall act as the principal committee for the control and supervision of the District Level Local Committees functioning under the provisions of the said Act in all the districts of the State. In particular and without prejudice to the provisions of the said Act, the Committee shall act as follows:-
(a) The Committee shall direct the concerned Local Level Committees to review the certificate of guardianship issued under the provisions of the National Trust Act under all the District Local Level Committees in the State and dispose of the pending cases within a specific time.
(b) The said State Level Coordination Committee shall direct the District Local Level Committees to comply with and regulate the National Trust Act and the notifications issued by the Central Government thereunder.
(c) The Coordination Committee shall work to bring uniformity and regularity in the functioning and functioning of all the District Local Level Committees in the State.
