The Proposal:
It was under consideration of the Government to cancel the appointments of all non-official chairpersons/ members appointed to various Government/ Non-Government Commissions/ Committees under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
Government's decision:
Maharashtra State Commission for Scheduled Castes under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. The following appointments of Chairperson and Members made under the Government Decision dated 28.10.2021 are hereby cancelled.
