The Proposal:
He said that the financial assistance to the eligible beneficiaries under the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme will be increased. Repeated demands were made to the government through people's representatives, various disabled organizations and voluntary organizations. Also, while presenting the budget for 2023-24, the financial assistance to the eligible beneficiaries of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme will be Rs. 1000/- to Rs. 1500/- was announced in the budget session. Accordingly, the government was considering to increase the financial assistance given to the eligible beneficiaries under the scheme. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken as approved in the Cabinet Meeting dated 28th June, 2023:
Government has decided to increase the monthly financial assistance of Rs.1000/-to Rs.500/-to Rs.1500/-per month to all the beneficiaries of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Grant Scheme and Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme. Accordingly, the amount of financial assistance to the widow beneficiaries having 1 child or 2 children will also be Rs. 1500/- respectively.
The distribution of financial assistance to the beneficiaries of the special assistance scheme should be made from the sanctioned allocation made for the general/ SC/ ST category.
The increase in financial assistance will be applicable to all the beneficiaries of the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grant Scheme, Shravanbal Seva State Pension Scheme, Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme, Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme and Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme.
