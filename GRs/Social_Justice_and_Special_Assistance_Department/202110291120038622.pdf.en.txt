Introduction: The State Government had presented a budget based on the concept of social justice in 2003-04. Since some of the themes in that budget were related to different departments, it was announced to appoint a Scheduled Castes and Tribes Commission to implement the scheme in coordination with all departments and to effectively implement the principle of social justice. It was introduced in the Legislative Assembly on 22.7.2003.
According to the decision of the Government, the Department of Social Justice, Cultural Affairs and Special Assistance No. Ramaiah-2003/ S.No.175/ Mavak-1, Dy. The Maharashtra State Commission for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes has been constituted under Act No. 1.3.2005 with 1 Chairperson and 4 non-official members for the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes and their appointments, their salaries and allowances and their duties and responsibilities have been fixed. Besides, Principal Secretary, Social Justice Department, Principal Secretary, Tribal Development Department and Director General of Police, Maharashtra State, Mumbai have been appointed as ex-officio members to coordinate the work between the Commission and the Government. Government decision The appointments of non-officio Chairperson/ Members appointed to Commissions/ Committees established by various Acts/ Acts under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance as on 10th July, 2020 stand cancelled. At present, all the posts of chairman and members of the Maharashtra State Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission are vacant. Therefore, the proposal to appoint 1 chairman and 2 non-official members on the Maharashtra State Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The following appointments are being made to the posts of Chairman and Members of the Maharashtra State Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission:
