The Proposal:
Various organizations, people's representatives, etc. have been working to fulfill the constitutional demands of the Other Backward Classes community.
The government has received complaints about them. Since the constitutional justice demands of the Other Backward Classes community are related to various departments of the government, the government was considering the formation of a cabinet sub-committee to study and recommend to the cabinet for the effective implementation of these demands and to propose additional concessions/ benefits.
Government's decision:
The Cabinet in its meeting held on 14.10.2020 has decided to constitute a Cabinet Sub-Committee to study in detail the reservation of Other Backward Classes and the concessions and other benefits provided by the Government so far and make recommendations to the Cabinet for effective implementation and to propose additional concessions/ benefits. As such. The Cabinet Sub-Committee under the Chairmanship of the Minister (Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection) is being constituted as follows:
