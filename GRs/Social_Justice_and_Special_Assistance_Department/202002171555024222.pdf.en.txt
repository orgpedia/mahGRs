The Proposal:
As per the decision of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance dated 4/3/2017, a scheme has been launched to confer the Late Vasantrao Naik Merit Award to meritorious students and girl students belonging to Other Backward Classes category who have stood first in the 10th and 12th class examination from the state and the department from the academic year 2016-17 and it has been approved to be awarded on 1st July or on the date fixed by the government. Accordingly, in the academic year 2016-17, meritorious students of other backward classes in the 10th and 12th class examinations from the state and zonal examination boards will be given. The Award of Merit was held on 04.10.2017. The State Government has decided to award merit certificates to the meritorious students who have passed the Class 10 and Class 12 examinations in the year 2018-19. Reference No. 4 dated 10.01.2020 mentioned herein as well as Reference No. 5 is mentioned here. The matter of issuing the revised Government Resolution by superseding the Government Resolution dated 13.02.2020 was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
10.01.2020 as well as d. The Synchronized Government Decision dated 13.02.2020 is being superseded. During the academic year 2018-19, 41 meritorious students of Form A, B, C and D, who came first in the 10th and 12th class examination from the state and zonal examination boards, were awarded the late Vasantrao Naik Merit Award and for this, the program was organized.
