Government Order Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, Assistant Commissioner on Establishment, Social Welfare and Equality, Group-A Cadre Mrs. Sangita Dawkhar, Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, have been receiving various complaints from the institutions, public representatives, beneficiaries of the scheme, so that they can be transferred elsewhere. The request has been made vide letter dated 29.03.2023. Accordingly, Mrs. Sangita Davkhare, Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune is being transferred and posted to the place indicated in column 3 below with the approval of the competent authority as per the provisions of Section 4 (4) (2) and Section 4 (5) of the Maharashtra Regulation of Transfer of Government Employees and Prevention of Delay in Discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005.

The transferred officer should be relieved immediately to appear at the place of his new posting and the concerned officer should report to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune and the Government immediately at the place of his new posting.
3. No leave shall be granted to the transferred officers, otherwise disciplinary action shall be taken against the competent authority granting leave.
4. All concerned are being made aware that officers who are transferred may, after relieving them, join the new post during the permissible period of posting, the absence of such officers, who do not adhere to this time limit and remain absent from the place of posting mutually, shall be treated as" unauthorised absence" and in the alternative, there will be interruption in their service.
The Commissionerate of Social Welfare should initiate administrative action against the officers who will not appear at the place of their posting and submit the proposal for the approval of the government as per the need. The matter should be reported to the government from time to time and the compliance report should be submitted to the government within the prescribed time limit.
