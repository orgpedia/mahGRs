Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Extension Building, Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
The date: December 27, 2021.
Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government Resolution, No. Savio-2021/ No. 182/ AJAK dated 12th November, 2021.
The construction of the multi-purpose Buddhist Bhavan in Thane was approved in principle by the Government Decision of 09/03/2018 and the Government Corrigendum of 28/07/2021 for the Bharat Ratna Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme. Instead, in-principle approval is being given for the construction of a social hall in the Mumbra-Kalwa area of Thane district. As per this in-principle approval, the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Thane should submit the complete proposal of the work to the government through the Commissionerate of Social Welfare along with the estimate sheet as per the provisions of the Financial Manual.
