Introduction: Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar has tried to unite the country in the trinity of equality, freedom and fraternity through the Indian Constitution. Article 46 of the Constitution of India lays down the responsibilities of the state towards the weaker sections of the society. Article 46 of the Constitution of India provides that the State shall promote with special care the educational and economic interests of the weaker sections of the people, and in particular of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes, and shall protect them from social injustice and all forms of exploitation. " It said. In line with the above directions, the State Government is making continuous efforts to provide social justice, economic and social protection to the Scheduled Castes and all kinds of oppressed people.
Accordingly, various welfare schemes are being implemented across the state with the aim of holistic development of the Scheduled Castes, deprived weaker sections. In order to make the general public aware of all these schemes and to achieve its objectives, the' Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Samata Karyakram" is being celebrated in the state. Under this, the matter of providing funds for the expenses of the state level program held in Mumbai is under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: It has been decided to celebrate" Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Samata Karyakram" in the State from 6 th April, 2022 to 16 th April, 2022 vide Reference No. 1 decision was made by the government. Also, for the management of this program, Reference No. The committee has been formed as per the decision of the government.
