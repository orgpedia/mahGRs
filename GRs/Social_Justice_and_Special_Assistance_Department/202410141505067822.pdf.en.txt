The Proposal:
The Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Quality Scholarship Scheme for higher education in reputed educational institutions of the country has been implemented by Government Decision Social Justice, Cultural Affairs, CRODAB Special Assistance Department No.EBC-2002 / P.No.223 / Mavak-II, dated 19th June, 2003 to enable SC (including neo-Buddhists) students to acquire the necessary skills and knowledge and prepare them for the competitive era in various fields. The revised Rules of the Scheme have been implemented vide Government Decision dated 08.11.2017 superseding all earlier Government Decisions regarding the Rules of the Scheme.
2. The Selection Committee headed by the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, as mentioned in the said Government Decision, shall conduct the selection process for the students admitted to the reputed educational institutions in the country for the year 2024-25 and give the same as per the terms and conditions prescribed by the application received by it. A proposal to sanction scholarships to a total of 29 students, 8 and 29 respectively, selected with a chronology of the proceedings at the selection committee meeting held on 12.09.2024 was submitted to the Government vide their letter dated 23.09.2024.

In pursuance of the above proposal, a total of 29 students were under consideration to be given the benefit of Rajshi Shahu Maharaj Quality Scholarship Scheme.
The Government's Decisions:
The selection committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 08.11.2017 for Rajshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for SC students to pursue higher education in reputed educational institutions in the country. A total of 29 eligible students selected at the meeting on 12.09.2024 are being sanctioned the Rajshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for the year 2024-25 under Forms-A-1, A-2 and Form-B-1 to B-2 attached to the Government Decision.
The full tuition fee and examination fee fixed by the respective educational institution for the students for whom the scholarship has been sanctioned shall be paid to the institution through the Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune. Students will be paid in full by the educational institution as per the hostel and meal charges charged by them. The amount of this expense will be paid only once in a year. A total of Rs.10,000 / - will be given every year for course books, educational materials and other educational expenses. For their course expenses, they should pay this year's tuition fee, examination fee B hostel rent, subsistence allowance, and other allowable GS to the college / institution concerned by seeking information from the university / institution concerned and obtaining a certificate of student's progress B attendance.
The students who have been awarded the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship mentioned in the accompanying Forms-A-1 to A-2, Form-B-19 to Form-B-2, the students who have received the Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship, the remaining allowable amount should be paid to the students by deducting the amount.
Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being authorised to provide scholarships to such students.
Expenditure in this regard shall be apportioned under the head "Demand No. N-32225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, of - Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 277 - Education, Schemes under Five Year Plans, Schemes under State Schemes (04) (22) Rajshi Shahu Maharaj Scholarship (2225 3823), 34 - Scholarships / Scholarships to SC Students for Higher Education" and from the provision of the current financial year made thereunder. For that, Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as "Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
