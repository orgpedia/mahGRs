The Proposal: -
The Central Government has decided to implement SNA-SPARSH (Timely System of Integrated Quick Transfer Real Time System of Integrated Quick Transfer) or Alternative Fund Flow Mechanism to disburse funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes through the Integrated Framework of Public Financial Management System (PFMS), State Integrated Financial Management Information System (State IFMIS) and Reserve Bank of India's e-Kuber System (e-Kuber) to achieve greater efficiency in cash management at both Central and State levels.
Now, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India has sanctioned an amount of Rs. A letter from 3 has been reported. It was under the consideration of the Government to disburse the funds through the Budget Estimates, Distribution and Control System (BEAMS) to the Controller Officer Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune.

Government Decision: - The Government is approving the expenditure of Rs. 21,54,28, 000 / - (Rupees twenty-one crore fifty-four lakh twenty-two thousand only) as Central Assistance for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PM-AGY), a centrally sponsored scheme under Pradhan Mantri Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (PM-AJAY) Scheme for the current financial year. The Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune is being declared as the Controlling Officer as well as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer to spend the said funds.
Similarly, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune as a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) is being accorded approval through this to disburse funds through Budget Estimates Distribution and Control System.
2. Expenditure of the aforesaid fund of Rs. 21,54,28, 000 / - (nominal Rs. 21 crore fifty-four lakh twenty-eight thousand only) to be divided by the expenditure incurred under the following head in the budgeted fund in the year 2025-26:

The Drawing and Disbursing Officer and the Controlling Officer should immediately operationalize the said fund on SNA-SPARSH system. The grant is TIAMAT as per Department of Finance Government Decision Date 12.01.2023. Payments submitted to the treasury are being authorised to be disbursed in the form of payments in the Maharashtra Treasury Rules 44-A.
4. The Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune shall ensure compliance with the guidelines and prevailing norms, delegations of financial powers and conditions of instructions issued from time to time by the Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India and Department of Finance, Government of the State while discharging the above funds.
5. Subsidies sanctioned as aforesaid shall be spent within the limits of the funds disbursed for the matter for which they have been approved to be spent.
