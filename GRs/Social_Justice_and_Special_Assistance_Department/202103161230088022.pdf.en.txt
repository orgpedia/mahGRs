Preamble: Guidelines for providing 15% margin money (Front and Subsidy) as per the procedure to be followed for sanctioning the benefit of Stand Up India Scheme of the Central Government Read 1 above as per Government Decision dated 08.03.2019 and revised orders/ guidelines as per Government Decision dated 09.12.2020.
As per the decision of the Government dated 08.03.2019, it was under the consideration of the Government to issue a Government Preamble to incorporate guidelines for providing margin money in the scheme.
GOVERNMENT RECOGNITION: In pursuance of the action to be taken for sanctioning the benefits of the Stand Up India Scheme of the Government of India, the following guidelines are being inserted as Para 1.5 after Para 1.4 of the Government Decision dated 08.03.2019.
" The beneficiary should submit his/ her application along with the prescribed documents to the concerned Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare Office. The office shall scrutinize, inspect and re-verify the application received and certify the authenticity of the document and submit the application to the office of the Commissioner of Social Welfare through the Controlling Officer for final approval. "
