Government Resolution, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department No. 2019/ No. 316/ Aastha-2, Dy. 26th February, 2020 Preamble: Special District Social Welfare Officer (presently Assistant Commissioner Social Welfare and similar Group A) Government Order for the period 1.1.1984 to 1.1.1999. Mr. D.D. Deshmukh, the then Special District Social Welfare Officer and others and Mrs. Vijaya Pawar, the then Special District Social Welfare Officer, have appealed to the Hon'ble Chief Minister to cancel the Service Seniority List issued under 20.2.2001. The original application to the Maharashtra Administrative Tribunal, Mumbai, vide No. 389/2001 and No. 846/2003 was filed. According to the original application. The court gave. dated 22.7.2004. As per the Government Order dated 20.2.2001, the Assistant Commissioner of Social Welfare and similar, Group-A category was ordered to cancel the fixed seniority and fix the revised seniority list. The Bombay High Court, Bench at Bombay, in Writ Petition No. 9453/2004 and Writ Petition No. 9449/2004 was filed. In response to the petitions filed. The court gave. The petitions filed by the government have been dismissed after hearing on 14.6.2018. Accordingly, in order to ensure the revised seniority list, it has been approved to grant date of regular promotion to the officers who have been promoted to the cadre of Special District Social Welfare Officer (presently known as Assistant Commissioner Social Welfare and similar cadre) during the period 1980-81 to 1997-98. Accordingly, a Special District Social Welfare Officer (presently known as Assistant Commissioner Social Welfare and similar cadre) was appointed. The matter of publishing the revised service seniority list for the period 1.1.1981 to 1.1.1999 was under consideration of the Government.
