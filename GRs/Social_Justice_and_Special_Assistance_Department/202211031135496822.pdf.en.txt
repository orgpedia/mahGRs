circular
The administrative approvals granted for various schemes under the District Annual Plan (Scheduled Castes Sub Plan), 2022-23, were suspended by a circular under reference from April 1, 2022 till date. It was also directed that after the appointment of the new guardian minister, the list of approved administrative works should be submitted for his reconsideration and a decision should be taken on how to continue those works.
Now the 2. On September 24, 2022, the Hon'ble Chief Minister has appointed Guardian Ministers for all the districts. Hence, the suspension is being revoked. Accordingly, the list of works that have already been given administrative approval in the year 2022-23 and the proposed works should be submitted to the Guardian Minister and the decision should be taken with his consent and further action should be taken.
