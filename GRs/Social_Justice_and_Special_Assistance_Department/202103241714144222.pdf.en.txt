Preamble: The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India has implemented the Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAY) on a pilot basis since 2009-10. Under this scheme, the works of basic amenities like water supply, roads, etc. in the villages having more than 50% population of Scheduled Castes and other activities are sanctioned in convergence with other schemes of the state government. The main objective of this scheme is to implement the welfare schemes implemented by the various departments of the state government in the villages and to develop these villages in a holistic manner. The main objective of this scheme is to provide basic facilities to the people of the village and to improve the living standards of all the citizens.
While spending the funds sanctioned under this scheme, it is expected to implement the scheme/ program by combining the funds received from other schemes of the Central Government and the State Government and the funds received under the Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana.
In the backdrop of the above facts, detailed guidelines to the field officers and constitution of various level committees for effective implementation of the scheme in the State of Maharashtra have been issued vide Government Resolution dated 26.08.2019.
