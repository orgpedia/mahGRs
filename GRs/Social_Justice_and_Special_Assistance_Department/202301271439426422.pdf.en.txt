Government Decision: In the financial year 2022-23, for the scheme of National Rural Health Mission under the Centrally Sponsored National Health Mission, demand No. N-3, 2210-Public Health, 789-Scheduled Caste Sub Plan, (01) Scheme under Scheduled Caste Component Programme, (01) (05) National Rural Health Mission (Central Share 60%) (Programme) Accounts Head 2210G609, 31-Subsidised Grants (Non-salaried) Below Rs. 305.0469 crore.
Pursuant to the guidelines for disbursement of funds issued vide Circular No. 1 dated 4.04.2022 in respect of Department of Finance, in this regard, the Department of Public Health vide No. In pursuance of the concurrence given by the Department of Finance to the proposal on 2nd instalment of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Infrastructure& Maintenance under National Rural Health Mission, (01) (06) National Rural Health Mission (State Share 40%) (Programme) Account Head 2210G609, 31- Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payment) from the budgetary provision of 2022-23 amounting to Rs. 3520.85 lakh is being sanctioned to the Principal Secretary, Public Health Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS).
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner. It should be ensured that the funds will be spent this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise and material objective-wise information achieved, the utilization certificate, the concerned field officer should ensure that it is sent to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, the concerned Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department and Public Health Department by the 10th of every month.
The Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer should ensure that the funds for SCs are discussed on the same points so that the Government can keep a check on the expenditure on these schemes.
4. While spending the funds disbursed under the said Government Decision, the Head of the Department and the Controlling Officers shall ensure compliance with the financial rules contained in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Fiscal Rights Rulebook as well as with the Finance Department. The instructions given in the Government Circular dated 4.04.2022 should be strictly followed.
