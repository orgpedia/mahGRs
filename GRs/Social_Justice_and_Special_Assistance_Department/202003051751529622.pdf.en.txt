The proposal: Minister (Revenue, Public Works) In the meeting held on 26/6/2019, it has been decided to implement the Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme and Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance Extension Scheme for the students of socially and economically backward classes from the academic year 2019-20. Accordingly, the. A Government Resolution was issued on 11/07/2019 and accordingly, the matter of opening a new Account Head was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Students belonging to the socially and economically backward classes have been given a new scheme" Rajshri Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Tuition Fee Scholarship Scheme. Approval is being given to open new sub-heads under ZG-03, Head Accounting 2225.
