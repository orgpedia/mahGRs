All concerned are being made aware that officers who are transferred may, after relieving them, join the new post during the permissible period of posting, the absence of such officers, who do not adhere to this time limit and remain absent from the place of posting mutually, shall be treated as" unauthorised absence" and in the alternative, there will be interruption in their service.
