The Proposal:
The Department of Social Justice has been mandated to study the social, educational, economic, cultural and political status of the Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes and suggest measures to the Government. The Maharashtra State Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission was constituted by the Government in its decision dated 01/03/2005. The term of office of the 27 non-permanent members of the Commission was to expire on August 31, 2020. Accordingly, 27 non-permanent posts of the Commission have also been given extension from time to time as per the decision of the Government in this regard. These 27 temporary posts were extended from 01.03.2020 to 31.08.2020 as per the Government decision at Reference-11. Also, the matter of extension from September 1, 2020 to February 28, 2021 was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
Out of the 53 sanctioned posts of officers/ staff for the State SC/ ST Commission, 27 posts indicated in the prospectus have been approved for filling up in the first phase as per the Government decision dated 2nd December, 2006.
