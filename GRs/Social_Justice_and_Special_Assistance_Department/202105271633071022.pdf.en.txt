The proposal: To buy a vehicle for the Minister (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare). The approval has been accorded vide Government Resolution dated 04.02.2020. Following that. The creation of a fictitious post of driver for the vehicle of the Minister (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare) was under the consideration of the Government.
Government decision: The Sub-Committee has approved in a cyclical manner to take the services of the Minister (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare) by creating a notional post of driver for his vehicle and taking it through an external source. Accordingly, this Government decision is being approved to create a notional post of driver for this department and take the service from an external source. The expenditure should be met from the grants sanctioned by the department.
