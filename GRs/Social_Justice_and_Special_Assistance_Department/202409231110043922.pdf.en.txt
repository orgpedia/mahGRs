The government's decision:
A total of 117 posts have been sanctioned for the establishment of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance (itself) Ministry, out of which 6 temporary posts have been made available for the functioning of the National Integration, Universal Fraternity and Thought Promotion Committee set up under this Department. Orders for extension of these 6 temporary posts are issued separately. Out of the remaining 111, a total of 23 posts indicated in the accompanying prospectus have been retained till the date 31/08/2024 vide Reference No. 6 here. The approval was granted by a Government Decision dated 01.03.2024.
2. Subject to the conditions contained in the Government Decision, Finance Department No. Post-2016, / Q. 8/16 APCCD 06.09.2024, these 23 temporary posts shall now be filled up as indicated in the accompanying return. 01.09.2024 that is. The deadline is being extended to 28.02.2025.
Expenditure in this regard shall be apportioned under the head "Demand No. N-2,2251-Secretariat, Social Services-090-Secretariat (01) Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, (01) Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance (2251011 3) (01) Salary (Compulsory) 01 Salary" and shall be apportioned from the provision to be approved for the current year.
