Corrigendum: Non-teaching staff of the Social Work College will be paid under the revised pay structure as per the 6th Pay Commission. It came into effect on March 8, 2011.
However, in the said Government Resolution, in Form A, it has been mentioned that the pay band for the cadre of Hostel Supervisor is' Pay Band 4440 7440 Grade Pay 1800'. Instead, the pay band should read" 5200- 20200 Grade Pay 1800."
