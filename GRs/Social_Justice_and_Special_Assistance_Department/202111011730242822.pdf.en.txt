Government Decision: Maharashtra State Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (MSDDFC) Various schemes are implemented for the economic upliftment of the disabled through Mumbai. The Corporation has been provided with a managerial grant of Rs.36 crore for the year 2021-2022 towards 36 ancillary grants (Salaries) to meet the expenditure on management and administration. 87,74,000 (Rupees Eighty Seven Lakhs Seventy Four Thousand only) has been provided in the budget.
2. Read out of the said budgetary provision No. Under the Government decision 1& 2, Rs. 36 crore has been provided for managerial and administrative support to meet the expenditure on management and administration within the limit of 75% of the total sanctioned fund. 87,74,000/- (Rupees Eighty Seven Lakhs Seventy Four Thousand only) out of the managerial grant of Rs. 52,63 lakh (Rs. fifty two lakh sixty three thousand only) is being approved by the Government subject to the following conditions:
1) Restrictions set by the government for spending in non-plan funds should be strictly implemented.
2) Limit the cost of operations.
3) The details of the amount spent on various matters and the expenditure incurred on it should be submitted to the government.
4) The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the rate of recovery of loans.
5) Reduce the cost of living.
The Deputy Secretary (Corporations), Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Mantralaya, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer in respect of this managerial grant. The amount is being handed over to the Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Navi Mumbai for further action. They are being empowered to disburse this grant to Maharashtra State Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation through payment in the form of M.C.N.44.
