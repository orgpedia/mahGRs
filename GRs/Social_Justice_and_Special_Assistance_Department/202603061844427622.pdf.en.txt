Also, Government Hostels under the Department is implemented for the education of backward class boys and girls with the objective of providing all kinds of education and higher education opportunities. At present, there are 449 government hostels for the education of backward class students under the department and 92 government residential schools for the education of SC / ST and neo-Buddhist boys and girls. These government hostels have a total intake capacity of 46,673 students and government residential schools have a total intake of 14,600 students. Out of these 449 government hostels, 301 are in the THATS government building and 148 hostels are functioning in a rented building. While 92 government residential schools are functioning in these government buildings.
