Government's stand:
As per the Government decision dated 17.03.2022, 6476 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune have been approved to be continued from 1.03.2022 to 31.08.2022. As per the decision of the government, the term of 1 temporary post of Joint Commissioner has been extended.
In lieu of this
Extension of temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune should be read as 6477 posts instead of 6476 and Joint Commissioner should be read as 02 temporary posts instead of 01.
