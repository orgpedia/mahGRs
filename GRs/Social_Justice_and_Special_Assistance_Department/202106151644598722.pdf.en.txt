The memo:
The State Government has started a new scheme named Shahu, Phule, Ambedkar Residential/ Non-Residential School Scheme of the State Government for a total of 165 Ashramshalas recommended by the State Government for the Centre's scheme but not funded by the Central Government and run by NGOs and has approved the grant under the Government Decision dated 8th March, 2019 subject to scrutiny from the academic year 2019-20 till further orders of the Government.
The following heads of accounts are being approved to meet the expenses of boarding, lodging and other educational facilities of the admitted students of the said residential/ non-residential schools sanctioned by the grantee:
