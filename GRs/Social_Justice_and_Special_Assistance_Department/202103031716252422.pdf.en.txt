In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) and sub-sections (a), (b) and (c) of section 3 of the Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes Act, 2005, the Government hereby notifies in the Notification No. Justice A. V. Nirgude (Retd.
The issue of fixing remuneration and providing facilities to Justice A. V. Nirgude (Retd.) was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision
The Government have accorded sanction to fix the remuneration and provide facilities to Justice A.V. Nirgude (Retd.) as follows: 1. The term of the Chairperson of the Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission shall be three years from the date he assumes charge of his office.
Chairman of Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission. As he is a retired judge of the High Court, his status will be that of a retired judge. The status accorded to the President shall be limited to that of a Royal Secretary.
The Chairman of the Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission will be paid a remuneration of Rs 1,60,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Sixty Thousand only) per month.
The Chairman of the Commission is not allowed to drive a government vehicle. Only when you are on a government tour, you will be given a government vehicle or train fare (first-class air-conditioned) for the government tour.
If the Chairman of the Commission visits various places in Maharashtra for the work of the Commission, his accommodation and food will be arranged at the Government Rest House as per the rules.
6) Government accommodation is not permissible in Pune.
