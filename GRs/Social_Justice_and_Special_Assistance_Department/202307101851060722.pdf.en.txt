The Proposal:
With the main objective of expediting the economic upliftment of the economically weaker families of the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist community in the state of Maharashtra, the Government of Maharashtra has established the" Mahatma Phule Backward Classes Development Corporation" under the Companies Act, 1956. It was established on July 10, 1978.
The Corporation implements the State Government's subsidy schemes, seed capital schemes, direct loan schemes and training schemes. The schemes of National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation, New Delhi (NSFDC) and National Safai Karamcharis Finance and Development Corporation, New Delhi (NSKFDC) are also implemented at the national level as a channelized mechanism. For this, funds are provided by the State Government as well as the Central Government to the Corporation for implementation of various schemes/ activities.
In view of the new technology and the growing expectations of the backward classes, it is necessary to implement new schemes along with some operational schemes. Students and beneficiaries are as empowered as other sections of society. All they need is equality and opportunity. Technology and social media have changed the way we live. In view of the above, based on the provisions of the Articles of Association of the Corporation, a subsidiary company named" Mahatma Phule Renewable Energy and Infrastructure Technologies Limited (MAHAPRIT)" has been incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013. Keeping in view the need of the changing times, the needs and growing aspirations of the backward and weaker sections of the society, and their eligibility, the company has formulated the' Navayuga' scheme for inclusive and sustainable development of the backward classes of the society by convergence of various objectives, schemes and measures of the central and state governments. Also, the deprived and backward classes will come to the mainstream.
Considering this, it was under consideration to instruct all the concerned departments to involve Mahaprit in the work being done by all the departments and offices under them.
Government's decision:
Post facto approval is being accorded for the establishment of a subsidiary company namely Mahatma Phule Renewable Energy and Infrastructure Technology Limited (MAHAPRIT) as a subsidiary of Mahatma Phule Backward Classes Development Corporation, with effect from 12th April, 2021.
