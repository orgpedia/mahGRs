In order to provide the necessary skills and knowledge to the students of the Scheduled Castes (including neo-Buddhists) and to prepare them for the competitive era in various fields, the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship Scheme for higher education in reputed educational institutions of the country has been implemented under the Government Decision Social Justice, Cultural Affairs, Sports and Special Assistance Department No. EBC-2003/ PR No. 323/ MAVK-2, dated 11 June 2003. By superseding all the earlier Government decisions in respect of the Rules of the Scheme, the revised Rules of the Scheme have been brought into force with effect from the Government decision dated 08.11.2017. In the said Government decision, the Selection Committee headed by the NMU Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, was under the consideration of the Government to conduct the selection process for the year 2019-20 for the students admitted in the reputed educational institutions of the country and to sanction scholarships to the students selected as per the terms and conditions specified in the applications received through it.
Government Decision: The Selection Committee constituted under the Government Decision, dated 08.11.2017, for Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for SC students to pursue higher education in reputed educational institutions in the country, in its meeting held on 16.10.2019, has approved the sanction of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for the year 2019-20 to a total of 66 eligible students out of the selected students in Form A-1 to A-5 and Form B attached with this Government Decision, and 8 in Form A, subject to the condition of submission of Guarantee, Form No. 16 and Income Certificate.

2.The students who have been approved the scholarship, the full tuition fee and examination fee fixed by the respective educational institution will be given to the institution through the Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune. The students will be provided accommodation and food as per their choice. The amount will be paid annually.
A total of Rs.10,000/- per annum will be provided for books, academic materials and other educational expenses. For their course expenses, the information regarding tuition fees, examination fees and hostel rent, living allowance and other permissible expenses for the year 2019/ 2020 should be sought from the concerned university/ institution and the certificate of progress and attendance of the student should be obtained and paid to the concerned college/ institution.
3. The students who have been sanctioned Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship in Form A-1 to A-5, Form B and Form C mentioned in the accompanying Form A should be paid the remaining admissible amount by deducting the amount received by the students from the Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship.
The Commissioner, Social Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being authorized to award scholarships to the said students.
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the provisions of the current financial year under the head" Demand No.N-3, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, 01- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 277-Education, Schemes under Five Year Plans, Schemes under State Schemes." For this, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
