Government Decision: - The continuation of 5983 temporary posts on the establishment of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune from date 01.09.2023 to date 29.02.2024 has been approved under the Government Decision dated 22.09.2023 in the context.
Under the Government Decision, Finance Department dated 21.02.2024, all the administrative divisions have been empowered to extend the temporary posts included in its draft to the administrative divisions as well as all the existing temporary posts created after the approval of the previous review but not included in the draft from the date 01.03.2024 to the date 31.08.2024.
3. Accordingly, 5929 temporary posts in the accompanying prospectus A to D proposed for extension on establishment of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune are being approved to be continued from date 01.03.2024 to date 31.08.2024 subject to the following terms and conditions: -
The extension is being granted subject to the assumption that the temporary posts, other than executive posts, are not vacant for more than six months.
2. The terms and conditions for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned shall be complied with.
3. As from the date on which the majority of the officers / staff are absorbed into the other posts, the majority of the posts will automatically stand cancelled.
4. The expenditure for the above posts shall be met from the provision approved in the year 2024-25 under the respective headings of the following - 1) Demand No. N-3 2225 - Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities
