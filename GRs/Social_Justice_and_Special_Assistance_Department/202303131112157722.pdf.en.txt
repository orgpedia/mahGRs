A sum of Rs. 1236.12 lakh has been provided in the budget.
Circulars No. 1 and 2 of the Department of Finance provide guidelines for the disbursement of funds for the financial year 2022-23. Accordingly, as agreed by the Finance Department, the Central share for the Scheduled Castes for the implementation of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana Watershed Development Component 2.0 scheme under the head Head Accounts 4402288 is Rs. 1845.00 lacs and State share under the Head of Accounts 4402 2897 Rs. 1230.00 lakhs out of which a total of Rs.
3075.00 lakhs is being sanctioned to the Principal Secretary (Soil and Water Conservation Department), Ministry of Water Resources, Mumbai for disbursement on Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS).
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. While spending the fund, the controlling officer should take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner. It should be ensured that the funds will be spent this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made, account-wise/ sub-account-wise and material objective-wise information achieved, the utilization certificate of the fund, the concerned field officer should ensure that it is sent to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, the concerned Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department and Soil and Water Conservation Department by the 10th of every month.
The Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer should ensure that the funds meant for SCs are spent on the same components so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on these schemes.
