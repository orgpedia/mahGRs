Government Decision: For the financial year 2019-2020, a budget provision of Rs. 70,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Seventy Crores only) has been made for the implementation of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme for the beneficiaries of Scheduled Castes. 75,00,00, 000/- and Rs. 25,00,00, 000/- by way of supplementary demand aggregating to Rs. 170,00,00, 000/- has been approved.
Out of the sanctioned amount, Rs. 115,00,00, 000/- has been disbursed to the District Collectorates as per the Government decision in Reference No.3, 4, 6 and 7. Now, the Finance Department has released Rs. 55,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Fifty Five Crores only) (Rs. 30,00,00, 000/- and Rs. 25,00,00, 000/-) has been approved. Accordingly, the Government hereby approve the release of funds under the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme for Scheduled Caste beneficiaries for the period from January, 2020 to March, 2020 as per the accompanying statement under this Government Decision. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
