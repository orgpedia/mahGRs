The proposal: The Cabinet in its meeting held on 08.03.2019 discussed various demands of the State Level Karnabdhir Association. As per the above decision of the Government, approval has been given for the appointment of a Cabinet Sub-Committee to decide on the various demands of the State Level Karna Badhir Association. A decision in this regard was taken by the cabinet sub-committee.
