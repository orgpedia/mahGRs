The Proposal:
Under the scheme of financial assistance to Scheduled Castes Industrial Cooperative Societies, the government was considering to suspend the approval of 13 industrial cooperative societies which were approved under the relevant government decision.
Government's decision:
As per the above decision of the Government, 13 Industrial Cooperative Societies have been sanctioned under the scheme of" Financial Assistance to Scheduled Caste Industrial Cooperative Societies." The approval given to these 13 industrial cooperatives is being suspended under this government decision.
