The Proposal:
The Constitution of India is a sovereign, socialist, secular, democratic republic and guarantees to all its citizens, justice, social, economic and political; liberty of thought, expression, belief, faith and worship; and equality of status and opportunity. The Constitution of India was adopted and enacted on November 26, 1949. The Constitution came into force on January 26, 1950. Every year, November 26 is celebrated as Constitution Day in the country to mark the day when the Constitution came into effect.
Due to the immense contribution of Babasaheb Ambedkar, 6th December is a day of sorrow for Indians, a day to be observed with seriousness and to express gratitude towards Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar. In order to express gratitude towards Babasaheb Ambedkar, the Government was considering to organize Samta Parv from 26 th November, 2022, Constitution Day to 6 th December, 2022, Dr.

Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare to issue orders for making available the SamataDoots working under Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute for the SamataParv to be held during the period from 26th November to 6th December, 2022.
The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the district will be fully responsible for organizing the program and the Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare will be responsible for supervising the strict implementation of the program.
Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute, BARTI, Pune has donated Rs. 2.00 lakh per district and Rs. 2.00 lakh and Rs. 2.00 lakh should be made available immediately.
All concerned should ensure that the Model Code of Conduct for Gram Panchayats, Local Bodies, Municipal Corporations, Municipalities, Municipal Councils or any other election/ bye-election declared/ enforced by the State Election Commission is not violated while organizing the Samata Parv.
