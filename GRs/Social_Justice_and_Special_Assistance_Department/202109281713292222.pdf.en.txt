Preamble: All Maharashtra Rural Development Board, Aurangabad Sanchalit Savli Residential Matimand Vidyalaya, Nandi, Ta. The government was considering to approve 50 residential students instead of the sanctioned strength of 40 students of Ambad, G. Jalna.
Government Resolution- All Maharashtra Rural Development Board, Aurangabad Sanchalit Savli Residential Matimand Vidyalaya, Nandi, Ta. The government is giving approval to increase the number of residential students of Ambad, G. Jalna to 50 instead of the approved student strength of 40.
The approved number of students of the said school shall be 50 instead of 40 residential students. The decision has been approved by the Cabinet of Ministers. It is issued with the consent of the Minister.
The decision has been made available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharshtra.gov.in and its computer code is 202109281713292222. This order is being issued by digital signature.
