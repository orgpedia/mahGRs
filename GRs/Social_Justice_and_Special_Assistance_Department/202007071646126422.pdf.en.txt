Government's decision
For the financial year 2020-21, Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (SARATHI), Pune has been sanctioned 36 grants-in-aid (salary) amounting to Rs. A budget of Rs. 12.00 crore has been allocated for the project. Out of the said provision, Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (SARATHI), Pune will be given Rs. The Government is approving the release of Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) amounting to Rs. 3.00 crore (Rupees Three Crores only) subject to the following conditions:
1. Restrictions prescribed by the Government for expenditure under the scheme should be strictly implemented.
Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2. Streamline the operations to limit the cost of management.
3. Detailed information about the amounts spent on various items and the expenditure incurred on them should be submitted to the government.
4) Cut down on office expenses.
The funds of the scheme are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
While spending the above funds, the concerned officers should make use of the funds by complying with the general financial rules, terms and conditions of the scheme, CVC guidelines, CAG observations, etc.
7. Every condition of the circular shall be complied with by the institution.
The salary subsidy provided by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries, in no case the salary subsidy amount will be distributed in a combined and offline manner.
