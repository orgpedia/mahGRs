Introduction: Refer No. 2 Government residential schools and hostels for boys and girls belonging to Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist communities. Administrative approval was granted for construction of building at Pandharpur G. Solapur. Now the reference No. Under Section 3, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune submitted a revised budget of the above residential school and the matter of giving administrative approval to it was under consideration of the Government.
Government's decision:
Government residential schools and hostels for boys and girls belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes. Pandharpur Rs. The revised budget amounting to Rs. 12,47,72, 160/- (Rupees Twelve Crores Forty Two Lakhs Seventy Two Thousand One Hundred Sixty only) has been approved by the Commissioner of Social Welfare, Pune vide order No. 3 is presented. The above proposal was approved by the Finance Department. As on 20.9.2019, the technical approval has been accorded by the Chief Engineer, Regional Department of Public Works, Pune. Accordingly, the said proposal is being accorded revised administrative approval as contemplated below.
