Government's decision:
A budgetary provision of Rs. 4,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores only) has been approved for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme for the financial year 2019-20. As per the Government Circular in Reference No. 2 of the Department of Finance, 60% of the total provision of Rs. 2,40,00, 000/- for the period April to December, 2019 for the Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme has been released, out of which Rs. 2,40,00, 000/- has been released for the period April to October, 2019 for the period April to October, 2019 as per the Government Decision in Reference No. 4 and 5. Now, the Finance Department has approved the release of 20% of the remaining 40% i.e. Rs. 80,00,000/- (Rupees Eighty Lakh only) i.e. 80% of the total provision.
Accordingly, the Government hereby approve the release of balance amount of Rs. 80,00,000/- (Rupees Eighty Lakh only) for Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme for the period November, 2019 to March, 2020 as per the accompanying statement under this Government decision. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
