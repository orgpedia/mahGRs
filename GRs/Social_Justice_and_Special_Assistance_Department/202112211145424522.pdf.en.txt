Preamble: With a view to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government vide its order dated 23rd March, 2021 has directed to adopt a revised methodology. Accordingly, the Department of Finance has issued revised instructions on Fund Distribution and Utilization Management (PFMS) of Centrally Sponsored Schemes vide Circular No. 2. Accordingly, a single nodal agency (SNA) is required to be appointed for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and a separate single bank account is required to be opened in the authorized bank for that scheme. The Finance Department has also directed to appoint at least one officer of the rank of Deputy Secretary as the Nodal Officer for coordination of these Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Accordingly, the appointment of Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Nodal Officer for Centrally Sponsored Schemes was made as per the Government Decision dated 3 rd September, 2021.
However, as per the proposal submitted by the Commissioner, Social Welfare vide his letter dated 20.10.2021, in order to ensure that there is no ambiguity at the field level in the receipt of central grants and effective disbursement of scholarship benefits under the scheme in future, in view of the implementation of the two schemes at the district level, namely, Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC students studying in classes 9th and 10th under the Centrally Sponsored Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme and Pre-Matric Scholarship to the children of parents working in unhygienic occupations, all the District Social Welfare Officers, Zilla Parishad have been appointed as nodal officers in each district office as the implementing agency for these two schemes.
Further, as per the decision of the Government dated 15.09.2021, the Government was considering to open a scheme-wise separate bank account for the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for these schemes at the Government level.

Government Decision: District Social Welfare Officers, Zilla Parishads are being appointed as Implementing Agency for implementation and coordination of Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Government of India relating to Pre-Matric Scholarship. Further, Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, is being appointed as the Implementing Agency at the district level for the implementation and coordination of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship.
Further, vide Government Resolution dated 3.9.2021, the Government has appointed the Under Secretary (Education-I), Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Mumbai as the Single Nodal Agency (SNA) and Joint Commissioner (Education) at the level of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune as the Nodal Officer. Further, the appointment of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune as Central Drawing and Disbursing Officer vide Government Resolution dated 8.1.2019 is being retained.
Approval is being given to open a Zero Balance Subsidiary Account in Bank of Maharashtra, the single nodal agency (SNA) and nodal officer, as well as the implementing agency for all the three centrally sponsored schemes.
