The Proposal: -
The Overseas Scholarship Scheme has been implemented since 2003 to enable SC students to pursue education in a reputed educational institution abroad. The Rules of the Scheme have been amended from time to time and the revised Rules of the Scheme have been notified vide Government Decision dated 27.06.2017. Thereafter, a comprehensive policy has been laid down to ensure uniformity in the various types of schemes like scholarships, scholarships, hostel / hostel admissions, subsistence allowance, Swadhar, SWAYAM, etc., as well as in the schemes to be proposed in future. 6 has been released. Thereafter, the Government vide the relevant Government Decision No. The final revised rules of the scheme have been laid down under Section 9.
A total of 75 SC students from Maharashtra who will be admitted to an overseas educational institution within the 200 FUT World Rankings (084 | 2) for post-graduate and doctoral courses are selected under the scheme and awarded foreign scholarships. Accordingly, it was under the consideration of the Government to select eligible students under the Overseas Scholarship Scheme and grant them overseas scholarships for this academic year 2025-26.

In addition to the above selection, a total of 19 vacancies, 1 post-graduate degree seat, 18 doctoral (PhD) course seats falling vacant out of the total capacity, are provided to be filled by vertical or horizontal interchange in accordance with the provision in paragraph (t) of Government Decision dated 27.06.2017, as per the recommendation of the Selection Committee to that extent by the Principal Secretary (Pr. 2.1 Further, after the selection of CSA eligible students approved under the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Overseas Scholarship Scheme as per the provision in the Government Decision dated 10.03.2021, the State Government is empowered to select in the same selection year, in order of merit, from the waiting list of the eligible student as per the adjacent merit recommended by the Selection Committee for such vacancies, if some of the students seek cancellation of their selection under the State Government's Overseas Scholarship Scheme on various grounds. In accordance with this provision, a total of 38 students are being scheduled for general waiting list under Form "D."
Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being authorised to provide permissible scholarships to students selected for overseas scholarships. They should take action to sanction permissible scholarships to the students concerned only after taking the guarantees prescribed under the government decision from the student selected for the foreign scholarship under this government decision as well as checking the original documents of the students. The Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune is also being empowered to sanction scholarships to the students concerned for the course and duration mentioned in the Government Decision as well as the scholarship payable accordingly in case of any increase or decrease in tuition fees of a university during the period of study abroad of the student.
4. Students who have been granted foreign scholarships by the government are required to serve the country after completing their education and make the most of their knowledge. Accordingly, it will be mandatory for the concerned students to write a letter of guarantee (BOND) before sanctioning the scholarship.
Students selected for overseas scholarships must complete a master's degree or PhD abroad within the prescribed time period. Under no circumstances will the student be given an extension or increased cost.
6. The scholarship amount shall be fully recovered from the student concerned if the student interchangeably changes the place or course of study at the educational institution, university or course in which the student is approved to pursue higher education.
