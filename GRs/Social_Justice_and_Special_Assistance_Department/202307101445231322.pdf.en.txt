Government Decision: For the financial year 2023-24, a budget provision of Rs. 117,18,62, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred Seventeen Crores Eighteen Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand only) has been approved for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grants Scheme for District Establishments and Rs. 46,74,70, 000/- (Rupees Forty Six Crores Seventy Four Lakhs Seventy Thousand only) has been approved for the establishments on the scheme for financial assistance to the aged landless agricultural labourers. Reference No. 3 Under the government decision here, 20 percent of the amount has been distributed to all the divisional commissioner's offices. Now, out of the approved budgetary provision of the Finance Department, a sum of Rs. 11,71,86, 200/- (Rupees Eleven Crores Seventy One Lakh Eighty Eight Thousand Two Hundred Only) for District Establishments and Rs. 4,67,47, 000/- (Rupees Four Crores Sixty Seven Lakh Forty Seven Thousand Only) for Establishments on Old Landless Agricultural Labourers for the purpose of 01-Salary, 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water, 11 Travel Expenses and 13 Office Expenses has been disbursed on BIMS System. The said fund is being sanctioned to all the Divisional Commissioner's Offices for the period from June to July, 2023 for the purpose of 01-Salary, 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water, 11-Traveling Expenditure and 13-Office Expenditure as per the accompanying prospectus under this Government decision.
All the Divisional Commissioners are hereby informed that they should allocate the grants distributed as per the attached returns A and B to the districts under their jurisdiction as per their requirement and the District Collectors should allocate the grants to the taluks of their district as per their requirement.
