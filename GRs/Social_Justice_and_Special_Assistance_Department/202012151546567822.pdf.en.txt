Government Resolution, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, No.Visyo-2018/ P.No.62/ Visyo, Dy. 20.08.2019.
Preamble: The decision of the Government in the context provides for submission of income certificate to the concerned Tehsil Office after the end of the financial year from 1st July to 30th September every year. However, this year, considering the outbreak of Covid-19 and the risk of corona to the sick, elderly people in the scheme, the government was considering exempting the submission of this income certificate to the Tehsil Office.
Government Circular: The beneficiaries of the Special Assistance Scheme are exempted from submitting income certificate for the financial year 2019-20 to the Tehsil Office till March, 2021.
