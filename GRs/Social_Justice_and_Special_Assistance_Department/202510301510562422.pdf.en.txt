The Proposal: -
Government hostels for the education of backward class boys and girls and Government residential schools for the education of SC and ST boys and girls have been started under the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department. Students enrolled in government hostels and government residential schools are provided with accommodation, food, educational materials, living allowance for daily expenses, and physical facilities.
Accordingly, the purchase of bed sheets, bedsheets and blankets was under the consideration of the Government to provide facilities to the students admitted in Government Hostels B Government Residential Schools under the Department.
