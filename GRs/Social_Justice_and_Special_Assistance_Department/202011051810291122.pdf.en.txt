The Proposal:
Foreign Scholarship Scheme has been implemented since 2003 to provide opportunities to the students of Scheduled Castes to study in reputed educational institutions abroad. The Rules of the Scheme have been amended from time to time and the revised Rules of the Scheme have been laid down vide Government Order dated 27.06.2017.
2. Out of the total 75 students from Maharashtra who will be admitted to foreign educational institutions within the 300 QS World Rankings for postgraduate degree and PhD courses, a total of 54 students have been approved to be selected by the selection committee as eligible, as per the aforesaid Government Decision, dated 29th September, 2020. Now, the Selection Committee has approved to fill up the remaining 21 vacancies in the second phase in accordance with the provision of paragraph (T-1) of the Government Decision dated 27.6.2017 by vertical or horizontal interchange in the same proportion and the matter of sanctioning foreign scholarship to them was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme, the Selection Committee constituted under the Government Decision dated 27th June, 2017 under the Chairmanship of Additional Chief Secretary (Services) selected the forms A-1, A-2, A-3, A-4 and A-4 as per the terms and conditions of the Government Decision

A total of 21 students from B-1 and B-2 are being sanctioned scholarships under this Government decision for higher education in the respective foreign universities.
Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being authorized to grant admissible scholarships to the students selected for overseas scholarships. They should take action to sanction the scholarship admissible to the student concerned only after taking the guarantees prescribed under the government decision from the students selected for foreign scholarship under this government decision and examining the original documents of the students. The Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune is being empowered to sanction scholarships to the concerned students for the courses and duration mentioned in the government decision and also to sanction scholarships payable accordingly if there is an increase or decrease in the tuition fees of a university during the period of study abroad of the students.
3. Students who have been sanctioned foreign scholarships by the Government are required to serve the country after completing their education and benefit from their knowledge. In this regard, it will be mandatory for the concerned students to write a letter of guarantee (BOND) before approving the scholarship.
The students selected for the foreign scholarship must have completed their Master's degree or PhD course abroad within the prescribed time period. Under no circumstances will the student be given an extension or increased cost.
The amount of scholarship will be recovered in full from the student selected for the foreign scholarship if the student changes the place or course of the educational institution, university or course approved for higher education.
(a) The Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune shall, after obtaining authenticated information from the University concerned, remit the amount of tuition fee to the University concerned and the amount of maintenance allowance and incidental expenses to the bank account of the students who have been sanctioned scholarship for the first year or for the second year of the scholarship.
(b) The amount of scholarship admissible for the relevant year as decided by the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune, shall be paid in accordance with the above paragraph 6 (a), after obtaining authentic information from the University regarding the semester-wise tuition fee, subsistence allowance and other incidental expenses for the subsequent year, attendance and satisfactory academic progress report of the student.
