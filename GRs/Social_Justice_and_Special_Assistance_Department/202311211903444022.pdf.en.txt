Government Order: Mr. Mahendra Harpalkar, Chairman, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, has been given reference no. 1 here. As per the order dated 06.10.2023, the additional charge of the post of Chairman, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Dhule was assigned. However, as Mr. Mahendra Harpalkar has not yet taken the additional charge of the post, there is difficulty in taking a decision as the caste claim proposal at the committee level is pending with the signature of the Speaker. This was informed by the Director General, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute, Pune. According to Shri.
Mahendra Harpalkar, President, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar. Mrs. Gitanjali Baviskar, Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Nashik is being given additional charge of the post of Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Dhule till further orders.
Mrs. Gitanjali Baviskar, Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Nashik, shall hold the additional charge of the post of Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Dhule, entrusted to her by discharging the duties and responsibilities of her office.
(3) The order shall come into force immediately.
