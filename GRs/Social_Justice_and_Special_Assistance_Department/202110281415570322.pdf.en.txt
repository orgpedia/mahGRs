All the three schools are being recognized under this Government decision on grant-in-aid basis as a special case subject to the following terms and conditions.
The terms and conditions are as follows: 1) The terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the Government from time to time shall apply to all the three schools.
2) The three schools are being recognized on the basis of grant and accordingly, the approval of the High Level Secretary Committee and the Finance Department regarding the post-pay grant and post creation should be obtained and only then the post-pay grant and post creation will be permissible for these schools.
3) All the three schools have been given the grant by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. The terms and conditions set out in Form B and Form C of the Government Decision issued on 8.4.2015 shall continue to apply.
If it is found that the special schools for the disabled are receiving regular grants from the Central Government, local bodies or other institutions, the institution will be ineligible to receive the grant from the State Government.
(5) If it is found that the terms and conditions and procedures prescribed by the Government from time to time have not been complied with, the recognition of the institution shall be cancelled.
6) All the three schools are being recognized on the basis of their approved number of students on grant basis. The proposal regarding the staff required as per the figure 18.8.2004 should be submitted by the Finance Department for approval of the High Level Committee of Secretaries. Without this recognition, these posts should not be given recognition.

The schools are being recognized on grant basis subject to the norms of the said policy.
