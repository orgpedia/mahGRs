Regarding making public the relevant certificates of the students admitted on the basis of Non-Creamy Layer Certificate/ Caste Certificate/ Disability Certificate.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Health, Family Welfare and Family Welfare
Government Circular No. Sankari 2019/ 460/ E-4095205/ PR No. 188/ Mavak Madam Cama Marg, Hutmama Rajguru Chowk
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: January 9, 2020.
Government circular: Save Merit Foundation, Nagpur on the statement given by various issues. In the meeting held on 14.07.2019 under the chairmanship of the Chief Minister, the organization pointed out that the certificates of non-creamy layer under SEBC/ OBC and EWS are being obtained by giving false information. For this, the income certificate/ caste certificate should be made public.
Therefore, the relevant certificates of the students admitted on the basis of Non-Creamy Layer Certificate/ Caste Certificate/ Disability Certificate should be made public. Strict action should be taken as per the provisions of CBC 2012/ Q.No. 182/ VIJAB-I, dated 25.03.2013.
