The Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015 (Maharashtra Act No. 31 of 2015)
Introduction: The State Government provides public service to the eligible people of the State in a transparent program and time-bound manner. In view of the growing expectations of the people from the local administration, the Right to Public Service Act, 2015 has been promulgated to provide prompt and quality services to them within the prescribed time frame. It has come into force on 28.04.2015. The public authority referred to in section 3 of this Act is required to notify under this Act, within a period of 3 months from the commencement of this Act and from time to time, the service provided, the designated officers, the first and second appellate authorities and the time limit prescribed. It was under consideration of the Government to notify the services being provided by the said public authority under the said Act and provide the relevant services accordingly.
The other members of the committee are the Director, Social Welfare Department, District Social Welfare Officer, Deputy Director of Health Services, District Social Welfare Officer, Deputy Director of Health Services and other health officials. In order to provide the benefit of various services and facilities provided by the regional and local offices under this department in the state to the people of the state/ citizens, the government is being allowed to provide services in the attached Form-A as per the provision of Section 3 of the Maharashtra Public Service Rights Ordinance 2015.
As per the provisions of Section 3 of the Maharashtra Right to Services Ordinance, 2015, the Director, Other Backward Classes Welfare Department, Pune shall notify the services related to them and take necessary action to make the said services available to the public/ beneficiaries within the prescribed time.

As per Section 3 (2) of the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act, 2015, the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune shall display the list of public services provided by him, the time limit prescribed, the prescribed sample or fee, the details of the designated officer, the First Appellate Officer and the Second Appellate Officer on the notice board of the office as well as on the website or portal and the same shall be communicated to their subordinate/ zonal offices.
The process of implementation of the services notified under the Maharashtra Right to Public Service Act 2015 as per the decision of the said Government should be started from the date of notification.
