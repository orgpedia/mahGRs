Among the proposals received from the pending Wedel Government are those mentioned in Annexure-A attached to this Government decision. The government's decision:
Financial Assistance to SC Cooperative Societies The scheme provides for grant of in-principle approval of the Government to the projects of the institutions mentioned in Annexure-A attached with this Government Decision, subject to the terms and conditions prescribed under the Scheme, subject to the first instalment of Rs. 50.00 Lakhs (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) and Long Term Loan of Rs. A total of Rs.100 lakh (Rs.100 lakh only) is being approved as grant-in-aid amounting to Rs.100 lakh (Rs.50 lakh only).
2. The Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune shall ensure that all the conditions and conditions mentioned in the Government decision of the scheme are met by these institutions and only after this is ensured that the financial assistance sanctioned to the institutions is disbursed.
3. Every institution to be sanctioned financial assistance. It should be ensured that the petition is not filed in the court.
4. Field level officers will conduct physical site inspection of the project before disbursement of funds to these institutions. The funds should be disbursed to these institutions only after the submission of the site report.
The process of determining the interest rate on government loans provided under the scheme is underway at the government level. The interest rate determined by the government will be the interest rate applicable to the loan.
6. Repayment of government loans to these institutions should be initiated after two years from payment of 70 of the project value due to the institution. The repayment period of the loan will be 6 years. The loan (principal) should be paid in 16 equal quarterly instalments within 4 years and then interest should be collected in 8 equal quarterly instalments over two years.
The amount of equity sanctioned under the scheme is required to be returned by the institution to the government in 10 equal installments within 10 years from the date of completion of the project.
