The Government is approving the release of non-salary grant of Rs. 50.00 crore (Rupees Fifty Crores only) subject to the following conditions:
1. Restrictions prescribed by the Government for expenditure under the scheme should be strictly implemented. Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2. Streamline the operations to limit the cost of management.
3. Detailed information about the amounts spent on various items and the expenditure incurred on them should be submitted to the government.
4) Cut down on office expenses.
5. The corporation should make concerted efforts to effectively implement the debt recovery ratio.
6. The funds of the scheme are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
7. While spending the above funds, the concerned officers should make use of the funds by complying with the general financial rules, terms and conditions of the scheme, CVC guidelines, CAG observations etc.
The Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Finance), Other Backward Classes Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai shall be the Controlling Officer in respect of the above grants. The Cell Officer (Cash Branch), Other Backward Classes Welfare Department has been declared as Aaharan and Disbursing Officer for the purpose of receiving the grant. They are being empowered to disburse this grant to Vasantrao Naik Vimukt Jati and Nomadic Tribes Development Corporation, Mumbai through payment in the form of M.C.N.44.
