Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance Government Resolution No.383/ Mavak-2, 2005. 30.04.2008, with a view to make Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI), Pune an autonomous institution, it has been approved to establish BARTI on the pattern of Yashwantrao Chavan Vikas Pradarshini Prabodhini (YASHADA), Pune. Accordingly, the said institution was registered under the Public Trust Societies Register, 1950. Therefore, as per the provisions of the Memorandum of Association, since Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI), Pune has been given autonomy, all activities are carried out with the approval of the Governing Body. However, in the Memorandum of Association of the organization, it is mentioned here in section 57 of Barty's Rules and Regulations as follows. The Government shall have the right to issue any general or specific directives on any matters of policy concerning the affairs of the society and such directives shall be binding on the society. Accordingly, the Centre at Sambodhi Competitive Examination Training Centre, District Aurangabad, which operates the Sambodhi Academy, is being approved to train a total of 1000 students at the rate of 200 students per annum for the next 5 years as above.
