The Proposal:
Mahatma Phule Backward Classes Development Corporation Limited. The Company was incorporated on 10th July, 1978 under the Companies Act, 1956. The Corporation implements various schemes for the economic upliftment of the neo-Buddhist sections of the Scheduled Castes. Mahatma Phule Backward Classes Development Corporation Limited to regulate the service matters of officers/ employees on the establishment of the Corporation. The service rules 1983 have been framed and these rules have been made effective from 01.08.1983.
The Service Rules provide that the Board of Directors with the prior approval of the Government shall constitute a Selection Committee to determine promotions in all cadres in the Corporation.
The vacant posts in the corporation establishment need to be filled immediately. For this, the government was considering reconstituting the committees to fill the posts of promotion in the category of class 1 to 3 on the establishment of Mahatma Phule Backward Classes Development Corporation under the administrative control of the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department.
Government Decision: The Government is approving the reconstitution of the following committees to fill up the posts of class 1 to 3 cadres on the establishment of Mahatma Phule Backward Classes Development Corporation by promotion.
