Cancellation of the appointments of Chairperson and non-official members of the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Grant Scheme Committee.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance
Government Circular No: VISSO-2022/ PR No.80/ VISSO
Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Ministry, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date of release: September 29, 2022
Department of Government Decisions, Social Justice and Special Assistance, No: VISSO-2018/ No.62, dated 20/08/2019
Government Circular: The Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grant Scheme Committees have been constituted under the decision of the Government in the context to approve the benefits of various schemes being implemented in the State under the Special Assistance Programme. The chairperson and non-officio members of these committees are appointed by the district collectors on the recommendation of the guardian ministers of the respective districts. The appointments of the President and all non-officials are hereby cancelled. All concerned should be informed.
The committees will continue to function under the chairmanship of the Tahsildar constituted under the decision of the government in this regard till the committees of the newly appointed president and non-official members are constituted after receiving the recommendations of the new guardian minister. These committees should hold meetings every month as per the requirement and take appropriate action on the applications received under this scheme.
