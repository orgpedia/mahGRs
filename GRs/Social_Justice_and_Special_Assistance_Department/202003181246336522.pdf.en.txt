Government's stand:
The relevant Government Decision is being amended as follows:
1. Paragraph No. The total number of members of the Board of Directors referred to in clause 2 shall be 11 (1 Chairman+ 10 Members), of which 7 shall be Government and 3 shall be non-Government members.
2. Paragraph No. In the 12th year, the Undergraduate Program Committee (Executive Committee) shown at the level of Sarathi, Amrit and Mahajyoti, all three institutions, is being canceled.
The composition of the Programme Committee referred to in paragraph 13 (2) of the Government Decision is being amended as follows:
1. m. Minister (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare) 2. Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Other Backward Classes Welfare- 3. Principal Secretary (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department)- Member 4. Principal Secretary- Member (Finance& Planning Department/ Social Justice& Special Assistance Department/ General Administration Department) 5. Managing Director of the concerned institutions- Member 6. Deputy Secretary (Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department)- Member Secretary
4. Paragraph No. 15 The composition of the Executive Committee and the composition of the Executive Committee are being abolished.
