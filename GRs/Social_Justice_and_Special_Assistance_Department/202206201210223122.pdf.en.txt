Government Decision: For the financial year 2022-23, a budgetary provision of Rs. 111,85,73, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Eleven Crore Eighty Five Lakh Seventy Three Thousand only) has been approved for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Subsidy Scheme for District Establishments and Rs. 42,51,22, 000/- (Rupees Forty Two Crore One Lakh Twenty Two Thousand only) for the Establishments on the Scheme for Financial Assistance to Old Landless Agricultural Labourers. 10% of the budget. 3 For the purpose of the Government Decision 01-Wage herein, Reference No. As per the decision of the Government here, 15 percent of the budget allocation for the purpose of 01-Salary and 14 percent each for the purpose of 06-Telephone, Electricity and Water, 11-Travel Expenses and 13-Office Expenses have been distributed to all the Divisional Commissioner's Offices. All the Divisional Commissioners are hereby informed that the distribution of grants distributed as per the returns A and B attached herewith shall be under their jurisdiction.
