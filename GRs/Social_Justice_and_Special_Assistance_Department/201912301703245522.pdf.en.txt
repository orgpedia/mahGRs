Preamble: A Government Resolution has been issued to amend the Yashwantrao Chavan Free Settlement Scheme under Reference No. 4. As per the decision, the Director, Directorate of Welfare of Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Classes, Pune was declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer for the implementation of the scheme and for the distribution of funds. However, it was observed that there was a delay in the disbursement of funds after administrative approval from the government level under the scheme. Therefore, in order to expedite the disbursement of funds, Reference No. As per Government Circular No.8, Joint Secretary, Vijabhaj, Insurance Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai has been declared as" Controlling Officer" and the matter of declaring Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare of the concerned district as Drawing and Disbursing Officer was under consideration of the Government.
