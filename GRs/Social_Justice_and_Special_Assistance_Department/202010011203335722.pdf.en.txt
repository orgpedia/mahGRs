Preamble: Under the General Administration Department. Instructions have been given to create more number of posts and transfer their services to more number of posts for the officers/ employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category under the Government decision dated 21.12.2019 but have not been able to submit the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes or have submitted the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes. Accordingly, the Commissionerate of Social Welfare has submitted a proposal to the Government for creation of 2 supernumerary posts in the office under the Commissionerate of Social Welfare to transfer the services of 2 employees to the post of Supernumerary. Accordingly, the matter of creating 2 additional posts for the employees in the subordinate office of the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune was under the consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: Subject to the decision of the Government under the General Administration Department, in the office under the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, it is being approved to create 2 supernumerary posts for the transfer of the services of 2 employees mentioned in the following table to the post of superannuation of those employees who have been appointed from the Scheduled Tribes category but have not submitted the caste validity certificate of the Scheduled Tribes or have submitted the caste validity certificate of the Other Backward Classes.
