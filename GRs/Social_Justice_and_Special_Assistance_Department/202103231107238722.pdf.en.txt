The proposal:
The Maharashtra State Commission for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes, Mumbai has been allotted 16 publications under heading 2225-4071 for the year 2020-21. An amount of Rs. 10 crore has been provided. However, since the expenditure is not being borne by the Commission under the said matter, the Commission was requested to approve the matter in terms of the following proviso 17- Computer Expenditure. However, since there is not much provision in 2020-21 for 17-Computer Expenditure, it is not possible to make available the provision for 16-Publications in respect of 17-Computer Expenditure. 1000/- has been made available through Supplementary Demand. Now, 16 publications worth Rs. 10 lakh instead of the original estimate of Rs. A sum of Rs. 2.50 lakh had been sanctioned for the project. Accordingly, in the Revised Estimates approved for the 16-publications under the head 2225-4071, Rs. 2.50 lakh was under the consideration of the Government to be made available under the subject 17-Computer Cost through redistribution.
Government's decision:
In respect of Maharashtra State Commission for Scheduled Castes (SCST) Demand No.N-3, Accounts Head 2225- Welfare of SC STs and Other Backward Classes, 01- Welfare of SC/ STs, 800- Other Expenditure, (01) (03) Maharashtra State Commission for Scheduled Castes (SCST) 22254071 16- Publications, Rs. 2.50 lakh is being approved by the Government to be made available under the 17- Computer Expenditure through redistribution.
