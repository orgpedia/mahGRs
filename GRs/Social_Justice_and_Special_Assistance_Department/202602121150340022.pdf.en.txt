A total of 43800 students are enrolled in these government hostels and a total of 14,600 students are enrolled in government residential schools. Out of these 442 government hostels, 294 hostels are in government buildings and 148 hostels are functioning in rented buildings. While 92 government residential schools are functioning in these government buildings. Government Hostels B Government Residential Schools under the Department are doing a commendable job to ensure the economic, social and educational progress of the student enrolled in the Government Residential School.
