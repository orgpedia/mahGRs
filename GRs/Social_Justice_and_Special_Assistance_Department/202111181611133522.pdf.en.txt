Preamble: In pursuance of the Central Government's notification dated 23.03.2021 in Reference No. 1, revised instructions have been issued for the management of funds disbursement and utilization of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. The Central Government has been informed of the appointment of the Coordinating Officer under Government letter in Reference No. 4 and the State Plan Coordinator under Government letter in Reference No. 5.
The Department of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare (erstwhile Department of Welfare of Women, Minorities and Minorities) vide Government Resolution dated 31.03.2018 in Reference No.6 has prescribed the modalities for implementation of Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Aided Schemes which are required to be modified as per the revised instructions issued by the Central Government.
The government was considering appointing a nodal officer, a single nodal agency and an implementing agency for the centrally sponsored and centrally aided schemes of the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department to adopt the revised methodology as per the instructions prescribed by the central government. Similarly, to make available the funds of Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Aided Schemes at the field level through Public Financial Management System, the Finance Department at the Directorate level, Regional Deputy Commissioner level, Assistant Commissioner level, District Social Welfare Officer level as per the instructions given by the Government Circulars at Reference No. 2 and 3

Coordinating Officers: The Coordinating Officers shall work in coordination with the Public Financial Management System (PFSMS) for implementation of Centrally Sponsored and Centrally Sponsored Schemes implemented by the functionaries under them.
3. Unique Coordination Mechanism:- It will work for the implementation of all centrally sponsored and centrally assisted schemes under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department.
Implementation Mechanism:- Considering the revised guidelines issued by the Central Government for the implementation of Centrally Sponsored Schemes through the Public Financial Management System and the guidelines of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes of the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Directorate should declare the scheme-wise and designation-wise implementation mechanism as per the requirement.
5. Separate Single Nodal Bank Account to be opened in a bank having enabled IT system as approved by the Government Circulars dated 1.07.2021 and 15.09.2021 of the Department of Finance for implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme as per the revised modalities of fund disbursement.

If a private bank has been entrusted with the task of disbursing funds for Centrally Sponsored Schemes, such functions should now be entrusted to a bank having a competent IT system as approved by the Government Circulars dated 1.07.2021 and 15.09.2021 of the Department of Finance.
For the Centrally Sponsored Schemes mentioned in paragraph 1 above, the current accounts at the Directorate level, as approved by the Department from time to time, should be converted into savings accounts and used as a single nodal account for the scheme concerned. Similarly, for these Centrally Sponsored Schemes, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare should take action to convert the current accounts in the bank at the level of Regional Commissioner, Assistant Commissioner and Social Welfare Officer into Zero Balance Subsidiary Accounts and add all these bank accounts to the single coordinating account at the Directorate level.
The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare shall fix the defined drawing limits for the withdrawal of funds from the single coordinating account to the zero balance sub-accounts to the implementing agencies.
The Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare shall fix the time limit for spending the unutilized amount in the existing bank accounts at the level of Regional Commissioner, Assistant Commissioner and Social Welfare Officer before converting them into zero balance sub-accounts for the implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes mentioned in paragraph 1 above being implemented by the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department. After this time limit, guidelines regarding the deposit of the amount in the Unique Coordination Account should be issued by the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare.
The Coordination Officers, Single Coordination Agencies (SNAs) and Implementing Agencies (IAs) appointed as per the aforesaid paragraph 1 shall strictly comply with the directions received from the Central Government under Reference No. 1 and the instructions given under Reference No. 2 and 3 of the Department of Finance regarding the fund distribution and utilization management of Centrally Sponsored Schemes.
