Preamble: To develop the habitations/ villages of the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist communities in the urban and rural areas of the State and to provide basic amenities to these communities. The Government has decided to implement Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana at the State level from the year 2018-19 to provide recognition to the works suggested by the people's representatives at the government level. Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme is being implemented on the facility plot of Development Proposal No. 89/129 near Kasarvadavli Police Station on Ghodbunder Road in Thane Municipal Corporation area.
The matter of giving in-principle approval for the construction of Babasaheb Ambedkar Cultural Bhavan and Vipassana Kendra was under the consideration of the government.
Under the Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme, the development proposal near Kasarvadavali Police Station at Ghodbunder Road in Thane Municipal Corporation area at 89/129 mauje Kavesar Survey No. 166/ 10A/ 2 and 166/15 A/ 2 is being approved subject to the recommendation of the concerned Chief Engineer (Architecture) and Chief Engineer (Electrical) regarding in-principle approval for the construction of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Cultural Bhavan and Vipassana Kendra on the facility plot.
The work is being sanctioned at a cost of Rs. 970.53 lakhs. The said expenditure is being sanctioned as per the financial powers conferred under paragraphs 134 (3) of the Financial Powers Rules, 1978, Part-I, Sub-Division-V, Maharashtra Public Works Rules, dated 20th September, 2019 and Government Circular, Public Works Department, dated 25th September, 2019.
