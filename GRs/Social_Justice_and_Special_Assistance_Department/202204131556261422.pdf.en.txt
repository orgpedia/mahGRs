Preamble: As per the decision of the Government, under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme, in principle approval has been given for the construction of a social hall at a cost of less than Rs.15 crore on the available land/ land in the Mumbra-Kalwa area of the local body of Thane Municipal Corporation, Thane. The proposal had been approved by the government.
Government's decision:
Under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme, administrative approval is being granted to the proposal of Thane Municipal Corporation, Thane for the construction of a social hall on the available land/ land in Mumbra Kalwa area at a cost of less than Rs.15 crore. Also, 10% of the project cost i. e. Rs. 1.50 crore is being disbursed in the first phase.
The expenditure on this project should be met from the funds budgeted for the year 2022-23 by covering the expenditure under the following heads:
Demand No.N-3 Main Account Head 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities
Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme (04) (01) Bharat Ratna Dr. J.O. (Program) 27, Small Constructions 2225 F085
