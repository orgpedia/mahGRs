Government's decision:
As per the relevant Government decision dated 1st June, 2006, a total of 353 Government Residential Schools, one in each Taluk, have been approved for the benefit of SC and ST children. Further, as per the decision dated 4th June, 2007, approval has been accorded for identification of 100 Government Residential Schools in the first phase. However, due to non-availability of land for the residential school at Pavani, G. Bhandara, the Government vide G.O. dated 25th April, 2008 has decided to shift the location of the said residential school to Mauje Kesarwada, T.T. Lakhni, G. Bhandara. But, after that, In Lakhni, G Bhandara, too, the school could not be started as there was no space available for a government residential school. Then there is Kondh (Kosra). The change in the location of Government Residential School at Pavani, G. Bhandara has been made as per the following:
