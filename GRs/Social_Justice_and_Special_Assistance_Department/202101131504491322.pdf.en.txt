The proposal: Cabinet sub-committee Pursuant to the decision taken in the meeting held on March 2, 2019, in pursuance of the proposal submitted by the Department regarding implementation of special programmes for the development of Dhangar community on the lines of the ongoing schemes for the Scheduled Tribes by the Tribal Department, the meeting was held on July 30, 2019. The Cabinet of Ministers decided to implement 13 schemes on the lines of tribal for the Dhangar community.
As per the Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Swayam Yojana implemented by the Tribal Development Department dated November 27, 2018, a decision has been issued by the Government in accordance with the A. No. 2 read here regarding the introduction of this scheme for the students of the Dhangar community who are deprived of hostel admission.
The Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Swayam Yojana of the Tribal Development Department has a provision for direct transfer of funds to the Aadhaar linked bank accounts of students for food, accommodation and educational material for higher education. However, since the hostel admission online system for hostel admission under this section does not exist at present, it was under the consideration of the Government to make necessary amendments in the Government Decision dated 6/9/2019 in A. No. 3 read above. The following improvements are being made:
