Preamble: AMRUT has been established on the lines of MAHAJYOTHI to develop students, youth and other candidates from economically weaker sections of the open category through various activities, programmes, etc. This has been done as per the Government decision dated 22.08.2019. Since AMRUT will be implementing various activities, the matter of opening a new account head for making budgetary provisions was under the consideration of Shasana.
Government's decision:
Cabinet of Ministers In the meeting held on 20.08.2019, it has been approved to set up a new autonomous institution, Maharashtra Research, Development and Training Academy (AMRUT). The Accountant General's Office, Mumbai, has taken various initiatives through this institution. As per the informal reference dated 04.12.2020, Demand No. Approval has been given for opening of new head of accounts under ZG-03, head 2225. Details of the new title are as follows. :
