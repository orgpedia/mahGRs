The Proposal:
The Department of Information and Technology has developed a single State level system of Direct Benefit Transfer (Maha-DBT) for delivery of benefits, financial assistance and services in the State since 2018-19. This is as per the Government Decision dated 12/10/2018. Accordingly, in the first phase, 39 scholarship/ free-ship schemes of 6 departments have been implemented in the State DBT and SEWA systems and the implementation of these schemes has been started in the State.
11 courses were approved by the Department of Finance. However, under the said order, the M.A. Details about the course in Tech (Food Technology) are not available. The mapping of this course was not done as the detailed information of the course in Tech (Food Technology) was not received. Accordingly, the government was considering to approve the schemes of reimbursement of scholarship/ tuition fee, examination fee, subsistence allowance for the students of the VJBJ, EMV and VIMA categories under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department on the state DBT and service system.
Government Decision: Government Decision, Agriculture and Padum Department, Dy. Under 05/03/2020, the courses under the Agricultural University have been declared as professional courses with the consent of the Finance Department. Accordingly, in the accompanying" Statement-A," M. The Government has approved the implementation of scholarship/ tuition fee, examination fee, subsistence allowance reimbursement scheme for the students of other backward Bahujan Welfare Department for the courses of Tech (Food Technology), Visva-Bharaj, Imav and Bima categories on Maha DBT and Seva system and payment of the same from the academic year 2021-22. However, the courses mentioned in this government decision will not be allowed any benefit as before.
(2) This Government decision was taken with the consent of the Planning Department and in informal reference to Finance Department No. 183/ Expenditure 14, Sec. Issued pursuant to approval granted under 04/06/2021.
