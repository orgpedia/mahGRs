In order to provide the necessary skills and knowledge to the students of the Scheduled Castes (including neo-Buddhists) and to prepare them for the competitive era in various fields, the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship Scheme for higher education in reputed educational institutions of the country has been implemented under the Government Decision Social Justice, Cultural Affairs, Sports and Special Assistance Department No. EBC 2003/ PR No. 323/ MAVK-2, dated 11 June 2003. By superseding all the earlier Government decisions in respect of the Rules of the Scheme, the revised Rules of the Scheme have been brought into force with effect from the Government decision dated 08.11.2017. In the said Government decision, the Selection Committee headed by the NMU Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, was under the consideration of the Government to conduct the selection process for the year 2022-23 for the students admitted in the reputed educational institutions of the country and to sanction scholarships to the students selected as per the terms and conditions specified in the applications received through the same.
Government Decision: The Selection Committee constituted under the Government Decision, dated 08.11.2017, for Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for SC students to pursue higher education in reputed educational institutions in the country, in its meeting held on 13.12.2022, has approved the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for the year 2022-23 to the total eligible students in Form A-1 to A-4 and Form B-1 to B-2, who have been selected as per the decision of the Government.

2.The students who have been approved the scholarship, the full tuition fee and examination fee fixed by the respective educational institution will be given to the institution through the Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune. The students will be provided accommodation and food as per their choice. The amount will be paid annually. A total amount of Rs.10,000/-per annum will be provided for books, academic materials and other educational expenses required for the course. For the year 2022-2023, for their course expenses, information about tuition fees, examination fees, hostel rent, living allowance and other permissible expenses should be sought from the concerned university/ institution and after obtaining the certificate of progress and attendance of the student, the concerned college/ institution should be paid.
3. The students who have been approved for Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship in the accompanying Forms A-1 to A-4, Form B-1 and Form B-2, the amount of post-matric scholarship received by the Government of India should be reduced and the remaining amount should be paid to the students.
The Commissioner, Social Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being authorized to award scholarships to the said students.
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the provisions of the current financial year under the head" Demand No.N-3, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, 01- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 277-Education, Schemes under Five Year Plans, Schemes under State Schemes." For this, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
