Government Decision: For the financial year 2021-22, a budgetary provision of Rs. 102,77,62, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Two Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand only) has been approved for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Subsidy Scheme for District Establishments and Rs. 39,01,36, 000/- (Rupees Nineteen Crores One Lakh Thirty Six Thousand only) for the establishments on the scheme of financial assistance to the old landless agricultural labourers. Of these, the Finance Department has referred to No. 10% of the budgetary provision approved under the Government Decision herein for the purpose of 01-Salary. 5% of salary and 10% of telephone, electricity and water and 13% of office expenses. 5 As per the decision of the Government here, 10% of the budgetary provision has been distributed to all the Divisional Commissioner's offices for the purpose of 01-Salary. Now, the Department of Finance has allocated 5% of the approved budgetary allocation for the purpose of 01- Salary, 06- Telephone, Electricity and Water and 13 Office Expenditure. Accordingly, the Government hereby approve the release of funds to the tune of Rs. 5,08,29, 650/- (Rupees Five Crore Eight Lakh Thirty-nine Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty only) for Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grants Scheme District Establishment and Rs. 1,94,63, 650/- (Rupees One Crore Ninety-four Lakh Sixty-three Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty only) for the establishment on the said scheme to all the Divisional Commissioner's Offices for the period of one month July and August, 2021 for the purpose of 1st pay as per the statement accompanying this Government decision.
