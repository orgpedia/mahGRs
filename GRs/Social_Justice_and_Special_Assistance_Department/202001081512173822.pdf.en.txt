Introduction: The Foreign Scholarship Scheme (FSS) was launched from 2018-19 to provide opportunities to students of the Visva-Bhaja, EMV and VIMA categories to pursue higher education in reputed educational institutions abroad. The above mentioned date has been made effective by the Government Decision dated 11th October, 2018.
Under the scheme, a total of 10 students from QS and THE world ranking institutions within an average of 200 are selected and granted foreign scholarships for postgraduate degree and PhD courses for students belonging to the Vijabhaj, Emav and Vimapra categories in Maharashtra. Accordingly, the government was considering the matter of approving foreign scholarships to eligible students after examining the applications received by the department under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme for the year 2019-20.
Government Decision: Under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme, a total of 10 students selected by the State Level Selection Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary (Education) under the Government Decision dated 11th October, 2018 are being sanctioned scholarships for higher studies abroad. The details of the selected students are mentioned in the accompanying Form-A.

Also, the students concerned will be entitled to grant of scholarships for the courses and duration mentioned in the government decision and payment of tuition fees of a university during the period of study abroad, if there is an increase or decrease in tuition fees, accordingly.
