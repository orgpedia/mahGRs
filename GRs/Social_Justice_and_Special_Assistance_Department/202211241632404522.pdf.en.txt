circular
In the context of the Government of India's awareness campaign on November 26, 2022, focusing on the fundamental duties of citizens in the Constitution of India, No. Circulars dated 7th and 11th November, 2022, dated 1st and 2nd November, 2022 have been issued to the concerned departments regarding the action to be taken by each department. Similarly, the programme will be held under the programme. The planning of the program to be held on November 26 should be done by the concerned department. Similarly, it has been suggested that the concerned departments, district collectors and coordination officers of the state should upload the details of the programs implemented on the website of the Department of Social Justice (www. doj. gov. in) of the Central Government along with the photos from time to time.
In this regard, the Central Government has issued the following instructions:
Undertaking a pledge (copy enclosed) at 11 AM on 26th November, 2022 with all the employees and stakeholders.
1 Coordinating with with Autonomous Autonomous Bodies, Attached / Subordinate Offices, Organisations and Institutions including educational institutions etc to celebrate the Constitution Day on 26th November, 2022.
2 Organising talks/Webinars covering the constitutional values and the fundamentals of the Indian Constitution.
Follow the instructions given by the central government.
