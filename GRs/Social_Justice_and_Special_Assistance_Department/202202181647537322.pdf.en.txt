Preamble Since the schemes being implemented by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation need to be publicized and promoted to the rural areas, it was under the consideration of the Government to give administrative approval to publicize and publicize the schemes of the Corporation with the message of precautions to be taken in the context of COVID 19.
Government Decision In order to reach the information of the schemes being implemented by the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation to the district/ taluka/ rural areas, administrative approval is being given under this government decision to promote and publicize the information through OCP display boards (with UV printing)/ PVC boards with the message of vigilance regarding COVID-19.
The Directorate General of Information and Public Relations has prepared a plan for the promotion and publicity of the schemes of the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation. The Media Plan has been approved by the Planning and Implementation Committee in its meeting held on 19.01.2022.
Media Planning and Planning and Implementation Committee of Directorate General of Information and Public Relations
A total of Rs. 3,97,96, 508/- (Rupees Three Crores Ninety Seven Lakhs Ninety Seven Thousand Five Hundred Eight only) is being administratively approved to be spent from the available funds as proposed by the Corporation during the financial year 2021-22. No funds have been received from the government in this regard yet.
