the Proposal. -
The Government has decided to directly credit the amount required for providing food, accommodation and other educational facilities to the students directly to the Aadhaar linked back account of the students so that they can pursue further education in professional and non-professional courses in classes 19B, 12B and beyond LQB of the SC / ST category who are eligible for admission to the Government Hostel in the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department but are not admitted to the Government Hostel due to lack of hostel capacity. Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Swadhar Yojana has been launched on 6 January 2017. Extension of the scheme to Taluk level and amendments in the scheme and terms and conditions have been made vide Department of Social Justice, Government Decision, dated 26.12.2024.
Revised guidelines for Post Matric Scholarship have been issued by the Central Government from the year 2020-21. The said instructions have been issued vide Government Resolution dated 7th July, 2023. The revised guidelines of the Central Government Post Matric Scholarship Scheme provide for payment of educational allowance to students. Subject to the Central Government's provision, the Government was contemplating repealing the provision in paragraph 2 (d) (4) of the Government Decision dated 26.12.2024 of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Swadhar Yojana that "the amount of subsistence allowance received under the Government of India Scholarship to a student eligible for benefits under this scheme shall be deducted and the remaining amount of subsistence FAAS allowance, residence allowance, shall be paid."
The Government's Decisions:
In view of the above fact, Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Swadhar Yojana vide Government Decision dated 26.12.2024 stated in paragraph 2 (d) (4), "The student eligible for benefit under this scheme shall be paid the balance of subsistence allowance, meal allowance, residence allowance, by deducting the amount of subsistence allowance received under the Government of India Scholarship." This provision is being repealed.
2. All other provisions, terms, conditions and conditions, except for the aforesaid amendments, shall continue to be in force in respect of beneficiaries of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Swadhar Yojana.
