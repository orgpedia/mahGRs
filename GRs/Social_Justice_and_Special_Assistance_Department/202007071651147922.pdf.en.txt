Memorandum: Under the Centrally Sponsored Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Other Backward Classes Students of the State, the Central Government has sanctioned Rs. 32,00,00, 000/-. has been distributed to the State vide letter dated 12/06/2020. Accordingly, students belonging to Other Backward Classes are entitled to receive Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship of Rs. 32,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Two Crores only), under the head Demand No.ZG-3, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, Welfare of 03 Backward Classes, 277, Education, (02) Scholarships/ Scholarships, (02) (12) Post Matric Scholarship (Centrally Sponsored 100%) Programme (2225 E991) to the students belonging to Other Backward Classes.
The Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Directorate, Pune will be the controlling officer for the scheme. The Controlling Officer shall spend the amount within the limits of the provision made as aforesaid for the prescribed purpose only. After the funds are spent, the Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Directorate, Pune will be responsible for submitting the details of expenditure (district-wise expenditure/ number of beneficiaries) and utility certificate to the government without fail.
Under the above scheme, the Controlling Officer will be responsible for spending the funds subject to verification by the Controlling Officer of the utilization certificates of the current financial year as well as the previous year's expenditure. The Director, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Directorate, Pune shall be responsible for ensuring that no financial irregularity takes place by spending the said fund as per the terms and conditions issued by the Finance Department from time to time as per the Financial Rules, Maharashtra Budget Rulebook.
