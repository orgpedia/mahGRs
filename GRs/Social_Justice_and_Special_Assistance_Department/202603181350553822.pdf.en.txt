The Proposal:
The Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Quality Scholarship Scheme for higher education in reputed educational institutions in the country has been implemented by Government Decision Social Justice, Cultural Affairs, CRODAB Special Assistance Department No.EBC-2003 / P.No.323 / Mavak-II, dated 11th June, 2003 to provide the required skills and knowledge to the students of Scheduled Castes (including Navabhuddha) and to prepare them for the competitive era in various fields. The revised Rules of the Scheme have been implemented vide Government Decision dated 08.11.2017 superseding all earlier Government Decisions regarding the Rules of the Scheme.
As mentioned in the said Government Decision, the Selection Committee headed by the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, vide its letter dated 05.03.2026 has submitted to the Government a proposal for sanctioning scholarships to a total of 17 students namely 9 and 8 respectively for the selected undergraduate and postgraduate courses with the minutes of the proceedings held in the meetings of the Selection Committee held on 18.02.2026 as per the terms and conditions prescribed by the application received by it for conducting the selection process for the students admitted in reputed educational institutions in the country for the year 2025-26. In pursuance of the above proposal, a total of 17 students were under consideration to be given the benefit of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Quality Scholarship Scheme.
Government Decision: - A total of 17 candidates selected at the meetings of the Selection Committee dated 18.02.2025 constituted under the Government Decision, dated 08.11.2017 for the Rajyash Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for SC students to pursue higher education in reputed educational institutions in the country.

The Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for the year 2025-26 is being sanctioned under Form-A-1 and Form-A-2 and Form-B-1B-B-2 attached to the Government Decision.
Students whose SAT aaa scholarship has been approved will be paid the full tuition fee and examination fee determined by the respective educational institution to the institute through the Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune.
Students will be paid in full by the educational institution as per the hostel and meal charges charged by them. The amount of this expense will be paid only once in a year. A total of Rs.10,000 / - will be given every year for course books, educational materials and other educational expenses. The cost of their course should be paid to the college / institution concerned by seeking information from the university / institution concerned regarding tuition fees, examination fees, hostel rent, living allowance, and other allowable expenses for the year 2025-26 and obtaining a certificate of student's progress and attendance.
The students who have been awarded the Rajshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship mentioned in the accompanying Forms-A-1 to Form A-2 and Form-B-1 to Form B-2, the students who have received the Government of India Post-Matro Examination Scholarship, the remaining allowable amount should be paid to the students by deducting the amount.
Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being authorised to provide scholarships to such students. Expenditure in this regard is "Demand No. N-32225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, 01 - Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 277 - Education, Schemes under Five Year Plans, Schemes under State Schemes (04) (22) For Higher Education."
