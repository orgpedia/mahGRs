Cancellation of appointments of Chairperson and non-official members of the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Grant Scheme Committee.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance

Madam Cama Marg, Hutatma Rajguru Chowk,
Mantralaya, Mumbai- 400 032
Date: 12th of March 2020
Government Resolution, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department No.Visyo-2018/ No.62Visyo, Dy. 20/08/2019
Government Circular: Benefit of various schemes implemented in the state under the Special Assistance Program to eligible beneficiaries
The Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme Committees have been constituted as per the decision of the Government in this regard. The chairperson and non-officio members of these committees are appointed by the district collectors on the recommendation of the guardian ministers of the respective districts. The appointments of all the chairpersons and non-officials are hereby cancelled. All concerned should be informed.
The committees headed by the Tahsildar constituted under the decision of the Government in this regard will continue to function till the committees of the newly appointed Chairman and non-official members are constituted after receiving the recommendations of the new Guardian Minister. These committees should hold monthly meetings as per the requirement and take appropriate action on the applications received under this scheme.
