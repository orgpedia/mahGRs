Introduction Maharashtra Research Development and Training Institute (AMRIT) has been established to develop students, youth and other candidates from economically weaker sections of the open category through various activities, programs, etc. and other means. The Company was incorporated on 22.08.2019 and approval for leasing out office space in Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
The Government has approved the reservation of 1st floor space at Tulip and Orchid in World Trade Centre, Mumbai for Maharashtra Research and Development and Training Academy (AMRUT) on lease basis for a period of 1 year.
