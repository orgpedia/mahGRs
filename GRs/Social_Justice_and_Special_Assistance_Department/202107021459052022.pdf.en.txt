3. Ma. The directions came from the meeting held on 21.06.2021 under the chairmanship of the Chief Secretary.
The Proposal:
The names of many villages, hamlets and roads in many cities and rural areas of the state are caste-based. It was approved by the Cabinet on December 2, 2020. Accordingly, the Government have issued a Government Resolution in this regard dated 11th December, 2020.
Also, committees have been formed at the state level, divisional level and district level to implement the decision to rename villages, hamlets and roads in the state.

4. Ma. The above Committee, constituted under the Chairmanship of the Chief Secretary, met on 21st June, 2021. At the meeting, The Chief Secretary directed to include IAS/ Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Revenue) as a member of the State Level Committee constituted under the relevant Government Decision dated 06.05.2021. Accordingly, the government was considering the inclusion of A.M.S./ Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Revenue Department) as a member in this committee.
Government's stand:
The state government has decided to rename all the villages, hamlets and roads in the state. The Committee is headed by the Chief Secretary and includes the Principal Secretary/ Secretary (Revenue) as a member.
