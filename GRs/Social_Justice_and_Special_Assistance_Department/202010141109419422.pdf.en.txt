In order to provide the necessary skills and knowledge to the students of the Scheduled Castes (including neo-Buddhists) and to prepare them for the competitive era in various fields, the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship Scheme for higher education in reputed educational institutions of the country has been implemented under the Government Decision Social Justice, Cultural Affairs, Sports and Special Assistance Department No. EBC-2003/ PR No. 323/ MAVK-2, dated 11 June 2003. By superseding all the earlier Government decisions in respect of the Rules of the Scheme, the revised Rules of the Scheme have been brought into force with effect from the Government decision dated 08.11.2017. In this Government decision, the Selection Committee headed by the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, after conducting the selection process for the year 2019-20 for the students admitted in the reputed educational institutions of the country, a total of 66 students have been given the benefit of this scholarship as per the Government decision dated February 18, 2020. Now, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, in a fresh notification regarding the selection of the remaining students under the scheme, was considering the matter of sanctioning scholarships to the remaining 8 students who qualified as per the prescribed procedure, terms and conditions.
Government Decision: The Selection Committee constituted under the Government Decision, dated 08.11.2017, for Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for SC students to pursue higher education in reputed educational institutions of the country, in its meeting held on 21.01.2020, selected students from Form A-1 to A-2 and the students who were selected in the form of merit list attached with this Government Decision.

A total of 8 eligible students in Form-B1 and B2 are being sanctioned Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for the year 2019-20.
2.The students who have been approved the scholarship, the full tuition fee and examination fee fixed by the respective educational institution will be given to the institution through the Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune. The students will be provided accommodation and food as per their choice. The amount will be paid annually.
A total of Rs.10,000/- per annum will be provided for books, academic materials and other educational expenses. For their course expenses, the information regarding tuition fees, examination fees and hostel rent, living allowance and other permissible expenses for the year 2019/ 2020 should be sought from the concerned university/ institution and the certificate of progress and attendance of the student should be obtained and paid to the concerned college/ institution.
3. The students who have been approved for Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship as mentioned in the accompanying Form-A-1A-2 and Form-B-1& B-2, the remaining admissible amount shall be paid to the students by deducting the amount received as Post Matric Scholarship by the Government of India.
The Commissioner, Social Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being authorized to award scholarships to the said students.
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the provisions of the current financial year under the head" Demand No.N-3, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, 01- Welfare of Scheduled Castes, 277-Education, Schemes under Five Year Plans, Schemes under State Schemes." For this, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
