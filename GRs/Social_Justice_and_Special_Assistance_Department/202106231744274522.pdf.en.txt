The Proposal:
Benefits of various welfare and social security schemes for sugarcane workers in the state
Gopinath Munde as per the Government Decision dated 13.9.2019 of the Department of Industry, Energy and Labour
The Sugarcane Workers' Corporation has been formed as per the Government Decision dated 24.2.2020.
Industries, Energy and Labour Department transferred the Corporation to Social Justice and Special Assistance Department
to meet the administrative and office expenses of the Corporation.
Opening of two separate new heads of accounts for providing funds for capital expenditure.
It was under the consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
The sugarcane workers of the state will get the benefit of various welfare and social security schemes.
Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Workers Corporation to provide office and office for the functioning of the corporation.
The Government proposes to open two separate new heads of accounts for meeting administrative expenses and share capital expenditure.
The decision has been approved.
