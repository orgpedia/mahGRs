Introduction: Various schemes are implemented for the welfare of backward classes through the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. For effective and effective implementation of these schemes, direct visits to the beneficiaries as well as individual beneficiaries of the scheme are required. For this, it is necessary to provide vehicle facilities to the posts to which the duties and responsibilities of implementing and controlling the scheme have been assigned. Accordingly, the vehicles have been made permissible to the officers under the administrative control of the department.
Six vehicles have been made permissible at the headquarters level of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune.
