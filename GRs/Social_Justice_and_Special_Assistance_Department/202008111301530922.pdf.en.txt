The Government is approving the disbursement of Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) amounting to One Hundred and Ninety Nine Lakhs Fifty Five Thousand only subject to the following conditions.
1. Restrictions prescribed by the Government for expenditure under the scheme should be strictly implemented.
Also, the funds should be spent for the same scheme for which the funds have been sanctioned.
2. Streamline the operations to limit the cost of management.
3. Detailed information about the amounts spent on various items and the expenditure incurred on them should be submitted to the government.
4) Cut down on office expenses.
5. The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the debt recovery rate.
6. The funds of the scheme are being disbursed subject to verification of utilization certificates.
7. While spending the above funds, the concerned officers should make use of the funds by complying with the general financial rules, terms and conditions of the scheme, CVC guidelines, CAG observations etc.
8. Reference No. Each of the conditions in Circulars 2 and 3 mentioned herein shall be fulfilled by the Corporation.
The salary subsidy given by the government will have to be provided through ECS in the bank account of the beneficiaries, in no case the salary subsidy amount should be distributed in a combined and offline manner.
The Joint Secretary/ Deputy Secretary (Budget), Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai shall be the Controlling Officer in respect of the above grants. The Cell Officer (Budget), Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department has been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer to collect the amount.
