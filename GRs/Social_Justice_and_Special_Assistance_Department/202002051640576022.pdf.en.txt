Government's decision:
For the financial year 2019-20, Rs. 87,66,39, 000/-for District Establishment and Rs. 33,16,83, 000/- has been approved. As per the Government Circular at Reference No. 2 of the Finance Department, the establishment expenditure on the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Subsidy Scheme for District Establishments and Financial Assistance to Old Landless Agricultural Labourers Scheme is 80% of the total provision for salary and other administrative expenses of the District Establishments i.e. Rs. 70,13,11, 200/- and Rs. 26,53,46, 400/- has been made available out of which an amount of Rs. 52,59,83, 400/- and Rs. 19,90,09, 800/- has been released for the period April to December, 2019 as per the Government decision in Reference No. Now, the remaining amount of Rs. 17,53,27, 800/- (Rupees Seventeen Crores Three Lakhs Seventy Seven Thousand Eight Hundred Only) for District Establishment and Financial Assistance to Old Landless Agricultural Labourers under the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Grants Scheme and the remaining amount of Rs. 6,63,36, 600/- (Rupees Six Crores Thirty Three Lakhs Thirty Six Thousand Six Hundred only) per month for the period January to March, 2020 as per the accompanying statement under this Government Resolution. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
All the Divisional Commissioners are hereby informed that they should allocate the grants distributed as per the prospectus A and B attached herewith to the districts under them as per the requirement and the District Collectors should allocate the grants to the taluks of their districts as per the requirement.
