The Government Hostel for Backward Class Girls, Rajgurunagar, District Pune is a hostel building at a cost of Rs. 1424.61 Attention / - Administrative approval is being given to the construction budget of this amount subject to the following conditions: - a) The prototype map, layout map as well as detailed map should be started only with the approval of the architect before starting the work.
b) Work on a broad budget should be undertaken when dealing with a broad-based provision.
e) Department of Environment, Government Decision No. ENV-2013 / P.S. No.177 / T.A.1, D. Action should be taken as per the guidelines mentioned in Yo January, 2014.
e) The cost of construction of the work should be met from the funds available with the Department of Social Justice.
e) Public Works Department Government Decision No.BDG 2017 / Q.No.60 / Buildings-2, D. Action should be taken under 07.07.2017.
f) Tender notices for the work should be published after obtaining a certificate that the planned site is in the possession of the consumer department.

l) These maps need to be checked by the Chief Architect of the Government of Maharashtra.
