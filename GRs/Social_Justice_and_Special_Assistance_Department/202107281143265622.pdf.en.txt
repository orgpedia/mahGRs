Preamble: Reservation for Other Backward Classes in Local Bodies in the State. In Writ Petition No. 980/2019 filed in the Supreme Court, Shri. In view of the directions contained in paragraph 12 of the judgment dated 4th March, 2021 passed by the Supreme Court, read with the Government Notification No.1, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, dated 29.6.2021, the Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission has been declared as a dedicated Commission for carrying out specific works related to reservation in local bodies. Considering the request made by the said Commission vide letter No. 2, it was under the consideration of the Government to constitute a Coordination Committee.
Government decision: The Supreme Court vide its judgment dated 4th March, 2021 in Writ Petition No. 980/2019 vide Government Notification, Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, dated 29.6.2021 has declared the Maharashtra State Backward Classes Commission as a Dedicated Commission. Considering the request of the Commission to carry out the works prescribed under the said notification, the following Coordination Committee is being constituted to provide manpower, funds and other resources to the Commission
