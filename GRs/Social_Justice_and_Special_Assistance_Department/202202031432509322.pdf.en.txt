The Proposal:
Under the aegis of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government of Maharashtra, three Corporations are functioning with a view to uplift the living standards of people living below poverty line belonging to Scheduled Castes, to provide them a respectable position in the social stream, and to bring about their educational, economic and social development. The Matang community, which has a large number of backward classes in Maharashtra, and 12 similar tribes, are economically, educationally and socially backward.
The Sahityaratna Loktantrik Anna Bhau Sathe Vikas Nigam Limited was established under the Indian Companies Act, 1956 to provide financial benefits in the form of loans for self-employment and employment to the needy beneficiaries by implementing various schemes of the State Government and the National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation (NSFDC) to bring them above the poverty line. The main objective of the Corporation is to implement credit schemes for the economic, educational and social development of the Matang community and 12 similar tribes, which constitute a large number of backward classes in Maharashtra, and to bring them above the poverty line.
Since 1993-94, Janadhikar Anna Bhai Sathe Vikas Nigam has been functioning as the official sister concern of National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation, Delhi. The Sahityaratna Loktantrik Anna Bhau Sathe Vikas Nigam is implementing the National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation's (NSFDC) Term Loan Scheme, Micro Credit Finance Scheme, Mahila Samriddhi Yojana, and Educational Scheme. Under this scheme, the funds sought by the corporation are received by the Sahityaratna Janadhikar Anna Bhai Sathe Vikas Nigam after getting approval from the National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation (NSFDC).
