Preamble: Guidelines for the Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme to be provided by the Government of India to the students belonging to Other Backward Classes category As of September, 2018, the Central Government has increased the income limit of the parents of the students from Rs. 1.00 lakh (Rupees One Lakh only) to Rs. Rs 1.50 lakh. The matter of increasing the income limit in the state was under consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The income limit of the parents of the students availing the benefit of Government of India's Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme (Centrally Sponsored Scheme) for Other Backward Classes is Rs. 1.00 lakh (Rupees One Lakh only) to Rs. The government has sanctioned Rs. 1.50 crore. The central government's list of Other Backward Classes includes most of the state's Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Castes. Therefore, this decision of the government will be applicable for the post-matric scholarship scheme for the people belonging to the Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes.
