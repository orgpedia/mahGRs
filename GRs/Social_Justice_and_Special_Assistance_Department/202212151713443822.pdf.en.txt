Proposal: The government's decision. as on 28.10.2021. As per the approval of the Government, the matter of giving post-facto approval to the funded special school Narsingh Mukabdheer School, Yelegaon, G. Buldhana in Samyak Sankalp Pune Sanchalan, which was approved on grant basis as a" special case," was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: transferred/ transferred before 1st October, 2002. The grant-in-aid sanctioned under Grant-in-Aid 28.10.2021 to Samyak Sankalp Pune Sanchalit Narsingh Mukabdheer School, Yelegaon, G. Buldhana is being sanctioned post facto on grant-in-aid basis subject to the following terms and conditions:
Terms and Conditions: (1) The expenditure on the salary and non-salary matters of the said school shall be brought to the notice of the Legislature by way of a figurative supplementary demand.
2) Once the expenditure on the said school is approved by the Legislature, the proposal regarding the sanction of the post should be submitted to the Finance Department for the approval of the High Level Committee.
3) The school board made the decision. All the terms and conditions of 28.10.2021 shall remain in force.
2. This Government decision is based on the informal reference of Finance Department No. 940/ Expenditure-14, D. is being issued as per the consent received vide 5.12.2022.
