Introduction: Students belonging to the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes who come first in the 10th and 12th class examinations from the state and zonal examination boards. Vasantrao Naik Quality Award. It was held on March 24, 2020.
The government was considering the formation of various committees for the smooth conduct of the event. Accordingly, the government has taken the following steps:
Government Decision: Students and students of the Depressed Castes and Nomadic Tribes and Other Backward Classes who come first in the Class 10 and 12 examinations from the state and zonal examination boards. In order to ensure smooth conduct of the Vasantrao Naik Merit Awards, the Government is approving the constitution of the following Committees.
Programme Controlling Committee: In order to ensure the smooth functioning of the various committees constituted for the purpose of organizing the programme and the tasks assigned to the concerned officers, the Programme Controlling Committee is being constituted from time to time to provide necessary guidance and suggestions to the concerned committees regarding the work.
