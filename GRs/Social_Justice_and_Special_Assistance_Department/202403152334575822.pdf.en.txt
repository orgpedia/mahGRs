The Proposal: -
The flats received by the Department under the Mumbai Hahar Vikas Yojana, 1989-90 are given on lease to NGOs / Corporations implementing special programmes for Women Welfare, Child Welfare, Disability Welfare, Mental Retardation, De-addiction Programme as well as SC / ST and Senior Citizen activities to eligible NGOs subject to the terms and conditions prescribed by the Government in the above Batch No. (1) (2) and (3). The issue of allotment of flats to eligible institutions allotted to NGOs under the scheme was under the consideration of the government.
The Government's Decisions:
The flats received by the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department under the Mumbai Hahar Vikas Yojana, 1989-90 are rented out to NGOs for the above purpose. As per the fresh application received from NGOs regarding allotment of vacant flats under the scheme, the Government is approving allotment of 02 flats to the following organization.
