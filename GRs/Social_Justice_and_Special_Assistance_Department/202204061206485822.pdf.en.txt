Introduction: Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar gave equality to the country through the Constitution of India.
The trio of freedom and fraternity has tried to create unity in the society. Article 46 of the Constitution of India lays down the responsibilities of the state towards the weaker sections of the society. Article 46 of the Constitution of India provides that the State shall promote with special care the educational and economic interests of the weaker sections of the people, and in particular of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes, and shall protect them from social injustice and all forms of exploitation. " It said. In pursuance of the above directions, the State Government is making continuous efforts to provide social justice, economic and social protection to the Scheduled Castes and the oppressed of all kinds of generations. Accordingly, various welfare schemes are being implemented across the state with the aim of holistic development of the Scheduled Castes, deprived weaker sections. In order to make the general public aware of all these schemes and to achieve its objectives, the government has issued a decision to celebrate the" Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Equality Program" in the state on the occasion of Reference Day. The government had been contemplating to provide funds for the purpose.
Government Decision: It has been decided to celebrate" Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Samata Karyakram" in the State from 6 th April, 2022 to 16 th April, 2022.
The Director General, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute, Pune has sanctioned Rs. 2.00 lakh should be made available to the concerned Assistant Commissioner immediately.
