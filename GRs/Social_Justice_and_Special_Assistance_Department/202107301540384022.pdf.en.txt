The Proposal:
Kunbi Samajonnati Sangh was formed with the aim of educational, economic and social development of the majority of backward Kunbi brothers in the districts of Thane, Palghar, Raigad, Ratnagiri and Siddhudurg. Mumbai on 01.10.1920. This social institution. Survey 21-123, 508.25 sq.m. plot at Kurla has been received by the Government. The construction of the new hostel building has begun. Approximately Rs. The estimated cost of the project is Rs. 7.00 crore. The community will provide some funds.
In the budget for 2021-22. As per the announcement made by the Finance Minister, the matter of disbursement of funds for Kunabi Student Hostel at Mulund, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: Rs. 5.00 crore for the financial year 2021-22 (one time only).
The said funds shall be provisionally disbursed from the funds available with the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation or Shamrao Paje Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation subject to the condition of repayment. The proposal will be tabled in the next winter session of the assembly.
The Kunbi Samajonnati Sangha should construct the building of the hostel as per the construction rules of the Public Works Department as well as the existing orders of the government. The corporation should get the necessary agreement done from the institution etc.
This Government decision is based on the Informal Reference No. 4 of the Finance Department. 659/2021/ Expenditure-14D. being issued with the approval granted under 23.07.2021.
