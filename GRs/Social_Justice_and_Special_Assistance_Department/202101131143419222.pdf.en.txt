The proposal: on 30/7/2019 Ma. As per the decision taken in the cabinet meeting, approval has been given to construct 200 entry capacity (100 for boys and 100 for girls) hostels each at the headquarters of the revenue departments of the state of Maharashtra for the meritorious students of the Dhangar community belonging to the nomadic ST-C category.
Accordingly, Government Decision No. 2019/ PR No. 05/ VIJABHAJ-1, dated 6/9/2019 was issued and accordingly, the matter of opening a new head of account for making budgetary provisions was under consideration of the Government.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: The Accountant General's Office, Nagpur, vide its letter No. TM/ Gr. ji/ UOR-SM- 101/402, has decided to open a new head office for the scheme" Construction of Hostel for meritorious students of Post Matriculation from Nomadic Tribes-C (Dhangar) category at the headquarters of the Revenue Departments of the State" under the Special Programme for Development of Dhangar Community. Approved on 25/11/2020. Details of the new title are as follows.
