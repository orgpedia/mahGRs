4. All District Collectors are hereby informed that they shall ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the grant handed over to them under any circumstances. They should send a copy of the reconciliation statement to the audit office of this department and to this office by matching the expenditure made from the grant handed over to them with the expenditure recorded in the office of the Accountant General. Utilization certificate should be sent to the Accountant General's office immediately and its copy should be sent to the planning office of this department and this office.
If the expenditure reconciliation work is not done properly and the utilization certificate is not submitted to the Accountant General's office within the prescribed time, the entire responsibility will be on the concerned district collector.
