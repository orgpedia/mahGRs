Preamble: To provide basic facilities to the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist communities for the development of their habitations/ villages in urban and rural areas of the State. The Government has decided to implement the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme from the year 2018-19 to give recognition to the works suggested by the people's representatives at the government level. Sadar
The activities to be taken up under the scheme will include development of places of cultural and historical importance, monuments and places associated with eminent personalities working for the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist communities.
To bring about cultural development of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist groups (e.g. Library, Study Centre, Dhamma Centre, Vipassana Centre, Training Centre)
The deadline is July 28, 2021. Accordingly, the government was considering declaring" Drawing and Disbursing Officer" and" Controller Officer" for the work to be sanctioned for the year 2021-22 under the" Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development" scheme.
Government's decision:
This decision will be applicable to the works approved under the" Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme" in the budget provision for the year 2021-22 to provide basic facilities in the habitations of Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist communities in urban and rural areas of the state.
