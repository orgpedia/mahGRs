Government Decision: For the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission for Scheduled Castes (SCs) in FY 2022-23, Demand No. N-3, 2210-Public Health, 06, Public Health, 789-Scheduled Caste Sub Plan, (01) Scheme under Scheduled Caste Component Programme (01) (04) National AYUSH Mission (State Share 40%) (Programme) Accounts Head 2210G583, 31-Subsidised Grants (Non-Paid) Below Rs. A sum of Rs. 200.00 lakh has been provided in the Budget.
According to the Finance Department. In the circular dated 04.04.2022, the modalities for disbursement of funds for the financial year 2022-23 have been decided keeping in view the pandemic situation in the State. Accordingly, as agreed by the Department of Finance on the expenditure under the Public Health Department, the budgetary provision for the year 2022-23 for the unutilized funds under the scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) for the year 2019-20 has been increased from Rs. 39.38 lakh is being sanctioned to the Principal Secretary, Public Health Department, Mantralaya, Mumbai as Head of Administrative Department on Budgetary Distribution System (BEAMS).
The funds have been distributed on the budget distribution system. Since the financial position of the State is likely to be very critical, the Controlling Authority should take precautionary measures in the prescribed manner while spending the funds in this distribution. It should be ensured that the funds will be spent this year as per the approved plan in accordance with the current government policy. Also, the report of the expenditure made from the provision made available, account-wise/ sub-account-wise and material objective-wise information achieved, the utilization certificate, the concerned field officer should ensure that it is sent to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, the concerned Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department and Public Health Department by the 10th of every month. The state share should be equal to the Centre's share.
The Head of the Department/ Controlling Officer should ensure that the funds meant for SCs are spent on the same components so that the Government can have control over the expenditure on these schemes.
4. While spending the funds disbursed under the said Government Decision, the Head of the Department and the Controlling Officers shall ensure compliance with the financial rules contained in the Maharashtra Budget Rulebook, Fiscal Rights Rulebook as well as with the Finance Department. The instructions given in the Government Circular dated 04.04.2022 should be strictly followed.
