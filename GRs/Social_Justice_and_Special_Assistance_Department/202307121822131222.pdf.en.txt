The proposal to set up Bhim Park, Vipassana Kendra and Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar Museum in Osmanabad was under consideration of the government for administrative approval.
Government's decision:
Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Welfare Scheme Omar G.
Bhim Park, Vipassana Kendra and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Museum to be set up in Osmanabad
Administrative approval is being granted to the proposal for an amount of Rs. 14,99,42, 000/- subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
The land available for the construction of Bhim Park, Vipassana Center and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Museum in the proposal should first be given in the name of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
The project will be owned by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
After completion of the above work, its maintenance and repair will be done by the local body.
