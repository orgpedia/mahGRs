Preamble: - The Sant Bhagwan Baba Government Hostel Scheme has been launched under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance under the Government Decision dated 15th June, 2021 for the boys and girls of migrant sugarcane harvesters. Under this scheme, a total of 10 YO hostels for boys and 10 for girls (each with a capacity of OO) have been approved to be set up in 10 taluks in three districts of the state, namely Beed, Jalna, Ahmednagar, in the first phase. To provide necessary facilities to the children admitted in the Sant Bhagwan Baba Government Hostel, Reference No. Under the Government Decision No. 4, the purchase of essential commodities / materials has been approved by the Administrator. Accordingly, the Managing Director, Lokneta Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Harvesting Workers Corporation, Pune implemented the e-tendering process and submitted the tender to the concerned No. The approval of the L-1 tender in the e-tendering process was under consideration of the Government as per the proposal submitted to the Government under letter No. 5.
Government Decision: - As per the proposal submitted by the Managing Director, Lokneta Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Harvesting Workers Corporation, Pune under reference No. 5, the total amount for providing facilities to the admitted students of Sant Bhagwan Baba Government Hostel under Social Justice and Special Assistance Department is Rs. The said THAT is required to provide necessary facilities to the students enrolled in the scheme.
In connection with the purchase of materials / items, it shall be mandatory to comply with the following terms and conditions: -
1. It shall be mandatory to supply necessary materials / items in Sant Bhagwan Baba Government Hostels as per the terms and conditions of the e-tendering as well as the specifications contained in the tendering.
2. The Managing Director, Loknayak Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Harvesting Workers Welfare Corporation, Pune, on receipt of the said order, shall issue immediate supply orders to the Purbathadara.
3. Supplies of required materials / items shall be made at their own cost within the time limit specified in the tender from the date of receipt of supply order. If the supplier does not deliver within the stipulated time, penal action will be taken against the supplier as per the terms and conditions of the tender.

D. The Supplier Organization, within 8 days of receipt of the Purbatha order, signed an agreement with the Managing Director, Lokneta Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Workers Corporation, Pune, for payment of Rs. It will be mandatory to sign the contract on stamp paper of 500 / -.
Supplier organizations should submit samples to Managing Director, Lokneta Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Workers Corporation, Pune as per the requirement of the items to be supplied. They should get the items inspected by the approved agency as per the provision prescribed under the Government Decision, dated 1.12.2016 of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour regarding whether the required materials / items are as per the standards in the Government hostels and the cost of sample testing should be borne by the respective bidders.
No amount will be paid in Sant Bhagwan Baba Government Residences other than the sanctioned price as the prices of essential materials / items are inclusive of Serb tax and cost. Hence, the supplier will be responsible for paying the government taxes determined by the government, including GST. Submission of invoices for supply of materials / goods will be required to separately mention the price of the goods as well as the amount of all taxes if GST B is admissible. Also, the cost per item / material includes all aspects like cost of installation / repair, out-of-pocket expenses, etc.
The supplier shall take the receipt (delivery 0191191) of receipt of goods / materials after delivery of the above mentioned materials to the respective hostels along with the dated signature and stamp of the Hostel Owner concerned. Thereafter, the invoice, along with the delivery challan as per the number of items / materials received, should be submitted by the concerned supplier to the Managing Director, Lokneta Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Workers Corporation, Pune.
The expenditure for this should be met from the financial provision available with the Managing Director, Lokneta Gopinath Munde Sugarcane Workers Welfare Corporation, Pune.
The Government reserves the right to cancel the supply order at any stage if any technical issues or other issues / complaints arise regarding the supply of goods / materials to Sant Bhagwan Baba Government Hostels.
