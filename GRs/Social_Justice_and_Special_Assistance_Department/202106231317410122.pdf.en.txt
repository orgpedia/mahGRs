The Proposal:
According to Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, the 7.10.2016 as per the directions of the Central Government. As per the notification dated 4.1.2021, the posts in Government service for Divyangs for the posts in the cadre of Group-A to Group-D on the establishment of the Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune under this Department. have been ensured as per the Government Decision dated 24.02.2021. However, the posts in the offices of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute, Pune, State Backward Classes Commission, Pune and Scheduled Castes and Tribes Commission, Worli were not earmarked for the disabled. The government was considering the issue of reserving posts in the offices of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute, Pune, State Backward Classes Commission, Pune and Scheduled Castes and Tribes Commission, Worli for the disabled.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per Section 33 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, in order to ensure the posts under the State Government, the Central Government vide notification dated 4.1.2021 has notified the list of posts reserved for persons with disabilities. According to the notification, the posts in the list published by the Central Government, whose duties and responsibilities, nature of work and prevailing pay scale are similar to the post in the State Government Service, although the designations of such posts are different, will be ensured for the disabled in the State Government Service. 5 decisions have been made.
