The Proposal:
Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Adharsh Gram Yojana (PMAJAY) for the State of Maharashtra, an amount of Rs. 27,35,60, 000 (Rupees Twenty Seven Crores, Thirty Five Lakhs, Sixty Thousand). The Finance Department has informed that the funds have been received from the Central Government in the Consolidated Fund of the State. Accordingly, the government was considering transferring this fund to the SNA account of the Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana.
Government's decision:

In order to ensure effective management of funds of Centrally Sponsored Schemes and to bring more efficiency in the process of allocation of funds, the Central Government had directed the concerned administrative department to appoint a Single Nodal Agency (SNA) for implementation of each Centrally Sponsored Scheme as per the revised methodology. The Single Nodal Agency (SNA) account has been opened under the government's decision.
