Introduction: The Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Overseas Scholarship Scheme has been implemented since 2003 to provide opportunities to Scheduled Caste students to study in reputed educational institutions abroad. The Rules of the Scheme have been amended from time to time and the revised Rules of the Scheme have been laid down vide Government Order dated 27.06.2017. Further, the increase in the number of students eligible for scholarships under the scheme from 50 to 75 has been effected as per the Government decision dated 30.6.2018.
A total of 75 students recommended by the Selection Committee under the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Overseas Scholarship Scheme in the year 2020-21 have been selected as per the relevant Government Decisions dated 29th September, 2020 and 5.11.2020.
Meanwhile, a large number of students selected for both State and Central Government Overseas Scholarships for Scheduled Caste students are accepting the Central Government Scheme, leaving some seats vacant in the State Government Overseas Scholarship Scheme. Therefore, the students belonging to the Scheduled Castes who qualify under the Foreign Scholarship Scheme of the State Government as per the merit list of the respective selection list should not be deprived of the benefit of the Foreign Scholarship Scheme and in order to fill up the full 75 seats under the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Foreign Scholarship Scheme of the State Government, the Government has decided to keep a waiting list in proportion to the total number of seats under the scheme to select the students who qualify as per the merit list of the respective selection list. It was approved in March 2021.

Accordingly, out of the total 75 students selected in the year 2020-21 under the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Foreign Scholarship Scheme, 9 students have demanded to cancel their selection under the State Government's Foreign Scholarship Scheme for various reasons. A meeting of the selection committee was held. In the meeting, the government was considering the selection of 9 students from the waiting list recommended by the selection committee to the vacant seats under the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Foreign Scholarship Scheme for the year 2020-21.
Government Decision: The Selection Committee constituted under the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Pardes Scholarship Scheme for the year 2020-21 under the Chairmanship of Principal Secretary, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, has approved the sanction of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Pardes Scholarship for higher education in the respective foreign universities to a total of 09 students in the selected form-" A-1,"" A-2" and" A-3" as per the terms and conditions of the relevant Government Decision.
Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being authorized to grant admissible scholarships to the students selected for overseas scholarships. They should take action to sanction the scholarship admissible to the student concerned only after taking the guarantees prescribed under the government decision from the students selected for foreign scholarship under this government decision and examining the original documents of the students. The Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune is being empowered to sanction scholarships to the concerned students for the courses and duration mentioned in the government decision and also to sanction scholarships payable accordingly if there is an increase or decrease in the tuition fees of a university during the period of study abroad of the students.
3. Students who have been sanctioned foreign scholarships by the Government are required to serve the country after completing their education and benefit from their knowledge. In this regard, it will be mandatory for the concerned students to write a letter of guarantee (BOND) before approving the scholarship.
The students selected for the foreign scholarship must have completed their Master's degree or PhD course abroad within the prescribed time period. Under no circumstances will the student be given an extension or increased cost.
The amount of scholarship will be recovered in full from the student selected for the foreign scholarship if the student changes the place or course of the educational institution, university or course approved for higher education.
