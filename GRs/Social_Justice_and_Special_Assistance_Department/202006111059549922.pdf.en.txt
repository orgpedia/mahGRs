Preamble: The State Pay Revision Committee, 2017 was constituted under the Chairmanship of Shri K.P. Bakshi, Retired Additional Chief Secretary, Government of India to revise the pay scales of State Government and other eligible employees, taking into consideration the decisions of the Central Government on the recommendations of the 7th Central Pay Commission. The Government has decided to accept the recommendations of the Committee with certain modifications. The government has taken 1 decision.
