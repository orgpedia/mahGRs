Preamble: To promote higher education for the students belonging to Other Backward Classes in the light of their educational interests, to empower them academically and financially.
Also, the government was considering to implement an education loan interest reimbursement scheme to provide interest free loans to students for education.
Government Decision: Under this Government Decision, it is being approved to implement the educational loan interest reimbursement scheme for students belonging to Other Backward Classes through the Maharashtra State Other Backward Classes Finance and Development Corporation.
The scheme and procedure will be as follows:
Objective of the scheme:
To reimburse the interest on the educational loan amount available through the bank for higher education to the students of Other Backward Classes category who have taken admission in state, domestic and international courses.
To encourage students to pursue higher education in the best interest of their education.
Educational and economic empowerment.
To provide interest on loans to students for education.
Objectives:
All the district offices will be assigned more or less financial and physical targets as per the scale of demand and requirement.
