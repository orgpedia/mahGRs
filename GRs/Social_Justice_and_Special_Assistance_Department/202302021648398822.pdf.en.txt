Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. Decision No.: 2012/ Q. No. 116/ Education-2, dated 2nd July, 2012. Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Govt. Decision No.: 2015/ Q. No.11/ Education-2, dated 18th June, 2015 and dated 17th November, 2015
Decision of the Government: As a special case, the Government has decided to fill up 10% of the seats falling vacant every year in all Government Hostels, except the hostels of meritorious students in Government Hostels run for Backward Class students under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
As per the provisions of this Government decision, the list of students to be admitted as private students in the Government Hostels for the year 2022-23 in the Backward Classes is being approved under this Government decision.
The students in the above list should be admitted subject to the following conditions: The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare concerned should ensure that no more students than the sanctioned student capacity of the hostel are admitted while admitting the hostel.
The students are required to submit the Bonafide Certificate of the current year of the school/ college in which they are studying to the Home Guard of the hostel concerned.
All the Assistant Commissioners, Social Welfare should complete the process of admission to private sector immediately and submit the compliance report to the government through the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune within 30 days.
