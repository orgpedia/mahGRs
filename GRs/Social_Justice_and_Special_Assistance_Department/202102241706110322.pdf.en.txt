Government's decision:
An amount of Rs. 25,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores only) has been sanctioned through Supplementary Demand in the Monsoon Session for implementation of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme for Scheduled Tribe beneficiaries during the financial year 2020-2021.
Accordingly, the Finance Department has approved the release of funds amounting to Rs. 25,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores only) which was sanctioned in the Monsoon Session.
Accordingly, the Government hereby approves the release of Rs. 25,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Crores only) towards expenditure for the period October, 2020 to March, 2021 for the implementation of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme as per the accompanying statement under the Government decision. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
2. All the Collectors are informed that in the accompanying statement, Column No. 3 Funds have been distributed to the respective districts on the new CO Code. The grants distributed by them should be distributed to the taluks of their districts in proportion to the beneficiaries. The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
