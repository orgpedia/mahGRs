Government order:
The Maharashtra Provident Fund Rules have been made applicable to the teaching/ non-teaching staff of recognized Non-Government Aided Divyangjan Special Schools/ Workshops with effect from 1st April, 2003. Further, as per the Government Order dated 28.01.2003 of this Department, the Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, Mumbai City/ Suburbs and all District Social Welfare Officers, Zilla Parishad have been authorised to maintain the accounts of the Provident Fund of the said offices. As per the Government Order dated 14.10.2004 of this Department, the Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune has been declared as the" Head of Department" for the establishment matters of the Commissionerate of Divyang Welfare, District level offices, all educational/ training institutions for Divyangjan under the control of the Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan. Further, under the Government Order dated 17th May, 2010, the Commissioner, Differently Abled Welfare, Pune has been empowered to approve the final disbursement of Provident Fund.
Proposals for final disbursement of provident fund are submitted to the District Office by the concerned institution and to the Commissionerate of Differently Abled Welfare by the District Office. Also, if there are any errors in the proposal, then the employees of the concerned social welfare office in the state or the employees of the organization have to follow up with the Commissionerate for its rectification. or
