The government's decision:
A total of 117 posts have been sanctioned for the establishment of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance (itself) Ministry, out of which 6 temporary posts have been made available for the functioning of the National Integration, Universal Fraternity and Thought Promotion Committee set up under this Department. Orders for extension of these 6 temporary posts are issued separately. Out of the remaining 111, a total of 23 posts indicated in the accompanying return are to be continued from date 01.09.2025 to date 28.02.2026 with reference no. 8 here. The approval was granted by a Government Decision dated 10.09.2025.
2. Subject to the conditions contained in the Government Decision, Finance Department No. Post-2016 / Q. No. 8/16 APCCD 28.02.2026, these 23 temporary posts shall now be demerged as indicated in the accompanying return. 01.03.2026 that is. The deadline is being extended to 31.08.2026.
