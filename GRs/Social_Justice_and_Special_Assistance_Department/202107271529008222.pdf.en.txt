The Proposal:
The Department of Information and Technology has developed a single State level system of Direct Benefit Transfer (Maha-DBT) for delivery of benefits, financial assistance and services in the State since 2018-19. This is as per the Government Decision dated 12/10/2018. Accordingly, in the first phase, 39 scholarship/ free-ship schemes of 6 departments have been implemented in the State DBT and SEWA systems and the implementation of these schemes has been started in the State.
Read 2. 1 Government Decision, Agriculture and Padum Department, d. Under 05/03/2020, the courses under the Agricultural University have been declared as professional courses with the consent of the Finance Department. Also Read No. 2 in the order. As per the letter dated 18/08/2020, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Bahujan Welfare, Pune has sent a proposal for grant of scholarship, friship to the above mentioned courses for the approval of the Government. Accordingly, the government was considering to approve the schemes of reimbursement of scholarship/ tuition fee, examination fee, subsistence allowance for the students of the VJBJ, EMV and VIMA categories under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department on the state DBT and service system.
Government Decision: Government Decision, Agriculture and Padum Department, Dy. Under 05/03/2020, the courses under the Agricultural University have been declared as professional courses with the consent of the Finance Department. Accordingly, the scheme of reimbursement of scholarship/ tuition fee, examination fee, subsistence allowance to the students of the Visva-Bharati, EWS and Insured Category under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department for the courses in the" Statement-A" attached with it is being approved to be implemented on the Maha DBT and Seva System from the academic year 2021-22 and to pay the said amount. However, the courses mentioned in this government decision will not be allowed any benefit as before. The decision will be applicable only to 10 courses in colleges under the Government Agricultural University.
(2) This Government decision was taken with the consent of the Planning Department and in informal reference to Finance Department No. 183/ Expenditure-14, D. Issued pursuant to approval granted under 04/06/2021.
