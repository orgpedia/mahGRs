The Proposal:
Ashramshalas/ Vidyaniketan/ Ashramshalas for the children of sugarcane workers for the Vimukt Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Special Backward Classes are run by this department by providing grants to NGOs in the state.
Every year, a provision is made in the budget of the department to increase the facilities in the ashramshalas and hostels of the department. However, there is no clear direction as to what facilities can be provided to the ashramshalas and hostels of the department from this budget provision. Therefore, considering the lack of clarity on what facilities should be provided to the ashramshalas and hostels of the department, the government was considering issuing a directive in this regard.
Government's decision:
Every year, the government makes a provision in the budget of the department to increase the facilities in the residential ashram and hostel under this department. The following instructions are being given as to what facilities should be provided to the ashramshalas and hostels of the department from the available provisions.
Educational innovations, virtual classrooms for ashramshalas, computerization of ashramshalas, clean drinking water machines, etc. will be provided by the government to the needy institutions.
In this regard, other matters that are beneficial in the interest of newcomers and students in the field of education will also be included with the approval of the government at that time.
