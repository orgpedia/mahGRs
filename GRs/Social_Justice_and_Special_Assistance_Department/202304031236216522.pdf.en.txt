Government circular
The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India has launched the National Helpline for Senior Citizens- 14567 in all the States. The objective of the National Helpline is to create a credible National Helpline for redressal of grievances of senior citizens of India to bring about a positive change in the lives of senior citizens, as well as to provide care and better services to elderly persons affected by abuse.
The National Helpline for Elderly in the State of Maharashtra is being run by Jan Seva Foundation, Pune in association with Rashtriya Samaj Raksha Sansthan, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government of Maharashtra and Jan Seva Foundation, Pune. The helpline is toll-free and provides information, guidance and emotional support to address grievances of senior citizens and improve the quality of life of the elderly. Also, homeless elderly persons are being provided shelter in old age homes as a help and efforts are being made to reunite them with their families.
The response system has started working in the respective areas in our state, along with providing information and assistance on pension, senior citizen schemes, legal issues, homelessness and abuse of the elderly, etc.
The Government of India has decided to extend the services of this helpline across the country.
In order to make this service available to all the elderly in the state of Maharashtra, the Regional Representative (FRO) at the district level of Jan Seva Foundation has started work in all the districts. However, in this regard, the concerned representatives will meet you to give more information about this concept and seek your cooperation in increasing the services of Elder Line 14567 from the district.

The representatives concerned will need the support of their department/ office to work on the following:
District Social Welfare Officer/ Assistant Commissioner to coordinate the Elder Line representative and support the collection of basic information of the service providers/ organization for the elderly in the district.
Identify competent community based organizations as support organizations for senior citizens in the district and invite existing senior citizen organizations in the district for meetings.
Under Secretary, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, to disseminate/ publish advertisements related to information and public awareness on National Helpline to the general public through its department and to appeal to all the media to disseminate this information at all levels through Information Officers.
