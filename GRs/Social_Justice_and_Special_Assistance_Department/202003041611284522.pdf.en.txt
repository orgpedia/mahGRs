Introduction: The Social Justice Department runs government hostels for backward class students and government residential schools for scheduled caste and neo-Buddhist boys and girls in the state. Under the scheme for providing facilities to educational institutions of backward classes, the government was considering the purchase of necessary items for the government hostels and hostels attached to residential schools.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: As per the proposal submitted by the Commissioner, Social Welfare Commissionerate, Pune under Reference No. 5, under the facilities to be provided to the students admitted in the Backward Classes Government Hostels and Hostels Department attached to Government Residential Schools under the Department of Social Justice, administrative approval is being given for the purchase of materials/ items required for the students of Government Hostels and Government Residential Schools through e-tender/ GeM portal as per the demand, at a total cost of Rs. 38,70,25, 214/- (Rupees Thirty-eight crore seventy-five thousand two hundred and fourteen only) as mentioned in the following section.

The terms and conditions in respect of the aforesaid procurement are as follows: 1. Procurement to be made through e-tendering/ GeM portal (GEM) portal as per prescribed procedures and subject to prescribed terms and conditions.
(a) The supplier shall submit samples to the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, as per the requirement of the goods to be supplied. They have to approve these samples and hand them over to the concerned agency.
(b) The supplier shall supply the goods to the Superintendent/ Home Guard of the hostel concerned by showing the above approved samples.
(c) The Superintendent/ Housekeeper shall submit the receipt of the goods from the supplier to the Assistant Commissioner Social Welfare, Pune of the concerned district.
(d) The Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune should take care to verify the actual number of students in the hostel section attached to the government hostels and government residential schools.
For this, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune is being declared as the Drawing and Disbursing Officer.
