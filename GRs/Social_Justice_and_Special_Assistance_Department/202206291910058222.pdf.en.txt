The proposal: In the meeting held on 23 rd February, 2022 under the Chairmanship of Minister, Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, in principle approval has been given for construction of Sant Rohidas Bhawan at Ashok Meal Compound, Dharavi, Mumbai under Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme at a cost of Rs. 5.00 crore. The proposal for construction of Sant Rohidas Bhawan at Ashoka Meal Compound, Dharavi, Mumbai was under consideration of the Government for grant of administrative approval.
Government's decision:
Proposal for construction of Sant Rohidas Bhawan at Ashok Meal Compound, Dharavi, Mumbai at a cost of Rs. 5.00 crore under Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana The administrative approval is being granted subject to the provisions of Sections 1 and 2 as well as the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
The proposal should be in accordance with the terms and conditions, provisions and rules of the present scheme.
The proposal should be in accordance with the provisions of the Financial Powers Rules i.e. with the recommendation of the Maharashtra Housing Area Development Authority (MHADA).
The proposal should be in accordance with the provisions of Public Works Department Circular dated 25th September, 2019 for grant of administrative approval.
