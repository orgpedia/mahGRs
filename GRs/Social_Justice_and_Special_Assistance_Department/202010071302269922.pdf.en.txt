Declaration of Drawing and Disbursing Officer (DDO) and Controller for Mahajyoti Sanstha.
The Government of Maharashtra
Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department
Government Resolution No. MAHAJYO-2020/ S.No.124/ Corporations
Ministry, Extension Building, Mumbai-32
Date: 4th of September 2020
Reference:- Department of Health, Family Welfare, Govt. 08/08/2019
The proposal: The Cabinet As per the decision taken in the meeting held on 30th July, 2019, the Government of India has decided to implement the following decisions: The Government has approved the establishment of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (MAHAJYOTI) under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013. Accordingly, the matter of declaring the Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Controller Officer for the Mahajyoti Sanstha was under the consideration of the Government.
Government Decision: For the purposes of" Demand No. ZG-3, Account Head (2225F 497)" for the schemes being implemented by Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti), Chief Accounts and Finance Officer, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti) has been declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer and Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare Department, Nagpur has been declared as Controlling Officer.
