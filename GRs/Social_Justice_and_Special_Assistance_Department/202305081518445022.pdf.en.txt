To get funds for the beautification and other works of the Samadhi Sthal of Shrimant Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj in the limits of Kolhapur Municipal Corporation.
The Government of Maharashtra
Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance
Government Circular No: Savio-2021/ PR No.132/ Ajak
Ministry Extension, Mumbai- 400 032.
Date: 28th of April, 2023.
Government Resolution, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, dated March 9, 2018.
Government Resolution, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, dated July 28, 2021.
Government Resolution, Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, dated June 28, 2022.
Preamble: Under the Government decision of Reference No. 3 for the beautification and other works of the Samadhi Sthal of Shrimant Rajshri Shahu Maharaj in the limits of Kolhapur Municipal Corporation, Rs. 9,40,56, 108/- has been approved by the Government. In this case, the government was considering relaxing the condition in the government decision of Reference No. 2 that the land of the project should be in the name of the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance.
GOVERNMENT DECISION: In order to beautify the Samadhi Sthal of Shrimant Rajshri Shahu Maharaj within the limits of Kolhapur Municipal Corporation and to carry out other works, the land of the project will be required to be transferred in the name of Social Justice and Special Assistance Department, the condition in paragraph 3 of the Government Decision of Reference No. 2 is being relaxed in this case.
