Government Decision: Mahatma Phule Backward Classes Development Corporation (MPBCDC) Through Mumbai, various schemes are implemented for the economic upliftment of the neo-Buddhist sections of the Scheduled Castes. The Corporation has been provided with 36 Subsidiary Grants (Salaries) for meeting the expenditure on salaries for the financial year 2021-22. A budgetary provision of Rs 10,00,00 thousand has been made.
Out of this, the Finance Department has made available 60% of the available funds on the BEAMS system at Rs. The Government is granting approval for release of funds upto Rs. 6,00,00 thousand (Rupees Six Crores only) subject to the following conditions:
1) Restrictions set by the government for spending in non-plan funds should be strictly implemented.
2) Limit the cost of operations.
3) The details of the amount spent on various matters and the expenditure incurred on it should be submitted to the government.
4) The corporation should make efforts to effectively implement the rate of recovery of loans.
5) Reduce the cost of living.
The Deputy Secretary (Corporations), Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Mantralaya, Mumbai will be the Controlling Officer in respect of this managerial grant. The amount is being handed over to the Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Navi Mumbai for further action. They are being empowered to disburse this grant to the Mahatma Phule Backward Classes Development Corporation through payment in the form of M.C.N.44.
