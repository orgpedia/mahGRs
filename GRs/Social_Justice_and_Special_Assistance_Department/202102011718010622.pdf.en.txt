Government's decision:
The Government is approving the revised pay structure for non-teaching staff of Government recognized Non-Government Aided Social Work Colleges under the control of Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance with effect from 1st January, 2016 as per 7th Pay Commission as shown in Form A attached.
Government Notification of Fixation of Pay and Payment of Arrears in the Revised Pay Structure Department of Finance, Govt. January 30, 2019, Government Circular Department of Finance. February 20th, 2019. May 14, 2019 and d. Ordered on May 30, 2019.
(2) To accept the revised pay structure, the non-teaching staff of Government recognized Non-Government Aided Social Work Colleges shall give their choice in the form of Annexure-I attached to this Government Order within one month from the date of issue of this order. They will also submit a bond in the form of a double bond. In case the option is not received within the prescribed time limit, the employee shall be deemed to have opted for the revised pay structure on and from the 1st day of January, 2016. In such a case, the head of the office will make a bond in the form of an addendum to this government decision from such employees. The designation of cadre wise officers and staff in Government recognized non-government aided social work colleges, pay scale and level etc. are as shown in Form' A'.
If any doubts arise regarding the rules for payment of salary in the revised pay structure, the cases should be submitted to the Commissioner, Social Welfare Commissionerate, State of Maharashtra, Pune. If necessary, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune should submit such cases to the government.
