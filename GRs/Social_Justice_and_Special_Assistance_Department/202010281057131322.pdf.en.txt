Government's decision
Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Institute of Research, Training and Human Development (SARATHI) has been awarded 31 Subsidiary Grants (Non-Payable) for the financial year 2020-21. A budget provision of Rs. 38.00 crore has been made. Out of this provision, Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), a company will be provided Rs. The Government have approved release of Subsidiary Grants (other than salary) amounting to Rs. 11.40 crore (Rupees Eleven Crore Forty Lakh only) subject to the following conditions:
(1) The funds may be spent on activities and welfare schemes approved by the Board of Directors as per the" Memorandum of Association" of the Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), a company.
(2) The Managing Director and the Chief Accounts Financial Officer shall jointly submit the utilization certificate of the expenditure of funds in connection with the activities and welfare schemes implemented by the Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), a company.
Joint Secretary (Budget) will be the Controlling Officer of the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department.
Further, the Chief Accounts and Financial Officer of Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Research, Training and Human Development Institute (Sarathi), Pune, shall collect the amount from the Treasury Office, Pune as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer." They are being empowered to disburse the grant in the form of payment in Rule 44 of the Maharashtra Treasury Rules, 1968.
