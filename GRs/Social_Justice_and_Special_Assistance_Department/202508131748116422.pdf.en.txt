Under the 07.03.2024, a permanent government committee has been formed to maintain, repair and preserve the monument.
Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI), Pune is looking after the maintenance and repair work of the memorial at Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Mukti Bhoomi Smarak Yavla, District Nashik.
Accordingly, the government was considering handing over the memorial to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI), Pune.
Government Decision: - Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI), Pune is being entrusted with the management, internal maintenance, security and other ancillary functions of the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Mukti Bhoomi Memorial, Yevla G. Nashik. The cost of the present case should be met from the funds available with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI), Pune. If additional funds are required, a proposal for the same should be submitted to the government.
2. AST, Social Welfare, Pune to take up the Vastu transfer of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Mukti Bhoomi Smarak, Yela G. Nashik Memorial and submit a report to the Government.

4. Reference Government Decisions. The Government Committee constituted permanently for the maintenance, repair and preservation of the monument under 07.03.2024 is being dissolved by the present Government decision.
