Preamble: As per the Government Decision dated 09.03.2018 of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme, the condition No. In 1, the funds given to the district council, municipal corporation, municipality, nagar panchayat are generally allowed to be spent in two financial years. The municipal corporation, the district panchayat, the zilla parishad and the taluk panchayat are the members of this committee. It has been said that the work approved in this scheme will be allowed to be spent till the next financial year. Further, as per the guidelines dated 06.06.2008 of the Finance Department, the Government has allowed the Zilla Parishad, Municipal Corporation, Municipality, Nagar Panchayat, Authorities to spend the funds released and remaining unspent in a financial year till the end of the next financial year. The policy decision has been taken in accordance with the Government Decision dated 16th October, 2023.
Government Decisions: Under the Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Social Development Scheme, development works have been approved in the entire state under various government decisions for the financial year 2021. Development works to be taken up in 2021-22 The Finance Department has decided to spend the funds drawn from the treasury but lying unutilized in the bank account before March 31, 2023. By the Government Decision of October 16, 2023. The deadline has been extended to February 28, 2024. It has been notified that the funds lying unutilized after 28.02.2024 shall be mandatorily deposited with the Government by 5.03.2024 and necessary action shall be taken accordingly.
The Finance Department has also taken a decision. The funds released in 2019-20 but lying unutilized as on 24.09.2021 have been allowed to be spent up to 31.03.2022. The remaining unspent funds as on 31.03.2019 are required to be deposited with the Government immediately.
