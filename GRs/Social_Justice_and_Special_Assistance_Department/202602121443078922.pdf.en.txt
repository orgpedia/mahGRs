The Proposal: -
A total of 353 government residential primary schools, one in each taluk, for boys and girls belonging to scheduled castes and neo-Buddhists, have been approved to be set up in the state by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Accordingly, in the first phase, Yoo Government Primary Residential Schools have been approved and a total of 92 Government Residential Schools have been started till date. Government Residential Schools are doing a commendable job in ensuring the economic, social and educational progress of the students enrolled in Government Residential Schools. The students admitted in Government Residential Schools under the Department are provided with facilities like free accommodation, food arrangements, health facilities, sports facilities, educational materials, boarding and lodging, living allowance, etc. as per the provision prescribed under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government Decision, dated 26th July, 2011. Pursuant to the proposal submitted by Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune under reference No. 3, the Government was considering the purchase of track suits and boots (canvas / sports) and jerkog / sweaters for the students of Government Residential Schools for this academic year.
The Government's Decisions:
