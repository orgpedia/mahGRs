The Proposal: -
Senior citizens should be able to spend their old age in a better way. The Senior Citizens Corporation has been constituted under Government Decision No. 92 AER, 2024 to facilitate their ease of living in the society, maintain physical / mental health, financially empower them in old age, provide medical assistance, protection from harassment, security, assistance in protection of life and property, shelter, accident insurance cover, etc. Under the Senior Citizens Policy-2013, financial planning for senior citizens, taking care of the health of senior citizens, and empowering senior citizens to cope with stress.
The Corporation's shareholding for these purposes is Rs. 50 crores has been fixed. Government to open new head of accounts to budget funds for the Corporation
was under consideration.
Government Decision: - The Senior Citizens' Council is being approved to open new capital and accounts headings as follows.
