Commissioner, Differently Abled Welfare, Pune As per the letter dated 22.11.2021, the District Social Welfare Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nashik and Jalgaon were informed to submit a detailed proposal regarding the said school. The District Social Welfare Officer, District Council, Nashik, has issued orders in this regard. The report has been submitted to the Commissionerate vide letter dated 10.12.2021. The District Social Welfare Officer, Zilla Parishad, Nashik, has inspected the school and submitted the inspection report to the Commissionerate. The Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune verified the report and found that the school was not providing online education. It has also been stated that the physical facilities are incomplete and the school management and administration are not able to run the school. However, the Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune has stated in his report that the new institution and the parent institution have given their consent for the transfer and shifting of the school. District Social Welfare Officer, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon also paid tributes to him. As per the letter dated 1.12.2021, the Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune has been informed that no school/ workshop for children with disabilities is functioning in Pachora taluka regarding the said school. The District Social Welfare Officer, Zilla Parishad, Jalgaon has also informed the Commissioner, Divyang Kalyan, Pune that there will be no problem if the school is transferred and relocated as demanded by the institution and the education of the underprivileged children in the area will be facilitated. GOVERNMENT DECISION NO.: EDD 2013/ PR No.121/ DC.1
A proposal was under consideration of the Government to approve the transfer of Gyanranjan Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Jalgaon, Aurangabad Sanchalit Residential School, Nandgaon, G. Nashik from Nashik district to Jalgaon district from the year 2020-21.
Government's decision:
The Government has approved the transfer of Gyanranjan Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Aurangabad Sanchalit Residential School, Nandgaon, District Nashik from Nashik district to Jalgaon district with effect from 2020-21 subject to the following conditions:
(1) The orders of the Government issued from time to time in the present and future shall apply to the said institution.
(2) The existing rules and regulations of the scheme of recognition and grant of Divyang Special School to the said institution shall be binding from time to time.
3) The institution concerned will have to bear all the expenses related to the transfer and relocation of the Disabled Residential School. A refund will not be issued.
