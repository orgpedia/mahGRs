Babasaheb Ambedkar Residential Matimand School, Mohadi, G. Bhandara. As per the government notification dated August 29, 2018, the number of residential students was approved from 40 to 55. The said Government decision is being given ex-post facto approval on grant basis subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: (1) The expenditure on the salary and non-salary matters of the said school shall be brought to the notice of the Legislature by way of a figurative supplementary demand.
2) After the approval of the Legislature on the expenditure on the said school, the proposal regarding the approval of the post should be submitted to the Finance Department for the approval of the High Level Committee. All the terms and conditions will be applicable from 29th August 2018.
