Government's decision:
For the financial year 2021-22, a budgetary provision of Rs. 102,77,62, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Two Crores Seventy Seven Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand only) has been approved for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Subsidy Scheme for District Establishments and Rs. 39,01,36, 000/- (Rupees Nineteen Crores One Lakh Thirty Six Thousand only) for the Establishments on the Scheme for Financial Assistance to Old Landless Agricultural Labourers. Of these, the Finance Department has referred to No. 10% of the budgetary provision approved under the Government Decision herein for the purpose of 01-Salary. 4 Under the decision of the Government here, 5% of the amount has been distributed to all the Divisional Commissioner's Office for the purpose of 01 salary and 10% of the amount for 06- telephone, electricity and water and 13- office expenses. Now the Finance Department has released 10% of the approved budgetary allocation for the purpose of 01-Salary. Accordingly, a sum of Rs. 9,89,92, 700/- (Rupees Nine Crores Ninety Nine Lakhs Ninety Two Thousand Seven Hundred Only) for District Establishments and Rs. 3,87,13, 100/- (Rupees Three Crores Eighty Seven Lakhs Thirteen Thousand Only) for Establishments on the Scheme for Financial Assistance to Old Landless Agricultural Labourers is being sanctioned by the Government to all the Divisional Commissioner's Offices for the month of June and July, 2021 for the purpose of 01-salary as per the statement accompanying this Government decision.
All the Divisional Commissioners are hereby informed that they should allocate the grants distributed as per the prospectus A and B attached herewith to the districts under their jurisdiction as per their requirement and the District Collectors should allocate the grants to the taluks of their districts as per their requirement.
