Government's decision:
6450 temporary posts in the establishment of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune. 1.09.2020 to dt. To be continued till 28.2.2021. This has been approved vide Government Order dated 10.9.2020.
Provisional posts included in the format of the Administrative Department under the Government Decision, Finance Department dated 15.2.2021, as well as all existing temporary posts that were created after the approval of the previous review but were not included in the format. 01.03.2021 to dt. All administrative departments have been empowered to extend the deadline till 31.08.2021.
The remaining 6450 temporary posts in A to T (excluding C& T) in the accompanying statement on establishment of Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune will be filled up with effect from 1.03.2021 subject to the following terms and conditions: Continuance up to 31.08.2021 is being approved.- (1) The extension is being granted subject to the assumption that other temporary posts, excluding executive posts, are not vacant for more than six months.
2) The reasons for which the temporary posts have been sanctioned, subject to the terms and conditions, should be complied with.
(3) As from the date on which the officer/ employee holding the post of Superintendent is absorbed in the other posts, the said posts shall stand automatically abolished.
