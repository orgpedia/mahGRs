The Proposal:
The reference No. The Government has approved the registration of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (MAHAJYOTHI) under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 for the social, educational and economic development of the Vimukta Jati, Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes, Special Backward Classes and other backward and neglected sections as decided by the Government/ MAHAJYOTHI.
The reference No. 2 As per the Government Memorandum mentioned herein, the said Mahajyoti has been paid Rs. 1.00 crore and Rs. A sum of Rs. 4.00 crore has been sanctioned. In the said Government Memorandum, it was stated that the non-salaried grant funds cannot be spent for any scheme/ purpose without the administrative approval of the Government under Schedule 3. The matter of making a provision for spending the funds with the approval of the board of directors of Mahajyoti was under the consideration of the government.
Government Decision: The Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Research and Training Institute (Mahajyoti), Nagpur, under the Other Backward Bahujan Welfare Department, considering the funds/ provisions approved in the budget, the various projects/ schemes to be implemented with the approval of the Board of Directors should be spent within the limit of the funds sanctioned to the Institute. The project has to be approved by the government. After the approval of the concerned government, the schemes should be implemented by selecting the beneficiaries with the approval of the board of directors. The Mahajyoti Sanstha undertakes schemes/ projects within the limits of the funds sanctioned by the Government in the budget
