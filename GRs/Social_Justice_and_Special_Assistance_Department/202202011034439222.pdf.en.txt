The amount will be transferred directly to the bank accounts of the beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).
Commissioner, Social Welfare Commissionerate, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as Drawing and Disbursing Officer for spending the said fund.
The Controlling Officer shall spend the funds in Rakaana 4 of the above-mentioned statement. Once the funds are spent, the details and utilisation certificates should be submitted to the government without fail.
It is being certified that every aspect mentioned in the Government Circular, Government Decision, Finance Department, dated 6th April, 2021, dated 24th June, 2021, dated 29th July, 2021 and dated 14th October, 2021 respectively, has been fulfilled.
