In order to provide the necessary skills and knowledge to the students of the Scheduled Castes (including neo-Buddhists) and to prepare them for the competitive era in various fields, the Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship Scheme for higher education in reputed educational institutions of the country has been implemented under the Government Decision Social Justice, Cultural Affairs, Sports and Special Assistance Department No. EBC-2003/ PR No. 323/ MAVK-2, dated 11 June 2003. By superseding all the earlier Government decisions in respect of the Rules of the Scheme, the revised Rules of the Scheme have been brought into force with effect from the Government decision dated 08.11.2017.
The Selection Committee headed by Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, as mentioned in the said Government Decision, had submitted a proposal to the Government vide their letter dated 4.1.2022, to conduct the selection process for the students admitted to the reputed educational institutions in the country for the year 2021-22 and sanction the scholarship to the selected students as per the terms and conditions specified in the applications received thereunder. In pursuance of the above proposal, a Government Resolution dated 2 nd February, 2022 regarding selection of students for the year 2021-22 under the Scheme has been issued, wherein a total of 26 students have been selected.
Thereafter, in view of the fact that a total of 74 seats remain vacant under the scheme for the year 2021-22, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, in pursuance of the direction of the Government to fill up the remaining seats as per the provisions of Article No. (4) of the Government Decision dated 8th November, 2017 (A) on the mode of applying for the scholarship, had conducted a meeting of the Selection Committee on 6.4.2022. Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune has submitted a proposal to the government to give the benefit of this scheme to a total of 18 new students. In pursuance of the said proposal, a total of 18 students were under consideration to be given the benefit of Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship Scheme.
Government Decision: Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for SC students to pursue higher education in reputed educational institutions of the country, has been constituted under Government Decision, dated 08.11.2017.

Out of the students selected in the meeting dated 6.4.2022, a total of 18 eligible students in Form A-1 to A-3 and Form B-1 to B-2 attached with this Government decision are being sanctioned Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for the year 2021-22.
2.The students who have been approved the scholarship, the full tuition fee and examination fee fixed by the respective educational institution will be given to the institution through the Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune.
The students will be provided accommodation and food as per their choice. The amount will be paid annually. A total of Rs.10,000/- per annum will be provided for books, academic materials and other educational expenses required for the course. For their course expenses, the information regarding tuition fees, examination fees and hostel rent, living allowance and other permissible expenses for the year 2021-22 should be sought from the concerned university/ institution and the certificate of progress and attendance of the student should be obtained and paid to the concerned college/ institution.
3. The students who have been sanctioned Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship in the accompanying Forms A-1 to A-3, Form B-1 to Form B-2, the amount of the post-matric scholarship received by the Government of India should be reduced and the remaining amount should be paid to the students.
The Commissioner, Social Welfare, State of Maharashtra, Pune is being authorized to award scholarships to the said students.
The expenditure in this regard shall be met from the provisions of the current financial year by making expenditure under the head" Demand No.N-3, 2225, Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Minorities, Welfare of 01 Scheduled Caste, 277 Education, Schemes under Five Year Plans, Schemes under State Schemes." For this, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune is being declared as" Drawing and Disbursing Officer."
