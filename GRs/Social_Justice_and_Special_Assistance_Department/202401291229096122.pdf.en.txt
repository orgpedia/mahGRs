Proceedings regarding allotment of Kunbi-Maratha caste certificate and caste validity certificate have been initiated by the competent authorities. However, based on the revenue records evidence of the Kunbi caste, Maratha-Kunbi, Kunbi-Maratha caste persons found, the government is facing difficulties in issuing the caste certificate and caste validity certificate to the eligible persons and matching their families as follows:
It has been observed that: -
The revenue evidence found is from the period 1860 to 1947 and does not mention the surname in front of the citizen's name and father's name, so today the applicant has difficulty in proving the genealogy.
2. The genealogy of records found today is difficult to match as the then citizens either sold their native village lands or migrated from the village for other reasons.
3. There is often no strong evidence to link the found revenue records to today's applicant generation, resulting in decisions to be made solely on the basis of the applicant's affidavit.

In view of the difficulties in proving the genealogy with respect to the Kunbi, Kunbi-Maratha, Maratha-Kunbi caste records without any change in this established system, it was under the consideration of the Government to set up a committee as follows to simplify the process of proving this genealogy.
The decision: -
Caste certificates and caste validity certificates should be issued to persons whose ancestry clearly matches, while maintaining the existing practice of issuing caste certificates and caste validity certificates of Kunbi, Maratha-Kunbi, Kunbi-Maratha caste to eligible persons belonging to the Maratha community. In view of the difficulties faced in proving the genealogy with respect to the records of the Kunbi, Maratha-Kunbi, Kunbi-Maratha castes without any change in this established system, it is directed to set up the following committees to facilitate the process of proving the genealogy: -
In cases where sufficient evidence is not available to match the genealogy or the applicant is not aware of how such evidence should be made available, a Taluk-wise Committee is being constituted under the Chairmanship of the Tehsildar as follows: -
