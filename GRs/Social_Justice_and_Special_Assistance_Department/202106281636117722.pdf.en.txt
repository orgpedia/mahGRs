The proposal:
The Maharashtra Academy of Research, Upliftment and Training (AMRUT) has been set up as a Society registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and the Public Trust Act, 1950 (Bombay Act 29 of 1950).
The Government was considering to approve the proposed Memorandum of Association and Rules for registration of Amrut Societies under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and the Public Trusts Arrangement Act, 1950 (Bombay Act No. 29 of 1950).
Government's decision:
The Government hereby approves the accompanying Memorandum of Association and Rules for AMRUT
