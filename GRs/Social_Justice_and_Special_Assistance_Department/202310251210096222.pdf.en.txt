Read: 1) Revenue and Forest Department, Government Order No. Promotion-2021/ Q. No. 254/ E-1, D. 04.02.2022 GOVERNMENT ORDER Smt. Jyotsna Vidyasagar Hirmukhe (Padiyar), Addl. 1 Department of Revenue and Forest, Govt. 04.02.2022 and has been posted as Chairman, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Ratnagiri. According to that. He joined the post on 08.02.2022. However, due to administrative reasons, the said post is being changed and Smt. Jyotsna Vidyasagar Hirmukhe (Padiyar) is being transferred from the post of Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Ratnagiri to the post of Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Raigad.
2. Mrs. Jyotsna Vidyasagar Hirmukhe (Padiyar), Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Ratnagiri, shall hold the additional charge of Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Raigad, Ratnagiri, till further orders.
3. Further, the additional charge of the post of Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Sindhudurg, held by Mrs. Jyotsna Vidyasagar Hirmukhe (Padiyar), Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Ratnagiri, is being terminated and the post is being handed over to Additional Shri Suresh Jadhav, Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Kolhapur, till further orders.
4. Mrs. Jyotsna Vidyasagar Hirmukhe (Padiyar), Chairperson, District Caste Certificate Verification Committee, Ratnagiri, should immediately report to the Government and the Director General, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute, Pune.
(5) The concerned officers who have been given additional charge shall hold the additional charge of the post assigned to them while discharging the duties and responsibilities of the original post.
6. The said orders are being issued with the approval of the competent authority.
