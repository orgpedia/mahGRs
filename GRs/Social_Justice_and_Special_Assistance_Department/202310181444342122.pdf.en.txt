Government Decision: In the financial year 2023-24, for the implementation of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhi Subsidy Scheme for Scheduled Tribe beneficiaries, Rs. A budgetary provision of Rs. 120,00,00, 000/- (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Crores only) has been approved. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 60,00,00, 000/- in respect of reference no. 3 and 5 have been distributed to all the district collectors. At present, the Finance Department has sanctioned Rs. 60,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixty Crores only) has been released. Accordingly, for the period April, 2023 to March, 2024, the amount sanctioned for the Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme for Scheduled Tribe beneficiaries is Rs. 60,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Sixty Crores only) is hereby approved by the Government to be distributed as per the accompanying prospectus under this Government Resolution. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
2. All the Collectors are informed that in the accompanying statement, Column No. Funds have been distributed to the respective districts on 3 New Co Code. The grants distributed by them should be distributed to the taluks of their districts in proportion to the beneficiaries. The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
3. The expenditure from the said fund should be spent under the head" Demand No. T-5, Main Account Head 2235-Social Security and Welfare, 02-Social Welfare, 796, Tribal Area Sub Plan, (02) Out of Tribal Area Scheme (OTSP), (02) (03) Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Subsidy Scheme (Programme), 50 Other Expenditure, (2235 C 161)."

4. All the District Collectors are informed to ensure that these payments are deposited in the treasury. Also, they should ensure that the expenditure does not exceed the subsidy given to them under any circumstances.
5. All the District Collectors are also informed that they should send a copy of the reconciliation statement to the Audit Office of this Department and to this Office by matching the expenditure made from the grants handed over to them with the expenditure recorded in the Accountant General's Office. Utilization certificate should be sent to the Accountant General's office immediately and its copy should be sent to the planning office of this department and this office. If the expenditure reconciliation work is not done properly and the utilization certificate is not submitted to the Accountant General's office within the prescribed time, the entire responsibility will be on the concerned district collector.
6. Informal Reference of the Finance Department to this Government Resolution, No. 903/ Expenditure-14, D. 05th October, 2023 and Tribal Development Department, No. BUD-2023/ S.No. 06/ Office- 6, Dy. It is being issued as per the agreed terms by October 17, 2023.
