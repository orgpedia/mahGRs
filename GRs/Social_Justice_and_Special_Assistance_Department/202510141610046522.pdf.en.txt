The Proposal: -
Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar was an age-old man and the architect of the creation of modern India. He was a learned jurist, economist, social reformer, and visionary leader. Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, as the architect of the Indian Constitution, has laid the foundation for the development of modern India based on the values of democracy, liberty, social justice, equality, and fraternity enshrined in the Constitution. He fought throughout his life for the abolition of untouchability, the spread of education, gender equality, and labour rights. This great WAM of Maharashtra has created a bright future not only for Maharashtra but for the whole of India. He has laid the foundation for the all-round progress of modern India by giving concrete ideas on education, higher development, water resources planning, industrial policies. India completes 75 years of its Constitution and is on its way to becoming a superpower. Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar has a huge contribution behind it.
The Government of Maharashtra has decided to celebrate the 125th birth anniversary year of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar with the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government of Maharashtra. As per serial number 6 in Part-I of the programme defined under 09.10.2015, development of sites associated with the life of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar, sites of historical cultural importance to SCs and neo-Buddhists has been approved.
Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar. On 8 July 1945, he founded "The People's Education Society," an educational institution in Maharashtra to provide higher education to the Scheduled Castes and other middle classes. It has been more than 75 years since the establishment of schools / colleges / hostels established by Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar on behalf of the institution; the structures are dilapidated. The government receives several proposals to renovate these buildings with renovations and repairs. The People's Education Society was founded by Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar.

Method of operation: -
Under this scheme, detailed proposals for the construction of the aforesaid Institute B Habitat Buildings for livelihood under The People's Education Society will be submitted to the SAGAS Proposal Government by the Assistant Commissioner of the concerned district along with all the required documents (Project Plan, Lay-out Plan, Work Budget, Feasibility Report certified by the Executive Engineer of the concerned department of the implementing agency).
2. The Assistant Commissioner concerned shall examine all the proposals received / prepared by him / her and, if any, rectify the errors and submit self-explanatory feedback with recommendations to the Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Regional Department.
3. The Regional Deputy Commissioner, Social Welfare, Regional Department shall submit the proposal along with their self-explanatory feedback to the Commissioner, Commissionerate of Social Welfare, Pune. The Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune along with his / her self-explanatory feedback will submit the proposals of all the institutions to the Government for approval.
