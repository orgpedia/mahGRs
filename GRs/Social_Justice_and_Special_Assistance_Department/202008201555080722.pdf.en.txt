Government's decision:
The government has taken the decision based on the proposal. 8/3/2019 and dt. Action should be taken as per the decision of the Government in the context of inclusion of the names of employees working in Higher Secondary Ashramshalas in the list with 17/9/2019 in the service system and payment of their salaries online.
The government has taken a decision. 8/3/2019 and dt. Employees working in Higher Secondary Ashramshalas listed with 17/9/2019 will be allowed to pay salary offline only till March 2021. After March 2021, employees whose salary is not paid online through the service system will not be able to pay the salary offline.
