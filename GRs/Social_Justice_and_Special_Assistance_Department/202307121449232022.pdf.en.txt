Government's decision:
The development of a memorial of Luhiji Vastad Salve in Sangamwadi, Pune district, to commemorate the 125th birth anniversary of Babasaheb Ambedkar has been included in the project. Accordingly, it is being approved to constitute the following committee to regulate the above memorial and other ancillary matters.
