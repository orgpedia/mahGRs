The proposal: Construction of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Auditorium at Matunga Labour Camp, Dharavi, Mumbai City under Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana at a cost of Rs. 5.00 crore has been approved in principle. The proposal for construction of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Auditorium at Matunga Labour Camp, Dharavi, Mumbai City was under consideration of the Government for grant of administrative approval.
Government's decision:
Proposal for construction of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Auditorium at Matunga Labour Camp, Dharavi, Mumbai City at a cost of Rs. 5.00 crore under Babasaheb Ambedkar Samajik Vikas Yojana The administrative approval is being granted subject to the provisions of Sections 1 and 2 as well as the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions:
The proposal should be in accordance with the terms and conditions, provisions and rules of the present scheme.
The proposal should be in accordance with the provisions of the Financial Powers Rules i.e. with the recommendation of the Maharashtra Housing Area Development Authority (MHADA).
The proposal should be in accordance with the provisions of Public Works Department Circular dated 25th September, 2019 for grant of administrative approval.
The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC)/ concerned local bodies should obtain a no-objection certificate for the proposal of new construction and its construction plan.
V. An undertaking should be obtained from the concerned local bodies that they are ready to maintain and repair the said House.
