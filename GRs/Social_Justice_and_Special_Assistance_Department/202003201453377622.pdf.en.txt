Preamble: The State Government has celebrated the birth centenary of noted litterateur, leader of the Samyukta Maharashtra movement, Sahityaratna, Loktantra Anna Bhai Sathe. Starting from 1st August 2019. It has been decided to celebrate the day on 1st August, 2020 and a supplementary demand of Rs.100 crore has been approved in the July, 2019 session.
To plan and manage the birth centenary celebrations. Government Decision of 30 July 2019. A committee has been formed under the chairmanship of the Minister of Social Justice for the centenary of Anna Bhai Sathe. The committee In the meeting held on 11/3/2020, the outline of the year-long programs to celebrate the birth centenary of Loktantrik Anna Bhai Sathe has been decided. The minutes of the meeting have been issued vide letter dated 11/3/2020. The matter of administrative approval of the programs fixed by the committee was under consideration of the government.
Government's decision:
To outline the year-long activities to mark the birth centenary year of Sahityaratna Janadhikar Anna Bhai Sathe, Reference No. The Government has sanctioned a sum of Rs. 2. Administrative sanction is being accorded for an expenditure of Rs. 100.24 crore (Rupees One Hundred and Twenty Four Lakh only).
