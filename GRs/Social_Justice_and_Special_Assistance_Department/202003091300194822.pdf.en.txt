Government Decision: For the financial year 2019-20, a budget provision of Rs. 200,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred Crores only) has been approved for implementation of the Centrally Sponsored Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme and a supplementary demand of Rs. 200,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred Crores only) has been approved in the session of December, 2019. An amount of Rs. 200,00,00, 000/- has been released to the beneficiaries of Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNAPS) for the period April to December, 2019 as per the Government decision in Reference No. 4, 5 and 7. Now, the Department of Finance has approved the release of 100% of the Supplementary Demand amounting to Rs. 200,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred Crores only) in the session December, 2019.
Accordingly, the Government hereby approve the release of Rs. 200,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Two Hundred Crores only) towards the cost of Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme for the period January, 2020 to March, 2020 as per the accompanying Government Resolution. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
