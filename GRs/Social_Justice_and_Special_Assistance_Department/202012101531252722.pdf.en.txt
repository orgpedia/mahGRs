Letter from the Commissioner of Social Welfare, dated 25th July, 2019 and 20th October, 2020.
Introduction: In the year 2015, when the Government of India and the Government of Maharashtra are celebrating the 125th birth anniversary of Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar in a big way, the Central Government has announced the Stand Up India Scheme. The Government has decided to provide margin money to the Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist community under this scheme. The decision has been taken by the cabinet. Under this decision, it has been approved to provide a maximum of 15% margin money (front and subsidy) out of 25% of the total project cost to the beneficiaries of the new industrial beneficiaries belonging to the Scheduled Castes and neo-Buddhist sections who are eligible to take the concessions of the Scheduled Castes of the State of Maharashtra under the Stand Up Scheme of the Central Government.
Under this scheme, the new entrepreneurs belonging to the Scheduled Castes and the neo-Buddhist sections eligible for the concessions for the Scheduled Castes in the state of Maharashtra are given 15% of the remaining front and subsidy by the state government after paying 10% of the self-interest and the bank approves 75% of the loan to the applicant under the Stand Up India scheme.
As per the above mentioned Read 2 letters, in pursuance of the above mentioned Read 1 Government decision, it was under the consideration of the Government to issue revised orders/ guidelines detailing the procedure to be followed in respect of certain matters.
Government Decision: In accordance with the procedure to be followed for sanctioning the benefit of the Stand Up India scheme of the Central Government, i.e. while providing 15% margin money (front and subsidy), the accompanying proposal should be completed by the concerned applicant as per the checklist.
The competent authority should make available 15% margin money (front and subsidy) under the scheme as per the opinion of the examiner after checking the fulfillment of the same by the concerned applicants as per the said checklist.
