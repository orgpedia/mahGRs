Separate orders will be issued regarding the transfer of Mr. Vijay Gaikwad, Assistant Commissioner Social Welfare, Pune.
In order to ensure that the transfer order is executed promptly and efficiently, the following instructions shall be strictly followed:- 1. On receipt of the transfer order, the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune, shall assign the charge of the transferred officer to the transferred officer for his appointment to the post under paragraph no. (2) In other cases and as indicated in section 2, be immediately relieved of duty by handing over to other officers.
2.The transferred officer should be reinstated without delay when he comes to join after being relieved from the office concerned. Establishment officers concerned will be held responsible if there is a case of non-payment of salary and allowances due to non-recruitment of an officer by fixing a mandatory waiting period.
(3) If the officer concerned directly or indirectly tries to pressurize the officer to modify the transfer order, such officer shall be liable for disciplinary action.
