In the State of Maharashtra, 54 Social Work Colleges have been approved, out of which 1 is closed, 3 unaided and 50 aided Social Work Colleges are functioning. The post of Principal in the College of Social Work is that of the head of the office. A regular Principal is required to be employed for the day-to-day administrative and academic functioning of the college as well as for obtaining National Assessment and Accreditation Certificate (NAAC). Since the post of principal is administrative, the post of principal is required to be filled for the smooth functioning of the academic and related activities in the college.
