Government's decision:
A budgetary provision of Rs. 40,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Forty Crores only) has been approved for implementation of Centrally Sponsored Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme for the financial year 2020-21. Out of the budgetary allocation of Rs. 30,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Thirty Crores only) in respect of Fund Reference No. It has been distributed to all the district collector offices as per the government decision of 4.
The Government hereby approve the release of Rs. 10,00,00, 000/- (Rupees Ten Crores only) towards the cost of implementation of Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme for the period October, 2020 to March, 2021 as per the accompanying prospectus under the Government decision. Also, facility to draw forward/ reverse funds as per monthly statement has been provided on Budget Estimate Distribution and Control System.
2. All the Collectors are informed that in the accompanying statement, Column No. 3 Funds have been distributed to the respective districts on the new CO Code. The grants distributed by them should be distributed to the taluks of their districts in proportion to the beneficiaries. The subsidy has been distributed as per the number of beneficiaries informed by the District Collector.
The expenditure from the said fund should be spent under the head" Demand No.N-3, Main Account Head 2235 Social Security and Welfare, 104 Welfare of Elderly, Disabled and Destitute Persons, (08) (11) Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (Centrally Sponsored Scheme) (Programme), 50 Other Expenditure (2235B 031)."
