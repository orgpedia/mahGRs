Government's decision:
As a special case, provision has been made to fill up 10% of the seats falling vacant every year in all the government hostels except the hostels of meritorious students in the government hostels run for the students of backward classes under the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department.
2. As per the provisions of the said Government Decision, the list of students shown in Form-" A" to be admitted as private students in the Government Hostels for boys and girls of Backward Classes in the academic year 2021-22 is being approved under this Government Decision.
Students in the above list should be admitted subject to the following conditions:
The Assistant Commissioner, Social Welfare, concerned should ensure that no more students than the sanctioned student capacity of the hostel are allowed to enter the hostel.
The students are required to submit the Bonafide Certificate of the current year of the school/ college in which they are studying to the Home Guard of the hostel concerned.
All the Assistant Commissioners, Social Welfare should complete the process of admission to private sector immediately and submit the compliance report to the government through the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune within 30 days.
