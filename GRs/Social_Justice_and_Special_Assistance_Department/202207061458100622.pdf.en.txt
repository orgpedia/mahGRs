Omshanti Multi-purpose Sports and Educational Library Serviceable Rural Development Women's Board, Hangarga, Ta. Rajiv Gandhi Residential and Matimand Vidyalaya, Panachincholi, Dist. Post-facto approval is being granted on grant basis to the 40 residential students of Nilanga, G. Latur Aided Special School subject to the following terms and conditions.
Terms and Conditions: (1) The said school shall submit a symbolic supplementary demand to bring to the notice of the Legislature a proposal for increased expenditure under Part II of the new item.
2) After the approval of the supplementary demand, the proposal of the required posts should be submitted to the Finance Department.
3) The school board made the decision. All terms and conditions as of 6.9.2018 remain in effect.
