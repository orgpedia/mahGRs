Introduction: The Social Justice Department runs government hostels for backward class students and government residential schools for scheduled caste and neo-Buddhist boys and girls in the state. Considering the delay and various difficulties in providing facilities to educational institutions of backward classes, the government was considering the formation of a state-level administrative department procurement committee to carry out a transparent and non-partisan procurement process.
The decision is as follows:
GOVERNMENT DECISIONS: The Department of Industries, Power and Labour has laid down the procedures for procurement of offices to be made by Government departments under the Government Decision dated 1st December, 2016. The Department of Industry, Energy and Labor has directed to form a state-level administrative department procurement committee in accordance with 2.9.2 of the said government decision.
Accordingly, e-tendering process for the purchase of necessary facilities for government hostels and government residential schools under the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department is being conducted and the proposal is being set up in the following state level administrative department procurement committee for final approval.
Principal Secretary/ Secretary (A.M.) ). Commissioner of Social Welfare, Pune. Secretary, Department of Education (2), Shri. Department of Education (2). Co-Director of Industries (BHANKH)/ Representative of Directorate of Industries. Assistant Director (Finance and Accounts) and V.S. Department. ... invited members.
As mentioned above, the State Level Administrative Departments Procurement Committee shall examine the proposal received in the e-tendering process by following the procedure prescribed for procurement under the Government Decision dated 1.12.2016 of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour and recommend to the Government for final approval after completing the procurement process in time and in accordance with the prescribed procedure.
